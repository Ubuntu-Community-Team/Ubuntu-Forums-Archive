### Post by cjm5229 on 2006-01-21
Go to the website that Abowman gave you. That is The Ubuntu download page. Scroll down to the bottom of the page to where it says Download. choose the architecture you want then click on that. This will take you to a list of Mirrors. Pick one close to you and click on it. You might have to try several to get one to work correctly. After you have it downloaded you can burn the ISO to a CD with KB3, or Gnomebaker, or Nero if your in Windows. Hope this helps.
