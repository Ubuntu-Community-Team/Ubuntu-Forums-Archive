### Post by ashkool on 2009-05-02
Which one are you trying to use?

[http://www.gnome-look.org/content/show.php/Original+Yahoo+&+MSN+Emoticons+4+Pidgin?content=59794](http://www.gnome-look.org/content/show.php/Original+Yahoo+&+MSN+Emoticons+4+Pidgin?content=59794)

or

[http://code.google.com/p/pidgin-ym-smileys/wiki/HowTo](http://code.google.com/p/pidgin-ym-smileys/wiki/HowTo)

The 1st one, just drag & drop the .tar.gz file on to "Pidgin>Tools>Preferences>Smiley Themes"

The 2nd one, download and unzip the file. Move the folder "yahoo" to /home/$USER$/.purple/smileys, and select the icons pack through "Pidgin>Tools>Preferences>Smiley Themes"
