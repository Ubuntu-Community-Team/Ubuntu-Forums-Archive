### Post by lightstream on 2009-06-06
it's possible you knocked a mounting clip on your heatsink/fan so it now rattles a bit unless running slow.

More likely though is that the fan is just old, and you cleaning all the dust out of it means that it is now free to rattle about on its worn bearings, where previously the dust itself prevented the rattle.

### Post by dcraven on 2009-06-06
One option is to blow some canned air at it to see if that helps. I've had that do wonders for some reason. Another option is to take the fan off and reseat it with some new paste. Sometimes reseating the fan will stop the rattling. Another option, and not to be overlooked, is to replace the fan altogether. They aren't that expensive generally. The thing is, that those bearings are not meant to run with a vibration like that and it will shorten the lifespan of the fan if it continues. 

Trust me when I say you do not want that thing to stop spinning :).
