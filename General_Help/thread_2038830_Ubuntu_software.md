### Post by Mark Phelps on 2012-08-08
Folks shouldn't say that connecting your phone via USB is "guaranteed to work" -- because, it is NOT.

IF it works, you should then see an icon popup on your desktop, or in the sidebar at the bottom left, which will be the filesystem of your phone.
