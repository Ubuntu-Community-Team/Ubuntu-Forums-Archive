### Post by dazvansgillio on 2013-06-10
On Linux, you need to configure it in [B]Tools|Options &#8594; Internet &#8594; E-Mail
Just tried this on Libreoffice 4.0.3.3
Mine was set to sensible-lomua.
Changed it to Thunderbird and it works.
[/B]
