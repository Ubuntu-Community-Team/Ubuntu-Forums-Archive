### Post by Comhra on 2008-03-09
Aaron, Extract them to the desktop, go gksudo nautilus, navigate to:usr/share/icons and drop them in the icons folder.

Then click: System/Preferences/Appearance/Theme/Customise/Icons.  Scroll down until you find them and select.
