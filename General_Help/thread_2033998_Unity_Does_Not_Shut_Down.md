### Post by evilsoup on 2012-07-29
I don't know if it's the same issue - I haven't installed any extra DEs - but I occasionally  (once every few weeks) have the same problem.  The computer refuses to shut down. What I do to 'fix' it is to force a shutdown in a terminal (sudo shutdown -h now): after the next startup, the problem is gone.

Until I do whatever it was to trigger it again. Hopefully that will help you.
