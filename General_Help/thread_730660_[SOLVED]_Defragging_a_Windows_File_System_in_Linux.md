Just thought I'd answer my own question. Of which the thread was locked due to crossposting. Anyway I have Crossover install and I should imagine Wine would also be a candidate for this. Download either of those. Then search on Google for a free Windows defragger. Download as many as you possibly can, you will find one that works I promise you. For me it was.

Defraggler

I have others. However, I have yet to experiment with installing them. But Defraggler picks up my USB 250Gb Drive that's FAT32 fine and defrags it.

Cheers.

ETA: After more experimentation it turns out that Power Defragmenter, is the best I have come across so far.
