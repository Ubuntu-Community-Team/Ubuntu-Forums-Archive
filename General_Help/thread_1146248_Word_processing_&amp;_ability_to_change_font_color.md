### Post by ajgreeny on 2009-05-02
Hi again texas.chef,  It's all there in the OOo help menu.
  	 	 	 	 	 	  ** Font Effects**

 Specify the font effects that you want to use.
  	 	 		 			To access this 			command...
 			 			Choose **Format - Character - 			Font Effects** tab
 			Choose 			**Format - Styles and Formatting** - open context menu and 			choose **Modify/New - Font Effects** tab
 		 	  


 The changes are applied to the current selection, to the entire word that contains the cursor, or to the new text that you type.
 

but it's much easier to customise a toolbar and add the font colour icon.

### Post by soro2005 on 2009-05-02
Enable the "Formatting" toolbar. (--> View --> Toolbars --> Formatting). If it's enabled and you don't see the font color button, click on the little down arrow at the right margin of the Formatting toolbar, --> "Visible buttons" and select "Font color."
