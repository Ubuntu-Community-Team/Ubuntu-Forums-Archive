### Post by undecim on 2010-09-08
If you disabled the bars themselves, right click on the bar that has "File", "Edit", etc. You should have options there to add the menus back

If you deleted the components from the bars, right click on a bar and click "Customize". There is a button that will reset your bars to default, or you can add your components back to where you like the by drag-and-drop
