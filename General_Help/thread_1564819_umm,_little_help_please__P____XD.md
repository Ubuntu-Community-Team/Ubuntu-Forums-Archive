### Post by wojox on 2010-08-31
For your icons Open

Alt+F2

Type: *gconf-editor*

Go to Apps > Nautilus > Desktop and check to make the icon visable.

On your Workspace Switcher Applet

Right click and select Preferences > Columns = 1
