### Post by wannadumpwindows on 2008-04-27
Go into Add/Remove programs, and do a search for "ppp". You'll get a big list of options. Gnome PPP seems to be what I used when I last had to do dial-up for a friend out in the sticks.

P.S. I think you can still even use KPPP in Ubuntu if you want to. It's also in the Add/Remove programs menu. It was the first one that popped up when I searched.
