### Post by oldfred on 2016-05-23
You may be in same state as some Dell laptops.
You will need 16.04, but may need ppa to get even newer kernel & drivers to have it fully work.

My Dell SFF with Haswell i3 just works with 16.04. But drivers/sputnik are all now included in standard distributions for that generation, so software has caught up with hardware. 
Your Skylake is very new and it may take a bit before everything catches up in standard distribution. In mean time you have to add ppa to get newer versions or test versions that may include update to make your system work.
