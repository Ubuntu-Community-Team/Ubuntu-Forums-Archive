### Post by bergfly on 2010-08-09
I'm not sure if this is the issue, but one of the things you may not know is that mixer does not show all the controls by default. Click on the system tray icon, then click the mixer button.

Once the mixer window is open, then go to Settings and click on Configure Channels. Here you have a whole list of options. Drag all those that you may need for recording and then click ok. Boost all the levels and try testing again...
