### Post by Spaceman9 on 2012-12-21
I'm using Ubuntu 12.04 LTS and I can't get any sound out of TuxGuitar. Sound works for everything else including but not limited to Hydrogen and the Totem Movie Player.

What do I have to do to hear the midi files TuxGuitar plays? Any help will be appreciated.

---

### Post by Spaceman9 on 2012-12-28
I discovered all I had to do was install the TiMidity++ MIDI sequencer package then go into "Settings" in TuxGuitar and set "Sound" to TiMidity port 0 [128:0].

Thankx for all your help guys.
