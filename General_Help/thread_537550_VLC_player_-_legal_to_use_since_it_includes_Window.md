### Post by fragos on 2007-09-06
Fair question but I hesitate to provide legal advice since I'm not a lawyer.  When I create media, I prefer the open source formats or those published by their authors -- Flash for example or PDF.  One is at greater risk if they have acquired stolen media or produce media for sale in a format requiring a license.  As a user of legally acquired media like a free MP3 podcast one would hardly display malicious criminal intent.  Technology which isn't GPL licensed is not necessarily illegal to use.
