### Post by cmost on 2009-05-25
As I recently reported to another user with a similar problem, Lexmark printers are very poorly supported in Linux.  According to Openprinting.org, your Lexmark is a paperweight!  ([http://openprinting.org/show_printer.cgi?recnum=Lexmark-3300](http://openprinting.org/show_printer.cgi?recnum=Lexmark-3300))  I urge you to write Lexmark a letter and demand they provide some rudimentary driver support for your Operating System of choice.  That being said, HP and Epson printers are very well supported in Linux.  Sorry.
