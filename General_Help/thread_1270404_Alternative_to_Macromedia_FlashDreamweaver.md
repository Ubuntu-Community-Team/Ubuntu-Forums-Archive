### Post by coldReactive on 2009-10-04

He meant the actual editor for editing FLA Files, not a player.

A editor of flash files was made, but abandoned rather quickly. One of them was Flash4Linux on sourceforge, but then it was merged with another project, but then the new project also was abandoned.

So no, there isn't an alternative editor to flash, sadly.
