### Post by bodhi.zazen on 2010-09-07
IMO restore points have extremely limited value on Desktops.

Just back up your data and re-install.

It is helpful if you have a separate /home or /data partition.

[http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/separatehome](http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/separatehome)

[http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/installseparatehome](http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/installseparatehome)

Unless you have a number of custom configuration files re-installation is much easier.

On servers one often has more customization, although backing up critical data or /var/www can work as well.
