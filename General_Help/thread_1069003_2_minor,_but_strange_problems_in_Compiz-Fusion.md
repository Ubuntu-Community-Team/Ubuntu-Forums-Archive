### Post by gettinoriginal on 2009-02-14
Go to CCSM, Window Management, Ring Switcher, click on it, go to Window Title Display tab and make sure window title is checked
While in CCSM, go to Animations, click on it, minimize tab, highlight airplane, edit, change duration to suit you.  (If max is set right for you, you can do the max tab, highlight airplane, edit, and see what the settings are and apply the same to minimize)   :P
