you dont have to worry about addware/ spyware/ viruses on linux though if you by chance share files with windows using people you may wish to install something like clamAV to make sure you dont pass along something by mistake.
As for defragmentation, its not needed really as the linux filesystem ext3 doesnt fragment nearly as much as NTFS and there is a routine check of the filesystem every 20 boots or so.
