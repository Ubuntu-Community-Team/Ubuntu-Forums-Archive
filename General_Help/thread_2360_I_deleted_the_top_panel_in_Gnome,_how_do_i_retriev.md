### Post by castrojo on 2004-10-27
Right click on the bottom panel, and then choose "New Panel", you can then move the new panel to the top by middle click and dragging it to the top, and from there, right click, "Add to Panel"  and add stuff. You probably want to add the menu bar immediately.
