### Post by Scunizi on 2007-10-04
I never said jpg's weren't readable, just that they might be the wrong format for what you want to accomplish.  If you convert a multi page PDF to jpg I think you would end up with a jpg image for each page.  If your psp also reads tiff, tiff is capable of multiple pages within one file.. a little easier to manage.  You're psp might also be able to read text.  You might have more luck using "PDF Editor" (you'll have to google it).  PDF Editor will allow you to extract the text of the PDF to put in just about any format you want.
