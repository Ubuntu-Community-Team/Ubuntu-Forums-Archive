### Post by fabounet on 2010-05-22
it's a know bug in the nVidia drivers (don't know if other cards have it too).
you can enter the config and hit apply, or just resize a little the desklet (with alt + middle-click) to refresh the applet
another solution is to launch the dock in "cairo" mode.
