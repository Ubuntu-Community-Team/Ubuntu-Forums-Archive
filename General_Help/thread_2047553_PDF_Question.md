### Post by ajgreeny on 2012-08-25
Or simply open the PDF, go to **File -print** it but choose only the page you want, not the whole document, and then "Print to file -PDF" instead of your usual printer.

A bit cluncky perhaps, but everything you need is already on your Ubuntu machine.
