### Post by kerry_s on 2015-01-05
that means there's no icon for it.
what i would do is go to /usr/share/applications copy the 1's you want to change icons for and put the in ~/.local/share/applications then just open with a text editor and change the "icon=* " line, you can use full path to a icon or a name of a icon the exists.
