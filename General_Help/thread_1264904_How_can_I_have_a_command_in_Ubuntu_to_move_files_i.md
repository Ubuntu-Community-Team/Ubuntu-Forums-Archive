### Post by Rhubarb on 2009-09-12
When you drag your selected files to where you want to move them to, press and hold the Alt button, then release the mouse button.

You'll be then presented with a menu asking you if you wish to Move, Copy, or Link here.

Alternatively you can cut and paste the files which has the same effect as moving them.

### Post by stderr on 2009-09-12
When you shift files around in a graphical interface, you make use of a clipboard. This remembers the files you had selected when you do a 'copy' or 'cut'. Then, when you do a 'paste' later on in the location you want the files to be, it retrieves the selected filenames from the clipboard, and either copies (=COPY) or moves (=CUT) them depending on the originally chosen operation. This is standardised across operating systems.
