### Post by snowpine on 2009-07-07
I think you might be very happy with sidux (an independent distro based on Debian unstable). It is rolling release, has KDE by default, and is an easy transition from Ubuntu. Another one worth considering is Arch; the Chakra live CD is a good demonstration of the possibilities with Arch+KDE. Honorable mention goes to Debian testing (squeeze); it is also rolling release, but it is conservative rolling release, not "bleeding edge." Good luck!
