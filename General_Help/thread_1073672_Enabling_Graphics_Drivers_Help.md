### Post by cariboo on 2009-02-18
For some reason the driver won't download, go to System-->Administration-->Synaptic Package Manager and search for **nvidia-glx-177**, mark it for installation and click apply. Once the driver is dwonloaded go to System-->Administration-->Hardware Drivers and enable it.
