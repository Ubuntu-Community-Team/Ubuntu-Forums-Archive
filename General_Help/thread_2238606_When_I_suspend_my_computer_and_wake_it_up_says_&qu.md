### Post by Dennis N on 2014-08-08
**dpm** would stand for **d**isplay **p**ower **m**anagement. This probably was output when the computer was put into suspend and apparently tells you the monitor could not be put into the low power state.

See: 
[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/VESA_Display_Power_Management_Signaling](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/VESA_Display_Power_Management_Signaling)
[http://www.tldp.org/HOWTO/Battery-Powered/displaytypes.html](http://www.tldp.org/HOWTO/Battery-Powered/displaytypes.html)
