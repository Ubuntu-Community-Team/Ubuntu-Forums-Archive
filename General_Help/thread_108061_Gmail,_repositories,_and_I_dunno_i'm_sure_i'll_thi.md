### Post by cmcgeecc on 2005-12-24
This answer is only for the gmail notification and the repositories questions.  I don't like how the ubuntuguide tells you to add repositories because it's out of date.  So I just use the graphical synaptic package manager and click settings, then repositories, then in the repositories window click add and then make sure all of the boxes are checked for each repository you click in the drop box on top.

After you update your repositories, you can search gmail in synaptic and you should find a gmail notifier for gnome.  I use it on my Desktop computer and it works great.  I'd tell you the name of it, but I'm out of town for the holidays and don't have access to my Desktop.

Hope this helps.
