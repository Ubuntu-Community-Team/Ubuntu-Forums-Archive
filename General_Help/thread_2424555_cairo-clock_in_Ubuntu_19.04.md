### Post by PaulW2U on 2019-08-10
cairo-clock was removed from Ubuntu 18.04 in March 2018, i.e. during its development period, as it had already been removed from Debian. The package hadn't been updated for years so any 'deb' that you may have is unlikely to work properly or at all with recent or future releases of Ubuntu.

For ref: [https://bugs.debian.org/cgi-bin/bugreport.cgi?bug=893769](https://bugs.debian.org/cgi-bin/bugreport.cgi?bug=893769)
