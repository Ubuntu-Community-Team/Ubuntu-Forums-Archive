### Post by OutOfReach on 2008-10-15
To be on the safe side you should burn at a slower speed, not necessarily the lowest speed possible, but maybe something like x18, or x21.

---

### Post by Sam on 2008-10-15
It should work at full speed, but keep in mind that the burning will be "less good" as if it was made at a lower speed. So if you plan to use you cd/dvd with an old drive later this may lead to read errors.

Personally I always use full speed except for stuff I may keep for a long time or backups.
