### Post by arpanaut on 2012-12-08
> Just want to be safe before upgrading, as I have a lot of work in progress that I can't afford to lose time on.
By all means back up your data, in fact that should be an ongoing SOP.
If you can't afford down time. I would not endeavour to do two back to back upgrades.
Too many things can possibly go wrong and may take time to fix.

Two Ideas.
If you have adequate disk space available, carve out some partition space and install 12.04 there and start building that up to accomplish your work load. Transfer your data later or use a shared space for your data. Thus not loosing the stability and usefulness of your present install.

Purchase a second hard drive and install 12.04 and build up to suit your needs and migrate to that install at your leisure. (at a later date you could try the upgrades on your original install)

Just some suggestions.
