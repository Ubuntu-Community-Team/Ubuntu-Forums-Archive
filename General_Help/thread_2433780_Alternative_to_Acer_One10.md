### Post by ajgreeny on 2019-12-26
I don't really know that machine but I suspect that you may have more success trying a much less resource-hungry distro than straight Ubuntu such as Lubuntu or Xubuntu.  You may also have to set "trust" in the BIOS for some Acer machines.

If you're prepared to look at alternatives, try Debian-10 with the LXDE GUI; I have found that even faster than Lubuntu on an old netbook with Atom 270N cpu and only 1G RAM; it's not speedy but it is certainly usable if I avoid libreoffice, Gimp and other high resource packages.  I also kep away from firefox or chromium, and use epiphany-browser instead
