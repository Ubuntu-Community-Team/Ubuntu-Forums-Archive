### Post by cascade9 on 2010-10-31
Possibly, its a huge problem. 

If your CPU fan isnt  attached or working then you will have problems with overheating. Its possible that the CPU fan is working, just whoever built it attached it to the wrong fan header. I'd guess with the "no hard disc detected" msg that somebody didnt finish of the assembly job, and failed to attach the CPU fan, HDD power and or SATA cable, and possibly forgot other things as well. 

Probably the easiest way to check this would be to take the side of teh case and check visually. If your not confident doing that, then take it back to where you bought it and get it fixed.
