### Post by Tenken on 2007-12-13
Network Monitor is the gnome panel applet, if that isn't what you're looking for you most likely removed your notification area. Network Manager is a start up service (you turn it on and off in System->Preferences->Sessions) and it appears in the notification area when you log in. If your minimized Pidgin, Rhytmbox, etc don't show up that means you're missing the notification area.
