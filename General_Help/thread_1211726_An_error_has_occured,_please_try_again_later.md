### Post by JohnnySage50307 on 2009-07-13
Sometimes, but not often, there is a slight coruption in how flash is handled in Firefox.  As silly as this sounds, deactivate the flash plugin, restart, and then reactivate the plugin.  I've found (usually when I'm rushing and not paying attention) that I have conflicting plugins running; yes it's possible to have two flash players trying to play the same file--I did this on Opera once.  When you try to reactivate the plugin, if there is a conflict, it ought to recognize it and prompt you to make a decision.
 
Mind you, I haven't had this issue yet in Jaunty, but actually on my Vista laptop, and this was the solution for that.  It should be applicable in your circumstance.
