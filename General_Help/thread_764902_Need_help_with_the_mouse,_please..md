### Post by northern lights on 2008-07-25
> **Archimedes0212 said:**
> if you are running compiz on your computer you have to specify the mouse theme in Advanced Desktop Settings under General Options

No, you don't. I'm sorry, but this is misinformation.

Even if you're using compiz for effects and even with metacity disabled, icon & cursor themes for instance are still gtk.

Navigate to "System > Preferences > Appearance". Any theme you downloaded as a tarball you can just drag & drop on that window to initiate installation. 

While having a gtk theme selected (pick your metacity theme, if you're using e.g. emerald it doesn't matter what you pick) press the button below titled "Customize...". Here you can select icon & pointer themes.
