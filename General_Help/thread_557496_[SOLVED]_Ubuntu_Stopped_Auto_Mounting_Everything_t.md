### Post by victorgreen on 2007-09-22
Flash drive mounts now. I took out the last line out of fstab, this: /dev/sdb1 /media/Mobile ntfs-3g defaults,locale=en_US.utf8 0 0, is no longer there and my flashdrive auto mounts now.

thanks for the help, I was stupid to have that line in there, and Im not sure how it did get in there.... 


this thread is solved, at least for me
