[This is only my first week using my new favorite OS, so I'm fairly new but still]

After hours of changing the different sound output options (Analog  Duplex and such) the way that i found to work for me to get sound from  my headphones was to plus the headphones into the Mic socket, then after  a second or two later unplug them and plug them in the Headphone port. I  am unsure of why this works for me, but it does. If it also does for  you, please post.
