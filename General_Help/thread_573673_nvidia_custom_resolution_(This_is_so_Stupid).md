### Post by FuturePilot on 2007-10-13
You could try removing all of the resolutions other than the one you want. You can also get a custom refresh rate by adding something like
```
Section "Screen"
    Identifier    "Default Screen"
    Device        "Generic Video Card"
    Monitor        "Generic Monitor"
    Defaultdepth    24
   [COLOR="Red"] Option          "metamodes" "1856x1392_XX +0+0"[/COLOR] 
```
Where XX is your refresh rate (i.e 60 or whatever it is) and the 1856x1392 is your res.
