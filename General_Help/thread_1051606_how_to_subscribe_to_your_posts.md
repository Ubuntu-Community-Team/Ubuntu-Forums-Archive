### Post by Egi_Power on 2009-01-26
Hi there!

> how do I subscribe to my posts?

Before you submit your reply, there is an option on the bottom of the page, called *Thread Subscription*.
There you can select if you wish to receive email notifications instantly, daily or weekly.

> When I sign back in on a future date, how do I find my previous question in waiting? 

On the top of the page from the Search menu you could pick:
[I]Find all your threads
Find all your posts[/I]
