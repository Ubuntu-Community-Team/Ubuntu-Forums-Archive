### Post by coffeecat on 2010-10-08
Press alt-F2. Type 'gconf-editor' (without quotes) and click on run. Now apps > Nautilus > Desktop and tick the icons you want.

---

### Post by TBABill on 2010-10-08
Or if they are in your applications menu, just right click on each you want on the desktop and select "add this launcher to desktop".
