When I first posted about this problem it was the first time I was turning on the computer with this keyboard with ubuntu already installed and the keyboard didn't work until I unplugged/plugged it in.

Now after a reboot the keyboard works just fine without having to unplug it.  I didn't do anything to make this happen.  It must have just needed to make changes to a config (xorg.conf maybe) on that initial boot and now it works fine.

Not all of the multimedia keys work, but the volume up/down and mute work and that's just fine by me.  I guess the play/pause, stop next/previous buttons would be useful, but not necessary.
