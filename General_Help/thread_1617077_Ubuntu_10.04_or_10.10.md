### Post by mister_playboy on 2010-11-09
If your install is working fine, I wouldn't bother upgrading just for the heck of it.  Wait until something goes wrong and use that as an excuse to install the new version... that is actually what I'm doing right now. :)

I prefer a new install over an upgrade because the upgrades always leave you with a weird mix of new and old... better to just copy your stuff, wipe and start fresh.

You definitely want to download updates released for 10.04, however.  You can download that stuff without having to fully upgrade to 10.10.
