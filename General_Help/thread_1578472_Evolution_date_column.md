Can I change the Inbox date column format to show the calender date all the time (instead of Today, Yesterday, N Days ago)?

### Post by plucky on 2010-09-20
> **Gen Vert said:**
> Can I change the Inbox date column format to show the calender date all the time (instead of Today, Yesterday, N Days ago)?

In Evolution

**Edit > Preferences > Mail Preferences > Headers** and change **Table Column**

Good Luck
