### Post by 3rdalbum on 2011-11-10
> **linuxuser12345 said:**
> Has the battery life problem with Oneiric been fixed yet? I am deciding whether to put Natty or Oneiric on my sister's netbook, and I know I had trouble with Oneiric before with the battery life issue.

No Linux kernel has a universal battery life issue, that is, there's no kernel that causes low battery life on ALL computers. Battery life regressions are caused by workarounds to support certain hardware, and it's a relatively small number of people affected.

In other words, Oneiric will probably be fine on the netbook, assuming it's a different computer to the one you've been having issues with before.

I have Oneiric on my netbook and I haven't noticed any difference in battery life.
