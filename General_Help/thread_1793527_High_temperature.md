### Post by Frogs Hair on 2011-06-29
Fans on graphics cards work differently depending on the model . Mine has no fan control and always runs at the same speed . You need to know if your card has a fan speed control . There is some software that may work for certain Nvidia cards . If the fan never runs you may have another problem.
