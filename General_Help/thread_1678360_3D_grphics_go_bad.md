### Post by pme 72 on 2011-01-30
If you are dual booting with Windows XP the good news is that you should be able to download SuperTuxKart and play it just fine on your Windows partition. The news is not as promising with Ubuntu version 10.04. 

That video card would play SuperTuxKart on Intrepid 8.10 with the default open source driver, at least on my machine. Since then changes have decreased the performance of the Xpress 200 to the point where STK would not open for me. 
 
If those games are important to you and you have a desktop machine you now have a great excuse to go shopping for a video card.
