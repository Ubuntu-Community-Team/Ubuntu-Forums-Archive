I recently installed ubuntu and for the life of me I can't find the trash can anywhere.  How do I go about opening it and restoring items?

---

### Post by phansiizwe on 2007-01-13
You could right-click on your panel (Gnome menu bar) and select 'Add to panel...'
Then under **Desktop&Windows** you will find the Trashcan!

Double-click the icon and it will be added to your panel. You can access it from there.
