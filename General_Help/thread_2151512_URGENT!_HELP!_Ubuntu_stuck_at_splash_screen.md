### Post by snowpine on 2013-06-04
The "good news" is that Ubuntu 11.10 is "end of life" and completely unsupported, therefore it is a waste of your time to try and troubleshoot this issue. I recommend you boot from a Live CD, back up your documents/files from the hard drive to an external drive, and reinstall a currently supported version (12.04 or newer).

More info here: [http://fridge.ubuntu.com/2013/05/10/ubuntu-11-10-oneiric-ocelot-end-of-life-reached-on-may-9-2013/](http://fridge.ubuntu.com/2013/05/10/ubuntu-11-10-oneiric-ocelot-end-of-life-reached-on-may-9-2013/)
