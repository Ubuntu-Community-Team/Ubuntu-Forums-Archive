### Post by schlameel on 2008-12-11
I prefer to the use the "search button" to minimize windows.  There were lots of posts for reassigning the search button to mouse button 2, but I could not get any of the solutions (xbindkeys, xmodmap, others) to make that button minimize windows.  

After two days banging my head against the wall, I was a bit embarrassed to find:
  System -> Preferences -> Keyboard Shortcuts

I found, under "Window Management", "Minimize Window".  I clicked it, then clicked the search button.  It said the button was already assigned to Search and asked if I wanted to reassign it.  I confirmed and it works perfectly.
