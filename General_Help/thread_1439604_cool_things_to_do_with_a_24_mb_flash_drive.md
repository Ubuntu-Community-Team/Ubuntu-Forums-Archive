SSD does lose its luster after a while. It takes a long time, however. I read an article which explained that if you had a 100 GB SSD with 50 GB of constant data (OS, apps, etc) and the rest with dynamic data (internet data, documents, crappy apps), it would take 80 years for your drive to diminish. Now I don't know about you, but I don't have any HDDs that've been around for anywhere near 80 years.
