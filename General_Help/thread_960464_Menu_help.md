### Post by plucky on 2008-10-27
> **giutax said:**
> Hello. I just upgraded to 8.10 and I am having a weird issue. When I go to Gnome menu then too places then to home and click home. My music player opens. It does that doe the Desktop and Home Folder. I have no Idea where to go with this one. Everything else with the upgrade went fine.

From a terminal window ```
nautilus
``` and right click on a folder and select **Open with other Application** and select **File Browser** will fix the problem.

This is a known bug with 8.10,mine opened rhythmbox.

See this [Bug report](https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/nautilus/+bug/260492)


Good Luck
