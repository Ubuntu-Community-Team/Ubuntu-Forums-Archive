### Post by bdtgp on 2007-07-12
You can also go to:
Filesystem>usr>share>applications>WINE or whatever its called in there, you should see it.  Pretty simple. 

If you want it on your desktop you can create a launcher for it by right clicking on it in the applications folder and finding the command to run it.  Then right click on your desktop and click create new launcher and just fill in the info and youre good to go.
