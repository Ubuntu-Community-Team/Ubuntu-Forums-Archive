### Post by bra|10n on 2012-10-24
You could search installed language-packs etc for Chinese via the software centre or whatever is the easiest for you as a start.  
After locating the name of the installed package you can then look at either your history to see when it was installed, or the package description may even tell you if it's a dependency of another package.
Once located it should be simply a matter of removing it.
