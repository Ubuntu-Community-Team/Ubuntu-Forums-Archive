### Post by dragonfly41 on 2012-05-30
In last month or so I've experienced very frequent crashes of Firefox 11.0 .. in Ubuntu 10.10

I now have to go into Firefox top toolbar > Help > Restart with Add-ons Disabled

and start Firefox in safe mode from the popup dialogue after restart.

[https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/troubleshoot-firefox-issues-using-safe-mode?redirectlocale=en-US&redirectslug=Safe+Mode](https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/troubleshoot-firefox-issues-using-safe-mode?redirectlocale=en-US&redirectslug=Safe+Mode)

I've also moved the plugins (other than flash) out of /usr/lib/mozilla/plugins into a temporary folder.

And I shut down tabs to a minimum.

You could try bleachbit to clear Firefox and Opera  cache (but keep your passwords).
