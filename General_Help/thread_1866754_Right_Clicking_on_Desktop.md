### Post by CryptoPaul on 2011-10-22
Where abouts are you right clicking?

With a default install you will have a folder view widget, it is only within this that you can right click and "Create New", if you right click outside of the folder widget you don't get those options.

If you want a "Conventional" desktop, (windows, kde3 or gnome like), where you can create icons, folders, or links anywhere on the desktop, then:

Right click on the desktop.
(Unlock Widgets if Locked).
Desktop Settings - View
Change "Layout" from "Desktop" to "Folder View". (Yes, not very intuitive!)
Apply.
