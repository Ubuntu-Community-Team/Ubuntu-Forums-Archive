### Post by dj-toonz on 2009-10-30
Yup 

It already exists, and its already installed on your system. Right click on an empty part of a Gnome panel, choose "Add to panel", find System Monitor and click "Add". Once System Monitor is running left-click on its display in the panel. Hey presto! Choose the "Processes" tab and click on a trivial process (try "init" near the bottom of the list) and click "End Process". Easy.
