### Post by kshane on 2008-01-01
I take it your question is:  Can I remove the Windows partition and give that space to Ubuntu?

If that is the question, the answer is Yes!  Easiest way would be to use G-Parted.  Just remove the Windows partition and stretch the Ubuntu partition to take the space left over.

Hope that helped!
