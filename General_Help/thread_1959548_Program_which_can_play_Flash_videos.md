Gnash works great for me.  I uninstalled Adobe and then went to youtube and clicked on a video.  A message comes up saying that you don't have the proper add-on to play the video.  It then offers to find the add-on for you.  Allow it to do so and you should be presented with 2 options: GNASH and ADOBE.  Pick GNASH and Firefox will install it for you automatically.  Close Forefox and reopen it and all should be well.
