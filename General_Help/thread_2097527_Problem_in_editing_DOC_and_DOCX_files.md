### Post by ajgreeny on 2012-12-23
> With Libre office, it comes as mal-formatted.

I don't know exactly what you mean by **mal-formatted** so am not sure what to say other than there is a known difficulty with the opening of .doc and .docx files which have complicated page formatting, perhaps with tables, columns and graphics etc etc, where things can be thrown completely out of line in comparison with opening them in MS Office.

I believe LibreOffice is better at opening these files than OpenOffice, so I don't think using OOo will help at all.  You may need to just accept that MS Office is needed and either use Windows in dual boot or virtual install, or perhaps try MS Office in wine.
