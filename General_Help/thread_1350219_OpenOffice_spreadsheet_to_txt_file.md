### Post by 73ckn797 on 2009-12-09
You may just have to copy each of the columns of words you speak about from the ODS file and when pasted into the ODT file create columns there. A straight copy and paste will appear as an inserted graphic but it can be edited. A double click on the area of text will make it appear as a spreadsheet with format already there. You will likely have to do a little setup once copied.

A special paste will give more the appearance of a "csv" file.
