### Post by papibe on 2012-04-05
Hi Beastcloud. Welcome to the forums!

Yes you can. There are 2 options depending on how did you install Ubuntu:
[LIST]
[*]Proper installation:
[LIST]
[*]Open nautilus (file manager), then 'Go -> Computer'.
[*]You'll see several disks one of them will be called 'OS' or 'Windows'.
[*]Double click on it.
[/LIST][*]Wubi:
[LIST]
[*]Open nautilus (file manager), then 'Go -> Computer'.
[*]Open 'File system'. And then 'Host'
[/LIST]
[/LIST]
There you are on your C:\ Be careful to navigate away from your system files and go to your personal files.

I hope that helps. And tell us how it goes.
Regards.
