### Post by deadflowr on 2015-10-24
I doubt it has anything to do with which kernel is running.

Sessions usually have local logs in the users own home folder.
I'd look at a couple of logs in 
/home/YOU/.cache/upstart.
primarily the files for Unity7.log, and gnome-session-Unity.log
But there are others in there, too.
So look around, if you want.

You might also look at the Xorg.0.log in the /var/log directory.
If X has a problem, it might be reflected in there.

Also, if you disabled apparmor, you might think about re-enabling it, 
as behavior like that should generate an error/crash report, 
which can be found in the /var/crash directory.
