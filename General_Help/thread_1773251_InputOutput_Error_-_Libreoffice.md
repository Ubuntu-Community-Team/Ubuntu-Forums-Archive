### Post by johngreth on 2011-06-02
The Windows formats are so ill defined that it is very difficult for libreoffice to follow all of the stupid things in them. It helps if you save in the 2003 formats or the odf (libreoffice) format. If you are really in a bind you could also upload the documents to Google docs which will convert them to the Google format and then allow you to download them in any format.

I'm sorry you are having this problem. Unfortunately Microsoft can be too unpredictable sometimes.
