On firefox toolbar, click on view.  At the bottom of that menu is "Full Screen."  Clicking will toggle it to normal mode and your m/max/close buttons should come back.  F11 also toggles this.  Note that when the Full Screen mode is toggled, Firefox is always on top and you won't have access to your panels either.
