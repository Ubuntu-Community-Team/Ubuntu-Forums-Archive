### Post by Mark Phelps on 2011-01-18
I'm going to break from the pack here ... and provide a couple of different suggestions.

Take a look at PC Linux OS.  It's a lot like the MS Windows look and feel.  It's not Ubuntu-based, but for her, that shouldn't matter.

Also, take a look at Mint.  It's also a lot like MS Windows.

But ... understand .. Linux is NOT Windows.  So, the more she's embedded into the details of doing stuff in MS Windows (for example, using Outlook for mail), the harder she is going to find switching to ANY Linux distro.
