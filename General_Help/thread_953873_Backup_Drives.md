### Post by gpsmikey on 2008-10-21
Couple of additional items to consider -- portable hard drives, while very handy, are fairly fragile if you are carrying them around all the time and drop one.  Another issue you need to address if you haven't is some sort of protection for the data -- assuming you have, as you say, lists of the people that owe money, you need to protect that personal data in case the media it is on gets lost or stolen.  I would consider two backups -- one "on site" and the other "off site" that goes home with somebody.  You may also want to consider imaging the existing drives and keeping those images somewhere so if you have a hard drive failure (like I just did this morning with a SATA Seagate 160 gig drive), you can restore to a new drive and be back up running quickly.
