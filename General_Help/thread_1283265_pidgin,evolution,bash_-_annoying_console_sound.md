### Post by Magnifique on 2009-10-05
When I press an arrow key (or backspace...) when there is no text, it makes an annoying beepy sound. How do I turn it off ?

---

### Post by Giblet5 on 2009-10-05
Turn off the system bell in System/Preferences/Sound. Click the "Sounds" tab. Disable "Play alert sound".
