### Post by stumbleUpon on 2009-09-16
You have logged into an xubuntu session (which is based on an xfce desktop). The default is not the usual 'Apllications System Places' setup in xubuntu as it is in the case of GNOME.

If you want to use GNOME, log out and at the login prompt choose GNOME in the sessions option and then log in. You will then get back your usual menu.
