### Post by spin2cool on 2007-04-21
Being bleeding edge comes at a cost.  For proof of that, search this forum for people using compiz and, more recently, beryl.  You'll find that these people have hundreds upon hundreds of problems, some of which are resolved, and others that are filed as bugs and fixed later.  The point is, some people are willing to sacrifice stability for cutting-edge features, but any packages that aren't tested and true shouldn't be part of the core distro.
