### Post by ellgor on 2008-05-12
Hi,

Try this, make sure you have the alsamixer-gui installed, open a terminal and type alsamixer, and press enter, you will be presented with a small window with the various devices listed as columns, use the arrow keys to move along until you reach microphone, use the up key to increase the volume level and press m to enable it (the square at the bottom of the column will change to green when enabled) hope this helps.

Regards, Ellgor.
