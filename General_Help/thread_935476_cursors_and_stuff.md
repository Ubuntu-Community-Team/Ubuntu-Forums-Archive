### Post by Therion on 2008-10-01
Save the file to your desktop.
*Navigate to System/Preferences/Appearance 
*Click on the "Customize" button, then the "Cursors" tab.
Drag and Drop the file you downloaded onto that window.
You should get a message that it was installed.

If still no joy, post back with a link to what it is you're trying to install.  Sometimes themes and things related are packaged in such a way as to require additional steps.
