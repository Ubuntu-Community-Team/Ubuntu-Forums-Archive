### Post by automaton26 on 2009-11-14
KK seems better to me, but there are plenty of people on these forums who appear to be having new issues with sound/graphics/networking.

If you're already having issues with JJ, I would create a LiveCD and try KK out first, but as usual make sure you:

[LIST=1]
[*]MD5SUM the .iso file before writing to CD
[*]Verify the CD after writing
[*]Use the LiveCD to check your memory has no faults
[*]Use the LiveCD to check your hard disk has no faults
[/LIST]
Finally, if you're happy it's OK, I'd say that people seem to have fewer problems when they do a Clean-Install, rather than an Upgrade (but don't quote me on that!).
