### Post by SeijiSensei on 2012-08-14
I have an XFX board with an NVIDIA 9500GT which works quite well.  I don't think that model exists any more, but here are some [other XFX options]("http://www.newegg.com/Product/ProductList.aspx?Submit=ENE&N=50001669&IsNodeId=1&Description=nvidia%20pci%20express&name=XFX&Order=PRICE&Pagesize=20"). My concern was having a card that could decode H.264 video in hardware; any of these will do that with the proprietary NVIDIA driver.  I'd probably choose the 1GB model for $40 since it has double the memory of the cheapest model for only an additional five dollars.
