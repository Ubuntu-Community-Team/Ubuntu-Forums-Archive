### Post by kurt18947 on 2013-02-11
The obvious question is, why not just get a wireless adapter for your Dell?  I'm assuming you have wireless access where the Dell is located?  It appears you have 4 USB ports probably USB1.1 which should work okay, just be a little bit limited in speed.  I'm guessing you also have PCI slots so an internal wifi adapter may work.  This seems a whole lot easier than setting up some sort of sharing arrangement.  

Another option might be to convert an old wireless router into a wireless bridge and use an ethernet cable but that's needless complication I think.
