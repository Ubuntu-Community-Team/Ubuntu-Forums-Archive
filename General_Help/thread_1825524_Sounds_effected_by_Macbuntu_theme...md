### Post by zero2xiii on 2011-08-15
Hay,

Easy:

Open up your sound preferences: System > Preferences > Sound.
Then change the button right to sound theme to "No sounds" and hit close.

If you just wish to disable the button and window sounds, leave the theme sound on, and untick the box to the bottom labeled "Enable window and button sounds"

Hope this works for you :)
