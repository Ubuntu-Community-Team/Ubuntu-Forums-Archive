### Post by todak on 2010-01-07
Start **System Settings**. Click on **Regional and Language**. Click on the **Time & Dates** tab. In the **Time Format** box, select the **HH:MM:SS** setting. Click **Apply**. Close System Settings and logout/in and you will have your 24 hour time format.
