### Post by ezsit on 2012-09-29
I can not stand Unity so I switched to Xubuntu and find I had a similar experience with constant application crashes, usually tumblerd and colord. So I removed all traces of tumbler and xubuntu-desktop (useless meta package where removal does not affect anything else). I now enjoy NO application crashes. I can easily live without thumbnails, and save HD space by not having them cached in my /home directory somewhere.
