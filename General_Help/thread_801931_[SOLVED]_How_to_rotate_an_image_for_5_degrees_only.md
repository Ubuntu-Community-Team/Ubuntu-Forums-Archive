### Post by stream303 on 2008-05-21
In Gimp 2.4.5, I can rotate with:

Tools > Transform Tools > Rotate

Tip for quality rotation:  if you nudge your rotation in many steps to get it to where you want, make note of the degree of rotation you end up with.  Cancel the rotation, and then directly enter the degree of rotation so that you do it in one step.

This may not be an issue anymore, but I remember it from ye olden days. :)
