### Post by Frak on 2008-07-04
The updates add new features and functionality in some cases, but in most cases provide security patches that keep people from intruding in your system and spoofing a security hole to either:

1. Crash your system
2. Steal your information
3. Hijack your system
4. Ruin your system altogether

As well as making sure the software runs in check and doesn't get carried away.
