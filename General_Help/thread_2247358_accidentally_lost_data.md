### Post by BBQdave on 2014-10-07
This is a general thought, but you can use a live Linux (Ubuntu if you like) disc to boot up your system and retrieve data from your hard disc. If you are able to see your data on your computer's hard drive (after boot up from live Linux disc) - you can copy the data to another hard drive, such as a portable usb drive.

Not to be a bummer, but if your hard drive is over-written (when you installed 14.04) then it will be difficult to retrieve data. A little late, but when you upgrade or install a fresh OS - copy your data to another drive :)
