### Post by pagaboy on 2009-02-11
OK, I've found a workaround.  Just disable any add-on, so you're asked to restart.  Then restart, and all should be fine.  Don't forget to enable your add-on though.

Looks like something's getting stuck, but by forcing a need for a restart, the mechanism resets itself.
