[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Raid](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Raid)

There are also several backup tools available for linux:

[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/ContinuousBackups](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/ContinuousBackups)

Good luck!  I'm sure you can find a simple solution to your need for RAID mirroring and backups of your drives.
