### Post by Vladlenin5000 on 2014-07-10
I don't use that DE but I suppose you have a shortcut for sound settings in your sound icon. If not then at the system settings > sound (or audio).

Ideally it should turn off the speakers (internal audio chipset) or HDMI output whenever it detects plugged headphones and conversely activate the last used output whenever unplugged. The same for Bluetooth headsets or speakers. However, it seldom works and is irrespective of the brand/model of the notebook.
