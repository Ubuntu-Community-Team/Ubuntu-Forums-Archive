### Post by p_quarles on 2007-12-27
Yes, many DVDs are encrypted with CSS. I guess it's possible that the audio itself is not encrypted this way, hence the problem. Do you have the libdvdcss2 libraries installed? 

If not, you can get them following these instructions:
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Medibuntu](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Medibuntu)
