### Post by Cappy on 2007-05-27
You need to turn off the audio control that records your sound. In my "volume control" and on the recording tab, the control that "records my output sound" is "PCM". Basically, make sure the microphone icon has Xs for anything that doesn't have "Mic", "Microphone", or "Capture" in the name. You may need to enable all controls in Edit-->Preferences so you can turn it off.
