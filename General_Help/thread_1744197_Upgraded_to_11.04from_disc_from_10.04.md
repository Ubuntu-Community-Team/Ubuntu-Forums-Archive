### Post by rfry11 on 2011-04-30
The only thing I can really say is that reinstalling Ubuntu from scratch each time you want to upgrade is normally the best thing to do. While it's a pain, problems that arise from upgrades are EXTREMELY hard to fix, and they almost always occur.

On the Firefox bookmarks front, you should try using this official Firefox add-on to easily backup your bookmarks to the cloud, preventing you from needing to make local backups:
[http://goo.gl/1bWkr](http://goo.gl/1bWkr)

### Post by Megaptera on 2011-04-30
You say you tried to go 10.04 direct to 11.04. Even if upgrades were always fault-free (they're not as you now know) you can't skip an edition in the way that you appear to have tried to skip 10.10.

I believe you can skip editions only if upgrading from one LTS edition to the next e.g. 8.04 to 10.04.
