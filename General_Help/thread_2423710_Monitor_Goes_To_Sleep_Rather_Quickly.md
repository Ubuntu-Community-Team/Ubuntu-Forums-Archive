### Post by SpikeLS6 on 2019-07-27
I am using an LG wide screen monitor. All works well, but the monitor goes to sleep and to wake it up I have to re-input the Password to get into Ubuntu 19.04, Is there a setting in Terminal that can keep the monitor screen going for an hour or two? I have to step away rather often, so I have to re-enter the Password lots of times per day.

---

### Post by sevendogs1337 on 2019-07-27
Look in "Settings" --> "Power" --> Blank Screen" and set the time to an increased value. You can also go to "Settings" --> "Privacy" and turn the screen lock off or change the time after the screen blanks before locking to an hour or so.
