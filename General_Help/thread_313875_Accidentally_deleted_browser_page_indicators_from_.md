### Post by ciscosurfer on 2006-12-06
Do you mean a list of open Windows?  You can re-add this to your panel by right-clicking a panel, choose Add to Panel, click on the Window List button, click Add, click Close.  You can move this wherever you'd like by right-clicking it and choosing "move".
