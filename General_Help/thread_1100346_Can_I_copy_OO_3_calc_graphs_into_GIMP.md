### Post by geirha on 2009-03-19
I just tried in ubuntu Hardy with OO 2.4, and copying a diagram and pasting in gimp only copies the background of the diagram, which is pointless. However, marking all the cells underneath the diagram (so the diagram gets covered by the selection), then copying and pasting to gimp, the diagram got copied ... at least a viable workaround.
