### Post by lisati on 2010-12-26
Like Wojox points out, you need Windows in order to run Windows. A recovery partition, the Windows disk that came with your machine, or a set of recovery disks you have made for your machine (preferably using tools that came preinstalled on it), are your safest bets. Your next best option is to contact the computer manufacturer.

Legal issues aside, you need to be extremely cautious about Windows disks of the "don't ask too many questions where they come from" variety because of the risk of malware.
