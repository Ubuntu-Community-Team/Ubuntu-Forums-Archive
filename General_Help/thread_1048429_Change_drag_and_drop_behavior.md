### Post by mcduck on 2009-01-24
While dragging inside the same disk partition the files are moved by default. However if you drag something to any other partition/device it will be copied by default.

By the way, you can also drag&drop with the middle mouse button to get a small popup asking if you want to move, copy or link the file(s).
