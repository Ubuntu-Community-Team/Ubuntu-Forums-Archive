### Post by ajgreeny on 2014-01-31
You can do it from the file manager, nautilus by right clicking on the drive and choosing **eject** or **unmount**.

There is also a small utility in the repos for 12.04, not sure about later versions, called ejecter, which puts a small icon in the panel when a USB is attached.  Click on the icon and you are offered a list of attached devices, and can click on the one you want to remove to unmount or eject it safely.

### Post by Dennis N on 2014-01-31
