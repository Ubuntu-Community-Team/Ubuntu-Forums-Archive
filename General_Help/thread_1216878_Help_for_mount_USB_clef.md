### Post by fballem on 2009-07-19
> **gaetoz said:**
> hello I would like to know how to mount a usb key on a system UBUNTU 8.4 thank you for your reply

May I ask if you have tried plugging the USB key into a usb slot on your computer?

If you have, what happened?

If it works, there will be an icon on your desktop. Assuming this is the case, then you can open the contents by clicking on the icon. When you are ready to remove the usb key, please remember to right-click, then select Unmount Volume from the menu. When icon disappears from your desktop, then it is safe to remove.

Hope this helps,
