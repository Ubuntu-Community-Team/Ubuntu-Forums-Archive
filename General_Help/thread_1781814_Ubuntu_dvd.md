### Post by 3rdalbum on 2011-06-14
No, it's not true.

There is an Ubuntu DVD image, but contrary to popular belief it does NOT contain any extra codecs or programs. It contains extra language packs ONLY.

Unless you need Ubuntu in a more obscure language that is not supported out-of-the-box on the CD, you will notice NO DIFFERENCE from downloading the DVD.
