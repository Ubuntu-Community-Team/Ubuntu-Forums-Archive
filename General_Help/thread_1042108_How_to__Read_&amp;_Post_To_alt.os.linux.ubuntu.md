### Post by tinker123 on 2009-01-17
When you can't get your Ubuntu specific question answered in this forum an alternative is to post your question to the Usenet group
**alt.os.linux.ubuntu**

Unfortunately, Google Groups, which provides an excellent and free web interface to Usenet does not carry alt.os.linux.ubuntu.  Many people have sent suggestions for Google to do so.  They will not and have never mentioned why.

However, you can read and post to alt.os.linux.ubuntu for free via this free Usenet server
[http://motzarella.org/](http://motzarella.org/)

HTH
