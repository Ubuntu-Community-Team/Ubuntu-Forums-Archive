Heres the odd thing . . . . the connection always gets lost when they're speaking on the phone to someone . . . . . and when that happens, you can hear the faint "peep peep" when the linksys is trying to re-establish the service.

I am fairly sure that should not happen. You shouldn't lose the ADSL, and you shouldn't be able to hear the linksys. I wonder if you have all the microfilters you need. Every phone should be on a microfilter, and so should the linksys (but in the ADSL outlet). Without all the microfilters in place, the phone and the ADSL can interfere with each other - the microfilter stops the ADSL signal from reaching the phone adn vice versa - it acts as a signal splitter.
