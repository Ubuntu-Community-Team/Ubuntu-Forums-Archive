### Post by kev187 on 2010-09-02
Ok, I got Ubuntu Installed on my Asus Netbook.

I have it set not to require password on log in.  But everytime I walk away from the computer for a bit, I get back & wake it up, it wants a password.  How do I tun this off, I jut want to sit down & wake up the computer & get back to work, not type a password every time?  

I tried to dumb down my password to make it less work to get logged in & Ubuntu told me it was too simple a password, to try again?  

Why is Ubuntu being so mean to me, making me have to log in 20x a day with some complex password.  I just want it to sit there ready to go :-D 

Thanks everyone.

---

### Post by wojox on 2010-09-02
Go into your Screensaver Preferences and uncheck "Lock screen when screensaver is active".
