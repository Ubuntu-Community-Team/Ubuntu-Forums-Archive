### Post by omeomi on 2013-02-04
You can write reviews via the Software Center in Ubuntu and you have to have the app you're reviewing installed on your system.

### Post by omeomi on 2013-02-04
If you are in the Ubuntu Software Center (not Synaptic) and go to the installed app 'page'. There you can scroll down to the reviews. On top of the reviews, just below the header 'Reviews' there should be a link 'Write your own review'.
