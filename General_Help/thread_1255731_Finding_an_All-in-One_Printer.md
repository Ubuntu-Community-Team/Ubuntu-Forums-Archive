### Post by bedhead75 on 2009-09-01
HP printers have very good Linux support with their own drivers, and they are easy to install and can be downloaded from their website.  The ones in the Ubuntu repositories are a little outdated. I helped my parents buy an all-in-one HP printer for their computer running Jaunty 64-bit just last week for $59.99.  Installation of the printer drivers was as simple as running a script, and they have detailed and easy to follow instructions on their site.  The scanner functions were instantly recognized in XSane and worked right out of the box.  They have a number of good inkjet printers for under $150.  Check to make sure it has Linux support first, but most of the latest models should:

[http://hplipopensource.com/hplip-web/index.html](http://hplipopensource.com/hplip-web/index.html)
