### Post by Nevis on 2009-11-29
I did the same thing! However, I have found how to get them back. 

1 Right click on the panel you want to add the applets to and choose "Add to panel...". 

2 When the window pops up select "Indicator Applet". 

This applet is used to consistently display notification from a variety of applications (notably Pidgin and Rhythmbox).
