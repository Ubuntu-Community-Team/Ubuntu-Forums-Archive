### Post by AaronStarr on 2010-08-08
It doesn't disappear completely. Basically it fades down to about 5% Opacity.

I figured it out. I went to: System > Preferences > CompizConfig Settings Manager > and DISABLED 'Opacify" under the Accessibility category. :p

### Post by DrTebi on 2012-11-30
Another possibility (in case someone needs this) is that the "Fading Windows" effect in Compiz is enabled.

To disable it open...
System -> Preferences -> CompizConfig Settings Manager

... and disable "Fading Windows" in the "Effects" section.
