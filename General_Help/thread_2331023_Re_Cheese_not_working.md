### Post by QDR06VV9 on 2016-07-17
When you open Cheese in the left top corner you see the word "Cheese"> Click that and down to Preferences>Now you will see a tab named Webcam>below you should see "Device"
That should give you the option to choose your Webcam.
You will see other Options there also Like Photo Resolution && Video Resolution on that tab.
