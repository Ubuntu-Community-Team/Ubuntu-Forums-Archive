### Post by fragos on 2011-06-08
The indicators that have replaced applets all have indicator in their names. You can search with Synaptic or the Software Center for those available in the repositories you have in Software Sources. Those available like for hardware sensors differ from their applet counterparts. They may be similar but many are a little less functional and may not use icons.
