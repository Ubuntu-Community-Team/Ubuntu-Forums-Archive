### Post by blur xc on 2009-10-02
I don't know what hardware you are running, but online flash games play wonderfully on my system- just a modest intel core 2 duo & nvidia video card.

The trick was to turn of hardware acceleration on the flash settings.  You can get to them by right clicking on any flash content, and select settings.  Then uncheck the box next to enable hardware acceleration.  Right now my wife is sucked into the 1min timed bejeweled game, farkel.  I hate farkel.  It's a horrible game...  but so addictive.
