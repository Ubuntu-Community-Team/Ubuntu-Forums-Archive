### Post by ngant on 2014-09-30
Looks like ocular will work, with some minor limitations.  It brings up the following message:  "This document has embedded files. _Click here to see them_ or go to File -> Embedded Files".

IOW ocular simply loads up the individual chapters for reading, in essentially the same way as I did in my short cut with Windoze/Adobe X and Xbuntu/Adobe Reader 9 in prev. message.  The great thing is that I don't have to switch back and forth between OS's and I can stay in Xubuntu to read entire PDF, chapter by chapter.
