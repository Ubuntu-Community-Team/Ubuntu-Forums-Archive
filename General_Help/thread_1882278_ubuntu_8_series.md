### Post by tpprynn on 2011-11-17
This is the nearest you could get with currently maintained distros I think:

Run an Ubuntu 8.04 live CD. Go to /usr/share/themes and copy Human onto a pendrive.

Install Ubuntu 10.04, 11.04 (not 11.10), Linux Mint 11 or Debian 6 and then copy the theme back in (paste it onto the desktop and then 

sudo mv ./Desktop Human /usr/share/themes 

in Terminal). 

I preferred Ubuntu 9.04 myself, slightly more polished theme, downhill after then...

The wallpapers and theme might also be in
 
[http://packages.ubuntu.com/hardy/allpackages](http://packages.ubuntu.com/hardy/allpackages)

as .debs but I haven't checked.
