### Post by notwen on 2007-05-28
Marieta has a Micro Center doesn't it?  Are you wanting a graphically able system?  Will you be running any graphic intense games(under Wine or dual-boot)?  Other than that specification, I'd hit up Micro Center for one of their monthly mobo/proc combo deals.  I would pick out the most expensive items first, (1-3) mobo, processor, gfx card) gfx card varies really depending on what you want/need.  Then your ram, power supply, hdd, optical drives and depending on your mobo, you may or may not need a sound card/gfx card.  I'd check out pricewatch/newegg and whatever other sites you prefer for part shopping.  I was able to build a nice 2.1ghz athlon xp 2800+ system w/ 1gb of ram for under $400 late last year shopping around for clearance/refurb items.  Happy building. =]
