### Post by colobix on 2010-12-19
Most mp3/mp4 players are able to take pls and m3u playlists.
You can make playlists with almost every music or video players here.
rhythmbox (best in my opinion), Songbird (One of my favorites) and Banshee.
And offcourse many many more, but these 3 apps are awesome and will do just fine.
But if your mp3 player will read the mp3 files after you have dragged/dropped them into your player's folder, I am sure it will read a playlist as well. So try this;
Open up rhythmbox, clean the playlist, drag and drop the music folder for your mp3 player to the rhythmbox playlist area, andstart organizing. Remember to save the playlist in your mp3 player folder where it should belong or otherwise the locations will not match.
Good luck.
