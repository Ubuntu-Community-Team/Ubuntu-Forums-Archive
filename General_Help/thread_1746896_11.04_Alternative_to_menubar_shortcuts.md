### Post by ingothomas on 2011-05-02
I use Kupfer:
[Ctrl]+[Space] => [one or two letters in the common application I call] => [Enter]

Kinda freakish, but suddenly I could do completely without any menu.
For the desktop, try [super]+[w] or [super]+[s]. Not showing the desktop, but better than anything I've ever seen before.

Sorry for providing an alternative over a solution. But this is a 'must know'.
