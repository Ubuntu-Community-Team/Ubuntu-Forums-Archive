### Post by Rogue1971 on 2009-07-11
Is there a way to use an image as a launcher shortcut icon? I have some movies I want a shortcut to so my 5 year old can start them herself and would like a picture of the CD cover or a still from the movie so she can identify it easily, is this possible?

---

### Post by jerrrys on 2009-07-11
right click on the launcher you have created and then click on properties. in properties, click on icon picture and then browse your files for the right picture you want to use.
