### Post by mikey.duhhh on 2009-05-26
I have some of the same problems with 9.04 on my laptop, but they were partly mitigated by installing the compiz-fusion icon and making it part of the booting process.  First, I was able to load Compiz by clicking on the icon, which set Compiz as the default desktop manager. It also meant that the system hanging increased.  Second, by then switching to Metacity after booting up, I rarely have system hangs any more. That is, my system hangs are down to 1 or 2 per week instead of two or three per day.  And note, this is on a laptop with the Intel 945G graphics drivers.
