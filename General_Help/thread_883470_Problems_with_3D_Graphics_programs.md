### Post by Dylock on 2008-08-08
1) What kind of graphics card are you using? You can find this out with by running ```
 sudo lshw -C video 
```

2) Have you installed any drivers for said video card

3) Could you paste the contents of /etc/X11/xorg.conf This will determine what video driver you currently are using.
