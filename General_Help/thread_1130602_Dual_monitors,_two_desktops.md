### Post by James_Lochhead on 2009-04-19
Nope. The only thing that gets close is that you can have two separate X screens. You can do things like cut and paste between them but you cannot move applications and such. Also some programs do not like it and will malfunction.

### Post by James_Lochhead on 2009-04-20
Basically two entirely different logins, if you get my meaning. They can communicate to an extent, for example, if you copy on one you can past on the other and if you update a file on one the file on the other will change. However, you cannot move applications between X screens.

In summary you get two fully functional monitors with limited ability to communicate between them.
