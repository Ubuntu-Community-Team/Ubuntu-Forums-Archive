### Post by JemW on 2008-09-30
I am using aMSN, from the repositories, with a Logitech webcam and Logitech USB headset. Cam and full audio with no problems.

I had to install Easycam2 to provide the cam drivers. The Easycam install also installs 'Cheese' with which you can test the cam. A reboot after installation is essential.

Ubuntu Hardy 8.04 / All Updates

Hope that helps.
