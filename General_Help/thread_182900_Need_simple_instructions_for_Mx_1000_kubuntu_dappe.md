### Post by Christmas on 2006-05-27
Try reading this thread:[http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=97936]("http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=97936"). I have this MX310 mouse and it works very well, I don't need all the "back", "forward" and "windows selector" functions, I didn't even bother to try making them work. The basic two buttons and the scroll wheel are enough for me. However if you manage to make all of your buttons work, keep in mind that there is an option "ButtonMapping" which lets you put the functions of all the buttons in order, and keep in mind that the scroll wheel is actually represented by two buttons (up and down), so you will have button 1 (left-click), button 2 (i think this is the scroll wheel's press button), button 3 (right-click), and buttons 4 and 5 which are the wheel's up and down "buttons".
