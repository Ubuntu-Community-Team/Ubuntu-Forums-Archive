### Post by Peripheral Visionary on 2012-03-28
For some reason, some CD burning software works on certain computers but not others. Brasero works only randomly on my computer. Half the time it just made coasters! K3B worked perfectly every time, and for me Xfburn works even better.

Yet others experience all three burners differently, so a lot apparently depends on your hardware. If you really must have a CD instead of a thumb drive (I understand - my computer will not boot from a thumb drive), you can always try installing and using different CD burning software.
