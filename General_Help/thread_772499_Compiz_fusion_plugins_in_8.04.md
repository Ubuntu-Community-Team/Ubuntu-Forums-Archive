### Post by dugonline on 2008-04-28
Pardon any errors I may suggest as I am still a Newb and am at work on a Win PC.  I don't have Ubuntu anywhere near me so I will do my best to remember the directories and such.

If you go to the Add/Remove programs, then search for compiz, you can install the compiz effects there.  After they are installed, you will have to find the "Advanced Desktop Effects" under System/Preferences/Desktop Effects.  From there you can select what you would like to run for effects and then go into the advanced setting to make it work the way you want right down to what hot keys are the default and if you would like to change them.

I hope that helps out.
