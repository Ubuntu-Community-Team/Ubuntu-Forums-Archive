### Post by speartip on 2014-04-21
A bit short of info. We could do to know what version & flavour of 'buntu you are using.
Assuming you are using Ubuntu 14.04, then just open the dash, (top left hand corner) & start typing Thunderbird, the icon should appear, then just click it.
The icon will then appear in the left hand panel. From there you can right click it & "lock it to the panel", for ease of access next time.
