### Post by whitesmith on 2013-10-14
I am a really big-time believer in FOSS, but sometime you have to be flexible. The Merriam-Webster 11th Collegiate Dictionary has all the bells and whistles I need and runs perfectly under Wine. You can still find this little gem on Amazon for a handful of dollars. Worth every cent to me because I work with words. You might be different and elect to hold out for a FOSS equivalent, but it could be a long wait. By the by, don't go with Merriam-Webster's "Unabridged" 3rd International. It lacks spoken pronunciation, and that's only one of its infirmities./
