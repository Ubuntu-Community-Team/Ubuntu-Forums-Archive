### Post by h0megr0wn on 2009-07-05
I would just like to share a neat trick for photo compression. Instead of using any script or code or even an image compression tool. If you can screenshot the image with a screenshot tool while viewing said image in a photo viewer, just take a screenshot of the image, then save, it's a digital replica and i have found i can get anywhere from 90% to 95% compression ratios. I know it kind of ghetto but the %'s are undeniable...

### Post by t4thfavor on 2009-07-06
Open said file in gimp, and save as jpg then slide the compression slider towards size as opposed to quality (or whatever it is). This will give you a much more controllable quality to your pictures.
