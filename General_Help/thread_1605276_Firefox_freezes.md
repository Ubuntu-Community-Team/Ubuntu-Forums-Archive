### Post by arpad9 on 2010-10-26
Firefox does some really annoying things lately though I have to say that I'm pretty happy with 4.x.

For your issue, I'd bet it's just coincidental with the kernel upgrade.

I had huge issues with FF3.  Disabling the anti-phishing "feature" helped a lot.  This is in Preferences->Security "Block known attack sites" and "Block known phishing sites."  Delete your urlclassifier3.sqlite database found in .mozilla/firefox/<your profile dir>/

Presumably, you know how to identify a phishing site and don't need FF to phone home on every page view.

Finally though, my answer was giving up in FF3 and moving to FF4 and I'm pretty happy.
