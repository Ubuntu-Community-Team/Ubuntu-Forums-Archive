During gmail setup I went to "Manage your Google Account Settings", selected Security (on the left), clicked on "2 Step Verification" (the "On" box was checked), again entered my google password, went to the bottom of page and clicked "Revoke All" to have my laptop declared an untrusted device (gmail has a feature to allow trusted devices to login without using the Yubikey even though U2F is On--I want the Yubikey to be required for all logins, hence I Revoked trust for All devices).

If I didn't uncheck the box, my computer became a "Trusted Device" and the Yubikey was not needed for login. Does anybody know how to permanently make my computer an Untrusted Device for gmail???  I have googled everywhere and all I find is instructions to do exactly what I have done (to no avail).
