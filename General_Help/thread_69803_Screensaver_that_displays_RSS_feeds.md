### Post by chusome on 2006-03-29
Actually there is built in support for this

Open up screensaver under system->preferences

-Under the display modes tab choose only one "screen saver" and set it to "flip text"
(also, click on settings and increase the point font size (by default its way to small)

-Under the advanced tab go to text manipulation and set the url of the feed you want to see as your screensaver. ie. [http://www.digg.com/rss/index.xml](http://www.digg.com/rss/index.xml)

Then click preview to see it. (be patient, it takes about 10 seconds to get started)
