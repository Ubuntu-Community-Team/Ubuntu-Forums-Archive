### Post by Ubuntist on 2006-10-12
Lately, I've had trouble getting OpenOffice files (Calc, Writer, ...) to open.  At first, they would only open if I opened them from inside an OpenOffice application.  Then finally, they wouldn't open at all.

For quite some time, I'd been hibernating rather than shutting down completely.  Once I did a complete shutdown, OpenOffice applications began to work again.

I hope somebody finds this useful.
