I installed 10.04 a few things were buggy so I had to revert back to 9.10 what I would like to do is run 10.04 on a second sata drive.I am not going to give up on 10.04 so I would like to dual boot 9.10 and 10.04 what steps do I need to take to do this.

---

### Post by ubername on 2010-05-17
It's easy.

Boot from a 10.04 liveCD, select install.

When you get to the partitioning bit, choose manual and then select the second hard disk as the target, with whatever partitions you like (e.g. separate / and /home partitions)
