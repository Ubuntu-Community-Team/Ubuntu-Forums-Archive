### Post by ugm6hr on 2011-04-25
Have you used SimpleScan?
It scans multiple pages in sequence, and then offers to save as PDF (single file) or jpeg (multiple files).
As mentioned - PDFchain will allow you to then combine as you wish if you need to scan more than about 10 pages per document.
