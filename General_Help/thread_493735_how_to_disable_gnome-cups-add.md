Every time when I plug in my USB printer GNOME displays that gnome-cups-add dialog and every time I have to  click Cancel since it is already configured.

### Post by scxtt on 2007-07-06
"System" --> "Preferences" --> "Removable Drives and Media" --> "Printers & Scanners"
