### Post by CatKiller on 2009-03-23
What do you mean by "something happens to corrupt the drive?" Hard drives do have quite a short lifetime. If your data isn't safe on it, then you ought to get a new one.

You certainly don't need to use a Windows computer to format a drive. Ubuntu has a number of tools to format a drive. GParted is an easy-to-use graphical program that will let you change the partitions on a drive.
