### Post by ringding on 2005-11-22
Not sure what you mean by "what package updates are expected" or "Is Breezy pretty much closed  except for fixes?"

You don't have to wait for DD to come out to install newer versions of software.
Breezy 5.10 is the final release for the moment....DD will be the next release. Ubuntu, as I understand, is on a 6 month schedule....so every 6 months a new version is released.

Try updating your sources.list....add some repositories.....
Check out this thread....may answer some of your questions.....
[http://www.ubuntuforums.org/forumdisplay.php?f=41]("http://www.ubuntuforums.org/forumdisplay.php?f=41")

You can also add some repositories to synaptic.....
open Synaptic > Settings > Repositories > Add
and you can add different repos there.....
