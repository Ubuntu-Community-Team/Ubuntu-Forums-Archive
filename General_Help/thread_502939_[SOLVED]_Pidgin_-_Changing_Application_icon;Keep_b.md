### Post by notwen on 2007-07-17
I'm wanting to change the main Pidgin icon, while keeping my buddy icons for chat windows.  Alas once I change the icon for the Application it becomes the universal icon for all chat windows.  Is there anyway to make this work?  I may also want to add in I use Pidgin as a launcher in AWN(not sure if this matters or not).  Hope that makes sense. ### Post by notwen on 2007-07-18
Found the solution at the [AWN forums]("http://www.planetblur.org/hosted/awnforum/index.php?shard=forum").

Delete the custom icon in "~/.awn/custom icons" and browse to /usr/share/icons/hicolor/48x48/apps

Replace the pidgin.png with your custom icon.

Drag the launcher and don't change the icon.
