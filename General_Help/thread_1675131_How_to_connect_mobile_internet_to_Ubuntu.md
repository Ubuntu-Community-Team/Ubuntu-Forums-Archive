### Post by charlie_b on 2011-01-25
Plug in your internet device and switch on. Then go to the network manager icon in the upper right hand corner of the screen. If your device has been successfully detected you will see an entry there with your device listed. Right click to edit the connection, then enter your account name and password for the mobile device. You should be ready to go, although a restart may be required.
