### Post by gnomeuser on 2008-05-09
And it was written in the book of FreeDesktop.org, thine gtk bookmarks shall be located in ~/.gtk-bookmarks and no place else, on pain of COBOL shall this law be broken.

(okay so I was bored.. the gtk bookmarks which nautilus uses are stored in .gtk-bookmarks under any users folder)
