### Post by Daxrembo on 2007-08-13
I'm having a problem with gedit leaving its temporary files behind when I close the program. Is there a trick to getting gedit to clean up these filename~ files when I exit gedit?

I am running gedit version 2.18.1 on feisty 7.04.

---

### Post by chriscando on 2007-08-13
In gedit look at the settings in Edit > Preferences > Editor 
and uncheck the settings 'Create a backup file before saving'

I can't stand those files either :)
