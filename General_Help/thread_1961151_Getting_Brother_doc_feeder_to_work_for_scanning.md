### Post by kurt18947 on 2012-04-18
You might also take a look at gscan2pdf in the repository.  A very useful app for scanning and saving as pdf (who'd a thought !:biggrin:) and manipulating pages prior to saving.  Only glitch I had was saving pages with columns and columns interrupted by graphics.  The problem was the 'scan  cleaning' app deleting text columns without graphics.  Once I got it to behave,  I was able to get perfect scans using both the ADF and flat bed.  I've never used the Xsane multidoc option.
