### Post by Matt Hood on 2011-07-03
Games are a bit hit or miss in wine. Well, actually everything is hit and miss in wine. Games require massive optimization, and running them through a compatibility layer (aka Wine) adds extra processing. Of course, 1st party drivers (made by the people who built your device) are generally a good idea, but the companies rarely make them for Linux. And even if they do have drivers for Linux they may not be included with the distro for licensing reasons. 

So in summary, try and find some first party drivers if they exist (and you don't already have them), and that Wine isn't all that useful for games.

Btw, what game are you trying to run?
