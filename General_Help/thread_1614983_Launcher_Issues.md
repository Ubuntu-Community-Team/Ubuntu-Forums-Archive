### Post by plucky on 2010-11-08
> **ve3rpm said:**
> I tend to watch the odd movie, and always use vlc.  As such, one time I checked the box so that vlc would open by default.  now however each time I go to open a folder, vlc starts up!  How can I undo this?

ALT+F2 and in the window that opens type **nautilus**

Then right click on a folder and select **Open with Other Application**

In the window that opens,select **File Browser**

Good luck
