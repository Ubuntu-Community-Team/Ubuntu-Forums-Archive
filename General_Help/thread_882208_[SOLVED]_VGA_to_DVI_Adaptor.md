### Post by ktamm on 2008-08-06
I would double check with your video card to make sure that this DVI port is DVI-I. There are actually some that only supply a digital signal and will NOT work with the above adaptor. There is a good chance you'll be fine but I would double check anyways.

[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Digital_Visual_Interface#Connector](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Digital_Visual_Interface#Connector)
