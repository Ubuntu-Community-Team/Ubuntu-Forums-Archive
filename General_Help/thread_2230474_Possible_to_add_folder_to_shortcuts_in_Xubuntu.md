### Post by slickymaster on 2014-06-19
You can add your own shortcuts by simply dragging folders to the shortcut pane. This will allow you to access important folders instantly.

Please refer to [http://docs.xubuntu.org/1404/guide-desktop.html](http://docs.xubuntu.org/1404/guide-desktop.html)
