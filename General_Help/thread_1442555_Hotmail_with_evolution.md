### Post by thebigob on 2010-03-30
Hotmail does not allow forwarding to a third party client. This is so Microsoft can gain advertising revenue. I have looked for a free solution to this and there unfortunately isn't one. 

Some people provide a software forwarding tool that will login to your hotmail and manually forward all your mail to another account but this has a charge attached.

I would strongly recommend moving to another email provider such as gmail which does allow this kind of forwarding.
