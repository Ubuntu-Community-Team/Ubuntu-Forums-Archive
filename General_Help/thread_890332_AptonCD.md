### Post by plucky on 2008-08-15
> **i.mehrzad said:**
> Just wanted to know if this application basically makes a copy of what is store on your /var/cache/apt/archives directory onto a cd.

It creates an APTonCD .iso file on your hard drive of all the .deb packages in the /var/cache/apt/archives directory and then writes the image file to CD.

Great for updating systems that are not connected to the internet.
