### Post by sudodus on 2014-12-29
What do you mean by recoverable?

- To make it a working drive again? - use ***gparted*** which can be installed into Ubuntu (it is already there in *live* Ubuntu systems)

- To recover files, that were overwritten by mistake? - try ***testdisk*** and/or ***photorec*** according to this link [http://www.cgsecurity.org/](http://www.cgsecurity.org/)
