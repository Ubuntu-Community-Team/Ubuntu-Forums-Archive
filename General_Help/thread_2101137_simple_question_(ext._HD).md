### Post by Bashing-om on 2013-01-03
paradive; Hi !

In ubuntu the "My computer" thingy is nautilus, if you installed a latter version then in the unity desktop the "launcher" on left of screen, the "home folder" icon at the top of the launcher under the dash launches "natilus".
The left side pane in this file manager list all devices possibly looking like "194 GB Files...."
Click on it  to mount and access that file system. Remember please remember to (un)mount that device prior to shutting the box down. Right click the device again in the file manager and choose a option to "safely remove the device", my 12.04 has "Unmount"

Now depending on your needs there are a number of ways to mount this external disk either as on demand or auto-mounting permanently when booting.(some info required to advise you how to make your wishes happen).
