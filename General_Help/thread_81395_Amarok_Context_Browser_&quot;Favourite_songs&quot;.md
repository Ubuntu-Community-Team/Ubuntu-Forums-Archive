Hi. 

I want to move away from Gentoo to Ubuntu for a experience and did a successful install last Friday. Everything is nice and like it should be, but one thing kept my attention. I use Amarok for music listening and under Gentoo I had last version of Amarok 1.3.3 with nice Context browser Home tab, where it showed statistics about albums instead of song. ### Post by taavi on 2005-10-31
The solution sat behind my nose and I didn't notice it before yesterday. In the "Configure amaroK" there is one flag-option - "Show statistics by album" and that changed look of context browser from showing favourite songs.

And by the way, in amaroK 1.4 there will be no Home tab, it will be displaced by Statistics.
