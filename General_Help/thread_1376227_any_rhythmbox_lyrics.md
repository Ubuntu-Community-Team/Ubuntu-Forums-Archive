### Post by bark50 on 2010-01-10
Go to "Edit" then "plugins" in Rhythmbox's menu.  There should be a plugin there called "Song lyrics."  Make sure it is checked. To the right of the "Song lyrics" plugin will be a "Configure" box that will allow you to choose from where you want the lyrics fetched.  After you've done all that, go to "View" and then "Song lyrics" and the lyrics, if available, will appear in a separate pane.  You may need to restart Rhythmbox after activating the plugin.
