### Post by suntom on 2010-03-05
Whenever you send a mail to someone, they will get automatically added as an contact. To remove a contact you can click on **[Contacts]("https://mail.google.com/mail/?shva=1#contacts")** in the menu to the left.

To edit the settings for the chat, you have to click on **[Settings and then Chat]("https://mail.google.com/mail/?shva=1#settings/chat")**, and you'll be able to change the settings for it.
