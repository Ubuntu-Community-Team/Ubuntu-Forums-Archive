I am new to linux and primarily use windows7. I have my cpu/ram OC'ed and was wondering if the over clock would affect my linux environment negatively?

---

### Post by mcduck on 2011-05-02
If your overclocking is stable, then it will have no negative effect no matter what OS you run.

On the other hand if you try to push your hardware a bit too far (at least for the voltages & cooling you are using) then Linux tends to crash quicker than Windows does, as it's less forgiving for hardware errors and crashes as soon as things go wrong (instead of continuing running errantly and possibly corrupting your data, like at least older Windows versions do). Of course this is just a good thing at least if it's stable overclocking you are looking for. :)
