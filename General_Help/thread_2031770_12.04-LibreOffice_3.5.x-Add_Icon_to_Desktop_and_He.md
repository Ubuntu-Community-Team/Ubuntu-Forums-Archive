### Post by ajgreeny on 2012-07-22
You can drag launchers for most applications from the menu to the desktop as in earlier versions of Ubuntu.  Unfortunately the launchers for all the LO applications do not work that way, and you have to mess around copying the .desktop files from either /usr/share/applications or direct from /usr/lib/libreoffice/share/xdg/writer.desktop as described in posts 22 - 25 of [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1966370](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1966370)

There is something weird about the LO menu items that means they do not work when dragged to the desktop in Ubuntu, Xubuntu or Lubuntu.
