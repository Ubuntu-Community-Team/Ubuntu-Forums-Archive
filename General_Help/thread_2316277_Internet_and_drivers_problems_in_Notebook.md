### Post by grahammechanical on 2016-03-06
The Lubuntu repositories are the same repositories as the Xubuntu repositories, as the Ubuntu repositories. In fact the Ubuntu family of Linux distributions uses the same repositories but not all of them are enabled by default.

You do not say what version of Lubuntu you are using. It can make a difference as labels change over time. What once was Software Sources in now called Software & Updates. You need to look in the Lubuntu menu structure for a utility that will let you enable the Multiverse & partners repositories and that might give access to more applications.
