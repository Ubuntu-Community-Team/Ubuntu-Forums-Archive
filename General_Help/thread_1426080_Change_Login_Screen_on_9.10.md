### Post by j7%&lt;RmUg on 2010-03-09
Thats because karmic uses a revamped version of gdm, there is currently no feature in karmic to change the login screen(gdm theme), but i do know of a project to help rectify this:

gdm2setup

LINK:
[https://launchpad.net/gdm2setup/](https://launchpad.net/gdm2setup/)

I havent tried it yet but i will at some point, basically it has most of the features that the jaunty gdm had, but it also provides a gui to configure and change around your karmic gdm.

Hope this helps.

EDIT: just tried it and it works great, better than what was in jaunty by far.
