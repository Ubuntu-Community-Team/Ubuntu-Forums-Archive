### Post by heir4c on 2013-12-07
Is it FireFox who taken the cpu or is it an other program? Open a terminal and type: top
Now you can see what taken the most of the cpu and memory. When you start your computer open the terminal and use this command and let it run. When you have a freeze than you click on the icon of the terminal and than you can see directly what program uses the cpu.
