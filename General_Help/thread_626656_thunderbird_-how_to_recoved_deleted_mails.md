### Post by ccprog on 2007-11-29
As long as a mail file/folder is not "compressed", nothing that is "deleted" gets really lost, with the exeption of Trash. Every other action only alters the entries in the associated *.msf file. Therefore, a rather crude but efficient way to get to "deleted" items is to delete, for example, the Inbox.msf file, if you think the mail would be in the Inbox. You loose all status information on all mails, but that should be a minor concern in your situation.
