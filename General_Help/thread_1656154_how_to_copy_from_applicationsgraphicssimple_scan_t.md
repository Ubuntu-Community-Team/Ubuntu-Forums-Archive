### Post by WorMzy on 2010-12-30
Right click on the menu applet (the thing that says "Applications Places System") and choose "Edit menus".

Alternatively, press Alt+F2, then type "alacarte" (without the quote marks), then click Run.

I expect you can figure it out from there.
