### Post by startling on 2009-08-13
I've finally found out what was causing this. I had copied a folder called pictures onto the stick and, just because of that, the stupid, stupid Nautilus assumed the USB stick was a Picture CD! ### Post by michy99 on 2009-08-13
In nautilus (file manager) go to Edit->Preferences->Media. You can tell it how to behave when inserting different types of media.
