### Post by mcduck on 2007-10-31
I don't think there's any way to make Gnome's screenshot program to do anything else than capture the whole screen at the actual resolution your display is.

But to make things easier for you, install Nautilus Image Converter. After that you can just right-click the screenshot and select 'Resize Image'..
