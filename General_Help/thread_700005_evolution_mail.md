### Post by amohanty on 2008-02-18
Hotmail has POP3 servers available at pop3.live.com and SMTP server at smtp.live.com. You can click on the help icon at the top right when you are in the hotmail inbox and look for POP3. You can add an account the normal way with the servers mentioned above. 

Keep in mind that POP3 downloads all the email to local disk. Alternative you can forward all your email to your 'regular' account and then all the mail will show up in your account.
