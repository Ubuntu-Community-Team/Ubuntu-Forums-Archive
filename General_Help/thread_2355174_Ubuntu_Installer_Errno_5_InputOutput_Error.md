Good day.

I have been attempting to install Ubuntu GNU/Linux onto my computer how ever I keep getting the following error message.

The installer encountered an error copying files to the hard disk:

[Errno 5] input/output error

This is often due to a faulty CD/DVD disk or drive, or a faulty hard disk. It may help to clean the CD/DVD, drive lens (cleaning kits are often avalible from electronics suppliers), to check whether the hard disk is old and in need of replacement, or to move the system to a cooler enviroment.

I have no trouble installing and using Microsoft Windows 10. I have checked the hard drives s.m.a.r.t status and tested with hdtune, no indication that they are failing.
