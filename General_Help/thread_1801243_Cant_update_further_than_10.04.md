### Post by grahammechanical on 2011-07-10
That is strange. I would expect you to get a notification that there is a distribution upgrade to 10.10 as soon as the upgrade to 10.04 was completed.

You need to look at the settings for Update Manager. I am using 11.04 not 10.04 and things have changed. So, I cannot direct you step by step. You will have to find this out yourself, somehow. It is also called Software Sources, which may be in the Administration menu or/also part of Update Manager.

On the Update tab there may be a line called Release upgrade - Show new distribution releases. You have the choice of Normal releases or Long term support releases only. If Update Manager is set to LTS releases only then this is why you are not getting to option to upgrade to 10.10 and then 11.04.

Regards.

### Post by deserthowler on 2011-07-10
You will need to do it in order 9.10 --> 10/04 --> 10.10 --> 11.04

You can't skip any in the sequence.

You might need to go to Update Manager and click on the "Settings Box" and then set it to normal upgrade if it is set to long term upgrade.
