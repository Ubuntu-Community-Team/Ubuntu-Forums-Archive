### Post by lawentzel on 2009-04-21
I successfully installed Ubuntu 8.04 on a Gateway computer with only 256 megs of RAM.  Like you, the video card used up some the that memory.  What you need to do to get it to install is get the "Alternative Install".  This is a text based installation program.  Read the screens and you won't have a problem.

Other then that, there is also DSL.  Which stands for Damn small Linux.  I played around with it on a laptop with a LOT less resources then what you have and it screamed.

Good luck.
