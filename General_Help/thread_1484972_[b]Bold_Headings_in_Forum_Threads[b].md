### Post by howefield on 2010-05-16
Unread subject lines are bold... once you have read them, they lose the bold attribute.

---

### Post by warfacegod on 2010-05-16
When you create your Thread and then look at in the list of Threads, you won't see it in bold because you just read it. Wait for someone else to post in your Thread and then look at it in the list before opening it and will be bold.
