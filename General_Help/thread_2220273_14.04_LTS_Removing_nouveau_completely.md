### Post by ilarinews on 2015-02-09
sudo nano /etc/modprobe.d/nvidia-installer-disable-nouveau.conf
add:
blacklist nouveau
options nouveau modeset=0

### Post by monkeybrain20122 on 2015-02-09
Why do you need to remove nouveau? If you install the proprietary driver nouveau would be disabled automatically on reboot. It is good to keep it around in case the proprietary driver blows up.
