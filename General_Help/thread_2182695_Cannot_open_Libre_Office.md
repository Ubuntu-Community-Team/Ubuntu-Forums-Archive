### Post by scouser73 on 2013-10-22
1 - Open your **Home Folder**

2 - Press **Ctrl & H**, to show the hidden folders

3 - Click on the folder named **.config**

4 - Locate the folder named **libreoffice**

5 - Right click and select **Delete**

What you have just done is to delete the profile folder of LibreOffice, restarting LibreOffice will create a new **libreoffice** profile folder for you when you restart the LibreOffice application.
