### Post by Jerry N on 2011-03-21
When UFRAW starts up there is a row of tabs, starting with a picture of a balance on the left.  On mine, on the right side of the row of tabs there is a symbol with an arrow pointing into a computer, at least that is what I think it is.  Select that and you can get a choice of formats to save to - JPEG, TIFF, etc.  Name the file and hit the SAVE button on the lower right.
