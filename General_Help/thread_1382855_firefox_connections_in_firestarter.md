### Post by JackRock on 2010-01-16
Many websites host portions of their pages on third-party servers.  For example, they may host all the images on Photobucket/Imageshack/or even another service.  Also, portions of their site may be on additional servers that the company/group controls, but would have a separate IP address.

I would say based on strictly the information provided, you're probably fine.
