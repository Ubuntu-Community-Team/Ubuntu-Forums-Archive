### Post by 5149.5 on 2011-05-01
You can open CompizConfig Settings Manager from system settings. Click the button at the far right of the upper right panel and looka the bottom of that menu. In CCSM click the Unity plugin box.  To get the windows borders and buttons back, you can click the windows decoration item to open it and put "gtk-window-decorator --replace" in the command box. Go back to the main window and click the box for window decoration.  Close CCSM.
