### Post by mgmiller on 2008-06-21
I just found this one in a google search:
open alt/F2 and type:
```
free the fish
```This will make Wanda the fish swim around on your desktop.

To make Wanda swim away quickly, click on her.  She will come back later.

To turn off Wanda, again open alt/F2 and type:
```
pkill gnome-panel
```I haven't tried this command yet.  I think it will kill the top and bottom panels.  You may have to rerun the command
```
gnome-panel
```to get them back.  I suppose you could also just log off and back on.
