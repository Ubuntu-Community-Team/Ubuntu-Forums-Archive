### Post by fballem on 2011-08-14
That depends on whether your hardware will support 64-bit. If it does, then you should be able to run 64-bit ubuntu.

Basically, it's a combination of your processor and memory. By the way, if you have 3 gigabytes of RAM or less, then there is probably no advantage to running 64-bit.

If you give some information about your system - specifically your processor and RAM, then others will be able to give you specific guidance on whether 64-bit will work and is appropriate.

I hope this helps,
