### Post by Rancoras on 2004-11-27
Usually you don't type "reboot" in a terminal window.  Try typing reboot at that anonymous prompt.  Most likely what has happened is the machine is stuck during shutdown.  All of the needed services are probably already killed.  You can also try pressing CTRL+ALT+DEL.  If none of that works, you probably should just bite the bullet and power cycle the machine.

In the future, when you want to reboot the machine, click Computer > Logoff and choose the restart option.
