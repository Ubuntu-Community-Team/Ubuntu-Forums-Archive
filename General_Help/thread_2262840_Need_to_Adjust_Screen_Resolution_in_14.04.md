### Post by grahammechanical on 2015-01-27
What kind of a connection are you using to connect the graphic adapter to the TV? The standard VGA connection is not good enough for modern digital TV/Monitors. I found this out recently when I purchased a digital TV to use as a monitor. I have a VGA cable and a DVI cable but the TV only had VGA and HDMI connectors. So, I used the VGA cable and I could not get the full resolutions available from the monitor. I did not have the particular problem that you had. I then purchased a HDMI cable and now every thing is much better and running at the correct resolutions.

Regards.
