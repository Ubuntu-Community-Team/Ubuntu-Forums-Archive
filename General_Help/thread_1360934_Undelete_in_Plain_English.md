### Post by 5BallJuggler on 2009-12-21
> **amommy said:**
> Where is that exactly? I did a search for anything mp3 and some showed up that were in shared but nothing near the amount he had on there.

If you go to the top toolbar and click on "places", then on "home folder"
Wait for the folder to display, there should be a side pane on the left hand side, about halfway down there is a folder that says "deleted items"

If the side bar is not there, click on "view" at the top of the window, then select "side pane"

click on the deleted items icon, when the folder opens highlight everything and then right click on the mouse, then select "restore"

Hope this helps
