### Post by boarder428 on 2010-06-18
I am running Lucid, 10.04 LTS, In the simple compizconfig settings manager under "animations" there is an option to "enable extra animations" that is greyed out (not selectable)  I have the option availiable on 2 other computers and am wondering why this option is not availiable on one pc.  The pc in question has an nvidia 8400 gts graphics card and I beleive I have the correct drivers installed.  The other pc's that the option works on both have ATI graphics cards.  If someone could help me troubleshoot this I would greatly appreciate it.

Corey

### Post by stinkeye on 2010-06-19
I think you need to install compiz-fusion-plugins-extra.
See if that's installed on your other pc.
```
sudo apt-get install compiz-fusion-plugins-extra
```

May have to restart your pc
