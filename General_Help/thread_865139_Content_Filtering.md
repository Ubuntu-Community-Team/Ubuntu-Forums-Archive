### Post by songshu on 2008-07-20
depends on what you call objectionable ;)

personally i love porn and violence but hate sites that tell i should stop smoking, drinking and drug abuse in general ;)

there are some things you can install to block sites, but the simplest way i think is to use [http://www.opendns.com/](http://www.opendns.com/).

you can use there dns servers anyway (unfiltered) but if you create an account (its for free) you can block certain content like violence, sex, gambling, virus etc.. depending on what you want.

p.s.

the only way they make money is to send you to a google like page with search results and some adds in case you mistype something like [www.ubunut.com](www.ubunut.com)
