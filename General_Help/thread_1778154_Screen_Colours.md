### Post by Frogs Hair on 2011-06-08
Right click the desktop and select change background . When Appearance Preferences opens take a look around in the other tabs , you will find other themes and appearance options.

### Post by nzjethro on 2011-06-08
Open up Appearance (System > Preferences > Appearance). If you click Customise in your Themes tab, you get the choice to change the colour of windows, selected items, etc.

So much choice!
