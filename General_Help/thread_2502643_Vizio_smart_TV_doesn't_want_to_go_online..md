### Post by yancek on 2024-11-23
>  What I had saved as my 'TV network/password' isn't recognized by the Vizio when I try to connect it to the internet.


If you reset to factory default that would be expected.  The link below to the VIzio site discusses resetting it and indicates the default password is 0000

[https://support.vizio.com/s/article/How-to-reset-your-VIZIO-VIA-HDTV-Clear-Memory-Factory-Defaults?language=en_US](https://support.vizio.com/s/article/How-to-reset-your-VIZIO-VIA-HDTV-Clear-Memory-Factory-Defaults?language=en_US)

The Arris modem default user/password=admin/password explained at their site at the link below.

[https://www.passwarden.com/help/use-cases/arris-default-password](https://www.passwarden.com/help/use-cases/arris-default-password)
