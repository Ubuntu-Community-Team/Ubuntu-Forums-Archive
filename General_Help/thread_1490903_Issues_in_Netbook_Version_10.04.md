Are the sites taking too much time to be loaded and all you see is Firefox saying the site is being loaded? If yes, then see the fifth item on [this list](http://firefox-tutorials.blogspot.com/2010/05/common-issues-solutions.html).

> **fercordovam said:**
> i let videos charge at 100% and then reproduce them, but still slower.

Since you have already loaded the video 100%, then is not an network issue. If you are talking about flash videos like YouTube, then see the [[COLOR="Sienna"]**Flash Optimization**[/COLOR]](http://firefox-tutorials.blogspot.com/2010/05/flash-optimization.html) section of [Firefox optimization and troubleshooting thread](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1193567).

If you are talking about other types of video, then see See [Comprehensive Multimedia & Video Howto]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=766683").

Also make sure you have the latest graphics driver installed. Go to "System >> Administration >> Hardware Drivers".
