### Post by Genius314 on 2008-09-08
You can change the size of Cairo-dock.

In the advanced settings, go to the Icons tab, and go to the section with "Launchers," "Applications," "Applets," and "Separators."
Change the "Icon size at rest" for each of them to something smaller.
This will make the icons smaller, which will also make the dock smaller.
