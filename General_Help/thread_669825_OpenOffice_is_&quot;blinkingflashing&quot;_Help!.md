### Post by VON_CAPO on 2008-01-17
I do not know if it is the same case but it could help.
I had the same problem with full screen pictures, but I solved it disabling Compiz: "System/Preferences/Appearance/Visual Effects" and choose "None".

### Post by WakkiTabakki on 2008-05-18
Uncheck the "Legacy fullscreen support" option in the Workarounds section in Compiz Settings Manager. This will fix the flashing OO problem and allow you to have the fancy compiz effects active as well...
(source: [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=600245](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=600245))
