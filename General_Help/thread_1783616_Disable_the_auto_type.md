### Post by papibe on 2011-06-16
If your're talking of Google itself, go to Google's home page. Click on the little wheel on the upper right corner. Select 'Search settings'.

In the settings page look for a section called 'Google Instant'. Select 'Do not use Google Instant'. Go to the end of the page and click on 'Save Preferences'.

Regards.
