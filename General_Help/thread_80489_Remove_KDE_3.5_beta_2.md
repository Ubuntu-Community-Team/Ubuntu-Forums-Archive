### Post by SGC on 2005-10-22
1- Install **synaptic** if you don't have it.

2- Open synaptic and click on search and enter **3.4.92** and choose **version** from the drop-down menu

3- Order the package by clicking on the **S** from the first column and selecte all the installed packages (the ones with a green square in front of them) and hit on **CTRL+E** from your keyboard

4- From the dropdown menu choose **4:3.4.3-ubuntu4 (breezy)** then click on **force version**. If you get a windows that ask you to mark additional changes, click on **mark**

5- Finally click on the apply button in synaptic
