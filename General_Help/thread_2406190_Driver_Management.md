### Post by oldos2er on 2018-11-17
According to [https://lubuntu.me/cosmic-released/](https://lubuntu.me/cosmic-released/) under the "Known Issues and Workarounds" heading, it says "We have not ported over the &#8220;Additional drivers&#8221; tab from  software-properties-gtk to software-properties-qt, which can be used for  installing additional CPU and graphics drivers. As a workaround, you  can use the ubuntu-drivers command line tool." Looks like it's still a work in progress.
