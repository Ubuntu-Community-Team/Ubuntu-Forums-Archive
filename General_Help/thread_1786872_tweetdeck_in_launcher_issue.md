OK, I think I may know what's going on with that, and I hope this is what you are talking about? 

When I first installed TweetDeck, it leaves it's icon on the Desktop - as you already know. From there I drag & dropped it into the top panel, and it leave a smaller version of the icon up there on the panel.

So, the second time I did it, I did the drag & drop up to the panel and left the Desktop Icon where it was. 

I restarted. Then I deleted the Desktop icon after that. The launcher in the panel stayed, and it worked.
