### Post by drewcoon on 2008-06-19
You didn't accidentally delete the window list, by any chance?

Try right clicking the panel > Add to Panel... > Then find "Window List" in the list of panel apps :)

### Post by Joeb454 on 2008-06-19

Add that, and see if it appears then :)
