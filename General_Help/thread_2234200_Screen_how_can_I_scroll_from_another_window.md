### Post by bapoumba on 2014-07-13
I'd like to elaborate a little on that, although I agree with Vladenin :)

Stay focused (as per desktop focus) onto the window where you want to input characters, and move your mouse over the manual instructions, you should be able to scroll without giving focus to this particular window. I do that all the time :)

Of course, you need a working mouse or trackpad.
