anything that is supposed to startup as daemon will be running, such as SSH or Apache for example.

logging out merely means you are not logged in to the system, after all you are merely a user to the system.  Any apps or processes that are unique to you during your session will cease (or should cease) the system processes and services continue to run, what they are is dependent on your system and what it is used for.
