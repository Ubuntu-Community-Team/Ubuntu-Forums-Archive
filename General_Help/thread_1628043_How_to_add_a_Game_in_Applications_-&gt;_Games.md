### Post by WienerWuerstel on 2010-11-22
Right Click on Applications and then click on Edit Menu.
After that click on Games and then you click on new Entry.
Here you can choose a Name, Icon and the Exe File for your Wine Game. Now it should be in the Menu where you want it to be.
