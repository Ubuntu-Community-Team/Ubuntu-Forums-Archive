### Post by mkoehler on 2007-06-03
I understand, but I would not recommend buying a dell computer.  You're better off creating a custom computer yourself - Most things that you buy will be compatible with ubuntu, but if I have one recommendation, it is to buy a motherboard with an integrated intel chipset (and you can buy other cards on the side).  Otherwise, you can buy HDD's and connect them via SATA, and link them via LVM (linux's free equivalent of RAID).  I recommend that because you can buy motherboards with approx. 6 SATA ports, and 500GB HDD's are more cost-effective than 750GB HDD's.  Hope this helps.
