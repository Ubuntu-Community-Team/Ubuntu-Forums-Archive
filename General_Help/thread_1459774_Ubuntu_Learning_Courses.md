From a server administration perspective, all of the core good practices have not changed over last 3 years. Details concerning service administration are always shifting anyway, so you have to *develop problem solving skills*. The certification is good for getting you in door, but if you can't figure things out because they don't match what you learned, the training clearly didn't do its jobs. You need to be able to adapt and not be thrown by something as trivial the move from sysV to upstart.
