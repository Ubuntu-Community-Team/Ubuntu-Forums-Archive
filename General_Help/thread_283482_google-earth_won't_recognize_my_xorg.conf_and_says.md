### Post by guix on 2006-10-24
Problem solved for me.
Composite is now enabled by default by the scripts that generate xorg.conf in the new xorg. And GeForce2 doesn't support it (and more nvidia-glx-legacy card I guess).

Add this at the end of your /etc/X11/xorg.conf :

Section "Extensions"
        Option "Composite" "Disable"
EndSection
