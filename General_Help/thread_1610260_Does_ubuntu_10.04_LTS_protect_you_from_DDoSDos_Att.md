### Post by holiday on 2010-10-31
If you genuinely suspect you might become a target of a DOS attack then you will have to harden your security with some good tools. A firewall, and something like Deny Hosts. 

On the other hand, if you're just interested in Unix security, get into intrusion detection. Take a look at snort. Run chkrootkit, tripwire. Set up a DMZ box and open it up to the wild internet. Hard-format that box every weekend and learn how to protect it. 

Unix has some great security tools and it's a very interesting area and skills with those tools are supremely marketable in these terrified times.
