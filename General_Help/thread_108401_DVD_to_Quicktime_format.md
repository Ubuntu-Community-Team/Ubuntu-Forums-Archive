### Post by sciurus on 2005-12-26
You should be able to do this with mencoder. Find out the exact video specifications that the ipod video supports*, then read the [mencoder documentation]("http://www.mplayerhq.hu/DOCS/HTML/en/index.html") (it starts at chapter 7). 

*There are many guides for converting video for the ipod using ffmpegx or handbrake that should be useful for this.
