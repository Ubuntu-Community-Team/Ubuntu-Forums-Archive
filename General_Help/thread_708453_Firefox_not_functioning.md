### Post by Chappy7777 on 2008-02-26
This is what I would do.

Go to Synaptic and do a search for firefox. If you see the version you installed there, mark it for removal (uninstall). Click apply and do it.

Then do the search again and look for Firefox,  the latest version. Mark for installation. Apply.
Let it run. You should, when done, have Firefox 2.0.0.12 installed. 

If you used the version that was there when you did the install, and entered bookmarks, then you should be ok w/them and Profile. Otherwise, backup your bookmarks. I use a program that works as an addon in FFox called Foxmarks.  Install that into your present version and it will save your bookmarks for you. Go to foxmarks.com and read the info. 

Synaptic should do the trick. Sorry I can't help you w/the beta version, but problems like yours are why I have learned to stay with versions that have been out for awhile and the forums are not filled with posts saying they've found this and that bug, problem, etc.
