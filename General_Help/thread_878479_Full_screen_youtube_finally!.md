### Post by Bucky Ball on 2008-08-02
Hi all.

Just thought I'd post this as I have been screwing around trying to fix it and last night a small miracle happened ... I fixed it!

I have HP dv6000 series laptop. Everything working fine (in fact, sweet!) but I have never been able to watch youtube in full screen. Always goes to full screen for a second and then snaps back to the youtube page again, embedded and small. I have hunted for help with this and no-one really seems to have any idea and there is precious little info out there that I could find. So here is what I did and it was, in the end, incredibly simple.

I went to this page and had a look about ...

[http://forlong.blogage.de/article/2008/4/26/How-to-set-up-Compiz-Fusion-074](http://forlong.blogage.de/article/2008/4/26/How-to-set-up-Compiz-Fusion-074)

I was actually looking to switch compiz off and played with a few things. I got to the part about 'CompizConfig - Settings Manager' and thought I would get that happening and see if it did any good. Followed instructions on how to install, installed, opened up System->Preferences->Advanced Desktop Settings Manager, switched off 'Desktop Wall', went to youtube, played a vid and clicked the fullscreen button and ... voila! Success at last!!!

About the closest I'd gotten in the past was advice to switch off the screen saver as this could be causing the problem. No joy. This seems to be the bomb though and maybe vaguely related.

If I could actually find Forlong (I only speak English and this blog has a different language login and I have searched the forums) I would genuflect my thanks for the tip, so if you're out there Forlong, thanks a heap for such an informative page. Helped me out and hopefully this post will help others with various problems.

If you do access this page or any of Forlong's projects, as he says on his blog, he is busy developing so give him any info and feedback you can (which is why I have pasted no code here ... I want you to go to the page!) :)

Good luck Ubuntu-ers world wide and may the community thrive forever more!

Afterthought: One strange thing though. I downloaded Compiz-Switch, a patch to switch Compiz on and off easily. That does kinda what youtube was doing, goes to a full screen then back again, then nothing happens. No GUI and back to the desktop. Doesn't seem to switch anything. Anyone else have this result?
