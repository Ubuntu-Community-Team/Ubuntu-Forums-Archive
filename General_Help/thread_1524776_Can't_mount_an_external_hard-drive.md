### Post by akxiii on 2010-07-05
I had an issue and managed to solve it, but wanted to post it up here in case it ever happened to anyone else.

I bought an external hard drive, plugged it in, and went to reformat it as ext4. Something went haywire and I had to reboot in the middle of the reformat. When it started up I couldn't find the hard drive.

The solution is to use gparted or disk utility, navigate to the hard-drive and continue the reformat from there.

Hope this helps someone, as it stumped (and scared) me for a little while.
