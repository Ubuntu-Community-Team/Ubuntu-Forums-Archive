### Post by celsofaf on 2006-12-09
OK, I "solved" it myself. Just deleted the ~/.kde folder (backing the old one up), logged out, then logged in. :) Drawback: many personal configurations were lost, but this is nothing crucial.

A tip for anyone with the same problem (if caused after themes changes): do one change at a time to your KDE theme, and always save each step. ;)
