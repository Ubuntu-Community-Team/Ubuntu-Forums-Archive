### Post by Sherioushlishp on 2009-12-18
I recently had a problem with my 9.10 rig. I went into hibernate and when I tried to wake it up the monitor stayed inactive. The fans, cpu, and hdd were all running but the monitor would state "No Signal" when the power button was pressed.

The problem persisted even when I did a hard reboot. I was sure that the monitor wasn't working but when I tried it out on another computer, it worked fine. Perplexed, I decided to open up the case of the malfunctioning tower and tinkered.

The solution to my problem was removing the RAM, powering the computer without it in, waiting for the beep, and then safely returning the ram.

Being new to linux I'm excited to have solved my first real issue with it! I hope this helps someone out!
