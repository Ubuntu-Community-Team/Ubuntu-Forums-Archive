### Post by devilears on 2007-10-16
we are misunderstanding each other:
In Evolution, click on EDIT
click on PREFERENCES, a small window will open.
Now click on the EDIT button on the righthand side of the Preferences box.
Another window containing a bunch of Tabs will open.
Click on the tab marked "Defaults".
You should now see something called "Drafts Folder" and "Sent Messages Folder"
Click on the buttons next to it and select the appropriate folder you want your sent items to be stored in.
Hope this explains it better.
