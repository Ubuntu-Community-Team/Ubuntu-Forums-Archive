### Post by aysiu on 2007-01-19
To hide files, rename them with a . in front of the name.

So if your file is called /home/atlfalcons/file.txt, call it /home/atlfalcons/.file.txt and it'll be hidden.

To view hidden files, press Control-H.

### Post by lilBeat on 2007-01-19
While browsing with Nautilus to some folder press CTRL+H. It will show you hidden files/folders. Pressing it again will hide them back. All files and folders that are marked as hiden have dot in front ( .[folder] or .[file] ).
