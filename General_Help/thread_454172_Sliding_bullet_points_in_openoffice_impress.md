Information taken from here;

[http://wiki.services.openoffice.org/wiki/Impress_Guide:_Slide_Shows]("http://wiki.services.openoffice.org/wiki/Impress_Guide:_Slide_Shows")

**Applying an animation effect**
[LIST=1]
[*]In Normal View, display the desired slide. Select the object you want to animate.
[*]In the Tasks pane, choose Custom Animation
[*]Click Add. The Custom Animation dialog appears. Choose an effect from one of the pages of this dialog, and choose the speed or duration of that effect.
[*]Click OK to save the effect and return to the Custom Animation page on the Task pane. Here you can choose how to start the animation, change the speed, and some additional properties of the selected effect.
[/LIST]

### Post by MegaJim on 2008-02-08
Add a custom animation.

At the bottom of the animation side panel right click the added animation -> effect options

Go to Text Animation tab, under Group Text, choose "By 1st level paragraphs"

Click OK

Back in the side panel, click the + next to your text, hit Ctrl+A to select all paragraphs, change the animationstart to "On click" and check its working.

Not the easiest by the oo.org devs, but still it gets you there :)
