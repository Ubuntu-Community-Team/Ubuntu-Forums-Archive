### Post by QIII on 2010-05-05
How does it behave when running a Live session from the LiveCD.

Your graphics card no longer has support from AMD/ATI on Linux, and the old driver that supports it, 9.4, will not work with the current Xserver in Lucid.

The best you can hope for is that the open source driver will work.  Even still, you may have to generate an xorg.conf file (it will not exist by default).

Try running a Live session and tell us what happens.
