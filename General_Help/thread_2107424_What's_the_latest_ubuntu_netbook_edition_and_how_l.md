Canonical abandoned the netbook edition back in 11.10. So there isn't one. So you need to use Xubuntu or Lubuntu for your netbook.

Or you could load Ubuntu 12.04 LTS and use Unity 2D all the goodies of Unity and Ubuntu, but with less requirements.
