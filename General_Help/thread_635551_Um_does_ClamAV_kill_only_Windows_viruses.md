### Post by Rocket2DMn on 2007-12-08
The only reason to keep a virus scanner on linux is to keep from passing viruses on to windows users.  There currently aren't any known viruses "in the wild" that affect linux, so we don't even need virus scanners.  Some people choose to run them to avoid passing on viruses to friends and familles through email and whatnot, but most of us feel it is their job to protect their own computers.
