### Post by mc4man on 2010-06-27
Well it means just that - the package(s) was successfully built and available.

If you click on 'package details' link you'll [get this page]("https://launchpad.net/~nvidia-vdpau/+archive/ppa/+packages"), notice that all completed builds aren't successful, some may fail for various reasons.

Then they are either corrected and built again, hopefully successfully or occasionally are abandoned.
