### Post by egalvan on 2011-11-20
as *searchfgold* stated, karmic is no longer supported.

If you want an older release, 10.04LTS (Lucid Lynx) will have support (for the gui stuff) until April 2013. 
the core will have support until April 2015.

Regular releases have an 18-month support life.

LTS (Long Term Support) have 3 years for the desk-top (gui stuff),
and 5 years for the core/server stuff (non-gui stuff).
