### Post by tvtech on 2008-12-08
YOU NEED A NEW HARD DRIVE repeat after me, you have had a catastrophic failure and will need to purchase a new piece of hardware, although the section of your drive which the MBR(Master Boot Record), it's a bios/windows thing, writes too appears to be okay from what you've written, you have had a serious mechanical failure, probably where the magnetic laminate overheated and began to physically peal off the platters, it sounds like your read heads are still functioning.  

purchase a new drive, install it and then install ubuntu.
