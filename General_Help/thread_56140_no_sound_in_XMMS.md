### Post by PMO6022 on 2005-08-11
You can try this:

1. Open up xmms and go to "options" --> "preferences"
2. Under the "Audio I/O" tab, select "CD Audio Player 1.2.10" and click "configure"
3. In the window that comes up, select "digital audio extraction"

Good luck.
