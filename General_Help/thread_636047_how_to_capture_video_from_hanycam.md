### Post by scorp123 on 2007-12-09
> **rustam_sandhu said:**
> i try finding a firewire now:confused: [http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Firewire](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Firewire)
[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/DV](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/DV)

... check the pictures there. Most modern machines (e.g. built after 2002) have this type of connector, it's just a matter of having the right cable. If you don't have one you will usually find them easily in any electronics shop (e.g. where they sell TV's, VCR's, camcorders and computers ... if they sell those things then they usually should also sell the right cables).

If your computer has no such Firewire port then you can buy a cheap PCI card that will add this type of board to your desktop machine; if you have a laptop you could add Firewire via PCMCIA or a CardBus add-on card (depending on your type of laptop) ... but as I said, most desktops and laptops built after 2002 should already have this type of connector.
