### Post by lovinglinux on 2011-07-07
Just copy the hidden folder ~/.mozilla to your new home folder. This will make all your Firefox settings, including bookmarks to be available in the new installation.

BTW, the bookmark manager is now located in the "All Bookmarks" option in the bookmarks menu.
