### Post by rraj.be on 2008-06-04
Nothing to wory.

Just get into your

```
Synaptic package manager [system-->administration-->synaptic package manager
```

It will ask for your pasword.

Enter it.

Select your vmware-server which you wanted to update.

right click on it and select upgrade.

Click Apply at the top.

Click install in popup window.

Your vmware will be upgraded.
