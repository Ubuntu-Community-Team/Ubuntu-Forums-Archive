### Post by Ramses de Norre on 2007-01-08
Have you already did a memtest? You can do so by choosing memtest86+ in your grub menu.
Segfaults could be a result of failing ram sticks, so if memtest finds errors I'd take out all but one stick and run it again on that and then continue by sticking one stick extra until you've got the one that needs to be replaced.
