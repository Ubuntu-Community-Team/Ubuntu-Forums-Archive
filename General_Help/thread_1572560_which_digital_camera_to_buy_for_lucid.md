### Post by efflandt on 2010-09-11
As long as it has some sort of memory card (like SD) that you could put in any USB memory card reader, it doesn't really matter what type of camera you get.  The image files should be able to be read by any graphics program.

For example I can get pictures from my phone by just putting them on microSD and using an SD adapter in a USB card reader.  My regular cameras use SD or SDHC.  I have never tethered a camera itself to a PC since the days of slow serial ports (before USB).

### Post by jamesisin on 2010-09-11
I would get a Canon (first choice) or a Nikon (second choice).  I have four Canons (three digital) and they are at the top of their game right now.  (I owned one Nikon and it died, but it took great pictures.)
