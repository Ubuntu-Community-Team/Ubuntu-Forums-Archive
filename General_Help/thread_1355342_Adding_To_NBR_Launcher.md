### Post by Brandon Williams on 2009-12-15
Those instructions say, 'From the Home Launcher, left side click Preferences, then find and click Main Menu.' If you don't see the Preferences menu item in the unr launcher, then press Alt+F2 and run alacarte (the app the is started from Preferences->Main Menu). Continue following the instructions from there.
