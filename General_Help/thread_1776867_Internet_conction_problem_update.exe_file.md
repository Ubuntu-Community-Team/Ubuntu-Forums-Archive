### Post by MrSpike16 on 2011-06-06
If I understand you correctly when trying to connect to the Internet at one location you get the error but works ok at another (when using Ubuntu).

If this is the case one possibility is the network you are connecting to "in my house" is infected with a virus and your Windows already has the infection.   Your connection when using Ubuntu is being redirected to try to infect it.

Another possibility is the "in my house" network or ISP has Internet security features that has been added or enabled and it is trying to protect you from going on the Internet until some protection has been installed on your computer.
