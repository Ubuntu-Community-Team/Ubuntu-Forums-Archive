### Post by Tamlynmac on 2009-07-01
You can delete any mail account listed in Evolution by:

edit/preferences

Left click (one time) on category "mail accounts" on left side when the preference window opens. Then left click (one time on the mail account you wish to delete and left click on the delete button on the right side of the window. This will delete that individual mail account. This is also where you would add a new mail account.

You can disable the account and not delete it by unchecking the box to the left of the account name.

Good Luck and I hope this helps.
