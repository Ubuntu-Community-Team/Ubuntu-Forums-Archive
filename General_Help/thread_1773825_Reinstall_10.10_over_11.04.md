### Post by MunkyJunky on 2011-06-02
If you set up your home directory (/home) to be on a separate partition, it helps when having to reinstall your system. Then you can just remount it during install and preserve your data. 

Otherwise, just using a 10.10 disk to do a regular install should sort you to format your partitions, removing 11.04 and giving you a fresh 10.10 install. Make sure you back up your data to DVD/USB HDD/similar first.
