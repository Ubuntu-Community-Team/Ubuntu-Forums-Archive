### Post by ivanovnegro on 2010-12-24
For what I know, just remove the old version and install the new one.
Your ratings, your database and so on will stay where it is.
But you can make a backup, you will find your database and config files of Clementine in your home folder in ".config -> Clementine", its a hidden folder.

---

### Post by WorMzy on 2010-12-24
Your personal data/ratings/whatever are stored in your home area (/home/username/), and is not touched by package updates.
