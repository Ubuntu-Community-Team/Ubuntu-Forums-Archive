### Post by ofnuts on 2011-12-25
> **glennbates said:**
> When a web site places a cookie on your computer, how do you know what it is named and where to find it?

And can you edit it to change a login time.
To find it on Firefox: Edit/Preferences/Privacy/Show cookies and you can filter by host name.

If you have the SQLite manager add-on to Firefox ([http://code.google.com/p/sqlite-manager/](http://code.google.com/p/sqlite-manager/)), you can navigate the cookies.sqlite DB in your Firefox profile and edit any value. But finding how the login time is encoded in the cookie is left as an exercise to the reader.
