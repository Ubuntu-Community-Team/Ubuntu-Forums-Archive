### Post by niteshifter on 2008-06-09
Hi,

To empty the trash folder in Evolution:

Click File
Click Empty Trash

What you've been experiencing is the way it's designed to be: You can easily place something in the trash, easily take it out of the trash (right clicking an item in trash presents an option to undelete it). But to really, actually make "deleted" email go away - with (practically) no hope of ever getting it back - you're required to shift **your** focus (use the menu).

### Post by niteshifter on 2008-06-10
Hi,

That link says basically the same as my post - but I forgot about Ctrl+E. So, there are three ways:

Click File, then Click Empty Trash.

Right click on the Trash folder, click Empty Trash.

While in the Trash folder press Ctrl+E, click the Expunge button on the dialog.

Myself, I avoid keyboard shortcuts for irrevocable deletes, even when backstopped with a dialog like Evolution does (but notice it's an optional dialog - you can turn it off). To easy for fingers to outrun brain :)
