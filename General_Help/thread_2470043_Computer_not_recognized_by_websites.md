### Post by guiverc on 2021-12-19
As already indicated, this has nothing to do with OS, hardware or web sites.

It's security settings. If privacy/security settings are high; web sites you go to cannot realize you're a returning user, and so they need to verify who you are each time.  This occurs regardless of browser, OS etc. though some OSes & browsers default to higher level settings when contrasted with others.

Lower your privacy/security settings until you achieve what you want.
