### Post by Usufruct on 2008-11-25
It looks like what you may have done is that you've removed the system tray icons from your panel.  Try right clicking on the area where they used to show up, selecting 'Add to Panel', and then selecting 'Notification Area'.  It might be called 'System Notification Area' in older Ubuntu releases.

Give that a shot and see if it fixes your issue.
