Every time I click on a pdf I get a popup that asks me what viewer I want to use.  Is there a way to avoid this extra popup so it goes directly into the pdf?

### Post by Slim Odds on 2009-01-17
In Nautilus, right-click on a PDF file. Select "properties" (at the bottom of the menu). Go to the "Open With" tab and select whichever program you want.

---

### Post by BobLand on 2009-01-17
Actually, I found the problem was in the browser.  Edit/Preferences/Applications.  At the bottom the pdf was "Always Ask."  I changed it first to arcoread but the font was too funky so I switched it back evince.  Now I can open a pdf from the browser and not get asked.

bobland
