### Post by Bucky Ball on 2016-01-18
> **mastablasta said:**
> Skype should be able to ring it for free. you only pay if you pick up the phone. you can (may need to) load some small money (prepaid service).

You need to have money in your Skype account to ring any landline or mobile, apart from another Skype user. This makes sense because Skype are expecting you are calling with the intention of connecting, not to find your phone. If you ring it and you don't hear it, it will connect. That will cost money. If you have none in your account, it won't ring in the first place to avoid this. :)

This is the point I was attempting to make before. Can't imagine there is any software that is going allow you make what would be a paid call, which is effectively what you are doing, connecting or not, without having some cash involved. 

Love to know about a 'phone finding' app if one does exist, though.
