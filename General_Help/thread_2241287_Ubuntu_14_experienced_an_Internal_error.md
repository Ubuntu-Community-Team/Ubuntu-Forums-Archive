### Post by grahammechanical on 2014-08-25
How did you get that information? If we receive a crash report we also get an option to see the details of the crash and an option to report it. If we accept that then the crash reporting utility, Apport, will collect all the necessary log files. Some of which may reveal confidential information and we will be asked to confirm the possible passing on of that information. And then the utility will open a web browser at the Ubuntu bug reporting web site, Launchpad.

[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Apport](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Apport)

[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Launchpad](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Launchpad)

You may find that the bug has already been reported. In which case you can confirm that you are also affected. It may be that the bug has already been confirmed as affecting multiple users and is being assessed. The developers do not need a large list of "me too" reports.

Regards.
