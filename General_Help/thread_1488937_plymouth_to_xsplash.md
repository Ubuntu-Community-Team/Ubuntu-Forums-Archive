### Post by gaaslight on 2010-05-21
Plymouth, like grub2 is the future. Ubuntu developers believe "if it ain't broke, break it.". There's no going back. There will eventually be better plymouth bootsplash themes available, but you won't be able to install usplash without gutting gnome and you won't be able to install splashy at all. As far as xplash is concerned, you will have to remove plymouth first.
