### Post by suhaswadkar on 2006-07-20
Hi All, This is my second post on this forum. First- [http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=218873](http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=218873). I did some googling & found the solution- WvDial. Guess what, ubuntu 6.06 installs it by default :).
    A friend had told me- **In Linux World, A man without "man" is not a man.**;) So I decided to read the manual of WvDial first- **$man wvdial**
    After reading it carefully, I also read wvdialconf manual- **$man wvdialconf**
    One more reading followed- **$man wvdial.conf**
    Next I edited the /etc/wvdial.conf & entered the dial no, password & username (U can get this info from your mobile service provider). I decided to go on & connected my sony ericsson w810i mobile to my acer aspire 5002 laptop. Time for Action- **#wvdial**
    Boom- It detected my mobile & did all the configuration. Right now I'm writing this post in firefox in Ubuntu. Bye Bye XP...Finally :)
