### Post by baracuda68 on 2008-10-27
try FEBE at[https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/2109]("https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/2109") for Firefox. You can copy/save to disc copies of .mozilla & .mozilla-thunderbird files, in your /home/username directory.These are hidden folders. Hope this helps.:)
