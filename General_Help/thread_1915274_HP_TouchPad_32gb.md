### Post by LeighT on 2012-01-25
If you plug in the Touchpad it will ask you if you want to use it as a usb drive touch on usb drive, then Ubuntu will recognise it as a drive so you can transfer files to and from it.
If you have a GMail account you can sync contacts with it you can also set up other mail accounts on the Touchpad, you may be able to sync other mail account contacts but I haven't tried
You can also set up calendar events through GMail and sync them with Thunderbird on Ubuntu.
