When Ubuntu updates are downloaded regularly, are these updates constantly getting larger in size on the HDD over time or do they only increase nominally and simply overwrite much of the older updates?

Thank you.

---

### Post by stefangr1 on 2008-04-03
It is the latter. Old packages are replaced with their newest version, so that hdd usage only increases as newer packages grow. When you update, both the size of the files that have to be downloaded and the additional amount of diskspace that is required are reported.
