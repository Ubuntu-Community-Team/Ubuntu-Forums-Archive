Update:

I resolved my issue.

MY solution was to reinstall Totem and Banshee (standard reinstall via Synaptic)

I can only assume what happened was that when I installed some extra codecs that totem and Banshee some how got confused. 

I now have full video playback regardless of the Media app being used.
