### Post by pastalavista on 2008-11-03
I think it is a compiz "window decoration" problem. Compiz doesn't play well with all graphics cards. Try disabling compiz (System > Preferences > Appearance > Visual Effects) and turn off the hardware acceleration and see if that fixes it. Of course, this would affect all your windows and not just Firefox...
