When a Kobo ereader is connected to a computer a screen on the ereader comes up allowing you to connect to the computer.

Pondering the situation, and having a cup of coffee and biscuit, up came the 'Connect' screen. Tapped the connect and everything worked fine. But it didn't stay that way.
