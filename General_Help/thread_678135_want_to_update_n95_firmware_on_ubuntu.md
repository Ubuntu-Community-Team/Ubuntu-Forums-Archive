### Post by kidders on 2008-01-26
Hi there,

I wouldn't recommend trying. If something should go wrong with your update, you could quite easily end up bricking your phone, so (as an N95 owner myself) I strongly suggest following Nokia's upgrade instructions to the letter ... and they simply don't provide a way for Linux users to do it.

Seems like you did away with your Windows too quickly :(

One possibility might be to run a virtualised copy of Windows, just for the sake of the firmware update, and then delete it again. If it were *my* phone, I don't think I would try it though.
