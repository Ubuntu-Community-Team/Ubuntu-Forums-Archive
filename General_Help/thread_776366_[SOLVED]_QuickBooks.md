### Post by jimmy the saint on 2008-04-30
For .pub files your best bet is to convert them into pdf and move on.  Publisher is among the most proprietary formats out there.  You cannot even open them in other microsoft programs.  Apparantly nobody is working (hard at least) to enable support for them because it isn't a professional level program, and so few people actually use it.  Sorry.

As for Quickbook support, GNUCash is supposed to be a nice finance program and it supports importing from Quicken, so I would assume it would also support Quickbooks, as they are made by the same people.  I did some quick research (around 3 minutes) and essentially it requires exporting your data to a a specific filetype from within Quck* and then importing that file into gnucash.

Hope this helps.
sorry about the publisher files though
