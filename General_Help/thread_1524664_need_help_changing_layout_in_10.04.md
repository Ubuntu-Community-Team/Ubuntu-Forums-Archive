### Post by davidmohammed on 2010-07-05
you can drag panels around e.g. move the main panel at the top to the bottom.

or you could try [this]("http://ubuntu.online02.com/node/14") script to customise your display to look like windows!

Personally I've deleted all panels after I've installed awn (search in software centre) - you can customise awn to have a menu launcher like windows does and also have all your favourite applications displayed and launched from the dock.

Good luck customising!
