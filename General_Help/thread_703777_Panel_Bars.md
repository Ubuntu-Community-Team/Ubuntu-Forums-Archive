### Post by wireddad on 2008-02-21
At fresh install of Ubuntu, there are 2 panel bars.  When you open/minimize a program it goes/minimize to the bottom bar.  If I delete the bottom bar, where do the programs go and how would I maximize the program again?  Thanks.

---

### Post by Shazaam on 2008-02-21
Right-click the panel (bar) and choose "Add to panel". In the window that opens up find and then click "Window List" and click Add.

---

### Post by Pandemic187 on 2008-02-21
Technically, they don't go anywhere. They're still running, but I would guess that the only way you can switch to them would be by using Alt + Tab, for example. Of course, you could always use the "Add to panel..." feature to add a new window selector.
