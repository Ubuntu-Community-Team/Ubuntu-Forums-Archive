### Post by Derek Karpinski on 2011-11-07
I uninstalled Thunderbird after trying in vain to get it to work with my MS OWA exchange at work.
 
I went to settings menu, and chose 'online accounts', and filled in my gmail info.
 
Then I installed Evolution, and it pulled my gmail and gmail calendar in automatically.  I don't remember configuring anything.
 
Try 'online accounts' settings in Ubuntu.
