Tilda is a terminal emulator that drops down (or up or sideways depending on how you configure it). Think YuKuake for GNOME (but a tad less developed).

Tilda saves its settings in the .Tilda folder in your home directory, so I guess your best bet is to have a look there, but it hasn't lost changes I've made to the settings on my system, so I guess I'm not much help.
