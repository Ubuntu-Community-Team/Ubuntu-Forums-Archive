### Post by donovan1983 on 2012-05-19
Since it's a Samsung, you might try Kies Air. Airdroid also may do what you need it to. Both are browser-based and seem to offer a lot of capabilities. Airdroid is better for uploading files to your device, while Kies Air might be a little better for contact management and SMS messaging. Kies Air likely is already installed on your phone and Airdroid is a free app from the Android Market (er, Google Play Market).
