### Post by fragos on 2008-07-05
Adjusting Power Management Preferences can help. I'd also install "powertop" from the Ubuntu repositories. Here's the aps home page [http://www.lesswatts.org/projects/powertop/](http://www.lesswatts.org/projects/powertop/). Powertop will help you tune your system for better battery life. My Dell Inspiron 1420n gives me about 5 hours with the 9 cell battery. I only run Ubuntu.
