### Post by quixote on 2009-07-31
You may have already done this, but in case not . . .

In Nautilus, > Edit > Preferences > click the "preview" tab.  Thumbnails options are the second one down.  If it's set to "never," it won't generate thumbnails.  I'm not sure why they'd be blurry, but maybe if there's some kind of mismatch between the size specified under previews and the size nautilus wants to display?
