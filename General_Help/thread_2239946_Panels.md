### Post by Ksiencha on 2014-08-20
If you want to be able to create a new panel:[INDENT]1. Right click on desktop -> *Unock widgets*
2. Again right click on desktop -> *Add panel* -> choose *Default panel* (standart panel with menu, clock, system tray etc.) or *Empty panel*
3. Your new panel as well as other panels (only if widgets are unlocked) on the right side have button somewhat like a cashew. Press it -> move your cursor to the *Screen Edge*  and drag your panel to the preferable side of the screen.
4. Also you can notice *More Settings* button where you can configure your panel indentation, autohide option etc. If you want to change the size of the panel, use arrows (they are on both sides of panel) and for width drag *Width* button.
[/INDENT]

Now about color of the panel, I'm not sure you can change it, cause it depends on the theme of your desktop actually. But you can find many themes on the internet that perhaps will suit you needs (Use *Get new themes* button also). For example, I use open suse theme right now.

Also about color changing there is [askubuntu answer]("http://askubuntu.com/a/70730/277426") that explains some tricks.

I hope you'll find these explanations useful. Good luck ;)
