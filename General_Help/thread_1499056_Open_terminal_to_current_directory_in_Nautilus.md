### Post by Penguin Guy on 2010-06-01
Just wanted to add to [dragos2's post]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?p=9395079#post9395079"):

Nautilus does have a plugin system, plugins are in the format of nautilus-*<plugin-name>*. You can find a list of them by searching for [FONT="Courier New"]nautilus-[/FONT]. The plugin you are looking for is called [nautilus-open-terminal]("apt:nautilus-open-terminal").
