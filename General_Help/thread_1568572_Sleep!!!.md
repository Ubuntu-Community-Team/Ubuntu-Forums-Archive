I may have found part of my own answer.

In: "System\Preferences\Screensaver" there is another setting (I think it conflicts with Power Management).

It says: Regard the Computer as Idle after *selectable" minutes. Mine was set at 5 minutes.

I wouldn't think Ubuntu would log me out with an application running though (unattended for over 5 minutes) - but it does...
