### Post by Lars Noodén on 2010-01-18
You can use [Konqueror](http://www.konqueror.org/features/viewer.php) for that.  It handles PDF that way by default.  If you want to use another web browser for your surfing, that works too, just have that other browser point to Konqueror

The web browser konqueror is a rather amazing piece of software engineering, in a very good way.

### Post by doublewitt on 2010-02-02

You can get a tabbed environment with Okular inside Konqueror. Aside from your internet browsing tabs, you can open as many pdf files (tabs) as you wish in Konqueror. All of the features in Okular are available as well. That's how you can make Okular a **multi-tabbed** pdf reader...
