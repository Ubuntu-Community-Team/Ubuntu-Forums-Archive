On Ubuntu, go to your home folder.

Hit CTRL + H to show hidden files.

Copy your .thunderbird folder to somewhere else. This contains all your info.

On Windows to backup thunderbird, copy the thunderbird folder from your my documents.

When you re-install just put your .thunderbird / thunderbird folder back.
