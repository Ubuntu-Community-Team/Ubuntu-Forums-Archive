### Post by guiverc on 2021-11-27
The reporting bugs page was written assuming release notes too were on the wiki itself; alas that's not the case for many recent releases (*they're on the community hub now*). The wiki links thus still reference another wiki page; but the alternate page just  redirects you (*your browser really*) to the community hub.  It's not great for readers, but it works.

To me the "**Docs**" header works & makes sense, but I'm aware the shorter the heading is, the better it is for being readable on all devices (phones etc) which may view the page influence my reading of it.


As for filing the bug; do the best you can, but don't let yourself be turned off by the attempts by *developers *to get people to file the bug in its most useful format,  and 

 **Thank you** for spending the time trying to file a correct & useful bug report, and helping make Ubuntu better.
