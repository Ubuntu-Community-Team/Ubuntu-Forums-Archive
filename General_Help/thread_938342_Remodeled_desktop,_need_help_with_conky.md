### Post by cammin on 2008-10-05
First things first, thin out what you're displaying in Conky.
There are plenty of things you don't need, or are displaying redundantly.

Second, break stuff up into multiple instances of Conky so you can put them at different parts of the screen. You can also play with the layout of each instance.

Third, would be to update your color scheme so it fits in with eveything else. Change the graph colors to match your icons or something. Make the conky fonts and colors and stalonetray fonts and colors match, stuff like that.

### Post by Sam Lars on 2008-10-16
Definitely thin it down.  I spent a lot of time customizing mine, changing the order and layout of my different elements.
Also, it really helps to have a desktop that accounts for your conky.  For instance, mine is on the right side, so I try to find desktops that aren't too busy on the right to help conky fit in and stand out.
