### Post by mwettach on 2010-08-31
I love the automatic colour correction of F-Spot for pictures, it creates just the results I want and without effort on my part. I tried that on some .bmp images from a USB microscope and wanted to save them afterwards. F-Spot creates a file name with a funny ending (random characters) which is unreadable and closes itself after the first attempt to save a changed bmp image. 

I did get around this by converting the images to .png. After that, F-Spot could save the changes without any problems.

Running F-Spot with Lucid Lynx and just did an update of F-Spot today - no change in the behaviour mentioned.

Not a big issue, but I wanted to point it out.
