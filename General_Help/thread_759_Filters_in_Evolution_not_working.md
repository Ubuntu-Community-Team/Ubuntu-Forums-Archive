### Post by ubuntu-geek on 2004-10-15
If you open up Evolution goto Tools &gt; Settings &gt; Mail Accounts then click on your account and choose edit. Then goto Receiving Mail tab and towards the bottom make sure "Apply filters to new messages in INBOX on this server" then click ok to exit.

You can do that for each account. Hope that helps solve it for you.
