### Post by has64 on 2012-03-26
Hello, I am using ubuntu 11.10 64-bit with thinkpad x220. When I lock the screen and later want to enter the password to unlock, the Num Lock is always enabled. So I will get "4,5,6" instead of "U,I,O". I tried to press Fn+NmLK but that does not work. I tried shift+ScrLk which does not work either. However, the CapsLK works fine, I can turn it on and off. I just can not turn the Num Lk OFF.


By the way, the NumLk works fine normally, I just can not turn it off when prompt to enter the password when I try to unlock the screen. Any suggestions and comments? Thanks in advance!

---

### Post by has64 on 2012-03-28
Hello, I figured it out. Use an external keyboard to disable the number lock, then restart the laptop. Next time it will be default to disable number lock.!
