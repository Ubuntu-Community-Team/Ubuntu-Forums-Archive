### Post by ankspo71 on 2011-02-16
Hi,
The analog clock and digital clock plasmoids both support having multiple time zones... by having multiple clocks.

Fist add your clocks.
Right click on each clock.
Choose digital clock settings (or analog clock settings)
Click on Time Zones.
Choose a time zone to display. 
Where it says "Clock defaults to [local]", choose your preferred time zone from the dropdown menu.
Click OK.
Repeat the above for all clocks.

Note: each clock allows choosing multiple time zones but I haven't figured out how to toggle the time zones. So I think it is better if each clock displayed it's own time zone.
