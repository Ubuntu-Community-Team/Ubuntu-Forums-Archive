### Post by fragos on 2008-09-30
The bouncing cursor can be caused by the surface you're using the mouse on. Granite counter tops are good example of what not to use. Try placing a solid color surface under the mouse. Piece of white paper would be fine. Changing the Acceleration and Sensitivity in the mouse preferences might also help some.
