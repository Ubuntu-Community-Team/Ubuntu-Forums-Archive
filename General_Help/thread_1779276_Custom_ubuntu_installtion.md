### Post by ajgreeny on 2011-06-10
Have a look at both **remastersys** and **clonezilla**.

Remastersys should allow you to make a new live CD/DVD with all your custom packages in it, and clonezilla will let you make a backup/clone of your current hard disk or partitions which can also be burned to CD/DVD.

I hasten to add that I have never used either of these, but others have with some success.
