### Post by CatKiller on 2009-08-14
Two points for reference.

The gweather applet used to work for the forecast. It got the forecast from the Met Office, and it worked really well. Then the Met Office changed how they provided the information, and it's not worked since. The developers are now trying to find a data source that will work for everyone, and in the meantime we get no forecasts.

Britain has more weather than anywhere else. It could be doing anything from one hour to the next. That's why it's always such a fascinating subject of conversation.
