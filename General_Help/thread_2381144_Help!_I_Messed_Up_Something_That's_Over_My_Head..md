### Post by speartip on 2017-12-27
You shouldn't need to go into the bios to boot from a live DVD. Depending on your computer make & model, you need to press a button at the beginning of the boot sequence, (with your DVD in the tray, obviously) , to list your boot options, one of them should be to boot from DVD. On my HP Desktop it's the esc button, on some it's F10 or F12. Googling is your best bet.

### Post by leunam12 on 2017-12-27
It seems like your computer is setup for silent boot, which means that it is not giving you any POST messages. 
You have to be really fast pressing whatever key you need to press to go into the temporary boot menu. 
Unplug the power source and the battery (if it's a laptop) and press the power button and hold it for 45 seconds, 
then plug it back in and press the power button and start pressing the temporary boot menu key repeatedly until it gives you the temporary boot menu.
