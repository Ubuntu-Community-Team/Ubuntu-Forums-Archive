### Post by cdtech on 2008-08-30
> Several months ago i had the problem with too big login screen, and then i heard a solution that you can put the resolution you want in the login screen in Modes in xorg.conf.
The login screen is the "Virtual 1280	960" setting and the desktop default screen size is the first one listed under "Modes '1280x960@60'". You can change these settings to whatever you prefer individually.
