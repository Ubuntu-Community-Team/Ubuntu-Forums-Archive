### Post by starkadder on 2008-11-07
It took some noodling around to figure out how to change the keyring password in 8.10 (Intrepid) -- its not straight forward.  It should be easier than this.

If you want to change your keyring password do the following:

Open a terminal
run "seahorse" from the command line 
  - under "Edit" select "Preferences"
  - click on "login" in the "Password Keyrings" box
  - click on "Change Unlock Passoword"
  - change your password and save...
