### Post by Shazaam on 2008-05-11
If Parallels is similar to VMware and others there is currently slim to no chance of getting any effects working in a virtual machine. The reason for this is that currently they use virtual instead of physical hardware. So no, your virtual Ubuntu won't recognise your "real" video card but will use whatever is coded into the Parallels software (probably vesa).
