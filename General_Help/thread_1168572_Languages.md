### Post by Sef on 2009-05-24
For setting up a language:

System > Administration > Languages

Also choosing a language:

System > Preferences > SCIM Input Setup Method

SCIM sets up a button on the top bar that allows me to switch languages.

For setting up the keyboard:

right click on the top bar > Add to Panel > Keyboard Indicator (drag to the bar.)
