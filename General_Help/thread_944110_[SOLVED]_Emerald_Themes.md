### Post by loomsen on 2008-10-11
Oh theres nothing special to be guided to. Just make sure they are of appropriate height, thats it. Sth between 15 and max 20 pixels should be a reasonable height. If you want them to glow, you may achieve it by slightly changing color, or contrast or sth.
The only thing you have to bear in mind is leaving at least one pixel free between them, and to make the background transparent(or completely fill it)

First three buttons of each row specify how the buttons on the focused window shall look like, 2nd three - well, u might figure out ^^ Really nothing to care about, just give it a go.
