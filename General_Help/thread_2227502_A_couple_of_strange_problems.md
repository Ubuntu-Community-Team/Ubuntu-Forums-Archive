### Post by dragonfly41 on 2014-06-02
Some ideas to localise the problem ...

System settings > Mouse and Touchpad

Set General Settings > switch from right to left handed

Now does the problem move to left click?

If "no" .. points to faulty mouse.

Also try your settings by clicking on the smiley icon

and try touchpad with mouse unplugged.
