### Post by SeijiSensei on 2011-05-01
As they say, "it's not a bug, it's a feature."

Silverlight contains proprietary digital rights management software that Moonlight cannot reproduce.  Most media companies using Silverlight are using it for the DRM controls it provides.  I doubt you'll ever be able to use Moonlight to watch these streams.
