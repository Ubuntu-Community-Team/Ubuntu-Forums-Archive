### Post by tuxxy on 2008-08-26
This is a GTK theme I beleive not Emerald, download the Glossy P.tar.gz to your Desktop.

Now right click on your desktop and navigate to **change desktop background > themes**, once the theme page is displayed you should be able to directly drag the tar.gz file into the theme window to install.
