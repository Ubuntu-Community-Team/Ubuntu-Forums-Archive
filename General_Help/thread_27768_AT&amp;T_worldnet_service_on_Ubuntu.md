I've yet to hear of an ISP that had software for Linux. Most (but not all) of them have no problem with you using Linux but you are strictkly on your own as far as support or software goes.

Assuming you're in GNOME, you'd need to go to the "System--->Administration-->Networking" menu and select "Dial Up Modem" and that is where you can plug all the necessary info into to connect with worldnet (of course you'll need to get this info from them - they may have it on their web site somwhere - I know Earthlink does).

Good Luck!
