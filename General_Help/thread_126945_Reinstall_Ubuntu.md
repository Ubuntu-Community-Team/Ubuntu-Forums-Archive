### Post by Protex on 2006-02-07
When you turn on your PC, do you see something that says "Press ESC to enter Setup"?

Well, whatever button it is, ESC, F1, F10, etc, press it to enter your BIOS (Basic Input/Output System). Then you want to look for the option that says "Boot Order" or something similar. Your hard disk will be first, you want to change that temporarily to your CD-Rom drive.

That will allow the Ubuntu CD-Rom to start up and boot into it's installation wizard.
