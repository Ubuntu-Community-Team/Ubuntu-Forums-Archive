### Post by nitstorm on 2010-04-24
Did you get the black border after you played around with compiz maybe???if so please try unchecking windows borders option in System->Preferences->CompizConfig Settings Manager , otherwise right-click on your desktop and choose change background, under the visual effects tab choose none.

Hope this helps, cheers!
