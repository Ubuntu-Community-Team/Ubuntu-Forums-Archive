### Post by nerdtron on 2014-10-25
Any viable reasons why you like touch screens?

There are lots of viable laptops out there. If I'm on a budget and not on much into new hardware, I'll get any laptop originally with windows 7 and doesn't have a dedicated nvidia or ati radeon chip.

Reasons: 
Older laptops (in my experience) almost everything works out of the box, video, HDMI, sound, function keys. No hassle on my end.
No nvidia or radeon chips - i won't have to deal with any proprietary drivers, sometimes even open source drivers fail, as long as the laptop can play HD videos and play some games (even low res 3D games), and do my regular internet and office stuffs then I'm good with it.
Less money to buy, less hassle to setup. You mileage may vary.
