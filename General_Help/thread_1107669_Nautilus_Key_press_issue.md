### Post by ryanhaigh on 2009-03-27
There isn't any way to change to the EXACT behaviour of explorer however you can simply press up and down to move the selection to the next file that matches the text in the dialogue that appears at the bottom of the screen. I actually find this to be superior to the explorer method as it allows more comprehensive filtering.

A little off topic but another great feature is that you can press CTRL+S and enter a term such as *filepattern* to select all files that have filepattern as a part of the filename.
