### Post by misterbeetz on 2008-02-18
> **PsychedelicWonders said:**
> When you "jailbreak" an iPhone, does it strip the software persay when they "Crack" them for other carriers other than ATT?

ATT is really expensive as far as their service is concerned but I didnt want to take the chance of not having full access to all of the features by using a cracked version on another carrier.

Jailbreaking gives a person admin installing privileges to the iphone.  With those privileges you can install third party apps. (like openssh to sync with linux/ubuntu).  When you jailbreak the phone, no orignal functionality is lost as far as I know.  However you must be aware that apple tries to fight jailbreaking with their firmware updates.  If you accidentally update the firmware through itunes, there is a good chance that you will temporarily disable your iphone.  However pretty much every mistake ive seen is recoverable by just restoring the iphone firmware through itunes. 

If you want to unlock your iphone so that the internal cell radio will talk to towers of another carrier, then that is usually facilitated by a dead simple third party app called anysim.  Having your phone changed in this way also tends to conflict with future iphone firmware updates.  Again, conflicts caused by accidental firmware updating can be remedied with a restore through itunes.

So:

 if you want to use the iphone in linux, the phone will need to stay jailbroken.

if you are interested in having the phone unlocked, but don;t really need it to keep it jailbroken, then have a gander at this:  [http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/TurboSIM](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/TurboSIM)

Unfortunately there is a constantly changing tide at play here and therefore my knowledge might already be outdated.  The new apple firmware updates keep changing the playing field.  If you are afraid, or don't like taking risks, then this isn't for you.  If you want to be absolutely sure about what you are doing, then you have quite a bit of reading to do:

 [http://hackintosh.org/](http://hackintosh.org/)

If you want day by day updates on the iphone hacking world then keep an eye on this site:

[http://www.tuaw.com/category/iphone/](http://www.tuaw.com/category/iphone/)

Have fun and good luck.
