### Post by Forlong on 2008-06-29
> **misterhead said:**
> [http://www.gnome-look.org/content/show.php/arpbook+dock+icons+01?content=76139](http://www.gnome-look.org/content/show.php/arpbook+dock+icons+01?content=76139)
Those are dock icons. That means it's not a complete set, only for certain applications.
You can put them wherever you want and then use them for your dock (AWN, Cairo-Dock etc.)

If you don't have a dock, just rightclick the icon you want to change (e.g. on the desktop or in the panel), choose properties and then click on the icon in the menu. There you will be able to set the icon to something else.
