### Post by tommygunner on 2008-09-27
I created a new menu item for the Applications pulldown menu called "File Sharing." The icon shows up as a folder. I'm wondering how I can change this icon.

---

### Post by ad_267 on 2008-09-27
You can right click on the menu and select "Edit Menus" then click on Applications. Right click on your File Sharing item and select properties then click on the icon to change it.
