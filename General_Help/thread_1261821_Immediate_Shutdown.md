### Post by Frihet on 2009-09-09
I want to shut my machine down by pressing the power button. I do not want a confirmation screen.

Is there an easy way to do this?

Thanks!!

---

### Post by rob-ward on 2009-09-09
System -> Prefrences -> Power Management -> General

Change the option "When the power button is pressed to Shutdown instead of Ask Me

---

### Post by imhotep59 on 2009-09-09
Go to: system->preferences->energy (manager)->general and choose what you want to do when pressing the power button (i.e shutdown the computer instead of ask me what to do).
(These options could be a little bit different on your computer because mine is in french)

edit: 2 minutes after rob-ward !
