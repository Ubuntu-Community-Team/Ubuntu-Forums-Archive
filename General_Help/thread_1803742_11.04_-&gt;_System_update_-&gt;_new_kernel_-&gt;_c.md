### Post by spareme on 2011-07-15
I am hoping you have a Nvidia Graphic Card. Even if you dont, steps are pretty much the same, just download the relevant driver for your graphic card.


Follow these steps:


1. Log-in with your old kernel

2. Open terminal and enter sudo update-initramfs -u -k 2.6.38-10-generic

3. Download the driver for your graphic card from nvidia.com and save it on desktop.

4. Rename it to nvidia.run and change permission to execute file as program

5. Reboot and select the new kernel at grub menu

6. Press alt+F2 to take you to command line log-in

7. Enter your username and password to log-in

8. Enter sudo /etc/init.d/gdm stop

9. Enter cd Desktop

10. Enter sudo ./nvidia.run (Yes to all options).

11. Enter sudo /etc/init.d/gdm start

12. Reboot


Tell us if it worked.
