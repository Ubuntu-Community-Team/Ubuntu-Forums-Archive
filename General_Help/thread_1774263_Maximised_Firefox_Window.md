### Post by Frantic_Earthling on 2011-06-03
I am running 11.04 with Unity (and I am a happy man for it).

When I start Firefox, it always opens in a "maximised window" state, which I do not want. I subsequently have to click on "minimise" so that it does not fill the whole screen.

It is the only application that behaves in such a way. All others open in a "minimised" state.

I tried to find a solution in gconf-editor, to no avail.

Has anyone an idea about how to solve this little problem?

---

### Post by HereInOz on 2011-06-03
The only way I can find around this is to un-maximise Firefox then reduce the size of the window from the right, to less than around 4/5 of the screen.  You will find that as you reduce the window size, you will get to a point where the window will open un-maximised the next time.

If you stretch the window out until it almost fills the screen, though, it will open up maximised.

At least this is how it works for me.

I am not sure if this is by design or by bug, but it sure has raised some hackles apparently.
