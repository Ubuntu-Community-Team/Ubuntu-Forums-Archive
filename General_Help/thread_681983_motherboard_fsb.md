### Post by edward4130 on 2008-01-29
From the math, if you have all slots of 800+ dual channel ram installed yes the FSB higher than 800 would be used, and even up to 1600.

My suggestion is just go for the most stable board.  I stick with intel and asus, they have been solid for me (if they have what you want at the right price).
