### Post by Bucky Ball on 2016-01-08
You could use [testdisk]("http://www.cgsecurity.org/wiki/TestDis") for partition recovery or [photorec]("http://www.cgsecurity.org/wiki/PhotoRec") for files. Go the testdisk route first.

If you download the ISO and create [SystemRescueCD]("http://www.sysresccd.org/SystemRescueCd_Homepage") boot media, that includes both testdisk and photorec and a lot of other useful things. Boots to an xfce4 (I think) desktop.
