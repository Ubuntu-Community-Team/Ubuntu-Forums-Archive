### Post by 3rdalbum on 2009-11-24
Just let your BIOS control the fan speed, or tell it not to control the fan speed at all (which will run it at 100%). If you want to push the CPU harder, you should DEFINITELY invest in a third-party CPU cooler. Look at Noctua's range of CPU coolers, they have good performance and are very quiet. (I have one in my system and I leave it running at 100% fan speed, it's still almost silent).
