### Post by JeevesBond on 2008-01-08
gFTP hasn't got a great reputation for stability. Personally I find either using Konqueror (if you're on Kubuntu) or [FireFTP](http://fireftp.mozdev.org/) (a Firefox plugin) works best for FTP.

You can also connect to FTP from the desktop and access it as a folder. On the Ubuntu desktop, go to Places -> Connect to server. :)
