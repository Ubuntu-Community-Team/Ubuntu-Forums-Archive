### Post by nzadLithium on 2007-07-28
I have a nvidia 7600. I play pc games on it like bf1942 call of duty etc. That should work fine. I duno how much that costs in pounds though it cost me about $200NZ. 

I dont think you should be basing your decision on a graphics card on ubuntu desktop effects though. You should be basing it on what you want to do with your computer. If you only want to do word processing then onboard gfx are fine. If you want to play graphics intensive 3d games or watch movies etc then i would recommend a graphics card like mine.

Also make sure whatever card you get will fit in your motherboard. I bought a pci express gfx card before for my old motherboard then discovered my old motherboard only had and agp slot.:oops:
