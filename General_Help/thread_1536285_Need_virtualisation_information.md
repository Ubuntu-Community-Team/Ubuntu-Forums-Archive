### Post by bigboy_pdb on 2010-07-22
I'm assuming that this is for an assignment, but you might want to ask if you can use information from company websites. There are three main companies who have products that you might want to consider.

[Oracle - VirtualBox]("http://www.virtualbox.org/")
[VMWare - VMWare Server and other products]("http://www.vmware.com/")
[Microsoft - Hyper-V]("http://www.microsoft.com/virtualization/")

The reason I'm suggesting this is because it may be difficult to find printed information on the subject. Large companies, such as Microsoft and Oracle, are going to be somewhat cautious about what they print in any media form in order to avoid negative press.
