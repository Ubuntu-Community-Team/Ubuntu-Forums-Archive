### Post by grahammechanical on 2012-03-08
I think that the drawing of a man in a circle is taken from a sketch made by some famous Italian artist who drew a man in circle to show that the human body had certain proportions.

The image here is being used to represent accessibility or access options for those with seeing, hearing and typing difficulties. There are some settings under this symbol in System Settings.

Regards.
