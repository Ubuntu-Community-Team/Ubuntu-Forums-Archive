Acer Aspire 5749 i-3 laptop:

After a period of some 5 minutes of inactivity, 11.04 goes into sleep/suspend/hibernation/suspend (I know not which), and requires a mouse click and password to get started again. I assume this is a keyring setting problem? How to change this way too short default time?

---

### Post by mikewhatever on 2011-12-16
It is more likely the screensaver, which locks the screen after five minutes of inactivity. You can disable this behavior under screensaver settings.
