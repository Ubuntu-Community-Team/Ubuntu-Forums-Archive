### Post by Lucrin on 2011-02-06
I am loving Ubuntu on my laptop but I really hate how every time I leave my computer for a couple minutes and it goes to the screen saver, or shuts the monitor off when I go to wake it up it requires my password again. Is there a way to shut that off?

---

### Post by korleon on 2011-02-06
Yes you can go to the main menu > Preferences > Screen Saver.  At the bottom of the Screensaver window you will see a check box for "Lock Screen when Screen Saver is active" uncheck that and your set.

---

### Post by andymorton on 2011-02-06
Go to System>Prefences>creensaver. Uncheck the box which says 'Activate screensaver when computer is idle'. You might also want to click on the power management button and change the 'sleep' settings.
