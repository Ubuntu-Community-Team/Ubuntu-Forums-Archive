### Post by spcwingo on 2011-08-16
You should be able to do this through synaptic on your desktop.  Just fire up synaptic and at the top press File>Generate package download script.  Follow the instructions, move the script to a flash drive, then the flash drive to your laptop, run the script, then pop the flash drive back into your desktop and install away.  :)
