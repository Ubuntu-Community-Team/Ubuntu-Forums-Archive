### Post by jakeyw on 2008-11-18
This exact problem drove me mad!!! Turned out it was a little crumb sitting on the motherboard, short circuiting two of the pins that go into the keyboard ribbon. Suggest taking a look for dirt nearby where the ribbon joins the motherboard. If not, might be worth trying a replacement keyboard for your laptop... you can get them on ebay.
