### Post by Mark Phelps on 2010-10-10
I don't know for sure, but it's likely that the propietary drivers won't work with your ATI card anymore.  AMD (what used to be ATI) dropped support for a lot of their cards over a year ago but they were somewhat fuzzy on the X2xxx cards. Some of the X2xxx cards were retained, some were not.

If you're not being offered a driver through Hardware Drives, then there is none available and forcing the installation of something you downloaded is likely to trash your display -- forcing you to drop into command mode, remove those drivers, and replace them with the ones you already have.
