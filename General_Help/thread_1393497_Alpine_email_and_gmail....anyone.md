### Post by inkhorn on 2010-02-04
Hi,

It depends on whether or not you have set up your gmail account in your folder collections settings in Alpine.  In the main menu screen of Alpine, type **L **for your folder list.  If you have everything set-up like I do, you'll see a screen like I show below:

[IMG]http://lh3.ggpht.com/_SLATbuFg4qM/S2r2E2ShupI/AAAAAAAAAKg/ZFWwRNtAWKA/screenshot_037.png[/IMG]

Highlight "Select here to See Expanded List", press Enter, and then you should see all of your folders.  I use Gmail to access 2 email accounts, and so I see the email addresses of those accounts as folder names in the expanded list.

If that doesn't work, you can check out a [blog entry that I posted]("http://nixliving.blogspot.com/2010/02/alpine-gmail-and-ssh-how-to-make-your.html") on setting up Alpine to work with Gmail and be accessed remotely through SSH.

Cheers,
Inkhorn
