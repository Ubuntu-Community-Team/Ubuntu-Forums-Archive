### Post by wildmanne39 on 2012-11-27
Hi, the information from nm-tool is missing do you have nm-tools package installed?

Please do:
```
echo "options rt2800pci  nohwcrypt=1" | sudo tee /etc/modprobe.d/rt2800pci.conf
sudo modprobe -rfv rt2800pci
sudo modprobe -v rt2800pci
```
Watch for errors.

Also your router is on channel 6, 1 or 11 is usually better.

Also for your wireless to take over you will need to disable your wired connection.
Thanks

Set your router encryption to just wpa2 only if you have that option.

Set your wireless setting to match the screenshots.
