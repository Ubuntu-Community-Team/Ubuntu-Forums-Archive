### Post by kostkon on 2010-12-29
I would like to recommend you *gm-notify*. It uses IMAP push so you will get notified when an email arrives almost instantly. Here is [some info about it]("http://www.omgubuntu.co.uk/2010/05/gmail-notifier-puts-gmail-in-your-messaging-menu/") (Gmail Notifier #1), and its launchpad project page is [here]("http://launchpad.net/gm-notify").
