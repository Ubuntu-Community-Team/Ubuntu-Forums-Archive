### Post by kailkitsune on 2011-04-07
you have two clocks on your panel, right click the far right clock and click "Remove from panel" if you need both clock i suggest adding the shutoff button to the bottom panel, right click the bottom panel then click "add to panel" and look for "shut down" and click add. or move the second clock to the bottom panel, to do so remove the clock from the top panel then right click the bottom then click "add to panel" and look for "clock" then add it.

hope this helps.
