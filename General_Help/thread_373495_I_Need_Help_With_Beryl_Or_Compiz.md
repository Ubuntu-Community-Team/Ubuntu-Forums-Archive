for beryl
=========

from [http://ubuntu.beryl-project.org/dists/edgy/main/](http://ubuntu.beryl-project.org/dists/edgy/main/)

beryl
beryl-core
beryl-manage
beryl-plugins
beryl-plugins-data
beryl-settings
beryl-settings-bindings
emerald
libberyldecoration0
libberylsettings0
libemeralengine0

it may be worth playing it safe and trying to download ALL the relevant architecture .debs on that link to cd... & still u might need a few other packages i have overlooked..
