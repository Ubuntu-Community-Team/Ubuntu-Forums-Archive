### Post by Chronon on 2009-06-02
Create a live CD by burning a .iso image to CD.  A LiveCD doesn't change anything on your disks.  It won't even mount them unless you choose to.  In fact, I ran a PC with no HD for several months using a LiveCD.  It worked great until I could afford a replacement drive.
