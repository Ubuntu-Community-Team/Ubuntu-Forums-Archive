### Post by N8K99 on 2008-01-04
Look under Office and start Kontact. Kmail will be a component within the PIM. Or you can press Alt + F2 and type 'kmail' which will start kmail.

If you want to make certain that Kmail is on the Kmenu you can use Kcontrol to edit the Kmenu and restore kmail to the Kmenu
