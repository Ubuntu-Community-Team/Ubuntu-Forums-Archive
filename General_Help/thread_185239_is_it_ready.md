### Post by SSTwinrova on 2006-05-31
[QUOTE=carlosqueso]It'll be out at midnight GMT, which is (I think) 6 EDT tonight.[/QUOTE]
I'm not sure about DST outside of the US (but I'll proceed assuming that GMT is **not** affected), but E**S**T is typically GMT-5. Under DST, it becomes E**D**T which would be GMT-4 (DST adjusts times by +1). Therefore, GMT 0:00 on Thursday would be EDT 20:00 (8:00 PM) on Wednesday.

There's your (hopefully correct) time zone lesson for the day  :)
