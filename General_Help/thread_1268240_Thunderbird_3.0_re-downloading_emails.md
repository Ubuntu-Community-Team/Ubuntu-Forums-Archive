### Post by revlarry on 2009-09-16
I had the same problem with one gmail account. It had stored up every message I had received over the last 20-plus months. Once I figured out what was going on, I just deleted all the "new" email from that account -- as I went. Never did find a way to stop the process, but after a couple of hours of downloads, it was over ... and no problems since.

If I had thought of it first, I would have logged into the account via Firefox first, and tried to dump everything from there before downloading.

Just a thought. Good luck!
