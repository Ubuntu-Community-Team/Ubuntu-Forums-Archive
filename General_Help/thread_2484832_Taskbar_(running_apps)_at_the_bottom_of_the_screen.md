### Post by Claus7 on 2023-03-12
Hello,

this seems to me to be window list extension enabled under gnome shell. Press the super key twice and open the applications grid. Find the extension application (puzzle piece), open it and from there disable the window list extension. It should get removed from the bottom of your screen.

Regards!
