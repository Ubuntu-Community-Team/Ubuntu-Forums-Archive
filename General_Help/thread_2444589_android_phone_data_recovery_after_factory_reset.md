### Post by dragonfly41 on 2020-06-01
Well I have a family Android but I don't use it often. My thoughts would be to look to photorec to recover photos (same stable as testdisk) but the same principles apply as on PC - don't try to install recovery apps on Android or you will overwrite the very files you are trying to recover. In fact lock up the Android until you research the next step. I would post a question to the testdisk/photorec forum for a start which specialises in recovery, I don't know how to access Android memory from a PC but I would research that rather than running on Android. Think of Android as an external hard drive which has failed and you need to extract files from your desktop PC with Android as a device.
