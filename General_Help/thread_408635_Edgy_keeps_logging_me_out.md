### Post by Smuve on 2007-04-17
Several people have had this problem since a recent xorg update, including me.  Just reinstall your video driver and it should be fine.

@ ielliot:  It seems to have something to do with GL.  If your screensaver is set to random, it will only log you off if it selects a GL screensaver.
