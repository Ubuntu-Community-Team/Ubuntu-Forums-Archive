### Post by pivanof on 2011-11-12
Let's say I have a full-screen browser with my gmail account open. Then I decide to reboot the machine. Everything works fine but during boot after the Ubuntu splash screen and before the login screen I can see a screenshot of my gmail account for several seconds. It's long enough for somebody to read something personal or confidential and thus it's driving me nuts.
This is not an issue with some particular programs running. Basically anything that I had on the screen before I rebooted is visible after reboot before the login, i.e. to anybody. The problem doesn't happen if I select to shutdown and then boot again right away.
I have now Ubuntu 11.10 and I don't remember having such problem in Ubuntu 11.04. Does anybody else experience this? Is this some known bug with tracking on launchpad?
