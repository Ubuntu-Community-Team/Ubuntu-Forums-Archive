### Post by pointone on 2007-12-06
With ATI video cards newer than the 9200, you need to either [update fglrx]("http://wiki.cchtml.com/index.php/Ubuntu_Gutsy_Installation_Guide") to version 8.42.3 or greater (hard), or [install Xgl]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/CompositeManager/Xgl") (easy).
