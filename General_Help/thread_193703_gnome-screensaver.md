### Post by _simon_ on 2006-06-10
I never use screensavers and have it disabled in Dapper.

I notice in System Monitor it lists "gnome-screensaver"

Is it safe to prevent this loading at all and if so, how do I actually do that?

---

### Post by Anduu on 2006-06-10
Open gconf-editor and go to apps>gnome-settings-daemon>screensaver and uncheck the start screensaver box.
