I passed over Edgy because Dapper worked great and one installation per year is enough. If you choose Feisty, I would recommend you to mirror your current hard disk and make a fresh installation. If you don't like it, copy the mirrored OS back to your laptop hard disk.

Just in case don't forget to burn a Linux LiveCD!

### Post by ubu-for on 2007-04-25
You prefer asking instead of searching, right? ;)

Forum: [Restoring Windows from a disk image ](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?p=2459979)

Windows: [Drive SnapShot](http://www.drivesnapshot.de/en/index.htm)

Linux: [Partimage](http://www.partimage.org/Main_Page)
Linux: [g4u](http://fbim.fh-regensburg.de/~feyrer/g4u/)
Linux: [dd](http://wiki.linuxquestions.org/wiki/Dd)

dd example:

```
dd if=/dev/hdb of=/your_usb_drive_for_example/hdb-Image.img
```
