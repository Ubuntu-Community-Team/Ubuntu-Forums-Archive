### Post by Aero Leviathan on 2009-03-01
I want to get rid of a third-party repository. Before I do so, I need to uninstall all packages from that repository. How can I find out the names of those packages?

---

### Post by Kevbert on 2009-03-01
I believe you can see installed packages for most repos in Synaptic. Check that the Origin button is clicked and then select the repo in the top left-hand window. Installed packages will be marked with a green box.
