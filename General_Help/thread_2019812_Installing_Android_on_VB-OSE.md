### Post by Paddy Landau on 2012-07-08
A VB Ubuntu partition? Do you mean in a separate partition on your hard drive or do you mean in Virtual Box?

You can do either (or both if you want). Here are [some easy instructions]("http://www.howtogeek.com/howto/22665/run-android-on-your-netbook-or-desktop/"). To run in a VM, don't follow the instructions for UNetbootin, but instead directly mount the ISO as a CD in your VM.
