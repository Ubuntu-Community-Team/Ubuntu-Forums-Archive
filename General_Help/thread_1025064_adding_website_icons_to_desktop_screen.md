### Post by redneckProgrammer on 2008-12-30
Try this:

1) Using Firefox (may work with other browsers as well), bookmark the site you want.

2) Adjust the Firefox window size so it doesn't take the whole screen (so you can also see the desktop)

3) Click the Bookmarks menu and hover over the bookmark you want.  Click and drag the bookmark to your desktop.  It should copy it there for you.
