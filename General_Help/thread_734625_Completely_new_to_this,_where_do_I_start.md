### Post by alexforcefive on 2008-03-24
There is actually a "new to linux" guide in Ubuntu. Click on System, then Help and Support. There's a topic called "New To Ubuntu?" as well as others.

Generally everything works straight out of the box, and most of the programs you will need are already installed. All you need to do is set up your accounts in Evolution (e-mail) and Pidgin (instant messaging)

My advice is to log in and start using it :D If you run into any problems, then come back and search the forums

PS, you must be patient on the forums. They are not for live help! People take time to read and reply to messages
