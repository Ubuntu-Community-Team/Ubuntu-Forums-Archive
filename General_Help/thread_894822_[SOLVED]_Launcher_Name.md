### Post by Genius314 on 2008-08-19
Cairo-Dock or Avant-Window-Navigator. Cairo-Dock has more options, but AWN is easier to use (and is a little nicer looking).
Try them both, they both have their ups and downs.

(I've used RocketDock on windows, and it is really nice. None of the docks for Linux are that nice yet, but they'll get there.)
