Well, the GNU/Linux bootloader, which is indeed called GRUB (with one B.), runs from the MBR of a hard drive. If there is no hard drive in the computer, I can't think it would be the same GRUB - especially if it has two Bs.

Have you tried taking the BIOS battery out and leaving it for a few minutes, as this may reset whatever is up. Otherwise, it does sound like a BIOS issue... Weird.
