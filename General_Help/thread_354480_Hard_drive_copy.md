### Post by tribaal on 2007-02-06
Hum unfortunately the short answer (without going into hard drive forensics), is no.

Formatting a hard drive, well... formats it. :( I know there are some companies that will restore some of your lost files for a price, maybe it's an affordable solution? Try to find one in your area.

I cannot stress enough to double or even triple check your drives names when it comes to reformatting - and to back up your data.
Actually when it comes to reformatting, I usually physically remove my /home hard drive (the one I store my media etc... on).

Sorry to hear about your problem. Hope you didn't have the only copy of your kid's first steps video or something lying on that drive... :(
