### Post by CatKiller on 2009-07-15
> **Jerriy said:**
>  Does this perhaps have anything to do with shortcuts?

Maybe. If you've got the Delete key on its own as a shortcut (either in Compiz or in Keyboard Shortcuts) it probably won't be available for other uses.

Is it everywhere that the key doesn't work, or just in the Terminal? Gnome-Terminal does have an option to mess with the Delete key.

Other than that, there is the option to map the Delete key to something else, using something like xmodmap. But I'm afraid I don't know much about that, so I couldn't tell you how it might have been invoked.
