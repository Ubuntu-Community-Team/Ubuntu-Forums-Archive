### Post by fluffman86 on 2009-11-13
Because "system requirements" are a really poor judge of what you can and can't do.

I have an old 500Mhz p2 with a 10GB hard drive and 256 megs of ram.  I ripped the HDD out and added my partitions on a faster computer, then installed standard Ubuntu on that old computer.  It runs great for me, GUI and all.

And yet some people complain about Ubuntu being slow on a 1.6GHz p4 with 512 megs of RAM.

Maybe I'm lucky.  Maybe I have a better tolerance for a jerky UI or something.  Either way, different people have different experiences.
