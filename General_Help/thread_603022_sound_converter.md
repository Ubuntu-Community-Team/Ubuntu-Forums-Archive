### Post by bakeneko on 2007-11-04
To encode to mp3 using soundconverter, you'll need the package **gstreamer0.10-plugins-ugly-multiverse** from the multiverse repository.  After installing this package, open soundrecorder and go to edit>preferences. Change "type of result" to mp3.
