### Post by joshsmith on 2008-05-01
are you saying you dont want to get the packages from the internet. 

even if synaptic asks, it doesnt mean it has to get the packages from the internet, disable cd-rom entry in system>administration>software sources, and you can get the packages from the net

otherwise, perhaps mounting it into the folder /media/cdrom (or cdrom0) would trick ubuntu sufficiently
