### Post by Locke on 2006-04-19
[QUOTE=njzillest]what does creating an .ISO enable you to do?? why would you create one? 


-THanks[/QUOTE]

Creating an ISO is pretty much a snapshot picture of the CD, so you can burn it to 5 different CDs and give a copy to each of your friends or just make a backup. It's better than just using the Copy CD method in most CD Burning programs because it lets you have a hard copy to burn again later.
