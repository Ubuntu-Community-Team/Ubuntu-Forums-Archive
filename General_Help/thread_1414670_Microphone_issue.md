### Post by fragos on 2010-02-24
Right click the sound/speaker applet icon and select Preferences, Input tab. I had two device options for sound input that were described the same. I ended up with the 2nd one. In either case there's a Connection option to select. The one that worked for me was "Analog Microphone / Input 1 / Microphone1". Your option may be different reflecting your hardware. There's an Input Level meter on that display that will indicate if the mike is picking anything up. Experiment with the various options until you find one that works for you.
