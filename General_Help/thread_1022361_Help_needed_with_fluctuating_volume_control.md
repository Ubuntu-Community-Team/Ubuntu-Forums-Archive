### Post by aliasgar14 on 2009-02-01
I have found a solution but I am not sure if it is a permanent fix or not. Apparently the HP dv5 laptops used metal under the volume controls instead of plastic so static builds up over there and that causes the problem. You should swipe your finger across the touch buttons, make sure you go through all of them a couple of times and that fixes the problem.
