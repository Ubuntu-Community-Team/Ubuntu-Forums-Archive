### Post by MARP1961 on 2011-08-17
This left side launcher (Unity Launcher) cannot at present be moved to a different position. Ubuntu have now discontinued the separate netbook and desktop versions. In Ubuntu 11.04 (the current version) there is just the one (similar to your 10.10 netbook version).

It is probably best for you to upgrade to Ubuntu 11.04 (Natty Narwhal). You can then install 'Advanced Desktop Effects Compiz(ccsm)' from the Software Centre. If you then turn on the 'Unity Plugin' you will find that you can get the launcher to hide or reveal in different ways. You can also change the size of the launcher icons.
