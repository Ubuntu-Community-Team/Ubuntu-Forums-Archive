### Post by prshah on 2011-09-07
> **Avistew said:**
> on windows it was set up to automatically accept calls and start his video.

Check the settings in the Skype menu (Blue "S" button at bottom left of main Skype window). Click Skype-Options-Video Devices, and ensure:

* Enable Skype Video is selected/ticked. 
* "Start my video automatically..." is selected/ticked as per your choice
* "Automatically receive video from..."  is "Anybody" or "only people I have allowed" (And allow your fiancee)
* "Show that I have video to..." to "only people I have allowed".

Also, under Options-Privacy, select/tick "Automatically answer incoming calls" and set "Allow calls from..." to "only people I have allowed."

If any of the options are missing, please check that you are using atleast Skype 2.1(beta).
