### Post by ankspo71 on 2010-05-19
Hi,
In Synaptic Package Manager, all you need to do is click on the little checkbox before the blackjack entry, or right click anywhere on the blackjack entry, then select "Mark For Installation". When you are done making your selections, click on the big apply button near the top.
Hope this helps.
