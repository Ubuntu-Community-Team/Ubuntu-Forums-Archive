### Post by GeneralZod on 2006-02-10
It's not very logical, and took me a while to figure out, but right-click on the Panel at the bottom and Add To Panel -> Panel -> Panel(!)  You can then drag it where you want it.

Here's another pertinent post that shows some of the things you can do with it:
