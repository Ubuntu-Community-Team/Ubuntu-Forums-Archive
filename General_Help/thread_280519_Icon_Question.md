### Post by CatKiller on 2006-10-19
Yes, it's possible. Since they aren't laid out for Gnome/KDE, you'll need to do everything manually. Use an existing icon theme as a guide. Also, I haven't checked, but if they're in .ico format or anything else that isn't a picture, you'll need to convert them to PNGs or similar.

Good luck.
