### Post by dabl on 2008-02-04
If you're referring to Skype, a lot of standard microphones won't work.  On my rig, the same sound system that does a great job recording through the mic or line-in wouldn't work with Skype when a "normal" headset was plugged in, although I could hear the other party.  I went to Radio Shack and paid $40 for a "Skype Certified" USB headset, and it works great.  Skype is picky, I guess.  :)
