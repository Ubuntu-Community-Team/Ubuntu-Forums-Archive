### Post by 3rdalbum on 2011-04-29
> **Kevin the Olden said:**
> Could somebody explain WHY the minimise, maximise and close buttons were moved to the left of the control bar in ubuntu 10.04? I know how to put them back where they belong, after MUCH searching. I'm just curious WHY somebody would move them.

Window buttons originally belonged on the left-hand side, on the original Macintosh. Microsoft changed them to the right-hand side. Ubuntu has changed them back where they belong, on the left.

More seriously though, it was because of a feature that never eventuated, called Windicators, which would appear on the right side of the window (to match with the system indicators on the right side of the top panel, see?). You might see windicators at some point in the future.

You should have just left them where they are, on the left side. When you upgrade to a later version of Ubuntu with Unity, when your window is maximized the window buttons appear in the left side of the top panel. Much less confusing to just keep them on the left; it'll only take you a couple of days to get used to it.
