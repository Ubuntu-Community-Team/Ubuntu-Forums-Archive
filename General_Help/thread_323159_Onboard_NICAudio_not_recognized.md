### Post by pjman on 2006-12-28
Mike at [General NanoSystems]("http://nanosys1.com/") helped me out. He confirmed that the NIC and Audio do not work out of the box. I guess it's kinda like a winmodem... The MSI K9N6SGM-V Mainboard has the same NIC so it doesn't work as well. I ended up upgrading to a MSI K9NBPM2-FID. Everything works fine now.
