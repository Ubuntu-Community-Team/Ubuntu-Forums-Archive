### Post by neonliitez on 2008-07-12
Hi, i don't know how relevant this is as your post is from last year. But i fell asleep drunk on my laptop the other day and i had exactly the same problem. It's an activex control called 'slow keys' and it's designed for disabled people of some sort. You can turn it off by doing this:

hold down the shift key for 8 seconds.

or:

go to the accessability options in kcontrol or system settings and fix it from there. 

Thanks anyway cuz i had no idea what went wrong as i was drunk and your post helped me to solve the problem.
