### Post by Mark Phelps on 2010-09-15
> **soldier1st said:**
> Hi should you always install the propietary drivers or stick to the default ones?

Depends critically on HOW you install the proprietary drivers ...

If you are offered them in Hardware Drivers, it is probably a safe thing to do and will provide you extra features not found in the open-source drivers.

If you manually download proprietry drivers and force an install, you can seriously hose up your display should you install drivers that are incompatible with your video card and/or the X-server.
