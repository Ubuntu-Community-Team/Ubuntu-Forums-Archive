### Post by akudewan on 2007-05-03
Avidemux is a good program that can be used to shrink avi's

Also, the "size" of a video-DVD does not depend on MB, but rather in *minutes*. You could use a program such as [DeVeDe]("http://www.rastersoft.com/programas/devede.html") to see whether your avi files are actually exceeding the minutes a DVD can hold.

For example, I am able to burn 800MB of data on a VCD using a  700MB CD.


PS: DeVeDe can also encode the DVD in a lower resolution. Look into that option if the videos don't fit.
