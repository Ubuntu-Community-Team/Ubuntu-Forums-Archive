### Post by sikander3786 on 2010-09-26
> 
Simply go to the settings button and choose Options from the menu, and in the Personal Stuff tab check &#8220;Use system title bar and borders&#8220; under Appearance. Above it you can also click the Use GTK+ theme button, and the rest of Google Chrome will be themed with the colours, textures and buttons of your current system theme.
 Quoted from here. [http://ubuntugenius.wordpress.com/2009/12/27/make-google-chrome-look-like-the-rest-of-ubuntu/](http://ubuntugenius.wordpress.com/2009/12/27/make-google-chrome-look-like-the-rest-of-ubuntu/)

In addition to appearance, it will also make chrome follow the native window behaviour.
