### Post by clinux on 2008-08-30
problem FIXED!!!
This is how to fix this:
1. Go to /home/tp/.qt and rename qtrc to something else so that Opera won't use KDE.
2. Then go to about:config and disable "Draw Anti Aliased Fonts".
3. Restart Opera.
