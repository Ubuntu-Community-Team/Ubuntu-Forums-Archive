### Post by PGScooter on 2009-01-07
Hi LoneWolfJack,

That is definitely annoying! The solution is to install mouse gesters: [https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/39](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/39)

The reason why it should fix your problem is because it brings up the context menu after you release the mouse button, whereas I think firefox natively tries to bring it up when you click. And if you experiment a bit, you should notice that if you hold the right-button down for awhile after clicking, you do not get the aberrant behavior (don't ask me why!). But its just so hard to remember to do a "hard" right-click everytime.

It's not a good fix, but its a pretty decent work around. The bug is actually quite common, but I have not seen any work towards a fix.

if that doesn't work, post back and we'll try something else.

good luck!
