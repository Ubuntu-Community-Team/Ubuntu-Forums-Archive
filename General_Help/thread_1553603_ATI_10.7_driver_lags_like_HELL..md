back to the OP's problem,..

I had severe screen tearing (moving windows) and watching movie files, etc..

For video.. i installed VLC and enabled the output option "OpenGl" - movies are nice now..

For the window tearing that was pissing me off as well after lots of searching and trying to disable detect refresh in compiz settings.. here's how i got it WAY better.. you can still notice it a bit if you move windows around on your screen like a crazy person.. but without doing that i cannot notice it at all anymore..

Here's what i did.

Disabled detect refresh rate, Disabled sync to Vblank.. and set the refresh slider up to 200.. Now everything looks great when i move it.. even with wobbly windows on.. but with that setting you can notice just a slight bit of tearing still.. but not moving a window normally around.

Hope this info can help someone.
