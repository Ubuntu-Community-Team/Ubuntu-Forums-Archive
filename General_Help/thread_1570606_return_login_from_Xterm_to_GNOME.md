### Post by sharathcshekhar on 2010-09-08
In your login screen, at the bottom right side, there will be an option to choose session : Gnome, failsafe Gnome and xterm. Choose xterm and boot it.
If you have chosen automatic login, just press Ctrl + D in the xterm terminal, It will exit and take you to the log in screen where you can log in to Gnome.
