### Post by lhtown on 2007-02-08
If you can locate the correct icon somewhere on your system, you can go to System>Preferences>Menu Layout and then on the left side of the screen that opens up, I guess you would have to click on "internet" and then on the right side right click on amsn and select "properties". From there, you should be able to click the icon button and point it to the correct icon.
