### Post by howefield on 2009-12-06
> **malacoda77 said:**
> i tried ripperx, but the only options that are offered are for mp3 and wav.  how can i use this to rip into flac?  is there anything that would work better?

Install the flac package with Synaptic, then open ripperx and hit the Config button, under the mp3 tab, change the Encoder plugin to Flac and rip.
