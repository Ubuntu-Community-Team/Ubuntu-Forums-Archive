### Post by SGAZ on 2008-06-18
I'm hoping somebody can clue me in on what Compiz-Fusion settings control the shading on the backside of Gnome-Panels and Open Windows.

I'm using the Hardy default Compiz-Fusion and Advanced Desktop Effects Settings.

Before upgrading to Hardy from Gutsy the backside of windows and panels visible on the other three Cube faces were unshaded/clearly visible and I'd like that set up again.

Thanks in advance for any assistance.

---

### Post by SGAZ on 2008-06-25
System --> Preferences --> Advanced Desktop Effects Settings--> General Options --> Display Settings --> Lighting (OFF/unchecked).

Disables the darkening effect on the inside of the cube

Most useful for monitoring/viewing auto-updating windows or movies on one of the other three faces of a transparent cube.
