### Post by Cajhne on 2013-01-12
There is one more thing I had to do to keep my mouse cursor from sticking on the way back from my second monitor:
I turned off the "launcher placement" on all monitors where my cursor must pass the left-hand side of the screen.

For example, my mouse was still sticking on the way back from my right monitor after turning off sticky edges until I set the "launcher placement" to just the first of my monitors.

Hope that helps someone.
