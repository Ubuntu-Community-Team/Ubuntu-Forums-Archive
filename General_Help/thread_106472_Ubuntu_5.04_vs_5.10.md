### Post by Mustard on 2005-12-20
Eeek..that's a pretty complex question. :)

For me it was that sound worked better on 5.10 and my tvtuner card worked as well.  On the downside my external USB drives have had more issues on Breezy than Hoary.

Breezy also has openoffice2.  
It has a built in Starter Guide in the help menu.
Its got a Disk Adminstration gui for mounting drives etc.
The location of the Terminal option in the menu has changed.
Its got an Add Applications menu option now, to install common applications.

There's probably more, but I can't recall them all atm.
