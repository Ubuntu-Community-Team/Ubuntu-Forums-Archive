### Post by ian-weisser on 2024-03-03
On a stock install of 22.04 and newer, the Ubuntu Software Center application ("ubuntu-software" deb) was replaced by the Ubuntu Software application ("snap-store" snap).

So maybe you didn't download anything at all. Or maybe you're using the older application. Or maybe you are using the newer and didn't notice the change.

Depends upon your release of Ubuntu. And whether it's a new install or a release-upgrade.

Also depends upon what problem you were trying to solve that led you to look for snap-store.
