### Post by crazyness003 on 2008-11-02
Sorry guy, cant help you there. What you could try to do is make the effect. Use some light effects, with blur and lens-flare or something, then multiply it at near random locations on the y-axis with random widths and lengths. Then take the person you wanna beam and alpha fade them away. make sure you have another scene of the same background without the person there. So when you fade the layer with the person, it looks like only they disappeared.

You might be bale to pull this off with any non-linear video editor.

Thats all i can tell you
