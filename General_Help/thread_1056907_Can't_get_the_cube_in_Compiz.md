### Post by issih on 2009-02-01
Assuming that in compiz config settings manager you have the "desktop cube" and "rotate desktop" plugins activated, the default key combination to get free rotation of the cube is to hit ctrl+alt+ hold left mouse button and then move the mouse about to move the cube.

In the end though, it will use whatever activation sequence you configure it to use.

Hope that helps

### Post by Uubnewb on 2009-02-01
To activate the cube go to System>Prefferences>CompizConfig Settings Management.  Then tick the checkbox next to Cube as well as Rotate Cube.

After this you should be able to rotate the cube by pressing middle-mouse button on an empty space on the desktop.  If you want to change any hotkeys and/or settings, simply click on the icon (ie Cube or Rotate Cube) to the right of the checkbox mentioned above.

Hope this helps.

### Post by kpatz on 2009-04-06
The cube doesn't have to be 4-sided either.

2 desktops gives you a flat plane that flips.

3 desktops gives you a triangular "cube" (3 rectangular sides + triangular top/bottom)

4 gives you the expected cube.

5 or more gives you a pentagon, hexagon, etc.

Have fun!
