### Post by gettinoriginal on 2009-01-26
Gtk themes cannot be used in emerald, and visa versa, they are two different window decorators.  Once you have downloaded a theme, go to:
home folder, hit Ctrl H to show hidden files, then copy/paste from where you downloaded the theme into the appropriate themes folder.
