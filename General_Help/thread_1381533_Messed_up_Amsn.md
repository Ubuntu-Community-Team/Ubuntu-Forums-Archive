### Post by howefield on 2010-01-15
First off, give your brother his own account. Let him mess up his own stuff ;)

And then, go to Places menu and click on Home Folder, then View > Show Hidden Files. Delete or rename the .amsn folder. 

This holds all the configuration data for amsn and won't get removed when you uninstall.

Now reinstall amsn. Hopefully that'll give you the application back to default.
