### Post by 3rdalbum on 2011-12-01
Not directly over the 3G network as a mobile call, but you can use VOIP software such as Skype to make a free "call" to other Skype users, or to "Skype Out" to normal telephone numbers (not free). VOIP software is a bit flaky over 3G due to latency and the general flakiness and coverage issues that 3G networks have. VOIP is best done over ADSL or Cable.
