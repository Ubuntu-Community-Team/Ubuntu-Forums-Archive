### Post by blueridgedog on 2010-04-25
Usually there is an iTunes folder under C:\windows\documents and settings\USER\My Documents\My Music.  That is for XP.  Vist is similar, but the path is different. 

Some general information is here:

[http://hifiblog.com/past/2006/05/11/howto-move-your-itunes-music-while-preserving-library-data-when-you-dont-let-itunes-manage-your-music-library/](http://hifiblog.com/past/2006/05/11/howto-move-your-itunes-music-while-preserving-library-data-when-you-dont-let-itunes-manage-your-music-library/)
