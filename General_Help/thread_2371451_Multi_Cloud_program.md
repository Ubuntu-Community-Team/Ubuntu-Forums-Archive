### Post by TheFu on 2017-09-15
Owncloud, nextcloud, seafile, there are probably others.  For many people, just using sftp to access their home computers from anywhere in the world is sufficient, with better security.

The 1st 3 listed above only are limited by your HDD storage and abilities.  If you use them with a VPN, they are next to bulletproof.
I use nextcloud myself.  Not the greatest thing for my needs, but lots and lots of other people like it.

All of them are free to download and free to use.
