Folks,
How do you turn off the auto capital letter, when you type the first word of a paragraph? I am typing up some instructions in openoffice, and the items are case-sensitive, so this is giving me a big deal of pain. right now i have to go back though to change each word, when it auto cap.

### Post by _sAm_ on 2009-02-12
Hi

Tools > AutoCorrect >tab called "Options" then uncheck the "Capitalize first letter of every sentence".


You can find guides/manuals for OpenOffice here; [http://www.oooportal.com/](http://www.oooportal.com/)
