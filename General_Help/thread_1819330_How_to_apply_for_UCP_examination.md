I think if you cant find how to take the exam you failed ;-)

There are 3 exams 117-101,117-102 and 117-199 the last one being the UCP specific.

You have to become LPI by taking the first 2 and once you take the last one (117-199) you will get the UCP also.

you can take them at any VUE or Prometric test centre.

There were plans last year to make the UCP independent of LPI not sure if its happened yet though.
