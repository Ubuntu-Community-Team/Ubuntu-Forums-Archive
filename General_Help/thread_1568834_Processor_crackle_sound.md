### Post by lombaardcj on 2010-09-06
Are you confident in opening the box and checking your cpu/fan and other items on your motherboard?

If so: 

I suggest you open the computer case (after disconnecting from main power), carefully look at all the parts inside, and look if you can see any that might be loose, not solidly slotted in, then remove, and re-insert.

I've seen RAM modules comes loose from thermal expansion and graphics cards come undone from their slot.

If you've done all, then lastly check your CPU Fan.

If you can remove your CPU and re-insert I'd suggest that.

Then re-apply power, and boot up.
