### Post by dreamquartz on 2010-08-15
When I open a slideshow in OpenOffice 3.2.1 Impress on my Ubuntu 10.04, and show it (F5), the Top and Bottom Panels of the Desktop stay visible and block part of the slides.
Normally they should disappear.

Is this an Ubuntu or OpenOffice issue?
Is there a solution?

---

### Post by mjuhasz on 2010-08-15
This is a known bug: [https://bugs.launchpad.net/bugs/525807](https://bugs.launchpad.net/bugs/525807)
In the bug report you can find some workarounds (disabling compiz, hiding the panels, etc.) but as far as I know no solution exists yet.

---

### Post by dreamquartz on 2010-08-16
Changed "Visual Effects" to "None" under "Appearance Preferences".
