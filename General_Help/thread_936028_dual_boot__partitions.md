### Post by _sAm_ on 2008-10-02
You cant do changes to a partition while the filesystem is mounted! 

But you don't need the large Ubuntu livecd, you could download just Gparted livecd(ca 90mb) and use it. 
Download Gparted here; [http://gparted.sourceforge.net/download.php](http://gparted.sourceforge.net/download.php)
