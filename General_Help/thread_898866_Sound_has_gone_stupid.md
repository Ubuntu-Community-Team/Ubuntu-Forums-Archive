### Post by tommcd on 2008-08-23
Try going back into system > preferences > sound, then the sounds tab, and set everything to "no sound" except for login and log out. That is how mine is set. If that does not work then uncheck "play system sounds" at the top of the dialog box, but that will likely disable the login and log out sounds as well, if you don't mind that.
