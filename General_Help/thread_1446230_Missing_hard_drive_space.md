### Post by Revolutionary101 on 2010-04-03
Check your hard drive space usage with Disk Usage Analyzer in Applications>Accessories>Disk Usage Analyzer. 

Scan your Windows 7 partition and look for a 14 GB block. When you find the 14 GB block, those should be your missing files.
