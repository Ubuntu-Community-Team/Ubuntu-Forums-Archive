### Post by papibe on 2011-10-08
Last week I made an rough estimate for a Home Server replacement.

For around $200 I could get a Dual Core Celeron, 2Gb RAM, 500Gb HD (no graphic card, no optical media). That would be very good to great as Home Server, but barely OK as a Desktop.

In other words, $200 is kind of low for 'decent' replacement, with a 'decent' graphic card.

IMHO, the lowest I would recommend would be a Core Duo 2. Better would be a 2nd gen core (like and i3). So my estimates would be more in the range of $400-450.

Just some thoughts,
