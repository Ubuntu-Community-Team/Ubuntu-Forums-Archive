### Post by linuxuser12345 on 2011-10-26
To all of you people having issues with Yahoo! on Chome and Chromium in GNU/Linux and Linux variants, I have found the fix. Just type the following into your web browser, instead of the traditional "www.yahoo.com" and Yahoo will force itself to load in the desktop version:
[http://www.yahoo.com/?m=1](http://www.yahoo.com/?m=1)


Hope this helps! :!
