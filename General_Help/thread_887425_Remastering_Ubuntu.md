### Post by linux saved my life on 2008-08-12
I've been looking for a lot of ways to try and remaster my ubuntu .iso and i stumbled across (not the social net work) this great aplication called **Reconstructor**.

[http://reconstructor.aperantis.com]("http://reconstructor.aperantis.com")

It's a fantatastic piece of software. It allows people new to ubuntu to easily remaster a live cd without needing to go terminal.

Changes that can be made include splash screens for bootup, pre-installed GTK themes and pre-installed aplications and more.

Just placing it on the forms so more people know about it and enjoy the freedom the is ubuntu linux.
