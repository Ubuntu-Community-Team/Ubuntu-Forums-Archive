### Post by StuartN on 2009-09-29
I can not think of an open source equivalent, but you can get a copy of Reaper for evaluation at [http://www.reaper.fm/](http://www.reaper.fm/) and you can get an unlimited copy of CMusic and energyXT on DVD with Computer Music Magazine ([http://www.computermusic.co.uk/](http://www.computermusic.co.uk/)). You can get a trial or buy energyXT for Linux/Mac/Windows at [http://www.energy-xt.com/](http://www.energy-xt.com/)

There are also free (function-limited but useful) downloads of players like Garritan or Native Instruments Kore if you hunt around their official websites.
