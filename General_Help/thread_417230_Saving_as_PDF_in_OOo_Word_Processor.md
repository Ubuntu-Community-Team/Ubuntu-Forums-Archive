### Post by spin2cool on 2007-04-21
To my knowledge, there is no good way to import pdfs into OOo.  There are utilities like 'pdftotext' (from the command line) that will let you extract the content, but you'll have to do the layout all over again.

There may be another tool for editing pdfs on linux - search around these forums and see what you can find.
