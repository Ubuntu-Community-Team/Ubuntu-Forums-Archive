### Post by tjk on 2007-10-19
My upgrade took about 8 hours and I did this before the current version was released (and before the mad rush had started). I am seeing many reports that servers are pretty much at a stand-still.  This will last for about a week (at least it did when I upgraded to Feisty).  If you want you can try getting a local - fast server and you may be lucky to get the files in a day or less.  I would suggest you wait if you don't want the added stress (the upgrade doesn't have any major changes -- as far as I'm concerned).  The wait will also give time for this forum to accumulate lots of troubleshooting information -- in case you need it. :)
