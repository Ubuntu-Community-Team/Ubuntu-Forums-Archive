### Post by joshedmonds on 2009-06-18
I am running 9.04 on an AA1 110 (ZG5) and everything worked out of the box except the led for the wireless - the wireless worked, just not the light.

To get the led working, I installed the linux-backports-modules-jaunty package from the repos (you may have to tick the backports repository box in Synaptic). That's it.
