### Post by DMAthlon on 2007-02-08
actually i fixed it for what appears to be a permanent solution.

I went into the synaptic and removed all 3 entries installed containing swfdec. then i installed adobe flash player 9, restarted and all is well. hope i help someone else who has this problem!
