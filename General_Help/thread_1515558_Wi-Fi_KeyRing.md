When you first installed the system you most probably did what everyone else does, left click the icon in your panel and started your network connection by choosing what's there and entering not only your security but your passwoerd twice.  I have been trying to tell people the correct way is right click the icon, then, Edit Connections placing the correct information in that way.  When you do, you set it up to "autostart"  "for all users" and put in your password for root once.  After a restart it never asks you again.  You may be able to correct this by right clicking the icon and entering the information manually.  Good luck.
