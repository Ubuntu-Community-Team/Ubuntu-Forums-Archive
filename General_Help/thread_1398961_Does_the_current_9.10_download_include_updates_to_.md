### Post by audiomick on 2010-02-05
Hi Alan. 
No, I don't think they update the .iso on a version that isn't an LTS.
You can probably expect upwards of 150 updates after the install.
You don't have to initiate this; the update manager will start nagging (assuming an Internet connection) about a minute after the reboot at the end of the install. Best to let it get on with it before you go on to anything else.
