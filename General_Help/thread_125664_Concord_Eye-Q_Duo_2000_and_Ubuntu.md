### Post by colsinc on 2006-05-29
Hi, afraid I can't be of w hole lot of help, but I have a Concord EyeQ due LCD digicam that has webcam functions which i had going under xp.  

It works as a disk drive, with mass storage device drivers, the camera has to be in the right mode for it to function as a disk drive.  (on the camera) set drivers to hdd.  on my camera, press menu button, scroll down to 'setup', > button to enter setup, scroll to 'driver', > button to switch between hdd (act as disk drive) and pc (webcam) modes.  

take a look at system>admin>device manager to see if it's been recognised.  mine seems to have been recognised, but when I run xawtv or tvtime it can't find it in /dev/video0 (or /dev/video1, 2, etc.  up to 23)
