### Post by JC Cheloven on 2008-09-29
> **nironan12 said:**
> I don't need multiple partitions or hard discs for that?
Yes, in the usual scheme you need to partition the disk, but that's not so difficult (there are other solutions like wubi or virtualization, but i'd suggest to follow the usual scheme). 

Start shrinking your vista partition (from vista itself). You will then be able to install ubuntu in the free space you just made. It depends on what you plan to use ubuntu for, but as litle as 7 Gb of free space will do if you only want to give it a try.
