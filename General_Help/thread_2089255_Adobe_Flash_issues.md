### Post by cottfcfan on 2012-11-29
If you install Google Chrome, it uses its own flash called Pepperflash.
[https://www.google.com/intl/en/chrome/browser/](https://www.google.com/intl/en/chrome/browser/)
This works well for most people.
You will have to disable native flash though by typing "about:plugins" in the address bar in Chrome, to make sure the 2 don't conflict.
