### Post by cyclobs on 2008-07-09
this is a compiz thing and there isn't any solution to it,

the only way to be able to play games in full screen is turn it off. there is a little app called 'compiz fusion icon' that you can turn it off and off with.

also another thing called 'advance effects desktop settings' has an option in the 'general settings' that does something with compiz when i full screen up is started, although it doesn't really work for me and i end up needing to turn compiz off through compiz fusion.

the other option you have is turning compiz off all together though system-->prefences-->appearence under special effects tab i think
