### Post by flar on 2006-06-02
You might look at using samba if you're computer is part of the department network or on campus.  Samba allows you to join windows workgroup or domain to share files. Lots of people do this at my university.   To use the printers you might need permission from the network admin.  If you're off campus you would probably need to go through a VPN.  The VPN makes your computer temporarily part of the campus network (ie: it gives your computer a campus network IP address)
