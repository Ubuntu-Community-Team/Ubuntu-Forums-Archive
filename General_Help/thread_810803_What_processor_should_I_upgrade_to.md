### Post by bingoUV on 2008-05-28
1. Simply upgrading the processor will likely get very difficult as newer processors will not support your motherboard. In that case, you will have to upgrade motherboard, processor, RAM, and possibly graphics card.

2. 100% CPU in games might actually mean a weak graphics card rather than a processor. Which graphics card do you have? Which do your games recommend? Post which games do you play? Do you play them in Ubuntu, or you boot into windows to play them?
