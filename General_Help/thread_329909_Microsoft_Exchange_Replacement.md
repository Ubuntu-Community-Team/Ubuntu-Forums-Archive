### Post by Brazen on 2007-01-02
If you are willing to pay, you should check out Zimbra.  Most of Zimbra is available for free, but all you get is a web interface.  You have to pay for an Outlook connector.  I believe you could you IMAP with Outlook, but you would miss out on some features.  I've never used Zimbra (I do admin an Exchange server), but from the demos and features, I would say the paid version of Zimbra blows Outlook out of the water.

If you want a completely free solution (I prefer fully open software) then eGroupware is the best I've found.
