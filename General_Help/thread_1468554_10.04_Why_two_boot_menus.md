### Post by oldfred on 2010-05-01
Even though it is wubi you should be able to edit the timeout.

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1195275](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1195275)

See section on /etc/default/grub and timeouts
GRUB_TIMEOUT=10
Change to a shorter time.
