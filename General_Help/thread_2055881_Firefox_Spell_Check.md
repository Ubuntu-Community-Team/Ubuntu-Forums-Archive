### Post by rickm1945 on 2012-09-10
I not sure if this is the right place to post this, if not feel, free to move.  I noticed that Firefox spell check only works on certain sites. It works here in the forums, but doesn't work in Facebook, or Twitter. I did a Google search and found that Mozilla had sent a bug report to Ubuntu. In the mean time there was a post that said to type "spell check" in, tools-add-ons the search spell check. Select one of the dictionaries. I ticked English-Bulgarian, restarted Firefox and it then worked in Facebook and Twitter. I hope Ubuntu adds a fix for this in one of their updates, but this works as a work around. Hope this helps someone who has the same issue.
