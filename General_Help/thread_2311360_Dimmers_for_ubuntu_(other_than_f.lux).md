### Post by mcduck on 2016-01-26
You could try [Redshift]("http://jonls.dk/redshift/"). It's mainly intended to work the same way as F.lux, but the config options also include brightness and gamma settings.

...also I remember seeing a Compiz color correction plugin that allowed you to invert screen colors. Not great for looking at pictures, but otherwise a neat way to reduce eye strain.
