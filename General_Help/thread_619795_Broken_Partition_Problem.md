### Post by mpierce on 2007-11-21
Nasty, very nasty... Happened to me once on a 250Gb USB drive.

You are going to have to get a WIN program to do this one. Linux is very dangerous when playing with partitions if you don't know what you are doing as most of the recovery tools are cli. 

I suggest you get the program "GetDataBack" and the version that is applicable for your needs, i.e., NTFS or FAT as it will cost. 

You will also need a spare drive on which to recover the info and save it. The process can take days if you have a big drive to recover from. 

DON'T DO ANYTHING TO YOUR DRIVE, i.e., writing, saving files, etc. 

Hope this helps...
