### Post by DrScum on 2013-02-20
Using Ubuntu 12.04 LTS, Gnome fallback, Compiz, after the most recent update a couple hours ago, the window borders are gone. How do I get them back?

### Post by omeomi on 2013-02-20
Probably a problem with Compiz not starting/functioning properly. Try these steps to see if that resolves your problem: [http://wiki.compiz.org/Troubleshooting#No_Window_Borders](http://wiki.compiz.org/Troubleshooting#No_Window_Borders)

---

### Post by DrScum on 2013-02-20
Thanks for the input. In CCSM under "Effects" "Window Decoration" was checked, but unchecking and re-checking brought the decorations back. In the course of doing this I also got a system error message. Now things seem to be back to normal.
