### Post by adam_kimber on 2007-11-16
Sounds like Nautilus is trying to preview the audio files in the folder and cannot.It might be something to do with CODECS either being missing or corrupt but if you are not wanting audio previews do the following. 

For now to avoid this, open any folder, go to Edit --> Preferences there and go to the Preview tab. Then go to the Preview Sound Files and change it to never.
