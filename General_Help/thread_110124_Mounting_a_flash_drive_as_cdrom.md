> Mounting the flash drive as /cdrom
Switch to the second virtual console during the first couple of dialogs (when asked about your preferred language for the installation etc.) by pressing the ""ALT-2"". Mount the flash drive to /cdrom using a command such as mount -t vfat /dev/sda1 /cdrom and then switch back to the first virtual console by pressing ""ALT-1"". Continue installing Ubuntu as if you were running from CD.
