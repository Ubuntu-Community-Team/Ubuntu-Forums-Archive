### Post by VanHammersly on 2007-06-22
Did you see the lifehacker article on this too?  I downloaded immediately after seeing that.

Go to Beryl Settings Manager.  Then go to the Desktop Tab.  At the bottom there is a Widget Layer.  Check that.  In the Widget Layer section, go to Shortcuts and assign a short cut.  I hope this helps.

Have fun!
