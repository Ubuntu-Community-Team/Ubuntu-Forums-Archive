### Post by charlie cat on 2012-06-17
If you are opening the files while browsing firefox, do this:
In firefox, go into Edit->Preferences.  Click on the "Applications" tab.  Look for the PDF content-type, and then use the drop down on the right to have firefox open it with Document Viewer.
