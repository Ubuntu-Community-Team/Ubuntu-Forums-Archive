### Post by 3Miro on 2009-04-07
Under KDE setting manager and gnome preferences, there is accessibility section. Slow keys are controlled from in there.

Under KDE settings -> appearance there is a GTK section. Under Gnome there is Qt4 section under prefs. GTK is the Gnome technology and Qt4 is the KDE one.

Firefox + Flash under KDE crashes a lot for me as well. Under Gnome it works sort of OK. Try updating everything you can (you installed flash from the repos right?). Try using Konqueror (you have to manually detect Netscape plug-ins). For youtube, I use Totem movie player.
