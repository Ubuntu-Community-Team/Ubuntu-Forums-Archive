### Post by cottfcfan on 2012-03-07
Problem Solved.
After adding Chrome or Chromium to the launcher bar, you need to logout or reboot. (something I very rarely do as I tend to use suspend)
After that it behaves fine.
Don't know why this is not needed for any other app, but regardless i'll mark as solved.
