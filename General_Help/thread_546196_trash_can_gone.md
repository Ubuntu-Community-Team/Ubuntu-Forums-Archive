### Post by tripokey on 2007-09-08
Hi! Yes you probably deleted it somehow..

Here is how to fix it:
[B][LIST=1]
[*]Right click on the bottom panel
[*]Choose add to panel
[*]Select Trash can
[*]Click add
[*]Click close
[*]Right click on your new trash can and select move
[*]When you have it where you want it just click again
[*]And finally right click on your trash can and select lock to make sure that you dont move it accidentally.
[/LIST][/B]
