### Post by brandon88tube on 2008-11-05
This is for a lot of new people who are using Wubi and are coming across problems. In most cases if it doesn't deal with the installer in any way, then it is probably not a problem with Wubi, but Ubuntu itself. Examples of this would be problems with your wireless not working etc. These are issues that are related to Ubuntu and not Wubi. If you are experiencing these problems then you might want to try looking at other sections of the the ubuntuforums. If you do feel that it might be related to Wubi then by all means, post away in here.

I am making this to help take the load off Ago and the others who are getting swamped with tons of threads that do not directly relate to Wubi.

This might change to better help those who may not understand.
