### Post by tacantara on 2009-09-09
I've been using Evolution for as long as I've been using Ubuntu.  I didn't notice it until you brought it up, but I checked it on one of the three mail accounts I have merged into Evolution.  Any messages I want to keep I put into special holding folders in Evolution's root.  The messages are also moved to the internet mail account's Trash folder.  On this particular account, I know that the trash automatically empties after a set number of days, or I can manually empty it at any time.  Out of sight, out of mind as far as I'm concerned.

### Post by t0p on 2009-09-09
Go to **Edit > Preferences > Mail Preferences**.  Under the **General** tab is an option to empty deleted items folder on exit: Every time, Once per day, Once per week, or Once per month.  Select as you see fit.
