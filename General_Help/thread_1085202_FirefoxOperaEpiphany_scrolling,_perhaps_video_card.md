### Post by ajgreeny on 2009-03-20
Have you tried without compiz running, if you use it, that is?  I does make a big difference on my system, with Mint6 (based on intrepid).  I have an ati card as well, a 9200SE, old but works OK for my needs, and I get about 890fps in glxgears, but still scrolling is slow.  I found one post suggesting a faster overall theme, eg nimbus, would help, and it certainly did, but it's compiz that slows my machine's scrolling in FF too much so, I stop it with [compiz-switch]("http://forlong.blogage.de/entries/pages/Compiz-Switch").

Sorry it's been a while answering this, and ignore me if you've found another answer that works better, but I subscribed to your post and then forgot it, but have just found it again.
