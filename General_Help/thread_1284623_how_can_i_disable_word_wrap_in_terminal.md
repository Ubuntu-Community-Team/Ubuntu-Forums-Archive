### Post by dcstar on 2009-10-06
> **fwaokda said:**
> how can i disable word wrap in terminal?

Terminal sessions - by their very nature - have fixed columns and are designed to wrap.

Some terminal based programs may give you the option to disable wrapping, but not the terminal session itself.

You may be able to increase to number of columns from the default, but that will depend on the particular terminal software you use.
