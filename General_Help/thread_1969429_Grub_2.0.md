### Post by oscarivera9 on 2013-02-03
We are now at the grub 2.0 release, finally.  Grub 2.00 was released on June 27, 2012 and it is the current boot-loader for Ubuntu 12.10, within the next couple of months grub 2.00 will also be available for Ubuntu 12.04.  One of the main features of this new release is not necessarily new, it's just been improved upon and it deals with booting on a UEFI motherboard.
