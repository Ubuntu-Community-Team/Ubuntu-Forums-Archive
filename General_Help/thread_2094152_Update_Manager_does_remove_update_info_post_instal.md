### Post by dcstar on 2012-12-14
> **tahseen said:**
> Now it is gone suddenly. I didn't do anything even then

If new Updates are listed in the Repository Catalogue even though the actual Update files are not yet downloaded and available then this sort of thing can occur.

Eventually the files arrive and are available to be installed, then the "problem" goes away.
