### Post by mb_webguy on 2010-05-06
OldGoat58:  Dropbox is cloud-based internet storage, sort of like UbuntuOne but cross-platform.  You can use it to sync and share files across multiple computers over the internet.

axe.gs1:  Reinstalling Dropbox is probably the way to go.  It won't affect any of the other computers you have it on, and as long as you use the same directory, it won't even have to re-sync anything.  On the other hand, you haven't by any chance removed the notification area from your panel, have you?  It sounds like Dropbox is still working, but you're just missing the notification icon.  Ubuntu now uses two different notification areas, the notification area (which is where the Dropbox icon would be) and the indicator applet (which is where the Evolution messages and volume icons are now located).  It would be easy to remove one of the two and not realize it...
