### Post by acormack on 2007-04-01
The key is to use the binary-only ATI driver that is not distributed with Ubuntu because it is closed source.

The best way to download it (and lots of other useful things) is to use Automatix2.

If you follow the instructions here [http://www.ubuntugeek.com/installing-popular-applications-on-your-ubuntu-desktop-with-automatix2.html](http://www.ubuntugeek.com/installing-popular-applications-on-your-ubuntu-desktop-with-automatix2.html)

you should be able to get your penguin racing a lot faster!
