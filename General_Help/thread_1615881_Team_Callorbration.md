### Post by SaintDanBert on 2010-11-08
What you describe, I have always referred to as a "contact manager." Another name is Customer Relationship Management (CRM).  In the windows(tm) world, one leading desktop application is called "Act!"(tm).

Take a look at [_vTiger_]("http://www.vtiger.com/") and see if that meets your needs.

If your staff and customer interaction is more technical in nature, your might want what is called a "group-ware" suite.

Take a look at [_egroupware_]("http://www.egroupware.org/").

I'm not suggesting that either of these are guaranteed successful solutions to your needs. I offer them as examples that might get your thinking wrapped around your requirements more completely.
