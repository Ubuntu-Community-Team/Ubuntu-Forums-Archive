### Post by bodhi.zazen on 2011-04-02
Your specs are over kill for a router.

While you will get various opinions on using an old box as a router, I am of the opinion you should use one of the router specific distros rather then Ubuntu.

There are several to choose from.

Samba is insecure over the internet, so I would keep that samba and ssh servers separate from the router.
