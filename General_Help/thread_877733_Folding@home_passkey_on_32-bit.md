### Post by comradekingu on 2009-12-19
the 6.* series is the first to support passkey. Use the "-k" flag followed by your passkey when installing. [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/FoldingAtHome/origami]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/FoldingAtHome/origami")
