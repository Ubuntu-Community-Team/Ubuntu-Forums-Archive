### Post by cmost on 2007-02-14
Most USB2 external print servers will work well with most operating systems.  See here: [http://rfsd.sourceforge.net/](http://rfsd.sourceforge.net/) for information on the HP Jetdirect en3700 External Print Server (USB, 10/100TX).  Another solution you might consider is attaching the printer to one or the other of your two workstations and sharing the printer over your network.  This method will require a bit of fiddling with Samba and CUPS but works well and only requires that you lave the computer with the printer turned on.  Good luck!
