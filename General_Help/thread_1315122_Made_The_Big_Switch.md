### Post by soapBAR2 on 2009-11-05
I can't help you with the Xbox thing, but generally, just download programs as you need them. Just remember that you don't download and run installers from your browser, rather, you download through the package manager (Add/Remove Programs) - much, much easier and safer. Since you asked, here are some programs/packages I like:

Restricted Extras (DVD playback, flash, etc): ubuntu-restricted-extras
MSPaint-esque program: kolourpaint4
Pull-down terminal (Quake-style): guake (GNOME) or yakuake (KDE)
Media players: smplayer, vlc
Video editor: kdenlive, kino, avidemux
DVD Author: dvdstyler, tovid
DVD Ripper: acidrip, k9copy handbrake (although this is currently broken) 
Virtual Machine: virtualbox
Desktop Effects: compiz (also install ccsm)
Network Manager: wicd
Nvidia control panel (for nvidia graphics cards): nvidia-settings

I would advise you to install Ubuntu Tweak: [[COLOR="Red"]**Ubuntu Tweak**[/COLOR]]("http://www.getdeb.net/release/4775"), this is an excellent program for installing lots of goodies on Ubuntu.

Once you have installed Ubuntu Tweak you will be able to find it under **System Tools**, click on the Ubuntu Tweak icon and let's start installing the necessary things.

Click **Applications** > **Third Party Sources**, click **Unlock** and enter your password.  Tick the box next to **Medibuntu** (*this is the stuff for playing DVD's on your PC and other goodies*)

If you want Google Earth installed then tick the box next to **Google Repository**. 

Now click the **Refresh** button

Next click **Add/Remove ** in the Applications menu.

Now I'm guessing that you've not added a dock to your desktop, so go ahead and tick the box next to **Avant Window Navigator**

Now click the box next to Ubuntu Restricted Extras & then click quit.

You will now have the majority of software that practically everyone that uses Ubuntu has.

As for your Xbox, I've no clue as to help you there but I'm sure that question has been asked previously, so feel free to use the search function.

Please feel free to ask for help when you need it.
