### Post by firefeather on 2007-12-21
It really depends on the program. Wine is constantly being developed, but in my experience it takes a while for wine to catch up on graphically-intense programs or those with custom (non-Windows-native) GUIs. That being said, Wine is great for some programs that you wouldn't think. Try looking at [http://appdb.winehq.org/]("http://appdb.winehq.org/"); sometimes they have tips there on what can make a program work better.
