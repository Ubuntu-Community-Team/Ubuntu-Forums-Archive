### Post by redmk2 on 2009-05-19
> Any help on how did this happen, and how to fix this will be very appreciated.

The terms sd*xn* are dynamically assigned on boot.  The order is as they are discovered.  There is no absolute order.  You should use UUID's to ID your /dev/<device>

Since these do not change you can mount to a specific mount point consistently.  See the UUID's at the top of your fstab file?

To view the UUID's use this```
sudo blkid
``` Use these in your fstab file.

See [_here_]("http://www.unixtutorial.org/2008/05/ubuntu-uuid-how-to/") for UUID info.
