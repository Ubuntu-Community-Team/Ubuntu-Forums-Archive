### Post by pseb on 2006-11-08
there is a built-in intelligent playlist (accessible via the playlist tab on the left) for the entire collection. just select it and you have all your songs in the playlist. (but if there are thousands of songs in the playlist, amarok gets quite slow. that's why i like dynamic playlists so much)
