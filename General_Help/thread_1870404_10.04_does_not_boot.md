### Post by collisionystm on 2011-10-27
> **A.D.E said:**
> yeah i installed from usb!!! How can i install the grub on my local drive?

**Using Boot-Repair-Disk**

 
[LIST]
[*]Download [Boot-Repair-Disk]("https://sourceforge.net/p/boot-repair-cd") 
[*]Burn it on a CD or put it on USB key via Unetbootin 
[*]Insert the Boot-Repair-Disk and reboot the PC 
[*]Choose 32 or 64bits 
[*][Boot-Repair]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Boot-Repair") will automatically start. Select 'First Repair' and apply. 
[*]Reboot without the CD. GRUB menu will appear and will propose both Ubuntu and Windows.
[/LIST]

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RecoveringUbuntuAfterInstallingWindows](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RecoveringUbuntuAfterInstallingWindows)
