### Post by Yellow Pasque on 2009-04-19
9.3 won't work on Jaunty (specifically because Jaunty uses X.org 1.6)
Use the version that comes with Jaunty or download 9.4 off ATI's site.
Instructions: [http://wiki.cchtml.com/index.php/Ubuntu_Jaunty_Installation_Guide](http://wiki.cchtml.com/index.php/Ubuntu_Jaunty_Installation_Guide)
