### Post by mikewhatever on 2012-02-03
I think this is a bug in 11.10. An easy workaround would be to kill nautilus and then restart it. You can either use the system monitor, find nautilus among the processes and kill it, or 'killall nautilus' in a terminal. Then open a folder, and it should come up with the regular colors.
