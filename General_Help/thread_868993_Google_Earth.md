### Post by oldos2er on 2008-07-24
Download Google Earth ([http://earth.google.com/tour/thanks-linux4.html](http://earth.google.com/tour/thanks-linux4.html)) to your desktop. Open a terminal, type these commands one at a time:

cd Desktop

chmod a+x GoogleEarthLinux.bin

sudo ./GoogleEarthLinux.bin
