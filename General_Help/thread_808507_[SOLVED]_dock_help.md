If you delete your bottom panel, just right click on your top panel and click New Panel and it will create a new one that you can drag back to the bottom. To see what windows you have open you can right click on the panel and click Add to Panel and add Window List.

To be able to access your documents folder, you have to install the extra applets and there is an applet called File Browser Launcher.

### Post by pmaconi on 2008-05-28
Open the AWN-Manager (you can right click the dock and choose preferences to open it). On the left side of the window click the button that says applets. You can enable Places, File Browser, or File Browser Launcher to do what you want. After one of those is enabled, right click it and set the preferences to open /home/username/Documents as the default folder.
