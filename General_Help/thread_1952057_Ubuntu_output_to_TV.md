### Post by papibe on 2012-04-03
Hi JBA2337.

If you don't have any proprietary driver installed, go to 'Monitors' to enable the output.

S-video does no have audio. so you have to pass the audio with other connection. For example a cable that goes through the headphone output, and to an audio input on the TV.

BTW, S-video is very low quality, just above using RCA cables. The max resolution would be 1024x768. VGA would be a much better connection, and of course, HDMI ideal.

Hope that helps, and tell us how it goes.
Regards.
