### Post by Butthead on 2011-12-22
Jesus, who rightfully has 800 email messages in their mailbox? :eek:

For one, why don't you try deleting some of them? :confused:

Kmail is kind of flaky.  Depends if the ISP puts a lot of anti-spam software on his mail server.  I know I always seem to be fighting mine installing all of the certificates that pop up for me.

One thing you might want to check though in the "Settings" "Configure" sub-menu is the amount of email messages KMail will try to download at one setting.  You might want to try setting that higher to see if that will work.  Then set KMail to purge old emails after a certain date has passed.

I would get rid (as in try to delete) a lot of those email messages though.  You're probably running up everyone else's storage charges on the ISP archiving all that old crap. :oops:
