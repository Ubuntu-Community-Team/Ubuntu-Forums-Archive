### Post by LinuxCharms on 2010-12-04
It's pretty simple.

You can go down, right click on your user panel the hit 'Add To Panel' and you'll get a list of various things. You search until you find the trash can and then just add it to the panel and move it around to wherever you like.

---

### Post by Habitual on 2010-12-04
gconf-editor /apps/nautilus/desktop/trash_icon_visible
Make sure it's checked.
