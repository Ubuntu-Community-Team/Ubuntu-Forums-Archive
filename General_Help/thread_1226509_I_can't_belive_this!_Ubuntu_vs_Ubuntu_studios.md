### Post by Arup on 2009-07-29
Ubuntustudio uses real time kernel for low latency in audio recording, for video there is no need for low latency kernels. Bear in mind that video drivers, apart from one in repos won't work on Ubuntustudio. Also another low latency alternative is to remove Alsa and use OSS4 which has no latency due to all mixing being done at kernel level. This way you get to keep regular Ubuntu and have do your audio editing as well, OSS4 has better quality but may not work with some MIDI.
