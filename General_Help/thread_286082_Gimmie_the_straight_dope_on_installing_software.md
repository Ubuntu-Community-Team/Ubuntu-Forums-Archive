### Post by lazyart on 2006-10-27
Applications->Add/Remove is the easiest way.  You can click on Advanced from there to get an even larger list of software.  For a self-professed noob (and I am one too) it's easiest to go there.

.deb files can be installed by double clicking on them.  You might get dependency issues, but going thru the top method everything is handled for you... otherwise you find yourself installing the libraries to let you install the software... and they might depend on something else.  It funny sometimes.

Stick the with the menus for now. :)
