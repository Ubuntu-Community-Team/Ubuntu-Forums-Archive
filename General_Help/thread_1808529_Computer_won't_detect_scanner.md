### Post by linuxuser12345 on 2011-07-20
Nevermind! I fixed it! :)
If anyone else experiences this problem, go to the Synaptic Package Manager and search up "HPLIP". Completely remove anything with "HPLIP" in it's name, restart your computer, and reinstall the HPLIP toolbox in the Software Center. This will reset all your drivers also. Reinstall your printer and you should be good to go! :)
