### Post by Curtisc on 2005-12-15
If you're looking for a big hard drive player,  I'm really happy with my iaudio X5.  It shows up as an external drive and auto mounts itself, and is the same size as the iPod... my favourite thing about it is that it's made out of aluminum and I've dropped it onto tiled floors, shoved it in my pocket with keys, etc... and there isn't a scratch on it (had it since July).  However, it doesn't auto-organize your files (build a database) - you have to set them up in folders yourself.

Another one I looked into (but decided against as it was only 6GB) is the Rio Carbon.  It also shows up as an external drive and doesn't need any software to transfer (drag and drop), but it builds a database when you add songs, just like the ipod.
