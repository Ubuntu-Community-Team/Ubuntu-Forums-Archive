### Post by aysiu on 2010-05-01
The Desktop CD doesn't have the software packages needed for an upgrade. It installs by making a slightly modified copy of the live session.

The Alternate CD has the software packages needed for an upgrade.

That's why you can't upgrade from the Desktop CD.
