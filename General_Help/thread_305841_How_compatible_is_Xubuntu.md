### Post by CatKiller on 2006-11-24
That computer could be **way** old. The last one I saw that **used** the turbo button had a 66 MHz processor in it. Of course, it could be a newer motherboard in an AT case, which might make it up to the 450-ish mark.

So it depends. At the top of the AT range, you could probably manage to get Xubuntu on there, although memory might still be tight. At the bottom end, not a hope. You could probably still use a text-based setup, possibly starting with the **server** install, and use Pine and Lynx.
