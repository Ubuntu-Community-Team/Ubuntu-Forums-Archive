### Post by sebikul on 2010-10-25
by "lost the contents" you mean to the files or the ownership?

if what's "lost" is the ownership just do a:
# sudo chown -R NEW_OWNER /foo/bar

NEW_OWNER would be of course the username of the new owner and /foo/bar is just an example, use absolute path, try to avoid relative

If what is lost are the files then the only thing you can do is to try a file recovery tool hoping that the data hasn't been overwritten

Hope it helps!!
