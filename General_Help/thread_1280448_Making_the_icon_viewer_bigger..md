### Post by StuartN on 2009-10-02
> **peanut_butter_kid said:**
> Is there a way I can view just the picture icons and them being much larger?

To view just the icon, select View->Icons. To change the size of the icons, Edit->Preferences and alter the Icon View Defaults to give you the size you want, 100% being the initial setting. You can find the right size by zooming in and out with View->Zoom before opening the Preferences.
