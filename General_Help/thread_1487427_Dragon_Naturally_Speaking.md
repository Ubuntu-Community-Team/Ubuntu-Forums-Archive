### Post by BslBryan on 2010-05-19
A couple of people have managed to get it to work with a program called Wine.

To get it, open up a terminal (Applications --> System --> Terminal) and type in
```
sudo apt-get install wine winecfg
```

Here are a couple of links that may help you, though both are a little outdated.

[http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=version&iId=13563](http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=version&iId=13563)
