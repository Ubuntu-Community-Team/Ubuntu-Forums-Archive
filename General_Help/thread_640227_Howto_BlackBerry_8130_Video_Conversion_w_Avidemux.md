### Post by 43moon on 2007-12-13
I have a BlackBerry 8130 and it took me a while to find something that properly converted video for my phone. Avidemux does the trick.

In Avidemux I open the original video file and I use these settings to convert it to a format that works on my Pearl:

Video: Mpeg4(lavc)
Configure: (keep default)
Video Filters: Transform / Resize / Configure / Width: 240 Height: 180

Audio: FAAC
Configure: Bitrate 64kbits
Filters: (keep default)

Format: MP4

Hit the save button and let Avidmux run.

Enjoy,
43moon
