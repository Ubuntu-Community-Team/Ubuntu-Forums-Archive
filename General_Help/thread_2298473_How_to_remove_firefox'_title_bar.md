You could just use the pretty universal Alt+Space+X to ma_x_imize, Alt+Space+N to mi_n_imize, Alt+Space+C to _c_lose. Your window manager *may* also have something like Alt+Space+D to toggle _d_ecorations (including the title bar). That'll work whether you have a title bar (or window decorations or not).
