### Post by Shazaam on 2009-03-15
1. Go here to update the gpg key for medibuntu...
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Medibuntu](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Medibuntu)
2. Go to System>Administration>Software Sources and uncheck any listing for your cdrom.
3. When done go ahead and update.
