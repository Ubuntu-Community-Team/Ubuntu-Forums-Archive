### Post by LeoSolaris on 2008-04-24
May want to give them a week or two to get all of the updates on the wiki. If you can't tolerate waiting that long, you could download it a few times from different sources and compare the various md5's. If they are all the same you're likely good, or only one is different but the rest match, the rest are likely right.

(you could also download 7.10, verify it, the do a distro upgrade after installing it)
