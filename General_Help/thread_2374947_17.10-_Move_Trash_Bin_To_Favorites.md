### Post by drs305 on 2017-10-20
In nautilus (Files), there is a trash bin icon in the top left panel by default.

If you want to add it to the bottom in your own favorites, add the following to ~/.config/gtk-3.0/bookmarks:
> 
trash:///


Trash should appear as the last item on the left bookmarks panel.
Note: The above doesn't 'move' it, but it does add it to the Favorites list.
