### Post by tuxcantfly on 2006-03-26
first, open synaptic (system - administration - synaptic package manager)
then click the menu settings, then click repositories
change every line that says hoary in the distribution section to breezy (or dapper, if you want the latest packages)
then click reload
then click mark all upgrades; use the smart upgrade if prompted
wait while it downloads and installs, reboot, and you have breezy (or dapper)
