### Post by panda726 on 2008-11-04
Adept was changed as part of the shift to KDE4.1 from KDE3.5.  I actually had gone to Aptitude before Intrepid came out, and personally I have no wish to go back to either Adept.  Open Konsole (or your favourite terminal, regardless of flavour), and type ```
sudo aptitude
```  The help functions are quite decent, and there is a wiki page on using Aptitude.

Added bonus of Aptitude:  It works beautifully over SSH too.
