### Post by ComplexNumber on 2006-03-31
downloadf the qt-gtk engine that you can get from kde-look.org. this will make the gtk apps mimic the kde theme.
as for making kde look like clearlooks, the nearest to clearlooks are the  qtcurve and klearlooks styles. in my opinion, the former is most like clearlooks. there is also a clearlooks colour scheme (in my opinion, it close, but not exact). there is also a clearlooks kwin theme. do a search on kde-look.org and you will find them :)
