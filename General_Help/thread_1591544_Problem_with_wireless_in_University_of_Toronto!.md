### Post by yasink on 2012-05-16
[LIST]
[*]Click on wireless icon on top right corner
[*]Click VPN connections -> Configure VPN
[*]Delete UTORwin and/or Uwin.
[*]Go to the wireless icon on top right corner again and connect to UTORwin
[/LIST]
By following these steps, you simply reset the configuration for the wireless network. This is a similar technique to one mentioned above re. Starbucks technique but you don't need to buy coffee :)
