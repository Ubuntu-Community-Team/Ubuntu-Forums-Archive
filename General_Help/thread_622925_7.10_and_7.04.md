### Post by fjgaude on 2007-11-25
You will have to have unallocated space for Ubuntu to install into that space. You can use the LiveCD to partition the drive as you would like it. Don't try to partition on a mounted drive with its OS.

You can start the install and slowly work your way through... you can always back out if you feel like you don't understand something.

Good luck.
