### Post by map445 on 2009-12-15
I have had this problem for over a month and found it was my ram.  I ran memtest and had over 1600 errors when checked using ram in (DS) Duel Channel mode.
Using Ram Man's suggestions, I changed the positions of the ram to Single Channel (SS). (used the second and third ram slots)  I ran memtest again and no errors were found.  I had crashes with every Browser I tried, and would be dropped back to the desktop or hard crashed and had to reset.

 DS=Duel-Channel - SS=Single Channel

I hope this helps...

-map445
