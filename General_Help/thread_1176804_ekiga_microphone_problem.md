SOLVED!

In the tool bar I went to VolumeIcon->RightClick->Open Volume Control-> Preferences

Tick "Input source" and then close preferences window

Go to options and change from "Mic" to "Front mic".

For me this SOLVED the problem


I think I changed from mic to mic/front

Good luck
