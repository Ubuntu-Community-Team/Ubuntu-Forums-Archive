### Post by Vladlenin5000 on 2015-12-04
It isn't clear whether you installed Kubuntu as a dual boot with your existing Ubuntu or just installed the Kubuntu desktop to your existing Ubuntu.
Or perhaps you opened the Kubuntu installation media in your Ubuntu and answered affirmatively when prompted about adding it to the software sources? If so then:

1. You shouldn't be surprised, it did exactly what you told it to do.
2. You can easily remove it in System Settings > Software & Updates.
