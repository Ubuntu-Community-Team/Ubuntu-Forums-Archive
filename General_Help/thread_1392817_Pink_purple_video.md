### Post by s.fox on 2010-01-28
Hello,

I had this problem too with my videos in Totem.    After reading through [this]("https://answers.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/totem/+question/7373") bug I resolved the issue by adjusting the hue in the video setting preferences.

I would try the following settings as an approximate guide:

 Hue set to 0 
Saturation up 10%
Contrast down down 10%
Brightness down 5%

Please post back if you experience any problems.

-Silver Fox
