### Post by IndigoRage on 2008-04-17
Avira (available at [www.avira.com](www.avira.com)) has a great free Antivirus for Linux.

Don't believe that whole "You don't need an antivirus for Linux" shpeel - there are plenty of Linux-specific viri out there - it's just not as popular to make web-distributed viri for Linux as it is for Windows.

The same goes for Mac OS (Anything) - there are pleny of Mac specific viri as well - just who wants to write a virus for 1% of the market? ;-)
