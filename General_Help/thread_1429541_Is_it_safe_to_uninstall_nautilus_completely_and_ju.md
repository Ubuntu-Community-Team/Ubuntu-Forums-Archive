---

### Post by mcduck on 2010-03-14
You'll probably loose quite many features from Gnome, and also have to set pcmanfm to handle your desktop as well (since Nautilus handles it by default in Gnome).

Unless you are very, very low on disk space I'd recommend leaving Nautilus installed. (and if you really are that low on disk space you should buy a bigger hard drive :))
