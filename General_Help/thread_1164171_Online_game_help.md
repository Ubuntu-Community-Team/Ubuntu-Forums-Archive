### Post by themusicalduck on 2009-05-19
If you type about:plugins into the address bar and look at what it says under Shockwave Flash, then post it here. It'll at least let people know what flash version is being used and be able to help.

Since you installed 3 different version of flash, firefox is probably using one of them. I find that the official version from Adobe is the best but you need to properly remove the others before you install it.

You could try uninstalling SWFdec and gnash from add/remove or synaptic, then try reinstalling flash from the adobe website.
