### Post by Toz on 2011-12-03
Goto Settings->Software Sources->Other Software tab and enable the "Canonical Partners" repository. Once enabled, you will have access to **acroread** (Adobe Acrobat Reader) in the Software Center. Looks like its version 9.4.6.
