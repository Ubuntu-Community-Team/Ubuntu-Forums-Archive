### Post by Zill on 2009-07-19
iwscanner is a useful GUI utility that shows signal strengths of surrounding networks in dB.

[http://art.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=834947]("http://art.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=834947")

The deb isn't in the repos (yet!) but can be easily downloaded and installed from the iwscanner home website at [http://www.kuthulu.com/iwscanner]("http://www.kuthulu.com/iwscanner")

I just hope your eyes can read the very dark website - it's a real struggle for my aged eyes. :cry:
