### Post by oldtraveler on 2012-07-18
In Ubuntu 12.04 is there a way to get to terminal other than by doing a search in Dash Home?  It goes on my desktop and then disappears.

---

### Post by drs305 on 2012-07-18
Ctrl-Alt-t

Once you have it open it should appear in the Launcher. Right click the Icon and select 'Lock to launcher' to keep it even when terminal isn't running.
