### Post by Tristam Green on 2008-02-21
Under your ccsm settings, 

General -> Desktop Size;

Keep Vertical size at 1, increase Horizontal size to any number you want.  For the cube, it's 4(horizontal):1(vertical).

Best of luck, hope this helps!
