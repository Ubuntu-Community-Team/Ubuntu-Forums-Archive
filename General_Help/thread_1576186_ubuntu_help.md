### Post by e79 on 2010-09-16
Indeed it is possible :

1. Download and burn a LiveCD
2. Boot your comp with it
3. Access your hard drive (should appear in Places --> Computer)
4. Copy the file to an external source and voila !
5. Reformat your comp with Ubuntu while at it  =P

Hope this helps.
