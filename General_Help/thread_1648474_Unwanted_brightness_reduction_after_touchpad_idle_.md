### Post by TBABill on 2010-12-18
Have you checked your power management settings to make sure nothing is set to dim the display when inactive?

---

### Post by Cpierce on 2010-12-18
TBABill is correct. go to system>preferences>screensaver then click on power management. Make sure "dim display when idle" is unchecked.
