and open up Firefox and go along to Privacy & Security and then click on

### Post by mikewhatever on 2021-08-27
Sure, select Custom under Tracking Protection, and then select "All Cookies...".
Here is more from Mozilla: [https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/block-websites-storing-cookies-site-data-firefox](https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/block-websites-storing-cookies-site-data-firefox)
