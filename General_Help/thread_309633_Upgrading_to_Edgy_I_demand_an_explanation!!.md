### Post by RAOF on 2006-11-29
If you **upgrade** to Edgy (using the update-manger, or changing your sources.list, or...) everything you have installed, and all your data should not be touched.  If you install Edgy from the CD - by this I mean fire up the LiveCD, and hit "Install" - then everything on the partition you install Edgy on will be wiped.

You've almost certainly successfully upgraded to Edgy.  You can check with System->Help->About Ubuntu.  It should say "Edgy", or perhaps "Ubuntu 6.10", I forget which :).
