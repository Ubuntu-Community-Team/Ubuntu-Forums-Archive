### Post by lariosa42 on 2010-05-24
I just downloaded Foxit and I want to set it as my default PDF reader.  However, when I right click, [FONT="Courier New"]choose Open With-->Other Applications[/FONT] and select Foxit, it just opens the PDF.  The next time I open a PDF, it opens in evince again.  I also tried [FONT="Courier New"]System-->Preferences-->Preferred Applications[/FONT], but there is no PDF option.  What else can I try?

Distro: Jaunty 9.10

---

### Post by Zemblan on 2010-05-24
IN the context menu you get from right-clicking on a pdf choose properties. In the window that opens click the "Open with" tab. Choose foxit.
