### Post by special ed on 2008-05-12
Honestly after 2 days of trying different things I stumbled upon it by accident.

First you need to find the Fusion icon. 
Applications-> System Tools (if it's not there you need to download it).

Launch it to panel and then in the panel, right click and then Select Window Decorator. Pick Emerald.
