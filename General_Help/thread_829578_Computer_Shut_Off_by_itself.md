### Post by tamoneya on 2008-06-15
heat was probably the reason then.  The first thing I try when having heat problems is I clean out any dust.  Dust results in less air flow and therefore increased heat.  The second thing I would do is add a additional case fan.  They are pretty cheap $5-10 USD and real simple to install.
