### Post by jonlemur on 2008-07-13
I got a chart on its own page just by cutting it and pasting it to another sheet.  Then to get minor axis tick marks on the log scale, I double clicked on the axis to select it, right clicked on the axis and selected "Object Properties"  I chose the "Scale" tab, checked the "Logarithmic Scale" box, and checked the Minor Interval Marks "Outer" box.  That put it in a log scale with minor tick marks.
