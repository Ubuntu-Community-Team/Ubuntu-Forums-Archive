### Post by yoshii on 2016-06-15
Sometimes downloaded PDF's can be filled out as forms (if the PDF display software supports it, and if the PDF itself has forms).  
You fill out the forms and then print them.  
You could always just print the PNG and then use an ink pen to fill in the gaps with handwriting.
