### Post by vnt87 on 2007-03-06
My favorite p2p app under Windows was Limewire. Upon switching to Ubuntu I was glad to hear that Limewire had a fork, called Frostwire which was almost identical to Limewire (both connect to the Gnutella network) but with torrent support. The search feature is a bit slower than Limewire Pro IMO.
There's a deb package for Ubuntu on their homepage: [http://www.frostwire.com/](http://www.frostwire.com/)
