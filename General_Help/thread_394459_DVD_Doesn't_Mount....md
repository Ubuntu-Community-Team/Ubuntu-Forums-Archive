### Post by acormack on 2007-03-30
All DVDs have a region number on the box. Europe is Region 2,  US is Region 1 etc.

DVD players/drives have firmware to control which region they can play - although many can be modified to be Region-Free.

Some drives llow you to switch region a numer of times (say 3 or 4) but are then locked and won't chamge region any more.  

I don't know if this is your problem - but it is one possibility.


Alec
