### Post by pytheas22 on 2008-06-26
It doesn't mean that it's impossible, but I've never heard of getting compiz to work on SiS.  It certainly didn't work for me.  I think the hardware is just not powerful enough.  As far as I know, only Intel, ATI and nvidia chips are supported.

It will run decently  on fairly old nvidia and ATI, however.  It works on an nvidia card that I bought new four years ago for sixty dollars, so I'd imagine that for as little as twenty euro you'd be able to find something.  Of course, if you have a laptop, that doesn't help you much.
