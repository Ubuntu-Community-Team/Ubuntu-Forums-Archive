### Post by colin.p on 2010-01-05
I had the same problem, more or less, in 9.04. It happened after fooling around with compiz settings, one day, and I would lose the bottom panel (the top one was fine)after clicking on the desktop switcher.
After several attempts to fix the problem, I uninstalled compiz and had no more issues with the panel disappearing.
I have since updated (not a clean install) to karmic and compiz was reinstalled by default. So far all is well, but I haven't changed any compiz settings and have all effects turned off (I found them pretty at first but have now found them irritating).
