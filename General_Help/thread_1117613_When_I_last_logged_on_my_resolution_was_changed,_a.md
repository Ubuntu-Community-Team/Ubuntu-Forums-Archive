### Post by Aizawa on 2009-07-28
Replying to this very old thread, because I solved it. Well, I solved it ages ago, but I thought it could help some poor soul.

Turns out the max resolution was lower than usual because I didn't turn on my screen until I had booted Ubuntu. In windows this never matters, that's why I didn't think of it until I made the connection.

So yeah, your screen better be on when you boot Ubuntu. :)
