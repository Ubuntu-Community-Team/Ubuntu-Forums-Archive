Your wifi hardware might need a proprietary driver that needs to be downloaded. So, if you connect your Ubuntu machine to Internet via *fixed* ethernet, after a few minutes Ubuntu hopefully will show a hardware icon in the upper right corner. If so, clicking on it will offer to download additonal drivers.
