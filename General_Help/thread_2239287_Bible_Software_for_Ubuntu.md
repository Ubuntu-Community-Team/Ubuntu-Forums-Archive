### Post by amanchesterman on 2014-08-13
I tried out various packages a few years back. From my experience, I suspect that what you want is not available.
I assume you've added the Crosswire PPA as instructed on the Xiphos website? That should give you the latest versions of Xiphos and Sword. But looking at the versions listed on Crosswire, they are -- as you say -- fairly old (2012). The Sword website says their developers are "working on it" but no doubt they have other things to do as well. If you want something that is really up-to-date, you may be out of luck.
