### Post by mlomker on 2005-11-07
Run **kdesu kcontrol** from the Run Command line or Konsole.  Go under Appearance & Themes and then Style.  You'll see a checkbox for the tooltips.

---

### Post by SGC on 2005-11-07
To turn them off, right click on the task bar and choose "configure panel", then in one of the tabs (maybe appearence, I'm using kde 3.5 which a little bit different) uncheck "Enable icon mouseover effects".

* See the screenshot, it looks similar to the version that cames with kubuntu.
