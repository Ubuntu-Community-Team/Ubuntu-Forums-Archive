### Post by coldraven on 2011-08-07
Remembering your router would not have helped, I could take my router and plug it in the house next door and nothing would happen. Your broadband is attached to your home phone line and nowhere else.
If you want to tether your phone to your laptop the see the phone's manufacturer specs for your model. I was investigating this for my own phone, trying to link via Bluetooth but found out that it will only link to a headset. The word missing in the specs was modem!
