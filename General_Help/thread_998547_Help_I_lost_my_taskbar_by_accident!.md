### Post by adult swim on 2008-11-30
right click the taskbar up top and select  new panel.  if its not on the bottom when it comes up you can drag it down there.  you may need to add a few things to it if shows up without your trash, you can do that by right clicking the new panel and selecting add to panel.  then choose what you need from the box that pops up.
