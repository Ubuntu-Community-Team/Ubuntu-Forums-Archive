### Post by yancek on 2014-09-03
Best not to use the computer for any reason until you can try to recover the data.  You can download testdisk from the site at the first link below and read the step-by-step instructions at the second link.  Best to use another computer to download from and burn the image to a CD.  The less you use the computer the better the chances you may recover data.  I believe the option you selected overwrote everything and you would have been better served by selecting the manual option (Something Else) where you could have selected the proper partition on which Zorin had been installed to install Ubuntu.  Good luck.
