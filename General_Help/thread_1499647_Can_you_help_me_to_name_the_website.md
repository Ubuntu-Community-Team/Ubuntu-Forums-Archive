### Post by James78 on 2010-06-02
If you do a search for the most popular keywords based on those names, you may find what website title is the best. The best website title would be a mix of the most popular keywords you can find (but don't make it a too popular keyword, because then there'll be too much competition and you'll get nowhere with the site, best thing to do is find a niche).

I like stressthat.com, but that's a bad title because it doesn't explain what it's stressing.

Likewise, testyourgadget.com and testyourdevice.com feel like they're conveying the wrong idea.

technocrash.com isn't one of the best either in my opinion. gadgetstress.com signifies stressing a gadget, so that feels like the best name too me. gadgetcrash.com, I might pick this one too, but again, it doesn't feel like it conveys the whole idea too me of the site, which is stressing gadgets.

And before thinking up names, do a domain name search, because the names you thought up are probably taken, as are SO MANY domain names (you won't believe how hard it is to find a name).

Good luck!
