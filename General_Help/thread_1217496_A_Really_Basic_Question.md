### Post by rbc on 2009-07-19
If you are talking about the colour scheme for terminal, open terminal, click Edit-->Profiles. Choose the Default profile (or create a new one), then click the Edit button. Then there should be a tab for Background and Colour

---

### Post by capngp on 2009-07-19
Thanks rbc! I was looking around the linux console everywhere and it was right
under my nose in the terminal edit preferences. I just chose the linux-terminal
scheme and my problem is solved.
Again, thanks a lot!
