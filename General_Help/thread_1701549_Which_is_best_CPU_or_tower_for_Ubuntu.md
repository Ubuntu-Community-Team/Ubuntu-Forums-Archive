### Post by lithopsian on 2011-03-06
You shouldn't have any trouble with any tower.  Laptops and netbooks are the main problems, because they have a lot more proprietary tweaks.  The very latest release of anything is always a risk since it may not be well supported by the kernel and or open source drivers yet.  Graphics cards are the biggest culprit.  For a while, onboard graphics cards were tricky but they seem fairly well supported now.  The latest bugbears seem to be with the dual-card laptops and I'd suggest avoiding anything like that.

Intel or AMD shouldn't make much difference.  Both are now very stable widely supported architectures.
