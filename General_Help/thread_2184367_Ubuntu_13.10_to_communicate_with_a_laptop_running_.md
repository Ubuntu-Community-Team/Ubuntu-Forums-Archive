### Post by Petro Dawg on 2013-10-28
Am I to understand that you only use the laptop for the WordPerfect program?  If so...

Have you tried running WordPerfect using a program called DosBox on your Linux machine?  I've had pretty good success running various DOS programs with it.  If you use WordPerfect on your Linux rig, that might keep you from having to transfer files from one computer to another.  Of course this may require that you have a floppy drive (that you can get to work with ubuntu) to install the program.
