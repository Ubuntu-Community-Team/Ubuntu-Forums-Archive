### Post by mcduck on 2013-12-22
Yes, there is subpixel font rendering, and it's enabled by default.

I personally think Ubuntu's implementation looks great, but as the font rendering is much a matter of taste (and, sadly, patents), you might prefer different settings. If you are not after subpixel rendering, but more specifically subpixel rendering with similar options as used in Windows, then you could try [Infinality]("http://www.webupd8.org/2013/06/better-font-rendering-in-linux-with.html").
