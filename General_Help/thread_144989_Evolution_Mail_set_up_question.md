Go to

Edit -> Preferences

And then set up your mail account. (I understand you have already done this, correct me if I am wrong).

Then when you click on "Send/Recieve" or click F9 then evolution will connect to your email, but will pop up a password box.

What I am trying to say is, when you click on send/recieve for the first time, you are prompted for the password, you can from there choose weather or not to save it.
