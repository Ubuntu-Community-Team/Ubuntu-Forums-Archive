### Post by lovinglinux on 2009-04-29
Whenever someone replies to your post, the thread is lifted to the top of the thread list and thus gets more attention. So when nobody replies, the original poster simply posts "Bump" because he has nothing to add to the thread, but wants to bump it to the top of the list.

As already said, is not cool to bump  your thread more than once a day.
