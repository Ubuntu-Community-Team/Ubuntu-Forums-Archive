Thank you!
If you don't see Add/Remove Programs go to System > Preferences > Main Menu

highlight Applications in the left pane and see if the Add/Remove Programs is there on the bottom on the right.

you can also search through Synaptic Package Manager for the program and uninstall it through there. (Add/Remove Programs is actually just a simpler interface for Synaptic.) -- Only have one open at a time.

look through this site for your questions on GRUB and the MBR: [http://users.bigpond.net.au/hermanzone/](http://users.bigpond.net.au/hermanzone/)
