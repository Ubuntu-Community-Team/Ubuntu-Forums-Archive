Your computer is telling you something is not going right with a program that you probably don't care about.  It is a service that runs in the background that will do things that you would likely want to do yourself.

I can't really discern the real meaning of that either.

This was one of the services that he mentioned can safely be disabled without the user having to worry about anything.
