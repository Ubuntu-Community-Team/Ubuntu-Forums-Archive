### Post by old_geekster on 2007-03-29
Make cetain that you have your burner set to burn an ISO.  Otherwise, it will not boot.  Also, use lower speeds to burn ISO's.  I usually burn at no more than 8X.

I used "CDBurner XP Pro", not a MS product.  It is free and has specific settings for burning ISO's.  It works great.

 When using Edgy, I use CD/DVD Burner "GnomeBaker".  Again, it has specific settings for burning ISO's.
