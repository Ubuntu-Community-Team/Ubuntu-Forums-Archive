### Post by PositiveK on 2011-10-27
I notice the same problem with Xubuntu 11.10 (Oneiric Ocelot).  I found that only a few of them obeyed Fitts Law with respect to the scrollbars and the titlebar buttons.

It seems that the "greybird" and "greybird-compact" "themes" (not "Style") fit the Fitts Law for the titlebar.

However, there's a more general problem with Xfce 4.8 and applications that use scrollbars on the right hand side.  These don't obey it.  I did find that the Chromium web browser does obey, though!
