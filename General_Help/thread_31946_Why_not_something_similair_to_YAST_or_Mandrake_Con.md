### Post by Xian on 2005-05-05
Those distros are already there if you desire that kind of environment. I'm not saying that it would not enhance Ubuntu to some extent and make it easier to config for some users, but to implement that and maintain it would take a lot of effort that IMO could be better put to use elsewhere. This is not a huge entity like Novell that can engage a staff of any size needed to push out a certain design, and so I just don't expect or want the Ubuntu devs to spend their already very limited resources on a project of that magnitude.
