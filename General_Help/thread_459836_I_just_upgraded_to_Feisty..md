### Post by sefs on 2007-05-31
When upgrading all your installed packages will be upgraded to the feisty versions of things.  Those are the one s installed thru synaptic.  I believe the incompatible ones may be removed by the upgrade though.  And then for the ones you installed manually they may or may not work, and others will need to be recompiled like vmware, and truecrypt and ndiswrapper if installed from source.  That to my mind is how i've observed things happen.
