### Post by bergfly on 2010-09-03
Have you tried saving the session? Get it like you want it, and then open the menu and choose --- system settings 
Choose the advanced tab, and then click on "session manager". At the bottom under "on login" Select "restore a manually saved session" and click APPLY

Now open the menu again, select leave and you should see a new icon in the top section called "save session"

Click this and then try a reboot and see what happens. Hope it works ;)
