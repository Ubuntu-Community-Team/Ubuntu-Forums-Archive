If you're developing solutions that MUST use IE9, then you seriously need to consider dual-booting using a native MS Windows 7 environment.

Why?

Because IE 10 is already out in Platform Preview 2 form. The Beta will be out soon.

And, to stay current, you probably need to be looking at how IE 10 affects the work you're doing.  And yes, you can have parallel, independent installations of IE 9 and IE 10.
