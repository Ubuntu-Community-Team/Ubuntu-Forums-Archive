### Post by audiomick on 2010-02-13
9.04 uses grub legacy, i.e. the old grub. Grub 2 started being used in 9.10, but only when it is a fresh install. An update from 9.04 retains the old grub.
There is info on grub here
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/GrubHowto](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/GrubHowto)

and grub2 here
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Grub2](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Grub2)
