### Post by drs305 on 2007-12-18
View, Standard Toolbar. There are two icons for spellchecking. One initiates a check and the other turns it on as you type.  Also, and this could be where you are having problems, I had to go to Tools, Options, Language Settings, Writing Aids and make several selections in the Writing Aids Dictionaries and Options blocks to get it to work.
