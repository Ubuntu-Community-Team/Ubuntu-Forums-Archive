### Post by dannystaple on 2008-03-21
For IRC, MSN and most other instant messaging systems, I find Pidgin is by far the best. As it runs across windows and Ubuntu, I can then have a consistent chat experience across all platforms.

Pidgin used to be known as Gaim. It is skinnable, it offers logging, and also triggering with automatic authentication on IRC servers. It is also part of the Ubuntu standard repository with a free (GPL I think) license.
