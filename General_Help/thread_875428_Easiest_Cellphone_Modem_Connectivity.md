### Post by Unanimated on 2008-07-30
Almost any unlocked phone that has Bluetooth and can get on the Web through a built-in browser can do it. Some carriers might require you to sign up for a service to allow the phone to do that, however. Many Motorola phones are unlocked, some great smartphones are unlocked (HTC Touch, for example). In fact, most of the phones that T-Mobile offers are unlocked.
