### Post by Morbius1 on 2013-01-18
There's the hard way and then there's my way :)

Install thunar, right click any pdf file, select "Open With Other Application", and you will see the attached screen.

As a side benefit you will get "Use a Custom Command" which you may remember from the olden tymes of Gnome - before the purge.

File associations created in Thunar apply everywhere else.
