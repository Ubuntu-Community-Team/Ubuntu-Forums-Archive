### Post by Mighty_Joe on 2009-09-09
Good choice. I've used JEdit for years as a professional programmer.  Sometimes Eclipse is just too much and I want to bang out some code without jumping through hoops.
As for themes, you can change the look and feel by clicking on the Utilities menu, selecting "Global Options" and selecting "Appearance".  There's also a couple of [plugins]("http://plugins.jedit.org/list.php?category=2") available, like LookAndFeel, which may help you out.
