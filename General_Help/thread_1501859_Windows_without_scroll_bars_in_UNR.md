### Post by kerry_s on 2010-06-04
so your in normal gnome now right?

what you can do is bring over some of the features from the netbook.
add **maximus** to your preferences-> startup applications
add the **window-picker-applet** to the panel
change the menu bar to main menu
you can drag & drop from menu to the desktop for quick launchers
etc..

gnome is really tweakable, you just got to decide what you want.

i did the right click on the menu & added the whole sections as a menu on the panel, once i got them on the panel i removed the main menu, creating my own netbook style launcher, added 2 menu sections "favorites & places" to complete it.

for the favorites you can drag & drop from other sections to favorites in the menu editor.
for the places, i had to add the entries manually.

i don't have my panel set to auto hide, but you could to get the max space.

you don't have to go that far though, just changing the menu bar is good enough. i only did it cause i hated the menus leading to other menus, that stair case effect.
