### Post by plucky on 2009-02-12
The first place to look is [Open Printing Database](http://www.linuxprinting.org/printer_list.cgi) to see if your printer has a working driver.You will need to search for the exact model number of your printer.

If it has,then that is the driver you need to install to get your printer working.
