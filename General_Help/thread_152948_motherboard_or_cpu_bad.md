### Post by dermotti on 2006-03-31
Try popping the battery out of the motherboard for a few minutes and put it back in. This will clear the bios, incase something in there got fubared. Its a long shot but worth a try.

Also, an easy way to see if your motherboard is screwed...look at the capacitors on the motherboard. Any of them leaking a rust colored substance?
