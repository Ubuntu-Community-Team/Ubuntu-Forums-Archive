### Post by inameiname on 2011-06-18
Well, if its an already-made launcher, such as one inside the Main Menu or on the desktop, its very easy. Just drag and drop ones from the desktop onto the the bottom panel. Or if its inside the main menu, just right click it and copy it to the desktop, and then drag and drop to the panel. 

Now, if its one you want to make from scratch, its not very hard either. Just rigtht click an area on the desktop and click Create Desktop Launcher (or whatever is similar to that) and then input the commands, and once your launcher on the desktop is done, drag and drop it to the panel, and delete the desktop version of it.
