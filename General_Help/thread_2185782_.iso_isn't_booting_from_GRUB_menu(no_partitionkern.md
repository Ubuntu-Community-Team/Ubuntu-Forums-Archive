### Post by mörgæs on 2013-11-07
If you suspect a failing hard disk it's even better to try a fresh install of 13.10. If that still does not work you have a strong case against the disk. 

You could also mount the 20 GB as master and the 15 GB as slave by changing cables, installing 13.10 on the master. When that works and all updates are applied you copy your user files to the new disk.
