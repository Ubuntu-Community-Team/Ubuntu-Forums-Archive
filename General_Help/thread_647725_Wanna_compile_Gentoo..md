### Post by tomchuk on 2007-12-23
If you've got time and are looking for a learning experience, [Linux From Scratch](http://www.linuxfromscratch.org/) is what you want. Yes, everything in Gentoo is compiled from scratch, but it's all handled by Portage, and you can bootstrap a Gentoo system without learning a single thing. LFS is a fantastic learning experience and much more rewarding (at least in my experience) than Gentoo.
