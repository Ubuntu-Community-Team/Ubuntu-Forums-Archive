### Post by teeleef on 2011-02-08
Hi All,  do I need some assistance!

I have a Officejet 8500 All in One wireless printer that works flawlessly, however a work colleague (and Ubuntu user for 2yrs) has recently purchased an Officejet 6500 Wireless All in One.  

I went round and set the printer up for him.  As I did with my own printer I gave the printer a static IP Address which is set up on the printers 'network interface'.  Then using the HPLIP Gui it searched for the wireless device and found it 3mtrs from the router...and printed a test page successfully.  For weeks now, printing wirelessly for him is now a hit and miss affair.  I have checked the CUPS settings.  I have reinstalled the printer 5 times with initial positive results but failure later on.  

Can anyone give me a clue as to where I am going wrong.  I visited his house reinstalled the printer, checked the ipaddress, switched off the pc and printer.  Restarted all, and it prints.  Days later after not being in use it won't print!  His desktop does say that the print job has been sent to the printer but no end result!   HELP!!!!

Teeleef

### Post by teeleef on 2011-02-12
UPDATE.

It turns out it is the printer!   The HP 6500 does not wake form 'sleep' mode irrespective of the OS, hence printer on minute, sleep mode kicks in then fails to awaken and print.

Unsure whether HP have released a firmware update to fix the bug?

So if you are after a printer...get the Officejet 8500, it's fantastic!


T
