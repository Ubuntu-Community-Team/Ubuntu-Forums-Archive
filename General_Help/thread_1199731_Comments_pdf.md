### Post by hugmenot on 2009-06-30
There is [some discussion here]("http://groups.google.com/group/comp.text.tex/browse_thread/thread/8b3c2956e33112f1/56ebfc2e08799d77")


My suggestion would be to forego the in-text annotation toys and make your teacher comment point by point in an email referring to [pages and line numbers]("http://people.bu.edu/rking/JME_files/DraftExample.pdf") instead. This will also force him to make substantial comments and not nitpick about small irrelevant details.
