### Post by cugat on 2010-10-21
I listen to Sirius through VLC. After you install that, you'll need the [mediaplayerconnectivity]("https://addons.mozilla.org/firefox/addon/446") add-on for Firefox. There is a config wizard where you can choose what player you want for various media. In the last step of the wizard, I have "smart play" checked (that may be the default, I forget) and when you log into Sirius, you get a pop-up. Click the link and VLC should open and start playing.

Something I only discovered recently--close the Sirius player window and it will play uninterrupted as long as you want (provided you don't want to change the station). If you leave the Sirius player window open, you will get the annoying "Are you still listening" prompt every 30 minutes or so. 

HTH
