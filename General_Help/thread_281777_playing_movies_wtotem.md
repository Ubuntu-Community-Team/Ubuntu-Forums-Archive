### Post by Bloch on 2006-10-21
.mov is a proprietary format (just like mp3 is) and cannot be distributed freely with ubuntu.
You need to install the codecs. Automatix does a good job of that.
 
Some players (like VLC and possibly xine) come with these codecs already.

---

### Post by foxmulder881 on 2006-10-21
As mentioned by Bloch above, install the codecs and you won't have any issues with playing .MOV files with Totem, along with many other formats.
