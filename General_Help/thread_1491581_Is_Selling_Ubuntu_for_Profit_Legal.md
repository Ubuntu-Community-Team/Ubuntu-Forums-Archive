### Post by James78 on 2010-05-23
Considering that he's selling a CD of it, I believe it is legal. Despite popular belief, the GPL DOES allow you to sell software, just within it's license. You can sell the software for example if you took your time and burned it to some CD's you bought. It's also legal to redistribute a program you bought, if it's under the GPL. Nothing they can do!
