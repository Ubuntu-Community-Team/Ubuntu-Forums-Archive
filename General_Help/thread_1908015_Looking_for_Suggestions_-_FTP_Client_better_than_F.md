### Post by bcrcontractor on 2012-01-12
I work with both Ubuntu and Mac OS X so cross platform is high on my list.  I like AnyClient.  It's free, platform independent and supports FTP, FTPS, SFTP and Amazon S3.  It also has a web based version that's great for when I am on the road.

[http://www.jscape.com/products/file-transfer-clients/anyclient/](http://www.jscape.com/products/file-transfer-clients/anyclient/)
