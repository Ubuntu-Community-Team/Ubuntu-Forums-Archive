### Post by TBABill on 2012-05-01
I'm not sure in LibreOffice, but in MS Office you right click the cell or cell range, click format cells, then choose "date" and select the proper locale "English (U.K.)" and it will switch from 1/17/2000 (US way of showing the date) to 17/1/2000, which is what you are looking for. I'm not sure what your default country locale is that comes up, but if you choose a different one it may give you options formatted as you have it.
