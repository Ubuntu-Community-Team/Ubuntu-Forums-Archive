This is my first post, although I've been toying with Ubuntu since Warty, and I've no idea if I post this question here or elsewhere.

This is really no biggee, but I've trained myself like a monkey to drag links from the Firefox url bar to specific folders I keep on my toolbar.  (I'm in grad school, and for research purposes I keep links of articles in specified folders and sync it all between my laptop and desktop.)

But for whatever reason, 90% of the time Firefox will not let me drag and drop into a folder on my toolbar.  *Sometimes* it does, but usually it doesn't.  I once thought it was because I had too many boomarks on my toolbar --some were dropping off the side --so I fixed that and bam, I could drag and drop again.  The next time I fired it up, though, it wasn't happening, even though there was enough space.

I've combed the Firefox forums and generally came up blank.  I don't recall this happening in KDE, either, only Gnome, but I may be mistaken.

Any clues?

------------
EDIT:

Great googly moogly... I've been looking for a solution for this for weeks, and 10 minutes after I finally post, I seem to find it.  If anyone's wondering, go to View > Toolbars > Customize and "Restore Default Set."  

So far, that's done it for me.
