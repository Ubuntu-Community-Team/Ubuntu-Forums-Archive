### Post by scragar on 2008-11-08
Hmn, if it's the panel itself that's gone you can make another if that's ok, right click a black space on the top one, and choose new panel.

Then you'll want to add stuff to it, the first thing you'll want is called the "Window list" and you can add this by right clicking on the panel and scrolling to the bottom, then either double click it, or click it once to highlight it, then click add. Close the box when you are done.

### Post by Idefix82 on 2008-11-08
To re-create the panel, right click on the upper panel, click "new panel" or something like that, place it, where you want (probably on the bottom), now right lick on the new panel and click "Add...", now choose what you want to have on the panel. By default, you had the trash on the right, workspace switcher next to it, the "show desktop" button on the left and the window list next to that. But of course you can add anything you want.
