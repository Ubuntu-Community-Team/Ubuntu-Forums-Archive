### Post by mcduck on 2010-02-24
Yes, and it's already installed.

When in the Print dialog just select the "Print to File" as your printer, and set the output format to PDF (it supports PDF, PS and SVG).

There's also the cups-pdf package that has bit more features, but for most purposes this works just fine.
