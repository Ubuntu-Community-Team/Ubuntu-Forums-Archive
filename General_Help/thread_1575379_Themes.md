### Post by themusicalduck on 2010-09-15
The text thingy on the right is called Conky. It should be in the repository. You need to put a different configuration file in your home space to theme it.

The music controls on the panel is similar to Panflute [http://www.omgubuntu.co.uk/2010/03/gnome-music-applet-dead-long-live-panflute/](http://www.omgubuntu.co.uk/2010/03/gnome-music-applet-dead-long-live-panflute/)

Look for glossy themes on Gnome Look - [http://gnome-look.org/](http://gnome-look.org/) You can also find Conky themes on there.
