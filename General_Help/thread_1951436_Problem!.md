### Post by QIII on 2012-04-02
If I might make a comment or two needicla --

The first thing is that you do not need to be a sys admin to use Linux.  You have to disabuse yourself of the expectation that Linux is Windows.

The second thing is tht software vendors are responsible for how easy (or not) their software is to install.  Adobe has a nice installer for Windows that installs Flash and then keeps track of updates.  They do not have that for Linux.  Worse yet, they are about to discontinue Flash for Linux.  That does not mean there is something wrong with Linux itself.  But we are often left outside in the cold in a Windows oriented world, and we must often make do.  That being the case, there is nothing at all wrong with choosing not to use Linux.  Use what works for you.

Finally, you must understand that even though you and many others come here with problems, there are millions of users who don't come here at all because things work and they don't need to.  That does not diminish your problem.  It is important to all of us to help you sort it out.

Wubi is the sort of thing that has its own set of issues.  It could be that some smart user in Islamabad will happen by your post after he gets off work and will be able to give you just the right answer.  Or not.  It may take a few tries for others to make some educated guesses to sort it out with you.
