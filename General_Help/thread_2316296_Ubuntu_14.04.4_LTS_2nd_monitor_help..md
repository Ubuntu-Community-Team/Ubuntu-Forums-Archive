### Post by QIII on 2016-03-06
Hello!

If this is a desktop, not all desktop boards (few, actually) allow for simultaneous use of on-board and dedicated graphics.  You will have to check your motherboard's documentation.  Most BIOS/EFI menus will give you the choice of one or the other, but not both.
