> **Cyriz said:**
> I realize that, but what I mean is that its not allowing me to talk to anyone with yahoo, while I am on msn. I cant even see my yahoo contacts
In which case you haven't configured your Yahoo! Messenger settings properly. Either that, or the service is down. 

It works like Trillian, in that you should be able to see all your contacts whether they are MSN users, Yahoo! users, Google Talk users etc. When you open up Pidgin you should be connected to all the chat services that you've setup, all at the same time. 

When I connect to PIdgin I can have a window open talking to a contact who's using MSN, while at the same time have another window open and talking to a user who's using MSN.
