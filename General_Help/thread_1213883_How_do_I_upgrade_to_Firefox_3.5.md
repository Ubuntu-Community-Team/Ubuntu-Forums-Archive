### Post by NilsE on 2009-07-15
> **Cheater87 said:**
> Its been weeks and the 3.5 upgrade has not come out in the update manager for ubuntu yet. I have no idea how to do a manual update. When will it be avialible to download or how can I update it manually???
It is in the repo but it will not appear automatically in update manager since it is a new install and not an upgrade to 3.0.

You can go into Synaptic and search on firefox-3.5 and select it to install.  It will take your 3.0 profile and your off and running.  You can then look in your applications and you will see an entry for 3.5 but it may be called Shiretoko. You can also create a launcher with firefox-3.5 as the command.

There are a variety of threads on how to make it your default.
