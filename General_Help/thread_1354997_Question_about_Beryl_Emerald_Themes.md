### Post by heppj on 2009-12-15
First, ensure that you have Emerald Theme Manager installed:

Go to Synaptic Package Manager (System>Administration>Synaptic Package Manager) and search for "Emerald" and mark for installation.

Once Emerald is installed, it's simply a matter of downloading the theme, opening Emerald, clicking the "Import" button, and opening the theme.
