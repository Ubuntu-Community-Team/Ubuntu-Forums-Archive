### Post by Schrute Farms on 2010-07-25
I wish I could be of help, but I think you might be screwed.

The more you use your system after your trash is emptied, the greater chance that a new file will overwrite the deleted one, then a program like photorec will not be able to recover anything.  Programs like those usually look directly at the disk, ignoring any file table information, to find any deleted files.  I used photorec once to recover a bunch of deleted pictures on an old DirectCD CD-RW.  I didn't write anything to the disk after my mistake, and photorec recovered everything.

I don't use Shotwell, so I don't know what could have gone on with that.

Good luck.
