### Post by utsuprainfra on 2008-06-09
If you restart with keyboard buttons other than the power button it will be your video card / software.  See if you can make beeps in a terminal (ctl-alt-f1 then ^G^G^G) and if so you know that it's the video card.  Or try a known good card / display.

This would be my first approach.
