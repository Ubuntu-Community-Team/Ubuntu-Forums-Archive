### Post by blazerw on 2008-11-07
To get Hotmail to work, apparently, you can change your "vendor" value to "Firefox" and it lets you in and works fine.

Here's how:
type "about:config" into the Awesome Bar, press enter.
In the filter, type "vendor"
Double click on "general.useragent.vendor" and change the value to "Firefox".
Click OK, try Hotmail.

Don't forget to change it back for visiting other sites. Give Ubuntu credit.

Dear Angelic_fruitcake.

I found for me a solution to prevent Firefox reverting back to Ubuntu in general.useragent.vendor.
Open as root (sudo nautilus)  the text file _usr/lib/firefox-3.0.3/defaults/preferences/**ubuntu-useragent.js**_ and change in the line:

pref ("general.useragent.vendor", "**Ubuntu**");
to
pref ("general.useragent.vendor", "**Firefox**");

close and save text file

worked fine for me
