### Post by taurus on 2008-11-06
You cannot resize the partition(s) that you are using.  Therefore, running gparted in Ubuntu would not work if you want to resize it.  So, you need to run gparted from a LiveCD.  Any reason why your machine won't boot from a LiveCD?  

Of course, you can always use GParted LiveCD to resize your harddrive.

[http://gparted.sourceforge.net/index.php](http://gparted.sourceforge.net/index.php)
