### Post by Dreamer Fithp Apprentice on 2012-03-12
Do you mean that the stuff that should be spread out over your whole monitor is only covering a rectangle that runs only part way down and part way to the right leaving a lot of unused space at the bottom and along the right edge? That's what I get out of looking at the picture. If that isn't the issue you might need to explain it a little more. If that is the issue I'd try fiddling with the actual physical controls on your monitor. Mine, for instance has a sort of round rocker button below the glass area (it's a crt) in the center and by pressing it in different patterns I can get menus that let me adjust things like that. I'd also look under the Adminiatration menu to see if you have some sort of software for tweaking settings of your video card and poke around in whatever is there if you have something like that. I know that is kind of vague. Details would be different on different hardware anyway. It wouldn't surprise me if someone gives you a better answer but these are things you can try in the meantime and they might even work.
