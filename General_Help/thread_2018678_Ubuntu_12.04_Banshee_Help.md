### Post by Paddy Landau on 2012-07-07
> **AbhiKap said:**
> ... a box appears saying python v2.7 needs to install...
Python should be installed already, but if not, just open Ubuntu Software Centre, search for *python* and install it.

> **AbhiKap said:**
> are there any good website to download free music?
No. There is precious little free music available — even unknown composers have to buy food and lodgings!

You can, however, find sites that stream music in exchange for you listening to advertisements (after all, they have to pay for the music, and if you won't, the advertisers will).

I have occasionally seen sites that allow you to download music for a small monthly fee.
