### Post by Rhubarb on 2009-11-03
The grub menu for grub2 is not shown by default (unless you're dual booting of course).

If you wish to see the grub menu to recover or run memtest, simply press and hold the **shift** button on your keyboard during bootup.

There's a good wiki page about grub2 that'll tell you how to configure it to your liking here:
[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Grub2](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Grub2)

Grub2 is quite nice once you get used to it :)
