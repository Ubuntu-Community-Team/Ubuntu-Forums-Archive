### Post by ptn107 on 2009-04-02
During the vista install you can tell it to erase the partition table and start anew.  When you get to the partitioning screen choose 'Drive options (advanced)' at the bottom which will bring you to this screen (see attachment).  Just remove all listed partitions till none are left and click 'next'.
