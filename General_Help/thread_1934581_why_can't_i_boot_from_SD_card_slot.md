### Post by BBQdave on 2012-03-02
> **hanzj said:**
> How come our Toshiba laptop computer will  allow booting from USB but not from SD card slot? Is there a way to let us boot from SD slot?

Depends on how your bios are set up.  When first starting your laptop, enter bios configuration and see if there is a choice of booting from your SD card slot.

In general, most laptops have default setting in bios of booting from CD (if you have a CD drive).  Might be your best option, for safely booting and using *live* mode and/or *install*.
