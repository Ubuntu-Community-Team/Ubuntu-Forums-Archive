### Post by Nico-dk on 2011-02-28
I assume she's not a poweruser, and just need an OS for browsing and reading email.
I recently migrated my 58 year old mom to Ubuntu.

My 'recipe'
> Make her part of a usergroup with almost no exec rights
> Create a superuser for myself (with remote access)
> Hide everything in the panels
> install Gnome-Do (invaluable for those remote access sessions)
> Move windows buttons to the right
> Show her around Firefox
> Show her around Open office (she still calls with help for this)
> Create links and place them on the desktop to the following dirs: documents, music, pictures

She uses Gmail so no need to setup any email clients.
She can now browse her fave sites, do random searches and watch all the YT vids she likes ... and the weekly calls about viruses has stopped.

If all else fails, try this theme: [http://ubuntu.online02.com/node/14](http://ubuntu.online02.com/node/14)
... or perhaps this distro: [http://www.zorin-os.webs.com/](http://www.zorin-os.webs.com/)
