Gully, get into the bios. Look around for a setting that says, restore bios defaults. You can also select restore setup defaults. I would chose setup, but either will work. Select that, and that only, then reboot. When it reboots, get into the bios again, and change your boot order to CDRom FIRST, then hard drive SECOND, then floppy THIRD and forget the rest. Put your CD in the drive and reboot. Your system should automatically boot to the CD at that point. Hope this helps...
