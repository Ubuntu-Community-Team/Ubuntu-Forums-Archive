### Post by mb_webguy on 2008-09-12
Crossover Office is a commercial version of Wine, originally optimized for installing MS Office.  It will work equally well in Gnome or KDE.

However, MS Office 2003 will currently run just fine on Wine, so I personally don't see any sufficient advantage of Crossover Office over Wine to justify the expense.
