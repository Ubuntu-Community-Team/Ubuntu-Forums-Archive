### Post by howefield on 2013-11-01
Not quite so simple to upgrade from an end of life version as 10.10 is.

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/EOLUpgrades](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/EOLUpgrades)

Once you sort out the repositories, you would need to go sequentially through the steps to 11.04 > 11.10 > 12.04.

Might be easier and quicker to do a fresh install of 12.04.3 over your current install.

As to which version you need, the desktop is the full install including graphical interface, the server edition is pretty much the same except it does not have a graphical desktop.
