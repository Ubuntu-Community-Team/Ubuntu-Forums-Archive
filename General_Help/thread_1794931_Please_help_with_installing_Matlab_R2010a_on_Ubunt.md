### Post by lovelinuxfreedom on 2011-07-02
If your PC work with the DVD. Then u may get a DVD partition mount in  /media. It may appear on your desktop or you may find it through places. 
We assume the dvd mount at /media/matlab. Then open on terminal, type the code "sudo /media/matlab/install", click "enter". Normally  a gui installation appearance may appear, install it with some clicks. May it help u!:)
