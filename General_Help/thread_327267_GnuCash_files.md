### Post by drpaul on 2006-12-28
If you put your gnucash data file in a subdirectory of your home dir, then gnucash will put all those files in that sub dir. Those are records of the operations you perform during your sessions with gnucash and can make it possible to recover some data if something happens to your data file in the future.
