### Post by HermanAB on 2007-06-25
Nope - no need.  Windoze viruses don't run on Linux and there are no Linux viruses at present.  There was one a few years ago, but Linus had to modify the kernel to make it work!

Linux is a good platform for running a mail scanner to provide clean mail to Windows users.  For this reason there are very good virus and spam scanners for Linux, eg ClamAV and Spamassassin, but you don't need it for your own protection.

Cheers,

Herman
