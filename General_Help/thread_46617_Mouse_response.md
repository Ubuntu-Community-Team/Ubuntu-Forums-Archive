### Post by virgule on 2005-07-05
yes.. for GNOME, there is **gnome-mouse-properties** This is invokable from a terminal as well as from the System menu in Preferences as **Mouse** item... Options you want are in 'Motion' tab.

for KDE its in the Control Center -> Peripherals -> Mouse -> 'Advanced' tab. KDE also has **kmousetool** thus I have never actually put it into use myself I have seen a few interesting options in there...

I found the KDE's way to be, by far, more complete and feature rich. GNOME seam to focus on simplicity and ease of use more than KDE.. I hope this answered your question 8)
