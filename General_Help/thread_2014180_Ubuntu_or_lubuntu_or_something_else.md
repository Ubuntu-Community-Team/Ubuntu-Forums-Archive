### Post by sudodus on 2012-07-01
Download the iso files of both flavours:

- Lubuntu with the ultra-light desktop environment LXDE
- Xubuntu with the light desktop environment XFCE

and ***try*** them live booted from the CD or USB drive before deciding what to install.

If you have less than 512 MB RAM, maybe you should download Knoppix and try that live too be deciding.

*Edit:*
- Yes you can easily get rid of the old Ubuntu version. But remember to ***backup*** your personal data before you wipe it!

- The old gnome-2 is not actively developed and maintained, and gnome-3 needs more horsepower, this is why LXDE and XFCE are suggested. 

- Lubuntu and Knoppix are distributed with some light-weight application programs. And you can easily remove programs you don't want, and install your favourite programs.
