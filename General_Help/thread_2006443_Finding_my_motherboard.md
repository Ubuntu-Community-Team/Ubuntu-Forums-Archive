### Post by Mark Phelps on 2012-06-19
Laptop sellers use highly-customized motherboards that are designed and built for them to resell in their laptops.  Such motherboards are not available for retail sale -- which is why they generally will NOT tell you the make and model motherboard.

Would help if you tell us WHY you need the info on your motherboard.
