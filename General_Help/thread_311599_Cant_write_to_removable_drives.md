### Post by sitedesign on 2006-12-03
After writing the data to the Memory card did you right click and say eject or just remove it?

If you did not eject it from the OS then the data may have been corrupted.

Make sure you reboot the computer before you start.

Quick answer will be to try another card, format it then write some files then right click on it and eject after it has finished try taking the card out then plug it back in and check it.
