### Post by Miljet on 2010-07-07
Your question(s) are a little confusing to me. Editing the source list has to do with telling the system which repositories to download packages from.

As far as setting your default home web page, open Firefox, Click on Edit and then Preferences. You can set your home page there.

You can select your favorite background by clicking on System->Preferences->Appearance.
