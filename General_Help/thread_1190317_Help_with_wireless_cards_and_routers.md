Hi.

You can buy any wireless router. Routers are platform independent means it doesn't matter if you have windows, linux or unix installed on your computer.
You can go for Linksys, Netgear or any brand but for wireless adapter you need to get the surety for the manufacture that they are providing the drivers for Linux or Linux based OS.

One more thing if your laptop already have a built in wireless adapter then you don't have to buy another one.
