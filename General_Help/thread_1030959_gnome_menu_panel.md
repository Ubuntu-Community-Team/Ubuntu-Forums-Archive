### Post by fubbleskag on 2009-01-04
right-click on the bottom panel and choose "New Panel", drag it to the top if needed, and then right-click on it to add items to it.

### Post by RedPhoenix on 2009-01-04
I got that, but is there a way to get the word drop down menus back?  or getting the original menu panel back once it is deleted?

---

### Post by semi_fiction on 2009-01-04
Right-click the panel, select 'Add to Panel', scroll down and select 'Menu Bar', then click 'add'. Move it to the left side, right-click it and check 'lock to panel.'
