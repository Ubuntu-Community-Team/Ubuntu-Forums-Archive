### Post by davidmohammed on 2010-06-05
little confused on what you are trying to do.

Generally wine just sees whatever you can see in nautilus.  Therefore - can you open that network share in nautilus?

Can you see that same share name in /media/<share> ?

when you've got a mount to that network share, from within a wine application such as notepad, you browse to the network share via open - / - media - <partition e.g. share name>
