Hi all...

This card died, so I replaced it with a Geforce 9800GT, which is dual DVI.  Since then, I have been unable to get the second monitor to display anything.  Nvidia-settings sees the first monitor just fine, referencing the make and model.  The second one, however, is seen as simply "CRT-1", with a max resolution of 1360x768 (the recognized, identical monitor shows 1920x1080).

I have used EnvyNG to remove and replace the Nvidia driver (180.44), which correctly identifies the card.

It sure seems to me like the problem is one of recognition on the second DVI port/Monitor (I have tried all combinations of monitors and DVI ports, and they all work fine individually).

I generated a new xorg.conf via Nvidia settings, which appears at the bottom of this post.

I have read an awful lot of dual monitor howtos, and they all seem to work with VGA/DVI (and, of course, they worked great for me previously!).  I have also seen a lot of posts from people who have dual DVI working, so I can't help but think this is possible!
