### Post by Ceyx on 2011-01-07
A quick and easy fix is to go to the top panel ( the one with Applications Places, etc ) and right click on an empty space. Select "add to panel" and you should see a "Brightness Applet".  Click add, and it will install a little slider that you can use.....

There are other ways, but this is by far the easiest.
