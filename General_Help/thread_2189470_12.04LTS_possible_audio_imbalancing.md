### Post by grier-devon on 2013-11-22
An easy way to test if the system is not pushing the sound correctly would be by testing other speakers. I am assuming you mean that the built in speakers are the ones giving you problems. plug in some external speakers or headphones and see if the problem persist even there and if so then you are right. If not then you just have a blown speaker. Let us know the results so we can better assist, if your speaker is blown I am sure a replacement speaker would not be to costly through a online source.

### Post by grier-devon on 2013-11-22
If it crackles then blown, but I would also if possible look at the connection to that speaker cause if it is not as loud then it is probably not receiving enough current which means something might have damaged it or it came lose. If you would have heard something in your head phones then I would have said it is a software problem but since this is not the case I am ruling in it is a hardware issue.
