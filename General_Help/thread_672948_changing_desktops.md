### Post by rosegarden78 on 2008-01-20
Beryl and Compiz have merged as of Gutsy Gibbon.  Those are the former programs capable of producing those effects on high end graphics cards.  It will not work on an Intel915 ie. it crashed under Edgy.  Now it does work on the Intel915 with Ubuntu Gutsy.  Only you're not going to get the cube thing.

I actually got it working and I must admit ... the effects get real tired very quickly.

I've evolved into (X)Ubuntu with kdm and xfce added from the repos.  Then I select XCFE Session and login.  I launch nautilus by pressing Alt+F2 and that is my ideal system.  Zero eye candy (but Plastik themes) with icon picture previews (Nautilus) and features Ubuntu.  Do you want cube rotations or multi-tasking with speed?

Settings - Keyboard Shortcuts to choose key combos for changing desktops.  Look for window one desktop to the right, and cycle one desktop to the right.  While exciting on video I assure you that 3d cube effect was overrated and tiring and quite useless.

Not to be a buzzkill...you should be able to run a LiveCD of Edgy Efy and install beryl from the repos if it's still there.  At least you could try it.  But you're not missing anything.  Trust me.

### Post by magump on 2008-01-20
I think I can help regarding more desktops.  If I understand your question properly, try to right click on the "square" in the lower task bar and select "Preferences".  The  Workspace Switcher Preferences window should open up.  Under Workspaces, if you are only running two columns, change the number to 6 or as many  as you wish.
