### Post by ozzman2 on 2010-07-10
Hey Everyone!

I realize this is more of a "Linux" thing than an "Ubuntu" thing, but I wanted to give something back to these forums (for all the help it's given me).

The image is 640x480px and in 256 colors.  If your ASUS motherboard supports Mylogo 2, you can flash it into your BIOS as the motherboard splash screen.  I think it's pretty cool to see this the moment you power on your machine (moments before it goes into the Ubuntu splash screen).

It's a retro thing...  ;)
