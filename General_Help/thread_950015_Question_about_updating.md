### Post by GuitarRocker2562 on 2008-10-16
If you download and burn to a disk and try to reinstall over your system (a clean install) then you WILL lose your files, unless they are on a seperate /home partition. If you just open Update Manager, you will see an upgrade to Intrepid button when it was released, using that button to upgrade WILL NOT delete your files. However, like Windows SP updates, there is an extremly small risk of something going terribly wrong, which means an even smaller chance of losing your data, your pretty safe though.
