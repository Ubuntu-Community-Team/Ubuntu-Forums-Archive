### Post by cjax1 on 2007-05-24
Hi,

I started a thread a few days ago because of FireFox crashing. Found out it's Flash version 9 causing all the problems. My post here:
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=447473]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=447473")

It doesn't seem to matter if it's on FireFox on Linux or Windows or even IE. Flash version 9 is crashing browsers. As I said in my other post I deleted a couple files in my home directory in .mozilla/plugins. I found out when I delete the file called libflashplayer.so flash is disabled and FF works good. I happened to have and old downloaded version 7 of Flash Player. I didn't go through the install process but just unpacked it and copied and pasted the libflashplayer.so from version 7 and put it in the plugins folder. Now I have flash and FF works good. But I have already come across a site that could not display flash but told me I need to download the latest flash. I think it said I needed at least Flash version 8 which I don't have. I hope Adobe gets this fixed soon.
