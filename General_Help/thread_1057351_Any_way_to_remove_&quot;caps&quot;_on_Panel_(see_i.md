### Post by puscifer919 on 2009-02-01
It's unchecked. If you look closely, those aren't the hide buttons, but the boxes you can click on to drag the panel to the left or right against the top of the screen. Checking that "Show hide buttons" box enables the hide buttons which appear over top of these boxes.
