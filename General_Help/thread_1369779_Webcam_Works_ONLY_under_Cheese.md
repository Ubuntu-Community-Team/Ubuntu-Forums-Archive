### Post by fragos on 2010-01-01
Cheese does a great job of finding your webcam. Other applications may require additional manual configuration. One issue with webcam configuration is that other devices like TV cards can confuse issues because a number of devices mount as /dev/video{#}. To make matters worse video devices don't alway mount with the same {#}. I use additional UDEV rules to my specific video devices under additional mount points which are then configured into the various applications like tvtime.
