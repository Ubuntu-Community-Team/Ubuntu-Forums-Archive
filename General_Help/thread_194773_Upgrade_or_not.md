### Post by CronoDekar on 2006-06-11
Ubuntu has an unorthodox method to their version numbers.  Basically it's (Year-2000).Month of when it goes stable.  So basically there's not necssarily a larger difference from 5.10 -> 6.06 than there was 5.04 -> 5.10.
