### Post by Mazate on 2012-08-14
Is there a way to get the Unity bar to do what it used to do before 12.04 which is to hide by default while an application is open, but it'll pop out when you move your mouse over?  I'd love to be able to go back to that if anyone knows how its done.  Thanks in advance.

---

### Post by ubudog on 2012-08-14
Hi Mazate, I think the closest you will get to that is turning on the auto-hide feature.  This can be done by opening up System Settings, clicking on "Appearance", then the "Behavior" tab, and then enabling the auto-hide feature.

You may need to adjust the "Reveal sensitivity" bar to fit your needs.

ubudog
