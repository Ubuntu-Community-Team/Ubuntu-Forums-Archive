### Post by blkno1 on 2006-11-10
I got it working!!!  :-D 

I compiled and installed vstream-client.
I then compiled and installed mplayer from sources.  With vstream-client present it enables it.  Thus allowing me to now stream my Tivo recorded shows  to my Edgy Box.
