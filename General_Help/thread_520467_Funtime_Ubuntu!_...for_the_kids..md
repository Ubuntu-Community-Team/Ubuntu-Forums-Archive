### Post by mbeierl on 2007-08-08
I "recycled" my wife's old laptop and put ubuntu on it when the hard drive crashed.  I loaded Firefox, Thunderbird and OpenOffice and gave it to my kids (ages 6 through 13).  They didn't bat an eye, but seamlessly went at it.  The learning curve for them (to do homework) was minimal.  Gaim works well for IM, and of course, install the non-free Flash player and all the multimedia codecs.

Oh - don't forget - load all the games from the repos.  That was a huge selling factor for the kids.
