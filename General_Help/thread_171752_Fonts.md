### Post by rcarring on 2006-05-07
Breezy will happily use true type fonts. I installed several Microsoft fonts into my font folder by copying them, opening Nautilus, typing CTRL-L and then typing fonts:/// in the location bar. Then I just pasted the new fonts into the font folder.

By default Breezy gives you 99 fonts, and you do not need to reboot the system to make the new fonts available to Open Office etc.

You can even use the new true type fonts as the display font on your desktop.
