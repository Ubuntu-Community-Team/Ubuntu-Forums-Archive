### Post by Sylos on 2012-09-17
Oridnarily most of the drivers for standard components will be continaed as modules within the linux kernel - so downloading them independently is not required. The best bet is to put a live ubuntu CD in the drive and boot into it (usually labled as "Try Ubuntu First" or something similar). Then you can see what works and what doesnt.
