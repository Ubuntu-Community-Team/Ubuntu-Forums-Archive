### Post by lukjad on 2008-07-13
Does it show you the documents you were working on or just your desktop? Does it show you it's folders? I thing that the lock screen function is tied into your screensaver. So try to change your screensaver to something else and then lock your screen. Does it still show everything? Try a screensaver that has a picture or background of a dark colour. It could just be the fact of having a screensaver that uses the desktop as it's image. I had one that grabbed my desktop and made whorls on it. That may be your problem.

Hope this helps!
