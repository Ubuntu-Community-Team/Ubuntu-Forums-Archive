### Post by ResQue on 2012-07-22
So i updated to Ubuntu 12.04 LST, and two new features that really annoy the hell our of me:

1. When you have a muti monitor (twin view / side by side) setup, and you enable:
System Settings > Appearance > Behavior > Auto-hide the Launcher, you get the most annoying feature (or maybe a bug) that your mouse will stick within the Unity  hot spot region until you wiggle your mouse free.

2. If like me you run multi monitors at different resolutions and you drag a GUI window from monitor a to monitor b, and this window is bigger that the visible area of monitor b. When you double click the title bar of this window to make is fill the whole screen, it will jump back to monitor a.

I am not really sure if you would call these bugs, they are more like annoying features and the reason i posted them here is to hopeful get this issues fixed or at lest some clarification as to why.

I feel bad that my first post on here is so negative, but i am more of an IRC guy, than a form user. I normal have very good things to say about the great work the Ubuntu team does.

Does anyone else experience these issues with Ubuntu 12.04?

---

### Post by ResQue on 2012-07-22
Ok so i fixed issue one, you can go into System Settings > Displays and set the following:

Launcher placement = Laptop (this will stop the unity bar appearing on both screens)
Sticky edges = Off (this will stop the mouse cursor getting stuck between screens)


The second issue still remains which is a little annoying, but i can live with that now issue one is out of the way.
