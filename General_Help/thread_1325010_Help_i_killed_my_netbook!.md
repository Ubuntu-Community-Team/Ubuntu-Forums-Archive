### Post by John Bean on 2009-11-13
Download Unetbootin for Windows and use it to make a bootable flash from your Ubuntu CD. Job done.

Edit: or, from the live CD install either Unetbootin or (probably better for you) Ubuntu's "USB Startup Disk Creator" and use that instead of booting Windows.
