### Post by SGC on 2005-08-13
**Kubuntu package of kat:** 
[http://dinton.no-ip.org/dists/kubuntu/main/binary-i386/kat_0.5.4-1~udt1_i386.deb](http://dinton.no-ip.org/dists/kubuntu/main/binary-i386/kat_0.5.4-1~udt1_i386.deb)
**install it with:** dpkg -i kat_0.5.4-1~udt1_i386.deb

**Katalog, seems to do what you are looking for, but I didn't try it:** 
[http://dinton.no-ip.org/dists/kubuntu/main/binary-i386/katalog_0.3-1~udt1_i386.deb](http://dinton.no-ip.org/dists/kubuntu/main/binary-i386/katalog_0.3-1~udt1_i386.deb)
