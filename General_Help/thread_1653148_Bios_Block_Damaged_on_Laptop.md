### Post by davidmohammed on 2010-12-26
That error message is common for both Windows and Linux - so I seriously doubt that its anything to do with Ubuntu itself.

Googling around - toshibas/portege are common culprits - probably with common hardware.

The CMOS chip holding the BIOS has probably failed...  its more common than you think for these to fail on a laptop - poor components, heat etc.

change your motherboard/laptop... sorry!
