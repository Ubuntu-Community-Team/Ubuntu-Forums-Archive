### Post by Maverick Meerkat on 2013-06-03
Hi,

You right-click on desktop, select "Change Background".
A group of thumbnails will appear, the upper-left one has a white clock drawn on it.
That is the background slide-show.

Rick

### Post by Maverick Meerkat on 2013-06-03
While you're at it, when selecting the background you will see the word "Behavior" written next to the "Look" tab that you are on.
Click on Behavior and you can turn on "Auto-hide" for the launcher ( the bar of icons along left-hand side of screen )
This hides the launcher, thus giving you more screen to use.
I like this feature, personally, and I also adjust the "Reveal Sensitivity" while I am at it with the slider that is also on the Behavior screen.
That sets how easy the launcher re-appears.

Rick
