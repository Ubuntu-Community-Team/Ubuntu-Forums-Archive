### Post by Grenage on 2010-03-09
In CompizConfig Settings Manager:

Go into Desktop Cube settings.
Click on behaviour.
Un-tick *inside cube*.

### Post by Grenage on 2010-03-09
It's always nice when it's simple, and it's always simple when you know how!

Edit: Under Compiz Settings there is an option called "Cube Reflection and Deformation".  Under Deformation there is an option at the top, choose *none.*
