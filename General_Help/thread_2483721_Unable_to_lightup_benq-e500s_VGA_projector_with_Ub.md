### Post by TheFu on 2023-02-09
Try a different converter.  Typically, an external powered adapter is needed.
Ensure the resolution and refresh rates match to what the projector supports.  Typically 800x600 @ 60hz is a safe resolution.
Try a different, shorter, video cable.  Sometimes signal strength isn't sufficient to meet the VGA requirements.
