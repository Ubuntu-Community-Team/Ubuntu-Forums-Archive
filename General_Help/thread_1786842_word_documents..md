### Post by amanchesterman on 2011-06-20
I use OpenOffice (it came with Ubuntu 10.10) rather than LibreOffice, but I believe the two programs are very similar.

I haven't encountered the problems you describe in your post.

Saving from your Office program and reading in Word: check which version of the Word format you are using. In OpenOffice it offers several formats when you save a document. The one I use is 'Word2000/XP' since, as far as I know, all later versions of Word can read that format.

Reading from .docx and opening in Office: I don't know why that doesn't work, I haven't had problems myself. Suggest you look on the LibreOffice help forum rather than here, since I guess it is an application-specific issue.
