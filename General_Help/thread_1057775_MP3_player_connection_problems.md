### Post by djbushido on 2009-02-02
Okay, when trying to connect my Sony Walkman (MP3 player, not CD), my Xubuntu machine crashes. Not hangs, but crashes. I get pop-up boxes with lots of control characters, and then slowly (minute or so) my machine goes under. I recently moved to a new drive, but I don't think that is the problem. I am using kernel *.11, and haven't had these problems with *.9. Could that possibly be the problem? Will check against that later, but any suggestions would be appreciated.

---

### Post by djbushido on 2009-02-03
Okay, turns out it was the computers fault. I had too many usb devices plugged into the same chip, and there wasn't enough juice going around to power them all. Moving my drive to a seperate usb port gave it the juice I need to do life and have my mp3 player. Sorry for posting my own solution, but i hope it will help others.
