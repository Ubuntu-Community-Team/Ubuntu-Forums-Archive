### Post by Sir_Sid on 2009-01-25
In Appearance Preferences under Visual Effects you get three options of:
None
Normal
Extra

What do these options actually control. Specifically when I turn it to none, what is turned off. I understand that ccsm options are configured with Normal and Extra, but when I turn them all off, compositing applications such as awn do not work. What is turned off to cause this.

---

### Post by gettinoriginal on 2009-01-25
By turning setting Visual Effects to none, you are disabling compiz as your window manager and reverting to metacity which is gtk, therefore animations and other eye candy will not work.  :p
