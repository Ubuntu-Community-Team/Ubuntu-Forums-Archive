### Post by cyrusrayne on 2007-05-26
Since you're on Windows, I would recommend [InfraRecorder]("http://infrarecorder.sourceforge.net/").  There will be a button on the toolbar (looks almost like a pencil drawing a box on a disc), and you'll click that, select the ISO file and burn it out.  Once your disc pops out, it should ready for you.  Hope this helps for you.
