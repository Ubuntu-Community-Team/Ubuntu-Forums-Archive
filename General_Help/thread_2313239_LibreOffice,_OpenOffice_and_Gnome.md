### Post by kurt18947 on 2016-02-11
I ran into envelope problems - margins - when printing to a Brother MFC-6490CW using L.O. 5. A Brother MFC-470xx prints envelopes fine. It appears to be a printer driver problem at least for me. We also have a Samsung laser multifunction mono was goofy printing envelopes -  I don't recall how. I played around and created an envelope-sized text box and left the paper size set to letter and saved it as a template. The Samsung doesn't check the paper size before printing unlike the Brother so that works fine for manually addressed envelopes.

EDIT:  I just had a chance to play around a little with L.O. 5.0.2.2 and envelopes.  Oh Boy. If I select the Brother MFC-6490CW to print a #10 envelope, it thinks it's printing a DL sized envelope. If I try to print the same #10 envelope on the Samsung laser, it thinks it's printing a letter sized sheet of paper, not an envelope. I'm not sure if the problem is L.O. or the printer driver but something is WAY out of whack with envelope printing.
