### Post by Telecaster72 on 2010-01-09
It happens to me as well it is Skype and Pulseaudio that does not play well together, the Skype developers are aware of this problem and hopefully a fixed version will come out. You can kill pulseaudio and skype in the system monitor and the restart pulseaudio by pressing alt+F2 and type pulseaudio and hit enter, you might want to check your soundpreferences after that if you use the mic on the webcam, to see if it is chosen under "inputs".
Google around, there is a couple of suggested fixes for this, i havent tried any of them so i can suggest any...
Good Luck!
