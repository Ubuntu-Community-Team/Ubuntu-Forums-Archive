### Post by geraldm on 2007-11-28
It is possible to do.  There need to be electronics in the cable.  One such manufacturer was
Prolific, who made the cable for low and full speed.  Since then, high speed USB became available, so you should chose the cable carefully according to your needs.  That cable was
used for computer to computer via USB, but it would also work for port to port.
