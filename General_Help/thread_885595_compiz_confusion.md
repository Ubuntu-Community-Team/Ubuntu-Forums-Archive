### Post by gjoellee on 2008-08-11
mostly all computers need a graphic card driver to use Compiz Fusion.

Go to System->Administration->Hardware Drivers and enable the graphic card driver (the latest is recommended), reboot your computer after installation.

Lets install compiz with a one click install, click on the link below:
[apt://compizconfig-settings-manager](apt://compizconfig-settings-manager)
(you may need to wait up to 15seconds before the download starts)
