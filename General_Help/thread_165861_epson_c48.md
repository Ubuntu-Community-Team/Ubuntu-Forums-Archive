### Post by jamesford on 2006-04-25
i bought the c48 yesterday in fact. it works great. all you need to do is to plug it in, then find the 'printing' thing under system > admin, click add printer and your printer should be autodetected (if not choose epson c48 from list) and it should then suggest a gutenprint driver for it (if not choose gutenprint c48 from list)
