### Post by winh8r on 2011-12-11
Just a thought, right click on an empty space on the panel at the top of the screen and select "add to panel"
look for the "shut down" application and select it and click add.

Then right click the new shutdown icon on the panel and move it along the panel a bit and select "lock to panel" from the right click menu. 

Now right click on the "original" shut down icon and select "remove from panel"

Might just have got confused, I had something similar happen a couple of years ago and this trick cured it.
