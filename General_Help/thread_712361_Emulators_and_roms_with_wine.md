### Post by 3rdalbum on 2008-03-02
Roms aren't specific to any operating system, so your existing roms from Windows-based emulators will work on Linux-based ones.

For the Playstation, the only one I've managed to get working is PSx ([http://psxemulator.gazaxian.com](http://psxemulator.gazaxian.com)). All you need is a Playstation BIOS, and you can run a game straight from its CD.

Not sure why I wanted an emulator for the Playstation, considering that I still have one :-)
