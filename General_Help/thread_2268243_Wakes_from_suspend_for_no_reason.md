### Post by tgalati4 on 2015-03-07
Cats have been known to program late at night.

Set up a web camera to catch them in action.

Do you have any hard-mounted remote shares?  Perhaps there is a driver that is waking the system.  Waving your hand over the keyboard will change the capacitance (like a giant touchpad).  Try a cleaning and reseating.  The touchpad may have dirt in the edges that can set it off.

Most likely cat hair.
