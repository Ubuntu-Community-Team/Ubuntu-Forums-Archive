### Post by fragos on 2009-07-22
If you select the layers view in the toolbox when editing your GIF you see small images followed by some text as in "Frame 2 (100ms)". Double click on that text and you can edit it as follows "Frame 2 (100ms) (replace)". The Enter key will store your change for that frame. I find it usefull that the time on frame can be edited -- each frame can have it's own unique time.
