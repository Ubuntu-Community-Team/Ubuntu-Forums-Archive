### Post by kokiri on 2006-01-24
Not sure where she went to but you can get it back by right clicking anywhere on the lower task bar, choosing "add to panel" and picking the trash icon out of the list.  To make sure it stays there when you logout or shutdown the computer, check the "Save current setup" on the logout box and it will reappear when you reboot or log back in.
