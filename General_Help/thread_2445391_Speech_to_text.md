### Post by HermanAB on 2020-06-13
No, there is no readily available solution for speech to text.  About 25 years ago, there was Dragon Naturally Speaking, which worked (badly) for some people, who invested a few weeks in training the software and themselves, to work with each other, when speaking in a monotone drone.  Since then, computers failed to become significantly faster, so there still isn't anything good out there.  Apple (and others) do it on a super computer, by sending the voice samples from your Macbook to the Apple computer and the text back in the other direction.
