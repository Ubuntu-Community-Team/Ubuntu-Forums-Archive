### Post by dvlchd3 on 2009-11-12
Monitoring: Snort
Blocking: Squid with either SquidGuard (blocks sites based on url) or DansGuardian (blocks sites based on url or content.  However, DansGuardian takes much more resources than SquidGuard.)

Snort guide:
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=919472](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=919472)

Squid and SquidGuard:
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/SquidGuard](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/SquidGuard)

DansGuardian:
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Servers/DansGuardian](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Servers/DansGuardian)
