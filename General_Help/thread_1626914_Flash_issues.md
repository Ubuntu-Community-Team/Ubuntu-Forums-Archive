### Post by efflandt on 2010-11-21
You have not said what Ubuntu version you upgraded from or to.  But after I did a 9.10 to 10.04 update, flashplugin-installer appeared to be installed according to Synaptic, but it was not.  Firefox did not show any Flash under Add-ons.

Also ubuntu-restricted-extras became uninstalled.  But reinstalling that did not fix flash which it normally includes.  So I had to mark flashplugin-installer for Reinstall and do that before it worked.
