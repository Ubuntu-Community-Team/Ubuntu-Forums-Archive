### Post by DuckHook on 2018-06-20
> **waltd said:**
> …There is no menu or menu options…I not getting the full range of GnuCash's options and it's very limited…
I run a small business on GnuCash. In GnuCash, the real power is only found in the menus, which in Xenial Unity appear in the Unity global menu that is shared by all apps at the top of your screen. You may have to move your GnuCash window to hug the top of the Global Menu strip to be able to mouse over the menu selections. Otherwise, in Unity, there is a bug that might prevent them from showing up. You can work around this bug by invoking the drop down menus with <Alt>+<f> which will always open the file menu (provided GnuCash has the focus). You can navigate among the menus with arrow keys or other shortcuts (<Alt>+<e>, <Alt>+<r>, etc).
