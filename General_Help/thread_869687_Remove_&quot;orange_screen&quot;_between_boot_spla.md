However, there may be a way to put something else in the place of the blank screen...  Honestly, just pick a color that matches your background/login screen.  You won't even notice it after a while.

Also, it looks like you are somehow being booted to the command line during the boot splash.  Can you post what text you see?  It is a shot in the dark, but you might be able to see the text again if you press Ctrl-Alt-F8.  If you don't, press Ctrl-Alt-F7.
