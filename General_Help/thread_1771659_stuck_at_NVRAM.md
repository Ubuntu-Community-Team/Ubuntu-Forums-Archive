Hello all,  am new to Ubuntu. I did have one problem and I found a fix else were, so I thought I would share it here. After restating I got stuck at checking NVRAM during boot up... I could not even go to bios etc. If this happens to you and you are checking for a fix on another computer... don't panic... just unplug your USB hub. After forcing it to restart it started up great. Hope this helps someone out there.
