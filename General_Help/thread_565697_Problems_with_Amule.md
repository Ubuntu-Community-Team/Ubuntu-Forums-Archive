### Post by Hallvor on 2007-10-03
You may have to show hidden files in Nautilus. Every folder with a ´.´ in front will be invisible.

From the desktop, press Alt+h to open your home directory. Then press Ctrl+h to view hidden folders. Your .aMule folder should be there, and your mp3s should be in the incoming folder.
