### Post by MichalPasternak on 2007-06-03
Hi,

here's a simple solution how to convert your KMail folders to mbox to import them to Evolution:

1) launch KMail
2) select a folder
3) select all messages in folder (Ctrl+A in default keymap)
4) choose File/Save As
5) save as ~/kmail-import-foldername.mbox
6) repeat steps 2-5 as required
7) launch Evolution
8) choose File/Import
9) choose "Import single file"
10) point to the file(s) saved in KMail
11) it should autodetect "mbox" format
12) that's it!

Google is full of references to "maildir2mbox", unfortunatley, I wasn't able to find a good one, so I came up with my own. Have fun with it.
