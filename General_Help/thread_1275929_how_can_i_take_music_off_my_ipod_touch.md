### Post by speedwell68 on 2009-09-26
If you have an XP licence kicking about you could setup XP in Virtualbox and add USB support to read your iPOD that way.  It is what I have to do to transfer music to my Sony MiniDisc player, as there is no alternative to the Sonicstage software it uses.  If you do use VirtualBox, be sure to get the closed source version from Sun's website and not the Opensource version in Ubuntu's repositories.  The opensource version has no support for the guest additions or USB.
