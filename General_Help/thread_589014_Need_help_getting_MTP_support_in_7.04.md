### Post by jcconnor on 2007-12-10
Don't know if you are still monitoring this but the View also supports MSC/UMS mode. If you turn the View on, put it into lock mode (slide the power switch to the down position) and then hold the thumbwheel in the rewind position (hold the thumbwheel on the left hand edge) and then plug your view in to the supplied USB cable it will be recognized by Ubuntu as a portable HDD.  Then you can drag and drop your music into the MUSIC folder, your iPOD formatted video into the VIDEO folder and your jpg pictures into the PICTURES folder.

The anythingbutipod.com site has a great forum on this player.
