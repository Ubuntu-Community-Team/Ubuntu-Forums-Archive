### Post by peter d on 2011-11-10
You cannot upgrade from 10.04 directly to 11.10. You must upgrade to 10.10 then to 11.04 and then to 11.10. It is only possible to upgrade directly to the next version. The only exception to this is upgrading from one LTS to the next. You will be able to upgrade 10.04 to 12.04.

If you're not able to do a clean install your options are to do three upgrades or to wait 5 months for 12.04.
