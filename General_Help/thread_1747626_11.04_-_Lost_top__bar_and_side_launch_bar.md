### Post by lakerssuperman on 2011-05-02
Log in with safe mode.  Open your home folder and turn on "show hidden files".  Delete everything that says Compizin your home directory and in '/home/.config'.  Reboot and see if you can log back into your account.  I botched a setting earlier in Compiz settings and had no launcher or panel.  I deleted these config files and it restored both.
