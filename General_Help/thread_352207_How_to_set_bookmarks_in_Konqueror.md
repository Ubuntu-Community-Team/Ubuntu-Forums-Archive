### Post by Cariboo1938 on 2007-02-07
:lolflag: I just figured it out!:popcorn: 
In my case I had to:
1) right click on any icon in the toolbar area, for example beside the location bar.
    ----> the Toolbar Menu opens.
2) scroll to "Configure Toolbars" and the 
    ----> Configure Toolbars Menu opens
3) in the "Availabel actions"  section on the left, select 'Bookmarks' and click on the arrow   
   facing to the right, to transport it to the "Current actions" section.
4) click "Apply" and the icon for bookmarks appears in the toolbar area.

Problem solved, Case closed!
