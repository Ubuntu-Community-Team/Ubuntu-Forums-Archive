### Post by dougalkerr on 2010-08-22
I've been there more than once when updates have knocked out the default sound card choice from Ubuntu.
Click on your Sound icon on the panel and go to Preferences and play about with choosing sound cards. One will probably kick your sound card into life. If it is just the microphone then it might just be muted.
Again the option is in the Preferences in the Sound Icon.

Hope this works for you, it is the easiest thing to try.
