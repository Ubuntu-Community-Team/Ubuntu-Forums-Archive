### Post by m_duck on 2011-10-30
Ctrl+Alt+t should bring up a gnome-terminal window as it always has.

Otherwise you can use the 'menu' thing - either hit the Ubuntu logo at the top of the left hand side and start typing 'terminal', or hit the super (Windows) key and start typing the same.
