### Post by topazcat on 2006-04-15
I wanted to note that I did ask at the Firefox forum (forums.mozillazine.org) and the gentleman who answered me suggested that I check the path of the webbrowser.

On the Ubuntu Wiki page, it said to use firefox %s in the preferences section as my webbrowser, so that is what I did.  I deleted the %s (and just use the word Firefox) and now my browser works perfectly and does not go to the unwanted webpage.
