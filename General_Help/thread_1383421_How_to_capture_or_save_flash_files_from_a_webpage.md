### Post by lovinglinux on 2010-01-17
[Video Download Helper](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/3006) extension.

You can also click the icon on the left side of the address bar , select "More Information" and then the "Media" tab. It allows you to save media files from the web site.
