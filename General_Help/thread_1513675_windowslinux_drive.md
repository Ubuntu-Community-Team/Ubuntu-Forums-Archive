### Post by Tim Sharitt on 2010-06-19
If your Linux partition is ext3, there are two options I know of.

Ext2Fsd [http://www.ext2fsd.com/](http://www.ext2fsd.com/)
It's open source, and seemed fairly stable when I used it.

Ext2IFS [http://www.fs-driver.org/](http://www.fs-driver.org/)
It is not open source, but still free to use. People who have used it seem to like it.
