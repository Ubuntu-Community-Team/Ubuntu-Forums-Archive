### Post by Baldrick_NZ on 2013-10-25
1 - Gksu was dropped as part of the installation in 13.04. You can install it from the repos though.

2 - Ubuntu Tweak works in 13.10, inc Janitor. For the moment though you need to use the 'testing' ppa,as I don't think the regular ppa has yet been updated for 13.10. [https://launchpad.net/~ubuntu-tweak-testing/+archive/ppa](https://launchpad.net/~ubuntu-tweak-testing/+archive/ppa). 

Hope this helps..
