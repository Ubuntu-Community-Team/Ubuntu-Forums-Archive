### Post by trench.me on 2009-05-23
Can you list a brief example playlist because I'm confused. I just created a new playlist and drag n' dropped two different albums into the sidebar listing of the playlist (onto its "icon"). The method worked seamlessly. 

Perhaps the problem exists in the way you are creating playlists? 

I created mine by going Music > Playlist > New Playlist.

Another problem could exist in the metadata of the songs within the album. Right click on a few of the tracks, choose "properties", and make sure everything is as it should be. If it''s not, correct it. (You can correct an entire album by highlighting all songs within it, right clicking, selecting properties, and then editing the details - like track numbers for instance.)
