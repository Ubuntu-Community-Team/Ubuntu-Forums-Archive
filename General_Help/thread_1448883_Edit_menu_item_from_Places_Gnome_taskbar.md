### Post by warfacegod on 2010-04-07
As far as I know, the only way to change the icons in the Places menu os to change your System icons. Right click any part of your desktop> select Change Desktop Backgrounhd> Under the Themes tab click customize> Icons tab will give you a list of different icon themes to choose from.

This is a site to find lots of themes and icon sets and splash screens, etc.

[http://gnome-look.org/index.php?xcontentmode=160]("http://gnome-look.org/index.php?xcontentmode=160")

Generally, you can install themes and such by dragging and dropping  into the Themes tab of Appearance Preferences.
