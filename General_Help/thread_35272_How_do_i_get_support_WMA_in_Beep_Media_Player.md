### Post by niels on 2005-05-18

### Post by jasplund on 2005-05-18
You can find a deb here [http://www.ubuntuforums.org/attachment.php?attachmentid=339](http://www.ubuntuforums.org/attachment.php?attachmentid=339)

untar it and then type 

sudo dpkg -i beep-input-wma_0.1.1-1_i386.deb

in a terminal
