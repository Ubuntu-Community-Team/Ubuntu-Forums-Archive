### Post by Yarui on 2010-07-03
> **gogogadgetleg said:**
> turn my laptop screen off when lisening to music - is this even possible?? If I'm listening to music I only need the screen on when I'm interacting with the laptop, which wouldn't be very often at all
Surely your laptop has a button or key that can adjust the monitor brightness.  Generally it can go down to the point of turning off the backlight.  That wouldn't save as much power as entirely turning off the monitor, but monitor brightness normally makes a pretty significant difference in battery life, in my experience.
