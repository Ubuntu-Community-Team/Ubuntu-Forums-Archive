### Post by blueridgedog on 2010-04-07
The only way to partition it without loosing data is to backup your data, then partition the drive.  You can attempt to RESIZE an existing partition, then add others, but that assumes that your repartitioning plans include keeping/resizing the partition that has data on it.

Here is a good overview of the process:

[http://www.howtoforge.com/partitioning_with_gparted](http://www.howtoforge.com/partitioning_with_gparted)

Regardless of the route you take, I recommend backing up your data before attempting the process.
