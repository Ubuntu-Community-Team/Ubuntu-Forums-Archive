### Post by 3rdalbum on 2011-10-15
I'm unsure exactly what you've said, so excuse me if I've understood.

If you're saying that you installed an Nvidia driver from a PPA, then I'm sorry to hear about your broken system. Unfortunately that's the risk you take when installing low-level things from PPAs.

If you're saying that you updated your system and it stopped your existing manually-installed Nvidia driver from working, then that's normal. You need to run the Nvidia driver installer again. That's why it's easier to use the version of the Nvidia driver that is installable through Additional Drivers.
