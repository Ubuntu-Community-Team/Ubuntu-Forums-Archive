### Post by winh8r on 2012-03-03
Make sure all the air vents are clear and free from dust or other obstructions. Another possible cause of excessive heat leading to unexpected shutdowns is a failing battery, check your battery performance.

Also try to keep as few applications as possible open/running at the same time,as heat generated by the cpu is a much greater issue in a laptop than it is in a desktop.

Sorry I cannot give a definitive answer, but these are some pointers.
