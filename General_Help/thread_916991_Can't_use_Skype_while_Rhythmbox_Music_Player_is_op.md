### Post by civillian on 2008-09-11
Can you playback audio from other sources (for example a radio stream from last.fm in firefox) at the same time as playing music from rhythmbox?

--edit--

I ask, because if you cannot it may be an issue with your sound setup, in which case under the 'sound' menu in the system->settings menu or control centre (whichever you use) you should set all your options to ALSA. This fixed the problem on my computer.

---

### Post by ubuntuforums on 2008-09-11
You're awesome, thanks. It works perfectly fine now that I set all my sound options to ALSA. :)
