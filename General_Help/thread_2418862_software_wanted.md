### Post by CatKiller on 2019-05-13
Pulseaudio already has the ability to duplicate audio streams. It calls them Monitors. I believe there's a toggle in the settings somewhere to make them visible. Then you can set the **Monitor of *x*** as the input to whichever recording application you're using. You can also pipe it over the network if you want.
