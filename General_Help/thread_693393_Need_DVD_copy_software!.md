### Post by VMan on 2008-02-11
There is also "K9copy" and "DVD::RIP".  Either of those should work.  I have used them both on home made dvd's.  K9copy outputs to a *.iso file and I've used dvd::rip to convert for viewing on the computer.  I've heard that they also work on commercial (ie. encrypted) dvd's, but haven't tried that.
