### Post by bettlebrox on 2007-02-06
> **Mark76 said:**
> Last time I downloaded gaim 2.0 beta 3 there was the option of having it run in the systray.  Why did they remove that?

And where would I find the notification applet?

See my other post: Right-click on the Panel (sys-tray) click on "Add to Panel" and look for the "Notification Applet".

The applet is part of the Panel not Gaim. Also, look under the Gaim "Tools" menu for the Plugins options (or press Ctrl-U) and see if the "System Tray Icon" is enabled. The "Notification Applet" is the "System Tray" (under Gnome).
