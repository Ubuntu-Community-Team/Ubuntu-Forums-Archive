### Post by yetiman64 on 2011-06-05
> **JamesRay757 said:**
> From Super-s to F4?

Install compizconfig-settings-manager open the manager, then open the expo plugin's settings in the manager and change the Super+S setting to whatever you want. If what you choose is already in use you will get a warning pop-up.

Edit: Be careful what you change or add in compiz, it can thoroughly mess up Unity. Especially the cube settings (can be done but is not for the feint of heart). Changing the expo key should be Ok though.
