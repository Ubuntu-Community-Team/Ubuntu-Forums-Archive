### Post by kidders on 2007-06-10
Hi there,

Different filesystem browsers work in different ways. If you can't make the one you're using work the way you want it to, you could always try a different one. Other possibilities might include not sorting alphabetically, prefixing your stuff with letters or numbers to indicate their positions in lists, or using something like Beagle to avoid having to look at directory listings altogether.
