### Post by ajgreeny on 2008-04-02
I suggest you try disabling ipv6 in Firefox and see if it helps.  In the address bar type **about:config** then in the tab that comes up type **ipv6** in the search filter.  If the line that shows ends with **false** double click to make it end with **true** and leave the page.  Now restart Firefox and you may see a big difference
