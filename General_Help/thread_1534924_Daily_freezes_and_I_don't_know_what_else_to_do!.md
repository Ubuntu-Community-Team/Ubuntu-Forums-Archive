### Post by coldraven on 2010-07-20
I once had a laptop that had a heat related freeze up.
The longer it was on the more frequent the freezes.
I had the whole thing in bits but never managed to fix it.

So to find out if this is your problem you need to time the intervals between freezes.
Starting with it cold, it should work for a longer period than if it was warm.
Good luck!
