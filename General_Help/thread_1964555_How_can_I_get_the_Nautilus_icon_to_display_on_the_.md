### Post by audiomick on 2012-04-24
I just did this to make sure. I am on 10.04 lucid.

Right click on the panel and choose "add to panel"
Choose "user defined starter" and click on "add" at the bottom.


The window in the screenshot will appear. Fill it out like in the screenshot."Befehl" means "command"
The word "Anwendung" in the drop-down at the top means "program", and is the default choice, so you should not have to change it.

Should be easy.
