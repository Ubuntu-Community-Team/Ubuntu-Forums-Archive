### Post by oldsoundguy on 2008-08-22
That appears to be an interesting program.  IF you have 20 bucks to spare and are in need of such (want to run ADOBE in WINE without the normal issues for instance), might be worth it.
WINE has come a long way ... but there is a long way for it to go still.

Really too bad you can't get a "trial" and actually see if it works as advertised.

BUT, it is CLOSED SOURCE, so if an issue occurs, no "update" is going to fix it, and the fine folks at Ubuntu will not be able to fix anything it kludges.

A VERY good reason to set up a non critical "test bed" computer in your house!

Open Office is just fine for most unless you are running a full blown business from your computer ... so MS office is not a sufficient reason for NOT going full Linux.
