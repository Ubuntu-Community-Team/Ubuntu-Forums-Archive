### Post by madmax75 on 2010-10-31
Maybe your download was somehow corrupted? You should always check the MD5 sum of the .iso file before burning it to a disk. If the .iso has even slight errors, it won't work.

Also, burn the disk at a low speed to avoid errors creeping in.
