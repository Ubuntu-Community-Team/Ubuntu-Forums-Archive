### Post by Hedgehog1 on 2011-05-13
One option that comes to mind is installing edbuntu onto a USB.  This will allow you to customize it further, give better run times, and allow the students to save data.

If they provide their own USB sticks, they could even take them home to use on 'moms' PC at home. They could hang the USB on a lanyard, making it a little harder to lose.
