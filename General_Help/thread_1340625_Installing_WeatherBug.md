[SIZE=2]I sure hope that I am doing this correctly! 

I am a fan of WeatherBug. As I have stated, it is like having a very elaborate weather station in my own back yard. My reporting station is less than one mile from my home.

I have been searching for an answer to my problem which was, I get the WeatherBug widget but I could not go any further. In searching for answers I see that other folks have the same problem but no answers. Well I finally found one and I would like to share it.

[/SIZE]                  [FONT=&quot]"Go to Synaptic from System>Administration, search for gij, right click and mark for complete removal. Then in the same application, search sun-java6-jre and right click, install. Then hit apply button at the upper left of the app. 
[/FONT]
    [FONT=&quot]This is what "should" be installed, but you may also need to reinstall WB in addition to this. 
[/FONT]
  [FONT=&quot]It's gij that is the problem, though".[/FONT]
