### Post by oldfred on 2010-08-02
You cannot change anything in linux from windows, which I consider a good thing.

You can use a liveCD and reinstall grub to the MBR or edit grub files if necessary. You can also chroot into a non booting system and do just about anything in the way of repairing editing etc.
