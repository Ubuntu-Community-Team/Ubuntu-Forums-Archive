I installed aMule and it works perfectly, well almost.  The only problem is that if I have it running I cannot connect to the internet with FireFox.  Even if I am only downloading 1 thing and it is at 1.0kbs then any other internet connection on my network times out!  As soon as I close it, everything else works again. ### Post by tom-ubuntu on 2005-08-11
Probably the number of open connections are too much for your adsl router. Try to allow not more then 200 connections, then it should be fine. Play around where the limit for your router is, or check the specs.

Check also the upload, it shouldn't be more then 60 - 70% with an adsl connection. Cable connections are handling this may better. At least this is my experience so far.
