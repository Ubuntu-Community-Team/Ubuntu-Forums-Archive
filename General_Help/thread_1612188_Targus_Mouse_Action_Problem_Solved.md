### Post by queendamidala on 2010-11-02
[FONT="Verdana"]i found an archived thread posted in may2007 from "gardara" with a question which was never answered [/FONT]:
[COLOR="DarkSlateBlue"]"I've got a brand new Targus pawm20 wireless mouse. I am trying it on my laptop and ubuntu seems to recognize it and I can move it horizontally but when I try to move it vertically it doesn't move. And the mousepointer stays at the top of the screen, so if I move the mouse pointer down with my touch pad and then move it vertically or horizontally then the pointer moves to the top of the screen.

Can anyone help me out?"[/COLOR]

[FONT="Verdana"]i had the same problem, the closest answer i found was in the targus website, but it still didn't fix the problem. i refused to believe, as the targus support pointed out after a series of troubleshooting guides, that the mouse is defective and had to be replaced.

so i took out the batteries from the mouse and switched their places, and voila! problem solved! i don't know why it worked. could be several reasons : contact points needed cleaning, loose contact points, etc, who knows. i'm sure it wasn't that the batteries were worn out because the red light underneath the mouse had not turned blue (an indication of weak batteries). my mouse is working perfectly now.
[/FONT]
