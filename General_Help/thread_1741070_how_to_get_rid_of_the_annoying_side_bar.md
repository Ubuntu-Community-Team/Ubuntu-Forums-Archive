### Post by SamyAdel9 on 2011-12-27
I found the solution to this problem...

first download "docky" this can be found in the ubuntu software center.

customize "docky" to how you want it, you can put it on the bottom of the screen like windows, mac, or old ubuntu

download "CompizConfig Settings Manager" this is also located in the ubuntu software center

open "CompizConfig Settings Manager" and scroll down to the "desktop" subsection 

click "Ubuntu Unity Plugin"

in the "general" tab there is a button that says left, click it

a window that shows a computer with red shapes and 1 green, click the green shape then click"ok"

a drop down menu is set to never (or dodge, or dodge windows), change it to autohide.

click "back" close "CompizConfig Settings Manager"

if effects are not immediate log out and back in.


slap a baboon, problem solved
