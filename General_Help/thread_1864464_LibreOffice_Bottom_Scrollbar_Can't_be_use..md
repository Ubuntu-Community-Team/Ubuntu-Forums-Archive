### Post by KimCR on 2011-11-03
This problem has been posted on forums all over the place including other distros. I am using Kubuntu. One of the easiest workarounds to use at present is to use the scroll-wheel of your mouse to click on the horizontal scrollbar.

Also out of interest, if you very carefully click very close to the top of the horizontal scrollbar (or the side arrow buttons) you can make it react to the normal (left) button - it may take a bit to find exactly the right spot (pixel) but a few tries will usually manage it - not very convenient though!

Some of the posts on other forums suggest that if you source LO from its Website rather than through your distro's repository, though I have not tried this.

Hope this helps for now!

:) KimCR
