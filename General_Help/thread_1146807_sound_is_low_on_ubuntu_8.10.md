### Post by pbpersson on 2009-05-02
If you double-click on the volume control the full panel will open which includes volume for:

Master
Front
PCM
CD
PC Speaker

I just set all mine to maximum and then use the knob on the speakers to control the volume as needed

My Front Mic and Line In I have turned down because those are inputs
