### Post by ben2talk on 2011-04-30
Under Alpha Blur, you can 'GRAB' and select windows by 'type' as well as by 'name'.

If you want it specifically for your terminal/terminator window, then just grab that one instead of using by 'type'.

I can't say I noticed any speed differences, but if you're pushed I think that's the way to do it.
