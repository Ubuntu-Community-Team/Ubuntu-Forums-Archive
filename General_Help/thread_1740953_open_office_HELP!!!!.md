### Post by Hedgehog1 on 2011-04-28
One option is to do the 'reset' on OO by uninstalling it, and then reinstalling it using the Ubuntu Software Center.

Also, if these are documents, you can also load and use **AbiWord**.  It will read and write your OO 'writer' documents.

Be sure to make copies of your documents before you experiment!
