"Password too short" speaks for itself.

> By default, Ubuntu requires a minimum password length of 6 characters,

I use a combination of alphanumeric characters and a 9 character string gets me a description of "Fair password," which is accepted. A strong password in the view of the Ubuntu security team would be 15 characters long.

[https://help.ubuntu.com/lts/serverguide/user-management.html](https://help.ubuntu.com/lts/serverguide/user-management.html)

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/StrongPasswords](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/StrongPasswords)

As far as I can remember Ubuntu's password policy in 15.04 is no different from what it was in 14.04.
