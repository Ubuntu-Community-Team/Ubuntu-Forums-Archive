### Post by fubarbundy on 2006-11-01
Hello community people. Here's the thing. Like a lot of you I've enjoyed seeing Ubuntu's Human icon theme growing and improving.

HOWEVER.

There are lot's of people who have gone to the effort of creating missing icons for all sorts of programs etc. to improve consistency and desktop polish, and these never seem to make it into the official theme.

I would love to see Human icons everywhere, and so there are two solutions I can see.

1. Create a central place for people to post their Human icons for review, and make constructive comments on other people's contributions, for eventual inclusion in the official Human icon theme. This would of course have to have a high standard of consistency to avoid lowering the quality bar.

2. (not mutually exclusive) Have a human-icon-theme-extras package, possibly in Universe, into which icons that haven't made  it into the official Human package for one reason or another can go. Quality should still be high, but this package would let people choose more theme-consistent icons with some possible rough edges over hopelessly outdated/aliased/mismatched fallback icons.

So, this thread is for two things:

1. If you come across any user-created Human-style icons, post a link here.

2. If you have any feedback on my ideas above, let me know! I am going to make this happen unless there's a really good reason not to - I'll harrass Our Fearless Leader Mark if necessary ;-) Having blanket icon consistency is one thing that reinforces the sense of solidity and quality for any desktop environment.

...Fubar Bundy
