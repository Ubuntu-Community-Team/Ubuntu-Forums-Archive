### Post by nami on 2007-06-09
When the login appears after loading ubuntu or when i want to log off, it plays a drumbeat when the login screen appears.  How do I get rid of that sound?

---

### Post by CautionaryX on 2007-06-09
Try this:

System > Administration > Login Window

go to the Accessability tab and uncheck the sounds.
