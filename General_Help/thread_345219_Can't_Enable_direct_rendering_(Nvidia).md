### Post by fragos on 2007-01-24
In your xorg.conf you have:

Section "Device"
	Identifier	"NVidia FX5900XT"
	Driver		"nvidia"
	BusID		"PCI:1:0:0"
	Option		"UseFBDev"		"true"
	Option 		"NoLogo" 	"true"
EndSection

Mine doesn't have either of those Option lines and it always works correctly.  I have an FX5200.

### Post by mistman123 on 2008-01-11
Go to the synaptics manager and uninstall xserver-xgl. This package has conflicts with NVIDIAs xgl driver.
