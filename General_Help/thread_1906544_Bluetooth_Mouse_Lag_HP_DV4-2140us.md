### Post by danidemi on 2012-01-13
Hi Simeo, I had exactly the same problem with my brand new Logitech bluetooth mouse.

Yesterday I restarted the notebook, I entered the setup and I disabled the touchpad from there. I don't know if this can be done on your PC... 
My notebook is a Samsung and its BIOS gives you that opportunity.

Since then my bluetooth is working perfectly.

I tried this becasue somewhere else I read that the lag could be caused by a shared IRQ... 

Hope it applies also to your case.
