### Post by Jack Waugh on 2014-09-14
If your mouse is too fast or too slow, the controls that come with Xubuntu don't help, because they only address acceleration.  As a workaround, to modify the base sensitivity you might find [http://patrickmylund.com/blog/lowering-gaming-mouse-sensitivity-in-ubuntu-9-10/](http://patrickmylund.com/blog/lowering-gaming-mouse-sensitivity-in-ubuntu-9-10/) helpful; I sure did.
