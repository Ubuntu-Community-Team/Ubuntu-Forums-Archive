### Post by Giblet5 on 2009-10-30
If you use a larger font, you'll be able to scale images a bit further before pixelization becomes apparent.

Weird huh.

Microsoft uses larger default font sizes.

This lets you comfortably sit a little further from the monitor.

The farther from the monitor your head is, the more you'll be able to zoom images before pixelization becomes evident. The reverse is equally true.

It's like a mechanical gaussian blur.
