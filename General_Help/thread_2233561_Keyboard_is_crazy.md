### Post by col48 on 2014-07-10
The Right-shift key is next to the /? key on an English keyboard.  (ie to get ? you expect to press Shift and /)

I recently bought a new (cheap) keyboard on which the Left Shift key also always operated the adjacent Caps Lock key.  I think that under the surface they were physically joined.  However, the link must have been weak, because a day or so after I had worked out what was going on, it must have broken and the keyboard started working normally.

Perhaps this is something similar.
