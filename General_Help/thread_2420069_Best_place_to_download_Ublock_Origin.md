### Post by DuckHook on 2019-05-29
Raymond Hill, uBlock Origin's founder and lead developer, offers this extension in Google's Chrome store.

However, if using the FOSS Chromium, the uBlock Origin extension is available right in Ubuntu's repos.

I would not trust any source other than just these two.

It is also worth noting that the "Origin" is important. A few years ago, uBlock was forked, an attempt made to monetize and, when unsuccessful, the fork abandoned. It has since been bought up by a rival adblocker. Versions of the fork are still floating around out there confusing the unwary.
