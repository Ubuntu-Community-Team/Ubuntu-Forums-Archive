### Post by plucky on 2009-02-23
> **Jasontarr said:**
> I am not able to send mail from Evolution Mail.  My SMTP uses port 2525 NOT port 25.  I could not find a place to change this. Anyone know what I should do to solve my issue?  Thanks!

In Evolution open **Edit > Preferences > Select Account > Edit > Sending E-mail** and under the server setting put **:2525** so it looks like ```
smtp.isp_name.com:2525
```.
Good Luck
