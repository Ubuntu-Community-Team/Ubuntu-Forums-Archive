### Post by WorMzy on 2010-07-06
You must not have installed it to the same partition as your Windows installation,otherwise it would have been wiped out when you formatted. Do you have any other partitions on your disks? (Do you see a D:\ E:\ etc in your "My Computer" on Windows?) If so, look inside those to see if there's a Ubuntu folder in any of them.
