### Post by _0R10N on 2010-04-12
It will depend on you outgoing and incoming traffic. I mean, if you're downloading a movie at home, and trying to download another big file from a remote location, your internet connection could be slow down.

It will also depends whether you have a router and several other machines are connected to it.

If your usual traffic is small documents and files, you shouldn't worry.
