### Post by tredegar on 2011-06-19
> I want to change the start up tune and the login screen sound.
Don't we all ;)

On 10.04 the sounds you refer to are in /usr/share/sounds/ubuntu/stereo/ as you are on 10.10 YMMV.

The files you need to change are (probably) **desktop-login.ogg** and **system-ready.ogg**

Replace those files with files of the same name, but a different (ogg-encoded) sound
