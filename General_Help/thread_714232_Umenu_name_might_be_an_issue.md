### Post by Kensey on 2008-03-03
I noted the debut of the "umenu" utility in 8.04.  I thought I ought to note that the name "umenu" could pose a problem, as the University of Virginia has had a [user-interface menu system]("http://itc.virginia.edu/desktop/unix/docs/u001a.html") on their Unix servers called "umenu" since before 1994.  It might be worth contacting them just to get an OK before releasing 8.04 -- I doubt they'll care, but it'd be good to get that on record (or at least get on record that they had actual knowledge of a similarly-named tool with a similar function).
