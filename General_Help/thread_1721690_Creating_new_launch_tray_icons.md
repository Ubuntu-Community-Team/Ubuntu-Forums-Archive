So I save my newly created icon to '/usr/share/icons/hicolor/48x48/apps/new-name'.

Right click on the current icon in the launch tray. Click on the app-icon in the window. It takes me to '/usr/share/icons/hicolor/48x48/apps/app-name'. High light the new-icon and close the window.

The new app-icon does not appear in the launch tray. Rename the new-icon to app-icon. No change. Still the original icon.

Copy the new-icon to another directory. Follow the link procedure and everything works. The launch tray icon changes.
