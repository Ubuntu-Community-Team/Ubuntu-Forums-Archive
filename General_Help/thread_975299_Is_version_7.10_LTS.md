### Post by JimmyBEng on 2008-11-08
There have been 2 LTS releases so far: 6.06 (dapper drake) and 8.04 (hardy heron).  neither 7.10 or 8.10 is an LTS release.

Whether a release will be an LTS is decided at the beginning of its development cycle, so if a release isn't one now it will never 'become' one.
