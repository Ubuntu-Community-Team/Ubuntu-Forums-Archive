### Post by plucky on 2010-11-12
POST=Power on self test.

This is all about the computer hardware and has nothing to do with the operating system.

Disconnect you drives and see if it still does the two POSTs.

Make sure that all the things that can restart your computer like "wake on lan" or USB are disabled.

Reset your BIOS to default just to make sure something isn't triggering the second POST.

Hope it isn't the motherboard.

Good Luck
