### Post by ibuclaw on 2008-04-23
Go into "System>Administration>Software Sources" in the upper menu bar.

And you'll see a drop-down bar with the title "**Download from:**"
The tab will say something like "**Main Server**" or "**Server for Country X**"

To change it, click on it and select one of the options.
Choosing "**Other...**" opens up the list of options.

ie:
```

United Kingdom:
    archive.ubuntu.com
    ftp.tickers.org
    mirrorservice.org
United States:
    ftp.unina.org
    lug.mtu.edu
    mirror.us.kernel.org
etc...
etc...

```
