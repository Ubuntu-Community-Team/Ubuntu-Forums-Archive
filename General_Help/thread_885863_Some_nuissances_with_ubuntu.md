### Post by ragflan on 2008-08-10
Why don't you install AWN (Avant Window Navigator) or Cairo Dock? Those two programs will give you a MAC-like dock and you can make the dock as big as you want, or as small as you want (that goes for icons as well). And it looks good too. You can easily add all your applications to the dock plus there are a number of good applets that come with AWN. 

Avant Window Navigator can be found in the repositories. Search for it through Synaptic Package Manager.
