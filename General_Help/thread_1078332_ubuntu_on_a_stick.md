### Post by issih on 2009-02-23
Been a while since I played with stick distro's, but there were two types, one worked as ou describe, being essentially a live cd, so you couldn't really upgrade or add to the distro. The other type is a persistent install, which allows you to do what you want.

Google about for persistent ubuntu install, or search these forums and the ubuntu wiki, theres loads of info:

[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/LiveUsbPendrivePersistent](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/LiveUsbPendrivePersistent)

Thats a good place to start.

Hope that helps
