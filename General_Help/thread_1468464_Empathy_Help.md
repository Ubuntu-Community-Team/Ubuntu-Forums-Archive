### Post by daqron on 2010-05-01
Hi Skyline.

I haven't found a way to do that. It seems to me that the priority with Empathy was simplicity over customizability. That's a great philosophy for MacOS, but I suspect Linux users are largely not going to buy it.

Anyway, I think the best you can do is make sure the notifications are turned on (Edit > Preferences > Notifications) so that the indicator applet (little envelope icon in the gnome panel) turns green when you receive a new message. I really like this feature, but I agree that it's not as immediately visible as a new window popping up.

Good luck!
