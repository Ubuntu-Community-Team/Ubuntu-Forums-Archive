### Post by Daniel on 2004-11-06
Yeah, it's hiding.  You have to go into the mail account settings and go to the Default tab and it's there.

### Post by cseg on 2004-11-06
For those of you having as much problem as I did, here it is in baby steps:

- on the Tools menu select Settings...
- select your email account and press Edit
- click on the Defaults tab

Then select your Sent Messages Folder.
