### Post by JosephC on 2011-05-03
I would also add the Firefox EPUB Reader Add-on to the list. It works very well, and is much lighter than these other options. You don't need to install a whole lot of otherwise unnecessary dependencies. If you don't need most of the features in Calibre, it's a better option.

Hopefully, EPUB support will someday be added to the default document reader, as it already is in Kubuntu's Okular (which you could also download and install in Ubuntu, if you don't mind installing most of Kubuntu with it). As it is probably the most popular open source ebook format, I'm kind of surprised Ubuntu doesn't read it out of the box.
