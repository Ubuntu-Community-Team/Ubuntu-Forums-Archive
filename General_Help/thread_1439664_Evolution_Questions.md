### Post by sites on 2010-03-26
Getting your gmail contacts into Evolution is easy.  Export your gmail contacts as vcard format and import that file into Evolution.

In Evolution Preferences, highlight your account and choose Edit.  Under the Defaults tab you can change the location of your Drafts and Sent folder to the appropriate folders under your account.

I'm looking for the ability to see all of my account Inbox items under On This Computer, with an Account column on the Inbox view.  That's the way Apple Mail works.  Then Evolution could stop freezing up so much.  I could go on but i don't want to hijack your thread.
