### Post by skippy81 on 2006-05-08
To my knowledge a MAC has a completely different architecture to a PC.  The only way you could run MAC applications on linux is if you installed linux *onto* and apple mac (like a power pc) and then used the MOL (Mac on Linux) environment to run the programs.   

Linux is pretty far behind with video editing apps. Theres a project here [http://www.diva-project.org/]("http://www.diva-project.org/") but its still in its early stages.
