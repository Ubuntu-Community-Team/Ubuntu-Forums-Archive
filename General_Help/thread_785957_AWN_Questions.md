### Post by zorba_the_greek on 2008-05-07
> **bmcase said:**
> Newbie here-- just installed avant window navigator. It appears in Apps>> Accessories but does nothing when clicked. The manager opens but I am unable to get the AWN displayed on the screen. Synaptic says I have compiz installed, what am I missing?

AWN starts when you click it on Applications-->Accessories-->Avant Window Navigator. AWN doen't work if you just have compiz-fusion installed, but only if you do use compiz-fusion. So you need to go to System-->Preferences-->Appearance and go to Visual Effects tab and click Extra. Then you can use AWN too... :guitar:
