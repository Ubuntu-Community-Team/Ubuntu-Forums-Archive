### Post by AlexKpow on 2009-08-14
Let's say I'm trying to copy something from /dir/dir/file.ext to the directory that I am currently at in the terminal.

```
cp /dir/dir/file.ext CURRENTDIR
```

What is the shortcut for this, rather than just writing it all out?

---

### Post by michy99 on 2009-08-14
The shortcut for the current directory is . (dot or period), so you could enter```
cp /dir/dir/file.ext .
```
