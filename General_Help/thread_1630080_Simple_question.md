### Post by WorMzy on 2010-11-24
Check [http://linuxwireless.org/en/users/Devices](http://linuxwireless.org/en/users/Devices) to see if there's a module available for native support. If not, then you may be able to use [ndiswrapper]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WifiDocs/Driver/Ndiswrapper") to make the Windows drivers work. It's not a great solution, but beggars can't be choosers. ;)
