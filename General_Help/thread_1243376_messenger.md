### Post by 3rdalbum on 2009-08-18
> **jojom271 said:**
> i like using my yahoo messenger list of friends. In msn am i able to use yahoo friends? or in cheese?

No, you can't use the Yahoo messenging system in aMSN. Cheese isn't a messenger.

You may have some luck using Meebo ([www.meebo.com](www.meebo.com)). You can start an audio/video chat with your Yahoo or MSN friends (Meebo connects to a lot of IM networks simultaneously). If they are also using Meebo they get the video window appearing within their Meebo session. If they are not using Meebo, they get sent a link to click on to join the chat.

This requires that your webcam is supported by Flash Player; some unfortunately are not, but maybe yours is.
