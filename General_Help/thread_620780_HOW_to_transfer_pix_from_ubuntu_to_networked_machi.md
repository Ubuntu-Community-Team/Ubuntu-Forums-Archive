### Post by zippy028 on 2007-11-23
Forgive me if I am not understanding your situation properly but to me it sounds like your shared file is on the windows machine; assuming this is correct it also sounds like you do not have write permissions on the windows side. If these assumptions are correct try looking at your sharing & security settings on the right click menu in windows of the folder you are sharing and make sure allow users to edit/modify files (can't remember the  exact terminology) is selected and you should be able to copy paste once that value is set.
