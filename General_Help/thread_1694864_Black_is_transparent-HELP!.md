### Post by Mirens on 2011-03-14
This thing gave me hedache for days now. And then I went to system>preferences>NVIDIA X server settings

In NVIDIA settings window go to X Server Display Configuration on the left pannel
than on the right pannel Resolution and Hz were set to auto. I changed resolution to my
1680x1050 and second field from auto to 60Hz and everything is working fine now.

I hope this will solve your problem too.
