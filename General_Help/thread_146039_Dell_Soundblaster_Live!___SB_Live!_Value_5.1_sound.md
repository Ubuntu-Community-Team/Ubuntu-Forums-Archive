### Post by bbrand on 2006-03-17
After installing a system that contained the above card the sound system worked but only in stereo mode, no 5.1 surround sound (both rear & woofer speakers were stum).

After looking in the forums I opened a terminal and at the command line used

alsamixer

I played around (navigating with the arrow keys) and unmuted some options with 'M', this solved my problem.

Hope this helps some of you out there.
