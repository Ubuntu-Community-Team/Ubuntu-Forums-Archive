### Post by Kirk Schnable on 2012-12-09
Your best bet for this will probably be to sync your notes up to an IMAP email account, where you would then theoretically have access to them from everywhere.

I believe you can also sync your notes to iTunes, but currently, iTunes does not work on Linux, even with WINE.  

Here is the Apple KB page on the subject of syncing notes from your device:
[http://support.apple.com/kb/HT4191](http://support.apple.com/kb/HT4191)

Apple is very specific about how you can access your device, so I don't believe you'll find a folder you can navigate to on the USB mount point for the iPod, because Apple hasn't chosen to make that information available that way. 

Without modified firmware on your iPod, there will not be a way I can think of to do exactly what you've asked.
