### Post by JRV on 2012-11-16
Do you mean that you want two instances of firefox running in different desktops?

If so:

1) Start firefox.
2) Switch to a different desktop.
3) Right click the firefox icon in the launcher.
4) Click "Open a New Window"
5) use the desktop switcher to change between desktops.
