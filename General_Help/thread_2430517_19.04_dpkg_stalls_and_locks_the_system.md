### Post by mörgæs on 2019-11-04
A series of upgrades often wrecks the operating system. I suggest a clean install of 19.10 and again a fresh install of 20.04 in July when support runs out. 

When doing package management in recent releases people tend to use *sudo apt* in stead of *sudo apt-get*.
