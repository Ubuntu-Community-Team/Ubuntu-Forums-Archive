### Post by get2gether on 2011-10-26
To add to this topic, from personal experience, after a period of generally 6 months connection to your Internet provider you may request to have your modem unlock, free of charge. This generally involves them issuing you with an "unlock code". A set of numbers. When you insert the new sim from your new provider, and plug the modem back in, a box will come up requesting the unlock code you should have been provided. This is how it works using Windows OS.
I would be interested to learn how or even if this is being implemented for ubuntu/linux. At present it means taking your modem to someone who runs Windows....thats a little degrading. :(
