### Post by smilingfrog on 2009-12-17
There are many.
Easiest to use if you don't want to use the command line is to right click on the picture you want to send, and choose 'Compress'. To the right of the filename is a pulldown menu of compression protocols. Choose zip, bz2 or gz for compression, and tar for archiving.
If you are sending it to someone who doesn't use linux, you may want to use zip.
