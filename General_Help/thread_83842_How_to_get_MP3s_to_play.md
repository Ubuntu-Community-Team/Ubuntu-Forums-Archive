### Post by Qrk on 2005-10-29
Click on System-->Help, then to Ubuntu 5.10 starter guide. You can enable Mp3 playback by following the instructions in "Music and Movies"

Ubuntu does not include mp3 or any other propietary format support by default for legal reasons.
