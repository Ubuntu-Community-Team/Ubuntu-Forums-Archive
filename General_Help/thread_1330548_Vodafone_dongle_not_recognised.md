However, I have resolved my problem and having cracked it thought that I would pass on the way. 
 
From*** Widows 7*** go to Ubuntu download for 9.10 location
 
Click on the **[COLOR=red]'Alternative download option etc[/COLOR]** - new window, 
 
Click on 'other download options'
 
Then back up to the top to the button [COLOR=green]'Begin Download of Windows'[/COLOR]
 
First click **Run** download  for install on Windows and then click** Run** for  the program Wubi and allow access
 
New window to create Username and Password.; confirm password cand lick **'Install'**
 
N ote; This may take upwards of an hour depending on the speed of the download.
 
You are given the option to Reboot now or later to complete the installation
 
On rebooting click **Esc and **go to Ubuntu - Click** Enter** and then sit back to complete the installation
 
Reboot in the Ubuntu partition.
