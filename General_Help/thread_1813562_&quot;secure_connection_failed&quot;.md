### Post by Whiteice on 2011-07-28
It looks like a issue with your computer accepting a SSL connection or other wise known as Secure Socket Layer, this is used when logging to systems the require a encryption on the connection. Basically assuming no problems your computer (a) in this case is attempting to connect to a website (b). (a) sends a request but then receives a user name and password to allow you to connect from the (b). You would normally enter in your info. Then across a secure port (b) says ok your allowed in then for a certain amount of time (a) can talk to (b) without the threat of anyone else ease dropping. In your case either Firefox or the web server is not configured right for this to happen as there are different versions and I know Firefox allows you to just click past important steps like this. I would attempt to check your Firefox settings for SSL and make sure that you are allowing the cert from Google.
