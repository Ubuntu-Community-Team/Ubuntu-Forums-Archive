### Post by cjastram on 2008-07-05
You have to turn on editable menu accelerators.  In Ubuntu 8.4, you need go to Preferences > Appearance, click the Interface tab, and enable "Editable Menu Shortcut Keys".

Personally, I think this is completely non-intuitive.  I went to Shortcut Keys, and Main Menu before I started going through all the preferences dialogs from the beginning.  I think it should be under "Shortcut Keys" since, duh, that's what it concerns.
