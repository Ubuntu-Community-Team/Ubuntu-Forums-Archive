### Post by QIII on 2013-08-27
Hello!

Often packages are held back because of dependency issues.  Sometimes simply waiting a day will allow packagers to address those dependency issues by getting other packages in the repo or PPA.

Take care with PPAs, though.  Make sure you read and understand which packages are going to be installed ... or you may cause a dependency issue with something you have installed from a repo or another PPA.

Cheers.
