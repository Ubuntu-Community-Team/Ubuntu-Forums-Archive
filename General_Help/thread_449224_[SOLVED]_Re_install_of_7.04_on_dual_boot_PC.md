### Post by Ub1476 on 2007-05-20
I'm running in dual-boot with Vista.. I had to reinstall Ubuntu a couple of times due to some to some problems, hehe:) Well, on my 90gb hd I gave 45gb to Vista and 45gb to Ubuntu. When I reinstalled Ubuntu, I just deleted the partition for Ubuntu (deleted it from windows), so it were clean (45 of 45gb left). Then I reinstalled Ubuntu, using the option: "Use the Largest Continuous Free Space". Ubuntu installed, and on restart I could choose between Ubuntu and Vista. I installed through live cd, don't know what offers you get when using alternate (except upgrade), but I guess you'll figure it out:)
