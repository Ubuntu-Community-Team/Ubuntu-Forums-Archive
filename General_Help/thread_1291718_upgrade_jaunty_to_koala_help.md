### Post by QIII on 2009-10-15
I have to agree with the posts above.

Unless you are really interested in testing (notice how the "distribution" to the left says "Ubuntu Karmic Koala (testing)"), don't upgrade or update.

There are too many people in too great a hurry to have Karmic.  They are encountering problems associated with a Beta on their daily use machines and cannot use them.  I don't use Karmic on my "production" machines that I use for every day use.  I have dedicated test machines.

If you are trying to upgrade or update to Karmic on a machine you plan to use for everyday use, I have one word of advice:  DON'T.

I don't even plan to put it on my daily use machines until a few days after the release when the dust settles.
