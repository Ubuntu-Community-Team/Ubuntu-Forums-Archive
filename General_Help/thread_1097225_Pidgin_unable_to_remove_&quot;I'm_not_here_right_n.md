### Post by zorkerz on 2009-03-15
Pidgin sets my status to away when I am idle. This is fine but I do not like it automatically setting my status to "I'm not here right now" because usually I am in the vicinity just not on the computer.

If you click on the button at the bottom of the buddy list, put the mouse over I'm not here right now and press delete the message is deleted. Whenever I go away this away message is brought back. 

Is there anyway to prevent this?

I would like my status to change away but not display any away message. Is it possible to have the default away message set to something else. I could disable my status changing to away automatically if there are no better solutions.

---

### Post by fatbotgw on 2009-03-15
What you'll need to do is create a "New status..." that has nothing in it.  Once you've done that go to Tools -> Preferences -> Status/Idle and under Auto-away select the status you just created.  Now whenever it auto-changes the status, it'll use the one you want.
