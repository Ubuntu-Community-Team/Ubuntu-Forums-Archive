### Post by trak87 on 2007-05-16
Pingus (a Lemmings clone) is in the Ubuntu libraries, so the installation for this is easy.

Run Synaptic by going to System, Administration, Synaptic Package Manager. It will ask for your password. Look for Pingus in the list. Right click on it and choose "Mark For Installation". This will mark other files required to play the game. Say OK. Then press the Apply button. It asks for a confirmation. Tell it to "Apply". The programs will be downloaded and installed. After installation, look in the Applications, Games menu and launch the game.

Apply this technique to any program you want to install.
