### Post by An Sanct on 2011-07-06
> **curtisjg65 said:**
> 1. How do you keep IGoogle as your home page?

In Firefox in the menu, go to Edit -> Preferences, select the General tab and in the input box saying "Home Page:" enter the address, you want to be your home page ([http://www.google.com/ig?aig=0](http://www.google.com/ig?aig=0))

> **curtisjg65 said:**
> 2. Does Pin App mean  the same as bookmark this page?

No, "Pin as App Tab" means that the tab will be constantly opened and running, not bookmarked. This can be used for Web radios, Gmail or any other webmail or just something you do not want to close. It is harder to accidentally close that tab, you have to unpin it and then close it. Also it consumes much less space in the tab bar.

### Post by scatteredstones on 2011-07-06
> 2. Does Pin App mean the same as bookmark this page?

2. Pin app simply places a smaller, semi-permanent  tab in the left corner of your tab bar. Essentially, this will save whatever pages you pin, and they will load whenever FF launches. It's not a full bookmark per se, as these app tabs can more easily be altered (for instance, by navigating away from the pinned page by entering another url).
