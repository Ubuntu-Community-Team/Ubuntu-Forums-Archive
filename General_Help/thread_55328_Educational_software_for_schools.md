### Post by plantwater on 2005-08-24
I have 12 out of 15 computers in my class running Ubuntu (I teach 2nd grade) and I use Tuxpaint, Abiword, and a lot of Firefox so far.  I've installed gcompris on a couple of machines, but haven't systematically implemented it yet in the actual instructional day.  I also use totem-xine for multimedia in the classroom, and xmms sometimes.   

The planning wikis for the devs working on edubuntu are really interesting though:  
check the software lists at
[http://www.edubuntu.org/ApplicationSelection](http://www.edubuntu.org/ApplicationSelection)  and [http://www.edubuntu.org/EdubuntuDesktop](http://www.edubuntu.org/EdubuntuDesktop)  

many of which are already available through the hoary uni/multiverses.  Excellent stuff.
