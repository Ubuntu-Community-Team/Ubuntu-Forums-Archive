### Post by gh0zt362 on 2016-08-21
I just wanted to post this fix that worked for me as I see alot of people that have the same problem . 
 I tried adding libplayer.so from chrome to chromium , didnt work . tried adding ppapi enable to flags , didnt work . Tried enabling flash always on , nothing . 

Youtube continued to crash and was getting to the point where no video would play .

I googled the issue and searched last month timeframe to see if a more recent fix had been posted .
And wablammMMM [https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/disable-youtube-html5-pla/enmofgaijnbjpblfljopnpdogpldapoc?utm_source=chrome-app-launcher-info-dialog](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/disable-youtube-html5-pla/enmofgaijnbjpblfljopnpdogpldapoc?utm_source=chrome-app-launcher-info-dialog)

A plugin was the first result . This plugin force disables youtube HTML5 player and Forces flash .   I havn't had one crash since I installed this plugin . The link above is to the plugin in the chrome store. 

This fix worked on Ubuntu Studio 16.04 using chromium .
