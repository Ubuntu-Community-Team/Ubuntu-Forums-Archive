### Post by lovinglinux on 2011-06-16
You are not alone. Other users also asked the same question after upgrading to Firefox 4. They renamed the option to "Show All Bookmarks". Click "Bookmarks >> Show All Bookmarks" or hit CTRL+Shift+O.
