### Post by tomchuk on 2004-11-14
preferences:///
computer:///
applications-all-users:///
favorites:///
preferences-all-users:///
preferences-menu:///
server-settings:///
system-settings:///

and anything else you have in /etc/gnome-vfs-2.0/vfolders
