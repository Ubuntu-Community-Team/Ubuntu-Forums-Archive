### Post by Spenzer4Hire on 2007-11-05
gDesklets could be called a "screenlets manager" but it's really more of a framework, I think.  Widgets made for aDesklets or Screenlets or SuperKaramba won't work with gDesklets.

So, Screenlets is a pretty young widget program/framework, but provides a similar experience to Vista's widgets.

Although I've used it before, gDesklets has been somewhat abandoned in recent years.
