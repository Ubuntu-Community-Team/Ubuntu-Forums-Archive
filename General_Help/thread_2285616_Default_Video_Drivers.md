### Post by grahammechanical on 2015-07-07
As far as i know the only default video driver in Ubuntu is the open source video driver that is provided by the Mesa project. If the video card is by Nvidia then the open source video driver is called Nouveau. If the card is by AMD/ATI then it is called Radeon.

[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mesa_%28computer_graphics%29](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mesa_%28computer_graphics%29)


I assume that the same must be true of every other distribution of Linux. But I do not think that this is the answer you are looking for. Is it?
