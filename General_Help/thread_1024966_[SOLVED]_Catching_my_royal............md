### Post by LordSmight on 2008-12-29
Extract the theme to your desktop then go to System->Administration->Login Window.
Click the "Local" tab then choose "Add". Then choose the Theme folder from your desktop and click "Install". After it's installed it should be shown in the window with the rest of the GDM themes. Choose your theme, select close, and log out.
It should be shown.
