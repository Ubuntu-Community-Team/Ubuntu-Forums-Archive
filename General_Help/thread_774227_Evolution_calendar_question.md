An old outstanding thread that I came across searching for an answer to another alarm problem I have.

Apologies if you have already solved this but one workaround (although not a complete solution) would be to enter your appointment details in the calendar (an alarm - little bell icon  should appear immediately in the appointment slot you are creating in the calendar). 

To make this icon disappear, double click the newly created appointment in your calendar to bring up the appointment dialog box. 

From here click the alarm (yellow and white clock) to bring up the alarms setting dialog box. Click the alarm drop down box where you will find amongst the options "none". Click "none" and close the dialog. Save the changes in the appointment dialog box and you should be returned to your calendar, where the appointment should appear without the alarm icon. This also means that you have cancelled the alarm function and will not receive an alarm reminder - which may not be what you are looking for.
