### Post by Rhubarb on 2008-04-11
This is a classic problem with PS3s.

PS3s can only handle a few specific resolutions - resolutions that are mostly suited to TV screens, not computer monitors.

What you need to do is to change the video mode to a mode that's more suitable for your sceen.
In YellowDog Linux this can be done with ps3-video-mode command.
With Ubuntu I'm unsure of how to do this.

Your best bet is to search the yellowdog linux forums, or maybe do a general search for it on the net.  (or obviously wait for a reply here in this thread).

I only have some experience with PS3s in yellowdog linux and computer monitors (and IMHO, I don't think PS3s are well suited to run linux, not for their cost and what their capabilities are).

I believe there's a good chance you can get this working, as it appears you have a HD capable TV screen.
