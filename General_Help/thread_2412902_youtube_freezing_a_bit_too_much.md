### Post by Autodave on 2019-02-19
Upgrading often causes problems like this.  Hopefully someone will have a better answer than I do, but if it worked OK in 16.04 (or 14.04) and it doesn't now, doing a clean install of 18.04 (after backing up all files to an external source FIRST) would be my preferred way of repairing.

If you had the problem previously, then you may want to check your fans and CPU cooler for dirt and proper operation.  Clean/replace as necessary.
