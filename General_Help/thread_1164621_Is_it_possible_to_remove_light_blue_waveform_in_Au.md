There are no two waveforms. Lightblue/dark blue is just the way how Audacity represents the amplitude of the sound, light blue closer to zero and darker blue as the amplitude gets higher.

When you zoom in closer you'll see that it just one waveform.

One channel of sound can only have one waveform. If you had two separate waveforms they would be on separate channels, and simply removing the extra channel would remove that waveform from the sound file.
