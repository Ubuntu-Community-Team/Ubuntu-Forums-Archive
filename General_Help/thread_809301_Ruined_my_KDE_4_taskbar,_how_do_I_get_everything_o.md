### Post by moss901 on 2008-06-24
Ok, I have a solution...

If you right click on the taskbar and select 'Add Widget', then double-click the ones you want,they automatically appear at the right hand side of the task bar. (Where they should be!)

You must then remove the other instances, and you are left with the default taskbar.

(Note: When adding new widgets, you must add them in order of how you want them to appear left to right, otherwise they will all appear in the wrong order, e.g. 'App Launcher' > 'Task Manager' > 'New Device' > 'Sys Tray' > 'Digital Clock')
