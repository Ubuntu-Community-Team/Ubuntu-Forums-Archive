### Post by CatKiller on 2012-05-09
Media players don't necessarily edit the tags; they store that kind of thing in an internal database. The fact that Nautilus isn't showing meta-data doesn't necessarily mean that the files weren't tagged; it doesn't read all types of tag.

Personally, I use Easy Tag to edit the tags on music files. It is able to do quite a lot automatically by filename structure. The tools that rip cds will also tag your files as they do so based on entries in online databases.
