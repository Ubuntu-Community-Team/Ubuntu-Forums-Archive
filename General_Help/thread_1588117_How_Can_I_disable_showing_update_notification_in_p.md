### Post by cariboo on 2010-10-04
Moved to General Help, as this is a support question.

You can't turn update notifications completely off, but you can set it to:

[list]
[*] Daily
[*] Every two days
[*] Weekly
[*] Every Two weeks
[/list]

It's isn't a very good idea to turn update notifications off, as most of the updates are either bug fixes, or security updates.
