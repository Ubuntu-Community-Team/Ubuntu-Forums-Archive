### Post by drink_stir on 2010-11-08
If you have had the problem of the box on the top left side of your screen when you have multiple monitors, all you have to do to get rid of it is kill the gnome-display-properties process. I have had some problems doing this though. Especially when i went from 9.10 to 10.04. Sometimes it killed my second display totally, but most times it worked. If it does kill your second display just unplug your VGA or S-vid or DVI cable.

Thats as far as ive gotten.

Please note any mistake.
