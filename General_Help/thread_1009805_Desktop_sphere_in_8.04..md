### Post by jlochhead on 2008-12-13
CompizConfig Settings Manager is not installed by default though.

Go to System > Administration > Synaptic Package Manager and install it first.

Make sure you have your graphics card driver installed and desktop effects enabled (System > Preferences > Appearance > Visual Effects).

Then follow the above instructions to find the Settings Manager and look for: 

Cube Reflection and Deformation

P.S. You may want to look into getting the fusion-icon application as it is very useful.
