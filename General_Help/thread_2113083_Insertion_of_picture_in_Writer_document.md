### Post by Chough39 on 2013-02-08
1)Open document in LibreOffice Writer.
 *2)From Menu* :-  Tools &#8594; Options &#8594; LibreOffice Writer &#8594; View &#8594; Display
3)&#9745; Graphics and objects [*enabled*]
 4)Picture will now be displayed instead of the frame marked "graphics1".


Having answered my own question I hope this helps someone else.
