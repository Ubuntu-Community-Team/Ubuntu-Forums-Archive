### Post by Casual Fan on 2008-01-09
Trying to pin down why a computer freezes is well nigh impossible--could be 1,001 different things.

When it does freeze, you don't have to do a hard shutdown. Press and hold CTRL+ALT+SYSRQ, and type reisub. This is a restart.
