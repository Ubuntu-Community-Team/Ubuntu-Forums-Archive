### Post by grw on 2009-06-10
Hi,

How do i get .doc files to open with OpenOffice by default on the click of the mouse?

When I click on a MS word document in order to open it, it never opens with OPenOffice. Gedit text editor tries (and fails) to open it. I have tried right clicking > Open with Other Application > choosing OpenOffice word processor --- and all goes fine. But this doesn't set OpenOffice as the default programme for opening .doc files (as it did in previous versions of Ubuntu). Next time I click on a .doc file, gedit tries and fails again to open it.

Can anyone help?

Gwyn

### Post by Coding Monkey on 2009-06-10
Right click your .doc file, go to Properties -> Open With and select Open Office.
