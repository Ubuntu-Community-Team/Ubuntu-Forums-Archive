### Post by soltanis on 2010-03-16
nVidia. nVidia. nVidia.

Don't bother with ATI. Terrible (awful, don't even) support in Linux and the cards are all more or less the same.

If you are looking for good performance consider getting a GT series card, the GeForces also work pretty well. The 6k series is a little lower end than the 8k or 9k cards. The GT ones are for high performance (i.e. games) but the 220 comes in a little weak if you are looking for newer (2007+) games; go for a 260 in those cases.

EDIT:
The upshot of the 220 is as mentioned, the variety of outputs, and obviously you don't play games so who cares about being more powerful; this card could blow away full 1080p/i easily.
