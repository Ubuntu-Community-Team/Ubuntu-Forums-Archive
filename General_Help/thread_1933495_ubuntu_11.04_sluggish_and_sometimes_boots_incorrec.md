### Post by imachavel on 2012-02-29
Some bench mark tests would be helpful. Reseat your ram around. At bios look for memory test. Could be a ram stick slowing things way down. Try taking out one stick at a time. And booting with only 1. If your laptop slows down with one installed, that's the issue. Could be paging fault, it could be a hard drive issue. Is it possible Linux can get a virus?? I'd check running processes and see what's eating up the CPU. If one process is doing it there is your answer. If its all them there is a more serious hardware or software issue

How about the heat, does your laptop feel too hot running just with power cable or just on the battery? So many things to check. Just so many, at this point isolating whethe it's a hardware or software issue might be nice.
