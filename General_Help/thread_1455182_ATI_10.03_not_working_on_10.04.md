### Post by QIII on 2010-04-15
That driver may or may not work.  For the last several Ubuntu versions, AMD/ATI has made the newest driver available only in the Ubuntu repos.  That is where you want to go to find the driver that will work.  Phoronix comments about this "free candy to Ubuntu" situation every time a new version of Ubuntu comes out.

When installed from Synaptic, the driver worked for me.  Also install fglrx-amdcccle from Synaptic.  That will get you the latest Catalyst Control Center.

But you have to remember to issue 

```
sudo aticonfig --initial
```

before you reboot.
