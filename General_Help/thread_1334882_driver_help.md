### Post by fluffman86 on 2009-11-23
Ubuntu doesn't use drivers the same way that Windows does.  Usually, you just need to go to System > Administration > Hardware Drivers to get any that aren't installed when you install your system.

Anything other than that and it's going to be very hardware specific.  If you have something not working properly, like a "JOE-SHMOE 0821479" wireless card, then I would recommend just googling for that and tacking the word "ubuntu" on the end of your query.
