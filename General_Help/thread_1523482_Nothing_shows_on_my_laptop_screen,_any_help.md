Although it is probably only a matter of using 'Monitors' to configure correctly, or perhaps checking if proprietary drivers are recommended for your graphics adapter and then using their config tool, to correct this without reinstalling: Could you consider just trying to reinstall without having an external monitor plugged in?

Just booting off the LiveCD without plugging in an external monitor will tell you whether or not the distro you are trying can manage that laptop's display!
