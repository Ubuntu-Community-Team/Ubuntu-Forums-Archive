### Post by lithopsian on 2011-01-18
512MB is adequate, but you won't want to be running more than one big app at once.  More is better.

The CPU may seem sluggish with loading big directories, etc, but it will certainly work.  You might find yourself turning off things like compositing, and maybe wishing you'd got a more lightweight desktop :)
