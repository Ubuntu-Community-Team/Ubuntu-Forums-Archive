### Post by wojox on 2009-12-01
You could try hacking around in System > Preferences > Sound > Hardware.

My system defaulted at Analog Stereo Duplex and my sub-woofer sounded horrible, so I changed it to Analog Surround 4.0 Output and it cleared it up.

Also there's Multimedia Systems Selector to try different Plugins.
