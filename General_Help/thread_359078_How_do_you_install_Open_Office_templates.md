### Post by merlinus on 2007-05-15
If the templates are in .ott format, you can do this.

In Nautilus or Thunar, select Show Hidden Files.  Then in your /home directory, you will see a folder named .openoffice.org2

Click on it, then on user, then template, and copy the template files into that folder.

They should then be available from Open Office in File/New/Templates and Documents.
