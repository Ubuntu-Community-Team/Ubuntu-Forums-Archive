### Post by snova on 2008-11-29
KDE is a little wierd. Rather than a static desktop folder, you have "Folder Views", each of which simply displays the contents of a folder. You can have as many of them as you like.

First you have to unlock the desktop so you can edit it. Right click on the desktop and press "Unlock Widgets".

Then you add another Folder View. Right click again and press "Add Widgets". Search for "folder" and you should find it. Drag it wherever you'd like to put it.

Then you have to point it somewhere. Mouse over the new folder view and click the Configure button (looks like a wrench) on the black bar, and change the target as desired.

I suggest you lock the widgets again after you're done.

(Probably a little over-detailed, but why miss anything?)
