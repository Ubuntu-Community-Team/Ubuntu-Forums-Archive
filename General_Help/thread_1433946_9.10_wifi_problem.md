### Post by pcdave98 on 2010-03-30
When I installed 9.10 I couldn't see any wireless networks either- even though my wireless card had been detected.
I went to system>preferences>network connections and added my home wireless connection by putting the SSID in the wireless tab and then putting the security type and key in the security tab.
It connected straight away - and I could then see all the other local wireless networks as well.

It was a stroke of luck TBH.
