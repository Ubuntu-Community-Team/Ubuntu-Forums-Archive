### Post by notwen on 2008-11-24
I use uShare when I stream audio/video to my 360.  I have a single shared folder, which I generally add/remove stuff from and have uShare setup to start upon bootup.  I generally stream more video over audio, although I will say audio files are a mess when trying to play them over upnp on the 360.  I'm sure it's fixable if I wanted to take the time.  I would recommend trying uShare to see it if suits your needs before paying for another app.  

[Here's]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=848144") a pretty simple straight-forward how-to for uShare.  Just copy & paste.  Hope this helps.  =]
