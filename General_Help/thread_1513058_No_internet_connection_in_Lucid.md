### Post by Pollox on 2010-06-19
Sort of hard to diagnose with that amount of information.  Are you connecting to a router?  Wired or wireless? If wireless, did you try plugging in to your modem/router with an ethernet cable?  Does nm-applet say it's connected, but then web pages won't load?  Note that not being able to connect to the internet is different than not being able to connect to your network.  When you right click on nm-applet, is the box to enable networking checked?  Those are some places to start.
