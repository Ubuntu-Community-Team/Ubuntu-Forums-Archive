### Post by jimcooncat on 2005-04-29
Found my answer, a Firefox extension called WebmailCompose.

[https://addons.update.mozilla.org/extensions/moreinfo.php?id=206](https://addons.update.mozilla.org/extensions/moreinfo.php?id=206)

Also found an extension to give the Yahoo mail compose box pretty fonts and smilies, "eWebMail - Color and Graphics".

[http://patsis.brownhost.com/extxpi.html#ewebmail](http://patsis.brownhost.com/extxpi.html#ewebmail)
