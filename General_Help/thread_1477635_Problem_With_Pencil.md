### Post by ju2wheels on 2010-05-09
I think you will have a better chance of getting it resolved by posting the problem to the application's forum here: [http://lesstooges.free.fr/pencil/forum/viewforum.php?id=4](http://lesstooges.free.fr/pencil/forum/viewforum.php?id=4) . However since their latest release was two years ago, it may be a while before you see a fix if any (but their dev wiki shows changes as late as December of last year so people are still working on it.

Also, when I manually resize the Pencil window to see how small I can make it, it stops at 747 pixels tall (not including the window's title bar) so taking that into consideration and adding the pixel height for top and bottom Ubuntu panels, I would definitely consider this an app layout bug.

If you are working from a laptop, you may have to connect to a monitor with higher resolution as a temporary solution.
