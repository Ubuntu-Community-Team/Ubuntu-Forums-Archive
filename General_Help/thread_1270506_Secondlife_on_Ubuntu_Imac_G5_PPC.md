### Post by tgalati4 on 2009-09-19
Snowglobe works on Jaunty with Intel hardware.  Don't know if snowglobe has been ported to PPC, but it's worth a search.

You need 3D accelerated drivers.  Your G5 probably has ATI Radeon graphics which are tricky to get working.  You might have better luck under Tiger where the graphics support is solid.
