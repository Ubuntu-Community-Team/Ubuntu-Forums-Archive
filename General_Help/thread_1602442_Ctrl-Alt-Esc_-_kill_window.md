### Post by sgosnell on 2010-10-21
It's the Force Quit Button.  You can add it to any panel, and it works well for me.  You can also bind it to a key sequence, I think, if you prefer that, but just putting it in the panel is the easiest for me.  Two clicks and the offending window is gone.
