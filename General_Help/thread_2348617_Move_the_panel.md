### Post by ajgreeny on 2017-01-05
That "Move" that you see in the context menu is not to move the whole panel but just an individual item in the panel.

After the right click in the panel you will see, greyed out at the top of the context menu, the name of the item you happened to be in at that moment, which if you choose an apparently empty part of the panel is often actually an expanded separator.
