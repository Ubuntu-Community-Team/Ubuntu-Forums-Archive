### Post by darthpenguin on 2010-12-14
If I may offer another suggestion. Try Gnome-Do. With the "Files and Folders" plug-in this is the best launcher I've seen on Linux. Like Qiicksilver on Mac. Tracker works but it's cumbersome. The search isn't live (ie. you gotta hit Return) and you gotta hit tab a few times to get around the Tracker window. Plus Tracker dosn't automatically close when you are done with it. With Gnome-Do you just press super+space, start typing, and hit enter when the desired file is found. That's it, 3 key strokes. Super easy. It also launches programs, log out, restart, shutdown, and more. Check it out.

sudo apt-get install gnome-do
