### Post by dilb on 2009-08-18
As far as I can remember (on a different computer, so can't check) you can disable Firebug one of two ways:

[LIST=1]
[*]There's an icon in the status bar that you can right click and press 'suspend'
[*]You can disable it completely by opening Tools > Add-ons > Extensions > Firebug > Disable
[/LIST]

That should do it.
