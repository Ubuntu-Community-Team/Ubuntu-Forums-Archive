### Post by mervg on 2007-01-27
Nautilus file browser randomly (& often) displays the two partitions from my external drive, even though it has not been asked to do this.  It is not a serious problem, but is a sizable pain in the neck, having to close these pages - which can be as many as three or four pages for each of the two partitions.  Any chance someone knows how to put the kibosh on this behavior?

---

### Post by fragos on 2007-01-28
Application-> SystemTools-> ConfigurationEditor-> apps folder-> Nautilus folder-> Desktop folder-> uncheck volumes_visible

This will remove the partition icons from the desktop but will also remove the icons that pop up when media is placed in a card reader.  You will need to open Nautilus to see these in that case.
