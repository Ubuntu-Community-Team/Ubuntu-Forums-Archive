### Post by coffeecat on 2011-04-12
> **jasonmania said:**
> 
[LIST]
[*]ISO maker and ISO mounter
[/LIST]
If you mean to make an ISO of a CD or DVD, you have that installed already. Applications > Sound & Video > Brasero. Click on "Disc Copy". In the little window, under "Select a disc to write to", select "Image File" in drop-down

For ISO mounting, no need to install anything. Right-click on the ISO file, and "Open with Archive Mounter".
