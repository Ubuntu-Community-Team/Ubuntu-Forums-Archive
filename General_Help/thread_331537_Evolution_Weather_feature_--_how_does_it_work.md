### Post by dninja on 2007-01-15
Go to the calender, right click and chose new calender. Change the type to weather then chose your location.

On my version, only the US and some Atlantic Islands are supported. I don't know how to get others but if someone can tell me how to add a forecast for the UK it would be appreciated.
