### Post by bobshowrocks on 2007-07-10
IMO (so take it with a grain of salt) Compiz hasn't matured to the point of being useful besides showing off to your windows using friends. It does look nice and some features I could really get used to, but I usually find myself just draging windows around just to see the wobbly effect. This may just be me though. I wouldn't consider Compiz a deal breaker, maybe you should try it out (and ask for help if the install doesn't work right) and see for yourself before your first paper is due :).

And with WINE, you can check out their application database ([here]("http://appdb.winehq.org/")), just put in the name of the game your looking for in the search box and it should come up with it, there you will be able to find it's rating ., and people's posts about bugs they have encountered and fixes. It looked like Diablo 2 was a fairly well running game as is. The other two might take some tweaking, but those tweaks are usually a one time thing, then the game runs fine. But you should check this out before buying if gaming is a deal breaker for you.

And have fun switching over to the Penguin!
