### Post by mkasper on 2007-09-07
Greetings.

After some issues setting up a printer (konica minolta), i decided to write this doc. Hope it helps some poor soul out there. 

I've written the document specifically for the Konica Minolta c450, but the process is the same for all other Konica Minolta printers ( and any other printers as long as you have the drivers)

I've also attached a document which is the same thing but with pictures.


1. First download the drivers for the Konica Minolta Printer 

   -  Open your browser and got to [http://onyxftp.mykonicaminolta.com/download/Search.aspx](http://onyxftp.mykonicaminolta.com/download/Search.aspx)

   

2. Choose your particular printer
   - In the “Product Group” section, Select “Color Products”
   - In the “Product” section, Select “C450 standard controller”
   - In the “File Type” section, Select “Drivers”
   - In the “OS” section, Select Linux
   - Click Show to view the drivers


3. Download the drivers
 - download the zip file onto your local machine 
  - Unzip the zip file onto a folder on your local machine

4. Install the drivers.
   - On your local machine, click “System->Administration->Printing”
   - Double Click on “New Printer”
   - Choose “Network Printer” radio button
   - Choose “TCP/Socket, HP JetDirect, Raw Connection” from the drop-down box
   - Put the ip/hostname of your printer
   - Click on “Install Driver”
   - Browse to the location where you unzipped the drivers
   - Choose a .ppd file and Click “Open”
   - Select the manufacturer of your driver (Konica Minolta)
   - Select the driver you just installed (C450)
   - Choose a name and voila “Bob is the aunty who molested you”
