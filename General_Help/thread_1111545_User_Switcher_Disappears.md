### Post by scathmandra on 2009-03-30
Using Ubuntu 8.10 on a Dell Inspiron 1501.

On my top panel, the nice little app which showed the user currently logged on and the status of a web messenger has disappeared.  Is there any way I can get it back/reset the panel?

---

### Post by Tibuda on 2009-03-30
Right-click an empty space in the panel and select "Add to panel", and you can add the user switcher.

---

### Post by scathmandra on 2009-03-30
I can do that, but it doesn't put it in the right place--top rightmost--and I can't seem to move it...

### Post by lisati on 2009-03-30
> **scathmandra said:**
> I can do that, but it doesn't put it in the right place--top rightmost--and I can't seem to move it...

Right-click on the items you wish to rearrange, uncheck "Lock to panel", right-click again, click on "Move", and rearrange to your heart's content. You might wish to re-check "Lock to panel" afterwards
