### Post by aeiah on 2011-03-07
its more difficult with things other than vocals as far as i know. the idea is that vocals are usually panned centrally in the mix. the method involves cancelling out any sound that's identical between left and right channels. if the drums are mixed this way, then they'll be removed too. give it a try:
[http://audacity.sourceforge.net/help/faq?s=editing&i=remove-vocals](http://audacity.sourceforge.net/help/faq?s=editing&i=remove-vocals)
