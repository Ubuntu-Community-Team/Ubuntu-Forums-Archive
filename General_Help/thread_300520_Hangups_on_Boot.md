### Post by the ev on 2006-11-15
I don't know if it's just my setup, but for a rather long time Ubuntu would hang indefinitely after a soft boot/reboot when the progress bar first comes up after the bootloader.  I'd have to interrupt the boot sequence with a hard boot, and only then would Ubuntu load (happened with both i386 and x86_64). 

On a whim, I decided to route my keyboard and mouse (both USB) directly into my PC and not through my Apple Cinema Display, which also dubs as a USB hub...and now it doesn't hang on the bootup.  Like I said, I don't know if it's just the ACD or any USB hub, or if it's a bug in Edgy (6.10), but I thought I'd throw that tidbit out there in case anyone is having a similar problem.
