### Post by ajgreeny on 2009-10-05
Epson printers are much more likely to dry out than HP as they have permanent inkjets; HP changes the jets with the cartridges, so I would suggest they are the best for small amounts of print output.

Remember, you can not compare an inkjet with a laser printer.  Laser printers seldom block up in the same way as they do not use liquid ink, so do not dry up.
