### Post by darolu on 2010-05-03
Not in such short time (a month), I think new versions of the .iso file/cd/dvd will come out but not in a month, probably in 6 months or more, just like it happened with the previous LTS (8.04).

You just claim there are bugs, but you don't mention any specific one, which makes it really difficult for any of us to help you solve your problem; the only one you -quite poorly- described is a Grub one, I personally didn't experience any problem with Grub at all, maybe ellaborating a bit more will get someone to help you or if is indeed a bug, it will help the developers to fix it (posting in launchpad-bug would be more effective though). When there are bugs, (and I know there are some of them -i.e. the plymouth resolution when using Nvidia proprietary drivers being the only one I've experienced- it's almost impossible to find a bug-free OS) they usually get fixed and you receive the upgrade/fix online, using the update manager or aptitude, this process doesn't take long and is (imo) always worth to keep your system up-to-date.
