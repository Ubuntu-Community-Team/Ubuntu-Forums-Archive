### Post by kevinlyfellow on 2005-03-01
On some motherboards the cd player won't work, because typically the cd player is connected straight to the sound card.  You need a program to read the cd in an indirect way.  I suggest using xine which has always worked for me in the past and present, and it supports cddb.  As for playing mp3's you can use many different pieces of software, my favorite is xmms, and for a command line player I use mpg321.  xmms can be installed through apt-get and you already have the xine engine under totem (which has never seemed to work for me even though it is just a front end).
