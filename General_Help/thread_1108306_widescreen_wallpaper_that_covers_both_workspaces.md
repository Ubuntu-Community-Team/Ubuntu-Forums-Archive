### Post by BslBryan on 2009-05-04
First, use GIMP to split the photo in two, three, four, whatever.

Next, go [here.]("http://anuragbansal.wordpress.com/2008/05/10/how-to-get-different-wallpapers-on-each-workspace-in-ubuntu/")

It works on Hardy and should work on Intrepid, also.  These instructions assume you have Compiz or equivalent, because you need to have the "Advanced Desktop Effects Settings" to follow the guide.  

I hope this helps.  If not, Googling things like "different wallpaper each workstation ubuntu" should.  

Good luck. :)
