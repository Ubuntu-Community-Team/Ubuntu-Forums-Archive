### Post by JackRock on 2010-02-07
1.  Yes.  The router stands independently, and is irrespective of varying OSes.
2.  Both.  Routers typically us TCP/IP protocols to transport data, and both Windows and Linux use this protocol.
3.  Price is subjective, as a low price router is often a low quality router.  I personally prefer Cisco brand, but that's just me.
