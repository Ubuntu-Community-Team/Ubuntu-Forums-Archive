### Post by cloyd on 2010-09-23
Simple Scan and Xsane are applications. 9.10 came with Xsane. 10.04 came with Simple Scan. I found them under the graphic section of the application menu. I like Xsane better, but both seem to work well with my printer/scanners. If you don't have either one of them, they are available under the graphic section of the Ubuntu Software Center. There are other scanning tools available there too, but I don't know much about them.
