### Post by kazztan0325 on 2012-02-19
Hi ELD,

You would be able to get rid of the strange menu bar with **Cinnamon Settings**.

1. Open "Cinnamon Settings" window.
2. Click on "Desktop".
3. Tick off against "Have file manager handle the desktop". Done.
