### Post by Ryan Dwyer on 2010-05-01
You could start running the development version after they create the repositories. Watch packages.ubuntu.com to see when maverick is created, then replace lucid with maverick everywhere in your /etc/apt/sources.list.

You probably won't notice any differences at first, but they'll come eventually.
