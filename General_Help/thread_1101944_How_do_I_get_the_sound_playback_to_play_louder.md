### Post by zvacet on 2009-03-21
Did you tried to play with **sound control**?  System>preferences>sound control and if you don´t see it system>preferences>main menu>sound& video> check sound control to show on your apps menu.When you open sound control be sure that you have master,PCM an front checked and of course other if you like it.This is very basic so if you try this and still having problems just post here.
