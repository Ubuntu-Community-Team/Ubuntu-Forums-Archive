### Post by NightwishFan on 2009-08-12
You have to be careful not to install in the wrong area. Ubuntu shows you what the disk geometry should look like after the installation. As for reverting, I doubt you will get a satisfactory result, though you may get some things back. I must stress if you are not familiar with partitioning to try the WUBI installer, which integrates with Windows and will not delete data.

As for the actual recovery, I am not sure how, though someone should be able to help you out.
