### Post by Therion on 2008-11-21
You need the Compiz Fusion icon:

[http://tombuntu.com/index.php/2007/08/26/compiz-fusion-tray-icon/](http://tombuntu.com/index.php/2007/08/26/compiz-fusion-tray-icon/)

With that in place you can choose what your default Window Manager is (you'll want Compiz) and what the default Window Decorator is (I'm thinking you'll want Emerald, but I'm not sure).  You'll have to play around with the options to figure out which combination hits on exactly what you want.
