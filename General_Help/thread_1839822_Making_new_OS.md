### Post by 2F4U on 2011-09-06
While shell scripting is important, all current distros make extensive use of more advanced scripting languages such as Perl and Python. You may wish to place those on your list too. An interesting project, which gives you an idea what it means to build your own Linux system is Linux From Scratch

[http://www.linuxfromscratch.org/](http://www.linuxfromscratch.org/)

If you follow their guide it will teach you how to build your Linux system from source code. While this doesn't require any programming, you get a feeling what is needed to build an operating system.
