### Post by &amp;KyT$0P# on 2019-08-25
Start by opening Firefox and going to [FONT=Courier New][noparse]about:profiles[/noparse][/FONT], which profile does it say is in use?  It should also give you the full path to the profile folder (it calls it "Root Directory").  That's where to copy the files to.

### Post by ajgreeny on 2019-08-25
Next time you need to do something like this, ie restore your FF profile (and I assume all your personal folders and files), simply backup everything in your /home folder, including all the hidden folders and files, and restore everything before you start the new OS.

Backups are very important and are something you ***must*** do at all times; hard disks can and do fail and without a backup you'll be in a very unfortunate position.
