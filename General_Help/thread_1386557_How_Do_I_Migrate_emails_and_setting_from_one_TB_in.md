### Post by aysiu on 2010-01-20
Copy /home/*username*/.mozilla-thunderbird to C:\Documents and Settings\Application Data\Thunderbird

or /home/*username*/.thunderbird to C:\Documents and Settings\Application Data\Thunderbird
