### Post by jmszr on 2009-08-05
jrizzlecincy,

Isobuster is a CD/DVD data recovery tool. To burn your .iso to CD you will need to use another app. I would suggest ImgBurn or InfraRecorder (both free); on either one you will want to "write image to disc" to burn your .iso to a CD.

This should be of help: [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BurningIsoHowto](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BurningIsoHowto)
