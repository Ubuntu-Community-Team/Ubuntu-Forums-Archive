### Post by ricardo.gloe on 2009-11-08
If your 2nd HDD is being recognized by the computer and OS, it should appear in Places, under computer.

You can also check the /media folder, the HD should be there.


If it's not, first thing is see if the computer is actually recognizing the 2nd HD. 

Enter BIOS (hit del or sth before boot sequence (GRUB) is initiated) to see.
