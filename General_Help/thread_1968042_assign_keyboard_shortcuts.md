### Post by Toz on 2012-04-28
Its a bit confusing.

[LIST]
[*]Click the "+Add" button and for the command, enter **gimp**.
[*]Click on "Ok"
[*]When the "Command Shortcut" dialog pops up, press the shortcut key combination you want.
[/LIST]
The new shortcut will appear in the shortcuts window and will be active.
