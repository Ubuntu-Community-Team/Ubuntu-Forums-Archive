### Post by pippy on 2007-03-14
Try installing The tablet driver in wine. Do this by downloading the tablet driver from the manufactures website. 
Then navigate to your wine c drive folder (usually in /home/YOURNAME/.wine/drive_c/) and copy it in with "cp /home/YOURNAME/Desktop/DRIVERNAME.exe ." then launch it with "wine DRIVERNAME.exe"

I had limited success in flash8 in wine, but your driver might work.
