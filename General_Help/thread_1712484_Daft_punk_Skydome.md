### Post by Rhubarb on 2011-03-22
The dimensions of skydome pictures are usually either 2048x512 or 4096x1024.
This little tutorial may be of help: [Compiz skydome - simple how-to]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=562867")
There's plenty of pre-done skydomes here: [http://compiz-themes.org]("http://compiz-themes.org/index.php?xcontentmode=6110&PHPSESSID=b473fbecd831cb85d44084a4a626925e")

It's a good learning exercise to do if you want to make nice-looking skydomes.  You'll need a panoramic picture / photo, and gimp.  If you have several photos that need to be glued into 1 panoramic photo I can recommend using hugin.

If you can find something that'd be nice to use as a daft-punk skydome, and you're having some troubles making it look right, send me a link to the picture, and when I have time I'll see what I can do.
