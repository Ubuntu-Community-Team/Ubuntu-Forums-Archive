### Post by (_8*(l)Homer on 2009-01-10
Depending on what features you are looking for, and how you intend to use it, there is a huge range of products available.  

I do not sail, but I do build maps.  I use software called Andren.  You can plot Lat/Lon, Loran -C, and GPS (I think).  It can then build a map of the coordinates you plug into the software.

Google the comany, Andren, and there is a plethora of information on their web site.
