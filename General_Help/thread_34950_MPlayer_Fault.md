### Post by dulljeff on 2005-05-17
I cannot help you with the mplayer issue, but I can tell you how I'm watching DVDs at the moment.

Before Ubuntu I used Gentoo with MPlayer for everything (but xine for DVDs) and I liked it very much. Still I wasn't very happy to have to use two different media players.

The normal Totem player isn't very good in my opinion, either. But try the totem-xine player (in Synaptic the totem-gstreamer player will be removed), for me it works like a charm plus I come to like it, too.

For installation guide of codecs and DVD support look [here](http://ubuntuguide.org/#codecs) . And the totem-xine can be installed, as described, with Synaptic.
