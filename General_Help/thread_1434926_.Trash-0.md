### Post by solitaire on 2010-03-20
.Trash-0 is basically where deleted files go when you delete them. If you want to delete the contents then check the bottom right of your desktop. right click on the bin icon and select "empty the deleted items folder" that will empty out the .Trash-0 folder.

it "should" be safe to delete the .Trash-0 folder.

Be Very Carefull with the next line:
You can also by-pass the "Trash" folder by holding down the "shift" key when you press "delete". (you will not be able to recover items from the trash bin if you use "shift+delete")
