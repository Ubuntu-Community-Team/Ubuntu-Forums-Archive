### Post by lisati on 2009-12-04
You might want to consider using a different server for your software. Click on System->Administration->Software Sources.
A box asking for your password should pop up - type in your normal system login password.
Then click on the "Download From" button, then "other" followed by "select best server".
After a delay while different options are checked out automatically (it could take a while depending on your connection), you'll be given the chance to choose your server.
