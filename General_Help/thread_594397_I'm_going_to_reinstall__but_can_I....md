### Post by taurus on 2007-10-27
If you have a separate /home, then you can mount that same partition to /home but don't format it.  (You do want to format / though).  Then, all your personal settings and files should still be there when you first log in.

p.s.  Did you remember to mount your external harddrive first before you tried to access it?
