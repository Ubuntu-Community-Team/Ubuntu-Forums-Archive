### Post by Alwimo on 2011-07-05
Unfortunately yes, it will take an hour or two to update from 11.04 to 11.10 if you do it through the update manager, and yes there will be a risk that the programs won't work properly. 
 
But it will take about as long as it took you to install 11.04 if you install it from a CD/DVD or USB device. If you have an external drive, you just put your documents on that before updating (which you should do anyway, just in case) and then copy the files back to the computer.
