Occasionally, the power off button on the panel goes missing on my Ubuntu 10.10 installation.  I can't seem to notice any pattern to it.  I can shut down from the command line just fine, but how can I get the button back?

---

### Post by yetiman64 on 2010-12-06
Right click on the panel and select "Add to Panel", from the pop up window scroll down and select "Indicator Applet Session", this should add it back.

Edit: I'm on 10.04 it may be named differently in 10.10 but I think it should be one of the "indicator applet" entries,
