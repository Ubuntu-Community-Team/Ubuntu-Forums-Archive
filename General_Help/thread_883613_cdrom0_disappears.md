### Post by Bucky Ball on 2008-08-08
SOLVED: In soundjuicer, go Edit-> Preferences-> Music Folder.

For some odd reason, the target folder (Music Folder) had changed to 'Media'! Can't figure that out but all works after changing this back to 'Music' (or whatever folder you wish to extract to). :)
