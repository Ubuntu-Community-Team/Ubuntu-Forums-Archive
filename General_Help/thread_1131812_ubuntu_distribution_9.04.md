### Post by blazemore on 2009-04-21
No you don't.
In fact, you won't even notice. There'll be a few updates between now and then, which will be bugfixes. On release date, there'll be an update which simply takes it officially out of Release Candidate and into the Final release.

If you had the Alpha from day 1, the situation would be the same, you'd be getting updates here, there and everywhere, and eventually you'd have the "final" release (remember, it doesn't end with the release, there's security and bugfixes after that as well).
