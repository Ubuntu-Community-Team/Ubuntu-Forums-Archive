### Post by TBABill on 2011-04-18
> **anoopm101 said:**
> -do i have to install these additional ati driversNo, optional
> -it says canonical could not develop it Correct. They are not permitted to modify the drivers in any way, but they do offer them should you choose to use them.
> -can i keep using the default drivers provided in ubuntuIf you are happy with the default drivers there is no problem using them. Things to watch for are higher levels of heat than normal. All people do not experience this, but something to keep in mind.
> -im using natty Without using a proprietary driver for your card you may experience the desktop differently (2D or just standard Gnome) than if you use a native driver for your card. If you are happy with it as it is, no need to change.
