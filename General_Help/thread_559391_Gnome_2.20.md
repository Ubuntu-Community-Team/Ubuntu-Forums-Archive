### Post by mcduck on 2007-09-25
No, there won't be an update for Feisty. Basically Ubuntu versions are frozen to those program versions they had at the time of release, and only updates provided are security updates and fixes for serious bugs.

Gutsy (Ubuntu 7.10) will have Gnome 2.20.

You *could* update it on your own, but that would require updating quite a large amount of other stuff as well. And since after that you would already be over half way into Gutsy I'd suggest just waiting for Gutsy release and then upgrading to it. It's much less trouble, most likely takes less time, and you'll get updated versions of many other programs as well as the new Gnome.
