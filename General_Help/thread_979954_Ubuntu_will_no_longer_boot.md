### Post by odysseusjak on 2008-11-12
You might try recalibrating your battery.  I noticed that mine did this and I was didn't know what to do.  I wound up reinstalling and found that only acpi=off worked.  So, I tried recalibrating the battery (my BIOS has this feature) and then it booted just fine.
