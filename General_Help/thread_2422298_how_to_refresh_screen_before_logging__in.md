### Post by dragonfly41 on 2019-07-05
I had a similar problem when my main laptop screen failed and I had to switch connection to an external monitor.
Try hitting <Superkey> + <P> through a cycle of three. 
This tip applies also before logging in. Look to see if your cursor disappears off the edge of screen.
Search this forum for SuperKey for relevant ideas.
Also look at System Settings > Screen Display.
