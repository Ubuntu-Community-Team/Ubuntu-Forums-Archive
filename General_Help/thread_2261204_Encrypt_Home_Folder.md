### Post by ajgreeny on 2015-01-17
Just be absolutely certain that you understand all the implications of encryption and what it means should you forget the password or phrase, ie, you're well and truly stuffed!

Also make sure you have good backups of everything, made incrementally at regular intervals, and kept somewhere safe, not on the same machine, just in case.
