### Post by mogliii on 2007-02-18
Please be careful with Fat32 and hibernation in Winxp!

When you hibernate winxp, and change something on the fat-harddrive with your linux, and reenter into winxp, the harddrive content will NOT! be updated unless you reboot or you go to Setting -> Computer Administration and first remove the drive letter and then assign a new one!

This is not accepted as bug, so there will be no fix for this.
