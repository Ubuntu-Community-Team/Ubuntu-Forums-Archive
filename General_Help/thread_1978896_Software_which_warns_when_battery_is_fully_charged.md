### Post by Enigmapond on 2012-05-12
Leaving your laptop plugged in all the time is not what destroys the battery life...constant depleting and recharging does. There only a limited amount of recharging the cell can endure. You should only use the battery when you absolutely have to. The charger is not constantly charging...it shuts off and switches to AC when plugged in and the battery is fully charged..in essence, the fully charged battery is inert when plugged in and only activates when you unplug it.
