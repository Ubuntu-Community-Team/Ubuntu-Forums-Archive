### Post by iaculallad on 2008-06-17
Navigate to your System->Administration->Software Sources, under the "Ubuntu Software" tab, select "Main Server" as your "Download From". Click on close and open your terminal:

```
sudo apt-get update && sudo apt-get upgrade
```
