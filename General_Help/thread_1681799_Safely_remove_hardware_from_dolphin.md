### Post by cascade9 on 2011-02-04
Device notifier is what pops up when you plug a USB device in. 

Its actually a little bit strange at first to use device notifier, and maybe counterintuitive, but it works. To remove a device, go to the system tray, find device notifier (the icon is like half a CD on top of the USB symbol), click it. 

Mouse over the device you want to remove, then on the right of the device name, a little icon pops up (its like an up arrow in a circle). If you mouse over the icon, you'll see a "click tp safely remove..." msg.  

When you click it and the device has been removed the device notifer icon in sys tray changesto a tick fpr a second or two. Also, when removed, if you go back into the device notifier the little up arrow is replaced with a USB (or whatever type of device it is) icon. You can click that to remount the device.
