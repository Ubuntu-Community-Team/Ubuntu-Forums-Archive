### Post by Mark Phelps on 2011-04-28
Running any pre-release versions in Wubi is generally a bad idea.  In many cases, updating them to later versions crashes the PC.

You would do better removing the Beta version and installing the Final version, instead.

Also, check the Release Notes before you install the Final version.  There are very often Wubi-related issues -- and they are mentioned in the Release Notes.
