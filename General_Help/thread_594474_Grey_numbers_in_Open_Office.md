### Post by strabes on 2008-01-16
You are experiencing [this bug](https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/openoffice.org2/+bug/134415) in openoffice. A very lame workaround that I posted on that page is to simply change the color of the field shadings to white because they can't be disabled. You can do this in Tools, Options, Appearance.
