### Post by itnet7 on 2010-01-04
If you are running compiz and have the compiz settings manager installed, you can go to Extras and enable the Screen-shot plugin. Once enabled you just need to click and hold your super-key, then left click (hold) and drag a box around the object you would like to screen shot, release the left mouse click and a screenshot should appear on your desktop.

I know that this is probably not as robust as the snipping tool that you speak of, but has made both reports and documentation extremely easy in my experience.

Not sure if this same plugin works similar in kde, I will check when I get the chance,and report back unless someone beats me to it!
