### Post by cariboo on 2009-03-17
There are two ways you can get docky. Install Jaunty alpha 6, I wouldn't advise  this, unless you really know what you are doing, as things are still breaking during updates. The second way is to go [here]("http://launchpad.net/~do-core/+archive/ppa") and setup the ppa in your /etc/apt/sources.list.

Do follow the instructions to setup the pgp key.
