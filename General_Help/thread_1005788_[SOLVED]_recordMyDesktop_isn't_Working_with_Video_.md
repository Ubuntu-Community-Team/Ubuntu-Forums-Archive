### Post by Sugi on 2008-12-08
After triple checking every setting within gtk-recordMyDesktop (gui front for recordMyDesktop) I found this option "Full Shots at every frame".  When enable it will record 3d games IE the games I play.  I guess my games are NOT "known  3d composition", so it didn't auto-enable for me.  Sorry for this silly thread.

THE FIX:
(Check) Full Shots at every frame
"This option should be enabled when recording 3d windows.  It is also auto-enabled when reocrding a known 3d composition window mangaer."

Sugi

PS: I couldn't get my sound working with recordMyDesktop for ages.  Since enabling "Full Shots at every frame" it's working now.  I don't know what to say, that option has nothing to do with sound, but yet my sound recording for my recordMyDesktop's videos are working now.  o.0
