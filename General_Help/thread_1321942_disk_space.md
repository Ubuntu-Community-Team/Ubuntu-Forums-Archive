### Post by mick55 on 2009-11-10
Hi Kmob810

Run the Disk Usage Analyzer GUI tool, that will let you figure
out what files and folders are taking up all the space on your hard drive.
You’ll find the tool on your Accessories menu.
When you start the tool, you'll have a choice of opening
the filesystem or a particular folder, choose filesystem.

cheers
mick
