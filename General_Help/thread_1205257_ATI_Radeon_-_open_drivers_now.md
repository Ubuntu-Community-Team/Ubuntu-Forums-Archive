ATI released the specs a long time ago.  Unfortunately they could not simply open source the current fglrx drivers so a few novell employees were hired to write a new driver from scratch.  The effort is progressing but since they only have 2 full time staff it is taking awhile to reach a polished state.  It could be awhile until it surpasses the nvidia driver in performance and reliability.
