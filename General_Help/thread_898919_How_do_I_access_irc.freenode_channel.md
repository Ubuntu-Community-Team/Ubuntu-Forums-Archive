### Post by Rocket2DMn on 2008-08-23
To access IRC, you need a client that can use the IRC protocol.  Pidgin is capable of this, though another very popular client is called xchat (I prefer this).
You will choose the irc.freenode.net server, then join the channel ##lmms - you can also get help in #ubuntu (though that channel is INSANELY busy).
