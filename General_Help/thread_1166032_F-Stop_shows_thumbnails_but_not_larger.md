### Post by graphius on 2009-06-22
I am guessing that you did not download the files to your own computer. F-spot has the images in its database, but for the full file it is looking for the camera. I had a similar problem when I moved my main photo directory to a different drive on my computer. You need to import the photos, and make sure you check "copy files to the photos folder" (this is selectable under preferences)
