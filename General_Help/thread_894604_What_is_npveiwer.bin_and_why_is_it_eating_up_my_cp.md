### Post by sdennie on 2008-08-19
It's the flash player.  If it's eating up your CPU and Firefox isn't open you can safely kill it.  If it's eating up your CPU while viewing Flash, that's normal.  As it's a closed source app from Adobe, it is generally not well optimized and causes many problems.
