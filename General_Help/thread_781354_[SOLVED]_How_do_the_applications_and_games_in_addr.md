### Post by tzulberti on 2008-05-04
The popularity of a packages is calculated using the option of Popularity contest in the Advance button that appears in the last stage of the installing progress (where it tells you what the installer is going to do).

Nevertheless, if you haven't activated it during install, you can activate it going toy System -> Administration -> Synaptic. There:
Settings -> Repositories, and in the tab of Statics check the checkbox.
