### Post by SuperFreak on 2017-03-30
External drives should not be disconnected until the drive is safely "Ejected" . This is done from Nautilus by right clicking the drive to the left of the Nautilus Window and choosing eject or safely remove. If this results in a pop-up saying the drive is still in use you will not be able to eject it until it is finished the operation (transferring files)
