### Post by coffeecat on 2011-03-15
> **mitk0o0o0 said:**
> Is there any possible system files to tweak to fix the problem?

It's easier than that.

Right-click on any folder and choose 'open with other application'. Tick the 'remember this application' tickbox and choose 'File Browser' in the list. Click on 'open'; problem solved.
