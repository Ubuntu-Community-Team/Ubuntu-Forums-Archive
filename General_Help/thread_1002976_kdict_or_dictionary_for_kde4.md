### Post by Lymer on 2008-12-21
You can still use KDict, the Plasmoid dictionary doesn't have any useful settings to enhance it's usability (yet), and intrepid does have the core libraries for KDE 3.5.10. Just go to [http://linuxappfinder.com/package/kdict](http://linuxappfinder.com/package/kdict) under "hardy-backports" you got both 32bit and 64bit. I needed the good ol' KDict for offline searching words :D
