### Post by unqtious on 2010-12-14
Recently, I had to find a new podcatcher. After trying and testing several programs--including gPodder, Rhythmbox, and so on--I had to go back to an old favorite, Amarok 1.4. For me, it has been the best podcatcher, one that will not only effectively download the podcast but also put it in the correct location in your iPod (I am using 5th generation video). 
 
While 1.4 does not have an easy GUI, like gpodder, it does use a tree that makes it simple to keep track of your podcasts, including what you've downloaded and what you have not. being such a huge Linux OS fan, it's sad that I'm not able to find a more recent piece of software that will work as effectively as this legacy software. :(
 
So, if you go this route, you have to follow specific instructions to install Amarok 1.4 on anything after Ubuntu 9.04. Refer to the following:
 
[http://thedaneshproject.com/posts/install-amarok-14-on-ubuntu-904](http://thedaneshproject.com/posts/install-amarok-14-on-ubuntu-904)
