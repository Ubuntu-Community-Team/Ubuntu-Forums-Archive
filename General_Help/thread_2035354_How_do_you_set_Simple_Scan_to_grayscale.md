### Post by lightning slinger on 2012-07-30
Current version of Simple Scan is 3.4.1. The 1.0.3 version dates back to 2010.

When I need to scan in greyscale I alter setting to text rather than photo.
Document>Scan>Text

Don't know if that works the same for your version!
