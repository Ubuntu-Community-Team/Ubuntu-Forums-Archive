### Post by Spykeware on 2011-02-28
Only the way to play games with a high demand of resources is that you have to have at least 4 GB of RAM memory, 512 MB Video graphic card and a very good Proccessor, like a 2.5 GHz Quad Core or something like that, when you use VirtualBox inside Ubuntu.
Remember, Ubuntu needs his own resources, VirtualBox needs a little bit more, Windows another and the game, a lot... That's why the resources goes down.
By the way, some games can run with Wine HQ, try with it if you have a chance.
