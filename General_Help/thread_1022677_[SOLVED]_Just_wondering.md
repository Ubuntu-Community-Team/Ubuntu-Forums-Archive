### Post by gTinker on 2008-12-27
[QUOTE=dmillerw]
Is there any reason not to use Ubuntu 8.04, like applications that wont work, or other types of problems?[/QUOTE]

Simple answer, no.

Since 8.04 is a LTS (long term support) edition, it will be supported with security upgrades as necessary and the software will continue to work (three years).

Perhaps, you might miss out on a few new features (only matters if you need those features) but you will also miss out on the few bugs that users have encountered with the 8.10. 

I think the LTS editions are a good choice for someone who wants a stable system and/or someone who has trouble solving those bug issues. Other people have different opinions about this.
