### Post by GSF1200S on 2007-09-28
The post right above bears a good suggestion. I could also recommend where I got my laptop...

[www.ibuypower.com](www.ibuypower.com)

Check out there HEL-80. For 2GB, 100GB 7200RPM, 2.16x2 Core 2 DUO@667, and  7600 GeForce (256MB dedicated), I paid $1600, and that was 6 months ago! Its better stats now, or cheaper prices for my stats. Just a thought.. Dell is a good choice. Ive heard there 1505 works well with Linux.

If you get an ibuypower laptop, make sure you get the Intel/Nvidia one, versus the AMD/ATI ones they have.

Keep in mind that a laptop with such power uses power. While my ibuypower came with 2 batteries, it will only go like 1 hour 15mins with Linux running. You could upgrade the battery and change the dynamic scaling of the processor, as well as avoid heavy programs (ie games) to keep the life up. You might want a more ancient set of stats to give you better batt life.

Good luck, and were glad youre using Ubuntu! :)
