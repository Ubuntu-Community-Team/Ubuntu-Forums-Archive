### Post by AndyCooll on 2007-11-18
No it isn't important and can be safely uninstalled. It will make absolutely no difference to your updates. ubuntu-desktop is simply a metapackage, i.e. in effect a one-stop shop listing for installing a collection of apps rather than having to click on each individual one in turn. However, apt stills keeps track of the individual apps once installed.
