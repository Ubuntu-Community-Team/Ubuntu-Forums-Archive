### Post by Mark Phelps on 2011-12-19
The avi extension is nothing but a packaging scheme for Microsoft -- it really tells you nothing about the video codecs needed to view the videos.

Also, I have found Totem to be unreliable over the years regarding which video formats it will use correctly.

You would do better installing VLC Player.  You can find that in the Software Center.
