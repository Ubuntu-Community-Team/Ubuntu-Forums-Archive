### Post by grahammechanical on 2015-05-26
The default installation comes with everything we need and a lot of what everyone of us does not need. I do not play games but games are included. I do not compose presentations but I have Libreoffice Impress. There are many other packages/applications that I could remove as I do not need them.

I would use the Software Centre to install and remove programs - no need for commands - the software centre does a good job of installing and removing applications.

What are your specific needs? Which type of applications do you need to use that are not part of a default installation of Ubuntu? Why not open the Software Centre and search through the categories and see if there is anything there that suits your needs.
