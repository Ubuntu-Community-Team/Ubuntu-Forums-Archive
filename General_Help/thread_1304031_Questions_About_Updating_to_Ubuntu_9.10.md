### Post by kyle2595 on 2009-10-28
I have a question about upgrading to 9.10 from 9.04:  If I upgrade via the update manager, will it keep all of my settings, applications, shortcuts, documents, and everything else?

Here is how to update to 9.10 from the update manager:

To upgrade from Ubuntu 9.04 on a desktop system, press Alt+F2 and type in "update-manager -d" (without the quotes) into the command box. Update Manager should open up and tell you: New distribution release '9.10' is available. Click Upgrade and follow the on-screen instructions.

Source: [http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/releasenotes/910overview](http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/releasenotes/910overview)

The link also has instructions on how to update a server system.
