### Post by Holger_Gehrke on 2024-10-11
What do you mean by "third party" ? The updates happen when your computer goes looking for them. You just have to take control of when it does. Admittedly, for snaps - and Firefox comes as a snap by default - this is not so easy because by default the snap background process goes looking for updates four times per day and does the updates in the background without telling you about it, but the documentation for snap has a page about [controlling updates]("https://snapcraft.io/docs/managing-updates").
