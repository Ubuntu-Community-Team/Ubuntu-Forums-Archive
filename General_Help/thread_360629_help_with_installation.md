### Post by rsambuca on 2007-02-13
Unfortunately you can only have 4 primary partitions on one hard drive.  With your current setup, you will have to delete one of your partitions and resize them if you want to install.  Is one of the partitions quite small (like about 2 G)?  Many off the shelf systems come installed with a recovery partition.  If that is the case, you can copy those files onto CD's and delete that partition.
