### Post by x1a4 on 2008-06-21
Canon provides no Linux support for these.  They do have a CUPS printer for Mac OSX which you could potentially get to work on Linux--CUPS is essentially CUPS regardless of whether it's on Linux or OpenBSD (what OSX is based on).

Look at [thread="836727"]this thread[/thread] for a link to the CUPS driver and how to mount the image
