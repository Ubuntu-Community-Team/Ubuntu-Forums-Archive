### Post by megabuffen on 2011-04-01
If the fans don't rotate as easily anymore you might want to try some lubricant spray. I have seen oil with teflon being sprayed into old CPU fans, making them work smoothly again.

### Post by Joe of loath on 2011-04-01
Open it up, and clean and polish the CPU and the pad where the heatsink attaches to it. Then apply new thermal paste, and your temps will go down significantly.

After 18 months I did this to my dell, and the fan almost never runs now. The stuff they use in the factory is pants, and polishing the CPU and heatsink works wonders.
