### Post by Mr_JMM on 2008-11-02
Many thanks. That fixed it.

For the benfit of others, I couldn't right click on the Home folder under the Places tab as this tried again to open in Gedit. Instead, I had to go to the Computer folder and work back to the / directory where I could then right click on the Home folder and select Properties -> Open Folder.

Doing this for the Home folder then corrected all other folder issues including the Wine -> Browse C: Drive.

Odd that it only effected the Home folder from Places Tab and no where else.
