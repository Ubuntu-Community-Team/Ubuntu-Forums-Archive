### Post by mmsmc on 2010-12-16
if you cant do it with a cd(i couldnt) download xp onto a USB or  transfer the files from the cd to the usb drive, make sure you have  atleast 2 free gb on the drive, then get onto your BIOS(restart your  comp and continuously press f2, f4 f8 or f10, depends on the comp, once  there go to start up and make it so that the comp will start up on your  usb) from there you will have the option to install windows xp
i suggest you dual boot
