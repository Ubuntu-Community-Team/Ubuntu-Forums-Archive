### Post by efflandt on 2010-12-25
Most Intel and older ATI cards work right out of the box.  With Intel you get what you get, because I don't think there are any other drivers.  Likewise for older legacy ATI cards that current ATI proprietary drivers do not support.  I have no experience with newer ATI cards.

A lot of people recommend NVidia for Linux.  My computer came with a GT 220, and I later changed that to a GT 430.  Either is much faster using NVidia drivers than nouveau drivers.
