### Post by InspiredIndividual on 2010-05-30
UPDATE: I can confirm that this is probably just another bug in the Open Office version in the repositories of Ubuntu 9.10. The original Sun version is working fine.

Thanks to Hagar de l'Est for his tutorial on installing the original Open Office, which can be found [here]("http://user.services.openoffice.org/en/forum/viewtopic.php?f=16&t=68").
