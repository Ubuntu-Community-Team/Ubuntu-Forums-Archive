### Post by scorp123 on 2008-12-24
> **73ckn797 said:**
> The scenario:
I am browsing the forums and make a reply to a post. Use tabs! When you reply to a post make sure you open the message editor in a new tab. You can do this by middle-clicking on the "Quote" button (e.g. if you want to include someone's quote in your message). With tab's you don't lose the web page you were on previously and therefore don't have to search for it using the "back" keys ...
