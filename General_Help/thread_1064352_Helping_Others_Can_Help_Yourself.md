I just reformatted my computer to remove years of cruft and perform a clean upgrade to Intrepid. Most of my hardware works perfectly out of the box, but my Acer scanner has always given me trouble. I dreaded the hunt for just the right set of instructions to get it running again.

I did a Google search for ubuntu and my scanner type and found an archived forum posting that sounded promising.

To my surprise, it was a posting from me from several years ago, giving directions to a fellow user on getting their similar scanner working. 
The directions still worked perfectly and within minutes I was up and running. 

Helping others can also help yourself!
