### Post by QIII on 2010-05-17
I have read several places that Emerald is a bit behind and hasn't kept up with Lucid, so it is a bit unstable.  Hopefully that will be rectified.

I recently weighed in on another post about Emerald where someone was having problems.

To experiment, I installed Emerald and found a theme that I used to use.  I had trouble with artifacts on screen when I moved windows.  

I also experienced the same behavior you are experiencing.

This does NOT prove that Emerald is unstable, it proves only that I was having issues.  For instance, it may just be a problem with particular themes.  There could be many other users for whom their chosen themes are working just fine.

I'm not at home, so I can't remember exactly, but there should be a folder in your /home with your Emerald preferences.  It's a hidden folder, so navigate to your /home and press CNTR + H to make them visible.

I believe you should find /.emerald, under which you should find /.theme or /.themes.

look through those folders and delete the one you no longer want.

If you would, please post your experience.  I would like to know whether this may be a problem for others.
