### Post by oxidas on 2007-05-30
As I don`t have a card reader and am not planning to buy one in a near future, I actually found an easier solution to the problem. All you have to do is set your connection mode to "PTP" instead of USB and miraculously the problem is solved, the camera is detected, the photos can be imported. However, Picasa seems to dislike it even in this case.
