### Post by FokkerCharlie on 2008-09-02
Hi Enzodad

I have just been having a similar problem.  It turned out that the volume control was changing the 'PC speaker' level.  Try System>Prefs>Sound, and choose ALSA PCM from the menu near the bottom.  If that doesn't work, try some of the other options!

Hope this helps

Charlie
