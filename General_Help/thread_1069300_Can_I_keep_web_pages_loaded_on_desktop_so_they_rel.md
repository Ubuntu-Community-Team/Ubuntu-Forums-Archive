### Post by personjerry on 2009-02-13
I'm fairly sure, that firefox in Hardy Heron also has that capability. At any rate, here's a quick 'hack': kill firefox's process, this will cause it to tell you it was closed prematurely and ask if you want to reload your session. You can make a script to do this and thus an icon from your menus, or there is a gnome applet called Force Quit which is a more generalized version for killing X applications.
