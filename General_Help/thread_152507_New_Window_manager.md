### Post by aysiu on 2006-03-30
Go to System > Administration > Synaptic Package Manager

Click Reload.

Search by **Name and Description** for *fluxbox* and *icewm*. Mark for installation (with a right-click) anything that interests you (*fluxbox* and *icewm* packages being the bare minimum, of course).

Then, click Apply.

Log out.

Click on Session.

Select the session you want (Fluxbox or IceWM) and log in again.

Warning: if you do the bare minimum, these window managers will seem very barren and featureless. That's why I asked you to browse through Synaptic instead of installing just the base packages.
