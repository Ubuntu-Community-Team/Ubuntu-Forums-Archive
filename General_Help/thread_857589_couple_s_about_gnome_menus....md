### Post by jerome1232 on 2008-07-12
As far as adding to the places menu if you just navigate to a folder, say 
/home/user/myfolder and press ctrl+D (or click bookmarks>>add bookmark), it will add whatever folder you are currently in to your places menu.

### Post by sdennie on 2008-07-12
I'm not sure if you can condense the menu into 3 icons but, you can condense it into one icon.  Right click on the panel, click Add to Panel and then add Main Menu.  That's a single icon to represent everything and you can then remove the original menu bar.
