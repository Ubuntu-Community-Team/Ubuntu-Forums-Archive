### Post by komputes on 2009-07-20
in the top right you have a speaker icon; click on it, then click "Volume Control" and then you can check if any are muted and check the levels, usually you would need to raise Master, PCM, Front, and Speaker (although not all of these may be available unless you activate them by clicking the Preferences button).

Hope this helps.
