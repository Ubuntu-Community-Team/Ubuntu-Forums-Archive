TightVNC is a free remote control software package derived from the popular [VNC]("http://www.realvnc.com/") software. With TightVNC, you can [COLOR=#007000]**see the desktop     of a remote machine and control it**[/COLOR] with your local mouse and keyboard, just like you would     do it sitting in the front of that computer.
