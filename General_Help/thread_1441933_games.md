### Post by gadolinio on 2010-03-29
> **nischalinn said:**
> I want to remove **games **from the Ubuntu and others like documents, musics, pictures, videos(from places). How can I do it?

I don't get what you mean by "remove **games **from the Ubuntu". If what you want is to make the Games menu disappear from the applications menu, press ALT+F2, and then type "alacarte" and press enter. The menu editor will open. On the right you'll see the Games menu with a tick; remove that tick and close the window.
To remove videos, etc from Places, open the file navigator (you can open ANY folder). Go to Bookmarks menu, then Edit bookmarks. You'll see your current bookmarks; select and remove the ones you want.
