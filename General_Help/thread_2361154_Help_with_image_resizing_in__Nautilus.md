### Post by Hagar Delest on 2017-05-13
Personally, I prefer to keep the each application for what they are best for. I mean, I prefer to use a picture manager instead of adding extensions to something else, not designed for that in the first place.
See if [XnViewMP]("http://www.xnview.com/en/xnviewmp/") can do what you need; It can batch resize pictures.
Still another application but it may be worth it.
