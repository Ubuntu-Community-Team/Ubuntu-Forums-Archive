### Post by pelle.k on 2009-03-18
Make sure you've not run out of space on your HOME partition. But, i guess you'd notice that anyway, since that would affect other apps as well.
To purge firefox of it's settings, remove the .mozilla directory in your home folder. It's hidden, hence the dot (.) so "show hidden files" in nautilus.
