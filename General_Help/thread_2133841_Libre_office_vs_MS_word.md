### Post by Impavidus on 2013-04-09
The older .doc is better for compatibility. If you don't do any fancy things, the older the better.

If your teacher only has to read your thesis, not edit it (why would he want that? It's your work), he probably wouldn't mind if you send it in as a pdf file. Nothing is as portable as that. Pdf is just not editable. Every word processor can export to that format.
