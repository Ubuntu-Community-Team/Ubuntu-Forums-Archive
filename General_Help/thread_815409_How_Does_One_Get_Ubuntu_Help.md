### Post by bmac on 2008-06-01
I would strongly advise you to repost your problems on the forum and be as descriptive as possible. If you still don't receive any support it may be a bug and you need to file a bug report. I've noticed that many individuals on the forum are more technically advanced than others and your problems (have no idea what they are) may require a member with an advanced skill set. Remember, this forum is a community of individuals with varying levels of experience and competence with respect to Ubuntu. The forum is designed as an interactive forum to share information. Most individuals can only share what they know. Often times it comes down to who is on the forum when you request assistance or who is monitoring post with zero responses - logic might suggest you re-post.

Don't give up simply re-post ask, hopefully someone with the skill set you need will respond. If not I'd suggest searching Google for a technical support specialist located in your region. I searched Google and found 10 in my region....

Good Luck
