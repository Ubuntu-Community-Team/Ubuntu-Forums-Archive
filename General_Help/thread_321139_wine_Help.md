### Post by bodhi.zazen on 2006-12-18
In general, navigare to the .exe in nautilus and doubble click.

Path is "Hidden" so enable show hidden files.

Something like: /home/user_name/.wine/c/Program\ Files/program.exe

From the terminal:```
wine /home/user_name/.wine/c/Program\ Files/program.exe
```

See also [How to wine](http://doc.gwos.org/index.php/HowToWine)
