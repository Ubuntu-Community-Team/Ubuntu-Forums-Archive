### Post by darolu on 2010-08-13
They are just names, the (imo) correct term would be "Command Line Interface" (CLI), the "terminal" we usually use is actually a Terminal Emulator (tty) an actual terminal refers to the hardware you use to input data to a system and the term console may refer to several things from video games hardware to a virtual console... the terminology stuff can go on and on... so lets say they are generic terms to refer to a CLI :p
