### Post by mk1w86 on 2010-02-21
> And how do I go about checking the status of the CMOS battery?

If your BIOS settings get reset every time you do a cold reboot then your CMOS battery is probably dead. :(

To replace the battery you will have to first find out what type it is, on most motherboards it is a CR2032.  The battery will be mounted on your motherboard somewhere. ;)
