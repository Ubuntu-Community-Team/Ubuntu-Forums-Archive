### Post by afrowildo on 2010-08-26
I can't help you with your login problem, but I can offer a bit of friendly advice to a newbie: the Synaptic Package Manager isn't the place to install/uninstall applications. You should use the Ubuntu Software Centre, found under the Applications tab, for that. The package manager is intended for power users who have very precise requirements for their system, and as a newbie, you should never have to go into it.

If I were in your current situation, I would use a LiveCD to recover any important files from the home directory before reinstalling completely, though that's just me.

Welcome to the forum and Ubuntu community by the way. Sorry your first experience of Ubuntu isn't exactly a great one.
