### Post by dez93_2000 on 2011-02-08
unlikely to be seen by people who've not run into the problem but just in case:
yesterday i opened a docx file created in windows vista at work and emailed to myself, edited it, saved, reopened, all was deleted except the title. The most advanced formatting it had was highlighting. Obviously unbelievably frustrating after the work i had done.

Suggestions on the boards (#4 from me) have been:
1. convert to doc (danger of the same problems? probably less since docx is never & less well supported)
2. covert to odf (potential conversion problems when going back into MS office)
3. convert to rtf (good inter-compatibility but eschew advanced formatting)
4. ignore both programs and email it back & forth to yourself (minimal advanced formatting guaranteed, but daily copies of incremental progress saved, potentially in the cloud)

Tonight I'll be trying #1 then #2 & 3 & 4 simultaneously to test tomorrow.
