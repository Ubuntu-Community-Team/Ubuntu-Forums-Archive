### Post by Cpierce on 2010-02-27
Guys, I need help.

 I had emerald installed which controlled my title bar. By title bar, I am referring to the bar which has the buttons to reduce, resize, and close the window. Then I installed some GTK 2 themes and the emerald title bar remained. So I un-installed emerald, including the libemerald file that was left over after removal. I then tried "gtk-window-decorator --replace &" in terminal, and the theme title bar appears. But as soon as I close the open windows, and open a new window, the title bar is gone. If I reboot, no title bar. 

How do I fix this ? By the way, I am using Ubuntu 9.04

---

### Post by Cpierce on 2010-02-27
Guys, This is how I fixed it. I installed "Compiz Icon" then used it to select to use "GTK window decorator". That fixed it !
