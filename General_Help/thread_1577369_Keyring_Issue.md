### Post by themusicalduck on 2010-09-18
Go to System > Preferences > Passwords and Encryption Keys

Right click on Passwords: login and select Set as default.

I haven't tested this myself, it's just according to this post - [http://ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=9217454&postcount=3](http://ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=9217454&postcount=3)
