### Post by saidinesh5 on 2009-04-13
looks like your graphics card's drivers has a problem. why not reinstalling them. download the latest NVIDIA drivers and reinstall them. i hope that will solve your problem.

installing nvidia drivers :
ctrl+alt+f2
sudo /etc/init.d/gdm stop
<goto the place where your NVIDIA drivers are present>
sudo sh <NVIDIA driver filename>
<follow the onscreen instructions>
sudo /etc/init.d/gdm start
