### Post by HammerheadNC on 2008-02-29
Thanks for the quick response. I got home this AM and printed the file from each computer. It looked like the fonts were the same but the spacing was off between them. So then I checked the line spacing options in the settings and they were the same. Then I looked at FONTS. I changed the font on the desktop and the document became the same size on the page.

Then I looked further and discovered that even though TImes was listed as the font at the top it was not in the drop down on the desktop. A little further research revealed that there were more fonts available on the laptop than on the desktop.

A quick check of the Add/Remove in Ubuntu revealed that the laptop had MS Core TT Fonts installed and the desktop did not. I installed this on the desktop and voila - they match!

Thanks again!
