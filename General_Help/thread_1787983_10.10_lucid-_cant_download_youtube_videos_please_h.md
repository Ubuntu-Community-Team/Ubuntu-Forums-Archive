### Post by nothingspecial on 2011-06-23
Youtube videos are now stored in our RAM.

This big long line of gobbldygook will tell you where it is.

```
f="$(ls -l  /proc/$(pgrep -f flashplayer)/fd | grep deleted)" && f="${f##*:+([0-9]) }" && f="${f%% -*}" && echo /proc/$(pgrep -f flashplayer)/fd/"$f"
```
