### Post by KCPokes on 2007-09-11
I had the same thing happen to mine, although it would light up; Windows wouldn't recognize it.  I found a Windows utility, as that was the only machine I had at the time, that allowed me to restore the files from it.

[http://www.pcinspector.de/Sites/file_recovery/info.htm?language=1](http://www.pcinspector.de/Sites/file_recovery/info.htm?language=1) 

Nothing fancy but it was one of the few that had a free version that actually allowed you to recover your files, rather then just see them and wish.
