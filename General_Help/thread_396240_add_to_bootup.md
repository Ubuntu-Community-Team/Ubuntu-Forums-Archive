### Post by chewearn on 2007-03-29
Since you didn't say so, I am assuming you are using Ubuntu (Gnome) instead of Kubuntu, or Xubuntu.

Goto:
System > Preferences > Sessions > Start-up Programs > Add

Enter in the "Start-up command"
beryl-manager

That's all! :)
