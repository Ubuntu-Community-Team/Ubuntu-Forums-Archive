### Post by cogier on 2010-03-23
You need to run System>Preferences>Appearance. Click on the visual effects tab and set it to "Extra" or if your card wont accept that try "Normal".

I suggest you make sure you have 4 desktops (columns) in 1 row. (Right click on the "desktops" on the right of the Panel and select "Preferences")

To test hold down the "Windows" key and press "E". You should see 4 desktops at once. Use the arrow keys to move and [Enter] to select.

You then need to install "CompizConfig Settings Manager" and play!
