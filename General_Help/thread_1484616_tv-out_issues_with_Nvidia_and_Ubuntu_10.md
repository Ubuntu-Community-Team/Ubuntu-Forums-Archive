### Post by Mechoption on 2010-05-30
Well, I was expecting some help from this forum :(

After many things, I found that by forcing the TV resolution to 720x480 in the nvidia settings GUI then everything worked :)  I can get twinview or TV only using only the nvidia GUI and no manipulating xorg.conf by hand, providing the TV res is set correctly (and not on auto.).
