### Post by gennatolls on 2011-11-30
Log into your router through your web browser by entering its IP address to check the channel then you can change it. I doubt that is your problem though since it works in Winblows 7. Your router address is probably something similar to 192.168.1.1 or 10.0.0.1 . I had to change my wpa encryption type to match the encryption deployed by the wifi and it then connected. That was in 10.04 though, good luck.
