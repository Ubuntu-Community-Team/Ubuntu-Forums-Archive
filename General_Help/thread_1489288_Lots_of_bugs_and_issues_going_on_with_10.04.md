> **Drybones5 said:**

I have an HP Pavilion DV5 and my trackpad stopped working twice - once under 9.10, I think, and once under 10.04 last night. Mine failed only  after GNOME login as well.

I don't have a solution per se, but in both cases I was able to resolve as follows:

[LIST=1]
[*]Log in with the mouse enabled.
[*]Disable the mouse.
[*]Press CTRL-ALT-F1 to switch to text terminal 1.
[*]Enable the mouse.
[*]Press ALT-F7 to switch back to X.
[/LIST]

Let me know if this doesn't work for you and I'll try to take the time to figure out exactly what work-around restored functionality for me.
