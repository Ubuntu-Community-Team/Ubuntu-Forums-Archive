### Post by j.bell730 on 2009-08-14
Oh, hmm, let's see if I can do this without being on GNOME...

1. Right click the Applications menu at the top, and click 'Edit menus'.
2. In the Accessories section of the window that has just popped up, click 'New' on the right.
3. In the 'Name' field type 'Scribus', in the command field type 'scribus'.
4. Do the same with any other apps you need added.

I hope I got it right.
