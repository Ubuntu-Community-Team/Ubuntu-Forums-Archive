### Post by Akavashi on 2009-12-23
Have you tried right clicking the little speaker icon in the top right hand corner in the system tray, and selecting 'Sound Preferences'?
Make sure that you have your sound card selected in the 'Hardware' tab, and also the right speaker configuration. If you've already done that, just try pulling the slider on the volume down to 0% and then back up to 100%, sometimes for some reason my sound card likes that :P.
