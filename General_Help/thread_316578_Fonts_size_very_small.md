RgdsYou can try changing that as well.  In Firefox, you can also try hitting the key comibination fo CTRL-+ (that's Control-Plus) that will increase your text size, likewise, CTRL-- (that's Control-Minus) will make the text smaller again.  You can either one of those combinations multiple times to get the desired text size you are comfortable with.  Hitting Control-0 (that's Conrol-Zero) will bring you back to normal font size.  You can find these also in the menu within Firefox under View > Text Size.
