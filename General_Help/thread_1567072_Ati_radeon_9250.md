### Post by Mark Phelps on 2010-09-03
There are no step-by-step instructions because (1) ATI dropped proprietry driver support for your card nearly two years ago, (2) the older drivers (the ones that DID work with your card) will not work in modern Ubuntu verions, and (3) the open-source drivers ( the ONLY ones that will work with your card) are already installed.

The good news -- you have nothing more to do.  The drivers are already there and working.
