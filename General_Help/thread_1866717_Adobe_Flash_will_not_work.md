### Post by Big Lizard on 2011-10-21
Another method of installing is _**IF**_ you use Firefox, there is an add-on called Flash Aid that automatically installs the flash. In the Flash Aid Wizard, it gives you 2 or 3 flash choices. One of them always works. I run Lubuntu as well, and this method works eveytime with any fresh Linux install, I always use this method. After install, go to YouTube and try and play a video. If it doesn't work, run the Flash Aid Wizard again and pick a different flash choice.
