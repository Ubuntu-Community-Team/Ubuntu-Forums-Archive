### Post by gldearman on 2009-08-31
I can answer two of these.

> **PowerSource said:**
> 
*In firefox in windows, when you press the middle mouse button a thingy pops up and when you drag the mouse up and down, then the page scrolls, I want that in ubuntu too.

In Firefox, go to Edit > Preferences.  On the "General" tab, look for the "Browsing" section.  Make sure that "Use autoscrolling" is checked.

> **PowerSource said:**
> 
*When you write e.g. info ls in the terminal, how do you get out of it without closing the window?

Just push the <q> button.  Q is short for quit.
