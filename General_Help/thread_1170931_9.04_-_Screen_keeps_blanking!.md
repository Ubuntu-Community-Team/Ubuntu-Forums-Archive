### Post by Mithrandir95 on 2009-05-28
This fixes it for ATI cards, don't know if it will do any good for you...

Add this to your xorg.conf... 

Section "ServerFlags"
   Option "BlankTime" "0"
   Option "StandbyTime" "0"
   Option "SuspendTime" "0"
   Option "OffTime" "0"
EndSection

### Post by lightenup on 2009-06-23
I do not have screen blanking during playback or while sitting at the xbmc main menu. I resolved my screen blanking issue with the following changes. 

In xbmc I have the screen saver set to 'black' after 5 min.

In /etc/X11/xorg.conf I added the following:

Section "ServerFlags"
Option "BlankTime" "0"
Option "StandbyTime" "0"
Option "SuspendTime" "0"
Option "OffTime" "0"
EndSection

Section "Monitor"
.
.
.
Option "DPMS" "false"

Here is my disable screensaver startup application:

~/.launchers$ cat disable-ss
#!/bin/bash
sleep 60
gnome-screensaver-command -i &


Using my MCE remote in XBMC will bring xbmc out of it's 'black' screensaver. No more VNC'ing into the console to wake up the monitor! Hope this helps.
