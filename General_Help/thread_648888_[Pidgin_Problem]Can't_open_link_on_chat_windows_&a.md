### Post by p_quarles on 2007-12-24
Any reason you haven't tried kopete? It integrates with KDE much better than Pidgin, and it's just as good (I think).

Anyway, here's what *should* fix the problem. Open Pidgin, go to "Tools," select "Preferences," and select the "Browser" tab. It says "Manual" by default, but you can change it to any of the popular browsers, including Firefox.
