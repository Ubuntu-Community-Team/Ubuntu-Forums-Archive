### Post by solwic on 2008-06-29
> **MangasColoradas said:**
> I recently noticed that I am getting graphic faults, like artifacts. I checked 'planet-penguin racer' to see if the problem showed up there and it does, it is now un-playable, the picture is badly broken up.

Try turning off your desktop effects.  Compiz doesn't play nice with ATI cards.  I had the same problem with the games.  Right click on your desktop, "Change Desktop Background"...go to the Visual effects tab and select none. 

That worked for me.  Good luck!
