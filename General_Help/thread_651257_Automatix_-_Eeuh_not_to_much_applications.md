### Post by ajgreeny on 2007-12-27
Just to make sure you get all the restricted format programs you might want and need, have a look at [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RestrictedFormats](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RestrictedFormats) which gives lots of info on things that otherwise seem to baffle new ubuntu users.  The medibuntu repos are most important, I think, especially for the media programs and dvd playback, and can provide everything that automatix could, but without the disadvantages.
