### Post by papibe on 2010-11-28
Google "utorrent server", or go to the utorrent site/forums. It is in alpha state. I haven't played too much with it, but it looks promising. It is not yet very well developed as a daemon/service (no installation, no init and down scripts, etc).

I would advice you to take a look at transmission-daemon. It is very stable and works great. It is specially design to run on a server. It has a cli client, and a web client.
