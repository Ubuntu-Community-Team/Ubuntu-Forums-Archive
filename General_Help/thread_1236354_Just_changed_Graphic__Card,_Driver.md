### Post by P4man on 2009-08-10
ati cards have opensource drivers in the kernel (nvidia too, but they are even more limited). So you don't need any extra drivers for at least basic operation. If you have a new ATI card, its probably still a good idea to install the closed source, binary  ATI drivers for better performance. If its an old card, they won't be supported by them anymore, and you're stuck with what you have now.
