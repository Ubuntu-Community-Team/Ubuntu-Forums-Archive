### Post by Takmadeus on 2008-09-28
Greetings, I am wrtiting this simple complaint because I have seen cases in launchpad where a bug is reported and even the solution added, but time passes and a fix is not released.

I am talking in this case specifically about a package that should be put into much attention since its one of the thing the ubuntu user miught want to use once he gets comfortable enough, and that's the **emerald theme manager**.

[Bug #205364]("https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/emerald/+bug/205364") was reported on march 22 / 2008 (even before hardy went out) which consisted on an erroneously commented line that left the repositories tab unaccesible. now we are in september (almost october) and no fix has been released, even if it only means uncommenting a simple line. **This**by definition is the package mantainer's job, yet he has not said a thing and has not contributed to fix the problem.

Now go ahead ubuntu users of the world, open up the emerald theme manager and tell me where you find the repositories tab, I'll wait right [here]("https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/emerald/+bug/205364") ;)

Now, it is not the unaccesible repositories that get in my nerves, but the sheer grade of incompetence of the mantainer, which just abandoned the package having such a simple and relatively easy to fix bug.

That's why I wanna start an initiative to have all the package mantainers checked up in order to see if they do their job which is to mantain package (Duh).

I would nominate myself to mantain the package, yet unfortunately I know just about nothing about package mantaining, and after al it is his job, and if he won't do it, he could better quit and leave the spot open for someone who will actually care.

Sorry for the rant, but this kind of issues will eventually degrade the quality of the distribution in my opinion.
