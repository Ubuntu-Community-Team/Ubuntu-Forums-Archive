How is do I move a panel from the right hand screen to the left hand screen?

---

### Post by scouser73 on 2010-10-07
I think that you right click on the panel you wish to move, select properties, I think select minimise, and then drag the panel across, and then right click, properties, select expand.

### Post by cklords on 2010-11-21
For those who find this thread on Google and realize that this isn't (i think) the actual solution (no minimize button- and doesn't move even if you uncheck *expand*) the actual Solution is much more elegant and simplier:

Hold down ALT, click any Gnome panel and move it anywhere you like.  

SOLVED ;-)
