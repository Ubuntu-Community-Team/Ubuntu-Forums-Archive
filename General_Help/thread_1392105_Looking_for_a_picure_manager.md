there is an add-on to nautilus that will allow you to right-click on an image or a group of images and compress, resize or rotate them individually,  or as a whole.
 
it is called "nautilus-image-converter" and it is in the repos/synaptic (system > administration > synaptic package manager).  or you could install it in a terminal (applications > accessories > terminal),  by typing or copy/paste the following command:

```
sudo apt-get install nautilus-image-converter
```press enter and when it finishes installing,  log-out and back in again or restart.

after that,  just open your home folder (nautilus), find your images,  right-click on them and see the newly added options.

hope that helps.
