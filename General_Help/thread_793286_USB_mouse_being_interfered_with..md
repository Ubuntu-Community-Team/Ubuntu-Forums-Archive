I am willing to bet you are dealing with something I had some experience with, tell me if this sounds familiar.

Back on Ubuntu Gutsy, I was noticing that my optical USB mouse for my notebook would work, then stop. Other times, it would start moving on its own. Then after about a week of that, it would light up the "laser" (the optical part) for about 20 seconds, only to go dark. When it would go dark, the mouse stopped working until I re-plugged it back in.

Sound familiar? It's a failing mouse. I opted to spring the $20-30 for a new one while hanging onto the receipt in case it was an Ubuntu issue. Sure enough, the problem never was repeated, be it me going from an USB corded mouse to a USB wireless control mouse with separate USB dongle (not Bluetooth however).

Later on, I then took my other USB corded mouse and it also worked just fine for any lenghth of time. Clearly, a failing mouse was the culprit.

Just sharing what worked for me.

Also, if this ever happens with a wireless mouse, change the batteries as this is almost always a symptom.
