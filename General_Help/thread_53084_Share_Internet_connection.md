### Post by spd106 on 2005-07-30
I'd recommend firestarter, which is a firewall that has the option of sharing internet connections through NAT. So you get the advantages of protection as well as increased usage.

It's in the universe repos, so you can use synaptic or apt.

[http://www.ubuntuguide.org/#firestarter](http://www.ubuntuguide.org/#firestarter)
