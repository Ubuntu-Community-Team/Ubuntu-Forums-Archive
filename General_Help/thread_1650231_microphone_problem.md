### Post by Tur Third on 2010-12-21
i had a problem with my mic in Skype.

It was the one where you have to install and go into 'pulseaudio volume control' and change the input control on the 'Input Devices' tab so that mic is not trying to record in stereo. Either change front left or front right down to zero, and leave one at 100%.

it worked for me
