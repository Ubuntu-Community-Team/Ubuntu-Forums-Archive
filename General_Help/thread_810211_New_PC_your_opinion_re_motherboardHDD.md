Your hardware looks fine. If I were making this system, I would get faster ram, and use integrated video (if the integrated video were Intel). That is my opinion, Intel graphics and faster RAM is better than what you have listed.

For 64 bit, you can use 32 bit or 64 bit. Since you are getting 4 GB of RAM, you should use the 64 bit. You can check out the 64 bit forum for installing things that might give you trouble (in my experience, it isn't troublesome). 32 bit OS's will work, but you will have the limitations of a 32 bit platform (not all your RAM will be used, and the processor won't be used to its potential). 

32 bit software works for the most part on 64 bit OS's.
