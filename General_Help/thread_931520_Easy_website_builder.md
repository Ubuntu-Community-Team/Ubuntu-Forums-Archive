### Post by gjoellee on 2008-09-27
Dupal is free and works *online*!
-Works for everyone that have internet
-If you crash it it is just to reinstall or reupload (smart to take a backup at least one time in a week)
-Several admins and other people can be logged in at once!
-There are plugins
-Requires a little knowledge of HTML, and other languages if your site should look good

NOTE: You will need a server to upload Drupal to. An easy FPT server uploader is "filezilla"
```
sudo apt-get install filezilla
``` I use it, and it is truly amazing it makes the job easier! :D
[www.drupal.org]("http://www.drupal.org")
