### Post by ankursethi on 2007-10-21
I know it's just a metapackage, but what are the implications of removing it? I prefer to keep away from clean installs every 6 months since mine is a family PC and I haven't (yet) moved my home to a separate partition. So will I have problems while updating?

Keeping ubuntu-desktop means keeping pieces of Evolution, Serpentine and Sound Juicer, none of which I'm particularly fond of.

---

### Post by por100pre1 on 2007-10-21
If you remove ubuntu-desktop your packages will be updated. However, if there are any new packages to be installed as part of the updated metapackage, they won't be installed. My suggestion to you is remove it for normal use and reinstall it just before upgrading.
