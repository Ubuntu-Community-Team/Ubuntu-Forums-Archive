### Post by drs305 on 2010-07-31
I believe Synaptic is asking for your installation CD. In Synaptic, go to Settings, Repositories and untick the CD in the lower left panel of the Ubuntu software tab. You may also want to check your "Other Software" tab to see if any CDs are listed therein.  When ticked, Synaptic looks for the CD and complains if you have it enabled and Synaptic can't find it.

Reload after deselecting the CD and the problem should be fixed.
