### Post by kidders on 2006-09-15
No way to know I'm afraid ... not all service providers use PPPoE to connect their users. One other common possibility is DHCP-based.

You may already know this, but be aware that PoE (Power over Ethernet) and PPPoE (Point-to-Point Protocol over Ethernet) are not the same thing ... just to be sure :-)

You should probably call your ISP and ask them for the setup instructions for any OS they want to tell you about. It'll be pretty obvious from what they tell you whether PPP (or something more high-level like DHCP) is what you need to use.
