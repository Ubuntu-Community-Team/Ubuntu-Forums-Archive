### Post by GriFF3n on 2009-02-10
Had to uninstall both Gnome-Do and gnome-do-plugins. Then went to ;

[https://launchpad.net/do/+download](https://launchpad.net/do/+download)

and downloaded "GNOME Do 0.7.96 amd64 package". Then went to synaptic and locked the version of Gnome Do and installed the gnome-do-plugins. Working again :).
