1. If I send a message to myself it does not show up in my mailbox even though it indicates it was send successfully and does not bounce back.

3. I have checked my filters and do not see anything that would indicate the messages are being blocked, and they do not show up in my spam or junk folders.

Questions:

1. I suspect that this is not a Thunderbird issue, but related to my provider. Do you think this is correct?
