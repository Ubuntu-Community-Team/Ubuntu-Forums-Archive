### Post by forrestcupp on 2012-02-22
[Here's a good video](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6MJUGVFAXKg) for an idea of a starting point.

If you put a huge amount of time and work into it, it's probably possible for you to make a very minimal OS. But you're never going to create anything as complicated as Linux. As of January, 2011, the estimated cost to redevelop the Linux kernel, figured by time and manpower, was about $3 Billion. That was before Linux 3.0 came out, too. And the Linux kernel is only the base of the entire operating system that we use.

It's probably possible for you to create a very minimal OS, but you're going to have to do *a lot* of research, and you'll be living it for a while.
