### Post by gandaran on 2011-10-15
> I believe that Google Chrome is supposed to have flash preinstalled
only the 32-bits version has it built-in, for 64-bits install the firefox flash-aid addon to get 64-bits flash and remove the 32-bits flash you have installed from system packages.
