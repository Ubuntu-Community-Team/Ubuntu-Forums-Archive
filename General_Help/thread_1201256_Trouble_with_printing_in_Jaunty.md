### Post by Plumtreed on 2009-07-01
I connected the printer to each PC via the USB connector and they each worked after a suitable printer was selected from the list.

I then hooked the printer to the desktop and followed the 'networking printer' procedure. Ticked 'Show printers shared printer etal' and 'Publish shared printers et al 

Went to laptop and, in Settings, ticked 'Show printers shared by other systems' pressed 'OK' and, it was. This time it worked and I still don't know what I was doing wrong previously!:frown:
