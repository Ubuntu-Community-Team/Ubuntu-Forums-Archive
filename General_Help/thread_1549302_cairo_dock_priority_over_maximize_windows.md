### Post by Cavsfan on 2010-08-09
Found it! :popcorn:

Click on the **Compiz** icon in the dock, right click on **Configure Compiz** (top left icon in box),
click on **Cairo Dock** and then **Configure**.

Then under **Behavior**, click on **Visibility** and make sure the **Visibility** at the top is set to 
**Keep the Dock Below Windows**.

Make sure it is in advanced mode or this may not show.
This way it will be hidden until you need it by moving your mouse to where the dock is and it appears! Cairo Dock rulz!
