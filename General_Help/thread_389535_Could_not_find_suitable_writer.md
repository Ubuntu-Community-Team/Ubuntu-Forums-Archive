### Post by hikaricore on 2007-03-20
If you goto the Settings tab, and choose Options, you should be able to click on the Devices item in the options box that opens.

Clicking add device you may be able to add your cd writer manually.

For example mine is /dev/hdc because I have two hard drives then two cd drives, the first being my cdrw.  Your drive location may vary.
