### Post by mb_webguy on 2009-01-26
Yes, all of the updates released through the official Ubuntu repositories are thoroughly tested by the Ubuntu team -- otherwise they wouldn't be in the official repositories!

Updates from other sources -- such as if you've added PPAs or third-party repositories to your sources -- may not necessarily be tested as thoroughly.
