Remember - "physical access is root access".

I know others will suggest encrypting your drive, etc, but those methods will only slow down anyone who really wants your information. Unless you are a "Secret Agent", then your info is useless to others and encryption will stop the casual thief.

Some helpful hints to keep your personal information "risk" at a minimum:

1) Do not store personal info on your computer (like banking info). 

2) If you do online banking, make sure to have Firefox (or whatever browser you use) set to erase all cookies, etc when it is closed.

As for Tomboy Notes encryption - I do not think you can.
