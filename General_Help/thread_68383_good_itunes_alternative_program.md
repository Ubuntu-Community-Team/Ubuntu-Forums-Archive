Rhythmbox is installed by default.  It should be in the Sound & Video section of your Gnome menu.  Banshee is another possible iTunes alternative.  You'll have to get Banshee out of the repositories though.

Amarok is a fantastic music player, but I've had some stability problems with it. Feature-wise, nothing else comes close, probably not even iTunes. It's also a KDE app, so if you're a Gnome user, that might create some extra issues for you.
