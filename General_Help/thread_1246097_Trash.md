### Post by ajgreeny on 2009-08-21
Any trash you delete from your own /home will be there.  All other users will have their own trash, and that includes root, and all usb drives will also have their own trash folders which are hidden, so if you just hit the delete key all the files will still be on the usb drive, but hidden.  A lot of people wonder why the usb drive has no room available after deleting files from it; that is why.

I always have the option in nautilus of adding a Delete, bypassing the trash, in the context menu, and find that I use that a lot, instead of using trash.  It just means I have to be careful that I don't permanently trash something I might need.
