### Post by Dr. Nick on 2005-12-01
You can change your /etc/apt/sources.list and replace breezy with dapper and then run apt-get update then apt-get dist-upgrade and you will have dapper :) No need to download an new iso and resinstall. You can update to pre release unstable version of dapper now but you will most likely have problems.

But when its stable all you need to update is a internet connection, no need for iso reburn
