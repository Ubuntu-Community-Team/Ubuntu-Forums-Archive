### Post by jrox717 on 2009-05-11
I'm a fan of open office, but since I share a lot of documents I find myself automatically saving my files in MS word format. I was just wondering if there was an easy way to make that the default format when I go to save a file? Or if I can do this using abiword (which I just downloaded and kind of like since it's faster on my computer) that would be good too. Thanks!

---

### Post by mb_webguy on 2009-05-11
Tools -> Options -> Load/Save -> General

At the bottom, select a document type in the left drop-down select, then change "Always save as" in the right drop-down select.
