### Post by smuthavarapu on 2010-12-22
Hi If dont care about the Security, then follow these instructions to disable the Key Ring.

From Menu System --> Preferences --> Passwords and Encryption Keys.

Select Passwords: default, Right Click, and Select Change password.

Give the Old Password and Leave the Password & Confirm blank.

Click Ok button. System will give Warning message igonre it.

Logout and Login back I hope the Key ring window will not come back.

### Post by Chirpz on 2011-01-21
This worked for me in 10.04 Lucid.

1.  Right click on the wireless icon in the upper right corner of the screen.
2. Click Edit connections and select wireless.
3.  Highlight your connection, click edit
4.  You may have to type your password
5.  Check the box, apply to all users, and click apply.

When I did this, the keyring password demand at startup went away.
