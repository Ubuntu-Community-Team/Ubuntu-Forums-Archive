### Post by Spin Doctor on 2006-12-07
Hi,

I have experienced the same problem of Ubuntu not able to shutdown. Most times it has happend after my palmpilot sync software failing on me. 

What I do for a quickfix is simply to restart the x window system, by pressing ctr+alt+backspace,  your session will logout, and you can shut down the system from the loginbutton. Or log in again, if you want to for that matter. Problem solved.
