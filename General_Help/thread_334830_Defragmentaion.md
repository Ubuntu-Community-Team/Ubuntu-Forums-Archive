### Post by az on 2007-01-09
Ext3 filesystems do not fragment in the same way as windows filesystems.  You don't need to defragment them.  Fragmentation will only become a problem when you disk starts to get full (ninety percent or more).

That's why there is no defragmentation tool.  It's built-into the way the filesystem works.
