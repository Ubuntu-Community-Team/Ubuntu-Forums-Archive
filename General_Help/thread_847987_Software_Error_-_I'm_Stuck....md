### Post by lisati on 2008-07-03
From the Terminal (it's on the "Applications"->"Accessories" menu), type in 
[code]sudo dpkg --configure -a[code]

You will be asked for your password - just use your normal login password - but don't worry if it is invisible, this is normal.
