### Post by ad_267 on 2008-06-15
You can change your window border in Ubuntu, but the change will affect all applications. You go to System - Preferences - Appearance. You can download more from [http://www.gnome-look.org](http://www.gnome-look.org) in the Metacity section. Metacity is the window manager in GNOME.
