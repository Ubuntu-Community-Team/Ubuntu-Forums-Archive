### Post by crlang13 on 2010-03-12
I think you're thinking of the Netbook Remix (I may be wrong on this though! :p)

The netbook remix is a stripped down version for netbooks (a smaller version of a laptop, small screen, less power).

I originally ran the netbook remix on my netbook but decided to go for the full version.  You can install the remix on a full sized computer (if you want).  It really doesn't matter.  There is no difference on compatibility etc. if that's what you're wondering about.
