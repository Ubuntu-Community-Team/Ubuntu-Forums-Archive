### Post by zataang on 2006-08-12
When you click on a .torrent file in your browser (firefox), it will ask you what application to open it with. Find the location of azureus (type whereis azureus to find it), and specify it as the applicatoin to open the .torrent with. Also check the 'Do this for all files of this type from now on' checkbox... That's it...
