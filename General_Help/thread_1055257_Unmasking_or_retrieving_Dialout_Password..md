### Post by sedawk on 2009-01-30
* The easiest way is to find the letter with the password you got
  from your provider.

* If you are using a DSL-modem (not a DSL-router) your PC stores
  the password. Even if it is stored in a crypted file you 
  can retrieve it my monitoring the communication to the DSL-modem.

* If the password is stored inside your DSL-router things are more
  difficult. Basically there are two ways, one is to eavesdrop on
  the wire (with special hardware or by faking a DSL-modem on 
  the "modem side" of the wire) and the other is to backup
  your DSL-router configuration and then search inside the backup.

The easiest way is still to find that letter or ask your provider
for a new password. Some providers offer online accounts to view
bills, configure e-mail accounts, etc.
There might be a small chance that you can set a new access password
there.
