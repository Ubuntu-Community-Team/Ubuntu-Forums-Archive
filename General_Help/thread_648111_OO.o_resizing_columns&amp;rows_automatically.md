### Post by drs305 on 2007-12-23
This is not an optimal solution, but you can highlight one or more columns/rows and then right click to select Optimal Width/Height. That will expand to include the widest existing entry plus a pad (default .1). This will not expand the width for future wider entries however.  You can also do this through the Format, Column/Row menu.
