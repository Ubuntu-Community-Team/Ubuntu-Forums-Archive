### Post by mcallenSchmee on 2008-09-13
You don't need post in bold and large font, it's kind of annoying.

It sounds like your files are deleted but still in the trash bin on the drive. Go to the drive and select 'View>Show Hidden Files' or press ctrl+H. There should be a folder named .Trash, maybe with some numbers after it. Your files are probably in here, probably in a folder named 'files', just move them from here to where you want them to be.
