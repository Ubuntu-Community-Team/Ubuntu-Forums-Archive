### Post by new_tolinux on 2010-11-28
First: The BIOS and the OS are different things.
That said, resetting the BIOS does not change anything in the OS itself, so you should not have to redo the whole OS.

There are pleny threads/topics/websites on the internet about resetting BIOS-es, so just try any search-engine with the type of your computer followed by the text "bios reset" (without "" quotes).

Besides that I don't know if I do violate any rule here if I give you the tricks, so I'll leave it with my hint above for yet.
