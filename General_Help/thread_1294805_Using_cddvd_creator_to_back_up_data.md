### Post by Lars Noodén on 2009-10-19
I hope you are using CD RW and not CD R, at least for practice.  

The burner, like [K3B](http://www.k3b.org/), will give you a choice of writing the whole disk at once or just the session.  If you choose the latter, the overall storage will be somewhat lessened because of waste between each session as the laser warms up.

Either way, when you are finished writing, the disk (or session) is full.
