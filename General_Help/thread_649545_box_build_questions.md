### Post by Rocket2DMn on 2007-12-25
The hard drive can only be plugged into one system at a time, but if you put a box next to your tv/dvd player you can plug the drive in there, encode and load the new movie, unmount the disk and plug it back into the dvd player.
For remote access to that box (say it has no monitor plugged in, though you could even use the TV as a monitor!) you can use VNC for remote desktop from another computer with any OS.
