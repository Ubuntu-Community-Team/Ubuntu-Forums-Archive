### Post by DaymItzJack on 2008-07-13
It's probably smarter to assume he's using Emerald by the looks of it.

System->Preferences->Emerald Theme Manager

Go to the "Edit themes" tab. Then the "Title bar" tab. Then in the top-rightish, there is an option for font.
