### Post by gdesilva on 2015-08-02
Under Settings there should be an app called Additional Drivers. When you click on Additonal Drivers it will allow you to install nVidia proprietary driver from the repo and hopefully this will fix your problem. I use xfce and not Unity so cannot tell you the exact key strokes to get this app...

### Post by sgian on 2015-08-02
Click on settings icon in the toolbar, then go to the software and updates icon.  Select the additional drivers tab, and wait a while until it gives you options.  Select the proprietary driver (there will be an updated version and a version that is not updated), wait for it to download and install, then reboot.
