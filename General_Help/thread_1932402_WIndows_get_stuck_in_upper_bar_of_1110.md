### Post by bogan on 2012-03-03
Hi!, j[B]ayhawker,

I recognize the symptoms you describe, but as they do not occur when logged into Gnome Classic, which I normally use, it does not concern me unduly.

In 11.10, Unity, in most cases a Window cannot be extended over the Topbar, but if it is then the size buttons are revealed when the Mouse cursor is moved over the bar, ( but not to the top extreme position,) and are accessible.

Most times when that is not the case, the Window is in 'FullScreen' mode and either a button shows: "Leave FullScreen", when the cursor is over the Topbar; which returns the Window to its previous setting; or it is necessary to press 'F11'.

Have you tried that keystroke when you are unable to grab &drag or resize a Window.

**Edit**: Also in some cases there is a Right Click, or Alt+Right Click, drop down menu, with  options to resize or: "Move TitleBar Onscreen".

Chao!, **bogan.**
