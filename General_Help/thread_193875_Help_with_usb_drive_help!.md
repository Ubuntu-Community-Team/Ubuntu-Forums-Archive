### Post by zugu on 2006-06-10
All USB drives have a physical read-only switch (just as floppy disks have). That switch has 2 positions:

1) full-access
2) read-only

or similar. Check it (it must be on full access) then try writing on it again. Don't forget to unmount it before plugging it out (right click on it's GNOME icon and choose Unmount).

If there are no label for the positions, just try both of them.
