### Post by bryanl on 2010-01-07
> my question is how i can convert an odp file to odt file, transform open office impress in open office writer
select, copy, and paste

impress and writer have two different goals so you will need to do some tweaking. You might want to change page from landscape to portrait and to letter size, for instance. Margins may also change and you'll probably want a different font size and the placement of text and graphics may need to be changed for better document layout. ... and so on.
