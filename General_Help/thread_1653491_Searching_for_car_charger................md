### Post by efflandt on 2010-12-26
If your phone, or other devices can charge from USB, web search: **12 volt usb** charger, power adapter, car charger or power supply.  You can find them any place with mobile phones or mp3 players.

Otherwise any place with mobile phones should have 12 volt adapters that can specifically plug into your phone.

However, I have a number of devices, including cameras, dangerous gas detector, and my laptop that I only have AC adapters for.  So I got a 12 volt plug to AC 175 watt inverter (for less than $13 when Circuit City was going out of business).  So that can even power or charge my laptop
