### Post by wojox on 2010-04-21
Yes they will be overwritten. You will want to back up what you want to keep in your Home directory.

---

### Post by blueridgedog on 2010-04-21
Using the disk will result in a new install.  If your /home directory is not on a separate partition, then your files will be lost (as well as files elsewhere on the system).  Backup what you want before you install.
