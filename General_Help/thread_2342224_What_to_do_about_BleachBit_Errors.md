### Post by monkeybrain20122 on 2016-11-04
Well first of all bleachbit has two modes: as root (when you have to enter your password) and as normal user. The former cleans stuffs in the system (such as APT), the later clean things in your $HOME (such as browser data, libreoffice, etc) You seem to be doing both in that screenshot. Secondly "memory" (system) is experimental. Uncheck it.

See screenshots. first one as root, second for normal user.
