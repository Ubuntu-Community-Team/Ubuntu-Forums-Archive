### Post by ron999 on 2010-10-08
Hi
In Firefox-4.0b8 they've removed the status bar from the bottom.

But some people may need a bar to show their add-on icons.
So they've introduced an 'Add-on Bar'.
That's what the grey bar is.

To get rid of it, go into View > Toolbars and un-tick the Add-on Bar.
