### Post by oldfred on 2010-07-28
If you only have one system installed, grub automatically by passes the menu as you do not normally want a choice. For those times you want to boot another kernel, recover,  or mem test you can use the shift key (it was escape in grub legacy). I think you have to hold the key down from BIOS until it appears.
