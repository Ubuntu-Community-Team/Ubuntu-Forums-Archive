### Post by Vaphell on 2012-01-11
most sub files are windows-centric, that is they use windows specific encodings for subtitles.
In settings of your media player look for subtitle encoding and try windows-1251/cp-1251 or whatever it's called (it covers eastern european cyrillic based languages, according to this list [http://scratchpad.wikia.com/wiki/Character_Encoding_Recommendation_for_Languages](http://scratchpad.wikia.com/wiki/Character_Encoding_Recommendation_for_Languages) )
