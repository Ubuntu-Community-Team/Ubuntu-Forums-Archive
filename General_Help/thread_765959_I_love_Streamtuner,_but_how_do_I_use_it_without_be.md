### Post by natrixgli on 2008-04-24
> **utUtu said:**
> You just need to edit the Streamtuner settings.  Change the xmms to something that you use, say audacious or rhythmbox for playing streams.


EDITED: don't forget the '%q' after.... so instead of 'xmms %q' put 'rhythmbox %q'

And all will be well. When you select a stream, then go to Rhythmbox, and select 'radio' from the left column. You will see the address of the radio station in the playlist, which you can then double click on to play. 

(and if it's not awful you could edit the properties to give it a proper name.)
