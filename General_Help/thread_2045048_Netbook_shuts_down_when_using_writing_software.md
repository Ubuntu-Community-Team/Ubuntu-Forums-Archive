### Post by DMaretta on 2012-08-20
Please, inform the model of your netbook, the Ubuntu version in use and wether the machine crashes at once.

If the machine crashes while you are typing, it may be a problem of poor contact in some internal socket (power supply, HD, memory, etc.)
