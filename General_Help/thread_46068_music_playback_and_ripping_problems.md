### Post by doclivingston on 2005-07-03
Are you getting any errors when you try to play the music (if so, what do they say), or are you getting no sound when you try to play music (can you get sound in other programs?).


I've never uses Musicmatch Jukebox, so I'm not sure what the equivalent would be; but there are quite a few music players to choose from:

Rhythmbox - installed by default, similar to iTunes (Gnome)
XMMS - Clone of WinAmp 2
amaroK - Lots of people swaer by this one (KDE)
Muine - fairly different to most other players (Gnome, requires Mono)
Beep Media Player (BMP) - GTK2 version of XMMS (Gnome)
Plus a heap more...


Which one is best depends on what exactly you are looking for in a music player; some maintain a "library" and some don't, some (especially Rhythmbox) pretty much require properly tagged music files, some have a variety of different features.

If you look around the fora you'll find quite a few "discussions" abut which one is best,  I'd suggest trying a few of them to see which ones you like. EasyTag is a fairly good music-tagging program, if you need to add/fix the ID3/ogg/whatever tags in your music files.
