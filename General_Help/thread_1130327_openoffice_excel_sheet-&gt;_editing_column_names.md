### Post by StuartN on 2009-04-19
If you have your column names typed into the cells in row 1 (and possibly product codes in column A) then place the cursor below the column names and to the right of the row identifiers - i.e. in cell B2. Then select Window->Split. The row(s) above and to the left of the split are now "sticky" and remain in view as you scroll around the spreadsheet. Select Window->Split again if you need to undo the split.
