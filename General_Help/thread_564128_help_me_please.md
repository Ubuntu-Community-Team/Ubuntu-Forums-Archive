### Post by HermanAB on 2007-09-30
No, most likely your battery is shot, although it is also possible that your power socket has broken off the motherboard and so the battery isn't charging.  Both of these are common laptop problems.

Every major city has one or two places that repair batteries - they'll cut the battery case open and replace the cells (you need a special spot welder for that).  Otherwise, get a new one from the manufacturer.
