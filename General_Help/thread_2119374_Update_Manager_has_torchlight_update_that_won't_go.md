### Post by r.stiltskin on 2013-02-23
Apparently transmission-common and transmission-gtk are included in a standard xubuntu-desktop installation even though transmission itself is not.  I can't tell you why, though.  Maybe because they wanted xubuntu to fit on a cd and there wasn't enough space to include transmission.

If you don't want them you can ininstall those two packages & then you won't be prompted to update them.

Not sure about the torchlight question, but I noticed [this thread]("http://forums.runicgames.com/viewtopic.php?f=5&t=40296") on Runic Games forum where some people said they had to uninstall the old version before the update could be installed.
