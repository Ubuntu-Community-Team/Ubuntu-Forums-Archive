### Post by John Jason Jordan on 2005-12-19
How do I get rid of this "feature"?

I was working away in an OO.o Writer document. My computer suddenly stopped responding to the keyboard. Nothing would work. Then I noticed a "program" slowly blinking on the panel. It said "Slow Keys." I clicked on it and it opened up a little window informing me that I had held the shift key down for eight seconds, which is the signal to turn on "slow keys," and did I really want to turn on "slow keys."

I can be pretty retarded all by myself without "slow keys" to help me. How can I forever nuke this from my computer?

---

### Post by psoleko on 2005-12-19
Go to System > Preferences > Keyboard, then choose the Accessibility button and uncheck enable keyboard accessibility features.
