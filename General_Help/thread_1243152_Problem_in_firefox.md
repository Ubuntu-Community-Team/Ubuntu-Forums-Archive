### Post by NathanLT on 2009-08-18
If you've tried installing and using other web browsers and you're still getting the same problems, it could be that your internet connection itself is no longer fast enough to run the programs you want to run.

Have you noticed any other problems with your internet connection, e.g. slow loading of pages in general, slow downloading of emails? Is there anyone else who shares your internet connection, and if so have they also been experiencing problems? If so, it is likely that the problem is with the internet connection and not with Firefox or Ubuntu.
