### Post by T700 on 2006-07-13
I found the answer!  I'm posting it here in the hopes that Google will pick it up for others that may hit this snag in their journey to Linux joy.  :-)

Somehow--not sure if I did it by accident or if it is a bug--a second calendar was created.  This is a common situation in an office.  Maybe you're the assistant for 5 people and you need to have access to all their calendars.  In my case, I have no use for it and didn't even think to check.  I was just looking through *all* the setting for *anything* that looked like it might be the key, and there it was.  I removed the second calendar and all is well.
