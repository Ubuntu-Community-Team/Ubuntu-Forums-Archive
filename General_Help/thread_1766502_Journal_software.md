### Post by Megaptera on 2011-05-24
Have you looked at Lifograph?

[http://www.etdsonline.com/technical/linux-administration/349-alternative-to-almanah-personal-diary-manager-for-ubuntu.html](http://www.etdsonline.com/technical/linux-administration/349-alternative-to-almanah-personal-diary-manager-for-ubuntu.html)
Features as at Wednesday, 03 November 2010
1. supports encrypted and unencrypted diaries
2. automatically logs out when not used for some time (to protect your diary when you forget to log out doing some other thing) (optional)
3. automatically formats entry titles and subheadings (we owe thanks to tomboy for the idea)
4. basic searching/filtering
5. editor themes
6. favorite entries
7. entry tagging
8. spell checking
9. wiki-like rich text formatting (*bold*, _italic_, =strikeout=, etc&#8230;)
10. links between entries
11. links in URI form ([http://,](http://,) file://, mailto://, etc&#8230;)
12. automatic backups
13. replace text in current entry or whole diary
