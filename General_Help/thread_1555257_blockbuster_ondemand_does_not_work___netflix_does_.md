### Post by earthpigg on 2010-08-17
hi,

i don't know about blockbuster.

but, we can talk about netflix: the various movie studios will not give permission to netflix to allow streaming to linux computers. it would take about five minutes of effort on the part of netflix to make streaming work perfectly on linux, but netflix would quickly find various large movie production companies filing law suits against them alleging millions of dollars in damages for contract violation and whatnot.

your beef is with Warnar Brothers, Walt Disney, etc.

there are many discussions on this, if you want to google around. key google terms would be "mono", "DRM", "silverlight", "linux" and combinations thereof.



All of that being said: if the legal streaming of movies from netflix, etc, is a priority for you, Ubuntu may not be a good fit at present as your primary computer operating system. Hopefully, this will change in time.
