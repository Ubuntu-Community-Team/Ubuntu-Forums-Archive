### Post by 3rdalbum on 2008-03-03
Booting places a lot of stress on your whole system - everything from boot media to motherboard to CPU and even the power supply. When people try overclocking, they know when they've gone too far when the computer crashes or turns off during boot.

It's probably a heat issue. Try putting your laptop onto a cooling pad.
