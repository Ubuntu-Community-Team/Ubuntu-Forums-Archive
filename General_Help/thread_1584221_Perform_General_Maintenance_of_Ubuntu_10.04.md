### Post by Mark Phelps on 2010-09-29
> **Robert R said:**
> ...I would like to know what are the **points of [B]maintenance**[/B] one would look at in Ubuntu and how to perform these tasks.

Unlike with MS Windows, in Ubuntu, you won't be faced with having to defrag your drive regularly, nor will you be troubled with driver updates.

However, like Windows Update, you will be offered updates from time to time -- but these are software updates, not antivirus patches, nor will you (in most cases) have to reboot your machine after installing the updates.

So, for the most part, Ubuntu is relatively maintenance-free.
