### Post by jespdj on 2008-04-29
I want to play videos from [MIT OpenCourseWare]("http://ocw.mit.edu/OcwWeb/web/about/about/index.htm"). Unfortunately, it looks like they have most of their videos in the stupid *.rm (RealMedia) format.

How can I play RealMedia streams in Ubuntu - do I really need the proprietary RealPlayer, or is there a free software program that can play these?

---

### Post by macaholic on 2008-04-29
Try the gecko-mediaplayer (sudo apt-get install gecko-mediaplayer). It uses mplayer to play various video and audio streams.

### Post by jespdj on 2008-04-29
