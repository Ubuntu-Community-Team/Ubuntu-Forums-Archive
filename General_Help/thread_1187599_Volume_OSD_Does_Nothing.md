### Post by FlintyVamp on 2009-06-14
Hey,

I have a Microsoft Wireless Laser Keyboard 6000 V2. It works fine and all but when I turn the volume down on the multimedia keys the little display comes up to show the volume but it doesn't actually affect how loud the volume is.. I can press mute and the speaker will have the little red X next to it but the volume doesn't change.

I know the keys are working as pause works with Rythembox, and as I said the graphic updates to show the volume lowering/muting but the volume never changes.

Any ideas?

Cheers,

### Post by kostkon on 2009-06-14
Go to your sound preferences (i.e. *System &#8594; Preferences &#8594; Sound*) and select the *Devices* tab.

In the *Default Mixer Tracks* select the sound device from the *Device* drop-down menu and below the volume you want your keyboard volume keys to control.

I assume that now your keys control a volume that is not the master volume of your sound device. The best would be to make them control the *Master* volume.

Hope that helps.
