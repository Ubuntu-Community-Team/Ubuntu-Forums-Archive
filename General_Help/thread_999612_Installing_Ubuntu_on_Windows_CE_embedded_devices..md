### Post by jespdj on 2008-12-02
Do you have a specific device in mind (brand / model)? Keep in mind that not all Windows CE devices use an Intel x86-compatible processor; [ARM processors](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/ARM_architecture) are also quite common in such devices, and you can't run standard Ubuntu on an ARM processor.
