### Post by bettlebrox on 2006-11-05
Go to System -> Preferences -> "Removable Drives & Media" (see attached image).

Then Click on the Multmedia Tab and look at the section titled "Portable Music Players". U should be able change it to use Gnomad2 (if Gnomad2 is installed).
