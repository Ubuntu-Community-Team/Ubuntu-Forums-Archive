### Post by SirSigma on 2009-01-07
I've had this problem before myself. I went into Synaptic and reinstalled Aspell. Then I turned off spell checking on Pidgin, then enabled it again and it was fine.

Also, make sure you're installing Aspell for English. I once did this and had no luck only to find out I was installing the Turkish version.
