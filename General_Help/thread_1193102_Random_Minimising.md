### Post by Rhubarb on 2009-06-21
This used to cause problems for me too ... I'm not sure if it still gives me problems as I don't play games that much anymore now.

Anyway, after a quick google it seems that your screensaver is interrupting your nexuiz gaming.  I suggest you turn off your screensaver before playing nexuiz.
[http://dev.alientrap.org/issues/show/235](http://dev.alientrap.org/issues/show/235)
**System --> Preferences --> Screensaver**

It's also quite probable that you need to disable compiz to fix up the problem.
[https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/compiz/+bug/216322](https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/compiz/+bug/216322)
**System --> Preferences --> Appearance**, select **Visual Effects** tab, select **None**

Hope this is of help.
-Rhubarb
