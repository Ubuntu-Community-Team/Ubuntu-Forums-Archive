### Post by EarlySight on 2010-04-08
I'm also using Evolution. It has everything I need and even more. Plus, it's already built inside Ubuntu Karmic Koala, so that's awesome too.

The thing I like the most about Evolution however, is that when you backup your account, not only does it store all your emails, contacts, and calendar entries, but it also remembers all your personal settings, such as how you set up your windows' dimensions, your whole layout, and all sorts of preferences like that, even after a complete format of the hard drive. Great stuff. Outlook never did that for me in the past. Nor did Thunderbird.
