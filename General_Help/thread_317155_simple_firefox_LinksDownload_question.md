### Post by JHawk24821 on 2006-12-12
Although not quite the answer you where looking for, try installing Devon Jensen's [Download Statusbar]("https://addons.mozilla.org/firefox/26/") plugin for Firefox.

I have mine set to automatically download everything right to the desktop without any annoying prompts.  This location is, of course, configurable as are many other cool options.  For example, the percentage complete bar is capable of dynamic coloring, letting you know at a glance which downloads are going slow so you can find mirrors if needed.  Very handy.
