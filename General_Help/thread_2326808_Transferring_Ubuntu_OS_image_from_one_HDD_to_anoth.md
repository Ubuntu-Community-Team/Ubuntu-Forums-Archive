Yes, you can do this but it is not a completely seamless operation.

Have a look at Clonezilla and Systemback.

[http://clonezilla.org/](http://clonezilla.org/)
[https://launchpad.net/systemback](https://launchpad.net/systemback)

The best piece of advice is, before you attempt to clone your OS:-

**Backup** your personal data and make sure that you can fully restore your data.
