### Post by westom1 on 2011-03-19
> **CyberPrime said:**
> I swapped out the graphics card, that's not the problem. I tried a new card and it's simply sitting at a blinking cursor after it flashed some random patterns and stuff a few times.  Swapping says little that is useful.  Just keep replacing good parts until something works.  That is what shotgunning is about.

Good diagnostic procedure means learning what is wrong before replacing anything.  Comprehensive hardware diagnostics.  Only then can anyone answer that question.

BIOS always starts in default text mode.  Other video modes are used by the OS.  Why are other modes not working?  Could be a long list of reasons including defective power supply voltages or even a broken pin on the PC board connector.  List of reasons for that symptom  are too long.
