### Post by heir4c on 2013-11-22
When you open it from inside Dash (so NOT from the Launcher) then remove the icon in the launcher and open it from the Dash. The icon will popup again.
Right-click on it and choose to add it to the Launcher. Close the Software updater and try now the icon in the Launcher. Will it work now?
