### Post by 3rdalbum on 2009-12-09
Meebo.com can use the Yahoo Messenger network. It doesn't have native Yahoo voice-and-video support, but it has a kind of workaround to allow you to do a video chat to someone on the Yahoo network.

Basically, when you open the webcam connection on Meebo, your girlfriend will be sent a link to a service called Tokbox. When she clicks on it, you'll both be able to see and hear eachother.
