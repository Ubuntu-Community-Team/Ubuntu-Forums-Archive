### Post by chewearn on 2010-01-31
It's not going to work without a hardware video converter box.  The VGA output is [RGB]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/RGB").  The component video expects [YPbPr]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/YPbPr/").  They are not directly interchangeable.
