I feel your frustration with it. I was running Debian Linux back when it first started to get popular back in 1998-1999. I was running apt-get update on a 56k modem. 

I was frustrated just like you with the small things. These problems being unresolved is why Linux hasn't made much of an impact on the desktop operating system market. I am assuming your computer did not come with Ubuntu pre-installed. When you buy a computer with Windows pre-installed you should realize it is the computer manufacturer or reseller that installed Windows on to it, gathered all the driver software needed for the hardware that was included (and only those) and burned that to a CD / DVD that he shipped with the PC.
