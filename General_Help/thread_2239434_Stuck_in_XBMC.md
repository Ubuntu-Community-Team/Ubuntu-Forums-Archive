### Post by papibe on 2014-08-13
Hi chris274. Welcome to the forum ):P

My guess is that  the xbmc-live session got set as the default somehow. 

Before login in, click on the wheel available next to where you are entering the password, and select 'ubuntu' instead of xbmc. In this [post]("http://askubuntu.com/questions/391563/how-to-uninstall-the-default-desktop-environment-of-ubuntu12-04") there's a picture of how to select the session you are login in to. 

Alternatively, XBMC may be set in the 'Startup applications'. You can get access to the desktop behind XBMC by changing it to window mode instead of full screen. While in XBMC, press \ to window the application. Once you gain access to the desktop open 'Startup applications' and remove XBMC from the tasks.

Hope it helps. Let us know how that goes.
Regards.
