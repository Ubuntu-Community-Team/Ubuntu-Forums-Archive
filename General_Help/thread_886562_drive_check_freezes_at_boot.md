### Post by dmizer on 2008-08-11
How big is the partition?  How long have you waited?  Is there still drive activity?

It may seem like nothing is happening, but it's probably a good idea to give the check enough time to do its thing.  Sometimes, especially if there is an error that needs to be fixed, that time can be quite lengthy. Whats more, you may have merely aggravated the situation by forcefully terminating the scan before it has completed.
