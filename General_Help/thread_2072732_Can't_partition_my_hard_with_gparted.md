### Post by dniMretsaM on 2012-10-18
You can't edit the partitions of a drive while it's mounted. You'll have to boot a LiveCD or LiveDVD and use GParted from there. GParted has it's own live image, so you'll probably want that. You can find it [here](http://gparted.sourceforge.net/livecd.php).
