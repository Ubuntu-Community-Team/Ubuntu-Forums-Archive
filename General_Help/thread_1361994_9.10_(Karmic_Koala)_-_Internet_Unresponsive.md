### Post by SpaceBison on 2009-12-24
This is a known bug, something to do with the way Ubuntu handles IPv6 and dns.

You can "fix it" in firefox by going to about:config, search for network.dns.disableIPv6 and set it to true.

[https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/glibc/+bug/417757]("https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/glibc/+bug/417757")
