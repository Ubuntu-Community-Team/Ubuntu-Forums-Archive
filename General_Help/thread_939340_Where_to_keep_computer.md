### Post by LoneWolfJack on 2008-10-05
there are noise dampening mats, low noise fans, fanless cooling solutions (aka heatpipes), watercooling... 

first, go check which fan is producing the most noise then check out puter stores for a replacement (be careful that the fan you buy is not just as quiet as possible but also powerful enough or your PC may die of excess heat).

if this still isn't enough, spend a couple bucks on noise dampening mats.

### Post by taladan on 2008-10-05
Servers are /loud/.  

That said, you may look at placing the server in a colocation site that specializes in providing safe, dry, secure environments with power backups and such like that if you're looking at seriously using this server for something web-based.  Otherwise you can try dampening vibration points and trying to find quieter fans, but likely choice is going to be either colocation or build a dedicated server closet with good humidity control & airconditioning (servers are also /hot/).

Good luck!

Tal
