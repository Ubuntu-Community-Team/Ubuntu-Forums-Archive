### Post by palmgrower on 2016-10-16
My 16.10 machine is connected to a TV. It loses audio after suspension. Solution is to log out and log back in. And I see both SPDF and HDMI in audio setting. I select HDMI and audio works. When it loses audio, only SPDF in audio setting.

How can I keep HDMI all the time?

PS: I just switched from 15.04 to 16.10. This audio problem was not with 15.04.

### Post by palmgrower on 2016-10-20
Searched internet. Not an unusual problem apparently. No permanent solution. Interim solution: Settings>Display>Click on a different resolution>Keep previous resolution. HDMI sound is restored.
