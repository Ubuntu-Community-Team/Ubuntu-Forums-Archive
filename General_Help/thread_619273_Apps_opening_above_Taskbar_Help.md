### Post by Bolorino on 2007-11-21
Alternative to the right click->move. Press Alt and then click on any place in the window to move while holding the Alt key. Not a solution, but less frustrating and faster.

I think you can tweak some options in Beryl settings for the windows placement, but don't know where, maybe compizconfig-settings-manager.

Good luck.

---

### Post by stinkyplant on 2007-11-21
Great man! The "ALT" move worked so much better. 
But then I went into Beryl Settings like you said and "Place Windows" wasn't checked.

I guess I unchecked it screwing around. Anyway, that fixed it right up! A stupid problem with an easy fix!
