### Post by aum11 on 2009-02-04
Just letting You know of a recent problem that I have had with Firefox. The  customizations I had in place would disappear after every restart of Firefox 3.0.5.  Also, all of the possible icons created by addon extensions were no longer available to choose.

Having played around with many options, eventually I disabled the Ubuntu firefox modifications.  This fixed the problem.

Hope this helps.
