### Post by KaeseEs on 2006-11-03
From within your desktop, press Ctl+Alt+F2.  This will pop you into virtual terminal 2.  Pressing Alt+[F1-F6] will switch you to the corresponding console.  Pressing Alt+F7 will bring you back to X.

Alternatively, boot into recovery mode.
