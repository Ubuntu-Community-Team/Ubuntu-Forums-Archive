### Post by mwhorn12 on 2007-08-23
I use ubuntu feisty fawn, and i also use a wireless keyboard. The problem is that my wrist sometimes presses the prt scr button on my keyboard, which I dont notice until all of the sudden about 400 screenshot dialogs open and freeze my system completely (no mouse, keyboard or anything).
My question is how would i disable the prt scr button?

---

### Post by fragos on 2007-08-24
Application-> SystemTools->  ConfigurationEditer-> apps-> metracity-> global_keybinding-> run_command_screenshot-> right-click->  Edit Key.  Change value to disabled.

### Post by mwhorn12 on 2007-08-24
