### Post by Fixman on 2009-03-20
The best way to reset a BIOS (note that, even if it doesn't look so, this is **100% safe**) is opening your computer and removing the internal clock (a little circular clock battery on the motherboard), waiting 30 seconds, and putting it back in. After that, your BIOS would be password-less!
