Don't know if anyone can help with this one?

Firefox rather nicely comes up with an option to allow you to subscribe to rss feeds in google reader among other options. However, it appears that if the feed is a podcast then you get the usual message

but no option to add to google reader. Google reader does support podcasts (although I don't actually listen to them there, I just use google reader as an online list of my podcasts as a backup)

### Post by surfdoc on 2009-08-04
The solution came to me eventually.

Select to add an RSS feed to google from firefox and it loads the url:

[http://www.google.com/ig/add?feedurl=](http://www.google.com/ig/add?feedurl=)[feed url here]

So to add a podcast to google, create a script that adds the feed url to the above url and asks firefox to load the combined url. Then select to open the podcast with said script when selecting the podcast in firefox.

Haven't tried it yet, but can't see why it wouldn't work
