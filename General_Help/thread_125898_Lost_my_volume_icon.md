### Post by Neo Ex on 2006-02-05
1) Open KMix. It will add the volume icon that you are missing. To make KMix opening at startup, put a copy of the /usr/share/applications/kde/kmix.desktop in ~/.kde/Autostart.
2) KDE Control Center -> KDE Components -> File associations.
