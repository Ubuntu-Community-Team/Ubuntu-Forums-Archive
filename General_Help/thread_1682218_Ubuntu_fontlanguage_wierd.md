### Post by Hedgehog1 on 2011-02-05
*** THIS POST IS INCORRECT - PLEASE SKIP ON DOWN TO THE NEXT ONE ***

The problem is not your language, but your font.  You have a font selected that has only graphical characters.

Please menu to: System >> Preferences >> Appearance.

Select the third tab (Fonts) and look at the Document font.  

Change it back to Sans.
