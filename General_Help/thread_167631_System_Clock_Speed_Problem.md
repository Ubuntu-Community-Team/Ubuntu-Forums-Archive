### Post by Ptero-4 on 2006-04-28
Do you have windoze? If you have it you should go to ubuntu and uncheck the option that says "use GMT time" or something similar and your clock should come back into normal speed. Or check your BIOS/CMOS battery. If it is running out of charge, that may be the cause of your computer's clock erratic behavior.
