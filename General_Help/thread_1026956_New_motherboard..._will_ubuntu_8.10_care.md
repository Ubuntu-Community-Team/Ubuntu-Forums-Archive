### Post by donkyhotay on 2008-12-31
Ubuntu won't accuse you of piracy like windows will when you do that (c: but, sometimes you run into driver issues swapping things around. Generally a simple update/install will take care of that. The real question of course is 'is the new hardware linux compatible'. As melojo mentioned you want something linux friendly. You're more likely to run into problems with the new hardware not working with linux regardless of what you do then you are of having problems due to the fact your hardware changed.
