### Post by darkcrimson on 2013-03-10
To anyone who's looking for somewhat of a workaround outside of unplugging/plugging back in:

[https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+bug/971321](https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+bug/971321)

A developer put together a neat little program (resetmsmice) that allows you to reset the Microsoft wireless mouse. It's suggested to work for the 3500, 3000, 1000, 4500, and 5000 models. There are 2 versions: 32-bit and 64-bit. So far it's worked just fine for me, which would have been great to discover before spending the cash on new hardware. Anyway, I hope this helps anyone looking. Even though it's an old post...
