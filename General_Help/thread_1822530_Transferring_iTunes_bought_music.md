### Post by Mark Phelps on 2011-08-11
If you installed Wubi in order to (1) use iTunes, and (2) play Windows games -- those were among the two WORST reasons to install Ubuntu.

Why?

Because: 
(1) iTunes doesn't work well with Wine.  Look through the forums and you'll see LOTS of posts about this.
(2) Most Windows games don't run well in Wine.  

You should go to the WineHQ website and use their application compatibility database tool to search for the Apps and Games you want to use.  If the ratings are any lower than Silver (or non-existent). you're wasting your time with Wine.
