### Post by Agent ME on 2009-04-16
You could take screenshots of each desktop and put them together into one image.

A quick way to get a screenshot of thumbnails of all your desktops is to go into Expo mode (super+E) and press print screen there. But the resolution of each of the desktops is just a thumbnail compared to full.


It does seem like a good suggestion for a feature though, guess you'd want to request it from the Compiz people.
