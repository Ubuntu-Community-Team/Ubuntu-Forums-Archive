### Post by PmDematagoda on 2007-11-18
The fact that you have 2 hard drives makes the partitioning, installing and dual-booting of Ubuntu and XP very much easier. Simply boot the PC using the Ubuntu Live CD, once you enter the desktop of the system double-click the Install icon to begin the installation. 

Follow the instructions as given in the setup process and once you get to the partitioning part, simply  choose Guided and allow it to use the entire HDD, just ensure that the HDD selected is the empty 40Gb one instead of the 160Gb one having XP. Then that is it:), the installer will do everything else for you, including the setup of the dual-boot between Ubuntu and XP.

### Post by Daveski on 2007-11-18
