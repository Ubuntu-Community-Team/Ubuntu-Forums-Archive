### Post by buzzingrobot on 2013-09-15
Basicaly, you need to find the URL of a station's streaming audio.  This is not the same as the URL of its web site. It is often the URL of the "Listen Live" link. Increasingly, though, many stations obscure or hide the URL within a Flash popup or similar device.

Once you have the URL, click  on "Radio" in Rhythmbox's Library panel (should be on the left).  Then, click on "Add" and a popup will appear to accept the URL.
