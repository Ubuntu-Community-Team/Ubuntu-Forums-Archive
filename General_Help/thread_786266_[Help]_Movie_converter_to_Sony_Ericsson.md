### Post by plucky on 2008-05-08
Its been a while now since I last used it but my Sony Ericsson needs 3gp video files.I have used **ffmpeg** as the converter with **Winff** as the front end.
You need to enable **Medibuntu** repository to get **ffmpeg** and you can find **Winff** [here](http://www.winff.org/)

To enable Medibuntu repository see this Howto [here](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Medibuntu)

Good Luck
