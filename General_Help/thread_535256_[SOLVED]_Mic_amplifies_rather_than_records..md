### Post by tgalati4 on 2007-08-26
Check your mixer settings.  Go into Preferences and activate all of the tabs so you can see all of the settings available.  You may have "monitor input" turned on.  On some sound cards this is set through software--for instance Audacity may behave differently than Sound Recorder.
