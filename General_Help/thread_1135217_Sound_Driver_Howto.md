### Post by Bender2k14 on 2009-04-24
Writing drivers will happen upstream from Ubuntu.  I think you can do this through the [Advanced Linux Sound Architecture (ALSA)]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Advanced_Linux_Sound_Architecture") project. From the [main page of ALSA]("http://www.alsa-project.org/main/index.php/Main_Page"), I found a link titled "Writing a driver?" which points to [this page]("http://www.alsa-project.org/main/index.php/ALSA_Driver_Documentation"), which probably contains the information that you are looking for.

Does this information help?
