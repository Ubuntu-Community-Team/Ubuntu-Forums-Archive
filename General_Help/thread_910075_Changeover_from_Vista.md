### Post by luckyuser on 2008-09-04
If you haven't already, download the latest Ubuntu iso via
[http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/download]("http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/download")
(make sure you choose the right options and know whether your comp is 64bit or x86 (most likely if it isn't as new). 

after you download this you can extract/burn the iso to a cd, reboot with the cd in the drive, and try out Ubuntu. This is kind of a quick way to see if it works with your hardware...and if it doesn't you may have to manually install/compile drivers, after you've installed ubuntu of course.

p.s. if you run Ubuntu in live CD mode, it will naturally be much slower than normal!
