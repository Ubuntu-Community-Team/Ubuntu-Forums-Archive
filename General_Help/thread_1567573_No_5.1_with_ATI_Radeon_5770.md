### Post by perrti-y02 on 2010-10-29
As far as I am aware there is no simple way of getting a "real" 5.1 output over HDMI in Ubuntu. What you can do (and even this is quite flaky) is get AC3 passthrough. Programs such as Mplayer and VLC will do it but there is a lot of fiddling around to do. Have a look through all the options on VLC.

I have had more success with VLC than any other program.
