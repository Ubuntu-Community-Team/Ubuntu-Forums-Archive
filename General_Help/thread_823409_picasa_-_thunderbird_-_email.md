Thanks guys but I have found out how to sort it and I have fixed it. The problem was with Picasa and after much mucking around (I seemed to have 3 versions of Picasa installed at one stage) but eventually managed to uninstall the lot and install the Picasa Version 2.7.0 (Build 37.3615.0) for Linux and presto, everything is working well. The solution is explained at [http://picasa.google.com/linux/thanks-deb.html](http://picasa.google.com/linux/thanks-deb.html) and it works well with Thunderbird 2.0 on Ubuntu 6.10.
