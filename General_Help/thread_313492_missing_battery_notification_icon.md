### Post by lucads on 2006-12-06
Excuse me for the dumb question:

I've removed accidentally the battery status notification icon from the traybar by selecting "remove from panel" option. 
Now when ubuntu starts on my notebook the battery status icon is not displayed anymore, and when battery is slow the balloon tip appears from the opposite site of the screen without being adiacent to any icon

How can I re-enable the battery status icon on my traybar?
(i've tried setting "always display icon" in Power managment preferences but this setting takes no effect)


Thanks.

---

### Post by mcduck on 2006-12-06
I think you actually removed the Notification Area from your panel, as that's where the icon for Power Management is shown.

To get it back, right-click on your panel, select 'Add to Panel' and then drag 'Notification Area' to your panel.
