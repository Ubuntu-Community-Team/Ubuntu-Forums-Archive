### Post by angelot on 2008-04-09
Last week I installed 8.04 with firefox 3b4. Firefox opted gnash and adobe. I selected gnash - yes, an open source project!

This worked well with ads, but most flashapps did not.

I disabled gnash and installed flash9 from adobe.com (not synaptic).

Now everything works well, and firefox is up to speed. Actually I have newer had a faster flash-experience!

Don't use gnash or the adobe-plugin offered in synaptic/firefox. Install from adobe :-)
