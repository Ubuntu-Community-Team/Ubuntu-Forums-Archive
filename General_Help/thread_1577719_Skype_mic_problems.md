### Post by blueridgedog on 2010-09-19
Make certain the microphone is not muted. Go to System-->Preferences-->Sound-->Input and either move the slider or click the box to unmute.

Don't forget to turn off automatic volume adjusting in skype program: Options > Sound Devices > Allow Skype to automatically adjust my mixer levels

### Post by 3Miro on 2010-09-20
Go to the volume control and hit preferences. Than add all microphone channels on output. Unmute them and pump the volume to the max. Then find the microphone and blow some air on it. You should be able to hear that. If it doesn't work, then the mic doesn't work. If it works, you can use this to find out how Ubuntu recognizes your mic (front mic, or simply mic).

If the mic works, go to input and make preferences show you all the channels. Then select input source and play with the input volumes as well activate/stop recording. I can never remember the correct settings, I just play with the until something works.
