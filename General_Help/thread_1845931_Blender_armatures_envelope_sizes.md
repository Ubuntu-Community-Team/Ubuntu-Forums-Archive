hi,

after joining two armatures together i found the envelope sizes were all messed up. i found this was caused by the scale size of the two armatures were different before joining. i fixed this by using the Apply scale/rotation to ObData before joining the two armatures.

another way to fix this would be to open the transform properties and make sure that both objects are the same scale along the x,y and z axis.
