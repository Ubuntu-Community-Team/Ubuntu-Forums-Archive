### Post by Usufruct on 2009-01-03
I've been enjoying the simple, clean, functional look for about a week now.  Remanded most of my screenlets to the widget layer, threw on Mac4Lin, etc.  I will be going back to Dust soon :)

And hey, OP, I don't know if this is something you'd want to do or not, but you can make your panel autohide completely, and it's quite easy.

Hit Alt+F2 and launch gconf-editor.  Navigate to /apps/panel/toplevels/ and select the panel you want to adjust (in your case, probably top_panel_screen0.  In there you'll find a setting for "autohide_size", I think the default is 6px.  You can also change the hide and show delay, I think the 500ms default is kind of slow.

Cheers!
