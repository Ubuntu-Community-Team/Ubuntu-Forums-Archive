### Post by Krydahl on 2008-08-17
Because emerald is a windows decorator that only controls the window frames. If you want to change the window backgrounds and such, you need to look in System->preferences->appearance.

---

### Post by crazyness003 on 2008-08-17
Thats all an emerald theme is. It makes your window borders look more neat. You can also add transparancy with some engines or even use pixmaps (.png's with rgb & alpha)

As for everything else, gtk themes(or qt for kde, i think) and icon packs change all the rest.

Like dude above me said , check out your appearances.

You can also (in ubuntu gnome) right-click a clean part of your desktop (not an icon), and click "Change Desktop Background" and go to the "Themes" tab (1st one).

Have fun.
