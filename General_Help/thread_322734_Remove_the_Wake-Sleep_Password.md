### Post by Gen2ly on 2006-12-20
For those who aren't paranoid or work in a private office it seems kindof unneccessary to have to enter a password when the PC wakes from sleep every time.  Good news is this is real easy to fix.

Goto the **Applications Menu**, then **System Tools**, and select **Configuration Editor**.

From there select **apps** then **gnome-power-manager**.  Scroll down to 

lock_on_blank_screen
lock_on_hibernate
lock_on_suspend

and uncheck.

Voila baby!
