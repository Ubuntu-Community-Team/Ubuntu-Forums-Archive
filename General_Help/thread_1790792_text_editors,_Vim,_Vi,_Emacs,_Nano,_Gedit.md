### Post by Bachstelze on 2011-06-25
Learing either vi(m) or emacs will definitely make you more productive in the long run. Even the simple operation of deleting an entire line in your file takes just two keystrokes (dd) in vim, compared to keeping the delete key pressed for several seconds in nano if your line is long.
