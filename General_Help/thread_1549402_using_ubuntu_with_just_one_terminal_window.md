### Post by WorMzy on 2010-08-09
Add an ampersand (&) to the end of your commands that launch GUI apps (for example: "gedit &"). It'll launch the program, then put it in the background and return you to your shell prompt. You can continue to use both the app and the terminal.

You can also create tabs in gnome-terminal with Ctrl+Shift+T, and close tabs with Ctrl+Shift+W.
