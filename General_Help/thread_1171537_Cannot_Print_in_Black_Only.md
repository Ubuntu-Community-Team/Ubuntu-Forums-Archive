### Post by edu1962 on 2009-07-13
System>admin>printing. Select "your printer" and click "copy" than a pop-up should appear for the name off your "new printer". When this is done select your "new printer" and change your settings under printer-options.

Now you have your virtual 2 printers : your original and a second for your black prints.
