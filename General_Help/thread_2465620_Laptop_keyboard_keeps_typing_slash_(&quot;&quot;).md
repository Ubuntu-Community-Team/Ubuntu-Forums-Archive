### Post by Autodave on 2021-08-06
Most likely a failure of the keyboard itself, quite possibly a stuck key.  With the laptop off, rapidly and repeatedly strike every key.  Blow some compressed air under the keys.  Turn the laptop upside sown and repeat striking the keys and the compressed air.

Another trick that might work is to remove the battery and all connections to the laptop and leave it sit for 10 minutes before reinstalling the battery and power cable.  Are you using a USB hub?  Leave it disconnected and see if that cures the problem.
