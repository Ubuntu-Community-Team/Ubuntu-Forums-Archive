### Post by wanger123 on 2006-01-29
Hey all, I've just completed a transformation of my laptop using the guide from here: [http://baghira.sourceforge.net/OS_Clone-en.php](http://baghira.sourceforge.net/OS_Clone-en.php)

I think this is even better than the guide above because it is from the official baghira site, and it includes the use of kxdocker to give an authentic mac OSX feel to the desktop (parabolic scrolling effects in the dock).

One point, instead of downloading from cvs, just download kwin-baghira, kxdocker and kxdocker-data via apt-get, synaptic etc. Then follow on from Step 2 in the guide. You can also skip the kxdocker installation part of the guide.
