### Post by malangaman on 2010-03-12
it is easiest to get started by downloading Qt4 from the repositories using synaptic:
(System/Administration/synaptic-package manager).

Then download QDevelop in the same way.
It is uncomplicated to start with. More experienced programmers have different opinions but to me the simpler the better.

The SDK, Qt Creator, can be downloaded from the link in my previous post. But I do not recommend it for a beginner.

Here are some useful links:
