### Post by BslBryan on 2009-05-02
Are you using Compiz? 
If so, in the Advanced Desktop Effects Settings, under "General Options", there's an option called "Unredirect fullscreen windows."

Uncheck it.  Hope I helped. :)

[I don't think I've ever seen a response as stupid as that offered

The solution to your problem may well depend on which version 
of "flash" you have installed.

While the video is in regular mode (not "full screen"), right-click 
on the video itself. From the drop-down menu, click "Settings". 
If you get 5 icons on the very bottom row, you might be in luck.:D

The first 2 icons should both show you a monitor. Click the one 
which shows a symbol on the bottom of its screen (usually the 
first icon). You should thus get "Enable hardware acceleration". 
Whatever the box shows you, click the opposite, and then click 
on "Close".:(
