### Post by knutschr on 2007-11-26
Did you solve it?
Slow keys can be activated by accident if a key is tapped more than 8 times in a row, or when [Shift] is pressed more than 5 seconds.

Te revert to normal, go to System-->man in chairs at top-->Accessibility--> continue at the top-->Disable Slow keys.
