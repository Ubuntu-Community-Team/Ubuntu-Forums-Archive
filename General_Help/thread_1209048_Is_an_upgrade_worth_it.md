### Post by synapsys on 2009-07-09
If they have all the applications they will ever need, I would say they have no need to upgrade. 8.04 is supported until 04/11 so they still have plenty of time to worry about upgrading. ### Post by tripolitan on 2009-07-09
Absolutely NOT. 

Parents, professional/business users, older and mature people in general like stability, security, familiarity and ease of use. They already have that with Hardy until 2011.

You can upgrade to Firefox 3.5 by Christmas (when it becomes stable-for real) by simply downloading it from Mozilla and removing 3.0. That will give them (and you) an extra 2 to 5 years on top of 2011. Flash/Java/LimeWire/Skype are just as straight forward as well.

Why mess with a good thing?
