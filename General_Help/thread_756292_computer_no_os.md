### Post by retrow on 2008-04-15
If you are looking for a laptop, then you have very few choices. There are some machines that come with pre loaded Linux distros, but I can't think of any laptop manufacturer that ships a piece with clean drive.

However if you want to buy a desktop, then you have tons of options. Search for 'barebone kits' and you will have everything from a very basic machine to something as powerful as dual quad-core kits; and they come without any pre-installed OS.
