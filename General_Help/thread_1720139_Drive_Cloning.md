### Post by Hedgehog1 on 2011-04-02
If you use a tool like Clonezilla ([http://clonezilla.org/]("http://clonezilla.org/")), you can make a complete copy that will restore you whole drive, OS and all.

You only challange is: Will your data fit onto a DVD.

Right now CloneZilla does not support multi DVDs for a single image.
