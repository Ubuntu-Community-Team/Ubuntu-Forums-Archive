### Post by 23meg on 2005-11-18
There are two backends for totem, one is totem-gstreamer and the other is totem-xine. When you install totem-xine, totem just becomes the interface you use to access the functions of xine. 

If interested in xine I also suggest you try gxine, which is in the repositories.
