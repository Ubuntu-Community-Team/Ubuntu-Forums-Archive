### Post by Charlie91 on 2010-11-13
Check your *preferences*.  Go through *Edit > Preferences > Composer Preferences > Forward Style*.  I guess the default is *Attachment*: that's not what you want.  Choose either *Inline* (I prefer this) or *Quoted*.  Now you can edit/delete part of the message.  Maybe while you're at it, change the different preferences there.

### Post by Charlie91 on 2010-11-13
That's the domino effect--solving one problem leads to another problem.  When you have time, examine your preferences.  The images get detached because it's text email and images are not loaded.

Go to *Edit > Preferences > Composer Preferences > General > Default Behavior* and check *Format messages in HTML*.  If that is not enough, go to *Edit > Preferences > Mail Preferences > HTML Messages > Loading Images* and choose *Load images in messages from contacts* (or the next choice). Readers, correct me if I'm wrong... ;)
