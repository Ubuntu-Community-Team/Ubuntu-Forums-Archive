### Post by BslBryan on 2009-05-06
Hello, mhaight.  :-)

Yes, that is very much a possibility.  In fact, a lot of Mac users like showing off their laptop's power by triple-booting Windows, Mac OS X, and Ubuntu. :P

It depends, though, on their Mac.  Tell them that it is possible, and just to Google instructions online.  

Also, if it will annoy them, I think that GRUB bootloader will select Ubuntu as the default OS, so they might want to take precautions before the dual boot process so they won't have to deal with that.

Anyway, best to you, and your friends, mhaight. :-)  I hope I've helped.
