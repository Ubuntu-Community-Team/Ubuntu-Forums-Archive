### Post by m_duck on 2012-06-07
You may have changed a preference (or it may have been inexplicably changed...) for gnome-terminal so it hides the menus by default. I think, if you right click in a gnome-terminal window, you have the option to get into preferences in there - have a rummage around and see if you can find a menu based option. There may also be a keybind to show/hide the menu, but I can't think what it might be.
