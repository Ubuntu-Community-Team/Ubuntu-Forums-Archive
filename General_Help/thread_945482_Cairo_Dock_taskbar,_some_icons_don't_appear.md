### Post by BandD on 2008-10-12
If I understand what you're saying correctly, then I think you need to figure out which program launchers (on a main or sub dock) are "stealing appli from the taskbar".  Once you track down which ones are not behaving as you would like, right click on that programs laucher.  Then select "Modify This Laucher".  In the window that pops us, click the little triangle next to "Extra Parameters" to show the extra parameters.  The click the little box for the option to "Prevent this launcher from stealing appli from the taskbar".  That should do the trick...I think.
