### Post by HeroOfCanton on 2012-02-23
Since you couldn't find info on the subject, and in case anyone needing it stumbles onto this thread:

In Xubuntu 11.10 these changes can be made at Settings | Settings Manager | Workspaces.

Extra info in a thread is better than not enough. :)
