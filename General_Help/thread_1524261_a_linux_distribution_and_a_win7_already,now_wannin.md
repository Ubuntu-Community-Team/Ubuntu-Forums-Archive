### Post by cbowman57 on 2010-07-05
When you install Ubuntu at step 6 (maybe 7) it will give you an advanced option for your grub.  You can choose not to install it.  After installation completes you can then run the grub update from your current linux distro.  Depending on what it is though it might not discover your Ubuntu partition, but that's a whole different question.

To summarize, you can elect NOT to install grub during the Ubuntu install and use the grub from your current linux.
