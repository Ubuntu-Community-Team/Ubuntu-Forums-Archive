### Post by Anthon on 2009-03-26
USB < 2.0 full speed is 12 Mbit/s, USB 2.0 goes up to 480 Mbit/s, you can do the math (while waiting for Ubuntu to start).

You are better of running this from CD. As long as you have enough memory you can do quite a lot, including updating and upgrading, and installing additional packages.

### Post by mb_webguy on 2009-03-26
An 8x CD-ROM drive has a transfer rate of 9.8Mbps, so an 8x drive is very slightly slower than USB 1.0.  But most drives are faster than that, so you're better off running a LiveCD than a LiveUSB with a USB 1.0 connection.

A LiveUSB with a USB 2.0 connection, however, would be much faster than the fastest CD/DVD drive.
