MITB is a form of MITM and almost 8 years old now.

It is a proxy trojan and not a virus, use upto date malware signatures, if in Linux i wouldnt worry too much about it as they are directed at Windows platforms, trojans can attack Linux but not in this instance, and viruses in general are not prolific in the Linux world (they all come under the term malware though, but viruses and trojans are not the same)

For security overall though you should run NOScript and things like tight inbound and outbound firewall rules and possibly Apparmor.

Read here for a good grounding [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/BasicSecurity](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/BasicSecurity)
