### Post by trikster_x on 2010-10-21
I had this problem the other day.  I found that mine stemmed from the compiz problems ubuntu has been running into.  Try changing your session to failsafe at the login screen after you select your user account and select "none" in the visual effects tab of the appearances menu.  That will disable compiz or any other compositing manager.  Then you get to have the same fun everyone else is having figuring out how to get their compiz to work right.

I posted what fixed compiz for me here: [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1601310]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1601310")

Hopefully some of that helps you.
