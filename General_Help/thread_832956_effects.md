Just FYI, my video card is an ATI Radeon 200M which apparently doesn't play well with Linux.  Anyway, yes Compiz Fusion is what you want to get the cube and wobbly windows and such but you may also want to look into Emerald.  Emerald is a window decorator that is very cool.  It allows you to do a lot more with themes and such than the default window decorator, metacity.  You can look at this thread:
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=809695](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=809695)
if you want more info on how this all works and how to install.  Hope this helps...  Good luck!
