### Post by lisati on 2012-01-01
"Curriculum Vitae" is from the Latin language. A rough translation is "The story of your life" - in other words, it's another word for "resumé", and is a chance for you to tell the interviewer something about yourself.

### Post by Dangertux on 2012-01-01
It's also a fancy word for information most interviewers don't read. At least in my experience HR looks for very targeted resumes and your interviewer doesn't care because most people lie on resumes. That's just my experience. I wouldn't try to make it too flashy most HR departments will expect to see it on doc format anyway.
