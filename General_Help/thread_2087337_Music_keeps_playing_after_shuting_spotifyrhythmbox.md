### Post by JRV on 2012-11-23
Welcome aboard!

Click on the speaker icon. (Upper right)

Click on the Rythmbox icon on the drop down menu.

From here clicking on the X in the corner will minimize the window but not close it. 
(Yes, this is weird , and inconsistant behavior.)

Quit is located at the bottom of the Music menu.
