### Post by lukjad on 2009-02-21
Here's the simple guide to Ubuntu naming. If it's a happy name (**Dapper** Drake), it's of the newer variety. If it's kinda sad (**Hoary** Hedgehog/ **Warty** Warthog) it's of the older variety. Anything before "D" is also of the older variety (**Breezy** Badger). From "D" onward, just follow the alphabet (Dapper Drake->Edgy Eft->Feisty Fawn->Gutsy Gibbon->Hardy Heron->Intrepid Ibex->Jaunty Jackalope->Karmic Koala). And that's all there is to it! :D
