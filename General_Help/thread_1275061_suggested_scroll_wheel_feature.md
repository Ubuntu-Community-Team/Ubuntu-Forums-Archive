### Post by amingv on 2009-09-25
Under Firefox:
Edit>Preferences>Advanced

On the General Tab, under Browsing, enable Autoscrolling (and Smooth Scrolling).

Click with the middle button in any position on the page, a small circle with some arrows appears. Move the mouse to the direction you want to scroll and it will keep scrolling until you click with the mouse or it hits the end of the page.

Is that what you were looking for?
