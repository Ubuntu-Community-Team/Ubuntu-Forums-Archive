### Post by ibuclaw on 2008-05-18
On Hardy 8.04, compiz should come installed by default.

Go into "System>Preferences>Advanced Desktop Effects"

You need a 3D enabled graphics card, such as NVIDIA PCI/AGP, to get it working (ie: Onboard graphics is a no no).

And then just scroll up and down to see what you'd like to enable/configure.

ie:
Go into "Effects" and enable "Wobbly Windows"
Then try dragging your current viewed window around the screen to see it wobble as you do so.

ie2:
Under "Desktop", enable "Desktop Cube" and "Rotate Cube".
Then under "Effects" enable "3D Windows".

If any Messages Pop-up, just click the "Enable" button.

Now press "Ctrl+Alt" and click the "Left Mouse Button".
While holding down the "Left Mouse Button", move your mouse around.
You should see a 3D Desktop Cube rotating along with your mouse movements.
See the Screenshot attached.
