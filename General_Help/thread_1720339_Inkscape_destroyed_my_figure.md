### Post by Vaphell on 2011-04-03
if that was your only copy then i don't think you can get your shape back. I assume you didn't enable autobackup feature in inkscape preferences? If there is nothing that looks like a backup copy in your working directory, in your main directory or in ~/.config/inkscape then most likely you are S.O.L.

Treat it as a painful but valuable lesson - if you don't want to lose something, avoid single points of failure like a plague. Enable backup mechanisms in applications, save manually with a different name every now and then, in case of very very important data do offsite backups (dvds/external drive, online storage, ...). From time to time threads about lost master thesis appear because affected person didn't bother with having duplicates and lost months worth of effort when their hdd died or office suite crashed corrupting the only existing copy.
