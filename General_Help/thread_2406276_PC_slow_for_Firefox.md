### Post by Autodave on 2018-11-18
2.0 with 2 gig RAM is really at the max for running Ubuntu.  And, Firefox is really heavy. So, you are just overdoing it.  I would first of all consider moving to a newer release such as 16.04 or 18.04.  Secondly, I would very seriously consider going tro a lighter desktop such as Xubuntu or Lubuntu.  (Xubuntu will be a little fancier than Lubuntu.)  Both should work nicely on that machine.  Please do a clean install: do not try to upgrade: it just rarely works out and you will save yourself a lot of time an aggravationg by doing a clean install.  PLEASE make sure to backup all of your important files to an external source first!!
