### Post by kellemes on 2007-08-11
During the first seconds of boot you should push **and hold** the key used by your motherboard to enter BIOS.
Could be DEL, F8 or another one, or maybe you should google to find out the magick key your motherboard wants you to push.
But this is **before** Grub shows up.
