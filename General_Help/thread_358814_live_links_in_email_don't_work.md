### Post by finer recliner on 2007-02-11
are you closing your email client right after copying the text? (and then trying to paste into another application) -- if so, this won't work in ubuntu because of the way the clipboard works. each application creates its own buffer to store copied material; when you close a program, the buffer gets deleted. to get around this problem, install glipper. now items will copy to glipper instead of their default buffer.
