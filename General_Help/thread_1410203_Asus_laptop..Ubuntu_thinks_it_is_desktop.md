### Post by lukeiamyourfather on 2010-02-18
VirtualBox itself likely doesn't even know its running on a laptop let alone the guest. If you want to take advantage of power saving features in your laptop, then install Ubuntu on the laptop instead of through VirtualBox. Or rely on power saving features like CPU throttling and battery information of the host. Cheers!
