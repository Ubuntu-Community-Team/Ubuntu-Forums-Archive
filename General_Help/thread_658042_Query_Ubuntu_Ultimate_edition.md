### Post by PmDematagoda on 2008-01-04
> Pleae do tell what is this Ultimate edition of ubuntu. I want to know whether it a flavour like windows vista ultimate or something ? 

It can be considered to be that, it is basically Ubuntu except that it comes preloaded with more software, configurations and may also be more updated than the official Ubuntu ISO.
> 
Has this been officially released by canonical ? 
No, Canonical has no part in Ubuntu Ultimate Edition, it is a third-party version of the original version of Ubuntu.

> who maintainer of this ultimate edition? what is this features?
The maintainer is TheeMahn2003 of the Ubuntu Forums. It has quite a good bit of features, but most of them are only the stuff mentioned first. 
> 
why they build this ultimate edition? 
I think(In my personal) opinion, it is built to help Linux beginners get used to Linux a little more easily than usual since most of the applications that would be needed are either preloaded or can be installed automatically and without much of a hassle.

> i was under the impression that that didn't exist
Ubuntu Ultimate Edition seems to be rather inactive at the moment, but work may be underway(I do not know myself) on Ubuntu Ultimate Edition 1.6 which is supposedly based on Ubuntu 7.10.
