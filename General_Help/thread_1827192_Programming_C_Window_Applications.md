### Post by Smart Viking on 2011-08-17
For starters, you'd probably want to use a GUI toolkit. The most usual ones are GTK and QT, but there are more of them. Then you want to find documentation about how to make a window with your toolkit and language.

There's lots of GTK C documentation, I'll give you a couple of links on that all though I haven't seen at them myself:

GTK tutorial for beginners: [http://zetcode.com/tutorials/gtktutorial/](http://zetcode.com/tutorials/gtktutorial/)
Other stuff: [http://www.gtk.org/documentation.php](http://www.gtk.org/documentation.php)
