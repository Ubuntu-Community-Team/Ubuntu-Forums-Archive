Well... i don't know how it happened to make it all go wrong to begin with, but i know what i did to fix it....

i thought i'd check on my restricted drivers... and i got an error saying that i needed some file wich i promptly copied and pasted into synaptic and downloaded that.... then i checked out my restricted drivers again and they were not enabled so since i'm not sure how to enable them i tried restarting the computer and what do you know.... my restricted driver was enabled.... so then i just went to my visual effects settings and selected custom and what do you know.... it works and all is wel.... fixed all the problems actually.... and don't tell anyone but i'm kinda proud i figured it out myself.... windows.... especially the monster vista is looking more and more like a plague of the past....
