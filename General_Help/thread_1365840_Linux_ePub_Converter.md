### Post by tent405 on 2012-01-07
Sometimes you can unzip the .epub file and you'll have all the content that way. 

Calibre has failed me with its focus on ebook-readers, a poor interface, and a general non-unix way of implementing a CLI utility ([FONT="Courier New"]ebook-convert[/FONT]). It's alternative, fbreader, scared me away at first glance with its interface also.

I ran ``[FONT="Courier New"]file[/FONT]'' on my epub and found out it was a zip, from there an ``[FONT="Courier New"]unzip[/FONT]'' and now I have the book to read. In html, but it will do.

EPUB is a [free and open format]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/EPUB"), did you know? :-\"
