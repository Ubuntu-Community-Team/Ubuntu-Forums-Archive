### Post by advocate 21 on 2009-08-30
theres an app for that at the top panel... its called "pointer capture". you press it, and it locks your pointer until you click it. right click it and press preferences. go to the second part, "release pointer, and click all of the options, "ctrl, alt, shift". that means you lock it into place and to get it out of the locked status, you press ctrl alt AND shift and click. that way you dont have to worry about your son pressing power button. otherwise, id remove the keyboard and mouse.
BTW you have to right click the top panel and select 'Add to panel' to find the app.
