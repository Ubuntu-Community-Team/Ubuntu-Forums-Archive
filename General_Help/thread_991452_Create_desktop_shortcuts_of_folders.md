### Post by ibuclaw on 2008-11-23
Firstly, in Linux they are called **links**, not shortcuts.

To create a link, Click and Drag the folder/files over the Desktop, but before you let go, press the **Alt** Key and you'll see the mouse cursor change from an arrow to a question mark.

Release the mouse button, and you'll get a choice of options.

Select the **Link Here** option and a link will be created on your Desktop.
