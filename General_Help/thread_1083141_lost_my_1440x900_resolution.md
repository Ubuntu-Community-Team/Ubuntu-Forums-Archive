### Post by bonfire89 on 2009-08-29
So, now that a decade has passed I have finally figured out how to get the proper resolution... it was all after being embarased by not being able to attach my laptop to the tv to watch some movies when some friends were over.. and while trying to figure that out... I figured out my monitor issue. Here is my xorg file... for my intel 945gm chip running on Jaunty 9.04

```

Section "Device"
	Identifier	"Configured Video Device"
	Option		"AccelMethod"			"uxa"
	Option		"EXAOptimizeMigration"		"true"
	Option		"MigrationHeuristic"		"greedy"
	Option		"Tiling"			"true" # i8xx users: see note in guide
EndSection


Section "Monitor"
	Identifier	"Configured Monitor"
	UseModes "Modes[0]"
#UseModes "Modes[1]"

EndSection

Section "Screen"
	Identifier	"Default Screen"
	Monitor		"Configured Monitor"
	Device		"Configured Video Device"
EndSection


Section "Modes"
	Identifier "Modes[0]"
	# TV      Modeline "1280x720_60" 74.160 1280 1352 1392 1648 720 725 730 750 -hsync -vsync
	Modeline "1440x900_60.00" 106.47 1440 1520 1672 1904 900 901 904 932 -HSync +Vsync
EndSection

```



the key lines are the modelines... I have two in there.. when I want to attach to the tv I will uncomment the the tv line and comment the 1440*900 line.

There is probably a way to have both in the file at once... but I don't even want to touch it right now since things are working so great and I don't often attach to the tv.
