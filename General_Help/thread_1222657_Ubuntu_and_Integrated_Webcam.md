### Post by balaknair on 2009-07-25
You could try installing Ubuntu with Wubi to see how well it works with your hardware. With the exception of Suspend/Hibernate, most other stuff should work fine with Wubi(including the cam and dongle- if it doesn't work on Wubi, it probably won't work on a regular Ubuntu install).
You could also create a bootable USB thumb drive and boot from it to check how compatible it is.

If you find everything works, you can proceed to wiping Vista(I still don't get why Sony had to put Vista on an Atom machine) and installing Ubuntu.
