### Post by 3xpl0it on 2008-03-13
I would like to thank whoever made the Braid screensaver for the following.
1. Thrashing my drive and making it unstable
2. Causing a super bad memory leak causing my ram to overheat
3. Causing my quad core computer to nearly blow out the processor

Anyways, I found a fix and here is the fix

sudo rm /usr/share/applications/screensavers/briad.desktop

Highly recommend that command to anyone ;)
