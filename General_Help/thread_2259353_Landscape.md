### Post by grahammechanical on 2015-01-03
Remember, the Ubuntu we download is the same Ubuntu that a business user will download. There is only one desktop Ubuntu for all of us. We, home users of single machines running Ubuntu do not get a free but crippled Ubuntu. And business users do not get a better quality professional or enterprise Ubuntu because they have paid for it. It is the same Ubuntu for all and it is free to download by all no matter how many machines we wish to install Ubuntu on. This is a different business model to that used by some other Linux distributions.

So, Landscape is included for those business users who might just be interested in such a service. The money made by Canonical from such services pays Canonical engineers to develop Ubuntu.

Regards.
