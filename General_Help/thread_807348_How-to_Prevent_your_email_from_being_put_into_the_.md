Prevent your email from being put into the junk folder Using Thunderbird
By: me

I hate junk mail as much as much as the next person, but when sending emails to friends, coworkers, or clients they tend to have spam filters on there email accounts that prevents them from receiving my massage. This tutorial shows you how they get the massage every time.


1.Using Firefox Goto [http://www.instantssl.com/ssl-certificate-products/free-email-certificate.html](http://www.instantssl.com/ssl-certificate-products/free-email-certificate.html)
2.Sign up for your own email certificate.
3.You will receive a email from comodogroup.com. Open the email and click on “Click and Install Comodo Email Certificate”.
4.Back in Firefox you will receive a message box saying the certificate is installed.
5.Now  goto: Edit > Preferences > Advanced > Encryption >View Certificates in Firefox.
6.Backup your certificate and follow the instructions to save.
7.In Thunderbird goto: Edit > Preferences > Advanced > Certificates > View Certificates.
8.Now click on “Import” and follow the instructions
9.To encrypt an email: click on “Write”  and click the down arrow to the right of the “Security” button now and check “Digitally sign this massage”
10.Type out your Massage and click “Send”

Hope you like.
