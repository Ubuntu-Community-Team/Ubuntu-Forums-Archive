### Post by #&amp;thj^% on 2017-02-13
Go to System in the Menu Area...and Select Control Center.
Open that... and look for "Preferred Applications". Click That.
Another window will now open... you will see Tabs at the top...choose "System"
At the bottom you will see File Manager....Click the Drop Down and Choose "Caja"
You can now close or make other changes to your Mate DE.
