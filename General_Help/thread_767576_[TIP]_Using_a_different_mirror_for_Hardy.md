### Post by xArv3nx on 2008-04-25
Go to [https://launchpad.net/ubuntu/+archivemirrors](https://launchpad.net/ubuntu/+archivemirrors) and select a mirror for Hardy Heron (or another version, if you like) and edit your /etc/apt/sources.list with the mirror you've chosen.

If you need any help, just post. I'll be here all day.

PURPOSE OF THIS: This will allow people to have faster download speeds and help out the Ubuntu devs (by you using less of their "resources").

Enjoy your fast download speeds!
