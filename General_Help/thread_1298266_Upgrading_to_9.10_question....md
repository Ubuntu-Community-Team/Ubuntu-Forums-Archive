### Post by prem1er on 2009-10-22
You can, but it is not recommended. If you have a separate /home directory on your current setup, I suggest doing a fresh install.  I would also boot 9.10 (when it is released) from a CD to make sure everything is working as you would like it before you try and upgrade.
