I've used Places/Connect to Server, to connect to an FTP site.  I opted to bookmark the location and it's appeared in the Places drop-down-list.

### Post by John Bean on 2010-06-03
Click on a place - any place - to open Nautilus. Click "Bookmarks" menu, select "Edit bookmarks" or just press Ctrl+B.

---

### Post by jerenept on 2010-06-03
Open "Computer" in the Places menu. To disconnect, right click on the icon that states:
ftp as <the server you connected to>. Select "Unmount"
