### Post by gsmanners on 2011-10-15
Actually, the 520M (the nvidia card your machine uses) is supported by the latest stable (285.05.09) Linux driver from nvidia. The driver in the repo (280.13) should be fine (see [http://www.nvidia.com/object/linux-display-ia32-280.13-driver.html](http://www.nvidia.com/object/linux-display-ia32-280.13-driver.html) for more details).

You should be able to get this driver using jockey ("Additional Drivers" in your System settings, wherever those are).
