### Post by Ian0426 on 2008-12-07
Whenever I open a new window, it opens minimized and I have to then click on it to see it, or it opens but underneath the window I am currently viewing. How do I change it that it opens on top of the other windows?

I've looked at the Window preference menu, but it is woefully lacking in options, and I cannot find anything in Compiz that would change this. 

Also, often, but not always,  when I click on a minimized window, another window on the bottom tool-bar opens on top of it, and I have to play a game of opening the window I want a couple times before this stops. 

Any help would be greatly appreciated.

---

### Post by Ian0426 on 2008-12-07
I figured the problem out. It was in the Compiz Settings Manager under General Settings. Focus Prevention was turned on, and this was causing a number of odd desktop actions.
