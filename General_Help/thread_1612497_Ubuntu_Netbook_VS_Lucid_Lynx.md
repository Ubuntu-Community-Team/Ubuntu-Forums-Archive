### Post by snowpine on 2010-11-03
The user interface is *very* different between the two. You can find lots of screenshots online, or for the most thorough comparison, download and burn Live CDs of each and take them for a test drive to see for yourself which you like better (and which is more stable on your hardware).

Personally I have a strong preference for the plain old Gnome desktop. It is an industry standard desktop environment used by hundreds of Linux distros and thousands (millions?) of users. The Netbook interface on the other hand is unique to Ubuntu, and people seem to either love or hate it. But in the end it is your call which works best for your needs and your specific hardware. :)
