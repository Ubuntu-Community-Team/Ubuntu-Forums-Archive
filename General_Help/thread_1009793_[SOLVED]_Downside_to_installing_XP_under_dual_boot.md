### Post by Tim Sharitt on 2008-12-13
I don't think that dual booting would have any effect on your current system (other than allocating hard drive space for the installation). If you resize a partition to make room for XP, just be sure to backup your imortant files first. I've never had any problems with a resize corrupting a filesystem, but it does happen from time to time.
