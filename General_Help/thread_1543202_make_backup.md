### Post by deserthowler on 2010-07-31
There is a program/script called remastersys I use.  It works great but requires burning to a DVD and not a CD.  I used it to transfer my desktop system to my laptop with no problem using Ubuntu 9.10.  It makes a live DVD with an install option.

Make sure to clean off most of the compressed files on your system, like isos, video and music because they are already compressed and might not fit on a DVD.  Disconnect from the network and close everything but remastersys.

Earl

Oh, yeah, and read the instructions on the site.
