### Post by justleen on 2008-12-04
I had something similair. All the music on a second drive wouldnt be found (well, only a tiny bit, say 1gb out of 50gb)
I unchecked "watch my folder", restarted Rhythmbox, added the folders by hand until i had all, then ticked the "watch my folder again"
