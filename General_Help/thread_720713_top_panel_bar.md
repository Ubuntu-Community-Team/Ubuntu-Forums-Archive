### Post by dje on 2008-03-10
Do you still have network and volume icons?
If not do this:
1) Right click on the panel and select 'Add to panel'
2) Click and drag 'notification area' to the right side of the panel
3) logout and then login again
If you do try just logging out and logging in again

Hope that helps you
dje
