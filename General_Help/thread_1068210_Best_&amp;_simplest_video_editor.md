### Post by mb_webguy on 2009-02-12
Depending on what you mean by "simple", Avidemux is certainly an option.  It doesn't have as simple an interface as WMM -- in fact, you'll probably be either intimidated or disappointed te first few times you use it -- but it can do most of what WMM can do, if not more.  Doing certain things, like conversion and simple editing, is pretty easy, but applying fancy transitions and filters can be a bit daunting until you've used it for a while.

Cinelerra is designed to be a professional media editor, and is probably overkill unless you're an aspiring filmmaker.
