### Post by HermanAB on 2016-09-05
The password field on a terminal is usually unresponsive - blind.  That ensures that others cannot see your password or even deduce how many characters it is.

I usually press backspace half a dozen times to be sure there is really nothing in the buffer, then type my password and press enter.
