### Post by Kevbert on 2008-11-18
Please take a look at the attached screenshots (just click on them).  When posting just scroll down to Additional Options, select Manage Attachments in the window that appears click on browse to find your picture and then click on upload.  The pictures will now show in Additional Options. The attachments have size limits and files with only certain extensions (eg jpg) can be used.
