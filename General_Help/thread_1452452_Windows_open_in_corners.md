### Post by offbyone on 2010-04-11
I have extra visual effects enabled on Ubuntu and whenever I start programs they always open in the top-right corner of my screen. Is there any way to change this?

Thanks for the help.

---

### Post by stinkeye on 2010-04-12
CCSM > window management > place windows > placement mode

**Edit**: CCSM= *compizconfig-settings-manager* which you will find in synaptic package manager.

---

### Post by offbyone on 2010-04-12
Works perfectly. Thanks!
