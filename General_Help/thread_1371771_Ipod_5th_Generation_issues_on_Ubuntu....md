### Post by switch10 on 2010-01-04
I am positive that you wont be getting your 5th IPod nano to sync with Ubuntu natively (not yet anyway).  Apple's new iPod storing system does not comply with Ubuntu.

Your only option as of right now, is to install VirtualBox and Windows. Don't waste your time installing stuff that wont work.

Have a look at these:
[http://brainstorm.ubuntu.com/idea/23130/](http://brainstorm.ubuntu.com/idea/23130/)
[http://www.watkissonline.co.uk/wordpress/?p=2707](http://www.watkissonline.co.uk/wordpress/?p=2707)

and here is a tutorial that will get you up and running with VirtualBox:
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/PortableDevices/iPhone#Syncing%20with%20Firmware%202.x%20possible](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/PortableDevices/iPhone#Syncing%20with%20Firmware%202.x%20possible)!

(it says for an iphone but its the exact same process)

good luck

Dave
