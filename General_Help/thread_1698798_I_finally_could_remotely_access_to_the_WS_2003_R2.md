### Post by rva1945 on 2011-03-02
I wish to thank all of the guys that took their time to answer my questions. To all those that read my posts, too.
 
Now I wish to share the solution I found in case it's useful to anyone.
 
As the XP partition doesn't boot anymore, I couldnt access to the to the WS2003R2 at the office. No Linux client could connect to that server.
 
I created a XP VM as a last and desperate try...that didn't work either. gathering as much info as I could here and there, it seems that I needed a mstsc version 6 or later.
 
In a Spanish forum, a guy had a similar problem but after formatting and reinstalling Windows. Another adviced him to upgrade to mstsc 6.1 from MS. That's what I did in the VM...now I can work from home again.
 
Sorry to detail a solution to a Windows application here. I hope we could easily connect from Linux to any server in the future.
