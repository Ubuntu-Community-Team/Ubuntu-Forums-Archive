### Post by beno1990 on 2009-01-15
Xubuntu uses the XFCE desktop, which people kind of see as Gnome-Lite. Gnome is the desktop environment (look and feel) in Ubuntu, and supports 3D effects and such whuich Xubuntu doesn't; Xubuntu only supports transparancy and a few other smaller effects.

On the whole, Xubuntu is intended to be a stripped-down version of Ubuntu for computers with less horsepower or where conserving resources is important.
