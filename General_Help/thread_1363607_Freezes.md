### Post by hal8000 on 2009-12-24
Next time you get a freeze type ctrl-alt-esc

This bings up a skull and crossbones cursor, left click the application that caused the freeze, this should kill the app.

If its only certain apps that cause the system to freeze, it may not be software related and an update may fix this.

I would also be looking through /var/log/messages to see if anything has been wrote to the log.
