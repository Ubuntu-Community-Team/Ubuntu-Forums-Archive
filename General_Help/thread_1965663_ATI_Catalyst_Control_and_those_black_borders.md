### Post by normcross on 2012-04-26
I use a 32 inch lcd tv for my monitor and with the ATI driver I was getting black borders all round the screen.

 In the Catalyst Control Center it stated:- "Display connected:-  1.[ Panasonic -TV] ATI Radeon..etc" but  was showing the monitor up as a "Projector". Eh? something is not being detected correctly. That meant I did not have any overscan/fullscreen controls.  Fiddling with ATIconfig etc etc did not help at all.

Now, I have another computer with an actual monitor which also has HDMI input. Using that monitor instead, the Catalyst Control detected correctly and offered me the overscan controls. I set up full screen and saved settings.

Re-connected my 32" Panasonic tv and I now have FULL SCREEN. Yippee .

### Post by iranai on 2012-05-06
I use a Sony 40" TV via HDMI for my monitor as well. 

Solution: Go into the Catalyst Control Center> under Display Manager select your monitor > select the Adjustments tab > under Scaling Options move the slider to the right  (Overscan). Good bye black borders. If you are using a really old version of the CCC, you need to right click on the monitor icon in the display manager section> select Properties > then you have the Adjustment/Overscan tab (if I remember right).
