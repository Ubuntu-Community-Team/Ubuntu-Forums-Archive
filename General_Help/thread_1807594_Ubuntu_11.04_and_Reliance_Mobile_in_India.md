Thanks for the reply. I wanted to upgrade to 12.04 and I was able to test my Reliance cellular modem by installing VirtualBox onto my Win 7 64bit host OS (the modem works fine in Windows). Then I installed 12.04 as a guest OS in VirtualBox. The 12.04 guest OS then easily recognized the internet connect thru VirtualBox. So that is the setup I'm using now. Works great. Once I confirmed this was working. I then deleted 10.10 and GRUB from my Windows. I'm happy with 12.04 running in VirtualBox.
