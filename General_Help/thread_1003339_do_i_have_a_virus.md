### Post by p_quarles on 2008-12-06
Well, I'm guessing it says "lsb_release" (where "lsb" is "linux standard base"), and "zombie" in Linux means an orphaned process, not a bot. 

So, while it's odd that this displays first thing upon booting, the words themselves aren't cause for concern, and malware usually doesn't go out of its way to announce itself in an obvious fashion. 

Short answer: no.
