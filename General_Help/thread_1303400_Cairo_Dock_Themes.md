### Post by fabounet on 2009-10-28
the package should be in your home, named : "name_of_the_theme.tar.gz"
it should be usable by anyone by just drag and dropping the tarball into the Theme Manager.

the themes are stored in ~/.config/cairo-dock/themes, and the extra themes (gauges, clock, etc) in ~/.config/cairo-dock/extra
