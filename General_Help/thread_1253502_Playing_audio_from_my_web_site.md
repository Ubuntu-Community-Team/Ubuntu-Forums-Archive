### Post by P4man on 2009-08-30
Your site works fine for me, I do get audio. However, you are using MP3 files. despite what many think, MP3 is not a free format, it requires a licence. Therefore, its not included by default on ubuntu. 

Either install MP3 codec on ubuntu 

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RestrictedFormats](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RestrictedFormats)

and/or start converting you audio to a format that truly is open and free. In other words, OGG. Works on linux, windows, mac, most "MP3" players.
