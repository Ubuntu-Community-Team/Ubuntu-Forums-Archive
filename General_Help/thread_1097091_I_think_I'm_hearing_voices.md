### Post by 3rdalbum on 2009-04-12
What probably happened was your mouse pointer got moved over a sound file icon (probably a podcast?) in an open file browser window or on your desktop, thus activating the soundfile preview function. When you opened your laptop lid, it may have jarred the trackpad just enough to move the pointer away from that icon.

I seem to be constantly activating this function; I throw my cursor away to the left side of the screen so I can read some text and it always lands over a sound file of a Zydeco song :-)
