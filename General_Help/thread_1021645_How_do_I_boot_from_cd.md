### Post by 1packer on 2008-12-25
You have to restart your computer with the cd in the drive. Then on the initial loading screen you can hit f11 or f12, look to see what the initial window tells you to do. My dell then gives the option to boot to internal hard drive or cd, since I have it configured to that, as well as change BIOS settings. If you have that option, select cd and press enter. Otherwise you need to change the boot order in the BIOS and enable booting from cds.
