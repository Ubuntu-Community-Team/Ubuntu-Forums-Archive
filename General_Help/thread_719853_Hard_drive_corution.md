### Post by Rocket2DMn on 2008-03-09
On the LiveCD go to System->Administration->Partition Editor
This program is called GParted and you can use it to delete all the partitions on the appropriate hard drive.  You can leave the space empty or format it with your choice of file system(s).
An alternative is to use the GParted LiveCD - [http://gparted-livecd.tuxfamily.org/](http://gparted-livecd.tuxfamily.org/)
