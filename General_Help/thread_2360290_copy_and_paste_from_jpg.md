### Post by mastablasta on 2017-05-03
in GIMP use select tool (the dotted square icon for example), then mark what you want to copy, then press contorl C or go to edit->copy

now you can paste it where ever you want (crtl+v is short cut for that).

other options for a "select tool" include elipcse (circle), freehand, fuzzy, colour based.

a similar method exists in other picture editors.
