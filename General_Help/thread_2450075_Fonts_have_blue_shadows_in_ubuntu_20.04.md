### Post by Impavidus on 2020-09-07
That blue shadow isn't visible in your screenshots. Large screenshots get scaled down on the forum and there are some jpeg artefacts, making it hard to see. Can you create a screenshot of a small segment of text (just a few letters) and save it in png format? And when you look at the screenshot yourself in an image editor at large magnification, is the blue shadow still there?

This could be related to font antialiasing. Try changing those settings.
