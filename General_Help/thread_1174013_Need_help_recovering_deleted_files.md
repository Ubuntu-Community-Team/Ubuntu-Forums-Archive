### Post by cubeist on 2009-05-30
I think your out of luck.  Even if you could recover a partial image of the truecrypt volume/folder, it is unlikely you will be able to decrypt it... From my experience, one time I accidentally removed a truecrypt pendrive in the middle of a data transfer and was never able to recover the data... so even though the file header was readable, having corrupted data in the volume prevented truecrypt from completing the de-crypt.

Anyway, long story short, if the data you lost is very, very important, or the only known copy of something (like an essay you put 40 hours into!) then you could try a professional data recovery service, but this will cost you big bucks.
