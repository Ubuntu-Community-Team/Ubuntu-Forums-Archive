ext4 is stable, or at least IMO.

There have been a few, IMO, minor problems and, again, IMO, they are being blown out of proportion. 

There can be data loss if you have a power outage. So you can lose 2 minutes of data.

Not to say that some people have not had major problems, they are in the vast minority. The majority of people on ext4 have had no problems.

Keep in mind, data loss happens, on every OS and every file system. This is why we make back ups.

If data is important to you, back it up. Problem solved ;)
