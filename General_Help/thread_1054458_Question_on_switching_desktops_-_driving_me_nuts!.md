### Post by Jameshardy88 on 2009-01-29
you should by default have a workspace switcher on your bottom panel, by clicking on any of your other workspaces you should be able to revert between them. If you dont have one simply right click on your panel (top panel even if thats all you have then "add to panel" and add a workspace switcher. If you decide that you definitely don't want more than one desktop(Personally i recommend giving it a bit more of a go once you get used to it they are a godsend, however i appreciate they are not for every one) simply right click on your workspace shifter and you can change how many you have in your preferences, reduce it to one and hey-presto.

hope this helped, James
