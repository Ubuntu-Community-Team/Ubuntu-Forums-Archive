### Post by warp99 on 2009-08-06
You have to have the Launcher/Taskmanager applet loaded in order to add icons. So right click on the AWN bar and choose "Dock Preferences" then go to applets. See if it's running. If not add it.

You can always add the launcher icons by going to the launcher section of dock preferences and adding them there.
