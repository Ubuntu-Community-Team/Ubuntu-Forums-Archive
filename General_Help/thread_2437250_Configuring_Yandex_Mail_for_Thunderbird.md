### Post by MZ250Supa5 on 2020-02-20
I had a problem configuring Yandex Mail so that it would send from the Thunderbird client. I checked that I had configured it properly, and still puzzled as to why mail wouldn't send I found this post: [https://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2409928](https://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2409928)  Reading through it I saw that no one had solved the issue, but I've found the solution and it's as simple as reading the Yandex guide to setting up the e-mail client, in this case, Thunderbird.

The solution is very simple, and it's just ensuring that the username is only the first part of the e-mail address, i.e. that before the @. Full details can be found here. [https://yandex.com/support/mail/mail-clients/mozilla-thunderbird.html](https://yandex.com/support/mail/mail-clients/mozilla-thunderbird.html)
