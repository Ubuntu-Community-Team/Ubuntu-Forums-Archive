### Post by Fire_Chief on 2008-05-23
Worldy,

It really depends on what factors are bottlenecking your performance on the PC. Do you have plenty of RAM? Decent video card? correct drivers? good processor? If one or more of these is on the low end then increasing/upgrading one of those will provide much better performance boost than just changing the kernel.

This is not to say that a kernel upgrade and architecture specific tuning can help but don't expect to see strong improvements in your day to day use.
