### Post by waspbr on 2012-07-01
Out of the blue the mouse will get stuck on  right click and keep on selecting everything, if it is on the desktop it will make a selection box. 

eventually it lets go, by closing the window or pressing escape but now I cannot right click anymore, or use the scroll wheel. 

This happens on both the trackpad and my bluetooth mouse.  

Logging off or rebooting does not fix the issue but the mouse and trackpad work fine in windows 7 (Different partition). 

Any thoughts? 

Thanks in advance

---

### Post by waspbr on 2012-07-01
The problem seemed to be related to the bluetooth. 

I had to turn off the bluetooth (wireless) switch at my laptop and the trackpad would work normally again. I proceeded to remove both the bluetooth mouse and keyboard and had them paired again. 

everything seems to be back to normal for now.
