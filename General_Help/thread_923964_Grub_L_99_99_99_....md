### Post by mb_webguy on 2008-09-19
That's a little bit odd, because the L would indicate Lilo rather than Grub...

Apparently there's a problem with your disk.  After a little bit of research, I found [this post]("http://expert.mandriva.com/question/39158").  The answer for that person was to do a low-level format of the disk and then reinstall.

However, before I did that, I'd try reinstalling Grub, and if that didn't work, I'd try installing Lilo (another bootloader used by some other Linux distributions).
