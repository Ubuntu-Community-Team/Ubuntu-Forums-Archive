### Post by mpintod on 2008-11-16
Problem Solved!!!

I found another thread to solve the issue (do not have the link). But what I did is the following:

1.- Open volume control panel
2.- Select edit and preferences
3.- Check external amplifier in volume control preferences dialog if it is not already checked
4.- Close volume control preferences dialog
5.- Select switches tab in volume control window
6.- Uncheck External Amplifier Option
7.- Close Volume Control Window

I do not even know what this instructions do (if someone could explain me it would be great), but now I have sound for DVDs and system sounds.

Thank you very much for your help.
