### Post by fromgi on 2008-11-16
I made mine work with 8.10 Evolution version 2.24.1.1.

I found my phone numbers are stored in the format (XXX) XXX-XXXX.  I have a space following the character ")" after the area code.  If I delete this space, then the mobile number for that entry appears in the listing.  I removed this space in over 100 numbers, and all the mobile numbers are now being displayed in the listing.

Hopefully a fix is on the way.
