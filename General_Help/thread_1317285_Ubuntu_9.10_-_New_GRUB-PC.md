### Post by wrgb2 on 2009-11-06
See this link for some details: [http://www.gnu.org/software/grub/grub-2.en.html](http://www.gnu.org/software/grub/grub-2.en.html).  The reason it's asking you what you want to do is that it's inherently unsafe to update the boot loader while Ubuntu is running.  I would say "keep my current version" and if you want grub2 udate it manually.
