### Post by Steveway on 2008-07-06
Yup, DTA like twoshadetod said and for those "one-click-hosts" like rapidshare or megaupload, use jDownloader. (jDownloader is sadly written in Java but the good thing is that it doesn't feel as bad as most other Java-based applications.) It deciphers most of their Captchas automatically and it can even restart your router so that you get a new IP if needed. (Router needs to be supported but there are guides to make your own restart-script.)
