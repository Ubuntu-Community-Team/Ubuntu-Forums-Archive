### Post by t0p on 2009-10-26
I expect Dell means that the motherboard is made to work with a maximum of 1 GB of RAM.  This is a *physical* limitation: meaning that 1 GB is the most it will work with, regardless of which OS you use.

The 32-bit 4 GB limit is something completely different.

### Post by empty_spaces on 2009-10-26
When the manufacturer says that the max supported ram for a laptop is 1GB, that usually means that each slot will support a max of 512MB (assuming you have 2 slots).
So do your research before you invest in a 1Gb stick.
