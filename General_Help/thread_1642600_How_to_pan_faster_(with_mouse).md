### Post by stinkeye on 2010-12-10
I'm using opera and the pan speed seems fairly fast for me.
I never use the pan though.
I just use scroll and have mouse gestures set up to go to end of page and top.
These are good for reading forums.
You can configure the opera gestures in tools > preferences > advanced > shortcuts

I turn off mouse gestures in opera and use easystroke instead for system wide gestures.
Then you can just bind a keyboard shortcut to a gesture.
eg I have the home key bound to gesture up
and end to gesture down.
