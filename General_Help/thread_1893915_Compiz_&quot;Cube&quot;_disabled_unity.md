### Post by mikegior on 2011-12-11
Hello all,

Out of curiosity, I opened up the Compiz Config manager to enable the desktop cube. The cube is enabled and works I suppose, but now it appears as if Unity isn't starting? When I log in, I am brought to my desktop with no icons, no side-bar, and nothing in the bar at the top. How can I turn off the cube function if that's what has damaged Unity?

---

### Post by cthom on 2011-12-11
back into ccsm and un-tick 'enable desktop cube'. you should have received warning, though, that setting desktop cube would disable desktop wall which unity needs. (apparently...)

---

### Post by mikegior on 2011-12-11
Thanks for your help. This is now solved. 

I disabled the two cube settings and checked the "Desktop Wall" box and everything is back to normal!
