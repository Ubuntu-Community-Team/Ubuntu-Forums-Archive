### Post by pytheas22 on 2008-08-22
Look at your system log at the time of the freeze to see what it says.  You can view the system log at System>Administration>System Log, or you can open the file /var/log/syslog directly in a text editor or whatever.  The log has timestamps; see what it's recording at the time of the crash.  Hopefully it will mention why it's shutting down.
