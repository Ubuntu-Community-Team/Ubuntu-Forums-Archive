### Post by d3v1ant_0n3 on 2006-10-16
To use the Beryl themer (Emerald), right click on the icon in the tray, and click 'Emerald Theme manager', you can select and edit themes here.

In 'Beryl Settings Manager', check you have 'Rotate Cube' plugin checked, and check the keyboard shortcuts under the tabs.

Do you have wobbly windows, etc? Always a good sign that something is working.
