### Post by tilii on 2011-03-25
Go to System -> Preferences -> Screensaver and make sure the "Lock screen when screensaver is active" box is unticked.

---

### Post by Rubi1200 on 2011-03-25
Go to System > Preferences > Screensaver and change the time from 5 minutes to whatever you want.

Also, you can uncheck the boxes for activating the screensaver and locking it.
