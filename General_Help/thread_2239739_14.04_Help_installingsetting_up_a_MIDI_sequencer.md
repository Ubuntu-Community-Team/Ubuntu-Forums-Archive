### Post by maika2 on 2014-08-15
SOLVED!
For future reference:
-Open QjackCtl
-Open Qsynth
-Open AriaMeastosa

From JACK, click "Connect". Go to the MIDI tab. Under Outputs, select aria_maestosa, then midi_out. Under inputs, secect Qsynth1, then midi. Click "Connect." And you're good! Thanks for the help.!
