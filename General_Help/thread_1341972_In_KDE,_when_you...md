### Post by Zorael on 2009-11-30
I imagine that it will be saved to the directory you have specified to be your Download directory, in System Settings -> About Me -> Paths (assuming KDE 4.3, if not you may need to manually edit **~/.config/user-dirs.dirs**).

Just beware if you're changing those paths if they're currently pointing to your home directory. It will offer to move files to the new directory, and if previously at home, it will relocate all your settings elsewhere and bork your user.
