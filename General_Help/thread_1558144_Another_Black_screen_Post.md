### Post by jerryrice on 2010-08-21
fixed the black screen on boot problem. Maybe this will help someone else, go into recovery mode from grub, then failsafex mode, run in low resolution mode (at least you get a display that works).   Then I downloaded my ATI drivers, ran catalyst to set up a dual display, and voila, back in business with a dual display.
