### Post by TeoBigusGeekus on 2011-02-18
You don't need them - delete them.
It's a good idea that you keep all the manually installed gz files; they are not only needed for reinstallation, but also for uninstallation (sudo make uninstall). Synaptic can't manage manually installed (from source code) packages.
