### Post by 3rdalbum on 2011-05-11
Check your RAM. Not using Memtest, I mean actually check it by taking some of it out while the computer is off, rebooting, seeing if the problem occurs. If the problem still occurs, put that RAM back in and take a different RAM stick out. Repeat until no problems or you've checked all the RAM modules.
