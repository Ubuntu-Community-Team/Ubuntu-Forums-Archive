### Post by Moezzie on 2008-11-22
The first thing you should try is go go into bios and change the bootorder of your devices. You want the cd/dvd to be booted before your harddrive. 
To get into bios, usually you have to press delete or F12 on the first screen you see when the computer boots up. The rest is pretty self explanatory.
Once you do that you should be good to go. Just pop the cd in and wait.

Good luck!
