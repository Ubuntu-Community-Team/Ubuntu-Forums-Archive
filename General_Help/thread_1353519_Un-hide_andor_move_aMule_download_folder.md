### Post by howefield on 2009-12-13
> **PepeLapiu said:**
> How do I make a specific folder unhidden, or how do move my files from that folder in order to move/edit/play/run/view them as I wish?

All your hidden files/folders have a period "." in front of them. If you open your home folder in nautilus and press Ctrl + H, you will see the hidden folders.

To unhide, rename the folder to remove the period, or simply use ctrl h when you want to see them.

Not knowing what amule is, I don't know how renaming the folder will affect or how you can tell it to use another folder.
