### Post by RJARRRPCGP on 2008-11-09
Has it worked properly with Windows? (if you used to use Windows)

And if not, are you overclocking? 

Even more of a concern if you're overclocking with a Via chipset. 
Because of the AGP frequency going too high. Especially higher than 71 mhz. 

Then means if you have a Via KT400 chipset, you're limited to roughly 180 mhz for the FSB. 

If you want 200 mhz FSB with a Via KT400 chipset, you can forget about it!
