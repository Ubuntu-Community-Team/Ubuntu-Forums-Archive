### Post by seventyeights on 2007-10-19
Try moving the bar at the top over to the sides or bottom, (just drag it) and then move the windows out of the way, leaving space at the top.  Now put the bar back at the top,  your apps shouldn't get caught underneath it anymore.

Edit: Also, if you are using compiz, alt+leftmouseclick in any window to move it.
