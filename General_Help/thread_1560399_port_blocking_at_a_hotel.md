### Post by mike555 on 2010-08-24
I have never known a hotel to block any ports , their isp might block port 25 , if so just set thunderbird to use another (if your mail server allows it) for instance on gmail they use port 465 for smtp.

  If you find that your email is somehow blocked just go to hotmails webpage with your browser and use it.
