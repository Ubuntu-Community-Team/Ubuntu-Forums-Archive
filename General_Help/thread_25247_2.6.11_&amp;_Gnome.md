### Post by brettlpb on 2005-04-09
The kernel doesn't really have anything to do with what DE you choose.  If you compiled it yourself, you need to make sure you configured it right.  There are no "compatability" issues with 2.6.11 and Gnome in general.

If you hard freeze when you start Gnome (from xdm) I'd check on your audio/video settings in the kernel, for starters... maybe.
