### Post by 3Miro on 2009-03-29
You can set up for how many days he history is to be kept form the Firefox menus. However, old history files would be deleted. You can browse and manually delete the history if you press Ctr + H.

If you wish to backup your profile, then you need to go to Places -> Home, then press Ctr + H to show hidden files, then go to .mozilla/firefox and you will see the profiles and history and everything else.
