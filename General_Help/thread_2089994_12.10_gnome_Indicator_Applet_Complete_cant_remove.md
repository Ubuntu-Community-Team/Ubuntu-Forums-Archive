I clicket Remove from Panel but it added a second instance of the Applet. Repeated Removes, now I have top panel full of Indicator-Applet-Completes, do not know how many.

### Post by Yumi on 2012-12-01
Found the answer by trial and error.

Alt+Right Click brought up the menu. Then the Down Key twice to highlight "Remove from Panel" and hit Enter. Gone

Moving the Mouse Pointer over "Remove from Panel" and Left Click results in a new instance of the applet.
