### Post by patchwork on 2010-03-23
Can you please post your /var/log/Xorg.0.log?

It is possible that your old driver was not properly removed, or the new driver not properly installed (i.e. the X server is trying to use a driver that is not loaded as it should be, and is defaulting to nvidia-auto-select)
