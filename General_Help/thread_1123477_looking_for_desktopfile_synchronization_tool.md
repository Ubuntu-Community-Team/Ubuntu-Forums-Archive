Check out [dropbox]("http://www.getdropbox.com"). Thats what I use for my important documents. As soon as a file in your dropbox folder is saved it gets uploaded to the dropbox site and synched up with your other computer. Also if you log onto the site you can get any revision you made to a file.

The best way to use it is to link to your important files or folders so that you don't have to actually move them into the dropbox folder.
