### Post by PmDematagoda on 2007-12-14
> I have 4 intel q6600 processors, so would that be the 32 bit version of Ubuntu?
Not necessarily, since the Q6600 is a 64 bit capable processor which most likely is operating on a 64 bit capable main board, you can also use Ubuntu 64 bit.

> And can Ubuntu run all 4 processors and let me control what each one is doing?

Ubuntu can run on all 4 "processors" but I am not sure whether you can control what each processor does on Ubuntu.

> 
How well supported is the nvidia GeForce 8500 and do wireless mice and keyboards work correctly out the box or is their installing needed??

Nvidia as a whole is usually well supported on Ubuntu, but you may have a problem with your particular Nvidia card since some people did report a few problems using the Nvidia 8series on Linux. Not very sure about wireless keryboards or mice(Is the plural of mouse, mice? I have no idea myself.;)), but I have seen posts given by people who have managed to use them on Ubuntu, a few even out of the box, so you might be lucky.
