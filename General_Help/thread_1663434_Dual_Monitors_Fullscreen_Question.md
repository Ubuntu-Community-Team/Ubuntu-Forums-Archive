### Post by Krytarik on 2011-01-09
I did a little web search on that matter, since I become interested in dual monitor configs, because I recently hooked up my mum's TV to her machine.

That's just a guess, because apparently there aren't much people which actively want to have that behaviour, it's more of an issue in most cases: Try modifying the output positioning in "System -> Preferences -> CompizConfig Settings Manager -> General -> Display Settings", you may even try to set faulty values.

This all could have some side effects, e.g. dialogs could appear in the middle of both screens.
