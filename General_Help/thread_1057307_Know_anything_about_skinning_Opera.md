### Post by adamlau on 2009-02-02
Preferences (Ctrl+F12, or Alt+P) > Advanced > Shortcuts > Keyboard setup > Edit. Map any key to what you want, taking into consideration the mappings of your DE. I have a custom keyboard.ini which maps the following:

```
F10 = Enable menu bar | Disable menu bar
```
