### Post by evilbuntu on 2009-05-13
When the usual reinstall does not work (as it didnt for me along with driver reinstalls and so much more)

go to your synaptic pack manager and just search "flash"

then scroll down the ENTIRE list and check EVERY (edit:decoder package).

(k after people saying dont)
edit: all i installed were all the decoder libraries. i looked in my package manager and freshened my memory of what i did. but it has been the ONLY thing that helped. if you are all worried, take an image first.

install them all and restart, you should have NO more troubles (to be honest i dont even think i had to restart)....


Thanks
