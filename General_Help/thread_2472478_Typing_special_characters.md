### Post by Impavidus on 2022-02-28
Use a keyboard layout that has dead keys. Usually those dead keys can be used for accents. Hit the dead key, then hit the key belonging to the letter you want to modify. For example, hit dead-acute, then e, and you'll get an e with an acute accent.
