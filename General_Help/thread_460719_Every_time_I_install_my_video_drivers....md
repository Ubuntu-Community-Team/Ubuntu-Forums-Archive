### Post by Kuoi on 2007-05-31
Why you ask ?
Do you have some issues with the videocard , or do you just don't understand the window about the restricted driver?

All looks fine by me , and it seems your videocard is installed.

If you're new to Ubuntu or Linux  , I suggest you leave it like it is , because otherwise it is possible that you cant boot anymore because of a Xserver (Xorg) error.

All can be fixed , that's not the issue , but I had many problems installing the correct video driver.

I've used  [Envy]("http://www.albertomilone.com/nvidia_scripts1.html") as last option , and it was working .
I suggest if you use it , that you uninstall the video driver first using ENVY , and then install the correct driver with ENVY.

Kuoi
