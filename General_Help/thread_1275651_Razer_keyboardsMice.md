### Post by stinkeye on 2009-09-27
I have a dual boot system... xp and ubuntu 9.04.
The only thing I use windows for is to play an old first person shooter sof2.
I use a nostromo n52 gaming pad with a G15 logitech mouse.
The G15 works perfectly but to change the default settings you have to set it up under windows and then it's stored in the mouse.
eg. In the normal browsing profile I have the mouse button 4 programmed to ctrl+alt+rightarrow to switch desktops.

The n52 is just a mini keyboard and requires no setup.
You can't setup the n52 to use programmable keys as you can under windows but becuase of its design it's not really necessary.
I keep the n52 plugged in next to my main key board and just switch over for games.
