### Post by Therion on 2008-12-22
Go to System/Administration/Synaptic Package Manager
Search on "OpenOffice" (all one word, not dot org, just the way it's shown).
Mark all Open Office packages that are found for complete removal by right-clicking on them.
Click on "Apply".
Let Synaptic do it's thing and you're done.
