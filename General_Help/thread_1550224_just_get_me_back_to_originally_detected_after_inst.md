### Post by MatthewAdams on 2010-08-10
So you're using digital flat panel projectors, according to your xorg.conf?

Instead of: "DFP-0: nvidia-auto-select +640+0, DFP-1: nvidia-auto-select +0+0"

Maybe try: "DFP-0: [desired resolution] +0+0, DFP-1: [desired resolution] +0+0"

This SHOULD set the resolution of each monitor. Xorg.conf has been infamously tricky when I've tried to edit it. 

Before I make you crash your x-server, can anyone else confirm or add their own ideas?
