### Post by IceReaver75 on 2008-11-04
I guess from what I have been reading here is that the Nvidia Drivers don't play nice with the new xorg version in Ibex and thats why you get the title bar flicker with Nvidia cards.  So what I did to work around it for now is installed emerald through the Synaptic Package Manager then installed some Emerald themes and I have been using that for my windows manager for now until Nvidia fixes their drivers.  That has taken all the flicker away.
