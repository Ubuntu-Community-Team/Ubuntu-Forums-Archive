### Post by kevinatkins on 2010-10-08
Hi,

As a starting point, have a look in the Open Printing database for any model you're interested in - 

[http://www.openprinting.org/printers]("http://www.openprinting.org/printers")

As a general rule though, HP, Epson and some Brother models work well; Dell and Lexmark are poorly supported (although I believe things are changing for the better). If you're thinking of buying a 'multifunction' or 'all-in-one' (ie, printer / scanner combo), I have found that HP are the easiest to get going.
