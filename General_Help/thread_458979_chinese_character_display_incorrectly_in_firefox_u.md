### Post by cisforcojo on 2007-07-12
Is it ALL Chinese characters or just some?

I had some trouble with characters that required Chinese encoding.

Try this:

View -> Character Encoding -> Auto-Detect -> Simplified Chinese (or whatever you're using)

The default is Off.

This fixed it for me.
