I was wondering if someone could tell me or link me to a page that explains how to change Folder Icon Images.  I don't want to do entire Themes.  I just want to have custom images for Folders on my desktop.  Thanks in advance for any help.

---

### Post by Sam on 2008-10-12
Right click on any folder in nautilus, choose "Properties", then on the first tab ("Basic"), click the button with the icon on the upper left and choose whatever picture you want.
