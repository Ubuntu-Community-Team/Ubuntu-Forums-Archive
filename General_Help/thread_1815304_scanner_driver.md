### Post by Mark Phelps on 2011-08-01
Folders are not executable items; thus, clicking on them will only display them.

Are you clicking on it using Nautilus?  IF so, you should see folders and files within there.

If you're not seeing anything, the folders and files could be "hidden".  When you next open Nautilus, look for an option that allows you to see hidden files.
