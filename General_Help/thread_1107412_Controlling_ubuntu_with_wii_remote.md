### Post by aeiah on 2009-03-26
you'll need bluetooth on your pc, and if you want to use it like you do on a wii (ie, moving the cursor by waving the wiimote around) you'll need a sensorbar. either a homemade one, an official one or one of those 3rd party ones. you can even use candles if you want. all the sensorbar is is two infared light sources spaced at either end of the bar. the wiimote triangulates from those two points.

ive not actually done it myself but i was looking into it. there are instructions somewhere on the internet. perhaps even on this forum.
