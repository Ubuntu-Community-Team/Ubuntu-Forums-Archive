### Post by cymbaline42 on 2011-05-01
You could use Wget ([http://linuxtuts.blogspot.com/2008/03/tutorials-on-wget.html](http://linuxtuts.blogspot.com/2008/03/tutorials-on-wget.html)) to download entire webpages. 

Possible?  Yes.  Advisable?  Since getting internet access is difficult, it is definitely advisable to keep a copy of it offline. As to the legal part, I do not know.  If it's a Wiki, it should be free which does not require a password and is editable by anyone, so it seems that you're on safe ground.  But you may want to read their fine print if you want to be sure.
