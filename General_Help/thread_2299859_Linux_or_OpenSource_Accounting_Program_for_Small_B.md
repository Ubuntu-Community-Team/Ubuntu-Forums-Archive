### Post by ian-weisser on 2015-10-22
Check with your accountant for recommendations, regardless of OS.
Or seek out a new accountant familiar with open source software, and get their recommendation.

My business' accountant, for example, is fantastic. Not too expensive, highly knowledgeable, friendly, and answers questions fast. Unfortunately...to get her best service, she wants our data in QB. That's what she knows how to navigate quickly and get accurate information from.

Do I want to pay her to learn another system? Not really. So every six years or so I pay the MS and Intuit tax...which adds up to less than one year of accounting fees anyway. Someday, when she retires, I will seek out an accountant familiar with open source software, install anew, and revise my workflow.
