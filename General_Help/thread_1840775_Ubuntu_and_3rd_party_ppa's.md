### Post by pagemaker on 2011-09-08
Is it possible to have a rolling-release-like 'buntu distro by using 3rd party ppa's? Can you get all updates and upgrades this way so you don't have to reinstall every six months but, on the other hand, stay always up-to-date?

What's the main difference with a real rolling distro?

---

### Post by ajgreeny on 2011-09-08
No not possible, I'm afraid.  The apps may be updated, depending on the ppa in question but the underlying OS would not.  That is the big difference between "rolling release" and version style distros, ie the OS packages are not continuously updated after version release in Ubuntu, but they are in a RR distro.
