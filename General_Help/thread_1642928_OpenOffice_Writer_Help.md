### Post by Wtower on 2010-12-11
At the formatting toolbar, the default first button is templates and formatting (or something alike as i am using oo in my native language). Pressing that opens a small window with all available styles. Within that window, I belive the fourth button is page templates. There you can create a new page template with its own headers and footers and apply it on any section you wish. You change sections by placing section breaks.
