I have tried both Easy Ubuntu and Automatix on Dapper.  Automatix is still in beta, I believe, but it did do the job very nicely on the release candidate version.

Easy Ubuntu worked for me just as well.  Overall, the interface on Easy Ubuntu is more sophisticated.

I personally have not experienced, a system meltdown from any of the automated installers.

Update:
I forgot to add, that I do prefer Automatix.  My preference is a combination of past experience of Automatix with Breezy, and the sheer amount of support and interest in Automatix.
