### Post by Sendervictorius on 2006-03-20
There are commercial products such as Applix Office (Applixware for Linux) and Xess Spreadsheet for Linux that will read wk4. But that's probably something you didn't want to hear.

There are converters published by Lotus for Windows, and Microsoft Excel will read wk4. If you have a friend with windows, you could ask them to save as .xls.
