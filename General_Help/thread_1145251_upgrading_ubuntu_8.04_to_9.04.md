### Post by SentientFluid on 2009-05-01
You can only upgrade to 9.04 from 8.10 so you're first step is to [upgrade to 8.10]("http://www.ubuntugeek.com/upgrade-ubuntu-804-hardy-heron-to-ubuntu-810-intrepid-ibix.html").

Now that you're running 8.10 you can [update to 9.04]("http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/upgrading").

**Note**: Make sure to read the *Before You Start* section on the last link.

### Post by ssdt on 2009-05-01
Just go to the update manager from the top menu: System>>Administration>>Update Manager and the new version will be listed for you to install.
