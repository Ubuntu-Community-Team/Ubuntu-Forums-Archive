### Post by 3rdalbum on 2009-04-18
Modern processors can slow down to save power when they are not being taxed hard, and then increase speed when it is needed. This is a feature, not a bug.

The easiest way to turn it off is in your BIOS - AMD calls it "Cool and Quiet". Turn that off and the CPU will run at full steam all the time.
