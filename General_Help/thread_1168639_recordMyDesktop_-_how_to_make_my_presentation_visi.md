### Post by Vaphell on 2009-05-24
you watch resized clip in native 640x480 resolution? try maximize/fullscreen and see if detail is good enough. It will be blurry but things should be definitely recognizable.

btw if you want to avoid stretching use exactly the same aspect ratio - 1280x1024 is 5:4, while 640x480 is 4:3


if you want to record stuff for some tutorials for other people you can use compiz zoom in - SUPER+1 (normal view), 2 (x2?), 3 (x4?)
things will be much bigger and will be clear even in much smaller resolutions
