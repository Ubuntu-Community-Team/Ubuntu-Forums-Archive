### Post by Mark Phelps on 2010-06-03
If you're asking about ATI fglrx drivers for your card -- there are NONE that will work with that card and recent versions of Ubuntu.

The only working video driver is the open source driver -- and it is installed by default during setup.

If you're seeing a graphical desktop, you have those drivers loaded.  There is nothing else you can load.
