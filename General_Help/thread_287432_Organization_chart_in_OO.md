### Post by Blutack on 2006-10-28
I did it with the standard drawing tools.  View --> Toolbars --> Drawing and then one of the buttons along the bottom gives you lovely flowchart boxes, which are great for doing organisation charts with.  Just click on them to edit the text.  You can connect them up with lines as well.  Hope this helps.
