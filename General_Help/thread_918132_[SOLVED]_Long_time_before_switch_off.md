### Post by alzie on 2008-09-13
I had exactly the same problem.  I had turned off the power manager thinking I didn't need it on a desktop.  When you shut down Ubuntu looks for the power manager (for 3 or 4 minutes) before it gives up and shuts down.

The link **terry_gardener** provided should solve your problem.
