### Post by Domhnull on 2006-05-01
Just in case any goes searching through the forums and wants an answer to this sort of problem - it was caused by defective RAM. I'd installed two new modules a couple  days ago and they were working fine at first.  Running memtest showed many errors.  I swapped them back out with my old modules and the system was fine.
