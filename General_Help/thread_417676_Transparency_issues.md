### Post by Toxicity999 on 2007-04-21
Are you talking about just the normal kcontrol (fake) transparency? Fake transparency will only show the desktop through the borders, and such, and only update very second or so if you were using that, _that_ is why it might of seemed choppy, it's actually not dependent on any form of 3d rendering and should work on almost any system. If you meant compositing (Real transparency, seeing to windows below it and such) We'll have to go into it more. It's possible that you are confusing the two and in fact are just using a border that has fake transparency, so playing with kcontrol wouldn't do much, short of modifying the theme.
