### Post by kaibob on 2008-08-18
> **EnGorDiaz said:**
> i just want to know where to find nautilus extensions i have been looking around but i cant find any at all

Open Synaptic and do a search on nautilus extension. You will find nautilus actions, nautilus image converter, nautilus open terminal, and nautilus share. I don't believe there are all that many nautilus extensions, which is unfortunate. However, there are a great many nautilus scripts, which often do the same thing:

[http://g-scripts.sourceforge.net/](http://g-scripts.sourceforge.net/)
