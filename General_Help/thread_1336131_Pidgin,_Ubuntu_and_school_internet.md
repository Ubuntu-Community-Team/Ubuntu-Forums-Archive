### Post by Darael on 2009-11-24
Can you get onto the internet in a browser from within school?  If not, ask your sysadmin (nicely!) for details of the proxy - many schools use a forced proxy to enforce internet content filtering.  Once you get that information, try putting it into system->preferences->network proxy, and applying it system-wide.  You may also want to look at your IM client's proxy settings and make sure that it's using the system settings.

If it's not that your apps aren't accessing the proxy, then I'm afraid I have no idea.

Edit: just to clarify - sometimes these things are set up so that Windows can autoconfigure to the proxy, but other ystems don't properly.
