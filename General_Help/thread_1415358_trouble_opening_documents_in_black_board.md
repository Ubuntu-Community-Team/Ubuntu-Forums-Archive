I understand a bit more now.  Blackboard is similar to eCollege which is what my school uses.  I doubt to problem lies in Blackboard, rather it's a problem with the document.

I know that I will occasionally find that some assignment documents are in .docx format, and while they *will* open in OpenOffice, there is usually a good deal of formatting errors and objects which will not convert.  Most of my professors have a strict .doc/.odt policy, where they will only accept those file formats.  However...  **There's always that one...**
