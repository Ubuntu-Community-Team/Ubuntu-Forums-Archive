### Post by Modax42 on 2008-10-14
I recently enabled my 3D cube desktop environment with Compiz, and added images for the skydome, and for the top face of the cube.  Strangely, though, there doesn't seem to be any obvious way to change the bottom face.  The 'cube caps' area of the CompizConfig settings manager says only 'list of PNG and SVG files that should be rendered on top face of cube'

Right now, my cube has a solid beige bottom, which is kind of an eyesore.

Any way to change this?

---

### Post by schauerlich on 2008-10-14
A lot of ccsm's options are under little collapsable tabs. The option to select a file for the bottom cube cap should be on the same page as the one for the top of the cube.

---

### Post by Modax42 on 2008-10-14
Nevermind.  Solved it.  The menu options under 'Desktop cube' under 'desktop' doesn't have the options I need, so I went to 'cube caps' under 'utility' and that has options to enable images for top and bottom.
