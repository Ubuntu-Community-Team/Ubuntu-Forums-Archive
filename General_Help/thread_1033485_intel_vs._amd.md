### Post by blazemore on 2009-01-07
Sorry, but you're getting confused.

The two versions to which you're referring are:

32 bit: i386
64 bit: AMD64

It's called AMD64 because AMD invented it (citation needed?) and they called it that.
It's called i386 (intel 386) because Intel invented it and they called it that.

All 64 bit processors are AMD64
