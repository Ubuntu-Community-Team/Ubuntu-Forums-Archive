### Post by RayH on 2006-10-19
Yes, click on System --> Administration --> System Monitor and see what your CPU and memory usage are reporting.  I doubt it's memory either, but maybe a service is maxing out CPU.  Another person mentioned the hard drive, is the light for the hd flashing constantly on the front of the PC?  

If it's not easily detectable, you could go the hardware route to isolate it, primarily by disabling/removing the network, sound card, usb and printer ports physically or via bios, and then reboot.  If the delay goes away, then re-introduce each component back in one by one until the problem returns.
