### Post by grahammechanical on 2011-06-16
May I suggest that you un-install any of the programs that your partner will not be using. As for truetype fonts, if the font is copied onto the Ubuntu machine, then you can right click on the font and select Open with Font Viewer, then click the Install Font Button and the font will then be available to your applications such as Libreoffice in the way other fonts are.

Regards.
