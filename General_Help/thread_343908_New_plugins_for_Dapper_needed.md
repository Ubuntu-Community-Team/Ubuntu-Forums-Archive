It seems it is quite possible to paint yourself out of mplayer by clicking the wrong boxes in mplayer config.  Unless you can find some app that re-loads mplayer, you won't get into config to click your way to re-enable things that shouldn't have been touched.  :P

Fortunately, nano to the rescue: I reenabled every "enable-" type in ~/.mplayer/mplayerplug-in.conf and was eventually able to load a video clip in firefox so I could right click the mplayer screen and restore my settings.

Note to self: never enable the direct playing without caching feature!
