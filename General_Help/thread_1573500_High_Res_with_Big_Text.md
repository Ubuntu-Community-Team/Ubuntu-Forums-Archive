### Post by robsoles on 2010-09-13
Can't help with an across the board solution to make all text bigger on your display but next time you wish the text was bigger in firefox try pressing and holding the [CTRL] key while scrolling your mousewheel with your pointer inside the firefox window - many good programs have a pseudo zoom feature that increases the size of everything it is displaying and re-lays it out to fit in it's window.

It is probable that an item added to /etc/X11/xorg.conf can set a minimum font size or simile.
