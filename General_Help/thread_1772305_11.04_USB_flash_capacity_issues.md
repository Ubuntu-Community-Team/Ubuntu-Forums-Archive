### Post by TeoBigusGeekus on 2011-05-31
Unlike windows, when linux deletes something from a flash drive, it sends it to the trash folder which is situated in the flash drive itself.
After you delete something from an external hd/pen drive, empty the trash as well, in order to reclaim your space.
Alternatively, press ctrl+h to see the hidden folders; you should see a folder named .Trash (or something); delete it and you should be fine.
