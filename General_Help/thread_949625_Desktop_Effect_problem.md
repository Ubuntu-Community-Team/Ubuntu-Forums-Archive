### Post by Afkpuz on 2008-10-21
Sorry for not responding sooner.

Compiz must be turned on.



Right click on desktop and select "Change desktop Background".  Select the "Visual Effects" tab and click normal or extra.  Then, you should be able to go into the settings manager and change things to your liking.  

Also, whenever you turn off compiz (same way) then back on, it saves your settings, but not which plugins are turned on.  So, you must go through and check the boxes to turn your favs back on.

Hope that helps.
