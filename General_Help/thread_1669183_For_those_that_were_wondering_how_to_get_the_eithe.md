### Post by Krazie_kid on 2011-01-17
Ok, I was surfing google, and came across a lot of topics of how to get the eithernet cable to work from my computer to my xbox... Other computer... PS3...Ect... Here is all you have to do. 

1) Take the eithernet cable and plug it into both machines.
2) Open up your wifi connections then go to VPN connections, click on configure VPN
3) Once you have that open, go to wired and edit
4) click on IPv4 tab and click on method: shared to other computers
5) Click on the check box in that tab as well
6) click on IPv6 and make sure it says ignore (Make sure the clicky box on the bottom is checked)
7) Click apply and test it, if that does not work, PM me on here and I will try to help you.

Every topic I looked up was saying something about bridging, you do not have to do any sudo or anything with this, it is just this simple, I hope this helped.
