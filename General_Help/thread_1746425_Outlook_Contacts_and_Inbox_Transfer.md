### Post by cymbaline42 on 2011-05-01
Use Mozilla Thunderbird ([https://www.mozillamessaging.com/en-US/thunderbird/](https://www.mozillamessaging.com/en-US/thunderbird/))

When installing Thunderbird in your Windows system, it will ask you if you want to import your Outlook email and contacts, say [B]Yes.

[/B]Now, use the instructions on this page ([http://kb.mozillazine.org/Moving_your_profile_folder)]("http://kb.mozillazine.org/Moving_your_profile_folder") to migrate your Thunderbird profile (emails, contacts) from your Windows computer to the Linux one.

Install Thunderbird on your Linux system, and import your profile and you're good to go.

Hope this helped.  Cheers.
