### Post by deadflowr on 2018-05-01
No need to upgrade anytime soon, and the updater will not notify you of the new release until sometime around late July/early August. 
(The option will come when the first point release (18.04.1) comes out)
And even then, if the current system is running fine, I'd recommend prolonging that even further down the line.

### Post by PaulW2U on 2018-05-01
Hi jgw, from the [Release Notes]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/BionicBeaver/ReleaseNotes")
> Upgrading from Ubuntu 16.04 LTS or 17.10

Upgrades from 17.10 will not be enabled until a few days after 18.04's release. Upgrades from 16.04 LTS will not be enabled until a few days after the 18.04.1 release expected in late July.
This will give the developers a little more time to fix the many bugs that will have been reported since Bionic's release and fully test the upgrade process. Going by my abandoned attempt to upgrade one of my laptops from 16.04 it's probably worth waiting a few months.
