### Post by bapoumba on 2007-10-21
To copy from the terminal or paste in the terminal, please use <shift><ctrl><c> et <shift><ctrl><v>. 
In the terminal, <ctrl><c> has a special meaning (stop the current command or process), and mouse actions are not all available.
