### Post by Taxman415a on 2008-09-22
I didn't realize you were referring to something specific when you said screenlets. So I don't know if that weather screenlet offers what you want, but you'd have to ask the screenlet's developer if you wanted to know for sure. [http://screenlets.org/index.php/Home](http://screenlets.org/index.php/Home) seems to be the place to go for them.

But if use the regular Gnome panel's weather applet, it definitely works for Canadian weather. Go to the top panel, right click and select add to panel and scroll down to the weather report applet. Add that then right click on it to set your preferences, including location.
