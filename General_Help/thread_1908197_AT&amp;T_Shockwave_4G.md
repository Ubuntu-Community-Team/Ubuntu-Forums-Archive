### Post by Cpierce on 2012-01-13
I got it. The Shockwave needed to be activated, and for some reason it wouldn't work on the USB ports on the front of the computer. I have a triple boot and it was the same for Mac and Win7. 

Once I started Ubuntu and plugged it in a back USB port, it started working right away.
