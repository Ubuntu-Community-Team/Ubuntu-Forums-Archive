### Post by ruzekle on 2015-08-04
Go to your settings and then your sound settings or right click on your sound icon and then go to sound prefences or settings. Check your hardware tab and see if there are any abnormalities. Hardware in your headphone jack my be on by default, and you may have to manually switch between headphone and board output.
