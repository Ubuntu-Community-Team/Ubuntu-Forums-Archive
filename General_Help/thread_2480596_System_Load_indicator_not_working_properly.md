### Post by deadflowr on 2022-11-05
The only real similarities between indicator-multiload and conky is they can both output system resource information.
But the similarities kind of end there.

Conky is a beast that can output anything you want about your system, and even somethings outside of that.
All in a variety of fun ways if you want.
In fact conky can run without showing you any system information.

So, in short, not the same. Not by a long shot.

That said, looks like it's probably a common problem with indicator-multiload  as posted over on askubuntu here: 
[https://askubuntu.com/questions/1409960/app-indicator-top-bar-flickering-22-04]("https://askubuntu.com/questions/1409960/app-indicator-top-bar-flickering-22-04")
Not sure what the fix is, if any.
