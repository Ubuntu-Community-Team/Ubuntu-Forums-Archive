### Post by Abu Azzubair on 2010-06-29
After I seen the mp3 link that I wanted to download

I hollowed over the link, right-click, Copy Link Location

Then

I used the short cut to get to Terminal - ctrl+alt+T

In Terminal, I typed: wget

Then I pressed the "Space" bar

Then right-clich, paste

Then I pressed "Enter"

(It downloaded in about 5 seconds)

I typed "exit", then I pressed "Enter"

Then

I clicked on on "Places" on the top tool bar (the main tool bar), then "Home Folder"

And I found my mp3 file that I wanted to download, and now I'm listening to it O:)
