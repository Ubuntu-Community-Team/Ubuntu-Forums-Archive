### Post by rl7467 on 2009-01-02
All of my incoming email is being put in the junk folder. (At first, I thought it was not coming through, but I just found it in the junk folder.) I do not have any active message filters, so I have no idea what is causing this redirection. When I installed Ubuntu, Evolution email worked fine (new messages went to my inbox). The redirection to the junk folder started after I installed openssh in an effort to get two Ubuntu computers to connect with each other. I have removed openssh and the problem persists.

Follow-up: I evidently fixed whatever the problem was when I selected all messages in the junk folder and marked them not junk. They moved to the inbox and Evolution resumed putting all new incoming messages in the inbox. I left the message here in case some other newbie has the same problem.
