### Post by FluffyElmo on 2008-09-03
If you're ripping video from DVDs try Handbrake [http://handbrake.fr/]("http://handbrake.fr/")

For the light video editing I do I like Avidemux [http://fixounet.free.fr/avidemux/]("http://fixounet.free.fr/avidemux/"). It's focused more on encoding video into different formats but it has enough basic video editing features to satisfy me.

Overall though I think you'll find video editing is one area Ubuntu is rather weak in. You'll find a lot of smaller programs, some very good at what they do, but not a lot of full featured packages.
