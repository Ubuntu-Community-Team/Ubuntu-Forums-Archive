### Post by Seb71 on 2012-06-27
If you are searching for hinting, sub-pixel order and DPI settings, those were removed from Ubuntu since the switch to Unity.

You can still adjust those in Xubuntu 12.04.

### Post by coffeecat on 2012-06-27
