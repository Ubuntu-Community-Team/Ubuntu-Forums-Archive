### Post by asmoore82 on 2010-11-22
This is puzzling.

But I am thinking that you can initially log in successfully because at that point the computer has "vanilla" keyboard settings. After you log in, your personal keyboard preferences are loaded and your password no longer works because it is mangled somehow by these settings.

So, I would take a peak in "System -> Preferences -> Keyboard" and the "Layouts" Tab.
Try typing your password in the test are and seeing how those settings affect it.
