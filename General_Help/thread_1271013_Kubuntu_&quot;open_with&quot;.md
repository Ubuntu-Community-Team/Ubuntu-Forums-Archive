### Post by munkeh on 2009-09-20
Seeing as you have had no replies within 7 hours, I thought I would say what has worked with me although I am not confident it will help you (me still new) and it's a GUI Gnome solution;

Right click on the file of the type you want to set rules for.
Go to >Properties. Then the "Open with" tab. There's a few programs in that list or if you go to the "+Add" box below there's even more or even the option to use a custom command.

If this is no help -sorry and I hope someone helps you get it sorted.
