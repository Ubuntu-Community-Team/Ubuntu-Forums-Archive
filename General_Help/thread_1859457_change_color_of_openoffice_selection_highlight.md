### Post by crdlb on 2011-10-13
In LibreOffice 3.4.3, there is a Transparency option in Tools->Options->LibreOffice->View->Selection. If you uncheck that, the selection will be a simple color inversion. I don't know if that is in OpenOffice 3.2 or not.

Edit: It is in OpenOffice.org 3.2.
