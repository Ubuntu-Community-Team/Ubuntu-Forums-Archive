Anytime I plug in my external drive I get a shortcut on my desktop while it's mounted, is there any way to turn this off?

### Post by CatKiller on 2009-08-02
Alt-F2. Run **gconf-editor**. Uncheck the /apps/nautilus/desktop/volumes_visible key. This will also mean that you won't get an icon on the desktop for CDs and the like.
