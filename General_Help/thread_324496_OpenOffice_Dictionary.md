### Post by shane2peru on 2006-12-23
What kind of output did you get with the Wizard?  Also if you are on dial up you need to be connected to the internet to get it. After that you will need to open a document called **DicOOo-1.6.2.swx**  After that you can just double click that document and Open Office will open it, then follow the instructions. ### Post by veli on 2006-12-23
what kind of a dictionary? Do you need the spell checking function in Open Office?

If so, go in Synaptic and search for myspell packages. Just selected the language you want, install it, and Open Office will check the spelling when you type.
