How to launch Skype at startup?

---

### Post by ebash on 2007-12-22
Add it to the start up programs in the Sessions dialog. First bring the session dialog: Main menu > System > Preferences > Sessions. Then select the "Startup Programs" and click the "Add" button, this will bring a small dialog that will let you add a program. In the "Name" field put whatever you want and in the "Command" field type in skype or the full path to skype.
