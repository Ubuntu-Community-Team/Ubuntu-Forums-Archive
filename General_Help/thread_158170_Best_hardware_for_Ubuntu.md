Hi I've been searching the forum for the best supported hardware for Ubuntu.

Please Contribute.


I own an Acer Aspire 1652 WLMi.

Even though Ubuntu (breezy) is the best distro for this model, It does not work out of the box. Installation requires kernel parameters and postinstall tweaking of configuration files. The steps for this can be found elsewhere in the forum.

Sound, touchpad, wireless (without wep), ethernet, most of the fn keys work.
Battery and suspend to disk (hibernation) does not. I haven't bothered with the tweaks for this.

Drains the battery very fast and dies without warning. Cpu fan works at full blast most of the time.
 
Because of these I only dare use ubuntu at home connected to AC power.

I've installed Dapper flight 6 by upgrading from breezy. Now xserver refuses to start.
