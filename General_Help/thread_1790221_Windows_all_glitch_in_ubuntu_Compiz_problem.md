### Post by Daedal on 2011-06-24
open up CCSM (CompizConfig Settings Manager) click where it says Preferences towards the bottom left of the window. 
there's a place to choose your profile and under it there's a button that says reset to defaults.  click that button and you should be ok. 
if not change the profile to unity then click the reset to defaults button.
