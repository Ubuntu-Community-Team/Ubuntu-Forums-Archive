### Post by aysiu on 2005-11-22
It's kind of hard to tell just by looking at the specs.
There are several methods for figuring out how well things will work:

1. Look at [the Ubuntu hardware compatibility page](https://wiki.ubuntu.com//HardwareSupport)
2. Do a Google search for *nameofsystem linux*
3. Find the system in a store and pop a live CD in to see how it goes.

I'd recommend #3 if possible, but Dell is usually mail order.
My eMachines computers work great with all flavors of Linux I've tried.
