### Post by Roasted on 2008-05-07
This is unacceptable. I'm going back to Gutsy.

edit - I just kicked my 3d settings to none, and now everything works. Something with compiz is f'n it up.

edit again - fixed it. The problem was I had "move windows" selected. Under your control manager for compiz, at the very bottom it's located there. Uncheck it by hitting the space bar when it's highlighted. It was somehow set so if it was checked, it was enabled 247, so instead of being able to click on functions, ALL I could do was click once and hold to move the windows around. That's it.
