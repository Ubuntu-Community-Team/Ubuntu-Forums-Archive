### Post by JGJones on 2005-09-29
Kino cannot capture video's from USB webcam - it only take inputs from DV devices.

There is really no video editor the likes of Windows Movie Maker - they tend to be high end applications.

But all is not lost...

There are two projects which aims to be like Movie Maker (or better, iMovie in OSX) and they are...<cue drumroll>

[PiTiVi/]("http://www.pitivi.org/")

[Diva]("http://diva.mdk.org.pl/about/")

PiTiVi is more ahead of Diva, but Diva sound the more interesting one.

Both are in early stages so it'll be a while before they are stable and of use.
