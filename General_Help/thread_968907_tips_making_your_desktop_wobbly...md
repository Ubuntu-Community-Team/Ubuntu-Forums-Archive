you want to make your desktop wobbly or something like this :-

[ATTACH]90906[/ATTACH]

[ATTACH]90907[/ATTACH]

cool eh??? :)

first go to System > Preferences > Appearance

then go to " visual effects " tab

and you will see something like this

[ATTACH]90908[/ATTACH]

then

tick the:-

" EXTRA " one

and just click keep settings and you're DONE!!!!!!!!!!!!!

now you have a wobbly desktop!
__________________________________________________________________________

Note:

If there is error saying something about your driver just go to

System > Administration > Hardware Drivers

[ATTACH]90912[/ATTACH]

as you see i already install my driver.

and now is your turn to install your driver. 
Just tick your Driver and it will start downloading, wait for it to finish and try the desktop setting again, it will work 100%.
