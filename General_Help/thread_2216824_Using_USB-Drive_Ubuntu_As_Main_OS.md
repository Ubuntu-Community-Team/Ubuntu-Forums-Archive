### Post by 3rdalbum on 2014-04-14
That all sounds okay. When you put thr laptop to sleep, use Suspend instead of Hibernate. It'll come back up quicker and put less wear and tear on the USB.

Before giving up on your data for good, see if you can replace the SATA cable in your laptop. See your other thread, I replied to it.
