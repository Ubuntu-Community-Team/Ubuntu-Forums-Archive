What's going on (?) because I called Qwest (my ISP) and the representative said that if I have a wireless router and the modem's internet access light is blinking constantly for a period of time, someone must be in my wireless network using my Internet if my computers are off and not on or connected. ### Post by Monicker on 2010-01-19
No.  If they are associated, and gave the proper wpa key to authenticate, they would be listed as a client on the router.  You could just be seeing activity from external hosts trying to conduct port scans and such.  The lights on my router blink even if all my computers are inactive.
