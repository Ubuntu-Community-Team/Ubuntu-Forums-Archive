### Post by bmac on 2008-05-26
I'm using an HP Officejet 5610 all in one printer that also prints photos. The unit uses HP56 & HP22 cartridges which are fairly inexpensive and are available in re-manufactured cartridges (Nukote @ approx. $10 to $15). As I don't utilize it to print photo quality pictures much I don't use the high color cartridge... I purchased the unit new for about $100.

Hope this helps.....
