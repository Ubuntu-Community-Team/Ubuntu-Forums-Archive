### Post by denham2010 on 2008-09-11
Hi Halibutt,

Create a folder to store your launchers and link it to your stacks applet.

Open your file manager and navigate to /usr/share/applications

Find the launchers you want and copy them to the folder you have linked to the stacks applet.

Cheers.
