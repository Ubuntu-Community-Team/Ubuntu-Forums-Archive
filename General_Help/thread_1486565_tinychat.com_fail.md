### Post by Trek1701 on 2010-06-07
Hi altjx,

You need to allow tinychat in flash settings.
Go to:
[http://www.macromedia.com/support/documentation/en/flashplayer/help/settings_manager06.html](http://www.macromedia.com/support/documentation/en/flashplayer/help/settings_manager06.html)
Scroll down until you find tinychat.com, select it and set it to always allowed.
Go to tinychat and your webcam/mic should be working.

Trek1701
