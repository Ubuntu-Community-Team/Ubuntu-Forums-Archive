### Post by NT4usB on 2007-11-06
Bought a couple 500 GB drives on sale for 95.00 ea. Thought I got a bargain...
Moved 300 gigs of stuff to one of them.
Next time I go to use it, I get an error filesystem corrupted, bad superblock, do not pass go, do not collect 200.00.
Drive wouldn't mount. What to do? I didn't know, but Feisty did. 
When I booted, it prompted I should run e2fsck -b32768. 
After closing my eyes and answering y about 500 times, then e2fsck -j, I have my files back.
I'm a happy guy now...
