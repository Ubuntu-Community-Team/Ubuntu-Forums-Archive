### Post by greenpeace on 2012-11-01
With Unity just hit the SUPER (windows) key and start typing "displays" and it will appears as an application.  

From there it's straightforward to configure.

fyi - my laptop now recognises the additional screen when I plug it in, and automatically extends my desktop in the appropriate direction!
