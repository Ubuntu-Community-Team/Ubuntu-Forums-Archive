### Post by WorMzy on 2010-08-04
There is no such thing as a task bar in Ubuntu, what you're looking for is a gnome-panel applet, which I assume you've accidentally removed. Right-click on the panel and choose "Add to panel", then search for an applet called "Window List" and drag it on to the panel. You can then move it around by clicking and dragging the little dots to the left-hand side of the applet.
