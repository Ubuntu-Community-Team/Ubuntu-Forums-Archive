### Post by Telengard C64 on 2011-05-25
> **ajcurran16 said:**
> What does it mean when I download a torrent with Transmission and it gives me an error saying "unregistered torrent"?

Usually I see a message like that when a torrent has been removed from the [tracker](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Torrent_tracker). Without a tracker you're depending on [DHT](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Distributed_hash_table) or other methods to find [peers](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Terminology_of_BitTorrent#Peer) who can [seed](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Terminology_of_BitTorrent#Seeder) the torrent for you.

HTH
