### Post by Taxman415a on 2009-01-12
> **metalf8801 said:**
> So should I take it that there are no Mobile Internet Device Ubuntu MID pre installed on them?

Just because no one had a chance to answer doesn't mean that.

But I'm not sure there are any Intel MID devices that have been released yet. If there are try the links on this page: [http://www.ubuntu.com/products/mobile](http://www.ubuntu.com/products/mobile) and ask the people involved directly if there are any available.

If you don't specifically mean MIDs and you really mean Netbooks, then there are lots of those available with Ubuntu on them. Specifically the Dell and Sylvania models.

I don't know for sure if Canonical gets any money directly out of it if you buy a device with Ubuntu MID edition or Netbook remix, but it would certainly help them indirectly by showing demand. I imagine they get at least some direct revenue though, at least from trademark licensing. The reviews I read for Ubuntu Netbook remix is that it is pretty much the best preinstalled Linux you can get on a netbook.
