### Post by deadflowr on 2019-01-18
Several factors can explain why it isn't showing when copying from external to internal.
File size, anything small will copy too fast for the notifier to even startup.
Different disks, one disk might be slower at writing than the other disk.
And probably other factors as well, but those are two I can think of off the top.


Also, system monitor has a subsection tab (marked file systems) that shows disk(s) space usage.
