### Post by irwand on 2010-05-17
dvd-slideshow is best if you just want a straight slideshow.
Use dir2slideshow (comes with it), and it'll generate the script for you to start editing.
I recently made dvd-slideshow-editor, a gui frontend for dvd-slideshow script:
[http://code.google.com/p/dvd-slideshow-editor/](http://code.google.com/p/dvd-slideshow-editor/)
So you can add kenburns or crop easily to all your pictures.
Once you're done, just invoke dvd-slideshow on your script, and you're done.

kdenlive and cinelerra are great video editors. They can generate picture slideshows as well, but since they're full-blown video editor which is a lot more powerful, it'll take you a lot longer than using dvd-slideshow for this purpose.
