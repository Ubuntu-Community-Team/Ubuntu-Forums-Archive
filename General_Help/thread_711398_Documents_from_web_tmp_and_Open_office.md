### Post by pjlehtim on 2008-02-29
This is kind of serious problem to new Linux users:
When opening a file directly from web browser (for example a document attatched to gmail) it is saved to /tmp. My friend (who isn't used to Linux terms yet) didn't understand that the file will be destoyed automatically when the machine is shut down. She lost many hours of work simply by using defaults provided to her. I don't know where this should be fixed? To Firefox or to Open Office? Open Office at least should tell people when saving a document to /tmp that it will be lost. 

Just to warn other people not to fall into same trap... Hope people in Ubuntu could do something about this.
