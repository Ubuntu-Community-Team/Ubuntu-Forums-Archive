### Post by rmjb on 2006-10-28
I assume that when you mean extensions you're referring to web search engines. Unfortunately, deskbar hasn't yet been upgraded to handle Firefox's new search engine definitions.

You can see more here:
[https://launchpad.net/distros/ubuntu/+source/deskbar-applet/+bug/63372](https://launchpad.net/distros/ubuntu/+source/deskbar-applet/+bug/63372)

And there you can see the link to deskbar's own "bug" on it and the progress.
