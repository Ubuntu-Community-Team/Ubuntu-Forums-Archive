### Post by xkalibur84 on 2008-11-07
I feel bad asking for help as I'm pretty good at searching for my answers.  In fact, I've fixed most of my problems just by doing searches for them here and/or on Google, but this one I can't seem to find an answer to.

The problem I'm having is that I'm not getting any sound from Pidgin when I send messages.  When people sign on, sign off, send me messages, I get sound.  It's only when I'm sending my message that the sound doesn't play.  Yes, that sound is enabled in the Preferences menu and yes the sound works when I press "preview".

I've tried the aplay %s thing, but I still get no sound when I send messages.  Any thoughts?


***EDIT***
God I'm such an idiot...I managed to fix my problem.  I completely looked over the option "Sounds when conversation has focus".  All this time I thought it meant it would ONLY play sounds when the IM box was in focus when it really meant it would play sounds when the IM box was in focus as well as being out of focus.
