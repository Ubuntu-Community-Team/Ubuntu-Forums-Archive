### Post by BkkBonanza on 2009-12-02
There is a program I used once. It was called PhotoRec. You won't be able to get anything that was written over but with software like this it looks at the data and identifies file signatures. I managed to recover a bunch but how well it will work for you depends on how contiguous the files were originally and how well it can identify and link together that space.

It's freeware released under GNU. [http://www.cgsecurity.org/wiki/PhotoRec](http://www.cgsecurity.org/wiki/PhotoRec)

I do remember another one as well but can't remember the name now. Sorry.
(edit: Ah yes, just looked at the site, it was TestDisk I recall using)
