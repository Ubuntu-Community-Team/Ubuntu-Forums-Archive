### Post by r-senior on 2011-05-19
> **Beowolf64 said:**

Don't know.

> Bonus question. Where is the control panel? Back in maverick days I had this little button that would show all administration and preference related buttons in one window.
Click on the power off button, top-right corner. The last menu option is "System Settings". An odd place to put it (same as Gnome Shell in fact), but there you go.

You can also find it in the Dash. Search for "Control", "System" or "Settings" and "System Settings" will appear in the list.
