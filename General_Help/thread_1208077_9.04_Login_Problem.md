### Post by merlinus on 2009-07-08
Select recovery mode at the opening menu, and then drop to a CLI (last menu choice when system is up-and-running).

```
passwd username
```will allow you to change your password.  It would seem that your username is whatever you entered when you first booted up after getting the machine.
