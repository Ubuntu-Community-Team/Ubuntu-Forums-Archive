### Post by soro2005 on 2007-11-06
> Bonus question: Why is the "synchronize now" button greyed out in the time settings?
Looks like you have to change it to "manual" for the "synchronize now" button to kick in. In other terms: If it's on "Keep synchronized..." it seems to assume that you're not supposed to mess with it by manually synch'ing. Or something like that.

Now, it seems that taking the manual path once is a good idea. There seems to be a bug with a missing dependency. If you try to manually synch, it's apparently going to prompt you whether you want to install the missing package.

Just reporting what I've found out. Mine synch's okay.
