### Post by Queue29 on 2010-05-11
The easiest thing to do would be to use a livecd on the computer with Windows7, plug in your harddrive through the usb adapter, and copy the important files onto another usb drive (formatted to FAT, of course).

Otherwise you'll have to find and install ext3 drivers for Windows. (bleh)

If the laptop boots, then it's time for a new battery.
