### Post by Therion on 2008-04-11
Open SuSE is a Linux-based operating system, but it is NOT the same thing as Ubuntu; which is also a Linux based operating system.  I'm not saying you won't like Open SuSE, I'm just saying that Open SuSE and Ubuntu are different operating systems.

Some pictures to illustrate:  [Open SuSE](http://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/c/c2/OpenSUSE_10.png/750px-OpenSUSE_10.png) ... [Ubuntu](http://www.michaeldolan.com/Images/scrnshots/Ubuntu_Gutsy_Gibbon_1.jpg).
