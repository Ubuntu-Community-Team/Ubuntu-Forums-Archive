### Post by russell.h on 2007-02-09
VNC isn't really used for file transfer (I'm not even sure if it can be, I always had to email stuff back and forth). Its just used to view the screen. I think you can configure some clients or servers to not automatically give you control of their mouse.

Another one I've used is FreeNX, which I preferred to VNC for use, but was harder to set up. Again, it is designed more for active control than passive monitoring, and I'm not even sure if you can use it to view an open login... I would try VNC personally.
