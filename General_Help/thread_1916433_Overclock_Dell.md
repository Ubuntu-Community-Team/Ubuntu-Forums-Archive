### Post by Maximus559 on 2012-01-28
The short answer is no. For a complete explanation as to why, read [this]("http://forums.extremeoverclocking.com/t196767.html").

That doesn't mean you can't squeeze some more performance out of an aging system, though. Depending on your current configuration, you may be able to upgrade your CPU, RAM, and / or graphics card at very little cost. I'd recommend searching around tech forums such as those at Tom's Hardware and Anandtech to see what people have been able to achieve with systems like yours. Good luck :D
