### Post by marshag63 on 2011-03-05
Are your other laptops and Desktops running Linux as well?

I had a situation where my Belkin router would allow a "guest" to access the internet, but nothing else (two different passwords - one for "guest" and one to access the network) and if I used the "guest" password then there is no printing allowed, if I use the regular password - voila!, printing allowed.  Just something to keep in mind if your outer is capable of that.

Does the printer have its own IP address or ESSID, etc?

I only currently have a printer connected to a computer which I can print to wirelessly.
