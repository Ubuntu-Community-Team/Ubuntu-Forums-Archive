### Post by DeMus on 2011-03-19
Re-install Ubuntu.
At step 4 choose manual partitioning
Choose to erase all partitions
Then make a partition to hold the programs (in Windows it would be the C: drive). Here it is called /
Choose to make a /home partition
Continue installation
Use Google to find more info about manual partitioning the drive when installing.
Success.
