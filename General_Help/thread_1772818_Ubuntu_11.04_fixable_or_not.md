### Post by Frantic_Earthling on 2011-06-01
I installed 11.04 + Unity on my Asus M70Vn (dual boot with Win7).

The driver for the on-board nVidia 9650M GT got installed without a hitch.
Everything worked out of the box, including function keys such as screen brightness, volume and sleep.:)

One big serious problem arose :( when I installed CCSM and tried to run the Cube. I almost entirely lost my GUI and was left only with an empty screen and a cursor. After much fidgeting, I re-installed 11.04 from scratch, stayed away from CCSM for the time being and have been feeling none the worse for it!

I think that people are offering solutions on how to use CCSM with Unity in this forum although I have not tried them yet.

Anyway, as far as I am concerned, 11.04 and Unity work fine. You may find that as some people say, Unity does sometimes require a few additional clicks to get to where to want to go, but you will quickly get used to shortcuts.
