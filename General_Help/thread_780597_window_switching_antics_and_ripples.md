### Post by damis648 on 2008-05-03
Ok, simply put, in synaptic install the package
```
compizconfig-settings-manager
```
and that is most of it.
Just go into System>Preferences>Advanced Desktop Effect Settings
and scroll down to "water" and check the box next to it. To see the ripples, hold down Ctrl and Super (the windows key) and move the mouse. As for the moving windows, go into "Animations" (the logo is a genie lamp) and go to the "Focus Animation" tab and double click the first item in the list shown. Where it says "Focus Effect", select "Dodge" and thats it! Just focus on another window to see that effect.
