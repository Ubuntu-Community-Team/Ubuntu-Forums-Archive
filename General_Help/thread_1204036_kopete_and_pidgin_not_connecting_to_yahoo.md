### Post by RD1 on 2009-07-05
If you've updated pidgin to resolve the recent connection issue, go into pidgin accounts ... remove your yaHoo account and recreate it. Even after upgrade, pidgin tends to retain old connection info.

At least, that's what fixed it for me. :p
