### Post by TBABill on 2010-08-06
hungerformore, one word of caution. The new Intel Core i3/5/7 chips require a more modern kernel than Ubuntu 10.04 uses. It uses 2.6.32-xx and you need 2.6.33 or later for that chip if the system uses the on-chip video processor as its video card (meaning it does not have a separate video card). Just something to be aware of when comparing performance/price.
