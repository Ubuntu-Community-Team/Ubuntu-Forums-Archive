may be a stupid question but my trash folder is no longer on the desktop. how do I get it back. 7.04

---

### Post by freakavoid on 2007-05-31
open gconf editor: alt+F2 -> gconf-editor.
go to apps -> nautilus -> desktop.
check the box for the trash_icon_visible key.
