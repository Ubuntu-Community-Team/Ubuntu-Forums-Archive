### Post by mcduck on 2011-01-06
> **realzippy said:**
> Great tool?
Really do not know why this "tool"made it's way into ubuntu.
Just opened mine,it suggests to remove
fakeroot and dkms.Great idea...:p

Like I said, it *is* a great tool. Much better than searching for all the stuff you want to remove by hand.

But you should know yourself what the packages are before you let it remove anything. It's not an automatic cleanup tool that you could just run blindly.

(in your case you probably haven't actually installed those packages, but instead have gotten them as dependencies for something else. Since you know you want to keep them, that shouldn't be a problem to you. Just uncheck them when yo use the tool. And you can also remove the "automatically installed"-mark from those packages in Synaptic, and the Computer Janitor should then leave them alone)
