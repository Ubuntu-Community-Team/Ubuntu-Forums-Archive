### Post by shewbox on 2005-12-22
I realize that most people on this forum probably know this already, but I was surprised how much faster my older NVIDIA Gforce FX 5200 runs than my newer ATI X800 XL does in Linux using the official drivers from each manufacturer.  With a 3ghz p4, 1.5 gigs of ram, and the ATI card installed, anything graphics intensive like video viewing, moving/resizing windows, and scrolling in webpages took up massive amounts of cpu and didn't run very smoothly.  After switching out the cards and installing the new drivers, those same things run twice as fast while using half the cpu power.  What a difference drivers make!  Maybe there are hacks or other drivers that improve performance in ATI cards, but if their standard drivers can't make an X800 XL outperform a Gforce FX 5200, they have some serious work to do in the driver department.
