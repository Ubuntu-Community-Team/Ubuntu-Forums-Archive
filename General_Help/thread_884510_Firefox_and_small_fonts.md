### Post by mali2297 on 2008-08-09
Have you tried disabling *Allow pages to choose their own fonts*?

The setting can be found in *Edit -> Preferences -> Content -> Fonts & Colors -> Advanced*. 

You can also try setting a minimum font size.

---

### Post by silkstone on 2008-08-09
+1 for that suggestion. You may not need to allow pages to choose their own fonts if you just set the minimum font size to something like 12. Also make sure you have the msttcorefonts package installed.
