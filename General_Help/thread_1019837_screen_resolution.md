### Post by ajgreeny on 2008-12-23
Using the figures for your own monitor/machine edit your xorg.conf file to show the info in the manner shown in my example below.
> Section "Monitor"
    Identifier    "Configured Monitor"
            HorizSync    30-80
            VertRefresh    50-75
EndSection

Section "Screen"
    Identifier    "Default Screen"
    Monitor        "Configured Monitor"
    Device        "Configured Video Device"
    DefaultDepth    24
    SubSection "Display"
        Modes        "1280x1024" "1280x960" "1152x864" "1024x768" "832x624" "800x600" "720x400" "640x480"
    EndSubSection
This worked for my setup, when intrepid got the resolution wrong, luckily I had a backup of xorg from dapper drake so used the appropriate info I needed from that, and BINGO!, it worked.
