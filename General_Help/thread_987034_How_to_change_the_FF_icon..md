### Post by eternalnewbee on 2008-11-19
Find the new icon you want. [www.gnomelook.org](www.gnomelook.org) is a good place to look, download it, and save it somewhere in your home directory. I have a folder called "icons" where I save them. Then **right-click** on the icon in the panel and choose **properties**. click on the icon, and navigate to the folder, and open. Tada...
Hope this helps.
