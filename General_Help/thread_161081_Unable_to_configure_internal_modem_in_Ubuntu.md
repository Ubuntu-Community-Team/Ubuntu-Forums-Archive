### Post by localzuk on 2006-04-16
You most likely can't - as most internal modems are 'winmodems' which means that they rely on the cpu to do the work - which only works with Windows as the manufacturers only create driver software for Windows.

Take a look at [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/DialupModemHowto](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/DialupModemHowto) for more information about modems. You may also want to take a look at [http://www.linmodems.org](http://www.linmodems.org) as this has lots of information about using winmodems in Linux.

This is not the fault of Ubuntu or Linux but instead the fault of cheap manufacturers only producing products that work with Windows.
