### Post by grey1beard on 2011-03-19
I have an Archos Vision A15VS mp3 player which I am recharging through the usb connection of my laptop.
Unfortunately, as far as I can see, there is no way of checking the battery state during the process as the screen of the player only shows a usb icon when the lead is connected.

This means that I don't have a visual confirmation that the battery is actually charging !

Obviously I could simply unmount the device, check the microscopic battery icon on the screen, and if not showing "full" repeat the process.

Is there a better way ? I'm thinking along the lines of a piece of software that would be the equivalent of a battery tester, but perhaps this is a "sledgehammer to crack a nut".
Any ideas ?

### Post by grey1beard on 2011-03-19
Problem temporarily solved, but I'm not sure how !!!

I switched it off while the usb connection was in place, and the display blanks. 
Removing the usb, then reconnecting, the usb icon appeared, then 4 seconds later changed to a battery symbol showing charging taking pace !
Never done this before !!

Hoping this is the solution, I'll mark this "Solved" until I find otherwise.
