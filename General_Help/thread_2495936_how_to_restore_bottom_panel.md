### Post by garyed on 2024-03-08
It looks like I jumped the gun after about thirty minutes searching the web & trying to find the answer. 
Right after I posted this I found that doing the same "alt+windows right click" on the top panel & clicking "new panel" would put the panel back on the bottom.
Then I went back to the bottom panel & did "alt+windows right click" then "add to panel" & then added "Window List" (switch between opn windows) & everything is back to normal.
