### Post by cottfcfan on 2011-04-08
Before you reinstall the drivers.
Click your sound icon, go into preferences, make sure all the sliders are at 100% or there abouts, and check the "connector" in "output" is set right.
 Play an mp3 while your messing with it, this helps.
Make sure the sound in applications isn't muted as well.
