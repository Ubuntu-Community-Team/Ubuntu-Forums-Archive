### Post by 3rdalbum on 2011-05-03
Two methods:

1. When you use Software Center to install K3B, at the bottom of the Software Center window you will be asked if you want to add K3B to the launcher. This only appears as you're installing K3B.

OR

2. Run K3b. Right-click its icon in the launcher and choose "Keep In Launcher".
