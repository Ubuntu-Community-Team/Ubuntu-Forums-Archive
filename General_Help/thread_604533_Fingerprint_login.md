### Post by Whoopie on 2007-11-07
Hi,

it's difficult to answer this question.

There are fingerprint readers from UPEK which are partially supported. -> [http://thinkfinger.sf.net](http://thinkfinger.sf.net)

There are AuthenTec fingerprint readers which could be working ([http://home.gna.org/aes2501/](http://home.gna.org/aes2501/)), but there's no PAM module at the moment to use  them for login.

And there's work in progress for some Microsoft readers.

Do you know the exact vendor for "your" fingerprint reader? That would help.
