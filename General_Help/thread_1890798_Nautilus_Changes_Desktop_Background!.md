### Post by cottfcfan on 2011-12-04
Every time I open up Nautilus as root from a terminal, my Desktop Background reverts back to Default (the purply one) instead of the blue planet I have chosen. I then have to logout and back in again, to get my customized Desktop back.
Does anyone else see this, & is there a workaround?

ps Using 11.10 64bit & Gnome-shell.

---

### Post by cottfcfan on 2011-12-04
After nearly 2 weeks Ive solved the problem within 30 mins of posting.
I needed "have file manager handle the desktop" enabled in advanced settings.
Problem solved.
