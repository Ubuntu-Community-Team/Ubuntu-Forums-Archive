### Post by daemonoid on 2007-06-19
vnc has a number of inherant problems like these. 

This is all of the top of my head and not all options are available in all viewers but:

First off, learn the refresh shortcut key for your viewer, you'll need it quite often to ensure you have an up to date page

Turn off compression in your viewer, jpg compression is great for bandwidth but really hurts picture quality.

Switch to high bandwidth / high quality mode in the viewer.

Hope this helps to some extent,
