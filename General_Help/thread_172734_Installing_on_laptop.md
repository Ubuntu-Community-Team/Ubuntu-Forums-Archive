### Post by MiniJames on 2006-05-20
Hey, I own an R50e and completely cleared windows from it (inc. the recovery partition). However, your laptop should still have it intact.

If you remove windows, install Ubuntu and then dont like it -- you can restore the laptop with windows by pressing the blue "Access IBM" button just up from the keyboard.

Also, if you ring the IBM tech support line -- you can request the disks from them and they will post them to your address free of charge. Although, you should only do this if you actualy have a problem that needs the disk ;)

Any questions, just PM me :) if you get Ubuntu Dapper Drake, there is full wireless support with nm-applet. Its fantastic. :)
