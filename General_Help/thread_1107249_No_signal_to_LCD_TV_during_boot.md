It finally starts displaying at the Ubuntu loading screen and works from there on.

I have tried hooking the tv up in several different configurations including DVI to VGA (current), s-video to s-video (doesn't work), and VGA to VGA (same as DVI to VGA).  I am considering trying to hook up my TV with a DVI to HDMI cable, but hesitate since I am reasonably satisfied as is.

Some of my hardware:

ASUS P4B266-C motherboard - BIOS Revision 1002

Nvidia Geforce3 Ti 200
Westinghouse SK-42H240S 42" LCD TV (connected DVI to VGA)
Acer 19" widescreen LCD monitor (connected VGA to VGA)
600W power supply and 1280 MB RAM
