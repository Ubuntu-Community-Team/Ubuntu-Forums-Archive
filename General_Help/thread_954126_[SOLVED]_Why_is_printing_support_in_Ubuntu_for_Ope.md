### Post by rmockler on 2008-10-20
I'm guessing that you haven't dug deeply enough into the art of printing envelopes of varying sizes using Open Office in Ubuntu.
For a quick look at the potential, open the Open Office Word Processor, in the tool bar at the top, click on Insert.  In the drop down menu, about two-thirds down is Envelope.  Click on it and then click on Format at the top.  Then in the bottom third of the screen under Size is Format with a drop down containing multiple envelope sizes.

### Post by clegends on 2008-10-21
Solved. Thanks for the above post, but that didn't do it for me. I've long been aware of the Envelope templates, but found it too restrictive for my uses. What I was after was paper sizes. It's possible this is a 'feature' with the latest release of Open Office... I haven't tried it on the latest with Mandriva or Puppy. In any event, when you go to File>Print>Properties>PaperSize you can select "Manual". This reads the paper format from your file, instead of the suggested template...lol.
