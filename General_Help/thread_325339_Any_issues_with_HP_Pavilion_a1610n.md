### Post by scott_g on 2008-02-23
Don't know if you've purchased this yet....  I have one of these; have had it a little over a year now.  Ubuntu seems to work fine, I upgraded the graphics card to an NVIDIA 7600GS before I installed Ubuntu and Compiz Fusion runs excellent.  I believe all the hardware is supported; all the memory readers and such work; it seems to recognize the special audio chipset, although I've only every used the one speaker output...  Overall I'm happy with it, serves my needs and is very fast with Ubuntu.
