### Post by reydempto on 2009-07-08
SOLUTION:

if anyone else has this problem here's your fix.

get to a computer with windows and iTunes. if you are a dual booter, even better :P 

open itunes, go to preferences and make sure that you turn off automatic syncing.

plug in your ipod to the usb connector. once itunes recognizes it, eject it. now go back to your ubuntu and plug it in! Voila! its just some flag that gets turned on if there's an error while syncing. and only itunes knows how to clear that error. once it recognizes the ipod in itunes, you'll see it syncing for a minute or so. that's itunes working its magic.

DONE.
