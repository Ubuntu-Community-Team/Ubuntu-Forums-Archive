### Post by ajgreeny on 2007-05-16
All the md5sum figures are here for all ubuntu iso downloads:-
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/UbuntuHashes](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/UbuntuHashes)
cd to the folder where your iso is and compare the figure shown at this site with the output of **md5sum name_of_file.iso** in terminal.  If they are the same you are good to go.
