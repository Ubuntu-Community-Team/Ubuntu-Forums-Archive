### Post by FahadMKS on 2010-08-19
To install the drivers you must be first connected to the Internet. then you may select SYSTEM >> ADMINISTRATION >> HARDWARE Drivers and then Ubuntu will search for the drivers by itself.

In most of the cases you will get the sound driver installed by its own, only the Nvidia driver will have to be installed.

Try this and let me know if it works.
