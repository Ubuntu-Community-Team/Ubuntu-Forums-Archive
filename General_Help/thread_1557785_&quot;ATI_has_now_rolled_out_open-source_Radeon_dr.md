If your video card is eligible for ATI drivers, you will be offered them in the Hardware app. Don't just go downloading and forcing a driver install.  That is a surefire way of trashing your display.

Unless you have one of the newer HD 3x/4x/5x cards, there are no new ATI drivers for your card.  For the older cards, the appropriate open-source driver is already installed by default.
