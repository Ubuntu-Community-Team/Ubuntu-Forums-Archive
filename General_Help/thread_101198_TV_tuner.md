### Post by emperor on 2005-12-09
[quote=Mr. Electric Wizard]There is a guy on here that is using a PVR-350 too... Not sure of the username but the avatar is animated .gif of a penguin typing on a keyboard.[/quote] 
That would be me!

The PVR150,250,350's will NOT work with TVtime or xawtv, not a standard v4l type driver. Mplayer or Xine can be used, but somewhat of a pain. So this series cards may not be a good choice for simple video game play. However, these cards are excellant for recording tv programming via MythTV. 

Check linuxtv.org for standard v4l compatible tv cards:
=========================================
See [http://www.linuxtv.org/v4lwiki/index.php/Main_Page]("http://www.linuxtv.org/v4lwiki/index.php/Main_Page")

I recommend the saa713x series cards. Get one with an external sound output that can plug into your sound card directly (outside the machine).
