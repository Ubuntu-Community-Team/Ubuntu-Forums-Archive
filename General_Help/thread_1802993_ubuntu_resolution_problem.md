### Post by seawolf167 on 2011-07-12
Welcome to the forums!

First thing to check - do you have the drivers installed for your video card? If you go to System -> Administration -> Hardware Drivers, you can install [any] available drivers for your devices.

On 11.04, click the power button in the upper right corner, select System Settings, then Additional Drivers.

Once you have any drivers installed, reboot and try readjusting your monitor resolution.
