### Post by aldenhg on 2007-05-23
As long as you don't install Ubuntu over your XP partition you'll be fine and the Ubuntu installer will set up a bootloader (grub) that will present you with a list when you start your computer. Make sure that when you get to the partitioning part of the installation that you shrink your XP partition (from the right) and then let the installer do the rest.
