### Post by Titan8990 on 2008-06-10
Download the alternate CD: [http://www.ubuntu.com/GetUbuntu/download](http://www.ubuntu.com/GetUbuntu/download).

Alternate cd is a checkbox on the bottom of that screen.


Install in "text mode" from the alternate CD. That should solve your problem but you still may need to change your xorg.conf file after installation. I had the same problem once on a BenQ monitor.
