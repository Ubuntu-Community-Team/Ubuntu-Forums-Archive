### Post by lovinglinux on 2009-11-07
Are you able to watch TV already? If not, then see [this](http://ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=5971890&postcount=2).

Once your TV card is properly configured, you could try my [FoxMediaCenter]("http://fmc.isgreat.org") extension for Firefox.

If you want a simple script to record TV, then check this:

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=921233](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=921233)

You can also use mencoder from commnad-line, VLC, Kaffeine...

If you want a full-featured Media Center, then take a look at [MythTV]("http://www.parker1.co.uk/mythtv_ubuntu.php").
