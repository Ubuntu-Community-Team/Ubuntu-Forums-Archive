### Post by Woody1987 on 2009-05-06
for the theme, you dont need debs, its really easy to do. Go to [www.gnome-look.org](www.gnome-look.org) and search for a theme you like. Download it to your desktop. Open up appearance preference and go to theme. Drag and drop the downloaded file into this window, or click install and select the downloaded file.
