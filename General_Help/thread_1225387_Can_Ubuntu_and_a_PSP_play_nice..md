### Post by HappyFeet on 2009-07-29
I have a PSP and it works flawlessly in ubuntu. I have no problem putting music or anything else on it.

[Handbrake]("http://handbrake.fr/?article=download") is a great app to convert videos to psp and ipod formats. (this app is made specifically for the psp and ipod) It takes like 2 clicks to convert a video. On the right side of the app, you will see "presets". Just choose PSP and go.

Sony products are generally linux friendly.
