### Post by Miles88 on 2011-07-05
I think Gantt Project or Zoho-Projects might be able to help you with that. They both have gantt chart features and have time lines for tasks. Basecamp would also be great since it's easy and simple to use but they just don't have gantt chart features.

My team uses Latitude which is a [project management software for surveyors]("http://www.latibiz.com/") and it works well. It has the features that satisfy our needs to manage projects, clients and documents. It can create quotes and invoices from the users timesheets and job costs.
