### Post by Rex Bouwense on 2013-02-11
Not a big problem.  Right click any open space on the lxPanel.  Choose Panel Settings and then Panel Applets.  Scroll down to Task Bar (Window List) and place a check mark in the box under stretch.  That should solve your problem.

### Post by Habermas on 2013-02-11
