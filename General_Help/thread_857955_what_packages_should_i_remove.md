### Post by WRDN on 2008-07-13
To start, turn off some of the unneeded services.
Go to System > Administration > Services and click Unlock, then enter the root password. Turn off anything here you don't need.
It's also worth disabling/ removing Tracker if its running.
As for programs you can remove, that entirely depends on what you use your computer for. For example, if you did mostly web surfing and no downloaded no torrents, then you could keep Firefox and remove Transmission, the included bittorrent client.
