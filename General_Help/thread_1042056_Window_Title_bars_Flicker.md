### Post by steeleyuk on 2009-01-17
This is a bug in the Human theme when running Compiz (or at least thats what I read).

Two options really, because I know it can be annoying:

1) Disable Compiz
2) Change to another theme (not based on Clearlooks)

Edit: Do you have an nVidia card? Some reports suggest it could be a bug in the nVidia driver.

Edit 2: Apparently nVidia driver version 180.06 or higher fixes this.
