### Post by MikeBenza on 2006-12-05
Cookies are entirely up to the browser, be it Firefox, Netscape, Opera, IE, etc.  You can turn them off in Firefox (Edit -> Preferences -> Privacy -> Cookies), but that will break almost every site you log into under the sun (almost).

A better idea would be installing something like [CookieSafe]("https://addons.mozilla.org/firefox/2497/").

- Mike
