### Post by grahammechanical on 2012-03-31
I have found some links for you that show what might be possible.

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/T101MT]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/T101MT")

[https://apps.ubuntu.com/cat/applications/xinput-calibrator/]("https://apps.ubuntu.com/cat/applications/xinput-calibrator/")

I am on 12.04 Beta 2 right now and there are installed libraries for touchscreen as standard. They are no good to me as I do not have a touchscreen. But it seems that work is being done on touchscreen use in Ubuntu.

The key to all of this will be the processor in the tablet and whether there is a Ubuntu ISO for that processor.
