### Post by KhurramM on 2009-05-29
U make your own software based on a GPL software.

U can sell it, ubuntu is sold also. 

;)

But GPL has to be preserved, its an International LAW. Its not compulsory to distribute it, but u have to put the GPL (C) into it, also if the person who gets it via u, demands source code, u have to give. It is forever the property of FSF.

BSD licences will have to undergo BSD rules, not GPL rules.

I hope its clear to u.

Carefully study:
[http://www.gnu.org/licenses](http://www.gnu.org/licenses)
