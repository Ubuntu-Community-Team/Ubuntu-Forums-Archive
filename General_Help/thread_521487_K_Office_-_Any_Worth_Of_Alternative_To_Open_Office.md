### Post by aysiu on 2007-08-09
KOffice and Gnome Office have flaky compatibility with Microsoft Office. Even though OpenOffice isn't 100% compatible with MS Office, it's the closest a Linux office suite has come. KOffice and Gnome Office have other things to offer, though (speed, for one).
