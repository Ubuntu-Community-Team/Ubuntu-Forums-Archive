### Post by Zorgoth on 2010-07-26
It depends on what you want.

What features do you want?

Price?

Graphics?

CPU?

Memory?

Weight?

Battery Life?

Usage Type?

I would seriously recommend getting a laptop with either Intel HD graphics included with i3,i5, and i7 processors or else with a fairly recent nvidia or ati card.  Other solutions may be unstable.  How powerful depends on what you do.

Apart from that, it all depends on what you are planning on doing with your computer and what you are willing to pay for it.

Personally, I have a Dell Studio 15 with an i7-720QM processor, 4 GB 1066 MHz memory, and an ATI 5470 graphics card and am very happy with my purchase.  It runs all my work applications and all my games perfectly.

Also; I would seriously recommend making sure you have a fairly high screen resolution (1600x900 or better); the difference is astonishing.  I have 1920x1080 and it is great.

Also, your thread title is very uninformative.
