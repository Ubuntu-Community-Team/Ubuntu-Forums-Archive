### Post by ajgreeny on 2008-08-27
There has been a thread about this very problem once before.
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=886526](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=886526)
I suggested renaming the hidden folder in your home folder from .mozilla to .mozillaold and restarting Firefox.  It worked that time and may well do so again.

I suspect the problem is due to add-ons or a profile problem of some sort, but have a look at the older thread to see the details of my solution then.
