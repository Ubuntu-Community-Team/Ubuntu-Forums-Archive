### Post by izod1991 on 2009-12-09
It's a bug with Songbird that doesn't allow it to display Compiz Fusion effects (ie Wobbly Windows, moving to another desktop, etc.) You can temporarily get around this by holding ALT + Click + Drag. When you hold ALT, it forces Compiz to use effects on the current window. As far as I know there is no current fix, but the Songbird dev team has been notified of the bug, so I guess they'll get it fixed as soon as they can:

[http://bugzilla.songbirdnest.com/show_bug.cgi?id=10053]("http://bugzilla.songbirdnest.com/show_bug.cgi?id=10053")
