### Post by viperdvman on 2011-10-09
Lots and lots of KDE packages are totally compatible with GNOME, Xfce, LXDE, etc. And some of the KDE apps like Konsole, K3b, etc. depend on a few KDE libraries, so it'll download those too.

Nothing to worry about. They won't affect GNOME in the least bit, as they are totally compatible, but might look a bit different since most KDE apps use the Qt toolkit rather than the GTK toolkit.
