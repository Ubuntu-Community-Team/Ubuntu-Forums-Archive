### Post by Jeroensum on 2008-09-28
I don't know what version of Spellforce you're running. The wine app database gives mixed results for different versions, check here:
[http://appdb.winehq.com/](http://appdb.winehq.com/)  and enter your game in the searchbox on the left.
It seems that SpellForce: The Order of Dawn version 1.52 should be playable, however that 1.52a is not.

Sidenote:
It's common knowledge that Wine often gives minor to major problems with games, it's not that wine is a magic app that automagically runs every and all windows games ;)  To be fair and all, the wine maintainers do a great job and more and more games are playable everyday.
