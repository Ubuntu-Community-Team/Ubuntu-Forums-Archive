### Post by mcduck on 2011-10-14
No, it's not "Gnome Classic" (Gnome 2) in literal or any other sense, it's as close as you can get with Gnome 3. The System menu isn't there because it doesn't exist in Gnome 3's fallback mode, which is what you are now using.

You'll find the settings in the menu on the top right corner, or alternatively in Applications/System tools/System Settings.
