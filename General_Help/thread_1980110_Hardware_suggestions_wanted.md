### Post by HiImTye on 2012-05-14
> counseling progress notes (libreoffice)

billing insurance companies (a web based application)
occaisional graphing with libreoffice
Print wirelessly to a HP Officejet Pro 8500
any decent machine should be able to do all of the above without issues (save maybe getting the HP printer drivers working and configured but that is not dependant on the machine itself)
> make voice recordings of some sessions (about an hour each) and store them on a hard drive
again, any decent machine will be able to do this assuming it has a hard drive with a reasonable amount of space (any modern laptop will do). I would get a gamer's headset if it is only your voice and noise cancellation is an issue (for better quality voice recording). again, not an issue with the machine itself
