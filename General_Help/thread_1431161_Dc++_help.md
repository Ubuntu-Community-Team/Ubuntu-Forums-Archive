### Post by DawieS on 2010-03-16
You will need to do port forwarding to use DC++ successfully to most of the host sites. Visit **[http://portforward.com](http://portforward.com)** to see how to do it on your specific router.

After adjusting the router, you can allow these 2 ports in your firewall (you don't have to disable it completely).

Now add the ports in DC++ (run in active mode, if I remember correctly), and you are good to go!
