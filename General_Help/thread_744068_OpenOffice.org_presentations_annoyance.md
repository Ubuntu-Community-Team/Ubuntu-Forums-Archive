### Post by Brucevdk on 2008-04-03
Technically it doesn't "take you back", the slide selection simply never changes. 

Also as far as I can tell you don't even have to exit the presentation. It's just another window so you should be able to alt-tab around without any problems.

Another workaround would be to not start the presentation but to use (Page Up/Page Down) to move trough the slides in the main interface.
