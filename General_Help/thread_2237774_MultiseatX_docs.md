### Post by T-W-X on 2014-08-03
Okay, just wanted to outline some of the problems that I've had with configuring MultiseatX (as far as I've gotten, not 100% done) and how they pertain to the documentation out there...

There's no true documentation for 14.04.  Docs for up to 13.04 exist but lightdm's complete personality change for 14.04 makes using docs for 13.04 and below useless.  I would really like to see more on this from the maintainers/community, as I really liked how it looked and how it didn't require a lot of dependencies.  Ended up having to use kdm to get it to work.

Would also like to see better documentation on Multiseat with multiple controllers, versus Multiseat with multiple heads on a single controller, versus a combination thereof.  I'm currently limited to two heads because of the two cards, even though both cards have at least two interfaces.  There was no mention of this in the documentation that I'd found for 13.04 and I'd have taken a different approach to getting this working if I'd known better.

I haven't yet gotten to fighting with audio yet, that'll be coming.  I've got two Sound Blaster Audigy cards with the "Live Drive" modules, the goal ultimately is to connect two displays on one card to my desk as seat0 with the integrated audio, then one of the displays on the second video card to my wife's desk with one of the Audigy sound cards, possibly through the optical-out to a receiver at her desk, and finally another video interface to the projector, with audio going over the second audigy's optical-out to the receiver for watcihng movies.
