### Post by Damanther on 2007-08-20
The resolution will really depend on your TV. Computer monitors in general have significantly higher resolution capabilities than a standard TV.

But, to answer your question. Lots of the Hauppage TV tuner cards work well with MythTV and others under ubuntu.

I have the WinTV PVR 500, which has 2 tuner inputs, I put my VCR on one for recording purposes, and hooked my cable TV -IN to the other for TV watching.

There are some good howtos in the ubuntu wiki under the Multimeda/Hauppage page. I don't have the link handy.
