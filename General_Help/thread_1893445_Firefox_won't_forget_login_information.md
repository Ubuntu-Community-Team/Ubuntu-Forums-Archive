### Post by cody1233 on 2011-12-10
In Firefox, you can go to the file menu then preferences, and go to the security tab, then click the checkbox next to "Remember Passwords for Sites".  You can clear your saved passwords by clicking on the button that says "Saved Passwords..." and then by clicking remove all.

### Post by lovinglinux on 2011-12-14

You don't need to remove all passwords, just the one for the domain in question. Just search, select and delete.

You also don't need to block all sites from saving passwords. Enable it, by ticking the option to "Remember passwords for sites", then click the Exceptions button on the right and add the Amex domain. It won't save AMEX login data, but will save other sites.
