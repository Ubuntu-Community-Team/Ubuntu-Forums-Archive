### Post by russo.mic on 2010-08-12
So,  Just upgraded my iPhone.  

I was concerned.  I must admit.  Steve Jobs has done a good job of making sure that I can only do with it what he wills.  

I nervously plugged my iPhone into the usb port,  then, my hand shook as I requested Rhythmbox open.  I witnessed the main window unfurl before my eyes, and saw the Russo's Iphone icon in the left window pane.  

I carefully clicked on a selection of my music collection, Michael Jackson, best of, as it were.  I SLOWLY drug it to my iPhone icon, my heart racing, my sweat dripping.  When I saw the copying status bar racing from left to right, I almost lost it.  

I couldn't believe it.  I had gotten this far.  I was terrified i would unplug my iPhone and all would be lost.  I was sure it was going to happen.  This was it.  My relationship with my trusty iPhone have to be rebuilt over a course of days, maybe even a few weeks.  It would have been a serious inconvenience. 

However, much to my shock and awe, I dismounted my trusty iphone, and hit the iPod icon.  And almost dropped it in suprise.

Michael Jackson, Best of, was in my album list.  Not only that, I realized later, it was originally FLAC, and rhythembox had made mp3s of it for me. 

Since i've used the iPhone sway to convert a few windows users tired of their trials to Ubuntu. 

Thanks to the Linux community for making what I'm sure was a really pain the *** a reality.
