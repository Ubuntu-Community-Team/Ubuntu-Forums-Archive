### Post by irotas on 2007-05-18
So I was finally able to fix this. I took a chance at a refurbished keyboard from Dell. It only cost $35, plus about $4 S&H. It took me about 20 minutes to install, but any normal human could probably do it in 5-10 minutes. Anyway, the problem has disappeared, and I'm finally able to enjoy my laptop.
