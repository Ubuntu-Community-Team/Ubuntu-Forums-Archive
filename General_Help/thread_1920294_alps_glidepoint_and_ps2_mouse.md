### Post by peter d on 2012-02-04
I have an ALPS Glidepoint which gave me problems in my Dell laptop. I installed psmouse-alps-dkms. It is now much better.

You can get it here [http://people.canonical.com/~sforshee/alps-touchpad/psmouse-alps-0.10/psmouse-alps-dkms_0.10_all.deb]("http://people.canonical.com/~sforshee/alps-touchpad/psmouse-alps-0.10/psmouse-alps-dkms_0.10_all.deb")
