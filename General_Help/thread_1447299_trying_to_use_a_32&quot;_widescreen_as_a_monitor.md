### Post by Mark Phelps on 2010-04-06
TVs may look like monitors, but their resolutions are very different -- which is why some video cards have TV Out jacks on the back.

Your problem is complicated by the fact that there are no ATI Linux drivers for your card.  ATI dropped Linux support for your card a long time ago.  You're relegated to using the open source drivers, and in many cases, those simply don't provide TV Out capability.
