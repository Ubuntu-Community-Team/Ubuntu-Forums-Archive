### Post by grahammechanical on 2011-03-18
Com ports are serial ports, if I remember correctly. How many serial connectors do you have on the motherboard? And which one have you plugged the Bluetooth device into? If you have one serial port/connector it is com 0. If you have two serial ports/connectors, then one will be com 0 and the other will be com 1.

Your BIOS utility might help find which one the device is connected to if you have more than one com/serial port. Otherwise, you will need to trace the cables from the port to the motherboard and read the label on the motherboard.
