### Post by bodhi.zazen on 2010-07-11
1. You can set your home directory any way you wish.

Part of what is overwhelming at first is the file system layout.

See:

[http://www.freeos.com/articles/3102/](http://www.freeos.com/articles/3102/)

[img]http://www.techbu.com/wp-content/uploads/2009/06/filesystemhierarchyhb8.jpg[/img]

2. Your netbook probably does not have good enough video card for advanced effects, I am not sure on that (I do not know the videocard off the top of my head). You can do transparency.

3. It is rare you will need software that is not in the Ubuntu repositories. As a new user , start there.

You need to enable "universe" and "multiverse".

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Repositories/Ubuntu](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Repositories/Ubuntu)

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/InstallingSoftware](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/InstallingSoftware)

And this may help as well

[http://wiki.linuxquestions.org/wiki/Linux_software_equivalent_to_Windows_software](http://wiki.linuxquestions.org/wiki/Linux_software_equivalent_to_Windows_software)
