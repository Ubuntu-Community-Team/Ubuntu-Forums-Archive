### Post by lmarmisa on 2009-12-10
May be this is a problem related with the low power mode.

Go to System -> Preferences -> ScreenSaver and click on Power management.

Select Never for Actions and Display and uncheck the button of Spin down hard disks when possible.

After those changes, the low power mode will be disabled.

I would like that this suggestion would be useful for you.
