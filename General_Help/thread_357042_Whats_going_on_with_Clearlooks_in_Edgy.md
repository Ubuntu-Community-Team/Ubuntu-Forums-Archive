That wont do much good due to package conflicts.

Anyway I figured out why the old color themes did not work. "Clearlooks-Quicksilver" was renamed "Clearlooks" so installing the old theme just caused duplicate names and it would always default to the one in /usr/share/themes so it would still show Quicksilver. It was easy to fix once I figured out what was going on.

Icons are another matter though as they have all been changed.
