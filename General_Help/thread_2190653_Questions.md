### Post by Kirboosy on 2013-11-28
Welcome to the forums!

So many questions, but I think I can get you pointed in the right direction

> There was also a black screen for most of that long wait. Is all of this normal behavior?
Yes, sometimes the splash screen doesn't display properly while the computer is booting. Its more common when booting off of a CD/DVD. As long as the computer comes up there is nothing to worry about.
> I'm concerned that Ubuntu won't run properly on my laptop if I install it
If the Live CD ran normal then generally you won't have hardware problems with Ubuntu. Did everything work properly? Wifi, sound, webcam, graphics card (maybe play a HD video or video game).
> Also, when I'm running more than one program at full screen, is there a  way to easily switch between them as I could with a task bar? 
Switching between full screen applications isn't straight forward. People have come up with work around but I know for the most part it isn't as simple as alt+tab. You can alt+tab between maximized screens. I would recommend using a maximized screen to make switching easier.
> will I hear the thunderbird sound notification if I'm viewing another desktop?
You should be able to hear Thunderbird no problem. Also it should still display a popup of a new email (if that is enabled)

You may want to look around at the different versions of Ubuntu. There are multiple desktop environments and different ways they work. Find the best one for you and whichever makes the most sense to you.

Hope that helps!
~Caboose
