### Post by 3rdalbum on 2010-04-26
> **hakermania said:**
> no, some sites let you log in with an OpenId.
What is this? Is it a number or something?
How do I create this?

When you register for Launchpad (or any of the other Ubuntu Single Sign-On services), it creates an OpenID for you. It'll give you a URL and password, and you provide those for any OpenID aware sites.

Alternatively, if you have a Myspace account that also works as an OpenID; the Myspace site should give you the details.
