### Post by WorMzy on 2010-10-02
I don't know how "u" does it, but you can do it by right clicking on a panel and choosing "New Panel". After it appears, right-click on the new panel, choose properties, and set the orientation to "Bottom". Voila, you now have two panels on the bottom of the screen.

### Post by WorMzy on 2010-10-02
That's probably because you haven't added anything to the new panel. You could add a Window List applet to it (by right-clicking and choosing "Add to panel"), but it'd just duplicate the one you already have. Increasing the panel size (as I mentioned earlier) is the only way of achieving something akin to what you are trying to do, I'm afraid.
