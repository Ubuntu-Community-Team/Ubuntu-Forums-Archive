### Post by mcduck on 2007-02-28
Because there are far too many packages available in Synaptic (20428 on my setup!), and many of them are different libraries and such that are not actually usable applications. Also many of them are not very commonly used, and even more are just command-line apps. Putting them all in Add/Remove would pretty much ruin the idea of having easy-to-use interface for installing common applications.
