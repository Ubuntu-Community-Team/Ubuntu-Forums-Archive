### Post by 3rdalbum on 2009-05-29
If you don't get the option in Update Manager to upgrade to a later version of Ubuntu, you need to go to System > Administration > Software Sources. Under the Updates tab, choose "Normal Releases" from the popup menu at the bottom. Go into Update Manager, click Check, and then in a few moments it will give you the option to upgrade to a later version of Ubuntu.

Updating could solve your problem, in that graphics drivers and installed software are also updated. If the Blender problem is a bug in Blender, it may be solved in the later version. If the problem is due to graphics drivers, then that may be solved too.
