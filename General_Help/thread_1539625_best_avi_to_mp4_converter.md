### Post by MaxIBoy on 2010-07-26
FFMPEG is a good bet:
[http://linuxers.org/tutorial/ffmpeg-tutorial-beginners](http://linuxers.org/tutorial/ffmpeg-tutorial-beginners)

If you want a GUI front end to FFMPEG, then VLC Media Player includes one. It's in "Media->Convert/Save." But calling FFMPEG from the command line will work just fine.
