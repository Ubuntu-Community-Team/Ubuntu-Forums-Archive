### Post by viperdvman on 2011-10-19
Go to System Settings and click on Workspace Behavior. Once in Workspace Behavior, you click on Workspace, find Workspace Type... there there is where you find Desktop and Netbook.

System Settings > Workspace Behavior > Workspace > Workspace Type

The nice thing is, you can switch it anytime you want :)
