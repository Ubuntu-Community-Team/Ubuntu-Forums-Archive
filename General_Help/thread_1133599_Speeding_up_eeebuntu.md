### Post by jojo1224 on 2009-04-23
Your cpu is probably under clocked, right click on the top panel and go to add to panel, type in cpu and add the CPU Frequency Scaling Monitor. Then click the CPU Monitor it and click 1.6ghz, it will then ask for your password. Type it in and check remember (or something like that, it has been a while since I reinstalled on my acer aspire one) you basicly want it to keep the password saved so you don't always have to enter it everytime you want to change the cpu speed.
