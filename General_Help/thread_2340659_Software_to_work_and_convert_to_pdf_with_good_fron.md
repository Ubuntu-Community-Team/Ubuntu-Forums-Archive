+1. And PDFShuffler can move pages around and add and subtract them, too. It's in the official repos (install from package manager or terminal). I use it often. Create documents in Libreoffice, export as PDF, add further PDF document pages from elsewhere (signed and scanned PDF documents for example) then bump the whole lot out as one PDF document.
