### Post by profeXorGeek on 2007-08-18
Have you recently moved your linux machine? Some things like microwaves, big power supplies and other things that emit EM radiation can interfere with wireless. Your wireless can also become sluggish if someone else nearby installed a wireless router and is operating on the same channel as your router. If the wireless linux machine is sluggish but a wired machine is not you could try switching channels.

I don't know why your connections would just disappear. At the bottom of the networking window there is a dropdown with eth0, eth1, etc... if you change that does it make a difference?

Sorry, this is kinda general advice but maybe it will help anyway. Good luck!
