### Post by kostkon on 2010-05-02
You can, easily. Install the *PulseAudio Device Chooser* utility and use the pulseaudio volume control.

You can add the device chooser utility to yous startup apps (System &#8594; Prerefences &#8594; Startup apps). You can then left-click on its icon in the tray (a mini-jack icon) to easily access the pulseaudio volume control.
