### Post by Therion on 2009-03-30
Let's make sure this isn't simply a Gnome-panel issue.

Right-click on a blank area of your top-panel.
Click on "Add to panel".
Scroll down until you find the "Windows Switcher" applets (there will be two of them and you want the one that lets you switch between applications using "Buttons").
Add that applet to your panel and minimize an application or two...

Joy?
