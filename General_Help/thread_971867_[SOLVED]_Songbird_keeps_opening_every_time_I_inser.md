### Post by leutnant on 2008-11-05

Songbird opens up every time I plug my ipod. I want this to stop happening. I want ubuntu to not do anything when my ipod is plugged. How can I achieve this?

### Post by drs305 on 2008-11-05
If you go to Nautilus, Edit, Preferences, Media - is iPod or Songbird listed in any of the defaults? If so, change the setting.
