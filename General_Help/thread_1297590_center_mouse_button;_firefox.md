### Post by superdav42 on 2009-10-21
Drive everyone else crazy too not sure why the do it. What's supposed to do is open the url that is in the clipboard. To disable it go to [about:config](about:config) and change the value
middlemouse.contentLoadURL
to false.
There you go more annoying middle mouse button woes.
