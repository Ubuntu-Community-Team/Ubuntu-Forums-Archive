### Post by xopher on 2006-11-28
If it's a movie editing application you should check out Diva - it's far from finished, but looks really promising.

[http://www.diva-project.org/](http://www.diva-project.org/) (down at the moment)

Here's the about section anyway:

> Diva is a project to build an easy to use, scalable, open-source video editing software for the Gnome desktop. Our goal is to provide users with a complete and tightly integrated set of tools that can be used to import, edit, enhance and export digital video material. The aim of the project is to chart the unexplored areas of video editing for the open-source platform.

Diva is written in mono on the top of the gstreamer multimedia library. It currently runs only on Linux. 

And here you can look at some screenshots: [http://files.diva-project.org/screenshots/](http://files.diva-project.org/screenshots/)
