### Post by gumpish on 2010-03-02
This isn't a request for help, just a place for anyone with the same problem I had to find the answer...

To create a trapezoid in Inkscape:

[LIST=1]
[*]Draw a rectangle.
[*]In the Path menu choose Object to Path.
[*]Switch to the Node tool.
[*]Click on one of the corners of the rectangle which is on the side you wish to make longer or shorter.
[*]Use one of the arrow keys to move it left/right or up/down and count the number of times you press the key.
[*]Select the corner on the other end of the side you are modifying.
[*]Press the arrow key in the opposite direction you used in step 5 the same number of times.
[/LIST]

Congratulations! You now have a perfectly symmetrical trapezoid! :D
