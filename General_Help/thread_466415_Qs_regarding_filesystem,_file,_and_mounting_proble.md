### Post by OttifantSir on 2007-06-11
2. About the reformat of harddrives, you can probably (quite noob here) use gParted. It can't be found in the Ubuntu menu system as far as I can tell, but the LiveCD has it listed under System -> Administration.

5. Audio CDs sometimes have copy protection. try searching for something like "audio protection" on here and read what posts seem to match your situation.

As a general rule, if you ask for hardware help, you should list what are the specifics of your hardware, such as:

Processor make, model and speed
RAM size
Network Internal Card (NIC) (Don't forget the model)
Wireless Card
What make and model external Hard Drive Disk(s) (HDD) you have.

And so on. At least in your case, you need:

PC model (Dell, HP, IBM, Toshiba + what model it is)
What kind of external HDD (Manufacturer, model, size, filesystem, how do you connect it)
What kind of USB-drive are we talking about? Pendrive or HDD?
Make and model of CD/DVD drive
Graphics card (make and model)

Any information beyond this might be needed, but this is, in your case, the bare essentials.

Read the sticky posts on forums generally called something like "Read this before posting"
