### Post by movrshakr on 2010-04-24
No answer forthcoming here, but I finally found something after an all-day search.  This has been a known issue (complaint?) since 2007 but never fixed!  

[https://bugs.launchpad.net/metacity/+bug/160311/comments/11](https://bugs.launchpad.net/metacity/+bug/160311/comments/11)

contains a workaround for "Resizing windows by grabbing window borders is difficult," although it is not a trivial thing to do.  It involves editing an xml file (owned by root) for whatever theme you are using.  

I assume these edits are at risk of being wiped out by an update.
