### Post by J_Stanton on 2010-03-11
go to system-administration-software sources and click the other software tab. at the bottom click the ADD button. add- ppa:c-korn/vlc to the apt line and add source. then open terminal and do- sudo apt-get update. after that, you will be able to get the latest vlc in synaptic or the software center.
