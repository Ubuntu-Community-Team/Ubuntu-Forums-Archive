### Post by Musicmaker384 on 2012-05-05
I can think of two possible issues:

One: Your audio jack is physically damaged. Boot into Windows (or OSX if that's what you have, if you have any other OS) and start up iTunes or something, and see if your sounds play in Windows. If they do, then the audio jack itself is fine. Alternatively, check to see if the device you're inputting into the audio jack is working properly. That could be the problem, too.

Two: I don't know about going under Ubuntu's hood, so I can't help you there. I can, however, say that hardware problems are often caused by driver problems. Maybe your audio driver isn't compatible with Ubuntu? I'm just giving you ideas.

Don't take my word for any of this - since no one seems to be posting to this thread I thought I'd try my best to help you. Your problem is most likely something wrong with Ubuntu, I've been seeing a lot of problems with 12.04 here on Ubuntu Forums, and I'm having problems myself.

Good luck.
