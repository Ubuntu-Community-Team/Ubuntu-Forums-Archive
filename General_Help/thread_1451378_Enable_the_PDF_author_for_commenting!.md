### Post by labinnsw on 2010-04-17
There are quite a few options avialable to you. I am listing some below.

PDF Editor - Available from software center or synaptic
flpsed - Available from software center or synaptic
Gimp - Available from software center or synaptic
Libre Office - Available from software center or synaptic. Requires Sun PDF import Add on (My favorite for PDF document creation)
[OpenOffice.org]("http://extensions.services.openoffice.org/en/project/pdfimport") - Requires Sun PDF import Add on.
[PDF-XChange Viewer]("http://pdf-xchange-viewer.en.softonic.com/universaldownloader-launch") - Requires Wine (This is so good I registered my copy.)
[Foxit]("http://www.foxitsoftware.com/Secure_PDF_Reader/") - A favorite of many

I believe there may be others but these are the ones that I can remember at the moment.
