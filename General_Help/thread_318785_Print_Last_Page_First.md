### Post by IanW on 2006-12-14
In OpenOffice, select "Print" from the menu (or use ctrl+p) to open the print dialog box.
Next click the "Options" button at bottom left.
One of the options offered here is "Reversed" - this is what you want.

BTW this is all in OO's help file.
