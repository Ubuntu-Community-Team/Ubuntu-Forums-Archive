### Post by Simian Man on 2008-09-25
It took me a long time to figure this out, but to fix it I simply fired up Kcontrol and changed KDE's icon theming back from Human to the KDE default.  The human theme made kde apps look more native on gnome, but without certain icons available, it's pretty useless.  Just posting this in case future googlers find this thread :).
