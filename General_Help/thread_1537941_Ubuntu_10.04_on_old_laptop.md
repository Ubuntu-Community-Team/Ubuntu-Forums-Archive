### Post by Joe of loath on 2010-07-24
10.04 should work OK, although I prefer to use LXDE instead of Gnome/KDE on anything with 512mb or less of RAM. Have a play with Lubuntu, see how you like it.

However, an extra 512mb of RAM won't be too expensive, and will make it a whole lot more usable.

### Post by snowpine on 2010-07-24
[1gb RAM is the recommended minimum]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Installation/SystemRequirements") for Ubuntu 10.04.

With only 512mb I would recommend a lightweight alternative (such as Xubuntu).
