### Post by Elfy on 2008-09-11
Well if you're new to linux I would post in Absolute Beginners - people understand that it can be hard to start afresh. But Genreal Help as well- I'd stick to those two for most things. Some of the other forums are a bit slower.

Although you'll probably not get a hard time anywhere and certainly shouldn't.

Make sure the title reflects the problem - not "Help it doesn't work" :) - be concise and give details of what you might of tried if you have.
