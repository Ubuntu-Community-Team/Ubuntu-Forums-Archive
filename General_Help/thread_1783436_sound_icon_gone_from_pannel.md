### Post by Tamlynmac on 2011-06-15
The easiest way is to simply drag them from the menu and place them into the panel.

For example: Go to "system/preferences/sound" and left click one time on the sound app, hold the left button down, drag it to the panel and let go. Then right click and select "move", then place the icon anywhere in the panel you wish.

You can do the same thing with the mail app.

Good Luck & hope this helps.
