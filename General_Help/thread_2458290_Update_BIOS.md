### Post by CelticWarrior on 2021-02-20
Although it is usually recommended to update the motherboard's firmware, BIOS or UEFI (FYI, UEFI in this case, regardless of the manufacturer's terminology), the F2c update you mentioned correctly as "beta" seems to have been released exclusively for adding support to a new CPU/APU. So, unless you're having issues the update isn't justified, in my opinion, and even if you are this specific update likely won't address those issues.
