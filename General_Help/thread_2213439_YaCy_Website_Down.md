### Post by orbiter2 on 2014-03-27
The YaCy website is **not** down!
We had an issue with the website service provider (strato) who blocked yacy.net after too much traffic, see (german) forum [http://forum.yacy-websuche.de/viewtopic.php?p=29778#p29778](http://forum.yacy-websuche.de/viewtopic.php?p=29778#p29778)

Maybe your browser or system DNS cache still thinks that yacy.net is unavailable. Please restart your browser and try [http://yacy.net](http://yacy.net), [http://latest.yacy.net](http://latest.yacy.net) and [http://debian.yacy.net](http://debian.yacy.net) or alternatively [http://yacy.de](http://yacy.de) or [http://latest.yacy.de](http://latest.yacy.de)
