### Post by Wim Sturkenboom on 2007-01-22
In general computer terms, a watchdog is a device (usually a integrated circuit) that needs to be triggered every X (milli-)seconds. If it's not triggered, it will generate a reset.

The first hit when googling for *ubuntu watchdog* gives [http://packages.ubuntulinux.org/warty/admin/watchdog](http://packages.ubuntulinux.org/warty/admin/watchdog)
