### Post by ubudog on 2009-12-08
There is a nice program called "startupmanager" you can install it from Ubuntu Software Center.  Once you download the splash screen you want, open startupmanager and go to the "Appearance" tab.  Click Manage usplash themes at the bottom of the window, then click add, select your file and reboot.  You should see your new splash screen now.
