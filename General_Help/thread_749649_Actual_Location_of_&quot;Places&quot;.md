### Post by edm1 on 2008-04-08
That bookmarks tab just appeared in the last few days, weird. They used to just be part of the places menu. Anyway it's in your home directory and is called .gtk-bookmarks you'll have to enable hidden folders (Ctrl+H) in order to see it.

You could open it by
```
gedit ~/.gtk-bookmarks
```
in the terminal.
