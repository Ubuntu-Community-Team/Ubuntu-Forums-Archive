### Post by mcduck on 2010-11-04
Actually that sounds a lot more like a broken monitor or graphics card, or perhaps the cable you use to connect them.

Can you test the display with another computer?

It makes no difference if the system recognizes a display or not, as long as the resolution and refresh rate are correct the image will display correctly. And even setting a wrong resolution or refresh rate wouldn't cause graphical issues like that.
