Recently I notice that some films I watch on my Ubuntu 10.04 machine have a distinctly blue tinge.

### Post by blueghoti on 2010-08-02
Hi no2498 - 

That's fantastic!!  Thanks for your help.

Just in case anyone else might have this problem...  I am running Totem v 2.30.2.  I adjusted the saturation as no2498 suggested, but the screen became more grey scale with less colour.  I played with a couple of other settings, then clicked the Restore Defaults button.  Voila - everything worked!

I thought it was a bit odd as I hadn't made any modifications to settings in the first place.  Greatful to no2498 for pointing me in the right direction...
