### Post by 73ckn797 on 2009-09-21
> **ConsumingFire783 said:**
> My system time clock on the toolbar periodically, two or three times a day, resets itself to five hours ahead of the actual time.  I right click and reset the time, but it still changes itself back after several hours.  Is there a way to permanently reset this to the correct time?

If you have not already done so, go to System/Preferences/Main Menu. There enable, under System, Control Center. From there you can find under System/Control Center, the setting for Date/Time. Synchronize with internet server.

You may also need to check the computer BIOS time settings.
