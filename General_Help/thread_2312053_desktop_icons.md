### Post by Vladlenin5000 on 2016-02-01
Hi and welcome.
> how can I keep the desktop icons on the guest sessions because they are deleted after each pc restart
You can't and that's by design. The guest session isn't supposed to save settings.

You can create a new user which by default won't be in the sudoers group therefore it'll be "limited" just like you want: [http://lubuntuhowto.blogspot.com.es/2014/08/how-to-create-new-users-on-lubuntu.html](http://lubuntuhowto.blogspot.com.es/2014/08/how-to-create-new-users-on-lubuntu.html)
