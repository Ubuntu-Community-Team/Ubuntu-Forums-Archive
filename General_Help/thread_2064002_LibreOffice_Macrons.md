### Post by LewisTM on 2012-09-28
You should use the Compose feature of Linux.
You compose key can be setup through your Keyboard settings, it's usually AltGr.
So to make &#257;, press the compose key (don't hold it) then underscore _ (= shift-hyphen) then a. Works for all vowels &#257; &#275; &#299; &#333; &#363; &#563;
[http://www.hermit.org/Linux/ComposeKeys.html](http://www.hermit.org/Linux/ComposeKeys.html)
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/ComposeKey](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/ComposeKey)
