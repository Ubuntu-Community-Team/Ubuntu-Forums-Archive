### Post by llamabr on 2010-03-28
the dot is just a character, but it makes it hidden from normal view.  In terminal, just do:
```

cd ~/.freevo/local_config
ls

```

### Post by NiGhtMarEs0nWax on 2010-03-28
~/ means your home directory,  the . means it is a hidden file.

so its a hidden file in your home directory. you should be able to view hidden files by clicking view up at the top of the window.
