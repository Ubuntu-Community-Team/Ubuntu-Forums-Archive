### Post by mcduck on 2007-03-20
They aren't located anywhere else than in the user's personal desktop settings. You can just drag new icons from your menu to the panel, or right-click on the panel and select 'Add to Panel' and then  'Application Launcher' and then drag ones you want to the panel.

If you really need to do this for all users the tool for managing desktop settings for users & groups is called Sabayon (not the Linux distribution, I'm talking about the Gnome Administration tool ;)).
