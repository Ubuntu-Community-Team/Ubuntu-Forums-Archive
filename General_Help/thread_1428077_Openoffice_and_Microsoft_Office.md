### Post by Anuovis on 2010-03-12
The compatibility between the two is still an issue. In my limited experience with presentations and word documents, MS Office is not that better at reading Open Office formats either. You can usually count on .pdf exporting though, this bypasses any of these problems.

Working for non-native formats, especially editing is really painful, I avoid that as much as I can. 

Not sure about MS scrambling open formats on purpose but they sure do not want to implement any compatibility. There are no technical obstacles for doing that, as far as I understand.
