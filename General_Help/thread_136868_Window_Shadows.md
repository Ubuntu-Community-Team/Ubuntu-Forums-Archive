### Post by Hachi on 2006-02-26
There are no easy menu options to enable shadow in the current version of Gnome, but there is a guide showing you how to do it here: [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/DropShadows](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/DropShadows)

If your a beginner and don't wish to risk instability I recommend you wait for shadows in future releases of Gnome.
