### Post by tamoneya on 2008-04-06
For your first two problems you should continue to post on their threads.  This way any solution that you find will be found by other users later when they go searching. 

As for RSS readers I personally like Google Reader.  I have multiple computers and it is useful to have it all online as opposed to local.  You can even use google reader offline by using the google gears plugin to download your last thousand or so feeds.  This way you can catch up on your reading while on the airplane or something.
