### Post by Bucky Ball on 2015-12-24
*Thread moved to **General Help**.*

To drag the window above the screen would mean you would need your mouse cursor to go out of range of the screen. Once you had the top menu bar out of range of the screen, how would you close the window? The only way would be blindly as once the cursor and menu bar are out of range of the screen they are out of sight. 

I'd think a bit more about this. It may be possible by setting the wrong screen size, or something similar, but practical? I think not. There are good reasons you cannot do it and you can drag it out of sight at the bottom (leaving the menu bar in sight). 

Good luck. :)
