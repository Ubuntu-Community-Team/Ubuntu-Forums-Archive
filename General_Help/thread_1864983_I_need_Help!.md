### Post by gennatolls on 2011-10-19
As a rule of thumb for burning a bootable cd (ie your live disk) burn at the slowest speed. I bet after you changed the BIOS settings you forgot to save before you reset causing it to boot HDD. I have had the same thing when booting usb with the sysLinux. You may try Unetbootin. You could boot up a live session and recover all your data from Winblows.
