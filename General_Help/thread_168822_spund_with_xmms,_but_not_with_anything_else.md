### Post by Ubuntist on 2006-05-03
For a while I thought I had the same problem, but it turned out that sometimes the analog/digital output jack was getting switched off.  If this happens to be what's causing your problem, you can turn the jack on by opening the volume control, selecting the switches tab and checking the appropriate box.  Hope that helps....
