### Post by CocoAUS on 2007-03-11
Taken from [http://othello.alma.edu/~07tmhopk/ubuntuhowto.html#intellimouse](http://othello.alma.edu/~07tmhopk/ubuntuhowto.html#intellimouse) , just replace the following section in your xorg.conf:

```
Section "InputDevice"
  Identifier "Configured Mouse"
  Driver "mouse"
  Option "CorePointer"
  Option "Device" "/dev/input/mice"
  Option "Protocol" "ExplorerPS/2"
  Option "Buttons" "7"
  Option "ButtonMapping" "1 2 3 6 7"
  Option "ZAxisMapping" "4 5"
  Option "Emulate3Buttons" "false"
EndSection
```
