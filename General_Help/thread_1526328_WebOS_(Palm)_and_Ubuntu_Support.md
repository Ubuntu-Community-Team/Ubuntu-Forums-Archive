### Post by nemilar on 2010-07-07
Hi,

I have a Palm Pre Plus (verizon) and yes, if I plug it into either my Ubuntu or my Fedora-based machines, I do get a USB mass storage device option.

However I usually find it easier just to enable the WiFi on the phone, and then I am able to ssh/sftp into it.  The phone charges much faster when it's plugged into the wall (as opposed to a USB port) so I actually never dock it with my computer; when I need to transfer files, I just use the wifi.

Hope that helps!
