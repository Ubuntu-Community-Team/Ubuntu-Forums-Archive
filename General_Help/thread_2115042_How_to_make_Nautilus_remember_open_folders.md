### Post by tgalati4 on 2013-02-11
Open a terminal:

```
man nautilus
```

You can create desktop launchers with the URL and -g switch for window geometry.  You can also drag frequently used folders to the left side bar.

You can put multiple directories with a single nautilus command.

You can suspend to keep your apps in the same place, or you can hibernate (RAM-to-Disk) to perserve your apps if you have to dual boot.
