LTS stands for Long Term Support every couple years they release a version they will provide longer support and is generally more stable than the normal 6 month releases that have the latest and greatest bells and whistles  but may have more bugs. The current version 10.04 is the latest stable release. If you have a normal GUI desktop not just a blinking courser then you are on the Desktop version.
