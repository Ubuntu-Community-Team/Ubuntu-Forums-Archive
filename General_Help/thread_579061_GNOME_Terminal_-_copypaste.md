### Post by aktiwers on 2007-10-17
You can copy/paste with Middleclick on your mouse. Highlight some text and middleclick.

Or you can right-click and pick Copy, then right-click again and paste.

Or you can Copy something, and in terminal press CTRL+Insert.

Or if its a path to a folder or a file, you can drag/drop the file/folder into the terminal.

Or if its a command often used or an application or a path or whatever, you can start writing the start of the word and then click the Tap key on your keyboard. 
Then it will write the rest for you.
