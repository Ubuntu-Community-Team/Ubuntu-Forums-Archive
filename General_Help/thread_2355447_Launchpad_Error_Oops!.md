### Post by rstrodrigo on 2017-03-16
I have solved the issue:

After I deleted my account Ubuntu single sign on, I created a new one with a different email (a decoy yahoo mail). Then when I tried to change to my default email it said that an account already had my email. It was a previous account that I forgot to delete on launchpad. So the site asked if I would like to merge my account with it, I clicked yes. After that it sent myself an email asking to merge with my own accounts, after given permission, it merged successfully. I've deleted my decoy email and now it is all OK.
