### Post by M.A.L.M. on 2014-08-28
Right-click on any of the column labels (e.g. right click on "Name", or "Size", etc) and choose which labels you want to show or not. Alternatively, choose "View items as a grid of icons" by clicking on the icon in the top right corner of the file mgr.

Also, you can go to the menu "View" and see all the possibilities. To show the menu move your mouse pointer to the top bar or press down and keep the [ALT] key to the left of the spacebar (e.g. for "View", it's [ALT] + [V]).
