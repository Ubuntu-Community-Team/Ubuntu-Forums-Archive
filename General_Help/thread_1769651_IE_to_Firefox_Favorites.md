### Post by prodigy_ on 2011-05-28
> **Grierson said:**
> However when I go to Firefox>file>import I get the dialogue box stating 'No programs that contain bookmarks, history or password data could be found'
Wrong menu. Use *Bookmarks* -> *Show All Bookmarks* to open bookmarks library. Then choose *Import HTML...* wizard from *Import and Backup* dropdown list.
