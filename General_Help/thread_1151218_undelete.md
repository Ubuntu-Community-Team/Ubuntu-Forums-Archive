### Post by yabbadabbadont on 2009-05-06
If the drive was formatted as FAT/VFAT (it most likely is), and it has not been written to since you deleted the images, then you should be able to use any of the existing DOS/Win undelete tools on the drive to recover them.  I don't know of any native Linux apps to do the same, but there probably are a few out there.
