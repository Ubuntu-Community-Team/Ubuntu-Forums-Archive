### Post by SeijiSensei on 2015-10-22
I don't see one in Settings > Configure Shortcuts.  You could add one yourself, or redefine one of the options for uppercase/lowercase/capitalize which use various combinations of Alt, Shift, Ctrl, and U.

Once desktop publishing became common, underlining was deprecated in favor of italics.  Underlining served the same purpose as italics when documents were produced on typewriters.  (Yes, I'm that old.)  With instant access to italic fonts, underlining went out of favor.
