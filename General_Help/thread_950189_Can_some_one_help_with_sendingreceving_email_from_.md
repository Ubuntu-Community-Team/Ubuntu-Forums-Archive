### Post by marshag63 on 2008-10-16
Maybe elmo?  Its like Mutt, but a little bit lighter.  There is even a script to help you setup your email - its called "elmonconf.pl"  It sets up all your mailboxes, etc.  Run it in a terminal.  

Then, when in elmo, the tab key moves you around from your inbox to selecting mailboxes.  There is even a tutorial section (press "q" once you have read each section).

If you have set things up right through elmoconf.pl, when you start elmo you will have a mailbox for draft, inbox, outbox, sent, spam, trash and the tutorial.

Its really easy to use.  Good luck!  Let us know how it goes.
