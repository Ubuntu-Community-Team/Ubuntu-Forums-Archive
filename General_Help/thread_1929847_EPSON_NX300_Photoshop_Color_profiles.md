### Post by Ms. Daisy on 2012-02-23
That is a very interesting help request.  You have posted it in a forum dedicated to helping people using the [Ubuntu Operating System]("http://www.ubuntu.com/ubuntu").  From what you posted, it doesn't appear that you're using Ubuntu, so it's unlikely that you'll find much help in this forum.

However... I'm bored. 

So I will offer you this. In regards to printing out all magenta pages, I understand your ink cartridges are full.  It's possible that they are clogged.  I recommend you run a printer self-test to see what colors print out.  If you google your printer, you will find the manual which can tell you how to do the self test if you don't already know.  The manual may also tell you how to clean the ink cartridges, too, which may resolve the problem.

I don't use Photoshop so I can't help you there.  

If you're concerned about the image quality of the photos you have printed at Costco, IMO you're best suited to talk to someone at Costco about that. I don't know how you would have any control over what they print on their machines.
