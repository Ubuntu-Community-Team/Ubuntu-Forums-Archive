How can I have something permanently saved in Firefox's cache? ### Post by editrix on 2008-10-19
There's a Firefox extension called, I believe, Scrapbook. It saves web pages, which you can then access from the side panel on the left. And you can edit the pages before you save them, so you don't have to archive the stuff you don't want.

But, as I recall, XKCD comics are graphics. You can just save the graphic.
