### Post by lisati on 2009-12-26
From Linux, there's the "shutdown" command, e.g. ```
sudo shutdown now
```

There's also an option available to delay the shutdown.

---

### Post by MartyBuntu on 2009-12-26
Immediate shutdown is:

**sudo shutdown h now**

...enter your password when prompted.
