### Post by 16777216 on 2008-04-20
Did you check to see if *all* of the gnome mixer sliders were up?
I say this because I have had this happen many times and it is the PCM volume being way down for some reason.
double click the volume icon or right click and chose "Open Volume Control" if everything looks fine go ahead and go to edit>preferences and check all of the tracks to see if one of them is the culprit.
