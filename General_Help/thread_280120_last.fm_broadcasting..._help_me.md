### Post by amo-ej1 on 2006-10-19
last.fm offers two services. First you have the free for all which allows you  to get a profile, to listen to 'sounds like radios', listen to 'recommended tracks' or listen to a certain 'tag' kind of music etc etc. Then you have a paying service where you can create you own radio station based on what you like or dislike.

Since you're no paying costumer, you don't have access to this last feature.
