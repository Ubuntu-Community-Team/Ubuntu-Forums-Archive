### Post by Ismar on 2010-07-14
Ubuntu comes with a working wireless (driver?) I heard. Also I tried a demo of Ubuntu on my friends computer and it worked out the box, all I had to do was connect to the SSID. Now for XP, I believe if you right click manage on 'My Computer' and go to devices you can see a list of devices, or you can try 'Add Hardware' in control panel. Then just download the driver from another computer and transfer over a USB flash/hard drive (or CD). If the download only provides a .sys file just go to C:/system32/drivers/ and put it in here along with any other files (unless its a .exe/setup).

Good Luck!
