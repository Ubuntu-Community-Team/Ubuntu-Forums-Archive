### Post by Galoot on 2005-09-26
[QUOTE=diffuser78]Can somebody please help me restore the GNOME desktop.[/QUOTE]
1. Right click on an empty area of the panel you still have and select "New Panel."

2. Using your middle mouse button, drag the new blank panel wherever you want.

3. Right click on the new panel and select "Add to Panel."

4. Scroll down to "Show Desktop" and click on "Add." (This adds the Desktop symbol that you click to see the desktop.)

5. Repeat steps 3-4 for each launcher you want on this panel.

6. Rearrange the launchers you've added by dragging them around the panel using the middle mouse button. Try holding down [shift] or [ctrl] or [alt] while doing this to see how each key changes the dragging behavior.

7. Right click on an empty area of the panel, select "Properties," and play with them until it looks good to you.

8. Once you've got everything the way you like it, you can optionally right click on your new launcher icons and "Lock to Panel" to make them stay put.

9. Log out of your session, making sure the "Save current setup" box is checked.

10. Don't call your girlfriend dumb. You're *both* new at this. :rolleyes:
