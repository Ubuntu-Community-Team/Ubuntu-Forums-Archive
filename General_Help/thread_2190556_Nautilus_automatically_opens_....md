### Post by drmrgd on 2013-11-27
In System Settings, under Startup and Shutdown, there is a section called Session Management.  Under there you'll have options to 'Start with an empty session'.  If you choose that, you should get a clean session when you log in again.  Sometimes, you have choose the "Restore manually saved session", close everything out, save the session upon logout, and then choose 'Start with an empty session', and sometimes it takes on the first try.  That was at least the case a while back last time I messed with it.
