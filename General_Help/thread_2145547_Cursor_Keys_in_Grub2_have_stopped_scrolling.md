### Post by Philip Gray on 2013-05-16
Good evening

This morning I spoke to some of my IT colleagues who prep and repair my company's PCs and laptops after having surfed the net for answers and come up blank. I discovered that there are a lot of folks out there with the same issue. While they do not know much about Linux they gave me some suggestions on what to try. Gladly one of their suggestions worked. The suggestion was to check in the BIOS and see if the 'Legacy USB' option is set to 'Enabled'. Mine was not. It was set to 'BIOS Only'. I changed it to 'Enabled' and I was able to scroll my grub menu.

Regards
Philip
