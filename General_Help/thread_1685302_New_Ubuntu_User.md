### Post by cipherboy_loc on 2011-02-10
1) Under preferences, click on mouse and try enabling the scrolling on the touchpad tab.
2) Try using synaptic to search for and install packages. Its much easier than searching the internet for stuff, and this stuff is almost guaranteed to work "out of the box".
3) For the basic messaging, try pidgin or empathy (search for each in synaptic), but for the games, not so much.
4) Why not use virtualbox? Personally, I think it is much better than vmware will ever be.



I think I have given you some stuff to think about,
Cipherboy
