### Post by coffeecat on 2010-05-03
> **kyle2595 said:**
> I have to click on the wi-fi icon on the bottom right and select my network each time.

I presume you mean top right.

Right-click on the network manager icon (top-right in Ubuntu/gnome :wink:) and select 'Edit Connections'. Click on the Wireless tab. Highlight your network name and click on edit. Tick the 'Connect Automatically' box and click 'Apply'.
