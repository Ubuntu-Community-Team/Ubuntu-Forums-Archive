### Post by koenn on 2008-03-11
Here's an excellent way to learn how things really work :
pick a support forum here, eg General Help, Server Platformw, Networking
pick a problem
figure out what information you need to further diagnose the problem
diagnose the problem
figure out what else you need to know to come op with a working sollution or a decent workaround
work out a solution to the point that you can actually post actions (statements to run, buttons to click, ...)
post your sollution. 
wait for feedback
if your proposed solution didn't work, find out why not and start over. 

You learn a lot, and you help others; win-win.
