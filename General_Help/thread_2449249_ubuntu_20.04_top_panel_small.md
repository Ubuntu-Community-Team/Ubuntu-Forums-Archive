### Post by carlo-sanguineti on 2020-08-23
Firstly thank you, **drpjkurian**!

I found some interesting:
I uninstalled  GNOME Tweaks, then I installed it again.
 I opened GNOME Tweaks (rev. 3.34.0)
Then I went on Fonts -> Scaling Factors. It was **1.00**
I increased it, and immediately the top bar became much larger; I decreased it back to **1.00** and now I have my top bar as it was four, five days ago.
So the problem looks solved. At the moment I didn't make any restart, but I'm confident the problem is fixed.

I really don't know what happened, but now it's OK.

Thank you again,
