### Post by Universal344 on 2009-09-19
Lightweight X11 Desktop Environment is LXDE.  This is just another desktop environment (like GNOME or KDE) and may have been accidentally installed when you were installing something else (or you might have installed an LXDE application that depended on the rest of the environment).  At any rate, at the login screen there should be an option called sessions.  Click on that and select gnome and login.  Everything should be back to the way it was.  You can uninstall LXDE if you want, but check that it won't uninstall something with it that you want.
