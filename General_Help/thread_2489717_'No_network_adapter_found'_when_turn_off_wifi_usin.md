### Post by L_Bradley on 2023-08-08
I'm using Ubuntu 22 on an old Macbook air 4,4 and whenever I turn off wifi, the icon disappears. By typing in wifi at the activity menu, I can bring up the wifi page in settings, where it says no 'network adapter found'. However, by switching it to airplane mode, I can make the icon reappear, and turn it back on from there, and it works. It's not a problem for me, but I'm sharing it here for anyone else, so they can avoid the difficulties with trying to deal with a seemly suddenly lost wifi adapter on a computer with no ethernet port.
