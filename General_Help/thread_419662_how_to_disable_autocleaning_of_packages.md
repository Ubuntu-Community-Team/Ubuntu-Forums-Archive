### Post by klytu on 2007-04-23
> **Janissary said:**
> Hi,
My ubuntu automatically cleans the old packages in /var/cache/apt/archives directory.
How can I disable that ?

thanks :)

Go to System>Administration>Synaptic; then in Synaptic go to Settings>Preferences. On the Files tab you can adjust how Synaptic handles the temporary files (they are the old packages in /var/cache/apt/archives)
