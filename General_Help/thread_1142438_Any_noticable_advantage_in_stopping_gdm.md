### Post by Bindsa on 2009-04-29
There are 2 scenarios possible:

- The things you want Hardy to do take up all your memory and processor capacities, in this case disable GDM, because this will possibly mean your other tasks get their necessary CPU load.
- Your usual things don't use the CPU/GPU etc. too much, there is plenty of capacity left, in this case just keep GDM enabled.

I hope this helped you.
