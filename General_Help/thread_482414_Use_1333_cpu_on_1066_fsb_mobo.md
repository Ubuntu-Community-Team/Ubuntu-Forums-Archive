### Post by CrispyFried on 2007-06-23
even if everything else matches (voltage etc) youll take a 30% hit in speed. your 2.3 Ghz cpu will only run at 1.5 Ghz

you also might also run into the bios not liking it but usually if its just a speed mismatch thats ok. much better to find a mobo that supports 1333 bus.
