The problems happens in every desktop enviroment I tried - Gnome 3, Gnome classic (without effects), Ubuntu 2D and Ubuntu. It is present even in the login screen.

I just installed Ubuntu in this machine, after it was a few months stopped due to lacking a mouse. Before, it had Fedora installed, without any similar problems.

The problem did not shown up during or just after install, and I was able to run Gnome 3 a few times without a problem before the yellow-ish areas started.
