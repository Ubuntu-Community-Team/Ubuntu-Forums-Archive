### Post by dudesks on 2011-03-04
Hey,

I know its very simple solution, but first time when I got this issue I was surprised and I got no solution.

Mistake : I was about to remove some extra panel from my task bar. But I deleted the panel.

Outcome : I lost my bottom taskbar, whatever window I open I can not see I always need to go Alt+Tab for that.

Solution :
In top panel right click on your panel and left click on &#8220;New Panel&#8221; Make sure new panel is at bottom &#8211; if not click and drag it to the bottom.

Once your panel is at the bottom &#8211; right click on the panel and say &#8220;add to panel&#8221;. A menu will open and from the menu select &#8220;Window List&#8221;. Left click on the &#8220;add button&#8221;.

Now when you minimize the window it will appear at the bottom panel.

Hope this will help.
