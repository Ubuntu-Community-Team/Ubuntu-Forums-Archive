### Post by tgalati4 on 2014-10-23
Not sure of the percentage, but I would guess that the number of routers with open source software is quite small.  I did buy a Netgear router that had open source firmware on it, but I don't have the model number.  I would have to search for it.

You can spend some time here:  [http://www.myopenrouter.com/](http://www.myopenrouter.com/)

Cisco is a large player in the router market, especially with their D-Link acquisition several years ago.  I have never seen a pie chart of world market share.  There are many different styles of routers.  Many newer services combine the router and the modem into one box.
