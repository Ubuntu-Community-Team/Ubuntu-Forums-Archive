### Post by fooman on 2008-11-23
open synaptic and first make sure the "quick search" box is empty. 
if the main window is blank,  then on the left side, bottom area...choose "status" (or anything besides sections).

after that,  choose "sections",  and in the upper left area,  choose "all"...if it is still blank,  choose "base system" and let it populate.

now choose "sections" again and see if it populates.

hope that helps.
