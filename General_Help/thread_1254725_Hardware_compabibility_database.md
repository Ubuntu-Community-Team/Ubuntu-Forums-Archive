### Post by earthpigg on 2009-08-31
hi,

first two google results for "ubuntu hardware compatibility database":
[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/HardwareSupport/](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/HardwareSupport/)
[http://www.ubuntuhcl.org/](http://www.ubuntuhcl.org/)

however, i am about 99% certain that, to Ubuntu, a USB storage device is a USB storage device. thumb drive, external ssd, external 1TB hard drive, whatever. only thing relevant to Ubuntu is weather the device physically functions or not, and how big it is.

i believe this because:

the outstanding ubuntu wiki list (first link) doesn't even bother having a hard drive category...

and the second link's list, [here]("http://www.ubuntuhcl.org/browse/search+hard-drives?category=31"), shows very few people even seem to bother submitting as _**all**_ of the ones that are submitted are rated as either "works perfectly" or "works great". not a single one is listed as having any issues.
