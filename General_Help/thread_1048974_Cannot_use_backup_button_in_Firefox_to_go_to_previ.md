### Post by mssever on 2009-01-24
Reinstalling won't fix Firefox problems, because nearly all such problems have something to do with your profile. If you back up ~/.mozilla then delete it, Firefox will create a new clean profile, where everything will work properly. Note that by doing this, you'll lose all your bookmarks, settings, add-ons (except those you installed through a package manager), etc.
