### Post by agim on 2008-02-28
First thing to do would be to check your md5sum and check the disk integrity.

md5sum:
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HowToMD5SUM](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HowToMD5SUM)

To check the disk integrity, there is an option, rather than install, on the first screen you will see in the livecd. 'Check cd for errors' or something to that effect.
