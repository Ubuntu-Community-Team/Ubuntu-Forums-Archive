### Post by howefield on 2009-10-28
I think that the main purpose for gimpshop is to make photoshop users feel more comfortable, and secondary purpose to convert photoshop pirates to gimpshop.

So in that respect, it's whole reason for existing doesn't come into your scenerio.

If you search for screenshots gimpshop, you find the main difference seems to be that the seperate gimp windows stay seperate but are all nested within one large window, and that much of the terminology is photoshopified ;)

So for me, straight gimp is fine, (in actual fact, as a former photoshop user, gimp was fine) Given that gimpshop is a modification of gimp, I am not sure how up to date it is.
