### Post by sectshun8 on 2011-05-27
So I was gathering some screenshots to use in a tutorial on how to do something in Unity.  For the most part that went well... however I did find an unfortunate quirk.

When I Right-Click any of the items in the Unity Launcher panel, and they bring up the submenu (for example I did the modification to add Pics, Docs etc to my Home Folder in the Launcher) I then cannot do a screenshot using my "PrtSc" key on my netbook.   This only happens when the submenus are visible.  Unfortunate because I wanted to get a screen shot of the submenus... is there another way?

:confused:

---

### Post by jerrrys on 2011-05-28
open up your screenshot utility using your 'MainMenu' then set delay to 5 seconds and take screenshot.  you now have 5 seconds to pull down your submenu and get it in your screenshot.
