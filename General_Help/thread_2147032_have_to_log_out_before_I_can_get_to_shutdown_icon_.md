### Post by markbl on 2013-05-20
Switch the [Alternative Status Menu extension](https://extensions.gnome.org/extension/5/alternative-status-menu/) on. GNOME Shell is great but hiding this option was a silly design choice which they have rectified (a little!) in later versions of GNOME Shell. In your version, you were expected to "discover" that you can press the ALT key while in that user pull-down menu to get the power off option, which is ridiculous.
