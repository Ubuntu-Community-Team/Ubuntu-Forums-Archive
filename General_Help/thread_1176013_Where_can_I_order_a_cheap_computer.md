### Post by anonymousturtle3 on 2009-06-02
Ebay if you don't care about the condition of your computer. I got my computer from there at about 250 and it's still kicking despite the bad CPU cache and relatively low amount of storage, I am still able to use compiz and all those wonderful programs that make ubuntu awesome.
I would look at newegg.com if you ever decide to expand your budget on your computer, it is a very reliable site, fast shipping, good parts, and I've had good experience with them. They only sell computers that are well over 300 dollars, however, so that site should be considered only after a (major) budget revision.
You could go to wal-mart and get a cheap netbook for about 300 (depending on the brand). If you are looking only for a desktop, I wouldn't recommend going to a store, online will do, search cheap computers on google and maybe you'll find something you like.
Craigslist is a possibility but I have no personal experience with them so I can't vouch for it, just a suggesion.
