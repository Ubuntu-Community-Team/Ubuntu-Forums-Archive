1st, try unplugging the mouse and plugging it back in. I have seen this with rarely with USB devices. Can you see your mouse list if you open a terminal and issue lsusb? Lastly, have you added an additional PCI card for more USB ports? Mice and keyboards won't be recoginized by many BIOS unles plugged into motherboard USB ports. USB devices that Linux uses before UDEV runs which aren't plugged into non-motherboard USB ports may not be seen until after UDEV runs. I know this can cause cause issues during bootup for keyboards not in motherboard ports. GRUB is run before UDEV.
