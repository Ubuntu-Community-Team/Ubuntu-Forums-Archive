### Post by taurus on 2006-11-02
I guess the question is does your BIOS "see" that new harddrive at all?  If it doesn't, then no OS can detect it.  So, I would go into the BIOS and look for it first...  And if it is a brand new harddrive, it should still be under warranty so you can call the manufacture up to see if they would replace it if it is dead!
