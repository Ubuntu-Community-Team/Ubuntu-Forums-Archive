### Post by morgengenuss on 2008-05-12
alright, basically this is not the mixer, it's just a control for the mixer.

you can access the mixer by adding it to the xfce-panel or by executing "xfce4-mixer" (in a shell or after pressing alt+f2).

of course there's also the option of installing other mixers (like gnome-alsa-mixer or amixer or aumix or whatever).
