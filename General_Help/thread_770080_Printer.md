### Post by ryanhaigh on 2008-04-27
[http://www.turboprint.de/english.html](http://www.turboprint.de/english.html) says that the BJC2100 (it doesn't mention SP specifically) is supported. Once installed you can change the quality in one of the preference dialogs so that the banner isn't printed on every page. I found this a great solution as I mostly used the printer I had for text, fortunately Canon came to the party and released drivers for my printer.
