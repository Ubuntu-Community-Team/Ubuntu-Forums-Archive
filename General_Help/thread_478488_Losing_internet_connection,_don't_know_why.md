### Post by weblordpepe on 2007-06-23
Well is it the connection between the dsl modem and internet that is acting up?

You might need to call your ISP then. If you can still access your dsl router (eg through the web GUI) while the internet is down, then its most likely the modem being silly.

If you need to call your helpdesk, don't tell them you're running Linux. ISP helpdesks have a silly thing with assuming its your application settings and so forth and will probably tell you to go Network Neighbourhood under My Computer.
