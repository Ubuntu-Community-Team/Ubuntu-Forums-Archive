### Post by QIII on 2010-07-30
While you do not deserve criticism, there is a valid point which may bear some explanation.

We are all here voluntarily as time allows.  A few hours is precious  little time for the right person to see your problem and respond.  He  may live in Bangalore and be asleep at the time you post.  The forum  policy is 24 hours for a bump for that sort of reason.

"Your aren't helping so I am going back to Windows" is, as you can imagine, not the sort of thing that would endear you to someone who would otherwise be willing to help.

You are certainly having a problem that is frustrating for you and we  should be helping.  However, bear in mind that it is not a universal  problem.  Remember that people come to forums like this seeking answers  to problems when things go wrong, not when they go right.  Thus, it is  not logical to assume that there is a universal or even frequent problem  because 20 people post here with the same problem  (Hasty Generalization). There may be  millions of people for whom everything is just fine, but since you don't see them you might conclude that there are none for whom it is working  (Argument from Silence). I'm one of the latter cases.  I don't come here to complain because things are working.

That does not invalidate your problem, of course.  You are having difficulty and that is what matters to you.

Let's look at one machine in isolation and see if we can sort this out.

Can you give us the specs on that one machine?

The specs on your router and modem?

Any applications you may have installed?

Which browser you are using?
