### Post by ajcham on 2009-01-11
Right-click on the panel, select 'Add to Panel'.

### Post by mcduck on 2009-01-11
Right-click on empty space in your panel, select "Add to Panel", find the "User Switcher"-applet and drag&drop it into your panel.

Now you can just left-click the applet and select "Guest session" from the menu.
