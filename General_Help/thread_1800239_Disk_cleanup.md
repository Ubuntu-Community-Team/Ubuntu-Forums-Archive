### Post by ajgreeny on 2011-07-08
> **TeoBigusGeekus said:**
> No defrag needed for linux filesystems.

Bleachbit is a CCleaner linux equivalent - you can install it from the Software Center.
I advise you to be very careful with bleachbit, or any other disk cleaner software, including the system default, Computer janitor, as they can all remove things you may have installed manually, and therefore want to keep.  Run them, by all means, but look at the list of supposedly un-needed packages, and make sure you deselect and keep any you wish to leave on the system.
