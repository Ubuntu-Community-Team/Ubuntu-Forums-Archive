### Post by linux4me on 2012-01-21
Some BIOS offer utilities that will shut down or limit fan speed when the system doesn't require maximum cooling and various other "green" things along those lines. Maybe check your motherboard user manual for options you can set to reduce power consumption.

If you're going to leave it on all night playing music, shutting the monitor off is about the only other thing I know of...
