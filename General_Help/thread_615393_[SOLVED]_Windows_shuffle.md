### Post by doctorgreg on 2007-11-17
Go to the Compiz Settings manager then click on Animations. Once in there click on the focus animation tab.  Click on the entry in there and then click edit once you have the entry highlighted.  Then where it says focus effect, choose DODGE from that menu.  Then you might have to restart the GL Desktop to have it take effect.  Enjoy.
