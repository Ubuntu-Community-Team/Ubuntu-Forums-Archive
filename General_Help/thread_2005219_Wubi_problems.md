### Post by na5h on 2012-06-17
It's really quite easy! :) Just head over to Ubuntu.com and download the latest CD image:
[http://www.ubuntu.com/download/desktop](http://www.ubuntu.com/download/desktop)

Then, simply follow the step-by-step instructions below!

Burning the ISO file to a CD/DVD:
[http://www.ubuntu.com/download/help/burn-a-cd-on-ubuntu](http://www.ubuntu.com/download/help/burn-a-cd-on-ubuntu)

Installing:
[http://www.ubuntu.com/download/help/install-ubuntu-desktop](http://www.ubuntu.com/download/help/install-ubuntu-desktop)


Edit: please note that a fresh install will overwrite your current wubi-installation (if you choose to erase and use the entire hard-drive for your new installation...which was the point, right?). There ARE also ways to migrate an existing wubi-installation to a "real" one...but it's easier to just make a fresh install.
REMEMBER TO ALWAYS BACK UP ALL YOUR IMPORTANT DATA BEFORE INSTALLING/UPGRADING YOUR SYSTEM!
