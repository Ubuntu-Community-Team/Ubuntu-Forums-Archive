### Post by Quackers on 2010-09-22
When I first installed 64 bit Lucid Lynx my webcam (Sony VCC6 or something like that) worked straight away. Unfortunately after a couple of updates it stopped working. I tried a few remedies but to no avail.
I just tried another Google search and came up with this from Launchpad
[https://launchpad.net/~r5u87x-loader/+archive/ppa](https://launchpad.net/~r5u87x-loader/+archive/ppa)
and all is well with the world again :-) I can see my ugly beak staring back at me when I use Cheese.
It seems to work on some HP laptops too (with cameras based on the Ricoh R5U87x chipset.
Thank you Launchpad people!
