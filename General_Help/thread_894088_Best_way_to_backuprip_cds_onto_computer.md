1. What type of files are they jpegs, pdf, etc....

For example:
Transferring files from a cd can easily be done in you Nautilus File browser. Copy and paste, etc... However you may wish to divide your files up into different folders based on preference. If 50 CD's have jpgs files, you may wish to place them into a jpg folder with sub folders defining each CD.
