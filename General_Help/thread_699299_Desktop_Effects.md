### Post by drs305 on 2008-02-17
System, Preferences, Advanced Desktop Effect Settings. Among the popular ones, Desktop Cube, rotate cube, and viewport switcher. Once you enable them, you can tweak the settings by double clicking on the ones you have selected. After you get things to your taste, I recommend saving the compiz settings by double clicking Preferences on the lower left side of the Adv Desktop Settings menu and saving the profile.
