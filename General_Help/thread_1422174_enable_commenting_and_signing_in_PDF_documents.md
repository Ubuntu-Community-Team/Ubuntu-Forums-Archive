### Post by darolu on 2010-03-05
I don't really use Adobe Reader 9, I make my pdf files with openoffice.org, but I have had problems with DRM'ed PDF's in the past, the easiest way to circumvent this restrictions is using Gmail's "view as HTML" and copy/paste it to a new file. In openoffice.org you just have to click on save as... and choose PDF, after that you can open it with Adobe Reader 9 and add the comments and sign it.
