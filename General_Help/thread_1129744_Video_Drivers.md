Open Synaptic (System - Administration - Synaptic), type your password.
Click Search and type Envy
There will be 3 packages found. Click the little square boxes at the start of the lines and choose Mark for Installation for EnvyNg core and EnvyNg gtk.
Click Apply in the menu bar
EnvyNg will now be installed. When done stop Synaptic

Click Applications - Systemtools - EnvyNg
Choose Ati in the left box and "Install the Ati driver (Automatic Hardware Detection)"
When done Reboot and all should be well.
Success.
