### Post by gettinoriginal on 2008-12-22
First of all, welcome to Ubuntu, this site can give you all the help you need to get started. :P

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=801404](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=801404)

As far as Compiz is concerned, go to:

System > Administration > Synaptic Package Manager (it will ask for your password)

In the search box at the top, type CompizConfig-Settings-Manager.  When you see it check it, and then click Apply at the top.  After it installs, you will find it under System > Preferences > CompizConfig Settings Manager.
