If I want to record background music I just need to select the **monitor** of the source in SimpleScreenRecorder. 
eg If I want to play a background music track, I just play a music track in clementine out to my desktop speakers and
choose "**[COLOR="#FF0000"]Monitor[/COLOR] of Built-in Audio Analog Stereo**" (Use "monitor" to record the output of other applications)
[ATTACH=CONFIG]256409[/ATTACH] [ATTACH=CONFIG]256412[/ATTACH]
If I want a background voice over I check my headset mic level in sound settings and then chose  
"**ClearChat Pro USB Analogue Mono**" as the source in  SimpleScreenRecorder.
You can see if sound is working before you start recording by hitting the preview button in SSR.
[ATTACH=CONFIG]256410[/ATTACH]  [ATTACH=CONFIG]256411[/ATTACH]
