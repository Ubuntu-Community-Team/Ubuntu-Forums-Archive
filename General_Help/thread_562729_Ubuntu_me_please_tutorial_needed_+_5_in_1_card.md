What kind of card reader is it? Is this card reader built into the hardware or external?
Have you checked with its manufactuer about Linux drivers?

The networking configuration in Ubuntu is pretty friendly. You have to go into Networking and when prompted for a password use the password for the user account that installed the system.

I suggest you concentrate on getting your networking working, and then worry about the card reader.
