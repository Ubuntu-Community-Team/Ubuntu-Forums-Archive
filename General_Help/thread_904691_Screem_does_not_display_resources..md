### Post by ubi-wan on 2009-01-29
There are some patches available at [[COLOR="Blue"]sourceforge.net[/COLOR]]("https://sourceforge.net/tracker/index.php?func=detail&aid=2543273&group_id=142&atid=100142") to solve this issue. However, you have to compile the package from source, with those patches applied.

The sources can be downloaded from [[COLOR="Blue"]screem (0.16.1-4.2ubuntu1)[/COLOR]]("http://packages.ubuntu.com/source/intrepid/screem"). If you feel yourself unfamiliar with compiling :(, read [[COLOR="#0000ff"]"Compiling Software"[/COLOR]]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/CompilingSoftware") or [[COLOR="#0000ff"]"Compiling Easy How-To"[/COLOR]]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/CompilingEasyHowTo").

I also recommend the visit of the [[COLOR="#0000ff"]"Report Bugs"[/COLOR]]("http://sourceforge.net/tracker/?group_id=142&atid=100142") page on the project's site at [[COLOR="#0000ff"]Screem HTML-XML Editor: About[/COLOR]]("http://www.screem.org/").
