### Post by RajivNair on 2006-04-10
go to "Search For Files" option in the "Places" tab on the desktop.Now click on "Select More Options",and in the "Available Options" tab,select "Show Hidden and Backup Files",now finally click on "Add" button and then do the search.

### Post by justleen on 2006-04-10
find / -name .*  searches for everthing hidden.. (might not want to search the whole root, so you can use ./ instead of /)
