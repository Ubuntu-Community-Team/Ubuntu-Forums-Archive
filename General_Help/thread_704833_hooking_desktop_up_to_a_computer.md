### Post by stevejesus on 2008-02-22
First, you have to understand what all of those colors mean...  Yellow, Red and White are Composite cables.  The red and yellow ones provides an audio connection, though the work the same as the yellow.  They are just color coded so that a normal person will match them up on each end.  If you are using s-video, then just go out and buy an s-video cable.  Have you googled "long s-video cable"?
