### Post by tubasoldier on 2006-11-14
I'm trying to switch to gnome on my laptop after being a huge KDE user. There are some things I like and some things that still bug me to no end. One of these are very small and I assume I can change it. I hate that my session locks every time I close my laptop screen. I have looked all over the preferences dialogs and can not figure out where I would disable it. I only want my session to lock when I want to lock it. Not every time I close my screen.

---

### Post by Phatfiddler on 2006-11-14
System > Preferences > Power Management

Under the **Actions** section

"When laptop is closed:"

Choices:  Do nothing, blank screen, suspend, hibernate
