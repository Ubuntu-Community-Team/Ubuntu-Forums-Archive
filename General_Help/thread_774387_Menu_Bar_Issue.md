### Post by munkyeetr on 2008-04-29
1) *Right-click* on the icon and *uncheck* **Lock To Panel**
2) Again *right-click* on the icon and choose **Move**
3) Drag it to where you want and *left-click*
4) *Right-click* and *check* **Lock To Panel**

### Post by munkyeetr on 2008-04-29
As a sidenote, which I just found out, you don't have to re-*right-click* to choose **Move**, you can just *left-click* and *drag* the icon to where you want after you've unlocked it. Saves a step!
