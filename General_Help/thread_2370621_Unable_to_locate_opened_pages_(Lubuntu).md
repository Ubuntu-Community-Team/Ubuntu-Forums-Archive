### Post by Rex Bouwense on 2017-09-09
ray9: What ajgreeny was referring to was the Task bar (Windows list).  If you right click any open space on the LXPanel you will be able to select the "Task Bar (Windows List)" Settings.  That will open a new window which has the option of combining multiple application windows into a single button.  Make sure that option is not checked and your problem should go away.
