### Post by Kyral on 2005-10-23
I second the disabling the Onboard in BIOS

Also a really fun trick to get Multimedia keys working across the board in GNOME is to fire up the Preferences -> Keyboard Shortcuts and assign them manually to the functions. A little tedious, but easy to do. Even allows you to customize them :D
