### Post by Ddaannkkoo on 2009-11-23
Problem solved! At least for me (:

When I chose Serbian as deafult language, I didn't realise that the keyboard layout was cyrillic by deafult, so I all I needed to do is to choose Serbian(latin) from the keyboard layout menu (bottom of the log-in screen).
