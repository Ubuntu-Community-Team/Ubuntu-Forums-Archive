### Post by Tamlynmac on 2009-07-19
I use a Logitech Model M-RCE95 wireless mouse. If I recall correctly it was purchased at Wal-Mart - not trying to advertise for WM. Only acknowledging where I purchased the unit. I have no idea if it's available from any other store or online.
