### Post by souled on 2006-01-14
This isn't really a solution to your problem, but I would suggest you get [hellanzb]("http://www.hellanzb.com/trac/") Hellanzb is a CLI nzb processor. It will download your NZBs, check the pars, and unrar the archives if necessary. It's not a newsreader, so you won't be able to download headers and stuff, but this program is well worth the install.
