### Post by nkri on 2008-08-20
Yes, you certainly can do this:).  

Go into CompizConfig Settings Manager; if you don't have it installed, ```
sudo apt-get install compizconfig-settings-manager
```

Go into the animations plugin, and click the "Open Animation" tab.

Click the "New" button under Animation Selection.

Change the Open Effect from None to Burn.

The duration looks good somewhere around 150, but feel free to play around with it and find what you like the most.

In the Window Match box, put > type=DropdownMenu

Now click close and select the entry you just made (it should be the last one on the list).  Click the Up button until the entry is above the two that say Fade.

Now you should have burning menus...good luck!
-nkri
