I"m not entirely sure I understand what you are asking... but I think it has to do with mounting ISOs and the like... 

Via a terminal:
mount -o loop /path/to/file.iso /path/to/mount/point

There's also a nice how-to here; to create a nautilus script action to mount ISOs with a right click:
[http://www.ubuntugeek.com/mount-isos-easely-in-gnome-nautilus.html](http://www.ubuntugeek.com/mount-isos-easely-in-gnome-nautilus.html)

And if you need a front-end; there's always Xarchiver:
[http://www.gnomefiles.org/app.php/Xarchiver](http://www.gnomefiles.org/app.php/Xarchiver)
