### Post by zbirdman777 on 2010-02-19
I have an XPS M1530 and even though its probably out of your price range, I'd just like to note 1 important thing.

Make sure you don't have an Intel Wireless card. I've used Ubuntu on my Laptop since 8.04 and my Wireless card has never worked correctly with WPA or WPA 2 Enterprise. (although its gotten better.) It connects and disconnects constantly.

Everything else works beautifully. Even bluetooth and my computers remote control.
