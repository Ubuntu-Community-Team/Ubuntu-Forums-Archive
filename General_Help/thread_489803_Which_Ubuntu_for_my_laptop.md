### Post by Songwind on 2007-07-01
What are your options as far as upgrading the RAM goes?  Your processor is more than fine to run any version of Ubuntu, but with only 224Mb of RAM, GNOME and KDE are going to be less than snappy.  Though Gnome would probably be sort of okay.

RAM isn't that expensive these days, if the system uses standard laptop DIMMS.  Bump that sucker up to 512 or 1024Mb and you'd be completely set.
