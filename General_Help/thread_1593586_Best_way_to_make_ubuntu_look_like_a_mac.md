### Post by roggenschrotbrot on 2010-10-11
never used it (i think osx looks rather ugly), but [http://gnome-look.org/content/show.php/Mac4Lin+Leopard+Emerald+Theme?content=68409]("http://gnome-look.org/content/show.php/Mac4Lin+Leopard+Emerald+Theme?content=68409") is the most mac-like theme afaik, though you would most likely be better off with elementary if you don't need to mimic it on the pixel. [omgubuntu]("http://www.omgubuntu.co.uk/") posted a tutoial on how to get a quite macish look and feel today, you might want to check it once they are back online.

edit: as macbuntu describes itself as a successor of mac4lin you should use it instead. have a look at elementary nonetheless, it follows the same philosophy and is a lot more polished imho.
