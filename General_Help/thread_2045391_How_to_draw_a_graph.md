### Post by mcduck on 2012-08-21
Those equations would actually give two different possible results, so you'll need to figure those out first, giving you two functions you can then use to draw the graphs.

Using my favourite calculator, [Qalculate!]("http://qalculate.sourceforge.net/"), I threw the equations into the calculator first to get the two possible functions, then went to File->Plot Functions/Data and added the resulting functions to the Expression-field and clicked the "Add" button.

I hope this helps a bit.
