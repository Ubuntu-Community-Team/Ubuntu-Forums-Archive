### Post by 3rdalbum on 2009-09-20
> **sunchiqua said:**
> Drag-n-drop it into Appearance settings ( Themes tab ).

Clarification: Download the .tar.gz that has the GTK and Metacity themes, and drag the archive onto the Themes tab of the Appearance window.

You might need to then click on the "Customise" button and choose the controls and window theme.

Don't decompress the .tar.gz archive, just drag it all on there.
