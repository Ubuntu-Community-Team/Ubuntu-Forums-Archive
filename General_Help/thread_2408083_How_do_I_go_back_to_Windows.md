### Post by Quarkrad on 2018-12-14
As QIII  said above, just download the latest Win10 ISO from M'Soft and backup all your valuable data before re-installing.  Burn the ISO to a DVD and pop it into your pc drive - on boot up you will be stepped through the process of re-installing win10.
