### Post by paladin677 on 2010-02-23
No.  There is no real better way to convert PDFs to the epub format.  For plain txt the conversion is okay but images do not convert well at all.  I found this out after I purchased my PRS-600.  

I would recommend visiting the website [http://www.mobileread.com](http://www.mobileread.com) for more information on this subject.
