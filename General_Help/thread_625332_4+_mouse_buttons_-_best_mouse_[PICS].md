### Post by bimmerd00d on 2007-11-28
btnx working like a champeen on my MX revolution.  search btnx on here and follow the guide to the T.  Make sure you have the newest version that allows revoco to handle auto-scroll for the wheel.  Also, it's worth noting that the scroll wheel does not have a click button, clicking the wheel changes the scroll-mode.  However, there's a button just behind the wheel that i mapped to the middle click and i'm getting used to it.

### Post by daou on 2007-11-28
With the latest version of btnx, you can use the revoco settings to manipulate your wheel scroll. A lot of possibilites, including getting the middle click to actually do a middle click and not change modes. (For example, set revoco to Free-scroll mode, restart btnx, then go back and detect the middle click).

Being the developer of btnx, I can't say much about it, biased as I am. Suffice to say, I use it myself.

I recommend the MX Revolution. It's the best mouse my hand has ever rested on. And after using smooth scroll, no mouse wheel will ever feel the same. It takes a bit to get used to, though. The VX lacks some functionality on its wheel, and it's a bit too small to hold comfortably.
