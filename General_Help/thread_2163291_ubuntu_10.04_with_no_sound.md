### Post by Irihapeti on 2013-07-17
Once a release has reached end of support, there will be fewer and fewer people who will be able to help with problems with that release. That is, in addition to the fact that it's no longer getting security updates.

If later releases don't appeal because of Unity, you do have the option of other DEs. Xubuntu is popular with quite a few users, for example, and may be worth investigating.
