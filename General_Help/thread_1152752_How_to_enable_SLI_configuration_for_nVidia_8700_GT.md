### Post by darryn.smith on 2009-05-10
ensure your using nvidia driver. (not nv)

In /etc/X11/xorg.conf, under Section Device for nVidia0 & and nVidia1 add:

Option "SLI" "AFR" 

(I'm told AFR improved frame rates for some users)
