### Post by jbo5112 on 2008-01-13
Well, since the thread has been revived (and emulation mentioned), [[COLOR="Blue"]KEGS[/COLOR]]("http://kegs.sourceforge.net/") is a native Apple II GS emulator for linux that runs well.  I'm not completely sure what the status is on their GUI, but it would be a lot better than emulating an emulator.  The game ROM can be found at [[COLOR="Blue"]classicgaming[/COLOR]]("http://classicgaming.gamespy.com/View.php?view=GameMuseum.Detail&id=266").  You're on your own for the ROM from the Apple.

There's also some version of Oregon Trail on [[COLOR="Blue"]facebook[/COLOR]]("http://www.facebook.com"), and probably some other sites as well.  My wife is playing it right now.
