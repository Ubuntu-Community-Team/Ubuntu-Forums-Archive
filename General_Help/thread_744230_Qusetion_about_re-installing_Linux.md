### Post by danwood76 on 2008-04-04
> **Irony said:**
> Hard drives have a rating as to how many times they can be written to, you are unlikely to reach it by installing operating systems.

Not really, they have ratings as to how many times they can start up, park and so on but effectivly the platters can be written and re written infinite times, the mechanics are sure to fail before the platters do.

Flash disks have physical limitations as to the number of writes as flash memory can only be re written 1 million times before it dies.
