### Post by btindie on 2010-10-16
If you click on "Files & Folders", then click on 'Documents' for example, in the top right corner there's a new button from where you can access Nautilus. When you press the 'Delete' key it moves it to the "Rubbish Bin"/"Wastebasket". On the 'Preferences' menu of the Nautilus, if you go to the 'Behaviour' tab you can tell it to include a delete option that bypasses the Rubbish Bin. Or just "shift-delete", it does prompt you to make sure you know what you're doing...
