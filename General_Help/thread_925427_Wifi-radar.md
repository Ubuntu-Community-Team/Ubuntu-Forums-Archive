### Post by fooman on 2008-09-20
is your windows partition mounted in ubuntu (is there an icon on the desktop)?

if not there then click on "places" in your top panel and see if the partition is listed there. 

wherever it is...double click on it to open the partition in nautilus...then browse to where you put the file in windows.  right click on it and choose "copy".  then just right click on your ubuntu desktop and choose "paste".

...or just "drag & drop" it onto the desktop.

after that, you can place it somewhere in your home folder and install.
