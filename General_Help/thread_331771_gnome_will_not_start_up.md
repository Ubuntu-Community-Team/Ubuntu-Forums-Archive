### Post by Bigbluecat on 2007-01-05
I had a similar problem although I think it was related to Beryl. When I selected AIGLX/Beryl and logged in I got just a plain brown screen and no more.

When I selected gnome it logged in but there was no window manager (no borders, could not move or resize windows).

It was resolved by opening a terminal and entering the following:

> metacity --replace
