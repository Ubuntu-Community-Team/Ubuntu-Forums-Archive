### Post by mb_webguy on 2009-04-30
+1

I haven't actually read *Linux in a Nutshell*, but I've been impressed with other O'Reilly books I've purchased, and the reviews of that one are pretty good.

As for Ubuntu-specific books, Mark Sobell's *Practical Guide to Ubuntu Linux* is pretty popular, as is Keir Thomas' *Beginning Ubuntu Linux*.  And of course there's the *Official Ubuntu Book* though I don't know how deeply it delves into the command line.
