### Post by eternalnewbee on 2008-11-24
> Im looking for an alternate menu, instead of the "Applications Places System"
I don't know about kmenu, but in Gnome you can delete the **Menu Bar** by right-clicking on it, and choosing **Remove From Panel**.
Then, right-click on the panel, and choose Add to Panel. Scroll down to **Main Menu**, and click **Add**. That's it.
Hope this helps.
