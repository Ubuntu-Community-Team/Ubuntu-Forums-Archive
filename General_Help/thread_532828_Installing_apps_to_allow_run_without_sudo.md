### Post by RealityDitch on 2007-08-23
HA, I just figured it out.  never mind.  you just go to launch menu for the item you want to launch without having to goto terminal and right click then right click and click edit menu item, and once dialog opens, check the box for "Run as a different user" but keep the name field empty.  then click save.
