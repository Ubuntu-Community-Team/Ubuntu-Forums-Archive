### Post by nami on 2008-08-21
If you go into the screen resolution setup screen, you should be able to enable 2 monitors, you may need to uncheck the clone option.  Or you may need to move one monitor icon down as they sometimes appear one on top of the other which results in the other monitor icon becoming hidden.  Once you can see both monitor icons, you should set your laptop resolution to off, and your external monitor to the correct resolution.

When done, accept the changes and it should work.  Worked for me.
