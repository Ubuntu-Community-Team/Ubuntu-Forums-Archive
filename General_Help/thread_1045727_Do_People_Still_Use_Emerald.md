### Post by germclown on 2009-03-06
I currently use Emerald mostly because I like being able to rearrange my buttons (and have stick and set-above buttons; Metacity NEEDS THESE IMMEDIATELY). Some of the built-in engines are actually really nice if you set them up right. I have yet to find a simpler pixmap engine for anything as well.

I hate, hate, hate (!) its shadows though. It does this weird thing where the top is darker than the bottom, and its gradient isn't as natural. And if you try settings that don't look like OSX or Vista's shadows, they break very easily. Needed another config program in addition to Appearances and Compiz is also a big minus for less tech-savvy users and anyone who can't be bothered to keep track of which programs change what settings.

I'm actually going to learn to theme in Metacity for these reasons.
