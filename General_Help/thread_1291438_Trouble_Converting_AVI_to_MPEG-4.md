### Post by jeremyswalker on 2009-10-14
[Handbrake]("http://handbrake.fr/") is an excellent video encoder. It is easy to use, and offers a variety of codec and container options. Although you seem to just be looking for a different container, I believe there are settings to copy the video and audio, selecting MP4 as the container. Are you sure that the Sansa won't play the *.avi? If it won't, it may be a codec issue instead. Nonetheless, [Handbrake]("http://handbrake.fr/") will help you out.
