### Post by m83 on 2006-12-25
Try pressing Ctrl+Shift+NumLock. This will turn the keypad on the keyboard into the mouse, so you can control the mouse from keyboard. Keys / and * on the numpad select left mouse click and right mouse click respectively. Mouse click is done by 5 on the numpad.

You can find other really nice Linux keyboard shortcuts at this URL: [Linux keyboard shortcuts you should know about]("http://blog.i64.pl/PiosBlog/200610/29-linux-keyboard-shortcuts-you-should-know-about/").
