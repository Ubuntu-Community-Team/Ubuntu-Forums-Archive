### Post by jvc26 on 2010-04-30
Right, by means of a check, just do:

```
cat /etc/group | grep admin
```

If your username isnt on the line provided, it looks like you've been dropped off the list of admin users. Psychocats have a good tutorial on fixing sudo:

[http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/fixsudo](http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/fixsudo)
