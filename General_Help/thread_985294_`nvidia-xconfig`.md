### Post by fooman on 2008-11-17
open a terminal (applications > accessories > terminal) and type or copy/paste the following:

```
sudo nvidia-xconfig
```

enter your password when prompted.

then press alt-ctrl-backspace to logout and restart the x-session by logging in again.
