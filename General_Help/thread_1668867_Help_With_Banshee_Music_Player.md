### Post by Dennis_D_Menniz on 2011-01-17
I had a similar issue; I'm an Ubuntu n00b, too.  My PC is dual boot and I copied my Win mp3's into /home/music.  Banshee scanned both copies of each mp3, so when I unmounted my Win disk, Banshee still had both copies in its library.  The best thing to do is to select all your music in Banshee then right click and pick Remove from library.  Don't worry, it won't delete your mp3's unless you pick Delete from drive.  Let Banshee delete everything then Media, Import media, Choose folders and point it to wherever you have your mp3's.
