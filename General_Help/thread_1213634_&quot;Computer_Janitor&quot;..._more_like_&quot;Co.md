### Post by fwaokda on 2009-07-15
I just wanted to make a recommendation and also note something in case others unintentionally get blindsided by this crappy excuse for a ubuntu app.  I went to "Computer Janitor" after installing countless apps thinking (because of it's name) that it was going to clean up unused/uneeded packages, files, etc etc

I soon found out I was dead-wrong in my assumption.  Now I consider myself quite a newb to Ubuntu which is self evident here, but I consider myself also a targeted user for Ubuntu... So, let me recommend the following:

1. Change the name of this program from "Computer Janitor" as it implies, I believe, that it's going to clean up unused applications and other things of the sort.

2. If your going to keep the application - please - at least somewhere put a warning or caution.  Now I realize that it had one prior to me removing some of the packages but it didn't sound to serious, at least for a newb like myself.

I'm still trying to repair the damages after 2 hours of work.  Just when I think I've got it all fix - I find something else that it damaged.

Please consider doing something about this in future releases for the sake of all the newbs! :(
