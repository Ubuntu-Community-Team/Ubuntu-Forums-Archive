### Post by x-shaney-x on 2010-12-23
I was a long time firefox user since the early days (firebird, betas etc) but in frustration at it's ever growing bloat and clunkiness I looked for a replacement and eventually settled for chromium.

Although I wasn't keen at first I grew to love chromium and it became my browser for the future.

However, things have changed.
In recent weeks it seems to be suffering from more and more problems, in particular:

1.  It randomly forgets usernames and passwords for websites.
It has remembered these for months on end then suddenly forgets them.
I rely on my browser to remember them for me since I often forget which username I have used for different sites and use some complicated passwords.

2.  Flash vids have become very flaky.  I often go to view a vid on YouTube and just get a black box.  Some play, some don't and it seems completely random.  If I load the ones that don't work into firefox, they work EVERY time.

3.  In the past couple of days web fonts seem a bit off.  Some look blocky, some look spindly but all looked perfect before.

I don't know if the official Google Chrome has these problems but I won't use that anyway for reasons I won't go into here.
