### Post by darkmatter on 2005-10-08
There are many alternatives.

One of my favorites is [ROX](http://rox.sourceforge.net/phpwiki/static.html). But other alternatives include Tux Commander, Krusader (a KDE app, but runs well under GNOME) and the classic Midnight Commander (another favorite)

There are others. But I could spend all day naming them.

For a comprehensive list, see.

[http://freshmeat.net/browse/860/](http://freshmeat.net/browse/860/)

Many of those are available through Synaptic, but not all.
