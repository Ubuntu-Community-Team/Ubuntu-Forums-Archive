### Post by BillDozer on 2007-09-15
This is one of the features I love in Amarok. It is totally doable.

You have to use dynamic playlists in Amarok. When you use these your playlist will be automatically filled and you can see what the Amarok has chosen to play, not only the next song, but x number of songs.

The best way to do this is to create a smart playlist with the types of songs you would like to hear, fx from a specific genre. And then create a dynamic playlist to use this playlist. And activate the Dynamic playlist. Your playlist will now be automagically populated with random songs from your smart playlist, and you can delete and or rearrange the following songs. If you delete a song, a new song appears automatically at the end of your playlist.
