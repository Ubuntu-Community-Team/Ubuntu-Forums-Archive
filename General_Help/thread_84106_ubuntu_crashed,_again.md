### Post by towsonu2003 on 2005-10-30
[QUOTE=autonomy]I came back to my computer after cooking and saw the login screen and cussed. [/QUOTE]

Hmmm, how long were you away? may be it went to suspend or something and it is well possible that suspend (or sleep, or even the screensavers) is not supported in your pavilion. I use blank screen as screensaver and no suspend/power off (System>preferences>screensaver>advanced  - power display management unchecked) to avoid that stuff... well, that's up to how good your video hardware is supported i guess?

[QUOTE=autonomy]
All I had running was Rhythmbox, Firefox, and Evolution. I have a hp pavilion.
[/QUOTE]

Was it playing music? maybe the file is corrupted somehow (very low probability)? Try testing with the same conditions (open those programs, sit and wait to see what it is doing). You can also look at the logs (Application>system tools>system log) to see whether something gave an error towards the date+time it crashed.

[QUOTE=autonomy]

uhm, which windows (their names)? try closing all windows before logging out
