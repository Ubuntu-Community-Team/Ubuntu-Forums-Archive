### Post by mocoloco on 2010-08-24
At the login screen, click your name, and then at the bottom of the screen change the session from "GNOME" to "Failsafe GNOME".  If that works then your problem is probably either a startup application or it's something bad in your configuration for gnome or some of it's components.
