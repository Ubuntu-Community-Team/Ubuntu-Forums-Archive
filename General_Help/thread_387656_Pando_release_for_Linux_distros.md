### Post by mhenriday on 2007-03-18
A week or so ago, I received an answer to a letter I sent to the people behind **Pando** a month earlier :

[INDENT]Hello,

 You wrote to us in the past asking for Pando version for Linux.

I am glad to inform you a Pando Linux Downloader (v0.9 Beta) is now available!

You can get it here:

[http://www.pando.com/phpbb/viewtopic.php?p=11555#11555](http://www.pando.com/phpbb/viewtopic.php?p=11555#11555)

 Please keep sending us feedback,

Shlomit[/INDENT]

When I tried to follow the installation instructions provided on the page linked to above by typing in the command «tar xvfj pandodl-xx.tar.bz2» into my terminal, I received a failure notice to the effect that no such file or catalogue exists. But that's about par for the course for me, and I am sure that other **Ubuntu** users who enjoy a better relation with their terminals that that I seem to have will be able to follow the instructions and succeed in downloading and installing the programme. During my **Windows** period, I found **Pando** very valuable when sending longer files - in particular ones containing music, and I know the issue was previously taken up in this forum, about a year ago. So good luck - and I'd be grateful to anyone who succeeds in installing the programme for a step-by-step tutorial !...

Henri

PS : Just in case any developer's listening, **Pando** would seem to be an obvious candidate for inclusion in **Feisty**'s **Synaptic Package Manager**....
