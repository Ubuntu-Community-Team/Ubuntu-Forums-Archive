### Post by sanderd17 on 2011-05-22
There are two supported upgrade paths. From LTS to LTS or from release to release. 

So you can upgrade from 10.04 to 12.04 (the next LTS) or from 10.04 to 10.10 (the next release), but the following is IMPOSSIBLE:

10.04 -> 10.10 -> 12.04.

If you upgraded 10.04 to 10.10 and you would like to go to 12.04, then you have to follow the path 

10.04 -> 10.10 -> 11.04 -> 11.10 -> 12.04

This forces you to do a lot of upgrades an probably one of the upgrades will break some settings. You will also have to download a lot more.

So I would stay with 10.04 until 12.04 comes out (it's less than one year away).
