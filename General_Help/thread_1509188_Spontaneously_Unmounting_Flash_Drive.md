### Post by ottosykora on 2010-08-16
did you think of simple hardware problem?

The usb connectors do not last for ever. The actual specs of the connector are 500 insertion cycles. OK, if reasonably done, they will last much longer, but not for ever.
Very often problems are with the connector in the computer, particularly laptops, soldered directly in surface mount to the main board. Freakvent use will brak one of the solder joints and then exactly those symptoms ocure.
