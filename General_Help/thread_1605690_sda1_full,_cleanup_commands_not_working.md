### Post by coffeecat on 2010-10-25
Simple answer: your root partition (sda1) is too small. Even with /home on a separate partition, 5.6GB is only enough for a fairly lean installation as far as apps go. You have 4GB in /usr which means you must have installed quite a few apps since you first installed 8.04. I see you have both gnome and kde stuff there. Do you have both desktops or just some kde stuff in a gnome installation or vice versa? Both desktops would take up a lot of room.

Do you have room to expand sda1? Because the alternative would be to uninstall quite a few apps. Uninstalling unwanted kernels might help but won't give you a dramatic amount of extra space.
