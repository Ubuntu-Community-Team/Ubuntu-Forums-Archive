### Post by ajgreeny on 2010-06-29
The router you use is unlikely to make a big difference, the difficulty is more usually the wireless adapter in your computer, and this is often the reason you need a wired connection first; to get updates which allow wireless connections to be made with updated, downloaded wireless drivers.
