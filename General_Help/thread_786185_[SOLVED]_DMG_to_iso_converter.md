### Post by fangorious on 2008-07-05
Last time I tried MagicISO this functionality wasn't in the free version. Look for AcetoneISO. DMG files are compressed (file myimage.dmg should say something like VAX COFF executable) so renmaing to .iso doesn't work. It's the cdr files that you can rename, or use Brasero or K3B to burn without renaming.
