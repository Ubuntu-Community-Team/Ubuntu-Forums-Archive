### Post by tuxcantfly on 2007-05-09
It's only supposed to take around 15 min. - 1 hour on hardware that's relatively recent (4 years old or newer) hardware, maybe 2 hours on really old stuff. Your install times appear to be WAY too long. How much RAM does your computer have? I've noticed that if there's any less than 256MB or so, Ubuntu installs take a really long time. You might also have a horribly fragmented hard drive, maybe defragging might help (though I think it's probably something with the RAM or hardware incompatibilities)
