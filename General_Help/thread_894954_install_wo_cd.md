### Post by falcon61102 on 2008-08-20
If you computer is fairly newer, you should be able to boot from a USB drive, you may just need to turn it on in the BIOS.  Have you attempted to do that.

If not, when you start up your computer your should see that you need to hit one of the function keys (F1-F12) to enter SETUP.  Press that key and take a look at all your BIOS settings, you may/should have one for booting from USB devices.
