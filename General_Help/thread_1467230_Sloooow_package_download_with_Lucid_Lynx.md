Indeed, its been the same for me and some others on the forum.  Just a sign of Ubuntu's popularity!  You can check that you're on the fastest mirror though.  From Synaptic, go to Settings>Repositories>Ubuntu Software then click on "Download From">"Other">"Select Best Server".
