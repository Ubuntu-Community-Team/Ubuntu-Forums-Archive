### Post by helloworld1215 on 2010-09-29
Installing man for me has caused dpkg to get stuck in setting up shared-mime-info. Any future apt-get installations cause dpkg to try to process shared-mime-info and fail with a segmentation fault. The only way to fix dpkg is to remove shared-mime-info.
