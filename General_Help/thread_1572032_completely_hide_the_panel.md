is there any way to edit the gnome panel so it can be completely hidden?  the most that it hides is 1 pixel.

### Post by coffeecat on 2010-09-10
Right-click on the panel and choose 'Properties'. Under the General Tab, tick the autohide box. The panel will then hide itself until you move the mouse pointer near it. Is this what you wanted?

### Post by coffeecat on 2010-09-10
I believe the one pixel is there to indicate that there is a hidden panel. It is possible to delete the panel entirely so I guess the design decision was made to leave one pixel to make the distinction. I know of no way to eliminate that last pixel.
