### Post by Mark Phelps on 2010-10-01
One of the following:
1) Boot from Ubuntu, choosing LiveCD (Try it) mode.  Open the Partition Editor.  Ensure that Swap is turned off, and that the partition is unmounted.  Then use the tools to shrink the drive.
2) Download and burn a GParted LiveCD from distrowatch.com.  Do the same as above but you don't have to mess with swap or dismounting because the GParted LiveCD doesn't do those by default.
