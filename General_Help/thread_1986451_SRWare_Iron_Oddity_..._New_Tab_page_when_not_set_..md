Through another forum user I was made aware I could hit ctl+home and that would then take me to my home page, which it does, but a bore going to the New Tab Page first (time-waster and I like to ignore the OS and apps if possible and just work ... and play).

Stranger still is that in 'Settings>Basics>Homepage' I have the options 'Use the New Tab Page' or 'Open this Page' where I can add a URL, which I did, and that works as my homepage when I click the 'Home' icon.
