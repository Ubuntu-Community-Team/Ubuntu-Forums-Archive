Bring up system monitor. I'll bet that after the screensaver engages, and slows things down, you'll find very high cpu usage. Check it out. Then look at the processes and see which one is eating your cpu. My bet is 'udevd'.

You can use** sudo killall** **<processname>  **to get rid of the offending process: i.e.
**sudo killall udevd** if udevd turns out to be the culprit.
