### Post by sotiris2 on 2014-08-20
Try reading [this](http://www.tldp.org/LDP/Linux-Filesystem-Hierarchy/html/dev.html)

In short block special files are files representing block devices aka hard disks (and hard disk partitions) , cds usb sticks etc.  All files in /dev are not files in the sense you might know from windows (images ,text files , pdef) but rather represent devices on your PC.
