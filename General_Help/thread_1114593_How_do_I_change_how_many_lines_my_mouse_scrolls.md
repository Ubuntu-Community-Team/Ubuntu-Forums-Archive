### Post by pbpersson on 2009-04-02
Not that I know of but if you go into Firefox preferences under Advanced-->General you can enable autoscrolling.

Then you can click the mouse wheel and use your mouse to scroll through pages faster.

### Post by Seiji338 on 2009-04-03
Yes, this can be changed. Type **about:config** in Firefox's address bar. Change the value of **mousewheel.withnokey.sysnumlines** to **false**. Then change the value of **mousewheel.withnokey.numlines** to the number of lines you want to scroll, say **6**.
