### Post by zika on 2009-01-11
(if You are using compiz):
Alt-F2->ccsm->Utility->Workarounds->Legacy_Fullscreen_Support->uncheck
(FF 3.1b and 3.2a do not have that bug, Swiftweasel does not have it either)
