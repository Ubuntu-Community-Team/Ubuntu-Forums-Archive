### Post by GeorgeVita on 2010-01-01
Hi **Silvertones**, happy new year!

Best option is to buy the external modem!
If you have time to spend (resulting to linux knowledge) you can try your internal modem starting from:

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/DialupModemHowto/](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/DialupModemHowto/)
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=190728](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=190728)
[http://www.linmodems.org/](http://www.linmodems.org/)

The idea is to determine the chipset, then find appropriate driver for your kernel version and finally use a program to connect (ex. wvdial or gnome-ppp).

Post your progress to get more help from other dialup users.
