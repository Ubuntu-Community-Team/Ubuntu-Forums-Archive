What I mean by [obsolete]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Linux_kernel#Maintenance")
In any case, according to [http://kernel.org/](http://kernel.org/), the current version of the 2.6.32 line of the kernel is -56. Even though 10.04 is an LTS, I guess they may not keep up with recent kernel versions?

For instance, I am running 3.0.0.16 right now.

If you are on the latest kernel available in the default repos, you may want to try out [this ppa]("https://launchpad.net/%7Ekernel-ppa/+archive/ppa"), which will allow you to install the most recent kernels but "backported" for Lucid.
