### Post by papibe on 2011-06-17
Any graphic card?

Unity or Classic?

In general I would say that your CPU is fast enough to run anything. If were you, I'd increase the RAM to at least 2GB (mainly because of Firefox). If you don't have a graphic card for hardware acceleration, you'll get better performance if you choose the classic desktop instead of Unity.

Now, if I understood correctly your HD has around 1-2% of free space. That's too much too little. I would advice you to get a bigger HD, or move some files (maybe movies or music) to an external HD.

Just some thoughts, I hope it helps.

EDIT: as mike555 suggested, I also think the safest move would be to stay in 10.04.
