### Post by jorgecarrillo150990 on 2009-11-21
Umm, I believe that the bar you are looking for is not in Compiz and it is not a screenlet.

I believe it is Cairo-dock.

I'll list some awesome bars that I've tried and loved.
AWN - by far the most stable and has plenty of plugins, basic effects. (No Fish-eye Mac effect)
Cairo-Dock - is a little experimental and has some neat effects.
Gnome-Do - IMO the most simple to use, yet has so much utility. But to see it you have to install Gnome-Do and then click preferences and choose the "Docky" theme. Gnome-Do is the one I am currently using.

I hope this helps :)

### Post by jorgecarrillo150990 on 2009-11-21
And to install the computer icon you have to do a tremendous search and trust me, it is not worth the effort.

But, you can install (YOU MUST) a little app called Ubuntu Tweak, trust me, you will love it. There you can put the computer icon on your Desktop as easily as just checking a square.

It also helps you install repositories and cool apps.
