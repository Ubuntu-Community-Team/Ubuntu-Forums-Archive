My bedroom (in the making)

Just wanted to see what people thought of this and how would you do it and what would you change or add. 

Ok so my ideal bedroom.

The lights: 

Ok so the light switches are gone. Don’t need them as there will be a touch screen tablet in the wall for turning the lights on and off. Also when you walk into the room the your room picks up the Bluetooth on your phone and turns the lights on. You leave the room and say 5 minutes later lights go off. The reason for all this obviously because I can and because pressing a switch on a wall is way too much effort. 

When you watch a film the lights turn off and the nice blue LED lights in the 4 corners of the room turn on and just give a nice effect to the room. 

TV: 

Obviously I would love a nice big 54inch LCD with 100Hz 1080P and blue ray player but that will be a bit down the line. At the moment my 32 1080i will have to do. I would like to be able to turn the TV and change the change with the tablet pc or with a remote that that on the TV also controls the lights heating curtains all that. 

PC: 

Id say run Vmware on a HP home server quad core 8gb ram. Then from this run Ubuntu, Media server, and Linux MCE. This should serve as an organiser for all my music, films and TV needs. As well as a PC with Ubuntu, can also setup Vm’s for testing new versions of Linux. Handy!!

Also I would like a NAS server with 3 or 4 terabytes of space for films music and for recording TV. 

The Toilet:

Your watching a film and need to wee. as my room is down stairs I would be handy to have a toilet button that turns on the hall light landing light and bathroom light and then when you get back turns off all the lights and the film resumes. I know that’s lazy but could be worse could get a lift to bring me up the stairs and get Linux to flush my toilet. 

The curtains:

Would be idea to be able to control them and for them to close by themselves when it gets dark. But in them mornings when it get bright early not to open until a certain time. 

Anybody like the idea of the Linux bedroom or got anything to add.
