### Post by eqisow on 2006-06-04
I don't know about MP4, but you can definitely rip to MP3 (which the iPod supports) with Sound Juicer, just make sure you have *gstreamer8.0-lame* installed. As far as the ones that are already ogg, you can also convert those to MP3 with Sound Converter. However, re-encoding from one lossy format (ogg) to another lossy format (MP4) will result in quality loss.

Edit: Another option is to add ogg support to your iPod with [Rockbox]("http://www.rockbox.org/"). There's a nice article about Rockbox [here]("http://entertainment.newsforge.com/article.pl?sid=06/04/13/165223").
