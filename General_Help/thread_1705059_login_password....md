### Post by coffeecat on 2011-03-11
For autologin, System > Administration > Login Screen. Click on unlock and tick the login automatically box.

You don't ask this but the other thing that often irritates  people is the default screensaver setting. If you don't want to be nagged for your password just because you've left the machine for a few minutes to have a comfort break, go to System > Preferences > Screensaver, and untick the lock screen box.
