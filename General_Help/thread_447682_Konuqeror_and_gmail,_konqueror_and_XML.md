### Post by donnellymp on 2007-05-19
I have the same problem. A workaround is to set gmail.com as IE6/WinXP under Settings --> Configure Konqueror --> Browser Identification. Restart Konqueror, log in to Gmail, and you'll get a message about ActiveX controls. Just click the link for 'classic view' or something like that, and you can see your email the right way. This will get you working until someone else posts a fix. Cheers!
