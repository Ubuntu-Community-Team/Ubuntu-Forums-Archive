### Post by Keith1212 on 2010-03-06
I started here: [http://wiki.compiz.org/FAQ](http://wiki.compiz.org/FAQ)

Theres a link at the top for controls also.

Edit heres a better link that may be closer to what you are looking for. [http://wiki.compiz.org/CommonKeyboardShortcuts](http://wiki.compiz.org/CommonKeyboardShortcuts)

Other than that you can type "ccsm" in the terminal to open the compiz settings manager. Then you can click on a function like Effects>Paint Fire On Screen and it will show the keys that work it.

good luck
