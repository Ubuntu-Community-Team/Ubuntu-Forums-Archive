### Post by Wtower on 2011-01-03
I have had issues with Belkin in the past. You might want to check combatibility in:

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WifiDocs/WirelessCardsSupported](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WifiDocs/WirelessCardsSupported)

If your model is not listed, chances are that it is not compatible. Ndiswrapper could be of help, first take a read at this:

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WifiDocs/Driver/Ndiswrapper](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WifiDocs/Driver/Ndiswrapper)

Finally, a good option in a sense that you won't spend any more time if you can afford is to get yourself a new compatible adapter.
