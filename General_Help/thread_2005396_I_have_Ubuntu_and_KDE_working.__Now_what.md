### Post by oldos2er on 2012-06-18
Themes are really a matter of personal preference.

Files and folders with a dot preceding their names are hidden. If you're working in a terminal, use **ls -a** to see them. In Nautilus, use Ctrl-h, in Dolphin use Alt . (hold down Alt and press ".")
