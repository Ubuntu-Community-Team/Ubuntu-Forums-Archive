### Post by dexter on 2007-05-17
When you see grub, select your kernel and press e. Now you are able to change the boot options, you should add the -nosplash option, this disables the "fancy" boot, instead you see what your os is trying to do. It will probably halt at one of those commands, post that one here and will be able to help further.
