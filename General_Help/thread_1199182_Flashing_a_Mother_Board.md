### Post by WRDN on 2009-06-28
Out of interest, why do you want to flash the BIOS?
It is very risky, and if it fails, you could end up with a dead board (not literal). There are solutions if it fails (e.g. some motherboards have BIOS recovery utilities), but I would never recommend it unless you MUST do it.

If you need to flash it, this guide may help: [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=318789](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=318789)
