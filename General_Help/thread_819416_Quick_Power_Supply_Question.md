The short answer is "yes, you need more than 250 watts".  Unfortunately a lot of branded systems have a proprietary power supply and you can't upgrade or if you can you have to purchase from them at an astronomical cost.
If yours is not proprietary (can't remember the last pavilion I worked on) then replacing should be easy enough to do.  As far as how much power, my answer to that is the same as the one to "how much memory" which is "all you can afford".
Best of luck.
