### Post by dorcssa on 2007-01-09
Well, I use vlc, so I don't know how to do it in totem, but (are you hungarian, coz u use ö? :D) in the preferences you have to change the type of the subtitle from utf-8 to win 8988 2, or something like that (the central european latin iso type). And for using two kind of subtitles. Let's have two subtitles, one is english, the other one is hungarian(in my case). I name the one to: videoname.en.srt, and the hungarian one to videoname.hu.srt. This way you can even choose the subtitle in a normal dvd-player for a divx.
