Okay, well things have changed since then. Beryl is no longer - it has been folded back into Compiz. Almost everything you could do in Beryl is now doable with Compiz if you have the "unsupported" plugins package installed.

Compiz comes preinstalled on Ubuntu, perhaps even on UNR.

However, you can't use the netbook launcher and Compiz at the same time.

If you want the netbook launcher, use UNR. If you want Compiz, use regular Ubuntu. You won't get much in the way of Compiz effects on an EeePC - not only do those machines use the basic Intel graphics, but they are underclocked; and the latest Ubuntu has performance problems with Intel drivers (not Ubuntu's fault; Intel is in the middle of rewriting their driver).
