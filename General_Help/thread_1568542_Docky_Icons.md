### Post by jrd on 2010-09-07
> **bigseb said:**
> What about the window not going fullscreen cos of docky? is that fixable?

If you mean not being able to maximize a window (full screen) because docky is in the way then yes. Go to the settings for docky and select "autohide" or "intellihide" depending on how you want it to work. then when you open a window docky will not be in the way and it will go all the way to the bottom of the screen.

### Post by CoolJohnB on 2010-09-08
I had similar problems with Docky first you need to enable compiz to do that go to System>Preferences> Appearance click the visual effects tab and select normal, you will then have full screen.

I found I couldn't drag programs onto Docky, the way round this is to start the program, the icon will then appear on Docky, you right-click it and select pin to dock.

Hope it works for you the way it worked for me.
