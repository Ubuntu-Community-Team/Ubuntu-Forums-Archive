### Post by angryhomer17 on 2011-01-06
Ooooo! I get to solve the problem to my two year old post. In qBittorrent v2.4.11 and probably a few previous versions a feature was added to clear the completion history.

Just right click in the text entry box and select "Clear completion history" :lolflag:
