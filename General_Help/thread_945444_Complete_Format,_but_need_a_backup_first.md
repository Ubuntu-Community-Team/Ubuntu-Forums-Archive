What I have done to back up is download AptonCD [http://aptoncd.sourceforge.net/]("http://aptoncd.sourceforge.net/") follow the instructions and burn to disc the stuff you got from the repositories, (this will save you so much time). Then download Remastersys [http://www.ubuntugeek.com/creating-custom-ubuntu-live-cd-with-remastersys.html]("http://www.ubuntugeek.com/creating-custom-ubuntu-live-cd-with-remastersys.html")  Those two programs have been a life saver for me. As for your music and games, the only thing I can suggest is burn them to CD/DVDs
