### Post by H.E. Pennypacker on 2007-05-01
You have strange problems that are, from what I know, isolated.  Don't consider them the norm.  If you want to be on the save side, you may want to consider wiping the entire hard drive, and starting all over.  A fresh install may be what you need.

There could be a number of problems.  Make sure you document them well, and it would be helpful for you to report them (on, say, Launchpad).

Be patient, and don't let seemingly difficult problems immediately lead to a reinstall.
