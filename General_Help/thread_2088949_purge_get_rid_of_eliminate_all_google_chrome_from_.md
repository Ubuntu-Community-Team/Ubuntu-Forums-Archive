### Post by sdowney717 on 2012-11-27
Ok, I found out the files in your home folder are in 

.config/google-chrome

I removed chrome
I deleted that folder
I logged out

I installed chrome
I installed xmarks
I logged into xmarks and told it to sync and all my bookmarks are back, including my extensions.
I can view my history

I just have one icon in the notification error and no more profile cant be loaded error messages, so I think a success!
