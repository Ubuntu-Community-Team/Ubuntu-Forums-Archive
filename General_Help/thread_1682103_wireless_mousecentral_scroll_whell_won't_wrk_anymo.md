Everything else seems to be working.  It seems just the scroll wheel is buh-duggered.

### Post by t0p on 2011-02-08
Haha!!  It turned out to be a mechanical issue.  Apparently my dog had the mouse for a while, running round the garden with it in her mouth and other such antics.  This abuse caused the wheel to become misaligned.  So I unscrewed the bottom of the mouse, realigned the wheel and hey presto!  Fixed!

Pretty fortunate it was such an easy fix.  Since I bought the wireless keyboard keyboard and mouse I've been using them endlessly.  It's so cool to be freed from the chains of a cable.

Any idea what it's likely to be?  And is the connection secure?  I'd hate it if an eavesdropper intercepted the transmission between my keyboard and computer, what with all the top secret documents I deal with every day.
