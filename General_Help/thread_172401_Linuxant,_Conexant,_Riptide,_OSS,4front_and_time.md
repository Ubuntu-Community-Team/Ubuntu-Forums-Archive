### Post by kumbakara on 2006-05-08
The OSS to ALSA move in combination with the Ubuntu 2.6 kernel left the
Linuxant driver for my Conexant Riptide audio card, which is written for OSS and kernel 2.4, unreachable (I am not going to port the driver to a 2.6 kernel :-( ).
Second option is to install OSS (which seems to support this audio card..based on the latest release notes) with a 6 month licence for free (requires re-install after 6 months). The Ubuntu forum does not register succes with this option. There is no HowTo..no cookbook for succes. The OSS tech (Dev) does respond to questions etc. on his own forum.  I challenge him to write a HowTo on the Ubuntu forum.
Third option. Buy a new soundcard...which i did...ripped out the conexant stuff (two pci cards..one modem..one other thingy). A Hercules Muse Lt.....14 Euro...this saves time :-)
