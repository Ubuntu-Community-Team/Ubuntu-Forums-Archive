### Post by Mark Phelps on 2012-01-24
Your graphics card was last supported with ATI proprietary drivers in Ubuntu 8.10.  ATI dropped Linux driver support for your card when Ubuntu 9.04 came out -- so no newer versions of Ubuntu will work with those drivers.

Instead, what you have to use today are the open-source video drivers -- and those are installed by default when you setup Ubuntu.
