### Post by aysiu on 2006-07-24
Sure there's a better tool. Just use *gnome-search-tool* or the KDE equivalent.

Search for *.mpg, *.m4p and copy all of the results to your computer. iTunes/iPods tend to mangle the names of the songs, so you might want to use something like TagTool to bulk rename the files based on their ID3 tags.
