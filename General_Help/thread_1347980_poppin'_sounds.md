### Post by audiomick on 2009-12-06
Hallo.
since I updated to 9.10, I get a rather nasty pop booting up and shutting down. Can't say anything about videos, as I rarely watch them on the computer.

I should say at this point that I am a sound technician, not a computer programmer, which means I can tell you what is happening, but not why.

My impression is the the sound system is not starting very elegantly. More than likely, as the sound card is initialised at startup and turned off at shut down, the driver or the sound system (ALSA, Pulse audio or whatever it is) is sending out random rubbish. I am confident that this will change with an update before too long, as it is totally unacceptable for anyone who is using the system for serious sound work, and has professional monitors connected.
Having said that, I must mention that it is standard practice for a professional to turn on the source (computer, mixing desk or whatever) first, and then the speakers, and turn off in the reverse order.
