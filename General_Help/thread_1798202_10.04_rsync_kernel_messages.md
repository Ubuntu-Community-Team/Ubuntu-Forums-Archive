### Post by deathadder on 2011-07-06
Looks like it's actually a bug with NFS and not rsync: [https://bugzilla.kernel.org/show_bug.cgi?id=15552](https://bugzilla.kernel.org/show_bug.cgi?id=15552)

There's a patch available (see comment #4) but I doubt it's been applied to your kernel source. 

[EDIT]
Actually, those posts are from a year ago (2010-03-23). I've just found a Ubuntu bug that say's it's been fixed upto Natty, although some posts are still having the problem. So I'd keep an eye on that for a possible workaround.

[https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/linux/+bug/585657](https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/linux/+bug/585657)
