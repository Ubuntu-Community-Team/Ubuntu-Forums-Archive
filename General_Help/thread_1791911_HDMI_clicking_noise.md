### Post by CatKiller on 2011-07-11
Sounds like a normal mode change to me. The resolution changes, so the monitor has to adjust itself. Normally it sounds similar to the noise it makes when you turn it on.

IME, CRTs make the noise when they change mode, LCDs don't make the noise because they can't change mode. Connection type has made no difference to me.
