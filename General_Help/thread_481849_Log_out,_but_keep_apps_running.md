### Post by Page on 2007-06-23
I know that KDE has a switch session function that lets someone log in in a separate session without closing your session.

There is also a session save feature in KDE that lets you save your session at log out and have your last session restored the next time you log in. It's kind of like hibernate, but you can leave the computer running.
