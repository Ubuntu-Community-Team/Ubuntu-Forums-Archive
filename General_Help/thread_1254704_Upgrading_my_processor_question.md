### Post by Rhubarb on 2009-08-31
Adobe flash is very CPU hungry, infact it can max out 1 thread pretty much regardless of what CPU you have.
Also a faster processor won't prevent flash from crashing / firefox from crashing with it.

I would also recommend a dual-core processor, though this would mean you'd also need a new motherboard (different CPU = different motherboard to suit it).
That way, if flash does consume a core / thread of a CPU, you've still got another spare one to process other applications simultaneously.

### Post by P4man on 2009-09-01
