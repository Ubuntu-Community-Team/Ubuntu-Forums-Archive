### Post by darolu on 2010-02-08
Do you have this problem with other applications? Say, gedit?
If you print from gedit and you still get green text it means your printer is not properly configured and you will need to reinstall the driver. To do this simply delete your printer from System-Admin-Printers and re-install it.
