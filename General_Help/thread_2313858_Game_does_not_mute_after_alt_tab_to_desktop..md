### Post by coldraven on 2016-02-16
Because Alt+Tab is just hiding your application. Say you had a program that was doing some intensive calculation, you would not want it to stop just because you need to check your emails.
Create a shortcut for Mute: Go to Settings>Keyboard select the Shortcuts Tab>Sound and Media and choose a key. I just chose F9 because Fn+F9 is "Reduce volume" on this laptop. It works!
