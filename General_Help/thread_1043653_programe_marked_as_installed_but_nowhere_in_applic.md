### Post by 62chevy on 2009-01-18
Windows and Linux are different in that area. most program files are held in /bin /sbin /usr/bin /usr/sbin or /usr/games.  The program you installed would most likely be in /usr/bin or /usr/games depending on what you installed. Tell us what program it was and we can help you find it. 

It's not hard just different.
