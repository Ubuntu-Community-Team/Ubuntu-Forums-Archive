### Post by Edward The Bonobo on 2006-04-03
Unplug it (ignoring the 'Do not disconnect').  Plug it back in.  Sync again.

Sometimes, especially when uploading lots of tracks, this will result in having tracks listed from the iPod database, but it won't play them.  Delete them and add again.

See also here: [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=151566&highlight=ipod](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=151566&highlight=ipod)
