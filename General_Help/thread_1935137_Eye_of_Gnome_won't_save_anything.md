### Post by winh8r on 2012-03-03
With regard to where firefox is saving downloads, just click on Edit > Preferences > and you will see a heading for downloads in that window , allowing you to select where firefox saves "everything" or select "ask me every time " so that you can choose where each download is saved.

The default setting is in the Downloads folder, as you will see if you have never changed it.

As far as the issues with the image editing go, I would choose GIMP to do the editing as there are far more options for resizing, scaling and saving in a wide variety of formats. Just a thought , but use what works for you.

Hope this is of some help to you.
