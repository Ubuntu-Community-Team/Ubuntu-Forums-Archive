### Post by redilyn on 2009-06-19
You have not provided enough information.

What AMD graphics card do you have? AMD has moved a fair number of graphics cards to legacy status. If you card is in the legacy category then you will not be able to use the proprietary drivers in jaunty.

What wireless card are you using? Depending on the wireless card you may need to install b43-fwcutter or ndiswrapper.
