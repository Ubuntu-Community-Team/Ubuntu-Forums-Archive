### Post by sagen on 2008-04-23
you have to switch back to metacity, by doing

alt+F2
metacity --replace

And to get compiz back you

alt+F2
compiz --replace


Found a bug report on it, seems to be a workaround for it:
[http://bugs.sun.com/bugdatabase/view_bug.do?bug_id=6429775](http://bugs.sun.com/bugdatabase/view_bug.do?bug_id=6429775)
