### Post by hictio on 2008-12-15
Fire up the CompizConfig Settings Manager (System > Preferences), and goto:

Effects > Animations > Shade Animation tab

On the Animation Selection, double click on the defaut "Roll Up", and, on the pop up window, on 'Shade Effect', select 'None'.

Hope this helps.
