### Post by pasti on 2008-11-05
Do you mean the login screen, where you enter your username & password? as I just helped a mate of mine who wanted to change his "usplash", but I didn't know what it was, so he sent me this link, they're login screens, and dead easy to install.:-

[http://hehe2.net/eye-candy/30-cool-linux-login-screens/](http://hehe2.net/eye-candy/30-cool-linux-login-screens/)

Anyway if this is what you meant, and you fancy one of these, just download the .deb package by clicking the one you want, (it'll take you to the download page), save the package to somewhere you'll find/remember, DON'T unpack it, go back to the login window manager, i.e

System > Administration > Login Window, hit the "local Tab", click "add" at the top right, navigate to the .deb file you need and click "install",& click the little radio button to us it, easy!

If this isn't what you meant by "Bootsplash", then feel free to ignore this, as I'm about to get flamed!!!!!!!!!!!!
