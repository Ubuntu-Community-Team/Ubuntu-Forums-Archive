### Post by buzzingrobot on 2016-07-21
The default Openbox installation includes only a gray background and a minimal menu triggered by a right clock on the display. You can get to a terminal from there to continue your configuration.

Ubuntu's mini.iso installation is very minimal.  Be sure you have installed and configured Xorg, an appropriate video driver and other packages needed to support Openbox. 

You'll also likely also need to configure some startup apps and services.
