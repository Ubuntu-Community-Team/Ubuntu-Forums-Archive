### Post by AmericanYellow on 2008-06-26
If it is just that you have large fonts, I can leave you with a key for key guide that will take you to 'Appearance' to change your font sizes.

Do this:

1. Hit Alt+F1
2. Press Right Twice
3. Down Once
4. Right Once
5. Down Twice
6. Hit Enter

Wait about 10 seconds...

7. Right Twice
8. Down Once
9. Press Enter
10. Hit Tab Twice
11. Press 1 then 0, then Enter to change the font to Size 10.
12. Press Down Once
13. Then repeat steps 9-12 four (4) times to set all font sizes to size 10

If this doesn't work, you are probably having a problem with your screen resolution. If you need help with your screen resolution just let let em know.

Good Luck.
