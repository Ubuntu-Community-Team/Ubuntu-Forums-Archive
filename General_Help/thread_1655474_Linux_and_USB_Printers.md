### Post by dandnsmith on 2010-12-30
I've found [this site](http://www.linuxfoundation.org/collaborate/workgroups/openprinting/database/databaseintro) a useful place to research particular printers for use with linux.

Canon, from my personal experience, used to be easy to use with linux, then they underwent a change after which they didn't support linux printing at all. Now, the posted requirement by lots of users for drivers usable from linux has meant that at least some of the more recent models can be used.

Alas that the particular one I purchased a while back is one they don't intend to support, and there is nothing which helped on the database.
