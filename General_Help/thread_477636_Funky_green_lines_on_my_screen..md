### Post by angryhomer17 on 2007-06-18
Might be a poorly seated video card or loose cable. Try reseating the card, make sure the video cable is ok (no tears and attached securely). Reset the monitor. (reset setting, unplug it, plug it back in). That should take care of it, if it's a hardware issue.
