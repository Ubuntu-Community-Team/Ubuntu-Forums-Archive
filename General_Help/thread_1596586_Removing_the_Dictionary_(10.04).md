### Post by hrickh on 2010-10-14
> **Thrasyllus said:**
> How does one remove the Dictionary listed under Applications/Office? It's not obvious - I could not find a way to do it either through the Ubuntu Software Centre or the Synaptic Package Manager route.
gnome-dictionary - It's part of gnome-utils. I wouldn't recommend removing it, but if you really want to remove that package, you'll also remove baobab, gnome-search-tool, gnome-system-log and gnome-screenshot.

A better idea is to just hide the Dictionary entry in the menu through system, preferences, main menu and uncheck it.

R.
