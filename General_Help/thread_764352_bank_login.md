### Post by ibuclaw on 2008-04-24
Okay,

A) Try clearing your Private Data. That is:
  Browsing History
  Download History
  Saved Forms
  Cache
  Cookies
  Offline Website Data
  Authenticated Session

Click OK. Then Firefox.

B) Open up Nautilus in your home folder.
Hit **Ctrl+H** to show your hidden folders, and go into "**.mozilla/firefox/**"
Then locate the file named "**pluginreg.dat**" and remove that too. Firefox will automatically create a new one on startup.

Now, re-open Firefox, go to the sire and try logging in again.

Hopefully, a fresh restart of Firefox should do it.

Regards
Iain
