### Post by shironeko on 2007-02-20
Sure there is... It should be activated by default. (It's on the upper right, next to the Shut-down button).

Not all programs run a notification-daemon and display an icon. As in windows: Some do, some don't.

---

### Post by strabes on 2007-02-20
It's called the "notification area." You can add it to your panel by right clicking on a blank area on your panel, clicking "Add to Panel..." then dragging the notification area icon onto the panel.
