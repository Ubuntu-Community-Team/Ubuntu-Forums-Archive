### Post by fooman on 2008-11-02
try a different server.  open synaptic and click on settings > repositories.  click the ubuntu software tab.

click the arrow at the end of the "download from:" box and choose "other".  from there, choose the "select best server" option and let it do its thing.  when its done it should have found the fastest server for you.

you can also just manually choose which ever server you like from there.

hopefully you will find a faster one there (i did).  :)
