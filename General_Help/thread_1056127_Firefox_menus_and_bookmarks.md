### Post by cariboo on 2009-01-31
I would suggest you backup your bookmarks and create a new Firefox profile. The bookmarks are located in ~/.mozilla/firefox/xxxxxxx,default. You need to unhide the hidden files and directories in Nautilus. To do this open Places-->Home Folder and press Ctrl-h. the xxxxxxx will be a random string of letters nad numbers.

Once you have backed up your bookmarks, just delete the directory and the next time you restart firefox it will be recreated.
