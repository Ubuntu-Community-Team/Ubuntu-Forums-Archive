### Post by NilsE on 2007-10-22
The specs show it as coming with 7.04 so probably 7.04.

I would play with it for awhile to get the feel for Ubuntu, do some reading here and then decide the best route to take.

If you are anxious to get to 7.10 I would go the update path which is pretty straight forward then if that fails you could do a fresh install and configure it the way you want.

Would not hurt to download the 7.10 .iso and create the LiveCD just to have it handy just in case something goes wrong with the update.
