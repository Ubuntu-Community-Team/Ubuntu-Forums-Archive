### Post by BlueSpecial on 2010-12-13
Regarding your questions:

1 - CD's sometimes due to storage, dust, etc can become unreadable even if they were good a while ago and only used for a few times... If it worked with another CD then the problem is not the drive but the 10.04 CD.

2 - Do you have ATI / Nvidia graphic card? If so did you installed the proprietary drivers (System -> Administration -> Drivers)? Did you also installed the "Ubuntu Restricted Extras" from Software Center? Just do a search for it on Software Center and give it a go!

Hope this helps.
