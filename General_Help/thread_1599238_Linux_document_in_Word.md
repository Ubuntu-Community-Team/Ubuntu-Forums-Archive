### Post by TBABill on 2010-10-17
Yep. When you do a "save as", just click the dropdown menu where you see the filetype .odt and select a .doc instead.

Edit: make sure you click "file type" first

### Post by fbobraga on 2010-10-17

### Post by beegary on 2010-10-17
Depends on the software they have really, what version it is and what version of OpenOffice you are using.
Office 2007 will open .odt files.

You should be able to save in .doc (MS Word), .pdf, rtf, or html at least one of these will be viewable
