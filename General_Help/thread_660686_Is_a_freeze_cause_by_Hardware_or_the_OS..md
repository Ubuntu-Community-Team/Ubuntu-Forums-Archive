### Post by Mike'sHardLinux on 2008-01-07
I have had freezes caused by hardware and software:
[LIST]
[*]new video driver that had bugs
[*]mouse or keyboard that became defective
[*]ram that has defects (in some cases it developed them over time)
[*]drivers that conflicted with each other
[*]back in the day, IRQ conflicts were common
[*]hardware incompatibilities (ie, a motherboard I had did not like my Win-TV PVR150)
[*]hardware overheating
[/LIST]

There's probably more situations I can't remember.
