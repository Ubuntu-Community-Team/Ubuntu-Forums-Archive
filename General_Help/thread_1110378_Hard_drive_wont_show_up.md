Ok after a little bit of work, I have found that the power supply shipped with the Maxtor Personal Storage 3200 device is faulty. I have replaced the power supply and I am in the process of backing up all my files from this device as I don't much trust it at this point.

Once again for everyone trying desperately to find their answer through google or what have you, **replace the power supply of the maxtor personal 3200 to fix it**
