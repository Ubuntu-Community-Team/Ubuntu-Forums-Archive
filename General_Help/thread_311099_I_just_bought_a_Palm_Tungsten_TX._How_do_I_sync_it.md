### Post by rioghal on 2006-12-09
I thought I would update this to let people know what I have found. I have a Palm T|X and I have found that the following to apps work well for syncing my T|X with my Ubuntu desktop:

jpilot - Not sure why they chose that name because it doesn't use java.
This app is a gui sync app for PDA's

pilot-link - I tried it and it works too.
This app is a command-line sync app for PDA's

Also, I found a very nice website that has tons of free PDA software:

[http://www.freewarepalm.com/](http://www.freewarepalm.com/)

I am currently using 15 apps I found here and they work great on my Palm T|X.
