### Post by apetresc on 2008-02-11
I think this is a problem with your monitor, because the screenshots you posted look just fine here. No problem that I can discern. It's probably caused by an impoperly-configured monitor trying to interpolate where to draw parts of the character.

Try finding out your monitor's native resolution and setting Ubuntu to use that. If your monitor has an auto-adjust button, try setting that.

If all else fails, try disabling sub-pixel hinting on your fonts.
