### Post by snowpine on 2012-02-27
You need to create a USB stick with "persistence." Look at the slider near the bottom of this **usb-creator** screenshot, you want to choose "stored in reserved extra space."

[https://wiki.kubuntu.org/Kubuntu/Netbook?action=AttachFile&do=get&target=screenshot-usb-creator.png](https://wiki.kubuntu.org/Kubuntu/Netbook?action=AttachFile&do=get&target=screenshot-usb-creator.png)

For more info: [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Installation/FromUSBStick](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Installation/FromUSBStick)
