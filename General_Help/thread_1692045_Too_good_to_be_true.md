### Post by Habitual on 2011-02-20
If they called you their ANI (Automatic Number Identification) would be on any modern telephone set.

If yours has this feature, call the number back, but RUN, DON'T WALK to your ISP's Customer Service Department for verification.

If they call back and you don't have ANI, just ask for their number ("I'm busy, I'll have to call you right back"). Do not let them call you back. Insist on a number. Then Verify the "facts" that you have.

And who ever heard of an ISP that doesn't charge for an upgrade in service?

I'd be suspicious.
