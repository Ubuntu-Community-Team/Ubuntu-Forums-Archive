### Post by mbeierl on 2011-05-26
Not sure if it's still the case, but Mac used to ship "fat" binaries that would have more than one architecture (ppc, x86) in them so they could run on either platform.  I know they also supported fat for 32bit and 64bit, so that might be the case of what you're seeing.
