### Post by pallinger on 2013-09-25
As I looked at their website, it seemed to me that they patented a lot of (software) stuff, and they are keeping the whole development process closed. That hints to me that there is little chance the layout will be ported to linux in an open source driver. (Except if someone else does it in a country where software patents are illegal, e.g. EU.)
It's a pity, as it seems like a nice idea to me. 
Meanwhile, you can look at some other chorded keyboards here: [http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chorded_keyboard](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chorded_keyboard)
