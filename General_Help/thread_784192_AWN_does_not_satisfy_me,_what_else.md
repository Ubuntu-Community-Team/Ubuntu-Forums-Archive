### Post by prshah on 2008-05-06
> **Cygoku said:**
> The problem I feel with AWN is that it is showing active window, pretty much like the Windows List does.  Also, it cannot be place on the right or left side.  This is all "as far as I know".  


Here's to expanding your knowledge.

You can drag-n-drop any program from your menu to create a launcher on your avant bar. If you run that program, either from the bar or from the menu, it will not create another icon for it on the bar; instead a small triangle will appear under your launcher icon to show that the program is running! Nifty!

You can align the AWN dock/bar as follows: Press alt+f2, give the command```
gconf-editor
```, browse to apps->avant-window-navigator->bar, then look on the right hand side for "bar_pos". Change it's value to "0" for left align, "0.5" for center and "1" for right align. You can give it any value from 0~1, even 0.25 or so to get it where you want it! When you click OK, the awn bar position should change immediately.
