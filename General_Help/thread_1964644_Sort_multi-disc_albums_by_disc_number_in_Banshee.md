Select all the tracks of each album and go in the "Edit Track Information" (right mouse button). 
Try to specify the Disc Number in the track information (to copy it to all selected tracks just click on the button with the two sheets)

An alternative way is to add the tracks to a Playlist or to a Play Queue in the order you prefer ;)

### Post by Holstener Liesel on 2013-01-12
I had the same issue. Here's what I discovered eventually: I've activated "sort artist's albums by date" in music settings, and it seems the dates of some multi-disc albums weren't formatted identically, thus screwing up the order of individual discs.

When I selected the entire multi-disc album and set the date of every track to an exactly identical string, e.g. "1979", discs got sorted correctly.
