From what I understand by your post you are a very inexperienced computer user.

Formatting a drive and installing an OS are both actions that erase data from drives.

I recommend that you take your machine to a professional to get the data recovered.

I would not recommend you trying this at home.

Stop using the computer immediately or more data could be lost!

Important lesson learned:

Don't format a drive with important data without proper partitioning of the hard drive, backup of all data and knowledge of what you are trying to attempt.

It is not recommended that you perform actions that you are unfamiliar with on machines with relevant data still installed on them. Practice on a machine that if you mess something up it really won't matter.
