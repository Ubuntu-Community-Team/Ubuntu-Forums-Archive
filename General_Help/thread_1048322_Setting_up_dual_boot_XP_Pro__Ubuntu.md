Okay I got it working, (1 failed attempt) here's what I did:

Installed Win XP on first hd.
Switched master and slave.
Installed Ubuntu 8.10 (on the new first hd)

Everything works out of the box and I can boot both from GRUB.

I am also glad to say that my ATI graphic card works fine in the new version! Everything just seems to work out of the box.
