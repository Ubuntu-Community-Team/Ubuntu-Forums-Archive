### Post by marpstar on 2007-12-04
Run some diagnostic tests. Try running the Memtest that Ubuntu installs in the GRUB menu by default.   If that passed, you might try running a hard drive test.  I recommend Hitachi's[ Drive Fitness Test]("http://www.hitachigst.com/hdd/support/download.htm")

Also try reseating your components such as your memory and video card. 

Is your machine dirty?  Make sure your CPU fan is clear of dust and dirt, it may be overheating...
