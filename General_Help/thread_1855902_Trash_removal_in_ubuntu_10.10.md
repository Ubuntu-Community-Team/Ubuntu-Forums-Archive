### Post by keesp on 2011-10-07
I have been having considerable trouble removing trash in Ubuntu 10.10. This was a problem because I need to burn an image. All the standard options did not work, like empty-trash, clicking the trash bin (blocked) and so on. I finally found out that if I move the contents of the trash bin (in ~/.local/share/Trash/files) to my usb stick, then I finally managed to work around this problem.

I am getting the idea that if you delete the trash bin, it puts everything back.

Anyway, I decided to post this for others who may be running into this problem.
