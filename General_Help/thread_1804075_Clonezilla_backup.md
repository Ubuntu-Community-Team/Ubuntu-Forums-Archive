### Post by Ghost_Mazal on 2011-07-14
If you can make the image to a secondary or external HDD you can simply specify for clonezilla to split the image into chunks of 4gigs. Then you can burn those to dvd's. By default clonezilla split the image into chunks of 2gigs.
