### Post by z_diver on 2006-05-10
Can't say I've had that problem but because of strange cursor theme issues in general I've decided to unzip all cursors manually in [COLOR=Blue]/usr/share/icons/[/COLOR] from now on.  That way they are available to all users and I have no more flakey behavior(like gnome apps using one cursor while kde use another).  A nice touch is putting your prefered theme in the [COLOR=Blue]/usr/share/icons/Human/cursors/[/COLOR] folder too.  That way, you get the nicer cursors from boot so GDM can use them too.
