In ubuntu 18.04, at the top of my screen it says - Thu 1:30pm .  I would like it to also display the date.  How do I do that?

---

### Post by #&amp;thj^% on 2018-07-12
You can use Gnome-Tweak to do this or just throw this in a terminal.
```
gsettings set org.gnome.desktop.interface clock-show-date true
```
