### Post by darthpenguin on 2013-02-18
Okay, I'm going to answer my own question here. I seems that the unity dash cannot handle large lists of files very well so the music lens only shows 100 songs and the albums they belong to but a search in the music lens will reveal results which are not shown in the default list. I understand (or at least I hope) this is a design choice forced upon the programmers because of technical limitations that will be resolved in future releases. It sucks that this information is so hard to find. It is not a bug but it clearly *looks* like a bug. A simple "BTW the music lens doesn't actually, really work in logical way right now. But we're working on it." is all Canonical really needs to do.
