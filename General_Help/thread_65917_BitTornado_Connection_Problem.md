### Post by mlomker on 2005-09-15
Are you sitting behind a firewall or NAT device?  You need to forward ports to your PC if that's the case.  The default is 6881-6890 or so.

The nice thing about Azureus: 

1) it works and many others don't.  
2) it has a test feature for testing incoming connections,
3) It supports UPNP, which means it'll automatically open those ports if your router supports it and you won't have to do anything...
