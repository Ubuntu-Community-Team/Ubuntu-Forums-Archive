### Post by coffeecat on 2023-07-05
The time a user must remain inactive before their login session expires is currently set to two hours. It's been like that for years, and has worked well. If you are being logged out after a "few minutes", then there must be a problem at your end, not with the forum software. To be honest, I'm surprised it's as short as two hours. I very rarely have to log in, and often find myself still logged in when I first visit in the morning. However, if your ISP-assigned IP address changes, the forum software will (usually) require login again as a security measure. 

Another possibility is that you have installed a browser extension that is interfering with or deleting cookies. 

This thread probably belongs in Forum Feedback & Help, but with the possibility of this being a local software-induced cookie problem I'll leave it here for the time being.
