### Post by RedSingularity on 2009-04-24
Desktop edition is a regular looking desktop environment.
Netbook remix has a desktop environment set up to be easily viewed on the small netbook screens.

In google search images of ubuntu 9.04 and ubuntu 9.04 netbook remix.  You will see the difference right away.
