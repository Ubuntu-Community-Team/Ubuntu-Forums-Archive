### Post by stevecaldwell on 2010-09-15
> **jacob.david said:**
> Very recently I started using Evolution as my mail client. Before that I was using MS Outlook. Everything seems to be OK with Evolution but I find 2 startling limitations.
1. How to set up out of office auto reply?

Jacob,

This doesn't directly answer your question but you may be able to set up an out-of-office reply using the webmail interface for your POP/SMTP account.  Many of these accounts have the out-of-office autoreply as an option in webmail.

> **jacob.david said:**
> 2. What is the equivalent of mail archive.
I tried to google it and looks like many people have the same issue but no proper solution. Is this really a limitation with Evolution

Evolution does allow you to archive and backup your account settings and saved email (very useful when moving your user data to a new computer).

Hope this helps -- good luck.
