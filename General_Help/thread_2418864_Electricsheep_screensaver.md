### Post by Rubi1200 on 2019-05-13
One rule of thumb is to stick with the official repositories. We have seen too many threads here with users experiencing problems because of these third-party or PPA applications, screensavers etc. And even more so if it is considered to be untrusted.

More generally, there are some good tutorials around (these are just a few examples):
[https://tutorials.ubuntu.com/](https://tutorials.ubuntu.com/)
