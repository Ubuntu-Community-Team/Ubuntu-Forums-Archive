### Post by MagisterDixit on 2014-05-06

### Post by MagisterDixit on 2014-05-07
Nevermind. Further tinkering found the problem. They are bleeding some of the improvements from 14.04 into 12.04 and one of those was the improved "Brightness and Lock" feature. I unchecked the box for dimming to save power, and I am back in business.
