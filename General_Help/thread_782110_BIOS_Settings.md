### Post by forrestcupp on 2008-05-04
When you boot your computer, it will flash a screen that will say something like "Press Del. to enter your BIOS settings."  What it says varies according to motherboard.  Some require you to press Delete, some F2 or F10.  Just whatever your computer says to press when you first turn it on, press it, and you will get to your system BIOS settings screen.
