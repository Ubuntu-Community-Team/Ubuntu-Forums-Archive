### Post by jerome1232 on 2012-03-30
It's not really possible to emulate a graphics card and get any noticeable performance boost. General processors are just not good at the type of math involved, that is the reason we have a separate specialized gpu to do the job. Often a video cards ram is quite a bit faster (2 generations ahead) than your general system ram as well.
