### Post by aeiah on 2010-11-08
problem is, you may find that things like turning off the monitor are done on a hardware level, and nothing to do with the operating system :/

if they are commands sent to the OS, then you could just find out what they are and then use xbindkeys to send the relevant command when you press a predefined key combo
