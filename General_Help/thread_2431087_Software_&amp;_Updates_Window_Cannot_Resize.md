### Post by CatKiller on 2019-11-12
In most window managers, Alt and left click will move the window from anywhere and Alt and a different button -  I can't remember if it's right click or middle click - resizes the window from anywhere.

I believe (I don't use Gnome and I'm typing from my phone anyway) that Gnome changes that modifier button to the Windows key, and disables the resize functionality by default, although they're are dconf entries for both of those settings. 

I also don't know how many of Gnome's design decisions Cinnamon inherits. Hopefully, though, that will give you enough to go on so that you can have a play and resize that window, or at least move it around more easily.
