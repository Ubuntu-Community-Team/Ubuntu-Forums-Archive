### Post by VanHammersly on 2008-05-03
For Cairo dock on startup, go to System, then Preferences, then Sessions.  In sessions, click on Add.  In the title put Cairo Dock and in the command put cairo-dock.

To look better, I guess its a matter of preference. I chose the OS X look and I find it quite sharp.

For new icons, you can drag applications from the application menu into cairo dock to add them.
