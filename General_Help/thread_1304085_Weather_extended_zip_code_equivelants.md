### Post by parkland on 2009-10-28
OK-

I did find a way to find your "ZIP equivalent"

Go to weather.com.

Find your local city.

On the link that says "Save location", if you hover over it, look at the bottom staus bar, and it shows a link location that will contain the "ZIP" code you are looking for.

A little rough around the edges, but it worked.
