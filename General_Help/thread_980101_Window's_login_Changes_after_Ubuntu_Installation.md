### Post by john183 on 2008-11-12
If all else fails boot into windows, login as an administrator, create a new user for yourself and just copy all the files from your old login's documents and setting folder to the new one. I have had to do that before after someone who knew just enough about computers to get theirself into trouble decided they were going to try to "optimize" my computer for me while i was gone.

(Note to self: do not allow others to logon to your computer as an administrator unless you really don't care what happens to it.)
