### Post by ztrange on 2008-10-19
Check System / Preferences / Sound

Im not sure if options available depend on your soundcard configuration but i use PulseAudio for all the playbacks and capture and in Device HDA intel (my soundcard) ALSA mixer.

Also check that the volume control is not muted, you can access by double clicking the volume icon
