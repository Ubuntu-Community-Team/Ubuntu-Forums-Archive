### Post by shantiq on 2011-12-08
well this might be of use to some


if you want to write in **cyrillic** as well as your original chosen language



go systems tools/system settings/keyboard/layout settings/ + sign [add] down the bottom   then pick Russian  or whichever language you wish to add



then a small keyboard will appear on topbar click and choose


really easy and handy


Many know this.....    but some do not
