### Post by Vaphell on 2010-10-29
you can always use synaptic and mark multiple packages for installation, that's pretty much what add/remove does behind the curtains. Besides how often do you install stuff?
Also if the 9.04 works for you there is no need to upgrade just for the sake of it. Unfortunately the security updates are no more, any non-LTS release is maintained for 18months.
