### Post by snowpine on 2010-07-29
Hi Mikey, within the current release (10.04) you will get bug fixes to your existing drivers through the Update Manager.

When a new or updated hardware driver comes out, it gets thoroughly tested, then ends up in the *next* Ubuntu release (i.e. 10.10 Maverick).
