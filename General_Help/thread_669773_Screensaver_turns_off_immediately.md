### Post by muadnu on 2008-01-16
A couple of days (or maybe weeks) ago the screensaver in my gnome desktop started acting weird: whenever it turns on, it immediately turns back off, and I'm back to my desktop. The issue is not related to compiz, I tried putting the visual effects option to "none" and the problem is still there.

I also have an external monitor at home, and when I close the lid of my laptop (which I set to turn off the screen but not suspend), the external monitor goes to stand-by, but after a while it turns back on, then off, and so on.... 

I don't have a clue about what's happening, any ideas?

### Post by Anduu on 2008-01-16
My first guess would be something "mechanical" interrupting it...

For example I once had a wireless optical mouse that I had to turn on its back otherwise the screensaver would quit or not start at all...I had noticed the mouse cursor would wander from time to time by itself.

Not sure if it was the optics or wireles interferance causing it.

Another possibility could be an app running in the background is causing problems.

### Post by muadnu on 2008-01-21
It seems that it was related with a bluetooth mouse or keyboard. That was my first guess, and I checked turning them off but the problem persisted. But then I tested it away from home and the problem wasn't there. The weird thing is that then the problem dissapeared also at home, with the bluetooth devices on (and the problem was there for a couple of weeks already). In any case it's solved for now, thanks for the reply...
