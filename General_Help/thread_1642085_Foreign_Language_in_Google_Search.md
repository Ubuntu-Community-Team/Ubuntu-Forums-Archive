### Post by Krytarik on 2010-12-11
I have just yet reproduced this with my own Firefox, with Bulgarian, funny!;)

You have to choose the correct language(s) for browsing in the preferences of Firefox:
"Edit -> Preferences -> Content", beneath "Languages" click at "Choose".

EDIT: Oh, you have to delete the cache and the cookies to make the yet chosen language affect Google.
