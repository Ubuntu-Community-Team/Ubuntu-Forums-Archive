### Post by VCoolio on 2009-12-08
Welcome to the forums. But your question is quite impossible to answer; what is best is what fulfills your needs. Just try things out and remove them if you don't like them.
As for video: vlc is always ok of course; check out smplayer too. For music it depends: if you want lightweight you could use mpd (music player daemon) with a client like quodlibet or sonata or ario; or you could use audacious, which works a bit like winamp; if you want a fullfeatured player look into rhythmbox or banshee. Maybe songbird is something for you, it's sort of a player / browser combination. 
First ask yourself what you need; then google around, search add/remove applications or synaptic, keep an eye on the forums.
