### Post by Mark Phelps on 2010-04-14
FYI ... Documents and Settings (as an actual folder) disappeared with the move to Vista/Win7.  

What once was a folder is now the MS equivalent of a softlink.  

The new folder is under \Users\<username>\Documents.
