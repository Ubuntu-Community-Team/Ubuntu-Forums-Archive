### Post by Nonno Bassotto on 2007-09-18
Try doing the login in safe mode. Then rename your .themes and .icons folder doing something like
```

mv .themes .themes_old
mv .icons .icons_old

```
and log in again. Then, delete just the offending theme and rename the folders with their usual names.

Hope it works, let us know.
