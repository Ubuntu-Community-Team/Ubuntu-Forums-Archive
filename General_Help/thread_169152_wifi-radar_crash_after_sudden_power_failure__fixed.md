### Post by encompass on 2006-05-02
I lost power on my lappy while running wifi-radar, and after rebooting wifi-radar crashed on me.  I thought it might be something to do with the .conf files and sure enough, my /etc/wifi-radar.conf file was empty.
Deleted the file... lost all my settings, but wifi radar works again.
Figured this may not be the first time this will happen so I have posted this thread to help everyone in need.
