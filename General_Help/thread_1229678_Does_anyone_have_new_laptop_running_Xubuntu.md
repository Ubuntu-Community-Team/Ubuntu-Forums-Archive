### Post by jocampo on 2009-08-02
In general terms, most of Dell laptops (recent ones, 5 years ago until present) run really well. Not a lot of compatibility problems and really fast and reliable.

My home laptop is a 4 years old Dell Inspiron 6000 with 2gb RAM. I've tried all Ubuntu flavors on it dual booting with XP: Ubuntu, Kubuntu and XUbuntu and last one runs really good. Xubuntu is light enought (the GUI part) so if only thing you are planning to do is browsing internet, is the way to go.

Finally, please take some time and review hardware compatibility list before you buy anything: [http://www.ubuntuhcl.org/]("http://www.ubuntuhcl.org/")

Good luck ;-)
