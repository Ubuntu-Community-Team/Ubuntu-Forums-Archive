### Post by ajgreeny on 2011-10-06
If transmission uses global menus you need to put your cursor in the top panel and those menus will magically appear there, not in the window itself.

I know.!  A stupid idea for a non-maximised window, and I may be completely wrong in the case of transmission, but global menus are something that makes no sense to me on a large screen, and I offer this as a potential reason for your problem.
