### Post by Paulgirardin on 2014-09-20
I did not have to do this at all.
I just installed the LastPass extension.
Open Chrome,go to the far right icon in the tool bar (Customise and control),select tools,extensions and at the bottom of the page 'get more extensions' and search for LastPass
