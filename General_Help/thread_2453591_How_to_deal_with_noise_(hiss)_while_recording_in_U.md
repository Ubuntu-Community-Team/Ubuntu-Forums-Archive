### Post by HermanAB on 2020-11-13
Howdy, 

Hiss can come from multiple sources.  Firstly, open Pavucontrol and mute all sound inputs except the one you are using.  If that doesn't help, add an audio preamplifier before the laptop line input, to increase the sound level before it enters the noisy laptop circuitry and improve the SNR.
