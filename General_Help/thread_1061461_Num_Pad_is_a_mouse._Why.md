### Post by darkhammer8108 on 2009-02-05
Ok so my numpad used to work just fine and about two days ago i tried to use the calculator in ubuntu and the numpad wasnt working. upon furthur inspection i found that the numpad was being treated as a mouse where:
1 - is down-left
2 - is down
3 - is down right
etc. etc.

i checked and the keyboard works fine in other installation of other os's i have. i really have no clue what happened and i dont think that i changed anything recently. numlock is on and it is still treated as a mouse for both on and off.
