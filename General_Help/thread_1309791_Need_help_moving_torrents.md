### Post by goldencako on 2009-11-01
As lovinglinux said, it should read your files that have already been downloaded. On Transmission, you open the torrent file and choose its destination to be the previous destination. Then click on "verify downloaded data" or something like that, and it should recognize the stuff thats there and just pick up from where deluge left out.
