### Post by joey-elijah on 2009-03-07
The reason why it dissapears when you close the terminal is because the command is 'running' - closing the terminal kills the command. 

You need to add 'emerald --repalce' to your sessions if you want it to be used as default and to start up with your system.

system > preferences > sessions > new/add (one of those) 

enter 'emerald --replace' as the command, give it a name like 'emerald' and then save. Log out and back in and et voila!
