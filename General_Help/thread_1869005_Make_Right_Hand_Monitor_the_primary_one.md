### Post by Vaphell on 2012-07-15
look at /etc/X11/xorg.conf and see if your monitors have Position defined. Primary/secondary doesn't mean much if the configuration behaves correctly. 

my monitors (1920x1200 and 1280x1024 on ATI card) are switched (right one is primary which is not what i wanted) but work well because they have

```
Section "Monitor"
  ...
  Option	    "PreferredMode" "1280x1024"
**  Option	    "Position" "1920 0"**
  ...
EndSection

Section "Monitor"
  ...
  Option	    "PreferredMode" "1920x1200"
**  Option	    "Position" "0 0"**
  ...
EndSection
```
