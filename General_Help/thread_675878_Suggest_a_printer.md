### Post by danwood76 on 2008-01-23
I have 2 HP printers that had no problems finding drivers for, also back home I have 2 newish epsoms which also seem to work without problems.
I have a lexmark at work which I had to mess around with for a while under ubuntu to get working.

The best thing to do is look on [http://openprinting.org/printer_list.cgi](http://openprinting.org/printer_list.cgi) at each printer you look at in that price range (I noticed that your printer is listed as working perfectly though :-S)
I tend to stay away from lexmark, their printers are in general very poor.
