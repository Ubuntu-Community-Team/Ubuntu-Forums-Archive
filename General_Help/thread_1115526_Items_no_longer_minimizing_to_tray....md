### Post by mocoloco on 2009-04-04
FYI it's called the "panel" (on Windows it was called the taskbar).  The tray is also called the notification area.  Not trying to be pedantic but some apps are supposed to stay in the tray, like Pidgin, and some are supposed to be visible in the panel (in the window list applet).  Terminology counts :)
