### Post by davideotape on 2008-11-23
Get DBAN from [here]("http://www.dban.org/"). You can get a floppy or a CD version, and after putting it on your chosen medium, boot from it and it will wipe your hard drive for you. I assume you have a XP install disk too, as you will need it to install XP again.
