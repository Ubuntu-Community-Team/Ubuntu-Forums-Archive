You can just delete them. I don't know if it really removes all the files, but they will not show up in the "Theme Preferences" anymore.

### Post by aysiu on 2006-05-19
Go to /home/morbid_bean/.themes and /home/morbid_bean/.icons

GDM themes live in /usr/share/gdm/themes

The . folders are hidden, so you have to press Control-H to see them.
