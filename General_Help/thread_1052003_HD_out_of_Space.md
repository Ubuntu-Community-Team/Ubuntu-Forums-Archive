### Post by drs305 on 2009-01-27
RAM and disk space do not deal with the same thing. RAM concerns your machine's memory and not space available on the physical drive(s).

Deletions do not free up disk space until you empty the Trash. Did you do this?

Here is a tutorial on finding out where your free space may have gone. It started out dealing specifically with Trash but includes other things to investigate as well:
[Disk Full? - Check Your Trash Bin(s)]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=898573")
