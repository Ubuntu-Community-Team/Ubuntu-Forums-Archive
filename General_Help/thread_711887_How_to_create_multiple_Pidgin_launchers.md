### Post by notwen on 2008-03-04
If you wish you could always close Pidgin w/ your wife's account enabled, so when Pidgin opens it automatically logs her in.  This way Pidgin itself is 'customized' just for her, you on the other hand when using Pidgin would need to disable her account and enable yours, but remember to re-enable hers before closing Pidgin.  I have a similar routine on my laptop, which I share w/ my gf, we both use the same Pidgin, just enable/disable each other's accounts when we're using it.  Your wife can enable/disable accounts w/ 2 simple clicks. =]

Accounts--->account_03--->Enable/Disable
