### Post by 3rdalbum on 2009-06-24
> **Emanet said:**
> Jesus Christ!

Is not this the UBUNTU forum? Am I in the wrong place!

1. Not a lot of users are familiar with the Windows-based installer. It's fairly new and the long-time people are using real Ubuntu installs rather than the limited "install-in-a-file" method that the Windows-based installer uses.

2. It looks like there's some sort of bug that you're running into, that I haven't heard of before.

3. Have you tried looking at the contents of the log file, that is suggested at the bottom of the error message? What does it say? Does it give you a clue to what the problem is?  Also, are you running with administration privileges on Windows?

4. If all else fails, defragment your hard disk, back up all your data, and install Ubuntu. Tell it to resize your Windows partition, create a new partition for itself in the free space, and it will co-exist with Windows.
