To set margins:
Alt > o > p. Page tab.

Alright this is what worked for me:

Margins
Left-1"
Right-1"
Top-0"
Bottom-1"

then moving over to the header tab
Header ON
Left Margin-0"
Right Margin-0"
Height-1"

which made it perfect for a MLA format paper

Well what I meant was you could set the margins to a very small amount and then make a table to fill the page. ### Post by chiefmanyrabbitguteat on 2008-04-21
The default space (In US-EN) is 0.4" for a header, and 0.2" for the spacing between it and the text body.  you could reduce the top margin by a total of 0.6" to compensate.

Otherwise, there are a variety of options in the "Headers" tab of the Format-Page dialogue.
