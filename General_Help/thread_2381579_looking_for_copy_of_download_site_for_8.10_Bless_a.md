Ubuntu version 8.10 will soon be ten years old and passed end of life many years ago. Please realize that you cannot expect to get much support for such an old version, and there are many security holes, so do not connect it to the internet.

By the way, there is a viewer for binary files in Midnight Commander, **mc**, that you can install in current versions of Ubuntu.
