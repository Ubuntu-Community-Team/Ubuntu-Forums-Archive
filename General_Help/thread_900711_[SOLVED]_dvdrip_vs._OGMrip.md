### Post by prince_niceguy on 2008-08-25
DVD rip is giving flexibility of ripping to avi, most of the divx players in market do not play the .ogm files which is a problem. If you are going to view the ripped files again on your comp then use ogm as it is better. You can also go for .mkv, I am not sure which ripper supports.
