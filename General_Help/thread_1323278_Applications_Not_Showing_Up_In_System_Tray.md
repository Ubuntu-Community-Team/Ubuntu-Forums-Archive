### Post by fooman on 2009-11-11
right-click on an empty space in the bottom panel and choose "add to panel"

in the window that pops up...scroll down to "window list", click on it once, then click the "add" button.

hope that helps.

### Post by fluffman86 on 2009-11-11
Pidgin and Rhythmbox still open in the notification area just like they did in karmic.  Nothing has changed for me.

Make sure you have the notification area enabled.  Right click a panel, then click Add to Panel, then add the Notification Area.
