### Post by ibuclaw on 2008-04-21
[http://archive.ubuntu.com/ubuntu/](http://archive.ubuntu.com/ubuntu/)
[http://archive.canonical.com/ubuntu/](http://archive.canonical.com/ubuntu/)
[http://security.ubuntu.com/ubuntu](http://security.ubuntu.com/ubuntu)

Are the three main servers.
Though, you can replace "http://archive.ubuntu.com/ubuntu/" with a server near your local area.
ie:
Great Britain - [http://gb.archive.ubuntu.com/ubuntu/](http://gb.archive.ubuntu.com/ubuntu/)
France           - [http://fr.archive.ubuntu.com/ubuntu/](http://fr.archive.ubuntu.com/ubuntu/)
