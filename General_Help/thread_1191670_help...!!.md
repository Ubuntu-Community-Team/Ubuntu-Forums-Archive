### Post by drs305 on 2009-06-19
Hi saurabh, welcome to the ubuntu forums! 

Generally questions posted here need a bit more information than you provided - we would like to know more specifically about what kind of problem(s) you are having. 

We have many different sections of the forum. At the top right of the page is a link to the main ["Ubuntu Forums"]("http://ubuntuforums.org/index.php") page. Clicking on this link will show you all the forum sections. Each one has a section at the top with permanent posts called "stickies". These posts contain information to solve common problems.

If you are having multimedia problems, there is a Multimedia forum and this "sticky" may be what you need to get you started:
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=766683]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=766683")

I would also highly recommend you take a look at the Ubuntu Guide link in my signature line. It is a short book that introduces new users to Ubuntu. 

Learning a new system can be challenging. You will find the people on this forum are ready to give you all the help you need if given the necessary information.

If you are not able to play mp3s and other audio/video files you will need to install some codecs

Go to Applications--> Add/Remove then in search tab type gstreamer just click on the boxes corresponding to various codecs and also click on the ubuntu restricted extras box. Then hit apply changes.... codecs will be downloaded and installed
