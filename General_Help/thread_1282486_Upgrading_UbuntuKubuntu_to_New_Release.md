### Post by mac9416 on 2009-10-04
I know that by upgrading from 9.04 to 9.10 you will not get GRUB 2, and you will not have the option of encrypting your home directory. However, you can upgrade "by hand" to GRUB 2 by following the [instructions]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Grub2#Installing%20%28Ubuntu%209.04+%29") on the Ubuntu Wiki. Also, Dustin Kirkland has lays out [how to encrypt your home directory without reinstalling]("http://blog.dustinkirkland.com/2009/06/migrating-to-encrypted-home-directory.html") on his blog.

Hope that helps.
