### Post by QuikZ06 on 2010-01-15
I have spent the last several weeks searching the web & this form for a fix to the pulse audio speaker pop, nothing worked. Lately I have been having wifi dropouts so I started searching for a reason. It turned out that I was using the WL (SP?) driver & it wasn't playing nice with my Broadcom 4311 nic. 

Thru searching this forum I found out about the B43 driver, a little more reading led me the the "B43 Guru" and his fix with upgrading to the 2.6.32.2 kernel.

I follow his very long post (32 pages as of right now), and all is well so far with wlan0.
A positive side-effect of the update appears that something in the new kernel fixed my speaker pop..
