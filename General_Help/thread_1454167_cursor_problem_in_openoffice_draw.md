### Post by jaggis on 2010-04-16
Solved: ---
The problem seems to be solved. I changed the monitor resolution to one step lower than what it was set. 
Caution: Do not change to a lower resolution than just one step since the screen gets garbled on restart and you have to reinstall linux
Please take a backup of all important files before proceeding
Now the cursor works fine in Draw and also in games. 
This is, therefore, the monitor resolution setup problem..
