### Post by Therion on 2007-10-26
Well I would say the easiest way, to at least get started is to go to Applications and click on "Add/Remove".  On the left you'll see a menu that will filter available applications by type.  Looking for internet applications?  Click on internet and so forth.  If you see something you want, put a check in the box next to it's name and click on "Apply Changes".  That application will then download and install.  There's also a "Search" bar if you want to look for something specific.

If what you see is NOT listed in the Add/Remove, post back with exactly what it is you're looking for and I'll see if I can't help you find what you're looking for and how to install it.
