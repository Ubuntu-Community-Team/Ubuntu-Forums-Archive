### Post by Holger_Gehrke on 2019-11-02
What do you mean by "Task bar" ? There's no such thing in nano. What nano normally has are two lines showing (some of) the available keyboard commands. This can be switched on or off with alt-x or Esc x. The keys will work whether or not the help lines are visible or not.

Holger
