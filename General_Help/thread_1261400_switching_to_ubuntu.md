### Post by P4man on 2009-09-08
short answer: no.
Linux (/ubuntu) is **definitely** not better than windows to play windows games.
Some of them may work using a windows emulation layer, but they will be slower, have issues and many of them simply won't work. 

If playing (those windows) games is what you want, stick with windows. Upgrade to XP or windows 7 if you dont like vista.
