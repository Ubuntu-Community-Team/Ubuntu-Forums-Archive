### Post by psyguy92 on 2005-11-17
Right mouse click on an empty area of the panel and select properties.  There, deselect (uncheck) 'expand'.
I think this is what you're talking about.  You can play with the 'size' option in there too.  
If you want transparency, click the background tab, select 'solid color' and move the 'style' bar.
