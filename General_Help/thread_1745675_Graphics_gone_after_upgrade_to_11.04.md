### Post by udippel on 2011-05-01
There's a lot of trouble with Nvidia in 11.04. Proprietary drivers, you know.
I am not sure what you actually see, but the easiest and fastest would be, to allow your installation to find a suitable driver on its own. It doesn't hurt, at least. In Failsafe (console), do
sudo mv /etc/X11/xorg.conf /etc/X11/xorg.b4_1104
sudo reboot

This is only the first step. It probably does not work, but could get us further at understanding what goes on.

When you're back after the reboot, tell us what is going on, and try to include the file /var/log/Xorg.0.log in your reply.
Also, add the content of /etc/X11/xorg.conf.failsafe
