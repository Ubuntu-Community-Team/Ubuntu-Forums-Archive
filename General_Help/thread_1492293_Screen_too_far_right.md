### Post by BoneKracker on 2010-05-24
Some displays have a control on them (typically labeled "auto") which will auto-adjust various settings.  Often, this will correctly resize and/or position the pixel array to fill the screen properly.

If your display doesn't have an "auto" button, you can achieve the same by adjusting the horizontal size, vertical size, horizontal position, and vertical position.

However, these adjustments are for fine-tuning; they are not for making huge changes.  If your picture is more than, say, 5% or 10% out of position, you should probably be making adjustments in another way (e.g. by changing the display mode being used by the X server).
