### Post by Montblanc_Kupo on 2009-01-18
For a background behind the cube, look for the skydome section under the desktop cube effect preferences.

If you want to have something on the other windows... either switch to those desktop workspaces and then open the applications... or drag the applications to them after they're open.

---

### Post by techturkey on 2009-01-18
Ill try to answer both questions: 
first, to get the picture, go into the compiz manager, go to desktop cube, select the appearance tab, then skydome and choose an image.

Second, press alt+ctrl+left to flip over to another cube face, then open your program on the other face.

Hope this works.
