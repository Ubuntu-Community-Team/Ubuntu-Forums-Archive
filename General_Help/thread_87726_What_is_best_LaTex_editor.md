### Post by Barking_Mad on 2008-05-15
I found this useful for quick Latex edits, simple & free latex image/formula production.

[CodeCogs Equation Editor](http://www.codecogs.com/components/equationeditor/equationeditor.php)

I found Emacs, Kile and texeditor all too much for what simple purpose i required, the time invested in learning the IDE offset the time saved using a PC editor in the first place to the point where it was just not viable to use them imo. (i.e Escalated complexity and work requirements.)

Anything that takes longer to produce the same as i can produce with a pencil and paper, in terms of communicating a message, is not really a replacement language for communication or indeed a viable tool to replace the pencil :P

I think sometimes people forget that simple is often best.
