### Post by donotbugme on 2008-12-24
You cannot run (compiled on) OpenSolaris apps on Solaris 10/9 or older...

But there is, let me call it "forward binary compatibility", i.e. apps compiled on Solaris 8 work fine on Solaris 9, 10 and OpenSolaris, apps compiled on Solaris 9 work on Solaris 10 and OpenSolaris, but not on Solaris 8 or older

I think you get the idea of how it works
