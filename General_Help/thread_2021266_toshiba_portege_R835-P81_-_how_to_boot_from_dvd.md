### Post by Tamlynmac on 2012-07-09
The "ODD" is your dvd drive. You should be able to use the up/down arrows (on your keyboard) to highlight the ODD. Once highlighted, use F6 (up) or F7 (down) key to move the ODD up or down the list. Just move it up the list to the number 1 position.

Good Luck & Hope This Helps
