### Post by goinglinux on 2010-06-02
The easiest thing to do is turn the screensaver off manually when you are playing the game. 

System > Preferences > Screensaver

Then uncheck "Activate screen saver when computer is idle"

You should also check the power settings to ensure that the screen doesn't go blank after a period of inactivity.

System > Preferences > Power Management

Uncheck "Dim display when idle."
