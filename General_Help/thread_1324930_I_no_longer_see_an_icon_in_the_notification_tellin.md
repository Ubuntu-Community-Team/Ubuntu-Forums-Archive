### Post by donato roque on 2009-11-13
There's a new update-manager behavior.  Actually it's a new update-notifier behavior, since 9.04 jaunty.  Whenever there's an update, the update icon does not appear in the tray icon, but the update manager is launched automatically and it appears minimized in the bottom panel.
