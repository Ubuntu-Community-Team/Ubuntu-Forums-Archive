### Post by darolu on 2010-05-03
Go to **System - Administration - Synaptic**; on the left side panel click on **Status** and then click on **Installed**, that way you'll see all the packages installed on your system and you can install or uninstall anything from there.

On a side note, XAMPP stands for X=**Cross** platfom **A**pache** M**ySQL **P**HP and **P**erl while LAMP stands for **L**inux **A**pache **M**ySQP and **P**HP, you can install Perl to it too. So LAMP is a XAMP, I see nothing wrong with your description of "a different lamp installed" as I don't see how can they be different.
