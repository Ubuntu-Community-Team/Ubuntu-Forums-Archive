### Post by cacofonix on 2004-11-05
To move delete or add a panel right click on any panel available and click properties and you can then delete add or move the panel. To move the computer menu or any button on a panel right click on it and then click unlock and then right click again and  select move and move it to the panel you want :D 

good luck and welcome to ubuntu zoey
