### Post by Tobine on 2008-07-24
I've just finished a fresh install of hardy and my disk usage analyzer thinks that I have a 450 gb hdd but its actually only 250 gb. How can I fix it?

---

### Post by Potatoj316 on 2008-07-24
Check your disk usage analyzer settings.  Somewhere you can tell it what disks and directories to analyze, you probably only want to have / in that list but you probably have another much longer path.  You can delete that path and it should work. 

Many people seem to have this problem, maybe search the forum a little.
