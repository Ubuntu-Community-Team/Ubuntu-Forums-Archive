### Post by TheFu on 2015-01-13
Almost never, but it depends on the network setup and security there.  Most larger companies will require the use of a proxy server to get HTTP access out of the corporate network except to a few servers specifically setup and hardened for internet access.  The proxy may or may not require credentials (usually for a user, not the machine) and those credentials may or may not be tied to AD.  

Best to contact the network admin and ask what is needed.
