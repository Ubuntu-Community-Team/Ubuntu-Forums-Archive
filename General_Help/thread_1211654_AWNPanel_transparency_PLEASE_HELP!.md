I would like to make my AWN dock and my panel (the panel that is all the way at the top of the desktop by default, it holds the main menus and stuff) transparent. If anyone can tell me a step-by-step guide on how to do it, that would be great. Thank you in advance. I am on Ubuntu Jaunty x32.

### Post by darolu on 2009-07-12
It is very easy, open the AWN Manager and go to "Crystal Engine" and change the colours to your likings, there is a transparency bar.
For your panels, is also very simple, right click them and go to properties - background and choose solid colour and change the transparency.
