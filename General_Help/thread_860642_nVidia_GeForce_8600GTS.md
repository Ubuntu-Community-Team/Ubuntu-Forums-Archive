### Post by ichundu on 2008-07-15
i have not used this card, but i bet you will not have problems with compiz effects. i've seen computers with "very modest" graphics card running compiz effects without any issue, so i don't see any reason why your card wouldn't carry these effects. however this is just a thought, i haven't tested that card.

sudo apt-get install nvidia-glx-new

hope this helps!
