### Post by Dayne89 on 2008-09-03
Is there a way to change Thunderbird to default mail client? so when i click on a e-mail link it opens thunderbird instead of trying to get me to configure evolution.

---

### Post by Too Late on 2008-09-03
In Thunderbird, go to Edit -> Preferences -> General (tab) and check "Always see..." or just press the Check Now button.

Alternatively, go to System -> Preferences -> Preferred Applications and select Thunderbird there.
