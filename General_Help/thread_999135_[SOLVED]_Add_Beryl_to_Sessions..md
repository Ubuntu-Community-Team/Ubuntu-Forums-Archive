### Post by gqstatus05 on 2008-12-02
Problem solved. It looks like I had Compiz on the sessions. I removed it and went to System: Preferences: Sessions: 

Give it a title: Beryl

and add this to sessions: beryl-manager

Then hit OK (You don't need to add a comment)

Hit Close

Voila that should fix your issue.
