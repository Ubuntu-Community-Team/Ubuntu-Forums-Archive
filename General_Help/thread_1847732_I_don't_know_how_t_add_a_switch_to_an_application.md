### Post by Paddy Landau on 2011-09-21
You need to edit the menu, which is where the command is started.

Right-click your applications menu and select "Edit Menus" (or go to System > Preferences > Main Menu). After a short pause, you will receive a window with the menu system.

Click on Internet, and on the right side click on Opera. Press the button Properties.

Change the command to add your switch. Press Close.

Test.
