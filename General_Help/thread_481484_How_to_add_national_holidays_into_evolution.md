### Post by reclusivemonkey on 2007-06-23
Go to File --> New --> Calender

In the box that appears, where it says "Type", choose "On The Web". In "Name", enter "Holidays", or whatever title you want. In the URL, paste the following;

```

 webcal://ical.mac.com/carmaco/Feriados%20Nacionais.ics

```

That should work for you =]
