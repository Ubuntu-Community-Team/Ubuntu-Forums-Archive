### Post by Gannon8 on 2008-11-20
Hi. I am trying to create my new Linux Distro with Reconstructor, and I want to use the theme that I have on my laptop's computer. The program takes themes in .tar.gz format, like System > Preferences > Appearance > Themes. So how do I export my theme so I can use it with reconstructor?

---

### Post by Cope57 on 2008-11-20
Your themes are located in your home folder, and **Ctrl+H** (*Show _H_idden Files*) and folders. Your themes are saved in a folder / directory called **.themes**.
Go into the folder and find the theme you want to archive to tar.gz
Right click on the theme in question and **Create Archive**, save your archived tar.gz theme where you can remember it.
Now you can use the tar.gz with reconstructor.
