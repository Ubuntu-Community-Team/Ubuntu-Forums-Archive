If I got to system>preferences the "sound" option is listed twice for some reason. The first listing doesn't work while the second one does. Is there a simple way to fix this?

---

### Post by radiocognition on 2009-05-16
Right click on your ubuntu menu and select Edit Menus.  This should bring up a GUI where you can customize what launchers appear where in your menus.  Pretty self explanatory at this point, go to System > Preferences and remove your bad launcher.
