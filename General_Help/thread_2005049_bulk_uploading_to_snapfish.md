### Post by wonderingwhy on 2012-06-17
A LIMITED SOLUTION

For 10x15cm prints only

DigiKam --> Facebook --> Snapfish

For anything else you need higher res than Facebook --> Snapfish can give you.  So if its a single poster or something that requires high res I advise individual upload direct from Snapfish or emailing your Snapfish account with that single image. 

If you want to make a photobook or similar - you're stuffed and will still need to upload individually to get the res high enough - so you will still need to spend many hours uploading all the pictures you think you might need individually - or go borrow your friends MS OS computer.

I'm gonna leave this unsolved for the moment - in case I or someone else finds a real solution to the high res bulk upload prob to Snapfish

EDIT:

I should add that in NZ this fix will work with snapfish.co.nz but not the Ohbaby Snapfish site - so if you're in NZ and are using the Ohbaby photocentre version of snapfish you will need to log out and log back into the ordinary snapfish site and then link to facebook.

I don't know how it will work for other snapfish sites sorry
