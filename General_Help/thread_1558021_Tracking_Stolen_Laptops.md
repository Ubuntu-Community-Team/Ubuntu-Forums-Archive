### Post by ram2500 on 2010-08-21
You can try a non-software (but very effective) solution. It involves having a metal "sticker" attached to the lid of your laptop. It has a serial number and a number to report the found laptop. Beneath, it has a tattoo, so if someone removed the  "sticker" which is really hard to remove, the police would know it was stolen. However, it does look tacky, though. It's called "STOP" (Stop Theft of Office Property").
