### Post by gmargo on 2010-02-20
The Guest OS (XP in your case) has access only to virtual hard drives; it is safe to partition and format them.  As far as XP knows, it owns the whole disk.  The actual "disk" that XP is writing to is a big file residing within your normal filesystem.
