### Post by Tamlynmac on 2010-02-20
The simplest way I've found to install restricted video drives in 8.04 is to use ENVY NG. Located here:
[http://www.albertomilone.com/envyngfaq.html#A](http://www.albertomilone.com/envyngfaq.html#A)

Just follow the instructions. I've haven't done it for quite some time.

I also believe it's available in the repositories for 8.04. But, you will need to enable (if they're not already enabled) Ubuntu's "universe" and "multiverse" repositories. Those can be found on your system here:
system/administration/software sources
It will require that you enter your password to open software sources.

Hope this helps & good luck
