### Post by anasyntes on 2010-08-28
Roboform for linux is **not** solved :-( One of the reason many of us used roboform in the first place was because it saved our sensitive information locally on your own computer. For those linux users that are ok with giving over that info to be stored online there have for a long time been Lastpass.com that does that.
The rest of us, security concerned, people have to make do with other options, like sxipper for example.
