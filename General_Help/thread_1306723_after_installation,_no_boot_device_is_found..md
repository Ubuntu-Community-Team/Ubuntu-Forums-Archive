### Post by rcarroll215 on 2009-10-30
If you want to wipe the drive and do a fresh install of Ubuntu you just need to pop the disk in, boot up, and when you get to the partitioning stage of the installation select "Guided - use entire disk".  

You may want to perform a check of you hard drive.  That "no boot device found" isn't the same as "operating system not found".  You can download a utility to check your drive here: [http://www.ultimatebootcd.com/](http://www.ultimatebootcd.com/)
