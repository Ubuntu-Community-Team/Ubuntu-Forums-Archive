### Post by lykwydchykyn on 2010-10-14
As long as you don't mind doing a bit of post-install troubleshooting, and aren't the type who totally flips when X won't start or the wireless driver doesn't load, then upgrading is probably the better path.  

The other option is to script out your customizations or move them to your home directory so that you don't have to slog through them every time.
