### Post by quixote on 2010-01-23
If you go to System > Preferences > Startup Applications, there should be an entry there for "Screenlets" when that's installed.  After authenticating, you can tick that, and you should be good to go.

If it's not there, you can click Add, but then you have to know where the executable is located.  /usr/share/bin perhaps??
