Well, let me get in on this one, since it is so dear to my heart because of all of the troubles I have been having recently trying to get my sister's computer setup on dialup.

First I am not sure about 7.04 since it has been a while since I have used it but on Gutsy/gnome (and I will warn you to NOT try dialup on Hardy because there is a BIG bug in it currently) you can use **Gnome-ppp** (available for download and installation in Synaptic) program to setup and do your connection/dialing.

Of course, this is assuming that you do have a good and proper Linux driver installed for your dialup modem.

One of the key things is that once you get gnome-ppp installed is the when you go in to do the dialup account setup that you go to the I think it is the second tab and detect your modem so that it will be configured to the correct port.
