### Post by WorMzy on 2010-05-19
You don't "need" an antivirus product when using Linux, but it doesn't hurt to have one, especially if you're dualbooting with Windows. I recommend [ClamAV]("apt:///clamav"). If you want a graphical front to it, get [ClamTk]("apt:///clamtk").
