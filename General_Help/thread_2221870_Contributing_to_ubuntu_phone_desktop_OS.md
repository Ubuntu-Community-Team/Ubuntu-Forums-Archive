### Post by Gyokuro on 2014-05-04
Hi

It depends what you want to do: in case you are fit with computer hardware you can subscribe the Ubuntu bugs mailing lists and maybe you find bug reports which you can try to solve and help the maintainers. Checking documentation and translate it in your mother tongue is also a great help for the OSS community in general - patching/fixing bugs in upstream packages is always welcome and to help people here at the Ubuntu forums could be a first step too. Maybe this could help: [http://community.ubuntu.com/contribute/developers/package-maintenance/](http://community.ubuntu.com/contribute/developers/package-maintenance/) or which I always suggest: [https://www.debian.org/devel/](https://www.debian.org/devel/) due that Debian is the mother of Ubuntu ;-)
