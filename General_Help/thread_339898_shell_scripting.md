### Post by 23meg on 2007-01-16
Shell scripting refers to making chains of commands called scripts, which mostly utilize the built in shell commands. It's used all across the system, from the very basic task of making your OS boot, to firewalls, power management, process control and automating commonly executed tasks.

For a primer I suggest [http://linuxcommand.org](http://linuxcommand.org) .
