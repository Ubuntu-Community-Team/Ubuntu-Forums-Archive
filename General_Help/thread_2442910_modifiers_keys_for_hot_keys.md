### Post by linux4me on 2020-05-09
For regular flavor Ubuntu 20.04, if you go Settings -> Keyboard Shortcuts, you can look through the combinations that are already used, and I *think* that if you click the "+" sign at the bottom to add a new one, and try to use a combination that is already in use, it will pop-up an error message telling you so, though I haven't tested that in 20.04. I haven't seen a list of unused combinations.
