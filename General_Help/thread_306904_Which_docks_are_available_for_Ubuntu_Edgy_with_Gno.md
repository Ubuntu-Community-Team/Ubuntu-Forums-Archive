### Post by cmost on 2006-11-25
You've already mentioned all the feasible docks for Gnome. None are really adequate and using any one of them will require compromises.  Somebody needs to write a really great dock application for Gnome.  Now that we have a proper and breath taking 3D desktop, the next step is to polish it up nicely.  Of the docks you mentioned, I find Kxdocker to be the closest to the one featured in OS-X (in fact it offers many superior features.)  The problem with it, besides what you've mentioned, are non-intuitve interface, horrid setup, and KDE bindings.  Good luck with your search.
