### Post by avtolle on 2008-07-12
Look at this [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BootOptions](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BootOptions) and try various ones out. If you have some sort of SATA drive, this boot option has helped some folks: all_generic_ide to be entered at the boot line as the option used as an example in the link.

---

### Post by darkhelix on 2008-07-12
Thank you "avtolle"!
I do have an SATA Hard Drive, and used your advice to go into the boot options, and use the "all_generic_ide" on the command line.
