### Post by Dennis N on 2012-05-01
Choose a different icon theme - some use the white system tray icons. It's trial and error to find out which. Example: I use Humanity Colors Dark-Yellow icon theme and the volume, network, and shutdown are all white in the same elementary mono style. Note that you may need to log out and back in to get them all to change over from gray.

Humanity Colors can be downloaded from gnome-look.org
