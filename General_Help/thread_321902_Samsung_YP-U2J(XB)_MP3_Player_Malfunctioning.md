### Post by Tallmom on 2007-01-23
Samsung YP-U2J

We have two of these nifty devices but they weren't so nifty when we upgraded to WMP 11.  We had the same problem (computer doesn't recognize player, flashes "MTP device Found" then clicks on/off every 5 seconds - very annoying) when we upgraded our WindowsMediaPlayer from 10 to 11. This is not a driver problem, fyi.  Samsung removed the driver from their websites.  

I called Samsung tech support yesterday (yes spent over an hour on hold).  I thought I had already rolled back WMP 11 to 10, but turns out there's one more component - the "runtime" was still on 11 and that's what's causing all the problems.  Make sure you roll back ALL versions of your WMP from your control panel/ add remove programs.  Reboot, make sure WMP can read your device again (this takes a little time to refresh using F5).  Then format your player (little pencil in upper right corner of WMP sync screen.  And start fresh.  I'm not a hard-core techie, but hope that helps.  It was such a pain.
