### Post by andrew_rs on 2010-12-15
oldfred, thanks for the reply.

I actually figured this out a few minutes ago after rereading my own post, where I noticed in the grub.cfg file mepis root was being set to /dev/sda8.  It's now on sda6 and ubuntu is on 8.  I did update the mepis fstab to point to the correct place, but I didn't correctly edit mepis's menu.lst.  

After editing menu.lst and running grub-update again all is well.
