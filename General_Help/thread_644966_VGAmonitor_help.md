### Post by Existentialist on 2007-12-21
Your motherboard most likely has a pcie slot you can use for your graphics card.  All graphics cards from nvidia should have working drivers supported by ubuntu already, except for the new 8800gt and 8800gts, which would involve installing the drivers yourself.  All of the nvidia cards will have at least a DVI and a VGA out, or more likely two DVI outs, but will come with the adapters to run VGA if necessary.  The card to get depends on what you plan on using it for.  If you want to do any gaming go with a 8800gt or something in the 880 line, if you want a good all around card for a decent price look at an 8600gt or gts, and if you want a budget card just to use dual monitors look in the 8400 line or something in the 7000 line.
