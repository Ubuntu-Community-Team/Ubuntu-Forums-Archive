### Post by 4Orbs on 2009-07-03
It would be a good idea to select Check Disc for defects as your first step. If the disc checks to be in good shape, then you might click Enter at the blinking cursor. Sometimes during the install you may not see any indication of progress, but I've never had to wait more than a couple of minutes before the install moved to the next step.
