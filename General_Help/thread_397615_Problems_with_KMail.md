### Post by mertle on 2007-04-04
While I dunno if KMail is able to archive mail, I do know how to apply filters to an entire folder (or at least, how I do it- but there's probably a better way). Simply select all the emails in a particular folder (I just 'CTRL+A' within the folder) and go to Message > Apply Filters > Apply all filters.

Hope this at least helps a little with your many mail woes.. I feel your pain- I have emails dating back to '01 that I can't bring myself to part with. ;) Sorry I couldn't be of more help.
