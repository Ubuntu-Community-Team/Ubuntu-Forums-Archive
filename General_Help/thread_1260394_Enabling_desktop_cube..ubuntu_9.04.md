At the bottom right of your screen, how many virtual desktops do you have? They are represented by the little squares on the bottom panel. There should be 4 to get each one a side of the cube.
Right click on a square and choose preferencess. Make it 4 rows and 1 column.

### Post by scouser73 on 2009-09-07
To have the cube working you need 4 workspaces, so go to the workspaces in the panel and right click, select Preferences and set Columns to 4. Now you will have 4 workspaces so you are nearly there in having the desktop cube up and running. Here is a tutorial for getting the best out of CompizConfig, [[COLOR="Red"]**Compiz Config Tutorial**[/COLOR]]("http://www.ghacks.net/2009/07/30/configuring-the-appearance-of-the-compiz-cube/comment-page-1/#comment-859972/")

The tutorial also shows you how to use the Sphere and the Cylinder as alternatives to the Cube, once you've got it up and running it's amazing to see it in action.
