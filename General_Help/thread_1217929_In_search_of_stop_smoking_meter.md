Stop smoking is to slowly kick the habit over time. That is the best  way to get rid of smoking. This is how you can easily do this and it is  VERY effective. When you begin this cycle you start out like a regular  day. Maybe you smoke one pack a day or two it really doesn't matter. The  first day smoke the usual amount of times that you normally do, but  then every day after that smoke one less cigarette. This slowly reduces  your cravings for nicotine over time until you get to the point where  you just don't need it any more. If it is too hard then try reducing  your cigarettes every two days or three, whatever you feel most  comfortable doing.
