### Post by Merrattic on 2012-12-20
Try opening the pdf in Gimp or Inkscape, you should be able to grab selections of your pdf with these tools (images especially), but because you have a scanned pdf they may not be as good as the original.

Alternatively, get the pdf up on screen with evince (document viewer) and take a screenshot, then edit with gimp.

(If you are using Ubuntu, gimp may not be installed by default ?)

If you need to OCR the document I have found tesseract to be quite good , but hard to learn and get working. You could also try unpaper.
