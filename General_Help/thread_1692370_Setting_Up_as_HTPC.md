### Post by aeiah on 2011-02-21
the most popular two are mythtv and xbmc. if you havent got a tvcard in your htpc, i suggest you go with xbmc. its rather excellent.

they provide an 'xbmc live' distro, which is a based on ubuntu and can just sit on a usb stick or hard drive partition without much configuration. its more fun if you get your hands dirty and build it on top of ubuntu minimal yourself though of course :p

oh, and there's boxee, which is based on xbmc but has a more social video slant to it
