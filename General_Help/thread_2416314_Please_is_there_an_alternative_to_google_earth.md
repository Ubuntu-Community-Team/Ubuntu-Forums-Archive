### Post by Impavidus on 2019-04-09
There are some map applications. Marble is available from the repositories, but I'm not really impressed by it. They only have maps, no aerial images, and support for local .kmz files seems a bit buggy.

But it seems that the latest Google Earth from the official website (7.3) works fine on my Ubuntu 18.10 laptop.

BTW, Lubuntu LTS releases only get 3 years of support. Try to get to 18.04 LTS this month.
