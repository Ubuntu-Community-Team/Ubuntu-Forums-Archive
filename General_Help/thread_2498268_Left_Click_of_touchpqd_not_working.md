### Post by paulouxps3 on 2024-06-07
Hello, 

It can be a default configuration of the touchpad.

To modify the behavior of the touchpad, you can go to settings -> mouse and touchpad. Then at the top of the window you can select "mouse" or "touchpad". Here you can modify the behavior of the touchpad and test it with the button "test the settings" at the bottom of this page.

I haven't my Ubuntu in English so the name can vary a little but it's the idea.
