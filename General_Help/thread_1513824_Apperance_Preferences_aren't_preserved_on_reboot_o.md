### Post by senning on 2010-06-24
I had the same problem with visual effects, and I think I solved it by starting up compiz and getting the system to remember my settings for startup (System->Preferences->Startup Applications, under the Options tab).  My font settings have been fine though, so it may be a different problem.

I was following the advice in [another thread]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1467153&highlight=visual+effects+settings&page=3"), and it has some similar, more involved, solutions too.

Hope that helps!
