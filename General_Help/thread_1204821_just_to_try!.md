### Post by Nevart on 2009-07-05
Do your other programs work with voice?  I mean have you confirmed that your mic and soundcard are working correctly?  

It could be that the software does not recognize your soundcard so that is why your voice not getting thru.  Have you tried Skype?  If you can do a voice call via Skype in Ubuntu then you should not have trouble in other IM clients that support voice comms.

If Skype does not work then simply installing YM client will not improve things for you.  You *could* try installing the Windows version in Wine, but I don't think that will fix your problem either.  It's worth a try though.

I nearly forgot too, you may be able to use the online version of YM.  Just log into your Yahoo account and select Chat (but this will not work if you have the wrong Flash plugins installed).
