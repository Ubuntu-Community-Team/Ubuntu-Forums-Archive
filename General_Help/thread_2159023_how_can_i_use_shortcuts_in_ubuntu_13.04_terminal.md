### Post by Peptid on 2013-07-01
When you full screen the terminal, on the upper left part, go to 'edit', then 'profile preferences' (or similar to that). there you can manage all the shortcuts for your terminal.

### Post by ajgreeny on 2013-07-01
Part of the problem you have is due to the conflict between default system keyboard shortcuts and those used in terminal, eg **Ctrl+C** cancels any command that is running rather than being a copy shortcut.
