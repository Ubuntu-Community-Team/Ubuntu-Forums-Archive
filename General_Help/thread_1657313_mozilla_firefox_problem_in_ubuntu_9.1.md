### Post by calavier62 on 2011-01-01
Firefox doesn't automatically "select all" of the address. There's nothing you can do about it, except drag your mouse across the address bar, or press ctrl+a.

---

### Post by Verbeck on 2011-01-01
type *about:config* in the url bar
search for [I]browser.urlbar.clickSelectsAll
then, right click it and select 'toggle'
[/I]
