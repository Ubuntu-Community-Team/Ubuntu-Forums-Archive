### Post by grahammechanical on 2011-02-26
1) Open a Ubuntu One account.

2) Publish your newsletter as a PDF file.

3) Upload the newsletter.pdf to your Ubuntu One account.

4) Mark newsletter.pdf as Publish and note the url.

5) Send the url to your contacts.

6) When your contacts enter the url in their web browser they will get an option to Open or Save the newsletter.pdf file. If they choose Save the PDF file will be downloaded.

A variation would be to create an index page in html format (like a web page) with links to newsletter Jan.pdf, newsletterFeb.pdf, etc., and put that index page into the Ubuntu One account and set it to Publish and send the url of the index file to your contacts.

Regards.

P.S. Test all this out before you send out the invitation to your contacts.
