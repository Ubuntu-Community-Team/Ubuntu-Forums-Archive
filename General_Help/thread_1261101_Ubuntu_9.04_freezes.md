### Post by tango_ninja on 2009-09-08
has the actual computer frozen?  Perhaps its a monitor/video issue.

Next time it happens try shortcutting to your terminal (you can set a custom shortcut from System > Preferences > Keyboard Shortcut) and typing:

```
sudo reboot
```

You may need to enter your sudo password... this may at least help identify whether the computer truly is unresponsive.
