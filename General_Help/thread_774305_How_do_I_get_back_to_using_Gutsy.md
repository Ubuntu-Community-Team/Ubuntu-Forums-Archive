### Post by Cannaregio on 2008-04-29
If you didn't use partimage to make a backup of your previous gutsy you'll have to reinstall ex novo.
My suggestions:
1) stick with hardy and finetune it slowly until you are happy.
2) make anyway (with partimage and starting from a live ubuntu CD) a complete backup of your actual hardy installation on an external harddisk, just in case. And from now on, whenever you install a new version make first a partimage complete backup.
   then reinstall gutsy and slowly recreate your settings.

It's indeed insane to update so soon after a new version comes out, whatever the good ubuntu crew might say :-) 
A good rule is to update at mid time between two versions, so 8.7 and 9.1, hehe :-)
