### Post by Mark Phelps on 2011-12-15
The heat and battery drain are most likely related to the same problem -- a bug in recent Linux kernels that prevents the CPU from throttling back when idle. This overheats the PC and it drains the battery.

See the link below for more information:

[http://www.webupd8.org/2011/06/linux-kernel-power-issue-fix.html]("http://www.webupd8.org/2011/06/linux-kernel-power-issue-fix.html")
