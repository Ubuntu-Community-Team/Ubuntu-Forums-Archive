### Post by kato223 on 2011-05-28
I hope this helps, and I see that this is an old post, but you can try this:

*Without a mouse press Alt+E while in Rhythmbox, then press G (for Plugins), Then Tab to the window of the plugins, then down to Portable Players - MTP, then Space Bar to deselect it, then at the moment the mouse should return to normal.*
