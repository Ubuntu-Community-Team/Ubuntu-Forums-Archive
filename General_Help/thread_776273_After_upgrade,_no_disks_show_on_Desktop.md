### Post by armeck on 2008-04-30
After upgrading, my disks (flash drives, samba shares) no longer show on the desktop.  they are mounted and show in the Places and Computer areas, but no longer on the desktop.  What could be causing this?

---

### Post by bmac on 2008-04-30
Open configuration editor.
Select apps/nautilus/desktop/
Check "volumes_visible"

Hope this helps.
