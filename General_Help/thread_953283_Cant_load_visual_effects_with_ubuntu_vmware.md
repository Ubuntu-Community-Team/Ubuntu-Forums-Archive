### Post by loomsen on 2008-10-20
I don't think there is one.

Virtual Machines usually provide their own virtual hardware for the machines they are hosting. So you basically aren't running hardy on a 8600, but on the devices your virtual machine provides for the virtual pc.

Just try it the other way round, you won't miss anything :)
