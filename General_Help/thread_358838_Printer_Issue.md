### Post by Havlock on 2007-02-13
According to the linuxprinting.org printer database, your Okidata C3200 is a "paperweight" at this time. This doesn't mean that there arn't drivers out there, either in development or complete, but it does put the odds against it. On the other hand, the printer database said my Canon i860 was a "paperweight," and I managed to find drivers for it (by way of Japan.) Try looking for the printer under differant names, there might be Linux drivers for a "foreign" version that'll work.
