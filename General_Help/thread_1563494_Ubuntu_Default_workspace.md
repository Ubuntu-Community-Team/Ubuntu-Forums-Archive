### Post by James78 on 2010-08-29
The only way I know of to do this is by finding all the Gnome related configuration files in your $HOME and deleting them. Once you do that, restart, and new ones will be generated with the default settings. You could pick just the right one to delete so it'll be regenerated, and keep all your other settings, but I don't know which file is responsible for that.
