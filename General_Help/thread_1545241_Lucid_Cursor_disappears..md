### Post by celldweller1591 on 2010-08-04
its a nautilus issue. I have same prob with a fresh install of Ubuntu 10.04 32bit. I solved it by changing monitor settings from 75Hz to 85Hz and now the cursor works fine ! Try changing resolution and play wid some settings, you might get it working. 
I installed "eyes" on my top and bottom panel to track the cursor location to change settings properly. Just click the panel and add "eyes" and it will follow ur cursor.
