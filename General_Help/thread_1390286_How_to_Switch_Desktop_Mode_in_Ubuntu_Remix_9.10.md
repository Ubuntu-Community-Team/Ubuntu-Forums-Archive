### Post by ubun_two on 2010-01-25
If you want to permanently make it the classic desktop, all you have to do is to go to System->Perference->Start Up Application (or something like that) and uncheck netbook-launcher and maximus, so they would not run at start up. Then add appropriate controls to your panel (like main menu). When you restart your netbook, it should look and act like more traditional Ubuntu.

If you want to switch at will any time, I don't have answer for that.
