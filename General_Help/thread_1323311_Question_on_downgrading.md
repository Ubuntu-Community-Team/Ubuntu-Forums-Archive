### Post by fluffman86 on 2009-11-11
if you upgraded from 9.04, then something could have gone wrong on the upgrade.  

I would recommend booting from a 9.10 live CD.  That will tell you if something is wrong with your install or with 9.10 itself.  

If you have the same problem with 9.10 Live CD, then drop back to a Jaunty live cd, then Hardy, until you isolate the problem.

Chances are your hardware works fine, but something just went wrong on the upgrade.
