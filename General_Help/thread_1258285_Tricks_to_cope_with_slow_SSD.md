### Post by fluffman86 on 2009-09-04
The problem is probably with your underpowered processor, not the SSD.  I have a mini 9 and my SSD is actually faster than my hard drive.  It does bog down on javascript heavy sites, though, which means the processor (and firefox) is having trouble loading it.

As far as flash goes, try letting more of the video load before playing it.  That helps.  Maybe upgrading the RAM would help.  But really, these little computers just aren't cut out for a lot of video and multi-tasking.
