### Post by XubuRoxMySox on 2009-11-23
Try opening a browser in your Kubuntu machine and navigate to [http://localhost:631/](http://localhost:631/). Click on **Administration** and explore (the username and password are the ones for your own computer). You should find all your networked printers listed - and if not you can add them so that every printer is shared by every 'puter on the network.
