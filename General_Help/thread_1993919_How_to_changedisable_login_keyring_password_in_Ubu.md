### Post by inashdeen on 2012-06-02
here is my trick. go to your home folder. press on view > show hidden files. now go to .gnome2 and delete the file keyrings. 

next restart you system, and prompt to setup a password, leave the box empty. when prompt to use unsafe storage , click yes. 

Alternatively, simply don't use autologin. fixed
