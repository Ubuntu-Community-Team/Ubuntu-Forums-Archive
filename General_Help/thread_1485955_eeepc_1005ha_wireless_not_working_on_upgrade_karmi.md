### Post by josephellengar on 2010-05-17
Fellow googlers (or bingers, yahooers, or aol searchers), I am just posting this thread to document a solution to the problem of the wireless not working when you upgrade from karmic koala to 10.04 lucid lynx.  The problem is that for some reason the wireless, among other things, get disabled in the bios, so all you have to do is press f2 repeatedly at startup and re-enable all of those items, bluetooth among them if you use it.
