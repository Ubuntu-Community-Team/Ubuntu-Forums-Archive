### Post by 3Miro on 2011-10-27
By HD you mean Sandy Bridge, right? (second generation i3/5/7)

On my Sandy Bridge laptop, I couldn't even boot 10.10 and I didn't bother with 10.04. Any kernel pre-2.6.35 will have massive problems with Sandy Bridge and I am surprised you even got it to work this far. Could you even enable desktop effects?

In short, the sad news is that you may be unable to solve those problems. What software do you use and why does it require 10.04? You may have better luck getting the software to work on 11.04 or 11.10.
