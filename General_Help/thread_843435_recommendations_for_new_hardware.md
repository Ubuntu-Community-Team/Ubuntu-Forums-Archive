### Post by markbuntu on 2008-06-28
ATI linux driver now supports crossfire but I do not know how well and I am not sure if it will support more than 2 monitors. The newer ATI drivers seem to be better than the latest from nvidia judging by the number of people asking for help on the forums but ATI has not yet fixed direct rendering for compiz though I hear they are working on it and expect a solution by the fall.


Dual keyboard, mouse and monitor setup is definitely doable in gnome.

Triple monitors should be doable in theory but I haven't heard of anyone currently doing it in Ubuntu. If I had a tv I would plug it into the video out of my ATI Radeon HD3650 and see if it can be setup as a third monitor but I am not going to go out and buy one just to try that.
