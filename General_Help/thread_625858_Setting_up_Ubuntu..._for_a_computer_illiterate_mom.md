### Post by johntuck on 2007-11-28
Here are the Extensions that I recommend and love

ColorfulTabs
Cute Menus - so you can customize it easier for her
Duplicate tab
FEBE - in case all goes wrong
Fasterfox - So she can browse faster
Forecastfox - for weather
Foxmarks - if she has more than one computer and also to save the bookmarks
ImageZoom
McAfee Site Advisor
MouseZoom
Session Manager
Tabbrowser Preferences - for your convenience in setting it up
update notifier - so she never has to manually update it
Undo CLosed Tabs Button 

These are some of my favorites to make it easier and to show IE people the superiority of the browser

### Post by JPotter on 2007-12-09
Your mom must be significantly more computer literate than mine, or she really doesn't do anything with that machine.  It has taken my mom all of the past ten years just to be able to write a resume, email it, and print it out without (her) having a meltdown from frustration.  If I do anything, no matter how small, to change her gui in any way, I get complaints.  I have her set up with Windows XP using AVG, Zonealarm, and spywareblaster. I gave her a list of programs that are OK to allow past Zonealarm, and she hasn't picked up a single adware, malware, trojan, or virus in over 3 years.

All that said, if you can keep your mom happy with Linux, Congratulations! You deserve alot of credit.

Anyways, If I were trying this with my mom, I would have to give her Open Office and set up her printer.  Good luck!
