### Post by UnterUn on 2010-10-08
*'System*' > '*Preferences*' > '*Keyboard Shortcuts*' > '*Windows*' > Under 'Movement Key' change '*Alt'* to '*Super*', will allow use of the *alt *key with mouse but not the super (windows logo) key
