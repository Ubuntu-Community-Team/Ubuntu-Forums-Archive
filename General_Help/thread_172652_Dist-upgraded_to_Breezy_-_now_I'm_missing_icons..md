### Post by rcarring on 2006-05-09
Most of the desktop launchers have an option to change the icon, or to pick one if nothing immediately springs to mind, review the properties of the desktop launcher shortcuts you have.

Look in /usr/share/pixmaps -- this is where most of the custom icons live.

Sorry I can't help you with the missing icons on the menu.
