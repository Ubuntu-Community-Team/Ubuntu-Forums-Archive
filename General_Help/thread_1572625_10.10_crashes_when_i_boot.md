### Post by Mark Phelps on 2010-09-11
As a (supposedly) new Unbuntu user, you should definitely NOT be messing around with pre-release software.  BAD idea to "upgrade" to 10.10 before it is released.

Why?

Because (1) is it still in testing and is, by definition, unstable.

And (2), there is no way to roll-back to what you had before.

Finally, you should be posting questions about 10.10 in the appropriate Maverick Development forum, not here. This forum is for Released versions of Ubuntu -- only.

### Post by WorMzy on 2010-09-11
Don't upgrade a Wubi installation, you're quite likely to bork the special grub that it uses.

Install a fresh copy, or install to a partition.

And as Mark said, 10.10 is unstable. Don't install/upgrade to it unless you understand that it's a risky endeavour.
