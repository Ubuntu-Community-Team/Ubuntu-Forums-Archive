Does anyone know how to change a mouse to a one-click option for clicking things like on the desktop or in folders?  I use Ubuntu 8.10.

Thx in advance.

---

### Post by 24*7 on 2009-04-01
Open the file browser (Nautilus).

On the top bar, click **Edit**, then **Preferences**.
Click on the **Behaviour** tab and select the **Single Click to open items** Option.

That's all!! Your problem will be solved :)
