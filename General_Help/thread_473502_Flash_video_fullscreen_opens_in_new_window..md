### Post by Fell on 2007-06-14
It is the pop-up blocker causing this behaviour. When you click on the full screen button (in youtube) a small box with an X on it appears at the bottom right corner of you firefox window. Click on it and select, allow pop-ups for youtube.com.

You should now be able to watch videos full screen.
