### Post by insert_name_here on 2008-05-03
The latest libmtp, a package called "libmtp7" works great with Amarok, and I assume, with Gnomad.  (Amarok is a kickass music player, kinda like iTunes for Ubuntu, and Gnomad is a smaller program just for transferring music to Creative players.  They both use libmtp)  So, just "sudo aptitude install libmtp7 gnomad2" in a command line, then after it installs, "gnomad2" to start the program.
