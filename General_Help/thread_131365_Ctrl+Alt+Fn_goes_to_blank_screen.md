### Post by robotron2084 on 2006-02-16
Have you looked at setting your kernel vga mode? Are you able to Ctrl-Alt-F7 back into X at all?

[http://www.antlinux.com/pmwiki/pmwiki.php?n=HowTos.VgaModes](http://www.antlinux.com/pmwiki/pmwiki.php?n=HowTos.VgaModes)

When you Ctrl-Alt-Fn you're switching out of x and into whatever vga mode is set in the kernel. On my laptop I had to add:

vga=0x31A

As a parameter in the /boot/menu.lst:

kernel          /boot/vmlinuz-2.6.12-10-386 root=/dev/sda1 ro acpi=off quiet splash vga=0x31A

In order to get it to display my other terminals. Perhaps this will work for you, too.

### Post by Irony on 2006-02-17
Thanks everyone, robotron2084 your suggestion works!!!

I changed my */boot/grub/menu.lst* to;

[PHP]title		Ubuntu, kernel 2.6.12-10-386 
root		(hd0,4)
kernel		/boot/vmlinuz-2.6.12-10-386 root=/dev/hda5 ro quiet splash vga=0x31A
initrd		/boot/initrd.img-2.6.12-10-386
savedefault
boot[/PHP]

And bingo I have virtual consoles! Also my ubuntu splash is now smaller in accordance with the 1280x1024 screen I have.
