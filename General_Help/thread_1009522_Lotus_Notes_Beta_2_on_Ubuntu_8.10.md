### Post by ChrisGaeth on 2008-12-14
I figured it out.  I was following instructions in the Ubuntu Kung Fu book on enhancing the font appearance.  Long story short, if I enable bitmap fonts the box is a thin vertical line.  If I don't everything is fine.
