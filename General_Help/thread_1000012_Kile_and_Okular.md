How can I configure Kile so that it updates an exsistng Okular window instead opening a new one when building Latex?

---

### Post by PatheticMoFo on 2008-12-21
Assuming okular is already open and looking at the PDF or PS which you are building, just keep it open. If you hit the LaTeX or PDFLaTeX buttons while okular is open, it will automatically update the file.
