### Post by Namarpus on 2008-07-06
Hi, I am new to Ubuntu.  Is there a way to "single click" an icon to start the associated application?

I assume there is a setting but I don't know where to find it.

( I am using a new installation of Ubuntu 8.04)

---

### Post by scragar on 2008-07-06
just go to places > home to get the file viewer open, then edit>preferences, go to the behaviors tab and turn it to single click instead of double click, easy as pie.
