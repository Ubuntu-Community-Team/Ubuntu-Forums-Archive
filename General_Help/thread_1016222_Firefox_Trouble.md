### Post by kg4tah on 2008-12-19
I have had this problem with firefox for some time now.  I finally found a fix to it tonight by going into the display properties and changing the visual effects to NONE.  That fixed my firefox taking up the entire screen.  This may not be a fix everyone will be happy with but I don't care about icons making little animated effects when I click them which is really all I saw the extra effects would create on my system.
