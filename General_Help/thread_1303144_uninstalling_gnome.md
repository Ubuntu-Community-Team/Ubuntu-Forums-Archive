### Post by XDevHald on 2009-10-27
Sorry to see you leave gnome, I find it to be much cleaner and sleek in it's ways of user management and design development with drag and drops for theme installations. If you REALLY want a 100% KDE desktop, download Kubuntu and you're set.

The following command in terminal will remove Gnome from your KDE desktop.

```
aptitude purge gnome gnome-core gnome-destop-environment
```
