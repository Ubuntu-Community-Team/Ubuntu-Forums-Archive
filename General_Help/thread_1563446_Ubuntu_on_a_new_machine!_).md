Well 64-bit gives you drastic speed improvements, it reduces compatibility a lot.

Since you mentioned Flash, 32 bit for sure. 64-bit WINE support sucks and is now discontinued. I used an old WINE, but it seems to work. I recommend 32-bit for Flash though.

Skype shouldn't have any problems, you can download the .deb (pretty much an installer) off their site, and check out [http://appdb.winehq.org]("http://appdb.winehq.org/") for the WINE app compatibility.

Finally, emulators shouldn't be much of a problem. Almost all will work on both arches and you can find them in Software Centre.
