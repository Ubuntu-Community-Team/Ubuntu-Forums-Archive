### Post by SeijiSensei on 2023-10-18
Take a look in System Settings > Hardware > Input Devices > Keyboard > Advanced. If you check the "Configure Keyboard Options" you can navigate down to Caps Lock behavior. Expanding that offers you an option to disable it.

You can do pretty much anything in KDE via System Settings.
