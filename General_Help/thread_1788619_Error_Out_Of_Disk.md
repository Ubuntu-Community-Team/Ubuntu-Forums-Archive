### Post by jerome1232 on 2011-06-23
Wubi means you were running Windows, stuck in the Ubuntu CD and ran the installation while Windows was still running. It's primarily a means of trying a full installation of Ubuntu out without mucking about with your partitions.

Side by side installation means that you stuck in the cd, rebooted your computer and ran the installation from the menu that came up. This is the ideal way to go about a permanent installation.
