### Post by Shay Stephens on 2007-02-04
If I remember right, the Canon SLR's don't act as a removable hard drive, so you can't just plug the camera in via the USB cable.  So your best bet is to just remove the memory card, and stick in a card reader and get the images onto the computer that way.

I have a Canon 20D, and I have installed two CF card readers in the floppy drive bays of my computer.  So I can download the photos off two cards at once.  So, any CF card reader you get your hands on should work just fine.
