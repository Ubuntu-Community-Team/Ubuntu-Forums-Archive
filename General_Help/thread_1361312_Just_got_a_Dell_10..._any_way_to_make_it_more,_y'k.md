### Post by snowpine on 2009-12-21
I have the Dell Mini 9 (the little cousin of yours) and I love it for web surfing and email! Dell makes good stuff. The Atom processor is comparable to an old Pentium 4, so don't expect blazing speed, but for modest internet needs, it's fine.

Do I understand correctly that you purchased this computer used? If so, I would recommend a re-install. You have no idea what the previous owner installed that might be slowing things down.

Also find out whether you have the 10 or the 10v. 10v is cool, but 10 requires the GMA500 "pouslbo" drivers... check out this forum's search feature and you'll find a tutorial to get it working correctly. Good luck!
