### Post by sdennie on 2009-08-14
Upgrading gtk is likely to break more things than it fixes unless you have some experience building applications from source.  I would file a bug on lauchpad and point them at the pidgin bug and see if they will pickup the patch.  If not, for a fairly trivial bug, it's probably best to wait until 9.10 rather than building your own version of gtk.
