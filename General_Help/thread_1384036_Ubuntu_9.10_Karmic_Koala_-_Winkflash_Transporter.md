### Post by Bucky Ball on 2012-12-16
9.10 is literally years out of support. This includes security updates. I would be reluctant to be on the net with that machine. You will not get much help with this and will be lucky to get much going.

I recommend a full install of 12.04 LTS, supported for five years, until 2017. If you don't like the new Unity desktop environment, perhaps you could try Xubuntu or one of the other flavours which don't use Unity. 

Good luck ...

PS: Your version of Wine is probably also well out of date, along with all your apps.
