### Post by robsoles on 2010-09-22
Different manufacturers like to make the key to enter BIOS different to each other.

The common one was [Delete] but some use [F2] and others [F1] and no doubt I am forgetting some other key I once found them using.

While the computer is starting (from power-on) there should be a brief message like "Hit [Del] to enter setup" - that is the invitation to access BIOS.

In BIOS settings look for Audio controls by going page to page and selecting items that lead to other pages to see if options are on any of the pages such as I described.

It may be that no option to disable certain output schemes for the sound card are available and it may also be that regardless of such options something software-wise has to be done for the system in question.
