### Post by daslinkard on 2012-10-01
IMAP provides a better method to access your mail from multiple  devices. If you check your email at work, on your mobile phone, and  again at home, IMAP ensures that new mail is accessible from any device  at any given time.


 Finally, IMAP offers a more stable experience overall. Whereas POP is  prone to losing messages or downloading the same messages multiple  times, IMAP avoids this through two-way syncing capabilities between  your mail clients and your web Gmail or Yahoo.
