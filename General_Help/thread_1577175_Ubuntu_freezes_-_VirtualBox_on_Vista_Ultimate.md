### Post by lmarmisa on 2010-09-18
My first recommendation is to disable the screensaver and the power management options.
Select System -> Preferences -> Screensaver, untick "Activate screensaver when computer is idle" and "Lock screen when screensaver is active" options, change the slider button to 2 hours snd click on the Power_Management button. Now select Never for Actions and Display. Close & Close.

Finally, a couple of questions:


[LIST=1]
[*] What visual effects are selected (System -> Preferences -> Appearance -> Visual effects)?. I got some problems selecting Normal. So, I recommend None.
[*]Have you installed VBox Guest Additions?. I recommend this feature. It's great.
[/LIST]
