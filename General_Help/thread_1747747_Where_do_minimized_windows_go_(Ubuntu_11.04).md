### Post by FerretWithASpork on 2011-05-03
What did you minimize? Anything you have running should be over there in addition to the sticky icons. If you open a chrome window and minimize it there should be a little white arrow on the left of the icon for chrome, that means that it's running, click the icon and it should bring up your minimized window.
