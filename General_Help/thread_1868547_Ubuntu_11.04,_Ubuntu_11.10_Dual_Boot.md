### Post by oldfred on 2011-10-24
I did a clean install with 10.10 and now still have every version since installed in 20 or 25GB root partitions plus a few other root partitions for tests. I do put all data in /mnt/data (ext3) or /mnt/shared where the shared is a NTFS partition for my Firefox & Thunderbird profiles to use in all systems including XP. But I am aggressive in moving data including hidden folders in /home  to my data partitions. My /home is about 1GB with most being .wine for Picasa as .wine is more difficult to move. I sometimes copy some user settings from one /home to another but do not attempt to copy all of /home from one to another system.

### Post by Mark Phelps on 2011-10-24
