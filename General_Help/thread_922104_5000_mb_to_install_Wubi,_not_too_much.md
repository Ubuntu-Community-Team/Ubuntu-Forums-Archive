### Post by gnuvistawouldbecool on 2008-09-17
OK, what you seem to be asking for is something that Ubuntu isn't -small and lightweight.  From the description of your computer, it sounds as if Ubuntu would only just run on it anyway, though Xubuntu might work better, as it is lighter.

However, for the purpose of what you want, you would be better off looking at a distribution like Puppy Linux or Damn Small Linux, as both are made to be small, Puppy is only 80 MB and DSL is only 50MB, though personally I would recommend Puppy, as it looks better and feels more workable, and for you it could be good since it is designed to resemble windows 98, at least superficially.

As for your later points, you seem to be describing the situation as if Ubuntu is the only Linux, which it isn't.  Ubuntu is made to compete with XP, Vista and OS X.  It isn't designed to be used for really old computers, and nor are it's competitors, either.
