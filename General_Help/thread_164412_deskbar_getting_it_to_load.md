### Post by engla on 2006-04-22
It's a panel applet, so it should be found like this:

Right-click on an empty spot in any of your panels (bottom & top, normally). Choose "Add to panel..." in the menu. In the new window that pops up, find deskbar-applet. It should be there somewhere, with either an arrow or a magnifying glass as icon. When you've found it, select and click "Add to panel"
