### Post by aysiu on 2006-11-19
It was pretty safe for me, but I know a lot of others experienced problems.

Best thing to do is back up before you do the upgrade. That way, in case things turn out badly, you can restore Dapper the way it was before.

### Post by aysiu on 2006-11-19
> **JohnPhys said:**
> This may seem like a ridiculous question, but what exactly should I back up?  Specifically, I don't have any critical data on the dapper partition, but I really don't want to have to spend hours tweaking my setup again if something goes wrong.  Suggestions?
You can [use PartImage to back up your entire Dapper partition exactly as is.](http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/partimage)

That way, if Edgy screws up, you can also use PartImage to restore your Dapper partition exactly as it was before.
