### Post by karhulitos on 2008-01-30
Hi,

I do not know about copying the local sent items to IMAP mailbox folder (drag & drop doesn't work I guess?). But, change:
"edit" -> "options" -> <your email account> -> "edit" -> "default settings" -> sent items folder
Change it to your IMAP sent-mail instead of the local default sent items folder.

Just to avoid you copying them again after you get answer how to copy current ones..
