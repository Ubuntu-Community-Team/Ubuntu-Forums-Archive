### Post by alex_anthony on 2008-06-19
It's a _good_idea_ not to draw previews for minimised apps. If you did, you would always be drawing the window. Minimizing gives a slight reduction in used resources. If you want to keep the previews, you can just put the windows onto other workspaces and not minimise.
You may also like the shelf plugin to put the windows out of the way, while having previews.
