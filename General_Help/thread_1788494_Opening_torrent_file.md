### Post by nothingspecial on 2011-06-23
You have not yet downloaded the video, merely the torrent file.

Right click it and choose to open it with transmission. That is the torrent program. 

After it has started you may want to go into the preferences of transmission and limit your download speed.

The idea of a torrent is that people download it from you while you download it from other people. It is courteous (but not necessary) to "upload" the same amount of data that you download if you see what I mean.

I generally limit my upload speed, to allow the download to happen faster. Then leave it uploading for a while.

Beware, most torrent files you find on the web are illegal.
