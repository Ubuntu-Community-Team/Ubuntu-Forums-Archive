### Post by Bartender on 2007-02-12
I've always just used the qwerty us version.  I don't know if UK keyboards are any different.  qwerty us is a standard 105 key keyboard.  Chances are you can just accept the three or four  screens it goes thru.  There's a color depth, resolution, etc.

One thing that's happened to me when I finally get to the partition map is the toolbar on the bottom of the page sometimes doesn't diplay properly.  You might only be able to see the top of the toolbar.  Right-click on it.  There's a button for resizing or something like that.  Left-click on that, resize it up one pixel from 48 pixels, then close.  The toolbar should show up properly.  I could then resize it back down to 48 and it would still display properly, so it's just a funny glitch of some kind.
