### Post by quixote on 2009-10-10
If you happen to have an external hard drive that connects with a USB, that's probably the easiest.  

When you say you have ethernet cables, do they just go to the router, or do you have another computer / other network drives / or something else?

Briefly, what you want to do is attach your other medium, whatever it is, boot with a LiveCD, mount the other medium, and copy or rsync.  It shouldn't be difficult, but without knowing more about what you have it's hard to give any specific commands to try.

### Post by howefield on 2009-10-10
