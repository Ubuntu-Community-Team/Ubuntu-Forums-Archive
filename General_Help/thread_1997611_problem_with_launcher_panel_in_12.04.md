### Post by zombifier25 on 2012-06-05
1. You search for it in the Unity Dash and drag and drop the icon from there.
2. Right click the icon and choose "Unlock from Launcher".
3. The list of apps locked into the launcher is probably stored in dconf. Sorry, I can't check because I'm on Windows.
