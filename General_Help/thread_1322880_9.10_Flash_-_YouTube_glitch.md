### Post by hwttdz on 2009-11-11
This is documented, search would have turned it up.

sudo gedit /usr/lib/nspluginwrapper/i386/linux/npviewer

Add this before the last line of text "export GDK_NATIVE_WINDOWS=1", then save and restart firefox.

Here's one source, there are also a few others in the forums.  
[http://helpforlinux.blogspot.com/2009/11/i-cannot-click-on-flash-in-ubuntu.html](http://helpforlinux.blogspot.com/2009/11/i-cannot-click-on-flash-in-ubuntu.html)
