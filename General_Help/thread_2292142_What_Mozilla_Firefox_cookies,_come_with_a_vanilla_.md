### Post by ajgreeny on 2015-08-25
Get the FF add-on "[Cookieculler]("https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/cookieculler/?src=ss")" which will let you protect any cookies you want to keep, eg those with saved logins or whatever, and then hit the "Remove all cookies" button to clear the rest.

There several other cookie managers for FF but that is the one I have used for some time.
