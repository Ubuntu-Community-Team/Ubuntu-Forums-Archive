### Post by tredegar on 2011-01-14
Your computer has failed to boot. I do not know why this has happened.

Ubuntu 9.04 and 9.10 are both out of date, and no longer supported.

10.04 is the current "Long Term Support" (recommended for people who do not want to reinstall "the latest" every six months-1yr.)

I suggest you download a copy of 10.04 from [here]("http://www.ubuntulinux.org/desktop/get-ubuntu/download")  burn it to CD and boot from that.

Then Select "Try Ubuntu" (NOT "Install")

Then you should be able to rescue your files from your HDD, and save then to a USB HDD.

Are your emails, bookmarks etc. important to you, or are you just interested in your music / documents / photos?

Then you can install a modern, supported version of ubuntu.
