### Post by TQLeaman on 2013-11-01
Hi,

First of all, check that you haven't got multiple keyboard formats active by looking at "Region and Language" under the settings screen. Specifically, you are wanting to check the "Input Sources" tab and remove / replace English (US) with English (UK) for instance.

Failing that, if I flip my English (UK) keyboard, to English (US), then the tilde (~) is accessible via SHIFT + ` (which is the top left key next to "1").

Hope that helps,

TQ
