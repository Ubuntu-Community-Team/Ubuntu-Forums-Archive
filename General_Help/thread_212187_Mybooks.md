### Post by cssutto on 2006-07-12
For anyone else that is interested, Mybooks will not cut it.

I downloaded the 30 day trial and have had several email exchanges with the tech person.

I am required by the IRS to pay withholding on the 1th of each month for the previous month.  In other words on July 15th I pay for the month of June.

They have no way to calculate that.  One would have to do it by hand.

PT allows you to print out a statement showing all of your tax liability for any period you select.  For instance, some companies are so large the IRS requires a weekly payment.   PT can print out what you need for that..

Mybooks only prints the quarterly 941.  Period.

I could go on and on because that is only an example.

You can not design your own forms or checks.  With PT you can.

For instance, I took a CA report and modified it to print the NCSUI101 and it works great.

Writing a check in Mybooks is a major chore.  You go through more steps than would be required in the US Government.

They will never make it.

I will have to look somewhere else.

CSSJR
