### Post by Hilikme on 2010-11-02
Hi,

The answer might be outdated but it might be useful for others :
 
To display the exchange rate on the taskbar I use the Invest applet for Gnome ([http://library.gnome.org/users/invest-applet/stable/invest-applet-intro.html.en](http://library.gnome.org/users/invest-applet/stable/invest-applet-intro.html.en))

Then I just add the currency I want to convert in the preferences ex :
"USDEUR=X" or "GBPEUR=X" just be sure the currency codes are correct.

Et Voilà
