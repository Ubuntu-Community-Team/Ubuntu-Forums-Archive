### Post by doas777 on 2011-05-29
well, there is a tool that is perfect for this particular usecase, called PhotoRec. if the images have not yet been overwritten by new data, they can likely be recovered.

be sure that you recover them to another drive instead of the one they were deleted off of. it is possible for you to accidentally recover a file and overwrite others that you had wished to recover.

here is the ubuntu data recovery documentation. it will get you started with photorec. I usually use the ubuntu-rescue-remix live CD for this kind of task.
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/DataRecovery](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/DataRecovery)
[http://www.waytoubuntu.com/2010/01/photorec-digital-picture-and-file.html](http://www.waytoubuntu.com/2010/01/photorec-digital-picture-and-file.html)

remember, dont wait. the longer they remain deleted, the less likely you will be sucessful with the recovery.
