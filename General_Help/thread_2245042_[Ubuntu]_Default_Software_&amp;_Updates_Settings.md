### Post by ian-weisser on 2014-09-22
Some of the out-of-the-box settings are not preserved. They can be changed back (if you remember what they are), but cannot be 'restored' unless you backed them up.

The out-of-the-box settings are generally to maximize convenience, and are not 'optimized'. Example: The default mirror setting may be slower than other mirrors in your country.

It also depends upon the setting.

Did you have a particular setting in mind?
