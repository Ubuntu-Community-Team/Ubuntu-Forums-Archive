### Post by hackb0y294 on 2010-04-14
Check for an e-mail in one of your folders that is extremely large, or of a strange format, and delete it or move it to an unused folder. Or you can go to your e-mail provider's website and delete the message there. You can also go to Edit>Preferences>Mail Accounts, select your account and click edit, then click Recieving Options and deselect 'Check for new messages in all folders' if it's selected. I had this problem once with a particular e-mail, and this solved it.
