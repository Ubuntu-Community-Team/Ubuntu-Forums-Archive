### Post by pravuil on 2006-07-09
Might just be the settings in compiz. You can disable the wobbly feature completely by typing gset-compiz in a terminal and deactivate the wobbly plugin. You could also increase the friction to keep the window from wobbly wildly in the plugin options of gset-compiz.

### Post by lazyd2 on 2006-07-09
Open gset-compiz, go to "Plugins" and "woobly". Scroll to the bottom where it says "Window types" and uncheck "Map" from "Unknown"
