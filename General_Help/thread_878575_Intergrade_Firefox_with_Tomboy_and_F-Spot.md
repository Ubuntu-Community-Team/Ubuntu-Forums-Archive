### Post by lvlo on 2008-08-03
There are two useful addons for Firefox:

[**Tomfox**]("https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/8276") - helps you create note from selection in Firefox via right-click menu.

[**F-Spot Importer**]("https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/7091") - imports pictures into F-spot via right-click menu or  drag&drop.


**NOTE**: their are still in development - may contain some bugs.
