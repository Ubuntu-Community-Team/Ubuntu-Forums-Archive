### Post by kaltoza on 2010-02-16
You can try reset the control to its default settings.
Right click on the drawing panel and select 
'Customize Toolbar...'
Scroll down the list under the 'Toolbar' tab util you get
to the 'Text' item. Highlight the item and select the
'Restore Default Command' option under the 'Modify' drop down
list.

Let me know if this works.
