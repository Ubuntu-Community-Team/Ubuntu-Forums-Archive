### Post by mtod1 on 2009-08-21
Hi all.

I thought I'd post a quick tip.

Check to see if your ISP is hosting an Ubuntu mirror.
If they are your Ubuntu updates, downloads and uploads will generally be counted as free traffic. (particularly useful if you have a download/upload limit per month).

Have a look in 'Software Sources' and instead of choosing the default server select 'Other' (i think) and see if your ISP is listed.

Official Ubuntu mirror repositories are only listed after meeting specific conditions.

Ubuntu relies on users to change their repositories post-install to something better than the default country repository because every (and I mean **every**) time there is new version released the default repositories are hammered and these forums are flooded with complaints (and posts pleading with people to use alternate repositories).
