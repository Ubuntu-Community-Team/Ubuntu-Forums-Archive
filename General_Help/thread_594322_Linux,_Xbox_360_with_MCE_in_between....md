### Post by loadeddreams on 2007-10-28
I actually do what you said last.

I have my shares setup via samba and mount them as network drives in Vista, and use Transcode360 / XBMC to stream my .avi's afterwards and it works pretty well.... Just set T360 to transcode for like... 15 seconds before streaming begins that way you get a good chunk to begin with because there might be slowdowns.
I also have wired network between the ubuntu to vista box, and wireless from xbox to vista box... Did that so I'd have good bandwidth for all the needs :)

good luck
