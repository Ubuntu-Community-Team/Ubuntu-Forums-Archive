### Post by arubislander on 2010-11-08
Maybe you should try deleting your aMSN configuration if it is corrupt. Try these steps: 
Quit aMSN. Go to Places > Home. Press CONTROL-H to show the hidden files. find the .amsn folder and rename it to .amsn_bak. Start aMSN. If it works then delete the renamed folder. When you are done, press CONTROL-H to hide the hidden files, makes your home folder look less cluttered.
