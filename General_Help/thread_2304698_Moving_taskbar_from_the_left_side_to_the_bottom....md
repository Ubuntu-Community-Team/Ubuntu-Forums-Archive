### Post by buzzingrobot on 2015-11-29
No, the Unity Launcher cannot be relocated.  It can be set to hide and the "pressure"' needed to make it visible can be set very high (use Unity Tweak Tool). You can find explanations of how to do this in combination with a third-party dock, like Plank or Docky, that can be positioned along the bottom edge.  Those docks, though, will not include the capability to launch the Dash, etc.

BTW, a look at the PPA that article requires shows it has been abandoned since November of 2011. (I wonder if these  derelict PPA sites ought to be fenced off somehow?)
