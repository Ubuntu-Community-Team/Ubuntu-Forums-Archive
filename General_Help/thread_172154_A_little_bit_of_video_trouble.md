### Post by louis_nichols on 2006-05-08
well, my only other idea, seeing I cannot see the xorg.0.log file, is to use the vesa driver, which I've seen working pretty well when odds were against X starting.

So, all you have to do for this, in that xorg.conf file, is to replace the line

Driver  "nvidia"

with

Driver "vesa"

Then (re)start gdm
