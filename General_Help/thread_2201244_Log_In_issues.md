Okey, here is how to (hopefully) solve the login-issue, press CTRL+ALT+F1 to get to a terminal, enter your username and password there.

To get the graphicscard working properly, just go into dash (unitys startmenu) and type software and updates go to -> additional drivers and select your driver.
