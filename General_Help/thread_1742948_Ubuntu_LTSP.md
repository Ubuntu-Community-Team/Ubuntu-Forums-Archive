### Post by Lars Noodén on 2011-04-29
You'll want to start by looking at the [community LTSP documentation](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/UbuntuLTSP).  Your LAN should be as fast as possible, if you can have 1Gbps that's great.  Since your outbound connection might be 3g, that will be slow, so you'll probably want to speed things up with a proxy cache once LTSP is up and running.
