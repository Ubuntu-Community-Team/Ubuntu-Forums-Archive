Which addon's do you use with firefox? 

I use addblock+, flashblock, Web of Trust, and better privacy. I highly recommend at least addblock+.

What you saw isn't really a "threat"(as in, nothing was exploited to display that ad) it's just social engineering attempt to get you to download and buy their "anti-virus". The addons addblock + and flashblock ought to be able to block the vast majority of those types of adds.
