### Post by mscad on 2007-10-12
Why yes, you can watch TV shows in Ubuntu.

Open Firefox and click in the Google bar. Type in
"EasyUbuntu" without the quotes and you are on your way.

Ubuntu doesn't ship with some MP3 codecs or video codecs that prevent you from the whole
Web 2.0 experience due to some licensing issues (red tape).

EasyUbuntu will address those issues and you will be able to go to abc.com, nbc.com and watch the shows from your computer's easy chair right in the comfort of your USA home. If you are not in the USA some shows block your access to those shows. Google for Proxy servers if you are outside the USA and enjoy the tv experience from your computer tube, lcd, plasma, or OLEd.

Enjoy, Ubuntu and fly and be free.
