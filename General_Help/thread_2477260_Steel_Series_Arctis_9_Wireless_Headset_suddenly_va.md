### Post by TheFu on 2022-07-19
pavucontrol is how to control audio and microphone devices. If they are seen as audio devices by the OS, then pavucontrol should control them.  Start from the right tab, enable only 1 output and 1 microphone device, then work left in each tab and ensure the device you want is enbled and set to 100%.  The far left 2 tabs are were the gain and volume are controlled. Only those should be touched once the other 3 tabs are set.
