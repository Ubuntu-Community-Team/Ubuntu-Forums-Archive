### Post by jasmuz on 2007-11-28
For the music applet, there is a gnome panel applet called "music-applet" you might want to check that out via synaptic.

For doing websearches there is the "googlizer" applet or the "search" applet already installed in your system. 

Remember to install them via synaptic, then add them to your panel via right click on the bar.

Good luck.
