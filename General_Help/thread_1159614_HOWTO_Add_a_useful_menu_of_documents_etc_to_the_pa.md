### Post by PaulWhipp on 2009-05-14
I find myself frequently working with a small number of files over time. The list gradually changes but its quite long and I like having a menu on the panel that lets me get to them.

The "Menu Bar" option in Add to Panel does not do what you would expect (Bug #311057).

The Drawer just gives you icons which is not very useful for half a dozen documents etc.

The command line is cool but the files are often in different folders so its inconvenient. You can set up shortcuts to each doc or even have a little script to reference each one by a short memorable name to make things easy but sometimes its just nicer to have a menu.

The solution:

Right click your Application menu and select "Edit menus"

Add a new menu under Applications. Give it a handy name like "Work".

Add commands to access your documents to this menu.

Now you have the menu you want but its buried in your Applications menu.

To get it onto the panel, right click on it when its displayed, choose "Entire menu" and "Add this as menu to panel"

Now you should have your menu there on the panel so that all those documents and other things you need in your own personal panel menu are just two clicks away.
