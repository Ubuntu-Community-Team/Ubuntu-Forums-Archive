### Post by Sef on 2008-06-11
> Could someone give me a step-by-step guide to installing it on Gutsy?

Applications > Add/Remove > Show: set to "All Available Applications" > Search: MPlayer > click on mplayer box > Apply Changes > Apply > close

For the codecs: [Codecs]("http://www.ubuntugeek.com/install-mplayer-and-multimedia-codecs-libdvdcss2w32codecs-in-ubuntu-710-gutsy-gibbon.html")

"All Available Applications" is the same as uncommenting the repositories.  (uncomment = remove the # before the line. # = do not read to the machine.)
