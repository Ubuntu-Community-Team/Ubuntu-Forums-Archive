### Post by lisati on 2009-11-20
First try closing your browser, disconnecting from the network, restarting your mobile device, connect again and open up a new browser.

If that doesn't help, it's possible that you might need to check the settings for your connection. These usually depend on which GSM service provider that you connect to.
