### Post by mattwestfall on 2010-10-26
So that you do not wear out the stereo jack that is hard-wired to your laptop or box:

...simply get a 6-inch stereo cord that permanently sits in the jack, and then wear out the female end of THAT one! Much easier to replace.

Just a common sense thing that is working for me. :)
