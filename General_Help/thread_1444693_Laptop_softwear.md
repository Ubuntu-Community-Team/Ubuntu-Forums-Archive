### Post by bpalone on 2010-04-01
Are you sure that 1) the drive is still working  2) that the CD isn't bad.

If CD drive isn't working or the CD is bad it will just march on through the boot order until it finds something to boot from or fail.  You might also try setting the BIOS to only Boot from the CD.  That would remove the possibility of it being a case where the hardware was responding to slowly.

Just some thoughts, good luck.
