### Post by seawolf167 on 2011-06-29
Icons on the left = unity, icons on the top = gnome.

Boot to the "icons on the left mode", then click the power button in the upper right, select "Login Screen", then select "Ubuntu Classic" from the drop down menu. Logoff/login and you should be good to go.
