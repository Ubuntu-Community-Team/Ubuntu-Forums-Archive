### Post by Falcorian on 2007-01-26
I use my iPod with Amarok no problems. The slightly annoying part though is that to sync playlists you have to right click each one (Amarok, at least on Gnome, doesn't let me select multiple lists and right click to sync all of them).

However, it does update playcounts and ratings from the iPod, so that's nice.

All and all, it works well enough, and better than anything else I've found on Linux (since my playlists are heavily based on tagging, rating, play counts and whatnot, I need it to be integrated with my music Library/Player).
