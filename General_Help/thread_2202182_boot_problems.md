I have never been lucky with updates. Always get the problem you're having. To me, it is always easier to do a fresh install of the most up to date version of the distro than trying to figure out what caused the problem.

Most of the time, the problem you're having got something to do with the video driver. What card are you using? Also "lspci -k" on terminal will tell you what driver is being used for your card.
