### Post by fragos on 2007-03-04
I let the burn software determine the fastest burn rate it can use and don't have problems.  Two things determine burn rate.  First is the drive itself and second is the media which will be marked as to the fastest burn rate supported.  Cheaper media has slower burn rates.
