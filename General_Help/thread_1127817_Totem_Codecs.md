### Post by Vaedrah on 2009-04-16
After some unrelated post-browsing I found syntax that seemed to work

sudo apt-get -y install gstreamer0.10-ffmpeg gstreamer0.10-plugins-bad gstreamer0.10-plugins-bad-multiverse gstreamer0.10-plugins-ugly gstreamer0.10-plugins-ugly-multiverse

Totem now plays with codecs that would not download through synaptic etc.

I am sure other solutions exist for equally legitimate downloads where isp setups are overly constraining.:popcorn:
