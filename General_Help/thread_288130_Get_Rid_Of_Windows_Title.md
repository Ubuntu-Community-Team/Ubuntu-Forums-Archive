### Post by _simon_ on 2006-10-29
You need to open Emerald Theme manager
Click on EDIT (middle tab)
Click on Titlebar (2nd tab from the right)

In the Title-bar Object Layout (last option on right hand side) remove the T
If you want to remove the icon as well then also remove the I
There's a key underneath that section explaining what each letter means.

Then press SAVE

Done! :)
