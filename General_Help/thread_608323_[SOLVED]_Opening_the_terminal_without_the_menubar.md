### Post by enchance on 2007-11-09
Can I open the gnome terminal without the menubar? I already took out the scrollbars, now it's just the menubar I want to take out.

---

### Post by enchance on 2007-11-10
I got it! I simply went to System>Preferences>Main Menu and edited the Terminal icon to go to
```

gnome-terminal --hide-menubar

```
