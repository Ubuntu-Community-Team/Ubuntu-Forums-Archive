### Post by vanadium on 2007-06-13
Because Ctrl+C and Ctrl+V have their specific meaning in terminals (since many years), the keys for copying/pasting text into a terminal is Shift+Ctrl+C and Shift+Ctrl+V. I struggeled myself a bit to get used to this, but it is going better every day ;)
