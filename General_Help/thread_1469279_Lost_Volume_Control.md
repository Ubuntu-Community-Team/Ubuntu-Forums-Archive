### Post by jediken21 on 2010-05-02
I accidentally removed the volume control icon on the top panel of my desktop. How do I get it back. I'm on 10.04 if that matters.

---

### Post by jerenept on 2010-05-02
You can right-click on an empty area on the panel and select 'Add to Panel'. then scroll down to Indicator Applet. Drag this onto the panel, or click the 'Add to Panel' button and you're good to go!
