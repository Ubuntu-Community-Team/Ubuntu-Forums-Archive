### Post by kostkon on 2009-06-28
Go to your sound preferences (i.e. *System &#8594; Preferences &#8594; Sound*) and select the *Devices* tab.

In the *Default Mixer Tracks* section select the device (in your case your MS LX-3000) from the *Device* drop-down menu and in the list below select the volume level that you want to control (in most cases you will have a *Master* volume to select).

Hope that helps.
