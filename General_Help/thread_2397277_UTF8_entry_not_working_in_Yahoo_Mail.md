### Post by lawrence-joy on 2018-07-27
When using my Windows 10 OS I would call up Google Chrome and then call up Yahoo Mail. I would be able to get upper case Omega by pushing and holding the Alt key and then enter 2-3-4 on the numeric keypad. Now that I have Ubuntu 18.04 LTS OS on a 2nd SSD I have used LibreOffice Writer and used UTF8 entry 'U+2126'Alt-X or 'U+03A9'Alt-X to get the upper case Omega symbol. However, when I called up Firefox and then went to Yahoo Mail neither entry worked. Is there some other entry sequence to get the UTF8 symbols?

---

### Post by The Cog on 2018-07-27
Ctrl-Shift-U, 2, 1, 2, 6, Enter or Space.

P.S. Linky: [https://superuser.com/questions/59418/how-to-type-special-characters-in-linux](https://superuser.com/questions/59418/how-to-type-special-characters-in-linux)
