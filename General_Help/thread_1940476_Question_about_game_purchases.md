### Post by PhantomTurtle on 2012-03-13
You can reinstall your purchased software after you sign in with your Ubuntu account. Just choose File &#8594; Reinstall Previous Purchases.


Source: [https://help.ubuntu.com/11.04/software-center/C/commercial-reinstalling.html]("https://help.ubuntu.com/11.04/software-center/C/commercial-reinstalling.html")
