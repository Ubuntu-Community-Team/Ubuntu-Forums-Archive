### Post by Chinedu on 2009-06-01
If i minimize any window in my ubuntu 8.04, it disappears. I can only call it up with ALT+TAB. Pls how can I fix this.

---

### Post by LinuxRules1 on 2009-06-01
Right click on one of the panels on your desktop and click on add to panel. Then scroll down until you see Window List, select it and then click on add. Then your windows should appear in the panel.

---

### Post by Legace on 2009-06-01
Also, make sure the windows don't mimize to another workspace.
