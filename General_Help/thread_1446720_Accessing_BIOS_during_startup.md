### Post by dnairb on 2010-04-04
An OS, *any* OS, doesn't start until *after* the machine has booted past the BIOS.

The key(s) you use to access the BIOS setup screen will not have changed because you now have Ubuntu as your OS.

Check your keyboard - is it plugged in properly, if it is a wireless keyboard, are the batteries still OK?...
