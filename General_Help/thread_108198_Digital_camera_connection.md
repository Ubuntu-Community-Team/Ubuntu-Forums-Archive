### Post by canadianwriterman on 2005-12-25
Here's another reason that Ubuntu is great!

I got my wife a Kodak C330 digital camera for Christmas. It included an install CD for Windows and Mac and warned that it would not work without the proprietary software. There's even some user feedback on the Web that warns That the C330 will not work within Linux:

[http://www.techgiftideas.com/electronics-172282-B0009MFV0I-Kodak_EasyShare_C330_4MP_Digital_Camera_with_3x_Optical_Zoom.html](http://www.techgiftideas.com/electronics-172282-B0009MFV0I-Kodak_EasyShare_C330_4MP_Digital_Camera_with_3x_Optical_Zoom.html)

Just because I'm a disbeliever, I simply plugged in the USB cable, connected it to the camera, turned on the camera and voila! I got I dialogue asking if I wanted to import the picture, I said "Yes" and up it came in gthumb.

The moral of the story is: Ubuntu is a lot more flexible than you'd ever imagine. For this camera to simply "plug and play" is an incredible testament to the work done to make Ubuntu "just work" (as much as possible, anyway).

So, if you get a digital camera this Christmas, just plug it in and see what happens. You may be pleasantly surprised!
