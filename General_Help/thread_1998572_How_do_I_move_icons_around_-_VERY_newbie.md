### Post by na5h on 2012-06-07
Just click and hold down the icon you wish to move for a few seconds. If you use Unity 3D you can also simply drag the icon off the bar (by dragging it to the right) and drop it back onto the bar at a place of your choice. You can add new apps right off the dash by dragging and dropping them onto the launcher (Unity 2D: open the app, right click on the launcher icon and choose "lock to launcher"). 

Off-topic tip: Holding down the Super-button will show you some useful keyboard shortcuts for using the dash, workspaces, etc. (Unity 3D).
