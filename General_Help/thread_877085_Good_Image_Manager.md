### Post by coffeecat on 2008-08-01
> **mman426 said:**
> I just tried gthumb and it doesn't let you sort by resolution.

I know this doesn't answer your question, but let me tell you about a very useful, but little known, feature of gthumb. Open it and navigate to a folder with several images. Click on one of them in the right pane and then ctrl-A to select them all. Or select a number of them however you like to do this. Right-click on one of them and select rename. It's quite a powerful little batch renaming utility. Quite handy with all those PICT0001.jpg filenames that cameras generate when you want something more useful.

The only pity is that it only works with the image types it can handle, not non-image files.

**Edit**: actually it does answer your question - sort of. Look at the Template field in the rename window. Choose %s as part of the name and you get image size in the name. Put that variable in the right place and you could sort your images by size.
