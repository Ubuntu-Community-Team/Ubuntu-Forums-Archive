### Post by kyphi on 2008-04-04
When you say that you want to share a dial-up connection **from** XP **to** Ubuntu, do you mean that you want to share a dial-up connection **between** XP and Ubuntu so that you can access the internet from either operating system ?

If that is the case then you do not need a network setup disc.

Go to System -> Administration -> Networking, click on the Modem Connection and then on Properties.  Fill in the information required and you are done.

I assume that your modem was recognised in Ubuntu.

Good Luck
