### Post by ninorc on 2013-06-19
Reviving this thread as a meenee user, recently re-enthused by excellent post-sales customer service;-)
 I had given it a five star review on eBay, which might be part of the reason why meenee were happy to honour the warranty when one of the hinges started breaking. The build quality of the 13" laptop I got off eBay for £160 in January was poor, but what d'you expect for so cheap? Nothing about this laptop is premium, but what do you expect for £255 (current eBay price)? Well, I did not expect it to start falling apart quite so soon, but meenee readily agreed to fix it. In fact they have returned a new, superior machine with my old hard drive in it. So, I recommend meenees for round the house. But it is a bit flimsy and its battery life is poor, rarely better than one hour, so it won't travel well. For a sturdy machine, I hear Lenovo is the brand to go for.
