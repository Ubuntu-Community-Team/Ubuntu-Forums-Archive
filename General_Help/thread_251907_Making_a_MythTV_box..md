### Post by GameGod on 2006-09-06
Personally, I've never had any trouble with hardware incompatibilities with my Myth boxes. I just buy the hardware, and it works!

Definitely get a Hauppauge PVR-250 (or similar) TV capture card, they rock...

Currently my main Myth box uses:
Antec NSK1300
Asus A8N-VM mobo
256mb DDR RAM (More than this would be nice)
PVR-250 capture card
GeForce FX 5200 (PCI - I got this puppy for $10 CAD, lol... It has no fan too, which keeps the noise down)
Western Digital WD1200JB hard drive

Good luck!
