### Post by southernman on 2007-09-02
You can buy an academic version of dreamweaver at a much reduced cost. Just need to show proof of academic eligibility. This version has complete functionality as I've heard and read. Your school can surely do this for you, or there are many e retailers that offer this. Just watch for bogus (read pirate) sellers on legit looking websites as well as ebay.

One OSS option is quanta plus available in the repos.
