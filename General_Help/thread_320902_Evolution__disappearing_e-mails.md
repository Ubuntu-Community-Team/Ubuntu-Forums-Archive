### Post by fraki on 2006-12-18
If you want to download all your mail, go to GMail settings, under Forwarding and POP settings and "Enable POP for all mail" (this will download all your mail that is found on GMail). When all your mail has been downloaded, set the option to "Enable POP only for mail that arrives from now on" so that only new mail is downloaded. If you access your GMail account with another POP client and download some mail items then the said downloaded mail items would not be downloaded again. Never had any "disappearing e-mails" personally.
