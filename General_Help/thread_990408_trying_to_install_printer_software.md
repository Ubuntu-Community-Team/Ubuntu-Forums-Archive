### Post by cariboo on 2008-11-22
Your Windows drivers aren't needed as the printer support is builtin. As you may have noticed Linux is not Windows, most of what you learned about windows does not apply here

Your printer is well supported. To install the printer go to System-->Administration-->Printing, click new printer, and follow the prompts. TO print CD labels, you can use Applications-->Graphics-->Gimp. or you can search in Synaptic for other cd/dvd printing applications.
