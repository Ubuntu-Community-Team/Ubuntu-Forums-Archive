### Post by shva on 2009-07-14
I use MSN in pidgin, but every time when I close the chatting windows and open it again, the previous chatting lines are gone. So I am wondering if something can be done to keep the chatting record in pidgin, just like gtalk can keep the chatting history in gmail. Thank you.

---

### Post by ManiacDan on 2009-07-14
Yes.  Turn on logging (it's probably already on) in the preferences.

To keep your last few lines of chats in the window when you re-open it, enable the "History" plugin.

-Dan

### Post by ManiacDan on 2009-07-14
No, the logging that shows up in your gmail account is stored by google as the messages pass through their servers.  If you enable off-the-record messaging in your pidgin client your messages in gmail will be gibberish.  

All pidgin logging is done to the local filesystem.  There are filesystem plugins that use gmail as a distributed filesystem, but it won't show up in your "chats" link like you want it to.

-Dan
