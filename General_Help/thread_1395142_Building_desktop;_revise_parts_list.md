### Post by cascade9 on 2010-02-02
Parts list looks fine to me. 

BTW, get 4GB. 6GB is normally found in triple channel motherboards (socket 1366), it makes sense there. 6GB in dual cahnnel makes less sense. Its fills up all your RAM slows for a mere 2GB gain. Better to just get 4GB and leave 2 slots open, or spend a bit more and get 8GB (either 4GBx2 or 2GBx4).
