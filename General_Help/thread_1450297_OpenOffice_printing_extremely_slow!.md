### Post by klausner on 2010-06-04
This is probably more general than just OO. If you have installed the 64 bit version of Ubuntu, your problem may be with your printer's drivers. This is definitely true with the Brother HL-2170W, and probably others.

One workaround is to delete your printer, and reinstall with the Generic PCL 5e Foomatic driver. See [this thread]("http://ubuntu-virginia.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?p=9411925#post9411925") for instructions. Almost all printers these days support PCL5.
