### Post by Mark Phelps on 2011-04-10
Sigh .. once gain, folks presuming that you can change whatever you want and STILL retain the original warranty!

The warranty only covers the original equipment with its original software -- and that includes the OS.  While the seller COULD refuse warranty service if you change application software, they generally do not because it's expected that folks will do that over time.

However, changing the OS will CERTAINLY void the seller's warranty.
