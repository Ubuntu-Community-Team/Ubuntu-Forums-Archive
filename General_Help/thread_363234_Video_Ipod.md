### Post by Marsolin on 2007-02-17
I don't own a Video iPod, but I've never heard of a feature that isn't supported on Linux with them. The programs you mentioned are a couple options for file management. Other programs like [HandBrake]("http://linuxappfinder.com/package/handbrake"), [podencoder]("http://linuxappfinder.com/package/podencoder"), and [thin liquid film]("http://linuxappfinder.com/package/thinliquidfilm") can convert videos to the iPod format.
