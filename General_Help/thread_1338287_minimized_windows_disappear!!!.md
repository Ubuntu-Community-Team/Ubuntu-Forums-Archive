### Post by amingv on 2009-11-27
It looks like you may have removed your task manager.
To get it again, follow these steps:

1-Right click anywhere on the bottom panel, choose "Unlock Widgets"
(If that option doesn't appear, just go to the next step)
2-Right click anywhere on the bottom panel>Panel Options>Add Widgets
3-In the new window that appears look for "task manager", select it and click on "add widget". The task manager should appear again.
4-Right click on it>Panel Options>Panel Settings
5-Move the task manager to your preferred position on the bottom panel.
6-Click on "Lock Widgets"

Did that solve the problem? Post any issues you may encounter.
