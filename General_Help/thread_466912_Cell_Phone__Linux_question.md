### Post by reclusivemonkey on 2007-06-07
I have a Nokia N80. It will connect to Ubuntu via bluetooth and USB cable; it appears as a standard USB device and you can use drag and drop. For syncing, I use zyb.com, which is great as I have an offline backup as well. I then just download a .vcf file and import it to Evolution, although my phone is my main contacts manager.
