> **OpenGuard said:**
> **Audacious** - sound too low .. 1/2 of my actual volume

**Banshee** - too bloated with all kinds of useless ( for me ) features, like album art fetching, suggestions, etc.

The relatively low volume in **Audacious** can be easily fixed. Enter the Audacious Preferences dialog (Control-P) and look at the 'Replay Gain' section. Uncheck the 'enable replay gain' box, or adjust the Default Gain to a less-negative value. The effect is more volume output. (Note: They warn that settings over 0 may cause clipping.)
