### Post by mustang on 2006-02-22
I'm pretty sure dpkg will get rid of 8.51 for you and install 8.52 leaving .opera untouched. It'd be a good idea to backup .opera though.

### Post by buntuguy on 2006-02-22
Just installed 8.52 over 8.51 by

sudo dpkg -i opera_8.52.deb

without any problem. My profile is untouched.
