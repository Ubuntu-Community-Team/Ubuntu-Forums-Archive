### Post by kvonb on 2007-10-11
You could try ripping the magnet out of an old drive, they are mega-strong.

Then place the magnet on different parts of the drive while it is spinning, trying to get as close to flat faces of the platters as you can.

The drive body and usually the lid are made of alloy which should allow the magnetic field to pass through.

No guarantee, but it might work.

You could always go for an MRI and wear the drive as a necklace ;)
