### Post by John Bean on 2010-01-24
> **emveedee said:**
> I can't find the right setting in the keyboard preferences window.Thanks!

Click on the "Layouts" tab and click "Layout Options" and select "Compose key position" from the list. Check the key you want to use (I use right ctrl) and you can produce accented and special characters by simply hitting the compose key then the two ordinary characters you want to combine - like (say) "c" and "0" to make a "©" sign. Dead easy.
