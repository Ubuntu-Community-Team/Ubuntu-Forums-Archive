### Post by stinkeye on 2011-12-08
This can happen when your gfx card overheats.
My nvidia card was cutting out when it reached 104 °C.
Found the fan wasn't working.
If you have installed the proprietary driver, nvidia-settings will show temp.
Mine normally sits around 50-55 °C.
