### Post by Mark Phelps on 2010-05-29
Every new version of Ubuntu comes with changes -- some have no effect, others cause machines to fail.

The only way to know for YOUR machine is to boot from a LiveCD of the newest version and see how well it works.  

Having others tell you it works on their machines is of no relevance to your machine -- unless it's exactly the same make and model as yours.
