### Post by FountainDew on 2008-12-13
I'm a newbie but I can tell you what I did to get spell check to work again.

1.) Tools > Extension Manager > Get more extensions here...
2.) Searched "english" and selected the a suitable dictionary of your choice
3.) Select "Open with OpenOffice"
4.) Restart OpenOffice Word
5.) Check Tools > Languages > For Paragraph > More... and check to see if your language is selected

Hope that helps, my memory isn't too great but thats what I can tell ya. Good luck.
