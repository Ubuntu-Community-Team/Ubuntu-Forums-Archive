### Post by snowpine on 2010-04-29
I agree with the advice in general, but should also point out that 10.04.1 is a completely arbitrary "target"--there would be no massive difference between upgrading 1 day before the 10.04.1 release vs. 1 day after. As far as I know, the ".1" at the end is just the label for a new CD image "snapshot" that contains all the upgrades as of that date. It is not a new release with new features and it is no different than installing today and keeping up to date on your upgrades. :)
