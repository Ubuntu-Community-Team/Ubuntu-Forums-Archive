### Post by realzippy on 2011-02-14
Why not just using nvidia-current?
Actually it is 260.19.06.
If you do not have a brand new card which explicitly needs the newest driver,there is no advantage at all.

When manually installing a nvidia driver,you have to blacklist
the nouveau kernel modules.See the nvidia readme file...
Also you have to reinstall the driver every kernel update.
If you want newer drivers easily without manually installing,
add [xorgedgers]("https://launchpad.net/~xorg-edgers/+archive/ppa") or [xswat]("https://launchpad.net/~ubuntu-x-swat/+archive/x-updates") ppa to your system....
