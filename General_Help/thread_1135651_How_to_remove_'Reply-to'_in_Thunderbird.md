### Post by Man_Beach on 2009-04-24
I've just upgraded to Thunderbird 2. When I compose a message, the first thing that comes up in the address bar is a 'Reply-to:'.
I don't want it.
I want the first thing on the address bar to be 'To:' and nothing else, but I can't find out how to remove the reply-to option.

Can anyone help?

---

### Post by Man_Beach on 2009-04-25
Solved it. In the account settings, I'd filled in the 'Reply-to-address' box. Clearing the contents removed the 'Reply-to:' at the top of the addresses. Now the first option is just 'To:' like I wanted.
