Hey everyone. I have a quick question about the possibility of a Linux equivalent to the autotagger feature in Winamp, which I was so dependent upon when I used Windows.

For those not familiar with the concept, the Autotagger program allowed Winamp to scan the metadata of many different (supposedly incorrectly tagged) audio files and contacted some kind of database to retrieve the correct information, editing the tracks to reflect the proper changes. For one who is anal-retentive about keeping their music correctly labeled, it was a pretty nifty feature. Much easier than editing all the tracks oneself.

My basic question is whether there is a similar program usable on Linux platforms. I'm currently using Amarok and XMMS and am curious as to whether there is such a feature native to either one or from a third-party program. 

Any insight anyone could offer would be much appreciated. Thanks and peace!
