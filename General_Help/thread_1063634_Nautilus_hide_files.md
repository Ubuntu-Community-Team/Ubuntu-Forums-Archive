### Post by mb_webguy on 2009-02-08
Nautilus hides files based on filename, rather than a flag as in Windows.  Files and folders preceded by a dot or ending in a tilda are hidden.  The only way to hide your files are either to change their names or put them in a folder with a name starting with a dot.
