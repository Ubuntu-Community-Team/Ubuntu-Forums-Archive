### Post by 73ckn797 on 2010-08-21
If the application is listed under Applications or even System, you can right click over the selection and will have choices to add to desktop or to panel.

You can also right click over the panel and create a shortcut, "add to panel". This method will require that you can designate the start-up file or application. Locating an icon will require further searching of program folders.
