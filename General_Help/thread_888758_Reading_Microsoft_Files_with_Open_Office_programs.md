### Post by Calmor on 2008-08-13
OpenOffice is capable of opening pre-Office 2007 files.  .DOC, .XLS, and .PPT should open just fine in OO, though there may be some minor formatting variances (just like going between versions of Office).  

I haven't heard of any support for the OOXML files that Office 2007 uses as default, though it's supposedly an "open" standard. :roll:
