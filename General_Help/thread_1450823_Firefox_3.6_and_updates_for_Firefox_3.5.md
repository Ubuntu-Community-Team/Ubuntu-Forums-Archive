### Post by abra1 on 2010-04-09
Using Ubuntu 9.10
I have updated to Firefox 3.6.3 and it is working fine for me. 
But I keep getting reminders from Ubuntu : " Other updates (LP-PPA-ubuntu-mozilla-daily)"  - - this time  6 updates for Firefox 3.5 . 

I don't understand why these are coming and don't know what to do with them.

---

### Post by lidex on 2010-04-09
You probably have more than one version installed. Open synaptic and search "firefox". Mark for complete removal the 3.5 packages. Apply. 
If you don't want the dailies:
Go to "Settings>Repositories" menu, click the "Other Software" tab and uncheck the entry for ubuntu-mozilla-daily. Close out.
