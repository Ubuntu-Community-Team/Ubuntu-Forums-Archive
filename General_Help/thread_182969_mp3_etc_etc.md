### Post by RudolfMDLT on 2006-05-27
In the terminal: 
> sudo apt-get install w32codecs

You may also need to install Xine instead of Gstreamer. There's a handy little application that handles most codec and player needs:
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?p=1057180#post1057180](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?p=1057180#post1057180)
