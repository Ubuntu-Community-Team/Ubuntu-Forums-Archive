### Post by WorMzy on 2010-08-19
I think the best thing to do would be to stop breaking your Linux installations.

Failing that, you can just have one main OS that just has the very basics and some form of virtualisation software installed (such as VirtualBox). You can then use that software to install guest Operating Systems, which are somewhat more disposable than "real" Operating Systems, since they're installed to easily backed-up files, rather than partitions.
