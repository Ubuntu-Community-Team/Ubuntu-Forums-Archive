### Post by PhantomTurtle on 2012-05-27
Open System Settings, under personal click on Brightness and Lock. Change the settings in their to what you like. If you want a screensaver read this article on how to do it - [http://www.howtogeek.com/114027/how-to-add-screensavers-to-ubuntu-12.04/]("http://www.howtogeek.com/114027/how-to-add-screensavers-to-ubuntu-12.04/").
