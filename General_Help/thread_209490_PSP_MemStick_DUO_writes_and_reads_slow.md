### Post by s_h_a_d_o_w_s on 2006-08-22
Really sorry we forum users haven't replied for this long. To disable the rythmbox/PSP automount feature, go to 

System -> Preferences -> Removable Drives and media

and uncheck the third chech button. As for the Memory Stick speeds, Are you using sandisk? I have found that with sandisk, it's good to format your Stick ( backup first!) before you use it with linux. It is also important to right-click the "psp" icon that appears on yuor desktop and click "Eject" before disconnecting. This will ensure all written data is well-writteen, as sometimes idf you go too fast you can corrupt your Memorystick. 

I hope that helped.
