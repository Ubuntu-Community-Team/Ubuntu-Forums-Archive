### Post by CharlesJWelsh on 2010-06-21
Im not sure what you are asking...Do you mean it has the default application shortcut icon? (the springboard) Or do you mean you want the Desktop Icon?

If its showing up as a Springboard:

   Right click the icon. Select Properties. In the left of the preferences window you will see the SpringBoard Icon. Click it and browse your icons and find your Firefox Icon and select it :]

If you want it to appear on your desktop:

   Click Applications. Internet. And drag the Firefox Icon to your desktop. Voila :]

### Post by Leppie on 2010-06-21
right click on the launcher, choose "Edit Launcher" then click the "No icon" button, from the pull down menu select "Image Files", in the main panel scroll down the list of files until you have firefox-*.*.png, select the file and click the "Ok" button, then click the "Save" button and voila ;)
