### Post by almufadado on 2010-02-04
The best until date is IPBLOCK ([http://www.ubuntugeek.com/ipblock-graphical-ip-blocker.html](http://www.ubuntugeek.com/ipblock-graphical-ip-blocker.html))

It uses the lists from bluetack BISS ([www.bluetack.co.uk](www.bluetack.co.uk)).

It has also a "whiltelist" and "unblock permanently" if you find that a site you want to access is being blocked.
