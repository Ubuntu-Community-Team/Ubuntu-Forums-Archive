### Post by Thelasko on 2010-01-26
I can get mp4 and flv files to play in OO Impress.  Perhaps you should try one of those file types.

Edit:  Sorry, swf are animations not video, I don't think it will work.

Unless you can get OO to recognize your flash plugin, it doesn't look good.  You may want to look into swfdec or gnash for this application.

Your best bet is to play the animation in your browser and capture it with some screeen capture tool and play it back as regular video.
