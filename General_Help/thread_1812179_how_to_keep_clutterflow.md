### Post by DawieS on 2011-07-26
Do you want the application to auto-start at every boot-up?

If so, you can add it to your System > Preferences > Startup Applications. The easiest way (for me) is to create a shortcut icon on the panel bar (drag from the menu and drop on the panel bar). Then right-click on the icon, and select Properties. Then copy/paste all details given straight into the Startup Applications window.:smile:
