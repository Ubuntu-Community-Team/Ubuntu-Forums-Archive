### Post by a2020vision on 2010-08-13
Right-click the panel, and choose "add to panel." You're given a few choices - one of them is "Menu Bar" which contains the Applications, Places, System menus. You can also add individual applications and the other applets you're missing that way; just scroll through the list, and pick what you want.

Add applications with one of the "Launcher" options.
