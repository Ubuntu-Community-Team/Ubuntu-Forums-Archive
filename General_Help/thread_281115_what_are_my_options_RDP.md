### Post by cogsprocket on 2006-10-20
Your options are simple.   Run a VNC server on the system you want to remotely access and use a VNC client to remotely control it.

Be careful about what client your using, however.  Not all VNC clients are secure.  Plan this out carefully because you'll probably want some form of secure VPN solution before you start using VNC to controll your systems.
