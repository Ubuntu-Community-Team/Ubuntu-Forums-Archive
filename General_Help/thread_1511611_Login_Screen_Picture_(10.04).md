### Post by sbergman on 2010-06-17
Click on System -> Preferences -> About me
And then on the picture of the blank face to change the picture.

You can go even further if you want: Click on System -> Administration -> Login Window (requires the admin password)
and then click on the "Local" tab and select a different GDM theme.

FYI - I'm using the Woodclean v0.3 theme (cant remember where I got it) but you can find a few more here
[http://gnome-look.org](http://gnome-look.org)
