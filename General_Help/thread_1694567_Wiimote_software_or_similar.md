### Post by marshmallow1304 on 2011-02-24
There's [CWiid]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/CWiiD"), which allows you to use a Wiimote as an input device over Bluetooth.  You can bind the buttons, accelerometer, and IR data to keyboard and mouse events, but it's no where near sophisticated enough as-is to use as a Smartboard replacement.

There's also [GTK Wiimote Whiteboard]("http://www.stepd.ca/gtkwhiteboard/"), but it's from 2008 and it may or may not work.
