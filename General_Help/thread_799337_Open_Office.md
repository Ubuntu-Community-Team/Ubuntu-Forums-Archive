### Post by mikeyd612 on 2008-05-18
I seem to be having issues with Calc. I am attempting to use it for a project at work which requires about 40 graphs. I split the numbers up so that there are about 10 graphs per file. So the question is this. . . Is there a way to have calc automatically resize and position graphs for printing or do I have to resize, move and preview for each graph until I get it right? Also if you could suggest another program that might do the same job with less fuss let me know. Thanks guys.

### Post by empthollow on 2008-05-19
i recently went through a similar situation.  the answers are in format -> page -> sheet tab
look at the bottom under scaling and choose fit print ranges to fit page.

Next, select the area of the page that the graph exists on and while it is selected go to Format -> Print ranges -> Define
this will set the selection you made as the print range.  hit print preview to make sure it worked like you wanted and print.
