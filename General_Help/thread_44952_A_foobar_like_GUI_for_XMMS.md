### Post by Teo on 2005-06-28
[QUOTE=Trojan1313]I've installed it, but how do I use it? :p[/QUOTE]
 1. Close Rhythmbox
2. Right click on a panel
3. "Add to a Panel"
4. from list: "Rythmbox Applet"
5. "Add"
6. run Rhythmbox
7. use Rhythmbox Applet

Good luck ;)
