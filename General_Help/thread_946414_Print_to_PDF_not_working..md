### Post by kaibob on 2008-10-13
> **Indigo7 said:**
> I suddenly can't print to .PDF. The driver goes through the motions then disappears. Any suggestions with troubleshooting? Thanks!

You really don't provide much information, but some items you may want to check:

* Does the Printer Configuration dialog show the PDF printer?

* If the answer to the above question is yes, what happens when you click on the "Print Test Page" button under the "Settings" tab?

* What application are you printing from (e.g. Firefox)? Does the PDF printer work with other applications?

* Have you accidentally changed or deleted the default pdf directory in your home directory? This is probably the most common cause of the behavior you describe. 

* Have you edited or overwritten as part of an update any of the PDF printer configuration files?
