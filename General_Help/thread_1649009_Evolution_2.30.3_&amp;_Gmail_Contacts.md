### Post by apochry on 2011-03-28
Hey guys,

the issue with the Google Contacts could be fixed by changing the Proxy Setting in Evolution's 'Network Preferences'-tab from "Use system defaults" to "Direct Connection to the Internet". Do not forget to restart Evo afterwards.

If you still have Evolution on your machines give it a try and leave feedback please.


Izzo
