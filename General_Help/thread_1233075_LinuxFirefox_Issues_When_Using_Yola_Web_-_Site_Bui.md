### Post by ajgreeny on 2009-08-06
If you're now on FF 3.0.13, you were probably on 3.0.12 before the update, and for some reason 3.0.12 did not have cairo support in the build.  All previous versions of FF and the new 3.0.13 do have this, and it could be that the lack of cairo in the build has had this effect.  I know next to nothing about cairo, except that it is something to do with image rendering I think, hence my suggestion that this could be the basis for your now, thankfully, solved problem
