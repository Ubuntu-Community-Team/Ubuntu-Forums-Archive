### Post by jespdj on 2008-09-11
There are several possible reasons why Ubuntu can seem to be running / is running slower than Windows.

One reason might be that you're not using the right driver for your graphics card, so many things on the screen go a lot slower than they should be. What graphics card do you have and what driver are you using?

Another reason is that CPU frequency scaling doesn't work correctly on your computer, and that the CPU is stuck at a lower frequency (and never switches to its max speed when you run a CPU-intensive program).
