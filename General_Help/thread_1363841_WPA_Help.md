### Post by 3rdalbum on 2009-12-25
You're going about it the wrong way.

You enable WPA2 on your router/access-point. Then, any computers that connect to your router will notice the WPA2 encryption and use it automatically (after asking you the passphrase).

This "tutorial" you found must be from years and years ago when the Linux wireless stack was very young and you needed to do serious tweaking in order to use wireless. DO NOT follow old tutorials. If it's a year old or more, don't follow it. Linux moves too quickly.
