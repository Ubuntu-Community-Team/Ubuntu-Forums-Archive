### Post by mikewhatever on 2009-10-07
You can try to repartition while running from the Ubuntu installation cd. The program to use is under System->Admin->Partition Manager. The default installation requires about 4GB of space minimum and takes up about 2.3GB after the installation. It might also be a good idea to reinstall, and write down your username and password.
