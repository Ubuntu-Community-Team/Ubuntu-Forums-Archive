### Post by finlost on 2010-09-10
I have it set to Ubuntu and not default and have Empathy sounds.  I don't have sound-theme-freedesktop installed and have Empathy sounds.

Go to the OS sound preferences menu and increase the "alert volume".  I also unchecked the "enable window and button sounds".  With that checked, every time you click something in Ubuntu, you get a sound.   I didn't want that.
