### Post by SeijiSensei on 2013-02-13
sagireddyrams,

Perhaps you would like to give us an idea of what sorts of activities you would like to script.  Having lots and lots of links to reference documents is useful though I'd imagine horribly overwhelming as a beginner starting out.  Why don't we begin a discussion of something practical you would like to do?  

Necessity is the mother of invention, as they say.  That maxim probably accounts for most of the scripts I've written.  I needed to create backups, so I wrote some backup scripts.  I needed a way to manage my mail server's queues, so I wrote a script for that.  I needed a way to create an effective iptables firewall and wrote a script for that.  I have never just struck out to learn a language without a rationale for doing so.  Maybe that just makes me a pragmatist.
