### Post by kraylus on 2007-08-17
the places you described sound nothing like a bios menu. you're not pressing the right button at boot time apparently. sometimes it's the delete key and sometimes it's F10. or F12. gotta read what comes up. usually says "press whatever to enter setup or settings".

vista itself has no control over what your motherboard says to boot.

from what i hear, i suggest you call acer tech support and find out how to access the bios.
