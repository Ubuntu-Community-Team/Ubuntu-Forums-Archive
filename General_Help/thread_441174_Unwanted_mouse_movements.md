### Post by nikki on 2007-05-16
I have used this mouse for quite some time (I'd say easily over a year) on both windows and Dapper/Edgy/Feisty. I can say that I have had this issue in all versions of Ubuntu. I never really posted about it, because I just never got around to searching the forums for such an error. When the issue occurs, the mouse goes side to side of the screen crazily, possibly opening/closing applications, clicking links, and generally being unpleasant for a period of time.

The temporary fix I seemed to find is that the issue seemed to resolve itself if I alt tabbed out and back into whatever application I was running.

However, I have also noticed this issue within wine, which has lead me to believe what the cause of it may be. When I have wine running a game, and another application like firefox open, my cursor sometimes bounces between the wine application and the secondary application as if they are both fighting for control of my cursor without any minimizing occuring in the process.

With the MX518, I also have noticed poor performance like some times a click  not accurately registering in applications. Sometimes my cursor will also get "stuck". My cursor will be over an area, but Ubuntu will give a tooltip over another previous object as if my mouse is over this object.

My MX518 has been giving me a lot of headaches for awhile.
