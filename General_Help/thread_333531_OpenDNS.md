### Post by pjbgravely on 2007-01-07
Is you problem in Firefox?, if so here is the fix:

Open a new tab in firefox,

Type about:config in the URL bar,

Type v6 in search,

You should see "network.dns.disableIPv6" in the results.

Double click that line if the value isn't true.

Once it says true, you DNS resolving should be much faster.

Close the tab and your done.

Paul
