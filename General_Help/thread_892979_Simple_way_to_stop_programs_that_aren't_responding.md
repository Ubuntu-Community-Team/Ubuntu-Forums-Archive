### Post by petedct on 2008-08-17
How about putting the system monitor on a Gnome panel? Then you could right click on it to open it and select the "Processes" tab to kill processes to your hearts content.

### Post by drs305 on 2008-08-17
If you don't mind a gui/mouse solution, put 'Force Quit' on your panel. Rt Click on the panel, Add to Panel, select Force Quit. When you click on the panel icon, it makes the cursor a + and the next window you click on is killed.

### Post by editrix on 2008-08-19
