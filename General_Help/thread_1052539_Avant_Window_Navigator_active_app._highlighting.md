### Post by detroit/zero on 2009-01-28
There's a couple things to look at. I'm not sure exactly, but any one or even all of these could be what you're looking for:

Right click the bar and select *Dock Preferences* (or just launch AWN Manager)

On the General tab of the *General Preferences* dialog, under *Bar Behavior*: uncheck *Alert when app window is updated*

Then, right below that, under *Icon Effects*, find the setting for *Attention* and set it to *None*.

Then go to the *Glass Engine* tab and make sure that everything there has zero opacity.

Any one of those or all of them should do it. Hope it works for you!
