### Post by ChazZeromus on 2010-05-18
Not at all, if there wasn't then there would be some kind of abrupt warning upon mounting an ntfs hdd describing any possibilities of corruption, or at least some other way. At least operating systems other than windows can read the NT filesystems without the hassle of security descriptors and permissions.
