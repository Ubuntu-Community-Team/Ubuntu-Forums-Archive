### Post by Rubi1200 on 2010-09-28
Yes and no.

Windows will overwrite the MBR, but you can then reinstall GRUB afterwards and boot both operating systems, choosing from each at startup.

This guide will show you how to do this:
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Grub2#Reinstalling%20from%20LiveCD](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Grub2#Reinstalling%20from%20LiveCD)

If you have any other questions, feel free to ask.
