Update: I finally DID open each photo, copy its caption to the insert-text window, and save under a new name. It wasn't that bad, and Digikam let me move the text box around on the photo, change font sizes, etc. before I saved. I just published my HTML Export to my web page; no slide show yet, but the album (each photo with a text box) looks great. Obviously I'm still WAY low on the Digikam learning curve but it looks good.
