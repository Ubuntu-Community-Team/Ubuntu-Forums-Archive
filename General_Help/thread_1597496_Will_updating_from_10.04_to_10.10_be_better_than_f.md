### Post by Frogs Hair on 2010-10-15
The jury is still out on this question , many people report no problems with upgrades and others swear by clean installations. As long as you read the release notes and purge your ppas , you should be ok upgrading.

You may have to install the 10.10 wallpaper and ambiance them from the repository because it has been reported that don't install automatically when upgrading. Again , beware of ppas , many that worked in 10.04 don't work 10.10 .

### Post by TBABill on 2010-10-15
If it were me and I had about 10GB to play around with I'd probably create another partition altogether and keep the production side working without risking it. Until you are certain it will work fine why risk the work of another reconfig of the machine to get all your settings back? Or create a copy of your install to just throw back on the machine exactly as it is today. That way if it borks you can go back to exactly the configuration you are using without install, update, drivers, tweak, config, download, etc.
