### Post by PhantomTurtle on 2012-03-28
Go to System Setting>User Accounts. In user accounts there is an option to change your language. Well it will show you your current language, but when you click on it you can select a different language. Also I believe that folder names will not be affected(I'm not entirely sure on this), but you might have to rename them yourself(maybe).
