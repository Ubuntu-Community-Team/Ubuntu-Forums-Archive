### Post by CatKiller on 2020-04-15
1920×1080 at 27" is pretty low-res: ~81 dpi. It's not going to look great.

You can make all the elements - text, icons, toolbars, and so on - smaller. There are changes in progress for adding more granularity to the scaling - Gnome is way behind the other desktop environments on that front - but they're mostly focused on scaling greater than 100% for high-dpi displays.
