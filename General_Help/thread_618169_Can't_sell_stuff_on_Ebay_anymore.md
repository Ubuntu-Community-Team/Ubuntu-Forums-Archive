### Post by smartbei on 2007-11-20
I suggest you try Epiphany (based on the gecko engine - so there shouldn't be a difference but you never know... :)) and if that fails Opera.

Both are available from the repositories, though you may have to enable different ones for Opera.

Lastly, it is also possible to use ie4linux to install ie6 on your computer and use that. This is obviously a last resort though :p.
