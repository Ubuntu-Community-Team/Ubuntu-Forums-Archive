### Post by Jimcando on 2009-04-22
I have just switched to 9.04 and am using two monitors. I like to have a panel on the second monitor aswell as the two on the first, and in the past I could create a new panel and then click and drag it over to the second.

However, I can't seem to find an option to move the panels in this new version. I can only add a panel and choose whether it goes on the left, right, top or bottom of Monitor number 1. Anyway around this?

**EDIT:**Found it out. For those interested, I Right-clicked on a new panel -> Properties -> Unchecked Expand. This allowed me to move it over. Then I Expanded it again. I must say, more awkward than before :/

### Post by maheshasolkar on 2009-04-22
If you right-click on the panel, there is an item that says 'Allow Panel to be Moved'. Can you try clicking on that and then moving the panel?
