### Post by mcduck on 2009-02-02
As far as I know .eml files are Microsoft's proprietary system for saving e-mail, used by Outlook and don't work reliably in other e-mail clients. Not even in Outlook Express.. :D

The best solution would be to ask anybody who sends such e-mails to send/forward their e-mails as plain text, as using .eml has absolutely no benefits over the standard way of doing the same thing.
