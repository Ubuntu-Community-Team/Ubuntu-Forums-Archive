### Post by soro2005 on 2009-05-09
To produce pdf from your document, use any program and "print your document to file"
To edit pdf files, try PDFedit or Scribus.
For commandline manipulation of pdf files (such as splitting, rotating, etc), try pdftk
Bottom line: There are many ways of working with pdf files. The question is what exactly you want to do.
