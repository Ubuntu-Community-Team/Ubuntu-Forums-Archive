### Post by jonny on 2005-04-08
The trouble with removing Open Office is that the Ubuntu Desktop package depends on it, and synaptic will remove that too.  Once Ubuntu Desktop goes, you've lost your mechanism for getting automatic updates to the default desktop environment and suite of applications.

That matters much less now that Hoary has been finally released, but if you want to start tracking Bandy you'll probably want to put OO and UD back.
