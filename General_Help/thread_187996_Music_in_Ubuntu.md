### Post by thasheep on 2006-06-03
For doing lots of tags (no don't use it as a player), to get data from the filename, directory structure or from online databases, I like to use easytag. Quod Libet (player/tagger) and Ex Falso (tagger) could also be worth checking out and have similar functionality.
As for a player, you really can't go past amarok - even on gnome. It isn't a lean application but it is really good. In my opinion it has by far the most intuative interface of any player ever. With it's xine engine (default) it has crossfading (gstreamer, used by most gnome apps like rhythmbox, and also another amarok engine, doesn't). It can do tags but is no where near as powerful as easytag.
