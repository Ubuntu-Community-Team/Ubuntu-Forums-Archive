### Post by verb3k on 2010-01-27
While many screencasting tools exist on Linux, none of them is able to really pull a high-quality screencast. I’ve tried almost all existing tools such as recordmydesktop, xvidcap, istanbul, wink etc..  and all of them produced poor results. Horrible video/audio quality,  bad sync, lots of limitations, and some even simply segfault while doing the recording. The solution and the ultimate answer is FFmpeg. I am surprised to see that most people would just overlook this awesome piece of software.
