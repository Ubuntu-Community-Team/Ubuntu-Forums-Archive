### Post by TeoBigusGeekus on 2011-02-04
It depends on what mail client you use.
In thunderbird for example, you can go to Address Book>Tools>Export and export all your address book.

Something similar applies to your browser bookmarks. I use Opera and when I go to Bookmarks>Manage Bookmarks>File>Export Opera Bookmarks, I can save them and reuse them wherever I want, be it windows or linux.

I am sure there are similar tools in your preferred programs.
