### Post by Mark Phelps on 2010-02-28
From what I could see in the Wiki, the R40 uses onboard Radeon grephics, and is limited to 26MB or 32MB -- indicating that's one of the really old 2000-era chipsets -- for which there are no ATI drivers in Ubuntu 9.10.

So, since you're using the open source drivers by default, and there are no other drivers you can use, there may not be much you can do about graphics problems.
