### Post by ron999 on 2010-03-17
Hi
There are two types of playlists.
One is 'absolute'.
Other is 'relative'.
Absolute playlists may be moved anywhere.
Relative playlists must stay in their correct folder.
So, I guess that Totem creates relative playlists and SMPlayer and VLC create absolute playlists.
Do some further research at Wikipedia etc if you're interested.
