### Post by jespdj on 2009-05-16
Note that how well your graphics work highly depends on what graphics chipset you have in your computer. Making a poll like this does not make much sense, since different people have different graphics cards. Jaunty works fine with my nVidia computers.

There is a [known issue](http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/releasenotes/904#Performance%20regressions%20on%20Intel%20graphics%20cards) with Intel graphics.
