### Post by Spr0k3t on 2007-02-19
Anything sans and nothing similar to comic.  Readable is key, so nothing cursive, freakish, or just plain whack.  If the font has a spacing issue between the I, J, L or other similar small characters, I won't use the font.  If the zero looks like a capital O but less rounded (ie: no line through it), I won't use the font.

Monofont: Modified Andale Mono
System: Dejavu Sans Condensed (working on modifying)

Now print medium is a different story.  If the print fits the font or vice versa, it can be any type of font you want.
