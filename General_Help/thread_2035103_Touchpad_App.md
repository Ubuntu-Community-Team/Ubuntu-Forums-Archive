> I believe that is a KDE app. I may be available in kubuntu.

Went to the KDE site and cannot find it.

**Google to the rescue:** Found the app **Synaptics Touchpad Management**, and the **Software Center** has it if you search _touchpad_ ;)

It's added to your launch menu (I'm using the GNOME Desktop), the firs time you execute it launches the server, the second time it opens the configuration dialog.

Yes, you can **Disable Touchpad with USB Mouse Connected**!
