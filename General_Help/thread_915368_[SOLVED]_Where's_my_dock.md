### Post by purplepaint on 2008-09-11
I don't know about the problem with installing Cairo-Dock, but to have wither Cairo-Dock or AWN displaying you have to enable Compiz-Fusion (System - Preferences - Appearance, click on the "Visual Effects" tab and chose either "Normal" or "Extra" depending on how fancy you want your effects), and you could also make sure that AWN is set to start when you log in (System - Preferences - Sessions).
