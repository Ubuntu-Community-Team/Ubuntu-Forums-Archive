### Post by logos34 on 2009-05-07
why don't you just do this in the terminal:

wodim -vv dev=/dev/cdrom -eject blank=all

for full format

or use 'blank=fast' for quick erase (table of contents only ~30 secs)

If you're on windows, maybe ask over in the windows forum.  (I've only used Nero, not familiar with Roxio)
