### Post by 3rdalbum on 2011-10-05
There is actually a school of thought that it's counterproductive to re-theme the new operating system to look similar to the old operating system.

It makes the person more frustrated that their computer looks like it used to, but now doesn't act like it. Especially with Linux, as it works totally different to Windows and Mac OS.

Just a thought you might want to consider; otherwise you can download the older version from the Mozilla website I'm sure. It won't be a ".deb" - it will be a .tar.gz that is already compiled for you. Just double-click on "thunderbird-bin".
