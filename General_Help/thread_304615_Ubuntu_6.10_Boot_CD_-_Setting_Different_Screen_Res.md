### Post by mcduck on 2006-11-22
What graphics card does your laptop have? If it's Ati or nVidia adapter, after you have installed Ubuntu you can install drivers for your graphics card and the resolution should then change to right one.

Anyway, after you have installed Ubuntu you can configure it to use whatever resolution you need. Doing it with the live-cd is a bit more tricky and the settings will of course disappear when you boot.
