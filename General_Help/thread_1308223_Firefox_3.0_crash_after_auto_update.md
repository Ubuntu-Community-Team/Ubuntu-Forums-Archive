### Post by lovinglinux on 2009-10-31
For backups, optimizations and fixes. See [Firefox optimization and troubleshooting thread](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1193567). I recommend making individual backups of your profile files using FEBE extension then restoring them one by one into a new and clean profile.

A new profile will probably solve the problem, but if it doesn't then try to re-install firefox via Synaptic.

BTW, patience is a virtue. Posting a new message every hour won't help you get faster answers. It's considered impolite to bump your thread that fast. Wait a least 24 hours so people with the knowledge to fix your problem spot your post.
