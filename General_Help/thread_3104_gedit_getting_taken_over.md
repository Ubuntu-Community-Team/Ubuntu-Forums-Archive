### Post by TravisNewman on 2004-11-03
I installed screem html editor, and now gnome wants to open text files in screem. When I go to preferred apps and switch it back to gedit, close it, and then go BACK to preferred apps, it's back to screem. I couldn't find any gconf settings so I'm assuming that's the only way. Any ideas?

---

### Post by jeremy on 2004-11-03
Try to right click on a text file, Properties, Open With,- then select (or add) gedit.
