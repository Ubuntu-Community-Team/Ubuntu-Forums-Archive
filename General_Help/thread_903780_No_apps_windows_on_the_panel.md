### Post by bmac on 2008-08-28
Right click on the panel that you want do show the open programs.
Select "ADD TO PANEL"
Find "Window List" and select it then click on the "ADD" button..
Close "ADD TO PANEL"

You will note two vertical perforated lines appear in your panel. This can be moved and placed in any position on your panel.

Hope this helps.....
