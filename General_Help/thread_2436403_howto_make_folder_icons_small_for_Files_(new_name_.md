### Post by Claus7 on 2020-02-05
Hello,

if I understand correctly you open Files and the size of folders has different size than the one you would wish to, yet all the rest of your system is fine. Then while at files, press &#8801;, and in the second line of the menu that will pop up you will see - xx% +. Press - or + in order to change the size of the folders to your liking.
