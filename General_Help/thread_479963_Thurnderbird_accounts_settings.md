### Post by coolglobal on 2007-06-21
I just transfered 6 accounts in Thunderbird from XP to feisty.  I simply looked at the account settings wrote them down and then started new accounts in feisty Thunderbird, entering in what I wrote down. No worries, it was all done in half an hour. 

I have a dual partition so any signatures I had I moved to the desktop of XP, started feisty, mounted the xp partition and copied them over.  If this is a bit difficult you could simply copy this kind of info onto a flash drive.
