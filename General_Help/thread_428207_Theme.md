### Post by NullHead on 2007-04-30
To make a theme use the theme app in System>Preferences>Theme. To make a new theme just pick a theme you want to base your theme off of and then click on the *customize* icon and do the chandes you want to your theme...when your done with changing your options to the way you like it you can click on  the close button and it will make a new theme called *custom theme*

There you have it your very own theme!
