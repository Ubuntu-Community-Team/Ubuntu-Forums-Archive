### Post by Dokuganryu07 on 2011-01-17
If your speakers are connected to the computer via USB, you may have to tweak some settings in ALSA.   I have had very little luck with USB speakers.

If your speakers utilize a Green 3.5 mm audio input jack, I may be able to help you.

More information is required.


Generally speaking, 3.5 mm speakers are more likely to be compatible with all OS, because they do not require drivers to install and work properly.  USB speakers are generally HEAVILY OS dependent.   I would suggest a set of speakers that utilize the 3.5 mm instead of USB for your future purchases, I have been a linux user for over 8 years and have never run into a single problem with sound until I tried using USB speakers.  

A buying hint, if you USB speakers say they are MAC compatible, IN GENERAL (important to note, not ALL) they are more likely to work with Linux/Ubuntu than ones that say a single specific Windows variant.


Hope that helps, update us with more information about your speaker system if you would like more specific help.
