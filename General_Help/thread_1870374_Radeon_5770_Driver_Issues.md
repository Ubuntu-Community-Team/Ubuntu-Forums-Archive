### Post by oscarivera9 on 2011-10-27
I have the same exact graphics card in my system.  Check out these pages, they have lots of info on the Radeon Drivers and will hopefully answer any questions you may have:

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RadeonDriver](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RadeonDriver)

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BinaryDriverHowto/ATI](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BinaryDriverHowto/ATI)

Fire up a terminal and type in some of the suggestions to help you gather information as to what driver you are using.  For example, type this in a terminal and wait for the results:

fglrxinfo
