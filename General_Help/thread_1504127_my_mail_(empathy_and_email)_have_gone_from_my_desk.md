### Post by pytheas22 on 2010-06-07
It sounds like the indicator applet was deleted from the panel for some reason.  Luckily that shouldn't be a big deal.  You should be able to add it again by right-clicking on an empty area of your panel, selecting Add to Panel and then choosing the Indicator Applet.  If that doesn't work, let us know.

Also keep in mind that you can always launch Empathy and Evolution (the email client) from the Applications>Internet menu.
