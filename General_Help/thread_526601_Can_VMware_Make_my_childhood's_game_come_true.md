### Post by EndPerform on 2007-08-15
You might look into something called Wine for your Windows 98 games, it should run most of those without too much trouble.  Additionally, there's DOSbox for those DOS based games.  I believe there are some good HOWTOs around for both of these somewhere.  VMWare does not do 3D support, so that's not really an option.
