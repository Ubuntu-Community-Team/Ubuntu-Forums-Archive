### Post by Ramses de Norre on 2007-01-23
This has nothing to do with the software, alt-ctrl-del just shuts down the hardware.
My machine also has the problem that if I turn it off I have to wait ten minutes before I can turn it back on. And if I were you I'd try to unplug the power cable and wait half an hour, then try to turn it on again. If that doesn't work: take it to a hardware shop and let them check your power supply and motherboard.
