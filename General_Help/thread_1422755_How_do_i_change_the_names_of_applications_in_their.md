### Post by lyall on 2010-03-05
in Opera click on the Bookmarks tab
then click on Manage Bookmarks
select the one that you want to rename and right click
then click on Properties... (or Ctrl - I)
in the Bookmark Properties window you can change the name of the bookmark

good luck and have fun learning
