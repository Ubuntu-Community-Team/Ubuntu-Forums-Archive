### Post by him610 on 2018-11-30
> I read that there were random freezes thta weren't solved.
Random freezes? Really? 
LTS 16.04 has been out for over 2-1/2 years now; LTS 18.04 has been out for about 7 months now. I have both (Xubuntu) installed on several different computers including one Asrock H270M-ITX/ac and one Asus AM1-A (both mini-ITX.) Can't say that I have ever experienced a random freeze on any of my machines; however, that does not mean it can not happen. Under adverse system loads, any system will freeze. One needs to be aware of the limitations of one's system for the CPU, GPU, RAM, and disk drive. 

If you have not experienced any system freezes using LTS 14.4 then it is pretty safe to upgrade to LTS 16.04 or 18.04. Be sure to backup all of your important files before you upgrade.
