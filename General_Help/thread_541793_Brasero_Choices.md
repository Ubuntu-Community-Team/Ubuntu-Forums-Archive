### Post by Cybergod on 2007-10-25
When you choose to burn an iso a dialog will open where you select the iso to burn.  At the top of this dialog you'll see the drive used to burn, to the right you'll see a "Properties" button.  Click "Properties" and then you'll have a drop down box where you can select different burning  speeds.
