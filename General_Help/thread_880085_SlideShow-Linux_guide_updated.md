### Post by frodemt on 2008-08-04
Hi all.

I just wanted to post that I have updatet my guide for SlideShow-Linux to meet the new Ubuntu 8.04.

Short about SlideShow-Linux:
SlideShow-Linux is a mini-guide that shows you how to make a automatic slideshow system. There are a lot of situations that this may be suitable. Examples are advertisement in shops, information systems in enterprises and public service. This guide is complete. You can require all needed to complete this system from this webpage.

The old thread is closed but can be reached at [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=640541&highlight=sendslide](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=640541&highlight=sendslide)

There is also a new version of the Windows client SendSlide. Changes include some security fixes, bug fixes and a new feature. You can now save your custom commands in SendSlide and execute them on will. A new version of SendSlide is due late August 2008.

You can find more information at [http://tviberg.gotdns.com/nettside/slideshow_linux_en.php](http://tviberg.gotdns.com/nettside/slideshow_linux_en.php) for the English version and

[http://tviberg.gotdns.com/nettside/slideshow_linux.php](http://tviberg.gotdns.com/nettside/slideshow_linux.php) for the Norwegian version.

Any question can be submittet to me at this thread, PM or my forum at [http://tviberg.gotdns.com/forum/viewforum.php?f=12&sid=bc85986a6c2271ffb9943ecdf9a33a4c](http://tviberg.gotdns.com/forum/viewforum.php?f=12&sid=bc85986a6c2271ffb9943ecdf9a33a4c)
