### Post by Typhoon on 2005-07-23
Sign up to Free World Dialup and download the Sjphone from their site. I have had good luck with it.

FWD: [http://www.freeworlddialup.com](http://www.freeworlddialup.com)

The SJphone is not free (as in speech) software, but it does work well.

A second and interesting option will be available in August. The Gizmo Project is releasing a new phone for Linux. I have used it on Mac OS X and am very impressed. It is similar to Skype but uses SIP protocols and has lots of nice features.

Gizmo: [http://www.gizmoproject.com/](http://www.gizmoproject.com/)

Both Gizmo and FWD offer free calls to a lot of phones around the world.

Asterisk runs nicely in Ubuntu if you can learn to get the damned thing configured. This allows you to sign up to any number of providers and use a single phone. It is neat.

Cheers,
Typhoon
