### Post by majutsu on 2006-09-16
fruity loops is never going to work in linux

period.  

gol based fl on directsound.  you must have directx, and linux never will, because it can never be free (let alone open source).  

music apps in linux are the big zero frankly.  best reason to dual boot there is.  wine and cedega are for the gamers.  the needs of electronic musicians are not high on anyone's list.  

your only choice in linux is to use inferior apps like rosegarden or lmms, or to use amazing programming languages like pd, chuck or supercollider.  

most big names in electronic and idm music (autechre, squarepusher, aphex, etc) use programming language apps or hardware anyway.  so if you are serious take the linux plunge and run pd or supercollider.
