### Post by qamelian on 2012-10-26
> **Shibblet said:**
> Enter Ubuntu 12.10
Same problem.

What the heck?  I can't even change my window theme anymore.  Why are things getting less and less customizable?

And if I just don't know how to do this, what am I doing wrong?  I've loaded gnome-tweak-tool to change the themes, but they never seem to work correctly.
GTK is currently a bit of a moving target. You need to make certain that the theme you want is actually compatible with the specific version of GTK on your system. For example, some themes that worked perfectly on GTK 3.2 look terrible in GTK 3.4 due to changes made between versions. Unless the themes get updated to reflect the changes to GTK, you may be down to learning to tweak/update them yourself. I know several themes I quite liked have been horrible on 3.4 and 3.6, and there is no sign that updates are coming.
