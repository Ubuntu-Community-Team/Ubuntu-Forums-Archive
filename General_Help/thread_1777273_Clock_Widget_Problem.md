### Post by Tamlynmac on 2011-06-07
Just a thought.

What location are you using for your default. Your location will determine what time it is in your region of the world.

Left click on your clock and check location to assure your actually setup for the region your located in. You may have selected a region that is 1 hour behind your location.

Good Luck & hope this helps.
