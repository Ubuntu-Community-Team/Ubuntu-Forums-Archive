### Post by prshah on 2013-04-14
> **MZ250Supa5 said:**
>  every time I try a Copy & Paste, Nautilus crashes when I try to paste a copied item.  

Have you recently activated any additional "language packs"? There is a known problem with a number of language packs, please see [https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/language-pack-gnome-el/+bug/1019845](https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/language-pack-gnome-el/+bug/1019845)

If you have one of the affected language packs as mentioned in the link, update to the latest releases of the language packs. If your specific language pack is causing the problem, but not on the list, please add it to the list.
