### Post by kiddo on 2012-01-02
> The other thing is where to find an up-to-date PiTiVi manual? The most recent one dates from 2009!

On the pitivi website (or bundled with the application since recent releases): [http://pitivi.org/manual](http://pitivi.org/manual)

And yes, you really want to update your pitivi version to 0.15 at least. Not sure about it being available for a nearly two years old distro release though, check out the GStreamer PPA (see the pitivi website's download page) or newer ubuntu releases (or distros that bundle newer versions of applications).
