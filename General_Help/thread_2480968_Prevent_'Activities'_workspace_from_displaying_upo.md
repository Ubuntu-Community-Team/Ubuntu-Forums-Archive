### Post by xanizen on 2022-11-15
When I left click anywhere on the screen, and (usually unintentionally) move the cursor to the upper left hand of the screen, the 'Activities' button, the workspace screen displays.

I already went to settings &#8594; multitasking, disable Hot Corner and Active screen edges.

A similar button for Firefox Web Browser, appears next to 'Activities' and this left click release problem does not occur.

**Update**: 
I was able to use 'Just Perfections' gnome extension to remove it completely.
[https://extensions.gnome.org/extension/3843/just-perfection/](https://extensions.gnome.org/extension/3843/just-perfection/)
