### Post by Bucky Ball on 2009-04-18
If you download Gparted Live iso and make a cd, boot from that, you can resize whatever partition, including the one your OS is on because all drives are unmounted (you are running from the CD):

[http://gparted.sourceforge.net/download.php](http://gparted.sourceforge.net/download.php)

Get the &#313;ive´ iso image. :)
