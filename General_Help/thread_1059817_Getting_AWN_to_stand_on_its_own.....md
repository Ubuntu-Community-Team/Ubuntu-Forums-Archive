### Post by crimlaw on 2009-02-04
I am not sure what shortcuts you are trying to make, but when I added shortcut icons to my awn I did it from the "applications" menu.  I have firefox, openoffice writer, openoffice spreadsheets, movie player, Rythmbox music player, weather, and trash on my launcher.  For example, to get firefox there, I went to "applications" - "internet" - "Firefox", clicked on "Firefox" and dragged it to the launcher.  I don't have any icons my desktop.  What you might try is to find the programs you want to use as shortcuts under "applications" or in your "home" folder and drag them from there instead of trying to drag them from your desktop.

Let me know if that works.
