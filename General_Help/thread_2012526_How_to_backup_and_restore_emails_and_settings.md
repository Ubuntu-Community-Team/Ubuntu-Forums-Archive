### Post by WanderingAlbatross on 2012-06-29
Agree with the above.  Also, a lot of other e-mail clients will work the same way, not just Thunderbird.  Your private settings and data are stored in your home directory for most programs.  That's one of the beautiful things about Linux.

The only major challenge to this is when you are switching e-mail clients.  If you are not doing this you should be fine.
