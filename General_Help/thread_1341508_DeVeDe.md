### Post by fluffman86 on 2009-11-29
yep.  Open Ubuntu Software Center and search for DVD.  DVDStyler, DeVeDe (my favorite) and more will show up.

edit: missed the word "better."  Yes, there is *other* dvd menu creating software, but there is not better, IMO.  Unless you're just wanting to burn DVDs, then I really like Brasero.  Or if you're wanting to rip DVDs then you can check out DVD95, [http://handbrake.fr](http://handbrake.fr) or the old standby DVDShrink works well under wine, as does DVD Decrypter.
