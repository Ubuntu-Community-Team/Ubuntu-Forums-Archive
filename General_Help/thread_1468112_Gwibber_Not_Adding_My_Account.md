I think what you're asking is how to see your messages/timeline etc... so:

The Me Menu (your username) > Broadcast Accounts is to only set up/configure your social networking accounts. To use the configured accounts, to see and send messages, you click the envelope icon next to the Me Menu, and click the Broadcast item to show the "Social broadcast messages" Gwibber window. It's in this window that you should see your incoming messages, and be able to post your own messages.

That Gwibber window has problems (more so, the gwibber-service package, which actually does the connections to the various sites). You can find many questions if you search these forums for Gwibber... and if you search on Launchpad.net too, where there are many bugs filed :-(

But let's be optimistic and hope that it works well for you :p
