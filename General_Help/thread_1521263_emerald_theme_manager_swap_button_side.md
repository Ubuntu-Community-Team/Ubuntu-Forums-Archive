### Post by scottykal12 on 2010-07-21
go to the *edit theme* tab. Under that go to the *titlebar* tab. On the right there is a dropdown box that looks like a bunch of random letters, in there put in *CNX:IT:HM*. under the dropbox it says what each letter means. (left title bar)**:**(Middle title bar)**:**(Right title bar).

If you click on the dropdownbox there might already be an option for it. might be called OSX layout.

hopes this helps

-scottykal12
