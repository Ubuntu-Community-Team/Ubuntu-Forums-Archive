### Post by bovender on 2009-11-20
Hi jango13,

if you're really under pressure to get your thesis done, I would suggest to just use good old Windows just do it. Don't waste your time trying to get a specific software setup to work...

Are you trying to run it under Wine, or what is your setup?

You could try to use VirtualBox, I use it all the time when I need to work with Microsoft Office. It integrates quite nicely with my Ubuntu system by virtue of 'shared folders'.


As for the Endnote problem, I'm sorry I can't really contribute anything constructive. Since you wrote you need to fix it 'urgently', I assume you already have a fairly large bibliographic database and a Word document with your thesis.

If not, you may want to look at the free, open-source alternatives to Endnote. I have both Zotero and Mendeley installed and actually use Zotero to write my own manuscripts with OpenOffice Writer (Microsoft Word extensions are available as well).

Good luck!
