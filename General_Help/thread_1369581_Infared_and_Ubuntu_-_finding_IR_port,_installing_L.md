### Post by HarrisonNapper on 2010-01-08
Does your computer have an infrared receiver? Is LIRC itself not working or is it simply not recognizing your infrared remote? Also make sure that the IRLED on your remote is working by getting a digital camera (standalone or in a cell phone), holding the remote up in front of the lens, pointed toward it, and pressing some of the buttons. On the picture display (if the remote is working), you will see the IRLED light up when a button is pressed.

Cheers.
