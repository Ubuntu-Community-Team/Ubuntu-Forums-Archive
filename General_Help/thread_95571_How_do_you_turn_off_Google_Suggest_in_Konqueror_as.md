### Post by carney1979 on 2005-11-29
I just upgraded to KDE 3.5 (on Breezy) and Google-Suggest is gone; not even an option as far as I can tell.

Add this line to your /etc/apt/sources.list:

deb [http://kubuntu.org/packages/kde35](http://kubuntu.org/packages/kde35) breezy main

Konqueror now has AdBlock, similar to Firefox, though I haven't figured a way to block Macromedia-Flash ads.

Konqueror also now passes the Acid2 test with flying colors!

David
