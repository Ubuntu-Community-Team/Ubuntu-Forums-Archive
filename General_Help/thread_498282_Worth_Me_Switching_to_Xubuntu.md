### Post by kadath on 2007-07-13
> **shavenlunatic said:**
> yeah, fresh install runs loads better.. very happy with it.. is there a difinitive list of GTK apps kicking around that I can have a browse around?

Well, anything made for GNOME is a GTK app, but some are loaded with GNOME dependencies that are preferable to avoid. It really depends on what kind of apps you're looking for and what your options are.

A quick way to get an idea of what kinds of dependencies a certain program has is to just browse its entry at [the packages site]("http://packages.ubuntu.com/"). The dependency lists will usually list whether or not GNOME components are necessary for applications.

Xubuntu comes with quite a few good straight-GTK apps out of the box. If you want any recommendations on anything in particular, I might be able to help.
