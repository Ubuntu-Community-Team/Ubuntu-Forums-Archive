### Post by AndyCooll on 2008-08-07
We need more information regarding your Belkin wireless adaptor, e.g. version number. 

Different Belkin wireless adaptors have different chipsets and in turn require differing drivers. Some work out-of-the-box, others may require configuring, and others may require ndiswrapper to get them working.

Try doing a search of these forums (or a Google search) quoting the version number of your adaptor.
