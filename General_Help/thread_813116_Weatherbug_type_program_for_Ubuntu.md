### Post by LeoSolaris on 2008-05-30
I will toss my vote on the AWN pile. Awn is rather nice to begin with, and with the bzr version, we finally have access to the applets that actually make AWN worth while. I actually stopped the Gnome panel from started, and exclusively use AWN as my "panel." The weather applet has a nice curved display of the 5day forecast when clicked, and when you right click on it there is an option to see the radar.

Very convenient. I have always had one minor bug with the weather applet. Every now and then it will freeze. Deactivating, and re-activating it in preferences brings it back though.

Leo
