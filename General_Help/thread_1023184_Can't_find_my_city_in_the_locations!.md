The time is easy enough - just select any of the Syrian cities available, as they all use the same timezone.

That's not as straightforward.  I believe the weather applet uses [METAR weather data]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/METAR") which usually comes from stations at airports and airbases. Certainly, every city/location I've seen listed in the applet has an airport or airbase in the area.

I don't think setting a nearby city is necessarily useful in your case, as it seems the nearest avaliable one is Latakia, still a good 100km away.
