### Post by Baldrick_NZ on 2014-01-21
I'm really not sure where to put this, but mods feel free to move to the most appropriate place...

If you have 'DropBox' installed on your PC, delete it immediately! 

My darling wife wanted to install it last night as part of a work contract, and against my gut feeling we proceeded. Worst mistake ever!

Be warned, version 1.6.1 appears to be malware afflicted. We allowed it to update from 1.6.0, which it did - then proceeded to immediately delete not only existing programs from our laptop - but also vital system files. By the time I realised what was going on and stopped it, it was too late.

I had to completely re-install Ubuntu and restore from back-ups. Fortunately, Michelle seems to have got off pretty much unscathed in terms of file loss. However, I have lost a great deal of my photography and music collection. The last back-up I did for me didn't take - the back up program said it did at the time.

Please share this around as I'd hate to see others become another completely unnecessary statistic.

Dropbox? More like Dropkick!
