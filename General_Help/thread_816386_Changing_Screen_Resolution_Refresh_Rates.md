### Post by cybrsaylr on 2008-06-02
It there anyway to change the Ubuntu Screen Resolution Refresh rates?
I've used FF, GG and now Hardy and all have the refresh rates locked at 60Hz which is a bit hard on your eyes. On Windows you can change refresh rates to 70Hz, 80Hz, 85Hz, etc., to ease eye strain. Is there any way to do this with Ubuntu?

---

### Post by rlhawk1 on 2008-06-11
Go to System->Preferences->Screen Resolution

From there, you should be able to change the resolution and refresh rate.
