### Post by madhater on 2012-12-05
One possible reason for this is that the **ABC **button located at the centre of the standard toolbar is not depressed. This button is marked **ABC** and has a red squiggly line line below it.  The red **ABC** button controls the auto spell checking features of Libre Office Writer.
