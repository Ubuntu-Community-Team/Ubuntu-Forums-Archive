### Post by Zill on 2010-09-15
I believe the ALLFINE PC703 may be similar to the [CnMbook]("http://194.150.201.35/cnmlifestyle/cnmbook/cnmbook.htm").  The main difference between the two versions shown is that one runs Linux and one runs Windows CE.

AIUI, the two netbooks use different processors and have heavily customised operating systems pre-installed.  This means that it is very difficult to modify the OS, and probably impossible to install Linux on the Windows version.

However, enthusiasts have made a few tweaks to the Linux units and you can find more details at the [Small Linux Laptop Forum]("http://linuxlaptopforum.ark2webdesign.co.uk/").

These are quite handy units and my wife uses our Linux version to keep in touch by email while away.  Having said that, don't expect *too* much from them. ;-)
