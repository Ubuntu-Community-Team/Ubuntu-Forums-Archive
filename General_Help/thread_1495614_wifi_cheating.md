I think this may be the result of your friend and the people at the store being posed different questions.  Your friend knows exactly what you want to do: use the internet at another house on **one** computer, so he says boost the antenna on your computer.  The people at the store may think that you want to: simply increase the range of your network, so they say to boost the antenna on your mom's computer.  I say: either will work fine if there's only one computer on your end, but is there's the possibility that you'll want more than one at some point, put the better antenna at the router.
