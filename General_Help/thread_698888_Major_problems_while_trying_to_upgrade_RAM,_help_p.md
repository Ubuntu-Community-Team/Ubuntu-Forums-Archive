### Post by jrusso2 on 2008-02-16
The beep may mean the ram is not seated all the way.  It is very easy to not push it into the slot all the way and still be able to get the clips on the end.  Be careful not to apply to much pressure as it is possbile to break the mother board.

### Post by Sudz on 2008-02-16
In general there isn't a lot of juice going to the RAM so to make it smoke so you probably screwed up something else likely undoing the 'unplug all the wires' phase.  
- Flip the computer over to see if you dropped a screw or something else inside
- Make sure that every wire is going where it is supposed to
- Double check that there are no wires or cables brushing up against fans

See if the new RAM that you took back out smells or it is something else in there.
