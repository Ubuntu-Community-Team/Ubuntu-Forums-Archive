### Post by jd65pl on 2007-08-18
You will more likely than not be limited by your mother board in this respect rather than the linux kernel. What size drive would you be looking to use and what are the specs of the computer it will be used in. You should definately check the spec/limitations of your motherboard to ensure it will be able to use the capacity of the disk.
