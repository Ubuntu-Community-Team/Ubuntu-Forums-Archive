### Post by lgp171188 on 2009-03-15
When the GDM login screen appears, I used to get the default Ubuntu start-up sound but after some time, the sound has become corrupt and it now sounds like the noise on the phone line. Is there any way to reset it to normal.

---

### Post by lgp171188 on 2009-03-15
It was actually not a corrupt sound playing and someone had changed the default gdm startup sound to something that sounds like a corrupt sound.
I went to System->Administration->Login Window->Accessibility and changed it back to the default Ubuntu drum roll sound.
