### Post by lswest on 2008-10-21
panels change with the gtk control theme (right-click the desktop go to "change desktop background" and then choose the theme tab).  You can find more themes at gnome-look.org  You can also right-click a panel and choose "properties" and change a number of things there too.

Emerald only changes the window decoration (read: the border) and has no affect on the panel apart from giving them dropshadows.
