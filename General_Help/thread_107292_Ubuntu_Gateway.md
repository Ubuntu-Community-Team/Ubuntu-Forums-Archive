### Post by schilcha on 2005-12-23
I guess you need, what many other home-users need: IP-Masquerading / Network Address Translation. There's a howto at the netfilter-site ([http://www.netfilter.org/documentation/HOWTO//NAT-HOWTO.html]("http://www.netfilter.org/documentation/HOWTO//NAT-HOWTO.html")) as well as at the linux documentation project ([http://www.tldp.org/HOWTO/IP-Masquerade-HOWTO/)](http://www.tldp.org/HOWTO/IP-Masquerade-HOWTO/)).
Have a look at those docs, if they match your situation.

Good Luck!
