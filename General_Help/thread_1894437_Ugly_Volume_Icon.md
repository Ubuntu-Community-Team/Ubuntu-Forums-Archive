### Post by petru.marginean on 2012-01-19
Solved it: install/run gnome-tweak-tool, select 'Theme', and then verify the 'Icon Theme' = 'Humanity'.

Change it to something else, like 'Gnome (default)', or 'Neu'; the icon will look better with any other choice (but 'Humanity')
