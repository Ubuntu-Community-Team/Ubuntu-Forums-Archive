### Post by x1a4 on 2007-03-10
Gaim is a fairly good GTK instant messenger which support all major IM protocols and most, if not all, of their features.  Personally though, I'm partial to SIM (Simple Instant Messenger) for KDE.  Kopete, also KDE-based, is quite good as well.  

To install 

Gaim: 

*sudo apt-get gaim gaim-data gaim-extendedprefs gaim-guifications gaim-themes*

SIM

*sudo apt-get sim*

Kopete

*sudo apt-get kopete*
