### Post by jdbaok on 2008-11-18
I have been having a similar problem. Finally from a terminal window in /usr/bin I typed alsamixer and hit enter. The text-based "gui" that showed up had my "front" speakers set at 75%.  I cranked that up and my sound is back to normal!

Hope this helps.:guitar:
