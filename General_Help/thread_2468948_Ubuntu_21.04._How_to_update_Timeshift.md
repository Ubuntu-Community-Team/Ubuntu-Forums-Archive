### Post by deadflowr on 2021-11-15
Scroll down the github page to section marked Installation. That part explains how to add the ppa.

Unless a package follows Ubuntu's [StableReleaeUpdates]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/StableReleaseUpdates") procedure, it'll never get an update.
