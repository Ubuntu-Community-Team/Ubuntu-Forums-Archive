How can I make workspaces have names under 10.04?

Under 9.10 I can given different workspaces different names, under 10.04 I can't. workspace-switcher-preferences has less options.

---

### Post by zoubidoo on 2010-05-26
Solved my own problem!

System -> Preferences -> Appearance -> Visual Effects needs to be "None" for workspace naming.  Nothing to do with the release.
