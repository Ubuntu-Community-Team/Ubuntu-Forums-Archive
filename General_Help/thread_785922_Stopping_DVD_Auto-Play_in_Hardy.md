### Post by Dr Sketch on 2008-05-07
Ok, I know this seems like a strange request, but bear with me.  How do you stop a DVD from automatically playing in Hardy?  In Gutsy it was simple, but for some unknown reason DVDs aren't listed in "Removable Drives" anymore...  I don't want the DVD to autoplay in a different program (there are threads showing how to change the autoplay to be VLC, but that's not what I want), I just want to keep it from playing period, until I choose to open the program myself.  Any ideas???

---

### Post by smartboyathome on 2008-05-07
Open Nautilus, your file manager, and then go to Edit > Preferences, click the media tab, and for DVD video, select do nothing.
