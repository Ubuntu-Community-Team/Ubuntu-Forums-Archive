### Post by Krytarik on 2011-03-06
> **luigi_mb said:**
> Preferences > Personal Stuff > Browsing Data > Importa Data from Another Browser > ...

That's actually the other way around. ;-)

It's like *marin123* said:
- in Chromium choose the wrench, then "Bookmark Manager"
- then click at "Organize", "Export Bookmarks..."
- choose a location, filename and append ".html" to it
- "Save", obviously

- in Firefox go to "Bookmarks -> Organize Bookmarks..."
- choose "Import and Backup", then "Import HTML..."
- the rest is intuitive

- then the re-ordering stuff

Greetings.
