### Post by mb_webguy on 2009-04-19
If your laptop's wireless card isn't supported natively by Ubuntu, you'll have to use the Windows wireless driver and ndiswrapper.  Here's the [ndiswrapper how-to guide]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WifiDocs/Driver/Ndiswrapper").  If you have any problems, have a look at the [comprehensive ndiswrapper troubleshooting guide]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=885847").
