### Post by avtolle on 2008-06-12
That's a FF issue, not at OS issue. Take a look under Edit>>Preferences to see how long FF is directed to retain this information; you may shorten it (as I recall, FF default is 90 days), which will allow some reduction.

EDIT: The tab to "click" on is Privacy; you can uncheck the first box that appears about remembering, or shorten the time, as you see fit.
