### Post by stinkeye on 2013-03-09
The nautilus scripts are not included in a default install.
They are included in ubuntu-tweak. Have a look in **/usr/share/ubuntu-tweak/scripts/**

Ubuntu-tweak allows you to add these to **~/.gnome2/nautilus-scripts**
You can just as easily download and add your own scripts to **~/.gnome2/nautilus-scripts** from
[http://gnome-look.org/index.php?xcontentmode=188]("http://gnome-look.org/index.php?xcontentmode=188")
