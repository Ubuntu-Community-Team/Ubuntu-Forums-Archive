### Post by jdunn on 2011-05-28
I was recently looking for a way to do this before finding this thread.  I ended up finding a solution that was near enough for my purposes but is NOT a conversion from PDF to PowerPoint.  LibreOffice (and OpenOffice) have an extension called [oracle-pdfimport.oxt]("http://extensions.services.openoffice.org/project/pdfimport") that allows LibreOffice/OpenOffice to read in PDF files.  Unfortunately, when I tried to read in a PDF (created by LibreOffice Impress), LibreOffice read it into LO Draw as several slides.  However, Draw allows me to print out multiple slides to a page (just as Impress does) so, my goals are met.
