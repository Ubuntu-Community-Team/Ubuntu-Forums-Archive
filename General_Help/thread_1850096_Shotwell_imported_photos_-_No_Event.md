### Post by eric-yorba on 2011-09-27
We provide Shotwell in source form primarily.  But for Ubuntu we have a PPA here:
[https://launchpad.net/~yorba/+archive/ppa](https://launchpad.net/~yorba/+archive/ppa)

You can simply add ppa:yorba/ppa to your repository list in Synaptic and then do an update.  The latest and greatest Shotwell will be installed automatically.
