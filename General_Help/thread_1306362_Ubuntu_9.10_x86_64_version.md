On the Ubuntu homepage, click on Download Ubuntu 9.10 link. It will take you to a different page - over there and under the "Begin Download" button, you will find a link titled - "Alternative download options, including Ubuntu installer for Windows". Click on that, and you should be able to select 64bit. Once you do that, the button will change to 64 bit OS.
