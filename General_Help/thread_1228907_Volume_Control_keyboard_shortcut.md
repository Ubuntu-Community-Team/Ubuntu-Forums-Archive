### Post by CatKiller on 2009-08-01
> **paradigm2 said:**
> How may I control the volume by using my new keyboard?

System &#8594; Preferences &#8594; Keyboard Shortcuts. Find the "Volume up" and "Volume down" entries and click in the box to select the shortcut of your choosing.
