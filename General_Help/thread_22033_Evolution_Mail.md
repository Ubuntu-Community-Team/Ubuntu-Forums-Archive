   Well, I don't know about importing from Thunderbird to Evolution, but why not just use Thunderbird on Linux since Mozilla makes one for it. You can get it from [www.mozilla.com](www.mozilla.com) and then you don't need to worry about importing anything. I simply copied my mail folders, went into the account settings after I had the account set up, and told it to use the specific mail folders that I copied over the account. Then just exit and startup Thunderbird again and your account shows up.
