### Post by howefield on 2009-03-20
1. Background is called the skydome. Open compizconfig setting manager and select the Desktop Cube option, then the Appearance tab, Skydome feature is in there.

2. Do you mean the cube caps, same place as above.

3. In the same setting, (desktop cube) press the Transparent cube tab and drag the slider for Opacity During Rotation to the required level.
