### Post by agnes on 2010-04-25
Soundconverter = GUI app for audio conversion. It uses Gstreamer.

Both ffmpeg and mencoder are great tools for respectively audio and video converting.
[mencoder cli usage]("http://www.mplayerhq.hu/DOCS/HTML/en/mencoder.html")
For the GUI: [URL="http://www.mplayerhq.hu/design7/projects.html"]
mencoder GUI's/frontends[/URL] (Ctrl+F "mencoder") (e.g. GMencoder; YAMF.)
[WinFF]("http://winff.org/html_new/") is a GUI for ffmpeg.
[Hyper Video Converter]("http://gtk-apps.org/content/show.php/Hyper+Video+Converter?content=88970") is a GUI for both ffmpeg & mencoder.

Avidemux is more used as a video editor I believe but can also convert videos.
