### Post by quixote on 2010-02-17
Unfortunately I don't know much about netbook remix (I've used the ordinary desktop version for years), but let's see how we go.  Are you using karmic (9.10)?  When you get "GNU GRUB V4" do you mean that you get the Grub boot screen?  Do you have a choice to boot into recovery mode?

Do you have data on the netbook that is very important?  If so, and if you have an external boot medium, such as a bootable USB or a CD drive you can attach, then the first step is to boot using that.  Open a nautilus file manager, and find the drive under "Places" that has the right size to be your netbook drive.  Click on it to mount it, and then move your vital data to a USB stick or somewhere off the computer first.  Anything that's done to recover the system could fail (hopefully it won't! :D), but then at least you have your data.
