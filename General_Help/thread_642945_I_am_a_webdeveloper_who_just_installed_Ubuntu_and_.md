### Post by d0nny2600 on 2007-12-17
For a GUI to control lamp try phpadmin

you can access it from localhost/phpadmin once installed. 

For the keyboard issue. Go to preferences->keyboard and try some of the various mappings there. I had a problem with my packard bell laptop and found a compatible mapping there.

For the iPod try getting gtkPod to manage it. It should work with that. Also YamiPod is another manager.
