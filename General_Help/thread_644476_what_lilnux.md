### Post by Ptero-4 on 2008-02-21
If you want a quick, sure-fire way of knowing which distro will run your box, do this.
Go to [www.distrowatch.com](www.distrowatch.com) and search for distros using (in order from fastest to not so fast) fvwm95, qvwm, icewm, jwm, ede, xpde and last kde, bonus points for bsd's or cli distros (they're faster). If you want to know why, it's simple, the less graphical and more windoze-like the faster, the more graphical and mac-like the slower. f.e: puppy is faster than geubuntu and geubuntu is faster than xubuntu, but kubuntu is as fast as puppy, also PC-BSD is faster than even puppy, on the other hand ubuntu is slower than xubuntu which is also slower than kubuntu (and that is knowing that KDE and GNOME are about similar sized DE's and they're DE'S not WM's yet KDE is as fast as IceWM).
