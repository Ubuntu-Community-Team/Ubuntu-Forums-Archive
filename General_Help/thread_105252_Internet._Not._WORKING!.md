But just in case someone else is wondering the same thing: Connect your dsl box through the ethernet cable.

Then go to System -> Administration -> Networking (which should take you to Network Settings), then click Ethernet Connection, select "Enable this device",and then choose what your using (Static or DHCP). Hope that helps someone.
