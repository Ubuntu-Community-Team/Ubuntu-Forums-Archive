As for adobe CS4 aplications there really isn't too many alternatives for that exept a bit primative version of flash called f4lm. Also, you most likely know this but, ubuntu is free and always will be free. If you need any help with ubuntu pm (private message) me or post here in the general category. If you want a choice between ubuntu and XP you should look into wubi, when you burn your ubuntu disk, go and find wubi.exe and a very easy instalation is waiting for you. I really hope this helped.
