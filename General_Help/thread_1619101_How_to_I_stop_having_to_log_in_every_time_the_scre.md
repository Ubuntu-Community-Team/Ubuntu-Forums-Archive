A few days ago I upgraded to Ubuntu 10.04 LTS Lucid Lynx and every time the screen saver kicks in I have to log on again. ### Post by ean5533 on 2010-11-11
Go to System->Preferences->Screensaver

Then uncheck "Lock screen when screensaver is active".
