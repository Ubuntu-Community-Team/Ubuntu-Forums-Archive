### Post by mickbuntu on 2007-04-21
Seems the password is working but you do not see  *** casters. In  linux and unix in general it is  normal.  Just hit enter after typing your password as you  would do normally. Note: Root does not exist by default just  use sudo with your first ubuntu  account created. Your  first  ubuntu created account  becomes a super user account. That allows you to do same things as root would. Just be careful even with  using sudo.
