### Post by avtolle on 2008-05-19
Did you remember to "unmount" the stick before you removed it (right click the icon, select "Unmount Volume")? If you didn't, I suspect, but don't know, that the system was not finished writing to the drive, and thus your problem. What I'd give a try to is putting the memory stick back in, copying the file over again, and then do what I suggested, and do not remove it until the thing is unmounted (popup comes up on my laptop, on my Mac Cube, icon disappears from the desktop).
