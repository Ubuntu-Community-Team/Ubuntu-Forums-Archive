### Post by MysticalRiotCandy on 2009-05-08
Most browsers prefetch data while you're reading their pages.  If you loaded up UbuntuForums.org on a less-than-great connection speed, it might take a while to download the page, because it was only prompted at that moment.  However, when you're on the website, it'll start cacheing all of the other links, so that when you click on them, it only has to load it up from there.  What you're being affected by is more of the fault of your ISP's service plan.
