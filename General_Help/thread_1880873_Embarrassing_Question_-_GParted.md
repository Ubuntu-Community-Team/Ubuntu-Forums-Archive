### Post by Rubi1200 on 2011-11-14
The recommended approach to working with partitions is from the LiveCD so that the filesystems are unmounted.

You may need to turn off swap with right-click > Swapoff.

Backups are always good and highly commendable that you took this approach.

If you still need advice, post a screenshot of GParted from the LiveCD before you start working with the partitions.
