I think the easiest way to solve this is to switch from stereo to mono.  

I can't test this right now without a stereo audio file, but I think that if you double click your speaker icon and go Edit -> Preferences:

Turn on "Mix Mono" and you'll have a new tab in Volume Control.  Make sure "Mix Mono" is checked.  I didn't see anything else that looked like it switches on mono, so this must do it.
