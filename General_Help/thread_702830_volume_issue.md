### Post by DoctorMO on 2008-02-20
It sounds like the device and/or channel that are currently set as your default are messed up for gnome.

Go into "System > Preferences > Sound" to see what sound sub system is being used and what is set as the default channel. I set mine to PCM since that controls all sound out. You'll notice that the headphone socket and the speakers have a different channel and for all you know you could be changing the volume for something you can't hear.

For the volume controller should follow the default your using.
