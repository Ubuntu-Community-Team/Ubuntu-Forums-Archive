### Post by MG&amp;TL on 2012-04-17
Play around and install what you like. Yes, some distributions are designed with power consumption, etc. in mind, but as long as your netbook can run it, I see no reason why you cannot simply install these tweaks if that is what you want. It's Linux-enjoy your choice. :)

On a side note, it may be worth getting an up-to-date distribution-a kernel update has much improved battery life for several people with laptops/netbooks.
