### Post by anjilslaire on 2008-12-03
To start, set up hotmail in Outlook/Windows Mail and export it to a file. This way, at least you'll have a backup i some form.

Looking through one of my hotmail accts, I don't see any way to retroactively fed mail to another acct, but you can easily start auto-forwarding any new mails.
