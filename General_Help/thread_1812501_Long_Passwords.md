Has anyone wanted to set real long passwords but choose not to because of the typing involved?

Create an empty dummy text document using kate, gedit or any package and save it to your desktop, and save as <PASSWORD>. 

<PASSWORD> would be hAHSKKAGSgjhaG OR 78394HHWQE8GY or whatever your password is the document name is your password.

When authentication pops up and requests the password or in Gmail or Facebook or whatever, then just right click the document, then select rename ---> right click ---->   copy and then paste it to the password request and click authenticate or ok.

Now you can get real creative with a looooong password.
