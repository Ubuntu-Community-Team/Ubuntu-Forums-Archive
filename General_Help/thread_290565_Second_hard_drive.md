To answer your question: you should be able to go to Administration~>Disks (I think... I'm on Winderz @ work now) and find the information that you need on your HDD as well as mounting it. 
You can also check out the [Psychocats web site]("http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/separatehome") (not *exactly* what you're asking about but in the ballpark) on editing your FSTAB file and mounting via the terminal. 
Good luck and check back if you need anything else.
