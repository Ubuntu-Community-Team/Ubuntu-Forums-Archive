### Post by Cavsfan on 2017-04-29
I finally found a PPA that will provide Emerald for 16.04. If you are one of those that cannot live without it, like me, here it is:

[https://launchpad.net/~manatails/+archive/ubuntu/emerald](https://launchpad.net/~manatails/+archive/ubuntu/emerald)

Once installed, just add **/usr/bin/emerald** to the CCSM Window Decoration command. It also installs the Emerald Theme Manager when you install Emerald.

I logged out and back it and BAM it was working.
