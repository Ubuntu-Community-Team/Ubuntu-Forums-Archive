The only one of those you mention that's going to put much load on the cpu at all is the app install. The Athlon 4200+ X2 is a dual core processor, so 60% load corresponds to 100% load on one core (handling the install) and 20% on the other (doing everything else), which, for that workload, is about what one would expect.
