### Post by fragos on 2006-10-27
I'm impressed with the DVI connection to my LCD monitor.  If your video card has a DA15 15 pin connector you'll need to use VGA.  There is no need for any different software because of a DVI monitor.  You may have some configuration changes to make if the aspect ratioo of your new monitor is different than that which you had.  CRTs are 4:3 ratio and widescreen LCDs are 16:9 or 16:10 and you'll want to use an appropriate resolution like 1440x900.  Your monitor documentation will tell you what the maximum resolution for that device is.
