### Post by sbraz on 2011-01-14
powertop said i had to do these two things
```
echo 1 > /sys/module/snd_hda_intel/parameters/power_save
echo 1500 > /proc/sys/vm/dirty_writeback_centisecs

```
i've added these two lines to my /etc/rc.local file, as this script runs at boot afaik.
