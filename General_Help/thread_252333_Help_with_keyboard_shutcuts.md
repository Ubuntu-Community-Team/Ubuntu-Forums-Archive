### Post by Ciego on 2006-09-14
I don't know if this is going to help you out with the headphone problem (I do not use headphones) but you can give it a try.  If you right click on the speaker icon, and choose preferences, you can choose the device and track that you want the master slider to control.  In your case, you would probably leave the device as is, but change the track to "Phone". I am not sure if that is the correct one for headphones and I do not have any here to test it with)  You can also get here from the Volume Control window  Edit>Preferences.
