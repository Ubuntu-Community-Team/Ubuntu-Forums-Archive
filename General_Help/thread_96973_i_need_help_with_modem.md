### Post by Randy Sparks on 2005-11-30
Sadly, support for HSF modems (ie Winmodems) like yours isn't built into Ubuntu at the moment. This means that you have to manually download and install drivers. The link given by the previous poster assumes that the modem's driver is installed. 

The people at Linmodems ([http://linmodems.org](http://linmodems.org)) provide the software but, for some reason, this can't be found in the Ubuntu software repositories. So you'll have to download the software from their site and follow their instructions. Luckily they seem like a bunch of nice people who will help you out if you ask.

Take a look at this to get started, and good luck!

[http://linmodems.technion.ac.il/first.html](http://linmodems.technion.ac.il/first.html)

Also bear in mind that people on this forum will help if you ask. :)
