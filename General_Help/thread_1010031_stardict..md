### Post by arrancaru on 2008-12-13
You can use babylon dictionaries and convert them do stardict format. I did that for english-portuguese dictionaries and works like a charm. You can grab the dictionaries [here]("http://www.babylon.com/category/11/Hebrew.html") and follow the tutorial explaining how to convert [here]("http://swik.net/opensuse/Planet+SuSE/Rossana+Motta:+Converting+Babylon+Dictionaries+to+Stardict+Format+in+OpenSuse/b7lz5")
