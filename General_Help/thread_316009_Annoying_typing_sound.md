### Post by bscbrit on 2006-12-10
It could be a hard drive doing something, but if its the first time that you have noticed it, it probably isn't good news.  

Just in case it is the start of a hard drive failure, back up all your important data to somewhere safe i.e. not onto a partition on the same drive.
