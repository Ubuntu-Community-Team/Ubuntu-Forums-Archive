### Post by Zimmer on 2006-05-24
Most, if not all, of the  MP3 players about have a USB connector. Breezy should (it does for mine) recognise it and mount it as a drive (mass storage device).
Drag and drop your MP3 files  to it and 'umount' it and wait a few moments before disconnecting it . Be aware that if you delete files on it it will likely create hidden trash folders. Make sure you empty and delete them, else you will quickly fill the player with hidden trash!
Which particular player were you interested in? It may be someone on the forums has one and will tell you for certain  how well it works !
