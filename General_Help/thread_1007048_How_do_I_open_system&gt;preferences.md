### Post by jocko on 2008-12-10
"Preferences" (or is it "Settings"?) is not a program, it's one of the sub-menus under "System" in the gnome menu, and contain the starters for lots of preferences/settings applications, like screen-saver settings, power management, network configuration and so on...

Note that there are many different language versions other than english, so if someone on the forum tell you to look under System-->Preferences, that may actually be translated from another language and should perhaps really be System-->Settings... (as an example the english words settings and preferences are both translated to "inställningar" in swedish versions of programs)
