### Post by WSmart on 2009-04-22
I think you need to try and be a little more patient.  These things take time.  :P     

I usually pause flash and let it completely load before viewing.  If it's caused by a bad wireless signal or slow download, you should be able to tell by looking at the progress bars.  I you have a lot of CPU loading, that can cause flash issues.  Sometimes one bad tab can cause slow downs.(and see the bold text below for another angle).  


I like Opera too.  I started using firefox for the autopager plugin, but Opera's great.  I think they don't focus development on it because the mobile platform is doing so well.  Check out the email client and RSS feeds support.  Opera also has a chat client.  You can get generally pretty good help at the #opera channel.  They tend to be a bit uptight and yelling seems to be a sport there, which is another reason I got turned off-I have my own frustrations believe it or not.  They aren't Opera employees, but it is the official Opera support channel.  Mostly it's active after 10am GMT, I believe it is, as most help is coming out of Europe.  

I like the 'paste and go' context mention option.  I like the crtl+tab that toggles your open tabs like alt+tab does for application interfaces.:)  The mouse gestures are very helpful.  Opera was an early developer of the tabs idea.  I like the text to speech and voice commands but there's no Linux support on those.  I like the fit to page button.  If you like customizing stuff, **check out the Tools>Preferences>Advanced options.  In the downloads section there you can change which programs Opera uses to handle different content.  You might look to see what is listed for your flash-make sure you uncheck the 'hide file types opened with Opera' box.  You might have multiple flash players installed and Opera might be using an old one.**  I actually came here today because my flash is not working at all, so that's one I need to look at too.  Ha!  That worked!   There's a million option in that Advanced section though.  You can change fonts all over.  You can create custom keyboard shortcuts.  Check it out.  There's also  a customize toolbars interface with a lot of options.  The different panels are useful.  Can't believe I fixed my flash just blabbering here.  Good grief.  I guess I should thank you.  

Thanks for the post!
