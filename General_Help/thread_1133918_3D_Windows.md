### Post by Merk42 on 2009-04-23
Go to Add/Remove and get **Advanced Desktop Effects Settings**
Once installed it will be under System > Preferences > CompizConfig Settings Manager
Check the box next to **Shift Switcher** (if it isn't already)
Click on Shift Switcher to change the settings, in the Appearance tab change if from **Cover** (iTunes like) to **Flip** (Vista, 7, what you pictured)
