Hi, well the best alternative that I can give you for fences will be KDE 4 where Stardock got inspired to create fences, or at least I think they got the idea from there

You can install it by opening the Software Center and searching for Kubuntu and installing the package kubuntu-desktop.

Then just log off and change the login settings to start into Kubuntu (KDE) and give it a try, that would be the best option I have at this moment for you, hope will help you
