### Post by ian-weisser on 2024-11-06
> **fran_3 said:**
> 
1 - Does it come with a GUI desktop?


Ubuntu Desktop comes with a desktop. Ubuntu Server does not.
Check which one you downloaded.


> **fran_3 said:**
> 
2 - Does it come with a server, PHP, and MySQL?


You add those after install. Not sure what you mean by "a server". If you mean a webserver, you add it after install.

> **fran_3 said:**
>

Yes, if you wish. It's a handy way to test that all your hardware is compatible.
