### Post by mysticrider92 on 2007-12-01
> **rahimveron said:**
> I have to install cd burning appz while in the live mode. Isnt it?

Most any live distro with a 'toram' (kernel) option (e.g. Knoppix) will have at least one cd burning app installed by default. That option will not work with the Ubuntu live cd's, or most other distros meant for hard drive installation.
