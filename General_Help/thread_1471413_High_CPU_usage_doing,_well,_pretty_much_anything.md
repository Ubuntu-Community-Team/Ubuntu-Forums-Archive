You may have answered your own question. A clogged CPU cooler & fan can cause the CPU to overheat and perhaps invoke performance slowdowns as a last-ditch attempt to keep itself cool.

Take off the cover, blow out everything with compressed air in an environment with Relative Humidity above 40%, preferably higher.
