### Post by KBar on 2021-12-07
Well, there are music streaming services. They don't cost a lot. I, for example, use Yandex.Music. Most of them prohibit downloading, though.

Rhythmbox has a built-in Internet radio. Choose whatever genre you like and enjoy. 

Soundcloud used to have the download feature. Perhaps it still does, although it may require an account.

Google also shows this site: [https://freemusicarchive.org/](https://freemusicarchive.org/)

### Post by him610 on 2021-12-07
> ...where do I go to download new music?
Download your newly acquired music (I normally get mine from Amazon) to your designated music library storage area. Within Rhythmbox, import your just downloaded music just as you did from your music library.
