### Post by Hedgehog1 on 2011-04-28
Are you running off a LiveCD/LiveUSB?  The LiveCD will not remember any settings after reboot as the entire session is on a RamDrive and thrown away.  A LiveUSB can be created with persistence to give you some storage, so things like this will be 'remembered' for next time.
