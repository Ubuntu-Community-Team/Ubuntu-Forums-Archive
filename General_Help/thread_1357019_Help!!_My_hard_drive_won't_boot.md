### Post by Leppie on 2009-12-16
press F8 after the bios screen and repeat pressing it until you get a boot menu (or your the pc starts beeping). then from the menu choose "last known good configuration".
you need to do this when the disk is mounted in your wife's pc, otherwise it may mess up the registry.
