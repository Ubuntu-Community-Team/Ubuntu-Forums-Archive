### Post by guiverc on 2023-08-04
You mention Ubuntu 23.04; but not which product (ie. Server, Desktop, or a *flavor*).

What you are describing though could be a number of things, **Power Save** option that reduces the *brightness* as doing that prolongs battery life (*will apply if using a Desktop system*), a Night Light feature (*which can be enabled/disable, though mouse won't change this on inactivity*), or** screensaver option** (*the mouse movements will stop **this as your describe & is triggered by inactivity*).

The power save/screensaver options do not generally impact external monitors (*they have their own power supply generally, though screensaver options can influence external displays too*).

Thus I'd explore all three:**  Power settings**, **screensaver** settings & even Night Light (*the night light option is a stretch as it's influenced by time & mouse movements won't impact that*).
