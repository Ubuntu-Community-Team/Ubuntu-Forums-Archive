### Post by 3rdalbum on 2011-08-12
Use a video encoding program to convert the videos to xvid at the resolution and framerate you quoted before. The audio should be (probably) mp3. And it should be inside an AVI container. For the video track, maybe use 500kbps as the bitrate; any higher and it probably won't be able to decode it. You could go a lower bitrate if you don't get good results with 500kbps.
