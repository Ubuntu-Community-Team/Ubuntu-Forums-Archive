I havn't tried this, but it suppose to work.
since a DVD is just a mpg video with a chaptering system

Convert your video to a mpg using FFMPG
fmpeg -i foo.avi -aspect 16:9 -target ntsc-dvd foo.mpg

then you can use something like dvdstyler to master the mpg file into a dvd file.
