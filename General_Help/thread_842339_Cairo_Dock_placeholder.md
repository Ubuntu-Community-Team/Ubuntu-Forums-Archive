### Post by easybake on 2008-06-27
all you have to do is right click on the cairo dock. Go into cairo-dock>configure. Simply delete the image address for the 'callback zone'. In easy mode 'callback zone' is under the personalization tab. in advanced mode its under the background tab.

### Post by freakNeck on 2008-06-27
As it turns out there is a setting for the placeholder image.

In Cairo Settings, under 'Background', the top section is 'Callback Zone'.

In there is a option..."Filename of an image to put in the callback zone:" and it was filled in with a default image.

Blanking it out clears the placeholder image. I was a little confused, since the options are for the 'Background' which I took to be the background of the Dock itself.

Thanks.
