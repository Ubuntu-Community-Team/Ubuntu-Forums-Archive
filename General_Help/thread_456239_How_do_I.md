### Post by shen-an-doah on 2007-05-27
I'm pretty sure he just did a screenshot while the cube was rotating. If you move the cube by holding Ctrl+Alt and your left mouse button, you can take your fingers off Ctrl+Alt, but keep the mouse button held down and you can keep rotating the cube. Then just hit "Print screen" on your keyboard and you'll get a screenshot of your cube.
