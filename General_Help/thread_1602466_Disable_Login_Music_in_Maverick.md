### Post by Elycian on 2010-10-22
I was looking for a solution to this too and found this answer in another thread. I'm about to test it to see if it works

>  Go to System>Preferences>startup Applications un-check GNOME Login sounds! 

Hopefully it works!

EDIT: Hi OP, I hope you're still checking your thread because this method does indeed work :) If you're also looking to disable the simple drum beat when it pops up at the login page you can change that in Ubuntu-Tweak fairly easy. (Although I'm sure there's a super easy native way to do it too)
