### Post by lovinglinux on 2012-02-01
I don't thin k there is a way to open an RSS feed from a link directly with Bamboo. The way you are trying will not work, because Bamboo is an extension and runs inside Firefox. So, don't bother looking for the installation path. It won't help.

Set the Web Feed option to "Preview in Firefox" instead of "Other". Then when you visit a page with a feed and click the feed icon, the rss page will open in your browser directly. Then, instead of adding it to Live Bookmarks, click the Bamboo reader button in the toolbar, then the "+ADD" button in the Bamboo interface. It will offer to subscribe to the current page feed.
