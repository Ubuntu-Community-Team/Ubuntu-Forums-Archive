### Post by strabes on 2007-04-08
Are you using OpenOffice.org Presentation to make your slide show? If your slide show consists only of pictures then that program is unnecessary. You can simply copy all of the pictures into one folder on your hard drive, open the folder in the file browser, and double click on one of the pictures. This will open up a window where the picture is shown larger. Next, on the menu bar, click on "View" and then "Full Screen" You can then hit left or right to cycle through all of the pictures in that folder.

Hope this helps. If you need more detailed steps, just reply.
