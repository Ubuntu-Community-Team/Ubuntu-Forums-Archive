### Post by mb_webguy on 2009-04-09
You must have accidentally deleted the Window List applet.  Just right-click on the panel, select "Add to Panel", and select the Window List applet.

Ok, right click on the lower bar, they are actually called panels, but that is not important. Select 'Add to Panel'.  A box will appear.  Choose the item 'Window List' and double click on it.  Your running apps should now appear as buttons on the bottom panel.  It will annoyingly put it right in the middle of the panel, but if you right click on it again you can move it all the way to the left and then lock it in position.  Hope that helps.:D
