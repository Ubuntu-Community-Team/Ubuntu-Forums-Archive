### Post by Quackers on 2011-05-15
To re-install Ubuntu you need to boot from the live cd/usb. Then choose "install ubuntu" and choose the "something else" option in the partitioning page, then use the same partition(s) that you currently use for Ubuntu.
Ubuntu has nothing to do with you not being able to boot from a cd. It does not change your bios or the ability to get into your bios. If you can't get to your bios then it is possible that you are using the wrong key, or not tapping it soon enough.
Incidentally why do you need to re-install Ubuntu?
