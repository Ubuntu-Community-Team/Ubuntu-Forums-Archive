### Post by Topsiho on 2008-12-30
The link given by eBoxNet is about a usb-printer, davehouston is talking about a printer connected to the parallel port of his router.
Years ago I had this same setup, with an Epson printer too. I am sorry that I do not know the details anymore, but I remember the most important one: that you had to use a printer queue called LPT1, which is typical for parallel printers.

Together with the other information you maybe will be set on the right track :) .
At some point you'll have to choose your printer from a long list, first by it's make (Epson) and next by it's model. Hope that it is in there.
