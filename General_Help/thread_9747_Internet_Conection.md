### Post by nbliang on 2005-01-06
To setup the ADSL connection, try this steps:

1.  Launch the Terminal or any console.

2.  Type "sudo pppoeconf" and press enter.

3.  Follow the instructions on the screen, and if you are not sure about the instructions just press Enter for the default value.

Good luck.
