### Post by dv3500ea on 2010-07-17
You should create a separate account for your child with limited privileges.
You can use a firefox addon to filter web pages: [http://busysphere.com/2009/09/safe-internet-for-children-parental-filters-in-mozilla-firefox/]("http://busysphere.com/2009/09/safe-internet-for-children-parental-filters-in-mozilla-firefox/") 
If they use empathy for IM, you can view their previous conversations by pressing F3 in an open empathy window when they are logged on.
