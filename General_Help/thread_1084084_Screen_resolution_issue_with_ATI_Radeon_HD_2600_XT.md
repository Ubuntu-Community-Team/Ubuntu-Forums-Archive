### Post by handy on 2009-03-01
I run the same GPU, it is in my 24" iMac, I use 1920x1200 screen resolution.  Most of the time the iMac is running Arch Linux.

I don't think you should run your screen at other refresh rates than the default 60Hz.

The ATi Catalyst Control Center really doesn't give you much to play with as far as settings are concerned.  I think you will very likely find that you are suffering from the inadequacies of the AMD/ATi Linux GPU drivers.

That said, I have been experiencing a great deal of improvement in these drivers, particularly over the last 6 months or so.  

I don't think it will be all that long before AMD/ATi lead the way in high quality Linux drivers.

Bottom line is, patience. ;)
