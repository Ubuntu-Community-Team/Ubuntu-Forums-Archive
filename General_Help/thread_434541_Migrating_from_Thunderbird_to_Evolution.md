### Post by aysiu on 2007-05-06
You may not use IMAP on a regular basis, but could you enable it temporarily just to ease the transfer?

I'd set up an "additional" account in Thunderbird that's IMAP, drag the messages you downloaded through POP3 to your "new" IMAP folders to temporarily put them on the server.

Then, close Thunderbird, open up Evolution, set up the account as POP3 and then redownload all the messages to your hard drive.
