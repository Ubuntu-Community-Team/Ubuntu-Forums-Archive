### Post by squimmy on 2009-04-26
Despite what some people will tell you you can actually get sound in wine using a creative x-fi sound blaster series sound, assuming youve already got it working with the OSS driver all you need to do is download the development version of wine (using instructions from their site) and then go to applications->wine->configure wine, go to the audio tab and click the box marked oss and untick the one marked ALSA then restart. simple as that.
