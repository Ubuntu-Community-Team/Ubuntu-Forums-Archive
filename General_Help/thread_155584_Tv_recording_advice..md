### Post by teet on 2006-04-05
I have a Leadtek Winfast TV 2000 XP RM (they choose the names, not me).

To get things setup, I would first just try to get live tv viewing working with a program called "tv time".  It should be in the repos.  It is actually a better program to view live tv with than mythtv (myth can record things though), since tv time just displays the signal while myth essentially records it to your harddrive and then plays it from there (so there's actually a 2-3 second lag between it and real time).

I have gotten mythtv to work with my card in the past (not exactly the same as yours, but uses the bt878 drivers).  I basically followed a tutorial I found somewhere (not here) that was for setting up a Happpauge PVR-150 with mythtv and figured out how to get the bttv drivers working myself.     

There's also a program called "freevo" that you might want to check out.  I'm currently waiting on parts to build my first PVR.  I'll probably end up going with mythtv, but I may give freevo a shot too.

Bottomline:  Try out tvtime first, then mythtv, freevo.  There's also xawtv but I didn't care for it much.
