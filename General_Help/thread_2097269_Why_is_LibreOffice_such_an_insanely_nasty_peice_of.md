### Post by lykwydchykyn on 2012-12-22
I've never seen this issue in Libreoffice, so I can't help you with that.  What you might or might not be interested to know is that the "openoffice.org" that shipped with Ubuntu for many years was actually extensively patched and probably closer to LibreOffice than the actual OpenOffice.org.

The point here is that there has been a change of name, and thus a change of perception, and the actual problem you're experiencing could as likely be linked to your version of Ubuntu as anything else.  

You could try downloading a newer version of LibreOffice from libreoffice.org, or (if names really matter) get Apache OpenOffice from openoffice.org.
