### Post by Altay_H on 2008-11-07
Bump.

I'm having the same problem. Pidgin separates my buddies by client (AIM, MSN, Jabber, and Facebook are each in a separate group) and I can't seem to merge them all into one. I just want to see all of my buddies listed by status regardless of client.


EDIT: I ended up solving my problem by dragging each buddy I have one at a time into a group, then deleting the now empty groups. I previously believed Pidgin did not allow more than one client in the same group, because the first time I tried to drag and drop a buddy from a different client into a group Pidgin froze up to the point where "sudo killall pidgin" wouldn't even kill it. Anyway, the problem is solved [for me], but I'd still be interested in knowing if there's a simpler solution. I don't understand why pidgin won't allow multiple selection.
