### Post by Mark Phelps on 2012-03-04
From what I read, folks are generally confusing what Ubuntu for Android really means.  Folks think it means installing Ubuntu on the Android phone -- and it does NOT.  What it means is running an Ubuntu (actually Unity) desktop on top of Android on the phone.

Similar to changing desktops on a PC running Ubuntu.  You can make it LOOK like OSX or even Win7, but it's not actually installing OSX or Win7.
