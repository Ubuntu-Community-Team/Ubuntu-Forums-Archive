### Post by Gandalf on 2005-12-16
skins and plugins cannot be installed this way, just go to [http://amsn.sf.net](http://amsn.sf.net) download the plugin/skin and extract the archive into ~/.amsn/plugins or ~/.amsn/skins (they must be inside a folder, like for example if u download the music plugin you must have ~/.amsn/plugins/music and files inside it
