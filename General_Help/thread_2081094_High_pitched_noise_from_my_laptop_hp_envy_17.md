### Post by 2F4U on 2012-11-06
Nobody here will be able to determine where the noise comes from, because we don't have access to the machine. Only you can locate the source.
Some areas to look at:
- Fans, CPU, graphics card, etc.
- Power supply unit (you would be amazed how many power units are the source of such a noise)
- Speakers, but I guess you already checked them

Certain ThinkPad models are known to emit a high pitch noise, but the reasons are different. This site lists some possible areas to investigate:

[http://www.thinkwiki.org/wiki/Problem_with_high_pitch_noises](http://www.thinkwiki.org/wiki/Problem_with_high_pitch_noises)
