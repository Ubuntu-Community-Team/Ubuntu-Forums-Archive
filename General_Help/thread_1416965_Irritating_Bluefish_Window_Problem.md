### Post by Steven_B on 2010-02-26
Assuming you haven't upgraded Bluefish, you might want to try deleting the .bluefish directory in your home directory.  There may be something corrupted that's causing Bluefish to crash.
Alternatively, you could just rename it (something like gone_bluefish) so you don't lose all of your old settings if that isn't the problem.
