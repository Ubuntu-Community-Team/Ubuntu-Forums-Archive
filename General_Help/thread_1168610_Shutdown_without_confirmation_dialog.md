### Post by zhhansen on 2009-05-24
First start 'systemsettings', then go to Advanced --> Session Manager, and finally deselect Confirm Logout

That should work

---

### Post by Cheesemill on 2009-05-24
Or you can right-click on the shutdown icon and go to preferences, and untick 'Show confirm dialogs for logout, restart and shutdown'.

Cheesemill
