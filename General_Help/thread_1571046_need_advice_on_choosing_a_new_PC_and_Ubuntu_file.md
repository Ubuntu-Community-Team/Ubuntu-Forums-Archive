### Post by Elfy on 2010-09-09
The AMD64 download is for all 64bit machines.

10.04 has been perfectly stable on all my machines - you can use 9.10, but bear in mind it will reach end of life in April.

Obviously you will hear more people saying that 'any' version is unstable as the people who have had few or no issues tend not to need to say anything.
