### Post by ivanovnegro on 2011-05-11
Right click on the trash icon and see the settings, change them, e.g., delete everything after 7 days or what you prefer, also you could just delete the files, not put them into the trash bin. KDE has this weird behavior to set it per default that the trash is full.
