### Post by Tibuda on 2009-07-15
The diffs between Ubuntu, Kubuntu and Xubuntu are basically the desktop environment (and its applications). Xubuntu have less hardware requirements.

Some other flavors are aimed at different users needs. The Netbook Remix is best suited for... hmm... netbooks. Edubuntu got some educational software for kids. The Christian Edition and Muslim Edition got some religion related software, like a bible/quran reader I think.
