### Post by peterkidd on 2008-10-21
Here try this. Very simple, just take whatever theme she likes in *Preferences>Appearance* then click *Customize* then *Colors*. This is what I came up with for my girlfriend.

I have the Clearlooks theme with color #FFCAF2 for *Windows-Background* and #ED829C9 for *Selected items-Background* 

The colors may be backward. If it looks off switch them.

Some themes do not let you customize the colors, especially those that are downloaded from the Gnome webpage. There is probably a workaround or something. I have no idea however. Later ya'll.
