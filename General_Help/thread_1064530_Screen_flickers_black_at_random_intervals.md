### Post by mikeytag on 2009-02-23
Hi elanning,
I have the same issue intermittently. I have had various degrees of success by messing with Compiz options like Loose Binding and Indirect Rendering. You can play around with those settings by installing fusion-icon. I think the best for me so far is having Loose Binding checked and Indirect Rendering unchecked.
Let me know if you find anything else out. BTW, I also have a dv9000 series laptop (a dv9233cl) to be particular and it has an nVidia graphics card (7600GS) I believe.
Mike
