### Post by Eric the Red on 2007-06-04
I'm planning on removing my "Ubuntu Edgy" completely and installing the newest version "Feisty", However, when formatting my HD, I would like to backup all my bookmarks from firefox. 

Where exactly are they located? Any help would be appreciated.

---

### Post by merlinus on 2007-06-04
/username/.mozilla/firefox/xxxxxxx.default/bookmarks.html

Make sure you have hidden files visible (Ctrl-H).

-merlin

### Post by ryanVickers on 2007-06-04
Yeah, that's right, and that's probably all you wanted, but there's another way:
> Bookmarks > Organize bookmarks > File > Export
And I think that lets you choose where you want it and some other stuff.
