### Post by ^^Snoop^^ on 2007-11-11
I deleted my bottom panel to add a dock but decided I didn't like the dock in the end, so added my bottom panel again.

I've added what I want to the panel but now my open windows are on the right side of my panel instead of the left like normal. How can I get my open windows to show on the left of the panel? Cheers!

---

### Post by qamelian on 2007-11-11
Just right click on the left side of the window list that you added and select move. Then drag the window list to the left.
