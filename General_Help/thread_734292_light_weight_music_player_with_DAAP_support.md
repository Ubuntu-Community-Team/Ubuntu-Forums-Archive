### Post by aidanr on 2008-03-24
According to [wikipedia]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Digital_Audio_Access_Protocol#Description") rhythmbox, banshee, exaile and amarok support daap. Not exactly lightweight apps but the eeepc is well capable of running them. Otherwise maybe consider [mpd and icecast]("http://blog.schauenburg.nl/foss/00046.html") instead if you really want to use a lightweight client.
