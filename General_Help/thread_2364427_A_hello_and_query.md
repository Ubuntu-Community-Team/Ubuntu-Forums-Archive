### Post by SeijiSensei on 2017-06-23
For the NVIDIA card, run the Settings > Software > Additional Drivers application and install the one Ubuntu recommends.  My guess is it will be nvidia-375.

For the sound card, it may be that you need to tell the sound system to use the card rather than the built-in audio jacks.  Take a look at Settings > Sound and make sure your card is selected.
