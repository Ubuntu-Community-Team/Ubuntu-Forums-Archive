### Post by ashz on 2007-04-20
Solved for some reason i could hear the internal speaker this was because Master Mono was on as default on my new feisty install...

Solution...

Double click on speaker icon (top right)

Click on Edit
Then Preferences
Check Master Mono
Mute Master Mono on Volume Control screen
Uncheck Master Mono

Problem solved internal speaker does not play any audio when im watching/listening to videos/music!!
