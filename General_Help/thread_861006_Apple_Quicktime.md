### Post by fromgi on 2008-07-18
I'm able to play most everything (exception is Real Media) with only 1 piece of software and 1 add-on. They are the VLC Media Player and the MediaPlayerConnectivity add-on.

A firefox plugin isn't necessary if you use the MediaPlayerConnectivity add-on.  VLC also lets me connect to my XM radio subscription.  I recently added RealPlayer, which I only use to watch BigBrother.

The thing I like about VLC is the ability for fast-foreword and reverse video's.  When I used Mplayer, about 50% of the video's could not do this.  I also like not having to add the codecs where VLC's are part of the download.

Lastly, VLC also gives me more stations for music than I could imagine.  They are under, View, Playlist, Manage, Service Discovery.
