### Post by Sef on 2008-07-10
1) What Ubuntu version are you running?

2) With Gutsy and Hardy, Compiz-Fusion is available from Add/Remove.  Applications > Add/Remove > Show: 'All Available Applications' or 'All Open Source Applications' > Search: ccsm > click on ccsm box > apply changes > apply > close
