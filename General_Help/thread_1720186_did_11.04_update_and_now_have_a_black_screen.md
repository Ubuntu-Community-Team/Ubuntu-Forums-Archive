### Post by timgood on 2011-04-03
Looks like you may have made an error with your driver installation. Hit CTRL+Alt+F4 and you should get a text login screen where you will be able to reset your settings to default. 

```
    sudo dpkg-reconfigure xserver-xorg
```

Reboot and you should be back to normal, but you will need to re-install your NVidia driver.

To fix it, start ubuntu failsafe and go to private drivers manager.
