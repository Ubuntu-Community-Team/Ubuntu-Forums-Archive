### Post by RobGoss on 2017-04-07
Hello and welcome, You can install **Tweak tool** from the Ubuntu repository, it will allow you to remove the icons on your desktop. I'm assuming you're referring to The Home, Trash and so on. After you have it installed it open it up and click on** desktop**, you will see the list of desktop icons simply Uncheck what you want removed and that's it
