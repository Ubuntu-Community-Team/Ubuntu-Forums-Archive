### Post by chipbuster on 2012-01-04
According to this page, read error rate is higher is better, lower is worse. Just a strange scale used to calculate the number you see.  [http://www.z-a-recovery.com/man-smart.htm](http://www.z-a-recovery.com/man-smart.htm)  According to wikipedia, SMART isn't even standardized yet, so I wouldn't worry too much about it unless you have good reason to believe your disk is getting close to failure.
