I am currently running Ubuntu 8.04, and I was wondering if there is a way for me to browse the files on my Zune. I have connected it to the computer, and the Zune begins charging, however, there is no indication of it being recognized by the operating system. Is there a way to access the files?

### Post by phidia on 2008-10-26
After you plug in your mp3 player you can open a terminal and type "dmesg" and/or "fdisk -l"

That should give you the ID the system shows for your device. You can also look at the wiki [here]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/MythExport?highlight=%28zune%29") and [here]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=790333&highlight=zune"). Unfortunately support for zune in linux is not good because of the software issues discussed in the last link I provided.
