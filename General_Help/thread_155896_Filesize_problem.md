### Post by Kevinator on 2006-04-06
I doubt your two problems are related. Your description of your first problem is not very clear, but I assume you are refering to the files as they are viewed in Nautilus. The reason you would see more information than normal there is that you have "zoomed in", and therefore see more detail. By default, in the Icon View you see file sizes at 150%. 100% is the default zoom level (List View is different, having a default zoom level of 50%).

You can also tweak what is displayed in the Preferences dialog, under the Display tab.
