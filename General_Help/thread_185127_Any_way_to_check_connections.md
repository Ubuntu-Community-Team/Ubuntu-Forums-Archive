### Post by uteck on 2006-05-31
If you had access to a machine outside your network that you could ssh into, then you could run tests that way.  I would be weary of any 'service' that offered this as they could easily monitor you and get passowrds and such.  I use a freinds ftp server were I have an account and test my network from there.

You could try visiting the shieldsup site at grc.com but that just tests open ports, not if the service is realy working.
