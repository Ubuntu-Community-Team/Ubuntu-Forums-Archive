### Post by bestiarosa on 2007-01-21
If you like something lighter which does the job nicely from Nautilus, download nautilus-script-audio-convert.
Once installed, copy it from  /usr/share/nautilus-scripts/ConvertAudioFile to ~/.gnome2/nautilus-scripts/
To convert, left-click on the audiofile, Scripts>ConvertAudioFile.
