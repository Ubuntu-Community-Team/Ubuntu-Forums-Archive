### Post by mixmaster on 2009-06-05
Live CDs or USB keys present a hazard to any machine to which a miscreant can gain physical access.  Setting power-on & bios access passwords help, particularly if your default boot list is just the hard disk.  Given time and knowledge, however, all these things can be bypassed and to protect yourself further you are into high-grade encryption of the hard disk.
