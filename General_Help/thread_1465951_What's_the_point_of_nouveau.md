### Post by solar george on 2010-04-30
Use the proprietory nvidia driver if you need 3d graphics or desktop effects, otherwise the nouveau driver will probably give you better suspend/hibernate support and a nicer splash screen.
Also the nouvea driver is open-source and can be fixed/updated by the devs if there's a problem as they can see the code.
