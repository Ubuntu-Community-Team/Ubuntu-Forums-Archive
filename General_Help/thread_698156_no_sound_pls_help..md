### Post by bbarnaby on 2008-02-16
You might try making sure AlsaMixer is installed, and try opening it. When I first installed Ubuntu I had no sound because the OS thought my mic was the pc speaker. Open a terminal and type in "alsamixer" without the quotes and it will open a GUI for you, and try turning everything up. Hope this helps.
