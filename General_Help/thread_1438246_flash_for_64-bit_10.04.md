### Post by drs305 on 2010-03-24
Edit:  Oops, the first part is the beta but not 64-bit. The link in the next post is for the 64-bit, which is still alpha. The install procedure is the same.

Install:  Download the tar.gz file, extract libflashplayer.so and copy it to /usr/lib/mozilla/plugins/ , making sure no other flash packages are installed. That's all I had to do to get Flash to work.

32-Bit:
I don't know if there is now an easier way, but I still go to the Adobe Flash Beta site. It's up to 10.1-beta3. 
[http://labs.adobe.com/downloads/flashplayer10.html]("http://labs.adobe.com/downloads/flashplayer10.html")

64:
See post 3 for address.
