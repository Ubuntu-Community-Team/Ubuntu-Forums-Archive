### Post by ajgreeny on 2015-08-25
Slides of what? Photograph slides, pages of a LibreOffice Presentation, other text pages?

If these are images of some sort you may have some luck with **photoprint** from the repos which allows you to put images on a single page for printing, or Photo layouts editor, which is also in the repos.  The second one is a KDE application and I find it more complicated to use and photoprint is much simpler, but it depends what you want to do and what the items are that you want to print on the one page.
