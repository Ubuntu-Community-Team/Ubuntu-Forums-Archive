### Post by taurus on 2007-05-16
Are you talking about the /dev/hda1, /dev/hda2, & /dev/hda3?  You just need to change the ownership of the mountpoints from root to your login name.

```
sudo chown -R **username**:**username** /media/hda1
sudo chown -R **username**:**username** /media/hda2
sudo chown -R** username**:**username** /media/hda3
ls -la /media
```
Replace **username** with your actual login name.
