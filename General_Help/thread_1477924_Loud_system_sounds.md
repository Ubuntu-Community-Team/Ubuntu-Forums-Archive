### Post by Zorael on 2010-05-09
Those are notifications, so head to System Settings -> Notifications.

Under the System Notifications section you can change or disable notifications (including sounds) for specific events (login, logout, etc), as well as change the overall volume they'll play at on the Player Settings tab. Note that you can scroll the Event source dropdown list. Login/logout sounds should be under KDE System Notifications.

I always disable login and logout sounds. I don't overall like the Oxygen sound theme much. To boot, the logout sound actually delays the logout process.
