### Post by Tomatz on 2008-05-21
Open **applications** (top left of screen)

**Accessories**

Click **Terminal**

In the terminal window type (or copy and paste) the following:

```
sudo apt-get install vlc
```

Enter your **password** (no text will appear during typing)

Once finished

Open **applications** (top left)

**sound and video**

Then click **vlc**

**Put your dvd in the tray**

Click file (top left of the **vlc** window)

**Open disc**

Then click **ok**



Should be working now ;)
