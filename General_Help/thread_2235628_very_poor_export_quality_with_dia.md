### Post by Impavidus on 2014-07-22
So between some letter pairs the horizontal displacement is missing, causing characters to overlap. That must be a problem with the font rendering, but I can't help you with that. The tiny bit that I can help you with is saying that in general converting line drawings/diagrams to jpeg is a bad idea. Jpeg gives severe compression artefacts when dealing with sharp edges. Png handles it nicely.
