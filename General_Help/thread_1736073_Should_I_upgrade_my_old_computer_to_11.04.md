### Post by Mark Phelps on 2011-04-22
I know of no reason why 11.04 should perform any faster on your old computer than 10.10.  New Ubuntu version add features, not performance gains.

Also, understand there is no roll-back capability in Ubuntu.  If you upgrade to 11.04 and things don't work (and being still in BETA, that's likely to be the case), there is no simple way to get your working 10.10 version back.  You're stuck having to fix 11.04, or wiping what you have and completely reinstalling 10.10 from scratch.

What I would suggest is that you back off your old computer to an external drive using Clonezilla. That way, if there are serious problems with your computer and 11.04, you can restore your previous 10.10 version.
