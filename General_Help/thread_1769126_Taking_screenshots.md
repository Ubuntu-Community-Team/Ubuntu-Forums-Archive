### Post by IWantFroyo on 2011-05-27
Usually, laptops have a PRTSC key or a PRTSC (FN) key. Hit it (or hit it while pressing FN).

If you don't have a key like this, just use the Take Screenshot application. Set it to take a picture a few seconds after the window closes, because it might accidentally take a screenshot of itself.
