### Post by Bucky Ball on 2012-09-23
Here's a tip: You can make named place markers (bookmarks) and then go to them by right clicking the page numbers at the bottom left of the screen. 

Simply put the cursor in the spot you want a bookmark (placemark, marker) then Insert>Bookmark, add a name, ok. Right click the numbers **/** in the bottom left corner and there it is. Click and you're there. ;)

Yea, the auto line is a pain and was in OpenOffice, too. You can probably switch it off but a nuisance, not a game changer.


PS: One problem I've had with this is it seems to auto-create bookmarks where you delete a paragraph or do a delete, like saving a heap of undos specifically for deletions only. This rarely useful but annoying. I haven't analysed the pattern but will if it gets annoying enough.
