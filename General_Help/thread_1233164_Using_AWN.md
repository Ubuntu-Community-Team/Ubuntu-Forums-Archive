### Post by prshah on 2009-08-06
> **franar said:**

1. the AWN is located over the botton bar, and i can not find a place where to move it up, to be over the botton bar creates conflicts between itself and the aplications open, when i click on them.

AWN cannot be relocated to any other screen edge except the bottom. Usually, those who are using AWN remove the bottom bar altogether. To remove the bottom bar, right click the bar and choose delete panel.

Ensure that you have added the Window List applet to your AWN bar, so that it will take over the functions of the bottom bar for you.
