### Post by coffeecat on 2016-01-05
> **shane_faulkinbury2 said:**
> Ctrl +N just closes the open window and opens a new one!

No, it doesn&#8217;t. It opens a new tab in the same window so that you don't lose your original document. I think this is what you missed when you said *"I have one Text Editor window open and when I open another the one open closes and doesn't come back after I close the new one!"* in your first post.

As far as right-clicking is concerned, have another read of howefield's post. Right-click on the gedit icon **in the launcher**.
