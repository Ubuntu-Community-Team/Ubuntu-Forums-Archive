### Post by doug1212 on 2008-11-18
Zoom in on the drawing and use info > distance to tell you that the line is 10. inch, the size you see on the screen isn't the actual size. the units don't really matter the line could be 10. miles long, as long as long as the units are always the same throughout the drawing.
