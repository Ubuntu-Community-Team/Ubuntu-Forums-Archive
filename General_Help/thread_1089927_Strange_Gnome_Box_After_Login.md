### Post by thtrgremlin on 2009-03-07
It is a loading screen. It is just telling you that stuff is still starting up. The computer can be quite slow until it finishes. The computer does not start up faster without it.

This looks like a good guide for changing it, or turning it off: [http://ubuntuforums.org/archive/index.php/t-11478.html](http://ubuntuforums.org/archive/index.php/t-11478.html)

Just did a google search for: gnome splash screen

### Post by landypro on 2009-03-07
All fixed, I installed gnome splash screen manager, and unticked 'Display Splash Screen'
