### Post by ju2wheels on 2009-01-19
1. It looks like theres some issue with your graphics driver not loading, this is what causes the panels to be that ugly grey color.

2. It also looks like the resolution changed from big to small or vice versa causing your taskbar icons to shift. You can right click on most of them and deselect "Lock to Panel" and then right click again and click move to move them back.
