### Post by hawthornso23 on 2010-09-20
It controls the threshhold mouse speed at which mouse accelleration cuts in. 

Sensitivity = low  => mouse acceleration cuts in at a low   speed
Sensitivity = high => mouse acceleration cuts in at a high speed

you won't be able to tell the difference easily if you have mouse acceleration set to a low value. To observe the effect of changing sensitivity set mouse acceleration all the way to the right and then experiment.
