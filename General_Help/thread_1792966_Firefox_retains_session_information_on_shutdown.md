### Post by John-B on 2011-06-29
I ran into this once with Firefox and fixed it by doing this:
Go into Edit/Preferences/General and choose anything other then blank page.  Then exit Firefox.  Then go back and do it all again but choose Blank Page and hopefully that will reset it.
