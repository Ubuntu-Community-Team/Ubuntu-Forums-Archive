### Post by lightstream on 2011-05-29
Nautilus (the normal Ubuntu file manager) can do FTP. Press Ctrl-L to Open Location, and type [ftp://ftp.myftphost.com](ftp://ftp.myftphost.com)

It's not always as smooth as it could be though.

My own preference is the FireFTP extension for Firefox. I particularly like its 'Keep directories in sync while navigating' option, which is really useful when you have a local mirror of your remote folder structure.
