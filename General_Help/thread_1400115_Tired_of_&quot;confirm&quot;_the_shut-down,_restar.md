[INDENT]found at:  [http://forumubuntusoftware.info/viewtopic.php?f=65&t=4051#p44300](http://forumubuntusoftware.info/viewtopic.php?f=65&t=4051#p44300)

Are you tired of having to confirm when you have to close, restart your computer etc. in Ultimate Edition 2.4 & Ubuntu 9.10 you removed it the following way:


    gconf-editor


Then, select "apps > indicator-session" and put check mark by "suppress_logout_restart_shutdown" and the annoying little confirmation window is gone ;)[/INDENT]
