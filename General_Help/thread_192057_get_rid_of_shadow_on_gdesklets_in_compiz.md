### Post by th3t1nm4n on 2006-07-06
Press alt+f2, type "gconf-editor" in the run box, then click apps/compiz/trailfocus/screen0/options and set the minimum_window_brightness_level to 100, this annoyed me too and took a while to figure out, have fun with all the cool XGL/Compiz features!
