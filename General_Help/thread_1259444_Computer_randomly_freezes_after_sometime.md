### Post by jnorthr on 2009-09-06
Is this a heat related issue ? Can you use your system with another o/s, like windose ? Perhaps it might help to run some diagnostics on your hardware to confirm it's ok first. Do you have unusual devices attached to your system, like camera chips, webcams, old printers, zip drives, etc. ?

If you boot your system and just leave it running for an hour without using it, does it still appear to hang when you try after an hour ? If so more likely a hardware/heat issue rather than o/s or application related.
