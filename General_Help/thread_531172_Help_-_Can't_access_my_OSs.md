### Post by eggdeng on 2007-08-21
Sounds like you have either a damaged MBR or else a hardware problem. Assuming the first scenario, you will need to restore GRUB (the linux bootloader). There are lots of threads on this forum, for example [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=224351]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=224351")
