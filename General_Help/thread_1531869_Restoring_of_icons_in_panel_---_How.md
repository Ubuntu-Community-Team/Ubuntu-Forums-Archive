I have Ubuntu 10.04 running on two different PCs. On one of them I have a volume control icon and evolution (email) icon in the top panel (placed there by default during installation).

### Post by WorMzy on 2010-07-15
When you right click on empty space on a panel, you get the option of "Add to panel", click that, search for "indicator applet", once you've found it, click and drag it onto the panel where you want it. :)
