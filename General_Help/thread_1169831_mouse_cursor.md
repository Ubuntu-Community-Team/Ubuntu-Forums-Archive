### Post by aesis05401 on 2009-05-25
1. Navigate to System/Preferences/Appearance
2. With 'Theme' tab open, and your theme package highlighted, click 'Customize'
3. Click 'Pointer'
4. Your mouse themes should be listed.

I just installed the oxygen-cursor theme to check, and it seems to work as expected.
