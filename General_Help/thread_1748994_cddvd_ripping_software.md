### Post by satanselbow on 2011-05-04
Dude - you might get a better response by:

1) Re-Title your thread - DVD Ripping (ie a retail movie) is a very specific subject covered very comprehensively here and all over the net ;)

"How can I print pictures from slideshow DVD"

2) Add a lot more detail about the dvd disc in question - is it a slideshow DVD at all? What files are on the disc - you may find there is a "pics" or "images" folder there already that you can copy into your home folder and print through and image viewer or manager such as Shotwell

3) Bumping threads just makes you look rude and does little to get you more attention ;)

From the very vague info you give I would suggest firstly exploring the disc in nautilus and seeing if you have a folder of image files you can preview and print - simple or if it is a true "DVD" and has a "VIDEO_TS" folder containing "VOB" files (among others) install VLC through synaptic / software centre and make use of the "Save Image" function by playing and pausing the movie on the pictures you are after ;)

I really would suggest changing the title though as "DVD Ripping" is not really what you need to do :popcorn:
