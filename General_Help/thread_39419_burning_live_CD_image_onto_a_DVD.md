you can burn ubuntu 6.06 LTS on dvd, it's easy as burning normal dvd.

here is the steps you follow & get ubuntu live cd on dvd

you need nero 7 for this nothing else, let's start

1.insert live cd in drive & open nero 7

2.choose copy entire cd option & make image of live cd on hdd

3.now open nero 7 if not opened, then choose burn image to disk option here is the magic that i found.

4. it give you to choose disk type for burning choose dvd here

5. insert blank dvd or dvd-rw for burning

it's done.
