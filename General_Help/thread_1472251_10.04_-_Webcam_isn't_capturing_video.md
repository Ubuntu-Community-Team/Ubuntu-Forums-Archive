### Post by Leppie on 2010-05-04
there's really quite a lot of threads about video issues.
a solution could be launching an app using the ld_preload variable:
```
LD_PRELOAD=/usr/lib32/libv4l/v4l1compat.so  skype
```
this works for me on Lucid with my notebook deluxe.
