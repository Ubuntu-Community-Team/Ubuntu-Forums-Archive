### Post by lomillialor on 2011-07-10
For any fellow beginners, I have a Netgear WNDR3700 router. I have a blueray and a PC running Vista  connected wirelessly to it, and now have a Netgear GS105 connected to it via about 50' of CAT5. GS105 has a desktop and two laptops hardwired through it all running Ubuntu 11.04. GS105 was a simple plug and play, and is much faster than the wireless connections I was using.
