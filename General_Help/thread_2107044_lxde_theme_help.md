### Post by mhaggard on 2013-01-21
Nevermind, I found a new way and I'll share:

right click the desktop and click desktop preferences.

go to the advanced tab and check the box that says: "show menus provided by window managers when desktop is clicked"

click close and right click the desktop again (this should give you a different menu)

choose window management settings and in the appearance tab you can rearrange the order of the buttons.
