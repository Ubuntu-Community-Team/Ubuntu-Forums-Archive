### Post by TheExplorer on 2007-11-04
Well, sooner or later you will need a working optical device because the only way to make a 'bootable live usb' is from an already running system, whether a live cd or from your hd. The howtos are [here]("http://edoceo.com/liber/ubuntu-live-usb") and/or [here]("http://www.debuntu.org/how-to-install-ubuntu-linux-on-usb-bar").
