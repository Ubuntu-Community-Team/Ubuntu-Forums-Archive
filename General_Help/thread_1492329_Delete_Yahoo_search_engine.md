### Post by cmcanulty on 2010-05-24
After lots of frustration with resetting google as default  keyword (or location bar) search engine and still having it go to yahoo I fixed it and tell the method here for others. I also deleted the creative commons search engine. Navigate (I used sudo but maybe not necessary to /usr/lib/firefox-addons/searchpluggins/en-GB and/en-US and delete the yahoo line. It works so far for me. However I am no expert so do at your own risk. The about:config keyword.url never did it for me.Hurrah! I hate Yahoo but hate more that it wouldn't let you change it as my default keyword.url is google but still went to Yahoo.
