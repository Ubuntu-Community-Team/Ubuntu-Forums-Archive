### Post by pastalavista on 2008-11-03
Hit alt-f2 to open a GUI command interface and type 'nautilus'. If it opens correctly, right-click a folder and choose 'file browser' or if that isn't a selection, click 'open with' and then select Nautilus. If it still doesn't work after that, you may have to boot in recovery mode and 'repair broken packages'... if none of this works you may just have to re-install Nautilus or possibly even gnome-desktop or ubuntu-desktop
