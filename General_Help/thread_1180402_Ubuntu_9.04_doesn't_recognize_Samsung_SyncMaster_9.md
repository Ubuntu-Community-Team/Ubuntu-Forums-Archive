### Post by systimatiktool on 2011-07-08
I have a Samsung SyncMaster 997DF. It works perfectly with Linux. I run Kubuntu 10.1. The only issue I have is it goes in to "sleep" mode every 15 minutes, even when I'm watching a movie. It's aggravating to say the least. I don't know why you were told this by Samsung "service", but I can assure you I'm not typing this on a blank screen. I never even had to update any drivers manually. Once Linux booted up, everything worked fine. The only advice I can give you is to install a newer version of Linux, or try to update your monitor/video drivers. Just don't give up, as there is no compatibility problems inherent between the two companies.
