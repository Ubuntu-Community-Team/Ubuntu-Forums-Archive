### Post by GandalfTheNerd on 2008-02-09
I have two E-mail accounts, one conventional POP with my ISP, the other is hotmail via "hotway".  Both have worked well for months.  Yesterday, however, both stopped working.  Evolution kept asking me for the passwords.

I solved this more or less by accident.  After checking (but not changing) the account settings on one of the accounts, it started to work.  Repeating the procedure for the other account made that work, too.  You can find the account settings under Edit/Preferences, select "Mail Accounts" then "Edit" for each account in turn.

I am running Ubuntu Gutsy, kernel 2.6.22-14-generic.  Evolution version is 2.12.1.  All current patches applied.

I hope this is of use to someone!
