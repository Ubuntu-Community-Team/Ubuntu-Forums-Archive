### Post by ~LoKe on 2007-01-03
Where are you trying to import them from?  If you've got it on paper, scan it.  If you've got it on a digital camera or something, plug it in and transfer.  If it's already on your computer, right click the desktop -> Change Desktop Background.  "Add Wallpaper" and find it.
