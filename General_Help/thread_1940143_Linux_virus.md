### Post by winh8r on 2012-03-13
Best suggestion would be change all passwords and open a new email account, with another provider.

Another useful tip is always have at least two email accounts, one for personal email between "trusted" contacts, and one for registrations,logins,general info and other less important things.

Use addons for firefox like adblock, ghostery and noscript too, this will tighten up what is able to get access to the browser.

And the all important , visual check of the address bar, before you type in your login details,even if the page looks like your normal login page, always check the address of the page.

If using wireless connection , ensure that connection is secured and not wide open to potential unwanted guests.

Hope this helps.
