AWN seemed quirky to me so I switched to Gnome-do with preferences=>Appearance=>Selected Theme to Docky. Which gives you a dock bar, limited but decent controls. Just drag icons to and from your bar. I'm currently using Gnome-Do version 0.8.2 . I think I used Ubuntu Tweak to install it.
