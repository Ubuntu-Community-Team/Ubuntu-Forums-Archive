### Post by stoat09 on 2010-12-04
The panel that the OP describes is the default upper panel. To show window tabs in this panel you need to add the "Window List" applet. To do this, right click on the panel and select "Add to Panel". Then in the window that opens, scroll down to "Window List", highlight it, and then click the "add" button. You should then see a small, double row of vertical dots which can be slid along the panel by right-clicking on it and selecting "move". Have a fiddle with it and you'll soon figure out how it works.
   If you wish to add another panel, right-click an empty space on the one you already have and select "new panel". Then, if you right-click on the new panel and experiment with the "Properties" and "Add to Panel" options, you should be able to get yourself sorted.
   Chris.
