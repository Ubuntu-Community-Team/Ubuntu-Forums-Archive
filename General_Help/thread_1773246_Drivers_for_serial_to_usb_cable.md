There are a few of these things out there and it can be hard to find the right one. Those that do not work in linux are totally software driven and are a waste of time (IMHO). There is some good information on the Mythtv wiki about serial 2 usb converters and gives some chip ids to look for when buying one. I have 2 running my mythtv box ---> Directv boxes for changing channels. Dont remember what kind they are though. Im not totally sure but one chip id is the PL5202 or similar to that. But Im going from memory.

Here is one that works fine in linux:
