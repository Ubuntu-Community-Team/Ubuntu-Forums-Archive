### Post by blueridgedog on 2011-04-17
It appears so.  You can log in to the classic desktop by selecting that option via the drop down box when you give your user account and password, and disable global menus if you wish (uninstall indicator-appmenu via synaptic).  Unit is part of compiz that runs on top of gnome.
