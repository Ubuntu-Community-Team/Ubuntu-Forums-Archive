### Post by dalee on 2012-01-14
Hi,

I'm using Chromium 15.0.874.106. To get rid of the extra bar with max/min/close buttons, go to Preferences - Personal Stuff. First I clicked 'Hide System Title Bar and use compact Borders'. That made the extra bar go away. Then I reset the tick box to 'Use System Title bar and Borders'. The extra border has stayed gone and I just have the standard Unity pop-up bar.

Perhaps this will help someone I hope.
