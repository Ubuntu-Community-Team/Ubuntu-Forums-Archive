### Post by Melk79 on 2009-06-30
I don't have my ubuntu in front of me now, but I think you need to activate and/or turn up your microphone.  Right click on the Speaker in the panel bar and click on Volume Controls.  Under one of the tabs or the menus there you can select which audio sources are available and then move their volume sliders up.  I'm guessing one of the ones you need is not turned on or not turned up enough.  Hope that helps.
