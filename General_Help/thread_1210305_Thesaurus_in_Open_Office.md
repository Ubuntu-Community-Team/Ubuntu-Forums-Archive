### Post by Anxious Nut on 2009-07-13
Hey buddy, If you wanna add it to your tool bar or add it to your menus, then follow these simple instructions:

**To add it to the toolbar:**
-Open your Word Processor --> Tools --> Customize
-Click on the "Toolbars" tab, Leave the selected toolbar as it is (default).
-lower your mouse a bit, you'll see "Toolbar Content" Click on the "Add" button that's next to it. A new window will open.
-From that window, in the "Category" field select "Options", move to the other field (commands) and select thesaurus.
-Click the "Add" button
-Close all windows and you'll see it next to the Open Icon.

**To add it to the menu:**
-In the "Menus" field change the selection "File" into "Tools" 
-lower your mouse a bit, you'll see "Toolbar Content" Click on the "Add" button that's next to it. A new window will open.
-From that window, in the "Category" field select "Options", move to the other field (commands) and select thesaurus.
-Click the "Add" button
-Close all windows and you'll see it next to the Open Icon.

;)
