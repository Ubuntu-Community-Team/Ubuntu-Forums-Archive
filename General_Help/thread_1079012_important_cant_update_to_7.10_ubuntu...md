### Post by Partyboi2 on 2009-02-24
Easiest way would be to download a hardy or intrepid iso and backup your important stuff and do a clean install.
[http://releases.ubuntu.com/intrepid/](http://releases.ubuntu.com/intrepid/)
[http://releases.ubuntu.com/hardy/](http://releases.ubuntu.com/hardy/)
The check the md5sum and burn it to disk.
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BurningIsoHowto](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BurningIsoHowto)
Then set cdrom to boot first in your bios and boot the ubuntu live cd and install.
