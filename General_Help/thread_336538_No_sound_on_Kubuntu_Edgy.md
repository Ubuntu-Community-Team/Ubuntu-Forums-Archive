### Post by Dave54 on 2007-01-19
Since no one's helping, I'll offer what little experience I have.

I don't know kde, but if you were running gnome I'd suggest the following.
1) Open the sound mixer
2) Under preferences, enable everything
3) Put every bar to max
4) Under File > change device, select the next device
5) Repeat steps 2 trough 4 as necessary. Note that when you select a new device, the preferences change.
6) Under each device, check switches to make sure your amps aren't disabled or something.

That's all I got.
Good luck.
