### Post by dvn1129 on 2010-02-08
Yes, there is. log in to his account. Go up to places in the gnome menu. click on Home. Then click on the arrow pointing up and open the folder with your account name on it.. Now go to where ever the files you wish to share are located. (probably in Desktop) and right click and select copy.. then paste them wherever you want them on to his desktop. from here you can jsut drag them off his desktop into wherever you want them to go such as in pictures, music etc.
