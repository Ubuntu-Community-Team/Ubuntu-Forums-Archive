### Post by hansdown on 2011-12-29
Hi,tears of the river.

Let's rescue your data first.

Open a terminal(ctrl+alt+t).

Enter

```
gksudo nautilus
```

Hit enter, input your password, hit enter.

You should be able to drag and drop your files to desktop, another usb, etc.

Test your files, to make sure you have read and write permissions. 

If yes, then reformat the usb.
