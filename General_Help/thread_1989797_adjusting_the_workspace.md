### Post by ubun_two on 2012-05-28
I am assuming that you are using Unity.

I don't know if you can change the behavior of it, but if you do the right mouse click on the Firefox icon on the Unity launcher, you have an option to open a new window.  Selecting that will open a new window in your current workspace.
