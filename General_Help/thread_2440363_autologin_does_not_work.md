### Post by CelticWarrior on 2020-04-09
Autologin is unrelated to the other issues. Problems with input devices have other causes, typical incorrect graphical driver or others.

To be clear: **Autologin shouldn't be used **and regardless of other considerations, it's annoyingly impractical. The system will ask for a password to unlock several other things anyway. So, it's much better to adhere to the best (and standard) practice of login in with a password.
