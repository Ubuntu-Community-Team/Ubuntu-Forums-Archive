Ive spent a long time training Thunderbirds junk mail filter, is it possible to copy that intelligence to another machine so I dont have to train that one too?

---

### Post by Brucevdk on 2008-04-16
[The file that stores your custom training data for the Junk Mail Controls is called "training.dat". It is stored in your profile folder.]("http://kb.mozillazine.org/Junk_Mail_Controls#Other_information")

See *~/.mozilla-thunderbird/<profile>/training.dat*
