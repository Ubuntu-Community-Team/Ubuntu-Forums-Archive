### Post by foofi22 on 2007-12-19
Problem sorted now.  Very very simple really!

I had multiple tabs open at some point and in changing my home page I clicked "Use Current Page" this proceeded to save ALL open tabs as my home page, hence they kept opening.  Thanks for everyone's help.
