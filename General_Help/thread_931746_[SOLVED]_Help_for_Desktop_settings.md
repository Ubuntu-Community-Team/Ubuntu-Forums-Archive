Note: there is information & help on the task bar, right click the task bar and click "help". It has good info on how to do the most common things with the task bars.

If you removed items altogether and want them back... right click the task bar and choose "add to panel" find the program that you want, highlight it, and click "add". If the items are there and are just not where you want them...

to move items around the task bar:
right click on the item and...
1. clear checkbox (if checked) "lock to panel" then-
2. click move... now move the items to where you want them by just moving the pointer, then click to place it. Then right click and check "lock to panel".

If that doesn't work, you can always try...
