### Post by Mark Phelps on 2010-03-28
If you're going to use Wine, you should first check the WineHQ App Compatibility site for the ratings for the apps/games you want to run.

Any rating less than Gold is essentially a waste of time with Wine.

On the linked page, enter Prince of Persia in the name contains box and click the Update Filter button.  You'll get a list of ratings for the Prince of Persia games -- and they're NOT encouraging:

[http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?bIsQueue=false&bIsRejected=false&sClass=application&sTitle=Browse+Applications&iItemsPerPage=25&iPage=1&sOrderBy=appName&bAscending=true]("http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?bIsQueue=false&bIsRejected=false&sClass=application&sTitle=Browse+Applications&iItemsPerPage=25&iPage=1&sOrderBy=appName&bAscending=true")
