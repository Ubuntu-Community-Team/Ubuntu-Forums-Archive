### Post by txwooley on 2011-09-28
Are you sure the display isn't going to sleep via the Power Manager?  Go to System->Preferences->Power Management.  There is a line to "Put display to sleep when inactive for:".  If you have a battery back-up there might even be two tabs.  One for AC power and another for Battery Power.  Make sure both of those are set to a nice long amount of time ('never' is even an option).
