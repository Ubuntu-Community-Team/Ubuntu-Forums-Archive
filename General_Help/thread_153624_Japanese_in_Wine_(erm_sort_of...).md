### Post by Trisa on 2006-04-01
Try this: Go to the terminal and type in "LC_ALL=ja_JP wine" and then the path to the program. This should get the Japanese words to display properly. When I play games such as AIR or Planetarian, I use this setting to get Japanese words to work. Try that and see if it solves your problem.
