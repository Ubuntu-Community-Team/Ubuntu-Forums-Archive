### Post by baskak on 2010-06-01
it seems ubuntu chooses wrong driver by default. install foomatic-db-gutenprint via Ubuntu Software Centre. go to System->Administration->printing. right click the printer, choose properties and then with "Make and model" press change. choose Foomatic/gutenprint-ijs.5.2. overwrite the setup, when asked. done. it also gives more appropriate options. thanx go to SmokeyD at #ubuntu channel.
