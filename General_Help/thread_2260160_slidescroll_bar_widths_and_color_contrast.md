### Post by bab1 on 2015-01-09
Install Unity Tweak and lool in the System section for "Scrolling".  When you open the Scrolling section you can click on the Scroll Bars radio button for **_Legacy_**.  This will turn off the horrible skinny scroll bars overlay.  I do this on all my desktops.  In fact I just did that to the lapop I a using right now.  It is Ubuntu 14.04 Unity so I know it works.
