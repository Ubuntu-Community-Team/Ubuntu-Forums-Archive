Hi, I just have a simple question. The shutdown menu thingy in the top right of the screen, on the toolbar is suddenly missing when I logged in today. How do I get it back?

---

### Post by zhhansen on 2009-05-23
This is an easy fix. Right click the top panel (preferably where you want the 'shutdown menu thingy') click add to panel and search for user switcher. Then click add, and it should appear fully functional.
