### Post by Cpierce on 2011-04-10
Highlight the first song, then hit edit>select all, then put your cursor over the highlighted songs, right click and choose either "delete from drive" or "remove from playlist". Remove from playlist just removes it from Banshee. Delete from drive completely deletes the song from Banshee and your computer. 

Hope that helps.
