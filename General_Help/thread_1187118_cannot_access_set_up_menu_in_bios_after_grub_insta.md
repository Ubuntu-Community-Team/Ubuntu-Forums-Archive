### Post by VMC on 2009-06-14
I had a Compaq computer that had a hidden partition that was used to enter BIOS settings. When I accidently deleted that partition I could use a floppy to re-install the partition. Check and see if you have either floppy or Cd disk that can access your BIOS settings.
