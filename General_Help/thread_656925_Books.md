### Post by SteveHillier on 2008-01-03
I have Ubuntu unleashed - SAMS, Ubuntu Linux Bible - Wiley, Beginning Ubuntu - Apress, and a host of other non Ubuntu Linux books.
They are out of date because it probably takes longer to write the book than to produce a new release of the OS.
Most books give you a clue as to what to do but a lot of them are based on other texts.  I forget which of these books it is but there are whole chapters in it that are identical with the RedHat equivalent.  I whinged at the publishers - not that it did much good.
A lot of what you need will be in this forum but not in one place.  Please when you have done something successfully remember to share it with others.
Good luck
