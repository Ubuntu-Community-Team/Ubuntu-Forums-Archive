### Post by 3rdalbum on 2013-06-10
I don't know why your son created those extra icons on your Launcher. This is not a virus.

If you are using Unity, right click the extra icons and choose "Unlock from Launcher" to remove them.

If you are using Gnome Fallback like the person who posted the original message in this thread, I think you need to hold down Alt and the Windows key, then right click on the icon and click Delete From Panel.
