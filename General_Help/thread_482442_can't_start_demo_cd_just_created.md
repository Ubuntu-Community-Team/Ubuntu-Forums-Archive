### Post by logos34 on 2007-06-23
at startup you need to hit the appropriate F- key for your computer to get into the bios--every manufacturer is diff.  Most common: F2, F10, F12, Esc, Delete,.  here I have a link with a list by pc make--someone posted on  another thread:

ALR PC/PCI - F2
ALR PC/non-PCI - CTRL+ALT+ESC
AMD - F1
AMI - DEL
AWARD - CTRL+ALT+ESC
AWARD - DEL
DTK - ESC
PHOENIX - CTRL+ALT+ESC
PHOENIX - CTRL+ALT+S
PHOENIX - CTRL+ALT+INS
ACER - F1,F2,CTRL+ALT+ESC
AST - CTRL+ALT+ESC,CTRL+ALT+DEL
COMPAQ - F10
COMPUSA - DEL
CYBERMAX - ESC
DELL 400 - F3
DELL 400 - F1
DELL DIMENSION - F2 or DEL
DELL INSPIRON - F2
DELL LATITUDE - Fn+F1 (while booted)
DELL LATITUDE - F2 (on boot)
DELL OPTIPLEX - DEL
DELL OPTIPLEX - F2
DELL PRECISION - F2
EMACHINE - DEL
GATEWAY 2000 1440 - F1
GATEWAY 2000 Solo - F2
HP - F1,F2
IBM - F1
IBM EPRO laptop - F2
IBM PS/2 - CTRL+ALT+INS after CTRL+ALT+DEL
IBM THINKPAD - (Windows) Programs-Thinkpad CFG
INTEL TANGENT - DEL
MICRON - F1,F2 or DEL
PACKARD BELL - F1,F2,DEL
SONY VIAO - F2
SONY VIAO - F3
TIGER - DEL
TOSHIBA 335 CDS - ESC
TOSHIBA PROTEGE - ESC
TOSHIBA SATELLITE 205 CDS - F1
TOSHIBA TECRA - F1 or ESC
