### Post by tgalati4 on 2014-09-22
Dragon Naturally Speaking works OK in WINE.  There are some projects that use Google Voice recognition API.  You will have to search for them and see what's new.  There's a website to play with:  [https://dictation.io/](https://dictation.io/)

In google chrome you can use voice-to-text in most google products like gmail dictation, google docs/drive, etc.  There may be plug-ins for firefox.

What kinds of dictation are you interested in?  Medical transcription?  Legal transcription?  Lazy email? Accessibility for all text input?

Google Voice is not reviewed in this [article]("http://www.pcworld.com/article/2055599/control-your-pc-with-these-5-speech-recognition-programs.html"), but it does work well.

I agree with ajgreeny that typing is quicker--especially if you can touch type.  You spend a lot of time fixing errors with dication software.  If you can't type, then it might be OK.

Time yourself with both methods and let us know which is quicker.
