### Post by Baji P. on 2010-09-10
Simple Scan by [Robert Ancell]("https://launchpad.net/%7Erobert-ancell") is an elegant scanning program. It will scan multiple pages and keep them in scan order, you can then individually rotate pages whichever way you want.

Finally you can print all of the accumulated pages to a single PDF in one step, I was quite pleased with the way it handled my HP PSC 750.

Thanks [Robert Ancell]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/RobertAncell") from Canonical :-)
