### Post by FBCrack on 2008-07-05
I had the same problem and it was really annoying.

Luckily there is a simple solution (I found it [here]("http://darksun.com.pt/mirage/irssi/"))

```

Q: I can't select/copy/paste text!
A: Hold shift to bypass the xterm mouse tracking.

```

So there you have... it's as easy as holding shift while marking text with the mouse :)

Regards,

FBCrack.
