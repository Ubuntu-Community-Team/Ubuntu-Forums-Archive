### Post by TheFu on 2020-12-10
During the upgrade process, all 3rd party apps, programs, drivers should be removed.  The admin needs to reinstall any that are desired under the new OS.

This is expected.  It minimizes upgrade failures, which is much worse than just having some missing programs or drivers.
