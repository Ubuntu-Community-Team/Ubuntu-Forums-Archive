### Post by haqking on 2011-09-20
> **donpeter06 said:**
> I want it to search facebook.

Well if you have a phone that you sync to facebook and gmail such as a device running android then the same contacts will appear everywhere, except only FB friends will appear on FB of course, but my phone syncs and so i have the same contacts in my phone as i do on gmail, which is also available in Evolution.

I know longer use FB however due to this reason as i didnt want my phone numbers being on the FB servers (even though not public) but thats not the point.

However i dont think evolution will sync to facebook on its own, but like i say if your phone syncs to facebook and hence to its own contacts then that will sync to gmail and then back to evolution if you know what i mean.

In short i have the same contacts in gmail as i do in evolution as i do on my phone, which happens to be the FB contacts also cos i used to sync them, now i dont however.
