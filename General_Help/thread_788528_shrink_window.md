### Post by DaymItzJack on 2008-05-10
You _can_ use ctrl+alt+mouse1 to move the cube from anywhere.

Other ideas are that, I would suggest getting a dock that leaves space on the left or right side or just setting your panel to not expand by right clicking on the panel and unchecking the "expand" button.
