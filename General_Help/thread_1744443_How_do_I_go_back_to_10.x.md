### Post by Hedgehog1 on 2011-04-30
There is no downgrade option.

You have to either:

* Reinstall 10.10 and get everything to work again
* Get everything to work in you upgraded system
* Reinstall 11.04 (for a clean system) and get everything to work again

Please backup your data if you choose either if the reinstall options.
