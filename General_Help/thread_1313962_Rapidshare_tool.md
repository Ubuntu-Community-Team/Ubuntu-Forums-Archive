You can use Flashgot and Downthemall. Log into your Rapidshare account and Setup Flashgot to pass the links to Downthemall, then it's just a case of highlighting your links and selecting "Flashgot Selection" from the right click menu.
That's what I use for Downloading stuff these days both in Ubuntu and XP. :)

### Post by Zoot7 on 2009-11-04
> **GabrielWolff said:**
> Thing is: This way nothing checks your RS links, thus you will spend hours trying to download dead links... :(
Here's an easy enough way to do that:

Install the Firefox greasemonkey addon:
[https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/748](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/748)

Then install this Rapidshare link checker script:
[http://userscripts.org/scripts/show/13709](http://userscripts.org/scripts/show/13709)
(Hit install - top right). Then hit Shift+R to investigate whether the links are good or not, if they are a "+" will be displayed and the link will be made live (if it's not already), if they're dead a ? will be displayed beside the link.
