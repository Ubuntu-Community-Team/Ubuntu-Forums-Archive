### Post by tlue on 2010-01-28
I was searching how to create a hyperlink when writing an email with Kmail (Kontact).
I only found this old post:
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=136967](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=136967)
As I looked at the options I found the solution, but I cannot reply to the old post, because it is already closed.

Therefore here is the solution:

When writing your mail activate the HTML-Toolbar via the menu.
There you have an Icon (Chain). This is what I was searching for.

As allways you can add your own Shortcut to that (i.e. Ctrl+H, like Hyperlink)
