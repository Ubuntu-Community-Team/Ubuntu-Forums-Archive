### Post by agim on 2009-03-05
[http://www.gnu.org/software/grub/](http://www.gnu.org/software/grub/)

This is the website for grub. Linux's grand unified bootloader. This is the booting program. It may have been installed on your hard drive or your external drive. One way to know is to boot your regular computer. If it boots like normal, then grub was installed on your external. Of course, I am sure there are command line programs that can be run and can tell you this, I just don't know them.

If you are asking for exactly where on the drive grub is stored, I don't know. But the first link and the name of the program should give you enough to find the answer.
