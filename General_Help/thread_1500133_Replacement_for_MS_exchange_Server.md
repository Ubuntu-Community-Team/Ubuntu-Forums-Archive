### Post by sebastianabate on 2010-06-02
Check Scalix ([www.scalix.com](www.scalix.com)). The Comunity version is free and support up to 10 full outlook users (vía mapi, like Exchange) and unlimited standard users (with imap, pop3, webmail and mobile access).
If you need Activesync support you can buy a Starter Pack with support for 10 full outlook and Activesync users for U$S 300 (or 5 users for U$S 200)
