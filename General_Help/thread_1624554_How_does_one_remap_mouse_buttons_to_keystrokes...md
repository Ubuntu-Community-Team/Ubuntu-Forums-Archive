### Post by stinkeye on 2010-11-18
An easy way of doing this is by using easystroke mouse gestures.
```
sudo apt-get install easystroke
```

In the preferences tab > additional buttons
click on the add button and either click inside the box with the button you want to modify 
or choose the button from the drop down box.

Now click on the actions tab and hit the *Add Action* button.
Choose "key" in the type column, name your gesture and click on *Record Stroke* tab.
When the record stroke dialogue comes up click the mouse button you added earlier.
The stroke field should now look similar to the second pic with your corresponding mouse number.
