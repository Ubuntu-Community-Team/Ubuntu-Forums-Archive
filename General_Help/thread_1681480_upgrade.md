### Post by grahammechanical on 2011-02-04
1) you wait until it is 11.04 is released during the fourth month of the year 2011. I usually wait a few weeks after that to let the rush of downloads die down a bit.

2) you either access the Ubuntu home page and select upgrade, or you run Update Manager, That program will tell you that an upgrade is available. Just click and enter your password.

### Post by coffeecat on 2011-02-04
@andycc55, in your other thread you describe your self as "a noobe to ubuntu". My advice: don't even think of upgrading to 11.04 until after it has been released in April. It is still in Alpha. It is available for testing only. It is not recommended for routine use.

I am running it, and enjoying testing it, but it is a rough ride - there have been several recent breakages. You need some experience of Linux before you run a pre-release test version.
