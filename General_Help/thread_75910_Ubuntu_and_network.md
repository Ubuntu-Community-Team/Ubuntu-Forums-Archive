### Post by zootm on 2005-10-14
More generally, if you go to "Places/Network Servers" you should be able to browse your Windows network and connect to other people's shared files.

As for letting other people access your files, check out "Configuring your computer as a server" on [this page](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/SettingUpSamba?highlight=%28CategoryDocumentation%29). Print sharing is also possible but I can't remember how, and I'm at work at the moment!
