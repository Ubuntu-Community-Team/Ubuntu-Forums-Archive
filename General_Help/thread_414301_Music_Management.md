### Post by Wormsie on 2007-04-21
One thing that I've found really disappointing in Linux is the audio and iPod support. All applications seem to have something wrong in them. That's why I'm getting a Mac.

You will have trouble editing the metadata your AAC files and viewing some metadata files. If you believe you need to edit the metadata of your AAC files, I'd recommend converting them to mp3 in Windows beforehand. I would recommend Amarok or Rhythmbox, Banshee is rather slow and on my machine, at least, Exaile and Listen have been buggy. Also, at least SoundJuicer's mp3 encoder has very limited support for metadata it seems. (And you have to set up the mp3 encoding manually.)
