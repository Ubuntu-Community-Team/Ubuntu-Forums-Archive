### Post by krytorii on 2012-05-13
What do you mean by sync?

Android doesn't work like ipods/phones/pads, there is no central, single piece of software it interacts with. You can manually copy and paste files to and from it (like a USB stick) if that's what you mean, and there are tools that will let you automatically update 2 folders to have the same content every time you plug in your tablet.

Also, I would recommend you don't use an iPad with ubuntu. There is no official itunes for linux (although you may be able to get it working through wine).
