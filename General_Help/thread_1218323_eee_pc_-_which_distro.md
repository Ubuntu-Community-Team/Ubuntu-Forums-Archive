### Post by hibliss on 2009-07-21
I am using Eeebuntu Standard. Depending on the exact model of Eee PC, you may be better off just grabbing the Standard Netbook Remix, but there were issues with mine that made me look for a tweaked distro. 

Eeebuntu 3.0 is using Jaunty anyway, just with a few extra repos and tweaks, nothing you couldn't do yourself if you wanted.

The array kernel is very helpful, as is Eee-control.
