### Post by Kirboosy on 2011-03-05
Hey Oversquared,

Welcome to the Forums! :D

Its actually really easy to enable single click. Open Nautilus (open your home folder or something) and click "edit" --> "Preferences" --> "Behavior" --> 'Singly click to open items."


Hope that helps.
