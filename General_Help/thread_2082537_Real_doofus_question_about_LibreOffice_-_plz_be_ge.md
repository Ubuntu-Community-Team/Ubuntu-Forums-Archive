### Post by Zill on 2012-11-09
At the bottom right of the Writer screen you should see a "slider" which controls the zoom factor (i.e. size) for the page.  I suggest you play around with this and see if you can get your pages back to a "normal" size.

There are also some other icons to the left of the slider which determine the arrangement of pages so try these as well.
