### Post by JBAlaska on 2007-10-09
There is a utility program called HDDRegenerator  that has the ability to recover physical bad sectors (magnetic errors) on a hard disk surface. It's totally non-destructive and can be used with any OS or a bare drive.

Many people that don't have experience with this program say it doesn't work, I personally have renewed 6 HD's with it.

That being said, it's not a miracle  cure, if your HD has Physical platter damage your out of luck, but if your HD is clicking (seeking) it most likely can be renewed.

The downside is that on a large HD, it can take a very long time to complete. I recently renewed a 80GB drive with many errors and it took 6 days to repair (now this drive is working flawlessly!)

If you want to give it a try, PM me and I'll help.
