### Post by laceration on 2010-02-04
Computer hardware displays at settable resolutions, but they are always progressive, not interlaced.  Your Nvidia card will do 1080p but not 1080i, while your display will support 1080i and 720p and smaller SD resolutions because those are the resolutions that TV are broadcast in.  Your display was intended as a TV Screen.  Several years ago 1080p capability was added to more and more TV's making them virtually the same as Computer Monitors.  The best you can probably do is set your display resolution for your card at 720p.  That will be ok for watching video, but I don't think it would be that good for computing.
