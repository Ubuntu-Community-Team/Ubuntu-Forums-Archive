### Post by QIII on 2009-06-05
Like trwww said, filters (and splitters) are bad juju with DSL.

You are also at the mercy of what your neighbors are doing on your node.  If they are all streaming live porn, you may be out of luck.

What browser are you using?

There are a lot of ways to speed up Firefox, for instance.

Again, as trwww said, if you are downloading from a source that can only serve 700kbps, that's the very best you'll get.  That will be degraded by all of the switches between you and the site you are downloading from, etc.
