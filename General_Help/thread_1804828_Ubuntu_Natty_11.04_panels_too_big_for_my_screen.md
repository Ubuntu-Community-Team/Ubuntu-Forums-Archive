### Post by Matt__ on 2011-07-15
set both panels to auto-hide (right click panel)..thats another 48px or more free and then in fonts (right click desktop/change desktop background/font tab) change title bar to 8pixels or so, that should get you a little more space. Maybe even reduce the application font by 1 pix.

you could also use compiz config to force window sizes.

If you still have probs, resort to holding ALT and dragging window (click anywhere on window to drag it)

### Post by ajgreeny on 2011-07-15
To get to the buttons at the bottom of any window that is oversized, just hold Alt then click and drag from any point in the window, not just in the title-bar.  That is a standard "move window" gui arrangement in all desktop environments, I think, or certainly in all those I have used.
