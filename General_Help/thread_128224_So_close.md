### Post by timetrap on 2008-06-03
Sorry to bump this, but the current state of gpodder (for podcasts) looks VERY good, better than itunes IMO. gtkpod is still a little confusing the way it is setup. EphPod (2.58) looks to work well under wine right now.

I ended up using EphPod, becuse of the audible support. Yes you can use Audible in Linux (under wine).

Here are the apps that I use:

**gpodder** (podcasts, since these are updated the most often I use gpodder's ipod update which is EXCELLENT, great job!)
**wine**
**EphPod**  (basic music, and audible content)
**AudibleDownloadHelper.exe** (under wine) 


Here is a link for setting up audible under linux: 

[http://maulik.freeshell.net/Miki.pl?page=2007-03-22.miki](http://maulik.freeshell.net/Miki.pl?page=2007-03-22.miki)

It is a lot of work to get all of the features of itunes in linux. I can't wait to get a ZEN . . . just a few more months before I hit my MTBF on my current ipod.:lolflag:
