### Post by coffeecat on 2017-06-11
If by "left menu", you mean the launcher bar in the Unity desktop, then click on the settings icon (cogwheel shape) top right -> System Settings -> Appearance -> Behaviour tab -> then switch "Auto-hide the launcher" to on. Good luck with revealing the hidden launcher. I've not found the "hotspot" and had to slide the mouse pointer up and down a bit for the launcher to reappear. Your mileage may vary.

If you mean something else, then you need to tell us which release of Ubuntu you are using, which flavour, and which desktop environment.
