### Post by MarblePanther on 2009-02-27
System, Administration, System Monitor
and click on the System tab
This will give you ubuntu version, kernel version, RAM, etc...

To find out what version of opera and thunderbird,etc...just look in the toolbar of each respective application and look for About "application" in Help.
