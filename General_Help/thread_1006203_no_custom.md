### Post by eternalnewbee on 2008-12-09
> under effects in appearance i don't have a custom button, only extra. how do i get the custom?
Same here. I guess they left it out in 8.10.
Assuming you have compiz enabled, you could install Compiz Fusion Icon from **Add/Remove**.
After installation is complete you can find it under **System Tools**. When you click on it, the icon will appear in your notification area, on the right, in your panel.
Right-click on the icon, and check your options.
