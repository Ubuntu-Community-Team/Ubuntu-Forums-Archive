No one will be able to help you unless you can explain your issue a little more descriptively. What problem are you having with it? 

Panicking and randomly switching around settings is not a good idea ever, so I hope you haven't found yourself stumbling down that dark path.

When you use the LiveCD you need to go to 'Networking' under the System menu and activate the wifi card, manually enter the name of your network, the type of encryption (if any) and the key (if any).

When you install Ubuntu, durring setup you'll be asked for those things if it sees the network.

If you have no luck at all getting it to see your network than I'd blame the wifi card first.

That's a general rundown from my memory of the experience. When you list some more specifics then someone can specifically address your issue. :-k
