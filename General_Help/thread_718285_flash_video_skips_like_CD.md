### Post by Super TWiT on 2008-03-09
This may be because you are running out of space on hardrive or your internet connection is slower.  If your internet is slower, a solution to this would be to wait for the video to load all the way and then play it.  The reason it could be your hard drive, is because all of that video is cached on the hard drive, and if it doesn't have enough room it will skip a lot.
