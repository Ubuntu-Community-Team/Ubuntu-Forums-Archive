### Post by ufugu on 2008-11-25
[Spicebird 0.7]("http://www.spicebird.com/") came out a few days ago and thought I'd mention it since I hadn't seen anything here.  I'm always looking for a better email client and am really enjoying it.

It's a PIM based on Thunderbird.  Like the 'bird, but has tabs across the top: one for a home page (rss feeds, Google Gadgets, inbox previews etc), one for your email, one for contacts and IM, one for calendar, one for tasks.

I set it up with a Google IMAP account and it works great (I chose not to import my Thunderbird settings because starting fresh seemed smart).  The Spicebird calendar syncs correctly and bidirectionally with Google Calendar (hello Evolution...) and it's not as hideous looking as Thunderbird, it actually picks up my GTK themes nicely.

I'm not affiliated with the project in any way, I just like good stuff and thought maybe it might be good for some others here as well.

Anyone else try it?
