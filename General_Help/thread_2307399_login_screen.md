### Post by deadflowr on 2016-02-02
Note that scale usually defaults to free form, meaning it will scale the x and y setting separately.
To set it to scale both proportionally, click on the chain icon looking things that are next the size listings in the scale dialog box.
They will link and then you can scale evenly.

hope it helps.
