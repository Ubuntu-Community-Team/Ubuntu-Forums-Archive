### Post by nothingspecial on 2010-01-15
You right click on the terminal and choose profile > profile preferences.

In there you can disable the scrollbar, take the menus off and turn it transparent.

There are a number of ways to remove the window borders.

I don`t like any so I use compiz and turn off the window decoration plug in.

This may not suit you as then no windows will have decoration - you know, the bar on the top with the x - and the little square to minimise maximize and resize the window.

### Post by howefield on 2010-01-15
Right click the panel and select properties. Then in the background tab, choose solid colour. The slider can be moved depending on the level of transparency you want.
