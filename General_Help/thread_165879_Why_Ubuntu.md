### Post by mscman on 2006-04-25
I came to Linux not because I hate Windows, but rather I was looking for something new to try out.  There are people out there who are better off sticking with Windows, particularly those not willing to put forth the effort to learn an entirely new way of computing (at least to them).

That being said, for anyone that's wanting to learn Linux, I think Ubuntu is the best choice.  Whenever people ask me what Linux distribution I would reccommend (this has been happening a lot lately), I always reccommend Ubuntu.  It has been the best at recognizing any hardware I throw at it, and Dapper is quickly becoming the most up-to-date and most stable Linux distro that I've used.

Unless some other Linux distro comes along that completely blows Ubuntu out of the water, I will continue to use and promote Ubuntu to the best of my ability!
