### Post by peterjakey on 2008-08-09
Hi, when Kubuntu starts up it loads programs that I didnt close last time I shut the computer down, how can I stop this behavior?

---

### Post by ChompTheMan on 2008-08-09
Open up Kcontrol(alt+f2 then type kcontrol) and go to KDE Components >> Session Manager and under On Login change *Restore previous session* to *Start with an empty session*.
