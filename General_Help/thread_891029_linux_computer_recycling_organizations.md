### Post by dawynn on 2009-01-30
Not the only such organization, but arguably the most well known, is FreeGeek:

[http://www.freegeek.org/link.php](http://www.freegeek.org/link.php)

Originally based in Portland, they have now a "family" of companies that share the same goals.  See the "family" link on the main web page for other locations.

Free Geek is not just techies that refurbish PC's.  The organization helps train volunteers to do the same, thus passing on valuable life skills.  They also teach general computing skills to those who are given PC's.  And volunteers are rewarded for their efforts -- after so many hours of volunteer work, the volunteers are rewarded with their own free PC.
