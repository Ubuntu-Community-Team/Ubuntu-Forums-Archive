### Post by coffeecat on 2011-01-31
Right-click on the panel and choose 'Add to Panel'. Select 'Menu Bar' from the long list and then click on the add button. That gives you the usual Applications/Places/System menus, but if you want them all from one menu select 'Main Menu' instead. You can move it into place with right-click > move, and then lock it there with another right-click.
