By "read the Ubuntu disk" I take it to mean from your CD Player. Is that correct?

If you have an Ubuntu **.iso** or a [System Rescue CD]("http://www.sysresccd.org/Main_Page"), if such is in the CD drive can you "boot" from them?

If you cannot boot, either your BIOS has changed or your optical (CD or DVD) drive is broken. You must determine the status here. If you need help with the BIOS, let me know. I'll follow this post for a few days.

If you **can** read a CD/DVD, use the Ubuntu CD (or Sys Rescue) and from the Panel Menu (in Live CD mode) open System/Administration/Partition or such, or from System Resuce, using GParted, you can access, and re-format your hard disk drive.

If this is what you mean by: gobbledygook, I can't help you further.
