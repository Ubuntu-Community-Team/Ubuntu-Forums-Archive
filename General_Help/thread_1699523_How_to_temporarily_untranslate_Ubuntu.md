### Post by Shiryu on 2011-03-03
Hello
I have installed Ubuntu in Portuguese.
Sometimes I need to show screenshots and command outputs to other people in English, but they are translated in Portuguese.
How to set Ubuntu to English and posteriorly set it back to Portuguese?
Thanks

---

### Post by I&#9829;HTML5 on 2011-03-03
On the login screen, when you click your name, look at the bottom of the screen for a box that says "Language:". Select English and log in. When you are done with English, log back out and select Portuguese.
