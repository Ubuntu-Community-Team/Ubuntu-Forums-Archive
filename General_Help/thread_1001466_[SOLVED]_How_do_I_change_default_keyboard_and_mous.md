### Post by Shwefty on 2008-12-04
Absolutely!

System -> Preferences -> Windows

You can change the "drag" key to Super (Windows Logo)-Click or Ctrl-Click there.

---

### Post by eternalnewbee on 2008-12-04
To change your keyboard layout, go to: **Main Menu > System > Preferences > Keyboard**.
For your mouse, go to: **Main Menu > System > Preferences > Mouse**.
