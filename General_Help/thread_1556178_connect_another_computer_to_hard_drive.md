### Post by anirudhtomer on 2010-08-19
you can connect the PC's using a CAT 5 cable making a crossover connection
Make a private LAN between these two PC's & then the other computer can access your PC's hard drives.

### Post by jespdj on 2010-08-19
You mean that you have two computers and one harddisk, and you want to connect the harddisk to both computers at the same time? That is not possible.

A CAT 5 cable is an Ethernet network cable. There are special versions of those cables with crossed wires, so that you can connect two PCs directly together, without needing a router or another kind of network device in between. That's what anirudhtomer means.

### Post by Calash on 2010-08-19
It sounds like you are looking for what is called Target Disk Mode on Apple computers, where you can connect two systems via Firewire and have one computer boot off of the other computers hard drive.

Outside of apple I don't know of any systems that have the ability to do this.
