### Post by OliverW on 2008-06-02
Have a look here:
[http://www.alsa-project.org/main/index.php/Matrix:Main](http://www.alsa-project.org/main/index.php/Matrix:Main)

And here:
[http://alsa.opensrc.org/Alsa_Preferred_Soundcards](http://alsa.opensrc.org/Alsa_Preferred_Soundcards)

All the cards there are supported. Sometimes they are called by their chipset but not their actual brand. I found that especially the cheaper C-Media based cards just work great. Be careful with Creative they can be tricky especially their new X-Fi cards.
