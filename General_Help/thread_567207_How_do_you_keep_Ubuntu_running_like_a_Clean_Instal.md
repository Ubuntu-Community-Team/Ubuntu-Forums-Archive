### Post by rsambuca on 2007-10-04
The one case where you may notice slowdown is if you start running mixed libraries on your computer, and you have low amounts of ram.  For instance, if you are using Ubuntu and then install a KDE program like Amarok, then you will have both the Gnome and KDE libraries loaded into RAM.  If you don't have enough ram, then you will have to use your swap and you will then definitely see a significant slowdown.  Perhaps that could be your issue.  How much RAM do you have?
