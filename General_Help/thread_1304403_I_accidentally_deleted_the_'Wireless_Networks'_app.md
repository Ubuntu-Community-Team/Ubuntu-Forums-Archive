### Post by tuahaa on 2009-10-29
The wireless network applet is a part of the "notification area" applet. You need to add "notification area" in the panel by right clicking and selecting "Add to panel" and then searching up "Notification area".

I had huge trouble with this when I was new to Ubuntu a couple of months ago
