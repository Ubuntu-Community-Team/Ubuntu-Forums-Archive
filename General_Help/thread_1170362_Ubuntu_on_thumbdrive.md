### Post by kpkeerthi on 2009-05-26
2 GB is too limiting. Atleast 4GB is recommended. Installing Ubuntu onto a USB flash drive is same as installing it onto your internal drive, so the general Ubuntu system requirements apply. During installation you just have to make sure that you select the external USB flash drive for the partitions and GRUB.
