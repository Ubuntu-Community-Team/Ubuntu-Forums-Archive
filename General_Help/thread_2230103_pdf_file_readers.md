### Post by craig10 on 2014-06-17
Hi Nosphky,

I don't have any suggestions for a different PDF reader, but Document Viewer doesn't have configuration settings in the menu system. (All it has there is a help option.) The rather limited settings are accessed by clicking the spanner/wrench icon near the top, right-hand corner of the wondow, just to the left of the cog icon.

Hope that's what you're looking for.


Craig
