```

```

### Post by Vaphell on 2011-03-15
try disabling hardware acceleration of flash
go to [http://derbauer.de](http://derbauer.de) (or any site that has flash settings active) - rightclick, settings, uncheck 'hardware acceleration'. In my case it helped with flash videos flickering and hovering over everything and leaving traces when scrolling.
