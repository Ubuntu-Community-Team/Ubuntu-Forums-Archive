### Post by arsenic23 on 2010-05-02
1>>>  Install compizconfig-settings-manager, I don't think you'll even have to mess with it.

2>>>  I'm not aware of anything you can do to change this one.

3>>>  Well you could run gnome-volume-control-applet manually to get the old volume control.  The new one may be ugly, but it seems to work better.  If you want to keep the old one just add it to Startup Applications under Preferences.
