### Post by lavinog on 2009-04-12
> **christo16 said:**

Although, it is hooked up a UPS and set to automatically shutdown.
Is the switch hooked up to a UPS?
A power surge permanently messed up my brother's ethernet port which was connected to a switch.

The leds on the network card and on the switch are good indicators of a problem.
Most network cards/motherboards have two leds...one for connection and one for activity.
Your switch should also show that your computer is connected to the appropriate port.
