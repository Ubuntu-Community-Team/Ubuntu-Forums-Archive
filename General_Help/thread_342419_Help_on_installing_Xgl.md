### Post by CowzRule on 2007-01-20
First you'll need to install the FGLRX driver for your video card.
[http://wiki.cchtml.com/index.php/Ubuntu_Edgy_Installation_Guide]("http://wiki.cchtml.com/index.php/Ubuntu_Edgy_Installation_Guide")
Once you have those drivers installed and working, you can then install XGL.
[http://wiki.beryl-project.org/wiki/Install_Beryl_on_Ubuntu_Edgy_with_XGL]("http://wiki.beryl-project.org/wiki/Install_Beryl_on_Ubuntu_Edgy_with_XGL")
