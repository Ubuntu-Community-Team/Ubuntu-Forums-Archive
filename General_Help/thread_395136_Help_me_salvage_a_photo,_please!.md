A reminder: don't recover files to the same physical drive that your damaged image is on, since it may overwrite the data that you may want to save. I forgot how Photorec works; if it doesn't warn you about this, do keep it in mind, even though you're only trying to save one file and the chance it will get damaged is very low, especially given that you didn't explicitly delete it.
