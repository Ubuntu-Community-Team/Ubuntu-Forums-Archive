### Post by lavinog on 2008-03-26
It depends on what drivers it is referring to...there are a couple of modems that have restricted drivers for them, but if you don't use your modem then it wouldn't affect anything.

for some systems the video card uses a restricted driver (like my ATI 200 video card) the open source driver doesn't support this card too well. Without the restricted driver scrolling through a web page on firefox uses 100% cpu...and 3d framerates are no higher than 10 fps on glxgears.
With the restricted driver enabled for my card, firefox doesn't use much cpu cycles when scrolling and I can get about 800 fps on glxgears (although that is still low compared to other cards)
