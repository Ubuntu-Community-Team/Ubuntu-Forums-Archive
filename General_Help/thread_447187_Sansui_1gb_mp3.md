### Post by MoLE on 2007-05-23
If the computer was on while you were trying to charge it, and it was a usb connection - then you may have a dead USB port.  Alternatively the battery may be DOA - this is not uncommon with lithium ion batteries which have a limited lifespan whether they are used or not.
