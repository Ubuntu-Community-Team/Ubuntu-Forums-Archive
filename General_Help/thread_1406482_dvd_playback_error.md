### Post by mc4man on 2010-02-14
> message "playback prohibited by area limitations"
That means your video was encoded to the wrong format for your standalone hardware and you'll need to redo it.
Depending on where you live you have either pal or ntsc and the video was done for the other one, most likely your ntsc (usa and a few other area's) and your converting app encoded as pal.

### Post by fooman on 2010-02-16
i use devede all the time to convert .avi to .iso....i then use k3b to burn the image file (.iso) to a dvd.

whatever program you are using to burn the dvd (k3b, brasero, etc)....you must choose *burn image* from the menu.   burning an .iso file as a dvd/video project will not work (as you have already found out).
