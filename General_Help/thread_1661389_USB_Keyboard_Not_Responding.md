### Post by fabricator4 on 2011-01-06
Intermittent USB problems are most often due to power issues.  Unplug all other USB devices (including removing cards from internal card readers), try a different port, and try a powered hub.  Don't use unpowered hubs if you can help it - I've had problems with those even if there's only one device plugged in.

It's understandable to have problems with things like external hard drives, but frustrating when it's something like a keyboard or mouse.

In my experience USB 1 ports were much worse, but you do still have problems with USB 2 ports and devices.

Chris.
