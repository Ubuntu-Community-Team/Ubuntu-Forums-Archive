I do have the radar working on mine.  What I did was goto what ever radar map you want to use and left click the radar map and click on copy image location. Then in the weather applet perferances click on enable radar map, click on use custom address for radar map and then paste the radar map location in the box.  Just make sure when selection map location you left click the map itself and not the url on top of the web page and it should work.  Hope this helps some.
