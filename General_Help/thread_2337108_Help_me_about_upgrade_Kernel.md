### Post by HereInOz on 2016-09-15
I get the feeling that you are wanting to use an older version of Ubuntu but upgrade the kernel using a ppa  This is probably not a good idea, if this, indeed, is what you are trying to do.  Things may break.

I agree with Bucky Ball that you are best to use Ubuntu 14.04LTS or 16.04LTS rather than try to upgrade the kernel on an older, unsupported system.  

If you can do a fresh install, you will probably be better off than trying to upgrade your system.

Good luck.
