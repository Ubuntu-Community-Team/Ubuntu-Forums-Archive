### Post by skippy81 on 2006-05-27
Try installing Mozilla Firebird Email Client on Windows.  Import your Outlook data into thunderbird.

Once it is imported you will have to seach through your disk to find the mbox file Thunderbird uses to save its data.  This file is linux/unix compatable and can simply be copied over onto linux.

I can't remember where exactly mozilla stores its mails, but it willl be somewhere in "My Documents" and the hidden "Local Settings" folder I should think.
