### Post by Hadaka on 2015-10-10
```
click your **Empathy Internet** icon >> **Edit** >> **Accounts**

[ACCOUNT BOX] Click the "**+**" (lower left)  

[Add New Account] click the **Down arrow** (same line default..Jabber) 

Choose **FB Chat** >> fill in password  and save
```
