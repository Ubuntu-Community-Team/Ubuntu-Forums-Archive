### Post by alyons3537 on 2012-05-04
Hi guys,

I've spent the last 2 hours searching the net for any info on why libreoffice 3.5 crashes when trying to configure a Bibus pipe.

typically, simply typing:

```
libreoffice /usr/share/bibus/Setup/UnoConnectionListener.odg
```Opens the .odg file that allows me to configure the Bibus pipe.  Works just fine on my 11.10 laptop (with libreoffice 3.4)  Now, libreoffice attempts to open the file, and crashes half way through.

Does anyone know of a fix for this?  I also attempted to navigate to the folder via the GUI, and selected Open with > Libreoffice and Open with > libreoffice writer with no luck.

### Post by alyons3537 on 2012-05-04
SOLVED:

Found the solution guys! Apparently, ubuntu 12.04 ships with individual installs of writer, calc, math, and draw.  If you search 'libreoffice' in the software center, you'll find that the actual 'libreoffice' COLLECTION is not installed.  Simply installing the entire 'libreoffice' collection (just another 6.4 MB, most likely from the LO version of MS Access) enables you to open the file 'UnoConnectionListener.odg' without crashing


Figured I'd throw this in here in case people need a solution to this problem.
