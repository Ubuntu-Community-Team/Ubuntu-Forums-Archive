### Post by wilee-nilee on 2010-10-13
> **LunarRoar said:**
> I have access to a thumb drive and the ISO for Ubuntu, yes. Would it be possible for you please give me step by step instructions, if you don't mind?

Here is a thumbloader unetbootin, you can download a Windows or Linux version. Notice the notice at the top of the page regarding using a Linux version.
[http://unetbootin.sourceforge.net/](http://unetbootin.sourceforge.net/)

If you already have a Ubuntu setup there is also a thumbloader installed it is in menu-system-admin-startup disc creator.

The thumb loaders are pretty straight forward they all allow you to find the ISO you have and choose the thumb your installing to. Reformat the thumb to a fat partition first to have it a clean install, you can right click to do this in Ubuntu, and in computer on the thumb in windows.
