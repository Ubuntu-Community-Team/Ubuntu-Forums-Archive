### Post by Paul Sodtke on 2016-06-15
Thanks, Tom_Carr and others in this thread. It worked for me, too. {At first I misunderstood and changed the keyboard settings. Then I read more carefully in the thread linked above and checked  Language Support.) In my case 16.04 is a new install (I upgraded my laptop to an ASUS convertible, model TP300LD, and just moved my Home folder over from by old laptop.) I was puzzled when Ubuntu Software resulted in no action. (From a terminal, it said it timed out.) I removed and reinstalled ubuntu-software and gnome-software -- no change. After reading this forum and the link, I checked System settings > Language support; it said no language support was installed. I told it to install, rebooted, and - presto! -- Ubuntu Software works, just as reported above. Thanks again.
