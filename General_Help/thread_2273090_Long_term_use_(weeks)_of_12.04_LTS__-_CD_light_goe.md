### Post by QIII on 2015-04-10
Hello.

If you have been running from optical media for that long, I would suggest that as the source of your issues.

You say this happens after a period of use.  The laser used to read the disk actually erodes the metallic medium sandwiched in the plastic.  After continued use, you have almost certainly degraded the image to the point where it has become unusable.

The fact that it works initially indicates that there is no bug -- only degradation.  Your instability observations likely have nothing whatsoever to do with the stability of the OS, but the stability of optical media.

As for 14.04, I have been using it as my daily driver since it was in beta without issues.  The fact that a problem with a particular piece of hardware won't be fixed may have more to do with driver support from the OEM than anything else.

One should not make a general statement like that based on the observations of a sample size of one.

I also see you got some answers on this very same question almost exactly one year ago.

Between those answers and this, I think your questions have been adequately addressed.  So that others do not expend their voluntary time further, this thread is closed.
