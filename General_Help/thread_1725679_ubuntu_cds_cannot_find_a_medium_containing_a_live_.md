Did you mean that the LiveCD will boot on *other* computers, but *not* on your laptop - but you still think the problem is with the CD?

For most people who have downloaded their copy of the Ubuntu iso, the problem is that they burned the file to a CD as a data file rather than as an IMAGE.

If your CD works on other computers but not on your laptop, perhaps the problem is with the laptop! The first thing I would check is to make sure the *boot options* on the laptop include the option to boot from CD. If not, perhaps a [network install of Ubuntu]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Installation/Netboot") will work for you.
