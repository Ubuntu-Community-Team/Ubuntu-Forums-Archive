### Post by MikeFlint on 2009-12-10
Have a look in your Power Management settings (System --> Preferences) - this determines the luminosity when running on AC Power (and battery power).

This might explain what is happening.

### Post by teward on 2009-12-10
Might also check your BIOS.  some newer BIOS from dell contains options for luminosity settings when you are on battery or AC power.
