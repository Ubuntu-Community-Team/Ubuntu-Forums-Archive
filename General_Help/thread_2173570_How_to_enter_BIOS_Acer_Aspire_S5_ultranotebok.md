holy sheeeeet, you were right! I rapidly tapped F2 then turned on the power button then kept rapidly tapping F2 and booted into an actual BIOS setup menu!!!

part of the problem was that this laptop is too damn fast, it literally is the fastest laptop on the market because of two sata drives in a RAID array for speed. So if you are too slow tapping F2 you get a non-bios menu of Windows 7 safe mode stuff and such. I had to be very quick tapping F2, so I did it BEFORE turning on the power and continued to rapidly tap it once the power button was on. That got me to an actual BIOS setup.
