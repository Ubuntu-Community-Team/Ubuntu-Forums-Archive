### Post by prshah on 2008-05-19
> **ThomasWii said:**
> The test have been where i have played around with the xorg. to me it dose not seem logical, to me it seems like something is blocking the pads,

Some laptops (notably, HP) have a button centered along the top edge of the touchpad that turns off the touchpad. It's concealed (moulded into the touchpad frame), but usually has a bluish light when activated and orangish when deactivated.

By any chance is this switch "orangish"/turned off? It's a pure hardware function, so nothing in software will point out or control this button.
