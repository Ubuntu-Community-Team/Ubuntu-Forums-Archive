### Post by Ampi on 2009-11-01
The version numbers in Ubuntu stand for the release date.
The first number stands for the year and the second number stands for the month. Ubuntu is normally always released in April and October. 
So 9.10 stands for Ubuntu in October 2009...

Hope that makes it less confusing ;)
