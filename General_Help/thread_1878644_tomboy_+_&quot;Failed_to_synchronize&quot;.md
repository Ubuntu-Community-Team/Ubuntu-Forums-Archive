### Post by notgary on 2011-11-10
I've had problems with syncing Tomboy notes before and there are a few things you can try:


[LIST=1]
[*]Disable and re-enable the syncing feature in Tomboy.
[*]Disable and re-emable Ubuntu One syncing on the computer as a whole i.e., log out vis the Ubuntu One Control Panel and remove the computer from the list of connected devices.
[*]Delete the Ubuntu One configuration files which I think are located in ~/.config/ubuntuone though I can't be sure since I'm currently not on an Ubuntu computer to double check, so be careful with that one.
[/LIST]
Also make sure you have the space to store the data. Rememebr that music purchased from the Ubuntu One Music Store counts towards your allowance.
