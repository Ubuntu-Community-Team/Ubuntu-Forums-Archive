### Post by soro2005 on 2008-11-02
This sounds very much like [this bug here](https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/linux/+bug/286285). I assume that a fix is in the works, so keep updating. In the meantime, I have found out that my computer runs for hours and days without a problem if I don't run my bittorrent client. So the number of connections, or perhaps the overall network load, seems to have something to do with it. If it's just you and your wireless router, you could also try whether a different encryption mechanism makes things better.
