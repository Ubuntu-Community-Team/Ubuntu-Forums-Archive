### Post by meng on 2007-02-01
Ubuntu/GNOME comes with a volume control applet in the top panel menu by default (top right of screen, left-click it). If it isn't there, you can right click the panel and Add to Panel ... the volume control.

In KDE, I think the volume control is in the lower right corner.
