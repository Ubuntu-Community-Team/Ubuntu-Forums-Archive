### Post by davbren on 2010-11-25
I don't think its possible to have consistent quailty settings, it all depends on the size of the original files. I think the standard res for DVDS is 600x480 (don't quote me), but thats what I use.

Then if you set the size at a gig you'll get consistantly sized files but you may well get varying degrees of quality. 

With the bitrate, i tend to try to get it just above when it says the bitrate is really good, just to try and get rid of the remaining blockiness. This *will* increase ripping time though.
