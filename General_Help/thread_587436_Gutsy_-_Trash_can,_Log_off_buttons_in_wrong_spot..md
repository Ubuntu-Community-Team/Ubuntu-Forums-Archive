### Post by gb5uqx on 2007-10-22
It took me a while to understand your question but I think the penny has just dropped :)

Right click on the icons and click the lock to panel tab to un-tick it, then right click again and select move then you can drag the icon to where ever you want on the panel. When its in position left click, then just right click and tick the lock to panel option again VOILA !!!  :)
