### Post by ridgeland on 2011-01-04
I have a Dell E520n that still hums along, it's my HTPC now, running Ubuntu 10.10 with MythTV.

I would try to install Ubuntu again.  If you have 10.04 or 10.10 that would probably be better than 9.10.

If you partition your hard drive correctly you can have multiple copies of Linux on your PC, i.e. Ubuntu 9.10, Ubuntu 10.04, Ubuntu 10.10, Fedora14, SuSE etc.  You can only boot into one and that one is chosen at the grub menu.  When that menu is flashing by just touch the spacebar and it will stay until you hit enter or arrow-right.

I have no idea what IRQ19 is or does.
