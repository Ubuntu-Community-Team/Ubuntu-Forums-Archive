### Post by Darael on 2009-12-18
PNGs and JPGs will (by default) open for viewing in Eye of Gnome (command "eog") or GPicView (command "gpicview") - at least AIUI.

.doc's can be opened with OpenOffice ("openoffice.org" or "soffice-writer") or, if you install it, with one of the other office programs available for Ubuntu, like AbiWord.

For other programs and/or file types, have a look at [https://help.ubuntu.com/9.10/switching/applications-equivalents.html](https://help.ubuntu.com/9.10/switching/applications-equivalents.html)
