### Post by voodoobettie on 2009-06-20
Did you install Ubuntu from within Vista or did you install it by booting the CD? Ubuntu will ask you if you want to overwrite your disk during the installation. If you did overwrite all your information you will have to use the restore disks that came with your computer as Wiebelhaus said above.

One thing to check first of all is that you're not just booting to the LiveCD over and over, make sure that the Ubuntu disk isn't still in your CD/DVD drive. :)
