### Post by Fritzed on 2007-12-20
There are two monitors that you may be referring to, system-monitor and hardware-monitor.  These are both panel applications.  System-monitor is included in the package "gnome-applets".  Hardware-monitor actually has it's own package simply called "hardware-monitor".   Both of these packages can be installed via synaptic, you will then have them in your "add to panel" menu.
