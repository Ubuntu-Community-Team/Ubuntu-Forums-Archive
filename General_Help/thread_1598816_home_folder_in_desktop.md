### Post by Vaphell on 2010-10-17
gconf-editor
apps > nautilus > preferences > desktop_is_home_dir set to TRUE

---

### Post by BlazeFire247 on 2010-10-17
Do you want the contents of the home folder in your desktop? This can be done by installing [Ubuntu Tweak]("http://ubuntu-tweak.com/"). On **Desktop**, click on *Desktop Icon Settings* then on the bottom, tick the "Show contents of Home Folder on desktop (logout for changes to take effect)" box.
