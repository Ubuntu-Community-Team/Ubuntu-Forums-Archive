### Post by jabeez on 2008-09-12
not in KDE, a theme is split up into the individual things you mention, and you change them all to whatever you want. Go into the Control Center and tweak to your hearts content. Color schemes can be downloaded from kde.look then imported (they're like .rc files), so find one you like, download, then go into the control center>desktop>colors and import the file. It is more complicated than gnome where a theme changes a bunch of things, but it does kind of force you to come up with your own thing. Pardon if these instructions aren't spot on, but I'm in gnome right now and am going off memory. It's all really easy though, just poke around in the control center and you can change anything you like.
