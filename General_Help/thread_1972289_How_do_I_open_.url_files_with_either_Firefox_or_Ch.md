From what I've gathered, you want to open a url from an imported favorites folder from IE, but you want to open it in Chrome?  Is that correct?

Are your favorites bundled into one XML file?  Or are they all separate files for each url?  Also, if they are each separate files, are they HTML files (do they have an .html suffix)?

If they are .html files, then all you need to do is right-click on them and on the popup menu click on Open in Google Chrome.  If this option is not listed on the menu, then go to Open With, and click on Other Application.  Then find Google Chrome in the list.  To make Google Chrome the default program to open .html files, right click on the .html file and go to Properties.  Go to the Open With tab and select Google Chrome.

Chromium is just the open source version of Chrome, and is the exact same software, except open source.  So anything you can do on Chromium you could also do in Chrome, provided they are the same exact version number.
