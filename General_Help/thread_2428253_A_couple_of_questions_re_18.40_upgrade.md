### Post by CatKiller on 2019-10-01
Nuking everything and just having the one OS partition and an EFI partition should be the default action when you install. If you want more control over the process, you can select the Something Else option.

Questions about other things should go in a different thread, so that the people that know about those things can find them. I would say that General Help is a fine place for your new thread, too.
