### Post by andymorton on 2011-01-20
Go to system>help and support then click on the music, video and photos section and then choose playing DVDs (2.1).

Make sure you have all four packages which are listed there installed (you can just click on them to check and then put the command a little further down into the terminal.

Once you've done that the DVD should play.

andy
