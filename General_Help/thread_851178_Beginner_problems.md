### Post by rocktorrentz on 2008-07-06
For the first question go: System, Preferences, Sessions (or the dutch alternatives). Then go to "session options" (the last tab), uncheck the check box, close all programs except those you want open when you boot your computer, and click the "remember currently-running applications" button.
