### Post by Steveway on 2008-11-30
In Linux the Middle-mouse-button is reserverd for a far more usefull feature, though you can change it in Firefoxs about:config.
It's there for copy and paste. To do this; simply mark the text with the left mouse-button you want to copy and the press the middle mouse-button to paste where you want.
