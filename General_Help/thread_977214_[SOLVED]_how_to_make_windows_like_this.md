### Post by stone@bruti on 2008-11-10
Hi, I tend to stick to Gnome look and the art manager  (install from synaptic under gnome-art or sudo aptitude install gnome-art). it automaticly downloads and installs themes (& icon packs) from [http://art.gnome.org/]("http://art.gnome.org/").

For single rather nice icons, I quite like [http://www.crystalxp.net]("http://www.crystalxp.net/galerie/en.cat.1.html"), just search for PNG's (I know, it's windows based but hey, icons are icons on whatever OS !!)

Those are the main sites that I use, other than that, google's my best friend, just search for png icon pack or svg icon pack and happy clicking !

hope that helps, have fun !

Stone

PS Sorry, didn't see your second question. for the icon packs, I apply them through appearance. Emerald is just for your windows decoration (I think, if someone can confirm that one, it's been a while since I've messed around with emerald !)
