### Post by papibe on 2011-09-09
It will be much easier to use the network to share files.

A few alternatives:
[LIST]
[*]Use nfs to share folders over the net.
[*]Use [samba]("http://www.simplehelp.net/2007/05/19/how-to-share-files-and-folders-in-ubuntu/") to do the same.
[*]Install OpenSSH-Server and use sftp in [Nautilus]("http://postneo.com/2005/02/26/when-things-just-work-nautilus-and-sftp").
[/LIST]
Take a look at the suggestions, and decide which one works (or is easier) for you. Tell us if you need more help.
