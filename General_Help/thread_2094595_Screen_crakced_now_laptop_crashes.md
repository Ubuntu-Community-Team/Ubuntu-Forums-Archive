### Post by Impavidus on 2012-12-14
The screen may have cracked due to some physical impact (otherwise it could have been a temperature shock), which may well have damaged other parts of your machine, like connectors or your HD. That last one has some protection against impacts when switched off, but if it does get damaged, anything may happen. An electrical short in your screen is not very likely to give you more errors than broken lines and columns in your screen, but you never know for sure.
