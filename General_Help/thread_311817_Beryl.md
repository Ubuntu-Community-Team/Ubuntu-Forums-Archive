### Post by cmost on 2006-12-03
Try using Aptitude to install gnome-desktop instead of Apt (Synaptic.)  You'll have to be on the command line though.  Aptitude bellyaches less than Apt and will usually just do what you want without a lot of hassle (at your own risk of course.)  It will give you several options to accomplish what you want; when it comes to resolving dependencies.  One of them may allow you to exclude OOo.  Good luck!
