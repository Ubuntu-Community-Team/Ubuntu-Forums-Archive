### Post by Lars Noodén on 2011-12-06
You can [check the MD5 checksum](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HowToMD5SUM).  Your [download site](http://ftp.funet.fi/pub/Linux/INSTALL/Ubuntu/dvd-releases/releases/12.04/alpha-1/) should list the expected checksums for the edition that you downloaded.
