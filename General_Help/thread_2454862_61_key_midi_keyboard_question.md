### Post by webaake on 2020-12-15
USB is the way to go today. Before keyboards with USB came to exist, you had to use a Midi interface between the Synth and the PC. With a USB keyboard you don't need that and the keyboard is often times powered via the USB as well. I have a M-Audio Keystation 61 that worked out of the box. No drivers needed for Linux. It's been working with JACK, Ardour and Reaper on Linux for years now.
