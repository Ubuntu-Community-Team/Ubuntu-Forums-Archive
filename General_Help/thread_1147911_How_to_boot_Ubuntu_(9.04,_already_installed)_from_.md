### Post by Merk42 on 2009-05-03
Download the 9.04 liveCD if you haven't already.  Put it in the drive and turn the computer on.  Then choose "try Ubuntu without making changes"

You **may** not have to reinstall XP.  It could be GRUB just doesn't know where it is installed.

Keep in mind, if you reinstall XP you'll most likely have to reinstall Ubuntu (or at least GRUB) afterwards.
