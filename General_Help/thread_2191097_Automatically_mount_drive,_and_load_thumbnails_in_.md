### Post by SuperFreak on 2013-12-02
I have the same issue with Nemo in Ubuntu 12.04.This is not really a proper solution but I found if I open Nemo folder directory and the open Nautilus directory, the thumbnails will refresh in Nautilus and the will be that way in Nemo (semi permanently- will remain in effect after reboots). The problem is I have 1000's of photos in 100's of directories and to get it so the individual photos are thumbnails I have to open Nautilus right down to the bottom subdirectory. I use coverthumbailer and presently most of my folders show thumnails by this method but not individual photos. If it were possible to open all folders in Nautilus down to the subdirectory level at once it might work for individual images.
