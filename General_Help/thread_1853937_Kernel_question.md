### Post by Frogs Hair on 2011-10-03
The kernel includes the latest drivers and security updates and I see no benefits in turning off updates  .  I have not tested this , but open the Synaptic Package manager  > Settings > Preferences > Distribution .

Select " Always prefer the installed version " this will affect other updates as well . Kernel updates should not break your installation and if they do there is another problem .
