### Post by chessnerd on 2010-07-11
I take it this is a laptop.

This happened with mine once. Here are the steps that got mine to work again: 

Unplug it and take out the battery, try to turn it on a couple times (obviously that won't work, but it will drain the on-board capacitors), give it a minute, and then hook the battery back in and try to get it boot.

If that doesn't work, I'm not sure what to tell you. You haven't really given much to work with so more information is required. What is the brand/model of the computer? Do you know if any components are faulty (power cord, battery, etc.)?

Even then, I can't make any promises...
