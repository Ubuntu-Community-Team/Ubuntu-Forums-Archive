### Post by Laobi on 2011-12-11
Press CTRL+N when the Nautilus window is active (or go to menu File->NewWindow.  You can also open just a new tab instead of new window by pressing CTRL+T.
If you want to open a particular folder in a new window then right-click on that folder in Nautilus and choose "Open in new window".
