### Post by coffeecat on 2009-06-18
Use Open Office to create an empty .odt file, call it what you will, and save that in ~/Templates. Then, when you right-click on the desktop, or in another folder, you'll find that template file as an option under 'Create Document'. You can create several different templates with appropriate names and they'll all appear under 'Create Document'.
