### Post by M7S on 2009-04-25
Hi. The developer of dockbarx (with a k ;)) here. The only way of changing the icons shown in dockbarx for now is to make a launcher for that program. If you drag a launcher from gnome-menu to a folder, you can change the icon by right clicking on it and choosing properties. Then you can drag the launcher to dockbar.

You can't change the icons that are shown in the taskbar area. Those icons are usually set by the program itself.
