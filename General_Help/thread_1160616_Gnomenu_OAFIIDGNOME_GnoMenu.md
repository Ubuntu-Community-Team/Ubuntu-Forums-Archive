I have the same problem, however, it's been submitted as a bug . One of the developers, technoshaun, says:

"We know we will keep working on this but this is, in reality, a gnome-panel bug. Though it has forced us to clean up the code a lot, so some good has come from it. However, its how gnome-panel handles its Bonobo backend that causes this. We're just looking for a way to control it, but that's all we will ever do, control it. Till gnome-panel corrects this its just going to happen."
