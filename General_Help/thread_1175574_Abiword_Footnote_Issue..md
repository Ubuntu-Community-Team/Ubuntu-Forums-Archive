### Post by Infinite Indefinite on 2009-06-01
I'm not sure whether this has been dealt with already; in any case:

On Abiword 2.4.5, there are times when, due to lack of space to place a footnote in the main text, it is placed in the footnotes section, (thus the section has the footnote number placed twice).  Deleting such a footnote does not help; subsequent footnotes will act as if the footnote still exists and will be numbered accordingly.  (Thus, for example, if one had footnote 4 and deleted it, the footnote series will go from 1, 2, 3, 5, 6, etc..)

A 'solution' of sorts is to increase the space in the area where the footnote is meant to be placed, (for example, press the enter key), then delete the footnote, and then insert it again.  I've found that this solves the problem, although ideally, the bug in the program would be fixed.
