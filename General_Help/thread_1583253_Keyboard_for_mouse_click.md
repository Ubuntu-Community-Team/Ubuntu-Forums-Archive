### Post by Zimmer on 2010-09-27
Use the Keyboard preference tool to configure the keyboard to simulate a mouse. To open the Keyboard preference tool, choose System  &#9656; Preferences &#9656; Keyboard. The Mouse Keys options can be enabled by selecting the Mouse Keys tab.

Use the arrow keys on the keyboard keypad (2,4,6,8 ) to move the pointer and the middle button (#5 on the pad) for left click. Have not found right click yet :)

EDIT: Found it.

Press the - (minus sign key) on the keypad to turn #5 into right click. It will stay that way until you pres either / or * on the keypad to flip it back to Left click...

a combination of this and the usual keyboard tab and arrow keys will assist you , too.
