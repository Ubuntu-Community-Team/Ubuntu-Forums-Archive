### Post by cicatrix1 on 2010-06-23
> **Salvia said:**
> Does any of an alternative to Gwibber that can connect with Facebook and do what Gwibber claims it can do. Thanks.:confused:

Gwibber does connect to Facebook and Twitter and the like, but it's about status updates.  If you're looking for Facbook chat, pidgin (as mentioned up there) is your best bet.  You'll also need pidgin-facebookchat.

```
sudo apt-get install pidgin pidgin-facebookchat
```

or find the 2 packages in synaptic.
