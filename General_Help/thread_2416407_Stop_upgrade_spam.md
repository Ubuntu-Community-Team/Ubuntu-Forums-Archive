### Post by archerbob on 2019-04-09
Everyday the software updater notifies me I am using 16.04 and wants me to upgrade to 18.04.2, I don't want to and the daily spam is annoying.  

How do I disable that message?

And before anyone asks why I don't want to upgrade it's because when I tried to install the 18.04 OS to this new system initially it would not work, thus I went back to a previous version that does work.

### Post by CatKiller on 2019-04-09
In the **Software Sources** - which may be called something else, depending on which flavour you're running - you can go to the **Updates** tab and select **Never** from the **Show new distribution releases** dropdown.

---

### Post by archerbob on 2019-04-09
Thanks I found it at "Software & Updates", Updates tab, "Notify me of new Ubuntu version"

I clicked the down arrow and chose "Never", hopefully that's fix it.
