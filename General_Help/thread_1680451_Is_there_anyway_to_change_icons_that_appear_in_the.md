### Post by Tamlynmac on 2011-02-02
Have you tried right clicking on  the icon and selecting "Properties". It should open a small window called Launcher Properties. By left clicking on the icon (on the top left side), it will permit you to change the icon.

The Launcher Properties are also available in the "Edit Menus". Right click on an app and select "properties". By changing the icon in the menu (regardless of it's location - submenu) it should also change the launcher icon in the panel.

Good Luck & hope this helps.
