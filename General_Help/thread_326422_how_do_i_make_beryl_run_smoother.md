### Post by d3v1ant_0n3 on 2006-12-27
I'd recommend:

Turn off Blurfx plugin
Turn off Water plugin (doesn't work on intels anyway)
Turn off XGLSnow
Limit Trailfocus to 4 or less windows to keep track of.
Turn off Transparent Cube

In 'General Options' turn off 'Sync To VBlank', and change the Refresh Rate slider up to 200.
