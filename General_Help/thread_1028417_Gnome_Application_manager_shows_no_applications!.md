### Post by ajgreeny on 2009-01-02
What do you mean by Gnome Application Manager?  I wonder if you are asking about the Applications part of the menubar, and if that has lost all its applications I can not imagine what has happened.

If I am right in my thoughts about this being a menu problem, right click in the word Applications and choose "Edit menu"  This should at least let you see if that is the problem.  You could also try removing the menubar and then reinstalling it or the gnome main menu by using the right click on the menubar.  Perhaps the menu applet has somehow become corrupted and a reinstall will put everything back to order.
