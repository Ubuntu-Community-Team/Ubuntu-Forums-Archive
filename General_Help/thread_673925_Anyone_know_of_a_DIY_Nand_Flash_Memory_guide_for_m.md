### Post by geraldm on 2008-01-21
You are getting into the area of how to program Nand flash, which is very specific to the manufacturer of the flash.  The best places to look are in the manufacturer's documentation.
Some manufacturers may have an Application Note on using the flash with a microprocessor.
Other places to look are various hobby groups for a specific type of manufacturer,
