### Post by Mark Phelps on 2010-03-16
The colours in the bar at the top of your screen and in the dock at the bottom of your screen look OK to me.

That rules out a generic monitor or video card/chip problem.

I my experience with monitor problems, color problems manifested themselves either uniformly around the screen or in vertical/horizontal bands.  I don't see any of that in your screenshots.

Suggest you check with your detailed theme settings.  Those would affect various components in your screen while leaving the top bar and dock alone.
