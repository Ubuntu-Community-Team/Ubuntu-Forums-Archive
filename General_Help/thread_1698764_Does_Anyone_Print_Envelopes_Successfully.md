### Post by decora on 2011-06-20
I was able to get an envelope print on my HP LaserJet p1006 by following these instructions: 

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=86179](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=86179)

Basically, "Insert", "Envelope", "Format", "#12 letter", "New Doc", "Printer/Center Feed"

Then in Open Office, I noodge the 'From' box to the right and up (by clicking on the rectangle and dragging with the mouse)

I noodge the 'To' box to the left and up. 

I increase the font-size and change to Arial to help the post office OCR machines

After a few test prints (face up, opening to the left), i got it.
