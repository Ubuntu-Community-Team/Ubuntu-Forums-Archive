### Post by tuxxy on 2008-07-30
You have two options, firstly you could press ALT + F2 and enter the command here, this way it wont be affected by the terminal.

Your other option is navigate to **system > prefs > sessions** , here you can add the command to your sessions so that it will be auto run at bootup, I recommend this way to save you the time of having to enter the command again.
