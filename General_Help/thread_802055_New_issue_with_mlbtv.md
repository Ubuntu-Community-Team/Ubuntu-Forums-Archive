### Post by trumpcouptimmy on 2008-05-24
It took me just a little bit, but for what it is worth, I have mlb.tv working on 8.04. Since it worked using just the live cd for pclinuxos-gnome edition and for linuxmint (which is based on ubuntu), I gave up blaming silverlight and thought there must be a way. 

Install the SeaMonkey internet stuff. When I started mlb.tv in seamonkey, it went for about a quarter of a second and then the video froze. I knew I was close. On the picture, I right clicked and chose configure and upped the cache in gecko. It's not 100% perfect, but it does work. If anyone finds some improvements, please post.
