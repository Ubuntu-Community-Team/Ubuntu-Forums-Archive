### Post by poodoopealeoaph on 2011-04-11
One thing you should try is to shine a flashlight on your screen after boot and see if it is just that the backlighting is not working. If that is the case then you can navigate to the monitors tab and make sure that you have the correct one selected. If that is all okay then you should just go back to your drivers app and deactivate your new drivers.... For some reason ati/amd cards can be very hit and miss with the drivers. So, even if it does look a little... off. it may be better to deal with that than to have a driver that craps out on bootup.
