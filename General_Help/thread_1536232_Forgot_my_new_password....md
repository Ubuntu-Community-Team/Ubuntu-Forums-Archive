And "It didn't work" is my number one pet peeve when replying to help given to you. Be very descriptive about what didn't work. Instead say

"I tried the instruction given by *insert name* and I couldn't get it to work, I rebooted my computer and never got the grub menu with different boot options to show up."

Saying "it didn't work" gives us nothing to go off of to continue helping you with your problem since that above solution DOES work, so you must have misunderstood a part, or something is truly borked on your system, or various other issues, knowing what exactly didn't work helps us, help you.
