### Post by Tibuda on 2009-05-25
Right-click the applications menu, and choose "edit menu". Find your game, and click "Properties". Click on the icon, and then you can choose your image.

### Post by CatKiller on 2009-06-22
> **addiebk said:**
> Only images in the .svg file format show up when I do that. I can't get it to use a .png (or any other image type).  :(

Yes you can. Click on Browse. Find the directory that contains the image you wish to use. Select that directory and click the Open button. You will then be presented with a list of images in that directory, not just SVG images.
