### Post by Mark Phelps on 2010-09-23
You didn't ask, but in case someone drops by and suggests you can achieve what you want by installing the proprietary drivers -- don't listen to them.

You're running the open-source drivers at present and that's all that is available for that card and current versions of Ubuntu.

If you get pressured into forcing an install of the proprietary drivers, that will only hose up your display and force you to spend a LOT of time restoring the drivers you're already running.

Just a cautionary note ...
