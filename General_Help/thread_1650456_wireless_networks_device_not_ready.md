### Post by jingaling on 2010-12-21
Hi Nick,

Welcome to the forums and also to the amazing world of open source! ha ha.

It sounds to me like your wireless device isn't working because Ubuntu doesn't have the correct drivers natively. The easiest way to fix this is to plug your device directly into your router via a cable then click System > Administration > Additional Drivers.

From here ubuntu will check the online repositories for an applicable driver for your device. Once it does, click on the driver then click activate. Ubuntu will then install the drivers and ask you to re-boot. Once you re-boot - hey presto! Wireless networks are available.

If the above doesn't work then please reply to this thread so that the community can try to help you some more.
