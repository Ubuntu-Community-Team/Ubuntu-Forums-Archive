### Post by vbmark on 2014-09-24
I have a thin panel on bottom.  When I a remoting in via x2go via one PC I can see the bottom panel.  But when I log in from the other PC I have x2go use a different resolution.  As a result I cannot see the panel across the bottom of the screen.

If I go into screen settings and layout the resolution matches what I have entered in x2go.

So what's going on and how do I resolve this?

Thanks!

---

### Post by vbmark on 2014-09-25
Here's my workaround:

Alt-F2
Type display
Change resolution to something random and when it asks if I want to keep the change I say no.

Bada-bing-bada-boom the resolution is fixed.
