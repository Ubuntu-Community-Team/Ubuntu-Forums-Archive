### Post by Jucato on 2006-02-10
What version of KDE are you using? You could try to upgrade to the latest version (3.5.1). It might be one of the bugs that was fixed.

To make Firefox (or anything else) as your default browser, go to KControl > KDE Components > Component Chooser > Web Browser then choose to open http and https "in the following browser:" and type in the command to launch your browser (firefox or opera or whatever).

Hope this helps
