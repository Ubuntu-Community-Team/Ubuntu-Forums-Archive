Just a quick tip that I had so much trouble figuring out.  When you are inside the PartImage program trying to change the options...TAB between the entries and use SPACEBAR to select or unselect an option.  The spacebar part is the one I had so much trouble figuring out as its not really made clear.  Hope this saves someone some time
