If you go to System -> Administration -> System Monitor and go to the "System" tab (should be the first one), it will tell you all of that information, including your Ubuntu information, your harware, and your remaining disk space... (where did 30gigs of my HD go? :) )  

If you go to the Resources tab, it'll show you how much of those you're using.

Enjoy.
