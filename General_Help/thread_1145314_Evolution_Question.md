### Post by codeseer on 2009-05-03
> **anw0625 said:**
> does anyone know how to convert WAB and DBX for evolution or is thunderbird better?  Thanks!!!

Thunderbird won't import them either. The best option for your situation would be to go back to the original outlook client you have the emails and address book at and export them to .csv format. If you no longer have that client or emails, then you can import the .wab and .dbx to a fresh outlook and then export them to .csv. CSV is pretty much the open, widely supported, format for things of that nature.
