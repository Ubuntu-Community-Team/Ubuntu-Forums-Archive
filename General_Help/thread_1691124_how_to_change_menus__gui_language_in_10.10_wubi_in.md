### Post by Rubi1200 on 2011-02-19
Have you tried this:

System > Administration > Language Support > Install/Remove Languages > Hebrew (place a check mark and choose to install)

Once it is installed, you can choose Hebrew at the login screen (there is a menu at the bottom of the screen). Haven't done this, but it should work.
