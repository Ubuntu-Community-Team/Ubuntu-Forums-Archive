### Post by Portmanteaufu on 2010-05-04
Both "gedit" (for gnome) and "Kate" (for KDE) are great, simple editors. They both do syntax highlighting (showing keywords in various colors) and they both have the tabbed layout. If I recall correctly, gedit comes installed under "Accessories" in the Applications menu.

Give those a shot and let us know if you find something about them lacking.
