Try going to your desktop, right clicking, clicking on "Change Background", click on "Theme" tab, click the "Customize" button, click on "Color" tab,  where is says "Selected Items", under the column "Background", choose any color you want. I think that should fix it. If not try changing the Theme, or Windows Borders.
