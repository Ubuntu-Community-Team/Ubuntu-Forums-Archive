### Post by jfinkels on 2007-04-25
I think you need to add the Window List back to your panel. Right click on the panel, choose "Add to panel..." and select "Window List".

By the way, you have a very outdated version of Ubuntu! The newest is version 7.04. Once you are ready, you may want to upgrade to it, as it might be more stable with your hardware, have more up-to-date software, etc.
