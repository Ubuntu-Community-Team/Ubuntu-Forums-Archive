### Post by pay on 2007-01-18
> **sloggerkhan said:**
> Is it just me or does XGL perform a lot better than AIGLX?AIGLX is built into xorg and performs better, but the radeon driver (opensource ati driver) doesn't perform aswell on some cards than the fglrx driver because ati have't released the specs for the cards. So if you were running an nvidia card for example aiglx would seem faster than running the same driver under xgl.
