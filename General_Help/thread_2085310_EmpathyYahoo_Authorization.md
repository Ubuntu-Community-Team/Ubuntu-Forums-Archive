### Post by cmoineo on 2012-12-18
In fact that doesn't come from empathy but from yahoo.
i already had same problem yahoo messenger on my smartphone, so I guessed what was happening!
When you change your connection, computer id, way of connection (not so clear from yahoo), you have to authenticate your account connection.
1- make your account in empathy, and you have the authorization request.
2- open a browser window, and go to yahoo mail login
3- enter you login and password, you'll have same authorization request!
4- answer captcha, answer question... eventually you'll receive an email with a code to copy&paste
It's done. You are authenticated, so you empathy is also.

If you have this problem with a smartphone with yahoo messender, same procedure to be done.
