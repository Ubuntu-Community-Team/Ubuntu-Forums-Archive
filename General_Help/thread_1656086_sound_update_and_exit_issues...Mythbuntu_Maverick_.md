I attempted to get some help on the update portion of this post in the updates section, after several days with no replies, I thought I would post here with the other issues that I am having...

1) Applications>System>Users and Groups>(Select your user name)>(password)>Advanced Settings>User Privileges>Check "Use Audio Devices"

(Also, you may wish to check some of the other boxes, look at them, for example, the floppy drive was also disabled on mine, etc.  If you use any of those other devices, several are disabled...)

This solved my problem...I found this solution for an older distribution, I am just reposting it here in case someone else finds it useful.
