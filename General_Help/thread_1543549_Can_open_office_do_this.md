### Post by WorMzy on 2010-08-01
You can add a table of contents into a Writer document by navigating to Insert -> Indexes and Tables -> Indexes and Tables; but I'm not sure about adding hyperlinks to all the entries -- you may have to do that manually.

Make sure you're using correct formatting, for headers at least, as that's what the table of contents picks up on.
