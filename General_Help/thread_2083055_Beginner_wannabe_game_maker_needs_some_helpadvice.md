### Post by thnewguy on 2012-11-11
Step one: Learn to program, preferably in C/C++. If you do a search on amaozn you'll find severl good books on learning C.
Step two: Learn to use third-party libraries, like SDL. ([http://libsdl.org](http://libsdl.org))
Step three: Learn to do character modeling.

It may take a few years to get the basics down, but it can be a lot of fun once you get the general concepts down.
