### Post by Sorivenul on 2009-02-02
This is rather vague. 

Perhaps you can be more specific in what programs you are trying to combine, and also why. In general, you can't just toss two pieces of code together and expect them to work. There is also the matter of the licensing of the two pieces of software and if the licenses are compatible, etc., etc. It may be best to take things slowly, if possible.

1.) How experienced are you with programming in general?
2.) What languages have you worked with or are you working with?
3.) Is this for homework? (The help we can offer is limited if it is...)

Also, this may be better suited for the folks over in the Programming Talk subforum. Don't double post, but if you have further, more specific questions, that would be the place to go.
