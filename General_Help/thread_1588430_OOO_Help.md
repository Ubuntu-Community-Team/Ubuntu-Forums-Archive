### Post by Thomas_Bates on 2010-10-04
The prompt you're getting asks you what content in the cell you want to delete. This is so if you've got both Text and Numbers in a box (I'm not sure why you would have this), you can delete one without the other. If you want everything gone, just hit Delete All.

That is what you're stuck with unless you use cut, as far as I know.
