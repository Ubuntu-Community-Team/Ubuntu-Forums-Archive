### Post by Mark Phelps on 2011-11-01
Yes, we DO mind -- about your repeated bumping.  The rule around here is no more often than once every 24 hours.

So basically, you encrypted your drive, didn't bother to record the key in a safe place, went ahead and did a Distro upgrade -- knowing that you had no backup of the data on your drive -- and NOW you're distressed that you can't get to your data! Right?

This is what encryption is for -- keeping folks out of your data.

There is no way around the encryption; if there was, there wouldn't be any point in doing it in the first place.
