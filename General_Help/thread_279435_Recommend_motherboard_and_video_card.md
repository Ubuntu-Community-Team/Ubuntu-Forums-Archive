### Post by Cable on 2006-10-18
I'm not too knowledgable in terms of the specifics of motherboards, but video card choice basically depends on what you are going to be using the computer for.  If for gaming, you'll need a higher end video card.  Also, you'll probably want to go nVidia rather than ATi.  I've heard of lots of problems with Linux and ATi cards.  Although, for whatever it's worth, I have an ATi 9800 XT and have had no problems with it at all.
