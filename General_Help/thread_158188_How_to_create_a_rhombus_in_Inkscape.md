### Post by graigsmith on 2006-04-10
well, theres not a rhombus tool, you can make a square tool with the square.

and then use object to path (ctrl-shift-C)
then you can move each edge around, or select 2 edges, and move them to make a rhombus.

edit, to make a perfect rhombus do this. draw a square, and then rotate it to a dimond shape while holding ctrl. then you can use scale to make whatever rhombus shape you want.

### Post by cwaldbieser on 2006-04-11
[QUOTE=monomaniacpat]That's the one! A trapezoid - how do I make one?[/QUOTE]
Here's how I would do it:
1) Draw a rectange.
2) Convert nodes to paths.
3) CTRL + drag corners to angle the sides.

A trapezoid is simply any 4 sided figure with 2 sides parallel.  If you want the legs to be the same length, (making it look symmetrical), I would angle only one side, then copy and flip it horizontally.  Paste the  two together.
