### Post by tredegar on 2011-01-09
I don't have this problem (2 users most of the time, one wired, one wireless, both ubuntu).

The thing is that your ubuntu PC will grab as much bandwidth as it is offered, and how the available bandwidth is offered and shared between the users on your LAN is a function of the software in your router, not your individual PC.

My router is a netgear dg834g and seems to spread the available bandwidth fairly between connected users. Maybe try (borrow?) a different router?

If you are downloading a file then the rest of the internet will normally slow down for *that* PC, because the DL is hogging *your* bandwidth, but I can't say I have noticed for it to bother me.
