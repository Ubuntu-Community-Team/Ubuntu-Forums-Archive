### Post by iaculallad on 2008-07-12
While booting the Ubuntu 8.04LTS LiveCD for testing and loading the the GNOME desktop, try to browse your windoze NTFS partition (particularly your My documents, Documents and settings). If you could successfully browse this folder (w/c I know you could), then, there's no need of installing other devices. Just install Ubuntu (using unused partition) and let it manage your ntfs drive.
