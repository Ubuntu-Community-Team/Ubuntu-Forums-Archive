### Post by mb_webguy on 2009-02-20
[Medibuntu]("http://www.medibuntu.org/") is an official repository that can't be included in the official distribution of Ubuntu because of legal reasons like copyright restrictions.

[Launchpad]("https://launchpad.net/") is an officially recognized collection of unofficial mini-repositories, called Personal Package Archives, designed specifically for Ubuntu.  These are maintained by individuals or groups which may have no affiliation with Ubuntu, though many people associated with Ubuntu have PPAs on Launchpad.  Many software projects also distribute their software through Launchpad PPAs.  Remember, though, that while officially recognized and sponsored by Canonical (the company behind Ubuntu), they are unofficial repositories not maintained by the Ubuntu team.
