### Post by offgridguy on 2012-10-01
If you installed alongside it will be in a separate partition. If you  go to the previous link
it will show you where to bring that up.  By all means follow the steps if you do uninstall
as it will corrupt your MBR {master boot recall} and you wont be able to boot any system on
your computer.  I just went through this myself about 2 weeks ago.  The steps are there at 
that site to restore the boot recall.  The short explanation is you will be able to wipe the
partition where ubuntu is installed from your windows 8 side, but don't shut down or leave until
you have done the boot repair.
