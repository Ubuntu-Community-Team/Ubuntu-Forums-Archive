### Post by TheFu on 2014-02-19
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1974241&page=2](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1974241&page=2) seems related.

On my AMD/ATI box, I have to reinstall the GPU drivers after every kernel rebuild.
sudo apt-get  --reinstall install fglrx xorg-driver-fglrx
fglrxinfo 
reboot

Do this without any GUI running or the reinitialization of the fglrxinfo won't work.  The reinstall does work however and might be enough.
