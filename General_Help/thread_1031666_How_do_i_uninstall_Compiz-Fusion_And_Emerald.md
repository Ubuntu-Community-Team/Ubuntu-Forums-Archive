### Post by Titan8990 on 2009-01-05
Compiz is installed on Ubuntu by default, it would be best not to remove it. You can disable it by right clicking on your desktop -> change desktop background -> Visual effects -> change to "none".

From that same menu you can disable emerald and re-enable the default Ubuntu look which I believe is "human". 


To remove Emerald completely:

sudo apt-get remove emerald
