### Post by KCormier on 2009-07-11
Did you install vbox through ubuntu?  Ubuntu distributes VirtualBox OSE (open source edition) which doesn't support usb passthrough as far as I know.  You need to head over to their website and grab their version released under the PUEL (personal use & evaluation license).

They've also just released a new version (3.0).  Personally I've had a little bit of trouble with 3.0.0.  I've found it to be a little bit buggy.  They released 3.0.2 yesterday and I haven't had a chance to try it yet, but considering 3.0 was released only a week or two ago, it will probably receive a few updates relatively quickly.  If you're looking for absolute stability I'd say try version 2.4 for at least a little bit longer while 3.0 matures a little.

On the linux download page on their site just grab the deb for your version of ubuntu and run it.  Ubuntu will open it with gdebi and it should install without a hitch.  No compiling or anything necessary!

Hope that helps!
