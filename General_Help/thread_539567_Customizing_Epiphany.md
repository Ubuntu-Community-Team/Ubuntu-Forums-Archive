to make epiphany look like that you will have to use gconf to edit epiphanys entries, there you will be able to hide the menu bar.  as for customizing the toolbar, rightclick the back/forward/etc buttons and uncheck show back, forward stop, then you will just have to move your booksmarks toolbar to the left of the address bar
