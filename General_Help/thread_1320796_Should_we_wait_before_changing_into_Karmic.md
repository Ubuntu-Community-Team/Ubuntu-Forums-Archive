### Post by QIII on 2009-11-09
There is never a problem waiting and using an old gizmo that works for you until you are sure that the new gizmo will work the way you want it to.

But remember three things:

1.  Karmic uses GRUB2 and ext4 by default.  Neither of those will "update" if you update rather than install fresh. (But you can update them manually.)

2.  Basing your assessment of the quality of the OS on the problems you see others having because they come to the forum for help is skewed.  People don't come to forums looking for help because things are going right.  All has been well with my installation, for instance.  And I had very few issues with the Alphas, the Beta and the RC.

3.  As stated above, you can use the LiveCD in a live session (it will be slow) to really exercise anything you want to.
