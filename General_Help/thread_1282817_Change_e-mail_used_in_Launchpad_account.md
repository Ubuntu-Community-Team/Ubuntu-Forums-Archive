### Post by zeroseven0183 on 2009-10-09
Actually YES, you can change your e-mail address.

Login to your Launchpad account and once the page [https://launchpad.net/~](https://launchpad.net/~)*yourusername* has loaded, you will see there your User Information. Immediately below that text is your registered e-mail address. Change it or remove it by clicking the yellow button that looks like a pen.
