### Post by loomsen on 2008-09-25
Pardon?

I compiled kiba without any complications a couple of times during the last few weeks. The only thing missing atm is the akamaru physics engine, but even this one is installable through svn without any problems.
Dont get me wrong, I've got both of them installed, and both of them running at the same time (- well, sometimes...)
Plus, me is a really degenerated and lost eye candy victim and addict, so that Cairo should be my first choice. Anyway, it is not, due to its a little lame responsiveness from time to time. And thats the problem with all docks imho. I expect a dock/panel to be the program with the highest responsiveness possible amongst the ones I have installed, similar to my webbrowser. There's nothing worse than a leaking webbrowser, like for example some time ago when IPv6 drove me nuts... leakin responsiveness plus to chewy browsing, in Opera as well as Firefox, nearly kept me completely away from the internet. Same, or actually even more important if its up to panels/docks. I tested AWN, Sim-Dock, kiba and cairo so far (and another one I can't remember the name... well, i guess that should be nuff said about it, whatever it was called)

AWN bored me pretty fast, like 3-4 days it took me to start searching for another dock. Tho AWN is very stable and offers high-responsiveness as well as many nice plugins, I still don't get why there can only be run one instance of it, centered at the bottom of the screen. Imho 2-3 plugins less, and the ability to run more than one instance, as well as placing it on the screen where I'd like to would have been a much better decision. But well, quite some time ago now... Guess I'll give it another try, tho I wouldnt if I had to compile it from source, wouldn't even be worth the five minutes it would take imho.

So, the longer it takes the kiba-guys to reimplement the akamaru engine, the better I expect it to be in the end. And for those who cant wait, theres still rev 602 I think it is if I remember correctly, which can be checked out from svn and is the last before akamaru was removed. 

So, I'd prlly say give em all a try, while cairo and kiba are pretty equal imho. kiba lags akamaru, tho cairo lags responsiveness. AWN is not a choice for me anymore.
