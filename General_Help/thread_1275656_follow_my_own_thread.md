### Post by Iowan on 2009-09-26
A couple more options:
If you are logged in, use the forum Search. The last option is "Find all your posts", the one above it is "Find all your threads". A less direct route is to click on your username where it says **Welcome, username**. That will open your profile. "Statistics" button presents an option to "Find all threads started by username".
