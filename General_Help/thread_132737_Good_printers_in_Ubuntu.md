### Post by stuporglue on 2006-02-18
There are so many printers and they're always being discontinued etc. so it's hard to say for sure. 

What I'd recomend doing if it's possible, is going to walmart and writing down the name of your top three or so choices, then looking at [http://www.linuxprinting.org/](http://www.linuxprinting.org/) to see which is best supported. 

That said, I have an HP 5150 which works very well with Linux, and a LaserJet 5L which also works great.
