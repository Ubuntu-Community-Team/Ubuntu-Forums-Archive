### Post by garryknight on 2009-12-23
Maybe it started up minimized to your system tray. Look for a green icon with a white checkmark or a grey icon with an X. Click that and the main skype window will open. Now you can go into your options (click the white S on blue icon at the bottom left) and uncheck the "Start Skype minimised to the system tray" option on the General tab.
