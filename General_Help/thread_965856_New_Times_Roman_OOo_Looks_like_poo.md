### Post by shane19174 on 2008-11-01
Does it print normally, or is the spacing problem present on paper too? If everything's fine on a hard copy, then it's probably just the zoom setting on screen (i.e. whether OOo is showing your document at 75%, 100%, 150% or whatever). I've found that quirks often appear with a lower zoom rate but disappear at higher ones.
