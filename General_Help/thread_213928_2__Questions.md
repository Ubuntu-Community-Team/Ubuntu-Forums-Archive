### Post by 3rdalbum on 2006-07-12
Loserboy, I've gotta say that I love your signature. I really sympathise with it :-)

I'm encouraging the opposite of what people tend to do. People tend to try something with Linux, find it doesn't work, then send off a complaint to the manufacturer, which is promptly deleted from the inbox.

What I want people to do is, when they find that something WORKS with Linux, they should e-mail the manufacturer and congratulate them for having a product that works with Linux. Since it's positive feedback, they're more likely to read it. Since it shows that people use Linux, they're more likely to write drivers for the products that DON'T work.

For a letter template, see: [http://wiki.ubuntu.com/TellThemAboutIt](http://wiki.ubuntu.com/TellThemAboutIt)
