### Post by ryanhaigh on 2008-05-26
You aren't likely to get much of a response to issues such as this on the forums. The ubuntu devs have a much better system for tracking bugs through launchpad. Here is the page which I would guess is relevant to your issue: [https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/gnome-power-manager](https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/gnome-power-manager). You might want to have a search through the existing bugs for your issue or create a new one. You never know there may even be a solution to the problem in the bug report/responses.

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/ReportingBugs](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/ReportingBugs)
