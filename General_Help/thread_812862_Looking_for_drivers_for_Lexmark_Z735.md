### Post by Sef on 2008-05-31
Lexmark is known not providing drivers for GNU/Linux.  

HP has the best printer support for GNU/Linux.  Read Open Printing's [suggested printers]("http://www.linuxfoundation.org/en/OpenPrinting/Database/SuggestedPrinters").  In Ubuntu, most HPs will work OOTB (out of the box: plug it in and it will work.)
