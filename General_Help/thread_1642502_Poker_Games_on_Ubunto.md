### Post by amauk on 2010-12-10
The version of pokerTH in Ubuntu is a bit old (0.7.1)
You need the latest version to play online

Goto
System > Administration > Synaptic Package Manager

Then
Settings > Repositories

On the "Other Software" tab,
Click "Add"

Paste the following into the box```
ppa:pkg-games/ppa
```and click "Add Source"

On the top menu bar,
click "Reload" and then "Mark all Upgrades"

Click "Apply"

You will now have pokerTH 0.8 (the latest version)
and should now be able to join an internet game
