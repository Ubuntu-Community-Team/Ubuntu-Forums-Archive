### Post by spartan777 on 2007-06-21
so you downloaded a "yourdistro.torrent" file? that isn't the actual distro. You need to get a torrent client to download the .torrent file. a good one is Azureus. you can get it on synaptic in ubuntu, or just off their internet site for windows. when you're in azureus, go to File->open->torrent_file and look for the .torrent file. then you'll have the .iso downloaded, and then you simply burn it!
