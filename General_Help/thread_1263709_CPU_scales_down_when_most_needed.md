### Post by loupy on 2009-09-11
Has anyone else had the issue where the CPU freq scales down when encoding media (e.g. dvd:rip, handbrake, audacity)

I have tried disabling CPU freq scaling all together and have had no success - so when I most need my dual cores @ 2.4ghz, I only get 800 mhz.  This is very frustrating.

---

### Post by 3rdalbum on 2009-09-11
It indicates that your CPU is not being cooled properly. It is scaling down to prevent itself from having to shut down. Check that your fans are running, check that there's no dust in your case, on your fans or in the dust filter. Check the thermal interface material that sits between the CPU cooler and the CPU, and reapply.
