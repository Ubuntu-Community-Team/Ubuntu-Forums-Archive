Hi, after I installed Hardy Heron, my screenlets keep moving.  I move it to a particular location on my desktop and lock its position, but when I restart my computer, it just goes back to its original position.  Does anyone know how I can fix this?

Also, my AWN weather applet doesn't seem to be fetching the weather data.

### Post by sayakb on 2008-06-01
Place them to correct position and Goto System->Preferences->Sessions. Then click Session options and **check** the Automatically remember... checkbox. Logout and login again.
