### Post by posburn on 2008-11-30
Yes, the template pack is what I meant.

As for installation:

Click on the "more information" link at the bottom of the description for the template pack.  On the next page, click the "Get It!" link - this will download a *.oxt file. Then, open up any openoffice program (e.g., Writer), click on "Tools" in the top menubar, then click on "Extension Manager".  In the dialog box that opens, click on "Add", then navigate your way to wherever you saved the *.oxt you just downloaded.  

Voila!  Templates.  :)
