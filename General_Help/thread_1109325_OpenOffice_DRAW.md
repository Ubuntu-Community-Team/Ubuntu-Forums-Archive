### Post by TheBuzzSaw on 2009-04-09
Be patient when waiting for a response. Posting after yourself won't make it happen any faster. ;)

Just do a Google search for free font downloads. There are quite a few all over the Internet. When you find several you like, put all the font files (.ttf and .otf files) into a folder in your home folder called **.fonts** (don't miss that period at the beginning of it), and that will enable those fonts for just about all your applications.
