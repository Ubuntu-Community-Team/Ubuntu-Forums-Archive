### Post by Eric Qel-Droma on 2008-11-02
I just found this in another thread.  Does this solve your problem?

Hit Alt-F2, type gconf-editor and then navigate to /apps/gnome-power-manager/general and unselect can_suspend and can_hibernate.

This worked for me in 8.10, but my shut down menu still offers suspend and hibernate--they just don't work.
