### Post by Jucato on 2006-12-03
In Kubuntu's Add/Remove Programs, you have to check the box for unsupported and commercial to show packages for universe and multiverse respectively. You also need to select KDE, GNOME, or Any Suite from the drop-down list to see other packages.

But afaik, Both GNOME's and Kubuntu's Add/Remove Programs use the same database/list of apps. They just display things differently.
