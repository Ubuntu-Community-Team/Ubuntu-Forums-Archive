### Post by sandyd on 2015-02-07
Lightscribe.com is created by HP.

HP has stopped supporting LightScribe (See [https://web.archive.org/web/20131126231444/http://www.lightscribe.com/default.html](https://web.archive.org/web/20131126231444/http://www.lightscribe.com/default.html))

While it is no longer supported by HP, LaCie still seems to have support. Someone needs to update the wiki page with the appropriate information.
[https://www.lacie.com/us/support/drivers/driver.htm?id=10081](https://www.lacie.com/us/support/drivers/driver.htm?id=10081)
[https://www.lacie.com/us/support/drivers/driver.htm?id=10061](https://www.lacie.com/us/support/drivers/driver.htm?id=10061)

You will still have to have the lightscribe packages that are on the closed site to get lightscribe working.
