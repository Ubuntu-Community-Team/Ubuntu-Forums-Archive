### Post by yetiman64 on 2010-03-28
Hi aankhr0,
Attached is a screenshot of an external HDD I use for storage (ext3) with only 14MB free on a 120 GB HDD. It works perfectly even though I'm due to upgrade it as its a backup for my Linux Storage partition files. By my reckoning its about 99.9998 % full and still working.
Ext3/4 partitions are far less prone to fragmentation then FAT or NTFS partitions which require the user to leave at least 15% free for the defrag programs use.
