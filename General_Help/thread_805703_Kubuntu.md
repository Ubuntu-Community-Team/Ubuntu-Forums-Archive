### Post by dabang on 2008-05-24
As it says:

"kubuntu-desktop" installs latest KDE 3.59

"kubuntu-kde4-desktop" installs KDE 4

Be aware that both packages will install Kubuntu's splash screen, too.

It should be no problem installing both. You can choose between both KDEs then.

### Post by Zorael on 2008-05-24
KDE 4 is more or less a rewrite ("refacturing" would perhaps be a better word for it); stuff is being redesigned and rebuilt from the ground up, with ambitions soaring high. As such, many areas haven't gone through enough testing, yes. And others still need fleshing-out and completion.

It's currently at version 4.0.4, but I wouldn't use it in a desktop environment until 4.1.
