### Post by jpoRS on 2008-11-03
Hey,

I am not 100% sure what your question is.

If you are looking for basic pre-installed features, they are in System>Preference>Appearance Then under the "Visual Effects" tab select either Normal or Extra.  While these aren't the most flashy effects, they are the easiest on your computer and should work without any problems.

If you are looking for more high end stuff, check out [UbuntuGuide]("http://ubuntuguide.org/wiki/Ubuntu:Hardy#How_to_enable_Compiz_Fusion_in_Ubuntu"). Compiz-Fusion is the heir to Beryl and will give you all the "pretty" you can handle.  Unfortunately it can be labor-intensive, and requires a bit of know-how to get it to work fully.  If you are a beginner with Linux I advise that you wait until you are more comfortable with everything.  But if you feel comfortable with Terminal, and are sure your computer can handle it, jump right in.

Does that answer your question?

Good luck,
jim
