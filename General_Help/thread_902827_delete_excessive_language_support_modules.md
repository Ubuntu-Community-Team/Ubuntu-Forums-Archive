### Post by squaregoldfish on 2008-09-25
I wondered this as well, and you inspired me to dig around to see if I could find out.

In the event, it's quite easy. Open up the GNOME Control Center, and select Language Support. You are then presented with an enormous list of available languages - simply uncheck the ones you don't want, and click Apply. It takes a few minutes to go through the list and uncheck every box, and a good while to uninstall all the languages, but it works.

Steve.
