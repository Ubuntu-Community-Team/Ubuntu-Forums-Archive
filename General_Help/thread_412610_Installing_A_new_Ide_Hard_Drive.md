### Post by dcstar on 2007-04-18
> **devil2 said:**
> I Currently have 2 hard drives installed one 10 gig and one 40 gig which with my cd recorder and dvd recoreder is using all my ide connectors what i want to know is how do i go about installin a 3rd hard drive without taking off any operating system or deleting anything i understand i can get a connector to run a 3rd drive just dont want to lose any files off previous drives please please HELP !!!!!

An IDE port will only support two devices, two IDE ports will only allow a total of 4 devices to be connected.

Since DVD recorders are also CD recorders, I suggest you remove the redundant CD drive from your system and install the new hard drive in its place (using the same Master/Slave setting as the CD drive it replaced).

I would also remove the extra CD recorder. If you need to keep it you could remove it temporarily, install a large drive, transfer all your data from your storage drive to your new large drive, then remove the old storage drive and re-connect the CD drive.
