Freemind broke when I upgraded to breezy. So I removed the files, then went to the Freemind site ([http://freemind.sourceforge.net/wiki/index.php/Main_Page](http://freemind.sourceforge.net/wiki/index.php/Main_Page)) and downloaded the "binaries for any operating system." The new version is better: prettier, faster, and plays well with Breezy.
