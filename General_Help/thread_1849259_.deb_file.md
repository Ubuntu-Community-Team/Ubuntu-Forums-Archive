### Post by dino99 on 2011-09-24
you dont need to do that, instead follow the virtualbox rule:

Add ( sudo gedit ) the following line according to your distribution to your /etc/apt/sources.list:

deb [http://download.virtualbox.org/virtualbox/debian](http://download.virtualbox.org/virtualbox/debian) natty contrib

then update
