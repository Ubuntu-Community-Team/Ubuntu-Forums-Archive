### Post by drmrgd on 2011-12-23
Probably one of the best tutorials around is [Psychocat's Tutorials]("http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/index.php").  There are specific instructions in the "Playing Around" section which show how to install other DEs on top of Ubuntu.  Also there are instructions on how to remove a DE if you don't like it anymore.

This may bloat up your system a little with redundant programs and processes, however, and will be a little different than running pure Kubuntu for example.  But, it works pretty well, and it's fun to switch around from time to time just to keep things fresh.
