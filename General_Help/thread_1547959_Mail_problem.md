### Post by dv3500ea on 2010-08-07
You need to give more detail about what type of mail accounts you are trying to set up. The configuration you need is in Edit -> Preferences. Select the account you are having trouble with and click 'Edit'. You need to ensure everything in the 'Receiving E-mail' tab is correct. It may help if you tell us the provider of the email accounts, eg. 'gmail'.

As a side note, you should check your keyboard configuration, as it appears caps lock is constantly enabled.
