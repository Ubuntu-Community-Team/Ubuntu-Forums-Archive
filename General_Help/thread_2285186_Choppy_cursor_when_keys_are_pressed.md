### Post by alextheatomsmasher on 2015-07-03
Hi, I'm having a bit of a problem on my MacBook Pro.  Whenever I hold down a key on my keyboard (using a USB-based one or the built-in one) while I move my USB mouse, the cursor movement is very choppy.  This may not seem like a problem normally, but it unfortunately makes most video games unplayable. I am currently running Ubuntu 15.04, and the problem does not exist when I use my trackpad.  I've also tried a different mouse but the behaviour stays the same.

EDIT: I have solved the issue by removing the **mouseemu** package. Everything works fine after this, no restart required.
