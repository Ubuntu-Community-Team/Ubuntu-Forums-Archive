Welcome to UF.
Have you checked the MD5SUM of the downloaded .iso?
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HowToMD5SUM](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HowToMD5SUM)

If that checks out, try pressing the Esc key when booting from the cd (before the purple slpash screen appears). You will get another screen up with options. That screen may be covered with a language choice screen. Choose language then on the original screen choose "check cd for errors" and see if any are found.
