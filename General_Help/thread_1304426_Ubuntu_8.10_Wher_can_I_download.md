### Post by alphaniner on 2009-10-29
LTS stands for Long Term Support.  It means that version will be supported with updates longer than a regular version.  The version name is in the form of <year>.<month>.  If you are wanting to upgrade to 9.10, install 9.04 which was the previous version.  Better to wait and just install 9.10 though.
