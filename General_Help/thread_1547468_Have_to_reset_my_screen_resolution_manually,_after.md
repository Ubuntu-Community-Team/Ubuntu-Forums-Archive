### Post by dreamsR4living on 2010-08-06
Did it myself, with this howto; [http://www.ubuntugeek.com/ubuntu-tiphow-to-removeinstall-and-reconfigure-xorg-without-reinstalling-ubuntu.html](http://www.ubuntugeek.com/ubuntu-tiphow-to-removeinstall-and-reconfigure-xorg-without-reinstalling-ubuntu.html)

After this howto I installed;
```
xserver-xorg-video-nouveau
xserver-xorg-video-nv
xserver-xorg-video-vesa
xserver-xorg-video-fbdev
```

for my Nvidia card. I also did a reinstall of nvidia-current.

Things are fixed and smooth now.
