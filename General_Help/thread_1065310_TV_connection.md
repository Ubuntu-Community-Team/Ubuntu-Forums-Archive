### Post by mb_webguy on 2009-02-09
Have you tried going to System->Preferences->Screen Resolution and clicking Detect Displays when the TV is hooked up?

If that doesn't work, you may have to manually add the monitor by editing your xorg.conf file (which someone else will need to help you with).
