### Post by gambothell on 2011-05-08
I was very getting frustrated with Ubuntu 11.04 unity on my netbook (Lenovo S10-3). I do not like how many clicks you have to do to get an application running. I then switched to classic, but again not totally impressed. The netbook had no problems running the os, but I really did not like the interface. So, I started looking at other distros. I looked at Fedora 14, Puppy Linux, and Open Suse. I kept coming back to Ubuntu and thought I may just go back to 10.10 until I tried Xubuntu 11.04. Folks, it has everything you need for a netbook. I downloaded Rhythmbox to replace Parole Media Player. I also downloaded Google Earth and some MySQL tools. Once I realized Gigolo would connect to my Window Shares, I was a very happy camper. I also have a MySQL server running on another computer and can access my databases very quickly with Xubuntu. The performance is very fast overall and the footprint is small. So, those of you who have a netbook and are not sure about Unity, my advice is to try Xubuntu.:D
