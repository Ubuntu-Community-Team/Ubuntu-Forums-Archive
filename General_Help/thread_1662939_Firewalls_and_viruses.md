### Post by dFlyer on 2011-01-08
I've not had a linux virus or been hacked since I've been using linux. I first installed RedHat back in 1995 or 96. Ubuntu has a build in firewall. Go to any of the web sites that offer free firewall testing, test it. If you do want to use a antivirus program do a google search for avast4workstation_1.3.0-2_i386.deb. It's a free antivirus and also has a GUI with it. There are also several antivirus programs offered via synaptic. Must antivirus programs for linux are mainly to protect your friends from receiving windows virus that you may have received via your email you forward.
