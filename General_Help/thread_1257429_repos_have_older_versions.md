### Post by drs305 on 2009-09-03
Ubuntu seeks stability by not doing major package updates other than security and bug fixes. A new release is introduced every six months which will introduce newer versions of apps after they have been thoroughly tested by Ubuntu maintainers.

There are ways of getting newer versions, as you have found, through external sources or by enabling the 'backports' and 'proposed' repositories.

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Repositories/Ubuntu]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Repositories/Ubuntu")
