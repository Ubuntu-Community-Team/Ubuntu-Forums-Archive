### Post by QIII on 2019-08-26
HP's products are fine.  Aside from an issue with some previous models that did not adhere to UEFI standards, now fixed in most models, HP makes good products.  Anything can break.

The issue is likely to be one of two things:  a failing keyboard or a corrupt installation.

You can test the former by changing keyboards (using an external USB keyboard if that's a laptop, perhaps) to rule out hardware first.
