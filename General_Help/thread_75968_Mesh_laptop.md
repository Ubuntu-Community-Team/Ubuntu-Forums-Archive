Hi, I'm now a happy user of Breezy, and I use a "Mesh Dynamo Plus WSXGA+. It appears it's quite a hot question as to which laptops work well under linux, and I though I'd just mention that in my case, this laptop works as good as perfectly under linux! Sound, wifi, graphics, laptop keys, they all work with no user-intervention (apart from installing the nvidia drivers via synaptic). I'm a very very happy bunny, and just to try to make it not look like I'm only here to advertise a laptop brand, I'd like to pose a question: Is there anyone else in the world out there using linux on a Mesh laptop?? I can't find any instances of it on linux-laptop.net or similar sites, and if other machines of theirs also work well, I think people should have a good hard look at their range!
