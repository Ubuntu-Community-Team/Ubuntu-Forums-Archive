### Post by haqking on 2011-11-30
the canonical domain canonical.com belongs to canonical the owners and developers of Ubuntu (Ubuntu belongs to Canonical)

People is a sub-domain of Canonical.

As for .debs i cant see any there but you should always use trusted repos yes.

Though repos can become compromised, trust does not imply 100% security and so always be aware of what and where from and why.
