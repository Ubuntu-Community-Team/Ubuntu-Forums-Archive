### Post by Pyrosopher on 2011-06-02
Under all variants of Natty I have tried - Unity, Mint and Pinguy - my screen resolution will spontaneously change to 1024 x 768 from its usual 1366 x 768 when I press a key on my laptop.

Most of the time I use a remote logitech keyboard on my Samsung R520 which has integrated Intel graphics. However, when I press a key on the laptop keyboard, the screen blanks and changes to the lower resolution or occasionally crashes. After a few seconds of not touching anything the screen usually returns to 1366 x 768, but not always.

I looked at the monitor setting screen when it last happened and it didn't say "Laptop" as the selected monitor and "unknown" in a smaller box (I don't have a second monitor hooked up). Instead, while the error occurred it read "Mirror screen". 

On boot the screen can also be at 1024 x 768 and then changes to the larger res. Again, sometimes it doesn't and only goes to 1366 x 768 after I've logged in.

This is a new problem, not one I've come across in any previous version of Ubuntu I've tried.

Any suggestions? Anyone else have this issue?
