### Post by AlexBellisBrown on 2008-10-09
Nope, 8.04.1 LTS (Long term support) basically means that security patches, and bug fixes will be officially released by Canonical, up until 2011. The server version, which is different to the desktop version is supported until 2013. You either have one or the other, and depending on which you use, support will end on the respective date. :)
