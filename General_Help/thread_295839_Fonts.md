### Post by bugz0r on 2006-11-08
I downloaded [these fonts](http://www.osx-e.com/downloads/misc/macfonts.html) and would like to load them, how do I do this?

---

### Post by Kingsley on 2006-11-08
copy them over to /usr/share/fonts/truetype. i think you have to run as root though.

That's a good solution if you want to have the fonts to be accessible when logged in as root, but otherwise copying fonts into the hidden ".fonts" folder in your home directory is fine too.
