### Post by zdux0012 on 2006-11-17
I think you can get any system from dell. You just have to hassle with the managers. There was a story about this just recentally. Dell might try to sell you a linux system, but I don't think they can do this legally. 

When you purchase from Microsoft you are asked to agree to a set of rules, typically called EULA. End Users Licence Agreement. 

If you do not agree to this, then you cannot use the software. (Don't click "I Agree"). So when purchasing a computer from *anywhere* tell the buyer that you do not agree to the EULA. If they sell the OS seperatly demand that they take the same cost off of the price of the computer. They won't want to do this because they don't actually buy windows for what they are selling it for. But they also do not want (or can't) tell you the agreement between themselves and Microsoft. They probablly spend around 50 bucks (american). 

This is always a difficult issue but in the end linux users will win this battle.
