### Post by olever on 2006-05-30
Hello,
you should make sure that your usb drive is selected as 1st boot drive in the CMOS of your motherboard bios (award, phoenix, ami....) 
press del during the post (beep) when you start your pc and enter the cmos to set your usb drive as 1st boot.  ;) 
Hope this will help.
