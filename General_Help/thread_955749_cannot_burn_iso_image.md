### Post by mgmiller on 2008-10-27
I suspect from your description that what you have is a DVD reader and cd/cd-rw burner.  If that is the case, it can burn cd's and cd-rw's, but not DVD's, as they would be read only on your hardware.  

These devices were pretty common a few years ago before DVD/CD burners got really cheap.

Do you have the actual make and model number of the drive by chance?
