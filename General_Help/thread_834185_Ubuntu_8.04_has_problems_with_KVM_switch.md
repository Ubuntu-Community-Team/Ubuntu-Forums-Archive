### Post by cmnorton on 2008-06-19
Is this computer a laptop? 

KVM switches are tricky. This could have something to do with the graphics driver you or the installation chose; the monitor type you selected; or something more complicated. If changing any of those settings does not work, I would pull this unit out of the KVM, and make sure it works just the way you want it.

Also, I suggest getting your KVM manufacturer involved in the support. I found Belkin to be very helpful, even though they could not help me. I had a much serious problem than you describe, however.
