### Post by mike115 on 2013-10-05
Wow can't believe I fixed this on my own... 
I started searching around in the forums here and saw some posts about people having issue with their displays.  Most were fixed by getting into there graphics driver controls and adjusting over or under scanning.  While digging in on that I found that it needed to install some drivers as well as the catalyst control.  Then I followed the instructions in the link here and away I go ....

[http://wiki.cchtml.com/index.php/Ubuntu_Raring_Installation_Guide#Installing_Proprietary_Drivers_a.k.a._Catalyst.2Ffglrx](http://wiki.cchtml.com/index.php/Ubuntu_Raring_Installation_Guide#Installing_Proprietary_Drivers_a.k.a._Catalyst.2Ffglrx)

Just wanted to provide an update.  After receiving some system updates the other day my display was stretched again.  Some how I think the updates must of turned the AMD drivers off or something.  I uninstlled the drivers and reinstalled them and now I am back up and running.
