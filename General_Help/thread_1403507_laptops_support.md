### Post by patchwork on 2010-02-10
[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/HardwareSupport?](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/HardwareSupport?)

Also, some manufacturers are shipping laptops with Ubuntu pre-installed, such as [system76]("http://www.system76.com") and [dell]("http://http://www.dell.com/content/topics/segtopic.aspx/ubuntu?c=us&l=en&cs=19"), to name a few.



EDIT:   Glanced over the fact that you wanted 9.04 or 10.04, not 9.10.   Most of these systems should work with 9.04, and will probably work with 10.04.  However, since 10.04 is still in Alpha stage, there is no guarantee for that.
