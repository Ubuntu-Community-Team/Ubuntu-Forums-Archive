### Post by orb9220 on 2007-06-27
> I tried to post a pic of this but couldn't figure out how to, 

just click on Go Advanced button which will show the manage attachments.
Make sure the pictures fit the guidlines and are not to large. It will show you sizes when you open attachments window.

For the panel icons have you right click and see that they are unlocked for each? If one is locked next to one that is unlocked you will still not be able to move it until you unlock every one between the one you want to move and the position you want to move to.
