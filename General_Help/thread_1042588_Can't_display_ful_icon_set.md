### Post by FAMUgolfer on 2009-01-17
1. right-click on the icon that you want to change...whether it's on your panel or on your desktop
2. at the bottom click on properties
3. then click on the "basic" tab....it should already do this by default
4. right under the basic tab is a box with the current icon in it
5. click on this box which will cause another browser to open up
6. search for the location of the desired icon you want
7. after you have found this click open or double-click on it
8. now the icon in the box under the "basic" tab should switch to your desired icon and simply exit out

Good Luck!
