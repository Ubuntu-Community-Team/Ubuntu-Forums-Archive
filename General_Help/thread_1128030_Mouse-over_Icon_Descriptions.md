Is it possible to have the desktop icons give a detailed description on mouse-over of what the program is? I mean besides changing firefox to web browser or evolution to email icons. ### Post by devosion on 2009-04-17
When you create your launchers just make your comment field as descriptive as you'd like. Upon mouseover the information placed into the comment section will be displayed. When I tested it out I was able to easily fit more than 4 lines of information so you should be able to make them to your liking.
