### Post by rbmorse on 2007-11-18
Sort by date: click in the bar labeled "date"  It's a three position rotary, no sort, ascending, descending. The "from" and "subject" bars work the same and Evo remembers the last selection. 

Message fonts: Edit > preferences > mail preferences > general.  It's the first item.
