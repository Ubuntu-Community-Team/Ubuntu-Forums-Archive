Just wanted to share that if you set your fonts to smaller sizes your ubuntu will look more polished and attractive, at least in my opinion.

Here is my set up under Prefs -> Appearance -> Fonts

App. font - Sans 8
Doc. font - Times New Roman 12
Desktop font - Sans 8
Window title font - Lohit Hindi 9
Fixed font - Monospace 8
