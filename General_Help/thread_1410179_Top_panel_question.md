### Post by mcduck on 2010-02-18
What options you *do* have when you right-click on the panel?

Note that you only get the option  to add new panel items, or to configure the panel, when you right-click on some empty space in the panel. If you right-click on any panel applet or item you get the right-click menu for *that item*, not for the panel itself...

### Post by mcduck on 2010-02-18
> **alvin_chan said:**
> From the right click drop down menu, I only have the following:

Preferences
About
Remove from Panel
Move
Lock To Panel


is it better to use the desktop version instead of the netbook remix even though I'm using a netbook ??

Based on that, you are right-clicking on some panel applet, not the panel itself.

(The panel obviously doesn't have options like "remove from panel" and "lock to panel", only panel applets have such options..) 

Depending on the layout of applets on the panel you might need to move some applet a bit to get access to the panel itself.

What comes to which is better on netbook, netbook remix or normal desktop, that's simply a question of which one you like more. Nobody else can tell you that kind of things. Also keep in mind that both are, in the end, one and the same Ubuntu so you don't have to choose between them, you can switch between the NBR remix and normal desktop as you like. (If I had a netbook I'd probably run Openbox on it, yet I'm definitely not going to claim that you or anybody else should do that, or that it would in some absolute way be any better than any other available option)
