### Post by satya_461 on 2020-09-25
I think I may have found solution for my problem from here [https://askubuntu.com/questions/780106/thinkpad-intel-graphics-issues-16-04](https://askubuntu.com/questions/780106/thinkpad-intel-graphics-issues-16-04)

```
sudo su
*password for su*
apt-get install --reinstall xserver-xorg-video-intel
dpkg-reconfigure xserver-xorg
apt autoremove
```
