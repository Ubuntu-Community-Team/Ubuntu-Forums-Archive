### Post by lmarmisa on 2010-09-18
I recommend Clonezilla Live ([http://www.clonezilla.org](http://www.clonezilla.org)). Download the Debian based stable version. This program works great both cloning disks or making backups.

You will need an equal or bigger hard drive in your new computer. Different devices (for example, different video cards) could hinder your migration.

Good luck.
