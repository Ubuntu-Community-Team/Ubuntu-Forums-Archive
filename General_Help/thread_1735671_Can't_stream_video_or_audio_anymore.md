### Post by bmathis on 2011-04-22
ClamAV wont help you with this problem since Virus are no really a problem for Linux, but if you really want to run it, then you will need to run it with sudo so it will have the ability to scan your entire drive.

What it sounds like to me is bandwidth issues. Your internet connection seems really bad which is causing the jerkiness of watching youtube videos. Check your internet speeds at [speedtest.net]("http://speedtest.net") and post back your results. 

If your speed is good, then it might be another program that you installed running in the background. You can try one of the apps in this [blog post]("http://www.powercram.com/2010/01/bandwidth-monitoring-tools-for-ubuntu.html") to try and see what is taking up your bandwidth.
