### Post by Mark Phelps on 2011-12-28
The link list mikewhatever provided is for graphics drivers.  You won't find links for processor drivers.

If your PC is freezing up, check to see if it's overheating -- the primary cause of freezing.  IF that's the case, graphics drivers are unlikely to fix your problem -- and you're another victim of the recent Linux kernel bug.
