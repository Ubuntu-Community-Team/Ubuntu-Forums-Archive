### Post by rubberglove on 2008-11-21
There are quite a few PPA (Personal Package Archive) repos with interesting packages as well. Note that these are almost always 'use at your own risk', but are sometimes well worth the risk.

Two that I use are:

(openoffice 3.0) [https://launchpad.net/~openoffice-pkgs/+archive](https://launchpad.net/~openoffice-pkgs/+archive)
(firefox 3.1) [https://launchpad.net/~fta/+archive](https://launchpad.net/~fta/+archive)
