### Post by tredegar on 2011-02-17
My guess is that after 10minutes of inactivity, something is kicking in that fails to restore things properly when you come back to your PC.

Like "Hibernate" or "Suspend" or "Sleep".

So, please click on Preferences... Power management
There are different tabs for AC & Battery.
Make sure it is set to "Do nothing" or "Never"
