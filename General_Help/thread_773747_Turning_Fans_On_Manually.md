1. Have you checked that you actually have those three extra fans?
Just because it is possible to connect three more fans, doesn't mean they actually are there. If nothing is connected, they will show up as not running.

2. To auto-shutdown your pc if it would get too hot, there may be some hardware monitor settings in the bios, where you can set what to do. My guess is that if you have such a setting, it is already set to turn off the computer.

3. Those temperatures look very good. As long as the cpu doesn't get above 70 degrees C during heavy load I think your cooling is good enough (but still I think 70 degrees C is probably not hot enough to harm the cpu).
