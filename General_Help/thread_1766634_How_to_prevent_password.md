### Post by ladlers on 2011-05-24
How do you prevent from having to enter a password everytime the screensaver comes on? It would be nice to just move the mouse.

---

### Post by 5149.5 on 2011-05-24
Go to system preferences and click the screensaver app. Uncheck the box for Lock the computer during screensaver.
