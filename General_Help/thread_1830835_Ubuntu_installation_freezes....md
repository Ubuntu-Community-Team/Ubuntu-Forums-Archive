### Post by Mark Phelps on 2011-08-22
It's generally a BAD idea to force an Ubuntu install on an untried PC.  You simply don't know what is going to work and what is not.

You would do better selecting the Try Ubuntu mode and waiting (maybe a LONG time) for it to boot into a desktop.  At least that way, you will see what works and what does not -- and you can come back here with the problems you found and, maybe, find solutions.

### Post by Basher101 on 2011-08-22
go to [www.pendrivelinux.com](www.pendrivelinux.com) and download the Universal USB installer and follow the steps to make a USB drive a Live USB. Then just change the bootorder in your BIOS or press F12 on bootup to choose your USB stick as the boot device.
