### Post by nerderello on 2012-05-17
using Thunderbird v12.0.1 in Ubuntu 12.04.

I found that for one of my regular email sources - Linux Forums - I couldn't get the "Always load remote content from ..." to work. I'd click on it and nothing would happen, although if I clicked on the "Show remote content" button, the images would load.

Turns out that I had the email address in my contacts/address book more than once (I've got all sorts of address lists from all over the place, that I've collected over the years). So I searched through all of the address books within Address Book in Thunderbird, and deleted all occurences. Then I clicked on "Always load remote content from ...", the "New contact for ..." window popped up, with the "Allow remote content" box ticked. And all is now working.

Sharing this in case anybody else bumps into this "problem".
