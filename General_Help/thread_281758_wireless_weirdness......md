### Post by lloyd_b on 2006-10-21
> If so this would make shipping a distro that simply grabs the most convienent dhcp lease over wifi and interesting proposition.

AFAIK - All operating systems will do pretty much the same thing.  The wireless card sends out "I want DHCP service" messages, and any router receiving it will (unless configured otherwise) reply to that message.  Ubuntu (or Windows, or whatever) doesn't have any real control over this, as the process is completely invisible to the OS.

So it's not like a case of breaking-and-entering.  It's more like somebody leaving their doors wide open and having a wild animal wander in and start eating their dinner :-)

Lloyd B.
