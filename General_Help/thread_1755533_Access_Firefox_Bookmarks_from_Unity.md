### Post by lovinglinux on 2011-05-11
I haven't played with Unity in order to provide a solution, but I would recommend that you introduce [Speed Dial]("https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/4810/") or [FastDial]("https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/5721/") to her. In my opinion, is a far better approach than saving bookmarks in the panel. 

You can set these extensions to load as home page and new tab, so all she needs is to start Firefox and then click the desired dial.
