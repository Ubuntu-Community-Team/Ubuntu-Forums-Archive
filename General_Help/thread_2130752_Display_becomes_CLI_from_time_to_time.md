### Post by tgalati4 on 2013-03-30
"Escalators don't fail.  They become stairs.  Thanks for the convenience."  Mitch Hedberg

It's possible that your graphics card is switching modes or failing.  Dropping to a command line is not entirely a bad thing.  It's like a spare tire that you can drive on--really fast.

Have you looked through your log files?  Since you are on a command line, how about:

```
less /var/log/Xorg.0.log
```  Spacebar to page forward, backspace to go back, "q" to quit.
