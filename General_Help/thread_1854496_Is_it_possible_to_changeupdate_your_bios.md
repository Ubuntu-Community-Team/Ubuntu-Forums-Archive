### Post by CompyTheInsane on 2011-10-04
It is possible to update your BIOS. Whether or not any BIOS updates are available depend on what computer/motherboard you have.
You can see if BIOS updates are available from the website of your computer manufacturer. If your computer is custom, you can check the website of your motherboard manufacturer.
