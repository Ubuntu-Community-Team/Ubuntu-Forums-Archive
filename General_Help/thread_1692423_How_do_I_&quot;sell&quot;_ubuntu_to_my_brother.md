### Post by scouser73 on 2011-03-17
Tell him that unlike Windows, Ubuntu doesn't suffer from viruses (none that are in the wild), Ubuntu doesn't need defragmenting, Ubuntu never slows down a PC.  Tell him about this fantastic community where people actually help others to solve their Ubuntu related problems should he have any.  Tell him that Ubuntu is always being constantly updated and that there is a release every six months and that once every three years there's an LTS (Long Term Support) release.  Tell him Ubuntu is free, show him what I've mentioned and then let him have a go for himself and let him get the feel for Ubuntu.  Tell him to try it out for two weeks, and not to use Windows in the meantime.
