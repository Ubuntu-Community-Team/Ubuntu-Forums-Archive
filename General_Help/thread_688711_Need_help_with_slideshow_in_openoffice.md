### Post by ajgreeny on 2008-02-05
Do you just want the pdf files as pictures or are you trying to get the text?  As pictures there should be no difficulty as you can select them and export them from some at least of the pdf readers available in ubuntu, eg kpdf, Adobe reader 8, and Evince, but the latter does not make it so obvious as the others.  In some, as you have found you can also export the text, but as far as I can see there is no way to keep the formatting.  Perhaps the best way is simply to take a screenshot of the pdf which can then be saved as a jpg or other picture format to import into OO.

A rather roundabout way I accept and will not let you edit the text, but it may be a useful way out for you.
