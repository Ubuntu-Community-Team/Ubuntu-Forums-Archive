### Post by bpilgrim1979 on 2005-07-29
Hello, I use both gedit and kate to edit text files.  

They both create tons of foo.txt~ files, which are swap/backup files of foo.txt.  I believe emacs does too.

Does anyone have helpful tips for these files?  They are kind of a pain when they show up in Subversion working copies.  As they are not entirely hidden like .foo files.

Could I have my .bashrc file put them somewhere else?  Thanks.

---

### Post by Sam on 2005-07-29
If gedit, go in Edit, Preferences, and uncheck 'Create a backup copy of files before saving'.
