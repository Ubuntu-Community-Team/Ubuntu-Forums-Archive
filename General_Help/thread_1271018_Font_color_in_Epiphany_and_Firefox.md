### Post by munkeh on 2009-09-20
Hi, I don't know if I am totally off the mark I am using the great excuse of still being only new. What I did was in Firefox 3.0.13 went to

Edit>Preferences>Content (at the top).
Next to fonts and colours went to the "Colours..." box and then made sure the "Allow pages to use their own colours instead of my selections above" box was ticked.

If this does not help I hope you get it sorted.
