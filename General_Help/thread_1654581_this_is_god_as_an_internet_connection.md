### Post by noah++ on 2010-12-28
Anyone who makes a wireless network can give it any arbitrary name. The person nearby who named his "This Is God" probably just thinks it's funny. It's very unlikely it is a malicious network -- and, in any case, it can't harm you if you don't choose to connect to it.

### Post by dodo3773 on 2010-12-28
If you do not connect to it then no. If you do connect to that network then yes your keystrokes can be logged. It depends on how their network is set up. Also, I would not worry about a virus in the traditional sense of the word. But, can your computer be exploited over a lan even though you are running Linux? Of course it can. If you have your own internet connection then use your own. Also, make sure that you have a good passphrase (a combination of letters numbers and special characters) on your router and use at least WPA2 yourself.
