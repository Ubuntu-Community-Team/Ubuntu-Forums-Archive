I've been having the same problem - [http://ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=11022423&postcount=7](http://ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=11022423&postcount=7) mentions that there's a problem with VLC's PulseAudio support, and the original poster solved the problem by changing the audio output module to ALSA. I tried it and it worked for me.
