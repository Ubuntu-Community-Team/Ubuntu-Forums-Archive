### Post by KPDXHAM on 2009-12-09
To make other Desktop short-cuts (Links) here's what I did:


[LIST]
[*]Click on "Places" located at the top left side of your desktop
[*]Click on "Home Folder"
[*]Then right-click on the folders that you want to have short-cuts to. This gives you a menu. Now click on the "Make Link." (Linux calls them "Links.") Now there should be a  folder next to the original but this one is labeled "Link to ..."
[*]Drag that folder to your desktop. The one with the curved arrow.
[*]That's your desktop short-cut icon.
[*]I renamed mine after moving them to the desktop. I took off the "Link to"
[/LIST]
Example : Music instead of Link to Music.
