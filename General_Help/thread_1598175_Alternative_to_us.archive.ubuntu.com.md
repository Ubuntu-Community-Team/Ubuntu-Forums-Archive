### Post by 3rdalbum on 2010-10-16
System > Administration > Synaptic Package Manager

Settings > Repositories

Click on the "Download From" popup menu, select Other... and then click on the "Find Best Server" button. All these are reasonable Ubuntu mirrors, and this button will find the fastest one for you.
