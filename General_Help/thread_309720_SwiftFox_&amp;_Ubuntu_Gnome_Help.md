### Post by RAOF on 2006-12-01
He's abrasive, but he's right.  You can't remove Firefox and still use the help browser, because the help browser is depending on a library that Firefox provides rather than the Firefox browser as such, and Swiftfox won't be providing the correct library.

That said, there's no reason why you can't reinstall Firefox and still use Swiftfox.  Just reinstall the **ubuntu-desktop** package, and you'll get everything back to normal.
