### Post by MaxIBoy on 2011-01-07
Ubuntu is [Free Software](http://www.gnu.org/philosophy/free-sw.html), meaning:

[LIST=1]
[*]You can use it for whatever you want, for profit or not.
[*]You can study and modify the source code.
[*]You can redistribute it (or even sell it- you are allowed to sell copies of Ubuntu without permission or without paying royalties.)
[*]If you made any changes or customizations, you are also allowed to redistribute or sell those.
[/LIST]
Pretty much, you are allowed to do almost anything.
