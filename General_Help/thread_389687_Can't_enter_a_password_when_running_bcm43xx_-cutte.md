### Post by hikaricore on 2007-03-21
I think you may just not be seeing the text, which is normal.

The password entry when using sudo is set at default to remain blank, so someone can't look
over your shoulder and guess your password by the number of characters you've entered and
things like that.  :)

Sorry if I'm mistaken, but alot of people don't notice this at first.
