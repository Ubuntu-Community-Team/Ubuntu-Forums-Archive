### Post by chitowner on 2009-02-20
I am using kopete 0.12.7 and kde 3.5.10 in hardy and I was trying to get a sound to signal incoming IM messages. I have seen other posts on this topic saying to click settings > Notifications and choose settings for sounds.

This mystified me for quite a while because the "MESSAGE" window has a settings tab, but it does NOT include anything for notifications or sounds. None of the forum exchanges that I saw specified that one must use the settings tab in the "MAIN" kopete window (which includes the "files" tab!).

I specify this to make it clear to others who may have experienced the same conundrum. None of the other forum messages that I found made this point.

People: please be aware that (probably the majority of) users who browse forums for help are there for one reason: they are inexperienced. When a description of 'go here click this' is given, let's try to be aware of peripheral details which might be confusing by their /omission/!
