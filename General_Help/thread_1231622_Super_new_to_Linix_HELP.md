### Post by 3Miro on 2009-08-04
I am hardly an expert on this, but rule number on this (and other) forums is to first search before you ask any question. Odds are that someone has already asked that same question and someone has already given the answer, it actually is much faster for you. Also there are tons of HowTo tutorials that would teach you how to set up your system step by step without assuming you know anything (there is an entire sub-forum filled with those).

Second rule is: be specific. If you have a question, be as specific as possible, what when how, what did you try, what exactly are you trying to achieve and so on. It is much easier to ask a specific question and then receive a specific answer, then to ask a vague question and then post 10 times back and forth "did you try this - yes it did not work - what exactly happened - message such and such - .... ".
