### Post by kurt18947 on 2012-08-02
You should be able to select your DE when you first log on.  To the right of your user name is a little symbol.  Try clicking on that and it should give a list including Ubuntu(Unity) Ubuntu 2D and Gnome.  You want Gnome.  Not Gnome classic or Gnome classic - no effects if it's listed, that looks and mostly acts like gnome 2.  There is also floating around somewhere a respin of Ubuntu with gnome-shell only, no Unity.  I tried it but saw no advantage over Ubuntu with gnome-shell added.
