### Post by Larkspur on 2011-05-21
The backlight around the launcher wanting your attention will glow a bit more than usual.  This could be a problem if you have Backlight Always Off in Compiz.

### Post by linuxinstalledfromhdd on 2011-05-21
Click on top left corner of Unity. Type in the search box for Login.  Unlock. And select from the dropdown menu Ubuntu Classic. And exit to  save. Reboot to make sure the changes have taken effect.
