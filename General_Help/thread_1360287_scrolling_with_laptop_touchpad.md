### Post by rCXer on 2009-12-20
Under System -> Preferences -> Mouse.  On the "Touchpad" tab make sure "Enable Touchpad" is checked.  Also make sure "Enable Vertical Scrolling" and/or "Enable Horizontal Scrolling" are checked.
