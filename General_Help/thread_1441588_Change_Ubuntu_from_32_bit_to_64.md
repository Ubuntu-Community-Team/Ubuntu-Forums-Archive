### Post by Arand on 2010-03-28
The easiest way to do this switch would be to backup settings and configurations and reinstall from fresh.

Switching architecture would mean replacing pretty much the whole system in terms of packages etc. so no.

If you only want to access extra memory, have a look and PAE: [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/EnablingPAE](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/EnablingPAE)

- Arand
