Do you mean the download of Ubuntu or Blender?  If Ubuntu, download the torrent file.  Any torrent client will allow you to pause and resume a download.  They'll even let you specify the download rate if you like.

If you mean Blender, you can download it directly from the homepage and use a normal download manager for that.  I don't think you can easily pause and resume downloads from the package manager.
