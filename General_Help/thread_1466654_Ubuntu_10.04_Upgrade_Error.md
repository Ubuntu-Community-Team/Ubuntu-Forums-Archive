### Post by koanhead on 2010-04-30
Sounds like launch-day blues to me.
The servers that provide the updates tend to get crushed under the wave of demand on the first day of a new version of Ubuntu. You could try choosing a different server from System->Administration->Software Sources or (if you are brave) use debtorrent.
