### Post by &amp;KyT$0P# on 2017-05-20
Should be.  Just completely quit Firefox, then copy your [FONT=Courier New]~/.mozilla/firefox[/FONT] into [FONT=Courier New]~/.mozilla/firefox[/FONT] on the live USB.

---

### Post by ardouronerous on 2017-05-20
Thanks that worked :)
I compress my .mozilla folder into a zip file, saved it on a spare USB, unzipped it on Lubuntu's home folder, now I have my addons, bookmarks and settings loaded.
