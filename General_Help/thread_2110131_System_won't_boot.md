### Post by Impavidus on 2013-01-29
Moving files within a partition defaults to real moving, from one partition to another defaults to copying. Only in the last case data is actually moved.

Use your live session to check whether the moved files have really disappeared from the full drive. If they haven't, make sure they're present on the new drive, delete them from the old drive and empty your trash. Your boot failure could have been caused by a full drive.

Edit: Actually, what happens by default may depends on how you do it exactly, but I think moving from one partition to another may lead to copying.
