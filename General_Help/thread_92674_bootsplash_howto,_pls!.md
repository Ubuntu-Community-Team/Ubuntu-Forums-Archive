### Post by Haegin on 2005-11-20
To change the ubuntu usplash image you need to make up a png image that is 640 by 480. It also must contain up to 16 colours. (To do this in the gimp use Image>Mode>Indexed and set the number of colours to 16). You then have to convert the image to an odd format and place it somewhere specific. Full instructions are available on the wiki here: [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/USplashCustomizationHowto](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/USplashCustomizationHowto)

I just follow that whenever I want to change. If you need any help just ask.

Hope that helps,
