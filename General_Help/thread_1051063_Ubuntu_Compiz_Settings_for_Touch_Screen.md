### Post by Melk79 on 2009-01-26
I'm not sure how you have your desktop setup, but if you have a panel at the top, or anywhere for that matter, you could right-click on it, Add to Panel, and add the Workspace Switcher.  Then you would just touch those small boxes. Likewise, if you have Avant Window Navigator installed, you could activate the Shiny Switcher under Applets.

Enabling Edge Flip Pointer under Desktop>Rotate Cube of CompizConfig Settings Manager would also allow you to drag your finger to the edge of the screen and it would flip (I think).
