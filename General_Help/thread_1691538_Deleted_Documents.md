### Post by Rallg on 2011-02-20
Were you Root at the time you deleted files? If so, then the material might be in your Root Trash folder, rather than your user Trash. Yes, those are separate locations. If they are in Root Trash, then you might have to be Root again to get them out, and (big nuisance) you might have to change permissions on the files, so that you (as ordinary user) own them again.

Did you try showing hidden files?

What about your backup folder?  Most programs automatically create backups - look at your config and see where they place them.
