### Post by fabounet on 2008-06-04
Just to say that now Cairo-Dock can run without a composite manager (eg with fake transparency, like the gnome-panel or the gnome-terminal).
The desktop widgets of Cairo-Dock also benefit from the fake transparency, and it should work independantely on Gnome, KDE or XFCE.

It can be appreciable especially for small configs which can't run a compositor. :-)

(available in the 1.6.0 in few days, or on the SVN from now, run cairo-dock -F)
