### Post by mcduck on 2011-03-28
> **resetreflect said:**
> Yeah, if anything I might just install MacDrive on my dad's laptop to get into the Mac External Drive, open the zip on windows, and just put all the music in a folder instead of a zip. Hopefully it won't take up too much on the External Drive, it's pretty full.

Assuming your music files are in MP3 or other compressed format, then zipping them doesn't really make much of a difference anyway.

Zipping compressed data like mp3 audio, jpeg images or video files  is pretty much useless, whatever tool was used to compress the data in the first place is usually much more efficient at handling that exact type of data than zip is, and there isn't much extra data left...

Actually in worst cases zipping compressed data leads to larger file size.

(...and I agree with Dark_Stang, that does sound like a corrupted zip file.)
