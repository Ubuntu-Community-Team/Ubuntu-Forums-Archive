### Post by IanW on 2007-11-17
You need the "Alien" package from the repo's.
This will convert your .rpm package into a .deb which is what Ubuntu requires.

However, you'll almost certainly find newer drivers on ATi's website.

Or you could try Alberto Milone's "Envy" graphics driver installer from [here.]("http://www.albertomilone.com/nvidia_scripts1.html")
This will automatically download, install & configure the appropriate driver for your graphics card.
