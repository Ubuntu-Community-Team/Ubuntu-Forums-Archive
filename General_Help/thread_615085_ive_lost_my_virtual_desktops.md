### Post by kefurd06 on 2007-11-16
you've lost your workspace switcher panel item? u should be able to add it back:
right-click a blank area on the panel > click add to panel > scroll down to desktop and windows > select workspace switcher.

you can also switch to other workspaces by press ctrl+alt+LEFT ARROW or RIGHT ARROW.
