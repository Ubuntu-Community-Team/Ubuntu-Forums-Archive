### Post by Ub1476 on 2008-04-22
[Themes for your Ubuntu (Gnome) desktop is available on gnome-look.org]("http://www.gnome-look.org")

Every icon, metacity and GTK2 ("controls") is in a compressed archive which can be installed through System>Preferences>Appearance.

X11 Mouse themes, must be extracted to ~/.icons, then selected in Appearance.

Emerald/Beryl themes can be imported from the emerald theme manager.
