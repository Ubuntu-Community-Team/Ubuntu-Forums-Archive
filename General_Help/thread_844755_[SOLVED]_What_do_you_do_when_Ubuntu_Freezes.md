### Post by Dutch70 on 2008-06-29
If the control>alt>backspace doesn't work for you, I would definitely recommend using this, before a hard shutdow..:) I have used it a few times. and it is in the tips and tutorials section. 

Hold Down the alt+printscreen buttons, and slowly press these letters "r-s-e-i-u-b" pause for a couple seconds between each letter. your system should reboot when you hit the last letter.
