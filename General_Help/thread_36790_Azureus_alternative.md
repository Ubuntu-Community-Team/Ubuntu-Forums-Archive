### Post by ow50 on 2005-05-24
There is no well-working GUI multi-torrent alternative to Azureus.

If you don't need a well working app, try linux hacks of G3 or ABC.
If you don't need a GUI, there's a couple in the link of the previous post.
If you don't need a multitorrent app, there's bittorent, bittornado and gnome-btdownload.

Out of those only G3 and ABC resemble Azureus, and they don't work well on Linux.
