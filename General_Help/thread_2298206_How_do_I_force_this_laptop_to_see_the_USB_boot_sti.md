### Post by howefield on 2015-10-09
Your screenshot looks like the grub options for Ubuntu that is already installed on the machines hard drive.

After powering on and pressing the Esc key, you are looking for a screen that looks something like the attached screenshot... from there choose the F9 key to select the USB key to boot from.
