### Post by neu2buntu on 2011-07-08
newer versions of LMMS have fixed this issue, its my guess that you are using version 0.4.5? .
Anyway the cause of this problem is that ubuntu tooltips default to white text on a black background , but LMMS(older versions) and Hydrogen both have white backgrounds hardcoded into the program, so the white text wont show against a white background. to solve this you can either edit your desktop themes tooltip text color to anything that shows against white, rem you may have to change the background tooltip color too, or just choose another theme altogether.

goto  System: Preferences: appearance: theme: customise: colours: tooltips
