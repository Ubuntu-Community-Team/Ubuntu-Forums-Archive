### Post by guimaster on 2009-11-05
I just installed Xubuntu 8.04 on my Mother's IBM Thinkpad 290x tonight. Anyways, other than having to install Wicd because of Network Manager key-ring problems, the laptop was running fine until I got the bright idea to place the laptop in Suspend mode.

 Well now the laptop won't power off or on, and just sits at a blank screen. I held the power button on for over a minute and it won't power off! The laptop is turned on and when I place a CD in it will spin around but nothing happens on screen.

 I unplugged the power cord and I am praying that the laptop will go back to normal once the battery runs out of juice. Hopefully then it will boot up properly.

 Anyone else ever have this problem?

### Post by P4man on 2009-11-05
Pure shot in the dark: try closing/opening the lid.
I once read somewhere of a machine where closing the lid was detected, but opening the lid was not detected. Closing  the lid again acted like opening the lid. And I could at least imagine a "closed" lid would render the softpower button useless.
