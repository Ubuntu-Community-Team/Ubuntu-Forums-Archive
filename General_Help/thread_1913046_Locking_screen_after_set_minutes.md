### Post by ioldanach on 2012-01-21
I have several kids and I'd like to enforce a lockout of 5 minutes after they've been on the computer for one hour.  (So they are required to take a break, and so there's an opportunity for another child to get some time.)

Does anyone know of anything out there that would help me with this?  Ideally I'd like to present a warning at 55 and 59 minutes and then lock their screen (i.e., switch user or lock screen) after 60 minutes of use and not allow them to unlock it for 5 minutes.  I don't want to forcibly log them out because I don't want them to lose their work.

As a side note, it would be useful to also know how many minutes in a day they spend with their session open and not locked, to see how long they're using the computer for.  (Magically, whenever I ask them how long they've been on the computer, they always have exactly 15 minutes left, its amazing!)
