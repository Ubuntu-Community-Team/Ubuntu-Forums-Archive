### Post by papibe on 2011-06-27
The ability to boot from a CD or a USB stick depends on the hardware. You have set your BIOS to do that.

You have a couple of options here. Look very closely your screen when your computer boot and look for something like:
```
F12 to boot options
```
or something like:
```
F12 to setup
```
The particular key and text should vary depending on your computer brand.

If you can't get into the BIOS like that, you'll have to research how to do it. Your computer brand site would be a good start (like dell.com, hp.com, etc). Also google something like:
```
how get BIOS Dell Inspiron 1560
```
or something like that.

Good Luck!
