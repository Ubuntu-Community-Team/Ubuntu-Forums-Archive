### Post by johnc4510 on 2006-06-01
x-chat is in the universe repositories. If you haven't enabled the extra repositories:
System>Administration>Synaptic Package Manager
Click on settings, Click on repositories
Put a check mark in all the unchecked boxes and close the window
Click on Reload, Click on Mark all Upgrades, and Click on Apply
X-chat should now be there and ready to install
