### Post by terry_gardener on 2008-07-31
this is how to register for skype account. 

Download skype and install it. 
click applications-internet-skype 

Skype will open asking for you to login but just under the username field there is a hyperlink saying dont have skype account yet click this and register for new account. 

You do not need a credit card tok download or register with skype, you only need credit card if you want to make calls to mobiles or landline phones. 

hope this helps
