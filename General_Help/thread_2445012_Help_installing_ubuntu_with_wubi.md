### Post by howefield on 2020-06-08
Hello and welcome to the forums, you should probably forget about installing Ubuntu with Wubi as the project has long since become dormant and unsupported.

An [unofficial fork of Wubi]("https://github.com/hakuna-m/wubiuefi/") seems to be almost alive but I'd suggest sticking to a VM or a proper dual boot.
