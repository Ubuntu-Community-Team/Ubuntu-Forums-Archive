### Post by prem1er on 2009-10-09
And your keyboard and mouse are attached separately? My point is that sometimes your trackpad is a little more sensitive in Ubuntu and your palms might be touching it when you are typing, which could cause your applications to scroll.  If this is the case, there is an option under System > settings to turn off the trackpad while you are typing.

edit: It may not be caused by this, but it is most certainly not a virus.
