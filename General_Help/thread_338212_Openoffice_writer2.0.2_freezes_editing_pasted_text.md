### Post by Hagar Delest on 2007-01-15
Perhaps the paste included some weird HTML code. What I would do is to paste as *Unformatted text* (long click on the paste icon in toolbar or on the little arrow on its right). If there are pictures with the selection, paste them separetely. NB: don't forget to cut the link of the pictures to embed the pics (menu Edit>Links, select all and select *Disconnect*).
