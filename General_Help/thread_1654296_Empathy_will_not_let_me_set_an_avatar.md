### Post by yetiman64 on 2010-12-28
I set my avatar in Empathy by going to Edit > Personal Information, click on the "gray man". Point the dialog box to a png or jpg appropriately sized, and click close. FYI, I use a 95 by 95 pixel .png image which works for me.

Now above my status indicator in the indicator applet my avatar shows.
