### Post by gldearman on 2009-08-24
Have you considered a book?

The *Ubuntu Pocket Guide and Reference* is available for free download as a PDF [here]("http://www.ubuntupocketguide.com/index_main.html"), or you can buy a hard copy in any bookstore. It technically only covers 8.04 and 8.1, but almost everything in there is still valid.  The same author wrote *Ubuntu Kung Fu*.  I find that he gears his works toward new users who are just trying to get started with Ubuntu.

Of course, if you have any specific questions, ask them in this forum.  That's what it's for!
