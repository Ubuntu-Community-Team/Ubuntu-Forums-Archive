### Post by compuwatch on 2010-12-29
I assume you have your avi rips of the movies in your video folder. Go to DAMNVID and click on the add button. Choose your videos and convert them to the following standard.

Create a new profile: SONY

Video Codec = MPEG-4
Frames per second = 20
Bitrate = 256
Resolution = qVGA (320x240)
EVERYTHING ELSE LEAVE IN DEFAULT EXCEPT
Audio Codec = AAC
Audio Bitrate = 96khz
Sampling=22050
Channels=2

OUTPUT FORMAT = MP4 format (.mp4)

Once the conversion is finished you find your SONY videos in the Videos (DamnVid) folder. Copy them to your walkman and enjoy!

Hope this helps,
Hans
