### Post by deadflowr on 2017-03-03
> **ahawkua said:**
> So how do I just share to my local network?

Simplest way is to open the Files program and right click on any folder.
Then select the Local Network Share.
When you click on the checkbox to start the sharing, it'll prompt you to install the needed software to use the feature.
Once that needed software is installed, that folder, and any other folders you want to share later on, will be automatically accessible to anyone you want it to be on your local network.
