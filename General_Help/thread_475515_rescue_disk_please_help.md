### Post by ryu kun on 2007-06-19
I solved my problem.

I used **Hiren's BootCD 9.0**, particularly **DiyDataRecovery Diskpatch 2.1.100**, an excellent data and partition recovery software.

I have recovered all my ext3 and ntfs partitions and saved all my valuable data.

Now I have come to my senses and decided to get a backup unit. :)
