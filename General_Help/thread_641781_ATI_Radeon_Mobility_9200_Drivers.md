### Post by MoLE on 2007-12-24
I have a mobility radeon 9000 series card in my laptop and it works beautifully with the most up-to-date kernel drivers (the ati driver).  It's capable of AIGLX which means you can run compiz and openGL applications fine.

So, in short, you don't need to update the drivers.  You automatically have the latest version  for your kernel.
