### Post by xrdguy on 2009-01-11
I guess you got your screen resolution changed. if that is the case then go to system > preferences> screen resolution and change the slide bar to increase the screen resolution unless you are confortable with or reaches the max screen resolution of your computer. if your laptop has resolution of 1200x800 which is think mostly mini laptops have and if you dont see anything more than what your native screen resolution is then you have to install video card driver. But i think you dont have to go that far. simply increasing the screen resolution should fix your problem.

### Post by blackened on 2009-01-11
This is a problem common to system dialogs especially on netbooks or low vertical resolution monitors (happens alot on my eee). 

To be able to access those hidden buttons you can hold alt and then drag the window to make the buttons visible.
