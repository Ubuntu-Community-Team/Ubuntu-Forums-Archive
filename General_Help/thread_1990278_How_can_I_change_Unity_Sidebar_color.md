### Post by jerome1232 on 2012-05-29
It changes to match your background, unfortunately (well I kinda like the automatic matching my wallpaper thing) due to a bug, it will override any custom colors and revert to it's default of trying to match your wallpaper.

Here is the bug report [https://bugs.launchpad.net/unity/+bug/975350](https://bugs.launchpad.net/unity/+bug/975350)
