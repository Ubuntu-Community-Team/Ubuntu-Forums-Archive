### Post by jsmidt on 2005-12-31
The trash can starts out by default on the panel.  Is there a way to get it on the Desktop.  Drag-drop does not seem to work.

---

### Post by 23meg on 2005-12-31
In Applications / System Tools / Configuration Editor enable this key: apps/nautilus/desktop/trash_icon_visible .
