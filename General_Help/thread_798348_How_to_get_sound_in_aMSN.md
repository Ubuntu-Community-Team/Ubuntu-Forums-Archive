### Post by angry_johnnie on 2008-05-18
Try going to 

Account > Preferences > Others

and then go down to the **sound server** option.

By default, it says **play $sound**.

Change that to **aplay $sound**.

Worked for me :-)

You may have to exit aMSN in order for it to take effect.
