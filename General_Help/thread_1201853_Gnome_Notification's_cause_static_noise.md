### Post by ajgreeny on 2009-07-01
Check in System >Preferrences > Sound and in the Sounds tab you may find something you can disable.  Alternatively look in the startup applications and disable the Indicator applet.  Maybe that is the noise producer.  It has no description of what it is in the startup apps dialog, but it is intended to eventually take over from the notification area, as far as I can make out, and at the moment is not really needed.  I have it disabled, anyway, with no apparent problems, and I know others do as well.
