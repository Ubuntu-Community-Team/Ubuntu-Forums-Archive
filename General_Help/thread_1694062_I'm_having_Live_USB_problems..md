### Post by crh0831 on 2011-02-23
F12 sounds like the "Boot Options" key on a Dell?  Are you sure your machine supports booting from USB device?

Assuming the Live USB was created successfully you may have to go into your BIOS to check your boot options and make sure that 1) your machine supports booting from USB and 2) your USB device is the first boot device.
