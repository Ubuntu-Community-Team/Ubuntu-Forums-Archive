Ok, first of all, there are a whole bunch of ways to alter the sound in Linux.  :)
Have you first tried opening the full volume controls?

Right Click on the Speaker in your panel.
Left click on "Open Volume Control"
Now, make sure you do not have any sliders at the bottom, or anything muted.  Volume and PCM control the normal sound output from your computer - so if PCM is down really low, you'll have very low levels of sound.

Also, there are different ports for you to plug your speakers into the back of your computer - typically, it goes light green to light green, but you might want to try other ones as well.  There might be a volume dial on the back of your computer as well, you'll want to make sure that's turned up.

Now, about the mp3s... what have you done to try and make them play?
