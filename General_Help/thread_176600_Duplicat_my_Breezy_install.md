### Post by endersshadow on 2006-05-15
Your best course of action will be to either use a freshly burnt CD of Dapper or your old Breezy CD and create a dual boot machine (there's an option to automatically set this up during installation at the partition section).  If you choose to do it with Breezy, simply follow the dist-upgrade procedure there.  I would recommend you do the dist-upgrade on the clean install of Breezy rather than on the customized one.
