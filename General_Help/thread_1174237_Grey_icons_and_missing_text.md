### Post by ajgreeny on 2009-05-30
Two possible solutions, though neither may work.
1.   If you are using compiz 3d desktop, disable that and try again.
or
2    Go to Firefox Preferences and in the Content page choose the Fonts and Colours section Advanced and deselect the box to let pages choose their own fonts.
