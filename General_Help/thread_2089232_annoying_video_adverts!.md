### Post by evilsoup on 2012-11-28
Two Firefox add-ons: NoScript blocks all javascripts on a website unless you add them to a whitelist; and Adblock Plus, which blocks most adverts that NoScript misses (even those in youtube videos).

### Post by SeijiSensei on 2012-11-28
