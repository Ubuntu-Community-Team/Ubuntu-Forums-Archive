### Post by Holger_Gehrke on 2018-05-15
Have you tried alt-drag (position mouse cursor in window, hold alt on the keyboard, hold left mouse button, move mouse, release lmb, release alt) ? In most window managers this allows moving windows beyond the area you can normally position them in,
