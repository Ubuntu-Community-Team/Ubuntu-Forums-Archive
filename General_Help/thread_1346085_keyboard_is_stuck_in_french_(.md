### Post by IllegalCharacter on 2009-12-04
Go to System->Preferences->Keyboard.
Click the Layouts tab.
If you don't see a "USA" option, click "Add" to find the USA model and use that. Otherwise click USA and click "Apply System-wide".

### Post by StuartN on 2009-12-04
> **Rainbow-C said:**
> a tiny keyboard icon where I could change it back and worth from french to english.

If you right-click in a clear area in the panel at the top-right of the screen (the strip where the clock is) and select "Add to Panel" then you can add the Keyboard Indicator applet. This works exactly like you would expect.

You can also go to System->Preferences->Keyboard and choose Other options in the Layouts tab. Here you can choose a key combination to rotate through your keyboard layouts - I use Both Shift Keys Together as well as the default Both Alt Keys (a combination that does not work).
