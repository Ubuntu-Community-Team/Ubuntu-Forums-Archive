### Post by ajgreeny on 2008-11-14
What hardware do you have, and is the machine going into hibernation or suspend, from which some computers find it very difficult to return to life.  If that is the case, just set the system not to suspend or hibernate in System->Preferences->Power management at least for now, until you find if they can be used successfully in your machine.
