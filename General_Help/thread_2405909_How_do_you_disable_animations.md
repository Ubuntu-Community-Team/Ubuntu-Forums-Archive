### Post by Dennis N on 2018-11-13
> **jacatone said:**
> I'm using Ubuntu Mate 18.04 64-bit. Is there a way to disable the fancy fade in and out effects? Had that ability in Windows and it makes a big difference in performance.

Look in Mate Tweaks in "Windows" section for settings than stop or minimize animations:
1) Be sure "enable animations" is not checked.
2) Try different window managers. Marco (no compositor) is most restrictive, I would think. It probably even removes window shading. You need to try them to see. OS may require restart when switching these.
