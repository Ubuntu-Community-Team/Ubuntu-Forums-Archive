### Post by logos34 on 2007-06-10
> **XxSnipedYouxX said:**
> Thank You...will installing Ubuntu get rid of xp? Will I be able to be hit F8 and choose which OS I want to use? I know I'm a newbie :p.

No, it won't get rid of XP, only shrink/resize it to make room for ubuntu.  You can use the guided or manual partitioning, or just use it in live mode without touching your hard drive until you are sure you want to install.  After it's installed, every time you boot you get a Grub menu where you can choose linux or xp, no need to hit F8 or anything.
