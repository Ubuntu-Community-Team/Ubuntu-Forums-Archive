### Post by synapsys on 2009-06-09
michy is right. RAM is supported by your motherboard not by Ubuntu. Make sure your RAM will work with your motherboard and you will be fine. 

If you are unaware of what type of RAM you need, check your motherboard's QVL (qualified vendor list) from the manufacturers website. Alternatively go to crucial.com and enter the model number of your computer or motherboard.
