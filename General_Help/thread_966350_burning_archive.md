### Post by strider1551 on 2008-11-02
Two solutions come to mind.  The first is to create one massive archive, then split it, and put it back together later. [[COLOR="Blue"]This post[/COLOR]]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=2922104&postcount=3") explains how to do it.  The downside is that the split archives will be unreadable until they are joined back together.

The other option is to use a script I wrote myself called tarlimit, available [[COLOR="Blue"]here[/COLOR]]("http://tarlimit.sourceforge.net/").  It will make several archives for you, making sure each one can fit on a cd (or whatever else you want).
