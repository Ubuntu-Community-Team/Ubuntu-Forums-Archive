### Post by strabes on 2007-10-03
Don't get:
1) A Broadcom wireless card. They are not fun to get working. Go with the Intel Pro/Wireless models. The 3945 model works perfectly and comes with many dells.
2) An ATI video card. ATI's linux drivers are terrible. They are supposedly releasing acceptable drivers this month but don't risk it. Get an Intel or nvidia video card.
3) A fancy sound card.

Other than that, if you go with dell, everything should work out of the box. I can't tell you to do anything illegal, but if you bought DRM-crippled music from iTunes, all I can say is that ONLY iTunes can play them, and iTunes doesn't work on linux. There are many "ways" to "solve" this problem, if you know what I mean.
