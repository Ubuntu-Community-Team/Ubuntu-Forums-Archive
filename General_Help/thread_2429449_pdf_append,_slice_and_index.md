### Post by Holger_Gehrke on 2019-10-18
I am not quite certain what you mean by index entry. If you mean the text-entries in the outline-view in the side-bar of the pdf-viewer, then those have to be generated at the source in Libre Office. If you use the format-templates for headings than those will produce outline entries when exporting to pdf; or you can manually set paragraphs to be outline entries with 'Format' -> 'Paragraph' -> Tab 'Outline & Numbering' -> Field 'Outline Level'.

Holger
