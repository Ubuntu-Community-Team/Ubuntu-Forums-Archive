### Post by spec-chum on 2008-08-30
I suspect there's not much you can do to play them.  They're encrypted with Microsoft's Digital Rights Management, so need a Microsoft licence to decrypt and play.  Funnily enough we don't have those on Ubuntu.

If you're dual-booting or running Windows in a virtual machine you could play them there.  That's really your only option I think.
