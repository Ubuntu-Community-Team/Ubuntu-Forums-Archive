### Post by urlacher3292 on 2009-09-05
I know that there is a way to force all windows to the middle of the screen, but I prefer the smart placement. I just want firefox to always appear in the middle. The compiz plugin has settings for this type of occasion, but I've no idea how to work them.

---

### Post by lovinglinux on 2009-09-05
Open "CompizConfig Settings Manager", click the "Place Windows" plugin, select the "fixed Window Placement" tab, click the "New" button in the "Windows with fixed positions" list and add **class=Firefox** to the "Positioned Windows" field, then select the position you want fr X ad Y then click close.
