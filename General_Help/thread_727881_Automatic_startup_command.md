### Post by lavinog on 2008-03-18
many computer BIOS have a setting (usually under the power management page) to tell the computer what events to wake on...one of them is to turn the computer on at a certain time.
to access your bios you have to read the boot screen (you usually get only a second to do this...
if your boot screen spends some time testing memory it may be easier.)
the most common methods I have seen are to press either:
DEL, F1, F2, or F10
when the computer just turns on
