### Post by Greyed on 2008-06-26
Thunderbird.  It is an email client and nothing but.  It has been rock solid for me after years of use and I only have one major complaint about it; no list-reply.

Evolution, on the other hand, is trying to be an Outlook replacement and that comes with all of the problems in slapping together a PIM and email client offer.  Evolution's email handling has never worked correctly for me.  One portion would work while another would be frustratingly broken.  Then the next time I test it they would reverse themselves.  Also there are some maddeningly idiotic choices on configuration.  Try, just try to configure the wrap for anything other than the default of 72(?) characters.  Lunacy!
