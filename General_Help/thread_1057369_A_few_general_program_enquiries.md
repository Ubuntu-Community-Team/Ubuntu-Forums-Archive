### Post by mb_webguy on 2009-02-01
All of those issues you listed have improved greatly in the last 2 years.  Some people still have issues with Flash, but I've seen considerably fewer posts about it since the release of Intrepid than I saw with Hardy or Gutsy.  Audio and video support has increased quite a bit.  I have Ubuntu installed on three different machines, each with different sound and video cards, and I don't experience any problems with any of them.  As for Pidgin, I don't use it much, but it's considerably better than it was as Gaim.  It still doesn't yet support voice or video chat, though.
