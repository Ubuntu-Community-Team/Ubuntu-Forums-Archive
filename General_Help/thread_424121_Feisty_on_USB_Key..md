### Post by Zuph on 2007-04-26
Yes, it is possible.  There are quite a few guides out there as to how to accomplish this.

Unfortunately, there is a bug that causes persistence to fail on Feisty.  This means that every time you reboot, nothing you change will be saved.  This is a known bug.  Edgy and Dapper work fine.

[http://www.debuntu.org/how-to-install-ubuntu-linux-on-usb-bar](http://www.debuntu.org/how-to-install-ubuntu-linux-on-usb-bar)  that is a very good guide.

[https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/casper/+bug/84591](https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/casper/+bug/84591)  this is the bug that breaks persistence.
