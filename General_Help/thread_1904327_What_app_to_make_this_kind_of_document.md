### Post by spacecheck on 2012-01-04
LibreOffice Calc is standard spreadsheet program on Ubuntu. Gnumeric on Xubuntu. You can also use tables from within LibreOffice Writer, as well as in Abiword (Xubuntu).

Of course, if that is too much work for you, you might try scanning the image at a better angle and at a decent resolution to allow using Gimp to despeckle and enhance the image and then save with or for OCR or as a text, etc. If scanned accurately, cleaned up & saved as a text-capable pdf, you can often get good results with a copy & paste from the pdf into the spreadsheet or writer file.

Good luck!
