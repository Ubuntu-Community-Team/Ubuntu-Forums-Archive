### Post by Lekensteyn on 2010-01-16
Therefore, I'm using Dead keys with Alt Gr.
To get accented characters, I press AltGr, then press the accent key (for example ") and finally the char (for example, e)
Overview:
While holding AltGr, press accent.
Then press char.
