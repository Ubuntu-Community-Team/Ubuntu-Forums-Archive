### Post by paydaydaddy on 2009-02-08
Lexmark printer are not well supported for Ubuntu/Linux. The fault is with Lexmark, not Linux, as they are not open with they codes for their drivers. A google search will provide numerous discussions about problems with Lexmark printers and linux distros. Many people have gotten them to print with Linux, although generally with limited functionality. The standard consensus is to check the hardware compatability list and get a compatible printer.

[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/HardwareSupport/](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/HardwareSupport/)
