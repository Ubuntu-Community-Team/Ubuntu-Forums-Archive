### Post by teachop on 2011-05-01
gconf-editor:  apps | gnome-power-manager | buttons | lid_ac = "nothing".  Nice and convenient!

---

### Post by cymbaline42 on 2011-05-01
Click System - Screensaver - Preferences - Power Management - choose "When laptop lid is closed - Blank Screen".
