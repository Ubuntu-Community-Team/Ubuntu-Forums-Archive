### Post by sdennie on 2009-03-08
Battery life depends on a lot of factors.  As stated above, the reporting mechanisms of Windows and Linux are different.  The only way to verify them is with a stopwatch under your normal usage.  However, depending on your hardware, Ubuntu generally provides the mechanisms to get much better battery life than Windows.  Though probably not specific to your laptop, an example of configuring your laptop to even exceed the overblown marketing hype about how long your battery will last can be found here: [ HOWTO: XPS m1330 power savings]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=847773")
