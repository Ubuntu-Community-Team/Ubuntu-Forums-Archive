I am currently running Ubuntu Intrepid with Compiz and the advanced effects utility installed. Back in Feisty with Beryl installed, when an applicaiton was in fullscreen mode, grabbing the titlebar would grab that part of the window and pull the application as if it were a piece of paper, but it would not disconnect it from the edges and move it. ### Post by Shardsofmetal on 2008-10-28
ok, a little more fiddling around and I figured it out. For anyone else who'd like to know, I went to the settings dialog, clicked the move window option, and unchecked the snap of maximized windows box.
