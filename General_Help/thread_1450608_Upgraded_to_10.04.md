### Post by JPN94 on 2010-04-09
Recently I upgraded from Ubuntu 9.10 to 10.04 Beta, straight away after upgrading my computer had slowed right back down, like windows slow. So I was wondering if there was any way I could downgrade back to 9.10?

---

### Post by Mark Phelps on 2010-04-09
Basically -- no.  There is no convenient "roll-back" capability in Ubuntu.  You would have to reinstall from scratch.

You could try backing off your /home directory (and all files underneath it, including hidden files). That way, when you reinstall, you should be able to get your settings back.

BTW, this inability to easily "roll-back" is one of the main reasons I try to discourage folks from "upgrading" to pre-release versions.  If your machine gets trashed in the process, there is no simple way to restore it to the previous working state.
