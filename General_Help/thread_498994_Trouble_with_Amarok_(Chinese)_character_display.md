### Post by tkclo on 2007-07-19
I had the same problem.

Try just changing the font.

1. Go to Settings > Configure Amarok...
2. Click "Appearance".
3. Change the fonts. (Mine was set to Arial. I changed it to Sans Serif and it worked.)

You might want to do the same in Konqueror.
