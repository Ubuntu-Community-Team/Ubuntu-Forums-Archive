### Post by Giblet5 on 2009-11-05
Nope. The closest thing would be the killer-applet. Click that icon, then click some other window to kill it.

For full-screen apps that don't respond to an app-switcher (Alt-Tab, etc) there is Ctrl-Alt-F1 and the kill command.
