### Post by halitech on 2006-03-26
not saying it can't be done but as tech support for one of the main laser printer companies of North America, I can pretty much guarentee a wireless print server won't be supported by many companies and you will have troubles printing to it. Now if you just want your laptops to be able to print, set them up on wireless but have the printer hard wired to the network, either on a server or direct to the router.
