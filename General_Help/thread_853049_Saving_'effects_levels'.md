### Post by sayakb on 2008-07-08
Install Ubuntu Tweak from getdeb.net
Launch Ubuntu Tweak, expand **Desktop** in the left column and click on [B]Compiz Fusion.
[/B]On the right column under **Compiz Extensions,  **check the *Install Simple Desktop Effects Settings Manager*
