### Post by drs305 on 2011-06-08
To allow the other repositories to update, go into Software Sources or Synaptic: Settings > Repositories > Other Software and untick the repositories named in the error message (palitinus and ppa doctor...). Once you have deselected these items, press the "Reload" button.

How you get into the above varies, but Synaptic is usually accessed via System, Administration, Synaptic or typing 'Synpatic' in Natty's Dash.
