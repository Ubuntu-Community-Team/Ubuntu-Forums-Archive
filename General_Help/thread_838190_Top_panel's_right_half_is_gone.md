Hi,

After the last set of updates, I think it was the compiz update that caused it, the toppanel was blank at the right side. I mean the icons (from user name through date/ time to Log off) were not showing. They did give their tool help texts, and worked when clicked on. 
It was easy to fix, System, Preference, Appearance, Visual effects: I chose none, and all icons came on, then back to normal, and they were still there.

I just wanted to log this, maybe someone finds it useful!
