### Post by aysiu on 2006-10-11
> **turbojugend_gr said:**
> Well first of all there never was an ubuntu 6.1 . You should know that 6.06 stands for June 2006, 6.10 stands for October 2006, and so on. I've edited the thread title appropriately.

Well, I just installed Edgy a couple of days ago, and I'm impressed. It actually feels stabler to me than Dapper did on release day (and Edgy hasn't been released yet!)

Things I've noticed:

1. **Speed** - Gnome, KDE, IceWM (of course) are all superfast and responsive, much more so than Dapper, Breezy, or Hoary--which all seemed about the same decent (not slow or fast) speed to me.

2. **Dialogues** - The A la Carte menu editor has been renamed the *menu layout*. To me, that new name sounds more confusing, but they must have thought about it. The software properties are now called *software sources*, and it's a lot easier to check the appropriate boxes.

3. **Logos** - The artwork is slightly different--more polished, more... basic, too. There's no longer a verbose part of the USplash during bootup--it's just a progress bar that goes from left to right.

4. **Newer versions of applications** - That's a given.
