### Post by Cyhawk on 2008-12-01
> **TDS_LeGenD said:**
> Thanks, it installed. But how do I now switch from GNOME desktop to KDE? er GDM to KDM?

From the login screen, Click on the option button (bottom left) then Select Session, and choose KDE. Then log in normally and it will boot.

Its a good point of interest that this is also the location of other Desktop managers and other useful utilities. (For example, The Secure Remote Connection allows you to connect to a remote x11 session (meaning any other linux system that allows it)) And the failsafes for when something royaly goes wrong and you dont need to go to the console.
