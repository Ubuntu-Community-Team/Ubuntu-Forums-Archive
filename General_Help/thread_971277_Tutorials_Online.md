### Post by cariboo on 2008-11-04
You don't have to stick with Ubuntu tutorials, in most cases I go straight to the source eg: 

[www.samba.org/samba/docs/Samba3-ByExample.pdf](www.samba.org/samba/docs/Samba3-ByExample.pdf)

This is not really a tutorial but it works a lot better than many tutorials i have seen.

Another good place is here:

[http://tldp.org/](http://tldp.org/)

This site has been around a lot longer than Ubuntu and the tutorials work.

What I do is check to see how many people have thanked the poster, if there are quite a few, you should be able to count on the howto being ok.

Jim
