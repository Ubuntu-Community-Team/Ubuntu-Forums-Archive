### Post by matt1988 on 2006-04-16
If you are wanting to install a new operating system over it, boot into the setup for the new operating system and format your partitions. If you want a blank hardrive, boot some sort of bootdisk (win98 floppy, or even a specialized boot disk for formating) and format the hardrive. (make sure your formating it to whatever filesystem you want.
