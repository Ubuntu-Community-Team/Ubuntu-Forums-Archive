### Post by Zorael on 2011-06-08
What widgets are we talking about? Like the folder view? Or do you mean the panel?

Try the **Aya** plasma theme; it's an opaque theme that colors itself to fit the colors you picked for your windows (in System Settings -> Application Appearance -> Colors). It's in the [**plasma-desktopthemes-artwork**](apt://plasma-desktopthemes-artwork) package.
