### Post by davarino on 2010-05-08
The two suggestions are good, but be forewarned that neither opens .pub files nor does either save in a format that is compatible with M$Punisher.

M$Punisher files are unreadable except by M$Punisher. You can get them converted online by using Zamzar.com.

If you *must* create Punisher files, my first question would be "Why?" Punisher is a 2nd rate graphics program. (InDesign is *so* much better... but also not available for Linux.)

If all you need to do is to make "publications" materials, then by all means look into both options suggested... just be aware that they do not mesh at all with Punisher.
