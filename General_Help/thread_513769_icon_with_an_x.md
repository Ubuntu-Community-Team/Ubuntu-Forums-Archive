### Post by kevinlyfellow on 2007-07-30
It means that you aren't allow to see what's inside that folder.  (ie you don't have read permission)

---

### Post by Cybergod on 2007-07-30
I believe that means the folder is owned by root and you don't have permission to view it.  If you right click on that folder then go to "Properties", choose the "Permissions" tab you'll see that it is owned by root.
