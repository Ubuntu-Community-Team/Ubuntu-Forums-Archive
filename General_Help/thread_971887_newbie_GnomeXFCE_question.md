I am no expert but I am 98% sure on this.  Gnome and XFCE are both two different Desktop environments.  XFCE is used in Xubuntu because it is so lightweight (meaning it is easier for the computer to process).  Gnome is the default Desktop interface for The popular, Ubuntu distribution.  When you look around you will see KDE also, (That is the Kubuntu Environment).  

To answer your question, yes.  You have to use a desktop environment to start your journey into Linux.  Gnome and XFCE are two different program that manage your desktop.  

Differences:

KDE - Graphic heavy but user friendly

Gnome - A good medium

XFCE - Lightweight interface a LIL less user friendly
