### Post by 3rdalbum on 2009-06-06
The Need For Speed games are available for consoles too. Your best bet would be to grab the basic Xbox 260 pack and pick up a copy of your favored NFS game.

Statistically, most Windows programs and games do not work with Wine. An impressive number do, but that number is under 50%.
