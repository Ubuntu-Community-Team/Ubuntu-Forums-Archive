### Post by DougieFresh4U on 2009-07-03
The only thing I can say about printers, is that Ubuntu has come a long way in support. I use very simple, basic, and older ones and have had no problems. Each one has been detected and the correct drivers have been offered and set up very easy. A few times I have plugged in printer and gotten 'pop-up' saying printer is installed and ready, and it was.
As far as suggesting a printer, it all depends on what you want a printer to do for you. I don't use scanner or photo for printer, so my printers are just 'simple and plain'.
