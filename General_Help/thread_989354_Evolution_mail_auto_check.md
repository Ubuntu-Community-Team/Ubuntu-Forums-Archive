### Post by Tamlynmac on 2008-11-21
In Evolution make sure your mail notification plugin is checked:
edit/plugins/

Then configure your mail notification:
edit/plugins/Mail Notification/Configuration tab

I believe Evolution must remain open in the panel to utilize this option.
