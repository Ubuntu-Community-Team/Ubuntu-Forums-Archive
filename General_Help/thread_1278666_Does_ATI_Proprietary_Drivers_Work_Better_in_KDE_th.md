### Post by undecim on 2009-09-29
The drivers are both handled by Xorg, so the drivers themselves wont work any better.

However, if you want something more lightweight than compiz, go to your Appearance settings (right clickon your desktop, select "Change Desktop Background" or go to "System" -> "Preferences" -> "Appearance") and in the "Visual Effects" tab choose "None"

This will use Metacity as your window manager instead of Compiz, making your desktop much faster.
