### Post by Kozar on 2011-09-19
Bluetooth cannot be simply a driver to be downloaded. A bluetooth transmitter is a piece of hardware. If you do have do have bluetooth hardware already in your comp, then we could talk about drivers.

Also, I don't quite see how it could be possible to use your monitor for motion tracking. On the front of the WiiMote is an IR (infrared) eye that views IR rays. The simplest low tech solution for something to do motion tracking with would be a few small candles. Just be careful about fire being right there by your PC.
