A similar thing has happen to me. What happens is when there's a new release of GNOME, they change up the "standards" (i.e. files are called different names). So, older icon themes might not be updated to the new "standards" which is why that set might not be working. Unfortunately, the only thing you can do is wait til the author has updated the set, or go ahead and fix it yourself. Do you have a link to where you downloaded the set?
