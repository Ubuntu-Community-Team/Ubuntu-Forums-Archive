### Post by Ranko Kohime on 2013-07-08
You probably need to modify the BIOS setting to allow CD's to boot.  And that is probably covered in the manual for your particular computer.  Not the little slips of paper they include in the box, either, the manual they no longer include in the box so they can save money on printing.  :)  Head over to the support section of the website for your computer's manufacturer.  Once you get the manual, you'll be looking for a way to get into the BIOS, and, once in the BIOS, you'll be looking for "boot order" or something to that effect.
