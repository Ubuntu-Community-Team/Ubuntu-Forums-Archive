### Post by drs305 on 2010-01-12
For Grub legacy (0.97), there is StartUp-Manager (startupmanager), a gui app that can tweak many of Grub's settings. Click on the "SUM" link in my signature line to learn how to install and run it.

It has limited capabilities in Grub 2, but until a newer version comes out you will just have to wait if you are looking for a gui app that works well with G2.
