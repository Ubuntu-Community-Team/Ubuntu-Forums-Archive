### Post by audiomick on 2010-02-04
I would try the "export to PDF" option from open office first. I use that to send my invoices to my clients, and it works well. The nature of PDF is such that it always looks the same, no matter what system it is opened on. When you have created the PDF, have a look at it before you take it off to print, in case there is still a mistake in there somewhere.

The idea with abiword portable sounds good. I didn't know that exists. That would allow you to make last minute changes before you print, which is not (easily) possible with  a pdf.

You could also, if you are a regular in the cyber cafe, ask them to install open office on their machines. It doesn't cost anything, after all. ;)
