### Post by kevdog on 2008-03-13
There isn't a great way to do this.  The pdf format does not save formatting information (that is part of the problem), so you really can't do a 1-1 conversion.  There are various tools on the internet that will do this for you, and depending on the complexity of the document they get really close or look horrible.  Ive been down this road many times and have really been disappointed with the results.
