### Post by Yoooder on 2007-12-17
Sure you might be in over your head, but it won't be anything you can't get out of.  The best way to go about it is to try and start with the default kernel settings, and slowly lob off modules/support you don't need--recompiling and re-running often.

Baby steps is the key, but if you take an afternoon and learn the steps you'll learn a lot.
