It is true that your questions have been somewhat unclear.

Are you meaning a clickable link on a webpage to send mail somewhere?

If so, when you hover your cursor over the link there will be something down in the status bar of your browser. It will be of the form, mailto:someone@somewhere.

If so, then clicking the link should open a new mail for you to type in, ready to send to the address in the link. The email client used for that is usually configured in the preferences of the browser. If you use Firefox, it's on the Applications tab of the preferences.
