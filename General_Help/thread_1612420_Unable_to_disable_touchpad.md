I read in an older thread about downloading a little program "disable tapping".

I used the Ubuntu software center, got the program, and can now disable the trackpad (temporarily it seems) by going to System > Preferences > Pointing Devices. 

It sees my Logitech trackball mouse, and my Synaptcs touchpad, and I can tick the "Disable touchpad" box, which works .. until I start typing anything. Then it becomes enabled again, even though the box still shows as selected.

Laptop: HP Pavilion dv5
Ubuntu: 10.10 Netbook edition (changed to the desktop interface)

Thanks in advance :)

...well, it's fixed :)

Had to go into System > Preferences > Mouse and untick 'disable touchpad while typing' under the Touchpad tab. Weird conflict with the Pointing Devices program. yay :D
