### Post by bogan on 2012-03-16
Hi**!, Linktopower.**

You Posted: > ...  in ubuntu 11.04 How would yo be able tell How fast a dial up connection is On ubuntu?The simple and accurate way, - that tells you the actual real speed, and not some figment of imaginative marketing - is the Download Manager display in the bottom bar of Firefox.

It shows - in very small digits, -  the instantaneous speed, in Kb/s, and the estimated time left to complete, during the actual download..

'Speed Test' shows my Download 'Speed' as 6.5Mb/Sec, in both Windows and Ubuntu; whereas I have never seen an actual speed exceeding  900Kb/sec, even momentarily; sustained speeds much lower still.

And that is not Dial-up, it is supposed to be "up  to 8Mb/s" adsl Broadband.

### Post by Frogs Hair on 2012-03-16
Dial up speed depends partly on the modem speed in your computer .  If you have a modem card in your computer the following command will display the type of card and speed  in the terminal output under communication controller. 
```
lspci
```
