### Post by WorMzy on 2011-10-13
You won't get the default Ubuntu setup onto a 1GB USB stick. You'd need ~3GB for that.

However, you might be able to get something like Lubuntu to fit.

### Post by WorMzy on 2011-10-13
What I meant is that a fresh installation of Ubuntu takes up approximately 3.7GB of disk space. I would expect Kubuntu to take up more than that. Xubuntu would take up less than either of those, and Lubuntu would take up even less than that.

If you can't get any of the above to fit, then you could always install a minimal distro, like Arch, and build up from a barebones system.
