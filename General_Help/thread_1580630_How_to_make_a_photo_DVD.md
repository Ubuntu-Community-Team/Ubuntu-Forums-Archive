### Post by Herman on 2010-09-24
:) I just recently started using a great program called kdenlive which is really for editing videos, but you can also use it for making a slide show instead whenever you like. I made a very nice slideshow with about thirty photos and fancy title I made in GIMP, it has background music and all kinds of wonderful transitions. 
Kdenlive has its own web forum and there are many helpful YouTube how-tos for it on the web. All you need to do is look for youtubes about what you want to do in kdenlive and watch them a few times, then try to immitate what the Youtube tutorial is telling you and pretty soon you'll be able to do anything you like with videos and slideshows. After only a little practice it becomes quite easy.  :)
