### Post by Thanoulis on 2008-04-05
Go to System ->Preferances -> Keyboard and at the *Layouts* tab click add and enter the keyboard layout you want.
Than go to the System -> Aministrator -> Language Support and click the language you want. Then press *Apply*.
Yoila!
If you need extra fonts, search for them in *Synaptic*
