### Post by 3rdalbum on 2011-09-04
A checklist here:

1. What are you using to check if the webcam is working? The program Cheese, which can be installed from the Ubuntu repositories, is a good way to test your webcam and check that it's not a software problem in the program you're currently using.

2. I assume you've gone to the speaker icon, clicked on it, gone to Sound Preferences and the Output tab and checked the options there? You need to choose the device AND the connector.

3. If nobody comes up with a solution for the audio problem, you could just get one of those plug 'n' play USB sound devices. If it's plug 'n' play with no drivers to be installed, then it will work on any operating system including Ubuntu.
