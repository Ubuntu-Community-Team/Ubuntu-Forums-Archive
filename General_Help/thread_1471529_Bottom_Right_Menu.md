### Post by physic.dude on 2010-05-03
Simply right click on any blank part of your panel (bar thing at bottom or top) and click **add to panel...**

Then scroll down and click on **indicator applet **then click **add**. 

Your done.
\\:D/


And to move it, you need to right click on items in the panel and un-check "lock to panel" then right click on the one you want to move and click move.
