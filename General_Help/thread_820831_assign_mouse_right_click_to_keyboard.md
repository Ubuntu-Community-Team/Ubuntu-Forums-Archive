### Post by FakeOutdoorsman on 2008-06-06
Your best bet is either [xdotool]("http://www.semicomplete.com/projects/xdotool/") or xte (part of xautomation which is in the repo) to assign or simulate key pressing.  I've used xte in Openbox to assign a keyboard and mouse combination to launch an application.  I don't use Gnome, so I can't give any advice on using xdotool/xte in that desktop environment.
