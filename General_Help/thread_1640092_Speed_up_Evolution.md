### Post by chammi on 2010-12-09
Depends on several factors. First off, how are you getting your email? POP?  IMAP?  Also, what email service do you use?

I discovered that when I use IMAP to check my Gmail in Evolution, I can REALLY speed things up if I just keep my inbox tidy. It was painfully slow, so I archived over 1000 messages. Now I have 100 messages in my Gmail inbox and things load much faster.  You don't have to delete anything; just archive it.
