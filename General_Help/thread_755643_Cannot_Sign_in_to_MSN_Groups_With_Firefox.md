### Post by mixmaster on 2008-04-15
Do you have noscript or a similar extension running with Firefox?  That will frequently cause such errors.  You can set noscript to permit scripting from the site on either a temporary or permanent basis.

Alternatively, given this is an MS site, it may be using some nasty ActiveX stuff that firefox does not support.
