I want to copy a TTF font from my Windows drive into Ubuntu. Does anyone know what directory they should be copied to? Thanks.

---

### Post by SilentCacophony on 2005-10-29
If you just want your user account to access them, you can make a '.fonts' directory in your home directory (~/.fonts)

or use /usr/share/fonts for system-wide use.

Take a look through /usr/share and you'll see, themes, pixmaps, icons and such.
