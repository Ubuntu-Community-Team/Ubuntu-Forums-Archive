### Post by Dennis N on 2009-08-27
Specifically for Ubuntu 8.10, how does one change the mouse pointer? I looked at System > Preferences > Mouse which brings up the "Mouse Preferences" dialog. I have three tabbed sections: General, Accessibility, and Touchpad. According to the Help button found at the bottom of the Mouse Preferences page, there should tabbed sections "Buttons", "Pointers", and "Motion". I don't have these tabs, and none of the tabs I have gives me any pointer options. Is there another section of System > Preferences that will contain mouse pointer options? Thanks.

EDIT: Figured it out. Found under System > Preferences> Appearance > Customize > Pointer. The Help file is out of date.
