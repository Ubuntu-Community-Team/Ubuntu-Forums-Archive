---

### Post by ymmotrojam on 2005-10-24
if you use firefox, it's the following...
/home/$user/.mozilla/firefox/(some arbitrary folder name)/Cache

:)

Note that when you go to your Home, you can hit Ctrl+H to view all the hidden files. That's the equivalent of C:\Documents and Settings\(your username)
