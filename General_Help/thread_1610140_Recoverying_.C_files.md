### Post by btindie on 2010-10-31
First off, don't do anything with your computer as there's a chance you can recover them. If the files are on another partition then unmount it immediately. You don't won't to do anything that will cause data to be written to the partition with your deleted files on otherwise they may be overwritten.

There's a program called *testdisk* that allows you to recover deleted files, it's available for most distributions and can be downloaded manually as a binary. As an alternative you can get SystemRescueCD or PartedMagic, which is a smaller download, and use that to recover your files from a bootable CD/USB.
