### Post by Mark Phelps on 2012-06-21
I don't believe there is a way to do a low level disk format from the LiveCD.

In my experience, that has to be done using software provided by the hard drive manufacturer -- and in most (if not all) cases, that software is designed to run only in MS Windows.

Check the make and model of your hard drive, and then see if the manufacturer has a website, and once there, if they have SW you can download and burn to make a bootable CD.  If they do, you can then boot your PC from that CD and use their software to do a low level format.
