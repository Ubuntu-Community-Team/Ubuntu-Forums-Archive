### Post by deadflowr on 2017-09-06
The mobile looking settings is the unity8 settings page. (The one on the left)
Useless on normal unity. So you can ignore it.

Did you install gnome (or gnome shell)?
Because the settings opened on the right look like gnome's settings page.

(Ubuntu settings page should have a Security and Privacy button, where as gnome's usually has just Privacy.
As well as the look is different from unity's settings app.)

It also explains why you have two system settings icons; one for unity and one for gnome.
