### Post by cubeist on 2009-06-21
If you have already custom compiled 2.6.30 RC2 then the procedure for compiling 2.6.30 should be familiar to you.  Just go through it step by step, make sure you have the dependencies required for building a kernel, and lastly, and I cannot stress this enough, DO NOT delete your old (aka 2.6.30 RC2) kernel until you are absolutely positive the new kernel is rock solid for you.
