### Post by hwttdz on 2013-04-08
I think the best way is to actually geotag the photos before they ever reach a computer.  And here there are a number of solutions, for canon, nikon, and other brands.  But what you describe isn't far behind.

Google's mytracks program is a pretty good smartphone solution for tracking location, and I believe you can get the data out of it in a reasonably manageable format.  That would then require pulling the mytracks file and the image files and tagging after you pull to your machine.
