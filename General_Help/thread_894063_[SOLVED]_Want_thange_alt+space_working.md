### Post by bimaljr on 2008-08-18
Ok.. I have found how to do this.
I want to share how I do this... so, here is step by step :

*** The default application for alt+space is [B]katapult** so first change it's settings :[/B]
1) press alt+space, the Katapult box will appear, then press ctrl+c to get katapult menu.
2) click "Configure Global shortcuts"
3) change shortcut with something different. I have selected win+space.

*** Ok the alt+space is now free to use, now we have to set the maximise, minimize menu with this shortcut :**
1) go to "system settings > keyborad and mouse"
2) go to "keyboard shortcuts"
3) in the "Global shortcut" tab, there is "window Operation menu"
4) change it's shortcut with "alt+space"
5) apply.. ok.. done
