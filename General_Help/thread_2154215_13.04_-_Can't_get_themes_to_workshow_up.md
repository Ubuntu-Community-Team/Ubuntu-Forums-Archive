### Post by stinkeye on 2013-06-14
With some downloads the GTK theme may not be the extracted topmost folder... it may be inside that folder. Reason for this is they may also contain a gnome-shell theme.
Look for the folder that contains gtk-2.0 ,gtk-3.0 folders.
Move the folder that contains these.

eg wih the Zukitwo download the theme is inside the **140562-Zukitwo** folder.
