### Post by soze on 2006-06-07
Having recently made the switch from windows, the only thing of any significance that I miss (and am quite frankly surprised is not available) is a clipboard manger.

This is a program which remembers the history of your clipboard and can be called-up with a hotkey sequence. You can then recall any of your past clipboard history.

The one I used under Windows was called "Ditto" but there are many others.

I can't find a single program for Gnome that works like this.

I figured I'd give it a few more months and then take it on myself, but if anybody else wants to give it a try, please do!
