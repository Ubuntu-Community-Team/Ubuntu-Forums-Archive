### Post by Mark Phelps on 2010-03-27
Do NOT use Gparted, LiveCD or otherwise, to shrink your Win7 partition.  Use the builtin Win7 Disk Management utility to shrink the partition and free up unallocated space.  Using GParted runs the risk of corrupting your Win7 OS and rendering it unbootable.
