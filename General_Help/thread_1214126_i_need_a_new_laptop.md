### Post by t4thfavor on 2009-07-15
Even slow Nvidia cards are faster than fast GMA cards. The only reason I go with Intel in a non gamer scenario is there are much better drivers available, and even some full open source ones that are usable.

Even if you can buy a new one you could buy a high end used one, which if your lucky will be an excellent buy. I bought my wife's laptop used (hp dv82830us) and it was an awesome buy.


If you need a warranty (its a good idea) buy new though.
