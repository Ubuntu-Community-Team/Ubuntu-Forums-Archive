### Post by Jesterday on 2009-02-20
If the battery icon keeps appearing and disappearing it could indicate a problem with you power adapter. When I unplug my laptop, the screen brightness control pops up, the screen dims and then the battery icon appears. When I plug it back in the brightness control pops up, the screen brightens and the battery icon disappears. So it sounds like your AC adapter is losing the connection causing this to happen.

It could be as simple as a bad connection with the wall or the ac adapter not being plugged in all the way, or it could be way more complicated and something could be lose internally.
