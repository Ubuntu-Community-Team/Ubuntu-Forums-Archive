### Post by fragos on 2010-06-24
I just starting using Empathy with this release of Ubuntu so I don't have a lot of experience with it. I would say that it appears that MSN either won't accept your login or is down when you tried. In the past I did on occasion have to retry MSN login to get a connect and occasionally MSN would disconnect for me. Never had any strange activity with the others.

Open the Messaging and VOIP Accounts and select your MSN account. The current status will be shown and should be "Available" and checked Enabled. Uncheck Eabled and check on again. This will cause Empathy to reconnect.
