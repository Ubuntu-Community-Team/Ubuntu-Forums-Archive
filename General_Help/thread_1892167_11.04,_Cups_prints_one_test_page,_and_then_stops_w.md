### Post by brntoki on 2011-12-07
I had:

Print Quality: Best
Resolution: 1440x1440

Which is what Cups didn't like. From the way things are listed, it would seem logical that, if setting resolution to anything other than automatic, you would choose manual as your print quality. However, other combinations, such as super photo along with resolutions of 1440x1440 work (as I suspect other combinations do), so I guess one just needs to make sure there is no conflict in those settings if Cups won't print.
