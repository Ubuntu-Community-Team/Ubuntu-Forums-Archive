### Post by higashi on 2011-05-14
I've grown to accept the choppiness of the unity launcher (when i click&drag to scroll, or when it's just hiding, the animations are extremely choppy and even lag whatever window i'm moving).

However, today I decided to play with the "Launcher Icon Size" in ccsm and i tried making the icons bigger and smaller. Coincidentally, I decided to see what would happen if i made them as big as possible (64) and i found that, when they're that size, everything runs faster. Not just the panel animations, but even expo, the effect that happens when i press super+w, and even switching desktops.

I found that this works with the icon size set as low as 62. Anything under that and everything goes back to being choppy.

I'm not sure why this is, but I thought I'd share this information and maybe help a few people out. :)
