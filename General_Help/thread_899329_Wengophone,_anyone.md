### Post by juaninse on 2008-08-24
You do not need a Wengo account to use wengophone.  I noticed that registration problem myself.  I have an account opened at ekiga.net, however.  All you really need those SIP servers is to be able to find your friends' IP address. :).  So, when starting Wengophone, tell it that you are using another service.  Then just fill in your preferred SIP address.  I like ekiga too, but it lacks two things that wengo has: conectivity to IM networks.  Availability for Windows platform.  None of that are impediments for me, but when I refer friends to a new software that we can use for all our communications needs, those are handy (if they are not linuxers). Anyway, Wengo is great in that it does not force you to have a Wengo account.
