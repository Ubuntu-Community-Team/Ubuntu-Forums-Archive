Hi.
Practically anything starting from 10,04,3 LTS will run very happily on your specs. Natty  will have its own set of problems so not really a good idea to use. For speed and best use of resources my personal recommendation is either Xubuntu or Lubuntu. Both are very fast and lightweight with usage of RAM. So a matter of personal choice. Take your pick of those or wait for other suggestions.
