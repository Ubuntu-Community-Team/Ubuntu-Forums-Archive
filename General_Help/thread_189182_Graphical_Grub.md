### Post by msak007 on 2006-06-08
That looks like all it does is change the timeout options and lets you rename the entries. I know what musther is referring to as I previously used SuSE, and it has a graphical grub loader where you can choose which OS you want to load (instead of a black terminal screen with white text). That is one thing I'd like to see (K)Ubuntu incorporate. It's not a big deal as I don't reboot often, but it does add a nice touch to the boot loader and I can see why some people would like it. It also didn't scroll anything while loading once you chose an OS to let you know what it's doing, and instead showed a little animation (kind of like windoze). That approach seems a little more user friendly, especially to users coming from windoze who are just used to seeing the little animated scrollbar. Most users don't care what's going on behind the scenes. I wish I could find a good screenshot of Suse's grub loader but can't find one now.
