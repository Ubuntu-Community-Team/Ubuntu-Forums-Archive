### Post by leopoldbirkholm on 2012-04-07
I had a similar problem with my Asus and other computer. I solved it by first download [UNetBootin](http://unetbootin.sourceforge.net/) and then chose the first option and re-downloaded the Ubuntu 11.10 32-bit. That solved the issue for me and now I am happily typing on my Asus running Ubuntu 11.10.
