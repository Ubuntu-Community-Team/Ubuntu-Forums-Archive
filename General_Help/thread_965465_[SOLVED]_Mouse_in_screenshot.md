### Post by MikeWanDo on 2008-10-31
When I push print scr the mouse now appears in my screenshot after installing 8.10 intrepid. The mouse was previously not visible in screenshots on 8.04 hardy and I'd like to know how to restore that behavior. ### Post by OrangeCrate on 2008-10-31
Menu > Accessories > Take Screenshot > Uncheck the box to show the pointer.
