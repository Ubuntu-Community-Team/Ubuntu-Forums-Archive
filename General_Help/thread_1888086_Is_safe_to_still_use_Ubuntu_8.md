### Post by dabl on 2011-11-28
> **feesgo said:**
> I have a very old computer.

I can not upgrade to Ubutu 11 because I don have enough RAM memory and my system freezes.

Well, it does not sound like you have a choice.   :(

You are not risking your "computer" by running 8.04 or 8.10.  There's no security threat that hurts the computer.  You do need to take care of your personal data, with normal security precautions such as:

- use a firewall, either in a router or with iptables
- use a complex password on your user account
- keep important data backed up frequently
- change passwords on online accounts periodically

etc. etc.
