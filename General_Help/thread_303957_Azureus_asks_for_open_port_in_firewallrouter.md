### Post by Rhubarb on 2006-11-21
Port forwarding has nothing to do with Linux specifically.
If you're behind a Router you need to configure it to forward UDP port 61574 to your IP address.

A good site that'll help you out with this is:-
[http://www.portforward.com/](http://www.portforward.com/)

Decentralised tracking is a feature in Azureus that you may not need, but can be good to have anyway.
You should be able to read up about decentralised tracking here:-
[http://azureus.sourceforge.net/](http://azureus.sourceforge.net/)

Hope this'll get you pointed in the right direction - I know port forwarding confused the heck out of me when I was starting to learn what it was all about.
