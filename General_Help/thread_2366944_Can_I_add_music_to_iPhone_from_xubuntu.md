### Post by sp40140 on 2017-07-24
You can try. Not likely to work. And if it did reliability will be big concern.
I wouldn't recommend it. If you still want to try (at considerable risk or loosing all the music already loaded), try "gtkpod". It has higher success rate than others I know of (not that it's any sort of approval).

I however use different method. I share music over samba share and have app access it and play music using third party apps. Or, as I need windows for few things, I run win7 in VMWare and have Itunes installed there so manage it that way as well.
