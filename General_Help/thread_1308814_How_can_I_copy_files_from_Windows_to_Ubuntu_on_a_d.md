### Post by Turtle.net on 2009-10-31
The install in dual boot will let you create a partition on a free disk space (so not touching any windows file) and then you'll be access your files (the windows partition should be automagically mounted as drives in your "Places").
Anyway, launch the LiveCD and test that first without installing anything.
It will be even safer to boot on the LiveCD, move your files on an external backup and then install (in dual or single boot since your data will be backed up on your external drive).
