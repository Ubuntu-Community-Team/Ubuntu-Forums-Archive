### Post by Abhi Kalyan on 2007-03-07
Get to the link below
[https://launchpad.net/](https://launchpad.net/)
This place is like a launch pad for makers of applications. 
"Launchpad is a collection of services for products in the open source universe."
If you are making Applications=products, then here is the place where you could register so that your app gets noticed, and bugs on your app get to you in time. Stuff like  that.

Please PLEASE read the FAQ
[https://launchpad.net/faq](https://launchpad.net/faq)

Lets for example say that you create an application that helps configure sendmail, then you could use launch pad to get reviews and bug reports on your application. This area helps in removeing duplicate bug reports and reports submitted by bots.
The faq is very interesting.

Hope that helped
