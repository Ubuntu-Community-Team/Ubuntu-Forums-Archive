### Post by chaanakya_chiraag on 2010-03-26
Then go to Edit->Preferences->Applications->Search for anything that says "Download" and change it to "Always Ask".  Then, the next time you click on a stream, it should ask you if you want to download it or open it.  Click "Open With..." and check the "Set as default" checkbox.  That should do it :)
