### Post by Rasa1111 on 2011-05-18
If you want Terminal in the dock..
You can also..
Once you have a Terminal open,
Right click the terminal icon (in the launcher/dock on the left)
and choose "Keep in Launcher" . and it will stay in the dock, 
so you can just click it whenever you want a terminal open. 
I use that, and ctrl+alt+t often.
