### Post by andrew.46 on 2011-07-09
I think the problem is that the page you looked at does not list free news servers as such, it actually lists ISPs who provide Usenet access *as a part of* their service. So if you have not actually signed up with these ISPs you will not get access to their NNTP service.

It depends on what you are after from Usenet. I get great access to text only newsgroups from individual.net in Germany and for this I simply pay €13 per annum. If you are after binaries the prices go up a little though and companies such as Giganews are the places to go to.

I also miss the good old days when all isps offered a news feed but those days are well and truly gone now :(. However if you can find a decent news server and are having trouble setting it up I am more than happy to guide you through the details, it is actually reasonably straightforward...
