### Post by weblordpepe on 2007-11-07
I belieeeve you should change the DPI setting.
Go to System / Preferences / Appearance

Then in the appearance preferences:
Click the Fonts tab, then the 'D_e_tails' button.

In that dialog box you'll have the DPI setting. Change it to 96.
