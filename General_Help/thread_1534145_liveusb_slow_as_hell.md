### Post by valbaca on 2010-07-19
ajgreeny's suggestion should work.
If not, have you tried another USB drive? Not all keys are made the same, and some show significant speed differences. For example, my 1GB drive is much faster than my 4GB so I almost always use it for files and installing from USB.
 
You may also try small tweaks to reduce writing/reading from the USB. One example is setting Firefox to have no cache. Webpages may load slower (it has to load it all over again each time) but the reduced read/write from the USB drive will make other programs faster. 
 
Searching for solutions for "solid state drives" could turn up solutions that are just as applicable to running from liveusb.
