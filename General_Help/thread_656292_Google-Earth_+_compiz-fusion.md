### Post by skain on 2008-01-11
Have you tried running Google Earth after disabling compiz-fusion first?

Hit 'alt-F2' to bring up the run command then type in "metacity --replace" without the quotes.

I have a much improved experience with Google Earth this way.

When you're done with Google Earth you can do a "compiz --replace" in the same way described above to get your desktop effects back.

I hope this helps.
