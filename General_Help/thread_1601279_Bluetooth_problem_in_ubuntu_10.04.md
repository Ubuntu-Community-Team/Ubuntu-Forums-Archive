### Post by P4man on 2010-10-20
First make your phone visible / findable. Then in ubuntu bluetooth menu, setup a new device, select the phone, then click PIN options.  Pick any you want, 000 or a custom PIN, just know which one you took,  click forward. Then on your phone accept the pairing request and enter the same pin you selected on your PC.
