### Post by bwhite82 on 2010-04-08
Alright, I managed to switch it from yahoo to google. Goto 'about:config' in the address bar. In the filter box type 'search' and change the following values:
[B]
browser.search.defaultenginename[/B] (change from Yahoo to Google)

**browser.search.order.1** (change from Yahoo to Google)

What I'd really like to do is get rid of the quick search altogether.
