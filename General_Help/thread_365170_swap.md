### Post by Patrick K on 2007-02-19
Probably the easiest way to check on the swap is in System Monitor (System>Administration). On  the Resources tab, look at memory and swap history. If the swap is on the size will be shown, of not "0" will be shown.

If you have the disk space go with 750mb to 1gb. 

Linux is a lot slower on my system than windows. Opening programs is where that is most noticeable. But opening the menus is quite a bit slower, too.
