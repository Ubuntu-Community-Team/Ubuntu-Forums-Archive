### Post by TheOnlyMrK on 2009-12-02
Look at my thread here: [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1343390](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1343390) this is a reported bug now and should be fixed in future updates.
For a temporary fix... Go to System > Administration > System Monitor > "Processes" tab > Select "nautilus" > Click "End Process" > Then go to the folder you were looking at before and your files will show.
