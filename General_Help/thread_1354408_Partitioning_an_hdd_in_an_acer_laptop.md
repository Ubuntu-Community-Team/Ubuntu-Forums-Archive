### Post by Sin@Sin-Sacrifice on 2009-12-14
Yeah... as long as your PC can boot from a flash drive. Check your BIOS for the boot order and see if USB is an option. Go to System>Administration>USB Startup Disk Creator. Pretty self explanatory from there.
