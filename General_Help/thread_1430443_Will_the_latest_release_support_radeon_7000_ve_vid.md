### Post by Mark Phelps on 2010-03-16

> With Karmic there's some support for the ati radeon 7000 ve, but it's the default driver with limited or no 3d function. Previous distros supported radeon 7000 ve. Is it likely to expect that Lucid will not support this video card.

More to the point is old hardware support fading away?

As far as AMD/ATI is concerned, "old hardware" support HAS faded away.  They stopped supporting what they now call legacy cards nearly a year ago, and in their Release Notes, clearly said they had no intentions of updating the legacy drivers. So, there's no point in hold out hope for any new driver versions for the legacy cards.

The "new" drivers that are being seen in Lucid, are the open source drivers, and while they might have better 3D support than the existing drivers, they are all that us legacy card owners can use now.
