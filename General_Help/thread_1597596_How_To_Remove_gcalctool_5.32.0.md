### Post by dino99 on 2010-10-15
ubuntu-desktop is only a meta-package: ie it list the default packages, but nothing else

So dont worry, let it be removed, you will reinstall it later, after installing gcalctool as you need (and choose, into synaptic, to not update it anymore)

choose one there:
[http://packages.ubuntu.com/fr/lucid/gcalctool](http://packages.ubuntu.com/fr/lucid/gcalctool)
