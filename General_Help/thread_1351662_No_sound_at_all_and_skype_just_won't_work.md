### Post by Chronon on 2009-12-10
I would recommend double checking the sound levels in PulseAudio.  I have found that in some cases mute has been turned on in pulse and using a tool to change this setting results in functional audio.  I'm not sure of the preferred tool, but I have had success installing padevchooser and using the "Volume Control" applet in it.
