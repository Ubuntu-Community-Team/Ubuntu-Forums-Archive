### Post by silverglade00 on 2010-09-16
You really don't want to share home folders, especially with a beta version. You can share swap partitions, but only if you don't hibernate your computer. Just let 10.10 install into the remaining free space. It will keep /home as just a part of that partition. You can mount your other home folder within it if you need to access files on there.
