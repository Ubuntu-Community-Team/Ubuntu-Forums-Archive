### Post by paydaydaddy on 2009-05-17
Hover your cursor over the desktop area in the upper left of your screen and a little slide out panel with three tiny icons will appear. Click on the one that looks like a little end wrench to open the "Folder View Settings" window. Click on "Display". At the bottom of the window you will find icon arrangement options.
