### Post by anotherdisciple on 2008-11-01
This may be a really old tip that I was to dumb to notice, but here it goes...

I have always had trouble with watching videos on VLC while my effects were turned on. The video was always flashing/blinking, but there is a simple solution.

Start VLC, navigate to (Tools > Preferences > Video) and set the "Output Module" to X11 video output.


Everything works fine now, I can't believe it took so long for me to figure this out.
