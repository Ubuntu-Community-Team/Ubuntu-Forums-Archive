### Post by ryu kun on 2010-08-27
I use Xubuntu 10.04 with Hamster for time tracking. To be able to enable Hamster applet on the panel I installed **xfce4-xfapplet-plugin** package. I do not know about Lubuntu but it solved this issue for me in Xubuntu.

I know it is not the solution you seek for but you can try to use gnome-panel as it is explained [here]("http://art.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?p=9443928") if it is possible in Lubuntu.

Again not a good answer to your question but another alternative coming to my mind is using [Grindstone2]("http://www.epiforge.com/Grindstone2/") with Wine.
