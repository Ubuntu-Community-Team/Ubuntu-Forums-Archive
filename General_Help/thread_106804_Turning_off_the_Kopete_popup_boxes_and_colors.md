### Post by thefish on 2007-03-01
I agree that this is really annoying, but its pretty easy to sort out: In the main kopete window, click settings > configure notifications. You need to click the advanced button to show that "Show a message in a popup window" is ticked, untick it for all the things you dont want (like "a contact has come online" etc) and feel the joy.
