### Post by Kinslayer on 2010-05-27
It does seem to remember settings with respect to an individual document. It is only that the first time you open a given file that it reverts to the predetermined behaviour.  

I long ago gave up on Evince remembering to maximise itself, and make Compiz handle it.  If you have CCSM enabled (the advanced settings) the very bottom item is Window Rules.  In the middle of the Matches tab is an option for Maximised.  Type class=Evince into that window, or into the Fullscreen area if you'd rather it be even bigger.  Alternatively, you can 'grab' any appropriate window after clicking on the plus at the end of the field.
