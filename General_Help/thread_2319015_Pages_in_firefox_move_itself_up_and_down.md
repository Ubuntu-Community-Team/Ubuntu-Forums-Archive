### Post by ajgreeny on 2016-03-31
In the FF **Edit->Preferences->Advanced** tab deselect the "Use Autoscrolling" option to see if that helps stop this.

It should only happen with a middle click of the mouse in an empty spot in the page, then moving the cursor up or down from where you middle clicked, so perhaps you are managing to middle click without realising it using your trackpad.
