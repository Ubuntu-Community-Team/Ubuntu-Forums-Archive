For suspend, check for other power management funtions and enable them.

### Post by sboutwell on 2008-02-21
Just picked one of these units up they are calling it the PC-1 PC2500  from PC-Club here on the west coast for 89.00 with Case and Power supply.  They are also including gOS with it and billing it as a Developers Box.  

I've been running it now for a week with a SATA 160Gig and thrown several installations of LINUX at it and been testing it.

Roughest part has been getting the Openchrome stuff working but found a small link that stated how to do it.  Worked great from there didn't have to do the MANUAL install and rough stuff.

It does fairly well with Stock and Modified Gnome installations however it doesn't like alot of the ADVANCED graphic FEATURES.

It didn't care for KDE 4.0 but then again, that still in pretty early states.

It REALLY wasn't happy with Ubuntu 8.04 RC2, it would lock up only after a few minutes of running and threw alot of strange errors when trying to modify things.  (Suspect it's more of the shape of the BETA's in that release.  Specifically some of the Python stuff it was choking on when I got to looking at the logs.)

Just blew it away tonight for the final time and getting set to build something useful out of it until I can see how it handles something like a TV Card and MythTV on it.  Going to look around and see if I can score one cheap.  

It you looking for a GREAT TESTING box, this would definitely FIT the bill.
