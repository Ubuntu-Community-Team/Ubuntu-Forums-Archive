### Post by hwttdz on 2010-11-10
gnome-screensaver-command -h will give you some information about how you can interact with the screensaver.  Such as forcing it to turn on right now, asking it if it's inhibited, and why.  It will even tell you the reason and the program inhibiting.  Hope that helps.
