### Post by psychicdragon on 2005-06-09
I think i figured out another way... it's not too pretty though  :roll: 
download this rpm:
[http://www-ftp.lip6.fr/pub/linux/distributions/Mandrakelinux/devel/cooker/x86_64/media/contrib/muinescrobbler-0.1.5-2mdk.noarch.rpm](http://www-ftp.lip6.fr/pub/linux/distributions/Mandrakelinux/devel/cooker/x86_64/media/contrib/muinescrobbler-0.1.5-2mdk.noarch.rpm)
open it with file-roller
and drop the .dll into ~/gnome2/muine/plugins
seems to work for me

yeah, it works!
These tracks are all played in muine
[http://www.audioscrobbler.com/user/psychicdragon/](http://www.audioscrobbler.com/user/psychicdragon/)
