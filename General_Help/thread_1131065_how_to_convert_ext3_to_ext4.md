### Post by Darael on 2009-04-20
The above post is incorrect.

Conversion of ext3 to ext4 can only be done AFTER upgrading to Jaunty, as Hardy and Intrepid don't support ext4.  However, there are clear instructions for converting an ext3 filesystem to ext4 at the bottom of [http://ext4.wiki.kernel.org/index.php/Ext4_Howto](http://ext4.wiki.kernel.org/index.php/Ext4_Howto) if you wish to convert.

UPDATE: slightly differing instructions [here]("http://kernelnewbies.org/Ext4#head-3891522e0601162aab24c73c1f148a1e28c6a9d4").
