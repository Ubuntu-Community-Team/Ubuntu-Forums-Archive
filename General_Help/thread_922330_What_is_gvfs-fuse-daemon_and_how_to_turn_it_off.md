### Post by dannym978 on 2008-09-17
Hi, looking at my disk usage only half of my 300gb hard disk appears to be available with 50% of it allocated to something called gvfs-fuse-daemon.

What is this feature and how can i disable it to free up all of my hard drive for use?

Thanks

---

### Post by drs305 on 2008-09-17
gvfs is the ubuntu virtual file system. It is not actually using any of your disk space. if you are using Disk Usage Analyser/Baobab, you can stop if from reporting the gvfs by editing the preferences and disabling gvfs reporting.
