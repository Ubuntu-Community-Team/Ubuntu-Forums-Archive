### Post by stoodleysnow on 2007-12-06
Try opening your PC up and check the capacitors on the motherboard. If they are oozing brown stuff or bulging, it may be causing a repeated hardware error, which would then mess up HAL during startup. If they ARE oozing brown stuff or bulging, you will need to replace your mobo, as it has Capacitor Plague. If not, listen carefully to your PSU. If it makes a high-pitched noise, it may be nearing the end of its lifetime, again due to Capacitor Plague. For more info, please visit [www.badcaps.net](www.badcaps.net)
Remember: HAL does hardware. If HAL goes wrong, check that hardware.
