### Post by blendmaster on 2006-11-03
Hello!

This tip is to help the problem of Frostwire stating that it is starting it's connection, but never doing so.

This is just a small tip that I thought I'd post here because I've seen the same problem over and over again with Frostwire and connecting.

First, this is for people who have already gotten Frostwire working. To do this, use [Automatix]("http://getautomatix.com/wiki/index.php?title=Installation&Itemid=38"), which makes installing a lot of things a really easy process.
Second, I'd also like to say that this isn't a definitive answer to the problem, and it may or may not work (but it did for me, and that's why I'm posting). And third, I don't know why this works, so someone else may have to answer that.

Okay, the problem is is when Frostwire begins its connection process. Usually it says that it's starting connection for a little bit, and then changes to say that it has an excellent condition. However, sometimes you may notice that it never says that it has reached a connection, and it continually says "starting connection".

This problem occurred after I upgraded from Dapper to Edgy, so I'm guessing that the people who have this problem will be on Edgy as well.

Well, when you start Frostwire, it puts an icon next to your system clock (see attachment). This icon has the solution to our problem. After opening Frostwire, simply right click the icon and click restore. It should now have an excellent connection!

I know, this sounds really dumb. I just wanted to help the other people that seemed to also have this problem so that when they search for it, they can find the answer.

Hope this is helpful!
