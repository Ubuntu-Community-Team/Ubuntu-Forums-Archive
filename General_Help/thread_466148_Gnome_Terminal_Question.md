### Post by stylishpants on 2007-06-06
Fullscreen mode (hit F11) looks windowless, but only if you have just one single tab.  It's not quite windowless otherwise.

Transparency is not hard to get:
Right click, choose "Edit current profile", choose "Effects" tab, select "Transparent Background" radio button, and use the slider to adjust the transparency to your taste.
