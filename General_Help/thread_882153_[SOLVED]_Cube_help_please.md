### Post by mb_webguy on 2008-08-06
Just change the desktop background in System->Preferences->Appearance on the Background tab.

EDIT: ...assuming you're talking about the desktop background, rather than the cube caps.  If you're talking about the area behind the cube, then that's the skydome that Ryadt mentioned.  Do what he said to change it.
