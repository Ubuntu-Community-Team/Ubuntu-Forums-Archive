### Post by fragos on 2008-03-11
I tried Gnucash and after 5 months of use my ledger data base vanished.  I don't know why or what role I played.  I didn't want to risk that again as I had to reenter all transactions from my paper files.  I started using Grisbi.  It's not as polished as Gnucash but it's been working for me.  I'm a consultant and have no employees to be concerned with.  I will check out MoneyDance.  A new release of Grisbi is due soon.

### Post by Wharf Rat on 2008-03-11
