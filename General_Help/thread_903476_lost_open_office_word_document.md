### Post by drs305 on 2008-08-28
It still might be located somewhere, perhaps with a ~ symbol attached to it.

You can do a systemwide search for it with:
```
sudo find / -type f -iname *filename*
```

You can reduce the hits if the filename is fairly common by eliminating the * individually.
Good luck!
