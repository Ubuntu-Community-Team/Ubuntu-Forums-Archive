### Post by jcobban on 2012-01-27
In 10.04 one of the items in the "Places" menu is "Computer". This place is above the File System, and includes, among other things, the DVD drive.  However I cannot find an equivalent "place" in 11.10.  What is the equivalent, and how do I get there?

---

### Post by 3rdalbum on 2012-01-27
Click on the desktop. Go to the top panel and choose Go > Computer.

When a DVD is inserted, its icon will appear on the Launcher (you know, the sidebar on the left). It'll be right at the bottom so you might need to scroll down to reach it.
