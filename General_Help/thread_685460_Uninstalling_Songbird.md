### Post by SpiderGorilla on 2008-02-02
Okay, try this:

System > Administration > Synaptic Package Manager

Click "Find"

Type "songbird"

Should bring up your app. Click that and click the box next to it and choose "Mark for complete removal". It'll tell you any dependencies that rely on that system if any. If none, good, go ahead and click "Apply".
