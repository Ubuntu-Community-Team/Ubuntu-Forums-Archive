### Post by yeeeev on 2009-08-07
I had the same problem.
You should try to properly configure the sound devices in your skype settings.
Open skype's options menu and go to "sound devices".
You'll then see options for sound in, sound out and ringing.
For me "sound out" and "ringing" are set to pulse and "sound in" is set to the actual device.
You should probably make sure that the recording levels on your AlsaMixer are OK. (Through the Volume Control). Skype tends to modify them. This behaviour can also be turned off using a switch in Skype's  "sound devices" screen.

Hope it helps...:)
