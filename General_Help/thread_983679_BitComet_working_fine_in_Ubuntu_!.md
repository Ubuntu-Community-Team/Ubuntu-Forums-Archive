Hello ,

Despite many messages pointing to the incompatibility of BitComet(BC) and Ubuntu , I've been using it for a month and it runs smooth 
(versions BC=1.05 & Ubuntu=8.04) , better yet than in Windows .
With BC has been possible to get a connection where Azureus and others were apparently blocked by the ISP ( better check this again !).
To have BC running , download it [**from here]("http://www.bitcomet.com/doc/download.htm") **, and launch the executable with **wine**. A minor inconvenience , is the flickering when refreshing its screen form besides to be a 'wine' solution.
Please, give a try !
Vkim
