### Post by tripolitan on 2010-01-03
Message says restart, you say it shuts down, I will go with your story and guess it is a heat issue but without any other info, I don't see how anyone else might help you.

Heat causes random shutdowns, look for clogged heat sinks, dirty/fried fans, under-rated power supply...

### Post by Rayn on 2010-01-03
The restart was me turning it back on.

This issue is now solved, the CPU fan died and the system was overheating.

(I checked the CPU temperature and it was at 260 deg F after a shutdown, holy crap)

I'm going to take this opportunity to replace the CPU.
