### Post by MasterXandrex on 2008-07-09
Ubuntu is a seperate operating system completely, you can move most of the files but you cannot migrate most programs (with few exceptions). It has nothing to do with windows and you will not find it or anything related to it in your program list. 

You will find many open source (aka free) alternative to the programs that you use in Windows if you are willing to go through the time to get used to the change.
