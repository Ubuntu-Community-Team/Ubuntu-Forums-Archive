### Post by scarleo on 2011-11-23
You can share the files with Samba from Ubuntu:
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Samba](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Samba)

And the install a samba browser from the market on the tablet, e.g. AndSMB.

Guess there are a ton of other ways too but this is pretty simple. Don't know how ushare works.

EDIT: Ah, ushare is upnp, that'll probably let you play media files on your tablet with the right app installed, not sharing other files if that's what you're trying to do. If you're trying to play media only then look in market for UPnPlay or just search for upnp for a lot of options
