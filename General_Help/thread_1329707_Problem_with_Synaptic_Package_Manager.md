### Post by coffeecat on 2009-11-17
Is this on a fresh install? Have you done a 'reload' in Synaptic yet? If not, click on 'reload' to get the package metadata.

---

### Post by Vermind on 2009-11-17
In Synaptic, if you click the "Status" button on the lower left, and then "All" on the upper left, the statusbar will show something like 28000 packages listed. This is all the packages available as defined by your software sources or repositories.

### Post by joaqle on 2009-11-17
Coffeecat and Vermind, thank you very much for your help.

In settings, I changed the server from my country to the main server, then reload, and now there are 6195 packages listed.
