### Post by limotux on 2011-01-16
Ok... sorted out and I'd like to share it with you.
Installed the following packages:
```
default-jre (1.6-34)
gcj-4.4-jre-headless (4.4.3-1ubuntu2)
libaccess-bridge-java (1.26.2-3)
libaccess-bridge-java-jni (1.26.2-3)
openjdk-6-jre (6b20-1.9.2-0ubuntu1~10.04.1)
```
