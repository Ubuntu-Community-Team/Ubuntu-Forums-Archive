### Post by Viper00 on 2013-01-30
Type "about:config" into the address bar.  A message will appear just click "I'll be careful, I promise!.". Search for "**plugins.click_to_play**".  Just double click that line and it should switch true.  Then just restart Firefox and it should work.

Here's an article about it    [http://www.webupd8.org/2012/09/3-hidden-features-in-firefox-15-you-may.html](http://www.webupd8.org/2012/09/3-hidden-features-in-firefox-15-you-may.html)
