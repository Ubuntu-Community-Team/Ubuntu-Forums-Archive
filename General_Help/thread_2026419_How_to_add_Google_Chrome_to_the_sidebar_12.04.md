How do I add Chrome to the launcher in 12.04? When I installed it yesterday, I didn't get the option to keep it in the launcher and can't seem to find a way to put it in there.

### Post by Pilot6 on 2012-07-15
Start Chrome. Then right-click the icon in the launcher and choose keep it there. Or just drag and drop the icon from dash.

---

### Post by Shadius on 2012-07-15
Yes, you can right-click on the icon in the Launcher and select "Keep in Launcher" or just drag the icon from the Dash into the Launcher.
