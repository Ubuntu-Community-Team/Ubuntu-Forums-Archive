I'll answer this now, but if you have any other questions about using the forum software, you are very welcome to ask in the [Forum Feedback & Help]("https://ubuntuforums.org/forumdisplay.php?f=48") section. 

To subscribe to a thread, click on the Thread Tools dropdown near the top right of the thread. Then click on "Subscribe to the this thread". You'll then have a choice of notification types. "Through my control panel" isn't very well expressed. To access your control panel, you click on the "settings" link near the top right of any page. Once you have 10 beans (posts in support areas) you will be able to auto-subscribe to all threads you post to through General Settings in your settings page.
