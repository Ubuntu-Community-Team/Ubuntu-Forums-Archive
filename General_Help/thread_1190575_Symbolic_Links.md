### Post by andrew.46 on 2009-06-18
Hi theozzlives,

Just as an example: if you are one of the many who watch youtube videos and then retrieve them from your temp folder a symbolic link from your Desktop would make this much easier:

```

cd Desktop
ln -s /tmp

```

And now you one click access to your temp folder :-). Lots of other uses but this might furnish a start and an example?
