### Post by Peter09 on 2010-10-12
Its sounds like you have installed Unity (which is the new netbook interface on Maverick). Unfortunaltly Unity was late to the party and my understanding is that it is still full of problems. 
 
You should be able to get back to a normal desktop by selecting Gnome from the Session menu at the bottom of the screen just before you enter you password. Other than that I think its a question of waiting for upgrades to clear some of the problems.
 
Someone may have a more uptodate fix for this so keep lwatching this space.
 
Edit: Also I believe Unity demands Composting - which on an old machine may not be possible.
