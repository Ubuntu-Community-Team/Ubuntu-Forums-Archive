### Post by PilotJLR on 2007-04-21
This happened to me too. Comment out the entry for your cdrom (which I can see you have already found) in /etc/fstab

By commenting out this line and rebooting, me optical drives automount again.

I assume some other daemon is controlling removeable media now, but I have not researched this yet...
