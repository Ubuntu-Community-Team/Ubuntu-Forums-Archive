### Post by ajgreeny on 2016-02-16
Click the network icon in your panel, go to Edit.
In the window that opens click on the connection you want to autoconnect, and in the General tab make sure the boxes for "Automatically connect" and "All users may connect" are selected.

NB: I am looking at this in Xubuntu 14.04 and with an ethernet wired connection, but I think it is very similar, if not exactly the same, in Ubuntu.
