### Post by Mark Phelps on 2011-07-11
OK ... STOP bumping every few hours.  The general rule around here is no more often than once every 24 hours.  Your impatience at getting an answer is not helping the situation.

There is no GENERAL solution for freezing problems as they can be caused by a variety of things, most usually, excessive heat.  This seems to affect 11.04 because we've been getting lots of complaints from folks with their laptops running very hot or crashing due to overheating.

So, is yours overheating? OR is it simply freezing?

Also, if it did NOT do it with Maverick, what have you installed that is different from Maverick? Are you running restricted video drivers (ATI/AMD or NVidia)?  Asking because there have been problems reported with these in 11.04.
