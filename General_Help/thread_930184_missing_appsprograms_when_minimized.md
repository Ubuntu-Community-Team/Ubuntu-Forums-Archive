### Post by pastalavista on 2008-09-25
Right click on any current panel and select 'new panel' and locate it at the bottom (or wherever you want it to be) then right-click on it and click 'add to panel' and then add the widow selector and workspace selector and whatever else you want on it. You'll find it pretty easy to configure exactly to your liking once you get the hang of it.
