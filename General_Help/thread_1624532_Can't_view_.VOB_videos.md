### Post by asmoore82 on 2010-11-17
*.VOB files are not meant to be directly playable.

You have to open the entire Collection of them as a DVD-Video.
Also, if it's a store-bought disc it's very likely encrypted so additional software is needed.

See: [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RestrictedFormats/PlayingDVDs](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RestrictedFormats/PlayingDVDs)
