### Post by lswb on 2008-10-04
The proposed repositories can be a gamble. IF you have a particular problem that you feel may be corrected with a newer version of a program from the proposed repo, it is worth trying, BUT sometimes the versions in proposed can bork your system. I've seen posts by people who had their networking for instance temporarily disabled by using an update from the proposed repository, or X stop working. While those examples may be extreme, it's best to be prepared.
