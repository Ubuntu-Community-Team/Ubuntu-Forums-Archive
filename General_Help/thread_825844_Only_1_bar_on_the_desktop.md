I'm very new to Linux. I was wondering how i could get rid of one of the start menus on the desktop. Just have everything be on 1 start menu instead of on top and on bottom. ### Post by roycrom on 2008-06-11
You should be able to just drag everything from one bar to the other.

Use the middle mouse button to grab an item on one panel, drag it to the other and then let go.

When you have moved everything, right click on the empty panel and choose "_D_elete This Panel".

HTH
