Hi everyone,

I found a replacement for every app I used to have on windows except one: Cropper ([http://www.codeplex.com/cropper](http://www.codeplex.com/cropper)). Cropper is basically an icon that sits in the system tray. When you click on it, a transparent window pop ups on top of your screen. You can resize it. When you're happy with what it overlays, you double click it, and bang, screenshot (in the format of your choice, jpg compression settings modifiable, etc). 

I can't find anything like that on linux - maybe one of you know something similar?
