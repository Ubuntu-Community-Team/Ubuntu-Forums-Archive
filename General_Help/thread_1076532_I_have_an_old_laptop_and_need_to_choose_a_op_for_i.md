### Post by yankeeDDL on 2009-02-21
I've installed Ubuntu on a Dell Inspiron 8000 (dated back in 2001).
It has 512MB of RAM (I expanded the original 256MB some time ago) and a Pentium 3 - I believe @ 600MHz or so.
You may also want to consider a new hard disk: if it's old it probably spins at 4200rpm. Today's 2.5" drives spin at 5400 (most of them at least) and have much more capacity, which is always a plus.

Ubuntu recognized all the hardware without problem. It's not as fast as I hoped for (I have used 8.10), but it is definitely usable; something I cannot tell of Win XP.

BTW, the laptop came with Windows ME. I wiped it out after 4 months of agony: how did you survive so long?
