### Post by brian-mccumber on 2015-12-20
I am also running 14.04 and I hit Super + p and Super + Shift + p cause I couldn't figure out if you just hit super plus lowercase p or held shift for upper case p and it did nothing. You can hold down the super key and it will show you all the hotkey combos and a description of what they do and there was no super + p hotkey combos in the overlay. Try to go to system settings ---> displays and set your resolution back to what it was before this thing happened. It could be that particular key combo is being used by some other software that you installed other than Ubuntu OS.

You  can also get to your key combos by going to System Settings ---> Keyboard --->Shortcuts.
