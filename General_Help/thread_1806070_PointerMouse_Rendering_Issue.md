### Post by Kchymet on 2011-07-17
EDIT: The beneath is WRONG. Turns out it was actually a glitch with Xinerama. It was ONLY happening on the right of my 2 monitors. Right after I tried changing themes I noticed it was not happening, but I observed this in the left monitor.

Well... I just fixed it. It would be immediately after I spent a week trying to fix it, finally asking this forum >.>. Turns out I'm missing some pointer images in the folder for some of my themes. The pointer image requested when highlighting that feature was not downloaded with Chrome (I had my preferences set to use the Chrome classic theme, rather than the GTK+ theme). In the other instances, my desktop theme was different, and those pointers were also not in my filesystem. It was simply a matter of changing themes on Ubuntu (and using the GTK+ Theme option on Chrome).
