This is a question that i've had for a long time.
 
Okay, lets say I download a torrent away from home, but I keep, and seed all of my torrents off my external hard drive.

How do I tell my Torrent client, that when I get home and move the files to my external, that it really is there just in a different place.

### Post by mb_webguy on 2009-05-14
It depends on your torrent client.  Most will let you change the location of the files.  If you still have the torrent in your client, look for some sort of torrent-specific settings, and change the location of the files to the new location.  Then rescan the torrent, and it should begin seeding.
