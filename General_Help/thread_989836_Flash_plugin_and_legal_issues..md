### Post by Bucky Ball on 2008-11-22
Your best option is to go to the Adobe site and try to find the license agreement for the particular plug-in version number of Flashplayer you are using in your Kiosk. One thing to keep in mind is that although they are proprietary plug-ins, they are free of charge. Just like the plug-ins for windoze. Hisham777 says he is using them to *play* Flash files. The problem I see is the Flash files you are actually watching. How were they created? If it was on dodgy software in Windoze, you might think again.
