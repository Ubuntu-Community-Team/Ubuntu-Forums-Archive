So, when patching use the Blizzard Downloader to navigate to 'View', then 'Preferences'.

**Check** the box that says 'Dont throttle Background Downloader'

This will make the downloader use ALL the bandwidth it can, and although it will speed up your download, it will slow down other processes that use your internet connection, so obviously dont try to play WoW or other MMO's.

Also,

**Uncheck** the box that says 'Enable Peer-to-Peer transfer.

This will obviously stop you downloading your patch from other people, but will give you a direct download, which will enable the patch to download roughly two times as fast.

NOTE: If downloading at night, it may be best to enable P2P, due to a larger traffic, however, experiment! it will save you hours off your download.

especially with WLK expansion coming out!
