### Post by Tim Sharitt on 2009-01-05
I've had ATI cards on my last two computers and they have both worked fine. The main drawback is that to use fully utilize the acceleration capabilities of the card, you must use the bianary-only drivers provided by ATI. That is not to say there is anything wrong with the drivers, but updates are confined to the whims of the ati developers and the closed source nature of them can cause other problems as well.
