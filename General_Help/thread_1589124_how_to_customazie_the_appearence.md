### Post by robert shearer on 2010-10-06
If you mean an applications title bar then what kerry_s said.

But if you mean the panels at the top and bottom of your screen then...

Right click on any free space on the panel and from the pop-up box that appears select "Properties"

This will open a window (Panel properties)where you can adjust the height of the panel.  (size  xx pixels)

If you mean some other 'Title Bar' then maybe post a screenshot of the one you want to change.
