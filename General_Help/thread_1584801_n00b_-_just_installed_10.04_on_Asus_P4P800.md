### Post by cascade9 on 2010-09-29
There should be no problem with the audio at all. If you arent getting any sounds, check the mixer (sometimes its muted). 


Normally with most linux distros, you dont have to find chipset/ethernet port/audio drivers etc, they tend to be built into the kernel or other software, like alsa (advanced linux sound architecture). Most video cards get working free, oppen soruce drivers as well, but they tend to be slower on 3D than the proprietary drivers and have less features. 

As for the video, there may, or may not be any proprietary drivers avaible (depends on the card). If there are any proprietary drivers around, they should be found if you check in- System-> Administration-> Hardware drivers. 

There should be a torrent client already installed (transmission). I'm not that fond of transmission myself, I much perfer deluge, but transmission should do the job. ****
