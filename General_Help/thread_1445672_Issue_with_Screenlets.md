I recently downloaded and installed the 'info panel' screenlet. It is lovely, and a great alternative to conky. The only issue I am having is that, no matter how many times I configure it, my weather zip code will NOT save across reboots, it keeps defaulting to the 'Czech Republic'

I have tried entering my zip code, and my location/weather code from right clicking on the info panel and selecting "zip code", and also from the screenlets configuration menu. I have disabled the other 2 locations, I have tried every option I can think of.

### Post by Johnny B on 2010-04-02
Quote from [http://gtk-apps.org/content/show.php/InfoPanel?content=100042]("http://gtk-apps.org/content/show.php/InfoPanel?content=100042"):
> This is a known bug of all screenlets.

Solution: You must configure the settings on this screenlet and then you have to click on "Re-Start All" in Screenlets Manager. And now is your settings saved.
