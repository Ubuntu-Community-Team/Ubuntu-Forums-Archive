### Post by kperkins on 2005-11-23
open up a terminal and type "whereis kstreamripper" that'll show you where the executable is (probably /usr/bin/*)
You can also try just typing in kstreamripper and see if that starts it.  If it does then you can add a launcher to your gnome menu and/or panel manually (there's lots of posts about that here.
