If you are going to be printing a lot I recommend AGAINST an ink jet printer.  The cartridge cost for a frequent printer eats you up.

Color lasers are a far superior way to print and the toner cartridges last FAR longer than ink jet.  You will spend a little bit more to buy the printer but far less in teh long run.

With that I recommend any HP color laserjet printer.  I own the 2650dn (bought for $430 at Costco) and it is an excellent printer.  If faxing is something you don't need then just get a plain old flatbed scanner.  The scanner will allow you to make a .jpg of what you are scanning and then you can print it to the color laser.

IMO those all in one print/copy/fax are POS waste of money.  I stopped using ink jets a long time ago and have never looked back.
