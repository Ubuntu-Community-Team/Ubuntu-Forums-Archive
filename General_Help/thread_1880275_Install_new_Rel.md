### Post by Mark Phelps on 2011-11-13
While some folks claim that 11.10 is presenting them no problems, a LOT of others complain about serious problems, especially with upgrades from previous versions.

I suggest that you download and burn an Ubuntu LiveCD of version 11.10, boot from that, and see how well that works -- before you attempt any kind of upgrade.
