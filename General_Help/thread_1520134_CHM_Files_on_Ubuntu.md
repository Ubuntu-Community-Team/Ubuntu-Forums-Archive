### Post by Leppie on 2010-06-29

scroll down to the bottom off the page, there's a box with some writings. the download link is where it says "all".

> **cancer10 said:**
> also it is possible to get the installer along with its dependencies in one single .DEB or in one .ZIP archive?
no it's not. basically because apt-get/aptitude normally analyses the installed packages on a system when retrieving dependencies. since most systems are not the same, the list of the to-be-downloaded dependencies is very likely to be different most of the times.
however, if you clicke the dependencies it will take you to their pages where you can download them ;)
