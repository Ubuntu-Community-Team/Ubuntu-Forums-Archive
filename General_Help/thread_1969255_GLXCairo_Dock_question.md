### Post by LakeWind on 2012-04-29
On the bottom right hand side of the Cairo Dock config screen there should be an "Apply" button and a "Cancel" button. There's also a "Save" tab under themes, where you can choose to save any changes made to customize a theme with the "save as" dialog. 

Not sure why these options aren't showing up for you. Do you have enough screen real estate to see the entire dialog box?
