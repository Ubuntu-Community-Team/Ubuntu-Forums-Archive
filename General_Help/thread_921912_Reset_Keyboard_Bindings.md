### Post by mali2297 on 2008-09-17
Remove the hidden file **.gconf** in your home directory (or move it to a backup). It will however reset other settings as well.

---

### Post by Kangarooo on 2011-04-28
remove the directories global_keybindings, keybinding_commands, window_keybindings from the directory ~/.gconf/apps/metacity and logout/login
