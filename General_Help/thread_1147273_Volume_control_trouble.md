### Post by faytaliti on 2009-05-03
Problem solved! Follow the mentioned thread to select the devices that the devices must control:

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1004978&highlight=volume+controls](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1004978&highlight=volume+controls)

Also, make sure that the "device" in use by the volume applet and the device used in system>preferences>sounds are the same. In my case it is HDA Intel (ALSA), the default device ALSA sound device.
