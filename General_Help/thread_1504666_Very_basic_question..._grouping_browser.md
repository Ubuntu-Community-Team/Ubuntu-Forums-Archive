For example, like in windows, all the firefox tabs will fall under the same group and only display as 7 (as in 7 browsers were opened) and all the openoffice will be grouped into another group

right click on the dots (or lines) left of your window list applet (the one that shows the open applications on the panel) and left click on "preferences"
then choose what you want for grouping
