### Post by merlinus on 2009-07-08
I you have a separate /home partition, then all of your configurations, setups, bookmarks, email and such will be retained during reinstall or install of a newer version.

Simply choose to not format that partition.

You will, however, have to reinstall the applications that do not come on the install cd.
