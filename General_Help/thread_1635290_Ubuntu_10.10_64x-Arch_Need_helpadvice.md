### Post by akand074 on 2010-12-01
About your graphics issue. Do you have your proprietary drivers installed? You can go to  System > Administration > Additional Drivers if you haven't. It's very important to get those installed. Also, when you added the indicator panel, what happens? You said you tried it many times. Does it just not get added? Seems like most of your issues are with your panel. Maybe try right clicking your panel and choosing "New Panel". Delete your old panel and add everything back to the new Panel see if that works. 

If you decide to try a clean install again, make sure you burn it at a slow speed and choose "verify after burn". I would stick with 10.10 64 bit, though you could always go with 10.04 there isn't much difference but I wouldn't drop to 32 bit. Not sure why your having all these problems but I hope this helps at all and you manage to fix it. Good luck.
