### Post by duojet on 2008-05-02
So I've almost got a stable hardy install. Only problem is that half the time when I start my computer, I get to the login screen with no issues, but after that, I get no splash screen, everything goes blank, and the hard drive activity stops. I've left it alone for 5 minutes or so, and nothing happens. 

If I do a couple hard restarts (without changing anything else) everything is fine.. This isn't a panic situation, but I'm pretty sure that a bunch of hard restarting isn't good for my box, not to mention it's a little inconvenient.

### Post by duojet on 2008-05-19
Well, I found a solution.. In the system settings / monitor & display / hardware I switched from plug 'n play (which had been automatically selected in the installation) to 1024 x 768.
