### Post by snowpine on 2012-11-25
Alt+` (above the TAB key)
or
Alt+TAB until you get to Firefox, down-arrow, left/right arrows
or
move mouse cursor to top left of screen for "spread/expose" view

None of this is hidden/secret knowledge. Open source software is very well-documented.

[http://askubuntu.com/questions/28086/what-are-unitys-keyboard-and-mouse-shortcuts](http://askubuntu.com/questions/28086/what-are-unitys-keyboard-and-mouse-shortcuts)
