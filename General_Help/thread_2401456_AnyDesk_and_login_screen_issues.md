### Post by Neil_Burcham on 2019-05-28
Same here...the workaround I found was to use the clipboard:  I use KeepassXC, and copy the pw from the entry I made (saves to clipboard).  Or you can use a text editor (pluma, gedit, etc)...the downside being the remote pw is exposed on your host pc.  I like the KeepassXC method because it does not require the remote pc's pw to be exposed.
There is a slight reconnect after the login button is pressed, but it comes back quickly, logged-in, and works as expected.

Hope that helps!
