### Post by rogue_0111 on 2010-01-24
Seems odd.


My troubleshooting steps would be:
1. bring up a terminal and ping the router (ping 192.161.1.1) (You are looking for how many packets get sent, received and lost)
2. try pinging let's say google and see what happens (ping [www.google.com](www.google.com)) (looking for lost packets again)
2. connecting directly to the router and see if issue persists
3. If issue persists, unplug modem, plug it back in, get a paper clip and hit the reset button back for about 60 seconds (modem will reboot) then check firefox again.
4. Another solution would be to see if another PC on r network is having the same connection issue.

good luck
