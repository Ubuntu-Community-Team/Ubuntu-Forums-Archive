### Post by warddr on 2010-08-13
Maybe I can explain some of the behaviour of your computer:
The cursor jumping around thing is probably you touching your touchpad while typing, and the text-insert is probably you pushing both the left and the right mouse button at the same time, if you do that the text on your clipboard will be pasted.
