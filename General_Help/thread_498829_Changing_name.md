### Post by John.Michael.Kane on 2007-07-11
Click on System---->Administration------>Users and Groups

You will be asked your pass code enter it.

A dialog box will then show. 

Click on the name you want to change. Then click on properties.

You will see two things

Username
Real name 

You should be able to change them to what you want.
