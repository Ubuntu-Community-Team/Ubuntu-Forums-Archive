### Post by gordintoronto on 2009-12-06
Evolution data is stored in a hidden folder called .evolution

Change your Preferences in Nautilus to "Show hidden and backup files."

When I converted to Ubuntu, I used Thunderbird under Windows to get my email into a form which Evolution could import.  It took a little time, but the result was perfect.
