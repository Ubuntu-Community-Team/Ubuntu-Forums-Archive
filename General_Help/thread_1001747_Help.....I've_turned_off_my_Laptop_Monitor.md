### Post by ddixonr on 2008-12-04
On my toshiba notebook, my screen mode is switched using fn+F5. I once lost both tv and laptop screen. I got it back by slowly pushing fn+F5 once, then waiting a couple seconds. Then twice, then waiting. And so on. I don't remember how many times I pushed it, but eventually you cycle around the options to get back to the laptop screen.
