### Post by heindsight on 2009-02-19
You can go to System>Preferences>Keyboard, then choose the USA International (AltGr dead keys) layout (click the add button to add the layout to the list, and then remove the previous layouts).
Next, click the "Layout Options" button and choose a compose key (under Compose Key Position - I use right alt) and choose a "Third level chooser" key (I use the win keys). 

Now you can use Right_Alt / o to get ø 
Right_Alt a e to get æ or
Win+' followed by E to get É etc.
Play around with different Right_Alt and Win combinations to find the characters you need.
