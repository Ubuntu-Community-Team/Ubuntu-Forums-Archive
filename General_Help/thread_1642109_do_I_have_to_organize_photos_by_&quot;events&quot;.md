### Post by senewag on 2010-12-20
Hi Princeofnam,

If you have imported photos from a folder then click on "last import", select all, then "add tag".  That way you can view all the photos in that folder by clicking on the tag.

I like having my photos organised by yy/mm/dd because if you click on any year, you get 12 photos displaying, one for each month with the number of photos showing for each month.  You can make any photo for that month a "key photo".  So you can arrow-up through the years looking at what you have done.  Lovely.  Using version 0.7.2.
