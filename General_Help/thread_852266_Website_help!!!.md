When I was wanting a unique domain, I played around with a random word generator. There's [a decent one at fourteenminutes.com]("http://www.fourteenminutes.com/fun/words/") that lets you enter the first few letters.

I also played around with [onelook.com]("http://onelook.com/"), a great searchable online meta-dictionary. It lets you mess around with wildcard searches, prefixes, suffixes, etc.
