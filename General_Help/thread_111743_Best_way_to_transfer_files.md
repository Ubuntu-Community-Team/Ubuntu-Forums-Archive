### Post by Griff on 2006-01-02
The last time I had to transfer that amount of volume I just took the hardrive out of the old computer and plugged it into the new one.  It'll show up as a seperate partition that you can just transfer stuff back and forth from.  That of course is assuming you have anouther spot in your new comp that will hold another drive.

### Post by docmanny on 2006-01-02
That will work. You won't need the CD for the transfer. Another option is to use an USB enclosure, but its much slower than connecting the HD internally.

If you plan on doing a dual boot in the future and you want to access files from linux and windows, make sure you make your data partition either a FAT or EXT3. Just another lesson I learned the hard way..;)
