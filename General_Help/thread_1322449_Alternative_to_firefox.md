### Post by fluffman86 on 2009-11-10
I use chromium, which is the open source part of chrome.  Get it at:
[https://launchpad.net/~chromium-daily/+archive/ppa](https://launchpad.net/~chromium-daily/+archive/ppa)

Or, you can get the official Google branded one from:
[http://dev.chromium.org/getting-involved/dev-channel](http://dev.chromium.org/getting-involved/dev-channel)

scroll down to get the .deb  But it's not as up to date as the ppa, and it's really no different...just has Google's name on it.  In fact, if you go to the About menu in Chrome, they admit it's based on chromium.  :)
