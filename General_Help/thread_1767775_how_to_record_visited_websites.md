### Post by mbeierl on 2011-05-26
There are programs such as dansguardian or squid that act as web proxies and can log all the requests put through them.  You can then either change the proxy setting in System Preferences or use a low-level firewall rule to force all HTTP or HTTPS traffic to go through your proxy.

For more info on dansguardian, see: [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/DansGuardian](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/DansGuardian)
