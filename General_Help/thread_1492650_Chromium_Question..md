To change the default text style, go to the Spanner icon in the top right hand corner and select **Options**, click the tab named **Under The Hood** and scroll down to the **Web Content** section then click on **Change font and language settings**

For the most frequently used bookmarks, I'd go with what Dessicus said about installing the Speed dial extension: [[COLOR="Red"]**Speed Dial**[/COLOR]]("https://chrome.google.com/extensions/detail/dgpdioedihjhncjafcpgbbjdpbbkikmi?hl=en-gb"), or a favourite of mine is [[COLOR="Red"]**Fav4**[/COLOR]]("http://fav4.org/") which can become your home page and will list your four most liked websites (there are limitations on the websites at the moment).

[[IMG]http://img180.imageshack.us/img180/5484/screenshotfav4orgchromi.th.png[/IMG]](http://img180.imageshack.us/my.php?image=screenshotfav4orgchromi.png)

As for changing the size of text in the tabs, I think that can be done under **Preferences** > **Appearance**
