### Post by sidious1741 on 2010-12-23
I see this is an old thread but you haven't gotten an answer. After creating an object like a disk, on the right side you should see a box with the title properties. you can click on any of those values and change them. I once tried to make a disk move at a constant angular velocity to move some other objects but the other objects actually changed the angular velocity. To make the angular velocity constant, I didnt even change the mass but made the moment of inertia a really large value. Feel free to ask if you have any more questions.
