### Post by Megaptera on 2011-01-09
In short ...'no'. This "The structure of the ext3/ext4 filesystem is vastly different from the ntfs filesystem Windows uses. The nature of ntfs creates a need for periodic defragmentation, which is why it is not uncommon for users to do that on occasion with their Windows installation.

The ext* filesystem does not have the same inherent fragmentation and therefore does not require defragmentation in the same way Windows does."

From here:[http://www.uluga.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1298370](http://www.uluga.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1298370)
