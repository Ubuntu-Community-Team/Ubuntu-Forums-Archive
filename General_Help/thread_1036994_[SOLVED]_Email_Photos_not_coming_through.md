### Post by fragos on 2009-01-11
ISP means Internet Service Provider like AT&T or Comcast. This kind of email problem seems to relate the email software used to forward messages with embedded images. I've seen images that couldn't be loaded, embedded images converted to attachments, strange line breaks, strange character conversions of quote characters and even the entire message duplicated. As the recipient there's little you can do except when forwarding one of these email -- you can frequently edit the message to bring it back to normal for those you forwarded to. Images you can't load are lost and likely not recoverable. These problems are common to emails forwarded a number of times. When I send images I either attach them or provide a link to a web page with the images.
