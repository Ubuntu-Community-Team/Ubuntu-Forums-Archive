### Post by Blasphemist on 2011-09-24
Your just installing an iso to the hard drive that way. Not installing the release. You do need to find or replace the usb and use unetbootin to install the iso to it, boot the usb and then install the release to the hard drive. Also, it sounds like you may not be selecting the disk image check box down below. You want to select that and browse to your iso instead of leaving the distribution checked at the top.
