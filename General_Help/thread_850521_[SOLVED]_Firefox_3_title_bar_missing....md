### Post by EKMA on 2009-02-02
I realized you solved it by uninstalling ubuntu...but...there is a better solution than what you did.

As you stated, you could hit F11 (2 times) and it would show the title bar just for that session...when you closed and restarted, it would be back to not showing.

The solution is to hit F11 twice and then pull the window down a little bit.  Then hit the maximize square.  Now, Firefox will open each and every time with the title bar showing.

Hope this helps someone else who may have this issue.............
