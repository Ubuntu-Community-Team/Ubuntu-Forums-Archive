### Post by pointone on 2008-03-30
How are you installing these applications? For the most part, you should stick with software included in Ubuntu's repositories. Please [read this]("https://help.ubuntu.com/ubuntu/desktopguide/C/add-applications.html").

If you must download something from an external source, most people will provide separate x86 and x86_64 versions. 

Your sound card should already be supported by ALSA. Please post a link to where you're getting these drivers.
