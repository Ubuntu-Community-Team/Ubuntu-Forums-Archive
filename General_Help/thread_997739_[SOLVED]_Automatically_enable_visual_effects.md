### Post by CMPITG on 2008-11-30
Hi, I got a problem with enabling visual effects automatically. Whenever I turn it on, it only works in that session and after restarting the computer I have to enable it again. I will be glad if anyone tells me how to enable visual effects automatically. I'm using Intrepid Ibex.

---

### Post by eternalnewbee on 2008-11-30
Go to: **Main Menu >System > Preferences > Sessions**, and under **Options** press **Remember Currently Running Applications**.
