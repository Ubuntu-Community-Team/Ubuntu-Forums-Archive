### Post by barnex on 2010-01-13
Perhaps you are looking for VirtualBox? That runs a full windows *inside* linux (be sure to download the one form the sun website, not the virtualbox-ose version from the ubuntu repositories, if you want USB support for your printer). Running *some* windows applications works with 'wine', but most likely wine won't help you with your printer.
Anyway, good luck with the switch! Like most people I also had some difficulties setting up everything when I had just started using linux, but for me it was totally worth it and worked out well.
