### Post by jhenager on 2008-09-08
Today my titlebar got lost again, and doing metacity --replace did fix it - but replaced compiz with metacity, and that's not really a fix if you want to use compiz. By chance, fishing around, I right clicked on the menu bar and chose Customize. Didn't make any changes, but clicked Done, and it was back.
Just throwing this out there to help anyone that might have this issue.
