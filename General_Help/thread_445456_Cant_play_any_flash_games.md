### Post by irish_flu on 2007-05-15
Can you elaborate on what didn't work?

Here's another method (I'm assuming your version of Ubuntu is the same as mine):

Open the Synaptic Package Manager in the "Administration" menu.

In Synaptic, go to Tools-->Repositories

Check the box next to "Software restricted by copyright or legal issues (multiverse)", then click "Close".

In Synaptic, click the "search" button and search for: flashplugin-nonfree

When it appears, click the box to the left of the name to install the package.

Click "Apply" and wait for it to finish.

If Firefox was open while you did this, you'll have to restart Firefox.
