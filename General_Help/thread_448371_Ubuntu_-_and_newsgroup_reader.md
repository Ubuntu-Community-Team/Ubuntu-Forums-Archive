Read it through GoogleGroups with your browser:

[http://groups.google.com/group/alt.support.diabetes/topics](http://groups.google.com/group/alt.support.diabetes/topics)

Or install the Newsworthy extension for Thunderbird email client and read USENET Newsgroups from within your email client.

[https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/thunderbird/addon/2129](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/thunderbird/addon/2129)
