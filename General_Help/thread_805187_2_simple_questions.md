### Post by atomkind on 2008-05-25
I found this a while back in another thread:
Advanced Desktop Settings> General> Opacity Settings
Click (+)Add

Opacity Windows:
dropdownmenu
Opacity Value:
between 250 and 300 (290 recommended) (don't make this value too low or you won't see the panel, menu, or text in them)

Add another
Opacity Windows:
popupmenu
Opacity Value:
between 250 and 300 (290 recommended to maintain text visible)

Restart X (ctrl+alt+backspace) and your panel bar should be semi transparent as well as your main menu... Enjoy
====================
Also, if you hold down alt and roll your mouse wheel you can change the transparency/opacity of most windows including the panel:)
