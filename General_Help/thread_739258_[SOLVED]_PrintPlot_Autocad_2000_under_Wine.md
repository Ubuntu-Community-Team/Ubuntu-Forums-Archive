### Post by Michl on 2008-03-30
I realize now that this is not really an ubuntu issue, but this
can help other newbs like me running wine.

You can update, install service packs and all the other
usual stuff in wine as well.  Just download the .exe
files for your windoze application, run with wine,
and your program will be updated as if you were doing
this in Windoze.

Quite nifty. I wish I could thank myself...:)
