### Post by 3rdalbum on 2011-05-03
In Ubuntu, the old-school "notification area" is depreciated and notification area icons no longer appear in Ubuntu 11.04.

There's been plenty of time for developers to change over to the newer Indicators, but some have been dragging their feet; so Ubuntu 11.04 has a "whitelist" of programs that are allowed to display notification area icons.

See here: [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1709517](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1709517) for information about adding programs to the notification area whitelist.

You might also want to send an e-mail to the program's developers, asking them to support Indicators.
