### Post by nickleboyblue on 2010-11-30
I see two possibilities.  One is that the PDF(s) you are trying to read are password-protected.  In that case, you might be able to use the information at this link: [http://www.ubuntugeek.com/howto-crack-pdf-file-password.html](http://www.ubuntugeek.com/howto-crack-pdf-file-password.html)

Otherwise, the features of the PDF reader you use might not be advanced enough to view the documents.  In that case, follow the instructions here for installing Acroread (I know you already installed it, but it might help to follow these instructions so you get it done the most complete way possible): [http://www.liberiangeek.net/2010/09/install-adobe-reader-acroread-ubuntu-10-10-maverick-meerkat/](http://www.liberiangeek.net/2010/09/install-adobe-reader-acroread-ubuntu-10-10-maverick-meerkat/)
