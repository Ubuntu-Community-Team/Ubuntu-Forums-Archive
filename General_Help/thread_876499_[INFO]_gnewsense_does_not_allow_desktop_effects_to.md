### Post by zalberico on 2008-07-31
The free software gNewSense derivative of ubuntu does not include the glx module for the xserver because it is under the GLX license rather than the GPL.  When attempting to enable desktop effects you'll get a dialogue box that says 'Desktop effects could not be enabled'.

I just wanted to share what I found from the helpful people in IRC #compiz-fusion.

Other than this I also really enjoy gNewSense and found everything else quite usable.

My graphics card is Intel X3100.
