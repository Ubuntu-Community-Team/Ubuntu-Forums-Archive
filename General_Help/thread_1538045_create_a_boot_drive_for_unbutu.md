### Post by robsoles on 2010-07-24
Assuming you have a desktop or even someone else's PC to refer to :-

"System"->"Administration"->"Startup Disk Creator" - should be available on a LiveCD session of 10.04 LTS but I'm not going to check till someone says it isn't. Also should be able to make a bootable USB flash drive from the running LiveCD but may need an ISO on HDD or SSD to make what you want.
