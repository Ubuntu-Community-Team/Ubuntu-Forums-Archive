### Post by omschaub on 2007-01-26
Same problem here.. I needed to select the correct audio family in mplayer to fix this...

Right click --> Preferences --> Codecs & demuxer --> then under Audio select FFmpeg option.  This worked for me, and might for you, but make sure you have all ffmpeg stuff installed.  YMMV

But for me.. I can hear mp3 correctly now.. ROCK ON
