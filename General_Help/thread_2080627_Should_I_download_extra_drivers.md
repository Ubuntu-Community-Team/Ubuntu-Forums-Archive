### Post by thnewguy on 2012-11-04
Unless you have a specific need, it's probably best to stick with the standard drivers. I recommend not downloading or activating any additional driver. The drivers which come built in with Ubuntu are usually more stable and work okay. The additional drivers are typically used if you need more performance than what you have, ie if you are running 3-D games.
