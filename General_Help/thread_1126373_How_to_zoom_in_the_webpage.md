### Post by mb_webguy on 2009-04-15
The default shortcut in Firefox for zooming a webpage is Ctrl++ (that's Ctrl and +) and Ctrl+-, not Ctrl+Alt++ and Ctrl+Alt+-.  I have the No Squint extension installed, which is similar to the QuickPageZoom extension, and I don't have any problems.

To change the preferences for a plugin, however, just go to Tools->Addons, the Extensions tab, select the Extension, and click the Preferences button.
