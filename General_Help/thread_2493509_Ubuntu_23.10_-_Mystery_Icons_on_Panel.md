### Post by Dennis N on 2023-12-18
Yes, they turned out to be indicators that there are unread notifications. I did not see them when clicking on them because I had the notification widget turned off in the date/calendar display AND in the Quick Settings Menu. I turned on notifications back on in the date/calendar display and cleared them out. The icons went away.
