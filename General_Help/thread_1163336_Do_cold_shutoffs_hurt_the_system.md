### Post by unutbu on 2009-05-18
Occasionally I come upon forum posts which suggest letting a laptop battery go dead while running Ubuntu may cause GRUB errors. See for example: [http://ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=7108245&postcount=1](http://ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=7108245&postcount=1).

Furthermore, if your laptop battery is lithium-based, perhaps it is not advisable to discharge it fully.

According to [http://www.batteryuniversity.com/parttwo-34.htm:](http://www.batteryuniversity.com/parttwo-34.htm:) 
> 
The [lithium] battery prefers a partial rather than a full discharge. Frequent full discharges should be avoided when possible. Instead, charge the battery more often or use a larger battery. There is no concern of memory when applying unscheduled charges.
