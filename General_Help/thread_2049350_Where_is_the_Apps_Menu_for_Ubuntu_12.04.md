### Post by vexorian on 2012-08-28
In the sidebar, click on the ubuntu icon. Then you will find four white icons in the bottom of the new thingy. One of them is the app section.

### Post by diesch on 2012-08-28
> **777funk said:**
>  Is it hard to go back if I do switch to classic menus?

ClassicMenu Indicator only adds a new icon to open a application menu to the top bar. It doesn't remove anything and you can still use all the features of Unity. 

If you don't like ClassicMenu Indicator anymore just uninstall it.
