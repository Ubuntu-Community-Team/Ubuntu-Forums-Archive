### Post by jimcooncat on 2008-03-18
For something completely different, curlftpfs.

This mounts the ftp destination as a folder in your computer. You can then use any command-line tool or GUI filemanager on it. Open remote files directly in your applications. Mix and match GTK and QT applications. 

I love mountable remote filesystems, I mostly use sshfs and smbfs.
