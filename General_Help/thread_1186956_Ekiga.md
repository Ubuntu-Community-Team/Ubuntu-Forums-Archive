### Post by kiridude on 2009-06-14
First step, go to ekiga.org and get a free SIP address. You can also do this by opening Ekiga (pre-installed in Ubuntu) and Edit > Configuration assistant.

Then, Ekiga recommends Diamondcard for buying credit, but it seems not to work for Europeans (at least in my case), but since you're in NY, you should be ok. You will also be directed there through the Ekiga configuration assistant at the "buy credit" part. [Here]("http://wiki.ekiga.org/index.php/List_of_PC_to_phone_providers") is a list of other providers you can check out. You can basically use any service that uses the SIP or H.323 standard.

You can find more detailed info about Ekiga [here]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Ekiga").
