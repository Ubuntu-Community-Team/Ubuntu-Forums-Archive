### Post by cascade9 on 2010-12-25
Yeah, it is possible to run 2 power supplies. Not much fun getting it wired up, or finding a spot for the 2nd PSU in most cases, but its do-able. 

I'm not sure that it would help in your case though, SATA drives tend use less than 10watts these days, and case fans dont use much power at all.... 

Your problem could be caused by dodgy drivers, or it could just be dodgy games. 

What hardware are you running? Phenom II X6 is all thats clear, and theres lots of Phenom II models.

*edit- video card model really does matter. Running 2 x GTS450s on 650 watts? No problem! Running 2 x GTX480s/580s on 650 watts? No. Bad Idea.
