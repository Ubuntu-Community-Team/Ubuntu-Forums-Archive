### Post by takayuki on 2006-03-15
to have gftp remember your passwords, you must first login to your ftp account, then create a bookmark: Bookmarks > Add Bookmark (ctrl A)

later...

if you launch gftp and try to login again it will ask for your password.  you must click Bookmarks, then select your bookmark, then it will remember your password.

hope somebody found that helpful!
