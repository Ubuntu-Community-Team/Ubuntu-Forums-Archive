### Post by Mortuis on 2008-07-16
Currently, when I plug my camera's SD card into my system, the card automounts to /media/disk (yay!) and then two windows open, one called F-Spot, and another (I'm assuming to do with F-Spot) asking me to "Select Photos to copy From Camera" (boo!).  I'd prefer to just have it open /media/disk in Nautilus (preferably into the subfolder that has the pictures, actually), but I'd settle for just not having it start F-Spot.

Looking around F-Spot, I see no settings that mention anything about jumping up when I put in a camera SD card.  I'd like some recommendations as to where to look to change this behavior.

---

### Post by bmac on 2008-07-17
Check:

system/preferences/Removable Drives and Media Preferences/Cameras/Command

to see if F-Sopt  is selected. If so remove it....



Also check:

File Browser (Nautilus)/edit/preferences/Media/Photos




Hope this helps.....
