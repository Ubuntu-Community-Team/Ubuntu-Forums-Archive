### Post by FaizanKazi on 2009-04-20
Leave it running whenever I have stuff downloading or when its my weekly virus-scan night (this virus scan is actually to scan my windows partitions which i use at work).

otherwise I shutdown.

dont waste electricity if you can avoid it. its not about your bill (if a couple extra bucks doesnt bother you), its just about your frame of mind. being mindful of saving something here and there just spreads a little awareness.

if youre worried about your laptop/desktops life, monitor its temperatures (processor, motherboard, harddisk, graphics card) and keep em low (high ventilation, low dust environment). for desktops 30 to 40 C is ideal, laptops 35 to 45 is ideal.
