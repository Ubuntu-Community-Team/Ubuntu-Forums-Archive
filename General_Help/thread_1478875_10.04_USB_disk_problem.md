### Post by john42 on 2010-05-21
Hi, 

Just had this problem too - exactly the same output from lsusb and dmesg. Reading around seems people were removing usbmount - but it wasn't installed on my system after upgrade - so I installed it via synaptic. A dependency, pmount, was installed at the same time. 

This result gave the mount output most people have been complaining about, all the usbs mounted /media/usbX, with root permissions - but at least they were mounting, and listed in /dev/. To solve the next part I did what most people have done and removed usbmount (but not pmount) which fixed the problem, usb mounts fine now.

I think pmount is the required package to fix the problem. I don't really understand this - but the fix might help you.

Cheers
