### Post by Penguineer on 2011-05-11
The easiest way is to click the panel button for the text writer application again after it is displayed.  When you view the panel, it will show multiple ticks on the left side of the button.  This shows that there are multiple documents.  Clicking the button while the application is active (works for most applications in addition to Libre) will show thumbnail type screens of each adjacent to one another.  Just click on the one you want.

It is a kind of cool way to do it.  I'm still getting used to it.
