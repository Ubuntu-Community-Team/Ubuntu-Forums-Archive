I just did a fresh reinstall of 9.1 today and went to set my screen saver and saw that many of the screen savers have disappeared including my favorite the fireworks screen saver. Anyone have any idea how i can get these missing screen savers back? Or at least the fireworks one. Thank you

### Post by lidex on 2010-02-19
If you search "screensaver" in synaptic you'll find various packages supplying a variety of them. The package "xscreensaver-data-extra" contains the one you mention.
