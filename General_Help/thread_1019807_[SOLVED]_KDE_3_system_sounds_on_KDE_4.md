### Post by Zorael on 2008-12-23
Could this be it? [http://packages.ubuntu.com/hardy/kdeartwork-misc](http://packages.ubuntu.com/hardy/kdeartwork-misc)
> This package contains miscellaneous multimedia goodies for KDE. Included are additional KDE sounds and kworldclock themes. 

edit: Then again, it does say *additional*. The surefire way would be to burn a live cd of Kubuntu Hardy (or earlier, with KDE 3), boot it up, and copy the **/usr/share/sounds** folder. I think it's called sounds. You get my point, though.
