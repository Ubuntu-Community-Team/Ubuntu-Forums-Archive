### Post by strabes on 2007-02-20
I installed ubuntu on my mother's and father's computer after they had filled it with virii and spyware in 1 month. All I can say is make sure you install codecs for everything, explain to them that they should save all their documents etc in their home folder, and be prepared for a few calls with questions resulting from the switch. Overall, after 7 months, I'm glad I switched them over. I even installed beryl/XGL to help them with the concept of minimizing windows and having multiple windows/programs open at the time.
