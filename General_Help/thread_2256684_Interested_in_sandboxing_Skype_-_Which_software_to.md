### Post by ian-weisser on 2014-12-14
Some good points on this from [http://forums.gentoo.org/viewtopic-p-7569340.html](http://forums.gentoo.org/viewtopic-p-7569340.html)

- Skype needs access to a lot of your hardware: Camera, microphone, speakers.
- It also needs to interact with your X session.

So easy sandboxing seems unlikely to work.
Perhaps a container or VM might meet your needs?

The best kind of protection to use depends upon what you are trying to protect from.
What is the threat you are concerned about?
