### Post by phorminx on 2015-12-17
For Eye of Gnome (eog) under ubuntu 15.10, I have the "reload image" plugin enabled. But this seems to be doing nothing. Or worse: When I am viewing 50 images and I edit image 25 with another application, eog will not reload. Then I try to go to image 26, but eog jumps back to image 1.

There was no such trouble with eog under ubuntu 14.04. The plugin gave me a menu item "Reload Image" (under "tools" and also bound to key r) which worked as expected.  There is no menu item / key binding for reloading an image under 15.10.
(Why is it necessary at all to enable a plugin for such a basic feature?)
