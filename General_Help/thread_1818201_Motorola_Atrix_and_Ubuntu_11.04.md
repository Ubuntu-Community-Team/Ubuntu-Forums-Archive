### Post by DracheMitch on 2011-09-26
You have to choose USB from the Android Notification bar.

Netflix requires Silverlight DRM, which you will probably never see on Linux.  Check with the Wine project to see if they have it working.

### Post by BeRoot ReBoot on 2011-09-26
As it is an Android-based device, you have to enable SD mounting from your phone first, after you connect it to your PC.
