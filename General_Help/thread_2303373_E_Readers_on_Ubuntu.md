### Post by QDR06VV9 on 2015-11-18
I Agree with slickymaster, I only use Calibre Reader along with Gespeaker with the [Mbrola Speech engine]("https://code.google.com/p/gespeaker/wiki/InstallMbrola"). The stock voices that come with Gespeaker are just OK, But mbrola voices are much better.(IMHMO) Then you can just set back and be read to.
Side Note: Calibre supports the conversion of many input formats to many output formats. It can convert every input format in the following list, to every output format.
> Input Formats: AZW, AZW3, AZW4, CBZ, CBR, CBC, CHM, DJVU, DOCX, EPUB, FB2, HTML, HTMLZ, LIT, LRF, MOBI, ODT, PDF, PRC, PDB, PML, RB, RTF, SNB, TCR, TXT, TXTZ

Output Formats: AZW3, EPUB, DOCX, FB2, HTMLZ, OEB, LIT, LRF, MOBI, PDB, PMLZ, RB, PDF, RTF, SNB, TCR, TXT, TXTZ, ZIP



To get books off google play to calibre
