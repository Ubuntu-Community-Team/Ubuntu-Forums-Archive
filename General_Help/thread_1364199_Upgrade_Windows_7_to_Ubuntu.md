### Post by tom.swartz07 on 2009-12-25
> **Jkwc said:**
> Is it possible to upgrade Windows 7 to Ubuntu? As I dont want to lose my files ^.^

HAHA! Love how you say "upgrade" to Ubuntu.

I would suggest installing in dual-boot, "side-by-side" and copying all of your files over.

This way, you have control over what you keep and lose, and you have an OS to fall back on if something goes wrong. When youre perfectly happy with your install then, you could satisfy the urge and delete Windows. 


When you do, Use a livecd, use gParted, delete the partition, and run ```
update-grub2
``` from the terminal before you restart on the Ubuntu-only system.

After installing Ubuntu, preferable with a separate /home partition, you can place all your data back in this /home partition.
