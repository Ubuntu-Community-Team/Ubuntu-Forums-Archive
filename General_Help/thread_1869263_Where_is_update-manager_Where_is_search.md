### Post by atentik on 2011-10-25
At the very top right conner, where your name appears, click it. Fourth item on the list is "Software up to date" or "Software available". Click it anyway, and it will take you to the update manager menu...

### Post by jzakilla on 2011-10-25
Natty uses a live real time search index. Open your applications tab, then go to applications and look for the little white link that says "show all 130" (130 in my case, prob different on yours) at the top of the window is a search field, simply put your cursor in there and start typing. Alternately you can open a terminal and type 'sudo apt-get update' (without the quotes) hope that helps! :-)
