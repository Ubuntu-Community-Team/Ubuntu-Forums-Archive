### Post by merlinus on 2009-07-26
Best to do it from Bookmarks/Organize Bookmarks.  The file is in a hidden directory in /home -- .mozilla/firefox/xxxxx.default/bookmarks.html.  (xxxxx are numbers particular to your machine).
