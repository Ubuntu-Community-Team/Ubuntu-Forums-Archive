### Post by evets on 2007-10-08
Actually, you can "pop" them pretty easily. Go to [www.fastmail.fm](www.fastmail.fm) and sign up for a free account. Then, set it up to retrieve your hotmail, which it will do-no charge, then "pop" fastmail into your email client.

I've used fastmail for years, and because it's the most reliable and configurable web mail host I've ever come across, I upgraded three years ago to the paid service, and now run 3 websites off of it, as well.

I've never had, even once, an outage. Can't say that for my other sites. :(
