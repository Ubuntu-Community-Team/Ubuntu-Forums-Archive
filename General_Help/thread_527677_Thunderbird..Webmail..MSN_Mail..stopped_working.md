### Post by LeadFingers on 2007-08-19
Metaljaz:

MSN mail stopped working on most folks using WebMail, update or not.
Seems the good folks at MSN changed a few servers, did little damage for the Windows world, but threw a monkey wrench at us Linux users.
         (According to the forum at WebMail)
Good news is ... same forum also had a solution ... worked for me!

Thunderird add-ons / Webmail - Hotmail / Accounts / Mode ... check "WebDav" ...Restart Thunderbird
... enter password when asked.

Hope this helps

    LeadFingers

### Post by Metaljaz on 2007-08-19
Something that I noticed started happening after the change was that i started receiving
three emails each of all my msn mail. 
I then made this change:

Thunderbird > Add-ons > Hotmail extension > Options > Accounts tab > POP tab > Check box "Download unread mail only"
