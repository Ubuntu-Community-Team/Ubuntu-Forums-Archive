### Post by I&#9829;HTML5 on 2011-02-08
[LIST=1]
[*]On Ubuntu: Go to System | Preferences | Remote Desktop
[*]Check the box next  to "Allow other users to view your desktop"
[*]Make sure the box is checked next to "Allow other users to control your desktop"
[*]On your iPod Touch: Open the App Store
[*]Search for an app called Wyse PocketCloud
[*]Install it (it is free)
[*]Open PocketCloud
[*]Click the + at the top of the screen
[*]Click the + at the top of the screen again
[*]Select "Remote Shadowing (VNC)"
[*]For the Nickname, put "My Computer"
[*]For the Host, put your computer's IP address
       You can find this on the Remote Desktop window on your computer
[*]Click "Save" at the top
[*]Click My Computer
[*]On your computer, you will have to approve the connection
[/LIST]
That's all!
