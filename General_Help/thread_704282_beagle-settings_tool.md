### Post by Mustard on 2008-02-23
> **Robbyx said:**
> So far I have not found it. I have Beagle installed but  I see no settings tools. Could you please indicate how they are loaded?

In your Applications > Accessories menu you should find an option called 'Search'.  Open that up and a window with the title 'Desktop Search' should open.  Click on the 'Search' menu in this program and you will see an option for 'Preferences'.  These are the Beagle settings.

Alternatively, open a terminal and type 'beagle-settings' at the command line.
