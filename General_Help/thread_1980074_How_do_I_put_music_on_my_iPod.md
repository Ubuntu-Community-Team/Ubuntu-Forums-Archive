### Post by kolinab on 2012-05-14
Plug in your ipod via the USB cable. 

Then open Rhythmbox (you may be prompted to do this anyway). Once you're in Rhythmbox, your ipod should appear in the list of music sources on the left side. From there you should be able to drag music on/off onto your ipod from your existing library.

If you haven't yet set up your music library in Rhythmbox, you can 'import' your music into the Rhythmbox catalogue first via the file menu.

Those instructions work for my old 4th Gen. nano.

Post back if you have trouble!
