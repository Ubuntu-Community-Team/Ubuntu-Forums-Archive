### Post by engdarkman89 on 2010-05-28
Hi,
If you want to set " Real Player " as your default player then you have two option:
1-Go search under OPTION or PREFERENCES tab then make it your Default player.
2-Right Click on your video file then choose PROPERTIES --> OPEN WITH --> Choose Real player. 
Note:
 *In the second way you have to do this once for each video format you have.

Good Luck.
