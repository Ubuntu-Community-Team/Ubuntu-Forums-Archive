Hi, I had the propriatary fglrx driver installed too when I found everything turning so sloooooooowwwww after upgrading to Interpid... Not at all like under Gutsy or Hardy. I reverted to the open source radeon drivers (since i have ATI Radeon Xpress 200 onboard graphics) and speed is back to normal proportions.
