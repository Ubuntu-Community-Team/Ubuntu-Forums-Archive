### Post by rune0077 on 2008-08-27
Not in my case. I just installed it, and after that, every time I boot I get the emerald theme instead of metacity. Try and open System > Preferences > Sessions, and see if "emerald" isn't already on the list. If it is, you should be fine.

Note: you can also do the emerald --replace by pressing alt+F2 and then entering the command, that way it won't run with the terminal.
