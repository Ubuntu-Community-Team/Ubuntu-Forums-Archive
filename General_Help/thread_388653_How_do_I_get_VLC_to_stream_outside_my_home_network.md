### Post by simonn on 2007-03-19
On your modem you need to forward the port the VLC server is running on to your PC. 

How you do this depends entirely on your modem (and possibly router to). You are probably better asking on message boards dedicated to them.

You may also need to open that port on your PC's firewall as well.
