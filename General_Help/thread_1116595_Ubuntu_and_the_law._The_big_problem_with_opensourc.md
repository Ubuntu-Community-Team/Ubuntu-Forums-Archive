### Post by Ilya Skorik on 2009-04-05
Hello!

In the Russian Federation (in an introduction kind in World Trade Organization (WTO)) there is an active counteraction the piracy software.

Unfortunately our police has no sufficient technical knowledge.They get definitions of the piracy software and counteraction methods from Microsoft's methodical allowances.

Typical situation when police officers come to the small company for legitimacy check software. They do not find Windows, do not find licence label from Microsoft with serial number on the computer box side. They find Ubuntu or any other opensource software like OpenOffice. Than they ask lawful question (under the Russian laws) - where licences for this software? They take computers from company office for software check. Which can proceed some months and end with confiscation.

All it occurs, because open software in our country does not have a legal substantiation. Our laws require the licence, any payment, any acknowledgement of purchase software. For Microsoft's representatives this fact is good argument for conducting propaganda against use FOSS. And they use it.

And now there exist the local companies like "Russian GNU Linux Center", which sell pieces of paper being "the License for 10 computers Ubuntu 8.10 (includes support)". This paper costs about 160$ ( [http://www.linuxcenter.ru/shop/licence/lic_i_tehpoddergka_ubuntu_810_10/]("http://www.linuxcenter.ru/shop/licence/lic_i_tehpoddergka_ubuntu_810_10/") ).

Therefore I have two questions to Canonical company.

1. Are these "licences" papers  legitimate and company Canonical knows about them? It is interesting to me, because I heard, what the small companies having purchased a similar paper did not get due legal and technical support from Linux Center.

2. Company Canonical can offer the expanded "shipit" service  for our country? Personally I with pleasure will pay money for disks with Ubuntu, with the enclosed personal company licence agreement in Russian language and a holographic label with any serial number.

I ask to understand depth of a problem. It not undermining of FOSS philosophy. It is a real life situation because of which business suffers, reputation FOSS suffers. The decision of this problem is in other place, rather than philosophy of open software. And the decision of this problem to my country can be proceed by a legislative way for years.

I will be glad to hear any opinions, in advance thanks for the answers.
