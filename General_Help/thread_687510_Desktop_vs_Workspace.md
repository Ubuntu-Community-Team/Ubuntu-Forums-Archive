### Post by bobbo85 on 2008-02-05
I figured out one thing:
The advanced desktop settings menu has this setting under general that is horizontal/vertical size of workspaces, and number of desktops...

When I set the horizontal/vertical sizes to 1, and the desktops to 4, I got 4 different backgrounds, but no cubes...

When I set the horizontal size to 4, and the desktops to 1, I got 1 cube with 4 sides, but all of them had the same background.  

Not really any progress, but I at least stopped getting stuck at desktops with no buttons!
