### Post by mac9416 on 2009-11-06
> What would you suggest me to do in order to have a lighter and faster Ubuntu?

I like to use Fluxbox. It's much faster than GNOME, though a little bit ugly.
This will get you started setting it up: [http://www.howtogeek.com/howto/ubuntu/installing-fluxbox-on-ubuntu-linux/](http://www.howtogeek.com/howto/ubuntu/installing-fluxbox-on-ubuntu-linux/)

Also, I would suggest [PCManFM]("http://pcmanfm.sourceforge.net/") four your file manager. Nautilus is a serious resource hog.

> Is there any other distribution that's easy to use and, at the same time, is lighter and faster than ubuntu?

I have heard of many folks using [Crunchbang Linux]("http://crunchbanglinux.org/"). It's based on Ubuntu, but much lighter.

Hope that helps!
