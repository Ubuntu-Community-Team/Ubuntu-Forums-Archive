### Post by HappyFeet on 2009-09-25

2.How to disable autorun of blank cds ie while burning cds through nero 3 a dialog appears asking for action to perform onto cd and ejects out the cd immediately when you cancel the dialog hence, ruin the cd as burning was taking on ?

System: -intel E64T,dualcore2 2.20 ghz
             -jaunty amd64bit

Open your home folder, then go to edit>preferences. Go to media tab. Towards the bottom you will see DVD audio. From the pull down menu, change it to blank cd. Then below it, change action to **do nothing**.
