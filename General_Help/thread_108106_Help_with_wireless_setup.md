### Post by reckless2k2 on 2005-12-25
In the case of both of those cards you are going to need to use a tool called ndiswrapper. The link provided by briancurtin will be helpful but require a lot of reading. The following wiki is basically what you are going to need to do:

[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/HowToSetUpNdiswrapper](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/HowToSetUpNdiswrapper)

The driver list can be found here:

[http://ndiswrapper.sourceforge.net/mediawiki/index.php/List](http://ndiswrapper.sourceforge.net/mediawiki/index.php/List)

Download your driver first before you begin. You have enough info right now to download the driver for one of those cards. D-Link is under the D's and Hawkins is under the H's. The rest shouldn't be difficult. 

Good luck and post back with any difficulties.
