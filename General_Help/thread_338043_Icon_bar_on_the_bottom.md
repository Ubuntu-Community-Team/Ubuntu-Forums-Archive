### Post by electricshoes on 2007-01-13
That looks like its just a panel. Right click on your panel and choose New Panel. Drag it to the bottom, right click and go to properties. Uncheck expand, go to the background tab and choose solid color and bring transparency down. There ya go. Drag some Icons to it.
