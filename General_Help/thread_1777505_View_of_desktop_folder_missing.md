### Post by Zorael on 2011-06-07
That's a Folder View widget!

Right-click your desktop and choose *Add Widgets*. Unlock widgets first if you have them locked. Find Folder View in the list, either by scrolling, by entering something like 'folder' in the search field, or by picking the *File System* category.

Once you find it, double-click it and it should be added to your desktop.
