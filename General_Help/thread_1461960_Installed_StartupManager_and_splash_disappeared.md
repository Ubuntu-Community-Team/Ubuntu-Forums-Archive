### Post by mikewhatever on 2010-05-18
You graphics card may be able to handle high resolutions, but that's irrelevant for the splash screen. The problem with the splash screen resolution is that while it is displayed, the graphics driver may may not be loaded as the system is just starting to boot. Change the resolution back to what it was.
