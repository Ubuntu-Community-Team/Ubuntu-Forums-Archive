### Post by ks07 on 2010-06-15
Afaik, the only way to limit the bandwidth of the ps3 is through whichever device is providing it's internet access. I'm guessing you have a router, so you could try logging into it's web interface (usually [192.168.0.1]("http://192.168.0.1/") or similar) and looking if it has any option for this. However, I think bandwidth limiting is quite rare in routers (or at least I've never seen it in the few I've used), so it is likely there will be no easy way to achieve what you want. :mad:

And, for the record, limiting the PS3's upload speed would probably impact online gaming performance. So it's probably not the wisest of ideas. :p
