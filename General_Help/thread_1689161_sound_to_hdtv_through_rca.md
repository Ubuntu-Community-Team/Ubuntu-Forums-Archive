### Post by gobbledigook on 2011-02-16
does the sound work with headphones? 

does the sound work normally?

if the answer to these is "yes" then it is an issue with the TV... check your TV menu to see if you can select the sound input to use :)

also **on my TV** you cannot have the sound input through the rca port this is for sound out... the only input like this is the red white and yellow for rgb - ie sound red/white and yellow for composite video... but in this case you would nees to use a s-video cable with it to get the video and sound in the same place ;)

in my experience the sound needs to go through an amp not the TV direct... but i'm not familiar with your TV so it may well have a sound-in :)
