### Post by forestpixie on 2008-04-21
Total - is the amount on the hdd or partition
Used - is the amount that has been used on the hdd or partition
Free - is the amount that is left on the hdd or partition
Available - is how much that is left which you can use

The available is less than free because linux reserves an amount (approx 5%) so that the drive does not become full and then stop you from booting and for journalling - at least that is my understanding of the way it works. I'm sure that if I'm wrong someone will say so.
