### Post by mystics on 2015-07-20
> **Tea_Royal said:**
> for having a server and still being able to use the computer?

Maybe I'm misunderstanding this part, but no matter what, you can use your own computer. It's just that you can't host it from your computer. That will have to be  handled by someone else, and there are plenty of companies that you can  get the server from. After you get it, you can interact with it from  your own computer. This includes logging into it, transferring files,  and working with those files as if they were on your own computer.
