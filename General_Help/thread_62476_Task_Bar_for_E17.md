### Post by SFN on 2005-09-05
[QUOTE=NaOH]engage seems nice, but i am having a little trouble finding some documentation on it.  How does one go about building a system tray and so forth.  I get a nice blank box when i run it[/QUOTE]

Here are some instructions for engage:
[QUOTE=SFN]
To use engage, you need to enable it. Do:

```
enlightenment_remote -module-load engage
``` 

Then, click on the desktop, mouse over Configuration, mouse over Applications and mouse over Modules, then mouse over Engage. Then click Enabled.

Now, click on the desktop, mouse over Configuration, mouse over Applications and click Menu Editor. In the new screen that opens up, click the button that says Engage. Drag programs from the bar on the left into the blank area under the buttons. When you have the programs you want, click the button marked Save, then click the button marked Quit. You might have to click Quit twice. That seems to happen on and off.

If you want to adjust the engage bar (like maybe center it on the bottom of the screen) right-click on it and choose Edit Mode at the bottom of the popup menu. You can then right-click on it again and you will have a whole bunch of options available to you. When you have it the way you want, right-click it again and choose End Edit Mode.[/QUOTE]
