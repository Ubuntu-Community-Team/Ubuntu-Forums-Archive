### Post by Breakar on 2008-09-16
So encase anyone else has this problem,

Applications>crossover>configuration

**manage bottles tab**
[I]click on the avaible 'bottle' for me it was winxp, click archive and save it somewhere you can access it.
[/I]
log into your other user account,
Applications>crossover>configuration

**manage bottles tab**
*click "restore archived bottles" button, access the .cxarchive file you saved from your other desktop and load it in.*

You now have all your games/applications on a new user desktop!
