### Post by BravoCharlie on 2004-11-02
This is due to the width of the "Application Computer" label on the custom menu. If you want you can right click on this menu and click remove it from  the panel and the right click again and click "Add to panel" and this time select the Gnome Main Menu, this will give you a menu with just the Gnome foot print instead of the long label.
