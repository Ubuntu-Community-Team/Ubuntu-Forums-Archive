### Post by mb_webguy on 2009-03-20
I use [xvidcap]("apt:xvidcap").  It's not as idiot-proof as gtk-recordmydesktop, but it's more configurable.  gtk-recordmydesktop can only save videos (as far as I can tell) as Ogg video.  xvidcap gives you the option of several different formats.
