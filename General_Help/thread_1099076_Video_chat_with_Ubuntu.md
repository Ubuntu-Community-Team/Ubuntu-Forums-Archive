### Post by mb_webguy on 2009-03-17
Skype and Ekiga both support video chat (actually video VOIP if you want to get technical).  aMSN, a Windows Live Messenger clone, also supports video chat.

You may or may not have to do a small amount of extra work to get your webcam working, depending on the specific hardware.  [Here]("http://www.quicktweaks.com/2008/04/10/video-voice-chating-in-ubuntu/")'s one tutorial I found that discusses setting up video chat on Ubuntu.
