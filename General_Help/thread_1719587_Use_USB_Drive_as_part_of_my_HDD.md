### Post by dragonfly41 on 2011-04-03
I think readers are a bit puzzled about the notion that you "extend your partition" to add on 2GB from your USB to your (full) 20 GB HDD.

You can have your existing 20GB hdd in one partition (as it is now) and then have an additional 2 GB USB drive with a new partition.

But the total 22 GB will not be contiguous.   Why is that necessary?

You could format your USB and distribute content between your 20 GB drive and your 2 GB USB drive.
They will show up as /dev/sda   and /dev/sbd in GParted
But a "petty" 2 GB is not very big.   You can go out and buy a much bigger USB stick or drive at low cost. 

So further explanation of your constraints is needed.
