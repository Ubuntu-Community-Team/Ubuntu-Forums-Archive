I had the same problem than you, installed Ubuntu on my new PC yesterday. It came out of the box with no OS. When you boot on the CD, even if your BIOS may recognize the keyboard, it's ridiculous but the installer doesnt! There is a way though: wait the 30sec countdown and let it boot from the CD. Ok it takes a few minutes, but then you should have your mouse and keyboard working and click the desktop icon install ubuntu etc.

That was with a logitech keyboard and mouse, and ubuntu 9.10 64bit.

I hope you'll be as lucky as i was :)
