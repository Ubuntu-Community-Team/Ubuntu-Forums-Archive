### Post by akiro.yamamoto on 2006-03-04
Install gnome-art with Synaptic.
This app will download and install backgrounds, Icon themes, Metacity / Gtk2 themes and GDM themes. So it's a good place to start. ;)
After you can check web-sites like gnome-look.org for ideas and themes for your system.
