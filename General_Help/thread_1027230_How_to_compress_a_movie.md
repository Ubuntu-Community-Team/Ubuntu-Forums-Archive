### Post by 3rdalbum on 2009-01-01
Tarring and Gzipping will not cause any quality loss. You can try Bzip instead of Tar Gzip, which gives better compression with no quality loss.

For video-specific compression, we'd really want to know what format the video is currently in. If you can find out the format and bitrate that the video is in, then I'd recommend using the same format and a slightly lower bitrate.
