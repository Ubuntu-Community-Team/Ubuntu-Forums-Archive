When you go to Edit>Preferences in FireFox, click on the Content tab and see what font is being called for the default font in the Fonts & Colors section. In my case, I use Verdana, which I prefer. You can choose other fonts by clicking on the Advanced button. When you are in the Advanced section, you might want to uncheck the line "Allow pages to choose....."
