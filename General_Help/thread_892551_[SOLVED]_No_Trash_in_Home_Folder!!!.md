### Post by jmate24 on 2008-08-17
wait i found the answer: it was so simple, just remove the trash icon from the panel and then log out and hit Ctrl+Backspace and then login and add the trash icon back to the panel, then move something to the trash in the right click menu and poof! about a second later the trash icon looks full!!!

Thanx for your help!!
