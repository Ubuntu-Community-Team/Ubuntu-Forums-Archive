### Post by sandy8925 on 2009-10-18
As someone said earlier there are problems with Intel graphics cards in 9.04. I have a Fujitsu C1321 with an Intel 915GM card and it was sort of slowbut not terribly slow. Anyway there's a sticky in the Multimedia and Video category that says "Jaunty Intel Graphics Performance Guide". Try the steps suggested there. It improved performance for me so I'm sure it will for you too. I've also heard that these problems were fixed in Karmic so hopefully we won't have any.

Better still compile the kernel ! changing the timer setting to 1000Hz really makes it faster and more responsive.
