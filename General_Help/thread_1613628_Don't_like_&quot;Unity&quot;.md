### Post by ajgreeny on 2010-11-04
Sorry, but you will have to reinstall 10.04 to get back to that.

The easier alternative is to use the gnome desktop that is already included by choosing it at the login screen.  If you login automatically by default, as I suspect many do on netbooks, just logout, and the login screen will appear.  Use the session menu, bottom right to choose "Gnome".  I have mine set up that way, but in fact with just a single panel, to save space, and the main menu, not menubar.
