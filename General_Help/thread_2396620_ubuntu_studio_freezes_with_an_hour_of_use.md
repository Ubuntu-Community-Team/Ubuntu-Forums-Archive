### Post by Autodave on 2018-07-18
Seeing as how it takes an hour, I doubt that it is software related.  Editing videos and surfing the net (depending on your browser) is very heavy work for your CPU and your memory.  There are several things that come to mind:

1. Heat build up. Make sure that the CPU and power supply are clean of dust, hair, whatever. Next clean the case fans. Try running the computer with the side cover off of it: that allows additional cooling. Make sure that all fans are functioning.
2. Run a memory test on it for at least 5-6 hours!  ANY error is bad!
3. Power supply could be on the way out.

After that, there are still quite a few things it could be, but none that are easy to test.  Start with point #1 and work your way through and let us know what is going on then.
