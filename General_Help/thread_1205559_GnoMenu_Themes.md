### Post by Giant Speck on 2009-07-06
Add GnoMenu to the panel, right-click it and go to "Preferences."

Next, go to the Themes tab and click on the little "Add" button.  I'm not sure if it actually says add or if it's just a plus sign, but clicking it will allow you to browse your computer for the theme file.
