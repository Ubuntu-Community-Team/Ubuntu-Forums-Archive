### Post by Duby1972 on 2009-05-02
Right mouse click is very sensitive in Intrepid and Jaunty, Everytime I right click it makes a new folder, and when trying to delete the folders it just opens them. I have went into the mouse pref and there is nothing there to adjust the right click sensitivity. I found a few other people with the same problem that have not fixed this issue..Is there a work around for this? The mouse works perfectly fine on my wifes xp box. any idea would be greatly appreciated. Thank you..


UPDATE!

The problem was with my logitech cordless mouse. Coverting the adapter from usb to ps2 seemed to do the trick. So if anyone is having a problem such as this with any cordless mice, try the ps2 mouse port instead.
