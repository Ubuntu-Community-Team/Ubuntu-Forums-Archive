### Post by Sef on 2007-11-05
1) Get a usb key (thumb drive, flash drive) and use Ubuntu's live cd to copy the pictures and data from your hard drive to your usb key.  (Note: If your pictures and data were erased during the install, then this will not be possible.) 

2) How big was the partition that you wanted to install Ubuntu on?

3) If your pictures and data was erased, then you would need [PhotoRec]("http://www.cgsecurity.org/wiki/PhotoRec") to try and recover them.

4) There is a command to reinstall Vista's boot loader.  I am not sure offhand what it is.  If there is no link, then search for it in here or on the net.
