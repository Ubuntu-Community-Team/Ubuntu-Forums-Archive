### Post by ian-weisser on 2013-11-21
All pre-release versions of Ubuntu are extensively and regularly tests for stability, with the goal that the released version is stable.
If it regularly froze or broke for testers who reported the issue it would be diagnosed and either fixed or, if unfixable in the time permitted, included in the Release Notes.
My 13.10 is stable and reliable...as was 13.04 before.

Have you checked the Release Notes? [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/SaucySalamander/ReleaseNotes](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/SaucySalamander/ReleaseNotes)

Rather than guess or make assumptions, why not investigate the problem? We can help with that.
