### Post by stinkeye on 2012-07-30
I think he's talking about the Hud not the Dash.

If you want to use the keyboard you could just hold down alt and press **e**
to bring up **E**dit in the global menu.
alt+v for the **V**iew menu etc.

Holding down alt reveals the global menu and you just select the first letter of the menu
item you want and use the arrow keys to navigate.
