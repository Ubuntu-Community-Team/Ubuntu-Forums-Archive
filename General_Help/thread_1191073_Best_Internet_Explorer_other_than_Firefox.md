### Post by Ms_Angel_D on 2009-06-18
Internet Explorer is a Browser; Firefox is a Browser, so I guess what your asking is what other Browsers are there for Ubuntu.

There a ton,

Go to System -> Administration -> Synaptic package manager and Search for the term Browser you'll get a bunch of different once to choose from, just pick the one you want to try and click apply.

Also you can do a search for the term "Browser" in add/remove programs.
