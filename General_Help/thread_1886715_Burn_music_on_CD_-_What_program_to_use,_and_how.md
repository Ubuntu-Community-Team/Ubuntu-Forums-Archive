### Post by ajgreeny on 2011-11-25
You just need to choose "Audio disk" on the front opening page of brasero, and similarly k3b, if I remember correctly.  If you have the right codecs on your machine almost any audio file, wav, mp3, ogg, etc etc, can be burned as an audio disk.

I do it often using brasero without problems.
