I have installed Ubuntu 12.04 on my new computer, as the _**only**_ Operating System. In spite of the steps in the 'user guide' I am unable to locate System Monitor, Disk Usage Analyzer or Disk Utility. Can you please tell me how to access these?

### Post by coldcritter64 on 2012-11-20
Tapping (once) the Super (Windows icon) key will bring up Dash in 12.04. Typing in those terms, System Monitor, Disk Usage Analyzer or Disk Utility, brings up their icons under the search term. 

Clicking on any of them from the Dash should activate their associated program.
