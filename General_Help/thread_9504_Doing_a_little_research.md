### Post by TravisNewman on 2004-12-29
XMMS, or Beep. Beep is an offshoot of XMMS using newer libraries, so it has a little more polish, and Beep updates a little more often. You can use Winamp skins in both, but they look better in Beep.

That's the Winamp EQUIVALENTS, but I personally use Rhythmbox or Muine as my music players. The way of organization is much better, but I wish Muine had some more options.
