### Post by yaroto98 on 2009-06-10
Well, dosbox takes the input from your mouse and keyboard. So if you're running a game in dosbox, your mouse works. There's probably some key combination to get out (like in a VM) but you'll have to look at dosbox's preferances to see what it is.

### Post by JordyD on 2009-06-10
OK, I got it (from the DOSBOX man page), CTRL-F10 captures/releases the mouse.
