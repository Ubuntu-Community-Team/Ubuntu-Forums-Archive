### Post by kyphi on 2008-03-24
You are confused? - I bet your computer is confused too.

As carrett suggested, remove your old sound card and while you are at it go into the BIOS and disable the soundchip (if you have one) on your motherboard.

That way, your computer has only one sound card and will not have to make any "decisions" as to which one to use.

If you need help with disabling the onboard sound just post.
