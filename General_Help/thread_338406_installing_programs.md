### Post by ajgreeny on 2007-01-14
Wireless can be a problem in linux generally, and ubuntu is no different to many other distros.  For installing programs, you should try to use apt-get, aptitude or synaptic as much as possible.  There is no one simple method of installing programs you've downloaded from some source or other as a gzipped or bzipped package and it will depend upon what the unzipped package contains.  It can also be a little dangerous to instal packages from unknown sources, that's why there is a trusted repository list in the /etc/apt/sources.list file.
