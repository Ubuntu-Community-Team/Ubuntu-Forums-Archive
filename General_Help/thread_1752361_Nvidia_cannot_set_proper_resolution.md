### Post by Rumpty on 2011-05-07
I think you will have to edit xorg.conf. In Section "Monitor" make HorizSync something like 28.0 - 85.0. In Section "Screen" add another metamode line for the resolution that you want, or alter the existing metamode to 1280 x 1024
