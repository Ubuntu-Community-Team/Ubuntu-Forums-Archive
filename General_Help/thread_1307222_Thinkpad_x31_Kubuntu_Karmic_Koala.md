### Post by herbieh on 2009-11-05
hello,

I had the same problem, when I installed kubuntu 9.10 (not a dist-upgrade) on my X31: 

"I only get corrupted graphic.  most of the apps are ok, but the bottom panel and all the menus are completely inaccessible. "

But I fixed it.

1. do a right click on the desktop and click on "Run Command..."
2. A corrupted window appears. Type into it blindly "systemsettings" and press enter.
3. The KDE System Settings window pops up. Click on "Desktop".
4. There click on "Enable desktop effects" and then click the button "Apply".

That´s it. I hope that solves your problems as well.
