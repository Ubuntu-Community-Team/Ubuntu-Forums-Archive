### Post by ajgreeny on 2019-03-02
Is this your computer or does it belong to somebody else?

If it's yours you must use a password, the same one as your login password, in order to update the OS or install new packages; if you can't remember it you will quickly be very insecure and should not go online with the machine.

It is possible to autologin a user but that is a different query to the one you cite, and in my opinion is always inadvisable.

Give us more details of your problem and we may have other ways around it for you to try.
