### Post by MatthewAdams on 2010-08-10
When I've installed Ubuntu on laptops in the past, I've found the most successful formula is to connect a network cable to the system after installing the OS, downloading updates, and then trying to find a proprietary wireless driver under "hardware drivers" which should be under "preferences" or "administration". 

For me, the physical cable has always worked in connecting to the internet, and the wireless has always been a bit trickier to install and configure.
