### Post by sgosnell on 2010-06-12
The easy way to do this would seem to be to synchronize your notes with a local folder.  I synch mine to a Tomboy folder in my Dropbox folder, so my notes are synced via Dropbox, and I can get them from anywhere.  My notes are synced to several computers I use in different locations.  If you sync your notes to a local folder, you can copy the notes you have elsewhere there, and they will be transferred into your regular Tomboy notes.  You get there by opening a window to search all notes, then Edit/Preferences.
