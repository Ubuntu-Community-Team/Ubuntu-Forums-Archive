### Post by learning curve on 2006-01-14
Got o Applications/Sound and Video and then to Volume Control.  You can view the properties of your alsa card there.  Your microphone is probably disabled.
P.S.  Skype will work best with a headset mic.  You will get feedback from your speakers with an external mic if your speakers are too loud.  Hope this info helps you.
