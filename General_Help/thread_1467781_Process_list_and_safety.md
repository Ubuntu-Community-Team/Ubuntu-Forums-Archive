### Post by P4man on 2010-05-01
If you only install from ubuntu's repositories, you shouldnt worry. Its almost unthinkable you will get a malware infection. For starters there is almost no malware for linux, Then the repo's basically only contain verified  opensource software (you cant hide malware in open source), they are signed and the downloads verified before they are installed, so even if someone hacks the repositories they couldnt infect the programs you end up installing.

Of course that doesnt guarantee you will not enter your credit card details on a malicious website, but you definitely need not worry about running processes.
