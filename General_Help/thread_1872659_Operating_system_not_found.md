### Post by mcduck on 2011-10-31
If I understood your post correctly, your computer doesn't always boot correctly on the first try, but might work after restarting again?

That's usually a sign of hardware problems. Most commonly related to either the motherboard or your power source. Although checking that all cables and such are properly connected would of course be a good start.

### Post by prodigy_ on 2011-10-31
Indeed, sporadic "Operating system not found" errors might indicate a hardware problem. Especially if you didn't change anything boot-related recently. Consider downloading some HDD diagnostics utility and checking your drive.

Make regular backups. Daily, if at all possible. Then you'll always have an immediate solution.
