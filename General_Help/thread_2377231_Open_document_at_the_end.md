### Post by amanchesterman on 2017-11-11
[https://ask.libreoffice.org/en/question/1144/how-to-save-cursor-position-in-writer/](https://ask.libreoffice.org/en/question/1144/how-to-save-cursor-position-in-writer/) 

That thread suggests that Writer will reopen a document at the last cursor position but only if
a) it's in ODT format
b) you're the person who last edited the document and LO knows it's you who is re-opening it

There's no indication that you can get it to open a document at the end if you haven't edited it before, which maybe is what you are looking for?
