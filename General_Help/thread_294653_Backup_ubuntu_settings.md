### Post by Joe_CoT on 2006-11-06
Well, you can back up the whole system :???:

To back up your personal configuration (user settings, firefox bookmarks, gnome desktop, etc), you can back up your home directory. Major system modifications (such as installing beryl) can't really be backed up without just backing up the system.
