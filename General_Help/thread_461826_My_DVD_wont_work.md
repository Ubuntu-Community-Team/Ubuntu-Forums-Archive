### Post by jjacobs2 on 2007-06-02
I've had weird problems like this on occasion.  You can try manually mounting the drive.  Open a terminal and type "sudo umount /media/cdrom" without the quotes.  Then type "sudo mount /media/cdrom" without the quotes.  Your drive may be named differently and you can find the name by navigating to the media folder in nautilus.
