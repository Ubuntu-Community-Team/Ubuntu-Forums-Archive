### Post by Tanker Bob on 2008-01-14
I do not believe that you can partition a mounted drive. You either have to unmount it or, if it is your primary/only system drive, boot a LiveCD or a rescue disk like [Trinity](http://trinityhome.org/Home/index.php?wpid=1&front_id=12) and do the partitioning from there.
