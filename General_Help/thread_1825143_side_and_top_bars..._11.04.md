### Post by bcbc on 2011-08-14
I guess you're referring to the classic gnome desktop instead of the Unity desktop. When you logon: click on your username, but don't enter your password, look at the bottom and select "Classic desktop", then enter your password.

Unity requires graphics acceleration which usually isn't available in a virtual machine.
