### Post by ajgreeny on 2008-07-20
I don't think it is possible to give a real answer to this question as it will depend on what files the new user wants or needs to save to his own home folder.  However, if it is just a few text files from OOo and such like with not too many photos and absolutely no videos, the configuration files for each user do not take a huge amount of space.  Nevertheless, I think you are probably being a bit ambitious wanting to make new users with just that amount of space available.  I suggest you look again at trying to free more space on the disk, perhaps using gparted on the ubuntu live CD, if you can.
