### Post by HermanAB on 2010-10-17
Howdy,

The mail client is only as good as the protocol that you selected.

The Exchange-Outlook DCE protocol was created by The Open Group and is a good one.  IMAP is also a good one.  POP is a bad one.

So, if your Evolution client is configured to use IMAP to talk to your mail server, then it can do everything Outlook does, and then it can access individual mail messages.
