### Post by sisco311 on 2009-11-21
Click [here]("apt://nautilus-open-terminal") (apturl link) to install nautilus-open-terminal. it's a nautilus plugin for opening terminals in arbitrary local paths.

Log out and log back in. Right click in nautilus -> Open in Terminal
