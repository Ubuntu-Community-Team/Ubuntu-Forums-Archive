### Post by tom4everitt on 2010-01-19
I'm not on a ubuntu system at the moment, but i'm pretty sure evolution has saved everything in a subfolder to your home folder called .evolution or similar. 

Just copy that to a new (ubuntu) system, and replace the current .evolution folder there. Then when you start evolution on this new system, all settings (and contacts and emails) should be as on your old system.
