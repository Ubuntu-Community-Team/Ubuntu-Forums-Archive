### Post by tgalati4 on 2010-05-11
Get yourself an account at [http://www.wordpress.com](http://www.wordpress.com) and start blogging so you get the feel of how that service works.  A google search shows there are several offline editors.  I just grabbed a link at random.

[http://www.masternewmedia.org/best-offline-blog-editors-and-web-publishing-tools-mini-guide/](http://www.masternewmedia.org/best-offline-blog-editors-and-web-publishing-tools-mini-guide/)

Wordpress is just one example.  There are several blogging tools and most of them you can run on your own server.

Depending on your hosting service, you could install drupal, a web content management system which is a blog and more.

[http://drupal.org](http://drupal.org)

For blog-related things that you can install on your system immediately; open a terminal:

apt-cache search blog
