### Post by Therion on 2009-03-19
Open Synaptic.
Search on, and install, **startupmanager**.
Go to System/Administration.
Open Startup Manager.
Go here: [http://www.ubuntugeek.com/startup-manager-change-settings-in-grub-grub2-and-usplash.html](http://www.ubuntugeek.com/startup-manager-change-settings-in-grub-grub2-and-usplash.html)
See the fourth pic down... "Appearance Tab" and so forth.
See the menu-box for "Usplash"?  Yours will say "Kubuntu" or something similar.  
Change it via the dropdown menu to "Ubuntu".
