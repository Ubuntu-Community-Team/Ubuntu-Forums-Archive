### Post by jonobr on 2009-07-11
mutt is a text based mail reader it may suit your needs
but if most of your apps are web based, you could use w3m 
w3m is a command line or text based browser which presents webpages as text

There is a help screen which may help you
and it takes a bit of time to get used to, but once you have the hang of it, its quick.

you can tab between tabs or fields,
for input fields you hit enter, enter your text again it just gets a bit of getting used to.,

Depending on how calendar and contacts are done it may work, if its a complex interface, chances are it wont.
