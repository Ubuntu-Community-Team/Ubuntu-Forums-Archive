### Post by ethanay on 2012-09-27
MG&TL -- while I can't speak to the OP's reasons for wanting a black mouse cursor, I can tell you that I work with blind computer users as well as computer users with visual impairments.  A giant, black mouse cursor with a white outline is much easier to locate than a small white cursor with a black outline.

That said, it's a shame that the included LXDE "customize look and feel" widget doesn't even successfully change the mouse cursor from black to white like it indicates it should do!
