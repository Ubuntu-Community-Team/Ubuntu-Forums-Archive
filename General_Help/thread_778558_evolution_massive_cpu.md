### Post by OMRebel on 2008-05-03
I found a thread on this issue here:
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=527700](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=527700)

There has also been a bug filed on this one:
[https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/evolution-data-server/+bug/151536](https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/evolution-data-server/+bug/151536)

Two things you can do:
1. Go ahead and open up Evolution, and run through the config process.  Then just open it and close it, as that will cause it to stop.
2. Remove Evolution since you don't use it.
