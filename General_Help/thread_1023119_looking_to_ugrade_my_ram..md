### Post by GCG199 on 2008-12-27
If your computer was bought from a company that builds them. It should include a spec sheet in the box of CDs/DVDS of programs for when it is time to reformat Windows.

It is possible that it is listed on that sheet. However, if that doesn't work. You can Google for "how to upgrade RAM" and it will list websites that can do a quick scan of your system that will show what the exact specs are.
