### Post by Sef on 2010-04-04
1) Just download the testing version of Ubuntu.  

2) Be aware that testing versions are buggy and may crash your system.  

3) Use it on a spare computer.

4) Do not use it on a production machine.

5) Report any bugs to launchpad.
