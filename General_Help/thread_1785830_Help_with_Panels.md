### Post by nzjethro on 2011-06-19
I think this is because your current theme has a gradient coloured panel (i.e. the top is lighter than the bottom). When a panel is vertical (as you've got on your screenshot), it stacks small panels on top of each other. As a workaround, look for a theme that has a constant colour for its panels? This will stop the "crinkly" look. Otherwise, you could add the applets you want, and then right click and deselect "Expand". This will shrink the panel to just the size needed for your applets, and thus there will be no crinkly look.
