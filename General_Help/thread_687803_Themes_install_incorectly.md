### Post by jrharvey on 2008-02-04
Sometimes you have to use the customize setting. Controls, colors, window border (metacity), icons and pointers are all options. You should find your theme under controls and your metacity under window border. Those are the biggest thing that comes with a theme setting. Icons and pointers are usually packaged seperately.
