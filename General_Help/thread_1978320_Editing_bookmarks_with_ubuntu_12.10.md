### Post by mcduck on 2012-05-12
From the menu: Bookmarks->Show All Bookmarks.

Right-clicking an individual bookmark in the Bookmarks toolbar works fine for me as well, does your right-click work correctly everywhere else (in the browser, and in the desktop/other applications)? Do you have any extensions installed which might affect how your bookmarks are working?
