### Post by DuckHook on 2017-10-23
Ever since Skype was bought by Microsoft, their Linux support has been an afterthought. They even announced cessation of the Linux version altogether at one point, but changed their minds after loud outcry and internal strategic reflection. To my knowledge, there is no current "stable" version of Skype for Linux. The beta version is all that there is. There are older non-beta versions, but they are now obsolete.

FWIW, this is one reason why I don't use Skype and use Google Hangouts instead.
