### Post by Lod on 2007-03-06
If you really want to keep it simple you should use some sort of preconfigured server like Clarkconnect or SME. The developers did all the hard word, you just have to do the administration (both with some easy to understand webbased tool). They have [horde](http://horde.org) webmail and are also able to do file-sharing, webhosting, ftp and more. I myself have been using Clarkconnect for years now and are quiet content about it. It took me a few days to find the wished configuration but then hadn't have to look back except for updates (once a year or so).

[Clarkconnect](http://www.clarkconnect.com) is based on Centos (Red hat)
[SME-server](http://www.smeserver.org/) is Debian based.
