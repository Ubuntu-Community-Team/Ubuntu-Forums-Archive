G'Day artisan,  
I have a HP PSC 1315 printer, the following works for me.   Pressed the On/Resume button, then press & hold the Cancel button while the PSC is going through it's startup.  The Printer will load a single sheet of paper and then eject it.  The printer will continues it's startup (clunking and making a fair amount of noise (probably cleaning the print heads), then all the green lights are lit and are steady, the I can Print, Scan or Copy with out getting that test page.

### Post by artisan on 2010-07-31
Hi TopEnder
Thanks.

The link above did solve the issue.
After changing an ink cartridge, it appears that the printer is programed to do this.
All you have is switch on the printer. Yes it automatically prints a test page.
But, this is the cunning part :D, you put the test page onto the scanner and press scan.

This "aligns" the print heads. And the printer stops printing a test page every time you switch on the printer.
