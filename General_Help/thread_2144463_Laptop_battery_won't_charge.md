### Post by girlggc on 2013-05-12
I managed to solve the problem with a fix I found on an HP support forum.  I don't know how or why this worked but it did.  I will copy and paste the fix I found in case anyone else runs into this problem.

1. Shut down the computer.
2. Remove the power cord.
3. Remove the battery.
4. Hold the power button down for about 10 seconds.
5. Reconnect battery.
6. Reconnect power cord.
7. Start computer back up and see if it's fixed. May need to wait a minute or two to see that the charge is increasing as it should.
