### Post by coldraven on 2011-05-04
Alt+Tab will flip between applications on most computers.
Pressing Super+s (Super is the Windows key) will show you all the application that are open on the workspace (desktop) that you are viewing.
using 11.04 you can also move applications to any of four different desktops.
Click the workspace switcher icon in the launcher, you can drag applications to the workspace you want. Press super+w to see all windows in all workspaces.
You will love it when you get used to it :)
