I think the new bookmark facility is geared toward mobile phones.  It makes syncing bookmarks across devices easier.  You may not have the same folder order on your phone as on your laptop or desktop, so put everything in one big folder and sync that.  I agree, I prefer the old method because I save more bookmarks on my laptop, where I have 12 categories (Rule of 12), but I don't have any folder categories on my phone.

Google is better than ATT or Samsung, both of which decide to install apps on your phone that change its behavior without your acknowledgement.  The updates are hidden under "security" or "system" updates, which then add new apps and new annoyances.

At least with Google, there is a roll-out process with an opt-in or opt-out button.  At some point, you know that the new app will become standard, so you have time to get used to it.
