### Post by ewanchic on 2008-02-07
You made need to update the DVD Drive's firmware. I've had similar issues before. Another test would be to do a checksum on a disk. Sounds like that has nothing to do with your problem, I know, but beleive I've had "weird" issues where the drive would 'sorta" work. At first I thought it was my computer. Took me months to realize it was the drive itself and it needed a firmware update.
