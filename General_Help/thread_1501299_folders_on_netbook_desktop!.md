### Post by kerry_s on 2010-06-04
just open your file manager & drag the folder to the left panel to bookmark.
or
enter it & click bookmarks-> add bookmark, from the menu.
or
enter it & press ctrl+d

also you can add it to favorites by drag & drop it on the show desktop icon
