I just installed Ubuntu 10.10 on my Dell Dimension 4500S  machine. The problem that I am having is that it looks like I have a think black vertical bar running down  the left hand side of my monitor. The monitor looks like it is pushed to the right by about two inches. How  can i fix this problem?

### Post by Topsiho on 2010-10-14
If I have this problem I usually tinker with the settings on the monitor itself :)

On the LCD monitor I have here there is a switch called Auto, and this usually centers the screen beautifully.

Else you should try and use System > Preferences > Screens (I have to translate from Dutch), and use the settings that you find there, especially the refresh frequency (translation again), putting this to a lower (or another) value might do it. These settings may vary with the videocard in your computer.

Topsiho

### Post by rmcellig on 2010-10-14
That was just too easy!! Your suggestion worked. I pressed the auto button on my Acer LCD monitor and it worked fine. Thanks!!!!!
