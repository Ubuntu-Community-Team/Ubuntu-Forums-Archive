### Post by kd7swh on 2006-12-23
if you think linux is your gateway to the hacking world remember that computers are only as smart as their users. 

The altering or modifying of something to change it's effect is hacking. Illegal computing activities, and malicious hacking behavior isn't something that I support. I would encourage you to learn what hacking is and is not before you get yourself into any trouble. 

That being said, learning linux commands is important 

[http://www.linuxcommand.org/](http://www.linuxcommand.org/) 
[http://www.computerhope.com/unix.htm#05](http://www.computerhope.com/unix.htm#05)
[http://www.reallylinux.com/docs/basic.shtml](http://www.reallylinux.com/docs/basic.shtml)

are all good sites for linux commands and shell scripting. You should learn how to script your bash commands. It makes life easier. Have fun, read a lot, and don't do anything illegal.

For a history of Hacking check out:

[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Timeline_of_hacker_history](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Timeline_of_hacker_history)
