Can't help you on how to stop it, but another easy way to get control is to use Alt+Space, this will bring the window menu (not the file menu) of any windowed app and then you can unmaximize, minimize or move it which is what I usually end up doing. Once you click on move, the cursor changes to a hand and the window "moves" with it. I can then resize it or just finish what I'm doing and exit.
