You can [convert Firefoxs bookmarks online]("http://www.linkagogo.com/go/Convert") to XBEL or one can use a feature of the [SyncPlaces add-on]("http://www.andyhalford.com/syncplaces/intro.html"). 

As to where Midori stores thse files, I don't know but they should be easy to find, look for a hidden folder or files for Midori in your home directory.
