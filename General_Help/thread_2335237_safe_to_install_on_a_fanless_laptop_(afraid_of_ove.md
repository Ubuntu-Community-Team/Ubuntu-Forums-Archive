### Post by Keith_Helms on 2016-08-27
I had an HP laptop a few years back that tended to overheat.  It got so hot one time that it fried a hard drive.  After that I got a device called a laptop cooling pad and never had problems again.  A cooling pad is a flat metal rectangle with 2 or more fans in the surface that the laptop sits on and which blows air up at the laptop to keep it cooler.  Usually they plug into one of the laptop's USB ports for power.  You can find dozens of models on Amazon or Newegg.
