### Post by pastalavista on 2010-03-05
Open Synaptic Package Manager, click the 'Custom Filters' button in the left panel and select 'Missing Recommends' and 'Broken' packages and look for any themes there. You can also search for 'gnome themes' for any you haven't installed or might need to reinstall.
