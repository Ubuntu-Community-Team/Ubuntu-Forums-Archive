### Post by merlinus on 2007-06-23
The latest kernel upgrade has caused problems for some folks.

Can you boot ubuntu from another one?

If not, it may have changed your grub root (this has happened twice for me on one of my computers, but not on the other two).

If you press e at the grub menu, you can edit this.
