### Post by fagulhaspt on 2011-12-24
This thread is already old, so I'll just update with current status

With Ubuntu 11
  - the printer is recognized easily and works fine, just connect your printer by usb to your computer, go to Applications/SystemTools/System Settings, click on the printer icon, and the printer will be recognized and show up in the options; add the printer and set it as default - and thats all

  - the scanner is recognized as being an "Epson CC-570L" (it's ok, it's another compatible model for the scanner) and works out of the box :). Just go to Applications/Graphics/Simple Scan, and then Documents/Preferences and you will see the "Epson CC-570L" option - select it and it's done! you can now scan :)

Ubuntu is becoming more and more simple - and that's very good :)
