### Post by cicatrix1 on 2008-11-25
Might be a RAM issue.  If you had power issues, it's possible it caused some collateral damage to some of your PCs components.  I'd run memtest86 (from the grub menu) and see if that shows any failures.  If it does, you'll have to replace RAM, but there still might be other problems with your board or chip/chipset.
