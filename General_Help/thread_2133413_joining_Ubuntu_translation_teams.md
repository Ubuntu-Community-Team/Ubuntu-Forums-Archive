### Post by Doug S on 2013-04-12
Try the mailing list for some help: [https://lists.ubuntu.com/mailman/listinfo/ubuntu-translators](https://lists.ubuntu.com/mailman/listinfo/ubuntu-translators)
You should join the list before sending an e-mail to it, otherwise the e-mail will be held for approval by a moderator, and then nothing happens. (I just went through this two days ago, and had to join the list.)
