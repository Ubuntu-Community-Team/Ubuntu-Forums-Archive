### Post by 3rdalbum on 2009-08-30
Sony Walkman. The one that's just the earbuds won't be appropriate for you, but the NWZ models including the new X series are excellent. They work with any USB-capable operating system, and when you turn off the player it remembers where you were in the track. (for the first 24 hours after turning it off, it actually just goes to standby mode, so as soon as you hit the Play button it comes back up).

The only times it forgets the playback position are when you plug it into the computer, and when you change track.
