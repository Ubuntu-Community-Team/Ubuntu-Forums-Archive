If you've got two **panels**, delete the one where your **window list** is not showing. Then right-click on the other panel and choose **New Panel**. Right-click on the new panel, and choose **Add to Panel**. Then scroll down, and add whatever you wish from the list, including the **window list**.
Good luck.
