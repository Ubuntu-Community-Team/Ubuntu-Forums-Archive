Fixed. NEC drives tend to not work with DVD-R media and are picky as to OS, drivers, the phase of the moon, etc...

Plugged in a USB DVD drive (Sony DW-Q120A) and all is well. I've used this drive for live CD rescues and it has always worked well.

Thanks to cdfreaks.com forum threads I am enlightened and will never buy another NEC drive. Life is too short to fight with poor firmware.
