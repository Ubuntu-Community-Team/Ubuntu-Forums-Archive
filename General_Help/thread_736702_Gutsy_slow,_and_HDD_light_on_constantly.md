### Post by rectec794613 on 2008-03-26
There could be alot of reasons for this:

1. You have to many processes running, go to System>Administration>System Monitor and see what you can do on the processes tab.

2. You have too much temporary files. Open up Firefox and go to Tools>Clear Private Data
3. You need some more RAM; how much RAM do you have:?:
4. You have too much hardware hooked up.
5. You need to get Prelink; a program which makes it take less time to open programs.
