### Post by fsmithred on 2008-03-22
Right-click on that icon and select Properties. When the properties window opens, click on the icon within it. Navigate to /usr/share/pixmaps. On mine, pixmaps is listed in the column on the left, so I just double-click on that, and it lists a bunch of icons. If you have a custom icon, you can save it anywhere in your home directory, and navigate to that file instead of to pixmaps.
