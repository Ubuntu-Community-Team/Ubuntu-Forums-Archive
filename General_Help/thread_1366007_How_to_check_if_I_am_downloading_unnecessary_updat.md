### Post by 3rdalbum on 2009-12-28
If they are listed in Update Manager under the headings "Important Security Updates" or "Recommended Updates", then those headings speak for themselves.

Anything under "Backported Updates" or "Distribution Updates" is not essential. You can uncheck these before clicking the Install Updates button.
