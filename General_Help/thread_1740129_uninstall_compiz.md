### Post by lithopsian on 2011-04-26
You could just turn off visual effects from the system menu, or you can replace it with something else to run your desktop.  metacity --replace is a likely one but you could pick a different desktop manager if you want.

Once you have a different desktop manager that you are happy with you can start deleting compiz if you want.

### Post by akand074 on 2011-04-26
Yeah don't uninstall anything until you have something else taking its place already set up. But yeah as the above post stated, usually just turning off visual effects is enough. Just right click on your desktop and go to the visual effects tab and choose "none".
