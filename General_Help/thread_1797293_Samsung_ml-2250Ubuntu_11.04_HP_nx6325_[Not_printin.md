### Post by crustle on 2011-07-21
So, here's one way:

Go to "system settings" in your Ubuntu menu.  
Select "printing."  
Your printer should be listed in that menu. 
Right click on your printer icon and select "preferences."
Be sure you are on the settings page.
Under "make and model," select "change."
A menu will come up that says "choose driver."  
Be sure the button for "choose from database" is selected.

Give your computer a moment to think.  At some point, a list of printers will show up on the left (your printer should be highlighted) and a list of drivers on the right.  For my Samsung ML 2250, I chose the "CUPS-Gutenberg" driver, but you can try different drivers and see what works for you.

Cheers!
