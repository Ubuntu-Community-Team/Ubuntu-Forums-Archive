you probably mean the yellow things with text? they are called "tooltips", and if you go into gconf editor (run gconf from a terminal), and go to /apps/panel/global/tooltips_enabled, you can uncheck that item, and at least for the items in the panel tooltips will go away.

don't know about the tooltips for all the other stuff, though...
