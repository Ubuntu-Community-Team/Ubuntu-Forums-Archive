### Post by louieb on 2007-11-10
So you think Windows is still installed on your PC but you just can't boot to it?  What you need to do is replace the pointer to GRUB in the MBR with one that points to the Windows boot loader NTLDR. Heres several ways to do just that:  [IDBS Remove/Replace/Uninstal Grub]("http://www.users.bigpond.net.au/hermanzone/p18.htm")
