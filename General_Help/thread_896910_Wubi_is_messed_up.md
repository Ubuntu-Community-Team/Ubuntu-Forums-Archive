### Post by rokytnji on 2008-08-22
Uninstall Wubi using the uninstall icon in the Ubuntu folder in your c drive. Delete the  Wubi icon on the desktop to trash after you do the uninstall. Go to Wubi's website and redownlad wubi to your desktop. Move the installer to your my documents folder and make a new folder and put Wubi's installer in it. And you know how to do the rest as you will be starting from scratch again.
