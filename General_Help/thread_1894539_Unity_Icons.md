### Post by Frogs Hair on 2011-12-12
If you are using Unity 3D and have the CCSM installed you can reduce the icon size . Open the CCSM find the Ubuntu Unity Plug-in and under the experimental tab reduce the size. Unity 2D doesn't have this option .
