I am using ubuntu 8.04 and changed my password a long time ago, and now it is not the same as the default keyring password. ### Post by spiderbatdad on 2009-12-01
Accessories>>Passwords & Encryptions

Under the Passwords tab click on the word "login" to highlight it.  You may also have other keys like wireless networks listed below login. Then right click it. You should see option "change password."
