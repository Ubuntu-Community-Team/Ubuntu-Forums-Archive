### Post by HuckleSmothered on 2009-05-29
Check your Preferences | Appearance Visual Effects options. You may need to install Compiz, or up your visual effects to normal from None.

I had this same problem with my Dell, which has an nVidia video card. There are several threads about this problem out there. If the above doesn't work for you, there are some more solutions posted.
