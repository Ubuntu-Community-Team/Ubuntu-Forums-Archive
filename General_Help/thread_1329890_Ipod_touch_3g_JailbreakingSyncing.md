### Post by ColdLunch on 2009-11-17
You can jailbreak a 3 g.  I think you need windows/mac to do it though.  There probably isn't a linux jailbreaking app out.

After jailbreaking, install OpenSSH and BSDsubsystem.

There are ways to connect it.  It's pretty hard to get working.  I followed a couple guides and after about 2 hours I finally got it to work. >.>

Just google "karmic ipod touch" and you'll get a bunch of guides/information.

Follow the ones that tell you to install "ipod-convenience".  That worked for me.
