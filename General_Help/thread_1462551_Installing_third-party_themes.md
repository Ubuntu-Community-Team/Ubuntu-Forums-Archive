### Post by CoffeeCoder on 2010-04-26
Hehe, I got it figured out. :)

All you have to do is go to System>Preferences>Appearance, then click on the "Install" button that appears in the Themes dialog. Then simply browse your downloads folder, click the .tar.gz file, and it will install the theme. :)
