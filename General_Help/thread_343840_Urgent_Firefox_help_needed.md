### Post by _simon_ on 2007-01-22
have a look in your home folder.

~/.mozilla/firefox/default/extensions/

Note that the mozilla folder is hidden. 
The "default" folder will have a bunch of letters and numbers in front of it.

Try removing the extension you are having problems with from that folder.
