### Post by Cheesemill on 2012-08-05
You need to install the VirtualBox Guest Additions.

When your VM is running go to Devices > Install Guest Additions.

[https://www.virtualbox.org/manual/ch04.html](https://www.virtualbox.org/manual/ch04.html)
