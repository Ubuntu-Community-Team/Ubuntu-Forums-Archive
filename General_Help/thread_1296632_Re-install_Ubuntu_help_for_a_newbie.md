### Post by Niko Johnson on 2009-10-20
yeah you can reinstall no problem and it will do nothing to your winblows partition, just pop in the live cd again and go along with the installation again, this time do the partitions manually and this may be a dumb answer to your questions but did you specify a swap partition on your drive or did you do all ubuntu? make sure you put at least 2 gigs swap, i can see a newbie skiping this step during installation... its an easy mistake to miss especially for your first time..
