### Post by coldReactive on 2009-10-12
It's best if you don't have any serious electronics even plugged into a wall socket of any kind during a lightning storm. If you have a surge suppressor though, then you should be mostly fine, unless the surge suppressors have already had their fuses blown (yes, they have fuses in them, one reason why they're called surge suppressors.)
