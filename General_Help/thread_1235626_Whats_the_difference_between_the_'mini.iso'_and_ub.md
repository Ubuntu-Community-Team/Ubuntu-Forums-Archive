### Post by slakkie on 2009-08-09
mini.iso is a netinstall, which will grab all packages from the web. So you are up-2-date from the moment you boot.

The alternate CD is basicly the same as the livecd, but without the GUI of the liveCD.

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Installation/MinimalCD](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Installation/MinimalCD)
