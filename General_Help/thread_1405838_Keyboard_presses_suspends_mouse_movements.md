### Post by foens on 2010-02-13
Hello Ubuntu community.

I just installed Ubuntu and I am having a problem with the keyboard and mouse.

It seems that if I repeatedly presses a key on the keyboard (say h), and at the same time moves the mouse around - then sometimes the mouse movements stops completely for ½ - 0,75 seconds.

Is this an issue for everyone else?

I am running Ubuntu 9.10 on a HP Laptop, and my mouse is my touchpad.

---

### Post by ironclaw on 2010-02-13
It's not a bug - it's a feature. ;)

It's meant to stop people from accidentally changing the focus of graphical interfaces with the touchpad while typing. The option to change this should be in System -> Preferences -> Mouse -> Touchpad ('Disable touchpad while typing').
