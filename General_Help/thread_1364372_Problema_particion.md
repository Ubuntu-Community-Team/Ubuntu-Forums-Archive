### Post by JC Cheloven on 2009-12-25
English, please. Everybody likes to understand ;-)

Yes, you can boot from live cd, run gparted, shrink your ubuntu partition by the desired amount (say 35 Gb), taking care to do it by the side next to your xp. It will take a long time, as probably many Gb of data have to be moved.

Then you can expand your xp partition by that amount (35 Gb or whatever). This will take only a couple of minutes.
