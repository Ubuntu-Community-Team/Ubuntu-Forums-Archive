### Post by shakin on 2006-06-06
I can't say I've noticed this at all. However, you may be able to boost the overall brightness by adding a gamma option to xorg.conf and increasing RGB values the same amount.

Eg.

Gamma 2.0 2.0 2.0

It should go into the monitor section.

Or you could adjust your monitor until it's right. Remember, color changes from OS to OS (I've seen large difference between Windows and OS X, for instance) so I'm not surprised that some people will need to adjust monitor settings when using a different OS. Sometimes changing your video card will change the colors a bit.
