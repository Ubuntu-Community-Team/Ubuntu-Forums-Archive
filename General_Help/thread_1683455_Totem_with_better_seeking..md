### Post by sashoalm on 2011-02-07
I like Totem, but I don't like how forward and backward seeking aren't the same - you go 4 minutes forward but only 1 minute backwards. Also, I'd rather have smaller intervals available, too.

So I downloaded the code and recompiled it, and changed the seeking intervals. Now I have these seeks:

left/right: 5 seconds
shift+left/right: 1 second
alt+left/right: 15 seconds
ctrl+left/right: 1 minute

Now it's much better :)
