### Post by greenpeace on 2012-08-29
I think it might depend on the browser you're using...

### Post by greenpeace on 2012-08-29
Just had a little look, and I hope the following helps you:

In Chrome you can rank the languages you would like to see the page in, for instance I have:

[LIST=1]
[*]English (UK)
[*]English
[*]Portuguese (BR)
[*]Portuguese
[/LIST]
So this will tell the servers to display English if they can.

put this in your address bar to configure it: chrome://chrome/settings/languages#lang

To do the same thing in firefox, open the preferences, go to the Content tab, and then you can choose your preferred languages
