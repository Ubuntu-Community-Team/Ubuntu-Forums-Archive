### Post by Mark Phelps on 2010-03-11
That's a great link ... but just be sure to pay attention to the Ubuntu version when you look at the particular cards.  While the "legacy" ATI cards ARE supported in Ubuntu 9.10, they do not have restricted drivers.

If you want an ATI card that still has restricted drivers that work, get one of the newer HD 3x/4x/5x cards.
