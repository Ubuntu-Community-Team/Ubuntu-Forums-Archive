### Post by dschaller on 2006-03-29
I'm something of a newbie myself, but from your screenshot it looks like your wireless device is already detected, so the hard part is done. If it's not an encrypted connection, I think you should just be able to click "properties", enable the connection at the checkbox, set it for DHCP, press "ok", then press "activate" and you'll be off and running. Have you tried that already? You might need to type in the SSID for the network you want to connect to also as part of the configuration.
