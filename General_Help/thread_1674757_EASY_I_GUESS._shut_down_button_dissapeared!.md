### Post by MARP1961 on 2011-01-24
Right click on the top panel, click 'Add To Panel' then scroll through the various applets available and select a new shutdown button and click 'Add'. This type of thing seems to happen from time to time but it is quite easy to add and remove unwanted applets from the panels.
