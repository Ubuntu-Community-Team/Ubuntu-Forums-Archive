### Post by blueridgedog on 2011-04-20
I don't know what mods you can do to an HP Pavilion.  However, some basics are:
-make certain your heatsink isn't dusty...clean it out with compressed air...this is the metal object between the fan and the CPU and the fan and the GPU
-make certain your case fans are working
-upgrade your case fans (they are standard sizes and you can buy one that puts out more cubic feet per minute)
-do not restrict air flow into or out of your case (keep areas clean and clear)
-make certain your CPU fan is actually working (feel for air flow)

Good luck!
