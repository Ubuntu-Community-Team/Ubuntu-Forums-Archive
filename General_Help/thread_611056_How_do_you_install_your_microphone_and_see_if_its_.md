### Post by patis on 2007-11-12
First, check the sound mixer to verify that the mic is not muted. You can right-click the speaker on the right side of the top menu bar and select "Open volume control".  If yoy don't see the mic control there try Edit-->Preferences and see if you can enable the mic controls there.

Second, visit Applications-->Sound and Video-->Sound Recorder and try to record something there.

Good luck
