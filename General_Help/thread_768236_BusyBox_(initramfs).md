### Post by brethren on 2008-04-30
this sometimes happens when you shut down windows/ubuntu irregularly ie holding down the power switch and doing a hard reboot, it also happens when you install under vista that has the latest patches (the installer crashes at the end of the install and you have to do a hard reboot)

anyway, its pretty easy to sort out. launch your windows os and just select restart to shutdown and restart your computer properly. next time you start ubuntu it'll load up normally

btw this only applies to the wubi install;)
