### Post by eurgain on 2006-12-28
I'd be a bit wary of some of the Dell 8xxx series with Ubuntu, because some of them have badly broken BIOSes.  My 8600 and Ubuntu do not get along at all well, but it runs SUSE 10.2 faultlessly.

OTOH, my Dell Latitude X1 works 100% with Ubuntu straight off the CD, but suspend to ram is decidedly flaky in SUSE.

Strange...

Generally, though.  If you are after built-in wireless, for an older machine, the Intel IPW2100/2200 is supremely well supported, and others (such as Broadcom) less so.  NVidia has great graphicas support as long as you do not want 3D.
