### Post by tgalati4 on 2013-01-27
What's the make and model of the laptop?  Several laptops have problems with delaminating graphics chips.  The graphics chip gets hot and lifts off of the motherboard resulting in a freeze and strange graphics.  Do a search on your laptop's make and model and see if others have the same experience.
