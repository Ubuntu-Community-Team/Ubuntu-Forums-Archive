### Post by efflandt on 2010-11-19
Sometimes even if you change boot order in BIOS, some systems still default to booting from the internal drive, unless you press a key during BIOS splash screen (often F12 or Esc on my computers).  That is for protection against inadvertently booting a removable disk that may have something nasty on it.

The only virus I ever caught was a boot sector virus spread by floppy disks, long ago before the internet as we know it now.
