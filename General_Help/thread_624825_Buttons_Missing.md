### Post by Grey Box on 2007-11-27
Hi. Well if you're saying what i think you're saying, then your gnome menu bar is missing the Workspace Switcher Applet.

You can easily add it again:

Find a spot on your system menu bar which hasn't got any other button or thingamiebob on it, right click it, then choose "Add to Panel"

You should get a box which lets you add applets. Scroll down until you find Workspace Switcher, and add it. 

I hope that solves the prob.
