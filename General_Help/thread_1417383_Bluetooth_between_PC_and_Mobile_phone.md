### Post by Post Monkeh on 2010-02-27
are you using gnome?

if so, i have a bluetooth icon in my system tray area (right hand side of top panel) that i click and go down to devices>send files.

to enable sending files from your phone to your pc, you need to install the gnome-user-share package , then go into system>preferences>personal file sharing, and your option to allow your pc to recieve files via bluetooth is in there.
