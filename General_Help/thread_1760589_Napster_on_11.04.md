### Post by 3rdalbum on 2011-05-21
Indeed, Mozilla Prism is no longer in the repositories. It can still be downloaded from [http://prism.mozillalabs.com](http://prism.mozillalabs.com).

However, you don't actually need Prism; you can just type that URL into your Firefox address bar. All Prism does is start a web browser instance with your chosen URL in it. So, just click on the link in your post, make sure you have all the necessary browser plugins, and you'll be able to listen to music as before.
