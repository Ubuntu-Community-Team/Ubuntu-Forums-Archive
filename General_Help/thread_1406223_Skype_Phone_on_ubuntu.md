### Post by Sparckus on 2010-02-16
I got mine set up using bluetooth for the mobile broadband.  Assuming you have a bluetooth adapter all you should need to do is switch on bluetooth on the phone and connect to it using the bluetooth program in System>Preferences.

Don't think there's a way to access the mass storage via USB with it, I know there's definetly not anyway to use it as a modem via USB.

You could also use a microSD card to transfer files onto your PC with a card reader.


EDIT:

If your both from the UK Tesco has their own brand bluetooth adapter (Technika) for under £10 (think it cost me £6-8 ) which works fine on 9.10.
