### Post by dbott67 on 2005-10-27
[QUOTE=graabein]:confused: Is that even possible?[/QUOTE]

Yes.  To change the icons (for Firefox/Thunderbird, in particular) follow the instructions here:

[http://www.ubuntuguide.org/#restoreoriginaliconsfirefox](http://www.ubuntuguide.org/#restoreoriginaliconsfirefox)
[http://www.ubuntuguide.org/#restoreoriginaliconsthunderbird](http://www.ubuntuguide.org/#restoreoriginaliconsthunderbird)

These instructions will work for any app (provided you know what default icons it uses).  Just backup the original icons and replace them with you preferred icons.

Attached is my desktop & you'll notice the original "flaming red firefox" in the title bar of the browser, as well as in the application task running in the top bar.
