---

### Post by raketax on 2015-11-21
Not sure if I understand the question right but: sensitivity usually means how sensitive the said device is to input (e.g.: if you pull your finger on the touchpad a little, or move the mouse a little, how much of that should the computer translate to on screen pointer movement). Higher sensitivity usually means that the pointer will move more, even if you move the mouse less. Drag and Drop threshols is the vaule of how far you would have to click and drag an on screen element (for example an icon) to make it consider itself dragged, and consider the poisition you are dropping it at as its new location instead of jumping back to its original place.
Hope that helps.
