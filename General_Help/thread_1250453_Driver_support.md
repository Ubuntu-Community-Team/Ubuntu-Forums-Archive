### Post by shae on 2009-08-26
> **coldReactive said:**
> Linux =/= Just Ubuntu.

That is true, but Remember that Ubuntu and every other form of linux both receive their drivers from the Kernel for the most part.  Ubuntu uses a much newer kernel than those listed in the comparability list on the manufacturers site.  Generally, if a driver was in the older version of the Linux kernel, it is still there, with the exception of bugs.

Thus, it is reasonable to conclude that his card should work.
