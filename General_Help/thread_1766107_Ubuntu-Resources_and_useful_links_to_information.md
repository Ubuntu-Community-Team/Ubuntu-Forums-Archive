```

sudo apt-get install xfce4

Thanks [wildmanne39]("http://ubuntuforums.org/member.php?u=508533") for sharing.

Here are some more from my side:

A tour of git: the basics - [http://cworth.org/hgbook-git/tour/](http://cworth.org/hgbook-git/tour/)

A link to the GConf Reference Manual - [http://developer.gnome.org/gconf/stable/](http://developer.gnome.org/gconf/stable/)

A link to the Minimal Ubuntu-ISO - [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Installation/MinimalCD](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Installation/MinimalCD)

Creating a distro with uck - [http://uck.sourceforge.net/](http://uck.sourceforge.net/)

And for easily creating your own distro of a already working system you could use remastersys: 
[http://geekconnection.org/remastersys/ubuntu.html](http://geekconnection.org/remastersys/ubuntu.html)

But for creating a 'real distro' try live-build with your customized live-scripts referring to:
[http://live.debian.net/](http://live.debian.net/) , [http://www.linux-live.org/](http://www.linux-live.org/) , [http://live.debian.net/gitweb](http://live.debian.net/gitweb) and also have a look at the manpages at:
[http://manpages.ubuntu.com/manpages/maverick/en/man1/lh_config.1.html](http://manpages.ubuntu.com/manpages/maverick/en/man1/lh_config.1.html) 

And last but not least a link to MariaDB a perfect alternative to oracle's mysql as it seems to me
[http://kb.askmonty.org/v/installing-mariadb-deb-files](http://kb.askmonty.org/v/installing-mariadb-deb-files)
