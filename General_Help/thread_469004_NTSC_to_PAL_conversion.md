### Post by vanadium on 2007-06-09
There are many ways, but it is not an easy and quick task. It would involve ripping the DVD's to hard disk, disassembling the streams, change the streams to PAL complient formats, reassemble (reauthor) the DVD and burn it. If it is your hobby, you want to delve in all that. If you just want to quickly convert to PAL, then I have to answer that there is no quick possibility.

I am a bit surprised though. Most nowadays DVD players play both NTSC and PAL fine. Perhaps you can change settings in your DVD. It is worth taking a look in its manual for that.
