Drag it to the panel, it should stay there.

### Post by stinkeye on 2012-07-02
It will be set to autostart after you have run it once.
Just run it by typing in 
**system load indicator** in the dash
until it appears and then click on it.
Once running in the panel, right click on it for preferences.
