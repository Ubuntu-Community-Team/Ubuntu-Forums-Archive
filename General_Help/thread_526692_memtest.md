### Post by HermanAB on 2007-08-15
Most memory test programs don't really work.  To properly test memory, you need a little hardware test device.  If in serious doubt about your memory, take it to a repair shop and ask them nicely to test it for you.  Better use an anti-static bag, else the memory may be *really* broken after all the transport and handling.
