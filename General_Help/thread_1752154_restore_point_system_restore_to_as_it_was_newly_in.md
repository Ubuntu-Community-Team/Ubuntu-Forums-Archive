### Post by coffeecat on 2011-05-07
There is no system restore in Ubuntu as you find in Windows. Even in Windows, System Restore wouldn't take you back to a true as-new condition.

To be honest, doing a fresh install replacing your present one would be quicker than tracking down all the applications and libraries you have installed. In your situation I would choose to back up my personal files and do a clean install. It takes well under an hour and then you know it's fresh.
