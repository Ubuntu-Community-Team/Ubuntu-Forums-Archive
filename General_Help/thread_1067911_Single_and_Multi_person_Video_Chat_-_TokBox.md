Anyone try TokBox for their video conferencing needs? [http://www.tokbox.com](http://www.tokbox.com)

They also have an Adobe AIR app for desktop integration. [http://www.tokbox.com/view/downloads](http://www.tokbox.com/view/downloads)

Its not as seamless as it could be, as it sends a link to a video chat page rather than a native video call for your buddie(s) app. It is pretty decent for a cross platform, cross messenger solution though, much more than non web based solutions and with better video functionality than Skype.
