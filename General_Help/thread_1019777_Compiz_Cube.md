### Post by Wartender on 2008-12-23
ok i'm assuming you have compiz-fusion (don't worry it comes by default with ubuntu) go to system->administration->synaptic package manager, and search compizconfig-settings-manager, download it, enable the desktop cube and rotate cube plugins, set your workspaces to 4(horizontal), press ctrl+alt+left mouse button, and enjoy!
