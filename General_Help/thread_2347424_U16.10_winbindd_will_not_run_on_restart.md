### Post by bobwdn on 2016-12-25
I kept searching and found a similar problem that lead to a [HTML]https://wiki.archlinux.org/index.php/systemd[/HTML] Archlinux (sorry) wiki systemd explanation that explained in detail the workings of systemd. That wiki explanation was a big help how to search and find the issue. In my case resolving a systemd-module-load problem allowed smbd, nmbd, and winbindd to restart on machine restart.
