### Post by Metacarpal on 2007-05-21
Here's a way to change all backgrounds which are white as default (as opposed to a white web page background, for instance):

System menu > Preferences > Theme

Click "Customize..."

Under the Colors tab, change the Window Background color to whatever's easiest on your eyes.
That'll change the OpenOffice background as well.
