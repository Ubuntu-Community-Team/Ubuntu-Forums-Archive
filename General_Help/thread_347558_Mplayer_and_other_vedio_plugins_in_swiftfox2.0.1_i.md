### Post by Lanky Juggler on 2007-02-25
You could always give a little program called Automatix a try.  It basically provides a checklist of commonly needed/requested things and programs (such as Swiftfox, firefox, some standard plugins for them, gaim, etc.).  Once you've selected everything you want, you just hit OK and it finds and install all of the packages for you.  There are two versions (Automatix and Automatix2) and I'm not really sure what the difference is, other than one being several months newer.  I'd assume you just want to go with Automatix2, since it still supports Dapper.
