### Post by pytheas22 on 2008-03-23
I'm a little confused--do you have dual monitors working correctly, except one of them doesn't work while Ubuntu is booting?  Or are you just trying to get dual-monitors working to begin with?  Either way, try using the configuration utility at System>Administration>Screens and Monitors and see if you can get what you need.  If not, copy and paste your /etc/X11/xorg.conf file here, along with information about your system (specifically, what kind of graphics card you are using and what kind of output to your monitors it has), and maybe we can figure it out.

Also, before you screw around with your xorg.conf file, you should back it up just in case:
```

cp /etc/X11/xorg.conf ~xorg.conf.bak
```
