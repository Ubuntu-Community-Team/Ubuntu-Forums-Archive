### Post by jflaker on 2009-02-28
As they are development releases, they are works in progress and may be buggy and may be prone to crashing.

If you are willing to trade stability for being on the bleeding edge, go for it, but remember, you can lose data so make sure that you use it for non-critical things and Be sure to report all bugs.
