### Post by supergrapeman on 2005-02-13
Just trying out one of the latest Hoary livecds (daily builds available at [http://cdimage.ubuntu.com/daily-live/current/](http://cdimage.ubuntu.com/daily-live/current/)), and it is looking good.

I'm typing this on an ibm x40 laptop, and this livecd is one of the very few to pick up all the important bits of hardware (esp. the wireless card, ipw2200).

I'm impressed. 

If you find the warty livecd isn't detecting your hardware, I'd recommend giving the latest Hoary liveCD a whirl.

Other big liveCD's which failed to pick up the wireless hardware on this machine include knoppix 3.7 and simplymepis 2004.06
