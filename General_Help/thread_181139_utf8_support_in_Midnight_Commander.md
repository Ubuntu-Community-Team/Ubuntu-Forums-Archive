### Post by bullgr on 2006-05-24
find it...
just uncomment the last two lines of backport in the file /etc/apt/sources.list
after that run in the terminal
> sudo apt-get update
and after
> sudo apt-get dist-upgrade

after that the midnight commander has utf8 support 8)
