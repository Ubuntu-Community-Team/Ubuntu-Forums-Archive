### Post by m_duck on 2009-02-11
Metacity is for gnome, though I suppose it probably could work in xfce but I've no idea how...

Anyway, to get the xfwm themes to achieve hopefully the same thing:

[LIST]
[*]extract the tar.gz you downloaded from sf
[*]go into the GTK folder and choose one or all of the tar.gz files
[*]extract them (right click -> extract here)
[*]copy the resulting folders into [COLOR=Red]~/.themes[/COLOR]
[*][COLOR=Red][COLOR=Black]go into Settings -> Window Manager (I think) and the new xfwm themes should display[/COLOR][/COLOR]
[/LIST]
Hopefully that should work.
