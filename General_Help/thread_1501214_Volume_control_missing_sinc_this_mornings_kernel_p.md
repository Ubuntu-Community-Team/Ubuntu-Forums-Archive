Update:
For some reason when the system did its kernel update is deleted the "Indicator Applet" that has the volume control, enigmail and bluetooth icons. I did a reinstall of the applet and its associated parts and then the aplet was available for adding to the task bar again.
I had to do a complete removal of atunes including the /home/peter/.atunes directory and then a reinstall of the program. It now works again.
There are still some weird distortion problems with my soundcard but otherwise everything appears to be working again.
