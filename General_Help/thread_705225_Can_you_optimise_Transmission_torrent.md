### Post by pointone on 2008-02-23
The main thing is to seed. Make sure you upload at least as much as you download or your download rate will suffer.

Also, some ISPs are throttling BitTorrent nowadays (legal or illegal, it doesn't matter!) Try using a port > 50,000, and try encrypting your traffic to see if this helps at all.
