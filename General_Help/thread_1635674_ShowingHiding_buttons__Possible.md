### Post by asearle on 2010-12-05
Hi everyone,

I worked this one out:  You need to right-click on the panel and then, under "properties" tick "auto-hide".  Then the panel automatically disappears until you move the mouse pointer to it.

I'm very pleased to have found that and then needed another piece of information:

I will be doing a presentation where I want to run a slide-show and an OpenOffice presentation.  The ideal scenario here would be to have two sessions running and then "hot-key" between them.

This can be done with CTRL/ALT + left/right cursor.

I found a list of keyboard shortcuts here ...

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/KeyboardShortcuts](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/KeyboardShortcuts)

They are extremely useful.
