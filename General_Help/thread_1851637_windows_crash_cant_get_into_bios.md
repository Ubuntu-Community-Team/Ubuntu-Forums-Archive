### Post by Frogs Hair on 2011-09-28
I have the Asus express gate installed with my mobo and I enter the bios from that screen using the delete key . If I miss the key before the screen is gone I have to try again . you could try holding the key down as soon as you press the power button .

The bios battery is a battery located on the mother board . If you have a manual for the board as I do please see that before doing anything . Some boards have a jumper that can be removed and replaced to accomplish the same thing . Check the manual first if you have one .
