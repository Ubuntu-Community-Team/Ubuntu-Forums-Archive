### Post by tm-hart on 2014-04-21
*Single Click Access to Google Drive with Ubuntu*

Facing the loss of Ubuntu One, I decided to try Google Drive on my Ubuntu desktop computer. Google Drive has features similar to Ubuntu One. Sadly however, Google offers drive synch software for Windows and Mac, but not Linux. It was annoying to open Firefox, go to the Google Drive website and log in to make file transfers. My solution is: 

(a) install Google Chrome; 
(b) launch Chrome and go to the Google Drive web page; 
(c) log in;
(d) set the Google Drive page as the Chrome homepage.

When I click on Chrome's desktop icon, the browser goes directly to my Google Drive page - no log-in is needed.   The result is that I am just one click away from access to files in cloud storage.
Firefox remains my browser of choice; Chrome is there to access Google Drive.
