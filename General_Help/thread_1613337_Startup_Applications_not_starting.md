### Post by Jawshie on 2010-11-04
I am running Ubuntu 10.10 on my XPS m1530. I am having a problem getting my startup applications to run. Yesterday, they were starting up just fine until I plugged my laptop into my TV via HDMI, changed the resolution, watched a movie and unplugged. 

Now, I must start nm-applet, dropbox, caffeine, etc manually. Does anybody have any experience solving this problem?

---

### Post by Jawshie on 2010-11-04
Nevermind, I solved it.

On the login page at the bottom is a panel. On it, somehow the "Safe Mode" choice was selected for desktop environment. I changed it back to the regular Ubuntu choice and it works.
