### Post by Ampersand. on 2009-03-24
I assume that you are talking about being able to sync music from your computer to your ipod, correct?

So here is what you do:
1. Figure out where your ipod mounts to and what its name is on your computer. You can do this by going to your desktop and if it is there then just use the name it displays. (eg. if the ipod icon is called 'MyiPod' then that is its name)
2. Open Amarok.
3. Go to the 'Settings' drop menu and then click 'Configure Amarok' at the bottom of this list.
4. Choose the 'Add Device button.
5. The name of the device is the thing we found out in step 1.
6. The mount point of the device should be /media/(the name of the device from above).
7. Click 'OK' then 'OK'
8. Back in regular Amarok screen now click the 'Devices' tab on the bottom far left menu strip thing.
9. There will be a grey square saying 'Media Device Browser' and above that will be a button saying 'iPod (mounted at /media/iPod)' or something similar. Click this button and choose the iPod you just configured.
10. Click the connect button just above that.
11. Drag and drop music then click the transfer button near the top.

Easy!
