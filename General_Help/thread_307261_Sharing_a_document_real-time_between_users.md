### Post by userundefine on 2006-11-26
Thought about using Google Writely?  It's probably the easiest way I know of.  Go to docs.google.com, start a text document, then on the right click Collaborate and "share" the document between friends.  If you've already got something going, you can upload it, too, instead of copying and pasting.
