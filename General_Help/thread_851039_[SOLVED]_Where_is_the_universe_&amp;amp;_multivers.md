### Post by angry_johnnie on 2008-07-06
To enable universe and multiverse, go to **System > Administration > Software Sources**

The first tab refers to Ubuntu Software, and the second tab to Third Party Software.  Check the boxes as you wish.  Leave the updates tab as it is.
