### Post by kabanta on 2007-04-15
The first time I started Open Office in new Feisty install, I tried out some of the options for changing the appearance. I chose: Options -> View -> Icon size and style. When I selected "Crystal" for the icon style, Open Office crashed. Now, whenever I restart Open Office, a) the "recover file?" option appears, and b) whether I select "yes" or "no" Open Office just dies. I have tried re-installing Open Office, but that did not help. Any suggestions (other than completely re-installing Feisty)?

---

### Post by kabanta on 2007-04-16
Note: I "solved" the problem by simply deleting the .openoffice.org2/ directory. Then, OO started up as if for the first time. Obviously, not a general solution for OO crashing when a particular icon style is selected.
