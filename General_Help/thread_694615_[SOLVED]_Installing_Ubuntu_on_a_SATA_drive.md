### Post by zozobra on 2008-02-12
Ubuntu's ability to access the drive will be based on what model your chipset is that acts as the sata controller not the actual sata drives themselves. You may want to check your MB documentation to see what controller it has, then search the forums for information on that controller to see if there's any special configuration needed.
