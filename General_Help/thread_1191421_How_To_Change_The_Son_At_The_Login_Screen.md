### Post by Shinobi3213 on 2009-06-18
I'm new to ubuntu and I was wondering if there was a way to change the song that plays when you get to the login screen. I added a new theme and wanted to add a song that matched it. Any Ideas? Thanks.

---

### Post by steveneddy on 2009-06-18
System --> Preferences --> Sound

Click the "Sounds" tab - go down to Log in and Log out sections and browse for a new sound

### Post by Zalbor on 2009-06-18
For the log in screen, you have to do it from System > Administration > Login Window > Accessibility > Sounds.
