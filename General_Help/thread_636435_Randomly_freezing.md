### Post by 3rdalbum on 2007-12-09
When the computer is working hard, there is greater draw on the power supply, and greater draw on the power supply's cooling system (the fan). I suggest on Ubuntu, right-click the top panel and select "Add To Panel...". Choose the System Monitor applet. When the computer next freezes, check what the graph in the System Monitor applet is showing. If it's showing high load when the system freezes, then the power supply is probably browning out under high load and causing problems.
