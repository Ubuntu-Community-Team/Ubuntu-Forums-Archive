### Post by damien-tt on 2011-02-23
Hi, you can checkout Barnacle (requires your mini to be rooted) or Easy Tether both of which are available on the android app market. Barnacle is free, but i think Easy Tether has a free 10-15 day trial. After that it doesn't allow connections to https sites (like banking, email, etc). Hope this helps. :)
