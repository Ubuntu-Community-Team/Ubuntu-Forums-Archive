### Post by mcduck on 2011-07-06
> **Dean_Grobler said:**
> Awsome, thanks forestpiskie... And doing it this way i'll still keep my current installed programs and files right?

Your files and all settings will stay intact, but of course the CD only contains the programs (and dependencies) that are included on the default Ubuntu install, so some additionally installed programs might have problems. Downloading the required extra packages manually and installing them afterwards should solve that, although if you have lots of extra programs installed it might be easier to figure out a way to get the machine connected to the Internet.
