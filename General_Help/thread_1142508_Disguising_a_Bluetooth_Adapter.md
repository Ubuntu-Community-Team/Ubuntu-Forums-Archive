### Post by 987poiuytrewq on 2009-04-29
Do you mean can you get any sound source to play through the bluetooth adaptor to a headset then the answer is yes. If you want good quality audio, then you need an adaptor and headset that both support A2DP (Advanced Audio Data Protocol or something). You may be able to do the same through a regular headset, but the sound quality will be VERY bad. The adaptor should have it's own soundcard built into it, so you change your soundcard to that one and all sound system-wide will go through the headset. You do not need any other software apart from a bluetooth stack, which come by default in ubuntu and windows xp / vista.
