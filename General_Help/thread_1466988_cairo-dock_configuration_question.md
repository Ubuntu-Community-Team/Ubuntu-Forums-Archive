nevermiiiind...i figured it out. you have to go into each launcher and specifically choose the icon for that particular theme set. so, when creating a terminal launcher, you have to choose the corresponding text-only icon. i was under the assumption that when the theme changed, the icons would change also.

### Post by fabounet on 2010-05-01
