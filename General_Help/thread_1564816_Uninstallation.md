### Post by lisati on 2010-08-31
If possible, I'd advise getting hold of a legal copy of an XP disk suitable for that computer. One advantage of having the original disk that came with the computer or one made from the original recovery partitions is that it will probably have the correct drivers on it.

You need to be careful of disks of the "don't ask too many questions where it came from" variety. As well as the potential for legal hassles, there's an increased chance of something nasty showing up.
