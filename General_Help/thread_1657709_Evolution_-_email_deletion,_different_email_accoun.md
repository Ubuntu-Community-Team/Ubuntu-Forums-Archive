> Can I delete all of X's emails without affecting my inbox in hotmail? (i.e. deleting these emails still leaves the email on the server.) The option to "leave messages on server" has been checked.

Yes, you should be safe enough deleting email from your local machine, but perhaps deleting the unwanted account from Evolution before deleting the mail messages will ensure it can't connect and wipe them from the server.

To be sure try it with a single email that doesn't matter to you. 

> Is there any way to view Y and Z as separate accounts, as in Outlook? Currently, all incoming email is grouped under "Inbox", with nothing to distinguish whether the email is sent to Y or Z
