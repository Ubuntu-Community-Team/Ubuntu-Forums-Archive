### Post by firebadger on 2006-12-05
You can't switch between the two operating systems without shutting one down first unless you use virtualisation.  Nothing to do with how your disks are arranged.
You might want to look at VMWare.  You could install it on your Windows system and run Ubuntu from there.  Or vice versa, but I'm guessing it would be easier for you that way round.
