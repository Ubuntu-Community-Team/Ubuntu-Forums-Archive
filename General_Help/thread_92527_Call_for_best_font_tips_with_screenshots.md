Hi Andras,

try XLinSans font, it's the font of my choice since long ago. On [http://temcat.narod.ru](http://temcat.narod.ru) you'll find a .deb for it and a screenshot of my Ubuntu desktop. You can install it by issuing "sudo dpkg -i dmtr40in-fonts_1.0-1_all.deb" in the console, after which you can choose it in System->Preferences->Fonts and have it on your desktop and in your apps, including Firefox.
