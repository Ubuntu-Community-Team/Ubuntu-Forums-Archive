### Post by The Olde Man on 2011-05-09
I am on Mint8.  Discovered that the error message was caused by the program thinking that the true date was 10 days ahead of the date that shows on the display.  And the incorrect date goes right along, changing every 24 hrs, the true date never catching up.

Short term solution was to set the date 1 yr ahead which then showed on the display.

Long term (?) solution fsck eventually worked.

We shall see how long.
