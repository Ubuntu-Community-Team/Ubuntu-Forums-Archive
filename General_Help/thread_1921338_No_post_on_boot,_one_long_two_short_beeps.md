### Post by xyzzyman on 2012-02-06
One long, two short is pretty universal of video failure. As it is giving that code with the add-on video card removed, yet has an onboard gfx card, then ibod's solution of the RAM becoming unseated is the most likely cause. Between getting moved around and the cold, it doesn't take much to shift a RAM module just enough. A stick can seem to be locked securely in but actually be off. 

Make sure all power has been removed, and press and hold the power button to make sure all power has drained from the mobo. Touching a grounded pipe or sink faucet is good for dissipating any static. And then reseat all the RAM. If you still have issues, systematically repeat by installing just one stick of RAM at a time. That way you can find out if just one stick has gone bad. Unlikely but possible.
