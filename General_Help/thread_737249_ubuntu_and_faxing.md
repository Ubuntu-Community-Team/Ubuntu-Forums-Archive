### Post by someonestolecc on 2008-04-29
It's a bit of an old thread but I don't get on often and people might read it searching...

I came across the thread searching for something and since I don't code and have a chance to contribute to the ubuntu community in that way I thought I'd drop by and offer some of my knowledge instead :)

I work for an unified messaging and internet fax company [http://utbox.net](http://utbox.net) - (this isn't an ad, just my disclosure..  I've been using ubuntu as my primary desktop OS since dapper) :)

The service allows you to send (among other things) PDFs to one of our servers via email. Our server then takes it and delivers it to the fax number you instruct it to. This eliminates any need for client side software, of course we do offer a windows print driver but unfortunately no linux ones... (yet!)

There are other companies around that do this too, my advice is to shop around and pick the best provider based on your needs. UTBox only provides incoming numbers within Australia for the moment but we do have and utilise our international links so if you're only sending/broadcasting faxes you can be anywhere in the world.

Hope that helps the OP or anyone searching :)
