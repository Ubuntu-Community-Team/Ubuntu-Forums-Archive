### Post by navneeth on 2008-11-29
GTK 2.x are themes created using the GNOME ToolKit, for your OS's appearance, in general. It changes the look of the panel, window, buttons and the like. But this does not affect the icons. For that, you need to download icon themes. (All can be changed by going to System->Preferences->Appearance)

GDM themes are solely for your login window.

Compiz/Beryl - fancy-smanshy window managers. I don't (can't) use either, so I haven't really been following their story.
