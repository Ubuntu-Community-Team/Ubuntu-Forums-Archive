### Post by efflandt on 2010-11-07
Any particular reason you prefer gnash instead of Adobe flash?  gnash may not work automatically or properly on all sites.

ubuntu-restricted-extras installs flashplugin-installer which installs Adobe flash.  Having more than one type of flash on your system can also result in issues.  So you may need to do some house cleaning to make sure that you only have one type of flash installed before trying to troubleshoot it.
