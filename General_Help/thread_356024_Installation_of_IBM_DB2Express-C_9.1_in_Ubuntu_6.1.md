### Post by xang on 2007-02-07
I just got finished installing IBM DB2Express-C 9.1 on Edgy. Here are some helpful links - >

Download Link (Requires free registration):

[http://www-128.ibm.com/developerworks/downloads/im/udbexp/?S_TACT=105AGX28&S_CMP=DLMAIN](http://www-128.ibm.com/developerworks/downloads/im/udbexp/?S_TACT=105AGX28&S_CMP=DLMAIN)

Download Mirror (Doesn't require registration):

[http://downloads.zend.com/core-new/2.0.0/](http://downloads.zend.com/core-new/2.0.0/)

Packages are as follows:

-r--   1   260482094  Nov 29 08:55  db2exc_91_LNX_x86.tar.gz
-r--   1   279612218  Nov 29 08:55  db2exc_91_LNX_x86_64.tar.gz
-r--   1   354286434  Nov 29 08:56  db2exc_91_WIN_x86.zip

Naming is pretty self-explanatory.

Here is a great  how-to:

[http://www-128.ibm.com/developerworks/forums/dw_thread.jsp?forum=805&thread=150513&cat=81](http://www-128.ibm.com/developerworks/forums/dw_thread.jsp?forum=805&thread=150513&cat=81)

Once you get it installed and are connected, here is a fantastic tutorial on start/stop, creating tablespaces, creating views, and much more!

[http://www-128.ibm.com/developerworks/edu/dm-dw-db2-hellodb2a.html](http://www-128.ibm.com/developerworks/edu/dm-dw-db2-hellodb2a.html)

It requires free registration, but it is worth it! Even comes with flash animations of the lessons. Enjoy!!
