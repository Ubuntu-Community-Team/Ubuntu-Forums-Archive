### Post by agustin.g on 2007-01-28
Check [http://www.gnome-look.org](http://www.gnome-look.org)  for themes. The selection color will depend on the theme. (Theme installation is pretty simple... just drag and drop the file into the "Theme Preferences" window found in the System --> Preferences menu.

### Post by Rippy on 2007-01-28
Hey, turns out I didn't have to download anything to get what I wanted :P

The IndustrialTango theme is already there, and features the nice shade of blue I wanted. I didn't like the icons, so in "Theme Details" I changed "Window Border" and "Icons" back to Human, and it looks perfect. (The Human border doesn't change the colour, just makes it look a bit glossier)

The result: I have the same icons as before, but they highlight blue when selected, and the drag-selection colour is also blue. Perfect :D
