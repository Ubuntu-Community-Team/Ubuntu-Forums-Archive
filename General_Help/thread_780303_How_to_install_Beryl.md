### Post by enchantedsky on 2008-05-03
To unleash the true power of Compiz-Fusion on your Ubuntu 7.10/8.04 operating system, you must: 

Click Applications ------> Add/Remove program ----> type in "Advanced Desktop Effects Settings" and install.

Then go into your System -----> Preferences ---> Advanced Desktop Effects Settings, and then you can fully configure Compiz-Fusion to do cool desktop effects like the rotating cube.
