### Post by JoelOl75 on 2010-04-14
Some laptops this is software controlled but many have a 'switch' inside the phono plug jack that pysically switches off the internal speakers.  I suppose if you are inclined to do so you may hack into your laptop and add a slider or mini on off button switch to your case to enable/disable this but it is beyond the easy mod to do.
