### Post by 2F4U on 2012-03-17
I would say you need some sort of card reader, either built into the computer or an external reader connected via usb. When you insert the card into the reader it usually mounts just like a usb flash drive.
Other option is to connect your phone with the sd card inserted to your computer and see if it recognizes the phone as external hdd.

### Post by 2F4U on 2012-03-17
Is the card automatically mounted when it is inserted into the card reader? Do you get an icon in the launch bar or on the desktop? If you open your file manager, do you see the card?
If you answered these questions with yes, i.e. if the card is mounted, you should be able to go to the folder where the files are, select them, right click and select "Copy", then go to the card and paste the files.
