### Post by damis648 on 2008-11-02
I have recently installed Ubuntu Intrepid, and found some weird bug in Emerald and the pixmap engine. Whenever I use an emerald theme using the pixmap engine, I get a weird issue with shadows: they are only drawn on the sides and corners, but never on the tops or bottoms. You can tell because the shadow cuts off at the top and bottom. Any ideas please? Thanks!:popcorn:

---

### Post by Anduu on 2008-11-03
I ran into this quite by accident the other night.I was tweaking my emerald theme's settings when I made the shadows do that.

Open the Emerald theme manager and go to "Edit Themes"

Select the pixmap engine and go to the Frame/Shadows tab.Try playing with the radius slider.Whenever I tried to make the radius too large the shadows would get cut off like in your screenshot.

Hope this helps.
