### Post by jazzgossen on 2006-08-15
I've had intermittent problems resuming from hibernate on my laptop. The computer would start, and show a locked screen, asking for my password, but after entering the password I would sometimes just get a black box and have to restart X to get in.

I just found out that gnome-screensaver was the culprit. I uninstalled it (and now I just use xscreensaver - before both screensavers were installed), and now I can safely resume from hibernation. 

Yet another reason to throw gnome-screensaver out (for now).
