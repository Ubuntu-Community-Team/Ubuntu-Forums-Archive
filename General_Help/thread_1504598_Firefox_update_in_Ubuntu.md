### Post by lovinglinux on 2010-06-08
There is nothing wrong with that logo. Is just an unbranded Firefox version. If you want to revert it, you need to disable the ppa you have added and re-install Firefox.

If you want a branded version then you can download it from Mozilla or use Ubuntuzilla. See the [[COLOR="Sienna"]**Installing Other Versions**[/COLOR]](http://firefox-tutorials.blogspot.com/2010/05/installing-other-versions.html) section of [Firefox optimization and troubleshooting thread](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1193567).
