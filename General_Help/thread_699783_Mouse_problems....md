### Post by 1337455 10534 on 2008-02-18
Perhaps hardware issue? (No, not the mouse..).
If the port in the back (or front) of your computer where you insert your mouse is damaged, then it will give you eventual issues.
Only way to test this is to use a USB mouse..
Else; go to synaptic (System-->Admin-->Synaptic) and search (Ctrl+F) for 'mouse'. Reinstall anythhing you find (right click and reinstall).
Try a reboot for good measure.
