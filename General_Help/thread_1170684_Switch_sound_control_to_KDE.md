### Post by qwertyuiop96 on 2009-05-26
I have been having sound problems.  The sound control in KDE does not affect the sound.  After a while, I realized that that was because the GNOME control was muted.  After that, I realized that the Gnome control had complete control of the sound.  In other words, to change the sound I had to launch the GNOME sound mixer as opposed to just using the controls on the keyboard as I can in GNOME.  I primarily use KDE now, so this is a pain.  Any ideas on how I can give control to KDE?

---

### Post by pro003 on 2009-05-26
Right click on the icon (speaker) down right at the bottom of your screen and select "select master channel".

See what works for you. One must.
