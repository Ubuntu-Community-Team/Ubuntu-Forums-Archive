### Post by ubuntu27 on 2009-04-20
1) Go to *System* menu/*Preferences*/*About Me*

A new windows will appear, 
2) click on the Square that is located on the left-up corner. Select your image and click on Open.

Now, you have selected an avatar.



What you need to do next is to select a GDM Themes that uses Avatar logins.

You can find them here:

[http://www.gnome-look.org/index.php?xcontentmode=150](http://www.gnome-look.org/index.php?xcontentmode=150)

1) After you have download an GDM theme, 
go to System menu/Administration/Login Windows

2) At the new window, click on the *"Local"* tab. 
Click on Add, and select the theme that you have downloaded.



Enjoy!
