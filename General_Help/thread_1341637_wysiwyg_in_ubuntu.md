### Post by fluffman86 on 2009-11-29
I like Kompozer.  It's in the repos, but if you can't find it you may need to go to System > Admin > Software Sources > check the top 4 boxes on the first tab.  Then reload and search again.

Also search for Amaya.  It's not in the repos, and should be available from w3c.com but I'm not sure if it's for linux.

edit: I've run tightvnc in wine.  That's nice sometimes for testing.  Um...I hear WoW runs in Wine.  DVDShrink.  Amazon Kindle for PC.  I could go on...

So to answer your question: not usually anything useful, and least for the person who actually *WANTS* wine to work. :P
