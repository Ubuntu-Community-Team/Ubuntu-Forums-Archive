### Post by pavarinboat on 2018-06-04
Zoom can be enabled in System Settings > Universal Access > Zoom, or use the following keyboard shortcuts:
To toggle zoom on or off: Super Alt 8
To zoom in: Super Alt +
To zoom out: Super Alt -
