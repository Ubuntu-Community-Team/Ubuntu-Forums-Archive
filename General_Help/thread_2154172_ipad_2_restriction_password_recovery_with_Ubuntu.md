### Post by Noah0504 on 2013-06-13
I don't believe you'll be able to access anything on the locked iPad from Linux or any OS for that matter.

I did a quick search to see if I could find anything for you, but all I really came up with was a few links involving already having a backup in iTunes on Mac OS X or Windows.  You might just have to bite the bullet and use iTunes (on another computer, of course) to restore the iPad.

If you happen to have a backup of the iPad on a computer, I did find this link:
