### Post by Zoot7 on 2010-01-07
Generally the thing to watch out for with Laptops if you want to stick Linux on them are to have nvidia graphics and either Intel or Atheros wireless as they're the most troublesome components.
I'd be a little leary about the Realtek wireless, I'd be shopping for one with an Intel card.
