### Post by jesuisbenjamin on 2010-06-22
Calibre can read this.

You can download this program via Applications > Add/Remove, and search for Calibre.

For future reference, you could have looked for "lit" in the search engine of Add/Remove and found Calibre. It's a handy tool.

Good luck!
B.
