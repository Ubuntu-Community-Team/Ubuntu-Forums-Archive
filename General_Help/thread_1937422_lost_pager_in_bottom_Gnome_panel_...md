### Post by jwbrase on 2012-03-07
First of all, can you switch to a minimized app with alt-tab?

Secondly, what version of Ubuntu are you using? When you right click on the panel, do you get a menu with options like "Add to Panel", "Properties", "Delete this Panel", etc, or do you get no menu at all?

If you get a menu, try clicking on "Add to Panel", then scroll down to find "Window list" in the dialog box that opens up. 

If you don't get a menu, try holding down alt while you right-click on the panel.
