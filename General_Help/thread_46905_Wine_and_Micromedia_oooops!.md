### Post by apollo2011 on 2005-07-07
Yeah, sometimes you get lucky and you get the programs to work under WINE and sometimes you don't.  WINE's attempting to convert **everything** that Windows does into the Linux equivalent, and since Windows isn't open source, WINE's best way of getting it to work is to take somewhat random stabs in the dark.  Eventually, hopefully, everything will work but that probably isn't for years.

You could probably still use Windows for stuff like Dreamweaver and use Linux for everything else.
