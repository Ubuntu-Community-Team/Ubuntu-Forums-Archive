### Post by Revolutionary101 on 2010-06-14
This may sound like an idiotic question but did you empty your trash bin?

If you did empty your trash go to Applications>Accessories>Disk Usage Analyzer. Then once you open that up scan your HDD to see what is taking up all that space and delete whatever it is.
