### Post by uroskriznar on 2008-08-19
David Contreras Ros <davcr@ugr.es> sent me the solution:

    Go to System -> Preferences -> Removable drives and media

    and change the command line for the Digital Camera to /usr/bin/gthumb

    You must have gthumb installed, of course.
