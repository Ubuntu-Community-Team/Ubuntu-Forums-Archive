You guys got me thinking so I did some googling around and from what I can gather there is NO way to do this.The workings of the recent documents menu apparently are hard coded into Gnome.

The Gnome developers seem to be lagging way behind the times when it comes to this.

I did find that recently used entries are stored in the ~/.recently-used.xbel file in your home dir and it keeps far more than just the 10 entries shown in the recent documents menu.Perhaps an ambitious coder could develop a panel applet or some such thing that could parse this file and give us a usable alternative ;)
