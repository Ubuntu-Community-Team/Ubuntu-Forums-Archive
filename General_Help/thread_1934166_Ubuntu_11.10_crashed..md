### Post by Paddy Landau on 2012-03-02
> **March1st said:**
> Could the new mouse be causing the problems?
The easiest way to find out is to plug in your new mouse again. Do you get the problem again? If so, that's the problem!

> **March1st said:**
> Or would it be because I had 2 mice loaded at the same time?
I believe that, at the moment, Linux cannot cope with more than one mouse. I recently read an article proposing that multiple pointers be supported. Ensure that you have only one mouse plugged in at any time.
