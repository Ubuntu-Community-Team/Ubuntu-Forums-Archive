### Post by mivo on 2007-09-22
The Xpress 1270 HM is actually fairly good as far as integrated graphics systems go. But I have not seen one in combination with Linux, so I can't comment on their suitability (same for the Intel one). The Xpress 1270 also comes in different configurations -- does it have only shared memory, or also dedicated memory? (The maximum is 256 MB dedicated and 256 MB shared memory, I believe.) Generally speaking, my experiences with integrated ATI cards are better than with Intel, but again, that is just a general observation and may not apply to the two options in question. :)

As for the CPU ... I've always only had Intel CPUs, but I believe my next notebook will have an AMD chip. Reason? More bang for the buck, and I rarely see complaints about them. I'll leave that one for someone with more technical insight into CPUs.
