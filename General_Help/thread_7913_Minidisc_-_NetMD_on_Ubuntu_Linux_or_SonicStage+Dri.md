### Post by Jonam on 2007-02-16
I managed to install Qemu, load Win98SE under Qemu and then SonicStage 3.1. All worked fine until I plugged in my HI-MD minidisc and connected USB to Qemu via monitor commands. Win98 happily connected the minidisc as USB Mass Storage but once SonicStage was started, Qemu slowed down so much it was unusable. I could see that SonicStage was communicating with the minidisc player but that was about all. Apparently Qemu does not support streaming USB well and I assume this is what the minidisc player is doing so Qemu slows right down.
