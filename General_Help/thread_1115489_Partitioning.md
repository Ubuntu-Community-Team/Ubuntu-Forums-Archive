### Post by 3Miro on 2009-04-03
gparted is the one. Look it up in Add/Remove.

A piece of advice. Before messing with partitions: BACK UP EVERYTHING IMPORTANT ON YOUR SYSTEM. If you only have little experience with partitions: BACKUP EVERYTHING IN TWO COPIES. (OK the last maybe an overkill, but still)

I have done a lot of rapartitioning on my system and I have never lost anything, but I am sure that if I did not had backups, something would have gone wrong.
