### Post by Smart Viking on 2010-03-27
I am a ubuntu noob, but i think i might help you on this one due to just doing something related to emapthy.

Go to home folder, the press "view" and press "show hidden files", then you go inside the folder ".local", then "share" and so "Empathy", there is "logs" of your conversations as .log files :-)
