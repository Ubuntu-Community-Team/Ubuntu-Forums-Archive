### Post by techunit on 2010-05-02
Ok well I am now among the crowd of people who Dual Boot There Systems.

I am posting this thread to give and get tips on what works best to keep everything synced and working great.

My Tips: 

1. Get Dropbox: With dropbox on both your installations and your files will be synced and backed-up over the internet to a secure source. The free version gives you 2GBs of free space and more as you refer people. I will link you to the website where you can install program not a referral (as I am unsure about the rules regarding such links in threads) Here is a plain link to the website... [www.dropbox.com]("http://www.dropbox.com")



2. Get Weave Sync on Firefox on both your installations. You can get it by going to "Tools>Add-ons>Search>Type In Weave>Hit Enter>Add" in Firefox. This will keep all your bookmarks, cookies, and passwords synced between your installations and other computers.

Have any more Tips to that work great for dual-booters post them below?
