### Post by Bucky Ball on 2017-10-09
> **Nickolai_Leschov said:**
> Does 17.10 work better than 16.04 on tablets?

No. If your machine came out five minutes ago and was very new, perhaps yes, but with a tablet released in 2013, no. 

In fact, you might find the opposite. 16.04 LTS is a long-term support release (five years support) and very stable. 17.10 is supported for nine months and is not released until the end of this month. Unknown what issues may arise with a 'wet behind the ears' release that is as yet not 'official' and until its release you would be 'testing'. 

Hope that helps and good luck.
