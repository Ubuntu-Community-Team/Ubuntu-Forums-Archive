### Post by ajgreeny on 2012-08-30
I'm not suggesting this as an answer, but can you use **Alt Gr+Print Screen+K** to kill whatever xsession is running, then login as a new user?

Also when the machine freezes on you try holding **Alt Gr+Print Screen **and slowly type** RIESUB **with a second or two between letters.  That will give you a safe, soft shutdown or restart, much better than the reset button or main power switch.
