### Post by pricetech on 2011-06-24
"best" is entirely a personal choice, as mentioned above.

Ubuntu tends to be more user friendly to a new user, in my opinion.

I would advise sticking with the LTS release (currently 10.04.2 Lucid Lynx) and installing the desktop for ease of use.  You can add whatever servers you want to the desktop.

Once you're comfortable, don't be afraid to experiment with whatever you want.  Just don't set up a mission critical server and crash it with your experiments.

Good Luck, Have Fun, and Welcome to the Forums.
