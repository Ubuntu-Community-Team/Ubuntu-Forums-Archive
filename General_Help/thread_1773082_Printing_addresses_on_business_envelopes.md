I just went thru this today believe it or not. Your not going to believe this but here you go.

In Openoffice:

1. Select insert / envelope.
2. Fill in your to and from spots.
3. Select the envelope size that best fits yours.
4. Click on insert.
5. Drag and drop your to and from fields to there you want them.
    -  click on the edge to highlight the box around it and then drag it to where you want it.

My only problem is that my return address's first few columns dont print. Just have to fine tune.

When printing the envelope select print and then tick selection (page one).
