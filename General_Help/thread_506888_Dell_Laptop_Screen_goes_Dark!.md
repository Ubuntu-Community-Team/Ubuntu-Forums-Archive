### Post by ghandi69_ on 2007-07-23
A friend of mine had gotten and exensive new fancy pancy HP laptop about two years ago that had the same issue, where it looked like the screen was off, but it was really dark and the back light was just off.

For him, it was a hardware failure, he sent it in and got it fixed, they sent it back, then 9 months later it broke and did the same thing, this time, right after the warrenty was finished.

It probably means one of a few things.

1.) - Video Card issue, where it tells the backlight to turn off and on, whatever controls that is screwing up.

2.) Connection between monitor and video card is screwy, Video Card knows backlight is supposed to be on, but the signal is not getting through.

3.) Backlight on monitor is actually going out.  The proper signals are getting there, but the monitor is just going bad.

-- This is just based on my experience with the same issue, if someone else knows of a software related issue that would cause this, then let it be known.
