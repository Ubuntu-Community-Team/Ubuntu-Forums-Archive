### Post by gldearman on 2010-09-03
The Gimp can do this. 

Of course, Gimp is not a small app, but you've probably got it installed already, so this won't take up any extra space.

Use the color picker tool (the eyedropper) and choose the color you want from the image.  Then, in the Toolbox, click the foreground color.  That will open a window that shows you (among other info) the RGB and hex values for that color.

Hope this helps!
