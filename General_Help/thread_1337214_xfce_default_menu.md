### Post by bodhi.zazen on 2009-11-26
Simply manually mount the share or if you wish put an entry in fstab

mount //ip_address/share /mount_point -o user=user_name

There are a number of locations for fstab entries, one is here

[How to fstab - Ubuntu Forums]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?&t=283131")
