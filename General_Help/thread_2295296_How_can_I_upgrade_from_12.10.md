### Post by Bucky Ball on 2015-09-17
12.04 LTS is not retired. It is supported until 2017. The PPAs are perfectly fine. 12.10, forget it. That is end-of-life and if you're trying to upgrade to that, that is the issue. 

You need to go to Software and Sources> Updates and set the notification to notify you of LTS releases only, then the machine will do an update when you close and will notify you that 14.04 LTS is available. Simply click a couple of buttons when required. Before the upgrade, switch off any PPAs you have added manually that are not official Ubuntu PPAs.

You can't update to 12.10, that is probably your issue. No longer supported.
