### Post by carney1979 on 2006-05-14
I don't know about earlier XFCE versions, but with the version in Dapper (4.3.90.2), if you're using  thunar as your file manager then you can add a Custom Action (like Nautilus's Actions, but easier to setup) that will move your files to your ~/.Trash folder. 

Of course you'll have no graphical trashcan, but it gives you a second chance to decide it you REALLY wanted to actually delete that background of your granddaughter, or what have you...
