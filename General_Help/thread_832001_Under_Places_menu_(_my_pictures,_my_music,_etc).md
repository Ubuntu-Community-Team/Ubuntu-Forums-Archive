### Post by sdennie on 2008-06-17
You should be able to do this in nautilus by going to Bookmarks->Add Bookmark.  Alternatively, you can simply drag a folder to the bottom left part of nautilus and it will become a bookmark.  Both of these changes will cause things to appear in the Places menu.
