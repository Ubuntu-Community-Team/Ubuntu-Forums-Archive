### Post by orb9220 on 2006-09-05
That is usally a problem with the specific player. Try different program to play it back on.

1) play file on orig computer hd.
2) play avi on orig computer from cd.
3) play avi on new computer from cd.
4) copy avi to new computer and play with different programs.

If 1 and 2 are ok then it is the playback software on new computer.
