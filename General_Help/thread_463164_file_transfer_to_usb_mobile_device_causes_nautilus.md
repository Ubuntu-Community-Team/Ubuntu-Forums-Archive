### Post by fanatik on 2007-06-04
hi,

i also have a sony ericsson w810i. and my pc only has usb 1.1 ports. i have noticed that when transferring large amounts of data to my phone it can take a long time - 20 mins or more for approx 200-300Mb. while this is happening the cpu is reporting 100% and the load average is very high. The CPU being 100% is due to I/O wait. I think this is purely to do with my USB ports being 1.1. I don't use Naultilus to do the transfer, I just use the command line.

I don't think there is anything you can do about it. I just accept it as normal now, and try not to do anything while the files are copying.
