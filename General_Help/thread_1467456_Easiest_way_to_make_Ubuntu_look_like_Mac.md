### Post by Solarisphere on 2010-04-30
If you just want the dock there is Cairo, Docky, and AWN. I use Docky and it works great, but AWN should work just as well. I believe Cairo is less mature than either, but I could be wrong. 

However, if you want an all around Mac-ish look to your computer try installing elemntary desktop. It includes many UI improvements (like Docky) and tweaks and borrows many ideas from OS X.

[http://www.omgubuntu.co.uk/2009/12/elementary-theme-gets-ppa.html](http://www.omgubuntu.co.uk/2009/12/elementary-theme-gets-ppa.html)
