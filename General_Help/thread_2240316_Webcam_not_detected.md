### Post by obake2 on 2014-08-20
You mention built-in webcam, so I guess it means that you own a laptop.

Some laptops has the ability to turn on and off the webcam with a hardware switch or buttons.

Check to see if there is any "camera" icon on your keyboard (usually around the F keys).
The webcam on my System76 laptop for example can be enabled or disabled with fn + F10 key


Press the camera key and re-launch Cheese.


Good luck
