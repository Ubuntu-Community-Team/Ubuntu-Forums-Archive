### Post by AlphaLexman on 2010-07-21
A hard drive may have one or more 'partitions' and each partition is 'formatted.'
You can format a single partition or you could completely remove the partition, even with data still on the partition. THIS WILL DELETE ALL YOUR DATA AND FILES!! Please use caution, and make backups of all your important data.
