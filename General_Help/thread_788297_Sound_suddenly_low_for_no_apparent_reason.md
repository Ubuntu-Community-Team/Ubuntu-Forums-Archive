### Post by ajgreeny on 2008-05-09
Double click on the volume control on the panel and make sure that PCM is showing and is high enough.  If it isn't showing, add it by going to Edit>>Preferences and enabling it there.  Hope that helps, but if not, sorry to waste your time.
