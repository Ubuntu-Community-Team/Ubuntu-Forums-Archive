### Post by accuser on 2005-04-11
Check your video playback settings. From desktop, select menu System -> Preferences -> Multimedia Systems Selector. Click the Video tab, and test the default sink. You should see a window with a test card appear. If this doesn't work, you will need to change your output method and re-test.

Enjoy!
