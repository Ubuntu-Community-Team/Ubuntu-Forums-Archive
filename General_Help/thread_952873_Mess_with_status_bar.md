### Post by Shazaam on 2008-10-19
Right-click the icon(s) and uncheck "Lock to panel", then click "Move".
As an aside, I have noticed that if you have the panel set up to hide (with the "Show/Hide buttons), every time you hide/unhide a panel the icons get re-arranged. :)
