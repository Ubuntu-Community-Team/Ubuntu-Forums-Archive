### Post by jamesisin on 2010-01-04
Open gconf-editor.  Look under apps --> panel --> top levels.  If you have no top panel there, you can copy the one under panel --> default setup --> top levels.

Alternatively you can always create your own panel by choosing New Panel from the right-click menu on an empty spot of any panel.
