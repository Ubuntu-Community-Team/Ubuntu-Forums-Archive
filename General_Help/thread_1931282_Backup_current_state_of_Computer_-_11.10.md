### Post by ajgreeny on 2012-02-25
Probably **clonezilla** would be one of the easiest ways to have a backup of absolutely everything.

It does what it says on the tin; ie, makes a clone of a whole disk or partition, and then allows you to restore that clone to another disk or partition.
