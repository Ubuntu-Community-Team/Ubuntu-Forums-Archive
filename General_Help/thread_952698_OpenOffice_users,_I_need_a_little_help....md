### Post by Tamlynmac on 2008-10-19
Open:
OpenOffice.org Writer

Select:
Tools/customize/keyboard tab

Under short cut keys - select F11 (or any other key) and modify as required.

One thing to check if no keys show up in shortcut keys is that Writer is selected and not Openoffice.org in the top right corner.

Hope this is what your looking for.
