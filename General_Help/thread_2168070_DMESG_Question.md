### Post by Toz on 2013-08-16
By default, dmesg timestamps are in the format "seconds:microseconds since boot". This format can be changed - see the dmesg manpage or: [http://manpages.ubuntu.com/manpages/raring/en/man1/dmesg.1.html]("http://manpages.ubuntu.com/manpages/raring/en/man1/dmesg.1.html").
