### Post by darolu on 2010-03-25
Yes it is the exact same type of RAM DDR @ 333Mhz; you can try a higher frequency memory (400Mhz) to get better results too.
I also read (in the first link) that by default, your computer has 1 slot available "Total Slots: 2 DDR Slots | Available Slots: 1 DDR Slots"; you may try switching your current module to that slot first, although less common, it's also possible that the memory slot is the failing one.
