I am wondering if there is anyway of logging onto Ubuntu and not being connected to my wifi?  If my adapter is connected I'm always on wifi once I boot up or if I put the wifi adapter in it automatically signs me in!  :confused:

---

### Post by Melnik_Hoogland on 2015-11-26
Click on the wifi symbol on the top right of your screen, click "Edit Connections...", click your network's name, click "Edit...", click on the "General" tab, and uncheck "Automatically connect to this network when it is available" and click on "Save" in the bottom right. Now ubuntu won't automatically connect you to that wifi network.
