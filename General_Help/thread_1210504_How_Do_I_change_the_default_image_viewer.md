### Post by credobyte on 2009-07-11
Click on your image ( eg. jpeg format image ) and browse to [COLOR=DimGray]Properties / Open With[/COLOR] ( there you can change your "default" image viewer ) ;)

### Post by nice_like_rice on 2009-07-11
One method: right click and "open with", then select the application you want, double click on it.

(if I remember correctly this is slightly confusing, you must select it from the "open with" list, NOT from the little list that comes up when you right click)

### Post by nr0mx on 2009-07-11
One way to do it...
Find an image, right-click and select Open With > Open With Other Application ... and choose from the list. If the app you want to use isn't in the list, then you can also provide a custom command.
