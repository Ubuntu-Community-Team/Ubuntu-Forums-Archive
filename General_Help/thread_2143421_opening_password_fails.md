### Post by Lightning Dragon on 2013-05-09
Hi,

Your password for Ubuntu, or BIOS? For the BIOS, you can turn your computer off, flip switch, press button to discharge computer (and make sure you are grounded), unplug and then disconnect the battery on the motherboard for about fifteen minutes. Put it back in and the password will be gone. For Ubuntu, hold/press SHIFT key on boot up to get into recovery mode. Drop down into Root Shell Prompt and then follow these instructions;

[http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/resetpassword](http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/resetpassword)
