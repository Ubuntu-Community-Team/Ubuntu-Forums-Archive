### Post by jamesisin on 2010-02-08
I used k9copy.  It's not very intuitive and I'm not sure it lives up to its own claims.  It wasn't successful at creating another smaller ISO (it says it did but there is no such file I could locate).  It created an AVI without video (700MB of super-lame coming in at about 52 seconds???).

However, in the end I was able to burn the 7GB ISO onto a 4.7GB DVD using k9copy.  Good enough for government work.

(For anyone following behind, be careful with k9copy as single-clicks open folders and run dialogs.)
