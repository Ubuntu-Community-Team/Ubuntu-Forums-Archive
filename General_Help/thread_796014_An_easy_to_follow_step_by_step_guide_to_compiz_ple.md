### Post by aroch1 on 2008-05-16
I can't help you with the ATI-specific issues, as I have Intel graphics, but I can tell you how to enable desktop effects in general.  Open the Appearance Dialog System->Preferences->Appearance.  In the visual effects tab, you should be able to choose your desktop effects level.  These levels are preconfigured by the developers.  If you want more control over the visual effects you have running (say you want a cube), use apt-get or aptitude or synaptic to install compizconfig-settings-manager, and you can have complete control over the desktop effects you have running.

Hope this helps!
