### Post by cdaringe on 2008-02-02
heres what you guys can do to help. when you are ready to shutdown, just log out
then do a ctrl alt f2 or f3 (you have terminals up to f6, f7 is your graphical display) and login at the terminal that shows up.

then do a 

```
sudo shutdown now
```

hopefully, it will output some error right as it hangs, and well have some more info on whats goin on. post back, keep us updated
