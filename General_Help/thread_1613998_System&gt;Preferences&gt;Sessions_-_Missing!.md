### Post by sikander3786 on 2010-11-05
System > Administraion > Login Screen lists available sessions.

At GDM Login Screen, once you click your username, there is a menu in the bottom of screen named Session which allows you to switch between GNOME, Failsafe GNOME, Xterm etc.

Ctrl + Alt + F1 is another option to bring up the tty.
