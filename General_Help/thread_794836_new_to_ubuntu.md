### Post by igfud on 2008-05-15
Are you having problems getting the card to work in Ubuntu?  If so, take a look at this site: [http://stevezemanek.com/articles/buffalo.html](http://stevezemanek.com/articles/buffalo.html)

You can get to the package manager mentioned by going to System > Administration > Synaptic Package Manager and finding the mentioned package.  Click on it, mark it for installation, and click apply.  Of course, you will need Internet access to do this.  If you can temporarily access the Internet by hardwiring it to the router, this would probably be the easiest solution.
