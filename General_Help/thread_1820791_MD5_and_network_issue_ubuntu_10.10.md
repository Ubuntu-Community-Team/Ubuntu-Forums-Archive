### Post by sikander3786 on 2011-08-08
If the MD5SUMs don't match, it mean the ISO you've downloaded got corrupted on the way to your PC. In that case, you need to download again. Using a Bit Torrent client automatically checks the hashes and reduces the chances of corruption, available at the alternate downloads page I believe.

If the ISO image itself was corrupt, the disc is supposed to be corrupt and behave abnormally ;)
