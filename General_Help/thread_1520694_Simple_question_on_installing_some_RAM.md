### Post by cariboo on 2010-06-29
The first thing to do is make sure the ram will fit in the slot, the second thing to do is to make sure the ram is the correct speed. For the last 6 or 7 years, almost all ram will have a label on it telling you the speed. Look for something like pc-2700, or similar. This of course depends on how new the system is.
