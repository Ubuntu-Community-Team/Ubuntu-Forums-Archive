### Post by wqawasmi on 2010-03-21
All you have to do is:

Open up a terminal.

File, New Profile.

Click the backround tab. 

Choose transparent backround, and set the transparency level to your liking. 

Save the profile. Do the same for another profile but with the transparency set to none. 

When you go full screen, right click the terminal, choose profiles, and choose the full screen one you set. When you go back to normal just choose the original transparent profile :)
