### Post by oldrocker99 on 2019-03-28
You don't need the Advanced Options. Just hit Enter at the menu and you'll be set. That text was not error messages, but the usual bootup procedure. If there was an error, it's usually a piece of hardware that wasn't found, or something like that. Nothing to worry about. If you're worried about not shutting your PC down, choose Suspend, and the system goes into standby mode, using little power.
