### Post by kerry_s on 2007-11-10
hmm, you can turn off checking on those drives/partions in your fstab.

sudo gedit /etc/fstab
find the drives and change the last # on the line to **0**

or

you can just turn all the checking off

sudo gedit /etc/default/rcS
FSCKFIX=yes
