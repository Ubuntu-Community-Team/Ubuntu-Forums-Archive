### Post by Ng Oon-Ee on 2008-11-10
If your only concern is web-browsing, and you're using Firefox, why not just install [Stealther]("https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/1306"), [Torbutton]("https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/2275"), or some of the others in the host of [privacy and security addons]("https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/browse/type:1/cat:12") it has.

In Ubuntu 8.10 I believe there's a new 'guest login' feature as well, which would allow you to browse without the data being saved for your next login, I think. But that wouldn't help with IP-hiding and the like.
