### Post by wak_wak on 2008-06-27
OK.  I had a similarly odd experience and I doubt my solution will help but here goes...

I installed 8.04 on a BRAND NEW shiny XPS M1530 with a 200gb 7200 RPM drive, 4gb of memory, 256mb video card and a SUPER new and "great" Intel wireless card.

While my wife's Inspiron with the same (but slightly older) Intel wireless card was getting an ave of 4mb down I was getting 9.5 kbs to 1 mbs MAXIMUM.

So anyway, in my infinite wisdom (and while she wasn't looking) I stole her wireless card and put it in my XPS and it worked GREAT.  I now average 3.5-4.5 mbs!  

So I put my wireless card back in her Vista box and it worked great.

So I have no idea why but that worked really well.

The point being, if you have another wireless card around you may want to try swapping them just to see what happens.
