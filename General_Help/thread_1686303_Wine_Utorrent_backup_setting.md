### Post by DanneStrat on 2011-02-12
Hi.

Actually there is a way. Do this:

Go to your home folder. Press ctrl+h 

to show hidden files and folders. 

Look for a folder called ".wine". 

In there you will find your wine "C-drive". 

Backup your "Application Data/utorrent" 

or "AppData/utorrent" folder

(Don't know exactly what it's called in wine).

Thats it! Now when you format your hdd and install utorrent again you can 

just put the old files back in place to regain your settings.

Good luck!
