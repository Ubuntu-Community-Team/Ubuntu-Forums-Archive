### Post by linuxuser12345 on 2011-07-29
What version of Ubuntu are you running?

If you are using 11.04, click the upper right-hand corner of your screen, and scroll down to "System Settings." Now look for "Preferred Applications" under the "Personal" section. Change your defaults as desired.
