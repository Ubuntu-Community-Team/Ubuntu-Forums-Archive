### Post by hardyn on 2008-04-30
There has been some reports of hard locks and kernel panics within hardy...  there is also a growing list of similar reports on the launch-pad.  I suggest you consider reporting this bug. but the are at a point where the patterns are a little blured, and you must be very specific about your hardware configuration.  read the lauch pad note about posting effective bug reports. include your log files. The direction that report has taken; the chipsets for your LAN, WLAN, Video and if you are using the Nvidia binary driver.  Also, what you were doing when it crashed; downloading, graphics intensive, screen saver, etc.

[https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/linux/+bug/204996](https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/linux/+bug/204996)
