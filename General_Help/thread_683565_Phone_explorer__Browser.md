### Post by RobJN on 2008-07-12
Unfortunately Wammu is far behind My Phone Explorer (MPE), in terms of both ease and functionality.  MPE can export great looking files of your phones sms messages to open office, html, excel and word.

A google search finds a group of people working on MPE using wine. Give that a try.
