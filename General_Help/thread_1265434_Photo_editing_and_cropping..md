### Post by beastrace91 on 2009-09-13
Gimp comes preinstalled on Ubuntu, it is basically a photo shop replacement (and a damn good one at that). It does have a basic cropping feature I use quiet often though. Right click on your image file and select "open with gimp" 

Once it opens in Gimp, grab the "selector" tool (Very first icon in the toolbox, upper left hand corner.) Once you have the section highlighted you want to crop to select Image->Crop to Selected from the menu at the top.

Hope this helps some,
~Jeff
