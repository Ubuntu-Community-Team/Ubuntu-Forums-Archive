### Post by jerome1232 on 2008-12-17
k9copy can rip and recreate a burnable image file with zero loss in quality.

dvdrip can rip and then transcode the movie to many formats. Then you could use some video editing software (avidemux for example) to reorder things to your likeing, and use De-Ve-De to recreate burnable dvd images. There will be some loss in quality since your transcoding but it shouldn't be noticeable if you keep everything at high qualities.

All of this software is avaible in Ubuntu's repos (synaptic) you'll have to give them a spin yourself to see if they do what you want.
