### Post by asmoore82 on 2011-03-05
There already is a "launch browser" shortcut. For me it's 4th down under the "Desktop" section.

At any rate, when you click "Add" to create a completely custom shortcut,
The "Name" field is asking what to call it,
The "Command" field needs the actual text command to run.
After you complete all of that, it will become like one of the built-ins
and you get to set a keyboard shortcut for it the normal way.

You do have to make sure you click in the "Shortcut" column to edit the keys,
Clicking in the "Action" column on a Custom Shortcut re-edits the Name/Command.
