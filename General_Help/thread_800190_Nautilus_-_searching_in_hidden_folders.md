How do I motivate Nautilus to search for files even in .hidden folders? (Display of hidden files is enabled.) By default this does not appear to be working.

---

### Post by ryanhaigh on 2008-05-20
On gutsy you can go to system>places>search for files (or something similarly named) which will give you the option of searching hidden directories.

---

### Post by N.N. on 2008-05-20
In the search dialogue, which you can access from "Places" > "Search for files", click "Select more options", select "Show hidden files and backups" from the drop-down menu of available options, and click "Add".
