### Post by Perfect Storm on 2006-05-27
Right click on the file you want mplayer to and click properties. Go to "Open with" tab and pick Mplayer. Now all file of this type will be open by Mplayer.

If Mplayer isn't in the list press "add"  and click "Use a custom command", there you write **gmplayer**. Now you can choose mplayer.
