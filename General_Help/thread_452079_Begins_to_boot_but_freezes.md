### Post by redwheelbarrow on 2007-05-23
What MotherBoard/Bios is it? 
I find it hard to believe that a p2 can't boot from a cd drive. Jump into bios at the post (del, or F2, less frequently tab or Esc )Y ou might have to alt-P to check options on other pages, or in sub menus. Dig around the bios a bit, I bet it's there.

If the bios itself is locked look around the motherboard for a reset jumper (normally has a jumper table/title next to it li). Close the jumper, power on, power off, return the jumper to the old config.


We can do this, it should be easy. Good cause btw.
