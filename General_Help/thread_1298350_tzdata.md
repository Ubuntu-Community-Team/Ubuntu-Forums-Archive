### Post by markbuntu on 2009-10-22
tzdata is usually changed for a reason. Countries actually do change their time zones  and tzdata needs to be updated every time some country decides to change the day that daylight savings time starts or ends or when one province or another's time zone is changed. With almost 200 countries stetched across 24 time zones this happens often. The most recent was Argentina I think changing DST with one days notice.
