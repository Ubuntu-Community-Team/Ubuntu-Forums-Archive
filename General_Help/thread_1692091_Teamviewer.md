### Post by CHRSB on 2011-02-20
Yes, I use it to connect to both Ubuntu and Windows 7 boxes. It is the best remote connection app on the market to me. Simple for the newbie user on the other end.

[http://www.teamviewer.com/en/download/index.aspx](http://www.teamviewer.com/en/download/index.aspx)

Click on the Penguin to download the Ubuntu .deb file and just click it and it will install with the Software Center app.
