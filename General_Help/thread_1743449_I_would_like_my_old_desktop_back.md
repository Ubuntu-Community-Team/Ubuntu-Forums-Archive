### Post by cymbaline42 on 2011-04-29
When the login screen appears with your username, click on your name.  **Now**, you will see an option at the bottom right of the screen that says 'Session'.  Click on that, and you will see a bunch of options.  Click 'Classic' and login, now you will have your old desktop.
