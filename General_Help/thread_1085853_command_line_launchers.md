### Post by InspiredIndividual on 2009-03-03
In order to create a launcher on your desktop, right-click and choose "Create launcher". If you want your application to open in a terminal (or you want to execute a script), choose "Application from terminal" in the "Type" drop down menu. Enter the terminal command to run your application in the "Command" box.

To create a launcher on the panel, right-click the panel and choose "Add to panel". Now, choose "Custom Application Launcher" and enter the specifications of your launcher as above.
