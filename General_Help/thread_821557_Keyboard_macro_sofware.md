### Post by diaa on 2008-06-07
I assume you mean a text replacement application(that helps you while writing text), in this case [take a look at snippits]("http://lifehacker.com/351285/automate-repetitive-typing-with-snippits")

In case you want a macro program that can automate keystrokes, not only text but other keys such as arrows and even the mouse(like Windows AutoHotkey), [take a look at xmacro]("http://ikester.blogspot.com/2007/01/im-huge-fan-of-autohotkey.html")
