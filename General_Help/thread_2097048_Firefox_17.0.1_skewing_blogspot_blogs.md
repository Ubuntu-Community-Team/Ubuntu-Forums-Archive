### Post by Buntu Bunny on 2012-12-22
Orb922, your suspicions that it was a system problem put me on the right trail. The problem was Google Disconnect 2.4.2. I disabled it and everything looks fine now. On Chrome, I have Disconnect 4.1.1 installed, and there are no problems with that. No similar extension for Opera that I've found. 

I reckon I'll just have to let Google track me when I'm blog surfing with Firefox. Or disable it when I want to read blogs. Or not use Firefox. Blogger doesn't cooperate with Opera either, so that rather dictates what browser I can use. 

Anyway, the help is greatly appreciated. Many thanks.
