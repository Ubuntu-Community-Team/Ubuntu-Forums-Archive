### Post by grahammechanical on 2022-10-30
Thunderbolt is a computer interface for connecting external peripherals. It is part of a progression of hardware improvements made over many years. Years ago machines had serial and parallel ports. Moving onwards we get USB and Bluetooth ports as well as Firewire ports. And now its Thunderbolt ports.
