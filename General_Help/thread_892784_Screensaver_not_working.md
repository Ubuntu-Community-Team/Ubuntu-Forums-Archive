### Post by TimboUK on 2008-08-17
Im not sure if this is a bug or a tip.  It depends which way you look at it.

I had no problems previewing screensavers, but once activated they didnt seem to work.

The solution (or bug) is to select your screensaver settings. then reset or shut down Ubuntu and then re-boot.  It appears (for me anyway) that you cannot activate your selected screensaver setting in the same session.

The only reason I posted this is because it had me stumped for a while and I finally found out the answer by chance.

If this is no help and all obvious, please feel free to slander my good name!
