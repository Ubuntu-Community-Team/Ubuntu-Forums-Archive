### Post by Elfy on 2008-08-17
It might have been possible at the time, but if 6 months have gone by then it is extremely likely that they have been overwritten. Generally if you have deleted something then it is best to stop using the harddrive to stop writes to the disk.

You can try photorec to see if there is anything left of them, but I would be surprised [http://www.cgsecurity.org/wiki/PhotoRec](http://www.cgsecurity.org/wiki/PhotoRec)
