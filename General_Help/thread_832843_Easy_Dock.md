### Post by jacobnorton on 2008-06-18
I looked all around for a dock for so long, and finally I was messing around with panels, and you can basically make your own. Heres how in case you care:

Right click on the top panel and click new panel.
Right click on new panel and click properties.
Unclick Expand, Change size to anything (between 54 and 60 looks best) and set "dock" to whatever side you want (i put mine on top).
Right click new "dock" panel and click "add to panel" and add all the shortcuts you want. I added and have my shortcuts in this order:

Main Menu, Seperator, Firefox, Trash, Chess, Sticky Notes, Quit, Weather

Than you can also customize your existing panel to what you want. I have my skinnier panel on the bottom with this:

Workspace switcher, Window List, Time.

Sorry if this has already been discovered/posted, but i thought it was pretty neat and maybe it could help out some other noob looking for a dock (like me).

-jacob.
