### Post by digital_k on 2005-08-14
try this, open xmms, then hit cntrl+p , look at the bottom of the preference where it says output plugin, open the drop down , and select the option that has libesdout.so
at the end of it in parenthesis. click apply and restart xmms and see if that does the trick.
