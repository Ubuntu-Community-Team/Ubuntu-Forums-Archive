### Post by dewdrop_world on 2011-01-15
Probably the Ubuntu Studio (or maybe multimedia) subforum is better for this question.

Have a look at [http://www.linux-sound.org/one-page.html](http://www.linux-sound.org/one-page.html). Also [http://linuxmusicians.com](http://linuxmusicians.com) is an excellent resource.

For multitrack recording/mixing, Ardour is probably the best, but it aims for professionals while garageband is mainly for hobbyists. Also, Ardour currently lacks support for MIDI sequencing, but this will be in the next major release "real soon now." Many people like rosegarden for MIDI sequencing.
