### Post by gsmanners on 2011-10-31
Interesting.

Oct 2009 to Nov 2010: 2 minor releases.
Feb 2011: Debian maintainer complains about low "popcon." Synfig deb goes poof from their repo.
Jun 2011: New version.
Oct 2011: A couple minor revisions later, people notice Synfig has gone AWOL. Thankfully, we still have [upstream]("http://www.synfig.org/cms/en/download/").

If you care enough, file a bug on [Launchpad]("https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/synfig/+filebug"). I won't bother as I never use it.
