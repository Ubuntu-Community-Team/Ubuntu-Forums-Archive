### Post by mcduck on 2011-04-18
Right-clicking on the panel applet that displays your workspaces gives you a menu for that applet. Go to it's options and you can set the amount of workspaces there.

And the "New Panel"-option in the panel's right-click menu does exactly what it says, it creates a new panel. (You can have any number of panels you want instead of the default two).
