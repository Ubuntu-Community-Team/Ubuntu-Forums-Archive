### Post by Xerosagas on 2011-03-23
You can remap the f11 and f12 and any other keys, for that matter,  to whatever shortcut you like, though there's not much use for them. This will help: [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?p=10484321](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?p=10484321)

The touchpad will work if you reflip the dev switch and reinstall the os.

As for the easy way to switch, follow this: [http://goo.gl/fyVbH](http://goo.gl/fyVbH)

Be careful.
