### Post by LewisTM on 2012-01-05
Not sure about the power manager issue. You may try installing [Jupiter]("http://www.jupiterapplet.org/index.html") applet which will manage lots of power settings for you, great for netbooks.

For the xkb plugin, it's a well known [bug]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?p=11499160#post11499160"). You can try some workarounds or remove it and set your keyboard options with a setxkbmap startup command or by editing config files.

Cheers!
