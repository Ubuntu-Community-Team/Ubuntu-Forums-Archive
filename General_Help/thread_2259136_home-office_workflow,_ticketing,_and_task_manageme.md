### Post by tgalati4 on 2015-10-06
Well, the web-client for Evernote works OK in Linux.  I tried to load the Android Evernote client using Arc Welder on Chrome and got a WebGL not supported error.  I have not researched it further.  But you are correct, Evernote folks have said repeatedly that they are not interested in creating a Linux client.

I use *zim* for local note-taking.  You can put your *zim* files in the cloud to get access to them on the go from multiple machines, or simply put them on a shared drive on a local network.
