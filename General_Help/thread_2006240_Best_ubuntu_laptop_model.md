### Post by PhantomTurtle on 2012-06-18
For $900 you can System76's Gazelle Professional. However there are other things to factor in such as shipping costs which will cost you more. Additionally I would look at some reviews for this laptop first before you buy to make sure it has everything you need, such as long battery life(if that is something you want). The Gazelle Professional itself has a base price of $899 and with it you get a 15.6" 1080p Full High Definition LED Backlit Display with Glossy Surface ( 1920 x 1080 ), Intel HD Graphics 4000, 3rd Generation Intel Core i7-3610QM Processor ( 2.30GHz 6MB L3 Cache - 4 Cores plus Hyperthreading ), 4 GB DDR3 SDRAM at 1600MHz - 1 X 4 GB and 500 GB 7200 RPM SATA II. Here is a good review for this laptop - [http://bhaismachine.wordpress.com/2012/05/31/system76-gazelle-professional-2012-review/](http://bhaismachine.wordpress.com/2012/05/31/system76-gazelle-professional-2012-review/). System76 also has a laptop for $700 which is similar expect with lower specs and a smaller screen.

The only thing I have to say is that for gaming, Ubuntu does not have as much games as Windows does(but it does still have a great selection of games, such as the humble indie bundle which has some great games).  

Here are some benchmarks for the Intel Graphics 4000 - [http://www.anandtech.com/show/5626/ivy-bridge-preview-core-i7-3770k/11]("http://www.anandtech.com/show/5626/ivy-bridge-preview-core-i7-3770k/11"). This will help you decided whether it is right for gaming for you. I have heard that system76 laptops are very good and their customer service is excellent, and you can get lots more help from them. Good Luck with the new laptop.
