### Post by 8bit on 2006-06-04
I had this problem for about a month with my system after doing a fresh install of Dapper. On occasion it would take as many as 7 to 10 reboots before Ubuntu would fully load. I finally went through all of my hardware and BIOS settings one by one and found that my sound card (Sound Blaster Live) was the culprit. I removed it and the issue was solved.

I thought I would post this in case others ran into the same problem.

Cheers,
