### Post by Spr0k3t on 2010-05-20
It's quite simple and works very slick.  You can also sync up your calendar as well from google.

**Contacts:**
In Evolution select File > New > Address Book. Give the address book a name, enter your gmail account name, and mark the address book as default.

**Calendar:**
For the Calendar select File > New > Calendar. Give the calendar a name, enter your gmail account name, and mark the calendar as default. 

I use this method to sync up my android based phone in realtime.  [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/PortableDevices/VerizonDroid](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/PortableDevices/VerizonDroid)
