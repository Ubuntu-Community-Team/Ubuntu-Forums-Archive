### Post by plucky on 2008-01-09
Ashenrose,

1) Can you see them in your BIOS display when the system boots?
2) Did you set one as master and one as slave?
3) can you see them in **Places > Computer**?
4) Are the boxes ticked in **System > Preferences > Removable Drives and Media**?

Good luck
