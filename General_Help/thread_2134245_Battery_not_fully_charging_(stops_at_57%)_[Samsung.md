### Post by yaoverstand on 2013-04-10

I charged it once while powered on and once while powered off, but neither has lifted the maximum charge.  It is actually only charging to about half power, this is not just a misrepresentation in the battery indicator (laptop lasted over 4 hours on win 7 and 8, lasts just over 110 minutes on ubuntu 12.10).  I have tried to enable and disable different settings; brightness adjustments, bluetooth, wifi on/off...but nothing has worked.  I really need for the battery to last longer because I am a student, and I would like to be able to take my laptop to class without bringing my charger.  Thanks in advance!
