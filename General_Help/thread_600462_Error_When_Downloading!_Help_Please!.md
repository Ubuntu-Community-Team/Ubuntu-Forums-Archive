### Post by FuturePilot on 2007-11-02
Looks like something is wrong with your Firefox profile.
Open your home folder and press Ctrl+H to show hidden files. Find the one called .mozilla (yes it has a dot in front of it) and rename it something like .mozilla_old. Restart Firefox and it will automatically create a new profile. If that fixes the problem you can go into the .mozilla_old and recover your bookmarks.
