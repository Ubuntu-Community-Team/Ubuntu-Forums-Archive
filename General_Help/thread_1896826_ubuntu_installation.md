### Post by mikewhatever on 2011-12-17
You can't upgrade directly from 10.04 to 11.10. The only way is to go 10.04 -> 10.10 -> 11.04 -> 11.10, or in other words, upgrade thrice. That said, when 12.04 is out in April, it might be possible to upgrade from 10.04 to 12.04.

### Post by astrognome on 2011-12-17
You could move your home directory onto a separate partition and mount it to /home in the fstab.  Doing this, you could easily change your distro to anything (newer version of ubuntu as well) by installing over your existing linux install.  Then all you would need to do is modify your fstab and all of your files would be there.

[http://embraceubuntu.com/2006/01/29/move-home-to-its-own-partition/](http://embraceubuntu.com/2006/01/29/move-home-to-its-own-partition/)
