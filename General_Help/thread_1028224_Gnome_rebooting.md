### Post by wylfing on 2009-01-02
I would suspect more RAM problems.

How did you determine that just one stick of RAM was bad? If you were replacing RAM sticks one by one and then booting up to see if things got better (which is what I probably would have done), you may have found that one stick was causing most of your issues but there still may be other sticks that are bad too.

If you can, try replacing more of your RAM with known good sticks.
