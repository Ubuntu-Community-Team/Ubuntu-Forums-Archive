### Post by maheshasolkar on 2009-05-07
Find the NetworkManager applet icon in the top-right corner of your screen.

Right-click on it and make sure wireless LAN is enabled

If it is, click on the icon. It will show all the wireless networks it can find. If your router broadcasts wireless network SSID, you should find your SSID in the list. Just click on it and enter authentication information to connect.

If your router does not broadcast the SSID, click on 'connect to hidden network'. Then enter your network's SSID and rest of the authentication information to connect.
