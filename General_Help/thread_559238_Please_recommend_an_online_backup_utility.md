### Post by quixotic-cynic on 2007-09-25
Anything accessible through a web browser or ftp should be usable on whatever operating system you choose (since almost any OS has web browsers and ftp clients).

I know that [http://www.hushmail.com/](http://www.hushmail.com/) supports file storage, but you have to pay for that one. You can also use webmail accounts (provided this is ok under the terms and conditions) such as gmail (though google is evil ;)).

It's probably also worth pointing out that a lot of online storage sites may be a little dodgy (do you know how secure they are). It may be worth pointing out that you can get 1gig usb sticks for peanuts now (£6 ~ $12) so that might be an option worth considering. External HDDs are also quite cheap but notably more.
