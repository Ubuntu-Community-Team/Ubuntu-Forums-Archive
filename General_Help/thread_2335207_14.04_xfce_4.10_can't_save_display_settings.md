### Post by ajgreeny on 2016-08-26
Tell us more about your hardware.

Normally the screen resolution is set automatically when the monitor is detected at boot, but it may be that you need a driver for your graphic card to be able to give you that resolution.

Show us the output of command ```
sudo lshw -c display
``` in terminal.

Please use **Code-Tags** for terminal output. See my signature below for a **How-to**
