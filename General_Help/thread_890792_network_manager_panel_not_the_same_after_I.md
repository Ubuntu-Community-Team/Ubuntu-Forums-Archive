### Post by Rich Stokes on 2008-08-16
I suspect what has happened is that you have removed the Notification Area somehow.

To restore it, try the following:

Right click on the Panel at the top of the screen and select Add to Panel

Scroll down and select Notification Area.  This is like the system tray in Windows.

For me, this worked, but the wireless icon didn't re-appear until I re-booted.
