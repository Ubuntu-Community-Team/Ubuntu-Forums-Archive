### Post by kurt18947 on 2012-06-25
Here is a brand which is not terribly common but I have had very good luck with:

[http://www.compusa.com/applications/SearchTools/search.asp?keywords=centon+usb](http://www.compusa.com/applications/SearchTools/search.asp?keywords=centon+usb)

I have a few of these with live USB installs and they seem to load faster than other sticks.  They also have a little LED that flashes when reading or writing.  I have a couple PNY sticks that have no indicator to show activity.  

[http://www.staples.com/office/supplies/StaplesCategoryDisplay?catalogId=10051&interceptedCatEntryId=337362&identifier=BI235376&langId=-1&storeId=10001](http://www.staples.com/office/supplies/StaplesCategoryDisplay?catalogId=10051&interceptedCatEntryId=337362&identifier=BI235376&langId=-1&storeId=10001)

These work okay for data storage but I don't like them for creating live USB or full O.S. installs.  There is no activity indicator on these PNY drives.  Plug one in, turn the machine on, the screen says it's booting but there's no indication whether it's doing anything or if it's stopped.
