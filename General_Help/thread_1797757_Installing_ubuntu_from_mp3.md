### Post by IWantFroyo on 2011-07-05
It won't boot if you just copy the files over. Your best bet would be to use something like Infrarecorder to burn the .iso onto a disc, or to use PendriveLinux or LinuxLive ([http://www.linuxliveusb.com](http://www.linuxliveusb.com)) to make a installable flashdrive (you may have to borrow or get a new one).
