Using Ubuntu 12.10 Quantal Quetzal 64bit version.

If I switch to gnome-session-fallback, windows do not have titlebars, minimize, maximize or close widgets  and I cannot move or resize them.

### Post by hal8000 on 2012-10-27
This may be a bug in Ubuntu 12.10

If you have installed gnome-session-fallback and have no window titlebars, widgets, and cannot move windows, try logging in with option
Gnome(classic) No effects.

This time titlebars re-appear and can be moved, resized etc.

I am using fglrx driver with ATI5770 card and running 64bit Ubuntu from fresh install.
Hope this helps someone.
