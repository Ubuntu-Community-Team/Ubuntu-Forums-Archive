### Post by blazemore on 2009-02-04
System->Administration->Users and Groups->Unlock

Enter your password and hit Enter

Now click on the faulty user, click Properties->User Privileges

Make sure the Connect to Wireless and Ethernet Networks button is ticked. And anything else appropriate.
