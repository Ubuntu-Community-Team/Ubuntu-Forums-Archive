### Post by hhh on 2010-03-06
Do you still have Xubuntu, either as a session or on a Live CD? You could take some screenshots of Xubuntu and save them to a USB drive, then get the color hex-codes via Gimp (already installed on Ubuntu) or Gcolor2 (available via Synaptic). Either one has an eyedropper tool that lets you click the screenshot (or anywhere on your screen for Gcolor2) and *presto* hex code.
