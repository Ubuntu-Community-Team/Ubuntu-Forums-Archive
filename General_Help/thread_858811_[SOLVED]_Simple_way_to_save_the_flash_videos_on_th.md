### Post by rushikesh988 on 2008-07-13
most of newbies have this quiestion in thier mind " How can I save videos on my favorite sides like  youtube , matcafe etc."


and the answer is You don'y have to download any additional application..

1. just go to your favorite video site 
2. wait till video loads/buffers into your browser
3.now open Computer From places-->computer(plz don't close the browser)
4. go to fileystem and go to tmp folder (you can type /tmp into the location bar by pressing ctrl+L )  
5. you will see file named as "Flash1OzAn7"  
6.just copy that file into the destination where you want to save video 
7 rename it as u like 
8 thats it  now play that video this is that video which is in the browser



This  trick will act with most of video sites

there are another options available like using  Pytube etc applications
