### Post by ram2500 on 2010-03-14
Video switches (usually) don't require drivers, so it should work immediately. Try shutting down and restarting the PC, and see if that works. If not, you might want to research your particular switch to see if it needs a driver or if it isn't compatible with Linux for some reason. Also, video switches shouldn't change the resolution, so the highest resolution available with your monitor and video card should be the resolution you get even with a video switch.
