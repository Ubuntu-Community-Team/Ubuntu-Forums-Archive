### Post by Ivo Moelans on 2008-12-15
The elements you are referring to are themed by GTK.

To get you started: [http://www.gnome-look.org/]("http://www.gnome-look.org/") and look for *GTK 2.x Theme/Style* entries.
After download drag them in the window of *System&#8594;Preferences &#8594;Appearance*. They will be installed and there you can select (apply) them.
Good luck.
