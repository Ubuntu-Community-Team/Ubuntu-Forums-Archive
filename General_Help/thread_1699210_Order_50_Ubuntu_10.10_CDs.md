### Post by Dragonbite on 2011-03-06
> **kanishkdudeja said:**
> @dragon bite..

no i will not be paid for it..

can you please send the link for the "ship it" option.

the custom thing you're talking about.

and yeah..there is no harm in trying..:)

From the Ubuntu site go into
[[COLOR="Blue"]Desktops[/COLOR]]("http://www.ubuntu.com/desktop") > [[COLOR="Blue"]CDs[/COLOR]]("http://www.ubuntu.com/desktop/get-ubuntu/cds") > ([[COLOR="Blue"]after signing into Launchpad[/COLOR]]("https://login.launchpad.net/CDV4xCWDC8M8rbvp/+decide")) [[COLOR="Blue"]Request a CD[/COLOR]]("https://shipit.ubuntu.com/") > [[COLOR="Blue"]Desktop Edition requests [/COLOR]]("https://shipit.ubuntu.com/myrequest")  > you'll see a link for [[COLOR="Blue"]special requests[/COLOR]]("https://shipit.ubuntu.com/myrequest") under "New request" and there is a section labeled **Why are you requesting these CDs?**.  That's where you fill in the information on what you want to do.

Or, for the short way, you click this link and try to get into the ShipIt special request form :popcorn: [[COLOR="Blue"]https://shipit.ubuntu.com/specialrequest[/COLOR]]("https://shipit.ubuntu.com/specialrequest")  :)

Good luck!
