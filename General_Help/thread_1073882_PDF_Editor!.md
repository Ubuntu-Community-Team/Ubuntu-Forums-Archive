### Post by zerubbabel on 2009-02-23
After trying all the native Linux pdf editing tools I could find, I went back to using pdf-xchange under Wine. You can find it at [http://www.docu-track.com](http://www.docu-track.com). The free version has limitations, but the paid "Pro" version is very cheap ($29.50) for its power,and runs fine under Wine. Even under Windows, I preferred it to Adobe Acrobat Pro. It's the only Windows application that I still use regularly, other than BibleWorks.
