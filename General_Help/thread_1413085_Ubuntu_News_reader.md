### Post by kellemes on 2010-02-22
Couple of options for automatic handling of nzb's, par2 fixing etc..
[sabnzbd]("http://sabnzbd.org/")
[hellanzb]("http://hellanzb.com/trac/hellanzb")
[NNTPGrab]("http://www.nntpgrab.nl/wiki/index.php/Main_Page")
Cannot recommend anyone of these since I use the build-in software of my NAS-device.

By the way.. long long time ago I've successfully used [Grabit]("http://www.shemes.com/") on [Wine]("http://www.winehq.org/") but surely this is not recommended.

For 'normal' news reading I recommend Google-Reader.
