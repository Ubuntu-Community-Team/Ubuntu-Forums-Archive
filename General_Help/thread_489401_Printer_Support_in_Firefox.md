### Post by linuxwizard on 2007-07-01
If you don't have a printer icon on your toolbar > open Firefox >  click on view (drop down menu) > toolbars > customize > in that window that opens look for a printer icon an drag it to your toolbar you should be able to print now. Also in Firefox click on File can print from there.
