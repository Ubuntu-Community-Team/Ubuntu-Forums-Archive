I've got another thread about my problems with Grub 2 in the Installation and Upgrades forum.  One of many about problems with being unable to do a successful 'upgrade-grub'.  And with no responses I kept searching the fora.  And FINALLY stumbled upon 'Grub-Customizer' ([http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1195275](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1195275) and [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?p=10340183](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?p=10340183)).  

:popcorn:WHY IS THIS HIDDEN UNDER A BUSHEL?):popcorn:

So, for anyone else with a dual boot with Windows system who is having difficulties getting GRUB 2 (1.98 really) to respond to the commands that everyone is telling you should work:

(1) Use KPackageKit (or Synaptic or whatever) to remove GRUB and GRUB-PC.  

(2) Reinstall GRUB and GRUB-PC.

(3) Follow the instructions in the other threads to install Grub-Customizer.  

(4) Reboot and memorize what your GRUB menu looks like.

(5) Run GRUB-CUSTOMIZER and make your customizations.

(6) Install BURG if you want the GRUB screen to be colorful.

Be happy for a moment and then go find some other setting to fiddle with.
