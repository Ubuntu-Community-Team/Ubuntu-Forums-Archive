### Post by mgmiller on 2007-05-02
If you stick with the nvidia drivers from the ubuntu repos, you will not have any problems with kernel updates.  I play UT2k4 with them and they work fine.  Just make sure you have the meta package for your kernel installed and then do the nvidia driver from synaptic or the new restricted drivers manager.  You will only have problems if you get the drivers from some where else.  That being said, if you "must" go outside the repos for your drivers, try installing the envy package to help manage them.
