### Post by dasunst3r on 2007-05-29
Ubuntu Ultimate may be using an antiquated method to get on the network (i.e. pre-NetworkManager).  Try this to get to the networking interfaces administration:
[list=1]
[*]Go to System -> Administration -> Networking
[*]Type in the password you used to log into your computer
[/list]

Nevertheless, ikonia's point about downloading [www.ubuntu.com](www.ubuntu.com) is perfectly valid.  You can always find the packages that were in Ubuntu Ultimate in Ubuntu.  Additionally, the site did not look very legit.  Sorry that you may have to reinstall.  I know how much it stinks.

Hope this helps!
