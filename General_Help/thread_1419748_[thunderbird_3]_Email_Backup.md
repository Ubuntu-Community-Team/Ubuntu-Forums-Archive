### Post by psusi on 2010-03-02
IIRC, thunderbird mail files are somewhere under ~/.mozilla-thunderbird/.  Drill down in there and you should find files with the same names as your folders.  Just copy those files somewhere safe, preferably after you have thunderbird compact them, then shut it down.
