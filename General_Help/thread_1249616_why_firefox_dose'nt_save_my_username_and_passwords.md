### Post by Penguin Guy on 2009-08-25
Open Firefox and make sure the [[COLOR="Blue"]'Remember password for sites' checkbox[/COLOR]]("http://ubuntuforums.org/attachment.php?attachmentid=126201&stc=1&d=1251228249") is ticked: [COLOR="Green"]Edit -> Preferences -> Security -> Remember passwords for sites[/COLOR].

### Post by ghostborg on 2009-08-25
Also make sure your Privacy settings are not clearing everything when you close the browser. You can find this under Edit=>Prefernces under the Privacy Tab and verify if your Clear History box is not checked, or click on the settings button right of Clear History.
