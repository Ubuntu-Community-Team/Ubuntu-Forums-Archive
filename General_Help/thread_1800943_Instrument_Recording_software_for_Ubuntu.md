### Post by CatKiller on 2011-07-09
There's a bunch. Which you use depends on whether you're recording sound or signals. Audacity is fine if you're just doing audio, and there are a few programs that will capture MIDI signals. Two good DAWs are available - Ardour and Rosegarden - if you want to mix-and-match. You can use pretty much whatever you like for a particular job & link it all together with JACK.
