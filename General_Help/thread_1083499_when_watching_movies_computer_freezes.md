### Post by deathadder on 2011-03-22
I've had similiar problems before with different distro's, installing the video drivers for my card always helps. Have a look at the [binary driver howto]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BinaryDriverHowto") for your card. If you're not sure what your card is, try using the lspci command. Although I've always found that the "Additional Drivers" menu option from System/Administration works wonders. :)
