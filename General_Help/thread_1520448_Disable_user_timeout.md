### Post by Austin25 on 2010-06-29
There is an inhibit sleep applet for the gnome panel. Right a panel (top or bottom, doesn't matter), click add to panel, scroll down, then double click inhibit applet. Once it's on the panel, click it, and your computer won't go to sleep after an amount of time.
