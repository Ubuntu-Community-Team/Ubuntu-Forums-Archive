Did you ever find a satisfactory solution? I noticed this thread and thought I'd throw in my $0.02 (well, more like $1.02 after what the fed's been doing to my money).

Aweber and GetResponse have been the Intel and AMD of email marketing services for several years now. And for good reason... double opt-in, spam filtering, high deliverability... all the tools you need to get the job done.

But there are up-and-comers that will gain some market share. I'm a big fan of Mailchimp. They have great tools, and they keep coming up with new ones. Also, last time I checked, there was a "free account" option for folks who mailed less than 500 a month.

The Achilles heel of email marketing services is transferability. If you choose one, and then later on, for whatever reason, you decide that you are not happy.... You can't just up and move your hard-earned list to a new service. Virtually all of them will require that every single member of your list opt-in AGAIN with their service. You probably already know this, but.... if that happens, you'll lose 80% - 90% of your list in the transition (unless you do massive promo work leading up to it, and even then, expect 50% - 75% loss).

The solution, in my opinion, is hosting your own email marketing server. The best solution I've ever come across (I'm in marketing, so my parameters are colored by this fact), is called Autoresponse plus. Essentially, it's software you host on your own server, that gives you the same (or a comparable) feature set as aweber, get response, mailchimp, etc... some more, some less, your mileage may vary (etc ad nauseum.)
