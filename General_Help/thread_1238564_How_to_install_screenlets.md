### Post by scouser73 on 2009-08-12
Assuming that you've already downloaded the Screenlets program, go to **Accessories** > **Screenlets** & select. Once running click **Install**, select **Install Screenlet** and click **OK**, then navigate to where the screenlets you have downloaded are.
