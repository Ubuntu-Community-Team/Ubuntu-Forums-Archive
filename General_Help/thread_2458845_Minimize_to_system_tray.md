In Kubuntu, using Strawberry (or whatever) as my audio app.
In opensuse, when I close the application, it minimises to the system tray.
How can I get it to do that??
Thanks.

---

### Post by speartip on 2021-03-05
In Strawberry. Tools - Settings. Make sure the "Show system tray" & "Keep running in the background..." boxes are ticked, then click apply.
