### Post by grahammechanical on 2011-06-29
Does the Monitors utility give you the opportunity to change the screen resolution? That is what you lose when you do not run the screen at the optimum settings. There will be a distinct lack of quality in the look of things. But you know this because you have used the low resolution mode of the recovery startup. That is what the screen will look like. You would be wise to check the settings for the monitor. It may not work too well in certain modes.
