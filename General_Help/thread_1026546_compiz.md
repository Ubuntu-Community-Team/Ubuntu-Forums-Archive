### Post by Ratscallion on 2008-12-31
Myself, as soon as I saw the compiz settings, I downloaded them and didn't actually look any further, I simply went ahead and tested every single setting out. Probably a long way of doing it, but it gets it done and you then know a few things:
[LIST]
[*]What things do
[*]How things work
[*]Which ones your Graphics Card can handle
[/LIST]
I suggest you try this to know these things, otherwise, if you find a plug-in that you like, you may be upset when you find out your graphics card cannot use it.
