### Post by merlinus on 2007-07-15
According to /etc/fstab, your swap partition will load automatically.

But because you re-sized the partition, the UUID has most likely changed.

```

blkid

```

will give the current UUID.  Compare it with /etc/fstab, and change if needed.

-merlin
