I just installed BleachBit form the jaunty repository, and it gives me two apps in Applications/System Tools:
BleachBit
BleachBit (as root)

---

### Post by cariboo on 2009-05-02
Running bleachbit as a normal user just clears files in your home directory, running it as root cleans out files that you don't have permissions to as a normal usr. If you want to hide a menu selection right-click on Applications and select Edit Menus, then uncheck the application you want ot hide.
