Hello xenex!

I assume that you have deleted the old panel on the top? If so, just make a right click on the panel on the bottom of the screen and choose "New Panel". This panel can be moved to all four edges of the screen.

Now, do a right click on the left rim of your notification area, choose move and move it to the top left corner of your screen.

Good luck and let us know if it worked out!

If you have any further questions, let us know!
