its not a virus, its an email sent from a known [phishing]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Phishing") account. That is something you have to beware off on any OS, email scams pretending to be from ebay/paypal/your bank luring you to a fake website to steal your identity or credit card or whatever. No OS is really immune to that, but an alert user is.
