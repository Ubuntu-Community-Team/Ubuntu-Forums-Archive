### Post by ajgreeny on 2017-12-20
**NO, NO, NO !**

It is never a good idea to offer this sort of information with no qualification at all about the potential dangers of doing so, and sudo nautilus should never be used as it could change ownership of files in your home resulting in you locking yourself out from the system and being unable to login.

Give us a lot more info about the files you believe you must remove, as the probability is that if left remaining they will cause no problems at all and use very little disk space, though I have to admit to total lack of knowledge about **xmr-stak Monero miner**.
