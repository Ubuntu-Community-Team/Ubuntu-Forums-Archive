### Post by 3Miro on 2011-04-15
What is your graphics card? Nvidia hybrid Optimus (i.e.GTX425M or something like that?)

Nvidia corporation has decided that they will neither provide drivers for their Optimus video cards, nor would they provide the necessary specifications for the community to create its own drivers.

Note: you can try alternate install, however, if you cannot boot from the LiveCD, then chances that Linux will not work at all are against you.
