### Post by sandydoull on 2009-07-28
There is a known issue with the linux version of flashplayer that has evidently existed for years now, the problem seems to be worst when viewing in fullscreen, see bug at adobe's aite and be sure and vote for the bug!: [http://bugs.adobe.com/jira/browse/FP-1692](http://bugs.adobe.com/jira/browse/FP-1692)

There are various fixes if you search around, with varying success rates, I have tried most of the fixes around and still have poor flash performance, but this may be partly due to the low spec netbook I am running, if you have a higher end system the fixes may just be enough to get it working on our system.

Looks like you are after a nice script called get_flash_videos-1.23, details can be seen here:

get-flash-videos
[http://code.google.com/p/get-flash-videos/](http://code.google.com/p/get-flash-videos/)

and below is a demonstration of a successful download from the site:

