### Post by Monotoko on 2011-02-22
I would say your hard drive is too new for the Windows XP CD to read it. You can boot from a Ubuntu LiveCD and restore Ubuntu.

As for Windows 7, did you get a restore disk? There might be a recovery option in your BIOS which will go ahead and restore 7.
