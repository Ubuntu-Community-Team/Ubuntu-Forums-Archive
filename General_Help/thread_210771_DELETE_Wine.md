### Post by Jagot on 2006-07-07
In Synaptic go to Wine and right click it.  Now select 'Mark For Complete Removal', then click apply.  This should delete your Wine config files.

Alternatively, there should be a hidden directory in your /home called .wine.  In Nautilus press ctrl+h to see hidden files/folders and delete it.
