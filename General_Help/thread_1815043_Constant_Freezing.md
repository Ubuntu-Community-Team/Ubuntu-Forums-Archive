### Post by ajgreeny on 2011-07-30
I presume that when you say you have gnome 2.32.1 that you are using classic gnome desktop.  Correct?

If so, is that by choice or the default that appeared when you installed and rebooted?  If you get it as the default it could be that your computer is only just recent enough and fast enough to run 11.04.  As a result it may be getting too hot, causing the freezes, or maybe it needs a better graphic driver.  Have you looked in System->Administration->Additional drivers to see if any are available?
