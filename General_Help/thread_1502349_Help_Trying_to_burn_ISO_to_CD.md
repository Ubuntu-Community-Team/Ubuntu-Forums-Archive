### Post by XubuRoxMySox on 2010-06-05
Some people have better luck with a different burning application. Brasero, on my machine, was hit-or-miss, but others think it's the best! I made alot of coasters with it. Xfburn works superbly and reliably on my hardware. Alot of people like K3B as well (it's a QT app, so you get some libraries with it, but it always worked reliably for me too).

make sure it's compatible with the hard drive, and the CD-RW isn't a DVD... 

The other thing is that you want to burn it as an *image*, not as a data file.

Just something else to try...
