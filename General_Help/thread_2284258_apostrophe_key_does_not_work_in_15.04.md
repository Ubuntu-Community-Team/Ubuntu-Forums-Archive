### Post by Vladlenin5000 on 2015-06-28
You may have selected some variant "with dead keys" in which case you need to press space after the apostrophe. It'll turn into an acute accent for the next character otherwise.
(Quotation marks / inverted commas likewise will turn into an umlaut if you don't press space before typing the next character).
