### Post by pthenning on 2008-10-20
Have either of you tried to delete the youtube.com cookies from your browser?

In Firefox:

Edit => Preferences => Privacy => Show Cookies

Highlight "youtube.com" from the list.

Choose "Remove Cookies".

Reload page.
