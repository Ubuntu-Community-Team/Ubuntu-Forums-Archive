### Post by TheFu on 2012-08-02
I always check [http://freecode.com/search?q=inventory+management&submit=Search](http://freecode.com/search?q=inventory+management&submit=Search) for options, then ask for opinions about specific software?  Often there are industry specific tools, since video rental stores have different needs from lawn equipment rental places.

If the clients and inventory are in a DB then there will be a unique number assumed, but you should never see those.  Perhaps they will have a "SKU" for the items and an account number for the clients that will work for you?

I think you'll find lots of options at freecode. This need seems pretty common for small businesses.  Good luck!
