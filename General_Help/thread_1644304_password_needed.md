### Post by piquat on 2010-12-13
Help from a newb....

I THINK I know what it is.  I'm in Mint right now but IIRC you have a network manager icon running in your systray in Ubuntu correct?  Right click that and choose "Edit Connections".  Find your connection under those tabs in that window and select it, then hit "Edit" on the right.  You see that "Available to All Users" on the bottom that's not checked?  Check that.  When you close this it will probably ask for your password again since you've modified it...

Now try it again.
