### Post by sanderd17 on 2011-05-10
There have been promises that skype will open-sourc it's client, so that other interfaces could be build or that the interface could be integrated with another voip-program. You would still not be able to call from skype to another voip-program that doesn't support skype, but it could be something like the integration of google-talk, msn, ... in pidgin or empathy.
