### Post by Linja on 2008-08-02
Doesn't matter, whichever you prefer. Separate X sessions gives you two separate screens (but you can move from one to the other just fine) while twinview gives you one big screen that spans both monitors. Feel free to experiment, I find myself switching between the two because I find neither good for all purposes, more like they complement each other.
