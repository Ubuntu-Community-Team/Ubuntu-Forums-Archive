### Post by quixote on 2009-12-04
I just bought an HP Mini 311 and initially made it dual boot with UNR on the new partition.  It ran *unbelievably* slowly.  Searching around, I found out others had the same problem.  Nobody seems to know exactly why it happens.  That machine is plenty capable enough to run straight jaunty, so I took UNR off, put jaunty on, and am 100% happy with it.  

There's maybe more than you want to know at the wiki page on hardware and UNR: [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/HardwareSupport/Machines/Netbooks](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/HardwareSupport/Machines/Netbooks)
