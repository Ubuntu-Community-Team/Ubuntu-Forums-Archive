### Post by aysiu on 2008-10-14
Well, a very simple first step to diagnosing this problem is to boot up the Ubuntu Desktop CD as a live CD (select the *Try Ubuntu without any change to your computer* option), go to Hotmail and see you get this spe.atdmt.com thing on your main Hotmail account.

If you do, I doubt it's a virus/spyware/adware installed on your computer. It's probably just Microsoft selling your information and surfing habits to some other company (i.e., "spyware" that's tied to Hotmail, not installed on your computer).
