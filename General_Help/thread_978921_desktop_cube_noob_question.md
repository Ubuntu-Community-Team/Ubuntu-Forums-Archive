### Post by tjwoosta on 2008-11-11
first make sure you have ccms installed "compiz config settings manager"
go to applications-add/remove then in the box that says "SHOW", change it to all available applications, then search for and install ccsm

go to "system-preferences-compiz config settings manager" then go to "rotate cube" and choose the "general" tab then adjust the zoom

you may also need to adjust the speed and timestep, to make it seem like a more natural rotation

you can also view the cube by pressing and holding ctlr-alt and left mouse button then move the mouse

### Post by Kellemora on 2008-11-11
Hi Not Yet

Actually, what you are asking for is NOT really a feature of Compiz.

You get that EFFECT when you press and hold the Ctrl key and the Alt key down at the same time, while HOLDING your left mouse button down.

THEN it shrinks down to a cube (if you have selected 4 workspaces)

You can have a LOT of phun with Compiz, make the sides of the cube semitransparent and add a set of gears inside.  Add your own images on the top and bottom of the cube, etc.

As already mentioned, in the settings, you can set how small are large you want the shrunk box to appear, whether you want the open programs standing out from the box or flat on it's face.
It's pretty neat to have handy to show Suckers, er I mean WinDOZE users, hi hi.........
You can get some neat screen effects from it too.

Nice toy, phun to play with!
