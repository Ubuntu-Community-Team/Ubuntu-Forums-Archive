### Post by Giblet5 on 2009-10-19
The 'unclean' (aka brutal) shutdowns have corrupted the root filesystem in some way.

The easiest "fix" is to restart the computer and select Recovery Mode. Go through the Xfix option.

Next, buy a good laptop pad that lets it breathe. I use the Aerogel board and it holds my 20"-wide laptop with room for a bluetooth mouse.
