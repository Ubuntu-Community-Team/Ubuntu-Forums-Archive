### Post by Dennis N on 2017-03-25
> How do I find simple things like dashes and superscript numbers?

If you just need these for occasional use in word processing, superscript and subscript are format options in Libre Office Writer, so are easy to create. There are no special characters to deal with. In fact, they are on the toolbar for convenience. Also, in LibreOffice writer, two hyphens are always converted to a long dash unless you disable this.

And, Libre Office has its own character map accessible from the toolbar for many special characters you may need.
