### Post by joshruehlig on 2010-08-07
Alot of HP netbooks need a special broadcom driver. **Try hooking your netbook into an ethernet cable and going to system>hardware drivers and it may tell you to download something.** This can also be done through your cd/usb that you installed with but ethernet way is easier.

If this doesn't work we may need to figure out what wireless card you have. Try running "lspci" in a terminal, (accessorier>terminal).

By the way what hp model number do you have?
