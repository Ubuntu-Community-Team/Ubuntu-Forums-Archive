### Post by philinux on 2008-11-19
System>Prefs>Main Menu. 

There you can add a new item/launcher to the wine submenu.

---

### Post by stubrownuk on 2008-11-19
Cheers, that's done the trick and it launches correctly. One supplementary question: can I customise the menu icon associated with the shortcut?

---

### Post by philinux on 2008-11-19
Prefs>Main Menu. Highlight your new launcher. Choose properties. Left click the icon and a dialogue box will open with other available icons to choose from.
