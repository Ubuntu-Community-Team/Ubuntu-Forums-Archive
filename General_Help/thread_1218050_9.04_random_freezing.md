Are you using KDE or GNOME?

Gnome by default has 2 panels, and text menus (Applications Places System)

KDE has one larger panel at the bottom by default and a menu that is a button that has a K logo.

If you are on KDE, then you disable desktop effects in system settings.

On GNOME go to System->Preferences->Appearance and choose None as the desktop effects preference.

If you had 'Ubuntu' and now 'Kubuntu' chances are you have both of them. At the login screen, find a menu, and look for sessions. You can choose between GNOME or KDE.
