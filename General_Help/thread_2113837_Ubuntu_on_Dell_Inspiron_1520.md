### Post by tgalati4 on 2013-02-09
Make sure you use a cable to hook up the internet, because wifi may not work out-of-the-box.  If your wifi doesn't work, and it will be obvious, then you will need to load proprietary drivers.  There are quite a few tutorials on these forums and on the wiki's on how to do that.  Similarly, if your graphics are not working correctly, you may need to load proprietary drivers.
