Clicking on "File" for example should give me options to open, save, save as, etc.  All they do is become highlighted with no drop down menu. I won't mark this solved, because it's not (though it may be in 11.10 - haven't gotten there yet), but the author pointed me to a work around.  When you go to a menu item drag down the menu item, and the drop down list will come down with it.
