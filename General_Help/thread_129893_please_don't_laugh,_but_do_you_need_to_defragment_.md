### Post by bored2k on 2006-02-14
[QUOTE=The ext3 wikipedia entry]There is **no ext3 defragmentation tool**. An offline ext2 defragmenter, e2defrag, exists but requires that the ext3 filesystem be converted back to ext2 first. However, defragmentation has long been** considered a non-issue for ext2/ext3**, since they are much better at placing files on the disk versus old FAT-based filesystems.[/QUOTE][http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Comparison_of_file_systems](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Comparison_of_file_systems)

Not necessary.
