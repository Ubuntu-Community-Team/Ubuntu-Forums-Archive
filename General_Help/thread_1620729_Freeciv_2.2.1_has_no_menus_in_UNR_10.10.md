### Post by milam on 2010-11-26
I too have the Acer 532h and running 10.10 (albeit in 2d mode, Unity just too slow) and had the same problem with Freeciv in that there were no menus.

I looked up the keyboard shortcuts for Freeciv and found F10 activates the game menu. Upon hitting F10 not only the game menu but all the other menus appeared, and remained visible throughout the course of the game. However it is something that I have to do each time I start the game, but they will remain visible once they have been "unhidden".
