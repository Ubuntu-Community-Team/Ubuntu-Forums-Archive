### Post by santiagoward2000 on 2007-11-23
> **Sam Plamondon said:**
> How would I set up my Windows Live Messenger account on Pidgin Internet Messenger?

Hi!
Just go to **Accounts / Add/Edit...**, click on **Add**. In **Protocol** select **MSN**, and in **Screen Name** type your adsress (including the @hotmail.com / @msn.com).
I hope this helps!
