### Post by anotherdisciple on 2008-09-26
Gmail offers IMAP... which is much better than POP in my opinion...

Pop will push all of your gmail to evolution (on one computer) and delete it from gmail. IMAP will make a copy to evolution (any number of computers) and anything you do in evolution gets synced with gmail. That way if you are at a friend's house and want to check your email... you can.

In Gmail under (Settings > Forwarding and POP/IMAP) enable IMAP and use the information [[COLOR="Blue"]here[/COLOR]]("http://mail.google.com/support/bin/answer.py?answer=78799") to set it up.
