### Post by TheFu on 2013-01-01
For really, really young kids, go with a whitelist.
As they get older ... 7+, open them up to content based filtering.
When they move out, then they can avoid the filters.

Here's a blog article [http://blog.jdpfu.com/2011/06/10/kid-safety-checklist](http://blog.jdpfu.com/2011/06/10/kid-safety-checklist) about this subject AND some interesting comments. There's a section on internet and computer safety with a few programs.

If you are really lazy, just sign up for an OpenDNS account that blocks the subjects you want blocked at the router.
