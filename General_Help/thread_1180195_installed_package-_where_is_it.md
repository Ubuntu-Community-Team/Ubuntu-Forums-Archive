### Post by michy99 on 2009-06-06
OK. Since it is a panel applet, left click on either your top or bottom panel, wherever you want to put it. Select 'Add to Panel' and see if it is in the list to choose from. After you add it, you can probably click on it to configure.

### Post by Therion on 2009-06-06
> Computer Temperature Monitor is a little **applet for the GNOME desktop** that shows the temperature of your computer CPU and disks on screen.
What you need to do right-click on one of your panels, click on "Add to panel" and then drag and drop the applet onto your panel.  From there, see the screenshots on the website for use and configuration:

[http://computertemp.berlios.de/screenshots.php](http://computertemp.berlios.de/screenshots.php)
