### Post by alan34 on 2008-12-05
Go here

[http://www.compiz-themes.org/](http://www.compiz-themes.org/)

Pick the one(s) you want and download. Do not open them with Emerald Theme Manager save them to a folder in your home directory.

Open up Emerald Theme Manager click the Themes Settings tab and the Themes tab. 

Click the Import button and navigate to where you saved the themes and Open.

You should now see the theme in the window - then double click to activate.
