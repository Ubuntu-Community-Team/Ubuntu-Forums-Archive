### Post by papibe on 2010-12-06
Since most of the copy/image of the root (/) partition needs to be done while the partition is not mounted, most of the most well know tools are "live" tools.

Gparted live is a graphical tools that allow you to copy/resize partitions. Clonezilla (text mode) let you create image of partitions or the whole disk. Since both of them are live tools, you can use them on most systems (Windows/Mac/linux).
