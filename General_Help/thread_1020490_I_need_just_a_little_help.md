### Post by bodhi.zazen on 2008-12-24
wicd replaces network manager.

Follow the directions on the wicd home page and personally I advise you then re-boot.

You then open wicd and enter your WAP information (protocol, password, etc).

I had issues with wireless in 8.04 and wicd fixed the problem. It does not work for everyone.

If wicd fails we need a little more information - wireless card, settings (WPA, WEP, etc).
