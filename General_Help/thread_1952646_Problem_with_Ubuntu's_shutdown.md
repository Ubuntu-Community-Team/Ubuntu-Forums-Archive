### Post by Paddy Landau on 2012-04-05
I take it you installed WUBI on Windows?

Did you recently upgrade Ubuntu, or was there a recent failure in Windows?

Unfortunately, WUBI is rather unstable when it comes to any sort of significant upgrade. I have never managed to recover a WUBI after a serious failure (but search the forums, in case someone else has managed. I searched but failed to find anything).

I would suggest you uninstall WUBI, reboot, and reinstall. **Warning:** Any data on your Ubuntu will be lost. Or, better, a native installation (dual-boot with Windows) would be give you a more reliable experience.
