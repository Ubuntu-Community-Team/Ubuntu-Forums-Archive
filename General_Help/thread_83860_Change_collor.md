### Post by sethmahoney on 2005-10-29
Click on the System menu, Preferences, then Theme.  Click on "Theme Details".  A dialog with three tabs will come up, and "Controls" should already be chosen.  Pick different ones until you find one you like.

You can download more from art.gnome.org or gnome-look.org - to install these, download the theme, open up the theme manager (according to the directions above), and drag the newly-downloaded theme onto the theme manager.  You can then select it the same way you would any other theme.

Good luck!
