### Post by BaffledMollusc on 2008-04-06
> **utah23 said:**
> I recently installed a new motherboard.  How does one go about updating chipset drivers?

Thank you.

You shouldn't need to, unless it's got a particularly weird chipset. Most drivers for linux are included in the kernel and the right ones are loaded on boot. 

You shouldn't have to ever manually install drivers unless you have hardware that's not supported by the kernel - and you'll know if that's the case, since that hardware won't work!
