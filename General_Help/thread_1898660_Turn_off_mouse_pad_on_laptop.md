It's my understanding that there's a command to turn off the little pad on a laptop when you're typing on the keyboard, do avoid messing yourself up while typing.  Does anyone know how to do that?  Any help would be most appreciated.

### Post by BBQdave on 2011-12-21
> **Mazate said:**

**System** > **Preferences** > **Mouse** > **Touchpad** > *Disable touchpad while typing* :)
