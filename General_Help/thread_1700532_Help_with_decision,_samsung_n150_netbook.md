### Post by Timokl on 2011-03-05
I have installed Ubuntu on a Samsung 148 Plus (I think it's the same hardware, but packaged for India and Southeastasia), that I bought without Windows pre-installed. It's an overall good experience, although the Unity desktop that comes pre-installed with the Netbook version of Ubuntu hangs now and then, but no crash up to now.

However, you have to install the [Samsung Tools]("https://launchpad.net/samsung-tools") to get the Fn keys working, and also to turn on or off WiFi, Bluetooth etc.

Other than that, everything works out of the box.
