### Post by Psylon on 2005-03-27
MythTv is packaged in Hoary's Multiverse archive.

So, add multiverse to your sources.list file and use synaptic to install.

See [www.ubuntuguide.org](www.ubuntuguide.org) or search in [www.ubuntulinux.org/wiki](www.ubuntulinux.org/wiki) for help adding multiverse to your sources.list if needed.
