Myself and several other people are experiencing the same issue (as you can see from the [LastPass comments]("https://chrome.google.com/extensions/detail/hdokiejnpimakedhajhdlcegeplioahd") page). It started a couple weeks ago and hasn't been fixed.

According to the comments, a temporary workaround for the moment is to press Alt + I after a page loads. That's the shortcut for LastPass to recheck the page. I haven't tested it yet, but I'm willing to bet that it works.

LastPass will find and fix the problem eventually.
