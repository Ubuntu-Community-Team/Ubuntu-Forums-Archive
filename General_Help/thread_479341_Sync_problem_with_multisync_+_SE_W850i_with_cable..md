### Post by Kevanx on 2007-07-28
I had this problem with the w810i, and what worked for me was to connect and then choose PHONE MODE instead of FILE TRANSFER MODE. Multisync worked fine after that. I'm still having trouble syncing from my box TO the phone, but they are communicating now on ttyACM0 or 1.
