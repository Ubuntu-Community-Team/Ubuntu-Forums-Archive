### Post by ~LoKe on 2007-01-12
Right click -> Create Launcher.  It'll open a window for you to fill in the information.

Type: **Application**
Name: **XLog**
Command: **xlog**
Comment: **XLog** (*you can put whatever you want*)

Click the "Icon" button on the left and navigate to the directory with the icon.  It should already be where it needs to be (/usr/share/pixmaps), but you may have to look for it.  You could also use any icon that you want.
