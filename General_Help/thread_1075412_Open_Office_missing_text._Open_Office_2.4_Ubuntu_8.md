### Post by Limlam on 2009-02-20
I wanted to contribute this fix for the problem of all text missing from Open Office applications. I found a lot of vague answers to this and finally discovered this and thought I would make it clear.
  
Go to System --> Preferences --> Appearance --> tab: Visual Effects and select "None" and give it a few seconds to take effect.
