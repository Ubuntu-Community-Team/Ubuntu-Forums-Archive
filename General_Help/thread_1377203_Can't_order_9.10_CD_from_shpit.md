### Post by Digikid on 2010-01-10
You do not need a burner anymore.  Download a program called Unetbootin.  It will allow you to use a USB Stick for booting and installing.  Ever since I discovered it I have never burned another CD/DVD again for Linux.

It is very easy to use and FREE.

I even wrote a review for it.

[http://www.raiden.net/articles/installing_linux_with_unetbootin/1/](http://www.raiden.net/articles/installing_linux_with_unetbootin/1/)
