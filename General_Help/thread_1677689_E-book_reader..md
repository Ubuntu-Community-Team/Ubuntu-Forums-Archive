### Post by ragtag on 2011-01-29
Calibre is a pretty nice e-book manager and has built in readers for a variety of formats. Including pdf and ePub. It's viewers have full screen mode, but it's not 100% full screen as there are navigation controls along the side. You might want to get the latest version from their site, rather than the one in the Software Center (apt-get), as it updates quite frequently.

For PDFs Evince let's you do full screen and presentation mode.
