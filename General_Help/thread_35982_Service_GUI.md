### Post by Sam on 2005-05-21
[QUOTE=veritas366]Is this bootup manager different from the system monitor (applications>system tools>system monitor)?[/QUOTE]
They do something different. The bootup manager is to manage your /etc/init.d entries (the "services"), and the system monitor is to list your processes, kill them and watch your cpu/mem/disc usage.
