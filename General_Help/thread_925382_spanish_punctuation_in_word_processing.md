### Post by Voynix on 2008-09-20
I find myself switching between different languages, and it is sometimes difficult to remember special characters, accents or symbols. If you add the keyboard indicator applet to the top gnome panel, and right click > show current layout, you can now see where the symbols are (if you have the right layout). The layout is interactive, and you can press the Alt, Shift and other keys to find the right key combination.
