### Post by richbarna on 2006-05-27
On amule, when it is open, click on networks.
In the space that says servers, copy and paste this :-
> [http://ocbmaurice.no-ip.org/slist/uvsxwgvaxj/server.met](http://ocbmaurice.no-ip.org/slist/uvsxwgvaxj/server.met)
Then click the update/download button next to it.
This will sort out the server.met list.

If you want to see different types of server lists go to :-
[ocbmaurice]("http://ocbmaurice.no-ip.org/index.html")
