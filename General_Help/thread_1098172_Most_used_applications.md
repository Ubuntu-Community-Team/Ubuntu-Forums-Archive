### Post by masterkoppa on 2009-03-16
There's no official list to display your most used applications, as in windows, there is though a list of recently used applications. Maybe you might what to request this feature to the KDE team.

I have used though, an application that tracks the amount of time I spend on any application or website. It called [Rescue Time]("http://www.rescuetime.com"). Its a nice free app with a friendly GUI that helps you track you time spent on a computer. Its cross platform and free. This might be more usefull than a more used list because you'll find out what are your most important apps.
