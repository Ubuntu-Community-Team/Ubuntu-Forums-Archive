### Post by Whiffle on 2008-02-20
Amarok is a KDE application, so its fonts are controlled by kde configs.


Settings > Configure Amarok  > Appearance > Use Custom Fonts

Or...


Do..

Alt+F2

kcontrol


There should be a Font section under the appearance menu.
