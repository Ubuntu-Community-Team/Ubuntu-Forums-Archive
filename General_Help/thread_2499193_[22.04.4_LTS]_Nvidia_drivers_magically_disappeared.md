[COLOR=#1A1A1A][FONT=&quot]Ok, I managed to solve the issue. Just FYI I have 2 OS on 1 drive.[/FONT][/COLOR]

[LIST]
[*]Remove nvidia driver by typing
[/LIST]
sudo apt remove --purge nvidia-driver-535 
sudo apt autoremove

[LIST]
[*]Reboot your sytem

[*]Go to Software Updates, and then to Additional Drivers

[*]Select [X.org]("https://x.org/") (last option from the list), apply and reboot

[*]Go to Additional Drives again and select your preferred (tested) one - in my case it was the first option, apply and reboot


--------------------------------
[/LIST]
