It is not easy though, since the fluff accumulates on the inside and most laptop PCs are well nigh impossible to open without breaking a cable connector or two - usually the keyboard or trackpad that suffers.  In desperation I have on occasion resorted to cutting open the bottom of the case around the heat sink and fan with a Dremel and cutting wheel, rather than attempting to disassemble the machine - then eventually epoxy glued the cut piece back again.
