### Post by el murciélago veloz on 2008-02-02
Hi, i am a 7.10 Ubuntu user and I've noticed that when you are seeing a video (google or you tube video) in the temporal directory (/tmp) a copy of the video is running. It will remain there (in /tmp folder) until you close the video page, so if you access to the /tmp folder you can save the video to another folder you want.
P.S. Please notice that in temporal folder the video has a "bizarre" name like "FlashsJfnKq" or something similar.
