### Post by rubinstein on 2006-10-27
There are several workarounds; the workaround which works for me on GNOME is to go to System - Preferences - Fonts (or something similar) and choose subpixel smoothing, and in the Details dialog choose full hinting. That should work for Firefox and other programs.
