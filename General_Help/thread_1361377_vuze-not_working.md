### Post by lovinglinux on 2009-12-22
What do you mean by "it doesn't work"? You can't even launch the application or are you having trouble downloading? 

If you are getting download problems, then see [BitTorrent optimization and troubleshooting guide](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1259923).

If the application doesn't start I would recommend another Bittorrent client like Deluge or Ktorrent, for Gnome and KDE respectively. They work great and have all the important features. Vuze is a powerful application, but it has too many configuration options that normally I don't use and it is bloated. But most important, it uses Java, which makes it a very resource demanding application.
