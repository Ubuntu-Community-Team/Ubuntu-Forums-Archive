### Post by coffeecat on 2009-11-06
> **bostonaholic said:**
> I just upgraded to Karmic and FF upgraded to 3.5.4 with it.  I love the new(?) font that is being used for the tabs and menus.  How can I find out what font this is?  It's really clean and pronounced it seems.

The tabs and menus font in Firefox is what is set in System > Preferences > Appearance > Fonts > Application Fonts. As far as I know the default font in Karmic is the same as in Jaunty. But if it looks fresher and cleaner to you, it's probably because font rendering is better in Karmic than in Jaunty. Except that this depends on your video card and driver. The quality of font rendering can be dramatically changed with different card/driver combinations.

What video card are you using?
