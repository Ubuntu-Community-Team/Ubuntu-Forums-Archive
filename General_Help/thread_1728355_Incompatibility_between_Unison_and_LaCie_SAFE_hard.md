### Post by quadra on 2011-04-13
Hi there

Has anyone tested Unison for synchronizing data to an external SAFE hard drive from Lacie? Lacie SAFE drives use a fingerprint sensor to encrypt data on the drive.

When testing, Unison reported the error "File modified during synchronization" for about 20%-50% of the files.

Confirmed with two different models of Lacie SAFE drives (one bought in 2006, the other in 2011).

I had no time to investigate further. **I just post this here so that other affected users may confirm or add more detail.**
