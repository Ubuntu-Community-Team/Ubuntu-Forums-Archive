### Post by ianmillington on 2012-08-01
Mozilla have announced that they will continue with security updates. There may well be some community development too but I'm doubtful as to how much further one can develop the standalone e-mail client before change for changes sake simply introduces unnecessary bloat. If you are happy with what you have got then I would suggest you don't panic.

I note you use kubuntu. FWIW I am currently trialling kontact (which incorporates kmail)alongside Thunderbird and Lightning. Kmail works rather well with imap. I did have issues with korganizer (the calendar component) accessing the google calendar but it now works.

See this thread

[http://www.kubuntuforums.net/showthread.php?58438-How-to-Make-Google-Calendar-work-with-Kontact](http://www.kubuntuforums.net/showthread.php?58438-How-to-Make-Google-Calendar-work-with-Kontact)
