### Post by Ron Petch on 2010-08-10
I managed to compile a i686 hernel with Gentoo and transfer it to Ubuntu. (I found the Ubuntu method very confusing.)
copy kernel to /boot
copy linux-<version> to /usr/src
insert commands from Gentoo grub.cfg in the "DO NOT EDIT THIS FILE" Ubunutu grub.cfg, adjusting partitions and header,of course
Change xorg.conf from nvidia to vesa.
Boot.
Reload nvidia .
Reboot.
uname -a will report smp and i686.
