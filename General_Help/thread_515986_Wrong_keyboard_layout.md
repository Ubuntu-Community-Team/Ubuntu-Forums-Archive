### Post by DeclanMcErlane on 2007-08-02
Hey, yeah go to System -> Preferences -> Keyboard. Then click the layout tab. Now from what you have said I take it you have a US English keyboard layout so what you have to do is select it from the "Selected Layouts" list and click "Remove". Now I'm also guessing you want a UK English layout so click on "Add", scroll down to and click United Kingdom, then click "OK". That should solve your problems. Post back if you still need some help.
