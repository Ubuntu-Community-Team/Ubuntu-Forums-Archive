### Post by mikewhatever on 2010-10-21
.tar is an archive, similar to .rar or .zip in Windows. You don't install, but extract it, by right clicking and selecting 'Extract here'. Once extracted, you should get a folder with a bunch of debian packages which can be installed.
[Canon MX340 howto]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1518425")
