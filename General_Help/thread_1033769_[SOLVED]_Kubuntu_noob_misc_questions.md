I am new to (K)Ubuntu 8.10 and I have the following questions. I would greatly appreciate if someone would answer them. 

1. When I have a number of windows open, all the windows show up in the panel (at the bottom) in all desktops. However I only want to see the windows that I opened in this particular desktop, and not the ones that I opened in other desktops. How can I do this?

### Post by gettinoriginal on 2009-01-11
Problem 1, choose an empty workspace, right click the "window list applet" and select preferences, then select "show windows from current workspace"  :p

Problem 2, right click on all your "widgets/applets" on the panel, make sure they are unlocked, then one at a time, right click each again and choose move, and put them where you please.  Just remember, if one is locked to the panel, nothing will move past it until it is unlocked.  :p

P.S. I do use ubuntu, not kubuntu, so hope everything works the same. :p
