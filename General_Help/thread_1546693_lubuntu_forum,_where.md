### Post by phillw on 2010-08-05
Hi, as previously noted, lubuntu tags are used on here. (Thank you the forum management team for that).

As it is a young project it is possible for postings to vanish under the general level of postings. I do try to get on here to check on them, but it is not always possible.
There is an IRC area for lubuntu, the details are at [IRC Lubuntu](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Lubuntu/DocumentationHelp#How%20to%20get%20help)

The wiki area with documentation, how to get in touch etc. is at [Lubuntu Wiki](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Lubuntu) (Also in my Signature). My take on lubuntu is an area where we can discuss things not fully to do with lubuntu (outside of the target audience) and where we can prepare and check documentation prior to adding it to the Wiki area.

[LXDE Forum](http://forum.lxde.org/) is an area for discussing things that are unique to the LXDE  system which lubuntu uses on top of the 'buntu' part.

Regards,

Phill.

By the Way, Welcome to Lubuntu :-D
