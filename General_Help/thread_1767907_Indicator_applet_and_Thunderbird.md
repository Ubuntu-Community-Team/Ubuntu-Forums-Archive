### Post by timgood on 2011-05-26
There is a fabulous app called Popper which you can download [here]("https://edge.launchpad.net/popper"). It integrates with system tray and will notify you of waiting email *without having to have a running instance of Thunderbird or Evolution*. It can be simply configured to launch Thunderbird, or any email client, when it is clicked on. Superb stuff, IMHO.
