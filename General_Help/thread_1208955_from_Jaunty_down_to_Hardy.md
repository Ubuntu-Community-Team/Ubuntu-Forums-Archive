### Post by zvacet on 2009-07-09
Check [md5sum]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HowToMD5SUM") and if it is correct burn iso on lower possible speed and check [disc integrity.]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Installation/CDIntegrityCheck?action=show&redirect=CDIntegrityCheck")If everything goes well you are ready to install.If you don´t have separate home right now on Jaunty [make it.]("http://www.ivankuznetsov.com/2008/04/moving-home-to-its-own-partition.html")Afte that install Hardy on top of Jaunty root partition.**Don´t format home partition during install.**
