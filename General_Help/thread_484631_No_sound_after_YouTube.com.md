### Post by SuperMike on 2007-06-27
Yeah, that's how it works on Linux, I've found. You get sound in one and then not the other, or vice-versa. You have to shut one down and only run one sound-driver-grabbing app at a time. And if sound doesn't work in your browser because of this, then shut down any other sound app, shut down your browser, and get back into it again.

If you'd like this to be different, perhaps you could suggest it on Launchpad? Perhaps there's a receptive ear for you there. I think the driver is called ALSA, if I'm not mistaken?
