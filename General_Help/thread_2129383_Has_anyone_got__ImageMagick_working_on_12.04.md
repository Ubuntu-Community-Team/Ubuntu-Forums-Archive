### Post by CaptainMark on 2013-03-27
It depends what you want to do, all I've ever used it for is converting picture files between formats easily, I know it can do a whole load of things though when it comes to editing photos but its command line only so you can't view photos with it,
type:```
man imagemagick
```for more information on the over all toolset, you can also do ```
man convert
```to get a manual for the convert command and so on for the other commands
