### Post by Gannin on 2006-11-26
System > Administration > Synaptic Package Manager

Type in your password, hit enter, then click on Search, enter "rp-pppoe" and hit enter.

Click on rp-pppoe in the results, choose "Mark for Removal", and then click on Apply.
