### Post by CrusaderAD on 2009-10-23
Here's a few things I did and it made a HUGE difference. Disabled Assistive Technologies in System > Preferences > Assistive Technologies and changed my theme back to one of the generic ones. I also removed some the the columns displayed in Nautilus. Much much faster.
