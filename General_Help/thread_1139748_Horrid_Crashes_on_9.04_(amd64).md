### Post by linuxvacuum on 2009-04-27
Hi NekoMaster, I hate it when no one replies to my posts so I had to reply to yours :)

I made the switch from 32 bits to 64 bits this week and I never experienced crashing at all. There is also a big performance increase. Browsers still crash but that's caused by adobe's flash plugin. I think you should try testing your memory sticks with the option in your grub menu. When everything crashes, it's almost always a hardware problem.

You can try starting the program that crashes the most often from a terminal, so you'll be able to analyze the text output.
