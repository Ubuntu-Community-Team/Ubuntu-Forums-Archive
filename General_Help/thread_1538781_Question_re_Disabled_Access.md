### Post by Tamlynmac on 2010-07-25
You might consider using the on screen keyboard, which I believe is located under system/preferences/onboard.

It places a full size software keyboard on top of the application and is fully functional. This would permit typing numbers by simply selecting the number on the on screen keyboard (mouse or trackball).

Not certain that is specifically what your looking for,  by I often use it on my mini and it works great.

Good Luck & I hope this helps.
