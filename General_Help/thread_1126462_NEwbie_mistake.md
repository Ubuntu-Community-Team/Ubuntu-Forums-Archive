### Post by silentrebel on 2009-04-15
-Find those applications in the Applications menu.
-Click and drag each in turn, either to the Desktop or to the upper panel.

-If in the previous step you dragged them to the Desktop, now click and drag the shortcuts on your desktop to the upper panel.
