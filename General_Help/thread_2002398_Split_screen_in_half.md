### Post by megabuffen on 2012-06-12
For some reason my graphics card will display two monitors as one. This means that windows will maximize across both monitors, which is inconvenient to say the least.

Is there any way to make ubuntu 12.04 use the two halves of this virtual monitor as two separate ones?

---

### Post by amsaha on 2012-09-06
Try "CNTRL + WindowsKey + LeftArrow" to make any window take left half of the desktop space and "... + RightArrow" to make any window take right half of the desktop.

More shortcuts can be found at: [https://docs.google.com/document/d/1MYNyM2E7E40jsQ0Sxw0MOjLPox4CGpSQEgc5WK4DM8M/edit#heading=h.cxpz2zijb42](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1MYNyM2E7E40jsQ0Sxw0MOjLPox4CGpSQEgc5WK4DM8M/edit#heading=h.cxpz2zijb42)
