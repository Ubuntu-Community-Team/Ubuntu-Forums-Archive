### Post by timgood on 2011-05-04
Hi: I'm afraid there is no way to 'roll back' an installation. But if you back up your home directory (Deja Dup is a very good way to do this, and can be installed from the Software Centre) you can re-install 10.10 and then recover your old desktop.
