### Post by doclivingston on 2005-08-25
You can use special keys, such as multimedia keys or others, by setting them up in Preferences->Keyboard Shortcuts. I think XMMS may need a plugin to use the multimedia keys though (they shuld work with totem/rhythmbox/muine/amarok/pretty much everything else).
