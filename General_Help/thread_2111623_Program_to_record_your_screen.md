### Post by deadflowr on 2013-02-02
If your intent is to record games, then the suggestion of post #2 of using command line is probably the best solution, as limiting the various graphical resources will keep the game streaming well.

But, if having to choose a decent screen recorder, definitely go with kazam. It's far easier to set up to record from the monitor(the sound from your system) than recordmydesktop, and not as overly option heavy as vlc.
