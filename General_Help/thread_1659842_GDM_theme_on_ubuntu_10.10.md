### Post by Vaphell on 2011-01-04
yes, GDM used to be highly customizable but because gnome recently went through a major rewrite in that area, that customizability is no more. Now it boots much faster but a lot of functionality has not been regained yet (if ever) and people who liked having a login screen eyecandy are left in the cold.
