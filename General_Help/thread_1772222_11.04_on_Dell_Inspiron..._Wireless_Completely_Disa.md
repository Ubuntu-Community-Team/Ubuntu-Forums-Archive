I have an Inspiron 8600 with Ubuntu 11.04; I know your problem you need to

sudo apt-get firmware-b43-installer

or

sudo apt-get firmware-b43legacy-installer

depending on your wireless card. My only problem is, I need b43 legacy, but Ubuntu won't let me install it, because it sees my ethernet controller and thinks it's the wireless card, then tells me that I need b43.
