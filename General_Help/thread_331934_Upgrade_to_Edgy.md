### Post by cmost on 2007-01-05
If you can, use AIGLX as it has better performance and you won't have the issue of having to turn it off to play openGL games.  Edgy has AIGLX enabled by default in its xorg.conf.  If you have an nVidia card, you may also consider using the 'Envy' script to upgrade to 9631 (the latest version of the driver) and run Beryl or Compiz directly that way.  The latest version of the nVidia driver is capable of compositing without the need for either XGL or AIGLX.  If you give more details about your video card, I may be able to assist you in setting this up.
