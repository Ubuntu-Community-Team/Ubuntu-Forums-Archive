### Post by DieselBaz on 2006-09-30
After much searchingon the internet ( with no luck ](*,) )and much trial and error, I finally stumbled onto  the cause of my dilemma. My computers' screen resolution was set at 832 x624 pixels, which I had selected as the appropriate screen resolution after the installation of Ubuntu 6.06, up from 800 x 600 pixels for Windows XP.
  I have since set the screen resolution to 1024 x 768 pixels to resolve another issue with Firefox - web pages were not fitting into the browser window. out of curiosity, I opened Open Office Writer and found that the font colour selection button was displayed on the task bar and that it actually worked. :D 
  I guess that Ubuntu is designed to work best at these higher screen resolutions.:confused: :-k .
  just thought that I'd post my findings in case someone else out there encounters a similar problem. I'm no computer guru, and an absolute beginner with Ubuntu / Linux.
