### Post by hawthornso23 on 2011-06-25
Edges work for me on my natty laptop. I don't know why they don't work for you.

After deciding to go back to the classic desktop I had to indulge in a rather massive compiz reconfiguration session to get everything working again.  It was probably something I did back then.  

Check your compiz plugins and make sure you haven't got any unity related plugins enabled. Unity steals the edge focus to do things like pop up its launcher, and I seem to recall there is some sort of snap to edge feature as well. As unity doesn't play nice with the rest of compiz, if anything like that is still enabled it could well cause the kind of problem you describe.

Sorry I can't be more specific. 

Good Luck.
