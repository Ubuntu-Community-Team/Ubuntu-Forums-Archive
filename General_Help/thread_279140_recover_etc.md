### Post by kuja on 2006-10-17
Why is formatting the partition not an option? Anyway, the safest way is to backup all of the data that you actually need across a network to another computer, or to some other medium (flash drives, external hard drives, and optical media are all options), and then to reinstall. Recovering deleted folders isn't quite so easy or safe afterall. In fact, I've not really found a way to do it for linux filesystems, though I know that it is (at least theoretically) possible.
