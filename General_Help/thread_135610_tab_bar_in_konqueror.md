### Post by Jucato on 2006-02-24
Settings > Configure Konqueror > Web Behavior > Tabbed Browsing section
But hiding the tab bar only works when your window has only one tab open. You could also configure (in the same section) to just always open new windows instead of new tabs. Except that, resource-wise, it's more efficient to have 5 tabs opened than 5 windows. :D
