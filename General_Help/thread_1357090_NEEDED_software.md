### Post by wojox on 2009-12-17
Live radar, right click the top panel and select Weather Report.

Then right click that and select preferences.

Click location and add your hometown or surrounding area.

Go back to General and click enable radar map. Check Use custom address. 

Go on the net and find a radar you like and right click to save image location and paste it in the address. For example I'm from Florida so I use the NOAA website and this link [Tampa Bay]("http://radar.weather.gov/ridge/lite/N0R/TBW_loop.gif")
