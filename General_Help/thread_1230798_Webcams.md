### Post by physic.dude on 2009-08-03
You should try Cheese first since Ubuntu can recognize most webcams automatically.
 
As I too was once a n00b at Linux, the easy way to install Cheese is to Go to **Applications > Add/Remove, **then search for **cheese, **(Make sure "All Open Source applications" is selected in the Show: menu)click the checkbox next to the program, the box should be checked, then click apply changes (You might need to restart.)
 
I hope this is what you are looking for??
