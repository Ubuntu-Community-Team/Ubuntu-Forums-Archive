Super button is the one that, in most keyboards, has a windows logo. (The original logo for this key was a diamond, used in some ancient Unix systems.)

Mouse button 5 would be scroll whell up or down, I can't really remember which way they go.

button 1 is the left mouse button, button 2 is middle button (scroll wheel click in most modern mice), button 3 is right mouse button and buttons 4 & 5 are scroll wheel up&down. Higher numbers would be any extra buttons your mouse might have.
