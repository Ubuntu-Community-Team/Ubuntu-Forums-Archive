### Post by Iandefor on 2009-03-29
Regarding DVD ripping: 

[Handbrake]("http://handbrake.fr/?article=9") rips like a charm for me on OSX and they added an official Linux GUI with the last release. It's pretty simple, too.

With the whole compiz thing: I suggest just turning it off if you're not that attached to it. There's a tab for it in the Appearances preference panel (System->Preferences->Appearances). I've generally found it behaves strangely and is just not quick enough to justify the coolness of having minimize and maximize animations.
