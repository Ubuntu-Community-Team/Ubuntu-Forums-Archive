### Post by ajgreeny on 2008-07-10
I suspect your "bland" look is simply that you do not have the system setup to take advantage of what is possible.  However, you can add **kubuntu** to **ubuntu** simply by using sudo apt-get install kubuntu-desktop or also by using synaptic; there is no need to reinstall your system.
To get the best from compiz and all the desktop effects have a look at [this]("http://forlong.blogage.de/article/2007/8/29/How-to-set-up-Compiz-Fusion") page which Forlong has put together with lots of good suggestions.
