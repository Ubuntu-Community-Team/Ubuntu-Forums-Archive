### Post by Uubnewb on 2009-02-01
As for the wallpapers not loading: I had the same problem, and although it's not serious, it's annoying.

To fix it, make sure all the wallpapers you want to use is on the same harddrive/partition as your Ubuntu installation.  Ubuntu has a "Pictures" directory by default.  I reccomend you put said pictures in there.  Otherwise you need to mount the drive with the pictures before the pictures can be used.
