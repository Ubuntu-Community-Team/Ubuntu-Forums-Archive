> **Matrix01 said:**
> i am on xubuntu power manager.
when lap top lid is closed 

nothing......i know this,
will u tell me what these actions are?

lock screen
hybernate.....
suspend....

lock screen means it will ask for your password to log back on

hibernate will turn off the computer but save the current state of open apps etc... for when you restart it (you need a pretty big swap file for this, at least 1.5x the system RAM)

suspend will keep the computer running but greatly reduce power consumption
