### Post by digby on 2006-07-01
VMware isn't really an appropriate platform to play games.  Because it's all running on top of linux, nothing will ever function (hardware wise) in windows that won't work in linux.  If you want to play games, you'll need to use wine/cedega or dual-boot.  To the best of my knowledge, there aren't really any other options at this point.
