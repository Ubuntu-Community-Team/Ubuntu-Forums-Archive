### Post by claracc on 2014-08-19
I think you are referring to workspaces, which seem to be disabled by default from ubuntu 13.04.

Please, read this link: [http://askubuntu.com/questions/260510/how-do-i-turn-on-workspaces-why-do-i-only-have-one-workspace](http://askubuntu.com/questions/260510/how-do-i-turn-on-workspaces-why-do-i-only-have-one-workspace) where there are various ways to recover the four workspaces, the gui one seems to be the easiest.

---

### Post by Cliff_Simonds on 2014-08-19
system settings->Appearence->Behaviour->enable workspaces
