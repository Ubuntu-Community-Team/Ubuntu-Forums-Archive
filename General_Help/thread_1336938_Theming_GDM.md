### Post by jacobs444 on 2009-11-25
Theming gdm is as easy as changing the images in /usr/share/images/xsplash. 

The only thing that isn't themed is the part with the usernames. 

If you replace those images remember to use the same resolution as the original, to ensure compatability. I for one used gimp to colorize the throbber, and changed the ubuntu logo to my user name. Upon initial look it lookslike there is nothing in some of the images, though if you selsect them with your mouse, you will see the ubiquitous ubuntu logo.
