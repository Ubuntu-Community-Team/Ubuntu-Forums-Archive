### Post by pirog on 2009-04-27
righty,

here is how to assign keys through XEV: [https://wiki.kubuntu.org/KDEMultimediaKeys]("https://wiki.kubuntu.org/KDEMultimediaKeys")

i finally realised how to get to the keyboard short-cuts in system settings. now, to see the the keyboard settings menu, one needs to enable Classic Menu (right-click onto K main menu symbol in the left bottom corner and select Classic Menu). then go System -> System Settings, search for keyboard shortcuts or language settings, depending on what you want.
when you are done, to change back to Kickoff Menue Style, just right-click K Menu and then choose Kickoff menu.

hope it helps,

Vlad.
