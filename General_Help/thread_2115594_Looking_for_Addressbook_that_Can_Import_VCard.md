### Post by jakfish on 2013-02-16
I did look into Evolution and eventually decided to go with Thunderbird, even though I use Claws for email.  If you mess with the Thunderbird .desktop and put in "Exec=thunderbird -addressbook" you'll bring up the addressbook immediately, without email check, etc.

Couple that with its add-on "Google Contacts" (a synching extension that works only when you start Thunderbird proper), I can keep things up to date.

Would've preferred something not so large, but Thunderbird runs faster than I remembered and I've always liked the layout of a mozilla addressbook.

But in context to the original post, I never did find a stand-alone addressbook that could do Google vcard.
