### Post by pinksoviet on 2008-04-23
While I know little about Ubuntu in general, this may very well be much simpler than that.

Roughly how old is your computer? If it's fairly old, you may need to replace the battery on your motherboard that keeps your system clock working. If the battery is running low, your computer will lose track of time, which seems to be the problem you're experiencing.

So just read your motherboard's manual, or the support website from which you bought your computer, and look up the exact battery you need for your motherboard. Purchase the specified battery, and insert it into your motherboard after you removed the old one.
