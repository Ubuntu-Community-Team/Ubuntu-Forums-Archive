### Post by aclaunch on 2005-12-09
I have been having a problem with connecting to my Linux box (running Ubuntu Breezy) from my Powerbook running OS X 10.4.3. After several days of trying lots of different things I found this thread:[http://www.macworld.com/forums/ubbthreads/showflat.php?Board=UBB12&Number=351250](http://www.macworld.com/forums/ubbthreads/showflat.php?Board=UBB12&Number=351250)
Apparently the current version of Samba 3.0.14 does not work well with Mac OS X. I updated my Samba from the Dapper repositories to 3.0.20 and now everything is well.
