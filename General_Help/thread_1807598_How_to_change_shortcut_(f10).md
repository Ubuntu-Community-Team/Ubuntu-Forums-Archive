### Post by cipherboy_loc on 2011-07-19
I don't believe there is a way to change the shortcuts in Unity other than downloading the source, editing it, and then recompiling it. You could, how ever, try logging into the Ubuntu (Classic) option (before entering your password, change where it says Ubuntu in the drop down menu at the bottom to Ubuntu Classic). This will take you to the old interface, which doesn't have Unity.
