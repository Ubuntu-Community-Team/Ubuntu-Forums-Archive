### Post by howefield on 2012-03-01
For the homepage, try the Edit > Preferences menu and set your homepage as required. 

I'd expect you can remove the google toolbar the from Firefox > Tools > Addons menu.

---

### Post by Lars Noodén on 2012-03-01
If you click on the Google logo in the search box you can then choose "Manage Search Engines..." in the menu.  There you can add or remove engines.
