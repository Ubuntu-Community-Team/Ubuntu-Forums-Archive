Pardon me for asking but what version of Ubuntu are you using? I ask because in 14.04 we can go to System Settings>Appearance>behaviour tab and find a section called "Show the menus for a window" and also two check boxes - In the menu bar - in the window's title bar. I did not think that this feature was present in Ubuntu 12.04 but may be I am wrong. May be it is in 12.04.4.

Also it is possible to increase or decrease the width of the side panel just by grabbing the slider. Perhaps you in the past have change the width and now the update has brought in a nautilus upgrade that reverted nautilus to its default settings. The image you show is not of the 14.04 nautilus.
