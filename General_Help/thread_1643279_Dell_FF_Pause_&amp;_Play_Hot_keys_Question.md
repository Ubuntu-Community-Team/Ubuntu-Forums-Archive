### Post by ender4 on 2010-12-11
In Amarok go to Settings->Configure Shortcuts, find the command you want to make a shortcut for, click the arrow for Global (or just shortcut if you don't want to make a global shortcut), check custom, click the custom button, then press the media key you want to use.
