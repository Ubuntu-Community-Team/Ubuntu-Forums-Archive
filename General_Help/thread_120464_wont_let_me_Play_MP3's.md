Ok, first thing is, you need ot know what a codec is. Basically its a 'addon' of some sort that allows your media player ot play certain file types...in your case prob wma and mp3.

I also had this problem. I suggest you d/l automatix...let it do its thing, and choose "install common codecs' or osmething like that...also you might wanan try getting "media players" form automatix as well. I chose both, they work fine. I can play mp3s and movie files and other file types now. Yay.

[Click here for automatix]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=66563&highlight=automatix")

then follow the instructions by going to the terminal in apps-->accesories

note: you might have to do sudo apt-get update in the terminal after your'e done installing and stuff to get updates
