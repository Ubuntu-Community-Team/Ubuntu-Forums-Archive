### Post by WinterRain on 2010-04-15
Sometimes deleting your .mozilla file can help. Open your home folder and do Ctrl-H. You will now see folders beginning with a "."

But first, (if you don't want to lose your bookmarks and other settings) rename the original .mozilla folder .mozilla2 or something. Then close firefox and restart it.
