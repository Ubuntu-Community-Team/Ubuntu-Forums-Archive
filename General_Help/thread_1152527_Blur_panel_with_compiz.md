### Post by CatKiller on 2009-05-08
AFAIK, proper transparency on the Gnome panel doesn't yet work (although other panels are available) but you can make it blurry with Compiz' Blur plugin. Enable Focus Blur, and put "| dock" at the end of the line that says which types of Window it should apply the blur effect to.
