### Post by edm1 on 2011-05-04
Confusingly System Settings is located in the session menu in the top right hand corner, that should take care of 1+3. The clock should already be white on black, not sure whats happening there. There is no way to move the panel and launcher at the moment, this is one of the biggest gripes people have with unity.

If you're not getting on with unity you can select Ubuntu Classic from the login screen after clicking your username to revert to the old version.

IMHO if your install is running well and without issues then it is worth giving Unity a trial run.

My own initial reaction was "WTF! I want Gnome back", but faced with redoing all my config of the cube and Docky etc I prevaricated. That gave me a chance to get used to Unity, and I'm actually growing to like it.

That "where the hell is...?" thing is probably the most frustrating bit, but you get past it pretty quickly.
