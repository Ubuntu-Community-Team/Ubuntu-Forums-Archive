Simple question.  I prefer VLC over Totem.  How do I make VLC the default application that plays videos and music when I click on them?

---

### Post by meng on 2007-01-30
Right click on a file with the required extension (.avi, .mpg, whatever):
Properties > Open With
(repeat for each extension type)
