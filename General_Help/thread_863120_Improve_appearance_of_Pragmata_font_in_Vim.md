### Post by bidigo on 2008-07-18
For those who like [Pragmata]("http://www.fsd.it/fonts/pragma.htm"):

On my Ubuntu Hardy, Vim renders Pragmata in standard 8 and 9pt sizes very blurry.

However, if you play a bit with the float size of the font, you can get a lot nicer and sharper rendering. For me, the best results are at 8.5-8.8pt size (8.9 is also ok, but the line spacing is a bit too wide for my taste). There is a huge difference in the quality of rendering between 8.5-8.9 and 9pt sizes.

Hope this helps a few of you.
