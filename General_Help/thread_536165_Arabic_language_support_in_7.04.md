### Post by bobbybobington on 2007-08-27
Well I'm not exactly an expert, but I would think that you can add keyboard layouts for languages through the keyboard Preferences. This can be found in System>Preferences>Keyboard . Once you have the Keyboard Preferences window open select the Layouts tab and then click on Add.. This should give you several options for the types of layout for arabic. and then its just a matter of finding the one that suits you best. Hope this helps you out.
