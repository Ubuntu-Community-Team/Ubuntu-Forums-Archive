### Post by Innova on 2011-12-15
I recently did a clean install of 11.10 on my Asus laptop.  I got it all all my software installed and the catalyst drivers.  I then did an update (there were about 260 packages that needed updating from the original install).  After rebooting it hangs on the purple screen just saying ubuntu with the 5 red dots below).

I have tried ctrl-alt-f1, and it does nothing.  I also tried to hit esc during the boot to turn of the splash and quiet, but the purple screen comes up right after my bios.

How do I figure out what is going on?

### Post by Innova on 2011-12-15
Finally got it figured out, it was the video drivers.

I booted into text mode (removed "quiet" and "splash" from grub line, added "text").  When I did this I was able to see the last boot message as checking battery status.  Did some searching and found out that is most likely a X/Video driver problem.

I then followed the instructs for reinstalling my ATI catalyst drivers:

[http://wiki.cchtml.com/index.php/Ubuntu_Oneiric_Installation_Guide#Installing_Proprietary_Drivers_a.k.a._Catalyst.2Ffglrx](http://wiki.cchtml.com/index.php/Ubuntu_Oneiric_Installation_Guide#Installing_Proprietary_Drivers_a.k.a._Catalyst.2Ffglrx)
