### Post by Maverickprowls on 2007-09-25
If you mean data-recovery there are companies out there that will disassemble your old HDD and retrieve data from it, but given the high-specificity of PGP keys, it's unlikely that you'll get an uncorrupted file back, so you old key wouldn't work.  The whole point of PGP, as I'm sure you know, is that it's almost impossible to crack, so I recommend keeping a safety copy of your secret keys somewhere physically remote from your PC.  Anything you encrypted with a lost key is essentially gone forever, sorry.

There's a great introductory guide to PGP [here]("http://www.andrebacard.com/pgp.html")
