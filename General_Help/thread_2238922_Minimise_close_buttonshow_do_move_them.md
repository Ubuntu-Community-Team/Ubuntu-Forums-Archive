### Post by vasa1 on 2014-08-10
In Lubuntu, those "buttons" are handled by Openbox, Lubuntu's window manager. I'm not sure that Ubuntu Tweak or any other Unity-specific app will do the job.

OP should look at Openbox Configuration Manager: obconf from the terminal or Menu, Preferences, Openbox Configuration Manager. In the image, I've used IMCL. So, IM and C are to the left of the title. Using LIMC would put the controls to the right.

You will see an "animation" as you play around. In the image, the three black boxes in the title bar are the buttons.
