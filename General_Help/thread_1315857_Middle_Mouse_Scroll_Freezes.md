### Post by Bulletproofmask on 2010-11-06
For anyone still suffering from this problem:

I had been using the Firefox add-on called All In One Gestures as a way to reduce the frequency and severity of this issue.  Yesterday I upgraded from 10.4 to 10.10 and decided to check whether any progress had been made in this case.

After disabling All In One Gestures and reenabling the standard Firefox middle mouse scroll option in about:config the problem seems to have been fixed.  I am not sure that this was due to the upgrade to Maverick, or if it had been addressed in a previous update, but in any case I am no longer affected by this bug.
