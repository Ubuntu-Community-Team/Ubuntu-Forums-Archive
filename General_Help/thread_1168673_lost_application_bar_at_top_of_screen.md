### Post by Kareeser on 2009-05-24
Right click the bottom panel, and click "New Panel". Pressing alt, click the panel and drag it up to the top.

Right click that panel, click "Add to Panel", and add the following from left to right:
Main Menu
Notification Area
Indicator Applet
Volume Control
User Switcher
