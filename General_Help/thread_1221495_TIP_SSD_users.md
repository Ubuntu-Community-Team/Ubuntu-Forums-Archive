### Post by bear24rw on 2009-07-23
If your BIOS has the option between the SATA modes IDE and AHCI make sure you choose AHCI. When i first installed hdparm -t told me 70MB/s which was realllyy low for my OCZ SSD after changing to AHCI my speeds are blazing at 192MB/s. Just a tip ;)
