### Post by zbeckerd on 2010-05-19
Search "rollback to an earlier state for Virtualbox" in your favorite search engine.  I found many answers.  Your question though leaves me puzzled.  To rollback you need to have made a "snapshot" of the OS you are working with.  If you need to rollback to before you installed the OS, just delete the OS.  As a last resort, the manual for virtualbox is quite good.
