### Post by yanik on 2005-12-19
You didn't get the point.

Connect to server means accessing a share on another computer.  You can then upload or download files from/to the remote computer.

A share isn't by any mean a streaming server.  You need a streaming server to stream media.  There are plenty out there. 

I use gnump3d to stream my mp3s and movies on my LAN.  Works quite well ans has a web interface.

[http://doc.gwos.org/index.php/Gnump3DServer](http://doc.gwos.org/index.php/Gnump3DServer)
