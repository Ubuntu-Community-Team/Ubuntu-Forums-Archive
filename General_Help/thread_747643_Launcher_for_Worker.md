### Post by drs305 on 2008-04-06
You should be able to right click on your panel, select Add to Panel, and your installed app should appear on the list in the appropriate category. I tried installing worker and then looking it up as above but couldn't find it. Therefore, I selected Add to Panel and then Custom Application Launcher. You can name it anything and in the command line I just entered  worker  and that seemed to work. I don't know if there are switches associated with that program but you could include them in the command as well.

For adding a shortcut on the Desktop, you would right click, Create Launcher, and basically do the same procedure.
