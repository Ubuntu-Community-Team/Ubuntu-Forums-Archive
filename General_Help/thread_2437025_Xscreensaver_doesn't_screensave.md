### Post by Dennis N on 2020-02-17
> I'm guessing that the blank screen is the normal Ubuntu behaviour. How do I get this normal screen blanking to stop and get the Xscreensaver to start?
Check Settings > Power Section > Power Saving > Blank Screen 
This should be set to 'Never'

Reboot and see if it comes on at the desired time. I assume you've gone through the Xscreensaver preferences dialog to set the screensaver and time?
