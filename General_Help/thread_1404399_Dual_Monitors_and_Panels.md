### Post by DanSchnell on 2010-02-11
I've got the dual monitors seutp correctly and have the restricted drivers installed.  Using TwinView, I would like to add a panel to my second monitor, it seems pretty bare right now.  How can I move/create a panel on the second screen?

---

### Post by scouser73 on 2010-02-11
**1** - Right click on a panel & select New Panel.
**2** - Right click on newly created panel, select Properties & uncheck Expand.
**3** - Place cursor on newly created panel and drag to second monitor.
**4** - Right click on panel you have just moved, click Properies and select Expand & finally place it where you like on the screen.
