### Post by wylfing on 2006-04-12
The minimum you need is very, very little. But a quality experience does need a touch more horsepower. In my experience, high screen resolutions (e.g., 1280x1024) will make your system feel sluggish on somewhat older hardware. For example: a 266 MHz FSB Athlon XP 2000 will feel like you're moving through water at high resolutions. Bump that up to a 333 MHz FSB Athlon XP 2800 and it'll feel just fine -- and that's still pretty old (and cheap) hardware.

Anything more modern than those specs will probably be lightning fast. You don't need a huge amount of memory, but why not get it when 512 MB is less than USD40.
