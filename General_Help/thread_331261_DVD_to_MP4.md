### Post by dannyboy79 on 2007-01-04
there is already a solution posted for this within this forum. just search for rip dvd's, then there is another thread that will convert the .mpg into the .mp4. I don't think you can do it all in 1 process but I can't say that for sure. the search function will find all your answers! good luck! basically it involves ffmpeg, mencoder, kino, dvdrip, devede and others.

edit:
in fact, i just did a search, heres to rip the dvd: [http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=327762&highlight=rip+dvd%27s](http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=327762&highlight=rip+dvd%27s)
and then transfer to mp4 for your ipod:mencoder 13112006031.mp4 -ovc lavc -vf scale=352:288 -oac lavc -o outpotmenc.mpg 
which I found from this thread; [http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=228027&highlight=mpg+to+mp4](http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=228027&highlight=mpg+to+mp4)
