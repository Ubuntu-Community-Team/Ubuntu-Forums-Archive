### Post by coldraven on 2011-02-23
My guess is that you somehow logged in as a guest when the PC came out of suspend.
As guest you would not be able to see any of the main users (your) files.
So in future if you want to lend your PC to someone move the cursor to the top right of the screen and login as guest.
