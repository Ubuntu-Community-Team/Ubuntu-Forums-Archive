### Post by jaezcurra on 2010-01-07
Just go to [http://www.skype.com/download/skype/linux/choose/](http://www.skype.com/download/skype/linux/choose/) and download either Ubuntu 8.10+ 32-bit or Ubuntu 8.10+ 64-bit.  As it will be a .deb file, just double click on it once in your Downloads directory.  It will self-install.
