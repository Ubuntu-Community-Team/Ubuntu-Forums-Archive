When I am running applications, none of them show in bottom panel.  In order for me to switch between the running application, I hit Alt+Tab.  How do I fix this problem?

---

### Post by dnairb on 2009-10-20
Right-click on the bottom panel.
Select "Add to Panel"
Near the bottom of the list is an entry "Window List"
Double-click this.... and you're done.

### Post by howefield on 2009-10-20
Right click bottom panel and select add to panel.
