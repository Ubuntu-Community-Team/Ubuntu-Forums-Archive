### Post by prshah on 2008-08-13
> **SilverTL said:**
> in vista when you are copying files it gives you detailed information of how fast you files are being copied and roughly how long it will take,


Ubuntu does this as well; it will additionally show the speed of transfer as well (1.5MBps, etc).

If you cannot see this information in your Ubuntu copy box, maybe the copying is too fast; ubuntu will show extended copy information only if the dialog box is active for over (about) 5 seconds.
