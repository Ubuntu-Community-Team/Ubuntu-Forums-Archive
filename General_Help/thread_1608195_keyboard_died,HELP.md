### Post by NeverHide on 2010-10-29
OK...After more calm searching i found out what the problem was.For some reason (don't know how:confused: ) an option was activated from the "Universal Access Preferences".That option was "Press and hold keys to accept them (Slow Keys). So for anything to work i had to keep that button pressed for at least 2-3e secs,which of course i didn't and i thought the keyboard stopped working...

Anyway no harm no foul:P
