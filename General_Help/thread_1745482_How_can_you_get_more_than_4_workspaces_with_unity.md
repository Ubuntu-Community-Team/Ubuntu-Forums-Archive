### Post by mc4man on 2011-05-01
open ccsm (compizconfig-settings-manager) > general options > Desktop size, add either to horizontal or vertical as you please
(ignore 3rd option

Also avail. in gconf-editor > /apps > compiz-1 > general > screen0 > options - right click on hsize or vsize > edit
