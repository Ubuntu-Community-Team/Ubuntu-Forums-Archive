### Post by Irihapeti on 2007-11-14
You can tell Firefox to use only system fonts by going to Edit->Preferences, choose Advanced (fonts and colors) and uncheck "Allow pages to choose their own fonts". Sorry I can't help with Open Office.

I've tried messing around with the subpixel rendering and nothing seems to make much difference.
