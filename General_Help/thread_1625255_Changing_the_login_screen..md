### Post by x1a4 on 2010-11-18
Hi.

Place your themes in [COLOR="Blue"]/usr/share/themes/[/COLOR], then use GDM Setup (/usr/sbin/gdmsetup) to set it.  You should note that if you are using Intrepid or later you will not be able to do it as the folks at GNOME decided to "improve" GDM by removing some of its most important features, including theme changes.  :sad:  :mad:
