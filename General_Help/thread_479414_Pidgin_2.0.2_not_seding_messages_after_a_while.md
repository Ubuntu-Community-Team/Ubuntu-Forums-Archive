### Post by Sionide on 2007-06-21
Sometimes the MSN service isn't as good as it perhaps should be. Try disconnecting and reconnecting; 
Accounts -> Add/Edit -> Untick enable -> Wait a bit -> Tick enable.

I often find that MSN de-syncs quite often if your connection is reliable - for example, IRC will stay connected if my wireless blips but the MSN protocol seems to have a lower threshold and gets disconnected, but this information isn't relayed back to the client so you think you're still online, when you're actually not - hence the messages saying "error in your connection."
