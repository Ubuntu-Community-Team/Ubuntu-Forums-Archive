### Post by pytheas22 on 2008-05-27
I'm not positive that it's related to your problem, but I've had problems in the past with compiz where the desktop would be white.  To see if that's what's wrong, log in and after you get to the white screen, press alt-F2.  Then type "metacity --replace" and see if you get a normal desktop.
