### Post by ajgreeny on 2015-11-06
You should be able to move the "start menu" from the panel preferences window by highlighting it in the list and using the up/down buttons on the right hand side.  See screenshot
You can change the icon by right clicking on the button, choosing **"Menu" settings**, and navigating to the correct image which on my 14.04 is **/usr/share/lubuntu/images/lubuntu-logo.png**

Good luck.
