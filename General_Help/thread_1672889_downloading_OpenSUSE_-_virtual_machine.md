### Post by CharlesA on 2011-01-21

### Post by Spice Weasel on 2011-01-22
I haven't seen an unsafe torrent for 2 years. In fact, I have seen more unsafe HTTP/direct downloads than torrents.

The .torrent file is just a file that tells your computer where to download from and where to upload to. Open it in a torrent client and leave it open overnight. It's good torrenting ethics to leave it open for a while (usually a day or two) after it has finished downloading, so you can upload ('seed') the data to other users.
