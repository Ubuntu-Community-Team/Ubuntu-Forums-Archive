### Post by 3rdalbum on 2011-06-08
1080p video files do not play well on Intel graphics unless you have a beefy CPU (which you do not) or are using Windows (the Intel drivers on Windows do video decode acceleration).

The table-top media centers are either using Windows or have a different GPU or a Broadcom CrystalHD decoding chip.
