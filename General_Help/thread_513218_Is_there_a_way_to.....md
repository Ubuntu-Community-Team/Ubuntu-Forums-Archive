### Post by fjgaude on 2007-07-30
Yes, you can move them... when you plug the flash card into your computer you likely get a "Photo Import" flag. Click ignore, then click on the icon on generated on your desktop. From there you click on the directories until you get to where your photos are and then move then using file manage. Your flash card will then be empty.

Hope this helps.

### Post by ugm6hr on 2007-07-30
How about using Cut and Paste in File Manager (nautilus / thunar).  Just select all photo files on SD card, right-click to Cut, then right-click in HD folder to Paste.
