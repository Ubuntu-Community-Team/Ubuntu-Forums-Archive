### Post by smautf on 2008-07-27
If you are using Ubuntu, open: system>preference>appearance and navigate to the "background" tab. OR, right click on the desktop and select "Change Desktop Background"

Now, there is a "style" dropdown, where you can change the style of your background. Play around with this and see if any of the settings solve your problem. I suggest "zoom", if you want your entire screen filled and see the entire picture, problem is, this mode ruins the aspect ratio of the image.

I'm assuming this is your problem, but if it is anything more than this, please give more details and screenshots if possible.
