### Post by Steve413z on 2008-07-20
I'm just wondering what pidgin plugins everybody here uses, theres a lot of pidgin plugins out there but not a central list of them. 

Here are the plugins I use:

Buddy State Notification  - Shows a buddies signons, signoffs, away, idle, etc in the conversation window

I'dle Mak'er  - sometimes handy, makes you look idle when you are not

Message Notification - makes new messages more noticeable

Off-The-Record Messaging  - Encryption

Pidgin-Thinklight - Makes thinkpad "thinklight" blink when you get a new message, it also works with other laptops

Psychic Mode - when a buddy is about to IM you, and is typing the message, it will notify you that they are typing the message and open a new window, before they even send the message.

There is also a facebook plugin so you can use the facebook chat with pidgin [http://code.google.com/p/pidgin-facebookchat/](http://code.google.com/p/pidgin-facebookchat/)

### Post by kevdog on 2008-07-20
Id like to make a list of all the pidgin plugins available (a master list).  Here are the ones I know about:

**OTR** (Off-The-Record) [http://www.cypherpunks.ca/otr/](http://www.cypherpunks.ca/otr/) -- need libotr
**pidgin encryption** - [http://gaim-encryption.sourceforge.net](http://gaim-encryption.sourceforge.net)
**Pidgin extended preferences** - [http://gaim-extprefs.sourceforge.net/](http://gaim-extprefs.sourceforge.net/)
**Purple Plugin Pack** (51 various Plugins): [http://plugins.guifications.org/trac/wiki/PluginPack](http://plugins.guifications.org/trac/wiki/PluginPack)
**Bot Sentry** - Prevents IM SPAM - [http://sourceforge.net/projects/pidgin-bs/](http://sourceforge.net/projects/pidgin-bs/)
**Pidgin RhythmBox Plugin** - [http://jon.oberheide.org/projects/pidgin-rhythmbox/](http://jon.oberheide.org/projects/pidgin-rhythmbox/) [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=622264](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=622264)

Please let me know if you have others
