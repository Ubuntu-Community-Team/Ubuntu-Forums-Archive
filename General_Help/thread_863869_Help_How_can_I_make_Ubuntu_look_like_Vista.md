### Post by kidux on 2008-07-21
If you've already gone through the process of downloading that 97MB thing, then it should be a simple matter of going to System->Preferences->Appearance (conversely you can just right-click the desktop, then choosing Change Background and clicking the Theme tab). 

After you get there, click the Install button in the lower right, then choose the file you downloaded, click Open, and it should install. If that doesn't work, you may need to actually extract the file to get what you need.

Keep in mind, however, it most likely won't change icons or anything like that. It's been my experience that most of it had to be changed by hand after the theme installation, which isn't always a bad thing.
