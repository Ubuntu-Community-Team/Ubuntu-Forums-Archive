### Post by gettinoriginal on 2009-02-11
As far as naming your workspaces, it only works in Metacity, not Compiz.  If you wish to do so, go to System, Pref, Appearance, Visual Effects, None.  Then right click the workspace bar and rename them.

Firefox issue:
F11 twice, then manually minimize the page and close firefox.  Reopen firefox and maximize with max button, problem should be fixed.
