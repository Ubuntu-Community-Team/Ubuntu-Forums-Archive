### Post by CEB2nd on 2009-02-13
System > Preferences > Advanced Desktop Effects Settings.

Effects > Wobbly Windows.  Remove the check from Snap inverted box.

You were told how to disable it, the second question. The first question, how to keep it from sticking to every border? Just go back in there and uncheck "Snap Inverted". That keeps it from sticking to everything, or that's how I got it to quit. But that's only if you like to watch the wobbly windows but annoyed from it sticking to everything, if not then just leave it off.
