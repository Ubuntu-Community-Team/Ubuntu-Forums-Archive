### Post by Claus7 on 2018-10-26
Hello,

open Libreoffice calc choose the cells with the dates. Then right click->Formal Cells...->Category: Date

From there you will see that the Format Code will be: MM/DD/YY 

Change it manually to DD/MM/YY and click OK.

EDIT: The solution *dino99* provided is permanent, which is what you would normally seek. Just to add that this can be changed from Tools->Options...->Language Settings->Languages->Locale Setting under LO 6.0.6.2

Hope this helps,
Regards!
