### Post by Kevbert on 2009-05-21
Of the two I'd go for the Nvidia based one. The Intel X3100 has had problems in the past with compiz (but may be fixed) and has been around for a while. The Nvidia one is newer and generally Nvidia are better supported. Both have had some problems in the past though.
The other thing you may want to check on is what wireless card is in each of the desktops as that could also be an issue.
