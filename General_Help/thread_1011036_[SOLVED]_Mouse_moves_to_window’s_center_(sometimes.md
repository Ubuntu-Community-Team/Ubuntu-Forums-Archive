### Post by rumpeltux on 2008-12-14
I’ve been able to at least fix the issue, though I still don’t know what causes it. I guess some weird bug in compiz.

Anyways the behaviour disappeared after disabling “Enhanced Desktop Zoom” in compiz. Some combination of Super+mousewheel seems to enable this cursor-moves-to-center feature.
