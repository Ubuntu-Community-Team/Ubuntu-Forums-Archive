### Post by NeoChaosX on 2006-06-27
To create desktop links to those folders, just right click on your desktop, highlight "Create New...", then select "Link to Location". When it asks for a location, just enter /home/<yourusername> for your home folder, or trash:/ for a trash icon.

Not a KBFX user so I can't help you with the second problem, though. Sorry.
