### Post by DGortze380 on 2009-05-03
I've found that Windows 7 runs VERY WELL in VirtualBox.

No 3D, so no gaming.

I do suggest VirtualBox and not VirtualBox OSE.

Last time I ran it, Guest Additions were not supported.

I ran it on a host with a dual-core with 1GB or RAM for the VM and it was faster than my Vista box with a dual-core and 2GB of RAM. Took a LONG time to boot, but I think that's the Beta more than anything.
