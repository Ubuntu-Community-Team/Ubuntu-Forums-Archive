### Post by wbee on 2010-04-05
Shaun:

The driver drives the sound card,not the speaker.

Here are the two things that might be most likely to need adjusting:

1. Upper right hand corner of the top panel,right click the speaker icon and left click preferences.

---make sure nothing is muted.

2.If you have on board sound on your motherboard and have added a sound card,make sure the on board sound is "disabled".

--See your owner's manual or connect to their messenger service,asking how to access the bios and disable the on board sound.

----------

If it is neither of those things,it could get complicated. The linked page might help.

[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/DebuggingSoundProblems](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/DebuggingSoundProblems) 


-----------

Good luck.
