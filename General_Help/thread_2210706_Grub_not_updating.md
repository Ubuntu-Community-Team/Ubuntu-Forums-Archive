### Post by d4m1r on 2014-03-12

### Post by d4m1r on 2014-04-27
> **oldfred said:**
> I have not seen this, but with 14.04 someone posted the grub update and it included this comment. Not sure exactly what it means.

Indeed, 14.04 users are complaining about this as well as can be seen in [THIS]("https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/grub2/+bug/1258597") bug report. I have found a workaround that I can confirm works for me. I have commented out the GRUB_HIDDEN* fields like this;


```
GRUB_DEFAULT=0
#GRUB_HIDDEN_TIMEOUT=0
#GRUB_HIDDEN_TIMEOUT_QUIET=true
GRUB_TIMEOUT=5 
```

Now I don't get that warning when updating GRUB and my countdown timer was changed to 5s from 10s. I have no idea why those 2 are not commented out by default to avoid this.....
