### Post by matchett808 on 2010-07-03
surround sound audio can only be carried through the following inter-connects:

optical (toslink)
digital coaxial
and HDMI (newest and is available on more expensive amps....probs not on your one that has been running for years....)

By the sounds of it I think that you may need a different soundcard on your server one that gives you the appropriate output.... from your post I would hesitate to guess at this as I have never touched an analogue amp.....but from what little I do know is that you'll need a different cable as a 'single jack' from your pc will at best get you stereo, possible even mono audio.....you will be getting dolby matrix atm, you'll need either multiple analogue cables or a single digital one.....
