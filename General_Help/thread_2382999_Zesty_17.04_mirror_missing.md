### Post by DuckHook on 2018-01-19
Please don't confuse "old" with "out of support". I advise general users to stop chasing after the shiny new car and stick to old reliable. Therefore, both 14.04 and 16.04 are still supported because they are LTS (Long Term Support), whereas 17.04 is not because it is just a standard release. Standard releases have the lifespan of mayflies. Best stick to LTSes.
