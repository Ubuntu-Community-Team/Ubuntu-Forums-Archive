### Post by Boomhauer on 2009-02-01
first go to System>Adminstration>Software Sources and click on the updates tab.  then look down at the bottom and make sure it says Show new distribution releases: Normal Releases.  now close that window and hit Alt+F2.  enter in ```
update-manager -d
```  up in the  top of the update box that appears, you will have the option to choose ubuntu 8.10
