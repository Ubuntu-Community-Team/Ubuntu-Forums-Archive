### Post by Tamlynmac on 2009-10-26
One quick thought:
Is your send/receive grayed out?
If so:
At the bottom of the evolution main window is a plug icon. Is it unplugged that means your offline? If so, left click on it and it will connect, putting you back online assuming all your settings are correct.

Also, you might check the list of supported types of Authentication for both send and receive and assure they are correct.

evolution/edit/preferences/user account/edit/receive (send) email/Authentication Type

Good Luck and hope this helps
