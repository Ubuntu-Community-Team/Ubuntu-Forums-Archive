### Post by geirha on 2009-06-24
I believe most people solve that kind of thing by using [webmail]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Webmail") instead.

Another option is to use a text based mail client, such as pine or mutt, on your home computer. Then you can connect to your box via ssh (putty is a common ssh-client for windows) from anywhere to read your mail.
