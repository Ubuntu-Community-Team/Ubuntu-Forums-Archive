### Post by Man_Beach on 2007-04-08
I built my own computer a few years ago. It's not difficult to do - most of the parts just plug together.

I used information from the web - I found mysuperpc [http://www.mysuperpc.com/](http://www.mysuperpc.com/) was very helpful, and magazines (I'm in the UK, and as far as I'm concerned Micromart is the best magazine by far for that sort of information).

The hardest thing is sorting out the BIOS. As well as mysuperpc, the best site I found for explaining it all is Adrian's Rojak Pot [http://www.techarp.com/show.aspx?fileID=18](http://www.techarp.com/show.aspx?fileID=18)
