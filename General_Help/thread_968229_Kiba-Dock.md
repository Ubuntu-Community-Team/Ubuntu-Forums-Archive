### Post by kelean on 2008-11-07
I looked at the kiba-dock website and found this in the faq's.

You can right click on  the dock, and then click ""Kiba Settings Editor".

Or open a terminal and type this command:

    * kiba-settings

 You also could edit ~/.kiba-dock/config manually,

but you need to make sure, that you match the syntax exactly.


I never used it but I hope it helps.  They also have a forum there.
