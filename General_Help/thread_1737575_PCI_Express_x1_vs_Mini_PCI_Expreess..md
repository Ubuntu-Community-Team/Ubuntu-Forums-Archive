[QUOTE=uRock]From looking at some search results it looks like they are completely different with the mini pcie having a much higher pin count.[/QUOTE]
Yup, looks like they are not even close to the same.

 
Have a look at this:
[http://content.dell.com/us/en/corp/d/business~solutions~whitepapers~en/Documents~wp-2004_pciexpress.pdf.aspx]("http://content.dell.com/us/en/corp/d/business%7Esolutions%7Ewhitepapers%7Een/Documents%7Ewp-2004_pciexpress.pdf.aspx")
"Unlike PCI and PCI-X, which are based on 32- and  64-bit parallel buses, PCI Express uses high-speed serial link  technology similar to that found in Gigabit1 Ethernet, Serial  ATA (SATA), and Serial-Attached SCSI (SAS). PCI Express reflects an  industry trend to replace legacy shared parallel buses with high-speed  point-to-point serial buses. "
