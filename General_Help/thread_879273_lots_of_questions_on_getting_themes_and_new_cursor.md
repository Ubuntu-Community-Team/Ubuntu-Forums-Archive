### Post by Hayesio on 2008-08-03
There are many parts of your system that can be themed.  You can change or add themes to Gnome, add themes to matacity or emerald, add themes to X like mouse pointer etc, add/change icons on your system, and much more.  Then of cause, you can get into the world of compiz-fusion!

The best place to start is gnome-look.org. download what you like and then drop the package into theme manager (you may have to extract the package prior to adding it, but try just droping it in first).  Your theme manager is found in System>Pref>Apperance (assuming you use Ubuntu). GTK themes are for Gnome, X11 themes for mouse/kbord etc.  

Adding emeral themes is slightly different, you must do this through Emeral theme manager.

You can edit each theme to tweak it your way as well.

Have fun.
