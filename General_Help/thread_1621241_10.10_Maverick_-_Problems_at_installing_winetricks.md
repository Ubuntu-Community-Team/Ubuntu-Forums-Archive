### Post by ABDELHADI on 2011-01-08
HI MAN
i found the same problem 
but it s resolved for my by this
----------------------------------------------------------
You need to copy and paste into a terminal window:

wget [http://www.kegel.com/wine/winetricks](http://www.kegel.com/wine/winetricks)
sh winetricks corefonts

and then:

wget [http://www.kegel.com/wine/winetricks](http://www.kegel.com/wine/winetricks)
sh winetricks vcrun2005sp1

-----------------------------------------------------------
try it 
and don't forget to reply

good luck  :KS
