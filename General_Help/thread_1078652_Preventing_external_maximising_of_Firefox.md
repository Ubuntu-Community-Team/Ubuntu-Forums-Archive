### Post by leewebb on 2009-02-28
They're probably using Javascript, so go into Preferences->Content. Next to "Enable JavaScript" there is also an "Advanced" button. You can select whether JS is allowed to resize windows/remove titlebars etc

It's fairly basic, but may do the job. Otherwise there's probably an add-on out there.
