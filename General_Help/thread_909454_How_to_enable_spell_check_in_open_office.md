### Post by brinkofacomplex on 2010-04-05
I've had this same problem from 7.10 until now. I decided to give it another go today and while none of the tutorials or suggestions I've read helped, I just changed my selected language from English (Canada) to English (UK). In the drop-down menus under Tools > Options > Language Settings > Languages as well as under Format > Character > Font some of the languages listed have a little blue spell check symbol next to them and some don't; I chose a language option (UK English) that did have the symbol, and voila! My guess is that this has something to do with myspell packages; I have myspell-en-gb installed but couldn't find a Canadian option. (Under System > Administration > Synaptic Package Manager do a quick search for spellcheck-en to see which options you have installed.)
