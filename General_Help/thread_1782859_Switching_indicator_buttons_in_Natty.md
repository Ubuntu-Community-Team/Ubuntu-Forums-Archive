### Post by 3rdalbum on 2011-06-15
In reality, it doesn't take very long at all for someone to get used to the window buttons on the other side. After all, all Mac users have had to get used to Windows' buttons being on the wrong side. It took my fairly-computer-illiterate girlfriend only a few days to become "ambidextrous" for window buttons.

The window buttons are NOT indicators - the indicators are the informative things on the top panel. Don't go messing with the indicators :-)

You can indeed switch to Gnome Classic and then change the order of window buttons in gconf-editor, and there are many HOWTOs about this; but Gnome Classic won't be around forever. It might just be easier to have her migrate to a new UI once, rather than twice.
