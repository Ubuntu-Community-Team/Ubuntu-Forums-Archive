### Post by bakegoodz on 2010-01-24
Open your web browser and go to [http://127.0.0.1:631/jobs/](http://127.0.0.1:631/jobs/)
Some may have a strange reaction to my reply and think I'm a spammer, but 127.0.0.1 is your own IP address and port 631 is set for CUPS web management. [http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/CUPS](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/CUPS)

### Post by Ubunt2u on 2010-01-26
Wondering this myself?  While I can cancel jobs through CUPS internet interface, what can I do to allow print jobs in the queue to be deleted from networked windows workstations?  Thanks

---

### Post by Ubunt2u on 2010-01-28
in cupsd.conf 
<Limit Cancel-Job CUPS-Authenticate-Job>
   Require user @OWNER @SYSTEM
    Order deny,allow
    Allow all
</Limit>

I commented out Order deny,allow

Windows workstation users can now cancel their print jobs from their workstation
