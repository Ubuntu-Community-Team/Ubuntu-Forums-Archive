### Post by kscelt on 2006-06-21
Check Sane for scanner support. Hardware support is infrequently added and spotty, so look carefully, not as to just whether a scanner is on the list, but also the extent to which it is functioning. 

[http://www.sane-project.org/](http://www.sane-project.org/)

If it is not listed as "complete", be wary. This a weak area for all linux distros, not just Ubuntu.
