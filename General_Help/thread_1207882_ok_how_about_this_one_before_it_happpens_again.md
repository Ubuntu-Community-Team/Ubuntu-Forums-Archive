### Post by philinux on 2009-07-08
Right click on the top panel and choose "add to panel". Select Force quit. That can be used to kill a misbehaving app. Or use system monitor.

Alt + SysRq + k will force x to quit and take you back to login screen

reisub will restart machine softly.
[http://lifehacker.com/298891/gently-restart-a-frozen-system](http://lifehacker.com/298891/gently-restart-a-frozen-system)
