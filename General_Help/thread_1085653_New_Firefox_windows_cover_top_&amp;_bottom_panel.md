### Post by Drone022 on 2009-03-03
Hey, whenever I open a new Firefox window the window stretches automatically to cover both the top and bottom panels on the desktop. The only way to get out of it is to put firefox into fullscreen then minimise it.

It's really annoying, does anyone know how to stop this happening?

Thanks!

---

### Post by zika on 2009-03-03
System->Preferences->CompizConfig_Settings_Manager->Utility->Workarounds->un_check_Legacy_Fulscreen_Support

---

### Post by Drone022 on 2009-03-03
Yeah that's fixed it. Excellent. 

Thanks for quick response.
