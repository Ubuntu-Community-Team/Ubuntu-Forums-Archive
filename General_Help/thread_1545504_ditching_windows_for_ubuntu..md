### Post by Rubi1200 on 2010-08-04
Hi and welcome :)
 
First try Ubuntu as a LiveCD before taking the jump and installing. Running in live mode offers an opportunity to see if Ubuntu plays nicely with your computer and allows you to see whether you really like it or not.
 
Remember that Linux is not Windows and that while some things may feel familiar not everything will work as you want/expect.
 
The important thing is to read first and then ask as many questions as you want here.
