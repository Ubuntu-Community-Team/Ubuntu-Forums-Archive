### Post by snowpine on 2010-12-12
1gb is the recommended minimum RAM for Ubuntu.

Lubuntu uses the lightweight LXDE desktop environment (instead of Gnome). You don't even need to reinstall; simply install LXDE from the Software Center, then log out, and from the login screenchoose Sessions, LXDE. It might give you a slight performance boost on your low-RAM system.

CrunchBang is a fine OS but it is no longer based on Ubuntu and may not be what you're looking for.
