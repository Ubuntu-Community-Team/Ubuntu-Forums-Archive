### Post by Lars Noodén on 2009-10-12
I'll add another 'me, too' for Thunderbird, but any IMAP client will do the job you are asking for.  

One Open Source mail client not yet in the Ubuntu distro is Mulberry, I'll throw that one in for variety:
  [http://www.mulberrymail.com/](http://www.mulberrymail.com/)

If you want something really fast, then Alpine or Mutt will work.  Those are fast, text-based, menu-driven clients.  Linus Torvalds uses Alpine.
