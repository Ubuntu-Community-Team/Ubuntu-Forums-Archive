### Post by King_Ragger on 2009-08-06
Go with one of the later Nvidia GeForce cards. The cards themselves are roughly equal with ATI in terms of hardware. However, Nvidia has the trump card in much better Linux drivers and you'll get better performance because of it. I have an ATI Radeon HD2600 and can't find good open source 3d drivers for it. The latest proprietary drivers from the ATI website are ok. But still ineffective and buggy compared to the windows versions.

[edit: my 2d performance is great with the open source radeon driver. But it currently has no 3d support for HD2000+ series cards. And I've never managed to get the radeonhd driver to work properly on my system.]
