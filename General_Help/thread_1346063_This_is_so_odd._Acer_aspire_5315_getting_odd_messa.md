### Post by pastalavista on 2009-12-04
Open System-> Preferences-> Keyboard and select the proper language setup. Then open Applications-> Accessories-> Terminal and enter ```
sudo dpkg --configure -a
``` and press enter. Then enter your password. The keystrokes won't show but hit enter anyway.
