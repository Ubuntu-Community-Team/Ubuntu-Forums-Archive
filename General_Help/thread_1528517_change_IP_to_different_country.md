### Post by new_tolinux on 2010-07-11
Probably the best thing you could do is ask someone you know in Holland to set up a proxy.
Let the usage be limited to your IP, so that it's not an open proxy.

Since there's a significant time difference, it should be possible to have an agreement about usage time and bandwith.
