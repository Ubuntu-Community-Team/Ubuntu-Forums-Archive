### Post by adam_kimber on 2007-11-16
The visual effects require a fairly powerful graphics card, if your laptop is fairly old teh installer might have disabled them by default to  keep the desktop fast. 

You could also try Ubuntu Menu --> System --> Preferences --> Appearance --> Visual Effects Tab

Then check either of the options, Normal or Extra to get the visual effects.

"wissh" in and out. Do you mean the effects that you see when you move the windows, you close the windows or open the windows?  If it is. Then you that's Compiz.

Compiz is enabled by default if Ubuntu detects that your hardware supports it. 
Probably your laptop is not in the "supported" list.  (Are you using ATI graphic card?)


If you want a good graphical support in GNU/Linux, you should use nVidia or Intel (Avoid ATIi)
