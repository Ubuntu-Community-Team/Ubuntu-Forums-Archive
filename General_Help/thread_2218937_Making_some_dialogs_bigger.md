Some other things you want are in Customize Look and Feel (*lxappearance* from the terminal).

As for the "fragmentation", most components of Lubuntu are optional and modular and drawn from a variety of existing software. People can swap one thing for another. My guess is that's why there's no single place for accessing the things you mention.

The other thing is that the Lubuntu team is very, very small. The lead developer is a volunteer and not a full-time employee of Canonical. You may want to take a look at [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Lubuntu/Testing/14.04](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Lubuntu/Testing/14.04)
