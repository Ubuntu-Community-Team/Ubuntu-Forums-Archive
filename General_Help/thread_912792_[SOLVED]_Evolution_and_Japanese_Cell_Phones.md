### Post by Chokkan on 2008-09-07
Hey there. Make sure that you are sending you message in the correct character encoding. In the Compose Message window, go to **Edit**, then **Character Encoding...** and choose something like **Japanese Shift_JIS**.

I just tried it out and it seems to work.
