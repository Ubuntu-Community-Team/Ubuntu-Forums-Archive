### Post by jimmydean886-2 on 2011-12-14
go to gnome-look.org and then go to the "GTK+ 3x" item on the sidebar and click.

This will give you themes. All you need do now is install it by extracting the files to an empty folder. If the archive extracts successfully, add the folder with the theme name to the folder titled .themes in your home dir. You will have to go to the "go" menu and click on location. Just type in /.themes after your username in the new text box that appears over your folder selection ribbion and hit enter to go to that folder. This is a case where copy and paste will come in handy with folders.
