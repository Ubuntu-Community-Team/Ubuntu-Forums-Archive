### Post by Mark Phelps on 2011-02-10
I get this regularly with 10.10 -- just decides NOT to boot to a desktop anymore.

Next time, when you get into Recovery Mode, choose the option to Repair broken packages.

After that is done, and you get a prompt, enter "startx".

If all goes well, you'll get a desktop -- where you can shutdown normally.
