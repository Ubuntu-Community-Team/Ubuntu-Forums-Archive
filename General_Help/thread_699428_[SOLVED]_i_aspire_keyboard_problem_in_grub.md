### Post by zenkaon on 2008-02-17
Hello, I have a usb i aspire keyboard.

When I boot my pc, I can use the keyboard to enter the bios - it works fine.

However, it does not work in grub. This means that I cannot choose between ubuntu and windows. This is a major pain in the ***.

My keyboard also doesn't work on the live-cd boot menu.

I have tried my keyboard in all 4 of my usb slots. It works fine in the bios, and after booting, but not in grub.

This didn't happen when I had feisty.

Any clues? ideas?

---

### Post by zenkaon on 2008-02-17
Solved it,

went into the bios and changed the keyboard support option from OS to BIOS.

I have a Abit KT7a-raid motherboard - really quite old.
