### Post by HavocXphere on 2009-05-29
> I want something that can call cell phones and land lines. 
The software itself can't call cells & land lines. For that you need a VOIP provider. So start by finding one that meets your requirements and then find software that works with that provider.

Skype uses a proprietary protocol. Try to go for a provider that uses a well-known protocol like

[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Session_Initiation_Protocol](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Session_Initiation_Protocol)
or
[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/H.323](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/H.323)

Also, a local provider will probably give better voice quality than one located on the other side of the world.
