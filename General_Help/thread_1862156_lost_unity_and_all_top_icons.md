### Post by Sbarton on 2011-10-22
demonboy here is a link to resetting unity, [http://www.webupd8.org/2011/04/how-to-reset-unity-launcher-icons-or.html](http://www.webupd8.org/2011/04/how-to-reset-unity-launcher-icons-or.html)

If at present you can launch system settings. scroll to the Unity icon and ensure the box has a 'x'in it. Reboot.

If you cannot do that then reboot and at login click the icon at the right corner of the login logo and a drop down menu should give you the opportunity to select 'gnome' as the desktop select this & enter password and enter. Once booted into gnome desktop use the terminal as instructed at the top of the page.
If this does not work go into system settings and ensure the 'unity icon has a tick in it. Reboot.
For some reason my unity box was reset and the tick was missing.. A gliche??
good luck
