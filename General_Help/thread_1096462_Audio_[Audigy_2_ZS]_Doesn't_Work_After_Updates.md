### Post by mc4man on 2009-03-15
Double click on little speaker icon in top panel, look for a switches tab and uncheck "Audigy Analog/Digital Outpit Jack"

If there is no switches tab then click on the 'preferences' box, scroll down and enable the switch. Then go back to 'switches' tab and make sure it's unchecked.

(note that it wasn't an audio update, it was included in a kernel update (what switched the function of the  A/D switch
