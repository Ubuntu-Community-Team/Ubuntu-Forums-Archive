### Post by TiredBird on 2009-03-27
SOLUTION!

On the official jBrout forum (Google groups), I was given the internet address for the archive of earlier version debs. I downloaded and installed the last one from the 2.x series and had no trouble making it work.

My guess is that after I upgrade to 8.10, (or something else that is later than the 8.04 that I am currently using), I will also be able to upgrade my jBrout to the 3.x series without any dependancy problems.
