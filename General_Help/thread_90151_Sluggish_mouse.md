### Post by Remmelas on 2005-11-14
I've a logitech mx310, and had the same problems.  In my case, it's cuz by default, linux runs it at a resolution of 400dpi.  I used lmctl to correct this, as outlined in this thread [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=4357](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=4357)
bumped it to it's natural 800dpi, and it's mouse goodness.
