I am right clicking on the thumbdrive and choosing rename.  I type the name in I want, click enter and that is when I get the permission denided message. ### Post by pissedoffdude on 2014-09-14
Try installing Gparted from the Ubuntu Software Center.  Then open it and find your flash drive > right click its main partition > Labels and rename it to whatever you want.  If that option is grayed out, right click its main partition > unmount
