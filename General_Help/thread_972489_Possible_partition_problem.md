### Post by pirattrev on 2008-11-05
if you have a copy of vista on disk, I wouldn't worry about the recovery partition.  I find they are unhelpful and the most useful way to ensure your stuff isn't lost is to just have an external hard drive, and keep around install discs just in case. Messing around with partitions, especially if they are actively used OS's, is always a scary thing and I make an effort to avoid it.
