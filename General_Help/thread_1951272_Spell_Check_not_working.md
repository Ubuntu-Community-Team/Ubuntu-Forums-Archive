### Post by ajgreeny on 2012-04-02
Check that you have **hunspell**, **aspell** or **myspell** dictionaries installed, as they are they standard dictionaries required for spell checker.

Also check it is enabled in your LO.  There is a toolbar button to toggle autospellcheck on and off on my version of LO (3.5.1.2), and it works on my system without a problem.
