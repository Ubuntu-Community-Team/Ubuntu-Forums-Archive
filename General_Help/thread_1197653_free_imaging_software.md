### Post by Greyfox_75 on 2009-06-26
I use clonezilla works great.  Only downside compared to Acronis is you can't put a large partition into a small partition. If I recall the newest version of Acronis can do this.  You just have to do any downsizing of your partitions yourself before your make the image.  It does try to make partitions bigger if there is free space on the drive however.
