### Post by navneeth on 2009-05-11
Found a solution at [http://scottbarnham.com/blog/2009/03/09/gvim-menu-bar-missing/](http://scottbarnham.com/blog/2009/03/09/gvim-menu-bar-missing/)

Delete the file ~/.gnome2/Vim and restart Vim.
