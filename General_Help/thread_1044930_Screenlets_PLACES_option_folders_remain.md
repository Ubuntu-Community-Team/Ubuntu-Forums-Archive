I got it.
Another user suggested this.  It worked.
I didn't know that there was this option for bookmarks!

"The Places screenlet always includes Home and Desktop and then builds the rest of the entries from the Bookmarks list in Nautilus.

To remove unwanted items, open Edit Bookmarks from the Bookmarks menu in Nautilus. In Edit Bookmarks you can delete the unwanted entries. You can also drag items in the list to change their order.
