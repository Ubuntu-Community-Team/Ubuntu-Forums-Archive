> My girlfriend of over a year recently bought a Toshiba laptop. Don't know much about it for I have not seen it myself. 3GB of RAM. Anyway, it's a Windows 7 Home Premium Edition. She has Microsoft Office 2007 pre-installed on it. Now, it's asking for a Product Key, which she does not have. Why? 
Why? Because pre-installed versions are trial versions.  You get 30 or 60 days (depending on what the OEM installed) and then you have to BUY it to keep it working.

> Anything that I can (LEGALLY) do to help her figure out the Product Key?
NO.  Even if you DO find the existing key, it's only for the trial version and won't help.

And, in future, if you have Win7 or Office questions, consider going to the proper WINDOWS forum for answers.  In this case, you should have gone to sevenforums.com -- the premier Win7 forum.  Then also have a forum on Office issues.
