### Post by 3rdalbum on 2012-04-28
> **jhall1990 said:**
> Hello everyone,

I made the mistake of updating Ubuntu 11.04 to 12.04. Now I have Unity sigh.

I have been trying new desktop managers for the past couple of hours.

What I'd recommend, in all seriousness, is to slow down your desktop-hopping. Try Unity for a few days AT LEAST - most people find they love it within a week. Otherwise, try a desktop for a couple of days at a time rather than a couple of minutes or hours.

Gnome Session Fallback is the closest thing you'll get to Gnome 2. It's still using Gnome 3 infrastructure so things are a little different, but this is computing and everything changes every so often in computing.

I'm surprised you didn't like MATE, because that is actually Gnome 2. No idea how much longer they'll continue to keep it alive, but I wouldn't bet on it in the long term.

The point of my post is to get used to change, because it will always happen in computing. Try Unity or any other desktop for a few days to get the feel of it.
