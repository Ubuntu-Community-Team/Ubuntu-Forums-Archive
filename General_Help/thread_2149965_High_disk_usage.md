### Post by vanadium on 2013-06-01
I do not have such folder. It might be used by a chat program. Usually, it is safe to delete the contents of such a .cache folder: the contents will be recreated as needed.

The cache is there to speed up the display of avatars on a subsequent time. Deleting the cache will make certain avatars display more slowly the first time it is needed,
