### Post by Frogs Hair on 2017-08-30
Open the file manger and use the show hidden folders option or press Ctrl +h . Delete the .mozilla folder reboot the computer and open Firefox . A new profile folder will be created when you do this. _You will loose your bookmarks and extensions if you do this and aren't using Firefox sync._
