short answer: no

long answer: yeah there are viruses for linux, but they're not 'in the wild', as in they're not actively spreading, in addition those non-active viruses weren't distro specific, they targetted people running linux as a whole and as far as I'm aware it would be impossible for them to spread today since the methods they used to spread have been patched up.
