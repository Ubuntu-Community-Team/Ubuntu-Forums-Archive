### Post by cascade9 on 2010-05-24
You are going to have to ceck the back of your computer, where the monitor plug in, to see what outputs you have. 

Here is a apic of the (typical) input ports on a TV- 

[IMG]http://images.televisioninfo.com/images/upload/Image/Reviews/Sony/sony_46w4100/sony_46w4100_ports_back.jpg[/IMG]

VGA is sometimes labeled RGB, and its normally blue. The other ports that your computer might have is HDMI, DVI (not shown here) and TV out. TV out can vary a lot, sometimes its just a component video out (yellow plugs above) sometimes is a s-video out (round plug at top left) and sometimes there is a plug for an external box that has all the ports on it.
