### Post by swoody on 2008-11-28
Try this out:
[http://support.mozilla.com/en-US/kb/Bookmarks+Not+Saved#Unable_to_add_a_new_bookmark](http://support.mozilla.com/en-US/kb/Bookmarks+Not+Saved#Unable_to_add_a_new_bookmark)

I have had similar problems in the past with upgrades. I found a lifesaver in Foxmarks. It's an add-on that will store all your bookmarks (and passwords if you want it to) into its online database, then you can get all your bookmarks on any computer by downloading the add-on and logging into it. It automagically updates your online database whenever you close FF, too, so you're always up to date. It saved my rear end on more than one occasion :)

Try that link from Mozilla, and let me know if it works for you or not :)
