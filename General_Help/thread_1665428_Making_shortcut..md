### Post by marin123 on 2011-01-12
on desktop, hit alt+f2 and type in gconf-editor to enter editor. then navigate to: apps/metacity/global_keybindings and you can assign shortcuts to whatever you want...
terminal is: run_command_terminal, double-click it to edit and type key combination you want. mine is <Mod4>F1, that means windows logo + f1...
