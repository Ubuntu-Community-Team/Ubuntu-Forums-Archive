Welcome to the forums! I do not recommend ever mixing software sources from a different Ubuntu release. It would be analagous to using parts from a 2013 automobile in a 2012 automobile with different specifications. At best it would work just fine; at worst it will make your system unstable or unusable. 

I posted a perfectly safe link where you can download the latest LibreOffice for any Ubuntu release back in my post #2.

However, since you are new to Linux, I recommend trying the "stable, well-tested software through a centralized repository" mindset for a while, rather than the "Windows Way" of downloading individual applications from a variety of different sources. It really is Linux's #1 "killer feature" in my opinion. :)
