### Post by Peepsalot on 2006-11-22
Try this.  Right click on the panel, select "Add New Item".  Scroll down the list of items to "Xfce Menu" and pick that if you find it.

For the samba stuff, once you get your menu back you should be able to Select "System"->"Shared Folders" and configure it there.
