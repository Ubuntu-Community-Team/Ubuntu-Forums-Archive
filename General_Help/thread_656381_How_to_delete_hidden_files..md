Hidden files are prefixed with a period.  An example:
> .file
Files with a tidal at the end are backup files.  An example:
> file~

To remove a hidden file, either open up Nautilus and press ctrl+h to display them, then select them and press delete or open up a terminal, "cd" into the directory then to rm .file
