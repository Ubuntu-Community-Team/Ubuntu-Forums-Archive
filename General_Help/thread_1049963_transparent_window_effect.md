### Post by gjoellee on 2009-01-25
That is actually not possible with Compiz. It is so far onyl possible with Xfwm or Kwinf (Xfce or KDE's window managers). You can however have motionblur which is quite cool.

Find motion blur in your compiz settings and enable it. Then hit "CTRL+F12" and trag your window.

### Post by mcduck on 2009-01-25
> **usien said:**
> what option do i need to turn on to have my windows transparent when i move them (with compiz 0.7.8). I have installed compiz settings manager.

Easy. In CompizConfig Settings Manager find the Move-plugin (under "uncategorized"-group, should be already enabled if you are able to move your windows ;)).

In it's settings you'll find a slider to adjust the opacity level of moving windows.
