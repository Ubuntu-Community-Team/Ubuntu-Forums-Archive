### Post by 2F4U on 2012-08-02
How are we supposed to know what happens?
WiFi drops and reconnects are not too uncommon and happen due to several reasons, for example a person standing between the laptop and the WiFi router.
I don't know in what region of the world you are living, but in some regions power outages are also not too uncommon. Since your laptop has a battery it is kind of protected from a power outage, but would behave as if it was disconnected from the power source. When the power comes back, it behaves as if it has been plugged to the power source.
