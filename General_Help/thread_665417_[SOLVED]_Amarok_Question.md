### Post by sancho panza on 2008-01-12
The Collection tab is just the list of files in your collection, whereas, the main window is your current playlist. So u need to add files to the playlist by selecting songs from your collection, or selecting a playlist from the playlists tab, etc.

### Post by .nedberg on 2008-01-12
You do not have to make playlists to use Amarok. To add your entire collection go to the Playlists tab, and select the Smart playlist. There you can add entire collection. You can add the artist/album/track you want from the collection tab. Check out the dynamic playlists, I use them all the time! If you have a last.fm account they are awesome!

### Post by rune0077 on 2008-01-12
Just double-click on the album from your collection and all songs on it will be added to the playlist.
