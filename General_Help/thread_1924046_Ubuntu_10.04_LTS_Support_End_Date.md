### Post by JKyleOKC on 2012-02-11
The EOL date will be April 2013. Ubuntu's version numbers are not like most programs; they use year.month instead of numbering sequentially. Thus "10.04" came out in April, 2010. The day is always the last day of the month, and the ".3" at the end of the version indicates it's the third "service pack" (which actually includes all of the updates for the first couple of years, so you don't have to download so many of them).

If you visit the forums regularly there will be lots of chatter as the EOL date approaches, but so far as I know there's not any specific notification. Once a release hits EOL, it simply quits getting automatic security update notifications.
