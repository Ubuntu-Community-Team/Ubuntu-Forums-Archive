### Post by t0lk on 2010-07-07
I'm using Ubuntu 10.04 64bit on a Toshiba Satellite L500 series laptop.

Every time I press ctrl c or v the touchpad freezes for a few seconds and the cursor won't move.

Any idea about this problem?

---

### Post by Yarui on 2010-07-07
Laptops often seem to do this when you are typing so that you don't accidentally hit the touchpad while typing and make the cursor jump, screwing up whatever you were typing.  I'm not sure where in Ubuntu you can change this setting, but it would probably help to check synaptic for laptop tools.

---

### Post by t0lk on 2010-07-07
Ah, you're right, that was the reason.  The fix is to go to System > Preferences > Mouse > Touchpad > Disable touchpad while typing.

Thanks!
