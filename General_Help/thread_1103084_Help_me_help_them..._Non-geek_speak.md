### Post by 3Miro on 2009-03-22
I have personally installed Ubuntu for some of my non-geek friends and they love it. Every now and then they will come to ask me a very newbie question, but I don't mind.

You don't need constant access to the repositories, only when you wish to download/update something. Most of the stuff in Ubuntu can easily be done with GUI interface. If you set it up once, then there should be no problems. (There are many HOTOs on this forrum that vary in difficulty, your friend can try and read some of those to learn some of the basics, also when you are helping him, don't just say "type this or type that", try explain the basics of what is happening).
