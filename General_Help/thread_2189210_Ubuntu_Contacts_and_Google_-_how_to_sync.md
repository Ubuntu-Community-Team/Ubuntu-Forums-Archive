### Post by grier-devon on 2013-11-21
As far as I know there is no way to move your contacts directly on Ubuntu. I know you have the "Contacts" in Ubuntu One but it does not link to your Android phone or Google accounts for that matter. Trust me I asked a very similar question about one year ago and this was the response I got. The best that can be done through Ubuntu is to link the google account to Ubuntu through Empathy which will link it through gmail which means anyone who has an Android device I have been able to basically text through google hangouts/Talk and they never know the difference really or at least no one has complained to me about it.
