### Post by Metaleks on 2008-04-22
Remove your AIM account in Pidgin.  Press Ctrl-A and then remove the AIM account.

Add it again.  Make sure you are using the server "login.messaging.aol.com" without the quotes.  As for port, the most common is 5190.  And, leave it on GNOME proxy settings, as that's the default any way.  Oh, and then just check for the obvious.  If your connection is being refused it could very well that you have typed your alias incorrectly, or even your password.  It's usually that.  But don't feel bad if it is, it gets the best of us :)
