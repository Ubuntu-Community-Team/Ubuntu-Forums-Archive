### Post by josephus on 2007-04-09
just look through other icon themes, either in /usr/share/icons or look at the ones available on gnome-look.org

basically it's just a bunch of directories with your different icons, followed by the index.theme which specifies what type of icons are in each directory

[http://standards.freedesktop.org/icon-theme-spec/icon-theme-spec-latest.html](http://standards.freedesktop.org/icon-theme-spec/icon-theme-spec-latest.html)
