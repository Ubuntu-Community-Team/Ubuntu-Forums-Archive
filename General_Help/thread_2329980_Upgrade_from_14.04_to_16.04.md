### Post by Impavidus on 2016-07-07
Many people have trouble upgrading from 14.04 to 16.04. That's because that upgrade isn't officially offered yet, so you have to jump through loopholes and get trouble. So best wait for another two weeks.

Now you have already tried and upgraded to 15.10. In my opinion, offering an upgrade from 14.04 to 15.10 is a bug and the upgrade is known to be problematic. To make things worse, you had a power failure during the upgrade. It may be possible to fix your 15.10 (but hidden problems may remain), but even then you still need an upgrade to 16.04. Better try a clean install of 16.04.
