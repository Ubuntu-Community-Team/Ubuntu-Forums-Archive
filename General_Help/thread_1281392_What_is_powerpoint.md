### Post by DeMus on 2009-10-03
> **nothingspecial said:**
> My wife says she needs it for college.

Do I need to get windows? Or is there a way round it.

Does your wife need to make Powerpoints, slideshows, or does she "only" need a program to play the slideshows?
If it is the last then openoffice.org Presentation is more than adequate. It's part of the openoffice.org suite.
When you need to make slideshows for a Windows audience then you probably need the real Powerpoint program, although Presentation gets better and better. I don't know if you can make a 100% Powerpoint substitute with Presentation though.
