### Post by imtheguru on 2008-03-16
> with similar features and feel?
Features, perhaps... feel, probably no. You will have to adapt to the new program.

Eye of gnome (default) is a simple image viewer. Supports saving in png, tiff, jpeg, bmp, ico.
GPicView is minimalistic, features buttons below the image. Save support is limited to jpeg.

F-spot, Gthumb and Picasa for Linux, are alternatives, but behave more like digital photo albums.
