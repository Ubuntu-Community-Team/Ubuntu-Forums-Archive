### Post by sonofusion82 on 2007-08-02
to remove the the side image, right click on your task bar -> Configure Panel -> Menus -> uncheck Show side image

for the themes, you can try installing from themes from [http://www.kde-look.org/](http://www.kde-look.org/)

### Post by Azzco on 2008-05-02
The menu is a standard kmenu, to achieve the effect you'd have to remove the sidebar as explained above. the colour is achieved through colour schemes.

Open systemsetting, go to appearance, click on the area under the dropdown menu and enter the value #191919 for the same effect (Yeah I just ripped the colour from the picture).
