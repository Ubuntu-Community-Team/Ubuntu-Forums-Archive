### Post by mooreted on 2017-04-06
Double-click the word. If you hold down the CTRL key you can double-click/select multiple words. If you hold down the SHIFT key you can select the first word then the last word to select a bunch of text.

### Post by dragonfly41 on 2017-04-07
Being curious, I tried this in LibreWriter.

Double click anywhere in first word to highlight this whole word.
Hold down Shift key.
Double click on any whitespace *after* the last word (i.e. do not click in the word itself).
This selects and highlights text from first word to last word (inclusive) but ignores whitespace.
Does this not meet your goal of less precision needed in selection of start and end words?
