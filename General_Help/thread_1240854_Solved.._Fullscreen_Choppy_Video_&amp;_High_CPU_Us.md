### Post by cottfcfan on 2009-08-15
After numerous posts & experiments ive finally solved this problem.
I`m running a Dell Inspiron 531s with an AMD Athlon64 Processor, and have suffered the above problem since day 1. Hope this helps others with the same issue.

At boot enter the BIOS its F2 on my compt.
Go to Advanced tab,
CPU Configuration.
Then "disable" AMD Cool & Quiet Function.
Save changes and reboot.
Voila!!!! No more choppy fullscreen video.
