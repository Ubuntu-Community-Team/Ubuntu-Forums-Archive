### Post by 3rdalbum on 2009-08-12
It looks like your original post got buried. This is an active forum and if nobody online in one particular hour can help you, then your post will get bumped off the front page and then few people will see it after that.

I suggest bumping your post every day or every couple of hours; you can do this by just replying to the message with the word "bump". This will push your thread back to the top of the front page.

Just because nobody was online at that particular time who knew about the contents of a Toshiba Quasimodo or whatever it is, does not mean that the support is "rubbish". I'll make allowances for you being frustrated, that's why I'm not taking offence at this.

You have had some replies to your original post now, which might have been your intention...

EDIT: Paid support is an excellent idea. It's not cheap but you'll have someone who you can call or e-mail between 9am and 5pm, for any problem with any package that's in "main" or "restricted". ALSA (the sound system) is in "main" so your sound problem is covered.
