### Post by hikaricore on 2006-11-07
You may want to take note of what is running when your system locks up.  What devices are being used?  Is audio playing, are you using a graphicly intense direct rending program/game?  Are you attempting anytype of network connection?  The solution to these issues often involves something very simple that is easy to overlook while you're frustrated such as video or audio drivers.  You might also want to run a memory tester to make sure you don't have any issues with your RAM.  :)
