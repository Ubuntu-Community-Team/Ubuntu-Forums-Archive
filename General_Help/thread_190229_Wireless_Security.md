### Post by cleentrax on 2006-06-06
Well, running Ubuntu is more secure than Windows, both by design and by obscurity.

WPA is more secure than WEP. If your campus network is on WPA, you will need to add wpa-supplicant to Ubuntu.

As you're interested in security, you should look into using PGP encryption on your emails.

And to minimize casual data theft, be sure to require a password after your laptop wakes up from sleep, screensaver, and the lid opening.
