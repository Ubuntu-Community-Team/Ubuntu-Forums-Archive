### Post by plucky on 2011-01-18
> **LonelyAspie said:**
> I have just installed Ubuntu 10.10 the other day and learned a lot about it. One thing I noticed quickly is that, the applications to not show up in the task bar, so I have to switch from one to another via ATL+Tab.

Many times I accidentally open another thing of Firefox and have two of the same thing open. How do I get it show you can see which apps you have open in the task bar?

Right click on the bottom panel and select **Add to Panel**

In the window that opens find **Window List** and select **Add**.

You should now be able to see your applications.

Good Luck
