### Post by p_quarles on 2007-10-07
Not all printers are going to support multi-page printing from the driver. You can always try looking for a Linux driver made by the printer's manufacturer, but it'll be a lot easier just to use individual applications to print multiple pages. Most apps that you would use for printing (pdf viewers, word processors, desktop publishing) support multi-page printing.
