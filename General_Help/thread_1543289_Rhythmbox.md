### Post by howefield on 2010-07-31
I think you need to play with the options in the status plugin.

Rhythmbox > Edit > Plugins > Status Icon.

---

### Post by thefallofroy on 2010-07-31
That did the trick all right! You have to change the "Status Icon" option to always visible. Thanks a lot man!
