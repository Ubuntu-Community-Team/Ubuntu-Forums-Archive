### Post by deadflowr on 2016-10-09
Do you want to tweak some eye candy and a configure a setting or two that you can't easily do otherwise?
Then it would still be worthwhile to install.
If you are fine with the setup you have and can tweak the setting you want with other means (cli/dconf-editor,something else), then it would not be beneficial to install.
But it does make life easier, since it's barebones simple to understand, compared to other methods of manipulation.
