There is also tilda and yakuake. They are also not exactly want you want, but provide easy access to a terminal. Cairo-dock also has a terminal applet, but I prefer the one available for AWN. Tilda is the best among the standalone ones, IMO.

### Post by fabounet on 2009-10-20
the advantage of Cairo-Dock is that you can detach the terminal from the dock and make it live on your desktop; then you can place it on the Widget Layer, or show/hide it with a keyboard shortcuts, which is extremely convenient.
it also has tabs and real transparency.
