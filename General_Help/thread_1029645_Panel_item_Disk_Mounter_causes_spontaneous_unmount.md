### Post by michiel33 on 2009-01-03
Since a few days, on my Ubuntu 8.04 installation on my laptop, I suffered from a strange phenomenon whereby after a few hours, all my USB drives suddenly are dismounted. They could not be brought back by e.g. disconnect/connect and were not visible in the Partition Editor.:( Then I realised that this coincided with the installation on my Panel of the Disk Mounter tool. So today I removed the Disk Mounter and the problem seems to be over! :D
My conclusion is that the Disk Mounter tool is wacky! Anyone else with a similar experience?
