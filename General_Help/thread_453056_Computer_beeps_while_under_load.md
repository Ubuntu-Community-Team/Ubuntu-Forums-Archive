### Post by eXisor on 2007-05-25
Your cpu temperature warning setting in the bios is maybe too low.

Go into bios (DEL or F10) and look for the System Health section. Either switch cpu temp warning off or set it to only warn when it gets to 80 or 90 Celcius. 90 is what I use since cpu intensive games like X2 and X3 regularly take me into the 80's.

If it's already thereabouts, then it may be that the heatsink is clogged with dust. You'll need to get new thermal paste before you try clean that.
