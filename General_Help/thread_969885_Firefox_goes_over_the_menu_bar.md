### Post by fooman on 2008-11-03
try this:

open ff and do the f11 to fullscreen and then f11 once again to get to a normal size screen.

then unmaximize firefox (not minimize to the bottom panel).

close firefox.

open firefox and maximize it.

see if that helps.

this was happening to me every time i opened ff after installing 8.10.  since trying the above fix...it has not come back!
