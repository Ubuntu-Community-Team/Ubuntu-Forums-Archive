### Post by KeyserSoze93 on 2009-03-20
OOCalc doesn't know you want to import is as a CSV (or TSV or whatever it is).

You need to click the filetype dropdown and select Text CSV (.txt .csv .xls).

Or, alternatively, you could rename the file from X.txt to X.csv, that way OOCalc would known what you wanted to open it as automatically.
