### Post by Kyral on 2005-11-02
*Note, didn't look at the links*

You can trim down GNOME nicely, but if you want a really quick performance jump you have to use a lighter WM. Swapping out MetaCity for Openbox in GNOME can help, but ditching GNOME completely (Don't remove it, it still provides useful stuff) and switching to something like XFCE or something even lighter like Enlightenment or Fluxbox will do wonders
