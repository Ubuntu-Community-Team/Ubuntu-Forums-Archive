### Post by estyles on 2009-10-31
first thing - stop using the installed OS.  Boot from a LiveCD and use that to try and fix the problem, because there's still a very slight chance that you can recover something if it hasn't been overwritten yet.

Once you've booted from the LiveCD, search the forums for "TestDisk".  You should be able to find a thread that explains how to use testdisk to recover lost data.

I agree with the previous poster that I unfortunately don't like your chances, but it's at least worth a try...  I wish you good luck.
