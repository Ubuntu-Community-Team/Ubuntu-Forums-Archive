### Post by shane634 on 2007-01-22
To add workspaces simply right click on the workspaces area and select Preferences then add as many as you want. For the gnome panel color right click anywhere and select change desktop background. Also you can to to System-->Preferences-->Theme to change the theme. Hope this helps.
