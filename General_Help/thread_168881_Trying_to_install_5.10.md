### Post by zxee on 2006-05-01
The other thing to check is if your bios is set up for booting from the optical drive-some bios' do not come that way i.e you have to enter bios and select the cdrom as your 1st or 2nd boot device. If the hard drive is listed before the cdrom the computer will always boot from the hard drive. Hope that helps.
