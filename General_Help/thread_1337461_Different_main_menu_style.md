### Post by coffeecat on 2009-11-25
Have a look here:

[http://packages.linuxmint.com/#8main](http://packages.linuxmint.com/#8main)

The package mintmenu comes as a deb and can be installed in Ubuntu to give you the Mint Menu. Make sure you download the package appropriate for your Ubuntu release.

You might want to search the forum for threads on the Mint menu. I seem to remember reading something about a year ago and there was some minor issue using Mint menu in Ubuntu, but I can't remember the details.

As an alternative, you could right-click on the panel, choose 'Add to Panel' and add 'Main Menu'. You get just an Ubuntu logo which when clicked gives you the usual menus all in one. You could put this bottom left if you want. 

'Menu Bar' is the standard 3-menu menu system.
