### Post by Hamchan on 2009-05-27
you actually can do this all using GUI and can forget about the terminal. Downloading the tar.gz from adobe labs, then go to your home folder. Click the View menu and choose "Show Hidden Files" and find the one marked .mozilla. That period means it's a hidden file. Go into that directory and create a new folder called "plugins"

Double click the tar.gz file and extract it to pretty much anywhere you can find it. Then just drag and drop it into this new "plugins" folder.
