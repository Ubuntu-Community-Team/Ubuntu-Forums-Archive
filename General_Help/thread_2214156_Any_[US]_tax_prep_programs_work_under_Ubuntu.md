### Post by efflandt on 2014-03-30
[http://www.taxact.com/](http://www.taxact.com/)

They are free, or $12.95 if you have less than simple forms and want them to keep info to make following year easier. State is extra, but many states have their own free e-file.

I usually do them on downloaded PDF forms first, then on taxact, and if everything agrees I am good to go. If they do not agree I figure out where "I" made a mistake.
