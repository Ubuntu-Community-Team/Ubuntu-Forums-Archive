### Post by mike41 on 2006-10-07
Don't worry about that. The screen is still there, you just cant see it. Its a small bug in compiz. Press space bar and the computer will exit the logout screen and go back to ubuntu.

One work around is to just open up a terminal and type

sudo shutdown -h now

OR, even better, just go to the first sticky of this forum, and install BERYL, which is the new project that has taken over compiz.
