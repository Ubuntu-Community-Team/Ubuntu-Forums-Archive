Yes you can and you don't even need to use the terminal. Go to system then preferences then sound and under the devices tab switch sound events, music and movies, and audio conferencing to ALSA - Advanced Linux Sound Architecture. Then I guess you are just going to have to wait and see if this solves the problem.
