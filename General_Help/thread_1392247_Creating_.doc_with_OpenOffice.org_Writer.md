### Post by Hagar Delest on 2010-01-30
Are you sure that the pics are not inserted as links instead of being embedded? Basically, better save the pic on your HD (in a dedicated folder) and then insert it with Insert>Picture>From File and make sure the *As link* checkbox is not checked.

NB: MS Office .docx is a shame, better avoid it (anyway, OOo can't save in that format, at least in the Sun version).
