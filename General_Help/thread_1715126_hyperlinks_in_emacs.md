### Post by jpkotta on 2011-03-31
> **TheHimself said:**
> Is there any mode which allows having hyperlinks in emacs? I'm just looking for a wiki that works from inside emacs.

[Org mode]("http://www.emacswiki.org/emacs/OrgMode") supports hyperlinks.  [Emacs Muse]("http://www.emacswiki.org/emacs/EmacsMuse") does too, which is the successor to [wiki mode]("http://www.emacswiki.org/emacs/EmacsWikiMode").  I've only used org-mode, but I haven't tried hyperlinks yet.
