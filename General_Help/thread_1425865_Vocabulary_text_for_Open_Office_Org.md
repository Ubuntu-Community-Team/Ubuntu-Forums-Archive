### Post by coffeecat on 2010-03-09
The good news is that I have found the location of the file. It's:

~/.openoffice.org/3/user/wordbook/standard.dic (where ~ = your home folder).

The bad news is that I can't see how to open it in an editable format. Gedit fails and if you open it in openoffice, you get some garbage as well as your added words.

### Post by Ilalmi on 2010-03-09
As described in [http://user.services.openoffice.org/en/forum/viewtopic.php?f=6&t=269:](http://user.services.openoffice.org/en/forum/viewtopic.php?f=6&t=269:)

> Tools>Options>Language Settings>Writing Aids, then in the right  area, in the middle select the *Standard*  entry and *Edit* button on the  right.
