 Only at random times I'll get nothing, no search result, it's just a Google page but it's not the regular Google page it's [www.google.com/firefox](www.google.com/firefox) but without any search results. My default home webpage is [www.google.com](www.google.com) not the one with /firefox.

 Weird. Has anyone else seen this result?
