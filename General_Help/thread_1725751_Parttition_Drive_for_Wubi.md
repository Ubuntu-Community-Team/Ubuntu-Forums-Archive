### Post by Rubi1200 on 2011-04-10
See the Wubi Guide:
[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/WubiGuide](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/WubiGuide)

The default is to install to the C drive, but when the installer starts you can also choose another location.

But, you do not need to format anything as Wubi uses a virtual file-system inside Windows.

Once it completes in Windows, you will be prompted to reboot to complete the installation.
