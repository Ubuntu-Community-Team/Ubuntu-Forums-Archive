### Post by o.besner on 2008-01-24
There's not much information in your picture... is it a laptop or a desktop ?

The alternate install would be a good choice, but I would also have a look at the help menu of the cd's main menu. (I think you can access it with F5 or F6) 

With my laptop (HP dv9417ca) I had to use a special boot parameter with Ubuntu 7.04 because my computer kept crashing during the installation: -no acpi -no lacpi , because my motherboard was buggy until they released a new bios. You can try it if you want, I know it solves alot of hanging and freezing during the install.

Good luck !
