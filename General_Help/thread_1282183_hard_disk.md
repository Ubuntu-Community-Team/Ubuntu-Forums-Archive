### Post by crolanx on 2009-10-04
I think that you want to enlarge the Ubuntu-Partition, right?

The safest way to do that, is to use GParted from a live-CD.

As a precaution, create a backup of all important files first and  be very careful, because with GParted you can really destroy your files if you make a mistake.
