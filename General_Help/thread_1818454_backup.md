### Post by ghostborg on 2011-08-04
For backing up your bookmarks in FF 5:
Click on Bookmarks=> Show All Bookmarks and a window will pop up and near the top is Import and Backup Options.

Or with FF in focus click cntrl + shift + O (letter O , not zero)
