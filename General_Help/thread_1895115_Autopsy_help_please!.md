### Post by Lars Noodén on 2011-12-14
> ***Kat* said:**
> I just want to know how you find out who the owner of a file is? 

In Nautilus, right click on the icon then Properties->Permissions

Or in the terminal, use [ls -l](http://manpages.ubuntu.com/manpages/precise/en/man1/ls.1posix.html) or [ll](http://manpages.ubuntu.com/manpages/precise/en/man1/ls.1posix.html)

### Post by BC59 on 2011-12-14
For a permanent solution you open nautilus and go to Edit--Preferences. From there check Owner, Permissions and whatever file data you need to see.
