### Post by Luffer on 2009-05-16
I've got a panel issue with my top menu. Somehow my "User Switcher" vanished on a reboot. I managed to get it back but now it sits the left of the date and time and other icons. How can I move it back to the right?

### Post by Mirge on 2009-05-16
Right-click it, click "Move", make sure "Lock to Panel" isn't checked... might have to unlock other ones on the panel to move it where you want. You can right-click & "Move" each of them, then lock to panel if you want.
