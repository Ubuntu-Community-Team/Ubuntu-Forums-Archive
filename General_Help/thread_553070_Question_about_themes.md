### Post by Wolki on 2007-09-17
That screenshot seems to be KDE, not GNOME.

You can edit the panel appearance by right-clicking on some free space and selecting properties. You can change the panel layout by right-clicking on free space and selecting "Add to panel" to add stuff, right-clicking on applets and selecting move or remove to reorder or delete stuff.
