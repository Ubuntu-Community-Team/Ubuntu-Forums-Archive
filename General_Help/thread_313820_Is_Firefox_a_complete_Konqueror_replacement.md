### Post by madcow72 on 2006-12-06
Probably, you just want to change Firefox to be your default browser so that when you click on a link, Firefox opens instead of Konqueror.  You can do this by going to "System Settings"->"Default Applications"->"Web Browser" and changing it to firefox.  (At least that's the way in KDE.  Is it different in Gnome?)
