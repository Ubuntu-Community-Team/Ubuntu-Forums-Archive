### Post by syklops on 2010-01-16
To fix this problem, open termianl/Konsole or another terminal emulator of your choice.

Type the followng commands into the shell:

```
cd /tmp
wget http://download.macromedia.com/pub/labs/flashplayer10/flashplayer10_1_p2_linux_121709.tar.gz
tar zxvf flashplayer10_1_p2_linux_121709.tar.gz
cd install_flash_player_10_linux/
sh flashplayer-installer

```

press Y and Enter to accept the questions. Make sure to close your browsers when it asks.

When the script finishes start your browser, and zynga poker should now work.

This also works if yo!ville and many other flash-based games on Facebook.
