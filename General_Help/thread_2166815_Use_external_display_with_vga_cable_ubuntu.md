### Post by bsamim on 2013-08-11
* Plug the monitor in the VGA slot.
* Go to System Settings and then choose Display, or just hit the windows key and type Displays and select the app.
* Afterwards see if your monitor is detected after plugging it in. It might be just enabling it and setting the resolution in this application.
