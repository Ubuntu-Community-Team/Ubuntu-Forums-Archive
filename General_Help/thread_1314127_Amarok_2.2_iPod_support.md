### Post by GeeK_KolA on 2009-11-14
Lol actually i figured it out...when i open up amarok and attach my ipod...i first open the "files" browser where you have the option to choose internet, local, playlists, and files. Then right above the browser that opens to the left theres a small icon, click it and you see options for HOME and ROOT, but your ipod should be in the menu...click that...wait a moment...and your ipod loads up. Then go back to the main options, select local and the ipod comes up. O.o I tried using gtkpod...and I have noticed that the files hash a lot quicker Karmic than the last release, but my music isn't organized right on my ipod and I'm very anal about my music.....HA...I said anal..but no times for laughs now O.o....anywhore....
