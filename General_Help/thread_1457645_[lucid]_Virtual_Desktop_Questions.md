### Post by Ryan Dwyer on 2010-04-18
This behaviour hasn't changed as far as I know. You should have the desktop thumbnails in the bottom right. Right click them and go to Preferences to change the number of desktops.

If you don't have the icon, right click on the panel, choose Add to Panel then choose Workspace Switcher.
