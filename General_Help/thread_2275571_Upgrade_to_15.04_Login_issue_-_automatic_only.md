### Post by Rex Bouwense on 2015-04-27
When you enter your password, 
1.  Is the caps lock off?
2.  Are you typing in the password that you used when you initially installed Lubuntu or have you forgotten the initial password?

If the answer to both questions is yes, then something is obviously messed up.  You can fix this using the procedures that are found in this site.
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/LostPassword](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/LostPassword)
