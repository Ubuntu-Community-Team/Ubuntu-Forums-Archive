### Post by aysiu on 2008-03-25
Boot the Desktop CD, and it'll run a "live session" that won't affect your hard drive. From that live session, you can see what works and what doesn't.

### Post by mlapaglia on 2008-03-25
For most people the worst that can happen is ubuntu doesn't boot up. just pop in your windows xp disc and you can put windows back on.

most of the time there will be one or two things that don't work with your computer, like a usb card reader or the webcam, not the <i>entire</i> system.
