Even then it will not not be my main OS. For day to day use it will be this same Ubuntu box or I'm sad to say my XP Pro box.

It wasn't till just before launch that Microsoft lost, in obscure court, their right to lock out vendors and deny code. I don't know about you but I'm not too trusting of Microsoft's own security (now or ever). Even all the new advances they promised in security melted away. Two years ago there was all these advanced features and month by month they vanished.

Don't get me wrong, I'm not a Microsoft hater. I would suggest waiting a bit before a so called upgrade. Let the bugs get fixed, maybe even wait for their first service pack. I plan on building a Vista based system but not just now. If you can't wait for all the eyecandy, get a Mac. Microsoft did after all copy them for the most part.
