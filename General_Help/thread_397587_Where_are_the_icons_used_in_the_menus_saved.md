### Post by johnc4510 on 2007-03-30
Right click on anywhere on the drop down menu and choose edit menus. This will launch ala carte menu editor. Go to the one you want to change and right click on it and choose properties. Then click on the icon in the window that opens. The path to the file will show just under the menu.
