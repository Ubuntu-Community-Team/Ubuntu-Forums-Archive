Does your computer connect to the wifi, i.e. when you click on the wifi indicator and show connection information, does the dialog box show a connection, give a speed and so forth?

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1857422](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1857422)

If not, there may be another issue preventing you from connecting.
