No. Ubuntu 10.10 (Maverick Meerkat) is still in alpha (will not be be released until October), and it's kernel and x server is newer than what ati's driver supports.
The last couple of ubuntu releases ati have provided support by releasing a beta version of their driver to the ubuntu developers late in the development cycle. So be patient. It will probably come in September or beginning of October...

And: This is not a "general support" question. Questions about Maverick belongs in the [Maverick development testing forum]("http://ubuntuforums.org/forumdisplay.php?f=385").
