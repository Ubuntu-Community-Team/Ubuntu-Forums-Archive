### Post by el3ktro on 2006-01-11
Hi, I also have an Athlon64, and at first, I had the 64bit-version of Kubuntu installed. Everything worked fine, except a few things:

- OpenOffice (which still is 32bit) looked ugly
- Skype didn't work very well
- No Flash in Konqueror
- Some popular video codecs can't be played

So in the end, I decided to use a 32bit-version of Kubuntu, which is really the same as the 64bit-version, only that the above problems are gone. To sum it up: As of now, I'd suggest you stick with 32bit, except you don't need/want the things mentioned above.
