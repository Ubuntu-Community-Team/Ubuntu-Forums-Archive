### Post by grahammechanical on 2013-05-03
Open Software Sources>Updates and set it to be notified of the any new release. It will be set to Next LTS. Then run check or reload the utility and it will invite you to click a button. You will get 12.10 and then you have to move up to 13.04 BUT 13.04 only has 9 months support. So, in six months time you will have to upgrade to 13.10 and then in another six months you will have to upgrade to 14.04. Just so that you know.

Regards.
