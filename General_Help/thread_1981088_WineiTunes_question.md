### Post by Mark Phelps on 2012-05-16
Just so you know, regarding the app ratings from the WineHQ site:
1) Garbage -- means it won't work, no matter what you do
2) Bronze -- means is barely works, some or most of the major functionality does not work.

So, trying to use iTunes in Linux is basically an exercise in frustration.
