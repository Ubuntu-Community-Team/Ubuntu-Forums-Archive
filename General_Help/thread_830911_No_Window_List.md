### Post by drs305 on 2008-06-16
Right click the bottom panel, Add to Panel, Window List.

But you say "switch between windows", so what you may really want is "Window Selector", same procedure.

On my machine it appears only as a very thin line. If you already have it installed and that is your problem someone else will have to reply.
