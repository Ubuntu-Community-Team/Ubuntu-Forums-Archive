### Post by jonrkc on 2005-08-26
You might try, if you haven't already, setting your font preferences in the edit > preferences dialog, and then checking "Always use my fonts" towards the bottom of the thing.  With very few exceptions, pages should then display in whatever fonts and sizes you wanted, not what the HTML or even the stylesheet dictates.  I've noticed a FEW pages where somehow my preferences were not respected, but very few.  

You can always change it back temporarily if you have a site you need to see the way the designer set it up.
