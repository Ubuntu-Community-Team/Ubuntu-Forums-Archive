### Post by tango_ninja on 2008-02-25
Looks like your BIOS settings are making the install cd go to your floppy drive. Go into your BIOS settings (when the computer starts it always displays that 16-colour screen allowing you 5 seconds to hit F12 or whatever for Setup). change your BIOS so that your floppy drive is disabled
