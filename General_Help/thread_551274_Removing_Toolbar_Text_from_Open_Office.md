### Post by mikewhatever on 2007-09-15
Open OO writer and go to Tools>Customize>Toolbars Tab. Now click Toolbar button on the right and select icons only. You may also want to make the icons smaller, by going to Tools>Options>OpeOffice.org>View and under Icon size and style selecting small.

### Post by p_quarles on 2007-09-15
Pretty easy to do:

1) Open an OO.org app
2) Click on "Tools"
3) Select "Customize"
4) Click the button labeled "Toolbars"
5) Select "icons only" from the drop-down menu
