### Post by hanzomon4 on 2006-12-19
Ok, I looked around and found this [QUOTE=steve_a_hart]FINALLY! I switched my video driver from i810 to vesa and everything works now. After doing ALOT of surfing and reading, the problem lies with the i810 driver (even the new one). So until Intel fixes this and the yum repositories make it available, I just plan to use the vesa driver.

good luck all![/QUOTE]

So change this ```
Section "Device"
Identifier "Intel Corporation Mobile 915GM/GMS/910GML Express Graphics Controller"
Driver "i810"
BusID "PCI:0:2:0"
EndSection
```

To this:```
Section "Device"
Identifier "Intel Corporation Mobile 915GM/GMS/910GML Express Graphics Controller"
Driver "**[COLOR="Red"]vesa[/COLOR]**"
BusID "PCI:0:2:0"
EndSection
```

Then restart the xserver: ctrl+alt+backspace
