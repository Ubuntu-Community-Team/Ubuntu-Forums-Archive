### Post by cookies on 2007-06-07
[quote="Wikipedia"]
In GNOME 2.14, a saved search is a virtual folder whose contents are the result of a Nautilus search, which has multiple backends. The contents of these folders are determined dynamically when the folder is opened, and updated automatically when files are created or modified.[/quote]

So, if you save a search, it becomes a "Smart Folder".

So, make folder, search for your stuff in it, and it becomes "Smart".
