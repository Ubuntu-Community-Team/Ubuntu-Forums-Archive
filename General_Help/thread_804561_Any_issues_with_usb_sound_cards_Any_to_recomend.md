### Post by ibuclaw on 2008-05-23
Not really, all generic USB soundcards are pretty much exactly the same.

Though, there is the exception (Having quick look at the page and the Tascam springs to mind), but [drivers already exist for the majority of them.]("http://alsa-project.org/main/index.php/Matrix:Main")

I have a [Zoom G2.1u]("http://www.amazon.co.uk/Zoom-G2-1U-guitar-effects-included/dp/B000K9NVUW") myself.

Though to get it working optimal with Ubuntu, you'll have to disable your onboard soundcard in BIOS.
