### Post by Tmcarr on 2011-04-18
You can right click on the panel, click "Add to Panel" and the things that are missing will be listed in a window that pops up. Just click and drag the ones you want back onto the panel.

Ive had this issue in GNOME as far back as I can remember. The best thing I can tell you is to add what you want, right click on EVERY button and "Lock to Panel". Seems to help alot.
