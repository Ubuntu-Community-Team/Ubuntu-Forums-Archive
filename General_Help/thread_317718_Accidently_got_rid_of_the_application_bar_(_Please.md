### Post by fragos on 2006-12-12
Right click an empty portion of the panel and select add to panel.  There's no end of interesting things you can add.  The one I think you're looking for is "Main Menu" or "Menu Bar".  While you're there, check out the Deskbar applet it's really usefull.
