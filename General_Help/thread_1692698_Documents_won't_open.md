### Post by DanneStrat on 2011-02-21
> **sewbiz said:**
> Folders open but the any of my documents in OpenOffice.org won't open.

Try to right click a document and select "properties".

On the "open with" tab select "open office". Now

try to open them.

You can do this procedure whenever files won't open at all

or in the program you want it to. Just set it to open

with the appropriate program.
