### Post by dcstar on 2008-01-24
> **Handssolow said:**
> Occasionally I receive emails that I can hardly read because the text is too pale. Why is this?

My guess is that the sender has sent me coloured text rather than in black and white.

Any help and suggestions?

Assuming you are using Evolution and are receiving HTML e-mails, you may try to change your Edit-Preferences-Mail Preferences-HTML Mail-Plain Text Mode to "Only ever...." and reload the e-mail, you should be able to then view the HTML attachment in a browser which may render it differently.

The problem is caused by mail formatted to a Microsoft "standard", rather than the standard that Evolution expects.
