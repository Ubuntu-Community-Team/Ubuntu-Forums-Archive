### Post by mcduck on 2008-08-08
It's a sign that the program in question is not responding any more. The black & white effect is done by Compiz (the "Desktop Effects") to inform you about the situation.

In some cases it might just be that the app is working very hard, and after a while starts responding again and the colors will return. But usually the program has crashed.
