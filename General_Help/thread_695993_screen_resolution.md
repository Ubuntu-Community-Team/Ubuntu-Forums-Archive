### Post by pointone on 2008-02-13
Just for your information...

I'm guessing this is an LCD display based on that resolution. LCD's have what's called [a native resolution]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Native_resolution"). Using a resolution besides the native resolution usually results in some ugly-looking text. Furthermore, you're using a widescreen display with a 16:10 aspect ratio. Switching to a non-widescreen aspect ratio will force the display to scale the image. Depending on what it's scaling to, this will usually cause warped shapes (i.e., circles won't be circular anymore) or other odd problems.

Long story short: If you're just having trouble reading text because it's too small, just try increasing the default font size (System > Preferences > Fonts).
