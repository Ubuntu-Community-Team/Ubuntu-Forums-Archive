### Post by codergirlruby on 2020-11-16
I was having this very same issue. [http://ubuntuhandbook.org/index.php/2020/10/enable-drag-drop-desktop-ubuntu-20-04/](http://ubuntuhandbook.org/index.php/2020/10/enable-drag-drop-desktop-ubuntu-20-04/) 
This web page helped me to resolve the problem. I made sure that DING was in my home folder, I toggled the icon to the "ON" position in my gnome extensions page. Opened my extensions setting which was located applications folder and has green/black/grey puzzle pieces and disabled desktop icons (DING) was automatically enabled. Hope this was easy to understand for you and helps you with your problem.
