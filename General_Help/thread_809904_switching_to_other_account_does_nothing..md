### Post by nelsonm on 2008-05-27
Hi all,

I have Ubuntu 8.04 running on on windows xp.

I have three ubuntu accounts. The problem is that i can't switch directly to the third account anymore if its currently active. Selecting the third account from "User Switcher" does nothing when i select it.

I can only switch to the third account;
1. by switching to the second account and then selecting the third account
2. if the third account is logged off initially.

The "User Switcher" was working for all account but not now.

Any clues?

---

### Post by nelsonm on 2008-05-27
Ok...

Appearently, the "User Switcher" instance on the panel must of gotten corrupted.

I deleted the instance on the panel then put a new copy of it from the "Add to Panel" window.

That corrected the problem.

Thanks to myself! ;)
