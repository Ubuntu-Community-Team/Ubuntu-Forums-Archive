### Post by Lauri Pirttiaho on 2010-03-03
Hello,

Canon and ALi (the maker of the chip in Canoscan 3200F) appear to have
that "classified" attitude towards the scanner products, so developing
driver for 3200F has to be made through reverse engineering and 
experimenting. That is, however, quite time consuming, and since
supported scanners are available easily, supporting Canon products
is not cost efficient.

-- Lauri
