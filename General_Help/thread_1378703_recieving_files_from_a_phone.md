### Post by scouser73 on 2010-01-11
When you say > I can pair to a phone but when they try to send me files do you mean another person with a phone?

When you go to pair a phone with a device using Bluetooth, both the phone and the other device need to use a pass-code.  So your phone will be **1234567890** and the computer will also use **1234567890**  The code can be whatever you want, but it must match to the device you are trying to pair with.

I hope this helps.
