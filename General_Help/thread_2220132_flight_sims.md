### Post by Impavidus on 2014-04-26
[Flightgear](http://www.flightgear.org/). It's in the repos, but it's an older version for 12.04. Better use the PPA, as it will be easier to get the recent add-ons. I added the PPA with```
sudo add-apt-repository ppa:saiarcot895/flightgear
```Note that it takes quite a lot of memory when flying through the Alps. I just upgraded my computer to 8GiB.
