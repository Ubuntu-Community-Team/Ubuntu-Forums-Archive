### Post by Holger_Gehrke on 2015-04-29
GIMPs built in help is actually quite good. There's also documentation and tutorials at gimp.org. There's also a free book named "Grokking the GIMP" which might still be found somewhere, but it's probably severely out of date (I recall that it was written for 2.2, or something there about).

One option people who aren't used to GIMP's multi-window UI should try is "Single-Window-Mode" in the "Window"-menu.
