### Post by joekilner on 2009-09-26
Sounds to me like a mouse problem. Maybe some software you are using is not letting go of the mouse for some reason. 

I have had similar problems in the past and find that switching to another console and back can fix this without rebooting. Try pressing Ctrl-Alt-F1 (which will put you in a full-screen terminal) and then Ctrl-Alt-F7 (which will switch you back to your desktop). Sometimes that can free the mouse back up.

Hope that helps
