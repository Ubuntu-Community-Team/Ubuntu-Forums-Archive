### Post by Impavidus on 2018-05-21
Take a word list, pick some random words. For example:```
$ sort -R /usr/share/dict/british-english | head -n 20
nectarine
morgue's
administered
zeros
sailboard's
scalpel's
marinades
chaffinch
Budweiser's
carbine
spasmodic
refugee
whisker's
Peterson
Jungfrau's
cinder's
carnal
balsams
maypoles
superman

```
