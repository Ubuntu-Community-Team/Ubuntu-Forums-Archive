### Post by mb_webguy on 2008-09-17
> **ruza said:**
> I have HP530 laptop with dualboot, Vista and Ubuntu...  Ok, I know it is illegal... Say it like that, if I had downloaded Mac OS X torrent, would I be able to install it? And how? I don't wish to reinstall neither Vista nor Ubuntu...

Dual-booting Vista and Ubuntu on your HP530 laptop is only illegal if you have a pirated version of Vista or if you stole the laptop.  Installing a version of OS X that you downloaded via bittorrent, on the other hand, would certainly not be legal.

However, let's assume for the sake of discussion that you purchased your laptop and have legal copies of Vista and OS X.  You'll need at least 9GB of space to install OS X.  If you can shrink one of your existing partitions by that much you should be okay.  After you install OS X, you may need to reinstall GRUB.
