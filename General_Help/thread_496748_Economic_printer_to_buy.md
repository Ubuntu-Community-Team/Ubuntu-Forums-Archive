Do they have 4 (or 3) cartridges or just a single one containing all colours?

Color lasers usually have 4 cartridges (black, cyan, magenta, yellow) and they sometimes run about $75 each.

Lasers don't need special paper unless you are printing photographs.  Inkjets will print on regular paper except that the ink will bleed.  For text inkjets need a special coated inkjet paper.

Color lasers are more economical to own.
