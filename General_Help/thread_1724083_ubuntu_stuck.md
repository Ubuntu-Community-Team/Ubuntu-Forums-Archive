### Post by timgood on 2011-04-08
You really need to give more information. Freezes can be caused by hardware faults, overheating, software faults, processor glitches etc. and without info about your machine, appropriate logfiles and how and when the freezes happen it is impossible for us to offer you any help.

After a freeze and reboot examine these logfiles and look for events at around the time of the freeze:

/var/log/dmesg.0
/var/log/syslog
/var/log/messages
/var/log/Xorg.0.log
