### Post by ajgreeny on 2010-07-28
If the problem is in FF alone, you could try changing the fonts setup in FF ->Edit ->Preferences ->Content Tab ->Fonts & Colours Advanced to set your own font size preference.  By default FF sets the fonts as defined by the page being displayed, but you can change that to your own if you want.
