### Post by akand074 on 2010-03-11
easist way is to just open up synaptic package manager and filter by status Installed. Start typing in 2.6.31 and all your linux kernels should pop up, mark all the old ones that you don't use for uninstallation. Also google Ubuntu Tweaks, its a good application that can do a lot of that stuff for you and some neat other stuff if your interested.
