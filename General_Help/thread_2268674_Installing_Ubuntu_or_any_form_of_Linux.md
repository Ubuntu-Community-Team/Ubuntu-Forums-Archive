### Post by yancek on 2015-03-10
Most computers will show a message at the bottom of the screen immediately upon start, if you have a manufacturers logo that should be the screen.  The message usually tell you to hit a specific key to "Enter Setup".  When you do that you should see a boot tab and you use the arrow keys on the keyboard to highlight it and check the options.  The key varies with manufacturer.
