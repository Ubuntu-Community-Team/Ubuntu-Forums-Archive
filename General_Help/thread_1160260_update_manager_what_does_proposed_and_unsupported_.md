### Post by coffeecat on 2009-05-15
By unsupported I suppose you mean the backports, because if you go into Software Sources > Updates tab, it says "Unsupported updates (jaunty-backports". For backports, see the last bit of that link. Backports are certainly safer than proposed I suppose.

So to answer your original questions: no, they won't make your laptop better. And, they're not downloaded in the first place for very good reasons - which the link explains.

Hope that's clear. :)
