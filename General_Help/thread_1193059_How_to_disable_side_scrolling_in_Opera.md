### Post by toasterj on 2010-06-23
The solution was in the shortcuts section but you must also disable the back and forward functions in mouse section. 

Here are the steps. 

[LIST=1][*]Click on Tools[*]Click on Preferences[*]Click the advanced tab[*]Click on Shortcuts in the left bar[*]Next to the mouse setup click on edit[*]Click on "Application (defaults)"[*]find the options that say Button6 and Button7[*]Delete both items in the list[*]Follow the steps above to enable horizontal scrolling
[/LIST]

I had the same problem but I'm using Ubuntu 10.04 and I two finger scrolling enabled on my Acer Aspire laptop. I Googled the problem and found this page. I don't like questions not being answered so I figured it out and posted the steps here. I'm a new user to the forums but no stranger to Ubuntu or lurking on these forums to find answers.
