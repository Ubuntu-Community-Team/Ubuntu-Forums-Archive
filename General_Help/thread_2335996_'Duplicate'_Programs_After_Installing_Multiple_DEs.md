### Post by mhaggard25 on 2016-09-03
So I'm running Ubuntu 14.04 with Mate. I love it. I was trying out some DEs before I ran across Mate, however, and installed a couple that I'm not going to use anymore. Those DEs installed all kinds of programs that I don't use and some seem to be duplicate programs. For example, I now have three Screenshot applications. Is there an easy way to remove these or do I need to do it one at a time? Thanks in advance.

---

### Post by speartip on 2016-09-03
They won't be duplicate programs, they will just be duplicated in the menus, as sometimes different DEs use the same programs.
Uninstalling the DEs you don't need/use, should remove the duplicate programs from the menus. Be careful nothing gets uninstalled that you need.
 On the other hand you could leave it as it is, & just edit the menu to remove the duplicates. Try right clicking the Menu button, & see if you can edit the applications.
