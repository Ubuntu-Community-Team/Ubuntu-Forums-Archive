### Post by kyphi on 2007-12-28
You can specify if you want some icons to be visible on your desktop by going into the Configuration Editor (mine is in Applications -. System Tools).

In apps, scroll down to nautilus and then desktop.  Insert or remove the checkmarks according to what you want to see on your desktop.
