### Post by grahammechanical on 2013-12-18
Do you have a floor plan? Why not photocopy the floor plan and mark the location of the lights on the floor plan.

I used to work a safety officer in a high street store. I photocopied the floors plans and marked the location of every smoke detector in the ceiling and I used this to record my testing of the smoke detectors to prove that each smoke detector was tested according to company policy.

I was not allowed to install software and the OS was Windows anyway. So, I used the drawing facilities of the default installed wordprocessor to create my own floor plans. It is a slow task but it can be done. I wanted to create detailed plans that could be given to the emergency services if they were called to the building. I wanted to create computer based master plans that I could reuse. I retired before I finished this. Well, there were two stores with a total of 8 floors including the roofs which were used for emergency escape. 

Regards.
