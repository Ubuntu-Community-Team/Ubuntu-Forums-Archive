### Post by Starmartyr on 2010-09-09
After a few days of searching, and searching, and installing the latest ALSA patch, which still didn't fix my issue, I have discovered that in Grub the acpi must be off, as follows:

You must modify the configuration file of GRUB:
 
sudo gedit /etc/default/grub

and modify 

GRUB_CMDLINE_LINUX_DEFAULT="quiet splash acpi=off"

then reboot.  Worked well!  Sound is live! Alive! It's alive! It's alive!
