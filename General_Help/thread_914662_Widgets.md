### Post by mb_webguy on 2008-09-09
Somewhat, yes.  There are desklets and screenlets.  Screenlets seem to be the more advanced of the two, but (I believe) require Compiz.  You can find both in the repositories, or you can get them from [Gnome-Look]("http://www.gnome-look.org/").

There are also things that some people consider widget-like but aren't, such as a dock like you see at the bottom of Mac OS X.  In Linux, these are applications, such as Cairo or AWN, both of which, I believe, can be found in the repositories.
