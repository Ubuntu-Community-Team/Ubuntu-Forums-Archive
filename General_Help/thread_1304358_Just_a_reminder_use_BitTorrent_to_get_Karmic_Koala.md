### Post by lovinglinux on 2009-10-29
Also, if you don't have limited bandwidth quotas, then don't simply download the torrent and close your BitTorrent client. Instead, keep seeding it (uploading) after the download is completed, at least until you reach a share ratio of 1:1. This will help to improve download speeds for other BitTorrent downloaders.

I already have Karmic running, but I will download via BitTorrent and will seed it as long as I can.

For those unfamiliar with BitTorrent or experiencing connection or speed issues, see [BitTorrent optimization and troubleshooting guide](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1259923).
