### Post by Dragonbite on 2011-02-22
If the LibreOffice can use your Evolution Address book then you can have Evolution connect to your Google Contacts and auto-sync.

Otherwise there should be a way from the GMail web interface to download your address book as a .CSV or something which you might be able to import into *office.
