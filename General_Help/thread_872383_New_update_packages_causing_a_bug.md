### Post by ramjet_1953 on 2008-07-27
Are you running the game with Visual Effects enabled?

Compiz Fusion often causes problems with full-screen games.

Navigate to [COLOR="Blue"]System>Preferences>Appearance[/COLOR]

Click on the [COLOR="Blue"]Visual Effects Tab[/COLOR] and then click on [COLOR="Blue"]None[/COLOR].

Re-try running your game and see if it is now stable.
