### Post by plucky on 2008-04-17
Can you post a snapshot of your Evolution window?

Goto **View > Preview** and make sure **Show message Preview** is ticked.

There is a possibility you need to drag open your preview window.
Click on **Inbox**.

 Point mouse pointer to just under the **Subject** header,left click and drag open window.You should now see your Unread emails.

Hope this helps.
