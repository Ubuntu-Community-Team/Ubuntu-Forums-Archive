### Post by jimbob on 2009-04-23
Personally I prefer to download and burn a clean copy of the new release and install that in the space occupied by 8.10

I have had discrepancies pop up in the past by upgrading so I prefer to start from scratch.

Back up everything you want to save first.

### Post by Jimmynemo2 on 2009-04-23
While I agree and do what jimbob does, if you want to do the upgrade, and not fresh install it, you can do that by opening your update manager, and it will say new version available. (as long you have show new version checked in settings- a shortcut to that setting is in the lower left box of the update manager)
