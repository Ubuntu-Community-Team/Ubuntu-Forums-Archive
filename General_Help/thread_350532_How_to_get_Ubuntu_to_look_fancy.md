### Post by Sqwishy on 2007-01-31
You should try going to [gnome-look.org]("http://gnome-look.org/"). They have alot of background images, gtk themes, and alot of other stuff you'll probobly wana look at. Once you download a theme you need to click on System>>Preferences>>Themes and then click and drag the theme you downloaded.
