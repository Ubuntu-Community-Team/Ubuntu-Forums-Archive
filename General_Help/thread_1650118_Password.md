### Post by mcduck on 2010-12-21
You can turn it off in the screensaver settings (diasble the "Lock screen when the screensaver is active").

The problem though, is that if you use the shutdown menu in the top right corner of the screen, it's not going to respect this (or any!) setting and always locks the screen anyway.

Suspending by any other means works correctly and will not lock the screen.
