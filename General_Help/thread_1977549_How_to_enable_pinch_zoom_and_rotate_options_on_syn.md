### Post by charlescarroll1 on 2013-02-23
So I figured out that you can actually use pinch to zoom by default (assuming you have compatible hardware).

All you need to do is **simply hold CTRL** while performing the gesture! 

I also realized that while in Google Maps, pinch to zoom works without having to hold CTRL.

Hope this helps!
