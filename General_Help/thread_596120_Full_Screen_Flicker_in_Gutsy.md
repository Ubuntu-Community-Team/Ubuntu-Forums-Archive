### Post by seul on 2007-12-15
Suggestion from [vasilis34]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?p=3820861#post3820861"):

System &#8594; Preferences &#8594; Advanced Desktop Effects Settings &#8594; General Options &#8594; Tab »General« &#8594; untick »Unredirect Fullscreen Windows«

worked for me.
