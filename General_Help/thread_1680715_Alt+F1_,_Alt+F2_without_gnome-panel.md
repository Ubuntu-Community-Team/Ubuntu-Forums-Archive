### Post by winchendonsprings on 2011-02-03
I tested Cardapio and wasn't totally happy with it.  I'll tell you what I was happy with though, Lauf. It seems the guy that maintained Alawalk has joined with Lauf. 

Reasons I like Lauf
- No installation, just extract and put the folder wherever you like.
- Required no dependencies that weren't default. (I think. zenity, notify osd, coreutils)
- Simple GUI
- Want it for Alt+F2 replacement? go to keyboard shortcuts and add it. took me roughly 12.3 seconds
- Plugins are good and simple. example -weather pops up in notify osd
- Beyond minimal resources
