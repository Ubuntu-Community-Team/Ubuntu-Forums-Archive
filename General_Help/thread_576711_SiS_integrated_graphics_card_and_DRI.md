### Post by tlacuache on 2007-10-15
My last motherboard had an onboard SiS card and I looked around for weeks. As far as I know, there's no way to get DRI with the SiS chipsets. If you really need it, your best bet is to buy an inexpensive graphics card and use that for the video instead.
