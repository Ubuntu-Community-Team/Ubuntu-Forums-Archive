### Post by earthpigg on 2008-10-12
right click -> edit menus

(edit: right click where the word "applications" is on the panel)

see if they show up there, but have the square box next to them unchecked.

if they are there, but the box is unchecked.... checking the square box will make them visible in the menu. its happened to me before, no idea why.

if you can remember what the 8 programs are called, you can add them to the menu there, too.
