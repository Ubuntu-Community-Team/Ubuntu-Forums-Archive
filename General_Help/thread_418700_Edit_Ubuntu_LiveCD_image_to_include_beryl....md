### Post by ramjet_1953 on 2007-04-23
Reconstructor is the official Ubuntu package that allows you make your own customised CD.

However, it is not for the Live CD, but the alternate CD, which is not a GUI installer.

However, Reconstructor allows you to completely customise the OS to the point of even making your own distribution.

That is how LINUXMint was made, I believe.
