### Post by AndyCooll on 2006-01-03
I think you mean using tags within your music collection. You can tag all your song collection using Easytag. 

I have a fileserver on which I store my music collection (which are stored in mp3 format). I then share this music directory.

Most music players will then pick up these tags. Amarok is particularly easy to use.
