### Post by Mark Phelps on 2010-07-23
Sorry, don't have an instant answer for you ...

ATI dropped support for LOTS of chipsets over a year ago, and while their documentation claimed their new drivers would continue to work with the 2xxx series, actual practice has shown otherwise.  Only the 3x/4x/5x have high success rates and recently, some of the newer 5x series aren't working well, either.

You could go over to the Phoronix forums and look through their threads dealing with the open source drivers.  They do a lot of detail work and often post some hints you can use to improve performance.
