### Post by nursegirl on 2006-05-06
A detailed explanation is found on [the Ubuntu website]("http://www.ubuntu.com/ubuntu/components").

The most important thing for the end user is that things in the Main repos are the only ones that the Ubuntu team guarantees to support with security updates, etc.

The Universe repos are free/open source, so although the Ubuntu team haven't guaranteed to support them, they're likely to have communities similar to ours who can help out and provide bugfixes/security fixes.

The Multiverse repos are not free, so the Ubuntu team cannot help with them in terms of security/bugfixes.
