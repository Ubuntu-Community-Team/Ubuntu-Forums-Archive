### Post by johngreth on 2010-03-21
It thinks your sound card is unplugged. either it was unplugged, is broken, or is supported by a new driver that gave it a different name. In any case, it should be ok to say yes. You can always reinstall the driver. I'll also assume that this warning was a surprise, so if you are still getting sound it is probably a new driver which is fine and if you aren't getting sound either the card or the driver are broken in which case saying yes won't make things any worse.
