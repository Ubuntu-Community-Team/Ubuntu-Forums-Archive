### Post by CatKiller on 2007-12-31
Realistically, only by opening the case and looking at the label. You might get some clues from the bootup BIOS initialisation screen. **sudo lshw** might tell you all that you need, though.

### Post by hardyn on 2007-12-31
like the above poster... pressing the "pause" key during post may allow you to find the manufacturer and model number during POST (power-on self test).

if your motherboard displays a POST splash screen, you will have to disable this before trying to read the POST screen.
