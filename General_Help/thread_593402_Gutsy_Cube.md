### Post by por100pre1 on 2007-10-27
> **toontastic said:**
> Just  realised I missed one step out. You need to then go to Synaptic Package Manager, download compiz-settings-manager then you'll have an extra option within settings to turn on the cube, burning desktop etc.

Yes, here's a command:

```
sudo aptitude install compizconfig-settings-manager
```

then look in **System> Preferences> Advanced Desktop Effects Settings**. :)
