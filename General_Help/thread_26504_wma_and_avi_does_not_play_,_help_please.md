### Post by lorap on 2005-04-13
well,all you've to do install GVLC player.
that'll solve almost all your troubles,this player doesn't even need any codecs been installed cause all of them are sawed in its executable.
go to Computer-->System Configuration-->Synaptic Package Manager.
after you got synaptic window open Settings-->Repositories.
once repositories' window opens mark all options possible and press Ok.
now you're back to synaptic's window.press Reload button.
once the process completes press the Search button then type in GVLC then ok.
the moment the synaptic finds it out for you mark it for install then press Apply button.wait until the process completes,close the synaptic and begin enjoy your music and movies :-)
have a nice day!
