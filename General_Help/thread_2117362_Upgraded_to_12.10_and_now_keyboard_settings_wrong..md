### Post by Sef on 2013-02-18
1) Click on System Settings on the toolbar (Gear and Wrench [Spanner]icon)

2) Click on Keyboard Layout

3) Click on the Plus Sign (+) bottom left

4) Scroll down to English (UK), highlight, then click ADD. 

5) Make sure English (UK) is highlighted.
