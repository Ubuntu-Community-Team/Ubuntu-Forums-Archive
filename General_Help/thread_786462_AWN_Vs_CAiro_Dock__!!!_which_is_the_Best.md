### Post by klange on 2008-06-08
> **speedemonV12 said:**
> im still not finding any options to turn off animation when clicking an icon, or to turn of zoom when mouse over.. is this not something that can be done with cairo?
Everything and anything can be turned off, but the config tool is easy to get lost in.

To disable animations on clicks go to the "Icons" tab and scroll down to the expanders labeled "Launchers", "Applications", etc. Expand each and set the "Number of times the icon will (animate)..." to 0. Personally, I found no animation to be a bit unnatural, but do what you wish.

To disable the zoom effect, at the top of "Icons" is "Wave shape". Set the crest to 0.0, and adjust your icon sizes to fit. (though again, it gives it a very unnatural feel... maybe a crest size of 1 and an effective distance of 30 or something would be good...)
