### Post by vasa1 on 2014-04-30
> **sudodus said:**
> I tried clicking of the x symbol at the right end of the window of the web address (the same place as the old stop button), and I think the x symbol is the new stop button :-)
That's it in the far right of the address box. There can be one of two things here: "Reload current page" (then you'll see #urlbar-reload-button) or "Stop loading this page" (represented by #urlbar-stop-button).
