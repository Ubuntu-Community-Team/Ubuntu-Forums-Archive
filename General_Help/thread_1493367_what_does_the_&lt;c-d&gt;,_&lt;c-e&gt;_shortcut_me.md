### Post by dasy2k1 on 2010-05-25
indeed, C-d, ^d are two differnt ways of writing the same thing, 
sometimes also written as CTRL+d 

you may aslo find M-<letter> the M refering to meta (unless you use some old school keyboard this is the ALT key)

or even S-<letter> for the super key (windows key on most standard keyboards, command key on a mac keyboard, little diamond key on a sun keyboard etc)


with reference to C-d the first thing i think of is EOF (end of file ASCII 0x04) rather than drop bookmark, useful short cut for exiting terminals amongst other things
