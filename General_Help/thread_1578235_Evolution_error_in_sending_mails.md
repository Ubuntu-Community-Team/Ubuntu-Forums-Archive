### Post by robsoles on 2010-09-20
Are you running more than one mail client to the same mail service at a time?

If your phone, laptop and main PC all try to be logged in at the same time the server may respond with that error, otherwise if you are only trying to login with one client at a time (leaving clients running usually means they log in at intervals, usually 10 to 30 minutes) then the server is overloaded and your host(s) need to upgrade their stuff.
