### Post by southernman on 2007-06-17
Lurker in the peanut gallery suggest an old mechanics trick to pinpoint an odd ball noise...

Use a long screwdriver... non-metalic of course. Place the tip on object and hold the other end to your ear. You'll know when you find the culprit.
