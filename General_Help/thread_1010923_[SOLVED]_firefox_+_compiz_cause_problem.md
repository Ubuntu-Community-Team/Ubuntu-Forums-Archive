### Post by sedawk on 2008-12-14
This is a known issue with Firefox, but I didn't know that is
related to compiz window manager ...

After starting Firefox press F11 to toggle fullscreen mode on and off.
This will give you back normal window.

Some say that resizing the window helps to prevent this from happening
again - I haven't tried that one.
