### Post by ajgreeny on 2015-05-10
Click on the network icon in your panel and choose Edit.
Now highlight the connection that has the problem and Edit again.  Now make sure that the two tick boxes on the General tab are selected so that all users are automatically connected.

Hopefully that will solve the problem for you.

PS:  I am using Xubuntu 14.04 with xfce-4.12 from the ppa, so your way of doing this may look slightly different depending on the DE you use.
