### Post by Wee_Guy on 2010-03-28
Maybe it's an issue caused by a dodgy update or rubbish drivers. Did it stop working after doing any updates or installing/changing drivers or anything?

Anyway, if you want a new printer, my HP Photosmart C1380 worked no hassle at all. Plugged it in and it worked.
Ubuntu comes with drivers for lots of printers, which includes 1,956 HP printers [http://hplipopensource.com/hplip-web/downloads.html](http://hplipopensource.com/hplip-web/downloads.html) 
so most HP ones are *probably* supported. Ubuntu probably supports many other manufacturers too, but I don't know anything about them (try looking on their websites).
