### Post by briguy on 2005-11-11
Nautilus (file manager) will do it for you automatically.  When you insert a blank disk, a window should open and you can drag and drop the mp3's into it.  You can also right click on the "blank cd" icon on the desktop and select 'make a new data cd' and do the same thing.  Whatever you do, if the program "Serpentine", you're about to make a standard "old fashioned" audio cd.
