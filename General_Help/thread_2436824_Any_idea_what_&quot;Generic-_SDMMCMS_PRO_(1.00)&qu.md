### Post by TheFu on 2020-02-13
That is usually a XY-in-1 USB multi-card reader.  Look for a slot that accepts an SD, SONY MediaStik or MMC memory card. Some are 8-in-1, others are 25-in-1 and some are 35-in-one. Just depends on how many different slots there are connected to the internal USB interface.  
On a desktop, it would usually be on the front-panel somewhere.  
On a laptop, those slots are on the side. Sometimes an inserted SD card will be completely flush with the case. 
The adaptor could be external too, usually with a USB plug.
