### Post by janek1108 on 2012-01-15
No HDMI audio. EASY FIX
1 click SPEAKER    ( upper right corner)
2 click SOUND SETTINGS
3 click HARDWARE
4 click SETTINGS FOR SELEKTED DEVICE
5 click STERIO HDMI OUTPUT

Good luck
