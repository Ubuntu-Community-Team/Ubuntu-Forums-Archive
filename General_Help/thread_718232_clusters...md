### Post by SpaceTeddy on 2008-03-08
a cluster is way more complicated than just "linking them together" - what you want, from your description is a network based operating system - which is a fairly special case and afaik not supported by ubuntu, debian or linux. (maybe the beowulf project did something along those lines, but i am not sure about that either) 
You probably will need to read, compile and install a whole lot of stuff just to get the two to work together - doing this for two computers is usually a waste of time (unless you want to figure out how it is done, proof of concept so to say)

i've just read through the article about the network operating systems in wikipedia, and it somehow seems confusing... i might be wrong all along.

But i am sure this is nothing that can be achived easily... I would suggest first you figure you what it is that you want to share between the computers... and then go about and search for a more special application to do this.

to get notification to a thread subscribe to the thread (button is top - right under the menu bar in the thread tools) and choose instant mail notification.
