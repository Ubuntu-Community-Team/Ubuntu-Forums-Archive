> **rainwalker said:**
> Ohhh, okay that explains it.

Any idea when OpenOffice might be updated?

For Edgy in the official repository, never. It's a "design" feature of releases - organizations (and some individuals) who're using Ubuntu on a desktop, don't want forced major changes, just the security updates - and that's what you get in any Ubuntu release. That won't stop you from installing later versions "manually", or you could take a jump to Feisty and get latest versions of just about everything.

I believe there must exists howto threads or pages in these forums or wiki.ubuntu.com about upgrading to latest OpenOffice, if you're interested in upgrading manually. If you can't find it, don't hesitate to ask, someone here will help you. (EDIT: FuturePilot already did.)
