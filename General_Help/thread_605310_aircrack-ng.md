### Post by wieman01 on 2007-11-06
Open a terminal and type:
> sudo aircrack-ng
For instance. Aircrack-NG comes with a whole range of other tools (e.g. airodump) and needs to be operated from command line. Check Aircrack-NG's web site for documentation.
