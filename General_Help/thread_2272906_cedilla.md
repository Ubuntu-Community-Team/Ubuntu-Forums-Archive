### Post by Impavidus on 2015-04-09
So AltGr+<comma>+c used to give you ç? AltGr usually doesn't work with combinations of ordinary character keys (like <comma> and c), only single ones, so I assume your AltGr key used to be configured to be the <compose> key. Open your keyboard settings and check the settings for your compose key.

Or did AltGr+<comma> produce a dead cedilla?

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/ComposeKey](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/ComposeKey)
[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Compose_key](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Compose_key)
[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dead_key](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dead_key)
