### Post by Autodave on 2019-02-13
There is usually a sticker on the HD that will tell you its power consumption.  You may want to check them against each other.

I ran into that kind of problem once on a desktop: after 10-15 minutes, the mouse would quit functioning.  Through another power supply at it and still had the same problem.  Ended up being a HD that sucking too much juice out of a weak power supply.  Through another HD in it a year ago and still running strong.
