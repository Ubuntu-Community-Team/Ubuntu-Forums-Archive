### Post by kokkus on 2008-10-01
in the address bar, write about:config
in filter, write google and change the resaults to your favourite search engine.
But make sure you know the right search, source and keyword urls to replace with the google urls with.
It's not just to replace [http://www.google.com](http://www.google.com) with [http://altavista.com](http://altavista.com) or something like that.
