### Post by Dragonbite on 2007-07-09
My mouse gets squirrelly a lot of times and it is due mostly to the surface I am using it on.  The other part is the abuse my kids put it through, but that's another story.

For a while I taped a piece of paper down on the table and the mouse worked fine after that (until the kids "removed" it).

If you don't want to use a mouse pad or tape a piece of paper down try it on a note pad of some sort (even newspaper should work) and see if that fixes it.  Try something non-glossy for best effect.
