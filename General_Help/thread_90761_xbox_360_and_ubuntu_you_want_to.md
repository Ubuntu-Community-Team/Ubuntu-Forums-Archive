### Post by pxgray on 2005-11-16
Casey is completely right about the next generation consoles not being suitable for general purpose computing.  Comparing the Xbox 360's processors to a desktop AMD or Pentium 4 is comparing apples to oranges.  The Cell processor (PS3) as well as the processors in the Xbox 360 are meant for processing graphics and controlling complicated peripherals and optimized game algorithms, but asking it to do intense server tasks such as multiple outgoing connections and disk writes might not yield the best results.  All in all I think "weak" is a bad term to describe them, maybe "specialized" would be a better term
