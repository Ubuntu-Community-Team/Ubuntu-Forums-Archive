### Post by vyrrch on 2010-05-07
flash sound problems
after upgrading to 10.04 i found i had problems with flash and sound.
this was caused by having two versions of flash plugin installed in firefox one of which wasn't appearing in package manager
to check how many versions are installed in firefox address bar type
about:plugins
if there are more than one entry for flash player enter the following into a terminal
rm ~/.mozilla/plugins/libflashplayer.so


more details and other possible fixes can be found here:
[http://www.willmcgugan.com/blog/tech/2010/5/2/no-audio-in-flash-on-ubuntu-1004/](http://www.willmcgugan.com/blog/tech/2010/5/2/no-audio-in-flash-on-ubuntu-1004/)
