### Post by DuckHook on 2017-10-23
Welcome to the forums, pedropirata.

Your reference to BURG2/GRUB makes your posting very ambiguous and therefore confusing. If you really are using BURG2, then you are using a hack that has been out of development for years, ceased support long ago, and is likely the source of your problems. No one here is likely to be able to help you sort out a BURG issue.

I recommend installing GRUB2 and deleting BURG altogether, thus using the default boot loader that is supported by Ubuntu.
