### Post by CatKiller on 2020-05-22
> **ovince said:**
> Lately, it happens to me that the OS freezes so I can't use the keyboard. 
Also, clicking the mouse on some applications does not give any result. Has that happened to anyone?

I've seen that happen when the display server, which translates input signals into actions on window elements, has some issue. Generally graphics driver related. 

Some information on your hardware would likely help. 

You might also consider moving to 20.04, especially with it being a recent install. The newer version might have fixed whatever the issue is, which would be handy, but 20.04 is a Long Term Support release, meaning that it's supported until April 2025. 19.10's support ends in July this year.
