### Post by Sef on 2006-12-27
1) You need to burn it as an iso and not a file.

a) Read  [how to write iso iles to cd]("http://www.petri.co.il/how_to_write_iso_files_to_cd.htm").  

b) Get [ISO Recorder]("http://isorecorder.alexfeinman.com/isorecorder.htm").

2) Don't forget to [md5sum the download]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HowToMD5SUM"), so you know the download was good.

3) Don't burn it at more than 4x.  Faster may cause a faulty burn.
