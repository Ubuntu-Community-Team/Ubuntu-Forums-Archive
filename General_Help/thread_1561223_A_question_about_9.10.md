### Post by s0rc3r3r on 2010-08-26
I think you must have unknowingly removed the** indicator-applet-session** .
You can right click on the panel 
select "Add To Panel"
Choose "**indicator-applet-session"** from the list which pops up
And you can have your panel and its components back

Well..I am using Ubuntu 9.10. Hope you are using the same version and the version you are using is not much different, Hope this  works.
