Only cards from HD2xxx and up are supported by the proprietary fglrx driver.

The older ones are supported only by the open source driver that is not that good ( i tested it on a 9000). For lighter office tasks will do. But even watching some flash videos are a pain if paired with a slower processor.

If you have an older computer, get a second hand nvidia card, they work perfectly with the proprietary nvidia driver.
