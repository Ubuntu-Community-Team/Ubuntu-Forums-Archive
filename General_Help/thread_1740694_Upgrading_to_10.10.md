### Post by 3rdalbum on 2011-04-27
> **Bikinguy said:**
> I have installed 10.04 and it works great. I have heard that going to 10.10 can hose the wireless setup.

That's just one person griping that either:

1. The driver they use for their wireless card has a regression in 10.10

2. They manually installed a driver in 10.04 and it needs to be recompiled in 10.10's kernel.

> Very interested in trying the  unity desktop but maybe should leave well enough alone and stick with 10.04lt

Boot up the 11.04 live CD. If wireless works in that for you, then you've got nothing to worry about - just install that and get your graphics driver installed and you'll be able to try Unity.
