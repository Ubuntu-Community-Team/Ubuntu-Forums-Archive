### Post by whiskers75 on 2010-03-06
Try the following:

Get your Ubuntu Live CD
Go to "Install Ubuntu"
When you get to the step that requires partitioning:

Make a note of what the current partition table looks like

Delete ALL partitions except the /home partition

Make the partitions that you deleted again

Click "Forward"

Fill in the required info and click "Forward" again

Click "Install"

That should fix your problem! NOTE: This will only keep your FILES. Your PACKAGES and PROGRAMS will have to be installed again.
