### Post by dmpp on 2011-02-24
I am a bit new to ubuntu/linux and have installed the Maveric 10.10 version on an HP Pavilion dv7-1262us laptop and am using Evolution 2.30.3 for email.  When I click on the contacts button in Evolution, and then click on a particular contact (in the personal contact list), I can't seem to modify the name or email; I can click in the boxes and cursor about, but no editing; the entire panel seems to be greyed out.  I have searched a bit, but have not seen this particular problem addressed.

Thanks in advance for assistance,
DMP

---

### Post by dmpp on 2011-02-24
Solved the problem.  The personal address book apparently needs to be the default.  I clicked on properties and selected "mark as default address book", then restarted Evolution, and now I can edit the entries.  The page still looks greyed out though, that is minorly confusing.
