### Post by pseudovere on 2009-05-04
I am a heavy user of tabbed browser, and an even heavier user of shortcuts.  However, the ever-so-convenient placing of my two most used shortcuts (ctrl+tab and ctrl+w) around a shortcut that can really be annoying if it gets pressed in the middle of writing an email (ctrl+q) is beginning to become fairly irritating.

I never use ctrl+q myself, as I am an alt+f4/ctrl+w kind of guy, so is there a way that I can just disable it, move it to the other side of the keyboard, or even just turn it into ctrl+alt+q?

---

### Post by Brandon Williams on 2009-05-04
Install the keyconfig addon. It allows you to change most (though not all) of the keyboard shortcuts.
