A minute before I had a problem. I connected second monitor to laptop with ubuntu 9.10. Then, after detaching the monitor i got my applet ordered in a wrong way (from right to left I first had sound indicator and Internet connection applets then button for turning of etc and clock applets ). To solve this problem i right-clicked on this applets then 'attach to panel' ('&#1055;&#1088;&#1080;&#1082;&#1088;&#1077;&#1087;&#1080;&#1090;&#1100; &#1082; &#1087;&#1072;&#1085;&#1077;&#1083;&#1077;' in Russian) then 'move' ('&#1055;&#1077;&#1088;&#1077;&#1084;&#1077;&#1089;&#1090;&#1080;&#1090;&#1100;' in Russian). Then drag you applet where you want it to be.
