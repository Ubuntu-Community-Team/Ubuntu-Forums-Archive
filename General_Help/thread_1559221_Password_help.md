### Post by Confucius! on 2010-08-23
Restart your computer and at the GRUB screen go into recovery mode. You may have to hit tab for that to appear during restart. Once you are at the command prompt for you mounted FS just type passwd and your users account name. An example would be "passwd catdog" where catdog is my account name. Then type shutdown -r now and when your computer restarts try that password for your account.
