your gnome settings may or may not work due to variations in the version of gnome and various patches that fedora may have applied differently.  All of the other programs should function normally presuming that there isn't a drastic difference in the version of the program you are running.  I don't see why you wouldn't retain at least 90% of you settings if you save your /home directory.
