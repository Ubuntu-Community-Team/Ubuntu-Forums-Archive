### Post by nikosapi on 2010-09-04
Use Ctrl+Alt+F1 to get to a virtual terminal, then login. Or choose the [recovery]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/RecoveryMode") boot option which should drop you into a root shell.

From there you can further debug your problem.
