### Post by ba_saish on 2010-06-24
Right click on your usename on the panel ( the restart shutdown button) 
select setup login screen 
click on the accessibility tab, and see what is there in the sounds option. 
Is the "login ready" option ticked under sounds ? 
There must be a audio file selected for "login ready" option, if not then select which ever you like by clicking on the folder icon. 
Hope this is what you were looking for.
