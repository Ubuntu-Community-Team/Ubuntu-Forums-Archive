### Post by Lukas666 on 2010-09-11
If you have more than one memory stick you HAVE TO test them individually.
Most of system freezes are caused by BAD memory. Did you use memtest86? 

Make sure you have the most recent version. Or even try a few different versions. In my case it took me a few days to find the culprit (all 4 sticks at once and 2x2 combinations reported no errors).
