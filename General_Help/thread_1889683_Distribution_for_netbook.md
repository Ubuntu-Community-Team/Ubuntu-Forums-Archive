### Post by snowpine on 2011-12-01
Does your computer meet the hardware requirements for Adobe Flash Player?

[http://www.adobe.com/products/flashplayer/tech-specs.html](http://www.adobe.com/products/flashplayer/tech-specs.html)

Probably you don't have a powerful enough video card (128mb graphics memory is the minimum recommended).

In that case you can download the movie and play it from your hard drive with your favorite movie player (I like VLC). This should be much smoother than streaming.
