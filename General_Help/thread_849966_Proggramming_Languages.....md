User mode linux just means non-root access.

For programming languages, why not go with C or C++? linux is written in this, as are most 'proper' programs.

Furthermore, many languages share much of the same syntax and usage, such as PHP and Java, giving you a headstart should you ever need to use one of those in professional life.
