### Post by TheShabz on 2009-11-30
Hi. I notice that my cookies in FireFox are stored in a cookies.sqlite file instead of in a folder like in windows. Now if I want to delete a cookie from a particular website and keep the others, how do i go about doing this?

---

### Post by ed-koala on 2009-11-30
In Firefox, under Preferences, choose the Privacy tab, and there is an option to remove individual cookies.

---

### Post by mkvnmtr on 2009-11-30
You can also choose to be asked to accept each cookie. That way you do not have a cookie on your system that needs to be deleted. It is only a pain the first day when it asks about each one.
