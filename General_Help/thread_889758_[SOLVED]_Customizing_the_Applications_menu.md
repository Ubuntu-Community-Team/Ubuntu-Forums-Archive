quick search here and google didn't turn anything up. Is there a way to move icons around in the applications menu? some things are in categories other than where I would like them to be. Some of them I don't want there at all, but cannot uninstall (due to dependent files).

### Post by sayakb on 2008-08-14
Right click on the menu bar and select **Edit Menus.** Then click the menu entry and drag it to the submenu you want it to move to.
