### Post by bodhi.zazen on 2011-03-31
Most of this will be application specific. With some applications you can simply go under menu -> placed -> clear recent.

With others you can use bleachbit.

With others, you can clear recent from the menu.

If all that fails, you will need to look at the hidden files / directories in your home directory.

.application_name
.local
.config
.cache

other ?
