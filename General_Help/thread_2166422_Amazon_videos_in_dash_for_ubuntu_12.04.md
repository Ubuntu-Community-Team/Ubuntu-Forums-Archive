### Post by deadflowr on 2013-08-09
The online video search has always been on 12.04.
It's search function is completely different from the "other" search function.
You can de facto disable it by clicking on the filters and selecting "My videos".
You'll notice that Amazon isn't the only video site listed as well.

You'll also notice it's only tied to the Video search lens.
Opening the dash and typing as normal, won't bring up any of those searches.
