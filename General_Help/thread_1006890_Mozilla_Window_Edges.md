For some completely odd reason, my FF window is larger than my desktop, and I no longer have the title bar at the top (along with the buttons to minimize, shut-down FF).  The rest of my programs are just fine, FF is the only one that is doing this.  I am running 8.10, any ideas?

### Post by fooman on 2008-12-09
press f11 to go fullscreen, then f11 again to get to a normal size.

that should fix it...if it comes back again when you restart firefox,  then try this:

do the f11 thing twice and when you get to the normal size screen...unmaximize the screen (not minimize).

then close firefox and reopen it.

maximize firefox and you should be good.
