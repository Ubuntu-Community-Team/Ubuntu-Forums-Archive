### Post by Ginsu543 on 2010-05-08
Glad you solved the problem, but just for your information, another way to fix this is to have fusion-icon installed and running before you make changes to Compiz. I've done exactly what you did several times, and all you have to do is right click on the fusion-icon and select Metacity as the window manager. That effectively turns off Compiz so the windows become opaque again. Then you can change the settings and turn Compiz back on.
