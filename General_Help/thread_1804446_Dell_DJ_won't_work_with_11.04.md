### Post by trebz32 on 2011-08-04
Hi. I was having a similar problem finding a way to get my Dell DJ30 detected since I'm new at this too. I tried installing Gnomad2 but somehow it would not work. What i did was upgrade my Dell DJ30 firmware to Version 2.20.07 under Windows XP since I have Ubuntu installed with wubi. The only thing is to back up your MP3 files off your Dell DJ because an upgrade erases all data. I then installed Rhytmbox, plugged in my Dell DJ30 and it was detected. I also tried Amarok and it was detected as well, but I prefer Rhythmbox. Hope this helps.
