### Post by wetinwales on 2010-04-25
I have karmic 9.10 on a 250GB HDD and my 'STORAGE DRIVE' on a 1TB HDD
There is no dual boot - just karmic. Both HDDs are in Caddies.
All has been well for many months. Suddenly Karmic will only boot if the STORAGE DRIVE is dis-connected.
Karmic will boot if it is the only drive in the machine.

The error message when both drives are in is:- 
Grub Loading Stage 1.5
Please wait
Error 22
blinking cursor and nothing else.....

I have searched Google but despite many posts on this error message, I cannot find a similar issue where the machine won't boot.

Have I lost my storage drive?
can anyone help?

---

### Post by wetinwales on 2010-04-25
SOLVED. SOLVED SOLVED.

Foolish me. The SATA connectors were in the wrong positions following replacement of Power Supply.
SATA 1 must be the system drive. Drive was plugged into SATA 3.

All now well again.
