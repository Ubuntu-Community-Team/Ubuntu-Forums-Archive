### Post by Rypervenche on 2010-10-04
Hi there. I am running Lucid and wanted to VM Windows XP to play Final Fantasy VII. Everything works fine, but afterward I installed the Guest Additions on VirtualBox and the game stopped working. I found the reason for this on the official website here:

[http://www.virtualbox.org/ticket/196](http://www.virtualbox.org/ticket/196)

My question is how can I uninstall the video driver(s?) as the OP suggested? I do very much enjoy the functions from the additions so I don't want to uninstall the entire thing.

Can anyone shed some light on this?

Thanks in advance :)

---

### Post by Rypervenche on 2010-10-05
I figured it out. I had to go into the hardware manager in Windows and disable the Sun video adapter then restart. It's not as pretty, especially since it shows both mice, one on top of the other, but it serves my purpose. And the good thing is I can always re-enable it later.
