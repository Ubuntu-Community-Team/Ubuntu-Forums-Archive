### Post by dustigroove on 2008-02-04
A couple of ways you can do this...

Go to the **Applications** menu, select **Add/Remove**, type ***"firewall"*** in the search field and hit **Enter**, put a check in the box next to Firestarter and click **Apply Changes**.

OR

Open up a terminal window, enter the following and hit Enter:

```
sudo apt-get install firestarter

```Cheers,
