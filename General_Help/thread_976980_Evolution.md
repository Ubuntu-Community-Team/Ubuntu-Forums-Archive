Today I just linked my gmail account to Evolution. It works great but I was curious if it was possible to link more than just one email address to it?

### Post by Seanuntu on 2008-11-09
It is possible.  Just go to "Edit --> Preferences", click on "Mail Accounts", then click the "+Add" button.

### Post by Redenbacher on 2008-11-10
What Seanuntu said will work. The only problem I've seen is that Evolution lumps all email from all accounts in to one account. 

You may have to set up some "Sent To" filters and make files for each of your other accounts.
