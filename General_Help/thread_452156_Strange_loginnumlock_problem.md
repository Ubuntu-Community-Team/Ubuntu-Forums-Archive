### Post by deeZee on 2007-07-09
A quick update:  I purchased a PS/2 Female to USB-A port adapter from Radio Shack ($14.99) and that solved the problem. 

EDIT:  If you purchase a PS/2 to USB adapter, make sure "USB support for DOS" is enabled in your bios (if you have an older motherboard like me).
