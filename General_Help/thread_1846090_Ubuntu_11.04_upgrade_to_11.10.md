### Post by Mark Phelps on 2011-09-18
However ... you need to understand that there is no "system restore" capability that will allow you, should you run into problems after the upgrade, to "restore" your ealier, working, 11.04 install.  You will have to completely reinstall from scratch.

Thus, BEFORE you do the upfgrade, it would be a good idea to use something like Clonezilla to image off your 11.04 install to an external drive or USB stick. That will allow you to restore it, shoule you run into problems.
