### Post by Vladlenin5000 on 2015-07-06
Proprietary design always have the potential for issues. Bleeding edge hardware such as the graphics in Asus 501 can be a problem too.
Your desired machine has been designed for Windows 8.1 (and probably "Windows 10 ready") and you should keep that in mind at all times.
And yes, you won't have problems with System76, ZaReason and a few others. They may not have 4K screens yet but [those in a laptop are dumb anyway]("http://www.techradar.com/reviews/pc-mac/laptops-portable-pcs/laptops-and-netbooks/asus-rog-g501-1294350/review").
