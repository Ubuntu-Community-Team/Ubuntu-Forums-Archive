### Post by lykwydchykyn on 2013-04-26
Solved it.  My ~/.asoundrc had some funky stuff it in (I'd tried to route my audio through a compressor at one point).  got rid of it and rebooted, vòila!  it's working.

Except that it locks up when I exit.  Well, nothing's perfect...
