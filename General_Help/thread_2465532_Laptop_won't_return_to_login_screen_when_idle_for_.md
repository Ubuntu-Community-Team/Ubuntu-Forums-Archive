### Post by smeg2 on 2021-08-04
My Laptop won't return to the login screen when idle for an hour or more.
I have my PC set to go to a blank screen after 5 minutes of inactivity and to lock straight away.
When I leave the PC for 20-30 minutes and come back it goes to the login screen when I wiggle the mouse or type on the keyboard.
When I do the same after an hour or so it refuses to go to the login screen and stays blank, I see no activity from the SSD light (I have torrents running) and I have to push the power button for 4 seconds to power off and restart.
I am using Ubuntu 20.04LTS with all updates.
Laptop details are;
Dell Precision 7520
Intel® Core&#8482; i7-7920HQ CPU @ 3.10GHz × 8 
NVIDIA Corporation GM206GLM [Quadro M2200 Mobile] / Quadro M
32GB RAM
1TB NVMe SSD

Software running;
Firefox
Transmission
Daedalus

The laptop is on mains power not battery.

---

### Post by smeg2 on 2021-08-06
Problem solved, it was a setting in settings>power settings>when on battery, had to switch off the option to go to standby after x minutes when on battery.
