### Post by Dayofswords on 2010-03-20
go to applcations > accessories > terminal

a window will open with some text 

XXXXXXX@YYYYYYYYY:~$

X will be your username as the OS sees it

Y will be the computer's name as it sees it


you want the name on the left side of the "@"

example:
[http://johnbokma.com/mexit/2005/10/25/vmplayer-ubuntu-terminal.png](http://johnbokma.com/mexit/2005/10/25/vmplayer-ubuntu-terminal.png)
the username here is vmware
on a computer named VMware-Ubuntu
