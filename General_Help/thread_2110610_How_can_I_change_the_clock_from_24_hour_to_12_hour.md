### Post by kanikilu on 2013-01-30
Right-click on the time and choose "Digital Clock settings". Then just change the clock format to something like: ```
%l:%m %p
```

For all the available options, see the man page:

[http://linux.die.net/man/3/strftime](http://linux.die.net/man/3/strftime)
