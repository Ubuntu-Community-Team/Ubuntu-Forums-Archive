### Post by Rallg on 2009-03-02
I have not used the paid support. But it is my understanding that any support - paid or unpaid, whether from a Linux project or a "major commercial software supplier" - is most effective when the problem concerns installation of well-known hardware over numerous systems. Unusual hardware combinations, for a single system, are not really a support issue. Rather, as the first person suggested, post a specific question to the forum, describing your hardware.
