### Post by caleb12 on 2008-10-21
Go here:

[http://download.openoffice.org/other.html](http://download.openoffice.org/other.html)

Select, your language and the "Linux Deb" 

Then:

tar xvf "thefileyoujustdownloaded".tar.gz

sudo dpkg -i *.deb

Here's more info... 

[http://download.openoffice.org/common/instructions.html#linux](http://download.openoffice.org/common/instructions.html#linux)

Careful though, I've had OO3 crash on me a couple of times in the middle of working with a doc. Save often!
