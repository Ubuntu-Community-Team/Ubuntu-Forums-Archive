### Post by sdennie on 2010-05-30
It sounds like you want this tutorial: [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RenameUSBDrive](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RenameUSBDrive)

The URL is misleading but, it's what you want to do.  If you label you NTFS disks, they will show up with meaningful names in Nautilus (though, you may have to re-mount them).
