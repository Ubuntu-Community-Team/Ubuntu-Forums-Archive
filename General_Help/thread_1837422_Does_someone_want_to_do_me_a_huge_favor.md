### Post by mbuell on 2011-09-01
If you can handle a CD, then I would strongly recommend you start out with a different mini-linux. There are several flavors that are designed to be smaller or for older hardware. You can check things out at Distrowatch. 

[http://distrowatch.com/](http://distrowatch.com/)

Enlightenment is a desktop, and is very efficient, and does more, imo, that busybox or a number of other super-light window managers/desktops. You can find it in SOAD - an OpenSUSE variant. I used it on an early Pentium desktop where it worked quite successfully. 

Good luck - my experience has been that hardware support for older stuff is not good.
