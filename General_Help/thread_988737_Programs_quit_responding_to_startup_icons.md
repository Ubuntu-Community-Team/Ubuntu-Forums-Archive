### Post by fooman on 2008-11-20
are these desktop icons? ...or icons in the panel?

either way...have you tried deleting them and then recreating them? 

to recreate the icons,  look in applications for the menu items,  right click on them and choose either "add this launcher to panel" or "add this launcher to desktop".

for the problem of openoffice opening when you click on bookmarks...go to places > computer.  on the left, click once on your user name. then on the right side...right-click on "desktop".  click the "open with" tab and put a check next to "open folder".
