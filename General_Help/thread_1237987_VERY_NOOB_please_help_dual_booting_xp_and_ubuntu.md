### Post by fluffman86 on 2009-08-12
oh, the joys of installing xp...lol.  You really start to take Ubuntu for granted until you do a normal XP install.  That's right--XP does not recognize any drivers out of the box.  You'll need to get online and download the ethernet drivers from your manufacturer's website on another computer and copy them over and install them.

People used to make fun of Ubuntu for not supporting some video and wireless drivers out of the box, but they never were in the pickle of install XP and not having any way to get online at all without an ethernet driver.  :D
