### Post by johnc4510 on 2007-02-25
Maybe. Do this, click the arrow in the search engine box and at the bottom, click on Manager Search Engines. A window will open. At the bottom, click on Get More Search Engines. At the bottom of the window that opens it says something like Additional Resources. Just below that is a link to make your own search engine. Follow it and see. Hope that helps!
