### Post by OMRebel on 2008-05-01
I have the Dell Inspiron 1501.  It's a cheaper Dell notebook, but has a fantastic display on it.  It would certainly fit your price range.

I have everything working on it except for Suspend (that just locks it up) and the modem (which I never use, but Dell supposed to be releasing Linux drivers for it).  But, other than those two items, everything else works great.  I would recommend installing NDISWrapper and not using the b43cutter driver.  It works, but it's slow.  

Of course, you can always get a Ubuntu notebook through Dell directly, already configured with Ubuntu.
