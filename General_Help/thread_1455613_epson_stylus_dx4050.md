### Post by ellgor on 2010-04-16
Hi,

Open up printing from the Admin section, right click on your printer icon and click on properties, click on Printer Options, click on Resolution (you might have to scroll along a bit to see the changebar) in there are the dpi's, there are some other options just above that also effect the printing , have a look at them as well.

Regards, Ellgor.
