### Post by bwhite82 on 2007-04-20
Whenever this happens, it's a good idea to switch to 'verbose mode' which will show you all of the text of the boot process, you do this by hitting ALT+F1 on your boot screen. Often times the text will tell you what the problem is, if it is hanging on something then you know exactly what's causing the issue.
