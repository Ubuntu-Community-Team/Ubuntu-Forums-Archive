### Post by lisati on 2009-07-27
I don't see any "quick and easy" way of solving your dilemma. Other forum users might have a few ideas time-saving tips. Here goes with a couple of suggestions:

The safest way I know of requires a little bit of work: make a backup of your important data, and then reinstall (including any additional programs).

Another option is to use [remastersys]("http://howtoforge.com/ubuntu-linux-mint-livecd-with-remastersys") to create a custom installation CD which will include all the extra programs you've installed. However, this works best if your system is in a good condition.
