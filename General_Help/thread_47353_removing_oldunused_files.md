### Post by SFN on 2005-07-08
In general, a distro that uses apt (Ubuntu does) is very good about cleaning out the cruft during uninstalls. There are a number of programs that help even further - deborphan and debfoster come to mind.

Here's a link with some good info on helping keep your system clean.
