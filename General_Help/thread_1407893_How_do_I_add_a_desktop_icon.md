### Post by ikehack on 2010-02-15
First, you should check if PasswordSafe has created a launcher for itself under the Applications menu, which is located on the Gnome panel in the top-left corner. Check under each section: Accessories, System Tools, etc. If you do find it, you can simply click-and-drag it onto your desktop. 

If PasswordSafe has not made a launcher for itself under the Applications menu, you can right click your desktop and create a shortcut/launcher. I don't remember how to do this off the top of my head, but it's pretty intuitive. When it asks you to specify the location of the binary/executable, you would enter the location you said it was installed to. EG: /usr/bin/passwordsafe.

I hope this helped!
