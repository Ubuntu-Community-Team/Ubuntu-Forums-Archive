### Post by Ocxic on 2011-01-27
according the manual for your motherboard, use the green jack for left and right speaker output, and use the orange jack for center/subwoofer output.

generally a 2.1 speaker system will just connect to the green jack, and will process the sound itself separating the Base for the subwoofer.

2.1 is just normal speakers with a subwoofer - higher end models will have a separate subwoofer input for your orange subwoofer jack on your computer.

To my knowledge Ubuntu cannot re-order/assign the output jacks on your computer like in windows.
