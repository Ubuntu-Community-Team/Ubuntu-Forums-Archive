### Post by lisati on 2011-03-01
When the live CD asks for a username and password, it sometimes indicates a bad burn. On the rare occasion that it has happened to me, waiting for it to autologin has sometimes helped.

---

### Post by oldos2er on 2011-03-01
I agree with a bad burn being a probable cause for being asked for a user/password. Check the disc with [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HowToMD5SUM](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HowToMD5SUM)
and make sure you're burning at the lowest possible speed.
