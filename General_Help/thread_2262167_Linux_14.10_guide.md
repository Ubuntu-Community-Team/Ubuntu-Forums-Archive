### Post by Holger_Gehrke on 2015-01-23
> **Morten_Fjellheim said:**
> 
Second question, when ubuntu closes because you have not done anything for sometime, will processes that was started before closing continue to run? Or will they close down too, i am thinking about downloads, programs performing tasks etc.......

Ubuntu doesn't close anything on inactivity by default. It just blanks the screen and it might spin down the HD if there was no access to it for some time, but it just keeps right on running otherwise. It does have standby / hibernation states, but you have to tell Ubuntu to go to these, for example by closing the lid on a laptop or by selecting an appropriate item from a menu.
