### Post by BkkBonanza on 2012-07-31
Menu > System, Preferences, Keyboard Shortcuts.
I have used this for several programs I put on the "Winbloze" key combinations on my keyboard.

### Post by na5h on 2012-07-31
Yes, you can find all keyboard shortcuts under System Settings->Keyboard->Shortcuts.

Edit: looks like BkkBonanza beat me to it.

You can also launch the apps in the Unity launcher by pressing the super key + number.
