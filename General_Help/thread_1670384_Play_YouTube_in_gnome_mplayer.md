### Post by lovinglinux on 2011-01-18
Get [FlashVideoReplacer](https://addons.mozilla.org/addon/flashvideoreplacer/) extension to watch YouTube, Vimeo and Metacafe with a different plugin, like gecko-mediaplayer or a standalone external player.

Next version will also support Ustream and Blip.tv.
