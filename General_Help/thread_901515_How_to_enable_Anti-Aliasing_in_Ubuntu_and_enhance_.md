### Post by conundrumx on 2008-08-26
I'm sure someone will correct me if I'm wrong here.

DirectX is basically the way your games and GPU communicate.  It's proprietary to Microsoft, and they're in no rush to open the source.  The equivalent in the *nix world is OpenGL.  Some games do use it (For example, Blizzard's games designed to work on OSX can render in OpenGL), but they are in the minority.

If you want to play DirectX 9 games on *nix virtualization may be your best answer.
