### Post by JackTheDipper on 2008-08-07
Hi, amadeus!

File->New
Apply

Alternatively delete ~/.gnome-color-chooser/config.xml, restart gnomecc, and click apply.

The default settings of GNOME Color Chooser are to not overwrite any colors of your original theme.

Jack ;-)
