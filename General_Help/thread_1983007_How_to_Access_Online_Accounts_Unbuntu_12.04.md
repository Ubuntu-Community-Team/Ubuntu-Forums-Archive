### Post by 74corvette on 2012-05-19
After upgrading to 12.04 the online accounts settings under system settings is gone.  (I am using the Unity interface) When I install gnome contacts, the first time running the application the application jumps me to system settings>online accounts to setup the online account, ie google.  Now that this is setup contacts works fine.  Problem is I want to change the online account to a different google account.  I can't seem to find where I can access the online account settings again. ---

### Post by Menthu_Rae on 2012-05-20
Hey mate,

You can access it by running a terminal or pressing ALT+F2 and then entering:

```
gnome-control-center online-accounts
```

Hope that sorts it for you. ;)

---

### Post by 74corvette on 2012-05-20
That did it.  Thanks for your help.
