### Post by vanadium on 2017-09-03
You can put as many as you want. When they do not fit, the icons will scroll. You can also reduce the launcher icon size to fit more icons.

Consider whether you really have 20 most used apps. Limit the dock to your top 10 or even top 5. This way, you still have the one click access to your (really) mostly used apps. However, managing running apps on the dock will be a lot easier. Other apps are so easily loaded with a press on the Super key and typing a few letters of their name. In the new forthcoming Ubuntu, you will have an icon on your dock that with one click shows icons to your 20 most used apps. So your top 20 can be launched with 2 clicks.

### Post by deadflowr on 2017-09-03
Well over 100 apps can fit on to the launcher.

When it reaches a point where the icon might not seem to fit, they'll auto-fold like an accordion.

But you can resize the icons in System Settings > Appearance, if you want to set a smaller, or larger icon size.
If that's something you want to do.
