### Post by corncob on 2011-01-22
Or right-click 'Applications' on the panel and select 'Edit Menus' (or enter 'alacarte' in the terminal).  Highlight 'System Tools' in the left pane and check the box for 'Configuration Editor' in the right pane.  Configuration Editor should now show up in your applications menu under System Tools.
