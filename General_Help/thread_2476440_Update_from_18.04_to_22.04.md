### Post by grahammechanical on 2022-06-26
An alternative method is to open Software & Updates>Updates tab and set Notify me of a new Ubuntu version to For long term support versions. And then update/upgrade. You should get a dialog inviting you to upgrade to Ubuntu 20.04.

At this moment in time the online upgrade path from 20.04 to 22.04 is not yet open and will not be until 22.04.1 is released in October 2022. So, we either wait or download the 22.04 ISO image and re-install.

Regards
