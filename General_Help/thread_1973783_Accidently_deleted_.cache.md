During that time, I didnt notice I shouldnt have deleted .cache, until I did and turned off the computer. Obviously, once i turned it on again, I lost all my settings, folder placements, etc.

### Post by Zvlwab on 2012-05-05
Mostly there aren't any really important files in ~/.cache, especially on a fresh install.
You should be carefull not to delete folders such as ~/.config or ~/.local and perhaps some other folders, because they do contain settings that may be annoying to lose, though still not fatal.
