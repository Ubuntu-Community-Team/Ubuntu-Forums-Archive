### Post by zami on 2007-04-24
ah HAH!  Evolution is quite capable of it.

But there does seem to be a trick...

You must start by right clicking on a message that examples what you want to filter, then >Create Rule From Message, then >Filter on Subject (or whatever you want to filter on, of course.)

I tried to create a filter through the menu first, and since everything was in that un-selectable de-highlighted state, I just figured I was  doing something wrong, or had the wrong idea of "filter".

Anyhow, here I am answering my own question... mabye someone will have the same and I can save them a moment of time.
