### Post by The Bright Side on 2009-12-11
It's strange: I have had the issue you're talking of, but it only happened to me when I installed the player. The player (controls etc) and playlist would both appear to the left of the screen, so I moved both to the right, then clicked the icon to minimize.

After some time, when I clicked the icon again to bring the player and playlist back, the player would appear to the left of the screen and the playlist to the right. Every hide/unhide seemed to reset that.

What I did: move both player and playlist where I wanted them, then QUIT Audacious without minimizing before. I figured that way it'd store the window position. And lo and behold, it did. Ever since, the player and playlist have been aligned neatly to the right of my desktop.
