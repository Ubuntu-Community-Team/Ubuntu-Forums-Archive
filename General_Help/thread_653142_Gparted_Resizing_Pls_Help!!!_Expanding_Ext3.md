### Post by geirha on 2007-12-29
Well, then you'll need to 
shrink /dev/sdc1
move /dev/sdc1 to the far right
move /dev/sdc4 right next to /dev/sdc1
grow /dev/sdc3 to use all the new space between /dev/sdc3 and /dev/sdc4

This should be fairly easy to do from the ubuntu liveCD or the gparted liveCD, though it will take a lot of time (could take hours). And also, when resizing fat/ntfs partitions, make sure to defrag first (in windows).
