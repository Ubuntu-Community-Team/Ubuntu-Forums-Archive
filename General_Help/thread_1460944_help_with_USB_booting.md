### Post by oldfred on 2010-04-23
Do you have 5 or 6 GB that you can make a new partition on your hard drive. Then you can just do another install from your liveCD. I have 3 installs on my drives, 9.04, 9.10 & beta now RC. I was going to overwrite my 9.04 but it is the one I have used for 3 years so I may save it for another 6 months and just create another 20GB partition for my install. All my data is in other partitions and my install with many added programs is just over 5GB.

since I have my partitions set up, it only took about 10min to install the RC from my USB key. Once you have a working grub you can edit it to boot & use your USB or anything else.
