### Post by KeyserSoze93 on 2010-04-16
You mean you don't see the laptop BIOS, as normal? It sounds like a problem with the laptop itself, but one thing you could try - if you haven't already - is to take the battery out of the laptop, unplug all power, and wait a few minutes.

This should make sure that if the laptop tried to hibernate, or standby, it is actually shut off properly.
