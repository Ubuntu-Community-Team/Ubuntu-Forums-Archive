### Post by Peter Maunder on 2013-06-24
Once you select a font on the Writer screen, you will find that it will have been moved to the top of the list. this will save you waded through the list just to find a font. The whole list is there but your active fonts are at the top.

You can also change the default font associated with a paragraph or character style. This allows me to write without having to select a font, unless I specifically need to override this option.

I believe LibreOffice and OpenOffice look through the fonts you have on your system and includes them all for your use.
