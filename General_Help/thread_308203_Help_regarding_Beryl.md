### Post by strabes on 2006-11-28
make sure the desktop cube and rotate cube plugins are enabled in the beryl manager. The default way to do it is ctrl+alt+leftmousebutton & drag. Then you can move it around. You can change the 'zoom' factor in the rotate cube plugin.
