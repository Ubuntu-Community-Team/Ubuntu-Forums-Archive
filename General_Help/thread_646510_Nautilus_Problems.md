Sometimes this will happen half a dozen times or more before I can finally open the sub folder I am aiming for.

2) If I open a folder in a new window, or open an application from Nautilus, and then close it, every time I open a different folder, the closed window or application will re-open.

Both of these things happen unexpectedly, more often than not, and without any apparent reason. They occurred in 7.04, and are still there in 7.10. At the moment, I'm using a different file manager (xfe), but it seems impossible to dispense with Nautilus altogether - for example folders on the desktop will always open with Nautilus, and it seems pretty closely linked to other aspects of Gnome. If anyone knows what's causing these problems, and what to do about it, I'd be glad to hear.
