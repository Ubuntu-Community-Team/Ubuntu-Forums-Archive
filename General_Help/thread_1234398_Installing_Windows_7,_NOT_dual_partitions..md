Hold up a second, you signed up to the Ubuntu Forum today just to ask how to install Windows 7 and wipe out Ubuntu? Don't you think that's kinda backwards? 

The answer is trivial, same as you did with Ubuntu. Backup important data on partitions your going to get rid of (presumably root and home).
