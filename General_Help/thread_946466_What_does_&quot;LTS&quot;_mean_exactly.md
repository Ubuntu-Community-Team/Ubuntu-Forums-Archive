### Post by snowpine on 2008-10-13
You will not get new versions of packages; package version numbers are "frozen" as part of a release. You can upgrade to newer versions of packages using the "backports" feature: [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/UbuntuBackports](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/UbuntuBackports)

What you do get with a LTS release is 3 years of bug fixes, critical updates, patches, etc. plus of course the repositories remain online so you can add applications/packages.
