### Post by Manuel_Olivier on 2016-06-19
Hello Camilia

If you aim to change fonts for specific styles of writing on LibreOffice Writer, you can follow these steps:
[LIST]
[*]after opening a spreadsheet, go to the headings window (top left) and click on "more styles" 
[*]a panel is opening on the right side of the sheet, then you have to select the heading you wish to modify by just clicking on it 
[*]after doing that, you can click on the "Properties" icon (2nd icon from the top, it looks like a tiny window) 
[/LIST]
            This is the final step, now you can modify everything you want including fonts!

I hope this answers your question.

Manuel
