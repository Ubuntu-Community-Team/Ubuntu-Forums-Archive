### Post by aysiu on 2008-09-24
Now that Ubuntu has reliable NTFS-writing support, I wouldn't recommend a separate shared partition. I'd just do two partitions and keep your data on (and make larger) the partition you'll be using more often.

---

### Post by FluffyElmo on 2008-09-24
Something you may want to consider is that the first partition is faster. You should try to get whatever OS you'll be using the most installed in that partition. Given laptop drives it will probably be noticeable.
