### Post by fragos on 2009-02-23
The ket to if a WiFi card will wwork is what WiFi chip set they use. Using the card's model you may be able to determine that. You could try searching this forum for the card's model name aalthough searching with the chipset is more likely to give you a result. Cards with the Intel chip sets work out of the box. Broadcom chip sets are very popular and they will work as well but you've needed to follow a series of steps to get them to work. Hopefully an open source driver will become available and that problem will disapear.
