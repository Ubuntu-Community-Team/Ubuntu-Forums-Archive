### Post by AusIV4 on 2007-03-08
I'm sure if you really want to you could find something to transcode them, but why? I use Amarok, and installing libxine-extracodecs was all I needed to get m4a support. They play fine. If I were to convert them to Ogg, there would be some quality loss. I'm not one to complain about lossy formats, but I don't see any reason to convert files unnecessarily.
