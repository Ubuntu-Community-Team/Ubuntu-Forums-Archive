### Post by howefield on 2010-07-18
If you don't feel compelled to upgrade, then don't, unless you are running an unsupported version.

There is always the risk of something going wrong, however there is a better than equal chance of it going sweetly.  

If you do, make sure your data is safely backed up before starting. (actually, you want your important data backed up irrespective of any reason)
