the beta of version 11 is available from Adobe and works very well and can be found here [http://labs.adobe.com/downloads/flashplayer11.html](http://labs.adobe.com/downloads/flashplayer11.html)

However as it is beta, to other users i recommend flash-aid

[https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/flash-aid/](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/flash-aid/)

though it is for firefox it finds and install the correct version for your system and the other browsers use it also.
