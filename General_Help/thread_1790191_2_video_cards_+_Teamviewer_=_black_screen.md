### Post by cyper.bg on 2011-06-24
So I'm a complete Linux newbie and need help :D
Version is 11.04

I installed Teamviewer which worked like a charm when there was only one video card. I put a second video card and now when I connect to the machine I get a black screen. The remote administration part is working as I can move the cursor and open menus. It is just that I can't see what I'm doing because of the black screen.

This is what I've tried so far:

> To resolve black screen after connecting to a remote pc, you need to perform this procedure on your end (not on the client pc).
 
On top of the Teamviewer screen, you need to click on the View menu, click Quality, click Custom Settings.
 
Next, click on the View menu again, click Quality, click on Edit custom settings...
Adjust the colors from 256 to between high and true color. You may adjust the quality in the middle.
 
Click Apply and click OK.

I also removed the screensaver and Put display to sleep when inactive to Never.
