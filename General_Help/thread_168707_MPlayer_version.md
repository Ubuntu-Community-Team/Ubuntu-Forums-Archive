### Post by yager on 2006-05-04
Open Mplayer and right click in the window where the video would show.  Select the About MPlayer menu item to find your version.

Here is the link to find the most current version.  [http://www.mplayerhq.hu/design7/dload.html](http://www.mplayerhq.hu/design7/dload.html)

Whether you have the latest version or not would depend on when you used automatix.  I am not an expert but when the script is running, it appears to be pulling the latest version of each app from the source.
