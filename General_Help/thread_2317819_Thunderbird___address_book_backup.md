### Post by howefield on 2016-03-20
Launch Files (file manager) which should open at your home folder and press the Ctrl + h keys together, this key combination will toggle viewing hidden files folders. You can also show hidden files by using the menu option.. Files > View > Show Hidden Files.

The Thunderbird profile including the address book will be in the .thunderbird folder. Take a copy of it. 

Or possibly even simpler, why not open the address book in Thunderbird and from the menu select Tools > Export, you can export the address book to an LDIF or comma separated or Tab Delimited file.
