### Post by gsmanners on 2010-01-07
Adobe works the best with the wide variety of Flash applications out there, but it's not open source. The other two are open source, so they have less issues with security, etc. but they don't have as good compatibility.

So, unless you have some qualms about closed-source apps, you should really use the Adobe version.
