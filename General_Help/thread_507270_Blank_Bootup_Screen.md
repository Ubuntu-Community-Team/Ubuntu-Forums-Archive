### Post by ReaderRat on 2007-07-22
I have Intel i810 on my computer, and it worked out of the box.
Did you check the CD you burned the LiveCD iso on? (Option when you run the LiveCD)
Did you never get past the bootup screen? If so, you may need to burn another LiveCD ***image***. Burn at a very low speed (2X, 4X,as low as the burner will go).
