### Post by michaelaly on 2008-07-31
Photoshop CS runs well under wine as well, though there are some oddities such as windows disappearing or certain hot keys not working. 
Running photoshop is wine is great if you just need to do something quick and simple, for more robust projects GIMP is a viable alternative(granted it has a steep learning curve), or running photoshop virtually under XP works well, if you have plenty of memory.
