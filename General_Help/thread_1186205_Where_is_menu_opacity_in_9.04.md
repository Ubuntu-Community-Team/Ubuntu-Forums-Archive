### Post by ACMiller on 2009-06-13
Hey, it's moved to under the Opacity, brightness and saturation tab in the accessibility section. Just add (type=Menu | PopupMenu | DropdownMenu) to the window specific settings and select you desired opacity.

Hope that helps, cheers

### Post by PureLoneWolf on 2009-06-13
I think I found what you are looking for. I presume you have installed the compizconfig-settings-manager?

In there choose "Advanced Search" and search for Opacity.  When I do that, the 8th plugin it finds is called "Opacity, Brightness and Saturation".

Select that and tick the "Settings Value" box - You will then see the option under Accessibility.

Hope that is what you were looking for
