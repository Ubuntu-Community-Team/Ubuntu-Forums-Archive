### Post by wwwil on 2010-05-08
I'm not sure about the separating protocols but you can use the show protocol option in the view menu to display an icon indicating the protocol of the contact next to their name.

The quickest way to reply to someone who talks to you is using the indicator applet, the little envelope in the top panel on the right next to the date.

This can be used to acess chat, mail and broadcast account programs integrated into Ubuntu and will turn green when someone talks to you. if you click it you will then be able to see the contacts name and can click it to open the conversation window.

I don't think there is any contact filtering facility in empathy yet.

Hope this helps.
