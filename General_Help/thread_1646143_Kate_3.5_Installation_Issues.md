### Post by lykwydchykyn on 2010-12-15
Since kate is part of kde, compiling it is going to mean compiling a lot of kde components as well -- probably not worth the trouble.

You are probably better off adding the kubuntu-backports ppa and installing whatever version they have there through the repositories.

See instructions here: [https://launchpad.net/~kubuntu-ppa/+archive/backports](https://launchpad.net/~kubuntu-ppa/+archive/backports)
