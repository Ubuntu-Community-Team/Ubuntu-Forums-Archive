### Post by jim_p on 2007-08-23
I suppose your question is if your card reader will work on ubuntu or not ...

I have recently bought a "noname" card reader that reads the majority of cards and it works flawlessly.
I have tried XD, MMC, CF, SD and MS cards and all of them are read and written (and even formatted) perfectly.

Just one thing though: ALWAYS use the "Unmount volume" command.
I have removed an XD card once without unmounting and the first 2-3 pictures were not readable from the camera anymore :( I just lost them.
