### Post by SirShaggy on 2007-04-02
I really am not sure if this will help, but may be worth a try. I have had this same problem before and this cured it for me. I noticed each time I logged in to Ubuntu, it was sort of restoring the previous session. As I got errors, they kept coming back. I did this:
Restarted the computer. When I got to the logon screen, I selected gnome from the sessions option. Then I logged in as normal. It asked if I would like to make this the default login and I said yes.
This got rid of my freezing gnome session. I still don't have a reason, it just worked for me.

Hope this helps, even a little.
