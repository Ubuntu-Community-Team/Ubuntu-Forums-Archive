### Post by Mark Phelps on 2013-05-30
It's most likely, that if you have a new Win8 PC, the wireless hardware is too new to have Linux drivers.

To see what you have, in Ubuntu, open a terminal and enter "lspci".  That will list out your devices.

Also, I believe that 10.10 reached end-of -life.  You should have installed a version that is still being supported -- like 12.04.
