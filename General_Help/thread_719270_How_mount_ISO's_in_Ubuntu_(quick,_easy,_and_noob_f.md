### Post by doorknob60 on 2008-03-09
First, download the attached file to your Desktop or wherever you want it. Then, right click on the file, and click properties. Then, in the permissions tab, allow it to be executable. Then, close the properties box, double click on installisomount.sh and click Run or Run in Terminal, it doesn't matter which. Then, when you right click on an ISO, go into the scripts menu and choose mount.sh. To unmount it, right click on the same ISO and choose unmount.sh.

I made this script from this guide: [http://www.ubuntugeek.com/mount-and-unmout-iso-images-without-burning-them.html](http://www.ubuntugeek.com/mount-and-unmout-iso-images-without-burning-them.html)

Tell me if something doesn't work right.
