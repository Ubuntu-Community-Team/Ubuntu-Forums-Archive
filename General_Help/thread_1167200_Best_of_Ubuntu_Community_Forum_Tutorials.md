### Post by duanedesign on 2009-05-22
The boucft team is collecting the best how-to/tutorials from the forum and converting them to DocBook for use in Yelp. Too often really great information gets buried in the forum. Our goal is to pull all the best how-to/tutorials and get them organized by topic in one convenient place. Just like your system documentation boucft can be accessed even when your offline. This will be a great tool for Ubuntu users of all skill level. Please help contribute to the project by posting your favorite how-to/tutorials in this thread.
