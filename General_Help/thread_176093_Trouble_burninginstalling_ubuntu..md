### Post by aysiu on 2006-05-14
There's no point in re-downloading or re-burning ISOs unless you do a checksum on the image to make sure it didn't corrupt during download or burn the CD at a slower speed to ensure that it doesn't corrupt during the burn.

For more details, go [here](http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/windowstoubuntu.html).
