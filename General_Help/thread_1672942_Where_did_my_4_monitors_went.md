### Post by Old_Grey_Wolf on 2011-01-21
The default installation has only 2 workspaces boxes. If you have 2 workspaces, right click on a workspace and select "Preferences", then increase the number of columns to 4. If you do not have the workspace boxes, right click on the panel, select "Add to Panel...", scroll down the list and select "Workspace Switcher". Then click the "Add" button.

### Post by Old_Grey_Wolf on 2011-01-21
Right click on the workspaces and un-check the "Lock To Panel".
Right click on the workspaces and select "Move".
Move it to where you want it and left click.
After you have it positioned the way you want it, right click on the workspaces and check the "Lock To Panel".

Edit: you can remove the second copy by right click and select "Remove From Panel".
