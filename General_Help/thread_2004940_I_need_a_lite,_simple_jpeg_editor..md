### Post by vasa1 on 2012-06-16
Depending on what else you have installed, Pinta *may* bring in a whole bunch of **mono** dependencies. This is relevant in 12.04 and wasn't the case in 11.10.

You could see that with [FONT="Courier New"]apt-get install -s pinta[/FONT] where -s does a simulation and doesn't need sudo.

Depending on your editing needs, GNU Paint may be the lightest.
