### Post by mcduck on 2010-12-26
> **L Style said:**
> I want to burn a mp3 cd but brasero can't burn mp3 cd. How can I do this. If any further software need burn mp3 cd please help me

An "MP3 CD" is just a normal data CD with MP3 files in it. *Any* CD burning application will do that for you.

(If you want to burn Audio CD, a disc playable on a normal CD player, Brasero will do that for you as well. At least if you have the correct codecs installed for decoding your original audio files)
