### Post by UbuntuNerd on 2009-03-11
for the future
Where are Icons and Themes store 

/usr/share/themes

/usr/share/icons

/usr/share/pixmaps 

~/pixmaps are generally used for menu icons
~/share/icons are generally used for theme icons
