### Post by gingermark on 2006-05-07
KBFX for the menu I think.
[http://www.kde-look.org/content/show.php?content=37934](http://www.kde-look.org/content/show.php?content=37934)

Download the package, run Konsole, navigate to where the file was downloaded to (for example, if you plonked it on your Desktop, then do "cd Desktop/"

Then do "sudo dpkg -i packagename.deb"

You can then right click on your panel, select "Add applet to panel" and select KBFX.
