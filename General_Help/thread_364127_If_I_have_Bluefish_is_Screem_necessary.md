### Post by aysiu on 2007-02-17
Bluefish and Screem are both web development tools. If Bluefish does everything you want, you don't need Screem. Go ahead and uninstall it.

Nothing will get messed up if you uninstall *ubuntu-desktop*, **but** it's recommended you keep it installed for a more likely smooth upgrade... when it's time to upgrade.
