### Post by dburnett77 on 2008-06-09
Could be a hack.  They're all over the printer server port 631.

In your browser, in the address bar, enter:

localhost:631

and see if your printer is listed correctly; then try and print a test page.

If you can't print a test page, re-identify it by selecting "management" within the pages listed in the CUPS interface in your browser session.

I know I'm having terrible difficulty even installing a driver.

People suffering the budget cutbacks, I'd assume, and they don't mind releasing rogue'ish codes to thwart and busy driver development.
