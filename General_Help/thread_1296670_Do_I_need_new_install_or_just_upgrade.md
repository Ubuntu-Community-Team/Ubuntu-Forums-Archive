### Post by wilee-nilee on 2009-10-20
Yes you could upgrade, but the general consensus is that a fresh install of 9.10 is probably best due to the extension change from 3 to 4. This can be dealt with along with other things, but no matter what have that important data backed up. A fresh install is going to be the fastest easiest way though in my opinion, unless you want to wallow in the terminal, and possibly end up borked. Make sure to wait for the release and stability before you do anything on your relied on main computer.  You might want to look through the Koala part of the forum before you do anything.
