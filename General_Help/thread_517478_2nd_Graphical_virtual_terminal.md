### Post by apswartz on 2007-08-04
Yes, I just tried it out to be sure.

Press Ctrl-Alt-F2 and log in.

issue this command...
```
startx -- :1
```

then toggle between the desktops with Ctrl-Alt-F7 and Ctrl-Alt-F9
