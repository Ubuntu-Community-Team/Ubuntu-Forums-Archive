### Post by eriktheblu on 2010-03-01
Short answer: no

What you actually have is a panel applet (terminology may be off here) that lists your currently open windows.

You can move this function to your top panel, or move all the items from your top panel to the bottom.

It can be done, but it won't be automatic.
