### Post by jockyburns on 2013-04-24
Solved it. Turns out it was Adobe flashplayer that had two versions running. I disabled the pepper flash version and video now works fine. But,,, why would updates re-activate the pepper flash version? TBQH If it ain't broke, don't fix it.
