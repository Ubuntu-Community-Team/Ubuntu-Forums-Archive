This bug [https://bugs.launchpad.net/unity/+bug/954446](https://bugs.launchpad.net/unity/+bug/954446) has been "bugging" me for a long time now. I try to use my TV as an external monitor to play movies directly from my computer, which should be quite nice. The bug is confirmed, but it seems it is completely ignored by the unity team. Is it actually normal that there is no reaction for more than a month and a half? If not, are we doing something wrong? Or is there just too much work to manage these bugs.

If that is the case however, it seems to me that the concept of Open Source Software cannot meet its own goals (e.g. being managed by the user community itself). If you report a bug and there is no reaction to it, it doesn't get fixed. So what's the whole point?

What are your thoughts and experiences on this?
