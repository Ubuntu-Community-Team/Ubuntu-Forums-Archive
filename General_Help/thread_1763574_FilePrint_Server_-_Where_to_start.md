### Post by AlphaLexman on 2011-05-20
It seems like you trying to bite off more than you can chew at once. Linux/Unix variants can have a fairly steep learning curve.

What is your level of experience with other OS's (i.e. MS, Mac)?
Do you have a hardware RAID already setup or are you trying to do a software RAID? Have you done it before, on another OS?
Is your router and/or modem setup and installed correctly?

I would get as many Live CD's and try them until you find one you like, or are comfortable with. Check out [distrowatch.com]("http://distrowatch.com/") for a broad selection.

I can't really give you one particular link that will guide you from beginner to complete working status of your requirements. You will have to narrow down your needs and identify your goals.

Good Luck!
