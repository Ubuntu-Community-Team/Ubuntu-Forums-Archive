### Post by mb_webguy on 2009-02-27
Try right-click on the Volume applet on the top panel and select "Open Volume Control".  In the drop-down box, look for capture devices.  There should be one that corresponds to your microphone.  Make sure the volume isn't muted or turned all the way down.
