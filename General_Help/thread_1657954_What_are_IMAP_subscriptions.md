### Post by dcstar on 2011-01-02
> **nbtrap said:**
> Can someone please explain to me the concept of IMAP subscriptions? I use IMAP with Evolution, and I don't have any folders marked as "subscribed", yet my inboxes download the mail fine. I don't understand, then, what subscribing to these folders will do.

Subscribed folders are checked for any changes. Using IMAP means that changes can be made to the data on the server from other places and your own e-mail client will only find out about these changes if it specifically checks for them.
