### Post by hyperdude111 on 2010-03-01

### Post by brjoon1021 on 2010-03-01
Another idea is to have 2 panels at the top, but to have them both pretty thin (saving real estate). The topmost panel is for app launcher icons, notification area and all of that. The second panel (bottom of the 2) is just for open applications. This is good if you have a lot of things open and you get tired of trying to read the small print of the crowded boxes in a one panel configuration, or are getting carpel tunnel syndrome from alt + tab'ing so much.

If it worked you will see a screenshot of what I mean. There aren't many apps open, but the bottom of the 2 panels shows apps and the desktop switcher only.
