### Post by darolu on 2010-03-01
If you have a speaker icon on the top-right corner, right click on it and then click on "sound preferences", play with the settings in the window that pops up, make sure you are using the right output mode (analog or digital) that nothing is muted (i.e. your master channel), etc.
You can do this from the terminal with "alsamixer".

### Post by dragonboss on 2010-03-01
Do you mean your mp3's and other audi files don't play or did you hear the ubuntu startup sound? If you heard the sound at strtup then all you need to do is install the ubuntu restricted extras in the software center in the applications menu. Also check the sound icon to see if audio is muted by right click.
