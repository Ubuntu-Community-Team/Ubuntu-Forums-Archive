### Post by Jackelope on 2008-06-20
> **fazillatheef said:**
> About the linsta theme... does it support blur on the decorations.. 


As far as I know, you can blur the background in any emerald window border by enabling the 'blur' plugin in the CCSM (compiz config settings manager) and setting your Visual Effects to custom. CCSM is in the repos if you don't have it.
