### Post by patjennings on 2011-08-15
Hi just wanted to post an annoying problem I had. When using a google search in firefox 3.6.18 on maverick, using the back button it would always send me to the 1st page of search. For example when i am on the 4th page of a google search, i visit a site and when i click back instead of sending me to page 4 of search, it will show page 4 for a split second then send me back to page 1. I realised the google instant  had something to do with it, so after disabling google instant in google settings the back button worked properly. So i marked this thread as solved even though it is only partially solved. It is a real pain in the toukas to disable autocomplete everytime the history has been cleared.
