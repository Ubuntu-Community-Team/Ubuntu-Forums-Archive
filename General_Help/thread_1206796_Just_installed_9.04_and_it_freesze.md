Does your computer feel warm? Do you hear fans spinning at full speed? Could be heat.
Try as soon as the system is operational to open a terminal
```
(Applications > Accessories > Terminal)
```
 and type
```
top
```
to see if there is a process which is eating your CPU.
Look in the column %CPU. The processes on top take the most of the CPU.
