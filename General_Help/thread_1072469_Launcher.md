### Post by InspiredIndividual on 2009-02-17
At my laptop (fresh Intrepid install), there's an option to enable/disable networking in the 'Network Monitor' on the panel. If the Network Monitor is not on your panel, you can add it as follows: right-click the panel choose 'Add to panel', choose 'Network Monitor'. If you're looking for other easy options to save battery life, you might also be interested in 'Battery Charge Monitor', 'Brightness Applet' and 'Inhibit Applet'.

Play around with the options in the 'Network Monitor'. I'm not sure there's an option to connect to a hidden network. I've never had need for it, so unfortunately I can't help you with that.
