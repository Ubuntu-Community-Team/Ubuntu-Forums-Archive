### Post by tgalati4 on 2015-02-18
Has the computer ever been dropped?  If so, then perhaps the heat sink is no longer bolted securely to the CPU.  Or, the heat sink paste has dried out and needs to be replaced.  Or, you have dust in the fan and that is limiting the air flow.
