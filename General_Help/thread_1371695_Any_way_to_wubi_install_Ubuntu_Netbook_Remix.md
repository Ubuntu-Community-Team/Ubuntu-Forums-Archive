### Post by t0p on 2010-01-04
Ubuntu Netbook Remix is mostly just Ubuntu with a different user interface.  Some people think it's better for use with a small screen; but I much prefer the normal Gnome GUI on my EeePC's 7-inch screen.  Down to personal taste I suppose.

As for doing a wubi install: would it really be that terrible to repartition your hard drive?  I've never used wubi myself; but I'm always seeing posts in these forums by users complaining that their wubi set-up doesn't do this, that and the other.  As far as I've been able to ascertain, a wubi set-up just isn't as capable as a regular install.  And repartitioning your HDD is a pretty simple operation.  I strongly recommend a proper install.
