### Post by Rasa1111 on 2011-07-24
The only way I'm aware of (though Im sure there must be a better way),
Is to do this....

 Right click on a media file, (ie. mp3, flv, mp4, avi,) etc.. 
Then when the menu comes up, choose "Open with other application"

When the window opens, Choose VLC player, and in the little check box at the bottom..
choose "remember for this type of file" , and click "Open". 

You'll have to do this once for each type of file you use, but that's it. 

Then you can just double click on any file types youve done that for, and they'll open right up in VLC

I know it's not the greatest method (can't be). lol
but it's what Ive used in the past and works fine. 

Hope it's useful.
