### Post by jarviser on 2010-05-19
> **omelette said:**
> Anyone else finding that just in the last few days, Hotmail no longer works with Firefox?

It does still work with Firefox, but there are certain things that can fail including loss of included message on reply or forward. Microsoft and Mozilla are still arguing who's fault it is, but I don't see MS spending a lot of time fixing their mail system to suit a major competitor, even though it seems to be Microsoft's bad.

I have noticed that if you disallow third party cookies and any other stealth measures like Noscript you can get these kind of problems.

So I would open up all stealthing measures including allowing all cookies, clear the cache (Edit preferences, advanced, Network tab) and restart Firefox and try again.
