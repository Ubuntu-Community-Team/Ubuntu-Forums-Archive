### Post by egeezer on 2016-10-31
Treat the data transfer in the same way you'd transfer it to a thumb drive: mount the other computer's file manager (it will be listed under Devices) and you'll see all the files and folders listed there; then do a Copy/Paste.  Same thing in the other direction.

### Post by vasa1 on 2016-10-31
Which file manager are you using? Look in its Preferences, under the "Volume Management" tab or something like that. In any case you *should* see your device listed in the side pane of your file manager. If you have chosen not to mount it automatically, right-clicking on its icon in the side-pane may give you that option.
