### Post by Meelad on 2011-10-19
> **Simeo said:**
> Alright, I know it's got to be something simple, but how do I place a link to the home folder on the Desktop in Gnome 3 on Ubuntu 11.10?

I did it with Ubuntu Tweak on Unity.. Under "Tweaks" you can choose to show a few icons like Computer, Home folder, Trash, and a few others.

And an easier way that worked in Unity too is to open the Home folder, and drog the "Home" icon in the top bar (that shows the location) while holding both CTRL and SHIFT and drop it on the desktop.. Could work with gnome3 too I guess..
