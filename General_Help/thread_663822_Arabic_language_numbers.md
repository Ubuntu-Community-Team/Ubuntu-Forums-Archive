### Post by diaa on 2008-01-26
To enable true Arabic numerals in OOo, from the menu[LIST]
[*] Open *Tools* > *Options, *then go to *Language Settings* > *Languages*
[*]Check the box titled *Enabled for complex text layout(CTL)* then click Ok(It's better to choose a suitable dialect of Arabic infront of CTL but this is not required to get Hindi numerals)
[*]Open the same dialog again via  *Tools* > *Options* then* go to * *Language Settings* > *Complex Text Layout* then under *General options*, *Numerals* choose *Hindi*[/LIST]
