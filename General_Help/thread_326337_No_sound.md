### Post by tzulberti on 2006-12-28
You should go to System, Preferences, Sound. There clic on the Test button. If you hear the BEEEEEEEEEEP then only restart the PC. If not, you should choose another driver for that sound event, and clic on Test Button.
Do the same with all the other events, but not with Sound Capture.
