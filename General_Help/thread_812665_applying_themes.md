### Post by billgoldberg on 2008-05-30
I'm not sure what you mean with "compiz" themes.

If you mean the transparent windows borders or  windows borders with the buttons on the left, then you need "emerald". Download and install it the normal way.

Then there will be a mentioning of emerald in "system -> preferences".

Import your emerald theme, log out and log back in *(or use [compiz fusion icon]("http://forlong.blogage.de/article/pages/Compiz-Switch") to restart compiz, or restart it manually using the terminal)* for it to work.

"Compiz" themes (emerald themes) can be downloaded on gnome-look.org

note: you can also use the "beryl" themes if you like
