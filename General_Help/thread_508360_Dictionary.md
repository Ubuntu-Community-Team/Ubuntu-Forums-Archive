### Post by zanglang on 2007-07-24
Check out Stardict (package name 'stardict'). You'll have to download dictionary files from its website:
[http://stardict.sourceforge.net/Dictionaries_dictd-www.dict.org.php](http://stardict.sourceforge.net/Dictionaries_dictd-www.dict.org.php)

For pronunciations download this tar file and extract it into /usr/share:
[http://prdownloads.sourceforge.net/stardict/WyabdcRealPeopleTTS.tar.bz2?download](http://prdownloads.sourceforge.net/stardict/WyabdcRealPeopleTTS.tar.bz2?download)
