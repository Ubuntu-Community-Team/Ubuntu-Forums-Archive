### Post by audiomick on 2010-01-14
Hallo.
If it is all the time, change to a different Internet provider. What is the rest of your internet traffic like?
If it is only once, try again tomorrow. There might be a traffic jam.

Also, just check in
system> administration> update(whatever it is called in english)
there is an "options" or "preferences" button down the bottom on the right.
on the first tab in the pop up that opens, make sure it is looking at a mirror server somewhere near you and not on the other side of the world.
