### Post by Vaphell on 2009-10-01
and what settings you had in windows?
Assuming you have LCD, 60Hz is enough (LCDs don't flicker like CRTs used to at that frequency)

Eyestrain could be caused by the saturation/contrast/brightness. Personally i hate having lots of full strong white on screen (it makes my eyes tired fast), for example my terminal is always black with white text

You can try some theme with darker calmer colors and see if it helps.
