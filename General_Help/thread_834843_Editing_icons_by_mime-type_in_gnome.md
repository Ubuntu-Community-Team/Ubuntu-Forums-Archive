### Post by Happy_Man on 2008-06-19
Sir, I applaud your willingness, but regretfully, I must inform you that you may have to go into the icon theme itself to change that, as GNOME provides no such function by itself. 

Fair warning: GNOME will not give you the level of customization that KDE offers, due to the fact that the twO DEs are developed with completely different philosophies in mind. KDE wants every option to be up front, while GNOME strives to display only those options that they think would be needed. YMMV.
