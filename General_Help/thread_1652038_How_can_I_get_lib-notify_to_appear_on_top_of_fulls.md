### Post by Steeperton on 2010-12-24
If you're on about notify-osd, the only way I could find to do it, was to modify notify-osd itself. Feel free to try out my ppa package:

[https://launchpad.net/~avesummathat/+archive/ppa](https://launchpad.net/~avesummathat/+archive/ppa)

This puts the notify in the top right (none of that floating halfway down the screen nonsense), plus allows pop-ups over full screen.
