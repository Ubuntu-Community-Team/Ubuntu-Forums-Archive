### Post by hansdown on 2009-02-15
Hi grs.

Click on system> preferences> sound. On this screen click sounds, and you will see a list with log in. You can set it to no sound.

### Post by Slim Odds on 2009-02-15
That one is in the System>Administration>Login Window.

Goto the Accessibility tab and uncheck the "Login screen ready" sound (and probably the other, but they're not checked by default).
