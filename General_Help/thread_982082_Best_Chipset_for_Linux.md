### Post by igknighted on 2008-11-14
> **LowSky said:**
> AMD and Intel processors work fine. Im using the newest AMD Phenom 9950 and it works like a charm

Graphic Cards go with Nvidia, they offer the best support.

Err... I'm not sure this is accurate anymore.

Nvidia provides a binary driver that works for most, but if you have issues with it you are SOL.  No other options really to have a usable system without nvidia's binary support.

Intel offers full open source drivers for their graphics chips.  there are some bugs out there, but they are things that linux devs can fix themselves rather than wait for proprietary vendors to fix.  If you don't game regularly, I would strongly recommend intel graphics.

ATI/AMD has always been the bane of linux, but they are providing excellent drivers now, at least on par with nvidia's.  Plus, if the binary ATI driver gives you issues, they have released many specs so there are open source options so you can still have almost full functionality and use compiz or other 3d apps.

All have their pluses and minuses, but the simple "go with nvidia" answer just isn't relevant anymore.
