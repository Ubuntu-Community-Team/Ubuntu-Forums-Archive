### Post by cortman on 2012-02-04
Different people have had trouble with the USC refusing to download even though the internet connection is fine and installation from the command line works perfectly. Sometimes the best solution is to uninstall the USC and reinstall it.
The "untrusted packages" [here's a page]("http://askubuntu.com/questions/71188/how-to-bypass-remove-disable-the-untrusted-package-authentication") that shows how to fix that.
