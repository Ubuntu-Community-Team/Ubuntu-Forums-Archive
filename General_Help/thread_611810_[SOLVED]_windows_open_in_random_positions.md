### Post by dwasifar on 2007-11-13
Using a new install of Gutsy 64 with compiz effects enabled, my windows seem to choose random corners to go to when I open them.  There's no rhyme or reason to it that I can see.  They remember the SIZE from when they were closed last, but not the position.  Sometimes it's upper left, sometimes upper right, lower right, lower left.  The one thing they usually do NOT do is open where I left them - except for Nautilus folders and Amarok.

I think I speak for anyone with this problem when I say: WTF?

### Post by airbornemist6 on 2007-11-13
If you have compiz-fusion settings installed check out your "put" plugin. It most likely is either not enabled (it probably is though), or it has a weird setting. If that solves your problem please mark the thread solved.

> **airbornemist6 said:**

If the "put" plugin is the same as the "Place Windows" plugin, that was indeed the problem.  "Place Windows" was set to "Smart."  I set it to Centered and it's much more livable.

I don't know why they call the random-placement setting "Smart," except possibly in the sense that it makes the user feel stupid.  :)
