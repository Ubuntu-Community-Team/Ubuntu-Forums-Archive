### Post by yabbadabbadont on 2007-05-04
Right click on the applications menu and select "edit menus".  In the left pane, select accessories.  In the right pane check "File Browser".  Close the menu editor.  Now Nautilus should show up under Applications->Accessories and you can right-click and add it to the panel.
