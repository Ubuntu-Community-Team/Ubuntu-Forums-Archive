### Post by onaridge on 2010-10-30
Go to CompizConfig, click on desktop cube under desktop, click on appearance, and you will see the places for you to put the image files that you can download for the cube caps and skydome (the background behind the cube). Go to Beryl to get these image files, download them, then browse to them in Compiz and select. Beryl-themes.org has what you need and more. Have fun!
