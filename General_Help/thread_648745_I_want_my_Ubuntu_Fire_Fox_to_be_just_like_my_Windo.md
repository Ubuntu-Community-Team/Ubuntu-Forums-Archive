### Post by Animal Mother on 2007-12-24
Two things that I really like about FF in Windows:

1) When I hit the Backspace key the browser goes back

2) When I click in the address bar or google search bar everything is highlighted, so I can just type away and have whatever value is in there cleared out, instead of having to hit <ctrl a>.

These are two things that have hounded me in every distro of linux I have ever used and would like to get it sorted! Thanks!

---

### Post by ~LoKe on 2007-12-24
1.  Navigate to **about:config** in your address bar.  Type this into the filter:
> browser.backspace_action
Double click the entry and change the number to **0**.

2.  Navigate to **about:config** in your address bar.  Type this into the filter:
> browser.urlbar.clickSelectsAll
Double click the entry that shows up so that it's changed to "true".
