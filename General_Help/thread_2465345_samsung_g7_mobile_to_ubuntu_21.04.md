### Post by mIk3_08 on 2021-07-30
What kind of data will you be transferring? is it jpeg, jpg, pdf, documents, .doxc .odt? 

There are a lot of alternatives that you can use for transferring your data from phone to your
Ubuntu Laptop.

1st. If your phone has a micro sd card which is very common now you can use some Micro SD USB Card Reader. Its Plug n Play.
2nd. If your Laptop has a Bluetooth and its working properly you can use this wireless transfer via Bluetooth from your phone to your Ubuntu Laptop
and you can use Blue Manager for more easy way to transfer your data from phone to your Ubuntu Laptop.
3rd. via Internet, you can transfer your data from your phone by uploading it from your phone to Internet/Cloud then download it to your Ubuntu laptop from Internet/Cloud.
The Upload and Download scenario scheme.

Actually their are a lot of them but that's the best and easy way I can suggest and its basic.

Good Luck.
