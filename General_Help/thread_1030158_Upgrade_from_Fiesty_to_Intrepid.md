### Post by Elfy on 2009-01-04
Not sure that you'll be able to upgrade from feisty now that it's out of support. On top of that you would be needing to upgrade 3 times to get to 8.10.

I don't know too much about kubuntu but I do know that the biggest change is from kde3 to kde4.

You might be better off backing up data from your feisty install, which should be in your /home and doing a clean install - perhaps go to 8.04 HArdy which is LTS as you appear to like to leave well aloneonce it's working and just upgrade to LTS versions as they appear.

If you have a seperate /home then you can have that mounted and unformatted by the installer which will preserve your /home.
