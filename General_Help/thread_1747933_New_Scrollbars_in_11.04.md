I don't know if you've been as shocked as i was when using 11.04, but i found the new "overlay scrollbars" completely useless. 

I mean please--forgive me for ranting a for few lines--what is the logic in hovering in one place to make an invisible scrollbar appear on the right-hand side of the mouse pointer and then move to that scrollbar and use it (if you're lucky enough the scrollbar doesn't disappear as you move towards it)? I've been struggling with that thing for 10 minutes and then i had enough already.

New things should not be implemented for the sake of being new or different. In my opinion, if something doesn't really improve the OS or desktop environment, then it's not worth making it default.

Luckily for those who don't like it, it's easy to remove--thank you so much for that.

I don't know what you guys think of it.
