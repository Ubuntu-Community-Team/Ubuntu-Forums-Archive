### Post by danwood76 on 2008-06-24
You wont be able to undo an update once you have done it.

What you can do is a full backup using the tar method (search the forums) which will allow you to completely restore your system to its current state.
That way you can try doing an upgrade and if it breaks anything just restore your backup.
