### Post by 3rdalbum on 2009-10-07
Ubuntu, like Windows, does not come with DVD decryption support out-of-the-box.

Also, Ubuntu does not come with MPEG-4 codecs as they are patented.

Go to [www.medibuntu.org](www.medibuntu.org) and use the "repository howto", and then install the "non-free-codecs" and "libdvdcss2" packages from Synaptic Package Manager. Also, Totem doesn't usually want to play DVDs, so you should use VLC as your DVD player.
