### Post by falconindy on 2009-09-07
Your own home folder. You can get to it from a terminal by doing any of the following

cd
cd ~
cd $HOME
cd /home/username

From Gnome, just go to 'Places > Home Folder'. You'll need to press ctrl-H to see hidden files though (anything starting with a dot).
