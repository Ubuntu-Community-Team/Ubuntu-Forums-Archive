When you manually modified your screen and graphics card, did you restart your system (or restart the X server) to see if the changes were accepted? From your description, I surmise you used the "Screens and Graphics" app to do what you did. If not, from terminal```
gksudo displayconfig-gtk
```find your monitor (if listed) and at the graphics tab look for your graphics card and make sure the correct card is being identified. Then apply the changes, restart.
