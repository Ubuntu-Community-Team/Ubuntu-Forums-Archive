### Post by GNUmonkey on 2005-01-16
Do you mean a bunch of files with extensions .r00, .r01, .r02 etc that you want combining back into a whole? If thats what you're looking for, install unrar from the multiverse, and in the terminal type 

```
unrar e name_of_file.rar
```

and it'll combine all the portions automagically for you

If i've misunderstood the question, feel free to ignore me  :mrgreen:
