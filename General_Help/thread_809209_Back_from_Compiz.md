### Post by Kevbert on 2008-05-27
The main supported cards are made by nVidia and ATI. you can check to see it your PC is compatable by using Compiz Check: [http://forlong.blogage.de/article/pages/Compiz-Check]("http://forlong.blogage.de/article/pages/Compiz-Check")
You can turn it on/off with Compiz switch
[http://forlong.blogage.de/article/pages/Compiz-Switch]("http://forlong.blogage.de/article/pages/Compiz-Switch")
It won't affect your video driver.

If you want to set up the desktop cube, for example, you can find the settings here: [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?p=5033243#post5033243]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?p=5033243#post5033243")
