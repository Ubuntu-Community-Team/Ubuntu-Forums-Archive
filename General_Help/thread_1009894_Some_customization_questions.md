Ok, blame me, i just found how to:

-remove the black arrow
([http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=991923&highlight=black+arrow](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=991923&highlight=black+arrow))

-edit the dividers
(1) Open /home/your-name/.themes/Human 2/gtk-2.0/Panel 
You will see your images for the divider.. simple edit them using gimp to transparent and save them
(2) Relogin and you should be done :)
