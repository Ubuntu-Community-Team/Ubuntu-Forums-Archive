### Post by fishyf on 2009-08-25
Start up Nautilus and select Bookmarks from the menu. Edit Bookmarks will do what you want.

You can start nautilus by going to places and Home Folder (but you probably knew that)

Edit:
I just realised this doesn't answer your question. And I don't know how to answer your question, but if you check this link: [http://bugzilla.gnome.org/show_bug.cgi?id=325476](http://bugzilla.gnome.org/show_bug.cgi?id=325476) there is an interesting comment:
" can disable their appearance by unsetting the volumes_visible option in
gconf, but then my 'truly hotplugged' filesystems don't show up either.""
