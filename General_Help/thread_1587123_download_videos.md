### Post by polardude1983 on 2010-10-03
To download videos is very easy. Wait for the whole movie to load

Then click on Places on the top of your screen, click Home Folder

Click on File System when the window pops up, then click on your TMP Folder

It will be right there, you will see a file that says something like FlashXXDkgy3i Just right click and hit rename to FlashXXDkgy3i.flv

Then copy it and paste it somewhere like your desktop or wherever you want
