### Post by Anduu on 2006-10-10
I'll use myself as an example so you can get some sort of idea what to expect.

I have been running this install of Dapper for almost 6 months now.Acording to Synaptic I have just over 1900 packages installed.
I store all my personal stuff ie movies,mp3s,photos,wallpapers yada yadda on a seperate hard drive so don't forget to factor this kind of stuff in for your space situation...

Currently my root partition is taking up about 10 Gig.

Home is now about 23 Gig.Now I should mention that I use the electricsheep screensaver and have 4.5 gig of sheep in /home plus around 3.5 Gig of stuff in my Documents folder so technically you could subtract almost 10 Gig from this number.There are also some things that got installrd into my / partition that I should have installed to /home but didn't know any better at the time :p

So basically I am looking at 10 Gig for /(...should be a gig or two less...heh) and 13 Gig for /home(...don't forget I don't store anything here...).That is on a 6 month old install with a bunch of extra stuff from the repos installed.

I will leave you to draw your own conclusions from this.
