### Post by iaculallad on 2008-06-27
Dwhelper is from your Firefox's extension "Download Helper". Open your Firefox, navigate Tools->Add-ons, select "DownloadHelper" and click the "Uninstall" button then try to remove your dwhelper from your /home directory.
