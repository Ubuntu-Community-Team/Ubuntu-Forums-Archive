### Post by 2hot6ft2 on 2010-05-11
Right click on the top panel and select Add to Panel.
Select Indicator Applet Session
Click Add then Close

To move it back to the right corner you'll have to right click on the other items that are there like the clock and clear the Lock to Panel box then right click and select Move.
drag them out of the way and do the same thing to move the indicator applet back over there.
Once you have them where you want them right click on each and check the Lock to Panel box again.
