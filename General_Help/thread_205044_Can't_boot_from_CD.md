### Post by Dr. Nick on 2006-06-27
I would double check your bios settings, then check the physical connections to your cdrom, If all that checks out Id look for another cdrom drive to try it on. Either try to boot it in another computer, or get another drive for your computer

Sometimes the drives get very picky and only work when they feel like it. If the drive is getting old this may just be its warning to you that it wont last forever.
