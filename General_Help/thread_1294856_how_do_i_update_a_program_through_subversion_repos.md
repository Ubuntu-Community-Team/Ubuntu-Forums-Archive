### Post by white-zilla on 2009-10-20
okay, I think i figured it out myself. I needed to first install svn client from the packet manager.  Then i punched in the URL that was on the wallpapoz website for the subversion (svn checkout [http://opensvn.csie.org/wallpapoz/trunk](http://opensvn.csie.org/wallpapoz/trunk) wallpapoz-trunk) and the file updated.  It didn't fix the non-random wallpaper glitch, but I think i at least understand this whole svn thing.
