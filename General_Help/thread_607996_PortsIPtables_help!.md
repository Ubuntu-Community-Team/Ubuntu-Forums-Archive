### Post by trak87 on 2007-11-09
If you didn't install and configure a firewall (like Firestarter), then you don't need to open a port on your computer. You need to access the router by getting its proper address (like 192.168.1.100 or similar). Look in the router's manual or search the net. When you access it via your web browser, look for the port forwarding options. Newer routers use UPnP and so you may not even need to port forward in that case.
