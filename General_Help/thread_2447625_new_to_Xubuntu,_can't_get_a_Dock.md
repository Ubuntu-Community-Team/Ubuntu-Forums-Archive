### Post by Holger_Gehrke on 2020-07-22
Yes, there is -- kind of. You can have multiple panels. Right-click on the panel at the top, select 'panel', 'panel settings'. In the form shown next there's a green '+' icon which allows you to add more panels to your desktop. You can put those panels anywhere you like, either free-floating or at the edges. You can put starters, dock-applets and xfce-panel-plugins on any of the panels.
