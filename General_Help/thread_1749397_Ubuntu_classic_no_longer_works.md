---

### Post by cottfcfan on 2011-05-05
Solved my own problem.
Didn't realize the 1st time you boot into Unity, the unity plugin in ccsm enables itself.
This has to be disabled before you log back into Ubuntu Classic, otherwise you get a mixture of the two.
Never stop learning with Linux.
