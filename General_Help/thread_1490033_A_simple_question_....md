### Post by Nythain on 2010-05-21
the source? naaa, just about anywhere. It will place the binaries and configs where they need to be when you make install the program.

i recommend creating a folder in ~, something like ~/source, then just unpack into a subdirectory in there with the apps name, like ~/source/program
