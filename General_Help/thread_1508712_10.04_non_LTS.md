### Post by Sef on 2010-06-13
> Having started full time with Ubuntu 9.10, I was wondering when 10.04 Non LTS will be released?

10.04 is an LTS only.

> How are the upgrades done in Ubuntu?

Every 6 months a new upgrade is released.  You have the option of upgrading or not.  NonLTS updates are released for 18 months for the desktop and server.  LTS updates are released for 3 years for the desktop and 5 years server.
