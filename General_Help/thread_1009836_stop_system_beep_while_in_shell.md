### Post by graysky on 2008-12-13
How can I stop the annoying system beep that gets played ever time I use the TAB while in a shell?

---

### Post by hikaricore on 2008-12-13
Assuming you're using gnome, right click in the terminal window and choose Profiles/Preferences.
Untick the box that says "Terminal Bell" and click the close button in the preferences window.
