### Post by zeroseven0183 on 2011-05-28
Hi drakejacob and thank you for asking. I'm pointing you to a couple of links where you can find how to report a problem in Ubuntu:

First one is [http://www.ubuntu.com/community/report-problem](http://www.ubuntu.com/community/report-problem). The content is barely general -- when to file or not file a bug report.

Second is [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/ReportingBugs](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/ReportingBugs). The page will walk you through sending a report via Launchpad and more.

By the way, if you still don't have a Launchpad account, please create one. [Launchpad]("https://launchpad.net") will be your gateway to reporting bugs, communicating with other users and developers and more.

And if you need real-time help, you can reach hundreds of people in the Ubuntu community by logging in to IRC and going to channels like #ubuntu and #ubuntu-bugs.

I hope those suggestions help. Happy computing!
