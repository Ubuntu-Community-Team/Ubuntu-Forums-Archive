### Post by 3rdalbum on 2011-10-08
There should be unlocked phones available.

The Huawei Ideos U8150 is advertised on eBay Australia for $89 unlocked, this should be capable of wifi tethering. That's still about 56 pounds, but at least it's unlocked. Oh, it'll also do USB tethering out-of-the-box. You'll just need to set up the phone for your data connection (so it knows how to access the internet).

Once USB tethering is turned on and you have a data connection, Network Manager will automatically connect.
