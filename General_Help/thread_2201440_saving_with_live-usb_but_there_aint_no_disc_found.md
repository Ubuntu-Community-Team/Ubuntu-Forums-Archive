### Post by Dave_L on 2014-01-24
Dropping the netbook may have loosened a cable, or it may have damaged the disk.  Try cycling power and going into the BIOS settings; see if the hard disk shows up there.  If not, the netbook probably needs to be opened and examined by someone qualified to do that.
