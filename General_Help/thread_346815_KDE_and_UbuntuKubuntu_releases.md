### Post by cmost on 2007-01-26
Ubuntu Dapper is a "LTS" or long term support edition.  It's intended for businesses or home users who value stability and reliability over cutting edge features.  Because of this, new and updated packages won't be placed into the dapper repos right away (if at all.)  If at some point in the future, a package is considered stable, it may be placed in the backports repo.  My advice is if you want cutting edge packages, upgrade to the latest Ubuntu release.  On the other hand, many new features will be stabilized in time for the next LTS edition of Ubuntu if you care to wait for its release.
