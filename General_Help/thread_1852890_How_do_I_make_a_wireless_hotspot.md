### Post by kurt18947 on 2011-10-01
Your thread is a little light on details:)  Do you have a wireless router or wireless access point (WAP)?  If so, what brand/model?  I don't know a way to get wireless connectivity without one or the other.  If you do have one or the other, do you have DHCP enabled on your router/WAP?  DHCP is what assigns I.P.  addresses unless you do it manually.  Are you using encryption?  If so, you might turn it off for a few minutes and see if you can connect.
