### Post by ThrobbingBrain66 on 2007-08-29
1) Download whichever GDM theme you like

2) Go to System > Admin > Login Window

3) Under the 'Local' tab, choose Add... and navigate to your theme

4) Make sure your new theme is selected in the theme window and log out.
