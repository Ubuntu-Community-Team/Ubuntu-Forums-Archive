### Post by robobot on 2009-07-15
You could try updating your video drivers, but GW just doesn't run very well in Wine. I tried it out on Wine a couple weeks back, and it had a slow framerate and texture corruption. Add to that the fact that the game servers are a little laggy to begin with, and the game becomes marginally playable at best. You're better off running it natively in Windows. It might work on a virtual machine, but I haven't tried it.
