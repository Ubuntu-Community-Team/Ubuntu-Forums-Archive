### Post by 3rdalbum on 2009-09-09
> **loosie said:**
> Hi, I have 'computer Janitor' but am wondering if I should have anything else, or there's something better for keeping my machine clean & 'tuned'?

Generally, your Linux machine doesn't need much in the way of tuning. So yes, Computer Janitor is the first such program.

> The other question is, is there a good program for searching for/finding anything & everything on your machine? I have Tracker Search Tool, but I still can't seem to find stuff such as samba with it - comes up as no file with that name. Perhaps it only searches files not programs?

That's right, Tracker is designed to help you find your files, not the programs on your system. Samba isn't a program that you launch to perform a certain task, it is called upon by your desktop environment (Gnome) whenever you go to Places > Network. The results from running Samba will be formatted and displayed in Gnome.
