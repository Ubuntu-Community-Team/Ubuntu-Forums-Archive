### Post by jesuisbenjamin on 2011-04-28
OK i found out a solution [on this page]("http://www.google.co.uk/support/forum/p/Chrome/thread?tid=6be29118206214ff&hl=en").

This happened after installing the VeeHD plugin for Firefox. According to the thread mentioned above, there should be an option in preferences, but i could not get into preferences so i uninstalled the plug-in instead.

The plug-in seems to lead to a [www.yeddo.net](www.yeddo.net) page redirecting to the [http://www.browsersearch.org/](http://www.browsersearch.org/)

I am now relieve there is still not serious threat on Ubuntu. Note to Ubuntu developers: Perhaps Ubuntu should take over the distribution of plug-ins for Firefox so they can be tested by the community and prevent malicious plug-ins to infest computers.
