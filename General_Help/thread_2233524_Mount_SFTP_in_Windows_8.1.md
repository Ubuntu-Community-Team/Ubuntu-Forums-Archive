### Post by yann-huruguen on 2014-07-22
Hello,

The best/simple solution I know is **Expandrive** but it isn't free, on the other hand it also allows to add online storage provider and is very easy to use...

Personally I use **SFTP Net Drive Free** which is less convenient but does the job for mounting SFTP drive in Windows.
I don't face your problem with using files and the speed access is acceptable for me.
Do you configure it to send keep alive feature to prevent problem with "long" period of inactivity of the connection? It helps me for such issues...
