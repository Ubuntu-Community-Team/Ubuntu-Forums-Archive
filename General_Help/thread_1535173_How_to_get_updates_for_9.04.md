### Post by kaelspencer on 2010-07-20
I'm not sure you can go back from 10.04 to 9.04. Someone else would have to speak to that.

But when you have 9.04 and Update Manager comes up, you can just update like normal without doing a version upgrade. I haven't seen one for a while, but as I recall the version upgrade button is towards the top of Update Manager window, while the button to do regular updates is at the bottom, below the list of updates.
