### Post by Jon Monreal on 2012-03-09
[This site]("http://tnid.us/") appears to work just fine to determine the carrier. [This page]("http://www.emailtextmessages.com/") lists the email addresses used by the carriers. You just have to enter the whole number including the area code before the @.

Any email address, including webmail ones like Yahoo, will work.
