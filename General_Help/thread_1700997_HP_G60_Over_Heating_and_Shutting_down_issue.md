### Post by dave01945 on 2011-08-25
i have the same problem from what i read it is a common fault with this model the heat sink get clogged with dust and needs removing to clear the dust 

also after removing the heat sink and cleaning you **must** clean the old thermal paste from the cpu and gpu then re-apply new thermal paste or it will get worse

some people have suggested adding some extra ventilation holes because the standard one don't allow much airflow i haven't done this to mine **yet** but the claim it helps significantly

there are many posts on google about this this is just one video of how to clean it but there are many more
