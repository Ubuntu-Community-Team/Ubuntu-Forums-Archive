### Post by lcruz007 on 2008-11-16
By default Ubuntu comes with 2 desktops. It's very useful when you have plenty of windows opened. Sometimes the two desktops aren't enough though. :p
Can I have more than two desktops? If so, how can I do so?


Thanks!

---

### Post by oldos2er on 2008-11-16
Right-click on the panel where the two desktops are showing, click Preferences. Increase 'Columns' to increase the number of workspaces.
