### Post by lhtown on 2007-03-01
To be honest, I am not exactly sure what is on the disk. I suspect it is mostly documentation and perhaps some utilities to tweak you motherboard more easily.

You don't need any of it, and you won't have any problem from not using it.

Come to think of it, I think I got one with my Asus motherboard, but I never opened it.

The only problem you might run into is if you need to flash update the BIOS. A lot of motherboards for home computers will only do it through Windows. On the bright side, it is almost never critical to flash update your BIOS.
