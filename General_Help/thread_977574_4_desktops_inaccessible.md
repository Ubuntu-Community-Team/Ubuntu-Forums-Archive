I wanted more than two desktops (not that I need more, but I wanted a full cube instead of just a two-sided plate that I could spin).

So I sought and sought and finally found Compiz' setup. There, in general options > desktop size, I increased the vertical size to 2 and left the horizontal at 2.

I think that 2x2 is four, but not according to Compiz:

- Number of desktops in the same Compiz Setup is 1 and is greyed out. I cannot change it to four or something.

- In my bottom bar, I see 4 desktops, but I can only click on both topmost desktops.

- With F10 (Exposé) I can access all four

- The "Cube" is just a two sided plate with both desktops on either side of it, no bigger 3d shape.


Where did I go wrong?

### Post by ChadMMc on 2008-11-10
Hi, 
Try having 4 horizontal virtual desktops with 1 vertical (the cube uses one row at a time it seems)
compiz config -- *General -> Desktop Size*
