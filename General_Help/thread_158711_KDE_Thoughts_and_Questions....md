2 - open system settings>appearance>style>effects tab - open the drop down menu for "menu effect" and select "make translucent". You can then adjust translucency in the bottom section of the window. You may need to also mess about a bit with the text colour if you select completely transparent to stop the menu text disspearing into your background colour.
