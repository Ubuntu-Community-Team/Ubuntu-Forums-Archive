### Post by FuturePilot on 2008-05-10
No. XGL does not play nice with Nvidia cards. You used to need it a long time ago if you wanted Compiz. But the Nvidia drivers have matured enough now that Compiz can run without XGL.

If you have slow choppy window movement, have you installed the drivers from System&#8594;Administration&#8594;Hardware Drivers?
