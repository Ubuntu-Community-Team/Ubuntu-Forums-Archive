### Post by tommynz1975 on 2009-03-14
The main thing is, DON'T PANIC. you have vista booting. The common vista complication is that vista won't boot.

Your HDD most likely will still be their.
some of your missing space will be the recovery partition.
Vista is not in a position to be able to read linux formatted drives, this is a good thing.


Generally you resize your partition from within vista.
here is a site called vista rewired and has such instructions

[HTML]http://www.vistarewired.com/200C7/02/16/how-to-resize-a-partition-in-windows-vista[/HTML]

The basic instructions are.
[LIST=1]
[*]Click on Start Menu

[*]Right click on Computer and click on Manager

[*]You may be prompted with a box asking if you really want to go into Manager.

[*]In the left pane, open up the Storage category and click on Disk Management

[*]Your intire HDD should be displayed. Right click on the drive you would like to work-on resize
[/LIST]
