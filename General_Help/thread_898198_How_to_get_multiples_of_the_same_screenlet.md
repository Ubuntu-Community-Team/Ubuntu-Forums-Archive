### Post by compnut41 on 2008-08-23
Right click on the bottom panel, then select add to panel. You should see Clock on the list of options.

---

### Post by damis648 on 2008-08-23
> **compnut41 said:**
> Right click on the bottom panel, then select add to panel. You should see Clock on the list of options.

The OP is referring to screenlets, not panel applets. OP, in the screenlets manager, just double click the screenlet as many times as you want the screenlet on your desktop. Then right click each one and head to Properties and change the time info.
