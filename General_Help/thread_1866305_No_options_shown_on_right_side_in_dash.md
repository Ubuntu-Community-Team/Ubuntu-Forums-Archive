### Post by grahammechanical on 2011-10-21
I think I see what you mean.

Click on the second icon from the left at the bottom of the dash. It looks like a ruler, pencil and a pen standing on their ends. This is a lens for finding applications. Then, click on the words Filter Results and you will get the list on the right that you talk about. You might need also to click the Rating of All to get icons to appear.
