### Post by kpkeerthi on 2009-05-15
**Device0** appears to be the active device. You need to add NoLogo to **Device0**
```

Section "Device"
Identifier "Device0"
Driver "nvidia"
[COLOR="Red"]**Option "NoLogo"**[/COLOR]
VendorName "NVIDIA Corporation"
BoardName "GeForce 8200M G"
EndSection

```
