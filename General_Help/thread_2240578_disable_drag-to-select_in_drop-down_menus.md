### Post by grahammechanical on 2014-08-20
Does not happen with me. But then I keep the mouse button depressed. The selection is only recognised when I stop pressing the mouse button. I can slide the mouse up and down the offered choices without experiencing what you say is happening.

This design allows one click action and not two clicks. Old design = One click to open the menu and a second click to make our selection. Instead we have one click and hold the mouse button down to open the menu and keep it open, move the mouse to the desired menu item and release the mouse button - action accepted. It will be brilliant on a touch screen device.
