### Post by radddi on 2010-06-13
Sounds like you have some display/driver issues - a fairly common problem. :) When you reach the blank screen hit "CTRL+ALT+F1", does anything happen? This should bring you to a terminal window. If it doesn't - then choose the second option from your GRUB boot menu - Recovery Mode. Then follow the this post:

[http://www.techsupportforum.com/alternative-computing/linux-support/107839-ubuntu-loads-then-goes-blank-screen-bahhh.html#post572469](http://www.techsupportforum.com/alternative-computing/linux-support/107839-ubuntu-loads-then-goes-blank-screen-bahhh.html#post572469)

Best of luck to you and PLEASE do post feedback if you're successful. :)
