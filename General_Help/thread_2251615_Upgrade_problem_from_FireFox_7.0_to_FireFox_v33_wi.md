### Post by ajgreeny on 2014-11-05
You say it is on a USB; is it a proper installation or just a live system?
You can not update applications on any live system, and you can not update any versions of Ubuntu apart from 10.04 (server only), 12.04, 14.04 and 14.10.

The repos for all others are now EOL and not available without a lot of palaver, which is a waste of time anyway, as they are not themselves updated any more.
