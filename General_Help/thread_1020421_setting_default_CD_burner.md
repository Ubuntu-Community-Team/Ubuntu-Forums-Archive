### Post by mc4man on 2008-12-24
In nautilus (home folder) go edit -> preferences -> media. Look in the drop down in 'Other media'

You can also enable 'file management' in system -> preferences. (same thing
To do right click on Applications -> edit menus, scroll down and highlight preferences and enable file management in right side column.
