### Post by Genius314 on 2008-09-08
If you right click just to the left of the "application tabs" (I usually call them buttons), a menu should come up with a "Preferences" option. Click on it, and you can change "Window list content" from "all workspaces" to just "current workspace."

Hope that helps.
