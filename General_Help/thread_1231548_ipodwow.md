### Post by Chronon on 2009-08-04
If they are .m4p it means they are DRM protected (from the iTunes store) and can't be played except on the iPod or through an authorized iTunes.

If you purchase iTunes-plus or from Amazon the music won't be encumbered by DRM and you will be free to listen to it on your computer or any other portable devices you may wish to use.
