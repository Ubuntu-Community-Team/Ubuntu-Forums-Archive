### Post by kevstar31 on 2007-08-22
i386 should work for you. The reason gentoo makes a separate build for i686 is because [nptl]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Native_POSIX_Thread_Library") does not work with i386 and other arch specific optimizations.
