### Post by pbrane on 2010-09-11
There is a GNOME applet that will control the CPU speed called **CPU Frequency Scaling Monitor** you can add to the GNOME Panel. There is also the **Power Manager Inhibit Applet** in GNOME. There may be something similar in KDE.
