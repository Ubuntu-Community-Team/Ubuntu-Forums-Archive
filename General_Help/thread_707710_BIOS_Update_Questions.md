### Post by Existentialist on 2008-02-25
Updating BIOS is a pretty safe thing, as long as the computer isn't powered off during the flashing.  Reading the change logs from the versions above 13, I would say there are significant changes that make it worth applying.  Many times, if the updates are just support for new products or some other feature that is not important to me, I just skip the updates.
