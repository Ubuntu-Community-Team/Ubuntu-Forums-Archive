### Post by WorMzy on 2010-05-07
Does it copy to the clipboard (e.g. Ctrl+V to paste) or to the primary buffer (e.g. Middle mouse button to paste)? The normal behaviour is to copy any selected text to the primary buffer, only deliberately copied text should be stored in the clipboard.
