### Post by jesse c on 2010-03-22
My computer is recently asking for my 'default keyring password'to get access to the internet.It never used to do this as I am single user and never created a keyring password as far as I know.I dont even use a login password.Luckily i tried my administrator password and that unlocks my keyring but i would like to get it back to no password or auto unlock mode.Any suggestions?

---

### Post by cogier on 2010-03-22
Right click on the "Network Connection" icon, select "Edit Connections", select the "Wireless" tab. Click on your connection and select the "Edit" button. At the bottom left of the screen make sure the "Available to all users" is checked.

That should do it.
