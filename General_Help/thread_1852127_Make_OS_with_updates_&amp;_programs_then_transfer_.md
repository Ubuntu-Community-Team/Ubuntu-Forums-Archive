### Post by WasMeHere on 2011-09-29
I think it would be easier to *carry your computer to the internet* and do the first installation on line even if it is a desktop.

Later on *for new packages*, you may download them, bring them home on a USB key drive and *install them offline* as suggested by *foureyesboy*.

As long as the computer is off-line, you need no security updates.
