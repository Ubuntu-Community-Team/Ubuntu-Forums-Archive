### Post by karuptdata on 2008-05-04
Hi Rabbitnightmare, Can you please tell us what your system config is as far as video card and monitor? Another thing you may want to try. 

1) Right Click on Menu Bar in Gnome and select "Edit Menus"
2) The main menu dialogue should appear. Select other under the Menus window (on the left)
3) In the Items menu (on the right) Check Screen and Graphics then select close.
4) Go to Menu->Other->Screen and Graphics-> And search for your proper monitor and screen resolution.

this should take care of the issue for you however if it does not post back with your configuration details and well see if we cant get this sorted for you..
