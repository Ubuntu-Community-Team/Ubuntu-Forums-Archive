### Post by Therion on 2009-03-04
Right-click on the panel you want to see your applications minimize TO (e.g. top or bottom).

Click on "Add to Panel".

Scroll down to to "*Application Chooser (Use Buttons)".

Drag it to the panel and move it around to where you want it.

More info on working with your Gnome Panels:  [https://help.ubuntu.com/7.04/user-guide/C/gospanel-8.html](https://help.ubuntu.com/7.04/user-guide/C/gospanel-8.html)





*It's something like that... You'll know it when you see it.
