### Post by ShodanjoDM on 2008-03-09
Do you mean icons like "user icons"?

If so, try checking the gdm themes installed by default:

Administration>>Login Window

Then click the "Local" tab. There should be a selection of gdm login themes. Some of them has "face browser" (user customizable icons/pictures).

If you want more you can take a look at [gnome-look]("http://gnome-look.org/index.php?xcontentmode=150&PHPSESSID=aa73058d3289afe11398e5b2d8926e18")
