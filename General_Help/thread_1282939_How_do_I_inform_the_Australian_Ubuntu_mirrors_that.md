### Post by dcstar on 2009-10-06
> **damagu said:**
> I installed Karmic yesterday and was not receiving updates. I found that it was a problem with the Australian mirror. How do I inform whoever it is I need to inform that there is a problem?

You don't, you wait until all the mirror servers have the new releases sent to them.

People jumping onto the main server only slow down the whole process by further loading the same servers that are trying to update many other servers around the planet.
