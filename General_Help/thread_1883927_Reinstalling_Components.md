It is disappointing to learn that you haven't resolved your networking issue after a week's pain.

If reinstalling Ubuntu is an option for you then I suggest you reinstall Ubuntu, fresh and clean Install. It will be lot easier and uncomplicated than re-installing Network packages. There will be a good chance that some hidden configuration file somewhere in the system will not be purged and it will keep causing problems even after you have religiously purged your system of all the Networking Packages. 

IMO it is waste of time (unless you want to learn fixing your Networking). Back up your important Data and reinstall Ubuntu.
