### Post by Minipalmer on 2008-09-03
Hey there

These are called "Panels." When you removed it, you probably right clicked and selected "remove panel."

By the same way, you can make a new one by right clicking on an existing one (the one on the top) and clicking "new panel." You can then orient what is on it, and where it goes.

Right click your new panel and select "add to panel" to select an app that will allow it to show your running programs and windows.

### Post by Genius314 on 2008-09-03
Do you still have the panel at the top of the screen? If so, just right click on it, and click "New Panel."
Then drag it to the bottom.
Right click, "Add to panel," and select Window List.


If you don't have any panels at all, you'd have to reset the panel settings to default. I forget how to do this, but I think it requires deleting a config file in your home folder somewhere.
