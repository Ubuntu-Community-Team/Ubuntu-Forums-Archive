### Post by jeremy on 2007-01-06
It means that the players will be mountable, and that you will be able to tranfer files to and from tham (as if they were external HD's), however, the supplied windows programmes (on a CD) will not work.

### Post by Craig Caldwell on 2007-01-06
That is one sweet player. I have one that is kind of the same, the iriver t10.
It is a drag and drop player and works so good with my linux box. The best part is that you can get files off of any pc.
