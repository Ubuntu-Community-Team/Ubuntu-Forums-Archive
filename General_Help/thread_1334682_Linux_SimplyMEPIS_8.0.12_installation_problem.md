### Post by XubuRoxMySox on 2009-11-22
Mepis is very cool, rock-stable KDE! Great for newbies... I didn't have that problem when I installed it on a test machine, but I can only suggest two things:

1.) It might be a "bad burn" on the CD. It happens sometimes... can be avoided most of the time by double checking the MD5 sum and burning at the slowest possible speed.

2.) Mepis has a wonderful, very supportive community of folks that are very welcoming of newbies. Try posing the question at [http://medpislovers.org](http://medpislovers.org)
