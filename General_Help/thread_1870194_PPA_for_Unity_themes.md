### Post by Frogs Hair on 2011-10-26
First install the Gnome Tweak Tool / Advanced Settings from the software center for changing themes and other settings . Elegant Brit , Elements , and Evolve  will  work with Unity . [http://www.webupd8.org/2011/10/4-beautiful-gnome-32-compatible-gtk.html](http://www.webupd8.org/2011/10/4-beautiful-gnome-32-compatible-gtk.html)

(Downloaded Themes) 

(Single User) Create a .themes and .icons folder in home / hidden folders . (All Users) Use   gksudo nautilus > File System / usr / share / themes or icons .

Download , extract the packages , and place the folders in either location making theme selections using Advanced Settings .  Logout - in may be required  and there are more themes here . [http://gnome-look.org/index.php?xcontentmode=167](http://gnome-look.org/index.php?xcontentmode=167)

Some themes at Gnome Look are shell only .
