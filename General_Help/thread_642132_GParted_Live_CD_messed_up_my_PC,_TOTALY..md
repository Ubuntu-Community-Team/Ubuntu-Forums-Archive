### Post by PmDematagoda on 2007-12-16
Gparted cannot be the cause of your problem since no matter how damaged your Hard Drives are your PC should be able to boot up to the BIOS at least, if not then it is a hardware issue like the RAM. Whatever you do as long as they are not hardware related does not cause your PC to not to boot, unless you mess up something like flashing the BIOS;).
