### Post by Tamlynmac on 2009-07-28
I'm not sure, but I think your asking to remove mounted volumes icons from your desktop. If that's what you want:

open Gnome Configuration Editor and uncheck Volumes_visible.

System Tools/Configuration Editor/Apps/nautilus/desktop/Uncheck the box to the right of volumes_visible

Close the Configuration Editor.

Hope this is what your looking for and that it helps.
