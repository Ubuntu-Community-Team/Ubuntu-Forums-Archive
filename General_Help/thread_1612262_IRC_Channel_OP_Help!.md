### Post by Thingymebob on 2010-11-06
```

/msg ChanServ OP #channel nickname

```

You should have a read of [http://freenode.net/channel_guidelines.shtml]("http://freenode.net/channel_guidelines.shtml")

which says:
> 
Don't keep channel operator privileges. Displaying these privileges on your nick with a "+o" attracts participants who are interested in gaining them and using them actively; it also attracts the attention of participants who react negatively to authority. Have your nick added to the channel access list and op yourself only when needed. 


and [http://freenode.net/using_the_network.shtml]("http://freenode.net/using_the_network.shtml")
> 
We strongly suggest that you avoid configuring your channel to "auto-op". Use the chanserv "op" command to obtain channel operator status only when needed. This will help to keep your channel temperature low and reduce conflicts.

So just op yourself when you need it
