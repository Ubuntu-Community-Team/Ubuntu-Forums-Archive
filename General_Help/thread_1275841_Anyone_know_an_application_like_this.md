### Post by mediax on 2009-09-26
Some time ago, I saw a program which allowed you to "type" by using the mouse only, and I'm wondering if there's anything similar available for Ubuntu.

I can't remember where I saw it, or even what it was running on, but it basically prompted you with the alphabet; when you hovered over any letter you were offered a list of valid next letters based on the program's dictionary (the area occupied by each letter was proportionate to the number of words in which that letter occurred); you selected the next letter by moving your cursor over the appropriate letter and the process repeated itself until you'd completed your word.  You basically formed words by moving your cursor from left to right across the screen.

The way I've explained it makes it sound very clumsy, but it was actually a very elegant and surprisingly quick way of forming words.

Does anyone recognise this sort of input?  And is there anything similar available for Linux?
