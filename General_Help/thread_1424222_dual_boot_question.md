### Post by Pascal11110 on 2010-03-07
You won't hurt anything if you mount the drives, as long as you don't force shutdown while they are mounted, and even then the chances are very thin. So I would suggest just suggest mount all of them and browse each until you find the one with the files you want. The one that has your windows files will have things such as folders named Users, Windows, Boot, in the the starting directory so you will know it is that one.
