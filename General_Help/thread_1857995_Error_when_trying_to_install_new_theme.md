### Post by 3rdalbum on 2011-10-11
Login screen themes do not work, full-stop.

There are different components that make up the theming on Ubuntu:

GTK - controls theme
Metacity - window borders theme
Icon - as you'd expect
Complete themes - contain GTK and Metacity and sometimes Icon themes.

When you download one of these sorts of themes, leave it in the .tar.gz and drag it onto the Appearance window. If it's a Complete theme, it should be listed right there for you to click on. If it's a GTK or Metacity theme you will need to click on the "Customise" button at the bottom of the window and then the GTK theme will be listed under the "Controls" tab, and the Metacity theme will be listed under the "Window border" tab.
