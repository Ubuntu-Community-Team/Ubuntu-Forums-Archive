### Post by sandwormblues on 2009-08-04
I've got an Intel ICH7 card and i've always had to do a little hacky-hacky to get my sound working.  

in the terminal:
```
sudo nano /etc/modprobe.d/alsa-base
```

and then add the following line to the bottom:

[INDENT]options snd-hda-intel model=3stack[/INDENT]

you could also try other values for "model", like 5stack or just auto. Hopefully that doesn't break anything, and if it does, just delete the line.


as far as your video goes, could you please copy and post the output of the following terminal commands:

```
glxinfo|grep direct
```

and also:

```
lspci|grep Graphics
```


This will furnish us with the information required to help you.
