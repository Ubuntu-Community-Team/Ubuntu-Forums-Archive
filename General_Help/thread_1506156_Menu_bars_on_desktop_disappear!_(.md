### Post by Smart Viking on 2010-06-10
Right click on the desktop, then select "Create launcher...", and where it says "command" there you write: "xfce4-panel".

Then you launch the launcher and you will have the panels back, and if it happens again you can just double click the launcher. I hope that helped. :)

Well, it depends. That happened to me yesterday but it have not happened after that(i have booted twice). :)

EDIT: The reason i said to make a launcher is because if you do the command in the terminal, the panels will disappear when you close the terminal, while a launcher behaves differently. :)
