### Post by mozetti on 2006-04-11
I was having a similar problem with my wireless mouse/keyboard combo. My receiver has a USB connection for the keyboard and a PS/2 connection for the mouse.

In my BIOS, I enabled USB legacy support for Mouse & Keyboard - this got them working during the POST/grub/boot.ini stages. HTH.
