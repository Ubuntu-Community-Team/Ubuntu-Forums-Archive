### Post by GerhardK90 on 2012-03-23
Hello I am using KDE and a Razer Deathadder 3500dpi. 
I have installed a razertool which gives me the possibility to setup the profiles on the internal storage in my mouse. 

My problem is, that my mouse is not moving very "precise" When I am close to something clickable it kind of sticks and if i move my mouse slowly the cursor on my screen does not move at all. 
If i would not move my mouse faster i could move it over my whole mouse pad without the cursor being moved for a mm. 

Anyone here has experienced the same or knows whats the problem?

Thank you in advance for your help!!

---

### Post by GerhardK90 on 2012-03-23
> When the mouse travels less than threshold pixels in a short time, it will use the actual device speed and resolution. But when it crosses the threshold, the speed will be multiplied by acceleration.

Setting the treshold to 20px and the acceleration to  1x removed my bug. 

Thank you.
