### Post by Katalog on 2009-07-30
There's a ton of choices for cusor themes. Take a look at _[GNOME-Look.org]("http://www.gnome-look.org/index.php?xcontentmode=36&PHPSESSID=f418da739c92276895db6546d0b4e6cc")_ and you'll find a load of them. All you have to do is download the theme of your choice, unpack it and go to "Preferences>Appearance". Then drag and drop the the theme package into the open Appearance window, and joila, you have a new cursor theme installled.
