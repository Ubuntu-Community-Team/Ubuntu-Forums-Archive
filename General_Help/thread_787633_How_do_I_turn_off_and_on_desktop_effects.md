### Post by girishv on 2008-05-09
Right click on the desktop, go to wallpaper settings, from there select the effect (the last tab) and in that you can select normal. You can similarly select the effects on from the same.

Most of the time, I keep the effects turned off, unless I want to impress somebody for obvious reasons
