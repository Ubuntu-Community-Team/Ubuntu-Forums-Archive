### Post by im_ka on 2004-10-27
i don't use evo, but you can easily associate ooo with the relevant files in nautilus.

right click on the .doc, .xls... whatever file, and choose open with (or smthg like that, i have a german desktop), and browse for the application. you can tell nautilus to remember the chosen app. the ooo starters you need to choose are in the /program folder within your installed ooo folder. oowriter, oocalc, ooimpress, whatever.

hope this helps
