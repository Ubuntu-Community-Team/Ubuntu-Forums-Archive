### Post by connor13 on 2014-10-21
So, yesterday I suddenly started having a sound issue in mint. I tried some fixes i found online when cinnamon completely stopped working and wouldnt start again. I decided at that point to just do a clean ubuntu install and move on, rather than try and troubleshoot my broken mint install.

So, I installed a fresh copy of Ubuntu 14, and I still cant get the sound to work at all. I tried multiple audio devices on both front and rear jacks. I also uninstalled and reinstalled pulse audio and alsamixer with no effect. The only audio output I get is a kind of popping sound periodically from the rear port. I also noticed that if I mute and then unmute my audio anything plugged into the rear port will make a louder and immediate pop, kind of as if it was just turned on, but then no actual sound follows except for more softer popping noises every 5 seconds or so. The problem is exactly the same using the live cd and the actual install from my ssd.

I'm really at a loss after doing a significant amount of googling with no results. Could my onboard sound card have died?

Edit: After playing with it for a couple more hours it seemed to fix itself. I turned off the onboard audio in bios, then booted up ubuntu, then turned the onboard audio back on in bios and it started working.
