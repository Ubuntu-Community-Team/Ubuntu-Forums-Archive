### Post by forestpixie on 2008-06-16
Open firefox - then about:config in address bar

In the filter put - layout.scrollbar.side

Double click and change integer to 3

[http://kb.mozillazine.org/Layout.scrollbar.side](http://kb.mozillazine.org/Layout.scrollbar.side)

### Post by Vivaldi Gloria on 2008-06-16
In about:config find the setting

```
layout.scrollbar.side
```

and make its value 3. Restart firefox.
