### Post by linux_tech on 2008-08-08
Super Grub Disk is a bootable floppy or CDROM that is oriented towards system rescue, specifically for repairing the booting process.   It can activate partitions, boot partitions, boot MBRs.  It can boot your former Operating System by the loading menu.lst from the hard disk.
