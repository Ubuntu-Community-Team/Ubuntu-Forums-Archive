### Post by Serpher on 2010-03-01
Not a whole lot, however I would suggest doing it. Firstly there are a few disadvantages of wubi listed [here](http://ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=2892557&postcount=2). Keep in mind that the post is dated, and disc performances is quite a bit slower than an ext4 partition (3.1 times slower if my sources are correct). The other reason to do this is you would have a full ext4 harddrive that would be easy to manage, with more space.
