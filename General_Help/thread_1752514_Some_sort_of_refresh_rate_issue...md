### Post by DrKnobblez on 2011-05-09
For anyone who was looking at this with the same problem; I fixed this issue by switching the Nvidia driver to type (173). This is the alternate to the recommended. It actually is a lot cooler and gave me this fantastic vertical toolbar. ^_^ All problems happen for a reason.

Goto: Additional Drivers
to make the switch.
