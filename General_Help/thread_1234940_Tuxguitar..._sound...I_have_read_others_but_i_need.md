### Post by georgetom on 2009-10-07
In my 8.04 LTS, I have TuxGuitar 1.0, and Java JRE and Timidity installed through Synaptic.

And in Tux Guitar, in Tools>Settings>Sound, these are the options I tried.

MIDI Sequencer: Real Time Sequencer
MIDI Port: TiMidity port 0 [128:0]

Worked for me. I saw this info in another TuxGuitar page here in ubuntuforums, but I cant find it now! Hope this works for you.
