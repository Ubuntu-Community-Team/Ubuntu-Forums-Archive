### Post by jmszr on 2010-07-01
psmc,

For adding packages offline you can use either: [http://aptoncd.sourceforge.net/](http://aptoncd.sourceforge.net/) or: [http://keryxproject.org/](http://keryxproject.org/) .

The reason for dropping some of the packages from the install CD was to open up room for other, more used, and new packages. Because the dropped ones are available for download it doesn't have much impact on those with broadband, but I can see where those with a limited, a low speed connection, or no connection could be unhappy.
