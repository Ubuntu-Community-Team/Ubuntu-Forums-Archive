### Post by gordintoronto on 2010-08-30
I bought an HP G62, on sale at Best Buy Canada, well within your price range. 15.6-inch screen, 3 GB of memory, 320 GB hard drive, 2.1 GHz AMD dual-core processor. Setting up dual-boot was a PITA because HP formatted the hard drive with 4 primary partitions. Other than that, it's a great machine. When I'm not travelling it will be a home theatre PC, with HDMI output for my high-def TV.
