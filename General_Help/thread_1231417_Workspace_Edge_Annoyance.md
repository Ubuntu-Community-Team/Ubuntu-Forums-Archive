When I have my mouse on the edge of my workspace and begin to scroll using the mouse scroll it switches between workspaces. How do I get it to stop this?

---

### Post by CatKiller on 2009-08-04
CCSM &#8594; Viewport Switcher &#8594; Desktop-based Viewport Switching. Disable Move Next and Move Prev.
