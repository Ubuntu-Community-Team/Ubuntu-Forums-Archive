### Post by tom.swartz07 on 2009-12-25

> Forgive me for asking, but how does one do that?

Heck no, dont apologize!

Right click on your panel bar that is missing the icons, (any empty space is fine)
click 'add to panel'
select notification area (its in alphabetical order)

That should fix it!
