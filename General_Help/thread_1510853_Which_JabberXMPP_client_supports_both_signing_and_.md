### Post by delcypher on 2010-06-16
A. Psi supports encryption using gpg keys

[http://psi-im.org/wiki/Encryption](http://psi-im.org/wiki/Encryption)

It is a little bit of work to setup gpg keys for yourself and with the person you want to communicate with but its worth it

[http://psi-im.org/wiki/Encryption](http://psi-im.org/wiki/Encryption)

b. I don't think Psi signs presence, why would you want that? Signing messages should be adequate as that still tells you whether or not the person you're chatting to is probably who they say they are.
