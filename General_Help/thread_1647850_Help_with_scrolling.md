### Post by sikander3786 on 2010-12-18
First of all, Linux/Ubuntu is vulnerable to Viruses. Don't ever format the whole OS if there are some performance issues. Viruses for Linux are very very rare.

Secondly, you don't need to provide your e-mail address in your post. That is like an invitation to the spam bots ;-)

Finally, we need to know about your hardware. Specially RAM, Processor and graphics card. Did you install the appropriate drivers for you card? Look under System > Administration > Hardware Drivers.
