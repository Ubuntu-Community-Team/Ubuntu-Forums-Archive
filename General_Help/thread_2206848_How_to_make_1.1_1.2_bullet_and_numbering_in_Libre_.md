### Post by amanchesterman on 2014-06-13
The solution is right in front of you. Select Format --> Bullets and Numbering --> Outline (i.e. the 'Outline' tab shown in the image above). It gives you a range of predefined outline styles, including 1.1.1 ... 1.1.2 ..., that Oshima_Kenji was looking for. Or you can define your own!

You can also increase/decrease the level of any entry in the list when you have typed it.
