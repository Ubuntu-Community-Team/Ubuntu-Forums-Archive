### Post by Xavier Oddmon on 2010-08-25
I don't have any experience with a Droid, but I got a Pre Plus a few weeks ago, and I have to say, it's got the greatest mobile OS ever made. 
As for synchronizing calendars and address books, there's no utility to do that. Instead, you put your calendars and contacts on google services, and have the palm log into those. You can connect to several accounts, and it keeps them all up to date. 

As for music, you plug it in via USB cable, and tell it to operate in USB mode. Then, it just shows up as a hard disk, and you can copy files to it. You can put music and pictures and videos anywhere on the storage device, it'll scan the entire thing for changes.

Also, i have to say the ability to put the phone in dev mode (enter upupdowndownleftrightleftrightbastart on the home screen) and modify the system as root is a very cool one, although i've only done minor tweaks.
