### Post by martini1179 on 2009-12-29
I'm not an expert, but you'd need to provide us with some logs. When a crash happens again, note the time and upon reboot, go into your Log Viewer (System>Administration>Log File Viewer) and provide us with the syslog and kern.log for the time right before the crash. Just don't cut and paste the entire log, as it would be a long read. 

After a crash, I would save that info rather quickly, since from my own experience logs quickly get overwritten as you go about your regular computing routines. 

Some more technically-minded users can then provide better help.

Also, providing more details than "it freezes" would be good too. I know sometimes this is difficult, given the seemingly random nature of most crashes.
