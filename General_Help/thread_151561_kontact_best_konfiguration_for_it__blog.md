> -how could i do it to subscribe to a blog? getting notified when there are new messages on the page? (best would be to use a feed and akregator right?) 
Yep, akregator will be your best friend for that. And best of all, you can either launch akregator separately or within Kontact. Most blogs have the XML button you could use to subscribe to a feed, or have instrunctions on how to do so. If you are using Konqueror to browse feeds/blogs, an RSS icon will appear at the bottom-right corner of the window if there is an RSS available for that page.
