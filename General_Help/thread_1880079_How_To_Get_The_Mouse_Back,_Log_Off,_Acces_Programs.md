### Post by Habeouscorpus on 2011-11-13
For starters, see if you can get your hands on a usb mouse. That will solve the immediate problem. 

See if this can get your touchpad to come back to life:

```
synclient touchpadoff=0 
```

Second, if you backup all your important files, you can always reinstall 11.04 or whatever you had before. Everything will be good as new :)
