### Post by col48 on 2010-07-20
Two problems which may be unrelated but there is a time coincidence at least.

Google maps does not load. It displays its page headings but most of the page is blank while it seems to get into an endless loop loading (alternately) mt0.google.com and mt1.google.com.

BBC iPlayer asks me to enable Javascript when I choose a program (TV or radio) to download or play. Javascript is enabled. I have the NoScript Add-on but even allowing all scripts makes no difference.

Both these started happening between 9 July and today (20 July 2010).

Any ideas?

Hardy 8.04, Firefox 3.6.6

---

### Post by col48 on 2010-07-22
Solved this myself, in the end.  Someone else (my "expert") had changed one of the NoScript options. (-> HTTPS -> Behaviour to 'Always' [forbid etc]).  Changing it back to the 'Never' default solved both the issues.
