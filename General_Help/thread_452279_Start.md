### Post by ijustam on 2007-05-23
Your boot sequence might be set to boot Windows first.

If you go into your BIOS (usually but hitting F1, DEL, or a specific key as your computer starts) you'll be able to edit the way your computer accesses devices as it starts.  The key itself is dependent on your motherboard, but is usually given to you as it boots (it'll say something along the lines of "Hit X to enter SETUP" or likewise.)

Once you've entered setup you should be able to change your boot order.  Make sure your CD-ROM precedes your hard drive in this case.
