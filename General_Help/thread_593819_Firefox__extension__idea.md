### Post by kidders on 2007-10-28
Hi there,

I'd say integrating a web browser too closely with a terminal could be *highly* dangerous. In any case, copy/paste is unnecessary on most Linux systems. Depending on how you're configured, simply selecting something in your browser and middle-clicking a terminal window should be enough to grab text from one and put it in the other.
