### Post by Chris1274 on 2010-09-14
If you're using Ubuntu One. switching that folder to lowercase would probably mess up the sync process. Also, you'll have to manually select your download destinations if you switch from "Downloads" to "downloads".

### Post by The Cog on 2010-09-14
The "Desktop" folder is used by gnome to display your desktop. Changing it to "desktop" will break that. There is a setting somewhere that says what your desktop folder is, but I can't remember where it is.

Your "Pictures" folder is used by some screensaver programs, and also by the desktop "change background image" setting.

It's probably less hassle getting used to them being there than it is changing them to lower case and correcting all the apps that use them.
