### Post by ineluki12 on 2007-06-01
If you're using Ubuntu, right-click on the small bar to the far left of your window list. On a plain Ubuntu installation, it'll be between the window list and the 'show desktop' icon. Click 'preferences' followed by the 'size' tab. Set your max and min sizes to the same.
