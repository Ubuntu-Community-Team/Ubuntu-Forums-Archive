### Post by kerry_s on 2010-05-02
your worried about speed with those specs? :lolflag:

1. don't use gnome or kde, xfce4 is better, a window manager is the best.

2. use the lighter alternative programs, why use the big dogs for simple tasks.

3. keep it simple, the fancier it is the more resource you use.

4. some times you just got to settle, speed over function is a balance, there are things you just can't change, example: firefox startup, it's just slow.


try lubuntu, it's minimal, uses openbox for the window manager, uses light alternatives, very simple default look.
