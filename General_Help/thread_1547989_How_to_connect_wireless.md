### Post by jereece on 2010-08-07
I just installed Ubuntu Netbook on my Lenovo S10 Netbook.  When I look at available wireless networks, it says "disconnected".  It does not show my wireless network nor my neighbors.  I thought the wireless must be manually turned off so I pressed the button to turn it on and nothing happens. I tried this several times.  Then I went into "Create New Wireless Network" and entered my wireless network name and password.  It still does not show this as a wireless network.  Is it possible that Ubuntu does not work with my Netbook's internal wireless adapter?  The light is clearly on showing my wireless is turned on and my laptops connect just fine.

### Post by jereece on 2010-08-07
Solved:  I connected my Netbook up to the Internet via cable and after it downloaded all the updates I saw there was a proprietary driver for the wireless adapter.  So I installed that and rebooted.  Now wireless is working just fine.
