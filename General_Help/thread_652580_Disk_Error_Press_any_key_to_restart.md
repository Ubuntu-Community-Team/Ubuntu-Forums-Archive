### Post by Craigus on 2008-01-01
If the drive is not behaving after being cleared with dban, then I suspect that the drive may be faulty.

Download Ultimate Boot CD :

[http://www.ultimatebootcd.com/]("http://www.ultimatebootcd.com/")

It will have manufacturer's HD testing utilities for your brand of HD. Run the appropriate one and test the drive.
