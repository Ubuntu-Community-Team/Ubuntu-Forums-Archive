### Post by Kenda on 2010-11-16
Not an expert on this but what I do is install Pulseaudio volume controller (PAVC). Start PAVC and the Sound Recorder. Then in PAVC go to the Recording tab and  adjust the stream that is being recorded until it shows up in the sound recorder. The level indicator in sound Recorder starts registering.
