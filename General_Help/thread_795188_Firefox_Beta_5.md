### Post by talz13 on 2008-05-15
Go to Bookmarks > Organize Bookmarks

You should be able to put the ones you want in the "Bookmarks Toolbar" folder to get them to appear on the toolbar.  Also, you may have just hidden the bookmarks toolbar.  Right click on the area above the address bar and make sure "Bookmarks Toolbar" is checked.
