### Post by thumper13 on 2010-04-29
As I'm waiting in anticipation with everyone else, I noticed nobody really said what a Release Candidate is.

A Release Candidate is really just the final draft of the finish product. It is meant to be finished, but still needs to be checked before it's released to the masses to make sure all the bugs are worked out. It's like the master record they make before pressing it to vinyl, they always check it first to make sure it's perfect.
