Hi all,

I've just setup thunderbird on Ubuntu as I wanted to share my email inbox between by UBUNTU desktop and my WXP laptop.

Previous to Ubuntu conversion (all hail the great penguin...) I shared my inbox between the WXP laptop and the WXP desktop easily.

The email folders are all stored on a networked HDD and its a simple case of pointing thunderbird in the right direction.


In the 'local folders' section of the account settings window i've added the location of the Inbox folder on the network storage.

T-Bird picks this up and shows me all emails in my folders and inbox.

There is one curious 'feature' and that is I have 2 folders marked inbox within T-bird. One is the actual folder on the network the other is empty and email never appears there.

Doesn't cause me any problems, it just looks untidy.


I hope this post may be of some use to other users.
