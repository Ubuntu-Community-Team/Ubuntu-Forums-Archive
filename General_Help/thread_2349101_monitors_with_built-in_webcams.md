### Post by TheFu on 2017-01-11
Just sharing my ignorance. ;) 

I've had a Logitech C920 webcam for a few years.  Initially, it didn't work well with Linux. Would die after 10 minutes of use.  Then I got a chromebook and plugged it in. It worked really well, but zoom in/out and panning controls don't work.  Since chromeOS is linux, I tried it on an Ubuntu install and it worked well there too. Still no zoom or panning control, but the audio pickup and video are very nice - best in class.  This is a highly rated webcam for Windows.  I only use it for recording presentations and only about 5 times a year.  I use OBS to capture the video and multiple audio screams from different microphones.

If you don't get any better options, look for recent (14.04+) posts on the C920 to know what you are getting into.
