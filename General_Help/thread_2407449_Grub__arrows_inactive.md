### Post by oldfred on 2018-12-04
What brand/model system?
Some have settings in UEFI/BIOS to enable USB and/or USB keyboard/mouse. Check that first.
Both Windows & Ubuntu have drivers for mouse/keyboard, but grub relies on whatever is default in UEFI/BIOS.
