### Post by Quackers on 2011-07-23
Welcome to UF :-)
Have you checked the md5sum of the downlaoded iso file?
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HowToMD5SUM](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HowToMD5SUM)

If that checks out ok boot from the live cd/usb again and at the first purple screen press any key. Choose your language then on the next screen choose the "check for defects" option. If that finds any errors at all the cd/usb is no good and must be redone. If it's a cd try burning the image at the lowest possible speed.
