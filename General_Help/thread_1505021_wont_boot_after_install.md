### Post by davidmohammed on 2010-06-08
sounds like a hardware issue.

are you sure your RAM is seated correctly, no loose cables for your hard-drive, pci cards are seated.

Disconnect all connected equipment apart from your monitor and keyboard.  Pull all the PCI cards apart from your graphics card.

When you manage to get grub to start, run the memtest for a few hours to see if you've got any RAM issues.
