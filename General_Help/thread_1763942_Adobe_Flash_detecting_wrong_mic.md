### Post by linuxinstalledfromhdd on 2011-05-29
You can try to wait for someone to help you that has had this issue before, but it might take some time.

What I would try if I were in your situation is..  I would probably try an older version of Flash. I would install Flash Aid plugin for Firefox, and play around with  the different versions available to install with it.  You can try the Beta version, or the Stable version. You can also download an older version, uncompress the package and use Flash Aid plugin to install it by pointing it at the uncompressed older flash plugin, and it will take care of the rest.  After trying a few different versions, hopefully one of them will find your mic again.

Or it could have nothing to do with the flash plugin itself, and something to do with changes to your OS.
