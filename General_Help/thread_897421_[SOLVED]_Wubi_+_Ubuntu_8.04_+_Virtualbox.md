### Post by rokytnji on 2008-08-22
Wubi needs a good  internet connection to work. All  the setup and files come from the Wubi website. Another way is to use [http://unetbootin.sourceforge.net/](http://unetbootin.sourceforge.net/)  which also requires a internet connection, and make a bootable pendrive. Other wise you are stuck running your live cd's as they are and if you want to update the repositories in Synaptic Package Manager, that requires a internet conection  also.  Everything requires a internet connection.
