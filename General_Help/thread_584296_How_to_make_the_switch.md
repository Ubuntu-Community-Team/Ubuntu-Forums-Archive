### Post by p_quarles on 2007-10-20
May I suggest Ubuntu? :D

More seriously, I would suggest you experiment with the major live distros. Ubuntu, PCLinuxOS, Mandriva, Sabayon, Fedora. This way you can test drive the OS without installing it, and see which one feels most comfortable.

As for building a system, I would just advise that you do a little research on the hardware. A lot of hardware runs great with Linux with no need for any configuration. The main problem areas are with graphics cards and wireless adapters. I'd recommend an Intel GPU (all three of my systems are running them, and I have zero problems), but I know many people have had good experiences with nVidia.
