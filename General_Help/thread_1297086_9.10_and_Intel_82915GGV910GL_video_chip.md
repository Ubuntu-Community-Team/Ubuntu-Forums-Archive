### Post by nostradamnit on 2009-10-21
I have a Dell Dimension 3100 with the Intel 82915G/GV/910GL video chip (2gb RAM as well). It worked fine with 9.04, but since my upgrade to 9.10 beta, the video lags quite a bit, scrolling is jumping, and alt-tabbing between windows in very laggy, sometimes taking several seconds before the app becomes completely visible - often I get a gray box where the app is, then the title bar, then the main window. 

I've seen that there were major changes to the Intel driver architecture (kernel mode, EXA > UXA) that were supposed to be an improvement, but in my case, it's a big step backwards. 

Is anyone else experiencing this issue, and does anyone know what I can do to fix it?

Thanks,
Sam

### Post by jhquentin on 2009-11-02
I had same problem, I tried:
sudo aptitude remove xserver-xorg-video-intel-2.4
sudo aptitude install xserver-xorg-video-intel
This solved my problem.
