### Post by Lord Illidan on 2005-08-24
Cyber-cafe...

Well, you will definetely need Firefox for one thing, or there will be no net browsing...

And maybe vnc

Also, I think you might have to fiddle a little with the user settings, so that there is no chance that they can muck around with the configuration files and stuff.

I also recommend Kubuntu instead of Ubuntu.... Considering that the vast majority of people entering your cafe` will be Windows users, KDE will look more familiar to them. 

Otherwise, I am not sure about what else you would need... virus scanning is a moot point, as there are no viruses on linux..
And maybe a firewall, as your machines will be always on, try searching for 'firewall' in this forum.

And maybe you might want to put a bookmark in Firefox to point to [www.mail2web.com](www.mail2web.com) so that people can check their e-mail online...
