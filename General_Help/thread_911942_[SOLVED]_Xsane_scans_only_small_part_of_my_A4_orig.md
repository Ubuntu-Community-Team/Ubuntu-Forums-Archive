### Post by drs305 on 2008-09-06
If you select Window, Show Preview, (with the A4 setting selected) do the dashed lines representing the scan area show the entire page? If not, can you drag them to include the entire page?

That and the 'Define Image' selector near the top right of the main page are the settings I tweak to get it to scan the correct area.
