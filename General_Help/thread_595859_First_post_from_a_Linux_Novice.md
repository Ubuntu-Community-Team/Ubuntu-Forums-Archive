### Post by stomponthis on 2007-10-29
Welcome to Ubuntu! 
Try this section of the documentation.  It uses a very good tool called "ndiswrapper" which uses windows drivers to get your wireless going.  
There could also be drivers for your wireless device for linux, and its pretty likely that there is a how-to to get your wireless device going.  You just need the details eg, brand and type.
Yeah, no plug and play can be a bit difficult sometimes.  I used to use XP, but glad I made a total change :-)
