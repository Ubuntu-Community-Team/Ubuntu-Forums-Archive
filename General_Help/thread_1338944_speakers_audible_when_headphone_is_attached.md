I  can tell you how to get rid of the speaker sound once you have the preferences back, but without more idea not how to get them back - if you could indicate what the "something" is that is indicated as missing, that would definitely help. (The solution to that is probably to install the package listed as missing, or to work out which package installs the library listed as missing and install/reinstall it.)

But once you've fixed that, what you need to do is:

1) Open the volume control (the icon in your panel which looks a bit like a speaker).
2) Click on the volume "Volume Control" button that you'll now see to get a fuller list of controls.
3) Find the one called "Jack Sense" and turn it on (un-mute it). This will then pick up that the phones are connected and turn off the speakers.
4) If there isn't a Jack Sense listed, click on the Preferences button - this controls what's listed.

Hope this helps!
