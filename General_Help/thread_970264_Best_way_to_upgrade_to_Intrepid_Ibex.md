### Post by cybrsaylr on 2008-11-04
Was going through the 'Installation & Upgrades' forums and the general consensus seems to be a clean install with a CD is better than an online upgrade with Synaptic Package Manager of Hardy to Intrepid. 

I had no problems with LiveCD on either my old PC or new laptop. Then I used that LiveCD to install Intrepid on the old PC and it runs great. Didn't want to do it on my new laptop with Hardy yet because Hardy runs great on my laptop and didn't want to take a chance on messing that up, not knowing how Intrepid would be.

The only problem I have with Hardy on my new Toshiba laptop is no wireless. Lost wireless after a Hardy upgrade and couldn't get wireless back in spite of many attempts and with the help of these forums to fix.
 Reports so far indicate  Intrepid has fixed these wireless problems of Hardy.
