[https://duckduckgo.com/?q=Ubuntu%2C+how+to+recover+your+password](https://duckduckgo.com/?q=Ubuntu%2C+how+to+recover+your+password)

I format my Edubuntu & Linux Mint hard drives with separate partitions for...

swap (= to the amount of installed RAM + 1 GB)
/ (root) (I've found that 40 GB is comfy and allows space for future expansion)
/home (the rest of the hard drive)

...so that I can reinstall an OS without losing my files. Note: DO NOT FORMAT your home partition when reinstalling an OS, just give it a mount point as /home .

*mother hen mode* Oh yeah, and back up your files to an external hard drive (I use two) ASAP.
