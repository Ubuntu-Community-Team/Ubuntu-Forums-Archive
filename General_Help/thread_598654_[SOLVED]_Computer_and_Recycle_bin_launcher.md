### Post by ThrobbingBrain66 on 2007-10-31
Recycle bin:
Hit alt+F2 and type 'gconf-edtor'
Navigate to apps/nautilus/desktop and check the 'trash_icon_visible' box

I am going to assume you mean your file browser when you say "my computer"
In this same screen as above, check home_icon_visible

If you meant a launcher for any program, just right-click and choose 'Create Launcher...' (you'll need to know the command to launch the app of course).
