### Post by blazemore on 2009-03-02
Since you'll only need to do it once, you're looking for an option called "Boot Menu"

It will say which button to press to access the boot menu when you turn your PC on. On my system, it's F8, but it might be different on yours.

When the boot menu comes up, choose the USB device from the list.
