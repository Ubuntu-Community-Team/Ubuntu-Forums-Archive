### Post by 2CV67 on 2010-09-04
I just gave up on Evolution mail, purely because it has no alternative fonts.
Pity - otherwise it is really excellent for my usage.

So I now have Thunderbird 3.0.6
This is working OK, using a profile I copied in from TB2 in XP, but I don't have any indicator to show I have unread mail in my inbox.
Sure, there is the "New Mail Alert" which appears bottom right for a short time, but it then disappears without trace, so if you miss it or forget it, you leave unread mails unread.

Evolution indicates the number of unread mails in its bottom-panel button.
Thunderbird2 in XP puts a special signal on the bottom panel whenever there is unread mail.

Surely TB3 in Ubuntu must have this essential feature somewhere?
I haven't found it yet.

Thanks for any suggestions!

### Post by ajgreeny on 2010-09-04
Try gmail-notify from the repos. It puts an icon in your panel that does what you want.
