### Post by aeiah on 2007-01-23
gaim-vv supports the viewing of yahoo webcams, but doesnt let you send yet. gaim-vv is a fork from gaim that concentrates on voice and video, hence the name. i havent used it so cant vouch for it. from a glance at the site it seems like msn isnt supported with this gaim fork at this time.

there is also [GYachE](http://www.phrozensmoke.com/projects/pyvoicechat/) which looks like your best option, but may be tricky for you to install. i suggest finding a good howto first for that if you're new to linux.
