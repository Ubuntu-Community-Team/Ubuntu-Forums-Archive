### Post by HermanAB on 2011-01-28
Howdy,

Most Ubuntu installs are perfectly stable.

Most stability problems seem to be with ACPI on some laptops and Nvidia video drivers.

So, where does it leave you?  Depends on your hardware.  If you run the average Lenovo, Dell or HP desktop machine, then it will likely work perfectly.  If you run a notebook PC (especially Toshiba or Sony), then you may have some trouble to get it to work properly.
