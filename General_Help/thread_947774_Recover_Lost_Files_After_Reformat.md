### Post by Hajo Harts on 2008-10-14
I used 'testdisk' ([http://www.cgsecurity.org/wiki/TestDisk_Download](http://www.cgsecurity.org/wiki/TestDisk_Download)) after I quickly formatted my Ubuntu disk instead of my other disk. Everything was restored. However, I only formatted the disk, I had not written anything on the disk yet.
Hope this helps.
