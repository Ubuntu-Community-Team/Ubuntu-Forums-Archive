### Post by ram2500 on 2009-10-07
Hard drives are cheap! Install it, for goodness sakes, on another hard drive (if you are unsure about partitioning) but either get another drive or partition your hard drive! Running the Live CD reminds me of running MS-DOS on a 2-floppy system with no hard drive (for you kiddies out there that think Windows 98 is ancient, these systems usually had no hard drive unless you paid a premium, and MS-DOS ran from a 360KB floppy disk). I'm glad those days are over! It's amazing, though, that a whole operating system could fit on a 360KB 5.25 floppy. Nowadays, it barely ekes its way onto a CD.
