### Post by BGFG on 2009-04-30
If you perform an upgrade through Update manager, nothing is deleted. packages are updated, new version installed, no data loss.

Procedure:
Alt +f2
```

update-manager -d

```

Update.

### Post by cwbar_1 on 2009-05-03
If you have /home on a separate partition, you can reinstall (leaving /home alone).  If this is the case, make all your hidden files show, and delete all the .* folders in your home folder.  (mine would be /home/cwbar_1/)  If you do not have your home folder on a different partition from "/", then back up all your data to a jump drive or burn a data CD.  Then reinstall.
