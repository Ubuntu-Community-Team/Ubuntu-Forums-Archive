### Post by colobix on 2008-08-17
You can set shourtcuts to almost everything you would like to do on your pc, and that also includes moving windows up, down, above, below etc,
ONe method is to go to keybord shortcuts and choose shortcuts there.
You can also use Compiz, enable the effect "move window". Then you can click on the Move Window button, choose keys to move your windows and how fast or slow you would like to move them.

But insted of moving the windows it goes faster if you use your 2nd desktop and maybe add some more. Then you can have 1 window on each of them.

I hope this helps you.

Good luck :)
