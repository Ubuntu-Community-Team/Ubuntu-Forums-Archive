### Post by Mark Phelps on 2012-08-22
Any SSID that says Belkin or Linksys, or any other network hardware provider name, is almost certainly a PRIVATE wireless network where one of your neighbors simply didn't bother to change the SSID to something else.

In some countries, folks have ended up being prosecuted because someone ELSE stole their bandwidth to illegally download movies or music.

IF you really wanted to do the RIGHT thing, you would quit using THEIR network and do the following:
1) Find out whose network it is
2) Go tell them that their network is unsecured
