### Post by Hedgehog1 on 2011-04-05
This typically means the boot is hanging on the Video Driver.

Please read and follow the 'blank screen problems' thread below.

You will be changing the 'nomodeset' boot parameter.

[ubuntumaverick-blank-screen-problem.html]("http://ubuntu4beginners.blogspot.com/2011/01/ubuntumaverick-blank-screen-problem.html")

You might need to to enter other boot parameters There are 6 of them. You may have to do them one-by-one until you get video.

[LiveCDBootOptions]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/LiveCDBootOptions#Main%20Page%20Options") (Common Kernel Options Section)
