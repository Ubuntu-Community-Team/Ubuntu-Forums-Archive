### Post by vanadium on 2007-07-27
I do not know anything about gallery, but in Linux, you can have your data organized in different ways using links. Thus, you could substitute the data directory of gallery by a link to where your pictures are now. You can also link individual files. If pictures are within a certain directory tree in one place, in another place links to these files can all exist in one single directory.
