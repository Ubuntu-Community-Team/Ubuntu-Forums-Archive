You can't buy Ubuntu/Kubuntu/Edubuntu... It's free buddy! You can download the current versions from [http://www.ubuntulinux.org](http://www.ubuntulinux.org) and the next version will be out in a few months...

If you just wanna grab the installer for Ubuntu, after you install it you can open up a terminal and type "sudo apt-get kubuntu" and you'll have all the KDE stuff...

Just incase you don't know, anything that starts with a "K" is designed for KDE(kool desktop environment) and anything with a "G" is for Gnome... You can choose witch you wanna boot into at start up...
