### Post by cmost on 2007-06-10
This is a known issue.  If you go into your Compiz/Beryl settings manager you'll notice that you have one big viewport stretched over four workspaces (or desktops.)  Therefore, you technically only have ONE viewport.  Because of this, the options you're looking for (to move to adjacent viewports) is nonsensical to the software and have therefore been removed.  Previous, more hackish versions of Compiz and Beryl got around this by using a modified libwnck file.  You might investigate whether this is still available.  You can also alleviate the problem by specifying four desktops and four viewports but you'll lose other functionality.
