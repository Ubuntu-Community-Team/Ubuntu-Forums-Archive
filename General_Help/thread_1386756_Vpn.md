### Post by jamesisin on 2010-01-21
What Hotspot Shield does is create a VPN tunnel from your machine to their servers, from which they access the Internet (as a proxy for you).

What sort of protection are you hoping to find?  You are running Ubuntu and so are remarkably secure in cases where you touch the Internet directly.  You can run local firewalls (on your laptop) and you can make your system quite secure through employing certain best practices.

I don't imagine you'll need to proxy through someone who claims to provide you with security.
