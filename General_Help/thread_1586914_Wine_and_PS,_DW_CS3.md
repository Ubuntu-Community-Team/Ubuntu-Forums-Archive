### Post by Mark Phelps on 2010-10-03
First of all, quit bumping every few hours.  It's rude and it violates the community rule of bumping no more frequently than once a day.

Second, if you're going to use Wine, you need to go to the WineHQ site and check out the compatibility ratings for the apps you want to use.  The link is below:

[http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?bIsQueue=false&bIsRejected=false&sClass=application&sTitle=Browse+Applications&iItemsPerPage=25&iPage=1&sOrderBy=appName&bAscending=true]("http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?bIsQueue=false&bIsRejected=false&sClass=application&sTitle=Browse+Applications&iItemsPerPage=25&iPage=1&sOrderBy=appName&bAscending=true")

If you check, you will see that CS3 for BOTH apps you mention is rated garbage -- meaning, they are NOT going to work no matter what you do.

Save yourself time and grief next time and check the WineHQ site first.
