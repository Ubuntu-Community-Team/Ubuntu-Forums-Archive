### Post by dcstar on 2010-12-29
> **0949er said:**
> how old is the HD? Sounds like the drive is failing to me.

Yep - possible bad blocks.

You can actually hear it when it occurs - a constant repetitive pattern as the heads home and then try to read the block again and again until it finally works or times out.

Once you hear this you get a new drive ASAP.
