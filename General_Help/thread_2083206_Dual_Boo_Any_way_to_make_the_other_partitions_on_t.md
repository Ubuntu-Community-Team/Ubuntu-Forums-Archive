### Post by kerberosv2 on 2012-11-11
Ok, I am stupid.  Figured it out.  Settings.  Desktop.  Icons, and uncheck "Removable Devices".  This seemed to have removed all but the Xubuntu partition from the desktop.  

Hope this helps someone!
