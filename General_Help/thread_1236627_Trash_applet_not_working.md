### Post by Tamlynmac on 2009-08-10
I know this sounds foolish, but I had this occur with a number of system and also helped another person in this forum by using this procedure. Of course there's no guarantee it will work, but it's a simple and permanent fix (at least for all I've been associated with).

Right click and (unlock from panel if locked) delete the trash icon.

Right click on panel and select "Add to Panel"

Find the "Trash Applet" and left click on it one time

At the bottom of the "Add to Panel" window left click on the "+Add" button

Move the trash icon to where the old trash icon was (by right clicking on the icon and selecting "move") and lock to panel, if it was locked to begin with.

Reboot

Basically this removes and reinstalls the Trash Applet. This has solved the problem for everyone I've know that experienced this issue. Perhaps it's a bug in the install of the trash applet - at least for certain installs, as I haven't seen it with every install?

As I said, no guarantees, but it's a simply procedure. 

Good Luck and Hope This Helps.
