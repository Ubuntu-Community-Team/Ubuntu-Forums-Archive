### Post by Butthead on 2011-06-27
Answer for what? :confused:

Unlock your widgets (either by right clicking on your Desktop, or by clicking on the "cashew" shaped thing on the top right of the screen), and then pick "unlock widgets" and then "add widgets."  Look for a widget called "Luna" and install it.  Position it wherever you want on your Desktop (after setting it up to your satisfaction), and then lock widgets again. Voila (or Walla for the non-French speakers out there. ;) ). :guitar:
