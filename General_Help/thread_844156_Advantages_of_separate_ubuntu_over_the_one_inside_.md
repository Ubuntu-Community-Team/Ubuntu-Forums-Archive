### Post by Canis familiaris on 2008-06-29
In Wubi mode (inside Windows) you cannot hibernate.
Also the seperate installation on disk is faster, for obvious reasons.
YOu could also remove Windows if you wish if you go with the seperate installation.

There are few more which I do not know of.
