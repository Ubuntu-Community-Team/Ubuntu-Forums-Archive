### Post by ronald_stall on 2007-04-12
Ok, I have worked this out and this is what I found just in case anyone else has this problem.  The printer install completed successfully but then when I tried to print I got the blue screen below. 

The work around that worked for me was I bought a USB 1.1 hub and attached it to my USB 2.0 jack and then plugged the printer into it and reinstalled the printer from scratch in the Windows guest.
