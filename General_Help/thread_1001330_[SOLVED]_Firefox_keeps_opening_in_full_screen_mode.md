### Post by fooman on 2008-12-03
try this: open firefox and do the f11 thing *twice* to get to a normal size screen.

unmaximize firefox (not minimize to panel). resize it to a slightly smaller window size.

close firefox.

open firefox...then maximize firefox.

problem should be gone.

---

### Post by todak on 2008-12-03
Resize the Firefox window by clicking and dragging on one of the corners until it is small enough to fit within the panels. Then use the maximize button to fill the area between the panels.
