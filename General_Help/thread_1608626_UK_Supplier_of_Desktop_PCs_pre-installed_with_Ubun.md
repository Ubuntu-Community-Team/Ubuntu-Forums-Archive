### Post by cchhrriiss121212 on 2010-10-29
I have seen numerous UK sellers with Ubuntu preinstalled, but they are all somewhat expensive when compared with a Dell machine or similar. Have you considered building one?

Compatible desktop hardware is not that hard to figure out: CPUs, RAM and HDs are not an issue, 99% motherboards will be supported, then go with an nvidia GPU if you'd like one. Wireless cards are tricky but eBay has a few Linux compatible options.

If that is too much work then PM me and I can send you a quote myself, I built and sold roughly 20 Ubuntu desktops this year through eBay, all of whom were happy with what they got.
