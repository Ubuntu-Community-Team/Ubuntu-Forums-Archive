### Post by MARP1961 on 2011-03-15
I think Ubuntu's 'Update Manager' needs the internet to get all the updates at installation. I  always used a wired / Ethernet connection when I am carrying out a fresh Ubuntu install. WiFi  sometimes needs 'fine tuning' later. Get your Ubuntu installation done first and ensure a reliable, wired connection to the internet for Update Manager to do it's job.
