### Post by birkopf on 2015-02-18
In the synaptic look for art-work or wallpapers. Alternatively try using
ubuntu distribution names in the synaptic and you will find package with
artwork and wallpapers from various distributions. This will be installed to
the shame /user/share/ folder as your other wallpapers. You can track it there
and copy from there. Alternatively you can open desktop properties and
change wallpaper to this one.
