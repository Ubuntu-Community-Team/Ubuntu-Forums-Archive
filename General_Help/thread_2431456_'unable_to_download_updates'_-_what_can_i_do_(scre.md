### Post by Holger_Gehrke on 2019-11-16
You can wait a few minutes. You've probably got automatic updates enabled and the updater was downloading updates while you were trying to install something. The package system is a (simple) database and only one program at a time can use the database. Once the updater is finished you can proceed with your installation.
