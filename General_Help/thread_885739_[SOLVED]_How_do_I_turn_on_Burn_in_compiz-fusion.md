### Post by Crushyerbones on 2008-08-10
To see the cube you need to press a key combination, I *think* it's alt by default.

To get the burn animation do the following in the advanced desktop settings:
1. Open animations and enable them if they're off
2. Choose the tab you'd like (close animation, minimize, etc)
3. Double click the animation selection lines with strange names for app windows. The others are fairly descriptive (Dialogs for dialogs,PopupMenu, etc...)
4. On the dialog you can use the dropdown menu to choose burn

5. Burn the *******



If your settings don't seem to do anything just say so, no use explaining what's not needed.
