### Post by JohnH on 2011-05-09
Hi there,
I lost my Home Folder out of the launcher after fiddling with some other desktops. In the process of deleting the other desktops I lost the Home Folder out of the launcher.  

I located the file Home Folder file in /usr/share/applications. As root I right clicked and opened the "Properties" window then made sure that the"Command:" box had "nautilus --no-desktop %U" written in it (minus quotes). I closed the Properties window and logged out and in again. And there it was.

I hope this might be of help to others.

Regards
John
