### Post by nortexoid on 2009-04-04
Is there any good bibtex management software that is not java based?

I tried Pybibliographer but it violates all the tenets of latex and the laws of the gods. E.g. if you type capitals in the title field it puts braces around them (ensuring they remain capitals in the built output) and it puts braces around the author's name (again ensuring that it comes out as typed). Obviously this goes against the entire point of having bibliography styles that control these things.
