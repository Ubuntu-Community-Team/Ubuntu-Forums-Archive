### Post by kaibob on 2009-02-12
> **theodorekon said:**
> There are some of the updates in the update manager for which I am not interested so I just unclick in order not to download them. Nevertheless the update manager keeps notifying me for these updates until I download them. How can I stop being notified for those updates??

In Synaptic, you can prevent a program from updating by selecting *Package > Lock Version* from the top menu. I assume (but have not checked) that this will prevent update manager from showing an update.
