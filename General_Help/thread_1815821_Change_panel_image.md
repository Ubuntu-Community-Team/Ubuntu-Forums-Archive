### Post by tigerlxxv on 2011-08-01
Did you change them as a theme? I've had good luck with right-clicking the panel in question, then clicking 'Properties' and using the 'Background' tab. also, make sure that they're of a suitable size. The top panel, for example, is 24 pixels high at the default setting. Most images will simply be stretched/squashed to fit, but you may have trouble if they're too large. Make sure they open correctly in Image Viewer, just to be sure.
