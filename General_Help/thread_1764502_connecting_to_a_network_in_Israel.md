### Post by dandnsmith on 2011-05-22
At base level it's most often the encryption protocol needs to be satisfied,
and then you need to know that the system is either 'open' or you have the password.

Some organisations (especially universities) add a level of logging in to validate you as a user which works fine for Windows, but is rather more of a hassle in linux-based systems
