### Post by scouser73 on 2009-11-13
Hi Donnie, 

**1 -** Go to **System > Administration > Synaptic Package Manager**

**2 -** Enter your password

**3 -** Click **Mark All Upgrades**

**4 -** Click **Apply**

**5 -** Then click **Apply** again

Your updated Pidgin should have successfully installed at this point, and you'll be able to find it under the Internet sub-menu.
