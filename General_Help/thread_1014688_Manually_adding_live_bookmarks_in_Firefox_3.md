### Post by vultaire on 2009-05-14
I ran into this issue too.  Kind of ugly; all the Mozilla docs about manually adding live bookmarks are rather unhelpful.

I found a workaround though.  Take one of your existing live bookmarks and copy it, or find any feed on the next that **does** work with FF3 and make one.  Then modify the bookmark to point at the feed you really want, either via the "Tools->Organize Bookmarks..." dialog, or by right clicking and choosing "properties" if you bookmarked it to your bookmarks toolbar.

I know this thread is long dead, but maybe this'll help someone. :D
