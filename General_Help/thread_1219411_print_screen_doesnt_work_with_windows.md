### Post by Codix121 on 2009-07-21
Go to APPLICATIONS -> ACCESSORIES -> TAKE SCREENSHOT

SELECT GRAB WHOLE MENU

then SET a "5 SECOND DELAY"
THEN CLICK TAKE SCREENSHOT,
IN THOSE 5 SECONDS OPEN YOUR MENU or whatever it is your trying to do.
