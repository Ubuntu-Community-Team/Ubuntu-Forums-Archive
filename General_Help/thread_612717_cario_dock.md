### Post by rawr2010 on 2007-11-15
I just installed Cairo. It is easy to understand and works well for me. 
Once you've downloaded and installed the .debs (the dock and the plug-ins), just right click on the panel, and Add to Panel->Custom Application Launcher->Name Cairo Dock, command: cairo-dock.  Then you've made yourself an Icon. You can launch the dock from there.

### Post by RavanH on 2007-11-15
> **onlyproductions said:**
> hi installed the cario dock debian package, now what do i do.

Start cairo dock by opening a terminal screen and giving the command ```
cairo-dock
```
It should now be visible on you screen, probably on the bottom. Right click it anywhere for options. If you can find a nice theme and behavior and you are happy with it... Add a new entry to your Sessions list (Ubuntu menu **System>Preferences>Sessions**) with the command cairo-dock to make it run automatically upon login.
