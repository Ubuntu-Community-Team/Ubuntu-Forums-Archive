### Post by rleck on 2006-02-27
xmms has done this to me a number of times, always when i have another sound program open which currently 'owns' the soundcard, other times seemingly at random. Close that other program if there is an obvious conflict, then try this next trick. Occasionally I have closed xmms using the 'X' without actually stopping the playing. When I open it again, I get your error message. Most times I simply clear the error message, press the square stop button and push the play button again and off she goes. Other times I have to close and reopen xmms to clear the old lock on the soundcard. Either way it sounds to me like a leftover lock on the soundcard, not a major problem. Good Luck!
