### Post by dragonfly41 on 2017-09-14
You can try this ...

Go to your Internet Settings.

Edit your Connection.

Select IPv4 Settings tab

In DNS settings field insert

8.8.8.8, 8.8.4.4

source:  [https://developers.google.com/speed/public-dns/](https://developers.google.com/speed/public-dns/)

> If you decide to try Google Public DNS, your client programs will perform all DNS lookups using Google Public DNS.

In fact I use OpenDNS settings

208.67.222.123, 208.67.220.123
