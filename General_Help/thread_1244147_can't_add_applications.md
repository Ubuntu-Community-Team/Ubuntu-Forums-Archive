### Post by t0p on 2009-08-19
Okay, the error message might be badly worded.  It says the apps you've chosen are not for i386 machines, but it means also incompatible with i686 machines.  i386 and i686 are pretty much the same really.  A better term is x86.

Anyway, it seems to me that you are selecting programs made for 64 bit machines.  Choose apps for i386 and all should be well.
