### Post by sayakb on 2008-07-13
It seems the normal size.. The panels are thinner than the XP/Vista taskbar, fonts are of same size (96dpi). Try changing the font to Tahoma (Copy Tahoma font from your Windows folnt folder to your ~/.fonts folder -- ie. the hidden .fonts folder in your home directory).
