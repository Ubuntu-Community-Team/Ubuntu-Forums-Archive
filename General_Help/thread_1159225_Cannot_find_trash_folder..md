### Post by avsd on 2010-04-05
The easiest way is to add Trach applet on the Panel:
[INDENT] In UNR right-click on the Panel in top-right part of the screen, select **"Add to Panel"**, select **"Trash"** and press **"Add"**.
[/INDENT]Also, you can access the Trash typing in Nautilus:
[INDENT]** trash:///**[/INDENT]
