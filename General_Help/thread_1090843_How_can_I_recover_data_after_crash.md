### Post by lindsay7 on 2009-03-09
You could set up a partition on your drive that has ubuntu on it, or keep you data files on an external drive or usb drive. The nightmare would be a hard disk failure so it would not matter if you were running windows or Ubuntu you would have a major problem getting you data back. You could also do regular backups to cds.

I would be more concerned with harddrive failures that problems with Ubuntu crashing.
