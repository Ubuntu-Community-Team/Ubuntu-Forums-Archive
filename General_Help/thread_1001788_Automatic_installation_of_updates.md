### Post by Therion on 2008-12-04
You need to configure the Update Manager to do this.  Go to System/Administration and select "Software Sources" and "Updates" tab.  
From there you click "on" the radio button for "Download all updates in the background".

You can NOT automate the restarts to my knowledge.
