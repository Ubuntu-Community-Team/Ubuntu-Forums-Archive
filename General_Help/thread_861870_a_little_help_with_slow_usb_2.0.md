### Post by Rocket2DMn on 2008-07-17
That is actually rather typical.  Just because they advertise that speed doesn't mean you get it.  When USB transfers data, if you were to look at a graph, it would be like a lot of spikes going up and down - it tends to send data in bursts.  Firewire, which goes at 400Mb/s keeps a more constant flow which is why it is considered faster overall that USB 2 which is marked at 480Mb/s.  I've never gotten more than 16 MB/s or so from a SATA to USB 2 drive, so USB 2 to USB 2 will be even slower.
