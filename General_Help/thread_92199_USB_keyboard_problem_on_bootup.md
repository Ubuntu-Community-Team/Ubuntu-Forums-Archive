### Post by LittleBlue on 2005-11-19
Hello Ryan:

I experienced the same sort of problem with my Asus motherboard.

In the BIOS, make sure that "USB Legacy Keyboard Support" and "USB Legacy Mouse Support" are enabled.

This fixed my problem.  I hope it works for you.
