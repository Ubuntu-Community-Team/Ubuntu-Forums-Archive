### Post by jmszr on 2011-07-13
ubuntistas,

If you haven't already, install "kubuntu-restricted-extras" and then install the Firefox add-on Flash-Aid: [https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/flash-aid/](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/flash-aid/) and run that - it should take care of your issue.

Edit: If you continue to have problems after that, then this thread may be of help: [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1799217](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1799217) .
