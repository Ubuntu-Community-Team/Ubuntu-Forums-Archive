### Post by hawthornso23 on 2010-08-10
Have you tried disabling IPV6 in firefox.

Type  about:config  in the address bar - void your warranty (joke) and type IPV6 in the search box. Click to set   network.dns.disableIPV6  = true  .

IPV6 misconfiguration on your network (many ISPs don't support it properly yet) can cause strange timeouts and slow connection.
