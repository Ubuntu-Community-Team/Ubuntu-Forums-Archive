### Post by wanderingarticfox on 2011-10-01
I recently went and linked my Ubuntu and Kubuntu Doc.'s,Music and Pictures to a logical partition. I do not know what I did but some how my Office menu listings are missing from my Applications menu. I have checked in Preferences under the system Menu and looked in the Main Menu > Office and verified that all of the LibreOffice boxes are checked.

Is there some 'command-line' entry I can use to re-establish the Office folder into the Applications menu?

Thank you in advance for your help.

[Solved]  System>Preferences>Main Menu>Office Folder   deselect all and close; reopen and select the Office folder check box, select items for folder, close Main Menu box and check Applications.  Office folder is now there.
