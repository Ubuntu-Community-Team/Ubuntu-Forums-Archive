### Post by Sef on 2013-06-09
> [COLOR=#000000]12.10 and 12.04.2 LTS.[/COLOR]

12.10 will have newer software installed by default, and is supported for 18 months.

12.04 has older software, but often it can be updated through PPAs, and it is supported for 5 years. LTS means long term support.
