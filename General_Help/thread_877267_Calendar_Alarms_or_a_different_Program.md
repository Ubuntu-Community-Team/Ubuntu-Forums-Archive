### Post by bmac on 2008-08-01
You can select and play a sound file that will play for a scheduled appointment.

Open Appointment pop-up
On the tool bar select the Alarm icon
In the Alarm selection box - select "Customize"
Then hit the "ADD" button
The Add Alarm screen will open
Under "Alarm" select play a sound
Under "Options" check "Custom alarm sound
Then click on the folder icon on the right side of the "Sound" box
Locate a sound file from your drive - I use wav. files.....
You can also choose to repeat the alarm at time intervals of your choice.

There have been some bug reports related to this function (check google) - but mine appears to work fine...

Hope this helps....
