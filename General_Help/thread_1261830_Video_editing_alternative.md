### Post by t0p on 2009-09-09
**XVidCap** will record video from your monitor.  So you could select to record from the game window and capture your gameplay.

Once you've recorded some stuff, you can edit it with **Avidemux**.  But Avidemux is a pretty basic video editor.  You might want to search Synaptic for something a little moe feature-ful.
