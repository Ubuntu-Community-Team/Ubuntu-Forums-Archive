### Post by dragonfly41 on 2023-03-27
On Ubuntu 20.04 I favour using Flameshot.


[LIST]
[*]Install from repo and activate to run at startup. I use Stacer for listing startup apps.
[*]In Flameshot you should see icon in top tray, click, Take Screenshot, or Open Launcher, or Configuration.
[*]Select a screen area to bracket with mouse (it does not take entire screenshot by default but leaves you to select the area which, for my batch usage, I prefer,  to clip areas of the screen).
[*]Depending on configuration you can then either save image, upload to Imgur, open in an app such as gimp, or copy to clipboard.
[/LIST]

Here I have chosen to upload screen capture to Imgur as example. Copy URL from popup and optionally post in thread rather than image.   [https://i.imgur.com/Ns3X4wx.png](https://i.imgur.com/Ns3X4wx.png)

 But you can choose other options from the buttons surrounding the bracketed area. This process can be automated if going through many captures in batch mode.
