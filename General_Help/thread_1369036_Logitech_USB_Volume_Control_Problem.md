### Post by Pollox on 2009-12-31
When I plug in my USB headset, I have to go to "Sound Preferences" (you can right click the icon in the notification area), then on the output tab there's an option to select the USB headset.  Then the volume buttons worked for the headset.  You might have to repeat this every time you plug it in.
