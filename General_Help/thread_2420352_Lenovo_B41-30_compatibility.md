### Post by Furycd001 on 2019-06-04
Best thing you can do is stick Ubuntu on a usb and boot the computer to the live desktop. Use the live desktop to test the computer and see what works, what doesn't work & how well it runs. If something doesn't work then [duck]("http://duckduckgo.com") it and you can usually get it working. If your experience is kind of meh or sluggish then you can always try a lighter version of Ubuntu like Xubuntu, Lubuntu, Ubuntu Mate etc etc.
