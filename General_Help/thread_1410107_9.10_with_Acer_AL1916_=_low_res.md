What video controller is in the computer?  The terminal command lspci will display it on the line that says, "VGA controller."

Did you go to Administration/Hardware Drivers after installation?

Is there any possibility your brother is booting into "failsafe" mode?

Is Ubuntu correctly detecting the monitor?  Administration/sysinfo will display what has been detected.
