### Post by 2F4U on 2011-11-01
A print spooler usually buffers your printing requests. So, for example, if you print two documents (or two people simultaneously print one document) the print spooler takes both documents and your computer is free to continue with other tasks. Without a print spooler your computer would have to wait until the printer has processed your document(s).
