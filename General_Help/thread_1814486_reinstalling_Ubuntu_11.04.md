### Post by wt8008 on 2011-07-29
If you have separate partitions setup typically: home (/home), root (/), and swap (for virtual memory), you can reinstall Ubuntu while keeping your home folder intact. 

During the install do not format your /home and set your current home partition as the new one. Make sure you note which partition is your home and which is your root, so you do not format the incorrect one.
