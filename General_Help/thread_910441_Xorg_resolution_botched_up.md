### Post by izalac on 2008-09-04
Managed to solve it myself, thanks to this post:
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=910431](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=910431)

I started displayconfig-gtk (didn't know about that...)
I selected the same display I did before (1440x900 flat display), but this time I turned on the "Wide screen" thingie, and it worked.
Manually adjusted sync rates.
