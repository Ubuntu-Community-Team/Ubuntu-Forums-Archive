### Post by doorknob60 on 2011-08-17
You can't use Windows hardware/drivers in Wine, only hardware supported by Linux drivers will work under wine. You might be able to try Virtualbox or something, but don't get your hopes up, I think doing that only works with USB (not PCI), and my USB capture card doesn't work in Virtualbox either. Unless you can find a native driver, you're probably out of luck :(
