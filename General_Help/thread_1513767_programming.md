### Post by QIII on 2010-06-20
Welcome to Ubuntu!  You'll have fun in your new adventure.

Despite persistent rumors, you need not be a programmer to use Linux.  Don't sweat that.

You may eventually want to dig under the hood, make changes, write scripts and (maybe) even do some programming.

For now, I would take a look at the materials presented here:

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=801404](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=801404)

Feel free to ask questions!  That's what we are all here for.

But you might want to post in the Absolute Beginners Talk forum for a while.  Pretty soon, you'll be answering questions for other newcomers.

Happy Ubuntuing!
