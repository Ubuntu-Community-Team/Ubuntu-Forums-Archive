### Post by Rui Pais on 2007-11-21
Hi neonl,
Bad luck nothing seems to work :(
Have you tried vga=ask on boot command line and test all listed resolutions?
 
Just 2 suggestions on websnail's line.
You should try to edit your "modes" line to your monitor specifics, instead of rely on nvidia detection. Note that console works different then the later xorg working mode. 
For some monitors console will work only with one mode/resolution and thats one must have an equivalent usplash resolution. 
Try too with and without a "Virtual" option line with the same resolution specifications.

You can use nvidia-settings to check possible monitor resolutions, and for console/usplash resolution, the previous app that websnail suggested it's a very good one (just a correction, the name it's startupmanager, all in one word).

Another suggestion it's to try comment out the Horizsync and Vertrefresh lines.
Btw, are you sure they correct for your hardware?

Good luck.
