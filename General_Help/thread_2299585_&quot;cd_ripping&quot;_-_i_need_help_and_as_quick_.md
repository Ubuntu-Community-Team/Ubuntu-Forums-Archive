### Post by mcduck on 2015-10-19
K3B should do this, as long as you set the target format to WAV, and then select the "Create Single File"-option. If you want compressed format, you'll have to encode it afterwards.

Or you can just rip+encode to separate tracks with any tool, and then use mp3wrap to join the files together to single track: [http://manpages.ubuntu.com/manpages/vivid/man1/mp3wrap.1.html]("http://manpages.ubuntu.com/manpages/vivid/man1/mp3wrap.1.html")
