### Post by logos34 on 2009-01-19
Never heard of 'AMD Athlon Antec'...Antec is strictly a manufacturer of cases and power supplies.  AMD Athlon is a very good processor.  Get the model # of the AMD Athlon (Processor) and what kind of mobo--the latter's specs will tell you a lot.

I doubt you'll have any problem getting the hard disk to work in the other machine (usually the hdd is the least of the worries). 

But, yes, you could have issues with nvidia graphics card
