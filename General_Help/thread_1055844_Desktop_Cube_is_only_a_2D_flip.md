### Post by gjoellee on 2009-01-31
Go open the Compiz Fusion window, click on "General..." find in one of the tabs you should fine a "Desktop Size" option. Set it to 4 if you want a cube 3 if you want a triangle etc...

---

### Post by Facetious Falcon on 2009-01-31
Or you could increase the number of workspaces by right clicking at the bottom right of the screen and clicking preferences. For it to be a cube it needs 4 workspaces.

---

### Post by falconed7 on 2009-01-31
Thank you! Setting it from the 'General' section worked like a charm.
