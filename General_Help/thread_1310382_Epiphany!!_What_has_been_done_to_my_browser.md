### Post by nortexoid on 2009-11-22
Add the webkit team ppa and the epiphany ppa and you'll at least get an "Open Link in New Tab" option in the right-click menu:

1. [https://launchpad.net/~webkit-team/+archive/ppa](https://launchpad.net/~webkit-team/+archive/ppa)
2. [https://launchpad.net/~webkit-team/+archive/epiphany](https://launchpad.net/~webkit-team/+archive/epiphany)

(Once you update you should have ver. 2.29.1.) You can also open links in new tabs by holding the Ctrl key when you left click a link. I do this on my laptop all the time.

I actually prefer the webkit backend. It has everything I need (almost) with a better rendering engine (than gecko). And it's blazing fast. I admit epiphany isn't perfect yet (which is why I'm forced to occassionally use Firefox) but it's shaping up nicely I think.
