### Post by Chris1274 on 2010-09-24
You can't fix it, some windows open at a fixed size that's just too big for netbook screens. But you can access the lower buttons by holding down alt+left-click on the window, which allows you to grab the window and move it upward so that the lower buttons appear.

### Post by tredegar on 2011-02-06
Just hold down the ALT key, L-click anywhere on the window to be moved, and drag it upwards until you can see the stuff at the bottom of the window. Then release everything. If it is a BIG window, this process can be repeated.

Never failed for me.
