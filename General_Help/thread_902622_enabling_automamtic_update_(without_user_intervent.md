### Post by fooman on 2008-08-27
i believe so.

open synaptic (system > administration > synaptic package manager)

click on the updates tab.  in the bottom section (automatic updates) put a tick in the box for "install security updates without confirmation" or "download all updates in the background"

either one of those should do it.  ...make sure you have enough disc space set aside for any big updates that may come your way.
