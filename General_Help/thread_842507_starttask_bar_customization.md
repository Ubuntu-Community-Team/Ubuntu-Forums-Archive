### Post by Forlong on 2008-06-27
> **jasontu said:**
> Firstly, the and bottom of my desktop cube is beige.  How do I change that?
I reckon you have already ccsm installed, launch it and enable the **Cube Caps** plugin -- there you will be able to customize the caps.
> **jasontu said:**
> Secondly my start/task bars (the bars at top and bottom) are that gray color.  How do I make them look nice and fancy with gradients and whatnot.
Right-click the panel you want to customize, choose Properties and there the Backround tab.
If you want gradients, you'll have to use an image. It's very easy to do in the GIMP.
There are also plenty of images around and you can even use transparent PNGs to get a glassy panel.
Like this: [http://fredericorama.deviantart.com/art/True-Glass-for-Gnome-Panel-50167682](http://fredericorama.deviantart.com/art/True-Glass-for-Gnome-Panel-50167682)
