### Post by AlphaLexman on 2010-08-14
Some cameras have a 'usb' mode and a 'camera' mode. If you take pictures in usb mode, you should be able to 'see' them anywhere (linux, win), but any pics taken in camera mode (sometimes) allows/helps the manufacturer's software automate the download process to your computer. Kind of a 'grandma' mode for the technically challenged.

Try changing to camera mode and use the software (in win) to download the pics.
