### Post by evilkastel on 2010-11-13
unfortunately, the only way is to overwrite the media-* icons in the theme, but as for rhythmbox, it uses also the 24 px. version. So, unless you want a 32 px. panel, (in which case you could overwrite only the actions/32/media-* ) if you want a mono dark panel panflute you also get mono light controls on your player.
