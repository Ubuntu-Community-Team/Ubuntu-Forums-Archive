### Post by Bucky Ball on 2014-10-30
... and on another side note, you have no /swap partition. Why? Add a 2Gb /swap partition at the end of the drive. You now have 222Gb spare which you can either extend your / partition into, or, as suggested by westie, create and NTFS data partition which both OSs can utilise.
