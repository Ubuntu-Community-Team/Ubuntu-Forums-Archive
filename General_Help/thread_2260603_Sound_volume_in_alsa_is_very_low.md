### Post by Dennis N on 2015-01-13
Use Pulse Audio Volume Control. With Alsamixer set at 100 on Master and PCM, Pulse Audio also shows 100% on Output Devices Level, but you can adjust the level even higher  - up to 150%. You can get still more volume from the Playback Tab and adjusting the specific player in use.
