### Post by Impavidus on 2017-12-21
Try a live disk. The computer may be too old to boot from a usb drive, but with a bit of luck it can boot from a Lubuntu live dvd. Lubuntu is the lightest flavour of Ubuntu and most likely to work on an old computer. Removing some dust may allow the computer to work a bit longer.

Alternatively, take the hard drive out of the old computer, put it in an enclosure and plug it into the usb port of a new computer running Ubuntu or some other Linux.
