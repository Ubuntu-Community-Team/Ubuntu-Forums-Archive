### Post by Sorivenul on 2008-12-06
Switching to Fluxbox on that machine wouldn't offer a whole lot of difference in the speed department.  You have a decent amount of RAM, a good processor.  

It also won't change your boot speed, though there are other ways to do this.  Search the forum - there are plenty of threads on this topic.  I'll try to post some links here, soon.

The switch may still be worthwhile, as you could learn a new window manager.  Of the *box window managers, Fluxbox is my favourite.
