### Post by PDA1 on 2011-08-27
How do I change my log in password to a password I want?

I've used System>Preferences>About Me but "the change password" won't change it to what I want it to be.  It says some nonsense like, "old and new passwords are too similar".  

I want my password to be what I want it to be....not what the computer wants.

Any ideas?

---

### Post by snowpine on 2011-08-27
Try the Terminal command:

```
sudo passwd pda1
```

(substitute your actual username for "pda1" obviously)
