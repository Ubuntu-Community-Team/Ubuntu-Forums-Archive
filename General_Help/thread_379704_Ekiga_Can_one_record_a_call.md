### Post by exaudio on 2008-01-24
I have one solution for recording calls you make with Ekiga. You can use Audacity (audacity.sourceforge.net) to record any audio that passes through your mixer / sound card. But, it's not ideal.

The ideal solution would record the media stream as whatever codec the call is using (G.711, PCMu, GSM, etc.). I don't know how to do that though.

FYI, There can be regulatory / legal issues with recording phone calls.
