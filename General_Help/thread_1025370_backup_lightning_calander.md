### Post by zeronothing on 2008-12-30
all you have to do is go into your home directory and press the Ctrl + h. this will show hidden files and folders. you will want to look for the .mozilla-thunderbird directory. This is the directory you need to backup to backup your Lightning Calendar. by backing up the whole directory you backup all of your thunderbird stuff as well. hope this helps...
