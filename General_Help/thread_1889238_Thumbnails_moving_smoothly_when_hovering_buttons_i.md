### Post by DeMus on 2011-12-01
I have found it. It has nothing to do with he effect that takes care of the windows previews, or thumbnails as they are called in KDE. It is the system setting: Application Appearance.
In there choose Style on the left and on the right side TAB Fine Tuning.
In the dropdown bar for Graphical effects I had chosen High Display Resolution and Low CPU. I now chose High CPU and the effect works again like I want it to. Choice Very High CPU doesn't bring that much extra so I don't use that one.
I just wanted to step through all the settings to find the right one. Hdppily the 2nd one was the right one already. Saves me some time.
