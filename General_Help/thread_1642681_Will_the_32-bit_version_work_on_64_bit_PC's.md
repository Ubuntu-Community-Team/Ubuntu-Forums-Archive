### Post by tijuana on 2010-12-11
If your processor is intel or and x86_64 then the 32 bit version will work no problem. If it's a different architecture (like ia64 intel's orphan 64 bit architecture for servers or ppc, used by macs before apple switched to intel) then you need a specialized build. About yor cd not working on your dad's laptop, it more likely that his cd reader is malfunctioning than an architecture problem (after all most consumer pcs nowadays support x86.

Regards!
