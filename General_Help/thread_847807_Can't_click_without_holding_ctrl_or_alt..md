### Post by Exacerbate on 2008-07-02
I was playing around with the advanced desktop effects settings and I believe I screwed something up. My issue is I can't figure out what. I am unable to click without holding down alt or ctrl, if I click without holding them down, it drags the entire window. Can anyone help? Thanks.

---

### Post by Doji on 2008-07-02
Assuming your using CCSM, go to the Move Window plugin and set "Initiate window move" to alt + button1. If it's disabled, things break.

If that doesn't work, check the same thing in the resize plugin. Setting the shortcuts on these two actions behaves strangely for some reason.
