### Post by mb_webguy on 2008-08-06
Try Amarok again.  You can group by almost any combination of attributes.  If I understand you correctly, you want to organize your collection by Album, and within each album, you want the songs sorted by Artist, right?  In Amarok, all you have to do is go to the Group By button and select First Level -> Album and Second Level -> Artist.

### Post by mb_webguy on 2008-08-06
Ah... I just looked up iTune's sorting fields.  Are you talking about sorting by "Album Artist"?  The Album Artist tag is specific to iTunes, and not a part of any container tag specification I'm aware of.  Consequently, you won't find any other media players that recognize or use it.
