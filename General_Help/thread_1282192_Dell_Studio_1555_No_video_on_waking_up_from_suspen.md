### Post by punkybouy on 2009-10-09
I had a similar problem about a year ago with an HP laptop with Nvidia graphics chip.  After much googling the only thing I could point my finger at were the restricted video drivers.  The laptop was a gift and Christmas was right around the corner so I wound up re-installing Windows.  In my case the laptop would suspend just fine but would not come back forcing a power off and on to get it to work. You could try not using the restricted drivers and see if that fixes the issue although if it does it may restrict using special effects.
