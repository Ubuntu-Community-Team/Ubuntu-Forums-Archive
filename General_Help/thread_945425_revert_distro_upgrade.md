For now, the best advice (at least I could give) is to back up any important files, and reinstall from the CD. I doubt a way has been provided yet to successfully downgrade.

It has been my experience that the best way even to upgrade is to download and burn an alternate installation CD and install from that. Distribution upgrades through the update manager have a nasty habit of going awry.
