### Post by An Sanct on 2011-09-19
Okay :) I'm back with some pictures, tell us which one is more accurate for your situation.

The smaller one shows a pencil, and that sign is there, when you edit text (saying: you are typing)

The slightly bigger picture shows a red circle with a white exclamation mark, telling you, that that message will not be delivered.

(note, pictures taken from www, not my screenshots)

If you hit CTRL+O, you get to the options dialog. In the Advanced tab, under Connection, you see a port nuber, this port has to be a port, that is opened and accessible from outside your firewall (your ISP is probably also firewalling you).

Also there is a list of [known issues]("http://blogs.skype.com/garage/2011/04/skype_22_beta_for_linux_with_s.html?cm_mmc=PXBL%7C0700_B6-_-linux-20110406") (your *logout and crash* also on the list)
