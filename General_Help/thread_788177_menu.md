### Post by bmac on 2008-05-10
Right Click on the panel you would like the menu in and select:
"Add to Panel"
an add to panel window will open
Select "Main Menu" and hit the "Add" button
Close "Add to Panel" window.

This will allow you to remove the group in your post image.
It may be necessary to unlock the applets before you can remove or move them from the panel.
Anything you remove can be add back....
You might try a number of the other applets in the Add to Panel - you can always remove them from any panel. You will also may need to unlock them to move them - any applet you add can be moved anywhere in the panel by right clicking on the applet and selecting move.

Hope this helps...
