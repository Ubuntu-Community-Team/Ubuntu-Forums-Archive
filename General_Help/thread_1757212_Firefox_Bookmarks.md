### Post by baltho on 2011-05-13
To get rid with the latest firefox, either go to the page you've bookmarked and left-click the yellow star - sould turn it off - or in the bookmarks menu, select "show all bookmarks", find it, and delete it.
Both of those will leave it in your history, though, which will make it appear in smart-searches anyway. Delete it from history as well and it should vanish, I'm thinking. 
(From a hard-core firefox user, not a developer!!)
