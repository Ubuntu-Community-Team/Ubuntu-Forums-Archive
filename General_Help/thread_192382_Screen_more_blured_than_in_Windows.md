### Post by grsing on 2006-06-10
To change how the fonts are displayed, go to System->Preferences->Font.  Monochrome will completely turn off antialiasing, which should look crisp, but blocky.  Try all of them out (you can go into more variations under the "details" button) and find what suits you best (it might take some time browsing the internet and such to really see if a setting is what you want, it's hard to tell immediately).
