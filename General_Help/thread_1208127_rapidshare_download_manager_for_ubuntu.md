### Post by kostkon on 2009-07-08
Another option is [*FreeRapid*]("http://wordrider.net/freerapid/").

I also recommend [*Tucan*]("http://www.getdeb.net/app/Tucan") because it's more simple to install and use and more lightweight when compared to [*JDownloader*]("http://jdownloader.org/") and *FreeRapid* which are Java-based apps.

But you could try them all and then use whichever suits you the most ;)
