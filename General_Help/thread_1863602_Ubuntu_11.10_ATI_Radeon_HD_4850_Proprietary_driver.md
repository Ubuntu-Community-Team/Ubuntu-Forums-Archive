I have the ATI Radeon HD 4850.  I'm fairly basic Ubuntu user, not extremely skilled.  I made the mistake of goofing around w/ video drivers and installed the ATI driver (ati-driver-installer-11-9-x86.x86_64) driver -- yeah -- don't do that.  I couldn't boot into Ubuntu, finally installed FGLRX from Recovery mode and got into Ubuntu.  The Dash and Compiz stuff was terribly slow until I re-enabled proprietary drivers through jockey.
