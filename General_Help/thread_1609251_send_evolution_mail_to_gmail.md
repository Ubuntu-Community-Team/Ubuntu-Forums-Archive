### Post by ajgreeny on 2010-10-30
The same as any other mail is forwarded to any other email account.

If you want to forward mail that you have not downloaded to evolution you can add other accounts to the gmail account from the gmail settings page.

### Post by vsh3r on 2010-10-30
To forward or CC copies to GMAIL/YAHOO from Evolution you need to click 

Edit->Preferences->Mail Accounts->Account Editor->Defaults

CC or BCC to any address you want.
