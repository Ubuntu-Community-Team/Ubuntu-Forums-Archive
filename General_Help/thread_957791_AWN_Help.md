### Post by brettnak on 2008-10-24
What do you mean when you say 'main menu'?

I don't know if I'm over simplifying this or not, but

```

System->Prefrences->Awn Manager->'Applets' tab->Cairo Main Menu->Activate

```

I'm not sure if that's what you were looking for or not, but it is an ubuntu main menu.
