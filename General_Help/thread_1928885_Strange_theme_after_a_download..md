### Post by Paddy Landau on 2012-02-21
Unfortunately, there is no way we can tell what the download did to your computer. The only *safe* move is to back up your data and do a clean installation.

However, if you are sure that there is no malware on your system, you can change your theme to the default (Ambiance); then try to find the unwanted theme in Synaptic Package Manager or Ubuntu Software Centre and uninstall.
