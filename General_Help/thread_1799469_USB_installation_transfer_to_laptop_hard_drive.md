### Post by ajgreeny on 2011-07-07
If this is a proper install to the usb drive and not a live session, you may be able to do it with **clonezilla** to clone the disk/partitions and then restore to your final destination disk.

As far as I'm aware, there is no simpler way to install from a full partitioned installation.  It may be simpler to just do a new, clean installation using live CD/USB, where you can choose your partition layout;  your decision!
