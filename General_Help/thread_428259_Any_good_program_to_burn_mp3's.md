### Post by Ifaistos on 2007-04-30
Hello

K3b is one of best burning software there is.
You will find it under Applications >> Sound & Video.

When you launch the fist time it will warn you that you do not have installed MP3 support.  This is not "incorporated" in Ubuntu for leagal reasons.

However it tells you what you need to install, and synaptic can help you a lot with that.
If you search for "k3b mp3", you will find the library that is missing.


Hope that helps :)
