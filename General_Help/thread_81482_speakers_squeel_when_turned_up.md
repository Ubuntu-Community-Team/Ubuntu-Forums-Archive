### Post by matthew on 2005-10-24
Is your microphone turned on? 

Right click the volume icon on the taskbar (it looks like a side view of a loudspeaker) and select Open Volume Control. This will bring up the Alsa mixer window. 

Click the Capture tab. make sure the micropone fader has a red x below it. If it does not, left click on the speaker icon below the microphone fader.
