### Post by vruum on 2006-04-01
If you're on a reasonably fast connection, the simplest thing might be to remove the cdrom from the list of installation sources, to do that, open up synaptic and go to settings>repositories(i'm on a localised version, so I'm not sure of the names) and uncheck the cdrom (should be the first entry) close that menu and update your sources (again, not sure about the names, but it's the two arrow circle button in synaptic) and then it should work
