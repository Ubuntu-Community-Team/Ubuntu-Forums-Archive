### Post by fela on 2008-02-26
I'm sure that the reason is that you're not using a compositing manager, like compiz or beryl. If you use Avant Window Navigator, a nasty black square goes round it if you don't have compositing enabled, sounds just like you're widgets. Are desktop effects enabled in System > Preferences > Appearence? (if you use Gutsy)

If it turns out they're not enabled then you know the problem. If they are, I'm afraid I can't help you any further, you could post your problem up at forum.compiz-fusion.org if you use compiz fusion. Keep me posted... :)
