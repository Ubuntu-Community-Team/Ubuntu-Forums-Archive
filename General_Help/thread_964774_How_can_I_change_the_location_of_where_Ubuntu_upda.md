### Post by riahc3 on 2008-10-31
How can I change the location of where Ubuntu updates/downloads its files/updates? Example, right now its downloading from a [http://us](http://us). server and I would (for example) like it to download from a [http://uk](http://uk). server. Where and how do I change that?

---

### Post by cyfur01 on 2008-10-31
System->Administration->Software Sources

Change the "download From" option. Select "Other..." for a larger list.
