### Post by opentux on 2012-03-19
Ancient thread, but perhaps I can still help those who (like me) stumble across it with the same problem.

1. Right click on the torrent and select "Verify Local Data"
2. When that completes (there will be no obvious indication that it has) select the torrent and press the resume button (big green triangle)
