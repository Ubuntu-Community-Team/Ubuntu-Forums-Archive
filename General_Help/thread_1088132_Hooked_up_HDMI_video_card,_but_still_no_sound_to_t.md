### Post by Psyrix on 2009-03-06
Now if your LCD is model # LN46A750 then go here
[Manual in PDF Format.]("http://http://downloadcenter.samsung.com/content/UM/200811/20081113100634343/BN68-01470A-04L02-0903.pdf")
go to Page 15 "Connecting a PC" > "Using the HDMI/DVI cable" to show how to hook up ur video with audio.
and page 28 "Setting Screen Size" and from what ur manual says, it looks like u need to set ur HDMI # to "Just Scan"
