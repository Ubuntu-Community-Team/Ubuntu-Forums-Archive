### Post by oldrocker99 on 2018-08-03
When you download the .iso, copy and paste, in a terminal or text editor, the SHA256SUM number, and then follow these instructions: [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HowToSHA256SUM](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HowToSHA256SUM).

This is the very best way to verify that the download is good.

---

### Post by Autodave on 2018-08-03
Also, burn your DVD at a very slow speed: I usually shoot for nothing higher than 50% of the speed of the drive.
