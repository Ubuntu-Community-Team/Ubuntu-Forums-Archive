### Post by Therion on 2008-11-18
You can turn of the **Snapping Windows** plugin and see if that does what you want, but there's also a subsetting of the Wobbly Windows plugin called: **Snap Inverted**.  You may, or may not, want to clear the checkbox for that option as well.
