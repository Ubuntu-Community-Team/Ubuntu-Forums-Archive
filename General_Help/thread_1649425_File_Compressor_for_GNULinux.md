### Post by a-user on 2010-12-20
the most used compressors on unix/linux systems is gzip and bzip2. gzip should be already installed on any linux distribution by default and bzip2 usually too.

bzip2 is the better one.

if you want anything better then get 7z.

zip and rar is also available, although i don't know why anyone would use them as they are worse, except for decompressing files got from other sources.
