### Post by ewankho on 2008-11-18
You can download more than one video at a time by changing the preference. Right click on the DownloadHelper icon then select Preference, then change the download mode.

### Post by 3rdalbum on 2008-11-18
When you go to a page with Flash video, and you get the video to buffer to the end (press Play and then Pause), the video downloads to your computer under the /tmp directory. You can't miss it.

Once the video has finished buffering, you can simply drag it to a different location and rename it. You can download multiple videos at a time and they will appear in /tmp.
