### Post by CaptainMark on 2014-03-12
No that's not possible, ipod wont be accepted as sound output hardware. 

If you can scrape a bit of pocket money together you can buy USB sound adapters on ebay quite cheaply, a while back when my pc was brand new and the default linux kernel didnt have drivers for my onboard sound I bought this one [http://www.ebay.co.uk/itm/7-1-Channel-USB-External-Sound-Card-Audio-Adapter-/141160367401?pt=UK_Computing_ComputerComponents_SoundCards&hash=item20ddd04529](http://www.ebay.co.uk/itm/7-1-Channel-USB-External-Sound-Card-Audio-Adapter-/141160367401?pt=UK_Computing_ComputerComponents_SoundCards&hash=item20ddd04529) and it worked out-of-the-box, just plugged in and it popped up in sound settings. This is the equivalent of about $3.50 in america, depending on where you are of course

There are even cheaper ones on ebay which will probably work just as well too, just search for "usb sound card".
