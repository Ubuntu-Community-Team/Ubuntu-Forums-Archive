### Post by Hanj on 2006-04-14
Superkaramba is just an application to show desktop widgets (clocks, calendars and such) and does not really do anything by itself. To display anything, you need to download widgets (called "themes"), for instance from kde-look.org. Then follow [these]("http://www.kde-look.org/help/index.php?type=38") instructions to install the theme. Hope that helps!
