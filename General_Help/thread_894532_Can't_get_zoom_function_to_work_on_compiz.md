### Post by AlexBellisBrown on 2008-08-19
Okay, Open Compiz, and got to the uncatogorized section, in there, you should see the Accesability section. In that section, youll see "enhanced zoom desktop". First, check, is it ticked? If yes, click it, and open the specific zoom tab. It should say something like "zoom specific level 1" and to the right, the keystrokes you should do to get it to zoom. Change them if you like, or just try them keys for now, to see if thats the problem! :) Hope this helps!
