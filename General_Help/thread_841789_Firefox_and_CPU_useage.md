### Post by p_quarles on 2008-06-27
> **waapwoop1 said:**
> Is there another flash i can install where this problem doesn't happen?
Heheh, you are certainly not the first person to ask.

The standard Flash player -- the one put out by Adobe -- is the best, insofar as its compatible with the most Flash content on the web. The Gnash project is attempting to create an open-source alternative, and it does a good job, but it's not 100% compatible with everything on the web yet (far from it). 

Anyway, go ahead and give both the Gnash plugin as well as the swfdec-mozilla plugin a try. You may find that one or both of them suit your purposes, but if you visit a lot of Flash sites, you will most likely need the Adobe one.
