HE Prof Usamah Bin Sadiq Tayeb, KAU President, has launched a new programme, which will allow KAU academic staff to apply for attending conferences and lectures electronically. 

Prof Abdullah Bin Omer Bafil, Vice President for Postgraduate Studies and Scientific Research, said the programme was being introduced to simplify and shorten the procedures KAU academic staff used to follow to apply for attending conferences and lectures.

Prof Bafil also highlighted the process of electronically receiving applications for attendance via the University's main website.
