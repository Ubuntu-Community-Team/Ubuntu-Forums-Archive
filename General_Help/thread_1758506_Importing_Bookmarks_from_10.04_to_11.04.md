### Post by bab1 on 2011-05-14
> **ksuorion said:**
> Is there a way to copy/import the bookmarks from the 'old' filesystem?  Or, as I fear, just stuck re-creating them?

Sure you can.  From Firefox: click on Bookmarks>>Organize Bookmarks>>Import and Backup>>Import HTML>>Next -- now browse for the bookmarks.html file you created.
