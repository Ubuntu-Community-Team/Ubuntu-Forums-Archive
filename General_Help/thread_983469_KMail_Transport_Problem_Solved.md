### Post by hoe on 2008-11-15
I'm running 8.10! What a frustration, l couldn't send mail out of KMail ... transport problem. I finally went into /home/wayne/.kde/share/apps and removed the kmail folder and then reinstalled kmail through synaptic and resolved the problem. Just doing an uninstall and reinstall didn't work ... l had to manual move it out of /home/wayne/.kde/share/apps and put it on my desktop before reinstalling kmail. By moving it onto the desktop l was able to retrieve mail from my old Outbox and bring them into my new Outbox so l could rescue and send them.

Hope this helps someone!
