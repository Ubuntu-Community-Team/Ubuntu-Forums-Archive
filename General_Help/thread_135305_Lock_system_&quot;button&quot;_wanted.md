### Post by dmh-bidlah on 2006-02-23
Just right-click on the panel and select "Add to Panel".
Enter "Lock" to the searchbar. There should be a "Lock Screen" there with a padlock icon. :) 
I configured Ubuntu to lock the screen when I press the "Sleep" key on my keyboard. Works great!

### Post by cjm5229 on 2006-02-23
Look at the menu under system, you should see a lock button there. You can drag and drop it where ever you want it. Or you can right click an empty spot on either toolbar and click "Add", pick what you want from the selection and click add.
