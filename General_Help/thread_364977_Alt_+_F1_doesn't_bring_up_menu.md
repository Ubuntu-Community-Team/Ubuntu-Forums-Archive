### Post by mb26 on 2007-02-27
i found a work-around to bring up the menu when u have no mouse support.

ctrk+alt+tab, select the toolbar which has the menu on
press tab until the menu is highlighted
press enter

then u can turn on the mouse-keys function by going to keyboard > accessibility.
