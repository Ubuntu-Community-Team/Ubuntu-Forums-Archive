### Post by nicks27 on 2011-05-11
This was giving me problems in Xubuntu 11.04, but I eventually figured out that the "xfce4-xkb-plugin" package was not installed. After installing it the "Keyboard Layouts" item became available to add to the desktop panel. Of course this solution is specific to xfce/xubuntu.
