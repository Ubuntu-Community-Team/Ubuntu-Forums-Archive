### Post by Frogs Hair on 2011-05-25
The theme in the second shot is a Windows theme. To get a transparent window border you would have to use Emerald and it would still look different than the Windows Aero theme. In Gnome , Firefox will draw something similar to your GTK theme unless you install a Firefox a theme. You would still need Emerald to get transparency .

### Post by lithopsian on 2011-05-25
You just want the tabs above the URL bar?  You need to check tabs on top.  You will find this by right-clicking on a blank part of any toolbar such as the tabs bar.
