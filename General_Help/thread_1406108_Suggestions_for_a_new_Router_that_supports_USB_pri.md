### Post by nothingface1 on 2010-02-13
I would strongly recommend the [ASUS WL-500g Premium V2]("http://www.newegg.com/Product/Product.aspx?Item=N82E16833320030") with the [DD-WRT Firmware]("http://www.dd-wrt.com/wiki/index.php/WL500G_Premium_v2") installed. You can literally turn a $60 router into a $600 powerhouse! No kidding, by flashing this router the the proper firmware you can unleash all kinds of options that you could only dream of before. Check it out, i use it and it works great, and yeah i have a printer plugged into a usb port on the router along with a usb hard drive and it works flawlessly. Hope this helps.
