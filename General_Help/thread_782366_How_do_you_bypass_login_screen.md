In Kmenu got to 'System Settings' click the 'Advanced' tab then choose 'Login Manager'. Click the 'Administrator Mode' button, type you password then go to the tab marked 'Convenience' and update the 'Auto-Login' for your user account. 

I highly advise against doing this unless you are absolutely positive that you will be the only person using this machine.
