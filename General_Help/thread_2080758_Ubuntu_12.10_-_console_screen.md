### Post by angry_johnnie on 2012-11-04
There's a terminal emulator, as in every other Linux distribution.  You can either go to the dash and type "terminal" or press alt + f2 and type gnome-terminal.

Other than that, you could press ctrl + alt + F 1-6 and get a tty. ctrl + alt + f7 will get you back on graphical interface.
