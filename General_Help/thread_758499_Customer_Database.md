### Post by razta on 2008-04-18
Do you need the customers to access the infromation or just your employees? I would recommend setting up the latest version of Apache for the webserver and MySQL for the databse, then hiring some one to do the website for you, find a student that can do it cheaply.

Depending on which country your in you will have to register to your data commisioner to ensure your keeping in accordance with the Data Protection Act before storing any customers data.

If its just for your employees to access the database then keep the website on a secure internal intranet.
