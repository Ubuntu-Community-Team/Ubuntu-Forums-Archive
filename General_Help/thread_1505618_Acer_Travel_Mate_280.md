### Post by dino99 on 2010-06-09
so all the cd fail to load ?

try booting by adding on of these words on the boot line (remove quiet splash to see comments):

acpi=off and/or apic=off, lapic=off, nomodeset, irqpoll

or holding a key down when booting

or try with a older distro (8.04 for example)
