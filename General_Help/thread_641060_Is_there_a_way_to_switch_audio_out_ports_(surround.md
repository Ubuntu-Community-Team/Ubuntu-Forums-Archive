### Post by tgalati4 on 2007-12-15
Depending on your sound chip, it's possible.  I know the intel on-board sound chips are reassignable (at the hardware level) but I have yet to see a configuration option that allows changing of existing 5.1 sound ports.  Normally you can turn digital signals on and off, and sometimes you can change behavior of microphone and line-in ports.

My front panel microphone and headphone jack are swapped.  No big deal.  I would need to change the pin connections on the header or move the decals around.  Or find a software config switch.
