### Post by akand074 on 2011-08-26
Yes go to the top right power button, choose logout. Then click logout. You'll be back to where you would be when you first boot where you choose which user. Pick your user and then at the bottom you'll see "Ubuntu" in a scroll bar. Scroll to "Ubuntu Classic" and choose that. You have to click on your user first, but don't put your password before changing to Ubuntu Classic.
