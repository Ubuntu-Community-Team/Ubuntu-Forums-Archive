### Post by brossatscu on 2006-05-12
I'm new to Ubuntu but have been with Linux for about 5 months. I have two sound cards installed on my computer:

-Onboard nForce 2
-Sound Blaster 512

Because I've had problems with the nForce card, I only use the Sound Blaster one. When Gnome goes to use my keyboard hotkeys to control the volume, the message pops up and chages the volume and mutes correctly, however the sound card does not respond and change the volume coming out my speakers. My guess is that its controlling the nForce audio device rather then the Sound Blaster one. I have set my Sound Blaster card as default under GNOME sound properties and for the slider in the top-right next to the clock.

Any Ideas? Thanks for the help in advance.

---

### Post by brossatscu on 2006-05-12
While not the best way to fix the problem, I did find a work-around. By disabling the other card in the BIOS, Ubuntu defaults to the right card.
