### Post by squaregoldfish on 2009-05-17
You can use [Bibus]("http://bibus-biblio.sourceforge.net/wiki/index.php/Main_Page") for references, which will happily talk to OpenOffice for inserting citations and building the bibliography. The style can be customised, and I believe it comes with an APA option.

I don't know if APA requires a specific template, but if you can download one for Office the chances are it'll work with OpenOffice too.
