As to why mp3 support is not included by default, this has to do with paying licensing fees which would violate Ubuntu's "always free" philosophy. I really don't see this being a problem provided the user spend a couple seconds doing an apt-get w32codecs or whatever package it is.

Regardless, its been discussed ad nauseum on the forums and I encourage to read up on the lively debates on this subject.
