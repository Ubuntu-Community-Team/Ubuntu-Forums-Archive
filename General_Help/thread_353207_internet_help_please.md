Firefox is the default browser for Ubuntu, so I think you won't have to mess with anything there.

To set up a mail account, you would have to click on the little envelope on the top of your screen. If you don't see it there, click on the applications menu, then go to internet, and click on evolution mail. When you do that, a "first time" wizard will appear and assist you in setting up your mail account.

With ubuntu you don't need, nor can you use, the broadband software that is on your disk. If your internet worked fine with windows, it will work fine (or possibly better with ubuntu).

I hope this was the information you were looking for.
