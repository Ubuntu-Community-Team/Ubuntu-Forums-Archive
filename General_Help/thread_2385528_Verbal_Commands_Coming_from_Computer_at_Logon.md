### Post by #&amp;thj^% on 2018-02-21
Pressing AltSuperS will disable or enable speech dispatcher.

The last thing you should hear is "screen reader off" :-

BTW The Super key is the "windows" key.

You can also, when logged in Go to Settings > Universal Access > Screen Reader > Off/On
