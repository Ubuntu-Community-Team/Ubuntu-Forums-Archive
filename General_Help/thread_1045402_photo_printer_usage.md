### Post by timcredible on 2009-01-20
well, i have an hp psc 5250, and i got openoffice to print to the cd/dvd tray ok, but i haven't figured out how to get borderless printing to work.  however, i print 99% of my photos at online places because it's a LOT cheaper, and the photos last longer.  for editing photos, i usually use digikam, which has everything you need except if you want to cut part of a photo out and work on it separately.  gimp is great, but was a little difficult for me to learn.  as for cameras, they pretty much all work anymore, but you may have to change the usb mode on it if it doesn't work first try.
