### Post by porterejohn on 2011-05-03
Go over to the launcher bar on the left of the screen. You should see a magnifying glass with a + symbol in it. Click it and it will bring up a list of your installed applications. The one below it will be a magnifying glass with a picture of a page in it. Click it to open your files and folders browser. Alternatively you can click the icon in the top left corner and type the name of the program you're lookin for and an icon for it should come up. type terminal into it to get the terminal. Or use the Ctrl + Alt + T keyboard combo. Don't give up on unity, its good when you get used to it.
