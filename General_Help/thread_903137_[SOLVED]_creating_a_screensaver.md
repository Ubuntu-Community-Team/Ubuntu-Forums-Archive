### Post by bmac on 2008-08-28
Place the image file in your home directory (don't care where - just remember where).

Right click on any blank area of your screen (not panel) and select "Change Desktop Background".
The "Appearance Preferences" should open and be on the "Background Tab"
Left click on the "ADD" button and find your image file - double click on your image file and close the "Appearance Preferences" window.....

I actually have a folder in my home directory labeled "Wallpaper" where I keep all my wallpaper - makes it easier to add and remove.....

Hope this helps.....
