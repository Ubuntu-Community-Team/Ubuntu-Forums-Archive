### Post by matt_symes on 2010-11-02
Hi

You could boot from the live CD, mount your hard drive and have a root through the logs /var/log. Or try to log onto the recovery console and check them. They might be able to shed some light on what is happening (because it could be a number of things).

Can you logon to the console or do you still get the same issue?

I would considering downloading the release version of 10.10 and performing a fresh install. Updating can be problematic and 10.10 beta was a beta. There could be fud on your system from that and it may be causing your issue.
Its a pain i know but it could well fix the issue. If you go down this route create an extra partition for you data and keep it there. Makes upgrading less painful.

Kind regards
