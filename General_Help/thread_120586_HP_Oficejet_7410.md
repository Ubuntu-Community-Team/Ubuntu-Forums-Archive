I bought an OfficeJet 7410 yesterday, and it works just fine under Feisty.

It didn't give me any hassle setting it up. All I had to do was go to System -> Administration -> Printing -> New Printer and click Next a few times. It even recognized the printer and suggested the OfficeJet 7400 driver, which I then used.
Scanning worked out-of-the-box as well.

Are you guys using Feisty? If not, maybe you should give it a try.
