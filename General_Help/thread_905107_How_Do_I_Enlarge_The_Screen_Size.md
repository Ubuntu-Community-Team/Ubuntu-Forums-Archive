### Post by nolster12 on 2008-08-30
I have kept the fonts the normal size.  I find that when you mess with making the fonts larger then you will run into problems with things displaying correctly.  

Instead I use the windows key (super key) and the scroll button on the mouse.  This will magnify the entire screen and keep the cursor in centered.  That way after reading you can zoom back out and all of the spacing and placement will be correct.
