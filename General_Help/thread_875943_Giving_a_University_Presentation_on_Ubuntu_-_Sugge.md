### Post by Diabolis on 2008-07-31
3 points that I would mention:

1.- security.
We don't need firewalls!

2.- open source.
We don't have to pay for software, everything is just one "apt-get install" away from us.

3.- customability.
You can stick with gnome while you get accustomed to Ubuntu and then you can modify and do whatever you want with it.

As I tell to my friends:
In windows you'll do with your computer what the system will let you, with linux, your computer will do anything you want.
