### Post by JeSTeR7 on 2009-10-31
Open nautilus (or just open your home directory) and go to Edit=>Preferences.  Choose the display tab and it will allow you to show what's listed under the icons.  I assume for your date to be showing, you've zoomed in quite a bit, but either way, this should take care of the problem for you.
