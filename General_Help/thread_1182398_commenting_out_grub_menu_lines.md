The grub menu contains lines that are commented out with a # or ##.  Does it make any difference it one or two # are used?

---

### Post by synapsys on 2009-06-08
No. Everything after the first # is ignored. Usually one # is used to comment out actual options while two ## are used to comment out text or explanations.
