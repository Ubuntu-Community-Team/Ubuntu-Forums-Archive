### Post by mrboojive on 2009-11-06
> **wrgb2 said:**
> Open Office word processor can export .ODT or .DOC files to pdf.  However, it can't open an existing document for editing.

Actually, you can import PDF files into OpenOffice Draw using the PDF import extension: [http://extensions.services.openoffice.org/project/pdfimport](http://extensions.services.openoffice.org/project/pdfimport), also available in the Ubuntu package openoffice.org-pdfimport. It's not perfect and it doesn't turn them into .DOC files, but it works reasonably well.
