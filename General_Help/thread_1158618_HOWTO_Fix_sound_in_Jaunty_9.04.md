Hey guys.
I've read some posts recently, talking about their's no sound in Jaunty. Well I have found the solution.
```
sudo apt-get aumix
```
Once it's done, you should get something like this picture;
You'll see a Vol bar that looks like a ruler. That's your sound, turn it all the way to the right using the right-arrow key. 
That's it! You now have sound.
