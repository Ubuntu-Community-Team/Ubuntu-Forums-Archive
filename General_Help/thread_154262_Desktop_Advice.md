### Post by Sutekh on 2006-04-02
You could move the top bar (menu and taskbar) right to the bottom and make it an auto-hide panel.  Then move the bar already there (big icons) up to accomodate it.

Hopefully when your mouse is away from the bottom bar (the menu and task bar) it hides looking nice and clean, but when you do move over the space it pops up without messing with the other bar.  

Hope that makes sense.  

It will look kind of strange when windows are maximised as there will be large chunks of desktop showing down the bottom.  I guess you can only experiment and see.



Alternatively you could create *two* new bars and put them either side of the large bar at the bottom.  You could make them auto-hiding too.  Put the application menu on one and the window list and notification area on the other.  I'll try to GIMP your screenshot so you can see what I mean.
