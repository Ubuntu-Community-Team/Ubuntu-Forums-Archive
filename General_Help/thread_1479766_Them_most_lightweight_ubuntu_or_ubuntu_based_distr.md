If you want to try out some non-Ubuntu distros, you have lots of options. Debian is similar to Ubuntu in most ways, but a little lighter. Some of the lightweight Debian-based distros include AntiX, CrunchBang, sidux xfce, and of course Debian itself (especially with Xfce or LXDE).

For non-Debian distros, Puppy and SliTaz are worth a look. SliTaz is probably the fastest of the bunch, if your hardware is really old...
