### Post by kryptikos on 2009-04-08
As a suggestion, when you come to the forums looking for assistance you need to approach the community in a positive and genuine way. Demanding statements or posts that do not include much information will most likely get ignored.

Scheduling is handled by the cron daemon. You can read the man pages by running "man cron" from the terminal. 

Also you can check this link for an explanation of how cron works:

[http://www.unixgeeks.org/security/newbie/unix/cron-1.html]("http://www.unixgeeks.org/security/newbie/unix/cron-1.html")
