### Post by mb_webguy on 2009-02-22
No, as long as you upgrade, rather than installing the new version over your current version, you'll keep all of your applications, settings, and data.

Edit:  [This page]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/IntrepidUpgrades") talks about upgrading from Hardy to Intrepid, but the majority of the information will be true of upgrading from Intrepid to Jaunty.
