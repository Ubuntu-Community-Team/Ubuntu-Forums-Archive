### Post by blur xc on 2009-10-26
I just noticed last Thursday there was an update that replaced the /boot/grub/menu.lst file, which removed vista from the boot menu.  According to the time stamps, it appears as though it was kind enough to back up my old menu.lst to menu.lst~, so I just copied and pasted those lines back into my new menu.lst.

I didn't notice right away because we don't reboot every day.

It was an easy fix, just thought I'd post in case someone else runs into the same problem.
