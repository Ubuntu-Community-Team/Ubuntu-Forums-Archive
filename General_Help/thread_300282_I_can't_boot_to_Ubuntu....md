### Post by the ev on 2006-11-15
Whoa - I just [posted]("http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=300520") something that might be related to this before I read your post.

I had the same problem, and I somehow discovered my monitor was causing the problem (Apple Cinema Display), 'cause I had my mouse and keyboard plugged into it (it's a USB hub) and not the tower.  I put my mouse and keyboard directly into the tower and unplugged the USB connection to my monitor and...Ubuntu no longer hangs on soft boots like you describe. 

I dunno if you have anything similar to this setup, but I'd try playing musical chairs with your USB periphrials and see where that gets ya.  I've no idea *why* this might work, I only know that it did for me. ;)
