### Post by hawthornso23 on 2010-12-04
These kinds of symptoms can be a sign of 
1. Graphics card and/or driver problems. 
2. Overheating (cleaning your fans can help).
3. Inadequate voltage from the power supply.

After a crash what do you see in dmesg?
Can you get into a terminal via ctrl-alt-F1?
