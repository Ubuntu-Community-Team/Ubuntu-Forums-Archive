### Post by jaygo on 2010-08-23
I have the same issue. Turns out it's a "wontfix" with GNOME at this point:

[https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/gnome-terminal/+bug/162000]("https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/gnome-terminal/+bug/162000")
[https://bugzilla.gnome.org/show_bug.cgi?id=123994]("https://bugzilla.gnome.org/show_bug.cgi?id=123994")
[http://mail.gnome.org/archives/desktop-devel-list/2009-November/msg00220.html]("http://mail.gnome.org/archives/desktop-devel-list/2009-November/msg00220.html")

For now, CTRL + pageup/pagedown will do what you're looking for, but you'll have to move your right hand off of your mouse, trackpad, or keyboard to use it.
