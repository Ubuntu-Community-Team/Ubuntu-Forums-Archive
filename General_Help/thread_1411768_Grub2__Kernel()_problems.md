### Post by PoHandle on 2010-02-25
Silly me.  It turns out that this was in fact two separate problems which were both caused by one update.  As it turns out, something in that update caused **recordfail** to be set to 1 in grubenv, and was in turn causing the Grub menu to display indefinitely.  Regarding the kernel issue, the update seemed to have done something funny with dkms, making me rebuild any drivers/modules after every reboot.

The Grub issue was fixed with
```
grub-editenv /boot/grub/grubenv unset recordfail
```
and the kernel issue was solved by reinstalling dkms.
