### Post by apethedog on 2007-09-20
You could check out with htop, when your system has crawled to a halt, where the slowdown is exactly and what causes it. (Maybe it is your memory that is full, maybe your processor can't handle it anymore. Maybe your hard disk is very full).

I've used azureus, although on windows, and it would slow my system down tremendously as well. Using µtorrent helped me in that department - it's a very lean and resource-unintensive program. There is no linux version for it, that I know of, though. 

Personally, I'm using KTorrent and Deluge and find them both to be doing a really good job.
