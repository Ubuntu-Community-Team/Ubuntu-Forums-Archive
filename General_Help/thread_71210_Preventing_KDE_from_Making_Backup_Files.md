### Post by claydoh on 2005-10-02
for Kword:
go to Settings/Configure Kword, then go to the "Document" menu and uncheck the relevent box

for kwrite and kate
Settings/Configure Editor, then to "Open/Save"

for kedit, it is under the Misc section of the settings menu.
