### Post by UnterUn on 2010-10-08
Hey, That motherboard looks like a perfectly good choice - Plenty of Space to upgrade and a good front side bus speed and can handle a good amount of DDR ram which is always good.

Anyway as far as CPUs goes i would try a Intel i7 875K @ 2.93 GhZ for $350, looking at lower cost there's the Intel i7 950 @3.07GhZ for $300, the Intel i7 930 @ 2.8Ghz for just over $260 and the AMD phenom II X6 1075T for $260. All four of them have 6 or more cores (intel's have 8 cores with a 8M cache). Although to be honest you'd be hard pressed to find a game which requires all 8 cores - 

Also if your looking for pure clock speed, look for AMD's 'Black Edition' processors which are perfect for overclocking - the best of which is likely the AMD Phenom II X6 1090T AM3 9MB 6 core @ 3.2 GhZ  - Black Edition for $350 (You can pick up ones with fewer cores/ slower clock speed for $200-$300 easily).

Either way I'd shop around and some things to bear in mind:
   -  Don't bother with Intel Xeons - Not worth it on single desktops/laptops
   -  Intel i7s Are generally better then AMD
   -  AMD is generally cheaper and
   -  AMD is generally better for over clocking (Black Editions particularly)

Finally check this site out: [http://www.cpubenchmark.net/](http://www.cpubenchmark.net/) - and make sure you have good cooling! (Especially if you start over clocking!)
