### Post by techunit on 2011-02-05
Try removing the drive and then starting the computer via a live cd. If you still have problems it isn't just your harddrive.

You should generally head windows harddrive warnings because they really only happen when your harddrive is immanently going to fail. Ubuntu integrates smart warnings so you will know very far ahead of time if there is a problem.
