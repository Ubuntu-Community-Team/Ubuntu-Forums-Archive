### Post by Giblet5 on 2009-11-12
My displays go to standby if I walk away for 15 minutes.

X sees the monitors and establishes correct resolutions, plus the monitors aren't sitting there gobbling watts and illuminating the cat's nefarious daylight deeds.

The typical monitor will consume about the same amount of power in standby mode as it will when it is "off", despite any claims to the contrary by the 'green conscious' vendor.

I checked this Dell 3007WFPHC and the HP F2304 next to it about a year ago: approx 20ma in standby or "off" for either... That's 2.4 watts at 120VAC.
