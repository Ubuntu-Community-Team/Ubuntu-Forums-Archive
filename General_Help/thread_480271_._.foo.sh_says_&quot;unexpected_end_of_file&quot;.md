### Post by tvst on 2007-06-21
i just copied/pasted your script and it works fine for me over here regardless of how i execute it. (i did a "chmod +x" too, of course, otherwise i wouldn't be able to execute the script with the second command.) 

my file didn't even have a "line 6", only up to 5. so i'm guessing you may have a bad non-printable character or line ending somewhere on that line. maybe try running 

vim --cmd "set ff=unix" foo.sh

to convert the line-endings.
