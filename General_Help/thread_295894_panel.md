### Post by codypumper on 2006-11-08
Well, prepare for lesson on Gnome (your type of desktop)
First up, that weird icon in the bottom left hand corner, when pressed will minimize all pages, very useful.
Second, to get your top panel back right click on the bottom panel -> new panel. Notice there is nothing on the new panel. To add stuff (applets) right click on your new panel and select add to panel.
