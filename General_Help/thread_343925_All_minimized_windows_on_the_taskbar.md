### Post by Forge42 on 2007-01-22
I've recently developed a small problem that I can't seem to fix.

Following the recent Beryl update, I noticed that all of my windows from all desktops were appearing in the taskbar on all of my desktops.  In other words, if I opened Firefox on desktop 1 and then switched to desktop 2, I could still see the taskbar button for the first firefox window in the taskbar.

Perhaps this is intentional, but I don't want this behavior.  I only want the windows on the workspace I'm looking at to appear in the taskbar.  I've searched and searched and can't find a Beryl setting that seems to be causing it.  Honestly, I'm not positive that is what's doing it...  it was just after that update that I started noticing it.  Can anyone help me out?

EDIT:
Nevermind... managed to figure it out.  I noticed during a mouse rotate (I usually just use an edge flip) that somehow my number of desktops had been bumped to 6.  I guess what I was talking about is the default behavior when you have more than 4 desktops.  Once I tweaked it back to 4, it fixed itself.  Strange.
