### Post by ronaldbrijo on 2012-04-12
Hey,

In catalyst you need to go to the display/monitor option, and look at the scaling options(youll need to select the monitor that has the black bars if you have more than one monitor).

Youll find that it is set to underscan by 15%. Just move the slider to 0% apply and presto... No more black bars...
