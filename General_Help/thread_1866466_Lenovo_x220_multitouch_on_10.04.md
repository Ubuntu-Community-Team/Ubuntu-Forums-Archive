### Post by Favux on 2011-10-21
You'll need to update your Wacom drivers to get multitouch.  The default Wacom drivers in Lucid do not support your model Thinkpad.  I would clone input-wacom and xf86-input-wacom.  See the [BambooPT HOW TO]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1515562") or the [Linux Wacom HOW TO]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1038949") for instructions.
