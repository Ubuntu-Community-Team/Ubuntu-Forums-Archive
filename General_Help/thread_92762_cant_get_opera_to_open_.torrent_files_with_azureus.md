### Post by timczer on 2005-11-20
Under tools-preferences-advanced tab, in left menu click on downloads.  In the right hand box, look for application/x-bittorrent with file extension torrent.  If not there, add it.  If already there, click edit and it brings up the menu for downloading.  Choose open with other program, and browse to /usr/bin/azureus ( or where ever you have it).  That should work.
