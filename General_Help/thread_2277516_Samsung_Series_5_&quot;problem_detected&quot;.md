### Post by trag on 2015-05-10
It may be a false positive, these warning came up for me with no actual problems presenting themselves so I just turned off the notices.If you feel this is a solution you want to explore here is how to do it.
  gksu gedit /etc/default/apportchange "enabled" from "1" to "0", then save the file.
sudo service apport stop
