### Post by ivarn on 2010-10-21
For all cool looking things you can check out [www.gnome-look.org](www.gnome-look.org). you'll find everything from themes, screenlets, desklets, icons, cursors, wallpapers and much more great stuff you can design and customize everything with.
To add or remove panels, just right click on one of them and you'll have the options "new panel" , "new panel", "add to panel" etc. and now when you have a new panel you can add things to your panel.
