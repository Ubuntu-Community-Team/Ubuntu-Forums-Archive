### Post by justsomedude on 2008-03-09
Let's do this the easy way. :)

Go to Applications --> Add/Remove

Set "Show" to "All available Applications" and enter "cairo" in the search field.

Cairo Clock will pop up. Mark it and hit "Apply Changes".

You will have to enter your password.

There you go.

You can find your clock under Applications --> Accessories.
