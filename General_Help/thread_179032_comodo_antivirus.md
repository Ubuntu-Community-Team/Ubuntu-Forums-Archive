### Post by Sef on 2006-05-19
> is comodo antivirus and Firewall available for Linux. 

No, they are not.  However, GNU/Linux does not need a anti-virus unless you have an email client.  If you have one, a virus may sit on your system and do you no harm, but it would infect your friends who are using Microsoft Windows.

As for a firewall, there is Firestarter.  It is available in Synaptic.  (System > Administration > Synaptic Package Manager > Search > Firestarter)
