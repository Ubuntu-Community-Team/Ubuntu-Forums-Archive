### Post by tommcd on 2006-05-12
To play video files consecutively, open totem, then click movie>open and browse to your video files. Build a playlist by clicking the files and hold down CTR, then click open to play. VLC playlists are similar. Go through:
[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/RestrictedFormats](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/RestrictedFormats) 
to make sure you have all the codecs. If all else fails, I guess you could try reinstalling totem and VLC again via synaptic. Hope that helps.
