### Post by geo909 on 2008-02-09
It's been a long time since I solved this problem and I will just write the answer down for the potential benefit of others asking the same question:

Open DVDShrink and go:
Edit->Preferences->Preview

Go to: "Select DirectX Video Renderer" and choose "Built in software renderer" from the scroll down 
menu.

 You should have preview then. Note that the preview quality may be bad, with bad sound/video synchronization for example. 
 But it's not about watching a movie, it's about seeing what each chapter exactly is. You will definitely be able to do that with this preview.

 Hope that helps someone someday...
