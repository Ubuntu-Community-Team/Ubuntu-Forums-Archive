### Post by noway on 2008-12-12
Live Messenger doesn't seem to work in Wine ([http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=version&iId=9426&iTestingId=16377](http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=version&iId=9426&iTestingId=16377)) 

Your best bet is either Pidgin (which comes with Ubuntu) or installing aMSN. aMSN handles more MSN features than Pidgin but you can use Pidgin for other protocols too. 

The MSN protocol is closed that's why it won't work with other clients as good as it does in Live Messenger. 

Jabber is an open protocol, you should check it out [http://www.jabber.org/web/Main_Page](http://www.jabber.org/web/Main_Page)
