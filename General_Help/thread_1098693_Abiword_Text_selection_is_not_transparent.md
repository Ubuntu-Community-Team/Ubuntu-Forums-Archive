### Post by rastignac44 on 2009-03-18
The problem is the ¨MurrinaStormCloud¨. To fix this issue, do the following:

Go to Applications - Settings - Settings Manager
A new window should appear. There, go to ¨User Interface¨.
Change your theme to any other of the supplied Xubuntu themes.
