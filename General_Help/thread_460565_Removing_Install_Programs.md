### Post by Sonix3D on 2007-05-31
Click Applications > Add/Remove

then at the top right where it says "Show:"
Click the drop down box and select "Installed Programs"
Just uncheck the one you don't want and click apply when finished.

All of this is assuming you haven't moved the add/remove button from the applications bar.
