### Post by calmasy on 2010-05-13
The 'current' Nvidia driver that comes with 10.04 appears to be buggy. After upgrading my workstation from Ubuntu 9.10 to 10.04, my Second Life viewer (an OpenGL based 3D virtual world viewer) began freezing up. I was able to fix this by deactivating the 'current' Nvidia driver via the Hardware Drivers panel, and activating version 173 of the driver instead.

This isn't optimal obviously, as I'm now using a much older version of the Nvidia driver, but it allows me at least to use my viewer which is a critical application for my work.

Is the Ubuntu team aware that the 'current' Nvidia driver is buggy/broken? Are there plans to address this?
