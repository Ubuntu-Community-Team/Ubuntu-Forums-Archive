The Core 2 Duo processor can be utilized by the default kernel in Edgy. It supports SMP (Symmetric Multi-Processing) out of the box. For more info, [this thread]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=281459") would be a good place to start. It was the first result of a Google search for "core 2 duo ubuntu kernel" btw.
