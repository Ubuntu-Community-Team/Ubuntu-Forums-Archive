### Post by Paqman on 2012-01-07
The easiest and best converter for Ubuntu is (IMO) [Arista (click to install)]("apt:arista"). It's in the repos, and will convert stuff for a specific device with a couple of clicks.

Tools like Handbrake, ffmpeg and Avidemux are great if you want to very precise control over millions of settings, but for most straightforward transcoding jobs they're ridiculously  overcomplicated.
