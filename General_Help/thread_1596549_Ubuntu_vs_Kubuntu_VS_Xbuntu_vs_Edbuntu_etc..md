### Post by snowpine on 2010-10-14
Welcome to the forums! You ask many questions, each of which is worthy of its own proper discussion. To get the ball rolling, a good first place to look is the [Ubuntu System Requirements]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Installation/SystemRequirements"). Note in particular that Ubuntu supports x86 processors only; so if the iMacs are PowerPC, you'll need to find a different distro (Debian perhaps?).

Your questions about parental control and creating a home media server are both good ones. I will let someone with more experience in these topics answer them. :)

(edit) I stand corrected, apparently there is a PowerPC "port" of Ubuntu: [http://cdimage.ubuntu.com/ports/releases/lucid/release/](http://cdimage.ubuntu.com/ports/releases/lucid/release/)
