### Post by markthefart on 2010-01-19
> **j.s.rood said:**
> Please, How did you solve your problem? I can,t get a sig in my mail either. tia

Evolution > Edit > Preferences > Composer Preferences > Signature tab. Click Add, edit your signature then save it. After that... (this is the part I had previously overlooked)

Evolution > Edit > Preferences. In Mail Accounts, select the account for which you want the signature for. In the account editor, on the Identity tab choose your signature from the list.

Hope that helps!!
