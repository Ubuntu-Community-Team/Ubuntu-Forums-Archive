### Post by DGortze380 on 2008-09-10
> **LingLing1337 said:**
> Excellent idea. Should I do 2x80GB HDDs, one main and one backup? Or just have a backup partition on the single drive?

It's up to you how you want to do it. Unless you're doing a full back (OS included) you can get away with a smaller drive for back-ups. I just used an old drive I had laying around. I tend to avoid keeping backups on the same drive as the originals, defeats the purpose if the drive dies, is accidentally formatted, etc.

Note: this doesn't eliminate the need to manually back up sensitive data. You should always have two back-ups and an original of important data.
