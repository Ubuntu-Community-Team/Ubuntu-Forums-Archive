### Post by Berserker v7 on 2009-04-11
The difference between ubuntu, kubuntu and xubuntu is in the desktop environments they use - gnome, kde, xfce, respectively. The associated applications also change with the the environment.

Edubuntu is just an add-on that can be installed on top of the others and contains some educational software and all.

For pictures, just google!!!
