### Post by Mark Phelps on 2016-05-10
Much as you apparently dislike Win10 (and, you're not alone in this!), I would hesitate to get rid of it until after you have booted Ubuntu in LiveUSB mode and confirmed that all the hardware gets detected and all the right drivers get installed.  If anything does not work, getting that to work can range from trivial to impossible -- depending on the availability of Linux drivers for the hardware in your PC.

Plus, since the new PC will undoubtedly be a combination of UEFI and GPT, you should do some reading on UEFI before it arrives:  [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2147295](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2147295)

Good Luck
