**IDE** was NEVER designed or engineered to be *hot-plug*.
Yes, it CAN be done, but it is **[COLOR="Red"]NOT SAFE[/COLOR]**.
You WILL, sooner or later, damage the drive/data.


SCSI and SATA *are* designed and engineered to be hot-plug.
Not all drives and/adapters adhere to the specs, but the specs are there.
