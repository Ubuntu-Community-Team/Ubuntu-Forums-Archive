### Post by ian-weisser on 2013-12-22
Did you get the torrent from [http://xubuntu.org/getxubuntu/](http://xubuntu.org/getxubuntu/) ?
If you look at a mirror page, it will give you the proper size.

For example, [http://mirror.anl.gov/pub/ubuntu-iso/CDs-Xubuntu/12.04/release/](http://mirror.anl.gov/pub/ubuntu-iso/CDs-Xubuntu/12.04/release/) shows the size of the xubuntu 12.04.3 i386 image to be 693M, and the x64 image to be 712MB.

So I find the only ~900MB to be rather suspect.
Congratulations on your first bad experience with a torrent.
