### Post by 2hot6ft2 on 2010-04-22
[wallpaper-tray]("apt:wallpaper-tray") can do that just click on the name then Ok to install it.
Then right Click on the top panel and select Add to Panel
select Wallpaper Tray and click Add then Close
Right click on it in the panel and select Preferences to set it up how you want it to run. 24 hours would be every 1440 minutes.
Enjoy.
