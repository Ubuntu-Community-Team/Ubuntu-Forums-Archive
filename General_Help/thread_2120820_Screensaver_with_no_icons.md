### Post by unheeding on 2013-02-27
What do you mean no icons?  Do you mean the Unity launcher?  Is the Unity panel also missing?  This can be caused by faulty graphics card drivers.

As for the networking problem, can you open a terminal and type "ping google.com"?  It'll tell you if it is able to reach google's servers, press ctrl+c to end the command.  If you are able to ping but cannot browse with Firefox, it might be an issue with your proxy settings.
