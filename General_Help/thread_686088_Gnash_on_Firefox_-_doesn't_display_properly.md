### Post by petercoh7 on 2008-03-12
> **werg said:**
> With Youtube videos, the nav bar display is choppy. It's cropped from under and the buttons are overlapping. How to fix this? Thanks.

I had this same problem.  I used the package manager to uninstall Gnash then I opened up Firefox, went to the Youtube website, clicked on a video and up popped the plugin dialogue.  This time I picked the first plugin on the list (Adobe).  You have to restart Firefox afterward.  I went back to Youtube and opened up a video.  This time the full plugin was visible with video controls, volume, etc..  Hope that helps !
