To change the default size of your desktop icons
in nautilus goto edit-preferences and change your default zoom level.
This will change the default size of icons on your desktop as well as in your folders.
Size of text can be changed in appearance-fonts-details and change your resolution.

As for some websites text being to small I just use the zoom keys.
Eg in firefox ctrl-+     in opera keypad+
