You do not need any program to download a video from YouTube or any other flash video.  Whenever you are watch a video clip, it downloads a copy to your Temp folder.  You do have to wait for the video to download completely (and do not move away from the web page as it will clear the temporary file) then go to your Temp folder and look for a file with the name Flash+random letters.  Copy the file and paste it into your desired folder and rename.  Done.
