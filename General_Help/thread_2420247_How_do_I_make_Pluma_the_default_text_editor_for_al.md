### Post by deadflowr on 2019-06-01
I simply right click on a text file
go down to properties
go to the* open with* tab.
scroll down to the wanted app then highlight it and click Add.
Then go up to the top of the listings and highlight the app in the Recommended Applications list and click on the *set as default* button.
Then close the properties window.
