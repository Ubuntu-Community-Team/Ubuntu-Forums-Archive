### Post by 3rdalbum on 2009-02-01
Known bug: When creating a window with the Nvidia driver that ships with Ubuntu 8.10, sometimes you get remnants of previous screen images appearing inside the new window area. This is not harmful, unless your other half is in the room and sees a remnant of something you don't want him/her to see! Nvidia's new driver appears to have fixed this.

I can't say I ever observed it in VLC, but it was very apparent when using Flock.
