### Post by wilee-nilee on 2009-11-21
A friend of mine who works on many peoples computers turned me onto the 1st two, basically he said the people who use them and go to the usual dodgy places call him like once a year now rather then all the time. The problem with the big name AV programs is that Trojans..etc are written to disable them so they either don't work at all or appear to work. Also I think a large percentage of people just let them run expecting that they update often they must be working, they don't actually run full scans if something seems strange. These will run along side Kapersky I believe you might try them out to see what they find. Another helpful program is a disc cleaner named ccleaner.
[http://www.ccleaner.com/](http://www.ccleaner.com/) 

I have seen multiple user of MS software just keep going even when their AV throws up a flag, kind of self evident if you don't at the least use them with full capability.
