### Post by t0p on 2009-09-02
System Monitor (**System > Administration > System Monitor**), under its "Resources" tab, shows the Receiving and Sending speed of network traffic.  You can add System Monitor to the panel, by right-clicking on the panel and selecting to Add To Panel.
