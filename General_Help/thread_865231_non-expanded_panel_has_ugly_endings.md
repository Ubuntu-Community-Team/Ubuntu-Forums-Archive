### Post by mcduck on 2008-07-20
Sorry, apart from editing the gnome-panel source code it's not possible to get rid of those ugly things at the ends of unexpanded panels. 

The easiest solution would be to use some other dock, like AWN. Or make the panel expanded, and use a nice picture as panel background to make it look like you had a short panel.

Or actually you could just enable buttons in panel ends, if you keep the button arrows disabled this might look OK, and at least better than the default panel endings.
