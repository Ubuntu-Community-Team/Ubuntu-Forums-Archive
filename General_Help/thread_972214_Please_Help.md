### Post by fragos on 2008-11-05
The simplest way to deal with this is to get a WiFi router and clone the MAC address on your PC that the ISP wants. You will have both wired and wireless ports on the router and all may share the same Internet access. Unless you've reconfigured things otherwise the default network configuration will DCHP will work for all PCs on the LAN.
