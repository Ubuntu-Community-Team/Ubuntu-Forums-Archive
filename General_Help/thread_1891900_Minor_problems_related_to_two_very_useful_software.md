### Post by Costas100 on 2011-12-06
I wish to report a couple of minor problems related to two very useful software items:
1. I am using two languages in my computer and discovered the **kkbswitch** (keyboard switch) which adds a very nice Flag on the panel that indicates the language in use and clicking on the flag you can switch to the next selection and so on. The minor problem is that the flag does not appear all the times on start up, but I found a work around. **I added in the panel a launcher for kkbswitch and when the flag icon is not there, I click on the kkbswitch launcher once and then click where the flag usually appears. After 3 or 4 and at times more clicks, the flag appears and stays on, the rest of the day.** This is not a problem, it is kind of strange, but I can live with that.

2. I installed **Krusader the twin Panel File Manager, it has some neat advantages and it is worth trying it**. But it also has a minor problem. When I first installed it all went fine for a few days. It was starting with the computer startup and only one twin panel was coming up. Slowly over a period of a few days, additional twin panel screens were appearing upon startup of the computer. It finally reached the total of twin panels at start of 18. At this point I removed the Krusader and then reinstalled the Krusader software and all went fine for a few days. Then it started again to add additional twin panels at start and as of today I have 4 twin panels. Again it is not a serious problem, since even at the 18 twin panels it was taking me less than a minute to remove the 17 of them. The good thing about it, is that they were all placed fully superimposed.

I am not asking for a reply, since these are minor problems, **I thought that the information here may be helpful in areas or multiple languages and a special file manager.**

Enjoy those two unusual software

Costas
