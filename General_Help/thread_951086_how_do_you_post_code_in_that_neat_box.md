### Post by steveneddy on 2008-10-18
While posting in the WYSIWYG text input device of the Ubuntu Forums, you will notice some buttons at the top of the space where you type in your statement.

The **# **sign will add the proper coding to make the box.

The **quote bubble** will add quoted text, etc.

I generally input the text, highlight it and then hit the **#** sign at the top of the editor.

Play around with it a little.
