### Post by drs305 on 2010-09-24
There have been quite a few posts of the same nature in the past week or so. I don't think anyone has isolated what the exact problem is.

I've suggested completely purging and reinstalling Grub2 from the LiveCD  and it seems to work. Just make sure you have a reliable Internet connection and power source.

See post #5 of this link on how to chroot into your real Ubuntu partition and complete the purge/reinstall. If you have questions, you can ask here.

In your case, replace **sda5** with **sda1** wherever the former is mentioned.
