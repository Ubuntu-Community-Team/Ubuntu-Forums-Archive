### Post by Tibuda on 2009-06-05
You should not use the password you use to open Gmail, but the password in your [Windows Live]("http://login.live.com/") account. (unless they are the same)
What password do you use to open MSN in Windows? Try to login in [http://login.live.com/](http://login.live.com/)
