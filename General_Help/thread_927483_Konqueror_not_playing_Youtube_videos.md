### Post by mndzmyst on 2009-01-14
I know this thread is a few months old, but I figured out how to play flash videos with konqueror 4.1. Just go to settings==> configure konqueror==>plugins==>plugins tab. Add new directory to search. I added /usr/lib/midbrowser/plugins. Then click on scan for plugins. For some reason I was only able to play flash if I had a flash site loaded in opera or firefox. This seemed to resolve the issue.
