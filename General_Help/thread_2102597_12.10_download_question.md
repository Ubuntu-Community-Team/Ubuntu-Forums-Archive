### Post by jerome1232 on 2013-01-07
12.10 doesn't fit on a cd, it is over 700 MB's. You must use a dvd. You must also burn it as an iso and not simply burn the iso file as a file.
Here is how to do that: [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BurningIsoHowto](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BurningIsoHowto)

### Post by jerome1232 on 2013-01-07
12.04.1 LTS fits on a cd. You can get it from the main site.

[http://www.ubuntu.com/download/desktop](http://www.ubuntu.com/download/desktop)

Scroll down to the "Long-term support" option. It is supported for 5 years where as 12.10 is only supported for 18 months. If you want to keep a version and stick with it for awhile, LTS releases are the way to go.
