### Post by Titan8990 on 2009-02-09
There are both ATI and Nvidia drivers for all recent cards. Also, there is both proprietary and opensource drivers for each. 

I would recommend getting Nvidia because their proprietary drivers are considerably better. ATI has only started to clean up its act as of recently.
