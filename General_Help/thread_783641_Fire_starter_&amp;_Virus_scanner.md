### Post by ramjet_1953 on 2008-05-05
Welcome aboard!

Firestarter is a GUI for your firewall. That firewall is iptables, and unless you need to do anything fancy, like port forwarding, it is best to leave iptables well alone. All ports are closed by default and your system is secure.

Also, unless you are forwarding-on emails with attachments, there is no need for an anti-virus package, either. Even if you are doing this, I doubt whether you need anti-virus anyway, as email providers automatically scan for viruses and if the person who is receiving the email is running Windows, they are very foolish if they don't have an active virus program running.

In short, relax, you are now running an OS that doesn't have anywhere near the security issues that Windows has.
