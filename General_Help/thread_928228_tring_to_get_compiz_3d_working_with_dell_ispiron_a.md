### Post by mwalimu54 on 2008-09-23
In Synaptics Pkg. Man. go to Settings/Repositories and open the Third-Party Software tab.  Make sure both options are ticked.  Then search for "compiz" in the tool bar.  You should get at least a dozen options, one of which should be compizconfig-settings-manager.
