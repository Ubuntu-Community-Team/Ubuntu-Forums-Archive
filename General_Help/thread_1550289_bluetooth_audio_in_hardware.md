### Post by Stardom on 2010-08-11
well, it wass actually quite easy. First of all, blueman doesn't work on lucid so dont bother. All i had to do was pair (which i had already done), click on bluetooth icon in panel, go to my device, hit sound preferences, go to hardware, and choose my bluetooth for connection. Oh, and i believe i installed pulseaudio-bluetooth.
