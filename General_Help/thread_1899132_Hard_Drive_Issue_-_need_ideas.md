### Post by QIII on 2011-12-22
First, before you go through the headache of trying to learn something new (it would be nice to say installing Ubuntu goes easy for everyone, the very fact that there are posts about difficulties in installation belies that) ...

Download one of the Live CD versions suggested here:

[http://www.cgsecurity.org/wiki/TestDisk_Livecd](http://www.cgsecurity.org/wiki/TestDisk_Livecd)

and use it to recover your data.  Photorec should be included.  It can recover a wide variety of file types.

Don't do anything with the HDDs other than have them plugged in and powered on until you start to recover your data.  The more you fiddle with the disks, the more likely you are to destroy data.

Get your stuff recovered and secure first.  That's the big thing.

Then come back and we'll convert you to Linux!
