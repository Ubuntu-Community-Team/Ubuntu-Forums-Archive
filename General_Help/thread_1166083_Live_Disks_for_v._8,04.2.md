### Post by snowpine on 2009-05-21
> **Bigneil said:**
> what would you recommend for a system with less ram?

Depends how much less (and the processor is important, too)... I consider 256mb the minimum for a usable modern Linux desktop. I have two 500mhz Pentium 3 laptops with 256mb of ram. One is running Debian Lenny LXDE and the other SliTaz. They struggle a little bit with flash videos, openoffice, multiple Firefox tabs, etc. but are OK for email and ubuntu forums surfing. 

Below 256mb, I have no personal experience, but I have read about users having success with Puppy and DSL.
