### Post by bedpotato on 2012-04-09
Have you installed the "ubuntu-restricted-extras package" from the software centre?

I am a fellow newbie and things like YouTube didn't work for me either until I installed that package. I was told that "the ubuntu package installs audio/video codecs, java, microsoft fonts and adobe flash in one go."

If you haven't got it yet, you just click on the icon for the software centre, and then when it opens you type "ubuntu restricted extras" into the search box and then when it comes up, you click to install it.

If that doesn't help I'm sure the expert people will be along soon to tell you what else to do. :)
