### Post by Tamlynmac on 2009-06-19
Just an idea:

Select from menu:
Format/paragraph/indents&spacing tab/

In the box to the right of section labeled "Below "Paragraph" the value should read "0.00". If any other value is in that box change it to 0.00 assuming you don't want a space below the paragraph.

Good Luck & Hope This Helps.
