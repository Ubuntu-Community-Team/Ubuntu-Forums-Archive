### Post by XCan on 2009-07-26
> **purple_pundit said:**
> Thank you for the reply. How do I "wipe the troubled user's configuration files?"

Make a backup of the files before you wipe them (in case it does not solve any problems).

In your home dir there is a folder called .mozilla. Enter it and it will contain your installed plugins, extensions and your config files. The config files reside in .mozilla/firefox/.
