### Post by todak on 2009-12-14
> **Twotest said:**
> This is what I am having trouble with. Any idea on how to do this?

Extract the icons to a folder of your choice. To use a particular icon, right click on a program's icon that you wish to replace and then click "Properties". Click on the icon of the program and a dialog will pop open to let you choose a location for the icon you want. Browse to the folder in which you extracted your icons and click "Open" and then you will be presented with all of the icons in a window. Pick the one you like.
