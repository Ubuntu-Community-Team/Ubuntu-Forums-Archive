### Post by abhi_69 on 2009-07-05
there is also a command line checking method. open terminal & type-
$ df -h
this will show u a list of available disk space for various drives including NTFS/FAT32 partitions.
1st item of this list is ur ubuntu drive & it will show u amount of free space+used space used by ubuntu 9.04.
hope this will help u.
