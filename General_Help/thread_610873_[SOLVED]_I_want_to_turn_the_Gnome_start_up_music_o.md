### Post by briwood on 2007-11-12
Have you tried, pulling down the System menu: Preferences -> Sound -> Sounds (tab) and un-checking Play System Sounds?

### Post by mcduck on 2007-11-12
You can also just disable the startup sound. The option is in the same place, System/Preferences/Sound, and in the very bottom of the Sounds-tab change the 'Log in' sound to 'No sound'.
