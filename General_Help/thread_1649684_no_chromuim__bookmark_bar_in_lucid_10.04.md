### Post by jamdox on 2010-12-20
I recently installed 10.04 on an old pentium  laptop I inherited -- 10.10 didn't work well. I installed chromium, which is my preferred browser, but the bookmars -- imported from xmarks -- don't appear.

Rather, there is no bookmarks bar at all. It's not small text, or any of the other issues described in other posts. The bookmarks bar simply doesn't exist.

I've tried changing the desktop theme and setting the bookmarks bar flag in the "command" box. Neither worked.

Your help with this issue is much appreciated!

---

### Post by Putnam11 on 2010-12-20
First you should go into Tools->Always Show Bookmarks Bar (Shft+Ctrl+B)
If that doesn't work, Go into Bookmark Manager and see if there's anything in there.  If not, adding a site in there should make it show up.  If this is the case you may have to manually add your bookmarks.  I hope this helps!
