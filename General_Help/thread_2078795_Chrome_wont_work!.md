### Post by Ace..... on 2012-11-02

Tahdas

It looks like you have installed ubuntu from within windows.

Normally, people install ubuntu from an install disk (the windows thing is just to give you a look at ubuntu).

First thing though would be to delete ubuntu from windows, using control panel.

Then download the disk image to windows (from ubuntu site). Then burn it to disk.
Then reboot and go into your bios (press f2 or del perhaps) as the pc starts to boot)

In the bios find the boot sequence and move your disk drive to the top.
Save & exit, and the pc will boot into the install procedure, which you just follow.

You'll then have a multi-boot pc, allowing you to boot into windows or ubuntu.

Then systematically check your system.
eg. Open Firefox, try youtube vids.

Get your browsers right, then move on to you're game.

You might find everything is perfect.
