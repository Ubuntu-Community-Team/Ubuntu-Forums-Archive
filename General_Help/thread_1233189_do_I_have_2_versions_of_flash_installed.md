### Post by ajgreeny on 2009-08-06
I had exactly this problem after the update from v22 to 32.  In Tools Add-Ons it showed v22 and also in about:plugins v22 was still showing.  When I went to the adobe site to test flash and see what was going on, it showed that actually it was v32 being used, and a right click in any flash movie also showed v32.  Even a reboot did not help.

I was completely baffled, so trying to sort things out I completely removed the adobe flash plugin, rebooted again and then reinstalled and it worked; everything then showed v32 as it should be.  I have no idea why this was going on, nor why the new version would not show, but anyway, all is well - in fact I think all was well before, but for some reason the system had not caught up with what was actually installed.
