### Post by TheFu on 2013-05-02
Updates are released as they become available.  I install them weekly and do not look for them before that time.  OTOH, my systems are not set to automatically check for updates either. I found the nagging too much.

What else should you do?  That completely depends on you and your needs.  I install about 20 other packages for monitoring, security, and just apps that I prefer over the standard installed versions. I also purge a few apps that I can't stand like nano.  You will probably have a different list than me.

Every computer on a network, even just a LAN, needs a firewall.  [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/BasicSecurity](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/BasicSecurity) will get you started.
