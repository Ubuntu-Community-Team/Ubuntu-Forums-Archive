### Post by ian-weisser on 2014-03-12
Beeps often mean the BIOS trying to tell you something is wrong.
The number of beeps, and their timing, is important. Also look for blinking lights on the sides or keyboard or panel. Note the number of blinks, and their timing.
Look up your laptop manufacturer's website for the beep/blink error code.
