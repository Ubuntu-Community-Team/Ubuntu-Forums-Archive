### Post by bigstras on 2008-06-25
you are using compiz, right?

assuming that is the case, open up ccsm:

to make sure focus stays in the correct window: in the general options, under focus & raise behavior tab, make sure click to focus is checked.

to make out of focus windows stay opaque: disable the opacify plugin under accessibility.

if that doesnt do it, i'm not sure what will
