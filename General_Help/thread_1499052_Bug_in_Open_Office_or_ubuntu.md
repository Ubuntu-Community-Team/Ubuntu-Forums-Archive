### Post by lavinog on 2010-06-01
I don't know about your specific issue, but I would recommend getting a dropbox account.  Ubuntuone is another option, but it seems to be having issues at the moment.

With Dropbox you can save all of your school papers in the dropbox folder.  If something happens, like your situation, you can go back to an earlier version.  So, if you saved it at 1:00 and again at 2:00, but the 2:00 save failed, you can retrieve the save from 1:00.

Also dropbox works in windows, if you do decide to switch to windows.
