### Post by paulocr on 2011-12-26
Just in case someone runs into this same issue, it appears that mine was related to using gnome-shell and having notify-osd conflicting with the appearance.
I remove notify-osd and the controls now appear in the center as in my first screenshot which was the way I wanted it.
