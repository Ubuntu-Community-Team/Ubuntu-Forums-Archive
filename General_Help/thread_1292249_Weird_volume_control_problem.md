### Post by fooman on 2009-10-15
try *right*-clicking on the speaker/volume icon that is in the top panel and choose "open volume control" from the drop-down menu.

when the volume control window opens,  check that all of the output sliders are turned up and not muted....master, pcm.   also surround, center and lfe if you have a 5.1/7.1 setup.
