### Post by marshag63 on 2009-07-03
Gnomad2 is an awesome program to transfer your music files to/from your mp3 player.  Menu > System > Administration > Synaptic.

Through Synaptics search box, type Gnomad2 and let it install all the necessary stuff.

Once installed, plug in your mp3 player and start Gnomad -either from menu listing or by Alt + F2 and typing Gnomad.  You will then be able to see your music on one side of the window that comes up and your computer directories on the other.  This works awesome for me with my Samsung YP-U3 mp3 player.  

Hope this helps!

MarshaG.

P.S.  If its an ipod, then instead of Gnomad2 use gtkpod
