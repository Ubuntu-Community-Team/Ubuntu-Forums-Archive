### Post by forrestcupp on 2012-06-16
You'll get only the app and dependency files that are updated. A lot of times, they'll all be updated together. It all just depends on the program. It's not always done the same way.

### Post by holycow131415 on 2012-06-16
The nightly term is basically a rename of the "alpha" term, where alpha is normally the most unstable, then beta, then release candidate, then release final.
