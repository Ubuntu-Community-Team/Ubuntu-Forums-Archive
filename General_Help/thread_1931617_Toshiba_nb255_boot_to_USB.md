### Post by Robynsveil on 2012-02-25
Went a-hunting on the Toshiba site and found that I needed to upgrade my BIOS to the latest version... and now it sees the USB as a drive. YAY! Now, I set boot priority and it is WORKING. :)

Thank you, Toshiba!
