### Post by mb_webguy on 2009-03-17
Unlike clients for some other p2p networks, bittorrent clients generally don't have an integrated search, because there are numerous trackers like [IsoHunt]("http://isohunt.com") and [The Pirate Bay]("http://thepiratebay.org") that do the job already.

To find a torrent, search one of these sites.  When you find what you're looking for, download the torrent.  Firefox (or whatever web browser you're using) should give you the option to open the torrent in Transmission, if that is your default bittorrent client.  Transmission will then start downloading the torrent.
