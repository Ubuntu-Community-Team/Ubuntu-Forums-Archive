### Post by zvacet on 2011-02-21
Read [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BurningIsoHowto](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BurningIsoHowto) and see if you checked **md5sum**.If md5sum is not corect you can download same iso with torrent ( and in case you still have iso you burned) and point download to the ffolder with existing iso.Torrent will just check your iso for bad files and replace them with good ones.After that burn iso on lower possible speed.Check CD for errors and if everything is O.K. then go for install.
