### Post by ym4546 on 2007-04-27
Hi,

I am having problems with Firefox's inline spell checker. I can't find a fix for it on the forums, at least not for the exact problems I'm having.

The problem is that although FF detects and underlines in red all misspelled words, it does not provide me the suggestions. This works fine on Windows versions of portable Firefox. On Windows, when a word is underlined in red, i can right click on it and it will list the alternatives. This is not happening in Ubuntu. When I right click on a red-underlined word, only the normal context menu, with "Undo" and the other standard options comes up

I'm using Firefox 2.0.0.3 with Kubuntu Feisty running KDE 3.5.6... (I really can't remember whether this problem was there in Kubuntu Edgy or not since I didn't use Firefox as regularly back then)

EDIT: I was looking the back pages of a similar thread and found the solution. While a lot of people were having problems with FF's spell checking not working at all, I finally found someone who was having my problem of having the entries underlined but no suggestions provided. It turns out I had the "All in one gestures" extension installed, which causes this breakage. That simply needed to be uninstalled or disabled.
