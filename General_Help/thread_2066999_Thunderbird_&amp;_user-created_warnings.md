### Post by daslinkard on 2012-10-05
I believe if you do the following you can turn this warning off.....

1. Go to Preferences in TB
2. Select Composition Tab
3. Under the General Tab (located under Composition) uncheck "Check for missing attachments"

I believe this will fix your warnings issue.
