The volume control is on the top right hand side in Gnome.  Right-click on it and select "Open Volume control".    There will be a number of sliders in there. In underneath them there should be little speaker type icons.  Look to see if any of the ones that do NOT say "Mic" have a little red "X" on them.  If they do, click on the icon and the "X" should go away ("X" means mute.  No "X" means unmute).  See if the sliders are up to at least 80% of the height of the path for all the channels that do not say "Mic".  This should turn all the output channels on and bring up the volume on each of them.

As for the "cross-path" problem you have, I don't know how to answer you.  You may have to get a new sound card, if this doesn't work.  In any case, I wish you the best of luck, and hope you solve your problem soon.
