### Post by DJ_Peng on 2008-10-30
Nvidia has released beta versions of updates to their [93]("http://www.nvnews.net/vbulletin/showthread.php?t=122139") and [71]("http://www.nvnews.net/vbulletin/showthread.php?t=122140") legacy drivers. Compiz still has some issues, but it should just be a matter of time before Nvidia releases drivers that play well with Compiz for the older video cards.

I have gotten AWN, Screenlets and Google Gadgets working with Xcompmgr, so that may help with what you're trying to do.

### Post by conundrumx on 2008-10-30
I use 173 (I have a newer card), and it works quite well.  Performance on some things (fancy animations) are lacking, but I don't have any screen update problems.  177 is blazing fast, but sometimes the screen does not properly refresh.  The biggest example being in gnome-terminal, a command's output won't show, and the cursor will just update it's position.
