### Post by LoneWolfJack on 2008-11-11
check if there is anything in your ISP contract or TOS about capping. if not, write a letter, they will probably offer you to void the contract. you will likely not be able to get a contract with another ISP if you're living in the US though.

if it's in your contract or their TOS, there's nothing you can do. you signed that thing, after all.
