I believe my little brother mis-typed the password when setting up his keyring, because it will not open.  How do I reset his keyring password, or delete his keyring and create a new one?

### Post by ronmarley1 on 2007-10-19
If you are using Gutsy, you can click on -->System -->Administration -->Keyring Manager.
Or, go to your home folder, hit Ctrl+H to show hidden files.  Navigate to .gnome2 and then keyrings.  Delete the default.keyring to reset.
