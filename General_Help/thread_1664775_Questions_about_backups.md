### Post by Jeruvy on 2011-01-11
I would hazard that the best way to verify this, is to actually do it.  Perform your backup, move your files and restore it.  Ensure that your package works to your satisfaction.  Using a simple tool such as 'dd' has a lot of versatility and I have never had issues with restoring data.  There are a lot of tools that work well.

But you need to ensure your method works for you, so it is best to go through the paces.  I find it sad that many folks do backups without actually testing the restore process, because as you probably will attest, sometimes it doesn't work as expected.

Try it out and share your experiences.
