### Post by snova on 2009-01-20
Gedit saves them wherever you tell it to. ;)

Probably your Documents folder by default, or possibly your desktop.

If you remember the filename, but can't find them anywhere else, then you can try searching for it.

**locate gedit** merely tells you where the gedit program itself is.
