Oh, well.

Video: I prefer nvidia over ati. Had less trouble for me so far. You should be fine with either of them anyway.

Audio: If you're in audio production and such (like me), you'll probably look for an external device. If you simply like to have good audio and the possibility of occacional recording, RME and soundblaster families are good pci choices. Otherwise, integrated intel sound chipsets usually "just work", at their expected quality level.

CPU: amd x64 work nicely in linux if you want 64 bit. And may lead to some money savings over intel.

HD: almost anyone will do...

... well, there is more than enough hardware. So, welcome: try to use free software, open formats, and let's make a better world ;)
