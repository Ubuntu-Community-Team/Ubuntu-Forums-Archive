The best method will be one which works well for you.  I would suggest taking a class taught by an experienced instructor but that may be impractical for you.  Perhaps a good option would be to get a book on the subject and let the book guide you through the learning process.  There are at least 5 books on the subject; most of them received reviews which were less than good.  There is some material available on the Internet; search for "bash programming" and you will find some things.  The quality of these varies from OK to poor so I wouldn't really recommend that approach.
