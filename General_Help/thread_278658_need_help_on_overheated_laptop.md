### Post by DJ Scribblinni on 2006-10-16
Depending on the Hardware, most laptops that come with SpeedStep technology will pull down the Processors speed, to help save it from dying.  If and when a laptops hardware does say it is just to hot to operate, most just shut off without warning.  If your laptop is pretty old, you may be in the buisness for a new one.
