### Post by donato roque on 2010-07-30
The login (to ubuntu) password is different from your keyring password. For convenience perhaps you may want to change the keyring password to the new login password.

Go to Applications>Accessories>Password and Encryptions keys.

Right click on the folder. Change the password to the login password.  Everytime you give the login password your keyring will also be unlocked.
