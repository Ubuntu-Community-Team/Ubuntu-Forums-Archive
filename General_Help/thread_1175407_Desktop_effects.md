After a fresh install, by default, the extra desktop effects were enabled, my card was coping just fine with is, then after 1 or 2 reboots, it was disabled, when I tried to re-enable it, it says: searching for available drivers. 

then it freezes and refreshes the page giving me the message: "Desktop effects could not be enabled" (probably not correct to the word)

### Post by JCoster on 2009-06-01
Try this: disable desktop effects, then fully remove the drivers for your card by Synaptic and restart.  Now attempt to enable desktop effects again and it should ask you to install the driver.
