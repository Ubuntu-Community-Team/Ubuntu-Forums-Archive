### Post by Stefano Bieler on 2010-10-28
Hey guys I figured out the problem. Under the hardware tab and under the profile drop down box it was set to analog surround 7.1 output, but it needed to be on analog surround 7.1 + Analog Mono Input for the microphone device to pop up under the input tab. i took a screen shot to show you guys what i mean.
