Hello, at the minute, when aMSN notifies me about someone who wishes to talk to me, the taskbar expends and contracts. Is there a way just to get the taskbar icon to flash, like in normal MSN. Thanks.

### Post by evereign on 2006-11-17
Go to Tools-> Preferences -> Session tab and then down in the box that says "My Messaging Interface" select Minimized/Iconified for both of the first options and check "Flicker chat window title...." 
I think this should work. it works for me.
