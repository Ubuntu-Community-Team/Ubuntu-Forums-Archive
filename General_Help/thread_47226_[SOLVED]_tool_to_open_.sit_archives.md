### Post by Magneto on 2005-08-05
[QUOTE=manicka]What program/library do I need to open .sit archives?[/QUOTE]
 get stuffit for linux from here [http://www.stuffit.com/](http://www.stuffit.com/) and
extract the files 

tar -zxvf the Stuffit*.tar.gz 
 
copy stuff and unstuff in the bin/ directory to the /usr/bin/ directory 
then

unstuff file.sit
