### Post by ajgreeny on 2010-12-11
I would also suggest in this clean install that you make a separate /home partition.  It makes life so much easier next time you have to repeat the operation, as /home will remain untouched, and although you MUST at all times have backups of your home files and folders, you will not need to make a specific backup just to install a new OS as it should remain untouched.
[Separate home at install.]("http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/installseparatehome")
