### Post by Ranko Kohime on 2012-07-21
It may be that the system is not idle.  Just because you are not using it, does not mean that something isn't running.  There are a variety of programs in Ubuntu that will momentarily use a large portion of CPU power, the update manager is one such program.

Now, as to why the fan won't slow down again after that's finished, it's hard to say.
