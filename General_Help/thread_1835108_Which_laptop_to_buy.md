### Post by LowSky on 2011-08-28
If you are buying in a couple months, asking what to buy now is a bad idea. Things change very quickly in the PC world. Who knows tomorrow a new chip might be released that is faster and cheaper than the current models.

if you said i'm going to buy in the next week or so then things change.
By the way Ubuntu has a nice chart of certified to work laptops located here: [http://www.ubuntu.com/certification/](http://www.ubuntu.com/certification/)

Sure newer stuff might not be on the list but it gives you an idea of how supported a lineup will be.
