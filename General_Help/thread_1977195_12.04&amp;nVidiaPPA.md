### Post by jefsview on 2012-05-09
open up Synaptic.

go to Settings tab

Go to Repositories (under Settings).

Click the checkbox for Pre-released updates (Precise Proposed).

Hit the reload button.

Search for Nvidia and uninstall your Nvidia-current and look for the nvidia-current-updates and any other that use 295.49 (except the -dev ones).

There you go.

-- Jeff
