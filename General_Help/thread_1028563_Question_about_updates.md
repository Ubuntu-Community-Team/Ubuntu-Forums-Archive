### Post by gettinoriginal on 2009-01-02
The updates should be categorized as to priority, I would download the critical ones, wait on the suggested ones.  Then after your updates are done, check the system and see how it works, if all is fine, then update the others 1 or 2 at a time so you know which ones are causing the problem.  If the update is for something you are not using and do not plan to use, then skip it for now.
