### Post by zacktu on 2010-12-03
The man pages for lp and lpr are dissimilar.  I think of lpr as sending standard output to the default printer.  There are many more options for lp, among them printing in landscape mode and printing multiple document pages on a single page (2up, 4up, etc.).
