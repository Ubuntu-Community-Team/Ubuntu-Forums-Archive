### Post by AmpersUK on 2011-07-12
I recently installed **Xara**, a terrific program. But it has taken over the opening of pictures.

Now when I click on a png or jpg file on my desktop, or any other directory, Xara tries to open the file, and it can't - it can only import these files.

I would prefer **imageviewer** to open these picture files again.

Any ideas? I can find nothing in system settings, and personal prefs only cater for a very limited number of applications.

---

### Post by SavageWolf on 2011-07-12
Right click on the file and go to properties, then "Open With", then select it.
