I'd have to see your screen to know for sure, but in the case of the bookmarks I'm assuming it's because of different fonts in the bookmark names. The bookmark names on Ubuntu are probably using a font just is a little bit bigger than on Windows, so each one takes up more space.

I'm assuming most of what you're seeing is just that; text and other widgets on the screen just aren't the same size as their equivalents on Windows. There are settings to change that stuff if you need.
