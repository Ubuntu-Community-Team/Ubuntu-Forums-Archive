### Post by mb_webguy on 2009-05-11
The folder you're talking about is probably called VIDEO_TS, right?  Well, this is actually a DVD directory structure, and is what you'd see if you browse a DVD.  

I believe Brasero and k3b can burn a DVD from a VIDEO_TS folder.  I know Handbrake will let you transcode a video to an avi or mp4 from a VIDEO_TS folder.
