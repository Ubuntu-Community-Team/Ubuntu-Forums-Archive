### Post by Rhubarb on 2008-04-12
You could try capturing the screen while playing the presentation.
In which case grab gtk-recordmydesktop off the repositories.
```
sudo aptitude install gtk-recordmydesktop
```

Then you'll find it in Applications --> Sound & Video --> gtk-recordMyDesktop
