### Post by buzzingrobot on 2014-02-10
In addition to the three choices offered during the Adblock install, in Firefox, go to Tools->Add-Ons->Extensions.  Select "Preferences" for AdBlock Plus. Select "Filter Preferences".  Then, un-select the "Allow some non-intrusive advertising".  Note that this option is selected by default.

This has been effective for me.  Whether it is completely successful for me or anyone depends on the sites we visit and how successful advertisers are at circumventing the techniques used to block their ads.  If someone finds a new away around ad blockers, then we will see those ads until the ad blockers figure out a way to block them.
