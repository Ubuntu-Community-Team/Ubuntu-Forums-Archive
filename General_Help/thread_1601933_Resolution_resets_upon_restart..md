### Post by decjedrek on 2010-10-28
Try to add this:
```
    SubSection "Display"
        Depth          24
        Modes          "1280x800_75.00"
    EndSubSection
``` to Your Section "Screen" Remember to modify Modes line to suit You desired resolution and refresh rate.

Hope this helps :)

Also check this out: [https://wiki.archlinux.org/index.php/Xorg#Nvidia_2]("https://wiki.archlinux.org/index.php/Xorg#Nvidia_2")
