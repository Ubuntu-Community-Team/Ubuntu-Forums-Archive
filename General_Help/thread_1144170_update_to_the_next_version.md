### Post by Monotoko on 2009-04-30
It depends on if you do an upgrade, or a reinstall
with an upgrade you do not lose your files, but there is always a chance of it screwing up with an upgrade, in which case you would have to do a reinstall anyway.

for more info see here: [http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/upgrading](http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/upgrading)
