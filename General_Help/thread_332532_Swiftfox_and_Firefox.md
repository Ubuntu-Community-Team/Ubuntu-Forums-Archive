### Post by Ensnared on 2007-01-06
Swiftfox is optimised for your CPU, which basically means it should perform faster than Firefox. The difference may not be very noticable though, but other than that they're the same application, and as such are 100% compatible extension/plugin/theme/etc-wise.

You can read about Swiftfox here: [http://getswiftfox.com/](http://getswiftfox.com/)
