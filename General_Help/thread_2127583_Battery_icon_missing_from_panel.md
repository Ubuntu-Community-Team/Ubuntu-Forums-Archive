### Post by Impavidus on 2013-03-20
The easy way to get the battery monitor plugin: Go to applications>settings>settings manager, click task bar (or something like it, I'm using dutch), select elements, add a new element and choose the battery monitor. I think it's included in the xfce4-goodies package.

About the default monitor: I don't know why is disappears for a while on your laptop. I see it sometimes disappear, but it comes back within a minute or so, which is too short for a reliable estimate of the power anyway (it's shorter than an on/off cycle of the fan on my laptop).
