### Post by saneearth on 2011-12-26
Likely your best option is to just reinstall with the 10.04 disk if you have it or burn a 10.10 disc if that is the version you want and install that. You can select the same partitions you had for root and home previously and select format on the root partition, but do not format the /home partition. Unfortunately, you will have to reinstall programs not included, but with synaptic it doesn't take long and the settings for most should be maintained by keeping your home partition in tact.
