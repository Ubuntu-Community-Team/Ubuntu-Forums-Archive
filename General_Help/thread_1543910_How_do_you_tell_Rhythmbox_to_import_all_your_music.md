### Post by Legendary_Bibo on 2010-08-01
I can tell you without even looking at Rythmbox. Open it up, click on Music on the top left. It's probably next to Edit, and click on Import Folder then find your music. It only seems to support one folder level at least for me so find your music folders. Hold Ctrl and select all the folders you want to import and hit OK or Open or whatever button is in that menu.

### Post by Spice Weasel on 2010-08-08
Edit > Preferences > Music. Change the "Library Location", and tick the box under it.

All of your music should import fairly quickly, and it also scans for new tracks that are added. ;)
