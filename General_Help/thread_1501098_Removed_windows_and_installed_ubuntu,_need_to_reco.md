### Post by ryan858 on 2010-06-03
Use a Live CD ... But you must have another hard drive to recover to, no matter what. If not, you'll need to get an external hard drive, or at least a USB stick (if it has enough space, or you're not recovering heaps of data).

TestDisk, and PhotoRec can help you recover your stuff. As well as many other recovery tools. You can probably find a LiveCD that comes with TestDisk and PhotoRec on it, or some other recovery software, or download them while you're in a LiveCD (they won't be saved when you reboot though).
