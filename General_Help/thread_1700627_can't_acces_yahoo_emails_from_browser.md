### Post by bongorider on 2011-03-14
It seems that the problem is google chrome/chromium, and is most likely related to the 
**Error 324 (net::ERR_EMPTY_RESPONSE): Unknown error.**

error that many people seem to be getting lately (and I also experienced on certain websites) that Google has offered no explanation for.  A problem that somehow affects all your browsers as long as you have chrome installed.

The only "fix" for this problem was to uninstall chrome/chromium and use firefox, now both the website and yahoo mail work fine.

The amount of people experiencing this 324 error is getting ridiculous, Google need to get people working on this if they want to keep their market share.
