### Post by Therion on 2008-12-12
Quick fix:  F11 two times.

The permanent fix involves preventing Emerald from auto-starting along with Compiz.  

Go to System/Prefs/Sessions and in the Startup tab uncheck "Emerald". 

That should put and end to the problem.
