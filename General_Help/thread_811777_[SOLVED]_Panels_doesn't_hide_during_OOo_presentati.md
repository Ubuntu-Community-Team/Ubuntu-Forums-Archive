### Post by BandD on 2008-06-12
I had a similar paroblem.  But It happened after I removed OOo and then reinstalled it.  The problem for me was the packages  openoffice.org-gnome and openoffice.org-gtk weren't installed.

So check to see if they are installed and if they are, try re-installing them and see if it helps.
