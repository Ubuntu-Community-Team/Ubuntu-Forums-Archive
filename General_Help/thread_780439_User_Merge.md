### Post by Zorael on 2008-05-03
Your best bet would be to export stuff in those apps and then import them with your new user. Transfering settings is easy but merging them can border to impossible, depending on how the app in question saves them.

I know for certain that Firefox can export bookmarks (Bookmarks -> Organise Bookmarks -> File -> Export), and it seems unlikely that Thunderbird wouldn't have the ability to, also.
