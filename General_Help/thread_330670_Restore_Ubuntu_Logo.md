### Post by tuxalot on 2008-02-21
Hey Rob,

Have a look at /home/user/.icons/(you may have another sub-folder here, such as Mac4Lin)

The files you must change are named main-menu.png and start-here.png.  Change those to whatever you like.  The size should remain approx. 128x125 or else your menu will get fuked.
