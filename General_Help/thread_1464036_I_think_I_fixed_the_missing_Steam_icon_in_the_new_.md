### Post by Hit in the Head on 2010-04-27
So, I thought it would be neat if I could make the icon that disappeared, reappear. My system is Unbuntu 9.10, using wine 1.2. I am a total Linux n00b, so keep that in mind as you are writing a flamed retort to my crappy Linux skills 

Here's what I did:

Click Applications -> Wine -> Browse C:\ Drive

Go to C:\Program Files\Steam\Public

Right-click steam_tray.ico and open it with Gimp Image Editor

From here you have a couple of choices, but the easiest is to just "File" -> Save as... and overwrite the original.

That's all. If you want the flashier version, you can remove the layer of the one you don't like and save.

Repercussions? I haven't seen any yet.

**Did this work for you? Let us know** (it may be nice to list the flavor and version of Linux you're running).

I hope this works for some of you. Cheers.
