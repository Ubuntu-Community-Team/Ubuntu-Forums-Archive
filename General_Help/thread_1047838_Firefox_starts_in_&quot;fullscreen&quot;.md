### Post by Aralhach on 2009-01-22
I've been having this trouble a few times now.  When Firefox boots, it covers the entire screen from the menubar down to the status bar.  I put F11 (the full screen) then it goes into the real full screen mode, and when I put it back, then it goes back to the normal layout.  This started happening when I activated the desktop effects.

I've also noticed that sometimes the taskbar stays kinda white with the previous window's letters faintly drawn, and no close and maximize buttons until I move the mouse over it.

Is this a bug? How can I fix this?

---

### Post by caro on 2009-01-22
Launch Firefox and hit the F11 button twice.
Unmaximize the window. (You may need to resize the window for good measure).
Maximize the window.
Close FF.
Your settings should be saved.
