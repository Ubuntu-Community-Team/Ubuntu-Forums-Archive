### Post by wyoming on 2011-03-18
Just wanted to share my satisfaction at the resilience of Ubuntu 10.04 LTS. I had weeks of erratic shut-downs and various other problems, including the sudden loss of Gnome, with apps unresponsive etc... Turns out my power supply was getting old, which set off random problems everywhere. New power supply and my system runs like new. The OS stood firm throughout and did what it could to function.
Possibly useful: the power supply first passed a stress test but was still leaking a little inside. This apparently can be a major source of problems that may really look like they are not hardware-related.
