### Post by coffeecat on 2009-12-18
By "drop-down menus" I suppose you mean the Applications-Places-System menus on the top panel. Oops. I sympathise. If you want to open the Display Preferences app under System > Preferences > Display, press Alt-F2 to open the 'Run Application' utility. Now type 'gnome-display-properties' (no quotes) in the app field and click on 'Run'. Hopefully, you'll be able to change your resolution with that.

If by chance the Display Preferences window is so big that it's spilling off the edge of the screen, you can move it about by holding down the Alt key, left-clicking anywhere within the window and then moving it with the mouse/trackpad. That's a tip worth remembering - particularly if you ever need to use a netbook.
