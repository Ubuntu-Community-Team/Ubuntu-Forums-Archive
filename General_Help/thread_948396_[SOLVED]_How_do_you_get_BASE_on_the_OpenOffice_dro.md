### Post by Aero-Z on 2008-10-15
I might be wrong but right click on gnome menu. Choose Edit Menu. Go to Office and check if there's Base listed and put a tick before it.
If it's not listed then open gnome menu. Choose Add/Remove. Open Office and from there tick OpenOffice.org Database and install it.
