### Post by JC Cheloven on 2011-07-07
Hope to correctly understand: You're using 11.04 with unity, right?

Things have changed a little, the panel isn't any more as the gnome panel was (less customization options). 

Try choosing "ubuntu classical" at startup (at the bottom of the screen, just after selecting your user and before typing your password). This is more or less the familiar gnome2 environment.
