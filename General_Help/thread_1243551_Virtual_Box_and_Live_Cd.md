### Post by Mighty_Joe on 2009-08-18
Sure.  Create a virtual machine.  When you finish,  Click on "Settings".  In the pop-up window, select "CD/DVD-ROM", select "Mount CD/DVD Drive", "Host CD/DVD Drive" and select your drive from the drop-down box.
