### Post by sikander3786 on 2010-08-27
Install "CompizConfig Settings Manager" from software centre or

***sudo apt-get install compizconfig-settings-manager***

It will show up in System >>> Preferences >>> Advanced Effects or something like that.

Then tweak it accordingly to meet your needs.
