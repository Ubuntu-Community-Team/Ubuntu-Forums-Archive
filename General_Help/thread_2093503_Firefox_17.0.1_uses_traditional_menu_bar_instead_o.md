### Post by finny388 on 2012-12-10
Not new in v17, it has always been this way:

On Windows at work there is a single orange firefox button that houses the menu tree.

At home on xubuntu it is the traditional File, Edit, View etc always in view menu bar.

Space is a premium and I wish there was a firefox menu button that could be placed by the address bar or tabs.

Is this possible?

p.s. Global Menu Integration addon was disabled. I enabled, restarted, but no change. So disabled again.

---

### Post by s3a on 2012-12-10
Right click on the empty space on the right of the plus sign that adds a new tab and uncheck "Menu Bar".
