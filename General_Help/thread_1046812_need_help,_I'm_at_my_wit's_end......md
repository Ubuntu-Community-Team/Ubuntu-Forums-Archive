### Post by Shazaam on 2009-01-21
A safer way to restart a frozen (locked up) pc...
Hold down the ALT + the SysRQ keys and type in REISUB
A few things that may be causing your problem since you state you were having problems before with Windows...
1. Heat issues (dust, fans not working).
2. Memory issues (bad ram modules; dirty ram module contacts).
3. Defective hardware (bad/worn out hard drive(s); motherboard defects; weak power supply; worn out cmos battery; etc).
On a desktop pc, if you are comfortable opening up your pc unplug it and pull the cover(s) off and clean out the dust/grime with a couple of cans of compressed air. Follow the directions on the can. Make sure ALL of the fans are turning. You might also want to pop your memory (ram) modules out and clean off the contacts. Check all of the cables and make sure they are pushed all of the way into their receptacles/ports.
