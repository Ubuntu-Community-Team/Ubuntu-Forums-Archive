### Post by tweedledee on 2007-01-09
> **allwin said:**
> How do you move menu items to the desktop?  Doesn't seem to allow drag n drop, and no amount of right or left clicking would reveal how you do something like copy the Terminal from the menu to the desktop.

Unfortunately, you don't.  The menu in XFCE still needs a lot of work; it does't support drag and drop, and even editing it is a real pain (although at least you have total control, unlike Gnome).  The menu is a large part of the reason I'm only running a "hybrid" XFCE/Gnome right now instead of pure XFCE, especially on my older machine.

Note that you can have a gnome panel in XFCE (and hence the gnome behavior, to some extent) even under XFCE by installing & running gnome-panel.
