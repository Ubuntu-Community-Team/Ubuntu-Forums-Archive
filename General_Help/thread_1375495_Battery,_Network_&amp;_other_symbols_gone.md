### Post by HiImTye on 2010-01-08
[LIST=1]
[*]right click on a blank spot on the panel (or one of the arrows if it isn't 'expanded')
[*]select 'add to panel'
[*]scroll down to 'notification area' and double click it
[/LIST]
to remove one of the icons you'll need to open it's configuration and uncheck 'add to systray' or whatever (from a right click menu)
