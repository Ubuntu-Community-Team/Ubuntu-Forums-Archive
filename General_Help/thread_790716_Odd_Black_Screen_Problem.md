### Post by Shazaam on 2008-05-11
Do this instead of a hard reboot....
Hold down the Alt+Sys Rq (Print screen) keys and type in R E I S U B
This will reboot your system. From here....
[http://fosswire.com/2007/09/08/fix-a-frozen-system-with-the-magic-sysrq-keys/](http://fosswire.com/2007/09/08/fix-a-frozen-system-with-the-magic-sysrq-keys/)

The black screen can be caused by many problems. Overheating, power management, suspend/resume settings, bad monitor, video drivers, etc.
