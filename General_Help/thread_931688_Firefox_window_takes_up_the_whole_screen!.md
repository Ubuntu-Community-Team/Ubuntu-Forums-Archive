I tried F11, but when I close Firefox and then re-open it, I have to hit F11 again to take out of full-screen mode. It's not that it's even full-screen mode; the title bar and status bar are both missing. This started happening after I got rid of conky.

Here's a fix.

Open FF.
Hit F11 to make it TOTAL full screen mode.
Hit F11 again to come out of TOTAL full screen mode.
Now look for the maximise icon in the top right of the FF window and hit this.
Now close with Alt F4.

When you restart FF, you should have your Ubuntu toolbars again.
