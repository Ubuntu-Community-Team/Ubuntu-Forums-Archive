### Post by skkeeper on 2010-03-02
From my experience Xubuntu is a real life saver for old pcs/laptops (especially if the graphic card can't stand the current desktop enviroments), but if you want to experience the best of Ubuntu, I would recommend you to wait for Lucid Lynx (10.4) the standard version (not Xubuntu nor Kubuntu).

Both are great flavors of Ubuntu, but has they are different flavors, sometimes you dont get the best of the Desktop integration that Ubuntu's tends to offer more and more. I doubt that Xubuntu 10.4 will bring the new "Social from the Start" features for example.
