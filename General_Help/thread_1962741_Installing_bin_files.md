### Post by jerome1232 on 2012-04-21
Open a terminal and simply drag it into your terminal, post back any errors.


If it doesn't work post back any errors, also type 'file' without the quotes, put one space after it and drag the file into your terminal again, press enter and paste the output here.


Any output from a command you post, please go into advanced mode, highlight it all, and press the # symbol on your toolbar to make it readable.
