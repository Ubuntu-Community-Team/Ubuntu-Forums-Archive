### Post by greggr on 2007-11-26
I know there's nothing like a good "DUH" to cure one's sense of expertise so I'll answer my own last post   FYI.  I investivated my printer settings: System>Administration>Printing>Properties>Paper only to find I had inadvertantly set my paper to A4 when I was using standard 8.5X11" Letter.   DUH!  Works like a charm now.
