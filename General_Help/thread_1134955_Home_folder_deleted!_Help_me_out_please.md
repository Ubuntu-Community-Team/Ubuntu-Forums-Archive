### Post by jimv on 2009-04-24
Is this Gnome or KDE?

If it's Gnome, open Nautilus (alt+f2 and type nautilus).  Then you should see a "trash" folder listed on the left.  Click it.  (Alternately, you can type "trash:///")  Do you see your stuff in the trash?

If not, you'll want to reboot from the Live CD and take a look at this guy's page about using Foremost to recover deleted files:

[http://www.ubuntu-unleashed.com/2008/04/howtorecover-and-undelete-text-file-in.html](http://www.ubuntu-unleashed.com/2008/04/howtorecover-and-undelete-text-file-in.html)
