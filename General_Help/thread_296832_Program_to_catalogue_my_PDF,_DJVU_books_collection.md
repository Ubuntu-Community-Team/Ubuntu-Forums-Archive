### Post by FryerFox on 2006-11-10
How about Beagle (maybe called Desktop Search now)?

You can specify what to index and what not to index to some extent. Or you could try one of the other live-search sofware (like Tracker, etc.) that work the same way as beagle.

This may not be what you're looking for, but this is how I find something in my document collection (which I have accumulated over more than a decade).
