### Post by Mike_IronFist on 2009-04-12
There is a Mozilla-based (like Seamonkey) browser that you can get from Add/Remove simply called "Web Browser". It's a very simple straight-forward browser, but short on features.

Additionally I'd recommend any of the Python-based browsers, as they're simple and lightweight.

Stay away from KDE web browsers (such as Konqueror) as these tend to be somewhat hefty compared to what you're looking for.

As a last word of advice, and I'm positive almost everyone knows this, but if you haven't tried it yet, typing "light weight web browser" into the search bar for add/remove produces lots of desirable results.
