### Post by aphatak on 2010-06-28
The sound icon disappeared from my screen too.  I took the easy way out - go to System -> Preferences, right-click Sound and click 'Add to Panel'.  I wouldn't call that a solution to the problem of the disappearing icon, but hey, I have a sound icon in my Gnome panel!
