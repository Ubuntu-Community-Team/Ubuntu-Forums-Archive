### Post by gradinaruvasile on 2010-07-10
The guy has dvi-d not dvi-i (most cards have i).
Dvi-d is made for digital transmission only (it doesnt even fit physically a standard dvi-vga converter) and you can only connect a digital monitor to it normally. So it needs an exotic adapter.

I know this because i have a ASUS M3N78-VM mobo and it has 2 ports - vga + dvi-d and i cant use the standard dvi-vga converter on the digital port because that (the converter) is made for dvi-i that has 4 additional pins.

[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Digital_Visual_Interface](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Digital_Visual_Interface)
