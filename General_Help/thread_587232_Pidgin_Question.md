### Post by HokeyFry on 2007-10-22
In Pidgin, is it possible to be logged in to two screen names, but have each managed separately? Like when i am at work, i want to be logged in as myself, but also logged into my computer help screen name for the university, and I want the lists to be separate (aka when one is away, the other is not)

---

### Post by Redenbacher on 2007-10-22
There most certainly is. What you have to do is launch pidgin twice, so you will have 2 buddy lists up. On the second list, disable the account it automatically logs in to, and log in to your other account. You should have 2 separate lists for each account! Hope this helps!
