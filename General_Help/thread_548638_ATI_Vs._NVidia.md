### Post by CarpKing on 2007-09-11
ATI hasn't released anything yet, so it would be a good idea to wait a few weeks until they do (supposed to start with better drivers and specs for newer cards sometime this week, followed by AIGLX support next month and specs for older cards sometime). You should be able to get a good idea of what ATI is doing before the release of Gutsy, but even in the best case it will be awhile before the drivers will see much benefit.  It comes down to whether you want Nvidia's good support now or ATI's excellent support later (provided they keep their promises).  If your getting a laptop, you might also want to look into Intel's graphics cards, which already have OSS drivers.  I don't know if they will suit your needs or not, but they are out there.
