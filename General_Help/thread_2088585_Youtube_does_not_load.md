### Post by Welly Wu on 2012-11-27
WiTopia has 50 different VPN gateways worldwide. They have contracts with regional VPN service providers and each one is set up slightly differently from one another. I have had problems with the New York, NY VPN gateway in the past with regard to Google thinking that I am sending too many anonymous search requests in the past. The Washington, DC VPN gateway features the OpenVPN protocol and it uses Blowfish CBC mode 256 bits 16 rounds cipher strength if you have their WiTopia Personal VPN PRO service for one year. This one never gives me any problems with any of my online accounts. It's not as fast, but it is fast enough for my needs.
