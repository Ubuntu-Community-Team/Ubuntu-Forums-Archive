### Post by RAOF on 2005-11-01
The thing here is that firefox **will** be using different fonts (and a different font renderer) - fonts are in general not free.  You can get a similar appearance by installing the msttcorefonts package, and setting the fonts in firefox to "times new roman", or whatever the font settings are in windows.
