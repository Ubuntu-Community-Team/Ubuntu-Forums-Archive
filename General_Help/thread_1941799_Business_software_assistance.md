### Post by oldfred on 2012-03-17
How large of a business. Do you just want accounting or more like a MRP/ERP system with just the account packages now?

Often payroll is best done by an outside contractor. Software requires regular updates as taxes change at least every year and often even more frequently. Most companies of any size over a few use a payroll service.

Accounting more business:
[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Comparison_of_accounting_software](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Comparison_of_accounting_software)
Similar to Sage, Java based for UK VAT
[http://jalia.sourceforge.net/](http://jalia.sourceforge.net/)
SQL Ledger Has been recommended to be a good solid general ledger. It's written in perl and uses mysql.
[http://www.sql-ledger.org/](http://www.sql-ledger.org/)
[http://www.bambooinvoice.org/](http://www.bambooinvoice.org/)
Web/MySQL database
[http://frontaccounting.com/wb3/](http://frontaccounting.com/wb3/)
