### Post by Randomskk on 2006-01-13
If it was auto-mounted, check your desktop for an icon you can use to browse it.
If there's nothing there, load up Konqueror and go to /media , it should be there if it got mounted.

If it wasn't auto-mounted, you'll need to mount it, which is slightly more complex...
Go to the KDE button, System Settings, click the digital camera bit. I haven't used it, but I bet there's something nice there :)
