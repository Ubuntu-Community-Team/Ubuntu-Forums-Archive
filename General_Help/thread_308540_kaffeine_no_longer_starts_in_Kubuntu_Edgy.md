### Post by hatstand on 2006-12-02
Just found a way: there are other threads on this. 

Go to Menu > System > KSysGuard

Open the "process Table" Tab

Kill the Kaffeine process.

It appears that Kaffeine had not shut down and still had process running. You have just killed them and can start Kaffeine.
