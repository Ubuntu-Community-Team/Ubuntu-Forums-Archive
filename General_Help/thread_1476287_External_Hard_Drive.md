### Post by srs5694 on 2010-05-07
*Most* external drives work fine. I have, however, seen one or two reports of drives that don't work well. Unfortunately, I don't recall the specific models that have been reported as being trouble-prone. I recommend that you buy whatever fits your needs (capacity, price, interface, etc.), but be sure to buy from a store with a good return policy. That way if you have problems, you can return it without too much hassle. Note that manufacturers sometimes change products without changing model names or numbers, so even if somebody says that model ABC from company XYZ works fine, you might have a different experience when you buy what looks like the same product because of behind-the-scenes changes.
