### Post by hawthornso23 on 2010-01-31
Lots of people have had video driver problems immediately after updating from Jaunty to Karmic. The video driver has to interface with the operating system and things change during the upgrade. ### Post by 3rdalbum on 2010-02-02
If you think this is malware, then go back to the 1990s where you belong.

These days, malware doesn't do anything as pointless as cause your computer to crash in such a visible manner. Malware will do something like prevent Internet Explorer from working, and then pretend to be an anti-virus program that can "fix this problem" if you give it your credit card details. Malware will run quietly in the background sending out spam and committing DDoS attacks.

Malware in the 1990s might have done such a pointless thing as crash your computer "for fun", but not these days. They now have a vested interest in keeping you from knowing that you have a virus, so they can continue to use your computer behind your back.

I'd suggest checking that your graphics card isn't overheating.
