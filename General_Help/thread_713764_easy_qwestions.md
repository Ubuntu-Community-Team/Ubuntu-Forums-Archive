### Post by kesman on 2008-03-03
you need to install ccsm to enable the desktop cube.
Search for it in add/remove applications. After installing, you can edit the compiz settings. You need to enable "desktop cube" and "rotate cube". Then you can grab the cube with ctrl+alt+left click on empty space to move it around.
