### Post by madmax75 on 2010-03-13
Yep, **Shutter** is a great screenshot program. When you launch it from the menu, it adds an icon in your upper Gnome panel. From there you just right-click and choose the option you want from a drop-down menu, which includes "Whole screen". You can also choose a specific area by dragging with your mouse, set a timer etc.

You can get it at least from the GetDeb repositories.

[http://shutter-project.org/](http://shutter-project.org/)

[http://www.getdeb.net/welcome/](http://www.getdeb.net/welcome/)
