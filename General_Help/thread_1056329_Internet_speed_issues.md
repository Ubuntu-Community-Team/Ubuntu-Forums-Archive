### Post by kavon89 on 2009-01-31
Head to SpeedTest.net and try a test on a server close to you. Make sure no one else, including yourself, is doing anything on the internet while you test. (Torrents, downloads, streaming music, webcam conversation etc).

Tell us the results, remember that the KB/s and Kb/s speeds are different and the capitalization of the B matters. (difference between byte and bit, one is 8x bigger).
