### Post by guiverc on 2021-11-17
Welcome to Ubuntu Forums :)

Providing details of your Lubuntu system may help.  

What release did you install?

Lubuntu releases are *year.month* in format, e.g. Lubuntu 20.04 LTS is the 2020-April release; and Lubuntu 21.10 is the 2021-October release.

LTS releases also come with a .point level that provides additional level of upgrades; 20.04 & 20.04.1 installs defaulted to using the GA or 5.4 kernel, where as 20.04.2 & later media defaults to use the HWE or *hardware-enablement* kernel so being specific can help. If this detail is remembered/known provide it; but Lubuntu 20.04 LTS is often enough.
