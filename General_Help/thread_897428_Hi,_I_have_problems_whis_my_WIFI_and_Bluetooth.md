I understand you well enough, not perfectly, but well enough.

First off, why do you need to reinstall?  Do you need to reinstall Ubuntu for some reason, or are you dual-booting and need to reinstall Windows or something?

Secondly, people sometimes want to "uninstall" Ubuntu.  This is not possible, unless you installed it with Wubi.  Ubuntu is not a program, it is an OS.  You can format it away, but not uninstall it.  It's not a part you put in your car that you can take out, it is the car (not the best analogy).  If you installed with Wubi, then just go into Add/Remove Programs in Windows and uninstall it.  If not, just format all the Ubuntu partitions.

If you are recovering a Windows partition from a backup disc or a recovery disc, it will overwrite Ubuntu anyways, no need to remove Ubuntu, since it will be done anyways.

Thirdly, by effects I think you mean Compiz-Fusion.  You need to search for "compiz nvidia (your card number)" or "compiz ati (your card number)" or whatever your video card seems to be.

Lastly, I think you can also make a post in your native language, unless it is something really obscure, there are bound to be people on the forums that speak it and can help.  It isn't that your english is hard to understand by any means, just you might feel more comfortable that way.
