### Post by sisco311 on 2011-02-08
Check out: [uhelp]community/ComposeKey[/uhelp]

It's AltGr+1 on the Hungarian & Romanian layout.

I think, on the Spanish layout, you have to press AltGr+4 (usually right Alt key+4).

Or hold down Ctrl+Shift, press u, then type in 7E and release Ctrl+Shift.
