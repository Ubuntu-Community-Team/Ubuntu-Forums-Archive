### Post by dtoronto on 2009-06-29
Just an FYI I had to reset a root password for a non-debian distro a few weeks ago and found that it is possible by removing the hard disk and mounting it as another drive on another Linux box.  Move into the Shadow directory and remove the entry for your password.  Replace the drive and login leaving the password bland and create a new password.
