### Post by ibuclaw on 2009-07-11
The file extension **.cpp** is deprecated.

It's wiser to get into the habit of naming them "**.cc**" instead.

[QUOTE="http://gcc.gnu.org/gcc-4.4/changes.html"]Use of the cpp assertion extension will now emit a warning when -Wdeprecated or -pedantic is used. This extension has been deprecated for many years, but never warned about.[/QUOTE]
