### Post by Liam_Murphy on 2016-06-29
This is a bit of a rough fix, but hopefully it will get you started.

Firstly, draw a line,

```
Insert > Shape > Line > Line 
```

Draw the line with your mouse.

Then,

```
Right Click Line Itself > Line 
```

After doing the above, a box will come up, with "Arrow styles" being displayed on the right.

I hope this does it for you!

### Post by grahammechanical on 2016-06-29
I do not know what verion of Libreoffice you have but with version 5.1 we open the Gallery. Select Arrows. Select an arrow shape and right click and select Insert. And the Arrow is inserted in the page where the insertion point is clicked. It appears in a resizable box.

As regards formula go to Table>Formula or press F2 and a panel will appear where you can type the formula
