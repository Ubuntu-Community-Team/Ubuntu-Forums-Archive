### Post by bodhi.zazen on 2008-12-05
This is a recurring discussion.

Antivirus is of very limited utility in Linux.

Antivirus will not protect you from zero day exploits.

Known exploits are patched (unlike Microsoft), to the solution is to keep your system up to date.

Linux security is different from Windows, and I suggest you look at apparmor and selinux (apparmor is on Ubuntu and SUSE, selinux for Fedora or Centos).

For general security :

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=510812](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=510812)

I am working on a how to for apparmor but it is not yet ready.
