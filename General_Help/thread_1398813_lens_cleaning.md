### Post by cariboo on 2010-02-05
From my experience as a hardware tech, those lens cleaners don't work, and in some cases can actually destroy the laser, depending on the type. The best way to clean a cd lens, is to get a can of air, open the drive and spray the compressed air at the lens. If your drive still doesn't work, it is probably time for a new one.
