### Post by sayakb on 2008-08-03
Extract and copy the themes to the **.themes** folder and icons to the .**icons** folder in your home directory. These are hidden folders (press Ctrl+H to reveal them). Then goto *System->Preferences->Appearance* and press Customize. Click on the **Icons** and the **Controls **tabs to apply the new themes/icons.
Also, start screenlets manager by typing:
```
screenlets-manager
```
at a terminal, and add the screenlets you want to.
