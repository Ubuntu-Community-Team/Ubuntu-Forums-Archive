### Post by ditsch on 2008-03-21
You have to use the video kernel parameter or ps3videomode to change the resolution. See [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/PlayStation_3](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/PlayStation_3) or [http://wiki.ubuntuusers.de/Playstation_3#Bildschirm](http://wiki.ubuntuusers.de/Playstation_3#Bildschirm) (German) for more information and assignment tables.
