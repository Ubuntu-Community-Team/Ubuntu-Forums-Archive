### Post by motorcity909 on 2010-01-31
Finally got this sorted thanks to an extension.

Manually Sort Folders - not version 0.4.4 on the [Mozilla website]("https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/thunderbird/addon/15102") but the new version 0.5 on the developers [site]("http://www.xulforum.org/go_code")

That new version has a new tab where you can specify the start up folder from any of your mail, news or RSS accounts.
