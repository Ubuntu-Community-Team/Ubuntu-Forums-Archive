### Post by HermanAB on 2009-06-19
That is usually an indication of a failing backlight power supply.  It may be wise to buy a new one online somewhere before it fails completely.  If have replaced several of these things over the years.  Open the screen (search with Google for disassembly instructions), find the little PSU board (it has some little coils and things on it) then search for the part number with Google and after a while you'll find a laptop parts shop in the far east that sells it.  It should cost about $50.

### Post by synapsys on 2009-06-19
It's called an inverter board. It inverts DC power to AC power to run the light bulb. Much like a ballast on a flourescent light fixture, when it begins to fail, the light flickers on and off. 

P.S. if you pay fifty dollars for one, you're getting ripped off. They should cost between $15 and $30 US. Search Ebay.
