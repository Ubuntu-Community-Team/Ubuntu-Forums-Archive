### Post by Rob_H on 2009-09-05
> **raptormanad said:**
> It might be a result of having some extra eye candy. Go to System > Preferences > Apperance and reduce your visual effects.

Yup, this can be a side-effect of Compiz and the way OpenOffice draws its menus. There is a setting to tell Compiz not to apply a window animation to windows that meet certain criteria (class, name, id, etc.). You can configure this in **ccsm** under "Window Match" in the Animation plugin. I played with it for a bit, but unfortunately, I couldn't figure out what criteria to set for OpenOffice menu "windows", but maybe someone else will know...?
