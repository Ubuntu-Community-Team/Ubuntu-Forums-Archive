### Post by Carbonfish on 2006-07-31
Hi Ulysses,

Although I haven't used "aptitude", it is described as an advanced version of the apt_get command. The aptitude command provides a low-level graphical front-end to various apt and aptitude commands. Not all Debian-style distros include aptitude. I certainly don't know what might be going on with your system, but you can get an excellent start on finding out at the APT HOWTO. Available on-line from 

[www.debian.org/doc/manuals/apt-howto/index.en.html](www.debian.org/doc/manuals/apt-howto/index.en.html) 

Hope that helps. ;-)
