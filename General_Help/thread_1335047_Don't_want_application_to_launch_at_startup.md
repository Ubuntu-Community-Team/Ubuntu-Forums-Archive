### Post by OldGrantonian on 2009-11-23
I now remember why Gnome Commander launches itself at startup.

This is what I did:

 -  System > Preferences > Startup Applications

 -  Select the "Options" tab

 -  Remember Currently-Running Application

So, the correct question is:  how do I force Ubuntu to "unremember" Gnome Commander?
