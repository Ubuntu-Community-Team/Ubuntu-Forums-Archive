### Post by asdir on 2007-06-24
As far as I know you have to do it manually. 

For that right-click on the bar you want to add the "app-bar" to. Then choose "Add to Panel...". In the first category you will find sth. like "List of Windows" (translation from German, sry). Click on it once and then click on "Add" down below.

Then you can remove the "app-bar" from the place it was before. Simply right-click on the beginning of it and choose "remove/delete" (whatever it says in your language). If you cannot do that simply remove the tick from the last point in the menu you opened by right-clicking and try again.

I hope that helps.
