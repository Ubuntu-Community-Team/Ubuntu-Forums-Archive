### Post by Mickser_52 on 2012-02-28
I am having a problem when I connect a recently bought usb mobile broadband dongle.

When I plug in the device the led flashes once or twice but then nothing happens i.e. it does not connect to the internet. When I click on the network/internet icon at the top of the screen it does not show any sign of a mobile broadband device. 

If however I take out the device and plug it into a different usb port it connects within seconds? There are three usb ports on the laptop and it does not matter which one I insert it in first, the result is the same. Change the port and it will connect.

I have a dual boot machine (windows XP and ubuntu 11.04) and there is no problem connecting through windows.

Any ideas on how to solve this as I would not like to damage anything pulling it in and out?

---

### Post by Mickser_52 on 2012-02-29
I have solved this problem. I was plugging in the dongle too soon after the desktop appeared. If I wait a little while (1 minute or maybe less) and then plug it in it works perfectly.

Why this is I don't know? But it is working now. Perhaps this may help others who are having usb problems.
