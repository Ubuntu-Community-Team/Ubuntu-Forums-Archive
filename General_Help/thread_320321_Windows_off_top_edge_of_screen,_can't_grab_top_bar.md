### Post by napsilan on 2006-12-17
Alt-click works for moving windows

Also, there should be a button in the settings menu to reset all of the options back to default, if you can't get anything else to fix it.

Also, a couple of things to find while you are searching though the menu.

1. holding win+alt and writing right on the screen.  win+alt+k to clear it.  

2. middle click the title bar of any window to send it to the background (works in metacity too, but I LOVE this feature)

3. "scale windows" is actually an expose feature.  It's usually bound to F6-8 if it's turned on.  

4. shift+f9 will make it rain.  I still haven't found an actual use for this...

5. if you don't like the beryl splash when it starts, you can disable it by unchecking the "splash" plugin
