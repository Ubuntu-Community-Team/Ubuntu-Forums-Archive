### Post by rsambuca on 2007-09-20
40x is really, really fast for burning an iso file.  I would suggest a much slower burn rate for iso's.  Single bit errors can easily occur at that speed.  These errors are no big deal on something like a music CD, but on an installation iso it can cause a lot of trouble.
