### Post by scouser73 on 2010-01-10
Yes, copy the file **.thunderbird** to the external drive.  The **.thunderbird** folder not only has your mail but your whole Thunderbird profile; email accounts, passwords, plugins and so on, so if you are installing Linux onto another machine to save time you can just move the **.thunderbird** folder back into your **/home** and install Thunderbird without having to go through the process of adding your email accounts again.
