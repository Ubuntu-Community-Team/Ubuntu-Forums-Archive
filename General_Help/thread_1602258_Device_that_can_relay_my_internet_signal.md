### Post by Grenage on 2010-10-21
Network over power is handy, but a word of caution: it totally depends on decent wiring.

I've had 200 AV+ units giving near 100Mb speeds at great range, and I've had them give 3MB max when they were a room apart.  The latter involved an electricity pay meter.
