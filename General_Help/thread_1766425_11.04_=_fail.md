### Post by nothingspecial on 2011-05-24
When you get to the partitioning bit of the install, choose manual (advanced)

Choose the partition that currently has Ububntu installed on it.

Choose to use it as an ext4 journaling file system

Check/tick the format box

Give it a mountpoint of /

Do not touch the one with windows on it

Make absolutely sure you choose the correct partition.

It goes without saying, back up anything you don't want to loose first.
