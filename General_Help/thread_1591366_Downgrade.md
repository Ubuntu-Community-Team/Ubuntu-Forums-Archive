### Post by xjesse on 2010-10-09
I've seen a few scripts that claim to be able to downgrade all packages back to the last release but they seem to be more trouble than help. The time it takes you to find and *try* these scripts you could have already reinstalled Lucid and be back to normal. Because of this, it's always a good idea to keep your /home on a separate partition. This way, you can happily install and reinstall distributions while keeping all of your Firefox, Thunderbird, Gnome Panel, etc settings.
