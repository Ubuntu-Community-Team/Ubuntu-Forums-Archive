It's been awhile since I used Windows, so bear with me.  What was C drive is your basic HD,  Linux calls it dev/sda1 and possibly dev/sda 5, your primary and swapfile partitions on what was C.  D drive was your CD/DVD drive, it is now your Media Drive.  I believe E drive or F were your USB and floppy drive designations.  Don't remember which.  The names have changed, but, they still do the same job, only better.  When you installed Linux if you chose to use the entire drive all windows designations were changed as well.
