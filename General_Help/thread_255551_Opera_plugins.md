### Post by ice60 on 2006-09-11
hi, i think you can just tell opera where the plugins are -

Tools>Preferences>Advanced>Content>Plugin Options>Plugin Path OR Change Path

you may just be able to click the Find New option, i'm not sure.

these are the paths i have with SUSE

/usr/lib/opera/plugins:/usr/lib/browser-plugins:/usr/lib/firefox/plugins:/home/**USERNAME**/.mozilla/plugins
