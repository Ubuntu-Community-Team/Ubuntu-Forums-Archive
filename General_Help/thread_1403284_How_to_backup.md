### Post by colobix on 2010-02-10
Ahh that one was easy.
THere are like 100 different ways to do a dvd backup in ubuntu.
My favourite is Remastersys
[http://scumbox.com/ubuntu/remastersys_2.0-5_all.deb](http://scumbox.com/ubuntu/remastersys_2.0-5_all.deb)
After installation you'll find it under system>administration
With Remastersys you can make either a distro DVD which means you can give it to your friends without your home folder in it.
And a version for private use with all your data such as vids, songs, pics, docs etc.
YOu can also exclude applications if the dvd comes over 4 gb or whatever it is, or you can make more cds.

OH, and remastersys makes live cd's as well as installation ready, which is awesome.

HOpe this helps you.
ANd good luck.
