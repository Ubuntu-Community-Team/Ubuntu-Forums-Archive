I'm sorry but I don't have a solution but a question. Did your network slow down after you pulled the Firewall out of the mix? I'm sorry but I miss read your post, as it states your connection has become intermittent. My solution is reconfigure your old PC back to a firewall. I use Smoothwall and I'll never have a network without a firewall again. For one my connection improved and my eyes have been opened as to the threat level of a network without. Smoothwall firewall is easy to set up and uses a remote web interface portal to admin. [http://www.smoothwall.org/](http://www.smoothwall.org/) Never leave home without it.
