### Post by fieroboom on 2009-03-20
This would best be suited in the [Hardware & Laptops Forum](http://ubuntuforums.org/forumdisplay.php?f=332).
Double check your cabling; SATA connectors are pretty small, while the cables are usually somewhat stiff, so sometimes it's easy for them to loosen when you're first putting it in. Also check your BIOS to see if there is any sort of SATA vs IDE/ATA option.
Other than that, it's not really an Ubuntu problem yet...
