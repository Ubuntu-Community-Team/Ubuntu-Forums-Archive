### Post by ghantoos on 2008-10-06
CCSM is CompizConfig Settings Manager.

System -> Preferences -> Advanced Desktop Effects Settings

Click on the "Desktop Cube" then, int eh General tab, uncheck Mipmap.

Hope this helps,
