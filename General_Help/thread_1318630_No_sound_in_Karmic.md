### Post by AlP36 on 2009-11-07
I don't know for sure that this will help, but it seemed to fix mine.
Go to System-->Preferences-->Sound. Go to each tab and increase the slider for the volume and make sure that none of the Mute boxes are checked. 
After that you can try try the sounds by opening the Sound Effects tab and clicking on each sound. I don't know that a music cd will play as I haven't gotten that far in using this new os. Hope this helps you some.
