### Post by Qrk on 2006-04-03
1) To install the theme you just download the tar file and drag in into the "install themes" dialog on the theme manager. (System->Preferences->Theme) Or you can extract the tar to /usr/share/themes.

2) Firefox has this same functionality in Ubuntu. Click and drag the link to the tab bar. Also, you can open a link in a new tab by middle clicking (on the scroll wheel) or clicking both the right and the left mouse buttons.
