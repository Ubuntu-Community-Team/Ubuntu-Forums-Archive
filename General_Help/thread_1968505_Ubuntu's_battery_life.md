### Post by jadtech on 2012-04-29
your battery charge  is going to vary depending on how old it is and how  hot your lap top get as well as what your using  it for at the time ..
if your monitor brightness is set more then 50% that  use more battery too..

video full scren will eat your battery up in less then 2 hours  a newer computer and battery fresh from the box may do slightly better video not only uses more power cause the fans to run  full speed none stop as well as the monitor...

two other things are as hard on the battery  the frist shutting down and rebooting over and over , and letting the lap top sit days on end not used ..

its not  the all the fault of the OS if the battery life is low the first thing is to keep your computer clean so it runs cool not near over heating all the time..

avoid have graphics and fancy themes over loading your desk top with icon heavy graphic loaded web pages  watching  movies and lots of videos, the battery on a lap top that is in use at some point  nearly 24/7 has a life  of 6 to 8 months  though you will quickly notice and downward  trend with its life over that time and beyond that point life can become very rough less then an hour per charge no matter how well the battery and lap top is cared for ..

lap tops used heavily  will benefit from having  several batteries and a separate charger  this way it can run till the battery need replacing  and never charged by the lap top its self  as regular practice ..

a year old battery weather windows 7 or ubuntu  will charge in about 3 and half hours  just simple surfing and email will have a life of 2 hours 20 mins watch videos the life would be roughly 90 mins  for over 3 hour charge ..
