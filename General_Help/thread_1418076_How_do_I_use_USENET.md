### Post by -Robert- on 2010-02-28
Does your ISP provide a news server? If that isn't the case you've to use a payserver or look for a free news server.
This site could be handy: [http://www.binaries4all.com/linux/](http://www.binaries4all.com/linux/)
Also read this: [http://www.binaries4all.com/beginners/](http://www.binaries4all.com/beginners/)

Which newsreader you should use is really your own decision, just try a few and decide which one is best for you.
You can even use Grabit (with Wine).
