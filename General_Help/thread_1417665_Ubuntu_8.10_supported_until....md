### Post by howefield on 2010-02-27
It's supported till April 2011 on the desktop and a further 2 years for server edition.

After that, it won't be updated, that essentiually gives you one year to update to the next LTS, which is Lucid Lynx 10.04 due to be released in April this year.

It means that after the support ends it will not get any more bug fixes or security updates, and the repositories will be closed.

..and of course any paid support you might have will end as well.

(you can still install the existing packages if you change your sources.list to point to old-releases.ubuntu.com but unless it's absolutely impossible for some special reason you should just upgrade to some supported release instead.)
