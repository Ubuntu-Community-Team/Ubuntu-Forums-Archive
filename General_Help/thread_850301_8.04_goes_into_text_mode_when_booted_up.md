### Post by jualin on 2008-07-05
Maybe this will help. The [quiet]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/GrubHowto#Modifying%20boot%20options%20in%20GRUB") boot option of Grub is in charge of the verbose mode (showing you words instead of the Ubuntu Orange bar). Make sure you have "quiet" on the Grub boot line. The link I suggested explains how to perform that task. Hope this helps!
