Burning the CD at a slow speed is generally recommended, as well as doing a Checksum to make sure the CD burned correctly. [http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/burn]("http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/burn")

Is your system capable of running Ubuntu? Most are, but of course if you have a much older system it may not handle it. Here are the recommendations: [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Installation/SystemRequirements]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Installation/SystemRequirements")

You might also try the Alternate CD. Sometimes it works when a normal CD won't: [URL="http://releases.ubuntu.com/karmic/"]http:
//releases.ubuntu.com/karmic/[/URL]

Hopefully the poor start you got with Ubuntu will quickly improve and you will get to experience the benefits it can bring.
