i think u are going to get a bsnl adsl connection with 2mbps or "up to 2mbps" speed. 

if it is 2mbps u will get 200kb/sec download speed for single computer and naturally 20kb/sec each, if you share it with 9 other machines. wich is near to 2g mobile internet speed which is 22-24kb/sec .


if it is up to 2mbps , than the speed will vary according to traffic at a particular time.
