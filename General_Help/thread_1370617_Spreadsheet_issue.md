### Post by Ron_ on 2010-01-02
I am using OpenOffice.org Calc v 3.1.1 OOO310m19 (build 9240) with Ubuntu 9.10.  I have reported this problem at [http://qa.openoffice.org/issues/show_bug.cgi?id=108036](http://qa.openoffice.org/issues/show_bug.cgi?id=108036) and also here, because the product says it was created by Debian and Ubuntu.

Drilling down on a DataPilot field by double-clicking sometimes causes the application to fail and Document Recovery to begin.

My DataPilot has two row fields and one column field.  Drilling down on the highest level row field is successful, but drilling down on the lowest level row field or on the single column field causes the error.

Someone else caught this problem six months ago.  Their explanation was, "it turned out to be an issue with the version of stlport Debian (and probably Ubuntu) ships with."
