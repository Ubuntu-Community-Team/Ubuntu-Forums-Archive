### Post by MustNotSleep on 2007-08-29
that looks like an awesome rig.. but if you really want to make it scream, and have the $$, why not go for 4GB of RAM right off the bat? 2GB is quickly becoming the minimum for gaming and you really ought to have some breather for highly-intensive games. bare in mind, this is a ridiculous suggestion, your machine will still excel at gaming with 2GB.. but seeing the specs and the use you're intending to give it, having 4GB wouldn't hurt at all, and will only delay your future upgrade to be on pair with the rest of your specs (that quad-core CPU, for one)..

as for Linux, you shouldn't have any issues with it.. i'm not sure if you'll have to do any specific patching to make the system use all 4 cores, but i wouldn't think so.. of course, you know, that 8800 GTS is going to be serious overkill for running in Linux, so i'd definitely keep that XP partition available at all times..

and btw, holy hell but when did that Q6600 drop to the prices of a dual-core? at $289, that is insanely cheap.. you're sure making a good purchase with that.. :)
