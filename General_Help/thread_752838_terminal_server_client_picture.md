### Post by Rocket2DMn on 2008-04-12
Distance has nothing to do with it, the problem is most likely your friend's upload speed on his/her internet.  If his VNC is set to allow a decent picture, but you're not getting it, then that is most likely the problem.
Remote desktop takes up a good amount of bandwidth, so any less than a few hundred kbps can make it difficult.  Have your friend test his/her internet speed on [http://speedtest.net/](http://speedtest.net/)
