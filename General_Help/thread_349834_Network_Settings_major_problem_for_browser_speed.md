### Post by PaulFXH on 2007-01-30
I'm currently using the opera browser which I like very much principally because of its speed.
However, its speed can change quite dramatically due to the DNS servers listed in System>Administration>Networking>DNS changing very frequently of its own volition.

This particularly happens after a reboot but can basically happen anytime even 5-10 minutes after resetting it to what it should be.

When it changes of its own accord, it always goes from the two DNS addresses I entered to one address which is actually the IP address of my router and not a DNS address at all.
While I can make the change in just a few seconds, it is extremely annoying to have to do so 3-4 times every hour.

Does anybody know what's going on here and how I can rectify it?

OK, I found out what was wrong here.
I had forgotten to change the connections settings to indicate that I have a static IP address for this computer rather than DHCP.
All's well that ends well...............
