### Post by mytwobears on 2008-08-08
I just had success setting up the Logitech Quickcam deluxe for notebooks.  Got it from Amazon for $36.00 including shipping.

I had to install easycam2, this will download and install the driver for the webcam.  I also installed camorama, which you may not need, and cameramonitor.  Anyway, after easycam2 installed the driver, Skype recognized the webcam with no problem.
