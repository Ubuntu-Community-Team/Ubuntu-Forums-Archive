### Post by geirha on 2009-02-27
With nautilus in gnome/ubuntu, holding down ctrl while dragging copies, shift moves and ctrl+shift creates a symlink. Also, dragging with the middle-mousebutton gives you a menu of options. I haven't tried kubuntu, but it is likely it uses the same kombos.
