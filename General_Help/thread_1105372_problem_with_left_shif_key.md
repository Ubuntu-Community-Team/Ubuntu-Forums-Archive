### Post by miegiel on 2009-03-24
It's probably a hardware thing. A lot of keyboards won't let you press to many keys at the same. Often you can press 4 or more keys, but with some combinations you can't even press 3 at the same time.

Only 2 solutions really. 1) Use a keyboard that can handle the combinations you use, or 2) buy a (gaming) keyboard that allows you to press more keys at the same time.
