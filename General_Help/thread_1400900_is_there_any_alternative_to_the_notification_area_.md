### Post by Ginsu543 on 2010-02-07
You might want to check out Tint2 at [http://code.google.com/p/tint2/](http://code.google.com/p/tint2/). Tint2 is actually a highly customizable panel program that can be used to replace gnome-panel. But it also has the option of displaying the notification area. Take a look at my desktop:

[[IMG]http://img534.imageshack.us/img534/8821/clean.th.jpg[/IMG]]("http://img534.imageshack.us/img534/8821/clean.jpg")

Tint2 is at the very bottom of the screen. As you can see, I have my notification area at the lower right of my desktop. If you have further questions, you can post them at [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1394464](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1394464). I first found out about Tint2 on that thread and asked around to learn how to implement it.
