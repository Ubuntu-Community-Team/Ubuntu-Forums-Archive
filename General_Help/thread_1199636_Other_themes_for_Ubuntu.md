### Post by kokkus on 2009-06-29
If you right-click from your desktop and click on "change background" click on themes. there are some themes there, but you'll find more cool themes on the website [www.gnome-look.org](www.gnome-look.org).
They are easy to install. Download your favourite theme and just drag-drop the archives to the theme window I mentioned above.
Same thing with mouse themes, icon themes etc.

### Post by hibliss on 2009-06-29
For standard Ubuntu with Gnome, go to [http://gnome-look.org]("http://gnome-look.org") as others have suggested.

Here are the top rated GTK themes for your window and font settings -- [http://gnome-look.org/index.php?xsortmode=high&page=0&xcontentmode=100]("http://gnome-look.org/index.php?xsortmode=high&page=0&xcontentmode=100"). These will also change the icons in your quick launch bar and desktop icons.

Top GDM themes (login splash screen when you start up) -- [http://gnome-look.org/index.php?xsortmode=high&page=0&xcontentmode=150]("http://gnome-look.org/index.php?xsortmode=high&page=0&xcontentmode=150")

They also have a ton of X11 mouse cursor themes and some really nice homemade desktop backgrounds.
