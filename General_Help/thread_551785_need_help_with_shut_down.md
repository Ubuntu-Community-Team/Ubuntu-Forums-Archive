### Post by Tobster on 2007-09-15
I think that is a hardware problem. it is a regular one no matter of the OS so it is not Ubuntu.  Disconnect your computer complete  all cable and make sure the power cable is removed - That very important.

Now press and hold the power button of a 30 seconds and make sure it is 30 seconds.  I am willing to bet money that the computer power light turns on?

That because there is too much power still inside the computer.  After 30 seconds release the power button for say another 30 seconds.  

Now press the power button on again and hold for 30 seconds.

Do the the above at least 4 or 5 times.

Because I do not know how many hours you been using your computer before shut down failure I need to ask you to leave your computer disconnected for a few hours 8  maybe best so do all the above in the evening and leave disconnected over night.

Don't be tempted to test the computer because you will only make the problem worst.  When you got time the next day re-connect your computer and it should work.

Parker Bell is a good brand but there PC are prone to this failure.
