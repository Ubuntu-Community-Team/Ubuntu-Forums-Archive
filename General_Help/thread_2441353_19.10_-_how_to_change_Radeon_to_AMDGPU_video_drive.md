### Post by GhX6GZMB on 2020-04-22
My laptop has an RV516/X2300 AMD/Radeon video chip, which is giving me problems when logging in (flashing screen).

According to "lshw -c video" the video driver is "radeon".

I've now installed the "AMDGPU" driver from the "oibaf" PPA site with success.

But how do I make my laptop use the new driver instead of the "radeon" one? Any suggestion is appreciated.

Thank You.

### Post by CelticWarrior on 2020-04-22
amdgpu does NOT support old cards.

---

### Post by QIII on 2020-04-22
When you installed Ubuntu, a determination was made regarding which driver supported your GPU.  Either the amdgpu module was set to be loaded or the radeon driver was set to be loaded, depending on which supported your hardware.

In your case, the radeon module was set to be loaded.  There is nothing more to do -- except to uninstall the amdgpu you installed to avoid a mess.
