### Post by mcduck on 2009-08-10
In Linux you don't usually download any software from web sites and such, but instead use the package management tools which will automatically download and install programs for you (and also handle uninstalling and updating of all your software).

You may want to read this: [http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/installingsoftware]("http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/installingsoftware")
