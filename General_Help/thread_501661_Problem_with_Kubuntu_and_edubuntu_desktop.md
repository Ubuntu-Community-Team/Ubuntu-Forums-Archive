### Post by Crakie on 2007-07-15
Please do no use the the big bold font, it is like shouting.

The clean desktop you see is the standard KDE-desktop which you can start customizing to your liking. Unlike Ubuntu, the Kubuntu desktop is not tweaked for eye candy by the developers: you have to do that yourself. If you do not feel like doing that, you can go back to Ubuntu/Gnome by selecting a Gnome session in the login screen (before entering your name/pw). I do not think you lost anything.
