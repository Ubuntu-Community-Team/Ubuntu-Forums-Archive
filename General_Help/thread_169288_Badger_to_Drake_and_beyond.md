Apologies, I just realised that this was better suited for the Install / Upgrade forums.

In the meantime, I found this:

To upgrade from breezy to dapper:
 1. Open /etc/apt/sources.list in your favorite editor
2. Search/replace ‘breezy’ –> ‘dapper’
3. apt-get update
4. apt-get dist-upgrade
