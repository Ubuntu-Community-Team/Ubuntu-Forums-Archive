### Post by amanchesterman on 2013-11-13
I have used Base, I have written a couple of databases over the years.

The way you phrase your question makes me wonder if in fact Base is what you want. You mention creating drop-down lists "for two of the table columns". The point is, a database isn't a table. It's a collection of information that can be indexed and sorted in various ways. People often display the data from a database **as** a table, but that's not how it's stored or organised by the computer. If what you want to do is to create a table which lists your photos (with information about them such as the camera, type etc.) then you need to be using Libre Office Calc, not Base.

I won't go further with advice now until I understand more clearly what you want to do. However I can recommend the Libre Office forum:
[http://en.libreofficeforum.org/](http://en.libreofficeforum.org/)
and the Open Office forum:
[https://forum.openoffice.org/en/forum/](https://forum.openoffice.org/en/forum/)

They are dedicated to the software and in my experience you are more likely to get expert advice there than in this forum, which is about the Ubuntu operating system. (Note that the Open Office software suite is very similar to Libre Office and much of the information and advice applies to both. Also the OO forum has a whole section of guides and tutorials, many aimed at beginners.)

I hope this helps!
