### Post by DeMus on 2010-05-16
When Karmic is running well for you stick with it. You can still use it for another year until support is over. As others say you could give it a try on another system or disk to try it but don't loose your Karmic installation yet. Even going back to Karmic means install it fresh, configure it the way you want it and still end up with things which don't work as they work now.
Lucid doesn't have so many new things on the surface, below deck is where they changed it. Yes, it has a new look but who is using the standard supplied look? You'll change it any way, right?
I would say: stick with Karmic when you are happy and can do all you want to do with your computer.
