### Post by mytwobears on 2007-07-18
Well, if you just wanted to back up your Home folder, you can use Tarlimit.  It's a great little backup program.

[http://tarlimit.sourceforge.net/index.html]("http://tarlimit.sourceforge.net/index.html")

There's  also partimage.  This will make an image of your hard drive or partition.  You have to use it on an unmounted drive or partition.

[http://www.sysresccd.org/Main_Page]("http://www.sysresccd.org/Main_Page")
