### Post by StuartN on 2009-09-10

You could use Easytag to rename and move all the files based on the artist and album names stored in the tags.

Personally, I would do it by hand in either Nautilus or Krusader (or even the terminal!) because nothing beats the human eye and brain for spotting and correcting anomalies in structured data. The time you spent collecting, and continue to spend listening to the files makes them one of the most valuable assets on your home PC.
