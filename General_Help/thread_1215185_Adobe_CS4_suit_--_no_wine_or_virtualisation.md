### Post by starcraft.man on 2009-07-16
I'm not a Mac expert, but I'm gonna have to agree with Sonn. I don't think you could get Mac CS4 to work, those apps as I understand are built against cocoa and other Mac libraries that linux doesn't have. Darwin is available, you can search for it and try to get it all working properly, but I don't think that'll work.

My opinion, your best bet is to just get an ol' xp install and put it in a VM like virtualbox. Works fine IMO, newer versions have implemented graphic support to the host.
