### Post by rbc on 2009-08-26
If you want an easy GUI way to do it, install startupmanager. Afterwards, it will appear under System-->Administration. Click on the Appearance tab, and at the bottom will be the options to change the usplash. Of course, you will have had to have downloaded and installed the other usplashes before starting the Startup Manager
