### Post by squaregoldfish on 2008-12-04
Actually, I've managed to get the camera working (just hadn't got round to posting back here).

Once you've visited the host site, go to the Flash Settings Manager:

[http://www.macromedia.com/support/documentation/en/flashplayer/help/settings_manager06.html]("http://www.macromedia.com/support/documentation/en/flashplayer/help/settings_manager06.html")

Find the site in the list, and select Always Allow. You can then return to the site, and enabling the camera & mic will work.

Steve.

**Edit:** I am using the x64 version of Flash, and it seems to be fine!
