### Post by P4man on 2009-10-23
there is no difference afaik between either approach.

upgrading firefox or OO is something entirely else. Ubuntu (Canonical) doesnt support it. They only provide official support for the versions that shipped with ubuntu. If you want to upgrade your applications, you need to use non official repositories. Have a look here:

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/UbuntuBackports](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/UbuntuBackports)
