### Post by efflandt on 2013-07-08
Of course you cannot "mount" a blank CD or DVD with nothing on it. You cannot mount an unformatted hard drive either. You need some recognizable file system on any storage device before it can be mounted.

If once you burn a CD or DVD it automounts okay, then you have no issue.
