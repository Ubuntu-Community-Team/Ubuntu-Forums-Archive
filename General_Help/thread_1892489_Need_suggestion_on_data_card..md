### Post by Mark Phelps on 2011-12-08
That could mean that it won't work in Linux.

I recently acquired 4G LTE USB modem through Verizon and it won't work at all in Linux.  I didn't have Linux in mind when I got it, but I was surprised to see that it won't work at all.

Apparently, some USB modems (the LG being one of them) use custom software to perform the telephone and data commands -- software that is not available for Linux.

So, I would suggest you do what you can to CONFIRM that this modem will work in Linux.
