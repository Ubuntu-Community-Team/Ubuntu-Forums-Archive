### Post by papibe on 2014-09-10
Hi porky.chops. Welcome to the forum ):P

It seems you have an equivalent of a P4 or at best a Pentium D, right?

The (vanilla) Ubuntu desktop was designed for relative modern machines. The desktop experience requires hardware video acceleration, You could gain performance by adding a discrete graphic card. Something at simple as a Nvidia 210 (about $30-40) would work.

Having said that, there are better alternatives for a machine like that. Take a look at [Xubuntu]("http://xubuntu.org/"), which uses a more traditional desktop with no effects. Also, if you are looking for the lightest desktop, which also happens to look closer to Windows, try [Lubuntu]("http://lubuntu.net/").

Hope it helps. Let us know how it goes.
Regards.
