### Post by Ripose on 2009-02-17
Do you mean cycle through screensavers when the sreensaver is running? If so click on RANDOM at the top of the list.

Do you mean cycle through the screensavers to see what each one looks like? If so just click on one and watch the preview window. If you want a full screen preview click PREVIEW and cycle through them with arrow keys at the top right of your screen.

This is assuming you are talking about the DEFAULT screensaver.
