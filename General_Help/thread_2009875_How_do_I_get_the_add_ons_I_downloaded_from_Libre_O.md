### Post by Toz on 2012-07-02
Here is one way:

When you click on an add-on (extension) to download it, instead of choosing the default to open with Archive Manager, choose instead to Save the file. It should be saved to your Downloads directory. 

Using your file manager (Nautilus), go to your ~/Downloads directory, right-click the file you downloaded and select "Open With Libreoffice" and follow the prompts.
