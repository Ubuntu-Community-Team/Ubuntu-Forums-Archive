### Post by CatKiller on 2011-08-17
Almost no CDs have CD text encoded on them. The automatic identification of tracks needs one of the many Internet services that do this. Most of the software that is able to rip audio is also able to do these look-ups.

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/CDRipping](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/CDRipping)
