### Post by midgemiles on 2007-11-21
When I installed Ubuntu, I was given option to use proprietary driver for ATI Radeon 9600 card on my system and I said yes.

That was a mistake, because (although I didn't realise it at the time) I kept getting sudden crashes in firefox, and sometimes the system would log out and ask me to log in again.

Without knowing the driver was the problem, I followed the instructions at [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RadeonDriver]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RadeonDriver")
to remove the proprietary driver and install the open source driver.   Those problems ceased.

So from my experience, use the open source driver for ATI Radeon cards (but check for compatibility in the lists found in the above link).
