### Post by LazyMech on 2011-07-07
Hello there...

I am deffinitely a newbie messing with ubuntu on EC2.

I just launched an instance on EC2, and everything seems work except the IP restriction by .htaccess. My .htaccess is only: 


    Order deny,allow
    Deny from all
    Allow from 88.246.163.6

And it doesn't restrict access.. What am i missing?

Thx in advance!

---

### Post by Habitual on 2011-07-07
try this instead:
```
<Limit GET POST>
order deny,allow
Deny from all
Allow from 88.246.163.6
</Limit>
```

---

### Post by LazyMech on 2011-07-07
Hi Habitual... Thx for the reply!

Actually i solved it. It's because of the AllowOverride None in the 000-default under etc/apache2/sites-enabled. I didn't notice that httpd.conf was empty and teh settings are delivered to 000-default. Changing AllowOverride None to All solved it!
