### Post by x64Jimbo on 2007-01-13
The UA switcher is a very cosmetic thing. It does not even attempt to disguise what plugins your browser has, so if a page is asking your browser for a list of installed plugins, it will easily discover that you are not IE and will probably lock you out that way.
