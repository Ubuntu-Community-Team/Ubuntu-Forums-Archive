I also have a Dell and have had many issues in the few months I have had it. So far, it appears all to be software related and so far all have been referenced with suggestions on how to solve. I also had the same crashing problem which ended in 3 or 4 full reinstalls and lots of frustration. It would update, restart and kernel panic on me. I think one issue is related to having the USB ports connected while booting or lacking a hardwired connection during the restarts after updates. Got a hardwire connector via USB and that helped overcome after a few hard reboots, the kernel panic.  

To me part of the problem is with updates. Dell came with Ubuntu 14.04. Since I got the laptop updates are troublesome and cause all sorts of unexpected things. Usage of my Ubuntu machine has been problematic to the point I go back to windows. I cannot attest that the problems are not due to me not knowing the consequences of what I am doing, but reading this message let me think it might not be just me.
