### Post by saundesj on 2008-05-12
If your rulers are displayed (Shift+Ctrl+R) the easiest way is to grab the ruler with the move tool (M) and drag. Alternatively, you can click Image (Alt+I) then Guides (G) and finally New Guide (G). After you've setup your guides try slicing the image like you were doing before.
