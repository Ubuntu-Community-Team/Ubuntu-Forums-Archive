### Post by dodle on 2009-04-19
If you don't see anything on the screen at startup and the backlight doesn't appear to be on, it is likely that the LCD inverter is bad.  Sometimes the inverter's connectors are loose or dirty, causing a bad connection.  If that is the case, it's possible to fix it by cleaning or securing the connectors and wires with electric tape.
