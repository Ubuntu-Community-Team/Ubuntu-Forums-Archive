### Post by Genius314 on 2008-08-21
You need to install the plugin package.

If you don't have it already:
Go [here]("http://developer.berlios.de/project/showfiles.php?group_id=8724"), and look for the version that you have of Cairo-Dock. Download the one that has your version, and says "plugins." Install it.
You may have to restart Cairo-Dock (close it and reopen it).

Now go to Cairo-Dock, and go to Configure. I think you need to be in advanced mode for this.
Click on the applets tab, and activate the "rendering" applet.
Go to the views tab, and change sub-dock to parabolic.


To get this effect to actually appear, right-click on the dock, and 
click "add a subdock."
Now when you want an icon in the subdock, just right-click on the icon, and click "Modify Launcher." Change the container drop-down menu from "_MainDock_" to the subdock that you created.


For the neon icons, right-click on the dock, click "Manage Themes," go to the Themes tab, and select "_Neon_".

Hope that helps. :)
