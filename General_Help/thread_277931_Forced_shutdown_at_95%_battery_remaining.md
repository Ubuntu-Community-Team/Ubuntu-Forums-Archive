You may be correct but it still represents an unnecessary risk.  It may also depend on the design of the laptop.  A design can accommodate power fail transfer or hot swap but it has to be considered in the design process.  I've had laptops running on AC deal with some kinds of power failure but those failures are rarely binary like pulling the plug.
