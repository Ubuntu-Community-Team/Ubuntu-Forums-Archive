### Post by elvis6 on 2016-04-01
I decided to look through the Firefox plug-ins and add-ons I had installed, and noticed the Ubufox extension.
The fact that the "Software Center" Reviews gave it an average of 2 stars concerned me, and rose my curiosity and suspicion.
So I read through the written reviews, and among many other questionable characteristics of it, there were claims
by users that it was causing Firefox to crash, and that removing the extension fixed the problem.

So I decided to give it a try, and removed the Ubufox.
I won't know right away whether this fixes the problem, because the dilemma did not occur repeatedly, or by an pattern.
But rather random and sporadically. So I will have to wait and see if I can go awhile without any of the same issues, to
definitively say whether this fixes it.
