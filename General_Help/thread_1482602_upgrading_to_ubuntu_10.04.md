### Post by colorlessprism on 2010-05-13
upgrading is not the same thing as format/installing. upgrading will, as best it can, keep your programs and settings intact. i prefer to do a fresh install. i have a partition setup that allows me to keep my documents, music, etc and still do a fresh install (i always format my /home). it boils down to preference though, some will upgrade while others will start fresh. to decide what option is for you is simple. is there anything on your computer you want to keep? if not you might prefer a blank slate. download the LiveCD and try it out. the LiveCD will give you exactly what it would be installed
