### Post by K.J. on 2010-06-18
Yes, you also might be able to recover them from your camera SD if you haven't taken any new pictures yet.

Otherwise, testdisk i a good suggestion.  Testdisk can find old partition tables that have been partially overwritten and restore them.  This will often let you get to the old data that hasn't been overwritten yet.
