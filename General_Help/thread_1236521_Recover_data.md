### Post by decoherence on 2009-08-10
you don't say why you think it crashed....

if you think it was a problem with the hard drive then you SHOULD NOT connect it to anything until you are prepared to back it up.

I suggest hooking it to a computer that is booted from the [clonezilla]("http://clonezilla.org") live CD, cloning it to another drive and trying to restore from that.

Just to be clear, if you think the hard drive is the problem, do not try to restore files directly from it. Clone it first, then work from the clone.
