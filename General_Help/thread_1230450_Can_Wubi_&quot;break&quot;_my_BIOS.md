### Post by philinux on 2009-08-03
Wubi doesn't even run until after the boot screen. Sounds like a hardware problem. Does the machine beep during post. If so make a note of them as they are the clues to the problem.

---

### Post by Arup on 2009-08-03
Nothing to do with WUBI, seems like you have a hardware issue, try reflashing your BIOS with a bootable CD if you can or reset the BIOS by taking the motherboard battery out and then putting it back in. You can also reset the BIOS with a jumper setting but for that you need to check your motherboard manual.

### Post by egalvan on 2009-08-03
A low or dead BIOS battery can sometimes cause this.

It's a standard 2032 "watch" style battery, but tends to be pricey at some stores.

Mom-&-Pop type computer stores usually have them cheaper.

Then again, driving around town to save two bucks may not be cheaper either :)

I buy them on eBay in packs of ten, 'cause I use them in various computers and remotes.
Ten last me a year or two, so it's worth it to me
(I also give a couple to friends/family)
