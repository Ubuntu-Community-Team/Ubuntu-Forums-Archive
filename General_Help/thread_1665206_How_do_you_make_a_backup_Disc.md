### Post by pbpersson on 2011-01-12
How large are your files?

I would create one folder called "backup 01-22-2011", then copy (don't move) all your folders and files into there, then open a disk burning utility such as K3B and drag the folder to the area where it says "drag your files and folders here to burn"

One CD can hold up to 650 MB of data
One single layer DVD can hold up to 4.7 GB
One double layer DVD can hold up to 8.5 GB
