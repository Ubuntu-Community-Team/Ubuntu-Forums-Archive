### Post by lavadisco on 2012-05-22
After dealing with low battery life and my fan running all the time for a few months now, I installed the proprietary ATI driver for my video card. The fan is running much less now, and only turning on at low speed when it does run. Don't know if I'll see my battery life improve, but I bet I will. Just posting this in case it's useful. A search of the forums has a lot of people tearing their hair out over this issue.
