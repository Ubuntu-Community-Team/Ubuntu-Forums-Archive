### Post by bhaveshnande on 2012-12-15
The minimum memory requirement for Ubuntu 12.10 is 768 MB of RAM and 5 GB of disk space for Ubuntu Desktop. 
[Source: [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/QuantalQuetzal/ReleaseNotes/UbuntuDesktop ]](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/QuantalQuetzal/ReleaseNotes/UbuntuDesktop ])

Your system won't slow down when you install Ubuntu, you can even try it out without installation using the Live DVD/USB and check whether it works as expected on your system.
Using Live USB: [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Installation/FromUSBStick](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Installation/FromUSBStick)
