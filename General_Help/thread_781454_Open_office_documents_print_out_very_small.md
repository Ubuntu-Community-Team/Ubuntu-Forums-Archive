### Post by bjcubsfan on 2011-09-26
I had this same problem, but found that it was actually a problem with my printer settings.  This is despite the fact that other programs did not show the same problems as openoffice.

The Fix:

1. Open the "printing" setting (System | Admin | Printing)
2. Right-click and select properties on the troublesome printer.
3. Change the per page setting (mine is under job options, pages per side and was set to 9 instead of 1)
