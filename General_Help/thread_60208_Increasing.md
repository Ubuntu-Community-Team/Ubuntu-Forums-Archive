### Post by amohanty on 2005-08-27
The reason it says 650 MB is because most standard CDs have that much space. When you are using a live cd and you dont have any valid formatted partitions that have been mounted, all you are going to see is the disk details of the CD. TO have access to more disk space you will need to partition and format the local hard disk on the machine and then mount it from within Ubuntu.
