### Post by Kateikyoushi on 2007-03-03
Avi is a container, can store different type of video and audio data, xvid is one of the video formats. Read more on the wiki. [LINK]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Audio_Video_Interleave")

### Post by mcduck on 2007-03-03
Actually Xvid is not a format at all, it's a codec for MPEG4 format.. (so it doesn't matter if you use DivX, XviD or any other MPEG 4 codec, they all use the same format and show the same video files :D)
