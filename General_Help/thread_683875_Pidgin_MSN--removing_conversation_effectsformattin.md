### Post by Therion on 2008-01-31
All I can suggest is opening Pidgin and under the Preferences menu click on the Smilies Themes tab and select the option for no emoticons at all.  In Pidgin 2.0, there is only a single option that toggles all formatting. When you disable formatting on incoming messages, bold and italic formatting should be ignored in addition to colors, sizes, and font face.  You may need to totally restart Pidgin to effect these changes.  I'm not sure why, but that's how it works for me.
