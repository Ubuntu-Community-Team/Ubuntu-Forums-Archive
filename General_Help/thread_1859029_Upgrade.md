### Post by snowpine on 2011-10-13
No. Applications are not stored in your /home folder, so you'll have to reinstall them if you reinstall Ubuntu.

Of course you do not need to reinstall to get the new release, you can also follow these upgrade instructions: [http://www.ubuntu.com/download/ubuntu/upgrade](http://www.ubuntu.com/download/ubuntu/upgrade)
