What brand of mouse is it ? Some Microsoft mice are known to have a scroll problem that sounds like what you describe when used in Linux/Ubuntu.

If your mouse is made by MS you may want to look at [http://sourceforge.net/projects/resetmsmice/](http://sourceforge.net/projects/resetmsmice/). There is a .deb file downloadable from that site that when installed here fixed the scrolling problem for me on a MS 800 wireless keyboard/mouse combination. Good luck, yeti.
