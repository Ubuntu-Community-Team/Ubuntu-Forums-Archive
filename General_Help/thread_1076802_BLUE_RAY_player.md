### Post by dorkdork777 on 2009-02-21
The problem with Blu-Ray is that there's quite a few different specifications, aka profiles - old/cheap BR players will only support the older ones (1.0/1.1), while new/expensive ones will support up to the latest, which is 2.0 (aka BR-Live), which requires an Internet connection to the player. The only player that's guaranteed to support all specs, past present and future, is the PS3.

EDIT: [learn more about the profiles here]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Blu-ray#Player_profiles").

That's one thing to consider when choosing a player.
