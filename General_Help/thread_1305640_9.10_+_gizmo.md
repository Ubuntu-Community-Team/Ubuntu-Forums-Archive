### Post by itkeepsrepeating on 2009-11-17
My Karmic + Gizmo5 combo is working, under certain conditions. I MUST start gizmo before anything else plays a sound [flash in web browser, rhythmbox], and once I start Gizmo, NO OTHER SOUND WORKS. I had to set the sound to OSS in gizmo's preferences [and restart gizmo], otherwise it complains there is no sound hardware.
