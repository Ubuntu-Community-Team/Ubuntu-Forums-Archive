### Post by ilikes2shred on 2010-11-25
I also have a similar problem.  My laptop running 10.10 randomly shuts down.  This morning when I went to use it, it was off (it shut itself off sometime during the night, even though it was on AC power), and it would not boot.  Apparently, when it randomly shuts down, it does not unmount the file-system or do any other normal shutdown processes, so my Ubuntu partition was corrupted.  Luckily, I was able to restore it by booting to a live CD and running fsck on the Ubuntu partition.
