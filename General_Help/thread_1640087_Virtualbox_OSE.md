### Post by snowpine on 2010-12-07
In your Home folder, there should be a hidden folder called .VirtualBox (Ctrl+H to view hidden folders). This is where virtual machines are stored. You can delete this folder if you want to permanently remove all of your VirtualBox virtual machines, settings, and data (don't forget to empty Trash). That should give you back the 5gb, good luck! :)
