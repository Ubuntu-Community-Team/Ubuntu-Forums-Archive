### Post by coffeecat on 2009-11-22
Or you could install the meta-package "kubuntu-desktop" (be warned - BIG download), and when you login, go to 'options' and choose KDE for the desktop. And when you get fed up with KDE, :wink: gnome in all its chocolate-brown glory will still be there for you.
