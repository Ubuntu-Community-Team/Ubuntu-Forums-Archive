### Post by Topsiho on 2018-08-10
Is it possible that your system is short on resources? To little or faulty ram? If swap is much used, the data in your ram has to be put into the swap partition on the hard disk, which, even if it is an SD, is way slower than your ram memory. Is the hard disk failing? In that case needed information can not be fetched from it, or put onto it.
No need to be paranoid, if you don't do anything stupid (install from untrusted sources) nothing serious can happen to your system.
