Install and Run Bleachbit periodically run at shutdown and reboot. Keeps the snoops out of your beeswax.

sudo apt-get install bleachbit
gksu bleachbit

Then your secret happy fun time will only be beknownst to you, and you alone.

Without that, anyone who is seriously nosey is going to find out what you are doing when you are alone with your computer.
