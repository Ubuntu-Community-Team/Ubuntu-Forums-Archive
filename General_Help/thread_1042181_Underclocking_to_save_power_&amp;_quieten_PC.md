### Post by Southtown on 2009-01-17
Underclocking and undervolting should be done through bios. But that will not save much power during idle time because the processor isn't doing much anyway. It will only effect power consumption when you least want it to, during serious use. The fans may be noisy but they don't use much power at all, and turning them down will likely cause your hardware to burn up. Have you tried downloading in sleep mode (System>Preferences>Power Management?)
