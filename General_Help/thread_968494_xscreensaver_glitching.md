### Post by OrangeCrate on 2008-12-11
> **krazykookmany said:**
> Any specific packages I should look for when removing the gnome screensaver package? Or some I should avoid?

It's been so long since I remove it, I can't remember what it removed. But, all you have to do is go to Synaptic, search for the gnome screensaver, right click on the box to the left of the entry, mark it for removal (I marked it for complete removal), and then click "Apply" at the top of the page. It'll remove itself.

I use Xfce on Xubuntu, and I assume you have Gnome. I did a search to be sure that you weren't running into any problems, and I didn't see anything. Here's an archived thread that discusses this topic. You can get some additional guidance from there.

[http://ubuntuforums.org/archive/index.php/t-193335.html](http://ubuntuforums.org/archive/index.php/t-193335.html)

Like I said earlier, any problems I had with Xscreensaver seemed to be fixed when I removed the Gnome Screensaver, but I can't guarantee for you, that that is the total answer. Just something to try.
