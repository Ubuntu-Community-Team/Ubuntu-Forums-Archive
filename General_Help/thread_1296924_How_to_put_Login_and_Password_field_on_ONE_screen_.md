### Post by 3rdalbum on 2009-10-21
It's possible to have a "Face browser" that shows what users are available on the system; you click your username/avatar and then you can type your password.

I think there are GDM themes that work with the old GDM that offer both entry fields on the same screen; check out the GDM themes at [www.gnome-look.org](www.gnome-look.org).
