### Post by amauk on 2009-02-05
you can either use the character map
(applications > accessories > character map)

or, Gnome has an applet for common accented characters

Right click top panel
click "Add to panel"
and add the "Character Palette"

this gives you easy access to common accented characters
select the one you want, then paste into your application
