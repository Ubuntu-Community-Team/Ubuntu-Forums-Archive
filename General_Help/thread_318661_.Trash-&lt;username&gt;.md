### Post by yabbadabbadont on 2006-12-15
Check in either the Nautilus settings (preferences, whichever it is called) or in gconf-editor.  There is a setting to enable a delete option that bypasses the Trash.  Then when you want to delete something from removable media, select it, right-click, and choose the Delete option.
