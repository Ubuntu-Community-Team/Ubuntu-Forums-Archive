### Post by mgmiller on 2007-02-19
This took me some digging.  I don't mind the tool tips.  They can also be made to do some cool things with Beryl, but I digress.  Here is how to make them go away:

Go to Applications>System Tools>Configuration Editor.

This is easiest to use full screened.

On the left side Click the little triangle to the left of Apps to make it point down instead of to the right.  This will  open a long list, scroll down to Panel and click to open that list.  Next left click once to high light the "global" entry in the list.

Finally, on the right side of the screen, near the bottom of the list is an entry called "tooltips_enabled"  Just untick the checkbox next to it and you're done.

I would suggest that before you close out of configuration editor, you bookmark the spot you are in so you can get back to it easily.  At the top of the window for configuration editor,  you will see the File, Edit, Bookmarks, Help drop downs.  Simply click the Bookmark drop down and select "Add Bookmark".  If you need to get back to this spot quickly, after starting config editor, click the Bookmarks at the top and you will see your saved spots, it brings you right to them.

Good Luck
