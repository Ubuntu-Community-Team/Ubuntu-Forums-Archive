### Post by MSPhobe on 2008-10-04
I'm very much a Ubuntu newbie... but...

I spent hours today struggling with a problem using ooBase under Ubuntu. The problem was much discussed in the past... and is with us to this day.

If you want the Forms Wizard to work...

Change your Java RTE.
Change your OpenOffice installation from the "integrated" one provided by the Ubuntu team to the "standard" one provided by the OpenOffice team.

Now.... the above are not without their perils. The "solution" to the Forms Wizard problem may cause more NEW problems than fixing the Forms Wizard is worth... but we'll find out, won't we?

My sanity was saved by....

[http://www.stchman.com/install_oo.html](http://www.stchman.com/install_oo.html)

where up to date (Oct 08) advice is posted.

Many of the old posts talk about using "alien" on the RPS files from OpenOffice. You don't need to do that, as long as you download the DEBS version from OpenOffice. Before you start the download, be sure to untick the "include JRE" box.

Good luck... enjoy...
