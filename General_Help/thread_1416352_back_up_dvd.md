### Post by Mark Phelps on 2010-02-27
Just so you know the limitations of what you're trying to do ... suggest you read the link below regarding K9copy:

[http://http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/K9Copy]("http://http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/K9Copy")

Basically, unless you have a Dual-layer DVD write and Dual-layer DVDs, you can't make a full copy of the original DVD.  You will have to leave some stuff out and/or use compression that will result in quality loss.
