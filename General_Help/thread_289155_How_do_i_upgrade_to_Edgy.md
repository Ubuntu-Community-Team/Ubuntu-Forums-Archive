### Post by AirRaven on 2006-10-30
> **Somenoob said:**
> From an edgy cd?

I'd suggest it. A lot of people have apparently been having major trouble upgrading from Dapper to Edgy- I'd reccomend waiting about 3 weeks or so until the teething phase is over and done with and then doing a fresh install from the Edgy Install CD. If you don't want to wait, a clean install's the best option anyway- an upgraded system tends to have a lot of stuff left hanging around. Just backup and reinstall.

It's not worth the hastle of upgrading quite yet.
