### Post by baconman on 2010-06-14
This might be of interest to other people here so I reply to my own question. I went out and bought the Pinnacle Dazzle Video Creator Platinum DVC107 (there's a mouthful!). It works great with (K)Ubuntu, both the 10.04 install on my main laptop and the Eeebuntu 2.0 install on my EeePC 701.

The picture is sharp (at least with S-video input; I haven't tried composite yet) and there's no discernible latency which is essential for me as my main use for it is to use my PC as a display device for old video games and 8-bit computers - it's hard to play fast action games if the video frames are delayed by half a second!

There is a noticeable delay in the sound when using the sound input on the Dazzle, but it's just as easy to route the sound through the computer's line in, so that's no big deal.
