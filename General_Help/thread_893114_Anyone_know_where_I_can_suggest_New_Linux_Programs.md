I have never heard of anything.  It seems to me that the vast majority of open-source programs are written by the developer first to suit his/her needs and only secondly for the benefit of others.  Everyone scratches their own itch, in other words.

This isn't necessarily a bad thing, but it means that not many developers are going to dedicate precious time to writing programs that they don't plan on using themselves.  The programs that are most useful to a developer are not necessarily the ones that everyday users are screaming for.
