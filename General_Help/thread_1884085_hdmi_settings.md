### Post by mr.farenheit on 2011-11-20
my goal is to be able to connect my pc to my tv via hdmi so i can watch my movies and such on a larger screen. is there any way to configure it so that i can close the screen on my notebook and still display the desktop image on the tv without the screen going blank or the computer trying to go into hibernate?

---

### Post by hal8000 on 2011-11-20
When you close the lid the default action is to suspend.
If you are using Gnome desktop change power management settings:

[http://projects.gnome.org/gnome-power-manager/gpp.html](http://projects.gnome.org/gnome-power-manager/gpp.html)

So that closing the lid has no effect. Depending on your laptop make/model
it may not be possible to do this anyway, as screen display may be necessary to output to the HDMI connector
