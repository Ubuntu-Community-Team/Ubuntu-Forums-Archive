### Post by pmg on 2007-09-06
In addition to having BIOS that supports a "wakeup at time" option, some systems support "wake on LAN".  If enabled, they watch for a special packet on the ethernet and turn on if/when they see it.  

[Here is another thread]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=317927") discussing pretty much the same question.  I quickly skimmed it and saw some description of setting a power on time in the BIOS as well as WOL.
