### Post by Dr Small on 2009-12-23
It seems to me, that there is a setting somewhere, that I have seen once before, but I can not seem to locate it, or even have an idea of where it would be...

Is there a setting that allows multiple users to log in (via fast-user-switcher) and switch between accounts **without** prompting for the password at the lock-screen dialog?

Thanks,
Dr Small

---

### Post by Dr Small on 2009-12-23
I solved it.
The solution to my problem was, log into the accounts, go to the preferences for the Fast User Switcher and uncheck "Lock screen after switching users". Now, we can switch users, and do not have to be prompted with the lock-screen dialog to enter our passwords to access each others accounts. :)
