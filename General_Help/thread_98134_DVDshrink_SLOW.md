### Post by RavenndudE on 2005-12-03
Ok, I found the fix for my self and thought I would post it just incase anyone searches and finds this post ....

To fix slow rip speeds on DVDshrink using WINE, after you hit the backup button go to the "Options" tap and un-check the "run backup in low priority mode" box.
