### Post by Bashing-om on 2013-11-24
lucas12zarzur;

Not to seem abrupt, not my intention at all.

The only way to learn linux (ubuntu) is to sit down in front of it ..in an installed operating system and begin using it ! .. Read a lot and try what you read and see the results.

From my experience, there is nothing in the many books that is not available on-line, and the fora are my number one resource.
The tools required for fault isolation and restoration is an agile mind and a knowledge of the tools at your disposal. You gain that knowledge by reading and comprehension.

Due to the many and varied problems - both hardware and software - there is no cook-book to give an answer. Learn the operating system is the only sure way to proceed.
Keep in mind, no one knows it all, make up your own notes as you go !

This forum supports many avenues to learn ... see the stickies and -for starters - the link in my signature, NewDocs.
