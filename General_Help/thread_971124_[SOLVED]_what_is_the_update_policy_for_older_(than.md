### Post by m_duck on 2008-11-04
I believe after a new release is out, the 'old' one will still receive security updates and patches but does not receive new versions of programs. For that it is necessary to upgrade. The LTS (long term support releases) like Hardy will receive the security updates and patches for longer than the non-LTS releases.

[More details](http://www.ubuntu.com/products/whatisubuntu/serveredition/benefits/lifecycle)
