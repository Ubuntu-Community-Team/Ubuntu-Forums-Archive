### Post by yoshii on 2016-01-19
Battery consumption really depends upon how you use your computer in terms of BIOS settings, which types of programs you use, the amount of hard drive activity you generate, and your power settings for idling, how you have your screen configured, how big your web and other caches are, and how many USB devices you use.  

That being said, WattOS is based upon Ubuntu and is supposedly designed to be energy efficient.  There are a couple of different versions of that.  You may want to give that a try.
