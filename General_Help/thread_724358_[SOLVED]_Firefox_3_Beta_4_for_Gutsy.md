### Post by zugu on 2008-03-18
You won't get an official backport of Firefox 3 for Gutsy from Canonical.

This is the same thing that happened to Firefox 2 and Ubuntu Dapper. People had to rely on third party repositories to get their job done, and even then, the newer version did not replace the original one, it just ran alongside. That is because replacing Firefox with a newer version actually b0rks your system.

So use a third party repo and assume the associated risk that comes with using it, or upgrade your whole distribution every 6 months, or whenever a newer version of Firefox appears.
