### Post by indytim on 2011-02-08
Unless the computers are evenly matched on the hardware, I think you'll run into more fidgeting around trying to get the hardware handshake to play nicely on the second computer than it's worth.  My $0.025 worth is to install fresh on the second computer, then selectively copy the settings of the major apps located in /home over to the child computer.
