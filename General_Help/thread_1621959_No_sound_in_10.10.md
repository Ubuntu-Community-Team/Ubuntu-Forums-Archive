### Post by jarmot on 2010-11-16
I'm entirely new at this as well, but I've been having tons of problems with sound.  The first thing I think most people would recommend is to check to see if the sound is muted.  Go to Applications>Accessories>Terminal to open the terminal and type in "alsamixer"  You should be able to tell if anything is muted because it will have a grey MM underneath it.  Select over with your arrow keys to that column and press "M" to unmute.  Check that out first.
