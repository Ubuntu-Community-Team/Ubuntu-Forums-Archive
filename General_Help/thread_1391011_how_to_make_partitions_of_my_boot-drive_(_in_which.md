### Post by Monotoko on 2010-01-26
Yepp its quite simple
Boot up from your LiveCD, then go to System -> Administration -> Partition Manager

From there, shrink your "main partition" and put another one in the free space, you can find a lot of guides online for doing that.

Hope this helps :)
