### Post by meho_r on 2007-11-16
I noticed that in Adobe Reader 8 dual pages view doesn't display correct set of pages. In Evince and Adobe Reader 7 the first page of document is right page, the second is left, etc., giving a view as it will be in the book (1st alone, 2nd and 3rd together, etc.). But in Kpdf and Adobe Reader 8 the first page is left, the second is right which is incorrect and makes review of document difficult as it puts together wrong pages -- 1st and 2nd instead of 1st alone, 2nd and 3rd together. Anyone has an idea how to make AReader 8 behaviour 'normal'? Thanks

---

### Post by meho_r on 2007-11-20
OK, solved. With help of a kind person from Adobe forum, I've learned the solution: When in Two-Up mode activate ''Show Cover Page During Two-Up'': View > Page Display > Show Cover Page During Two-Up.
