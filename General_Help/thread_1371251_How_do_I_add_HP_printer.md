### Post by Sidewinder1 on 2010-01-03

### Post by sgosnell on 2010-01-08
Click on New, select the type of printer, either local or network, and have the wizard find the printer.  Select it, and then the manufacturer and model, and the drivers will be installed.  Printers are pretty much plug and play in Linux.
