### Post by vasa1 on 2015-09-10
I don't think there's an absolute *yes* or *no*. 
Excel sheets may contain macros which LibreOffice can't handle.
Excel sheets may contain formats which LibreOffice can't handle.

If you use Windows at your workplace and work from home as well, it would make sense to use the same operating system in both places to avoid compatibility issues.

### Post by The Cog on 2015-09-10
I use libreoffice at work on windows even though everyone else uses office (which is also on my PC). I do this because I am more familiar with LO and prefer the interface to that of Excel.

I haven't seen any compatibility issues for a long time although I gather there still are some issues with advanced macros. We have an expenses spreadsheet .xlsx which is full of macros, and I always use MSOffice for that just to be on the safe side, although it always seems to work if I accidentally open it in LO instead.
