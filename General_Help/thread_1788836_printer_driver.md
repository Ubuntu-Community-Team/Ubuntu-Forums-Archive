### Post by inameiname on 2011-06-23
Googling this printer, It seems the Dell 810 doesn't have any Linux drivers available, and probably won't ever. 

I too have a Dell AIO (might even be this very one actually) and have come to the same conclusion about it. 

I've also heard Dell printers often are rarely capable of working on Linux. I guess there are a rare few (mine not being one of them) that can use Lexmark Linux drivers to work, but very few. And of those, I've never heard of one that can actually do more than print (not scan, fax, or etc).
