### Post by JKyleOKC on 2012-05-19
Try just typing in your password without waiting for any response, then hit Enter. The absence of any response is normal when entering passwords in the terminal; it's a security feature. However it's quite confusing, since most of us normally wait for some indication that the keystroke was accepted.
