### Post by rmoreno79 on 2010-06-13
I have a Acer Aspire One netbook running Lucid Lynx.  Sound quality is fine through my speakers, but when I plug in headphones things go awry.  The headphones are working because the speakers mute and I can hear musical instruments playing on the track, but I can't hear any of the voices in the music.  It's as if the voices are muted, but I can still hear the guitars, drums, bass, etc.  When I unplug the headphones the track plays normally, voices and all, through the speakers.

I have looked through the forums for weeks trying to find a fix.  Please help if you can.  Thanks.

### Post by stinkeye on 2010-06-14
I had a similar problem where the sound through headphones was very faint 
even though volume was set at max in sound preferences.
I installed **gnome-alsamixer** which showed the usb sound volume as minimum.
Changed the volume to max for usb in gnome-alsamixer and
changed the connector to analog speakers in sound preferences as shown in pic
and it solved the problem.
