### Post by Existentialist on 2008-03-21
In terms of directx, any card bought today will be at least dx9 if not 10, so there are no worries there.  The biggest thing is budget, you can get really good performance for $200 or less not, but if you really want more you can spend upwards of $500.  Recently, I have been telling other people that I think the best cards for the price right now are the nvidia 9600gt's which go for around $130-150.

As for the motherborad, do you want to stick with AMD, or go Intel?  Again price plays a factor here, but an Intel based board with an nvidia 680i chipset will get you really good performance, and the ddr2-800 ram is as cheap as it is going to get right now.  With rebates, you can get 4gb for under $75 of good name brand ram.

The price on dual and quad cores is getting pretty good to, I finally made the switch from AMD to an Intel recently because the price/performance ratio of the Intels is really good right now.
