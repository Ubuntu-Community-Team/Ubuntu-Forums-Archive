### Post by sudanmas on 2010-10-19
I did a fresh install of 10.4 and everything worked out of the box except for the video.  I'd visit the wrong website and the video would turn into a Picasso painting.  The only solve at that point was a reboot or a Left Alt SysRq K.

I did a tonne of poking around the forums and found;

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1471599](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1471599)

I followed Landie_UK's solve and my problem disappeared. I uninstalled **all** references to ATI drivers using Synaptic and took care of the FGLRX drivers as per his post.

Machine specs:

Gateway LT3501
2Gb Ram
250Gb HD
ATI Radeon 1200 video card
AMD Athlon 64
