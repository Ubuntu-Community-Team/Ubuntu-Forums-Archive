Is it possible to configure two seperate hard drives to work as a single hard drive? ### Post by x64Jimbo on 2006-06-27
It's called RAID (Redundant Array of Independent Disks)
You have to have the hardware to support it - a motherboard with RAID controller, or a separate RAID card.
