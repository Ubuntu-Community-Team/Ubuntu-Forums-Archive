### Post by CocoAUS on 2007-06-12
I've been quite pleased with my ATi TVWonder.  The card is loaded automatically using the open source V4L(2) driver, plays great with tvtime, plays fairly well with MythTV, and even comes with a remote (in the Pro version) that is detected and configured automatically in recent Gnome and KDE releases.  It has inputs for composite, co-ax, and s-video.

Also check [here]("http://doc.gwos.org/index.php/VideoTV") for other cards you might check out.  I just happen to know that the TVWonder is one of the cheapest and still has a great picture.
