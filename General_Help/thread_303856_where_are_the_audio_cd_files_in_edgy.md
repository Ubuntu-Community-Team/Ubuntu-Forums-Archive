### Post by orb9220 on 2006-11-21
.cda are not files they are pointers to the start of tracks.
AudioCD are one continious stream of data burnt to the cd as one giant file.

You CANNOT drag and drop audioCD tracks from an audioCD .

You have to use special software to rip audioCD to the hardrive as mp3's,ogg,flac,wav's as example formats.
