### Post by CatKiller on 2019-10-25
Your display has an entirely fixed resolution. There are exactly that many pixels. You can make things more blurry, by making the image pixels no longer line up with the display pixels, but the display will always have exactly that many pixels of exactly that size.

If you want to make text bigger, just make the text bigger.
