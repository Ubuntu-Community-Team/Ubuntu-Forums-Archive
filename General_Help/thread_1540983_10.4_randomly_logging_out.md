### Post by cavalier911 on 2010-07-30
Check /var/log/Xorg.0.log

Random logouts can be caused by xorg crashing and no fault of the display or window manager.

There is a bug report about flickering,flashing caused by the intel video driver.
[https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/xserver-xorg-video-intel/+bug/574449](https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/xserver-xorg-video-intel/+bug/574449)
