### Post by taurus on 2009-04-30
Stop using/writing to that harddrive right away.  It makes it harder to recover your files the more you write to it.  Hopefully, TestDisk can do something about it, [http://www.cgsecurity.org/wiki/TestDisk](http://www.cgsecurity.org/wiki/TestDisk).
