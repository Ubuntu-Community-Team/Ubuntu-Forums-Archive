### Post by drs305 on 2009-08-30
Refer to the "SUM" link in my signature line. It's a guide originally written on how to limit the number of displayed kernels, expanded to include how to use StartUp-Manager - a great Grub 1 menu.lst manager/tweaker.

The guide will explain how to remove kernels or limit the number of displayed kernels and explains a lot of the other options StartUp-Manager can set.

Many users prefer to keep at least two kernels visible in case the newer one causes problems after an update.
