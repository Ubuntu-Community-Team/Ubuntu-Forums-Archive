### Post by undecim on 2010-10-23
Watch out for that "unlimited" bandwidth and disk space promise. If that's the only plan a company offers, then they are going to be limiting you on something like CPU or inode access (inodes are files, devices... Pretty much everything on the server). If you want unlimited bandwidth, make sure you're getting it from a company that has multiple plans, and not just a single "one size fits all" unlimited plan, because those are just flat-out lies. No company can make a profit giving everyone unlimited resources.

I use and personally recommend [Beyond Hosting]("https://beyondhosting.net/portal/aff.php?aff=013"). I've used both their personal shared hosting, and their VPS, and both are PHENOMENALLY FAST.

Their customer support is crazy fast, too. I've only had two problems that I've needed help with (once when I was transferring my old blog over to the new hosting, and once when I misconfigured my VPS) and both times, my problem was resolved in *under 5 minutes* via the live chat on their web site.

Their unlimited personal plan meets all your requirements for $8/month.

You should look into them. Trust me, you won't be disappointed with them.
