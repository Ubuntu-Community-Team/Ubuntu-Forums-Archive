### Post by MighMoS on 2008-02-11
A rather abrupt test is to try CTRL+ALT+BACKSPACE which will kill everything that's graphical and then bring you back to the log in screen.  If that works, then its not an issue with your keyboard. If it doesn't you could try CTRL+ALT+SYSRQ+B which will insti-reboot your system -- this can be bad, but shouldn't be much worse than just powering it off.

Do you have 3d effects enabled?
