### Post by codeseer on 2009-04-16
Open Evolution and click Edit->Preferences, then click on the account and double-check the settings.

Edit:

Are you a 'Yahoo! Mail Plus' customer?

You can only use POP, if you are; or you are a customer with some other service, like ATT.

If you are a 'Yahoo! Mail Plus' customer, use [these settings]("http://help.yahoo.com/l/us/yahoo/mail/original/mailplus/pop/pop-14.html").

You might also consider ditching Yahoo! and getting a [Gmail account]("http://www.gmail.com").
