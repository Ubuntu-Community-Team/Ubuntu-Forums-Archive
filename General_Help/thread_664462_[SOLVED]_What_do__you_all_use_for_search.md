### Post by AndyCooll on 2008-01-11
You can edit the Tracker Search Tool config files and preferences so that it searches more than just your desktop. I had issues with it too initially, now I've got it working properly (and it now searches all my folders, including those on my server) and use it all the time.

I'm not at my Linux box at the moment, but I think you just right click on the Applet and choose preferences (it's also under System > Preferences > Indexing something or other).
