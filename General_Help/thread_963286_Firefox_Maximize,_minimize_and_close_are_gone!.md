### Post by zika on 2009-02-11
F11,F11,resize FF window so it fits comfortably onto the desktop, close, reopen.

**fix ***(if that happens in Compiz)* (in order to keep using Compiz):

System->Preferences->CompizConfig_Settings_Manager->Workarounds->Legacy_Fullscreen_Support(uncheck)
