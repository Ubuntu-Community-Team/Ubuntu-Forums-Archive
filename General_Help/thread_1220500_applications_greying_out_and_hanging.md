### Post by Strongman332 on 2009-07-23
> **scholzilla said:**
> thanks. Can you add more ram to a laptop?

yes there is susaly a small panel on the bottom of the laptop that says memory. open it. there should be two slots. the ram chips have metal clips that hold them in. the ram chips are spring loaded so dont be surprised when they move. take out the old chips and put the new ones in. make sure they are all the way in then push them down until the clips engage.

oh and make sure the computer is off, you have discharged all static in to a metal object, and you have the right ram for your computer
