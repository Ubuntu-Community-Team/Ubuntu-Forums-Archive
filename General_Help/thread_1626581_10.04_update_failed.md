### Post by sikander3786 on 2010-11-20
> failed to fetch [http://ppa.launchpad.net/ubuntu-mozi...86/Packages.gz](http://ppa.launchpad.net/ubuntu-mozi...86/Packages.gz) 404 Not Found

That error is pointing to a Launchpad PPA. PPAs are individual repositories often maintained by a single person so they might be down anytime with the 404 error.

Go to Software Center > Edit > Software Sources > Other Software Tab and disable the offensive PPA. Again try apt-get update and see if there are any other errors.
