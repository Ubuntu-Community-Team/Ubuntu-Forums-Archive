### Post by ciscosurfer on 2006-09-03
Try installing kivio, dia, tgif, tkined, or xfig (I like XFig, TGif, Dia, and Kivio)

If you want to pay, then SoftPlatz is a nice place to look (bottom of page, [http://www.softplatz.com/software/network-diagram/5.html](http://www.softplatz.com/software/network-diagram/5.html))

Also, you can try running other Windows apps via the WINE interface or CrossOver Office (I love SmartDraw, and have used it for many years :D)...for that matter, you can try running Visio thru WINE or CrossOver Office (it's supported through CrossOver Office!)
