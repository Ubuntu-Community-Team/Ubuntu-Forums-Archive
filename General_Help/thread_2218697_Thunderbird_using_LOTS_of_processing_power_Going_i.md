### Post by SeijiSensei on 2014-04-21
Are you disconnected from the server for long periods of time?  Do you have a lot of folders?  Use IMAP? I find if I use a computer for the first time in a while, Thunderbird takes a very long time synchronizing its views of my folders with the content on the server.  You'll see lots of "downloading" notices in the status bar on the bottom of the window.  Other possibilities include misbehaving addons.  A search for "[thunderbird slow linux]("https://www.google.com/search?client=ubuntu&channel=fs&q=thunderbird+slow+linux&ie=utf-8&oe=utf-8")" brings up a lot of discussions.  You might try compacting your folders as suggested in [http://forums.mozillazine.org/viewtopic.php?f=39&t=2720489](http://forums.mozillazine.org/viewtopic.php?f=39&t=2720489)
