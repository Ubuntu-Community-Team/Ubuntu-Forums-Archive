### Post by col48 on 2017-12-14
I don't know Inkscape, but could this be due to the properties of the shape/path/stroke?  Such as fill?

Consider what you've drawn as a filled snake-like shape rather than as a simple line.

The black regions are those which are inside the border of the shape, where any region with an odd number of border crossings from the edge of the drawing is regarded as inside.
