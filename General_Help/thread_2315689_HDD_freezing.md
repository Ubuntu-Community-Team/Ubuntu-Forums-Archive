### Post by oldos2er on 2016-03-01
I hope you have good backups of your data on the HDD, because a clicking sound might mean it's about to fail. See [http://harddriveclicking.net/](http://harddriveclicking.net/)

### Post by Bucky Ball on 2016-03-01
Backup all valuable data from that drive as soon as possible as a precaution. Clicking usually = dying.

Open 'Disks' and check the s.m.a.r.t. status of the drive/partition.
