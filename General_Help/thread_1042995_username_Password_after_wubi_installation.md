### Post by ufusername on 2009-01-20
I had the same problem myself.  One possible explanation is that for those of us on slow links, wubi installations can take 8-12 hours or more.  Wubi asks for the username and password early on, then a half day can easily pass before ubuntu finally pops up the login messages.  So, the link between the two events can get lost in the long process for busy people who have installed ubuntu/wubi by occasionally walking over to the pc to see if anything has happened yet.
