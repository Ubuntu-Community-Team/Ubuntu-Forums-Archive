### Post by Autodave on 2022-03-31
Linux is causing your keyboard problems.  Try an external keyboard.  11+ years on Linux and I have yet to hear that the OS caused certain keys not to work.  Rather, I have HEARD of complaints of that happening, but another keyboard has ALWAYS cured the problem.

Get compressed air and blow the dirt out from under the keys on the keyboard.  Rapidly and repeatedly press every key.  Blast with air again.  Repeat.  You may be able to save that KB yet.  Otherwise, you need a new KB.
