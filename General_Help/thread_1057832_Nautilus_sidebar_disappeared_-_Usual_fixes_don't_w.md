### Post by drs305 on 2009-02-02
Sometimes the sidebar *disappears* but is really there. You can't see it because it has been forced all the way to the left edge. Try moving your mouse cursor around the left edge of the window and see if you can get the horizontal arrows  <-->. If you can get them, left click and drag right to widen the sidebar panel.

### Post by Pipps on 2009-02-02
I have just unchecked the following:

Edit / Preference / Behaviour / Always open in browser windows

And it would appear to have rectified the problem.

For some reason, selecting this option previously, also removed the sidebar pane. 

How strange!

### Post by gnomeout on 2009-02-23
I just had this problem, and i went to my behavior preferences like you mentioned and found that box(open in browser windows) already unchecked.
I decided to check it, and closed the preferences window. double clicked a folder, it hung for a while, then opened up just a whole new window with the sidebar loaded, and then it just worked from then on.

went and unchecked it again and i went back to no sidebar or navigation bar.
so i guess for me, it has to stay on.
