### Post by hithisisatempaccount1532 on 2010-02-23
Yes and no, it depends how you set it up.

Ubuntu not only has the basic power saving features as windows but also has the option to manually set whether or not to have the drive spin down during idle times.

You can also modify the brightness and things of the sort on your monitor to conserve battery life too... so yes, it is better if you know the shortcuts to take to utilize the entire battery charge.
