Hi all. A strange problem - I am trying to download the Greek subtitle files for some films I have. I have green language support on my laptop (Ubuntu 9:10), and in firefox. ### Post by bryncoles on 2010-02-18
Ok, I figured it out. Gedit Doesn't OOTB support decoding Greek characters. After downloading the file, attempting to open it with Open Office brings up a menu asking me to re-encode the file in the proper character set. Then I can save it as an srt file again, and problem solved! 

Phew!
