### Post by drs305 on 2011-12-01
Click the HOME button (upper left) to open DASH. Type "Startup" and the startup applications app should appear. As soon as it is the first in the list as you type, press ENTER to open Startup Applications and add the app you want at startup.

---

### Post by BC59 on 2011-12-01
Another way to do it:

From the up right corner click on the wheel and choose the third option Start Up Applications.

Then open the dash from the opposite corner and on the search box, write the name of the application you like to add at startup. When it appears drag and drop it to the open window of the Start Up Applications.

### Post by pretty_whistle on 2011-12-01
I got it.  Here's how:

HOME>.config>autostart

Note:  To find the .config you must hit CTRL+H first because it's a hidden folder.
