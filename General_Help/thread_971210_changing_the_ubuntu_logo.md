### Post by pirattrev on 2008-11-04
the icon is dependent on your current icon theme. you can change it by going to /home/you/.icons or by installing a different icon theme by going to gnome-look.org or something similar.  For the /home/you/.icons method you'll have to manually change the image, but you can make it anything you want, you can even mess around in the_gimp
