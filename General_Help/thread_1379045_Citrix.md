### Post by patchwork on 2010-01-12
To add local drives to the drive mapping for Citrix Receiver 11:

Open Citrix Receiver from Applications>Internet>Citrix Receiver

Click on Tools>Settings>Preferences>Drive Mapping

Toggle the small Enable Read/Write box next to the drive letter you would like the disk to appear as

Click Modify

Select the directory you would like to map

Hope this helps
