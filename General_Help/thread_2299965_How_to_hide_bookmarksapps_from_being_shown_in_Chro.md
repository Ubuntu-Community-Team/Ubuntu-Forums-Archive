### Post by mystics on 2015-10-22
A few ways to do it:

1. You can use the keyboard combination for showing/hiding the bookmark bar. I believe it is Ctrl + Shift + B by default.

2. You can right-click on the bar with the bookmarks and deselect "Show bookmarks bar" from the menu.

3. You can click on the menu button on the far right, go down to "Bookmarks", and deselect "Show bookmarks bar" from the menu.

The first and third options can also be used to get the bar back. The second method only works to hide it.
