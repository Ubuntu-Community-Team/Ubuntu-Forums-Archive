### Post by Damanther on 2007-06-15
Right click on one of the folders in Konqueror then properties.

There is a little wrench icon button just to the right of the "Type:     Folder" text.

Click that and it will bring up a window with Application Preference Order.

Move whatever you want to open folders to the top. I think this is specific to kde so whatever your default in gnome is should remain the same.
