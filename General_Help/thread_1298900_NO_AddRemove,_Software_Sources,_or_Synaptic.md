### Post by Giblet5 on 2009-10-23
Look for your username in the /etc/group file. Are you in the admin group?

Let's say your username is "fred".

```
grep fred /etc/group
``` should return something *similar to* ```
adm:x:4:fred
admin:x:115:fred
fred:x:1001:fred
``` and probably others. Those are important though.
