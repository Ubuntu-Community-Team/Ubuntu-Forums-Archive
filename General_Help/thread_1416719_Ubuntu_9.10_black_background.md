### Post by Timothy Taylor on 2010-02-26
This happened to me.  I discovered by chance that changing System\Appearance->Appearance Preferences\Visual Effects from "Normal" to "None" did the trick.

See [here]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1415778")
