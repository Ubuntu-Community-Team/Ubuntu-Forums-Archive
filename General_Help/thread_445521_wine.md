### Post by jediborger on 2007-05-16
you can uninstall windows sofware in wine by typing "uninstaller' in the terminal. On one of my computers wine links were created, but goto "Main Menu" under the System-Preferences menu and see if the wine submenu is disabled. Personally I prefer to make the links myself because windows installations put a lot of junk in the menu and the apps aren't sorted into the standard categories. Hopefully that helps.
