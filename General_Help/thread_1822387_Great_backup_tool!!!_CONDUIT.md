For all you looking for a tool to keep your data constantly backed up and up to date on something such as a USB drive.. I suggest you use "conduit"

I just discovered this on my Fedora 15 install and I believe it is also available for Ubuntu. Anytime I add/ delete something my Home Folder, it is immediately changed on the external usb drive I have connected.
