### Post by LaRoza on 2007-12-08
Open you home directory, press "Ctrl + h" and all the directories with a leading "." which were hidden, are what hold your settings.

Copy them to something (don't copy .thumbnails, or .Trash). 

For a program to image your drive, use Clonezilla, see the link in my sig.
