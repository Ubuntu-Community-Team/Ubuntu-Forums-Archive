### Post by JimBuntu on 2008-09-17
Boy do I feel stupid. So I built a computer about 6 months ago. Pretty good parts. My video card is a BFG NVIDIA 8600 gt oc 512mb. I downloaded the correct drivers and so I thought everything was working properly. Well just bought a new screen, the Hannspree HF229H. Its a 22" screen with 1650x1050 resolution. Plugged it in and it did not work properly. Couldnt figure out why. Searched forums and could not get it. It was displaying 1024x762 stretched. So finally I went into the System>Administration>Hardware drivers and noticed that the driver said in use but there was no check in the "Enable" box. So I checked it and immediately Ubuntu told me I needed a restart. Did that and holy crap I have perfect resolution coming from my new screen. I couldn't believe it was that easy. And to think I was about to take the screen back to Best Buy.
