### Post by Genius314 on 2008-08-17
In the Reflection and Deformation plugin, change the "Cube top color" and "Cube bottom color" so that they're completely transparent. Now delete the "fusioncap.png" and "compizcap.png" from the top and bottom image lists.
After that, add a blank option to both lists (click "New" but don't enter anything into the box that pops up.)

For some reason, even after deleting the images from the list, they stay on the cube caps.

(Btw, once you see that it worked, you can delete the blank "images" from the list)
