### Post by jerome1232 on 2009-01-26
define "proper" lol.

/mnt and /media are there for mounting drives.

mounts under /media show up on your desktop

mounts under /mnt don't show up on your desktop

I believe anything mounted under ~/ show up on the desktop as well.


Basically I mount partitions under /mnt if don't want an icon on my desktop, /media if I do.
