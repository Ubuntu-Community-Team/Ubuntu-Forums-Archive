### Post by bmasterflash on 2008-06-02
Go to the Junk Tab in your Mail Preferences (Edit -> Prefereces -> Mail Preferences -> Junk Tab) and uncheck the box that says "Delete junk messages on exit".  You will see that you can also change this option so that it will only delete your junk messages once a day, once a week or once a month.  Hope that helps.
