### Post by jpietari on 2007-11-13
I want to resize my partition by splitting it into 2 using gparted.  When I do this, will my data be erased?

---

### Post by jpittack on 2007-11-13
No.  Just make sure you do not reformat the drive, because that will delete your information.  I have resized and moved every partition in my hard drive at once, and it all came out intact.  But be warned.  I suggest backing up data before doing any such changes.  I have not heard of anything going wrong, but you can never be too safe.
