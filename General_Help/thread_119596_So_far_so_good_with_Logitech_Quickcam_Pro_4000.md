### Post by ahood on 2006-01-19
Hi All,

I just want to report that the Logitech Quickcam Pro 4000 works out of the box with Ubuntu Breezy (5.10). This is what I did to get it to work.

(1) Purchased cam at Best Buy and went home
(2) Removed cam from box
(3) Plugged in cam into a free USB 2.0 port
(4) Started Gnomemeeting 1.5
Strange lines appeared in the video window of Gnomemeeting
(5) Edit --> Preferences --> Video Devices
      Video Plugin = V4L
      Input Device = Logitech Quickcam Pro 4000

(6) Click on Close

Worked!!!

P.S. This webcam also works with amsn, but this application runs very slow on my machine (Athlon XP 2100, 512 Mb RAM)

Now I can see me and it is great. Now I just need to figure out how to connect to someone elses webcam.

Dr. Hood
