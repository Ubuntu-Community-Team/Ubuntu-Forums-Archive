### Post by crossedout on 2008-07-16
You didn't mention what type of theme, so..:
-For gtk you can check out:
[http://www.gtk.org/tutorial/]("http://www.gtk.org/tutorial/")
-For emerald you can open emerald theme manager and edit a theme you like.  You can edit just about any aspect of the themes.
-For metacity you can check out:
[http://developer.gnome.org/doc/tutorials/metacity/metacity-themes.html]("http://developer.gnome.org/doc/tutorials/metacity/metacity-themes.html")

If none of those are what you are after, then be more specific so we can give more accurate answers.  Those are only a small sample of some of the 'theme' customizations.

You can check out thewidgetfactory to help you preview your theme(s) once you get going.
