### Post by spikoley on 2010-10-17
It sounds like you want to turn of the notifications for Empathy.  

1.  Open Empathy
2.  Edit
3.  Preferences
4.  General tab
5.  Uncheck Display incoming events in the notification area

What are the other programs you want to turn off the notifications?  I think you turn them off with each program, but I don't know for sure.

### Post by hlpguru on 2010-10-17
Notification system in Pidgin is plug-ins called 'Libnotify popups' In order not to be annoyed when your contact becomes online

[LIST]
[*]Tool -> Plugins (Ctrl + U).
[*]Select 'Libnotify popups' then *Configure Plugin.*
[*]remove 'Buddy signs on' checkbox. Done.
[/LIST]
