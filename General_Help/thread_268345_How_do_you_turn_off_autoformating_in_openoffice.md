### Post by Rui Pais on 2006-09-30
hi, 
you can try to disable default autoformating somewhere in the options, but that usually cut other things too that you may or may not wanted turned off...

My solution to any automatic formatation that i don't want is simply press the 'Ctrl+Z' keys combination imediatly after format was applied. 
That unmake it and one can continue to type normally. 

If, after undo the auto format, i find that I prefer, after all, the auto-format style, it can easily put it back with Ctrl+Y.

Hope that helps.
