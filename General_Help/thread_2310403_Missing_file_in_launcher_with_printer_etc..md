### Post by deadflowr on 2016-01-18
Go to your top right corner and click on the cog icon, there should be a system settings listed in that drop down.
If it is listed, click on it to open it and when it is open you should see that the launcher has added an icon for it.
Right click on that icon and select the option Lock to Launcher.

It should stay like that after you close the system settings window.
