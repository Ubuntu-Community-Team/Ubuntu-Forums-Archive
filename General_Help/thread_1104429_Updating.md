### Post by ibuclaw on 2009-03-23
No, updating your system via the software upgrades will only upgrade your system to the most recent packages available in the **Ubuntu 8.04** tree.
OR to put more bluntly, you'll have the most recently patched edition of Ubuntu 8.04.

If you wanted to upgrade your system from 8.04 to **Ubuntu 8.10** run:
```
gksu update-manager -d
```
Either in a terminal, or via pressing **Alt+F2**
