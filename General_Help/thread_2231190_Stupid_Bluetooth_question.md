### Post by 3rdalbum on 2014-06-27
I would probably keep the devices at least half a meter apart, actually. If you know what side the Bluetooth radio is on the laptop, try putting the phone on that side to minimise the amount of computer in between the Bluetooth card and the phone.

If things still aren't working well, you could try using your phone as an FTP server connected to the same wifi network as the computer; or just plug the phone into the computer and copy files across that way.
