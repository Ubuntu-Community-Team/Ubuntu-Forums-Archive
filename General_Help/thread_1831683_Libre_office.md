### Post by coffeecat on 2011-08-23
The version of Open Office used in earlier versions of Ubuntu was not the standard Open Office at all. It was the Go-OO build of OpenOffice. When the LibreOffice developers left Open Office (because of the Oracle goings on) they used the Go-OO build to create Libre Office. So Libre Office in 11.04 is simply the latest iteration of what you had in earlier versions of Ubuntu, going under a different name.
