### Post by der_joachim on 2007-08-12
Another idea is to map a compose key. Mine is set to right alt (which is consistent with some European layouts). That way, an ä is created by pressing Compose+"+a. 

Setting it in KDE is simple. Open Kcontrol, go to the Keyboard Layouts section and click the tab 'Xkb Options'. Tick one of the compose key options, click 'Apply' and you're set. I do not know about Gnome though.
