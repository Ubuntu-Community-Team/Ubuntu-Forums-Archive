### Post by Frogs Hair on 2010-11-06
Right click the icon , go to properties and on the left side of the box is a picture of the icon. Click no the picture to navigate to the folder where the icon you want is stored and select the icon > open > 0k . You can download an icon if you want a different one , just navigate to downloads. If you download an icon be sure to save it in a folder of your choice.
