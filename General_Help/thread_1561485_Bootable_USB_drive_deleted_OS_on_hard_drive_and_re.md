### Post by viralmeme on 2010-08-26
> **Vorksholk said:**
> I just booted one of my computers from a usb drive .. when I powered down the computer and booted it back up to the main hard drive, it booted to the same that my flash drive was running .. How is this possible?
Only if you booted from the CD, installed to the USB and selected to install the bootloader in the MBR of the harddrive, else booting from a USB drive will have no effect on your other system.
