### Post by tramir on 2008-05-01
Do you have any 3rd party repos enabled? I did and that caused problems (see [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=776709](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=776709), no solution yet) with fetching updates. So, if you do have 3rd party repositories, try removing them from the list and see if it works. Or maybe this is not your problem...
