### Post by jpeddicord on 2007-04-09
Have you tried deleting the Notification Area by right-clicking a blank space near the icons (or possibly on the right)? You can then re-add it by right-clicking the panel and selecting Add to Panel and then Notification Area.
Sometimes just doing that will refresh the panel to the point where it will work fine again.

Jacob
