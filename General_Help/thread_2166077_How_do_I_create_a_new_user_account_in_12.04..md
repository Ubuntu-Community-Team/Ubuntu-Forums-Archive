### Post by deadflowr on 2013-08-07
When you get to the users and groups(or users account part), you need to unlock it first.
Then in the side panel listing the users at the bottom is a plus/minus, click the plus and make a user.
For admin users make sure to change it from standard(normal) to administrator in the dropdown box at the top.
And give her a password.
