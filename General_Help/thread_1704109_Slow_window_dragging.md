### Post by ptptaylor on 2011-03-10
Hi,
I've recently been having a few issues with dragging windows in ubuntu 10.10 (64bit). When I drag a window too fast, it now jitters and lags behind the mouse. I am not sure what I may have updated mistakenly (it could have been a graphics driver in the update process which I didn't pay any attention to) or it could even be the beginning of a faulty graphics card, which is starting to make funny noises in my laptop when it's activated...

Anybody else facing a similar problem recently? Any ideas?
Videos playback fine, and I am able to play games as I could before.

### Post by ptptaylor on 2011-03-20
I'm not sure if anybody is interested in this, but it could be useful.
I found out it was to do with my new mouse that I installed. The 'Scan frequency' or Polling rate was 125Hz previously with my old mouse however, my new mouse worked at 1000Hz by default. I changed the setting so it now scans at 125Hz again, and the window dragging speed is back to normal. I suppose my computer can't handle the number of refreshes whilst dragging things about.
