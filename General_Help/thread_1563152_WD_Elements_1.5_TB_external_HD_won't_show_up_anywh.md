### Post by Ginsu543 on 2010-08-29
Does your CD drive work with other CDs? Is it only the Ubuntu LiveCD that won't boot? One possibility here is that one or more of the subsystems on your motherboard just went kaput. Recently, I heard the infamous "click of death" on a hard drive in one of my older computers. So I thought the hard drive was dead. But I realized that my CD-ROM wasn't working right either. It turns out the IDE subsystem on my motherboard had died. I didn't know because the board posted in BIOS and there were no error messages. I put that same drive in another computer and it works just fine.
