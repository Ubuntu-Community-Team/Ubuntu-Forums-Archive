### Post by Antioch on 2008-11-14
Hi guys. I'm new to Evolution and want to like it. Unfortunately, I'm a stickler for details and the following is a deal breaker for me (hopefully it's an easily fixable problem):

In the e-mail inbox, the relevant information regarding each e-mail is split into columns such as "subject" and "date." Among these columns is the "from" comumn. Unfortunately this column displays both the name of the sender, as well as the e-mail address. 

This can make your inbox rather cluttered and difficult to look at (especially on smaller resolution displays). I would like it if there were some way to change the from column to only show the name of the person and hide the address.

I haven't figured out how to do this by poking around, and google seems to fail me. There was a mailing list thread from 2002 referring to this problem and said that the functionality was in the works. I'm hoping it's there as it's been 6 years since those e-mails.

Can anyone tell me the secret? :)

Thanks for your help!

---

### Post by Antioch on 2008-11-14
Further poking has led me to the solution!

For those in the future that encounter this same problem, here's the solution:

Add the "Sender" column and remove the "from" column. The sender column only has the name of the sender, while the from column has both.

Cheers!
