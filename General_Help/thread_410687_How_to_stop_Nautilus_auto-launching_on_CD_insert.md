### Post by akirashinigami on 2007-04-16
How do I stop Nautilus from opening when I insert a data CD/DVD?

---

### Post by aysiu on 2007-04-16
System > Preferences > Removable Drives and Media > Removable Storage > Browse removable media when inserted --> uncheck that last box.
