### Post by DapperMe17 on 2011-12-07
You have an integrated ATI graphics card in your new laptop. 

Certain ATI cards have been tricky in Ubuntu, especially trying to run 3D with Compiz. That's why you're 2D works nicely in comparison. 

Here's an older web article, that should shed some light for you.
