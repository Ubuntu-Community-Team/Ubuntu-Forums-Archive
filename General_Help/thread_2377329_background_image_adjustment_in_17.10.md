### Post by JamButty on 2017-11-11
Getting used to Aardvark 17.10 A lot of good improvements. One thing I have not found yet is a way to adjust background images (wallpaper). Previously, under display settings, there were options like span, center, scale etc. I have not found any replacement for that. Is there one?

---

### Post by Perfect Storm on 2017-11-12
Install Gnome Tweaks.
Open Gnome Tweaks ---> Go to Desktop.
Then change "Adjustment" of the wallpaper.
