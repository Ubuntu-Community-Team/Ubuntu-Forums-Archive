### Post by zbirdman777 on 2010-04-18
Yes! Depending on which e-mail you are using, you may need it to check for messages at regular intervals. I think GMAil is this way for example.

Go to Edit -> Preferences -> Then click on your email account, select "Edit" on the right.

A window should open. Go to the "Receiving Options" tab and check the box that says "Check for new messages every [   ] minutes. Put how often you want Evolution to check for messages. 

Hope this helps!
Good luck!
