Oka I will attemp to help you:

Right click on your desktop,
Go to the bottom most entry, Click on it.
A dialouge should come up (Appearance Prefferences)
Click on the third tab from the left
There are 5 drop down boxes underneath each other,
These are the system wide default fonts for displayed text,
Click on them and change them to:
1. Sans 10
2. Sans 10
3. Sans 10
4. Sans Bold 10
5. Monospace 10

Do do this, if the text is unreadable after the font select dialouge box pops up:
Click inside the left hand white rectangle, and type out "sans" it will be highlighted, click on the right most bottom button to accept is your new text

After this, the issue should be resolved, if this was the issue.
