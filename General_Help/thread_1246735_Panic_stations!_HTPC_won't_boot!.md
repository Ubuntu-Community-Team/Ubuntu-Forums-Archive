### Post by Alfiegerner on 2009-08-22
PANIC OVER!  EVERYBODY STOP PANICKING :)

Sorry, just relieved now.  Went into my bios, turned off HD audio, rebooted all good but I had no sound.  Went back into bios, enabled HD audio again and now everything's working as it should.

Grieves me that this may be the first symptom of a new issue but for now it looks to be working again.
