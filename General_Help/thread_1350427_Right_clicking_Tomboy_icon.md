### Post by sharm on 2009-12-19
In Tomboy 1.0, we forgot to add the Sync menu item to the applet's right-click menu.  However, if you remove Tomboy from your panel and run it in your notification area instead, the right-click option is there.

We have added the option to the panel applet in our latest unstable release, Tomboy 1.1.0.
