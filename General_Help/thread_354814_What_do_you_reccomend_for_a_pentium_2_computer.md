### Post by Ramses de Norre on 2007-02-06
In fact you only need a light window manager like xfce (package xubuntu-desktop), fluxbox, openbox, blackbox, icewm, windowmaker, ...

But they're not all as easy to setup as gnome or kde, I think xfce is the easiest and I personally like xfce and fluxbox the best.

I'd say try them out and see which you like the best for your own.
