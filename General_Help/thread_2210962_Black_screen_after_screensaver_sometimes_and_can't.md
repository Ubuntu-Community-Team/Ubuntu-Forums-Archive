### Post by GammaPoint on 2014-03-26
Well, I'm not sure what the underlying problem is, but I figured out (I think) how to fix it. Since it randomly happens it sort of pointed at the possibility that my random choice of screensaver was responsible (i.e., that one of the screensaver choices was somehow messing stuff up). So I turned off random screensaver and just put it on blank screen. It's been working a week or so without messing up now, which is the longest it's ever gone. So this is one possible workaround.
