### Post by mcduck on 2006-11-17
You can not use the 'cube' and 'desktop plane' plugins at the same time. Cube can only have one row of desktops, because other ways they wouldn't actually fit in the cube. PLane can have many rows, but doesn't look as nice

To get more faces to the cube, open Beryl Settings Manager, and under General Settings/Numeric Values change the 'Horizontal Virtual Size' to your liking.

If you want to use the plane instead, disable 'Desktop Cube'-plugin, enable 'Desktop Plane'-plugin and in the same place under general settings change Horizontal and Vertical Virtual Sizes to what you want.
