### Post by x64Jimbo on 2007-02-02
More info is required when you ask a question like this. What CPU does it have? What chipset does it have on the mainboard? Can you see CPU temp in your monitoring program? If so, what is the heat tolerance of your CPU, and does the temp program show the CPU temp approaching that number if you don't turn the fans on manually? 
Nearly all computers that are available will actually shut themselves off if the CPU temp approaches its max temp - it's a BIOS setting. Not controlled by the OS at all.
