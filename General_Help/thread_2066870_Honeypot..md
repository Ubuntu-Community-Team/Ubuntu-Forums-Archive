### Post by raja.genupula on 2012-10-05
In computer terminology, a honeypot is a [trap]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Trap_%28tactic%29") set to detect, deflect, or in some manner counteract attempts at unauthorized use of [information systems]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Information_systems"). Generally it consists of a [computer]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Computer"), data, or a network site that appears to be part of a [network]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Computer_network"), but is actually isolated and monitored, and which seems to contain information or a resource of value to attackers.

[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Honeypot_%28computing%29](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Honeypot_%28computing%29)
