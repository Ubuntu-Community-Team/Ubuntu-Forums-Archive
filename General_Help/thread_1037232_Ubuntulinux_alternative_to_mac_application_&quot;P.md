### Post by radioboy on 2010-05-07
I think that Mendeley makes a good free alternative:
[http://www.mendeley.com/](http://www.mendeley.com/)

You can add documents directly from your browser (once you have an account) using a button you can add as a bookmark to your bookmark bar. It extracts citation information, it allows exporting to LaTeX citations, etc.
It also has tags, capacity for pdf annotations (if you download the document), synchronization with [Zotero]("http://www.zotero.org/") (a program similar to Mendeley, available as a Firefox plugin), etc.
