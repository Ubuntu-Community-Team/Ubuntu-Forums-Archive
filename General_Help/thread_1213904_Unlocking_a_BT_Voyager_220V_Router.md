### Post by oswaldkelso on 2009-08-06
This is what I just did :) anyone else at your own risk :)

I reset the router pressing the small reset at the back with a pin

open a browser enter address for router: 191.168.1.1

advanced > advanced > system > upgrade > "From local pc" 

navigate to the downloaded firmware (it's gpl afaik) click UPGRADE WAIT........several mins for the upgrade to finish

At some point your asked for user and password use (admin)

user: admin

password: admin

I got the unlocked firmware here at todays date it is 303c-unlock
[http://www.route2030.co.uk/BT-Voyager-220v/](http://www.route2030.co.uk/BT-Voyager-220v/) 

Then login to your non BT isp. You will of course need to know your user name and password. Get this info first :)
