### Post by 3rdalbum on 2012-02-28
> **Blue TeaTree said:**
> Hello, I am new to Linux. And I love it! Please, let me know what drivers should I get for DVD/CD and printer. I have Ubuntu 11.10. Thanks in advance.

DVD and CD drives don't require drivers, but as the previous poster said you might need a codec and decryption library to play encrypted DVDs.

As for your printer, Ubuntu comes with a bunch of drivers for printers. Have you tried just going to the cog menu on the top-right corner of your screen, come down to Printers, click on Add and see if your printer is already recognised. Make sure the printer is already turned on and plugged in, of course.
