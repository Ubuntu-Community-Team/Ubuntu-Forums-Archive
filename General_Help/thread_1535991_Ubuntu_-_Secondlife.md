### Post by Shalmainia on 2010-07-21
This is for those users like myself that very recently made the switch to Ubuntu.

If you're a SecondLife user, I must advise you not to use Viewer 2.1 Beta for Linux nor the primary viewer Second Life Viewer 1.23 for Linux. Both have them have severe SLVoice issues and you will be pulling your hair out to get it to work, it won't.

Instead, I suggest that when you go to download SecondLife, check out the download below those two on the offical downloads website, there's another program there called Snowglobe for Linux, which is another client you can install on Linux, the voice works absolutely perfectly on x86/x64 and it renders extremely efficiently.
