### Post by Rasa1111 on 2011-05-07
Nope.

 No 'downgrade'.

If you want 10.10 back, you have to reinstall it from a disc (or USB).

 Best bet would be to save all your files to some external media, reinstall 10.10, and then move your files back once it's installed. 

 Or you could just switch to 'classic' mode.

Good luck.
