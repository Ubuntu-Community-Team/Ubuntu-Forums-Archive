### Post by timgood on 2011-04-16
If you don't want to have to put in a password on resume from standby then go to System/Preferences/Screensaver and untick the box that says 'Lock screen when screensaver is active'.

To re-enable your login go to System/Administration/Login Screen and unlock the settings. Then check 'Show the screen for choosing who will log in'.

Hope this helps.
