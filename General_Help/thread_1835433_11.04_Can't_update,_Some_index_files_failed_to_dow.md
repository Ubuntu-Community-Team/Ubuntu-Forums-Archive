### Post by lintoon on 2011-08-29
I've found the solution.

[https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/apt/+bug/346386](https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/apt/+bug/346386)

The solution was to remove the problematic files from /var/lib/apt/lists/

In may case I removed gb.archive.ubuntu.com_ubuntu_dists_natty_multiverse_source_Sources

You will have to remove them with sudo.

Open up Terminal

cd /var/lib/apt/lists/partial
sudo rm gb.archive.ubuntu.com_ubuntu_dists_natty_multiverse_source_Sources

OR 

sudo rm /var/lib/apt/lists/partial/gb.archive.ubuntu.com_ubuntu_dists_natty_multivers e_source_Sources
