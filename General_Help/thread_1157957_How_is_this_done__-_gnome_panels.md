### Post by mcduck on 2009-05-13
The button look simply comes from the GTK theme used.

Panel backgorund may be part of the theme as well, but can also be a separately assigned background image. Gnome-panel supports (at last) JPG, PNG and SVG images and both PNG & SVG can have transparency/semi-transparency which allows creating rather nice looking panels.

Icon for the menu button is probably a separate custom one, can't say much about that as I haven't played around with alternative menu applets for ages.
