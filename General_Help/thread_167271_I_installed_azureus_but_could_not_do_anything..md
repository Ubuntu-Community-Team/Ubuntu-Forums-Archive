You may be using a tracker that blocks the default port Azureus/bittorrent uses. A lot of trackers does this due to some ISP's reducing the speed on the default port for bittorrent. You can change this in Azureus' options. (Set it to 7000 or something).

Azureus should work fine without opening ports in your router, just that you won't get maximum speed when other people can't connect to you. You could also try the default bittorrent client in Gnome and see if that works better.
