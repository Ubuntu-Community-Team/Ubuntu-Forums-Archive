You say you have a Mac partition. On your pc or iPod only? Can you mount the partition in Ubuntu? If you can, boot the Mac partition and plug the iPod in. Move the files from the iPod to somewhere in your Mac home folder. Reboot to Ubuntu and browse to the Mac file location then copy/paste it somewhere in your Ubuntu home folder.
