### Post by CatKiller on 2013-04-19
The extra animations (including the burn effect) are no longer available for 12.10. The development team is pretty much one guy, so only effects that are used for Unity are supported as a workload priority method.

You can get some effects back by using the Compiz Experimental PPA, like Window Previews and Reflection, but still no burn.
