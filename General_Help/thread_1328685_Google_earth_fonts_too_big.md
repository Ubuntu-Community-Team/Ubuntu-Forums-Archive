I am having a problem with Google earth on ubuntu 9.10. When I open Google Earth the menu's appear in huge fonts and are spread over several desktops. There was a post in relation to this some months ago but it is closed and seems to have been inconclusive with no solution that works for me.

### Post by Mickser_52 on 2009-11-17
Finally worked out what sorted this problem previously. 

I went to System/preferences/display and reset the resolution to 1024X768.
Then I opened google earth and the menu sizes returned to normal. Closed google earth, opened display again and reset resolution 1280X800 which was the original setting. 
When I opened google earth the menus were still at normal size and problem solved.
