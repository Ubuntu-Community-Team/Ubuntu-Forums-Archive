### Post by Who on 2006-10-28
Try the 'mail-notification' and 'mail-notification-evolution' from the repositories (you may need universe activated). They don't do _exactly_ what you say, but you can have the mail-notification applet stay in your systray and check your email. When you have new mail the icon changes and you can click on it to start evolution.

I tend to keep evolution open on a separate desktop these days and then clicking on the icon takes me to it.

There is a setting in mail-notification that allows you to force it to display and icon the whole time
