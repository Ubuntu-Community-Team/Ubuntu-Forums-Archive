### Post by ajgreeny on 2015-09-26
Have a look at **xournal** which is in the repos.

You will need to open the PDF, add your notes etc etc, and then export it again as another PDF file.

### Post by kurt18947 on 2015-09-26
You might be able to accomplish this in Libre Office Draw. I do 'file' -> 'open' to open the pdf. You could use callouts from the bar on the left or insert a text box or floating frame. Make edits then export again as ajgreeny suggests. I've never used  xournal; it may be a better tool but I can't compare.
