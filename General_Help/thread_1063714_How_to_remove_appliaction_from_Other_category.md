### Post by overlord.gaurav on 2009-02-08
From what I can understand: You've installed some applications using Wine, but as they were too slow, you've uninstalled it. But, inspite of the un-installation, they are visible under "Other" tab in the menu.

If that's the case, then, right click on your menu > click on 'Edit Menus' > Navigate to 'Other' and remove the desired entry.
