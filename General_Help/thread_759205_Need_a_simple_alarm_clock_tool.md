### Post by Herman on 2008-04-19
:) A computer with Ubuntu in it can wake you up, boil the kettle for you and even make your breakfast if you know how.

It's not hard to set up and you don't need anything hard to get or expensive, just common everyday household items will do everything you need.

You'll need a marble, a popsickle stick, some blue-tack (or gum, as long as you chew it first), a mouse trap, a power board and an electric kettle and maybe some other appliances (optional). 

Take a look at my set-up in this link, 
[http://picasaweb.google.com/herman543/HowMyComputerMakesCoffee](http://picasaweb.google.com/herman543/HowMyComputerMakesCoffee)
Make sure you click to enlarge the pictures.
After you look at that link make sure you find the link at the end of it that leads on to another web page about how to edit /etc/crontab in Ubuntu and you can try something like that yourself. 

I'm sure with a little imagination you can change it to do whatever you want! For example, you might decide to use a microwave instead of an electric kettle if you want it to cook your breakfast, but you can use both.

Regards, Herman :)
