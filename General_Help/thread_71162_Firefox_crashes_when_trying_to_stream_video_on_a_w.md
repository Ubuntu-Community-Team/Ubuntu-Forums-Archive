### Post by sizzam on 2005-10-21
I fixed this problem on my machine by installing the [Adblock]("https://addons.mozilla.org/extensions/moreinfo.php?id=10") extension on Firefox.   I'm not sure why it works, but it does.  Maybe its blocking the flash banners that usually appear on video sites like that.


On a side note, I'm also using the [MediaPlayerConnectivity]("https://addons.mozilla.org/extensions/moreinfo.php?id=446") extension.  It looks for embedded videos in webpages and turns them into a link that you can click to launch the file in the player of your choice.
