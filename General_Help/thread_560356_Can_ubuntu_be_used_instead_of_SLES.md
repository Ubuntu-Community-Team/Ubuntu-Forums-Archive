### Post by matthew on 2007-09-26
I think the best answer is "probably." The class may have some distro-specific moments. My thought is, why not install SLES with the help of someone there? You are still welcome to come and hang out here and ask us questions if you need to, but this way you can be sure to have someone locally to help you during the transition process. 

Most of what you will learn, if not all of it, will be transferable from SLES to Ubuntu and to other Linux distros.
