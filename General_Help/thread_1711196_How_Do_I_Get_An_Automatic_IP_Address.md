### Post by Script Warlock on 2011-03-21
you can edit your network by right clicking the network applet and edit connections. edit the autoeth0 and go to ipv4 settings and select the method: automatic (dhcp).. apply.. close and click the network applet again and disconnect and reconnect again by clicking the Autoeth0
