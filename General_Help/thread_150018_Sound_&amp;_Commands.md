### Post by eholly on 2006-03-25
If your problem is that you have not heard a sound from any player, you need to un-mute the proper line to your speakers.  This is done in the volume control, under Applications >> Sound & Vido >> Volume Control >> Edit >> Preferences.   Select and adjust all volumes to 50%.  If you then get sound, re-adjusting the volume controls will tell you which ones are nessasary for where your speakers are connected.
