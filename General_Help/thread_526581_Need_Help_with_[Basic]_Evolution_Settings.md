### Post by gobbles414 on 2007-08-15
Hi moore.bryan.

This topic was recently discussed [here]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=334049"), although no solution was mentioned. A quick look at my version of Evolution gives no new information.

However, your problem may be with another aspect of your email. Evolution does not always use the same connection specifications as Apple Mail, Outlook or Outlook Express. It may be worth checking your current settings against your email provider's recommended settings for Evolution. Those settings might be listed under EVOLUTION or OTHER EMAIL PROGRAMS on your email provider's support page.  Also, you can get a timeout message if your username and/or password are incorrectly entered. It's worth noting that some email services are very picky about usernames. Gmail, for example requires that one use the entire email address as the username.
