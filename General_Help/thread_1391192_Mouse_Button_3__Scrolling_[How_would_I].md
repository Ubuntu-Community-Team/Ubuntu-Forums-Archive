Actually this is not related to the operating system at all.
It's a firefox feature and you can enable it from the preferences, advanced tab. And from there check the "use autoscrolling" feature.

### Post by mcduck on 2010-01-26
That's simple. :) (for Firefox, at least, since middle click has a comletely different purpose in general which is why it's not used for scrolling by default. Middle click is the traditional way to copy/paste inn Linux/Unix...)

Type "about:config" in the address bar, find the "general.autoScroll" key and right-click it & select "toggle" to change it's value to "true".

edit: Oh, it's even in the preferences dialog. I suppose I've spent too much time doing configuring less common settings in Firefox when I didn't even think about checking if the option is available in the preferences... :D
