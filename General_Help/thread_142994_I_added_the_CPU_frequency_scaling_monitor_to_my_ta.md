### Post by taurus on 2006-03-11
If you don't do anything on your machine, it will go down to the lowest value.  But when you compile or run something, the your CPU speed will increase!  If you want it to stay at the max value, then don't run the frequency scaling!!!

Place an icon on top of it and click the right button.  Then, change it to whatever city you are in...
