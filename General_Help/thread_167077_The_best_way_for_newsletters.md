### Post by gingermark on 2006-04-27
Depending on layout, density of graphics / text I'd use word-processors like OpenOffice.org 2 Writer or KWord, or go with graphical packages like The GIMP and/or Inkscape.

Inkscape might be best as it's a vector drawing program, and would probably give you the greatest flexibility in terms of layout.

When you're finished, all these programs allow you to save as a pdf, which would probably be the best format to then email out.
