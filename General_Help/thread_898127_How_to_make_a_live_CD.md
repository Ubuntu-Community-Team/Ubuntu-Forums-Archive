### Post by Shazaam on 2008-08-22
Download it to where you want (I put my downloaded files on the desktop), fire up your favorite cd burning app and burn it as an .iso IMAGE to a cd/dvd.
Once you get that done, reboot your pc into the bios setup screen and make your cd/dvd rom first in the boot order. Then, pop the cd/dvd in and reboot to the game cd/dvd.

If I misunderstood you Loaded.len and damis648 have your answer.
