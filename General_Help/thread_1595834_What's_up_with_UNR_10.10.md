### Post by jmckean on 2010-10-22
Here is what I did.

1. Open terminal
2. Start nautilus in terminal
3. When Nautilus was running, right click on the Nautilus icon in launcher and selected "keep in launcher" (or some such language).

Now I have a full featured file manager available from the launcher.

On a related topic, this is the only way I found to access USB drives or my iPhone when I plug into my EEEPC 901.
