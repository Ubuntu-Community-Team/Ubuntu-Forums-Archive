### Post by Seq on 2010-03-06
You don't need the facebook plugin anymore as Facebook has implemented XMPP. This means you can add it to an account in almost any chat program (Empathy, Pidgin, Kopete, etc).

See the [Facebook Chat]("http://www.facebook.com/sitetour/chat.php") section of their site-tour. They even have instructions for Pidgin.
