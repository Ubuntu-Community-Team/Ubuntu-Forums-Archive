It looks like you have some option enabled that gives you the functionality of the [numpad]("http://img181.imageshack.us/img181/8223/post481641169941988qd8.jpg") on a full keyboard.  If you look closely at your keys I bet you'll see another set of labels that correspond to what you are typing.  Then again maybe there's an Ubuntu option to toggle this, but I doubt it.

My advice, look at the user manual of your laptop.
