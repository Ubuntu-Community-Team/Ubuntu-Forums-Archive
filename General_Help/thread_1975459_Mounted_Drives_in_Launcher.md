### Post by Quarkrad on 2012-05-07
I can't find an answer to this one.  I have edited my fstab file so two of my internal ntfs partitions are mounted on started-up. These two partitions show as mounted drives in the Unity launcher - I do not want them visible in the launcher.  How do I remove the visibility of these two partitions/drives in the launcher (but leaving them phyiscally mounted)?

---

### Post by scouser73 on 2012-05-07
**1** - Install **Compiz Config Settings Manager**

**2** - Select **Ubuntu Unity Plugin**

**3** - Click on the tab labelled **Experimental**

**4** - Scroll down to **Show Devices**

**5** - Click the drop menu and select **Never**

*The mounted drives will no longer show in the launcher, they're still mounted, just not showing.*
