### Post by Frogs Hair on 2011-11-19
With the exception of LTS releases you must upgrade in order 10.04 > 10.10 > 11.04 . With 10.04 you would be able to directly upgrad to 12.04  when is released because 10.04 and 12.04 are long term support releases . 

Since you have an 11.04 disc you could backup your files and do a clean installation or wait 5 months and upgrade to 12.04 when it is released .

### Post by Frogs Hair on 2011-11-19
