### Post by jmszr on 2009-03-18
shatered soul,

              I can help with a few :
             #3. [http://ubuntuguide.org/wiki/Ubuntu:Intrepid](http://ubuntuguide.org/wiki/Ubuntu:Intrepid)
                 [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=801404](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=801404)
             #4. [http://documentation.openoffice.org/manuals/userguide3/](http://documentation.openoffice.org/manuals/userguide3/)
             #5. In terminal (Applications > Accessories > Terminal) copy and paste:```
sudo apt-get install ubuntu-restricted-extras

```

          In umg6hr's [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=801404](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=801404) you will find a lot of information. I would suggest the links for the Free Ubuntu Pocket guide by Keir Thomas and Psychocats Ubuntu Guide as of particular interest but there is just a lot of good information contained in the entire guide.
