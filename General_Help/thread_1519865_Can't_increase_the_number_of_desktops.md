### Post by stderr on 2010-06-28
The normal method is to use the Workspace Switcher on your top panel (if you've removed it, you can add it again by right-clicking the panel, clicking "Add to Panel", and adding "Workspace Switcher"). You can then right click the workspace switcher, click Properties, and select the number of workspaces you want.

### Post by hansdown on 2010-06-28
Hi Winalways.

On the bottom toolbar, on the far right, you should see how many desktops are available.

To increase the number of desktops, right click one of them, the left click preferences.

A window will open in the top right corner of your screen

The two options are:columns
                   :rows

Columns adds more desktops.

Rows will add more rows of how ever many desktops you have chosen.
