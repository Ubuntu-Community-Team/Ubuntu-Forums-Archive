They may appear in your regular menu with any luck, aside from that, using the wine command on the terminal can also work, but you'll need to know and remember the path to do it that way. The virtual drive where wine installs things to should be located at ~/.wine/drive_c/

Then you can use something like 'wine "C:/Program Files/somecompany/somegame.exe"'
