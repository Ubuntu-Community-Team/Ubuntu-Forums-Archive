### Post by CatKiller on 2009-06-03
If it's a laptop then you'll want to keep it at its native resolution in any case.

You can change the system-wide font sizes at System -> Preferences -> Appearance -> Fonts. You can also generally make icons larger in all the applications; for example you might make the panel icons larger by resizing the panel itself, or make the file browser icons larger in Nautilus by increasing the zoom.
