### Post by Jucato on 2006-03-17
There is, but you still need to be connected to the internet.

At the side panel, choose Context. Then there will be tabs on top of that side panel (Home, Current, Lyrics, Artist). A song must be playing for you to have these activated. Choose Lyrics. Depending on the song playing, you will be given a list of possible lyrics. Choose the one you want to view.
