### Post by choup on 2008-02-03
Yes, it has become very buggy since release 7.10. I have encountered similar problems to the ones you mentioned as well as others. One problem I ran into was with the evince pdf viewer on the only pc I had installed v7.10 on, while all other pcs were still on 7.04. The problem was when the pc running v7.10 printed a pdf file to an hp laserjet 2100, it would simply freeze all print jobs, basically stacking them up until someone reset the printer. I removed evince from the v7.10 pc and installed the Adobe Acrobat Reader and the problem was gone. Like you, I have had a number of really strange problems crop up with v7.10; flash not working, gnome blowing out, extremely slow performance on clients, high cpu utilization. In fact, I have encountered so many problems that I am seriously considering not installing edubuntu nor recommending it. I have since moved the server to the CentOS 5 LTSP and have found that not only is it extremely stable, but also faster.
