### Post by silkstone on 2008-08-29
This is a long-standing bug with Adobe Flash - it doesn't handle transparency properly. It has been fixed in Flash 10 beta 2 and Flash 10 release candidate, but unfortunately there's another bug (in xulrunner) which can cause Firefox to segfault with these versions of Flash.

Very frustrating, but perhaps it will be OK in the next release of FF - if that finds its way into Hardy.
