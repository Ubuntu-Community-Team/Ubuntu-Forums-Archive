### Post by jeicrash on 2010-07-01
IMHO presentations are meant as a way to display information and audio can take away from peoples focus on the information being displayed.

However sometimes it may be appropriate to have audio in a slide show. For this I would recommend creating a video instead of a impress project. Assuming the audio track is talking about particular slides the slide show itself would need to be timed. So a video would work just fine.

Ubuntu has many video creation tools Avidemux and Pitivi are two I have used before. These allow you to add in images, or video then a separate sound track.

If its a matter of background music This yahoo answers article may be of help.

[http://answers.yahoo.com/question/index?qid=20080527003902AAYuk1N](http://answers.yahoo.com/question/index?qid=20080527003902AAYuk1N)
