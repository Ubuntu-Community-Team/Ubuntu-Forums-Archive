### Post by Grenage on 2012-12-07
> **Aaron96 said:**
> Can I segment a large (say 7GB) rip file into two smaller files that fit in the android file limit of 4 GB?

You can (does android use FAT32 or the like?), but it's probably better to just re-encode to a lower res;  the extra data is effectively worthless on a small screen.

Unless of course, you can't be bothered to re-encode and just want a quick short-term solution.

Android uses ext2 or ext3 internally since it is Linux based, but most external SD cards are formatted for FAT32. Although they don't have to be. They can be formatted ext2 or ext3.
