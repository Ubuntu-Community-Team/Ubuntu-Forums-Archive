### Post by owlcroft on 2010-12-23
The default desktop Search is all you need for finding *files*--it uses the aforesaid **search** command as well as the **find** command, and allows many features.

What I would be interested in hearing about is a desktop-search facility that searches out files based on *content*: that is, for example, list me all my system files that contain the phrase *desktop search* (optionally with or without attention to case).

Of the OP's list, I would--based on numerous vehement posts around the web--eliminate Google's toy from consideration unless you have zero concern for personal privacy: it seems to marginally qualify as malware.  (But you could, ho ho, Google it up).

As to the rest, I, too, await more responses on personal experience.
