### Post by mcduck on 2008-03-30
It's quite unlikely that the drive would change it's spinning speed. Hard disks always spin at the same speed when they are powered.

It's more likely that it was some fan in your system that increased it's speed. If no other fan in your system is temperature-controlled, at least the fan in the power source usually is.
