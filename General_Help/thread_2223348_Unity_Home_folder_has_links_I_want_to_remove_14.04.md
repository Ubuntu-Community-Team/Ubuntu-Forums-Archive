### Post by deadflowr on 2014-05-10
"Computer" is now what "file system" is called.
Look under the devices section in the left pane.
Perhaps you need to enable sidebar to see it.
(view > sidebar, or press F9)

It will also list bookmarks as well.
Right click has remove of bookmarks, if any are there.

Although, I don't know if this is what those are.
("Those" being what you have in the quicklist)
