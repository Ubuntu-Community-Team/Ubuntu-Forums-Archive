### Post by sikander3786 on 2010-11-06
No one can give a perfect answer unless you try it yourself.

Most people have reported that their boot times improved with 10.10 as compared to 10.04. But remember, that is the case with fresh install, not an upgrade.

Most probably, you need to boot from a 10.10 Live CD and test all your hardware at first. If everything is running just fine, you can make the switch then.

Regarding boot time, if it isn't better than 10.04, it should be the same as 10.04 at least.
