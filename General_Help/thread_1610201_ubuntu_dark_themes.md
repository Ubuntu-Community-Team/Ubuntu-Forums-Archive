### Post by TeoBigusGeekus on 2010-10-31
Go to [gnome look.]("http://gnome-look.org/") Pick up a theme of your liking, depending on what you use of course (Gtk, gtk2, emerald, etc).
Download it. Unless otherwise specified, the standard procedure for installing it, is to right click your desktop, select change desktop background, themes tab and drag and drop the theme archive into the tab.
