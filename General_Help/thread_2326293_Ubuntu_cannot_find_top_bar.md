### Post by Enigma12 on 2016-05-30
Since I rarely use LO, and it is on a hard drive, this may not work, but it is what I would do if I had that problem. Hold Alt and press the key for the menu items, e.g., Alt-F for _F_ile or Alt-T for _T_ools. If that brings up the menu items, explore them, looking for settings. You may find what brings it back, assuming that you had that menu bar when you installed LO. 

And, even though you didn't ask, I do almost all my letter writing in Abiword. It loads up faster, uses less RAM, and usually does everything I choose to do with a word processor. 

Hope this helps.
