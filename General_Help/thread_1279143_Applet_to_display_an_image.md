### Post by Giblet5 on 2009-09-30
I'm not aware of one but I haven't looked for one either.

There's a Screenlet that displays/cycles images, if that's what you're after. Search in Synaptic for "screenlet", install, then start the Screenlet manager from Applications/Accessories/Screenlets.

I can't imagine what a drop-down image applet that displays one image could do that a printed image, taped to the monitor frame, wouldn't do better and more conveniently...
