### Post by KhaaL on 2008-01-22
A while ago I looked for a skype replacement and my choice fell almost naturally on openwengo since it's opensource and has half the rates of skype. After I charged my account with 10€ I noticed that the audio quality is poor, there's a delay on the audio and the client crash every time I use it (though not during the calls, lucky me...).

I was told to use their new 2.2 alpha client, but [has a bug]("http://graphicdream.deviantart.com/art/Light-from-window-73064645") that makes it incomaptible with newer versions of alsa - and this bug has existed for over 3 months.

To make it all worse, [they don't offer support on their forums anymore]("http://forum.wengophone.com/viewtopic.php?t=6115&p=#32013").

I hope that this warning will stop others from making the same mistake as I did. There's better alternatives and even skype is more expensive and propeitary - it is at least *working*.
