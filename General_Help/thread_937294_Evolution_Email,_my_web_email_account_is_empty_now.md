### Post by rajan on 2009-03-22
if you're using a pop server, then your emails truly are gone off your .edu server. sorry, that sucks. it happened to me the first time i used evolution. for some stupid reason, the option to leave emails on the server is not checked by default, which results in everyone clearing out their entire inbox at least once in their lives. 

as for the outgoing mail server, you should try to find the smtp server for the ISP that you're on at the moment (your school's or your home's). whoever it is must provide an outgoing mail server or nobody would be able to send emails.
