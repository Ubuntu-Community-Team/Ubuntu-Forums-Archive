### Post by ramjet_1953 on 2007-07-31
Just download Ghost for Linux (G4L)

It is an iso that you burn to a CD and boot from the CD

It allows you to make a complete 1:1 backup of your HDD.

You can get the iso from here:

[http://sourceforge.net/projects/g4l](http://sourceforge.net/projects/g4l)

ps Don't forget that you must burn iso's SLOWLY, no faster than 4 X.
