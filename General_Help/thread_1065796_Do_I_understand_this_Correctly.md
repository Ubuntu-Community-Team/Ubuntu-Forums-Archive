### Post by brandon88tube on 2009-02-10
For the most part Linux uses opensource drivers for all of the hardware. So they will most likely be new drivers, but in some cases you can use windows drivers. Either way it won't copy over your drivers in Windows for you, but it will try to automatically setup drivers for you.

EDIT: Still give it a shot though because it couldn't hurt if you are trying it out on wubi. You could also give a LiveCD a shot and see if it works for you that way as well.
