### Post by lovinglinux on 2012-02-01
I am not sure how Konqueror handles bookmark imports, but Firefox can export as html.

Hit CTRL+SHIFT+O to launch the bookmark manager, click "Import & Backup >> Export Bookmarks to HTML".
