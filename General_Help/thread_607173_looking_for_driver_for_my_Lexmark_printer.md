### Post by Sef on 2007-11-08
Can't find a match to your printer, but open printing has two Lexmarks that are close to your printer:

[X5150]("http://openprinting.org/show_printer.cgi?recnum=Lexmark-x5150")

[X4250]("http://openprinting.org/show_printer.cgi?recnum=Lexmark-X_4250")

Try the 5150 first.  Lexmarks are notoriously difficult to get to work with GNU/Linux.  This is due to their lack of open source drivers for their consumer desktop models.
