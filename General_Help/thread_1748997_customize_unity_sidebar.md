### Post by r-senior on 2011-05-04
Rearranging is a simple matter of either dragging out to the right and back in again, or press and hold, then drag.

The icons are specified in the corresponding .desktop files in /usr/share/applications and depend on your icon set as specified in "Appearance". Did you want to change them *en masse* or individually?
