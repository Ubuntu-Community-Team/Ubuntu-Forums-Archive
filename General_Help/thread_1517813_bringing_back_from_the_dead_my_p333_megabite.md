### Post by snowpine on 2010-06-28
Unlikely Ubuntu will run well on a p333. Here are the [Ubuntu Recommended System Requirements]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Installation/SystemRequirements"):

> Ubuntu Desktop Edition

    * 1 GHz x86 processor
    * 1 Gb of system memory (RAM)
    * 15 GB of hard-drive space (although this can be split onto 2 drives, a 5Gb / and a 10Gb /home fairly easily)
    * Graphics card and monitor capable of 1024 by 768
    * Either a Cd/Dvd-drive or a Usb socket (or both)
    * Internet access is helpful 

I would recommend a Linux distro targeted specifically at older hardware, such as Puppy Linux.
