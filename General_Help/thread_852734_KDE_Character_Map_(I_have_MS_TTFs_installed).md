### Post by threeRd on 2008-11-20
No doubt you have solved the issue by now, but since I just did, I just wanted to point out that I first installed charmap.app, which is a GNUstep type char map, and I hated it.  Not just because it was GNUstep, but because it had a strange type of lag to it, and it seemed way too complicated for a character map.

I learned that the KDE charmap is also inside a package, rather than a standalone, so you won't find it if you just search for charmap in package names.  Its in the kdeutils package, and its called KCharSelect.  Its...adequate.
