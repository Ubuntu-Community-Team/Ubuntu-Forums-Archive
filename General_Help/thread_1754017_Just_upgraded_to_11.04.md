### Post by hawthornso23 on 2011-05-09
login again and select "Ubuntu Classic" for session type. That will put you back in a gnome 2 environment which you can configure as much as you want.

I don't advise trying to configure the unity environment. It doesn't like being configured and you may lose your desktop environment completely.
