### Post by nalmeth on 2006-12-23
There is no Program Files folder in the linux filesystem, but most of the launchers go to /usr/bin

Do the applications not appear in the applications menu?

You can launch an app manually by pressing ALT-F2 and typing its name (case-sensitive). 

If you restart your session, they may appear in the menu like they're supposed to.
