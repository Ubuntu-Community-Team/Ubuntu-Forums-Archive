### Post by 3Miro on 2011-04-08
Go to craigslist.org and find something cheap (the case is the most expensive thing to order and ship on-line, consider getting a totally busted computer to just use the case).

I would go with AMD CPU. I also have a similar purpose computer with AMD Athlon X2 (probably the most power efficient next to Pentium Dual Core) and Nvidia motherboard (got it on sale, it has integrated video, for cheap, you should get integrated video). 2GB of RAM.
