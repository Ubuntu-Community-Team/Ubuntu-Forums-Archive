### Post by lechien73 on 2011-12-08
I remember this from a long time ago...I don't know if this will sort the problem, but you could try:

[LIST=1]
[*]Right-click on your Network Manager applet (tray icon) and select "Edit Connections"
[*]Click on the Wireless tab, make sure your wireless network is selected, and click on "Edit".
[*]At the bottom on the new window, check "Available to all users".
[*]Close all the windows, reboot, and test to ensure that this solves the issue.
[/LIST]
