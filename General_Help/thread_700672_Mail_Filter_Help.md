### Post by mivo on 2008-02-18
I'm relatively sure that a full-fledged email server is the only approach to what you have in mind. But what are your concerns about letting your mail client handle the filtering? Most email clients for Linux either have a trainable spam/etc filter or work well with Spam Asssassin and similar software. If you have to download the the mail anyway, that would seem like a more stream-lined approach. (And you'll want to monitor this, since false positives are common early on in the training of a filter.)
