### Post by lithopsian on 2011-01-03
Please post the contents, or at least the interesting bit at the end, of /boot/grub/menu.lst.  Or grub.cfg if you are on Grub2.  Also the contents of the /boot/ directory.

And where is /boot anyway?  Perhaps it is only created when you install on sda1 and then destroyed again when you remove that.  You might need to do something as simple as updating/installing Grub for hda5.  Perhaps easiest to do it from the update manager (grub_pc package, I think) although you can do it on the command line too with update-grub.
