### Post by cj.surrusco on 2010-07-10
The fact that you have it installed on an external drive may be enough. You can't compare it to a XP install on an internal hard drive; Internal hard drives have a better transfer rate than external and will cause slower speeds, especially when trying to read a lot of data, such as when you are burning a DVD. 

Maybe you should try installing it on a internal drive. If you still have those troubles, then there is probably a bug in your system, because your specs show a computer that should be able to handle those kind of loads.
