### Post by arch0njw on 2008-08-27
That screen with the orange bar is called your "usplash".  Take a read here and let me know if this is too much informatino all at once.
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/USplashCustomizationHowto](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/USplashCustomizationHowto)

I suspect you might need to reconfigure the usplash for it to pick up on some video settings.  I had a usplash that wouldn't show up because the resolution for the usplash was set higher than what my LCD could support.
