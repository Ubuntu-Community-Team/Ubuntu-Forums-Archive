### Post by redilyn on 2009-01-29
In case this is related to your previous post.

If you have been storing your personel files in your /home directory you could just resize the ntfs partition, create an ext3, move /home to the ext3 partition, then delete the ntfs partition, resize the /home partition to your liking, create another ext3 partition and install ubuntu.

This way you will not lose your personel files and settings.

Post back if you are interested in this solution.
