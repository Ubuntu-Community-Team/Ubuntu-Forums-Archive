### Post by Frogs Hair on 2011-01-21
Right click the panel and select add to panel for more applets , also there are some indicators that don't appear on add to panel  menu and can be installed from the Synaptic Package Manager.  Look in System > Preferences > Power Management > General tab to display battery icon.
