### Post by drs305 on 2010-03-20
Open Evolution.
Edit, Preferences. Mail Accounts. 
Highlight an Account.
Edit. Receiving Options tab, set the time interval for checking.

I haven't tried the Lucid version yet, but in other versions of Evolution I've used, there's a setting associated with the account settings. On my machine I have to select Edit->Preferences, select the relevant email account to change, click "Edit", then click on the "Receiving options" tab.
