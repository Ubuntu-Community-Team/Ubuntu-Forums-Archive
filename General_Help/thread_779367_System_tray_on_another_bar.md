### Post by agaudio on 2008-05-03
Glad you solved the problem. Actually in the English version, it is called Notification area (system tray is a Windows term). And it is not an entirely intuitive process to add it to another panel, I admit. On my machine, if I add a notification area to my bottom panel while there is one on the top panel, nothing happens .... until I log in again, at which point the notification area is now on the bottom panel instead of the top. On a similar note, I have noticed oddities when adding a trash applet to the panel. Sometimes, it will just be missing when I log in.
