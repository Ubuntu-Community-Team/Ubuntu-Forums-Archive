### Post by CatKiller on 2009-08-16
Just as an FYI, it's not freezing because it's grey, it's grey because it's freezing. That behaviour is provided by one of the Compiz plugins.

As to why your browsers are freezing, it's impossible to say at this end. Possibly there's a runaway script on whatever pages you're looking at, or possibly your computer just isn't powerful enough to run whatever you're trying to run on those pages and still have enough processor left over to do everything else.
