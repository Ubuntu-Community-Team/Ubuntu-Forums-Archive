### Post by silviur on 2008-01-22
Do NOT try the nvidia drivers.

Go to Screen and Graphics in Administration, and choose there different types of monitors. Remember, press only Test until you find a good monitor type. During the test, some settings wont work (the image is garbled or absent), but after 15-30 seconds it will revert automatically to the current 40Hz setting. Then test another monitor type.
