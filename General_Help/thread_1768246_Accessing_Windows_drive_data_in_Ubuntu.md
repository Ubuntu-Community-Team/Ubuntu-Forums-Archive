### Post by coffeecat on 2011-05-26
Are you getting the Unity desktop - the one with the dock-like launcher on the left? If so, click on the topmost icon (Home Folder) to open a Nautilus window. Click on "File System" in the left pane. Now double-click on the host folder. Voila!

**EDIT**: And if you have D: or E: so-called drives (partitions), you will be able to see them listed in the "Places" left pane of Nautilus when you first open it.
