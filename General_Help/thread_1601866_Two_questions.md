### Post by alexandari on 2010-10-20
Ubuntu releases are supported until a certain date,and after that the support for that release,for example 9.10 drops,and things go on with the further releases. By the time the support for 9.10 drops,there will already be new releases (3 I think? ) 
Usually,linux requires your username password when applying some kinds of settings that are system-based (let's put it that way) . That's why you were asked for you password. Happens when you try to install things too (for example) etc etc.

Oh yeah and,swiching monitors...yeah that's required a password hahaha
