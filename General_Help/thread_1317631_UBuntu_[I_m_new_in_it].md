### Post by Ceedub2 on 2009-11-06
Is it Ubuntu 9.10 you mean. 

You have to install flash for youtube to work. Go to your taskbar- to system then click on admin. Then look for Synaptic Program Manager and open it.

It will ask for your password. Once in the manager search for Flashplugin. it should bring up two flash plugins. Click on the non free version. I don't know why they say non free or what it means but anyways click on the checkbox and select mark for installation. Click apply and it will install it. You may need to restart afterwards but it will tell you. 

As far as mp3 compat, Rhythm box is compatible dont really know what you want to do so more info is needed. 

And then a simple program installer is at the bottom of the Applications menu called Ubuntu Software Center. All sorts of free apps there.

> **Ceedub2 said:**
> I don't know why they say non free or what it means but anyways click on the checkbox and select mark for installation.
Non-free doesn't mean it costs money. See [http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Free_software](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Free_software)

> As far as mp3 compat, Rhythm box is compatible dont really know what you want to do so more info is needed.

Rhythmbox will play mp3s if you have the codecs installed. They're not supplied with Ubuntu by default due to legal reasons, but can be installed using the ubuntu-restricted-extras package. See here for more info: [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/RestrictedFormats](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/RestrictedFormats)
