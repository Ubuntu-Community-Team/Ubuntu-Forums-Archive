### Post by s3MA00RRNY on 2010-11-22
If you want great performance, choose NVidia. Their drivers are always up-to-date, but not open source. There is a project to change that called Nouveau, but it's still young.

If you're a huge supporter of open source, go with ATI. They recently GPL'd their drivers. Their Linux support tends to lag one or two distributions, though. Of course that probably has changed since I last used them.
