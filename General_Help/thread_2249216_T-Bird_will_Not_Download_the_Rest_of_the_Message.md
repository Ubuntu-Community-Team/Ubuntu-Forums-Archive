### Post by amanchesterman on 2014-10-23
Ah, ok, I understand now: you are using a POP mail account. I have IMAP, which doesn't offer the 'download headers only' option.

I guess I can't help you, sorry, other than suggesting:
1) As a one-off check, access your mail with a webmail interface to check that the messages are still in your mail account, i.e. to confirm that the problem is with TBird not downloading them rather than them having disappeared from the server
2) Check your POP mail settings in TBird are right, e.g. that you have ticked the option to 'leave messages on server' (so that they aren't being deleted when the header is downloaded)
3) As a temporary measure, set up the same account in a client like Evolution -- again to confirm that the problem is in fact with TBird and not with the server or the account settings.
