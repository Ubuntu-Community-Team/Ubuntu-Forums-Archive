### Post by mbuehl on 2009-08-23
sounds more like a bios issue than anything else. your keyboard isn't being recognized until the OS is loaded. Mine does that too - I have to use a PS2 keyboard so I can actually arrow up/down or enter setup, anything really.

You may want to check for an updated BIOS, or just poke around in the setup portion of your BIOS (this is, assuming, that you CAN get in to that... which then I have no idea.)
