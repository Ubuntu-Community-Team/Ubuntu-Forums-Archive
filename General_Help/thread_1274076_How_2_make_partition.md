### Post by Jose Catre-Vandis on 2009-09-24
Do you really need to do it in terminal?

Why not use gparted (You should find it as Partition Editor/Manager in your apps menu)

However two programs to do this via cli are **fdisk** and **cfdisk**

You'll need to read the man pages to understand exactly how to use them, and take care that you only make changes to your flash drive, suggest you back up your important data first
