### Post by Paddy Landau on 2012-04-28
Hello Sam

The Ubuntu prompt would read something like, "Enter your password to perform administrative tasks".

You can recognise the format of the question if you press Alt-F2 and enter the command:```
gksudo :
```You'll notice that it darkens the rest of your screen and takes full focus of your keyboard and mouse.

Does the prompt look like that, or is it different?
