### Post by grahammechanical on 2014-09-06
Canonical developers use using 3 Nexus devices to develop Ubuntu for phones. They are the Nexus 4, 7 (2013) and 10. The images for those phones are the only official images being provided. Anything else is a port by other developers.

[http://developer.ubuntu.com/start/ubuntu-for-devices/devices/](http://developer.ubuntu.com/start/ubuntu-for-devices/devices/)
