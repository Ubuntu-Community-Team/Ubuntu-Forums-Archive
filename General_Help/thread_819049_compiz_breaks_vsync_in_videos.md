### Post by fdagear on 2008-06-05
I have a problem where compiz breaks the vertical synchronization in mplayer after sometime--I use opengl as the video output. Here is an example scenario: I watch a video, vsync is working. Then after browsing the web or going afk for a bit, I watch another video and vsync is broken. I can fix vsync by toggling compiz off/on or rebooting.

I have the ccsm refresh rate set to 60 and have unchecked both automatic detection of refresh rate and compiz vsync. At the default settings of 50Hz and automatically detect refresh rate, checked, vsync was always broken for videos.

So it seems that the compiz process settings are being reset to the default after sometime.

Looking for a permanent fix, besides turning off compiz effects.

### Post by dobran on 2009-03-12
Install CCSM (CompizConfig Settings Manager) - Applications - Add/Remove...

Once installed, open it from System - Preferences - CompizConfig Settings Manager. 


Open it, go to "General Settings" from there "Display Settings"

Disable:
Detect Refresh Rate

Enable:
Sync to VBlank

Set:
Refresh Rate to 60 or more if your monitor supports it.


This worked for me :)
