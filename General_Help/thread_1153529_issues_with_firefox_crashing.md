### Post by mb_webguy on 2009-05-09
The problems you're having are definitely video-related, and not specifically related to Firefox.  Firefox is probably just the most graphically-intensive application you happen to have open.  Try switching to a different video card driver.

To make sure that Firefox isn't actually part of the problem, you can try opening Firefox from the terminal.  That will show you error messages that aren't normally displayed by the GUI.
