### Post by lovinglinux on 2009-04-22
Is not the torrent client that specifically limit your speed, is the the protocol that works this way. Nevertheless, I see a lot of reports about some clients being faster than others. But in my opinion, speed really depends on the client/router proper configuration. uTorrent is very popular and people talk a lot about it's speed, maybe because the default configuration is better than others. Anyways, I use Deluge and I love it. IMO, is the best torrent client out there. It has a nice interface, it's easy to configure and has the all important features.
