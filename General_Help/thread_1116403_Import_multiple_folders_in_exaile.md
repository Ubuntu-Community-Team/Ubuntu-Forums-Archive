### Post by AllRadioisDead on 2009-04-04
Hi,
I have a zune, which is incompatible with linux. I plugged it into my laptop, dumped my collection, and put it onto my external hard drive. My music player of choice on ubuntu is exaile. When I dumped my zune collection, it neatly stored all my songs in folders by the artist. I need a way to import all of those folders into my collection, because it will take forever importing 80 folders individually. Thank you.

---

### Post by Tim Sharitt on 2009-04-04
I believe you should be able to add the folder containing all of you music folders to get everything added. The only problem you may have is if there are a lot of other files and folders in the same directory as your music folders, it may take longer for exaile to search through all the folders for music files.
