### Post by capribex on 2010-10-24
It's probably trivial but... I can't find anywhere how to disable the downloads window from popping up every time I... download. The browser is anyway indicating the downloads at the bottom of the frame and I wish for the downloads window to not pop. How do I disable it?

---

### Post by howefield on 2010-10-24
Try Edit > Preferences > General Tab and uncheck the tick beside "Show the Downloads window when downloading a file".

Of course, assuming that you are referring to Firefox.
