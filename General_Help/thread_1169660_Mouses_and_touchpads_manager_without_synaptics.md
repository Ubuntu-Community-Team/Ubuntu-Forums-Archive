### Post by SkyPlooM on 2009-05-25
Hi,
after a lot of problems to disable my touchpad in my laptop, I found the solution.
Gsynaptics doesn't works for me. SHMConfig error was present all the time. 
I tried a lot of things, and nothing happened.

Well, I developed a mini graphic application to enable, disable or see the mouses and touchpads properties.

It's programmed in python and I have made a .deb
The program will be installed at system -> preferences -> mouses and touchpads manager

You can download here:
[http://www.mariodiges.com/mouses_touchpads_manager/touchpads-en_1.0-1_i386.deb](http://www.mariodiges.com/mouses_touchpads_manager/touchpads-en_1.0-1_i386.deb)

to uninstall it:
s[I]udo dpkg -r touchpads-en

I hope will be useful

Cheers

I don't know if is the better place to post this...
