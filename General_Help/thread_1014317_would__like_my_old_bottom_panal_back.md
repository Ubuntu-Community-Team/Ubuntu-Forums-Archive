### Post by Ivo Moelans on 2008-12-17
If you want to keep your other settings you could also simply right click on your top panel and select *New Panel*. A new panel will be created (probably on the bottom; if not put it there by right clicking and selecting *Properties*). Then install on it *Show Desktop* etc. by right clicking on it and selecting *Add to Panel...*. You'll probably want *Window List*, *Workplace Switcher* and *Trash*.
