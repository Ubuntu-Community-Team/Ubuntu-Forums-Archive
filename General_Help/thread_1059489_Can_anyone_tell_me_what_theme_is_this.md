### Post by Brightbelt on 2009-02-03
This is possibly the Theme 'Slickness Black 0.9' or very similar to it. 

Go to: [http://www.gnome-look.org]("http://www.gnome-look.org") 
  Click on the GTK 2.x category on the left, and then choose 'The Most Downloads' tab. Click to the 2nd or 3rd page and it should be there. 

I'm familiar with this because this is the default theme for PCLOS Gnome 2008, which I have used a bit.  

I hope I hit the right one - like I said, the one I'm talking about may just be similar to what you're actually looking for. :)
