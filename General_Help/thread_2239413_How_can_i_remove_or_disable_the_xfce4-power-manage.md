### Post by Toz on 2014-08-13
To disable it, simply uncheck it from Settings Manager >> Session and Startup >> Autostart applications. Then on the next login, it won't be autostarted. You might also want to clean out your saved session cache in case its getting started from there (Settings Manager >> Session and Startup >> Sessions >> "Clear saved sessions".

As for what it does, see [http://docs.xfce.org/xfce/xfce4-power-manager/start]("http://docs.xfce.org/xfce/xfce4-power-manager/start").
