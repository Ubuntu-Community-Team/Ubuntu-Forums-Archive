### Post by shane19174 on 2008-11-03
The default look is not radically different from Heron. The most immediately perceivable difference will be the new wallpaper. There is also a dark theme called "DarkRoom" which, while not on by default, is preinstalled and can be turned on via System > Preferences > Appearance. You might also be interested in the package community-themes, which will add the "Dust," "Kin," and "New Wave" themes for you to choose from.
