### Post by SVEN1 on 2009-03-26
Couldn't get the Lexmark to work, couldn't get the Brother to work.

Well, stopped at a Church garage sale, there sat a couple of printers......picked the HP Photosmart 8150. A bit dusty, still had ink cartridges in it. No USB printer cable. For a measly sum of $5.00

Well, had my doubts it still worked, took the gamble.

Found a printer USB cable in my drawer, plugged it into my PC hooked up the power cable to my power strip.

Ubuntu was already up and running and looks like the printer was never turned off.

The printer came alive, Ubuntu configured the printer in about 15 seconds and it printed a perfect test print. Already printed a couple pages. Hmmm, that was way too easy!!!

What is the model number of the Brother?

Use Synaptic package manager to install the Brother drivers for your printer.Then plug in the printer and off you go.

Or 

Look for the driver on the [Brother Driver website](http://solutions.brother.com/linux/en_us/index.html) which also has the installation instructions.


Good Luck
