### Post by tszanon on 2007-07-26
I had some minor issues with Edgy and Feisty, so, since its release, I've been using Dapper.
In Edgy, the UPS battery indicator don't work.
In Feisty, the multimedia keys don't work.
So, my suggestion: try using Edgy or even Dapper, if they meet your needs.

One more thing: just because your hardware works flawlessly under windows, it doesn't mean it's not broken. I had a mouse that worked fine under windows, but sometimes, under Ubuntu, it became crazy ([http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=109985](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=109985)). Soon after, it died. Good software, bad hardware.
