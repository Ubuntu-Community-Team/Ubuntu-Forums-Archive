### Post by deadflowr on 2013-12-09
Is this normal Ubuntu?
If so, then the apps minimize to the launcher.
You can locate which apps are minimized by looking for an arrow on the left side of the app.
Normally, re-clicking on the app's launcher will bring it back as it was before minimizing it.

Of course this ONLY applies to unity/Ubuntu and not other desktops such as KDE/Kubuntu, Xfce/Xubuntu gnome-shell/UbuntuGnome, or Lxde/Lubuntu.
Among others.
