### Post by rustydragon on 2010-03-31
Hi all.

I have very weird issue with my 9.10 x64

My screen dims when there are large black areas on desktop.

For instance, if I open [http://www.blackle.com/](http://www.blackle.com/) in fullscreen  then my screen dims to almost unreadable state. Not only browser, but the whole desktop.
And it's not browser problem, it happens in both chrome and FF.
It also happens in terminal, if background is black.

Even more, "dimness" of the screen is relative, so if I open before mentioned site not in maximized browser, then screen will dim but not so much.

It happens on my workstation, which is not laptop. No funny settings in gnome-power-preferences. Compiz disabled, visual effects set to "none".
My video is Gforce, drivers 185.18.36.
Two monitors, but second one is disabled.

Any ideas of what that could be?
This dimming gets quite annoying.

### Post by rustydragon on 2010-04-01
Solved, it was a "feature" of my SyncMaster monitor, called MagicBright.
Turned it off, and will never use it again.
