### Post by SGAZ on 2010-10-26
You might try looking up your Motherboard at the Motherboard Manufacturer's site and checking the specs for what kind and how much RAM your set up is designed for.

If you are adding additional RAM to existing RAM then I would recommend sticking to the same brand and type as your existing RAM to minimize the potential for getting annoyed.

Have fun.  More RAM is always Better.
