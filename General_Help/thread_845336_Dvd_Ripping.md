### Post by mc4man on 2008-06-30
> Is it illegal to rip copyrighted dvds that i went out and bought
No , not exactly. Technically under the dmca it's illegal to circumvent copyright protections in the course of exercising your fair use rights, distribiting the means to do so (rippers) and probably advising others how to do so. 
Practically speaking no one cares as long as your doing it for your own use.
