### Post by pipodnmy on 2005-10-09
there is a bit of "song and dance" that you need to perform.
mount the ipod in /mnt/ipod (i believe)
then there is a connect button in the media panel.
when it is connected it will actually show the mp3 in your ipod.
then schedule the songs and hit transfer, you will see a status bar moving along.
then hit the connect button again to disconnect and you are (in theory) all set. unmount the ipod.
that being said it usually crashes on my ALL THE F*CKING TIME at 100%. hope there is a bug fix for that. happens only with 5.10 - 5.04 was working like a charm.
