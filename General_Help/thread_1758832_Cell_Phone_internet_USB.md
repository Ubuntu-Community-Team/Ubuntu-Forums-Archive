### Post by 3rdalbum on 2011-05-15
Depends what sort of phone and, if you're in the US, whether you've paid your carrier to let you do this.

If you've got the appropriate USB cable, then just try it and see if it works.

Android phones come with this functionality built-in and Network Manager lets it happen no problems, but your carrier might have disabled it.
