### Post by lovinglinux on 2010-03-10
You have probably added the *ubuntu-mozilla-daily* ppa to upgrade Firefox without knowing it will also upgrade Thunderbird. Shredder is just the codename of development version of Thunderbird 3, just like Namoroka is the codename for Firefox 3.6.

If you want to revert to Thunderbird, disable *ubuntu-mozilla-daily* ppa and re-install Thunderbird. 

I would also recommend using the *mozillateam/firefox-stable* ppa instead of *ubuntu-mozilla-daily* for upgrading Firefox. See [this](http://lovinglinux.megabyet.net/?page_id=220##3---Installation-from-PPA-repositories-3).
