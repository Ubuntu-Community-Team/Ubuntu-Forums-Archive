### Post by dcstar on 2011-08-06
> **waloshin said:**
> I just got a Macbook air and only have a 128 gb ssd and would like to install ububtu on a 8 gb  class 6 sd card to conserve space.

SD cards - and other similar media - are not designed to handle the many write cycles an O/S will be doing as a normal part of its operations.

The SD card will basically die a premature death if used for any length of time in this manner.

There are resources on the 'net describing on how you can minimise the writing to media like this and extend their life.
