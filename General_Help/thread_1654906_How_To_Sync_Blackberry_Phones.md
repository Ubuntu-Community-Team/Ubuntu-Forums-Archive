LindBerry is an application to manage BlackBerry devices on our distribution. It helps us to make backups of mobile device to your system, synchronize and manage contacts. It alows installation  and uninstallation of applications with few clicks.

It is still in beta. I have successfully tested it with Ubuntu 10.04 and 10.10.

LindBerry has been tested with handheld BlackBerry RIM 8520 and 8900 Curve, Bold 9000 and 9650 but I think it is compatible with all models.

You can download  and use LindBerry as Desktop Manager in Linux from [here]("http://linberry.webcindario.com/indexdescargar.html").
