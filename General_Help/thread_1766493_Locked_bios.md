### Post by seawolf167 on 2011-05-25
I meant that if you could boot off a LiveCD, you could mount the internal hard drive and copy/modify/delete any/all files on the hard drive. This of course is a huge security risk, so even though students have physical access to the box, they try to mitigate this risk by locking the BIOS with a password and disabling other boot media.

I of course can't speak for your particular school IT Administrators as to why some are open and other's aren't, but that is the general reason.
