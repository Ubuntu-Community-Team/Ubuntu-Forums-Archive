### Post by meanmrmustard on 2007-01-18
I made a discovery this morning that may be news only to me.

I had been experiencing a lack of sound on sites like you tube, but xmms, etc. would play fine.

I have onboard sound in addition to a PCI sound card.

Long story short... once i disabled the onboard audio in the BIOS, all sound apps and sound in  embedded video sites played through the PCI card.

Maybe this will help someone.
