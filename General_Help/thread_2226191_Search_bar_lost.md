### Post by Dave.YNA on 2014-05-26
Hello.... I think you may have accidentally removed it. 

First right click on the bar near where you enter a URL. It can be a bit awkard to do in the right place so try to do it on the menu icon (three horizontal bars to the right of the URL box). 

Then select customize. A new tab will open where you can add the search back in. You can do this by dragging and dropping the search to the top bar again.
