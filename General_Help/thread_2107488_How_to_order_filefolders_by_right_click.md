### Post by slickymaster on 2013-01-22
I don't think that what you want is available through mouse right click. At least there's no such option in Dolphin configurations.

You can customize the way files get sorted in the currently selected folder. They are sorted in alphabetical order by default but that can be changed via menu **View -> Sort by**
