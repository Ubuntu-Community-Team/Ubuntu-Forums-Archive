Hi did you know Linux works better on a dongle than windows, because it is a simpler program. It also costs less to run on pay as you go.  
You can use a GSM magmount antenna on a dongle, most dongles have an antenna socket inside on the side of the device.
You need a connector lead to connect to the FME conector on the magmount.
I have a 9db gain magmount antenna on the roof.
