### Post by vgrisham on 2007-10-31
SOLVED!

I cleared my cache and cookies and was able to upload immediately after doing so. 

In Firefox, go to Tools and choose Clear Private Data. I deselected everything except Cache and Cookies and then hit Clear Private Data Now.

Phew. :)
