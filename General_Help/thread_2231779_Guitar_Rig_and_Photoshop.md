### Post by lisati on 2014-06-27
Photoshop and Guitar Rig are programs intended for Windows. They won't work out of the box with Ubuntu without some serious help from utilities such as Wine or PlayOnLinux. Even then there's no guarantee that you'll be able to get them to work.

You might be able to get some ideas about alternatives here: [http://www.osalt.com/](http://www.osalt.com/)
