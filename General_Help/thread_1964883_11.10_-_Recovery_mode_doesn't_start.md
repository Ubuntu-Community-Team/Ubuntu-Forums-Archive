I don't know how to fix the recovery, but you can always make a copy of your system with Remastersys for now in case it does fail and you need to reinstall/restore everything. 

[http://debianhelp.wordpress.com/2012/04/20/how-to-install-and-use-remastersys-in-ubuntu-os/](http://debianhelp.wordpress.com/2012/04/20/how-to-install-and-use-remastersys-in-ubuntu-os/)

I don't know if it can be fixed. But I would definitely backup your system with Remastersys for now until there is a fix. Especially if you are going to be messing around with your grub settings, and hose your presently working system with unhelpful advice found on here. :)
