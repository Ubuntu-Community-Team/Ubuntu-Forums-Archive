### Post by Mr_J_ on 2005-10-16
1.Does the antena need to be Omni-direccional?
-If yes forget it.
-If not, then read on.

This is the cheap satelite dish.
To make it you need an antena that has a wire, and a metalic mesh bowl (I hope I said it right.).

The bowl because it's made of metal will increase the range of your antena, but make it direccional, and because there are holes in the metal it's light and allows for the antenas wire to pass threw. :D 

Just picture a satelite dish and use those items to replicate it. I've seen tripod mounts for these, but I say be practical. Nail it to a wall or just lean it on something.

U could always put a little bit of something, like velvet or suede, an old rag maybe and glue that behind the mesh to stick the antena to the bowl.

I lost the site or i'd give it to you so you could see pictures before thinking of doing it.
There is quite a jolt in db. Mine was only a test so I remember little, but I think even behind a wall there was a +4db jump.
