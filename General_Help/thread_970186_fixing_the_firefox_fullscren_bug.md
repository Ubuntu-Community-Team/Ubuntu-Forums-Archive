### Post by ju2wheels on 2008-11-04
The workaround for when Firefox opens and you cant see the top of the Firefox window to move it:

1. Press F11 twice.
2. Hold ALT and click on the top of the window and move it down.
3. Resize the window smaller now using the resize handles so the whole thing is visible on your desktop.
4. Maximize the window and the top of the window should now remain visible as normal.


It seems to be a bug where it doesnt remember that the top panel is actually there and so it draws the top of the Firefox window below the panel (at least for me it does, sounds like it draws it above the panel in your case).
