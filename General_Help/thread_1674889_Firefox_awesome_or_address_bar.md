### Post by JGJones on 2011-01-24
Going via your screenshot, above your address bar is the search box. 

In that search box, look at the icon to the left of the box, with a small "down" arrow in it. Click on the icon.

It will show a menu. Click on Manage Search Engines...

Quickest way is to click on "Restore Defaults" to restore all search engines as they were, with Google as the top choice.
