### Post by snowpine on 2010-12-31
Ubuntu is Ubuntu, whether you are running from an SD card, a hard drive, a Live CD, etc. If Compiz Fusion works on your computer using the Live CD, it will also work running from SD card.

My only words of warning are that 1) not all computers are capable of booting from SD, so make sure yours can; and 2) an SD card is much slower than a conventional hard drive.
