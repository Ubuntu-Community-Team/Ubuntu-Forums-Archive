### Post by K2712 on 2008-08-03
If you still have the lower toolbar(assuming you're using gnome), you should be able to right-click on that->Add New Panel to get the top toolbar back.

---

### Post by isaacj87 on 2008-08-03
On the panel, right click it and choose "Add to Panel."

Then in the new window, add the "Menu Bar" applet.
