### Post by BathroomNinja on 2005-12-02
As I have learned from my mistakes in the past; make sure you have at least a decent layout and some good articles/'stuff' on the site before you pimp it.  Nothing bugs people more (aside from whats stated above) then going to a site to see it 'under construction'.  It's probably also very important to explain how they can get help installing open source software/ OS's.
