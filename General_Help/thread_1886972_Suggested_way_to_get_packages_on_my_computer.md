### Post by holycow131415 on 2011-11-26
Get a flash drive, find and download .deb files of the program/s on to your flash drive while in windows. Take flash drive out and plug into ubuntu computer. Double click .deb files and it will start the install of the program you want.

### Post by T.exe on 2011-11-26
