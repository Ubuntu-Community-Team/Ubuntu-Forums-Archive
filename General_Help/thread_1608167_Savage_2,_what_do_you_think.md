### Post by joeoshawa on 2011-03-07
If your still interested i gave this game a go so here is my thoughts.

The game design is great the only thing i would say they need to change is the server should decide the teams based on player stats cause almost all the games i played were very very stacked and very very short. They have a shuffle feature but its a vote and it almost never goes through.

The human team you can get the hang of pretty quick but the beast team camera i find horrible its like a 6 year old with a hand held camera floating around behind you while you fight and that's how it looks to you.

The players are ignorant rude and abusive to their team mates. They treat new players like dirt and definitely don't ask questions or you'll get called everything in the book for the rest of the game. I had two good experiences and that was it and i played quite a lot. 

It can be a lot of fun when the teams are even but thats rare and the voice chat seems to be strictly to throw insults at your fellow team mates or you get some person on the mic who can't stop talking over your commander or team leader because he wants to tell you how well he's doing.

To be honest i like the game in most respects and i am sure you could get used to the camera but the real deal breaker is the players. If i want to be insulted in my spare time i will call my ex-wife.
