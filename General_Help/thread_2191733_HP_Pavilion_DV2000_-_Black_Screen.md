### Post by fantab on 2013-12-05
It could be RAM... I have this HP Laptop, where the Screen seems dead. I could hear the CPU fans and HDD and most importantly the LEDs keep blinking on the Caps Lock and Num Lock keys. The blinking of the LEDs, on investigation, turned out to be a code. Three Blinks and a Pause, it goes on until powered off. The code indicated that RAM was not working. I am yet to breathe life into it.

On my friend's laptop the BIOS would NOT boot. It turned out that the 'thermal paste' had dried up, causing heating issues, and eventually damaging the HDD.

A Bad Graphics card is also probable, as your neighbor already had both Graphics and heating issues.

The Point is it could be a hardware failure... pay attention to the beeps and flashing LEDs if any. Check on the Vendor's website for the listing of their codes and its meaning.
I don't think is a software issue at all.

My two cents...
