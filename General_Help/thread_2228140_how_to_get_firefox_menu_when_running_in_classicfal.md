### Post by deadflowr on 2014-06-05
IF you right click on the area next to the url bar of firefox, you should get an option for main menu.
That should put the menu above the url bar.

### Post by monkeybrain20122 on 2014-06-06
On the right most there is a rectangular thingy with three horizontal bars, click it would reveal a menu, go down to the bottom click "Customize" where you can add back the menu bar. However,  in DEs  other than Unity the menu bar will not be neatly embedded on the top of the screen, instead there will be a big margin of nothing on top of the menu so  you will lose a bit of vertical space.
