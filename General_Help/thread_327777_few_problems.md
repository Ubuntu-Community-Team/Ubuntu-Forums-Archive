### Post by d3v1ant_0n3 on 2006-12-29
In the notification area of your panel (by default it's in the top right of your screen on the top panel) there is sometimes an orange icon- this is to inform you you have updates- It will pop up a text notification when you first have new updates. Just click it, and it will start update-manager.

Have a look at [ THIS](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=87798&highlight=iPv6) thread concerning your internet speed- worked for me.
