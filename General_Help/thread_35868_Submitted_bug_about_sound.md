### Post by testingubuntu on 2005-05-20
Hi all I submited a bug about the "[COLOR=Blue]Enable sound server startup[/COLOR]" & "[COLOR=Blue]Sounds for Events[/COLOR]"
[COLOR=Red]System -->Preferences-->Sounds[/COLOR]
When you diasable the "Enable sound server startup"   the sounds for events doesn't play. But your able to use a application that requires the sound card. 
If the  "Enable sound server startup" is Enabled  you get the Sounds for events  but your applications won't be able to access the sound card. Hence forget about playing music or watching a DVD with sound. Unless you like watching silent movies :) 


This is I believe a hack so that the system and applications don't fight over Who has control of the sound card.
