### Post by Mark Phelps on 2012-03-09
Folks are not going to post drivers here, that is not what the forum is used for.

Unless you are doing high-performance gaming and really need hardware accelerated 3D support, then you don't need the AMD restricted drivers.

The default Radeon open-source drivers have improved a lot in recent Ubuntu releases.  You should remove your restricted drivers and try using them.

Instructions in the link below:

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RadeonDriver]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RadeonDriver")
