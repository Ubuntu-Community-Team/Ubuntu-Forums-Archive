Big plus 1 for clonezilla.  I've done about 10 drive images in the past few months rescuing off damaged hard drives.  It does not do a very good job with bad sectors especially on NTFS, so not great for data recovery, but it does work well really well on healthy drives.  On that note if anyone has a recommendation, other that ddrescue for data recover on damaged drives I'm all ears.
