### Post by gosh on 2007-05-10
use synaptic and search for "Dutch".
Then you find a number of packages. The ones you'll need are probably language-pack-nl, language-pack-gnome-nl-base, language-pack-gnome-nl.
There are also language packages for OpenOffice.Org and Firefox and many more.
