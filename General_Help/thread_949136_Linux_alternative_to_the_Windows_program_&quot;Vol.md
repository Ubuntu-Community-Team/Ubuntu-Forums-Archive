### Post by Yellow Pasque on 2008-10-15
If you have the type of mouse where you can push the scroll wheel to the side, it might control the volume in GNOME without installing anything (not sure about KDE). You can configure what the keyboard and mouse control in System -> Preferences -> Sound

### Post by ryanhaigh on 2008-10-16
I know it isn't what you were asking for but you can use the scroll wheel to adjust the volume by hovering over the volume applet on the panel.

You could probably do something a bit tricky using brightside and a couple of scripts to map your mousewheel to other buttons then remap them to their original settings. I'm not on my ubuntu machine right now to check if this would be possible with brightside but at least its a possibility.
