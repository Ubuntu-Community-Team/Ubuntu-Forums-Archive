### Post by SeanTater on 2007-02-04
Swap will most likely never make a system faster. However, it will keep it from crashing. If your computer is dead out of memory, you need more swap. 

How many GB's is your Hard Drive?

If you don't have much space on your Hard Drive, or you are not experiencing lock-ups, leave it that way. But otherwise, making it about 350 MB to 500 MB would be safer.
