### Post by fragos on 2007-02-23
RTF, Rich Text Format, is an excellent choice.  It's an all ASCII format with markup commands -- similar concept to HTML.  I've used it as a format to transfer between some very unusal devices and it always worked well.  If things don't look quite the same in OpenOffice your problem is usually the choice of fonts.
