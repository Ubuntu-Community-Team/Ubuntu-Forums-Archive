### Post by monkeybrain20122 on 2015-05-11
Please super to open the dash (or click the Ubuntu icon on top of the bar), then click the "A" in the bottom to see the application lens , A stands for applications I suppose (Alternatively, just press Super+a), then click the entry "installed" to see all applications.
