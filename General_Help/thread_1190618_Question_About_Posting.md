### Post by lisati on 2009-06-18
Posts you make yourself won't always show up on clicking "new posts" yourself until someone else replies - they will, however, show up when others click "new posts". If you want to monitor your posts and/or threads you can click on "search" then "Find all your posts". Chances are, however, that someone will have seen it. There's also a group of volunteers who regularly check for unanswered posts.
