### Post by NSDragon on 2009-07-27
I own a 8930g, and indeed, sound does not work out of the box. But it's relatively easy to fix. Just make sure to read this post: [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=921637](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=921637)

This is also a good resource: [http://monespaceperso.org/blog-en/2009/05/09/upgrade-alsa-1020-on-ubuntu-jaunty-904/](http://monespaceperso.org/blog-en/2009/05/09/upgrade-alsa-1020-on-ubuntu-jaunty-904/)

As for the graphics card drivers, get the 185.XX ones from the NVidia site. I've noticed a major performance boost compared to the 180.XX from the official repos (mostly in WoW and Second Life), although Compiz is still kinda sluggish without the Loose Binding option set, but YMMV.

So far, it runs flawlessly here; haven't had any other problems with it. Even wi-fi and 3G are supported out of the box.
