### Post by linuxinstalledfromhdd on 2011-05-30
I would try burning a disc of 10.10 and booting from that to see if the problem still remains, or try using a USB flash stick of RAM and installing Ubuntu on the flash USB with Ubuntu's Startup Disc Creator.  You can find that in Gnome in System >> Adminstration >> Startup Disc Creator.  Hook your flash stick to your computer. And then format the flash drive. And then you might need to download Ubuntu 10.10.   Here is a guide with some links to 10.10, in case you need it:
