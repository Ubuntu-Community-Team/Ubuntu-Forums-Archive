### Post by Lars Noodén on 2011-11-03
> **Zlatan said:**
> Hello, please advise how should I preinstall Lubuntu from Debian Squeeze or Ubuntu Oneiric? Thank you

If you have Ubuntu Oneiric already installed then you can add the package [lubuntu-desktop](http://packages.ubuntu.com/oneiric/lubuntu-desktop).  It will put Lubuntu on your computer.  You can find it in the Software Center or via Synaptic.

Now another question is how to remove all the Ubuntu-desktop packages that are no longer needed.

### Post by Lars Noodén on 2011-11-03
> **Zlatan said:**
> Sorry for being not-so-clear, but I want to PRE-install Lubuntu and do not install. That is, to give the machine to someone who could put in his own username and settings after my Lubuntu PRE-installation.

From the normal desktop installation CD, press F4 to change modes.  On the list you'll see "OEM install (for manufacturers)".  Choose that.
