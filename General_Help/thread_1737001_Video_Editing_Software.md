### Post by Tamlynmac on 2011-04-22
There's a number of them located [here]("http://www.getdeb.net/updates/Ubuntu/10.04/?category=Video%20Tools&page=1") (I've used Openshot - it's pretty simple - see page2) and a few listed in the Ubuntu Software Center under "sound & video" - just search that section for video editing.

It's always been my opinion that one should test a few apps before making a decision. Asking others for their opinion may not yield the best results, due to individual preferences and abilities.

Good Luck & hope this helps.
