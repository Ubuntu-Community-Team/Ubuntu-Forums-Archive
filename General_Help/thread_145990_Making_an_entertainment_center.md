### Post by Denstark on 2006-03-17
If I was to make my own TV/Media Center type computer, I would build it like this (I think)

I would have a large, large capacity to store recorded tv shows and the like, maybe 500 gigs (two 250 Gig SATA Drives in striping mode, or something like that).

I would have a TV-Tuner card, maybe an ATI All In Wonder. I would have to check the compatibility with Linux on that one, though. To record TV Shows, I would check out MythTV. I would also get some nice surround sound speakers and a nice sound card. That would be nice. You could also make the box headless and hook it up to your T.V., and get an infared controller to control it. Some googling should find you all that your looking for.
