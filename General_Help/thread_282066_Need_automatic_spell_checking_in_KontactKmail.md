### Post by heikki on 2006-10-22
There are spell Checker settings in Control Center - KDE Components - Spell Checker.

I use Finnish spell checker (called Voikko) which uses Ispell, so I have set Client to Ispell and Dictionary to Finnish. I don't know what to do with other spell checkers, but there seems to be an English spell checker too in the list.

When you have configured your spell checker, it works in many KDE applications, for example in KMail and Konqueror.

### Post by ianrutt on 2006-10-31
After selecting compose new message, or reply or whatever (i.e. when you have a composer window up), enable Options -> Automatic Spellchecking.

This setting seems to persist after the composer is closed (thankfully).

A word of warning, though - the KMail handbook states that automatic spellchecking doesn't work in HTML mode. I haven't checked, though.
