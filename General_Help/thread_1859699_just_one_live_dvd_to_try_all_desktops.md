### Post by ajgreeny on 2011-10-14
I see you are in the UK, and I can tell you that almost every time Linux Format magazine has ubuntu on its cover DVD it does just about what you want, ie a live version with all the major DEs on it.

Whether or not they will go all the way with 11.10 and add gnome-shell, and the gnome-session-fallback as well as kubuntu, xubuntu and lubuntu options I can not say, but it will be worth keeping an eye open in the next month or two.

The alternative, of course, is simply to use a live USB with persistence, and add the other DEs yourself.  It will need a fair size USB thumb drive, I imagine, though I have no idea exactly how large.
