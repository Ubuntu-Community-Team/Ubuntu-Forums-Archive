### Post by Zill on 2012-10-15
dmikulec:  In Evolution Calendars you should have a side-bar visible on the left hand side.  If this is not currently visible then go to the View, Layout menu at the top and check the option for the side-bar.

Then you should see one or more check boxes for each of your calendars, such as "Personal", "Birthdays and Anniversaries" etc.

Make sure all your required calendars have a checkmark against them.  If not then check the boxes.  If they already are checked then it is worth unchecking them and then closing and restarting Evolution.  Then recheck the boxes and hopefully your calendars will return.

HTH.
