### Post by ryanhaigh on 2008-06-26
Thats not unusual as I think that key combination must be associated with the driver. Is it possible to switch prior to the system being fully booted? What if you have the primary display closed. There may be an option in the bios to display on both monitors/internal/external but of course you have to get there first.

You may be able to use xrandr to turn on the external monitor from the commandline even if you can't see it.
