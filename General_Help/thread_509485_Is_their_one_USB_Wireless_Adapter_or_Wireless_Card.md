### Post by Scheater5 on 2007-07-25
Well, to my knowledge there aren't any that are built specifically for Linux.  And to tell you the truth, at this point that would just be bad business sense.  But there are many that are supported natively, either through reverse engineered drivers or because they are built for Linux *as well as* Windows and/or Mac.  For instance, the Intel wireless card in my Inspiron is natively supported.
