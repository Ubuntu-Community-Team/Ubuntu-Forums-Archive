### Post by requeth on 2010-10-26
If your worried about the page of wierd ASCII symbols while shutting down I wouldn't worry. The words on top of the symbols are all normal, as is the rest of the shutdown process. Where yours is ASCII crazy mine is off center quite a bit and looks to be a bit of a video card problem. It's probably shutting down some video compontents before it's ready, which is fine. If you hit your  left arrow key it'll give you a much more detailed shutdown rundown which may help.
