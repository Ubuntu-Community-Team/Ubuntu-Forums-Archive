### Post by Enigmapond on 2011-06-26
I would suggest installing the FF Add-on "Flash-Aid" and running the script. It will update your system with the proper flash for all the browsers and uninstall what's not needed.
[https://addons.mozilla.org/en-us/firefox/addon/flash-aid/](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-us/firefox/addon/flash-aid/)

Also if you have No-Script installed in FF make sure you allow scripting for those sites.

Hope this helps..

Cheers!
