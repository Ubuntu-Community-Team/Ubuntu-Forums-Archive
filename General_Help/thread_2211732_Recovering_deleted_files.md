### Post by whitesmith on 2014-03-17
If "supra shift" means "shift delete" then you removed the file. There are utilities that allow at least partial recovery in cases like these. The less you use your machine before trying the utility, the greater the probability of success. Delete without the shift would have the same effect. Have a look at **Scalpel**.
