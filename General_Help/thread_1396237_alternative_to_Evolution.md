### Post by Roasted on 2010-11-03
> **Maximiliano said:**
> Ok, but what are the main differences between Evolution and Thunderbird?

Thunderbird is for email. That's about it.

Evolution is a full featured email suite. It has contacts, tasks, memos, calendars, etc. Thunderbird has extensions for a lot of these, but I have spent many headaches trying to make Thunderbird "act" more feature packed like Evolution.

The bottom line... for a simple email program, nothing more, nothing less = Thunderbird.

For a feature packed email program with additional features most people in the business environment would use = Evolution.
