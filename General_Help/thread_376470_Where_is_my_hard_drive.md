### Post by arvevans on 2007-03-05
When you pick "Places/Computer/Filesystem" you will see that the mount point for Filesystem is your Root Directory or "/".

If you have any other hard drives they may appear in the same screen as "Filesystem" with whatever drive name you might have assigned.
