### Post by joriad on 2009-07-25
Here is a direct quote from the website

"we only use official branding for our default browser (default for jaunty is 3.0)also we explicitly want both to be installable side by side
and same branding would make them indistinguishable on your desktop
another point to consider is that branding is part of top-level UI
and changing (top-level) UI is not something we do in stable/security updates anyway"
