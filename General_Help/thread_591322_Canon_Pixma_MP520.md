### Post by GrandMasterV on 2007-10-28
Hi everyone.

I have a Pixma MP520 too and found the only way to get print support in ubuntu is to download turboprint.  You can use it free for low resolution printing or purchase it for high resolution support.

[http://www.turboprint.de/english.html]("http://www.turboprint.de/english.html")

I have had issues with sharing it over the network to a windows vista pc.  Whenever they print anything other than plain black text it prints all funny.  I'm able to print ok from linux though with graphics and such.

Hope this helps. :)

- V
