### Post by jtc on 2007-02-05
Just a note.

Myself I like Ogg-vorbis allot. Still I wouldn't convert existing mp3:s to ogg:s. When it comes to soundquality it is generally not a good idea to convert from one lossy format to another.

Of course, if you are happy with the resulting files, don't let me stop you :-)
