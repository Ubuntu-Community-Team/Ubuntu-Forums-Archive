### Post by fyo on 2008-01-25
I have an 8500GT PCI Express that can do all that you appear to require. It's not a available in AGP, but the 7600 GS is about as fast (doesn't have "DirectX 10", though). You can get one on Newegg for $90.

If you can find a place that sells them, you should be able to get the significantly faster GT model for not too much more. We're talking like > 50% faster, so it's nothing to sneeze at (e.g. 3DMark and Prey). Technically, the difference is 40% faster core and 75% faster memory.

Looking around, Newegg does actually have a 7600GT with AGPx8 PRO interface (check you have PRO to make sure):
