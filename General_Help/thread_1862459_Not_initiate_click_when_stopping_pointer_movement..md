### Post by international on 2011-10-17
I solved it. 

To start the simulated secondary click from the command line,         open the Terminal and type the         following:
 mousetweaks         --ssc

To stop the simulated secondary click from the command line,         open the Terminal and type the         following: 
mousetweaks         -ssc
