### Post by drs305 on 2010-10-20
In Synaptic, highlight the package, then from the menu, Packages, Lock Version.

You can also look at the "Holding Packages" section here:
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/PinningHowto]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/PinningHowto")
