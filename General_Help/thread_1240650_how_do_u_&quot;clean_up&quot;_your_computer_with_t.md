### Post by Finalfantasykid on 2009-08-14
I think what you are refering to is...

```
sudo apt-get clean
```

and...

```
sudo apt-get autoclean
```

and...

```
sudo apt-get autoremove
```

I usually do this every couple of weeks.  Its a a good way to keep your harddrive clean of old crap.
