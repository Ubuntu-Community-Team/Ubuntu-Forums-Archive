### Post by Fell on 2006-11-07
To view realplayer files full screen on the BBC website first click the video link as you normally would. When the window with the embeded realplayer appears, stop the clip from playing.

Right click anywhere on the window except the player, I tend to click on the description beside the video. Select **View Page Info** and then click the **Media** tab.

There will be a list of addresses, find the one that ends in **.ram** (it's type will be **Embed**) 

Right click it and select copy. Then go up to the address bar in firefox and paste it into it. Press enter and it will load up Realplayer and start the clip. You can then make it full screen.
