### Post by Bucky Ball on 2012-03-03
You need to hit a key at boot (the grub screen is after BIOS so that won't help and can't do anyhow - not related). 

Generally this is F1, Esc, or delete but it can vary depending on machine and BIOS. Check manual or have a look on net.

### Post by papibe on 2012-03-03
I think Toshiba's key to get into the BIOS is F2.

Turn on your laptop and press F2 repeatedly (click, click, click...) without stopping until you hear a beep. That should get you into the BIOS.

Hope it helps.
