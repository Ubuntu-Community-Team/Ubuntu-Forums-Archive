### Post by mokshadaya on 2008-05-30
There are a couple of different ways to do this.  The first is the following:

<altF2>
killall firefox

The second is to add the System Monitor applet to one of your panels.  In the future you can right-click on it and select "Open System Monitor" and the kill the name of the process you want to end.

The third way is to do the same by going to the System Monitor under the System/Administration menu.

Hope this helps.
