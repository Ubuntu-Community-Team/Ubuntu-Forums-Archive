### Post by kyphi on 2008-04-19
You might have dislodged something when installing your new RAM.  Unplug your drives, both hard and optical and reconnect.  If you have PATA drives (IDE) check your master/slave jumpers.

Remove and reseat your RAM modules.

If you take out the modules you have just put in does everything work again ?
