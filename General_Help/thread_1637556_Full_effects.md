### Post by Frogs Hair on 2010-12-04
In order to use some of the Compiz effects such as the cube and water effect to name two , you have enable extra effects. The Compiz advanced settings and plugins can be installed from the software center. The ability to run some of these effects is dependent on the quality of your graphics card.
