### Post by Cannaregio on 2009-01-17
I'm afraid nothing will give you exactly what you want (not yet, maybe soon, see evince maillist).
Currently you can choose between evince and

Ghostscript, ghostview, GSview	
Gpdf		
gv	
KPdf	
pdftotext	
ViewPDF
xpdf	

This said, remember that pdf files are good for printing, and for printing alone. They should NEVER be used for online presentation (slow, cumbersome commands, bad search, bad navigation, DRM-nightmares etc.)

So a pdftohtml or pdftotext converter is often a better option if you'r working on a file for online presentation and further elaboration.
