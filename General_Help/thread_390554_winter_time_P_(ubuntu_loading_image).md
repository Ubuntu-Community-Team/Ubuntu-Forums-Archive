### Post by mcduck on 2007-03-22
Easiest way would be to get a Usplash theme you like from [http://gnome-look.org/]("http://gnome-look.org/") and then get [Startup Manager]("http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=295524&highlight=startup+manager+usplash") and use that to install your new Usplash theme.

I've never even looked how Usplash themes are made but most likely you could just get one theme, and then use Gimp to change the pictures into something you like if you can't find a theme that's exactly what you want.
