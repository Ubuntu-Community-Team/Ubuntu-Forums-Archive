### Post by amac777 on 2009-09-07
Assuming it works the same as for Chinese support, just go to: System->Administration->Language Support.

Then you can Install Japanese, and also make sure the "Use input method engines (IME) to enter complex charactes" is checked as well. Logout and then login and you should be able to press CTRL-Space to switch to typing Japanese. The little Scim icon in the upper right will let you configure the input method settings.
