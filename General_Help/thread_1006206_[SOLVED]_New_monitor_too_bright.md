There should be an brightness adjustment, not just default settings in the OSD.

Failing that, in system->preferences->Power Management there should be a display brightness adjustment, but many monitors for desktops are not supported.  Usually it is laptops only, so you might be at the mercy of whatever the hardware's OSD can do.
