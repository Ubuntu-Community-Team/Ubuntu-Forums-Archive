### Post by mjuhasz on 2010-07-01
> **dgrafix said:**
> I am pretty sure i used to be able to type an address or location into nautilus before upgrading to 10.4, now they have this silly windows vista looking thing that you cannot type in.

Press Ctrl+L and you will get back the text field where you can type. With the gconf-editor, you can change the location bar permanently to show the textfield by default. The key is: /apps/nautilus/preferences/always_use_location_entry
