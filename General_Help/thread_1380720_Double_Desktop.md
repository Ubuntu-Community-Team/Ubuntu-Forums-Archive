### Post by ronniestamps on 2010-01-14
it should be there by default...

right mouse click on your taskbar, select add to panel, scroll down to the bottom and select window switcher, click add. once it appears on your taskbar, you can select how many desktops you have by right clicking on it and going into options. you can then switch between them by ctrl-alt-left or right arrow keys, or just by clicking the workspace you want to switch to... this can also be customized.
