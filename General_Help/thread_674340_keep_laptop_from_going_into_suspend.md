### Post by Casual Fan on 2008-01-29
Some options are available under System>Preferences>Power Management.

For even more options, press alt+f2.
Type gconf-editor into the box.
On the left side, click on Apps>gnome-power-manager.
You can change options for a bunch of power scenarios there.
