### Post by iceman227 on 2012-05-06
[color=magenta][color=blue]solved problem by tinkering with xserver.org
to get tv out:
1) set config as "twin view"
2) set monitor to "left" of tv box
3) hold shift key down drag pc monitor box over the tv box
4) under "position" select "clone"
5) at bottom select "apply"
6) at bottom select "save to x configuration file"
7) done, play videos or movies will show on tv whatever is on pc monitor.

I hope this may help someone else. [/color]
