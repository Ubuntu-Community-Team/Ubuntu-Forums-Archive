### Post by Hedgehog1 on 2011-05-01
Typically the screen staying dark, but you here the beep at power up indicates:

(1) Monitor Failure
(2) The video cable has been pulled out at one end or the other
(3) Monitor card failure

I would start with the cheapest thing - check the video cable.

Also, can you temporarily use a different monitor to see of the ones with the stuck-power-button has died?

By the way, when the machine is power up, in additiong to the 'beep' (we call the sound the POST: **P**ower **O**n **S**elf **T**est sound), do you later hear the XP or Ubuntu startup sound from the speakers?
