### Post by aysiu on 2006-03-26
[QUOTE=terranz]Don't do it!

I've had nothing but problems with 1.5 and I'm not alone.[/QUOTE] I've had no such problems, and I'm also not alone.

It really doesn't hurt to "install" 1.5, as it's quite easy to "uninstall."

Here are the instructions to remove 1.5:
[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/FirefoxNewVersion#head-51054869b8a40ea50859b0163c806e76f882499c](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/FirefoxNewVersion#head-51054869b8a40ea50859b0163c806e76f882499c)

All the "installation" really does it replace the launcher link with a link to the new Firefox, link the new Firefox to Ubuntu's Firefox plugins, and back up your user profile.

To "uninstall" it, you just remove both links and restore your old profile.
