### Post by raggamuffin on 2006-01-02
[QUOTE=taseal] is there another way of doing this?[/QUOTE]

:yes:

in KDE, you use a program called Ksnapshot to take screenshots...it should be in the K Menu under "Utilities," or you can just "Run Command.." "ksnapshot"...(you can save in ksnapshot, no need to past the img into an image editor)...

### Post by GeneralZod on 2006-01-03
I've noticed this - CTRL+Prt Scrn seems to be the key combo to take a snapshot of your desktop, and ALT+Prt Scrn is used to take a shot of the current window.

If you have klipper running in your systray, you can quickly [preview](http://etotheipiplusone.com/klipper-screenshot-preview.png) your screenshot to make sure it's worked! :)
