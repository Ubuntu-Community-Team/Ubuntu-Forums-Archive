### Post by clk99 on 2008-12-20
Alright, somebody showed me a quick workaround for the problem.  I just right-clicked on the titlebar - Advanced - Special Window Settings - and set it to remember the window size in there.

The friend who helped me had the same problem too.  Are view profiles not supposed to remember window size now?
