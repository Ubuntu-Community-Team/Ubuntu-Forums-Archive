### Post by Garyu on 2006-01-18
I don't know about the plugs, I think you will have to try that out for yourself.

BUT about making sound work with more than 2 channels... It's not always easy.

I had to install alsamixergui, not the text-based one because that worked to change volume but not to fix 5.1 sound. It has to be the one with gnome-gui. After you get that up and running you have to select "Duplicate front" or something like that (scroll right to find it). That worked for me in Breezy at least, but it didn't work in Hoary.

Try that and pray for the best. If it doesn't work I can't help you more than that I'm afraid.
