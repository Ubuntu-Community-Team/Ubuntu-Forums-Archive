### Post by kgarbutt on 2010-08-29
Hi everyone, 

I recently got a new AT&T HTC Aria, and I wanted to sync my Thunderbird contacts & Lighting calendar with my Google gmail & calendar. As for the sync'ing of the calendar, it's pretty well documented and easy to find out how to do this. But for my Thunderbird contacts, it's not as well documented so I wanted to share with everyone what is available to do this.

There is a wonderful Thunderbird Add-on called Zindus & can be found here: [http://www.zindus.com/](http://www.zindus.com/). I am not sure who the programmer is but he/she/they did a great job. 

So with that said, I have a perfectly sync'd android with my Thunderbird contacts, my Lighting calendar to Google's Gmail, contacts, and calendar. I also sync'd my Rhythmbox's music to my Android as well. 

All is good for me & I hope you too. By the way, you will need Thunderbird 3 for this level of sync'ing. I run both Ubuntu 9.10 & 10.04.

Ken
