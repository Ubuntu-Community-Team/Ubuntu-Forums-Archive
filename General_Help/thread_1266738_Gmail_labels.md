### Post by 7raTEYdCql on 2009-09-15
This has nothing related to ubuntu of Linux, something related to Gmail.

Is there anyway i can make gmail label the emails i get depending on the incoming sender, so i don't have to keep labelling the new mails from that sender every time i open my inbox. I did read the new help tips that gmail has posted, but there is nothing related to this mentioned, thought this might be the best place where i'd receive help.

### Post by Whiffle on 2009-09-15
Go to Settings, Filters, Create New Filter, put the sender in the From box, click Next Step, and check the box that says "Apply the label"
