### Post by maddog39 on 2010-10-01
Firewire is definitely well supported under Linux as I've been using firewire webcams for instance in it for years. As with all devices, they will show up under /dev when attached to your system. And it is my understanding that (though I could be wrong) FW gives you more access to the drive protocols itself than USB2.
