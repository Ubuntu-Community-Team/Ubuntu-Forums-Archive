### Post by redilyn on 2009-06-19
Are you sure its not setting up the driver automatically?

Plug in your printer and go to System -> Administration -> Printing

If your printer is listed you should be able to right click it and choose properties.

Under policies check Shared.

Then close the properties and click server then settings. Check Publish shared printers connected to this system
