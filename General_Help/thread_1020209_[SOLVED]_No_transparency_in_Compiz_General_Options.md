### Post by Tanker Bob on 2008-12-23
Actually, you don't have to mess with the accessibility section. What you are looking for is the Trailfocus setting under the Effects section. You can govern the amount of transparency of inactive windows by the number of windows tracked and degree of transparency. Fading windows also covers situations when windows open, close, and minimize.
