### Post by pneedleman on 2007-04-09
easy as hell and its amazing.

1) get AutoProfile plugin for GAIM and install
2) once installed goto the plugin settings and tab to the component settings. 
3) under executable insert this line 'dcop amarok player nowPlaying'
4) use the %e command in profile to view current amarok song.

Good Luck!
