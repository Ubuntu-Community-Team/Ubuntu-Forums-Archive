### Post by kokkus on 2008-09-13
You can install them by drag-and-drop them to the screenlets program (applications>accessories>screenlets)
Screenlets are allways-on-top like a sidebare or something else you always are able to access nomatter in which program you are in. and desklets are on your desktop like e.g a clock on your background.
You can download them from [www.gnome-look.org](www.gnome-look.org)

Enjoy, good luck and have fun:)
