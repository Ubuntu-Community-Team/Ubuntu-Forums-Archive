### Post by Coreigh on 2008-10-29
This is really hit-or-miss because some phone companies disable features so they can force you to buy stuff, but here goes.

If it is available, look in the phone menu for something about connections or USB and enable USB, or cable, or data connection, or USB data or something like this. Then when you plug the phone in to the USB on the computer the computer should see it as an external hard disk or USB stick.
