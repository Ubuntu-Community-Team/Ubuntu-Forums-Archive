### Post by Jay Car on 2011-01-02
> **fluft said:**
> hi 

what if my mouse stops working ? is there anything in the update i can 'untick' to keep these mouse drivers?

can i ask a silly question>?

do I have to back up any files/ folders on the desktop for example ? will the update erase any documents?

Oh my! I've never seen an update or upgrade erase any user-generated files or folders. But still, you should **always** back up files and folders on your desktop, whether you're updating, upgrading, or not. 

Seriously (this is the voice of experience talking) Back up often. It's the first rule of staying sane! 

Also, I have no knowledge of mouse issues with any recent updates.  But I keep a rag-tag collection of spare mice in a cupboard. I've only had a mouse issue with Ubuntu once, about 2 years ago.  I swapped the USB mouse for regular "mouse-port" mouse and went back to work. 

If allowing the upgrade makes you nervous, maybe just wait a few days, most problems get ironed out pretty fast. 

But whatever you do, please back up your files.
