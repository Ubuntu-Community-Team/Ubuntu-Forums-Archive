### Post by Chemical Imbalance on 2009-04-18
You need to access your BIOS boot menu and choose to boot from your cd.  You have to press a specific button right at computer startup (before the grub menu appears) to get to this in order to boot a cd.  Check your manual or online.  Some common keys are F8, F2, F10, F12 and DEL.
