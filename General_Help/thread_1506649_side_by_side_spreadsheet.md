### Post by 73ckn797 on 2010-06-10
When I load 2 OO Spreadsheets I can click at the top to unmaximize the window. You may find OO opens 2 windows for the 2 files. Adjust the size down to where both fit on screen. You can still press Window on the tool bar where you select which file to use/view. A mouse click to the other window works also. I do not know if this is the solution you want but it does work. There will be no synchronized scrolling.
