### Post by anilp on 2011-01-06
Textpad on Windows did a great job with this. How to make gedit save tabs when closing and reopen the files when restarting?

---

### Post by kostkon on 2011-01-06
You can use the *Session Saver* plugin. It's in the [*gedit-plugins* package]("http://packages.ubuntu.com/maverick/gedit-plugins").

Hope that helps.
