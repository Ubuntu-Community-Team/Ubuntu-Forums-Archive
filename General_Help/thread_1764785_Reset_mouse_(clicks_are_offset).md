### Post by ajgreeny on 2011-05-22
Print-screen+Alt-Gr+K will kill your xsession and log you out, but it is not the kindest way to do it, and you will lose any chance of a log to find out what has happened with your mouse.

Have you another mouse to try, just in case it's a hardware problem?
