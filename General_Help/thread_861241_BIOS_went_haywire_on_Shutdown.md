### Post by rbmorse on 2008-07-16
The messages weren't from the BIOS. What you were seeing were the normal Linux shutdown messages. Sometimes they get suppressed, sometimes they will display, depending upon hos some settings are set, but in either case they are nothing to worry about. 

There are similar messages when Ubuntu starts up, too, but those are normally suppressed because they scroll by so quickly they can't be read.

### Post by jimv on 2008-07-16
Yeah, all that text you saw was from Linux.  Windows doesn't do that, so it can look a bit freaky at first.  When you have Ubuntu installed on the harddrive, you usually see a progress bar instead of all the text.

Your BIOS is fine.
