### Post by Alterax on 2009-05-07
.....Okay.....

So, what can WE (we being the self-sustaining community of fellow users that is ubuntuforums) do about it?  The issues that you are discussing seem valid enough and aggravating, but it's insufficient and self-defeating to vent about the problem to people that can't really do anything about it.

I'd strongly recommend condensing down your post into its main points:  (i.e, Firefox 3 and Network manager need ability to negotiate session token), then describe your setup (rather than how inconvenienced you are) in detail.  Then provide these details to the proper channels, which are listed in the sticky on "FILING BUG REPORTS".  With the proper information provided to the proper people, you get a better chance of resolving this.

Going to the forums to complain about a fundamental design flaw is about as useful as going to a Mustang Enthusiasts convention and telling people off because of some flaw in Ford Motors' engine design.  You may get empathy (or hostility), but what you won't get is the actual bug fixed.  That requires the developers.
