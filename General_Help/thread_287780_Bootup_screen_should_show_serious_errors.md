### Post by martijndenerd on 2006-10-29
The new ubuntu bootup screen doesn't show the classical loading of all the modules and services. This fits well in the philosophy of creating a non-geek linux for human beings. But it also has a serious backdraw; the user is unaware of serious errors on his system. (See for instance the [topic]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?p=1681908#post1681908") on the swap that is not mounted).

So my point is; the bootup screen should show a warning in case of a serious error.
