[QUOTE=UnHoly]I was just wondering if Ubuntu came with the flash and java pluggins, dvd playback, notebook support, and maybe even the KDE dekstop?[/QUOTE]

Keep in mind, Windows doesn't even come with flash, java, or dvd playback.
