### Post by stinkeye on 2010-10-05
If the icons are in compressed format(eg tar.gz), extract them and place in 
the **.icons** folder in your home directory.
If you don't have a .icons folder create it.(don't forget the dot - this makes it a hidden file).
Ctrl + h to show hidden files.
