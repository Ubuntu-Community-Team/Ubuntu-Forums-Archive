### Post by eric-yorba on 2011-09-13
All the options for what opens when you plug in a device are in Nautilus. Just go to Edit->Preferences->Media.

From there, you can check "Never prompt or start programs on media insertion" if you don't ever want these windows opening when you plug things in.
