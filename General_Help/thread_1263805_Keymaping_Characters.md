[/LIST]

First off, are these keys present on your physical keyboard? What locale are you using?

Go to System->Preferences->Keyboard and check that the Keyboard model matches your keyboard. Generic works if you have an ordinary, standard keyboard from any manufacturer - choose a specific manufacturer is it has an unusual layout or special keys, like some laptops do. Then check the layout matches the keyboard - if you click the "+" then you will see a picture of the keys, but you will need to increase the size of th window that opens to be able to read the key legends. Choose your country, or maybe USA International if your keyboard is not regional.

The characters '"|$ should be on most keyboards. I get ç (I have no ç key) by typing compose+","+"c", but it may be on your keyboard.
