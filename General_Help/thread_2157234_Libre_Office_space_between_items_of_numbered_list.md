### Post by pwabrahams on 2013-06-24
I have a Libre Office document that contains a numbered list.   I would like to put some (vertical) space between the items on the list.  None of the settings I've looked at seem to provide for that.  How can I do it?  And is it possible to have two paragraphs within a single item?

---

### Post by DeathByDenim on 2013-06-24
You can. Select the entire list, then right-click and choose "Paragraph...". Go to the tab "Indents & Spacing". There you can play with "Line spacing" until you find something you like.
As for two paragraphs within a single item: Press <Shift> + <Enter> to go to the next line without creating a new item.
