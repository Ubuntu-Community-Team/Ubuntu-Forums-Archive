### Post by papibe on 2015-01-22
Hi Ivan_Gil.

There are several spanish keyboard layouts. I believe you are currently using the 'Spanish (Latin American)' one, which in fact has the accent over the '[' key when you use an American keyboard.

Open 'Text Entry' and add the layout simple called 'Spanish'. That one, if I remember correctly, has the accent over the quotation key.

If you remain in 'Text Entry', once you add a keyboard layout, you can see a big picture of the mapping keys by pressing the little keyboard icon.

Hope it helps. Let us know how it goes.
Regards.
