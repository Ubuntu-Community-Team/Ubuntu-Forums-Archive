### Post by lukeiamyourfather on 2010-08-17
Most video sites use H.264 which is a very resource intensive codec but provides great quality and small file sizes (relatively speaking). In Windows and OS X most of the graphics cards assist in decoding that H.264 stream be it in Flash or another player, but that doesn't happen in Flash for Linux (yet).
