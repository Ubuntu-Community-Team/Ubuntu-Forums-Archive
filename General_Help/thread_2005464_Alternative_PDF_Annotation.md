### Post by Ambimom on 2012-06-17
Here's another way to annotate PDF files in 12.04.  It's a little convoluted, but it does work and will do until the pdf import extension in Libre Office is made operative for Linux. 

To annotate an existing pdf file 

1. Open pdf in Gimp
2. Annotate with gimp text tool
3. Save as xcf (just in case)
4. Export as jpeg
5. Open blank document in Libre Office
6. Insert jpg into the document
7. Export as pdf


Voila!
