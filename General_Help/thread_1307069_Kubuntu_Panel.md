### Post by exploder on 2009-10-30
You will have to manually fix the panel. Just click on the cashew and place the cursor over what you want to move and click and drag it where you want. Once you get used to how it works you will not have any problems. When you finish making your changes, right click on the panel, panel options, and lock widgets to avoid this problem in the future. :D

### Post by lovinglinux on 2009-10-30
I have experienced the same problem and the culprit was the System Monitor widget. It is that one with all the other monitors. Once I removed it, the panel stopped messing up after boot.
