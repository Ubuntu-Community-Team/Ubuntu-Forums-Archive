Does such a thing exist and/or have a name?
You are free to make and sell anything you want, open source or not.
You can even sell Free Software if you want.
Free Software is a matter of liberty, not price.

With true Free Software, you are even Free to buy it, change it, and sell
your modified versions of it. But you cannot restrict the freedom of others by
preventing them from buying your Free Software and doing the same with it.

The problem is, if you choose to be closed source. You cannot benefit from
the monstrous amount of *good* open code that is already out there. A lot
of people do so; but they are, in fact, *stealing* Free Software and are legally liable.
