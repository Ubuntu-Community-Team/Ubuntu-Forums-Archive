### Post by Spr0k3t on 2010-05-20
Try downloading a public torrent like a popular Linux livecd and see what the results are.  Generally when the torrent is near completion, the number of peers start to reduce as the allocation tables become full.  Something to keep in mind as well, some trackers require sharing at a specific percentage before completing the torrent.  It's a way of enforcing a torrent to stay alive with peers so it's not here today gone tomorrow type of torrent.
