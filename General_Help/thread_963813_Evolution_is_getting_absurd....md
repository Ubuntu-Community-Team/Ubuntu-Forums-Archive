### Post by rbmorse on 2008-10-30
I don't know if this works for Intrepid or Hardy, but at one point you could run:

sudo apt-get purge evolution

and it remove evolution without taking everything else with it. 

As for the other things, it's all a matter of settings, which you can adjust to your liking. Hardly garbage. Just different.
