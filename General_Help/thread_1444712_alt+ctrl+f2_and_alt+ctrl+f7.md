### Post by Ancanus on 2010-04-01
ALT+CTRL+F2 is to switch to Terminal 2.
ALT+CTRL+F7 is to switch to Terminal 7, which is used for your graphical magic.

You can also use F1, F3, F4, F5 and F6.

You can use these terminals to do anything you do in your usual terminal (except for executables involving graphics, that is.) I usually use them when my graphical interface needs a few **pkill's** and it's too lagged to open me a terminal.
