### Post by p_quarles on 2007-12-28
Scriptkiddies, probably. It's not hard to find an open port on a networked computer and attempt to access it. 

The best advice would be to completely turn off all desktop sharing / remote access on both Ubuntu and Windows. If/when you need it, you can always enable it temporarily.
