### Post by Herman on 2012-02-27
I have an EeePC 4G and I have / in the SSD and /boot and /home in an SD card.

At first it worked great, I forget what grade of SD card I had then.
After that I had a lot of trouble with SD cards, I was trying grade 4 cards because that's all I can get locally. Most consumers go for the cheapest, so that's all country stores can afford to stock.

After a recent trip to the city I returned with a Grade 10 SD card and the SD cards seems to be working okay now. Prices seem to have come down and it only cost a few dollars more.

I'm not sure about a Grade 6 card, but if you value your time, stick with a 10. 
Also note I don't have / in the SD card, but in the built-in SSD.
