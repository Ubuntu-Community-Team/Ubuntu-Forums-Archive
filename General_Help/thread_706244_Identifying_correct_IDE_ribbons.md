### Post by chrismine on 2008-02-24
Rather use the thin 80 cable ribbons. They are compatible with all motherboards. They still only use 40 connectors but the extra cables in between reduces noise.

See this article: [http://www.mikeshardware.com/howtos/howto_connect_ide_hd.html](http://www.mikeshardware.com/howtos/howto_connect_ide_hd.html)
