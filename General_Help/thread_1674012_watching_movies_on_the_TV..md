### Post by vacco on 2011-01-23
If both your laptop and your TV have S-Video, you can just purchase an S-Video cable and plug them together. Pay attention to how many pins your S-Video ports have though. Also, in my experience, S-video does not always give the best quality. I used to connect my laptop to an old "fat" TV with S-video, which gave *acceptable* quality for video, although some nuances like a narrow black bar on the right side. I have not tried S-video with a flat tv though.

S-video works, but if your TV have HDMI, DVI or VGA input, I would rather try to use these.
