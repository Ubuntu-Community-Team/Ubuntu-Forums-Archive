### Post by geirha on 2008-07-31
There's a "random" bug with nautilus that makes it not draw the wallpaper and desktop sometimes. If this is the case, try running "killall nautilus" in a terminal. It will kill the currently "faulty" running nautilus, and a new one will be spawned by gnome, hopefully with wallpaper and desktop-icons.
