### Post by cjmabry25 on 2009-08-08
Navigate to your Home folder, then hold Ctrl+H to enable views of hidden folders, then find the folder named .config, then inside that find cairo-dock, then inside that find themes and extract the folder that the theme is in into the themes folder.

Hope I helped.
