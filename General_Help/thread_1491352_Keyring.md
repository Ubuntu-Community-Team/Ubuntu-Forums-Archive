### Post by formaldehyde_spoon on 2010-05-29
To disable the key ring, remove it from System > Admin > Startups 

To not be asked for your wifi password, right glick on the wifi symbol on your panel, Edit Connections, go to the connection in question, enter the password, and select Enable for All Users.

Problem solved ;)
