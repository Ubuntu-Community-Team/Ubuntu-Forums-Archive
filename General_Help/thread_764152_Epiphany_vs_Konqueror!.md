### Post by p_quarles on 2008-04-23
Konqueror has more features than Epiphany. Whether this is a good thing or not depends on whether you need those features. Konqueror is essentially the basic building block of KDE, whereas Epiphany is an isolated component of GNOME. 

The Khtml rendering engine (in Konqueror) scores better on most rendering speed tests than does Gecko (in the current version of Epiphany). Nonetheless, Epiphany is a smaller program, and may take fewer system resources in a lot of use cases. 

Only you can really decided which one better meets your needs.
