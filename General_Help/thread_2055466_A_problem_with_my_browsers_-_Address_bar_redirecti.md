### Post by critin on 2012-09-09
> **Fady10 said:**
> How could I find "**Don't allow sites to redirect my page."** in firefox ?

I can only see WARN ME now, but I have seen DON'T ALLOW.  Must be on another version or browser.

Click Edit at the menu along the top panel of FF, click Preferences.  In the Advance Tab, look for Warn me when....and put a check in the box.

Click each tab and see what privacy protection is offered, click the ones you want to use.

If this is a search engine issue, click on the tiny arrow next to the google icon (if you're using google)  at the top right of the browser.  click on Manage Search Engines and remove the ones you don't want.

A friend may be sending you mail from an address connected to 163.com and not even know it.  

Run the virus/malware checker.
