### Post by cptrohn on 2014-01-19
OK, so I use a multiple monitor setup, so if I am watching video on say youtube in a browser and I move it to another monitor and fullscreen it, then go back to the main screen to do something else, as soon as I click on the other monitor I lose the full screen on the secondary monitor where the video was playing.  Anybody know of a way to fix this?    I am wanting to watch training videos in full screen on the secondary monitor and perform the exercises on the main monitor.   

I'm at a loss...:confused:

### Post by tgalati4 on 2014-01-19
Before maximizing the video window, right-click on the top window bar and select "Always on Top".  Then maximize the window to full screen and see if that fixes the issue.  

If that doesn't work, then change the alignment of your screens, so instead of side-by-side, set them corner-to-corner.  There is less of a chance for the mouse to go over the second window.
