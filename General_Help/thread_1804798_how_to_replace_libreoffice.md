### Post by grahammechanical on 2011-07-15
You are confusing Libreoffice with Ubuntu. These are two different things. You have an issue with a program. Please, do not condemn the operating system.

It would be a mistake to think that issues with Libreoffice would not be issues in Openoffice. The first is an offshoot from the second. I doubt that there has been sufficient time for the two to diverge greatly. Have you tried going onto a Libreoffice forum or an Openoffice forum and asking there? 

As for testing Libreoffice, that is what you are doing right now. With Linux all users are testers because the OS and the applications are under development.
