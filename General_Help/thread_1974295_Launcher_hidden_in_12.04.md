### Post by parajulik on 2012-05-05
By default, the launcher is always shown. If it is set to auto-hide, try moving your mouse up and down a little bit (in the left border). The sensitivity might be too low for you.

If that indeed is the reason, which it most likely is, you can change it by doing to.

Appearance -> Behavior (tab) -> Reveal sensitivity.
