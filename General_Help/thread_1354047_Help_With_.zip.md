### Post by t0p on 2009-12-13
There's a program called **rarcrack** that is supposed to be able to crack password-protected archives, including .zip archives.

Unfortunately, it's not in the repos and I can't remember where I got it from.  But a simple google for "rarcrack download" should turn it up.

I doubt it'll do you much good though.  It works by bruteforcing the password, which is a time-consuming and often fruitless task.  You might be better off googling for "file.zip password" in the hope that you can find the password posted somewhere (of course replace "file.zip" with the archive's actual name).
