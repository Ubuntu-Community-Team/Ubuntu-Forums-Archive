### Post by matt_symes on 2013-06-16
Hi

> **Niaj Morshed said:**
> I'm using Ubuntu 12.04. When I start my pc then monitor shows a report " CMOS Checksum Error". What does it's meaning & how to fix it?. Please help me.

I means that your CMOS is corrupted and the checksum does not match when recalculated at boot.

How old is the PC ? How old is the CMOS battery in  the PC ?

What is the PC make and model ?

Depending on the age of your PC, you will have to use jumpers to clear the CMOS or boot into the BIOS and do it from there.

Taking the battery out for 5 minutes will also clear the CMOS.
