### Post by DanneStrat on 2011-02-11
> **UncleMonty said:**
> I want to delete Pidgin and replace it with Empathy. Is it possible to retain my chat logs?

Yes it is.

Go to your home folder.

Then to show hidden files and folders, press ctrl+H

Your logs will be stored in ".purple/logs/".

You can just copy them from there.

Good luck!
