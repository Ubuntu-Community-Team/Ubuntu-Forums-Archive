### Post by Mark Phelps on 2012-03-06
Windows and Linux use very different drivers, so knowing which one works in Linux is likely to do you no good.

If you have a laptop, then check the manufacturer's website to see if they have a driver update.

Also, if you look in Device Manager in Windows, you may be able to see the existing driver version (it's typically Synaptics) and go to their site to see if they have more recent drivers.  I recently upgraded my touchpad drivers on my Win7 laptop doing just this.

And finally, if you have Win7, then go to the Win7 forums (sevenforums.com) and look through their Drivers forum.  This is where I found the thread that had a link to the new Synpatics drivers.
