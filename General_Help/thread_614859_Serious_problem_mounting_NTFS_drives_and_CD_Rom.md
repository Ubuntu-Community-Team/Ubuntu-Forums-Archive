### Post by minus198 on 2007-11-16
You cant mount it in /media. You must mount it in /media/<The name you want to use>

LABEL=500GDISK1 /media/500GB ntfs-3g rw,uid=1000,auto 0 0

Thats how I have it.

rw = readwrite
uid=1000 means that you are allowed to do whatever you want.
auto = Automounts on startup.
