### Post by sinner99 on 2010-05-30
I have accidentally removed the volume icon several times, I click far too fast at times. I found to get it back simply right click the gnome-panel where you want the icon to appear and left click 'add to panel' and I do believe adding 'Indicator Applet' restored it for me.
As far as adding icons to the menu, I assume you mean the gnome menu, its under System, Preferences, Main Menu - this edits your menus. 
The text stuff is easy, its in Nautilus, Edit, Preferences, take a look around you'll find it.
