### Post by firebottle01 on 2008-11-14
hi

just a bit of advice for anyone looking for a simple and basic way to manage their personal finance, and wean themselves off ms-money or other non-free software.

I wanted a credible alternative to Ms-money - specifically i wanted to be able to predict the future balance of my current (checking) account. I tried Moneydance but was unimpressed.

i've got no interest in producing fancy pie charts for showing money already spent, i just want to know how much i am likely to have in my account in the middle of august 2009 (for example)

i've solved the problem, by adapting the Personal Money Management Template on the OpenOffice website

[http://documentation.openoffice.org/Samples_Templates/User/template_2_x/calc/index.html](http://documentation.openoffice.org/Samples_Templates/User/template_2_x/calc/index.html)

By adapting the Budget sheet contained within the template i am able to get a detailed idea of my likely current a/c balance on ANY given day between now and end 2009. 

hope the link above proves to be useful

firebottle01
