### Post by lixy on 2007-06-14
A few days ago, to my suprise, new emails I received in my inboxes started sorting themselves below original emails. That is, they formed a sort of thread instead of being sorted by date (which I ask them to). I also get a little arrow on the subject line that enables hiding subsequent emails.

I'm not entirely sure that made sense. So I'll try again. I want new emails NOT to be linked to older mails from the same sender and same topic. I want them on top of the list, not somewhere in between the old ones.

I looked around the preferences, and there's doesn't seem to be anyhing to control this. 

Be most grateful if you could point to a workaround,

P.S: Evolution 2.6.1 on PPC Dapper

### Post by lixy on 2007-08-12
Well, two months later, I stumbled across the solution - which was starring me in the face I have to admit.

There's an option in the main menu as follows Evolution > View > Group By Threads (Ctrl+T). I must have hit a Ctrl+T by accident, and that set it up to group by thread mode.
