### Post by seawolf167 on 2011-07-12
IMO you are pretty much stuck with the good ol' Nvidia vs. ATI.

Your two choices for each of these is the manufacturer provided proprietary graphics driver suite available from their website or the open source drivers available from Additional Drivers. In the second case, [Fglrx ]("http://www.thinkwiki.org/wiki/Fglrx")is proprietary, [nouveau ]("http://nouveau.freedesktop.org/wiki/")is open source but the stable version only supports 2d, the [development ]("http://nouveau.freedesktop.org/wiki/MesaDrivers")release supports 3d.
