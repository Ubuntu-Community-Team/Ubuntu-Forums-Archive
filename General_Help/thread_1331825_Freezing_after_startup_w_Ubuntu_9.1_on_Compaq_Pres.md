### Post by infaction on 2010-01-30
Yes I upgraded the video card per the recommendation of my IT friend and it worked absolutely perfectly. He gave me an ATI Radeon card of some sort (don't remember, the computer resides with my brother). Anyway, Ubuntu is apparently picky about the quality of your video card so I enthusiastically recommend that as your first avenue of testing. :D

Also, it was an older (but not ancient), used ATI RAdeon card...I remember it had an onboard fan. I think the point was that it was just simply better than what was built into the machine. Good luck.
