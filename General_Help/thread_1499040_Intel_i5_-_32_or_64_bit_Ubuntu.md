### Post by WorMzy on 2010-06-01
The name "AMD64" doesn't mean that only AMD processors can use it, just as Intel 80386 (or I386 as it's commonly known) doesn't mean it'll only work on Intel processors. It's just named after the company that developed it. i3, i5, and i7 are all AMD64 compatible, as far as I'm aware.
