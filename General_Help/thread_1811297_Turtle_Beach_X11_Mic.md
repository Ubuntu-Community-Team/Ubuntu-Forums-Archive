### Post by -tr on 2011-07-24
Hi there. Finally got myself a headset for my PC / 360 gaming, but immediately ran into a problem. Ubuntu is not recognizing the X11 microphone, under choose a device for input, there's nothing (and yes, the mic itself works. there's a playback feature).

Anyone know what I need to do to get it to show up?

EDIT: I've changed everything that would make it work in alsamixer, still nothing. Also USB mics work fine, just the 3.5mm mic is not being recognized.

EDITEDIT: Just found the solution, needed to change the settings option in Sound Preferences > Hardware to Input+Output. Solved!
