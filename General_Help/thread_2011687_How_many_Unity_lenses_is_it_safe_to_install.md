### Post by josephmills on 2012-06-27
You can install all them 
you can have up to 40 (I Think) Lens before Unity Dash runs out of room. There are also Some real cool scopes over here. 

[https://blueprints.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+spec/desktop-p-100scopes](https://blueprints.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+spec/desktop-p-100scopes)

I mean I am sure that one scope is not going to get along with others 

Take alook at the youtube lens you 11.10 and also the video lens for 11.10 then there is the Ubuntu TV Video lens for 11.10 

if you try to install all of these they will not work because there can only be one "video.lens" running at one time.This  
means that 3 different developers made 3 different lens and scopes and all of them called them 

"video.lens"

That is the only trouble that I have run into. 

the google contacts lens is not that great and does not show all the names 

as far as the 100 scopes they are all(well most) "bleeding edge" meaning that they are in development. 

Have fun with all you new lens and scopes :)
