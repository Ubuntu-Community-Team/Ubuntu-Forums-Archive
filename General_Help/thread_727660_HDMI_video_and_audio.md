### Post by Rocket2DMn on 2008-03-18
You only selected 1024x768 as the max resolution when you did the reconfiguration?  Your HDTV should have much better than that, and it should be widescreen.
Although the fglrx drivers work for your card, you could try the open source "ati" ones and see what happens as well.
