### Post by piquat on 2010-12-31
> **intoit said:**
> I am hoping someone here may have an idea which may **spark** my tired mind.

Ironic wording, it could have been static.

One idea:

I have two 900's.  They come with standoffs already mounted on the tray.  There are so many of them that it wouldn't be hard to not realize that one of them didn't line up with a hole and instead, is touching the back of your motherboard grounding something out.

The standoffs were not right for my mATX board on the one I just built, I had to move some.  Pull your board and make sure it's mounted properly, if you haven't already.

Another idea is the I/O shield.  Make sure nothing is sticking out off of it towards the board touching something it shouldn't be.
