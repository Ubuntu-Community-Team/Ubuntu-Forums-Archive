### Post by ajgreeny on 2009-05-04
It all looks fine to me.  The resolution can not be far from your required 1680x1050 judging from the look of the panels.

You can reduce the icon size easily, however, by opening nautilus and setting the preferences in the Edit Preferences menu.  The first tab "Views" shows Icon View Defaults, which I always set to 66%.  I always reduce the other sizes as well, by the way, but that's just a personal preference.
