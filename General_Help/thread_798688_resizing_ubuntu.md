### Post by TeoBigusGeekus on 2008-05-18
Step 1:
Backup everything crucial from both Ubuntu and Window$.

Step 2:
Download G-Parted live cd.
[http://gparted.sourceforge.net/livecd.php]("http://gparted.sourceforge.net/livecd.php")

Step 3:
Burn it on a cd and boot with it. Make any alterations you want (i.e. shrink window$, expand ubuntu, etc.).

Step 4:
Cross your fingers and reboot...

NOTE: Before you do all this, make sure you defragment your window$ partition 5 or 6 times.
