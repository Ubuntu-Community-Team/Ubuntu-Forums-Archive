### Post by CLomax on 2008-11-09
It's likely that getting ubuntustudio-desktop from apt-get is causing these problems since the program you have open at the time works while the desktop doesn't. Try removing ubuntustudio-desktop the same way:
```
sudo apt-get remove ubuntustudio-desktop
``` and see if that makes a difference.

Just in case it doesn't make a difference would you mind posting your hardware specifications here? It's always a possibility that your hardware might be causing problems.
