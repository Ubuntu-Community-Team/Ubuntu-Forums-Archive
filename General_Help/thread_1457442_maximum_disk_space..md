Do you know in ext4 what 1EB means? in Maximum volume size
The "E" means the exa prefix; that is 10^18.

Today's big disk drives hold about 1 terabyte; an exabyte would be one million of these.  It takes about 60 bits to specify a particular byte in an exabyte sized space.
