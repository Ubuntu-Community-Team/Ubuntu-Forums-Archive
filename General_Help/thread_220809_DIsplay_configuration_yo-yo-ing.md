Yeah your xorg calls out ATI-strange but you have the nvidia driver. Is it working? Well that's why your posting this. 
If I'm understanding this the problem is that you can't get the highest resolution that your monitor is capable of.
Perhaps the easiest way to try and correct this is to have all your video i.e monitor and video card info in front of you and then open a shell and type > sudo dpkg-reconfigure xserver-xorg
You could also try to edit your current xorg.conf but for me running the dpkg script is easier-at least it's easier to explain. Hope that works-let us know how it goes.
