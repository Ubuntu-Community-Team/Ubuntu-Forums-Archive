### Post by liljetw on 2007-11-15
I use a add-on extension called new mail notification. I tarred it to bz2, just untar it and open Thunderbird and go to tools>add-ons and select install. Select where it is saved and you are done. You can also open tools>add-ons and select preferences to have it there always or just for new mail.
