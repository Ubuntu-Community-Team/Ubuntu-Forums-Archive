### Post by TheFu on 2017-12-29
Standard network troubleshooting stuff.  [http://blog.jdpfu.com/2013/03/01/linux-troubleshooting-101-networking](http://blog.jdpfu.com/2013/03/01/linux-troubleshooting-101-networking)

Simplify the connection until the problem area is determined.

* are local transfers fast or not?
* are all websites slow or just some?
* are websites slow the first time or always? Slow for 2nd, 3rd, 4th visits in a few minutes?
* is DNS slow?
* did anything change on the network?
* did your ISP start/stop doing something?  When mine started capturing all DNS and redirecting them to their DNS servers (without my explicit permission), things got slower.
* Change the ethernet cables.
* Is the internet slow for all your devices or just this 1 PC?
* Change the switch port used.
* Is the speed impacted by rain?
* Did any gophers/chipmonks eat your cable connection? I've had this happen twice.
