### Post by anaconda on 2008-03-17
did you do it already.. 

it should be simpple enough to explain even without trying it with a liveCD

just right-click on the existing panel (a place where there arent any icons inl)
and then select "Add to panel" from the menu that appears..

then choose "Window list" and click on the add-button.

and you are done!

BTW.. having a liveCD is a good idea, if you need to troubleshoot something for them  in the future.

PS. I prefer to have the hide buttons in the ends of the panel, because if the panel is full it can be difficult to click on the panel.. and not any items in it
to get the hidebuttons just right-clik on the panel, select properties and choose the show hide buttons..
