### Post by upforthedownstroke on 2008-05-17
In the Screenlets Manager you can click "options" and check the box that says "widget". That will bind all screenlets of that type to the widget layer. To bind a single screenlet to the layer, right-click it, click "Properties" and in the option tab you will find another box for "Treat as Widget".
