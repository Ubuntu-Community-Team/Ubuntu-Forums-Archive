### Post by labinnsw on 2009-12-29
According to Linux Magazine issue # 93 pg 30, the top 2 data recovery tools are Foremost and Scalpel. I have never used either of them, and can therefore give no further testament to their effectiveness.

This is too late for you now, but in case others are reading, backing up is every bit as important as the most important thing you do on your computer. It cannot be over emphasized. It is the easiest, quickest, and cheapest means of recovering data. This is especially true when the hard disk fails.
