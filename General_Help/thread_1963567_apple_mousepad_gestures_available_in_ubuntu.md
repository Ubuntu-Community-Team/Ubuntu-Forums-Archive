### Post by gmcd on 2012-05-03
I'm pretty new to Ubuntu, but I think the app you're looking for is [TouchEgg]("http://code.google.com/p/touchegg/").

HowToGeek ran a nice article about it a little while back: [http://www.howtogeek.com/howto/43097/how-to-get-macbook-style-finger-gestures-on-ubuntu-linux/](http://www.howtogeek.com/howto/43097/how-to-get-macbook-style-finger-gestures-on-ubuntu-linux/)

Unfortunately, it appears that there is an unresolved bug at the moment w.r.t. to 12.04 (see [http://code.google.com/p/touchegg/issues/detail?id=148](http://code.google.com/p/touchegg/issues/detail?id=148)). I can't get more than two finger gestures to work at the moment myself... However, by the sound of things, they're hoping to have this resolved pretty soon.
