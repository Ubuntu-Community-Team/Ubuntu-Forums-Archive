### Post by defishguy on 2008-01-17
Most of the time, there are a series of folders inside the window when you click the icon.  One of them is probably labeled Music or something like that.  The easiest way to get music into the player is to drag an mp3 into the folder.  Your player doesn't support the ogg format, so if you've ripped CDs using the default settings then that explains why your player can't play them.  Another tip is that you need to "unmount" or eject your player before you unplug the usb cable.  Right clicking on the icon that popped up when you plugged it in and selecting eject (or unmount if that is what you see) needs to be done before removing the device.

Hope this helps but if  it doesn't let us know.
