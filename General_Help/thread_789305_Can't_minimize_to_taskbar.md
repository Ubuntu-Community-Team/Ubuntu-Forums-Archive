### Post by DemiAlbedo on 2008-05-10
Solution found!!!

Stupid me I closed an application that needs to be open in order for websites, applications, etc to go into the taskbar when you minimize. In order to fix I did the following.

1. Right click the mouse button on the taskbar panel
2. select "add applet to panel"
3. then select "taskbar" from the list of applications
