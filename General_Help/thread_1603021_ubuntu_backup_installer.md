### Post by Mark Phelps on 2010-10-22
A different option would be to make an image backup of your partition(s) such that you could restore them to their current state.

This is real easy to do with Clonezilla.  After you make and store the image, it's a simple matter to boot later from the Clonezilla liveCD and do a restore.
