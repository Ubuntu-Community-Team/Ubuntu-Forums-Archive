### Post by mcduck on 2009-02-18
Do you mean you wish to use spatial mode instead of browser? Good choice, I'd say. :D

Anyway, it's simple, just open Nautilus, go to Edit/Preferences and on the Behavior-tab disable "Always open in browser mode".
