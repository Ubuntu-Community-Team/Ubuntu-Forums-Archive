### Post by mr_luksom on 2011-02-13
You will need to disable the propreitry drivers, I have to same issue. For me, its a hardware issue (faulty cable I think), where the onboard graphics cannot read the edid from the monitor.

Settings > Administration > Additional Drivers and disable the nvidia drivers.

You should then have more resolutions to choose from.

### Post by mr_luksom on 2011-02-13
Also, once you have done that, resolution is changed in

System > Preferences > Monitors
