### Post by Browser_ice on 2008-10-11
As I am trying to figure out why SecondLife is running in one Ubuntu partition (office layered) and not in my personnal Ubuntu partition, I came across confusion about what driver I should use.

From this [link]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BinaryDriverHowto/ATI"), it says that my ATI 9600XT is supported in the [xorg radeon driver]("http://xorg.freedesktop.org/releases/X11R6.7.0/doc/radeon.4.html"). My personnal Ubuntu partition is using fglrx and its this one that SecondLife says its not supported. My motherboard is an [MSI-6712 KT4VL]("http://www.firingsquad.com/hw/127/MSI_KT4VL_MS-6712-020/") .

Until now, I did not encountered any problems with my personnal Ubuntu partition. Its the first time I get a software telling my graphic card is not supported when I am using that software in my Ubuntu office-layered  partition.

Last time I tried to change something, I had messed up my graphics and took a while to fix. So I rarely change anything now.
