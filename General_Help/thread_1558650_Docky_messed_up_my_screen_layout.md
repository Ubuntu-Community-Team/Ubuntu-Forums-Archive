### Post by CoolJohnB on 2010-08-22
I had the same problem and this has been mentioned a few times here, you just need to enable Composite.

To do this got to System>Appearences and click on the Visual Effects tab, then click "Normal" you will now have all your screen and Docky works just great!

Hope it works for you.
