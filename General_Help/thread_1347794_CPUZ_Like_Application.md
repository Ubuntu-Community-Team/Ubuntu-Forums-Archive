### Post by Flying caveman on 2009-12-06
The linuxinfo package may be what you want. 

"Displays system info, such as kernel revision, glibc version,
processor type and memory size, in general by parsing /proc/cpuinfo."

or  procinfo

"Procinfo-NG is a small program that gathers some system information from
diverse files under /proc and /sys and prints it to the screen.
Procinfo-NG is a complete rewrite of the old system monitoring app procinfo."


or you may just want to add the cpu scaling monitor to your panel?
