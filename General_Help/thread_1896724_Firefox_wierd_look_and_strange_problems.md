### Post by Frogs Hair on 2011-12-17
Open the home folder , use Ctrl + H  to show hidden folders  , delete the .mozilla folder , and reinstall . This will remove the old configuration . You will need to reinstall any add-ons and if you have a lot of bookmarks you may want to export them to a folder first .
