### Post by Holger_Gehrke on 2015-01-15
Looks like the title bar of this window is hidden by the upper border of the desktop. No problem. You can move the window: move the mouse pointer into this window,hold the Alt-key, hold the left mouse button, move the mouse down, release LMB and Alt key. The title bar an the button for closing the window should now be visible.
