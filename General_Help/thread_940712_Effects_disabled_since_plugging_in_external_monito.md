### Post by bobx11 on 2008-10-07
I couldn't understand how the Xorg log file thought I had a resolution so big, but sure enough, I checked the x11 conf file and there was a giant resolution - likely from my monitor being plugged.

I ran:
sudo dpkg-reconfigure -phigh xserver-xorg

And magically everything is good again.
