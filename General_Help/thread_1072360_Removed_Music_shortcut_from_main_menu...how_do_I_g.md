In the main menu under places I did something and removed the music shortcut...how do I get that back?  Is this a general procedure for editing what folders are there?

### Post by RD1 on 2009-02-17
Nautilus acts much like a web browser. All you need to do is add a "Bookmark".

Open the folder you want to add to "Places". (in your case, Music)

Click on the "Bookmarks" menu at top of window.

Click "Add Bookmark"

"Music" should now show in your "Places" menu. :D
