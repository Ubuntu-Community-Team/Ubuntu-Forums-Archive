### Post by crip720 on 2019-05-08
I use Lastpass myself, has extensions for most browsers and works well as a free password manager and also has a paid version, but free does most things you would want.  It has a decent help section and can generate passwords for you.  There are other password managers like keepass if you google password managers, but have no experience with them.

### Post by crip720 on 2019-05-09
If you are concerned with your passwords being in the cloud, I think keepass lets you keep them on a USB stick in your control.  There are dozens of password managers and hundreds of opinions of which is best.  A simple google search of ubuntu password manager will give you lots to read.  Just remember that most are very hard to get back into if you forget/lose the main password for them.
