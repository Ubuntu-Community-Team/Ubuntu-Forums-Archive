### Post by jeremyswalker on 2009-10-07
In Synaptic, toward the bottom left corner click 'Origin'. After, is there a selection above there that says local? If so, click it and see if you can find the flash you installed, and select it to be removed.

Then, select 'flashplugin-installer' to be installed, and click apply.

Simply installing the new flash may automatically uninstall the old one, as well. I am not sure though, beings it sounds as though you installed flash manually.
