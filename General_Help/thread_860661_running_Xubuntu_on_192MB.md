I have an old (circa 2000) compaq laptop with 192 meg of ram. Its a P3 750 and I have Xubuntu on it. Wouldn't call it a speed demon by any means but it does work well enough for me to use it online but wouldn't want to try and use GIMP and Open OFfice at the same time.
