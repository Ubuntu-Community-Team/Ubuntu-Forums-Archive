### Post by NeoLithium on 2007-01-15
On Startup, by the memory check, you should be able to press either ESC or Delete to get into BIOS settings, just check the powersave options in there.

### Post by NeoLithium on 2007-01-15
It actually varies between computers, it might be under a heading like POWER MANAGEMENT or POWERSAVE, something along those lines, you can just take a look around through the pages without having to worry about doing anything bad; I found it actually, by accident in my own bios.
