### Post by Ptero-4 on 2006-05-18
First. Get info about the brand of your mobo's on-board video to see if you need special drivers.
Or get yourself a video card with dedicated memory/CPU. It's the best way to speed up your graphics since the on-board video subsystems usually lack hardware acceleration at all.
