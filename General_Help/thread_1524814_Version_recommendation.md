It will not be slow, I am just saying that if you use Lubuntu 10.04 instead of Ubuntu 10.04, you will see that Lubuntu is faster. However, this does not mean that Ubuntu is slow. Think of it this way, you have a Corvette and a Lamborghini, both cars are fast but the Lamborghini is faster.

I currently run Ubuntu with my laptop. Here are my specs:
1.6 GHz Pentium M CPU
1024 MB of RAM
80 GB HDD
Intel integrated graphics

As you can see, my laptop has specs that are similar to yours. Here is my final suggestion:

If you want the fastest version available, choose Lubuntu. If you want a somewhat easier interface and some eyecandy, choose Ubuntu. You can't go wrong with either choice.
