### Post by rossjman1 on 2008-07-13
Go into Pidgen and go to Tools > Preferences. Show system tray icon (always). Then you can make Pidgen start when GNOME starts by going to System > Preferences > Sessions. I'm not sure if this is what you wanted or not...
If it still doesn't show then you don't have the notification area on your GNOME Panel. To do that: right click on the panel and click "add to panel". Highlight notification area and click add.
