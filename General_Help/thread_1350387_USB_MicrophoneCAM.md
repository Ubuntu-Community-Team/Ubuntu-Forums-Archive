### Post by krfulton on 2009-12-09
Here is the problem:
I am running Ubuntu 9.10, I have an Nvidia motherboard, and have connected a webcam, with a built in microphone to one of the USB ports.
Using Skype I can connect and see and send video, I get sound in, however I can’t send sound with that webcam microphone.
Anyone have a fix? Or know of some webcam software that works?

---

### Post by krfulton on 2009-12-14
Okay, it was meeee.
There is a configuration menu item, Preferences>Sound, when dialog box comes up you can choose your sound source, one of them is the USB mic on the HP webcam.

Problem solved. :D
