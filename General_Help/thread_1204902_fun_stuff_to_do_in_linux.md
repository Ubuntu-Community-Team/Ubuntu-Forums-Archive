### Post by dhughes on 2009-07-05
Using ffmpeg to strip audio from a Youtube video in mere seconds and then save it as a .wav file to use as a ring tone, is fun.

```
 ffmpeg -i /home/user/video.mp4 -vn -acodec pcm_s16le -ar 44100 -ac 2 soundfile.wav 
```

 Of course, change "user" to your account, video.mp4 to the video you want to convert and "soundfile" to the name you want.

 I used pwnyoutube to download the video first.
