### Post by jkxx on 2010-05-17
This may not be the answer you are looking for, but if you plan on having more than one program doing audio I/O, then you'll probably want to use JACK. Pulse is well... not very good at it, hence you'll be wasting your time trying to configure it (pulse) if you go that route.

See these links:

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=806730]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=806730")

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HowToJACKConfiguration]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HowToJACKConfiguration")

You'll likely have to do some extra fiddling with the apps to get them to use JACK as well but it will be worth it if you plan on doing anything half serious involving audio.

Good luck.
