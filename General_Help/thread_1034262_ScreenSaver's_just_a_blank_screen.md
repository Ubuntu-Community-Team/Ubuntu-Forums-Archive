### Post by EmanresuusernamE on 2009-01-19
I know it's a bit late, but I don't come here that often anymore.

System > Preferences > Power Management

Uncheck one of the bottom two options, raise the amount of time that: "Put display to sleep when inactive for:" or lower the amount of time before your screen saver kicks in.  They may be the reason you're screen is completely blanking out.

If it's running on battery, it's better to turn the screen off rather than run a program and eat up more power from the battery.
