### Post by jkugler on 2008-06-17
> **jtravnick said:**
> will Sweet Home 3D do building design? What I'm looking for is something that will give me a breakdown of materials needed when building something. Like say I'm doing a garage and I have my size down how many 2x4s I need now many roof trusses things like that. 


It will give you a list of things needed, such as toilets, sinks, doors, and other things you place, but won't do the calculation of 2x4's needed, etc. And right now, it's limited to "one floor" per plan, so if you did a two story house, you'd need to do two separate files.  It's been great for me figuring out wiring diagrams, spacing, etc.  But it's no Punch Home Design yet, if that's what you're looking for.
