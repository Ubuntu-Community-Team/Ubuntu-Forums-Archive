### Post by bra|10n on 2012-09-07
The two icons you see are part of the Quicklauncher plasma-applet. 
To remove it permanently go to SystemSettings > Startup & Shutdown > Session Management, and choose "Start with an empty session". 
Remove the applet from panel as you have been doing, and on a reboot it should no longer start.
