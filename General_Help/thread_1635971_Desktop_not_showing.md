### Post by Jahid65 on 2010-12-02
Did your right click option work? if not, then press alt+f2. Type "gconf-editor" in the text field & click run. A window will appear. Navigate to apps=>nautilus=>preference. There you can see "Show Desktop". If it is unchecked, then check this & close the editor.
