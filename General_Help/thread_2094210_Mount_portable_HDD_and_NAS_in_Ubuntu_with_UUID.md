### Post by apochry on 2012-12-13
Hello,

please be some more specific about your intentions. 

If you want to have the media attached only to your pc you can configure the mounts using [fstab]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Fstab#Mount_point"). I you are looking towards sharing over a local network, than you should probably use [Samba]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Samba#Samba_and_LDAP") to setup a sharing server. There is also a GUI for it, which make the setup very easy. Take a look in the software center.
