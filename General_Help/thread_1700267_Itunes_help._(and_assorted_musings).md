### Post by X-Legs on 2011-03-04
Unfortunately, iTunes doesn't play well with WINE (as shown [here]("http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=application&iId=1347")).

As for your other question, there is a program called remastersys that will create a system installer with all of your programs and files. If you have a large amount of files, it might be productive to transfer some to a USB drive first.
