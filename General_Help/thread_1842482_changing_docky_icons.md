### Post by Whoop365 on 2012-02-15
I have problems with this too. What I did, was replace the icon being used with what I wanted.

For example bring the program you want to change the icon for to top panel, right click, go to properties, click the icon to find it's current location.

Then rename the icon you want to the current icons name, and copy over it to replace it.

Then restart docky, or computer, and it should be changed (I think i actually had to re-add the icons to docky, because it changes the default icon and what not.

May not be the best way, but it worked for me.
