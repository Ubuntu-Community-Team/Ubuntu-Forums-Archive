### Post by 3rdalbum on 2012-03-04
Ubuntu comes with most of the drivers you need. A driver makes a piece of hardware work, it doesn't make things faster.

Usually the only additional driver available is for certain ATI or Nvidia graphics cards so you get 3d acceleration. If these are available they will be listed in the Additional Drivers program. If not listed then you are already using the best driver available.
