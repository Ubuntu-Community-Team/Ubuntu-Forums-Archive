### Post by y-lee on 2008-08-21
For firefox the backspace key acts  differently in Linux than on Windows or Mac. As you have probably noticed on ubuntu hitting backspace scrolls your current page up and  shift+backspace will scroll down. This behavior however can be changed.

Type **about:config** into Firefoxes address bar and search for **browser.backspace_action**. When you find it double click it and change the value from a 1 to a 0.
