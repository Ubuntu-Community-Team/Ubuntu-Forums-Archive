### Post by kidders on 2007-03-31
Hi there,

If I were going to do this, I would boot my PC with a CD and reorganise my disk partitions so they looked the way I wanted them to end up, deleting my old Linux install, and putting everything I wanted to keep in one place.

Since you have more than one hard disk, how to make the most efficient use of them both deserves a little thought, but other than that, there's nothing wildly complicated involved. Naturally, for best performace, you should try to make the fullest possible use of both hard disks, no matter which OS you're booted into.
