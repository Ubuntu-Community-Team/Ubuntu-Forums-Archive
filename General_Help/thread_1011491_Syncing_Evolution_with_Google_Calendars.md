### Post by GonZo on 2008-12-18
You can configure Google Calendar in Evolution through the special option in "New->Calendar->Type->google" but just the main calendar. All options avaliable (read, write, delete)

The other calendars can be avaliable in Evolution but just with read permissions. Follow this instruccions : [http://www.flickr.com/photos/12955574@N00/135314724/](http://www.flickr.com/photos/12955574@N00/135314724/)

I hope this may usefull for you.
