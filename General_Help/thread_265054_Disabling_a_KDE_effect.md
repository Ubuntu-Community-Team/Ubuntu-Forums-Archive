### Post by Rhapsody on 2006-09-25
I've gone on a recent spate of changing and disabling KDE appearence settings (in an effort to minimize clutter and memory usage) but found one effect that I can't find an option for. I've no idea what to call it, but it appears whenever I hover over a toolbar button, the Wastebin, K Menu, or some other things with a fancy effect, icon in it, and some text. I've no need for whatever it is and I want to turn it off, but I can't find out how!

---

### Post by aysiu on 2006-09-25
Press Alt-F2 and type ```
kcontrol
``` Then, turn off the Mouseover Effects.

### Post by Rhapsody on 2006-09-25
