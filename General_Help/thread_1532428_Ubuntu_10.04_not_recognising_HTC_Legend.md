> **clortomirta said:**

Not sure what you're trying to sync, but if you just want to connect your HTC legend as effectively a USB drive (e.g. to download photos), then connect with a micro USB cable and on your phone go to Settings > Connect to PC > Default Connection type.

Once there, select 'Disk Drive'
