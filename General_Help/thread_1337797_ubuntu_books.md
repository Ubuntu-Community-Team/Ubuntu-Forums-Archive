### Post by Digital Magi on 2009-11-25
If you are looking for good introductory books, here are a couple of my favorites.

**Moving to Ubuntu Linux** by Marcel Gagné, ISBN: 9780321427229. Covers installation, setup of email, office apps, etc and does a good job of understanding linux basics for new users migrating from another platform. I buy copies of this one to give away to new Ubuntu converts, can't recommend it highly enough.

**Moving to The Linux Business Desktop**, also by Marcel Gagné, ISBN: 9780131421929  Similar to the first one I listed, but focused on business applications, networking and server setups.

In both of them, the writing is friendly and easy to understand and the author takes time to explain linux concepts that might be confusing to new users or someone transitioning from a different platform.
