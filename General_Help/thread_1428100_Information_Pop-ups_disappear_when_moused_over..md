### Post by stovicek on 2010-03-27
> **PapaGeoff said:**
> Whenever one of these balloons appears and I try to mouse over it, it disappears.   As soon as the mouse is moved away from the space otherwise occupied by the balloon, it will reappear.  It is very frustrating.  Any ideas?
That's the way they're designed. They're not clickable if that's what you're hoping. They're meant to not interfere with whatever may be beneath them. The old notifications used to get in the way so the new ones were designed with that in mind.

[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/NotifyOSD#Interaction](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/NotifyOSD#Interaction)
