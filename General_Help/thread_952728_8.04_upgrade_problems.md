### Post by jimv on 2008-10-19
I think the whole concept of upgrading is terrible anyway...dropping in a new version of an OS while leaving all your old settings just doesn't seem like it's going to work very well.

What I do is install the new version onto a different partition, migrate all my setting and stuff over, and then when everything is the way I want it, I delete the old partition and resize the new one to fill up the space.

For this reason, I've found that while there are chances of major screw-ups, it's generally a safe thing to do.
