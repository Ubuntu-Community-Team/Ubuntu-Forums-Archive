### Post by susema on 2010-12-14
Open a folder (for example Home directory) and right click any folder, select **Open with Other Application**.

When the list opens, select **File Browser**. And select remember for folder.

### Post by coffeecat on 2010-12-14
Try this. Right-click on your desktop and choose 'Create Folder'. You don't have to name it anything special. Now right-click on the folder itself and follow susema's instructions (from 'Open with other application').

Once you've done that you can delete the desktop folder - it's served its purpose.
