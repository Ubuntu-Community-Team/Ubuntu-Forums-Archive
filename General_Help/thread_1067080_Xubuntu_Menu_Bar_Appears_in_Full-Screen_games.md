### Post by Elviswind on 2009-02-14
I have my top and bottom panels setup to hide automatically.  Go to Settings Manager, Panels, and select Autohide for both panels.  This should force them behind a fullscreen instance of Wine.  It's the only way that I've figure out how to make this work.
