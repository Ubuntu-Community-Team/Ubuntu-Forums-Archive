### Post by emperon on 2007-10-19
Hi I am using nvidia property drivers with my dual core laptop. I ocassionally have freezes and blinks to my desktop. Some times video playback becomes corrupted to. The only resolution I could come up was restarting X till now. 

I figured out a better solution today:  Applications -> System Tools -> NVIDIA Settings-> X Server Display Configuration.  Change the refresh rate to auto or a number like 60HZ. IF it is out then make it 60HZ (what ever your normal refresh rate is). IF it is already at 60HZ or any other number make it AUTO again. And click apply. 

This way glitches are gone and you buy some more time before restarting X. IT's not perfect but better than restarting X
