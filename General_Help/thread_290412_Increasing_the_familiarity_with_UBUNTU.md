### Post by Stochastic on 2006-11-06
I'd say keep sending them lessons in which ever way you can.  Personally, I try to help the spread of GNU/linux by helping even with the simplest howtos and walkthroughs.  After working in tech support call centres I've realized how much people can be afraid of new technologies - even windows technologies.  By walking your parents through making openoffice documents and such you may make Ubunut an indispensible tool for their everyday life (depending on what their daily life entails).

Just my 2cents.
