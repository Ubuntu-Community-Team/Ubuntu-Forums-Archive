### Post by Mr. C. on 2007-06-08
It is best to determine the drive mfg. and see what diagnostic utilities the mfg offers many will have DOS-based diagnostics that you can download and run. 

The smartmontools site provides some assistance in finding said tools:

[http://smartmontools.sourceforge.net/#FAQ](http://smartmontools.sourceforge.net/#FAQ)

(search for "Where can I find manufacturer-specific disk-testing utilities").

You can try smartmontools itself, but you will have to get it installed.

MrC
