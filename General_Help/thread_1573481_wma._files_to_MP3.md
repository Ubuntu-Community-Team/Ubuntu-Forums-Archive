### Post by Lars Noodén on 2011-03-17
Just a note on sound quality.

Going from one lossy codec to another is going to multiply the degradation of sound quality.  If you can, find the original files (not in WMA) and rip them directly to MP3.  Otherwise the sound is goind to suffer.  On cheap headphones it might not be as unbearable, but if you have large speakers that you are used to hearing good sound  from, the transcoded files will sound terrible.  

Lossless formats include raw data, FLAC and WAV.  Use those for your originals.
