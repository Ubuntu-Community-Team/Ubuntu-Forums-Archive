### Post by torfig on 2010-01-28
I had the same problem.

Here is what I did:

Changed back the visual affects back to none in Appearance Preferences.
Then I got back the title bars and could work in the terminal.

Then I followed the instruction on this page:
[http://www.pendrivelinux.com/ubuntu-desktop-effects-fixing-the-missing-titlebar/](http://www.pendrivelinux.com/ubuntu-desktop-effects-fixing-the-missing-titlebar/)


after that I enabled the Extra visual affects again and restarted the computer.
Now it is working.

Hope it helps.
