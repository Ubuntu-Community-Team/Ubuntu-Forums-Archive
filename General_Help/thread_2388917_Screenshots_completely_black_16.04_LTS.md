### Post by him610 on 2018-04-09
I just checked my only machine with Ubuntu 16.04. When the "Print Screen" key is pressed it activates "gnome-screenshot" displaying a preview of what is to be captured. In my case, it was the desktop. On my other machine running Xubuntu 16.04, pressing the "Print Screen" captures the whole screen - open windows, messages, status bar, everything.
There is probably some item mis-configured in your settings if you are seeing a black screen. You might want to review the settings for "gnome-screenshot", or whatever screen capture application you use.
