### Post by c0mput3r_n3rD on 2009-07-08
Ok, I got it!!!

First I cleared everything out of my /etc/NetworkManager/nm-system-settings.conf file.  Then;
i ran these commands:
```

/etc/init.d/etworking restart
pkill nm-applet
nm applet
lshw -C network
shutdown -r now

```I had my ethernet cable unplugged when the computer booted up, and when it was done starting I plugged the wire in and WALLAH! I'm now connected via auto eth0.
