### Post by papibe on 2013-05-09
Hi Rex Bouwense.

You don't need an account to chat in freenode. You choose a nickname, go there, join a channel  and chat.

Here's a simple [guide]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/XChatHowto") to get you started.

You can, of course, register so you can reserve your nickname for future chats. [Here]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/InternetRelayChat/Registration") is how.

Hope it helps. Let us know how it goes.
Regards.
