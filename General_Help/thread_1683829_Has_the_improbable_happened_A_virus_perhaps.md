### Post by Yougo on 2011-02-08
it sounds to me like like your graphics card is dying on you, or something else graphics hardware related. 
have you tried hooking up a different monitor? different cables (though that ususally results in r, g or b dropping out)?
have you tried booting in recovery/safe graphics mode? 

a live cd is a good way to check if it's software related indeed.

by the way, if your system does boot normally, only with no visuals to speak of, pushing the power button once should give you the shutdown dialog in GUI, just wait for the minute countdown and it should shut down nicely, or press spacebar, trusting the focus is on the right button.

when in CLI, the power button should work immediately. no need for hard resets :)
