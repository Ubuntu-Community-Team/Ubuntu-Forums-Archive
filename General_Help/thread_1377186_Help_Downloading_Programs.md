### Post by Maheriano on 2010-01-10
Ya dude, iTunes doesn't work in Ubuntu. In fact, any .exe file/program will not work in Ubuntu so basically any program you ever had before is no more.

Click Applications and then Ubuntu Software Centre, from there you can search for any program you want to install. Instead of having to go around the internet searching for programs to download, they're all in one easy to find place where you just click them to install. And most of your programs are here, just in an Ubuntu clone version:
Photoshop = Gimp
iTunes = Banshee
Firefox = Firefox
Office = Open Office
