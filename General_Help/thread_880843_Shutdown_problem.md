### Post by eximius1 on 2008-08-05
I had this problem on an old pc.

Go to System > Prefrences > Sessions > Untick the box next to Power Manager

Then hit Alt + F2 and type in "gnome-power-manager" (Without the "")

Then shutdown and restart the computer. It worked for me.
