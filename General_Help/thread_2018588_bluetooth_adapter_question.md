### Post by Paddy Landau on 2012-07-07
Bluetooth is a specific standard. In theory, *all* Bluetooth devices are compatible with each other.

In practice, of course, it's hard to say. If the specific model you order has a non-standard interface that requires (say) Windows, then you may have a problem.

My personal experience (which is limited) is that Bluetooth on Linux works flawlessly.
