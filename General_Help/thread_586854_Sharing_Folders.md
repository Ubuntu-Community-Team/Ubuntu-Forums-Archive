### Post by dayvy on 2007-10-22
This is the easiest way to connect to your windows machine:

-click "Places"
-click "Connect to server"
-click "Windows share" from the drop-down "Service Type" list
-fill in all the relevant information
-this will create an icon on your desktop and also in the "Places" menu
-when you click (or double-click) on one of these icons you will be asked for your password and then you'll be able to see your windows share
