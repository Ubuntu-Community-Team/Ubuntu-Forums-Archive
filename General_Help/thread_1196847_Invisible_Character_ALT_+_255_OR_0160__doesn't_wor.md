### Post by Bachstelze on 2009-06-25
It is not an "invisible character", it is a [non-breaking space]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Non-breaking_space").

In GTK+ apps, you can do it with Ctrl+Shift+[font="monospace"]U00A0[/font].
