### Post by ryanhaigh on 2008-05-27
You could try finding an app that will allow you to bind the right-click of your mouse to the alt-f1 key combination, btnx could be useful here. For this to work though you will need to delete the menu from your panel otherwise it will show up there instead of at the pointer location. I still don't know whether this will stop the regular right click menu from popping up and I don't know how you plan to replace the functionality that menu has either. Perhaps it may be better to bind to a different button on your mouse if you have one available.
