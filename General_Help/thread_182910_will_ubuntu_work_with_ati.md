### Post by mjziegle on 2006-05-27
Try out the Live CD and see what happens.  The dapper live CD even let's you install from it.

Most modern ATI cards are supported, nearly all the radeon series with either an ati proprietary driver or an open source one that's installed automatically.

ATI cards work great.  The above poster is a nay-sayer.  

The key is to correctly identify your card and then find the right driver.  There's lots of info in the wiki about ATI drivers.  You could also check to see if your laptop or a similar one is there.
