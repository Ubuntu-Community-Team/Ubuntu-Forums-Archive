### Post by earobinson on 2005-12-05
It could be a java thing, or it could be that the dev team just dident put it in there, there are far less programs in the offical ubuntu repos than the debian repos. This is due to a lot of factors, but they seem to have 90% of the popular ones. you could check if Azureus is in the back ports then add it that way, and that would let u update it like the rest of your system. IMO Azureus is a huge resource hog and not worth it, but yes it has the best features.
