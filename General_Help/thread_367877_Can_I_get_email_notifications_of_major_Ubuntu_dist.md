### Post by mcduck on 2007-02-22
New versions are released every 6 months, one in April and other in October. (6.06 was delayed by 2 months). The upgrade isn't handled automatically, as all normal versions are supported for 18 months so it wouldn't make any sense to force people to upgrade, even less so on server use.

So if you keep April and October in mind you'll always know when to expect new version to be released :)
