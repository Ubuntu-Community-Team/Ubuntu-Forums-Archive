### Post by toleolu on 2010-01-15
Hope this saves someone a lot of time, but if you're having any kind of audio problems, run this command. I spent about a week trying to figure out why my mic wasn't working, came across this command and when I ran it, the alsa mixer panel showed that the line in and mic volumes were muted, even though the desktop sliders showed they weren't. Adjusted the volumes from the panel and I was able to record.

So if you're havin any kinds of audio problems run that command, see if that shows any problems.
