### Post by gordintoronto on 2015-10-09
It's definitely a hardware issue. If you can shut down by holding the power button, and then boot up on the second try, it is probably a connection which doesn't work until it is warmed up. This is often memory, and sometimes all it takes is to remove the memory, then put it back.
