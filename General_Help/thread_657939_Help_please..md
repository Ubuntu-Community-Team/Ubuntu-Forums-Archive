Just because something is new does not mean it will work.  It could be defective.  Since you made a new copy on the cd, it would a defective cd, so check out icheyne's suggestion.  Instead of clicking Install/Start, go down the list to 'Check CD for defects' (I believe or something similar.)  If the cd is ok, then check that the wires are all tight and not loose.  If not, then likely you have a defective piece of hardware.
