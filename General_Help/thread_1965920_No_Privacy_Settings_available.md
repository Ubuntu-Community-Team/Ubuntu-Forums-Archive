### Post by mrspoke on 2012-04-27
Hi everybody!

The right answer is to install the meta-package "activity-log-manager-common" that include "activity-log-manager" and "activity-log-manager-control-center".
The last one put the privacy icon in the system settings center.

And do it with synaptic, it's easier :D
