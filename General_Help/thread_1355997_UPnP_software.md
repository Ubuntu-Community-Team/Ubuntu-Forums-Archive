### Post by Shhnap on 2009-12-15
I second fuppes. It's very nice and I have personal experience sending it to my xbox. It handled videos and music well (though if you are using .avi files be careful; you have to set the mime type in the fuppes.cfg to 'video/avi'. That's a microsoft stupidity.)

Just go here to learn how to setup fuppes: [http://fuppes.ulrich-voelkel.de/wiki/index.php?title=Main_Page](http://fuppes.ulrich-voelkel.de/wiki/index.php?title=Main_Page)

The fuppes wiki has most of the answers that you will need.
