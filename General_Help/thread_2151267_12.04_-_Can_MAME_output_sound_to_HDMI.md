### Post by MrBackhand on 2013-06-07
Temujin solved this for me in thread [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2147651](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2147651) by suggesting I do the commands below which I added to /etc/environment:


export SDL_AUDIODRIVER=alsa
export AUDIODEV=hw:1,3
