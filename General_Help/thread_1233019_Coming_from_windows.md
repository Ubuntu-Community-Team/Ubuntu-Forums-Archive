### Post by 3rdalbum on 2009-08-06
Why specifically OFFLINE dictionary?

Look in the repositories for "dict" and "dictd". Dictd is a dictionary server. Dict is a client - there are many GUI frontends for Dict in the repositories. That's the way I'd go. Those GUI frontends can also query other Dictd servers running online.
