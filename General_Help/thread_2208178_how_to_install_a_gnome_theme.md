### Post by deadflowr on 2014-02-26
Shell Extensions means nothing to the ability to change themes.

If you want to change to your new theme, then double click on the downloaded zip
(I presume it's a zip, or something like that)
Then when archive manager opens, hit extract, then in the main window,
right click and select show-hidden files and folders.

Now scroll down f you see an entry for .themes shows up.
If yes, click this one to open it up.
If no, go to the top right corner and select new folder.
(When making the new folder, name it .themes. Do Not Forget about the Dot.)

Now make sure you move into the .themes folder and then click Extract.

Once the files are extracted, go to the section in gnome-tweak-tool for theme.
Your theme should now be listed.

If you also, while your at it, want to see what extensions there are go here
[https://extensions.gnome.org/](https://extensions.gnome.org/)

Hope something here helps you.
