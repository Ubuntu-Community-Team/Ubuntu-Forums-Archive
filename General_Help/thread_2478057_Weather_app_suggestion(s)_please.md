### Post by ajgreeny on 2022-08-15
Which weather app do you mean?

I use the weather-update applet in Xubuntu's panel by installing **xfce4-weather-plugin** which uses Weather and astronomical data from The Norwegian Meteorological Institute, and I have found to be accurate enough for me.
This is, of course, usable only in Xubuntu.

I also use data from both the UK Met Office and the BBC, both of them  viewed in a web-browser, not a separate application.
