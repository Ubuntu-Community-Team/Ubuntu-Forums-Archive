### Post by wmcbrine on 2009-09-07
As it's trying to connect, Gnome's Network Manager applet shows a kind of blue flowing circle, with two fake green LEDs that light up as it connects. Often when it fails, I'll have the one in the lower left light up, but not the one in the upper right. Does anyone know what that means? I haven't been able to find an explanation.

---

### Post by steveneddy on 2009-09-07
The bottom one lights up to show there is a wireless connection - the second one lights to show there is a connection pending.

Finally the pretty blue bars showing a successful connection.

If there is a low signal or other communication error there will be a failed connection dialog box displayed.
