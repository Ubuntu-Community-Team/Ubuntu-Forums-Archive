### Post by 3rdalbum on 2011-11-24
The netbook editions of Ubuntu were the same as the ordinary ones, except for a space-optimised GUI.

They put the space-optimised GUI into the desktop version so they didn't need to have two different versions.

In other words, the regular Ubuntu 11.10 version is also appropriate for netbooks so just use that.

Don't use 10.10 netbook edition; it's really not very good, and it's a year behind the times.
