### Post by Cavsfan on 2011-01-16
> **mouser868 said:**
> What is the easiest way to have multiple wallpapers on the cube??

You need the wallpaper plugin. And in gconf-editor, you turn off show_desktop.
Via ALT+F2 and type in gconf-editor. apps > nautilus > preferences.

Once you have unchecked show_desktop, you add each one of your wallpapers in  CCSM wallpaper which will be under Utility.
