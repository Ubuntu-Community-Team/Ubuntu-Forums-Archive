### Post by An Sanct on 2011-02-13
Hmmm :)

This laptop has two graphic cards, that might confuse the system. Optimus, Nvidia's 2D/3D acceleration :)

Try to boot into safe mode x. You can do that, if you press 'e' during boot and select the "recovery" option, then from inside "failsafe X" enable the proprietary drivers or download the driver from here
> [http://www.nvidia.com/Download/index.aspx?lang=en-us](http://www.nvidia.com/Download/index.aspx?lang=en-us)
Find the Nvidia GeForce 310M and make sure, to select the right architecture (i386/64Bit)
