### Post by OrangeCrate on 2008-12-01
I found these instructions somewhere, and stored them in my journal. I haven't tried them (don't like desktop icons), but this may be what you're looking for...

> Right click where it says applications on your top panel, click "Edit Menu's" in the system tools section, click the checkbox next to configuration editor. Now click ok and get out of there, click applications go to system tools, and open the configuration editor. Now browse to /apps/nautilus/desktop, and click the checkbox next to what you want on your desktop, trash can, computer icon, home folder, network etc.

Hope they work for you.
