### Post by JCoster on 2009-06-01
Chances are it's related to your graphics drivers...  (or at least in my experience it usually is).

What card are you running?  nVidia?  AMD?  Intel?  Which model?  Have you got the latest drivers installed?  If so are they the Ubuntu restricted drivers or proprietary drivers installed from the manufacturer website?

Are desktop effects enabled?  If so, try to disable them and then see if you still get logged out; Compiz could easily be causing problems.

Need a bit more information...
