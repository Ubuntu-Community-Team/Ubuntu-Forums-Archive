### Post by rbmorse on 2008-04-24
Try this:  

System > preferences > appearance > fonts

Click on "sub-pixel smoothing"

click on "details"

click on "subpixels" in the smoothing box and

click on "slight" in the hinting box.

click on close, then click on close again. 

All better?
