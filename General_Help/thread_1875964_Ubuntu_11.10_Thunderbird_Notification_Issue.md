### Post by Jacob111 on 2011-11-10
Thanks, that was bugging me too.  Allow me to give specific instructions: From Gmail, click the gear on the way top right of the screen(took me forever to find) and select "mail settings".  Click the "labels" tab and on the right, uncheck "show in IMAP" for any folders you don't want to see in Thunderbird.

In our case, we unchecked the Important folder.
