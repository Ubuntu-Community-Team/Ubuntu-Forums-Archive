### Post by eternalnewbee on 2008-12-12
> in my preferences there is no File Management...
Right-click on your **Main Menu**, and click on **Edit Menus**.
Then scroll down to **System Preferences** and look for **File Management**. Select it, and you're done. Close the menu, And you'll find it under: **System > Preferences**.
