### Post by Wayne Twine on 2014-06-24
Hi 

I have to use MS Word in Ubuntu, running in Wine.  Word is in my Unity Launcher, but when I launch Word (or any other MS Office application), it appears as a new Wine icon in the launcher.  This is not a problem, except that it appears at the bottom of the launcher, which is a pain, because I have many apps in my launcher.  Is there a way to make the Wine icon appear higher up in the launcher, preferably before or after the Word icon?

### Post by deadflowr on 2014-06-24
What happens if you move it and mark it lock to launcher?
(right-click for the lock to launcher option)

### Post by LastDino on 2014-06-24
By moving it deadflower means grabbing it with mouse and while left mouse button is held on, dragging it to above that MS word icon of yours. 

Then lock it by right click on it> ''lock to launcher''
