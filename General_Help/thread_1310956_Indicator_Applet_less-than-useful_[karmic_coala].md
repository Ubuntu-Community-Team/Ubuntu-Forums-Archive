### Post by issih on 2009-11-02
Hmmn, probably not the ideal solution if you like the rest of the icons, and I'm sure someone else can advise you on how to change just that specific one, but as an easy work around you can just change the icon set.

Go into System->Preferences->Appearance.

Select the theme you are using (it will almost certainly be "Human")

then hit customise - navigate to the icons tab and select a new set, hit apply/ok until you are out and there you go.

You can find lots of different icon sets on websites like gnome look if the ones provided don't suit you

You may well also find that not many icon sets include an icon for the indicator applet, as it is fairly new, so you may not actually get a solution this way, but its worth a look

Hope that helps
