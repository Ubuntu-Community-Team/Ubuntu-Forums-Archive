### Post by iponeverything on 2010-07-30
You can start anywhere. Linux, Cisco, Microsoft. Networking concepts are easily transferable across platforms. 

Linux would probably be your best bet as a learning platform as you would have a great many advanced tools and opportunities to implement many infrastructure scenarios without the added cost of licenses or additional third party software. 

With both Cisco and Microsoft there artificial barriers and limitations to what you can accomplish without additional investment in software, licenses and hardware.
