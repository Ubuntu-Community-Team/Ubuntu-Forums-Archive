### Post by Ralph L on 2013-04-13
I have been doing my income tax using TurboTax-online with Linux/Firefox for some years.  Yesterday, I tired to log into turbotax to amend my return and found that tubotax wanted firefox updated.  I updated it to the latest version (20.0), but it didn't satisfy turbotax.  Apparently within the last month Turbotax did an update that inadvertently excluded linux firefox users that already had done their taxes on turbotax.  However, I found a way around that bug:

```
 1  Google TurboTax
 2  Select second item on left—Free federal tax filing
 2.1  Second method--select “Personal Taxes in top left item.
 3  Select “Start For Free” blue box.
 3.1  Second method—Select “Start for Free” under the Free Edition (left column)
 4  Select “I used TurboTax”
 5  Log in
 6  Accept the license agreement.
 7  When it says to check and update your browser, select “No Thanks, Continue Without Upgrading”
 8  And you are in!!!
```

Hope this helps somebody to make the April 15 deadline--without having to resort to Windows.
