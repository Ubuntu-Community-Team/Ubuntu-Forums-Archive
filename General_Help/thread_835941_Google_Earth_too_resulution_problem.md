### Post by AndThenWhat on 2008-06-22
If you want to uninstall Google Earth:

1. Open a Terminal: (ALT+F2 and enter "gnome-terminal")
2. In the Terminal type:
```
cd /opt/google-earth
```
3. Hit Enter
4. then type:
```
sh uninstall
```
5. Hit Enter, and Google Earth will be uninstalled.
