### Post by ellis rowell on 2008-01-25
Open Office v2.3 does not appear to understand pages written in Star Writer v7 and earlier, although OO v2.2 does. 

Going back to some previous years documents saved as .sdw I tried to load them into OO v2.3 to no avail. I then tried to load them into v2.2 on a Windows laptop and it worked. I then saved them out as .odt, then loaded them into v2.3 which (subject to minor adjustment, change from landscape to portrait) worked. So if you have been using Star Writer, make sure you keep it if you update to OO v2.3.
