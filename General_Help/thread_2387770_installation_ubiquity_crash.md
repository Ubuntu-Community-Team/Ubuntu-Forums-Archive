### Post by ajgreeny on 2018-03-23
Hi mocoloco-pinza, and welcome to the forums.

You may find that it's easier to install without bothering with the wifi connection.
make sure the "install updates" and "Install third party packages" are both unselected when you get to that stage in the install process and just keep clicking through the pages; I have never bothered to update or add those third party packages when installing but do what is needed after the OS is running properly.

If the machine is old, as  you say it is, I wonder if you might find it better to use another *ubuntu version with a lower resource-hungry desktop such as Xubuntu or Lubuntu.
