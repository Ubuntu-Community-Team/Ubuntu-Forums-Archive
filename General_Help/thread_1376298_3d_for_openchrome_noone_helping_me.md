### Post by warfacegod on 2010-01-09
Go to System> Administration> Hardware Drivers and make sure you have the recommended video drive activated.

Then Go to System> Preferences> Appearance> Visual Effects tab and enable advanced effects.


I agree with Leppie, Compiz migth be a good idea.

Go to System> Administration> Synaptic Package Manager and search ccsm and simple-ccsm and install both. They'll appear in your Preferences menu as Compizconfi..... and Simple Compizcon....... I suggest starting with simple. But remember, Compiz won't work if you can't get advanced effects working.
