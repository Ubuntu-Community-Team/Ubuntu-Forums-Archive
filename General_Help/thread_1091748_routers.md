### Post by fragos on 2009-03-09
Your modem must be registered, probably by MAC address to your ISP. You need their cooperation for that. Most routers connect via Ethernet to a modem. This you have more freedom with. To use a different router you may need to clone the MAC address of the router they supplied or if you're adding a router clone the MAC address of the Ethernet port on your PC that was used to connect to the router.
