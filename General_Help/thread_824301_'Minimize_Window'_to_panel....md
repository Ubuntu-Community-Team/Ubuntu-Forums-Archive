### Post by Cariboo1938 on 2008-06-10
Hello,
Recently I upgraded via alternate CD from Ubuntu 7.10-amd64 to 8.04-amd64.
I use gnome desktop. When minimizing a window I just work in, it does not show up in the panel as it did in 7.10. 
I could not find how to get this feature back and ask for a help/hint/idea, please.:confused:

Thanks in advance...

---

### Post by mcduck on 2008-06-10
Right-click in te panel, select "Add to Panel", find the Window List-applet and drag&drop it into your panel.
