### Post by ThePainter on 2005-09-03
Hi,
Ive got my printer working if anyones interested.
I have TurboPrint 1.84 with the reg key.
I also have 1.91 & 1.92 but no key.
I installed 1.92 and it worked but printed the Logo on each page as it does on the free version.
I then installed 1.84 over the top of it and registered it and it works fine now.

I presume that the newer version has some additional files that must be necessary with the newer kernels and they must remain when the older one is installed over the top which allows it to work.

So if your older TP drivers dont work on newer kernels try downloading the newer trial drivers from turboprint and try this method of install.

Good Luck,
