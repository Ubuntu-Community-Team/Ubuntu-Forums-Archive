### Post by The Thunder Chimp on 2010-08-21
If you really must you use the family pack with Ubuntu 9.04, why don't you choose "Try Ubuntu Without Any Changes To Your Computer", then from the desktop of the Live CD, you can choose "Install Ubuntu".

You can also try to "Check Disk for Errors"

You could also try to get a new version of (K)ubuntu, such as [Ubuntu 10.04 Lucid Lynx](http://www.ubuntu.com/desktop/get-ubuntu/download).

At last, you could check if you have the minimum system requirements (which you probably have since the computer is fairly new).
