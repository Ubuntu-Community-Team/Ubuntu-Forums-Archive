### Post by plucky on 2011-01-20
> **BNZBKK said:**
> When I click on 'Places' / 'Music' or 'Video' one specific song or movie always automatically starts up without giving me a chance to select which music or movie I want to hear/watch.

Open **Places > Computer** and select your **Username** at the top of the left column.

Then on the Music folder right click and select **Open with Other Application**.In the list that opens,select **File Browser** and tick the box that says "Remember this application for "Folder" files".

Good Luck
