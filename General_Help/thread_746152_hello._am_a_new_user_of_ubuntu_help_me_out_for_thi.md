### Post by LeoSolaris on 2008-04-05
What brand of sound card do you have?

You can get it through System->preferences->Hardware info, or you can go to your terminal and type in 
```
lspci
```
The second makes for an easier time copying it to the forum.

Try doing a forum search for your card, there might have been someone with that issue already, and you can google it, too. It seems that most of the real Linux whizs are not awake yet.

While people here are quite friendly and helpful, you should also try looking a bit before asking. If nothing else, it will cut down on the numbers of repeat questions. There are many excellent tutorials in the forum, and in Conical's Documentation.

Welcome to Ubuntu, friend! If a sound card is the only real problem you have on installation, count yourself lucky, I had sound, wireless, and ATI woes in my old laptop when I first started ;)
