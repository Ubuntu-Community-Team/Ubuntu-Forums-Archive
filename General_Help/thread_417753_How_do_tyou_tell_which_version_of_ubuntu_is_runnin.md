### Post by elephant007 on 2007-04-21
I had the same question as you, after poking around this is how I found out what version I was running (after an upgrade to Feisty, I wanted to make sure I was running Feisty)

on your menu click
SYSTEM>ABOUT UBUNTU

a few paragraphs down you should see "THANK YOU FOR YOUR INTEREST IN UBUNTU" after that will say your version.
