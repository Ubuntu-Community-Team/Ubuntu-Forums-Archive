### Post by motumboe on 2007-06-15
I'm looking for a good RAW image editor.
At the moment I've tried out ufraw, lightzone, raw studio, and Bibble.

**Bibble** is the most complete tool (lots of features) and it also can easily manage batch jobs. Its major drawback is that it is closed source.
**Ufraw** can be used as a GIMP plugin; its features are limited if compared to Bibble; if I'm right, no batch jobs.
**Lightzone** is closed source but its linux version is free. A bit better than ufraw, weird interface.
**Raw Studio** is not yet a mature project.

I still have to try out **Digikam** and **Raw therapee**.

In your opinion what is the best RAW image editor?
