### Post by Thelasko on 2009-02-12
C: is in your /home folder.  It's a hidden file, so you need to press ctrl+h to see it.  It should be in /home/*username*/.wine where *username* is your username.

P.S. there is a [whole section of the forums for wine related questions]("http://ubuntuforums.org/forumdisplay.php?f=313"), I suggest you look there first next time.
