### Post by xopher on 2007-01-31
Right click on the panel -> Add to panel -> Menu Bar.
This'll get your menu back.

Are you on a laptop? Since gnome-power-manager has this feature to automatically dim the screen, if not running on AC-power. Other than that, it could be some gamma/color settings.. But that's not likely, since you probably haven't played around with such.
