### Post by Dave54 on 2007-08-21
What way are you trying to minimize the window? Are you clicking the bar at the bottom of the screen, or are you clicking an icon at the top-right of the window?

do you have beryl installed?

If you are clicking an icon at the top-right of the window, there may be some confusion between the buttons. There are usually three buttons:
The first one is called "minimize". This makes the window collapse into the bar at the bottom of your screen.
The second one is called "restore". If you opened a window and it came up maximized, clicking "restore" will make it move a little, but stay the same size. To make it smaller, you then have to drag the window down by the title bar, and click and drag the edges of the window in.
The third button is called "close".
