### Post by happyhamster on 2008-03-11
I just found this:
[http://blog.verkoyen.eu/2007/10/08/howto-ubuntu-logitech-mx3200-cordless-laser-set/](http://blog.verkoyen.eu/2007/10/08/howto-ubuntu-logitech-mx3200-cordless-laser-set/)

So, try changing the mouse part of xorg.conf to look like this:
```

Section "InputDevice"
        Identifier      "Configured Mouse"
        Driver          "mouse"
        Option          "CorePointer"
        Option          "Device"        "/dev/input/mice"
        Option          "Protocol"      "ImPS/2"
        Option          "Buttons"          "7"
        Option          "ZAxisMapping"  "4 5"
        Option          "ButtonMapping"    "1 2 3 6 7 4 5 6"
#        Option          "Emulate3Buttons"       "true"
EndSection

```

Warning: don't directly copy&paste the text from the linked page, because it will likely cause problems because of the ” and “ characters it uses (instead of " and "). Good luck.
