### Post by scragar on 2008-10-18
Pulse audio is a planning for the future solution that is supposed to allow sound to work via remote logins(where currently it doesn't), so there is no harm in disabling pulse audio, as long as everything continues to work for you I wouldn't be worried(I would enable it again after every update it has though, just to see if that solves your problem, not a major deal though).
