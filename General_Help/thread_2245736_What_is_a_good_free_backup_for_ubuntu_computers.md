### Post by nerdtron on 2014-09-25
I think online backups (such as dropbox and copy.com) are good and enough for personal use if your data fits on their online capacity.
Pros:
You don't have to worry about redundancy or availability
Setup once and you're good.
Easy when you use multiple devices even when not at home
Cons:
bandwidth requirements
need online connectivity
limited storage

You can also setup your own separate backup storage, and setup RAID for redundancy.
Pros: 
Larger capacity
Fast access
Cons:
harder to setup
maybe costly as you monitor/replace your own hardware
data is only available on your local lan
