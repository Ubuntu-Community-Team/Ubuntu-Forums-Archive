With the recent price drops in Athlon64 CPU's, here is what I would suggest.

Athlon 64 3200
Nvidia Nforce4 motherboard. (Asus, Gigabyte or DFI)
Name brand value RAM - (Never use generic) pc3200 DDR 400
Western Digital Hard Drive (SATA 3.0) for both reliability and value.
Nvidia Geforce 6600 GT - (It is 128 but performs better than most of the 256 cards in it's class and is a great buy around $100 USD. It will do everything you just quoted with ease and it plays 3d games at great resolutions and framerates.)

Any cheap case will do but DO NOT go cheap on the power supply or you will be sorry.

You can build that set up for around $400 USD and it is completely Linux compatible. The performance will knock your socks off for a budget pc.
