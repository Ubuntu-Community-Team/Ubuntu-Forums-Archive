### Post by t0p on 2010-11-01
> **firefox599 said:**
> how do i format my hard drive i want to re-install from scratch im on ubuntu

Insert your Ubuntu installation CD, reboot, select the option to install Ubuntu to your hard drive.  You will be given a number of options, such as installing Ubuntu side-by-side with whatever OS is already on the computer or using the whole disk for your new installation.  Choose the option to use the whole disk.  The installer will use the whole disk for your new Ubuntu installation, effectively getting rid of whatever was on the disk in the first place.
