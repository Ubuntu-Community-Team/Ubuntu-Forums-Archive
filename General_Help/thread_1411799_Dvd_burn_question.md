### Post by mkarr on 2010-02-20
Don't know how much storage you have on your computer but you might think of using the program "Handbrake" to make a permanent mp4 copy to your hard drive. That way you can burn a dvd of the movie whenever needed because blank dvds are even less reliable than the prerecorded ones, especially the cheap ones. Mp4 copies don't take up a lot of space.
