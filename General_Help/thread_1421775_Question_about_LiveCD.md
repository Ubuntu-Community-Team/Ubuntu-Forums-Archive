### Post by snowpine on 2010-03-04
Does your computer have a swap partition on the hard drive? If so, the Ubuntu Live CD will use that, too. 

But otherwise, yup, everything is stored in RAM. You'll lose anything you install when you power down, as well as any documents you've created (unless you save them to your hard drive, a thumb drive, etc.).
