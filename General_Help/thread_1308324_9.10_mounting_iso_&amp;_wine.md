### Post by tcpa41 on 2009-11-01
I solved the problem by adding a uid switch to mount option so  that the files on the iso are mounted with permission to current user,nor root.

i did it like this and it worked: 
sudo mount -o loop,unhide,uid=1000 /path/to/iso /mount/path
