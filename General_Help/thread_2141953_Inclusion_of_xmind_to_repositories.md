### Post by tgalati4 on 2013-05-04
Send an email to the authors.  It would be their decision to package it for the Ubuntu repositories.  Although I see some problems: their license, the dependency on LAME for mp3 encoding, and reliance on GTK libraries which may be different than the default Ubuntu GTK stack.  So by providing a *.deb package from this link:  [http://www.xmind.net/download/linux/](http://www.xmind.net/download/linux/) probably makes more sense from a maintanence standpoint.  Otherwise they would have to reroll the package at every Ubuntu release.  And you know that happens quite frequently.
