### Post by insomnesiac on 2005-09-13
Playing Audio CDs in Beep Media Player:

Open Preferences (Ctrl + P)
Select "Plugins" on the left
Select "Audio CD Plugin"
Click "Preferences"
Find "Play Mode"
Select "Digital audio extraction"

Now you can simply "Add CD" in the playlist and it should work.
