### Post by jorok_tupur on 2013-01-27
According to [a thread on Ask Ubuntu]("http://askubuntu.com/questions/101790/disable-or-change-gnome-drum-beat-sound"), replacing /usr/share/sounds/ubuntu/stereo/system-ready.ogg will do the trick.
