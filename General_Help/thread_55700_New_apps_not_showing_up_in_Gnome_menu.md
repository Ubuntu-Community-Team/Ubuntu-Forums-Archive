### Post by dave9191 on 2005-08-09
Most apps installed from synaptic will add a menu entry in the debian menu rather then the gnome one. You can enable the debian menu (instructions are somewhere on the forums). You can also add the menu items yourself to the gnome menu. Right click in the place that you want to add it (at least I think thats how its done, I havent used gnome for a while :)) There is also a gnome menu editing program in the works on the forums.
