### Post by Dipper on 2011-07-08
I have Kaffeine 0.8.9 installed in Maverick.  Every time the update manager informs me of updates, it wants to update me to the latest version of Kaffeine, which I do not want.  Is there a way to exempt Kaffeine from being upgraded without having to manually check it off in the list every time I update?

---

### Post by bowens44 on 2011-07-08
If you have synaptic installed,run it,  search for  Kaffeine, select it by clicking on it, click on package (menu) and select 'Lock Version'
