### Post by fooman on 2009-01-08
with an older card like that,  you may want to turn off the visual effects....see how that helps.

go to system > preferences > appearance > visual effects tab...try setting to none.

---

### Post by ram2008 on 2009-01-09
****Hi Fooman,

Thanks a lot for your post.  You hit the nail on the head!  As soon as I turned the visual effects off and rebooted the system, everything was back to normal.  This forum has really been great in helping me navigate a few problems with Ubuntu.  Thanks again for your help.
