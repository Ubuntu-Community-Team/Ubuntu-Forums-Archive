### Post by uRock on 2011-05-14
Does anyone know how to turn off first auto capitalization? I am creating a long list of cisco commands that I will be copying and pasting and I can't have capitol letters in the commands.

Thanks,
uRock

---

### Post by squenson on 2011-05-14
Click on the menu Tools > Autocorrect Options, then on the tab Options, then uncheck "Capitalize first letter of every sentence".
