### Post by Sigma1 on 2011-11-06
Hello, try a mixture of xsane and tesseract-ocr. You'll find instructions for getting tesseract up and running here: [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1613501](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1613501) post #2. Good luck.
By the way, tesseract is easy to use from the command line, too, and this is in fact the only option for working on image files which you have not just scanned.
