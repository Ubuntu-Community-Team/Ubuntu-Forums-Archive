I am using Ubuntu 10.10, is it possible to update Ubuntu 10.10 with Ubuntu 10.04.3 LTC released this week?

---

### Post by Quackers on 2011-07-23
No, as 10.04.3 is an update to an older Ubuntu version than the one you are using.
If you want 10.04.3 you can install it over the top of your 10.10 installation, or even side by side with it if you want to. The first means that 10.10 will be gone and 10.04.3 will have replaced it.
