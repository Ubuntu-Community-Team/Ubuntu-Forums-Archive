### Post by 3rdalbum on 2010-06-10
IDE doesn't have a specific power plug, it just uses Molex.

Molex is used to power fans, lights, and other miscellaneous things you might install in your computer.

Your power supply usually comes with an adapter, that turns one molex into two SATA. It's odd that you don't have one already, I've got three or four lying around from various builds.
