### Post by freddiespagheti on 2010-05-26
That should definitely fix your low disk space issue. If it hasn't said anything about it since you reinstalled it then it's just fine. Also, if you are just browsing the web, etc. having low disk space isn't really an issue. It has the potential to be one if you begin downloading other programs, games, or movies, etc. but it sounds like you won't be doing that. Nevertheless, it is always best to remain on the safe side and I would say definitely a safe bet.

Also, I really wouldn't be worried about viruses in Ubuntu, or almost any Linux distro for that matter. To be safe though, just be sure you only get your programs from the 'Ubuntu Software Center'. Don't download them directly from your web browser (e.g. firefox or chrome).
