> Hello,

Perhaps [this tutorial]("http://www.chipbennett.net/wordpress/index.php/2008/05/synchronizing-a-blackberry-in-linux/") will help?

Note: I wrote that tutorial based on Kubuntu Hardy, synching my BB with KDE-PIM. All the necessary packages are applicable to Ubuntu Hardy and for synching a BB with Evolution.

The one change you will need to make is to replace all instances of KDE-PIM with Evolution (including installing the "opensync-plugin-evolution" package rather than the "opensync-plugin-kdepim" package, and replacing the sync group member "sync-kdepim" with "sync-evo2").

Please let me know if this helps!
