### Post by higashi on 2009-02-18
> **WaffleCode said:**
> Why can't you use a painting program?

a number of reasons:

1: Its just not as fun.

2: when using a messenger-like interface, after pressing enter, or sending, it then goes to the top section, where all your drawings, etc, are temporarily stored. This way, you can easily scroll up and down to look at ur previous work. In a paint program, you'd hafto add space to the canvas, and once the canvas gets too big, it starts to lag, etc etc.

3: There's just something about paint programs (probably all the tools and the UI itself) that just make you want to go into detail. Which kinda defeats the purpose of brainstorming.

4: if you were to make a bunch of diffrent sketches..
on a paint program: you'd hafto save, open new, etc  each time.
on my idea for a program: you just press enter, then start drawing your next idea

5: adding text..
on a paint program: you hafto select the text tool and choose where to write on your sketch
on my idea for a program: you just press enter to make ur sketch go to the top section, and then type whatever notes you want (or there can be a caption feature or something)
