### Post by zapcojake on 2007-04-22
Hello, I would just like to comment on the improvements in Ubuntu from Breezy (the first release I used) to Feisty).  ATI is finally easy to setup.  It used to take about 2 hours to get ATI and Beryl working.  It can be done in about 10 minutes now.  It used to take about an hour to recompile a kernel.  Low latency kernels are in the repos now.  Upstart works great.  I have a Sempron 2800+ in my Linux box and a 1.8 ghz g5.  The Linux box boots as fast as the Mac with out using Bum or sysv-rc-conf.  The whole desktop is faster and apps launch much more quickly.  The contrast between Edgy and Feisty is amazing, Well done everybody!
