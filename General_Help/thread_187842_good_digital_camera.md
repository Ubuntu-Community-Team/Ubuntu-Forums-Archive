### Post by zxee on 2006-06-03
If you're going to have to put a pci card in to get usb I would check out online suppliers that sometimes mention linux compatibility (computergeeks.com or mwave.com) and the hardware database at linuxquestions.org although most recent pci cards should work fine. I have a sony DSC series and if your grandparents eyes are anything like mine ( I'm approaching 60 ) then they'll apreciate the larger lcd screens that come with that series. Good luck!
