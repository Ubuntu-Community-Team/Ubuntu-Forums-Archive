### Post by bionnaki on 2006-03-24
sudo apt-get install crip

the guy who wrote the app wrote it specifically for ripping his classical music collection: [http://bach.dynet.com/crip/](http://bach.dynet.com/crip/)

it's a commandline app - just run crip from the terminal - pretty easy to configure. I emailed the guy & he responded quickly to my questions.

I rip to flac with crip - works great.
