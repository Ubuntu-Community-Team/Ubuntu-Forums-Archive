### Post by HunterK on 2008-04-27
Man what an idiot I am.  Somehow "Snapping Windows" and "Wobbly Windows" were both enabled.  The widows were sticking to the screen edge as well as other open windows.  This made it seem as though the mouse was freezing when I was moving the windows.  

GAH!!!!!!!!!
