### Post by marin123 on 2012-01-11
The phone wants to install PC Companion, you don't want to do that.

Go to Settings -> Sony Ericsson -> Connectivity.

Uncheck Install PC Companion.

USB connection mode: Mass storage

Check Auto Connect in MSC.

Connect your phone to the PC, wait a few seconds and you should be able to explore the contents of your phone and SD card.
