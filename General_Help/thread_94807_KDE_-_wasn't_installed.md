### Post by aysiu on 2005-11-26
The KDE package in the repositories isn't really KDE--it's a metapackage. Here's the description from Synaptic: > A metapackage containing dependencies for the official suite of KDE including arts, kdeaddons, kdeadmin, kdeartwork, kdebase, kdeedu, kdegames, kdegraphics, kdelibs, kdemultimedia, kdenetwork, kdepim, kdetoys, kdeutils, and kdewebdev, kdesdk and kdevelop3. A metapackage is like a pointer--it tells Synaptic (or Adept) to install a bunch of packages.
