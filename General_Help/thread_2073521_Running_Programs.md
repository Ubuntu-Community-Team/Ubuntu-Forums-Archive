### Post by CryptAck on 2012-10-19
> **rancid98 said:**
> Thanks.  Now I feel dumb.:(

No problem, and don't feel dumb, all environments have a learning curve! Hopefully once you get used to using the launcher you'll notice a speed increase over opening Software Center each time. 

Also, if you notice at the bottom of the dash there are some icons. By clicking through those, you can access different "views" of the data/programs on your computer. The second one from the left is your applications, and when you select that one you'll notice that the right panel becomes a "filter". From there you can drill down into your applications in the event that you aren't certain of the name.

Typically it's faster to access the application by name (in the search), but you can use that if you don't recall the name. 

I hope this helps!
