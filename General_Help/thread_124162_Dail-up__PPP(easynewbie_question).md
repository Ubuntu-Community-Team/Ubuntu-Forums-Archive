### Post by towsonu2003 on 2006-01-31
well, it's not really an easy question...

first, there should be a "autodetect modem" under kppp. if it does not work:

you most probably have a winmodem... check out the second link in my signature on how to configure it. basically, refer to the wiki page, start with the scanmodem tool, read modemdata.txt, and refer back to the wiki while praying really hard... you might wanna use wvdial (end of the wiki link in my signature) to dial with a winmodem if you manage to configure it...

in case it's a winmodem, you will need a lot of patience, another computer with net access (or dual boot), and free time to configure (find drivers for) it.
