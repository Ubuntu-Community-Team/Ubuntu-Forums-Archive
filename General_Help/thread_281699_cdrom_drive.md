### Post by DoctorMO on 2006-10-23
hmm, this sounds like either two problems.

1) The CD-ROM drive is physicaly damaged in some way or has dirt on the laser.

2) The CD-ROM disc is physicaly damaged in some way.

3) The CD-ROM disc is burned incorrectly with errors in the software.

Have you had any problems with the CD rom drive before? have you tried re-burning the cd-rom disc? have you run the cd disc on another machine without fault?
