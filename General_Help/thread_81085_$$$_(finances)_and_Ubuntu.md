### Post by kperkins on 2005-11-17
I posted this in the other thread, but I'll post here also:
Here's how:
Edit > Preferences > Resources > Currencies > Click on add and add your Currency from the list tree there. (You can add more than one currency if you want.)
Then in the same window go to Display > Totals Currencies and change all the boxes to the currency you want to use. Close it and you're all set.
