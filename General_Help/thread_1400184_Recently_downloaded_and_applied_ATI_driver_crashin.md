### Post by rcayea on 2010-02-06
It sounds like you would benefit from a livecd. Boot a livevcd and see if you can mount your hard drive and possibly remove the driver that way. Search for ati. I would just go to a terminal after you have mounted the hd and type, "locate ati" or "locate radeon" Personally, I would then delete all of it after that because you could always boot into ubuntu with minimum graphics.
