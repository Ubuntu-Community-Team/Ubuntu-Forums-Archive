### Post by Jeff Johnston on 2009-07-27
I saw your post and thought that you might be interested in the keyboard layout that I designed. I call it the Shift layout. It is designed in the same spirit as the Colemak, but is much easier to adopt.

What I learned from trying Dvorak and Colemak is all that really matters is that the most used keys are on the home row. With that in mind there is little, if any, benefit in moving so many keys around. In fact the Shift layout has the most common keyboard shortcuts left in place.
