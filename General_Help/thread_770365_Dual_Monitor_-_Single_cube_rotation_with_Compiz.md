### Post by redjess on 2010-09-13
I know this thread has probably been forgotten about but I found it earlier when I was having similar problems so I thought I would post what I had found...

Firstly I couldn't even get Compiz to work but then I realised my advanced desktop setting had been turned off, turned them back on, rotating lovely now!
but to keep one thing open and showing on one monitor while zooming around the other there is an easy cheat. Right-click on the panel icon of the window you want to keep displayed and select "always on workspace". 

Voila!
