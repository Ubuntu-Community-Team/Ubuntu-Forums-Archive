OK, I think I <might> have a handle on it. You'll notice this post looks fine. Unlike the previous. Both written here, not in OOo. But this time no asterisks were added. See below, if you can bear the tortured explanation.

I had a few issues which may  be related.

-On forum posts space was replaced by asterisk.
-In OOo Writer, keyboard navigation was erratic ( Ctrl+arrow wasn't jumping one word at a time).
-Random line breaks in Writer.
-In forum posts end of line was never being reached, Lines were running on for ever across screen requiring a <Return key entry.


I remembered that the other day I was tidying up my menus and in Preferences was something called SCIM Input Method Setup. I didn't know what it was so I hid it. i didn't do anything to it, since I didn't understand it! 

At some later point I was writing a new doc in Writer. I noticed Autocorrect wasn't working. The default language was English (Eire). BUT I remembered (before I reinstalled Intrepid on a new laptop) that apparently Hiberno (Irish) English is screwed when it comes to auto-correct, there are no dictionaries setup.
So most of you may know Autocorrect implementation is quite different in OOo compared to Word ("sucks" would be term I and many others would use), requiring changing more than one option. You have to use English (UK) or (US) really for Autocorrect, and at least the Brits know how to spell colour & grey :-) so I switched to that. I also set it as default for new docs. 
Meanwhile my input location is still Eire. However my keyboard is a Dell Multimedia Keyboard which should be Irish (but which I suspect is somehow a US one, so some keys are reversed, " and @ to name a few.)
You'll notice by now your head is hurting trying to link link these together. Me too!
Also you'll notice, I don't mention CHANGING anything to cause my problems. Yes, that's true. I didn't. So that's still confusing.

Anyway, I put SCIM back. And had a look at it.
I changed the Input method to UK English. (from English US).
AND changed the hotkey from Control>+Space> to Alt>+Grave>.
That might be the root cause as I had Control>+Space set as my hot keys for Gnome-Do. So, that's where I am now. Everything seems copacetic now, in that I can get back to rewriting my CV for for the job application. (except the default keyboard layout is now incorrect).
Think of this as a long note to myself in case of future problems. I would have used tags for the post as I usually do but I was getting a weird "tag can't contain censored words" message, tags were "space, asterisk, compose, writer".

All in all, a pain in the hole of a day and still no closer to finding a job.
