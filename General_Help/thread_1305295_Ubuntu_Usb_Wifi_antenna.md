### Post by Giblet5 on 2009-10-29
There are lots of USB WiFi adapters. I imagine that they all work fine with Ubuntu.

I like anything that uses Marvell or Intel WiFi chips - they just seem to consistently provide a bit more range - but your mileage will vary.

An antenna is a hardware part of any WiFi adapter - pretty much just a tiny piece of wire in a bulky plastic gob. The antenna would have nothing to do with Windows or Ubuntu cuz, again, it's just a piece of wire.
