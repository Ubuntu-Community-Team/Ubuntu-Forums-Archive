### Post by Robin T Cox on 2008-07-20
Before you upgrade, be aware that for many posters on this forum 8.04 freezes randomly. The cause is uncertain, but seems to be related to the 2.6.24 kernel. See:

[http://lwn.net/Articles/287829/]("http://lwn.net/Articles/287829/")

From my own experience, using a system with 3 hard disks, on which I have 7.10 running sweetly on one, but 8.04 regularly freezing on the other, I can personally testify to this.
