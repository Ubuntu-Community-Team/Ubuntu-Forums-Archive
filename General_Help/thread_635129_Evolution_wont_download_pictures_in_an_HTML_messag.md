### Post by earlra on 2007-12-29
I am using Evolution 2.12.1.  In this version, go to Edit > Preferences, then select Email Preferences, and then click the HTML Mail tab.  Once there you have three options to choose from: Never load images, load images from contacts, or always load images.

Also, as a keyboard shortcut for doing it manually, just us Ctrl-I.
