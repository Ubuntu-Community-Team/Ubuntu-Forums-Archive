### Post by snowpine on 2008-10-14
Xubuntu is slow because your processor is slow (equivalent to 500mhz Pentium 2). Windows XP is 8 years old and requires a minimum of 64mb of ram, so if you are looking for something "faster than XP" your choices are DSL, Puppy, SliTaz, or possibly a minimal install of Arch or Debian Etch. Xubuntu 8.04 or 8.10 with Compiz will not compare to XP in the speed department on older hardware.
