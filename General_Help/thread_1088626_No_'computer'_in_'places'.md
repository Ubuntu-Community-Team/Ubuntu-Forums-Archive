### Post by tyk on 2009-03-06
AFAIK there has never been 'Computer' in "places in Xubuntu. If by computer you mean the root system, then go to 'File System'. If you want to see mounted drives, they are in /media/. In Thunar you can drop the mounted folders onto the left side to make shortcuts.
Cheers.
