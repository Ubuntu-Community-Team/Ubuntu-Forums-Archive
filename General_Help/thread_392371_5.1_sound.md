### Post by Cappy on 2007-03-24
In Gnome, on the _Volume Control_, you can goto Edit-->Preferences and you should be able to  select some controls such as "Surround" "Center" "Front" "LFE" "Side". Then you can raise those control's volumes. It might be easier to do it in **alsamixer**.
