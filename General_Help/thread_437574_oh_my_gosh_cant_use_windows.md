### Post by shae on 2007-05-09
Did you use the Live CD?  If you did, you may have to change the boot order in BIOS if you had to change it to get it to work.  You also have to remove the live CD to boot into windows.

If you installed Ubuntu, did you correctly partition the hard drive?  If you did not, you may have accidentally deleted windows.  Then you will have to reinstall it from the disks that came with your computer and with the key found on the COA on your computer.
