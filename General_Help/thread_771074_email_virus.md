### Post by catalina on 2008-04-27
Hi there,

Virus's will pass through a linux machine but not necessarily be affected.  The primary purpose of anti-virus in linux is to keep the virus from circulating to other machines.

There have been documented occurrences of virus's attacking windows based programs installed in wine on a linux box, however, it will not affect your entire system, just the native wine files.
