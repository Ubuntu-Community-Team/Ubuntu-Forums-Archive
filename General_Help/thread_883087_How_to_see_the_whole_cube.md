### Post by finer recliner on 2008-08-07
do ctrl+alt+[click and hold left mouse button]

now move your mouse around and you should be able to see all parts of the cube.

### Post by issih on 2008-08-07
Don't worry about extra toggling on and off, extra is just a predefined set of ccsm options.By choosing to use the cube instead of the wall you are no longer using that set of predefined plugins so the toggle switches off, you are almost certainly still running compiz unless the radio button next to "none" is selected. 

To see the whole cube and play about with it hit ctrl+alt+left mouse button, then move the mouse about.

Hope that helps

### Post by steveneddy on 2008-08-07
In the desktop cube plugin, at the bottom there is a setting for "Zoom" that will pull the cube out a little farther when you use the mouse to spin the cube.

### Post by Mgiacchetti on 2008-08-08
if you have a third mouse button (middle scroll wheel)  middle click and hold on an empty area of your workspace.  This should zoom out the cube so you can move the mouse and select the workspace you wish.
