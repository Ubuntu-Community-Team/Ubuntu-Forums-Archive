### Post by jondonym on 2010-04-08
See JonDonym help for using proxychains at [http://anonymous-proxy-servers.net/en/help/proxifier.html](http://anonymous-proxy-servers.net/en/help/proxifier.html)  You may use HTTP proxy for JonDo, not SOCKS proxy like Tor. For most applications, you have to use premium services of JonDonym, because free services can only connect to destination ports 80 and 443.
