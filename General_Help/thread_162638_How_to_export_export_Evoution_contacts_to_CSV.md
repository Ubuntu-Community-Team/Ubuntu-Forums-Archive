### Post by Zimmer on 2006-04-19
Open the OpenOffice database app and use the Wizard :Address Data Source.

This will create a datasource of your personal address book.
You can now use Calc to access the address source and create a CSV file.
Open Calc.
View>Datasources
and select  Adresses>Tables>Personal  
and the data will appear at the top of the spreadsheet.
select all data (click in Left hand corner of data table)
and then click the  Data to Text   icon (probably to the right of the filter icons)
to populate the spreadsheet. Press F4 to close the datasource.

(I had thought of just trying to access the address.db file, but stumbled on this method whilst trying. You learn something new every day, eh?)
