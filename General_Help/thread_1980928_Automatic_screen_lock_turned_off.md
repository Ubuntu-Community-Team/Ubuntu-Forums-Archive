### Post by Deepak J on 2012-05-16
In the system settings there is a tab called brightness and lock and in that you can adjust the time for the screen to turn off. Reduce it to either 1min or 30sec.

Also about the sudden locking don't you worry. Its not a problem. Its like that even on my system.

Do let me know if the above advice doesn't help you.
