### Post by Jerry N on 2011-03-05
The HP disk is not going to work in Linux.  The drivers on the disk are for Windows and possibly Macintosh.  Most HP printers work fine in Linux, in fact HP is the most "Linux Friendly" of the printer manufacturers.

I know nothing about printing from a command line in Linux.

Jerry

### Post by jbiggs12 on 2011-03-06
The drivers given to you on that disk are worthless, usually (especially if it's an HP printer) you can plug it straight into your computer and set it up through System->Administration->Printing, and it'll automatically select & install the necessary drivers for you.
