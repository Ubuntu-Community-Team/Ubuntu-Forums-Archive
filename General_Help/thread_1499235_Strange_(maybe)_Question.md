### Post by richardjh on 2010-06-01
Right click on the panel and choose "Add to Panel..."

From the menu select the "Window List".

You should be able to see if it is on the panel already as it has a small button at the leftmost edge (normally next to the "Show Desktop Button"). Right clicking on the little button as selecting "About" should show that it is "Window List".

I am sure the Window List has either been removed or moved.

Once you get the Window List back, you can lock it to the panel by right clicking on the little button on the left edge and ticking "Lock to Panel".
