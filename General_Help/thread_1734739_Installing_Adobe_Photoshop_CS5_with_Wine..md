### Post by ragtag on 2011-04-20
First, instead of torrenting Photoshop, why not look at GIMP, MyPaint, Krita or InkScape to do your graphics with. All are available in the Ubuntu Software Center at the click of a button. I use GIMP and InkScape a lot, an am pretty happy with them.

That said, see [http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=version&iId=20158](http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=version&iId=20158) for details on running Photoshop. Generally, it doesn't run that well under Wine. You should also note that Adobe recently announced that you can now rent Photoshop on a monthly basis, if you don't want to spend all the $$$ it costs to buy it up front.
