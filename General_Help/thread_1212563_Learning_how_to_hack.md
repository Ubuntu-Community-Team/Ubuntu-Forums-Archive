### Post by t4thfavor on 2009-07-13
Hacking is a pretty broad sword. Pick a subject within the spectrum, and get some knowledge of programming. All hackers are programmers, there is no way you can evaluate the security of an OS, application, or website without a knowledge of what it takes to build such a "device". I suggest that if you are not a programmer already that you start there, and get a knowledge of how stuff works under the hood.

You will not get anywhere by running scripts that other real hackers have written, so things like metasploit should be avoided at all costs.
