### Post by Dennis N on 2012-01-24
Go to [https://read.amazon.com/](https://read.amazon.com/)
Click on "Sign In or Create New Account" 

If you have an account, the cloud reader is opened in Firefox to your last read book and page, or the library page of your books, whichever you last exited from. Otherwise create a new account.

Difference from Chromium or Chrome version: in those, the Cloud Reader is one of the apps on the new tab page. No need to go to the website to start.
