### Post by ChasHutch on 2010-01-14
I know this reply is a bit late but, I thought I would respond for others that may be come across this in a search.

I googled "WYSIWYG blogging client for Ubuntu" and found this [excellent post]("http://bigbrovar.wordpress.com/2009/09/06/the-ultimate-blogging-client-for-linux/").

I installed Bilbo on my EeePC with Ubuntu Netbook Remix.  While it is for Kbuntu, it installed fine and runs great.  This can also be installed from the Ubuntu Software Center under System on UNR and the icon will be installed for you under the Internet tab.

I have been blogging with Windows Live Writer and was hoping to find something comparable for Linux.  I think Bilbo may be it as it allows you to blog  off-line and post to your saved and configured blogs when you are online.

Hope this helps,
Hutch
