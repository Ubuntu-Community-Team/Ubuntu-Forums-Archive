### Post by kurt18947 on 2015-12-01
Pop3 or IMAP? I have one of each account. I don't see anything archive related on the IMAP account. I don't have anything in the archives of the Pop 3 account. I suspect however that archives would be found at /home/.thunderbird/account(random_characters).default/mail/pop.isp.(net)(com)/archives.sbd. There is quite a useful importexport addon for Thunderbird. It will export the entire profile or just messages.
