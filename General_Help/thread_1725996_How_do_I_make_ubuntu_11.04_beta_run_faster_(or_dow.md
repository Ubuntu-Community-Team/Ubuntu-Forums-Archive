### Post by lithopsian on 2011-04-10
Most of the performance hit is probably due to the resource-intensive Unity desktop.  It needs more memory and a certain level of graphics card before it will perform well.

You can easily replace Unity, either with the Metacity you are used to, or something even lighter, and performance should then be very similar to 10.10.
