They won't mention Linux support because the software packaged with their products won't run on Linux. Their products have been well supported by the software packages available for Linux aand the drivers included wwith the Linux kernel. Pick a model you're interested in and then do a search in these forums for that model name to see posts relating to that model. I use their PCI based products and have no direct experience wwith their USB based products. I would think your PC would need USB 2.0 ports to properly support TV over USB.
