### Post by claracc on 2013-05-01
In the ppa page in launchpad, [https://launchpad.net/~linphone/+archive/release](https://launchpad.net/~linphone/+archive/release), there is a part in the upper left side which says "Adding this ppa to your system" (see attached image), below there is a link saying "read abot installing" with step by step instructions on how to add this ppa to your system, you only click on it.
