### Post by HermanAB on 2019-01-14
What is your threat and risk assessment - how paranoid are you?

If you are paranoid, then set up an OpenBSD server.  It comes with a mail server and installation of OpenBSD takes about a minute, so there isn't any easier way.

Here is a book for you: [https://nostarch.com/obenbsd2e](https://nostarch.com/obenbsd2e)  Absolute OpenBSD - UNIX for the Practically Paranoid.

If you are not paranoid, then why bother with your own mail server?
