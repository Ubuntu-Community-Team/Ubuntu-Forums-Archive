### Post by BQAggie2006 on 2009-11-14
Is the ISO around 750 MB (CD ISO) or a couple of GB (DVD ISO)? Did you follow the download path on the Ubuntu page or did you find the ISO in a mirror? If you are downloading it from the Ubuntu page, did you follow the default selections, or did you click "Alternative download options" then "DVD images..."? If you are downloading it from the Ubuntu page and you selected the defaults, more than likely, you are download the CD ISO.

Though the CD and DVD ISOs don't really have different "formats" because they both are data disc images. The only thing you have to be careful of is to not burn the DVD ISO to a CD because it won't fit. If all you have are DVDs, then you should be good burning the CD ISO to a DVD, besides the fact you'll waste close to 4 GB of space.
