### Post by Thirsteh on 2006-01-12
It's possible if your PC supports booting from a USB drive. No matter what you do you should download the CD that fits your architecture. If you're on a normal PC, meaning, a PC with a 32-bit processor, download the x86 - if you're on a Macintosh (PowerPC) download the PowerPC edition, and if you have an AMD64 CPU download the 64-Bit PC version. Simple enough.
