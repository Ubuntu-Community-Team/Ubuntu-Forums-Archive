### Post by T.J. on 2014-05-15
If the computer makes noise when using other applications as well when you put the CPU under load, then I'd say you have a noisy (and possibly inadequate) fan that may need replacement. One thing I would try is seeing if the Libreoffice hardware acceleration is on or off in the settings.  Changing the setting may fix your problem.  I can't say for certain because I do not know what hardware Apple crammed in your MBP, which tend to be packed pretty tight.
