### Post by forrestcupp on 2012-04-27
You need to tell us what version you're using. If she needs to run a bunch of Windows stuff, she needs to be using Windows and not Linux.

But you can get Flash and a bunch of codecs by installing the **kubuntu-restricted-extras** package, assuming you really have Kubuntu. Flash works in Linux, but Shockwave will not work with Linux at all. Most things out there don't need Shockwave anymore, though.
