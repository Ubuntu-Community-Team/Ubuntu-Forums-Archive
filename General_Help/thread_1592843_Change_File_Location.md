### Post by Aydos on 2010-10-11
In Windows you cant right click a folder, for example my documents, and go to properties and change the location or path to go to a different folder while using the same icon and everything.

Basically I have all of my music and what not on another partition. Is there a way I can make the music folder under home actually point to my music folder on the other partition.

That way on my Docky that I have set up I can use the default Music folder with the cool icon and click it but it go to the other folder.

Thank you.

### Post by nothingspecial on 2010-10-11
Open up 2 instances of nautilus, the file browser (the thing that opens when you click your home folder)

Have one at your Music folder

Have the other at the other partition, where the music is.

Hold Ctrl and Shift.

With those 2 keys held drag the folder with your music in it (on the other partition) and drag and drop it into your music folder.
