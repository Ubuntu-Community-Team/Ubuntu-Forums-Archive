### Post by kinematic on 2006-08-11
there's also an option to check the cd for errors when you boot it.
see what it turns up.
if it reports an error check the md5sum of the iso against the md5sum from the download server.
if that's ok try burning at a low speed...say 4x.
in my experience that error is almost every time caused by a corrupted download or a bad burn.
