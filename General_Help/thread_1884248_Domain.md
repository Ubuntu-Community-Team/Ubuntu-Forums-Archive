### Post by azmyth on 2011-11-20
I use [www.no-ip.biz](www.no-ip.biz) for my domain forwarding. There's a script they have that checks your IP if your IP is dynamic as a result of your ISP. You then need to setup port forwarding on your router to the PC that has apache and your website. You'll probably also need to set your apache to listen to the port number you've decided to use. Just as a warning some ISPs don't like it that your run a server.
