### Post by Titan8990 on 2008-12-10
Anything newer nvidia will work. When I say newer I am talking 5000 series + (way older than you should ever consider buying).

Nearly all video cards these days a PCI x16. AGP x8 is dead technology and would be considered "legacy" equipment. I personally could not recommend investing any more money in to a machine that only has an AGP slot.
