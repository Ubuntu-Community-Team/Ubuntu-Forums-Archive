### Post by armageddon08 on 2009-01-17
You can find new themes here: [URL="http://www.gnome-look.org"]http://www.gnome-look.org.
[/URL]
To install new themes go to System --> Appearance. Under the 'Themes' tab, click 'Install' and browse to the .tar.gz theme package.
