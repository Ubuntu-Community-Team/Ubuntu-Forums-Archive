### Post by Cimmerian on 2005-04-23
AFAIK, there are several ways to transfer files in the MSN protocol. One of them is to send via a server, which will lead to slow transfer rates. I believe gaim only supports this (not sure though). 

I think I read something somewhere about some developer(s) not wanting to implement it because file transfer and instant messaging did not belong together, but don't quote me on it ;) 

A solution would be to use a different MSN messenger, like [amsn](http://amsn.sourceforge.net)  or [mercury](http://www.mercury.to)  (others?). I believe their support for the MSN protocol is better and stuff also gets implemented faster, as MSN is their main focus. Or you could of course hack on gaim yourself :D
