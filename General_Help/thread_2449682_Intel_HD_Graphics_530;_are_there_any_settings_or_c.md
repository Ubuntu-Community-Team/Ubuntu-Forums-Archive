### Post by punchcard on 2020-09-01
Hello,
Solution found.


I found very old post in another Linux forum where a cable was at fault. Swapping cables made the pixelation stop. 
The previous cable is very small diameter (0.175 in) compared to the one (0.276 in) that works.
What is confounding is the goofy cable had worked on another computer for several years. And still does work in that situation. Damn that noise stuff.
I read a lot last night and considered many options but was hesitant to modify my system as an experiment so went searching more.
