### Post by DuckHook on 2018-05-09
As far as I know, T-bird inherits its default language from your global language settings. Change: Settings &#8594; Region & Language to UK and this should work. Note that this will also change your default language settings in Firefox, LibreOffice and all other apps, so be sure it is what you really want.
