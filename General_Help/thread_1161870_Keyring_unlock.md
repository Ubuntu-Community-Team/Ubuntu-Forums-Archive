### Post by Sashin on 2009-05-17
Whenever I check my email I get a message about a keyring, and I have to press deny...

Also when I try to install Ubuntu one I also get something about accessing a keyring, but I have no idea how to fix this. It is preventing me from installing it (although I was invited and all).

---

### Post by mb_webguy on 2009-05-17
Delete all of the files in the ~/.gnome2/keyrings directory.  The next time you're asked for a key for the keyring, leave everything blank and click through.  You shouldn't be bothered by the keyring after that.
