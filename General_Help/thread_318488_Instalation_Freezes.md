### Post by Sef on 2006-12-14
> I am trying to instal Ubuntu on my computer dual with windows but when the installer gets to 66% it just freezes what can i do..




1) Have you done an md5sum?  If not, then the download could be bad.

2) Can you try the disk on another computer?  If it works, then the problem is with your computer.

3) Can you reburn the iso?  The burn or the disk could be bad.

4) If you are using the Live CD, then try the alternate cd.  Sometimes the alternate cd will work where the live cd has failed.
