### Post by tgalati4 on 2013-12-19
Most browsers support fill-in PDF forms, just open them up in the browser.  You can save them as well.  There are probably also plug-ins that extend PDF functionality in browsers, but you would have to search for them.

*evince* handles simple PDF forms.  I've done my taxes using U.S. Federal and California tax forms on *evince* (also known as Document Viewer, 3.5.0 in this case) without problems.
