### Post by testube_babies on 2007-06-17
When you upgrade to Vista, it will overwrite your GRUB with the Vista bootloader.

If you use the Super GRUB Disk after the Vista install, you should be able to restore GRUB and go back to dual booting:
[http://supergrub.forjamari.linex.org/](http://supergrub.forjamari.linex.org/)
