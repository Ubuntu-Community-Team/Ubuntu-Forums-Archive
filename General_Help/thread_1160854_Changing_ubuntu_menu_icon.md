### Post by pbpersson on 2009-05-16
Assuming you have your PNG file in the directory where you want it to be....

Right-click on the Ubuntu icon for your main menu

Click on "edit menus"

Go to the menu item you want to change

Click on Properties

Click on the icon and then browse to where your PNG file is
