### Post by Titan8990 on 2008-06-06
Also if you are confused about a particular command you can google "man [command here]".

These man pages are much easier to read IMO than in the terminal. Usually you will find the correct page is top three results.

Example: 

Googled: "man find"

first result: [http://unixhelp.ed.ac.uk/CGI/man-cgi?find](http://unixhelp.ed.ac.uk/CGI/man-cgi?find)
