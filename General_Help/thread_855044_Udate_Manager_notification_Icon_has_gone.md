However I am running gnome and for some reason there is a conflict between the notification icons in the top right panel. PC

### Post by iaculallad on 2008-07-10
Try: System->Administration->Update Manager. 
It would display again on your tray when it detects new updates.

---

### Post by kostkon on 2008-07-10
Right click on your panel, select *Add to Panel* and then add the *Notification Area* applet.
