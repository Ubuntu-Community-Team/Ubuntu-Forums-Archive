### Post by akakingess on 2009-12-17
I am by no means an expert, but HAL stands for Hardware Abstraction Layer and is necessary on basically every system out there (well a lot of them anyway), but it is the pipeline for communication between your software even just daemons or what-nots and your actual hardware. So, it shouldn't be causing a problem and if it is, then you are going to either have a hardware problem or need a complete clean install.
