### Post by Maxtorm on 2007-06-23
For purposes of email and manuals, there is virtually nothing that you can accomplish in vi(m) or emacs that you could not do in Evolution (or the email client of your choice) or OpenOffice.org.   Once in a blue moon, I'll use vi(m) in conjunction with LaTeX ([http://www.latex-project.org/](http://www.latex-project.org/)) to create a publication, but I'm quite sure I could do the same thing in OpenOffice.org with less effort (it's not that I'm a sucker for punishment -- just that I have been using the same LaTeX templates for years).
