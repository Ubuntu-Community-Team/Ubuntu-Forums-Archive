### Post by audiomick on 2010-01-19
The thread can only be marked as solved by the person who started it. If it is marked, it means he or she has the issue sorted out, so there is probably a solution that worked for that particular case in there somewhere. Either that, or they have found a solution elsewhere or just given up.

The solved tag has no bearing on any general issue or bur that may have been the cause of the specific problem.
