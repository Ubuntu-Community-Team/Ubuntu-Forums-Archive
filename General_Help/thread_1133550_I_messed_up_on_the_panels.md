### Post by Tim Sharitt on 2009-04-23
Right-click your top panel and select 'New Panel.' 

Once you have the panel added back, you will need to add your applets back to it. Right-click on the new bottom panel and select 'Add to Panel...'

The default applets for the bottom panel (if memory serves) are:
Window List 
Workplace Switcher
Trash
Show Desktop

Once the applets are added, you can right-click on them, select move, and place them wherever you like. After you have them placed, you may also want to select 'Lock to Panel' from the right-click menu.
