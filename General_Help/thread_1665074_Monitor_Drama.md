### Post by papibe on 2011-01-11
It's probably a TV setting. Most TVs are set to do a mild zoom on the signal they receive. It's very annoying and it seems inherit from analog practices.

Use your remote and look for a setting to change how the signal is being scan. In my Sony Bravia it's under the Display manu and needs to be changed to "full pixel". In my brother's Samsung is in the Picture menu (don't remember the exact name).

Let us know how it goes.
