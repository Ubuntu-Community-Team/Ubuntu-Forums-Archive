Hi!  I am trying to utilize separate power schemes for AC and battery under xscreensaver.  Is this possible?  Xscreensaver-demo makes no such distinction and i prefer to stick with xscreensaver rather than fire up gnome-screensaver.

I'm using a Toshiba AMD 64 install of Ubuntu Studio (10.04).  I added the "Lubuntu" package to lighten up the DE and then added xscreensaver. ### Post by JC Cheloven on 2012-02-12
This should be possible to accomplish from the energy options window. 

If you installed the Lubuntu-desktop meta package, you probably have the xfce enegy administration program (it is so in 11.10, not sure in previous versions; other energy admins should offer you similar options though). From there, you can choose different inactivity times for quiet screen and for turning off the screen, and make those selections different for AC or Battery. That's what you want, if I at all understood.

Hope it helps.
