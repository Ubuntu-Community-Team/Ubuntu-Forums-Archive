### Post by hal8000 on 2009-10-30
Ideally you need to know exact make/model of the usb dongle.
It may work with ndiswrapper using a windows driver but if you buy something cheap and unbranded, may not work at all.


Cheack the linux hardware database or the Ubuntu HCL to see what other users have working.
