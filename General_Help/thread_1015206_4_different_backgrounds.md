### Post by Usufruct on 2008-12-18
Although the guy developing it has abandoned the project, you can use [wallpapoz]("http://wallpapoz.akbarhome.com/") to accomplish this.  

Unfortunately, all it really amounts to is a work-around for switching wallpapers when you switch desktops.  I believe the underlying issue is that gnome doesn't support multiple desktop images on multiple desktops.

I do believe you can configure compiz to display different background images while you're rotating the cube, but that doesn't solve your issue.

### Post by Wartender on 2008-12-18
btw after you activate the wallpaper plugin and put 4 different images, you have to press alt+f2, and type gconf-editor, then go to apps->nautilus->preferences and uncheck the show_desktop box, or the wallpapers won't appear.
