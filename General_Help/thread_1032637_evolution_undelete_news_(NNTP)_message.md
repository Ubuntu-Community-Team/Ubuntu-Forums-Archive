### Post by meonkeys on 2009-01-06
Using Evolution 2.24.2 in Ubuntu 8.10, I am subscribed to several gmane NNTP newsgroups. I accidentally clicked the "delete" button while viewing a news message. Anyone know how to recover said message?

---

### Post by meonkeys on 2009-04-08
I found a workaround. Under the "View" menu, there is a checkbox labeled "Hide Deleted Messages". **Un**checking this box allows accidentally deleted news messages to be seen.
