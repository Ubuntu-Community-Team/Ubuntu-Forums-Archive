### Post by selittl on 2010-08-09
Sondra, it is quite easy to customize Ubuntu.  Go to System/preferences/appearance and that will allow you to customize windows and controls.  As far as creating shortcuts just go to any menu and right click on whatever you want to launch, you will then get an option to add the shortcut either to the panel or to the desktop.
