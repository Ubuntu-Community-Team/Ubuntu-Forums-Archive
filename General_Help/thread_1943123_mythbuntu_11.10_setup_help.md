### Post by swright007 on 2012-03-19
I used to run a similar setup with a Hauppage card.   Your problem, I believe, to be one of 2 things.  Sometimes the computer became confused and would re-arrange port assignments without being told to.  Check that .. you may have to make sure your happage card is what is selected.  every now and again it would switch to my webcam and I would have to switch it back.   

If that isn't it... then you might try this trick.  It worked for me a bit too.   There is one menu (front or back, I am not sure because i don't have it in front of me) that turns off all recordings and resets the card.  That will without a doubt stop any recordings that are currently going and let you use your card again.   Best of luck with it.  I hope one of these is helpful to you.
