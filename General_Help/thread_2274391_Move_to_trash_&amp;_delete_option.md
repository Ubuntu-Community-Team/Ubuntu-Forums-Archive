### Post by BazBear on 2015-04-20
I'm a little confused what you're asking, but if you press and hold the shift key while the right click context is open, the Move to Trash option will change to a Remove (permanent delete) option.

### Post by yetimon_64 on 2015-04-20
> **xsidewinder said:**
> For some reason ive never had the option to "delete" files straight up in my options, only move to trash. Is there anyway to **remove that option from the list**? I find myself hitting it a lot instead of move to trash.

Open your home folder (any folder really will do), at the top of the window open the Edit menu > Preferences > Behavior Tab. Then detick the box "Include a Delete command that bypasses Trash". That should remove the delete option from your context menus. Cheers.
