### Post by allwin on 2007-01-15
I bought, from ebay, an ATI Radeon 7000 AGP 32MB video card, for like $15.  Used it to replace an old nVidia one.  It plays nice with Beryl 0.1.4 and the ATI driver.  Slow scrolling, but when I changed it from default depth of 24 down to 16...it is now responsive as without Beryl, and this is on an old P III 550MHZ.  This might be a nice way to get the eye candy if you have an old nVidia card.

I can't say it's the stablest thing in the world, in that Beryl goes away quite often, but you can reload it as window manager and it picks up again.  Dare I say this was more effective than trying to get a cranky old nVidia card going.  Natch, YMMV.
