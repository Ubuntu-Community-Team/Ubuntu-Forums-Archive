### Post by nchase on 2008-04-04
Be sure that you have the proper settings in your hellanzb.conf file. Important ones to check may be:
Hellanzb.SKIP_UNRAR = False
Hellanzb.UNRAR_CMD = "/usr/bin/unrar"


I actually just wrote a post about this at my blog that you can read [here]("http://blog.ahfr.org/2008/04/newsgroup-binaries-with-hellanzb-and.html"). It is a brief walkthrough of how to use HellaNZB with the LottaNZB GTK frontend.
