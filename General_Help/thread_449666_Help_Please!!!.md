### Post by bung33 on 2007-05-28
That depends upon what kind of sound card you have. If you didn't make your computer yourself, then you can go to the manufacturer's website, find the drivers section, locate your computer, and download the applicable driver. If you did make your computer, then you should know what kind of sound card you have. You'll need to uninstall the current driver (which isn't working) before installing the new one. You'll find lots of help for this on the internet...
