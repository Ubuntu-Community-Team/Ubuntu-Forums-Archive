### Post by Rasa1111 on 2010-09-01
pretty sure it is possible. 

For example~
 Open your home folder~

Find the folder you want to change~
 right click it, choose properties..

 when properties window opens,
 Click on the folder icon to the left..
Then choose whatever image you want for it. 

 That should do it.

( I think that;s what you wanted anyway)
sorry if not.

 also~
if you change the size "zoom view" of the directory your looking in,
the larger you go, the more it will show, of what is in the folders.
