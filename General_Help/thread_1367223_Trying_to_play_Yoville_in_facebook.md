> Hi can some1 help me to play yoville in facebook please

Hi!!
If you install *Flash Player 10.1 Beta 2* works fine to play *yoville* and other *zynga games*.
Because the *flash* is *Beta*, you can do this
without change something of your system.

**1)**go to   [http://www.mozilla.com/](http://www.mozilla.com/)
and download Firefox for Linux

**2)**go to [http://labs.adobe.com/downloads/flash...]("http://labs.adobe.com/downloads/flashplayer10.html")
and download *Flash Player 10.1 Beta 2*
Download plug-in for Linux (TAR.GZ, 4.3 MB)

**3)**extract two archives 
open firefox folder - go to plugins folder
delete archive *libnullplugin.so*
open* install_flash_player_10_linux* folder 
copy archive *libflashplayer.so*
and paste into the folder plugins.

Then close Firefox and
open with double clik firefox archive(of the folder) 
and play yoville! :)

See the video [http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=M9vyyiYuLZ0](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=M9vyyiYuLZ0)

But never use the same time the two *Firefox*!:idea:
