### Post by mikewhatever on 2010-09-19
That is a graphical glitch in the boot process. Ideally, you should see either the splash screen or the one that shows before the login screen. Anyhow, what you see is the login prompt of the system that runs underneath the GUI. It's a prompt, so that its job is to ask for login. I am tempted to say, 'Don't worry about it', as it doesn't impair any functionality, and yet, the black screen with white letters is probably not aesthetically pleasing. Hopefully, it will get fixed by one of the updates or future releases.
