Yes, just add comments in the code to say that it's GPL licensed.

> Also is there any fee i've to pay to any organisation in order to attach a GPL to my script.I'm a complete novice in this field so a detailed explaination will be admired.
Ofcourse not, that would be totally against the spirit of the GPL; the aim is to make software freely available. GNU would ofcourse not limit use of their license by asking you to pay any fees.

See [http://www.gnu.org/licenses/licenses.html](http://www.gnu.org/licenses/licenses.html)
