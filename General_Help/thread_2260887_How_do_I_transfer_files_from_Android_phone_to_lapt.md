### Post by Gordonbp531 on 2015-01-15
I have an Xperia E3 - just connect it via the same cable you use for charging.

(BTW I'm surprised you have a "lot" of files on the internal storage - the E3 has very little internal storage.....I use an external micro SDHC card for my music and photos....)
