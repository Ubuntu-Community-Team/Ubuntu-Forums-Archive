### Post by WorMzy on 2010-05-22
"Best" is entirely subjective. Try them all out and see what works best for you. I'm currently using Rhythmbox. It's got a bizarre way of playing files that I don't like, but it's pretty bug-free. I used Exaile as my default music player on Karmic, it's got plenty of bugs, but it has a logical playlist/queue system that I like. I used Amarok2 for a while back when I had KDE libraries installed, and it's pretty much the best of both worlds, but it looks out of place on GNOME.
