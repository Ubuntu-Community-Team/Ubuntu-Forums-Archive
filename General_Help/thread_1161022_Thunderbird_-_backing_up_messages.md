How can I do that without separately saving each message in a file? Are all the messages stored in a specific folder I could just copy over to another installation, or what would be a quick/reliable method of backing up all the messages?

### Post by DeMus on 2009-05-16
> **hackapelite said:**
> How can I do that without separately saving each message in a file? Are all the messages stored in a specific folder I could just copy over to another installation, or what would be a quick/reliable method of backing up all the messages?

Look in your home folder, use ctrl-h to show hidden files.
Then you will see a folder called .mozilla-thunderbird. In there you will find one or more folders with your profiles. In this folder (those folders) you find folders with your e-mails. Just look around and find the account(s) you are using. The name of the folder(s) depend(s) on the name of the account(s) you have.
