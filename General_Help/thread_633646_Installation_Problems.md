### Post by psyguy1 on 2007-12-07
When you get to the command line, trying entering **startx** and see if that helps.  If it does anything worthwhile do a little searching on startx and you'll probably find some decent information.  If it does nothing you might consider trying a different means of installation.

Also you could try installing updated drivers for your video card (or a new video card altogether).  I'm sure it's supposed to work to some extent out of the box, but maybe for some reason you're encountering a conflict or some sort that won't let you get into the desktop.
