### Post by him610 on 2018-11-03
Many wireless devices, including Logitech, communicate over the 2.4 GHz frequency band. There is also a WiFi frequency band of 2.4 GHz which most wireless routers use; dual-band routers also use the 5 GHz band. The frequency band of 2.4 GHz has become more crowded over the last few years. 
So, to address your question: "Yes, it is possible for a  2.4 GHz wireless device to be susceptible to radio frequency interference (RFI) from other nearby devices that transmit in the 2.4 GHz band."

Note: Be advised that I am not a scientist, physicist, nor electrical engineer. I offer my opinion based on readings, observations, and experience gained over the years.
