### Post by SeijiSensei on 2017-03-09
If you and your recipients have set up public and private keys, then your messages to those recipients should still be encrypted.  If the recipients themselves don't have keys, and  they choose to quote parts of your decrypted messages back in replies, then they will be visible to anyone.  I think Google is the least of your worries if encryption matters so much to you.
