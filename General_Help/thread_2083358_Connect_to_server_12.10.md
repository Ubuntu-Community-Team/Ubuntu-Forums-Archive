### Post by chideock on 2012-11-12
In 12.04 if you mouse over the "Ubuntu Desktop" in upper left unity desktop screen and then select "file" one of the drop down options is "Connect to Server". 
 
The other way in 12.04 is to click on the "Home Folder" icon on the left side and then mouse over the "Home" name in the upper left corner and then click on "File" you will find it in the drop down menu.
