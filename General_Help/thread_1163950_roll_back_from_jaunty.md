### Post by lovinglinux on 2009-05-19
Did you upgraded from 8.10 or did you installed fresh? 

Unless you have a blacklisted video card, you might want to consider installing Jaunty from fresh again. Since there is no way to roll back without reinstalling, it wouldn't hurt to try. If you have a [separate home partition](http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/separatehome), this would be pretty easy and will preserve your settings.

If it doesn't work this way, then install Hardy or Intrepid over it, preserving your home.
