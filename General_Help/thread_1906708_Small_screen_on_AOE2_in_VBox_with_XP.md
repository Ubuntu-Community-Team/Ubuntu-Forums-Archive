### Post by layers on 2012-01-09
After a couple of attempts to stop the game crashing at start-up both with Wine by itself and with PlayOnLinux, I decided to give VBox, downloaded from their website. As that was my second last resort, I really wanted to get it working properly. My last resort would be to install XP as a dual boot, but I've been Windows-free for more than a year now, let's not open old wounds.

The game actually runs, and everything is good, except the graphics - it shows into a small screen, instead of being in the whole screen. In Ctrl+F and Ctrl+L, it just centers a good resolution screen in the middle, with huge black frames as in the picture.

In Ctrl+C, the game is full screen, but bad resolution, and only with Ctrl+C, the mouse is laggy, which makes the game unplayable there.

I do have Guest Additions installed (without the 3D thing). The Ctrl+F works as it should out of the game, on the Desktop. The max resolution of the screen I'm using (host) is 1600:900. AoE is just from a time of low resolutions - go to settings and see how high you can get the resolution, for some reason this is only in game play - videos and menus are still smaller. The only way I got this going semi decent was to change the host display down, the guest colour pallet up! also if the sea looks really naff - like grainy and stuff - kill the explorer.exe before you play - you can start it up whilst playing.

hope it helps - sorry it's not perfect!
