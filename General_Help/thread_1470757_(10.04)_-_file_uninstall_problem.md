### Post by sanderd17 on 2010-05-03
A safer way:
```

sudo chmod -R +rw /path

```
Than your files should be editable (and thus deletable) in nautilus.

In the previous post, if you hit enter to soon, you can remove your entire distribution. If you mistype something in this solution, you can only lose your read-write permissions and thus make your computer writable by anyone. You won't lose your files but you'ld have to fix it cause it's a security issue.
