### Post by NateDoggUK on 2008-07-24
I may be jumping the gun here with my first post but I found that I had to disable the "Extra" function in Visual Effects when playing World Of Warcraft in Hardy on my Inspiron 1501. This disabled all my Compiz Fusion special effects but made the game run perfectly without the regular blacked out frames.

Annoyingly it resets some of the Compiz Fusion options each time but I can live with that.
