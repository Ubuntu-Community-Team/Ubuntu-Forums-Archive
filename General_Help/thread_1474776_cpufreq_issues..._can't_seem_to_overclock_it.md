### Post by cascade9 on 2010-05-06
You *might* be able to crossflash the BIOS (i.e. flash the BIOS with the BIOS from the same chipset, but produced by a different manufacturer..one that hasnt 'losked' the BIOS) but that always has some element of risk. 

I'm not sure its worth it, the fastest P4 released was a 3.8Ghz model, and pushing much past that can be hard......and it puts a lot more stress on the chipset. 

Depending on exactly what model P4 you have, upgrading the CPU could be cheap, easy and give you more than overclocking anyway.
