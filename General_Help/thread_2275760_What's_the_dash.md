### Post by grahammechanical on 2015-04-28
What computer operating system are you using? We have a family of Ubuntu distributions and each has its own desktop environment and user interface. The Dash is a component of the Ubuntu Unity user interface.

Think of it as a slide out - slide in desktop wide panel that presents us with icons of recently used applications and files as well as icons for all the installed applications. It can be searched and filtered. There are special filters called Scopes for Applications; Folders & Files; Videos; Music and Pictures.

The Dash has the ability to search local storage (the hard disk) and online storage (the internet). We can limit the Dash's power to search. The Dash has a large collection of search plugins that can all be disabled.

To see what I am talking about just do an internet search on Ubuntu Dash images.

Regards.
