### Post by maxbur on 2009-05-11
My Jaunty install on a MBP 4.1 needed a simple tweak to >System>Preferences>Keyboard>Layouts to change the Keyboard Model from "Default" to "Macbook/Macbook Pro." That got most of the F keys working.

Likewise, try tweaking >System>Preferences>Mouse>Touchpad. I got best results with only the "Enable Touchpad" selection box checked, leaving the others unselected. Two-finger scrolling and right-click functionality work fine without checking those boxes. The "Enable mouse clicks with touchpad" selection caused too much flaky and unwanted touchpad behaviour.

The Mactel support pages or a forum search should get you started on the fan speed control.
[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/MactelSupportTeam/CommunityHelpPages]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/MactelSupportTeam/CommunityHelpPages")
