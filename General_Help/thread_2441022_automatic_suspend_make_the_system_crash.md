### Post by CelticWarrior on 2020-04-18
Hopefully you installed in UEFI mode as it should be. Then:

[LIST]
[*]Update UEFI ("BIOS") and SSD's firmware
[*]Ubuntu 18.04 is probably older than your hardware so, not a good idea. Prefer 20.04.
[/LIST]

If the problem persists with 20.04 a workaround is to disable automatic suspension.
