### Post by Cheesehead on 2012-08-13
There are no good free *accounting* programs available in the Ubuntu repositories. Couple reasons for that: For example, no volunteers seem willing to keep the myriad of tax tables updated, so you cannot necessarily trust that the amounts are correct. Also, most developers understand bookkeeping, but don't understand the difference between bookkeeping and accounting. So there are lots of good bookkeeping programs...but no good accounting programs to use all that bookkeeping data.

Finally, most businesses (that grow enough to support the owner) will use a professional accountant anyway to file taxes...and so will use the system that accountant prefers. Accountant per-hour charges are much higher than software costs, so you will use whatever system you need to minimize those hourly charges.
