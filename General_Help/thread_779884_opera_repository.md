### Post by bingoUV on 2008-05-03
There seems to be no repository. Opera does not play with Ubuntu's rules. You just need to download the deb from Opera's website like morons in other operating systems. On the bright side, it is a reasonably good browser. 

Go to [http://www.opera.com/download/index.dml?platform=linux](http://www.opera.com/download/index.dml?platform=linux). Double click on the downloaded file. Then you would likely find opera's link in Applications -> Internet menu.
