### Post by nzadLithium on 2007-07-28
I have this problem everytime i restart. 
I have a package installed called alsamixergui

install that.

then click applications, sound and video, alsamixergui
in there it says Mic above that and the two funny grey bars are two white circles. Click them and they will become red. (if anyone knows how to do this without alsa mixer gui plz tell)

Also if you are using a front input on your case you may need to double click the sound sign in the top left and click edit preferences. then add Mic front input. Then click switches and check Mic front input.

Then goto the capture tab and make sure neither is muted.

then try and record a sound.
