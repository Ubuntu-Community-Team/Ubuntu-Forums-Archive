### Post by pauper on 2008-08-21
It seems that some keys, such as Super L, Alt Gr, Menu, can't be bound together
with anything other than Ctrl, Shift, Alt in "gnome-keybinding-properties".
Though, something like Ctrl-Alt-t or Alt-g, for example, is configurable.

If you intend to set up those pesky keys look into "xbindkeys" application--
it's not installed by default.
