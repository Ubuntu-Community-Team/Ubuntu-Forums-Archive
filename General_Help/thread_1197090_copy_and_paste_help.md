### Post by Tyke91 on 2009-06-25
make sure to keep your mouse inside of the window that you are copying from, i know that sometimes i make a sweeping highlight with my mouse and it winds up outside the window. This takes focus away from your current window and puts it on whatever was behind it... so you wind up copying from that source instead :P


Alternatively, just highlight the text you want and middle click where you want to paste it :)  convenient, eh?

When you copy text from a window, then close it, you can no longer paste it, you must keep the window you copied the text from, open.
