### Post by woedend on 2006-11-08
have you tried other bootable disks?  
CD Booting is all bios related rather than ubuntu.  Meaning, nothing you can install in ubuntu(or on the HD for that matter) will affect this ability.  Unless, I suppose, a program changed your bios settings, but I don't see how this would happen to you.  I'd check your bios settings again, then make sure the cd-rom works properly.
