### Post by grahammechanical on 2014-09-17
Open the Dash - click on the Ubuntu icon at the top of the Launcher (side panel on the left edge of the screen). At the bottom of the Dash click on the second icon from the left out of the 7 icons. You want the A = Applications. Then click Installed see xx more results. and scroll through what is listed. Or look to the top right of the Dash and click Filter and select Education and see what you get offered

Or Press Super key + A to open the Dash at the Applications pane. And go from there as above. The super key is the key with the flying windows on it. Holding down the super key will bring up an overlay with all the Unity keyboard shortcuts.
