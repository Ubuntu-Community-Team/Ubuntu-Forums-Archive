### Post by daengbo on 2008-01-19
DVD is an ISO standard. The CD/DVD creator makes DVDs following this standard, which is the same as the "Windows' DVD." There is no difference.

I'm going to suggest that the DVD burned incorrectly and you created a coaster. You should try burning at a lower speed and avoiding disk-intensive operations while burning.
