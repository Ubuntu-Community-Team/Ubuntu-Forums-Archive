### Post by wdaniels on 2008-05-08
There are quite a few factors to consider in this, not least of which is the airflow through the rest of the chassis.  Sometimes what looks like a relatively small difference in the cooling power of the fan can produce a disproportionate overall effect if a less powerful fan ends up trying to cool the processor with warmer air in the first place.  So it's difficult to say for sure, but it doesn't seem like the best idea to me personally.
