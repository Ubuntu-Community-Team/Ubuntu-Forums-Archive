### Post by SecretCode on 2011-09-05
Do you mean the panels in GNOME 2, as with Maverick and earlier versions?

The problem is these panels don't support true transparency, they only fake it by copying the wallpaper (and do not support blur). So the alpha blur feature in Compiz doesn't help. (It works fine on Emerald decorations and apps like gnome-terminal which have true transparency.)

And if you use Compiz to make it transparent (and blurred), then all the icons are transparent not just the background, which is not what you want.

I can't offhand think of a replacement panel / dock that does what you want including supporting gnome panel applets ...
