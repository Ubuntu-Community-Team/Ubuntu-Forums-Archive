### Post by meierfra on 2007-07-15
Maybe this helps:

Gnome and Kde use different trashcans.

The gnome trashcan is located in .Trash

The kde trashcan is located in .local/share/Trash/Files

So if I delete files in a gnome session and then switch to a kde session, I will not see the files in my kde  rash can.
