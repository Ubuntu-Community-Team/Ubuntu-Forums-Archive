### Post by Morbius1 on 2010-02-27
If the "Windows" in your description is another box on the LAN:

Open Nautilus
Right Click on say .... /home/your_user_name/Documents
Select "Sharing Options"
Put a check on all three boxes
Select "Create Share"

If the gods are in a good mood you should be able to access the "documents" share from WinXP.
