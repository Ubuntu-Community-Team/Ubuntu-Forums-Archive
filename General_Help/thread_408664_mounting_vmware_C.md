### Post by raja on 2007-04-13
No, you cannot directly mount the windows partition in Ubuntu. You  have to connect through a samba network. If you have not tried virtualbox, I would recommend that. It seems to be better than vmware (in my experience of about a week) and it is easier to set up a shared folder.
