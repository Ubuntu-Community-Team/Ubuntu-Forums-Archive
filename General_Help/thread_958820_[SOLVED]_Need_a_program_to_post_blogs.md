### Post by zachthejones on 2008-11-06
okay, I finally got Kblogger working with my Wordpress-based blog. I notated all the details on my [linux blog]("http://linux.zachjones.net/?p=82#more-82"), but basically I had to add "/xmlrpc.php" to the end of your blog URL. So your blog address reads as "http://your.blog.com/xmlrpc.php" in the account profile.
