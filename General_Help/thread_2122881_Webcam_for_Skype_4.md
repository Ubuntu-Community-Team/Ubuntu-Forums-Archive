### Post by scbingham on 2013-03-06
I initially had problems with two different web cams not working at all with Skype.

After searching the forum for answers, a common suggestion is download Cheese & see if your cam works properly with that. If so, you will probably get it to work with Skype.

Mine didn't. Both were made by Trust, who apparently got into bed with MS years ago; Trust & MS cams don't seem to work with Linux.

There is a hardware compatibility list somewhere you can check. My solution was a cheap web cam off ebay.

I hope this nudges you in the right direction.
