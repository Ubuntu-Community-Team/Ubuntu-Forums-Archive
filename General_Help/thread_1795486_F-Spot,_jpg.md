### Post by ajgreeny on 2011-07-03
I always import photos using nautilus into my own file system, rather than allow f-spot or any other photo manager to do it, so I'm not sure exactly how f-spot works.  If you've got a card reader I suggest you use that and not plug in the camera, then copy across photos with nautilus.  You can then import them into f-spot from the pictures or photos folder where you have put them in your filesystem.

Sorry I can't specifically help with your f-spot query.  I don't use it any more;  I've gone into gthumb, which is much quicker and less resource hungry.
