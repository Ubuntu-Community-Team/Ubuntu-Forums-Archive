### Post by john183 on 2008-12-16
I am guessing that all of your menus are gone if the locations menu is gone also. To get it back right click on the upper panel and choose "add to panel" from the list that pops up choose "Menu Bar" and move it to where ever you like on the panel. To add your music folder, open it in nautilus and choose add bookmark from the bookmarks menu at the top of the window.

### Post by john183 on 2008-12-16
If you are using Ubuntu and have not added a different file browser/manager nautilus will be your default. Another easy way to tell is to open a folder and on the choose about from the help menu in the file browser window, that will tell you what program you are using.
