### Post by Matsukaze on 2009-03-08
> **Leischa said:**
> However, I can't even get into my machine at the moment - when I switch it on, I get a double beeping sound and a black screen - no GRUB, no command line options, nothing. The live CD doesn't run either.

This has absolutely nothing to do with Linux. The two beeps are a POST (Power-On Self Test) code indicating some type of hardware failure. The meaning of the beeps depends on what type of motherboard you have. If it uses an AMI BIOS the two beeps indicate a memory problem. You'll need to check the manual for your motherboard, or the manufacturer's website, to find out what they mean on your system.
