### Post by ian-weisser on 2013-11-06
No. The keyring requires a password.

You should receive a keyring password prompt *only* if your keyring password does not match you account password.
Change one or the other to match, and the prompts should cease.

Basic security: Nobody but you should be using your account anyway.

Or are you trying to say that you are getting keyring prompts while using the guest account? That's a whole different question and answer.
