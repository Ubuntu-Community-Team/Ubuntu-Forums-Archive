### Post by winotree on 2010-01-22
> **JiuJitsu500 said:**
> Google's "safe browsing" extension was cool a few years ago :) I don't know if it's still around though... Firefox is supposed to have built in protection too with the new versions....
In **about:config** set these two preferences to **true**

browser.safebrowsing.enabled
browser.safebrowsing.malware.enabled

They can also be more easily accessed [on Xubuntu] by checking, or ticking

Menu | Edit | Preferences | Block reported attack sites 
Menu | Edit | Preferences | Block reported web forgeries

Hope this helps.  :)
