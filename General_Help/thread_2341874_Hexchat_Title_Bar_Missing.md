### Post by vasa1 on 2016-11-02
What happens if you press alt+spacebar? Do you see something similar to the image at the top left of the hexchat window?

It's possible that the part in the red rectangle needs to be toggled. In other words, by pressing alt+spacebar and then "d" to Decorate or Undecorate your window, you can hide or show the title bar. This is true for apps which don't rely on client-side decorations.
