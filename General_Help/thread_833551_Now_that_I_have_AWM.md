Just right-click on the top panel and choose **Add to Panel...** to add all those features back.

Also, you might want to try [installing AWN a different way](http://news.softpedia.com/news/Install-AWN-on-Hardy-Heron-82611.shtml) since the one in the repos is like one and a half version behind.
