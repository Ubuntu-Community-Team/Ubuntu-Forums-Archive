### Post by niteshifter on 2007-12-20
Click - and hold - on the icon at the far left in the address bar (just to the left of [http://.](http://.)..), and drag it onto your desktop or folder.

---

### Post by njparton on 2007-12-20
Right click on desktop > Create Launcher... > change "Type" to "Location" > give it a name > type the url (including http;//) into the command field.
