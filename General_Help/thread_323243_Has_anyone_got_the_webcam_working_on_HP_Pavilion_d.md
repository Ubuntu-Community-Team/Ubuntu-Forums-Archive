### Post by MikaelHolber on 2007-03-08
If you have a Richo webcam with usbID 05ca:1870 you might want to check out the R5U870-driver. It is brand new but works very well on my dv2115. You'll find it at [http://lsb.blogdns.net/ry5u870/]("http://lsb.blogdns.net/ry5u870/"). It doesn't work with every app but try effectv or ekiga which works for me.

If someone has the same camera and knows how to make effectv and vloopback work together I would appreciate to get to know.
