### Post by PreviousN on 2008-03-23
Try System76. They're excellent and offer support if you're a new user. 

Also, you could try a dell that comes with ubuntu installed. I'm pretty sure they've got intel graphics which support desktop effects and hibernation outta box (though I don't use em..)

Currently I've got a gateway (which was about 500dollars on newegg. It's got a Pentium D and 1 GB of ram with dvd burner. Good deal if I say so myself) that I've run into quite a few problems on. I had to use NDISwrapper for wireless (not so bad, I actually prefer it. I tried open drivers and got poor performance, so you might try this on your current laptop, and recomplie alsa for sound (PAIN IN THE A). Since the graphics card is ATI I don't have working suspend nor hibernate....

But I do have wobbly windows, rain effect, and desktop cube :-). 

Good luck in your purchase. I've found that laptops don't get as good of battery life with ubuntu as with windows, so that may be the selling point for you, since the vast majority of problems between laptops and ubuntu are overcomerable (except suspend or hibernate...get intel graphics). 

Cheers!
