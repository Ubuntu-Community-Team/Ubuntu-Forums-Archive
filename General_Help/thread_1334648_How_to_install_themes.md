### Post by stinkeye on 2009-11-23
If you just have a folder containing a gtk and/or metacity theme 
place it in /home/yourusername/.themes directory.
Create a .themes folder if you don't have one.
Same applies for fonts...../home/yourusername/.fonts
and icons........ /home/yourusername/.icons
These should now be available in System>Preferences>Appearance.

By placing them in here they are not available to other users.
They would have to do the same thing in their account.
The benefit of doing it this way is that when you backup your home directory
your also backing up all your downloaded fonts, icons and themes.
