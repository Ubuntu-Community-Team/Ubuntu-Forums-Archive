### Post by snooo on 2005-11-23
Amarok certainly rocks, but getting ipod support to work with it is a pain. As far as I know, you need to have libgpod compiled and installed before ipod support will work. You need to use the CVS version, but it is currently full of bugs in the automake process. You *can* get it working though. 

Download libgpod from here: [http://www.gtkpod.org/libgpod.html](http://www.gtkpod.org/libgpod.html) . Let us know if you hit any probs. #amarok on irc.freenode.net is a good place to go if you have probs compiling as well.
