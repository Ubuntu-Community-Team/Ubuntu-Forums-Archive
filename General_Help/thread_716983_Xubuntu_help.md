### Post by analog_G on 2008-03-06
Depending on how old and messed up your hardware is, it may be difficult to see the true potential of xubuntu from the CD.  At least you can get an idea of the 'look' but you can't get the 'feel'.  I recently installed xubuntu on an older laptop.  This laptop was much newer than what I assume your win98 machine is and that didn't run all that well from the CD.  Pop in the CD, wait for the initial boot and select "Install" from inside the "applications" menu.  I say jump in with both feet.  There are plenty of nice people here to help you out.
Or...
search this forum for the disk partitioning /dual boot writeups if you'd like to keep win98 around as a backup.
