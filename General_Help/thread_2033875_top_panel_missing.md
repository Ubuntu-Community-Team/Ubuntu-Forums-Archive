### Post by OxentroT on 2012-07-27
I have had this happen on accident a few times when I was new. If you are talking about what I think you are talking about. 

This is going to be a pain in the eyebrow to get it back to how you wanted it but here goes. 
Minimize everything you have up. Go to your bottom panel and right click on where there is nothing. You want to choose option 'Add Panel'. When your new panel appears you drag it to the top. You will have to add everythign you had there piece by piece.

To get your menus back, just right click, scroll down the list until you see 'Menu Bar' add this first. Right click the newly spawned menu bar and choose option 'Move" now place it where you want it to sit. Now right click the menu bar and choose option 'Lock to Panel'. (do this for each item hereafter) AND (be careful not to choose delete panel!) 

To get back your time and stuff, right click again and find 'Notification Area' and add that as well and place where you want. 

To add whatever applications you use the most you will choose 'Application Launcher' And you will be able to choose from your applications menu your choice app to have an icon on your panel.



Hope everything works out, and you learn to be careful what you click if this was accident caused by you :)
