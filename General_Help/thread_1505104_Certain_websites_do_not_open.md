However, the user's local network or Internet environment may not be equipped to handle IPv6 and you may see an "Address not found" or "Server not found" error for many websites. To disable IPv6, change the preference [network.dns.disableIPv6]("http://kb.mozillazine.org/Network.dns.disableIPv6") from "false" to **true** . Here are the steps: 
[LIST=1]
[*]Type [about:config]("http://kb.mozillazine.org/About:config") in the address bar, press Enter.
[*]Find **network.dns.disableIPv6** in the list.
[*]Right-click -> Toggle.
[*]Restart your Mozilla application and try again.
[/LIST]If this doesn't work, you can re-enable IPv6 by resetting the preference to "false" (double-click to toggle).
