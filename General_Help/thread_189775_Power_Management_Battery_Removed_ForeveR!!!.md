### Post by tcrace on 2007-02-04
I also accidentally removed the power management icon from the panel on Ubuntu and wondered how to get it back. In the interest of sharing the solution and making it available online (I might delete it again and forget how I got it back :) ) I'll post it here.

Right click on the panel and select "Add to panel...", scroll to the bottom of the "Add to Panel" dialog and select "Notification Area" to add to your panel. If the setting under the "General" tab of the "Power Management Preferences" dialog "Always display icon" is checked you should have your Power Management icon back just like before.
