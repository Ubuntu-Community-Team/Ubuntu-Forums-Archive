### Post by arpanaut on 2012-04-27
The removal of Dodge was a top of the line decision by SABDFL himself apparently new users were confused by this option and rather than just changing defaults, the decision was made to remove the function altogether & not maintain code that was not going to be used on Unity You might want to look at this thread in the old Ubuntu +1 section: 
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1937199&highlight=Dodge](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1937199&highlight=Dodge)  
Some were trying with a script  to restore this function, I never pursued and just adapted. 

For the sensitivity of the dash reveal try  r-click on desktop > change background > behaviour, from there move the slider to the right until the reveal works for you, for me it's a little past halfway to the right.

HTH
