### Post by SpikeyB on 2007-06-01
I came across something I found of interest last night and wondered if anyone was aware of how it happened.

I was in XP and created and inserted some screenshots in Word.  I decided to crop the pictures and then saved in .doc format.

I moved into Ubuntu to e-mail the document.  I took a quick look at the document using Abiword to check on formatting and realised that the pictures were not cropped.  I opened the document in OO and the pictures were cropped.  I am guessing that the information was still stored in the cropped images but just wasn't displayed.

Anyway, if you're cropping images in Word to hide some information, the information will still be available to someone with Abiword.
