### Post by TheExplorer on 2007-08-11
Try this:

- For e.g. in OpenOffice.org's menu go to 'File' - 'Printer settings'
- Click 'Properties' and go to the second tab 'Device'
- In the second last row 'Color' choose 'Grayscale'.
