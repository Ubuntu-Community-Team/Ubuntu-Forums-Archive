### Post by wylfing on 2005-03-15
You are probably having a conflict with other rendering modules. Open a terminal and type 'sudo lsmod' and paste the results here.

Also, as another poster said, edit XF86Config-4 and comment out the GLcore line so it looks like this:

# Load	"GLcore"

I would also comment out the VideoRam line in the Device section.
