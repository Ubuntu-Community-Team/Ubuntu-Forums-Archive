(1)  I don't know of any way to "improve bandwidth usage".  But do check to make sure that "Use Surplus Bandwidth" is enabled in "Preferences"/"Bandwidth" (I believe this is the default) so that it will use bandwidth not required at the moment by Gnutella for searches and such.

(2) You can get much better connectivity by going to "ultra-peer" mode, but you need meet certain requirements (not being firewalled, for example).  It also required a lot more bandwidth for Gnutella overhead than operating in "leaf" mode. 

Be advised that, thanks to the way TCP operates, maxing your upload channel can affect downloading, and vice versa.  I keep my upload and download bandwidth limited to about 80% of my internet connection's max to prevent such problems...
