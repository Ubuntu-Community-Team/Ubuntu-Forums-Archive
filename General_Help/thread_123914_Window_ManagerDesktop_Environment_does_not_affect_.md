### Post by MrNamjama on 2006-01-31
I would expect the single most important factor to be screen brightness. 

On full brightness, my dell 510m gets ~2 hours. Two steps down from maximum it gets ~3:30 - ~4 hours. That's a very significant difference, obviously, so I cannot see increased cpu usage (even 2 or 3x higher) making the same kind of difference, given that CPUs use far less electricity than monitors
