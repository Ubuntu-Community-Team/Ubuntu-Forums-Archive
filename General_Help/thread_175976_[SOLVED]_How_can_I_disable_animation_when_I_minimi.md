### Post by aysiu on 2006-05-14
It's a two-parter.

First step, get rid of those lines: Go to Applications > System Tools > Configuration Editor > Apps > Metacity > General. Check/tick the box that says **Reduced Resources**.

Well, now you've gotten rid of the lines when you minimize windows. New problem? You get this wire basket thing when you try to *move* Windows.

So, step two: System > Preferences > Assistive Technology Support. Enable it.

Now, no lines, no wires.
