### Post by taurus on 2006-04-14
First, make sure you burned the ISO as an image file, not a data file.  And when you turn on your machine, press F2, F12, Del, or whatever key to get into your BIOS and make sure you have selected to boot from CD-ROM first.  Otherwise, it will by-pass the CD and boots directly from your harddrive.  However, I would strongly recommand you check out the LiveCD first to see if it is what you want before you install it on your computer or you don't care and just want to install it and get on with it like everybody else...  :mrgreen:
