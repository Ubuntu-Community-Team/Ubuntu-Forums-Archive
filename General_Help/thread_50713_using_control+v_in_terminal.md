### Post by Lunde on 2005-07-21
[QUOTE=gammyhand]Is there any way to enable control+v (for pasting stuff) in terminal. It annoys me having to right click and press paste.[/QUOTE]
 Shift+insert works by default

Another funky thing is if you have a mouse wheel or a third mouse button. Just select any text in any window, then click the mouse wheel, this will paste the selected text into the activated program, even terminal. (No keystrokes at all...)

I'm afraid I still hav'nt looked into changing these wonderful shortcuts in terminal, but I'm sure it's possible. Just keep in mind that shortcuts like Ctrl+C and Ctrl+X and some others are already being used by terminal or closely related programs
