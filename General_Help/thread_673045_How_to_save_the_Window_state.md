### Post by editrix on 2008-01-20
I've never figured out what all the settings mean, but what I've done is, once the window is the way I like it, hit the icon in the top left corner (above File) and choose "Configure Window Behaviour."

You'll get a window with lots of options, one of which is "Window Specific Settings." Choose that.

Then, hit New, give it a name, and you'll get a menu with geometry, preferences, workarounds, etc. I just kept clicking stuff till it worked the way I wanted it to.

I should say, though, that I never tried to rejustify it--just got it to always open on one desktop.
