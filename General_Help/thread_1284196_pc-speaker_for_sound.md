### Post by Giblet5 on 2009-10-06
It's theoretically possible and I've seen it done under DOS.

Hack-a-Day has a video of a grand piano being made to talk. That's a lot harder than making a beep-type speaker produce complex sound.

But then, why would anyone bother? A good sound card can be had for free if ya don't mind digging through trash.

A gasoline powered sweater makes as much sense.

If you are determined, take a look at the source for a package called "spkez" (if you can find it). It is 8088 assembler code for programming the square wave generator that drives the speaker on PC-style motherboards.
