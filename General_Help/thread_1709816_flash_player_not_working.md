### Post by gatman3 on 2011-03-29
I ran into problems with Flash on Youtube, and found advice elsewhere that suggested disabling hardware acceleration in Flash.  In what I've read, there is some suspicion that Youtube recently started using hardware acceleration in Flash by default, which is not supported by the Linux Flash plugin.

To disable Flash hardware acceleration, you need to go to a site with Flash content that does not use hardware acceleration, and then right-click in the Flash window, select Settings, then uncheck "Enable Hardware Acceleration".  Then go back and try Youtube again.  Worked for me.
