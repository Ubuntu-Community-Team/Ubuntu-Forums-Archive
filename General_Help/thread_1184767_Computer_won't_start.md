### Post by synapsys on 2009-06-11
First check all power cords coming from power supply to make sure you didn't unplug anything on accident. You may have unplugged the power to cpu. Then check RAM, make sure it's seated properly. Test the power supply with a power supply tester if you have one. Sometimes it puts out enough power for the fans and nothing else. Also make sure you have a pc speaker so that you will hear bios error codes if there are any.

Hopefully you haven't ruined the motherboard in the cleaning process......
