### Post by ziekfiguur on 2010-12-29
I recently found out about Prey (preyproject.com). It's an open-source program that you can activate online if your computer gets stolen.
It will than try to gather information about its location (and thus the thiefs location) and can even make picuters of the thief (if you have a webcam).
The report is then send to you, so you can inform the police to in order to get you computer back.

That's all very great, but I am rather paranoid, and have some mixed feelings about installing spying software on my laptop.
So, I would like to know, did you know about this program, and do you trust it? (and why?) Or do you even use it?
