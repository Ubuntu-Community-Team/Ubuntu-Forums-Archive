### Post by Revolutionary101 on 2010-07-07
Well not all thumbnails have an original picture that is larger than the thumbnails themselves. Some websites give you a thumbnail that is the same exact size as the original picture. When a website does this there is no way to get a larger picture.

Other websites will give you a thumbnail that is scaled down from the original image. You can download these larger images by clicking on the picture and it will take you to the original image. Then just right click it and save the picture.
