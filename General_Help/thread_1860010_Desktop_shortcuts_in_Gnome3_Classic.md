### Post by alex2112 on 2011-10-14
I had the same problem but instead of firefox it was the Disk Usage Analyzer program kept coming up. Right click on any folder inside Nautilus, choose "Open with other application...", right click on every item and choose "Forget association" except for "Files". That did the trick for me. Hope this helps.
