### Post by RonJohnson on 2006-02-13
I personally love my Dell Inspiron 8600 running Ubuntu. Everything was recognized during installation except for my wireless card. I have a TrueMobile 1400 which uses a Broadcom chipset. I was unable to get ndiswrapper working on it with Breezy so I purchased a D-Link DWL-G630 that works great. If I were purchasing a new lappy then the only concern I would have is the wireless device. Research which ones work and which ones do not.

I think you'll be very happy with nearly any laptop you purchase!
