I found it in the forums already.  For anyone in the future who has this problem:

1) goto top taskbar and right click.
2) In the top box that appears type 'Notification Area' , btw it popped up for me after the first 4 characters.
3) Press 'Add' on the bottom 
4) Like magic it appears before your eyes...
