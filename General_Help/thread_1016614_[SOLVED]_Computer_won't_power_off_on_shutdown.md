### Post by hivelocitydd on 2008-12-20
The symptom of this particular bug, failure to power off when shutting down, is specific to the hardware and BIOS used. So while many people may have the same symptom each should be a separate bug. The bug report should contain the information requested at [http://wiki.ubuntu.com/KernelTeamBugPolicies](http://wiki.ubuntu.com/KernelTeamBugPolicies) and the output of 'sudo dmidecode'. A new bug report's title should contain information about either your motherboard or system make and model.
