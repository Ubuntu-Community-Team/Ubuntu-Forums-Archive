### Post by ken_34 on 2009-02-19
Did you start with vista installed and then added Ubuntu on another partition? And now you can no longer boot to your vista partition? If that is case the grub (ubuntu boot loader) does not properly recognize your Vista partition. To fix this, boot from your vista installation cd and choose the repair option instead of install. This will hopefully repair your vista startup.
