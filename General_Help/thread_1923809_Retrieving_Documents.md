The easiest way to get files is to create a bootable USB, or a Live CD and boot your computer from that, then mount the drive that holds your documents and copy them to another drive. 

Probably the toughest lesson we all have to learn is the importance of backing up our files - regularly - in case something goes wrong...as it almost certainly will at some point. Backing up is a good habit to cultivate.  

Hopefully you will be able to retreive all your files, this time.

:)

[Edit: Here's where to find all the info about creating a live CD or USB drive [http://www.ubuntu.com/download/ubuntu/download](http://www.ubuntu.com/download/ubuntu/download)
