### Post by elmer_42 on 2008-10-04
If you have one panel, do as scouser said and make a new one. If both panels are gone, try running [FONT="Courier New"]gnome-panel[/FONT] in the terminal and seeing if they reappear.

### Post by lswb on 2008-10-04
