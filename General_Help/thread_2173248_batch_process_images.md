### Post by ian-weisser on 2013-09-08
Imagemagick ( [http://www.imagemagick.org/script/command-line-tools.php](http://www.imagemagick.org/script/command-line-tools.php) ) has a robust command-line interface, does batching, and -since it's part of the default Ubuntu install- is probably already on your system. 

Specifically, take a look at **man convert**
