Then, once gdm starts and I expect to be presented with the login screen with multiple users, the screen is totally dark. By chance, under the reflection of a light source, I noticed the white box where gdm is expecting a password - but the box is very very faint. After login, I can go to System Settings to change the brightness of my screen, but that is only for that login session; on reboot, the same problem appears. 

I am running Ubuntu 11.10 64 bit, with all updates and patches applied.
