I'm not exactly a pro at this, but maybe I can help.

First of all, you need to know that you can't jump from 7.04 to 9.10 easily... 

You'll either have to step your way there (upgrade to the next version (Gibbon?), then the next (hardy), then the next(intrepid), etc... it's a pain, but it works.)

Or make sure your /home directory is on a different partition and "do the windows" aka wipe and install.

Ok, now getting the upgrades... I'd recommend cd-rw's for this task as they're less of a waste, and download them via bittorrent. Make sure you grab the "Alternate" version - the normal version doesn't clue into the upgrade process.

I have no clue if you're running into a specific issue, and probably couldn't help you beyond the general / basics, but at least now you have a place to begin.
