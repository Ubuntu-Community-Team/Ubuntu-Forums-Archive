### Post by carlwsnyder on 2013-10-31
If you are wiping the entire drive, DBAN - Derik's Boot And Nuke.
If you are wiping data, Eraser may be the goal of your seeking.
If you are simply removing Ubuntu for use again, you may just want to re-partition the drive.

### Post by tgalati4 on 2013-11-01
The best part of UBCD is the collection of Manufacturer's low-level disk utilities.  You can do low-level formats and wipes through them as well as get more detailed health status information.  It makes a convenient package when building garage/curbside PC's.
