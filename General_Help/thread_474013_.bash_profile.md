### Post by tgalati4 on 2007-06-15
man rbash

You could create a .bash_profile that contains aliases that change the standard commands:

alias rm "Sorry You Can't Do That!"

Most commands are harmless in userspace without root privileges.  Of course the user should always be able to control his/her environment.  Taking away that ability can sometimes cause more problems than you can imagine.

Perhaps you can share with us what you are trying to accomplish;  there may be better ways to do it.
