### Post by foolsh on 2006-02-16
```
Section "Screen"
Identifier "Screen 0"
Device "NVIDIA 0"
Monitor "Generic Monitor"
DefaultDepth 24
SubSection "Display"
Depth 24
Modes "1280x720"
EndSubSection
EndSection

Section "Screen"
Identifier "Screen 1"
Device "NVIDIA 1"
Monitor "HDTV"
DefaultDepth 24
SubSection "Display"
Depth 24
Modes "1280x720"
EndSubSection
EndSection
```

in these two sections are the arguments to set the resolution for the individual monitors 
sometimes just changing the Modes value doesn't work if the Horizsync value, in the monitor section, is to low
make sure any changes you make are supported by your monitor
