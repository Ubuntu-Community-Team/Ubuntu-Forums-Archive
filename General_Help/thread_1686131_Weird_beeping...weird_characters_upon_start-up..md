### Post by AlphaLexman on 2011-02-11
I would look at the motherboard documentation, usually system beeps are clues to bios errors, power failures, or something else that is about to fail.

Listen to the beeps and count them, are they long or short, are they in a pattern three short beeps and a long one?

Whatever pattern the beeps are, are telling you the problem the motherboard is having.
