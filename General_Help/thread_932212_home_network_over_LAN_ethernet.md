### Post by howefield on 2008-09-28
Wish I could give you a more technical explanation, but if you create a folder called shared (or whatever) eg, in your home folder and right click it, then select sharing options. 

Tick the options you want, it will likely download the required files automatically. Reboot and go back and right click the folder and apply the sharing options. (It won't work without a reboot)

Your machine should now show up in your network and be seen by the other comps on your lan.
