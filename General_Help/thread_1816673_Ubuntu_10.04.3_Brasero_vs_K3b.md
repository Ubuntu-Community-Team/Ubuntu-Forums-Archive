### Post by hashky on 2011-08-02
I have 4 computers.  All are dual boot Windows XP Pro and Ubuntu 10.04. 

I have been using Brasero for burning .iso images to a CD.  Brasero works fine on 3 of the 4 computers.  On the 4th computer, I kept burning coasters.  I could burn the .iso in Windows, but not Ubuntu/Brasero.

Therefore; I knew that the burner was good.  It had to be a software or buffer problem.  I slowed the burn down to 4x and Brasero burned the .iso.

I read the forums about bugs and problems with Brasero.  Everyone recommended K3b.  I installed K3b.  What a dream.  The first time I tried to burn an .iso in K3b in Auto, K3b stated that 48x was not supported by my CD burner, and burned at a slower speed.

The K3b user interface is much cleaner and more informative.  Also, it burned in Auto with no problems.  

So if you are having problems with Brasero, get K3b.  K3b is a superior program compared to Brasero.
