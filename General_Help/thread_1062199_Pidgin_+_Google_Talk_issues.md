### Post by Antioch on 2009-02-06
Hi all. I recently tried to add google-talk to my Pidgin accounts. While it technically worked, it didn't go to well.

First of all, all of my google-talk contacts appeared in my other contact lists (which are all sorted alphabetically). I don't like this, I prefer to have each chat protocol's contact lists separate. How can I fix this?

When I do chat with a google-talk contact, many "garbage characters" appear with my name. So instead of showing:
(12:00:00 PM) Myself: Hi!
It would show:
(12:00:00 PM) Myself/D3F45GH90: Hi!
Again, I do *not* like this. How can I get rid of that garbage string from appearing?

Thank you.

---

### Post by Antioch on 2009-02-06
For those that care to know:

Pidgin automatically stacks groups (contact lists) with the same name. So, for example, if two of your groups are called "friends" they will automatically merge into one group. To have them separate, name them different things.

Also, to change what you name while chatting appears as, change the account nickname in the settings options. This will then showup after the timestamp instead of something else.
