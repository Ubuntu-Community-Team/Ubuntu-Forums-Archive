### Post by beastrace91 on 2009-08-27
Unless the sound card on your other laptop requires proprietary drivers (which shouldn't be the case for an intel card) it should just work on the other laptop. (On Linux all the open source drivers are built into the Kernel) Try running "restricted drivers" on the laptop that does not have working sound or if that doesn't work Google around for your laptop model # and see if the sound not working is a know issue on Ubuntu/nix
