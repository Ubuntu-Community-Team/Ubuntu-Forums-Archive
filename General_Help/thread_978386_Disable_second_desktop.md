### Post by eternalnewbee on 2008-11-11
In the panel, right-click on the widow switcher and change 2 into 1.

### Post by eternalnewbee on 2008-11-11
Right-click on the panel and click **Add to Panel** and scroll down. Choose **Workspace Switcher**.
