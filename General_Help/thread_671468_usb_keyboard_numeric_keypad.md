### Post by blclark on 2008-01-18
I recently installed xubuntu 7.10 on my Dell laptop with dual boot windows xp. 

I have a Dell usb keyboard with a numeric keypad at the right. 

In windows, when I plug in the usb cable the computer beeps twice, so I suspect the keyboard is two devices - keyboard and numeric keypad.

On Xubuntu the keyboard works but the numeric keypad doesn't work.

Any suggestions please?

Brian Clark

---

### Post by cdenley on 2008-01-18
Is numlock on? I think ubuntu will disable numlock by default. However, my dell keyboard has the numlock LED lit even though numlock is off. If I press numlock once, the LED stays lit, and numlock is on for real.

---

### Post by blclark on 2008-01-22
thanks - pressed numlock and it works. --Brian
