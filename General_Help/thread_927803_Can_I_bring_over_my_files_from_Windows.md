### Post by jerome1232 on 2008-09-23
Generally Cameras just hook up as 'mass storage usb devices' and are completely cross platform.

Itunes music though, if it's DRM protected may be an issue. DRM media files are encrypted and can only be read by the program that created them (in your case itunes) and there is no Itunes client for Linux yet.

If they are DRM protected there should be a way to crack it off though (At the very least burning them to a cd-rw and then rip them as any none drm protected format (flac, ogg, mp3, etc...)
