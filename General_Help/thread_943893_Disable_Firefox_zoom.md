### Post by sci-fi guy on 2008-10-10
How can I disable Firefox's page zoom (or at least easily set everything back to normal size)

---

### Post by advoss on 2008-10-10
Under the view menu there is a sub-menu for zoom where you can select zoom.  You will see there that the shortcut, Ctrl+0 will reset the zoom of the page to default.

Hope that helps

---

### Post by Sam on 2008-10-10
Go to URL "about:config", then edit "zoom.maxPercent" and "zoom.minPercent" to 100 both. Then restart Firefox.

However it doesn't work very well (on my pc it allows one step zoom in and one step zoom out)... But for you maybe this limitation will fit your needs.
