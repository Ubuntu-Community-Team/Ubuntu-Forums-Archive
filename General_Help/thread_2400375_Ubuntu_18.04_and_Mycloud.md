No clue how to help, but figured you'd like to know about this issue:

WD MyCloud is "Everyone's Cloud" now.  [https://www.theregister.co.uk/2018/09/18/remote_access_bug_turns_western_digital_my_cloud_into_everyones_cloud/](https://www.theregister.co.uk/2018/09/18/remote_access_bug_turns_western_digital_my_cloud_into_everyones_cloud/)
This happens a few times a year for these sorts of storage devices.

It isn't just WD with these issues.  Pretty much any vendor who makes it easy to access your files from anywhere has had global sharing problems.  The same applies to router-base storage.  Please don't connect storage directly to routers that face the internet unless you intend to share everyone on the storage with the world.
