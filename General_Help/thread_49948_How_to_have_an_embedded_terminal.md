### Post by frodon on 2005-07-18
What do you mean by "embedded" ?
To start with a terminal opened, just keep your terminal opened when you close your session and choose to save the configuration in the close session window. If you mean by "embedded" a terminal embedded in your wallpaper this link will interest you : [http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=38938](http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=38938)
about multimedia player I use totem-xine and really like it, I have also mplayer but only to use the mozilla-mplayer plugin for video streaming.
