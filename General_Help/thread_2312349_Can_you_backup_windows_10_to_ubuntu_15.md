### Post by Vladlenin5000 on 2016-02-03
Sorry, me neither... I've spent the best part of the last hour trying, while watching a Bill Maher stand-up comedy...

Many notebooks provide easy access to the drive (and RAM), simply by unscrewing a couple of screws then lifting up a small plastic cover at the bottom. For others like the HP 250/255 G3 entry level professional line you must disassemble almost the entire machine: Unscrew 18 screws or so from the bottom cover, then turn it up again, loose something in the hinges, decouple the screen panel, turn it upside down again and remove some circuit board connectors, up again and remove the keyboard, then some more internal connector, remove optical drive, then some more connectors, then an internal cover... You got the picture.
I'm afraid yours is the same but it's just speculation. I suggest you take a look at the bottom and check whether or not it has independent plastic covers. If so, one of them should have the HDD underneath. If not, then the above is pretty much what you should expect.
