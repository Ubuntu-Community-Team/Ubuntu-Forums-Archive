### Post by Decommissioner on 2009-05-29
Hello, I am new to the forums, just installed Jaunty a couple of days ago and I am a complete linux beginner.

Here is my problem. I installed compiz about a day after I installed ubuntu, and it's been working great for me with no glitches. Today I was messing with the general setting under my compiz setting manager, and I expanded the amount of desktops (both "horizontal virtual size" and "number of desktops") to the maximum limit. Shortly after that, when I tried to click on the other desktops in the workstation manager (located on the taskbar in the bottom right) my desktop would switch over but all my icons would be gone, as well as the taskbar on the top of the screen (housing the "applications, places, system" buttons). All other aspects of the desktop cube work fine, I can switch over with my mouse, I can zoom out and rotate my cube, and you will see all the different windows open on all sides. There is also the problem of the taskbar on the bottom displaying all the windows open on all desktops even when they are not visible to the desktop I am on. All the windows are set to "Only on this workspace"

I have already completely uninstalled all aspects of compiz, enabled metacity in the terminal to go back to 2D, and reinstalled compiz. Upon reinstallation, everything seemed to be fixed aside from a glitch which made my cube a cylinder even though I had cube selected. When I restarted my computer, the cylinder glitch was fixed as I expected, but my taskbar desktop switcher started to glitch on me again! So frustrating! Any help would be greatly appreciated, as I am a beginner and I have tried everything I could within my grasp of linux.

---

### Post by Decommissioner on 2009-05-29
Nevermind I fixed it myself. I went under General Setting in the CompizConfig Setting manager, set the Horizontal Desktop size to two, and the amount of Horizontal Desktops to 1. This seemed to fix the issue of of icons, taskbars, and desktop opacity (set under compiz) problems I had before when clicking on the other desktop on the taskbar. I then wen to Simple CompizConfig Settings manager, and changed my amount of desktops back to four, and everything is working just as they were before! So relieved.
