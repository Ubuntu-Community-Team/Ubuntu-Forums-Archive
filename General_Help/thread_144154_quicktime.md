### Post by RAOF on 2006-03-13
[QUOTE=kuyaedz]...If I remember, however, some of the quicktime trailers at apple use a new tech that isn't, as we like to call, 'open' and has to be viewed with new quicktime itself.[/QUOTE]
**None** of the quicktime trailers will be what we call "open" as in free.  They'll all be either MPEG-4 (ASP) or MPEG-4 AVC (aka h.264).  Either way, they're covered by patents.  The standard may be available (for a price), but you need to pay the patent holders to do anything about it.
