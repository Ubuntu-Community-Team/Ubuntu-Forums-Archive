### Post by bumanie on 2009-01-05
In general, HP has the best linux support for printers - [see here]("http://hplipopensource.com/hplip-web/supported_devices/index.html") for a list of supported printers. Also there is a self-extracting installer that does does a good job of ensuring all dependencies are met. If plugged in during an installation of ubuntu, HP printers are installed during the OS installation. Up to you what you get but HP and linux is a good combination.
