### Post by ankspo71 on 2011-03-05
Hi,
I think you will be needing "LVPM" to resize Wubi installations. 

For more info about resizing Wubi installations, see:
"How do I resize the virtual disks?" at [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/WubiGuide](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/WubiGuide)
Homepage of LVPM at [http://lubi.sourceforge.net/lvpm.html](http://lubi.sourceforge.net/lvpm.html)

Screenshot of LVPM resize options: 
[http://lubi.sourceforge.net/lvpm-screenshots/use/1.png](http://lubi.sourceforge.net/lvpm-screenshots/use/1.png)

Hope this helps.
