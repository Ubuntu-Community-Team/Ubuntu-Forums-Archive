### Post by deadflowr on 2018-06-02
There's never a terminal console icon on the desktop by default. Or at least I've never had one.
Press the super key (the windows icon key on the keyboard)  type the word *terminal* and click enter to open one.
(Or press ctrl + alt + T to open it directly)

Once open find the icon in the launcher and right click and set it to favorites.
