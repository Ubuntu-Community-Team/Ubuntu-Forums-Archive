### Post by ChadMMc on 2006-10-14
Hi again,
Ok. I just looked at it. gropernicus is part of the Assistive Technology Support.
Go to System->Preferences->Assistive Technology Support
You can disable the screen reader on login (which is the gropernicus) or even the whole support package.

That should do it. :)
