### Post by foldingstock on 2010-01-28
Really it depends on what kind of servers will be in operation. 

Basic, non-specialized knowledge should include: 

[LIST]
[*]Some knowledge of simple scripting (bash, perl, python)
[*]Knowing how to create, schedule, and edit CRON jobs
[*]Basic networking setup/routing
[*]Account creation/editing
[*]Configuring system services (goes along with scripting above)
[*]Backing up data (this is very important, also goes along with scripting)
[*]Filesystem structure
[*]Good knowledge of permissions and access control 
[/LIST]

I believe it is important to know how to do all of the above from a console/ssh session.
