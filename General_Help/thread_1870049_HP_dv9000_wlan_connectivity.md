### Post by crutch145 on 2011-10-29
Just an update to this question.  I re-installed an older version of Ubuntu (10.04) and the WLAN card works great now.  All I had to do was activate the drivers in the Additional Hardware/Drivers section.  It is odd that it will not work in version 11.10.  I wonder if I upgrade to version 11.10 from 10.04, if the WLAN card will still work?  I was helping a friend, and he now has the laptop... so whether he decides to do this is up to him.
