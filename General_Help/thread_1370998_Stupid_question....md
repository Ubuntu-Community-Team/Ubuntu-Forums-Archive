### Post by todak on 2010-01-02
Select the files you want to delete and then hold down the **Shift** while pressing the **Delete** key. Or you can open your home folder and click on **Edit> Preferences** and go to the **Behavior** tab and check the **Include a Delete command that bypasses Trash** box. Then when you right-click on a file, select the **Delete** command and off into the digital toilet it goes!
