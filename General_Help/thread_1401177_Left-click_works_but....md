I'm embarrassed to admit that I hadn't tried that.  :)   I plugged in a USB mouse and it works fine.  My laptop has a touchpad with right/left mouse buttons above and below the touchpad.  The primary buttons (below the touchpad) exhibit the issue I described.  The ones above it, touchpad tap, and external mouse work fine. ### Post by mojojones on 2010-02-07
Thank you, Michael -- that did the trick.  In the Touchpad settings, it defaulted to "Edge Scrolling", which appears to treat the left mouse button like pushing the scroll-wheel on a mouse, locking it.  I disabled that and all is great -- thank you again!
