### Post by Jacobonbuntu on 2011-11-25
Hi halovivek!

to install guest additions, run your Virtual machine, choose from the Virtulabox menu "devices" (I believe that's how it is called; I have the Dutch version, then it is called "apparaten") and choose "install guest additions" (see screenhot).

then go to my computer (in the windows machine :)) and open (if necessary) the "Virtualbox guest additions"

to add a shared folder, click on your virtual machine in the Virtualbox Manager window (your virtual machine should not run), choose "settings". in the left section of the settings window, choose "shared folders" and define your shared folders :)
