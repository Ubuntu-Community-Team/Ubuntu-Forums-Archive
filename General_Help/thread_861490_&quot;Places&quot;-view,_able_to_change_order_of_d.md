### Post by DJ_Peng on 2008-12-29
The Bookmarks portion of the Places menu can be ordered manually. Open Nautilus and select *Bookmarks > Edit Bookmarks...* and you can drag the items up and down the list until they're in an order that makes sense to you. As far as the order of the mounted partitions goes, I have no clue how that's ordered.
