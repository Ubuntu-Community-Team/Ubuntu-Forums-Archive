### Post by cj13579 on 2010-11-03
It sounds like you have got your account set up as a POP account. In evolution got to Edit>Preferences>Mail Accounts and look under protocol under the list. If it says POP there is the reason. This setting, unless other wised specified will download all your mail to your computer.

I believe there is an option that allows you to "leave a copy on the server" which will allow you to look at your mail from elsewhere. If your mail provider supports it, I reccommend using IMAP.

### Post by cj13579 on 2010-11-03
Just had a quick google about it and it looks like at the moment there is not IMAP support unless you have an account that ends in yahoo.com.

In which case, if you are going to want to check your mail from elsewhere you will need to enable the "leave messages on server" option.

In Evolution goto Edit > Preferences > Mail Accounts and select your account. Click Edit. Under the receiving options tab make sure that you put a tick in the box next to "leave messages on server". Unfortunately this won't put all your mail back but any new messages that you download will be left on the server allowing you to access you mail via webmail etc.

Hope this helps
