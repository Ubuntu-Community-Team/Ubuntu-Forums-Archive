### Post by Sef on 2009-05-29
Dell printers are rebranded Lexmarks.   Lexmark is not known for being Linux friendly.

OpenPrinting says the [940]("http://openprinting.org/show_printer.cgi?recnum=Dell-Photo_AIO_Printer_940") and [944]("http://openprinting.org/show_printer.cgi?recnum=Dell-Photo_AIO_Printer_944") are paperweights, so your 942 (not listed) would most likely be one too.

Your options are

1) Buy a new printer that is Linux friendly, like HP.

2) Get an old P3 with XP and use it as a print server.
