### Post by strabes on 2006-11-30
the scrollwheel is the middle button. Besides scrolling, it also is a button if you press it down. If you don't have a scroll wheel, you can click the left and right buttons at the same time to 'emulate' a middle click. Or you can right click on empty space in the panel and go to properties, then go to the general tab, an then choose Orientation: Top.
