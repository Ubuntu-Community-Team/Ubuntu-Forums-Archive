### Post by Rocket2DMn on 2009-08-16
I learn a lot from practice, and a number of years of accumulating knowledge using unix and linux systems.  However, Ubuntu does a pretty good job of keeping documentation.  Official system docs are available on your system in System->Help & Support and are available at [https://help.ubuntu.com](https://help.ubuntu.com)

The community maintained wiki is at [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/) and is always under constant development.  I often google "site:help.ubuntu.com my query here" to find stuff in the system docs and on the community docs that relate to what I'm looking for.

And finally, these forums :)  Many of the users here have a large repository of knowledge and favorite resources on different subjects that they are happy to share.
