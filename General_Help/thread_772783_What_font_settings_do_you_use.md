### Post by Ralphie on 2008-04-28
I use whatever is the default, and I don't find the fonts to be blurry.

Are other things blurry for you or just the fonts?

If just the fonts are blurry, you might be seeing the anti-aliasing, which as I remember was not set up by default on XP. When I first turned it on in XP, I remember it looking strange, but I soon got used to it.

Ubuntu does a much better job of anti-aliasing, and I've never noticed it at all.
