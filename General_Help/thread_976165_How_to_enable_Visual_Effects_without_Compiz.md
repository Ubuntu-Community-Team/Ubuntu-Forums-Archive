Compiz is the simple effects. It is installed by default. Check your driver is enabled like eternalnewbee said, what Nvidia card do you have?

If you can't enable Compiz you can get basic visual effects (shadows and compositing for things that require it) by enabling compositing in Metacity.

### Post by ad_267 on 2008-11-09
Well if you've uninstalled compiz then you can't enable the visual effects. It's compiz that provides those. 

See this if you want to enable compositing in Metacity:
[http://tombuntu.com/index.php/2008/03/31/enable-metacity-compositing-in-gnome-222/](http://tombuntu.com/index.php/2008/03/31/enable-metacity-compositing-in-gnome-222/)

That will give you shadows around your windows and compositing for applications like AWN or gnome-do and Alt-Tabbing. If you want to be able to select normal effects then you have to reinstall compiz.
