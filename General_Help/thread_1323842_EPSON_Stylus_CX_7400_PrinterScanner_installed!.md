Hey guys, how to get my Epson scanner working.

Mainly just a reference.

Goto:
[http://www.avasys.jp/lx-bin2/linux_e/spc/DL1.do](http://www.avasys.jp/lx-bin2/linux_e/spc/DL1.do)
Click the radio next to your model scroll down to the bottom fill out the "Questionnaire" -- regarding your system infos.

You will then be presented with another page.
Scroll down to:
"DEB 32bit package [libltdl7] (for Ubuntu 8.10 or later)"
Downloaded the package > installed!
Working!

Imagescan is rather slow but XSANE works great and much faster.
