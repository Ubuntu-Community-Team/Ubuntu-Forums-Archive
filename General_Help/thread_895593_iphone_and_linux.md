### Post by Zorael on 2008-08-20
You'll need iTunes to do that. I believe some virtual machine servers have proper USB support, likely vmware which isn't free though. VirtualBox should likely get it soon. Anyway, if you have a Windows/Mac box nearby, the easiest way is to do it the normal way instead.

Beware though, it's impossible to transfer songs to v2.0x iPhones in Linux. See [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/PortableDevices/iPhone#Firmware%202.0%20and%202.0.1%20will%20not%20sync](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/PortableDevices/iPhone#Firmware%202.0%20and%202.0.1%20will%20not%20sync).
