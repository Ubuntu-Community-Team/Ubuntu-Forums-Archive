### Post by borth92 on 2010-08-02
is there a way to upgrade to 64-bit without losing everything in a clean install?

---

### Post by chrisinspace on 2010-08-02
No.  You have to reinstall.  You can, however, back up your home folder first and then restore that after your installation is complete.  You can also [save your Synaptic markings]("http://www.ubuntugeek.com/howto-reinstall-all-of-currently-installed-packages-in-fresh-ubuntu-install.html") and then import them again to reinstall all of the same software from your original build.
