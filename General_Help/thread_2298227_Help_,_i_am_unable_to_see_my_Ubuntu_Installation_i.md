### Post by oldfred on 2015-10-10
For Boot-Repair to work correctly it needs to know you have the separate /boot partition, tick the box and mount the encrypted partition so it can see fstab and other data. Then it will also tell you that you have a /boot partition.
You may have to manually mount encrypted partition and give pass phase as Boot-Repair may not be able to mount and ask for pass phrase.
