### Post by expatCM on 2007-08-16
If it was me I would change the power supply.  

You know there may be a problem with it since you detected a smell.  Power supplies are often poor quality and may fail partially, completely or at random.  The problem is that when they fail there can be a power surge which trashes the motherboard and / or chip.  This does not appear to be your problem yet and so it may be a good idea to change the power supply as a precaution if nothing else, they are not expensive.

I guess you are not going to attempt to use the front USB ports again since you know that they cause problems now.

I would also discuss the cause of the problem with my son ......  :)
