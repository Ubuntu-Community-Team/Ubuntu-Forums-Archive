### Post by Spice Weasel on 2010-08-20
Clear your cache. In Firefox you can do this by pressing Ctrl+Shift+Delete, selecting everything and clicking on details. Make sure the only boxes that are ticked are "Cache" and "Site Preferences".

It might be possible that the Google page is zoomed in. While at Google, hold down your Ctrl button and press the + and - keys to zoom in and out. You can also do this with the Ctrl button and scrolling the wheel on a mouse.
