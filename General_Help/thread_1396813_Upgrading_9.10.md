### Post by howefield on 2010-02-02
What version are you on now ?

You will need to go through each intermediate release from your current version, the upgrade in any case should keep your settings and applications intact.  

However, it makes sense to back up all your data before hand, and also your home folder. Just in case.

FWIW, the upgrade should work well enough, but I can't type this reply without recommending a clean install for best results.
