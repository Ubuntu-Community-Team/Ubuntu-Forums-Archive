### Post by Mark Phelps on 2010-05-30
It may not be the Wine version that's the problem ...

If you're going to mess around with Wine, you need to go to the WineHQ site first and look up your app to read about compatibility and testing problems.  The link below is for that app:

[http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=application&iId=2113]("http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=application&iId=2113")

If you click the test results at the right, you'll see the detail results, which in this case, are NOT encouraging.  You'll notice of the three results, two are rated Bronze -- meaning, the app will barely work at all.

Wine is NOT the "miracle cure" that lots of folks make it out to be!
