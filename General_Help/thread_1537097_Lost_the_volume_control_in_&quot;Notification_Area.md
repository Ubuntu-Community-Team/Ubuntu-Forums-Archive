### Post by hhh on 2010-07-23
Right click on the panel you want this applet on, choose "Add to Panel...", scroll down and choose "Indicator Applet", click it and click "Add".

To remove just the envelope icon from your panel go to System>Administration>Synaptic Package Manager, enter your password, enter "evolution" in the Quick search field, right-click on "evolution-indicator", mark it for removal and click the Apply check mark on the top toolbar. Log out and back in and it will be gone.

Hope that helps.
