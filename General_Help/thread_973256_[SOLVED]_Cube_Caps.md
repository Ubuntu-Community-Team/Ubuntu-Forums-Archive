### Post by n00g33 on 2008-11-06
Read this thread [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=939998&highlight=compiz+cube+caps]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=939998&highlight=compiz+cube+caps")

In CCSM its under effects. Cube reflection and deformation.

Enable it and go to the deformation tab and set deformation to none so you still have your cube instead of a cylinder.

You can unable reflection too in that tab if you don't want it.

Yeah it's weird how theres no bottom caps preference in rotate cube though.
