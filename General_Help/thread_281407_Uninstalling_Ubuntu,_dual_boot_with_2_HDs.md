### Post by nyinge on 2006-10-21
If I could remember correctly...

-right_click on "My Computer" and choose "Manage"
-in the left pane, choose "Disks" or something similar, but not defragmenter
-you then will see your 2nd HD (~40GB), possibly with "Unknown Partition" or similar.  There could be 2 partitions, one for root and another for swap.
-right-click on them and reformat to your desired partition.

Hope this helps.
