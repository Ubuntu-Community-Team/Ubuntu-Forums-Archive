### Post by Aearenda on 2009-10-17
The simple answer is yes, you can do all this, but not necessarily 'out of the box'. The details vary according to what hardware you have and how far you are willing to go with customisation. I suggest you search these forums for threads relevant to the particular hardware you are considering. I am using a HP TC1100 tablet to write this, which is no longer available, but I can handwrite and rotate quite easily.

Also search for threads about cellwriter and xournal.

### Post by Aearenda on 2009-10-17
Just one thing comes to mind that isn't so easy, AFAIK: handwriting conversion to text, as in OneNote for example. Cellwriter lets you print characters for instant conversion; xournal lets you take handwritten notes as if on paper, but leaves it as a graphic. Just thought I'd add that in case instant conversion of flowing handwriting is important to you. I never found that accurate enough to bother with on Windows anyway.
