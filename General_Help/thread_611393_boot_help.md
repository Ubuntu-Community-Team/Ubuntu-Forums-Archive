### Post by taurus on 2007-11-12

### Post by taurus on 2007-11-12
When you first turn on your machine, there should be a quick message either at the bottom or on the top right corner telling you what key to press to get into the BIOS.  Usually either F2, Del, or F12.  So, start pressing F2 when you first turn it on and if it doesn't work, try Del next time.
