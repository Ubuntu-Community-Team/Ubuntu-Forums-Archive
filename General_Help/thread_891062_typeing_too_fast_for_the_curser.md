### Post by wilbbe01 on 2008-08-15
Try opening up an open office word document or something.  Type a line, then click somewhere in the left bottom of the text area, and try clicking around in different spots, I think you will find the cursor will move whether you click right on the line or not.  A lot of touch pads have a little button above them to dissable the thing while typing, but then again some don't.  If you have a button there you could always try pressing it while typing documents.
