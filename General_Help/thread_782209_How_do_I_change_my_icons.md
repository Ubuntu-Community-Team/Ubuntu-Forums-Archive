### Post by RAOF on 2008-05-04
Presuming that you're running Ubuntu (not Kubuntu or Xubuntu or whatever), System->Preferences->Appearance is pretty much a one-stop-shop for theming.

In your case, you'll want to hit the "Customise" button down the bottom.  This will edit your current theme, and give you a window with options for changing the window border, the application (gtk) theme, the colours, the mouse theme and the icon theme :).
