### Post by speartip on 2014-10-27
Hi Timmoore001,
2 things you can try.
1st. For large files, that you want to delete, open up your home folder, click edit - preferences - behavoir, & tick the box "include a delete command....."
you should now be able to directly delete large files without sending them to the rubbish bin.

2nd. If you have sent files to the rubbish bin from an external drive, then that drive has to be on & mounted before you can empty those files from the rubbish bin.
Hope this helps.
