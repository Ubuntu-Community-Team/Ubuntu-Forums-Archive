### Post by jakupl on 2011-02-28
The packages should be installed allready. Just go into System --> Preferences --> Remote desktop, on the computer that you want to log into, and enable remote login;

Then switch computers, and go to Applications --> Internet --> Remote Desktop Viewer, and connect to [s]****US****ERNAM****E@****192****.168.IPADDRESS.
Write your username on the other computer before the @ and your internal ip address after the @[/s]
the same as you do when you use ssh.
