### Post by Seanuntu on 2006-01-28
To elaborate further, this is the path I would take...

Step 1:  Make 3 identical copies of the picture within Ubuntu File Manager
Step 2:  Open the first of your 3 files and drag a box around the first pic and then use the crop tool. (if the pic is not perfectly horizontal, you won't be able to crop completely)
Step 3:  Rotate into desired position.
Step 4:  Crop again, this time leaving no white space.
Repeat Steps 2 through 4 for each of the remaining files you created in Step 1.

Hope this helps.
