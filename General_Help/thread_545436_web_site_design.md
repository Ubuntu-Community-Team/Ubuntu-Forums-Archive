### Post by p_quarles on 2007-09-07
Screem is a good choice, and it does have an upload feature.

IMO, Screem is the best of the web site composers/managers, in terms of design features and site management. But it doesn't come with a WYSIWYG editor like the ones included in FrontPage/Dreamweaver. This was done deliberately on the part of the developer. WYSIWYG editors are notorious for producing awful code. It does have an internal "preview" view, so you can see what you're doing, of course.

If you think you do need a fully graphical editor, your best bets are Bluefish and Quanta Plus.
