### Post by dale.walker on 2007-04-02
I have recently installed Ubuntu version 6.10 on my T43p and my bluetooth basically worked out of the box. Needed some additional configeration in order to pair my laptop with my phone for dial up networking but that was about it.

One thing you may want to ensure is that the bluetooth device is enabled on your machine. This can be turned on and off by using the FN +F8 button on the T43p.
