### Post by RedSingularity on 2009-12-30
In the part that says 

SubSection     "Display"
        Depth       24
        Modes      "nvidia-auto-select"
    EndSubSection

Edit this to it,

SubSection     "Display"
        Depth       24
        Modes      "800x600" "1920x1080"
    EndSubSection

Then reboot and see if you can select the mode you want in nvidia-settings
