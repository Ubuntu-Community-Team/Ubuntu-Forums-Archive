### Post by libssd on 2010-11-22
Note the disclaimer: **"Building and using a custom kernel will make it very difficult to get support for your system."**

When I was running 9.04, I used a later kernel to work around a suspend problem; it introduced another, subtle problem that wasn't evident until I tried to restore my system from a backup.

I'm not trying to discourage you from experimentation, but **ALWAYS** have a (proven) backup available if you decide to go messing around with things like custom kernels filesystem tuning, etc. I have learned more from my disasters than from things that went well on the first try.
