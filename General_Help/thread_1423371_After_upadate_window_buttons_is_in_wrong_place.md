### Post by ajgreeny on 2010-03-06
There have been other posts about this, so do a search and you'll find that you can change it back quickly and easily in gconf-editor ->apps ->metacity ->general ->button_layout and make the entry for that **menu:minimize,maximize,close**

The colon must be where I show it and the other buttons separated by commas.
