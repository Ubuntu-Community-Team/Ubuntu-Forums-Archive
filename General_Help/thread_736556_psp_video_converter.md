### Post by Pijits_1 on 2008-03-26
A good video converter I use is Pitivi Video converter

```
sudo apt-get instal Cinelerral 
```
or browse the Add remove programs (I recommend this way)

it downloads a few other editing tools such as a paint and another video making program.

Although it doesn't give a projected size (actually some movies i've converted got bigger :S) it does the job quite well. I know you can convert to Ogg, Mpeg, Avi, MP4... theres lots to choose from. You can also choose the screen resolution of the files. Really cool program
