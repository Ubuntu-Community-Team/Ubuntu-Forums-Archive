### Post by bruenig on 2007-01-22
Making menus is tough. I tried to figure it out from the command line with dvdauthor I think and couldn't get it going. I know devede is a good app for making dvds from video files.
```
sudo apt-get install devede
```
Also for good results on the forums, it is important that you know the difference between burning or writing a dvd and making a dvd. Because burning is extremely simple it is creating the iso where the problem is and from what I have seen those two jobs are generally covered by two different apps.
