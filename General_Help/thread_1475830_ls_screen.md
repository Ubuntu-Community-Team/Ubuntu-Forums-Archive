### Post by StuartN on 2010-05-07
> **Directive 4 said:**
> is there any info that will tell me if the repair shop switched my screen to another one instead of fixing it?>>

hwinfo (in the standard repositories) might print a serial number, and your brand of lcd panel might have one.

I get "Unique ID: rdCR.EY_qmtb9YY0" for mine, but a search returns many hits to "Unique ID: rdCR.EY_qmtb9YY0 Hardware Class: monitor Model:", so it is not actually a serial number.

An alternative is an anti-theft UV pen and light, but you would probably need to find a bit of the panel that is not going to be cleaned and is firmly attached (i.e. inside the display casing).
