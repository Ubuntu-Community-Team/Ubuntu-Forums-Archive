### Post by Pelekophori on 2007-02-01
I looked for something similar last summer but unfortunately also couldn't find the kind of program you describe.  It seems to be an odd gap in the Foss market  

The solution I adopted instead was to buy an external HD (which are quite cheap these days) and dedicate it to storing backups.  As well as being easy to backup to, the HD has the advantage of being much quicker than writing multiple CDRs when backing up a large amount of data.
