### Post by biebel on 2009-09-13
I had the same problem with super grub disk. The grub on it was not yet supporting ext4.

Just mount the usb stick and upgrade the grub on it:
(supposing it is /dev/sdg and mounted on /media/usb)
```

sudo grub-install /dev/sdg --root-directory=/media/usb --recheck

```

That should do the trick.
