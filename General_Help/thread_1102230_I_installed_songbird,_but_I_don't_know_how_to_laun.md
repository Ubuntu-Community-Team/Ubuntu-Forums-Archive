### Post by zvacet on 2009-03-21
For launching from terminal I think it is enough to type name of package.To add it to hte program menu go to the system>admin>main menu choose on the left **sound & video** then on the right choose **new item**.

1. name = songbird
2.command = /usr/bin/songbird   (this is in most cases so you need to browse)
3.icon = usr/share/pixmaps
