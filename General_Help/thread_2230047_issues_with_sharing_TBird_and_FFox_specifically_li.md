### Post by vanadium on 2014-06-17
I think you must make sure to use exactly the same version number of Thunderbird and Firefox both in Windows and in Linux to avoid this compatibility check. Alternatively, and better I feel, is to selectively share only your specific user data (mails, calendars, bookmarks), and keep the addons specific to the Windows or Linux version.
