### Post by Mark Phelps on 2012-01-26
Since you are apparently trying to do this in Win7, then you should go to a Win7 forum for your answer -- recommend sevenforums.com

But ... I will tell you in advance that there is no way you can "turn off the power" to your graphics card, short of removing it from the computer.  It gets some power through the main bus, and you can't disable that.
