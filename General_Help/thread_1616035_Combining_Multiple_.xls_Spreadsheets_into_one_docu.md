### Post by corrytonapple on 2010-11-07
Welcome to the forums! :)
Although I do not know what the Spreadsheet program in Lubuntu is, I can give you some General help and it should work. Open the spreadsheet you want all the text combined in to and the spreadsheet you are importing text from. Copy all the text from the spreadsheet you are exporting from by dragging your cursor and it should highlight all of the cells you want copied. Then make a new tab on the import spreadsheet, and paste all the data you got from your export spreadsheet. Do that for each document, and you are good. Only takes 20 seconds per spreadsheet too. :)
