### Post by TorontoStorm on 2007-12-01
I'd just like to share how I got Wubi 7.10 Gutsy to install (didn't do anything special though):

1) Using Vista's built-in partitioner, I made a 50GB partition for Wubi and and formatted and labelled it W: (for **W**ubi :))
2) Manually downloaded the 7.10 desktop .iso and put in into the the same directory as the Wubi 7.10 installer
3) Ran the Wubi installer and made a 30BG install on W:
4) Rebooted into Wubi 7.10 Gutsy
5) It ran the graphical installer.
6) Rebooted into Wubi 7.10 Gutsy

I'm writing this post from Gutsy right now, and it works without flaws. Great job, Ago!
