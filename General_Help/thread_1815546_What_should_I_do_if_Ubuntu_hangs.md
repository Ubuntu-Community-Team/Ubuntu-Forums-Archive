### Post by AlphaLexman on 2011-08-01
There is a 'magic' sequence of key-presses that the kernel can interpret. It will safely un-hang a seemingly frozen *nix machine.

See [http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Magic_SysRq_key]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Magic_SysRq_key")
