### Post by oleink on 2010-03-25
Sometimes when saving in rtf if you have something formated (bullets and/or numbering) You will lose bullets and numbering.  I had instances of saving tables that are stored in openoffice in rtf format and the words are either jumbled or like mixed together.  My table went from having bullets and descriptions to having 1 bullet in each and having a phrase like "Robert E Lee was the General of the Confederate army in Gettysburg" turning into "berRt Eo eLe swa he teneralG ot fhe Cfnedatero mrya n Gtyteisbrgu"
