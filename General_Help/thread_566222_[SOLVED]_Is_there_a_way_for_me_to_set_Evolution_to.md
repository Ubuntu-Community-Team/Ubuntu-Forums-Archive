### Post by wersdaluv on 2007-10-03
Whenever I create an appointment in Evolution, I prefer to have alarms for appointments so I always define a "15 mins before the appointment" alarm. I do not want to do this over and over again every time I create appointments.

Is there such a way for me to set Evolution to automatically add "15 mins before appointment" alarms for Every appointment that I create?

---

### Post by praet on 2007-10-03
Edit > Preferences > Calendar and Tasks, General Tab

On the bottom there is an option under alerts to Show a reminder before every appointment.
Change that to 15 minutes.

Now when you create a new appointment, a default alarm 15 minutes before the appointment time will exist.  You can override it in the alarms setting of the appointment, as well as remove the alarm completely.
