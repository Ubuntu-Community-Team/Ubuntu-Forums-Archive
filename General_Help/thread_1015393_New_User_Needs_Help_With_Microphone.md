### Post by spcwingo on 2008-12-18
I believe the mic is muted by default.  Try double-clicking the speaker icon in your tray.  It sholud bring up alsa mixer.  From there you can adjust the volume, unmute, etc.  That should get it working for you...but...Wine is kind of hit and miss with sound, especially mics.
