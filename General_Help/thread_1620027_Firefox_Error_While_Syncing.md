### Post by nucleuskore on 2011-01-24
I had the same problem and was racking my brains. I have found the problem to be related to the sync key.

**Solution:**
[list][*]In firefox click on tools-> Addons
[*]Select firefox sync and click preferences
[*]Click on Manage account
[*]Click on My sync key
[*]Click Generate
[*]Click cancel
[*]Now against my sync key, there will be a message saying update sync key. Click on that and enter your actual sync key (old users have a passphrase). [/list]
Save it and your done !
