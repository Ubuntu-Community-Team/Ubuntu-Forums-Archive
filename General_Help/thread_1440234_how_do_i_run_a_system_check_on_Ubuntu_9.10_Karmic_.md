> **shayli147 said:**

What is the second option? How do I do it?The second option does a brief check on your computer. What you do is reboot. When your computer shows the BIOS screen, hold down the "SHIFT" key. This will bring up the GRUB menu. From the GRUB menu, select your current kernel's "recovery mode" option. You'll get a menu that allows you to do various checks to your system.
