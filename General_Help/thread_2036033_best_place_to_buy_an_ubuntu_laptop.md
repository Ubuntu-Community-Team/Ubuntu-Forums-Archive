### Post by rich1842 on 2012-08-01
I used to work with specification of computer equipment for disabled students and know that there are experts around (especially attached to universities and colleges in the UK) who are able to do a detailed analysis of what a particular disabled person needs. I would advise you to find such a person (a university person or your doctor/hospital/social worker) and ask for help. They may be able to think of things you may not have thought of (because of the accumulated experience they have) and they will know the best places to find the stuff. They can arrange for you to try before you buy - will probably have stuff in their place of work you can try. Special screens and talking software for visually impaired - all kinds of stuff.
