### Post by rbmorse on 2008-11-16
Open a terminal and run 

top

that will show you which process(es) are most active and may lend a hint as to what is occupying the hard drive

### Post by Russty of Oz on 2008-11-16
Thanks, I found from doing that, that it was mythtv  causing the problem.  I uninstalled mythtv and now my HD is resting peacefully  :) zzzzzzzzz
