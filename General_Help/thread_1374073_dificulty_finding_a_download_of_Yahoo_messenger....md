### Post by squaregoldfish on 2010-01-06
You can use pidgin for both your Hotmail and Yahoo accounts simultaneously.

In Pidgin, Select Accounts -> Manage Accounts, and then click Add.. in the resulting dialog.

You can then select your account type, and enter your user info.

Hope this is enough to get you going! If not, come back here....
