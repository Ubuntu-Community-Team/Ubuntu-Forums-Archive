### Post by Yellow Pasque on 2014-07-07
Looking at the changelog for 340.43, I wouldn't count on a version for Linux (it's one of those "GameReady" releases that provides special optimization for the latest game titles).

I can't say 340.17 is "safe" for everyone, but at least it's already packaged in xorg-edgers (and it works for me on my Debian system). Ultimately, it's your choice whether you want to try it and assume the risk or whether you want to wait for an official version.
