### Post by cowboy.bebop on 2015-07-12
I have seen several people with this CAPS delay issue. And I have also seen some really nasty keyboard police come in telling people how they should and should not utilize the shift and caps keys. There is no proper way to type, caps and shift have the same functionality, its how we use it and not everyone can type 'professionally' like the pros, due to handicap issues or other reasons. 

I have this issue with utilizing the caps lock and suffering it's delay. I have found a workaround or slight solution to resolve this issue for anyone still experiencing this issue. I managed to find a shell script to aid in this recovery for the delay, thanks to [ErkanMDR]("https://github.com/ErkanMDR") on GitHub. He calls it the [Linux-CAPSLOCK-Delay-Fixer]("https://github.com/ErkanMDR/Linux-CapsLock-Delay-Fixer"), and it works great. Make sure you restart the machine after you set it as a startup item in Ubuntu.

So again to all the pro kb'ers, *brushes dirt off shoulder* and to all suffering the issue, good luck to you all and enjoy =)
