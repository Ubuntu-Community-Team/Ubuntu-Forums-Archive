### Post by gh4ever on 2010-06-26
I could never get YPOPS working, but I got Yahoo to send emails to Evolution.
I don't remember the webpage where I found the trick, but basically from what I remember you change your Yahoo account to a Yahoo Asia account, then go to options, POP and forwarding, and it lets you set it up without having to sign up for a premium account.
The email client Thunderbird (sudo apt-get thunderbird) has a plugin that lets you do it, too. I never liked the Thunderbird ui, though.
The plugin is at:
[http://webmail.mozdev.org/](http://webmail.mozdev.org/)
Hope this helps!
