Sounds like you're talking about Compiz, which is preinstalled on Ubuntu. If your graphics card needs special drivers then install those first, then go to the Visual Effects tab in Appearance and choose Extra.

Now install CompizConfig Settings Manager which puts a new item into your Preferences menu, where you can customize to your heart's content.
