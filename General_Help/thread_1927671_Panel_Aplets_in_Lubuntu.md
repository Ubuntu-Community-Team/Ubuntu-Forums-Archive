### Post by Rodney9 on 2012-02-18
To move the Panel Applets

Right Click on the Panel
Select Panel Settings, that will bring up Panel Preferences
Select Panel Applets here you can Add Move Etc

For Applications on the Panel
Select Application Launch Bar
click on Edit, then you can move them up and down

Rodney

For your Wireless
