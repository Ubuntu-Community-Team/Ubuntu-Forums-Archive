### Post by Praill on 2009-01-27
I can confirm that this is really a big problem.
It seems that after upgrading to hardy, my repos were reset to some server in the United States. I live in Canada and have always used the Canadian repositories. There's actually a repo server at the university in my city.

To fix this open Synaptic. Settings -> Repositories -> Download From -> Other

Then you can even do a test for the best server for your location.
