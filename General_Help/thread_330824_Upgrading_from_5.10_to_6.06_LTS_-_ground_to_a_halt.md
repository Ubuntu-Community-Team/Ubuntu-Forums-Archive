### Post by Sef on 2007-01-11
AFIK (As far as I know), if it froze and you could not upgrade any further, you would most likely have to do a clean install, i.e. install from a disk.   This will wipe out your current settings and files if you don't have a separate home file.   Even then you should always back up your files before upgrading.
