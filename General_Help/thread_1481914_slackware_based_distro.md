> hey all. im looking for a slackware based distro that is basically recompiled and easy to install. anyone know of any?

You will find that slackware itself is quite straightforward to use, contrary to legend :). There are very explicit instructions for the installation that are quite easy to follow:

[ftp://ftp.slackware.com/pub/slackware/slackware-13.0/Slackware-HOWTO](ftp://ftp.slackware.com/pub/slackware/slackware-13.0/Slackware-HOWTO)

and the addition of extra packages has been made very easy by slackbuilds.org. My only extra suggestion is to use cfdisk rather than fdisk for partitioning...

All the best,

Andrew
