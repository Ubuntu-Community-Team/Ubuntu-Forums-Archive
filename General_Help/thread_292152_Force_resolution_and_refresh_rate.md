### Post by taurus on 2006-11-03
You can do that by modifying /etc/X11/xorg.conf.  Of course, make a backup first in case you screw something up and X would crash...

```

sudo cp /etc/X11/xorg.conf /etc/X11/xorg.conf.bak
gksudo gedit /etc/X11/xorg.conf

```
