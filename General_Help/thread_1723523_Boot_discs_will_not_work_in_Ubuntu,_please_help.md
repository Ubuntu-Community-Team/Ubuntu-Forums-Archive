### Post by plucky on 2011-04-07
> **ezza121 said:**
> How do I get onto the ubuntu bios?

It is not Ubuntu BIOS it is your Laptop Bios.

All desktops and laptops have a BIOS as it is the means of booting the operating system from cold.

On the First picture that comes up it will say something like press F2/Del/F10 or something similar to enter Bios.

Or read your User manual as that will tell you how to enter the BIOS setup page.

Do so and then change the first boot device to CDROM.

Good Luck
