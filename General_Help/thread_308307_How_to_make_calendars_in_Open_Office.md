### Post by lubeck on 2008-03-25
Hello all.
Finally found a winning approach to making calendars in OPEN OFFICE.  (The link in the 2006 posting was no longer active.)
Here are the steps - I hope understandable:
[LIST=1]
[*]Download the Calendar making Macro/Extension from [url**]http://www.ooomacros.org/user.php#217442**[/url]  and save on your Desktop or someplace you can get to.
[*]Download should provide you a "xxx.oxt" folder which Open Office will recognize (mine was called Calendrier-1.0.2.oxt)
[*]OPEN Open Office's WORD Processor.
[*]Select Tools, Extension Manager, My Extensions, ADD - then browse for and double click on the .oxt folder mentioned earlier.  This attaches the Calendar making template to Open Office.
[*]To make a Calendar, simply open a new document in Open Office, then select Tools, Add-Ons, and select the Calendar you wish to make.
[*]Note that once a Calendar is presented to you, you can change margins, fonts, colors, and anything else on the generated Calendar.
[/LIST]
Worked wonderfully for me.
Best.
