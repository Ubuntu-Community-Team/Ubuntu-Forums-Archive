### Post by bsmith1051 on 2009-10-18
Some people are having trouble with their USB ports not working in recent versions of Ubuntu, e.g. 8.10 onward.  The problems include:
[LIST]
[*]slow performance (1/10th normal speed)
[*]random disconnects
[*]ports only working momentarily, or not at all
[/LIST]

Here's a quick (mostly serious) guide to troubleshooting:
[LIST=1][*]Try more than 1 device, and also confirm that these devices work on other computers.
[*]Remove any USB hubs or splitters.
[*]Disable the built-in USB and install a USB add-on card.
[*]Buy a new motherboard.
[*]Edit your start-up configuration files and/or rebuild your kernel.
[*]Tear your hair with frustration that such a basic function doesn't work correctly and reinstall your computer with Windows.  I hear v7 is nice?
[/LIST]
