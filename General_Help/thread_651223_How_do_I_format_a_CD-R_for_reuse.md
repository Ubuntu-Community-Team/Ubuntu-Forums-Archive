### Post by twin_57103 on 2008-01-06
You cannot delete files from a CD-R, but if you leave it open (i.e. a multisession disk), you can add as often as there is still disk space.

In Brasero, there is an option to leave a session open for later additions.  It is the primary reason I use Brasero.  This is similar to the way Windows handles writing to CDs, which may be what you're referring to.  It is a small check-box near the bottom of the dialog box that appears after you click burn.
