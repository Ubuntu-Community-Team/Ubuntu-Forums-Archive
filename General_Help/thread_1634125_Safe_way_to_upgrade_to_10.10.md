### Post by Thras0 on 2010-11-30
i did an upgrade to 10.10 not so long ago because i messed up my 10.04 version.everything went well but it took forever to upgrade (might have been my internet connection, i really can't say for sure).
   i found out later what was the cause of my problem and came back to 10.04 (being a LTS and what not). proprietary ATI driver didn't work so good for my video card (HD 3650) so i left the default install driver (and all is good).
   IMO the best way is a clean install,but if you want to go with an upgrade then i don't see why it shouldn't work out fine.

**back up is always a good solution, 'cuz you never know ;)
