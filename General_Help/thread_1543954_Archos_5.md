### Post by chris.olive on 2011-01-29
Yes I have had an Archos 5 for about two weeks now and so far am pleased, though still waiting for the GPS car mount from Amazon, I have never had any freezing problems [so far].
I do find that some of the Android apps do not work but as they are mostly "toys" i.e. levels, compass etc it does not really matter.
It does work well in the car as a music player saves loading 6 Cd's in and out.
Have not got round to using Video player.
As to GPS I cannot buy the license  from the apps store, keeps asking for a discount code ? which I do not have, I hope the mount when it comes will sort that, if not a big row with Archos.
It could do with a much better e-book reader for though the screen [5"] is a bit small the pocket size with music, video, GPS, maps etc make it great for traveling. App from Kindle or someone?
As an aside I did find the mini USB mains charger for my Samsung phone also charges the Archos much faster then the supplied USB cable.
Does want to only work with Windows MP but syncs to Ubuntu 10.10 fine, but as far as I am concerned there are no music players for Linux that suit me so use WMP or Songbird in Win 7.
But so far not a bad device.

### Post by chris.olive on 2011-03-21
Now some time later two months? and somewhat less happy I got the GPS holder from an Amazon store, and it refused to load the DVD supplied also the supplied in-car power cable made listening to music impossible due to engine interference, the GPS unit was returned for a refund with no problem.
I have found that the built in GPS/Maps/ satellite locater works very poorly if at all, I am at 400m in the French country side with a very clear view to the south.
I have to say that my main complaint about Archos is the very poor service you get from the "help line" by phone or e-mail.
I also e-mailed the MD over a week ago with no reply yet.
Where as HP's MD rep[lied in two days to an inquire!
I much regret now buying this device and should have bought a Garmen GPS and perhaps the new HP tablet later this year.
Do not expect customer service from Archos.:mad:
