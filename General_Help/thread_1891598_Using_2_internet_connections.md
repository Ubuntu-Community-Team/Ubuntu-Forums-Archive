### Post by killermist on 2011-12-06
In hotspot mode, does the phone actually share itself to the using system, or just pass internet to the connected device?  If so, there should be a way to set the system to use the wired connection for internet and use the other "network" or "subnet" to communicate with the phone.

As to using both connections to "boost speed", that is a science not yet refined enough to be usable without specialized hardware (which is to say commercial/industrial routers and such).  That being said, there are ways to use both connections, but there is NO GUARANTEE of an actual increase in speed, and they are generally more trouble than they are worth.

In both of these tasks, the specifics of how-to are beyond me.  I only know that item 1 should be possible, and 2 is doable, but probably not worth it (especially if you end up charged over a certain volume by your provider, or throttled).
