### Post by Sef on 2007-04-23
System > Administration > Printing 

Specify a port (on the bottom) > Click on parallel port > Change to HP > Scroll down to Desktjet 694C > Click Forward > Fill in Description (Deskjet 694C) and Location (parallel port) > click apply.
