### Post by vishzilla on 2008-09-03
Linux is a branch of Unix. Richard Stallman introduced the GNU project to develop "free" Unix-like OS. Its only when Linus Torvalds introduced the Linux Kernel, Linux was born.

GNOME and KDE are user-interfaces: is what you see and do with the OS along with the apps they provide.
