### Post by ash211 on 2006-07-08
If your motherboard has the slots for it, sure.  They can either work together for better graphics (called Crossfire with ATI cards or SLI with NVidia's) or run more than one screen at a time if you have enough monitors for it!  Usually just one card with two outputs is a better choice though if you want a multiple monitor setup.
