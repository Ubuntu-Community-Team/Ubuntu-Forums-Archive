### Post by audiomick on 2010-04-10
Your screen shots are showing palimpest, which is the application that is known to have been reporting false "disk error" messages.
I would, in you situation, make sure that anything that is critical is backed up, and wait it out a bit longer.

You could also try running smartmontools
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Smartmontools](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Smartmontools)
on the drive. That will give you another "opinion".
