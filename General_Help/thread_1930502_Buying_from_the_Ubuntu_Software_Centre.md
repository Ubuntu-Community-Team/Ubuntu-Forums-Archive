### Post by Krytarik on 2012-02-23
This doc indicates that purchases *are* tied to your Ubuntu Single Sign-On, and that you can reinstall already purchased software, and presumably re-download ebooks, magz, and such:

[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/SoftwareCenter#Commercial_software](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/SoftwareCenter#Commercial_software)
