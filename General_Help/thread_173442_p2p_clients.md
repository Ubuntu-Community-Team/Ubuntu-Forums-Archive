### Post by Resurrection on 2006-05-10
Well if your looking for just a straight p2p type of network then Limewire is the way to go.  aMule uses ed2k magnet links, little bit of a different system.

Pesonally, I use Bittornado.  It deals with .torrent files, which is a much better system.  Azureus also works for these as well, and has much more features, but is also a bit of a resource hog IMO.

There is a variety of ways to get these, through Synaptic, Automatix, or direct download off the web.  I encourage you to read up on torrents first though prior to using if you don't understand what they are.  Again, just my opinion, but torrents are a better option to Limewire, much less fake and corrupt files to deal with.
