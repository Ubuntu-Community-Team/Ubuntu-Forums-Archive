### Post by jmacdougall.ca on 2009-01-18
Stopping by to confirm a workaround.

The problem seems to originate with either:

a) root bookmarks with long titles
b) root bookmark folders with long titles

I've alleviated the bug by moving all root bookmarks to a root folder and ensuring all folders have short (one word or two) names.

Hope this helps others.
-JM
