### Post by MartinJ on 2006-03-23
To get the drives on your desktop:
Right click on Desktop, select 'Configure Desktop', go to the Behaviour section, click on Device Icons tab, select what you do and don't want to see.

Drives and devices are also available from the System Menu (second from left on the taskbar at bottom of screen).  They're in the Storage Media section.
