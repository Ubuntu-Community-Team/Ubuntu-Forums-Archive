### Post by MaindotC on 2008-12-25
If you click on your name on the left side of the post, there should be a drop-down menu that appears and you can click "Find More Posts by <your_name>".  Also, you can go into your forum settings and set a default action to subscribe to each topic you post.  Hope that helps!
