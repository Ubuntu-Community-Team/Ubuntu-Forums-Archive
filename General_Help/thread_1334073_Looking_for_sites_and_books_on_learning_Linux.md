### Post by Minsky on 2009-11-22
If you prefer to buy a book and your interest is in using Ubuntu, then I can thoroughly recommend **Beginning Ubuntu Linux** which is published by Apress and is currently in its 4th edition. This is the first book that I bought whilst trying to get to grips with Ubuntu and I still refer to it now and then. Once you've got over the initial learning curve and want something more advanced, then I don't think you can beat **Ubuntu Unleashed**, published by Sams.
