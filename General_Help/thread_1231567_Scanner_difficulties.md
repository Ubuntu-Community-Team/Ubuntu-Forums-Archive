### Post by CatKiller on 2009-08-05
The method used to access scanners in Linux is called SANE. [This]("http://www.sane-project.org/man/sane-artec_eplus48u.5.html") is the manpage for the SANE backend for your scanner. Hopefully that will get you started. If you post back with specific steps that you're having trouble with, hopefully someone with experience of that scanner will be able to guide you through it.

You can change the registered email address in the User Control Panel, but I don't think you can change your user name. Perhaps the mods can? You could post to ask in the Forum Feedback. Since you've only got four posts under this user name, though, why not just create a new account under your name with your email address?
