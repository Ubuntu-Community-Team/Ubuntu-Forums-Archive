Right!  I followed the links and here's the tricky bits:
1. Open Firefox
2. Go to; [https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/flash-aid/](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/flash-aid/)
3. Download Flash Aid 2.2.1 (or whatever version it is)
4. There will now be flash button on the right by the bar; open it
5. Use the wizard to set up said Flash *STUFF*
6. Bob's your uncle!

Anyway, it does work just fine now!  Thanks for that!

Oh, I also found out the 'problem' with special characters not showing properly on *LibreOffice*.  There were settings for each kind of non-printing character available to show, and for some reason they were all disabled, so that was an easy fix.

Thanks again for the help on Firefox!
