### Post by ajgreeny on 2008-10-06
You must try to forget your older Windows ways, and remember that you will seldom if ever need to "download" something from the internet as you would for windows.

To get and install applications in Ubuntu simply open up **synaptic** (Package manager)or use the **Add/remove** **applications** from the menu.  These will get everything from dedicated repositories of safe and easily installed applications.  It is actually much easier than windows once you get used to the different way of doing things.
