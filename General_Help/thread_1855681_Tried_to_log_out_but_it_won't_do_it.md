### Post by ajgreeny on 2011-10-06
Do you have the quickstart libreoffice applet enabled?

There is a bug that will stop you from either shutting down, restarting or logging out if you have used that in the session from which you are attempting to logout, etc etc.

If this is your problem just right click on the quickstart icon and quit from it, then logout will work OK.
