### Post by chitowner2 on 2010-06-15
The HD-Homerun hardware with MythTV software is likely to be your best bet for a serious solution, with the caveats that, since HD-Homerun is a direct connect between your cable service and your PC, any HBO or similar subscription channels can not be viewed. 

If you are looking for an ad-hoc "VHS recorder" kind of thing you would have to pipe from your cable-box output to a TV tuner card in a PCI slot, then find some application that can record that. Again, MythTV might work, but I'm not sure about this angle.
