### Post by MilosDusan on 2007-05-01
Your standard good ole fashioned XP disk will boot directly from the CD-Rom drive if you set your BIOS to boot from CD first.. When you boot off the disk, it'll run you through the whole Windows Setup process and ask about your partitions.. Delete the partitions there, then create a new NTFS partition.. Do a NTFS Quick Format.. Once that's done, just follow ye ole *cough* friendly commands that Windows has given you to install..

Easy peasy..
