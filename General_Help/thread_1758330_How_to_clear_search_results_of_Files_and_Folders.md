The Files and Folders lens uses Zeitgeist to track your history of files opened. You can clear it as outlined in this link:

[http://www.xp4g.net/linux/how-to-clear-the-zeitgeist-history-quick-tip/](http://www.xp4g.net/linux/how-to-clear-the-zeitgeist-history-quick-tip/)
