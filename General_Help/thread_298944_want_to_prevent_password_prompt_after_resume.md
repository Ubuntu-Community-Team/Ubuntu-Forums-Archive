### Post by sitedesign on 2006-11-13
When I close the lid of my laptop I have set it to suspend. But when I open the lid and resume it asks for the password.

Is there a way to prevent being asked for a password after resuming.

---

### Post by Biker on 2006-11-13
Open gconf-editor, goto Apps/gnome-power-manager, disable lock_on_suspend
