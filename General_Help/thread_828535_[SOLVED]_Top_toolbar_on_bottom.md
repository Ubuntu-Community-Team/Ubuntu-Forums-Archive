### Post by y-lee on 2008-06-13
Have you tried moving the toolbars?

If you left click say the bottom tool bar and holding the left mouse button down move the bottom tool bar say to the right to get it out of the way. Then do the same for the "top" tool bar on the bottom and move it back to the top. Now do repeat again to the bottom tool bar you placed on the right and move it back to the bottom.
