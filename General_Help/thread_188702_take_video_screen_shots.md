### Post by Rhubarb on 2008-04-26
```
sudo aptitude install gtk-recordmydesktop
```
Applications --> Sound & Video --> gtk-recordMyDesktop

This application will work in 32 or 64bit Ubuntu, allows you to record the whole screen, or just a window.  It saves the video in ogg theora format, and can also capture sound from your microphone / applications sounds.
And best of all, it's quite easy to use too.
