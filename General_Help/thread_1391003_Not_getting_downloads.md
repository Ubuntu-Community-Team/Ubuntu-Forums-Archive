### Post by t4thfavor on 2010-01-26
Find your Home Directory in Nautilus, its the directory at /home/yourName.

Right click, and say Compress, or create archive. then save the resulting archive on a USB or external hdd or somewhere it will not get deleted.

Then later on begin to pull files out of it, and place them in your new home directory after you install 9.04, or 9.10.
