### Post by t4thfavor on 2010-10-09
There are threads about installation from livecd, you should do lots of homework. 

If your PC is acting so bad, put the cd you burned in, and reboot to the livecd.

If your lucky, you should be able to do all your research on the installation while getting a feel for the new OS.

Then when you feel comfortable, you can just click the install now icon on the desktop, and your off.

Careful as if you do it wrong, you will lose the contents of the XP drive. Make sure you move important files onto something like a usb disk, or flash drive, and remove it from the system (unplug external disk) before installing the new OS.
