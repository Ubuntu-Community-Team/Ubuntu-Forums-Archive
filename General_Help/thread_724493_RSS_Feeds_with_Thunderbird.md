### Post by Dennis on 2008-03-14
In Thunderbird:
Edit - Account Settings - Add Account - Rss News & Blogs  - Name (whatever you want to call it eg RSS Feeds)- Finish.
This gives a new folder News & Blogs within your email folders.

Right click on News & Blogs - subscribe (new window opens) click Add

The rss feeds for the different forums are here:
[http://ubuntuforums.org/cv_rss_feeds.php](http://ubuntuforums.org/cv_rss_feeds.php)

Right click on the orange RSS for the forums of choice - copy link location - paste into the url box in Add subscription - OK.
Exit the list of feeds 

The feeds are now under News & Blogs, click the feeds and read.

I'm sure there must be a simpler or more elegant way of doing it but that's how I do it! :)

p.s. If you have changed Preferences in Firefox to open RSS feeds with T/Bird change it back to show preview so you can get the url of some feeds.
