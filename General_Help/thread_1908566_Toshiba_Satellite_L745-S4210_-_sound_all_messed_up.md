### Post by phaed on 2012-01-16
Have you tried messing with alsamixer? Type "alsamixer" in the terminal and make sure master, headphone, speaker, mic, etc. are turned up. It may just be that your mic is turned down.

I have a Toshiba L550 and haven't experienced any sound problems in the last 2 years with Ubuntu, but on the few occasions when I didn't have sound, it was usually fixed by tweaking with alsamixer.
