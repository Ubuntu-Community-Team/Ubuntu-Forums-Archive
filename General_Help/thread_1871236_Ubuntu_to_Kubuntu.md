### Post by SeijiSensei on 2011-10-28
On the login screen, there's a icon which brings up a menu of desktop choices.  You can specify the default there.  On the Kubuntu login menu, it's in the lower left-hand corner of the password dialog.  I don't know where it appears in other Ubuntu flavors, but I'm pretty sure it's available in all of them.
