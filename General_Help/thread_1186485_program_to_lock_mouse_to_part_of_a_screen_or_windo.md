### Post by jenkinbr on 2009-06-13
Right-click on panel >> Add to panel 

enter 'pointer' into filter box

There should be one option - Pointer capture

add that to your panel wherever you choose.

Close out the Add to Panel dialog

Click once in the green box.  It will turn red and say 'Locked' or 'L', and your pointer will dissappear.  Click again to release the pointer.

You can change the settings by right-clicking the applet and choosing properties.

Hope this is what you are looking for!
