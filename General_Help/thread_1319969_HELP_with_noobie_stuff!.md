### Post by fooman on 2009-11-08
you can turn off the remix desktop and go to a normal desktop by clicking the ubuntu icon in the top left panel,   then go to system > preferences > startup applications

scroll through the list and uncheck maximus and netbook launcher.

reboot and you should see the desktop.  right-click a blank spot in the top panel and you should see an option for "add panel"...if you see it,  click it and you will get a bottom panel.

if you do not see that option...then you are clicking within the "window list".  in that case choose "remove from panel".  *then* right-click a blank spot in the panel again and you should see the option for "add panel"....click it to get a bottom panel.  you can add the window list back in again after that.  put it in the bottom panel to act like a normal ubuntu desktop.

you can then go to system > preferences > appearance and enable the desktop effects for the cube and such.  to get the cube plugin working,  you will need to install the "compizconfig-settings-manager" package from synaptic.  

hope that helps.
