### Post by aysiu on 2006-11-05
All KDE applications will work just fine in Xubuntu, but Xubuntu generally uses GTK libs, and KDE applications generally use QT libs, so any KDE application may take a little bit longer to load in Xubuntu at first.

One thing you may want to try is *kde-core* instead of Kubuntu.

This will help you get it in Kubuntu Dapper:
[http://psychocats.net/ubuntu/kde-core](http://psychocats.net/ubuntu/kde-core)

I'm shortly going to update it to include Kubuntu Edgy as well.
