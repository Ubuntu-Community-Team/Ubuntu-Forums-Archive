### Post by Drain on 2006-01-03
[QUOTE=madsalty]every time i try to install call of duty, i can't because i cannot put the second cd in.[/QUOTE]

When the first cd is finished and you're prompted to insert the next cd, you'll have to go to the terminal and type something like "umount /media/cdrom" to un-mount your cd. You can then eject the cd, put in the next cd, and go from there. 

Good luck!
