### Post by lundbymads on 2008-12-10
You should choose to *completely* remove/purge Thunderbird thru Synaptic.

Anyway... to start over with Thunderbird without reinstalling the application:

Close Thunderbird. Go to your user's home-folder. Press ctrl+h to show hidden files. Delete the folder .mozilla-thunderbird. Restart thunderbird. Voila!
