### Post by Muzik83 on 2007-02-17
Totem draws to your screen at a lower level, so you will end up with a blue screenshot from anything (VNC, or gimp).

A workaround is to use totem to take the screen shot, then open that screen shot with the gimp.  While watching a movie, hit ctrl+s or Edit -> Take Screenshot from inside totem.
