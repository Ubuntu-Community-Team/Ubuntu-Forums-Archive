### Post by kaamos on 2006-03-13
1) enable universe and multiverse -> [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/AddingRepositoriesHowto](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/AddingRepositoriesHowto)

2) Install "unrar-nonfree" with synaptic. Then you should be able to just double click on rar files.
