### Post by mac9416 on 2009-11-07
Howdy, honse246!

For installing updates (and really any software) on an offline computer, [Keryx]("http://keryxproject.org") is the key. With Keryx, you create a snapshot, or "project", on the offline machine, take it (on a flash drive) to your online computer, and download updates, games, and anything you want. Then you take it back to the online machine to install them! 
APTOnCD will be the first app you want to get with Keryx so you can put all the software packages you get with Keryx onto a CD.

You will want to read the Keryx tutorial: [http://crashsystems.net/2009/01/keryx-tutorial/](http://crashsystems.net/2009/01/keryx-tutorial/)

If you have any questions or problems, let me know!
