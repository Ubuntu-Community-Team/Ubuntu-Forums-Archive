### Post by ezsit on 2010-08-06
Yes. I went back to Karmic after having freezing problems and booting problems. 

The computer would randomly freeze or reboot without any warning and nothing in the logs was helpful. 

Booting was hit or miss, it would hang at the Ubuntu with the five dots and never go any further -- this happened every few reboots without leaving any trace of a clue as to why. 

After a few days I couldn't take it anymore and went back to Karmic, which works flawlessly on my hardware. I am now watching Maverick with anticipation.

This is the third LTS I have had major issues with. I seem to have far better luck with the October releases (5.10, 6.10, 7.10, 8.10, & 9.10 were the best releases for me, far better than the April releases). I hope 10.10 continues this tradition.

### Post by vivatsemiramis on 2010-08-08
i did, a few weeks ago, first with my desktop, then with my other laptop. with my desktop, it just hang after splash screen, sometimes telling a lot of errors, after which i could no longer proceed in any way (i think there was something about the nvidia card in that computer that was a major issue even when i just installing and trying the live cd of 10.04; so many problems), so i went back to karmic which worked so well with it (except for the no apic mode, everything worked fine).

as for my acer laptop, i'm using ralink2860 wifi card. so when i tried to replace my wired connection with my wireless one, the wireless worked flawlessly until shutting the machine down. by next boot, it can no longer connect. i later confirmed it was a bug that was never resolved with regard to wireless connectivity, lucid and ralink2860 wifi card. the connection worked with karmic flawlessly, so that was why i reverted. never had any of those problems with karmic (problems include random freezing, that i had to choose force quit, and the desktop seems sluggish and less responsive in lucid). i'm using intel video chip btw.

i've been using ubuntu since 8.10 and i'm quite disappointed with 10.04. i fell in love with it, but with 10.04 i think i've fallen out of love with it. i just hope 10.10 is really good (because at the back of my mind i'm already contemplating buying windows 7 or snow leopard; karmic koala is not forever, since it is not an LTS). such a shame that an LTS has so many glitches and fails in terms of performance when compared with its predecessors.
