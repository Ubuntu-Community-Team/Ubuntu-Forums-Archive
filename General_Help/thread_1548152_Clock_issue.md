### Post by spyrule on 2010-08-07
Go to System->Administration->Time and Date. Click and enter your password and you can change your time zone from there. I had this same problem. It's weird that it's not more self-explanatory. 

Also, on my comp, there was no Time and Date under Administration so I had to right-click on the panel applet and go to edit menus, and then you can navigate to the appropriate spot and set a tick mark.

Good luck :D
