### Post by matthew on 2006-02-11
The guy who originally did the UbuntuGuide had heart problems...thus it was never updated. He gave permission for others to use/update at will and I've seen some sites like that, but I'll have to look for the links. Ubuntu itself did a nice FAQ guide that details most of the same questions/answers. The link for that is in my sig, and you can access the same info on your computer in the System->Help menu. Another great resource is the Ubuntu Document Storage Facility, also linked in my sig. Finally, the Ubuntu Wiki has great stuff as well.

I'll look for those links I mentioned. In the meanwhile, take a tour of the links in my sig and see if they help you.

EDIT: Here's that link for you: [http://easylinux.info/wiki/Ubuntu](http://easylinux.info/wiki/Ubuntu) 
Note: I haven't tried this site's info and there were some (many?) who had concerns about the original ubuntuguide site. Use caution (like anywhere) and don't do stuff if you don't know how to undo it. :)
