### Post by sigurnjak on 2008-11-27
In synaptic you can find this :
i Pod Touch & i Phone sync setup
This package provides a set of scripts for increasing the usability of an
i Phone or i Pod touch.  In order to use it, however, your device must be
running the SSH service.

After enabling an SSH service on your device, all artwork and music can be
properly mounted and synced.

I do not have itouch but for my 80 gig ipod classic i use songbird 

Hope this helps somewhat !:)
