### Post by howefield on 2011-01-16
Nice quick way without having to open up your graphics application is to use the terminal with the following command... 

```
import screeenshot
```

Your mouse cursor will change to a selection icon, left click the mouse and drag around the portion of the screen that you want to snap and then let the mouse button go.

The screen shot will automatically appear in your home folder.

In the example command above, "screenshot" can be replaced with whatever you want to call the picture.
