### Post by mali2297 on 2008-04-17
Open the **File Manager Preferences** dialog, either from **Thunar** through Edit -> Preferences..., or from the **Xfce Settings Manager**. Then go to the **Advanced page** and click on the **Configure** link, right below the **Enable Volume Management** button. The **Removable Drives and Media** configuration dialog will be displayed. There you should be able to change the default actions.
