### Post by yaknowwat on 2008-05-31
> **Teasdale said:**
> Would a graphics driver issue be the cause of my Open Office opening slow in Gutsy? It seems to work okay once it's open, but it takes forever to load the program.

no it would not cause a slow opening most of open office's load time is drive read time.

a graphics driver would make things that access opengl such as drawing or even rendering with antialiasing and similar things to be slowed down especially on an application like openoffice.

this would cause an overall slowed down experience.
