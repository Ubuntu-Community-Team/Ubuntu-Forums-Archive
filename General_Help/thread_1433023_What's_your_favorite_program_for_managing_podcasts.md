### Post by sgage on 2010-05-14
Gpodder works for me perfectly, and downloads mp3's just fine.

Add their ppa to your repos so you get the latest version. I've never had it fail to update my podcast subscriptions. You can set it up to use whatever player you want to actually play the podcasts. I use audacious, because you can have it resume a playback where you left off when you shut it down. 

I've been using it for quite a while, and it has become very smooth.
