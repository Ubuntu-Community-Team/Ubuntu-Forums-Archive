### Post by Garandir on 2010-11-20
My DiamondBack3g has worked perfectly fine for almost a year. I don't know of any Razer products that don't function to the point of no use under Linux. If they have a problem, it's usually not being able to use extra mouse buttons.

EDIT: For the extra mouse button, some folks seem to have luck with [RazerTool]("http://sourceforge.net/projects/razertool/").
