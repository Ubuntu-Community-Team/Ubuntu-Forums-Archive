### Post by AlphaLexman on 2011-09-10
<**Ctrl+P**> will bring up the Preferences dialog box where you can select the skin of your choice.

You can get more skins [here]("http://gnome-look.org/index.php?xsortmode=high&page=0&xcontentmode=130").
And put them in '$HOME/.local/share/audacious/Skins'
