### Post by dandnsmith on 2010-01-02
I don't think that is really a different question - my experience of TBird (and a number of others) is that when you change to a new version you are likely to re-use the old setup, but modify it somewhat. In this case the emails are in a different position and you have to get to it.

After a bit of prowling the Windows version, why not look to see where Local Folders reside? I found there are things called 'inbox' and 'sent' in mine - which are the mail messages in what I believe to be 'mbox format' (essentially plain text, with one message after another). I think a small amount of trial copying might get you there.

The copying to gmail might not be successful - you'll need to check the gmail settings (are you using POP3 or IMAP?)

HTH
