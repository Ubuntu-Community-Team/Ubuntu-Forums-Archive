### Post by Kevbert on 2008-03-11
Yes, try using another PC, if possible.  It may be that your computer won't boot from USB as USB support can be flaky to say the least on some PCs.  I suggest you post your PC model and BIOS version on the Forum.
I have a PC that is supposed to support booting from external devices (in bios), but will not boot from USB.  
You could also search the Hardware support Wiki [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/HardwareSupport]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/HardwareSupport") for your Motherboard/BIOS.
Hope this helps.
