### Post by devendra.r.deshpande on 2011-01-24
This happened to me quite frequently while using nxclient.

1. Installed compizconfig-settings-manager
2. System->Preferences->CompizConfig Settings Manager
3. Disable "Fading Windows" in the Effects section

Seems to be working fine now.
