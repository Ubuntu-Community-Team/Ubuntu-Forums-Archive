### Post by donsy on 2010-08-22
The same thing happened to me until I deleted the indicator applet and followed the instructions [here]("http://ubuntugenius.wordpress.com/2010/05/04/restore-missing-volume-button-to-system-tray-after-upgrade-to-ubuntu-10-04/") to restore the old volume control. Read the part where is begins:

[CENTER][COLOR=#ff0000]_**OR**_[/COLOR][B] (if you don&#8217;t want to use the *Indicator Applet*,
but want the _old_ volume button back):[/B]
[LEFT]
[/LEFT]
[/CENTER]
I haven't had a bit of trouble with the old volume control. If you don't want to use the old volume control, try not releasing the left mouse button until the slider is absolutely stationary and where you want it. I noticed that sometimes I would release the mouse button prematurely and when the slider was still moving ever so slightly.
