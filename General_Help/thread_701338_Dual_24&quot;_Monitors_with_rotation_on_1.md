### Post by OoooMatron on 2008-02-19
Instead of setting up multiple device and screen sections try removing all the stuff referencing the 2nd monitor or card and just add 

option "twinview" "true"

 to the device section and it should pick up and configure the 2nd monitor automatically while keeping the borders between screens but still treating it like a single desktop.
