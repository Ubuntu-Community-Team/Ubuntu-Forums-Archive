I am running Intrepid on a 64 bit system and all is very well. Can anyone tell me if I buy a standard pci firewire card (as against one which specifically states 64 bit) should it be compatible on both 32 and 64 bit systems?

### Post by jespdj on 2008-11-27
There are no PCI cards that are specific for a 32-bit or 64-bit operating system - the hardware is the same, whether you use a 32- or 64-bit OS.
