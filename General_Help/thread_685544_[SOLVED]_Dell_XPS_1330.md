### Post by herger on 2008-03-25
Hi all.
Since nobody could help me, I tried to go though the problem by myself....
The cause is not compiz, but the network settings.
Besides all the problems that Gutsy has with LAN dhcp connections (I didn't have cable connection working, at all..), if You change Your working place (i.e. office-home), the booting-up is much slower due to the previous connection setting You used and it continues slower all the functions of Gutsy.
If You change network settings or disable them (as I have to do at home, since cable+dhcp doesn't work at all), the problem vainshes.
