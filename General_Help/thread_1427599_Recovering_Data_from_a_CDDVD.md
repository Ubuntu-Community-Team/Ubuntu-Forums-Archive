### Post by Daveski on 2010-03-11
Is the disc scratched? A good CD recovery polish done with care could resurrect the data (some suggest toothpaste, but I cannot say if this works or not).

Once you have the disc as clean and polished as possible, try ddrecover and then scalpel, photorec or foremost the image to extract any usable files:

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/DataRecovery]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/DataRecovery")
