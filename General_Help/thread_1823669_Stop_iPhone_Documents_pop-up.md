### Post by ycp101 on 2012-03-05
I found the solution by myself. Those problems I had are actually configurable in Ubuntu. To prevent Natilus popup upon iPhone connection to USB, open Configuration Editor and go to app-nautilus-preferences and uncheck media_automount_open. To stop popup dialogs asking photo application and music music application, I could make it in Nautilus. In the menu, go to Edit-preferences-Media tap, change Music Player and Photos option to "do Nothing".

I am using Ubuntu 11.04. Hopefully, this work in other versions of Ubuntu, too. Thank you for issuing this thread.
