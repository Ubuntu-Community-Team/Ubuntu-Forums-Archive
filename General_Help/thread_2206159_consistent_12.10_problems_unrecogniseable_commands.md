### Post by ajgreeny on 2014-02-17
> 12.10 is annoying in that it wants to pre-install (I don't remember the  correct term) everything under the sun. I want my computer to run basic  important things in my life, and I want it to run fast.In which case, depending on your hardware, Ubuntu 12.10 is probably not the best OS for you.  Ubuntu with the unity desktop, particularly in its earlier versions of unity was prone to run slowly, and in my opinion is not much good on anything but the latest machine.

Have a look at Xubuntu 12.04, or wait a couple of months and look at Xubuntu 14.04; it looks to be a real winner already and if it keeps going as it is, though only in alpha at the moment, I will certainly be upgrading to it in April.

If you want a real lean, mean system with only the applications you really need, not what someone else says you will need, see if you can manage to use a minimal installation CD,  [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Installation/MinimalCD](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Installation/MinimalCD), then add your own choice of WM or DE and applications.
