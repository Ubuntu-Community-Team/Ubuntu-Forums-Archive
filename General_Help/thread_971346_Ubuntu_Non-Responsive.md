### Post by Axis on 2008-11-04
Hi jon_gault, I'm glad you have decided to try and take the dive into ubuntu.

This very well may be the hardware. Ensure that all of your cables are correctly connected to their spots and so on. I had a very similar problem before and it turned out that by reconnecting my HDD sata cable it solved the problem. Where is that was the problem with me I would be sure to try all connectors.

However as I said, it sounds EXACTLY like what I had problems with.
