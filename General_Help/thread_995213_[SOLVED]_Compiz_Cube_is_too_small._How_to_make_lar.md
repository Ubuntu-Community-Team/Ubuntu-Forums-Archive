### Post by chamber on 2008-11-27
b. If you notice, the top and bottom of the cube do not show the desktop. How do I make that a desktop or maybe change the logo?

c. How do I change the background behind the cube?
[COLOR="Red"]CompizConfig Settings Manager Desktop cube - appearance - skydome then change the skydome image  [/COLOR]
