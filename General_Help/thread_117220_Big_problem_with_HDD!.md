### Post by aysiu on 2006-01-14
[QUOTE=Co2]
Can i resize the windows partitions without loosing any data?[/QUOTE] Before you do that, even though you won't probably lose your data, I would back up your data first. 

[Mount your Windows partition in Kubuntu](http://www.psychocats.net/linux/mountwindows.php) and then copy the files to an external hard drive, burn them to CDs, put them on floppy disks... do what you have to do.

Then, I would use [DiskDrake](http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=114142) to resize your Windows partition.
