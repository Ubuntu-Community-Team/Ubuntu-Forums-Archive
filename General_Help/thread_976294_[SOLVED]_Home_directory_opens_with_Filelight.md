How do I make my home directory open from the places menu with nautilus like it used to?

---

### Post by fooman on 2008-11-09
go to places > computer

click on your user name in the left column.  on the right side, right click on "Desktop".  choose properties from the drop-down menu,  click the "open with" tab and put a check in the box next to "open folder".

that should fix it.
