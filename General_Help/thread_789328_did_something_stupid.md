### Post by pbpersson on 2008-05-10
I did a Google on "linux undelete" and there are all sorts of articles on this.

However, I would NOT install some software package to accomplish it, since any updates to your hard drive are likely to overwrite the files you are attempting to recover.

The one article says boot onto a live CD ASAP to keep from overwriting the files.  Just the act of reading this post could overwrite files on your hard drive.
