### Post by KManZ on 2007-05-24
As the title asks, how do you disable the drums at the log in screen? I have already turned off the log in and log off tribal music, but I can't seem to find the option to turn off the drums that play when it asks you to enter your name and password. ---

### Post by tino27 on 2007-05-24
System -> Administration -> Login Window

Click on the Accessibility tab

Uncheck "login screen ready"

---

### Post by benanzo on 2007-05-24
Go to **System** -> **Administration** -> **Login Window**
Then click the **Accessibility** tab and uncheck the sounds.
