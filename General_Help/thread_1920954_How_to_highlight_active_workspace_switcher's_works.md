### Post by crocomut on 2012-02-06

### Post by bro brian on 2012-02-06
I had the same problem just now, and was searching the forum for an answer. In my case, it didn't matter what theme I was using, and it had to do with some sort of bug in the compiz. What I did to solve it was simply to switch the Window Manager from Compiz to Metacity, and it started highlighting again. I then switched back to Compiz, and the workspace switcher highlight function stayed working.
It's like it had a glitch or something.
