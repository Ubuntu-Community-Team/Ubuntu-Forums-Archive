### Post by K.Mandla on 2006-08-23
Not to be contrary, but I would disagree. I've done installations before and didn't blank the drive first, and got leftover information (bootloaders in particular) that corrupted my new system. 

If you want something that will wipe the disk pefectly clean, start to finish, I would suggest [Killdisk]("http://www.killdisk.com"). It's a small download, you can install it to a floppy and it eradicates any leftover information on the drive. It can be time-consuming, depending on the size and speed of your hard drive and connecting hardware, but worth it, in my opinion.

Anyways, just my $0.02.
