### Post by jpeddicord on 2008-07-29
The white screen is probably Compiz trying to convert your desktop into a texture, but is failing to do so properly. To get access to a terminal, at the login screen hit F10 (Options) and then Select Session. From there, pick Failsafe Terminal and then continue to sign in.
