### Post by gdonwallace on 2011-11-27
You can save Libri-office (open-office) files in the MS Office format.  However; only .doc, .xls.  Not the new format.  Libri-Office will accept the new format, but it will not save in them.

When saving, click on the drop down and choose the windows format, that should take care of your problem.
