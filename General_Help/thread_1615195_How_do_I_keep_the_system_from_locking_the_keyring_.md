It's getting really annoying now. If I leave the netbook idle for like 2 mins it will lock the keyring and ask me to log back in.

---

### Post by coffeecat on 2010-11-06
That's not the keyring. That's the screensaver.

Go to System > Preferences > Screensaver and untick 'lock screen'.

Also System > Preferences > Power Management and change settings to your needs.
