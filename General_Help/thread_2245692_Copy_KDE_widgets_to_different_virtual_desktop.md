### Post by T.J. on 2014-09-25
As far as I know, there is no mechanism for duplicating  "activities"  inside KDE 4.  I could be wrong.  It has been a while since I bothered to use KDE at all - different versions have different features.  I would check the activities panel before abandoning all hope. 

 The configuration files are under ~/.kde  and ~/.config respectively.  The control files aren't well documented at all.  i do not understand why DE's in the last decade or so can't have decent documentation.  This may help, although I doubt it.
