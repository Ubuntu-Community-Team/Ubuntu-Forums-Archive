### Post by hardyn on 2007-04-06
Even if your note exceeding the advertised output of the power supply, you are likely running it pretty ragged.

the pop was likely a capacitor exploding... return the power supply get your warratied unit.

you might have to abandon the use of the compact case and go to a full size case to get a reasonable powersupply in there.  OR have a look for a higher quality powersupply in the same form factor... but it won't be cheap... expect to pay alot for it.

you have all high performance parts, you cant skimp of the power supply, that videocard probably draws 150watts alone.
