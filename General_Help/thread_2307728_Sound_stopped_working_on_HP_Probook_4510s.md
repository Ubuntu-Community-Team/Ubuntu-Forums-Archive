### Post by michael-piziak on 2015-12-29
I watched an HP video where one resets computer to factory defaults/drains electricity and now sound works again.

To do this on an HP Notebook, you 1 remove power and all devices, 2 remove battery, 3 hold down the power on button for 15 or more seconds to drain the electricity out of the computer.
