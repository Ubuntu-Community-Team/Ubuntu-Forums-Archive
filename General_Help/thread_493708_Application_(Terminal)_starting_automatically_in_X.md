### Post by UJoe4 on 2007-07-06
The terminal program xfce4-terminal is starting automatically every time I log into an Xfce session, and I don't want it to. It's not listed under Settings > Autostarted Applications, and I've disabled remembering the state of the current session when logging off.

I would just uninstall it and reinstall, but I can't because it's bundled to xubuntu-desktop. Any ideas about how to fix this?

---

### Post by mcduck on 2007-07-06
Enable saving the session, close all programs and log out. Then log back in, and disable saving the session again.

---

### Post by UJoe4 on 2007-07-06
Worked perfectly. Thank you!
