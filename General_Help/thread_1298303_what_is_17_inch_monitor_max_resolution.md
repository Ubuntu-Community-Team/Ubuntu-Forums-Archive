### Post by P4man on 2009-10-22
Is it an LCD (flatscreen) or CRT ?
You can push a CRT slightly over its limits sometimes (although frankly thats already a very high resolution for a 17' CRT). With an LCD is simple: you cant. It just has no more pixels than that.
