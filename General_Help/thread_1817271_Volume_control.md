### Post by garvinrick4 on 2011-08-03
Right click on bar and it says and you can add things to panel by going to panel settings.
To panel applets to add and can add volume control if you add a launcher application bar and you will not see anything
but if right click at the next spot and choose application Launch bar you can choose anything
in you whole menu of Applications. You can also go to panel settings and in Geometry choose top and
it puts the bar on top it is kind of nice have all your open apps on top instead of bottom. Indicator applets 
gives all three or four I believe. I put a panel on left and make it  Height 70 and width 54 and icons 50 or so
and get a nice little Launcher pad to add things to with the original bottom bar switched in geometry to top.
Once you get it (some videos on You Tube) it is real nice. I put it on a friends computer today with an old processor
and 500 meg of Ram and Lubuntu runs fast. Flash uses a whole lot of processor but flash is a glut. I enjoy lubuntu any which way.
