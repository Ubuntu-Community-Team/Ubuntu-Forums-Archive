### Post by stoneybroke on 2008-08-10
You could always download the KDE desktop to use it has a nice user interface and is very easy to use and can give a windows type desktop appearance. To swap between KDE and Ubuntu just log out of Ubuntu and in the log in screen go to Options in bottom left of screen select Options then KDE once you enter the username and password you will be into the KDE screen.

Hope that helps you gain a few brownie points with your mam and dad
