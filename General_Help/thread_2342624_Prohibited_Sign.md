### Post by iliasgnrs on 2016-11-10
Finally i found a solution. The laptop cooling pad was causing the problem. I was searching the problem by trying each one of my external devices,(USBs, HDDs, Mouse, Cooling-Pad, etc.). Now i have only the mouse on my laptop, i removed the pad and it just works the way it's supposed to.
 Thanks everyone.
