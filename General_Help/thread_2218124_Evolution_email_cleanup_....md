### Post by ian-weisser on 2014-04-21
Well, did you empty Evolution's 'Trash' folder? Silly question, but some users forget to do that.

Beyond that's it's not clear what you mean by 'clean up'
If you told Evolution to keep it by not deleting it, Evo will keep it.
Evo isn't smart enough to make value judgements on which old e-m,ail are important enough to keep.
