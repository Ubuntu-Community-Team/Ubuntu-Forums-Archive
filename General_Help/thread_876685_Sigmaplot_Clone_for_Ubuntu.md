### Post by NikoC on 2008-08-01
Well, I personally use R (available via the repos as r-base) for all my statistics and plotting. You can export your plots to pdf, jpeg, etc. which is excellent for producing quality graphs in papers and presentations...
Also, this way I only need one piece of software for both statistics and plotting..

There are of course other free options which will only do the plotting for you:
e.g. GNUplot, rlplot, ...

Cheers.
