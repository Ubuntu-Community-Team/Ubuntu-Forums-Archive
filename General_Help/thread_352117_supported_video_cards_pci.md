### Post by pseudonym on 2007-02-02
> **stonerrob420 said:**
> Hello! im looking for video cards that are PCI.......that are supported in ubuntu 6.06 LTS.....such as nvidia and ATI radeon......does anybody have a list or where can i view one? Any help would much be appreciated:)
Most cards by both those manufacturers are supported well in Linux, apart from the most recent ATI cards. It doesn't matter whether they are PCI, AGP, or PCI-Express.

From my experience, I've used a PCI Nvidia MX4000 and Nvidia Riva TNT2 and both worked fine (you would need to install the legacy 7184 version of the nvidia driver for those cards if you didn't want to stick with the open-source nv driver). Neither were that good when it came to 3D graphics, but I'm assuming this feature is not that important to you.

More recent models that come in PCI versions should also work fine, and probably better then the above two.

HTH
