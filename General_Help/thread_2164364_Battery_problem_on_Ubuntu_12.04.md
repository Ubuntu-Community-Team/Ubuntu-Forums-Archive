### Post by BBQdave on 2013-07-31
> **John_Gone said:**
> Is there a problem with the battery itself or can I fix this directly with some command ?

Sounds like your battery is toast.

Usually through the cycles of charge and discharge, battery time decreases. You use to get 2 hours on battery, now you only get 1 hour, and so on.

If your battery is showing a set time left before charge - but then suddenly no power and system goes off - probably damaged cells in the battery.

Sorry to say, probably time for a new battery - or run plugged in :)
