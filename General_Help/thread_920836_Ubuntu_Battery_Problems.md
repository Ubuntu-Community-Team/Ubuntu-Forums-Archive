Your results are an exception, definitly not the norm.  

According to powertop, my laptop uses around 17 watts on average on a bone stock ubuntu install.  Under WinXP, thats around 12 or 13.  If I tweak Linux enough, I can get it down to 13-14ish.  XP definitly uses less power on mine.

As far as the OP goes, I'm not sure what to tell ya.  As far as I'm concerned power management under Linux is not quite there yet.  If you go to [www.lesswatts.org](www.lesswatts.org) and poke around a bit, they have lots of helpful suggestions on how to make that battery stretch longer.  I don't think your battery is bad.
