### Post by jesuisbenjamin on 2010-06-25
Hi there,

"accessing this site" denotes some web-browsing. It is most probably your browser. Are you using your browser at the time the message comes up? 
If the message comes up despite you not attempting to go to any website, then perhaps you should clear your browsing history (including cookies etc) and there would no apparent reason it should attempt to connect to a site you do not wish to connect to.

Hope this helps.
