### Post by Shay Stephens on 2006-04-30
I don't see it my font list (default ubuntu), the closest to it looks like URW Gothic L.  But if I had to choose between that and Bitstream Vera Sans Mono, Bitstream wins for overall readability.

Test out a proposed font by comparing lowercase "L" and the number 1, also compare the difference between zero and lower case "O".  If I can't see much of difference between them, I pass on the font.
