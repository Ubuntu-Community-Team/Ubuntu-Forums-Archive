### Post by jerome1232 on 2011-07-01
If you only formatted it and have not written any new data to it, then testdisk may be able to restore the partition table back to original state.


Failing that photorec (comes with testdisk) can recover your multimedia files  (pictures, videos, documents, etc...) Although they won't retain original names and directory hierarchy.

Here is a little write up on how to use these programs [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/DataRecovery](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/DataRecovery)

best of luck, report back with questions or progress

edit: To use photorec your going to need another disk or partition besides the one your attempting to recover from to write the recovered files to.
