### Post by cta16 on 2008-12-10
1. Right click the top panel and click "new Panel". A new panel will appear at the bottom
2.Right click the new panel you just created at the bottom and click "add to panel"
3. A new window will pop up with a list of stuff. Scroll to the bottom of the list and select "Windows list" and click the "add" button on the bottom'. You should now be able to view all the windows open on that panel.
4. Now go back to that list and select "Workspace switcher". Then click "add". This will let you switch between desktops. 
5. They are probably not where you want them on your panel. So just right click and and click "move". Then you can place it where you want it.
