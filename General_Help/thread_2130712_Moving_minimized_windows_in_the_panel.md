### Post by The Cog on 2013-03-30
I wish there was, but I haven't found one yet. 

One thing I really like is that you can have the windows buttons draw a frame round each window as you hover over the button. This makes finding the right button really quick if you keep all your windows in different positions on the screen. Just run your cursor over the buttons and see a frame jump from location to location. You really need to try it to understand what I mean - open a few thunar windows for instance, put a maximised something in front of everything and then wave over the window buttons.


Excited Edit:
I just looked again at the Window Buttons settings, and there it is! 
One of the button ordering options is "None - allow drag and drop". Without rebooting I can't be sure, but I suspect this is a new feature in Xubuntu 13.04.
