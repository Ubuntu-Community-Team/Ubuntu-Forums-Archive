### Post by Arquis on 2009-09-24
My mother once deleted 10Gb worth of photos on Windows. I recovered a lot of them (not all) by using **[PHOTOREC]("http://www.cgsecurity.org/wiki/PhotoRec")** and a live linux cd.

It is command line, but very easy to use. On the same site you'll find **[TESTDISK]("http://www.cgsecurity.org/wiki/TestDisk")**. Both will do the job, but I guess PHOTOREC suits better your problem.

I'm not sure, but I think you'll need another partition or a USB drive to copy the recovered files to.

Hope I helped. Good luck.
