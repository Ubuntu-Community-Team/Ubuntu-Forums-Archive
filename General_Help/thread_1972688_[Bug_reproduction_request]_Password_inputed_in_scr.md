### Post by ubuntu27 on 2012-05-03
I don't know where to put this thread. So, if an admin or mod wants to move it to an more appropriate forum, go ahead.

I am creating this thread to see if people can reproduce [bug#994094]("https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/gnome-screensaver/+bug/994094")

You need a laptop with Ubuntu 12.04 to reproduce the bug.

STEPS TO REPRODUCE:

 1) Lock Screen (Ctrl+Alt+L)

 2) Type your password, but do NOT press Enter NOR click on Unlock

 3) Close the laptop's lid. This will suspend the laptop.

 4) Wake up laptop from suspend

 5) Password that was typed in step 2) is still remembered.

[https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/gnome-screensaver/+bug/994094](https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/gnome-screensaver/+bug/994094)
