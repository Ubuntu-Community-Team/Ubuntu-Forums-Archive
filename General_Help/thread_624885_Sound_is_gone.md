Hmmm, only yesterday I had this problem when I tried to tweak Kaffiene, another multimedia player.  Next thing the sound vanished without a trace!

I managed to get it back when I found that all the settings on my volume control bar were muted.

I suggest you try "System/Preferences/Volume Control", and if the boxes (beneath the sound controls) have red crosses on them, click on them to remove the crosses and unmute your sound.

Hope this helps.
