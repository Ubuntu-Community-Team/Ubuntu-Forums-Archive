### Post by richardjh on 2010-03-09
I have a Cowon X5, on which I had the same problems. I have to re-encode video to 160x100 resolution and the key part was only 12 frames per second, and audio 44000 else it wouldn't play at all, just a blank screen.

This was a lot of work in ffmpeg.

I have used [WinFF]("http://winff.org/html_new/") for encoding videos to make them suitable for websites and I am fairly certain there are presets for some media players in there. Might be worth a look.
