### Post by Dragonbite on 2012-01-18
Online accounts allow you to connect Gnome to your account, such as Google.

Afterwards, if you were to install Evolution you would find it automatically pulled in your email settings, email and contacts (but not calendar).

It is more implemented in Gnome-shell than Unity but it will probably be built/improved upon in the near future.
