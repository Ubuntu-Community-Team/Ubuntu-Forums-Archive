### Post by the yawner on 2007-09-14
Go to Preferences>Theme on your menu. On your current theme click the customize button. On the tab named Controls, select a different theme to match your window decoration.

This instruction is for changing the GTK theme which is responsible for the appearance of buttons, scroll bars, panels, among others. Emerald on the other hand just replaces Metacity, the default window manager for GNOME. So whatever window border theme is set on Metacity is replaced by Emerald.
