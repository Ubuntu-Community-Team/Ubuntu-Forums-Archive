### Post by scbingham on 2013-04-21
iTunes won't run on linux, not even with wine.
Find a windoze pc with iTunes, initialise & add at least one tune.
When you connect iPod via usb to Ubuntu, Rhythmbox will auto start & find your iPod. You can then drag & drop files onto it.
