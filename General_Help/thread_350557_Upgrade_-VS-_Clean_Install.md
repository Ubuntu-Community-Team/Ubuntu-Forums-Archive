### Post by diepruis on 2007-02-01
I would recommend an upgrade. It really depends on how comfortable you are with Linux and your own computer.

I just did an upgrade from Dapper to Edgy. The upgrade went smooth, except that the new drivers for my wireless didn't work and that I had to reinstall my video drivers. Luckily I knew how the fix the first, and the forums helped me with my other problems.

It can cause problems, but fixing those will mostly be quicker than having to backup every single file and every single setting and reconfiguring your PC afterwards.

That's just my opinion, however, and you should really listen to people who have had more experience with upgrading.
