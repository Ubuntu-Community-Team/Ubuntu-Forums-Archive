### Post by QIII on 2010-03-10
Hardware.

A frequent culprit is memory modules.  Bad or loose modules wreak havoc during POST.

If you can't even get to your BIOS, you have issues well before the OS.

Some motherboards use they system speaker to issue a specific set of beeps to indicate what the POST checks are finding.

Are you getting a series of beeps out of your system speaker?  Can you research your motherboard and see if the OEM has a troubleshooting page?
