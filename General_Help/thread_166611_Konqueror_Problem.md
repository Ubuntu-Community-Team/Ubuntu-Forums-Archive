### Post by WebDrake on 2006-04-27
[QUOTE=Harold P]I'm trying to log in to MySpace.com, but it just redirects me to the make a new profile page. I think the reason why is because Konqueror won't open the secure connection with myspace.[/QUOTE]
Could it be something to do with your cookie settings?  Check in Settings -> Configure Konqueror and click on the Cookies icon.  Check that the default policy is to ask (not reject all), and check in the Site Policy section underneath that cookies from .myspace.com are not rejected.
