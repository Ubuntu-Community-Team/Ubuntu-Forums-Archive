### Post by fragos on 2006-11-30
I haven't found a parameter for that.  I seem to remember that KDE had an option for that.  When I want to scroll large amounts I use the scroll bar.  Click and drag it for the fastest path to the beginning or ending of a file.  Clicking in the scrool bar area causes a one screen jump.  There is also a mode of operation where you click the wheel and an icon appears at the cursor.  The direction and speed are controlled by moving the mouse off the center dot.  Returning to the center dot stops the scroll.  Clicking the center wheel again will turn the mode off and your back to a few lines at a time as you move the wheel a click at a time.  This method is also very fast and actualy the one I like the best.  In truth I use all of them depending on my needs at that time.
