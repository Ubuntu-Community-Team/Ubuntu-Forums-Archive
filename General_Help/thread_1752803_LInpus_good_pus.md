### Post by timgood on 2011-05-08
I also have a Revo 3700. Linpus is underwhelming, and I got quite tired of it after a few days. I installed 10.10 (now upgraded to 11.04) which is much better. You will need to tinker with minor annoyances like Plymouth boot splash and blacklisting a wireless driver to prevent hang on shutdown, and there is a Revo thread in this forum which should help you. The 3700 is a good bit of kit, and with XBMC and Ubuntu installed is a great streaming media player as well.

Hope this helps.
