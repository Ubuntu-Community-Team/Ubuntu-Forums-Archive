### Post by ago on 2007-07-11
with fat32 you are limited to 4GB max file size. Which means that you can have at most 4GB root + 4GB programs + 4GB home and fat32 as a filesystem is worse than ntfs.

The advantages are: 1. suspend _might_ work,  2. the linux driver is more tested
