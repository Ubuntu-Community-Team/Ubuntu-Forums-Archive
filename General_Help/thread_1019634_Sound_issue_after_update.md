### Post by redilyn on 2008-12-23
If you install the updates and your sound stops working:

Go to System -> Preferences -> Sound

Try each of the options, Click test for each one and see if you can get any of them to work.

I do not know what update may have caused your problem, but if i had to guess I would watch out for any that have to do with pulseaudio.
