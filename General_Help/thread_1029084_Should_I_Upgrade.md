### Post by -kg- on 2009-01-03
My opinion?  "If it ain't broke, don't fix it!"

> There are some new features in Intrepid Ibex, you can google for the new features.

By all means, check the new features out, but if you aren't just *really* interested in one or more of them, and you have a stable working installation, I would just leave it alone.

There are always issues with any upgrade and you have to play and play with it to get it to a stable point again.  If you are an experimenter, or if you just *really* feel you need or want one of the new features, I'd leave well enough alone.

There are still people posting to the forums that are running *way* previous versions of Ubuntu, and I figure they do that for a reason.
