### Post by bubbalouie on 2007-09-14
I don't know if this will solve the problem for you, but:

 - Check you mixer settings (in the volume control), make certain the microphone is enabled, this took me 10 minutes to realize (always the simple things :confused: )
 - Make certain Skype is using the right sound device in the settings dialog, I had this issue with Skype 1.3
 - Older versions of Skype, I found hated sharing my sound card, make certain nothing else is using the sound device

1.4 worked first time for me (it even shares the sound card), however I also use Kubuntu so there may be some very slight differences, I did however have to enable the microphone in the mixer after the last update to get things rolling.

Good Luck
