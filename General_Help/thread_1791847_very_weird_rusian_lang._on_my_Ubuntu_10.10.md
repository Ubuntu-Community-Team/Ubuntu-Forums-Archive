For Libreoffice you go to Tools>Options>Language Settings>Languages and see if somehow Russian got set as a default somewhere.

Your system language is not Russian so I do not see why you get some Russian in Software Centre. Have you at some time installed Russian language support or a Russian Keyboard layout?

My understanding of Russian is very limited but I think that you are getting genuine Russian words and not English words typed on a Russian keyboard layout, and so with Russian characters.
