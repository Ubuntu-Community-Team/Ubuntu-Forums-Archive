### Post by imariot on 2007-03-29
You can set up a filter that only applies to outgoing messages. 

Settings ---> Configure Filters

Click the "New" Button in the lower left corner, then you can create a filter that does an automatic filter on subject, to, or what ever may be constant, Then set your filter actions to "forward to" the address to which you want to copy. 

Go to the Advanced tab and remove the "Apply this filter to incoming messages" checkbox, and check the "Apply this filter to sent messages".  Click Apply then click OK.....Send a test message.
