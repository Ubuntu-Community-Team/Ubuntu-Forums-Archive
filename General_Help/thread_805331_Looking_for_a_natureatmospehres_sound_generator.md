### Post by Tweak42 on 2011-02-17
Stumbled across this thread while looking for a white noise generator and had to add what I found.

[Boodler]("http://boodler.org/") can generate nature and atmospheric sounds using addon packages.  For white/pink/brown noise, I turned [SimplyNoise.com]("http://www.SimplyNoise.com") in to a Mozilla Prism app.  Also if it's allowed in your country, [Pandora Radio]("http://www.pandora.com/") has a nature sounds station.
