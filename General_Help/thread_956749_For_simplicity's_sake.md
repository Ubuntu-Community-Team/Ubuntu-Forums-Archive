### Post by strongfan on 2008-10-23
Is there any way to have a "guest" account without a password that has little privileges?  I don't like having to enter my password all the time, and I don't like having other people enter my password to get on.  Thankyou!

---

### Post by gimlithedwarf on 2008-10-23
Create a new account called guest and give it total unpriveleged status. Then set the password to something easy to remember like guest123. This will allow you to let anyone onto your system who you trust, but not just anyone to get into your system.

---

### Post by woodenbrick on 2008-10-23
If you don't like entering your password everytime, go to System->Admin->Login Window->Security and check automatic login for your user. It would depend on who else uses your computer whether or not this is a good idea.
