### Post by afderrick on 2008-01-10
Alright, so here is my question.  My system was wanting to load the GIMP a little slower than what I like. ### Post by afderrick on 2008-01-11
OKay, so I figured out the problem on my own.   I went in and tried to open one of my folders and when it didn't open I got system monitor up and going and I just killed the process of nautilus hoping this wouldn't take down my entire system.  It didn't, it immediately came back up and jumped down to about 38MB of memory being taken up.  So I'm good now, and I'm happy.
Just wanted to post that I was able to do that and it fixed the problem in case someone else is having the same problems.
