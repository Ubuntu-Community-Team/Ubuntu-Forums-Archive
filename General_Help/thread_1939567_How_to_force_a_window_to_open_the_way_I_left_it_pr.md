### Post by rg4w on 2012-03-11
This may be hard to believe, but it seems that preventing the user from controlling their own window sizes is a feature, and not a bug, in 11.10.

Fortunately there were apparently enough people on the team who doubted the usefulness of denying this basic user control that they've provided an option so we can adjust it.

To access this you'll need to get Compiz Config Settings Manager from the Software Center, and in the Unity plugin in CCSM you'll find an Advanced tab; in that you'll find what you're looking for with the "Automaximize" slider - just turn it up to 100%, and at last you'll have 100% control over your window sizes.
