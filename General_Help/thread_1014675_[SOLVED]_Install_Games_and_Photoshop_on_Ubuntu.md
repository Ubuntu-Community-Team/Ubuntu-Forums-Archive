### Post by PocketDog on 2008-12-18
I can't say whether COD or Photoshop work with WINE, but you can search apps and games, and see people's experiences with them [here](http://appdb.winehq.org/appbrowse.php) at Wine HQ. As Photoshop is seriously expensive I wouldn't cut your nose off to spite your face - Windows is a better platform for a few specific applications, and if you insist on the full functionality of those - dual boot. Nobody here will shout at you for doing so, your PC is yours to do with what you will :-)
