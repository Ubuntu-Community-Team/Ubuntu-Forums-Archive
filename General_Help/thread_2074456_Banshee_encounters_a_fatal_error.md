### Post by CaptainMark on 2012-10-21
Ive had this error before, the hard way if you want to keep all your play counts and album art and what not is to google how to rebuild the banshee database, there are plenty of guides available I believe.  If you are not worried about keeping cached metadata you can either remove the file ~/.config/banshee-1/banshee.db or failing that just remove ~/.config/banshee-1 altogether

edit: heres the link i followed way back when this happened to me [http://robertbeal.com/608/banshee-crashing-rebuild-the-database](http://robertbeal.com/608/banshee-crashing-rebuild-the-database)
