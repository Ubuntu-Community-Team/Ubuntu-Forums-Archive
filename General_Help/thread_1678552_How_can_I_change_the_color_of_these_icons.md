### Post by SaintDanBert on 2011-01-30
"Icons" are actually very tiny digital artwork.  If what you see is a blue star icon, someone drew a star using blue dots.  To get a red star,
you need to draw a star and use red dots ... or copy the blue star to a different file and edit the blue dots to become red dots. In short, you need different icon files.

If you do digital art, feel free to make your own icons.  If not, there are loads of available icon packs online as other posters suggest.
[http://goinglinux.com/articles/DesktopLaunchers.html](http://goinglinux.com/articles/DesktopLaunchers.html) 

Enjoy your freedom to change things,
