### Post by chris billington on 2010-03-13
Yes, adding that icon back is a breeze. In fact, you can add it anywhere on either panel. Simply right click where you want it to go and click 'add to panel'. Select 'workspace switcher' and click add.

If it isn't quite where you want it to be, right click it and theres an option to move it.
