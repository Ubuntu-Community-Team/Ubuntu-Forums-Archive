'brute force' is a term meaning, you just try things over and over again until it works. I have a very bad memory and I used to keep some of my data in password protected .rar and .7z files, and when I forgot the password and it was something important I was trying to keep, I would use RarCrack ([http://rarcrack.sourceforge.net/](http://rarcrack.sourceforge.net/)) to get the password back.
