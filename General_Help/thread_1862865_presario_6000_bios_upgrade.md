### Post by Mark Phelps on 2011-10-17
There's nothing in Ubuntu that will do BIOS upgrades.

In general, these are done in MS Windows (or DOS) -- because the BIOS suppliers usually ONLY write BIOS update apps for MS Windows (or DOS).

Some PCs have the option of pressing a special key combination that will then allow you to do BIOS updates without booting into an OS.  You will have to research your motherboard documentation to see if you have that.  Or, do a Google search on your make and model of motherboard.

Be advised, that hosing a BIOS update is a sure-fire way to turn your PC into an "electric brick"!
