### Post by QIII on 2017-05-05
Hello!

Your screen is not "bad" at all.  It just manages to hold 4x the "space" of an HD monitor.  That is, the pixel density is much greater.

You can start to change some of the appearance settings by clicking the "K" button (by default in the lower left), going to "System Settings" and modifying settings in "Application Appearance" and "Workspace Appearance".

### Post by QIII on 2017-05-05
Right click on the panel (what you are calling the "task bar") and you can change settings.  There is a panel height setting available.  Making the application icons larger will also make the panel height larger.

Cheers!
