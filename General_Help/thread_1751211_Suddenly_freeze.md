### Post by Rubi1200 on 2011-05-06
Have you tried booting an older kernel? Does the problem also occur there?

Please try and avoid hard shutdowns; you can cause damage to the drive and the file-system.

Instead, on Linux use the magic key for a soft reboot:

Press Alt+SysRq (Print Screen key) at the same time and slowly type out these letters R E I S U B
