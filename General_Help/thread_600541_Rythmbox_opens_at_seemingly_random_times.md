### Post by hikaricore on 2007-11-02
Only thing I can think of is that you need to check the keyboard shortcuts in your preferences and change the key for rhythmbox to something else.
My best guess is that somehow the way you have your joystick mapped is causing this strange behavior.  Changing the keybinding for the program may resolve it.

**And for the record removing ubuntu-desktop is fine, it won't affect upgrades in the current version.**

_If you decide to upgrade to a new Ubuntu release, you will just need to reinstall it.  This process will take all of 20 seconds anyway._  ^_^
