### Post by HermanAB on 2007-07-29
A VGA cable is usually quite conveniently colour coded as red, green, blue.  Use those three and the earth braid - ignore the rest of the wires - just cut them square so they won't short out - otherwise Google for the VGA pin-out.  Look at the back side of a VCR - it probably uses RCA connectors - get three at Radio Shack (or whatever it is called nowadays) and grab a soldering iron.

It is easy to do, but the result will look somewhat like that cloak avatar of yours. It sure ain't gonna be purty. It is a long time since we thought that a Commodore 64 or BBC computer had state of the art graphics - say hello to the seventies for me...
:)

Cheers,

Herman
