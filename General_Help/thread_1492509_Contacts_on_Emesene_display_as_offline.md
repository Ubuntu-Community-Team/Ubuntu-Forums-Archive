### Post by phara0h1 on 2010-05-31
The way emesene works for me is whenever one of my contacts signs on as 'appearing offline', changes their status (from say available, busy, away) to 'appearing offline' or actually signs out or is disconnected, signing them out, emesene notifies me that they are 'offline'.

This has the upside of enabling you to know when someone is signing in invisibly but can, as you've discovered, get confusing as it can be hard to know whether someone has just signed out from being invisible, or is signing in as invisible... if that makes sense.

Don't know what you mean by people appearing online only to those they're chatting to if they are 'offline' in windows live messenger, they always look the same as if they were actually offline whenever I used wlm to chat (except, obviously, that they responded, and frustrating sent one way nudges :P).

So, either you have the same 'problem' inherent in emesene as I do, or some bug which as you said randomly names your contacts as 'offline'. Asking them when this happens could clear it up, or asking someone to appear offline and see if it changes on your screen.
