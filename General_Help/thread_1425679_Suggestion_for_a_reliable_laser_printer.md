### Post by lyall on 2010-03-09
act like you are installing a printer to see which printer have the drivers for already in Ubuntu.

System > administrator > Printing

try creating a New printer
Select Device - LPT#1 (click Forward)
select the Makes ( the one you want to try)
then click on Forward
see what Models are available

try several different Makes and see which 
Models are available

it is just a thought

I just brought 2 Kyocera mita FS-1800+ printers for $10 each for work.
they had a new fuser put in about 1 1/2 years ago
yes they are 5 + years old, but they had less than 30,000 prints

they work GREAT

good luck and have fun learning
