### Post by HermanAB on 2009-07-13
Note that in a rural area, you could get multiple phone lines and use multiple modems to increase your bandwidth.  Of course it costs money, but what doesn't?  Two modems running together is almost as good as a normal congested city DSL.  The LART project has details on this.
