Thing is, iTunes is Windows/Mac only by default, You can try emulation, but nothing is guaranteed. Rhythmbox has Jamendo and CD ripping and tons of the stuff iTunes has.

You're right about those other apps supporting podcasts and iPods. If apps can handle them, why bother with the unsupported iTunes? That's just a question jumpin' around in my mind.

If you really really need iTunes and full functionality, use Windows or Mac. I like Rhythmbox personally and love it very much.

Another thing to consider is support; if iTunes crashes in Wine, then who will you go to for help in the Linux community?
