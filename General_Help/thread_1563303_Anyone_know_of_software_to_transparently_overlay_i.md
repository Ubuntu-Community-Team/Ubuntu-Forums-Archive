### Post by junapp on 2010-08-29
> **ubun_two said:**
> Can you use Gimp for this?  Just each image on separate layers and drop transparency of one on the top.
On the top most layer, change the mode to "difference" for a very quick identification where the two layers differ. Black will represent where they are the same.
