### Post by Drewvt on 2013-06-24
Well, since you are using Gnome on 12.04, same as me: I find that whenever Firefox does strange things, especially when it displays certain pages in weird ways at seemingly random intervals, the culprit is two add-ons: Global Menu Bar Integration and Ubuntu Firefox Modifications. I know that these are supposed to be useful somehow or other, but they really don't do anything for me that I can see, and more importantly, disabling them corrects whatever weird behaviour Firefox might be going through. You should give that a try at least.
