Hi

When I minimise a document in 10.04, it 'disappears' into an icon located on the bar at the bottom of my desktop. This behaviour makes it easy to re-activate/unminimise a specific document - I just click on the associated icon.

In Unity, in 11.10, minimised documents seem to disappear into the launcher of the parent application, so that it becomes impossible to unminimise a specific document. ### Post by stinkeye on 2011-10-29
If I click once the last minimized window raises.
If I click again while the  window from that group is in focus I get the window picker.

If you have a window from that group open but not in focus it will bring that window to the front.
 
ie the window picker only initiates when a window from that group is in focus.

If you use the alt+tab switcher  after a short delay the grouped widows will be shown.
You can then cycle through these while continuing to hold down the alt key while pressing the ~ key.

You can turn off the automatic showing of grouped windows in  ccsm > unity plugin > switcher .

So you can alt tab to the group then hit the ~ key to see the picker and cycle through with subsequent ~ key clicks.
