### Post by dabl on 2011-03-17
1. There are square buttons in the lower right corner.  The next to last one is "Track Progression".  Put a "bullet" in your preferred mode, probably "Standard".

2. You can right-click on the "K", and choose "Menu Editor" to edit the entries.
