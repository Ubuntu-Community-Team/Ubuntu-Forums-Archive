### Post by HotShotDJ on 2007-07-01
Some private networks (such as college/university, business, coffee shops, etc.) block access to certain services, such as P2P networking.  This does not seem to be your case.  You may need to configure your firewall via firestarter to allow incoming connections for whatever ports frostwire uses.
