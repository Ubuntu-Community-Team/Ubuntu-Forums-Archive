### Post by Breepee on 2006-04-03
Native Windows games work through wine or cedega, or not at all. Generally the high profile games like Half Life 2 and Grand Theft Auto 3 work better than the 'smaller games'. I happen to know for a fact that Age of Empires 1 and 2 cannot be played at all, the other ones, I don't know. You can check the wine and cedega playability lists.
