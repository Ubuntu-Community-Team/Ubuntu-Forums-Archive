### Post by BoneKracker on 2010-06-06
Most of them are integrated with email programs.

One option would be Mozilla Lightning, a new product in beta now, which will be available with either Thunderbird or Sea Monkey.  Since you were going to use Firefox anyway, you could simply use Sea Monkey instead, and you'd have both your web browser and your agenda in one program.

You could also use Google Calendar, which would only work when you're online, but would have the advantage of being able to access the agenda from other computers (a likely requirement, I think) without any fuss.

Since both of these are accessed in the browser, you achieve your "Full Screen" requirement by virtue of the browser's ability to operate in fullscreen mode.
