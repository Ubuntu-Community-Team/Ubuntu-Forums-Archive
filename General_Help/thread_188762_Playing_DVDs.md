### Post by Donshyoku on 2006-06-04
If you look at the 3rd party list of applications on the forums index page, you will find a program called BUMPS.  This script will install tons of codecs for you, included in those are the codecs and players required for DVD playback.  You can even customize which packages you want, and just get the DVD software if that is what you want.

Alternately, you can install MPlayer or VLC which will play DVDs out of the box.
