### Post by im_ka on 2004-10-17
the main difference is gnome vs. kde. if you haven't used kde yet, grab knoppix (debian based live cd) and check it out. it's a matter of taste, but i don't like kde. i find it bloated (they have an own sound daemon for example  :roll: ), and gnome just "feels" better imho.

suse is imho not easier to use than ubuntu. they have a nice gui for installing and configuring (yast) things, but that's all. some fancy gui installer is not necesserely easier than ncurses.

the only thing that is _slightly_ simpler in suse is the nvidia driver. but it's really easy in ubuntu too.

just my 2 eurocents.
