### Post by Genius314 on 2008-10-23
You don't need a *specific* card, but some cards don't work too well. 64 MB (I think) should be enough for most effects, but you might want a slightly better card to be able to do more with Compiz.

You can read the pros and cons of Intel, ATI, and NVIDIA [here]("http://wiki.compiz-fusion.org/Hardware").

If you're still unsure, there's [Compiz-Check]("http://forum.compiz-fusion.org/showthread.php?t=8383"). And, of course, you can always just enable the effects to see if they'll work or not.

Hope that helps. :)
