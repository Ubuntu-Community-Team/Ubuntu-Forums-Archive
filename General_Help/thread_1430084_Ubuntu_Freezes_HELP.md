### Post by Groucho Marxist on 2010-03-14
The next time your computer freezes up, you can type the following command to safely restart;

```
Alt + Print Screen + R E I S U B
```

In terms of YouTube not working, it could very well be a problem with Flash on your computer. If you are using FireFox, type "about:plugins" into your address bar. At the top of your screen, it should say that both Shockwave and Flash ("Futuresplash") are enabled.
