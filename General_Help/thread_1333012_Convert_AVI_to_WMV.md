### Post by 3rdalbum on 2009-12-12
FFMPEG can transcode to Windows Media Video 8. It's quite easy to use on the command-line; (see "man ffmpeg" and "ffmpeg -formats" for more information) otherwise you can try and find a GUI frontend for FFMPEG.
