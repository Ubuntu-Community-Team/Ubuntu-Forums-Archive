### Post by hsweet on 2007-09-14
If you make individual accounts for users, they will each have a home folder and the system will save backgrounds, and whatever other browser or os preferences they choose.  Everything gets saved in /home/username somwhere or other.  (Sometimes in hidden folders)

Pretty much anything that you can download and get running on the server from the repository should work on the clients.  Apps, games, whatever.

Your mileage will probably vary with wine stuff, as it always does.

There is a good listserve for Edubuntu at [https://lists.ubuntu.com/mailman/listinfo/edubuntu-users](https://lists.ubuntu.com/mailman/listinfo/edubuntu-users)
