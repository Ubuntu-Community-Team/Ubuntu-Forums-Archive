### Post by ovis23 on 2010-10-12
Here's a workaround:

1. Create a launcher somewhere that it won't get in the way (I put mine in my home folder, but pretty much anywhere should be okay).

2. Make the name something like "Nautilus Launcher"

3. Make the command "nautilus ." without the quotes.

4. Press okay, and drag the new launcher into Docky.

It should work now - even figures out the right icon by itself.

Maverick, AMD64, Docky 2.0.7.
