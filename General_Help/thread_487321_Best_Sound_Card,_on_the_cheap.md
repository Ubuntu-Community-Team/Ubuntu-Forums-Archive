### Post by orasis on 2007-06-29
Soundblaster AWE 64, if you can still find it - should be no more than 10$, great card even by today's standards.

I make music myself and this card makes some great professional sounding stuff - really after 16bit soundcards.. the human ear really can't tell the difference anyways :P 

This is why 128bit soundcards etc, are just a waste of money you won't actually be able to tell any difference and audio always depends on the fidelity of itself, so if you have MP3's compared to WAVS for instance you notice a quality difference if you pay attention...

So yeah AWE 64, great card. 

Even an SB16 will do, again once you get to 16 bits - anything above that, bar a highly trained ear, there is no real difference.
