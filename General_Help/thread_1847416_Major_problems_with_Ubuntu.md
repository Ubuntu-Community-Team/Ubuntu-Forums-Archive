### Post by David Andersson on 2011-09-20
> **RAIDa23 said:**
> 
First off, the keyboard keys. Every time I want to use an apostrophe, it ends up like this: ¨You&#341;e¨

In System>Preferences>Keyboard tab Layouts you can add several variants of UK and US layouts, some of which called "international" including "dead" or "dumb" keys, such as your current apostrophe. Try selecting a non-international UK or US keyboard layout. Maybe (uncertain about this) you may also find "dead" or "dumb" settings in the Options dialog of the Layouts setting.

(Note. The menu path System>Preferences>Keyboard is for Gnome, I don't know about Unity.)
