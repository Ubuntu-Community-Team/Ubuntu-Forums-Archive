### Post by JayTee on 2006-11-17
another way of taking a screen shot of the cube is to use The Gimp and you can set Gimp to take screenshot with a variable delay in seconds so it gives  you time to position the cube the way you want it. Just go to Applications and open Gimp, from the File menu choose Acquire | Screen Shot and the dialog lets  you put in a time delay. It's so easy!
