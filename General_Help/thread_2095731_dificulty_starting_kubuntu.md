### Post by whatthefunk on 2012-12-17
Your machine should run it fine.  What kind of graphics card do you have?  
It might just be a bad install.  Download the .iso again, check the md5 sum, reburn it and try again.  If it still doesnt work, open up System Settings > Desktop Effects and try to disable some of them.  They can slow down a system and that might explain why some of the desktop utilities arent working properly.
