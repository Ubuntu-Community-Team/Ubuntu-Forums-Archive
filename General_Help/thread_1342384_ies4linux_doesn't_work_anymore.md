### Post by rem on 2009-11-30
Unfortunately, it looks like ies4linux project is not maintained anymore. For me, IE6 is the only browser that works properly with latest Wine versions. In order to test my websites with IE7 / IE8 and bypass methods such Virtualbox or others of this sort, I am using a Firefox extension called ["IE Net Renderer"]("http://nicopensource.free.fr/pages/ienetrenderer-en.php") which is using the great, real time, web service. However, even it helped a lot and I find it really great and simple to use, it is far from being perfect and not even close to the real thing.
