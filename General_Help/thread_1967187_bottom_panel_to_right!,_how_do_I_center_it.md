### Post by eco2geek on 2012-04-27
Add a separator to the right-hand side of the panel. (Right-click on the panel to bring up a context menu; choose Panel > Panel Preferences > Items > Separator.) Once the separator is added, right-click it to bring up a context menu, select Properties, and check the Expand box.

Make sure there's an expanding separator on the left-hand side, too, but from the sound of things, there already is. Both separators will expand in order to center your bottom panel.
