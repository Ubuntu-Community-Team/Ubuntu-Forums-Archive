### Post by 3rdalbum on 2014-08-17
Relax.

It sounds to me like radio interference. It's possible for radio waves transmitted in your area to be inducted by any amplifiers you have - including the one in your sound card, the one in your TV, any guitar amps, etc.

Or, to put it another way: Remember those sounds you used to hear on the radio when somebody nearby was using a mobile phone? The phone was transmitting radio frequency waves to the transmission tower, and some of the electromagnetic energy was being inducted into your radio's amplifier.

Simply, try moving your computer to a different part of your house away from any sources of radio transmissions.
