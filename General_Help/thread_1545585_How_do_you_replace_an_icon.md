### Post by ajgreeny on 2010-08-04
Right click on the icon that you have for "Computer" launcher on the desktop and go to properties, then click on the icon that will be showing in the top left corner and you will be taken to a dialog where you can choose another icon to use.  Most icons are in /usr/share/pixmaps, but there are lots of other places, so search around and you'll find them.

Doing that will only change that icon on the desktop, not other "computer" icons in nautilus, so it may not be quite what you're looking for, but it's a start.
