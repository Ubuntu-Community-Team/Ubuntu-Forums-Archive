### Post by finferflu on 2007-06-16
If you use sudo, logging in as root is not recommended, in fact is not safe at all. Use sudo instead, it gives you all the powers you need (superpowers!). It is always recommended not to log in as root anytime whether you use sudo or not. It's not a safe practice.
