### Post by winh8r on 2012-02-19
I am not running my 11.10 machine at the moment but I believe you will need to go into system settings> keyboard > shortcuts and look for the offending key combination and either change it or disable it.

Screenshots can be taken when required by pressing the print screen key (or PrtScr as it may appear).

So you can safely disable whatever is causing you the problem in keyboard settings.

You can just search for keyboard shortcuts in the dash search box too.
