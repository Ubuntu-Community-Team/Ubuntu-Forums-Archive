### Post by jazzgossen on 2007-05-17
I think the beagle icon comes from starting "beagle-search --icon". Have a look in your sessions settings (System/Preferences/Settings/Sessions). I just edited the beagle-search entry there to remove the "--icon" part, and then I chose "quit" on the beagle-search icon in the notification area. I think that did it.
