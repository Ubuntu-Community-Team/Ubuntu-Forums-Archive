### Post by DeMus on 2009-07-07
> **anw0625 said:**
> How do I save my folders and emails with thunderbird?

Thunderbird stores the mail, addressbook, etc in a folder called:
home/**[COLOR="Red"]username *[/COLOR]**/.mozilla-thunderbird
To be able to see it, open Nautilus, for example by clicking on menu Places > Home folder.
Then type ctrl + h (press the ctrl key and while holding it press letter h, then release both keys)
Now also the hidden files and folders (starting with . ) are shown.
In the .mozille-thunderbird folder you find a folder with a difficult name, probably a number. In that folder you will find more info about your account(s)

* Replace username with your actual username
