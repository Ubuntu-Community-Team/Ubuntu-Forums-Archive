### Post by -kg- on 2010-08-02
It's the same with my installation.  The only way I know of to copy a file from a location to your USB stick (or anywhere else, for that matter) is to select "Copy" from the right click menu, navigate to your USB stick (or other location) and select "Paste" from the right click menu.

If you drag and drop, you will also copy the file, unless it is on the same drive (partition).
