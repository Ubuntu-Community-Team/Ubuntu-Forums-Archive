### Post by LakeWind on 2012-04-30
First, boot from the 12.04 live cd (or usb if that's what you're using). After booting up, click on the "Install Ubuntu 12.04 LTS" icon on the live desktop. Click through the welcome screens and you will get to "Installation Type". At this point you will be presented with several options. Choose "Erase Ubuntu 10.04 and re-install". This will erase your current 10.04 install and provide you with a clean install of 12.04 LTS.

---

### Post by LakeWind on 2012-04-30
I forgot to mention, if you have a dual boot setup (10.04 & Windows, etc...) on the "Installation Type" screen you will be presented with an option to "Erase Everything". DO NOT select that option or you will erase your Windows (or other os) installations as well.
