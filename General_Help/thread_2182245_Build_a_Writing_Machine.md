### Post by tgalati4 on 2013-10-20
What you run depends on your hardware.  What processor?  How much RAM?  How big is the hard disk?  What is the graphics chip?

Very little difference between 64-bit and 32-bit for power consumption.  Very little difference running X-Windows versus just running a console.  If you need more battery life, then buy another battery or a bigger capacity battery.  Most laptops have crappy battery life and will always have crappy battery life--regardless of what you do.
