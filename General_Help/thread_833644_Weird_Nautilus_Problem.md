### Post by iaculallad on 2008-06-18
Try doing this workaround if it works with you:

Open your Nautilus, navigate to Edit->Preferences: In your "View" tab, Change your "View New folders using:" to "List View". Under the "Preview" tab, Change "Preview Sound Files:" to "Never".
