### Post by shae on 2010-01-25
Intel coordinates with Xorg to provide the driver for Linux.  You are already using Intel's official driver for Linux, unless Ubuntu did not install properly.  You should just consult setting the resolution to the proper one as sometimes, Ubuntu has problems detecting the correct resolution of your monitor.  Look here: [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/X/Config/Resolution](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/X/Config/Resolution)
