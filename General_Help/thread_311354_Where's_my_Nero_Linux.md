### Post by syxbit on 2006-12-02
it shouldn't be in the package manager, cause you installed it manually with a ".deb"
you can just type "nero" in the terminal. Or, better still (what i've done) just click "add a panel" on the panel. Then click "custom application launcher"
for name, and command, type in "nero"
then give it an icon if you want (i did a google images search and got a nero icon.)
then you can just click it
