### Post by flyingbrass on 2009-03-10
You could open multiple tabs in firefox with results from each city.

Start firefox and paste in your first search.  This searches Phoenix for "junk":

[http://phoenix.craigslist.org/search/sss?query=junk](http://phoenix.craigslist.org/search/sss?query=junk)

Open a new tab and paste in another city's search:

[http://prescott.craigslist.org/search/sss?query=junk](http://prescott.craigslist.org/search/sss?query=junk)

When you have all the tabs open you want, choose "Bookmark all Tabs" from the Bookmark menu.  Give the folder a name.  Then, to open them all at once, choose Bookmarks-> yourfolder->"Open all in tabs".
