### Post by neube on 2011-04-13
I was having problems burning mp3 cd's via Brasero.  It would burn them and look successful, even the track info was there, but the mp3's wouldn't play in my car stereo - they just sat at 00:00.  Yes it's an mp3 capable player :)

Here's the fix:
In Brasero, go to edit, plugins, and uncheck "Normalization"

There ya' go, worked for me.  Now I don't have to boot back into xp to burn an mp3 cd.

Hope this helps! :D

### Post by seawolf167 on 2011-04-14
Just wanted to add - I know there was a bug in Brasero a year or so ago with the normalization feature. Even with it fixed, normalization is a long process (especially for mp3 cds) and will sometimes take hours, so if you don't care to wait for Brasero to normalize the audio unchecking it like the OP said is an easy fix.
