### Post by manosx on 2010-01-06
Hello
Open the chat window, press **Font** (right above where you write) and then **Foreground colour**.
You can choose the colour. This applies to the current conversation only.
**To make a font colour default:** 
Go to **Tools**-> **Preferences**,choose **Conversations** tab.
You see part of the chat window, you can do the same as before there and that will be the default for all conversations.

hope it helps :)
