### Post by monkeybrain20122 on 2014-05-30
Well if it works for the other login but not you obviously the culprit is your user's configuration and re-installing GE would not help. Open the file manager, either press ctrl+h or choose 'show hidden' from the menu, find the hidden folder .googleearth and delete it should fix the problem or, less drastically, it may work by just deleting the file instance-running-lock in .googleearth.
