If what you want to do is put a whole bunch of emails in one "name" and then just put the "name" in the To: or Bcc: line so everyone gets a copy. then what you're looking for is usually called a "Distribution List"--don't ask me why, but when I went searching, it took weeks for me to find that out :-). 

Most email programs have the capability. I use Kmail because it's very easy to set one up in Kmail.
