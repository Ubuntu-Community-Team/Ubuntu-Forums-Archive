### Post by KonoKanji on 2007-03-07
I had a similar problem a while ago.

Take a look and make sure that your CD Drive can write on the specific CD-R[W] 
that you are using.
Also, after you click the 'Burn' button, go to 'Properties' in the window that pops 
up and make sure that your writing speed doesn't surpass your Drive's abilities.
Brasero can be very picky.
