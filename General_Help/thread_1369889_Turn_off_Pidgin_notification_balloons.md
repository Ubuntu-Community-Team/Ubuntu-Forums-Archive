### Post by scjody on 2010-01-13
1. Select Tools->Plugins
2. Look for "Libnotify Popups"
You can uncheck the plugin to disable ALL balloons, or:
3. Click "Configure Plugin"
4. Uncheck "Buddy signs on"
5. Close, Close.
