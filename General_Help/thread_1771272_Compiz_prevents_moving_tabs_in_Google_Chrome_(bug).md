### Post by stinkinrich88 on 2011-05-30
On Natty (11.04), when I log in an Ubuntu Classic session, I cannot move / reposition the tabs in Google Chrome. Instead, when I drag a tab, they instantly break off into a new window. 

When I try the same thing on an Ubuntu Classic (no visual effects) session, it works fine.

### Post by stinkinrich88 on 2011-05-30
Apologies for not googling this before. I found this forum:
[http://www.google.com/support/forum/p/Chrome/thread?tid=4c5a1e9e8825dd5f&hl=en](http://www.google.com/support/forum/p/Chrome/thread?tid=4c5a1e9e8825dd5f&hl=en)

Weird!

Still not a perfect solution, as I'd like my dock at the top.

### Post by stinkinrich88 on 2011-05-30
Turns out you *can* have your dock at the top of the screen, but you need to move your Chrome window almost half way down your screen before you try to reposition the tabs. Again, not ideal, but it's a work-around, I guess!
