### Post by mediajunkie on 2009-01-18
Hi All,

I changed HP5Si driver to CUPS+Gutenprintv5.0.2 instead of the foomatic one. and then I could print 11X17. 

(options now give me tray 1, 2, 3, ... as it is on the printer what makes it easier than the upper and lower tray) 

Note: 
In open office it still lists the upper and lower .... But if you select the paper source to be used from printer settings, you can make it work to print on the third tray in 11x17 or legal (depending what you want to use) 

hope someone finds this useful :)
