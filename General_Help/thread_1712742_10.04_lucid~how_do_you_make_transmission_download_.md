### Post by darolu on 2011-03-23
Torrent download speed depends mostly on the seeds/leeches ratio, make sure you choose torrents with a 1+ ratio (more seeders than leechers) for optimal download speed. You can help the download speeds by unblocking your client's port from any firewall or firewalls you may have, specially the physical one that is usually built-in in your router. In transmission you can see what port you're using by clicking on Edit - Preferences and going to the Network tab.
