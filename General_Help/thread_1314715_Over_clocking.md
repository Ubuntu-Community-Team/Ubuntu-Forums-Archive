### Post by jnew93 on 2009-11-04
If you mean from your CPU, the BIOS is where you need to overclock from.

If you mean for your GPU, I know of nvclock:  [http://www.linuxhardware.org/nvclock/]("http://www.linuxhardware.org/nvclock/") for nvidia cards. If you have an ATI card, I do not know of any overclocking utilities. If you don't want to compile nvclock from source:

```
sudo apt-get install nvclock
```

Cheers! :D
