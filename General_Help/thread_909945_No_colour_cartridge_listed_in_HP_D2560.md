### Post by editrix on 2008-09-06
Not sure what your question is. Is HPLIP saying that the colour cartridge is not installed, or is it saying that the 2560 doesn't come with a colour cartridge?

I had the former problem (different printer) and it turned out I hadn't shoved the cartridge in hard enough. If it's the latter, you could try a different driver--just install a new printer with a driver in the same number range, and see if that works.
