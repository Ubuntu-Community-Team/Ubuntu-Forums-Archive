### Post by drs305 on 2009-03-22
Envy just helps install proprietary drivers. Normally you will have to uninstall any drivers currently installed before proceeding. Whether or not envy will help depends on which driver is currently installed and which one envy is going to install.

I have used envy in the past and it worked well for me. Since Intrepid I have just let the system handle the driver updates and no longer needed envy.
