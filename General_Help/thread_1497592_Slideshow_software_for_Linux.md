You might want to look into the slideshow authoring software "Imagination" ([http://imagination.sourceforge.net/](http://imagination.sourceforge.net/)), particularly if you are interested in creating a DVD. Audio track and transition effects are supported, as are so-called "Ken Burns" effects (zooming in/out of a still picture to impart a feeling of motion).
