### Post by cmnorton on 2008-11-30
I am not sitting in front of my Kubuntu system right now. Other than question 2, you should be able to click on the "K" icon and find your system settings. You'll get a panel that will display what are the current settings, and an Administrator mode button at the bottom right. You'll click on that and use your password, assuming you were the one who installed Kubuntu.

When I get back to work tomorrow, I'll try to find the name of the applet that stores all my active programs.

As to #2, you can try this. Run the Taskbar applet on the KDE panel. You can add this by right clicking on the KDE panel and selecting Add Applet and selecting Taskbar.
