### Post by dandnsmith on 2011-01-08
If the browser an mail stuff is still working under XP, then I agree that there probably is no hardware fault.
This, however, doesn't mean its FireFox at fault (does it open, and then say it cannot find the server?).

How do you connect to the 'world' - do you have a wired connection, or is it wireless?

Have you looked at the network icon (right hand end of top taskbar) to see what it says (hover the cursor over it)?

If you have a wireless connection, you may have lost the 'connection', so need to click on the icon and re-select network and password.
