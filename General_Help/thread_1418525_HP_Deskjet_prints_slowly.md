### Post by Jared Norris on 2010-03-01
I can't say I've encountered this problem before as I have found HP printers to be great under ubuntu. If you are having problems I would suggest trying to delete and then re-add the printer as a new printer under "System > Admin > Printing".

If this still does not work have a look at [http://hplipopensource.com/hplip-web/index.html]("http://hplipopensource.com/hplip-web/index.html") and see if the latest HPLIP has and performance increase.
