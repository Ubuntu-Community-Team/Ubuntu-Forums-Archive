### Post by betrunkenaffe on 2010-07-12
Right click on them, if Lock to Panel is checked, click it and right click again. Click on move, move applet.

Note: If you want to reconfigure the panel layout, I'd recommend unlocking all the applets that are in the area of the applet you want to move. Specifically all the ones that are between start point and desired end point.

### Post by VCoolio on 2010-07-12
Don't click the panel, click the applet you want to move. In the case of the pandora icon I guess it's the notification area or what it's called. Right click the three little vertically aligned bullets and move the applet. You can't move the icons within that applet (you'll need to start them in the right order for that), so you can't move pandora to the left of the chat icon.

### Post by libssd on 2010-07-12
Position can be tricky:

[LIST=1]
[*] If you right-click in an empty part of the panel, you get the add, properties, etc. choices.

[*] If you right-click on the Notification Area or Window List applets, you have to click slightly to the left (depending on your panel preferences, you may or may not be able to see a faint vertical double-dotted line -- click on this).

[*] For most other applets, right-click on the icon should offer the move/lock options.

[*] Icons for some applets (*e.g.*, Disk Mounter) are invisible unless active; if Disk Mounter is installed on your panel, insert a USB flash drive or other device, then move/remove the applet.
[/LIST]
