I installed a new program. The installation placed a link (short cut in windose terminology) on the desktop but the icon was somehow lost.

### Post by Gourgi on 2008-05-18
right click the "shortcut" and select **properties** 
then you will see the **basic** tab of the properties
all you have to do is click on the icon (just under **Basic** and left of **Name** )
then browse to your .jpg or .svg file you want your "shortcut" to have ;)
that's all !
