### Post by Skripka on 2008-11-23
Is Open Office an acceptable (freeware) alternative for your purposes?  This would be linux native--and will work and run fine on your system.

According to:

[http://appdb.winehq.org/appview.php?appId=31](http://appdb.winehq.org/appview.php?appId=31)
