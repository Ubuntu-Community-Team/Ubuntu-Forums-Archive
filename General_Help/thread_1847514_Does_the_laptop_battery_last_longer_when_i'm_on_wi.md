### Post by Mark Phelps on 2011-09-21
Battery life is going to be longer in whichever OS conserves power more.  A laptop preconfigured with Win7 is going to already have the default CPU scaling options turned on.  It may also have other power saving options like screen dimming, sleep mode, etc.

If you can get the same power saving options configured in Ubuntu, you should get better battery life in it, than in Win7.
