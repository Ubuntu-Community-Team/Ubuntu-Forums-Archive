### Post by seawolf167 on 2011-02-27
There could be many causes, but the two that jump immediately to mind are (if the clicking occurs at all times):


[LIST]
[*]loose cable/wire in your box which is coming into contact with a running fan causing a clicking noise
[*]hard drive failing, off balance, etc. causing a clicking noise
[/LIST]
If its something actually coming out of your speakers, well then we'll need alot more info on what your computer setup is like
