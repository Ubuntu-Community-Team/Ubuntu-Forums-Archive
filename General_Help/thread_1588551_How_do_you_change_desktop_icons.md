### Post by brujoquizz on 2010-10-05
It should work the same as ubuntu. Click on the desktop and choose "change background". Then go to "themes" tab and drag and drop the icon theme you want to install.

If you only want to change only some specific icons, move them into a folder. After this right click on the icon you want to change. Then go to its properties, click on its icon and select the icon you want. It should be intuitive.
