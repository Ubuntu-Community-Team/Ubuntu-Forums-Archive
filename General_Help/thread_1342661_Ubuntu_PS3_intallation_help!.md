### Post by jbrown96 on 2009-12-01
I believe you need to type something like boot-game-os at the kboot prompt to get back to the PS3. You can also hold the power button for ~15seconds. The downside to this is that it resets your resolution, which is kind of a pain (but much better than a unusable system!).
