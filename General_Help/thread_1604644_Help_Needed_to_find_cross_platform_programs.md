### Post by Boondoklife on 2010-10-24
This answer will not be exactly the one you want, but you could use 7zip to do all of this except the burning.

You could store your passwords in a text file and then use 7zip to package and encrypt it, decrypting it with a master password when you need to see your list. 7zip can also edit ISO images.

The great part is it meets your free requirements. As far as the burning bit, you could just make the iso and use the default burner software for the computer and burn the ISO to an image.
