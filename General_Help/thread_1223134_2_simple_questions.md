### Post by starcraft.man on 2009-07-25
I can only answer second. Simple question. Just right click on system, and select edit menu. After that, scroll down on the left pane, click system entry and on the right you'll see 3 options. The first two are preferences and administration menus, the third is the control centre. Check it and it will add to your menu. If you want a desktop icon after that just drag and drop the icon in the menu to the desktop or menu bar.
