### Post by Nosferax on 2010-07-14
Have you ever heard of StackExchange (StackOverflow)? It's a type of Q&A site which allows people to vote on questions and answers to problems. I think the Ubuntu community would benefit greatly from using such a site as it would give some structure to the asking and answering of questions, as well as incite people towards answering questions. The programming variant of those Q&A sites, StackOverflow, has greatly reshaped the world of programming Q&A - I think it could do the same with Ubuntu. The forum media is just not the best for this type of activity.

There needs to be a reasonable amount of people who commit to participating before the site is launched for real.

If you want to learn more about this go here and commit to participate in the Ubuntu Q&A website!
