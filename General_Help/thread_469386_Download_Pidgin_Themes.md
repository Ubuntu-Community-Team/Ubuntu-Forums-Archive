### Post by Hairy_Palms on 2008-05-19
just for the record you can make pidgin or indeed any gtk app use its own gtk theme instead of the selected system theme by running it with

GTK2_RC_FILES=/usr/share/themes/insertthemefolderhere/gtk-2.0/gtkrc pidgin
