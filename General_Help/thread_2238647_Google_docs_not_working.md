### Post by JDorfler on 2014-08-12
Not real sure, but I do believe Google Docs is going the way of the Dodo.  You'll find your documents in Google Drive.  so, here is what I recommend while you are in your home directory, or the directory you want to sync your Documents in.

Be sure you are logged into your Google Account via your web-browser of choice.

[CODE}sudo apt-get install grive
grive -a[/CODE]

You will see a URL generated by grive.  Click, or copy and paste the URL into the web-browser you are using to log into your Google Account.  Follow the instructions and you will get a key.  Copy and paste that into your terminal and hit enter.

Anytime you want to sync, make sure you are in your folder you wish to sink up and just type grive.

```
cd ~/"folder you wish to sync"
grive
```
