### Post by synapsys on 2009-07-30
It sounds like you didn't give the root partition enough space when you installed ubuntu. The root partition is where all the operating system Files are stored. Your crashes probably have more to do with lack of disk space rather than the updates. There are ways to increase your root partition, but I think at this point it would be best for you to re-install and give ubuntu enough disk space this time. There is a great guide at [howtoforge]("http://www.howtoforge.com"). Go there and search for the "perfect desktop" for your version of ubuntu.
