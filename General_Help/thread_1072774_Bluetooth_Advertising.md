### Post by mb_webguy on 2009-02-17
The phones must have an application running to receive messages sent to them, and (I believe) would have to be paired with the sending device.  Neither of these can be done automatically (unless you're the manufacturer of the phone and/or the phone company).

Good luck getting your customers to install a Bluetooth messaging client and pair with your device just so they can get to decline your ads.  ;)
