### Post by dasunst3r on 2008-04-03
There was an applet in Avant Window Navigator (AWN) that makes your libnotify popups look like that.  Go into the preferences of that, and then take a look at your Applets section.  Disable the one that is something along the lines of "Awn Notification-Daemon."

More information: [http://wiki.awn-project.org/Awn_Notification-Daemon](http://wiki.awn-project.org/Awn_Notification-Daemon)
