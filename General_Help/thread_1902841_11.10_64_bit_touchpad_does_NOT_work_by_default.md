### Post by mswoon on 2011-12-31
Googling around, I found that I had to add the following in /etc/rc.local: 

modprobe -r psmouse
modprobe    psmouse proto=imps

---

### Post by mswoon on 2012-02-03
As of today, the touchpad is now working fine and I don't have to use my USB mouse. I apply new patches everyday, and the last time I tried to live without my USB mouse was around Mon / Tue, so some new patches in the last 5 days resolved the problem. 

To whoever who developed the bug-fix, thank you!
