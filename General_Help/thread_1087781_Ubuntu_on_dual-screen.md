### Post by modestmelody on 2009-03-05
I love running with multiple monitors.

On the cube, I've had it work different ways (I think Beryl it had one cube that rotated together as a single face, on Compiz it was two cubes rotating, although I'm pretty sure this has to do with how you configure your dual monitors through xinerama or twinview).

There are a few ways to configure it but my favorite was as a single desktop spread across two screens but with maximize buttons only maximizing to one display.  Drag windows from one to the other with ease, multiple documents up simultaneously, widgets/jukebox/IM on one screen and internet on the other.

It's one of those things that as soon as you use it you get it.
