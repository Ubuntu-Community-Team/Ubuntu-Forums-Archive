I suspect that your wireless card is the problem. The "strange shutdown" you mentioned was probably a symptom or maybe the cause of the card failing.  You may need to get a new wireless interface (ie a card or an adapter).  If you decide to get a usb wireless adapter, check that the model you want will work with Linux before you buy it - I've heard that some are not compatible.
