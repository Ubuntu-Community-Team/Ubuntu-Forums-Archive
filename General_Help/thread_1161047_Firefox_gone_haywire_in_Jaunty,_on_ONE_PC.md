### Post by Yingy on 2009-05-16
Hi, I'm new to Ubuntu but it sounds like you may have font trouble.

Try to install more fonts, example the ttf-mscorefonts-installer.  Also FF allows you to use the website page fonts or fonts of your choosing.  In FF go to Edit->Preferences->Content.  Under Fonts and Colors choose the advanced tab.  You can check or un check the box to "Allow web pages to choose their own fonts, instead of my selection above"  

Good Luck.
