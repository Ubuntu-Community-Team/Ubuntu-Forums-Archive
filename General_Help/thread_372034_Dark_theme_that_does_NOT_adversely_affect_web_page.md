### Post by kurbach on 2008-04-10
Do you mean only for good reading?

I had the problem as well, solved it simply:

Firefox: Edit -> Preferences -> Content -> Colors

Then unchecked the "use system colors" box and checked the "websites may use their own colors" box

Works ok, but still i have some dark boxes in some websites, like the one i am typing in right now. But at least all content is readable - which is the main thing imho.
