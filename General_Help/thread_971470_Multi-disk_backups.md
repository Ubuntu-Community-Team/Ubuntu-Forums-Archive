### Post by arch0njw on 2008-11-08
The "old school" way of spanning multiple discs with a backup is to use *tar* with the "M" option.

If you are talking about "only" 15GB, you might want to consider even a small external HDD so you can back everything up without having to worry about splitting it.

Is this a regular backup you want to perform?
