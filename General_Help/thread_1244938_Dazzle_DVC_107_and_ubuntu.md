### Post by kvarley on 2009-10-09
You need to go to: System>Administration>Users and Groups

Unlock the groups.

Manage the groups, find one called vboxuser or something similar and tick the box next to your username.

Now run vbox and pass the dazzle through using the device menu. You will then be able to see it in vbox.

Hope this helps.
