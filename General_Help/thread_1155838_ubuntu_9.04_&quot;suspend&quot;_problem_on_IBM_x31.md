### Post by Rogbert on 2009-06-21
I have an x31 (looooove it) and had this problem too.  Spent tons of time trying to figure it out.  In the end it wasn't the software.  On the bottom right of your screen is a little nub that depresses a tiny button at the top right of your keyboard.  Grab a paperclip or pen or something that you can depress the button and see if something happens.  If so you need to adjust the screen position or find some way to enlarge the little nub.
