### Post by linuxuser12345 on 2011-07-01
Open Firefox, then go to:
Tools... Clear Recent History... Time range to clear: Everything... Details: Have all check boxes checked.

Click "Clear Now" and you should be good to go! :)

---

### Post by linuxuser12345 on 2011-07-01
You can also do this:

Open Firefox... Edit... Preferences... Privacy.
Make sure the box next to "Tell web sites I do not want to be tracked" is checked. Click "close".
