### Post by Frogs Hair on 2011-12-03
Ubuntu Tweak won't tailor the size of the image to the size of your monitor like appearance preferences . I have found this when using the same login and background image . The login image will appear larger than the desktop background even though it is the same image .

Using the proper image size for your resolution helps and some times you can compensate with the adjustments in appearance preferences . When I choose background images larger than the screen resolution I scale them to the correct size with gimp .
