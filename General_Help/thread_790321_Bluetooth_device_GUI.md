### Post by NilsE on 2008-05-11
If you are on Hardy and have the Bluetooth icon on the panel, right click on the icon and select preferences.  Then go to the Services tab and single click input service. You will then see an ADD button on the bottom so click on it.  Make the mouse discoverable and it should show up and you can select it.  You may have to repeat this until it sees the mouse.
