### Post by cryogenic on 2008-04-29
The easy way to fix this is to install the extended prefs plugin and check the box that says "allow buddylist to shrink below normal size constraints". That won't solve the problem of it randomly stretching out to 75 screen widths wide but it'll at least allow you to bring it back in line when it DOES do it (and it will in fact do it as it has to me at least 5 times in the past day).
