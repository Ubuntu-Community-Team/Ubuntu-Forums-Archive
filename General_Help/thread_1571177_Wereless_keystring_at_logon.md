### Post by ajgreeny on 2010-09-09
Right click on the network-manager icon in your panel and choose "Edit Connections".  Go to the wireless tab and highlight your connection, then click "Edit" and in the dialog box that appears put check marks in both top and bottom left boxes, "Connect automatically" and "Available to all users".
