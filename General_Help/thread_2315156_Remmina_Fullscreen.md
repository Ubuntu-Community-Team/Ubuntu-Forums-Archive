### Post by newbie-user on 2016-02-29
Edit your preferences for your saved remote connection. Change the Resolution to "Use client resolution". Alternatively, choose a custom resolution, then click on the options button (...), and within the Options tab, change the Default view mode to Viewport fullscreen.
