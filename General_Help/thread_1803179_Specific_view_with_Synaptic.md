### Post by ClientAlive on 2011-07-12
If you go into Settings > Filters from the menu items you can make custom filters. Then, once you have one (or more) made, you can click the "Custom Filters" button near the lower left corner of the main screen to choose it/ run it.

I'm not sure what all options are available for custom filters but it might be worth checking into.

"Orphaned" is an option under one of the tabs . . .

The "section" tab looks interesting . . .

There's also Settings > Set Internal Options but warns you that you should really really know what you're doing before you mess with it.


HTH
