### Post by nedecor on 2007-05-26
First of all, this is quite a task for someone new to Ubuntu. Wine isn't the easiest thing to deal with.

Secondly, this forum is specific to multimedia production, and multimedia production only. Install, setting up, configuring and running wine belongs in another forum topic. Even though Flash is a multimedia program, your question is more directly related to Wine, which probably belongs in General Help. (NOTE: MODS please move this topic elsewhere)

[jumps off soapbox]

First of all make sure you have run winecfg at least once after installing. That allows everything required for wine to be initialized. If you don't programs will not run properly. Second, Flash MX is confirmed by the Wine AppDB to be be supported at a Gold Level, meaning it >90% works. Please post what you have done and what hasn't worked. Find out more details at [http://appdb.winehq.org/appview.php?iVersionId=1027](http://appdb.winehq.org/appview.php?iVersionId=1027)
