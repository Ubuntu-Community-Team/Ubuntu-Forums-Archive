### Post by Chrissy on 2008-03-09
I'm a KDE newbie, so there may be other ways to do this that I don't know of, but in the bottom right corner of my screen, there is a battery icon. Click on that, it will bring up the Power Manager, where you can set what action to take (suspend, hibernate, shutdown) after so many minutes of inactivity.
