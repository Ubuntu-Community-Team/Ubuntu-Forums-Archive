### Post by edd07 on 2007-01-21
> **taurus said:**
> <Alt>F2 -> xfce4-panel

I think he means he has the panel (as he can launch Firefox) but lost the 'Start' button only.

To get it back, right click anywhere on the panel and click on Add New Item. You just need to select the Xfce Menu from the list, click the Add button and drag it wherever you want it.

Hope this helps

### Post by DigitalAxis on 2007-01-21
You can also configure XFCE (if it doesn't come that way) to launch the XFCE-menu when you right-click anywhere on the desktop.

I've got it set up that way, and it's so much more convenient than having to find the button on the menu I removed the panel icon altogether.

The relevant setting is on the 'Behavior' tab of the 'Desktop Preferences' setting.
