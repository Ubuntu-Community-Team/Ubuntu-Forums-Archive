### Post by LxP on 2006-11-12
Many people are advising clean installs over upgrades, and many people are proclaiming that their Edgy systems are running better than with Dapper, but my personal advice after performing two *clean installs* of Edgy on two computers (i.e. not upgrades) is this:

If you won't have the patience to re-format the computers afterwards and re-install Dapper if things do go ugly (i.e. too many bugs that really reduce productivity, like random freezes, the screen becoming garbled and X refusing to return to the login screen after a logout), then now is not the time to think about Edgy.

I hurried into installing Edgy as soon as the ISOs were available and while I really like the selection of newer software packages, my systems really aren't running well at all right now and I'm going to have to spend time re-formatting them again.  I really wish I'd left the machines alone.
