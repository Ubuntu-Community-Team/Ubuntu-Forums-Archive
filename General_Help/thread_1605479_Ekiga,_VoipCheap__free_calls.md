From the [Ekiga]("http://ekiga.org/ekiga-softphone-features") site:

> 
*Audio and Video free calls through the internet.
*Free Instant Messaging through the internet with Presence support.
*Audio (and video) calls to landlines and cell phones with support to the cheapest service providers.

If you are making calls strictly over the internet to another computer, then they are free of charge. However, if you are calling a landline or cell phone then a fee will be applied.
