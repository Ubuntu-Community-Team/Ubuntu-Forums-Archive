### Post by spcmediaco on 2011-01-15
Thunderbird issue fixed:

I hold my thunderbird profiles in a separate FAT32 partition...

I tried scanning the partition within Ubuntu but it said it was fine, however I noticed that when I tried to open Thunderbird it would stop letting me write to the drive.  I booted up Windows and did a quick scan disk and apparently there were issues with the thunderbird mail index files so it repaired them, now thunderbird works again!

Still not sure why the printers disappeared, however I will just reinstall them.
