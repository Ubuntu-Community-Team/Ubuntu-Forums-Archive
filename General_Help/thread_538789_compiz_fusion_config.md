### Post by phibit on 2007-08-30
I've had a similar problem with Compiz Fusion, and what I did was a complete reinstall.

First I removed compiz completely with:

```
sudo apt-get --purge remove compiz* libcompizconfig*
```

Then reinstalling Compiz Fusion as per the normal directions that are found in the "Desktop Effects & Customization" of these forums.

You lose all your settings but at least it works...
