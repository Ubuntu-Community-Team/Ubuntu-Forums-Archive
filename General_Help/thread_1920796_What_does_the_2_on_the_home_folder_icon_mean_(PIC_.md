### Post by grahammechanical on 2012-02-05
The Big Funny Button, also called Dash Home, also the Ubuntu icon, is number 1. The next icon down is number two and so on. When a certain key is held down the Launcher reveals itself and all the icons have numbers on them. Press a number and the program will launch.

I am not sure what the key is that you have to press. On 12.04 it is Ctrl. I am testing 12.04 and tapping Ctrl will open the Dash. Holding down Ctrl will reveal the Launcher and the icons will be number from 1 to whatever from the top down. Also a overlay will appear giving keyboard short cuts. But this is for the next release of Ubuntu in April.
