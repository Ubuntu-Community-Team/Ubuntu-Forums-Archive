### Post by deadflowr on 2015-03-03
RAM most likely isn't the problem.
If your system has enough ram to allocate to the game, then the game will get the ram, without the need for user interference. 

The more likely cause of slow laggy game play would be the cpu and the gpu.
What are the specs for the machine?
Primarily, the specs for the cpu and the gpu.(the graphics card)
