### Post by Hippytaff on 2010-12-21
You might have a dodge cd. Try downloading a new iso, check the [MD5SUM]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HowToMD5SUM") and burn it at the slowest speed possible. then try installing again. Also what are the specs of the machine your trying to install ubuntu on? I've never been able to even run a liveCD of 10.04/10.10 on anything less that 1GB RAM.
