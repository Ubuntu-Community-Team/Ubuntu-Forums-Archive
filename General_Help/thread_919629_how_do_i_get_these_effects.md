### Post by xj0hnx on 2008-09-14
The first one is Ring Switcher, activate it in CCSM. The second is Paint Fire on the Screen, also activated in the CCSM. Open the terminal and type ccsm, or go to **System**  > **Preferences** > **Advanced Desktop Effects Settings**. Once there find the desired effects, and make sure they are checked, and play with the options until you get them how you like.
