### Post by CatKiller on 2011-07-27
Those aren't the files. Those are the hashes that you can compare with the files (when you get them) to make sure they weren't corrupted by the download process.

I've downloaded the Humble Bundle no problem, you just have to click the "Download" link for Linux. As said above, the MD5 are hashes to ensure that the downloaded items are not corrupted. You can learn how to MD5Sum [here]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HowToMD5SUM").
