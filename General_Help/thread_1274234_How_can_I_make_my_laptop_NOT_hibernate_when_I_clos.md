### Post by bruno9779 on 2009-09-24
in  power management you have the option to chose what to do when the lid is shut.

the fastest way to get there is by right clicking the battery icon in system tray and chose preferences

---

### Post by bruno9779 on 2009-09-24
the choice i had on my laptop was "blank screen", but be carefull, some models overheat when the lid is shut

---

### Post by steve161 on 2009-09-24
system-preferences-power management.  Change the command for "When laptop lid is closed" .  You will need to do this under both "on ac power" and "on battery"
