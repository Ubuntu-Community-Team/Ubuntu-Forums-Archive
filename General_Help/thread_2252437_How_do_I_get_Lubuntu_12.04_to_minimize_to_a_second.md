### Post by Dennis N on 2014-11-11
> **jacatone said:**
> I'm use a Mac theme on my Lubuntu 12.04 install and wanted to have a second taskbar on the bottom with windows minimizing to that second bar. Anyone know how I do that?

Right click on the existing panel, and "create new panel". Right click on new panel, and then panel settings > geometry > position > edge > bottom. Configure the other choices for the panel appearance, and then in the panel applets tab, click add >  task bar (window list).

All windows will mimimize to all window lists. If there is one on the first panel, you will get windows minimizing to both panels, so delete the task bar from the first panel.
