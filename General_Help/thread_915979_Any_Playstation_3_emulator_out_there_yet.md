### Post by sdennie on 2008-09-10
The nature of the PS3 processor is such that it's unlikely that there will be an emulator for it anytime in the near future (at least not a realtime one).  If an off the shelf CPU were powerful enough to run the games, they would have just used that instead of a Cell processor.  Add in a layer of emulation and you are looking at a long, long time before a PS3 emulator comes out.
