### Post by erfahren on 2008-07-17
each of those are panel applets and can be removed, moved, or others added

to move them right-click on one (the date/time for example) and first "unlock" it if it needs it - then just right-click and "move" and you can move it anywhere on the panel

-- sometimes you need to unlock them all when clustered in a group to move them

-- sometimes you need to right-click in different little areas of the applet in order to get the correct menu

others can be added by right-clicking somewhere on the panal and "Add to panel"
