### Post by MoLE on 2007-02-03
Being able to do this depends pretty heavily on what sort of modem you have.  If it's an external or PCMCIA modem, your chances are pretty good, if it's a "winmodem", it can require some work and in some cases impossible.

There's a short intro to this with the appropriate links in the [official documentation]("https://help.ubuntu.com/6.10/ubuntu/desktopguide/C/connect-to-internet.html") (I assume you're running 6.10 - Edgy Eft).  Go right to the bottom of the page.
