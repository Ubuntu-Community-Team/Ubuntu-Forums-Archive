### Post by Mark Phelps on 2011-07-30
> **paulies said:**
> Also if I set it up for home folder encryption and my laptop gets stolen if they managed to change the password will the home folder still be encrypted with my old password details or for the new password.

Answer actually depends on who "they" happen to be -- really!

If "they" is just some person who walked off with your laptop, then your data is safe.  What they would probably do is wipe your drive and install whatever they want on it.

If "they" is some GOVERNMENT organization with access to state-of-the-art hardware capable of breaking decryption -- they will have everything they need from your hard drive (which they will remove from your laptop) in only a few hours.

The sorry fact of the matter is that encryption only really protects you against amateurs.
