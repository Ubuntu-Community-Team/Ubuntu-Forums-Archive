### Post by cariboo on 2008-12-27
I  would suggest backing up your bookmarks, and then create a new profile. Open up Nautilus (Places-->Home folder), then press Ctrl-h, this will reveal the hidden files and directories. Navigate to .mozilla-->firefox and delete xxxxxxx-default, where xxxxxxx is a random string of letters and numbers. When you restart firefox a new profile will be created.

Jim
