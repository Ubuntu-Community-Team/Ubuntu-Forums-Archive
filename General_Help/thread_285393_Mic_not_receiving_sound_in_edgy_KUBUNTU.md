### Post by Geomas on 2006-10-27
I had a similar problem after upgrading to Ubuntu 6.10. Other parties could barely hear me. The problem seems to be fixed after going to Volume Control: Intel82801DB-ICH4 (Alsa Mixer) -> Edit -> Preferences and selecting "Microphone Capture" and "Mic Boost (+20dB)." I then checked these options under the "Switches" tab.
