### Post by rfrayer on 2009-06-30
I wracked my head over and over trying to get this to work for a friend and it turned out to be way simple.

tried installing anywebcam.coms broadcaster through wine and it didnt work. turns out there was a firefox solution.

I simply added this to the menu

```
firefox -chrome http://www.anywebcam.com/webcam/start/
```then blam
