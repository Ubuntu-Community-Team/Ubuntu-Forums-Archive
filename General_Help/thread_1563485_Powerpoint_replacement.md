### Post by Lars Noodén on 2010-08-29
You can find several presentation graphics packages in the Ubuntu repositories. 

I presume you've not looked at OpenOffice.org's Impress nor KOffice's Kpresenter.  Both work well.  I've used OOo's presentation graphics heavily and prefer it to MSO's alternative.  However, neither are copies of MSO, and that is a good thing.  

The best proprietary one I've seen is Apple's Keynote, which is a bit like the old Concurrence from Lighthouse Design on the NeXT.

OpenOffice.org's Impress nor KOffice's Kpresenter are both easy to use, and both support the OpenDocument Format.
