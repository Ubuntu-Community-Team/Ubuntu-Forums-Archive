### Post by poptones on 2004-11-14
I have to do my downloading at public places that offer fat pipes because there are none locally. I really don't want to waste that time fighting with torrents that are running slow or people running p2p servers from their homes. 

Most ISPs do offer usenet as part of the package, but most customers don't even know what it is - and fewer still of those who do know even realize it's still in use. Because of that lack in popularity once you get outside big providers like roadrunner and earthlink the service does pretty well tend to suck. 

Though Astraweb isn't free, they have about a week's retention and they're fairly cheap - 25GB for $11 or so, with no expiration date. Easynews is more expensive ($10 for 12GB) and has way, way better retention (pushing a month). and I can pull from either of them as fast as my poor little 500MHz thinkpad can handle. Don't find many torrents that will sustain 3-5MBps.
