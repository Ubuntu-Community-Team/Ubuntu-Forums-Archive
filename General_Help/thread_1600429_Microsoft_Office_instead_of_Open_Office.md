Open Office does open MS formats and can retain its formats as well. You just need to click keep original format when open/closing MS Office files opened in Open Office.

You can also install MS Office 2007 on Wine 1.2.1. It runs Word and Excel perfectly for, especially after applying the necessary Winetricks additions as well as the two overrides in libraries: Riched20, usp 10.
