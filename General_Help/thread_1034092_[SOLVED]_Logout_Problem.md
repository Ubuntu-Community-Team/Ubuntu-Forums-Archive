Well hope it help, as i mentioned previously, i used ATI Radeon Graphic card, and since i activated the ATI/AMD proprietary FGLRX graphics driver (System > Administration > Hardware Drivers), i can no longer use my *suspend, logout, and hibernate* feature, only restart and shutdown work so far..., perhaps we should call it as a "bug":frown:, 

Solution : 
You can deactivate the proprietary hardware driver, then your system will go back to normal, at least for my laptop.
