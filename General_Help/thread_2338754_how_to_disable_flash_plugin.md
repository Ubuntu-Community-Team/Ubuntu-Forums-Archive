### Post by deadflowr on 2016-10-01
In the top bar of firefox, find the Hamburger icon click and select addons.
 in this new windows navigate to the Plugins sections.
Then toggle shockwave flash to Never activate.
Now flash is disabled.

Or, if Ubuntu, move your mouse to the top panel of the Ubuntu screen and the firefox menu will show, in here go to Tools, then addons and do the same thing as memntioned before.
