One is gnome-screensaver, the default screensaver for the Dapper release (to be released on June). The other one is xscreensaver, a more complete and tweakable version. They are both pretty much the same, since gnome-screensaver is based on xscreensaver. xscreensaver is almost completely tweakable and gnome-screensaver don't. Different approaches for different people. 

Choose the one fits you best and remove the other using **synaptic** (**System --> Administration --> Synaptic Package Manager**)
