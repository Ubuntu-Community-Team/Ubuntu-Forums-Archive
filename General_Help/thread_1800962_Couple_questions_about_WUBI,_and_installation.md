### Post by Frogs Hair on 2011-07-09
A traditional dual boot Is more stable and faster . The file system in a Wubi installation is virtual and performance may decrease over time . Wubi is ideally for trial use and you could move a Wubi installation to a partition if you wished to . [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1519354&highlight=wubi+partition](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1519354&highlight=wubi+partition)
