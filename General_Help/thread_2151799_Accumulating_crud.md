### Post by searchfgold6789 on 2013-06-05
When a computer (especially a laptop) is up for that long, it might heat up a ton, causing a CPU hang. This problem is caused by dust accumulating in the ventilation passages, which is why laptops are more susceptible. To me, it sounds like this is probably your issue - it would be worth it to take the machine apart and give it a thorough vacuuming.
