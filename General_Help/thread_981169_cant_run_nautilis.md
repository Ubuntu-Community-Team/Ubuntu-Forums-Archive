### Post by ricardisimo on 2008-11-13
I'm not sure I understand what you are describing, but many of us had a problem with Intrepid mixing up nautilus with all sorts of other apps. I would get F-Spot whenever I tried to open a folder.

Anyhow, try this:
[LIST=1]
[*]Right-click on your desktop and choose "Create Folder";
[*]Right-click on this new untitled folder and select "Properties" at the bottom;
[*]Go to the "Open With" tab and make sure that Nautilus is selected;
[*]Now try "Places" >> "Home Folder" again. If it works, go ahead and delete the untitled folder.
[/LIST]
Good luck.
