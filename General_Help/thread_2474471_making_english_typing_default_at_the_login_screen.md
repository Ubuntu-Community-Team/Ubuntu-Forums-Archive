### Post by grahammechanical on 2022-05-04
Try this. Open Language Support>Language tab. Hebrew should be at the top of the list with English lower down the list. Drag English to the top of the list. That might make English the default. 

If we have more than one keyboard layout then whatever keyboard layout is active at the time then that will be the alphabet used in the terminal and in applications and in the password panel. I am not sure if a password typed in an alternative keyboard layout will be recognized as a password. I am not willing to test it out.
