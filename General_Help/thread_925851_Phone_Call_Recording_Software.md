### Post by retrow on 2008-09-22
Do those phones have a audio out that can be used as line-in input for the sound card? If not, you can still open up the phone and tap into the audio out and solder a mono/stereo jack that can be used to connect to your line-in of sound card. Once that's done, its just a matter of using some recording software line Audacity as some suggested earlier.

This technology has been thoroughly tested since the cold war days, so you can't go wrong in implementing it :p
