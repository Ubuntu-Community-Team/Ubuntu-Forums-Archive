### Post by Tomosaur on 2008-04-17
I THINK (not 100% sure) that sometimes headphones are a seperate volume control. If you're on Gnome (Default Ubuntu), just right click the little speaker / volume icon in the system tray, then click 'Open Volume Control'.

In the box that pops up, click 'Edit', then 'Preferences'. Check through the menu that appears and see if you can find a check-box for 'headphones'. If there is one, make sure it's selected, then click 'close' and go back to the first volume control window. Look in there and see if your headphones are muted.

I think this only applies to some sound cards / setups, but it's worth a look.
