### Post by stoffe on 2008-05-03
Current flash status in Hardy:

swfdec is compiled without pulseaudio support (and draws tons of CPU with bad quality). No easy workaround.

flash from adobe needs libflashsupport installed but that crashes oftan and hard for many users. You might as well try it, works for some.

gnash has horrible quality and works on very few places, but it has sound.

No good solution exists at the moment that I know of, unless you are lucky with adobe's flash.
