### Post by Mardoct909 on 2008-11-15
Assuming that what was d:\ is now disconnected, you can safely, and are recommended, to choose the guided-use entire disk option.

If d:\ is still connected, I'd recommend shutting it all down, disconnecting it and starting again. 

Doing this will partition and install everything on what was C:\ for you.

Also, in the land of Linux, there are no drive letters. No C:\ or D:\ or whatever.
