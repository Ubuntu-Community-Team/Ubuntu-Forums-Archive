### Post by aeronutt on 2010-12-12
Many years ago (4ish?) HP done some weird thing on their desk top mother boards that required the MBR on the hard-drive to be at a specific location or format or such. Sorry I don't remember the details. But the bottom line was, you had to be very careful when dual booting or reformating the hard drive. Else, the computer became useless.

I don't know if they did it on their laptops, and I don't know if they still do it. But it made me avoid HP computers. But, I'm not sure others don't do similar things. I remember getting very frustrated with an HD reformat and Linux install, calling my friend that's the CTO of a small IT support company, and getting a 'yea, HP does that, I've got a handfull of HP's that are bricks because of it' response.
