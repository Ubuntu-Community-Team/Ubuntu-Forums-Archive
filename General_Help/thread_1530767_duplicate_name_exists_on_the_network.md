Your router will hold onto the information of network nodes until it does a refresh and finds that the name has changed to a particular MAC address.

You could reset your router by power cycling and it will then have to acquire a new map of your network with each MAC address and its associated names or just wait until the router updates.

I do believe it is a setting in the router how often it updates its network map,but I could be wrong.


Good Luck
