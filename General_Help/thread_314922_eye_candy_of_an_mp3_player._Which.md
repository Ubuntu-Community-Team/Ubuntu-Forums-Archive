From what I understand, MP3 has a restriction on it being MASS distributed.  So Ubuntu cannot re-distribute the ability to play MP3's without paying for a bulk license, which is against the 'free' policy that Ubuntu abides by.  That is why MP3 ability is not automatically included.  Please read through this guide:  [here]("http://easylinux.info/wiki/Ubuntu_Edgy#How_to_add_extra_repositories")
I have spent some time installing from this guide.  I don't recommend everything they say, I don't use Automatix2.  If you hit ctrl-f in FireFox you can search for amarok, or MP3.  You probably will want to go to 1.6.22 - 'How to install Multimedia Codecs'  You will probably need to enable a few more repositories. That is also covered in this guide.  Be carefull about adding repositories.  Just a word of warning.

Shane
