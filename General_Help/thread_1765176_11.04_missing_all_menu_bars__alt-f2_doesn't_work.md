I was however able to log in to Gnome Classic, though the option doesn't show up until you click on your name in the login screen. After selecting your login name, look at the bottom of the screen for a drop-down menu with a list of environments to run.

I have been looking, but so far have not found a way to run Unity with my Nvidia graphics card yet. Apparently a lot of other people are experiencing the same problem. If you think this is your problem, I can post a link to the thread I started about it, which may give you a little more insight.
