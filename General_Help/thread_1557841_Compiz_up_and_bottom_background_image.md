### Post by Ginsu543 on 2010-08-22
Open up Synaptic and install compizconfig-settings-manager. Once it's installed, run it. Click on the "Cube Reflection and Deformation" setting. Under the "Cube caps" tab, click on "Appearance" to expand it. You'll see two places, one called "Top image files" and the other "Bottom image files." For each, click on "New" and set the directory path to where the image you want resides. Once you've defined your images, use the "Up" button to make it come up at the top of the list. Afterward, your top and bottom cube caps should be set to what you want.
