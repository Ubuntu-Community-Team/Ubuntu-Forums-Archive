### Post by stuporglue on 2006-01-06
Make a backup of your xorg.conf first. It's at /etc/X11/xorg.conf

Then open a terminal and try reconfiguring xserver-xorg. It will generate a new xorg.conf for you.

$ sudo dpkg-reconfigure xserver-xorg

Log out, log in, and check if 3ddesk works. If it doesn't, try reconfigureing xorg again with different options.
