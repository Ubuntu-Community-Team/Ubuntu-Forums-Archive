### Post by jakemb21 on 2006-10-30
Poison0985, I am having this same freezing issue. Beryl works great for me when I manually start the beryl-manager after I log in. The way I was able to log in and remove beryl-manager from the startup programs was to change my Session. At the login screen, I changed the Session to Gnome Failsafe login. You should be able to remove the beryl-manager entry after logging in. Hope this helps and hopefully a fix will be out soon.
