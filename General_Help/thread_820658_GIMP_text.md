### Post by DM was on fire! on 2008-06-06
You can recreate the icons, but if you want them to look *really* good, you would need the layers so that you could make the text invisible. But, if you DID have the layers, yes, it is possible. :)

To make it so the text and the checkered transparent background on the GIMP would be visible, open up your Layers palette (Dialogs -> Layers) and click the eye next to the icon image (you'll see it in the little preview next to the name). :)
