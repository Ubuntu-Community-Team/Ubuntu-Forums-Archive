### Post by Partyboi2 on 2009-01-28
One way to change the picture when your screen is looked is to use F-spot photos. You can go to Applications>Graphics>F Spot Photo Manager and import the picture you want to use. Then right click on the pick and choose "Attach Tags>Favorites" Then go to screen saver (system>Pref>Screen Saver) and choose "F-Spot Photos" which should then play your favorite as the background when the screen is locked.
