### Post by deadflowr on 2014-02-18
> **chillering said:**
> The one listing icons of different programs on left side of desktop. I want to permanently remove amazon and firefox, and add google chrome in there.

That has nothing to do with nautilus.
That's unity's launcher.
Try dragging the launcher you want to remove  from the launcher dock into the trash can at the bottom.
Open the dash menu(the top icon in the dock) and type chrome.
Chrome should show up and then click on it to launch it.
When chrome has launched, a new icon will appear on the dock, right click that icon and select lock to launcher.

To fully remove both the amazon and firefox programs, try removing them from the software center.
