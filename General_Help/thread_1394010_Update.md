### Post by Zorael on 2010-01-30
> **Woody1987 said:**
> just go to system->administration->update manager
That's the GNOME location.

Just open the normal application menu type 'update' (don't hit enter). Click the entry that matches the search ("Software Updates: Check and Install Updates"). Follow its instructions.

Beware that you're potentially setting yourself up the bomb if you don't know how to recover from a packaging error and stuff goes bad. Beta is beta.
