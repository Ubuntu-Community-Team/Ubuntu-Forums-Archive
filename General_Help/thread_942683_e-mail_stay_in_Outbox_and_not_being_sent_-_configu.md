### Post by ellgor on 2008-10-10
Hi,

Go to the settings tab and pick configure kmail from the drop down options, a new window will appear with options for changing accounts , appearance etc., click on accounts, more options will be shown namely sending and receiving, click on sending, down towards the bottom of this window is the options on how your mail is to be sent, adjust accordingly, hope this helps.

Regards, Ellgor.
