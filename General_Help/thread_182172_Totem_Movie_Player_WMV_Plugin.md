### Post by Hezekiah on 2006-05-25
The easiest way is probably to use EasyUbuntu - [http://easyubuntu.freecontrib.org/](http://easyubuntu.freecontrib.org/)

Another option is to add the Ubuntu PLF repository and install totem-xine and the w32codecs package.  I don't know the link off of the top of my head, but a search for "ubuntu plf" on Google or Yahoo! should point you in the right direction.  Or here on the forums.

Once Dapper comes along, totem-gstreamer should be able to handle wmv fairly well too (it does for me on my test install).
