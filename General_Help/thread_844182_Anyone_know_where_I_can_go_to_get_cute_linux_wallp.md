### Post by Forlong on 2008-06-29
You can find lots of cute Tuxes here: [http://tux.crystalxp.net/en.2.1.12.html](http://tux.crystalxp.net/en.2.1.12.html)

They come in PNGs, thus you can use them as a wallpaper. Just right-click your desktop and customize the wallpaper. Choose the downloaded Tux and a fitting background colour.
