### Post by stooshbunutu on 2008-02-28
You could create one from you current system using[Remastersys]("http://www.remastersys.klikit.org/").

It will make a full backup of your System and make a Live CD out of a ISO Image of your Ubuntu Setup. You can set it to ignore the /home directory so that your data wont be backed up it will be just the system, with all the updates and any other software you want to add to the basics

For burning the ISO image on to a CD or DVD just insert the blank disk into your drive, right click on the ISO and go to Burn to Disk 
I say put the Speed of the burning down to 1x for best burn.

That will atleast cut out the 190 updates like I found out earlier this week when I re-installed my system (low band-width :( so much time)

Hope this helps
