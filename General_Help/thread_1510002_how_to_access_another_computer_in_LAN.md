Greetings,
Click Places, and then Network.
On the Network -File Browser click View and make sure that the "Location Bar" is checked. 
You should see the pencil|paper icon on the far left of the location bar. Click it to toggle the entry field on|off. 

I do not have my 10.4 machine in front of me... and something may have changed... but I think things still work this way.

Has something changed on your system, or is this new for 10.4 ?
