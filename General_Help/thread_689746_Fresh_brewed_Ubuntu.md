### Post by pointone on 2008-02-06
The main obstacle for running Compiz is usually the video card. Please let us know which video card is installed in your system.

Based on the other specifications you provided, though, I'm willing to bet your system can handle it. Recent NVIDIA and Intel GPUs can handle it right out-of-the-box. ATI cards require Xgl or manually installing the latest version of the fglrx driver. The fglrx driver included in the repositories does not support Compiz without Xgl.
