### Post by Brunellus on 2007-01-30
New fixes mean new bugs.  Beryl is alpha software.  Running it means you accept the risks of running alpha software--including crashes, breakage, and instability.

If you have detailed records of what you did and what went wrong, file a bug report.  Otherwise, chalk this down to experience and think twice before relying on alpha software.
