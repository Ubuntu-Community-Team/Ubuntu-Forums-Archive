### Post by Giblet5 on 2009-11-16
I've never used it and wouldn't consider it "safe" to burn a BIOS from a higher level than MSDOS ... with absolutely no drivers loaded.

Any error at all will "brick" your motherboard (may as well strip any useful parts off it and throw it in the trash at that point).

I keep a cheapo MSDOS usb stick around JUST for flashing BIOS upgrades.

You also don't want to ever flash your BIOS unless you have a UPS to prevent power failures. A power-fail during a flash will brick your Dell. Dude.
