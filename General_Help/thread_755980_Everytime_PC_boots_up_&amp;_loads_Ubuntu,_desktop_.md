### Post by JaxJagsfan on 2008-04-15
Everytime I turn on my PC, it boots up and loads Ubuntu but when the desktop starts, it appears stretched.  I find this so annoying because I have to move the mouse around to get to System on the top panel and then select Preferences then scroll down to and click on Screen Resolution.  Although it is set on 1024 x 768 but it is too stretched past the monitor borders.  The only way I can fix this is by selecting another screen resolution and after it changes resolution, I have to click on "use previous resolution" then click Ok.

### Post by JaxJagsfan on 2008-04-17
I think I've got the stretched out desktop problem figured out and fixed it by right-clicking on the desktop and selecting "Change Desktop Background".  While I was in that Change Desktop Background window, I changed the style from Zoom to Fill Screen.

Now it's been loading Ubuntu to my preferred 1024x768 screen resolution perfectly ever since. :)
