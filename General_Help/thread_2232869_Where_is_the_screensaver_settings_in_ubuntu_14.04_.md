### Post by ajgreeny on 2014-07-04
Screensavers have been removed from the default applications in 14.04.  If you want them back I suggest you add xscreensaver, not gnome-screensaver as it is much better and a lot more configurable.  You may need to remove or disable light-locker, depending on whether it is installed with xfce (it comes with Xubuntu but not sure about adding xfce to ubuntu) as it may conflict with screen locking settings in xscreensaver.
