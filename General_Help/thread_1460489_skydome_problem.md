### Post by labinnsw on 2010-04-24
Open CCSM (Compiz Config Settings Manager)
On the left panel under Category, click on "Effects"
On the right, click on Cube Reflection
On the right of the new window, select the Reflection tab
Increase the intensity "slider"
Adjust the "Reflection ground size" to your liking and close
