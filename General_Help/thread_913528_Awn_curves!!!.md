### Post by dadslayer on 2008-09-07
> **Q: I want my AWN to be curved like I see in all those screenshots!**
**A:** In gconf-editor, set apps->avant-window-navigator->bar->bar_angle to -1.
Source: [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=762363](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=762363)

Hope that helps :)
