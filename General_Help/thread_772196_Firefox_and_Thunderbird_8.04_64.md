### Post by ryanhaigh on 2008-04-28
You can just copy your profiles from windows and put them in the relevant places for ubuntu, although keep in mind that hardy uses firefox 3 so you may run into issues with themes and extensions.

Firefox profile is in ~/.mozilla/firefox
Thunderbird profile is in ~/.mozilla-thunderbird

So for example you would copy profiles.ini and your whateverishere.default folder into ~/.mozilla-thunderbird/

Incase you are not aware ~/ mean your home directory as in /home/yourusername/
