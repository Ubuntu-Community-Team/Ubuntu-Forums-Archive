### Post by garvinrick4 on 2010-10-17
Sounds like you are not booting off the cd drive. Make sure your BIOS has Cd booting in first order then reboot with CD installed in machine.

---

### Post by krishnandu.sarkar on 2010-10-17
Ya go to BIOS and set your DVD Drive to 1st Boot and save it and reboot.

You are actually booting from your HDD now.
