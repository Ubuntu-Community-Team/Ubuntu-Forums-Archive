### Post by Agent ME on 2008-12-29
Of course there are licensed and proprietary programs for Linux. I mean, computer games fall under that category and there are lots with Linux support.

Also remember: Wine supports many Windows programs, so you're not really limited to only things advertised for Linux.


You could probably google "Linux store" and find some websites advertising products that are for linux. Though I'd recommend trying out free alternatives to any sort of utility program; ie. try out GIMP and the Photoshop demo together to compare if you were thinking about getting Photoshop.
