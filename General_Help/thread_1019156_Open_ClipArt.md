### Post by todak on 2008-12-23
When you have started openoffice Writer, Calc, etc., click on Tools> Gallery and you will be presented with all of the clipart at the top of the document. Or you can click on the Gallery icon on the toolbar, located to the right of the yellow Navigator icon.
