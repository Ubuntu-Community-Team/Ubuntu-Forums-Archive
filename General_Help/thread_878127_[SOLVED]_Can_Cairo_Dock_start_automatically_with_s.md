### Post by collinp on 2008-08-02
Go to System - Preferences - Sessions. Open the tab that says "Startup Programs" if you are not already in it. Click the "Add" button on the side, under Name put: Cairo Dock. Put under command: cairo-dock. You can leave Comment blank if you want. Click on Ok, and the next time you reboot Cairo Dock should start automatically.
