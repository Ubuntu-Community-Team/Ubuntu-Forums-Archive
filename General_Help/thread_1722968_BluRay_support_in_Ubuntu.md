### Post by 3rdalbum on 2011-04-13
In short, it's possible to extract Blurays and play the extracted files using a piece of software called MakeMKV. It's much easier than using DumpHD (that is suggested on that linked page... I must really update what I wrote on there).

MakeMKV is commercial software, but it's very good. It doesn't work for all discs, but it can extract most of them in my not-too-recent experience. Playback works in any half-recent version of Mplayer.

So, in short: You can't play Bluray videos directly, but you can usually extract them and play the extracted files, and that's nearly as good.
