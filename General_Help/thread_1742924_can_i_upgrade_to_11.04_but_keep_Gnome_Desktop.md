### Post by noriek on 2011-04-29
Yes, you can. At the login screen, click on the login name, add the password and then you'll have the option to change desktop (at the bottom of the screen). Just select the Ubuntu Classic option et voila.

### Post by Elfy on 2011-04-29
You can't change unity to gnome. Unity and gnome are 2 different things. 

You can though use the fallback mode which is similar to the gnome2.x setup.

Logout and from the dropdown menu at the bottom choose classic.

I am impressed with the potential of Unity but wanted the option of the classic desktop as a fallback till I can see my msn and irc alarms at a glance.
