### Post by pollywog on 2008-11-24
I just had the exact same thing happen. I un-installed Firefox, deleted the firefox folder from within the ".mozilla" folder, which is itself hidden in the home folder. I then reinstalled firefox, and the problem was fixed. I just couldn't trust the browser after that. I've heard of firefox viruses that can attack the browser, regardless of OS. Supposedly you must delete the hidden "/home/user/.mozilla/firefox" file prior to re-installation to get rid of the virus. Luckily, it can't do much to the rest of our systems. Thank God I run Linux!
