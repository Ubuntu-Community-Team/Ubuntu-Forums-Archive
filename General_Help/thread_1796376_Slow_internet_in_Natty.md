### Post by linuxuser12345 on 2011-07-03
Do you have any cordless phones, televisions, microwaves, etc. nearby? Do you have a CRT monitor? Any of these can cause interference, thus can screw up wireless interfaces, like WiFi, Bluetooth and small radio frequency interfaces.
