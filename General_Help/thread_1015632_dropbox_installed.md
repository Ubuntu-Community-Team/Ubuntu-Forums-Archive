### Post by jtuchscherer on 2008-12-19
I don't have a real answer for you, but I might be able to point you in the right direction. Dropbox required Nautilus to run. Nautilus is the FileManager of gnome. When you run Xubuntu you don't use Gnome, but XFCE. (Gnome and XFCE are Desktop environments. If you are not sure what a Desktop Environment is, it is really well explained here: [http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Desktop_environment](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Desktop_environment)). So, to solve your problem, you need to either run nautilus within XFCE, which is possible or you google for a dropbox port for XFCE. I am sure you would fine one.

I hope that helps at least a little bit.
