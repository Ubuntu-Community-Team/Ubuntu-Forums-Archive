### Post by nrwilk on 2006-03-19
Also check out [www.linuxprinting.org](www.linuxprinting.org).

There you will find a database with listings of printers and drivers with excellent notes on how well the printer/driver works and which driver is best to use with each printer.  The site has thousands of printers, I'd bet it's pretty close to every printer available.

to get an example, check out this page which talks about the printer which I just purchased:
