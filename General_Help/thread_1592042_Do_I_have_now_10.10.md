### Post by PaulW2U on 2010-10-10
An alpha, beta or RC is only a snapshot of the release so far. Whatever you start off with will give you the final release once it is fully updated.

To check, type **lsb_release -a** in a terminal. If there is no mention of 'development version' then you have the final release.
