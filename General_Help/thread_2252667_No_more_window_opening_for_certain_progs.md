### Post by TheFu on 2014-11-13
Are you certain you mean to say "console" - since that means there isn't any GUI loaded at all, no windows at all, only 80x25 text.  Perhaps you mean terminal?

Based on the rest of the data above, I think you just need to reset the screen resolution - there are a few different programs for this - lxrandr, arandr or xrandr - any of them can be used.  Also, you could just have activated a different virtual desktop and need to switch back to the main one - different DEs have different ways of doing that - mine uses the <ctl>+<alt>+arrow keys.

Hope it is one of those issues. Good luck.
