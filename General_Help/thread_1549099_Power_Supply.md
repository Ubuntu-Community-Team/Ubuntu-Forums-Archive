### Post by lukeiamyourfather on 2010-08-09
Test the power supply outside of the case by itself. Short the green wire on the ATX connector with an black wire and it should power up (a bent paper clip does the job). Be careful as not to get shocked or burned by shorting out anything else. If you have a voltmeter you can check the basics like 12V line and 5V line to make sure they are working.

The motherboard could be the issue but it would probably at least start the power supply and fans even if the motherboard was bad. If the power supply works and the motherboard works then there might be a short somewhere in the system causing the power supply to trip and shutdown.
