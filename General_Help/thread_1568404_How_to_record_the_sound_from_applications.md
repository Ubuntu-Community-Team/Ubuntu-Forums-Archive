### Post by stinkeye on 2010-09-05
Install pavucontrol (PulseAudio Volume Control)

Start up whatever you want to record.
Open PulseAudio Volume Control and click on recording tab.
Open sound recorder and start recording.

In PulseAudio Volume Control > recording tab ....switch to the monitor of your output.

Now whenever you hit record it will be recording from the monitor
of your chosen output.
