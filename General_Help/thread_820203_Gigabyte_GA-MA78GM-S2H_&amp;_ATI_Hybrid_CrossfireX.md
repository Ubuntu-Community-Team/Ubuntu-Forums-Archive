I have read a couple of pages that add "for right now" at the end with speculation that maybe the open sourced radeonHD driver will eventually support it. I have also read that the radeonHD driver will soon support this IGP: [http://www.phoronix.com/scan.php?page=article&item=ati_r500_3d&num=1]("http://www.phoronix.com/scan.php?page=article&item=ati_r500_3d&num=1")

### Post by supercommode on 2009-01-28
I was wondering the same thing about Linux support. However, the point of the hybrid crossfirex is to use the on board HD3200 (780G chipset) and a lower end graphics card HD2400. You don't only have to use the 3400 series. crossfirex was designed so that the budget gamer could have a good gaming experience. Like you stated "might as well get the 3800x2" unless you are on a really small budget...
