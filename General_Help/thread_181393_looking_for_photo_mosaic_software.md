### Post by corzaker on 2007-06-04
Hello,
There are also some web services who create your photo-mosaic on line!
Example:

[pictosaic.com]("http://www.pictosaic.com/photo-mosaic")

You can choose your topics (cats, flowers, love, birthday, cars,...),
the resolution and some other options.

Then, you have just to send your picture, it will be back a few second later, as a photo-mosaic!
... And that is free :D

### Post by corzaker on 2007-06-17
Hello,
A new option on [pictosaic.com]("http://www.pictosaic.com") is now available
Your photo-mosaic can be merged with your original picture.
So, some details can be more preserved.
The options are:
- No merge (as the previous version)
- low merge
- high merge
- and centered merge. Useful if your subject is centered into a landscape, for instance.
