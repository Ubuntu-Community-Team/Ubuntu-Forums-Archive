### Post by Ben Wright on 2009-12-26
Generally community members only post in threads they believe they can provide help in if your question is to vauge it makes it very difficult to diagnose the problem. I would like to point you towards: [http://catb.org/~esr/faqs/smart-questions.html](http://catb.org/~esr/faqs/smart-questions.html) where you can find information on how to ask smart questions so you get your problem solved. Some tips to keep in mind include: A descriptive title, a solid description of the problem you are having including your version of Ubuntu, additional information such as your computer model if it has to do with a hardware issue or your graphics card if it has to do with graphics etc. Anyway if you read that paper you will get a better understanding of how an open community system works.
