### Post by Mark Phelps on 2014-11-17
> I think it's because of the video card drivers, How can this be solved? 

Basically, in terms of video card drivers -- it can't!  AMD dropped Linux driver support for the X600 cards YEARS ago, so today, you're using the default Radeon drivers, which are installed as part of setup.  Sorry, but there are no AMD drivers available for your card anymore.
