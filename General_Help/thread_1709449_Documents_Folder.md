### Post by galacticaboy on 2011-03-18
Try this, I had a similar problem, this may help. Right click on your desktop, click create new folder. Then right click on the new folder, you do not have to name it. When you right click make sure you click open with other application, then click on File-Browser, make sure at the bottom remember is clickd, then click ok, you can now delete this folder and try to open you documents folder, hope this helps...
