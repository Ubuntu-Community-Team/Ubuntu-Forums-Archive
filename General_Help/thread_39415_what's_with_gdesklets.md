It works fine on Ubuntu. Just install the "shell" from Synaptic and get your desklets from [www.gDesklets.gnomedesktop.org](www.gDesklets.gnomedesktop.org). Don't install anything old. Just the new ones. I use goodweather, news grabber, mailnotify and battery monitor.

You can also get the newer version of the "shell" from their site and install it yourself. It is supposed to be a little less of a drain on memory.
