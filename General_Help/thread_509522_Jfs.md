### Post by kidders on 2007-07-26
Hi there,

I use JFS quite a lot ... it's perfectly reliable. :-) No two filesystems are the same though, so you should take a look at a few options, and choose the one that best suits your needs. Don't forget that there's no particular reason not to use several filesystems on the same system, if that's what works for you.

Other commonly used filesystems include XFS and ReiserFS.
