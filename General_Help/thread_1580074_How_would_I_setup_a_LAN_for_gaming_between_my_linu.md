### Post by macem29 on 2010-09-22
right click on the wifi signal meter, scroll down to create new wireless network...
give it a name like "on the road" would be my choice....select none for security
as you shouldn't need to encrypt rolling down the road....select create....
left click on icon, edit connections, choose your new one....edit, select adhoc mode
and save


right click on signal meter again and connect to your new network....then
scan for it on the win machines and connect...
