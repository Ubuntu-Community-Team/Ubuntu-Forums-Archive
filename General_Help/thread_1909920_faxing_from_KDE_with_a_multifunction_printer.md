### Post by xyzzyman on 2012-01-30

### Post by xyzzyman on 2012-01-30
> **uqbar said:**
> Do you mean that I can only send faxes with a modem?
Is there any MFP I can actually use under (k)ubuntu to send and receive faxes?

Your printer can send/receive faxes as it has a modem built in, you just have to do it with the printer and not paperless via-computer.

HP does have HPLIP which you can google. It will allow you to send faxes from the computer through various HP MFP's via virtual printer, but it doesn't let you receive the faxes onto the computer. You'd still have to scan the paper fax into the PC.
