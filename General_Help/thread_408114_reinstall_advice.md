### Post by rai4shu2 on 2007-04-13
If you already have your drive partitioned the way you want, you can get by with simply reformatting and reinstalling the Windows partition with Windows, then recover grub after that's done. You don't need to touch the Ubuntu partitions.

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RecoveringUbuntuAfterInstallingWindows](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RecoveringUbuntuAfterInstallingWindows)
