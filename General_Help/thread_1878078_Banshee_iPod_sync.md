### Post by wile_e8 on 2011-11-09
I was searching for a solution to a similar problem and was about to add on to this thread, but after a bit of searching and playing around, I may have found a solution.

NOTE: I trying to sync multiple playlists from Banshee to an Android phone, not an iPhone. So this might not work, but I think it's worth a try to see if Banshee handles both the same.

I eventually found [a thread in Banshee help]("http://mail.gnome.org/archives/banshee-list/2010-October/msg00036.html") suggesting to use a smart playlist combining all the playlists I wanted to copy. I didn't like this at first, since I assumed the individual playlists wouldn't be copied as well. However, upon sync, I found that every playlist in Banshee was copied over to my phone. Including playlists that were not part of the smart playlist - they were edited to only have the songs that were synced.

So I don't know if that will work for your iPhone, but it's worth a shot.
