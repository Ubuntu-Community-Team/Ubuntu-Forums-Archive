### Post by storm99999 on 2008-05-17
I believe the problem is that since flash is made by a different company, and the bug is in the code from a different company (in this case, flash refusing to work with pulseaudio) that, although the bug is recognized, it is difficult to fix without complex workarounds.

Additionally, it may just be low ranked because it's limited in problem effect, in number of users experiencing it, or is just not high up on developer worklists as more pressing matters are to be dealt with (such as security problems and critical system problems).

I do hope it gets resolved for you soon though.
