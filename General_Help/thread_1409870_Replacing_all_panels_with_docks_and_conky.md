[LIST]
[*]Cairo dock for app launching, at the left of the screen

[*]Docky for Places, and partion mounting etc at the right
[*]Conky for system information such as Time, date, volume, Battery life, wifi signal strength, weather etc.

### Post by fabounet on 2010-02-18
Why using so many programs ?
you will waste a lot of RAM/CPU, and have an incoherent desktop.
You should stick with 1 dock, for instance I think Cairo-Dock could do all the jobs.
