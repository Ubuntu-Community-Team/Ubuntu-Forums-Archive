### Post by cybrsaylr on 2008-08-25
All I did was plug a S-Video cable into the appropriate jacks in the laptop and the TV, then turned on the TV to the correct source and everything ran as it should. For audio I plugged in a common audio cable into the laptop and TV jacks and I got audio.

Maybe you have a bad cable or jack.
