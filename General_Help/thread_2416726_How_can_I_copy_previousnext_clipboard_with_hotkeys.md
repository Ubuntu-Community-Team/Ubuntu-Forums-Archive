### Post by dragonfly41 on 2019-04-14
If you are managing citations I recommend [Zotero]("https://www.zotero.org/") as your client.

Install the stand alone Zotero GUI and also the extension to go into your browser.
In the Zotero stand alone under My Library you build your collections and sub collections.

[P.S.] If Zotero is too powerful you can try [CopyQ ]("https://hluk.github.io/CopyQ/")which keeps a record of clipboard sessions.
