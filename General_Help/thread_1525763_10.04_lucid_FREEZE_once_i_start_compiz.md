### Post by warfacegod on 2010-07-07
It seems to me that either your video card is failing, or the driver for it doesn't get along with Lucid.

I'd recommend trying a different driver. You have Intel graphics and I have no idea how to go about doing that for Intel. There drivers are treated differently than the nVidia or ATi drivers.

Your other option, if you aren't on a laptop, is to get a new video card. nVidia has the best support for Linux.

There may be other options and ways to go about fixing this that I don't know about. I just don't know that much about Intel graphics cards. Frankly that's because of issues just like this.
