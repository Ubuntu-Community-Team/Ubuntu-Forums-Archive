### Post by VMC on 2008-07-11
This has been debated for years. All kinds of "proof" has been stated.
I turn mine off at least 5 - 10 times a day. I've done that for over 20 years!

No ill effects whatsoever. No defective hard drives, or motherboards, power supplies, etc.  To the contrary of what some "experts" advise.

I've heard almost all the reasoning as to why to leave the computer on, even the one about the surge of power that the capacitors and other electronic devices take.

Why waste the power if it's not being used in some fashion. I understand the need regarding servers and the like. For normal everyday home use, if your not going to use it for several hours or overnight, turn it off.
