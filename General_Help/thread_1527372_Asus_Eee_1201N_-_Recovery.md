### Post by Lucradia on 2010-07-09
Recovery DVDs (the ones you make yourself) don't restore the recovery partition anymore. What they do now is they read information directly off that partition, just in case you can't boot to it.

This is basically because they don't want you to install other operating systems to it other than the one that shipped with it. (The manufacturer doesn't even support a Windows you install yourself, or an upgrade.)
