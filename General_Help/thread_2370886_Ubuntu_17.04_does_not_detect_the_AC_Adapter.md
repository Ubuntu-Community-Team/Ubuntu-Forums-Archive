### Post by 1skavau on 2017-09-08
Confirmed.

When you take out the power supply, that's it. You're stuck on battery until it dies naturally regardless of whether or not you plug it back in. Then it'll die and charge normally enough for it to be turned back on, and will remain charging until you happen to unplug and plug back in the power supply.
