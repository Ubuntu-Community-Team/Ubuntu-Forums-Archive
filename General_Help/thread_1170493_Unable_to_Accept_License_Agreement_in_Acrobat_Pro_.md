### Post by Taxman415a on 2009-05-26
Generally Wine problems are not related to Ubuntu, so the Wine App db is the best place to look for hints followed by googling for the application name and wine. [http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=application&iId=847](http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=application&iId=847) is the Acrobat Pro AppDB entry. It looks like your specific error is solvable, but that the application is not entirely supported.

Also try upgrading your Wine to the latest version following:
[http://www.winehq.org/download/deb](http://www.winehq.org/download/deb)

Wine is developing fairly rapidly and the latest unstable versions may contain fixes (though also bugs)
