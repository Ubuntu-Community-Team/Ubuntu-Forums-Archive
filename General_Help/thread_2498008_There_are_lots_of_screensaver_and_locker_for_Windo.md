### Post by Dennis N on 2024-05-26
> **usoviet said:**
> There are lots of screensaver and locker for Windows, what about Ubuntu?
There are a large number of screensavers for Ubuntu. They are found in this collection of packages:

xscreensaver
xscreensaver-data
Xscreensaver-data-extra
xscreensaver-gl
xscreensaver-gl-extra
rss-glx

They were created many years ago to work in an X11 (Xorg) user session. They do not work in the Wayland session.
