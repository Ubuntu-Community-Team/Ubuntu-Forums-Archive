### Post by ljrmorgan on 2010-06-10
Right click on your gnome panel (taskbar basically) and click "add to panel..."

You should see a workspace switcher applet there, add it and it ought to appear.

You can also switch with Ctrl+Alt+left/right/up/down depending on how your desktops are arranged (4 in a row, 4 in a square, whatever).
