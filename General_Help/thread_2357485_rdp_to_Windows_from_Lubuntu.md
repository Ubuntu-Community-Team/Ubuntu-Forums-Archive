### Post by gordintoronto on 2017-04-03
My favorite is krdc. If you have not installed any other "K" programs, such as k3b, the installation will include a bunch of other packages.

I use vnc to log on to the network at the office, then use krdc to bounce around from (Windows) workstation to workstation, installing new versions of applications in the evening.
