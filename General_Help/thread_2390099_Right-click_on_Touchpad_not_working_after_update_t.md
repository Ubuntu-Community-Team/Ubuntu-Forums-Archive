### Post by winicius on 2018-04-27
Download Gnome Tweaks (apt-get install gnome-tweaks). Open up the program. Click on Keyboard & Mouse tab. On the bottom it says "Mouse Click Emulation" select Area. Reboot your machine for the change to take effect.

You should now be able to right click.
