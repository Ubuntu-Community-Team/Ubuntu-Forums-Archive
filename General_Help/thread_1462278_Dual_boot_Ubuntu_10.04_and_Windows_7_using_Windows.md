### Post by Mark Phelps on 2010-04-25
First of all, I don't believe that you can intall 10.04 without installing GRUB2.  If that's the case, you will end up with GRUB -- like it or not.

Second, if you later want to remove GRUB from your MBR and use the Win7 boot loader, suggest you go to the NeoSmart Technology website and check our their EasyBCD forums and tutorials.  You will need to download and install EasyBCD to your Win7 partition, and you will need to read their tutorials about how to use that to manage the boot loader to add Ubuntu under GRUB2.
