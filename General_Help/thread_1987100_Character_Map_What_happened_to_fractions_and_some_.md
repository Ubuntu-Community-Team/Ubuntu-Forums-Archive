### Post by Colin Keenan on 2012-05-25
I've been adding more and more mathematical fonts, but still, I can't even find simple fractions like 1/2, 1/3, 1/4, that used to be easy to find in Character Map.

What happened to those characters?

---

### Post by diesch on 2012-05-25
They are still at U+00BC (¼), U+00BD (½), U+00BE (¾). The font "Ubuntu" has them for example.

---

### Post by Colin Keenan on 2012-05-25
> **diesch said:**
> They are still at U+00BC (¼), U+00BD (½), U+00BE (¾). The font "Ubuntu" has them for example.

But, I still couldn't find those characters in the Character Map, even with Ubuntu chosen. 

Eventually I decided to go up to the menu to see if there was some preferences or something and noticed the View drop-down was set to "By Script". I changed it to "By Unicode Block" and all of a sudden I could find everything just fine.
