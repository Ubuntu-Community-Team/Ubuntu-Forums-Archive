### Post by lswest on 2008-05-01
to add custom open/close effects you need to install compizconfig-settings-manager (i read your last thread, i'm not sure if you had installed it or not), and compiz-fusion-plugins-extra.  you can do this with the following command:
```
sudo apt-get install compizconfig-settings-manager compiz-fusion-plugins-extra
``` in the terminal.  After that (since i'm not sure what the titles are in serbian) go to the corresponding place on your Ubuntu installation:
[IMG]http://img209.imageshack.us/img209/2303/screenshotsp9.png[/IMG]
and then there will be a window like this:
[IMG]http://img360.imageshack.us/img360/4206/screenshotcompizconfigsum7.png[/IMG]

where you have to go to "animations" (it has a picture of a genie lamp next to it)** and from there you will have tabs with things such as "open animations" etc. where you can change them to your preference.

** the option is the one circled on the below pic:
[IMG]http://img396.imageshack.us/img396/7809/screenshotcompizconfigsll4.png[/IMG]

### Post by itmanvn on 2008-05-01
> **macaholic said:**
> If I understand you correctly, you are mentioning the fire when he right clicks? If that is indeed what you are referring to, then you would set that by the following:
1. Enter CCSM
2. Click on Animations
3. Select Close Animation
4. Click New, select burn as the effect
5. CLick the '+' select 'type' under window type, and enter 'PopupMenu' udner window match, and select a desired duration. (I like 150)
Repeat this for 'Open Animation', and whenever you right click it will burn open and burn close.
