### Post by Tenken on 2007-12-13

For one if you open say 12 windows or anything more than like 8 they start to get pretty small and with a single panel it would just add windows across to the other display and give you more room to work with.[/QUOTE]

Try right clicking your second panel, clicking add to panel and selecting window list. That way any a window open on your second monitor will appear in the window list, and all the ones on the first monitor will appear in the window list on your first panel.
