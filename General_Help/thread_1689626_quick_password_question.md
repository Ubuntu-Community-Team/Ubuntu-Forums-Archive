### Post by Grenage on 2011-02-17
If you have nothing truly confidential on your PC, and there are no remote services (Remote Desktop runs by default), then it's not the end of the world.

If you have trouble remembering complicated passwords, you could always use a phrase.  While still dictionary words, the number of them will drastically improve its complexity - while still being easy to remember.
