### Post by ajgreeny on 2007-09-29
It's got to be Open Office Writer.  There are others available but none of them, as far as I'm concerned, are anywhere near as good as OO.o.  It has the best of the MS Office import filters of any, and like it or not, you have just got to be able to import word docs, etc, as they are the world standard for most people.  I have tried Abiword, which is fast and reasonable for simple documents, but sometimes makes a complete mess of the formatting of Word docs, so I seldom use it.  Koffice also has a long way to go to reach the same state as Open Office.

Open Office is the one!
