### Post by Tyke91 on 2007-12-23
3 and 4 require sound editing software, try the program "audacity". you can get it by going into add/remove and searching for "audacity"

then just select the music you want to increase the volume of, and use the amplify effect. don't forget to export your song as an MP3 or OGG at the end of the session.

### Post by rune0077 on 2007-12-23
1. System > Preferences > Removable Drives and Media. Choose Multimedia and then uncheck the box under iPod that says "Play music files when connected" (or some such).

2. Instead of disconnecting it from Amarok, right click it's icon on the desktop and select unmount, that should do the trick.

Can't help you with the rest, sorry.
