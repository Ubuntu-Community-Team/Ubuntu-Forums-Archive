### Post by Murwiz on 2008-06-10
Recently I've experienced regular lockups using Amarok 1.4 and Ubuntu 7.10. The symptoms are that the system works fine after rebooting, and Amarok will work as well, but if it's been up for more than a few hours, Amarok will start trying to "clean house" (I think, at least based on intermittent messages and dialogs that sometimes appear during these times). After less than 30 seconds, the system is unresponsive, the HD is spinning and chattering at full tilt, and only a hard power-off reset will regain control.

I use Amarok mostly for podcast subscriptions, and I have a dozen or two of those, if that helps.
