### Post by Bucky Ball on 2017-04-02
Hard to help when you can not be specific about what you installed. You installed 'something' but haven't got a clue what is not really enough to go on. ;)

If you install things with little knowledge of what they are or what they do and no record of what you installed, yes, problems are going to happen.

Suggest you re-open Synaptic, search for 'openvpn' and then click the 'installed' column a couple of times to see what you have installed that relates to openvpn. That might jog your memory.

Good luck.
