### Post by scouser73 on 2009-08-19
If your PC has an nVidia card, then setting up dual monitors is extremely easy.  Go to **Terminal**, and copy & paste this command:  **gksudo nvidia-settings**.  The **nVidia X Server Settings** console will appear, go to **X Server Display Configuration** and adjust the dual monitor set up to your liking, once you are satisfied, click **Apply** then click **Save To X Configuration File**.
