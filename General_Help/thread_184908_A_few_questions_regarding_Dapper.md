### Post by Skye on 2006-05-30
Once you get the default gnome login, you can log on, correct?

I ask because I don't think your nvidia drivers are broken at all.  The fact that you can see the Nvidia splash screen means that the drivers work, and that your computer is  erroring somewhere else.  The 800x600 resolution may just be an effect of the default gnome login, rather than your drivers.

I think your gdm may be broken.  Here's what you can try to do to fix this:

(run these from a command line)
```
sudo apt-get clean
sudo apt-get install --reinstall gdm

```
This cleans the cache of downloaded programs from apt, and then reinstalls GDM.  Since the cache was cleaned, the program is forced to get a new copy from the ubuntu servers, eliminating the possibility of a corrupted package.

Post back here and tell us how it goes.
