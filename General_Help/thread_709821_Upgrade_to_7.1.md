### Post by Xyhthyx on 2008-02-27
As long as you upgrade (changing to the 7.10 repos and upgrading via apt/update manager) and don't install over it (using install script on 7.10 live cd), all your personal files should be intact. I would recommend backing up important personal data as a precautionary measure.

### Post by 3rdalbum on 2008-02-27
If you upgrade "in-place" (that is, upgrade your system from the Update Manager while it is running), you will retain your personal files. But it's a good idea to make a backup of your data whenever you make a major system change. Just remember to enable "Show Hidden Files" when you drag your home directory to a DVD or portable hard disk!
