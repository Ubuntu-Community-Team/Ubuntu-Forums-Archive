### Post by ajgreeny on 2022-09-15
There should be the option after filling in your username, if needed, then typing your password, but before hitting Enter; it does not appear until that point.

Look for the usual cog-wheel icon. I use Xubuntu with lightdm not Ubuntu with gdm3 or whatever display manager is used so can't give more detail but it is definitely there in the login dialogue.
