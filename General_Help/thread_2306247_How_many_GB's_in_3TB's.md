### Post by sammiev on 2015-12-13
GB (gigabyte) and TB (terabyte) are units of measurement for storage size. Another common measure is MB (megabyte). 1024MB = 1GB and 1024GB = 1TB.

### Post by oldfred on 2015-12-14
Is tool working in TiB or TB. 
       1 Gigabyte (GB) = 1,000,000,000 bytes.
1 Gibibyte (GiB) = 1024x1024x1024 = 1,073,741,824 bytes

Different tools report in GB and others in GiB, so not always consistent.
