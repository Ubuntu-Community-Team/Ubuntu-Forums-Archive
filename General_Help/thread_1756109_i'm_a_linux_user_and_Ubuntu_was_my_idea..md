### Post by collisionystm on 2011-05-11
I'm not sure how many will read this. Hopefully alot. I know we have all been reading about Natty, Unity, Jumping Ship, Etc... not really any reason to go into it, so I wont. 

I use 11.04 myself, and today curiosity grabbed control of me and I tried a gnome 3 live cd from their website. Now maybe its just me...but it flat out sucks. Everyone says canonical took away our freedom with Unity? Wrong. They saved us from gnome. If you dislike unity, you will surely dislike gnome 3. Gnome 3 doesn't even let you minimize windows. I suggest you fire up virtualbox and give it a go. You will see unity is far better than gnome.

Let's face the facts, the gnome we love, gnome 2, is dead. Gnome 3 is no good, and unity is viewed by many as a horrible alternative. Its time to move on. Ubuntu is all about our freedom of choice and we have the power to make changes. we can make our voices heard to developers much easier if we keep requests all in one place. 

I propose the following...

We all post something that we wish Ubuntu had, or should keep, or improve on. After a few days, I can share the link with Claire Newman at canonical who is kind enough to respond to emails. I am sure she will be more than happy to pass it on to developers.
If they built unity, they can build another one that is community approved.

Ill start off with a few ideas

Gnome 2 style menus. The search function is horrible. Not everyone remembers the name of every program they want to run.

Removable panels with gnome 2 style function

If we are going to have a unity bar, let's share features that docky has. I would love to right click on the unity bar and be able to see a menu of my open Firefox windows.

I will end this by saying

I am a Linux user and Ubuntu is OUR idea. If we work together.
