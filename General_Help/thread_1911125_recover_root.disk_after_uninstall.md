### Post by bcbc on 2012-01-18
There might be a way to undelete it, but only if parts of it haven't been overwritten. But if you've been using your computer since or reinstalled Wubi it's very likely that it's overwritten parts of the file.

So, I'd say the chances are not good that you'll be able to recover it. Sorry to be the bearer of bad news.
