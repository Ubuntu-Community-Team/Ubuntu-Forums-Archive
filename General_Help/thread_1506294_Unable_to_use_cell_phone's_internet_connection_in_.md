### Post by Nick_Jinn on 2010-06-10
Windows has a program to automatically recognize the phone. With linux you may have to enable it like you enable a non phone device. You will have to make your phone and computer both visable and go through a few more steps.

There are also syncing tools specific to your phone, so you may want to check out the package manager (Synaptic) and download some of the syncing tools specific to your brand of phones. 


We seem to have a lot of support for the Palm OS phones and some compatibility with Android.
