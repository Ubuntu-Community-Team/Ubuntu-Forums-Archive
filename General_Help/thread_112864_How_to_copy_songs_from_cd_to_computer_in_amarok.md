### Post by soops1966 on 2006-01-05
Use Konqueror.

Go to your CD and open it you will be offered three versions of the CD - one will be .ogg terminated files, anothe .mp3 and the the third I can't remember.

Drag and drop copy/paste the ones you want in the format you want and you're done.

Dead easy.

If ripping is slow you may need to enable DMA on your CDROM - use hdparm to do this, I'm sure there's a HOWTO somewhere on these forums for it.

soops1966
