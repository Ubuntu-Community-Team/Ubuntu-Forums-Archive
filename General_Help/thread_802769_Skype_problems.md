### Post by Robin T Cox on 2008-05-22
Check your sound settings in the Skype Options>Sound Devices dialog. Also make sure that, if you are using headphones that your system is switched to those (e.g. use KMix to check, via a drop-down in the KMix mixer settings window).

If you've done all that, but still have had no luck, why not try asking at the Skype forums?
