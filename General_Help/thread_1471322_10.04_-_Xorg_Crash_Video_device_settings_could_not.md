### Post by Irihapeti on 2010-05-08
This sounds like one of the typical xserver-xorg-video-intel bugs. Others involve having to use the vesa driver (and possibly being stuck with 640x480 resolution).

You may find some specific workarounds for your chip in the bug reports on Launchpad.

I'm affected by something similar on my EeePC 900. I have added 
```
i915.modeset=0
``` to grub. Others have had success with ```
i915.modeset=1
```

### Post by djscribe on 2010-08-20
This may be unrelated, but something to think about is whether or not it becomes more frequent as you reboot or if it takes the same amount of time. If more frequent, then you might want to benchmark **temperatures**. Crashing or erratic behavior can occur if the CPU or Video Card/Chipsets are running too hot for example. It may very well be the bug, but if this was something that worked fine before and then started crashing, maybe looking at those temps will help you reach a more solid conclusion.

Good luck.
