### Post by bluevoodoo1 on 2006-03-25
You can also try right-clicking on the menu area and choose "edit menus." Then click  once on "accessories" on the left side. On the right side a listing will come up of all the items in the accessories folder. Simply choose "new entry" and you'll be on your way.
