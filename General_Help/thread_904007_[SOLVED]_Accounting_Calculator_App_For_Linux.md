### Post by zhangweiwu on 2011-01-30
Just for your information, if you don't like the gnome calculator, there are many emulator of the famous HP-12 financial calculator. To name a few:

This one is made of html/javascript:

[http://epx.com.br/ctb/hp12c.php](http://epx.com.br/ctb/hp12c.php)

The Poor man's Financial Calculator, written in Java, can be downloaded from Softpedia:

[http://linux.softpedia.com/get/Office/Finance/Poor-man-s-Financial-Calculator-7945.shtml](http://linux.softpedia.com/get/Office/Finance/Poor-man-s-Financial-Calculator-7945.shtml)

There is a freshmeat project that might be the source of the softpedia software:
[http://pt.sourceforge.jp/projects/freshmeat_poorcalculator/](http://pt.sourceforge.jp/projects/freshmeat_poorcalculator/)
