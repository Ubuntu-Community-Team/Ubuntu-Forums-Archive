### Post by howefield on 2009-12-15
I protect against hard disk failure with a clonezilla disk image, and user error with files copied to multiple disks. When saving a file, a copy goes to a seperate internal disk and the NAS, more "important" stuff also to an external disk and possibly "the cloud". 

In the past have also used rsync and sbackup, which are both pretty good.
