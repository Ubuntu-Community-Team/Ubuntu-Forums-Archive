### Post by SunnyRabbiera on 2008-08-12
here is what you need to do, when you load the theme manager hit the "install" button to go though your files to where the theme downloaded to, so say its in the desktop directory click "desktop" on the side pane of the window that will pop up when you hit install.
Once you find your theme click it and then hit "open"
simple.
Most themes work like that, however not all themes work by default as some need extra steps to get working, just tell us of any errors you get and we can do our best to help you.
