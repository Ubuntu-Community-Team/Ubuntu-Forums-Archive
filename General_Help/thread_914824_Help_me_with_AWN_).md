### Post by ligaguine on 2008-09-09
> **Danon132 said:**
> Also, if i reboot my computer i have to open AWN again, can i do anything so its always there? (so i dont have to open it each time) :b

To add the AWN dock to your start-up items:

- System > Preferences > Sessions
- Click Add button on Startup Programs tab
- In Edit Startup Program dialog: Name=avant, Command=avant-window-navigator

I recently found this info from the following article:

[http://blogs.howtogeek.com/tuxgeek/2008/09/08/pimp-your-gnome-in-7-steps/](http://blogs.howtogeek.com/tuxgeek/2008/09/08/pimp-your-gnome-in-7-steps/)

Hope that helps.
