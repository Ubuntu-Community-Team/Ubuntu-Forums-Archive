### Post by cryptotheslow on 2011-10-13
Re-install the extension - Software Centre or Synaptic, choose your poison on that one. Then invoke Nautilus from a Terminal.

i.e. 
Open a Terminal - Ctrl-Alt-T
Start Nautilus with command:

```
nautilus
```

Maybe you will see some relevant errors in the Terminal window when Nautilus bails out that you can then use to investigate further.
