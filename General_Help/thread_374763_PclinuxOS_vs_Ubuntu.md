### Post by darkmaster on 2007-03-03
I suggest you having a look [here]("http://distrowatch.com/") for informations about distros.
Ubuntu uses Gnome, Kubuntu KDE, Xubuntu Xfce. As much as I know, PC Linus OS uses KDE, not Gnome. I suggest you using vmware player to try them out without even installing them for real. You can find a lot of pre installed distros images [here]("http://www.thoughtpolice.co.uk/vmware/") too. Try google to find out some others pre installed virtual machines. Just download them and run with vmware player, from windows or Linux. You cannot say wich one is the best. If you whant a distro straight away ready for multimedia and 3D, I suggest you giving a shot to simply mephis too. Bye!
