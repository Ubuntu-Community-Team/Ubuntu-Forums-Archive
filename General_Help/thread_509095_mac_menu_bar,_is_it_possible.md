### Post by DoktorSeven on 2007-07-25
In KDE it's Control Center (KDE menu->Settings->Control Center)->expand Desktop->Behavior, General tab, select "Current application's menu bar (Mac OS-style)" under "Menu Bar at Top of Screen".  Won't work with non-KDE applications though.
