### Post by ankspo71 on 2010-05-17
> Another issue is that I can't find how to disable the KDE alerts coming from Firefox, it's just too much to get the KDE popups every time a file is d/l. 

Hi, I think you can do this by right clicking on the system tray, then click on system tray settings, then select your options there. You can choose to turn off "application notifications" and "file transfers and other jobs". For this to work, you might need to unlock the widgets first.
Hope that helps.
