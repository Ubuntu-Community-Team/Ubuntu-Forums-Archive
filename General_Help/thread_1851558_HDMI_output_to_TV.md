### Post by heyandy889 on 2011-09-28
Hey papaapa. I've done this with a VGA output, so I don't imagine an HDMI output would work any differently. I didn't need an extra graphics card for the VGA-out extended desktop.

After plugging in the TV, you're gonna hunt down "Monitors" settings. This is where you change your resolution and you can also change the display mode, like extended desktop or twin.

That's where I would start. :)
