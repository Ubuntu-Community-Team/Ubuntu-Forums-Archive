edit:
also, to answer your earlier question. Most media players don't contain their own codecs, but instead use codecs that are installed separately. If you are looking for a good media player I suggest SMPlayer. Here is a link:
[http://smplayer.sourceforge.net/downloads.php?tr_lang=en]("http://smplayer.sourceforge.net/downloads.php?tr_lang=en")
There are 2 PPAs linked to on that page, one has a new version of MPlayer (which is debatably the best media player for linux). the other has SMPlayer which is an awsome front end for MPlayer. I suggest you add them to you software sources and try them out.
