I have 10.04 installed on my machine. When I boot the computer, I get the initramfs error which is shown in the picture below.

[http://computergyan.files.wordpress.com/2009/12/dsc02210.jpg](http://computergyan.files.wordpress.com/2009/12/dsc02210.jpg)

I tried to boot from Live CD to deal with this problem. Live CD just boots up fine, I can even see the main menu of Live CD.  When I choose "Try Ubuntu without installing", the screen goes blank and  I get this error;

"Buffer I/O error on device fd0, logical block 0"

Can anyone help me with this problem?

UPDATE: Solved. For those having the same problem: Just press "Shift" when the computer boots up, it will take you to the Grub menu. And choose one of the older Linux images in that menu. Once Ubuntu boots, open the Synaptic Package manager and just re-install the broken Linux image. Then restart your computer and you are all set.
