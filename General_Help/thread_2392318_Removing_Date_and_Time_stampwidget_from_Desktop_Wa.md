### Post by PaulW2U on 2018-05-19
Go to Budgie Desktop Settings | Top Panel | Desktop time & date.

From there you can toggle both the time and date on or off. 

Not easy to find. :)

---

### Post by Frogs Hair on 2018-05-19
Do as above, hi-light the desktop time & date applet in the list, and select the recycle icon to remove it.
