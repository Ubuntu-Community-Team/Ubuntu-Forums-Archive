### Post by Mr. Swiveller on 2006-02-05
Also, you might be able to find more information on getting your particular hardware to run by querying Google. Try combinations like 'ac97 alsa' and 'ac97 oss'. (ALSA and OSS are Linux sounddriver implementations.) The links I found when I tried this myself suggested, though, that 'ac97' is an audio architecture rather than a specific soundchip. You might be able to find more specific info by adding details like the soundcard's manufacturer to your query.
