### Post by cariboo on 2010-12-27
The devs have admitted that the Maverick version of Unity has quite a few problems, that is why it is being completely re-written for Natty. There is a work around to get to Nautilus, Click on the Files and Folders icon in the panel, then click on one of the folders in the bottom row, you should see a folder icon in the upper right, click that and Nautilus will open, from there all the regular features are available.

Personally I would suggest going back to the Lucid netbook version, and waiting until Natty is released in April.
