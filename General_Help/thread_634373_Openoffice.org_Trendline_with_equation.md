### Post by quinnman on 2007-12-07
Hello. Trendline cannot be displayed in OO.o, but Gnumeric is capable of this. Trendline in OO.o will be featured in future 2.4 version [http://wiki.services.openoffice.org/wiki/Features#Planned_Features_for_2.4_Release]("http://wiki.services.openoffice.org/wiki/Features#Planned_Features_for_2.4_Release")

### Post by quinnman on 2007-12-07
In Gnumeric: (1) select your data and plot a graph, (2) in chart components select the series for which you wish to plot the trendline and add it by clicking on the add button, (3) once the trendline is ploted, select it and uncheck affine (this will force the trendline to begin in zero) and (4) finally select the trendline and add its equation.
