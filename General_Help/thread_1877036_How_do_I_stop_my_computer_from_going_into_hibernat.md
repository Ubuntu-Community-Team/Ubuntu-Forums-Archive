### Post by fantab on 2011-11-07
> **glennbates said:**
> I have just recently installed Ubuntu on my laptop.  Very new here.  How do I get my computer to not go into hibernation when I am not doing anything on it or doing it automatically when I close the lid.

From Dash go to **System Settings- Screen- Turn off after** (choose NEVER from the dropdown list if you don't want your screen to dim. Also switch off the LOCK).

**System Settings - Power - Suspend when Inacitve for** (choose whatever you desire from the Dropdown list)

I don't have ubuntu on Laptop so I cant really tell you what you should do to hibernate when lid is closed.
