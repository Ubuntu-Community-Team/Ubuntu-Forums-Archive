### Post by robbie 348 on 2014-08-13
This worked for me in LXDE. Right click on desktop, go to create new shortcut, give it a name like shutdown, click on browse files and it should open executables, scroll down to Lubuntu logout or lxde logout and click on it, click OK and it should be there.
