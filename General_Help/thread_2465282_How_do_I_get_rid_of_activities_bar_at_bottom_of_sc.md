### Post by dora5 on 2021-07-28
I figured this out.   In Extensions in Tweaks I had enabled "Window list" because somehow I thought a window list at the bottom of the screen might be a good idea.  It's gone now.  

Honestly, never saw anything that ugly in my life - never thought it was something I did!
