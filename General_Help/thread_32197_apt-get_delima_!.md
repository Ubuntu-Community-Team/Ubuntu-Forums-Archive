### Post by az on 2005-05-06
From what repositories did you get the newer apps?  

Probably, the next thing I will be saying to you is: 

"you really shouldn't do that..."



Basically, if you used debian repositories, you are going to need to remove the packages you got there.  Since they are compiled with a version of libc6 greater than in Ubuntu, you either need to get that version of libc6, or remove the offending packages.

You can always go and get the source packages for the gimp and compile it in Hoary...

You may also use the gimp from Warty, too, if that worked for you...
