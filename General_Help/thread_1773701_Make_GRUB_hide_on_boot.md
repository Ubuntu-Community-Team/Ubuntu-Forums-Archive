### Post by Toz on 2011-06-02
Not a good idea to directly edit grub.cfg - it will get overwritten with every grub update. Best to make updates to /etc/default/grub file and files in /etc/grub.d. 

By default, grub will not hide the screen on multi-boot systems. However, intuitive and inquisitive minds have found a way. Have a look at: [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1287602](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1287602) - specifically #11- Hiding the menu on multi-os systems.
