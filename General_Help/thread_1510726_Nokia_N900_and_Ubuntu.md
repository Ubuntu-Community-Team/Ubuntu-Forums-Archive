### Post by fragos on 2010-06-17
When you N900 is detected on USB you're given the option to open with a default of Rhythmbox. Select "file System" as your opening method and you'll get your N900's external drive and the internal drive both mounted and open in Nautilus. My N810 has an internal drive but I'm not sure about the N900. You can then navigate to the folder you want and and drag & drop from or to Ubuntu. Any device which has separate storage is mountable as a storage device, e.g. PDA's, media players and cameras. On my N810 and I assume the N900 your default file storage is in main memory which isn't available over the USB because it's in use to run you N900.
