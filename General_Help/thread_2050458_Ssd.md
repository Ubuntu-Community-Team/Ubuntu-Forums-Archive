### Post by drmrgd on 2012-08-31
As others have said, 12.04 has not compatibility problem with SSDs, and so it shouldn't be any concern.  It seems Samsung, Crucial, and OCZ are the leaders of the pack, with Samsung being the highest touted in terms of performance and reliability.  Just looking at Newegg.com and sorting by price, looks like you have a few options that will fit within your budget:

[http://www.newegg.com/Product/ProductList.aspx?Submit=ENE&N=100008120%204027&IsNodeId=1&name=%2475%20-%20%24100](http://www.newegg.com/Product/ProductList.aspx?Submit=ENE&N=100008120%204027&IsNodeId=1&name=%2475%20-%20%24100)

Don't go too small, or you'll fill it up quite quickly, and you should leave ~30% (or so I've heard) free in order to maximize the read / write efficiency of the drive.  I'd personally go with that 128 GB Samsung drive since it runs a little faster and has plenty of room for your OS and programs.
