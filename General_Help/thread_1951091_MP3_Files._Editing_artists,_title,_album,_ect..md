### Post by papibe on 2012-04-01
Hi BandP.

Been there! I hear you!

Most multimedia files have internal 'fields' to store metadata (like artist, album, etc). MP3 files have [ID3 tags]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Id3_tag").

In order to edit the data you need an 'Id3 Tag Editor'. There are several on the Software Center including EasyTAG, and MP3 Diags.

My favorite is Kid3-qt.

I hope that helps. Tell us how it goes.
Regards.
