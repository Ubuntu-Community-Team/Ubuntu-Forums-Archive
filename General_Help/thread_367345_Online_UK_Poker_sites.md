### Post by wxnker on 2007-02-26
I play at Ladbrokes with Wine and it works great. The only bug I have is a couple of banners/graphics missing in the top of the lobby, everything runs almost like in Windows.

Have a look at this guide:
[http://mormanski.net/](http://mormanski.net/) 

I used Firefox 1.07 though and not Mozilla 1.72 and the activeX plugin for Firefox 1.07 instead, as I could not make the Ladbrokes flash plugin work with Mozilla 1.72.

You may or may not need some fonts. 

If you'd like you can also use Winetools to install fonts, Firefox 1.07 and activeX for FF 1.07.
