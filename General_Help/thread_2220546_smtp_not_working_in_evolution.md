### Post by woodart on 2014-04-28
Well I resolved my issue:

under smtp preferences I changed to no encryption and then hit check for authentication types - I chose plain and said OK. Then i resent my message in outbox and it asked for a password and sent the message.

Maybe it is just new to me but it seems more complex than it has to be.

I hope this answer helps someone in the future.
