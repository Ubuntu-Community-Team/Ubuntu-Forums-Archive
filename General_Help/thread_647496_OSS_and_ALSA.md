### Post by dlegend on 2007-12-24
Just yesterday I was having a problem with my sound -- music/movies weren't playing any sound and was freezing up my media players so I went to the sound preferences and set the music/movies sound setting to OSS when I had ALSA for other options. This got my sound to work again -- I had a couple options that were set to OSS and a couple that were ALSA.

But somehow for my issue I was able to get all my sound-related stuff to work again and set all the options to Autodetect.

So yes it is possible to have both working (unless I am missing something about how the sound preferences work).
