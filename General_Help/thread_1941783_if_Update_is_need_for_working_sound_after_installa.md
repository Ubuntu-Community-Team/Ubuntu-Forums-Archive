### Post by gordintoronto on 2012-03-16
Sound should work right away. If you can see a speaker icon near the top-right of your screen, click it and choose Sound Preferences. Make sure the correct sound device is chosen under Hardware, and that Output looks reasonable.

If your computer is very new, you might be better off with a more recent version of Ubuntu. 10.10 is only supported until the end of April.
