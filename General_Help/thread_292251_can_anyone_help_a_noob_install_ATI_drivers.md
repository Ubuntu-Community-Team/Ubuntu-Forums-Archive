### Post by igknighted on 2006-11-03
you have two options... either you can download ATI's proprietary drivers (there are several how to's, look at the stickies on top of the multimedia forum) or you can manually edit your xorg.conf to use the open-source radeon drivers (if you want to go this route, just ask for the proper configuration here and we can help out, however for someone new to linux the first option is probably easiest).
