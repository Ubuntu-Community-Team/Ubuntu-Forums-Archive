### Post by jnorthr on 2007-08-19
does your machine have a dvd or cd reader ? If so you can burn a 'live cd' copy of ubuntu without actually installing it. then your buyer can make that decision for them selves. Go here: [http://www.ubuntu.com/products/WhatIsUbuntu/desktopedition](http://www.ubuntu.com/products/WhatIsUbuntu/desktopedition) and see the download button at the bottom, assuming your machine is an intel x86 variety. It will need to have 128MB of memory, but you may find that after all this work you will begin to love ubuntu as much as we do. :popcorn:
