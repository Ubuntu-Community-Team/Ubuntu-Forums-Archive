### Post by Townley89 on 2011-11-15
I had that problem a while ago. It turned out to be a video card issue (most likely with yours as well if you're getting into tty1.) 

What kind of a laptop do you have? I know 10.10 was a bit wonky with my laptop's card because I had a laptop with both integrated and dedicated video cards that weren't playing nice with eachother on Ubuntu. My short-term solution was to switch to Linux Mint, which worked just fine, but since then the laptop has worked better with Ubuntu 11.04 and Ubuntu 11.10. 

If you don't want to change distros or versions, are you able to get into failsafe graphics mode? If you are, that might signify that it is a video card issue (any chance you were tinkering with something / had a new install the day before it went down?)
