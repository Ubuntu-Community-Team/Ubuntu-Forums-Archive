I'm not sure whether downgrading is possible. I just had a quick scan through some google results and it seems that most people suggest that you just do a fresh install. There is a guide: [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/DowngradeHowto](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/DowngradeHowto)

Though the writer says that they are yet to test it.

I would say that you're probably best backing up your personal files, taking note of programs you installed post install then doing a fresh install. Probably less hassle! 

Take care not to upgrade to Hardy until it becomes stable, which I think is at the end of April, though if you ask me, I'd wait a little while longer because if upgrading from Feisty to Gutsy was anything to go by, I had a few problems that took a month or so to be resolved. 

Hope that helps
