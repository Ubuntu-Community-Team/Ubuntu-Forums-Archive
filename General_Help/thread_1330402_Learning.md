### Post by QIII on 2009-11-18
And, as always:

Whenever you are about to launch into something new, do BACKUPS of your data (or just don't save anything important during your "familiarization" phase).

If you break something (and we ALL did, we were ALL newbies at one time) the worst you will have to do is reinstall.  If you created a separate /home partition from the start, even that is pretty easy.
