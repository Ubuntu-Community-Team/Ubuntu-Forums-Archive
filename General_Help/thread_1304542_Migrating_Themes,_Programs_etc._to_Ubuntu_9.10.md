### Post by fluffman86 on 2009-10-29
All of your themes and program settings are stored in your home folders, as hidden files that begin with a dot.  So just open your home folder, press ctrl + H to see the .hidden files, and back those up.

Then when you reinstall, you can just pull those back over, and re-download the applications that you need from synaptic.
