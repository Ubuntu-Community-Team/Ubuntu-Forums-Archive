You should read up on the basics of bittorrent and setting up your client (Transmission). Try wiki for that.

As far as torrents go, try Piratebay or Demonoid. Be careful that what you are downloading is legal.

Here is a decent first movie, if you want a film -- [Steal This Film ]("http://thepiratebay.org/torrent/3515729/STEAL_THIS_FILM_%28XviD%29") was copyrighted in order for you to be able to steal it.

If you've got something in mind, a search for "something torrent" will probably suffice. Otherwise, Star Wreck is CC-licensed, so will be fine for your experiments.

Click on the .torrent file. Firefox (or whichever browser you're using) will ask you what you want to do with it. Select Open With Transmission, and you'll be good to go.
