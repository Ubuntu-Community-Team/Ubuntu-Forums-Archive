### Post by zenwalker on 2008-07-31
Well even i faced the same slowness of copying data from USB stick via USB 2.0 ports on my system with Zenwalk. Well one thing i figured out was that FS on USB stick will play a bit role in maintaining the speed. If the FS of usb stick is of Linux based viz Reiserfs, XFS, the speed increases a bit.
