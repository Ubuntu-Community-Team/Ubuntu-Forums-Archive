### Post by Sparkalo on 2006-01-28
I don't know of a work around for the dvd eject button if there is a mounted disk inside the drive, but, here's another solution.

To make a keyboard shortcut to eject, go to System -> Preferences -> Keyboard Shortcuts

Scroll down to the category "sound", open it, and the last option should be "Eject".  Click under the shortcut column and press the buttons on your keyboard that you want to eject your cd.  Good luck!
