### Post by angkor on 2006-04-10
I take it you're after miniwin?

[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/compiz#head-4df0efde6c6470f3da45f1dcaf02094c411a5f67](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/compiz#head-4df0efde6c6470f3da45f1dcaf02094c411a5f67)

In Gconf -> Apps -> Compiz -> General -> Allscreens -> options and edit the active_plugins to add miniwin.
