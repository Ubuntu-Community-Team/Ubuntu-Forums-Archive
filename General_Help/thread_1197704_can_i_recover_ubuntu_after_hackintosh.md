### Post by Tipped OuT on 2009-06-26
Yes you can. If you mean "recover" as in re-install, then yes, or if you mean add it back to your boot options, then yes too. Just pop in the Ubuntu CD and boot into the Live CD at start up.

If you would like to re-install, well then that's self explanitory.

If you would like to add it back to your boot options, then you're going to have to re-install GRUB to get it as your default boot manager, I believe
