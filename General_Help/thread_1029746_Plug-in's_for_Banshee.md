### Post by L8erG8er on 2009-01-04
When playing Banshee, an icon is put into the notification tray.  If you right click on the icon, there are a few play controls that you can use.  It's almost like having a small player.  I like it because I can get to it no matter what I have on the desktop at the time.  Also, if you hover over the icon with the mouse pointer, it will show what Banshee is playing.  Again, I find that very handy, and I don't have to install anything else.
