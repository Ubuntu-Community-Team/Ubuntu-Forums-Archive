What do I need (to do) to fully control _all_ startup/boot applications that runs during or after system startup/login?

---

### Post by undecim on 2009-11-04
For services, take a look at "bum" which is a graphical runlevel editor.

To control apps that run after you log in, look at System -> Preferences -> Startup Applications
