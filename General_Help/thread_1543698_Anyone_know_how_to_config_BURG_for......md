### Post by stinkeye on 2010-08-01
Press F at the burg screen to hide recovery options and it should be saved as default.
F7 will then drop down recovery options.
If your triple booting you can't hide the other ubuntu install.
Press the H key for key bindings.

In  BURG-manager you can test settings by clicking the burg-emu button.
