### Post by plucky on 2011-02-27
> **sainath90 said:**
> when ever im trying to minimize an application,the application is not minimizing to the panel at the bottom and its getting closed...how can i get rid of this?

Right click on Bottom Panel and select **Add to Panel**

In the window that opens, select **Window List** and select **Add**

Your applications should now be visible in the bottom panel.

Good Luck
