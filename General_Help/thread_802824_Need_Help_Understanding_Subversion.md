### Post by diaa on 2008-05-21
I can explain but I don't think I can do better than the [official subversion book]("http://svnbook.red-bean.com/").
I suggest that you read chapters 1 and 2 from the book to have good understanding of subversion and VCS concepts in general.
Finally you should know that Tags, Branches and Tags are just best practices for using VCS, but they don't necessarily have to exist and you can do everything without them.

If you don't like books for some reason or you want a gentler introduction, you can check some of the [presentations available at subversion.tigris.org]("http://subversion.tigris.org/servlets/ProjectDocumentList?folderID=9246&expandFolder=9246&folderID=0"), also use google, you should find many.
