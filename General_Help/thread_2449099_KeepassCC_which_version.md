### Post by tea for one on 2020-08-20
KeepassXC 2.4.3 is a regular Ubuntu package which can be installed with **apt**

KeepassXC 2.6.0 is a snap package

Here is the info about snap packages:-

[https://snapcraft.io/](https://snapcraft.io/)

There are many positive and negative opinions about snap packages and I suggest you conduct a little research to understand the various points of view.
