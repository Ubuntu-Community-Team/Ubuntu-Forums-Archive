### Post by amp_man on 2008-11-17
It's because of the updated interface. Evolution cannot access hotmail directly because MS doesn't offer POP3 access (nor does yahoo, gmail does). So, you have a plugin that accesses the webpage, "reads" the email, and parses and formats it for evolution. When MS updated the interface, they moved everything around, so the plugin doesn't know what to do. Best solution IMO? Switch to gmail.
