### Post by philipacamaniac on 2005-04-22
From universe:

rosegarden is a notation editor / MIDI / audio sequencer.
ardour is a digital audio workstation. (Mostly for recording studio work)
audacity is an audio editor.
galan is a modular audio processing and synthesis system.
hydrogen is a simple drum machine / step sequencer.
tk707 is a drum sequencer for a sound card or MIDI device.


I highly recommend taking a look at Hydrogen and Galan.
