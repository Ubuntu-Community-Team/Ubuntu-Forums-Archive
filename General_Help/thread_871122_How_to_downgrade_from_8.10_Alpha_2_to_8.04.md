### Post by cariboo on 2008-10-02
IF you are going to ask questions about Intrepid go here:

[http://ubuntuforums.org/forumdisplay.php?f=346](http://ubuntuforums.org/forumdisplay.php?f=346)

There is no way to downgrade from a newer version to an older version. You have to do a clean re-installation. Trying to downgrade using apt, won't work, because all the files are newer than the previous version. The only thing I would suggest, if you have been updating your distro as the update come out, you should be up to the beta version.
