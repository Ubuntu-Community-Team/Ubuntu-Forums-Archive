### Post by BoneKracker on 2006-06-03
Just be happy you're getting KDE 3.5.  Some distributions don't have it in their "stable" releases yet.

There is a period of time during which upstream releases of software must be integrated into downstream packages.  A distribution like Ubuntu isn't going to automatically have the "latest" version of anything -- it's got to be tested and possibly patched; other parts of the distribution may have to be remediated or upgraded to work with it.

Maybe you could redirect that energy to bug the KDE people to get 4.0 out.
