### Post by Dex73 on 2010-09-18
Just put the disk you used to install Ubuntu in and restart. Instead of installing when it ask you if you want to try it out with out making any changes or install it, choose the try out option. It will look like you've already logged in, but what's really happening is your running Ubuntu off of the disc.(this can be done with or without Ubuntu installed) Go to Places and you'll see a very large external hard drive listed. Click something so you have a side pane, right click on the external hard drive, and click unmount. Though, Really you can use any method to unmount that you want. Then restart. Once the computer gets things in order it will ask you to remove the disc and your done.
