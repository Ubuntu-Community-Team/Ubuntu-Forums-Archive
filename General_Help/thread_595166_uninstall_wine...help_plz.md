### Post by ed-koala on 2007-10-28
To uninstall Wine installed software, go to Applications - Wine - Uninstall Wine software to remove programs installed.

To remove Wine, open Synaptic Package Manager, search for Wine, click on the box and select Mark for complete removal, then click Apply.  Wine will be gone from your system.
