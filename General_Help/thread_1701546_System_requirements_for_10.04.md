### Post by lithopsian on 2011-03-06
Your system should be just fine for a basic desktop, but will probably be slow if you have lots of effects enabled.  Turn off anything like compiz and think about switching to a more lightweight windows manager.

I have a machine with a 2.8 Celeron D, 512MB, onboard graphics and it is really pretty fast using Openbox and no compositing.

For Firefox, switch to 4.0 which will be released in a few weeks.  Currently a fairly stable beta is available.  It is significantly faster than 3.6.

Fragmentation shouldn't be a major problem for you although occasionally disks will get fragmented.  I wouldn't expect it to really hit performance until a disk gets nearly full.
