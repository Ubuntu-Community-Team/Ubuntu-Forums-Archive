### Post by Jimmy McKellar on 2010-02-10
Hi

Glipper does seem to tick most of my boxes though I still think a command line solution might be easier to taylor to my purpose of always having the text "to hand" ready to paste in. Glipper can have a bank of phrases (Email addresses, regular long texts etc) which are always available.  These can be set up under Plugins / Snippets (note you have to highlight the Snippets line before the Preferences appears).
