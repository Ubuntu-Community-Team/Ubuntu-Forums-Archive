### Post by DeadSuperHero on 2010-02-27
You can mess with icons as much as you want, really. 

Any icons you find on Gnome-Look can be put under 

/home/user/ .icons/

(just remember to get them out of the tarball first)

If you want icons to be accessed across users or across desktop environments, use sudo to copy them to

/usr/share/icons/
