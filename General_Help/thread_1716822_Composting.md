### Post by stinkeye on 2011-03-29
Composting means convert to compost.
Compositing permits advanced visual effects by using buffers and manipulating the images before they appear on the screen.

Compiz uses compositing.

To enable in metacity, in the terminal enter...
```
gconf-editor /apps/metacity/general/compositing_manager
```
and tick **Compositing Manager**.
