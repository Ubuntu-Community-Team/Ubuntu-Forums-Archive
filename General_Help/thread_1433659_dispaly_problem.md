### Post by akku0503 on 2010-03-20
Hai friend,
goto Systems->Preferences->Power Management
Select "On AC Power" tab, then select "Display" and set 
"Put display to sleep when inactive for:" to "Never"

Also turn of your screen saver.

Hope this may solve your problem.
