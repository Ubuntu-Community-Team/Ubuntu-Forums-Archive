### Post by MeURi on 2008-04-15
If I understood properly, that color you want to change is related to the theme you use.

In Gnome (hope you use Gnome, anyway it should be similar for other DEs) right click on the desktop and choose *Change Desktop Background*, then move to the *Theme* tab and choose *Customize*
--OR--
form the Gnome menu, choose *System>Preferences>Appearance*

Next move to the *Colors* tab and change the *Selected items* color

Note that not all themes allow you to change their color scheme
