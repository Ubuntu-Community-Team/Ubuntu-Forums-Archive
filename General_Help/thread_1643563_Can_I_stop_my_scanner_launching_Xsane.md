### Post by 2CV67 on 2010-12-12
Whenever I switch on my scanner (Epson V200) then it launches Xsane automatically.
Now I know Xsane is wonderful, but often I want to use Epson's iscan instead, and for other users I prefer Simple Scan.
Another thread would be a good place to discuss the reasons.

So I would like to stop my scanner launching anything when switched on.
After a lot of searching, I have not found out how...

Thanks for any tips!

---

### Post by 2CV67 on 2010-12-12
OK - I just found it:

System > Preferences > Removable Drives & Media > Printers & Scanners > Scanners > Uncheck *"Automatically run..."*

Call it Solved! :D
