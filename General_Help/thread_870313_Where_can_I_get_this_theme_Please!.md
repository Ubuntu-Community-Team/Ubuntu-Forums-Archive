### Post by ARhere on 2008-07-26
> **sistoviejo said:**

Well, there was not much of a "how-to" to it.

1. I found the desktop on [Gnome Art]("http://art.gnome.org"), there is also something similar on [MAC desktop]("http://macdesktops.com/") as well.

2. I right-clicked on both of my panels and selected properties; then change the panel bar color to a reddish-brown and set transparency to 60%.

3. Use gnome-color-chooser to change taskbar fonts to white.

4.  I left the Icons on Human theme.

It looks similar, not exactly the same though.  Try it and tell me what you think?
