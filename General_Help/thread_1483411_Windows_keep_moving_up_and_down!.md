### Post by cbecker78 on 2010-05-14
This is a bug with using the wrong screen resolution, if I remember right.  Go in and change the resolution of your screen to something less tall, and you should be ok.  The option should be somewhere under system -> preferences -> screen (or something in the preferences window).

Until you get it fixed, just make the window un-maximized in the middle of your screen so you can click on things.  I remember this was a real anoying behavior-
