### Post by Quackers on 2010-12-11
Welcome to UF :-)
Connect your pc to your router with an ethernet cable then go to System > Admin > Additional Drivers (or Hardware drivers) and run that. If drivers are available for your graphics card and/or your wireless card you will be given the option to download and install them.
Do you know which Wireless network card you have?
