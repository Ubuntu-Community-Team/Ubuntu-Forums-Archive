### Post by treehouse on 2008-05-08
Is there a way to get a view with the hours down the side, so you can drag stuff up and down it, but for more than just Mon-Fri?

---

### Post by asrai on 2008-05-08
Not the most elegant of solutions, but if you go into edit/preferences/general in evolution, you can tick the boxes to add saturday and sunday to 'work week' - this will allow you to see the weekends in the workweek view.
