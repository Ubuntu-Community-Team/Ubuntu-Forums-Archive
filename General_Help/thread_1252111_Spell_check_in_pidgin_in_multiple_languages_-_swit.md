### Post by my_key on 2009-08-28
Spell check in pidgin in multiple languages doesn't work in ubuntu jaunty due to the switch spell not being in purple-plugin-pack (although the package description says it is).

I've been searching for some time now to solve it and in my search I have found out that a lot of other multilingual ubuntu people have too. That's why I decided to post the solution here.

Michael Budde solved this bug in his PPA: [https://launchpad.net/~mbudde/+archive/ppa](https://launchpad.net/~mbudde/+archive/ppa)

Just install the "purple-plugin-pack - 2.5.1-0ubuntu1~ppa4j" package from the deb or add his repository.

You don't even have to restart pidgin. It'll just work. 

Hope this helps someone.

Michael
