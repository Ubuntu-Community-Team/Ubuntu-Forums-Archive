### Post by Cytochromec on 2009-11-25
Holy cow, so the default package "Document Viewer" actually does what I need. Its just a bit odd in that you have to press enter after you fill a field and it wont prompt you on close about there being changes and do you wish to save; it will just close and discard changes. However, if you make sure to remember to press enter after you fill fields, and remember to save, it will indeed save the fields just as good as Adobe Acrobat....except its completely free and a million times less bloated.
