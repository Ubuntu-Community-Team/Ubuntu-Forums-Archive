I am running Ubuntu 11.04 and am trying to find out how to mininize a page and them maximize it again . At the moment when I minimize a page it just disappears and I have to retart the application . ### Post by Frogs Hair on 2011-08-29
If you are using Unity look at the launcher on the left and the open applications will have arrows next to the icon .

If you are using Ubuntu Classic make sure the window list is enabled . Right click the bottom panel , select add to panel and choose window list from the menu .
