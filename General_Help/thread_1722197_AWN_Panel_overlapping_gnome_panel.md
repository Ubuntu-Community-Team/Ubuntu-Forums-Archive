### Post by Frogs Hair on 2011-04-05
I'm not sure there is a way around this if choose to fully extend the dock , which ever is active , the dock or the panel will appear on top. I have the same behavior . If you let the dock  length to adjust according to  the number of launches and use the position on screen slider to intersect with the with the panel it will not overlap  , but you won't  get full extension on that side

You could also try a wing panel moved to the top right side that would solve the problem.
