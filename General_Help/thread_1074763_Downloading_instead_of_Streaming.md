### Post by Herman on 2009-02-19
:D Try installing 'Download Helper' add-on in your Firefox Web Browser.

Just open Firefox and go 'Tools'-->'Add-ons', and then look for the link in the lower right called 'See All Recommended Add-ons', and look for the one called 'Download Helper'.

Here's a link for video how-tos about Download Helper so you will be able to see how to use it, [http://www.downloadhelper.net/tutorials.php](http://www.downloadhelper.net/tutorials.php)
