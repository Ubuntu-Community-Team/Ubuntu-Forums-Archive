### Post by mdpalow on 2008-01-19
In aMSN.....    click     ACCOUNT -> PREFERENCES

Click 'OTHERS' tab at the top right

Change: [COLOR="Red"]mozilla $url[/COLOR] to  [COLOR="Red"]firefox $url[/COLOR]


Change Sound Server: [COLOR="Red"]play $sound[/COLOR]  to  [COLOR="Red"]aplay $sound[/COLOR]    (note there's an 'a' in front of 'play')
