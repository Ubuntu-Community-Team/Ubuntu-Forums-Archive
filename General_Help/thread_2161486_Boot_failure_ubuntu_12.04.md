### Post by oldfred on 2013-07-10
When you say putting it on the drive, you do not copy the ISO to a hard drive?

You need to use a DVD or flash drive and install the ISO to the DVD/Flash drive. That extracts all the files and makes the device bootable as a live installer. You then can boot in either live mode to test or repair your system or install to your hard drive.

       Also instructions for DVD or USB
[http://www.ubuntu.com/desktop/get-ubuntu/download](http://www.ubuntu.com/desktop/get-ubuntu/download)
