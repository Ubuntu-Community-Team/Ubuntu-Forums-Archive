### Post by dayvy on 2007-10-30
"System" -> "Preferences" -> "Sound" -> "Sounds" tab. Have a look at this and disable some or all of the system sounds to your liking. I don't actually see a sound on delete option but it may be grouped with one of the other options.

### Post by jcarrete on 2007-11-13
It seems like the Sounds tab has no effect on the beep.  On the other hand, the System Beep tab does.  If you uncheck the 'Enable system beep' then the annoying beeping sound when deleting files in Nautilus is eliminated.  

I wonder if disabling the system beep is a good idea though as there may be more important messages needing a beep.  Certainly hitting the delete key is not one of them.
