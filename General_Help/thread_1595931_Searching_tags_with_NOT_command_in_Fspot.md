### Post by pieman711 on 2010-10-13
Is this possible? I've got some photos with two or more tags on and I was wondering if there was a way of only showing photos that had one tag and not another. I've found a way of doing it with AND and OR (the default) but I really want to search, for example, all my 'holiday' photos that don't have any pictures of person 'X' in. 
Any help will be greatly appreciated

---

### Post by pieman711 on 2010-10-13
Solved it. You have to right click one tag and go to find (as normal) click the next tag you don't want to see and right click and go to 'find with' and go to your original tag. Then right click on the new icon at the top bar an click exclude.
