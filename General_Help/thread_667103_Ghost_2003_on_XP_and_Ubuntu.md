### Post by erpo on 2008-01-14
Bravo for thinking to test your backups! Many people make backups, only to find out they don't work when they are needed.

The best thing would be to try to restore to separate, blank hard drives, which is what you would have to do if your current hard drives stopped working.

If you don't care enough about your data to spend the money for extra hard drives, then this option won't be available to you. In that case, I would recommend making tar files of your most important directories (like /etc and /home/your_username) and burning those to DVD. Archives in tar format are easy to check, support for them is good, and you'll know whether or not you've successfully backed up your most important files.
