### Post by K7522 on 2010-02-10
First let me point out that with wubi you are using a virtual hard drive, it's not the real thing. To edit that virtual drive, please consult the following link under "How do I resize the virtual disks?"

[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/WubiGuide#head-c1b3095de0e43733f9336427bb90d7ef322de99c](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/WubiGuide#head-c1b3095de0e43733f9336427bb90d7ef322de99c)

Personally, I'd repartition the drive and set up a dual boot system with a dedicated partition to Ubuntu though.

Most of your files, settings, etc should be in your home directory you can just throw them on a flash drive or CD and be good to go on your fresh install.
