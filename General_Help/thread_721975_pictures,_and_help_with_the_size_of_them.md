### Post by mindheavy on 2008-03-11
what program are you using to pull the images off of your digi cam? its possible there would be settings to do this automatically when you import if you want all of your images a certain size.

if you don't mind learning a little, and being as you obviously own a digital camera, i suggest playing around with the gimp. its a great program for editing pictures.

first tip:  to resize images...

after opening the image in gimp, at the top of the window, click image->scale image.

from this window you can now adjust the resolution to whatever size you enjoy.

hope this helps some.
