### Post by cmnorton on 2008-08-05
This is my experience; others' milages may vary.

Usually, a lot of this behavior is based on your chipset, even if you do something like install a USB 2.0 PCI card.. I had older USB 1.0 computers and even installing a USB 2.0 PCI card did no good, especially for disks and network devices. The throttling point was the chipset in the hardware. USB backup disks threw a lot of errors, and network devices would freeze.

The bottom line is, do not count on wireless  until you purchase new hardware.
