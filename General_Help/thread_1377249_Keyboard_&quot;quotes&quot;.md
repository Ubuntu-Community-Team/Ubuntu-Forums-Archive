### Post by goiggles on 2010-01-10
your keyboard layout is almost certainly wrong. 

goto system>preferences>keyboard

then select the layout tab. click the button next to keyboard model.

more than likely you will want the 105 key(Intl) or 104 key under generic

### Post by SeattleOtaku on 2010-01-10
You might have your keyboard set as "USA International (with dead keys)"; the dead-keys version has two sets of quotes, so [' ] vs [''] (and [" ] vs [""]) are different.
