### Post by Luisnux on 2010-02-12
I forgot to mention this, but you may want to check and see if the bios detects them.  Also if you have many peripherals attached the system may not have enough juice to power your peripherals.  I've seen that happen a few times with laptops, and although the keyboard and mouse should not consume that much power, if you have several devices attached, such as external hard drives, pc cards and stuff like that, it might keep those two devices from being powered (and therefore recognized).
