### Post by dschaller on 2008-05-15
The Lexmark 1100 is not well supported in Linux and will not autodetect according to Linuxprinting.org

[http://www.openprinting.org/show_printer.cgi?recnum=Lexmark-1100](http://www.openprinting.org/show_printer.cgi?recnum=Lexmark-1100)

It looks like there is a driver (lm1100) that might partially work, but the development on it stopped some time ago.

Unfortunately, it would probably be less trouble to go out and find another printer that is more compatible with Linux than to try to get your Lexmark working. When you shop a printer, do some searching online to make sure it is well supported in Linux first.
