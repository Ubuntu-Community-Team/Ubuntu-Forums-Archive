### Post by hyperdude111 on 2008-12-31
To fix the poblem in wine you need to obtain a windows xp theme follow this guide [http://tombuntu.com/index.php/2008/01/04/stop-wine-from-beating-your-windows-apps-with-the-ugly-stick/](http://tombuntu.com/index.php/2008/01/04/stop-wine-from-beating-your-windows-apps-with-the-ugly-stick/)

To fix the open office problem search open office in synaptic and download the open office human theme.

If these do not work and you have changed the gtk theme try to reset it to Human or clearlooks as some themes have visibility issues with text.
