### Post by grahammechanical on 2014-07-27
What version of Ubuntu are you using? If 12.04 is running on them then you have until April 2017 when 12.04 reaches end of life. There is nothing stopping you from downloading Ubuntu 14.04 or one of its flavours and testing out how they run. You might want to look at Xubuntu and Lubuntu. At lot depends upon the specification of the graphic adapter and how much of its own memory it has.

Linux will run fine on those machines as long as the machine is running fine. But can they keep up with the needs of modern desktop environments? That is the question.
