### Post by mikewhatever on 2010-11-24
The current stable iteration of Unity - the new netbook interface, is somewhat slow and crashy. Use the regular desktop GUI instead and [Macbuntu]("http://www.dedoimedo.com/computers/macbuntu.html") to make it look like OSX.

PS: I think comparing an OEM install of W7 to Ubuntu on your netbook is meaningless. Consider that a host of Asus engineers must have worked hard to optimize W7 for that hardware, whereas it's your job to do the same with Ubuntu.
