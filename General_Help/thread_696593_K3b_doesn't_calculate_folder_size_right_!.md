### Post by IanW on 2008-02-14
I assume you're trying to burn your entire /home? Don't forget your /home directory is full of hidden config folders for all your installed programs. 

Right-click in K3B's browser window and select "View / Show Hidden Files". Then you can pick out just the ones you want to burn (your email program's folder would be a good choice to backup)
