### Post by ft23002 on 2008-10-05
Hello,

Wondering if theres a way to stop the touchpad on notebook from changing the desktops? Using compiz fusion with 4 desktops. When the pointer is over the desktop (in focus or not), its switching desktops by mistake with the touchpad. Use the touchpad scrolling for other things, so don't want to turn that off although that stops it. Can't find anything in compizconfig settings manager to do this. 

Is there a setting other than having to turn off scrolling altogether to stop switching desktops by the touchpad?

---

### Post by Borlag on 2008-10-05
Disable the Viewport Switcher from the Compiz settings and it wont switch it with wheel anymore, or the touchpad.

---

### Post by ft23002 on 2008-10-05
That was easy enough. lol
It was getting to be a real annoyance.
Thanks

### Post by jtheriot on 2008-11-18
CompizConfig Setting Manager was not installed by default with 8.10 on my Dell D610.  After about 30 seconds, it was dowloaded, installed and the Desktop Switcher was disabled.

Thanks for the post.
