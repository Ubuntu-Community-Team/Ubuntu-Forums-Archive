### Post by machiner on 2005-01-22
It's a difficult task in linux. There are people that have success under WINE with some proggys. I have used Dvd:rip but it transcodes to an AVI file...there are other options, none I found appealing and you need a ton of space...one movie demanded 80GB of space to transcode the movie. No thanks.

 Not bad if you will watch your movie on your computer. There aren't many dvd players that will play the format.

This is the reason I boot to winxp on occassion to use my copy or dvdcopy platinum. Which, as I understand is a bastardized collection of open source programs anyway...but it works like a charm, quickly and without errors.

Linux native programs come up short in this area but you may have luck with mencoder, dvd:rip vobcopy or one or two others.

Good Luck
