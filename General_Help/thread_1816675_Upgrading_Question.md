### Post by cogier on 2011-08-02
BE CAREFUL - If 10.04LTS is not a problem I would suggest you stay where you are.

10.10 is fine but unless there is a particular feature I would not bother with an upgrade. Remember that the support will last longer for 10.04 that for 10.10. 

11.04 MAY be OK but it is very different from 10.04 & 10.10 and I have only seen one installation where the user is happy.

I suggest you wait until 11.10 or try 11.04 on another computer before taking the plunge.
