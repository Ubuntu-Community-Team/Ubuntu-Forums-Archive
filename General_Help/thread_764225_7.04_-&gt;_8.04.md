### Post by Pancetilla on 2008-04-23
Changing your /etc/apt/sources.list (replace Feisty Fawn with Hardy Heron) and sudo apt-get update && apt-get dist-upgrade should work. I hacen't done it yet with ubuntu, with Debian worked OK.

---

### Post by astoltz on 2008-04-23
It's generally not advised to skip versions while upgrading - 7.10 -> 8.04 is OK, but 7.04 -> 8.04 might cause problems.  6.06 LTS to 8.04 LTS may be an exception.  I'd do a fresh install of 8.04.  If you have a separate /home partition, all your settings should be carried over.
