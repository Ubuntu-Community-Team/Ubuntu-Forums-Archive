### Post by chip616 on 2008-11-24
OK, got it.  You use the + sign, but there can be NO SPACES between the search terms and the + sigen.

So, in my case, I had to type:

mplayer+"seek failure"

Man, who thought that up?  And why isn't it noted on the advanced search page somewhere?

### Post by PocketDog on 2008-11-24
You could have achieved the desired result with a simple search (top right, this page), omitting the 'and' altogether ```
mplayer "seek failure"
``` If you prefer, you can search this site (or any site) using the Google operators by entering```
site:ubuntuforums.org mplayer +"seek failure"
``` into Google itself. Again, though, the '+' isn't necessary. This may not find recent results though, as the spiders may not have cached the page yet.
