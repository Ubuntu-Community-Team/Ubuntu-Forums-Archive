### Post by MG&amp;TL on 2012-01-02
Possible, yes, but as far as I know, difficult.

Drop down menus, I'm not sure about, but applications should be fairly easy in comparison.

Look at the concept of .desktop files, [here]("http://standards.freedesktop.org/desktop-entry-spec/latest/"), they occur in /usr/share/applications. You could probably make some sort of compiz plugin or something.

Sorry I'm being very vague and can't be of more help, but it's a very big project. 

You probably want to appeal for help in the programming section of the forum at some point, for specific bits of your project.

AFAIK, there is no package that will do this. But I may be wrong.

Wireless hotkey, in Ubuntu, hit F10, then move along (with arrows) to wireless.
