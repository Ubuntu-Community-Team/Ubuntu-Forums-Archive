### Post by IsmAvatar on 2009-05-04
The Ubuntu symbol is a shortened version of the menu, kind of similar to Window's single Start button. If you click it, you'll see a new way of looking at your 3-menu thing.

If you still want your 3-menus back, click on some free space on the panel, and click "Add to Panel". Scroll down and locate "Main Menu" and add it.
