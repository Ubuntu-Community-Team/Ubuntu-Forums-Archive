### Post by SoFl W on 2011-07-11
Did you try System-> Preferences-> Keyboard->  Layouts tab, and then select your make and model in "Keyboard model"?

Also check:
System-> Preferences-> Keyboard Shortcuts and see if there are any associations with those keys and the letter that is typed.
