### Post by johnc4510 on 2006-05-26
I can only give you my experiences:
A. I use XMMS and my sound is pretty outstanding. Ofcourse I have a decent sound card and great speakers. I burn all my music in the ogg format at quality level 7.
B.Streamtuner and Streamripper work in harmony with XMMS for internet radio support.
C.XMMS is skinable, maybe not the best or most modern, but useable if you look around and find some decent ones.
D.XMMS has quite a few plugins available, try infinity visual plugin if you have a descent graphics card.
E. The music management is not the best but I get by.

The great thing about Linux is the freedom to try new apps, and if you don't like them, uninstall and try something else.

XMMS is the Linux equivalent of Winamp.
