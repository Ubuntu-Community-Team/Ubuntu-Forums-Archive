### Post by sikander3786 on 2009-12-07
You can remove those medibuntu repo's by going to System--->Administration--->Software Sources.
Just unmark the repositories you don't want to get software from.

There is an other way of editing sources.list

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Repositories/CommandLine](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Repositories/CommandLine)
