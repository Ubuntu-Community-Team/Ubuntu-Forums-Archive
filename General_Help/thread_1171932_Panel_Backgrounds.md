### Post by Jebtrix on 2009-05-27
None I know of. Your best bet is to google for tileable textures. If you have the motivation all you need to do is grab a screenshot of what you've seen that you liked and crop just the 'panel' background and save it from Gimp. If you want a transparent panel background you should change the opacity and save it as a png file. Then you will see your wallpaper thru the panel.
