### Post by deadflowr on 2020-09-06
But monitors are showing the screen, right?
Then it's working as expected.
You still only have one vga connection.
Multi-monitors refers to utilizing multiple outputs, 
such as connecting to a vga and dvi or hdmi or displayports simultaneously.
Having a single connection is only going to output that single connection.
Essentially all you can get is a cloned screen.
