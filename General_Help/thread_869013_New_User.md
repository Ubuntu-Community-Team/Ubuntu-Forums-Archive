### Post by Fingers &amp; Thumbs on 2008-07-24
Googling about, it is clear that there are various different experiences with this card. Some users say it works out of the box in hardy, yet there are many bug reports saying otherwise.

I don't use this card myself, but I'm sure it can be made to work. My advice would be to install ubuntu and google something like "Nvidia 9600GT hardy" and follow any advice that is reported as solved or working, particularly from a thread which has many "Thank you" comments. That way if it still isn't working then you will be able to continue here describing the steps you have taken and the links you have followed. Hopefully this will enable forum members to be able to assist you with a better understanding of why it has not worked on your system.
