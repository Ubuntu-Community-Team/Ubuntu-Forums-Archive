### Post by dragonfly41 on 2015-05-01
Click on menu button at far right of Firefox top bar (with three horizontal lines in icon).

At bottom of popup window which opens ..

Select Customize .. another Customize popup window opens ..

Select bottom left drop down .. Show / Hide Toolbars

Tick Menu Bar in the drop down list.
