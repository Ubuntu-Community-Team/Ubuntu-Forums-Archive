### Post by mcduck on 2014-10-01
You *could* do it in Blender, but that might be a bit of an overkill (and also quite a steep learning curve) depending on how deep into compositing and other stuff you want to go...

Take a look at [Synfig]("http://www.synfig.org/"), it might do what you want. KTooN and Pencil are also fairly common Linux animation tools, but more intended for traditional animation work. They might be worth checking out anyway.
