### Post by decoherence on 2011-07-15
Your screenshot looks okay to me, but if you're viewing this on a TV, you could be seeing sub-pixel anti-aliasing.

In the Appearance control panel, there is a setting under 'Fonts' to turn of sub pixel anti-aliasing (could be called 'hinting'). Try adjusting those settings or turning it off and see if the problem goes away.
