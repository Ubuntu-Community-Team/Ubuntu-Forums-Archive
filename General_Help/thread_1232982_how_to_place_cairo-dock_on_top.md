### Post by sam1948 on 2009-08-06
i want cairo-dock to be always on top and windows not to be over it 
just like a normal gnome panel, but I couldn't find how to do that
so I would love to get some help

i figure out a solution but it's not the best way.
1) clean the bottom gnome-panel from all the applets
2) right click on it end expand it to fit the cairo-dock's height
    select background --> solid color and make it transparent.
3) right click on the cairo-dock --> configure and uncheck the auto hide.
4) somewhere on cairo-dock-->configure -->advance select its distance from the bottom to be 0 and it height to fit the panel's height
