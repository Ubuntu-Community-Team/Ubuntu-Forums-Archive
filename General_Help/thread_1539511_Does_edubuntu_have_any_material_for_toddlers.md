### Post by pricetech on 2010-12-01
I have a 3 year old granddaughter who loves to sit in grandpa's lap and play on the computer.

Key phrase here is "sit in grandpa's lap"

My 13 year old granddaughter, when she was 3, watched her older brothers play on an old 486 I had given them and learned, by watching them, to start and play the games she enjoyed.  She is a well adjusted child, at least as well as a 13 year old can be, in spite of her exposure at such a young age to the evils of technology.

Okay, a little sarcasm there, but I think the key is indeed that your children / grandchildren / nieces and nephews / etc. have someone playing with them at that age and not try to use the computer or the TV as a baby sitter any more than you would use the toaster oven to baby sit.

An early introduction to technology is a good thing in my opinion, but your time and attention is worth more to them than anything in the world.
