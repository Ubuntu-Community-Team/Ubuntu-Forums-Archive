### Post by hackerb9 on 2010-07-28
> **miggols99 said:**
> I currently have a minimal Ubuntu installation which I use for a media centre, Inkscape (only Ubuntu works with my graphics tablet) and general web browsing. But it's a pain to login whenever I turn it on. Is there a way to get automatic login, then start Xorg without GDM?

I suggest "nodm". It'll automatically log you in without gdm (or xdm or kdm or startx) getting in the way between you and inkscape joy. 

Alternately, if you had room for gdm on your media centre, it has an option for automatic logins in the menu.
