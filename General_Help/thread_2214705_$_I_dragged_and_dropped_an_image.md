### Post by coffeecat on 2014-04-02
Using a Macbook? It's best if you specify this when you ask for help. The peculiarities of the Mac keyboard affect the advice given.

The other way of deleting is simply to right-click for the context menu, and "move to trash" or equivalent is one of the choices.

It's interesting that the Macbook has no delete key. There is one on the Mac aluminium keyboard. I'd find the absence of a delete key a real irritation because to hard delete a file, you need to move to trash and then empty it from the trash. With a PC type keyboard, it's one simple keyboard shortcut followed by a confirmation mouse click.
