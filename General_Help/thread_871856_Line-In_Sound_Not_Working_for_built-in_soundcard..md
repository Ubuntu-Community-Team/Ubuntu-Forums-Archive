### Post by todak on 2008-07-27
Right-click on the little speaker icon. In the dialog that appears, choose "Open Volume Control". In the mixer window you should see a "Line-In" port that is probably muted or has the input level slider all the way down. Either click the "x" below the sliders to make the port active, move the sliders up or both (as I had to do).
