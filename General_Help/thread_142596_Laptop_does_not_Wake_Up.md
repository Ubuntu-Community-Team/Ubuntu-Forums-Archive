Hi,

Here is one place to check. The OS has controls for things like sleep and power management. The BIOS also has controls for the same features. In the past I have seen conflicts between the OS and the BIOS. I usally diabled those features in the OS and relied on the BIOS but you can try it either way. Assumes you know how to manipulate your BIOS.
