### Post by tgalati4 on 2009-07-30
512 is really minimum, 1 GB is nice, 2 GB is styling.

Also is your processor a Celeron or Pentium?  Celerons will "feel" slower than Pentiums at the same speed because of smaller L1 and L2 cache on Celerons.

Get a newer harddisk (especially a 7200 RPM) install Ubuntu on it's own partition and you will be styling.
