### Post by giantoz on 2008-12-19
I'm sorry, I'm not sure exactly what you're asking.

Do you want to upgrade to 8.10?  the update manager doesn't show non-LTS versions by default.  You can change this by going to software sources-updates tab, and at the bottom you'll see "release upgrade"- change that from "long-term releases only" to "normal releases"
