### Post by 23meg on 2006-05-22
Depends on the wireless networking devices that your portable uses. I have an Intel Pro Wireless 2200BG in my Centrino (Sonoma) equipped laptop and it worked out of the box with both Breezy and Dapper. If you have a more obscure device (such as a Broadcom I guess?) you may have to do some work to get it working. In short, [make sure your hardware is compatible]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/HardwareSupportComponentsWirelessNetworkCards") when buying.
