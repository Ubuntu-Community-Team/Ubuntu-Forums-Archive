### Post by 3Miro on 2009-06-29
The drivers that come with Ubuntu are in System -> Admin -> Hardware Drivers.

1. Network cards can be tricky sometimes. There are way too many cards for Ubuntu to be able to support all of them. What is your network card?

2. Sound should be fixable. Have you check all the settings in the mixer (right click on the speaker icon and then select volume control, you will get way more options that way).

3. Graphics support under Ubuntu should not be a problem. Nvidia, ATI and Intel are all well supported.
