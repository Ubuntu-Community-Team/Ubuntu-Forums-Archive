### Post by aysiu on 2006-04-16
Some programs don't have launchers.

Sometimes they create launchers, but you need to refresh the Gnome panel (Alt-F2 and *killall gnome-panel*).

Sometimes the programs are command-line utilities and not point-and-click programs, so a launcher would be no good anyway.
