### Post by imaginashawn on 2010-09-12
I've gone a whole week since my original post with no response. Where's the support?
Well, I think I finally figured it out on my own. I was experiencing the problem several times an hour and now I haven't seen it act up once in two days so pretty sure I got it.
The difference? Send me ten dollars and I'll tell you .... just kidding. :p
What worked for me was, going to Synaptic Package Manager and uninstalling 'Netscape plugin support for Konqueror' called 'kongueror-nsplugins'.
Big deal huh? But it made all the difference in the world on my box.
God bless, Shawn
