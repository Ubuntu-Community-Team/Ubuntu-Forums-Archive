### Post by Roasted on 2009-12-04
One thing I liked about Gnome is I could drag/drop the currently opened windows in the panel so they were in a certain order, so I could have pidgin first, thunderbird second, exaile third, then firefox, etc.

Can I do that in KDE?

---

### Post by amingv on 2009-12-04
Yes.

-Right click on an empty space in the task manager, choose "Task Manager Settings"
-Under "Grouping and Sorting" set Sorting to "Manually" from the drop-down menu.
-Click OK.

You should now be able to drag and drop programs to any position you want on the taskbar.
