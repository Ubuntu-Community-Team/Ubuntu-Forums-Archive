Update the add-ons is a good idea.
In my case this message just appears some times and for now I am solving this problem just restarting the Thunderbird.
But I found some people that solved this problem by setting the webmail-hotmail add-on preferences to "Hotmail Live". 

To do that, in Thunderbird, click in 'Tools'->'Add-ons'. In the window that opened, click in 'Webmail - hotmail' and then in 'Preferences'. In the new window go to the tab 'Accounts' in the top of the window and choose the tab 'Mode' that is located above and select 'Hotmail Live'.
