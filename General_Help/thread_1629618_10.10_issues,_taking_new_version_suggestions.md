### Post by sikander3786 on 2010-11-24
Support for any older releases is likely to end within the next 5 months or so.

The best options are 10.10 or 10.04.1. In fact, support for 9.04 has already ended and for 9.10, it is scheduled to end in April next year. No big deals there.

It would be better to pop in the live CD and try 10.04.1 and all your hardware before doing an install.

And I were you, I would be inclined to solve the issues regarding 10.10 or 10.04 rather than falling back to an older release.

Good Luck!
