### Post by krytorii on 2012-05-13
If you want a shortcut to a file, open up your home folder, find the file, copy it and paste it to the desktop (either the actual desktop, or the desktop folder, it doesn't matter).

If you want a program, click on the top icon of the bar on the left, find the program you want and when it's running right click its icon on the left, and pin it there. It will then stay in the bar for you.

Lists like these are a great place to learn stuff that will make your experience better.
[http://www.omgubuntu.co.uk/2012/04/10-things-to-do-after-installing-ubuntu-12-04/](http://www.omgubuntu.co.uk/2012/04/10-things-to-do-after-installing-ubuntu-12-04/)
Just google for "things to do after installing ubuntu" for a load of them.
