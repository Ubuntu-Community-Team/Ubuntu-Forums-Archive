### Post by doclivingston on 2006-07-04
The plugin systems of the applications are usually similar, but a bit different. Probably the most useful thing to do would be to send a post to the applications mailing list, or ask on the IRC channel, saying what you want to do and seeing if anyone has a guide on how to go about it.


Most Gnome projects have their mailing lists at [http://mail.gnome.org/mailman/listinfo/](http://mail.gnome.org/mailman/listinfo/), and IRC channels on either GIMPnet (irc.gimp.net) or Freenode (irc.freenode.net).

For Rhythmbox in particular they are #rhythmbox on irc.gimp.net, and [email]rhythmbox-devel@gnome.org[/email].
