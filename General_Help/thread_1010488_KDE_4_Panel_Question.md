### Post by bobmendon on 2008-12-13
Is there any way to move the panel from the bottom to the top of the screen in KDE4?

---

### Post by Denestria on 2008-12-13
Click on the plasma (cashew) icon on the right side of the panel.  The configure bar for the panel should open then with options on it like 'add widgets' and 'remove panel'.  Click and hold the mouse button somewhere in the middle of this configure area and drag to where you want the panel.  Don't drag on the edge or it will resize instead of move.
