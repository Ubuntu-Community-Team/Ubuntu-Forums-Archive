### Post by squenson on 2009-09-19
There are pros and cons in hosting yourself your website. First, consider the time to properly set up your server; take into consideration the costs of electricity if you keep your server up and running 24x7; verify also that your upload bandwidth is adapted to your forecasted traffic. 

Free hosting providers usually display advertising to cover their costs. I am using freehostking.com but there are many others free ISPs around, and even many more if you are in the USA.
