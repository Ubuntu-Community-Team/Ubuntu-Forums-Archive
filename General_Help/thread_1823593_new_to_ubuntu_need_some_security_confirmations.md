### Post by haqking on 2011-08-12
It looks ok.

Antivirus is not really needed for Linux though there are some available such as Avast or ClamAV, but really only needed if you share files with Windows.

Though he shows his sources.list dont use it, always check that the sources are valid.

The best security for any system is common sense and vigilence.  Dont always update or upgrade just cos something asks you to or tells you to, check that it is needed.

The things to do list looks fine, and gets most of your apps up and running, but again no need to do them all, only do what you need for your system and your usage
