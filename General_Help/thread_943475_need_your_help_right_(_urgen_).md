### Post by lukjad on 2008-10-10
Try Ctrl+F7 to get back to the login prompt. If that doesn't work, when you restart hit Esc to enter the GRUB Bootloader and choose recovery mode. Once you get to the desktop, simply restart the computer and try to log in normally. Sometimes that actually is all it takes.

It is advised to use a descriptive topic title; that means use a topic title that gives some clue about the content in the thread itself...

A generic topic title like "noob here" or "need help" does not help at all. As you may have notices, just about everyone posting in here has some kind of a problem or issue or question
