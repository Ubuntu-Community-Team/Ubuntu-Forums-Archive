### Post by Anthorn on 2011-03-31
> **Joe of loath said:**
> Why are you asking about a problem in IE 8 on an Ubuntu forum?

Could be because people who are investigating Linux for the first time are likely to be using Windows and Internet Explorer.

In answer to the OP, are you entering the URL of your OpenID page at your OpenID provider's web site and are you at the same time logged in to your OpenID provider's web site? When logging in with OpenID you need to be already logged in on your OpenID provider's web site in the same browser window. If trying to log in to a page supporting OpenID on an Ubuntu page, most of Ubuntu only accepts the Launchpad login (baaad Ubuntu) and you'll need to sign up at Launchpad and use the Launchpad login which is said to be OpenID enabled. Confused? You will be! In other words most of the Ubuntu web site does not in fact support OpenID even though it might say so!
