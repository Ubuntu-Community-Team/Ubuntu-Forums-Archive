### Post by megabuffen on 2011-02-18
This thread seems to have the solution.

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=967188](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=967188)

You should open the terminal window and issue the command "sudo apt-get remove gdm-guest-session". It will prompt for your password. Then restart your computer.
