### Post by white_tiger_daniel on 2006-04-18
You can get icons on your desktop by running **gconf-editor** and go to apps->nautilius->desktop and you should be able to show the computer, tras and home icons.

### Post by z_diver on 2006-04-18
Gconf is good for adding the defaults like "My Computer", "Network Places" or their equivalents, but for adding program icons to the desktop, how about just right clicking on the launcher in the Applications menu and selecting '[COLOR="Magenta"]Add this launcher to desktop[/COLOR]'?
