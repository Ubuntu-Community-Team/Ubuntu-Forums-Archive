### Post by budgie9 on 2006-12-17
I don't have a lot of experence with HP printers, but here is something that may help you.
I hope that you are not wasting your cards, use cheaper paper for your testing.
Why not try a variety of the printer drivers in the settings. You may well find as we do with Canon that one of those printer drivers actually prints at A5 size, basically a 1/2 page size.
The other thing is are all the settings as they should be in the printer driver? It is often beliieved that you only need to set up say OpenOffice and all will work, that is often not the case, as you will find the driver itself also has a lot of settings that can be changed.
Hope this helps you.
