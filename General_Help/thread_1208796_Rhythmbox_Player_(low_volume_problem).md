### Post by craig10x on 2009-07-09
Thanks Don1500....the problem was is that my headphone sound volume was not turned up high enough (even though the "master" volume was)....so, simply by turning it up to maximum, i now have plenty of volume on Rhythmbox when playing music......

I had forgot to check the headphone volume setting....which is important when you have external speakers plugged into the headphone jack.....](*,)

That resolved the problem and now i can really enjoy the music..... ;)
