### Post by tuxcantfly on 2007-08-05
The Ubuntu-installer team is working on it. According to [https://blueprints.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+spec/ubiquity-automation](https://blueprints.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+spec/ubiquity-automation) and [https://blueprints.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+spec/installer-for-windows](https://blueprints.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+spec/installer-for-windows) it is being done by Evan Dandrea, Colin Watson, and the rest of the ubuntu-installer team. Since none of us are members of the ubuntu-installer team, we haven't had too much access to the fine details about progress on the code, but it's progressing well.
