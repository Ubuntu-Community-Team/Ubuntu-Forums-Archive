### Post by |{urse on 2008-05-22
all you have to do is go to [www.gnome-look.org](www.gnome-look.org) look under gtk2.x themes and pick one you like. 

it will download to your desktop.

menu > system > preferences > appearance

drag and drop the theme you downloaded into the window. 

you do the same thing for icons ^^

You won't be able to install the emerald themes without emerald theme manager. Stick with gtk2.x.
