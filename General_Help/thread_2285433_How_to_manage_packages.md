### Post by tristan16 on 2015-07-06
Ubuntu automatically checks for updates and lets you know when they're ready to install. You can change your update settings by searching "Software and Updates" in the dash, and going to the updates tab.

To see a list of changes (installs, uninstalls, and upgrades), open the Software Center and click on History. This shows you a list of changes and when they happened.

Synaptic Package Manger is a GUI that makes it a little easier to manage installed packages. It's available in the Software Center.
