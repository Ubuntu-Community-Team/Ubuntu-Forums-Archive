### Post by Cephyr on 2006-02-21
Well, wireless can be a rather difficult proposition in Ubuntu (and all Linux for that matter) because the card manufacturers prefer to just manufacture Windows and Mac versions of the drivers.

Do some research into "ndiswrapper", you may be able to convert the Windows driver for Linux use.
