### Post by netJackDaw on 2009-04-27
I am not totally familiar with your laptop model but it seems like an ordinary laptop, ie not a netbook like EEE PC. Netbook remix is intended for netbook computers with smaller screens. Note that the name is "Netbook remix" and not "Notebook remix".

I'd say go with a normal desktop Ubuntu release for your laptop.
