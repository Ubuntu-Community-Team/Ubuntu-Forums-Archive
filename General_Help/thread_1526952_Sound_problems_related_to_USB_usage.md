Any suggestions?

### Post by JohnDMcClane on 2010-07-09
For those who wonder what happened, I went to System -> Settings -> Sounds and I thought I had tried everything there, but what had happened, was that:
A) Under the tab Sound Effects, the Volume For Warnings was muted
B) Under the tab Output, it had set the wrong output unit (I needed to use the internal sound, analog stereo)
