### Post by oldtimer7777 on 2011-11-27
You need to install PlayOnLinux PPA, and then you need to update everything first.

And if you get prompts for updates, you need to accept them too..  No way around it.

Here is where you can find the PPA for PlayOnLinux to update your software:

[https://debianhelp.wordpress.com/2011/09/12/to-do-list-after-installing-ubuntu-11-10-aka-oneiric-ocelot/](https://debianhelp.wordpress.com/2011/09/12/to-do-list-after-installing-ubuntu-11-10-aka-oneiric-ocelot/)

It is located somewhere in the middle of that long list.

Just cut and paste the PPA into your terminal, and update and upgrade.

Also for good measure, make sure you have the PPA for wine installed, and upgraded too.
