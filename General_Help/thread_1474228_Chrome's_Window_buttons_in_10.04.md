### Post by bdig85 on 2010-05-05
In Chrome, click the icon in the righthand corner that looks like a little wrench. Click on options, then make sure that you're using Chrome in its "classic" blue theme rather than the GTK+ theme, which will reflect whatever theme the system is currently in. The next thing is, certain themes still have the buttons oriented to the right, like Clearlooks, Darkroom, Dust Sand and New Wave. If you don't want the classic blue theme for Chrome, pick one of those and the buttons will stay on the right.
