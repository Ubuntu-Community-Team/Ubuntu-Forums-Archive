### Post by Toadinator on 2009-10-30
I have just upgraded to Ubuntu Karmic Koala (after a little trouble, but I fixed all of that this morning). I have this weird problem and I don't know how to fix it. On the Gnome Menu (both kinds; normal and the small icon) I'm not getting icons for certain menus. Due to restrictions at the moment I can't upload an example, but the System/Places Menus (on the small menu) don't have an icon, and the Bookmarks/Preferences/Administration sub-menus don't have icons either. The menus themselves are mostly fine, but the icons for the menus (the icon representing the menus I mentioned) are absent. Anyone know of a fix?

### Post by Amazona aestiva on 2009-10-30
Go to System->Preferences->Appearance->Interface and tick the upper checkbox

For lucid Try this out :

Right click menu -> edit menu -> Under "System Tools" select "configuration editor" (it will make configuration editor available on your menu bar).

Open up Configuration Editor -> Desktop -> Gnome -> Interface

Check-Mark 

1) menus_have_icons
2) buttons have icons

Done !
