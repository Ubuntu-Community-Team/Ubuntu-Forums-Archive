### Post by kanikilu on 2012-11-02
I don't think you're going to find a 100% fully-featured online shell, but there are some cool ones out there:

[http://cb.vu/](http://cb.vu/)
[http://unixblogs.blogspot.com/2010/11/online-unix-shell-terminal.html](http://unixblogs.blogspot.com/2010/11/online-unix-shell-terminal.html)

Or setup your own:

[http://antony.lesuisse.org/software/ajaxterm/](http://antony.lesuisse.org/software/ajaxterm/)

Not exactly what you are looking for, but kind of cool for bash scripting (and other languages):

[http://www.compileonline.com/execute_bash_online.php](http://www.compileonline.com/execute_bash_online.php)
