### Post by Frogs Hair on 2011-06-01
Hi and Welcome

_I have not tested this !_

Open the Configuration Editor > Apps > Gnome Power Manager > Time out . You will see AC/Battery  sleep times displayed in seconds . By right clicking the line of the option you want to change you can edit the option . Change the value to the number of seconds you want . I am not sure if this will work and you can always reset the time values.
