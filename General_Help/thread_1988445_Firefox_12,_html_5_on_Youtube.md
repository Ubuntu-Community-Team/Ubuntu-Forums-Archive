### Post by PhantomTurtle on 2012-05-27
Get the flash video replacer extension for firefox. I have used it and it is very good. Here is a link to it - [https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/flashvideoreplacer/]("https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/flashvideoreplacer/"). It will replace all youtube videos with totem. I use it and it is very good. It is made by one of the members on the forums, lovinglinux. Also make sure to disable html on youtube first.
