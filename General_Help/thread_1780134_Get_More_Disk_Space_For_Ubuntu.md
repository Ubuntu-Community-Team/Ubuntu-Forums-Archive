### Post by sanderd17 on 2011-06-11
Since you installed it with Wubi, you won't be able to remove windows. You first need to follow a migration guide: [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1519354](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1519354)

Once it is migrated (so then it will not be a wubi installation anymore, but a full installation), you can remove windows.
