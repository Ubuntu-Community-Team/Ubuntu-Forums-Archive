### Post by stevebond001 on 2008-09-22
Hi
Can anyone please help.  I have installed Hardy and everything seems to be running OK with one exception.
I have a Logitech cordless keyboard which has various function buttons such as sleep, mute, volume up / down, etc.
The sleep button on the keyboard works fine.  However, the mute button, volume up/down buttons, etc do not seem to work.  The icons appear on screen when the buttons are pressed (i.e. the mute icon appears) but I can still hear sound.
When I installed Hardy I had to mess about with the sound settings to get any sound from the speakers.  I now have sound from the speakers but this will only mute when I mute either the headphone or PCM output from the volume control applet (screenshot hopefully attached).  When I mute the Master output from the applet nothing happens.
I am guessing that that the keyboard mute, vol. up / down buttons aren't configured to mute the PCM output so that's why they aren't working properly.  Does anyone know how to change these settings to allow me to mute correctly?

Many thanks in advance.

---

### Post by lian1238 on 2008-09-22
Go to System->Preferences->Sound
Then go to the lower section "Default Mixer Tracks". I believe that's what gets muted/unmuted by the keyboard shortcuts.
