### Post by Th3Professor on 2008-11-18
That is odd. I cannot remember if I have ever encountered that issue. You might try a variety of troubleshooting steps. Here are some ideas for consideration (they may not work but may be worth a try)...

1. Reboot.
2. Enable settings and close their windows.
3. Disable settings and close their windows.
4. Reboot.
5. Enable settings and close their windows.
6. Reboot.

That might not do anything at all, just a thought though.

Actually, save that as a last resort. Try this first:

1. System>Preferences>Appearance>Visual Effects>"Normal" or "Extra".
2. Log out and back in.

If it still isn't working, and you selected "Extra", try selecting "Normal".

I discovered that with compiz I must select "Normal". My $300 GPU (video card) (with all the necessary drivers) is more than capable of "Extra" though at the moment the system doesn't like pairing the GPU with "Extra" for Visual Effects.

You might also check to make sure you have all the necessary drivers. I'm assuming you do since you are able to achieve what you want at least within one boot/log-in. So, it looks like it might be a matter of "tricking" the system into getting the settings to save, which might require some experimenting with how you adjust settings and re-logging in to test them.
