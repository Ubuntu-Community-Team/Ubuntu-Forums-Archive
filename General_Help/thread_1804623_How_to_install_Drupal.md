### Post by dragonfly41 on 2011-07-15
Before starting to install drupal you will need a platform such as LAMP

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/ApacheMySQLPHP](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/ApacheMySQLPHP)

I also suggest that you start a prototype development site (free) on

[http://www.drupalgardens.com](http://www.drupalgardens.com)

to get a "feel" for drupal .. and you can later export your first prototype drupal site into your ubuntu drupal site.
