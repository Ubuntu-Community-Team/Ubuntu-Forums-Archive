### Post by 3rdalbum on 2010-02-06
Source: You.
Destination: You.
Looks like you're running some programs that are split into two parts, and those two parts communicate with eachother via sockets. That's a perfectly valid way for programs to communicate.

Port 16001 appears to be Enlightened Sound Daemon, which is odd because Karmic doesn't ship with ESD (it has Pulseaudio instead). Maybe it's something to do with how Pulseaudio interacts with Gnome.

In any case, this traffic never goes outside your computer - so it's nothing to worry about. And this is one of the reasons why I advise ordinary users not to use Firestarter - its logging ability just causes paranoia and worry amongst new users, although you're not so new.
