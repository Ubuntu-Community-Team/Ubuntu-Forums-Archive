### Post by grahammechanical on 2015-11-21
Please do not shout. I does me head in. After all, this situation is nothing to do with us.

What you are seeing is the boot menu (Grub). You should also see in that list Advanced options for Ubuntu. That is a sub-menu select it. The load Ubuntu (recovery mode). At the recovery menu select Resume.

Does that get you to a working desktop? If it does go to System Settings>Software & Updates>Additional Drivers tab. If you are connected to the internet and you allow the utility a little time your should get an offer of some video drivers that you can experiment with.

Not much else to be said, really, There surely is a reason why the loading of Ubuntu stopped. But there is not enough information even to guess. And we can indeed use a Ubuntu live session to back up our important documents.

Regards.
