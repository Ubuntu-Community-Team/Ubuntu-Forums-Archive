### Post by trlkly on 2009-04-17
Well, first reboot to make sure LimeWire isn't running. 

Then try the open-source alternative FrostWire (there are others, but this is the closest to Limewire).

Just go to [http://www.frostwire.com](http://www.frostwire.com) and follow the instructions to install. I don't think it's in the repos.

ETA: Note that Frostwire IS exactly the same program as Limewire, just updated. So you may still have the same problem. Then you'll need someone else's help.
