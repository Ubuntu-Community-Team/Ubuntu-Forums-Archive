### Post by Timotea on 2008-03-02
Me i've use this way:


- Go on Application bar and open "Add / Rem.."

- On the pop-up windows, on "show" (top right) select: Installed Applications

- Now you suppose to see KMESS on the list (to go faster you can select "internet" on the left panel)

- Uncheck KMESS and do "Apply Change" on buttom

- Enter your user password and that's it
