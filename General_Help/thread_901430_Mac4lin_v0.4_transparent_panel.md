### Post by xj0hnx on 2008-09-17
Don't know if this is exactly what you are looking for but maybe...

System > Preferences > Advanced Desktop Effects Settings

Click on "General Options" at the top, and then click on the "Opacity Settings" tab. Click on The "Window Opacities" arrow, and then select "New", and add "name=gnome-panel" and then set the slider to where you want it. Hit close and you're done.
