### Post by kanikilu on 2008-11-26
Open a new or existing image in the GIMP and go to:

Edit -> Preferences -> Window Management

Then in the "Hint for the toolbox" dropdown, select "Keep above".

It's not very obvious, but it should work...

### Post by bogdan.veringioiu on 2008-11-26
Hi.

Just right-click on the gimp entry in the task bar, and check Always on top.
You can do this for all the wished toolbars that you want to keep on top in gimp, as they are separate windows.
