### Post by Ub1476 on 2008-03-17
If you're trying to connect to a wireless access point, here's how:

1) Click on the networkmanager applet on the panel
2) Click your network
3) Set the correct protection from the dropdownmenu #This one is often a point were people fall off..
4) Write the password/key #And this one as well. There's a different between a password and a key!
5) Connect
