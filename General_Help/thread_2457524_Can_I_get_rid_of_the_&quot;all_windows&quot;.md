### Post by TheFu on 2021-02-04
These sound like GUI questions, specific to Gnome.  There are about 20 popular GUIs.  Pick a different one or look for addons/plugins for the one you have to accomplish whatever you like. You don't need a DE either. Try using just openbox or fvwm or fluxbox or i3 (if you like pain) [https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/X_window_manager](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/X_window_manager) has more.

"Terminal" isn't anything but a program. I bet there are 20 different terminal programs. Pick one that does what you like.  I have 15 **xterms** on my system running now.  There are lxterm, lxterminal, aterm, rxvt, rxvt-unicode, not including all the gnome-terminal, mate-terminal, and other options.  I dislike tabs.  I want new windows, so most of those terminals in my list don't support tabs.
