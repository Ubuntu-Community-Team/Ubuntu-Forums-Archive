### Post by AliTabuger7 on 2009-09-09
When you press the caps/num lock keys on the keyboard, do the lights change?

How far into booting does it go? Do you see the GRUB menu? Do you see the bios?


Right off the bat, I'm tempted to say there might be something wrong with your hardware, so if you have the chance, try a memtest. You boot into memtest86 from the CD you installed with.
