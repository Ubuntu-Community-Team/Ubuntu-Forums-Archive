### Post by crichard on 2010-08-27
Press Write button to Create a new message,
Type your content
In Formatting Bar, you've the options
to select text color & the background, 
& even more text styles like Bold, italics, & underlines are availble. 

In case if you're not familiar with this, Goto Menu bar Format -> pick Text Style & Color options. :)
