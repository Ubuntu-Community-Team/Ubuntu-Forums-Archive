I am not 100% certain I have understood your question, but I suspect that what you want is installed by default.

Are you looking to be able to scroll windows vertically from the touchpad?  If so, you should find that rolling your finger along the right edge of the touchpad should achieve this (without any keyboard presses).

If you want to do something more complex than this - have a look at this thread, which gives a few links to how to change touchpad settings:
