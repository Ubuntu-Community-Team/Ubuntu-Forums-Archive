### Post by drs305 on 2008-07-05
Do you have 'Workspace Switcher' installed as a shortcut on one of your panels, and is that inoperative as well?

There is also compiz's Viewport Switcher. You can take a look at your settings there as well (System, Prefs, Advanced Desktop Effects Settings). I don't have it enabled and can still switch workspaces but you should check those settings regardless.
