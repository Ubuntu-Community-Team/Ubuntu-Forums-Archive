### Post by Rallg on 2009-03-03
If the problem is that the messages are pretending to be from addresses in your list, then allowing only addresses on your list might not work.

Are the messages being generated from within Pidgin, or are they coming from the mail service (Hotmail)? If from within Pidgin, consider doing a complete uninstall of Pidgin, including settings, and also look for any hidden pidgin folder in your home diretory (delete it).

If from Hotmail, does Hotmail have a filter that allows you to junk messages by title or content? This can be done in Gmail, For example. You would instruct it to look for a certain pattern of words, then junk it no matter who the sender claimed to be.
