### Post by aysiu on 2006-05-10
You can't get rid of certain packages (I think *evolution* is one of those) without removing *a lot* of other packages.

However, if the only package being removed is *ubuntu-desktop*, you can safely remove that. It's not a real package, just a pointer.

If you ever decide to upgrade to Dapper, though, you should reinstall *ubuntu-desktop* before doing the upgrade.
