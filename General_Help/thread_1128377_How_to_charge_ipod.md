### Post by quickk on 2009-04-17
Hi everyone, 

   I have an ipod shuffle, 2nd generation (the clip), and have been searching for days to find a way to charge the ipod without using itunes.  For some maddening reason, the ipod will only charge after it's been synched with itunes.  

Anyway, I finally found how to do this and I thought that I'd share the info with everyone. 

Using Ubuntu 9.04, plug the ipod into the usb port.  Right click on the ipod icon on the desktop and choose "eject".  If you choose unmount instead, the ipod will not charge and you have to unplug/replug it in to get the eject option back.  That's it!  The ipod should now be charging (for the shuffle, you should see a solid orange light instead of a blinking one).  

This seems really simple, but I swear that it drove me crazy for way too long.  It is also possible that the eject option did not show up with previous ubuntu versions, explaining why I didn't see it (I don't know) until I installed the 9.04 RC yesterday.  

Hope this helps!
