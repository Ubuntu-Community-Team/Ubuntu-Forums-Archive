### Post by DagMan on 2007-06-13
Have you had a look at the motherboard for any problems that can be visibly seen like capacitors that are bulging at the top a little or have some brown glue looking stuff or encrusted stuff leaking from the bottom at all?  Also maybe pulling the ram out and sticking it back in can sometimes solve bizarre problems.  Same things with cards.  Make sure the heatsink isn't clogged up with dust while you're in there and clean it out if it is... that sort of stuff.
