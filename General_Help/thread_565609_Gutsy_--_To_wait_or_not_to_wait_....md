### Post by cwhitehouse on 2007-10-02
That really depends on what you use your computer for.  Is it mission critical?  Can you *maybe* put up with more bugs using 7.10 that you may not have with your current version?

I for myself have used both 7.04 and 7.10 on my new thinkpad.  I initially used 7.10 and ran into some issues where OpenOffice wouldn't open after an update.  So I talked myself into going back to 7.04.  However, after a day of fighting with drivers I went back to 7.10 since my drivers were installed automatically.  Since then (Tribe 5) I have been applying all of the updates and I haven't run into any major issues.  But that's just my point of view, I'm sure I don't use all of the same applications that you run.  But overall I have been very happy with the stability of 7.10.
