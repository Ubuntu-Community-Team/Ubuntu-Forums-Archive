I strongly recommend that you read the [Firefox optimization and troubleshooting thread](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1193567). The solution to your problem is in the Troubleshooting section. Nevertheless, you might also want to read the Profiles and Backup sections, since they have pertinent informations related to your questions.

About the auto-start problem,  go to "System >> Preferences >> Startup Applications", click the "Options" tab, then untick "Automatically remember running applications...", then restart.
