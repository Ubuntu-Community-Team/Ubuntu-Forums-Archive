### Post by Mark Phelps on 2010-11-09
No-one can really tell you how "well" any version of Linux will run on a specific PC -- which is why you need to do the LiveCD thing yourself to see.

As to MS Windows apps, Wine (and its companions) do support SOME of the Office components -- but Access and Outlook are no-go.

As to the "Live" series, they don't fare well, either.

If you are dependent on MS Windows apps, you are not going to have a positive experience moving over to Linux.  The apps here offer many of the same features, but the look-and-feel tends to be different.
