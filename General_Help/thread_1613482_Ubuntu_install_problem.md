### Post by Carl Hamlin on 2010-11-04
Are you installing from CD or USB?

If you're installing from CD, check the integrity, and let us know how that turns out for further instructions.

If you're installing from USB, it sounds like you might have tried to write a new ISO to an existing installation archive, resulting in a mixed (and corrupt) installation. When I did that a couple of weeks ago, I hung at the same spot, and it took a couple of hours of head scratching to figure out where I'd boneheaded it.

One way or another, I'd look at the installation media first and make sure it's solid.
