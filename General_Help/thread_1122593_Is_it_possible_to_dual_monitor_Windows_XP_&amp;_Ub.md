### Post by ubudog on 2009-04-11
Well in virtualbox, you can make it into fullscreen on one monitor and have ubuntu on the other.  So yes you can run windows in one monitor and ubuntu in the other at the same time.  I think in Virtualbox you hit the right ctl key to take control of windows and hit again to go back to ubuntu.  Just drag the window with windows xp in it to one monitor and make it fullscreen and hit ctl and you will have windows.  Hit it again and you will have ubuntu.
