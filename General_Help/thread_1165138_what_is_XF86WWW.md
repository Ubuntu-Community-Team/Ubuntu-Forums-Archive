### Post by Brandon Williams on 2009-05-20
If you have a keyboard that has a special key for accessing the web, it should map to that symbol. If you don't have such a key on your keyboard, then you won't have a hotkey already assigned to that symbol. You could use xmodmap to map some other keystroke to that symbol, but that's typically not worth the trouble. Just change the keyboard shortcut to something else instead.
