### Post by Armed Nuke on 2008-06-06
hey, i know what ya mean. just a few days ago i struggled with that thing. what you should do is go to the general options. it should be at the top of that menu. then go to desktop size. set the horizontal virtual size to 4. then go back and go all the way back to just before you clicked on general options. go down to the desktop section. make sure there the boxes beside desktop cube and rotate cube are checked. uncheck desktop wall if it is checked. now you should be able to click in your mouse wheel over the background and move left and right with it to zoom out and around the cube.
