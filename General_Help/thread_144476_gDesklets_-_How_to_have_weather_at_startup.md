### Post by ronmarley1 on 2006-03-14
Jim,
Have you tried this weather applet?  Right click on the top panel and then click "Add to Panel..."  Under "Accessories," click on "Weather Report" and then click "Add."  Once it's added to the panel, right click on the icon and then click "Preferences" to change the units (Celsius/Fahrenheit, etc...) and your location.  Just another option to gDesklets.
