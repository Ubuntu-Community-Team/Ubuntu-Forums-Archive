### Post by clehel on 2005-08-13
Hi,
I recently looked for a good GUI ftp client, and I saw that some other people were in the same shoes. Now I would like to report that the Windows version of the open source client, FileZilla, works well under wine. The versions I use:

Kubuntu 5.0.4.
wine 0.0.20050725-winehq-1
FileZilla 2.2.13a

wine during installation and upon starting FileZilla reports some errors related to windows, and FileZilla starts with little crowded windows. But in full screen it is just perfect.

Csaba

### Post by steffen on 2005-09-25
They now have a Linux client in development. You can download nightly builds from [http://filezilla-project.org/nightly.php](http://filezilla-project.org/nightly.php)

This is one of the very few apps I've missed in Ubuntu, and I'm extremely happy to see it become available. Simply download the file, unzip it and click on bin/filezilla in Nautilus and it will start. Includes locales and icons as well.

I just found it by accident, and it hasn't crashed yet - but I've only been using it for 10 minutes  :-P

Note: Doesn't seem to crash, but it is not as fully featured yet as the Windows version.
