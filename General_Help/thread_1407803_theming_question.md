### Post by paulmmj on 2010-02-15
(Top Left Corner) System > Preferences > Appearance

I suggest tinkering until you come across something you like, make sure to play with all the settings. There are infinite variations. Also, Google search "GNOME Themes", "Ubuntu Themes", or something of the like. There are loads of fan created and official themes available for download, including things like "Make Ubuntu Look like Mac OSX/Windows."
