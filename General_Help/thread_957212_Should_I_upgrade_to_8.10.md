### Post by waspbr on 2008-10-24
I get the update fever, most of us techies get it often. If you are going to make big changes to you OS you have to answer yourself these questions

[LIST=1]
[*]If this my only/main/work computer, can I afford that something goes wrong?
[*]Have I backed up my essential files?
[*]Do I have time to tinker about with the new OS?
[*]Is it compatible, (live CD test)?
[/LIST]

if you can answer yes to all the questions then sure go ahead
