### Post by Zeroangel on 2006-02-22
I use Firefox 1.5.0.1 and it has been unstable for me sometimes when viewing media. 

I've managed to remedy this problem by installing the 'Tab Mix Plus' extension. It has session saving capabilities, so if the browser crashes, you can just open up firefox again and it will give you the opportunity to recover the crashed session.

The crash will repeat itself with a small amount of media, so when you recover, you can just close the tab before the media finishes loading.
