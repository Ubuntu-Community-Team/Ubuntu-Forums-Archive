### Post by adampyre on 2007-06-05
This is just a shot in the dark... realy going out on a limb here, but how is the rating on your power supply? I have heard of people who don't have a heavy wattage power supply who upgrade their computer, and then it suddenly locks up and powers off on them because the power supply can't handle the equipment when it starts pulling more wattage... maybe doing the aptitude update causes the hard drive, NIC and other elements to pull more wattage than the power supply can handle? I know this is a shot in the dark, but worth considering?

Did you recently upgrade your computer at all?
