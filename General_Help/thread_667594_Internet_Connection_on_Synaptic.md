### Post by Timbothecat on 2008-03-25
For the record, I was having a similar thing. My son's pc has Ubuntu 7.10 on it and every time he tried to play some music he couldn't get it to work. I knew the solution was in the repositories so I ticked all the boxes as per the images posted by ugm6hr (great stuff, thanks for that). I had left the bottom box checked though, "Installable from CD..." and as a consequence it kept asking for the CD. The problem is that my son's DVD burner doesn't work -I had to install Ubuntu using my DVD burner- and the ISO that I downloaded didn't have all the packages on it. Therefore I kept getting an error telling me that I needed to insert the disc.

Once I unchecked the box at the bottom Synaptic automatically checked online for any updates etc.

Hope this can help stop someone else having heart palpitations and what not.:)
