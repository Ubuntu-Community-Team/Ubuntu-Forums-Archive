### Post by prem1er on 2010-01-05
So, just download unetbootin. Insert your flash drive. Run unetbootin. Select the Ubuntu 9.10 distribution. Let unetbootin create the live flash drive for you. Reboot your computer and have it boot from the flash drive. You can then begin to test Ubuntu on your system and install if you like it.

Depends on your system. You could try to google for boot order for your computer. Different computers have different keys that will prompt for boot options. Try looking. Some common ones that I know are esc, F12 or F8. These need to be held down while your computer is posting.
