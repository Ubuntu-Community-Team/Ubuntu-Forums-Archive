### Post by boonshanka on 2010-04-03
Scanning to work with your Ubuntu 9.10 OS is easy. 
1. Plug your lexmark X5630 into your computer through USB port. 
2. Plug in a Flashdisk or flashdrive into your lexmark printer. 
3. From the printer : A) Scan B) Scan to USB Flash drive C) Press OK D) Press Start
The flashdrive/flashdisk will automatically mount onto your desktop for you while it is plugged into the printer. No driver even needed for scanning photos and images.
