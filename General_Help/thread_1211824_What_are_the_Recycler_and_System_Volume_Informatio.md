### Post by ad_267 on 2009-07-13
These are Windows directories that are hidden in Windows. [Recycler]("http://support.microsoft.com/kb/171694/en-us") is for files you send to the recycle bin and [System Volume Information]("http://support.microsoft.com/kb/309531") is used by system restore.
