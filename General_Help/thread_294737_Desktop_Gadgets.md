### Post by RikoW on 2006-11-07
lot's of those you see are probably *gdesklets*. It's in the repositories. There are several applet, which you get from the gdesklet web [http://gdesklets.org/]("http://gdesklets.org/") site providing weather info, systom monitors, rss newsfeeds etc. There are also some of these in the repos in the gdesklets-data package.
I usually prefer just to get what I want from the home page.

In my opinion a nice system monitor is also *gkrellm*, which can be extended by plugins, but already comes with most of what you need.

Cheers,

              Riko
