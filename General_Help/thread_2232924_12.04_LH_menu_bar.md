### Post by coffeecat on 2014-07-05
Start firefox from the dash and the Firefox icon will appear in the Launcher (what you are calling the menu bar). Right click on the Firefox icon in the Launcher and choose "Lock to Launcher". Once it is locked, you can move it within the launcher by dragging it out into the desktop and then back into the Launcher in the position you prefer. In 14.04 you can drag a Launcher icon directly to your preferred position within the Launcher, but in 12.04 I believe you have to drag it out into the desktop area - without releasing the left mouse button! - and then back into the Launcher again.
