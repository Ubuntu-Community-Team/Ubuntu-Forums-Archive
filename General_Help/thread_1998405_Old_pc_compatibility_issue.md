### Post by snowpine on 2012-06-06
Personally I would remove the hard drive and insert it into your main computer.  (If your main computer doesn't have a spare drive bay, then you can purchase a USB hard drive enclosure for under $20.) Then you can easily copy the old data from that hard drive to your main hard drive.

I am not an expert in data recovery but there is some info [here]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/DataRecovery") (maybe a little outdated) and I know that [testdisk]("http://www.cgsecurity.org/wiki/TestDisk") is popular.
