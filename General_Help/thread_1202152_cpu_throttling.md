### Post by 3rdalbum on 2009-07-01
CPU scaling happens automatically. If the CPU is under load, it runs at full speed. If the CPU is not under load, it goes to half speed.

If you want to increase battery life, look into Powertop. It's in the repositories. You can also find power-saving suggestions at [www.linuxpowertop.com](www.linuxpowertop.com)
