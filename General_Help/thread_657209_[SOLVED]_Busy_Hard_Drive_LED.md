### Post by gilloz on 2008-01-03
In Windows, I would check the Task Manager to see what was running when my Hard Drive LED was constantly illuminated.  What can i check in Ubuntu 7.10 to find out what is running in the background when the HD LED is illuminated or blinking many times?

---

### Post by -Zeus- on 2008-01-03
<Alt+F2> and type "gnome-system-monitor"

### Post by gilloz on 2008-01-03
Thank you Zeus.  That did the trick.
