### Post by niyam on 2010-08-17
All right folks! While waiting for gammu/wammu fixes, I found a quick workaround to this problem. The alternative is to use my gmail account. 
1) Transfer all my contacts as a vcf file to my gmail account so my entries appear in gmail's contacts.
2) Use 'ActiveSync' on the samsung wave to sync all my contacts, calendars, and tasks with the phone.

More details to the steps here:
[http://niyam.com/blog/2010/08/sync-your-mobile-while-saying-bye-bye-to-ms-outlook/](http://niyam.com/blog/2010/08/sync-your-mobile-while-saying-bye-bye-to-ms-outlook/)

However, am still waiting for a gammu/wammu fix whenever it's available.
