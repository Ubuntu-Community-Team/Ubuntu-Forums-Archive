### Post by FahadMKS on 2010-11-11
Yes, 

you may first connect the computer wired and check for the Hardware Drivers and the Ubuntu Updates.

You can do that by selecting SYSTEM >> ADMIN >> Additional Drivers.
You will get the Wireless drivers selected by Ubuntu.
Install the recommended version and then restart the computer and then try to connect wireless and you will have the issue resolved.
