### Post by mb_webguy on 2009-02-23
The security model is meant to keep you from doing bad things to your computer, and to keep other people from doing so as well.  If you choose to bypass that security, any damage you may do is entirely your own fault.  That doesn't mean you can't bypass it if you want.  It's your computer.  You have all of the passwords.  If you want to break it, go right ahead.

As for the website, that's the default repository mirror for Ubuntu users in the US.  That's where all of those applications you see in Add/Remove Applications and Synaptic Package Manager come from.  Yes, there are plenty of applications in there to modify and customize your system.
