### Post by fragos on 2009-09-04
Scrolls about 4 lines per scroll wheel click for me with a default configuration. I also have a USB touch pad which scrolls in the same increments. My best guess is that you'll be able to control the scroll wheel in /etc/X11/xorg.conf. Xorg will override the automatic settings by Hal. A bit of Googling should find the settings for you to try.
