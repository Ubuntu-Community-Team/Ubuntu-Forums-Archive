I've looked in several places, and I haven't found a good simple answer for this. Until this one:

If you get a black screen and there is a white terminal in the upper left corner of the screen. Also, your not able to even type in the terminal, try this!

1) boot up in recovery mode
2) go down to "root terminal w/ Internet access"
3) write this in the terminal.  "sudo apt-get install gnome-session ubuntu-desktop"
4) And all should be fine and dandy.

** This action does remove some programs that didn't come with initial install, but you'll find that all least you have your computer back without losing your files.
