### Post by aysiu on 2006-06-17
All the templates I see are a .stw extension, not .cab, and they appear to open with OpenOffice by default.

**Edit**: You can open a .stw template right away, or if you want it available for future use, you should copy it to /usr/lib/openoffice/share/template/en-US/internal/

Well, substitute your actual locale for *en-US*.
