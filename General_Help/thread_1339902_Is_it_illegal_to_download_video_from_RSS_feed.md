### Post by jamesisin on 2009-11-28
If the owner of the material is allowing the RSS feed to supply the material you are in fine shape to download and view what they have authorized downloaded and viewed.  If NASA is delivering video content, they presumably have retained ownership over that content and you would thus be prohibited from redistribution.  For a more detailed account (and probably one that is more accurate too) contact a copyright attorney.
