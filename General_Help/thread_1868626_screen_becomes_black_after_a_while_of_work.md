Recently I have installed new Kubuntu after the partition format. After few seconds from KDE start, the screen suddenly turns black and I can see and move only a mouse cursor. I realised that switching the session to the console (ctrl+alt+F1) and then return to X (ctrl+alt+F7) or X restart (ctrl+alt+backspace) helps - everything works correctly until reboot. Closing and opening the flap of the laptop not helps. In Kubuntu 10.10 which I used earlier nothing like this happened.
