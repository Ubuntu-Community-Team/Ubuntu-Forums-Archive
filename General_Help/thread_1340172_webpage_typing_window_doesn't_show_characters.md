### Post by AlanInVancouverBC on 2009-11-28
I found the fix-around.
By resetting the "Configuration Mania" add-on in Firefox, the vertical insertion bar and the typed text appear. Some optional check box in the add-on was the culprit. I'm not going to study which toggle it is.
