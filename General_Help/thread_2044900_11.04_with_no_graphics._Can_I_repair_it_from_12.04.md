### Post by Glynnux on 2012-08-20
My solution was to - 
1]    boot into Recovery mode. It was necessary to be hard connected to the internet (as WiFi wouldn't connect).

2]   select dpkg - wait while it did it's thing. 

3]   choose the Safe Graphics Mode

4]   install the drivers

Advice courtesy of Wolfman on the Pinguy Forums.
