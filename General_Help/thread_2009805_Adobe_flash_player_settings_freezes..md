### Post by efflandt on 2012-06-25
For some reason that happens when the flash player is not full screen.  So make the player full screen before attempting to change flash settings.

If you have any trouble with Smurf faces on YouTube videos (blue and red reversed) try unchecking flash hardware acceleration.  I have never had that problem with ANY other videos, only YouTube videos (but no problems with ads on YouTube).
