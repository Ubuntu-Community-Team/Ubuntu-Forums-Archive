### Post by mjpatey on 2008-06-17
This may not be a solution you like, but here's my experience... I was having a similar problem before upgrading my video card.  I had an old ATI with 64MB RAM, and in Firefox, sites like Gmail, Digg, etc. took FOREVER, especially with the move to Firefox 2.

I was due for a new video card anyway, so I broke down and got an inexpensive nVidia 6200 (itself a pretty old card) with 256 MB RAM.  The problem vanished.

Also... do you have any other browsers / OSes on the same computer that handle the scrolling better?  For me, Ubuntu's Firefox browsing performance has always felt slightly slower than in Windows.  Now that Firefox 3 is out, I'd be willing to bet it's better, though I haven't been back to my ancient Windows partition in many, many months to be able to confirm.

-Mark
