### Post by mali2297 on 2008-03-31
I believe that they are called *kernel threads*. Don't worry, I have them too. In fact, I think every healthy linux system has a few of them.

If you go to **Setup -> Display options** in htop, you can see an option to **Hide kernel threads**. Perhaps you somehow disabled this option.
