### Post by spin2cool on 2007-04-01
Check the WINE appdb to see if your software is known to work: [http://appdb.winehq.org/](http://appdb.winehq.org/)

For the vast majority of windows-only programs, you'll be better off finding out what the linux-native equivalent is.  There are numerous video-capture programs written for linux, google or search these forums and find one.  It's likely that you'll save yourself a lot of hassle.
