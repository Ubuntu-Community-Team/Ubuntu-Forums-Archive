### Post by kolo-01 on 2010-08-23
my indicator applet in my main panel has lost all of the icons including skpe spotify and the network indicator and now only has the sound and battery power. i do not know how this happened as there is no option for removing those icons from the indicator, so does anyone know how to display them again? thanks

---

### Post by Pollox on 2010-08-23
Note that your indicator applet is different from your notification area.  They are by default side by side on the panel and may not be able to tell apart.  Skype and the network indicator appear in the notification area when they are running only.  If they aren't there, they probably aren't running.  To start them again, start skype again and start nm-applet again (not sure how one manages to accidentally stop it in the first place).
