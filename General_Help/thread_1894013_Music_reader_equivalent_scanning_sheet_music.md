### Post by searchfgold6789 on 2011-12-12
Audiveris is great. You can convert things to MusicXML (or secondarily MIDI) and then from MusicXML to Lilypond.
I have tried it before. It's a bit frustrating, and only on the verge of practicality, but if you can't really retype the score without a lot of tweaking you might try Audiveris instead. I remember it being very easy to compile, learn, and use.
