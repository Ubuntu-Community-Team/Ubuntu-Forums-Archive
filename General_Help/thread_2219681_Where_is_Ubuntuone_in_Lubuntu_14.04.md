### Post by speartip on 2014-04-25
The Linux client for Google Drive is insync.
[https://www.insynchq.com/](https://www.insynchq.com/)
You can download a 15 day trial, & then sign up for a moderate one off payment. I think it gets you 15 gig of cloud space.
I have used it & it works reasonably well.
