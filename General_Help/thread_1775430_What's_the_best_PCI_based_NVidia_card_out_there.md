### Post by Deadtrooper on 2011-06-04
Do you mean PCI or PCI Express? Different sockets not compatible with one another. PCI was superded for graphics by AGP which in turn was superseded by PCI Express.  Post your system spec, if it really is that old you might be better replacing the whole box. Even the most basic box with only onboard graphics from the last 3yrs would leave it in the dirt.
