### Post by seawolf167 on 2011-12-15
Ubuntu 8.04 LTS has past its [end of life date ]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Releases")- I suspect this could be because you may be using an old[er] kernel which doesn't support drives this large, the easiest way to find out if this is indeed the cause would be to download and burn a new[er] Ubuntu LiveCD, boot off of it and see if it recognizes your 2TB drive.
