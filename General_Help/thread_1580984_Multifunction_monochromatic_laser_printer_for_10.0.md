### Post by helbent forleder on 2011-01-31
Threw caution to the wind and bought the SAMSUNG SCX-4828FN.
It works extremely well on the network for printing. I use the USB connection for scans though.
It worked well under Lucid and now under Meercat.
The driver was relatively simple to install.
In this printer's price range it's far more economical than the competing HPs if you buy the 5000 sheet toner cartridges.
I'm very happy with it.
