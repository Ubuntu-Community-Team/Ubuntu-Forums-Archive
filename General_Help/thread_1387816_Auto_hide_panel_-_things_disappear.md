### Post by Saiko Berry on 2010-01-24
Well Gnome panel has really tacky transparency issues. Since I dont use panel widgets or anything ridiculous like that, I use tint2 customized to my liking in tint2rc ~/.config/tint2 which includes a place for time/date and a transparent working tray menu. It just basically does the two things you want from a panel, minimize, tray and time.. and if I want to put the time in my conky you can remove it and customize the fonts colors and whatever to your liking in tint2rc.
