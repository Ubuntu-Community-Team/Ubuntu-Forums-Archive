### Post by ramjet_1953 on 2007-07-09
Sound Juicer should do this nicely for you

If you don't already have it installed, just copy and paste this into a terminal:

[COLOR="Red"]sudo apt-get install sound-juicer[/COLOR]

You will also need to have the gstreamer plug-in for mp3

It may also be an idea to have a look at the audio codecs how-to in the Absolute Beginner Forum.
