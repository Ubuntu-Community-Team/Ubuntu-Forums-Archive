### Post by David Marrs on 2005-08-09
[QUOTE=redismycolor]Please help.  I have tried both Knoppix and now Ubuntu LIVE CD's.   Neither one will load fully.
They stop at 80-86%  trying to load ATAPI CD-ROM.[/quote]
It's possible that your disc didn't burn properly or that the image you downloaded is corrupt (I'm presuming you downloaded it). You could try doing a md5 checksum to ensure the integrity of the disc, try re-burning at a slower speed, or try a different make of CD-R.

Failing that, I presume it gets as far as the start-screen. You could try running one of the other options available.

>  I thought the LIVE CD's would load without having to change ANYTHING on your current system.....so you could TRY IT OUT...
That's the idea. It's not allowed to write any data to the hard discs, only to your RAM, and that gets erased as soon as the system is rebooted. So in theory it should leave your system completely untouched, and so far - touch wood - I've not seen that theory proven wrong.
