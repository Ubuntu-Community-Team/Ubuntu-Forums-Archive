### Post by Frogs Hair on 2014-08-19
Hello and Welcome

I believe Internet is referring to a category of applications including Firefox , instant messengers, and so on. Older versions of Ubuntu had this category in the menu , however in Unity, if you type Internet you will see related applications that have been  previously used. 

> To start Skype, choose Applications->Internet->Skype. It usually takes a minute or two for Skype to get started, and you may think that nothing is happening, so be patient.
