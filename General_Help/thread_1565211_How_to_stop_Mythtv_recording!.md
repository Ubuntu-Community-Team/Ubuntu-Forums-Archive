### Post by LewisDre4m on 2010-08-31
To stop Mythtv recording Simply go to "Media Library" from the main menu then "Watch Recordings" navigate to the recording that's still in progress and click your menu button or "m" key on your keyboard, then select "Stop Recording" 

Hope this helps, it was driving me crazy so I thought I would share the knowledge :).
