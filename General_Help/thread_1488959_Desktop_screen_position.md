### Post by silkworm2.5 on 2010-05-20
Have a look at your settings in System> Preferences> Monitors and look at what that says there.
Do you have Compiz Desktop Effects enabled? If so, go into it and disable Mouse Polling.
Remember that you can move a windows by holding ALT and click-dragging any part of said window
Forth would be right click on the top bar and look in properties. In my config (Default i think) is only ticked expand is yours likewise?
