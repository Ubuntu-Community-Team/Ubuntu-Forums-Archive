### Post by Andreas1 on 2010-01-13
the gnome panel is a mess, but...

you can edit the gconf settings instead of dragging things to their place: run gconf-editor, go to /apps/panel/[applets or objects] and edit the values "locked", "panel_right_stick" and "position" of the items. that's the only way i can keep my panel in order.
