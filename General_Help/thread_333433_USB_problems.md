### Post by rbmorse on 2007-01-08
That could be a hardware problem, too.  Shorting the +5V line to ground is what the "reset" button on the front of the case does, so if you have a bad connector or connection in the USB chain it could cause the symptoms you see.  

Since the problem occurs with any port you try take a look at the plug on the device, or try it on another machine, if you can.
