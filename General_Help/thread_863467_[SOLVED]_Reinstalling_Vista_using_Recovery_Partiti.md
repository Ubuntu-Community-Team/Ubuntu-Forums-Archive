### Post by Titan8990 on 2008-07-18
You need the recovery partition + recovery CD. Many computer manufactures require that you make your own recovery CD via their software installed on the OEM OS.

### Post by Mgiacchetti on 2008-07-18
actually with my compaq, (xp mediacenter)  you only need the cd's that are made on your pc if you want any important documents/pictures/movies     i totally removed my windows partition, didnt make a cd and hit f10 at boot and was back in windows in a matter of 1/2 an hour.

Also, i learned that (with xp mce at least) if you can obtain a OEM disk to go with your key, you can use nLite to basically create your own recovery partition without all the preinstalled crap that comes with the pc.  (although you do have to go about the tedious job of finding ALL the drivers for your specific pc and then save them to the recovery partition as well...)
