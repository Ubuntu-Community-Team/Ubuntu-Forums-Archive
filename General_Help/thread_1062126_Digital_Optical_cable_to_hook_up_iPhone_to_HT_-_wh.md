### Post by mb_webguy on 2009-02-09
> **PsychedelicWonders said:**
> How can a cable work with ipods & 1st gen and not work with 3g phone?

I mean a cable is just a cable right?

Yes, a cable is just a cable.  There are 6 standard connectors for USB cables, and several non-standard ones, but as long as your cable has the right connectors for your devices, and it isn't damaged in some way, then it's going to do its job.  If the devices don't then communicate with each other the way you should, it's not a problem with the cable.

Apple is notorious for trying to prevent their devices from working with any software than their own.  iPhones and iPods have always posed problems, though people have found ways to work with the older models.  Since Apple keeps coming up with new ways to make it difficult for people to use Apple devices without Apple software, newer devices are going to more problematic that older models.
