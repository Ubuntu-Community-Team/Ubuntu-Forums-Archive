### Post by 2F4U on 2011-12-29
Here are two posts which explain on how to create templates:

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=241677](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=241677)
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1617815](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1617815)

Depending on your desktop environment, there are several editors (Notepad replacements). For the Unity desktop the equivalent is gedit (Text Editor in the dash), for Kubuntu it is either KWrite or Kate (depending on the distribution you are using) and for Xubuntu it is leafpad.
