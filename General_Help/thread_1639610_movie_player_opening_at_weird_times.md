### Post by Frogs Hair on 2010-12-06
Hi,
 
From the places menu open the file browser from the computer icon . Right click on one of the home folders and select open with another application . When the menu appears select file browser . This will tell the folder open with that application . Make sure the remember this application box is checked. Do this for the home folders , music , pictures , and so on. Some how your folders where told to open with the movie player.
