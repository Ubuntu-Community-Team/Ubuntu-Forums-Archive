### Post by nanotube on 2006-05-02
bittorrent does not open any of your files to others to download. the only things it uploads is the very file that you are currently download, and nothing else. 
for more info, check
[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bittorrent](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bittorrent)

### Post by Sutekh on 2006-05-03
They way torrents should work is people can connect to other people who either have all of the file or parts of the file.  So while you are downloading parts of the file from n many people, there are m many people copying bits from you.  

Its give-and-take, share and share alike.  Some torrent trackers 'reward' people who have allowed as much uploading as downloading, some won't let you use them unless your ratio is a certain level.

So don't worry that there is uploading going on, you are just helping the cause!
