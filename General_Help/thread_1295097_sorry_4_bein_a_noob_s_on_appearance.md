### Post by iTheBadGuy on 2009-10-19
Make sure you have the drivers installed for your video card. I had to enable them - Nvidia drivers..

Then right click on your desktop -> Change desktop background -> Visual effects Tab -> Click on extra. If you can't enable them then you need to get your video card drivers sorted out.. it should tell you on the top right if there are some drivers to be enabled if you want effects.

After you do this, if you want the Cube/burn/beam-up/explode effects like you see on YouTube you need compiz.

If you like video tutorials:

How to install compiz: [http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=vhqevALhYF4](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=vhqevALhYF4)

Cool settings: [http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=LGY9cwSjZsU&feature=player_profilepage](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=LGY9cwSjZsU&feature=player_profilepage)

Hope that helps..
