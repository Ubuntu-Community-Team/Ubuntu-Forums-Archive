### Post by Crakie on 2007-06-27
I can certainly imagine your reluctance to get an Intel after those negative experiences. My Ipod recently died and I am not replacing it with another Apple product - fortunately as far as mp3-players go, there is a lot more to choose from than processors are concerned. The fact of the matter is, however, that Intel products are superior at the moment. They are faster, more energy efficient and cost about the same, if not less than equivalent AMD processors. Reliability is another issue of course, but the latest product lines have not been out long enough to say something sensible about it. Personally, I haven't seen any complaint about the Core 2 Duo line. All in all, you might want to consider an Intel after all.
