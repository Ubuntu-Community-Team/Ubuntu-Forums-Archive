### Post by 3rdalbum on 2013-11-26
Just because you installed it and don't know how to remove it, does NOT make it "spyware".

The Facebook web app is just another package in the repositories - or rather, two packages. They are unity-webapps-facebookapps and unity-webapps-facebookmessenger

Remove those using apt-get or Software Center, and remove their .desktop files from .local/share/applications and they wont 'bother' you again.

### Post by katalepsykills on 2014-03-14
It's easy to uninstall all these Web Apps.  Just go to your Ubuntu Software Center and search for unity-webapps and it will list all the web apps that integrate with the Unity desktop.  You'll have to click the Show technical items box at the bottom first in order to see them.  But the ones that you have installed will have a green check mark over the icon on the left.  Simply select and remove. One at a time.
