### Post by az on 2007-07-04
I would suggest a laser printer since you can get a cheap one for about $100.00.  If you buy an inkjet you will end up spending more than that on ink every year or so.

Check out this site: [http://www.openprinting.org/printer_list.cgi](http://www.openprinting.org/printer_list.cgi)

for printer compatibility, or ask the vendor.  The sales people at your local computer store should be doing this work, not you.  And if linux users don't let hardware vendors know why we chose the brands that we do, nothing will ever get any easier.
