### Post by EricCFlem on 2007-02-02
Go ahead and download deskbar-applet, along with xfce4-xfapplet-plugin.

The first item is what I think you've been using, and the second... well... it has no real functionality by itself, but it lets you use Gnome applets within the Xfce panel.  Once you load this utility, you'll need to tell it which Gnome applet to display.  Click on deskbar-applet, and you should be good to go.

Hope that helps.
