### Post by bodhi.zazen on 2008-02-18
I have seen this debated without conclusion so you are likely to just solicit opinions.

One problem with running 24 / 7 is you are leaving the door open to crackers 24 / 7 as well. The most effective deterrent to things like bot nets is to turn you computer off when not in use.
