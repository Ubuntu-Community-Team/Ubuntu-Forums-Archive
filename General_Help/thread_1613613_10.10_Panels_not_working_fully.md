### Post by P4man on 2010-11-05
I guess you are using multiple workspaces without realizing it?

Try the workspace switcher bottom right. Or control+alt+arrow (left or right arrow). If you want to disable that feature, add the workspace switcher to your panel again (assuming you removed it), right click it, preferences and set it to 1 horizontal and 1 vertical.
