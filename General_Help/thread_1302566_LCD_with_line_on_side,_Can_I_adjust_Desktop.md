### Post by Giblet5 on 2009-10-27
The correct answer is: replace the LCD.

The alternate answer is: buy a roll of duct tape.

Nothing can be done about the dead pixels on the right of the damaged LCD. The whole screen is backlit and you can't shift those liquid crystals around to block the light, so your only option is to fix it or cover up the damaged section of the LCD.
