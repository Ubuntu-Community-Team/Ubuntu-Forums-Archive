It is possible that your hardware cannot cope with the Unity interface. To get round this at the login screen click your user name and type your password. Before clicking on Login look at the bottom of the screen for Ubuntu. Click there and choose Ubuntu classic, now login and you should get the Gnome Desktop with the familiar layout.
