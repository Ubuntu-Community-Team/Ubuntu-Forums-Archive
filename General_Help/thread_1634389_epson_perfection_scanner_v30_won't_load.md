### Post by guildofghostwriters on 2010-11-30
You can downland the avasys linux driver for your scanner from this website [http://www.avasys.jp/lx-bin2/linux_e/scan/DL1.do](http://www.avasys.jp/lx-bin2/linux_e/scan/DL1.do)

Once you've selected your scanner and all the other options, you'll be able to download deb or source files (one data and one core package) & instructions to get it up & running.
