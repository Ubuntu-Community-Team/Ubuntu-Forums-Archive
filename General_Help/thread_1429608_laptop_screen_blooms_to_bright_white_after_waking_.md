### Post by pbrane on 2010-03-15
It's probably not the refresh rates if your monitor works otherwise. nvidia-xconfig probably chose the right values. I think it may have to do with the POST after you resume. The nvidia card should POST and restore. You could try using a more up to date driver. I use 190.53.
