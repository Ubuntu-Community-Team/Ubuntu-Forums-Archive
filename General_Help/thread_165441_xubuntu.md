### Post by aysiu on 2006-04-24
[QUOTE=sinkxdie]What do you mean brown rectangle? I boot my ubuntu and there's a grey, lined screen for about a second at the begining. Then it goes to normal with my wallpaper. under System, it says About Gnome, so does that mean I'm running gnome?[/QUOTE] It's a brown rectangle, whose longer sides are horizontal and whose shorter sides are vertical.

If you went to System > Preferences > Sessions and unchecked the box that says "Show splash screen at login," then you won't see that rectangle, but I'm talking about the default settings.

The easiest way to know whether you're using Gnome or XFCE is to click on the menu button (whatever it is). If you see a bunch of applications in there that start with *x*, you have XFCE. Otherwise, you probably have Gnome.

My guess is that you have Gnome. You can't really accidentally get Xubuntu, especially on Breezy. You have to go out of your way to download and install it.
