### Post by yugnip on 2011-12-31
Pinguy OS 11.04 (based on Ubuntu 11.04) uses Gnome2 and therefore can ship with Mint Menu. Pinguy OS 11.10 (based on Ubuntu 11.10) however uses Gnome3 and Mint Menu no longer works. Pinguy's solution for 11.10 was to use the Cardapio Menu in it's stead. Cardapio, while not as full-featured, seems to be a decent substitute for those users not yet interested in Dash or that style of browsing.
