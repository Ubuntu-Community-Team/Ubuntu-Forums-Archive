### Post by ajgreeny on 2012-01-13
Silly question.  What did you do with whatever OS you used before ubuntu?

Just use it as you would have done in windows, assuming that was your previous system.  Ubuntu has just about everything that you could want in an OS, including of course, all the applications that you needed to add to windows to actually do any work, eg an office suite, graphics application, photo manager, music player etc etc.  Enable the **medibuntu** repos or search for **restricted-extras** in the software center and you will also find how to use mp3s and other media file types as well.
