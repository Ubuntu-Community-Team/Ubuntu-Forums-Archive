### Post by JProgrammer on 2004-11-07
Not evolution per-se but if you compile and install [mail-notification](http://www.nongnu.org/mailnotify/) you can use that to check for your mail and let it open evolution when clicked. It is non intrusive and only displays in the notification area when you have new mail. And it works for gmail accounts too which is really good.
