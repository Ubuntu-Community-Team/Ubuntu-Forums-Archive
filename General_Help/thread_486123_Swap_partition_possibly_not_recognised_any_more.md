### Post by rajasekhar.yeruva on 2007-06-28
@ azertus  ,

After resizing the partition your UUID listed for swap in /etc/fstab no longer valid. So you can correct the problem by updating your UUID with new UUID in /etc/fstab or replacing UUID with /dev/partiitonname(sdaXXX). Once you are done with that you can specify $ swapon /dev/sdaxxxx or $ mount -a
