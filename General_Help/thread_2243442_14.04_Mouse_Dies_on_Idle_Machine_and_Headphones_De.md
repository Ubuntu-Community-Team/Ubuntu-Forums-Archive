I have continued applying updates as they come along and have not noticed the mouse die for several days now.  The headphone issue was a setting I had on the former system that was not automatically copied.  More importantly an issue which I have had for almost four years now, that my Dell AMD based laptop would fail to wake up from suspend when the cover was opened, now seems to be resolved as the system has recovered every time for the last several days.
