### Post by cryptotheslow on 2011-10-12
Do you routinely apply the proferred "automatic updates"?

May well be a recent kernel, module or driver update resolved whatever was the underlying cause of your conniptions.

If that is the case and you have time on your hands, you could review versioning information for updates applied prior / around the time to conniption cessation and refer to the associated change logs for what was patched / fixed in each update. Chances are you'll stumble across a fix that was made that coincides with some particular hardware or combination of hardwares you have.
