### Post by Terrycymru on 2009-11-14
Do you mean you want to set the correct time and date for your location? If so, click on the time and date at the top right of the screen. Then click on Locations and click on the world map for your location.

If you mean you want to just correct the time or date, click on Locations>Edit>Time Settings and then you can edit time or date.

### Post by kjohri on 2009-11-14
Try from the terminal command line,

[I]sudo date MMDDhhmm
[/I]
where MM is month, DD is day of the month and hh and mm are hours and minutes respectively.

*date*

See if the date printed is correct.

If yes, do a restart.
