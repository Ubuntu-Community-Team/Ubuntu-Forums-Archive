[http://www.bbc.co.uk/radio2/realmedia/fmg2.ram](http://www.bbc.co.uk/radio2/realmedia/fmg2.ram)

rtsp://rmlive.bbc.co.uk/bbc-rbs/rmlive/ev7/live24/radio2/live/r2_dsat_g2.ra

as far as i know rhythmbox still does not have good support for rstp streams, so i doubt that it will work for you (at least i couldn't get to work out of rhythmbox)
