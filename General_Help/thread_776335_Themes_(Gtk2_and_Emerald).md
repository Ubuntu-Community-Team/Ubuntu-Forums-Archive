### Post by RSJ on 2008-04-30
Go to system, preferences, appearance, there should be a theme tab. Click install in the bottom right, navigate to your tar.gz file.

### Post by CarpKing on 2008-04-30
The Appearance window refers to GTK2 themes as "Controls" (click "Customize").
