### Post by Exsecrabilus on 2008-05-06
You have to download the [Alternate CD .iso file](http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/download).

Check the box that reads *Check here if you need the alternate desktop CD. This CD does not include the Live CD, instead it uses a text-based installer.* under the **Start Download** button.

After, just pop the disc in and the upgrade will begin.
