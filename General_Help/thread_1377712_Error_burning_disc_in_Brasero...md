### Post by Tamlynmac on 2010-01-10
I had similar problems with Brasero back when switching to 9.04. Perhaps a driver problem with the writer. I installed "K3b" from the Synaptic package manager and the issue was gone. It immediately identified my writer and I  returned to burning.

I believe if you search the help sections, you'll find that a number of users switched to K3b after installing 9.04 due to this issue.

Good Luck & Hope this helps.
