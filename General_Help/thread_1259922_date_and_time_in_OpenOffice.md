In an OpenOffice spreadsheet, column A contains this type of data:

2009 08 29 22 07 40
2009 08 30 09 59 05
2009 08 30 13 04 01

This is date and time data represented by YYYY MM DD hh mm ss
These cells have a "General" format.

In column B, I would like to copy this data and make it look like this:

2009-08-29, 22:07:40
2009-08-30, 09:59:05
2009-08-30, 13:04:01
