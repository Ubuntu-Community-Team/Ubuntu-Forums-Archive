### Post by Rubi1200 on 2024-07-04
Not sure if this will help at all but check if somehow you or others were added to a blocked senders list:
[https://www.spectrum.net/support/internet/blocking-and-unblocking-senders-spectrum-email](https://www.spectrum.net/support/internet/blocking-and-unblocking-senders-spectrum-email)

Also check your account storage limit. Happened to me once with a webmail client that stopped sending or receiving because I had gone over the storage limit.

Try sending a test email to yourself. In other words, log in and compose a test email and send it to your email address. Is it sent/received?
