### Post by blazemore on 2010-05-13
Go to the Synaptic Package Manager and look for the ubuntu-netbook package.
Mark it for installation.
Click Apply, and wait a while for the installation to complete.

Now on the login screen, after you choose the user, there'll be a Sessions option at the bottom. Here you can choose Ubuntu Netbook or Ubuntu Standard. It will remember the settings on a per-user basis, so if you want it permanently you'll only have to choose it once.
