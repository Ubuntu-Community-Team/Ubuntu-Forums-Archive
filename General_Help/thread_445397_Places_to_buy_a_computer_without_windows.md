### Post by irish_flu on 2007-05-16
CyberPower (in California, USA) offers very reasonably-priced desktops and laptops, and I believe that most or all of their computers can be purchased without Windows.

[http://www.cyberpowerpc.com/](http://www.cyberpowerpc.com/)

I've ordered a few machines (probably five or six) from them in the last five years or so for myself and others, and I've never had a bad experience.  The first time I ordered a box from them, they ran out of the video card I wanted.  They called, apologized profusely, and said that "if it was OK" they would put in a much more expensive card (the 128MB model instead of the 64MB model) for the same price, in order to meet the ship date they had promised.  :KS
