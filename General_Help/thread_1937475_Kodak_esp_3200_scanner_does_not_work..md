### Post by timgood on 2012-03-08
Kodak have no support for Linux. You can get some Kodak printers to work using the compiled CUPS driver mentioned above, but as far as I know no-one has yet managed to enable scanner support on Kodak devices. My advice is to move to HP, which will print/fax/scan out of the box with Linux.

Hope this helps.
