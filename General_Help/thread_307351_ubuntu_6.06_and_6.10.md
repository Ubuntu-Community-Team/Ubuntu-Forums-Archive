### Post by ~LoKe on 2006-11-26
6.06 is Dapper, the version with long term support.  6.10 is Edgy, it's the bleeding-edge release and is likely to have the occasional stability problem.  I would suggest sticking with 6.06 on a workstation or anything else important.

One of the key features in 6.10 (in my opinion) is built in AIGLX.
