### Post by tom4everitt on 2009-12-29
I don't know, but the standard thing to check is the output of 

```

sudo tail -f /var/log/messages

```

when it happens. This will show you log messages from the kernel, which might tell you whats happening. 

So do the command, plug in your usb, and wait for it to unmount while looking at the output of tail. 

If you can't make anything out of the output yourself, you can try to post it back here and maybe someone else can.
