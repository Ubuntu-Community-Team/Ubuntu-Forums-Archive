### Post by Bubesi1 on 2011-05-08
OK so here's my work around...from Time & Date Settings I changed the time zone to one 2 hours west which is two hours later than where I am. 

This at least stops the BIOS time being altered and I don't have to correct the time each time I log in. It happens to be Freetown, Sierra Leone that I selected and there being about 4 other Freetowns in the US of A, my location is now reflected as being USA at the log in screen :o

There is definitely something screwy with the time zone settings that are used in Ubuntu 11.04 as this happens on both systems I've installed it on :(
