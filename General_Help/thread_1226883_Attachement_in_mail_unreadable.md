It means that whatever is needed to open the attachment is not available to Evolution.

Save the attachment to your Desktop or wherever you download to and then right click on it and check its **properties** to see what type of file it is.Then use the appropriate application to open it.

### Post by tornadof3 on 2009-07-30
If the file is called "Part1.2"... etc, it may mean the other person has sent you the wrong file. When eg firefox in Windows downloads a file from the internet, it names it .Part etc until the download finishes. Maybe they've sent that to you instead of a complete file?
