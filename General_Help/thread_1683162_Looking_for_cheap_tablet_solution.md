### Post by aeiah on 2011-02-07
you'll be lucky to find anything in that price range that runs ubuntu in a usable form. some android tablets can be flashed with linux, but i think a lot of the ultra-cheap ones cannot (and would perform rather slowly). 

but yes, ubuntu (or debian at least) can run on ARM chipsets. doesnt mean it'll perform well though, and you may find yourself having to take the thing apart, attach jumpers to the serial pins and flash a bare-bones debian via tftp. not something you'd probably want to do, especially without specific instructions guiding you through.

if someone's done it before with a device, then that'd be the way to go (or shell out more money and go with something more netbook-like)
