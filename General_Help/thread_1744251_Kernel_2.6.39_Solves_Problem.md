### Post by CoolJohnB on 2011-04-30
When I upgraded to 11.04 Natty 64Bit it came with kernel 2.6.38 and every time I booted up the screen would flicker constantly and lists would scroll round, also lists behind lists.  The only way out was to continually press the "Esc" key, eventually it would stop and boot normally.  If left alone it would continually flicker and scroll.

I then downloaded and installed Kernel 2.6.39 RC4 and this has solved the problem, not sure what or why it occurred, but at least it has now gone!
