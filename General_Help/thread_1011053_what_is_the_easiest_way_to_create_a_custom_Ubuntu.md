### Post by Izek on 2008-12-14
Right-click the desktop and choose change desktop background to change your background.

You can even change the login window theme by going to System > Administration > Login Window > Local Tab. You can get GDM Themes from gnome-look.org, GDM Themes are themes for your login window.

If you want to make your own stuff, I suggest googling certain things. Though "Making GDM themes" won't give you much.

[HOWTO: Making Metacity Themes](http://developer.gnome.org/doc/tutorials/metacity/metacity-themes.html)
