### Post by coffeecat on 2008-07-17
It's called the upper panel. Right-click on a clear area of the lower panel and choose 'New Panel'. A new but empty panel will appear at top. Now right-click on that to choose which panel apps you want. First choice would indeed be 'Menu Bar' I would think. If you want specific application launchers, the easiest way is to drag and drop them from the main menu (once you've added 'menu bar' :wink: ).
