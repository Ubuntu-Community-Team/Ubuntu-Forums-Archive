### Post by oldefoxx on 2012-01-14
This is a combined Ubuntu & VirtualBox Upgrade issue, but the fix is a simple Ubuntu step.

After a VirtualBox update, the information for the screen height and width seems to go to huge values, so that when you try to open a client under VirtualBox, the client window rushes off to the right as it enlarges.  Then you cannot see it or access it.

Okay, the simple step is to go under System/Preferences/Monitors, make sure that your correct monitor is selected, then Apply the settings.  Keep the new settings, and now the VirtualBox client's window will fit properly in your viewing area.
