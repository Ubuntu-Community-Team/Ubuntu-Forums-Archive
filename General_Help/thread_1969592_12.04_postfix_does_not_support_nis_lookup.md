### Post by bardov on 2012-04-30
Well, my solution was to manually download and install
postfix_2.8.5-2~build1_amd64.deb, which was the 11.10 postfix, and it does support nis.
simply dpkg -i postfix_2.8.5-2~build1_amd64.deb
