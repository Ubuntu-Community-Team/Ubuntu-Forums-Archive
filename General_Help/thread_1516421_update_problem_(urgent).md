### Post by mr clark25 on 2010-06-23
if you have grub2, (ubuntu10.04 comes with it), you can hold the SHIFT key during boot to display the grub menu. you should then be able to re-install the driver and boot into ubuntu (non-safe mode). 

you may also need to boot from an older kernel. there may have been a kernel update that caused your problem.


as for a long term fix, i dont know...
