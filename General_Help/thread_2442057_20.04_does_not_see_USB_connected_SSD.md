### Post by CelticWarrior on 2020-04-29
It shouldn't have changed and if before it didn't need external power supply it won't need now. SSDs generally require less power than HDD so it's not that.

It should be tested elsewhere, another computer, to confirm but there's a possibility the SSD is now broken, the upgrade to 20.04 being a coincidence. Or it lost the partitioning information. Have you checked in Disks?
