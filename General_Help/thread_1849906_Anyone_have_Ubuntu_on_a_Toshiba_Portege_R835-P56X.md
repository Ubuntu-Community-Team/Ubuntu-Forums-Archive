### Post by svast on 2011-10-08
Hi 
I have a Satellite R830 (almost same hardware as you, surprising. It is R830-136 which has an amazing 128GB SSD), wiped out win$ and put Ubuntu 11.04 x64 on it.

### Post by globalsquatter on 2011-11-16
I installed it a couple of day ago ago on my Toshiba Portege R835. I did a dual boot with Windows 7 as well, and as others have shared it was straightforward. The volume control (esc, F6, F7) work. The F3 for hibernating also work. The rest of the hot keys are not responsive. I haven't tried to adjust them. 

The battery drains a bit faster than on Windows 7. I am probably getting about 4 hours as opposed to 6-7 hours on Windows 7. 

Apart from that it runs perfectly.
