### Post by fragos on 2010-05-01
Ctrl-L will bring up the old style location bar and it reverts when you hit return. Also found in the "Go" menu as "Location..."

---

### Post by WorMzy on 2010-05-01
If you'd rather have the text based address bar permanently, you can make that happen by running gconf-editor and going to: apps -> nautilus -> preferences -> always_use_location_entry, and setting the key to true.
