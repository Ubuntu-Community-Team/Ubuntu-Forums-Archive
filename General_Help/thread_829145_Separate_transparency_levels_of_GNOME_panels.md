### Post by DaymItzJack on 2008-06-14
In the general plugin for ccsm, go to the Opacity tab and hit 'New'.

In the new popupbox, hit the '+' and select "Window Title" and then hit the "Grab" button and select the bar you want. This will work if your panels aren't both in the same position (both on top or bottom). "title=Bottom Expanded Edge Panel" is an example.
