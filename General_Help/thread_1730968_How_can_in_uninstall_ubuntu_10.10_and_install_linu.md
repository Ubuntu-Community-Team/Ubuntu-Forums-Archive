### Post by sikander3786 on 2011-04-16
I would recommend UNetbootin. Don't forget to [verify your ISO]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HowToMD5SUM") first.

[http://unetbootin.sourceforge.net](http://unetbootin.sourceforge.net)

[http://ubuntu4beginners.blogspot.com/2011/01/create-ubuntu-live-usb-using-unetbootin.html](http://ubuntu4beginners.blogspot.com/2011/01/create-ubuntu-live-usb-using-unetbootin.html)

For installation, as you don't want to keep Ubuntu you can simply select "Use entire disc" or something similar and it would automatically create necessary partitions and erase everything which is there previously.
