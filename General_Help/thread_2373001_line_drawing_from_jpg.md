### Post by SeijiSensei on 2017-10-01
Imagemagick is a suite of programs run from the command line that can apply all sorts of transformations to images.  See: [http://www.imagemagick.org/Usage/photos/#color-in](http://www.imagemagick.org/Usage/photos/#color-in)

While browsing the Imagemagick site, I came across a reference to a program call mkbitmap.  It looks like your best bet.  [http://potrace.sourceforge.net/mkbitmap.html](http://potrace.sourceforge.net/mkbitmap.html)
