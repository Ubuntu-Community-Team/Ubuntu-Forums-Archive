### Post by apochry on 2012-03-28
Hello,

I don't have any experience with collection on network drive, but all music is on an external hdd, which is often not mounted. That should be quite similar...

I am using Clementine and I am perfectly satisfied with it. It doesn't forget my library when the drive is not mounted, you can browse the folders of your collection directly from the player (in case your mp3s are not organised in a library with matching title, artist, etc. tags, or you just prefer the file browser view). It can also manage my ipod collection, which is great, 'cause I don't need to use the stupid iTunes <Sorry>excuse me for the bad words</Sorry>.  And last but not least, and actually the reason why started using Clementine several year ago, is that I liked the UI from the very beginning.

So if it does the job for you, why not sticking to it? 
Sure thing, you might encounter some small bugs, but that's allover.
