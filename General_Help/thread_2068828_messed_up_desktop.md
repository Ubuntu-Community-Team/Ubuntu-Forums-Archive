> **chevyiron420 said:**
> Im running 10.04 on my laptop and I did something and the tool bar at the top of the screen is gone. How can I get it back?

I assume by "toolbar" you mean the top panel.  Go to the bottom panel and right click and click on **New Panel**.  Then you can move that panel to the top.  The fun part comes when you "add to the panel" all the items that were there.
