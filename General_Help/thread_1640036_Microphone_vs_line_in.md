### Post by QLee on 2010-12-08
[QUOTE=viktorzk]... When I insert a plug in the jack, the built-in microphone stops working.[/QUOTE]

It is common for a jack like that to "break" and disconnect the existing circuit when an external is plugged. You don't want them mismatching impedance.

[QUOTE=viktorzk]...Should I expect any difference between the built-in microphone and an external microphone connected to the input jack? [/QUOTE]

Yes, depends on the quality of each individual one.

[QUOTE=viktorzk]Is there any way to manually switch between the two?[/QUOTE]

Plug and unplug.

Maybe your audio interface and it's driver (module) could switch in software, I haven't seen one that could but I haven't seen everything, I don't even listen to music while I'm working, haven't recorded anything for a couple of years.


[Edit] There is a multimedia engineer who scans these forums, if you keep checking you may get a better answer than mine.
