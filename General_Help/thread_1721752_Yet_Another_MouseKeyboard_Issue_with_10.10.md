### Post by wolfen69 on 2011-04-04
This won't help you right now, but in the future, do all updates and graphics drivers *immediately* after installing and before mucking around in the system. Things tend to go smoother that way. This is just an observation from years of using it. Someone else may disagree.

On a side note, my own personal way of updating ubuntu is to update it for the first 2-3 months, then lock it down and only install security updates of relevance. If things are running well, I don't believe in updating the kernel just for the heck of it. But that's just me, and it has worked well *for me*.
