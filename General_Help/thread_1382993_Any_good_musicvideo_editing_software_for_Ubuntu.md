### Post by tlacuache on 2010-01-16
For Audio:

Ardour - the digital audio workstation (very powerful) - [http://ardour.org](http://ardour.org)
Jokosher - simple multitrack studio - [http://www.jokosher.org/](http://www.jokosher.org/)

For Video:

OpenShot - non-linear video editor for Linux - [http://www.openshotvideo.com/](http://www.openshotvideo.com/)
Kino - DV video capture/editor - [http://www.kinodv.org/](http://www.kinodv.org/)

I believe all of the above are available in repositories (although you might have to add a PPA for openshot).
