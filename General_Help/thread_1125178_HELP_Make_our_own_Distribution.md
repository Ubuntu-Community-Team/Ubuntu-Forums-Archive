### Post by mb_webguy on 2009-04-14
Do you want to make your own *distribution*, with its own repositories, or do you simply want to make a custom variant of Ubuntu (or some other distribution)?  You can do the latter by simply remastering the LiveCD and perhaps offering a few custom deb packages (such as for a custom interface) on a website or Launchpad PPA.
