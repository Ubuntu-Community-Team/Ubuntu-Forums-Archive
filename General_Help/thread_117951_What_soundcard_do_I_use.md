### Post by BathroomNinja on 2006-01-15
click on SYSTEM > PREFERENCES > SOUND

look where it says 'Default Sound Card'.

That should tell you what you need to know.

### Post by learning curve on 2006-01-15
You may have to go into the bios and disable your onboard sound to get everything to work properly.  Even if you choose which one you want to be default some of your applications will still be looking for the other one and you will get no sound.
