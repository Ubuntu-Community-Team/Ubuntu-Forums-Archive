### Post by phillw on 2009-07-19
Hi,

I also have a Gateway laptop (not the same model) - Ubuntu found my resolution okay. But, in trying to help you I had a 'play' ...

System --> Preferences --> Display

Then select "Detect Monitors", hopefully, it will say 14" (mine says 15", but my display is 15")

Then Select the resolution, as you correctly state  1280:800, I have a refresh rate of 60Hz, and rotation of normal.
Then hit 'apply'

If the screen looks 'sort-of' okay, accept it.

What does 'sort-of' mean ? well, after deliberately messing mine up, it was close, but had windows hanging off the edges. I then re-booted & it came back okay, at my desired resolution.

Hope that helps.
