### Post by makariolewis on 2009-09-06
If I understand your question, then yes.

1. Open a nautilus window. (Just open any folder.)
2. Go to Edit > Backgrounds and Emblems...
3. Drag whatever color or pattern you want to the white background of the folder window.

Hope that helps!
