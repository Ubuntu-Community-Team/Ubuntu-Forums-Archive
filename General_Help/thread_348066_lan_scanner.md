### Post by kidders on 2007-01-29
Hi there,

I'm not quite sure what you're asking, but if you'd like a way to check for open ports commonly used in filesharing, try nmap.

Files can be shared in quite a variety of ways (eg Windows networking, AFP, NFS, HTTP, etc.), none of which have very much in common, so finding a list of shares network-wide, let alone a list of shared files would be very much a brute-force operation.
