### Post by 50words on 2007-12-27
I like Evolution. It has not advanced to the point that Outlook is at, but it is a good, solid PIM. There are some nice mini-apps that will pull up just tasks, contacts, and appointments (look under those names) in Synaptic, as well, if you don't want to pull up Evolution.

Another one I like is [Rainlender](http://rainlender.net), which is a nice, lightweight PIM. Rainlender does not do contacts and e-mail, of course. Neither does Sunbird.

Another new and promising PIM is [Chandler](http://chandlerproject.org/wikihome), which promises a GTD-ish and e-mail-centric approach to managing your calendar, to-do list, etc. It's a neat idea, but still in the early stages of development.
