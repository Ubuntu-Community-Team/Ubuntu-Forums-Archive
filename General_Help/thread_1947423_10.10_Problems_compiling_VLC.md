### Post by mc4man on 2012-03-26
There was a period of time where ffmpeg sources that were statically built needed to be patched when used in a 64 bit build of vlc

It would appear that's what you have - it was discussed very early in this thread, post 4 or so
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1398119](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1398119)

It may be possible to produce a decent vlc-2.0.X build in 10.10, you may need to provide some newer libs - there is a very small chance that some of the posted asm patches would work with the ffmpeg source you have, though that ffmpeg may be to old for vlc anyway
(if so x264 needs to be built first with -fPIC, overall this is pretty much ancient history
