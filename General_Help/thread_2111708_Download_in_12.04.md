### Post by prodigy_ on 2013-02-02
If you're using Firefox or Chromium and it opens PDFs instead of downloading them, you can always use **Ctrl+S** to get the **Save As...** dialog.

You can also also make your browser always d/l PDF. In Firefox: Edit > Preferences > Applications tab > Adobe Acrobat Document > Save File. In Chromium: type **chrome://plugins/** in address bar and disable the PDF Viewer plug-in.
