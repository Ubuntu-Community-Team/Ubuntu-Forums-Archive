### Post by sum1nil on 2011-01-19
If you are comfortable with this sort of thing, the setting is in '/boot/grub/grub.cfg' on your 64bit install (at least on my OS)

This file tells you not to edit it but I just tested this and it boots to text w/o grub. At the little menu choose 'continue normal startup'

Change set default="0" to 3 and reboot.

This will avoid the grub menu and do text only.

Change it back to 0 for normal startup.
