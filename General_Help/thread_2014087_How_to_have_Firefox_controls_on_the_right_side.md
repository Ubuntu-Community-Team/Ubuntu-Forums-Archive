### Post by stderr on 2012-07-02
If I'm understanding you correctly, you're referring to the window manager controls on the window titlebar. I'm not aware of an easy way to change their location on a *per application* basis, i.e. just for Firefox but not for other applications. I imagine that would require a code change.

On the other hand, to change this globally for all windows is but a simple tweak in gconf. There's a nice step-by-step guide [here]("http://www.howtogeek.com/howto/13535/move-window-buttons-back-to-the-right-in-ubuntu-10.04/").
