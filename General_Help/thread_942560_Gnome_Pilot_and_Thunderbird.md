### Post by celem on 2008-11-17
Seeing that you've received no response, I'll add what I know. Palm sync is vital to me and I've been working quite a bit on it lately, as I plan to purge Xp from my dual-boot PC.

First, gnome-pilot is a gnome product, as is evolution. Thus, I believe, that gnome-pilot will only work with evolution, not thunderbird. That said, gnome-pilot has bugs with some palms, such as my Clie. In order to sync I had to not use the "usb:" interface and instead load visor and use ttyUSB1. It does sync to evolution then. However, evolution's calendar is really lame in its lack of formatting capability - I must have all seven days present in a monthly printout, starting the week with Sunday. Evolution appears incapable of doing this.

Anyway, jpilot also syncs well with my Clie and it is a clone of the Palm Desktop. Thus, while it isn't integrated with evolution or thunderbird, it is similar to my existing Windows/Palm Desktop environment AND it correctly prints the monthly calendar.
