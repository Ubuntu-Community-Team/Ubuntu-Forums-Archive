### Post by DawieS on 2011-07-18
Try disabling Nautilus > Edit > Preferences > Media > **Browse Media when Inserted**.

(I also once had this behaviour when inserting flash drives, but cannot repeat it now. Maybe the setting is a one-way street?):confused:
