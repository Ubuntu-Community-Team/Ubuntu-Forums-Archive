### Post by k.sangeeth on 2009-09-30
I think I exactly know what you are looking for. Few months back, I myself struggled to get it set on my machine ...  but it's doable.
1. open Compizconfig-settings-manager
2. click on "advanced search"
3. it will open up a new window .. click on "animations"
4. then click on "close animation"
5. in the "Animation selection" frame below is the place where you have to add your customized effect.
6. click on "New"
7. a pop-up will come up asking for details.
8. select any effect of your choice from the drop-down menu
9. in "Window match" field you can give "any" .. if you want the animation to work for all windows
10. "close" the pop-up and check if the effect is working .. it should
