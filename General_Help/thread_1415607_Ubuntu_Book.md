### Post by Scunnered on 2010-02-25
My experience is that with the 6 monthly updates that books are often well behind.  I would suggest that you use the free pocket guide and when in need ask on the forum.

Another possibility would be to wait in the next update which is a LTS version and look for a book specific to it.  As the LTS is supported for years using that and a book specific to it you should have the best of both worlds.

Either way don't be afraid to ask.  The bulk of my knowledge of things have come from this forum
