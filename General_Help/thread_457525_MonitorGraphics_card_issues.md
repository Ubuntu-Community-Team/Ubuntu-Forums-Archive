### Post by MoLE on 2007-06-05
The live CD is unfortunately not without fault.  Have you thought about reporting your bug  on launchpad?

As a workaround, I would suggest downloading the 'alternate' CD - which has the original text-based installer (still very easy to use), and should work faultlessly on almost any video hardware.

I prefer to use the alternate CD, just because I've always used it since warty came out.  You do miss out on some of the newer whizz-bang features, like importing MS Windows accounts and data.
