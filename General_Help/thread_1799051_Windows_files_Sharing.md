### Post by fredrik-fyksen on 2011-07-07
You can right click on a folder, -> sharing, try to share a folder. If you are able to, you have "samba" installed, if you get a download request, download. After a reboot, open a folder. in the adress bar type: smb://IP_or_name_of_windows_pc_here

Edit: if you cant find address bar, press ctrl+l
