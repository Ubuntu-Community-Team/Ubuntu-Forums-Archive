### Post by lykwydchykyn on 2009-01-29
I cannot comment on the mx300, but I got a pixma mp620 for Christmas and it was a major pain to get working.  I had to download a .deb off some taiwanese site, compile CUPS from svn, and do a bunch of hacky things to make it work.  And that was just the printing part.

The mx300 may not be as bad, but personally I'd stay away from Canon.  They do not seem to be a linux-friendly company and will not help you if you want to run their products with Linux.  I would go for an HP instead, they tend to be much better for Linux.

Just a personal opinion, I'm in no way affiliated with HP.
