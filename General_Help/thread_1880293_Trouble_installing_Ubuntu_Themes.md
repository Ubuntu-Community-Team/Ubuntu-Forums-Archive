### Post by Frogs Hair on 2011-11-13
What version of Ubuntu ?  Gnome 2 themes won't work with 11.10 because it is based on Gnome 3 . Emerald / Beryl is no longer supported on Gnome 3 either . The Bisigi project (Gnome 2 compatible ) is dead , but some of the themes may be ported to Gnome 3 in the future by another developer .
