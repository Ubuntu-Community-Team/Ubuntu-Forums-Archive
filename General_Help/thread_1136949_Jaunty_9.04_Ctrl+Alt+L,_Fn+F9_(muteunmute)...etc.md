### Post by Noma on 2009-04-25
Hi, hope this helps!

To ALL having troubles with hot keys on laptops (and desktops as well).

I almost went crazy trying to make my hot keys work. After three installations and reading bug reports I gave up setting Fn+mute/unmute to work, untill I tried to uncheck the "Poll mouse position with Control key" in the mouse settings in gnome. Now everithing works perfectly!!! Try before reporting a bug, this may help you.

My laptop is RoverBook Pro.

Jaunty is GREAT!!! Thanxs to ALL contributed to this marvellous distro!
