### Post by ndw18 on 2012-06-06
The 10.xx or 11.xx refer to the year such as 2010 or 2011 and the xx.04 and xx.10 refer to the month it was released in.

### Post by lukeiamyourfather on 2012-06-06
> **ndw18 said:**
> The 10.xx or 11.xx refer to the year such as 2010 or 2011 and the xx.04 and xx.10 refer to the month it was released in.

Yup. If the release is followed by an LTS that means long-term support, like 10.04 LTS and 12.04 LTS. The latest LTS release is supported for five years on server and desktop, so until April of 2017 which should give plenty of time to upgrade to future LTS releases which have been ever two years. Regular releases have 18 months of support.
