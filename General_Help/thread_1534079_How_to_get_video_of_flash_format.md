### Post by dnguyen1963 on 2010-07-19
> **mycoolgirl said:**
> Now there are a lot of video or music are flash format. I want to download to my computer and can see it when I can not connect to internet. But when I click the right button I did not find the download item. I do not know whether I can download from it? Or how can I save it to my computer?

Very easy...every time you play a youtube or any video, the file is downloaded to your Temp folder.  The key is to play the video for a few seconds...pause the video until the download bar is complete (do not exit the page or the video file will be removed)...then go to your Temp folder and find a file with a name Flash+some random letters...right click the file and copy...paste the file into your desired folder...rename it to yourfile.flv...you're done.:popcorn:
