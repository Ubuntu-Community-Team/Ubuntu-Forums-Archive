### Post by Robbyx on 2010-05-06
Is it possible to prevent the panel from expanding to where the -ox buttons appear on a full sized window. I use the panel hide pref with the panel not fully extended to the width of the scree. ### Post by WorMzy on 2010-05-06
If you right click on the panel, click Properties, and uncheck "expand", that will prevent the panel from taking up the entire width of the workspace.

I think you might be after a dock, rather than a panel. Have a look at Cairo Dock, and see if it fits your purposes better.

### Post by WorMzy on 2010-05-06
Unfortunately the windowlist applet will increase it's own width as more windows are opened. If you want something that works in a similar way, but only uses a small amount of space, have a look at the Window Selector applet.

(Right-click on a panel -> Add to panel -> Drag a Window Selector onto the panel)
