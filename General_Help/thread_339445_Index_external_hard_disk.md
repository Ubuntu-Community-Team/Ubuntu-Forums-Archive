### Post by zanglang on 2007-01-18
You _can_ use beagle-build-index for Beagle to build a static index of your disk on your disk, check its manpage for details: [http://www.die.net/doc/linux/man/man8/beagle-build-index.8.html](http://www.die.net/doc/linux/man/man8/beagle-build-index.8.html)

Although... i'm not quite sure how do you let the beagle daemon automatically load and unload the index when you plug in the hard disk. Should have something to do with tweaking removable drive options and bash programming... Interesting idea, though. :p
