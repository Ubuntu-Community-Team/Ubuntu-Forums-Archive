### Post by sudodus on 2013-10-11
Your computer might have too little horsepower for Ubuntu and its Unity desktop. In that case try a flavour with a lighter desktop environment, Lubuntu with LXDE or Xubuntu with XFCE.

Or the freezing is due to problems with the graphics driver, which might be possible to fix with a proprietary driver.

In any case, please post the specs of your computer, brand name, model, cpu, ram (size), graphics chip/card and wifi chip/card. It makes it easier to give good advice.
