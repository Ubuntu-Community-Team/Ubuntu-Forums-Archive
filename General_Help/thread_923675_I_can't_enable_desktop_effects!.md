### Post by semitone36 on 2008-09-18
The first thing I would try is to go into Synaptic and search for "'make and model of your graphics card' drivers." 

Another solution (if you have a NVIDIA gfx card) is to go here: [http://www.nvidia.com/Download/index.aspx?lang=en-us](http://www.nvidia.com/Download/index.aspx?lang=en-us)
This is where you can download the driver for your NVIDIA card.

As far as I know the other gfx card manufacturers have given up their drivers to the open source community so you should be able to find them in synaptic.  Hope this helps.
