### Post by Colro on 2007-11-04
Applications -> add/remove
Select "show all available applications" in the top right and search for "torrent". Take your pick.

I've personally used both Azureus and Deluge. I'm using Deluge now because Azureus tends to suck alot of system resources. Go ahead and experiment around with the different programs offered, though, you might find another that you like :)
