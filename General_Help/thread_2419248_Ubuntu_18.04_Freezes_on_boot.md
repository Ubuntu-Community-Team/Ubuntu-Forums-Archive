### Post by oldfred on 2019-06-19
When you say you power down with switch, that is the icon in the upper right corner. 

You  never power down with actual power switch as that may cause file corruption and then system will want to do a fsck or need other repairs. 
And then it boots to recovery mode, so you can run fsck or repairs.
