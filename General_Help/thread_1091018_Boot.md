### Post by stanz on 2009-03-09
still have your m$ disk?
boot with the m$ installation CD. when prompted, press **R** 

 If you have only one operating system on that drive, enter 1 to select it.
  Enter your administrator password if prompted.
 to fix the **M**aster**B**oot**R**ecord, use the following command: **fix mbr**
type **y** and press ENTER to fix the mbr.
 type **exit** to leave the recovery console and reboot.


search these forums for infomation concerning your type of installation. you will find that other users worked out problems, and your experiance will be less troublesome.
