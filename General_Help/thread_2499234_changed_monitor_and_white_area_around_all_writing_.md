### Post by Impavidus on 2024-07-20
I don't see anything special on your screenshots. Just black or coloured texts on a white or grey background (as expected). Under magnification I can see some colours along the edges of the letters, but that's the ordinary sub-pixel anti-aliasing. Which suggests that the image generated on your graphics hardware is fine and the problem appears further downstream.

If you take a picture of your sceen with a camera and magnify the result, what do you see? And maybe there's a problem with your sub-pixel anti-aliasing. It may not be properly tuned for your new monitor. Some software uses that technology, other software doesn't, so that may be the cause of your issue.
