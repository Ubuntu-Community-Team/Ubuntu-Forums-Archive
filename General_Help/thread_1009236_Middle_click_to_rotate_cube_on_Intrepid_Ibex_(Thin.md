### Post by gettinoriginal on 2008-12-12
It's all according what you are trying to do, if you only want to rotate to another desktop, then install Simple CompizConfig Settings Manager, go to edges and set up which side/top/bottom/corner you want to control it.  Then when you move your curser to that area, it automatically flips.  For instance, I have mine set to bottom right corner flips right, bottom left flips left, and upper right corner initiates "window picker".
