### Post by grahammechanical on 2014-02-18
What you are describing could happen if we have two or more windows of the same application open at the same time and in the same workspace. For example, 4 Libreoffice writer documents open and they are stacked up behind one another. If we then click the Libreoffice icon in the Launcher the workspace will shrink and spread the windows allowing us to switch focus from the top window to another window.

Holding Alt and pressing the Dead Grave key above the Tab key will do it. As well as holding Alt and pressing Tab. Perhaps you have sticky keys.
