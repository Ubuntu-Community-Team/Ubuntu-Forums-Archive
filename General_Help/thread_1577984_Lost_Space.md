### Post by drs305 on 2010-09-19
You can take a look at this post, which discusses both GUI and command line methods to inspect your system to find out what's taking up the space.

The most common problems are backups which end up on the system partition instead of the backup partition, unemptied trash bins (yours and root's) and large log files.

[HOWTO: Recover Lost Disk Space ]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1122670")

I second Ubuntu-Tweak as a great app.
