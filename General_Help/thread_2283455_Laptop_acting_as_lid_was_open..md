### Post by Bucky Ball on 2015-06-22

Nothing to add that might help, but I will say that to keep the screen on, the machine would need to be on, so if you close the laptop and the screen remains on, so does the machine. Not good. Can cause overheating and damage the innards of your computer. Switching off sensors so the machine thinks the laptop lid is not a good idea. That will mean the machine will consider itself always on I would think. Fine until you close the lid for 24 hours. 

Good luck.
