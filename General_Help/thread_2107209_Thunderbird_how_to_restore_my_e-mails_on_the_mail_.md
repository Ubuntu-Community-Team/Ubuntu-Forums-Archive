### Post by sudodus on 2013-01-21
Find a mail service with IMAP. Then you can connect to it with Thunderbird and simply copy the mails from the local folder to the IMAP folder, which actually resides on the mail server.

I did it for a friend maybe one year ago, so I know that it works. (But I don't remember all the details.)
