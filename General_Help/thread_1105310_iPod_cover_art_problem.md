### Post by mb_webguy on 2009-03-24
One easy solution would be to use [Album Cover Art Downloader]("http://www.unrealvoodoo.org/hiteck/projects/albumart/") to add the album covers directly to the mp3.  That way you don't rely on your media player to correctly transfer separate cover art.

Just go to the website and click the Debian Linux Download button to download and install the deb package.  Then use the command "albumart-qt" in the terminal or Alt+F2 Run dialog to start the program.  Select your music directory and click the download button, and the program will download and add cover art to your mp3s.
