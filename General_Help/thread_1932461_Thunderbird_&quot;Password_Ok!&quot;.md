### Post by Paddy Landau on 2012-02-28
As long as "Autodetect" is set, the settings are not final and so Thunderbird cannot continue.

Your incoming authentication is set to "Autodetect". Change it to the required setting, *Re-test* if necessary, and *Create Account*.
