### Post by donkyhotay on 2009-03-03
In my experience all hardware will work with ubuntu, the question is always the *difficulty* in getting it to work. Beyond that I've never used most of those particular components so I don't know what problems you may encounter. Your best option is to use the liveCD or do a wubi installation and see what does/doesn't work. Be aware the liveCD will only show you what works 'out of the box' while the wubi install allows you to do more configuration.
