### Post by jim_deadlock on 2012-01-30
@felixcat there is a solution, you just have to adjust your Unity plugin. Run (install if necessary) ccsm and go to Ubuntu Unity Plugin and on the Behaviour tab at the top it says Reveal Mode, if you click the first button (the large one which probably says None at the moment) you'll get a tool where you can set the Edge Reveal Mode. Click the red areas that you want to activate (in your case bottom). Now when you move your mouse to that edge of the screen your launcher will appear.
