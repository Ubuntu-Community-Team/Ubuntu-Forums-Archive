### Post by matt_symes on 2011-05-02
Hi

> Anyhow i managed to mount the usb on my installed account and i saved my important files.

I am glad about that. 

The first thing i would do is run fsck from the LiveCD just to check the integrity your file structure.

From the LiveCD, make sure the hard drive partition is not mounted and type

```
sudo fsck /dev/sdXY
```

where sdXY is the partition containing your Ubuntu distribution. You can find this out from

```
sudo fdisk -l
```

Small L above. 

*IMPORTANT. Make sure the partition is not mounted. *

If that does not return any errors and you still have problems, i would the reset your gnome settings after booting into your real distribution on your hard drive.

```
gconftool-2 --recursive-unset /
```

Kind regards
