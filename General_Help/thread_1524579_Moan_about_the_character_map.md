### Post by The Cog on 2010-07-05
I don't understand your problem. I just opened the character map and searched for the word "pound". This turns up two results, and I guess the one you want is **U+00A3 POUND SIGN** rather than **U+FFE1 FULLWIDTH POUND SIGN**. A search for "ampersand" finds maybe half-a-dozen different ones, and there are four different "dollar" signs. 

To find "sterling", I had to tick "search in character details" which also found some chinese-looking glyphs.

Actually, I think the character map is brilliant. I suspect you just didn't realise you could search for words like "pound".
