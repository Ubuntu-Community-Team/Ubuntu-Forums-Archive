As far as i know, no such software exists... The closest one is still under development [http://gnupdf.org/](http://gnupdf.org/)

But what do you want to do with a PDF file...?

There are tools that can handle many kinds of tasks.

(1) pdftk [http://www.accesspdf.com/pdftk/](http://www.accesspdf.com/pdftk/)

(2) pdfjam [http://freshmeat.net/projects/pdfjam/](http://freshmeat.net/projects/pdfjam/)

Both are in ubuntu repos


If you want annotation and the kind take a look at 

pdfxchangeviewer [http://www.docu-track.com/product/pdf-xchange-viewer](http://www.docu-track.com/product/pdf-xchange-viewer)

This is a WINDOWS program, but free and works fine with WINE. It has been discussed a number of times in the forums. Search for the posts.
