Just to clarify: Originally I checked the "Always do this" check box, and clicked the "ignore" button on the little dialog box that popped up whenever I plugged in the camera.

Then the computer did just that. It COMPLETELY ignored the camera when I plugged it in (i.e. it would no longer automount. Seems to me that dialog box should have a "Mount but don't Import" button on it...
