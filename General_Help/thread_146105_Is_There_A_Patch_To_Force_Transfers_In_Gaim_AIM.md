### Post by Tipo on 2006-03-17
I believe the reason that Gaim isnt too good with transfers is because it doesn't have access to the official AIM transfer servers (i.e. AOL blocked third party clients).  I believe that the Gaim guys came up with a solution for this in the next major release by having Gaim set up a proxy server or something.

In the meantime until Gaim 2.0.0 comes out, the best thing you can do is to make sure ports 5190-5200 are open on your firewall (those are the ports that AIM uses)
