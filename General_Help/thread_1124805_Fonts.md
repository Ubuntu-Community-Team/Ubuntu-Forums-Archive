### Post by aaronp on 2009-04-13
In my previous install of Ubuntu before I recently upgraded my PC and started fresh I installed a pack of fonts that had about 1900 different fonts in it.
It appeared to slow down GIMP when it was trying to load fonts but other applications didn't seem affected at all - they just loaded instantly like normal.

The problem I did find is that with so many fonts on my system it was a lot of overhead just trawling through them trying to find the right one when I needed one.

(The other issue with the specific font pack that I installed was that about 1880 of them were rubbish and I would never use them anyway.)

So, after all that, my answer is that other than GIMP I don't believe a lot of fonts slows down Ubuntu. However, I only have my own experience to back that up.
