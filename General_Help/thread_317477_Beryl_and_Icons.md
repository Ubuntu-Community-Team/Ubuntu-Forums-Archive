### Post by ayoli on 2006-12-12
go to System>Preferences>Themes as pricechild said, then clik 'edit theme' or  'theme settings' button (i dont know the exact label in english), then pick icons tab and choose your favorite icon theme.
you can install more themes in your home folder in a hidden dir named .icons (create it if it doesnt exist, dont forget the leading '.')
