### Post by Impavidus on 2022-02-24
.deb files are the same as those used by the official Ubuntu repositories; it's just that your .deb file doesn't come from a repository. No need to compile anything; just a few clicks or a simple command to install it. You get a standard warning, because this .deb comes from your web browser, not from a trusted source, and could contain malware that may harm your system. It's your decision whether you trust the source.
