### Post by Spaceman9 on 2008-07-02
In Sound Preferences make sure Sound Events, Music and Movies are set to Autodetect. In Audio Conferencing Sound playback should be set to Autodetect and Sound capture set to ALSA unless a different setting works better on your hardware.

Are you sure you installed everything you need for Audacity?
