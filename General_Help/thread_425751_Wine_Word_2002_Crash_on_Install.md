### Post by jkzubu on 2007-04-28
I presume you are referencing 'Word' to be MS Word.  It is my understanding that MS Office and its components are not compatible with Wine and will not run.  Word (and other office components) run very well in Code Weavers Crossover Office. This is part of what CXO was designed for.

I would recommend that you set the preferences in Open Office to open and save files in the MS formats that you need and see if that is suitable.  Otherwise CXO is pretty nice and worth the investment of $35.
