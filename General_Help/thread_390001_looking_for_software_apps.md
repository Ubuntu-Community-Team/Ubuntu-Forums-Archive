### Post by g8m on 2007-03-22
Klibido (kde) works good for downloading binaries. The beta version of pan (0.125) does a great job too, although I had some problems with downloading incompletes. At the moment I mostly use klibido, and sometimes newsbin pro 4 (wine). Par2 is natiive under linux, although you have to get used to the command line interface. Rar is reasonable integrated with the gnome/kde archiving tools.

And for burning, there is nero for linux, but i recommend k3b.
