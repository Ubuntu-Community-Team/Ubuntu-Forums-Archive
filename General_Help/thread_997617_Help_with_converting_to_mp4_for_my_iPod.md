### Post by /////// on 2008-11-30
You can also use ffmpeg or mencoder to convert it. A good guide on building ffmpeg is here [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=786095&highlight=libx264](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=786095&highlight=libx264) , after building it all you have to do is run ```
ffmpeg -i *input file* -sameq -vcodec mpeg4 -acodec libfaac -ac 2 -mbd 2 -flags +4mv+trell -aic 2 -cmp 2 -subcmp 2 -s 320x180 *output file*
```
