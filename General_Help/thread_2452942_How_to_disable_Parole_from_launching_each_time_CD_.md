### Post by Ralph L on 2020-10-30
I am running Xubuntu 18.04 LTS.  Every time I put a CD in the CD drive, Parole launches.  I would like to disable this.  I goolged it but found nothing.  Does anybody know how to disable this annoying Parole launch with each CD insertion???????????

Thanks ahead of time for any help.

---

### Post by Ralph L on 2020-10-30
I found it.  Go to Whiskers Menu>Settings>Removable Drives and Media>Multimedia and uncheck "Play audio CDs when inserted".
