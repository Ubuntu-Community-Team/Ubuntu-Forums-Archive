### Post by hhh on 2010-11-12
I don't think there is a quick way, you can drag and drop items one by one. Right-click your main menu and choose "Edit menus", then click the "Other" category in the left pane. Now you can drag items from the right pane onto a new subject header in the left pane. The problem is that the "new" subject will steal focus from the "Other" menu, so you have to click back to it and proceed.
