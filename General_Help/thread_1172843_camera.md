### Post by lindsay7 on 2009-05-29
If your web cam is going to work "out of the box" it will work with these programs. Here is the discription of cheese. You can get it from the add/remove menu under applications.

Cheese Webcam Booth
Cheese is a cheesy program to take pictures and videos from your web cam.  It also provides some graphical effects in order to please the users play instinct  



Skype as you know is a program to voice and video chat and also phone call.  You should be able to get this from the Synaptic menu under System/Administration or from there web site.

If these work then that means your web cam is good to go if not, then I would start another post asking for help getting your web cam to work. Give all the details of your computer and web cam.
