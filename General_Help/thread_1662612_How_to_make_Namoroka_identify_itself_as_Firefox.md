### Post by Verbeck on 2011-01-08
try installing the addon 'manually'
```
wget https://addons.mozilla.org/firefox/downloads/latest/59/addon-59-latest.xpi?src=addondetail
```
and open the downloaded file  'user_agent_switcher-0.7.3-fx+sm.xpi' in your home folder with firefox(namaroka)
