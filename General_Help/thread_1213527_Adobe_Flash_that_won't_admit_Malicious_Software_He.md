### Post by tommcd on 2009-07-17
> **cooper77z said:**
> I was concerned about the supercookies that adobe flash apparently uses.
When a flash video is playing, right-click on the video and choose "settings". Then click the little folder icon at the bottom of the settings window. Move the slider all the way to the left; and check the box for "never ask again". This will prevent flash from storing any cookies or info about you.
This will also cause flash to forget any settings you may have set for flash videos or games on sites you visit. It will preserve your privacy though, as far as I know.
