### Post by mrfuzzemz on 2006-11-15
Hi there,
   I have the ATI Mobility Radeon X1600 and from what I understand if you have one of the newer ATI cards then you will have to wait until ATI releases a driver that supports AIGLX.  Older cards supported by the open source driver work great with AIGLX, but for now we will have to wait until either ATI releases a driver or until their is open source support for the newer cards.
   There has been rumor that AMD will open source the ATI driver, but we'll have to see what happens. 

You'll have to stick with XGL for now if you want a 3D desktop.
