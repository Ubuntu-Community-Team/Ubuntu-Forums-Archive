### Post by Giblet5 on 2009-09-30
Having done a lot of speech-rec work for phone systems, I can tell you that your results will be directly proportional to the time and effort you put into selecting a good headset microphone and quieting-down your workspace.

Most gross errors in speech rec are the result of noisy space and/or cheap microphones.

A telephone is a very VERY bad microphone, BTW...
