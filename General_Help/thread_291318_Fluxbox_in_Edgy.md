### Post by paul6 on 2006-11-02
I think this will work:
Right click -> Fluxbox Menu -> Configure -> Tab Options -> Tabs in Titlebar.

I tried completely disabling them in the init file by changing "session.tabs" to false, but that doesn't seem to work (at least not in Fluxbox 1.0 RC2).
