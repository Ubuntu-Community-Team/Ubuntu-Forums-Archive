### Post by WIGGMPk on 2009-07-21
If your referring to the Blackberry Desktop Manager, than your answer is No.. Apparently to RIM its hell no..

You can try with WINE, but the last time I checked it doesnt function correctly. RIM is releasing a Mac OSx version and some good might be able to come from that, but not any time soon.

You can install BDM through a Virtual Machine guest OS, but upgrading the Operating System on your Blackberry will most likely fail. However backing up contacts and settings works fine.

I have not tried any "Linux Native" solutions yet. These are my experiences based off of:

Ubuntu 8.10/9.04
Blackberry Storm 9530
Virtualbox 3.0 (Windows XP Pro SP3 Guest)
