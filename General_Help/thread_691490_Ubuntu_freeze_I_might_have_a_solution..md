Hi!

I just want to spread my solution to the notorious (?) Ubuntu freezes I've noticed others have here on the forum. In my case it was with high probability Compiz that caused it. I disabled it by going to: *System->Preferences->Appearance->Visual Effects* and clicking on *None.* This did of course disable all those nice desktop effects, but I don't have to suffer through a 5 second freeze every other minute.

I hope I help someone with this.
