### Post by ajgreeny on 2011-07-10
Possibly a corrupt profile.  Try renaming the ~/.mozilla/firefox folder in your home as a backup and see if that works.  You will then need to restore all the bookmarks and add-ons, but it is probably best to download those again to make sure they are all good add-ons.  Your bookmarks are in the bookmarksbackup folder in your old profile as .json files.
