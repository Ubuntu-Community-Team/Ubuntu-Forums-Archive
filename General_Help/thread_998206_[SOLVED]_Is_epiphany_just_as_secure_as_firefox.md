### Post by x1a4 on 2008-11-30
It's at least as secure as Firefox but, just like Firefox, it could be more so.  If you want a secure browser you may want to try [GNU IceCat]("http://www.gnu.org/software/gnuzilla/").  GNU IceCat is Firefox without any of the non-free stuff with additional security.  Another secure browser is [SeaMonkey]("http://www.seamonkey-project.org/") which is the next evolution of Netscape.
