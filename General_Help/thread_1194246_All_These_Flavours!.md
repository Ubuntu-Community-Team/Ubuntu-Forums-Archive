### Post by Therion on 2009-06-22
> **Spaceman7o said:**
> Thanks very helpful but are these called flavours or am i imagining things?
*Properly* speaking they're called "distributions" or "distros" for short.  We call them "flavors" around here in s good-natured, conversational, sort of way since those three are all based on Ubuntu but are variations thereof.

Flavor = Distro.
