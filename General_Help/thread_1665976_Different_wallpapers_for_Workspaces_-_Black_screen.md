Hey guys, I was wondering if i could have different wallpapers on my workspaces. So, i searched the net. I found out we could do it using Compiz. Ticking the "wallpaper" under the "utility" and adding wallpapers under it. And the second step was to uncheck "show dekstop" in Apps>nautilus>prefrences (gconf-editor). Sadly, it doesn't work for me. It just gives black screen on all of my workspaces.
