### Post by howefield on 2012-11-28
Press the Super (Windows) key to open the Dash and start typing the name of the application that you want to find. Once you launch the application, you can lock the icon to the launcher if required.

Holding down the SUper key will reveal a list of shortcuts that might help in navigating around.

### Post by howefield on 2012-11-30
> **Bill Z said:**
> When I find my utility, how do I add it to the Icons on the left side of the screen?

Right click on the icon that appears in the launcher once your application is running and select Lock to Launcher. Or search for the app in the Dash, and drag the icon to the launcher.

> Can I put several icons into one icon, like Writer, Calc and empress into one I create called O_office?

Not sure that you can do that.
