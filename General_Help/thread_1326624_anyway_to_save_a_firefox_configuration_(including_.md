Note the "." before mozilla - it's a hidden directory.

### Post by Zoot7 on 2009-11-14
Ah good to hear! :)

One thing that might be of interest to you in the future is to use a separate partition for home. The advantage of doing this is that you can install a new version of Ubuntu (or even another distro) and keep all your documents and settings (like firefox configs etc.).
I've had a separate home partition since Hardy. There's a good guide here:
[http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/separatehome](http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/separatehome)
