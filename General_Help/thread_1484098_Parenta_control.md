### Post by Drop D on 2010-05-15
Not to my knowledge, but there are administrative settings that let your kids do/not do certain things like change passwords, delete files, create files, or download files.  

You can find these settings under System > Administration > Users and Groups.  Then just manually select what options you want/do not want.
