I have a question about the Evolution mail reply.  Is there a way when you reply to someone for it not to quote what the other person wrote?

### Post by Het Irv on 2008-08-05
Hit reply, then before typing anything press "Ctrl+A" and then "Delete".  That will delete the quoted part and give you a new blank e-mail.
