### Post by SeijiSensei on 2020-08-20
/etc/fstab has an entirely different syntax from the mount commands used at the prompt.  fstab is not a script; it's a list of filesystems to be mounted.

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Fstab](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Fstab)
