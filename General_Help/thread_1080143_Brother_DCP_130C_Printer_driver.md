### Post by plucky on 2009-02-25
Open the Synaptic Package Manager and search for DCP-130C and it should display the Cupswrapper and LPR  driver packages.Select both and apply.
Your drivers should be installed.


Good Luck

And look at the [url=
http://solutions.brother.com/linux/en_us/index.html]Brother Website[/url] to download the driver for the scanner,if it has a scanner.
