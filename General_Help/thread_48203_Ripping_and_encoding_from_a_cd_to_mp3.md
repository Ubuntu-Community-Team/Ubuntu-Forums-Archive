### Post by t2kburl on 2005-07-11
I use grip

sudo apt-get install grip

please see the [www.ubuntuguide.org](www.ubuntuguide.org) to enable mp3 support (install Multimedia Codecs)

once you have these things accomplished, open grip click on the config tab then click the Encoder tab in the secondary menu that opened when you clicked config.

Use the combobox to select lame as your encoder
 Now click Rip on the primary menu (assuming you have your CD in the drive and the desired tracks are selected under the Tracks tab)
click the Rip+Encode button

wait a few minutes


enjoy your mp3s  :)
