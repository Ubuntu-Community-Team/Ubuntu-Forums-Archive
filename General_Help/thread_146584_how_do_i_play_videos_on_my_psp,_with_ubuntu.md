Yep, but it's not perfect yet. There's a program that's really promising, called Vive, which encodes to the right kind of MP4 for the iPod and PSP. I use the current version, but the most agrivating thing is that there's no progress bar (which would matter for longer movies) and there's no error when things fail. If it won't encode something, it just sits there and smiles at you, leaving you to look at your hard drive light and processor to see if it's actually doing something or not. Sometimes i wonder.

I bet with the next couple releases that'll be fixed.

Try it out for fun if you're interested.

Here's a URL: [http://vive.sourceforge.net/](http://vive.sourceforge.net/)
