### Post by ubiwankanubi on 2007-04-29
Do you mean that the "headless" computer is one without a monitor/keyboard/mouse? Are you connected to a network or do you want to do remote backups? If you're on a network, you can share the folders and you can use a different network computer to burn the dvd or cd. if you need to backup something on that computer from a remote site, you might try enabling FTP or some kind of content management system. If you're trying to burn the dvd from the computer that is without a monitor, but which you can access via a computer on your network, I think it is more feasable to share the folder over the network and burn from a different computer...

you can also look into webmin. and also checkout freenx or tightvnc if you need gui...
