### Post by QIII on 2017-09-29
Hello!

Do you have a backup of your system?

No, Yahoo can't do that force you to do that.  They might give you a warning if they have been compromised.  That happened at the end of 2016. Someone using a social engineering attack might also send you a bogus warning to trick you.  Your Yahoo account may have already been compromized.

If you know how to create a new Firefox (or other browser) profile, I'd start there.  (If you don't, we'll help.)  If the issue persists, then the compromize is deeper.  Likely at Yahoo, or you visited a site compromized and planted with a "drive-by" script.
