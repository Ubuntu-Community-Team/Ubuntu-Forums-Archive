### Post by XBMC old School on 2013-07-06
Hey all, this was a quick find on google. I will paste the answer and link.> Hello, Recently I have attached my pc using HDMI to my 46" Panasonic Viera tv. I have an NVIDIA 1gb video card and  I noticed that when the resolution is set to 1920x1080, the edges are cut off on the screen, especially when im using a browser.   After playing with the resolution, I figured out that you simply have to go to your "Menu" then "Picture" settings on your tv and "Advanced Settings".  Then at bottom of this menu, select "HD Size" and set it to 'Size 2'
    This fixed the edge issue and now I can browse and enjoy![http://www.tomshardware.com/forum/332423-33-panasonic](http://www.tomshardware.com/forum/332423-33-panasonic)
