### Post by hoosemon61 on 2014-02-22
So, I'm both a little baffled and embarrassed.

Turns out the batteries in my Logitech wireless mouse were at about 80% and that was enough to cause this strange operation.

I had tried a wired mouse, but with the wireless mouse still plugged in, so for some reason, it behaved the same as the wireless mouse.

I tried the wired mouse alone and it was fine.

I changed the batteries in the wireless mouse and it's now fine.

I still find it baffling that the left click would work for some functions, but not others.

But - I guess I don't care if it's fixed.
