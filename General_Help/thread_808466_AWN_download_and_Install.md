### Post by LeoSolaris on 2008-05-26
You have to have some sort of net connection to download anything.

Look around the forums for a how to on making a dvd copy of the repo. It will take a while do get everything and to burn the DVD's, but then you will be able to add and remove programs from the DVD's in the same way you can from the repos (although they will not update the way the repos do, obviously)

Another way would be downloading the .deb by itself from sites like [www.getdeb.net]("http://www.getdeb.net/app.php?name=Avant+Window+Navigator"), put it on a CD or a usb stick, then just click on it in the system you want to install it in. Just be aware that the version of awn in getdeb is for 7.10, not 8.04, and it may not work. 

Hope that helps atleast a little.
