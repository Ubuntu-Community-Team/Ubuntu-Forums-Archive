### Post by ptn107 on 2008-04-02
In Evolution...

1) Edit -> Preferences
2) Select the appropriate account in the Mail Accounts window.
3) Click Edit
4) Click the 'Receiving Options' tab
5) Click 'Leave messages on server' option
