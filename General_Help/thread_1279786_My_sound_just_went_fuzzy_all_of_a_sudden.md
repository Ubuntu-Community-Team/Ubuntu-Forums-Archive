### Post by Giblet5 on 2009-10-01
I came here all prepared to suggest Q-Tips® but the snark isn't with me today.

Try opening your "Volume Control" by right-clicking the speaker icon.

Start a music file playing, and make sure your external speakers' volume control is around 1/3 from dead silence.

Lower the PCM volume.

Is it clearer? Increase the Master to compensate.

It could also be a blown speaker, a blown amplifier output transistor, or ... the Q-Tip thing. ;)
