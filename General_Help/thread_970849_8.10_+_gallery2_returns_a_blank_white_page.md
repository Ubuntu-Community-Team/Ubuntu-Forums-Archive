### Post by ben@kietzman.org on 2008-11-14
Okay, I just figured it out.  Change the /usr/share/gallery2/lib/smarty symbolic link to point to /usr/share/php/smarty instead of /usr/share/php/smarty/libs.

Issue the following commands to fix it:

sudo rm /usr/share/gallery2/lib/smarty
sudo ln -s /usr/share/php/smarty /usr/share/gallery2/lib/smarty
