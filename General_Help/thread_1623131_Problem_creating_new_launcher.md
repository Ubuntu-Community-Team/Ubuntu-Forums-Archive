### Post by ajgreeny on 2010-11-16
What are you entering in the launcher boxes?  If you put the application you wish to use (firefox?) followed by the URL, or even just the URL it should work as far as I can see.

I am just about to try it myself, just to find out if it works.

EDIT:
Yes, just the URL of my googlemail in the command box opened firefox and everything was fine from there.  The launcher, however, must be for a location, not an application or you will get an error.

If you make a launcher for an application, rather than a location, the entry needs to be **firefox <URL>**.
