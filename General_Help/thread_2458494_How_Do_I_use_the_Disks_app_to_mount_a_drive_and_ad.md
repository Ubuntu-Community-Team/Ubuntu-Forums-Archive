### Post by tea for one on 2021-02-25
Open Disks > Select the Drive > Select the Partition > Additional Partition Options (gearwheel icon) > Edit Mount Options

[https://www.ubuntubuzz.com/2019/08/quick-guide-to-awesome-gnome-disk-utility.html](https://www.ubuntubuzz.com/2019/08/quick-guide-to-awesome-gnome-disk-utility.html)
