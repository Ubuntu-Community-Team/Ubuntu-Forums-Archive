### Post by sanderd17 on 2010-06-14
If you ask why there exists gnome and kde? Well, Linux is all about choice. There are more than 300 well known distributions, some distro's come in gnome, kde, xfce, lxde or another flavor, some have more flavors.

If you are new and have to choose, I would pick gnome. I don't say Gnome is better than KDE but Ubuntu is the most used and therefore the best supported. Kubuntu is less used and I have the feeling it's more buggy.

BTW: Gnome has eye candy: search for compiz on youtube.
