### Post by niteshifter on 2008-06-11
Okaaaay.....

How long has this been happening? And if you answer "I don't know, my screen clock doesn't have any 7's ...."

Ok, serious now. It's a strange problem, but I've seen similar. Real Q&A time:

Have you upgraded distributions? Like from an earlier version to 8.04? What version are you using?

Installed any non-repository software lately?

You say periodically. How often? Is this event regular in timing or random? Seriously, how long has this been happening and can you place any kind of event that occurred before it started - and I mean any event: computer knocked over, electrical storm. software action, etc. Can you ascribe any event at the time it is happening? **

Do the keyboard and mouse use separate connection points to the system or does the mouse plug into the keyboard?

What kind of computer? Manufacturer, model and some system specs, please.


** I'm not jerking you around here, it can be very weird world we live in: Years ago, I had a system that suddenly developed a crazy mouse - just at random times, with nothing touching the mouse, the pointer would flit all about the screen and right-click and left-click itself. Changed mouse; no joy. Tore down the box, reseating connections: no joy. Disconnected mouse so as to be able to use a reliable system. Noticed on my way to work a couple of days after psycho mouse showed up a nice, new shiny radio tower (CB radio) down the street from me: dug an old reciever out of my junk box and ... yep my neighbor, running illegal power and a badly tuned transmitter. A bit of counter-EMI work on my end reduced it from psycho-mouse to somewhat nervous mouse, livable with. A hellacious wind storm a few months later finally solved the problem.
I'm not saying external EMI (interference) is your problem. Could be anything. These questions and your answers give us a direction to take.
