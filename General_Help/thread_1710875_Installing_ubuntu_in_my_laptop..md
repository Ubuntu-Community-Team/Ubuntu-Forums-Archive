### Post by Quackers on 2011-03-20
Welcome to UF :-)
If your laptop has only 3 primary partitions (or less) and some unallocated space can be created (probably by resizing a Windows partition), then yes. Any pc can support more than one operating system. But it's a good idea to read up about the pitfalls of partitioning hard drives first. 
It is a good idea to make a set (or two) of recovery dvd's and a Windows repair cd. It is also good to have separate copies of anything you wouldn't want to lose!
Partitioning carries risks.
