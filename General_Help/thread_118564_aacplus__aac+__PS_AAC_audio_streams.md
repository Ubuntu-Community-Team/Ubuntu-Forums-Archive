### Post by stream303 on 2006-01-19
I've had very good luck with the **gstreamer0.8-faad** decoder and Rhythmbox for AAC audio streams.  To get it, you'll have to enable your Universe/Multiverse repositories when using the Synaptic Package Manager.

I know what you mean - I was amazed at how good the quality was with low-bandwidth streams compared to mp3.
