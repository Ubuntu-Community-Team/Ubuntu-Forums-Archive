### Post by seawolf167 on 2011-02-27
Not all programs work fully or even at all in WINE. The best resource is to see if anyone has submitted comments or stats at WineDB for [your game.]("http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=application&iId=9687")

I also recommend [PlayOnLinux]("http://www.playonlinux.com/en/") for games that receive less than a "gold" WineHQ rating.

Sorry its not any type of fix, but for an older game not designed with *nix in mind, its hit or miss at best.
