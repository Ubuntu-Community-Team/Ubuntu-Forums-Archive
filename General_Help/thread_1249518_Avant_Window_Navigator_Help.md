### Post by nothingspecial on 2009-08-27
I`ve not used awn for a long time but if my memory servers me correctly .........

Create a folder in your home directory, I think I called mine launchers but it doesn`t matter call it Suzanne if you like.

Go through your menus and right click on any icon you want on the dock. Choose add to desktop (or whatever it says).

Drag each icon from your desktop into your new launchers/Suzanne folder.

From there drag each icon onto the awn dock (the icons will stay in the folder). Now they should be there every time you log in.

After you`ve done all that install gnome-do and realise that docks, panels and launchers are a waste of time and space and that the keyboard is faster than the mouse. ;)
