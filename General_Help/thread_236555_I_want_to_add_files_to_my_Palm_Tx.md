### Post by rioghal on 2006-12-11
I have installed lots of files to my Palm T|X, without syncing, and here's how I did it.

FIRST METHOD:

1) email the files to yourself - Use your desktop PC to email the files (as attachments) you want on your Palm T|X, then email them to yourself. You might want to email only a couple files per email because too many email attachments gets truncated in Versamail.

2) Open up a wifi connection and set up Versa mail for your email account (I used gmail).

3) Check your email for the files you emailed yourself and download them all and the attachments.

4) In Versamail, click on each attachment and the device will install them automatically - providing the files are in a format the device can recognize.

I did this with 15 Palm programs and they all installed and work great.

SECOND METHOD:

I have a friend who copied Palm apps to an SD card from her desktop PC, slid the SD card into her Palm T|X, clicked on the files and they ran/installed. I haven't tried this but I saw her do it and it worked fine.
