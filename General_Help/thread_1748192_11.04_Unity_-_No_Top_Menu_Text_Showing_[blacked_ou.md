### Post by stevedude on 2011-05-03
As you mentioned, changing the theme could be the root of some of these issues. I noticed this happening to me after I changed the Compiz Settings. If you haven't already, install the "Advanced Desktop Effect Settings (ccsm)" manager program, and play around with the Open GL settings.

Mine would disappear but come back after a log off/ log in.

Global menu may have to be reinstalled too if the video settings are not the real issue.

I noticed last night that a Global Menu update came through, so try doing an update first before making other changes. Good luck!
