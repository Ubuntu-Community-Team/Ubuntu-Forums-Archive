### Post by Cope57 on 2009-07-06
Sounds like what you need is a embedded version of Linux installed and not a regular operating system. Unlike desktop and server versions of Linux, embedded versions of Linux are designed for devices with relatively limited resources, such as cell phones and set-top boxes.

You could use [Ubuntu Netbook Remix]("http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/download-netbook"), it could be what you are looking for.

If you are still wanting the user to be even more limited to what they can do, you could [build your own distribution from scratch]("http://www.ibm.com/developerworks/edu/l-dw-linux-embedded-distro-i.html").
