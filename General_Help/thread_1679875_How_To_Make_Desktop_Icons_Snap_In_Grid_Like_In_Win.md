### Post by Dr_Frost on 2012-07-22
Copy paste or download the "small slab", go to System -> Preferences -> Appearance, select 'Background', select 'Add', find and select the "small slab" you downloaded from my post, In the 'Style' select 'Tile'.

Now this will not arrange your desktop icons for you but will give you a grid to move your icons to, this way you can arrange your icons.

--

Note: the slab will need to be changed to comform to unity or other overlay desktops, this is mostly used for Gnome 2 or MATE, also i only have a panel in the bottom of the screen.
