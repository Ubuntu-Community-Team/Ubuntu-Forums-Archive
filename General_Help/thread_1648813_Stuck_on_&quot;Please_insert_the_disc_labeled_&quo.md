### Post by pfnorris on 2010-12-19
It sounds like the second disc is not mounting when it is inserted, so the install application cannot reach it. A quick workaround for this might be to launch Nautilus after you have inserted disc 2. You should see the second disc appear in the left hand tree column in Nautilus. Just click on it there, and it should be mounted (you can then see the contents in the right hand pane). Just go back yo your installer and click OK, and this time it should proceed.
