### Post by blackened on 2009-03-15
From the way you describe it, you could be talking about one of three or so things.

Right-click an empty spot on the panel, select "Add to Panel". You might be missing the Window List applet, the Window Selector applet, or the Notification Area applet. Try each in turn to find the one you've lost. The ones you don't want can be removed by right-clicking on the applet and "Remove From Panel".
