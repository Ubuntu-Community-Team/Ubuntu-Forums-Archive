### Post by Mark Phelps on 2010-03-22
Fan speed is set automatically.  If the fans now are running MUCH faster than then did before, this indicates that the CPU is running MUCH hotter than it did before.  Slowing down the fans will only burn out your CPU.

Better to find out WHY the CPU is running much hotter first.

Could be that you clogged the heatsink fins when you blew air into the machine.  Could be that you broke the seal between the CPU and the heatsink.

But you need to check this out before you start trying to lower the fan speeds.
