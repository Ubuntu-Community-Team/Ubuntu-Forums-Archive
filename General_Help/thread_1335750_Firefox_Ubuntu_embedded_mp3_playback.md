This is just a follow up of  [_a this link about playing sounds in wordrefenrece.com site_]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1280102")_ _[U]

[/U]I had the same issue and I noticed I have mozilla-plugin-vlc installed
I removed mozilla-plugin-vlc and installed mozilla-mplayer instead and now I can play sounds in wordreference with one little problem, every time I click in the pronunciation link I can hear the sound is played twice. 

I don't know why it is played twice but as they are just single words I prefer this to no sound at all.

Give it a go and see if that fixes your problems, additionally make sure you don't have more than one FF plugin managing the same media, like vlc and mplayer for mp3, as this could probably cause problems.

As a side note I have a combination of vanilla mplayer+vdpau and default mozilla-mplayer from jalope.
