### Post by t0p on 2010-06-22
I currently use Eeebuntu 3 (based on Ubuntu 9.04) on my lowly EeePC 701; the best thing I ever did was ti upgrade from 512MB to 2GB RAM.  Some might say that's overkill - and for sure I rarely get anywhere *near* using most of that RAM - but that RAM upgrade has made everything much snappier, and RAM is so cheap nowadays there's little reason *not* to upgrade.

So stick a bunch more RAM in your netbook.  You won't regret it!
