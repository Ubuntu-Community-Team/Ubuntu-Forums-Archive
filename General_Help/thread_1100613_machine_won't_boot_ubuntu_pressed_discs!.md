### Post by john183 on 2009-03-19
i have to agree with Yashiro, SP3 won't make any changes to your hardware. especially before windows has booted. the only advice i can give you is to try using the boot menu (what key to press to bring it up varies by machine, but most will show what it is during the POST process at boot) instead of just letting the computer decide whether or not to boot the cd. I cannot promise that that will help, the computer might still overlook the CD rom, but it's worth a shot.
