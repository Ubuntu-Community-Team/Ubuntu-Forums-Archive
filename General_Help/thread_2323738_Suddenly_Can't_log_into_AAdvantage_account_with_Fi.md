I suddenly can't log into my American Airlines Advantage frequent flier account from Linux.  The login page just comes up blank.  I can log into any other web site with no problem. I suspect that I might have a Preference set wrong or that I have an incompatible add-on or plugin. ### Post by TheFu on 2016-05-07
This is a common issue for complex websites.  Clear all cookies and the FF cache. Restart firefox.
I like to use chromium for really complex websites that I don't have any choice, but to use. I do NOT use chromium on a daily basis.

Another option is to create another userid and use the FF under that userid. Bet it works fine.

### Post by Ralph L on 2016-05-19
Thanks TheFu.  I cleared the American Advantage cookie (didn't want to clear all) like you said to do, and the problem went away.  Thanks again.
