### Post by ajgreeny on 2016-03-24
Having looked at the pdf I suspect even the best Windows OCR application might have problems with it as the text is not particularly clear even when zoomed in to considerable high resolution.

I occasionally use tesseract with some success but the scans have to be of very high quality to get any success, and I am sure you will get only gobbledegook from that in Ubuntu on that pdf file.

I see there is a package called pdfgrep but I have no experience of it; maybe worth trying, but don't expect too much.
