### Post by DandyDon on 2008-05-04

Did you tinker with your Fonts under System-Preferences-Appearance-Fonts?.
The Office loaded with 8.04 isn't the full package. It may be using Unbuntu's fonts.
What I did was install the full package, that's what fixed mine. I first removed what was installed.
Applications-Add/ Remove-Office-Installed Applications-Open Office-Remove
uncheck the boxes and remove what you have.

Then Reinstall everything for Open Office.
Under Office select All Applications, then check all Open Office components you need, especially the main one.
