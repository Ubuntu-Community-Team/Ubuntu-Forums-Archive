### Post by fluffman86 on 2009-10-04
Just downloaded the karmic ISO to test it, even though I upgraded to it months ago.

A few things:
Yes, Karmic will (and already does) have Wubi on the CD.
Yes, you will be able to have it shipped to you free.
But, if you try to order on release day, you may not even get through on the website, and it will take a few weeks to process, even before it ships, because of the high demand.  

Also, if you wait until release day to download, it will take a long time for it to be mirrored properly, and the servers will be really slow.

Personally, I would recommend that you go ahead and use the beta.  Like I said, I've been running it for months now, and while the alphas had some major problems, the beta is REALLY stable.  Just a few minor UI problems left to deal with before release. :)

You can download the beta here:
[http://www.ubuntu.com/testing](http://www.ubuntu.com/testing)
And here's the instructions for burning it to CD to test, install, or install with Wubi:
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BurningIsoHowto](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BurningIsoHowto)
