### Post by carranty on 2012-05-03
> **clonezonedude said:**
> ...... I have been looking to install a virtual pc to play games that wine can't support (like simcity 4 and the upcoming new simcity)

I'm wondering which one you think is the best virtual pc, and how do I set it up please?.

As people have already stated virtualbox and VMware player are your two best bets, whichever one works best for you.  However virtual machines are not very good if your motivation is to play games.  Firstly they run quite slowly  -- since they're running within an existing OS -- but more importantly 3D acceleration often doesn't work well.  

Of course there's no harm in trying it, and if it works great, but I'd recommend dual booting windows and ubuntu if games are what you have in mind.
