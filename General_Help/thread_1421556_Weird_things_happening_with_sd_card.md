### Post by joeknoodle on 2010-03-04
> **Romulus I V said:**
> When I remove music from my sd card, I looked at properties, and it said that all the data (1.8 gb) is still on in, and I can't get it off. Please help me!
Do:

Places > Computer

Right Click on SD Card > Unmount 

It should then ask if you want to empty trash.

Or

Within the SD Card folder, you can empty the trash there. You may have to enable hidden folders by Control+H to find it.
