### Post by Ramses de Norre on 2006-02-23
Right click on the panel and choose properties. Then the tab Background, set solid color and transparency at full.
To get such transparent windows you need to choose a theme that has that. I'm using Grand Canyon and I have a transparant window list. Just search untill you find a nice one for you.
