### Post by mondoUNC on 2008-07-27
If you mean what i think you mean...

System > Pref > AWN Manager

Then Under the APPLETS enable the stacks applet.

You might have to close and restart the dock to see the applet.

Once its their (the little basket icon, with a down arrow) Right click and select preferences.

Under the "Stack Layout" tab, in the dropdown under "Layout" select curved gui.

You can adjust a few other cool settings in that preference window. I like to make it a Composite applet icon under the Applet layout tab.

Just in case you don't have any applets in the AWN manager you might need to uninstall from Synaptic and reinstall following [this guide]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=762363").
