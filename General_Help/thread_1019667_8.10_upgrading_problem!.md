### Post by taurus on 2008-12-23
Press <Ctrl><Alt>F2 and log in with your username and password.  Then, kill that PID with

```
sudo kill -9 13157
```
Then, press <Alt>F7 to get back to your GUI and see if the upgrade has finished.
