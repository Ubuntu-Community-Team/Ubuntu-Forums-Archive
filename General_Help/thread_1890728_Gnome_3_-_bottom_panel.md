### Post by vanlong441 on 2011-12-04
every gnome-shell user must know this already [http://www.webupd8.org/2011/10/things-to-tweak-after-installing-ubuntu.html](http://www.webupd8.org/2011/10/things-to-tweak-after-installing-ubuntu.html)

instead of alt tab, I suggest open Activities menu or press Alt F1 or press the Super (windows) key or move your mouse to the top left corner.
