### Post by phidia on 2007-08-04
The bad news is that your printer is unsupported in linux-that's according to one of the best sources on printer compatibility [here]("http://openprinting.org/show_printer.cgi?recnum=Lexmark-P4350").
There is a non-free program called turboprint but since I don't use it I can't say how well it might work in your situation. 
Sometimes the best thing to do is research a new printers compatibility. Some hardware suppliers (and lexmark seems to be one of those) often aren't interested in supporting linux or releasing driver info to the developers.

Added: I checked the [turboprint site]("http://www.turboprint.de/english.html") and unfortunately they don't mention lexmark printers either-sorry.
