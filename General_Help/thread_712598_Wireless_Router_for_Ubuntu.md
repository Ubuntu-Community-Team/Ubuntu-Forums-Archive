### Post by ugm6hr on 2008-03-15
> **chewit said:**

Indeed.

Routers are generally independent of OS.

Just remember that the "instructions" and "installation CD" may be for Windows, but it will still work just fine with Linux.

You just need the admin page IP address (usually [http://192.168.1.1/](http://192.168.1.1/) or [http://192.168.0.1/](http://192.168.0.1/))
