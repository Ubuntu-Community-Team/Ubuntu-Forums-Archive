I read your posting in Forums, so wanted to respond to you here so you know the "community" is here.  First of all, welcome to Ubuntu  :P  
As to your icon, right click on it, choose properties, then left click on the icon image (upper left corner), and once it opens up, choose the icon image you want, or add your own.  
Also, if your postings do not get a response within 24 hours, you can bump it to bring it back to the top of the list.  There are many of us who peruse the "unanswered posts" daily to try to help, so be patient an someone will usually get to you.
