I haven't tried the sleep function on my 11.10 install. Only upgraded it today.

However, that sounds broken to me. In the shutdown menu, there are two options. I think one is called "suspend" and the other "hibernate", but I don't really know. Mine are "Bereitschaft" and "Ruhezustand" because my computer speaks German for the sake of my girlfriend.

Any rate, one of them writes the contents of the RAM into the swap space on the hard drive. Is your swap as big as your RAM? I'm guessing, but maybe if it isn't, this might have something to do with it.

Just for the sake of clarity: I tried both options on this machine which is running 10.04 before posting. One runs the machine down and leaves the power light blinking. The other runs it down such that it looks like it is switched off. Both come back with Firefox open as it was before switching.
