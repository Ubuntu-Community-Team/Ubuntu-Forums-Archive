### Post by tehwa on 2005-09-25
Excessive hiss can also be caused by an inbalance between your volume controls. Before you change configurations, browse through your Applications menu to Applications > Sound and Video > Volume Control, and try turning your PCM volume down and your Master volume up (or vice-versa) and see if it improves.

If that doesn't work, take a look at [http://www.ubuntuguide.org/#configuresoundproperly](http://www.ubuntuguide.org/#configuresoundproperly).
