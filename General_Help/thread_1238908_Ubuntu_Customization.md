### Post by Katalog on 2009-08-12
As far as visual effects go, are you sure you have Compiz installed? I would assume so since if you didn't the effects options would all be greyed out, I believe. If yo want to get busy customizing, head over to [http://www.gnome-look.org](http://www.gnome-look.org). They have hundreds of themes for GNOME over there. Just download the theme and go to "Preferenes>Appearance" and drag the theme file into the Appearance Manager window and you'll have a new look to choose from. Once you get into the Appearance Manager you can change everything from your window decorations to your mouse cursor. Now, when it comes to Compiz and Emerald themes, you'll have to get advice from someone else as I am running Ubuntu Netbook Remix and don't use any desktop effects, so I don't really have any experience with that type of thing.

And welcome to Ubuntu!
