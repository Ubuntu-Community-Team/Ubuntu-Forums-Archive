### Post by ad_267 on 2008-10-27
Why don't you post it over here: [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=934741](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=934741)

That's also a good place to get some inspiration. The Human theme in Intrepid is quite nice. The new Dust theme available in the community-themes package is also very nice and has a unique Ubuntu look.

If you're wanting to stay with Hardy you can get the Dust theme from here: [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Artwork/Incoming/DustTheme](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Artwork/Incoming/DustTheme)
You also need the Murrine SVN version which is available from that site.
