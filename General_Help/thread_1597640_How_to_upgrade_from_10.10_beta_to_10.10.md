### Post by -Ziggy- on 2010-10-15
How can I upgrade from my current 10.10 beta to 10.10? I want to upgrade it without loosing any files. What option should I choose in the installation process?

It would really help me if someone could explain it in an easy non-technical language. Thanks.

---

### Post by cpmman on 2010-10-15
You do not need to re-install.

Goto System - Administration - Synaptic Package Manager - Mark All Upgrades - Apply 

When it finishes downloading and installing you will be on 10.10
