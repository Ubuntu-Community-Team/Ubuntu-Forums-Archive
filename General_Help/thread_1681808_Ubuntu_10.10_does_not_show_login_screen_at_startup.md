### Post by eddiesaliba on 2011-02-10
Good afternoon,

After much research and help from friends and others, I discovered that there is a palliative solution for the problem described above. 

By connecting the laptop (on battery only), as was said before, Linux starts to load but  the login screen doesn't appears. 

If you are experiencing the same problem, then follow the steps below: 

- When the screen goes dark, instead of the login screen, then press "Ctrl + Alt + F1". You will see the login screen in text mode. 

- Once you see the login screen in text mode, then press "Ctrl + Alt + F7". Then, the login screen in graphics mode will appear. 

Well, this solution does not solve the problem, but it give to user, conditions to enter and use the operating system.
