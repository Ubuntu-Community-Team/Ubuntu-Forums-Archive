### Post by egalvan on 2010-04-15
Me Myself and I...

(  feel free to disagree with us :)  )

Lucid Lynx 10.04 LTS is a Long Term Support, with the emphasis on Safety and Stability over-riding newness.

My "toy and play" machines (running various Ubuntu's, 8.x,9.x) will likely get fresh installs, mainly because I want to play with the latest-and-greatest, and that way I'll have a better idea of where any problems may be hiding...
(or I may "up-grade in place", and wait until a problem annoys me enough to "steam-clean" the install :) )


But I will wait until 10.04.1 comes out (six months later) to be doing a fresh install on my work machines (running fully patched 8.04.4) in order to insure that as many of the niggling bits have been eradicated, and that NOTHING remains of any old cruft.
My work machine all have the data totally separate from the OS, so this is easy.
