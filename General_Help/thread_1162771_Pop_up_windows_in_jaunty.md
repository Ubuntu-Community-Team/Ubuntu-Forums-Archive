like when I go to shut down, a window pops up and says "Shutting down in 60 seconds [shut down] [cancel]"

---

### Post by Bindsa on 2009-05-18
Right click on the shutdown button in the corner, preferences, and then uncheck: "Show confirm dialogs for logout, restart and shutdown" 
This will disable the popups.
