### Post by HermanAB on 2009-04-07
It depends on whether you like playing with Lego.  If so, then anyone of the above is great and will keep you happily amused for years.  

However, if you just want a mail server that can do everything and 'Just Works' (TM), so you can get on with your life and do more important things, then you have to look at Zimbra and Citadel.  

Citadel especially is famous for its Easy Install script which can have a complete groupware system with more features than you can shake a stick at up and running in about 20 minutes flat.  It can handle tens of thousands of users and requires zero maintenance.  I had it running on an Eee PC 701 as a demo, so it is very efficient.  A single Citadel server can literally replace dozens of Exchange servers.
