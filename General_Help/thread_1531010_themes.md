### Post by daemonkat on 2010-07-14
There's also a host of various themes available on DeviantArt. Depending on what window decorator you use there may be some additional settings there. Emerald, Metacity, and GTK2 have some decent content. [http://browse.deviantart.com/customization/skins/linuxutil/](http://browse.deviantart.com/customization/skins/linuxutil/)

Compiz Fusion may be a utility you may want to investigate as well if you like to use the more advanced settings or if you want an easier way to switch the window decorator. It should be available in Synaptic.

[http://linux-users.deviantart.com/](http://linux-users.deviantart.com/) may have some other interface-centric content for you as well.

Happy Hunting~
