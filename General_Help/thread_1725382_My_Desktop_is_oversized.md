### Post by seawolf167 on 2011-04-09
Two things to check (it's probably #2 below):


[LIST=1]
[*]System -> Preferences -> Monitors, make sure you have a "legal" resolution set for your new monitor
[*]Check you monitors settings menu (the buttons on the monitor), there should either be an autoconfig setting, or a couple of settings where you can change the display area of your monitor (resize, move, etc.)
[/LIST]
