### Post by Mark Phelps on 2013-06-07
Are you reposting this here because you didn't get an answer on askubuntu in three hours?

What COULD be happening is that even though your USB slots are supposed to be downward compatible, they are not because the USB Legacy setting in your BIOS is disabled.  You will have to boot into your BIOS settings (the documentation that came with your PC will say how to do this), look through the USB settings, and see if there is one for Legacy -- and ensure that it is enabled.
