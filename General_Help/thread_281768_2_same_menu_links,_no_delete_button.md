### Post by CatKiller on 2006-10-22
> **Epperly said:**

There's a file in your Home folder called ~/.config/menus/applications.menu that might include that category. Or it could be in /etc/xdg/menus/applications.menu, or it could just be in /usr/share/gnome/apps. Or somewhere else I haven't thought of. Sorry. You could look at whichever package installed it for some indication of where it puts its menu entries.
