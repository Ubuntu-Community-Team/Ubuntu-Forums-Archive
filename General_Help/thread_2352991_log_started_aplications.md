### Post by SeijiSensei on 2017-02-17
You can see when a program started with "ps auxw" if it's less than a day; otherwise it reports something like "Feb16".  I don't know of anything that automatically logs the start time of every process to a file.  *nix systems run lots and lots of programs even over the course of a single day.
