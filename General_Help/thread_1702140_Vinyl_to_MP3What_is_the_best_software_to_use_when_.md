bhkino,

Audacity will do an excellent job for you. I just ran across this site: [http://nostarch.com/audacity.htm](http://nostarch.com/audacity.htm) where you can get a free .pdf download of Chapter 3 of the book "The Book of Audacity" which serendipitously happens to be titled: "Transferring Vinyl LPs (And Other Legacy Media) To CD". Hope that helps.
