I turned off dri (which is Okay for me because I don't really need 3d support. You might not be so happy with the outcome).

I added:        
Option          "no_dri" "yes"

In my device section of /etc/X11/xorg.conf

Restarted X (ctrl-alt-backspace)

Now my screensaver works just fine - err, non 3d screen savers work just fine.

Maybe this helps you.
