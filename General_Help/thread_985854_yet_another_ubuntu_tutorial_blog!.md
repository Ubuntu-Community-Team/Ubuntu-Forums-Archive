### Post by bscbrit on 2008-11-18
There is nothing wrong with what you propose - although as you say it is not a new idea - but if you search these forums you will find the answers to the 2 proposals that you yourself made.

I would suggest 2 things:

The success of the blog will depend partly on the quality of the content.  Therefore, before you release your blog to the world you have to be sure that it is good enough to win readers.  Get some experience by writing several howtos beforehand.  If they are original, place them on this forum. Let others try them out and, if they are not perfect, let them suggest changes.  You will quickly learn what others want to read.

Find an original topic for your howto.  You already know that your proposals are not new.  Why, then, should anyone read your blog in preference to any other?  You need to identify original content.  This is not easy because, in addition to finding an original topic, you also have to become expert enough to write the howto explaining in easy terms the skills that you have mastered.

Good Luck.
