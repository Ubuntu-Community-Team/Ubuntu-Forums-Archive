### Post by varunendra on 2011-07-31
Assuming it has default Gnome interface, **right click on the desktop > change desktop background > goto 'visual effects' tab > choose 'extra'**

Your graphic adapter must have 3d hardware acceleration enabled (would be already enabled if your current graphics driver properly supports the adapter).
