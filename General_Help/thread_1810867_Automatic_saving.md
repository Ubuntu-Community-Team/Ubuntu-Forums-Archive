### Post by nosehat on 2011-07-23
> **JC Cheloven said:**
> [This]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BackupYourSystem/SimpleBackupSuite")?

That probably won't work well for this case, which involves open files.  

Often your software will have a built-in feature for periodically saving work in progress.  In Open Office, for example, this is called "AutoRecovery" and it's default setting is to save your open work every 15 minutes.

Perhaps your video/photo editing software has a similar feature?
