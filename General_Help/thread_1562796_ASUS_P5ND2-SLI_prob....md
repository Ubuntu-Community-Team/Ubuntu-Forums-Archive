### Post by lmarmisa on 2010-08-28
I suppose that you have not modified any parameters of your BIOS recently. Check it your bios menu has an option similar to Load default parameters or something like that. I would recommend you to select this option in order to correct a possible misconfiguration of the parameters of your BIOS. For example, a low CMOS battery could be the origin of this error on the configuration. 

I recommend also to boot your PC from an Ubuntu Live CD. Check if the system is able to boot from this alternative device.
