### Post by grahammechanical on 2022-02-03
You can do this in  Firefox. Click the icon of three horizontal lines that is at the top right of the page and scroll down to Settings. Click on Privacy & Security and scroll down to the setting called Cookies and Site Data and tick the box with the label Delete cookies and site data when Firefox is closed.

From that same drop down menu you can chose Zoom and from there you can increase the size of the text that Firefox displays.

### Post by Holger_Gehrke on 2022-02-03
Firefox -> Settings -> Privacy & Security -> Cookies and Website Data (about a third down the page) -> Checkbox "Delete Cookies when closing Firefox"
There's even a button for exceptions from this deletion to keep cookies from specific sites, for example cookies that store your settings for a site.
