### Post by Tamlynmac on 2009-08-21
Have you right clicked on the icons and attempted to move and separate them? Everything in the panels is movable and you place them in a position that won't overlap. I also noticed that you've move a number of icons from your lower panel to you upper, which is probably why they may be overlapping.

If you right click on any icon and (assuming it's not locked to the panel - if so unlock it) you can select "Move" from the menu and place it anywhere in the panel. You may wish to consider how you want your panel to look prior to moving the icon around.

Also by right clicking on any blank area in the panel and selecting "Add to Panel" you can add the applets you wish to add to any panel. This would eliminate the need for dragging the applet icon from the lower panel to the upper panel which may be causing the over lay. It also appears you have duplicate icons "Applications" in your top panel. To delete duplicates simply right click on the icon and select "Remove From Panel".

If this is the problem, you may wish to look at the "Help and Support" documentations located in your "Systems" menu. It will open a window on your desktop and from the topics on the left select "Customize Your Computer", then select "1. Customizing Panels". This should help you to navigate the panels and customize them appropriately.

Good Luck and I hope this helps.
