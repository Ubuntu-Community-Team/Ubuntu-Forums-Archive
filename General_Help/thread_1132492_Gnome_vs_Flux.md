yes, but you got take into account there's nothing in flux, gnome has all kinds of things to help you along, flux you have to add programs to do the same things.

you might want desktop icons(idesk,rox-filer,pcmanfm)
you could want a better panel(fbpanel,lxpanel,bmpanel,...)
etc....
