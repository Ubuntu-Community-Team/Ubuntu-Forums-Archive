### Post by Spaceman9 on 2008-10-25
Do you meant there's no beep from the computer speaker? Have you tried running the live cd?

When your computer locks up try pressing Ctrl+Alt+Delete first. That should bring up the shutdown menu. If that doesn't work try pressing Ctrl+Alt+F2. That should let you logout so you can reboot without any problems.

### Post by ryanhaigh on 2008-10-26
The fact that you are not getting a system beep at all is a little worrying. All the PCs I have owned beeped at you if they could not detect a video card. I would try removing the graphics card and see if the computer beeps indicating an error. If it doesn't there may be another issue such as bad memory, PSU or even a failure on the motherboard itself.
