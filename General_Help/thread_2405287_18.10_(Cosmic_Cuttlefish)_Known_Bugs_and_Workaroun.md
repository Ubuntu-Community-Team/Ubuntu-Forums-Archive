### Post by Frogs Hair on 2018-11-07
Alsa reload was required after an upgrade due to losing sound. This occurred after an unknown package was updated.  

[https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+s...o/+bug/1793640]("https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/pulseaudio/+bug/1793640")


```
B Enhanced Host Controller #2 (rev 04)
00:1b.0 Audio device: Intel Corporation 6 Series/C200 Series Chipset Family High Definition Audio Controller (rev 04)

```


```
 sudo alsa force-reload
```
