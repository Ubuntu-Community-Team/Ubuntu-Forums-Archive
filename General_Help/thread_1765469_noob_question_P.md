### Post by Cope57 on 2011-05-23
Right click into the workspaces and select "Preferences" from the menu.  You will then be able to change the number of workspaces to your liking, up to 36.

Then open it.

There are to settings one is horizontal and one is vertical, you set the number of work spaces with those 2 settings.
