### Post by arizonagroovejet on 2008-05-14
So you have a panel at the bottom of the screen and another one at the top, right?

It sounds like you're right clicking on the panel at the bottom, slecting 'Configure Panel..' and expecting the config changes to affect the other panel too. That's not how it works. You want to configure the panel at the top of the screen then right click on the panel at the top of the screen and select 'Configure Panel...'
