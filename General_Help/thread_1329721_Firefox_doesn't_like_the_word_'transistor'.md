### Post by oddeyed on 2009-11-17
Woop woop! I figured it out.

Turns out, to make a cool transparent terminal I changed some compiz window rules for anything with 'trans' (i.e. transparent) in the window title. Guess what other word has trans in it?

So as soon as I created it, it disappeared below my other windows, disappeared from my taskbar and alt-tab, and lost its window decorations. Stupid me. Didn't even crash at all!
