### Post by simptechmike on 2011-03-08
> **Spyderkid said:**
> Or how can I create a drawer/Menu object?


For the menu object, look at my tutorial here:
[http://www.associatedcontent.com/article/1802582/windows_style_menu_in_ubuntu.html?cat=59](http://www.associatedcontent.com/article/1802582/windows_style_menu_in_ubuntu.html?cat=59)

For the drawer, right click the icons and choose properties. In the center is the icon. Click it to choose another icon. It appears to default to /usr/share/icons, but you can navigate to wherever you have an icon saved.

Hope this helps!
