### Post by cody1233 on 2013-04-12
Good to have you back :D

It's looking like the OS isn't finding your hard drive...load a CD of the ubuntu installer or (what I prefer) get a copy of puppy linux and see if either of those can find your HD...if neither can, check your HD to make sure it's plugged in tightly.  If it is and you still can't see it, your HD might be trashed.  If you can see it, try booting again.  Post back with your results!
