### Post by terrrorr on 2010-02-14
Make sure you do not have any USB memory-sticks or hardrives attached when you are trying to boot it up. If not, then your GRUB tries to boot it from wrong partition (it cannot find it).

You could try to configure your GRUB settings pressing e when you see the  GRUB menu.

I hope this helps
