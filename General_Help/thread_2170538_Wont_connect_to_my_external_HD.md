### Post by sudodus on 2013-08-27
If you describe your hardware and software in a detailed way, it will be easier to help.

- Computer brand name, model, cpu, ram (size) and graphics card
- USB HDD brand name and model, file system(s)

Have you tried the USB drive in another computer, and another USB drive in this computer (for example a pendrive)?
