> Can I make Evolution pull up email address when typing in the "to:" box??  I set up auto-complete, but still nothing.  any ideas?

Perhaps it is looking at an empty address book ? (eg, if all your contacts are in "personal" and it is looking at "Ubuntu One" address book)

Try going to Contacts and right click on "Personal" address book, then properties, and check "Mark as default folder".
