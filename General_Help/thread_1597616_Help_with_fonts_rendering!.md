### Post by dtfinch on 2010-10-15
On mine I switched to the Liberation fonts both in Preferences->Appearances->Fonts and in Firefox's Preferences->Content->Fonts&Colors->Advanced. The Liberation fonts try to match the sizes of the Arial and Times New Roman fonts on Windows, and are thinner than the default fonts on Ubuntu. And I set font hinting to either medium or full instead of the default slight.
