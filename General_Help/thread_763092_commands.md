### Post by mgmiller on 2008-04-22
Welcome to Ubuntu.  It is handy to become familiar with some  terminal commands.  It's probably not necessary for you to learn all, or even most of them.

There are many resources on the internet that list linux commands.  Here is one of them:
[http://www.oreillynet.com/linux/cmd/]("http://www.oreillynet.com/linux/cmd/")

Try googling linux commands.

You can also enter the word "man" without the quotes in a terminal, followed by the command you are interested in to get more info about it.  For example:
```
man ls
```
will give you info about the ls command.

To get commands to run that require root permissions, put a sudo in front of them.  Be careful with that though, as you can now do some real damage to your system.

Have fun and try not to break too many things.
