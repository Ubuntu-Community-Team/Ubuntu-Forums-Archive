### Post by HermanAB on 2010-09-12
Howdy,

You can click-drag-drop mail around on an IMAP server.  If it is POP, then you cannot.

Google has IMAP available, so you could open an account on Gmail, then highlight the folder in Tbird in one account and drop it on the other account.
