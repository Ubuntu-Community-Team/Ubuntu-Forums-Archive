### Post by strabes on 2006-12-08
if beryl-manager is running there should be a red gem icon in your notification area on your panel that you can click on and select Beryl Settings Manager.

You can run beryl-settings to open that window up directly. Just browse through the keyboard tabs of all the plugins to find out or change all the keyboard shortcuts.
