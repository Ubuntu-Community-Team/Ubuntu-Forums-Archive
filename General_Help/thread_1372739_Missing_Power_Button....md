### Post by jamesisin on 2010-01-04
Those panels are modifiable by the user.  It's possible you deleted the widget from the panel.  You can add it back to the panel by clicking on an empty space on the panel and choosing Add to Panel.  The widget you want is called User Switcher (I think).  There are several widgets in there.  Have fun.

### Post by Miljet on 2010-01-04
Or possibly you are looking for the "Log Out" widget. 

Anyway, when you get it added, it probably won't be where you want it. You might have to move several others to get it where you want. You can move widgets by right clicking, uncheck "Lock to panel", and drag to where you want. Don't forget to lock them back to panel when finished.
