### Post by EYEdROP on 2007-11-17
I just installed ubuntu 7.10 last night. I must say it is fantastic!  
 
I installed an app called gDesklets, which basically has little widgets on my desktop. I have them set on the left hand side of my desktop. But whenever I create a directory on a desktop, the icons are hidden behind the widgets. Is there a way to make desktop icons always on the right hand side instead of left?

---

### Post by Aquaman420 on 2007-11-17
Right click on the desktop.  uncheck keep aligned.  Drag your icons to the right side of desktop.  Then click keep aligned again.  and they should align themselves on the right side.  Best of luck.
