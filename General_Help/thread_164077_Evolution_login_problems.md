### Post by elamericano on 2006-04-22
I use it daily, and I've never had flaky password behavior. You might try to save the password, so that once you get it right, it may continue to get it right from then on.

Edit->Preferences->Mail Accounts->Edit->Receiving Email and Sending Email tabs.
