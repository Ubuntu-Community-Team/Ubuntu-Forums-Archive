### Post by mb_webguy on 2009-04-03
Canonical also provides Launchpad, a hosting service for officially recognized unofficial mini-repositories (called Personal Package Archives).  Some PPAs are maintained by software development projects, such as [OpenOffice]("https://launchpad.net/~openoffice-pkgs/+archive/ppa") or [Deluge]("https://launchpad.net/~deluge-team/+archive/ppa"), while others are maintained by individuals.  While packages in PPAs aren't tested by the Ubuntu team, they're generally reliable.

Quite a bit of software can be found on Launchpad, including [Handbrake]("https://launchpad.net/~handbrake-ubuntu/+archive/ppa"), [KDE4]("https://launchpad.net/~kubuntu-members-kde4/+archive/ppa") (which includes Amarok 2), and [VLC]("https://launchpad.net/~c-korn/+archive/ppa").
