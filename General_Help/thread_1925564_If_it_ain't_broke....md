### Post by Bucky Ball on 2012-02-14
If there is a more recent BIOS update available there is absolutely no reason why you shouldn't flash the BIOS. 

I am a big fan of 'If it ain't broke, don't fix it,' but in this case, the latest BIOS can help your computer cope with more modern hardware (and software demands) and a bunch of other things (enhance stability, performance to name a couple).

There is no guarantee an outdated BIOS version is causing your crashing problems, though. ;)
