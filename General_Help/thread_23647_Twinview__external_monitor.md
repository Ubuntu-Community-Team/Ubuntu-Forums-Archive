### Post by c_dog on 2005-04-04
The 1280x800 shouldn't be any problem, but you'll need define the modelines for 1280x1024  for at least the default bit depth, which you're setting to 24, before you call 1280x1024. Also are you sure the the laptop is talking to is LCD panel as a DFP. I'm not sure the 4200 chipset is fast enough to do both digital and analog dotclocks needed to handle two regions that big. The LCD in the laptop is probably talking analog, or CRT also.
