### Post by cchhrriiss121212 on 2010-08-15
Studio installs an extra kernel that is tweaked for audio work. Kernel updates also mean that you may have an extra generic kernel. Use startup manager to configure which kernel to use a default.
You can upgrade studio just like you would with normal Ubuntu, though I always prefer a fresh install.
