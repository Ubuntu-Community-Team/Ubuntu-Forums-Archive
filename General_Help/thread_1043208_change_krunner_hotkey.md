### Post by HavocXphere on 2009-01-18
Works fine on mine side.

Settings->Keyboard and mouse->Keyboard shortcut->"Run command interface"->run command.

and change that.

Mapping the windows key sometimes goes pear shaped. Not sure why...something about a super key or something. But it works in principle, I've got WinKey-Space mapped to the kmenu.
