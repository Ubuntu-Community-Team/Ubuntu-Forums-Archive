### Post by mcduck on 2009-08-09
Check these two options in Evolution's preferences:

Mail Preferences, "HTML Messages"-tab, set the "HTML Mode" to "Only ever show PLAIN" or "Prefer PLAIN".

Composer Preferences, General-tab, disable "Format messages in HTML" .
