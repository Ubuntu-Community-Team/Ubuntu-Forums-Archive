### Post by Tsingi on 2008-08-21
I've been recently having problems with Nautilus becoming painfully slow and unresponsive.  This is not good because in all other ways I absolutely love it.  I posted a bug somewhere, but I can't find it.

But I found a fix, works for me at least.

I uninstalled compiz (6 or 7 packages in all) and the problem totally disappeared.  I don't know exactly what it does, something to do with Open GL and fancy 3D desktop effects.

I will now go and file a bug with Compiz if I can find out how.

See this thread regarding FireFox problems, it relates to Nautilus as well: [http://ubuntu-virginia.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?p=5463907](http://ubuntu-virginia.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?p=5463907)

Thanks go to a sysadmin at work who suggested the fix.

Cheers!
