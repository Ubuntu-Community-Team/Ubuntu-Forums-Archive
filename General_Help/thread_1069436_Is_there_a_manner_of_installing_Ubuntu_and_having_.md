### Post by lykwydchykyn on 2009-02-14
That's really what a lot of the ubuntu derivatives have tried to do -- Linux Mint springs to mind, for instance.  Certain codecs and drivers have legal issues involved with them (notably DVD playback), so they aren't included in most distros.

You can help lobby to abolish software patents, that would certainly help!
