### Post by tgeer43 on 2009-08-21
A couple more ideas.

Try burning at a very slow speed like 2X or 4X and if your burning app has a 'burnproof' feature (most do although the terminology varies), make sure it is enabled. Lastly, try not running any other apps while the burn is in progress and verify the integrity of the image after burning. There should be an option for this in your burning app.
