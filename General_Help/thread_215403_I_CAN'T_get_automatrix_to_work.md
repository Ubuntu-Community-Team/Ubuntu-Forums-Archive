### Post by T700 on 2006-07-13
Just press Alt-F2 (or open a terminal--either is fine) and type:

```
automatix
```

*Spelling and case count; type carefully.*  Press Enter.  Follow the prompts.  It will ask you for your password several times (as I recall) by just stopping and waiting.  Enter the one you use to log on to your PC.  Don't worry that it doesn't show up as you type.
