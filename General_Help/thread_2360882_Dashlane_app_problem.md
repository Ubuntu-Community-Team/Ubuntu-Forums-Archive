### Post by xavier.dashlane on 2017-05-10
Hello, this is Xavier from the Technical Support team here at Dashlane.

If your master password does not work in Dashlane, could you please try logging in to our [Web app]("https://app.dashlane.com/")? If it does not work, please make sure that your e-mail address contains **no upper case letters** and that your e-mail address contains lower case letters only, and try again in an Incognito window in Chromium. Please follow the steps below:

1. Click [here to open the Web app]("https://app.dashlane.com/login")
2. Click **Log in with a different account**
3. Click the drop-down menu where your e-mail address is displayed
4. Select **--Use another account--** in the menu
5. Now enter your e-mail address in the box **in lower case letters**
6. Enter the security code if it is required and then enter your master password

Our team is currently working on this issue with the upper case letters  and it will be fixed shortly. We apologize for the inconvenience.

If it still does not work, please let me know in a private message here what ticket reference ID you have received when contacting our support. You may also want to [have a look at this article here in our Help Center]("https://support.dashlane.com/hc/en-us/articles/202698981").

Also please note that the Dashlane application and extension do not work officially on Xubuntu, but we are working on supporting more platforms; thank you so much for your patience in regard to this.

Please let me know on it goes!

Best,
Xavier
Dashlane Support
