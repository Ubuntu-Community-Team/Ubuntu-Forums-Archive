### Post by winh8r on 2011-04-30
Hi, You can alter the appearance of fonts, themes etc by going to System>
Preferences>
Appearance>

and selecting what you want from there.

To keep the File, Edit tabs visible, select : View>
                                              Toolbars>
                                              and make sure the "navigation bar" check box has an "x" in it.

hope that this is of some help to you.
