### Post by Philsco on 2009-06-18
Was pulseaudio one of the devices updated?  Or firefox?  If it was firefox, you may simply have to close and reopen firefox.  Or, you may have to reload your flash plugin.

If it's affecting ALL of your sounds, not just your internet-based sounds, then, yeah assuming you have already checked your audio settings, check your output devices, as well.  If it was a software update that reconfigured things like pulseaudio, alsa (which is the "Advanced Linux Sound Architecture"), or your sound card drivers, there may have been some resetting of default sound devices.
