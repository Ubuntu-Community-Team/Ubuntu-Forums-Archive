### Post by vamega on 2009-12-28
For all the locations you have in the Places menu (except for Home), you can just drag the location out of the menu onto the desktop.

For any other location, just right click on the desktop, and hit create launcher. In the dialog that open choose location, and type in the location you wish to have a shortcut to (for example /home/mike/some_folder will link to some_folder in mike's home directory, and this is how you would create a link to the home directory).

Hope that helps

Vamega

### Post by warfacegod on 2009-12-29
Hey, calm down. On the top left of the screen click places with your mouse. Hover the mouse over the item you want to move in this case we'll use Computer. With the mouse pointer over Computer, push and hold down the left button on your mouse, wait a split second, then move your mouse (still holding the left button) over your desktop, let go of the button. You should now have an icon on your desktop and you can move it using the same mouse technique.
