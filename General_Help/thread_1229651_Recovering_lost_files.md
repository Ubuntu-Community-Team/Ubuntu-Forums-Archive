### Post by joriad on 2009-08-02
I do believe there is a way to recover deleted files, however I believe anything that you write to the harddrive after the deletion will replace that information almost immediately. That means any program you download to recover the files could very well wipe out what you want to recover. I think the best way to do this is to use a separate computer and hook up your harddrive to it and run the program from the other harddrive so you can recover your files.
