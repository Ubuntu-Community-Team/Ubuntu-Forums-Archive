### Post by Bucky Ball on 2016-10-30
Here's a tip. Switch everything off. Plug everything in, HDMI to monitor and computer. Switch the monitor on and once one, make sure it is set to the correct source, the laptop input. Now switch the computer on and wait. You may even need to login before you see anything. See anything?

This is a trick that works with some TVs, but just wondering if it may also work in your case.
