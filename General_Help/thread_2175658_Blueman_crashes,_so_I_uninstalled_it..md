### Post by ajgreeny on 2013-09-20
No problem.

I've never had bluetooth hardware on any of my machines and therefore always have removed blueman, without any problems.  I also remove many of the other bluetooth packages such as bluez, bluez-alsa, bluez-cups, libbluetooth3 and obex-data-server.

Don't blindly remove everything related to bluetooth or you will also take several packages and libraries that you probably still need.
