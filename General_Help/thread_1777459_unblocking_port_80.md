### Post by ruffEdgz on 2011-06-07
No, you can not 'unblock' a port without taking to Cox Cable. I believe the only way to unblock it would be to upgrade to a business network which should open up port 80. See link from cox to learn more: [http://ww2.cox.com/business](http://ww2.cox.com/business)

You could also find a free hosting site that will allow you to upload an html page and in that html page, it will redirect you to yousite.com:8080 or whatever.

I believe the best solution is to use a hosted solution to solve this problem myself. I am using a service called justhost.com but other hosted solutions are out there and might be better. Here is a list of other hosting companies out there: [http://www.hostaz.com/](http://www.hostaz.com/)

Cheers!
