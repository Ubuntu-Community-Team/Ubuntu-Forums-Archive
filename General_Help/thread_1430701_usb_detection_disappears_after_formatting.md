### Post by Helkaluin on 2010-03-15
By 'usb icon' I assume you mean the icon on the desktop?

If yes then I suppose it's simply a matter of not mounting the drive. Try System->Admin->Disk Utility and see if the drive is really detected or not. If it is there you can mount it by simply clicking the 'Mount filesystem' button on the top left hand corner after selecting your usb drive.
