### Post by Jonor on 2014-03-19
Try using the Display the Thunderbird Menu button on the right hand side, 
particularly the topics under Preferences; most things can be unticked to remove them 
and also double clicking on a email should open it in a new tab that has no to/from lists or folder pane.
