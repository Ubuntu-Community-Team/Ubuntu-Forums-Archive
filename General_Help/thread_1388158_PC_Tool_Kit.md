### Post by louieb on 2010-01-23
From an old guy - worked on cars since to 60's and on computers since to 80's. 
This handles 95% of my computer stuff.
Phillips screwdrivers #2, #1, #0 
Small flat head screwdriver.
Nut Drivers 5/16, 1/4, 3/16, 5mm
Never have cut a wire - but a small wire cutter for plastic ties come in handy.
Needle nose pliers or tweezers for jumpers.

For most tools Craftsman, Cooper, Klein work just fine. IMHO - Wiha screwdrivers are the best I've ever used.   (German made)
