### Post by russo.mic on 2007-06-22
No Problem Brother!

Open up the manager and look at the left colum.  If you look at the bottom of the left colum, you'll see some buttons.  click the one that says Custom Filters.  That changes the contents of the box above it.  Click the word Broken in that list.  You'll now see all the broken packages in your system.  Write back with what package it is.

Good luck!
