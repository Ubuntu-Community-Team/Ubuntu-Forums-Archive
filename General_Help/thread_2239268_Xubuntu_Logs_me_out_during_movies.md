### Post by tomkat3 on 2014-08-12
Hey everybody! I'm on Ubuntu 14.04.

Basically, Xfce locks my computer automatically whenever it's idle for 10 minutes. As in the title, the only time this is really an issue is when I'm watching something on Netflix or Youtube. I'll be in the middle of a video, and suddenly the screen goes black and I'm looking at the login screen. I've usually frantically typed my password in and it starts up again like nothing happened, but it's still extremely annoying.

In the power manager settings, I already specify that I never want the computer to go to sleep when idle. Where do I tell it not to log me out like this? I didn't see anything obvious in the session and startup menus.

---

### Post by amanchesterman on 2014-08-13
Have you checked your Light Locker settings? (In the Settings menu.) I have mine switched to 'off', but yours may be on and causing the trouble.

---

### Post by tomkat3 on 2014-08-13
That did the trick! Thanks!
