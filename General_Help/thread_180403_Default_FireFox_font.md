### Post by AlanR8 on 2007-06-07
Had a similar issue with FF.

Personally I like the MS font Tahoma and installed it along with the other MS Core Fonts. I then changed all the fonts and sizes to Tahoma and they displayed everywhere just as I liked them OTHER than in FF.

The fix, in Kubuntu, was to go into System Settings/Appearance/GTK Styles and Fonts then under GTK Fonts clicked "Use my KDE fonts in GTK applications".

Job sorted
