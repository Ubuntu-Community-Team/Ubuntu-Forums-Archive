### Post by darolu on 2010-03-05
I think Button 1 to 9 are reserved for mice. Button 1 is the left click button, 2 is the right click button (unless you use lefty settings), button 3 is the scroll will click button, button 4 is scroll up (mouse) and button 5 is scroll down (mouse), 6 to 9 are reserved for mice with more buttons (as far as I know, they may work for other devices).

Up arrow is "Up". Edit the second tab (key binds.) not the third (Button binds.)
