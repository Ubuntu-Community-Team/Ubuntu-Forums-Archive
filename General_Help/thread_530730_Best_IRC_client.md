### Post by Scunizi on 2007-08-20
Xchat will do all that.  A lot of servers are having problems with DCC exploits.  Freenode servers want you to change the server address and port number to chat.freenode.net port 8001 to avoid exploits.

Another IRC client that is not neccessarily the easiest but fun to learn with lots of capability is irssi.

Both of these are available from synaptic.
