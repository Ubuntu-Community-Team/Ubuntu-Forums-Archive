### Post by dandnsmith on 2011-05-26
The no-squint addon for firefox (and chrome, I think) has been a great thing for me - you can change things on a 'global' basis, and adjust them for troublesome pages. It can help someone who has, like me, a partial color blindness.

If you need to alter everything, then you might need to change the screen resolution to lower figures (increases the pixel size, and therefore fonts size, icon size, and so on.
