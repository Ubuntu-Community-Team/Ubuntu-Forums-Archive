### Post by 73ckn797 on 2009-01-28
Right click on the mouse out in the desktop and select change background. If you have any pictures of whatever you want to use you will need to click on the "add" button and go to the directory where those file(s) are located and select that file.

You will find it also under System/Preferences/Appearance.
