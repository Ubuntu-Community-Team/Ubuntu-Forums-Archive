### Post by Mark Phelps on 2013-02-09
Intel doesn't supply drivers through Additional Drivers as does AMD and Nvidia, so going there will not help.

Ubuntu installs the default Intel driver during setup -- so it's likely there is no other drive available.

However, you can check at the link below and see if Intel provides a driver for your PC that is different from what you already have:

[http://downloadcenter.intel.com/Detail_Desc.aspx?agr=Y&DwnldID=17045&lang=eng]("http://downloadcenter.intel.com/Detail_Desc.aspx?agr=Y&DwnldID=17045&lang=eng")
