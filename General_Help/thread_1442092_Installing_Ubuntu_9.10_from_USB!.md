### Post by pcdave98 on 2010-03-29
I had the same problem, last week, when I made a bootable USB using UNetbootin. I hadn't clicked the *Select Distribution* and *Select Version* tabs at the top. I'd also neglected to click *Diskimage* on the left.
 
Apart from that, I was spot on.
