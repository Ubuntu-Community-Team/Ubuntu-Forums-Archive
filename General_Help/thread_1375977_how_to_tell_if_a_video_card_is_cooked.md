### Post by RedRat on 2010-01-08
Do you see any graphic during bootup or is it just text? If you see the Ubuntu logo, which is a graphic, you have some graphic capability. Check your cables. 

Graphic cards do go, but usually it is a total fail. You get nada on your screen. Do you have a spare card? Maybe a friend might have one.

Maybe a good time to upgrade your video card.
