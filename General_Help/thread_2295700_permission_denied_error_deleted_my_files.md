### Post by jean_rivire on 2015-09-21
Hi,
The natural thing I would do is to try finding the temporary files which may have register or 'memorized' your files.
First, when your file browser is open, type ctrl+H to show all hidden files
Try searching your file by typing the name of your file + '.tmp'; or just look for .tmp first and then do ctrl+f and search for the name of your file.

Btw, to avoid that this happens again, you might want to click right on your file (if you ever find it), to go into properties and to change the access permissions that you have from 'read only' to 'read and write' 

Cheers, and good luck,
