### Post by Doctadrez on 2010-03-07
This might not work for you, but check your bios to see if you can start up your laptop at a particular time. I've checked mine and am able to set a time for it to turn on. 

If you can, then just set your account to automatic login, and set an [alarm clock program]("http://alarm-clock.pseudoberries.com/") to play a song/sound at whatever time you want.

Of course, you may want to check that things work properly by going through a trial run.
