### Post by Paddy Landau on 2020-07-18
Please would you use a better title than "Help me!!!!!". Titles help us to find threads where we have knowledge. An example title for this thread might be, "Installation doesn't complete, because stuck on copying files."

The other thing is that you have given barely any information. Are you installing Ubuntu or a different flavour? Which version? What installation medium are you using for your installation &#8212; DVD, USB stick, or something else? When you run the installation, are you choosing to create a dual boot, or wipe the entire disk, or what?

Also tell us what you've tried. Where did you obtain the download file, and did you check its integrity (as @ActionParsnip asked)? How did you copy the downloaded file to your installation medium? How much space do you have on your hard drive? Have you tried restarting the installation from scratch (sometimes it fails on the first attempt but succeeds on the second because of some small error)? How long have you waited for the final process to complete (older hardware could take quite a long time before responding)?

Remember that we can't see your screen or watch what you did, so you have to help us to help you. We can't just guess &#8212; there are too many variables.
