### Post by Therion on 2008-09-15
Right-click on your bottom panel (assuming this is where you want your windows to GO when you minimize them) and click on the "Add to Panel" option.

From the options presented click on the option for "Windows List as Buttons".  I think that's the correct name for it.  I'm sorry but I'm stuck behind a WinXP machine at the moment at my office.  Pretty sure that's what it's called though.

Now when you minimize windows, you'll see them as buttons on your bottom PANEL (not "taskbar", Ubuntu has "PANELS" ...  :)  ).
