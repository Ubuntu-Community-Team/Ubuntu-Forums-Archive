### Post by Quackers on 2010-09-21
Do you have Compiz fusion and Cairo Dock installed?
When this happens to me I can click on the compiz icon (in the Cairo dock) and just click on "reload WM" (window manager).
If you don't have these installed I think you will need to restart the Window Manager.
