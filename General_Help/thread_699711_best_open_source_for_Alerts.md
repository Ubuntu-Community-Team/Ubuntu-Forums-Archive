### Post by honeydew on 2008-02-18
the ones I have used/heard of are

zabbix
nagios
zenoss

zenoss seems to be more of a networking tool.. has a really good auto discovery interface

nagios is good but is very arcane I have not used it in a few years

zabbix rocks out it has a client application that you install on each machine and gives you the very fine tuned statistics I think you are looking for.

(nagios and zabbix are available in the repositories)
