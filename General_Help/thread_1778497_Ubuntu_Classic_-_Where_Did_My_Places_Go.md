### Post by matt_symes on 2011-06-09
Hi

> Despite rebooting my Places menu is still missing things like Documents, Music, Video etc. Where are they, any ideas?Nautilus seems to have lost them as bookmarks. 

Try this. It may work.

Open Nautilus. Navigate *into* your Documents folder. Go to the bookmarks menu item and click "Add Bookmark" from the drop down menu.

Then check under the places menu to see if Documents is there. If it is add the other missing menu items as bookmarks .

Kind regards
