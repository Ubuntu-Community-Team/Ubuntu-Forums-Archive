### Post by bored2k on 2006-02-16
You can "force"-fullscreen any application, so that shouldn't be a problem (go to your keyboard shortcuts and set a shortcut for fullscreening an application).

**[http://packages.ubuntu.com/cgi-bin/search_packages.pl?keywords=educational&searchon=all&subword=1&version=breezy&release=all](http://packages.ubuntu.com/cgi-bin/search_packages.pl?keywords=educational&searchon=all&subword=1&version=breezy&release=all)**
[LIST]
[*]**TuxTyping** is an educational typing tutor for children starring Tux, the Linux Penguin. [http://www.geekcomix.com/dm/tuxtype/](http://www.geekcomix.com/dm/tuxtype/)
[*]**Tuxmath** is an educational math tutor for children starring Tux, the Linux Penguin. [http://www.geekcomix.com/dm/tuxmath/gallery/](http://www.geekcomix.com/dm/tuxmath/gallery/)
[CENTER][IMG]http://www.geekcomix.com/dm/tuxtype/data/gallery/4/small.jpg[/IMG][/CENTER]
[*]**GCompris **is an educational game which propose different activities to kids from 2 to 10.
>  
Some activities are game oriented, but always educational. You will find some activities in the following topics:
# computer discovery: keyboard, mouse, different mouse gesture, ...

# algebra: table memory, enumeration, double entry table, mirror image, ...

# science: the canal lock, the water cycle, the submarine, ...

# geography: place the country on the map

# games: chess, memory, ...

# reading: reading practice

# other: learn to tell time, puzzle of famous paintings, vector drawing, ...

All in all, gcompris proposes more than 50 activities and it continues to evolves. gcompris is a free software, you have the possibility to adapt it to your needs or to improve it, and, why not, to share your work with the kids of all the world.
[http://gcompris.net/-Pillanatfelvetelek-](http://gcompris.net/-Pillanatfelvetelek-)
[*] I know KDE has **many** educational applications/games for youngsters, I just don't know their name.
[/LIST]

### Post by az on 2006-02-16
Childsplay rocks!

The backgroud music is changed in Dapper (actually, there are more varietites.)  The inane music is hilarious!  [http://packages.ubuntu.com/breezy/games/childsplay](http://packages.ubuntu.com/breezy/games/childsplay)

Be sure to install the childsplay-plugins package so that you have more than just one game.....

My four-year-old is a whiz at computers and she uses childsplay to show her two-year-old sister how to do it....



Tuxpaint is also a winner.

There are many more...
