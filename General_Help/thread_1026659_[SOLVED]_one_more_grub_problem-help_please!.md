### Post by caljohnsmith on 2008-12-31
You could edit your Fedora's grub.conf similar to what forestpixie suggested, but if I were in your shoes I think I would simply link to the Ubuntu menu.lst; that way when Ubuntu gets kernel upgrades you don't have to copy them into your Fedora grub.conf. It's totally up to you though. If you want to link to Ubuntu's menu.lst, you could do:
```
sudo grub
grub> root (hd0,4)
grub> setup (hd0,4)
```
