### Post by Melk79 on 2008-11-03
I had becomed very addicted to this as well... it seems during my upgrade to 8.10, the Opacity, Brightness and Saturation option under the Accesibility section of CCSM had been turned off.  This is where the Alt+MouseWheel/TouchpadScroll (Button 4 & 5) setting to increase and decrease the opacity of the active window is located.  Re-enable this option and you should be back on track.
