### Post by uRock on 2010-11-19
My experience with rewritable DVD/CDs has been that they are slow.

You can format thumb drives to EXT4.

An external HDD would probably have close to the same transfer speed, if not faster, as a thumb drive, especially with USB 3.0. The external HDD will most likely outlast a thumb drive.
