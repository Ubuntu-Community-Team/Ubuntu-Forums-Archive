### Post by Nalum on 2007-05-06
Hello all, I was just wondering if there was a way to stop getting updates for certain programs?

As an example I have beryl installed but the upgrade that I get from the upgrade manager cause it to stop working so I would like to stop it from trying to upgrade beryl.

Thank you.
Nalum

---

### Post by Snowcat on 2007-05-06
In Synaptic, choose the relevant package, go to the 'package' menu and choose 'lock version'. Done!
