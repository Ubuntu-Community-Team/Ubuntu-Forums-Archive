### Post by dragonfly41 on 2012-01-23
Yes, when I've got many tabs open ...

Rather than trying to find the cause I installed Firefox **session-manager** as addon.

This allows recovery of crashes by deselecting previous session tabs.   

And you can save sessions (like tab bookmarks).
