### Post by diskmaster23 on 2010-10-29
Okay...I figured out that my BIOS was outdated. I updated the BIOS I was able to get the OS up and running, but I kept running into all kinds of problems. I eventually figured out that I had two sticks of memory that were causing problems. I took the memory out of the machine and everything worked out just fine.
