### Post by Dupointx on 2011-04-06
> **nsk tacticzz said:**
> hi, i have just got a vga cable that fits my tv and my acer aspire one netbook, when i connect them together my tv reads no sync. im on ubuntu 10.10 please help me get this sorted out

You have to tell Ubuntu to send a signal to the TV. I don't think it will auto send to it. You can select 'Monitors' under Administration and change the display. Also, make sure your TV is on VGA.

This can be buggy, and your desktop may crash.
My experience trying this:
Tried connecting my AAO D150 to my Dynex TV using the VGA port. Ubuntu didn't like that at all. Gnome crashed every time, and display options would always say 'unknown display' for the TV.
