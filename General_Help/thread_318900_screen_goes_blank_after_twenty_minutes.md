### Post by wpshooter on 2006-12-14
Well here is the short answer, because this O/S still has a lot of bugs in it !!!

Now for the long answer.

First make sure that your automatic saving of SESSIONS changes is marked.

Then go into your screen saver and turn it ON and set the slider to 2 minute.

Then go into your power management and set the slider to 3 minutes.

Let your computer set and it should go into screen saver after 2 minutes.

Then after approx. 1 more minute the screen should go BLANK (power saving).

After this RESTART your O/S.

When it comes back up go into the screen saver section and turn the screensaver function OFF but do NOT more the slider from the 2 minute mark.

Then go into the power management section and put your slider on NEVER.

Then RESTART your computer again.

When it comes back to the desktop, just let it set there for about a half your to see if it stays on.

Good luck. - They really need to work on this !!!
