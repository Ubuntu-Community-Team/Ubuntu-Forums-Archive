### Post by shinkaide on 2007-12-15
> **nathangrubb said:**
> adblock is the version where you have to make your own filter. Are you sure you're not looking for [adblock plus?]("https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/1865")

Okay, I was really stupid not to notice this before:

In the windows implementation, Adblock Plus for FF automatically prompts you to select a list that will automatically block/remove majority of the ads you'll commonly run into. The one we've got here on Ubuntu (well, for me, at least, on 7.10) does not do that. That's because **the Adblock package in Ubuntu 7.10's Applications > Add/Remove Applications is NOT Adblock Plus**, therefore, it's not supposed to block anything automatically at the outset, as Nathangrubb said.

Really sorry 'bout this; I still have these noobish tendencies. :)

Anyway, thanks to nathangrubb for pointing out what I should've noticed in the first place. Just installed [Adblock Plus from the FF Addons]("https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/1865") site and now it works like I expected it to. :D
