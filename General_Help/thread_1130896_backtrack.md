That's the problem with backtrack as opposed to just building your own setup. If your wifi usb card works with other distros, then there's a good chance you can make it work with backtrack; however, you might have to custom burn it or set up some type of shell script on the drive or another device that can be ran when you fire up backtrack. I'm not 100% sure on it, but I'd guess that the most used wifi cards are supported only.

If you're serious about pen-testing, I recommend you build your own setup.
