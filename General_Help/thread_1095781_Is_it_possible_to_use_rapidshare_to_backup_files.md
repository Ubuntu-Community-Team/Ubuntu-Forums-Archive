### Post by arsenic23 on 2009-03-14
Since there are (supposedly) ways to index and search rapidshare I would avoid it unless you want to run the risk of someone else downloading your files.


You could always purchase a very small piece of a server and ssh your file and and from it with rsync, or just setup ftp on it.
