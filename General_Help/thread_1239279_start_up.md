### Post by pi4r0n on 2009-08-13
Best way and easiest one is to add this job to [COLOR=SeaGreen]**Start Applications**[/COLOR] in System -> Preferences

To add new job just press **add** and provide 3 simple infos


[LIST=1]
[*]Name [COLOR=Orange]**(Name of the think you want to run)**[/COLOR]
[*]Command [COLOR=Orange]**(Command you want to run or program)**[/COLOR]
[*]Comment [COLOR=Orange](if you wish to have one)[/COLOR]
[/LIST]
Job done
