### Post by The Cog on 2008-09-21
The installer goves you the option to resize teh windows partition to make room. It will then make the extra partitions it needs.

Although it is not strictly necessary to remove the other disks, since you seem not to be very comfortable with making partitons, removing the other disks during the procedure reduces the likekyhood of an awful mistake trashing them.

You should always make sure you have a good backup, even in the normal course of using a puter, but especially before repartitioning etc. External USB drives are a good backup solution these days.
