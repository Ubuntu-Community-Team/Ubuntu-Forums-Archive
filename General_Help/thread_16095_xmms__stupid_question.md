### Post by kassetra on 2005-02-19
Here's what you do:
 Down in the bottom of your playlist in xmms is a button labeled "add" or marked with a plus (it's all the way over on the left at the bottom of the playlist)
 
 click & hold down that button, and a small menu will popup.
 The middle item in that menu is the "dir +" ... which means add a directory.
 
 If you choose a directory that has many subdirectories in it, it will add all of the music files recursively.  example:
 
 I have all of my music in a "music" directory, but then I have many subdirectories for each artist... so if I want all of my music recursively, I would go to and add the directory /home/kassetra/music
 
 and then all of the music in all of my subdirectories is added.
 
 make sense?
