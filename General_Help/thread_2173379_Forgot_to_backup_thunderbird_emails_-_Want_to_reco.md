### Post by dragonfly41 on 2013-09-09
You might be very lucky and find that your mail service provider has an archive of all your emails.

If you are out of luck on that .. your XP profile will be found here ...
[URL="http://http://www-archive.mozilla.org/support/thunderbird/profile"]
http://www-archive.mozilla.org/support/thunderbird/profile[/URL]

C:\Documents and Settings\[User Name]\Application Data\Thunderbird\Profiles\

**testdisk** is one Ubuntu utility to help in scanning your drive to look for any XP files which might be recoverable.
But this depends on what formatting steps you took (e.g. NTFS to EXT4 to accept Ubuntu?). 
  
Meanwhile I suggest that you stop using Ubuntu you have installed and run **testdisk** to analyse your hard drive from a Ubuntu live CD or USB to avoid overwriting any further XP files.
