### Post by ninjapirate89 on 2009-05-01
Go to System -> Preferences -> Mouse.

Click on the Touchpad tab and under General uncheck the box labeled Enable Mouse Clicks With Touchpad.

Edit -> just noticed you said you're using xubuntu. I'm not sure if its the same or not.
