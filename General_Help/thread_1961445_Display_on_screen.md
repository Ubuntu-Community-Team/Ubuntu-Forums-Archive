Hello.
I have a Toshiba laptop where I use Ubuntu 11.10 in dual boot with win7 and sometimes I prefer to connect the laptop to my Samsung Monitor.

 When I do it in Windows, it automatically switches off the laptop screen, and besides there is Fn options where I can set both screens displaying or just the monitor.

However I don't know how to do it on ubuntu. When I connect the monitor's cable, it displays on both screens, and I would like the laptop screen to be switched off while connected to the monitor.

Where/How can I configure this?

### Post by IWantFroyo on 2012-04-19
Hit the Windows key, and go to Display or Monitors (just type it in). Then, select your Samsung monitor, change it to the proper resolution if it's not already set to it, and select it to not mirror and to be to the left of your laptop monitor.

I know this won't turn your laptop monitor off, but it'll use the Samsung as your main monitor.

Edit: I don't think there's a easy way to move the Unity launcher.
