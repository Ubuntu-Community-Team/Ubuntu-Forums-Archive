Anything with a recommended (see the so-called "Peacock thread" in the dd-wrt forum) developers build (do NOT use the v24 sp1 release it is extremely buggy!) should work out great.  I recommend you get a model with at least a 200mhz (250mhz is better) processor and around 16MB of memory plus.

[http://dd-wrt.com](http://dd-wrt.com)

I hear tomato is great as well but I have no experience with it.

If you do go with dd-wrt, spend a day or so studying the forum and all the recent posts within the last 30 days.  It will save you from mistakes.
