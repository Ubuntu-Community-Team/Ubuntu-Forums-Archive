### Post by audiomick on 2009-12-21
If that is an IP form Kabel Deutschland, they wont tell you who it is. German law forbids it, and there have been enough scandals about leaked personal info here in the last few years that they should be very wary. About all you can hope for is, if you can convince them of a crime, that they pass it on to the police, or that you pass it on to the police and let them follow it up.
