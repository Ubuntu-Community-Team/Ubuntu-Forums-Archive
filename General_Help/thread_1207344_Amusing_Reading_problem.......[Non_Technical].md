### Post by frankwakeman on 2009-07-08
Is this a bit of an OCD thing?  I have relatively mild OCD but it doesn't manifest in this way.  The thing is, the only way to break the patterns is to put your foot down with yourself and decide to, before it mutates into something else.  It's like you have an internal argument with the part of you that decides to do the click-highlighting, "No we are NOT doing this anymore, pal!"

Does your eyesight come into this at all?  You can make the text bigger easily enough (ctrl and +, if I remember right) or specifiy bigger font sizes in Firefox (Edit > Preferences > Content > Fonts & Colours).  I'm doing this on a netbook without my contact lenses though, so I imagine the compulsion aspect is the main problem.  How about leaving the computer alone for a few days - even if you have to go and stay somewhere where there's no internet facility to achieve this) and then starting afresh with the above-described internal argument.

Good luck.
