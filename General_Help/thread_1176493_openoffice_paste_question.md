In OpenOffice spreadsheet, how can I copy a column of cells (column 1) and then paste these cells onto another column (column2), but keep the background colors that are already selected in column 2?

JBA2337

---

### Post by rudy.b on 2009-06-02
Try using "Edit > Paste Special..." and uncheck "Formats" when pasting your cells.  This should paste the data without changing the formatting of the destination cells.
