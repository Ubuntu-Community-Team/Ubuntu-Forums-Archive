### Post by bobcollard on 2010-08-31
> **wilbuzz2 said:**
> i am not a big fan of the xubuntu icon so i was trying to change it to a nicer one but i cant find an ubuntu icon the right size any ideas:(:(
Find one on the Internet, crop it and scale the image using Gimp.  Then place it where you can find it and don't move it.  Next, right click the icon in your Panel and under Properties click on the Button Icon, point to the picture and it will be replaced.  Attached are two I used before.

### Post by inameiname on 2010-08-31
Yep, Google searches often work for me in finding better icons. You can also search only icons through Google, which is nice as a way to weed out those larger images you don't need. If you want too, you can also define the exact size of the icon if you wish, so the only results are the same as the one you are trying to replace.
