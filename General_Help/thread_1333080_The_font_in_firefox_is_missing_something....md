### Post by garvinrick4 on 2009-11-21
You have to have msttcorefonts. They are in ubuntu-restricted-extras
Older distros download individual package.
go to Edit to content to fonts to advanced and change 2nd 3rd and 4th fonts.
Times New Roman is nice and Courier New for 4th.

Do same for Desktop  System Preferences Appearance  Try firs 4 Times new roman Bold
size to fit eye.  And courier new Bold for 5th.

fonts are true type and look sharp yours are bitmapped and look kind of crappy.

True type or Clear type have nice sharp edges. Do not forger subpixel smoothing in Desktop. You cannot miss it in fonts.
