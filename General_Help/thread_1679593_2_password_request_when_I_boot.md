### Post by vanadium on 2011-02-01
In System - Preferences - Passwords and Encryption keys, change the password of "Passwords: login" to match your login password.

This is what FahadMKS hinted to. The keyring is automatically unlocked is the password is the same as your login password. If you change your login password, the keyring password remains unchanged.
