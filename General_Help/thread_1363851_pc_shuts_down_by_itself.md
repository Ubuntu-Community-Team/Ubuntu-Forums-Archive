### Post by bodhi.zazen on 2009-12-25
The most common cause of this behavior (shutting off without warning) I have seen is over heating.

Check the temp of your CPU, open the case and see if it needs cleaning (unplug the power cord first).

It shouldnt but it can happen. Funny enough, I've lived in a house with really bad power, and while everybody elses computer would do just this every now and again, mine never did. I've also seen some a computer do this when the power cable wasnt firmly connected to the back of the computer. Minor movements (like heavy walking around the house or a truck passing the house) would make it restart suddenly.
