### Post by XubuRoxMySox on 2009-11-23
Try opening a browser window and go to [http://localhost:631/](http://localhost:631/)

Click on the **Administration** tab, choose **Add Printer** (you will be asked for your username and password - it's the same as your computer username and password). There's also a little box you can tic for sharing the printer on a network.

-Robin
