### Post by ifokkema on 2006-05-26
Hi,

I reckon you clicked 'Save current setup' when you logged out? That'll make Ubuntu remember whatever you had open at the time you logged out, and it'll try to open it again whenever you log in.

Try closing all of your programs and log out with the 'Save current setup' checked. Then log in again and see if it still throws that error.

Hope this helps,

Ivo
