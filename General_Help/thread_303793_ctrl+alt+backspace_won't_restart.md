### Post by macogw on 2006-11-21
Ctrl+Alt+Bksp isn't restart.  It just kills X.  If it's a process that's running that isn't X-related, just infinitely looping in the background, killing X won't stop it.  You have to actually restart (possibly by shutting off and turning back on your computer).
