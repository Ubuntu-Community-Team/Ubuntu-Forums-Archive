### Post by dcstar on 2010-06-24
> **CharlesA said:**
> Start a new thread with the specs of the machine and what kind of video card it has in it.

AFAIK SIS chips have never had Linux hardware drivers, and hardware driver support for old Nvidia cards was dropped after 8.04.

The bottom line is if you want to have full video support then you need recently modern video hardware (not just released) that you know is supported by Linux.
