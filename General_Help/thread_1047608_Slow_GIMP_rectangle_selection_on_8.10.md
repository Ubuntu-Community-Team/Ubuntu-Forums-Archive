### Post by perstromgren on 2009-01-22
I have a weird problem.

In GIMP, whene selecting a rectangle with the rectangle tool, the response is vey slow. I can move the mouse over the image and it can take nearly a second to resize the rectangle (an outline, no image redraw).

On the other hand, if I move the whole window on the desktop, the window follows the mouse as fast as I can move it! 

Is this a GIMP problem or a driver problem?

I'm using GiMP 2.6 under Ubuntu 8.10 on an Asus EEE 901 laptop.

Per.

---

### Post by perstromgren on 2009-02-15
Problem is solved.

It turned out that by having some "eye candy" enabled, it slowed down the computer immensly. 

I did this:
System->Preferences->Appearance->Visual Effects->None
