### Post by lmarmisa on 2010-11-13
Ubuntu is trying to find the picture in an unmounted drive.

So, select again your preferred picture for wallpaper from the copy stored in your pictures folder. Select System -> Preferences -> Appearance -> Background and then the image you want to chose.
