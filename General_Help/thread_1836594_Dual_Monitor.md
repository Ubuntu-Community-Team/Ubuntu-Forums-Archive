### Post by seawolf167 on 2011-08-31
You can use [synergy ]("http://synergy-foss.org/")to redirect your mouse/keyboard to the other OS screen.

You can use a [KVM switch ]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/KVM_switch")to hook multiple computers to the same monitor/keyboard/mouse
