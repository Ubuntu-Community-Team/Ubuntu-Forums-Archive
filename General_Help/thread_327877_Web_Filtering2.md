Have you been able to connect your ubuntu to your father's xp and share the internet? First try that, I suppose that to go from there to filtered internet shouldn't be that hard (having never tried it) ;) 

There are quite a number of threads on these forums about sharing an internet connection (either wireless or broadband).

Have a look at 
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=297675&highlight=share+internet](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=297675&highlight=share+internet)

If you are using cables, you might need a crossover cable to do this, actually. They look like a regular broadband cable, but have some wires that cross over. You can buy these at most computer stores, and they are fairly cheap (I got one in the uk a few months ago for 4 pounds).
