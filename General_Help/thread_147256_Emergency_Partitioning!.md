### Post by Bradl3yJ on 2006-03-19
For the LCD screen problem, you can try changing the resolution to a higher resolution

"System > Preferences > Screen Resolution"

or try using your monitors buttons and menus to autodetect the screen size (i found it on a dell LCD at work, had the same problem, used dell monitor menus to find an Auto-Detect option), look through all of the menus on your monitor, for me it was under an option that i had no idea the meaning of.
