### Post by salvachn on 2009-05-07
Use Truecrypt. It is open source, and among the best cross platform encryption tools. You can allocate most of your drive for a TrueCrypt volume, leaving some space to store the binaries for different platforms. 
Take a look here:[http://www.ghacks.net/2008/12/14/encrypting-usb-devices-data-with-true-crypt/](http://www.ghacks.net/2008/12/14/encrypting-usb-devices-data-with-true-crypt/)
