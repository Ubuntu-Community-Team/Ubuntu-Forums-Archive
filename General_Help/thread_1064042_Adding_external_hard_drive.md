### Post by scouser73 on 2009-02-08
Any external hard drive will do, I personally have four Maxtor 500GB 3200 Home Basics, one Maxtor 3200 home basics 1TB and one LaCie 250GB drive and they all work perfectly.  When you connect your new drive you may need to change permissions so that you have full access to it.

Also the transfer rate will differ; if the USB ports on your laptop are USB 1.0, and the external hard drive is 2.0 then there is a lag, but hardly noticeable.

Good luck choosing your new hard drive.
