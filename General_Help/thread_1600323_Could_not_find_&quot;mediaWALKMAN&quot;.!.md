### Post by saveanescapist on 2011-02-25
This might be a little late for you, but I'll offer my two cents anyway. I've had similar problems, but it seems to work fine, although slowly and with difficulty, in Rhythmbox. 

Regarding the album art issue, however:

I've noticed this same issue with Rhythmbox and my own Walkman. It appears to me that the album art only shows up on the Walkman if you've listened to the music in rhythmbox, thus making rhythmbox access the internet and download the album art. 

I'm not sure if it updates the album art on your walkman everytime it plugs in, but I don't think this is the case. I believe that when you put the music on your walkman, it must have been listened to up to that point.

This is my experience, anyway. Purely anecdotal, however.
