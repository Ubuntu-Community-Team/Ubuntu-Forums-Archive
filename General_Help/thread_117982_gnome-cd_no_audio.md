### Post by learning curve on 2006-01-15
lick on SYSTEM > PREFERENCES > SOUND

look where it says 'Default Sound Card'.

You may have to go into the bios and disable your onboard sound if you have a soundcard also.  When your system is booting check to see if it says loading alsa 0 and alsa 1, Alsa is your sound cards.  You may have 2.
