### Post by ajgreeny on 2022-10-28
You still have not said which bookmarks you are asking about and though guiverc seems to know what you mean, I do not.

If they are your firefox bookmarks it should not be such a huge job as you seem to be making it.

First you can open the bookmarks manager (Ctrl+Shift+O) then in the top panel of that click Import & Backup where you can choose to backup bookmarks.
Choose the Export to HTML to create the html file and copy that file to your USB or transfer however you wish; email it to yourself if you find that easiest.
On the second computer open firefox and again use the Bookmarks manager and this time choose to import the html file you saved.
