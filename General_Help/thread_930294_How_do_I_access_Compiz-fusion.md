### Post by fragos on 2008-09-25
1st you need an acellerated (direct rendering) video card like an Nvidia or others. Run "glxinfo |grep render" in a terminal window to see if you do. Then System-> Preferences-> Appearance-> Visual Effects tab. "Normal" is a good place to start.
