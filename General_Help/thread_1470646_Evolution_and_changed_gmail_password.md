Hi All

Yesterday I changed my google password, and it seems to have caused Evolution a bit of a problem.

I have a bunch of calendars and a gmail account on Evo, and it's not happy about the new password.  Instead of popping up a dialog asking me for the password, I see the message 'Checking for new mail (...)' at the bottom of the window for several minutes.  I have tried unchecking 'remember password' in the account settings, to no avail.

On trying to close the window, it goes grey and I often have to kill Evo using the task manager...

How do I insert my new password into evo?  It's most perplexing.

### Post by mcduck on 2010-05-03
Evolution stores it's passwords into Gnome's keyring, so you can use the Keyring Manager to change/remove the saved passwords. You'll find it in Applications/Accessories/Passwords and Encryption Keys.
