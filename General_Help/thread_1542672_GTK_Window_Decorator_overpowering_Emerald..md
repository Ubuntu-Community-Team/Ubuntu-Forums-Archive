### Post by WorMzy on 2010-07-31
That's normal, Emerald is just a window decorator - it just provides the decorations (Title bar and buttons). The windows still run on your GTK+ theme.

If you want to control the window colours and still have cool-looking Emerald window decoration, may I suggest using gnome-color-chooser to create your own custom GTK+ theme.
