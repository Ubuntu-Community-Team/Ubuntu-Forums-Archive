### Post by Therion on 2009-03-18
It might be best if you start with a more current version of Ubuntu.  Even 6.10 (Edgy Eft) is woefully outdated and unsupported at this point.  

Go here: [http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/download](http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/download)

And download 8.04 (not 8.10, **8.04**).  Burn it to an .iso following these instructions:

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BurningIsoHowto](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BurningIsoHowto)

Also, be sure to burn your media s..l..o..w..l..y.  No faster than 16x for CD, 8x, really being my preference.
