### Post by lisati on 2008-06-18
There might be a setting on your TV to adjust picture size, position, and orientation. (A few months back I connected my laptop to my TV using s-video. At first I thought there was a problem with my laptop, because there was a triangular black area at the bottom right of the display. It turns out I'd accidentally changed settings on the then-new TV)
