### Post by modmadmike on 2008-09-01
Try updating your BIOS and make sure to do this when your pc wont lose power. Or reset the bios by 1. setting to defaults, 2.moving a jumper next to the battery on the motherboard after removing power 3. if its a laptop try holding the power button for 10 seconds with the battery removed. I recommend reseting it first :).
