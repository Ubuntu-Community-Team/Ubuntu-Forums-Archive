### Post by stefangr1 on 2011-08-11
Really, I think people are mistakenly comparing the use of MacBuntu with, say, illegally downloaded software. However, this is completely wrong. In case of downloading illegal software, the product has been illegally obtained but the product itself does not (neccesarily) infringe any patents or trademarks. If you, however, use a (bought and paid for) Samsung Galaxy or MacBuntu, you have obtaned it legally. The fact that the suppliers may have infringed copyrights does not change that IN ANY WAY. Therefore, while the makers of MacBuntu may have to pay a fortune to Apple, the users are totally fine.
