### Post by grahammechanical on 2013-01-05
In 12.10 when we are not connected to a network or router we only have the option to Enable Networking in the icon menu. You may find that enabling Networking in that menu may cause Network Manager to connect to your wireless network or put a list of available wireless access points in that menu.

Alternatively, you can open System Settings and open the Network utility. Is your wireless adapter turned off? You will see a slider that can be used to turn Wireless Off and On. When wireless is turned on you will see a list of wireless access points that are in range of your wireless adapter.

Regards.
