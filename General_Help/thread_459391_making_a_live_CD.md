### Post by merlinus on 2007-05-30
If you burn it as a disk image, the contents will be lots of files and folders, not just the .iso.

If you are seeing only the .iso, then it has been burned as a data disk, and not a disk image.

Your burner software should give you this option in one of the menus.

-merlin
