### Post by darolu on 2010-08-07
Torrents' speed depends on a variety of factors, imho the main ones are 1) seeders and 2) closed ports. If there are, lets say... 10 people seeding to 1.000 peers, no matter what kind of Internet connection you have it will crawl. Also if your firewall is blocking your Transmission set ports it won't be able to connect to your peers correctly. So make sure there are enough seeders and that your ports are open in your router firewall.
