### Post by camaron1 on 2011-07-21
I use [**back in time**.]("http://backintime.le-web.org/download_page/")

It will make incremental backups to anywhere you choose (eg. external drive) of any folders you choose. You set up the frequency of the backups and all is done automatically. The restoring process is very simple and the software works very well. Also important, it doesnt use any kind of fancy format and you can access the backup files directly with your file manager.

I recommend it.
