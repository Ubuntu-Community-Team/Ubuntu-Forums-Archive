### Post by edd07 on 2007-11-13
You may need to change the channel that's controlled by your keyboard. Assuming you're using GNOME, you need to go into System>Preferences>Sound. At the bottom of that window there's a list. The mic will be selected. Change that to your speaker's channel (you may have to experiment to find the one. Names vary from card to card)

Hope this helps.
