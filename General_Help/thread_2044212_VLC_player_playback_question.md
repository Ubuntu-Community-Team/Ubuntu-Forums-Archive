### Post by Gatorade on 2012-08-19
I would like to know if anyone knows how to change the settings(if possible) on VLC media player to use the scroll wheel on the mouse to control the video playback instead of the volume.

When I play a video using movie player , I can scroll the wheel and the video will roll back , but doing this using VLC decreases the volume. I'd like to set it to be the same as movie player.

---

### Post by sid0972 on 2012-08-19
you have to take the pointer of the mouse on the bar where the progress of movie is shown, and scroll.
If this doesnt do it, then click on the bar once and then scroll.

### Post by wallaroo on 2012-08-19
You can also change it in preferences to default to position rather than volume.
Tools -> Preferences -> Hotkeys -> MouseWheel x-axis Control
