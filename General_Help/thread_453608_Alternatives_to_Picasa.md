### Post by fragos on 2007-05-25
I 1st tried F-Spot but found it's filing system hard to work with.  Every day has it's own folder which may work well for those that take occassional snap shots.  I'd much rather organize fotos by subject or job.  When I take stills for a movie production or a book illustration the work covers many days.  I've ended up using gThumb for management and some basic editing with Gimp brought out when required.  GThumb is missing the export functions of F-Spot to Picassa on the web, Gallery catalof or others.  The export does however become very clumsy because its difficult to control the order and grouping of photographs.
