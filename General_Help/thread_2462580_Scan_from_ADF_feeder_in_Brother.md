### Post by him610 on 2021-05-23
I have a Brother mfc-7360n that probably works about the same as your mfc-l3770cdw.
Here is what you should be able to do...
[LIST]
[*]Load pages to be scanned into feeder tray.
[*]Open Document Scanner (Simple Scan)
[*]Next to the Scan widget to the right is page-looking widget, click on it, select All Pages From Feeder, select Text or Image, click on Preferences widget, review or change Preferences to suit.
[*]Click Scan widget - all pages should be scanned.
[*]Click the download widget (arrow in box pointing down), give it a title and save to directory of choice.
[*]Inspect the saved document
[/LIST]
