### Post by Bakon Jarser on 2008-09-25
The point of LTS is that it is a stable version.  Doing things like upgrading the kernel and upgrading gnome could cause it to become unstable.  So the answer is no, no updates to the kernel or gnome will be issued by ubuntu.

However there is something called backports.  I don't think they would backport kernels or gnome, but other software packages could be kept up to date.

More info on backports here.

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/UbuntuBackports](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/UbuntuBackports)
