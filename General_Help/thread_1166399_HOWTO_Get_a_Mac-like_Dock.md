Hello.
Today, I'm going to tell you how to get a Mac-like Dock like in the picture shown below. The dock is called an AWN (Avant Windows Navigator). And I'll tell you how to get one Step by Step
1. keep this URL in mind, [https://launchpad.net/~awn-testing/+archive/ppa](https://launchpad.net/~awn-testing/+archive/ppa).
2. Open up Software Sources, System>Administration>Software Sources.
3. Click on the "Updates" tab and enable the "Recommended Updates" for your distribution. For example, if you're running Jaunty (9.04), then you would enable "Recommended Updates (jaunty-updates)". 
4. Click on the "Third-Party Software" tab. 
5. For each line from the gray box on the PPA page (the URL I mentioned earlier), copy it, click on the "Add" button in "Software Sources", paste the line into the new dialog box, and press OK. 
6. Back in the tab, make sure that both of the new entries are checked. 
7. Follow the instructions at the Launchpad PPA help page to add the PPA's key to your system. Note that they provide both a text and screencast version of the instructions. 
8. You can now close the Software Sources dialog.
9. Open the "Synaptic Package Manager", located in System>Administration> Synaptic Package Manager.
10. Click the "Reload" button. This will download the new Awn package information. 
11. Install the packages named above. 
12. Click on Avant Windows Navigator in Synaptic, and install it. 
That's it! Once it's done, go into Applications>Accessories>Avant Windows Navigator. Then click on it!
There you go, you now have a Mac-like Dock on your Ubuntu :p
Note: In order to do all of this, your Visual Effects must be on Normal or Expert. To do this, right-click on your desktop, click Change desktop background, then click the Visual Effects tab.
Thank you ):P
