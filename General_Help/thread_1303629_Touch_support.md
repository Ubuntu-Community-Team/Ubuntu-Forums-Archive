Jaunty recognized the n-trig stylus out of the box but not touch.  To set up in Jaunty or Karmic see:  [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?p=7863677#post7863677](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?p=7863677#post7863677)
