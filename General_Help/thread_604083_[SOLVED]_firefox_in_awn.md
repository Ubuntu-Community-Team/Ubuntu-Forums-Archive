### Post by stapler123 on 2007-11-05
fairly shure you can make a custam app launcher just like adding a launcher to the gnome pannel.

In the preferences of awn (right click on a blanks space on the dock on the left or right to get there) there is a "launchers" section

In that you can add a launcher
give it a name - firefox
and specify the command to run - firefox
It should even find the right icon for it.

You may have to restart awn for it to show up. I think it handles it a bit oddly too though, I can't get it to start multiple firefox's from the same icon.
