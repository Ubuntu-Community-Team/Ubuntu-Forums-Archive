### Post by ajgreeny on 2018-11-01
Portable, ie host powered USB disks sometimes have a double plugged USB cable meaning you can use one plug for the data and the second simply to provide more power for spinning the disk which is useful when trying to use it with a laptop.

I am not aware whether such cables are available as add-ons, separately from purchase of the disks but I imagine they can be found and may be worth trying in your case.

EDIT:
Yes, they are available and cost very little, so that's a good place to start, assuming your laptop has two USB ports close together.
