### Post by blueberry17 on 2007-06-12
Is there a way to change the names of applications, especially under the "Applications" menu? For instance, even though OpenOffice Calc is still Calc, I would like to change it to "Calcutta". How would I do not only this example, but for changing the names of other applications as well?:D

---

### Post by phollos on 2007-06-12
For KDE just mouse over what you want to change in the menu then right click > Edit Item > then change the name.

Not sure for gnome.

---

### Post by 67GTA on 2007-06-12
You can change the names in the menu by right clicking on the panel icon and selecting "edit menu". Then find the app, right click on it, select properties, and then type what you want. You can also change the icon.

### Post by Hendrixski on 2007-06-12

just click on system-->preferences-->main menu and then you can edit the layout and the names... you can change the name by clicking on properties
