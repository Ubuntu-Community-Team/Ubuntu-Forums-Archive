### Post by CMR_98823 on 2008-11-09
Hi, I've been noticing some very very slow update speeds from the title server, ca.archive.ubuntu.com.

I have fiber optics internet, and I typically get about 10kb/dl+ from bit torrent clients...so is there a way I could change which server to download from?

---

### Post by ciscosurfer on 2008-11-09
Go to System > Administration > Software Sources.  Click on Download From and choose Other.  Click Select Best Server.  When it completes, it will have chosen the server with the fastest ping response (the most accessible server).  Click Choose Server.  Click Close.  Click Reload.  You're done!
