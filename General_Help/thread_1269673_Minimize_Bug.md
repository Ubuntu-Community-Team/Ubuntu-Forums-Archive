### Post by Tamlynmac on 2009-09-18
Has the Window List applet been removed or deleted?

Right click on a blank area of you panel and select "ADD to PANEL"
Find the "Window List" applet and left click on the "ADD" button at the bottom. Close the window and you will note two new vertical lines in the panel. Move them (right click on the lines and select move) towards the left side of your panel and when you minimize an application it will show in the panel to the right of the vertical lines.

Good Luck and hope this helps.
