### Post by Kyle- on 2006-03-12
I have a Pentax *ist DS DSLR that works fine.

Most cameras made within the last three years connect to your computer via USB and will show up as an "mass storage" (UMS) device.  In other words, your camera is treated exactly like a USB thumb drive.  There's no need to spend 15 bucks on a card reader...most cameras have this function built in.  Just look in the manual or ask the salesperson.
