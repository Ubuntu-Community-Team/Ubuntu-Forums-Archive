### Post by 3rdalbum on 2013-04-21
> **jooge said:**
> I want to burn Django Unchained for a friend but the movie is 2hrs 45 mins long. Is there a burner that has a good overburn function?

"Overburn" is not what you want. An ordinary DVD can only hold 2 hours of video compressed in the "normal" mode of compression; with "overburn" turned on you'd only get a couple more minutes if you're lucky.

I haven't had any recent success burning from Brasero, but I have with Devede. It's very easy to use and it has a button you can press to automatically choose the right level of compression. You could put 3 hours on a DVD because Devede will compress the video more when necessary.
