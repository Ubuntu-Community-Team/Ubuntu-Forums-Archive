### Post by Agent26 on 2013-01-13
Hi there all, I have updated to Xubuntu 12.10 and have a problem with my lower panel.
I like to have my added items on the right that is the old default from previous versions but now all items automaticly go on the left. Ive been searching for ages now and can't see any way to change it. Can anybody help me please.
Thank you.

---

### Post by Max Blyss on 2013-01-13
Right click the panel, go to 'add items'.  Add a separator.  Right click on the separator and check the 'expand' option.  Then position Icons where you want.  Should push them to the right.
