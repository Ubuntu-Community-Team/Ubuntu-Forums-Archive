### Post by ean5533 on 2011-01-07
In general, when you want to know how well a game or other application will work in Wine, you should consult the [Wine App Database]("http://appdb.winehq.org/"). It's full of reviews of people like you and me who rate how well a program works and give tips on how to install things.

ME1 has pretty low ratings (bronze), and ME2 isn't much better (silver). You'll probably be able to get the games to run, but it'll be pretty hacky and won't be just out-of-the-box installation.

As far as syncing your account with Steam, as far as I know the only way to do that is to install the game from Steam itself (though I could be wrong). Steam **does** work on Wine, though there can be problems there as well.

Finally, assuming you DO get the two games to install, it shouldn't be hard for ME2 to import ME1 saves. As long as you didn't have to do anything too abnormal to get the games running, then I assume the import would be automatic.
