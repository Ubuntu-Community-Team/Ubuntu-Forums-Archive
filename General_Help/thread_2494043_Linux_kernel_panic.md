### Post by MAFoElffen on 2024-01-03
Welcome to Ubuntu Forums.

Ubuntu is great, sound and dependable. The support here in this community is unmatched. The members here are knowledgeable and helpful.

BUT... You have tried two other Linux distributions. One you seemed to have been running for almost a year... Now you are suddenly having problems.

I would highly suggest that you download and run Dell's SupportAssist Diagnostics and test your system. I would concentrate firs on testing your memory. Then check to see if your BIOS is up to date.

That is what I would recommend doing before trying any more distributions. Once you have confirmed that you have sound working hardware, then download a Flavor of Ubuntu, create an Installer LiveUSB and use "Try". Then, if it runs okay, run the 'system-info' script from my signature lie to let people know what they are recommending anything for. Upload the report and post the URL of where it uploads.
