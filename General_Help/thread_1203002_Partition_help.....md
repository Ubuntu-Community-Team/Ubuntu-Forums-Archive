### Post by Denis on 2009-07-03
I'm not sure what you are trying to do. I assume you want to have only Ubuntu on your computer (no Windows) and copy the system you have in Wubi. 

In the [Wubi FAQ]("http://wubi-installer.org/faq.php#use") I have read the following: 
> 
Can I move my virtual disk file to a dedicated partition?

You can use [LVPM]("http://lubi.sourceforge.net/lvpm.html") to transfer your installation. A guide and support forum for LVPM is available [here]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=438591").


LVPM copies your Wubi Ubuntu system to a real partition. I think this is what you want. The following guide should give you all the information: 

[http://lubi.sourceforge.net/lvpm.html]("http://wubi-installer.org/faq.php#use")
