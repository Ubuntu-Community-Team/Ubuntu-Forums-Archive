### Post by gwaar on 2008-07-21
Your windows driver CD can't be used with Ubuntu, but there are several drivers in the repositories depending on which type of tablet you have. Simply plugging in should work with a Wacom or Aiptek tablet, though Aiptek tablets are known to have problems with pressure sensitivity. There's a walk through if you have a Wacom tablet [here]("http://ph.ubuntuforums.com/showthread.php?t=765915") , if you've plugged it in to no avail.
As for handwriting recognition, I can't help you much there, but google's your friend.
