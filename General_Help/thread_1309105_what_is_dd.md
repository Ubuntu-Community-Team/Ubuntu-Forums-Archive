### Post by Oblivion_4 on 2009-11-01
dd is used to burn bootable media. It can be used to burn an .ISO to a DVD or CD creating a bootable CD or DVD. It can also be used to flash a bootable operating system with filename ending in .img to a flash drive of some sort. I recently used dd to create a bootable usb of ubuntu netbook remix. Its pretty awesome.
