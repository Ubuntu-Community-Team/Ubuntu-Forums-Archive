### Post by Tibuda on 2009-02-11
> **oldcelt said:**

You should not download anything, but use your package manager. Open Applications > Add/remove (if you use Gnome, the default), and search for Thunderbird there. You can find more info about installing programs in Ubuntu here: [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/InstallingSoftware](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/InstallingSoftware)
That's a lot more intuitive than Download installer -> Double click installer -> click next -> agree to license without reading it -> click next -> click next -> click next -> click next -> click next -> click next -> click done
