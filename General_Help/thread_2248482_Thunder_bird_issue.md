### Post by Cbhihe on 2014-10-15
Have you recently transfered all or part of your email files from one installation of Tb to another ? Your email files will include:
Inbox, Inbox.msf, Sent, Sent.msf, Trash, Trash.msf, etc...
They are located in a folder of your choice, defined in the setting panel of each Tb email account you may have.

Transfer can be from one computer to another, or more simply from one directory to another.
 Sometimes, by doing so, Tb will read the new email directory as corrupted. Some email will seem to be missing although the corresponding files are well in place or email bodies will be mixed with other emails' subject lines, just as you described.  

This does not seem to be a lx specific problem. You might be better off posting for help on [https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/get-community-support](https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/get-community-support).
