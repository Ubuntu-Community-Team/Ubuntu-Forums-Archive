### Post by badfish591 on 2008-04-09
the easiest way to install blender is probably through the synaptic package manager. I would just open that up and do a search for "blender", and check off the boxes you want, click apply, and it should resolve your dependancies and you'll end up with a launch icon in your applications menubar under graphics.
