### Post by SchnappiJedi on 2013-07-17
At minimum just about any motherboard should work with a 32bit Ubuntu version. I recently installed 12.04.2 LTS on a 10+ year old Gateway. Not sure what kind of processor/motherboard it had but I do remember it had an IDE drive (as oppossed to SATA), so it was probably more dated than anything you are looking at.

Bottom line you may have to go with a 32bit version but you should be able to use Ubuntu version 13.04 (newest) if you wanted to do so.
