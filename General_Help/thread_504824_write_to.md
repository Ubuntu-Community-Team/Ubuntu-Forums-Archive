### Post by brent113 on 2007-07-19
you'll need root access.  Open a terminal (applications->accessories>Terminal) and type sudo nautilus.  It will prompt you for your password.  Enter the password of YOUR username.  Hit enter, then be careful, you have full access to modify everything on your system.
