### Post by noneofthem on 2006-12-03
The easiest way to do so is by doing this:

1. go to [www.getautomatix.com](www.getautomatix.com)
2. go to install/download
3. follow the instructions for your release of Ubuntu
4. once automatix is installed just open it from your menu
5. you will need to enter your password to use that software as it will temporarily change your sources list
6. select Realplayer (and any other thing you might need) and click "start"
7. Make yourself a coffee and wait until it's done.
Now you will be able to use Realplayer from the menu.

Hope that helps...
