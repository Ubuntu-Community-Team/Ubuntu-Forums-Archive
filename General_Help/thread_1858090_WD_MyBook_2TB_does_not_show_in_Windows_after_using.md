MyBook 2TB does not show in Windows after using on Linux

### Post by Mark Phelps on 2011-10-12
These kinds of problems most often occur when folks have external drives formatted NTFS (Windows) and simply unplug the drive. But, you did't do that; instead, you said you safely removed the drive. That should not have done anything to it.

What you can try, on your Win7 PC, is after you connect the drive, if you don't get an indication in the system tray that the drive has been connected, then open Disk Management and scroll down through the list of drives.  If it shows up in the list but doesn't have a drive letter, see if their is an option to assign a letter (effectively, mounting the drive).
