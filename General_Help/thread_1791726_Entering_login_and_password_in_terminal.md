### Post by WorMzy on 2011-06-27
Your keypresses are registering, but there's no visual feedback. This is the intended behaviour of sudo. Just type your password and press enter.

Be aware though, that apt-get/aptitude use the same software sources as synaptic and the Software Centre. If the software you want isn't available in one, it won't be available in any of the others.
