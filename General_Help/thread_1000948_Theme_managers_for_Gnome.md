### Post by Izek on 2008-12-03
Well, Gnome isn't a "theme manager," it's a desktop environment. Themes are managed by window managers (IE: xfwm4 and metacity), GTK and QT I believe. Another window manager is Openbox, you can get themes for openbox over at [http://www.box-look.org/](http://www.box-look.org/)
