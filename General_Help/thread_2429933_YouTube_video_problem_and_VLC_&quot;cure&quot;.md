### Post by steviejonesie on 2019-10-25
Hiyall
After a lifetime of misery with MS and its updates choking my PC to death I finally switched exclusively to Ubuntu OS. And loving it...
Except for video playback in YouTube. (Only in YT by the way). It's jerky and pauses for 2 seconds every minute or so and there doesn't appear to be a cure on the forums here.
(Or at least one I can find/understand).
HOWEVER, I found a work-around that works for me.
Copy and paste the YouTube video URL into VLC drop-down tab "media" - "open network stream" and hit Play. Hey presto, seamless hi quality video. And as far as I can tell to date, no ads.
It might be a pain in the **** if you're watching 3 minute pop videos all day but I'm into hour long+ podcasts and its no bother to me.
Happy days.
