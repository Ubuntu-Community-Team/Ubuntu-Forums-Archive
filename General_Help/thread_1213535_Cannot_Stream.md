### Post by tommcd on 2009-07-16
Well, I can stream videos on hulu.com in Firefox with flash with no problem on Ubuntu 9.04. You must have something set up incorrectly.
Did you try just going to hulu.com in firefox and streaming the videos directly in firefox??
In firefox type: 
about:plugins (that is: about<colon>plugins, with no spaces in between). 
in the url address bar and hit enter. You should see a plugin listed for "Shockwave Flash" in the list.
