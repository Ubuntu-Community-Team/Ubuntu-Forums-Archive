### Post by Yumi on 2007-11-17
I tried various suggestions on the forum without success. It took me a long time and I even tried to edit /etc/X11/xorg.conf.

Finally I established that I bought a new Monitor cable, which for some reason does not allow the computer to determine the type of monitor used. In my case a hardware problem causes Gutsy to fail. I got stuck in a resolution of 1024 x760, the monitor was determined as "Default". 

In Gutsy it is easy to change this manually in "System - Administration - Screens and Graphics". Just select your Monitor from the List and all is well 

Hope this helps somebody else.
