### Post by bcbc on 2012-03-19
Why do you have 'wubi' in your thread title? You don't have a wubi install.

Don't try to install grub to your windows partition /dev/sdc1 - it will overwrite the windows boot sector and stop windows booting. 

From what I can see you are running the boot repair... why don't you ask Yannbuntu for advice on this: [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1769482](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1769482)
