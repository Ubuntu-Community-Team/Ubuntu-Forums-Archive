### Post by Guille Damke on 2009-02-15
It is a problem of skype itself, you have to choose the right output in options>sound in skype. If you are running Ubuntu 8.10, then "pulse" for "sound out" and "ringing" should make it. For input, if you have an intel card, one of the HDA_Intel (something like "hw:Intel,0") options will make.Fix sound playing the test_soundtry then, the sound input doing the test call.
good luck.
