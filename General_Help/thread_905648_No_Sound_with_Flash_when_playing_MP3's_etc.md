### Post by mb_webguy on 2008-08-30
Check the links in my signature.  This is a well-known incompatibility between Flash and PulseAudio.  You can install the libflashsupport package, which somewhat patches this incompatibility, but the best solution is probably to install Flash 10 from the Intrepid repositories.

Also, you're likely to encounter this issue with some other combinations of programs, so you may consider looking at the PulseAudio fixes link as well.
