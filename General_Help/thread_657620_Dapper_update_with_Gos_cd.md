### Post by cmost on 2008-01-03
What exactly do you mean by "update"?  Are you intending to overwrite Dapper with gOS and save your home directory?  That's possible.  As to "upgrading" Dapper to gOS, that might prove disastrous as they're two different OS versions.  While gOS is based on Ubuntu, it's a highly customized derivative.
