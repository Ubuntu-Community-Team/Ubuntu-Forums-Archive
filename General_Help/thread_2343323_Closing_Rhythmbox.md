### Post by wildmanne39 on 2016-11-15
Rhythmbox will keep playing when minimized if you already have something playing when you click on the "X". You have to go to the menu and close it or stop the sound file first.

### Post by deadflowr on 2016-11-15
You click on the play/pause button, then close the window and it'll stop and shutdown rhythmbox.
