### Post by le1 on 2009-11-30
Install PulseAudio Volume Control (pavucontrol).

Start it up and go to "Recording" tab, 

now run "Skype Test Call" and you will see that another "Gnome volume Control Dialog" will appear, 

choose your device (if you don't know what is what try to talk all the time and keep on switching available options) 

when the call is over you'll hear yourself and smile, enjoy.
