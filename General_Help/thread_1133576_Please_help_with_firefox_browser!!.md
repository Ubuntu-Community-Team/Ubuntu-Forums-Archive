### Post by Jimmyfj on 2009-04-23
> **bigswagg said:**
> Nope that doesnt work. It just makes everything way to small and unreadable. Any other suggestions? :(


Are you sure that you installed the driver for your graphics card? You can do that in System>Administration>Hardware drivers. Be sure to install the recommended driver while your graphics might crash if you pick the wrong driver. After driver install, and a reboot you should be able to set your screen resolution higher, which seems to be the problem you have.

If you check your screen resolution I'd bet it says 800x600 or so.

After driver install go to System>Administration and look for nVidia X Server Settings, if your graphics card is an nVidia based card, and Programs>System Tools>ATI Catalyst Control Center - In either one you can set your screen resolution.
