### Post by Ramses de Norre on 2006-06-09
Easy pseudo-transparency can be enabled in gnome-terminal settings, for real transparency you'll need xcompmgr or xgl. xcompmgr can be installed by aptitude and you just run xcompmgr to start it but it's a bit buggy sometimes and isn't developed any further I think.
There are many guides about xgl on the forums.
