### Post by sonoma on 2006-11-28
For those who are used to Windows, make sure you don't right click then immediately left click on "Open With", there's no provision there for setting your file type associations.

Just like 23meg said, right click, then left click on "Properties" then click on the tab that says "Open With" then click your radio button for whatever program you want (or add a new one here) and click "Close". It will be changed permanently at that point.
