ubuntu is amazing.

You can upgrade to the newest version of Compiz-Fusion by opening System->Administration->Software Sources, opening the "Third-Party Software" tab, clicking the Add button, and entering the line "deb [http://ppa.launchpad.net/compiz/ubuntu](http://ppa.launchpad.net/compiz/ubuntu) hardy main". You'll need to restart X using Ctrl-Alt-Backspace before you can use the new plugins (of which the most notable one is the Cube Reflection and Deformation plugin, which is how you get the cylinder and sphere effects).

### Post by mb_webguy on 2008-09-10
The pictures you can use for skydome are probably limited to certain filetypes and possibly sizes.   I'm not sure.  I don't know why the skydome would look faded.  

As for the desktop sphere... First, you should have "Sphere" selected for the Deformation on the Deformation tab of the Cube Reflection and Deformation plugin.  Also, in the Sphere section, make sure "Deform Caps" is checked.  You may have to change some of the Appearance settings on the Cube Caps tab for them to look right.  You can change the shape of the sphere with the Aspect Ratio slider -- a setting of 1 is perfectly spherical, while less than 1 is flatter.

If the desktop sphere takes up too much of the screen, go to the Rotate Cube plugin and move the Zoom slider on the General tab to zoom in or out.

### Post by mb_webguy on 2008-09-11
It seems like I had a problem with this at first, as well.  I don't recall exactly what I did to remove the caps, but there are only a couple of places that let you modify the appearance and behavior of the cube caps.

First, go to the Cube Reflection and Deformation plugin, the Cube Caps tab, and make sure Draw Top Face and Draw Bottom Face are not checked.  Next go to the Desktop Cube plugin, the Appearance tab, and click on the button out to the side of Cube Color, and move the Opacity slider all of the way to the left to make the cube transparent (which will not affect any wallpapers you have, only the color of the cube itself).  Also on the Appearance tab, make sure there are no images listed in the Cube Caps Image Files area.
