### Post by 3rdalbum on 2011-05-27
The instructions you're trying to follow are quite old; you actually couldn't even follow them on Ubuntu 10.10.

Instead, use the instructions for "Karmic and above" - they will work on Ubuntu 9.10 right up to 11.04.

With any new version of Ubuntu, old tutorials stop being relevant. It's the price of rapid progress. In fact, I think my own signature is outdated now! I'd better change it.

If you want to get to the Software Sources, you can access it through the Ubuntu Software Center (click the Ubuntu logo and start typing 'software'), then go up to the menubar at the top of the screen and go to Edit and Software Sources.
