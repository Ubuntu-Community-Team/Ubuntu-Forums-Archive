Yes... I am brand new. I have a couple of applications that I have installed (usually from tar.bz2 archives) and the applications work fine. ### Post by argail1980 on 2008-07-24
just right-click on these starters, choose properties and click on the icon field. Then you should be able to choose an icon from the list or open a location where to find the application's icons
you have to select the folder the icons are in, the icon files themselves are not shown in the dialoque.

### Post by Joe88 on 2008-07-24
In the launchers properties window just click on the icons picture, this will bring up a window where you will be able to find and select the icon you want to use.

To edit the launchers in the Ubuntu menu right click on it then left click edit menus. Then like the launchers on your desktop or in its panel, you can edit the menus launchers by right clicking on them and then left clicking properties.
