### Post by Tony Flury on 2010-04-01
I am not going to help you with your homework assignment - noone here will - but it might not be a bad idea to start by thinking of what the challenges might be in an OS which has to handle multiple processes running simultaneously - solving those challenges is what the range of techniques known as  Process Control management is intended to do - as a concept - as all muktitasking OSes will do something like that (even Windows).

I might suggest that you look again through your course notes - as i can't imagine that you have been given an assignment without the main topic being taught, at least at an introductory level.

If you have no notes - think about this : identify the key things that an OS has to do when running multiple processes simultaneously - that might lead you down the route of some key items which can specifically do Google searches on in terms of the strategies that Linux uses to implement that key functionality.
