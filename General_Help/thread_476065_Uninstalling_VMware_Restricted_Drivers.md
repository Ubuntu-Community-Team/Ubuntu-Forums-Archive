### Post by enortham on 2007-06-16
I uninstalled VMware Player the other day via Add/Remove Applications but just realized that the restricted drivers are still installed and enabled. The following appear in Restricted Drivers:

VMware Virtual Machine Monitor
VMware Virtual Networking Driver

How do I uninstall them?

Eric

---

### Post by yabbadabbadont on 2007-06-16
In Gnome, System->Administration->Synaptic Package Manager.  Search for vmware.  Right-click and choose complete removal on any of them that are still installed.

### Post by enortham on 2007-06-16
