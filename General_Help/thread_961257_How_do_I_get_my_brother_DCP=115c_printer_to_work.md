### Post by wizard10000 on 2008-10-28
Brother's Linux drivers are available here -

[http://solutions.brother.com/linux/sol/printer/linux/cups_drivers.html](http://solutions.brother.com/linux/sol/printer/linux/cups_drivers.html)

I don't think you'll find a driver for the 115c but Brother says to use the MFC-210C driver instead.

Good luck -
