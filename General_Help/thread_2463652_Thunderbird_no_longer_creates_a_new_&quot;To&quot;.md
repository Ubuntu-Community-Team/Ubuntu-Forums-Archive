### Post by TheFu on 2021-06-15
The current TB uses a single-line for multiple TO entries. After <enter> or <tab> are pressed when the first entry is done, just start typing for the 2nd, 3rd, 4th, entries.  Makes it much easier to select/paste many different email addresses now that there's only 1 textentry field.

They've simplified the GUI while retaining functionality.
