### Post by ajgreeny on 2011-02-26
Audacity will do it, but it first decodes then re-encodes the mp3 file resulting in a small but possibly noticeable quality reduction.

Have a look at mp3splt, which can do it with no encoding needed.  It's a cli app but very easy to use with time and length figures for the chunks needed.  I use it a lot.

Also while looking at that, see mp3wrap and mp3gain to join mp3s and increase volume.
