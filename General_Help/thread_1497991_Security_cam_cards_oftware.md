### Post by sedawk on 2010-05-31
You can try zoneminder which is part of Ubuntu and seems
to work with lots of video equipment. I tried it with two USB
webcams which basically work with Linux but not with zoneminder.

More info about support hardware and stuff at
[www.zoneminder.com](www.zoneminder.com)
You can also find some reports on the web from guys who use it
with "multi cam" hardware.
