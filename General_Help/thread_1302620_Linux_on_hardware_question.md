### Post by Giblet5 on 2009-10-27
Buy the book "*Building Embedded Linux Systems*" by O'Reilly and associates. ISBN-978-0-596-52968-0

Then you should apply for patent on any clever ideas you have developed.

Then talk to a hardware geek or two and get recommendations for an appropriate CPU and I/O architecture. Design the first circuit and prototype it. Load your software and test it.

How's that for a little nudge? ;)
