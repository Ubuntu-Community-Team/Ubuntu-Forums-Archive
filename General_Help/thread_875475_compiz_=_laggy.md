### Post by voteforpedro36 on 2008-07-30
No, NVidia has drivers for Linux, although they aren't "free", although they cost nothing. However, I have the 9100 card (don't know the difference between the 2, but), and I had the same problem. I know it's a stupid suggestion, but I downgraded to Ubuntu 7.10 (Gutsy), and things run really smooth for me.

If you don't like to do that (I couldn't blame you), try either [this]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/BinaryDriverHowto/ATI") or [this]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RadeonDriver"), but not at the same time. For me, the first one didn't work (when the driver did load, the resolution was off), and the second one didn't help the problem whatsoever.
