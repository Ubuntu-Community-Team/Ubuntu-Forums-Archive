### Post by David D. on 2019-03-25
There are a couple of things you can look at to see if some preference selections have changed.

Under Edit > Edit Preferences make sure that "Clear article cache on shutdown" is not selected.

Again, under Edit > Edit Preferences see if "Mark entire group read when leaving group" is selected.  If it is and you have "Match only unread articles" selected on the toolbar, the article headers other than those downloaded in that session will not be visible.
