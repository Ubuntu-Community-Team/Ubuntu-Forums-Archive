### Post by PsTJsT on 2006-02-06
You may have worked this out already, but in case someone else encounters the same irritating issue...

The admin mode button is there, it's just that the way the window is drawn on the desktop it's sometimes not visible (it's below the bottom of the screen).  To see the admin button (starting from scratch):

K Menu > System Settings > Network Settings.

Hide the kicker panel (sometimes this is necessary, sometimes it's not).

Grab the title bar of the Network Settings window and move the window down a couple of inches.  After you've moved it down, maximize it and you should see 5 buttons at the bottom of the window: "Help" and "Defaults" should be on the bottom left; "Administrator Mode," "Reset, and "Apply" should be on the bottom right.
