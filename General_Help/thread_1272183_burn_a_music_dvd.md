### Post by directhex on 2009-09-21
Audio CD doesn't contain any files or have any filesystem. There's no valid way to define an "Audio DVD" as a big Audio CD

You could do a new data project and transcode all your files onto it as WAV, if your playback device can deal with that, but audio CD definitely is NOT just a directory filled with .wav files
