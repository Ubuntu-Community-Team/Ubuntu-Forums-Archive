### Post by sgosnell on 2010-10-13
What I do is have mail from my gmail accounts forwarded to one account, so all I have to have open is one, and I get mail from all of the accounts.  Gmail also lets you send mail from any of your Gmail accounts from any of the others, and it defaults to replying via the account which originally received it.  I've never even tried to keep multiple Gmail accounts open at the same time, so I can't really offer anything further.  There are at least two Gmail notifiers available, but you would have to try them, or at least read the docs, to see if they will work for multiple accounts at the same time.

### Post by WorMzy on 2010-10-13
Dunno if it's any use to you, but I use [Gmail Checker]("http://winged.info/") for Firefox. It can log into multiple Gmail accounts, alerts you if any of them receive a message, lets you preview the message, and lets you go straight to the message.
