### Post by Paanini on 2010-11-29
Btw I've found a temporary workaround for it :
If you want to have a workspace switcher at the bottom of your screen anyway, 
1. Add a bottom panel (Right click on the top one and "add new panel"
A new panel will appear at the bottom, and will partially hide your dock. 
2. Right click on the new panel and uncheck 'Expand', 'Autohide' and 'Show Hide Buttons'
3. Now drag the panel to the bottom right of your screen.
4.Right click on the panel, select "Add to panel", ad select workspace switcher.

You can make the background for the panel completely transparent if you like, but the Panel will be too small for such a change to be perceptible, so it won't really matter.
I'm assuming your dock doesn't spread across the length of your screen, so this should work as a temp fix, putting the workspace switcher back to where it originally was :)
