### Post by Dennis N on 2017-05-25
Don't think you can upgrade Trusty version of Rhythmbox to 3.4 because of dependency problems. 3.4 depends on versions of some common libraries not present in Trusty. R.B. 3.4 is used in 16.10 and later. Upgrade your OS to 17.04 would be a solution.

Won't rip with Rhythmbox? Try a dedicated ripper: asunder, or ripperx are popular.
