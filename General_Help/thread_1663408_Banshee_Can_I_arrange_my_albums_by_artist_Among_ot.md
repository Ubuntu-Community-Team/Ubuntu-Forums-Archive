### Post by ufugu on 2011-01-10
Ditto. I would love to use Banshee because of some of it's awesome features, except that you can't browse/filter by genre.

I wave way too much music to lose an entire hierarchy level-it's a deal breaker for me.  I have seen dozens of similar comments over the internet.  Sadly, this seems to be such a low priority for the developers that they are likely to implement support for the Newton long before we see genre browsing in Banshee...

Edit: FWIW (and I know this was only one element of your post) there's a bug for genre browsing: [https://bugzilla.gnome.org/show_bug.cgi?id=540873](https://bugzilla.gnome.org/show_bug.cgi?id=540873)

The Banshee page on Gnome.org says genre browsing will be available as a community extension in 1.9.1.  As far as I can tell though, this is not the case.  No updates on Newton support, so all bets are off.
