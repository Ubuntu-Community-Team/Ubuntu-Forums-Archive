### Post by marsbt on 2007-07-10
How did you make it to go to sleep? 

There are two kinds of sleep modes actually: suspend to RAM = sleep, suspend to HDD = hibernate. If the PC is in suspend to RAM mode, it should wake with any keystroke or mouse movement. Hibernate waking needs a simple push at the power button (the push shouldn't last more than a few milliseconds though or it could do a hard shutdown).
