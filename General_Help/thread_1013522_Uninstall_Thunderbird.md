### Post by jessdog9001 on 2008-12-17
To uninstall Thunderbird without using the command line, go to Applications->Add/Remove. In the upper right hand corner, type in Thunderbird. Then uncheck the box next to Mozilla Thunderbird Mail/News, and click Apply Changes, then Apply.
