### Post by ashimono on 2009-01-17
I am not an experienced user, and lately my synergy stopped workind, and I could not find out why. I was using the quickSynergy, and it simply wouldn't connect. After some time trying, I finally found out what happened: I made an update on the libgccs and kernel, and synergy was compiled for the old kernel.

I had to uninstall it and install again via synaptics (actually, a typical windows user maneuver, haha).

Just posted it to warn about possible causes of malfunctioning and spare some time.

See ya.
