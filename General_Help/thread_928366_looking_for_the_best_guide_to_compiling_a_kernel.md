### Post by xeth_delta on 2008-09-23
> **CoffeeBreath said:**
> I would like to recompile the one I have already as well as combine anew one. Can you give me the best guides for either?

Here are a couple of resources:
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Kernel/Compile](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Kernel/Compile)

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Kernel?action=show&redirect=KernelHowto](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Kernel?action=show&redirect=KernelHowto)

Though not necessary to do, one can learn a lot from compiling the kernel and reading the configuration options. Besides, one can obtain a leaner one this way.

Just remember not to erase your current kernel, so that, in case the new one does not work, you can boot the previous one.

Good luck, don't hesitate to ask if you have questions.
