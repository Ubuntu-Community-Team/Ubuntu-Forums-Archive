Trying to have only a select few pages in OpenOffice be split into two columns. If you want to set it up, you go to Format > Columns and set up how many columns you want. However, I would like to have, say, the very first page be without columns. And the last five pages to also be column free.

How can I do this?

### Post by Dirjel on 2009-03-15
I think you're on the wrong forum, but w/e.

I did some experimentation, and what you need to do is insert --> section at the beginning of your document, and make that part two columns.  Then just click outside of it, and continue typing on the single column rest-of-the-document.  Hope that helps.
