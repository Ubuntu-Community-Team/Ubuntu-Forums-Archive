### Post by snowpine on 2009-04-04
Hi Bobbin, the Dell version of Ubuntu is a separate version from "regular" Ubuntu. You can't upgrade it to 8.10 or 9.04, or use the standard repositories. A lot of Dell Mini users are replacing the Dell version with a fresh install of 8.10 or 9.04 beta--check out the forums at mydellmini.com for some tips and tricks.
