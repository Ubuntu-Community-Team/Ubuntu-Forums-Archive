### Post by David_UK on 2010-02-22
No question.  Just documenting this so others can find it.

After a fresh install of 9.04, the display was shifted about 5mm to the right.  As I'm dual-booting I didn't want to simply use the auto-align function on the monitor.
I changed the screen refresh rate from 60Hz up to 70Hz.  This realigned correctly, but text did not display properly.  I then tried 75Hz, but it was worse.
However, when I changed it back to 60Hz, a pop-up window appeared asking me if I wanted to change something (soz, can't remember what - something to do with virtual resolution, I think).  I clicked on 'yes' as was told to log out and back in - after that the display was correct - alignment and all.
