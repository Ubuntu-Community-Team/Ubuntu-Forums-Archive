### Post by jerenept on 2010-08-07
Open System>Administration>Software Sources.

Enter your password. now de-select "CD with Ubuntu 10.04 Lucid Lynx"

I think that will help you.

### Post by happyhamster on 2010-08-07
Go to System-->Administration-->Software Sources and make sure there's nothing ticked in the "Installable from CD-ROM/DVD" section. Good luck.

### Post by oldfred on 2010-08-07
When you click on software sources, in System, Administration do you have to CD checkbox checked?

You should update source to 'best' server for your locale. In the download from combo box choose other and ask it to Select Best Server.
