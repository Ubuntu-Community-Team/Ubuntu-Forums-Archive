### Post by max-power2717 on 2010-02-17
Hi Lars,

Well done mate! Great idea for a site. If I have it right, your intention for the site is to record the things you learn about Ubuntu as you go. This is a great idea, not only for your own learning (writing the events and solutions down as you go reinforces what you've learned,) but for anyone else who has the same problems and happens to find your solutions. 

The page layout looks clean and consistent, that's important. Nicely done. 

One area you might want to give consideration to is the structure of your content. The menu along the top seems to have a section dedicated to a bunch of things you've done to improve the appearance of Ubuntu (good), and a section dedicated to a single problem you had with a flash driver, and another section dedicated to a solution you found to make a SAMBA share. What about when you write about another solution you found to a problem that is unrelated to any of those matters (say, you decided to get a bunch of windows applications to run with WINE)... where will you put those pages? Another section? I think you need to give that some thought and planning before you create too many pages otherwise, when you try to clean the site up later with lots of content, you will have a massive task on your hands. In the early days this sort of plans are much easier executed. 

One thing that struck me straight away is the grammar / spelling / language issues. Not everyone is as critical as I am about these issues, but when I see someone using 'u' instead of you, and '2' instead of to, or if they're just misspelling words (purposefully or accidentally) such as "lokated", "kontact" or "apear" immediately my respect for the page begins to fall. Then, the content of the page has to be of higher quality for me to remain. That's just my opinion though; and I should declare that English is the only language I know (I have sympathy and respect for people who have to learn English so they can communicate with philistines like myself). You may or may not agree with my opinion on writing style, but that would another area I'd suggest you work on.

However, I don't mean to pick on your site. I think it's a great idea and I say good on you for the contribution. Very worth while site to be building I reckon. Good luck with it!

Regards,
Max.
