### Post by r7av7en7 on 2010-07-31
I recently picked up an Asus 1005HAB netbook from my local retailer, I was happy to see that mostly everything worked out of the box with 10.04. However, I cannot stand touchpads and always hit the button to disable it and connect a wireless usb mouse. 

I managed to get the fn keys working perfectly by following [http://www.webupd8.org/2010/05/enable-fn-keys-on-your-asus-eeepc-and.html](http://www.webupd8.org/2010/05/enable-fn-keys-on-your-asus-eeepc-and.html). I immediately disabled the touchpad by the fn key and went about my way. I kept noticing however that the touchpad seemed to be reactivating itself. I toyed with it more and finally found the solution. Within System>Preferences>Mouse you must uncheck "Disable touchpad while typing". It seems even with the touchpad disabled, every time you type it reactivates it when you are done. Since unchecking this option I've not had the problem again.

Not sure if this is applicable to any other models of EEEPC or other manufacturers but this worked like a charm for me.
