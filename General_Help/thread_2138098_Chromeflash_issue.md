### Post by deadflowr on 2013-04-23
It's most likely pepperflash.
That's the plugin/program/whatever chrome uses to load their built-in flash plugin.
If you open chrome click the tab and enter
```
chrome://plugins
```
Then click the details icon at the top right.
Then scroll the list till you find the flash part and see, if you've installed flash on your system, two versions.
The first version is the pepperflash chrome version. As of now 11.7.somethng.
The second would be the flash installed on your system. It's what you'd be running on firefox.
Click the disable link on the first one and then load a flash content and see how it plays.
