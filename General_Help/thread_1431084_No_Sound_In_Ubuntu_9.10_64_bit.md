### Post by audiomick on 2010-03-16
First thing is to make sure nothing is muted. I am not sure what the possibilities on a default 9.10 are, but look for things like a volume control in the task bar and /or a sound mixer / control panel in applications> accessories or in applications> multi-media or somewhere like that.

I have seen several posts indicating that the sound was muted by default for some reason just after an install.

Hope that helps.
