### Post by pointone on 2008-01-20
Simply pulling the device out without unmounting will cause data loss or corruption. Some data is not written until the drive is unmounted. To avoid this, you could turn off all caching / buffering, but this would probably degrade performance a great deal, and would still not guarantee data integrity. There are also several automount programs available that will unmount the device after a period of activity, but again, these do not guarantee anything.

Easiest solution is to just unmount the device.
