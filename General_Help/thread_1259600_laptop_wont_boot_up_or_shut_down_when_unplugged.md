### Post by brabender on 2009-09-07
Press and hold any key when the progress bar stops; it works with my Compaq V6610 and with other HP and Compaq laptops, apparently due to a buggy DSDT file. Check this thread: [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1153966](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1153966)
