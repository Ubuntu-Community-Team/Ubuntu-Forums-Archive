### Post by Jagot on 2006-07-02
Go to System -> Administration -> Printing.

Now click 'New Printer'.  Your printer should show up when you select local printer.  Make sure your printer is higlighted and click 'Forward'.  Now you just need to select the correct manufacturer and the model and click 'Forward' again and it's installed.  I've checked and your model is on the supported list.
