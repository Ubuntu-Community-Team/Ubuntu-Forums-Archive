### Post by fredbird67 on 2008-11-22
I created a Christmas-ish GTK theme here while back called "Yulebuntu" and posted it to [GNOME-Look.org]("http://www.gnome-look.org"), which also automatically shows up on [Xfce-Look.org]("http://www.xfce-look.org") too, since GNOME and Xfce both use GTK themes.

The direct URL to the latest revision of my "Yulebuntu" theme can be found [here]("http://www.ubuntu-art.org/content/show.php?content=92501").

I've also stated what Metacity theme and icons I recommend on that same page, too.  I haven't included a particular wallpaper though, because my personal favorite is copyrighted, and in fact, hasn't been available on the web for years.  Therefore, you're on your own for wallpaper, but other Christmas wallpapers are very plentiful on the web.

Enjoy! :-)

Fred in St. Louis, MO USA
