### Post by nad on 2005-04-30
You could do this either way, ubuntu -> exPee or exPee -> ubuntu. You will need a second ethernet adapter in the server computer and do some system configuration.

To tell you the truth, it is easier to just purchase an ethernet hub, about $20-30, and plug both computers into it. You also won't need both computers on when you wish to use the second one.
