### Post by amitst on 2007-04-10
I was playing with my Ubuntu panel and right-clicked and removed the icon of Gaim from the panel. Now re-logging on to Gaim doesn't bring the system tray icon back. :confused: I also checked the Tools-> Plugins -> System tray icons. But the icon is totally lost. :( 

Closing the Gaim window now also closes the application.

What can i do?

---

### Post by mcduck on 2007-04-10
You probably removed the Notification Area applet from your panel. Right-click on any empty space in the panel, select 'Add to Panel', find 'Notification Area' and drag&drop it to your panel.

---

### Post by amitst on 2007-04-10
Yes, Adding the notification area solved my issue. Thanks a lot. :KS
