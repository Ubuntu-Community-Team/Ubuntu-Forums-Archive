### Post by aysiu on 2006-10-19
I just delete the Totem plugins.

Note: I didn't say *uninstall*. I said *delete*. And not Totem itself, just the plugins.

P.S. Removing *ubuntu-desktop* just makes upgrade transitions unofficial (and more likely to break)--i.e., from Breezy to Dapper or Dapper to Edgy.

If you don't care about that, you can remove *ubuntu-desktop*. It's not a real package, just a pointer.
