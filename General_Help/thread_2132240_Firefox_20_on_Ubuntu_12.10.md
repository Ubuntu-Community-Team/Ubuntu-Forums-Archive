### Post by The Spectre on 2013-04-04
That PPA is for the Firfox BETA.

[https://launchpad.net/~mozillateam/+archive/firefox-next](https://launchpad.net/~mozillateam/+archive/firefox-next)

It usually takes a couple of days before it shows up in the Ubuntu Software Updater.

There is a good chance that the last BETA ([FONT=Ubuntu, Bitstream Vera Sans, DejaVu Sans, Tahoma, sans-serif][COLOR=#333333]20.0~b7+build1-0ubuntu0.) is the same as the Final of Firefox v20.0 but it is probably better to wait for it to show up [/COLOR][/FONT]through[FONT=Ubuntu, Bitstream Vera Sans, DejaVu Sans, Tahoma, sans-serif][COLOR=#333333] the regular channels.[/COLOR][/FONT]
