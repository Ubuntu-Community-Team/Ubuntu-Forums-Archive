### Post by Autodave on 2017-10-26
Funny that you posted this now. I just had the same problem with mom's computer. So, I took over a mouse and tried it. Same thing. So, I slid the desktop off of the desk and onto the floor. (I was going to pull the side cover and have a peak for loose connections, etc.) As I pulled the running PC off of the desk, the mouse that I had just plugged in fell to the floor and landed upside down. As it laid there, the red laser light would go off for a second and then back on for a few seconds!

What it came down to was an overheating power supply. I took the PSU apart, cleaned it with compressed air, put it back together again and all is well.
