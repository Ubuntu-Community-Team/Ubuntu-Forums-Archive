### Post by Kevbert on 2008-05-30
Xubuntu 8.04 works on my old desktop.  Basic spec is 900MHz Athlon, 16Mb display card, 512Mb ram, 40Gb Hard disk.  It is noticeably slow but seems to work OK.  I can increase the number of desktops in two ways.
1) Right click on workspace(bottom RH corner), select customize panel.  Where it shows + button click on it twice (for 4 workspaces in total).
2) Go to Applications - Settings - Settings Manager. Click on the Workspaces and Margins icon (four quadrant window, just visible on edge of screen) and set Number of Workspaces to 4.
