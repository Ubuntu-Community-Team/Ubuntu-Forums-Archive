### Post by _sAm_ on 2008-04-23
When you have installed Glipper you should add it to your Sessions(System > Preferences > Sessions). Choose «Add» and type:
Name: Glipper
Command: glipper

Glipper works very well, and also has a memory so you can copy more then just one thing. I find it strange they don't add it to the default installation.
