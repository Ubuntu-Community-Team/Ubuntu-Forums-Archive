Windows refuse to focus by clicking just anywhere in their area.  I must click their title bar to actually bring it to the front so it's visible, but even after I do that, the window still doesn't have input focus!  The title bar is grayed out instead of being orange, and all keyboard events are processed in the last window I had focus on.  The only way to gain complete focus on any window is to alt-tab over to it.  I've found that it's nearly impossible to gain input focus on the desktop to do simple things such as rename files.

I have had this issue once before when I installed Ibex the first time immediately after it came out.
