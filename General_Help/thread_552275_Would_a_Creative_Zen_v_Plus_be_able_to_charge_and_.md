### Post by qamelian on 2007-09-16
I have a 4 GB Zen V Plus that works fine. As far as charging goes, that doesn't depend on the OS on the computer; it is handled by the player's own firmware. There are a number of applications including the the default audio player, Rhythmbox, that will allow you to manage the contents of the player. Off the top of my head though, I don't know if any of that actually sync the way the Windows tools do. ANy that I have tried have required me to manage the contents myself, which is how I prefer to handle it anyway.
