### Post by Old_Grey_Wolf on 2011-06-23
At the moment you can't.

Unfortunately Netflix uses Digital Rights Management (DRM). The Microsoft DRM used by Netflix is an access control technology that is used by hardware manufacturers, publishers, copyright holders and individuals to limit the use of digital content and devices. There are no open source applications that I have found that support the DRM access controls.
