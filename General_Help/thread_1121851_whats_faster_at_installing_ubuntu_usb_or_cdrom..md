### Post by mb_webguy on 2009-04-10
On a USB 1.0 connection, a LiveUSB and a LiveCD are essentially the same in terms of speed.

On a USB connection, a LiveUSB will be an order of magnitude faster than a LiveCD.

### Post by mb_webguy on 2009-04-10
Well, the transfer speed only comes into play when reading from the CD or USB drive when loading applications into memory -- basically startup and opening applications.  After that, everything is in memory, so at that point the speed of actually running applications is dependent on the system resources.
