### Post by jerryk1234 on 2013-02-04
I need Paperport too.  Or something reasonably paperport -like.    But I do NOT
need all the features of Paperport.  I need:

  * The ability to have thumbnails that look like the files.
      ( Would be NICE but not REQUIRED to be able to arrange them on the screen by dragging the thumbnails around )

  * The ability to view PDFs by clicking on them.  And a quick & dirty editor
    to type stuff into them.

  * A PDF print driver to let other programs output PDFs into the filesystem

  * Reasonably well integrated scanner, so I can scan paper into the filesystem,to wherever the document software is "pointing".

...And that's about it.  I don't use the Paperport "search" feature or much of
the other fancies.  I bet a lot of people don't.

    Paperport is one of the few things that keeps a Windows desktop on my desktop.
