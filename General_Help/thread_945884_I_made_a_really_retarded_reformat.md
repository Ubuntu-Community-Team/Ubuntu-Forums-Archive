### Post by jerome1232 on 2008-10-12
If you actually installed Ubuntu I'm not sure if testdisk will still be able to recover the partition. You may have to resort to photorec (get's installed along with testdisk), foremost, or scalpel. These are all file carving programs and are pretty good at recovering your personal data.

This is a good walkthrough of various data recovery techniques. You will need another drive to write all your files to.
