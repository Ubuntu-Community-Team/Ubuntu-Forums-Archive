### Post by lithopsian on 2011-03-23
All public Firefox options are under Edit->Preferences in Linux and Tools->Options in Windows.  If you are using Firefox 4 then you may be seeing the new unified menu which might make it easier to spot where the options dialog is.

However, the tweaks at the link you gave do not requere this dialog.  For example, you can move the tabs by right clicking on the tabbar, or from the view menu.

Similarly, to move buttons about, right click on any toolbar and select Customise ....  There are often multiple ways to do these things, but on the whole I'd suggest you find a different website for your Firefox tips because that one is garbage,
