### Post by ajgreeny on 2009-12-06
Try a new profile and it may run great again.  The change from v3 in 9.04 to v3.5 in 9.10 seems to have an upsetting effect on FF, or it did on mine until I made a new profile.

Just rename the ~/.mozilla folder in your home as ~/.mozillabak and restart FF.  Yiou can then use the old folder versions of your bookmark backups to restore them back to the new FF.
