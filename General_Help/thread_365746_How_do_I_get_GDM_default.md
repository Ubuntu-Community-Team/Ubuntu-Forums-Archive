### Post by tubasoldier on 2007-02-20
You would still have GDM. GDM is the gnome display manager. Gnome is the desktop environment. When you get to GDM (or your login screen) you click on menu and then it should be something like sessions. Select Gnome and when you log-in it will ask if you want to make it default.
