### Post by grossaffe on 2008-05-12
i have experience with both gutsy gibbon 32 bit and 64 bit, but only hardy heron 64 bit.  I can tell you that gutsy is a solid OS.  hardy, on the other hand, has a couple issues that bug me.  first off, i've noticed that it boots slower than gutsy did (i mean once you log into your name, it takes longer to be able to truly interact).  Also, Hardy comes with Firefox 3 beta 5, which isn't the most stable web browser when compared to Firefox 2 that comes with Gutsy Gibbon.  I personally haven't noticed any real advantages in my useage of Hardy Heron at this point.

keep in mind i only really have experience with the 64 bit version of Hardy Heron, i haven't done much with the 32 bit version
