### Post by &amp;KyT$0P# on 2017-05-24
No need to upgrade pulseaudio.  Just create/edit [FONT=Courier New]~/.config/pulse/client.conf[/FONT] as described here - [https://firejail.wordpress.com/support/known-problems/#pulseaudio]("https://firejail.wordpress.com/support/known-problems/#pulseaudio")

Fixed the problem for me.
