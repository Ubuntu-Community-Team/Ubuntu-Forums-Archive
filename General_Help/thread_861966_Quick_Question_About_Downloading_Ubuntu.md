### Post by mcduck on 2008-07-17
There are many benefits from using torrents. The first would be the fact that Torrent protocol automatically checks the file it's downloading, so using torrent instead of normal download it's almost impossible to get a corrupted download.

Then there's the possibility of higher download speed (depending n your internet connection and location, of course). And finally using torrents greatly reduces the stress on Ubuntu's servers, while this isn't a great benefit to you, I'd say it's polite to help them as much as we can, as they are giving theis great OS to us for no cost at all.. :)
