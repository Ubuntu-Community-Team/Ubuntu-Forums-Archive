### Post by ranch hand on 2009-10-22
Just use gparted on the LiveCD or use the "format whole drive and install" option.

I would recommend using gparted to format your drive and then set it up the way you want it and then install on 2 partitions, / (root) and /home.  This will make life easier for you in the long run.

There will be no malware left on your drive, or anything else, for that matter.  Be careful, take your time, if you run into something you do not understand - ask first.

I have a 320Gb drive with 9 Linux OS' on it and another larger drive with 13.  These drives are my test platforms for 9.10, they will be wiped and reformatted here directly.  If this was hard I could not do it.
