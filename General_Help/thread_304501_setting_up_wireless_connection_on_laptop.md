### Post by Dual Cortex on 2006-11-21
Go to System>Administration>Networking

Yould see a "Wireless Connection" if your Wireless Adapter has been successfully recognized. Just click on it and then select properties and fill the needed settings (ESSID, and/or password, etc.).

Wish you the best on luck with your wireless network, you should tell us the model of the adapter that you have so we can tell you if it's supposed to be recognized, etc.
