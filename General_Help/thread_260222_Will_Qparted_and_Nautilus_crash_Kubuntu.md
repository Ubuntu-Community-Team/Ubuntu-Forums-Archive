### Post by Ramses de Norre on 2006-09-18
I don't know whether the same issue occurs in kde but in fluxbox when I execute "nautilus" the process takes over my whole desktop (making it impossible to get to a fluxbox menu).
In gnome this is the right thing but not in other wms..
So if this happens to you: kill nautilus and change all your launchers to "nautilus --no-desktop".
