### Post by zvacet on 2009-08-05
If you still have iso then check [md5sum.]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HowToMD5SUM")If it is not correct then download same iso with torrent and point download to the folder where your iso is.Torrent will just check your iso and replace bad files with good ones.After that burn iso on lower possible speed.Then do [CD integrity check.]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Installation/CDIntegrityCheck?action=show&redirect=CDIntegrityCheck")If everything is O.K. you are ready for install.Of course bad CD can be reason.
