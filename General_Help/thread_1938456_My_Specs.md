### Post by 3rdalbum on 2012-03-09
> **ColeTurner said:**
> Will Youtube Video in 480p or 720p be laggy or jerky?

Not with a 3GHz CPU.

Most computers with a 3GHz dual-core CPU would have more than a gig of RAM, but nothing you've told us would be a barrier to Ubuntu 11.10 working perfectly well on that computer.

If you have integrated VIA or SiS graphics you might only be able to use Unity 2D (rather than the slightly-better Unity 3D interface) and Full HD video playback might not be as smooth as if you had a proper ATI or Nvidia graphics card, but otherwise you should be alright.
