### Post by Bakon Jarser on 2008-09-04
I'm not positive but I believe pre-released means they are in the process of being tested.  

The unsupported updates (backports) are updates done by the community.  I don't believe they've been tested by Ubuntu.  If you want the latest versions of programs a lot of times they are in the backports.

### Post by mb_webguy on 2008-09-04
From my experience, the packages from the backports repository are usually good.  They may occasionally have issues, but I've never really had any problems with them.  I'd be a bit more wary about the pre-release updates, since by definition they're still in testing.
