### Post by egalvan on 2010-04-15
[B]Repos not updating

Ubuntu not updating
[/B]

Those are two different things.

Apropos the repos....

Go to

System -> Administration -> **Software Sources**

On the first tab (Ubuntu Software) go to the chooser window called

**Download from:**

Pick "Other"

A window titled "Choose a Download Source" opens up

Highlight your country or geographical location

on the right side is an option titled "Select Best Server"

click it.... let it run, it may take a few minutes.

close windows, and accept the "reload repositories" option so that the information is updated.

this should leave you with an updated repo list.


If this fails, then you may have network connectivity issues.

Hardy is up to 8.04.**4**
