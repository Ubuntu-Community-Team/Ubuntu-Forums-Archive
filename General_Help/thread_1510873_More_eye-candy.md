### Post by realzippy on 2010-06-16
...make the "cube" better:

Set a "skydome";a backround image for the cube.Equirectangular images are
first choice:
[http://www.flickr.com/groups/equirectangular/pool/](http://www.flickr.com/groups/equirectangular/pool/)

Then "animate" the skydome,so it looks like as you were rotating around the cube and *in* the skydome..



Another optical improvement (you need nvidia hardware/driver) is to set FSAA and AF in the graphics driver so that
the cube gets antialiased...no more "steps";smooth cube.
