### Post by lisati on 2008-07-07
Right-click on the menu bar (a.k.a. panel) and click on "Add to panel". What to do next should be fairly self-explanatory.

---

### Post by mcduck on 2008-07-07
Right-click on empty spot in the panel, select "Add to Panel", find the "Menu Bar" applet and drag & drop i to the panel. That's it. :)
