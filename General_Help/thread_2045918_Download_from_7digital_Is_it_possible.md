### Post by The Cog on 2012-08-22
**Update:** 
I just got an email from 7digital support. It looks as though it is a problem their end. The music I ordered is a new release, so it would appear that I was just unlucky that my first ever attempted download from them was of a bad upload. I'm quoting their mail below, and marking this thread as solved. Thanks to everyone who tried to help.
> Hello,

We're really sorry about the problem you have experienced. We have checked the download and we can see an error with it. Thank you for bringing this to our attention.

We have requested a redelivery of this product from the record label. As soon as we receive the corrected version, it will get uploaded to the site, but at this point we do not have a specific date for this, so we do not know when the fixed download will be available.

In this position you have two options. You can exchange the download for a different one of similar retail value (please let us know the artist and title of the download you would like) or you can have a refund.

Please let us know which option you'd like to take.
