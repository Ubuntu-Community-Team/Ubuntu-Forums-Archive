### Post by xtnsgo on 2010-08-26
Kdenlive works very well, and there's a liveCD if you don't like the KDE libraries on your system.  Efficient, no nonsense interface, it's pretty easy to use, and it has a raft of input/output options.  I hear good things about Openshot, but I can't get over that interface: it wastes space and it's too glossy for me.
