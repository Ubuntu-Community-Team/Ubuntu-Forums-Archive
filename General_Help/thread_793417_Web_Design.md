Not so that you would notice, unless you get a LOT of traffic. 

There's a lot more to running a webserver than just putting things in an Apache folder. If you're going to serve a website from your house, it may be worthwhile to get a second computer to use as a server. You will also need to see if your ISP provides a static IP, and if not, register with an organization like DynDNS.com to provide DNS services. And you may wish to register a domain name.

You might be interested in reading [this]("http://philip.greenspun.com/panda/server") to get a better idea of what running a website entails.
