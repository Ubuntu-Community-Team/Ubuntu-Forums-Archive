### Post by BigJules on 2010-11-01
I've never used anything else other than Recoll for some years. It's lightning fast, including the initial index, and it's awesomely accurate. Best part is that it has a powerful query engine that enables complex searches, and fully configurable indexing options. Results in context preview with options, and indexes just about everything there is on a working computer. It's on Synaptic, trivially easy to install (and remove) so what's not to like/try?
