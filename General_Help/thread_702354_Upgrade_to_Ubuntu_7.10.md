---

### Post by ago on 2008-02-20
No it's not possible unless you use LVPM and install to a dedicated partition first.

8.04 will support upgrades, but you will have to uninstall 7.04 first.

In any case, it is well possible to save the home.virtual.disk and then mount that in another installation to copy over relevant files.
