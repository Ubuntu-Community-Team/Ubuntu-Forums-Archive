### Post by lelolelo on 2020-12-29
You can't go wrong with Electrum. 

This is the reliable, old school wallet that you know you can trust. Learn how to verify signatures with gpg if you don't know how to do this already (never caught anything suspicious doing this, but it is a good habit).

[Bitcoin.org can help you choose your wallet too.]("https://bitcoin.org/en/choose-your-wallet")
