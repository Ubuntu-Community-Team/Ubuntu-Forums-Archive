### Post by ajgreeny on 2009-09-24
Seems like some problem with your instant messenger program, eg pidgin or msn etc. client that you use.  Try logging into a command line from the Options on the login page and from there try uninstalling and purging pidgin, or whichever IM application you use. You may then be able to get back into gnome, and try installing the application again.
