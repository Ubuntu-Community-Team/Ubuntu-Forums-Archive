### Post by mike248 on 2018-09-15
This has to be one of the most frustrating things to happen.  I have 4 monitors arrainged in a 4 square and I often get a window (usually a browser - FF or chrome) that the top of the window is right on the screen division line and there is no way to grab any of it to move it or even close it.  I can resize horizontally and from the bottom, but that doesn't help.  

I also go to the task bar, right click (software tab) and select more options that  "move", or "resize" or ANY of the options and none of them work

EDIT - I found that by clicking "Alt" and holding it down while anywhere on the window it will move the screen!  That tip needs to be shared more often b/c I used to get a lot of people with this problem.  I had tried shift, ctrl but never alt.. dummy
