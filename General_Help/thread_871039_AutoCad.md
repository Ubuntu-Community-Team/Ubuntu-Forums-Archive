### Post by AnonCat on 2008-07-26
Here's a list of CAD programs available for Linux:

[http://www.linux.org/apps/all/Graphics/CAD/CAM-1.html?sort=author](http://www.linux.org/apps/all/Graphics/CAD/CAM-1.html?sort=author)

If you don't have a copy, the 2006 ACAD trial also supposedly works in Wine if you do some of the tweaks mentioned at the site linked to below (it also has a download link to get the 2006 trial copy of ACAD):

[http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=version&iId=12461](http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=version&iId=12461)
