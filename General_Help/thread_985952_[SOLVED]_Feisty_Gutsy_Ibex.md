### Post by squaregoldfish on 2008-11-18
If you're getting full lock-ups, it's unlikely that it's your original install disk that's the problem - you'd be far more likely to to be getting errors rather than system freezes. Besides, the upgrade process will have replaced virtually all the critical files on your system, so any issues there are most likely to have been fixed at that stage.

I think it's more likely that you're having hardware issues, maybe faulty RAM (run memtest86 from your GRUB bootup menu), overheating or a hardware conflict.

Steve.
