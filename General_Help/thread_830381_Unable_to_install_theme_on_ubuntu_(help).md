### Post by angry_johnnie on 2008-06-16
Your link points to cairo dock, which is not a theme, but a really cool app, which sits at the bottom of your screen, much like the Mac OS X dock.

It is, indeed, provided by two .deb files, and should be installed.

You can't find it in the themes tab because, again, it is not a theme.

Press Alt+F2 and type cairo-dock to launch it, and see for yourself.

There are mac-like themes.  Do a google search for mac4lin.  I've never used it, but, judging from screenshots, it's pretty cool. :-)
