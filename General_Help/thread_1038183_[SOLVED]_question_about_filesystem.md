### Post by PGScooter on 2009-01-12
Hi gd6noob,

I would suggest not using exFAT. Its purported advantages do not seem that great, and if you are able to find support for it in ubuntu in the near future, my guess is that it will not be stable for awhile.

I know how you feel-- it's tough to install something when you know that a better alternative exists. But I would strongly suggest that you don't worry about exFAT. I doubt you would notice any advantage, and the risk (usually associated with all new things) just doesn't seem worth it.
