Try using the new version of the Tab Mix Plus add-on available from here [http://tmp.garyr.net/forum/viewtopic.php?t=9178](http://tmp.garyr.net/forum/viewtopic.php?t=9178) which I have always found to be invaluable for the management of tabbed browsing in FF.
