### Post by anaconda on 2007-09-03
I have a problem with my panels. Everything worked perfectly until I tried a smaller resolution (800x600). After changing the resolution back to normal (1280x1024) the lover panel was left to the middle of the screen.

I can get it back to the bottom by right-clicking on the panel and going to preferences and selecting "expand" and then unselecting it again.

However after each reboot the panel will always move back to the middle of the screen "#"#¤"#%#!!

How can I get it to stay on the bottom? Any ideas. (If I expand the panel and leave it expanded it will work correctly, but I dont want my lover panel to be expanded.)

---

### Post by anaconda on 2007-09-03
Solved it!

I went to configuration editor, and adjusted the values of x and y to be correct (from Apps>panel>toplevels). Now it will probably work until I next time try smaller resolutions.

It seems that when changing to smaller resolution the x and y values are adjusted, and they wont change back automagigally! So it is an obvious bug in gnome panels.
