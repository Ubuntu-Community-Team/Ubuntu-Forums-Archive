### Post by TheFu on 2015-01-09
I doubt anyone has tried this.  This is one of those times when virtualization really is the easiest answer.
When using encryption, backups that you KNOW can be restored are absolutely critical - perhaps before you attempt this, it would be a good time to get your backup house in order?  Then there isn't anything to risk, but time.  Plus, you'll know that you can recover if anything bad happens. HDDs/SSDs fail all the time and usually when it is NOT convenient.
