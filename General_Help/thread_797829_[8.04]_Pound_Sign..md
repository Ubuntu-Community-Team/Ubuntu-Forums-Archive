### Post by Zill on 2008-05-17
If a UK keyboard was selected at the time of installation then maybe your /etc/X11/xorg.conf file has got corrupted.

Mine (Dapper - 6.06) has the following section in it:
> Section "InputDevice"
        Identifier      "Generic Keyboard"
        Driver          "kbd"
        Option          "CoreKeyboard"
        Option          "XkbRules"      "xorg"
        Option          "XkbModel"      "pc105"
        Option          "XkbLayout"     "gb"
EndSection
Suggest check your xorg.conf and edit if necessary.  Then restart X.
