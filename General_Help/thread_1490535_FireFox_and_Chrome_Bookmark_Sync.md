### Post by akakingess on 2010-05-23
I used to use [www.mybookmarks.com]("http://www.mybookmarks.com"), there are others like it, but just a website to upload/download all of your bookmarks so that you could access them anywhere, even at the office or someone else's computer, etc. Now I just use Firefox and Xmarks, and just use Chrome/Chromium for testing purposes, but that's the site that I used to use.
