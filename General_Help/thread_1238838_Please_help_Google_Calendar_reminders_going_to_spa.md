### Post by TuckLive on 2009-08-12
I had a problem with a certain alert email I received regularly.  The To: and Reply-To: fields didn't match, but from the Gmail webpage I did the following:

Check box by email
Selected create filter
Fill in criteria 
Click next step
Check the box never send it to spam

Maybe that will help you
