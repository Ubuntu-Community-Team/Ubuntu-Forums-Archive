### Post by Yayestechlab on 2010-06-05
Your problem was choosing KDE as your default session.
KDE has a problem with your graphics card that caused the black screen. 

To fix it you need to change GNOME back to the default session.  

Here is a step-by-step fix. 
1. Reboot. 
2. Click your user name.
3. At the bottom of the screen click the sessions menu.
4. Chose GNOME.
5. Type your password and login.
6. Go to System -> Administration -> Login Screen. 
7.Unlock.
8.Chose GNOME (This session logs you into GNOME).
9.Click close.
