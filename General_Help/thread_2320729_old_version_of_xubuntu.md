### Post by oldos2er on 2016-04-16
Don't use an unsupported version; they no longer receive any updates, including security updates, and the older they get the more insecure they become. And, as you noticed, their repositories go offline.

[Lubuntu]("http://lubuntu.net") may be more suitable for your old computer, but knowing its hardware specs would enable us to do more than just guess.

You should never use a version that's **no longer supported** it will be nothing but trouble. It's always recommended to use the latest or at least a version of Ubuntu that still has support.

If you're using a very old machine and depending on the specs you should try a** lighter version **of Ubuntu it tends to work well on older machines and requires less resources
