### Post by link141 on 2006-10-21
Let me guess, Intel P4 CPU?  I've had the same problem with mine, and did quite a bit of research on the issue.  The intel P4s have and overheating Problem apparently in some machines, (especially home built,(mine)).  You are supposed to be able to dump excessively large loads on the darn thing without it overheating.  But if the CPU is overheating, then I've heard the hardware will deliberately slow the load, (thus making it run more intensively during a movie, especially if you don't have a graphics set running. You may have an unusual amt of dust around it, or some other factor speeding this up, and from my experience, the thing will shut off faster and faster after running.  The best solution is heatsink grease, which can be purchased cheaply in 1 and/or2 use packets.  You put it between the sink and the CPU.  I also added a couple of case fans, cleaned out the dust, and took preventive measures toward the dust problem.  By the way, I also run Kubuntu, and windows on the same machine.  Let me know about any of your questions about this issue.
