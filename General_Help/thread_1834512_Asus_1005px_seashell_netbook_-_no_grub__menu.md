### Post by Mark Phelps on 2011-08-27
You don't get a GRUB menu by default anymore if Ubuntu is the only OS installed on the PC.  That was changed a while back.

If you want to see a GRUB menu, next time you boot, hold down one of the SHIFT keys.  That will force the display of a menu.
