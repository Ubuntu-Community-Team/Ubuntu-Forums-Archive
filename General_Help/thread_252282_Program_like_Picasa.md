### Post by kkinder on 2006-09-07
Word of warning: be careful with EXIF data. Older versions of digikam and the gimp will save your photos without EXIF data. EXIF has what camera you used, information about how the photo was taken, and most importantly (IMHO), the date/time the photo was taken.

You'll lose it without noticing, but you'll want it back.

So make sure your programs are saving the modified versions of your files with EXIF data intract.

Digikam is nice.
