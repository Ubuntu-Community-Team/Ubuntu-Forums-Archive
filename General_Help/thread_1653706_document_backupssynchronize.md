### Post by darkstaar on 2010-12-27
There are a few easy-to-use apps that will probably do what you want. Deja Dup is nice and will compress your files. Back in Time uses rsync and works well too without using compression.

Of course there are many others as well.

---

### Post by tredegar on 2010-12-27
I just use **rsync** for this.
First backup takes time (if 100GB+).
Thereafter it's *fast* (a minute, maybe three) because it just backs up the changes.

Back in Time has had good reviews, but it's just too complicated for what I need (keeping my backed up **/home/*** in sync with my current one).
