Normally on the bottom panel a tab is displayed for each windou you have open. e.g if I have Firefox open and also say Open Office they both have  a tab down on the bottom panel. I open something new and a new tab is created. Then you click on each tab and switch between them.

### Post by Tamlynmac on 2011-06-02
Right click on a blank area in the panel. Select "Add to Panel". When the "add to panel" window opens find the applet "Window List" and left click on it one time (highlighting it) at the bottom of the window select the "Add" button.

This will place two vertical dotted lines in your panel which can be moved by right clicking on the lines and selecting "move". It can then be locked to the panel after moving by right clicking and selecting "Lock to Panel".

One thing to keep in mind is that the open apps are displayed to the right of the dotted lines. I'd suggest you move the lines as far to the left as possible, leaving open space to the right of the dots.

Good Luck & Hope This Helps.
