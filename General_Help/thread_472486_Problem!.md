### Post by Anonii on 2007-06-13
> **Solarat said:**
> I think I cant go back to windows because some of the files got deleted when I tried to install but then stopped.
Indeed, that must be the problem.

Also, the installer starts by partitioning your HD, so you probably stopped the process while it was formatting your Windows partition.
AFAIK, this is not recovable (except if you browse through Windows with another way, and back up your stuff like that. But I don't know if it's possible.), and I'd suggest a new reinstall of Windows, for you to be sure.
If you had valuable stuff in there, I'd suggest waiting for an answer of someone more knowledgable than me.

Finally, if your sister is not into games, Photoshop or AutoCad, I'd install Linux in her PC - since it's already pretty much screwed up - just to see her impressions. She may need a little help in the start, but if you help her, she will greatly benefit from it.


Anyway, good luck, and post back if you need anything.
