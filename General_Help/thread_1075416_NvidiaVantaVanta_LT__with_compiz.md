### Post by Kevbert on 2009-02-20
I do not believe that this card will work with compiz as I don't believe that it supports 3D graphics acceleration. You could try running
```
glxgears
```
in terminal to see what results you get. Another thing to try is [[COLOR="Red"]compiz-check[/COLOR]]("http://forlong.blogage.de/entries/pages/Compiz-Check"), but I think you need a more up-to-date card for compiz.
