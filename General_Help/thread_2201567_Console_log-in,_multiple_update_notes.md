### Post by ian-weisser on 2014-01-24
That post-login text is generated by the scripts in your /etc/update-motd.d/ directory.
The duplicates are generated by /etc/update-motd.d/90-updates-available 
 Have you, by chance, looked at the directory or script for issues?
