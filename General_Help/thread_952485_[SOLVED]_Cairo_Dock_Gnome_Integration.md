### Post by northern lights on 2008-10-19
If I was to use a dock, my choice would be cairo-dock over AWN or kiba, especially if it's meant to entirely replace panels. I'll agree with loomsen though on that being entirely personal gusto.

All three can be set up to completely replace the panel(s). Whether that's more functional is up to you to decide. Daily use will show fairly quickly.

I don't see the danger of "ending up without a panel" at all, though. You can put launchers on the desktop to launch the dock as well as the panel in case of a crash or freeze. The panels can simply be started via the 'Alt+F2' dialog (gnome-panel) in the event of a crash.
