Hi, I'm facing a problem with GNOME 3 notification area, I searched around and it seems I can't find any solution, here's my problem.
I have GNOME 3 with ATI Driver 11.11, it works fine and much better than older ones, but it's no fully compatible yet.
I have installed Screenlets but it causes alot of graphical glitches when I click on Activities or hover down its icon on notification area, they're other apps that work fine but I think they're some don't and Screenlets is seem one of them.
So I was wondering is there anyway to remove Screenlet Icon from notification area? disabling it? Or anyway to disable the whole notification area?

### Post by lechien73 on 2011-12-10
I'm running the same version of Ubuntu as you, and when I open the screenlets manager, I have the option to "Show daemon in tray" at the lower left-hand side.

If I uncheck this box, then the screenlets icon disappears :)
