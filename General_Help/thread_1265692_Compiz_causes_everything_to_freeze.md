### Post by stderr on 2009-09-13
I'm not sure what to recommend regarding the freezing; it may be a graphics driver issue as opposed to compiz. On the other hand, it may well be compiz, or one of the compiz plugins you have enabled.

Instead of using the power button, perhaps magic keys will work. They allow you, in many cases when systems freeze, to converse directly with the kernel and tell it to close open programs, sync unwritten data to hard drives, unmount them, and thereby reboot safely.

You hold down both Alt and PrintScreen and type, slowly (with a second or so gap in between) R E I S U B

See [http://www.acepcs.co.uk/index.php?a=knowbase&kbs=linux&kba=magickeys]("http://www.acepcs.co.uk/index.php?a=knowbase&kbs=linux&kba=magickeys")
