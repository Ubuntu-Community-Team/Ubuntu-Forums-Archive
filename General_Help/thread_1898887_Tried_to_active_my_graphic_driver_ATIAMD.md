### Post by Mark Phelps on 2011-12-22
Is there a particular reason you feel you need the fglrx drivers?

Asking because 11.10 does a really good job of handling graphics with the latest open-source drivers.  I have multiple desktops and multiple resolutions -- using the open-source drivers.

Unless you really need 3D hardware acceleration for gaming, or CCC for fan control of your graphics card, you don't really need to put up with the hassle of installing the fglrx drivers.
