### Post by davidmohammed on 2010-10-10
It's tied to your Ubuntu Single Sign On account, and you can install your purchased apps on any computer you own by clicking File->Reinstall previous purchases.

... but given that there is only one app in 10.10 - perhaps a little academic.  Wait until 11.04 for more apps to buy.
