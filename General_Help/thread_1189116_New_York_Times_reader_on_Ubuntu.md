### Post by s.mcclatchie on 2011-01-30
They seem to have improved the installation process since Ubuntu 9.0.4. I just re-installed it on 64-bit Ubuntu 10.10 from the web site <http://timesreader.nytimes.com/timesreader/index.html?campaignId=34W88>.
Installation of Adobe Air went flawlessly, the application started, and I could login via the settings menu or just use the limited free version. Another nice improvement is that I could move the application between desktops and resize the window without problems. Very smooth.
