### Post by spuny on 2010-05-01
Well, you can right click on the panel and choose "Add to panel" then click on "Custom application launcher", and on location put "file:///home/username" replace username with your name, or simply open from the Places menu the folder you like, then from file browser dialog drag the tab name and drop on the menu.
