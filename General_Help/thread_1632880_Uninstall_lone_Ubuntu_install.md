### Post by restorator on 2010-11-28
Same way you "uninstall" Windows!

Wipe, nuke, zero out, etc., the drive securely if you are getting rid of it, or you can just install a new OS over top if you are keeping it and are not overly concerned with any remnants of the previous install. You can also smash the drive with a sledgehammer. This is pretty darn effective as long as you make sure the platters are in pieces.
