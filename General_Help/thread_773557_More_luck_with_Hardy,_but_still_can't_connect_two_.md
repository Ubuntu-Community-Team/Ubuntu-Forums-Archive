### Post by .nedberg on 2008-04-29
I used the Gentoo wiki when I sat up my dual screen with Kubuntu. Have a look at it: [http://gentoo-wiki.com/HOWTO_nVidia_TwinView](http://gentoo-wiki.com/HOWTO_nVidia_TwinView)

And here is my Device section of xorg.conf
```
Section "Device"
        Identifier      "nVidia Corporation NV43 [GeForce 6600 GT]"
        Driver          "nvidia"
        Busid           "PCI:5:0:0"
        Option          "AddARGBVisuals"        "True"
        Option          "AddARGBGLXVisuals"     "True"
        Option          "NoLogo"        "False"
#Following added 2007-10-20
        Option "Twinview" "true"
        Option "MetaModes" "1280x1024,1280x1024; 1280x1024,1024x768; 1024x768,1024x768"
        Option "SecondMonitorHorizSync" "30-65"
        Option "SecondMonitorVertRefresh" "50-75"
        Option "ConnectedMonitor" "CRT, CRT"
        Option "TwinViewOrientation" "RightOf"
EndSection

```

The only change I made is the lines under the comment.
