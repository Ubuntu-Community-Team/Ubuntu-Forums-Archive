Hello Sid1980, and welcome!  From the del.icio.us web site at:

[http://del.icio.us/help/firefox/extension](http://del.icio.us/help/firefox/extension)

The instruction to uninstall is:

To uninstall these buttons, go to Tools -> Extensions and choose the "uninstall" button for the del.icio.us extension.

Remember to stop and restart Firefox for the change to take effect. Good luck!
