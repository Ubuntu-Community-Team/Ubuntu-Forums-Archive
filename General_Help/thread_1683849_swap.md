### Post by sanderd17 on 2011-02-08
swap is a partition on your HDD that is used when RAM is not sufficient (e.g. when you don't have enough RAM to run an app or when you want to power off you computer while leaving all apps open - also known as hibernating )

If you have enough RAM, don't run heavy applications (like virtualbox, movie editors ...) and don't hibernate, you can perfectly live without swap.
