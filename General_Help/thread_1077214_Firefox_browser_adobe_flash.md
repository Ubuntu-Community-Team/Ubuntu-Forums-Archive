### Post by mb_webguy on 2009-02-22
Go to System->Administration->Synaptic Package Manager.  In the search box, type "flash", and scroll down until you see "gnash".  Click the checkbox for gnash and select "Mark for Removal".  Now look for "flashplugin-nonfree".  Click the checkbox next to it and select "Mark for Installation".  Now click the Apply button.
