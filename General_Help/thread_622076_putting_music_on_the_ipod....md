### Post by flaggh on 2007-11-24
You can try RockBox: [http://www.rockbox.org]("http://www.rockbox.org")

It will install an alternate firmware on you ipod which does support ogg files.  I have tried RockBox myself but was having problems with my ipod freezing up so I can't fully endorse it.  It is still a work in progress, as are most open source projects.  I can say that I was impressed with a lot of the features it included even though it complicated things a little.

One nice thing about RockBox is that you can just drag and drop files into your ipod without having to deal with iTunes or gtkpod, or any of those other syncing programs.  

Also, installation and uninstallation were painless for me, although I can't make any guarantees that you will have the same success.
