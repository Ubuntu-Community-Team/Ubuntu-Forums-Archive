### Post by sbeattie on 2008-07-02
As you may be aware, Ubuntu is intending to release the firest point release for Hardy LTS, 8.04.1, on thursday, July 3rd 2008, including all of the variants (kubuntu, xubuntu, etc.). There's been a number of Wubi related bugfixes that are being rolled into the 8.04.1 releases (see the thread on 8.04.1 preleases for some specific Wubi fixes) and it would be appreciated if people interested in Wubi could test out the isos  on [http://cdimage.ubuntu.com](http://cdimage.ubuntu.com) and post your results on the QA iso test tracker at [http://iso.qa.ubuntu.com/qatracker/build/all/all](http://iso.qa.ubuntu.com/qatracker/build/all/all) .

You can look at [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Testing/Cases/Wubi](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Testing/Cases/Wubi) for an outline of steps to run through in testing the iso images. Also, people will be available for questions on the #ubuntu-testing irc channel on irc.freenode.org.

Thanks!
