### Post by DJ_Peng on 2008-03-26
Don't feel bad. I bet every Windows user went through that when they switched to Linux. Although the text zoom hotkey is one I use often myself. Use Ctrl-- (Ctrl and the Minus key) to zoom out and Ctrl-0 (Ctrl and Zero/Zed) to reset the zoom level.

Be warned though, Firefox 3 wants to make zooming the entire page the default. There's an option on the View menu of Firefox 3 to select if you always want to zoom the text, not the page.
