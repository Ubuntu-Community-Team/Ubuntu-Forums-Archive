### Post by Habbit on 2008-06-24
Well, there's ALT+TAB for application switch, you can navigate to the terminal there, and as it places recently switched-to apps closer, you'll probably won't even have to navigate.

You might also want to try Yakuake, an app in the repositories that works kinda like the HL or Quake console, rolling down from the top of the screen on a keystroke, and hiding back when you press the same key again. Very useful for development cycles.
