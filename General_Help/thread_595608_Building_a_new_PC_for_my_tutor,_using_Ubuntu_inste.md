### Post by Monstroxus on 2007-10-29
"Sure it may be redundant but a proper Australian English dictionary and thesaurus must be included by default."

In Gutsy you will see a search applet in the top right taskbar, one of the search options this applet will offer is a dictionary search.

"Last but not least he likes to rip music and then burn it to cd."

When you put a cd in the tray, automatically a standard cd rip program will be activated which is very easy to work with.

So what i am thinking of doing for file management is creating a directory "Music" within the Home folder. What other directories should i create?

In Gutsy a bunch of these "My documents type" of folders are now standard included.

"I might also have a little crack with compiz fusion, any suggestions for a good setup?"

Go to synaptic and install the full fledged compiz fusion (just click most of the compiz options there (especially the manager), before doing so, deactivate compiz fusion in the appearance settings. After installing the full version of Compiz Fusion, go to the appereance setting and you will see a "custom" button. Activate this one. Then go to the setting manager and play around. You said it will be a newly buid pc, so good chance compiz fusion will run fine.
