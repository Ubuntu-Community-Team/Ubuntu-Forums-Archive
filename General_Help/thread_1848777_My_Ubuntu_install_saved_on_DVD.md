### Post by seawolf167 on 2011-09-23
Additionally (though it is a little extra work as compared to remastersys), you could image your hard drive with [Clonezilla]("http://www.clonezilla.org") to an external/internal drive, then burn the image parts (default size for files is 2GB, though you can change this to 4.7 GB for a normal DVD) to a DVD and have a DVD archive of your system (how many DVDs completely depends on the size of your system, regardless of method used).
