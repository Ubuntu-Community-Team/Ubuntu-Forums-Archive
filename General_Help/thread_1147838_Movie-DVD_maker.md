### Post by mb_webguy on 2009-05-03
Ignoring the legally questionable second part of your post, there are several ways to put a video on a disc.

Some video players support DivX videos.  If yours does, then all you have to do is make sure your video is an Xvid-encoded avi, and copy it do the disc.

Otherwise, you can use one of several applications that convert a video file into a DVD, and even allow you to create menus.  Take a look at DeVeDe, ManDVD, and DVD Styler in the repos.
