### Post by undertakingyou on 2008-06-23
I just did the same thing a week ago.  I just copied certain homefolders over to my new pc.  .purple for pidgin  .evolution for my evolution calendar.  .wine for the wine stuff.  .gnupg for my gpg key.  .tomboy for tomboy notes.  etcetera etcetera

all of the . folders are hidden.  To view them in the file browser press <CTRL>+h
