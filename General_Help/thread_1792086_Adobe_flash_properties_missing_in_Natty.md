### Post by uRock on 2011-06-27
Use the flash-aid plugin for Firefox to install the proper flash for your system. [https://addons.mozilla.org/en-us/firefox/addon/flash-aid/](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-us/firefox/addon/flash-aid/)
