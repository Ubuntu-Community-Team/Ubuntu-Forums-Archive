### Post by tgalati4 on 2008-10-30
You might have to completely take the machine apart.  Clean it with compressed air.  Use a magnifying glass to search for burned components.  Take a digital picture and post it.  It may be repairable.  

Use a voltmeter and check the 1.5V, 3.33V, 5V and 12V lines coming out of the power supply.  If your BIOS has a voltage readout, take note of the settings.

After diagnosis, reassemble the motherboard with minimal components and try to boot.
