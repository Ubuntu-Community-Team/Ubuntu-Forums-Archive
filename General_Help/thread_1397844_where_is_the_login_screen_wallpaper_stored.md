### Post by gmargo on 2010-02-03
It is probably one of the files in the **xubuntu-gdm-theme** package.  A list of the files can be found in
[http://packages.ubuntu.com/karmic/all/xubuntu-gdm-theme/filelist](http://packages.ubuntu.com/karmic/all/xubuntu-gdm-theme/filelist)
or with **dpkg -L xubuntu-gdm-theme** at the command line.
