### Post by Mac181 on 2010-02-15
Seems 9.1 installation didnt include the newest version. Upgrading changed the sound options to only one option: Pulseaudio server local. Sound worked. But mic didnt. So I went into Alsa volume control and switched from Mic 1 to Mic 2. Now it seems to be working. And my headphones still do. So, so far it appears to be working, muchas gracias. Sometimes its the simple solutions.
