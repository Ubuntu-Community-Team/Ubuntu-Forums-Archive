### Post by darolu on 2010-03-07
Hey, almost all IM clients support Yahoo Messenger. Empathy (the default IM client) can be used with Yahoo Messenger, emesene, pidgin and kopete are also popular and they all support Yahoo Messenger protocol along with msn's, icq's, aim's, google talk, etc...

Try some of these apps, all of them (imo) are very intuitive, and when you find one you like let us know which app you chose should you need further help.
