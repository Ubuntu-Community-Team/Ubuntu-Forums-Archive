### Post by psychicdragon on 2005-07-17
Yeah, most of the extensions are garbage. I have no idea what the sidebar is for. The  actions extension is just half-baked I think. It basically runs a command (wget, gedit or whatever) on the page or selected image. Not very useful.

The last 3, all the tabs extensions, are good. They let you move tabs between windows and detach them to form a new window. You can probably do that in Firefox too though.

Not sure about the search boxes.

I use it because it feels lighter than Firefox and I never really used any extensions to begin with.
