### Post by grahammechanical on 2016-02-21
Ubuntu has app indicator icons in the top panel. The icon for sound is like a loud speaker with sound waves coming out of it. Click on the icon and select Sound Settings from the drop-down menu. Or, go to System Settings and load the Sound utility. It is 2 ways of getting to the same place.

The Sound dialog has tabs for output; Input; Sound Effects & Applications. The Sound Output tab will only show outputs that are active. So, plug in some earphones and earphones will become an output option. Plug an audio cable into a line out socket and line out becomes an output option. If we are connected to a video adapter by an HDMI cable then HDMI/Display Port should be an output option and not just the built in audio.

We have to plug cables into sockets for various bit of audio hardware to appear in the sound output tab. This is a recent change in the way that the Sound utility works.
