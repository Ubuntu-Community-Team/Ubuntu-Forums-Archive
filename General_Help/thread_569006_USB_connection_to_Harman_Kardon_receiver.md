### Post by tonafernandez on 2007-11-09
this is what i got from the HK Rep
basically it states that there is a handshake before the stream begins

The output recognizes the handshake for the specific device (computer) connected to the USB input. It then allows the stream from iTunes or WMP to flow into the recievr and then it is decoded internally. Those are the only streams that work with the USB for audio...Possibly with manipulation (which could cause damage to the processor) you can get some other protocal to work. But we do not support that.
