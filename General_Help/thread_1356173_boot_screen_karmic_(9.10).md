### Post by jamieleshaw on 2009-12-15
These are older kernels which appear when you update your kernel you can use the computer janitor to remove them which can be found at System -> Admin -> Computer Janitor.
This can be risky.
Its should have some old linux kernels listed under 'unused' just tick them and click do selected tasks.
