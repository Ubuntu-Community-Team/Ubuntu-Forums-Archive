### Post by lykwydchykyn on 2009-06-30
From what I know the kmail authors aren't real keen on the idea of HTML email to begin with, and kmail's mail editor has pretty rudimentary HTML capabilities anyway.  kmail may not be the best client for you if that's the sort of thing you want to do.

Thunderbird has some extensions which will do stationery.  Thunderbird works just fine under Kubuntu, I've used it under KDE for years.
