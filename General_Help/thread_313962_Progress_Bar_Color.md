### Post by GreenHawkIA on 2006-12-06
Change your GTK theme.  Go to System-Preferences-Theme and change the theme.  If you just want to change the progress bar/GTK click on Theme Details, and pick a new theme under controls.  To download more go to gnome-look.org and download a tar.gz file which you can drag-n-drop into your theme window to install.
