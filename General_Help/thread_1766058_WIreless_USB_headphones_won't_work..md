### Post by 3rdalbum on 2011-05-23
Plug in your headphones. Click on the speaker icon near the top-right corner of your screen. Go to Sound Preferences. Choose your headset in the Output tab.

Now your headphones are set as the default output device, and all audio should be directed to there if they are plugged in.
