### Post by scouser73 on 2009-10-31
Do you mean the boot screen or the X Splash screen?
If you mean the boot screen then yes you can change it.

Install StartUp Manager: > **sudo apt-get install startupmanager**

Download your bootsplash theme, go to **System** > **Administration** > **StartUp Manager** & click the **Appearance** tab. Click **Manage usplash themes**, click **Add** & locate the theme you have downloaded & select it.

You will now have your nice new bootsplash theme in place.
