### Post by Autodave on 2018-11-26
Upgrading from one version to another often causes problems like that.  I would suggest that you do a clean install of your choice of version. Keep in mind that 18.04 is an LTS release and will be updated for at lease 5 years. 18.10 is only updated until the next short term release.

### Post by MoebusNet on 2018-11-28
Perhaps a dual-boot system with both 18.04 & 18.10 would allow you the stability of 18.04 for production needs with the cutting-edge applications/bugs of 18.10 as desired. The LTS versions are better -suited to production systems that require stability, whereas the interim versions allow development of more current hardware versions, operating system updates, and applications with bugs being squashed as they appear. Only you can determine what best suits your needs.
