### Post by fooman on 2009-01-25
if your referring to the firefox browser opening in a kind of fullscreen mode and blocking out your gnome panel,  then try this:

open firefox....press f11 twice to get to a normal size screen.

next click the unmaximize button.

close firefox.

reopen firefox.

click the maximize button.

...should work properly now.

hope that helps.  :)
