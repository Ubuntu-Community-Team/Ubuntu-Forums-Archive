### Post by fluffman86 on 2009-11-17
It's not a bug it's a feature.  There are two separate clipboards: one for anything that's highlighted, one "traditional" clipboard for copy/paste combos (ctrl+c or menus).

They both work in all applications, not just firefox.  I constantly use them both.  It just sucks if I need to, e.g., highlight a word or link, then I can't highlight the address or search bar in firefox without losing what I highlighted previously.  So that's when to copy/paste like normal.
