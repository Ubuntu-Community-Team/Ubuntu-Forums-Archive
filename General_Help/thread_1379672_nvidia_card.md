### Post by pizza-is-good on 2010-01-12
Yes, you will need to enable the driver for it.

Here's how:

Alt+F2, then type Hardware Drivers. You should see it as an option on the lower half of the window. Run it.
Then, look for the 'non-free' or proprietary driver for your nvidia card, and enable it.

Hope this helps...
