### Post by Peturrr on 2006-05-31
I have been running dapper for a few months on my own PC, and am very happy with it. Our PC in the livingroom of our studenthouse (P3 500, 384 Mb ram), was running Breezy. It was a bit unstable and didn't perform very well.

Today, i've reinstalled the PC with Dapper. And I am amazed. It is much more responsive, everything installed very fast, raid 0 is working flawlessly.
I was especially enthousiast about the installation of the printer. It autodetect every setting and the setup program was lovely. Performance now is great, shared folders are working cool, and we all are very happy with Ubuntu.

A few housemates are even starting to become interested in Linux and Ubuntu. :)
