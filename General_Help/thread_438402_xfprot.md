### Post by masam on 2007-05-09
Step 1:

as far as the minimize goes, you may need to type in a terminal window to find out the options.
(ie: "xfprot /help")


Step 2:

System->Preferences->Sessions

Left click on Startup Programs.

Add the program with the supplied options for minimize


Hope this helps!
