### Post by yetiman64 on 2010-07-01
> **Cam! said:**
> When I right click and remove it, it also removes the power icon, with the shut down/restart/lock controls. How do I add JUST the Power applet?

Right click, add to panel, Shut down. 

This gives a on screen display with shutdown, restart, suspend and hibernate (depends on which features your machine is capable of re. suspend and hibernate etc). You will however also be missing the lock screen option and the session switcher if you choose to remove the user name. Though lock screen can be added as a separate button.

Edit: also switch users can be added with the log out button.
