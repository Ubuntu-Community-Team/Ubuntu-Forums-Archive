### Post by 2F4U on 2011-07-19
I guess that depends on where the bottleneck is. On my Thinkpad X31 with 1.5 GHz Pentium M and 1.5 GB RAM the bottleneck clearly is the CPU. So I care much more about applications that consume CPU than RAM. With 2 GB of RAM, this shouldn't be your first concern, but with an 1.6 GHz Atom processor you should aim more at applications that are light on the CPU.
