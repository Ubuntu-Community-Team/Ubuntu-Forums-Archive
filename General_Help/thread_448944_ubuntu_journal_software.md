### Post by thinkbuddha on 2007-05-24
I've tried a few things, and had a bash with eJourn - which works nicely - but I've gone for a roundabout solution of putting up a blog on the local server, adding a couple of bookmark buttons to my firefox toolbar ('blog' and 'edit') so that it is easy to use, and writing a journal this way. The advantages of this are that blogging tools like Wordpress or (my favourite, for some reason) Textpattern are generally very extensible, they are customisable in terms of look and feel as you want them to be, and - like with any old blog - the journal is easily searchable, fairly easy to export to other formats and so forth.
