### Post by ajgreeny on 2011-08-28
Many flash problems in FF are overcome by the add-on flash-aid, which you can get from [http://www.webgapps.org/download/add-ons/firefox?download=5:flash-aid-add-on-for-firefox](http://www.webgapps.org/download/add-ons/firefox?download=5:flash-aid-add-on-for-firefox) as the version 2.1.1 on the mozilla-addons page is having problems with FF6.

Just download the xpi file and in FF go to File->Open to install the newer 2.2.1 version.
