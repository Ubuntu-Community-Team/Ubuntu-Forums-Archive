### Post by exploder on 2008-10-12
If you are just trying to get the data off the drive, you might be able to access it with a Live CD. The only other option I can think of is to connect the drive to another laptop that you have administrative privileges on. 

Possibly a recovery type of distribution might give you more options for data recovery. Ultimately, you will need to reload the laptop after recovering the data you need.
