Look again at Specimen Font Previewer - it can do this. 

You can compare multiple fonts - select a font from the left pane and use the ADD button to add it to the preview pane (see attached image). Add as many as you wish. Click on the triangles on the left of the font family names to expose variations on each font. 

You can also use your own phrase by editing the top box. You can choose the font size as well.
