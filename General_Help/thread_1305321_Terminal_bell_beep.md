### Post by alphaniner on 2009-10-29
I'm not 100%, but I think I looked into this once because one of my computer mobos doesn't have the connector for a buzzer.  The way I remember it, the method used to activate the buzzer doesn't lend itself to 'redirection' to a sound card.

On the other hand, if your buzzer isn't built into your motherboard, you could always replace it.  I actually think I've seen a few motherboards that have a built in and a connector, on which the connector would override the built-in if a buzzer was attached.
