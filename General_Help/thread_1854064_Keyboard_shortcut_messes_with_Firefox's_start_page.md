### Post by Paddy Landau on 2011-10-15
> **M.A.C said:**
> ... how is it that you created the custom shorcut to go around it?
Open **Keyboard Shortcuts**.
[LIST=1]
[*]Desktop > Launch web browser: disable the shortcut.
[*]Custom Shortcuts > Add: Name = Firefox; Command = firefox. Apply.
[*]Click on the right side of the added command and press your shortcut.
[/LIST]
