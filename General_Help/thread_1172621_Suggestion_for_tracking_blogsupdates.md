### Post by Ms_Angel_D on 2009-05-28
You can always use [google reader]("http://www.google.com/reader") and combine it with the firefox extension [Google Reader Watcher]("https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/4808") it will sit in your status bar and notify you when there is a new unread feed. I use it for all my feeds.
