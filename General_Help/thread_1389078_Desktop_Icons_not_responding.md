### Post by lidex on 2010-01-24
Try rebooting and at the grub screen (where you see the OS boot options) select the second option for recovery mode. Once that loads up you will be presented with repair options. Choose the xserver one and let it run. Reboot.

If your ubuntu is 9.10 you may not get a grub screen by default. In that case hold down the shift button after the bios screen to make it appear.
