### Post by jonian_g on 2008-07-28
It is because you have changed the fonts the system uses.

Right click on your desktop and choose change desktop background. Go to the fonts tab and use Dejavu Sans or Sans (size 10) on the first three lines. On the forth use Dejavu Sans Bold or Sans Bold (size9) and on the last one use monospace (size 10).

Choose also the font rendering to make the fonts look smoother. If you have an LCD monitor choose the one that says Subpixel Smoothing.
