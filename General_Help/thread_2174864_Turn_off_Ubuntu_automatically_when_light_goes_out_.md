### Post by tgalati4 on 2013-09-16
If your UPS (uninterruptible power supply) is compatible with APC units, then there is software that will automatically shut down the computer after a few minutes of running on battery.


tgalati4@tpad-Gloria7 ~ $ apt-cache search apcups
apcupsd - APC UPS Power Management (daemon)
apcupsd-cgi - APC UPS Power Management (web interface)
apcupsd-doc - APC UPS Power Management (documentation/examples)
gapcmon - apcupsd monitor GUI
