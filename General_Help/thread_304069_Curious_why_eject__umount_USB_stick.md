### Post by PriceChild on 2006-11-21
> **gosh said:**
> I'm just curious: Why do you have to unmount or eject an USB stick before you take it out of your machine? What goes wrong when you don't?
It automounts itself, why not auto-unmount?Because when you copy/save to the disc... you aren't really ;)

Infact you're saving to a cache, which will be written to the disc when the computer hasn't anything better to do.

Ejecting writes the cache and makes sure your data is all there.

It caught me out a few times thinking that I'd saved my data :(
> **gosh said:**
>  In XP I just take the usb stick out.You really shouldn't ;)

Right click the drive in My Computer and select "Eject" or use the "Safely remove hardware" icon bottom right near the clock.
