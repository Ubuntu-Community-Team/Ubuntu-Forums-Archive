### Post by angry_johnnie on 2008-06-01
Pidgin can't do offline, but amsn and emesene can.  They're both available in the repositories.  Go to **System > Administration > Synaptic** and then search for either **amsn** or **emesene**.

If you can't find them, go to **System > Administration > Software Sources** and make sure you have **universe** and **multiverse** enabled.
