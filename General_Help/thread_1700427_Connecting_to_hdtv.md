### Post by Hedgehog1 on 2011-03-05
rlarson123,

If your TV has true 1920x1080 resolution (LCD or Plasma TV), then the top/bottom you cannot see can be fixed by setting the 'over-scan compensation' to '0' in your NVIDIA X Server Settings screen.

The audio over HDMI is a second matter.  It takes a little fooling to get it going; all of with NVindia cards and HDTVs have to go through it.

Here is the thread:

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1668737&highlight=nvidia+hdmi+audio]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1668737&highlight=nvidia+hdmi+audio")

Read to the end before you start trying things, some steps are not necessary for newer installs of Ubuntu.
