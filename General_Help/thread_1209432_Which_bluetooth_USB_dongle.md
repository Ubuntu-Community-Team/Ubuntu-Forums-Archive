### Post by t4thfavor on 2009-07-10
I have this one, hope it helps. it was 19USD retail.

Bus 003 Device 004: ID 0a12:0001 Cambridge Silicon Radio, Ltd Bluetooth Dongle (HCI mode)
 Its very little about 1/2" long, and works well. I have seen smaller ones though retail for 15USD. I got it at Meijer (US midwest wal-mart like store)


I forgot to mention it doesn't have a brand name on it. I would probably go with the little one, and hope for the best. If it doesn't work, you are only out 9£ (which is like 22.50 US or so) Which is alot, but at the same time not so much.

I would say that there are only a few chipset manufacturers out there, and you could probably find out what kind of chip is in each one before you buy it.
