### Post by plucky on 2009-03-17
"Workplace Switcher" and "Window List" are two different things.You need to add "Window List" to the bottom panel.

Right Click on the panel and select "Add to Panel".Then scroll down the list and add "Window List".Your programs will then minimize to the bottom panel.


Good Luck
