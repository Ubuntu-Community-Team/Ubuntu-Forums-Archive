### Post by brent113 on 2007-07-19
Hi, you're looking for a storage format, not a file system.  Isos are handy, and no, they don't always have to be mounted.  many media players can play dvd isos directly.  Another option is converting them to divx, but to make a quality divx movie takes a lot of time (around 8 hours when done right).  I would use the iso, and use an iso compatible media player such as vlc
