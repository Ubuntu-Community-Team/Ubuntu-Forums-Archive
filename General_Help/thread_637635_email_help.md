I never did it my self but evolution has a backup utility.

> With the Backup and Restore plugin enabled, you can successfully backup Evolution to your machine and restore it whenever it is required. The back up saves your Evolution gconf settings, mails, contacts, tasks, memos and calendars and creates an archive.

Select **File > Backup Settings** ....
