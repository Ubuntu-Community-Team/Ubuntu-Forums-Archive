I am trying to hide the window listing in the plasma workspace panel when I switch workspaces, but unable to do so. ### Post by Anusiya on 2009-08-25
Never mind, I figured it out. Right-click plasma workspace to bring up task manager settings and check "Only show tasks from current desktop". I don't know how I missed it earlier.
