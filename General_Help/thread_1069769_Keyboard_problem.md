### Post by ajgreeny on 2009-02-14
There is no mention of what your usual keyboard layout is, but it sounds as if you have reverted to a keyboard that is not normal for your system.  Go to *System > Preferences > Keyboard* and make sure your language setting is correct in the *Layout* tab.  The US and UK keyboards, for example, put @, " and various other characters in different places.  Check that you have what you usually use.
