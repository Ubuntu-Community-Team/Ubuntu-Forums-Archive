### Post by pctek on 2010-10-07
It's most likely Ubuntu's lack of support for older Intel Drivers starting with version 9.04. I have seen that flashing screen before and had to 1. Get a newer graphics card or 2. Install the 8.04 or 8.10 versions.

Even a cheap ATI or Invidea card will work fine with the latest versions of Ubuntu.
