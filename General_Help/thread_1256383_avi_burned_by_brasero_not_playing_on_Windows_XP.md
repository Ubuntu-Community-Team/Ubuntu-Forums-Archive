### Post by GoldenSun on 2009-09-03
I use a program called DeVeDe.  It's in the repos.  It will let you add multiple movies, and then compress them is they are too large for the dvd.  Make sure that the setting are set for your region.  They are pal by default.  

DeVeDe makes an iso file out of the movies you want to burn.  Then right click on the iso, and burn to blank dvd.

edit:
The "time" on a dvd doesn't really matter.  Just worry about the size of the dvd and movies you want to put on it.
