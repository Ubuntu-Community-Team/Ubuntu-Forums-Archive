### Post by VCoolio on 2009-08-18
It's a problem with the win key, not with key combinations. You can solve it by setting the behavior of the win key somewhere but I forgot. Or you can insert the keybinding manually in gconf-editor under apps > metacity > global_keybindings. Here you can set the bindings manually. They correspond to the commands set in apps > metacity > keybinding_commands. For the super/windows-key enter <mod4>.
