### Post by P4man on 2009-09-07
the "input not supported" comes from your monitor, it means its getting a signal it can't display. Could be too high or too low resolution or refresh rate.

Before trying anything else, wait a while. See if it comes up with a log in screen after a minute or so. I got one computer with a 15" LCD unable to display the bootsplash screen (also get input not supported), but once you get to the login screen, it changes to the native monitor resolution and all is well.

If that doesnt happen, press control+alt+F1 and let us know if you get a black terminal window. If so, perhaps there are a few things we can try. Did you already install nvidia restricted drivers?
