### Post by Holger_Gehrke on 2019-08-27
Look in 'Settings'->'Sessions and Startup' (or words to that effect, I'm retranslating from German). Or simply run 'xfce4-session-settings'. There's a setting 'Ask before Logout' on the first tab.
