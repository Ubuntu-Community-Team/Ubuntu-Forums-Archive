### Post by der_joachim on 2006-12-25
Yesterday I bought a Syncmaster 205BW (not the 215, but close). The only real problem I had is that I could not use a DVI cable with my NVidia GeForce5600FX. The resolution could not be bigger than 1280x1024 which is not good for a widescreen monitor. Apparently, that is a GF5600 related problem, so that is no real problem, because I will be buying a new video card soon anyway.

Apart from not being able to work with DVI, the resolution is well handled by my card, although it has only 128 megs of memory. 

I did some gaming. There was no ghosting (although I have an ancient video card). The big resolution is very easy on the eyes, although on a pure black screen, there is a slight whitish shadow on the top and bottom edges of the screen. It's not very bad though. 

A word to the wise: when I bought the monitor yesterday, I bought a DVI cable along with it. I did some searching in the internet and read that (as with most monitors) a DVI cable was not included. When I opened the box though, the first thing I saw was a DVI cable. So I had to go to the shop twice. 

A bit late, but I hope it helps.
