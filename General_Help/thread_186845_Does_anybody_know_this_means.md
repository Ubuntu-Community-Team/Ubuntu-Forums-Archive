### Post by mat1t on 2006-06-02
It's a feature of Azureus.

If you don't have a uPNP router, then you either want to open that port to your computer, or disable "decentralized tracking"

---

### Post by kyldere on 2006-06-02
I have seen this pop up when Azureus is running. It means that one of the (default) ports used to share is blocked. It will go away when Azureus is shut down.
