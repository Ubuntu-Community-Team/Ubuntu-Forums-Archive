### Post by snowpine on 2012-02-28
Not familiar with "Easy Peasy" but: If you boot your computer with any Linux Live CD or Live USB (such as [an Ubuntu Live USB]("http://www.ubuntu.com/download/ubuntu/download")) then you can use the GParted Partition Editor to delete your Windows partition and expand your Easy Peasy partition to fill the empty space.

I strongly recommend backing up all data to an external drive before proceeding!

[Gparted info, help, and screenshots.]("http://gparted.sourceforge.net/")
