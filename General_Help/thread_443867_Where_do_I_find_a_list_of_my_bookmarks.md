### Post by vtel57 on 2007-05-14
Your bookmarks are in your own /home directory. You can use Nautilus to navigate there:

/home/<your user name>/.mozilla/firefox/<random numbers profile>.default/bookmarks.html

Just copy that bookmarks.html to wherever you need it.

Luck!

EDIT: By the way, you'll have to allow Nautilus to display hidden files to see this... Nautilus --> View --> Show hidden files.
