I assume you mean the original XBOX. In which case, Yes, I suppose you could take parts out and put them into your PC. According to Wikipedia, the XBOX has a *Custom 733 MHz Intel Pentium III-based Mobile Celeron*. Not the speediest for general computing, it also has a *233 MHz nVidia NV2A*.

 Interestingly also, the XBOX was said to run a customized version of the Windows 2000 Kernel. So a very stripped down Windows 2000.
