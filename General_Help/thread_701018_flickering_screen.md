### Post by Yellow Pasque on 2008-02-19
If it happened right after the update I would recommend filing a regression bug report on Launchpad. Try to be as specific as possible and include your /etc/X11/xorg.conf and /var/log/Xorg.0.log files (make sure you switch back to the intel driver and boot with that to get the right Xorg.log, but also note in your report that the same thing happens with the i810 driver)

The link is:
[https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/](https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/)

If you need further assistance with filing the report, let me know.
