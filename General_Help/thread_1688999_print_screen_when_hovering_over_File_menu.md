### Post by sanderj on 2011-02-16

You should not press the Print Screen key; just use the app Accessoires -> Take Screenshot, set delay to 8 seconds, click Take Screenshot, do your File-hover-thing, keep the mouse there, wait until the screen capture is taken ...

See the included screen shot how to make a screen shot. :-)
