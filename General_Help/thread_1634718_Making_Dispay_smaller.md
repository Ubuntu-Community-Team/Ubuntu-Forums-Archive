### Post by efflandt on 2010-11-30
What is the native resolution of your laptop display?  If it is less than 1280 by something, you might want to try the netbook edition [http://www.ubuntu.com/netbook](http://www.ubuntu.com/netbook)

Otherwise you could play around with font settings under System > Preferences > Appearance.  Although, sometimes making the fonts smaller (less dots) affects how they are rendered.  Or under those Fonts > Details you could try setting Resolution dots per inch lower.
