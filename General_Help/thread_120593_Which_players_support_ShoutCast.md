### Post by slux on 2006-01-23
Pretty much any player claiming to have some sort of unspecific internet radio streaming support refers to shoutcast as it has become a kind of a standard for mp3 (and ogg) streaming.

Rhythmbox is one player I'm certain of having it, I think AmaroK claims so too but I've never really found how. :P Banshee is lacking it for now.

Then there are the clients made specifically for tuning into net radio such as TunaPie that also show channels from a channel directory.
