### Post by efflandt on 2010-10-11
It depends what theme you use.  In the default theme the icon is a typical international symbol for an on off switch, a circle with vertical bar going up from center (as in binary 1 on, 0 off).  However, if you change the theme, the icon may be something else, like a representation of a light switch on the wall.  Not sure what it is in other than gnome.

PS: If all else fails you could do **sudo shutdown -h now** to shutdown from a terminal, or **sudo shutdown -r now** to reboot.
