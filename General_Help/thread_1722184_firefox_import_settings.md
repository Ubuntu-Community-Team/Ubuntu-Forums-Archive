---

### Post by lovinglinux on 2011-04-05
Yes, just copy that folder and you will be fine.

Keep in mind that depending on the source of the packages, the location of your profile may vary. For example some ppa repositories clone your profile from ~/.mozilla/firefox to ~/.mozilla/firefox-4.0.
