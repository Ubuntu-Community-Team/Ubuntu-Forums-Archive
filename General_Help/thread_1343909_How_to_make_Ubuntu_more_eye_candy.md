### Post by ManiacDan on 2009-12-02
5)  Compiz is visual effects as well as controls for windows.  You can set up hotkeys for scaling, docking, attaching, minimizing, floating, etc etc.  Check out the compiz settings manager to see what it can do.  Compiz is already installed and running on your system, installing and opening the settings manager won't break anything.  When you do get it installed, the desktop cube is one of the coolest features.  Enable desktop cube, cube rotation, and cube transforation and deformation.  If you have more than one active desktop, it's very slick.

6)  Beryl was an offshot of compiz.  It was eventually merged back into the compiz core and the new merged product was called Compiz-Fusion.  This is what you have installed.  Compiz-Core is the core of compiz without the Beryl branch.

-Dan
