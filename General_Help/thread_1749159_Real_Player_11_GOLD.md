### Post by pv_selva on 2011-05-10
Problem is resolved by changing the hardware setting in Preferences. In Preferences under the Hardware tab, by default it chooses the hardware as "ALSA". So the playback was not even working for the standard mp3 files too. I changed it to "OSS" and now its playing the mp3 files. There is no option to add plugins/codec to play the .avi, .mp4 files or any other formats.
