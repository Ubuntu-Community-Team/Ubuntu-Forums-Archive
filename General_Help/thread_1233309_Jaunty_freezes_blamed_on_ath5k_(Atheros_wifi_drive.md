### Post by edantes on 2009-08-06
I was plagued by frequent freezes since upgrading to 9.04 - Jaunty.  It turns out they were caused by the ath5k driver interaction with my wifi card (Atheros AR5001X).

Installing the proprietary madwifi driver solved the problem.
