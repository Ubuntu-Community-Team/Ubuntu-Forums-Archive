### Post by neilevan814 on 2009-03-27
You can try this site for downloading wallpaper:
[http://www.gnome-look.org/index.php?xcontentmode=170x171x172x173x174x175x176x177x178x179&PHPSESSID=0aac5ad0f1f4c9a31cdf8cfe96416d15]("http://www.gnome-look.org/index.php?xcontentmode=170x171x172x173x174x175x176x177x178x179&PHPSESSID=0aac5ad0f1f4c9a31cdf8cfe96416d15")

Then go to ->System->Preferences->Appearance->Background and hit +Add then go to the directory you downloaded your wallpapers to and select them to add to your backgrounds.
