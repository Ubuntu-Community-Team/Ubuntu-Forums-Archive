### Post by deadflowr on 2014-11-12
Ubuntu 11.10 is safe from Windows viruses.

But it is a completely, unsupported version of Ubuntu, and in and off itself is full of security risks.

It would be advisable not to use it for banking, and instead upgrade to a supported version.

See here for staff recommendations 
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2229730](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2229730)
