### Post by bluefrog on 2010-02-10
use screen. there is an option to copy/paste

<Ctrl> <a> <Esc> puts screen in copy mode
move the cursor to the beginning of the section to copy, hit enter
move the cursor to the end of the section to copy, hit enter

<Ctrl> <a> <]> to paste


You could also install gpm to copy/paste from console with the mouse.

### Post by audiomick on 2010-02-10
you can use "alt" and the underlined letter in the title of the "edit" menu to open that menu, the cursor arrows to step down to "select all" and then enter. This will only select everything in the terminal.
