### Post by Azdour on 2011-07-26
Hi,

I have a similar signature at work in Thunderbird (Ubuntu 10.04, Thunderbird 3.1.7).

I used html to format the signature with links and images on. I then went to Account Settings and choose the html file I had created.

The thing I found was that the images need to be http based not local.
