### Post by damu on 2006-05-31
Faster, smoother, nicer, with better hardware support.
Boots much faster, open applications much faster, ect.
The last version of gnome is such an improvement!
Never managed to get my wireless dongle to work before...with Dapper, I just plug it in and it works.

Easier to install applications that are not in "add/remove applications" or in synpatic. Let me give you this example which I am sure will help soo much getting  newbies to try Ubuntu : To install automatix (this application which will install for you all the needed codecs for audio, video, DVD, and loads of other applications ), you will now just click on the link to download the last automatix deb file. Once downloaded, you just double click on it, enter your password, and here we go : Automatix is installed and ready to use....no more command line, terminal thinggy! sooo easy :) 

As for the upgrade from Breezy, I just let the update manager do its job and less than an hour later (the download takes quite a while for such a big amount) I was up and running!

It's Ubuntu really...just make my days of work nicer !
