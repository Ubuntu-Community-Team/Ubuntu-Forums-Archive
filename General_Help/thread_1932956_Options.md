Some options of a command begin with **--** (a double hyphen)

For example** -i** and **--interactive** are same.

---

### Post by greenpeace on 2012-02-28
This depends absolutely on the program... the shorter options are meant to be more of a short-hand for the longer options:  faster to type but harder to remember.

Don't depend on these being the same between programs, and don't read too much into it!
