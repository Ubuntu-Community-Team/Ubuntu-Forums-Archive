### Post by BuffaloX on 2008-01-27
I believe software patents are not recognized in EU,
In US mp3 is protected by patents, so as long as you just sell it in EU including UK you should be in the clear.

But you must comply with Licenses of what you distribute, that means for most open source, that you must make all sources available.
If they are kept at original state, I believe you can refer to the original source.

You should be aware that most legal systems penalize harsher if you overstep licenses to make profit, and if you are considered a pro, or for other reasons should now what you are doing....

This advise is of course given without any warranty.
