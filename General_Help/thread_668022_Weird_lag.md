### Post by tgalati4 on 2008-01-15
Unmount your NTFS windows drive.  There are reports that the NTFS mount causes lags--either a driver issue or a kernel interaction issue.  If you don't need access to the Windows files, then dismount the drive and only mount it when you need it.  Just another reason to make the switch.
