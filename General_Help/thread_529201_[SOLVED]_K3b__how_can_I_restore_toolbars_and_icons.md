### Post by Jucato on 2007-08-19
Oh ok. I get it.

1. Press Ctrl+M to make the menu bar visible
2. Go to Settings -> Toolbars and enable all three toolbars
3. Go to Settings and enable "Show Directories", "Show Contents", and "Show Document Header".

Hope that fixes it.
