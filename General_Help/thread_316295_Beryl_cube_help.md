### Post by daou on 2006-12-10
I don't think cube was meant to be used like that. The cube only shows when you rotate it (CTRL+ALT+Mouse1 or CTRL+ALT+left or right arrow). After rotation it disappears.

### Post by daou on 2006-12-10
Go to the Beryl settings manager. On the left, choose the Rotate Cube plugin. Then on the right side choose the "Numeric Values" tab. Change the Flip Time, Acceleration, Speed, etc. Play around with them until you get something you like.

---

### Post by daou on 2006-12-10
Or if you would just like to rotate the cube with your mouse for as long as you want, hold CTRL+ALT and your left mouse button, then move the mouse around.
