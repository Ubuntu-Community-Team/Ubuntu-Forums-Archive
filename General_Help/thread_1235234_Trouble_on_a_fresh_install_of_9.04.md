### Post by Redache on 2009-08-08
You could try installing the Drivers through the Restricted Driver Manager. It should pop up after you've finished installing Ubuntu. It should install the right drivers for your card AND adjust the Screen Resolution, if it doesn't then you can second click on the desktop and change the resolution on there.

As it's a laptop I'd say 1280x800 is a good place to start.
