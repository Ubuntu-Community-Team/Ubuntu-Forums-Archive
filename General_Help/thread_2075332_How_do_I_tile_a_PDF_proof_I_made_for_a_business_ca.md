### Post by GreatDanton on 2012-10-23
It's not very clear what do you want to do, but I assume that you want to have multiple business cards on the same paper. I would duplicate that card (in Inkscape) and then drag and drop them around the paper. You can also make guidelines, and double click them and write suitable cordinates (according to your cards.)

Also I am not sure why you want to print it as a PDF if you can print it like a vector graphics (the lines will be sharper and nicer).

Hope this helps.
