### Post by Johnny B on 2009-10-19
you can make your own icon theme.
mkdir ~/.icons
copy a icon theme there (from /usr/share/icons or from gnome-look.org)
edit the icon files with gimp or import your own           (the panel uses 24x24)
gedit index.theme and change the name to whatever you want
