### Post by yeeeev on 2009-08-19
I ran the peacekeeper benchmark on both FF3.5.2 and Chrome3.0 on My Jaunty machine (Intel(R) Core(TM)2 Duo CPU T7700 2.40GHz 3GB RAM)

Firefox 3.5 scored 1270
Chrome 3.0 scored 3172
And the canvas rendering test difference between the two (which is not included in the final results) is REALLY amazing. Chrome shows full game-like graphics while FF chokes and barely renders some of the frames.
I hate to dis Firefox, but when it comes to performance, Chrome is much better. And I don't think minor optimizations will make the difference.
On the other hand, Firefox's ability to disable flash (using the FlashBlock extension) wins a lot of points since there's no need to render useless flash ads.
