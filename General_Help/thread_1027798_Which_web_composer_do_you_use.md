### Post by Sorivenul on 2009-01-01
There are many - take a look in Add/Remove or Synaptic.

I've used Screem, Amaya, and Quanta Plus (all of which are available in the repositories). I don't do a lot of web development, but Amaya is what I usually use when I want to do something fast and see my results. However, I try to stay away from WYSIWYG editors as much as possible and do most of my coding in Vim or Geany.
