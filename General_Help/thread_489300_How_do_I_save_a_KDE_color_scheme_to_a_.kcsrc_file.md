### Post by jaygo on 2008-02-21
In case you haven't solved this yet ...

.kcsrc files are saved under your user folder: /home/username/.kde/share/apps/kdisplay/color-schemes/

If you copy some there from kde-look, they should show up immediately.

I just got the Ultimate Pack 1.3 from kde-look, and it's got loads of nice, dark color schemes.
