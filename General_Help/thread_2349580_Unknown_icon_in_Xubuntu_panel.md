### Post by ajgreeny on 2017-01-16
If it is a launcher you can right click on it and choose Properties or just see what the top greyed out item in its context menu says.  If that is no help right click in your panel and go to **Panel ->Panel Preferences** then to the **Items** tab where you will see all applets and icons listed.

You should be able to find from that list what tghe icon relates to; my guess is it's a launcher for an application you do not have installed, or for which you have manually chosen a launcher icon (image) that does not exist.
