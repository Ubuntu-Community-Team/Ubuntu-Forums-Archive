### Post by cascade9 on 2010-10-12
AMD64 is the name that AMD gave the 64-bit extensions to x86 (x86-64). i386, i486, i586, i686, etc are 32bit, AMD64 (or x86-64 is you prefer) can use 32bit instructions, or 64bit instructions. 

AMD64 runs with 64bit Intel CPUs, like your Core2Duo. ;)
