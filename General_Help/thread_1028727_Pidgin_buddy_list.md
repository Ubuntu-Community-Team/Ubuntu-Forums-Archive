### Post by squidfish2 on 2009-04-13
I figure as its so long since you posted, that you either dont care anymore, or have found another way, and you probs wont see this reply. But i have found a way to make the buddy list much more compact, without any plugins.

Simply go to:

Buddies > Show

And untick "Buddy Details". The change should be instant and VERY obvious :P
