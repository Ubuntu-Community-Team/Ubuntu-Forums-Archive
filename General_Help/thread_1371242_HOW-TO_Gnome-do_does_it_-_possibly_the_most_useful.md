### Post by magozoom on 2010-01-03
When I came across Gnome-Do a few days ago, at first I couldn't figure out what it was trying to do, or how to use it.  
There were also some installation problems on Karmic / Firefox 3.5

Today I can not imagine using Ubuntu without it. 

It's hard to put a word on Do to describe it...
- Do is a lightning fast application launcher
- Do comes with a fully integrated application dock, Docky
- Do searches the web or your desktop documents with a few keystrokes
- Do plays music from your music collection
- Do opens any URL
- Do can post to microblogs/twitter/
- Do can convert long URL's to tinyURL on the fly
- Do can manage your Tomboy notes
- Do is an APT package installer 
- Do can interact with most Google services, maps, calendar, 
- Do can initiate Skype calls
- etc etc ect

I wrote a How-To and introduction at
