### Post by cbraxton on 2013-04-05
A friend recently had this problem on a Windows 7 system.  At first nothing seemed to fix it, including uninstalling Firefox, completely removing the user profile, and installing a clean copy with no addins.

It turned out we had to disable hardware acceleration in Firefox's advanced settings to "fix" the problem, presumably this was conflicting with the display driver or something else on the system.
