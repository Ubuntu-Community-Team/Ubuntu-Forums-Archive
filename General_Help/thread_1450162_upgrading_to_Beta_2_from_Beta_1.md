### Post by slakkie on 2010-04-08
You should update your install on a daily basis (weekly is also acceptable) and when you do that you will end up at beta 2 level if you started at beta 1. The development versions of Ubuntu are basically rolling releases. You just update and update and then you will hit every milestone. If you update your current Lucid release now you will reach beta 2 stage, if no updates are available you are already at beta 2. But looking at the release schedule you already had the big changes happening before april 1st (the beta 2 freeze).

[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/LucidReleaseSchedule](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/LucidReleaseSchedule)
