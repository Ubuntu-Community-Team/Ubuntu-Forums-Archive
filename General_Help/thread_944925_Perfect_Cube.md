### Post by prshah on 2008-10-12
> **icyest said:**
> My Desktop doesn't uite look like a cube, its a rectangle. How do I solve this? It bends and warps the cube.


Open System-Preferences-Advanced Desktop Effects Settings-General-Desktop Size and ensure that "Horizontal Virtual Size" is set to 4 and the rest to 1.

Changes take effect immediately, no need for a restart. Is it ok now? Post back otherwise.
