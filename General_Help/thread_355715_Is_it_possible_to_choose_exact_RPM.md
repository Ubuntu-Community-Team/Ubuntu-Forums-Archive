### Post by Imsati on 2007-02-07
Why would you want to? Most Mobo's control the fan speed based on CPU usage/temp automatically. Say, for example, your CPU is running at a temp that requires 2800 RPM to dissipate heat at the best efficiency. You want to lower that to 2550 RPM, and therefore degrade the efficiency of the heat dissipation?

I'm rather confused why you would want to operate at anything other than the setting for the coolest CPU.
