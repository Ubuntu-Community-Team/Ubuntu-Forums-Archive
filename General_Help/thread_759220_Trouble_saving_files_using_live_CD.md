### Post by kyphi on 2008-04-19
That could very well be the fault of the USB drive.

USB drives can fail suddenly or they can be read in one computer but not another or by one operating system and not another.

There are lots of questions on the sudden failure of USB drives being recognised but no answers.

It could be an age-related issue - they do not last forever or a driver corruption.

Flash drives really are not drives - there are no moving parts.
