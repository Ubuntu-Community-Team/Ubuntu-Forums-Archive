I've no problem with making the default Ubuntu theme better, but please, no dock by default. The idea is to make Ubuntu look better than OSX, not to copy it. Lots of people like docks, I know, but I find them a poor solution - trying to be both a launcher and a application switcher at the same time, doing neither well and taking up lots of screen space. 

Sure, the existing menus are poor (personally I use USP2 which I find a big improvement) but that doesn't mean we need to bolt on a dock. Let's come up with our own solution. If people want a dock, that's what customisation is for.
