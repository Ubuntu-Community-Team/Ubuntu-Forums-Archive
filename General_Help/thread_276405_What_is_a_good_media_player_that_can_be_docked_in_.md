### Post by RikoW on 2006-10-13
There are tons of players out there, that will put an applet on the panel which at least lets you control play, pause, stop, skip forword and backward ... I assume that's what you meant by remote!?

amarok is good but also very, very powerful and might give you more than you asked for ... and it docks to the panel also under gnome

try the default ubuntu player, which is rhythmbox

banshee is nicely integrated into gnome, but it never caught on with me

currently, I'm running exaile ([www.exaile.org]("http://www.exaile.org") where you find a dapper package), has everything I need but seems "simpler" than amarok, which I used before.

They all dock to the notification area in the panel! Really it depends on your own taste, I guess you need to try and see what fits your needs/likings best.

There a others, too, but these are the ones I personally used over the last few month.

Cheers,

              Riko
