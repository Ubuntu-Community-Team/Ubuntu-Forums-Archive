### Post by julian67 on 2008-01-03
They should all be standard USB devices which will work on Linux kernel 2.4 onwards, Win 98SE onwards and Mac OS 9 onwards. It's easy to find an all in one reader that does every type of card, and it should be very cheap. I sell them in UK for £6.99 and you can buy them on ebay from Chinese sellers for very little (+ shipping of course).   Just buy the one you like, it will be fine.
