### Post by Shazaam on 2008-12-09
You can do it without a reinstall. You would use either a Ubuntu livecd or the stand-alone version of GParted (livecd). But, it will require editing of menu.lst and fstab when you are done re-partitioning. Depending on your linux skill level that can be easy or confusing (not hard). Seeing as you have only used Ubuntu for 3 weeks a reinstall would be easier unless you are on dial-up like me. :)
