EDIT:
Ummm sorry... I was thinking one thing then typed another. I did have an issue in 9.10 where my processor fan would spin down and stop, then when it got warm it would fire off at full speed and repeat. I didn't have a crash from it but maybe you are having a similar issue and it's over heating as kmsalex suggested. For me it turned out there was some sort of an issue with my motherboards "CPU Smart FAN Control" under Ubuntu and turning if off in my bios allowed the fan to turn as it should without the cycling it was doing.
