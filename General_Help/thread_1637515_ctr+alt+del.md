### Post by pme 72 on 2010-12-06
There is also a Force Quit button which allows you to click on a window to force an application to quit. Instructions for installation and use are in  Desktop User Guide/Using the Panels/Buttons.

Basically add the applet to a panel by "right-clicking any vacant space on the panel. Choose Add to Panel, then choose Force Quit from the Add to Panel dialog."

"To terminate and application, click on the Force Quit button, then click on a window from the application that you want to terminate. If you do not want to terminate an application after you have clicked on the Force Quite button, press Esc."
