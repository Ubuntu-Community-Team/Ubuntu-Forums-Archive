### Post by daou on 2006-11-21
There are utilities which allow you to save to NTFS from Ubuntu. For example [here]("http://ubuntuos.wordpress.com/2006/08/02/howto-write-to-windows-ntfs-drive-from-ubuntu-ntfs-3g/").

Some will warn you about some instabilities concerning writing to NTFS, but these are rare and most bugs have been ironed out by now.
