### Post by egeezer on 2014-01-30
Your 11.10 installation passed its end-of-life almost a year ago, and is no longer supported.  You would do well to back up just your data on a thumb drive and consider installing a distro that is currently supported.  At the moment, the two supported Ubuntu distros are 12.04LTS (which will reach 12.04.4 one week from today) and 13.10.  If you plan to keep your next installation for any significant length of time, I would suggest waiting one week and installing 12.04.4.  That will be supported until 2017.
