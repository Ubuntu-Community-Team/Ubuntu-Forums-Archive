### Post by adamant715 on 2008-07-30
Well to do that you need to install CompizFusion from [here]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/CompositeManager/CompizFusion"). Then you go to System => Preferences => Appearance and go to the Effects Tab. Click "Good" or "Extra". Then you go to System => Preferences => Advanced Desktop Effects settings. and  the 3D cube is in there. I may be wrong. I'm not much of an Ubuntu Genius.
