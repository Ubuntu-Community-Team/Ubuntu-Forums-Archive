### Post by Fass on 2006-08-10
About break.com, mplayerplug-in works well with them. No slowness or choppiness, and if you add "zoom=yes" to your ~/.mplayer/config file, you can fullscreen them at your leisure as well.

It's in the repos, and is called "mozilla-mplayer."
