### Post by /\/\@/\/ |_| on 2009-07-15
How much RAM you have ? I think swap can increase speed only to a certain extent , coz its used mostly during context switching when you dont have much space left in RAM. So if you have a lot of data in swap , that means it is going to take some time to switch the data from swap to RAM and vice versa .
Try to check the amount of RAM and swap space used using 'top' command.
