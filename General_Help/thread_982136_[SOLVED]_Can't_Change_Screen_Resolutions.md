### Post by prshah on 2008-11-14
> **cybrsaylr said:**
> 
Tried changing it to 640x480 to see if I could change the refresh rate back to 70Hz.
In doing that I'm now locked in at 640x480 screen resolution and can't make any changes because I can't get to the 'appy changes' box, at the bottom, to make changes!

a) You can make the changes, then just press Alt+A to trigger the "Apply" button.

b) If you have compiz running, and the scale plugin enabled, you can press Win+P to "shrink" the window to 1/2 it's size. Win+Shift+P will bring it back to normal size.
