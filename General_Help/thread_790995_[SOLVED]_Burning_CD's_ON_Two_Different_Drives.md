### Post by russo.mic on 2008-05-12
This is really simple.  Most burning ROMS from any OS i know of can do this.  Simply set one to the be source drive, and one to be the write drive in the burning rom.  I can't imagine the system 76 guys didn't know this.  One could even just be a cheap CD-ROM, you don't need two burners.
