### Post by fragos on 2009-10-30
Right click the Bluetooth icon in the applet bar and you'll have access to what you need. You will need to pair your phone or other Bluetooth device using those menues. Chances are you'll have to tell your phone to be discoverable or the pairing won't work. Likely your phone will only enable discovery for a fixed time. Some Bluetooth devices are always discoverable.
