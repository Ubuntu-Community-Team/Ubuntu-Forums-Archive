Open your webbrowser and go to gmail.com.
Log in with your user account and password
On the top right of the page you find the word settings, click it.
On the next page use the TAB Forwards and settings Pop and Imap (roughly translated, I use a Dutch screen here)
For both POP and IMAP you find settings for several E-mail clients. Pick the one you use and follow the on screen help to setup the mail for your client.
