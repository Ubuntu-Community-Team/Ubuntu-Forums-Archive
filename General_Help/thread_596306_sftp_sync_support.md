### Post by p_quarles on 2007-10-29
There's always rsync. It doesn't support sftp specifically, but you can direct it to connect through ssh, like sftp does. It's one of the more powerful command line tools, which means it's also pretty complicated. There's a Gnome front-end for it called grsync, if that's of any interest.
