### Post by bobonthenet on 2009-03-02
I found a solution to my problem thanks to a friend.  It was actually extremely simple.  First of all to explain in a little more detail.  My linux pc is connected to the residential gateway that ATT provides using a wired ethernet connection.  I previously had Comcast internet but switched to ATT hoping for a better deal.  When the tech guy was here we tested the network with my windows laptop which worked without any additional configuration and after he left I tried to use my Ubuntu pc to no avail until this morning.  To resolve this problem I simply typed "sudo dhclient" even after using Ubuntu for several years I still feel like a noob.
