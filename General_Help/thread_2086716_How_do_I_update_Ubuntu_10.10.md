### Post by Hagar Delest on 2012-11-23
Upgrade and:
sudo apt-get install gnome-shell and enjoy Gnome 3!
In the session log-in change the default session (you should have the possibility to have the old look and feel IIRC).

NB: for a distro upgrade, better install from scratch IMHO. A soft upgrade may leave remnants of the former flavor and lead to troubles (personal experience). Partitioning is a key point for upgrades and to avoid losing your documents.
