### Post by Xian on 2006-03-11
Use the Gimp to acquire your screenshot and then when saving the image, just compress it (if .png) or select a lower image quality (if .jpeg).

---

### Post by Zeroangel on 2006-03-11
Yea, PNG is lossless but to get it under 300k you would have to resize it. Just go with JPEG at 80 - 90 percent quality. You won't even notice the JPEG noise in almost all areas of the picture if you set to 85'ish, but sharply contrasting colors might look washed out.

Depends on your screen rez though. If you're running at a high resolution then maybe resize to 800 x 600. If you're just showing off your pimped out ubuntu install there is really no point.
