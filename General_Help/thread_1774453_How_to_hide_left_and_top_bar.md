### Post by ajgreeny on 2011-06-03
You could always use the classic desktop from the login screen sessions menu instead of unity, which at the moment seems to be a "work in progress" to me.

That will give you the standard desktop that you were using on previous ubuntu versions, and you can customise that to your heart's content, just like before.
