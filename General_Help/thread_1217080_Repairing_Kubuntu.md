### Post by rocket16 on 2009-07-20
Hello! To repair Kubuntu, switch to GNOME.Now, go to System>Administration>Synaptic Package Manager and search for the package :Kubuntu-Desktop"  . Now, click on it, and select "Mark for Reinstallation". Now, it will get repaired.
