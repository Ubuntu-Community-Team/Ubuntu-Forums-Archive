### Post by Tim Sharitt on 2009-01-15

### Post by vegetarianshrimp on 2009-01-15
If you decided you want to go back to ubuntu and you want to get rid of xubuntu-desktop, removing just the that package (xubuntu-desktop) doesn't remove all the xubuntu files. You will have to do [this]("http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/puregnome"). Or, if you like xubuntu and want to get rid of the ubuntu files, do [this]("http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/purexfce")
Thanks to [taurus]("http://ubuntuforums.org/member.php?u=43398") for telling me how to do [this]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=998162")

Remember to [mark your this thread as solved]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=4527051&postcount=6")
