### Post by Tamlynmac on 2009-06-08
> deck
I noticed that OO.org has a setting for e-mail of "sensible-ooomua"; what is this and can it be edited to fix the problem.

Your on target. left click on the button containing four dots to the right of the box containing "sensible-ooomua" listed in "tools/options/Internet/E-mail".  Find your e-mail program executable double click on the executable and it should replace the "sensible-ooomua" then left click on OK at the bottom.

I don't use Thunderbird but if the executable isn't located in your directory, look in file system/usr/bin.

A number of bug issues have been noted (search Google) regarding this. However, I would attempt the above procedure first.

Good Luck and I hope this helps.
