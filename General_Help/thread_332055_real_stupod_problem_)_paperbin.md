### Post by jocko on 2007-01-05
Do you mean you lost the trash can icon on your desktop?

In that case start gconf-editor (if you use gnome, I don't know how to do it in kde). (to start it, hit alt+F2 and type "gconf-editor" in the "run program" dialogue). Activate the checkbox next to the key /apps/nautilus/desktop/trash_icon_visible.


Or did you loose it from your panel? If that's the case, just right click the panel and click "add to panel". Select the trash can and click "add".
