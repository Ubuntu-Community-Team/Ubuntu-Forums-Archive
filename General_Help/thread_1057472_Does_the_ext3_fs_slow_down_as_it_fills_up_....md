### Post by mb_webguy on 2009-02-01
Not really.  I mean, if you're using upwards of 95% or so of your available space, then things might slow down a bit, but that's true of any filesystem.  But since ext3 minimizes fragmentation, it doesn't have to do a lot of jumping around to grab all of the bits of a file like ntfs has to do.
