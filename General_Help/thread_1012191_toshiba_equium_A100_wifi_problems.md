### Post by john183 on 2008-12-15
First off, just to let you know subject lines like yours are most likely going to be looked over because there is no indication of what your issue is. It helps if put something like "wifi issue with "laptop model," switch doesn't work"

But down to your issue. It may be possible that the switch it actually broken, I have a toshiba satellite laptop that has a wifi switch also, and i had to take it apart because the external switch mechanism had come off the actual switch on the motherboard. If all else fails just right click on the networking icon in your notification area a de-select the "Enable Wireless" opeion. that will shut off that wifi card at the os level.
