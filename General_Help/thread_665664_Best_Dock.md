### Post by cmnorton on 2008-01-12
I know of good manufactures from whom I've purchased KVM switches, print servers, and so on, who offer docking products, but I do not know the quality of the docking units, because I have purchased and used one from these manufacturers: Kensington, Hawking, Targus, and Belkin. Of course, if your laptop came with its own dock, or you can get one, that might have some advantages.

On a completely tangent, please note that having a dock plugged into some KVM switches (like Belkin) can cause a delay in keyboard and mouse response when switching back from another system.
