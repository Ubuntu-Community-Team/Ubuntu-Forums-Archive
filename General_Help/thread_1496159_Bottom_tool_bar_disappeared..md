### Post by jerenept on 2010-05-28
sorry to nitpick, but it _is_ called a panel.
Anyway, all you need to do is to right-click the panel 'at the top' of the screen, and select 'new panel'. 
you will need to restore what was on the panel before, so right-click on the new (blank) panel, and select 'add to panel'
ADD (by dragging the icon from the list to the panel):
Show Desktop button
Window List
Workspace Switcher
Trash

In that order, left to right.
