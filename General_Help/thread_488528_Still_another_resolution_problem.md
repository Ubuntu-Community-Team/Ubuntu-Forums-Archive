### Post by Wim Sturkenboom on 2007-07-05
Check the VertRefresh and HorizSync in your xorg.conf. They should match the specs of your monitor.

[edit]
For 1280x1024 to be valid at 60Hz vertical refresh, the maximum HorizSync should be greater than (roughly) 63 kHz.
[/edit]


PS
You might have to add the following line to the *device section* in your xorg.conf if the above (on its own) does not give the required result
```
Option "UseEdidFreqs" "False"

```
