### Post by ahaslam on 2007-04-16
Enter 'winecfg' in a terminal, go to Drives, select or add your cdrom ensuring the mount path is correct, click Show Advanced & select cdrom from the different types.

Did you know that there's a native application called tovid that does everything you mentioned?
