### Post by Frogs Hair on 2011-12-14
I have not seen this , as far as I know a connection is established only when  radio is selected from the left side pane and terminated when the quit selection is used .

I also use Last.fm and because I store no password I have allow the stream each time it is used . I don't have the Magnatune  plug-in enabled , which is the only music store option I have by default . The IM status plug-in will also connect to the internet and  both of these can be disabled from Edit > Plug-ins in Rhythbox .

Ubuntu one has a disconnect option and  some dictionaries will auto connect as well unless they are locally stored . I think the help center / Ubuntu desktop guide also connects to the internet .
