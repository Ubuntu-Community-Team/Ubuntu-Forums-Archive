### Post by rsambuca on 2007-09-18
I would suggest first browsing through the "Add/Remove Programs" menu.  If you go to the top menu bar, select "Applications -> Add/Remove..."

You will see a variety of categories that contain many, many programs with brief descriptions.  All you have to do is select the desired program(s) and click apply.  They will then be downloaded and automatically installed for you!  Perhaps check there first for an Irfanview replacement, and if you can't seem to find an equivalent, let us know.
