### Post by bark50 on 2010-01-25
I also had the problem of my volume applet not responding to volume changes on the slider.  What worked for me:

1.Go to System > Preferences > Sound > Hardware.
2.At the bottom of the box is something that says, “Settings for the selected device:” and underneath that a drop-down menu.
3.My drop-down menu was originally set for “Analog Stereo Duplex.”  I changed it to “Digital Stereo Duplex” and that got my applet volume control slider working.

If fiddling with those settings doesn't help with your problem, then sorry but I can't help.  I'm a novice here!
