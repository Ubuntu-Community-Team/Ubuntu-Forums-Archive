### Post by lisati on 2011-11-21
The menu is provided by a program called "grub". The idea is to provide you with options when you start your computer. The good news is that the menu can be hidden. Have a look here: [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Grub2#Boot_Display_Behavior](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Grub2#Boot_Display_Behavior)
