### Post by rewyllys on 2012-08-25
> **KaisaUbun2 said:**
> I was hoping more for like an easy read book that you can learn from, thanks though guys and girls.
I highly recommend *Ubuntu Made Easy: A Project-Based Introduction to Linux*, by Rickford Grant with Phil Bull; published in 2012 by No Starch Press, San Francisco. ISBN: 978-1-59327-425-2.

All of Grant's books are very readable, and they cover a wide range of things that can be done with Linux (in particular, Ubuntu) and FOSS programs.

A glance at Amazon.com's listing of the book's table of contents will give you a more detailed idea of its coverage: [http://www.amazon.com/Ubuntu-Made-Easy-Project-Based-Introduction/dp/1593274254/ref=sr_1_1?s=books&ie=UTF8&qid=1345934829&sr=1-1&keywords=ubuntu+made+easy](http://www.amazon.com/Ubuntu-Made-Easy-Project-Based-Introduction/dp/1593274254/ref=sr_1_1?s=books&ie=UTF8&qid=1345934829&sr=1-1&keywords=ubuntu+made+easy)
