### Post by gettinoriginal on 2009-06-03
Not really sure from your pics which one is supposed to be the desktop.  Are you wanting compiz or nautilus to draw the desktop ??  If you want compiz to draw it, then open your compizcomfig-settings-manager, choose Utility > Wallpaper, open it and choose the wallpaper you want.  Then open configuration editor > Apps > Nautilus > preferences, and untick "show desktop".  If you are wanting nautilus to draw it, make sure that "show desktop" is ticked in conf editor and that you do not have any selections for wallpaper in compiz.  ;)
