### Post by loukingjr on 2010-11-28
I'm running various ubuntu guests but I had a question about Lubuntu. How or where do I change the default behaviour when I dbl-click the title bar in LXDE? Right now it maximizes/minimizes the window. I would prefer it roll up/down.

thanks

---

### Post by Wobblybob on 2010-12-10
Just installed Lubuntu myself and found this option under [Preferences], [Openbox Configuration Manager], [Mouse] and changed [Double click on titlebar] from   "Maximizes the window" to "Shades the window" I found changing the time from 200 to 300 improved the option.
