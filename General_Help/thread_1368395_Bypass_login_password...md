### Post by scouser73 on 2009-12-30
Hi Robert, go to **System > Administration > Login Screen** click **Unlock** and enter your password.

Select **Login As** **[COLOR="Red"][USERNAME][/COLOR]** **Automatically**

You will now have set up automatic login.
