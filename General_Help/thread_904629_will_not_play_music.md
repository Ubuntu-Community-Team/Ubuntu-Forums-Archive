### Post by cooke77 on 2008-08-29
All bar the Start-up/Shut-down Sounds are 'disabled'...by default.
Quickest way to find out if you really do have NO Sound...is to play a Music CD.
When inserted, open up your Music Player (whatever it may be)...and, select......to play it.

IF you want 'default sounds'..like those of Windows.......look in Sounds. (You may have to use "Administrative Mode"...to Enable them.)
