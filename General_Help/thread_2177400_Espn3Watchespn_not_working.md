### Post by Eromatic on 2014-02-15
For anyone that happens to stumble upon this thread, the solution for me with ESPN3 was to install HAL (Hardware Abstraction Layer), clear the .adobe Flash Player cache, and then do the ESPN3 login process again. Make sure to later enable popups in your webbrowser (FireFox) for ESPN3, which will then give you the player window.

There is some information in relation to this also on Adobe's support site:
[http://helpx.adobe.com/x-productkb/multi/flash-player-11-problems-playing.html](http://helpx.adobe.com/x-productkb/multi/flash-player-11-problems-playing.html)
