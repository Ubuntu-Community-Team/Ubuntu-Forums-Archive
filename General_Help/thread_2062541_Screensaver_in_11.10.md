### Post by newb85 on 2012-09-25
Did you use the upgrade feature in Update Manager, or did you do a fresh install?   Recent versions of Ubuntu don't come with screensavers installed by default.

If all you're looking for is the control for automatically turning the screen off, it's in System Settings under Brightness and Lock.  (Click the button in the top-right corner of the screen to find System Settings.  Or you could just hit the Super(Windows) key and start typing Brightness...)
