This is what was missing (for me) to understand it properly.

u want the resolution to be dynamic and correct according to u'r screen.

- start the virtual machine(the xp installation).
- [B]start full screen mode.
- in the bottom of the screen[/B] u can see "Devices"
 (if u can't get there with the mouse press the right ctrl button and try again)

- select "install guest addition" 
now just agree to whatever the installer asks u.
