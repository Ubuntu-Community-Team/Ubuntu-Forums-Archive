### Post by Joe of loath on 2011-05-09
Seems it installed a UK dictionary for some reason.

Open up a text entry box (Such as a reply to this thread), right click it, click 'languages', then 'Add Dictionaries'. Then find the one you want, click install, and follow the prompts. It'll ask to restart, so do so, grab your text entry box, open the languages menu, and select the dictionary you installed. Done :D
