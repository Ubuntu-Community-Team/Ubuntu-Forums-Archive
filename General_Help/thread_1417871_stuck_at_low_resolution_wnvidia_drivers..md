### Post by frankm on 2010-02-28
After 24 hours of searching every forum on the internet and banging my head against the wall repeatedly I was finally able to figure it out...

For some reason or other I had to manually reconfigure my monitor and disable EDID

here's my new settings

```

# nvidia-xconfig:  version 1.0  (buildmeister@builder58)  Wed Dec  9 16:34:26 PST 2009

# nvidia-settings:  version 1.0  (buildd@crested)  Mon Nov  3 08:46:04 UTC 2008
```
