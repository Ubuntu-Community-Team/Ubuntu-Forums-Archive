### Post by Pipps on 2009-04-28
In OpenOffice, I used to be able to click 'File' > 'Print', and then have an option in the main dropdown window for 'Print to PDF', as well as the option for my installed printer.

I find that I no longer have the 'print to PDF' option. How can this be reinstated?

Thank you in anticipation!

---

### Post by darthmob on 2009-04-28
you don't really need it in openoffice as you can directly export to pdf.
simply click on *file -> export as pdf* or on the pdf symbol in the toolbar.
