### Post by Herman on 2011-06-17
Yes, you can certainly do that, most people would find that easier. That way you can 'see' what you're doing better, (or at least a graphical representation of it).
There's no reason why not and being able to 'see' what you're doing tends to make the operation safer too, less likely-hood of accidentally wiping out something important that you wanted to keep.
