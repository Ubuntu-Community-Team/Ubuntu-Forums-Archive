### Post by 2hot6ft2 on 2010-04-10
I don't think you can since some versions between 7.04 and 9.10 are no longer supported so a step by step upgrade wont work.
7.04 > 7.10 > 8.04 > 8.10 > 9.04 > 9.10

You may be able to backup your home folder and restore it after a fresh install but I suspect you would run into a bunch of errors because a LOT has changed in that time.

I also don't believe a 7.04 > 9.10 direct upgrade is going to work at all.

So a fresh install of 9.10 is what's left.
