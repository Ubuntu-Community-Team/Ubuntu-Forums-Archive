### Post by stig on 2005-07-14
How embarrassing! Having just written the above plea for help I now find that I was assuming too much about Scribus - it needs some setting of the Preferences to get it to work properly. I never even looked at them because I had assumed the default settings should work.

Under Settings, Preferences, General if I change "GUI" from Default to Windows I get a usable display - as they say, you live and learn! Sorry if I wasted anyone's time.

But let me take this opportunity of saying that Ubuntu is wonderful after trying Mandriva and Red Hat and a couple of lesser known distros.
