### Post by coffeecat on 2018-05-06
> **kerry_s said:**
> elementary os is based on ubuntu 16 lts, so still ubuntu.

Elementary OS is **not** Ubuntu. Neither is it one of the [official flavours of Ubuntu](https://www.ubuntu.com/download/flavours). It is simply one of over 60 distros listed on Distrowatch which is based on Ubuntu. Whilst it is, no doubt, an excellent distro in its own right - something that can also be said for many of the other Ubuntu-derived distros - it is misleading to say it is Ubuntu. Just as it would be misleading to say that Ubuntu is Debian.
