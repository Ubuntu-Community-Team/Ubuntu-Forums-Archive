### Post by seawolf167 on 2011-04-19
The updates & upgrades 

i.e.

```
sudo apt-get update
sudo apt-get upgrade
```

are a good to keep current on, the dist upgrades are optional and you only need to do that if you want too.

I.e. If it is asking you to install updates, accept them and install. If it is asking you to upgrade your distribution, you can say no.

### Post by ajgreeny on 2011-04-19
Ordinary upgrade?  Yes.

Distro upgrade to 11.04?  Not yet in my opinion.  Wait a week or two after the final version is released, then have a look at it.

I would always suggest a clean install, not a distro upgrade, but that is another question entirely; some will upgrade to 11.04 successfully, others will have numerous problems after doing so.  It's your choice!
