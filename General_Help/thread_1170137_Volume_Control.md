### Post by 73ckn797 on 2009-05-25
Right click on the speaker icon in the upper panel. Go to volume control and set all levels to max and make certain that no settings are muted individually.

### Post by ASchweitzer on 2009-05-26
While doing that, though, make sure to net set them too loud. If you open System > Preferences > Sound, and play a test sound, you will notice a pitch change if you pull all the sliders to max. Even pulling a single slider down (such as PCM) will eliminate this.
Having all the sliders at max will cause distortion, which you will notice soon :)
