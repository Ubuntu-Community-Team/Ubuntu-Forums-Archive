### Post by kaginken on 2008-02-16
That's plenty of horsepower for gutsy - what does the System Monitor show?  Is your processor busy?  Is your memory nearly full?  There must be something misconfigured or a process hung...reboot.

If nothing else helps - try disabling the compiz 'special effects' eye candy. (System -> Preferences -> Appearance -> Visual Effects).  This locks up most of my laptops as none have very high end graphics cards.  

You can also go into the Sessions (System -> Preferences -> Sessions) and uncheck any services that you don't need, i.e. bluetooth (if you don't need it), evolution mail notifier (if you don't use evolution), etc.  BE CAREFUL though, most of these services are important.  When in doubt, don't uncheck it.  :)

Hope this helps!
