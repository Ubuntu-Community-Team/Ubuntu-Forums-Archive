Where can I find more icons to use in the menu and where are the ones stored that are used?

---

### Post by yetiman64 on 2011-01-22
My favourite 3 places to start looking are /usr/share/icons, /usr/share/app-install/icons and /usr/share/pixmaps. There are no doubt many more locations to discover.

Checking the icon in the launcher Properties window will often indicate where it is stored in the filesystem.
