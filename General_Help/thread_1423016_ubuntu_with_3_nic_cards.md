### Post by Fenris_rising on 2010-03-06
I have 2 cards one to the net and the other to my xbox.

My router has an IP of 192.168.0.1 so my DHCP addresses always end 192.168.0.?

My second nic is a static IP and I set this to an address like 192.168.1.1 My xbox on the end of this is 192.168.1.10

The third figure (1) prevents IP conflicts occuring which may cause connection problems.

So I would make your non internet NIC static but my experience is limited on this to what I do

hope that helps
