### Post by rudyj on 2007-01-10
whoops nevermind, I found the problem.  In case anyone else had this problem:

I had to change the section in xorg that said 

Section "Extensions"
    Option        "Composite" "Enable"
EndSection

since these proprietary drivers don't support composite yet I had to disable that:

Section "Extensions"
    Option        "Composite" "0"
EndSection
