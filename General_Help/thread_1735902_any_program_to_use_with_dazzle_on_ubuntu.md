### Post by BuntuNooob on 2011-06-21
I've had some success with tvtime to view the video and recordmydesktop to capture it.  Unfortunately , I have had some issues with getting sound to record with the video. But there is a work around. If you take the composite audio leads and instead of plugging them into  dazzle, plug them into a rca to 3.5mm audio converter. Then take that to your microphone port on your computer. Then recordmydesktop will record the audio just fine. Don't forget to turn the audio down in the pulseaudio settings or it will be really loud and will record poorly. 
 
I hope that helps.
