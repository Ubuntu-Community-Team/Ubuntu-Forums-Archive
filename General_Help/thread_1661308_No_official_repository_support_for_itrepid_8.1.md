### Post by drs305 on 2011-01-06
You can change the part of your repository address to "http://old-releases.ubuntu.com/ubuntu/dists/" in /etc/apt/sources.list.


Of course the packages therein are no longer udpated but the last packages are preserved in this location and can be installed to your system.
