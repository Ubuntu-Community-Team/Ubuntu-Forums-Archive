### Post by Favux on 2011-04-03
Hi Ocarina,

I just went through doing that with tristram60 on this thread:  [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1719514](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1719514)  So like him you could use a method 1 script.

We're working off the [Rotation HOW TO]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=6274392&postcount=1").  Magick Rotation (method 4) will also work for you.  Except the tc4200 & 4400 report the hinge value backwards from other HP tablet PCs.  So before you rotate with Magick, if you decide to try it, right click on the green arrow icon in the tray and chose Setup.  Check the box in front of *BIOS hinge switch values reversed?* and Save.

The same would apply with the *Tablet PC Automatic Rotation Script* (method 5) except 0 becomes 1 and 1 0.
