### Post by george.brindeiro on 2011-11-28
I just solved this issue accidentally, so I decided to share the solution.

Turns out that I had pressed the CTRL+SHIFT+B key combination, which changes an option (obscure to me) from LINE to BLOCK.
I think it's quite standard for text editors, but even knowing the shortcut I couldn't find out what it is easily.

Anyway, hope this helps someone else.
