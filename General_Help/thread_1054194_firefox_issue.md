### Post by cariboo on 2009-02-16
Your issue isn't with firefox, it is a compiz problem. I've had the problem, but it went a way with and update. You may want to stop compiz, then remove the .compiz directory, then restart compiz.

To stop compiz right-click on the desktop and select change desktop background, then click the Visual Effects tab and set it to none. Next open nautilus (Places-->Home folder) the press Alt-h to reveal the hidden files and directories and delete .compiz. Log out and then login again and check to see if your problem is solved.
