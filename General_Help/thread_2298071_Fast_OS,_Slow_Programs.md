### Post by coldraven on 2015-10-09
I suppose that you could have bad sectors on your hard drive. To check the drives status run "Disks" and select "SMART Data" from the cog icon in the top right corner.
There is a summary on the page, mine says "Assessment: Disk is OK, 35 bad sectors (38° C/100° F)".
It might not be this but looking will not hurt.
