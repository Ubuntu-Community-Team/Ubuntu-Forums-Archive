### Post by coldraven on 2011-01-13
I know nothing about icom but when I cannot find installed utilities this is what I do.
Open the System>Admin>Synaptic Package Manager .
Type "icom" in the text box.
Right click on the installed package and select Properties.
Then open the "Installed files" tab.

Quite often you will discover some handy documents as well.
I hope this helps.
