### Post by Ghost_Mazal on 2012-05-10
Ok I found the procedure. Here it is for someone else who also struggles.

On the phone go to Wireless and Networks
Then go to Tethering and portable hotspot (there's no usb menu entry)
But it does say usb in the desciption.
Then tick on USB tethering so that it's marked.
Then pull down the blue menu.
Then tick on usb
Then tick on connect storage to pc.

All of this on the phone.
