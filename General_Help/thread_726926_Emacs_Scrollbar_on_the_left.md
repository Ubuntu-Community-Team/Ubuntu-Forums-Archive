### Post by scramasax on 2008-03-17
The only place I've ever seen that is in GNUstep applications:

[http://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/f/f5/Gnustep.png](http://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/f/f5/Gnustep.png)

That was a hangover from NeXTSTEP.   AFAIK, the reason was that, with a left-to-right language like English, you can have an application window partially off-screen to the *right* (where you can still see the beginnings of lines of some text in a window (which is more important than what comes at the ends of lines for identifying which window you want)) and still access the scrollbar.

Never come across it elsewhere.
