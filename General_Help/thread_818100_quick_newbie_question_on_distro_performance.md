### Post by bingoUV on 2008-06-05
yes, but different linux distributions provide different window managers as defaults. Puppy runs JWM, or ICEWM as default. Much lighter than XFCE, GNOME etc. 

There could be other optimizations too. A light theme and light equivalents of other applications go a long way in speeding up the distro.
