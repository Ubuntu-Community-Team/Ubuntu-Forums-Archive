### Post by Dougie187 on 2009-03-01
Well, I couldn't tell you if it works this way in hardy, but in intrepid it appears to be a setting in Keyboard Accessibility.

If you go into System->Preferences->Assitive Technologies
And then Select Keyboard Accessibility

Uncheck the box "Accessibility features can be toggled with keyboard shortcuts" and check the box "Simulate Simultaneous keypresses"

This should help fix your issue.

### Post by Dougie187 on 2009-03-07
Well, if it is required you might think about upgrading to intrepid.

As for the usefulness of sticky keys, it can be really useful for people with hand problems. Maybe they can't stretch their fingers that far and would rather not try to hold down Ctrl+Alt to hit d for the desktop, or maybe Ctrl+Alt to hit L to Lock the screen. So instead, they push ctrl, then they push alt, then they hit L and it does the same thing. That way you don't have to hold all of them down.
