### Post by finferflu on 2006-12-18
You could try installing Kubuntu, it seems that KDE is way faster than Gnome on my machine. In fact I've switched to Kubuntu for good!

In a terminal just type:

```

```

Then log out, and at the log in choose KDE instead of Gnome, from the menu Session Type (if I remember correctly).
