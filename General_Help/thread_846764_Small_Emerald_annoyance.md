### Post by tuxxy on 2008-07-02
FIXED! =P

In the end after the re-install of emerald, I thought of anything else that could be having an effect and remembered the script for themes I downloaded from these forums was not so long ago installed.

I removed all these themes from synaptic and re-installed Emerald, whoooo its back in all its beauty! smooth animations and transitions.

I cant say why it made emerald stop functioning although I think the script was designed for an earlier ubuntu, however the script author claimed this was no problem so I advise people in future please test these scripts before releasing and yes themese can have an effect on Emerald without even being loaded :confused:
