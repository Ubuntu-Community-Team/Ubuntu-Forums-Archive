### Post by missbliss on 2009-04-02
Okay, well I was adding some widgets to the right corner of my top panel and accidentally deleted these icons: RhythmBox, Update Icon, Pidgin Icon. 


So, before when I ran RhythmBox it would create an icon in the right corner of the panel which I could click to clear it from the desktop and still be running.  Same with Pidgin.  How do I get these back??  I've also noticed the update icon hasn't been around and there is usually an update or two every few days.  


Suggestions?

---

### Post by blackened on 2009-04-02
Right click on the panel, select "Add to Panel", then select "Notification Area" and press the "Add" button.

---

### Post by lindsay7 on 2009-04-02
Go to the application menu and find the program. right click on it and choose add to panel.
