### Post by kokonobs on 2010-06-03
This looks like a gold mine for later use.

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=205449&highlight=low+sound]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=205449&highlight=low+sound")



1. To find the sound settings go to System>Preferences>Sound

There you can increase the volume to your hearts  desire. From my experience you will not get it as high as windows for some reason. 

2. In the terminal (Applications>Accessories>Terminal)
Type ```
alsamixer
```

It will bring up a mixer and look on the top right for instructions on navigation and increase the ones u need to your hearts desire.
