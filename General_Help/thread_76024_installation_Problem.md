### Post by vayu on 2005-10-14
It could be your graphics driver and/or your xorg configuration file.
Can you use ctrl-alt-f2 to get to a login prompt?  If not can you restart and choose recovery mode.

Can you post the file /etc/X11/xorg.conf?  Also describe which video card you have.  
There are also two other files useful to check:  ~/.xsession-errors and /var/log/Xorg.0.log
