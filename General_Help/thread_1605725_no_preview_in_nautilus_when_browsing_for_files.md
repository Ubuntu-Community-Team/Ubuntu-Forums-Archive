### Post by yetiman64 on 2010-10-25
> **princeofnam said:**
> often times when I browse for files from a site nautilus will open up and i can't see a picture preview of the files.   this makes choosing photos to upload nearly impossible.

does nautilus elementary fix this?
From any nautilus window have a look at the settings in Edit > Preferences > Preview tab, the default setting is for local files only. Under the section "Other Previewable Files" try setting "Show Thumbnails" to "Always". Note that on remote sites this can take quite a while to generate a thumbnail for previewing (probably why the default is "Local Files Only").

Edit: just as an afterthought you may need to install ubuntu-restricted-extras to preview some non standard (particularly MS filetype) video files.
