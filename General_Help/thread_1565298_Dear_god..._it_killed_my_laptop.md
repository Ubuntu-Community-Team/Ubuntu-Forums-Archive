### Post by Bryan55 on 2010-08-31
This is a known issue with the Aspire One net books.

The Black Screen of Death
You just need to  flash the bios.

See here
[http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=l6GRhsvP-og&NR=1&feature=fvwp](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=l6GRhsvP-og&NR=1&feature=fvwp)

and here is Acer's instructions how to do it.
[http://netbooks-us.custhelp.com/app/answers/detail/a_id/133/~/update-bios-on-aoa110-or-aoa150]("http://netbooks-us.custhelp.com/app/answers/detail/a_id/133/%7E/update-bios-on-aoa110-or-aoa150")
