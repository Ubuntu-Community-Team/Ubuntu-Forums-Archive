### Post by QIII on 2016-03-05
Hello!

That may indicate a hardware issue, as the BIOS/EFI start occurs well before control is passed to the OS.

Did you check to be sure the monitor cables are properly connected?  They can jiggle loose.  That happened to me just last night when transferring my machine to a new case.

Do the monitors flicker and change brightness slightly when you power up?

Is your graphics card properly seated?

Are all interior cables securely seated?

Do you hear any POST beeps?

Is this a laptop or a desktop?

Could you provide us with the machine's specs?
