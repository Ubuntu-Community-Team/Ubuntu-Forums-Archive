### Post by lovinglinux on 2009-03-07
> **grubster said:**
> I tried moblock but there are no updates for this program since 2006, do you know of any other programs?

There is a person maintaining moblock stuff with regular updates and excellent support here in the forums. Check moblock's general thread at [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=803183](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=803183)

I use moblock and it works like a charm.

Alternatively, you could use [iplist]("http://iplist.sourceforge.net/"), which is very similar to peerguardian in terms of interface and functionality.

Both work just fine. Better than peerguardian for Windows in my opinion, which used to crash every time I updated the blocklists.
