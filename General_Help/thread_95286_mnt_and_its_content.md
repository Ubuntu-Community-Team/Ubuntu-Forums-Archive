### Post by frodon on 2005-11-26
Well, it depends of the users, some of us prefer to mount their partitions in /media but personally i prefer to mount them in /mnt.
Like you i prefer to have my floppy, cdroms in /media and my partitions in /mnt.
So there isn't any logic behind it, in my mind it's logic to mount my partition in /mnt but it' not the case for everybody.
