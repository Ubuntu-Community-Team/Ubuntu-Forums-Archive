### Post by stylishpants on 2008-10-31
[http://ubuntulinuxtipstricks.blogspot.com/2008/10/intrepid-update-faq.html](http://ubuntulinuxtipstricks.blogspot.com/2008/10/intrepid-update-faq.html)

Quote:

Why is my Places menu launching applications instead of opening Nautilus?

    * Right click a folder, go to Properties -> Open With, and change it back to Nautilus. There's a bug where if a folder is opened in an application, it automatically associates folders with that application on your system.
