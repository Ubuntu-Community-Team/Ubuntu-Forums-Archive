### Post by coffeecat on 2015-05-20
Did you have a further question, or were you posting in order to provide information for others with the same problem? If the latter, please post details of your hardware - make and model. The F keys used for accessing the BIOS and the BIOS/UEFI boot menu varies from make to make in UEFI systems, and the keys you mention may mislead others with different hardware.

Also - the ubuntugeek link you posted is four years old. Here is a better link for your bookmarks from the Ubuntu community documentation, and which is kept up to date by Yannubuntu, the author of boot repair and maintainer of the yannubuntu/boot-repair PPA repository.

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Boot-Repair](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Boot-Repair)
