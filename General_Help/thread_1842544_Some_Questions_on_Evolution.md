### Post by howefield on 2011-09-13
I think you would need IMAP access to your Hotmail account to get your Junk folder (or any folder other than your inbox) downloaded to your email client, Hotmail Plus accounts may offer IMAP, standard (free) accounts do not.

You should be able to add Yahoo accounts but if I recall correctly - mail from both accounts would share the same inbox.

### Post by JD8182 on 2011-09-14
I cannot comment on the hotmail issue at hand, however I had a free Yahoo email for years and wanted to keep all the email from that account, so my solution was to change my email address. I opened a free Gmail account and Gmail will import all your mail and folders from Hotmail, yahoo, AoL, etc.., and you can set up an Imap connection from Evolution to Gmail and it will then transfer all your email from Hotmail to Gmail then to Evolution. This process of importing from hotmail to gmail can take up to 2 days, however I had most of my mail with in the first day. Hopefully this helps.
