You should describe what steps you have already tried, that have "failed".

Have you tried plugging in the device and going to the Network Manager applet on your top panel (a couple of icons to the left of the clock)?

Right-click the network manager and go to "Edit Connections". Click on the Mobile Broadband tab and go to Add... then follow the steps there.

When it is all set up, you can left-click on Network Manager and select your connection.
