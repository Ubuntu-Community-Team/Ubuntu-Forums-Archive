UPDATE:  I swapped the malfunctioning mouse with an identical model - the replacement worked perfectly on my computer, while the defective mouse replicated the problem with the scroll ring (erratic behavior, mostly non-functional) on the second computer.  Kensington support (online discussion thread) was very helpful, and the technician assigned to troubleshoot the problem is sending me a free replacement - I don't even have to return the defective mouse!
