### Post by manlyman on 2008-04-29
To activate your graphic controller, turn the console on and the receiver status light should flash. Turn on the controller by sliding the power switch labelled I/O to the "I" position. The status light will flash and the controller will search for the wireless receiver. To establish a connection with the receiver, press any action button on the controller.
