### Post by anil_robo on 2006-01-02
It happens because Ubuntu is configured to play only one sound stream at a time by default. There are many tutorials here, I used the one by Gandalf, on [how to play multiple sounds in Ubuntu at the same time]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=32063"). :)
