### Post by Scott Harrison on 2012-07-26
Go ahead and try all of the online and free options that have been suggested above, they're all quality guides/references to learning the CLI.

However, if you want a handy reference, I recommend [Working at the Ubuntu Command Line Prompt]("http://www.amazon.co.uk/Working-Ubuntu-Command-Line-Prompt-ebook/dp/B004RYVI0Q/ref=sr_1_1?ie=UTF8&qid=1343305013&sr=8-1") by Keir Thomas. It's cheap but it really does the job. With that said, bash really isn't a difficult thing to learn - a bit of practice and you'll be **bash**ing away like a pro... :p
