Nope - sorry - no feedback yet, and after 4 months I didn't hope for any anymore either.  I guess one curse of the increasing popularity of Ubuntu (and the resulting increasing traffic on the forum) is that these "trivial" problems don't get a lot of attention anymore :-(

I finally fixed it by wiping my disk clean, repartitioning it, and installing 7.10 clean.  OK - I didn't do that just because the screen saver didn't work anymore, but even though I was never really happy with 7.10, it fixed the screen saver and the adept notifier problem.  Meanwhile I am on 8.04 - no screen saver or adept notifier problem there either.  But I never figured out root cause :-(

So all I can really tell you is that if you're not on 8.04 yet, you may want to consider upgrading.  Admittedly, this is a "windows-style" fix, and on Ubuntu a real fix is always possible iff you want to spend enough time learning all the idiosyncrasies of it.
