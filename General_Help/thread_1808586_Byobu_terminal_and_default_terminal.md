### Post by nothingspecial on 2011-07-21
Byobu is a configed version of gnu screen.

There are many many things you can do with it.

To get you started, you can think of it like a window manager.

Press F2 to create a new one.

Use F3 and F4 to cycle between them.

Press F8 to name them.

You can also split them and toggle what information you see on the status bar.
