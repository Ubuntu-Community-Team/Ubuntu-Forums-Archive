### Post by astrobeaver on 2010-04-29
If you have noticed that for the same resolution as with Windows, text in your Ubuntu looks "big"... here something you can try:

1) Go to System --> Preferences --> Appearance --> Fonts 

2) Click on "Details" at the bottom of the window 

3) REDUCE the Resolution (in dots per inch) to your liking and viola!

89 works great for me in Ubuntu 10.04
