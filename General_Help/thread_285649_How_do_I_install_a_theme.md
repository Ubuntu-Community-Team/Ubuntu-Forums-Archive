### Post by ayoli on 2006-10-27
for gnome themes:
1) download themes you want from [www.gnome-look.org](www.gnome-look.org)
2) uncompress it in your .themes folder (which is in your home dir: /home/your_username/.themes)
3) go to menu Systemes > Preferences > themes to select/edit themes

### Post by IYY on 2006-10-27
It's even easier than that. Download the .tar.gz file to your desktop from [www.gome-look.org](www.gome-look.org) or art.gnome.org, and then drag it into your theme list (System > Preferences > Theme to open it).

In fact, if you're getting the theme from art.gnome.org, you can drag and drop right from the website! Just drag the download link to that list, and it'll be automatically downloaded and installed. It just doesn't work with gnome-look because they don't give you a direct link to the file.
