### Post by klange on 2008-08-06
> **slugicide said:**
> Oh.  I see.  Double click is my only option.  Thanks.

Actually, no, find "exit expo", it should default to "Button3" (right click), and make it Button1. You'll have to disable the Drag & Drop option, though. Single clicking on a workspace should now exit expo to that workspace.
