### Post by dnairb on 2009-02-18
Right-click on the double-dashed vertical line (the edge of the notification area or "systray") and untick "lock to panel"
Do the same with the volume icon.

Right-click the volume icon again, select "move" and move it into the desired position.

Lock the notification area and volume icon to the panel again to prevent moving them accidentally.
