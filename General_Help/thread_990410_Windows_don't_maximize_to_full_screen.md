### Post by Uncle Che on 2008-11-28
Found a solution on this thread:
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=982133](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=982133)

A quicker way to get a solution is to go to System-Appearance and change to no effects. Next open a terminal window and type: rm -r ~/,compiz and then restart your computer, when it comes back, all is well.
