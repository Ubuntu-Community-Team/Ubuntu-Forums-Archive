### Post by miluns on 2008-08-05
Hi,

Open Evolution and look at the bottom left near the panel. There should be a small thing that looks like two cables connected. If there is a red x in between them, you are in offline mode. Just click on the x and you should be back in online mode.

Hope this helps

Mike
