### Post by ayoli on 2008-05-14
Right clic on an empty part of your panel will pop up a menu. Pick "Add to panel" then chose "window list".

In the right clic pop up menu you also can choose to create a new panel if you need.

Another solution is to use a dock like avant window navigator or cairo dock. they provide launchers, tasks (windows) list and much more with a cool design.
