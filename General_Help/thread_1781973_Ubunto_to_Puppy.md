### Post by kc1di on 2011-06-14
Hi rmcellig,

Puppy is a great little distro and I have used it many time over the years.
My biggest concern with it though is that you are always signed in a the root user.  and that is a security risk many of us are just not willing to take with our data.

Unless there is some pressing need to use puppy (say: small footprint , speed etc.) I'd stick with ubuntu or other distro that at least used sudo or su to get to the root account and doesn't us it all the time.

Cheers and have fun,
Dave
