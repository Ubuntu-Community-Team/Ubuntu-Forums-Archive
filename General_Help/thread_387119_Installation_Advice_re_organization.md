### Post by hikaricore on 2007-03-21
Personally I try and keep my home directory and my root filesystem in general clean.

I have a second drive where I keep all of my things it's layed out something like this:

/media
---------/devistate (second drive)
----------------------/archive (backups of things I don't want to lose or have to download/rip again)
---------------------------------/linux (everything from installers to tarballs)
----------------------------------------/packages (debian/ubuntu packages i feel i need to keep)
---------------------------------/windows (random windows based installers or other things i need to keep)
----------------------/avi  (all video files go here sorted into two catagories, don't know why it's called avi lol)
--------------------------------/movies (my personal dvd rips)
--------------------------------/series (mostly contains episodes of [Hidden Frontier]("http://hiddenfrontier.com/") and my Doctor Who collection.
----------------------/bin (windows based software I currently run in wine lives here)
----------------------/docs (text docs and what not that I need for whatever)
----------------------/image (self explainatory)
----------------------/linux.bin (extracted tarballs containing precompiled software in directories, this is also where I install games and such from .run files)
----------------------/mp3 (lol)
----------------------/rom (my video game backups, i have an atari/nes/snes/sega/gameboy/n64 still these are as legal as want to think)
----------------------/trash (things I don't actually care enough to delete, but usually do eventually)

It's not the best thinig ever but it keeps me sane.
