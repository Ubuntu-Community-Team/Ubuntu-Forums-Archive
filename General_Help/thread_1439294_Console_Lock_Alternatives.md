You have to encrypt a separate folder for Data.  Your home folder holds hidden files for configuration of everything under the sun.  I just called mine Data, you can name it anything but Home or your user name.  You can dismount it at anytime you leave the computer and it will work better than anything else including logging out because no one can get into it even with a Live CD.  There is a way to carry just the encryption software on a USB stick so they don't even have that to play with on your computer.  I never tried it.  The stick could get stolen or lost, then you would lose the Encryted password in the disk's own hidden config file.  There is also a way to lock your computer with a USB stick, again the problem of lost or stolen.  May you always remember your passwords.  The best wishes I can give you.
