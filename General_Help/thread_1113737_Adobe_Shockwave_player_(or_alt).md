### Post by mb_webguy on 2009-04-02
Adobe never released a Shockwave plugin for Linux, and since it's an outdated technology, it's unlikely that they ever will.

Your best bet would be to install the Windows version of Firefox under [Wine]("http://www.winehq.org/download/deb"), and install Shockwave on that.
