### Post by todak on 2009-01-14
Under **Places> Search for Files** there is a listing for **Select more options**. Click on that and enter the terms for which you wish to search. For example type ***.html** or ***.pdf** into the **Name contains** field and in the **Contains the text** field, type in the search term you want to locate, then click **Find** and you will be presented with a list of documents that contain the text you entered.
