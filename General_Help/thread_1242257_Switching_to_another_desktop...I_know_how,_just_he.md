### Post by CatKiller on 2009-08-17
Drawing screensavers on the background means displaying them on the *root window*. Which is generally taken up by Nautilus drawing the wallpaper and icons on the desktop. You can't have both at once. As you've found both ways now; having the wallpaper and icons means that you don't have the Matrix screensaver, and having the Matrix screensaver means that you don't have wallpaper or icons. I have no idea how you've managed to get an instance of each, though.
