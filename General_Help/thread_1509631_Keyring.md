### Post by sites on 2010-06-14
If the keyring prompt annoys you, follow these steps.

Open Applications > Accessories > Passwords and Encryption Keys. Right click on the Passwords once inside the application, and select ‘Change Password’. Enter the old password and leave the new one blank. No more annoying prompt for passwords when connecting to wireless networks or using an email application.
