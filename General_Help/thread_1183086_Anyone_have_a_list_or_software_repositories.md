### Post by michy99 on 2009-06-09
Many developers maintain their own repositories, but I don't know of any central list. Basically if you wnat a certain package and it isn't available in the standard repositories, or you want a newer version than is available in the standard repositories, you use google to find a repository (if one exists) and add it to your sources.

No need to have a bunch of repositories you don't even use cluttering up your sources list.
