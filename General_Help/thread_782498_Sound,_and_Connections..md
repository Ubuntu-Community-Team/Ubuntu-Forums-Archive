### Post by Sparkalo on 2008-05-05
Just to clarify, when you say that the volume is turned up all the way, are you refering to your hardware or your software?  That is to say, did you adjust your volume by turning a knob on your speakers or did you click on the volume icon and then turn it up?

Oh wait a tic!  Xubuntu doesn't have a volume icon on the panel by default these days, does it?  If not, you can add one and then possibly turn the sound up.  Just right click anywhere on your top panel (the grey bar), hit add application (or something like that) and scroll down in the pop up window until you see volume (I think the items are arranged alphabetically, so it'll be down towards the bottom).  Then, a little speaker should appear on your panel.  Click the speaker to bring up your volume panel and see if you can't adjust it to get the sound working to your liking.
