### Post by w_barnes on 2011-02-11
Hi! Nautilus crashed and now when I try to open folders under places I get the message "Could not open location 'blah blah blah' - No application is registered as handling this file".

How do I fix?

---

### Post by DanneStrat on 2011-02-11
Do this:

Press alt+f2

and type "nautilus"in the box without the quotes.

Now right-click a folder of choice and select properties.

Under the tab "open with" set it to nautilus.

Try to open your folders now.
