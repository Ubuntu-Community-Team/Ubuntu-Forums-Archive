### Post by PeterJS on 2008-01-07
When the client is closed nothing is being shared. The only things that are shared are things that you have downloaded in the past and chosen to continue to upload, and the file that you are currently downloading.

The way bit torrent works is by trading partial files so if you have a section of the beginning of the image, and someone else has a section from the end your client would connect to theirs and send a section of the portion you have, and they would send you a section of the portion that they have.
