### Post by perspectoff on 2009-01-27
In Wubi, Ubuntu is run from within Windows. Your Ubuntu install is dependent on your Windows install. If you lose your windows OS, to malware or something like that, you also lose your Ubuntu install.

If you have Ubuntu on a second partition and it runs independently of Windows, then if you lose windows your have something to fall back upon.
