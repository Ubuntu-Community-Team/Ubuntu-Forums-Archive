### Post by NT4usB on 2007-10-30
You probably already found them, if not... bookmarks are in a hidden folder in your user folder:
./mozilla/firefox/profiles/xxxxx.default/
It's called bookmarks.html
copy the entire profile folder (randomchars.default) and you can drop the contents into the new profile folder and all your Firefox settings will be as they were.
