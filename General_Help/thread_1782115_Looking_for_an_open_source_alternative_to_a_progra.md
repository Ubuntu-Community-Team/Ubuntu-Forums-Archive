### Post by Jerry N on 2011-06-14
This looks like something that could easily be done with a spreadsheet such Excel, Gnumeric, Libreoffice or Openoffice.  However, to create the spreadsheet you will have to have a complete understanding of all the parameters associated with the frame shapes.  The creator of the program you reference has a very limited audience and is charging for the knowledge involved, not the time it took him/her to write the program.  

Several years ago I did something similar with a program to design closed and ported speaker cabinets starting with the published parameters of certain speakers.  Definitely not something I would try to sell to anyone though:p:p

Jerry

BTW, you will most likely spend a good bit more that $275 worth of your time researching the data and creating the spreadsheet.  A great educational experience, if you are looking for such experience.
