### Post by mcduck on 2006-12-10
Actually the place where toolbars are configured is in Gnome's menu, System/Preferences/Menus and Toolbars. Open that and set 'Toolbar button labels' to 'Icons only'.

This will also change toolbars in every other Gnome app..
