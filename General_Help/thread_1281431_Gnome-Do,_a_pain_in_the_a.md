### Post by falconindy on 2009-10-10
> **NoaHall said:**
> Why not just use alt + f2 to bring up the default runner.
Because alt-f2 doesn't provide a dock. Also with gnome-do, I can type 1 to 2 letters to access programs rather than 75% of the /usr/bin executable:
t - gnome-terminal
c - chromium
f - firefox
vi - virtualbox
v - vlc
s - songbird
g - geany

To me, it's a huge convenience, and makes people who enjoy keyboard shortcuts (like me) feel a lot more at home in a GUI.
