### Post by Lars Noodén on 2011-11-04
What version of Ubuntu are you running?  

If you are running 11.10 (Oneiric) and plain Ubuntu (not Xubuntu or Lubuntu), then you should have an icon over on the left called "Ubuntu Software Center".  It's one of the orange ones and the text should show when you hold the mouse over it.  Click on it once and it should come up.  In the search box, enter either 'bluefish' or 'kompozer'.  

Then when the package shows up in the search results, click on it, then find the 'install' button and click on that.
