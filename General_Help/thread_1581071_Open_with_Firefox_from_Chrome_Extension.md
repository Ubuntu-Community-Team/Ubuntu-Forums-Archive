I use both Chrome and Firefox... As a google apps user, I have Chrome persistent and open on one monitor for various apps Google related, and Firefox for regular browsing. This works well for me. There are times when I would like to click on a link received in Gmail via Chrome, but have it open in Firefox. I have yet to find an extension that does that for Chrome (although one exists that allows Firefox to open a link in Chrome, albeit for Windows only).

Is anyone aware of a way to selectively open links in Chrome with Firefox?

---

### Post by lovinglinux on 2010-09-24
To open links and pages with any browser from Firefox get [Open With]("https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/11097/"). Is compatible with all platforms.

To open a link from Chrome into Firefox, just drag it from Chrome to Firefox tab bar.
