### Post by daengbo on 2007-10-29
It sounds like you are talking about the notification area, not the taskbar, so I'll go with that.

The notification area doesn't actually show running programs. It only shows programs which want to notify you of something, like the update manager, Rhythmbox, and Pidgin. Is that the area that you mean? If so, it may have crashed or been unintentionally deleted.

Right click where you want the notification area to be. Choose "Add to Panel." Under the "Utilities" section is "Notification Area."

See if that solves your problem. If it doesn't, let us know.
