### Post by plucky on 2008-02-26
> Is there any way I can change the colour of the text to something else, perhaps white?

Open **Terminal** Navigate Edit > Profiles > Select default > Edit > Colours

Deselect "Use colours from system Themes"

Navigate to "Built in Themes box" and select " White on Black" or create your own custom scheme.

Good luck
