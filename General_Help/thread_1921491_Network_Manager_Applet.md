### Post by maverickaddicted on 2012-02-07
When you right click on the panel and select Add to Panel, there is an entry called as "Notification Area", you have to just add it to your panel to get it back. First check if it is already present in the panel or not, it is displayed as three vertical dots, if it is there right click on it and remove it. After that again add the Notification Panel and restart your machine.

If that doesn't work try this -

[http://www.ubuntugeek.com/possible-solutions-to-fix-the-missing-network-manager-icon-in-ubuntu-9-10.html](http://www.ubuntugeek.com/possible-solutions-to-fix-the-missing-network-manager-icon-in-ubuntu-9-10.html)
