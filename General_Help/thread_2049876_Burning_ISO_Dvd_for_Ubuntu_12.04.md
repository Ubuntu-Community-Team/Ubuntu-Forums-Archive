### Post by GreatDanton on 2012-08-29
If there is nothing wrong with your burner, program or dvd/cd then you most likely won't get any errors. It's recommended to burn cd's/dvd's at lower speed because sometimes you can produce a bad cd/dvd. Till this time I never had any problem with burning at maximum speed (the only problem was bad burning program) but if I were you I will check once again if you have lower speed option. I had once problem with bad quality of cd's but that's another story. After that problem I am usually making live USB, because they are working 100%, but this is up to you.

Hope this helps.
