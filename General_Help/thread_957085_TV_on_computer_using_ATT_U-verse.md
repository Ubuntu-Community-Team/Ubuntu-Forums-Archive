### Post by TransitMan on 2008-10-24
Even though AT&T U-Verse is all IP oriented, you still need a TV tuner card and a STB to watch TV.
The only other choice is to go to HULU.com or Veoh.com and watch shows from there.
There is also the rare occasion you might be able to watch from one of the 4 network (ABC, CBS, NBC, FOX) web-sites.
