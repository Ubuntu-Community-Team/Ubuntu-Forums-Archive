### Post by bingoUV on 2008-09-14
I am not sure, but does this help [https://answers.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/openoffice.org/+question/33973](https://answers.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/openoffice.org/+question/33973) ?

Alternatively, you could use google Indic transliterate service: [http://www.google.com/transliterate/indic](http://www.google.com/transliterate/indic)

Marathi is not available for this service, but Hindi may be close. Marathi specific characters cannot be typed with this. The advantage is that typing is extremely simple. You don't have to learn which key on an en_US keyboard means what.
