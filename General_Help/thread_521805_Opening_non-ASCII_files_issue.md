### Post by kidders on 2007-08-10
Hi there,

"Notepad" applications won't give you a very good look inside non-textual files, since they don't have any particularly useful way of representing most of the characters. In addition, trying to modify such a file with one would almost certainly break it. I would suggest a hex editor.
