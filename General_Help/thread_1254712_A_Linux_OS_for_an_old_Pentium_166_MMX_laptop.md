### Post by Finalfantasykid on 2009-08-31
I don't know of any Ubuntu based distros which suit those requirements.  The closest that I know of are the Netbook remix and Xubuntu, but those require over 300MB of ram.

However DSL(Damn Small Linux) and Puppy Linux would probably work just fine.  Puppy Linux says that 128MB ram is the recommended minimum, but you could probably get away with 98MB, as long as you have a decent sized swap file.
