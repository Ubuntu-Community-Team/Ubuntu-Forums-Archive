### Post by Nepharlinack on 2009-03-06
You do need to take into account that someone else is paying for the creation and shipping of the CDs you order. Large orders are unlikely to be processed.

### Post by beno1990 on 2009-03-06
Like I say, it's perfectly legal to make as many copies of Ubuntu as you like with the ISO file or another CD.

And as I said, they often refuse large ShipIt CD requests, but if you're in need of a large number of pre-made/official CDs for whatever reason (like you don't have access to a CD burner) you can still buy them at the Ubuntu shop. It's £17.02 for a pack of 20 (which is far better than 20 Windows licenses, I might add ;) ).
