### Post by matthorton2 on 2017-10-31
im no expert with half-life but i do know that audio buffer size plays a part in audio delay. see if you can find audio buffer size option. smaller=faster, larger =more delay. however if you make it too small you will get audio glitches and errors. nothing to worry about but it wont sound nice. if you get these you will need to make the buffer number larger. you will need to experiment with it because it's a trade-off.

...assuming its the buffer that is the problem and that there is an option for it!
