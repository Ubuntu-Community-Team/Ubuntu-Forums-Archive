### Post by drs305 on 2008-04-21
> **MosMusy said:**
> It used to work before, but now everytime it says "type password" I can type in anything!
 Could you confirm this? Normally the complaint is that you CAN'T type anything when asked to enter the sudo password. Since we can't know your level of experience, please remember that in terminal when asked for a password, when you type you will not see anything. There will be no stars, asterisks or any other symbol - but the password is being entered nevertheless. So when it asks for the password, type it and hit enter, even though you don't see anything happening.
