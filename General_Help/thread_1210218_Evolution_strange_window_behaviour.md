### Post by Maupertus on 2011-04-27
It's the switcher icons that take up to much space in the standard resolution of most netbooks. When you choose not to display them or set them to icons only, the jumping windows should stop.

### Post by Slopwith on 2011-06-15
Same problem on my EeePC with 10.10 full version. Just tried above and the jumping stops and I have full screen evolution! I thought at first is was a resolution thing. How strange. Yet there is still a taskbar at the bottom. 
Any way right click on the "Workspace Switcher"icons that allow the 4 desktops, Select "Unlock from Panel" and "Remove from Panel".
Evolution now works normally full screen without jumping up and down. 
To add again, right click on Taskbar near waste basket icon, click "Add to Panel", then scroll down to "&#7812;indow Switcher" and select "Add". Thank you to above. One step closer to ditching MS.:)
