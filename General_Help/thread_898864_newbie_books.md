### Post by k8bopibu on 2008-08-23
Depending on your expertise you may want to use different books, if you are a beginner "Ubuntu for Non-Geeks" is quite simple and gives some basic info, if you are looking for something more indepth I recommend any of the O'Reilly books. I've always been happy with them, including "Unix in a Nutshell" and many others that they have.
