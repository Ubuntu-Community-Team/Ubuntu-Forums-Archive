### Post by haqking on 2011-09-07
> **fuzzylogic25 said:**
> I see in system monitor there is STOP PROCESS, CONTINUE PROCESS, END PROCESS, KILL PROCESS.

What is the difference between them all (except CONTINUE)?

stop is like pause, then you can continue it.

End is the normal shut down gracefully if you like.

Kill sends the TERM signal (usally without switches) which is like pressing the power button and not shutting down
