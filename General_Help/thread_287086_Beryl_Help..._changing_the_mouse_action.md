### Post by DarkNemesis618 on 2006-10-28
Open up the Beryl Settings Manager
System > Preferences > Beryl Settings Manager

Choose the Scale Option in the side bar
Go to Screen Edges and Corners
Uncheck everything

Now choose the Show Desktop Option in the side bar
Go to Screen Edges and Corners
Uncheck everything.

That should do it.
