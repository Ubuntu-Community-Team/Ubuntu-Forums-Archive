### Post by mannheim on 2006-06-04
With gconf-editor, is the setting that you changed the one "apps/nautilus/desktop/volumes_visible" ? When you uncheck that option in gconf-editor, all the volume icons on the desktop **should** disappear (in real time, as it were). This is how it works on my system.
