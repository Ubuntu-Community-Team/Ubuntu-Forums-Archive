### Post by DarkN00b on 2006-12-10
Right-click the volume control icon and click Preferences. Make sure your sound device is selected in the drop down box and that Master is highlighted in the bottom part of the dialog. Click the Close button and you should be good to go.

### Post by Dave54 on 2006-12-10
Actually, "master" may not affect your sound at all. Double-click the icon and play around with all the controls to find which ones work. For me, "Master" and "PC Speaker" don't change the sound coming out of my speakers. Both "Headphone" and "PCM" do. After finding what works, go to preferences and make the main bar affect something that works.

Edit: PS. No, my speakers are not plugged into the headphone jack on my computer. I checked. ;)
