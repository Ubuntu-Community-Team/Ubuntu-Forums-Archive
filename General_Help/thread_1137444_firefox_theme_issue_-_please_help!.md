### Post by joeyknuccione on 2009-04-25
When you uninstall firefox, there's still some stuff mucking about. Uninstall, then do:

Places > Search for files

In that search for "firefox"

Delete what you can there and then reinstall.

Seems like you used to could just delete .firefox in your home folder, but a recent reinstall (for me) needed the steps above.
