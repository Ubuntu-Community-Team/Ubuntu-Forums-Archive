### Post by him610 on 2024-10-07
An online search brought up this...
```
To connect an Amazon Echo device to Wi-Fi, you can follow these steps: 
    Plug in your Echo device 
    Download the Amazon Alexa app on your phone or tablet 
    Open the Alexa app and sign in with your Amazon account 
    Select Devices 
    Select your Echo device 
    Select Device Settings 
    Select Change next to Wi-Fi Network 
    Select your Wi-Fi network and enter your password 
    Follow the remaining prompts to complete the setup process 
If you're having trouble connecting, you can try these steps:
    Check that you have the latest version of the Alexa app
    Restart your Alexa-enabled device
    Reset your Echo device
    Try setting up again using your phone as a Wi-Fi hotspot 
```
