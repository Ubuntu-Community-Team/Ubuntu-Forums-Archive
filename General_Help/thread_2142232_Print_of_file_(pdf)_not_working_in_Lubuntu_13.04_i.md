### Post by ask_ on 2013-05-06
I found the resolution to the issue. It happens that cups-pdf was not installed in the system. Installed it via Synaptic Package manager. Issue resolved. I was not aware of Cups-pdf earlier. When I installed Ubuntu 12.04 on a different system it was printing pdfs out of the box. Here things were different it seems. Cheers.
