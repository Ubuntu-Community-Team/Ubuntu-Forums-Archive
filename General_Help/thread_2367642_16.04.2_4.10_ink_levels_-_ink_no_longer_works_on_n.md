### Post by DuckHook on 2017-08-01
Is your printer supported? From:```
apt show ink
```
```
Printers of Canon, Epson, HP and Sony are supported. ***Complete list can be found at README.Debian of libinklevel5 package***
```
Try following advice of apt show and go through the README.Debian file. If your model printer is not there, then it may be too new/old/obscure for the app.

I don't use Canon printers so am of very limited help, but the app's SourceForge link follows: [http://ink.sourceforge.net/](http://ink.sourceforge.net/)
From that page, the following seems to be important:> You need read/write permission on the device the printer is connected to.
