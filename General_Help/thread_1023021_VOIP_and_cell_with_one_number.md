It was more the ideas presented on the page which should be useful rather than the particular company. So, you wanted a single number to be contactable on:

> 
I use a WiFi handset in the office and my Vodafone mobile phone when away. How will a Mobile Roamer number help me?

By subscribing to the basic Mobile Roamer package you can configure VoIPtalk to divert to your VoIP handset when in the office and to your Vodafone handset when you are away from the office. The cost benefit of free diverts means that you can advertise a single number, which can be divered to a number of destinations for absolutely free.

I'd be surprised if there were not Canada/USA VoIP concerns which offered the same service. Terminology? Cellular and mobile technologies are basically the same.
