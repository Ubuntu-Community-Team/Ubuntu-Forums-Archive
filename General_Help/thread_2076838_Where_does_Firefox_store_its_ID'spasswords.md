### Post by zombifier25 on 2012-10-27
Go to Edit > Preferences, choose the Security tab, and make sure "Remember passwords for sites" is checked. You can also view saved passwords there.

### Post by Abhinav Kumar on 2012-10-27
go to Edit->Preferences->Security Tab.
Assuming "Remember Passwords for sites" is checked
Click on saved passwords . see if the sitename and username for the site is listed there. 
if it is not listed, you may have to enter your username and password again.
Also check if you have not checked "use a master password" option under
 Edit->Preferences->Security Tab. If this option is checked, passwords and usernames will not be filled unless you enter your master password :)
