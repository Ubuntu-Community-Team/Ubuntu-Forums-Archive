### Post by David D. on 2013-11-08
KVM is Kernel-based Virtual Machine, and as the message states, this is disabled in your BIOS.  I get the same message.  It affects nothing unless you are using a virtual machine.  If you do not plan on using VM, then you can do what I do and ignore the message.  As you state, it only adds a few seconds to the boot.

If you really want to get rid of the message, you will have to go into the BIOS settings when you boot your computer and search for that setting.
