### Post by shirilover on 2006-10-15
bittorrent does not a download in a linear fashion, so, even if your are at 40% that does not mean that any files are actually complete.

### Post by Polygon on 2006-10-15
like he said, what bittorrent does is download "blocks" of the files, which is why it is most of the time faster because it can download different blocks of the files from different people and download a lot more at one time.

So you will most likely have to wait till the download is 100% done before you can watch the videos without errrors

and be sure to seed your files after you finish download for a while to keep it goin ^^
