### Post by chiliman on 2010-06-01
I had a similar issue when i first installed ubuntu.  i was useing the workspace switcher on one of my panels switching rapidly between the workspaces and that caused it to crash and gave me a message that work space switcher stopped working.  after a reboot i didnt see the workspace switcher on my panel.   i got it working agian by clicking the panel selecting add to panel and selecting workspace switcher and that solved my issue of not being able to switch workspaces.   

maybe you horizontal virtual size is not set right, i would reinstall compiz config settings manager, then run it and click on general options >> then click the "desktop size" tab >>> then see what "horizontal virtual size" is set too.  set it to the number of desktops you want to switch between.  i hope that might solve it. 

you could also look into if your graphic drivers are up to date.
