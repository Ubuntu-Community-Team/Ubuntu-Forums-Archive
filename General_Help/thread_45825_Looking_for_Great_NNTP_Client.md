### Post by claydoh on 2005-07-01
Pan is a great newsreader, and does multipart binaries. It is highly configurable and is somewhat similar to the Windows newsreader Agent.

Knode (a KDE app) and Thunderbird are ok as well, but do not handle binaries.

All of them handle high post counts well for me, so other than binary attachment issues, it would be more of a personal preference thing.
