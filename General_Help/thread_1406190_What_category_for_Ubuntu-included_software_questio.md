### Post by lovinglinux on 2010-02-13
Yes, "General Help" is the place to post all sorts of subjects not covered by other forums, but since you are new to Ubuntu, I would recommend the [Absolute Beginner Talk](http://ubuntuforums.org/forumdisplay.php?f=326). Is perfectly OK to ask questions there that are related to software included with Ubuntu.

Additionally, since F-Spot is an image editor, you could also post in the [Multimedia & Video](http://ubuntuforums.org/forumdisplay.php?f=334) or [Art & Design](http://ubuntuforums.org/forumdisplay.php?f=16) forums.

BTW, questions regarding how the forum works, how to post and where to post would be better p&#314;aced in the [Forum Feedback & Help](http://ubuntuforums.org/forumdisplay.php?f=48).
