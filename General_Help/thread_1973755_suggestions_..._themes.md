It is, I believe, a hardcoded part of Ubuntu's file manager, not part of the theming team and not specific to Ubuntu.

You may be able to submit your feature request to the Gnome Bugzilla, under the "Nautilus" package. Word your request in such a way that it doesn't reference one particular distribution ( for example, instead of saying "light orange", say something like "a very light shade of the theme's selection colour")
