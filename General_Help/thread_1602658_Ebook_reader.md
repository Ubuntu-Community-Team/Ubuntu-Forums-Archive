### Post by ragtag on 2010-10-21
You should check out Calibre. It's in the repository, though I would recommend you go and get it from [http://calibre-ebook.com/](http://calibre-ebook.com/) as it's updated quite frequently. Calibre is a software for managing e-books, and includes a nice software reader. It can also be used to convert e-books between different formats, and upload them to a number of different e-book readers. It supports most e-book formats, but it can't open DRM infested books.

I don't know about a good book on bash scripting, but [http://oreilly.com/](http://oreilly.com/) might have some, and as far as I know their books are DRM free, and come in several different formats. :)
