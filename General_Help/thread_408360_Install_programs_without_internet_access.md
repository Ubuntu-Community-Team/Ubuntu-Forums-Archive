### Post by iXneonXi on 2007-04-13
Since you don't have access to the internet, you're probably going to have to resolve all the dependencies. You might not have to install from source, however.

If you get all the files together (*.debs if you want) and put them on a CD, you may be able to develop your own custom repository.

You could also customize a Ubuntu CD and use that as your source for software.  May even be able to download large application CDs.

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Repositories/Ubuntu](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Repositories/Ubuntu)
