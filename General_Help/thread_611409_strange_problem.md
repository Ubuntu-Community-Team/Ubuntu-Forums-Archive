but i'll explain it in case you (apparently) don't know what it is.

*ubuntu "identifies" partitions/hdds by UUID now and has for a while.  but its not always perfect apparently.

but unfortunately, i don't know how to fix it.... ;(
i guess you'd have to figure out what the real/correct UUID is for the partition/hdd you're trying to boot and compare it with the UUID listed in fstab to check that it is correct.  (but, it looks to me like it most likely is not correct, hence it not being able to boot),
