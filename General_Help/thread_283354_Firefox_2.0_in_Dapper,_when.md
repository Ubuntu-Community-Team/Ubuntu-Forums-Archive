### Post by cmost on 2006-10-24
It seems to me that some people don't understand the concept of Ubuntu Dapper LTS.  The LTS = Long Term Support.  Dapper is the first version of Ubuntu targeted to corporations, small businesses and people who value stability over cutting edge.  Perhaps "Edgy" is aptly named as it is only supported for 18 months and contains the most cutting edge features.  You cannot have your cake and eat it too.  If you want stability and long-term support, then stick with Dapper.  If you want the latest versions of packages as they're released, and you're willing to put up with a few bugs here and there then upgrade to Edgy Eft on Thursday.  Alternately, there's nothing stopping anyone from compiling the latest version of any package yourself.
