### Post by mkvnmtr on 2010-01-25
I do not use wireless but I found that my router must be getting old and could not handle many connections. I think the default for Deluge was 200. I cut mine down below 100 and it gave better browser speeds. As I recall I also changed the number of what I think was connections on hold. I forget exactly what they are called but you will see them in the settings. Play with the settings until you find what works for you. Maybe when you are not using  the computer unplug the router at least for 10 minutes or so. I found that unpluging mine when I turn of the computer helped,
