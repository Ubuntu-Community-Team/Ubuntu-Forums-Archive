### Post by kostkon on 2009-07-11
You need to install the *PulseAudio Device Chooser* utility. More info [here]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1130384").

Also, it would be better to put the sound playbacks in your sound preferences back to *Autodetect*.
