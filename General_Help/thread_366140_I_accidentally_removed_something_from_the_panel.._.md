### Post by shironeko on 2007-02-20
I think I know what's your problem!

Right click on the toolbar (on a blank space) and select "add to panel" (first option)

Here you can select applets and launchers to personalize your toolbar.

You did accidentaly delete the "notification area" applet. Just drag and drop wherever you want to have it again.

---

### Post by strabes on 2007-02-20
Right click on a blank area of your panel and click "Add to Panel..." The item you're looking for is the Notification area. You can drag it from the Add to Panel window to your panel.
