### Post by apollothethird on 2011-08-03
> **b1lancer said:**
> Thanks for the suggestion. I was able to download the file onto a computer with MS Word, and I did what you suggested. Copy and paste worked fine. My questions are: Should I continue using OpenOffice? What caused the problem? What about the other PDF file (not the one I created) that had half of it gone?

You were most likely experiencing a glitch where an unseen formatting character was causing the missing portion of your document to not be visible.  This has never happened to me with OpenOffice but has happened many times with Microsoft Word.  The text editor and the email didn't retain the invisible formatting character.  your PDF attempt brought over the same type of view that you were seeing from OpenOffice.  An attempt to print it would have most likely done the same (printed only what OpenOffice was displaying).

Microsoft Office has a better Grammar checker than OpenOffice, but that's about the only advantage it has over OpenOffice.  If you can do without the advance grammar checking facility of MSWord, you'd probably do better to use OpenOffice.  OpenOffice's spell checker is just as good as MSWord's spell checker.
