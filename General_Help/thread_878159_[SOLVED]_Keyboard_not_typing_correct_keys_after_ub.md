### Post by geirha on 2008-08-02
I'm guessing you have a laptop, and that your laptop has a numpad around those keys, and you hold down the fn button to get the numbers instead of the letters. There's usually a key combination to toggle locking this into place so you don't need to hold down the fn key. On my laptop, the combination is fn+numlock. Perhaps it is similar at yours?
