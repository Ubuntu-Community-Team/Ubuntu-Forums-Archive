### Post by BenAshton24 on 2008-11-07
Lots of windows games do work on wine however most of them have they're flaws such as, you cannot play network games and stuff like that. if you want to know what games work and what ones don't, visit [http://appdb.winehq.org](http://appdb.winehq.org) and search the name of the game. there are tons of reviews and even tips on how to get the games working. even if a game doesn't work, you can always have xp on virtual box and run them on that :D

Hope this helps,
