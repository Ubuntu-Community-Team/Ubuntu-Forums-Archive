### Post by Exsecrabilus on 2008-06-19
Go *System -> Preferences -> Advanced Desktop Effects Settings*.
Under section *General*, click on *General Options*.
Click on the **Opacity Settings** tab.
Under *Window opacities*, click **New**.
Type in _dock_ for **Opacity windows**, and for **Opacity window values**, choose what you want. (_100_ is solid background, _0_ is complete transparency, etc.)
