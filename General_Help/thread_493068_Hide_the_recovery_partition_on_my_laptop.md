### Post by orb9220 on 2007-07-05
> But how do I rename sda2 to Vista?

Create a folder in /media called Vista then

Change
UUID=B65A0A4E5A0A0BB5 /media/**sda2** ntfs-3g defaults,locale=en_US.UTF-8 0 1
to
UUID=B65A0A4E5A0A0BB5 /media/**Vista** ntfs-3g defaults,locale=en_US.UTF-8 0 1

Hope that helps

Be sure if you have capital V in vista then also have it in fstab.
And backup your fstab so you have a backup.
