### Post by WorMzy on 2011-02-25
Forcing your monitor to display at resolutions above it's native resolution may cause it to cease functioning correctly (sometime permanently), and *will* result in inferior image quality.

If you want a higher resolution image, I suggest you invest in a monitor which can display it.
