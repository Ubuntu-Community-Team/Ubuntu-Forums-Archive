### Post by fooman on 2010-06-20
right-click in the panel and choose "add to panel"

in the menu that pops up....scroll down to clock,  click on it once and then click the "add" button (or just double-click it)

if it does not show the weather....right click on the clock in the panel: choose preferences,  put a check next to "show weather",  then click the locations tab > add

and find the location closest to you.
