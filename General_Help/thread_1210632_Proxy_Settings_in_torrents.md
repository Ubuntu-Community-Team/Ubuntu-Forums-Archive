### Post by Alias1407 on 2009-07-11
Just use Transmission, the standard torrent client in Ubuntu. To set the proxy: Open Transmission, should be under the "Internet" tab in Applications.

Then go "Edit --> Preferences --> Trackers" then fill in your proxy information.
