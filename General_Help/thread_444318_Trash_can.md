### Post by dpar on 2007-05-15
If it's on the panel, but in the wrong place, right click it then uncheck lock to panel. Then click move. That will turn your cursor into a little hand that will drag it where you want:) .


Edit: I just noticed........you may have to move your workplace icon to make room for it too.
