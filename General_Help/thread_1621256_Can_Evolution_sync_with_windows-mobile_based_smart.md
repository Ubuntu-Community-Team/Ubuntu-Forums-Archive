### Post by Wtower on 2010-11-14
I understand your concern as the company for which I work use windows smartphones a lot and I prefer Ubuntu for my desktop environment on my pcs already. To be honest with you, I have not found a truly reliable direct method to sunchronize data with evo. My search though lead me to [Funambol]("http://www.funambol.org"), which we have already installed and use heavily in the business Ubuntu server. 

With Funambol you can reliably synchronize among msoutlook, evo, smartphones etc. The only drawback is that you obviously cannot have it installed on a dual boot pc and have outlook sync data with it. Also mind that it is a relatively memory demanding app, and that the installation with mysql server, although easy, requires some reading first.

Other that, I seriously consider my next smartphone to be android. Any other ideas would be welcome.

Regards.
