### Post by wojox on 2010-03-10
Press Alt+F2

Type: gconf-editor

Go to apps->nautilus->desktop

Tick the box beside whatever you want visible computer_icon_visible, home_icon_visible, and trash_icon_visible. The changes take effect immediately.
