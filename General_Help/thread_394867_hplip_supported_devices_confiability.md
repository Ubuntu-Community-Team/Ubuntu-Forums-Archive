### Post by 11hjpphty76lkjj on 2007-03-29
The statement means that although we do the best we can for making sure that the printers listed are supported we are not reponsible if something should not work. Of course our goal is to support as many printers as possible and if there would happen to be a discrepency then we could re-test the problem printer and update the supported document list as needed and/or work to update HPLIP so that the printer would be supported.

Although you can feel fairly secure that any printers listed on the supported page are supported.
