### Post by linuxinstalledfromhdd on 2011-06-09
Don't use WEP. Use WPA.  WEP is obsolete and can be cracked in about 5 minutes.  WEP is all around not the way to go.

Why is WEP bad to use:
[http://www.computerworld.com/s/article/9015559/Don_t_use_WEP_for_Wi_Fi_security_researchers_say](http://www.computerworld.com/s/article/9015559/Don_t_use_WEP_for_Wi_Fi_security_researchers_say)

If you want to change it, but you are not sure how, start a trouble ticket with the tech support help line of the company that made your wifi router. 
Most routers have the ability to switch to WPA, otherwise you are going to have to upgrade your router.
