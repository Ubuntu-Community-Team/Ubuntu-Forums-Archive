### Post by s.fox on 2009-12-22
Hello

1) Navigate here on the top bar:

**System** > **Administration** > **Synaptic Package Manager**

2) Then do a search for drscheme.

3) Check the box next to it when it is show.

4) Click Mark for installation

5) Click Apply

Hope this helps.
