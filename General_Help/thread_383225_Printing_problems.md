### Post by Big_Croc7 on 2007-03-14
Firstly, is the job being sent to the printer? If you open the jobs list (system, administration, printing, then right-click on the printer and choose jobs) you can see the status of the jobs; if the file appears there, it might be just taking a very long time to send to the printer (which is more likely with a large file size).  What does the job status say?
