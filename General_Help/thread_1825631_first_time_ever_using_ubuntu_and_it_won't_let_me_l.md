### Post by YesWeCan on 2011-08-15
The Ubuntu live CD doesn't normally show a login screen or ask for a password. It just boots straight in.
The first Pentium III was introduced 12 years ago. But it's probably more your graphics which may not be up to the relentless demands of 11.04.
It may boot more reliably if you select "nomodeset" (press space when you see the pink screen with bizarre icons at the bottom, then F6 key at the CD start menu).
