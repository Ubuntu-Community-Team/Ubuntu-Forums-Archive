### Post by arcturusarc1 on 2013-08-08
Hi I am new to linux but I had the same problem with you, at gnome desktop.
I have also nVidia graphics card and there was a problem with my drivers.
Try to change your drivers from **software & updates > Additional Drivers**  and instead of **X. Org X server** blablabla....  drivers, select **NVIDIA &#935;org**.... drivers and at the end of the line says (**proprietary, tested**).

P.S.: I'm using **304 "Driver's Version"** (an older one) because I've tested in DOTA and the framerate is better. Also I feel my PC working better with that version.

Good luck, and let me know if I helped.
