### Post by AirRaven on 2006-05-26
I seriously reccomend you stay away from aMSN if you want a messenger that feels "good". 

I find it slows down your computer whenever you try and use something like Webcam. Mercury Messenger'd be closer to what you're looking for **IF** you don't mind having to use something made in Java. It supports MSN games- which is somehing you're looking for, apparently.

I heartily reccomend Kopete if you want a **good** messenger, though. It supports Nudges and Webcam, if you must have those. Games aren't supported, which is unsurprising, since they're not exactly the most looked-for feature.

(On a side note, Gaim *does* support display pictures. If you go into your account options, you can give yourself a "buddy icon"- that's what Gaim calls display pictures. It's an AIMism which I don't really agree with, but it's there.)
