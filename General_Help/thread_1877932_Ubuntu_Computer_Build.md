The ATX specification is standard across multiple manufacturers, so a full-size ATX case should be able to successfully mount ATX, Mini-ATX, and Micro-ATX mobo's; a Micro-ATX case can only take Micro-ATX mobo's due to size issues.  An upgrade power supply may be a worthwhile investment, as it will provide sufficient power stability for all components; many stock PSU's are sized for the stock hardware and a minimum of upgrade parts.
