### Post by mcduck on 2010-09-06
> **borth92 said:**
> Looking for a good program that lets you make your own beats and music. Please give me something...

There are hundreds of such programs and related tools.

The question really is what kind of program(s) you exactly want.

Do you want something suitable for a beginner or something that a professional sound engineer would use? Integrated studio app or separate apps for each specific tasks, working together through audio/midi routing between apps? Synths, samples or recorded tracks? Audio or MIDI or both?

Installing the ubuntu-studio packages will of course bring you a wide selection of different apps and tools, but if you are looking for a simple integrated software studio suitable for a beginner, for example, that would be serious overkill. :D
