### Post by ajgreeny on 2010-11-16
Have a look in System ->Preferences ->Assistive Technologies and the keyboard section has an **Ignore fast duplicate keypresses** which may be selected.  If so deselect it.  All such keyboard preferences are probably in that dialog, so search and play.
