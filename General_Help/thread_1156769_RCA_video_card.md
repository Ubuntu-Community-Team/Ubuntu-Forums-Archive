Also quite a lot of cards do component out also to allow some HDTV support or the most modern ones have HDMI for full HD.

### Post by danwood76 on 2009-05-12
The normally provide and RCA off a cable.
No cards these days will provide an RCA out as its old analogue technology with low resolutions.

But if you plan on making something to sell maybe you should look at the current market.
Chipsets like the Realtek RTD1261 give you component/composite/HDMi output with an IDE/SATA/Ethernet/Composite input. Many boxes are built around these that do everything a HTPC would do and have their own flavour of linux installed.
