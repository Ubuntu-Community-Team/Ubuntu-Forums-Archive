### Post by cilynx on 2006-04-22
You've fallen prey to an "intelligent" download manager.  I'm assuming that you had to restart / resume the download twice.  I've seen this on my system, but I don't remember what I was using to download at the time.  When it went to download a file that was already on the system, it incrimented the number at the end.  My best advice is to blow away the iso's that you've got as they're all partial and do the download all at once.
