### Post by sgosnell on 2009-12-02
Either HP or Brother should work fine for you.  Pretty much any model should do, it mostly depends on your budget and preferences.  I like the all-in-one models, but if you only want black, you might look at laserjet models, because the replacement ink for inkjets is rather expensive.  You'll need to compare the initial cost of the printer, the amount of printing you expect to do, and the price of the toner/ink, to find the best buy for you.
