### Post by PmDematagoda on 2007-10-05
If the torrent you are trying to download has only a very little amount of seeders then the slow download speed must be expected, try a torrent with a higher number of seeders and a lesser number of leechers. The more seeders you have over the number of leechers in a torrent, the faster your download speed for that torrent will be.
