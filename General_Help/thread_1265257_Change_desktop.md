### Post by shadowhawk2008 on 2009-09-13
To install KDE follow these steps:

Go to system > administration > synaptic package manager type in your password and then do a search for kubuntu. Chose the the package kubuntu-desktop and mark it for installation. Then apply it and let it install. You'll be promted to chose gdm or kdm from a drop down menu. This is basically chosing a kde login window or a gnome login window. Chose according to your preferance, it can be changed later. In my case I'm going to chose the Gnome login window (gdm). When its finished quit synpatic and log out. At the login screen click on options and then select session. Chose KDE and then click change sessions. You will then be asked if you want it to be default, your choice, just pick default or just this session. Then you'll be logged in as KDE. Enjoy!
