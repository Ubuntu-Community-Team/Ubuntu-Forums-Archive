### Post by black veils on 2012-06-12
playlist by nature wont work if transferred from one system to another, because the playlist only points to music files in certain directories, those music files and directories will not be on the other system. so even if you got the playlist file, you wont be able to get it working, unless *maybe  *you have the same directories on the phone as your ubuntu, and have all the music files there.

i know playlist files can be exported with the simple music app **Audacious**.

**Playlist  **>>**  Export**  >>  choose a location to save, name your file, add the extension** .m3u**
