### Post by bookie2 on 2015-06-12

Found the solution ...

 						Solution: Drop out of XFCE with Ctrl + Alt + F1, navigated to  ~/.cache/sessions/ and delete the contents... 

    

Restart, log in to XFCE, set up panels as required and save the session on exit to see your panels appear at next log on...

### Post by ajgreeny on 2015-06-12
Once you have the panel(s) set up as you want you can use the Lock tick-box in Panel-Preferences dlalogue to make sure they stay where you put them.  If you don't it is easy to inadvertently grab the handle at the end of the panel and drag it away from where you want.
