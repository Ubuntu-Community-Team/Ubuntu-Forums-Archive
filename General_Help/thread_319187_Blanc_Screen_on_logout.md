### Post by rlx01 on 2006-12-18
I've had that happen occasionally with some kernels.  I change my kernel levels a lot though and I normally wait for a cycle or two and update the kernel again and it gets fixed.  It might be worth updating you kernel, or going back a level to one you know worked.  But only downgrade if you know you don't need any of the later functionality.
