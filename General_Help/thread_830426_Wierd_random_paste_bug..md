### Post by nirvino on 2009-02-10
yeah, it's not a bug. it's just a touchpad issue. here's what i did to fix:

1. Go to Main Menu -> System -> Preferences -> Mouse
2. Click the Touchpad tab
3. Uncheck "Enable Mouse Clicks with Touchpad"
4. Click on Close
