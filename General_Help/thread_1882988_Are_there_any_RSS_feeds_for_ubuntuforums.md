### Post by stinkeye on 2011-11-18
In **User CP > Edit Options  > Default Thread Subscription Mode**
set to instant email notification.
Whenever you post in a thread you will be subscribed and notified of new posts.
**Quick Links > Subscribed Threads** to view.
