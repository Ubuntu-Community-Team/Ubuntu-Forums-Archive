### Post by jasonmchristos on 2010-02-25
heres what i did for my scansnap s1500
Click_menu: File>Scan

standard_tab > scan source_dropdown= ADF- Duplex

 
page options_tab > #pages_radiobutton=All > Source_radiobutton=double sided > side to scan_dropdown= facing > Click scan
