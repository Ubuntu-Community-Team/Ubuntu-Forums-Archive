One large partition?  Several smaller ones?  Drive/Partition(s) format?  If those aspects have not been decided or you are waiting to decide on an encryption method, you can take a look at [[COLOR="Blue"]truecrypt[/COLOR]]("http://truecrypt.org").  It can handle any data encryption you will need.  The latest versions have a gui, if that's important to you.  It's very easy to install and setup and just as easy to use.
