### Post by prshah on 2012-01-23
> **mazi1313 said:**
>  going to boot(bios) than my pc doesn't see this usb. 

Sorry, your BIOS has nothing to do with Ubuntu. Some BIOSes have an option on how to treat USB Mass Storage devices. It may help to change this option to treat USB pen drives as "Fixed Hard Drives" (or words to that effect; exact wording varies fom BIOS to BIOS).

It may help to post a screenshot of the relevant BIOS page; more exact information can be given.
