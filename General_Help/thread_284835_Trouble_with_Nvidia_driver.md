### Post by InternetNightmare on 2006-10-26
I think X.org 7.1 doesn't support nvidia-legacy drivers and normal drivers won't work I think.

Also NVIDIA driver is "nvidia" not "nv"

Some tips how to get legacy drivers working: [http://gentoo-wiki.com/TIP_XOrg_7.1_With_Older_NVidia_Drivers](http://gentoo-wiki.com/TIP_XOrg_7.1_With_Older_NVidia_Drivers)

You would need to get old driver installer from nvidia.com

### Post by shane634 on 2006-10-26
Open up xorg.conf and look for this section:

  Section "Device"
    Identifier    "NVIDIA Corporation NV17 [GeForce4 MX 440]"
    Driver        "nv"
    BusID        "PCI:1:0:0"
EndSection 

  Change that "nv" to "nvidia" and save the file. 

  Now restart X or reboot. Should work just fine after that. 

  I have a TNT2 riva card and this is working fine for me in Edgy.
