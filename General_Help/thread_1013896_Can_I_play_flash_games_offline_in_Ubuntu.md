### Post by DJ_Peng on 2008-12-17
You should be able to drop a Flash game into Firefox (or whatever browser you use) and play it with no problem, as long as it doesn't want to "phone home". There is an app called [Swfdec]("http://swfdec.freedesktop.org/wiki/") that may be able to handle the saved flash files as well. [Stéphane Marguet]("https://launchpad.net/%7Estemp/+archive") has it in her PPA for easy installation.
