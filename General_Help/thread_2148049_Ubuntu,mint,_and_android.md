### Post by 2F4U on 2013-05-24
Different Linux distributions have different target users. Ubuntu, at the moment, places an emphasize on mobile devices, which may be the reason why your experience with Android is better than with Mint. Anyways, Mint is based off Ubuntu, even using the same software, but with a different desktop environment. Therefore, I am sure you could get Mint to play as nice with Android as Ubuntu, but it may require more work. My advice is to choose the OS that suits your needs best.
