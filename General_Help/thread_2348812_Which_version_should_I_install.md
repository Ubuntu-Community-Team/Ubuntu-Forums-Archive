### Post by QIII on 2017-01-07
The kernel/HWE combinations of 14.04.2 - .4 are out of support and may simply not work.  15.04 is entirely out of support.

That leaves 14.04, .1 and .5.  14.04.5 has many of the kernel/graphics stack components that make it incompatible with some AMD graphics adapters.

What model is your graphics adapter?

### Post by ayup on 2017-01-07
Hi, 
since 14.04.5 is the only version compatible with your driver that is still getting updates until April 2019, I would strongly recommend installing that one because of security reasons. Without a supported version of Ubuntu, you won't get security updates and your system will be more vulnerable. Security would definitely be my priority.
Hope that helps!
