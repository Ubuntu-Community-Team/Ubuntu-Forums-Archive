### Post by FLeiXiuS on 2004-10-28

Section "ServerLayout"
Identifier "Default Layout"
Screen "screen1"
Screen "screen0" RightOf"screen1"
Option "Xinerama" "on"
InputDevice "Generic Keyboard"
InputDevice "Configured Mouse"
EndSection

### Post by Heavy-G on 2004-10-31
(we gaan maar in het Engels voortpraten, zo kan de rest ook volgen ;))

GDM is the Gnome Display Manager. It is the program that takes care of your login screen and it handles your X session. I had a problem with it, it kept appearing on my second monitor instead of my primary, so I changed the Xinerama option in /etc/X11/gdm/gdm.conf so it would appear on the correct screen (look at the file, you'll figure it out :))
