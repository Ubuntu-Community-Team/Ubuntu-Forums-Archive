### Post by Frogs Hair on 2012-01-02
Hello and Welcome 

If you are interested is a particular project such as an application or operating system , start with the project's home page . Gnome , Mozilla , and others usually have a get involved section on the home page .

What and how much you can do is dependent on your skills and what the project needs done .

[http://www.ubuntu.com/community/get-involved](http://www.ubuntu.com/community/get-involved)

[http://www.mozilla.org/contribute/](http://www.mozilla.org/contribute/)
