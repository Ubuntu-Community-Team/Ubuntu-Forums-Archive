### Post by Kirboosy on 2013-10-03
Welcome to the forums! :D

Tools-->Options-->Libre Impress-->General-->'Enable Presentor Console' (Make sure to uncheck that box)

That should get you what you want. Just remember if you are using other windows the presentation will lose focus. You will have to click back inside it to procede further in the presentation 

Hope that helps!
