### Post by hawthornso23 on 2010-12-14
The teensy tinyness of window borders is even a launchpad bug. But from memory I think its status is `wontfix' ... or 'invalid' ... or one of the other "nothing is going to happen any time soon about this bug" type classifications - so I wouldn't hold your breath about it getting fixed. 

Depending on your theme the corners of each window should have a grab area (of greater than 1 pixel size) which allows resizing. Suggest you get used to resizing windows by grabbing them by the corners rather than by their edges
