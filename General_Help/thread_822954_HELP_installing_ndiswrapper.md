### Post by Frogster on 2008-06-08
Go to System, Administration, Synaptic Package Manager. 

Instal ndiswrapper-common and ndiswrapper-utils-1.9

Download ndisgtk via a google search and instal.

You will need two files from your windows driver disk (go for the XP versions) and place these in a folder on your desktop.
