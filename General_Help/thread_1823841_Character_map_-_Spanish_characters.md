### Post by rewyllys on 2011-08-12
If you have selected a "Compose" key for your keyboard (see the thread

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1416999&highlight=compose](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1416999&highlight=compose)

for details on using a "Compose" key), then the sequence Compose ?? will produce ¿ and the sequence Compose !! will produce ¡ .
