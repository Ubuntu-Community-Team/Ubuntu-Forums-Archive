### Post by ajgreeny on 2015-03-09
You may need to look either in **power-management** or **screensaver** configuration (even if you don't use a screensaver) to make sure neither of those are set to lock screen when coming out of suspend.

---

### Post by deadflowr on 2015-03-09
You should turn off the lock, too.
The lock setting is different from the suspend setting so whether you uncheck the password setting, which is for suspend, it doesn't affect the lock setting.
Lock setting will always need your password/login again when set.
