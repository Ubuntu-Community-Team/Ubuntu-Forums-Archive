### Post by Haakon_KL on 2009-09-21
Since Comix has already been established as a good comic book reader, let's move on to your audio player.

First of all, go to the install/remove button on your menu, and choose "All applications" as well as "All 3rd party and all that jazz" options from the menu.

Now search for "gstreamer" these are the codecs you want.

I don't know if you googled it first or not, but there may be more people who find this through searching, so... (That's how I found out everything when I was a newbie :))

Then, just try some of them out.
Amarok is really nice, I suggest you try that.
Other than that, there's minirok, audacious, vlc, and rythmbox to test out.

I hope you find something you like.
