### Post by grahammechanical on 2013-04-20
Are these Japanese and Korean languages used as system languages? Or are you seeing this effect in applications such as a web browser or word processor? So, for a web brower we may look at the Encoding and for Libreoffice we look at Tools>Options>Language Settings>Languages. You may need to investigate CTL (Complex Text Layout). Another are to investigate is the Input Method. You may need to activate an certain input method for those languages. We do that under Language Support>Keyboard Input method system. You may find that you get options for those languages that are not there for English.
 
This is the best I can do. Regards.
