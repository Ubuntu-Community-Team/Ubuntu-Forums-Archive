### Post by kansasnoob on 2011-01-29
This is mostly a rant, but also a warning, and a reminder to backup anything you'd rather not lose :P

A few months ago when Xmarks announced they might be going away I began to look for alternatives and settled on Firefox Sync. Until the last few days that had seemed like a great decision, but a few days ago I began to notice many, many duplications of many items in my bookmarks. Quite often 4 or 5 of everything I'd recently bookmarked or edited the title of, even multiple folders and such.

I should mention that I multi-boot a lot testing various distros, mostly different versions and variants of Ubuntu but, long story made short, my bookmarks were a total mess so I created a backup and began to tackle the problem. Two days later I'm about halfway there :(

It seemed that no matter what I did, as long as Firefox Sync was installed, I'd get things straightened out in my stable Maverick, then boot into my stable Lucid and, after Firefox Sync did it's thing, I'd end up back at square one.

So now I'm back on Xmarks, thanks to LastPass, and gradually getting things sorted out, and I'll start backing up my .mozilla more frequently :lolflag:
