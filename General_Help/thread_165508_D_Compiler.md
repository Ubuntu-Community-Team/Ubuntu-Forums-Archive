### Post by DJ_Max on 2006-04-24
Currently there are only two D compilers I know of. DMD and GDC. The orginal DMD compiler is closed source, and the open source GDC. Neither one of these are mature, or well known.

But to answer your question, no, there are no D compilers in the repository, not even in Dapper. You may want to make a request for it in Dapper+1(Edgy, I think).
