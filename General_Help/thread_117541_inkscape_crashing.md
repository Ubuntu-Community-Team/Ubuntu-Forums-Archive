### Post by wanger123 on 2006-01-15
Hi there, had same prob and solved by going to 
system settings-->appearance-->colors-->uncheck apply colors to non-kde applications then go to gtk styles and fonts and choose "use another style" -->raleigh

that should do it. Unfortunately this affects the look of some other progs like synaptic but at least inkscape is rock-solid

ps I dont think the first one (the apply colors to non-kde.......) is really necessary but you can play with that yourself
