### Post by Bucky Ball on 2016-12-08
There's a couple of add-ons you might find helpful. Tab groups and Tab Mix plus. You can add them to Firefox and get a better look at your tabs. Firefox used to give the option of seeing all open tabs at once with its own tab grouping but that has been dropped (unfortunately). 

There is an add-on that does exactly the same thing and that would be exactly what you'd be looking for. Easy to see what you have open and delete duplicates, but I can't remember the name of it and can't find for the moment.

Hope that's of some help.

(PS: Additionally, in the upper right hand corner directly down from the 'x' that closes the FF window you'll find a small downward arrow. Click that and it will show you all the tabs you have open.)
