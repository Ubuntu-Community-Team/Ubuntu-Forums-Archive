### Post by mb_webguy on 2009-03-10
Transmission is the default torrent client in Ubuntu.  You don't have to install it.

My personal favorite, though is [Deluge]("http://dev.deluge-torrent.org/wiki/Download").  It has an interface and features similar to uTorrent, but it's native to Linux.  You could also use uTorrent under [Wine]("http://www.winehq.org/download/deb").

My favorite torrent search sites are IsoHunt and The Pirate Bay.  Demonoid is another good site, and has high-quality torrents and some rare torrents that are hard to find elsewhere, but requires membership (which is free but only itermittently granted without a referral) and doesn't have the volume of torrents of other sites.
