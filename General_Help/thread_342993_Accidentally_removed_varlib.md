### Post by peterLinux on 2007-01-20
Restore from your last full backup :D 

No backup?
Another suggestion is to start the system with Knoppix and backup your files
Re-install and restore your backup.

If you have a similar system you can try copying from there but absolute no warranty!

If you re-install you can go into 'expert' mode to partition your disk and skip
the formatting of /home.

Good luck, next time double check your typing before you hit enter after
rm -rf *
