### Post by techstop on 2009-02-07
The launcher is not compatible with compiz. You can use either the compiz effects, or the launcher, but not both.

---

### Post by NinjaBunny on 2009-02-07
Thank you for the response.  Where do I turn off compiz?  I didn't know I had it on.

---

### Post by techstop on 2009-02-07
System>Preferences>Appearance>Visual Effects

If it is "None", then compiz is turned off. You probably have it set to "Normal" or "Extra" with the symptoms you have though...
