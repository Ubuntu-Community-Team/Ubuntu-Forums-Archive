### Post by Shampyon on 2009-10-18
Just a quick update for those few who may be interested:

I recently purchased a [[COLOR="Blue"]_USB micro bluetooth adapter_[/COLOR]]("http://us.kensington.com/html/14409.html"). I don't have the full functionality I would have under Windows (backing up phone-book data, etc) but I can easily transfer files to and from my handset, which was impossible with the phone's USB cable.

If any of you have a mobile (aka cell) phone that doesn't support Linux and your main concern is transferring images, audio and video to and from the device, this should be an easy and affordable fix.
