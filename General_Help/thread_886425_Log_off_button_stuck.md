### Post by celtic426 on 2008-08-25
Make sure the Power Manager service is running and auto started.  Check this in System-Preferences-Sessions.  Scroll down and make sure Power Manager is selected.

---

### Post by Aanidaani on 2008-08-25
That worked great. I turned the service on, then logged in and out, and now everything works just fine. Thanks for the tip!
