### Post by vcvmuv on 2012-07-10
my 12.04 computer started failing to shutdown or suspend yesterday, I read somewhere that removing "splash" from grub commandline fixes this. It worked for me
If it fixes your problem, to make it permanent

remove "splash from GRUB_CMDLINE_LINUX_DEFAULT in /etc/default/grub so will probably have
GRUB_CMDLINE_LINUX_DEFAULT="quiet"
then
sudo update-grub
