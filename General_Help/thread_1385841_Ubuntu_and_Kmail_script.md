### Post by ellgor on 2010-01-20
Hi,

You can do this in Kmail, click on "settings" click "configure kmail"
in the new window select Accounts, near the bottom is a little tab with "Other Actions" on it, click this in the little box that appears there is a tab marked "Log to a file" choose your file and there you are.
