### Post by zemko on 2011-09-26
adding 

```
/dev/sdx {
apm = 254
apm_battery = 254
}
```

to the end of /etc/hdparm.conf **solves the problem**

and of course you must disable (set to 0) Laptop-Mode and laptop-mode-tools in /etc/laptop-mode/laptop-mode.conf
