### Post by rajeev1204 on 2008-05-02
I had a similar problem with my motherboard.Would get stuck on the ASUS boot logo or would take a minute or 10 min getting to bios or boot screen.


Disabled front USB connectors and front audio panel and it booted fine from then on.

Lot of cabinets have cheap usb ports or audio connectors so they can give the motherboard a tough time trying to detect.
