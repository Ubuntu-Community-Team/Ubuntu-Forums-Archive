### Post by inameiname on 2010-05-04
I currently use it on Lucid. Works just fine. No issues whatsoever. Easier than using Synaptic Package Manager. Also used throughout the life of Karmic as well, and worked just fine. Oh, and I only use default settings. 

Basically for any and all cleaning, I use BleachBit for most things, Ubuntu-Tweak's cleanup ability to remove the few config things that can be removed, and gtkorphan to remove orphaned packages.
