### Post by ProfBib on 2008-08-05
Sorry to hear you are having some trouble with your camera.  Which brand and model are you using?  If you give me that, I may be able to provide you with some meaningful assistance.

Before replying with that information, you may (if you have not already) want to try placing your camera in PTP (Picture Transfer Protocol) mode instead of UMS (Universal Mass Storage) mode.  This is done on your camera, usually in the main settings menu.  I believe that my Nikon Coolpix dial names it "SETUP."  Look for something similar to that on your camera and, if PTP is not selected, then select it.

Once you do, the auto transfer may work just fine, because the program that controls this action in Ubuntu uses (if I am not mistaken) PTP to do the auto transfer.
