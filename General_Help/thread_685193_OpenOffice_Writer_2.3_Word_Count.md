### Post by goldfalcon on 2008-02-01
I'm getting ludicrous word counts in Open Office. I've read of other problems with word count in much older versions of OO, and of disparities between MS Word and Writer due to how each determines what a "word" is, but these counts are so far off that they would actually be impossible with a 12 point font. 

For instance, I currently have 3.5 pages of double-spaced text in 12 point Times font. This should give me about 250 words per page on average, but when I select the text and press word count it tells me I have 1615 words. It would be impossible to fit 1615 double spaced words in a 12 point font into 3.5 pages. The number is likely half of the reported word count. This is not the first time either. I have had these ludicrous counts since upgrading to Gusty on release (possibly before, I only noticed it once I started writing longer documents). Anyone else experiencing word counts twice what they ought to be?
