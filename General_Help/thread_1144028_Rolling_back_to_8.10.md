### Post by snowpine on 2009-04-30
If you have your /home folder on a separate partition, you can carefully reinstall 8.10 and maintain your settings and documents using the manual partitioner. If not, your best option is to back everything up and reinstall 8.10.

In other words, there is not an easy "downgrade" feature.
