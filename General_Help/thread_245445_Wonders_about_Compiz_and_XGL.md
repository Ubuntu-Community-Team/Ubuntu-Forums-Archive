### Post by AirRaven on 2006-08-28
> **beerorkid said:**
> well intergrated is gonna prob not work, or be a hassle.  You kinda need a nvidia or ATI to get it working.

Not true.

Compiz works perfectly well on a lot of integrated Intel graphics chipsets- you just have to use what's called "Aiglx" instead of "XGL". 

[Check this thread out for more in-depth information](http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=148351).

Bear in mind it might not work with your chipset if it's a particularly old or non-supported one.
