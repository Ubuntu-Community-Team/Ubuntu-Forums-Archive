### Post by adam814 on 2010-01-26
> **t4thfavor said:**
> 
I also have a Toshiba, which works Great, yet I still use the Acer since it sees all of my RAM, and not just 3GB.

Ubuntu will recognize as much RAM as your BIOS will, with the exception of 32-bit non-PAE kernels.  Try using either the linux-generic-pae kernel or the amd64 version which will recognize much more RAM.
