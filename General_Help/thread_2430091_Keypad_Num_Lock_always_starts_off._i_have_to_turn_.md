### Post by Skaperen on 2019-10-27
for one particular user, the keypad Num Lock always starts in the off state. i have to turn it on each time, manually.  how can i make it always start in the on state?

*answer*:

1.  set it, manually, to the on state.
2.  log out
3.  log in
4.  verify that it is in the on state.

you may be like me and leave your userid logged on until you shutdown or reboot.  so it never actually does a real log out.  shutting down does not do that.  the only time that keyboard states are saved is at log out.  but if you never actually do a log out then it never saves the keyboard state.
