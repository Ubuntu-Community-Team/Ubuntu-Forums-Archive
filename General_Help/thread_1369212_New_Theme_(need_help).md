### Post by HalfEmptyHero on 2009-12-31
Go to gnome-look.org to look for themes and download theme from there. Normally, they will be be packaged in a tar.gz file, and you can simply drag and drop it into the System>Preferences>Appearance window. Some are archived differently though, and can't be done like this. For those, simply extract the theme into your theme directory (/home/your_username/.themes for themes, or /home/your_username/.icons for icons) and then load up System>Preferences>Appearance and choose it.
