I installed Google Earth with Automatix and when I discovered that it would not work with my card I used Automatix to uninstall. The problem is that the uninstall left behind a folder of files in my HOME directory and who knows where else. Is this a common problem, and, is it OK to just delete that folder and it's files? Also, why does Automatix not completely remove what they install - it kind of turns me off to Automatix.

### Post by majoridiot on 2007-02-22
automatix removes the packages instead of purging them. the only difference is that remove leaves
behind configuration files, etc.- so settings, etc. will survive if you decide to reinstall later.

if this bothers you, it is fine to remove the left-behind files by hand.
