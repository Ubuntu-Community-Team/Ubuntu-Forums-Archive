### Post by marcw on 2005-09-24
[QUOTE=redwinder]when i log in, gaim starts automatically and i don`t want that, i installed version 1.5 but gaim still starts automatically, and i have look at the options page and i have found nothing,does anyone know an easy way to disable that?,thanks in advance  :)[/QUOTE]

Is it that you don't want Gaim automatically logging in to your accounts at startup or that you don't want Gaim starting up at all?

If the former, go to Tools -> Accounts and uncheck auto-login for those accounts you wish to log into manually.  If the latter, make sure Gaim has actually exited (check your System Tray) and perform a system logout-login making sure to check the 'Save current setup' checkbox when logging out.

I dont know about GNOME, but for KDE it will restore your previous session on start (unless you change it to start a new session). If you had Gaim open when you shut down your session, it will reload it on the next. If you use KDE try going to the Control Center, KDE Components, and Session Manager and clicking Start With Empty Session.
