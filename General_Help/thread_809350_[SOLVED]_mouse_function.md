### Post by stalkingwolf on 2008-05-27
I want to be able to open everything, files drives etc. with a single mouse click instead of double click.  How do I do this?

---

### Post by aJayRoo on 2008-05-27
Open a Nautilus (file browser) window, any one will do. Then click on the 'Edit' menu and click 'Preferences'. Click on the 'Behaviour' tab and click the radio button next to 'Single click to open items'. This should do what you want.
