### Post by vgillestad on 2007-05-24
To integrate tomcat and eclipse you can use the sysdeo-plugin found at this page:
[http://www.eclipsetotale.com/tomcatPlugin.html](http://www.eclipsetotale.com/tomcatPlugin.html)

(If you have problems with starting/stopping tomcat from eclipse with the sysdeo-plugin, try starting eclipse as root. (not recommended, but works:))
