### Post by hw-tph on 2006-03-04
[Ardour](http://ardourg.org) is not a mixer - it is a complete digital audio workstation. Think of it as a possible replacement for Emagic/Apple Logic or Steinberg Cubase. It is not easy to get started with but neither are the Windows/Mac equivalents. You can record as many tracks simultaneously as your hardware supports.

If you want something that is a tad easier to get started with I suggest you look into [Rosegarden](http://www.rosegardenmusic.com/).
