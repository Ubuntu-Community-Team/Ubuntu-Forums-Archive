### Post by ryanVickers on 2007-11-28
in System > Preferences >CompizConfig Settings Manager, or just Appearance > (Tab)Visual Effects > Custom (Button)Preferences, either or, but once your there, look for a button from the list that says "Resize Window" at the bottom, and click it.  Then change the default mode to "Normal" instead of "rectangle".
