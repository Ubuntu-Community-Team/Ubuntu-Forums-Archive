### Post by pytheas22 on 2008-09-03
It depends on which repositories are enabled.  If you don't have anything enabled, it wouldn't take very long to download and install.  If you have all the standard repositories (universe, multiverse etc.) enabled, I suspect that it would take longer than ten hours to download all of the packages, but I guess if you have a really fast connection, it's possible.

Also you can't download *everything* in the repositories because some things conflict with other things.
