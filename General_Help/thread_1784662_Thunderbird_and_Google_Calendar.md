### Post by collisionystm on 2011-06-17
I got it!

Logged into my account. Went to Calendar.

Calendar Settings

'Calendar' Tab

Clicked on my Calendar

At the bottom, 'Calendar Address'

Clicked on ICAL

Copied the link and pasted that into the setup.

Works great!! That was all I had to do....
