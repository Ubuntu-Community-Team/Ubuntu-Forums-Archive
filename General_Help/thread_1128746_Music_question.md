### Post by sunlitlaz on 2009-04-17
There are lots of music tools for Linux, two of the best are:

**Audacity** - [http://audacity.sourceforge.net/](http://audacity.sourceforge.net/)
This is great for editing audio files.

**Ardour** - [http://ardour.org/](http://ardour.org/)
This is a digital audio workstation, along the lines of Cakewalk and such, and maybe better.

There are lots more out there, and there is even a distro of Ubuntu dedicated to such tasks called Ubuntu Studio ([http://ubuntustudio.org/](http://ubuntustudio.org/)) which is packed with loads of tools by default.

Hope that helps!
