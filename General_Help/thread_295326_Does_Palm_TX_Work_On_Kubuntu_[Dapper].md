### Post by wastrel on 2006-11-08
Palm support in Linux is quite robust but unfortunately many people have had serious problems getting Hotsync working under Dapper.

This problem affects USB sync and is fixed in kernel 2.6.17.  So the options are to use wifi hotsync (which I think you can do with a TX) upgrade your kernel in Dapper, or upgrade to Edgy.
