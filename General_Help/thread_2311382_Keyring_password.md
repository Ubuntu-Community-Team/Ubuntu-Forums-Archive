### Post by ian-weisser on 2016-01-26
If you are using automatic login, or if your login password is different from the keyring password, then the keyring is not automatically unlocked upon login.

Then you will receive the keyring prompt the first time you do an action that requires the keyring.

For example, network access often requires a password or two from the keyring.

Are you asking how to disable your network access?
Are you asking what actions require the keyring?
Are you aking how to change the keyring password to match your login password?
Are you asking how to disable automatic login?

### Post by ian-weisser on 2016-01-27
Removing the keyring is unwise.
You will get dozens of error and warning and password pop-ups every session, since the keyring will no longer be doing that task for you.
