### Post by DeMus on 2008-09-04
Open nautilus or whatever filebrowser you use and use the search function. Fill in desktop.xml and you will get a list of files with that name in it. Look for the one and only real desktop.xml file. When clicking on it, your webbrowser opens and you can see the contens of the file.
Whay you want to open it? What do oyu want to do with it?
Btw, in my installation the file is located here:
/usr/share/apps/katepart/syntax

DeMus

### Post by Scunge on 2008-09-04
