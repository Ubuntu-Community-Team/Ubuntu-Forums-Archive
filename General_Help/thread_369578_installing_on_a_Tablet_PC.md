### Post by tribble222 on 2007-02-24
So you restarted the computer with the cd in, but it booted from the hard drive instead of the cd?  If that is the case then you need to look in the bios and set it to check the cdrom device for booting before the hard drive.  Usually you press F2 or del during the POST screen to get into the bios.
