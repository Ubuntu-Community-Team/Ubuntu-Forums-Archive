### Post by 73ckn797 on 2008-12-24
In OO3 at the bottom right there are 3 icons of pages and a slider control to the right of that. The slider adjusts the zoom level of the page viewed. Zoom smaller and it will display, from what I can tell, 18 pages at once if the document has that many. I loaded a 3 page doc and at the smallest setting it appears there is room for 3 rows of 6 = 18. You can adjust it to a 2 page display to fill the viewing area.
