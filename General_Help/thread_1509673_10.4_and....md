### Post by cbrunhaver on 2010-06-14
Flash has gotten better in Linux (make sure to download flash 10.1 via the updates manger) but it is still a long ways away from the performance under windows.

ATI drivers under linux leave something to be desired (though both the binary blob and open source ones are getting better pretty quickly) are getting better.  

I would try this, first:

[http://www.omgubuntu.co.uk/2010/06/fixing-fullscreen-flash-in-ubuntu-1004.html](http://www.omgubuntu.co.uk/2010/06/fixing-fullscreen-flash-in-ubuntu-1004.html)

I would also use chromium instead of firefox and download the chroimium-codes-ffmpeg-nonfree package in synaptic and switch over to HTML5 in youtube by going to [www.youtube.com/html5](www.youtube.com/html5).
