### Post by Rocket2DMn on 2010-03-27
In order to make things appear smaller on the screen and therefore be able to show more on the screen, you need to increase to a higher resolution (more pixels).  By dropping the resolution (decreasing the number of pixels), objects appear larger on the screen, but less can fit on the screen.  It is usually best to run at your monitor's native resolution, which is usually the maximum that it can handle - this results in a clearer picture.

What make/model monitor are you using, or what is your laptop make/model?
