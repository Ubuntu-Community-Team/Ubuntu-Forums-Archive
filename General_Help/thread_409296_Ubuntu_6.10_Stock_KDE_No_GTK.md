### Post by Awayne on 2007-04-14
I recently installed Ubuntu 6.10 off a DVD with a stock build, generic KDE 3.5. It is not part of the Kubuntu desktop so misses packages that are added in Kubuntu. I added what I needed including Adept to update the system but I have no controls in the KDE control center for applying GTK themes to GTK apps instead of using QT ones. Anyone know how to add this menu item?

---

### Post by allix on 2007-04-14
What you are looking for is the [gtk2-engines-gtk-qt]("http://packages.ubuntu.com/edgy/kde/gtk2-engines-gtk-qt") package.

---

### Post by Awayne on 2007-04-14
That worked wonders, thank you.
