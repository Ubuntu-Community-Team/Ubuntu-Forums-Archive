### Post by drigloi on 2005-10-16
Please also check that the drive is connected to your PC correctly:

If it is a Sec. Master, place its jumper to the correct location and plug the your IDE cable's end connector to the drive.

If it is a Pri or Sec. Slave, place the jumper accordingly and plug the middle IDE connector to the drive.
