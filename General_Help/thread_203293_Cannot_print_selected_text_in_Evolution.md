### Post by asimon on 2006-06-25
Yes, Evolution always prints the whole email. It's a known bug, so far still unfixed. See [Bug 322219 – Print selection doesn't work.](http://bugzilla.gnome.org/show_bug.cgi?id=322219)

### Post by asimon on 2006-06-26
[QUOTE=redPirate]Btw is it a bug for Gnome or Evolution?[/QUOTE]
Yes, it's a bug in Evolution. Because Evolution is part of Gnome it's also a bug in Gnome. ;-)
It's not distribution specific, no matter what Linux distribution you use, Evolution will always print the whole email.
