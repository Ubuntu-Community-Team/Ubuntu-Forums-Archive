Hello,

I am curious as to how something like Deal Cabby got "installed" onto Firefox.

Deal cabby set a cookie in my browser, and acted as a proxy.  So all pages I request went through their server.  Giving me double underlined links on random words with custom deals for me.  VERY ANNOYING and I curse their name.
[http://www.dealcabby.com/about.php](http://www.dealcabby.com/about.php)

Anyone else have problems with them?

If you have it installed and want it removed, do not follow the instructions on the site, they are incorrect.  There is no add-on for it for Firefox.  

Just go to "Edit" -> "Preferences" -> "Privacy" tab -> then click "remove individual cookies"

Then search for "dealcabby" and delete those cookies.
