### Post by lovinglinux on 2011-05-11
Have you tried [Video Download Helper]("https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/3006/")? If that extension cannot download the video, then most likely no other will.

I suspect the problem is that the ads are streamed without any protection, while the episode itself is streamed using rtmp. I have already encountered sites that worked in a similar way. If that is the case, then you will need to actually record your screen while watching the entire episode, using a tool like recordMyDesktop, or try rtmpdump. See [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1390764](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1390764)
