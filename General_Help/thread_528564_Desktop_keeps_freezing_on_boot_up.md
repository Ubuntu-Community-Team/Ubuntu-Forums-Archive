### Post by notwen on 2007-08-18
Do you have any USB devices connected?  If your BIOS is set to check for USB devices during boot, it will attempt to boot off say any additional device.  My computer won't boot up if my I leave my iPod connected during a reboot.  You may/may not have the option in your BIOS to disable this.
