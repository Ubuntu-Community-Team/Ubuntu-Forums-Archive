### Post by deadflowr on 2020-02-23
> I want to disable the annoying feature whereby GNOME closes a browser window when i middle click it. 
Happens in other desktops.
Not just gnome.
It's a browser setting.
Unfortunately, there is no setting to disable, and it's unknown if there is an add-on that can do it.
Here's wishlist request for firefox: [https://bugzilla.mozilla.org/show_bug.cgi?id=1439247]("https://bugzilla.mozilla.org/show_bug.cgi?id=1439247")
Chromium and chromium-based browsers also seem to suffer from this too.
