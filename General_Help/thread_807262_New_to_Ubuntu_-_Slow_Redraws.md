### Post by EXCiD3 on 2008-05-25
Go to System > Administration > Hardware Drivers and check the box for your video card. It will take care of installing and configuring your drivers for you. Hopefully everything goes good. You can also check out EnvyNG as another method of installation. Although it is not officially supported, it is a very good tool.
