### Post by cozmicharlie on 2008-06-27
Setting up ports is done at the router interface through your browser and it is different for different routers.  Most newer routers make it easy.  Look up your router here ([http://www.portforward.com/routers.htm](http://www.portforward.com/routers.htm)) and follow the instructions for your router.  If you get stuck post back and we can help.
