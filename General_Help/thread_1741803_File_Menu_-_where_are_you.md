### Post by Peter09 on 2011-04-28
There are two options, the lenses (grey at the bottom of the launcher) or the Super+a and the Super+f.

For example - hitting Super+a and starting to type - appearance - will very quickly get you to the appearance application. Similarly Super + f and typing a filename will get you to a file.

Also the shutdown button menu has a system settings option.
