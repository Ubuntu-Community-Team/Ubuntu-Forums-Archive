### Post by kokkus on 2008-10-08
Go to [www.scumbox.com](www.scumbox.com)
There are all kind of streams there (direct and P2P), so some of the channels won't work unless you add the ActiveX applets to your firefox temp folder, but most of the channels will work fine at once.
There are channels like Disney Cinemagic, FOX sports and SKY sports, euro sport, cnn, bbc world, discovery and more. Offcourse it's free!
