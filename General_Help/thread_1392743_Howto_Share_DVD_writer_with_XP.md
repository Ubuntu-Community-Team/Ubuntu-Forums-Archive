### Post by mk1w86 on 2010-01-28
I think the best thing to do would be to setup samba on your Ubuntu machine (assuming that is the machine with the DVD burner) and copy the files to it from the XP machine then burn them in Ubuntu.  You could of course transfer the files using another method e.g. USB flash drive.

Although it might be possible to share a DVD burner over a network I don't think it would be a good idea for burning disks.  I have shared a DVD drive over a network but only for reading files. ;)
