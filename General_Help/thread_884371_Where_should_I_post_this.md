### Post by ad_267 on 2008-08-08
It's not working for me too but I found a way to fix it. The problem is that there is a flash "skin" covering the video so it's playing but you just can't see it. I have the adblock plus extension installed and I blocked [http://www.reuters.com/resources/flash/video_skin.swf](http://www.reuters.com/resources/flash/video_skin.swf), now I can see the video.

Also, next time just post with a title like "Can't play some flash videos" and if it's in the wrong place a mod will move it.
