### Post by _Purple_ on 2009-04-17
If want to kill any particular process, you can use the System Monitor.
To kill an application in GUI at once, you can press Alt+F2. This will bring a dialogue box. Type "xkill" on it without the ". The cursor will turn into X. Anything that you left-click will be killed. To turn it off, right-click anywhere.

P.S. DO NOT CLICK ON DESKTOP.
