### Post by p_quarles on 2007-07-29
Personally, I'm very happy with my Sandisk Sansa. There are different models, depending on how much storage space you need, whether you want video, etc. Their distinguishing feature is an add-on port for a MicroSD card, which is handy. It works fine with any Linux file manager.

There are others, though, that are better integrated with Linux. Take a look at the Rockbox project page:
[http://www.rockbox.org/](http://www.rockbox.org/)
Rockbox is open source firmware that is compatible with several brands of mp3player, and will allow you to use OGG, crossfading and other stuff (on certain brands).
