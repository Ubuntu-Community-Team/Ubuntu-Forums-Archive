### Post by Zoowey on 2009-03-25
Whenever I play any 3D full screen game everything works wonderfully, however when I exit the game to go back to the desktop the game closes but its resolution stays and does not switch back to my native 1280 x 1024. 

However, its not as if the resolution is changed, after I exit the game only the top left of the screen is rendered and how big the top left is rendered depends on the resolution of the game. The screen is not frozen and I can move my mouse however I cannot see my toolbar or anything expect for icons or anything that happens to be in that top left corner. 

I tried making a shortcut of the "Screen Resolution" utility to change the resolution back to 1280 x 1024 but whenever I switch the resolution the system will crash to a blank black screen which requires a hard reboot.

I am using the latest ATI Catalyst Driver, version 9.2 and am using a ATI Radeon HD 4850 card. I have Compiz working great and the games play very smoothly on even the highest settings. So I highly doubt it's a driver issue and thinking it's a X.org problem. I always disable Compiz before starting any games so it's not a Compiz problem.

Your help will be greatly appreciated, thanks.
