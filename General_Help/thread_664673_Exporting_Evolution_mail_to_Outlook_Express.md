### Post by beesthorpe on 2008-01-11
OK -  your Evolution inbox is stored as an mbox file called (logically enough!) "inbox" in  home/user/.evolution/mail/local. I've never had any trouble moving them between Thunderbird and Evolution butr I'm not sure about Outlook. However I believe it too has a facility for importing mbox files.
