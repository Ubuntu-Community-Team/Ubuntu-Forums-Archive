### Post by XturnLGod on 2009-04-22
This was a problem I was living since yesterday.
I upgraded to Ubuntu 9.04, and when I tried to run logmein to remote control a computer (using firefox + the logmein client), once the "desktop" would be just about to appear, firefox would crash on me.
In my case, an AMD Athlon PC w an nvidia onboard video card; here's what fixed it for me.

system / preferences / appearance under the visual effects tab, turn that onto "extra" or "normal".

I had turned this off so that games like Alien Arena and Nexuiz would run; unfortunately, having this setting turned off causes firefox to crash when trying to logmein into another computer.

just thought I'd share.
-XturnLGod
