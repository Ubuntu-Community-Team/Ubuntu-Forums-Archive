### Post by DFlame on 2009-08-28
Support pages for this laptop say it has a CD drive. Why not download and burn a LiveCD and try it out? Starting the computer from a CD like this will not alter anything on your computer, while you get the chance to test everything to see if it works the way you want it to.

If you like the way it works, you can install Ubuntu from disc without getting rid of Windows (a "dualboot") or just have Ubuntu be the only OS installed (wiping Windows). If you don't like it, just shut down the computer, remove the disc and everything will return to normal.

You can download a LiveCD from [here](http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/download) or borrow one from a friend :)

EDIT: There's also a free beginners guide [here](http://www.ubuntupocketguide.com/index_main.html)
