### Post by Rsever on 2010-09-05
So today my dell mini 9 started having problems. if the laptop is plugged into the wall (charging) the trackpad will not allow me to move the mouse. It will still click (both left and right) but it will not move.   Now when it is unplugged from the wall everything works normally. 

I started playing around with the charger and as soon as it makes contact with the metal node in the laptop, the mouse wont move. If i put it in halfway (the laptop is still running on battery power) it stops working.

I havent done any recent updates, this just started today for no reason. Running the most recent version of netbook remix.

Appreciate any help, thanks!



Update:
when plugged in, if i use my finger by just barely touching the pad I am able to get some movement. This leads me to believe (according to other laptops trackpad issues) that I have a problem with my power supply, but I am not totally sure.

---

### Post by Rsever on 2010-09-06
So I solved the problem!  It turns out it was the outlet. I switched outlets and she purrs like a kitten once again. So if you're having the same issues I was, change outlets.
