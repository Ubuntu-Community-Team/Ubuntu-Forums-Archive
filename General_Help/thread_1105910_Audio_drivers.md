### Post by mb_webguy on 2009-03-25
First, turn your speakers back down to normal volume.  Then right-click on the Volume applet on the right side of the upper panel, and select Open Volume Control.  Look at the "mixer" and "playback" devices for any volume controls that are turned down or muted.
