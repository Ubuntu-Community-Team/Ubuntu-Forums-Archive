### Post by ajgreeny on 2009-01-26
If you disable the function to raise focus when you hover over a window it will then only do so when you click any mouse button on the new one.  That is the default, I think, and much easier to use in general situations, I would suggest.
EDIT:  Sorry I've just re-read your post andd see you want focus only on a right or middle or left+right click.  I certainly can't help there, but it may be possible somewhere in the gnome config files.  Have a look in **gconf-editor** to see if you can find anything.

### Post by phantom3113 on 2009-01-29
This isn't necessarily a fix for the settings of focus, but another way to do what I believe you want to do. Under the window options (upper left corner of the window) there's an option to "set above" or "always on top". if you check that, the window will stay on top of everything whether it's active or not.
