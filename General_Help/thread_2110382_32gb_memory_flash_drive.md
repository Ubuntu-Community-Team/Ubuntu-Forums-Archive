### Post by Laiquendi on 2013-01-30
You just insert it in USB port, wait a few seconds until it's mounted and an icon should appear, depending on your desktop environment, on the left or on the desktop :)

Then you click on it and delete files, like usual, using delete key or right mouse-click->remove.
