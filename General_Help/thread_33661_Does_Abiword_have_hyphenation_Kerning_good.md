### Post by GTKpower on 2005-05-11
Just installed Abiword 2.2.7.

1.) Hyphenation is either broken or galactically unintuitive.  My justified paragraph shouldn't have any white rivers running through it.

2.) Tracking is horribly flawed, so printed text looks butchered.  Kerning is bad as well. No, it isn't my printer, either.  My installation is not only CLEAN, I also have a Deskjet 5150, a fairly standard printer, complete with HP drivers from the repos.  Lousy tracking (read: none) was also present in previous Abiword versions.

3.) SmartQuotes doesn't work.  HELLLOOO!  Character mapping!  Generic inch marks don't count as quotation marks.  

Back to OpenOffice I go.

Although, I must say, I appreciate the work that went into compiling Abiword packages for Ubuntu.  The dev team works hard to give us all the necessary tools to get work done, but they can't be faulted for underperforming applications.

Oh well.  Maybe in the next version . . . .
