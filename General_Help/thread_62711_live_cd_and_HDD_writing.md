### Post by Arne Caspari on 2005-09-06
Actually it will install the package into the RAM disk and whether this will succeed depends on the amount of free memory available ;-)

Nothing will be written to the disk however your application will be gone on the next reboot.

---

### Post by rafakl on 2005-09-06
Exactly.

You can install apps, mp3 support, music files, and they only be on RAM... when you shut down you computer the new installed progs will be lost, and your hard disk untouched.

If you dont want to create a partition for ubuntu in your friends hd but want to keep the files you download, you can use an USB drive to save them  :razz:
