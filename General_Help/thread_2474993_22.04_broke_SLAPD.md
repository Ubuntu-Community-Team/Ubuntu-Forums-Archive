### Post by QIII on 2022-05-19
We've relaxed the 24 hour wait to 12 hours.  The problem with 24 hours is that bumping at 24 hours puts the thread to the top right back at the same time of day.  Waiting 36 hours would not allow a pressing issue to be addressed in a reasonable amount of time.  Going with 12 hours allows a user to get the issue in front of a different audience -- and hopefully someone with an answer.

Bumping is not only allowed.  It is encouraged.  If you don't bump, your thread will sink into the depths of the abyss and be buried in the muck, never to be seen again.  We'd like you to get a helpful answer.
