### Post by Afkpuz on 2008-11-22
I think that emerald mostly affects the drawing of the bar on top of each window and any border around it.  To change things actually within the window, you need to change your GTK theme.  that's when you right click on desktop and select "change desktop background", then click the theme tab.  You can also get these on gnome-look.

### Post by malachi on 2008-11-22
Emerald is a window decorator, meaning it handles your window borders. (Like the title of the window, the minimize, maximize, and close button, and the borders around the window.) GTK handles the rest.
