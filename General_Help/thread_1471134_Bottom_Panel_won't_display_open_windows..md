### Post by oldtraveler on 2010-05-03

### Post by nothingspecial on 2010-05-03
Right click on the panel and choose add to panel.

Then select window list and click add.

---

### Post by sunk8 on 2010-05-03
Right click on a blank area on the bottom panel.

Add to Panel >> Window List.

U can also have a tiny Window Selector...
Saves a lot of space on my cluttered panel...
