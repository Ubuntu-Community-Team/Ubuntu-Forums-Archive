### Post by marshmallow1304 on 2011-02-02
Webpages can tell the browser to open links in new windows.  Firefox allows you to modify this behavior to open "new-window" links in a new tab instead, which is probably why some links open in a new tab.  You can disable this and have links open a new window in the Tabs section of Firefox's Preferences dialog.  Alternately, you can follow [this]("http://www.ghacks.net/2009/07/03/force-firefox-to-open-links-in-same-tab/") to force all links to open in the same tab unless otherwise specified.
