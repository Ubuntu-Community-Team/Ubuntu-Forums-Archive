### Post by mister_playboy on 2010-11-15
1. Create a new folder on your desktop. (right-click > Create Folder)
2. Right-click on that folder and choose "Open with Other Application..."
3. Choose "File Browser" and make sure the box "Remember this application for 'folder' files" is checked.
4. Click "Open"
