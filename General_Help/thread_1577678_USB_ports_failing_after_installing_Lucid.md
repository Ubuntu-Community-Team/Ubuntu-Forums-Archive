### Post by Strategist01 on 2010-09-19
Ok, two things to check:

Can you plug in a USB flash drive or storage device and does it work? If it does, then your USB ports are fine, maybe they need a bit of cleaning as they might be dusty.

If they do work, then it could be that your mouse might not be recognized by Ubuntu for some odd, odd reason - most likely drivers? Is your mouse fairly common? If it's quite common there should eb drivers for it...that's just the basics. Some others will help you in greater detail.
