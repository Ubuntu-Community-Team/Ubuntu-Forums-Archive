### Post by P4man on 2009-09-25
It could be a LOT of things Im afraid. Your best bet is probably to start by removing/disconnecting anything not strictly necessary (harddisk, cdrom, perhaps some cards you have in there besides the videocard).

If that doesnt change anything, remove the videocard. See if it beeps. most motherboards would beep when they can't detect a videocard. You could even try removing the RAM. Same thing, motherboards should beep when they find no RAM. If none of this gets any life in it.. you could try resetting the bios (find the MB manual), you could try a different power supply.

If none of that helps anything, consider the MB dead.
