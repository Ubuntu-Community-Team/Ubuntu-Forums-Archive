Just upgraded from Hardy to Ibex on my laptop.
I use 4 workspaces and when switching from one to another they 'slide' back and forth which annoys me. How can I switch it back to the way if was in Hardy where there is no 'slide' when switching between workspaces? 

I did it on my desktop which has Ibex awhile back but forgot how it's done.

### Post by eBoxNet on 2008-12-18
Go : System>Preferences>Appearance>Visual Effects
And disable Visual Effects

> **eBoxNet said:**
> Go : System>Preferences>Appearance>Visual Effects

Or, if you want to keep visual effects (wobbly windows, etc) but just turn off the "slide" then click on System-Preferences-CompizConfig Settings Manager-Desktop-Desktop Wall, and disable the plugin by unchecking (unticking) the "Enable Desktop Wall Plugin" option.

Don't forget to enable "Visual Effects" first, if you want to use them!
