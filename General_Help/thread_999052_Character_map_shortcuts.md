### Post by geirha on 2009-03-23
> **milamoto3 said:**
> I am experiencing same issue.

I came across website that says that you need to press ctrl+shift+u and the number of the character, but it feels like my fingers are playing Twister on my Keyboard,

You don't need to hold all those keys down. Hit Ctrl+Shift+u, then release all keys. You'll see a _**u**_ at the cursor. Type in the four digit unicode followed by space.
