### Post by markbl on 2012-05-24
I really like gnome-shell also but like you I find the notifications a little frustrating, particularly for chat. I disable the native empathy app (i.e don't configure any online accounts for chat) and then I just use pidgin which works well. Make sure you enable the buddy notification and libnotify plugins in pidgin.
