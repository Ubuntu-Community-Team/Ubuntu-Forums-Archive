File transfer is easy, as he mentions, just plug it and you'll see that it will automatically be mounted.

### Post by Bharath on 2009-02-27
Hi,

I wanted to post how I overcame issues while working with E71-Evolution. I dont have a direct solution. But am using 3rd party solutions.

As mentioned in this post, I also use Google Apps work email. When I tried to use native email application on E71, sent items was lost (when I downloaded the mail on Evolution later - Gmail works on labels). On the other hand if I used Gmail application, I could not download or  attach any Docs or access contacts on my mobile to choose email IDs. Also could not open ziped attachemeents.

So I came up with a funny solution:
On E71 I use both Gmail Application for sending email as well as Native Email Application (configured to IMAP). For just viewing / sending / forwardng email without attachments I use Google Application. If I have to send an attachment, I use Native application. 

On my evolution I have configured both IMAP and POP for the same account. And in Gmail Apps settings I have selected keep a copy in "Inbox" under POP access.

Now when ever I leave my computer / laptop I empty (deleete or move to local folders) Inbox and sent Items. So what ever I mails I send and receive from mobile there is a copy online. When I come back I move them to local folders.

This way I am not loosing any mails. I have tried all other combinations..... but this is the best.

For Sync google Calander I am using free Goosync (on Evolution we can view google calander but not edit) So I use my mobile as input gateway for all calander entries (not evolution)..... 

I am not able to sync to-do/tasks or memos...

For Contact Sync I am using zyb

For now I am happy with this setting...... can anybody suggest a better and easy one......
