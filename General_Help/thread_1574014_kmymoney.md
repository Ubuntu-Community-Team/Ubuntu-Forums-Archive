kmymoney is probably the closest open source, free option.

The help files in kmymoney could be better IMO.

The second best, IMHO, is gnucash. Gnucash has better documentation, but the reports are not as nice.

In terms of online banking, it is usually easier to download the information from your bank (export it as a OFX ? I think) and then import it.

[http://kmymoney2.sourceforge.net/online-manual/details.impexp.ofx.html](http://kmymoney2.sourceforge.net/online-manual/details.impexp.ofx.html)

You can try plugins 

[http://kmymoney2.sourceforge.net/plugins.html](http://kmymoney2.sourceforge.net/plugins.html)

After my initial attempt a few years ago, the first method works better (download -> import).
