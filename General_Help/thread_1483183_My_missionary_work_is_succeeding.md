### Post by StephanG on 2010-05-14
Ubuntu has a tool called Startup Disk Creator.  Its installed by default since 9.10 (I think).  Just launch it, point to a .ISO, and a USB and click on "Make Disk".

Then you just have to boot from the USB, and you can run it like any other Live CD.

Hope that helps.
