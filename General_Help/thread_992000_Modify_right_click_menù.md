### Post by kaibob on 2008-11-25
You cannot remove items from the Nautilus right-click menu. You can add items that provide the ability to perform various file-related tasks (such as copy or move).

The easiest option is to use Nautilus Actions:

[http://www.grumz.net/index.php?q=taxonomy/term/2/9](http://www.grumz.net/index.php?q=taxonomy/term/2/9)

The other alternative--as mentioned by binbash--is Nautilus scripts:

[http://g-scripts.sourceforge.net/](http://g-scripts.sourceforge.net/)

Both Nautilus Actions and scripts take a bit of time.
