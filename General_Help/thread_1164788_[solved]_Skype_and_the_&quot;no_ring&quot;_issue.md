### Post by ghohn on 2009-05-20
I was trying to fix my "no sound in Youtube with Firefox" issue.  I found it from another forum so I can't claim it as my own.  This did the trick as well as fixed the lack of ring or audio notification in Skype 2.0.  As a brand new user, I'm just happy to give back to an awesome online help community.  Peace!

Remove the "pulseaudio" package and set the output software in System -> Settings -> Audio to Alsa.
