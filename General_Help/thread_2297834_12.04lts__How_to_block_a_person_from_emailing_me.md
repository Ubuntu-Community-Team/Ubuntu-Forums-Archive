### Post by DuckHook on 2015-10-07
I have found that the easiest way in general is not fussing at the OS level or with the e-mail client, but blocking specific e-mail addresses at your ISP/e-mail provider end. Most providers have blocking functions on their web-based e-mail pages, from where you can also train your provider's spam filter.
