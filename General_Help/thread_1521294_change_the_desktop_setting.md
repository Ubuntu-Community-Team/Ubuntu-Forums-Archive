### Post by ajgreeny on 2010-06-30
When you login, enter your username, or just choose it if it is showing, but before you enter your password, go to the menu at bottom right for Desktop, click it and choose Gnome, instead of UNR 3D or UNR 2D.  Now enter password and hit enter.

Voila!  A standard gnome desktop, which you can configure the same as in any other gnome setup.

PS:  If you netbook is set to autologin, you will need to logout and in again to get the system to offer you that menu I mentioned.  Once set, the system will retain your choice until you change it again.
