### Post by hunteramor on 2005-09-11
Until today, I had been struggling with my old Brother MFC-4420c which is apparently impossible to get working in Linux.

Today, following the recommendations on linuxprinting.org, I went out and purchased a new HP Officejet 6210 all-in-one.  It's big, it looks good, has all kind of flashing lights and beeps, and a page feed (!).  Quite an improvement.

Ran into a little bit of trouble with the drivers.  For anyone trying to install this printer in Ubuntu, select the Officejet 6200 in the wizard.  Selecting only "OfficeJet" will get you printing, but only in B&W.  6200 is apparently close enough and will get you printing in full color.  Couldn't be happier when I printed my first page in Linux today, ending several months of frustration stemming from being too cheap to go out and buy a new multifunction. No more rebooting into Windows every time I want to print/scan/copy/fax!

(This post is primarily for anyone searching the forums for the printer models mentioned above.  Also, to gloat.)
