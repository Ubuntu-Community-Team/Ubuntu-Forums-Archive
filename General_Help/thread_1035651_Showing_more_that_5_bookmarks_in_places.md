It *is* unlimited. You have full access to the source code, so you can change whatever you want. Of course its up to you to learn hoe to do that, but unlike in other operating systems you are free to do that without any legal, or other, restrictions.

Being free to do something is not the same as being able to do it.

By the way, it *definitely* is not hard coded to the kernel. Kernel has hardly anything to do with your graphical desktop. It's hardcoded in the panel applet that draws the menu. :D
