### Post by lswest on 2009-01-11
Is the location of the wallpaper mounted?  Make sure that you can actually access the wallpaper, since the brown shows that it can't reach the wallpaper and defaults to the solid brown color.

For example: I share a home partition with 32bit and 64bit Ubuntu, and my wallpapers are on my windows partition, but the config file in the /home display the wallpaper in the theme dialog, but when clicked upon shows the dark brown, since the windows drive isn't mounted.  Once I mount it, it all works fine.
