Hello, I am new to ubuntu, switching from windows and one thing I really like is having multiple workspaces. I just have a couple questions though.

How do I get more than just two? When I installed ubuntu I got 2 as default but how do I increase this to 4 or 5?

Secondly, is there a way to set keyboard shortcuts to switch between workspaces? It would be really nice not to have to move the mouse back and forth to do it everytime.

---

### Post by taurus on 2008-12-27
Place a cursor at either workspace (bottom right) and click the right button.  Then go into Preferences and increase the Number of _w_orkspaces to whichever number you want.
