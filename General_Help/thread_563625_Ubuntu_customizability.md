### Post by PmDematagoda on 2007-09-30
If you want to really rip your Ubuntu installation then you have to get used to the Terminal. It isn't difficult and can really pay off in the end, especially when you face problems, since one of the most stable and powerful features of Linux is the terminal.

And of course once you get used to the terminal, things become really easy and you can do any change you want to the Linux system. Working with different software also may require you to use the terminal often since not every program can be installed via .deb(The Debian/Ubuntu version of .exe) and you may need to compile them.

If you don't still get the power of the terminal, it was the terminal itself that got my heavily damaged and modified X-server to run.
