### Post by xyzzyman on 2012-01-18
You have to pay for pop3/imap access for Yahoo email in most countries. There are ways around paying for it but it's against Yahoo's TOS so we can't get into it here.

I would recommend creating a gmail account, and give it your yahoo information and it will import all your email into gmail (It'll leave it in your yahoo account so don't worry). Then you can set up pop3 or imap access whichever you prefer on gmail. I'd also recommend thunderbird as you could then export everything it downloads in what should be a pretty reliable format as it's cross platform.
