### Post by sikander3786 on 2010-09-06
Might have happened because of a system vide language change. Did you change the language? If that is the reason and you want to keep the same language, try this.

Right click what ever the icon you want to move, uncheck "Lock to panel", right click again and select "Move" and then move the icon where ever you want to place it. For network and volume icons, right click on the small lines that represent system tray and repeat the process until all the icons are on the desired positions. Don't forgot to lock them again.
