### Post by ads52 on 2012-08-13
It is not impossible, particularly if you are compiling your *other* copy. For the compiled copy you could either change the *--prefix=xxx* in ./configure to something different or even not install at all and run the compiled copy from its compiled source directory. Can I ask why you want to do this though? It sounds a little unusual :).
