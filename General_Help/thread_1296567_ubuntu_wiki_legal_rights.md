### Post by benj1 on 2009-10-20
i suppose if you were just using the images to illustrate something and the only logos that appeared were within those screenshots (ie the logo in the top left), that would be fine.
you are also allowed to write about ubuntu, what you are not allowed to do is make it appear you are associated with ubuntu.

so an ubuntu branded website called 'the official ubuntu tutorial site' wouldnt be allowed but writing a review or article about ubuntu is fine
