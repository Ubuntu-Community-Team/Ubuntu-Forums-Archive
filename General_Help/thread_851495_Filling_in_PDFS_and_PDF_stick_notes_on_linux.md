### Post by chrisby on 2008-07-06
While I wish there was a full version of Acrobat for Linux, sadly it still isn't available.  I have tried Pdf Editor which I found to be very awkward and buggy.

Recently I tried the portable version of the free [pdf exchange viewer]("http://www.docu-track.com/home/prod_user/PDF-XChange_Tools/pdfx_viewer") with wine, and found that I can make sticky notes, and fill in/save pdf documents very easily without any errors.  

I hope someone else who needs this same functionality finds this post helpful.
