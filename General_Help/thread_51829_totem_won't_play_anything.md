### Post by damonw5 on 2005-07-25
[QUOTE=mr_bean]Anything I try to play with totem media player, avi, mp3, I get an error saying cannot play, no decoders found to handle it, or something like that. Shouldn't I be able to play mp3s in totem w/out installing anything?[/QUOTE]
 No. Because of patent issues, you must install "codecs" to play avi and mp3 files.

Here are step-by-step instructions:
[http://ubuntuguide.org/#extrarepositories](http://ubuntuguide.org/#extrarepositories)
and then
[http://ubuntuguide.org/#codecs](http://ubuntuguide.org/#codecs)

Also try installing "totem-xine" in Synaptic... many people think it's better than the default "totem-gstreamer".
