### Post by Bucky Ball on 2013-11-06
Good to hear. It's great. I have a laptop with a monitor on a shelf above it. Works a treat. You can even have separate wallpapers! ;)

Little tip: When you get it how you want it, go 'Layout>Save As' and save the layout. When you restart the machine, it is as simple as 'Layout>Open' which should open the folder you saved to. Double click the layout file, click the green tick, and you're there.
