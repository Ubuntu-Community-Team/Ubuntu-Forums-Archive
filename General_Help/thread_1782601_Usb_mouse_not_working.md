### Post by objetora on 2012-02-18
I have System 76 Starling 4 netbook. My Logitech optical cordless USB mouse stopped working with the USB receiver plugged into any of the three USB ports. I thought perhaps it was some upgrade problem, but using earlier kernels didn't help. I looked at dmesg (from terminal) and it wasn't recognizing my mouse.

My fix: I plug the little USB receiver only part-way in and it works fine now on any of my USB ports. I do carry the netbook around in a backpack a lot, so maybe the receiver got a little distorted. Just a guess.

Weird solution, but it worked for me so it's worth a try.
