Flash-player 11 beta now works in firefox 7 beta. I am not sure why it works.


I uninstalled nspluginwrapper, gnash, and libflashsupport for jack. 

After that I installed flashplugin64installer. 

I removed all libflash.so from all possible browser plugin folders. 

I opened up firefox used flashaid and installed the flash 11 beta.

Restarted firefox and it worked great. I really do not know what the fix was however, something above did the trick.
