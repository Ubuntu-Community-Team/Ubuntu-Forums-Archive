I created my xorg.conf in /etc/X11/ and have the following settings...

```
Section "Device"
    Identifier    "Configured Video Device"
EndSection

Section "Monitor"
    Identifier    "Configured Monitor"
            HorizSync    30-80
            VertRefresh    50-85
EndSection

Section "Screen"
    Identifier    "Default Screen"
    Monitor        "Configured Monitor"
    Device        "Configured Video Device"
    DefaultDepth    24
    SubSection "Display"
        Modes       "1024x768" "800x600" "640x480"
    EndSubSection
EndSection
```Anyone have any ideas as to why its not picking up the support for higher resolutions?
