### Post by Sand Lee on 2008-09-10
Actually I did figure out a partial solution. The mail you receive from your forwarded email address probably lists your main address in the recipients list. Make a filter that searches for your main address and moves all that mail to another folder and marks it as read. Or you could have the filter automatically delete it!
