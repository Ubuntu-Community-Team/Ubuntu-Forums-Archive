### Post by Mark Phelps on 2012-01-08
If you mean READ the files (as in music/picture/video files), then just look under /host -- that is where Wubi automatically mounts your Windows filesystem.

If you mean RUN your files -- NO.

I would advise strongly against CHANGING any of your Windows files as Win7 does not react well to filesystem changes from outside itself.
