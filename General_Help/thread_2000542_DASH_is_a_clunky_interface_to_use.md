### Post by deadflowr on 2012-06-09
Use the super-key, aka the windows key. It 's the default key bound to the dash, simply tap it and the dash will open.

Hold down the super-key to get a popup window with a shortcut listing.

### Post by deadflowr on 2012-06-09
You can always add applications to the side launcher(assuming your using unity) by either dragging the apps icon to the launcher or opening up the app and right-clicking on apps icon, newly added to the launcher, and selecting lock to launcher. Then if you want to move it to a new section of the launcher-dock, simply hold left-click down, pull it out and place it in the new place.
In the dash, at the bottom, you'll see several icons, the second icon is the apps icon. It will list recent used, all installed, and downloadable apps. Click on the Installed arrow and all installed apps will appear alphabetically.
