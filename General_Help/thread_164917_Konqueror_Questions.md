### Post by SpEcIeS on 2006-04-24
To deactivate the Side bar press F9. If it still appears upon reload..  Press F9 again then go to "Settings" -> "Save View Profile", which would probably say, "Kubuntu Web". This will perserve your Web settings for browsing.
