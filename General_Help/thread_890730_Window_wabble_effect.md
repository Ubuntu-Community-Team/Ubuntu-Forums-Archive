### Post by Genius314 on 2008-08-15
You need to enable Compiz-Fusion.
Go to System>Preferences>Appearance, go to the "Visual Effects" tab, and select either Normal or Extra (depending on your video card, it may or may not work).
I don't know if wobbly windows are used by default. If not, install CCSM, go to System>Preferences>CompizConfig Settings Manager, scroll down until you see the Wobbly Windows plugin, and check the box next to it.
