### Post by Lars Noodén on 2009-09-28
Looks like beagle got installed by accident.  You might try one of these instead:

[pinot](http://packages.ubuntu.com/jaunty/deskbar-plugins-pinot)
[strigi](http://packages.ubuntu.com/jaunty/deskbar-plugins-strigi)
