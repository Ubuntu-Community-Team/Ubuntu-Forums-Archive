### Post by approaching on 2007-12-17
Yeah, make a link to that folder. But to tell you exactly how to do it, it would be a little easier if you told me what the deal with your computer is. Is that windows install on this physical machine? That would be the easiest setup to manage. If the drive is automounted go to nautalous (Places>Home), and make sure you are in your home directory (/home/yourname). right click on the whitespace and select new > link (or possibly symbolic link, please forgive me i am not at home right now. A dialogue should pop up, then you enter in what you want to call it (documents), and where it is pointing to (probably something like /media/disk/Documents and Settings/yername/My Documents). 

I would then add that to your main bookmark folders by dragging that link to the side pane in the file browser, or fiddling with the menus. but no matter what you do once the link has been made, that is essentially that folder.
