I am making progress.  First, when you goto  [http://members.harmonyremote.com/Eas...asp?Start=True](http://members.harmonyremote.com/Eas...asp?Start=True) and press next you are told to log in.  You will also have another option.  If you have your remote, and its plugged in, you can tell the system and, in theory, it will read the stuff on the remote and you can go forward.  DO NOT DO THIS!  If you don't have an account then get one as the other option is a straight path to disaster.  

Once you get to the page where stuff gets changed also remember, there is a little thing, at the top of the screen to deal with the remote directly, all the rest of the stuff has to do with your devices and activities.  There is also help.  I think this system, with concordance and congruity, gets set with the site and then the information gets put into a file.  You will have an option to open or save the file created and, as far as I can tell, you should open the file and, again I think, it gets automatically saved.
