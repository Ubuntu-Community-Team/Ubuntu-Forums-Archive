### Post by DanneStrat on 2011-06-09
Hi.

> **Darris said:**
> every time I disconnect from power, the computer goes into suspend mode.

This has happened to me in 10.10 on my notebook.

Right after it happened, I had a look at the system logs and it did indeed 

do a full suspend of the computer when I pulled the plug.

The first thing I tried after it happened was to power down the laptop and 

remove the battery, then I re-inserted the battery and powered the system 

back up again. 

After this procedure everything was back to normal. After installing 11.04 

this has not happened even once.

I hope this will help you.
