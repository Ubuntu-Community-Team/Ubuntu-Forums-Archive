### Post by Hedgehog1 on 2011-04-28
HD Video uses a great deal of computer resources.  The issue is typically not the CPU as much as the whole I/O subsystem keeping up and the Video card getting fed fast enough to avoid dropping frames.

I sized my PC to accommodate this level of I/O; but at times when other background processes fire up I can still drop frames.
