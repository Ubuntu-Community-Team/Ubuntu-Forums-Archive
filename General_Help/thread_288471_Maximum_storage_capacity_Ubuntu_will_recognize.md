### Post by po0f on 2006-10-29
boon4376,

The question isn't "does Ubuntu have a limit" as much as it is a question of "does Linux have a limit".  And the answer is more dependent on which filesystem you choose to use.  Ext3 has an [eight TB maximum](http://lwn.net/Articles/187321/), and with certain patches, this limit can be increased to 1024 PB ([petabytes](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Petabyte)).
