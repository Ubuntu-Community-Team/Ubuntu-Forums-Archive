Hi, I was trying to change pointer speed yesterday in settings->Mouse&TouchPad, that worked but it somehow changed how tap to click worked. I can single click by tapping once, but double click is not working anymore. It's driving me crazy, as I can't get my work done. ---

### Post by anshole on 2014-04-10
Just solved the problem. 
For future reference: Settings->Mouse&TouchPad->set Double-click to full-ish Slow. Making it Fast apparently disables double click. 

Sorry for the thread, didn't understand the settings well.
