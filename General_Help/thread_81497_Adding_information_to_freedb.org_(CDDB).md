### Post by asimon on 2005-11-09
You have to use some program with CDDB support. For example the KDE CD-Player (KsCD) has a dialog to enter and upload the data. Grip, a CD ripping tool too.

For more apps see the [List of freedb-aware applications](http://www.freedb.org/freedb_aware_apps.php?query=1&submitfilter=supported).

PS: I you upload data, please ensure correct capitalisation. I hate it when people upload titles with all lower or all upper case. ;-)
