### Post by DaymItzJack on 2008-06-12
I'm not exactly sure what you mean by "jumps" up, but I think you're talking about the dodge effect.

Go to the Advanced Desktop Effects Settings, go down to "Animations", select the "Focus animation" tab, and my settings look like:

Focus effect: Dodge
Duration: 150
Windows matchs: (type=Normal | Dialog | ModalDialog | Utility | Unknown)

### Post by poo_22 on 2008-06-12
that's called the dodge effect.
in your compiz-config settings manadger (otherwise called the "advance desktop effects settings" reached by going to system ==> preferences)
enable animations. in the animations settings go to the focus tab, select the setting, click edit and change the effect to dodge. put something liek 400 for the duration.
