### Post by yancek on 2014-08-26
Either you haven't used a correct method to put a bootable version of windows on your flash drive or the changes in the BIOS aren't being saved, or maybe your computer is not capable of booting from a flash drive - although I expect that is not likely unless your computer is really old.

If you downloaded it from the official microsoft site, the instructions below should work.  If you got it some other way, google is your friend.

[http://www.microsoftstore.com/store/msusa/html/pbPage.Help_Win7_usbdvd_dwnTool](http://www.microsoftstore.com/store/msusa/html/pbPage.Help_Win7_usbdvd_dwnTool)
