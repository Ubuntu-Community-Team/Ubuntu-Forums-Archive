### Post by HereInOz on 2009-01-24
Firstly, check if your panels are set to auto-hide.  If they are, then remove this option.

I have found that if you full screen Firefox, and the panels are not on auto hide, it actually puts its title bar up under the top panel when you take it off full screen - like it goes fully maximised.  

The way I fixed it was to move the top panel to the side of the desktop, which allowed me to get at the control buttons to restore the Firefox window and resize it, then click File > Quit (don't use the X to close it) once you have the window back at the correct size, and it should come back to the same size when you restart it.

Hopefully this is the same as your problem, but if not, then I apologise for perhaps giving you false hope.

### Post by diablo75 on 2009-01-24
Hit F11 twice.  Then un-maximize Firefox, and resize it down to something that's just under "fullscreen", and then close it.  Wait a few seconds.

Upon re-opening it, it should open up at the size it was when you last closed it.
