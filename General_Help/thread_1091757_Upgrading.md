### Post by avtolle on 2009-03-09
I presume you mean 6.06, not 6.04; its release was delayed. There is a direct upgrade path between 6.06 and 8.04, as both are LTS (Long Term Support) releases. To upgrade to 8.10 from 6.06 requires a fresh install of 8.10 or a network upgrade from 8.04.
