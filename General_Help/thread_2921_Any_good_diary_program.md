### Post by cmpilato on 2008-05-19
I'm the original creator and one of two developers of ThotKeeper ([http://thotkeeper.org](http://thotkeeper.org)), so I'm disappointed to see that some folks have had problems getting it to work.  I can say though that I and my wife regularly and successfully use ThotKeeper (she on Windows, me on both Windows and Ubuntu Linux).  The datafile format is XML, and we version our shared journal in Subversion for safe keeping and access from multiple machines.  Our other active developer uses ThotKeeper primarily under Xandros, but occasionally on Ubuntu, too.

If you have problems with ThotKeeper, we'd love to hear about the details at thotkeeper-dev {at} googlegroups -dot- com.
