### Post by jrozo17 on 2011-06-04
Yes, there's a way to look at the categories. Unity isn't very efficient though for some. :P

Simply go to your dock, and right click on Applications(the magnifying glass with a plus sign) and it should have a list you can choose from.

I hope that helps!! Or, you can log out, click on your user, and choose the classic session and you will be back to gnome.
