### Post by rainwalker on 2008-06-23
Are you trying to get that panel at the bottom?
If so, just right-click on your bottom panel (the one with your window list, workspace switcher, etc) and choose Properties. In that window you can change all the size and color settings for the panel. 
For all of the stuff in the panel, right-click on it and choose Add To Panel. In that window you can choose from a bunch of different applets to add to the panel or add launchers for programs.

### Post by PeidGnourt on 2008-06-23
It is so easy, you just choose click right mouse on the panel, choose Properties ---> Background ---> Solid Colour and get the bar in Style to your left hand, back to General, you choose "Show hide Button".
After that, you can go to Right click ---> Add to Panel and choose what you want to have on the dock. Have fun!^^
If you want to add a shortcut to your program, on the default shortcut in panel, eg: Firefox, just right click and choose: Add this launcher to panel.

### Post by Forlong on 2008-06-24
For the sake of clarity: the desktop environment used in this screenshot is KDE.

You are most probably running GNOME atm, but you can install KDE by simply running
```
sudo apt-get install kubuntu-desktop
``` in a terminal.
Next time you log in, you can choose between GNOME and KDE.
