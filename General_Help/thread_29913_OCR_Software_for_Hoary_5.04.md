### Post by cillian on 2006-05-02
A dodgy corel pdf landed in my inbox to convert it to text and nothing was working. Copying and pasting the text left behind blank spaces as did pdftotext, pdftohtml got the images correctly but also ditched the text. The gimp couldn't read the file correctly either but evince could so eventually I figured out I could take a screenshot with the gimp and export it as a pgm and then use gocr to extract the text. 

It's impressive that evince could open it without any trouble. I guess google uses pdftohtml as it also failed to handle it.
