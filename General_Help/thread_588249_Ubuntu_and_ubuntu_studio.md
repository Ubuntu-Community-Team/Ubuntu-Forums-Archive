### Post by dayvy on 2007-10-23
UbuntuStudio comes with a low latency kernel which is needed to do any serious audio work (i.e. recording). I suspect it's also tweaked in other ways for multimedia work.

### Post by xhilyn on 2007-10-23
UbuntuStudio also comes with a large selection of Audio Applications like Ardour and Rosegarden, as well as some video Programs and Blender, which is for 3D modeling. But UbuntuStudio does not come with the full OpenOffice or any of the little games that come with the normal Ubuntu. So you gain a lot but loose a bit
