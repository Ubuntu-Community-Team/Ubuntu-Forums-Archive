### Post by 23meg on 2007-01-21
> None of my programs will minimize to the task bar on the bottom of the desktop anymore. They now minimize to the workspace and I have to alt+tab to get them back.
You may have removed the Window List applet. Right click empty space on your panel, choose "Add to panel" and then "Window List" to add it back.
> 
Also, programs that normally minimize to the system tray (where the clock is) arent doing that anymore either.
You probably removed the notification area from your panel accidentally. Right click empty space on the panel, hit "Add to panel" and choose "Notification Area" to add it back.
