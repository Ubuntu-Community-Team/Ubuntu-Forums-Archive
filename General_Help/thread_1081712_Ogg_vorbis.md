### Post by mb_webguy on 2009-02-26
As to the first part... I don't know if it's the best way, but Audacity can convert an mp3 to an ogg.  However, no matter what you use, you're going to lose some sound quality.  Both mp3 and vorbis (ogg is acutally the container, not the codec) are lossy formats.  Converting from one to the other is always going to involve some loss of information.
