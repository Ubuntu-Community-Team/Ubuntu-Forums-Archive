> How do I make Gnome MPlayer my default player rather than Movie Player?

System > Preferrences > Preferred applications > Multimedia tab > Dropdown, pick it or if its not listed, give the command for the app.

Can be done on an individiual mime type (i.e. extension basis) by right clicking a video or music file and > properties > Open with > select app.
