### Post by HermanAB on 2018-09-23
It depends on how you installed your system.  If the /home is a separate partition (as opposed to a directory on /), then you can always re-install without formatting and losing your data.  Many/most Linux distributions do not do this by default, you have to select the disk partitioning schema during installation.

So, all you can do now, is to backup your data before you re-install.
