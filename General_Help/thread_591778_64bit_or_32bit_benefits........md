### Post by hikaricore on 2007-10-25
Most programs, if not availible in debian packages, "should" be complileable for 64bit.  However you will probably have to compile many things yourself.

Nvidia drivers have a 64bit version, which can be downloaded from the *nix drivers section on their website.
I don't imagine you'll be seeing much difference aside from the complications of compiling things and using work arounds for things such as flash which do not have a 64bit version.

As usual unless you need 64bit for some reason I'd suggest keeping 32bit until the software world catches up a bit more with the hardware world.  ^_^
