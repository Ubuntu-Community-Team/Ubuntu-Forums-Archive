### Post by swoody on 2008-11-24
I would agree with the others. The best thing you can do right now would be to learn as much as you can in your free time to learn programming on your own. Concentrate and do very well in any math/science classes you take in High School, as this will help you out greatly in college. The best way for you to learn right now would be to visit your library - I never realized how many programming books they have!! If you don't like to read... learn to :) and then get your hands on as many books as you can, and as many websites that you can, and you should be able to breeze through a computer degree!! I'd also not reccomend to spend TOO much time mastering one language, but to get a good grasp of as many as you can, because by the time you graduate college, what languages are popular today might not be in a few years.
