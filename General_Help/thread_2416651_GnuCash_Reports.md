### Post by rbmorse on 2019-04-13
Your best source for help with Gnucash is the Gnucash user mailing list. Much of the traffic on that list pertains to getting the reports function to behave as the user desired. The report function is very flexible and powerful, but like most things that are flexible and powerful it requires some study and effort to beat into submission. 

Here's how to connect with the list: 

```
gnucash-user mailing list
[EMAIL="gnucash-user@gnucash.org"]gnucash-user@gnucash.org[/EMAIL]
To update your subscription preferences or to unsubscribe:
[https://lists.gnucash.org/mailman/listinfo/gnucash-user](https://lists.gnucash.org/mailman/listinfo/gnucash-user)
If you are using Nabble or Gmane, please see [https://wiki.gnucash.org/wiki/Mailing_Lists](https://wiki.gnucash.org/wiki/Mailing_Lists) for more information.
```
