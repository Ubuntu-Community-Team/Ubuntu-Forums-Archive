### Post by alzie on 2008-12-04
It sounds like you have flashblock installed.

Look in Tools>Add-Ons>Extensions

Personally I run flashblock to avoid all those annoying flash ads,  I just use the preferences to allow flash from sites I trust.

I hope this helps
