### Post by oddin85 on 2007-08-29
i did an advanced google search for psp icon filetype:png and found one that i liked with nice transparency, then when the PSP is plugged in, just right click the icon and click on properties, now just select the new icon and click close

now every time u plug it in, you'll get your custom icon :)
