### Post by coldraven on 2013-09-05
You could try this:
press F11 to go fullscreen,
press the Ctrl and the + key to make the font bigger (Ctrl and the - key to makes the font smaller)
Then quit by pressing Alt and F4.
Open Firefox again and see if it remembers the last settings.
