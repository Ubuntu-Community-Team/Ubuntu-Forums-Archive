### Post by pkolloch on 2008-04-20
I know that this posting is quite old and I am not using the ubuntu version that you were using. Still, the problem stayed probably the same.

You probably just needed to install a software midi synthesizer like timidity (with the proposed sound data files)... At least that helped me out there. After that you go to "settings/midi setup" and select the first timidity channel. After that I heard some sound.

I heard that their are some freely available (though probably not really free) sound data files which timidity sound even better...
