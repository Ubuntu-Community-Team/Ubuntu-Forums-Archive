### Post by will103 on 2008-03-19
Hi,

Don't worry. I am quite sure that your data is ok. Its just that the system is now unbootable from the internal hard drive. Download and burn a livecd distribution (Knoppix is good for this) and boot from it. You should then be able to 'see' your internal hard drive and verify that your data is ok. If the data is fine get a copy of it (on a USB pen drive or external hard drive). Then probably best to do a fresh Ubuntu install.

If however you cannot 'see' your internal drive when booting from the LiveCD get back in contact with me.

Best of luck

will103
