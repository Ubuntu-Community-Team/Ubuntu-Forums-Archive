Welcome to Ubuntu.

To get the menu bar back on the top panel again, do this:
right-click the panel on an empty spot.
Choose add to panel.
Choose menu bar and click on add
Maybe you have to move it to the right position.
To do so right-click it and choose move. Move to the left and click the left mouse button.
