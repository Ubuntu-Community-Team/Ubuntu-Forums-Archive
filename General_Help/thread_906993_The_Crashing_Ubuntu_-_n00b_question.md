### Post by tamoneya on 2008-09-01
okay well its good that nothing is broken.  Now its more a question of security than usability.  When you attempted firestarter last time how did you do it.  Did you have a guide you followed.  Did you just give it a try and see what happened?  Did a friend give you some instructions?

Also how is your network setup.  You should note that almost all routers have built in hardware firewalls and this may make your firestarter firewall rather redundant and it may not be worth the trouble.  It isnt that I dont recommend a good security policy but on the assumption that there isnt anything super sensitive on your computer i think the redundant firewall is not necessary.
