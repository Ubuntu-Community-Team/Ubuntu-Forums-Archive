### Post by shyam5 on 2014-10-02
Hello all

I am using Ubuntu on a Dell Precision T1650 workstation. I have a problem that crops up once in a while, when the OS turns super responsive to mouse clicks. Suppose I click on the calendar to move from month August to September, the calendar shifts to November with a single click. My one click registers as multiple clicks in the OS. Similarly when I try to open a menu, the menu opens and closes rapidly. 

This problem sometimes disappears and things become normal in every few months, only to recur again. Not sure if this happens especially close to April and October.

Is there a solution to this? What dependencies could result in this?

### Post by Impavidus on 2014-10-02
The last time I had that problem it was because of a bad mouse. Sometimes it clicked double, sometimes it didn't click at all. I spent €10 on a new mouse and the problem was solved.

### Post by mcduck on 2014-10-02
Yeah, I'd place my bets on the mouse itself as well.

My current mouse (Logitech Anywhere MX) is known for such issues, something related to static charge bulding up in the switches, and switching the mouse off and clicking/holding the buttons for a while always fixes the problem for a while... (Simply normal wear in the switches could also make them more prone to register multiple clicks instead of just one).
