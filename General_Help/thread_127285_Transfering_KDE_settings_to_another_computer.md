### Post by MartinG on 2006-02-08
Copy the hidden directory /home/<username>/.kde onto the new machine to replace the one generated by the installer, but be wary because if the new machine is in any way differently set up you may have one or two anomalies.  It's a very good idea to copy the directory generated by the installer as /home/<username>/.kde_install, in case of trouble.
