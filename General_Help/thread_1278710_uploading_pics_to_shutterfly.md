### Post by bobterri on 2009-11-02
If you're running ubuntu you have nautilus.  Just go up to Places and then select home.  That will open nautilus.  A temp file is one that I created on my Desktop.  Right click anywhere on an open area on your Desktop and selection Create Folder.  That will place a folder on your Desktop named untitled folder.
