### Post by ppirilla on 2009-09-01
unfortunately, you're most likely out of luck.  The companies making these devices don't think there is enough demand for them to make linux drivers, and they go to lengths so that 3rd parties (like the open source community) can't make their own software for windows.

All I can suggest is to do what I do: write to the manufacturer and request that they release linux-compatability software for your particular phone.
