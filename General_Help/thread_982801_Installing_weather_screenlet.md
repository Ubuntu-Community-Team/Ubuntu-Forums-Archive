### Post by razy60 on 2008-11-15
just done this for Cairo dock.
Go to, [http://www.weather.com/](http://www.weather.com/)
Then find your country, then nearest city/large town to you.

Right click on the city/town you have chosen, choose properties, at the top of the window you will see an address, at the end of that you will see a code something like UKXX0075.(copy this code, then close element weather)

right click or open your weather applet go to configure insert that code into the relevant box.

now when you go to weather and search for location you can put in your town,city name(dont type in the country as it wont work),
 it will then give you a drop down box choose your town/city, it should then show the weather for your area. 
it may take a few minuets to collect info, if not close applet then reopen it should work.

Hope it helps
