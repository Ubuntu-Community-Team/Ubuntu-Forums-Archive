### Post by welshmike on 2010-09-03
I see that you have reinstalled Ubuntu to no avail.
It is possible that the media you installed from was incomplete or corrupt.
Maybe you can end up with a working system by proceeding as follows:
Boot from your current live CD and save anything important to you, saving /home and and other directory contents you need to save to an external hard drive or similar media. 
Download a fresh ISO of the Live CD.
If your PC hard drive contains only Ubuntu you may wish to reformat the whole drive and then install a fresh copy of Ubuntu from the new live CD.
