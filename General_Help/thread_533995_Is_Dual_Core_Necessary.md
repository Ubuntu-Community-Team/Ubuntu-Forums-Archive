### Post by apetresc on 2007-08-24
> **dcast said:**
> Hi, I am thinking of getting a new computer, probably a laptop, Just wondered if getting dual core is really necessary. I am currently running 6.06 on a P3 550mhz with 256 megs quite happily. Should I spring for dual core or not?

Dual core processors perform very well, but if you're accustomed to a 550mhz machine, ANY modern machine will be a HUGE improvement for you. Unless you do a lot of hardcore gaming or serious video processing or otherwise intensive tasks, you do not necessarily need to pay for a dual core. Especially not if most of what you do on the computer can be summarized as "reading and writing various forms of text" (most people).

That said, they're very good processors and you'll definitely be future-proofing yourself for a long time coming. :)
