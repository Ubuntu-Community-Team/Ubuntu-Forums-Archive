### Post by kenl100 on 2007-03-11
For those of you trying to do the same thing, I think I answered my own question...  In NoMachine's NX client window:

Go to the first tab, "General"

In the Desktop area, choose "Custom"

In the Settings choose "Run the following command"

enter "kontact" or whatever script/application you would like to run.

Save the configuration.

Then start the NX Client.  From my initial testing, the window is pretty snappy and works well.  I'll provide more feedback as I go.
