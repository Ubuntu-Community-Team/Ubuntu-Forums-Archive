### Post by supercheetah on 2008-12-01
What are you using to connect to their cell network?  A PC Card?  A lot of their PC Cards have unofficial Linux support from the manufacturers or some places in the OSS community.

I'm using a GT Ultra Express, and I first had to use [HSO Connect](http://www.pharscape.org/OptionSoftware.html) to get them to work, but it seems to be supported now in 8.10.

I haven't been able to figure out how to log into their WiFi Hotspots that are supposedly supposed to be free to us, but I'll be asking around.  Apparently that requires the AT&T Communication Manager, but they don't have a version for Linux.

We're not the only people that have had that trouble with Hotspots though.  See [here](http://forums.wireless.att.com/cng/board/message?board.id=pccards&thread.id=10057&view=by_date_ascending&page=1).  Yes, that's me at the last post.
