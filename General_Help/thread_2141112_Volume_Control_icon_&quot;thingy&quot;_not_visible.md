### Post by Dennis N on 2013-05-01
With Lubuntu 13.04 on old Dell Laptop:

Here's what I have:

Adjusting brightness with Fn+Up-arrow Fn+Dn-Arrow brings up the brightness-level indicator. It times out in about 5 seconds if no further up/down keypresses.

Left-click on volume icon bring up the dialog with slider for adjusting volume level, or muting. You can also adjust volume with mouse wheel while cursor is over volume icon - with this there is no popup level indicator, but level shows in tool tip (0-100). 

That's it.
