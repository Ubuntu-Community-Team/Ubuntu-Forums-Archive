### Post by 3pinner on 2010-12-20
I am trying to print a page from a workbook that has data on it, but keep getting the error 
"There is nothing to print. The selected print range or sheet is empty"

Any suggestions on what to look for?

Thank you!

---

### Post by TeoBigusGeekus on 2010-12-20
Format>Print Ranges>Remove
Perhaps you accidentally inserted a print range pointing to empty cells.

---

### Post by 3pinner on 2010-12-20
Thank you!
That was it. When I opened that dialog, there was no selection at all for the print range. setting it to 'entire page' solved the problem.
Thanks
