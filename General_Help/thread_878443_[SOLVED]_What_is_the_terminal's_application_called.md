### Post by mb_webguy on 2008-08-03
Do you not have Terminal in Applications->Accessories?  If so, you can just right-click on the panel, select "Add to Panel", then "Application Launcher", and select "Terminal" from the list of programs.  No need to make a launcher from scratch...
