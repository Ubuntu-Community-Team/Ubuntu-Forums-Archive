### Post by ReaderRat on 2006-11-11
Welcome to Ubuntu Forums, oystersuck.
You cannot run (K)(X)Ubuntu Live CDs with 127 Megs RAM. You need at least 192 Megs for an install.
There are other distros that need less RAM (In fact, very little RAM). Some of these are -->>  [**Damn Small Linux**](http://www.damnsmalllinux.org/download.html) and  [**Puppy Linux**](http://www.puppyos.com/download/downpage.htm)
Good luck whatever you do....ReaderRat
