### Post by fragos on 2007-01-25
I'd check out "ffmpeg" a powerful command line conversion tool.  I use it to convert .flv videos to mpeg.  Flv is also a flash video format which is popular on sites like youtube.  I haven't tried to convert .swf with it.  The mpegs I created from .flv videos work well in Impress.  If ffmpeg doesn't do it for you there may be another conversion utility that will.
