### Post by benpack101 on 2011-06-20
I asked around the LibreOffice/Openoffice community and I've gotten that you cannot do it (its a known defect)

Two work arounds include:

Make the axis title yourself using the drawing text box ("T" from the Drawing toolbar).

Use superscript numerals (you must have a font that provides them)
