### Post by Lupi on 2009-05-05
SOLVED. I went to SYSTEM > ADMINISTRATION > PRINTING. I Right-clicked on my printer, chose "Properties". Once the window opened, I went to "Settings" and clicked on "Change" for the Make and Model. HP was already selected, so I clicked on FORWARD and instead of choosing the recommended driver (HP LaserJet 4000 Series Postscript [en]) I chose HP LaserJet 4000 Series hpijs [en]. 


I guess it was a matter of drivers.
