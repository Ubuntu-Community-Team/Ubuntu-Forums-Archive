### Post by cwillu on 2007-08-19
> **superyounan1 said:**
> I have ubuntu fiesty but also installed KDE. Using gnome, I notice my computer gets slow and sluggish after just a few hours of use, I have to kill X or restart for better performance. But with KDE I can run smoothly for as long as I want - what gives??

In all likelyhood, there's something you have installed in gnome that isn't installed in kde that's causing problems.  Applets are the prime suspect: old versions of deskbar and weather have had issues with this, for example.

Open a terminal, run 'top', and then use the computer until it gets sluggish.  Then, in the terminal, see if anything is constantly using a significant amount of cpu (>30%), and then hit 'shift-F' and then 'n', and see if anything is using an obscene amount of memory (%MEM, RES and VIRT columns) other than firefox.
