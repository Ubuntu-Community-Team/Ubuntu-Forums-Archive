### Post by GeorgeVita on 2010-02-15
Hi **Angel_Caido**,
all 3g modems have a minimum compatibility to ETS07.07 ans ETS07.05 GSM standards (using AT comands) but all manufacturers use proprietary commands for their 'special' functions ('3G preferred' supposed to be 'special'!).

As this kind of s/w must be difficult to find, you could try to use us (owners of 3g modems) to do the tests for you. The only we need is to have a specific & strictly procedure to use, ex. download xyz package, setup with abc configuration, try 1-2-3 tests (no warranty for results or respond time). Additionally you could buy some used modems or a prepaid account from a local provider.
