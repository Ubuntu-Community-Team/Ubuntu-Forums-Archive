### Post by bigseb on 2010-05-31
I'm running Karmic x64. I have disks that I borrowed from someone with Ubuntu 10.04 x86 and x64 and Kubuntu 10.04 x86 and x64. How do I make copies of these disks that are bootable. I tried with Brasero but although the files were on the disk I could not boot from them.

---

### Post by Ozymandias_117 on 2010-05-31
Do a 1:1 Disc Copy with Brasero. You can either save them as a .iso to then burn later, or burn them right then. (To correctly burn them, right click on the .iso and select "Burn to Disk")

If you've already done this, ensure that the disks you're creating from aren't faulty.
