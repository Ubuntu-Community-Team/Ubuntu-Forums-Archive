### Post by rbmorse on 2007-07-22
Spamassassin works, but it has to be trained to recongize what you think is spam and what you don't think is spam.  Frankly, it's not a good tool on the desktop..  Some folks think Bogofilter, which also needs training, may be a better fit.  Like spamassissin, check the Evolution plugins to ensure it is enabled. 

note:  I've noticed that Evolution seems to crash more than usual when both the spamassissin and bogofilter plugins are enabled. I can't tell if that is  congenital or just a local mistake. 

note: Feisty has Evolution 2.10.1.  Gutsy has 2.11.2 (or maybe even 2.12 by now, I haven't checked) and my experience is that spam filtering w/ bogofilter works much better in the later release.  Unfortunately, there are dependency problems that keep Evolution 2.11 from building on Feisty, and trying to resolve those results in broken Feisty instead.
