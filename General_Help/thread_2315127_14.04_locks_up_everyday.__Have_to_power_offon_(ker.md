### Post by ian-weisser on 2016-02-26
Two things to try:

1) Use cron to output your memory ('free') and temperatures ('sensors') to a logfile every few minutes. The last entry in the log will tell you if you are out of memory/swap or have a thermal issue.

2) Boot from a LiveUSB and run Memtest overnight. You might have a bad RAM stick.
