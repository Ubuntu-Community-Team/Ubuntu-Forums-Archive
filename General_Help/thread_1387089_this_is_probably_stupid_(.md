### Post by stomakmonkee on 2010-01-21
No stupid questions, that what a community is about.  Everybody starts somewhere.
Anything stored on your Windows partition, or any NTFS partition, should be able to be accessed by Ubuntu as well (as Linux has abilities to read NTFS).  If you look in your "Places" it should say something like "XX GB" File System.  That is your NTFS.  If you click, it will ask you to authenticate and when you do, it will mount your NTFS partition.
Hope this helps.
