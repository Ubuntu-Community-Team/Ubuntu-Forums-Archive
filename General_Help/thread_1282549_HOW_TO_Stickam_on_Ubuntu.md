### Post by beastrace91 on 2009-10-04
This one is pretty simply really, it is just not very straight forward on how you do it.

Step 1)
Install flash, note this will only work with the non-free flashplugin from Adobe.

Step 2)
Go to stick am and log in to your account, then open the player Window.

Step 3)
Go to - [http://www.macromedia.com/support/documentation/en/flashplayer/help/settings_manager06.html](http://www.macromedia.com/support/documentation/en/flashplayer/help/settings_manager06.html)

And set player.stickam.com to be "always allowed" to access your webcam/microphone - works like a charm.
