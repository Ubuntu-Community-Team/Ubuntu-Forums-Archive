Peter-

The problem is the Ethernet drivers 11.10 and 10.04 uses are the wrong ones.  It defaults to the RTL 8619 driver, which sometimes may or may not work.
You need the RTL 8618 Driver to work the onboard ethernet card.
I have the same problem with my Z68 Gigabyte mobo.
I just went back to 11.04 because I can't compile the driver in 11.10, it just won't make the module.

see my rant here:  

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1863793](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1863793)

and driver here: 

[http://www.rvdavid.net/how-to-get-gigabit-speeds-from-rtl81118168b-pci-express-gigabit-ethernet-controller-on-ubuntu-linux/](http://www.rvdavid.net/how-to-get-gigabit-speeds-from-rtl81118168b-pci-express-gigabit-ethernet-controller-on-ubuntu-linux/)

Best of luck, Tony
