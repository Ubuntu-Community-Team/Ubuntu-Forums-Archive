If you didn't know:

[LIST]
[*]**Fix Commited** means that the community has already submitted a fix for that bug. So, practically, the bug is fixed. But it's not still on Ubuntu's repositories...
[*]**Fix Released** is the status for a fix that has been submitted on the Ubuntu repos. So, for applying it, you just have to go to Updates Manager and you'll see an update for LibreOffice (e.g).
[/LIST]
