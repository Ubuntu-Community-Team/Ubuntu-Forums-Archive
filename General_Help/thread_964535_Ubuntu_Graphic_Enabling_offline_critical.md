### Post by 3rdalbum on 2008-10-31
Intel's 3D should be enabled by default. A handful of ATI integrated graphics should be fully enabled by default too, if my understanding is correct.

For all other ATI and Nvidia, you need the self-contained driver installer and the kernel headers for your particular kernel version, plus "build-essential"; or you need the "linux-restricted-modules" package for your kernel. Unfortunately, it's not likely that you will find either linux-restricted-modules or build-essential on any PC magazine coverdiscs.

Your best bet is probably to use your own internet connection to download linux-restricted-modules from [http://packages.ubuntu.com](http://packages.ubuntu.com), and then distribute this package to your friends.
