### Post by I3aH on 2008-05-10
I only know in gnome. You can right click on the top of the bar next to Applications, Places and System. Make sure it's the blank part and click on **Add to panel**. Select **network monitor**

It should come up on the bar and then you can set it up by right clicking the Network monitor icon and clicking on Properties. Select your network, mines wan0 since it's wireless, the others i think are eth0.
