### Post by logos34 on 2009-03-14
just don't use one of those cheap patches--I made that mistake long time ago and it never worked properly...It glued itself to the cpu and I had to practically use a chistle to get it off!  The paste--low-end paste is fine unless you're a gamer or video-editor in which case maybe arctic silver would be a good idea--goes on and cleans off sooo much easier.  Just remember not to put too much, which can be as bad as too little or none (excessive amounts creates a "blanket" effect trapping the heat in rather than radiating it out to the heatsink)
