### Post by Prodromoi on 2008-01-15
Right click on the desktop and select the "create launcher" option.
At the very top of the pop-up window is the "Type" option with a drop-down menu; the second option on that menu is "Application in Terminal", which I suspect is what you need.

Good luck.
