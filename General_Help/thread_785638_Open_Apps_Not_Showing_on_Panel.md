### Post by boneman1 on 2008-05-07
I'm running 8.04 and I've done something that has caused my open apps to quit showing up on the bottom panel.  If I minimize an app the only way I can get back to it is alt-tab until I get to it.  I'd like to have the icons back on the bottom panel so I can see what I have open.  Any ideas on how to get things back to normal?  Thanks in advance.

### Post by boneman1 on 2008-05-07
I was able to find a fix for my problem.  I had to go into 'Add to Panel' and select 'Window List'.  Now my running apps are back.
