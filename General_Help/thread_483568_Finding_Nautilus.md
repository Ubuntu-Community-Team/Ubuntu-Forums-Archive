### Post by steveneddy on 2007-06-24
Nautilus is the equivalent of Explorer in Windows. If you look at you /Home folder, the window that open up and shows you the contents of /home is Nautilus.

You can type

```
nautilus
``` 

in a terminal and get your /home folder opened in Nautilus.
