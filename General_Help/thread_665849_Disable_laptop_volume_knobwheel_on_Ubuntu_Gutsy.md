### Post by erpo on 2008-01-13
If it's a hardware knob, you may be out of luck. If it's a software knob that pretends to be part of a multimedia keyboard, try Ubuntu menu->System->Preferences->Keyboard Shortcuts, and disable the volume up and volume down shortcuts.

---

### Post by skinind95 on 2008-01-13
erpo - I could have sworn i've tried that at least twice before, but this time it worked.  Under "Sound" in Keyboard Shortcuts i selected Volume Up and Down and pressed backspace.  The wheel is now completely disabled.  To return it's function you would select it in Keyboard Shortcuts and scroll the wheel "Up" while editing the Volume Up shortcut, and the same, but opposite, for down.  \
