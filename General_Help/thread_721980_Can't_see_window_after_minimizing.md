### Post by forrestcupp on 2008-03-12
You need to make sure you have the Launcher/Taskmanager applet in your AWN dock.  

Some apps show a separate instance for each open window, and other apps minimize to the launcher.  It all depends on whether the name in the title bar is the same as the launcher's name or not.  But if you don't have that applet, you won't see anything anyway.
