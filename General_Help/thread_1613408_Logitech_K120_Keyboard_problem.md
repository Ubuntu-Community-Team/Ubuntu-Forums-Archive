### Post by fragos on 2010-11-04
Here's one possibility. Keyboard-> Keyboard-> Mouse Keys Tab. Make sure it's not checked.

---

### Post by easy_target on 2010-11-05
Thank you for your replies. The keyboard didn't come with any CDs. Unchecking the box "Pointer can be controlled using the keypad" in System>Preferences>Keyboard>Mouse Keys tab worked for me.
Thanks!:)
