### Post by fragos on 2007-03-23
Proportional spacing between characters differs between fonts.  DejaVu fonts offere regular and Condensed versions.  Spacing on the Condensed is much less and you may find it better for your purposes.  I even used DejaVu Sans Condensed on my desktop and like it very much.  Install package ttf-dejavu from the Ubuntu repositories.  Synaptic can tell you if it's already installed and provide installation.
