### Post by johnsmoke on 2011-09-16
Ok, I've actually figured this out! I used this link to figure out how to clear the recent documents - [http://ubuntuguide.net/clearprevent-recent-documents-under-files-folders-in-ubuntu-unity](http://ubuntuguide.net/clearprevent-recent-documents-under-files-folders-in-ubuntu-unity)

And then I used Activity Log Manager and just disabled Logging. So the recent items are cleared and nothing is being added!
