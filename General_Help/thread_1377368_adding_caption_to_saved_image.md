### Post by ron999 on 2010-01-10
Hi
You open your image with Gimp.
Look on the 'Toolbox' panel. There is a capital letter A. That's called the 'Text tool', click on it.
Then move your cursor anywhere on the image and click the mouse.
A box opens up to type in your caption, then close it afterwards.
Then click on the 'Text tool' A again to disable it.
Now click on the fancy + sign on the toolbox panel, that's called the 'Move tool'. Use that to drag your caption to the required position.
Then when you File > Save you select the 'Merge Visible Layers' option.

That's how I go about it anyway, hope it helps.
Perhaps there are other tweaks also to alter the colour, font and size of the caption.
