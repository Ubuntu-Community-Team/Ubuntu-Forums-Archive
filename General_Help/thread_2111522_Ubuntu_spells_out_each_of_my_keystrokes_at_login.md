### Post by sudodus on 2013-02-02
I don't understand what you mean by spell out, it seems to be the opposite of what it should be :confused:

When you type the password, you should not see the characters typed: instead either nothing or stars or dots.

When you type in a word processor , you should see what you type (at least letters, and you can set the word processor to show special signs also for whitespace characters).

These things should act similar as in other operating systems and desktop environments.

### Post by Laobi on 2013-02-02
Do you mean it speaks what you type?

If that is the problem then check if the Screen Reader is turned on: at the login screen click on the icon in top bar that shows a man in a circle, then make sure the Screen Reader option in the menu that shows up is not checked. Or you can simply pres CTRL+S to togle it on/off.
