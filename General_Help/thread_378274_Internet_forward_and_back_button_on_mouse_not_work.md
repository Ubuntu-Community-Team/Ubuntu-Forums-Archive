### Post by Spr0k3t on 2007-03-07
Quick Fix for Firefox: Install Mouse Gestures (it's an addon)

Long Term Solution for use with Nautilus and Konqerer: Modify the /etc/X11/xorg.conf for your mouse.  You will need to know what button numbers are which and make sure to backup your xorg.conf before editing it.
