### Post by gettinoriginal on 2009-01-02
If you pass your cursor over the menu, does it show even temporarily ??  It can be the font color of your theme is the same as your title bar, or you can try this:
System > Pref > Appearance > Fonts set rendering to "subpixel smoothing" then go to details set smoothing to "subpixel" and hinting to full.
