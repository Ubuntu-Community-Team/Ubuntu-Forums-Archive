I use a service called Ninite when, I do a reinstall of my Windows7 Operating System or when I am setting up a machine for somebody. The service lets you pick popular apps and then lets you download a installer that will install those apps for you. It can also be used to keep those apps current. They now have a Linux version of the service... Some of you might find this useful. Here is the Link.....
