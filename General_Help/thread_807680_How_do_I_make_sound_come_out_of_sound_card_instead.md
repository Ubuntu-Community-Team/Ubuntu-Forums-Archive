I recently installed Hardy and my sound no longer works.  However, when I plug my speakers into my on board card I do hear sound.  How do I switch the card being utilized?  Thanks.

---

### Post by todak on 2008-05-26
Boot into your BIOS and disable the onboard sound device. From what you say you are trying to have two sound devices compete for resources. Disabling the onboard sound will load up the driver for your add-on sound device on next reboot.
