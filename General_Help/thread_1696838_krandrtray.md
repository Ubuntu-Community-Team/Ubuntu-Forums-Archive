### Post by Zorael on 2011-03-01
A long time ago there were talk of replacing krandr with a plasmoid. I'm not sure what happened to that.

One solution is to add a small panel (non-maximized) that you place somewhere out of the way of your dock - like on the left or right side of your screen - and set it to hide automatically. In it you only place a system tray widget, and keep using krandr through there. Then you can mouseover where it is when you need to access your trayed icons.
