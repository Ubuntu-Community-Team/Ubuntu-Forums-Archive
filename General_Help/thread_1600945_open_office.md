### Post by blueridgedog on 2010-10-19
From this site:

[http://www.helium.com/items/785844-comparing-open-office-calc-and-microsoft-excel](http://www.helium.com/items/785844-comparing-open-office-calc-and-microsoft-excel)

"in an Excel If statement the format is " =if(A1=10,1,0)" meaning if cell A1 equals 10 then put 1 in the target cell, otherwise put a zero in the target cell.
In Calc the same formula is written "=if(A1=10;1;0)" The difference is slight, but significant. The use of a semi-colon in the place of a comma might be confusing for heavy excel users"

I did not find a way to convert this automatically in my quick google search.
