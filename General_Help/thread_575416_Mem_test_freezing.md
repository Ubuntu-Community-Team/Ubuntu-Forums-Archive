### Post by PmDematagoda on 2007-10-14
Use a soft, clean and dry cloth and clean along the gold contacts of the memory modules, do it very carefully and do not damage the gold contacts whatsoever, then blow some dry, clean air softly to the slots in such a way that any dust is cleared out of there, then replace the RAM modules and see if you get an improvement.
