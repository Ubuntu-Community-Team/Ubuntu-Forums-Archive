### Post by ridgeland on 2010-10-06
Did you try [Ctrl]+[Alt]+[F1]?
[Ctrl]+[Alt]+[F7] is the default, where the other person connected to via SSH.
Use F1 to login and start a new session.  Toggle to F7 when needed like multiple workspaces.
