### Post by NoMoreWindoze on 2007-07-20
I am quite new to Ubuntu and I would like to find out about a window that I get from times to times.

The window comes up with "**Another user is trying to view your desktop. A user on the computer '(some sort of IP here)' is trying to remotely view or control your desktop**", There is the option to Refuse of course, but, could you please tell me why is this happening and if I can prevent this from happening somehow? I am not using any extra firewall or anti virus, 

Thank you in advance.

---

### Post by az on 2007-07-20
Go to System - Preferences - Remote Desktop and un-tick the "Allow other users to view your desktop" box.  That is off by default - you must have enabled it by accident.

### Post by NoMoreWindoze on 2007-07-21
Thank you... I had no idea it was enabled.. it's solved now :)
