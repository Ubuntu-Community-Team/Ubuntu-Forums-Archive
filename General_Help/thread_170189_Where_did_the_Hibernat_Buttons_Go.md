### Post by beniji on 2006-05-29
Under Dapper KDE, you right click the battery icon in the task tray and select "hibernate". If this option is not there, check your ACPI settings.

Unlike Dapper Gnome, the hibernate option is not on the main apps menu by default (at least on my Tecra M5 laptop).
