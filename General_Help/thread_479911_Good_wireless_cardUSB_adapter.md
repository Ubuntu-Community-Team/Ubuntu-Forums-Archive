### Post by Cogman on 2007-06-20
Netgear makes some of the best wireless stuff. USB would work just as well (should be no speed penalties) but be warned that in general USB has a bit more of a problem with wireless connectivity.

I would stay away from Pre-N Draft-N or any other-N based card, It is better to just wait for the real N standard to be made. A G standard card should do you well. If I was going to rank the companies, I would go something like

Netgear
Linksys
Dlink
Generic (eveyone else)

As far as quality goes, some may disagree but whatever.  Newegg.com is you friend, pick a card and read the review on it.
