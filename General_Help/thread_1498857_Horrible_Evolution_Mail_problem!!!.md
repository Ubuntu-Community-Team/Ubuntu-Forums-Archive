### Post by sgosnell on 2010-06-01
I see no attachment.  The password for sending email through Google in Evolution is the same as your gmail password, but you need to put your entire email address, including "@gmail.com" in the user account fields.  

If you're using gmail, why not save your documents as a Google doc, and you'll have access to it permanently, including past revision versions.  Another possibility is using Dropbox, which will let you save any files of any type you want, simply by saving them in your local Dropbox folder, and they're automatically synchronized on the Dropbox server, and on any other computers you register with Dropbox.

### Post by sgosnell on 2010-06-01
To delete the old account, edit preferences, Mail Accounts, check the account you want to delete, then click Delete.  You might want to keep it for awhile, though, and just not use it, by unchecking the Enable button.

You need to save the document in Google Docs as whatever it will take, probably a .doc file.  Open Office can open .doc files, then save them locally as .odt or whatever you want.

[Dropbox website](https://www.dropbox.com/).  It's a service which lets you access your files from anywhere, and lets you sync and edit them on any computer which has Dropbox installed.  It's a free service for smaller accounts, you have to pay a little if you need to store more than 2GB of data.  For your use, I think the free account would work fine.

BTW, your horrible problem is with Open Office, not Evolution.  You need to sort out the crashes in Open Office.
