### Post by PuddingKnife on 2009-02-03
Try clicking on the animations button

Then go to the minimize tab, delete the current animation and click the 'New' button.

Choose the type of animation you want and in the 'Windows Match' box type:

(type=Normal | Dialog | ModalDialog | Unknown)
