### Post by procras on 2006-02-27
lm-sensors is your best bet
Run sensors-detect to set you your system

Then use a program to show you your sensors readings.
eg. gdesklets modules
eg. gkrellm

[http://www.newlinuxuser.com/howto-monitor-system-performance-with-gkrellm]("http://www.newlinuxuser.com/howto-monitor-system-performance-with-gkrellm")
