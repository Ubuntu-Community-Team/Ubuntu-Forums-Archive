### Post by ajgreeny on 2009-07-04
Firstly your link is taking us to the wrong page, so you need to sort that out.

Secondly, as far as I'm aware, digikam only adds tags to the exif data, and can not edit it any more than that, but if it is all you need, then simply add the tags you want to the photos and it will be saved.  I'm not sure this is actually part of the exif data for the photo, but it appears to be saved even between OSs.

To make changes such as date of photo etc I suggest you have a look at gwenview, which certainly will do this.  It's a long time since I did it, but I used it when I set my camera to the wrong year and wrong time (12 vs 24 hr setup).   It was very simple to change that data, so depending on what you want to do, it may be just as easy for you.
