### Post by senewag on 2008-06-09
Hi,
I've just started with F-spot too and it looks good.
From what I can see (but please test all this), I would "select all" then "remove from catalog" then "import" (without ticking "copy files...") and this would rebuild the catalog.  Your tags and comments would be intact as they are stored on the files.
Shouldn't take that long to complete...

### Post by senewag on 2008-06-10
Glad to hear it worked, I had to do the same myself.
If you hit ctr-I  on a photo, you will see that it changes only the "amended date".  You can do "edit"  "adjust time" to change the "original date" (when the photo was taken)  i.e. the order the photos are displayed in.  F-spot is great.  I have 5373 photos which needed sorting out :)
