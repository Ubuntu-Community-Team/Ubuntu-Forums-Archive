Q1: Will it be easy (or necessary) to upgrade to 5.10 from 5.04? easy Yes, necessary No not necessary it depends on you.

Q2: Should I just wait till 5.10 is released? 5.04 is very good and a vast improvement over 4.10.

Q3: Will 4.10 be easily upgradeable to 5.10?  That would be the intention, but as 5.04 uses XOrg and not XFree86 as the default, and a much later Gnome and kernel, and 5.10 will be a much larger step forward than the step from 4.10 to 5.04.  It could be difficult.



I'm already running Hoary 5.04 and have been since it was available in the repos. I had 1 glitch early on, but since then it's been rock solid! When 5.04 release next week I'm going to do a full rebuild and stay with that until Grumpy is available in the repos and then upgrade to that. Grumpy is the permanent name for the development version. I'll stay with Grumpy until 5.10 releases. Then I'll decide whether to go dual boot on my production pc. But i will still go to the next Grumpy on my test pc.
