### Post by Soarer on 2010-01-11
It's the same as for a gmail (or googlemail) account for the IMAP and SMTP servers (imap.gmail.com and smtp.gmail.com with appropriate ports) except...

you use your full username (i.e.with the domain) when asked for username, and the password for this account (not your gmail account if you have one).

So username is: [email]yourusername@yourdomain.com[/email] not simply yourusername.
