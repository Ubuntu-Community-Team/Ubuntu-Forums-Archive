### Post by wyndlass on 2016-07-19
well, somehow i accidentally turned on slow keys. slow keys makes you have to hold down a key before it accepted and displayed. i finally went into ubuntu help searched for keyboard and chose the keyboard link and learned about slow keys. slow keys is in setting-universal access-typing. you can adjust the delay for the key press.
