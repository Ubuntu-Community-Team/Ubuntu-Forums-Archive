### Post by Rui Pais on 2007-02-10
hi,
i don't known if theres a such extension... 
but there is a file manager called [pcmanfm]("http://pcmanfm.sourceforge.net/") that do that. 
It looks much nautilus, but it offers less options. On the other side it's lighter and faster...

Nautilus and thunar offer more or less the same "tabbed" behavior with buttons (instead of location bar) for each parent folder... its an hierarchal, kind of, tabbed restricted to the parents folder... on nautilus you need to go to browser mode, not spacial.
