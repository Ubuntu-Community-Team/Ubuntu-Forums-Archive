### Post by ragtag on 2011-01-29
In System>Preferences>Windows in Ubuntu (not in Wine), you'll find an option to use either Alt or Super ("Windows Key") to move your windows. Change this to the Super key, and you'll be able to use Alt in in your game as you normally would.
