### Post by claracc on 2010-02-28
You only have tu use the arrows (up and down, more specifically, the down arrow) untill being in the line of grub saying: ubuntu kernel the number xx.xx.xx.x (recovery mode), then you click on this line (enter) and now you are booting in recovery mode in order to fix whatever problem you have.
