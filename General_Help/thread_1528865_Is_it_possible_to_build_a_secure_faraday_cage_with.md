### Post by QLee on 2010-07-11
[QUOTE=Bernie Gallagher]...  Making a good electrical connection at the door seams is the bigger issue.[/QUOTE]

You could try this, get some thin strips (maybe an inch or so) of sheet copper; make a continuous band around the inside of the door frame; attach one edge of the strips to the door all around (floor has to be considered too) and bend (form) the strip by bending the unattached edge out so that it acts as a "wiper" when the door is closed, contacting the door frame strips and "sealing" the door.
