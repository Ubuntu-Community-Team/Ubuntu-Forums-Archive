### Post by hawthornso23 on 2011-08-28
It sounds like a video card/driver issue. You don't have full video acceleration working which the games need to run properly.

First off, try installing the proprietary video drivers for your hardware through the "install additional drivers" tool. If that doesn't do the trick then you'll need to specify what video hardware you have and what drivers are installed for it.

### Post by Mark Phelps on 2011-08-28
What's your make and model video card/chip? The older ones don't have driver support for gaming anymore.
