### Post by howefield on 2010-06-03
> **MechaMechanism said:**
> I am having a tough time trying to get Empathy to download a list of rooms.

Type /help to see the meagre list of supported commands. Empathy forms a very basic way of connecting to IRC.

If you want to join a room known to you and have a chat, Empathy is fine, if you want more than that, use a dedicated application.

An alternative might be to use Pidgin, it will connect to IRC, but I do not know if it will give you the level of features that you are looking for.
