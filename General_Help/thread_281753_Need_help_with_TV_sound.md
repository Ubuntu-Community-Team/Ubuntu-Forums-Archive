### Post by teet on 2006-10-22
First worry about getting sound in TVTime...then worry about myth second.

In gnome, try opening up the volume control (double-click on the speaker icon in the upper left corner of the screen).  Then go to Edit --> Preferences and put a check mark next to everything.

Now, open up tv time (leave the volume control screen open) and start fiddling with the volume control settings.  Put all of your volume bars up to about 80% and unmute everything.  You probably will have to set your "line" or "aux" to capture (the little microphone icon without an X means that it is set to capture).  

Basically, you should just "fiddle" with the volume controls until it works.  That's what I did when I was first learning about tv tuners.
