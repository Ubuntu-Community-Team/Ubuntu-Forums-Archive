### Post by kid1000002000 on 2010-05-15
go to System>Preferences>Sound.  Turn on the Ubuntu Theme on the window that pops up, and you should be good to go.

---

### Post by mahela007 on 2010-05-15
It was already set to Ubuntu... I tried changing it to one of the other sounds but I still don't hear anything.
EDIT:
Problem solved. The Volume slider in the same window was set to mute.
