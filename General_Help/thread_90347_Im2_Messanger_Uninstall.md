### Post by matthewv on 2005-11-14
If it was installed through wine, you will have to uninstall it the same way you would uninstall in windows. Try going to your home folder, pressing ctrl+h, to show hidden folders, go to .wine folder, then drive_c, then Program Files and then into the programs folder and looking for an uninstaller
