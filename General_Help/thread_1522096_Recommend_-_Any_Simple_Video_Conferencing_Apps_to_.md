### Post by YannBuntu on 2010-07-05
Hi,

for basic video+audio calls, I would recommend Ekiga (+ Gobby + Coccinella) or EmpathyPPA or Skype.

for your professional needs, have a look at :
- [BigBlueButton](http://www.bigbluebutton.org/) , it is open-source, and it should works on Ubuntu 10.04
- [OpenMeetings](http://code.google.com/p/openmeetings/) , it is also open-source, but I don't know if it works.
- Cisco WebEx Meeting Center : closed-source, should be compatible with Ubuntu 9.04 ([source]("http://www.webconferencing-test.com/en/webconferencing_top5/webconference_system_requirements.html"))
- Webex : closed-source, should be compatible with Ubuntu 8.04, free demo 14 days ([website]("http://webex.com/"))
