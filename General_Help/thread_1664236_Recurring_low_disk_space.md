### Post by hawthornso23 on 2011-01-10
The disk usage analyser under  applications > accessories  might be able to show you where the space is going. 7GB is a big enough chunk to be easily visible in its graphical display.

If 7GB is freed when you clear trash perhaps there is a large hidden file in your trash. Did you click `show hidden files' when browsing your trash?
