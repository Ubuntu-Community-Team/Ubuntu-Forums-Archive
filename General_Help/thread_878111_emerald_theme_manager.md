### Post by rune0077 on 2008-08-02
> **Kaneda187 said:**
> Am how do i make my panel match my emerald theme??

You can't. Emerald only handles window-borders, it has no control over your panel. Your best bet is to find a background image that looks like your emerald-theme, and then use that for your panel.

### Post by crhylove on 2008-08-03
> **rune0077 said:**
> You can't. Emerald only handles window-borders, it has no control over your panel. Your best bet is to find a background image that looks like your emerald-theme, and then use that for your panel.

Actually, it's pretty easy to change the color and transparency of any panel to match most Emerald themes.  Another idea is to make the panel mostly transparent, making the desktop more of a focus.  Right click the panel and select properties.  Then slide that transparency slider on down.  It always improve the look, though, depending on your desktop may make some icons/words on the panel hard to read.
