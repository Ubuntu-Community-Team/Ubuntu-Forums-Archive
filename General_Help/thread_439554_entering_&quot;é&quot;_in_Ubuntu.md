### Post by qpwoeiruty on 2007-05-10
You could always go into the insert menu in OpenOffice and choose to add a special character.

Alternatively (if you have to do this a lot), in Sytem/Preferences/Keyboard and add an international layout. You can then press ' and then e to make an é.
