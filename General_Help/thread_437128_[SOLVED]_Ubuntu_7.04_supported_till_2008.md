### Post by apunc1 on 2007-05-08
no you don't lose your documents by upgrading. i would back up your files to be safe though.

upgrading does not reformat your hard drive. if you wanted to do a clean install, thus reformatting your hard drive, then you would need a separate /home or /documents partition that you would not alter when doing a clean install.
