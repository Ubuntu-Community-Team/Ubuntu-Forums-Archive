### Post by bodhi.zazen on 2008-10-13
When I come across a Vista box I wipe the hard drive and never look back.

If you wish to go back, however, you can either make a recovery CD, or better, IMO, image the relevant partitions before you wipe them.

You can make an image of the hard drive and store it on a DVD and restore it later. There are several options , I personally use partimage.

[http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/partimage](http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/partimage)
