### Post by asmoore82 on 2011-03-06
The lower right corner of most application windows has a drag
handle that's a much better target.

You can also Right Click the window title bar or Right Click the button
on the window list at the bottom of the screen and choose "Resize."

The other resize shortcut is Alt + Middle Click or Alt + Wheel Click.
On a two button laptop, you would have to Alt + Chord Click both left and right.

The Chord Click is a slightly ridiculous last resort. So, there is a
mildly obscure option to change this to Alt + Right Click:
run `gconf-editor` and check the key "/apps/metacity/general/resize_with_right_button"
