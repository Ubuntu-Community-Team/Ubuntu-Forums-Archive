### Post by ryanhaigh on 2008-04-26
To remove the icons press alt-f2 and enter gconf-editor, the easiest way I can remember is to search for computer (check both boxes) and one of the results will relate to desktop icons, click that and from there you will be able to find the preference for home and trash.
