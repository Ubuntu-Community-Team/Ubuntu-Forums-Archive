### Post by Robgould on 2006-02-15
close the stuff you don't want....click save changes again and log off.
 
A better way to start stuff when you log on is to go to the startup programs tab on the sessions menu...add whatever you want to run there.
 
desktop-->preferences-->more preferences-->sessions
