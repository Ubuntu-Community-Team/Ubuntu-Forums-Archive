### Post by tredegar on 2011-08-16
The bug you refer to relates to 2006

I expect it is fixed by now.

The golden rule of backups is "Once you have made the backup, have you tried restoring it?"

If the answer is "No", then it is not safe to proceed with deleting anything.

This advice applies equally to Mac, Win. and linux.

You may get some help (and data back) from the **photorec** or **testdisk** utilities.

Good luck.
