### Post by coverup1128 on 2012-01-11
Go to Edit-> Preferences -> Mail accounts. Select the account on the right, and click Edit. Look under the tabs "Receiving options" and "Sending options". If your server requires authentication, click the checkbox, and you will be asked for the password next time evolution polls your account or sends email.
