### Post by jazzor on 2013-11-04
In my power options (system settings) I only see 5,10,30 minutes and 1 hour for suspend. How to make it set 2 hours, 4 hours etc?

Thanks.

---

### Post by varunendra on 2013-11-05
Open **dconf Editor**, navigate to **org > gnome > settings-daemon > plugins > power**, change the values of **sleep-inactive-ac-timeout** or **sleep-inactive-battery-timeout** in seconds as desired.

For example, setting the sleep-inactive-battery-timeout to 7200 will make the timeout 2 hrs when on battery power.
