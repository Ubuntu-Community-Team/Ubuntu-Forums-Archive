### Post by AndyCooll on 2007-08-02
Well, any conversion is always going to result in a loss of quality. Whether its noticeable is dependent on how well trained your ear is.

To mass rename files and tags you can use something like Easytag, Audio Tag Tool, or Cowbell. All these can easily bulk rename files etc.

### Post by strabes on 2007-08-03
Use **soundconverter**/soundkonverter to convert your files and then use **easytag** to tag, re-tag, and organize them by tags. It has the ability to rename files and directories based on files' tags.
