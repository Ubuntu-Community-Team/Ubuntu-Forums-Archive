### Post by fragos on 2006-12-10
Do System-> Preferences-> Fonts and you can change your fixed width font used for terminal.  Also check out the rendering selected.  Four options are available and you can select the one that displays best for you.  I like "Best shapes" better than the recomendation for LCDs.  I have an LCD monitor.
