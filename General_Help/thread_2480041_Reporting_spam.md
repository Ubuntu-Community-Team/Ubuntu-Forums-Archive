### Post by col48 on 2022-10-20
Reporting to whom?

There's feedback to Gmail etc, who might tweak their systems to make spamming harder.

And the authorities - police, government, banks... - may be interested in order to catch criminals and prevent crime.  For these cases, you would have to report it yourself, usually by forwarding the email to a special address advertised on their website.
