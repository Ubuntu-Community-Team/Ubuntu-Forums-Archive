### Post by ajgreeny on 2015-03-11
Go to the Insert menu and choose Image.  You can then right click on the image and choose Image and change many of its atributes such as its size and many other things.

PS: I'm using LibreOffice not OpenOffice, but I think they are very similar so even if the details are different I imagine you will be able to sort out how to do this.

### Post by Holger_Gehrke on 2015-03-11
Do you mean you can't open image files ? Well, obviously not. OO Writer is a text processor, not an image viewer. But you can 'insert > Picture > From File'.
Or do you mean you get some kind of place holder element instead of a picture ? Check 'Tools > Options > Writer > View > Display > Graphics and Objects'.

### Post by bapoumba on 2015-03-11
