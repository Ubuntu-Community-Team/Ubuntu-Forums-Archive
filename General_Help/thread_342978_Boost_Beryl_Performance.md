I don't know THAT much about Ubuntu and Beryl, but I managed to get it installed on my computer. So I can't give you any help, but I can tell you my experience, since my system seems to be kind of like yours.

I have an AMD Athlon 64 3700+ (2.2Ghz), and only 512 MB of RAM (the two-slot kind).

I have an nVidia Geforce 7600GT, which is 256MB VRAM, like yours.

I installed beryl on Edgy Eft a week ago. I used the Beta nVidia drivers. I don't have any serious problems with it, and I'm using 512 megabytes, and my processor is slower than yours.

The only problems I have with it is that Google Earth is jerky when Beryl is working, and Nexuiz and some other graphics-related programs are jerky. So, on my computer, which seems almost identical (but worse) than yours, Beryl is nice and smooth, except for 3d graphics programs. I don't know what your problem is, but I'm not having it.
