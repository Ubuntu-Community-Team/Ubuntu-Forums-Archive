### Post by Habeouscorpus on 2011-02-28
The driver from here works: [http://support-nz.canon.co.nz/contents/NZ/EN/0100159003.html](http://support-nz.canon.co.nz/contents/NZ/EN/0100159003.html)

Just download the file, then go to System > Administration > Printing > Add Printer, and go through the steps until it asks you to specify a printer driver. Choose the PPD file option, then point it at where you saved the file.
