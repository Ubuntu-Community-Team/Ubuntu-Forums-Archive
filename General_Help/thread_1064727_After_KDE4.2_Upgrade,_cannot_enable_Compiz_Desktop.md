You don't have to install any of the compiz stuff as kde4.2 has had compositing built into the window manager. Also I believe the native kde4.2 compositing will try to take account of your graphic card and only allow effects that won't bring your machine to its knees.

To turns things go "System Setting" -> "Desktop" pick the "Desktop Effects" in the left hand window, then pick the "All effects" tab in the right window. You can then turn on/off what every you like.
