### Post by WorMzy on 2010-05-07
_Q1-How Do I Restore Something I Deleted in the File Manager?_
So long as you did a 'soft' delete, it will be in the trash bin. I believe you should have an icon in the bottom right of your screen which, when clicked, will take you to the trash bin, where you have the option of restoring the file(s). If you don't have the icon, then open the file manager and look in the list of places. Alternatively, press Ctrl+L in the filemanager and enter "trash:///" (no quotes)
 
_Q2-How Can I Fix Sound Problem on Ubuntu?_
No idea, sorry.
 
_Q3-How can I install Microsoft Office Word on Ubuntu Computer?_
Is Open Office no good for you? So long as you have a legal copy of Office, you can install it with a program called Wine. Install Wine with 'sudo apt-get install wine', insert your Office CD, open the CD folder in the filemanager, right click on the setup .exe, and choose "run with Wine Program Loader" or something like that. After it's installed, you should be able to find the shortcut under Applications -> Wine -> Programs

_Q4-How to hide a public IP address?_
No idea, sorry.

_Q5-How to find an IP address in email ?_
No idea, sorry.
 
_Q6-How can I copy photos from My Mobile Phone to my ubuntu computer with Bluetooth?_
You have to make your PC visible, and have the phone connect to the PC, or else have the phone visible and have the PC connect to the phone. I can't remember which way around it is, but if your PC has bluetooth capabilities, you should have a bluetooth icon in the top right of the screen.
