### Post by matthew on 2006-11-03
I hate to resurrect an ancient thread like this, but in this case it's appropriate as there is a solution and it should be posted in the same tread in case anyone is searching for help with this issue.

I just installed Edgy a few days ago. The newest version of OpenOffice (2.0.4) that is included supports embedded sound in presentations. You will need to convert your powerpoint presentation to an openoffice format and re-embed the sound file, but the process is quick and painless. From the menu Insert->Movie and Sound.

(For those that are wondering, I erased Windows a long time ago and have been Linux only for over a year. I solved this problem originally by running XP in vmware when I needed this functionality.)
