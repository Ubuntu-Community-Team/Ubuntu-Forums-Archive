### Post by jgw on 2021-12-18
I was running with a geforce display card gf 9500gt and suddenly my display went black.  Everything was working, all fans running, etc.   I just couldn't get a display.  I think I was running with the xorg ubuntu supplied driver instead of the nvidia driver. I did this with two boards and the same thing happened.   The display wasn't quite right but I was living with it.  Then I lost my display.  I then plugged into the motherboard display, re-booted and everything was dandy.  The reason I am writing this is to warn anybody who is having a problem with the xorg ubuntu supplied nvidia driver to be careful.  I haven't yet tried either card in another machine but will when I have time to test it.  I will also update this when I do that.
