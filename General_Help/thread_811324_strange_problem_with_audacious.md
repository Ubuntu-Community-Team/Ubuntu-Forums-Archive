### Post by kpkeerthi on 2008-05-29
Does the volume change in other players?

If not you may want to map the volume control to corresponding mixer channel in alsa. To do this, go to System -> Preferences -> Sound and select 'Master' & 'PCM'. You may also select 'Headphone' if you want your headphone volume controlled using the slider.
