### Post by kragen on 2007-05-10
There may be a flash player that works on x86-64, but the recognised way of getting flash to work is to install the 32 bit version of firefox, and then install the 32 bit flash plugin. You can find a guide here:

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/FirefoxAMD64FlashJava](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/FirefoxAMD64FlashJava)

Ah, found a 64 bit flash plugin now, you can find a guide here: (search for "GNASH open source alternative" or scroll down a bit).
