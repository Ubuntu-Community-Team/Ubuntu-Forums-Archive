### Post by davbren on 2007-05-01
Hi I have a USB hard drive and ubuntu seems to want to boot from it each time and it stops the bot entirely so i have to unplug it and the plug it back in once the OS has started. How can I stop ubuntu wanting to run from usb devices?

---

### Post by Turellius on 2007-05-01
Go into your BIOS and either disable booting from USB or move USB further down the boot order.  Most of the time they are numbered 1,2,3 etc you can move items up and down the list just make sure that the hard drive(s) are higher up on the list than USB.
