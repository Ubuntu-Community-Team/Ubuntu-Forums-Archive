### Post by taurus on 2009-02-24
If 7.04 is the only OS on your machine, you could tell the installer, 8.10, to use the whole disk, wiping out your old install.

Otherwise, you need to use the Manual option when you get to the partition part and mount whichever partition that you have as root filesysten to / and reformat that partition.
