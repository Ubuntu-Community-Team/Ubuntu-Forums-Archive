### Post by HermanAB on 2007-11-27
First check the motherboard for leaking capacitors.  If any electrolitic capacitors exploded, replace all of them or buy a new mother board.

Secondly, I would turn the clock speed down a little bit in the BIOS and re-install the system.  If that also doesn't help, start swapping parts out, starting with the ethernet adaptor, followed by the video adaptor.
