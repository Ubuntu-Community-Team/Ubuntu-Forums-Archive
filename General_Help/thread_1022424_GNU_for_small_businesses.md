### Post by bodhi.zazen on 2008-12-27
GNU applications are free as in free speech as well as free as in free beer.

It is the closed source applications and copyrights where you may run into legal issues.

My only reservation would be, does your aunt have permission to install on a corporate box ?

Also, FYI, you can run many open source applications in windows. One can run Firefox, Thunderbird, and Open Office on Windows (many others too). All free and legal.
