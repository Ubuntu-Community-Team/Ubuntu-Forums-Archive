### Post by kwaanens on 2007-04-12
Wikipedia ([http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Click%27N%27Run](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Click%27N%27Run)):
> Amid rumors that Linspire was going to port the CNR service to the Ubuntu distribution, the company announced plans on April 24, 2006 to release CNR under a free software/open source license. It could then be used by other Linux distributions.

On January 23, 2007 (almost 9 months to the day that CNR's relicensing was announced), a "CNR for all" website was launched; however, the launching of the warehouse itself was announced for Q2 of 2007. The release of the free/open-source CNR client is planned to coincide with the release of Freespire 2.0 and Linspire 6.0, both of which will include the free/open-source CNR client.

C'n'R forums ([http://forum.cnr.com/showthread.php?t=55](http://forum.cnr.com/showthread.php?t=55)):
> There will be functionality for ubuntu Feisty Fawn (7.04) with better integration planned in Feisty +1 (7.10)...
> The goals to have something available on the heels of Feisty (7.04) release in April...
The 3rd thread after stickies, so it was really not exactly hard to find...
