### Post by Hedgehog1 on 2011-03-20
If the gateway to too old to boot from a USB, you can use the 'plop' CD.  It will use drivers on the CD to get your gateway to boot from the USB.

It offers a lot of booting options:  [plop.bootmanager]("http://www.plop.at/en/bootmanager.html")
