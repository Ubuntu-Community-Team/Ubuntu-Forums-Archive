### Post by hal10000 on 2009-09-12
You have to install flashplugin. You can do this via Add/Remove Software or in a terminal window with
[COLOR="Red"]sudo apt-get install flashplugin-nonfree[/COLOR]

[HINT] FastesT way is to mark the line above with your mouse and paste it with the middle mouse button to a terminal window and press ENTER. you then have to type in your password. Don't wonder if your password is not displayed, that's ok.

Then restart your firefox and try again.
