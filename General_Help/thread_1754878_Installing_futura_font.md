### Post by Rasa1111 on 2011-05-10
> **zaubereien said:**
> Thanx

Where can i finde the .fonts folder ?


 No problem. 

In Ubuntu, Go to your "Home" folder. 

Once Home folder is open, press Ctrl+H, this will show the hidden files/folders.

Now scroll down until you get to the folders that start with "F", and look for one called .fonts

If you do not see it, You simply create it! 

So, To create it, you would just right-click somewhere in your home folder, and choose "Create folder"  then name the folder  ".fonts" [without the quotes].

The .(period) in front of .fonts, will make the folder hidden, So when you want to view it or add to it,
Just Hit Ctrl H again, and it will hide, or show the hidden folders. 

Then you can drag your fonts into this new .fonts folder, and you're good. :)

Also, when you install any new fonts , they will also go into this .fonts folder automatically.

and to install new fonts, all you have to do is double click a TTF file, and a window will pop up, then you click the "Install" button, and it's done. 

 Hope this helps.

:) Good luck
