### Post by theller on 2014-07-19
I installed the sphere icons [http://potzblitz7.deviantart.com/art/Sphere-1-2-5-373679678](http://potzblitz7.deviantart.com/art/Sphere-1-2-5-373679678) and they looked really nice! One problem, Clementine and a few other programs used the old icons, even though I saw them in the sphere folder with all the other icons. The only solution I found was from 2012 and it didn't work.

My solution:
go to /usr/share/applications and right click on the Clementine icon > Properties > then click on the icon and navigate to the image you want

This changed the icon in the menu and launcher but not the top bar when it's activated.

Any other ways to do this?

If not hope this helps someone!
