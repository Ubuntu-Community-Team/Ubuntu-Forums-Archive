### Post by beachutman on 2010-05-31
have upgraded to 10.04, no major problems, but i am prompted for my  default keyring password on finishing the boot up, and also if the  machine is left long enough to go idle and blank the screen. Not a  biggie, but annoying,and i can't seem to see how to switch it off?

---

### Post by formaldehyde_spoon on 2010-05-31
Remove it from System > Preferences > Startup,
delete all files in ~/.gnome2/keyrings/
right click on your wireless in the panel > Edit Connections > edit your connection > enter password and select Available to All Users.
