### Post by oldfred on 2012-01-10
Welcome to the forums.

I am an Engineer not an Accountant, so the Accountants can explain better. I use kMyMoney, but most of the Linux accounting systems are more like real Accounting systems in that they use a chart of accounts. Each has a default set of accounts and you can add more as your business/household may need. But you have each transaction in the account and run the reports summing on the accounts. The accounts are the categories.
