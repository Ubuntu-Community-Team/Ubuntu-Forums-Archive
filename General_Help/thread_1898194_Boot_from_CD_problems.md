### Post by hackb0y294 on 2011-12-20
That's odd; I think this problem usually occurs with installations, not the Live CD. You might try selecting the Check disc for defects option by booting the CD, then pressing any key when you see the keyboard logo at the bottom of your screen. Press the down arrow key until you reach that option, then press enter. The CD may have been burned with errors, or your downloaded ISO might be corrupt.
