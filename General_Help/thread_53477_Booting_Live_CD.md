### Post by Sam on 2005-08-01
Go in the BIOS (press Del or F2 or whatever you should press to enter your BIOS) during the pc boot, then find somewhere 'boot sequence' and make sure your CD-Rom drive is before the hard disc. Then save and exit BIOS.
