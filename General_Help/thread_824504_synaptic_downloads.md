### Post by Pjotr123 on 2008-06-10
Some applications are programmed to make a menu entry during installation. Others not. It depends on the makers of the applications.

You can always start them in the terminal. Look in /usr/bin for the name of the launcher. You can always make you own menu entry, if you wish.
