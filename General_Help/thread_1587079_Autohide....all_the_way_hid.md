### Post by sosaudio1 on 2010-10-02
One issue I have always had with panels and autohide is that they don't hide all the way. Its not bad, it's as if I have an outline over the top of the app that I am running. Is there a file that I can edit that will tell the autohide of the panel what position is truly auto-hid?

I know may take a bit to understand...try this:

make a panel at the top of the desktop make sure the color is different from your window color. Mine is mostly transparent with a black color used to tint it.

Put some shortcuts up there on the new panel...Filezilla works well. You will see it if you select autohide.

Now, select properties and make it autohide.

You should see a thin bar at the top...real thin....like it is a line at the top...

I know...petty, but, if I can affect that, that would be grrrreat
