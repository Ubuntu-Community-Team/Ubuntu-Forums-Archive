### Post by Exsecrabilus on 2008-05-23
The Emerald Theme Manager does not automatically come with themes.

You must install them by downloading **Compiz** or **Beryl Emerald** themes from [http://gnome-look.org/](http://gnome-look.org/) or other sites.

Then use the **Import** button to locate your .emerald file and decorate.
