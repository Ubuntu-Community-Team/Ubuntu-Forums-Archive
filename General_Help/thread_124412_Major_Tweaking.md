### Post by towsonu2003 on 2006-02-02
[QUOTE=Micro Rotors]
Another thing I would like to do is get rid of the brown behind the splash screen.[/QUOTE]
System>Administration>Login Screen Setup>GTK+ greeter
Change "background" to "color" (I think it already is) than choose the color you'd like in "background color" in the left bottom of the same window (click on the color and you'll be able to choose another). 

be careful with it as it runs with root privileges.
