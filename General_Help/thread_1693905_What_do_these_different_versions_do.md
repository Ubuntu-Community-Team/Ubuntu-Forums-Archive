### Post by JRV on 2011-02-23
There are hunderds of different versions of Linux.  They are usually referred to as distros.  Short for distributions.  A good list of distros can be found at [http://distrowatch.com/](http://distrowatch.com/).

Look over their list and try to find a distro that suits you best.

The reason for so many different versions is because Linux does not try to be a one size fits all operating system, like Windows.  All kinds of different people customize it to make the operating system best suited for their needs.

Ubuntu and Puppy are the ones I usually use.
