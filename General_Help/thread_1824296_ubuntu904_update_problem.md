### Post by snowpine on 2011-08-13
Ubuntu 9.04 is "end of life" and so there won't be any more upgrades.

If you are happy with your 9.04 system then you can continue to use it for as long as you like. You won't receive any support, bug fixes, security patches, etc. but depending on your needs, you may be able to live with that. :)
