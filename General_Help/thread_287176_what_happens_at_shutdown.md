### Post by ejket on 2006-10-28
The shutdown should be orderly, and you should be prompted to save files.  When you shut down with firefox open, for example, it's considered an irregular termination and when you start it again it offers to reopen your last session.  The key point is that the session has been saved, and it will put you where you were when you shut down.

Whether it's a good idea to rely on prompts or saves is another question.  The best idea is to save things yourself.
