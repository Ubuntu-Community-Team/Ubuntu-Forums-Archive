### Post by SquireCD on 2011-01-21
Hi phazixc,

You are able to mount your phone, right? If so, when you plug it in, it should automatically ask you if you want to open Shotwell, then it'll ask you if you want to open  Rhythmbox. 

When  Rhythmbox is open with your phone mounted, you should have it listed under "Devices" on the left. If you just left click on your iPhone, "Sync With Library".  Rhythmbox will take all of the music on your hard drive that it knows about, and it'll sync it with your phone.

So, when you do that, everything in ~/Music should be synced to your phone. You don't need to actually drag files into any directories on the phone.

Hope that helps!

PS:  Rhythmbox 0.13.1
