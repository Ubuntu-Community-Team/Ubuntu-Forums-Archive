### Post by MoLE on 2007-06-06
Bit of a tricky one.  Depends on whether you've got another palm you can use to synch your old data to.  If you have, then it's fairly simple - just give the new palm the same username as the old one, and Palm Desktop should accept it and transfer your backup onto the palm.  You can then sync the data into Evolution on Ubuntu using gnome-pilot.

If not, then I suspect it may be an arduous process of trying to export the addressbook data from the Palm Desktop program - from memory, the process is somewhat repeditive.
