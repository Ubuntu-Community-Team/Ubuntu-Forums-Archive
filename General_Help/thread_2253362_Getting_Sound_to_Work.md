### Post by SeijiSensei on 2014-11-20
Do you have both a phono jack and an HDMI port?  It may be the audio is being sent to the wrong output.

I recommend installing **pavucontrol** which gives you better control over the audio system.  See if that helps.

### Post by Rob Sayer on 2014-11-28

Unlike with ubuntu, Mint comes with all those codecs preinstalled.
