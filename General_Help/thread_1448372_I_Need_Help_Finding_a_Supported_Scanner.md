### Post by sv87411 on 2010-04-08
Not sure if it suits your needs or desire for open source software, but I have been using VueScan ([http://www.hamrick.com/](http://www.hamrick.com/)) for a long while now in Windows and, over the last couple of years in Linux.

It's reasonably priced and I can vouch for the Linux version working very well with my Epson Perfection scanner.

The compatibility list includes the Epson CX4400, but doesn't list the 4450, but I would have thought you could email the author to ask. If you want full control of your scanner to produce high quality results then if your scanner is compatible, I'd definitely recommend it.
