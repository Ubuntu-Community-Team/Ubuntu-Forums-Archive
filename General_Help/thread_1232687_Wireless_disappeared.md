### Post by fragos on 2009-08-06
I'd try re-seating the card. You could also remove the card and visualy examine. Some card failures can be seen as in burn marks, chips that have changed to white, or perhaps a cap that overheated and released pressure through the top of the can. Not seeing anything doesn't mean the cards OK but visual confirmation can be reasurring when you do buy a new one.
