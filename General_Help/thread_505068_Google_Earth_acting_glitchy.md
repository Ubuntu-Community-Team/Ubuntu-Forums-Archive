### Post by Langleyo on 2008-05-12
Try this:

From the desktop with everything closed go to System - Preferences - Appearance - Visual Effects and select "None" option.


It looks as if the display is constantly updating screen info when the  effects are enabled and its affecting how google earth appears. Remember to turn effects back on when you've finished, if you like that stuff.

This is for Hardy Heron but should work equally well for Gutsy and probably other versions.


Thanks to Ricardoramos for pointing me in the right direction.
