### Post by upbeat.linux on 2008-10-27
When you boot into XP check to see that the sound is not muted.

Also, is your sound card built-in or an add-in? Run windows updates and check your motherboard or system's manufacturer for drivers updates.  Also, right-click on my computer, go to manage, go to device manager, expand "Sound, video, and game controllers" is there a red X or yellow warning shield next to any of these.  Try to find the drivers that correspond to the device listed.

Also, this seems more like an XP related question than an Ubuntu related question.
