### Post by ruibernardo on 2007-08-08
Check what language settings you have in Firefox.

This on the menu "Edit", "Preferences", click the "Advanced" tab. On the bottom there is "Languages" (or similar). You can change what languages you want Firefox to report to the websites as your favorite language.
