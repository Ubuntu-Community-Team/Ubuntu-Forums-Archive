### Post by rbmorse on 2008-12-28
[http://ubuntuforums.org/forumdisplay.php?f=326](http://ubuntuforums.org/forumdisplay.php?f=326)

Is a great place to start. The "search this forum" works really well. 

The "new to Ubuntu?" sticky section at the top of the page is particularly valuable and probably addresses most of your "starter" questions.  If not...just ask. It's probably the friendliest forum in the Linuxverse. 

Especially don't be afraid to speak up if someone tells you something, or points you to something you don't understand...Linux usn't hard, but there are some basics that help you to understand how you can best use the system. When I first went from Windows to Linux I really, really wish I has a resource like "Absolute Beginners" available.
