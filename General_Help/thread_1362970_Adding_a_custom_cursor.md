### Post by skykooler on 2010-02-11
You need to click the "Customize..." button in Appearance Preferences, then select the "Pointer" tab. Your cursor should be in the list. Select it, log out and log back in. You will now have your custom cursor!

Before you log out, some apps (such as Firefox) recognize the new cursor but most don't - don't worry about that, it goes away after you log out and log in again.
