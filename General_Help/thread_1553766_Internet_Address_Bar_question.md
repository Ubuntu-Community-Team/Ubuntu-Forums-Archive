### Post by libssd on 2010-08-15
Ctrl-L followed by Ctrl-C selects the entire URL and copies it so that you can do something with it. I can't speak for MSIE, but this behavior is the same across FireFox, Chrome, and Safari, Linux, Mac (using the Apple key instead of Ctrl), and Windows.
