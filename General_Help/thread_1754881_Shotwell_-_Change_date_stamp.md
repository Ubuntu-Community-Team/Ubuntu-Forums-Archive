### Post by Roasted on 2011-05-10
Somehow the pictures I have are dated 2006, however they were taken last month. Due to this, Shotwell organizes them incorrectly. Is there a way I can change these date stamps?

EDIT - Fixed. I had to go within the actual picture folder, highlight all pictures, then at the top menu go to Photos - Change Date/Time. Changed the date and they fixed themselves on the fly. Very nice.
