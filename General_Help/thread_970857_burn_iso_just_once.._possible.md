### Post by oldsoundguy on 2008-11-04
once the ISO is on their computer, you have no control of it.  You can control the amount of downloads, but that requires a bit of work from your site.
Also, once the ISO has been burned, there is no way to stop it from being used on multiple computers other than installing a self destruct clock in the program and going through a registration/authentication process such as Windows and some Windows programs make it's users go through in order to shut that clock off. (that dreaded "trial ware" crap!)
If this is open source .. think not!!
