### Post by mgarrett682 on 2016-12-24
Yes, something like that, although a separate mouse and keyboard would also work so long as they were reliable and worked well. I went through wireless mouse/keyboard issues five or six years ago with a Windows box used as a htpc. My first attempt was a Rii product which didn't work very well at the distance I was from the receiver (ten to fifteen feet). I eventually found a combo that worked well but it won't work with the Ubuntu system I have set up now. Also, the Rii required a charger every few days. When it went dead just dropping in a couple of batteries had me back in business immediately.

### Post by Grail King on 2016-12-24
I have 3 logitech k400r they work fine, not all key combos may work with your media center, I use Kodi.
I did find that depending what type of computer you have affects the keyboards; the GBox in my daughter's room running open elecKodi has no issues, same with my bedroom setup, but the LR using an ASUS mini had issues with signal, I had to add an extension cable and tape it up so the Asus could receive.

Range on each is 35 -40 feet no problems, even messed with my daughter's Gbox from outside back yard to make her go crazy.
