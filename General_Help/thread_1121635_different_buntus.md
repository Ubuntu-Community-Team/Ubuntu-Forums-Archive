### Post by 3Miro on 2009-04-10
Anyone can make a variation of Ubuntu and really any distro. The variations usually mean different desktops (Gnome for Ubuntu/ KDE for Kubuntu/ Xfe for XUbuntu) or simply different themes and/or default software installed (Eubuntu).

My guess is that Christian, Satanic (there is also Muslim) editions would have some appropriate theme installed and perhaps Bible/Qur'an shortcuts on the desktop (I don't know what satanists read, Stephen Hawking and Charles Darwin perhaps ;)). I think there is also parental control software that might be installed by default.

### Post by mb_webguy on 2009-04-10
Ubuntu Christian and Muslim editions are simply Ubuntu with appropriate religious applications installed -- bible readers, prayer time schedulers, etc.

The Satanic edition doesn't really have anything to do with Satanism.  It's simply Ubuntu with a different, darker interface and apparently some metal metal music included.
