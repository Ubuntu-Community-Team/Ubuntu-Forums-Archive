### Post by robsoles on 2010-09-14
If UFW is reporting strange stuff and you have a CPU locked at 100% I would wonder if your system hasn't been compromised.

I would unplug it's ethernet lead (or disconnect from wifi) and then restart it, keeping it off the network, and try to determine if stuff is running that neither I or Canonical intend to run.

Of course I can't be certain but I think that if a core died in CPU it wouldn't be locked at 100%
