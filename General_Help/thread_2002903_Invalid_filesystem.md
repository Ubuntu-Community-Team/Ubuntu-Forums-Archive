### Post by Cheesemill on 2012-06-13
If you are trying to install Windows on a GPT formatted drive then your motherboard has to use UEFI instead of BIOS.

If your motherboard doesn't support UEFI than you will have to erase your drive and format it with an MS-DOS partition table.
