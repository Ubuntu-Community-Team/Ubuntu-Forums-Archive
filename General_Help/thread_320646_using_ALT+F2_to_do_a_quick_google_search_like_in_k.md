### Post by QwUo173Hy on 2006-12-17
I've just moved from Kubuntu Dapper to Ubuntu Edgy. One feature I used very frequently was to use ALT + F2 to bring up a Run Program dialog.

Then, instead of running a program, I could enter "gg: test" to do a google search for "test" or "wp: titanic" for a wikipedia search for titanic. Is it possible to get this functionality in gnome?

Thanks for reading,
Jarlath

---

### Post by mcduck on 2006-12-17
try the Deskbar Applet. (right-click on your panel, select 'Add To Panel', find 'Deskbar Applet' and drag it to your panel)

The keyboard shortcut for Deskbar is alt-F3
