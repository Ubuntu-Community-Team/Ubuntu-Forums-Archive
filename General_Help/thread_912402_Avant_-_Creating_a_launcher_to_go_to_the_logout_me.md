### Post by kestal on 2008-09-06
Right now I have Avant set up, the majority of what I want is all ready there.

Is there a way to easily add a launcher that will go to the logout screen? (logout/switch user/restart/shut down/etc etc)

---

### Post by Genius314 on 2008-09-06
Yes.

> /usr/bin/gnome-session-save --gui --kill

Now, you might want to get a second opinion on that, first. It works for me, though.
