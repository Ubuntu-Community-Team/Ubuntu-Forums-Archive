### Post by yonkman on 2007-10-05
I am on gutsy and am watching my CAPS kick some thrasher A@#$% right now.


I am amd64, but am using firefox32 for flash.  I also have the mediaplayerconnectivity extension for firefox and have configued it to call kmplayer when opening the video.  I also use the codecs from mediabuntu

I can tell you from streaming all the NHL comcast games last year that you have to have to be patient.

There are multiple streams that come through so you have to pick the right one.  Also, the trial NHL center ice online times your session so if you try to connect to the stream late then it will time you out.

I would love if there was an easy way to play this stuff in linux because the Win version is SOOOOOO sweet ( watch 4 games at a time, mute/ unmute, etc........)

Hope that helps.
