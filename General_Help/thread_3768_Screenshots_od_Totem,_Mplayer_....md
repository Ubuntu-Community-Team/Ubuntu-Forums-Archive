### Post by paul.hendrick on 2004-11-09
You can do it with Totem by using the Edit->Screenshot menu. But that'll just grab the video, not the whole desktop.
Try switching video output to Xshm, then take the screenshot. if you're using totem-xine, you'll have to edit ~/.gnome2/totem_config so that the video driver is Xshm
