### Post by helpme on 2006-04-27
As others already mentioned, if by schedule you mean running a program repeatedly, for example every day at a given time, or every week, etc., cron is what you are looking for.

Take a look at this nice howto, it should give you all the information you need:
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=102626](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=102626)

Of course there are also graphical frontends that make working with cron easier.
I haven't really tried any, so take what I say with a grain of salt, but there's a package called gcrontab in the repos and here's a nice howto on installing an other frontend:
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=93611](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=93611)

Hope this helps.
