### Post by srunni on 2007-06-01
Hi, I'm running Kubuntu Feisty, and I just installed Beryl. One of the things that has really been bugging me is how whenever i mouse over a window's corresponding rectangle in the taskbar, I get two tooltips - one from KDE, and one from Beryl. I only want the one from Beryl, but I can't seem to figure out how to turn off the KDE tooltip. Does anyone know how I can turn it off? A screenshot is attached displaying the problem.

Thanks in advance for the help!

---

### Post by EndPerform on 2007-06-01
Right click on the panel and select Configure Panel.  From there, select Appearance, uncheck Show Tooltips, click apply and you're done :)

---

### Post by srunni on 2007-06-01
Unchecking "Show Tooltips" didn't work, but unchecking the option next to it - "Enable icon mouseover effects" - did. Thanks for the help!
