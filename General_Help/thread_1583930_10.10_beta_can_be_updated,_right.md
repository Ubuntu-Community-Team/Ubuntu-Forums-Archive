Talking of upgrades in Maverick, **do not** do a partial upgrade if Update Manager offers this. More here:

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1479146](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1479146)

While Maverick is still in the testing phase, use apt-get or aptitude from the terminal to update. It's safer.
