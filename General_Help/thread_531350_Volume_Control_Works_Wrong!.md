### Post by Dave54 on 2007-08-21
So, you're saying that ubuntu can't change the volume of your main speakers?

Double-click the speaker icon, click "File > Change Device", select a different source and raise the bars. If that doesn't work, click "Edit > Preference", check off every track, then raise every bar. Repeat for each device.
