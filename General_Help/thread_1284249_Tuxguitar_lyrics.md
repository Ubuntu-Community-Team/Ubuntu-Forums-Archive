### Post by StuartN on 2009-10-06
After a little experimentation, the "proper" Unix answer works - enter a non-breaking space. On my keyboard I can use either AltGr-Space (the right alt key and space) or Ctrl-Shift-U 00A0 (press control and shift and u, then type 00a0).
