### Post by ajgreeny on 2014-01-08
You could try opening it in Libreoffice then exporting again to pdf.

Using that I have in the past managed to get a 1.9MB pdf file, produced many years ago using Serif Page-Plus, down to 63Kb, a huge reduction in file size.  The only problem I see is some font replacements, but the overall look of the document is just as good as the original.
