### Post by nodrogkooc on 2009-07-01
OK for those of you having problems with firefox 2.0/3.0 and 3.5 you can quickly do this.
Select Tools > Add ons > Get Add-ons > Get Adblock Plus (I think 1.0.2 is latest)
Install and restart firefox.

Go to [https://www.national-lottery.co.uk/](https://www.national-lottery.co.uk/) and login
Go to Play Games > Lotto
Window pops up and then "daft" bloke swings across your screen..........great hey!!! 
you are now knackered......:icon_frown:
Not for long....

Select ABP  (RH corner of search bar) > select "Open Blockable Items"
Search on "swf" > should be 4 in list > select [https://www.national-lottery.co.uk/player/g/flash/c171/c171.swf](https://www.national-lottery.co.uk/player/g/flash/c171/c171.swf) > Right click "Block This Item" > Select "Add filter"
Close add blocker > refresh screen, no bloke swinging across your page.....sorted!!!
