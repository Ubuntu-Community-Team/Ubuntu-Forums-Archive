### Post by caljohnsmith on 2008-08-03
Since you're new to Linux, probably what you are experiencing is when you are asked for your password (when you use sudo/gksudo or similar), the terminal will not show any ******** or similar as you type your password. Just type your password, trust that the terminal is listening :), and then hit enter when done entering your password.
