### Post by Mark Phelps on 2010-10-31
First of all, what make & model is your video card?  If you don't know for sure, open a terminal, enter "lspci | grep VGA" -- that will show the make and model of your card.  Post that info back here.

If you're running one of the LEGACY ATI cards, and you're connecting your TV through a DVI port -- you may simply be out of luck.  The open-source drivers, which are the only ones you can use now with those cards, may simply not work well through the DVI port.

What do you see when you do System --> Preferences --> Monitors?
