### Post by CA_Warren on 2008-06-09
The google-calendar plugin for opensync, I'm pretty sure, isn't supposed to support recurring events yet.

That said, there's been a [patch]("http://www.opensync.org/ticket/473") put out there for giving it support, but it's probably not extensively tested. Use at your own risk.

Best of luck! If you have more problems, the OpenSync community's pretty active in addressing bug reports over on [their site]("http://www.opensync.org/").
