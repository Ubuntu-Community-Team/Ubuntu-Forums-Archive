### Post by Tamlynmac on 2009-08-14
Just a thought:

You say the data on the 8.04 cd is OK. Can you run 8.04 from the live cd without installing? If so, it may be a video driver, xconfig issue. The new version may be loading the incorrect driver.

If you can run any of the new versions, you should be able to view your installed 8.04 xorgconfig  on your HDD (file system/etc/x11/xorg.conf). That may help to identify any video problems.
