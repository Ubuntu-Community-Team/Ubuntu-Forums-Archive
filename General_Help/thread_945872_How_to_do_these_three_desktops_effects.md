### Post by armageddon08 on 2008-10-13
> **jfinner1 said:**
> 1. The multi-colored glittery closing. It kinda looks like Burn, but it's prettier, so I'm not sure.

Go to Compizconfig-settings-manager-> Animations->Effect Settings. Under the option Fire check Randomly Coloured Fire particles. Under Fire Direction select Up. For the maximize/minimize effect select MagicLamp Effect and tweak it.

And yes, that's my favourite Ubuntu video.
