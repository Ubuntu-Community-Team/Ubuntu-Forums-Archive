### Post by Leigh on 2006-03-03
Are you talking about [gdesklets]("http://gdesklets.gnomedesktop.org/")? These are the little utilities that sit on your desktop and give you a variety of information. To install these, from the menu click **System->Administratrion->Synaptic Package Manger**. Do a search for *gdesklets* and install them.

### Post by jdong on 2006-03-03
There is also a built-in weather forecast applet for the GNOME panel. Right click some empty space on the top panel (the one that contains your menu and system tray), choose Add To Panel, then select Weather Report.
