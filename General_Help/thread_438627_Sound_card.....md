### Post by strabes on 2007-05-10
I don't think you're going to find a 5.1 sound card (internal or external) for $15-$30. One that I have heard good things about and apparently works well with alsa is the [Creative SoundBlaster Audigy NX 2](http://www.soundblaster.com/products/product.asp?category=1&subcategory=204&product=9103)

You should have gotten speakers with Dolby Pro Logic II which decodes stereo tracks into 5.1 channels so music comes through all speakers instead of just two. I have the Logitech Z-5500s which have this feature and they are amazing, especially for the money. They're only $50-$70 more than the ones you already have so you could trade them in or something... The sound quality is GREAT, even through analog from my terrible onboard notebook sound.
