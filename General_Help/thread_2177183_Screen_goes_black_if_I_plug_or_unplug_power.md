### Post by Kirk Schnable on 2013-09-27
When you plug/unplug your laptop, depending on your settings, the software might try to adjust your screen brightness. Perhaps there's a quirk in your situation causing this symptom when that is done.  (Misconfiguration where you've inadvertently set your brightness to 0, driver problem, etc.)

Please check in your Power Settings, and try disabling the screen brightness changes on power events (or setting brightness levels equal on both power states).
