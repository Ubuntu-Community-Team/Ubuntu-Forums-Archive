### Post by ajgreeny on 2010-11-28
Next time you login, choose the username from the list, but before you enter your password, choose **Ubuntu-desktop** from the session menu, bottom right.  Now the system will have a standard gnome desktop, and will keep using that unless you choose netbook from the session menu again.

If you have the system setup to auto-login, you simply need to logout, and then login again to get to the login screen with the session menu.
