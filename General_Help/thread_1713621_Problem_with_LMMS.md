### Post by neu2buntu on 2011-04-23
this was a bug in older versions of LMMS , in that lmms was using the systems font color and a white background. In your case you are using a dark desktop theme which has white fonts. 
the 2 options you have are either update lmms to the latest version (0.4.10) or edit your desktop fonts in your theme goto >System >Preferences >Appearance >theme(tab) >customise >colours >tooltips - text, and change this to any color that shows up against white ;)
