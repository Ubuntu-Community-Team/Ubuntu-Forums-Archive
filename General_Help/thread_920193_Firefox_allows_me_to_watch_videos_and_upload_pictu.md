### Post by Robin T Cox on 2008-09-16
If you have got the relevant plugins for your videos etc. to work in FF, you use those same plugins in Opera. I assume that you are using Opera 9.52.

Use the Tools menu and go to Tools>Preferences>Advanced>Content. Check 'Enable plugins', and click 'Plugin options'. There you can use 'Find new' to detect your FF plugins, if they are not listed already. Or use 'Change path' to point to the location where your FF plugins are stored, so that Opera knows where to find them.
