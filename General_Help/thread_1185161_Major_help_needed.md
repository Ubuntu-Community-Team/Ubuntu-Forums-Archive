### Post by LepeKaname on 2009-06-12
Maybe you have compiz enabled? If so, disable it. I think with the graphic card may be enough depending of what kind of software you use. 2GB is more than enough. I highly recommend you to use 32bit version rather than 64bits. There is no much difference in performance and the 32bit is more stable. 

As for the 8Gb issue, the 32bits version can handle 8Gb but not in a native way as the 64bit version.

### Post by ramjet_1953 on 2009-06-12
Your problem may be associated with two issues.

1. You do not have the proper video driver loaded.

2. There is something going on in the background, using lots of CPU time.


You could try opening a terminal and typing in the [COLOR="Red"]top[/COLOR] command, to see if something is chewing-up processor time.
