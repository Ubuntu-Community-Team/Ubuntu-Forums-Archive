### Post by ltmon on 2005-11-22
I don't know if there are any phones that are by definition easy, but that may change before Dapper is released.  See this: [http://www.kdedevelopers.org/node/1635](http://www.kdedevelopers.org/node/1635)

If you want to have a chance of synchronizing right here and now make sure you get a phone that supports the IRMCSync protocol.  My SonyEriccson K700i does, and eventually I got it to sync a couple of times.  Some of my trials and tribulations are described here: [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=39478](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=39478)
