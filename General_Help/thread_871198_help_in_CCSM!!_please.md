### Post by mc4man on 2008-07-26
To rotate the cube enable 'rotate cube' in the ccsm setting. 
To add more workspaces r.click  workspace box in lower panel -> preferences and add columns (up to 16)
alt+ctrl+down will flatten cube, alt+ctrl+L or R will slide spaces 
I prefer mouse scrollwheel to 'flip' spaces (mouse cursor must be on desktop, ie. not good for maximized windows, use alt+ctrl+ L or R in those cases
