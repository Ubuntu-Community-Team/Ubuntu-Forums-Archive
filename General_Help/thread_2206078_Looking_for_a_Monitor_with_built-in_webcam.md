### Post by mmf2 on 2014-02-17
Might I ask why you want a monitor with a built in webcam? I find this to be a bad idea for two different reasons:

1. If your webcam, for some reason, needs to go to the warranty, you'll also lose your monitor.

2. The webcam will, probably/possibly, eventually, become obsolete (much better quality ones will be available) while your monitor still functions perfectly.
