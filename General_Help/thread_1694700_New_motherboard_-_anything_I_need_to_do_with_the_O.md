### Post by Hakunka-Matata on 2011-02-24
Check under "System > Administration > Additional drivers" to see if any other drivers are available, usually this is in reference to Video drivers.  However,if everything seems to be working, there is no real need to look further.  Ubuntu is very forgiving that way, you can take the hard drive out of one machine and stick it into another, and usually it works straight away.  That being said, sometimes you'll find problems with Video drivers, or Wireless cards, but if it's working, it's working.  Good Job.
