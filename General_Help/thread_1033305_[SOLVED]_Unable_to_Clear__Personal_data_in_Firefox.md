### Post by adamlau on 2009-01-07
I am assuming you set your Private Data settings to clear Browsing History. Keypress:

```
Ctrl-H
```

Is a history list available? You may be referring to the Location bar drop down menu. Those entries can be manually deleted. The drop down menu can disabled entirely as well.
