### Post by Irihapeti on 2019-11-26
The Thunderbird calendar extension is Lightning. Is this installed? If so, it should show up in your list of add-ons.

If you don't have it, you can get Lightning from addons.thunderbird.net, or install it (xul-ext-lightning) from the repositories.
