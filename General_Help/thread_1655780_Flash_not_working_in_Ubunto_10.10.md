### Post by judedawson on 2010-12-30
Hi vikrantalmelkar,

You will need to install a Flash plugin for it to work.

You can download Adobe Flash from the Ubuntu Software Centre. Click on 'Applications' and select 'Ubuntu Software Centre'. Just type 'adobe flash plugin 10' in the search window of Software Centre and download the app. 

Since you've only recently installed 10.10,  you may want to also download the 'ubuntu restricted extras' as well. You will also find this in the Software Centre. Alternatively, you can visit Medibuntu & read more about 'restricted' packages at [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Medibuntu](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Medibuntu)

Enjoy!

From,
