Use [Scanmodem](http://www.linuxant.com/drivers/modemident.php) to tell you what version of Conexant modem you have.

Then download the .deb driver from the same site for your version.Install instructions are also given there.

Then read [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=190728](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=190728)
