### Post by dj-toonz on 2006-04-02
managed to get the http: & https: links working in Ubuntu :-( a quick scan of these forums here helped me out , a quick trip into the config editor for thunderbird solved it, the network.protocol-handler.app.http & https were setup for www-broswer instead of being setup for firefox, so a quick change to firefox and working again, now i can use all the links in all me programs i.e openoffice,thunderbird,gedit & the rest again , just gonna remember to write down what i need to do if i ever have to reinstall Ubuntu again lol
