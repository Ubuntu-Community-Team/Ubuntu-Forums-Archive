### Post by lithopsian on 2011-05-13
You have a separate /home partition?  How big?

Find out what is using the space.  Might be trash.  Might be email.  Might be thumbnails.  Might be browser cache.  Most of those you can clean up to give you some breathing room.

Then pick a long term solution.  Make /home bigger by grabbing space from another partition?   Use gparted from a Live CD.

Move some stuff from /home to elsewhere.  The place you are storing photos maybe?  Are you downloading stuff like music or torrents under /home?  Put them somewhere else.

Maybe restrict something like browser cache.  Or move it elsewhere.
