### Post by husnos on 2012-05-26
In my BIOS settings, I've always had the 'on battery' option set on 'power saving.' Today, I changed it, for the heck of it, to 'full power,' and noticed that the scrolling in the dash is smoother and more responsive. I always have my laptop connected to AC. I don't know how changing the 'on battery' setting affected the 'on AC' performance, but it works.
