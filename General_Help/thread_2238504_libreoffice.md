### Post by DogMatix on 2014-08-09
I use Libre Office day to day in my work, often communications with publishing companies. Compatability between MS Office and Libre Office is not perfect although in my experience it is generally improving. I'll agree simple formatting is usually OK, more complex formatting does throw up inconsistancies, most noticably for me, anchored text boxes and objects in Word documents opened in Libre Office are often out of place or the content is missing. I haven't had any issues, as yet, reported to me from people I send Libre Office documents too, saved in MS formats. The issues seem to be mine, when opening native MS office documents in Libre Office.

If I think that maybe I am missing some content I'll ask them to send me a PDF version of the document. That usually shows everything as it should be. Although, obviously, its not editable in the way an office document is.
