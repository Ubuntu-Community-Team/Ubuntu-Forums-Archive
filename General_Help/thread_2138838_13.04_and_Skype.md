### Post by Lars Noodén on 2013-04-25
Yes, Skype is probably going away.

What you are looking for is SIP.  There are a number of good SIP clients available.  Ekiga, Jitsi, and Blink are the first three I can think of.  Jitsi is probably the best right now.  There is also Linphone and Twinkle, but I haven't tried them.  

You will need a (free) SIP account and then you can call other people.  The sound quality is much better than what I remember from Skype.
