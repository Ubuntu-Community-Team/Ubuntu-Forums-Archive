### Post by floke on 2007-01-10
As far as I know you just go System/.Admninistration/Printing (using Gnome here), select the 'New Printer' option, and click through the menus with your model. With a HP all the drivers should be there already installed and it should 'just work' once you do this!

One problem I had with a HP printer on a desktop, though, was solved by my switching the old 'pin-plug' cable lead thing with a USB2 lead. Ubuntu picked it up straight away with no probs.
