### Post by macaholic on 2008-05-11
I don't know about a grey human theme, you could porbably find something after searching around for a while. However for the icons from the leopard theme, you isntall the entire theme and choose its icons under the appearance preferences. As far as a custom ubutnu logo, you can look around in /usr/share/icons/<theme name> for the right logo, if it is a link to a file, just change the original file to the icon you want. As long as that theme is selected in the settings, it should load.
