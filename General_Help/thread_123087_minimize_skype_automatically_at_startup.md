### Post by KillerBOB on 2006-01-29
Hi! To make Skype start minimized you just have to Skype's "Options" dialog. I.e, bring up Skype main window and "Tools" - "Options..".
There you have the option that is called "Do not show main window at startup", mark that box and you're set.

### Post by jordon on 2007-06-23
> **chrismcollins said:**
> Hello,
does anyone know if it's possible to do the same thing with other programs? like rhythmbox for example? Like so that it just goes to the taskbar and doesn't show a window?

Minimizing to the notification area is a feature of some programs, such as Rhythmbox. When you run Rhythmbox, an icon appears in the notification area. Just click the icon and the window will be hidden (and your music will continue to play). It's also an option in Gaim (and, presumably, Pidgin, although I haven't upgraded).

I minimize Thunderbird to the tray with the New Mail Icon extension: [http://moztraybiff.mozdev.org/](http://moztraybiff.mozdev.org/)

As for other programs, alltray (available in the repositories) is often suggested because you can use it to minimize any program to the notification area, but I can't vouch for it myself.
