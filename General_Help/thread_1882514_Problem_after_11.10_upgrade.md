### Post by Tjofalt on 2011-11-17
You are not providing much info, but I had similar and even more and stranger problems after upgrading from 11.04 to 11.10.

After too many hours of trying to fix things, what I did was did a backup of my own stuff (Documents, Pictures, keyrings, chromium and thunderbird-directories, and more) and did a complete reinstallation. Do not re-use the old users home directories, since this also seem to cause problems.
