### Post by john newbuntu on 2010-06-25
If you have a dual boot system, does the same problem persist in the other OS?

I doubt if any of the recent updates would have caused a fan to stop.  However, I suggest keeping your case open and then running your OS to see which of the fan stops working - the case fan, PSU fan or the CPU fan.  

Do the same with just mounting a liveCD and running it.  Confirm if it is the same issue.

If its a problem with a fan, have it replaced.
