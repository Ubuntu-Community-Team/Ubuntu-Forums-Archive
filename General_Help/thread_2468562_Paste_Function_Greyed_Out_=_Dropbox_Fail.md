### Post by Kurt_Alan on 2021-11-05
**OP: PROBLEM SOLVED! It was a long shot but from terminal I upgraded to the next iteration, Hirsute Hippo, and my problem disappeared! I guess that whatever was corrupted was replaced. So now we have another tool in our toolbox. This solution saved me many hours of re-install and re-config. **
