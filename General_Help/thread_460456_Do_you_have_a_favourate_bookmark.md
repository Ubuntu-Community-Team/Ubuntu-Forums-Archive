### Post by Shazaam on 2007-05-31
If you have access to Windows from Ubuntu...
Start Windows. Open Firefox. Click "Bookmarks", click "Manage Bookmarks". Click "File", scroll down to "Export, click "Export". It will ask you where to export to, choose "Desktop". Restart pc then log into Ubuntu.
Find and open your Windows partition in Ubuntu. Go to "C:/DocumentsandSettings/YOUR USER NAME/Desktop.
There you will find a html file called "Bookmarks" Copy and paste (or drag&drop) that html file to your Ubuntu desktop.
Open Firefox in Ubuntu. Once again go to "Bookmarks", "Manage Bookmarks", "File". Scroll down to Import and click "Import". When it asks where to import from choose "File" and choose "Desktop". Your done.
