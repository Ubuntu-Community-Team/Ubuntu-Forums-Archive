### Post by 0016 on 2008-11-19
This is what I do:

Click on Insert at the menu bar
Then go to the footer menu and there should be an option for default.

Click in the footer box at bottom of page
Go back and select Insert menu
Then go to the Field Menu and you should see a option that says page number.

I hope this works!
