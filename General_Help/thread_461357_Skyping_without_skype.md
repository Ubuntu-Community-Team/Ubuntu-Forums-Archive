### Post by bmartin on 2007-06-01
Skype is proprietary; AFAIK there is no way to connect to Skype other than through Skype's official client. The Linux version doesn't support many features, such as video. There are alternatives, but if your family's using Skype, you're going to have to use Skype to connect to them. I've heard good things about Open Wengo, and there may be other programs out there that have voice/video.
