### Post by DZ* on 2010-04-08
> **hakermania said:**
> I use aMSN and it not seems to be available for this job. The video is not playing at all!

Can you suggest an other messenger or an online service that makes this possible?

If you have a gmail account, you can use it in Pidgin to video chat. It works great for two people with gmail that are both running Pidgin under Linux. I video chat with my wife this way during the day. It also works with Emphathy but in my experience, video quality is better with Pidgin.

I also video chat with a Windows-using buddy. He is using this stuff -- [http://www.google.com/chat/video](http://www.google.com/chat/video) on his end, while I'm using Pidgin under linux. It also works Ok. There is a bit of video delay perhaps due to the fact that he is 8 time zones away.

Edit: it doesn't have to be gmail -- video chat over a XMPP account should work, e.g. from jabber.org
