### Post by coffeecat on 2019-04-01
Assuming you are using paste.ubuntu.com, after you've pasted your text with the paste button simply copy the URL in the address bar and include that in your post. If you paste the URL into your post, the forum software automatically renders that as a clickable hyperlink.
