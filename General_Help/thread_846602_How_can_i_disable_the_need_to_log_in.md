### Post by sancho panza on 2008-07-01
I'm not sure about Kubuntu, but in Ubuntu, one can enable automatic login for specific users via the login prefs under system>administration.

Look for something similar in Kubuntu. And just in case you do not know, enabling automatic login is strictly NOT RECOMMENDED unless you are the only person having physical access to your machine at all times.
