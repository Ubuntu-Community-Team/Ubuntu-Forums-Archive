Running 32 bit Edgy on a 2 GHz Turion powered laptop with ATI X700 graphics. Using the fglrx driver.
I installed xgl/beryl to play with the eyecandy. Its sweet.
When I logged out of the xgl session, X server crashed big time, screen grew bright white; only way to get out of it was the power button.
After restart, I logged into a normal gnome session; upon logging out, the same white screen appeared. Prior to the crash logging out of xgl, I could logout and back in anytime.
Thats just weird. Workaround that I don't understand. I booted and chose Gnome desktop, instead of letting it default there. Poof, now I can login and logout without X crashing.
Edgy means edgy, and I do enjoy it. But this is just weird.
