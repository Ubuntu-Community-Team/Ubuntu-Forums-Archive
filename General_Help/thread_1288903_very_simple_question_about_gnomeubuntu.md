### Post by CatKiller on 2009-10-11
Ubuntu comes with Gnome. Kubuntu comes with KDE. Xubuntu comes with Xfce. You can chop-and-change as much as you like (installing KDE within Ubuntu for example) and they are all **very** customisable. I suggest you take that live cd for a spin.
