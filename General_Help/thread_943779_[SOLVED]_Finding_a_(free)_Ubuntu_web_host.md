### Post by cilkay on 2008-10-11
[We provide hosting services]("http://dinamis.com") using Debian, Ubuntu, CentOS, and Mandriva but it's not free. Unless you are going to be using a VPS (Virtual Private Server), in which case you would have root and be able to administer the machine yourself, it would make no difference which distribution your hosting provider uses. You just need some way of transferring files to the web root on the hosting provider's machine and you'll be all set.

By the way, it is important to understand [why Apache market share went down and IIS market share went up]("http://apache.slashdot.org/article.pl?sid=06/06/08/1444205") in the graph above.
