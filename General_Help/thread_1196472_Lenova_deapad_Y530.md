### Post by michaelzap on 2009-06-27
I just got one of these (Core 2 Duo, 4 GB RAM, 320 GB HD) for $550 from Lenovo directly (using a coupon code that I got from [TechDealDigger.com]("http://www.techdealdigger.com/")). It's a beautiful laptop, and I highly recommend it. The very first thing that I did was shrink the Vista partition and install 64-bit Jaunty, and I haven't done anything at all in Vista except uninstall the (minimal) bloatware.

For the most part, everything works perfectly in Jaunty. I had to do a minor Alsa tweak to get 5.1 sound, and I still have an intermittent screen flicker that I haven't resolved. Suspend doesn't work for me either, but I haven't spent a whole lot of time trying to fix that because it's not essential to me.

My feeling is that everything important works right out of the box, and what doesn't will probably be resolved by an update or me putting a little more effort into figuring out why.

I'm writing this on it now, and I just finished packing up my desktop computer to donate to someone else!
