### Post by scxtt on 2007-08-02
if you're new to linux, there's no reason to use Gentoo - especially if you have no real foundation to build off of ... you have to know your own box well enough to use Gentoo in addition to linux in general ...

that's no knock against Gentoo, it's a great (great!) distro - and even the LiveCD will give you a great approximation of how well your h/w is supported -  but it's up to you to make use of the *power* Gentoo gives you -- if you're not ready for it, you're just not ...

i don't think it's in the scope of a distro like Ubuntu (or any "we provide binary packages" distro) to also provide something like the Portage Tree - just doesn't make sense ...
