### Post by grahammechanical on 2011-03-05
Wow! You have set yourself a challenge. Do you have another computer? Why not take the hard disc out of the Thinkpad and connect it to another computer and install Linux on it that way. It will need to be a version of Linux that runs on your machine's specification. You will need to make sure that grub is installed on the Thinkpad hard disc. When you put the drive back into the Thinkpad you will get all sorts of errors but you might get a command line interface and you might be able to upgrade things 
over the Internet.

Regards.

P.S. Keep a record of what you are doing. If it works, then submit as an article to Full Circle magazine.
