### Post by tomauty on 2007-12-09
Make sure Place Windows is checked and check Workarounds. If that doesn't fix it change it from Smart to something like Centered.

Also even though this should fix it, just so you know, you can always hold alt and drag the window to move it regardless of mouse position.
