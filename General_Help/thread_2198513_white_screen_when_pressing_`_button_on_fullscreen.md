### Post by Impavidus on 2014-01-09
Before pressing the power button, try [alt+SysRq+REISUB](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Magic_SysRq_key#Uses). SysRq is often on the same key as PrintScreen, but may be somewhere else. You can also try and get to the console and kill the video player from there, but this may not be easy if you don't know your way around in the terminal.

I've never seen an issue like that. What kind of video is it? I assume it's played in a browser. Firefox, Chrome, something else? Flash or HTML5?
