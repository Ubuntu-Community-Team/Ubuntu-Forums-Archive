### Post by d2globalinc on 2009-04-22
They are not for the same use.. Gnome-Do doesn't index ALL of your files, where thats what google desktop does and is made for - Gnome-DO is more of a main/menu / start panel replacement - where you can easily access your apps, and web content by using phrases and keywords.  Google desktop is used for searching your data, etc

- In fact, what is needed is a combination - a plugin for gnome-do for google desktop..

However, - google desktop does not seem to be so popular in the linux community mostly because ubuntu, kubuntu, etc - all come with their own file indexing apps... 

Just some thoughts ;) - so I would get rid of this poll and highlight these apps differences rather than comparing the 2.
