### Post by macaholic on 2008-05-03
The cause of a crash is usually hard to diagnose as usualyl simple things like firefox don't cause system crashes like that. Depending on what type of crash you experience there are some options, for example if the graphics just freeze you can always drop to a tty terminal with ctrl + alt + F#, but in the crash you are describing that might not work. As far as "force-quitting", when you force your system down, it will send kill commands to all processes, unless they are doing something active with memory, there should be little to no ill effects.
