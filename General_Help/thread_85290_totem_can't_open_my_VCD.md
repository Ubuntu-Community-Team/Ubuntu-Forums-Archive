### Post by frodon on 2005-12-09
Darren, totem works really nice, you don't need mplayer except if you really like it. All you have to do is to install the codecs.
First as many of us here i advice to install the totem-xine packages, and here is a list of popular needed codecs : w32codecs, libdvdcss2, gstreamer0.8-plugins, gstreamer0.8-lame, gstreamer0.8-ffmpeg, gstreamer0.8-faac, gstreamer0.8-faad, libxvidcore4, lame, sox, ffmpeg, mjpegtools, vorbis-tools, mpg321
