### Post by yetiman64 on 2010-06-04
Try clicking on,  System > Preferences > Main Menu. In the window opened select "Programming" in the left panel. Then in the right panel select your entries and use the "move up" or "move down" buttons on the far right of the window to organize your entries. Good Luck.

Edit: just realized you want to sub group your entries, this can also be done via this window, by select New menu under Programming, then adding new entries to the sub menu and using the properties button to copy the details from the old entries to the new, then if they work ok delete the old entries directly under Programming.
