### Post by bonzodog on 2007-06-30
Trust me, you *are* typing the password, it's simply the way the bash shell works; passwords are invisible when being typed.

So, you need to just type the password, and hit enter - you just won't see the password as it is being typed. There is no current way of changing this as it's part of the core structure of the bash shell.
