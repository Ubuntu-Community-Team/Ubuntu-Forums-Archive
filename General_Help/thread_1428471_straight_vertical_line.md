### Post by The Cog on 2010-03-12
It's unicide 007C. You can probably enter it with Ctrl-Shift-U, 7, C, Enter. If not, use Applications->Accessories->Character Map, find it near the end of the basic latin code page, double-click it and then copy/paste it.

On a normal US keyboard, it's right near the Enter key and you don't need to mess around with key maps etc, though.
