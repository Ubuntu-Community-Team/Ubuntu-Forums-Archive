### Post by mcduck on 2009-08-17
Sounds like you have removed the Notification Area-applet from your panel..

Just right-click in empty space in your panel, select "Add to Panel", find the Notification Area-applet and drag&drop it into your panel.

---

### Post by supremedalek on 2009-08-17
mcduck, you were right on the money: Notification Area-applet had left the building. I put it back and life was good once more.
