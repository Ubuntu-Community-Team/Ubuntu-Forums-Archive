### Post by quixote on 2009-05-17
Is it maybe trying to mount the camera twice for some reason?  Then the second attempt would give you the error message about not mounting (since it's already mounted).  Have you tried right-clicking on the second icon, and see if there's a choice to unmount?  Alternatively, what happens if you just double-click the first icon?  Maybe that's a successful mount, in which case you could get your pictures, at least.

Don't just yank the cable or card out when you're done.  Right-click on the icon and unmount.  Otherwise the memory card can get damaged or lose data.  But you probably knew that, right?
