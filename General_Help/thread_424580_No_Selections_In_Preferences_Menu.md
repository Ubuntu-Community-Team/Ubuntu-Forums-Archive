### Post by rjpilla on 2007-04-26
I just activated the compiz desktop effects in Ubuntu 7.04. Everything so far seems to be working fine except when I go under system -> preferences -> that menu panel comes up white and no selections are visible. However, placing the mouse in the panel highlights the selections that are there. Its like the foreground and background colors of the menu panel are both set to white. All other menu selections in other various categories are visible. Any ideas?

### Post by rjpilla on 2007-04-27
Well found the answer to the problem. Have to go into Alacarte uncheck everything under the preference category and then recheck everything again and all menu selections come back.
