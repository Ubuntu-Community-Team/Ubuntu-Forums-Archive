### Post by jonny on 2005-10-08
Audio players aren't good or bad - they're appropriate or inappropriate.  And to know what player might be appropriate, you need to give some more clues about what's important to you.  Apart from the players you list, you could try Quod Libet or Banshee.  If you like XMMS (I loathe its idiosyncratic design and tacky skins), try Beep Media Player.

I value simplicity and like my music player to harmonise with my desktop.  For me, Quod Libet is just perfect.  You might think otherwise, of course.
