### Post by louis_nichols on 2006-10-22
"Better" is always a matter of perspective.

I like it because it uses much less resources than superkaramba and because it will work on almost any desktop environment and look and the same in all.

The downside is that it's not as easy to use. It's not very difficult, either. It has an automated desklet downloader and installer and starting desklets involves just a command in terminal. Then, you can register the desklets you want to be started at login and you just put *adesklets --nautilus* in Preferences>Sessions>Startup pograms.
