### Post by shafin on 2007-12-09
Exit uTorrent through right click. Go to /home/**usename**/.wine/drive_c/windows/profiles/**username**/Application Data/uTorrent and delete the files settings.dat and steeings.dat.old. Start uTorrent, go to options and close hide to tray and minimize to tray.
