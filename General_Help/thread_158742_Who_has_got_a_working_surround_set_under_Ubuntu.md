### Post by Jason_25 on 2006-04-11
I don't know about speaker sets, but the best way for movie surround is to use spdif output with ac3 passthrough to a dolby digital receiver.  If you want 5.1 more for gaming, many of the nforce2 boards with soundstorm audio can encode all audio into the spdif output, mimicking an analog 5.1 speaker set.
