### Post by drs305 on 2008-08-06
I just did it in Hardy. I **selected** the partition I wanted to copy and clicked on *Copy*. Then I selected/clicked on an empty/unallocated space on the drive to highlight it. Finally, I selected Partition > Paste and then 'Apply and it copied the old partition to the unallocated space. Of course, the empty space has to be larger than the partition you are trying to copy. 

**Added**: 
It appears to work on copying to an existing partition as well - same procedure.
