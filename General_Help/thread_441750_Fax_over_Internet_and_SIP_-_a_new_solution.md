### Post by bcalder01 on 2007-05-12
Hi all,
I am using a Sipura SPA3000 to connect to my ITSP & save on long distance. I recently had need to send a fax, and thought, "Gee, I oughtta be able to use my VOIP line to do that!!" After a lot of research, hair tearing, etc, I found that if your ITSP doesn't support H323, you're SOL as far as what ships with Ubuntu (I'm using Edgy).

However, I came across a company, [Uniqall]("http://www.uniqall.com"), who makes a server, Gridborg, that works with not only H323, but SIP & I think MGCP as well. The pricing is attractive, the system requirements are modest and the documentation looks good.

Has anyone tried Gridborg Server? Are there other equivalent solutions out there (or in Ubuntu) that I've missed?
