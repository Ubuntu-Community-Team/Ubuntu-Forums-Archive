### Post by Nokkis on 2008-07-21
Exactly what does fwcutter do to the firmware? Does it extract it from a device or replace it somehow...? My WLAN card is an Asus WL-138g v2 and I'd like to know if i can still use it on a XP machine after running the driver install process in Hardy.

---

### Post by Tim Sharitt on 2008-07-21
It does nothing to your card. It extracts the firmware from a windows driver to be used with the linux driver. It will not have any adverse effects on your card.
