This apparently a known bug with bolded fonts in the terminal.

[https://bugs.kde.org/show_bug.cgi?id=226308](https://bugs.kde.org/show_bug.cgi?id=226308)

Workaround on Yakuake is to disable the embolding of fonts. Marking as solved because nothing more to be done.
