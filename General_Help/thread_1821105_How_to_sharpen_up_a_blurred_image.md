### Post by halibaitor on 2011-08-08
As far as sharpening goes, there is no way to put detail into an image where none currently exists.  Edges can be sharpened, and perceived sharpness can be enhanced, but that's about it... 

Your most powerful sharpening option in both PhotoShop and Gimp is the one called _unsharp mask_.  Strange name, I know.  It takes some playing to determine how sharp you can make an image without introducing artifacts, and that depends mostly on how much "noise" is in the image.

Everybody wants the kind of enhancement that is shown in the CSI type of show, but that simply isn't possible.  Sorry.  (It is a good pipe dream however.)
