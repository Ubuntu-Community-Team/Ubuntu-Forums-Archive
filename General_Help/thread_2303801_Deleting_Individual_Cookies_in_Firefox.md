### Post by brian-mccumber on 2015-11-21
[FONT=arial]**Firefox**[/FONT]
[FONT=arial]
[LIST]
[*]To manage cookie settings, from the drop-down menu under "History", select Use custom settings for history. ...
[*]To view or remove individual cookies, click remove individual cookies.
[*]To remove all cookies, from the History menu, select clear your recent history.
[/LIST]
[/FONT]

### Post by ajgreeny on 2015-11-21
I use the **[cookie-culler]("https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/cookieculler/?src=ss")** add-on for FF which works well to do exactly what you want.  You can protect any specific cookies you want to keep from the complete list that shows, and use a single button click to remove everything else.

It was last updated in 2009, but as it is just an extension of FF's own cookie management system, it still works brilliantly.
Definitely worth a look-see.
