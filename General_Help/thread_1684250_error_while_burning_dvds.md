### Post by quadproc on 2011-02-08
> **mattgeb84 said:**
>  error message that says something of the effect "power calibration error"
This might indicate a fault in the DVD drive, or it may be that your drive is not designed to do the -R format.  Please check the specs for your drive to see if it can handle the -R format; if not then you will have to use disks designated as +R.

Also, please check the cables to the drive to be sure that they are fully seated.  A poor connection there might cause strange problems.
