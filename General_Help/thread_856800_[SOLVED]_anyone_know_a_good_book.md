### Post by sdennie on 2008-07-11
Unix and Linux are similar enough that it's not unreasonable to consider them the same thing.  The reason I recommend something like Unix Power Tools instead of an Ubuntu Unleashed type of book is that the latter will be out of date quickly.  Unix Power Tools has been in print for over 15 years and, the information is not something you are likely to easily find online whereas Ubuntu specific stuff is quite easy to find online.
