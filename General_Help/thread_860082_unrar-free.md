### Post by Vivaldi Gloria on 2008-07-15
There is unrar in multiverse and unrar-free in universe. Enable those repos in synaptic and do NOT forget to click refresh. Now search and install one of them using synaptic, not add/remove programs.

Synaptic is in the system menu > admin.
