### Post by dodo3773 on 2011-03-02
> **searayman said:**
> hmmm ok i downloaded Nautilus elementary and Its better but not quite there yet



O.K. so now you need to enable breadcrumbs in nautilus elementary ( in nautilus window go to edit > preferences > tweaks > show like breadcrumbs ), you may have to reload nautilus or log out and log in for this to take affect.

 After that download the icon sets. It looks like they are talking about these two.

[http://gnome-look.org/content/show.php/AwOken+-+Awesome+Token+icon+set?content=126344](http://gnome-look.org/content/show.php/AwOken+-+Awesome+Token+icon+set?content=126344)

[http://gnome-look.org/content/show.php/Faenza+Dark+Icons+%28edited%29?content=132327](http://gnome-look.org/content/show.php/Faenza+Dark+Icons+%28edited%29?content=132327)

You should be good to go after that.
