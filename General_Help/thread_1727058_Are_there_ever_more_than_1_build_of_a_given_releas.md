### Post by Elfy on 2011-04-12
The LTS versions get point releases. 8.04 is at 8.04.4 and the latest LTS is at 10.04.2

The others stay the same, if you installed x today and between now and June there were 200Mb of updates, someone installing x in June would have 200Mb of updates to apply after install.
