### Post by cariboo on 2008-07-23
If you don't like extra toolbars (I don't) in firefox go to Tools-->Addons, in the search bar type downloadhelper and hit enter it will bring up Video Download Helper. Click add to firefox and let it install. It will install an icon in the navigation toolbar. Just click it when you want to download a video.
