### Post by Ramses de Norre on 2007-01-06
From linux you can perfectly read ntfs volumes, writing is being developed and already quiet stable but not 100% yet.

For windows: there are drivers available [online](http://www.fs-driver.org/) which can perfectly read/write ext3 partitions.

Fat volumes are perfectly read/writable by both OS's.
