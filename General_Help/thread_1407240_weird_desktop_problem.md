### Post by anoop999 on 2010-02-15
Have you tried moving the icons around?

If you right click on a panel icon it will show you a menu from which you can unselect 'Lock to panel'. Then right click again and select 'Move'. Move the icon to where you want it, and right click again to select 'Lock to panel'.
