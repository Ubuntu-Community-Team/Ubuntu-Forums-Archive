### Post by Omnios on 2006-05-14
Hi as for dial up I helped my dad set up his dial up and he realy had a beef not being able to tell if he was online. Anyways we solved this by using the dial up monito applet. Right click on an emply spot on the pannel where you want your monitor.  Click on add to pannel, Go down to system and hardware and there will be a modem monitor. Also you can connect and disconnect with this applet. There is also another applet called netspeed available in synaptic universe  but will tell you what your connection is running at.
