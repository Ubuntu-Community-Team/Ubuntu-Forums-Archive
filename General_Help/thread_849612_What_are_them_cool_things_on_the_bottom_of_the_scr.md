### Post by sayakb on 2008-07-04
The one in picture looks like a panel with Bigger size, without Expand and with transparency. Right click on a panel and select Properties to adjust these parameters.
You may have a dock instead. A dock looks better that what shown in the image + has smooth animations: [http://news.softpedia.com/news/Install-AWN-on-Hardy-Heron-82611.shtml](http://news.softpedia.com/news/Install-AWN-on-Hardy-Heron-82611.shtml)

Note: AWN Dock (link) needs compiz to run. Make sure you have desktop effects enabled to get it working.
