### Post by rdsnc on 2009-07-07
This does not appear to be an Ubuntu or a Firefox issue.
It's a trojan that changes the DNS entry in your wireless modem/router.
It may be limited to SMC modems/routers.
There's a fix for it here:
[http://forums.techguy.org/web-email/717589-firefox-address-bar-keyword-search.html](http://forums.techguy.org/web-email/717589-firefox-address-bar-keyword-search.html)
