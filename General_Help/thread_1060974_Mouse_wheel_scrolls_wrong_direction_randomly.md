### Post by SteveHillier on 2009-02-11
Are you sure it isn't the mouse itself.
Is it flakey?  If it is optical what surface are you using it on?  On an uneven surface they can do wierd things, remember with opticals the mouse does not have to be in contact with the surface only in close proximity.  ALso if using mouse mats with opticals you can get some very odd behaviour of the sort you have described.
