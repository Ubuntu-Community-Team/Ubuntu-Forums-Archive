### Post by grahammechanical on 2016-05-14
If you are using a HDMI cable to connect to the TV as a monitor and the video adapter provides audio output then that is what happens. Plug an audio cable into the audio card and open the Sound Settings. Then in the output tab you should see an output for the HDMI/DisplayPort 2 as well as any built-in audio outputs that have a cable connected to the port.

With the latest versions of Ubuntu sound inputs and outputs appear in the Sound Settings utility when we plug cables into the ports. They do not appear if no cables are plugged into the ports.
