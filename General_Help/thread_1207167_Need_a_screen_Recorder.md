### Post by Jebtrix on 2009-07-07
Just to clarify, tell it where to save it first and to name the output file. Once you hit Record you will see a Stop icon appear in your Notification toolbar, if you Right-Click on it it will Pause, if you Left-Click on it it will Stop recording and you will see an Encoding Progress Bar as its saved to where ever you told it too.
