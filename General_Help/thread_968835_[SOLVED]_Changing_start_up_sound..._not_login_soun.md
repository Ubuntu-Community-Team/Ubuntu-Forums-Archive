### Post by jac0117 on 2008-11-03
How do I change the start up sound? Not the one after logging in, but the one when the log in screen comes up.  I went to preferences > sounds and there is no option for that.



figured it out:
 Re: disable drum sound at login screen
System/Preferences/Sound won't help. It only configures users personal sound settings.

Open System/Administration/Login Screen Setup, and 'Accessibility'-tab. Then unmark 'Make a sound when login window is ready'. The drum sound is gone
