### Post by Brucevdk on 2007-04-16
Did you remove your profile directory? If not, then the emails should still be there in mbox like format (human readable) in:

```
~/.mozilla-thunderbird/<profile>/Mail
```

You could even access them again by reinstalling Thunderbird.
