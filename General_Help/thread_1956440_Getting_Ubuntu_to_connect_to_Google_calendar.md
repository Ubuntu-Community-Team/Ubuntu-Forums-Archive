### Post by unknown user on 2012-04-10
This is not a request for help, more a way of letting people know about a great little application that I have found.

For a bit now I have been searching for a way to get Ubuntu to connect to my Google calendar without having to go through Evolution. I only access my emails via firefox and wanted to keep everything online.

A few days ago I came across the Google calendar indicator 

[http://www.webupd8.org/2012/02/calendar-indicator-displays-google.html](http://www.webupd8.org/2012/02/calendar-indicator-displays-google.html)

After installing it, I have found that it is the closest thing that I have found to what I was looking for. The only two issues I have found is that there is no way to link to your calendar via it and it creates a separate calendar and does not use the one at the top of the screen. Apart from that it is what I was looking for.

I thought I would post this in the hope that others may find it of use.
