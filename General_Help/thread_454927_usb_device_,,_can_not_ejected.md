### Post by fragos on 2007-05-26
Unmount the device and it will be "ejected".  For some reason in 7.04 they replaced the term eject with unmount which is all that eject did for USB storage anyway.  If you don't have desktop icons for these devices you can open nautilus and right click on the item in the places list.
