### Post by arubislander on 2009-12-29
Hi,

You'll need at least 1GB to put ubuntu on a stick. (Well, really at least 700 MB, but they don't sell those)

The easiest way is to boot from a LiveCD and choose System->Administration->USB Startup Disk Creator if you are using Karmic.
