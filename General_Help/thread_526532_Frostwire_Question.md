### Post by lloyd_b on 2007-08-15
> **CheeseQueen452 said:**
> I just found out that LimeWire will soon become a "pay-to-play" service. What does that mean for Frostwire? Isn't that basically the same network?

Limewire is just one of many "servents" that access the Gnutella network (though it is one of the most popular).  There are also Frostwire, Bearshare, GTK-Gnutella, Morpheus, Gnucleus, GiFT-Gnutella, Shareaza, and several others.

What I *suspect* that "pay-to-play" business is about is charging for some kind of "premium" services, in addition to normal Gnutella access.  Which won't affect anyone using any of the other servents...
