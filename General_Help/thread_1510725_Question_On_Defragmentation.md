I have 10.04 installed inside Windows Vista (wubi installer), and I wanted to defrag in Windows. My question is that if I execute a defrag while in Windows, will it affect the addressing of the files within Ubuntu and mess it up, or will everything be fine?

### Post by Revolutionary101 on 2010-06-15
I doubt it will mess it up, in fact I'm almost certain that it won't. Think of Ubuntu installed via Wubi as a program and not a whole separate OS. When you defragment your HDD do all your programs get messed up? No they don't.

Don't worry about defragmentation messing up your Wubi Ubuntu install.
