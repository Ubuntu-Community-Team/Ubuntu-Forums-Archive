### Post by 2F4U on 2011-09-05
So are you saying there are similar problems in Windows and Linux? This would point to an overheating issue. Since the laptop is old, you may, if that is permitted, try to open the laptop and see if there is dust collecting inside the case. Dust often has a negative effect on air flow and prevents the fans from working properly.
