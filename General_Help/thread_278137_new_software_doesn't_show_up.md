### Post by dolphinholmer on 2006-10-16
Once you know the commands to run the programs that aren't in your menu, open Alacarte, navigate to the sub-menu category you wish to enter your new launcher into(in the left pane) and then click on 'File' 'New Entry.'Add under 'Name' what you want it to say in the menu and type your command under 'Command' You can also add your own icon if you so desire. Click 'OK' and Bob's your uncle.
