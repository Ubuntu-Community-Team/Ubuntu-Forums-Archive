### Post by c0mput3r_n3rD on 2009-08-23
I removed the little divider with the three small horizontal bars, and unbeknownst to me my running windows are not longer displayed on the bottom panel.  Any body know how to get it back?

### Post by BinaryFeast on 2009-08-23
Right click on the panel and there should be an option to add items. One of the items in the list that shows up will be the window list that you accidentally removed.

---

### Post by scouser73 on 2009-08-23
Right click a space on the panel, select **Add To Panel** and scroll to **Window List**.
