### Post by _sAm_ on 2008-08-30
Polar Protrainer 5 is a program made for Windows, not Linux(or Mac). You could try installing it with wine on Ubuntu and see if it works. 

If not, there is programs for Linux that you might replace it for you. First of all you can use the Polar web service to upload your training, and use the trainingprograms they offer.

Take a look at these:
[http://www.saring.de/polarviewer/index.html](http://www.saring.de/polarviewer/index.html)
[http://www.saring.de/sportstracker/index.html](http://www.saring.de/sportstracker/index.html)
[http://pytrainer.e-oss.net/](http://pytrainer.e-oss.net/)
[http://www.workoutware.com/](http://www.workoutware.com/)

Article about them here: [http://www.linux.com/articles/60528](http://www.linux.com/articles/60528)
