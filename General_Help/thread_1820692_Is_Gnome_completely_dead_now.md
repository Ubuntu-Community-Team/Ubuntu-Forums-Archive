### Post by snowpine on 2011-08-07
A quick Google or Forum search will give you much information on the topic. :)

The quick, non-snarky answer is, have you seen Gnome lately?

[www.gnome.org](www.gnome.org)

The Gnome 2.x that you know and remember is dead and no longer supported. Ubuntu took the controversial decision to ignore Gnome 3 and develop their own desktop instead: Unity. Whether you agree or disagree with this choice is a very popular topic of conversation on these forums. :)

### Post by mikewhatever on 2011-08-08
No, Gnome is not dead. Gnome2, although no longer developed by the Gnome Foundation, will be available in Ubuntu 10.04, 10.10 and 11.04 through their life times. Looking beyond Ubuntu, distros like CentOS and Scientific Linux will have Gnome2 for another 5 years.
