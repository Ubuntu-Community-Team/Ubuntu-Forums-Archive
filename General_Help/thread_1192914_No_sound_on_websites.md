Try reinstalling flashplugin-nonfree; used to not get sound either in websites, but worked fine in audio apps like audacious or vlc. A reinstall of the plugin restored sound.

$ sudo apt-get remove flashplugin-nonfree*
$ sudo apt-get install flashplugin-nonfree
