### Post by meho_r on 2009-10-31
Try downloading from [here.](http://ftp.opera.com/pub/opera/linux/) You may choose version (I downloaded 10.10beta1). Choose the one with qt4 not qt3 (e.g. opera_10.10.4672.gcc4.[COLOR="Red"]qt4[/COLOR]_amd64.deb). From Opera website you got qt3 version, that's the problem.
