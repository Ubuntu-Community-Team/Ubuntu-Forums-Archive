### Post by LeoSolaris on 2008-03-11
Do a search of the forum, this is a commonly known issue. What it will boil down to is you will have to download the .rpm version from adobe and convert it into a .deb, then install it. (Alien is the converting program)

It isn't actually complicated, and there are simple instructions on the forum, or failing that, you can Google "Ubuntu Flash Install Alien" or something of that nature.

This is one of those questions that everyone asks. They really need to just take the plugin out of the repo if it is not going to work properly.
