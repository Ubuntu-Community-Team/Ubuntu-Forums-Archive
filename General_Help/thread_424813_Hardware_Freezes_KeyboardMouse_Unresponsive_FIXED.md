### Post by ness0216 on 2007-04-27
Hey, I noticed a few other users were having this problem so I figured I'd post my fix. When I first installed fiesty I had just put in two new hard drives and reset my bios. After running fiesty for an arbitrary amount of time I would get freezes that would lock the mouse/keyboard and make me hold down the power button to restart. I fixed it by messing around with a few bios settings. I first tried disabling power management but that didn't work. I ended up changing a performance setting on my bios from automatic to disabled and now I'm running 100% stable. So if you are having these kinds of problems I would suggest tweaking the bios till you find something that works. Btw I'm running a p4 on an asus p4p800e-deluxe.
