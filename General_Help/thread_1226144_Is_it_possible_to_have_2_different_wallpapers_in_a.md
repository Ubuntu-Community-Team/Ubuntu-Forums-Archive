### Post by Psycho.mario on 2009-07-29
In short, No

In long, Yes

You cannot choose a different wallpaper on each screen. There is a was around it though, If you download the two wallpapers you want, make sure that they are the right resolution for your screens, then paste them side-by-side in gimp, with white space if they are different size screens, then, when you apply the wallpaper, it would look as though you had two different ones. It's not very easy, but i have not yet found a working option.
