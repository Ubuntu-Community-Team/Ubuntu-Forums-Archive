### Post by fooman on 2010-03-12
open your home folder (places > home folder).  in the toolbar,  click view and put a check next to "show hidden files"

then find and open the .icons folder.  see if the themes that failed to install are listed there.  if they are....right click on them and choose "move to trash"

try to install again.  hope that helps.
