The original image fits a single layer DVD without problems, so there's probably something wrong with your file. Have you checked it against the official MD5?

Ubuntu: [http://cdimage.ubuntu.com/releases/7.10/release/MD5SUMS](http://cdimage.ubuntu.com/releases/7.10/release/MD5SUMS)
Kubuntu: [http://cdimage.ubuntu.com/kubuntu/releases/7.10/release/MD5SUMS](http://cdimage.ubuntu.com/kubuntu/releases/7.10/release/MD5SUMS)

If the checksum doesn't match, I recommend not to use the image file.
