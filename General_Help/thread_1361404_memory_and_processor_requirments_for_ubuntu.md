### Post by schmolch on 2009-12-22
Abiword (textprocessing) and Gnumeric (spreadsheet) are very lightweight. I think they might even be the standard Office-Suite for Xubuntu. 
256MB is still very little though, your 1.8Ghz CPUs are probably crying at night.
Try Xubuntu with Abiword/Gnumeric/Epiphany(webbrowser) and watch the swap-space usage. If it constantly swaps out and annoys you look on ebay for some cheap ram-upgrades.
