### Post by vanadium on 2007-07-15
With a Powershot S50, I also have issues under Feisty that i did not have under Edgy. I can download, but after a number of pictures, the remaining ones are copied as files with zero length. I then can disconnect, reconnect and select the remaining pictures and eventually have them all downloaded this way. Your problem seems worse as you even can download pictures. If all fails, you could purchace an USB card reader and use that to read the data from your memory card. Such readers are quite cheap, and you access them as a regular USB drive. It will give you the added benefit of higher, USB 2 transfer speeds compared to the USB 1 speed of your camera.
