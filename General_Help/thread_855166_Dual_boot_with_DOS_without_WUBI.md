### Post by Kevbert on 2008-07-10
I'm not sure what you mean.  Compiz will work with most Intel, ATI and nVidia cards (but not Via).  To check whether compiz will work you can try running [[COLOR="Red"]compiz-check[/COLOR]]("http://forlong.blogage.de/article/pages/Compiz-Check").  This will give you pointers as to why compiz doesn't work.  Compiz is mainly used for fancy display effects such as the desktop/rotating cube, raindrops etc.
