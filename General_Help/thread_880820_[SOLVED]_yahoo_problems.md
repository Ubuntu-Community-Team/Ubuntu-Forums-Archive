### Post by dustman on 2008-08-05
well...it's pretty easy actually once you got the hang of it :D. Open Pidgin, then go up in the menu, at Accounts->Manage. You'll see there that you can choose the Yahoo Protocol (from the first drop-down list). In the Screen name section enter your Yahoo Messenger ID, in the Password section your password (obviously), and at Local alias, whichever name you desire (in all the message windows you'll have, this name will be displayed from your part of the conversation). Optionally you can tick the "Remember password" field, "New mail notifications", and choose a picture for your account (this will show in all your friends lists as your picture). Then click Save. That's about it!

Cheers!
