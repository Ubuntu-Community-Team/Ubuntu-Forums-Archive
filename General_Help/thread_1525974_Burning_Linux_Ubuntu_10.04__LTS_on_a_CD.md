### Post by CharlesA on 2010-07-07
On some machines you need to go into the BIOS setup and set it to boot from the USB stick under "storage configuration" or "hard drive boot order" or some such thing.

I've had to do that on a couple machines. Others have a boot menu you can access.

As for burning the iso to a cd, just verify the MD5SUM and then burn it with whatever burning software you like.
