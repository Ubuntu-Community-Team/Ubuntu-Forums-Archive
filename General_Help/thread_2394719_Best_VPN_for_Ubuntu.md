### Post by antigonish2 on 2018-06-24
I have used VPNarea, AirVPN and most recently, NordVPN.  VPNarea are great, their config files work fine on Ubuntu, they don't overload their servers and their help desk is pretty efficient.  AirVPN, however are another matter.  They have a fantastic service with a lot of options that you'd never hear of unless you looked into their website, but they could well use some brushing up on their social skills.  NordVPN are good value for the money, speeds are blazingly fast and their server list seems to get larger and larger.  I don't think you could go wrong with either VPNarea or NordVPN.
