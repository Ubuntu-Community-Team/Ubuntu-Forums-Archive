### Post by pricetech on 2010-12-07
Assuming you're using VirtualBox;

Click on Storage.
Click on your optical drive, which will be the secondary drive, probably says "empty".
On the right where it says CD/DVD Device choose Host Drive .......

That should do it.

If you're using some other virutalization product, you'll have to check that product's documentation.
