### Post by raul_ on 2007-03-24
Dunno but those things are normally stored in an hidden folder at your home folder. Open your home folder, enable hidden folder viewing and search for opera. Then browse and you should find your bookmarks. Maybe a corrupt profile did this?

### Post by Claus7 on 2007-06-25
Hello,

Simply awesome! I was trying to update my version of opera and I while I did manage to do it, I lost all my bookmarks. I enabled the hidden folder viewing and I went to .opera folder. There, there was a file named : opera6.adr which had all my bookmarks before the update!

So we have to keep backup from time to time even on bookmarks.
