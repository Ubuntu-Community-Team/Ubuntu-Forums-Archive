Copy-Paste Problem with Chromium 11.0.660.0 (73896) Ubuntu 10.10

I want to copy text from website and paste directly to OO Writer. Currently, I can:

a) paste red squiggles into Writer, highlight area, hit Cntrl + M (default format); or,

b) first paste to gedit, then highlight, copy and paste to OO Writer.

I do a lot of copy-pasting from websites. What can I do to fix the problem and eliminate the annoyance of doing a or b? TIA

I figured it out finally:

[solution]Copy text from website to clipboard > (with Writer open) Edit > Paste Special (Ctrl +shift +V) removes the HTML codes
