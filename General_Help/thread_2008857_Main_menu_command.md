### Post by HiImTye on 2012-06-23
open a new terminal (ctrl+alt+t) then go to edit>profile preferences. move to the 'title and command' tab and at the bottom, change the drop-down beside 'when command exits:' to 'hold the terminal open' now just hit close and type 'exit' - the terminal should stay open
