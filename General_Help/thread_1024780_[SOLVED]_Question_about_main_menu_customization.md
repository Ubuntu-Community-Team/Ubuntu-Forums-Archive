### Post by NEUR0M4NCER on 2008-12-29
I should've said how to do it, really, shouldn't I?

So to be safe, you right-click on the panel where you want the buttons (doesn't matter where, you can move it later). Choose 'Add To Panel' from the context menu. From there, you should be able to just scroll down to the 'Main Menu' entry, and either click it, and then click 'Add', or just drag and drop it onto the panel. MAke sure it works how you like, if it's not to your taste, simply right-click the icon and choose 'Remove From Panel'. If you're gonna keep it, go to the old buttons, right-click and 'Remove From Panel'. Then you can move the new button to where you would like it - maybe locking it to the panel afterwards, to prevent crazy moving icons when you linger on a click :P

Regards
