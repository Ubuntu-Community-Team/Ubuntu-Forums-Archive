### Post by cottfcfan on 2011-02-17
All you have to do with gmail, is go to settings/forwarding & pop/imap.
Then tick the "enable imap" box (make sure all the rest are unticked)
Then when you open Thunderbird for the 1st time just enter email add & password and your done.
To forward other email adds to gmail look in accounts & import, its pretty straightforward.
Hope this helps.
