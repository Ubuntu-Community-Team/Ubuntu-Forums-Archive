### Post by ibuclaw on 2009-04-07
In **System->Preferences->Sound**, change all sound playback/record options to **ALSA** or **PulseAudio Sound Server**. (Try ALSA first, then Pulse).

Then close and reopen firefox, then try playing audio again.
