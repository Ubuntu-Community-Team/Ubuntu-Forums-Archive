### Post by dragonfly41 on 2021-08-18
> [COLOR=#000000]I thought it might be a faulty power supply but why would it do it only in the mornings?[/COLOR]

Your description does suggest a dirty power supply causing shutdown.
To verify, can you compare notes with neighbours?
There could be some local equipment or plant being switched on in mornings causing a power supply blip.
Check with your energy supplier. They can install a monitoring device for a few days if you make a good case.
Simplest solution is to run PC on battery mode in morning then switch to power later in the day.
