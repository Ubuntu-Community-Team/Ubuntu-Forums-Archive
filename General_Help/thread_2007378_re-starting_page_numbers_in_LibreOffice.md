### Post by LewisTM on 2012-06-20
This is really easy. You just have to apply page styles. In Libreoffice each page has its own style and you can set it to hide the header.
Hit F11 and click the Page Styles button (4th).
Apply style 'First Page' for you page 1. For page 2, apply 'Default' and add a header with a page number. For finer control over the page number, double-click the page number field and offset it by the desired amount.

That's it!
