### Post by Pjotr123 on 2010-03-01
Sub101 is right: the mouse settings for sensitivity and such, also directly influence the touchpad settings. Ubuntu basically sees the touchpad as another mouse.

There is also a touchpad tab in the mouse settings, which offers control of some touchpad-specific settings.
