### Post by agim on 2009-05-03
I really like Kmail. I use Kontact, which has kmail, and a calendar, allows me to keep notes and a to do list and has a journal. It even has a very good rss reader (akregator). There are some annoyances (I wish I could use ctrl-f to search my notes), but overall its very good. I also really like the control kmail gives me to filter my messages. I haven't used thunderbird in a long time, so I don't remember its is as good or better at this.

Of course, if you also use windows, or if you just want an email and calendar app, thunderbird is a great client and might make more sense.
