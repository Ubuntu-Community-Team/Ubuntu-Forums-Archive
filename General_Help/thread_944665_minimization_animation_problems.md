Hi and welcome! :)

Open CCSM go to the "Animations" slab, and click the "Minimize animation" tab.
Under that, you'll see an "animation selection" box, which has the columns "Minimize effect", "Duration", "Window Match" and "Options".

What does the "Window Match" column read for you?
Mine reads:
```
(type=Normal | Dialog | ModalDialog | Unknown)
```
Is yours very different from that?
