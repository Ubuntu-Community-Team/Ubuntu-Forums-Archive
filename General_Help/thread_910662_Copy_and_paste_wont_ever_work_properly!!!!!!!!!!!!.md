### Post by Cheesehead on 2008-09-04
This isn't Windows or OSX. A few of the rules are different. Not better or worse, simply different. 

Copy and paste works great, once you know the rule - and you found it: Simply don't kill the process you cut/copy from until you've finished pasting. When you kill the process you cut/copy from, the memory (including the clipboard cut) is released and promptly reused by another process.

Probably some devs somewhere are working on orphan-paste. Heck, it may already be available in the repos (have you looked? Googled?) But, as you can see from the thread so far, there's not a large groundswell of demand for it.
