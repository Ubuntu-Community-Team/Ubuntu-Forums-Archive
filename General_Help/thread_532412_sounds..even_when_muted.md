### Post by marsmissionaries on 2007-08-28
are you using your multimedia keys on your keyboard to mute the sound?

If so, go into the system menu, go to preferences and click the sound menu item.

At the bottom will be a section called "Default Mixer Tracks" and in that section it will say "Device:" And then it will list some devices in a drop down menu. You need to select your sound card. 


If this isn't the issue, then i have no clue.
