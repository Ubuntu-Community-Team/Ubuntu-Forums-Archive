### Post by martrn on 2009-10-21
Visit the wubi guide at : [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/WubiGuide]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/WubiGuide") and scroll down until you find the section marked "Uninstallation".

From there at the bottom there is a utility called "Uninstall-Ubuntu.exe" which should be the utility you appear to be looking for.

If you would like a breath of fresh air and are re-installing ubuntu then may I sudgest your first partition your hard drive using [http://gparted.sourceforge.net/]("http://gparted.sourceforge.net/") and then goto [http://www.ubuntu.com/GetUbuntu/download]("http://www.ubuntu.com/GetUbuntu/download") and download the version of ubuntu you would like.  Burn it onto a cd, and then install.
