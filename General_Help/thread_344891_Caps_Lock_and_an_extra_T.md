### Post by Quicky on 2007-01-29
I had a very similar issue recently - as well as getting an extra character with caps lock, a number of other keys were also registering additional key presses.

This was on a Fujitsu Siemens laptop. I had a feeling that the problem was hardware rather than OS related, so tested it by plugging in a USB keyboard, which as it turned out didn’t have the same problem when typing. I would suggest trying that, or booting into another OS to see if you can isolate the problem. 

On my machine, the laptop suddenly corrected itself – I’m at a loss to explain why it broke in the first place, or how it managed to repair itself. However, I have an inkling it may have been something to do with the small amount of lager that dripped onto the keyboard one evening which caused the problem.
