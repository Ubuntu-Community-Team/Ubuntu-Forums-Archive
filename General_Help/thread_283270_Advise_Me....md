### Post by meng on 2006-10-24
Go to user management/administration (as the default user) and change the other user permissions so that they have access to audio devices. (I know exactly where to direct you in GNOME, but not in KDE, but the principle is the same.)

As for resizing partitions, I recommend using a GParted LiveCD to do so.
