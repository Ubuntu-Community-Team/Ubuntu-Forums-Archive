### Post by SoFl W on 2010-06-04
I installed Thunderbird last night and lost the dictionary.  (un-installing the ubuntu version of Open Office must have caused it) 
1) To get it working again I made a message with spelling mistakes.
2) "Sent" the message. (I have check spelling before sending turned on)
3) When the spelling box comes up it is blank.  At the bottom there is a box for "Language" click on that and select "get more dictionaries.
4) from the Mozilla site select the dictionary of your choice.  (USA- the second dictionary is a medical dictionary, you want the first)
5) DOWNLOAD the dictionary.
6) Go to the Thunderbird add-ons and select language.
7) add language
8) restart Thunderbird.
