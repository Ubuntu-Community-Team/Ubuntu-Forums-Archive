### Post by nhandler on 2008-04-27
It looks like 5.8.8 was also in gutsy. I would have to guess that they didn't update perl because Hardy is a LTS release. Since it is meant to be stable and reliable, they probably didn't feel it would be worth upgrading perl, since they knew 5.8.8 ran fine.

If you really want 5.10, just download it and install it yourself.
