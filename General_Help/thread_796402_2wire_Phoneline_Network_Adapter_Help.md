### Post by fragos on 2008-10-09
All pnones in the US are two wire which will work with a standard modem. The two center wires of the modular phone connector are used, the outer two can be used for a second line. These wires are also used for DSL. In either of those cases a special driver shouldn't be required. I don't recognize the term PNA.
