### Post by mike2357 on 2008-06-20
If I understand correctly, you want the type of information that Windows has in the bottom panel.  To do this with Ubuntu/Gnome:

1.  Right-click in an empty area of the panel.
2.  Select "Add to Panel"
3.  Scroll down to "Window List" and click Add
