### Post by Polygon on 2008-07-21
it works fine for me on both windows and linux (firefox 3.1)

if it still keeps on crashing, you could try creating another profile (basically just quitting firefox, moving your .mozilla folder in your home folder to your desktop, running firefox again) and then try browsing DA, and if it suddenly works, then your old profile is busted. Move back your .mozilla folder, save your bookmarks and whatever else to a file and then delete your old .mozilla profile and let firefox create a new one, reimport your bookmarks and your all good
