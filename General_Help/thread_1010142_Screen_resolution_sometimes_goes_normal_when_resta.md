That's exactly backwards from what I'd expect.  Seems that your resolution should be right when the i2c is detected correctly.  Maybe the driver for the i2c device isn't being loaded?

Have a look at your syslog and messages logs.  Look for i2c.

In addition you might do a Google for i2c VSC 554d
