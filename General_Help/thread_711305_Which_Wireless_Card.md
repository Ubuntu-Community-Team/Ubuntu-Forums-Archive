### Post by nlong on 2008-02-29
The B130 has an ExpressCard 34 slot for which, there are many wireless adaptors available from online stores or even brick and mortars like Best Buy,etc.  You could also use a USB Wireless adaptor.  I'm not sure what you're referring to by "connector cord" for the internal card, unless you mean the antenna, which should be completely internal.

Additionally, getting wireless networking to work in Linux can be difficult.  Do some research before you purchase.
