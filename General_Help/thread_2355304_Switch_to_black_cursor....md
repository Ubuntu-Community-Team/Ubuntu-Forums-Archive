I'm currently running Ubuntu 16.04.02 LTS, but I also have Kubuntu 16.10 installed. I really like the black cursor in Kubuntu, so I tried to change it to black in Ubuntu, but nothing would work. I did a search and came up with various terminal commands, such as "sudo sed -ri 's/DMZ-White/DMZ-Black/g' /usr/share/icons/default/index.theme", but nothing worked. Then, I came across the Unity Tweak Tool, and *voilà*! I now have the choice of white, black or even a red cursor. Give it a try, if you want.
