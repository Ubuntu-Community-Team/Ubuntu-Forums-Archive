### Post by VCoolio on 2009-05-19
Hi, welcome to Ubuntu. Some answers that I hope will be helpful:

1. The desktop reflects the folder /home/yourusername/Desktop. Also it will show devices when connected or mounted partitions. You can enable / disable these icons on your desktop in the configuration editor (apps > nautilus > desktop). 

2. That would be in system > administration > users I think, or press alt+F2, type gnome-control-center <enter> and find the button "about me", you can set your password there.

3. Depends on your taste. I hope you followed install instructions on restricted-extras to play mp3 etc like you find [here]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RestrictedFormats/")? I recommend Banshee or Songbird.

5. What is wrong with copy-paste?
