### Post by Arby on 2007-03-22
If it's a gaming machine then it should have enough power to do composite. The thing I would be cautious about is checking what make and model of graphics card he has. For Beryl it seems to be the case that Intel cards are straight forward. I got beryl running on an Intel 945 card in edgy and feisty in about 10 min.  Just from browsing these forums, Nvidia cards can be done with a bit of tweaking. The biggest problems seem to occur with ATI cards. If he has an ATI card I'd think pretty hard before you begin. But that's just my 2cents :)
