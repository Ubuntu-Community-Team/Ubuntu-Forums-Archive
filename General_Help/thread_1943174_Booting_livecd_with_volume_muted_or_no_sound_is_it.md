### Post by fparri on 2012-03-19
Is it possibile to boot a livecd with no active sound or muted volume? I need it to start on my notebook without making noise since I am in a public place :)

---

### Post by Mark Phelps on 2012-03-19
Unless you can mute the sound in your netbook BIOS, then no.  You would need to change a setting to do this, and with a LiveCD, you can't write to anything on the CD, which means you can't change any settings during boot.
