### Post by MenloMark on 2011-11-26
I struggled through this  issue.  The commands up at the top, but are invisible until you roll over them.  

Thus when you Show All Bookmarks you will see the commands when you roll mouse up to the top of the page.  Proceed with Import and Backup then Restore then select your file location for bookmarks.

My goodness, not so very intuitive, but that's the way it works.
