It depends on what you define the best.

What I define the best media player EVER is Songbird, it can make playlists not only from files on the hdd but the Internet, makes it easy to download music files, plays vids on the Net and more, It is the Firefox of media!

What I like is that SB displays music files on a site and displays them  as a playlist and you can add a link to the file in your music library by draggin it to your library. SB also supports the iPod. and is skinnable and extended with extensions like FF!

Easy installation: [www.getautomatix.com](www.getautomatix.com)
