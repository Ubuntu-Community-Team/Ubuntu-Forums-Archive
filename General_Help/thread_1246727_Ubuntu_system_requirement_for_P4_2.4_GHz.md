### Post by earthpigg on 2009-08-22
256 mb of RAM and ~10 gb of free hard drive space are the min requirements.

so, you should be good.

ensure you burn the install cd at the *_slowest speed possible_* for best reliability, and burn to a plain old boring CD-R. not CD-RW or DVD-R or anything else. a single 1 or 0 in the wrong place on the install CD, and your install will be corrupt or freeze, and CD-R at slowest speed is the most reliable.
