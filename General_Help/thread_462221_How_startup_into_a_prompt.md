### Post by Metacarpal on 2007-06-02
When you turn on the computer and see the GRUB menu loading (there's a countdown, defaults to 3 seconds), hit Escape.  That will take you to a menu where you can choose a different kernel - pick the one marked (recovery mode).  That'll take you to a command prompt.

Did Ubuntu boot normally before and recently stopped, or has it never booted right?  How much RAM do you have?
