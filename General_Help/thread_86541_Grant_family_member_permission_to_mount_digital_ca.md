### Post by poopdog on 2005-11-05
Hey.

So, I've got a Fujifilm 5100 digital camera, and if I'm logged in and turn the camera on it comes right up.

No need to mount or anything. Ubuntu is great like that.

But if my wife is logged in, she is unable to mount the camera at all. She can't even sudo.

What kind of permissions do I need to set to allow her to do this?

Thanks a ton.

---

### Post by kzutter on 2005-11-05
From your login - System/Administration/Users and Groups
Pull up her Properties
On the "User Privileges" tab:

Place a check next to the item "Enable access to external devices..."


-Ken
