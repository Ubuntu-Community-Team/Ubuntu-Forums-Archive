### Post by Kopkins on 2012-08-09
Usually it is with an external mouse. Pressing down on the scroll wheel would be the middle click.

Some laptops have a middle click, but you would know if yours does. In 12.04, on most laptops I've used it on, tapping the top right corner of the trackpad will give a middle click. But I'm not sure if this behavior is the same in 10.04, I haven't used it.

Best of luck,
Kopkins
