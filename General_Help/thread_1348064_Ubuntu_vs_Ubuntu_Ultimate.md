### Post by ranch hand on 2009-12-06
I have used UE.  It is not too bad.  It is very bloated.

A couple of the scripts used are warned against by the Ubuntu devs as a threat to your system.

The repos that are for UE are not dependable.

The main thing that you get is different art work.  You can get a bigger selection from "gnome looks" website with out the bloat of silly scripts that you will most likely not use and may damage your system.

There is also a lot of behind the scenes fighting on the UE forums.  TheeMahn basically is running a cult.  Fun if you are into that sort of thing.

I never had any real trouble with the OS except getting updates that had to come from the UE repo.  That repo works great for a while and then it needs updated.  That is when you can depend on  a few days to a couple months down time.
