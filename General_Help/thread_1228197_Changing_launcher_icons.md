### Post by michy99 on 2009-07-31
A) I'm not sure what you're asking here.

B) Right-click on Applications and choose Edit Menus. Select the Celtx launcher and choose Properties. Click the icon in the top left corner. Click browse to go to the folder which contains the icon you want to use. (Note: you won't actually see the icons until you click Open to choose the folder.)
