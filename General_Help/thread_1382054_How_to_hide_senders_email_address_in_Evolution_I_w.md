### Post by mbeierl on 2010-01-15
That's not an evolution thing, it's an email protocol thing, and no, it cannot be done.  You can spoof someone else's email address (that's what a lot of spammers do) but you cannot send an email without there being a "from" field.

For obvious reasons, I am not going to explain how to spoof someone else's address.
