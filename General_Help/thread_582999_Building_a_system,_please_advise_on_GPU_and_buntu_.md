### Post by Solitus3989 on 2007-10-20
All i can tell you is that for the video cards... well, with almost any motherboard with built in graphics capabilities, it's probably better than both of your cards.

for a moderately priced, excellent video card, try a geforce series. (i have a geforce 8600 gts, cost me $190 approximately). 

GeForce 6600 Graphics Card PCI 256MB will run you about $90.

pick and choose your price range for decent performance.
