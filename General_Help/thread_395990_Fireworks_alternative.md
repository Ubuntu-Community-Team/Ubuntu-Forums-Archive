### Post by willie_wang on 2007-11-20
fireworks is an program that deals with Scalable Vector Graphics (SVG) something with GIMP doesn't do too well at handling.

for SVG on ubuntu linux, check out inkscape or xara extreme. both programs are extremely good. while development on xara has halted (temporarily?), inkscape is being continuously developed. it may not have as many features as the macromedia fireworks, but it's certainly very capable. i think you'll find that with a little practice, it's very easy to get used to.

the only thing missing from inkscape which is one of the main reasons i use fireworks, is the ability to slice and dice sections of your artwork easy.

but using wine, other websites have successfully installed fireworks on ubuntu. maybe another avenue for you.

Willie
