If you wish, you could also try setting the "Reveal Mode" to none. To do so click on the Reveal Mode button that says "left", then click on the highlighted side to deactivate the mouse triggering the Unity bar, click ok to apply the changes.

You will still have access to the sidebar with ALT + F1, and dash with ALT + F2 from the keyboard. Cheers.
