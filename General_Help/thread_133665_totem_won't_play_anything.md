### Post by carlosqueso on 2006-02-20
For mp3's and MPEG video: [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/RestrictedFormats?highlight=%28restricted%29#head-7a73b076c82c7d7bb567bdac3322e53274f1d1f3](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/RestrictedFormats?highlight=%28restricted%29#head-7a73b076c82c7d7bb567bdac3322e53274f1d1f3)

For windows media (wma,wmv,avi):
[http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=411934&postcount=12](http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=411934&postcount=12)

These codecs can't legally be included with Ubuntu because they are patented, and Ubuntu is (except for a few easily-removable and highly-necessary drivers) 100% free-libre software.  Also note that for the second link, the codecs may not be 100% legal in the US ;)
