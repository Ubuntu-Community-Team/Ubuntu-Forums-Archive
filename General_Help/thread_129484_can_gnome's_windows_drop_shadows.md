### Post by RAOF on 2006-02-13
Yes, they can.  But not by default.  You need to try one of poofyhairguy's eye-candy guides.  Either [this one]("http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=75527"), which is the basic version, or the [Knome guide]("http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=115974") which gives more options/control but is more difficult.

Neither of these are production quality solutions - there will be some things that don't look right, and it makes it more likely that your system will crash.
