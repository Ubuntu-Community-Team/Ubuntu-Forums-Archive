### Post by acormack on 2007-03-28
If I were you I would create a Live CD (on another PC)  of either Kubuntu [http://kubuntu.org/special-cds.php](http://kubuntu.org/special-cds.php) or Knopix.

Then boot your PC from that disk.

Once the PC is booted you should be able to inspect the hard disk to see what is on it.

If the data you want is there you could copy it off onto a USB stick.

Hope this helps
