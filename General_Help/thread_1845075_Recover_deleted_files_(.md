### Post by Lisiano on 2011-09-16
First of ... TURN OFF YOUR PC. That being said. Boot from a LiveCD and download a program called PhotoRec ([http://www.cgsecurity.org/wiki/TestDisk_Download](http://www.cgsecurity.org/wiki/TestDisk_Download)) and go find a USB Drive large enough for the files you need to recover.

EDIT: Actually. Find a USB drive that is around the size of the free space you have on your partition where the college stuff was. Or if you have a extra partition (Win7/WinXP) you could restore the files there.
