### Post by white3454 on 2009-03-15
You can only SMS TO US numbers currently, Activate &#8220;Text Messaging (SMS) in Chat&#8221; from the Settings tab in Gmail.

Once you save the changes you made in your settings, you can type a phone number directly into the chat window, and then select &#8220;Send SMS&#8221; to send your message.

Official Gmail Blog Post: [http://gmailblog.blogspot.com/2008/12/really-new-in-labs-this-time-sms-text.html](http://gmailblog.blogspot.com/2008/12/really-new-in-labs-this-time-sms-text.html)
