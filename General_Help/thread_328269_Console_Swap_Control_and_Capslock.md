### Post by mish on 2008-03-19
The easy way is to go through the menus.  System -> Preferences -> Keyboard.  Go to the "Layout Options" tab (or in Hardy go to the "Layout" tab and click on the "Layout Options" button).

Go through the options for "Ctrl key position" and "Caps Lock key behaviour"

For more fine grained control, look at

[http://ubuntuguide.org/wiki/Ubuntu_Edgy#How_to_remap_the_Caps_Lock_key_as_another_Control_key](http://ubuntuguide.org/wiki/Ubuntu_Edgy#How_to_remap_the_Caps_Lock_key_as_another_Control_key)
