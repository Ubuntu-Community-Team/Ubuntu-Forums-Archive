### Post by deadflowr on 2013-12-30
Open the "Files" program and then in the menu(it's the thing on top that says Files in the top panel on the screen) click it and go to, I think, preferences. Then in here, there is a setting for executables, reset it for run or ask ever time.
The default is view.
