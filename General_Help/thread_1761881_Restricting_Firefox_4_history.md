### Post by Zeikcied on 2011-05-18
In previous versions of Firefox, I had the history restricted to two days.  Since upgrading to Kubuntu 11.04, I've found that Firefox 4 doesn't really have the same configuration options.  I did a Google search and found some postings that confused me as to whether or not it's actually possible to force Firefox to delete history after a specific amount of time.

So, is it possible to restrict my Firefox 4 browsing history to two days like I did in the past?  Or am I just forced to let the browser keep history for as long as it wants?

### Post by lithopsian on 2011-05-18
> **Zeikcied said:**
> 
So, is it possible to restrict my Firefox 4 browsing history to two days like I did in the past?  Or am I just forced to let the browser keep history for as long as it wants?
No.  Firefox 4 will keep history until it runs out of room.  You can control the maximum amount of space it will use for history, although by default Firefox decides based on your RAM.

Why do you want to restrict it to a particular number of days?
