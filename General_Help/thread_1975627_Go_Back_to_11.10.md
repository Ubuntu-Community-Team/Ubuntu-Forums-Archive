### Post by pablo180 on 2012-05-07
I think that it is fair to say that 12.04 is not completely ready for release, certainly it is the most crash happy OS I have used in a while. 

Hibernate seems to have been removed by default (another annoying Ubuntu regression) - I tried re-adding it, but it didn't work anyway so I wouldn't be in too much of a hurry to add that back if I were you. I also have similar problems as you with Suspend, sometimes it comes back, sometimes not.

Perhaps a bit late for you now, but every time it comes to upgrading Ubuntu I make a list of the most important features I need, back up everything, upgrade and then spend a day testing. If those features I need aren't working, or I have serious problems, I simply restore my backup and wait six months.
