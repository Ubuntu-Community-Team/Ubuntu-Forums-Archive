### Post by Forlong on 2009-01-02
Well, even if you do have the Nvidia driver installed, it is not "enabled".
In other words: your X server doesn't "know" you have it installed.

You could try to fix this yourself but I'll recommend un-installing the driver, whatever way you installed it.

Afterwards, go to *System &#8594; Administration &#8594; Hardware Drivers* and enable the driver that is offered to you. Then reboot and try again.
