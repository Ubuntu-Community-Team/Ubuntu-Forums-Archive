### Post by Aizawa on 2010-04-09
Click on "Applications" in the upper left hand corner, and look in Accessories. Click on "Search for files".

You have a dropdown there called "Look in folder", where you can choose the folder or alternatively your whole file system. Choose your back-up drive, and simply search for * (star).

I hope that helped!

EDIT: I don't know how to do it in the CLI.

### Post by Jguy on 2010-04-09
The find command is a great way to do it from the CLI.

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/find](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/find)

```

sudo find / '*'
```

Should work, I think.
