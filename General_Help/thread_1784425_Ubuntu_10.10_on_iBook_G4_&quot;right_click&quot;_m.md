### Post by Ottis on 2011-07-02
Well, after a short rest from working on it, I looked around and found the "secondary click" (mac version of right click...) is also controlled by the f12 button (with f11 as left click).

So.....

If anybody else has had this same trouble with the mouse button, just use the f11 & f12 buttons.
