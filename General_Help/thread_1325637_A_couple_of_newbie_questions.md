Hi.
If you go to the Ubuntu supported Laptops page
[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/LaptopTestingTeam](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/LaptopTestingTeam)
or to this one
[http://www.ubuntuhcl.org/browse/search+notebook-pcs?category=2](http://www.ubuntuhcl.org/browse/search+notebook-pcs?category=2)

As far as your mail goes, by the look of the error message, the provider is upset about something.
> To send your message, please sign into your account online first 
I know that Evolution gets very upset when it can't do what it has been told to do. It looks like Evolution thinks it has a message to send but can't get it out, and that you need to log in at your e-mail account via the web-browser and do something there.
