### Post by howefield on 2009-09-02
Open compiz settings manager, and enable scale.

The default key combo to initiate is shift + alt + up.

If you select the Initiate WIndow Picker from the bindings tab, (the one to the right of the screen icon, you will be able to choose a part of the screen for your mouse to travel to in order to initiate the scaling.

Hope that makes sense.
