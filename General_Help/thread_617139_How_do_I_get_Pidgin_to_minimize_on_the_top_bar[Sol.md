### Post by shellbend on 2007-11-25
I just ran into this problem too - here's how I fixed it:

Go to Tools > Preferences, and on the Interface tab change "Show system tray icon" to "Always".

Now when I click the "x" in the upper right on the buddy list, Pidgin minimizes to the system tray in the upper right
