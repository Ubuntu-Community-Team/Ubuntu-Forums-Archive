### Post by Psycho.mario on 2010-01-03
If your ubuntu computer has internet, and the wireless is plugged in, you can share it by clicking on the network applet, click 'Create new wireless network', input the wireless name, and security. Once that is done then you should have a working wireless 'access point' for your other computers.
