### Post by WorMzy on 2010-11-30
You don't uninstall an operating system, you delete it's partition. Boot into a LiveCD, open gparted (System -> Administration -> Partition Manager [or something like that]), mark the Ubuntu and Swap partitions for deletion (you may need to righ-click Swap and choose "Swap-off" first), then click Apply.

After it's finished, reboot and put the Windows installation disk into the CD drive.
