### Post by monsterstack on 2009-05-06
Unfortunately for you, DRM'd wmv files are unplayable in Ubuntu.

You can, of course, strip the wmv of the DRM, thus rendering them playable. You'll need Windows to do it, of course. Maybe you have Windows on another compiter, or a friend can help you out? Go [here]("http://forum.doom9.org/showthread.php?threadid=89243&perpage=20&pagenumber=1") [doom9.org] for details. Beware, however: [color=red]if you are a US citizen, doing this is illegal thanks to the DMCA[/color]. Currently the software works for wmv DRM up to version 10.

Hope this helps.
