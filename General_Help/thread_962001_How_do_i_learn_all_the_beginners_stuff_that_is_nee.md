### Post by OneRingShort on 2008-10-29
I didn't go quite cold turkey, but my transition from Windows was a rapid one.  What I did to learn all the ins and outs (I use the word "term" *very* loosely) was to just play around with everything.  A lot of it is like Windows, it's just locations that change.

If I wanted to do something in the terminal, I would look up the commmand to do it, try it out.  If I later forgot it, I would look it up again, and eventually through repetition you learn it.

I also bought "The official Ubuntu Book" for the sake of having a hard copy reference.
