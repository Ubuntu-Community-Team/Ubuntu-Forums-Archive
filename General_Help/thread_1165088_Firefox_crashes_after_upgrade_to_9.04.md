### Post by kcrenshaw on 2009-05-20
After upgrading to Jaunty from Intrepid, firefox (3.0.10) crashed whenever I tried to go to mail.google.com or yahoo.com.  The problems seems to have been with the Shockwave Flash pluging for Firefox.  I tried removing Firefox and re-installing as well as disabling my add-ons, but none of that worked.  However, after disabling all of my plugins Firefox worked again as expected.  Re-enabling the Shockwave Flash v10.0.r22 plugin caused the issue to resurface.

Hopefully someone else will find this useful. (Hopefully the plugin issue will be fixed soon!)
