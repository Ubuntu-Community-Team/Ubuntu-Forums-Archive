### Post by Dr Small on 2009-02-10
Pidgin can use OTR (Off the record) for encrypting messages, but the contact must have OTR installed too. Gajim provides support for GnuPG encryption, but both parties must have GPG keys. Either way you look at it, both contacts must use compatible encryption to each other.
