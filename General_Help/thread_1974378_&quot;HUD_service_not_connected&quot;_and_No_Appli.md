### Post by colin.p on 2012-05-06
I did a clean install leaving my "home" from 10.04 intact. However, nothing showed up in HUD and I had no idea how to access any installed applications. On another thread, it seems that any hidden folders from a previous install causes the problem.
So do a complete backup of your home directory, then remove all the hidden folders from "home" ( I left the thunderbird hidden directory).
Then HUD should work and then after installing software, go back to your backup and bring the backed-up hidden directory over and replace the new one.
