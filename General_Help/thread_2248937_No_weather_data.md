### Post by gordon99 on 2014-10-18
Thanks for your interest Impavidus. I followed the thread you suggested and seemed to get nowhere. However, seeing reference to 'Software Updater' I went into Whisker Menu then Settings and, low and behold, found 'Software Updater' which I ran. When it had finished and the laptop re-started the weather icon was active again. It doesn't seem to be from the Norwegian source anymore. 
One thing that did surprise me was that it took the best part of an hour for the updater to download and unpack about 270 Mb of updates. I would have thought that 10 minutes would have been more than sufficient time. I will have to check my download speed as I have fibre to cabinet broadband.
