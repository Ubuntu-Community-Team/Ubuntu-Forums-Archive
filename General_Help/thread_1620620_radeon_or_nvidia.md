### Post by efflandt on 2010-11-13
Earlier someone kept recommending nvidia GT 220 or 240 and I have had NO video issues at all with a GT 220 in 10.04 or 10.10, even though some people with GTX or GTS cards had issues with some nvidia driver versions.

I did have to add an /etc/modprobe.d/sound.conf and /etc/asound.conf to get HDMI audio, but my regular 2.1 PC speakers have better sound anyway.

It is not the fastest for Windows games, although, it works smoothly with directx 9, and seems to work great in general for Linux and any Linux games I have tried.
