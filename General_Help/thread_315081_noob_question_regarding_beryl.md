### Post by hadiriazi on 2006-12-08
Hello there and welcome to Ubuntu :)

In **Beryl Manager** (Right click the icon "red gem" on top right) select **beryl settings manager** then choose **Fading Windows** from the tabs on the right hand side, **tick** the checkbox to turn this feature **ON**.

This should make it work.
