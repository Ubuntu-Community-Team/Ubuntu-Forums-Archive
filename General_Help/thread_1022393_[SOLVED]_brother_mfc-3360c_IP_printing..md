Where did you search?

Have you looked in synaptic?

**System > Administration > Synaptic package Manager** and search for **mfc-3360c** and install the lpr and cupswrapper drivers.

brother-cups-wrapper-bh7
brother-lpr-drivers-bh7

Also [Brother website](http://solutions.brother.com/linux/en_us/index.html) for troubleshooting tips and drivers.
