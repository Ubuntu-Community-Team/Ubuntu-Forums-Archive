### Post by choclatboy on 2012-06-16
go to [http://www.adiumxtras.com/index.php?a=xtras&xtra_id=2160](http://www.adiumxtras.com/index.php?a=xtras&xtra_id=2160)
and click download on the right to download zip file "renkoo_5_3613_2160.zip"

extract the downloaded zip file, move the folders "renkoo.AdiumMessageStyle" and "renkooNaked.AdiumMessageStyle" to this folder in your home directory: ~/.local/share/adium/message-styles
(create this folders if they don't exist)

in empathy you can change to the new theme in preferences/themes.

hope it helped.
