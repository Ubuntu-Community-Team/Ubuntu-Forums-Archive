### Post by boomtisk on 2008-05-04
Damn, I'm stupid! It actually got fixed! For those who're not getting it to work, follow this guide:

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/SCIM](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/SCIM)

Al you have to do is go to System > Language Support and check "support" and "enable support to enter complex characters" for the languages you want to input and reboot!
