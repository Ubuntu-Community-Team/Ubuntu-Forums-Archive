### Post by mithras86 on 2006-11-05
You don't need any specific folder. It's obvious that to keep your filesystem clean, a separated folder for you backgrounds is a good one to have. 

To change your background: right mouse on your desktop -> change background. There you see a list and you can add wallpapers.
