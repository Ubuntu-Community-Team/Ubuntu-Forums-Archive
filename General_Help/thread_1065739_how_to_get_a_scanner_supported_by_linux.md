### Post by OrangeCrate on 2009-02-10
On Hardy, try installing the hpoj package from synaptic. It'll walk you through a wizard to find your scanner. (Worked for me anyway.)

If you're looking for a new scanner, the consensus around here, is anything HP is good. Personally, I have an HP Officejet J4550 All-in-One, that works great, and was well under $100. In Hardy, I had to install the hpoj package to find the scanner, in Intrepid, it picked up everything automatically.
