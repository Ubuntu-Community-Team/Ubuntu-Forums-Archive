### Post by t4thfavor on 2010-02-01
There are regular releases scheduled at 04(Apr), and 10(Oct) every year.

Hence the naming convention, 9.10 was released in 2009, 10, and 9.04 2009, 04. with the 04 release geing a LTS release gaining support for a longer period of time.
