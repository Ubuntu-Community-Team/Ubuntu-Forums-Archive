A TV with a built in MPEG encoder will indeed take the strain off your CPU. To get picture in picture you need two tv tuner cards at least. This will also allow you to record two tv channels at once.

The MythTV forums and wiki will be helpful to you;

[http://www.mythtvtalk.com/forum/](http://www.mythtvtalk.com/forum/)
[http://www.mythtv.org/wiki/index.php/Main_Page](http://www.mythtv.org/wiki/index.php/Main_Page)

To the other poster, the Wiki has a list of compatible tv cards for MythTV;

[http://www.mythtv.org/wiki/index.php/Category:Video_capture_cards](http://www.mythtv.org/wiki/index.php/Category:Video_capture_cards)
