### Post by TeaSwigger on 2007-08-11
Hello. Understand that I don't know a thing about Beryl, nor much about Gnome. That said, have you tried checking the "output module" settings for the display problems w/beryl and poking around the "interface" options for the timeline in the mozilla plugin? You can find all that by starting VLC itself, then settings > preferences, and click the Advanced Options check box down near the lower right of the preferences window. I have it set to use the X11 option, it works for my setup... Also I seem to recall you can open the vids in a dedicated VLC window rather than just view them in the web page in firefox; perhaps it'd have a timeline etc when viewed that way. All just thoughts. Great video player, VLC. Good luck.
