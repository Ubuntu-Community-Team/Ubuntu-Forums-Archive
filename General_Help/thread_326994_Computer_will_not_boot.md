### Post by Tomosaur on 2006-12-28
The beep is the result of something called 'POST', which stands for Power On Self Test. This is where you motherboard checks everything is working. You will need to find the documentation for your particular motherboard to find out what the beep means. In my experience, 1 beep means nothing is wrong, but since as far as I'm aware there's no standard for POST, it could mean something different with your mobo. A sequence of beeps generally means some component is malfunctioning, but like I said, you need to the documentation to find out which.
