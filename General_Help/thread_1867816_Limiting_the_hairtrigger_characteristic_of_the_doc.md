You can either:

1) Adjust the timeout threshold in ccsm>Unity
2) Change the Reveal corner to Top Left (also in ccsm>Unity); the method to reveal is different on this setting, to solve exactly the problem you're having: put the mouse in the top left corner (so it can't move any further) and move downwards in a straight line
