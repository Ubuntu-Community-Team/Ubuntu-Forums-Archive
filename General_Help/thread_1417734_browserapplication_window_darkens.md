### Post by johngreth on 2010-02-27
applications and the screen get dark when applications aren't responding. It may mean that the applications you are using were poorly written, don't work well on Ubuntu or don't work well with your specific hardware configuration (the latter is the most likely) You could look for alternatives to those applications or report bugs to the developers. 

Also, if the browser freezes, it is more likely the website's fault rather than the browser's fault.
