### Post by sikander3786 on 2011-02-27
Welcome to the forums :-)

As you said that it was working last month and stopped working now, I assume your PC is capable of booting USB drives.

Did you create a start-up USB for Ubuntu using one of the available software? If yes, the problem shouldn't be there.

If you did a complete install of Ubuntu on to your USB drive and you were on your home PC, I suspect you installed Grub to the MBR of your internal HDD which should be on the USB drive for booting the OS.

We can help you further, let us know which of the case applies to you.
