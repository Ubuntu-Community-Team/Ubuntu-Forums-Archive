### Post by phiphedog on 2007-01-31
I ran XP in VMware and had no problems writing to an NTFS share. I gave my XP a minimum of 256MB and a max of 512MB and was running the programs you mention and I had no problems at all. I also had VM running non stop with no problems at all. The only issues I had was setting up samba so that the 2 OS's could see each other.
