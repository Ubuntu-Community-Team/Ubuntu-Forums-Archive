### Post by Tamlynmac on 2011-03-11
One quick question. Are you using the global menu applet? If so, it removes the shut down applet from the panel and puts the shut procedure in the global menu. 

If Not:
Not sure what occurred to cause the problem. However, you might try deleting one of the name icons and using the shut down applet in the "ADD to PANEL".

Before you start the sequence below check the "ADD to PANEL" to assure the shut down applet is in the list. Right click on a blank space in your panel and select "ADD to PANEL" from the drop down  box.

1. Right click on name icon and un-check the lock to panel (if checked)
2. Delete the icon by right clicking on it and selecting "remove from panel".
3. Then right click on a blank section of the panel and select "add to panel".
4. When the add to panel window opens, find the "shut down" applet and highlight it. Then at the bottom of the window left click on the "ADD" button.
5. This will add the shut down applet to the panel. Move it all the way to the right and lock it down.

Hope this helps.
