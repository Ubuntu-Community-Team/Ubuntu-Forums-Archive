### Post by nubbe on 2007-03-08
I got it working by following [http://openprinting.org/show_printer.cgi?recnum=Canon-PIXMA_MP110](http://openprinting.org/show_printer.cgi?recnum=Canon-PIXMA_MP110) 
and going to the suggested [http://mambo.kuhp.kyoto-u.ac.jp/~takushi/](http://mambo.kuhp.kyoto-u.ac.jp/~takushi/) and following the directions

The driver is for PIXIMA iP1500 Ver.2.50

I used cupsys configuration ([http://127.0.0.1:631/](http://127.0.0.1:631/)) not gnome's printer-progs. (Don't know if it makes a difference...)
