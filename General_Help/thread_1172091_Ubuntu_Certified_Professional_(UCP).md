The UCP is taken through one of the The Linux Professional Institute test partners.

You will need to take three Level 1 exams.

The three exams you need to passed, are LPI 101, LPI 102 and LPI 199 <-- the 199 is the Ubuntu exam.

The exams are offered through Prometric and Pearson VUE test centers. 

The exams cannot be taken online, in person only.

There is some information on the [Ubuntu Wiki]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/EngineerCertification") regarding what might be covered on the 199 test.

Books.
Ubuntu Certified Professional Study Guide (Exam LPI 199)
LPIC-1 In Depth

For online practice you can use the links previously posted.

Also, since you are trying for a distribution specific cert it might be best to use a distribution geared for that cert.
