### Post by Tamlynmac on 2009-08-04
To make a screenshot, simply open "Accessories" and select "Take Screenshot". This will save a copy of your screen (.png) in your folder of choice. Then simply select the paperclip in your forum post toolbar and select/add the file to your post.

I'm not quite certain why this is occurring, but you could try turning off visualizations in Rythmbox either by left clicking on the toolbar icon "visulizations" or select "View" in your and uncheck "visualizations". Does the problem go away when visualizations is turned off?
