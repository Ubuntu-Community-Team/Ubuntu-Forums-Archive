### Post by 3rdalbum on 2009-07-04
The Blu-ray drive will work.

With the DumpHD program, you can rip Blu-rays up to MKBv10 with BD+ protection. With MakeMKV you can rip Blu-rays up to MKBv12, but only when they don't have BD+.

Discs from this year are all MKBv12.

So, for data discs or last year's Blu-ray movies, you'll be fine.
