### Post by kemtnbkr on 2011-11-03
Hi Kirix, I'm having a similar problem here.  I just got a sound card from my friend for free, but I can't figure out how to make it the default sound card.  The default is still the integrated.  My sound card works; I used aplay and it output audio, so that's not the problem. 

I just got applications to output to the sound card by installing pulseaudio and pavucontrol (the GUI control for pulseaudio).  Then I changed my sound card to 'analog stereo' output, and my integrated to 'off'.  I still have to reboot and see if it's a permanent solution, but I think it might be worth a try for you.
