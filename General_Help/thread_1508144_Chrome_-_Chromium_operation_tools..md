### Post by The Cog on 2010-06-12
All of the menus are under the two icons to the right of the address bar - a page of paper and a spanner.

All your history access is at the bottom of the new tab page - the page with eight miniature screenshots that you get when you open a new tab.

Yes, chromium is a little light on options. I like it for two reasons. Firstly, the fact that there's no status bar, and it can remove the title bar means that I get significantly more useful page on my netbook where every pixcel counts. Secondly, we use pages that do a lot of SVG DOM manipulation (graphs with rollovers etc) at work, and chromium is several times faster than firefox at this kind of thing - maybe 5 times faster.
