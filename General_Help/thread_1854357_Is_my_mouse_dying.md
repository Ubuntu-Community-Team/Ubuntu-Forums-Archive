### Post by Mark Phelps on 2011-10-04
I've been using Logitech mice for years with various versions of Windows and Ubuntu, and they all work fine with all the versions.

Have you checked the battery levels in the mouse?  Sometimes, if the batteries are even a little weak, the mouse will behave erratically.
