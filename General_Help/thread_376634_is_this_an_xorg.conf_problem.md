### Post by Sizzler on 2007-03-05
After a quick look you seem to be missing the Horizontal refresh settings.These are the settings from my xorg.conf yours are probably different maybe that will help.

Section "Monitor"
    Identifier     "Generic Monitor"
    HorizSync       31.0 - 95.0
    VertRefresh     50.0 - 160.0
    Option         "DPMS"
EndSection
