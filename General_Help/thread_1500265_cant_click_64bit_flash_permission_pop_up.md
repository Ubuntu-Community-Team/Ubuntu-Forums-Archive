### Post by squaregoldfish on 2010-06-03
I've had this problem for a while too.

The workaround is horrible and repetitive, since the changes you make won't stick between sessions.

1. Load the Adobe Flash settings manager page:
[http://www.macromedia.com/support/documentation/en/flashplayer/help/settings_manager06.html](http://www.macromedia.com/support/documentation/en/flashplayer/help/settings_manager06.html)
(Yes, it's a Web URL, but manages the Flash settings on your machine.)

2. Go to the fourth tab (Website Privacy Settings)

3. Find the website in the list. Select it, and click Always Allow. Your browser will now automatically allow the site to use your camera etc.

As I say, I haven't worked out how to make these settings stick, but then I rarely use it. If you manage to work it out, let us know!
