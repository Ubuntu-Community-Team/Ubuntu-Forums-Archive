### Post by tvphil on 2007-11-07
Try Kompozer, it's not available from Ubuntu, but it's the closest thing in Linux to what you want that works. Go to [www.kompozer.net](www.kompozer.net) and download the latest Debian version from the download page. You'll notice it calls itself the bug free version of NVU. NVU was spawned from the loins of Linspire and even though it's not bad, Kompozer is an improvement on it.
