### Post by snowpine on 2011-02-11
Welcome to the forums! This is a very frequently-asked question, both on Ubuntuforums and on the Web. If you search around you'll find some great comparisons of lightweight Linux distros. My personal suggestions would be Puppy or SliTaz, though to be honest 128mb is on the low end of useful, and I would recommend maxing out your RAM if possible.

I would recommend reading KMandla's excellent blog for some suggestions of how to make the most of your old Thinkpad: [http://kmandla.wordpress.com/](http://kmandla.wordpress.com/)
