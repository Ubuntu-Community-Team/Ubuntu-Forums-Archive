### Post by twanas on 2009-09-30
Hey there,

firstly my apologies if the content of this thread is not exactly suited for 'general help' but i struggled finding a more appropriate match.

I just had to say that after trying many years ago debian, redhat, suse and fedora (at a very elementary level) how absolutely pleased i was to discover ubuntu today..

i am mostly doing financial analysis, programming and day to day tasks such as email etc and after downloading and installing ubuntu today (due to windows 7 crashing 10 minutes after install) i feel there's finally a linux distribution available for the general public in terms of ease of installation, setup and time to functionality.

my application setup steps couldnt have been easier: 
- vmware: provides me with full microsoft office functionality (i need VBA support so open office isnt sufficient)
- matlab for linux
- samba client 
- azureus (bitorrent)

after mounting my network drives, setting up a virtual machine so to run excel in unity mode and had my base analysis tools installed i even had time to enjoy the visual eye candy beryl (maybe renamed now?) and was up and running within an hour. the longest single item was ironically working out why the su command didnt work which was resolved by a quick google search.

i congratulate the ubuntu team for delivering such a wonderful distribution and only regret it took me so long to discover it.

please keep up the good work.

comments/suggestions for a new ubuntu user would be appreciated

(PS: apt-get has also increased the appeal 10 fold, as my previous experiences of installations on linux involved worrying about dependancies and its great to see the initiative taken to remove that mundain tasks from the user)

-twans
