### Post by oceanfirehawk on 2011-05-05
Hello all,

I have been frustrated with Ubuntu's latest edition a number of times. This is because it often requires bug workaround. However, instead of complaining, I installed 10.04.2 LTS. It works great for me and is exactly what I need out of an OS. I would suggest that all of the Ubuntu beginners stick with LTS until they become more comfortable with the OS.

I love Linux these days. My 1.5 year old laptop with "great" specs runs slow on Windows 7. It idles using over 1.5GB of RAM! I use my laptop mainly for school and have spent hours waiting on my system with Windows 7. Ubuntu, however, helps me get my work done fast!

Much thanks to the Linux community for creating OS that people like me can use. At this rate, I won't buy a new laptop until this one dies! I love that, no more upgrading systems!
