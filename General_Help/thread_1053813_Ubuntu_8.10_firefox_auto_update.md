### Post by Yashiro on 2009-01-29
You don't get Firefox updates like that on this distribution.
Firefox and its framework are integrated into the OS to some degree.

Updates are handled by system auto updates/synaptic package manager. 
It's not like Windows even though it looks like it.

When bug fixes are released for your version of Firefox an icon will appear in your tray.
