### Post by t0p on 2009-12-30
To anonymize my browsing, and to circumvent ISP filters and the like, I use [**Your Freedom**]("http://www.your-freedom.net").  It's a Java app so it works on all platforms, and it's free of charge for light or medium use.  There's also a paid-for version for heavy use and if you need port-forwarding; but I've never needed it.
