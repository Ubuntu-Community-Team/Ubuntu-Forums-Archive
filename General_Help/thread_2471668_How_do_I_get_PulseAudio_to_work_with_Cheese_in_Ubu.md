### Post by flyingvluvr on 2022-02-06
I figured out how to get PulseAudio to work with Cheese in configuring your microphone. There are boxes with green check marks on the right side of the PulseAudio screen where your input microphone and output speakers are. 

You just have to click on which input microphone and which output speakers you want to use. The box with the check mark will turn grey when it’s clicked and that will enable them. HTH
