### Post by TheFu on 2018-07-13
Seems to be a common issue with this laptop.
[https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=h7XoUS-o7rU](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=h7XoUS-o7rU) shows how to clean the GPU and CPU heatsinks and put fresh thermal grease on.  I'd vacuum the fan blades while I was at it too.
