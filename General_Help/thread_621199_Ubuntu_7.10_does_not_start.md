### Post by neko18 on 2007-11-23
Recovery mode is not the correct way to run your computer for normal operation.

Try booting normally, then when the computer stops working (wait a couple minutes and be sure the hard drive light isn't still blinking) press Ctrl-Alt-F1. Report back if you get a login prompt.

Edit: Also, while you're at it, try pressing Ctrl-Alt-F7 after you do Ctrl-Alt-F1. When you respond, tell me what happens after each key sequence.
