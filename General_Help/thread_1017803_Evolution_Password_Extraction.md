### Post by pytheas22 on 2008-12-21
If you used the Keyring Manager to store the password, you should be able to unlock it using the Applications>Accessories>Passwords and Encryption Keys utility.  Otherwise, I'm afraid I have no idea where Evolution would store the password.  Could you also contact your email provider to get it back?
