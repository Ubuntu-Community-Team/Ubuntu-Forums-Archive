### Post by cascade9 on 2009-12-01
No, most 'modern' OSes dont use BIOS for I/O. They dont even use the BIOS directly AFAIK. 

Its pretty much used only to ID, test and initalise devices, and to store settings (system clock, CPU/memory/FSB settings, etc) and bootstrapping.
