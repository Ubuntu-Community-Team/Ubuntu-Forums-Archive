### Post by Mark Phelps on 2010-03-22
Here's the link to the Play on Linux download page for Ubuntu:

[http://www.playonlinux.com/en/download.html]("http://www.playonlinux.com/en/download.html")

You will need a network connection to get the .deb file.  But if you just download the .deb file on someone else's machine, you can copy it to your machine.

However, if you persistently do NOT have a net connection, you're going to have major problems installing ANYTHING -- due to dependencies.
