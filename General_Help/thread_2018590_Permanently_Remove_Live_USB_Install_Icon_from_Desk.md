### Post by Karlchen on 2012-07-06
Hello, CerealCypher.

Basically, a live system is a read-only system. So there is no direct way of removing anything from a live system for good.
Your only choice would be by remastering your own live system. Too much hassle just to get rid of 1 single harmless programme launcher.

Cheers,
Karl

I agree with Karlchens explanation, but ...

How do you want to use your live USB system?

- ...

There are different solutions for these options.
