### Post by CatKiller on 2011-07-21
The grey window isn't a problem (you can easily turn it off). It's an indicator that the window has become unresponsive. Why the window is unresponsive is a different question entirely.

To the OP: The fact that it's browsers that are problematic suggests that it's either a plugin problem or a routing problem, depending on whether particular sites or all sites.

To tmy: You've not said which application you're having problems with, but it's a similar solution; work out why your application might become unresponsive.

If your computer can't handle what you're trying to do then you might get unresponsive windows for a while even if there's no problem. The application can't take new input until it's finished what it's doing.
