The only issue that I had was that the first time that I opened Evolution after the update to 2.30 was that Evolution just showed a gray screen and I believe the menu bar also.  I fixed this by doing a reboot of the my system.  Also I did this on a new installation of Ubuntu 10.04 with out Evolution configured.  Quite possible a fix might be to delete the Evolution config/setting files in your home directory and recreate the mail connections.
