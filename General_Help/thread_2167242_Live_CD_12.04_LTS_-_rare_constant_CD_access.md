### Post by coldraven on 2013-08-13
Have you tried cleaning the CD lens? You might just have a hair that occasionally gets lodged in front of it, especially if you have pets.
Cigarette smoke is another bad thing, you can get a film over the lens, it looks clean but does not work correctly.
Also check the CD for marks or scratches.
I clean mine with a cotton bud and methylated spirit. **Very gently** stroke the lens a few times. The lens is supported by **very delicate** springs, do not put any pressure on it or you will damage it.
