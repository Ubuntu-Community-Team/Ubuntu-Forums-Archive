### Post by angrykeyboarder on 2005-05-24
[QUOTE=Jaromba]Is Live DVD faster than Live CD?
Would the higher rotational speed make for better performance?[/QUOTE]

Nope, because a DVD at 1 X is many many times faster than a CD at 1 X.

The data transfer rate on a DVD is 1.5 **mb**/sec.  CDs at 1x are 150**kb**/sec.
Therefore a DVD spinning @ 12 x is transferring data ***much much*** faster than a CD spinning at 48x.

On the other hand, I've used both the live CD and DVD and noticed very little difference in performance. The DVD seems better, but not that much better.
