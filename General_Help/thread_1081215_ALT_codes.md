### Post by mcduck on 2009-02-26
Normal way to type extra characters in Linux is to use a Compose key or AltGr, depending on your keyboard map.

[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Compose_key]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Compose_key")
[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/AltGr_key]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/AltGr_key")

Compose key is used to combine a letter with some other character to form new characters, while AltGr is used directly with a letter to access additional characters, and together with Shift to get access to even more characters.
