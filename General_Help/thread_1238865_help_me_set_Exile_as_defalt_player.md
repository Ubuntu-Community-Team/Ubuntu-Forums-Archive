### Post by fooman on 2009-08-12
right click on one of your mp3 files and choose properties.

when it opens, click the "open with" tab....if exaile is in the list,  put a tick in the box next to it and close the properties box.  if it is not in that list, click "add" and a much larger list will pop up....find exaile in that list,  click once on it and then click "add".  close the properties box.

click a mp3 file and see if exaile opens...it should.

hope that helps.
