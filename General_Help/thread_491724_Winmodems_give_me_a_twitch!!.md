### Post by Bartender on 2007-07-04
I bid on some US Robotics Sportsters on eBay.  You gotta watch out - you'll see auctions for 28.8K and 36K externals, and they look just like the 56K models.  Also, lately it seems a lot of them are being sold without the wall wart (the thingie that transforms 120V AC down) and even without the serial cable!
You can flash the firmware to the latest version by hooking it up to a Windows or Linux PC, going to the USR website, and finding the "stand-alone" flash utility.
Ubuntu is harder than MEPIS or PCLOS because of some stupid things you have to do, but it is doable.  Here are directions for two of them
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=480717](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=480717)

I've seen a couple of USR externals on craigslist too.
