Sometimes people have trouble like this if they are typing on a laptop and their thumbs or palms get near the touchpad. If you are on a laptop you could try disabling the touchpad while typing which is a mouse preference setting on the touchpad tab.

If it is not a laptop, does the table bounce while you are typing?
