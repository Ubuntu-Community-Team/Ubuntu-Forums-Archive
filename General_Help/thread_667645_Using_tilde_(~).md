Now this is probably the shortest tutorial(just a tip really) you will ever see lol but it is a good tip, and helpful to those that are always changing anything from ~./conkyrc to ~./muttrc
so this is done in one step lol
open your terminal and instead of typing <shift tilde> juat click the <page down> key, and behold, you have a tilde in one simple key.
I know this probably isn't the most helpful piece of information, but when I was editing my conkyrc and muttrc it did come in handy.

Note, this only works in the terminal, but it does save time when you are working on a project or any rc file.
