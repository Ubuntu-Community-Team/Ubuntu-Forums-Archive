### Post by Tenken on 2008-01-15
GIMP can't make panel themes, but can make images that can be used for the background of the panel. The panel is actually controlled by the GTK theme, (Crimesaucer wrote a nice [guide]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=377397") on it) but if you right-click on the panel and go to Properties->Background you can use an image you created in the GIMP, like [this one]("http://www.jhenryassociates.com/glasspanel3.png") (I know the page looks blank, but right-click in the upper left corner and you can save the image).
