### Post by Pitbull11188 on 2007-02-02
No it shouldn't take that long, unless you have a massive (750GBish) drive, and even then it should take less time. Resizing my 200gb drive takes ~45-50minutes. I'm sure the partition sizes and amount of information on the disk being resized plays a role, but I've never heard of a resizing taking this long.

What program are you using? gparted, qtparted, or ?

If there is anything of vital importance on the disk I'd wait for the experts to reply, but if you're not that worried about it I would just startover.
