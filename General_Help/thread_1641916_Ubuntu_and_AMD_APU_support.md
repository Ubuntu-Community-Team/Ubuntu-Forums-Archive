### Post by LMP900 on 2011-02-18
Teodore,

According to a [**webpage consisting of a lot of information**]("http://jackharvest.com/?p=15") gathered from [**a very long DM1z thread**]("http://forum.notebookreview.com/hp-pavilion-notebooks/501683-hp-dm1z.html"):

> **Q:** Linux / Ubuntu / Mint Support?

**A:** Almost everything is working. Granted, the Zacate is brand new, so more updates will be coming from individual dev teams to accomodate for the different flavors. Currently, the only thing that’s a head scratcher is the wi-fi.

**craig1984 Mentioned this regarding it:**
Quick update for folk interested in Linux support. Several of us were struggling to get the wireless card working under Ubuntu 10.10 64-bit.  ’Ricardon’ over on the Ubuntu forums has figured out a method. It uses a patched version of the native driver source using mods from SuSE. A little cumbersome but it works. [**Details here**]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?p=10456999#post10456999").

So it looks like it's partially supported, but a bit of work is required to get it fully working. My DM1z won't be arriving until March, so I can't confirm the post.
