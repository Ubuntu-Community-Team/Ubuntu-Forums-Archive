### Post by _sAm_ on 2008-04-23
I installed the media player VLC and the vlc plugin for mozilla. You will find them in Synaptic, do a search for vlc and mozilla-plugin-vlc

Then I installed a add-on for Firefox called MediaPlayerConnectivity([https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/446](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/446)). So now I can open flash that don't work in Firefox in vlc, and it works great.
