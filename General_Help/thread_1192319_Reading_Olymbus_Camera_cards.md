### Post by jm2 on 2009-06-20
I just wanted to share my experience reading the camera card from my Olymbus camera in case it might help someone else. 

I was trying to read my camera card using the Olympus Camedia usb reader/writer Mausb-10. I was able to get my computer to recognize it was connected. But I could not get it to mount. I checked all plug-n-play componets, ports, logs and couldn't figure it out.

I verified I could use that port with a different usb flash drive. No problems there.

So today, I used the usb plug from the camera directly into the computer, and Ubuntu saw the pictures and mounted them with no problems. I could then view my pictures and copy them or whatever.
