Many people have been reporting to the forums that repositories are slow, causing problems with updates and installs via apt/synaptic. This is an appeal to the mods to sticky something to perhaps prevent so many duplicate posts about this problem.

A good temporary solution is to go to System > Administration > Software Sources and under "Download From" select "Other..." and click "Select Best Server".


Presumably, this will go away once the mad dash to update to Hardy has passed.
