### Post by steevven1 on 2011-07-12
Hello,

I have my monitor set to turn off after a certain amount of inactivity in xfce. This feature works perfectly when my screen is not locked, but when I have my screen locked via xscreensaver, my monitor remains turned on (but blank) indefinitely.

How can I fix this?

Thanks in advance!

---

### Post by steevven1 on 2011-07-12
Solution: The screensaver wakes up the monitor from xfce's Power Management settings, but if you set the screen to power off in the Screensaver settings, it does not wake the monitor up. Very strange behavior and setup, but it works.
