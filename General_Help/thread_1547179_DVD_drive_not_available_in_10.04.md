### Post by Adem Stival on 2010-08-09
I had the same and exact problem and it wasn't a hardware failure. I  don't play much DVDs and it took me a while to realize that it was  malfunctioning. The only thing that I had done to my computer that I  could think of, was upgrading my Media Player to version 10.

I went to the website of the DVD drive manufacturer and got the latest drivers and installed them. It fixed the problem.
