### Post by rvk on 2009-08-21
There are quite a few options for speech recognition (voice recognition deals with recognising speakers and speech recognition deals with recognising speech). For example Simon and gnome-voice-control. However, none of them is very end user friendly. All of them are based on either Julius or Sphinx (speech recognition engines).

The best place for you to start would be the forum at VoxForge.org because the number of people with knowledge on speech recognition is rather limited, so you need to go to a website that focuses on speech recognition.

Don't forget to submit some speech as well!
