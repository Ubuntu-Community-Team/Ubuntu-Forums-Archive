### Post by Adam_GUI on 2011-11-26
There's a setting for this in The Compiz Settings Manager.
ALT+F2 ccsm
(If the compiz settings manager isn't installed "sudo apt-get install compizconfig-settings-manager")

There are checkboxes for move, resize, and Maximumize, etc.
Checking resize will allow you to click a corner of a window to resize it.
Checking move will allow you to move a window.  (By top bar or by ALT + Left Clicking it.)
