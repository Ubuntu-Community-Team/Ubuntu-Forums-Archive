### Post by thnewguy on 2012-11-22
Ubuntu 12.10 has some weird quirk that makes it run really really slowly if you're running a 3-D desktop. My guess is you do not have the NVIDIA driver installed and your CPU is at 50% because your processor is trying to render the 3-D desktop. With the NVIDIA driver installed it should transfer the work to your video card.

Or you can downgrade to Ubuntu 12.04, or install GNOME fallback mode. Either would lighten the load of rendering the desktop.
