### Post by Subban on 2007-07-29
When I moved from winXP I to was looking for something to replace Newsleecher (registered user too)

Anyways, linux binary usenet clients are a bit lacking compared to Newsleecher but there is good news, Newsleecher2 (afaik this is right) works pretty well under wine, however I switched to using hellanzb and its done just fine.

Its a native linux client for one, its console only, it monitors a download folder (like you prolly had newsleecher setup) then will download, get pars if needed and unpack to a folder you define.

Its a little fiddly to setup the conf file, but nothing to worry about, give it a look :]
