### Post by darolu on 2010-04-19
Go to Edit - Preferences, then click on the "Behavour" tab and make sure the "Always open in browser window" option is enabled.
Sometimes the left panel is hidden, press F9 to toggle, try resizing it, sometimes it is so small you can't see it.
