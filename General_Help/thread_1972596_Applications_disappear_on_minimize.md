### Post by Tamlynmac on 2012-05-03
> Michael GrabbeIt sounds like you've either moved or deleted the "Windows List" applet. On your bottom panel you should see two, parallel, perforated, vertical dotted lines. From the sounds of your explaination it's probably by the trash icon. This is the Windows List applet. If it's there - right click and select move (or uncheck the "lock to panel", then move) and move the applet to the far left just to the right of any icons. You want a good size blank area in the panel - to the right of the Windows List applet. 

If the applet isn't in the panel, then right click on a blank area in the panel and select "Add to Panel" from the drop down menu. Then select the Windows List applet from the add to panel window. This will place two parallel, perforated, vertical dotted lines in the panel. Right click on them and select move. Move the lines to the left as far as possible without jumping over any icons. Once again, there needs to be as much blank space in the panel as possible to the right of the applet.

After that, when you minimize an application it should show up in the panel - to the right of the Windows List applet. I find it a good to lock the applets and icons to the panel. It helps keep them in place. Just right click on them and check "lock to panel", if it's not already checked.

Good Luck and hope this helps.
