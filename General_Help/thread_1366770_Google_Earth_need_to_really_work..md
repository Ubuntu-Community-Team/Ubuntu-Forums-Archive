### Post by ankspo71 on 2009-12-28
Hi,

Is the problem you are having the fonts are not being displayed properly and streetview does not work? If so I found that the googleearth package from Google.com worked best in Ubuntu 9.04, but the medibuntu version works best in 9.10 and 10.04 (alpha).

Before you try my advice, remove the other googleearth to avoid conflicts.

Go to [http://www.medibuntu.org/](http://www.medibuntu.org/) and click on the "Repository Howto" link.
Follow the instructions for "adding the repository". Paste that big code into your terminal.

This will allow you to add googleearth from the repository using apt-get or Synaptic Package manager. You can also add the files needed to play encrypted dvd and non-native media formats.

Also, Google Earth works best with a 3d capable graphics driver installed.
Hope this helps.
