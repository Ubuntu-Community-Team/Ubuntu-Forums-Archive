### Post by tuxxy on 2008-09-02
Emerald shouldnt change your bottom and top toolbars, emerald will change your window borders.

To change your toolbars to a darker theme which match your emerald theme, install a similar GTK theme from here

[http://gnome-look.org/index.php?xsortmode=down&page=0&xcontentmode=100](http://gnome-look.org/index.php?xsortmode=down&page=0&xcontentmode=100)

EDITR: Also you could just right click on the panels and manually select a solid darker colour, but I would prefer a GTK theme.
