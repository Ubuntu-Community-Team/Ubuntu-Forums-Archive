### Post by Mark Phelps on 2010-09-29
From unforunate personal experience, I've found that the best utilities for recovering files from FAT32/NTFS partitions are MS Windows based utilities.  And the best of them (that I used) is GetDataBack for NTFS from Runtime Software.

You will need a Windows machine to install and run GetDataBack, and you will also need to connect your current drive to that PC.

You can download the free version from their website.  It won't actually recover the files, but it will show you what it found and what the chances of successful recovery are likely to be.

It's your money ... it's your choice.
