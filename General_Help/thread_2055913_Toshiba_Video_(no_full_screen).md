### Post by geronl on 2012-09-10
I feel stupid. I'm just really new though.

After reading all of the similar problems online I thought Toshiba didn't support my drivers with Linux.

Then someone at the Ubuntu facebook page told me to use the command to make the screen resolution work properly or something.

**sudo apt-get install screen-resolution-extra**
