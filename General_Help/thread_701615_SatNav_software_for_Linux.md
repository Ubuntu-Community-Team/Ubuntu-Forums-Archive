### Post by lotus49 on 2008-06-30
Although there has been at least one Linux satnav app, it is old and no longer maintained.  It went by the catchy name "Navigator" and was written by a Czech company, the name of which I forget.


The app is now called PC Navigator and the authors say that they may produce a Linux version but the last time I checked (about a month ago) they had not.


There is always the option of using Wine (sorry to all the people that have undoubtedly worked very hard on Wine but ... eugh!) or VMWare but it's not going to be quick (but then it probably doesn't need to be).
