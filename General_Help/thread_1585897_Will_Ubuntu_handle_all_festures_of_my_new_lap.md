### Post by rhhoek on 2010-10-02
On my 2540p most things work out of the box (Ubuntu-maverick). For the internal mobile-modem you have to install gobi-loader. After that you have to copy the right firmware files from, for example, you windows partition. I found a bug that the gobi-loader does not load at bootup. (work around is to toggle wireless off and on).
What does not work at the moment are the touch-pad disable and auto-screen-brightness buttons/keys.
Also external screen toggle sometimes freezes my system.

### Post by Old_Grey_Wolf on 2010-10-02
Rather than WUBI, try the "live CD" or "live USB" options. Download Ubuntu then burn a live CD, or download Unetbootin as well and make a live USB. That way you can try Ubuntu without making any changes to your computer. When you boot from the Live CD or USB, it gives you an option to try Ubuntu without making changes to your computer.
