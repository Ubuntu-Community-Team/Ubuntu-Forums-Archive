### Post by Spaceman9 on 2008-10-17
Ubuntu and Kubuntu are the same distro. Just different desktops. If you've tried KDE 4 on other distros Kubuntu isn't much different. I have KDE 3.x installed in Ubuntu and that way I can use either one. You don't have to have just one or the other.
