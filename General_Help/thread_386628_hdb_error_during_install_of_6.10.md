### Post by chewearn on 2007-03-17
Need to know what drive is hdb.  This is normally the IDE0 slave.  Is it a harddisk or CD/DVD drive?  You can go into BIOS to find out.

If it is the CD drive, boot into liveCD again on your laptop, but this time select to check disc integrity.  I know you said it is ok on another computer, but maybe you laptop CD drive is just not able to fully read this particular disc (could be due to old age, or just the way the optics are tuned).
