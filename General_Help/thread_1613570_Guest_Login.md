### Post by trikster_x on 2010-11-06
While in a priviliged account, go to:

System>>Administration>>Login Screen

Set the computer to log in as the guest account automatically.  Reboot and it will bypass the login screen completely.  You can also set it to stop at the login screen for a limited amount of time before logging in.
