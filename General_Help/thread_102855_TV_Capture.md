### Post by RAOF on 2005-12-13
Well, there's tvtime or xawtv for just plain watching of the tvcard.  There's mythtv for a full-blown personal video recorder solution.

If linux drivers exist for your card, chances are Ubuntu has already got them.  You might want to check on the linux tv website: [www.linuxtv.org](www.linuxtv.org)
