### Post by forrestcupp on 2008-04-30
Ubuntu is better for old people than it is for young people who don't know what they're doing.  Older people are just going to be happy running the apps that are included and leaving the system how it is without screwing around with things.  It's great for someone like that.

It's when you get a young person who is used to Windows and they want to screw around with things without knowing what they are doing that you'll have headaches.
