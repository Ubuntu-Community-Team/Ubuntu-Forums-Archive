### Post by Whiffle on 2008-03-11
On my T43 those two keys generate regular old keycodes, and are reported as XF86Back and XF86Forward.  You should be able to go into whatever shortcut dialog you have (not sure what it is in gnome..), and map them just like any other key.

That is, assuming yours is like mine...
