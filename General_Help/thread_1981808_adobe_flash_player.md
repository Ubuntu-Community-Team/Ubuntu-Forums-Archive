### Post by shashanksingh on 2012-05-17
Is your machine 64bit?

You should try the firefox 'flash-aid' add-on.
It would make it much easier.

It will automatically detect and install the required version.
You just need to click on it and execute its script.
It'll be a small flash icon top right once you restart firefox after installing it.
