### Post by ShinHadoken on 2010-07-03
First of all, congrats, and welcome to the Ubuntu family!

As far as learning more about Ubuntu, help.ubuntu.com is a really great resource, with community written commentary on more complex subjects.

This forum is also a great place to start. Although it's really more of a place to ask questions and seek help when you have a specific problem or objective, the following link is to the Tutorials and Tips section, which is filled with tutorials and general information, great place to learn.

[http://ubuntuforums.org/forumdisplay.php?f=100](http://ubuntuforums.org/forumdisplay.php?f=100)

One of my personal favorites is Linux Magazine. They always have a lot of interesting articles, some of which are VERY informative and practical. You can register there, subscribe and get emails when a new article comes out in a specific category that you're interested in. The link is:

[http://www.linux-mag.com/](http://www.linux-mag.com/)

Hope these help!
