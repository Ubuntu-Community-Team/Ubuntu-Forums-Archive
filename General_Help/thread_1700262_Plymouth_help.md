### Post by Krytarik on 2011-03-05
The script is meant to fix the possible issue that you don't get a graphical splash screen at all. Judging from what you wrote, that is not the case at your system, instead the splash doesn't fill your whole screen. To solve those, you have to use a splash which supports such an high resolution, take a look at [http://gnome-look.org/](http://gnome-look.org/) , just search for "Plymouth" to filter out all the old splashs, maybe you are in luck.

I use those splash, but I don't know if it supports your resolution:
[http://gnome-look.org/content/show.php/Ubuntu+sunrise+plymouth?content=129696](http://gnome-look.org/content/show.php/Ubuntu+sunrise+plymouth?content=129696)
