### Post by ajgreeny on 2012-02-06
The nearest you can get to that in gnome 2 is to use a right click on the desktop, and enable the "Keep Aligned" option, unless you go for the "Clean up by name" where all desktop icons are arranged in vertical columns, rather than horizontally across the screen.  You can then arrange them to your liking by naming each icon with a number, so they sort by number, if you get my drift.

It is a little annoyance that I also have as I like my desktop icons arranged neatly as well;  I guess we are the old style of computer user, where unity *et al* is a non-starter.  It's certainly not for me, that's why I'm still using 10.04.
