### Post by david_2001 on 2007-02-03
My two cards plus kaffeine get me:
[LIST=1]
[*]UK Freeview TV + Radio (not DAB!).
[*]Record one channel while watching another or record two channels simultaneously.  With one card it's only possible to watch another channel in the same mux (group of channels) while recording.
[*]Pause live TV.
[/LIST]
What's not really possible is:
[LIST=1]
[*]Watching two channels at once.
[*]Reliable real-time encoding to divx/xvid.  I don't know whether mythtv can manage this trick but I've tried using mencoder to record Freeview broadcasts as xvid.  It worked, sort of, but the quality wasn't brilliant even with both cpu cores running at full tilt transcoding the transmission.  Anyway, ProjectX only works with dvb streams, so there's no point.
[*]HDTV.  As far as I am aware, there aren't any Freeview HDTV channels being broadcast in the UK at the moment.
[/LIST]
