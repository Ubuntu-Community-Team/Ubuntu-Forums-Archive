### Post by scout4536 on 2009-11-02
Found The Fix!!!!! by playing around.  Left click on clock panel, expand Locations, and click "set" at the bottom right.  The "set" icon changes to a "home" icon.  Now make sure Show Temp and Show weather are checked in the preferences.  There ya go!!!
