### Post by Mighty_Joe on 2009-07-29
Hard drives are magnetic.  CD's are optical.  Can't use one in the other.  
You could use the case and make a [USB Flash Drive]("http://blog.makezine.com/archive/2009/03/weekend_project_flash_memory_hard_d_1.html"), but the platters just look pretty.
