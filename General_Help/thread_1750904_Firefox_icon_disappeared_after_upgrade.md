### Post by cymbaline42 on 2011-05-06
Happened to me too.  I manually added the logo.  Download the Firefox logo (64 x 64 pixels) from Google Images or similar site, save it to your hard disk.  Right click on the Firefox launcher - Properties - in the launcher properties window, click the image.  Choose the downloaded logo and you're all set.
