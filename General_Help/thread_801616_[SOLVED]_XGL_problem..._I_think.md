### Post by trebor on 2008-05-20
Well I solved it myself.  I did a sudo dpkg-reconfigure-phigh xserver-xorg.  Which cleared all my xorg. file.  Then I had to renable to the ATI driver.  Then I added the section for my ATI diver back into the Xorg file.  Suddenly, not fault at start up, direct rendering, and compiz is working as well.
