### Post by ajgreeny on 2016-09-11
There will most likely be a key to press to bring up a menu for the boot device.  Look carefully at the screen or in your machine manual to see the key; the screen message is very quickly on and off again so you can often miss it.

Installing Ubuntu will not have affected your ability to boot to another device as that is a setting in the BIOS/UEFI which all happens before the OS has even started.
