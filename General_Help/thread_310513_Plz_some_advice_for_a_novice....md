### Post by az on 2006-12-01
> **princemackenzie said:**
>  The legacy drivers might help but I'm not sure how to make them work with new software...

That's one of the problems with vendors releasing binary-only drivers.  Had they been open source, someone surely would have maintained them (even just keeping them working).  Those drivers are written and compiled for an old kernel, on an other distribution.

You will need to buy another modem.  I do not suggest a USB modem.  When you shop around for one, make it clear that you need it to work in linux.  You should not have to be doing a ton of research to buy hardware for your box - that's up to the salespeople at your hardware store.
