### Post by notgary on 2011-11-22
I'm not sure what is it you're asking. Are you wanting to run both Gnome 2 and Gnome 3 on the same system? If so then that may be rather difficult since a loy of the gnome 2 packages have been replaced by or upgraded to a newer Gnome 3 version.

If you're refering to the interface shells in Gnome 2 and 3, then that may also be a problem since the Gnome 2 interface is no longer being maintained by Gnome. There's a [community project]("https://github.com/Perberos/Mate-Desktop-Environment") that's taken up the mantle of maintaining the old Gnome 2 interface, but I'm not sure how successfull they're going to be.
