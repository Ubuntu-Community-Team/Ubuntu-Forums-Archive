### Post by UltimateCat on 2013-02-04
> **jlh68 said:**
> I have an Acer Aspire ONE netbook that I have Ubuntu 12.04LTS (32-bit) installed on.  I can not get it to update, I keep getting the following messange "failed to fetch . . . ."  It is a long list of source sites that it fails to fetch.

What is the solution?

Add the Ubuntu repositories to your sources.list

For example these http sites; my long list of source sites for the Debian distro that I have in my souces.list in order to update or upgrade my system.

```
deb http://ftp.us.debian.org/debian/ squeeze main contrib non-free
#deb-src http://ftp.us.debian.org/debian/ squeeze main contrib non-free

deb http://security.debian.org/ squeeze/updates main contrib non-free
#deb-src http://security.debian.org/ squeeze/updates main contrib non-free

deb http://www.deb-multimedia.org squeeze main non-free

deb http://ftp.us.debian.org/debian/ squeeze-proposed-updates contrib non-free main

deb http://backports.debian.org/debian-backports squeeze-backports main contrib non-free

deb http://mozilla.debian.net/ squeeze-backports iceweasel-release


```


Your sources list is in /etc/apt/sources.list
I used Nano to edit my sources.list in the terminal
You might have Gidget instead-

Adding the repo's to your sources.list is the solution.
