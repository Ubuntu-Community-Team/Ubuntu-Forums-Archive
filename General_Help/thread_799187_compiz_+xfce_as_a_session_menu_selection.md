### Post by Forlong on 2008-05-19
You can use the "save session" feature when logging out to load Compiz by default.

A separate "Compiz session" would be way too much of a hassle, after all, Compiz is just a window manager.
Use applications like Compiz-Switch (see below) or fusion-icon to switch between window managers.
