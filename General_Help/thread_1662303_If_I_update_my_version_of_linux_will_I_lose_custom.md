### Post by judedawson on 2011-01-07
Hi  block g raptor,

I agree with sammiev. Your PC's desktop is fabulous! 

Yes, since 9.04 isn't an LTS, the safest path would be to first upgrade to 9.10 and then 10.04 and finally to 10.10. 

Do the upgrade carefully. I would recommend a day or two between each upgrade just so that you are able to check and determine each upgrade goes well.

Full details on upgrade procedures are available at :
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/UpgradeNotes](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/UpgradeNotes)
