### Post by mixmaster on 2008-06-13
The problem with "just use it" is that you end up using just a subset of the features ('cause you didn't know to look for the others) and you may often be doing things awkwardly because you don't know that there is a better/simpler way.  It can also be disheartening.

Forum monitoring is good - even after using linux for years I still sometimes see something and think "I didn't know you could do that" but it is worth following some of the links to getting started tutorials.
