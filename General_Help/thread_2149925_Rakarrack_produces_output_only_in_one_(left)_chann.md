### Post by rva1945 on 2013-05-30
To connect the electric guitar to the PC mic input, I built a mono-to-stereo adapter and it works: when running VLC (VideoLan) opening the audio capture, the sounds comes out of both channels, though in mono mode. The same when I run Audacity and playing back while recording.


But Rakarrack (Jack dependent) produces sound in left channel only. I even tried plugging the stereo mic that comes with the headset and Rakarrack does the same (left channel only), and yes the mic produces stereo sound in VLC and Audacity, so this is not a hardward or mono-to-stereo adapter problem.


Any hint?

---

### Post by rva1945 on 2013-05-30
In Rakarrack, Settings/preferences/jack, system:playback_1 and system:playback_2 stand for left and right channel, by ctrl+clicking on them you can turn both on.
