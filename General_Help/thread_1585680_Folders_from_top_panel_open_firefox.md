### Post by fooman on 2010-09-30
press the alt-f2 keys to bring up the run application dialog box.  when it opens,  type "nautilus" (no quotes) in the space and press enter.

when nautilus opens,  right-click on your desktop folder and choose *open with > other application* from the drop down menu.

from the *open with* menu....double-click on "open folder"

that should fix it.
