1.Enable brightness, opacity, and saturation. 
2.Set the increase and decrease buttons for any of the categories and test one. 
3.Are you able to make something completely saturated, dark, or  transparent? 

On mine you can't, unless you have the step set to 1 or 3.

other info:
I actually have practical uses for this compiz feature and rely on it, depending on the situation. I would rather press a button to completely saturate something; it's very good for reading in a dark room when you invert colors.
