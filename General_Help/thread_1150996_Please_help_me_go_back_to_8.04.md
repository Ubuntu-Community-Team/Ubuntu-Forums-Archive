### Post by dabl on 2009-05-06
> **Bulova said:**
> 

If I save my home folder to a thumb drive, how to I connect it to the re-installed 8.04?

Any other tips, tricks about going back?


To avoid version conflict problems, I would advise NOT saving the hidden "." files in your /home/user folder -- meaning you will need to start out with no custom settings in your new 8.04 installation.  Just save all the data files and folders on your thumb drive.

So, with that done, you can use your installation CD, and mark the partition to be formatted during installation, and you should end up with a new 8.04 system into which you can copy your data from the thumb drive, and proceed to use it.
