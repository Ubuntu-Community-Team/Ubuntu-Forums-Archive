### Post by fooman on 2008-10-05
you can grab some emerald themes (and a ton of other eye candy) from here:

[http://www.gnome-look.org/](http://www.gnome-look.org/)

look for *emerald* themes under "compiz" or "beryl" on the left.  to install them...download a theme of your choice to your desktop or folder in your home directory.  right click the file and choose "extract here".  then open emerald theme manager (system > preferences > emerald theme manager).  look under themes settings > themes...click on the "import" button and navigate to the file you just downloaded/extracted (look for file ending with .emerald).  click the "open" button and the theme should install.
