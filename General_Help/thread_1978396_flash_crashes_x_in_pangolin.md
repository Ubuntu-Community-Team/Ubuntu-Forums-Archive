I installed pangolin a week or two ago and X has been (seemingly) randomly restarting. I finally narrowed down the problem to flash player. I tried manually installing any version I could find off of adobe's site to no avail. Finally fixed it by pulling the libflashplayer.so file off of my version of Lucid. No more crashes so far. 

The version of flash that Chrome is reporting is 11.2 r202. I spent about 15 minutes trying to figure out how to file a bug report, and gave up and decided to post here.
