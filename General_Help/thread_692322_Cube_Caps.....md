### Post by fooman on 2008-02-09
i think you may be looking in the wrong section.  if you go to CompizConfig Settings Manager > desktop > desktop cube > appearance ....the "cube caps" option will only change the *top* image.  

if you look under > CompizConfig Settings Manager > utility > cube caps ...you will find the options to change both the top and bottom images.
