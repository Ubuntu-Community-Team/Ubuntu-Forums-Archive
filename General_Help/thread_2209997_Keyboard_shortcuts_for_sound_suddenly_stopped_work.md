### Post by davy jones on 2014-03-08
After installing some regular updates, the keyboard shortcuts for volume up, down, and mute suddenly stopped working. I tried to change the shortcuts but none of them are working. Please help

---

### Post by davy jones on 2014-03-10
The problem was caused by an update in the gnome-settings-daemon. Downgrading it to version 3.4.2-0ubuntu0.6.4 solves the problem.
