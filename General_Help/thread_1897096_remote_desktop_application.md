### Post by Lars Noodén on 2011-12-18
You can [tunnel VNC over SSH](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/VNC).  VNC is useful but by itself, it is insecure.  If you tunnel it, you can get the security benefits of SSH.  

For maintenance, you want just SSH.  But for assisting her through on screen tasks, VNC will give you a view of her desktop.
