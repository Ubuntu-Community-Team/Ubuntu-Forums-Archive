### Post by Ek0nomik on 2007-06-15
So you have it formatted as FAT32 and it isn't being auto mounted?

You can try adding this to your /etc/fstab file:

```
/dev/sdb1 /media/yoursharefoldername vfat auto defaults 0 0
```

I believe sdb1 is your external USB device per your fdisk.
