### Post by SuperSonic4 on 2009-10-13
With 6gb ram you do not need swap.

Swap is used when there is not enough RAM - bear in mind computers had next to no RAM intially.

You can still use it if you want to hibernate in which can Swap = RAM

### Post by jeremyswalker on 2009-10-13
Well, like SuperSonic said, swap is used when your available RAM is low and when/if you hibernate your system. My desktop has 4GB RAM and my laptop has 2GB. My desktop has never used any swap space, to my knowledge. The only time my laptop has ever used swap was when I used Virtualbox, and allocated a large amount of RAM for the VM. So, I would say that you do not need any swap. Of course, it won't hurt to make one just in case.
