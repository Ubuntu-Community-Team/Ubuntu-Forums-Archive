### Post by Karl S. on 2009-06-16
bump. same problem.

----

Later, Fixed problem. Close rhythmbox. Go to your home folder, CNTRL+H to show hidden folders, go to .local/share/rhythmbox and move rhythmdb.xml someplace else (or if you're feeling dangerous, just delete it). Open rhythmbox and rebuild your library. If your situation's like mine, your problem should be fixed.
