In Ubuntu 10.04 there is no 'Keyboard Indicator' applet from the applet list.

What you do now is you configure a second keyboard layout in System»Preferences»Keyboard»Layouts and automatically the Keyboard Indicator will appear.

Avoid installing additional packages as it may mess up your desktop; the keyboard indicator change comes from the GNOME Desktop. See [http://library.gnome.org/misc/release-notes/2.30/](http://library.gnome.org/misc/release-notes/2.30/) and search for 'Keyboard Indicator' for the details.
