### Post by mb_webguy on 2008-09-08
I've reinstalled several times in the past year, mostly to tweak my partitioning setup.  Reinstalling is fairly fast and painless.  If you're reinstalling to fix problems, and you have a separate /home partition, you may want to consider backing up your /home partition and then reformatting it, since your problems may stem from bad configuration files.

Also, reinstallation is a great time to reconsider your partitioning scheme.  A separate home partition is definitely useful, as is a separate partition for data if you have a dual-boot setup...
