### Post by aysiu on 2007-04-12
It's safe to delete those folders. If you want to be extra safe, try renaming one of the files. If you see unfortunate side effects, you can always rename the folder to its original name. If you don't see unfortunate side effects, you can just go ahead and delete the folder.

Just so you know, though, those folders and files don't take up much room.
