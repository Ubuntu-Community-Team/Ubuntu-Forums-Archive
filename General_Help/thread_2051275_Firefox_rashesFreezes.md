### Post by critin on 2012-09-01
It could be that some of your plug-in's are not compatible with this new version of FF.  You could check if some need updating or disabling.  Although FF usually disables any that are not compatible, it may have not have this time.

Have you already looked at Help and Troubleshooting from the Help link on FF?
Is your "Submit Crash Reports" checked in the Advanced tab in preferences?  That might give you some answers.
> 
It seems when the monitor shuts off and I have to log back in is when this happens most often.So sometimes it works?  There is a setting to NOT lock the screen when inactive.  The first thing I do when installing a new version is to change the default to NOT require a password, do NOT lock screen when monitor goes to sleep.  You could try that.

I'm not on Precise ATM so cannot point to the exact spot, but DO NOT use the LOCK Screen link that is located under your user name in the top panel.  That will immediately lock your screen.  Look in the menu settings.
