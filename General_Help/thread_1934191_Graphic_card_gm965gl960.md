### Post by Mark Phelps on 2012-03-04
If you were really NOT using the right graphics drivers for your Intel GM965 chipset, then you would be seeing only a Command Line Interface, not a desktop.

Unlike with AMD and Nvidia, there are no Additional Drivers offered for Intel chipsets; instead, the working Intel driver is installed by default during system setup.
