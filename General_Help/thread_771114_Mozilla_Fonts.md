### Post by MaindotC on 2008-04-27
In firefox, click "Edit" and then "Preferences".  On the "Content" tab there should be an option for you to change the font sizes.  The "Advanced" button allows more granular control.  You probably want to modify the "minimum" and "maximum" font sizes.
