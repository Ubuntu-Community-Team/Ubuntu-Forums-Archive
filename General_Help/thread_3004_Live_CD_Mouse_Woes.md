### Post by BWF89 on 2004-11-19
Some live distros (like Damn Small Linux and Feather) have a thing where it won't accept your mouse unless it's plugged into a USB slot. I have a USB mouse but the back of my computer (where I plug in my mouse) doesn't have any USB plugs so it has an adaptor and it still won't accept it. So your best bet is if your mouse is USB is plug it into a USB slot on your computer. Or if it isn't go out and get a cheap USB mouse...
