### Post by Lunx on 2010-03-06
Can't help you with the second part of your post relating to pulse audio, but I'm wondering if your 3D problem is because you haven't enabled Extra visual effects (you'll find it under **System > Preferences > Appearance > Visual Effects **). 

Have you got **CompizConfig Settings Manager** installed? You can find it in Synaptic. Also a good idea to download **Fusion Icon** as this will let you set your window manager, and also provides a quick link to Compiz Config Settings Manager.
