### Post by jespdj on 2007-07-10
Which burning program are you using? Does it give an error?

Try a different brand of empty CD-R's.

To update the firmware of your CD writer, search Internet for "firmware <brand and model name of your CD writer>". Or look on the website of the manufacturer of your CD writer, search for downloads or support or something similar, they might have a firmware update available.

What is the brand and model of your CD writer anyway? Is it very old? It's strange that it doesn't recognise a CD-R.

By the way, if really nothing works, you could consider getting a new CD writer. I just bought one last weekend (because my old DVD writer was acting up). DVD writers are incredibly cheap these days, I paid &#8364; 32 for it (US$ 43, with today's record exchange rate of 1 &#8364;  = 1.37 US$...).
