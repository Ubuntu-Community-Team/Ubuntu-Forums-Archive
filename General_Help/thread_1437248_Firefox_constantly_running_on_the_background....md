### Post by alberto ferreira on 2010-03-23
Try updating firefox. Have you got an old user profile in firefox? It may be loading a lot of your history, passwords, and other login details.

Try also running "firefox -safe-mode" in the terminal or with Alt+F2.

If it doesn't cause trouble that way it's because your profile is too bloated and you should create a new one or clean up up to some degree your current profile.

If you didn't notice any change it is a problem of your edition I believe.

good luck
