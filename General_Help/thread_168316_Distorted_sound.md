### Post by Bloch on 2006-04-30
Good that you got it sorted out.
On my system too the sound used to randomly become distorted. Then I opened Applications --> Sound & Vision --> Volume Control and set PCM down from the maximum.

Hopefully other people with sound problems will try to see if this solves it.
