### Post by infinitybot on 2012-02-17
Give any text box focus and hit [Ctrl + Shift + U]. You will see an underlined letter "u" appear. Now enter the hexadecimal string you want (But keep holding Ctrl and Shift , you can leave U ) . Example : ctrl + shift + u + 263a GIVES  ":)" (without the colour)
