### Post by nimonika on 2011-10-14
I accidentally manage to drag an image onto the top panel for gnome and now the background of the image is visible on the desktop. I do not know how to get rid of that image. Please can someone help.

---

### Post by crdlb on 2011-10-14
If you're on Oneiric, hold Alt and right click on a blank space on the panel -> Properties -> Background: None.

If you're not on Oneiric, do the same thing but without the Alt key.
