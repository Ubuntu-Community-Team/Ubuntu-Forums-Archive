### Post by Favux on 2011-11-25
Hi Silvenium,

The stylus is suppose to automatically shut off touch when it is in proximity.  But if your natural drawing position holds it far enough away then touch will work I suppose.  You can use the touch-toggle script attached to the [BambooPT HOW TO]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1515562") instructions to bind a tablet key to the script in part VII.  This duplicates the functionality in Windows.
