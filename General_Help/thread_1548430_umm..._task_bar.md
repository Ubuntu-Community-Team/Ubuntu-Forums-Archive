### Post by Zorgoth on 2010-08-08
You may have deleted your bottom panel, or deleted the taskbar from it.  You can get it back by adding a panel on the bottom if one isn't already present, and adding an applet to the bottom panel (just right click somewhere without an applet), and select the taskbar.

### Post by WorMzy on 2010-08-08
There's no such thing as a taskbar on Ubuntu. The thing that windows minimise to is called the Window List applet, and it lives on a panel.

Right-click on blank space on the panel, click "Add to Panel", and look for the Window List applet. Once found, just drag it onto the panel where you want it.
