### Post by paul71 on 2005-03-03
I use mp3Tag – a good mp3 tag editor, that works with mp3, wma, ogg, mp4, ape, m4a.

Fortunately it can import information from FreeDB, and also from Amazon.
[http://www.maniactools.com/soft/mp3tag/freedb.shtml](http://www.maniactools.com/soft/mp3tag/freedb.shtml)
[http://www.maniactools.com/soft/mp3tag/import_tags_from_amazon.shtml](http://www.maniactools.com/soft/mp3tag/import_tags_from_amazon.shtml)
Besides, mp3Tag can automatically search for lyrics and album cover.
