### Post by iaculallad on 2008-07-14
> **solarwind said:**
> Is there a cookie cleaner for Firefox in Linux? Something similar to CCleaner but for Linux.

You don't need to install a second/third party extension/addon/utility to do that, Just press Ctrl+Alt+Del when you're on Firefox to clean your Cookies, History, Saved Password, etc..

You can configure this by editing various settings under Firefox's Edit->Preferences->"Privacy"->"Private Data" section.

### Post by Psimon on 2008-08-01
There is a firefox extension called Cookie Culler. You can find it here:

[https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/82](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/82)

It enables you to protect the cookies you want and then with a single click delete all the others.

Highly recommended.
