---

### Post by mc4man on 2011-10-16
No one got around to rebuilding the extension for gtk3 - A bug I have on this has languished.
What i do here to get working, has been fine over the last 4 months or so thru the dev & release.
 ```
sudo cp /usr/lib/nautilus/extensions-2.0/libnautilus-gksu.so /usr/lib/nautilus/extensions-3.0/
```
