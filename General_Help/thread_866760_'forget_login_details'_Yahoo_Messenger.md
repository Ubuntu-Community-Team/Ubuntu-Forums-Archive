### Post by loell on 2008-07-22
yeah, there is one

[gyachi]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=773802")

after installing, click setup --> options

**uncheck** the **auto-login at startup**

and

**uncheck** the **save password in configuration file**


hope that helps.. :)
