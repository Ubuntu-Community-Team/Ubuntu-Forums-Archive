Well, after fighting this for several hours, i  figured it out.  the solution to "get-back" the disappearing launchers/icons was the following:

1. bring up system settings,
2. find and click on appearances, then click on
3. the Behaviors tab.

then turn OFF things or click on the "Restore Default Behaviours"

Presto: the vertical launchers are back.
