### Post by cmost on 2009-05-06
Come on now, if all the hype and buildup to Jaunty hasn't convinced you to switch than nothing will.  All kidding aside, if your current system is meeting your needs just fine, then leave it alone. You don't need to worry about what the community thinks.  Do what YOU think is best.
