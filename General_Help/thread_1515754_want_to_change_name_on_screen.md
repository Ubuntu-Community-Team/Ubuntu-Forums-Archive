### Post by ba_saish on 2010-06-24
I feel the simple solution for you is to create new users by the name you want. 
go to  
system->administration->users and groups

Click on unlock, and enter your password. 
Click on Add user, now give the name that you wish to see on the panel and the respective password.
Do the same for the second user. 
If you need administrative privileges for these users then click on the privileges tab , and  check the administrative privileges options.

Now log off, and log in as the new user. 
You should see the new user name on the panel 

I hope this is what you were looking for.
