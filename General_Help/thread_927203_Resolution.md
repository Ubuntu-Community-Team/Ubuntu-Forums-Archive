### Post by JustAnotherVagueAnon on 2008-09-22
I solved this one... Actually, the last time I did a hard restart was accidental... I tripped the power switch of the power strip that my computer and monitor were connected to. I turned my computer off and unplugged everything and now it's recognizing my monitor correctly again. Some kind of system setting is being stored in a volatile way after shutting down? Whatever it was is fixed now.
