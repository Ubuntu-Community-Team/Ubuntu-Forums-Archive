### Post by SoFl W on 2010-05-24
Yes, turn off your screensaver, or increase the timeout.

SYSTEM->Preferences->Screensaver

---

### Post by stinkeye on 2010-05-25
You can also turn off lock screen in SYSTEM->Preferences->Screensaver.

You can disable power saving options (including the screen saver)
using the Inhibit Applet.
Right click on panel > add to panel > Inhibit Applet

Left click on applet toggles on/off.
