### Post by bollix47 on 2008-04-24
Feisty was a regular release as opposed to LTS.  All regular releases are supported for 18 months, so support for Feisty should end in October 2008.

[More info.]("http://www.ubuntu.com/products/whatIsubuntu/releases")
