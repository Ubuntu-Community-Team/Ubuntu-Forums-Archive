### Post by peabody on 2008-01-14
Is tap-to-click (some times called tapping) enabled on the laptop's touchpad?  Mine was per default.  I installed the gsynaptics package which put a Touchpad control panel in my preferences.  From there I disabled tapping.  See if that works for you.
