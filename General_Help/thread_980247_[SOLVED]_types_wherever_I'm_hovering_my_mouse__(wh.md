### Post by jsegars on 2008-11-12
If you go to Applications->Preferences->Mouse. Select the Accessibility tab and there's a section called "Dwell Click". Underneath, the first checkbox says "Initiate click when stopping pointer movement"....I'm assuming this is checked for you (at least it sounds like your issue). If it is then uncheck it and you should be fine.
