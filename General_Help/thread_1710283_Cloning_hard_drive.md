### Post by howefield on 2011-03-19
> **TimmyK. said:**
>  What does OP stand for?

OP = Original Post(er)

Burn the iso image to a cd, just like you would with an Ubuntu downloaded iso (if you did that).

From the Applications > Sound & Video > Brasero Disc Burner and select the Burn Image option.

You should then end up with a bootable live cd from which Clonezilla will be run.

You might want to do a search on youtube for clonezilla demos, there is bound to be plenty. Might give you an idea of the process.
