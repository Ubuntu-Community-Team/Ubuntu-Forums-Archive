### Post by cariboo on 2008-12-01
I would suggest you create a new profile. Open Places-->Home Folder and press Ctrl-h, this will reveal the hidden directories and files. Make sure Firefox is closed the delete .mozilla directory. Next restart Firefox and everything should be as it is supposed to be.
