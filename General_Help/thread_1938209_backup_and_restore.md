### Post by jerrrys on 2012-03-09
Three that I use:

[clonezilla]("http://clonezilla.org/")

[grsync]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Grsync")

[luckybackup]("http://luckybackup.sourceforge.net/")

grsync and luckybackup is part of the [rsync]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rsync") family (installed by default in ubuntu) and both can be found in the software center.
