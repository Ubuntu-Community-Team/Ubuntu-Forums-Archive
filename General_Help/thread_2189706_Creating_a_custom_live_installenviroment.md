### Post by heir4c on 2013-11-23
Use a usb and install Ubuntu on it like you do on a HD.
Than you can use that to boot Ubuntu where you want and on any computer you want. 
With online datastorage like UbuntuOne or DropBox or other you can always on documents or other stuff you want to use.
Of course you can put files on that install too if you want.
It will work slower than a install on a HD but that depentent on the speed of the Usb and the computer you use.
