### Post by swisscow on 2008-10-23
One way is to put in the ubuntu livecd and use gparted (partition editor in administration menu) to resize your partitions.

You do however risk breakage so a back up is obviusly essential, both windows and ubuntu important stuff, and what the effect of a resized partition will have on vista I have no idea at all.

In the past I tried a resize with xp and ubuntu - the outcome wasn't pretty. Just deleted everything in the end, resized, reformatted then installed.

good luck
