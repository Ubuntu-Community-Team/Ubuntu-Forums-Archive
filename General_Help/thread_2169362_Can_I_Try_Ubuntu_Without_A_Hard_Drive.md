### Post by SweetAurora on 2013-08-21
Sure you can! Ubuntu dosen't need a hard drive to work or test drive in Live Mode. 

If you have a spare, large (8GB or larger) USB flash drive lying around, you could install Ubuntu to that untill you obtain a new Hard drive! But it takes a bit of work to do that though. Or if you want, just make the USB "persistant" to store your files and settings.:)
