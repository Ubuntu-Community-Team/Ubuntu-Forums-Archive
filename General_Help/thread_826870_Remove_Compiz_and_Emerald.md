### Post by ajgreeny on 2008-06-12
> **Houli said:**
> Hey I wanna go back to a default Xubuntu look. I have Emerald, AWN and Compiz installed plus the extra plugins. I want to use xfce4 for my desktop and xwfwm4 for window managing and I want to use xfce4-panel for panel I guess. Please reply.
There should be no problem with this, assuming you can remember what you have installed.  If you look in Synaptic>>File>>History and find emerald you will also find any dependencies needed for that, just libemeraldengine0, I think.  Uninstall that and also compiz if you want.

To be honest, why bother to get rid of them; unless you are very short of hard disk space you might just as well leave them but simply not use them.  Set your compiz settings to "None" and use another theme instead of emerald and all should be well.
