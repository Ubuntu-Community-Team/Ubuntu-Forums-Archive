### Post by stinger30au on 2009-08-25
sounds like hardware for sure


check the basics

make sure the cpu fan is clean

maybe even remove the cpu heat sink and clear away old heatsink compound and apply fresh stuff, dont go stupid with it as too much is worse then none at all

check the hdd for defects with this [http://www.ultimatebootcd.com/](http://www.ultimatebootcd.com/)

check power supply fan is spinning as it may have frozen solid and the psu is cooking itself and going nuts

check all capacitors on the mobo and make sure no swollen ones there

reseat all cables,plus and ram

then tell us how u went
