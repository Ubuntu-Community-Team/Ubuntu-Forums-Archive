### Post by Krytarik on 2011-04-28
To reset Unity:
- press Ctrl+Alt+T to launch a terminal
- run:
```
unity --reset
```- relogin

Greetings.

EDIT: If you haven't fixed it already, I just earlier noticed that  without having the "Dash" running, Ctrl+Alt+T will also not work. But  you can instead create a launcher at the desktop with either  "gnome-terminal" or the command itself, or log in to "Ubuntu Classic" or  "Ubuntu Classic (No effects)".
