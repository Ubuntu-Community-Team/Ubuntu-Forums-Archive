### Post by Elfy on 2008-12-15
They are a really 2 different things, a mount point could be a folder in a folder .....

There are 3 basic types of partition, primary, extended and logical. There can be a maximum of 4 primaries - one of which can be an extended, logicals are made inside an extended and  while there is a limit - it is quite a few.
