### Post by rosslaird on 2005-05-01
I think you're stuck. I've spent dozens of hours researching this over the past year, and there are no good calendar alternatives that I have found (unless you want to go web-based or into php, and then there are some marginal options). If you don't like Evolution, stick with Thunderbird and Sunbird (which is supposed to have a 1.0 version available by mid-2005). I know that the Sunbird extension for Thunderbird is not ideal, but it's OK, and it does work, and it's faithul to the ical standard (as is the Evolution calendar).

I use both Evolution and Thunderbird/Sunbird extension. Evo is smoother, and more integrated in terms of the various functions (such as the calendar), but TB handles mail much better. The weak point in TB is the calendar. It's a toss up between these two apps. I suppose you could switch to KDE and use Kontact, but that seems a long way to go.
