### Post by 73ckn797 on 2009-05-25
Are all available sources selected in System/Administration/Software Sources/Third-Party Sources? Also make sure you have restricted and multiverse sources selected. That might be why you do not have the video drivers available.

For sound, right click on the icon on the upper panel and open volume control. Run the levels up on all and make sure that they are not individually muted as would be seen on the small speaker icon below each slide control. I lost sound the other day and it was just a simple unmuting where I describe, at each slide. The main volume showed it was not muted when doing a right click on the desktop upper panel icon.
