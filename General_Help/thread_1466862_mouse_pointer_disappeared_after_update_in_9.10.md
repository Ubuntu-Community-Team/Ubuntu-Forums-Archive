Running 64 bit Ubuntu 9.10, just went through update manager (today, April 30, 2010) and downloaded and installed a bunch of stuff.  Then after rebooting, no mouse pointer at all.

---

### Post by raymondvillain on 2010-05-01
Solved!

Downloaded and installed the latest graphics driver from ATI.

Motherboard has built in Radeon 3200 graphics chip, and, unkown to me, ATI has recently come out with the latest Linux driver for this setup.  So all you folks with ATI graphics are advised to keep their ATI drivers current, especially when updating Ubuntu.
