### Post by M.A.L.M. on 2014-08-28
Just COPY the .deb file you downloaded from the Epson site over to your flashdrive. That file is the installer for your printer drivers. Whenever you need to install those drivers again you can just do it from your flashdrive, by executing (double clicking) that .deb file.
