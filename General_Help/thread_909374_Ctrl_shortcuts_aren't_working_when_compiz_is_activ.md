### Post by andreasoz on 2008-09-03
As the headline reads, my Ctrl-shortcuts stops working when visual effects in Ubuntu are activated. When I turn them off, the shortcuts work again.

Thanks in advance

### Post by tuxxy on 2008-09-03
This is likely because compiz once enabled will have shortcuts of its own assigned to the CTRL key, I would suggest go through your effects and remove any that have been binded to that key or just make a new shortcut for firefox?

---

### Post by andreasoz on 2008-09-03
I installed ccsm and removed all shortcuts that used Ctrl, and that solved the problem.
