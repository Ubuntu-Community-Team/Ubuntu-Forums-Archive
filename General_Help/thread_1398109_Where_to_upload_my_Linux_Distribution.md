### Post by ks07 on 2010-02-04
Hi,

In case you don't find a suitable host for your 2gb iso, you could try splitting it as part of an archive into smaller chunks that will be accepted by other sites you have tried. You can do this in Ubuntu's default file archive program, when on the save dialog click 'Other Options' and there you can specify a max size for each part of the download. Not the easiest of solutions, but if you really dont want a torrent and cant find a suitable host, that may be your only option. :)
