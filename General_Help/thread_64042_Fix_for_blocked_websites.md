### Post by essexman on 2005-09-09
I am posting this fix for anyone who is expiriencing the wierd and rare problem of having a few unexplained, blocked websites.

The fix was on [Linuxquestions](http://www.linuxquestions.org/questions/showthread.php?s=&postid=1844560#post1844560)

I was unable to browse (on any browser) or ping either [www.gnome.org](www.gnome.org) or [www.ebuyer.com](www.ebuyer.com) (either from my two machines or the modem's own ping facility).  This has been dogging me for 3-4 months until tonight. First I rebooted the modem as per the post - no luck. Then I rebooted my wireless router.

All is well and all websites are now accessable.

This has nearly driven me mad.

I have checked this a hundred ways.  I've played around with the IPV6, and different browsers and settings. 

Reboot the router. Switched it off for 20 seconds. Who would have believed it?

Hope this helps someone else.
