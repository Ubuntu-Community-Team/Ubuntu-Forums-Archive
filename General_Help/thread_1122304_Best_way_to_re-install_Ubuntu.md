### Post by zephyrcat on 2009-04-10
OK. A few questions:

Do you have your /home directory on a separate partition? If you don't know the answer, it is probably no.

Do you have files that you want to keep? I am assuming the answer is yes.

Do you have applications or modifications that you are concerned about keeping? (Rather than reapplying/installing them later.)

If the answer to the first question is yes, then you should be able to simply put in the Live CD and install, but choose custom partitioning and choose to mount your exisitng /home directory as /home.

Otherwise, back up your files and do a fresh install. This will not, however, preserve your installed applications and any modifications/settings.

As always, back up your data first!
