### Post by prem1er on 2009-05-11
Just click on the java or flash test and then select a server that has a green bar underneath it.  It will send measured packets to and from the server that will give you a good idea of your speeds.  You should run the test at different times of the day and get an average.

Also, the speed from your provider to your computer is not important.  You should be looking for the speed from the source (i.e. somewhere on the web) to your router/network that is important.
