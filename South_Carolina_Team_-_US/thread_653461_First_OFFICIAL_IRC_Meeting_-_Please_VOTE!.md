We will be having official meetings stagged once a month (during weeknights Monday - Thursday at 8:00 P.M. EST) for members.

*Members **must **attend ONE meeting in a 6 month period of time on IRC (the official meeting) to be considered "active" in the team. *This was decided at the meeting. This rule will go in affect starting at the next meeting. The date and time will be announced in early February on here, the web site, the Wiki and the mailing list. Plenty of opportunities will be offered.
