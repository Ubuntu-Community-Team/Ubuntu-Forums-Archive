### Post by recluse2 on 2008-08-29
UCLUG will be hosting an event at the Greenville County library in downtown Greenville on September 20th, 2008 from 10am until 3pm.

Included among the events that we will be hosting:

*Installfest - Bring your PC and have UCLUG members help you install Linux on it.

*FLPC - UCLUG's Free Linux PC (FLPC) program will be distributing several free PC's to a few lucky families and individuals on a first-come-first-served basis.

*Classes - Want to get some entry level computer training on using applications in Linux?  Join us!

*Free Software Distribution - Get a Linux "Live CD" and a copy of the Open CD for free.

Just thought I would pass this on to those who do not follow UCLUG or FLPC. I may try to be there, it would be nice to see some of these PC's make it to people who need them.
