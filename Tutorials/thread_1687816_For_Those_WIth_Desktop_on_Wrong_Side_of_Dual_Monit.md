### Post by shibby4555 on 2011-02-14
Ok,
So I had this issue when activating the video driver for Ubuntu in 10.10 on my dual monitors. The desktop panels were all on the right side monitor when I definitely wanted them on the left. After looking through a million threads nobody seemed to have a solution, and most thought it was a bug.

Well turns out its quite simple. Right click on the panel and uncheck "expand." Then drag the panel to the side of the monitor you wish to have. Then recheck expand. That simple.. I noticed a lot of people had an issue with this so I thought that I would share this with anybody if nobody has posted this yet.
-shibby
