### Post by halvdansk on 2006-11-14
[SIZE="3"]Step 1[/SIZE]

[SIZE="2"]Open a terminal..[/SIZE]

**gconf-editor**
**Apps > Metacity > General**
check the **reduced_resources** box

 :: 

[SIZE="3"]Step 2[/SIZE]

[SIZE="2"]In the menu..[/SIZE]

**System > Preferences > Assistive Technology Support**. Enable it.
[I]
(to get rid of the wire frame)[/I]

*done!*
