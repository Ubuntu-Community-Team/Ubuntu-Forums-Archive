### Post by Elfy on 2012-10-21
Further to Forum Council meeting of 20th October 2012 and previous discussions in Forum Feedback and Help and during previous Forum Council meetings, this forum is now closed to new threads.

Existing threads can be posted to for now, but the forum will be closed completely to posting after a transitional period.

Outdated Tutorials and Tips is now closed to all posting.

New tutorials should be written to the [community wiki]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community").

Further information can be found here [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1949027](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1949027)
