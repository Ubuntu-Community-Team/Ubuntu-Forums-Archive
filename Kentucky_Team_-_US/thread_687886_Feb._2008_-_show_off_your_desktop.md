### Post by jackdale on 2009-12-23
Here's mine for what it is worth... :)

[IMG]http://img51.imageshack.us/img51/6575/screenshot19r.png

Why do I like it?  With minimal customisation, I have a desktop that fits in much better with the default brown Human Theme. 

FYI
Background = Manti Utah Temple (The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints)
- Top Panel: 34 pixels; Added Force Quit; Connect to Server (for FTP, etch); Weather Report (more functional than the Clock weather)
- Bottom Panel: Removed and replaced with Cairo-Dock (Curve) + Desktop Switcher + custom launchers as seen.  Also has a the weather applet (I like to know my weather).
- Other stuff (not seen in the pic): Compiz Fusion + Emerald added.  Also, (just a little quirky thing) the terminal has a nice translucent brown background with white font.  Firefox has the Persona "A Web Browser Renaissance", which is a nice brown FF persona.  Also the ugly gray window background was replaced with #F6EAD8 (goes much better with the Human theme brown).
