### Post by jonathonblake on 2007-10-23
[QUOTE=asdfqwert]Would ubuntu CE be suitable for Orthodox christians?[/QUOTE]

It should.

The major issue is that none of the Bible Study Software includes all of the material that is found in the Orthodox Canon.

The minor issue is that the tools that precalculate the date of Easter, do so according to the rules of Catholic Christianity, not Orthodox Christianity. 

I'm not aware of software that is written specifically for Orthodox Christians.
