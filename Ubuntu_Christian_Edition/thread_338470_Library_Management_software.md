I'm using Alexandrian Book Collection Manager for my private library -- you can check it out through Applications>Add/Remove Applications.  As I say it works for my private library -- your mileage may vary.

### Post by chaplanger on 2007-02-07
UPDATE:
Alexandria Book Manager crashed on my system and is unusable -- so far I have received no help from the folks at Ruby.  Before investing much time and effort in this program you may want to think twice.  Also has not been actively developed since 2005. . . 

BTW there is an online solution -- Library Thing.  For more than 200 books the cost is either $10/year or $25 for 'life'  you can check this out at [http://www.librarything.com/](http://www.librarything.com/)  It's had rave reviews in the PC press (either PC World or PC Magazine fairly recently)
