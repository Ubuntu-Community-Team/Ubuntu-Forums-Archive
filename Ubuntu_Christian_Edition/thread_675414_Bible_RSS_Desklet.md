Well I don't know if this is the right procedure or not, but it worked for me.
[INDENT]1. Main Menu, Sys, Admin, Synaptic Package Manager. Search, gDesklets. Two Items came up. I installed both.[/INDENT]

[INDENT]2. Main Menu, Accessories, gDesklets, Up at the top of the gDesklets window there are three locations (File, Profiles, Help) I chose files, Install package. I pointed to your tarball, which was on my desktop & finished[/INDENT]

[INDENT]3. Ctrl, Alt, Backspace. I had an error line 50 something I think 56. I was a news URL. I selected to fill in the ignore errors radio button on the pop up window. I might look into that later, but I will probably forget about it soon enough 8)[/INDENT]

[INDENT]4. Main Menu, Sys, Pref, Sessions, >add gDesklets< Ctrl, Alt, Backspace[/INDENT]


It seems to work great!
