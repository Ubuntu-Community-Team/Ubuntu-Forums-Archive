There are 2 Italian versions: Italian Giovanni Diodati Bibbia 1649 and Italian Riveduta Bibbia (1927)  Not sure if they are what you want.  But anyway, to install them:

Click on "Edit->Module Manager"  Then on the module manager" panel, under "SWORD" click on "Configure->remote->Refresh".  Then under "Modules" click on "install->Biblical Texts->Italian" and select the versions you want then click on the Install button at the bottom.
