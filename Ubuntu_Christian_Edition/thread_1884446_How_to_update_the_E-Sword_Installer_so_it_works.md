### Post by Archangelos on 2011-11-21
If you need to update your E-sword installer, and download E-Sword 10.05 the easy way it takes about 20 seconds.

*Go you My Computer.

*Search for E-Sword Installer

*Open E-Sword Installer in Gedit.

*And replace the 960.exe url with this
                                 

**[http://www.e-sword.net/files/setup1005.exe](http://www.e-sword.net/files/setup1005.exe)**
 
and in the cache with 

**/setup1005.exe**

*Save.

*Download E-Sword 10
