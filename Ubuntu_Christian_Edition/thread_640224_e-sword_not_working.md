### Post by jonathonblake on 2007-12-16
[QUOTE=Drate]e-sword?  Is that another front end to the Sword libraries? [/QUOTE]

No.  *e-Sword* is a Windows Bible Study program.   It is gratis, but not libre. *The Sword Project* programs are libre, but not necessarily gratis.  

I think that e-Sword is far superior to any the front ends that are part of *The Sword project*.  (Disclaimer: I wrote *The e-Sword utility Program FAQ* and *The e-Sword Module Database*.)
