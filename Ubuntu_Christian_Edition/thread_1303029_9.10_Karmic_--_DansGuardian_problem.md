In the mean time, why don't you try below package:

[http://ubuntuce.com/repos/Ubuntu_CE/jaunty_i386/dansguardian-gui_2.2_all.deb](http://ubuntuce.com/repos/Ubuntu_CE/jaunty_i386/dansguardian-gui_2.2_all.deb)

Download and install it manually. Once it is installed, find the menu at System>Administration>Dansguardian GUI.

After launch, choose:

 Autoconfigure/reset dansguardian and Tinyproxy

Make sure all three indication on the top bar is on. If there is one off, try Stop Dasnguardian, dan then Start Dansguardian, it should be all on now.

After that try again those websites that dansguardian having problem.
