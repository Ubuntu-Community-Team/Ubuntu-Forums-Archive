[SIZE="3"]There are two things that you can do right away to help your situation: 1) when in your browser, push the CTRL key and the + key as many times as you like until you get a font you are comfortable with ( push CTRL key and the - key to do the opposite), and 2) you can go to System, Preferences, & Font to pick the font size you would like for most other things.

I hope this helps! :)
