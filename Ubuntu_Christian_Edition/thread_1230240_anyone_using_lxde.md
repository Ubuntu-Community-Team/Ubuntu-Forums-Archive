### Post by Caraibes on 2009-08-03
LXDE is nice, but I recommend Fluxbox for an older laptop... As a matter of fact, I am using LXDE tools in Fluxbox, such as PCmanFM as a file manager...

As of myself, I enjoy Fluxbox with Nautilus as it gives me the freedom to ssh or smb into other PCs in my local network...

Keep in mind, in Fluxbox, the Nautilus command is: 
```
[exec] (Nautilus) {nautilus --no-desktop --browser}
```

I haven't find anything faster than Fluxbox, with its ease of use... It seems to use less resources than OpenBox...
