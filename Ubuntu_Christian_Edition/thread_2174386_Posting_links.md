Construct an extension for Firefox, Chrome, etc, that automatically configures the browser to display the links on the start page. Place that extension on the respective extension page, and then announce its availability here.

When Ubuntu Christian Edition was first released, Firefox came with bookmarks to various Christian sites, and a Christian orientated theme.  Perhaps your proposal could be included in the 15.3 release of UCE. (I am assuming that it is too late for the 13.10 release.)

> My message was deleted as spam.

With a religiously oriented distro, the line between software and the underlying theology becomes extremely thin. As a general rule of thumb, the mods delete posts which are on the software side of that line, but could be interpreted as being religious in nature.
