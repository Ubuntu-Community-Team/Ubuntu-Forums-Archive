Im sleepless tonight...
And im starting a new job in the morning.

But im dreaming here.
And I have to say this outloud.
Or post as the case may be.

Im thinking about how crazy the idea is...
of a bunch of seemingly individual unconnected dreamers could over time
become what is emerging as linux.

Im thinking about how crazy people told them they were to believe something could ever even exist let alone function.
