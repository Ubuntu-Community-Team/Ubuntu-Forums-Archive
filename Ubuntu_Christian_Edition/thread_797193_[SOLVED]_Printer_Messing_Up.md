### Post by retiree on 2008-05-18
Go to HP.COM and click on support and drivers.Type in your model number and follow the links to Linux operating systems. You will then be redirected to Sourceforge.net, and there you will find drivers for your printer. When downloaded and installed you can then control your printer settings from your computer. I have a HP D7260 printer and that's how I did it. Hope this helps, Regards Retiree
