### Post by mysticrider92 on 2007-06-05
53c is not that bad for a video card (my slightly overclocked nVidia 7600 GT stays around 50-52c when I am playing America's Army, and about 49c idle). Most computer chips are made to take well over 70c without problems. If you are really worried about it, you could put a pci fan under it, but I wouldn't worry about it.

I don't know why the screen would mess up though. What kind of card is it? Does it have a built in fan or not?
