### Post by stlsaint on 2015-04-08
Greetings Alex,

As much as I wish it was, simply updating to the latest LTS is just not that easy. UCE is ubuntu with the addition of multiple apps, customizations, etc so if you were to update the entire distro you would lose what makes UCE, well UCE. Works of a meta-package were underway but ceased and not much else support given. If you are looking to build a UCE on top of the current LTS shoot me a message and I will gladly aid you. As far as a fresh UCE 14.04 release, that is some time down the road or until further developmental support is garnered.
