### Post by stlsaint on 2010-04-16
Since UCE was made to be used not-only on desktops but primarily in churches there are a few things in place for security. IE: Dansguardian firewall along with rules. Also it is catered towards studying the bible so there is alot of bible software in there. The 10.04 release will have some new software along with some apps from previous releases. We cannot tell when the 10.04 UCE release will be considering that DK (the primary developer of UCE) will have to test and ensure stabliity before he releases it. Its really up to him when the release will be.
