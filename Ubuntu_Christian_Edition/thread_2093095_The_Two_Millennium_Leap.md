This author was musing upon "cowboy baptism" one day and whilst searching the world wide web, found two, rather remarkable, images.  

One image is that of where, supposedly, Jesus was baptised by John the Baptiser.

The other was a somewhat "modern" version of a "cowboy baptism" which is usually done in a "stock tank" but this had occurred in what seemed to be a.... how shall one say it..?  A "construction" of plastic sheeting and some kind of filled bags or berming.

Be that as it may, the two images merged in this author's mind and the result is what is linked below which is provided as a background for the use of one and all.
