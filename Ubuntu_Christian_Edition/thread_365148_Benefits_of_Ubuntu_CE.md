### Post by chadders on 2007-02-19
I'm Agnostic, but despite my choice of religion I would like to open a thread that commends the creation of Ubuntu CE.

First and foremost, Linux is a great OS and the introduction of a Christian OS (remembering that a large majority of people are Christians) is opening these people to a world of free software. Secondly it allows Christians to have programs they would use included as default and they wouldn't need to download the software after the installation of the OS.

Please feel free to add further benefits Ubuntu CE.
