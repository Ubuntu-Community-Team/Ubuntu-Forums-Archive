### Post by mysticrider92 on 2006-12-26
In Ubuntu (or any other Debian-based distro) the packages are converted to work better with the distro before being put in the repositories. This causes some delay in releasing new packages and kernels, but the programs work much better and work together, saving a lot of confusion. If you really want the new version, you can download and compile it yourself, but I would recommend waiting until it is in the repositories.
