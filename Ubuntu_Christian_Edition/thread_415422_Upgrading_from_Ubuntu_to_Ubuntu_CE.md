### Post by mysticrider92 on 2007-04-21
You can use the [convert_me]("http://www.whatwouldjesusdownload.com/christianubuntu/2007/02/ubuntu-ce-v2x-edgy-downloads.html") script to go from Ubuntu to CE. Simply download, unpack it, and click on the convert_me file. Tell it to open in terminal, enter your password, and it will do everything for you.
