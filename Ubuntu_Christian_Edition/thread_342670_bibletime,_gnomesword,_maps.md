### Post by chaplanger on 2007-01-21
> **anv said:**
> I miss some of maps which were on e-sword, is there any similar for these linux versions?

One clarification:
gnomesword and bibletime are not versions of e-sword (developed by Rick Meyeres -- [http://www.e-sword.net/](http://www.e-sword.net/))

gnomesword and bibletime are projects of "The Sword Project" from The Crosswire Bible society ([http://www.crosswire.org/index.jsp](http://www.crosswire.org/index.jsp))


To answer your questions about maps:
At this time -- no maps are listed on The Sword Project site. ([http://www.crosswire.org/sword/modules/index.jsp](http://www.crosswire.org/sword/modules/index.jsp))

### Post by integr8e on 2007-10-10
Though it's been some time since the last post, I am bringing new information to this thread.  If you visit [**_*this*_**](http://www.crosswire.org/wiki/index.php/Module_Repositories) URL, you will see a list of Sword repos; navigate to BibleTime>Settings>"Bookshelf Manager">"Install/Update works">"Add library" to add the repos listed.

In the "Kleinpaste" repo, there are numerous map modules you can choose from.

Just thought y'all would like to know,
integr8e
