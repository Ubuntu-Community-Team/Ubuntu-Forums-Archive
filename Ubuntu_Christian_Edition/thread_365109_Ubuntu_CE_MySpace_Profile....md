### Post by mhancoc7 on 2007-02-19
Let me first say that I do not particularly care for MySpace. I find it to be a very dangerous place for our youth to spend time and I think the design off most profiles speak for themselves.:shock:

Now with that said, I have recently been tinkering with MySpace. It all started when my wife's friend from college convinced her to get one to help them stay in touch. When I saw my wifes profile, which actually looks pretty nice, I decided that I could make a cool looking MySpace profile. So I spent the next few days tweaking a personal MySpace profile. It was actually fun. Mainly because it was such a challenge to work around the terrible design of it to create something that I could be happy with.

Once, I finished my personal profile I decided to build and Ubuntu CE profile. I have ripped it to shreds and built it back being careful to stay within the TOS (barely).

You can check it out here. [www.myspace.com/ubuntuce]("http://www.myspace.com/ubuntuce")

I would love to hear what you all think. This is not really an official marketing effort. It is more for my personal enjoyment of tinkering.

Thanks, Jereme

[http://www.bytemycode.com/snippets/snippet/556/](http://www.bytemycode.com/snippets/snippet/556/)
