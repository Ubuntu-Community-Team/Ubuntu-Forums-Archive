Hi Jonathon,

Can't answer your last three question but adding a religious or Bible entery to your menu is very easy.

right click on menu button >click Edit Menu> Add Menu > type Tittle (I used bible study)> click ok.  Now you should have a menu Item named Bible Study in your menu list.  Next go to Accessories and drag and drop E-Sword , Etc into your new menu. That's it. 
You may have to restart X to see them all.
