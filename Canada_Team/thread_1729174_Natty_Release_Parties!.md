### Post by flyingsquirrel625 on 2011-04-14
The Natty Narwhal (Ubuntu 11.04) is coming out in just two weeks!  Are you throwing a release party?

If you are, please put it in the [LoCo Directory]("http://loco.ubuntu.com/teams/ubuntu-ca").  So far, we have parties in Kitchener, Toronto, Montreal and (probably) Vancouver.

You don't have to do anything big and fancy (although bonus points and kudos if you do).  Just get a bunch of fellow Ubuntu enthusiasts out to a local cafe, restaurant or pub to celebrate a new release!
