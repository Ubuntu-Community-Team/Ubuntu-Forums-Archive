### Post by BobJonkman on 2014-03-15
Happy St. Patrick's Day, Everybodeee!  On Monday, that is. Let's have
a traditional celebration at Egg Roll King and drink green tea and
have Vietnamese green curry!  OK, maybe that's not really a tradition.
Yet.

Some people have mentioned to me that they can't make it to Ubuntu
Hours on Thursdays and Fridays, so I've booked a spot at ERK on
Monday. Also, I mentioned it to some Open Data people, so I'd like to
invite them along as well, and anybody else who'd like to come.
Although it's an Ubuntu Hour, we're not checking your Operating System
at the door; in fact, computers aren't even required (although some of
us do bring them). I figure there's enough overlap in the community to
carry on a couple of hours of interesting conversation.

**What**: O'buntu Hour Kitchener
**When**: Monday, 17 March 2014, 7:00pm to 9:00pm
**Where**: Egg Roll King Restaurant, 85 Courtland Ave. W., Kitchener
**Map**: [http://osm.org/go/ZXnwSzXf?m=&way=195517589](http://osm.org/go/ZXnwSzXf?m=&way=195517589)
**Register**: [http://loco.ubuntu.com/events/ubuntu-ca/2720/detail/](http://loco.ubuntu.com/events/ubuntu-ca/2720/detail/)
(optional, but appreciated)


Hope to see you Wearin' the Green!
