### Post by BobJonkman on 2013-04-22
Hi Everybodeee! This is the week of the Ubuntu 13.04 Raring Ringtail release, and so there are Release Parties:

The [KW chapter of Ubuntu Canada]("https://launchpad.net/%7Eubuntu-waterloo-region")  is having a release party for the latest version of Ubuntu, named  “Raring Ringtail” on Thursday, 25 April 2013 at 7:00pm at the [Kwartzlab Makerspace]("http://kwartzlab.ca"). 

You’ll meet Ubuntu people from all over Canada during the [IRC meeting]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/CanadianTeam/Meetings/2013-04-25")  from 7:00pm to 8:00pm; the Kwartzlab Radio team will be recording a  podcast at 8:30pm; and there will be a live installation demonstration  on a fancy new Lenovo laptop with UEFI and SecureBoot.  

And, of course,  there will be cake, deviled eggs, and we may order out for pizza…
