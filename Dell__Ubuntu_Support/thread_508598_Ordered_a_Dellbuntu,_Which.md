### Post by wvmac on 2007-07-26
I just received my inspiron 530n.
1.6 ghz pentium dual core
1 gig of ram
dvd burner
nvidia geforce 8300gs
everything else is stock

I am suprised at how nice the case is.  I am replacing a g4 mac mini that is running gutsy (x86 linux is just better).  The case matches my mini with the white and silver.  It doesn't look cheap.  The video support could be a little better. I installed the nvidia drivers with Envy but I still had to mess with the refresh rate and gama.  My mini had the colors dead on but  my dell made the colors washed out on the same monitor.  It is a 19 inch wide screen so it has problems with all distros at the initial setup but I have had even more issues this time.
Other than the initial video problems everything works really well.  Desktop effects are smooth.  Everything is so much faster than with my 1.4 ghz g4 mac mini not that it is slow.  With OS X it would get bogged down even with 1 gig of ram.  With ubuntu it never bogged down, but it was never really fast either.

I am really happy with my new dell.  A few months ago I would never have bought a Dell, but I felt compelled to support a major company preinstalling linux (especially ubuntu) and I needed/wanted a new pc. It appears that Dell is trying to change their image. I told my dad what I bought and he thought I was joking.  I had nothing good to say about them before. It's funny how much little changes in a company can affect its customers. Hopefully my purchase does not come back to bite me. 

And did I mention that it is FAST!
