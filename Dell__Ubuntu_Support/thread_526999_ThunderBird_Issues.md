### Post by mackis5 on 2007-08-16
Account Settings - "Choose your account"  - "Composition & Addressing" - Check the "Automatic quote....." AND select "start my reply above the quote"

---

### Post by DonA on 2007-08-16
Try this..,

Go to Edit => Account Settings => Composition & Addressing (for the appropriate account, if you have several).  In the Composition section of the window, check the "Automatically quote original message when replying" box, and change the "Then" selector to "start my reply above the quote'.

Regards:   ..DonA
