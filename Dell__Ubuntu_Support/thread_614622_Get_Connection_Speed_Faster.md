### Post by Dellfan on 2007-11-16
What wireless card? Some cards definitely work better than others with Linux. 

Also, are you in exactly the same place with the same environment when you are doing your comparison (moving even a couple of feet can change things dramatically, and other RF noise like cellphones, microwaves, other wireless routers, cordless phones, cordless mice, etc can also make a difference).

You certainly can get decent wirless performance on Linux... I have 3 different 802.11G cards that all deliver full performance.
