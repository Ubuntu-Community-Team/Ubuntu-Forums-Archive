### Post by notwen on 2008-01-15
I have a first gen Inspiron1420n that came w/ Feisty pre-installed and have had no issues.  I run Virtualbox for any windows applications I was unable to find a alternative for, but would not recommend graphic intensive gaming in V-Box.  The restore image available via GRUB will wipe your HDD and restore your system to like-new status.  Installing Windows after Ubuntu is fairly simple, although installing Ubuntu after Windows is pretty straight-forward, just search for one of the multiple how-tos available here on the forums or net.  The new 1420n(Gutsy) comes pre-installed w/ DVD playback standard, the original Feisty version needed you to install various multimedia plugins/packages.  Good luck w/ your future purchase, should you choose to go w/ the Inspiron 1420n. =]
