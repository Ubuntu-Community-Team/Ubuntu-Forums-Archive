### Post by the-kernelpanic on 2008-03-02
Just thought I would post to let everyone know that I've had great success with the install on my D420.  Here are some highlights:

Currently dual booting with Windows XP, but haven't booted into it since my install of Ubuntu. I'll probably remove it and claim back some disk space soon.

Power management works - although, I can't suspend to disk since I made my swap too small.  Not a problem, and hibernate works fine.

All apps are working great. With Crossover Office installed I have my full Office Suite, Project, and Visio working - no issues.

I have NCPFS installed to access my Novell network drives.

I have Novell Groupwise Client installed to access work e-mail on the native client. Used an rpm and converted using Alien. Installed a missing lib and it works great.

DVD is great - DVD playback via Ogle.

MMC/SD card reader works great.

Intel 945GM video works great and I use it dual head at work with no issues running 1280 x 800 on the laptop and 1280 x 1024 on an external in extended desktop mode using xrandr.

My local HP2300 USB printer was detected and works great as does my networked HP 2605 Color Laser printer.

Both wireless and wired network interfaces worked with no problems and connecting to my home WLAN using WPA was a simple task.

Overall  I am very happy with the distro and plan to keep this one around for a while.

Chris
