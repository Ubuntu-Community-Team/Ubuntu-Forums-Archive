### Post by snaildarter on 2007-11-02
I hear you.  My 600m crapped out too upgrading from Feisty to Gutsy.  Seems there are some pretty serious issues with the Radeon used in the 600m.  You can look at the Gutsy release notes for more info. 

After the upgrade went kaput, I tried completely reinstalling Gutsy from scratch.  Still no love.  I'm currently dloading Fiesty and will just keep it until support runs out.

I think that the lesson I've learned is to just go for the LTS releases, and don't upgrade unless I've got a darn good reason to.

Good luck!
