### Post by gbrainin on 2008-02-19
I have no idea what your problem is, but since it's telling you that it's a hardware problem, have you tried calling Dell about it?  If it's still under warranty, they will swap out the bad hardware, once they're convinced it's a hardware problem.

If your computer came with Ubuntu preinstalled, call the Dell Linux support line at 866-622-1947.  DO NOT call any of their regular support numbers: Linux is not on their script, so most of the CSRs don't know what to do with you, even if the problem is obviously hardware.
