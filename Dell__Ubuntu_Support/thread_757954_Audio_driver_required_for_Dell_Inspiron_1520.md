### Post by edajai on 2008-04-25
the driver is installed by default but the volume is very low. Follow the instructions given below:

1. right click on the speaker icon on the system panel on the top n click on preferences.
2. Now click on front and then close.
3. Right click on the icon again n open the volume control.
4. Increase the volume of front all the way up.

this will do. test `n c if the sounds are working..
