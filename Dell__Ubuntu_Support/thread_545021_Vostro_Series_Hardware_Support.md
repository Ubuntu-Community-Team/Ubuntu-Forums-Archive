### Post by PeggySue on 2007-09-09
I have a Vostro 1000 with AMD64 X2 Athlon, 1390 wireless card and Radeon video card.

The wireless didn't work out of the box but this fixed it for me:
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=297092&highlight=wireless+network](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=297092&highlight=wireless+network)
saru411 added a post saying this method was better/easier but I haven't neede to try it.
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=405990&highlight=1390+wireless](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=405990&highlight=1390+wireless)
I understand that the Intel 3945 chipset works out of the box but I'm not sure.

Suspend works reliably but I don't use hibernate. 

The battery power options for sleep timing are available but the screen brightness control doesn't work.  There are advanced power management applications around but I don't need those. 

I think the Vostro is stunning value for money and hope this partial answer helps.
