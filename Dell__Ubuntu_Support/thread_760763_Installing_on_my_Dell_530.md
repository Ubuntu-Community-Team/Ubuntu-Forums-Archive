### Post by sandmanfvr on 2008-04-21
I got a Dell Inpiron 531 (AMD Version) and just downloaded from Ubuntu.  You can go to Control Panel -> Administrative Tools -> Computer Management in Vista and resize any partitions and leave some free space and then in the install when it comes to the partitioner, point to the free space and put the mount point to "/" without quotes and format it and let it install.  It will put a boot loader and it will let you boot Vista, Ubuntu, and even the Dell Utilities Partition.
