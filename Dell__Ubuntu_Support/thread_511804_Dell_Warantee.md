### Post by 5-HT on 2007-07-28
I was hesitant about getting CompleteCare...but it has saved me.
About return to depot or on-site service, I guess it depends on your needs. Can you afford to be without for a week or two? If you have a pretty customized setup that can't be replicated on another system while you're waiting, or you don't have access to another system, on-site can be a godsend. I was quoted as having a week-long turn around to get some work done; from the order going through, to sending the packing material, to having the system back in my hands (not bad at all IMO, and they'll pay for shipping....other OEMs not so much). At the time, I forgot I purchased NBD on-site and was back up and running the next day. Overall, I'm very happy with Dell's support.
