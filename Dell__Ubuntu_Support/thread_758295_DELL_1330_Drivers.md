### Post by LeoSolaris on 2008-04-17
Try running a few searches.

The codecs for things like .wmv are located in the medibuntu repository. (google medibuntu or look through Ubuntu's Documents in the support page.)

The wireless, the mic, and the cam most likely have HowTo's already on the forums. You will need the exact names of the hardware, then just search the forums for that. You can most likely get the names from System->(either Admin or Pref, I forget which)->Hardware Information. (i am in Windows at the moment, so if it's not the exact names, look for something reasonably close and your most likely right) If the hardware is not listed there, then there is something rather wrong. Look it up on your Window's side or in the owner's manual.
