### Post by rcdegges on 2007-09-03
What are the stats of the computer you are using? Specifically, how much ram and what kind of hard drive is it (IDE, EIDE, SATA, etc). I've had slight problems with the installer before, but they were simply solved by rebooting the machine and trying again. If you have very little ram (256MB and under) the install process will take a while and may even hang up, in which case you should just... try again.
