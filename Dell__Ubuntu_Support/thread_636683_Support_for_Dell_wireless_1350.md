### Post by kgr132 on 2007-12-20
On my D600 with Gutsy I did this.
Go to System...Administration...Restricted Drivers (type password)
Check the box for the "Firmware for Broadcom 43xx chipset family".
It will tell you need to supply the firmware. I connected via wired LAN and used the download source suggested. If you don't have wired access, I don't see any reason why you couldn't download the firmware to a usb stick or something using another box and point it to that instead.
Good Luck.
