### Post by Deathmoon on 2007-10-31
I purchased the Dell XPS 410 with Ubuntu 7.04 on it (first PC I've bought ever!).  The best thing about it, is out of the box everything worked.  Video, audio, NIC, etc.  The most difficult part about building a computer and getting linux on it, IMO, is that you have to research every single piece of hardware to make sure that you can find drivers for it and that you get the full functionality.

I ended up taking the wireless NIC out of my old comptuer and placed it into the XPS therefore no extra sunk cost.  

I recently upgraded to 7.10 and everything except my dual monitors worked; that took some tweaking.
