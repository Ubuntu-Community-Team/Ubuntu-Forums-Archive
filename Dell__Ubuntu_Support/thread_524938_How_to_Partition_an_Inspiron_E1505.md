If other CDs boot properly, but the Gutsy CD does nothing, I would guess that either your download or your CD burn messed up somehow.  Take a look at [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BurningIsoHowto]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BurningIsoHowto") for some ideas on how to check or re-try, as appropriate.

Also, if your Dell BIOS is like mine, there should be an F-key (F12 on mine) you can press on boot to get a boot menu (not the BIOS configuration menu).  This allows you to override the BIOS settings and specify exactly what you want to be the boot device on a one-time basis.
