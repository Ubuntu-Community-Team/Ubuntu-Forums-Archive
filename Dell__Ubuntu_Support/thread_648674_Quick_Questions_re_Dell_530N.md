### Post by gbrainin on 2007-12-24
> **poorbadger said:**
> Just finished up w/ an online chat w/ a dell rep but am not convinced I received accurate info (she didn't know what SATA or eSATA were) so I thought I'd turn to the forum in case someone has one of these machines.

1. I understand Dell only configures a single hard drive in this machine but is there a mounting bay for a 2nd or 3rd and the SATA connectors to hook them up?

There are a total of 4 SATA connectors on the motherboard, for both hard drives and optical.  There are two bays for optical drives and two places to mount hard drives (vertically).

> 2. Is there an eSATA connection (external SATA)?  I'm guessing no.

You are correct, no eSATA.  There are 8 external USB connectors, and you can order a firewire connector as an add-on.

> 3. How many open PCI slots are there? PCI Express?

IIRC, there are a total of four slots (2 PCI, 1 PCIe x1, 1PCIe x16); the 16 is used by the video card.
