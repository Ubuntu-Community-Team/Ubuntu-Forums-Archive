### Post by virtuallinux on 2008-03-27
Yeah, definitely try the live CD first and see what happens.  As the wireless is a Broadcom chipset, it's not likely to work out of the box, but you can usually get them to work with a little work. Personally, I've got a Dell Latitude D820, same processor, same ram, and I've not had any hardware problems to speak of, although the audio port on my docking station doesn't work with Ubuntu.  I've also worked with a couple of Broadcom chipsets in the past, and have generally enjoyed success in making them work.
