### Post by neptho on 2007-09-06
Does it completely freeze?  What happens when you press Caps Lock?

If the keyboard is responding, try pressing Ctrl-Alt-F1 to get to the primary screen, then Ctrl-Alt-F7 to go to the 'logging' screen.  This should tell you at least where it 'is' when it hangs.
