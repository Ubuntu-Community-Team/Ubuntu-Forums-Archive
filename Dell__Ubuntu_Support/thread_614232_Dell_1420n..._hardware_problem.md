### Post by inchaos on 2007-11-16
Just FYI, if you're hardware savvy, it doesn't void your warranty to open up your computer to check the connections, as long as you don't damage anything.  I also have a 1420n and I've taken the keyboard out (which is what you'd need to do) and it was very easy.

Your comp should have come with a user manual which has instructions in the back as to how to do it.  If it's something simple, you might be able to take care of it yourself without waiting for weeks to get your comp back.

It might be something more complicated, but it's worth a try.
