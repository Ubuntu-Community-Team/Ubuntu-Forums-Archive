I know this is an older thread, but thought I would add to this in case someone stumbles upon it searching as I did. 

I would highly recommend checking with the following two locations to be sure that your card is supported. I have recently underwent a search for PCI cards that will work and seem to pick all of those that do not work. 

If you are looking for a card be sure it is listed as working or better and that it is the right speed (400 vs. 800) that you are looking for.
