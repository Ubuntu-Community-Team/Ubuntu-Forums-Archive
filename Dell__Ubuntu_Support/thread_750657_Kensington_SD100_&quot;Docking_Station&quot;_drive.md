### Post by shizeon on 2008-05-01
More than likely this would have worked just fine.  Most peripherals in linux do not need 'drivers'. In most cases it will just work (graphic drivers being the most common exception).  Things like USB ethernet adapters and USB sound cards are fairly common and supported out-of-the-box.  Most manufacturers will claim they don't support linux, but that doesn't mean someone hasn't already hacked support for it in to the kernel...  The SD100 doesn't have usb video, if it did, it would probably be the exception to this rule.
