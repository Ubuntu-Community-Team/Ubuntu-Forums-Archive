### Post by detroit/zero on 2007-07-08
slackorama: The battery icon and network selector are in the notification area. Just to the left of the leftmost of those two you should see some kind of indicator (depending on what theme you use) of where the notification area starts. After unlocking that and the other applets that stand between where the notification area *is* and where you want it to be you can right-click>move it to wherever.

It's a little hard to make out in my theme, but here's an illustration:

[IMG]http://i140.photobucket.com/albums/r6/james48089/Screenshot-2.png[/IMG]

Those little dots indicate the notification area and it contains the two icons to the right, which are the same two you're talking about (I believe).

As far as having the icons move around and all that, I have the same problem from time to time.  I'm not sure how to fix that part... I notice that if I just leave them wherever they appear they don't ever move around again. Occasionally, though, my notification area and the logout button will change places. It's been about two weeks now since I had to mess with them, though. I think I did a Save Session after I got them right last time.. maybe that had something to do with it?
