### Post by ImNeat on 2007-09-23
Dell N-series computers, which come with Ubuntu pre-installed, have the integrated intel X1300 video card which doesn't play nice with wine - therefore making it impossible to play games like WoW.  The problem, to my understanding, is that the linux driver for the card is extremely poor compared to its windows counterpart.
