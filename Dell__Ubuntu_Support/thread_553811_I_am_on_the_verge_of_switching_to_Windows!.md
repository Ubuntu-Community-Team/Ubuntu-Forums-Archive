### Post by strabes on 2007-09-20
Here's a post about the Dell 720. It worked for me on edgy. Dunno if it will work on gutsy. [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=215657](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=215657)

To play DVDs you need to install libdvdcss2 which can be found in the [medibuntu](http://medibuntu.sos-sts.com/) repository.

I also recommend installing the ubuntu-restricted-extras package, and the gstreamer ones which will play .mov files and other proprietary formats. By the way, VLC is the best media player available in my opinion.

The reason you are not able to convert music purchased on iTunes is because 1) it is apple's proprietary format and 2) it is DRM infected. It is absolutely NOT ubuntu's problem. You will not be able to convert it with any program on windows or OS X either. 128kbps is not even worth listening to, let alone paying for. There are many options available to you, none of which would be appropriate to mention in this forum.
