### Post by kuja on 2007-09-11
I think for now you're likely better off with the intel X3100 chipset. It's true that the nvidia chipset would net you better performance, but Nvidia seems to be having trouble all over the place with the nvidia geforce 8xxx series linux drivers (a real shame because they work really well with my 7900 ...). I'm sure they'll lock down the problems and fix them but that will take time. It seems the drivers for the x3100 on the other hand are for the most part stable and trouble-free [color=red]right now[/color].
