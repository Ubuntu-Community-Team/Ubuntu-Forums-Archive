### Post by strabes on 2007-06-04
You could try adding an option to that line that specifies the color depth and resolution of the bootsplash screen by editing your menu.lst: ```
sudo gedit /boot/grub/menu.lst
``` > kernel /boot/vmlinuz-2.6.20-16-generic root=UUID=e0e37017-1b07-48b9-a965-7d01c5bb50f1 ro quiet splash **vga=791**

Or you could try disabling the bootsplash by removing "splash" from the following line: > kernel /boot/vmlinuz-2.6.20-16-generic root=UUID=e0e37017-1b07-48b9-a965-7d01c5bb50f1 ro quiet **splash**

I hope that either of these fixed your problem.
