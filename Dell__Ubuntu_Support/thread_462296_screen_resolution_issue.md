### Post by RichPicker on 2007-06-02
I'm using a Dell Inspiron 1300 and I installed 915resolution from the synaptic package manager. It fixed my screen resolution. Before installing it, everything on the screen was squashed somewhat and distorted, because it could not display the prefered resolution. After installing it, all has been great.
