### Post by MrSootentai on 2007-12-27
I've got a 1420 not the N mainly because I wanted the webcam feature that the N does not offer but my post should be relevant (the 1420 and 1420N aren't that different).

1) Yes it comes with ethernet,modem, and wireless networking builtin. As far as I understand to get the modem working you need to get some drivers installed but I believe that is on the Dell Wiki.
2) Compiz Fusion works for me perfectly, but when I try to play games the laptop crashes
3) I've gotten my battery to last a good 3 hours with some power managment tweaking
4) The keyboard is hard to use a first and honestly, typing for a long time DOES hurt my fingers (even though I have relatively average size hands)

Other than that I LOVE my 1420 with Gutsy. My only main concern is getting my Compiz and games running perfectly, and fixing my hibernate and suspend issues.
