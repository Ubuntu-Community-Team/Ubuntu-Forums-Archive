### Post by x001 on 2008-01-21
I currently own a Dell XPS M1530:
[LIST]
[*]Core2 Duo 2.4Ghz
[*]3GB Ram
[*]160GB HD (Not SSD)
[*]1680x1050 LCD
[*]Nvidia 8600M GT (8400M Video Card works with linux too)
[*]Dell Wireless 355 Bluetooth Internal (2.0+Enhanced Data Rate)
[*]Intel Next-Gen Wireless-N Mini-card 
[/LIST]

I have Ubuntu 7.10 (used the Dell Ubuntu 7.10 DVD) installed and everything works great out of the box. The only thing in your list that will not work is the Creative Sound Blaster X-Fi Sound Card. Creative is currently working on a 64bit driver but it is in the early stages and I dont know if they are even planning a driver for 32bit.
