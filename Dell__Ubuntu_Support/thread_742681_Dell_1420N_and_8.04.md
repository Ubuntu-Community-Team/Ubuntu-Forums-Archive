### Post by drsaamah on 2008-04-02

It didn't even recognize my wireless card (it only gave eth0 and lo interfaces).  But this is my personal experience, when I went on the forums I found no one else with my wireless card who had this experience.  I would suggest backing up your data and making an install disk for Gutsy, and then trying out Hardy.  If you end up not liking it, just go back.
