### Post by HermanAB on 2007-06-22
The backlight will eventually change colour. You can replace it yourself.  It is not difficult.  Some googling will find places that will even cut cold cathode tubes to size for your laptop. Just search for CCFL - cold cathode fluorescent lamp and zillions of shops will pop up.  

Here are two that look reasonable:
[http://www.lcdparts.net/CCFL.aspx](http://www.lcdparts.net/CCFL.aspx)
[http://www.ccfldirect.com/](http://www.ccfldirect.com/)

You have to disassemble the screen in order to measure the tube - don't guess.  I have fixed my laptop twice already - the invertor keeps breaking.

Cheers,

Herman
