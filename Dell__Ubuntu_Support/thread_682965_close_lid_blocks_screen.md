### Post by neptho on 2008-02-01
When you re-open the screen, let the computer sit for about 30 seconds.

Press the power button.  Does it prompt you for a username and password?  If so, try [the i8042 unbind](http://ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=4039288&postcount=214) hack, which will at least get your input devices back after a suspend attempt.
