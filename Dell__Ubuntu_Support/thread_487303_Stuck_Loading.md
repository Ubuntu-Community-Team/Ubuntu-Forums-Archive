### Post by neptho on 2007-06-29
> **AuroraAura said:**
> what do i do lol i just rebooted my computer and everythings new cause after that 1/2 install of ubuntu i lost everything lol

Unless you still have a restore partition, or a reinstall CD, you're, to be technical, hosed, until you can get another installation CD burned.  I assume since you are on the internet now, you have a computer capable of burning another CD.

Burn the CD at a very low speed, if possible, I suggest 4x.  This works with most cheap CD media, and even if it says it will do 48x or 52x, 4x is almost guaranteed to produce a stable CD.
