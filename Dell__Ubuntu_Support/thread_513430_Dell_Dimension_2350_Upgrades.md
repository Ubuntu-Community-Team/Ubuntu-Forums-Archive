### Post by Redache on 2007-07-30
hmm looking at the Tech specs a PSU upgrade might be worthwhile. 200 watts is insufficient to run anything decent.

Since the documentation seems to state that the Mobo can only support 1gb ram, then it might be worth just reaching that limit for maximum performance.

As for Video card, you could try any Nvidia card, but I should warn you that I do not advise this without a new Power supply, you will get problems with the current offering.\

 Soundcard wise it really depends on what you want to achieve.

if you just listen to music it's always better to invest in high quality speakers than it is into a soundcard, as most of the sound quality you perceive will be coloured by the speakers regardless (polishing a turd comes to mind).

That's it really unless you want to wack a DVD burner into it.
