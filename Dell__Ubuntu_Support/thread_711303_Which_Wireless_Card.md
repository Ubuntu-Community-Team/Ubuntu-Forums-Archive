### Post by gonesolo on 2008-02-29
The slot you have on your laptop is called an express card slot.

It's smaller than the old PCMCIA card.

You can find more information (and source an Expresscard adaptor) from this site

[http://www.expresscard.org/web/site/wirelesswan.jsp#adap](http://www.expresscard.org/web/site/wirelesswan.jsp#adap)

Also can you identify which wireless nic is in your 1300? There are a few options on that laptop!!
