### Post by flamacue on 2007-12-22
I was running Feisty, just upgraded to Gutsy, on my 700m.  I have 1.5 GB RAM, 2 GB swap, and I am NOT using any desktop effects (beryl/compiz).  I am pleased with the performance.

I agree with the above poster, try disabling beryl/compiz/"desktop effects".

Also, you may find it helpful to add the "System Monitor" icons to your panel.  Personally, I go crazy if I can't at least see what my CPU usage is; I also like to see my RAM and HDD.  To add them:

[LIST]
[*]Right-click on the panel, and choose "Add to panel"
[*]Scroll down to the "System & Hardware" section
[*]Click the "System Monitor" icon and choose Add
[*]Notice the icon(s) that appear in your tray.  Right click on it (them) and choose preferences.  Customize it to your liking.
[/LIST]
