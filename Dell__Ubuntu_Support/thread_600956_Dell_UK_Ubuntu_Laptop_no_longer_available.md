OK, I guess you have never changed HOST name on your computer, as the default is 'Dell' (so you see 'Dell login:' - what this is is 'Machine NAME + login: prompt).  After Ctrl+Alt+F1 just enter your EXISTING username and then password.  You will then be logged in to a console.  To exit, just type 'exit' and hit return.

Now to get back to your original console, just hit Ctrl+Alt+F7... or easier, use Alt+Leftarrow (or rightarrow) to cycle through the consoles.

No need to hit the button :-)
