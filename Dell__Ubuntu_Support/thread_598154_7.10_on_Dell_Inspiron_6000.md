### Post by dacap06 on 2007-10-31
Ubuntu 7.10 uses a newer kernel than 7.04 does.  If suspend and hibernate are important to you, then do not upgrade to Ubuntu 7.10 yet. Instead, keep what you have and watch the Ubuntu wiki for news saying the suspend and hibernate problems have been fixed.
