### Post by Simon G Best on 2007-10-19
> **WrathofthePenguin said:**
> 1. Does it have a parallel port (my relative still has a parallel printer that Ubuntu supports)

No, it doesn't.  At least, mine doesn't.  I doubt you can get one from Dell with a parallel port, but you might be able to get a card from elsewhere with a parallel port on it (that you then plug inside your 530n).  Or, I suppose, you could look for a USB-to-parallel-port adapter thing, that you could then plug into one of the USB ports.

Basically, parallel ports, and the like, are so old now that they've already been replaced with USB ports.
