### Post by sc3252 on 2007-08-23
You can buy laptops directly from dell that come preloaded with linux.

[http://www.dell.com/content/topics/segtopic.aspx/linux_3x?c=us&cs=19&l=en&s=dhs](http://www.dell.com/content/topics/segtopic.aspx/linux_3x?c=us&cs=19&l=en&s=dhs)

The program you are talking about that is sorta some what, but not even close to like photoshop is gimp.  To be honest I did a lot of photoshop in school and I cant do much with gimp at this time.  So if you have people skilled with photoshop I would recomend getting xp.  I love ubuntu, but I would hate to see a nonprofit organization waste time and money on programs they cant use. 

Of course if you really want to use open source then I recommend checking out gimp and find out for yourself if it meets your needs.  [http://www.gimp.org/](http://www.gimp.org/)
