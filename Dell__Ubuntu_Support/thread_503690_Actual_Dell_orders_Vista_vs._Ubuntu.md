First, let me say that this is NOT a commentary about Vista vs. Ubuntu (or Linux in general). Neither is it a commentary on Dell - so far Dell has far OVERperformed on one order (the other has not shipped yet, but it is far from being late). So far, so good on both orders.

I recently had reason to order two laptops for family members. One of them insisted that they wanted Vista, the other was a surprise birthday gift, and I ordered a 1505N (that's one of the models that ships with Ubuntu). I ordered the Vista laptop on 7/1/07, the Ubunut on 7/4/07.

The Estimated Ship Date for the Vista laptop is 7/20/07.
