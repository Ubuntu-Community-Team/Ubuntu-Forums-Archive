### Post by bluedragon436 on 2007-11-02
I don't guess this is soo much an Ubuntu related post...but I have noticed that since my HD does seem to spin a good bit faster since I installed Ubuntu on my laptop that the area around my HD seems to heat up a bit more and faster...so I have found that on my Dell D610 that below the cooling fan for the laptop that there is a little screen that I took my computer apart and removed it and then ran my computer and noticed it stayed a bit cooler, then I removed all of the heat sinks and cleanned them off and then reinstalled them using some new thermal paste instead of the crap that the Dell came with and it seems to stay even cooler.  I have done these same "upgrades" with the last few Dell laptops that I have owned...still runs hotter than I would like, but lower than it was running....well worth the free mod time spent....  but that is just my opinion...
