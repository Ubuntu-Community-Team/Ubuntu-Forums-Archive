### Post by ahaslam on 2007-08-24
It's warm but If it's max temp is 64C in the hottest time of year I wouldn't worry too much. Core 2 Duo's should not exceed 70C, it's at this point you should be concerned.

I don't know much about your specific model, but there's often bios settings for temperature warnings. I recommend you set it to 70C, you could also lower this to 60 to check the accuracy of your applet.
