### Post by notwen on 2007-10-17
What exactly do you need?

> Big hold up is getting oti-of-the-box system that includes printers, scanners, photocard readers, etc. I went to Dell web site and started to configure a system only to find I could not buy printers, scanners, etc., which would be compatible and factory-supported.

Printers, scanners and photocard readers are out there that are compatible w/ Linux.  I highly recommend HP printers/scanners for Linux compatibility.  There are lots of options you just need to research them.  You may not want to limit yourself to just Ubuntu/Debian as lots of offices use RHEL and SLED and an abundance of productivity is guaranteed w/ those OSes.  Ubuntu should be capable of what you're wanting, but you'd need to find the compatible equipment and set it up correctly.
