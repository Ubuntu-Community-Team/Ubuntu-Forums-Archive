### Post by soupwell on 2007-08-12
I haven't called for support for my 1420N yet, since I just ordered it.  I will say that none of the sales people knew what I was talking about when I called with ordering questions.  None of them (I talked to 5 different people) were able to give me any information that I couldn't pull off of the website, and you could tell that they were reading it from the same website.  I even had to direct 3 of them to the open source PCs section; they had no idea that it was there.

I am grateful that a large manufacturer has chosen to offer Ubuntu to the general public, but it seems that it will take some time for even the Dell sales staff to get up to speed.  I was surprised that my call wasn't directed to a specialty Linux Sales/Support unit.  The creation of such a unit would seem to be a speedy and relatively inexpensive alternative to training the whole staff on the new machines.
