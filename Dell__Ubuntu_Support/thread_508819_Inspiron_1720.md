### Post by cstrippie on 2007-07-25
I'm running the 1705 with a very similar config (except video) - the 3945 works just fine, as does the audio, with the possible exception of the subwoofer: doesn't dynamically adjust to volume level.  Other than that, the machine is an Ubuntu monster.

Oh, if you have a problem with proprietary drivers, then the binary drivers for the Intel may be a problem for you.  For god's sake don't get broadcom.
