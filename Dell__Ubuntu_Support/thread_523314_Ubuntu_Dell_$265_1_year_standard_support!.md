### Post by arkmundi on 2008-04-29
I just purchased a Dell Inspiron 1525, and was forced to ponder the question of support.  I bought the "Basic support" for $125, which comes from Canonical, for peace of mind for someone new to Liunux and Ubuntu (I'm moving from Windows XP).  

It took Dell routing my call six times before they could answer my simple question of what I'm buying in "Basic Support."  I was finally able to ascertain that indeed its the standard level agreement described at [http://www.canonical.com/services/support/sla]("http://www.canonical.com/services/support/sla") .  A call to Canonical and a friendly representative confirmed that the more expensive package is for advanced users, and that the $125 "basic support" from Dell is sufficient.  Reading the Desktop Service Level Agreement version 1.2 has provided reassurance to me of the value having the agreement.
