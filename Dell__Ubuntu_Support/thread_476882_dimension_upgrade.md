### Post by joe dude on 2007-06-27
Last weekend I tried installing feisty on my daughter's old Dimension 2400 (dual boot w/XP).  

I'm running a E520N ubuntu preinstalled (very nice and solid) and installed feisty dual boot on an old HP pavilion (ati video card) without too much trouble.

Eventually gave up on the Dimension (after about 12 hours solid work).  Problems with very slow boot and intel video drivers - all well documented problems on the forum without easy fixes.  Also I had a problem getting the CD-roms to read the install discs consistently.  

Just a word of warning - getting ubuntu onto an old Dimension 2400 may be a bit of a challenge (more than I was ready for).
