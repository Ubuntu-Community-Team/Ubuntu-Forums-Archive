### Post by rockin_goliath on 2007-12-18
I recently went through this problem as well getting a computer for my dad. He wanted a desktop that had a 256 mb nvidia card, and they were only offering 128 mb. He called the sales department, and the guy we spoke to had never even heard of Ubuntu! I couldn't believe it, even after he figured out what Ubuntu and Linux are, he was puzzled as to why we weren't buying any anti-virus software! He finally ended up selling us a desktop with a 128 mb card in it and a separate 256 mb card and credited the cost of the 128 mb. 

My point is, if you talk to someone in the sames department and tell them exactly what you want instead of ordering it online they will probably work something out with you. I've heard of some people in Germany getting IBM laptops and getting refunded the cost of their *unused* Window$ licenses. Is that possible with Dell?
