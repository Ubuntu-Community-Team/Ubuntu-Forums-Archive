### Post by rivalarrival on 2007-09-11
Piece of cake - drop the Ubuntu disk in the cd drive, boot, click the icon that says something about installing ubuntu, and when it starts asking you where you want to install the drive, tell it to set up the partitions on the new drive. 

That's the gist of it, more instructions are available here:
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WindowsDualBoot](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WindowsDualBoot)
or here:
[http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/installing](http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/installing)

There's a short (14 minute) tutorial here - Not exactly the most professional piece of work, but whatever works...
[http://video.google.com/videoplay?docid=-6104490811311898236](http://video.google.com/videoplay?docid=-6104490811311898236)

You can install ubuntu from within Windows
[http://wubi-installer.org/](http://wubi-installer.org/)
(I've never done this myself - your mileage may vary)

Good Luck!
