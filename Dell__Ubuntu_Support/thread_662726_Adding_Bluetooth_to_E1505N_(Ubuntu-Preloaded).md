### Post by neilius on 2008-01-10
The 355 works without problems. It's easy to install, you open up a little trap door inside the battery compartment and plug it in to the existing cable in there. Dell has pictorial guides in the service manual on their support site: [http://support.euro.dell.com/support/edocs/systems/ins6400/en/sm/btooth.htm#wp1000550]("http://support.euro.dell.com/support/edocs/systems/ins6400/en/sm/btooth.htm#wp1000550"). I ordered one off eBay after buying my E1505/6400. Here in the UK, Dell don't offer a bluetooth option when purchasing this laptop for some reason.
