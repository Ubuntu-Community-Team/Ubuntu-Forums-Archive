### Post by sr20ve on 2008-03-11
One thing I've noticed when connecting an external display, either vga or svideo. Is that it must be connected when X is started, or it won't detect it. So either attach the svideo and boot, or if it's already running, attach the svideo and hit ctrl-alt-backspace.

hope that helps.
