### Post by tbroderick on 2007-09-23

> Are you using the normal 7.04 disk, the Gutsy release, or the Dell Remastered CD?

I used the netboot mini.iso to install. Tried both Gutsy and Feisty. Everything works except hibernation and suspend on Gutsy and hibernation on Feisty. I got suspend to work on Feisty by editing some values in /etc/default/acpi-support. Didn't work in Gutsy. Gutsy's restricted driver manager found both the ATI driver and wireless firmware while Feisty's only found the ATI driver. Vista wasn't as bad as people have said. It booted and ran fairly well. That being said, Vista is no longer installed. :)
