### Post by fragos on 2008-01-19
On my 1420n the following three lines in xorg did the trick for me.  They are a good starting point.  The mouse preferences "touchpad tab" dosen't give you these controls but you may wish to use it to disable taps.

Option "MinSpeed" "0.5
Option "MaxSpeed" "2.0"
Option "AccelFactor" "0.035"
