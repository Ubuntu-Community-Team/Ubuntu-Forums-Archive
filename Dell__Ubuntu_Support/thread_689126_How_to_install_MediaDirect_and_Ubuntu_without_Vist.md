### Post by mattyp1 on 2008-03-01
KimH,

The Ubuntu version of the M1330 does not come with MediaDirect. It's not likely we will ever see a "how to" that does not require Vista. I believe the reason for this is the MediaDirect software is windows based (XP embedded). I tryed to do away with it with no luck. You're better off following the directions for triple boot:
[http://www.daryl.mu/2007/12/29/howto-triple-boot-dell-xps-m1330-with-vistaubuntu-and-media-direct/](http://www.daryl.mu/2007/12/29/howto-triple-boot-dell-xps-m1330-with-vistaubuntu-and-media-direct/)
