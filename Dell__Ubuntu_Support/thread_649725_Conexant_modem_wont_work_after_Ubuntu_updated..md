### Post by duanekf2jc on 2007-12-25
Hi everyone,
   I bought my son a Dell 1420N laptop with Ubuntu 7.04 & the conexant modem.  We got the modem configured & working at home, then my son went back to college. In his dorm, we used the wireless network to upgrade the software with Synaptic Package Manager. My son is back home for Christmas vacation, and the modem doesn't work. The modem doesn't seem to dial out - no dial tones are heard. Other computers work with the same phone cable. I checked all the modem settings, and they have not changed. We suspect the software upgrade may have disabled the modem software, but we don't know what to do. Any help would be appreciated. Thanks!

Duane

---

### Post by duanekf2jc on 2007-12-31
Hi Everyone,
   Our modem problem has been solved. When we did our first software update, the Dell rep said that the kernel was updated and the modem software was deleted. We downloaded & reinstalled the modem software.

Duane
