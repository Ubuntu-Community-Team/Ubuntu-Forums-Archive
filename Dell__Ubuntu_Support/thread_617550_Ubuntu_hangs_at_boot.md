### Post by saru411 on 2007-11-20
boot into recovery mode...you do this during the boot process....hit esc and pick the recovery option....then while it reboots look to see if its doing fsck on your hdd. if so this will happen every 20-30 reboots and this is one way to see whats going on...but i think if you leave your laptop alone on a normally reboot it will do this also...the thing is, you will not see it on the screen. this tends to scare alot of people.

well thats what i do and it works just fine...after you let the laptop check the hdd it will boot into recovery mode, from here just reboot like normal and you should be just fine.

hope this helps =)
