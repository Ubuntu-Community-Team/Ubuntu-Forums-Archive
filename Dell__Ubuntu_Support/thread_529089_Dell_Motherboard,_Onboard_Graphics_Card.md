### Post by pgar23 on 2007-08-18
If u have a DELL motherboard (possibly other motherboards, but I am only familiar with the Dell) and it has an onboard GRAPHICS CARD, and you are having trouble with resolution...

1. Upgrade your BIOS 
> A. go to dell.com 
B. search bios 
C. click bios for your dell computer
D. select model , select product family , select product line , select product model , confirm
E. download BIOS driver then reboot
2. Enter BIOS and go to the INTEGRATED DEVICES.....<enter>

3. Change Onboard Video Buffer from 1 MB to 8 MB. 

This should solve your resolution problem.
