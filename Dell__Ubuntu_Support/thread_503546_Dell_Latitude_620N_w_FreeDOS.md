### Post by strabes on 2007-07-18
Really the only hardware that most people ever have problems with nowadays are some brands of wireless cards (broadcom) and some specific video cards. (mostly ATI) When you buy it, just make sure you get an Intel PRO Wireless card and an nvidia video card. If you do, everything *should* work 100% out of the box.
