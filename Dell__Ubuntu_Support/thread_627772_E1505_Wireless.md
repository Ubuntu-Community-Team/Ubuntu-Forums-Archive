### Post by Dellfan on 2007-12-01
If you bought it with Ubuntu preloaded you have a Intel 3945 a/b/g card which is rock solid and really well supported. Unless something has changed on your system you may have an actual hardware problem. If it is flaky from the Live CD and from Vista you likely have a hardware issue. 

Are you having issues connecting to know good networks (ie ones that either you can connect to from another machine, or someone else can connect to)?

I would call up Dell tech support and get a new wireless card.
