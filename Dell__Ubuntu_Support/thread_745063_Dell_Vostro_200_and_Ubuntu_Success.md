### Post by ryank82 on 2008-04-04
Just figured I would share my recent success with my new Dell Vostro 200 Mini and Ubuntu 8.04. I purchased the Dell Vostro 200 Mini tower with the Intel Core 2, 2GB,250GB, integrated intel graphics for $349. As soon as the machine arrived I didn't even bother booting in to Vista I just dropped in the Ubuntu cd. Wiped the partitions and installed Ubuntu. Everything was automatically detected after the install. Sound, network, video etc.. Even my 24" Widescreen lcd was detected and ran at its native resolution of 1920x1200. After I ran software updates it seems as if even everything got smoother and better yet. This is great as previous machines it seemed I always had a problem with 1 or 2 pieces of hardware. Just figured I would let everyone no that it works great and its a super nice machine.
