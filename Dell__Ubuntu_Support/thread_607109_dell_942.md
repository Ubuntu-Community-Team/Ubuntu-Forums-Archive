### Post by kuja on 2007-11-08
I don't see it listed on openprinting.org, but by the looks of this list: [http://openprinting.org/printer_list.cgi?make=Dell](http://openprinting.org/printer_list.cgi?make=Dell) the odds of it working in linux at all aren't looking too good.

AFAIK Dell printers are rebranded lexmark printers, and well, Lexmark is notoriously awful with regards to linux support. 

I recommend looking for a new printer, and do be sure to check that it will work at linuxprinting.org before getting it.
