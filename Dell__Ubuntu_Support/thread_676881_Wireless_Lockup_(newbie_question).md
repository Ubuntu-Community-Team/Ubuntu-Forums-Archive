### Post by kplaxmaster on 2008-01-25
Does the laptop only freeze up when your wireless is on??

Also, you may want to search to see if your router has any firmware updates.  Sometimes routers lock-up if a certain type of device attaches to the network, same goes for laptops if connecting to a specific router.  Although this is rare, it actually has happened to me.

Give us more information on when your laptop freezes and try starting your laptop without wireless enabled.
