### Post by DonA on 2007-12-24
Out of curiosity, do you have headphone sound?  Several months ago my E1505 speaker sound suddenly disappeared.  After checking the usual configuration settings (mixers, etc.) I finally booted the live CD.  Still there was no speaker sound.  However headphone sound was find.  I found an earlier posting on this site that mentioned that there was a known problem with the headphone jack that caused this problem.  Unfortunately it's not an easily-replaced part; it requires a motherboard change out.  With some reluctance, I sent my E1505 back to Dell for repair.  Sure enough, they swapped out the motherboard, and speaker sound has worked fine ever since.  (Frantically knocking on wood as I type...)
