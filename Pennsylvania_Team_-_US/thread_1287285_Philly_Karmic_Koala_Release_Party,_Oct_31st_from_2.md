### Post by elizabeth on 2009-10-10
At the Mythbuntu Jam last weekend Andrew Keyes and I brainstormed our way into deciding upon the Manayunk Diner for our Karmic Koala (9.10) release party on Halloween from 2-6PM. By Monday Jim Fisher had booked the location.

***Details:***

**Date :** Saturday October 31st, 2009
**Time :** 2:00 pm
**Location :** Manayunk Diner
3722 Main St
Philadelphia, PA 19127
(215) 483-4200

**Wiki page:** [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/PennsylvaniaTeam/EventsTeam/PhillyKarmicRelease](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/PennsylvaniaTeam/EventsTeam/PhillyKarmicRelease)

And costumes are ENCOURAGED! Every year only a few of us dress up, everyone else brings along the excuse "I didn't want to be the only one" - you won't be :)

And you can enjoy the obnoxious graphic I put together because I fail at design ;)
