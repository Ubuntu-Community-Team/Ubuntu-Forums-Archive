Hello!
The FOSSCON 2017 Call for Speakers is now officially open! This year
FOSSCON will return to IHP ([http://ihousephilly.org/]("http://www.google.com/url?q=http%3A%2F%2Fihousephilly.org%2F&sa=D&sntz=1&usg=AFQjCNEKQrgO9uhuaWH2E_O0sCpkRUNTVw")) in University
City, and will be held on Saturday, August 26th.

We are looking for speakers on topics involving open source
technologies, software and communities. You can submit your talk for
consideration at [https://fosscon.us/node/add/speakersub]("https://www.google.com/url?q=https%3A%2F%2Ffosscon.us%2Fnode%2Fadd%2Fspeakersub&sa=D&sntz=1&usg=AFQjCNGU36gkFWc7FGvmPUrehcAhxhPgCA")
For more information on FOSSCON, visit [https://fosscon.us/]("https://www.google.com/url?q=https%3A%2F%2Ffosscon.us%2F&sa=D&sntz=1&usg=AFQjCNG2GR5_Xg_p5SB1Vv9rlmMlCd20tA")

If you have any questions, you can reach out to me directly here or on
IRC (JonathanD in #plug or #fosscon.
