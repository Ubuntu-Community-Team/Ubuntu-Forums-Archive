[COLOR=#000000][FONT=Times New Roman]The [Ubuntu Local Community Team]("http://ubuntupennsylvania.org/") always sponsors an [Installfest]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2154886&p=12693454#post12693454") at Fosscon. This year, we had an open Installfest. Any [distro]("http://distrowatch.com/dwres.php?resource=major") that wanted, could represent themselves and be part of the Installfest.[/FONT][/COLOR]
[COLOR=#000000][FONT=Times New Roman][Fedora]("http://fedoraproject.org/") stepped up to that challenge. Ben and his wife drove all the way from North Carolina to represent Fedora. They are wonderful people and wonderful advocates. It was a pleasure working with them.[/FONT][/COLOR]
[COLOR=#000000][FONT=Times New Roman]

[COLOR=#000000][FONT=Times New Roman]The Installfest yielded 2 new installs and 1 issue resolution (performed by the Fedora Team). The 2 new installs came via [NTR]("http://ntrweb.org/").[/FONT][/COLOR]
