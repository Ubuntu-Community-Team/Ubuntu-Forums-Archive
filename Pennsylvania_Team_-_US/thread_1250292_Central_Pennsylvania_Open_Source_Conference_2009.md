### Post by bfledderjohn on 2009-08-26
Hi all!  Our illustrious Lyz has been selected to give a talk at [[COLOR="Blue"]CPOSC 2009[/COLOR]]("http://www.cposc.org") in Harrisburg, PA on Oct. 17th.

Besides attending to support her, we'd like to be one of the sponsors and get a table at the event.  $100 gets us a Bronze sponsorship, which will give us exposure on their handouts, marketing materials, and a table at the event.  I've requested a conference pack, and have listed the event on [[COLOR="Blue"]Conference Appearances[/COLOR]]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/ConferenceAppearances") .

Here's the rub.  We need to drum up $100.  Not a large sum of money, but I am asking for some donations to offset the expense.  This will be a great event to promote Ubuntu, and to really get the ball rolling in Central Pennsylvania.

Besides Ubuntu and Lyz, there are going to be some great talks, so I would encourage everyone who is close to consider coming to CPOSC!

I've put a Paypal donation button on my [[COLOR="Blue"]CPOSC entry in my blog[/COLOR]]("http://breadthofreality.blogspot.com/2009/08/cposc-2009-and-pennsylvania-loco-team.html"),
so if you can throw a couple dollars at this, it would be greatly appreciated!

Also, after the event I thought it would be nice to get together at the  [[COLOR="Blue"]Appalachian Brewing Company (ABC)[/COLOR]]("http://www.abcbrew.com/harrisburg/brewpub.htm") on Cameron Street for some local micro brew and socialization!
