### Post by jedijf on 2013-10-13
[h=2]What is CPOSC?[/h][COLOR=#555555][FONT=arial]The Central PA Open Source Conference (CPOSC) is a small, low-cost, one-day conference about all things Open Source. It was started in 2008 by a few of the members of the [Central PA Linux User Group]("http://cplug.net/") and the [Central PA Ruby Meetup]("http://www.meetup.com/ruby-102/").[/FONT][/COLOR]
[h=2]Will there be a CPOSC this year?[/h][COLOR=#555555][FONT=arial]Oh, yes! CPOSC 2013 wil be held on Saturday October 19th, 2013!  It will be held at the IU13 Conference Center in Lancaster, PA.

More Info: [http://cposc.org/](http://cposc.org/)[/FONT][/COLOR]
