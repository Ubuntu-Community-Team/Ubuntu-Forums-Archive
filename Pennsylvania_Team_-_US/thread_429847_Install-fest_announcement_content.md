******** START ********

**[SIZE="5"]Ubuntu Install-Fest June 2, 2007[/SIZE]**

[SIZE="4"]You may have heard about Ubuntu.  You wanted to try it but didn't know where to begin.  Are you sick of computer viruses, malware, spyware, and constant hardware upgrades?  Come to our install-fest and let our volunteers help you become a part of this amazing computing phenomenon by installing Ubuntu Linux on your PC.
