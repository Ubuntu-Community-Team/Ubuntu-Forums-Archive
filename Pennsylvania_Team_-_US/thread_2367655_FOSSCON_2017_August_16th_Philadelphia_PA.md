### Post by jedijf on 2017-08-01
[FOSSCON]("https://fosscon.us") 2017 is coming up fast! [FOSSCON]("https://fosscon.us") this year is on Saturday,
 August 26th at International House of Philadelphia. FOSSCON is [FREE to attend]("https://fosscon.us/attend"), with an optional donation.

This year at[ FOSSCON]("https://fosscon.us") we have great [talks from speakers]("https://fosscon.us/Speakers") around the
 world. Learn about the Open Sourcing of Infrastructure, how to better
 monitor your environment, how punch cards are still with us today, or
 even take your [Amateur Radio License Examination]("https://fosscon.us/node/12643"). 

You can visit [https://fosscon.us/Speakers](https://fosscon.us/Speakers) to see a selection of the speakers for
 this year.

[Registration is also open]("https://fosscon.us/attend"), and you can visit [https://fosscon.us/attend](https://fosscon.us/attend)
 to learn more and to register today.

Stop by the Ubuntu PA booth for some swag!
