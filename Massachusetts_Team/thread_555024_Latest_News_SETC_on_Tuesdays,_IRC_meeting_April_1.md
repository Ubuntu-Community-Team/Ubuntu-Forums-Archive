**[size="4"]Classes[/size]**
**What:** Help spread the word on ubuntu by training the Boston community on using ubuntu and computers in general. We also help clean up donated computers and install ubuntu on them. These are provided back to the community at little or no cost to people who need them. All are welcome each Tuesday to either help out, learn or just hang out and listen. 
**Where:** [South End Technical Center]("http://www.tech-center-enlightentcity.tv/contactus.html") ([Map]("http://tinyurl.com/3y9qox"))
**When:** Every Tuesday 6:30pm - 8:30pm EST
More information at [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/MassachusettsTeam/Events/Educational/SouthEndTechCenter](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/MassachusettsTeam/Events/Educational/SouthEndTechCenter)

In addition, there are meetings on Wednesdays at the SETC to do advocacy work and preparations.


Information on current, past, and future events can always be found at [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/MassachusettsTeam/Events](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/MassachusettsTeam/Events)
