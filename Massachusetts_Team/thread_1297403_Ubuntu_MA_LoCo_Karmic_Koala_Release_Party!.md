### Post by leftyfb on 2009-10-21
Come join us for our Ubuntu 9.10 Karmic Koala release party. It will be held at the Globe Bar & Grill on Boylston St in Boston, MA on November 7th at 7:00 PM EST. There will be finger food provided as well as wait staff taking orders for drinks and dinner if you want something else. Donations are welcome to help pay for the reservation. 

More information can be found on our wiki for the event here: 
[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/MassachusettsTeam/Events/ReleaseParties/9.10-KarmicKoala](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/MassachusettsTeam/Events/ReleaseParties/9.10-KarmicKoala)

Please add your name as well as any guests to the list at the bottom if you are coming.

We should have burned cd's of the final release that night. Pressed CD's aren't available yet on the release day. 

Bring friends and family and even a laptop or 2 if you would like to install the new release there or to show off your existing install. 

Hope to see you there!


***UPDATE:** The date of the party has been changed from Thursday, October 29th to Saturday, November 7th. Same place and time.
