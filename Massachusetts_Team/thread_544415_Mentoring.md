Nobody has yet offered to mentor work for Ubuntu United States MA.

You can learn more about this capability in Launchpad, which allows you to create an "onramp" of bite-sized chunks of work for new people who want to contribute or to prove their worth.

In summary, you can encourage members of your teams to offer mentoring on bugs and blueprints that they know how to fix or implement, but which they don't have time to get to personally. This list then makes a good starting point for new community members who want to make a contribution but don't know where to start.


This would be a good opportunity to get involved. 

Should we set up a poll and see what we might want to work on?
